Time travel     time travel is the idea of going back in time to the past or forward to the future . time travel can not be done , so far as we know now . many science fiction and fantasy stories are about time travel . `` the time machine '' ( 1895 ) by h. g. wells was one of the first and most famous . much later , the american movie `` back to the future '' tells a fictional story about a professor who builds a machine that can take people into the future or back in the past . the popular british tv program `` doctor who '' is about an alien who has adventures by time traveling . another example of a made-up story about time travel is in the series `` charmed '' . instead of using machines and science , they use magic to go to other times and places . some series that are not mainly about time travel have episodes about it , including `` star trek and `` stargate '' .
