Taste bud     taste buds are small 'bumps ' on the tongue . they are the sense organs which detect the taste of things that go into the mouth . they are involved in detecting the five ( known ) elements of taste perception : salty , sour , bitter , sweet , and umami . other websites . - beyond the tongue map - taste perception : cracking the code - scientists explore the workings of taste buds from national public radio 's `` talk of the nation '' , july 22 , 2005
