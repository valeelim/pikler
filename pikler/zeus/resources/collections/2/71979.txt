1952 Winter Olympics     the 1952 winter olympics , officially known as the vi olympic winter games , were held in 1952 in oslo , norway . a total of 30 nations sent athletes to compete at these games , which was the highest number to that date . this was the first time new zealand and portugal participated in the winter olympic games . australia , germany , and japan returned , after having missed the 1948 winter olympics . korea , liechtenstein , and turkey did not participate in 1952 , after having competed in 1948 .
