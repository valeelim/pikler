Chi-square distribution     in probability theory and statistics , the chi-square distribution ( also chi-squared or formula_1 distribution ) is one of the most widely used theoretical probability distributions . it is used in statistical significance tests . it is useful , because it is relatively easy to show that certain probability distributions come close to it , under certain conditions . one of these conditions is that the null hypothesis must be true . another one is that the different random variables ( or observations ) must be independent of each other . other websites . - chi-square tutorial by khans academy
