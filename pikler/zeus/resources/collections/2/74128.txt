Senna (herb)    senna ( herb ) senna candolleana or golden senna associates with `` lithrea '' and `` porlieria chilensis '' in central chile matorral or forms pure stands.. traditionally `` senna '' , a genus including 250 species , was classified as `` cassia '' . near 200 species appear in south america , fourteen of them in chile . this evergreen shrub , 5 to 6 feet tall ( 1.5-1.8 m ) , 6 feet wide ( 1.8 m ) ; dark green leaves is suitable as ornamental . http : //www.ildis.org/legumeweb/6.00/taxa/12954.shtml
