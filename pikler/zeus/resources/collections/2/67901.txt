Warwick Estevam Kerr     warwick estevam kerr ( born september 9 , 1922 , santana do parnaíba , são paulo , brazil ) is a brazilian engineer , geneticist , entomologist , and professor . he made many discoveries in the genetics and sex determination of bees . he is also responsible for the spread of africanized bees across the world . other websites . - warwick estevam kerr biography . brazilian academy of sciences .
