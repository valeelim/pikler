Björk     björk was born björk guðmundsdóttir on 21 november 1965 in reykjavík , iceland . she is an icelandic singer , composer and actress . she mostly writes her own songs . she is best known for telling people how she feels with emotions and for her unusual voice . she sings mostly in english but sometimes in icelandic . she often makes music on her laptop . she has a son and a daughter , sindri , and isadòra . she lives in new york city , but also owns a home in reykjavík . björk was in the 2013 television documentary `` when björk met attenborough '' with sir david attenborough . discography ( albums by björk ) . - `` björk '' ( 1977 ) - `` gling-gló '' ( 1990 ) - a jazz album where björk sings mostly in icelandic - `` debut '' ( 1993 ) - `` post '' ( 1995 ) - `` telegram '' ( 1996 ) - remixes from `` post '' - `` homogenic '' ( 1997 ) - `` selmasongs ( 2000 ) '' - music for the movie made by lars von trier , `` dancing in the dark '' - `` vespertine '' ( 2001 ) - `` greatest hits '' ( 2002 ) - `` family tree '' ( 2002 ) - mini compact disc boxset of rare and unreleased works
