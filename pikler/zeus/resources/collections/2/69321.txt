Themistocles Leftheris     themistocles `` themi '' leftheris ( born december 20 , 1982 ) is a figure skater from the united states . leftheris has skated pairs for most of his life . he has had many partners and has done very well with them . in 2005 , he started skating as a team with naomi nari nam . they placed 5th at the 2006 nationals , a very good result for a new team . they won the bronze medal ( third place ) at the 2006 skate america competition , which was even more good . they won the bronze at the 2007 nationals and then placed 6th at the 2007 four continents . themistocles leftheris is a very powerful skater . he and nam are a good pairs team . they do very hard jumps for a pairs team , which is helped by nam 's good singles career . other websites . - naomi nari nam and themi leftheris online - official website - international skating union biography - u.s. figure skating biography
