Mansur Al-Hallaj     abū al-mughīth hossein ibn manūr al hallāj ( c. 858 - march 26 , 922 ) ( ) or hallaj was a persian poet and writer . hallaj was born in shūshtar , iran . hallaj memorized the quran when he was young . he taught sufism in baghdad . muslim scholars were against his teachings . he was imprisoned in baghdad for eleven years . in the end , he was tortured and he was beheaded by abbasid rulers .
