Silicone     silicones ( ) are polymers that include any inert , synthetic compound made up of repeating units of siloxane . this is a functional group of two silicon atoms and one oxygen atom frequently combined with carbon and/ or hydrogen . they are typically heat-resistant and rubber-like . silicones are used in sealants , adhesives , lubricants , medicine , cooking utensils , and thermal and electrical insulation . some common forms include silicone oil , silicone grease , silicone rubber , silicone resin , and silicone caulk . silicone is also used for cosmetics . silicone has very little to do with the element silicon ( ) .
