Population pyramid     a population pyramid is a graph . it has two back-to-back bars . these bars show the population in groups of ages , with woman in one side and man in the other side . the pyramids usually show the development of a country . when you look a pyramid you can see the life conditions of the country . there are three types of population pyramids : - stationary pyramid - expansives pyramid - constrictive pyramid in this type of graph , a wide base means a very high birth rate and a narrow base means a high death rate .
