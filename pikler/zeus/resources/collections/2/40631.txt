Chord     a chord in music is when two or more notes are played together ( some text books say that it is when `` three '' or more notes are played ) . a chord which is built up of the 1st , 3rd and 5th notes of a scale is called a `` tonic triad '' . if it is made from a major scale , it is a major chord . if it is made from a minor scale it is a minor chord . however , any combination of notes is a chord . chords can be played on instruments such as piano , keyboard , organ , harp , guitar , harmonica , ukulele and xylophone . chords with two notes can be played on string instruments by bowing on two strings together . by bowing on two strings and then quickly on the other two strings a violinist can sound as if he is playing a four-part chord . some players of wind instruments are able to make chords by playing two or more harmonics together . related pages . - harmony - interval ( music )
