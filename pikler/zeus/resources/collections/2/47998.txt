Journalism     journalism is a type of writing . the term is used to describe the activities of newspapers , news shows on television , news shows on radio , and news magazine . there are many different news and information jobs in the field of journalism , such as the jobs of newspaper reporter , television news anchor , writer , editor , sub-editor , illustrator , and photographer . most people use journalism to express themselves and put their feelings into what they are writing . people who work in journalism jobs are called journalists .
