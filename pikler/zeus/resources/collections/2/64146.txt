Phosphine     phosphine is a chemical compound . its chemical name is phosphorus hydride . its chemical formula is ph . it is also called phosphane and phosphamine . it contains phosphide and hydrogen ions . it is a colorless , flammable gas . it is highly toxic ; it can easily kill , even when concentration levels are low . it smells like fish . it can ignite without a spark . when it burns , it makes a white cloud of phosphorus ( v ) oxide , which is irritating . it is made when hypophosphites and phosphites are heated . it is also made when white phosphorus reacts with warm alkali . the gas is used for pest control . it is also used in making semiconductors . related pages . - ammonia
