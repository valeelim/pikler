Limburg-Weilburg Rural District     limburg-weilburg is a landkreis ( rural district ) in the west of hesse , germany . 1867 the `` oberlahnkreis '' with the capital weilburg was created . in 1886 `` limburg an der lahn '' district was set up , with the capital limburg . in 1974 both districts were joined together . the district is located between the two mountain areas westerwald and taunus . the main river is the lahn . other websites . - official website ( german )
