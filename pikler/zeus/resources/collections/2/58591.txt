Buxtehude     buxtehude is a town in northern germany . it has 39,000 inhabitants . it is by the este river which joins the city to the important river elbe just 6 miles away . buxtehude is known as `` the fairytale capital '' of the world . the tale of `` the bunny and the hedgehog '' by the brothers grimm is set in this town . the `` pistennacht '' is an event where bars , restaurants and clubs in the old part of town are open all night long . the `` pfingstmarkt '' is a big fair which is held at whitsun ( pfingsten ) . there is a lot to do including rides on a ferris wheel . on the first weekend of june there is a big feast in the old part of the town , which also has a wine festival every year .
