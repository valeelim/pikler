Roquefort cheese     roquefort cheese is a kind of blue cheese which comes from the south of france . it is made from sheep 's milk . french laws about aoc say that only cheese from the region of roquefort-sur-soulzon ( close to millau ) may be called roquefort cheese . this cheese breaks into big pieces easily , and it is a very moist . blue mould is added to roquefort cheese , which gives it a particular taste . roquefort is produced in big wheels which weigh around 3.5 kilograms , and which are about 10 cm thick . roquefort needs to ripen around 5 months . it is best consumed april to october , but of course it is available year round . in the year 2002 , there were seven roquefort producers . the largest by far is roquefort société . roquefort papillon is also a well-known brand . the five other producers are carles , gabriel coulet , fromageries occitanes , vernières and le vieux berger . another cheese , which is similar , but made from the milk of cows , is goregonzola . other websites . - http : //www.roquefort.fr/
