Oral tradition     an oral tradition or oral culture is a way of transmitting history , literature or law from one generation to the next in a civilization without a writing system , by telling stories and talking . an example that combined aspects of oral literature and oral history , before being written down , is the homeric poetry of the `` iliad '' and the `` odyssey '' . other websites . - the center for studies in oral tradition - the milman parry collection of oral literature online - oral tradition journal
