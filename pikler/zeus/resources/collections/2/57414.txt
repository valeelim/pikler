Diabetes insipidus     diabetes insipidus is a medical condition caused when vasopressin does not appear in a person 's blood . vasopressin is released from the brain , and travels in the blood to the kidneys . there , it pulls water out of the urine , which reduces the amount of urine stored in the bladder . if there is no vasopressin in the blood , the kidneys leave the water in the urine , and the bladder holds more liquid . this means a person needs to urinate more often . it also causes thirst . increased thirst and urination are symptoms of regular diabetes ( diabetes mellitus ) . that 's why diabetes insipidus shares the name `` diabetes .
