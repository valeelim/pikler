Imp     an imp is a mythological creature similar to a fairy or demon , usually described in folklore and superstition . imps are usually described as more than a serious threat , and as lesser creatures , not important supernatural beings . the small helpers who attend the devil are sometimes described as imps . they are usually described as small and very active . imps were the common name given to familiar spirits that served witches in the middle ages . imps were usually kept inside artifacts such as gemstone pieces or vials and summoned for service with magic . other pages . - gremlin - goblin - kappa
