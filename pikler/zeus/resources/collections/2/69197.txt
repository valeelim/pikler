Boeing B-29 Superfortress     the boeing b-29 superfortress was a heavy bomber aircraft with four engines used by the united states army air forces in world war ii , and by other countries ' military after that . the name `` superfortress '' came from its famous previous model , the b-17 flying fortress . the b-29 boeing model 345 was one of the biggest aircraft to serve during world war ii . it was one of the most advanced bombers of its time . it was the most used aircraft in the u.s. bombing against japan in the final months of world war ii , and b-29s carried the atomic bombs that destroyed hiroshima and nagasaki . the b-29 stayed in service for a long time after the war ended . by the time it was retired in the 1960s , some 3,900 planes had been built .
