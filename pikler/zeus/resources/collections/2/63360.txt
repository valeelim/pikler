Yamuna     the yamuna ( ) , sometimes called jamuna ( , , ) or jumna , is a major river in north india . it is the largest branch of a bigger river called the ganges . it is 1370 km long . it begins at the yamunotri , which is in the himalayas . it goes through the indian states of delhi , haryana , and uttar pradesh . at the end , it flows into the ganges . rivers that come out of the yamuna include the tons ( the largest of the ones that come out of it ) , chambal , betwa , and ken . other pages . - list of rivers of india other websites . - the geography of the rigveda - yamuna action plan
