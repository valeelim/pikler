Sertoli cell     a sertoli cell is a cell found in the testes . its main function is to help the developing sperm cells through the stages of spermatogenesis - the process of turning stem cells into sperm . it was also called the `` mother cell . '' the cell also helps in the creation of stem cells . sertoli cells get their name from enrico sertoli , an italian physiologist . he discovered them while studying medicine in the university of pavia , italy . he published a description of this cell in 1865. as of 2006 , two textbooks have been published that are almost entirely about the sertoli cell .
