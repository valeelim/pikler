Atlas Mountains     the atlas mountains ( amazigh : idurar n watlas , ) are a mountain range in northwest africa . they extend about 2,400 km ( 1,500 miles ) through morocco , algeria , and tunisia . the highest peak is tougkal , with an elevation of 4,167 metres ( 13,671 ft ) in southwestern morocco . the second highest mountain is the m'goun of 4071 meters . the southwestern part is called `` anti-atlas '' . the atlas ranges separate the mediterranean and atlantic coastlines from the sahara desert . most of the people living in the atlas mountains are part of berber tribes in morocco and in algeria . the mountains are named after the ancient greek titan , atlas .
