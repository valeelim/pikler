Mahmoud Hashemi Shahroudi     ayatollah seyyed mahmoud hashemi shahroudi ( born in 1948 ) has been the head of the judiciary of the islamic republic of iran since 1999. he was born in najaf , iraq . ayatollah shahroudi is best known for ordering a moratorium on stoning as a form of the death penalty . however later it was shown that executions by stoning are still being carried out , in spite of his claims that they have stopped . stoning is still happening inside prisons , where the public can not see it.. other websites . - text : human rights watch protests media crackdown , detentions in iran ( april 2000 ) - iran veil crackdown criticised april 24 , 2007
