Allmänna Idrottsklubben     aik ( which stands for allmänna idrottsklubben ) , is a sports club from sweden . it is one of the biggest and one of the oldest in the country . it was founded in 1891. the name means `` common '' ( or `` public '' ) `` sports club '' . it has departments for many different sports including football , ice hockey , bandy , golf , table tennis , bowling , and handball . other websites . - official website
