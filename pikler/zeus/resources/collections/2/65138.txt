Sohrab Sepehri     sohrab sepehri ( ; october 7 , 1928 - april 21 , 1980 ) was an iranian poet and painter . he was born in kashan , iran . his poems are in persian . his most famous poetry book is hasht ketab ( eight books ) . his poems have been translated to french , english , arabic , italian , swedish , hindi and russian . sohrab sepehri died in tehran .
