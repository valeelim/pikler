Mohamed ElBaradei     mohamed elbaradei ( born june 17 , 1942 ) is an egyptian scientist , administrator , politician , diplomat and 2005 recipient of the nobel prize for peace . elbaradei was born and raised in cairo , egypt . he has a doctorate in international law . elbaradei is best known as the former director general of the international atomic energy agency ( iaea ) `` ( 1997-2009 ) '' . the iaea is an inter-governmental organization related to the united nations . it is based in vienna and tries to bring about the peaceful use of nuclear energy . elbaradei and the iaea were both awarded the nobel peace prize in 2005. they won the prize for their efforts in iraq , finding that there were no weapons of mass destruction in iraq . related pages . - list of nobel prize winners by country
