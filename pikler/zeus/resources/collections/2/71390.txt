Choline     choline is an organic compound . it is usually grouped with the vitamin b complex . it is a vitamin that is needed by the human body . adults should take between 425 and 550 milligrams of it daily . choline is used by the body in the walls of cells and as a neurotransmitter . it also helps to prevent heart disease . there are claims that it helps reduce body fat , but this has not been proven . choline was discovered by andreas strecker in 1864. in 1998 choline was classified as an needed nutrient by the food and nutrition board of the institute of medicine ( usa ) . sources of choline are egg yolks , soy and cooked beef , chicken , veal and turkey ( bird ) livers . many foods contain small amounts of choline , even iceberg lettuce .
