Cape Horn     cape horn ( ; ) is a cape . it was named for the city of hoorn in the netherlands . it is the southernmost land area of the tierra del fuego archipelago of southern chile . many people see cape horn as the southern tip of south america . cape horn is the most southerly of the great capes . the drake passage is directly to the south of it . for many years , it was a major milestone on the clipper route by which sailing ships carried trade around the world . the waters around the cape are very dangerous because of strong winds , large waves , and icebergs . these reasons have made it notorious as a sailors ' graveyard . today , the panama canal has greatly reduced the need for cargo ships to travel via the horn . however , sailing around the horn is widely regarded as one of the major challenges in yachting , and a number of recreational sailors continue to sail this route , sometimes as part of a circumnavigation of the globe . several prominent ocean yacht races , notably the vend√©e globe , sail around the world via the horn , and speed records for round-the-world sailing follow the same route .
