Anita Ekberg     kerstin anita marianne ekberg ( 29 september 1931 11 january 2015 ) was a swedish actress . she was born in malmö , skåne . she was best known for her role in the movie `` la dolce vita '' ( 1960 ) , which was directed by federico fellini . ekberg died from a long-illness in castelli romani , italy , aged 83 .
