FIBA Basketball World Cup     the fiba basketball world cup , known as the fiba world championship from 1950 through 2010 , is a world basketball tournament for men 's national teams held once every four years by the international basketball federation ( fiba ) . after the 2014 fiba world cup , the tournament will move to a new four-year cycle to avoid conflict with the fifa world cup . to that end , no tournament will be held in 2018 ; the fiba world cup will resume in 2019 .
