Truman Capote     truman capote ( born truman streckfus persons , september 30 , 1924 - august 25 , 1984 ) was an american author . he was born in new orleans , louisiana and grew up in monroeville , alabama , new york city and greenwich , connecticut . he is best known for writing the half-true novel , `` in cold blood '' . the novel is based on a murder that happened in kansas in the 1950s . he became good friends with the man accused of the murder . capote was openly gay . he died of liver cancer in los angeles , california , aged 59 .
