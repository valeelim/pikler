Toul     toul is a city in the northeastern part of france . there are about 17,000 people living there . toul is a fortified town , with a long history . the people who started building the city were the romans . it was the capital of the leuci tribe . when the carolingian empire was split by the treaty of meerssen , in 870 , toul was given to east francia . in the early 1800s , the importance of the city diminished , and nancy took its place .
