Monte Alto solar power plant     the monte alto photovoltaic power plant in spain has a generating capacity of 9.55 megawatts peak ( mwp ) and will generate 14 million kilowatt-hours of electricity per year . it cost 65 million euros [ us $ 87 million ] . the plant covers an area of 51 hectares on agricultural land near the locality of milagro ( navarre ) and contains 889 solar structures , of which 864 have automated solar tracking . the rest are fixed structures adapted to the terrain . other pages . - photovoltaics - renewable energy - solar power in spain - solar cell
