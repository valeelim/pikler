Soldering iron     a soldering iron is a device used to attach pieces of metal together with solder , which is called soldering . soldering irons are used mainly for attaching electronic components onto a circuit board with tin , but can also be used for other tasks such as fixing jewelry . there are several different kinds of soldering irons , such as ones that are hotter than others , and ones that use electricity or natural gas . a soldering iron stand can be used to hold the iron . that is a flat metal stand with a coil that holds the iron .
