Dodoma     dodoma is the capital city of tanzania . it is the third biggest city in the country . the city is also the capital of the dodoma region . the city has a population of 410,956 people . the name dodoma means `` it has sunk '' . during german colonial rule in east africa , the town of dodoma was founded at the same time as the creation of the tanzanian central railway . the british took over the city after world war i. dodoma became an administrative center for the region until the independence of tanzania in 1964. in 1973 , plans were made to move the capital from dar es salaam to dodoma . tanzania 's national assembly moved there the next year . many government offices are still in dar es salaam . other websites . - dodoma region
