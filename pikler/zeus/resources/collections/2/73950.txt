McDonnell Douglas F-4 Phantom II     the f-4 phantom ii was an american fighter-bomber . it was mainly used during the vietnam war . it was first flown in 1958 and was shown in 1960. the plane could carry four aim-9 sidewinder missiles and four long range aim-7 sparrow missiles , but it did not have a gun . later , the e model had a cannon mounted on it . after the f-15 eagle replaced it , it was used as a bomber and the g model was used as a wild weasel . other pages . - f-8 crusader - f-15 eagle
