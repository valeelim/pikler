Euronext     euronext is a stock exchange in europe . it has buildings in belgium , france , netherlands , portugal and the united kingdom . euronext was created on september 22 2000 , when the stock exchanges of amsterdam , brussels stock exchange and paris bourse were joined together in a merger . in recent years , euronext has become the fifth largest stock exchange on the planet . in april 2007 , it merged with new york stock exchange to form nyse euronext ( ) .
