18     18 is a year in the 1st century . it was a common year starting on saturday of the julian calendar . at the time , it was known as the year of the consulship of augustus and caesar . events . - tiberius and germanicus are roman consuls . - a `` vexillatio '' ( sub-unit ) of legio iii `` augusta '' is destroyed by an ambush in africa . - the german chieftain arminius destroys the kingdom of the marcomanni . - in india , the indo-parthians control taxila . - caiaphas becomes the high priest of jerusalem . deaths . - ovid , roman poet ( or 17 )
