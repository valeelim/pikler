Deutsche Welle     the dw ( deutsche welle ) ( until february 5th 2012 : deutsche welle ) is germany 's international broadcaster . it broadcasts news and information on shortwave , internet and satellite radio in 29 languages ( dw ( radio ) ) . it has a satellite television service ( dw ( tv ) ) that is available in four languages , and there is also dw.de , a 30-language online news site . `` dw '' , which in english means `` german wave '' , is similar to international broadcasters such as the bbc world service , radio canada international , voice of america , and radio france internationale . on february 6th 2012 deutsche welle was completely relaunched and now operates only as dw . other websites . - official site - dw livestream - the bobs dw international weblog award - dw on satellite tv in the united states
