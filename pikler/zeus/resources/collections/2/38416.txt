Kindergarten     kindergarten , literally meaning `` children 's garden '' , is the first stage in the move from home to formal education . children learn through play . in most countries kindergarten is part of the pre-school system . in north america and some parts of australia kindergarten is the first year of school . kindergarten children are usually between three and seven years of age . kindergarten ages vary from country to country . in malaysia , for example , kindergarten children are six and when they are seven years old they go to primary school . the name kindergarten was coined by friedrich fröbel ( 1782–1852 ) . his work influenced early-years education around the world .
