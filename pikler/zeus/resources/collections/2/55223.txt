Vålerenga Fotball     vålerenga fotball is a norwegian football club from oslo . it was founded in 1913. they are named after the neighbourhood of vålerenga . they finished as runners-up in the 2010 norwegian premier league , the last time they won was in 2005. vålerenga 's home ground is ullevaal stadion , the national stadium for norway . their current coach is martin andresen . `` as of january 15th 2010 '' other websites . - official website - vpn ( vålerenga på nett ) - klanen , the vålerenga fan club
