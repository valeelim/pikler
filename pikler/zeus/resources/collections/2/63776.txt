Speed (band)    speed ( band ) speed is a japanese music group . the group is made up of four female singers . they are from okinawa , japan . the members are hiroko shimabukuro , eriko imai , takako uehara and hitoe arakaki . hiroko and eriko are lead vocal singers . when they debuted , hiroko was 12 years old . they made their debut single “ body & soul ” in 1996. they have many songs which sold million records . they broke up in march 31 , 2000. from 2000 to 2008 , they have broken up and came back together several times . they made their latest come back in 2008. members . - `` hiroko shimabukuro ( 島袋寛子 born april 7 , 1984 ) - `` eriko imai ( 今井絵理子 born september 22 , 1983 ) - `` takako uehara ( 上原多香子 born january 14 , 1983 ) - `` hitoe arakaki ( 新垣仁絵 born april 7 , 1981 ) discography . singles . - `` body & soul '' ( 1996 ) - `` steady '' ( 1996 ) - `` go ! go ! heaven '' ( 1997 ) - `` wake me up !
