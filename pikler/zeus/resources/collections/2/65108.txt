Centrifuge     a centrifuge is a device with a motor in it , which is used to spin an object with high speed . this high speed spinning forces the object outwards . this force is used for several purposes : - a uranium centrifuge is used to separate two isotopes of uranium ( named u-235 and u-238 ) from each other ; - in medical laboratories , serum is separated from the blood samples using a centrifuge ; - in chemical laboratories , centrifuges are used to subtract solid parts of an emulsion from its liquid parts . - huge centrifuges are used by nasa to expose astronauts to high forces for their training .
