Ayyubid dynasty     the ayyubid or ayyoubid dynasty was a muslim dynasty of kurdish origins which ruled egypt , syria , yemen ( except for the northern mountains ) , diyar bakr , mecca , hejaz and northern iraq in the 12th and 13th centuries . the ayyubids are also known as ayoubites , ayyoubites , ayoubides or ayyoubides . the ayyubid dynasty was begun by saladin ( salah ah-din ) . saladin and his uncle shirkuh conquered egypt for the zengid king nur ad-din of damascus in 1169. the dynasty 's name comes from saladin 's father and shirkuh 's brother , najm ad-din ayyub .
