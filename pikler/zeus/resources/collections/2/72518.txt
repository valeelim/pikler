Daniel Schneidermann     daniel schneidermann ( born april 5 , 1958 in paris ) is a french journalist . he mainly reports about televised media . he is most active in weekly columns . schneidermann has written in `` le monde '' and is presently in `` libération '' . he also can be seen on the television program `` arrêt sur images '' ( `` freeze-frame '' ) , broadcast by the public television channel france 5 .
