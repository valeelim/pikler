Tangerine     a tangerine is citrus fruit related to the mandarin orange . tangerines are smaller and easier to peel than common oranges . the taste is considered less sour , but sweeter and stronger , than that of an orange . very often , tangerines are simply peeled and eaten by hand , but they are sometimes also used as an ingredient of salads . in sichuan cuisine , the dried peel of tangerines is used as an ingredient . clementines are sometimes considered as alternatives to tangerines .
