Tattoo     a tattoo is a permanent mark made by putting ink into the skin . tattoos may be made on human or animal skin . tattoos on humans are a type of body modification ( a way of changing the body ) , but tattoos on animals are most often used for identification . people sometimes get tattoos to show that they belong to a gang or culture group . people get tattoos in `` tattoo parlors '' . getting a tattoo is painful , although some people have more pain than others , and some body parts are more painful to tattoo than others . most tattoos today are created using an electric tattoo machine , which uses needles to put ink into the body . tattooing is an ancient art .
