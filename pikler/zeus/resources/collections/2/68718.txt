Joe Rogan     joe rogan ( born august 11 , 1967 ) is an american comedian and actor . he is best known for his role as joe garrelli in `` newsradio '' and as host on the television game/reality program `` fear factor '' . rogan is also a color commentator for the ultimate fighting championship . he holds a brown belt in brazilian jiu-jitsu under eddie bravo and jean jacques machado .
