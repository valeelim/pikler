Halibut     halibut is a kind of flatfish often used for food and living in the north atlantic and the north pacific oceans ( they live in warm water ) . the halibut from the atlantic ocean has been overfished . it might become extinct . their names come from `` haly '' ( holy ) and `` butt '' ( flat fish ) , because it is said people like to eat them on catholic holy-days . other websites . - international pacific halibut commission - alaska department of fish & game
