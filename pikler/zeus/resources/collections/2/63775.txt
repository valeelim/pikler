Crystal Kay     crystal kay williams ( born 26 february 1986 in yokohama , japan ) is a j-pop singer . she is also known as `` kuri '' by her fans . she is well known for her songs `` koi ni ochitara '' and `` boyfriend -part ii- '' . she has released ten japanese studio albums . in 2003 she released an english album called `` natural : world premiere album '' . she started singing on television commercials when she was four years old . when she was twelve she was signed to epic records . kay 's first album `` c.l.l crystal lover light '' was released on march 23 , 2000 when kay was fourteen . albums . - 2000 : `` c.l.l crystal lover light '' - 2001 : `` 637 -always and forever- '' - 2002 : `` almost seventeen '' - 2003 : `` 4 real '' - 2005 : `` crystal style '' - 2006 : `` call me miss ...
