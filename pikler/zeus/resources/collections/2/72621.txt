Battle of Rhandeia     the battle of rhandeia was a battle fought between armenia and parthia against the roman empire . it was fought just after the mithridatic wars . the romans were led by lucius caesennius paetus . they were defeated by the parthian-armenian army led by tiridates i of armenia . the parthians made tiridates the king of armenia . he was the brother of the parthian king vologases i. tiridates replaced tigranes , a roman ally , as king . the romans fought to take back the throne , but were forced into surrender at rhandeia ( autumn 62 ) . other pages . - armenian empire
