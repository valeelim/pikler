1992 Los Angeles riots     the 1992 los angeles riots , also called rodney king uprising or rodney king riots , started on april 29 , 1992 when a jury set four white policemen free . the jury was mostly caucasian . the policemen had beaten up african-american motorist rodney king badly and filmed this , after he fled from police . thousands of people in los angeles , mostly african american and hispanic , rioted . in all , 55 people were killed in the riots .
