East of England     the east of england is a region in england . there are nine regions of england . it was made in 1994 and was used as a category for statistics in 1991. the counties of bedfordshire , cambridgeshire , essex , hertfordshire , norfolk and suffolk are in the region . also peterborough is in the region . in 2001 the census in england found that 5,388,140 persons lived in the region . the land is mostly low . the highest place is the hill of ivinghoe beacon , near tring in the county of hertfordshire . the top of the hill is 249 metres above the level of the sea .
