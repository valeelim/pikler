Influence     social influence means that someone 's emotions , opinions , or behaviours are affected by others . it can be either planned by others on purpose , or an unplanned result of other events . the adjective influential means `` having much influence '' , so influential people or things have the power to change other people or things in some way . this word is often used to describe famous people . for example , mohandas gandhi was a very influential person , because the things he did and said changed many peoples lives , and many people believe he has even influenced the world . influence can be a bad thing too . for example , world war ii influenced many people 's lives , making it very difficult for them to live happily even after the war ended .
