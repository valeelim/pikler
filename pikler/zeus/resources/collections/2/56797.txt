BI-LO (Australia)    bi-lo ( australia ) bi-lo is a supermarket with 214 shops all over australia . it used to have shops in western australia called newmart . it changed the names of all its stores to coles supermarkets in 2006 and 2007. john weekes started bi-lo towards the end of the 1970s in the city of adelaide in south australia with three shops . it was called `` bi-lo '' because it sells food and everyday articles at relatively cheap prices .
