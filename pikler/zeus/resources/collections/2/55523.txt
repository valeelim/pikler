Love Gun     love gun is the sixth studio album by the american hard rock/heavy metal band kiss . it was released on june 30 , 1977. track listing . - `` i stole your love '' - `` christine sixteen '' - `` got love for sale '' - `` shock me '' - `` tomorrow and tonight '' - `` love gun '' - `` hooligan '' - `` almost human '' - `` plaster caster '' - `` then she kissed me ''
