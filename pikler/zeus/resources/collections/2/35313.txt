Middle East Airlines     middle east airlines ( ) is an airline based in lebanon . it flies between the middle east , europe and africa . middle east airlines opened for business on may 31 , 1945. it was opened by saeb salam , with support from boac . mea 's work started on january 1 , 1946 with flights between beirut and nicosia . then its airplanes started to fly to iraq , egypt , syria , and cyprus . the company bought two douglas dc-3 planes in mid-1946 . on september 7 , 2006 , israel ended its 8-week long air blockade on lebanon . a middle east airlines plane from paris flew to the rafik hariri international airport at 6:06 p.m. lebanese time ( 3:03 p.m. gmt ) . mea resumed its normal flight timetable on september 11 , 2006. middle east airlines flies its planes to these countries and their cities ( since june 2006 ) : africa . - cÃ´te d'ivoire - abidjan - egypt - cairo - sharm el sheikh ( sometimes ) - ghana - accra - nigeria - kano - lagos - niger asia . - jordan - amman - kuwait - kuwait city - qatar - doha - saudi arabia
