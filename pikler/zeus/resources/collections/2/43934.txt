Dimorphodon     dimorphodon was a prehistoric flying pterosaur from the early jurassic period . it had a big head , a small brain , a tall beak , and two types of teeth . variation in teeth is rare in pterosaurs . like `` rhamphorhynchus '' , `` dimorphodon '' is only found in jurassic strata . it was the first pterosaur found in england , by mary anning , in 1828 .
