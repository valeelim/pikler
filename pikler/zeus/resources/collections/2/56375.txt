Port Royal     port royal was a city in jamaica . it was the focus of trade in jamaica in the 17th century . it was known for its piracy , but also for the riches accumulated , and for the very bad moral values that could be found in the city . an earthquake in june of 1692 largely destroyed the city . two thirds of the city sank into the caribbean sea . today , the city is covered by at least 8 metres of water . the city is among the most important archaeological sites in the ocean . its role as a center for trade was taken over by spanish town , jamaica , and later by kingston .
