Edmund Burke     edmund burke ( 12 january 1729-9 july 1797 ) was an important irish politician , and writer . he became a member of the british parliament in 1765. burke supported the american revolution but made attacks against the french revolution . his most famous book was `` reflections on the revolution in france '' , which created many of the ideas in conservatism . other websites . - ( including his collected works in 12 volumes )
