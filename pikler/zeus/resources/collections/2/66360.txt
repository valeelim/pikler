Everest College     blair college is now everest college a for-profit college in colorado springs , colorado . everest college offers career college programs in many areas . the college was founded in pueblo , colorado as brown 's school of business in 1897 , changing its name to blair 's business college in 1918. in 1981 , blair business college changed its name to blair junior college . in 1996 , the college was bought by rhodes colleges , inc. , a division of corinthian colleges , inc. and the name was changed to blair college . in 2006 , the name was finally changed to the current everest college .
