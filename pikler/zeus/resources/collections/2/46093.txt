Vegetation     vegetation means the plant life of a region or the plant community . it means all ground cover by plants , and is the main element of the biosphere . when you say `` vegetation '' you do not think of any species composition , life forms , structure , spatial extent , `` naturalness '' , or any other specific botanical or geographic characteristics . that is meant by `` flora '' which refers exclusively to species composition . `` vegetation '' can refer to a wide range of spatial scales . primeval redwood forests , coastal mangrove stands , sphagnum bogs , desert soil crusts , roadside weed patches , wheat fields , cultivated gardens and lawns ; all are meant by the term `` vegetation '' . other websites . classification . - terrestrial vegetation of the united states volume i â€“ the national vegetation classification system : development , status , and applications ( pdf ) - federal geographic data committee vegetation subcommittee - vegetation classification standard [ fgdc-std-005 , june 1997 ( pdf ) mapping-related . - usgs - nps vegetation mapping program
