Khula     khula is the right of a woman to ask her husband for a divorce and to offer him a compensation in case he does so . following the sharia the husband has no right to ask for a compensation of a higher value than that what he gave his wife when they married . after divorce the former husband is responsible for education , food and residence of children . the children live with the mother for 7 years . after 7 years children have the right to live with father or mother , as they decide .
