Forsyth County, Georgia    forsyth county , georgia forsyth county is a county in the american state of georgia . the county seat of forsyth county is cumming , georgia . forsyth county has one of the fastest growing populations of any county in the united states . the number of people has grown from 98,407 to 140,393 between 2000 and 2005. the county was made in 1832 and is named after john forsyth . the county has five main high schools including ; north forsyth , west forsyth , lambert high , forsyth central , and south forsyth . other websites . - government site - forsyth county georgia , local web portal and directory - john forsyth , biographical directory of the united states congress - history and records , forsyth county , ga - north forsyth high school - west forsyth high school - lambert high school - forsyth central high school - south forsyth high school
