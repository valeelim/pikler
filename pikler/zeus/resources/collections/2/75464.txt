Declaration of the Rights of Man and of the Citizen     the declaration of the rights of man and of the citizen ( ) is one of the most important papers of the french revolution . this paper explains a list of rights , such as freedom of religion , freedom of speech , freedom of assembly and separation of powers . all men have these rights . it also talks about some rights many people have together . this paper was written using some of the ideas of natural rights , these rights are for all men : they are supposed to be valid in all times and places . they are said to be rights of human nature . the last idea of the declaration was accepted on 26 august 1789 , by the national constituent assembly ( `` assembl√©e nationale constituante '' ) .. it was a very important first thing to do before the people could write a constitution . the paper explained these basic rights , not only for french people but for `` all men without exception '' , it did not say anything about the rights or role of women . it also did not talk about slavery . people now think that it was an important step towards international human rights or universal declaration of human rights :
