Chocolate chip     chocolate chips are small pieces of chocolate . they are used to make desserts , usually in baking . chocolate chips are most common in cookies . chocolate chips are also used in some cakes and other desserts . chocolate chips were invented in 1937 when ruth graves wakefield of the toll house inn added cut-up chunks of a semi-sweet nestlé chocolate bar to a cookie recipe . lots of people liked the new cookies , and wakefield made an agreement in 1939 with nestlé to put her recipe on the chocolate bar 's packaging . in return , nestlé gave wakefield a lifetime supply of chocolate . at first , nestlé put a small chopping tool in the chocolate bar package . in 1941 nestlé and one or more other chocolate bar companies started selling the chocolate in chip ( or `` morsel '' ) form . the nestlé brand toll house cookies is named after toll house inn .
