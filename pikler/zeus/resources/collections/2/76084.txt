Lares     lares ( pl . ) were ancient roman deities who protected the house and the family - household gods . see also genius , larvae , di penates , manes . lares are presumed sons of mercury and lara , and deeply venerated by ancient romans through small statues , usually put in higher places of the house , far from the floor , or even on the roof . in the early roman times , in every house there was at least one little statue . later , a sort of confusion connected their figure with those of manes , deities of hades ( and the most virtuous dead persons of the family ) . finally the confusion included the penates ( other minor deities ) as well . related pages . - turan , the etruscan love goddess
