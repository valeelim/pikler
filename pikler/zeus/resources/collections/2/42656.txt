Anne Redpath     anne redpath ( 1895-1965 ) was a scottish artist . she was born in galashiels , scotland . she was the daughter of a tweed designer . her father 's work had an effect on her use of colour and texture in her paintings . `` i do with a spot of red or yellow in a harmony of grey , what my father did in his tweed '' , she said . images . - altar in pigna - national galleries of scotland - still life with two figures - tate gallery
