Singular     singular is a form of the noun that shows that there is only one . the opposite of the singular is the plural , which means there is more than one . for example , in english the word `` cat '' in `` one cat '' is singular , but in `` two cats '' it is plural . concepts are nouns which usually take the singular . thus `` rhythm '' as a concept is singular . but when we talk of different types of rhythm , those are particular rhythms < /u/l > .
