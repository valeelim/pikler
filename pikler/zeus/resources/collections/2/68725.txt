Atomic Kitten     atomic kitten are an english girl group from liverpool composed of liz `` lil '' mcclarnon , natasha `` tash '' hamilton , and jenny frost - who replaced original member kerry katona in january 2001. atomic kitten has sold over 12 million albums and 8 million singles around the world . many of their singles and albums , such as : `` right now , '' `` whole again , '' `` eternal flame , '' `` the tide is high , '' and `` ladies night entered the top 40 charts in several countries . the group reformed in 2012 , with katona and without frost .
