Solon     solon was a statesman in ancient greece . he is one of seven sages ( or wise men ) of greece , because of his political reforms to the laws of athens . many of these changes helped the poor people . he lived from 630 b.c.- 560 b.c . he introduced the court and judges . about 594 b.c. , the nobles elected solon to lead athens . he made reforms that helped prevent a revolt by the poor . first he freed people who had become slaves because of debts . he made a law that no citizen could be enslaved . solon also organized citizens into four classes based on wealth , not birth . rich men had more power ; yet this was still a fairer system than the old one that limited power to nobles . solon allowed all citizens to serve in the assembly and help elect leaders . he also reformed the laws to make them less harsh .
