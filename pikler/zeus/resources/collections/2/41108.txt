Bérurier Noir     bérurier noir are a french punk band which formed in paris in 1983. it is made up of loran ( guitar ) , françois ( vocals ) and dede ( drum machine ) . in 1985 masto joined , playing the saxophone . they sing about french politics and anarchism . they are one of the most famous french punk bands . discography . - 1983 : `` macadam massacre '' - 1985 : `` concerto pour détraqués '' - 1987 : `` abracadaboum '' - 1989 : `` souvent fauché , toujours marteau '' - 1990 : `` viva bertaga '' ( live 1989 ) - 1995 : `` carnaval des agités '' ( best of/live 1983-1989 ) - 1998 : `` la bataille de pali-kao '' ( best of 1983-1989 ) - 2003 : `` même pas mort '' ( dvd+cd best of+news ) - 2005 : `` l'opéra des loups '' ( dvd+cd live 16 titles or dvd only ) - 2006 : `` chant des meutes '' ( live 13 titles ) - 2006 : `` invisible '' other websites . - official site - http : //www.fzm.fr/
