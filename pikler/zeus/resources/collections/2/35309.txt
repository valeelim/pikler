El Al     el al ( ; ) is the national airline of israel . it is the country 's flag carrier airline . it also flies to africa , asia , europe and north america . el al serves 48 destinations . it has only one hub : ben gurion international airport in tel aviv . in 1948 , after a conference in geneva , the first president of israel had to return to israel with a government aircraft . however , this was not possible . so a c-54 was converted into a civil aircraft and repainted with the `` el al '' logo in order to bring back the president to israel . el al became the official israeli airline in 1948. the first international flight took place in 1949. it flew from tel aviv to paris .
