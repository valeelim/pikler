Sam Walton     samuel moore walton was an american businessman . he was born on march 29 , 1918 in kingfisher , oklahoma . walton created the well-known american stores , wal-mart and sam 's club . walton was born to thomas gibson walton and nancy lee walton . he was part of the walton family , which is one of the richest families in the world . walton died on april 6 , 1992. he died a billionaire but was known for driving a pick-up truck instead of an expensive car .
