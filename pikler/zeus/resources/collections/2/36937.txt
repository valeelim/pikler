Delta Air Lines     delta air lines , inc. is a large airline from the united states . they are headquartered in atlanta , georgia . their largest hub is also in atlanta . delta 's first flight was in 1929. in 1972 , delta merged with northeast airlines . in 1987 , delta merged with western airlines . in 2009 , they merged with northwest airlines . until 2013 , delta was the worlds largest airline . as of 2016 , the airline goes to 334 cities and has 819 planes . other websites . - http : //delta-airlines-reservations.blogspot.in/
