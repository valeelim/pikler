Jens Pulver     jens johnnie `` little evil '' pulver ( born december 6 , 1974 in sunnyside , washington ) is an american mixed martial artist ( mma ) . he was the first ultimate fighting championship ( ufc ) lightweight champion , and was a coach on `` the ultimate fighter 5 '' reality show , which aired on spike tv . he is currently signed as a featherweight with world extreme cagefighting ( wec ) , which is owned by zuffa ( the parent company of the ufc ) . other websites . - jens pulver official website - video interview about `` the ultimate fighter 5 '' - may 2007 interview
