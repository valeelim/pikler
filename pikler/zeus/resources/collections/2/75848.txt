Coat of Arms of Mali     on 20 october , 1973 , the coat of arms of the republic of mali was made . the motto of the republic of mali is `` un peuple , un but , une foi '' ( one people , one goal , one faith ) . the coat of arms of the republic of mali is a circle . there is a light blue background : - in the middle , the mosque of djenné , in gold - above the mosque , a flying vulture , in gold - below , the rising sun , in gold - in front of the sun , two bows bent by their arrow , in white - in the surround , the writings `` république du mali '' above and `` un peuple , un but , une foi '' below , in black capital letters .
