Abuja     abuja is a city in nigeria . it is the national capital of nigeria . around 2.5 million people live there . abuja is in the middle of the country , in the center of the federal capital territory , nigeria . the capital of nigeria used to be lagos , but it was moved to abuja in 1976. abuja is a planned city , which means that architects designed it specifically to be nigeria 's capital city . construction of the first parts of the city was finished in the late 1980s .
