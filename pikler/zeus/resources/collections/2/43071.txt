Cairns, Queensland    cairns , queensland cairns is a city in queensland , australia . 120.000 people live there . cairns is about 1720 km ( 1,069 miles ) north of brisbane and about 2500km ( 1,553 miles ) from sydney by road . it is built on the shores of trinity bay . cairns is an important travel destination for tourists because it is warm all year round and is near many attractions . the great barrier reef is only one-and-a-half hours away by boat . the daintree national park and cape tribulation , about 130km north of cairns , are popular areas for experiencing a tropical rainforest . it is also a starting point for people wanting to see cooktown , cape york peninsula , and the atherton tableland . captain james cook named trinity bay when he arrived there in his ship hm bark endeavour on trinity sunday , 1770. the first europeans to settle arrived in 1876 when gold was found near cairns . it was named after the governor of queensland , sir william cairns .
