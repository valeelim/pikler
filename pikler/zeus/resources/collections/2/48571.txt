Democratic National Committee     the democratic national committee ( d.n.c . or dnc ) is a group of people who coordinate the activities of the u.s. democratic party in all fifty states . the d.n.c . is headquartered in the nation 's capital , washington , dc . it also has state democratic party headquarters in each of the 50 state capitals . the current chairman is thomas perez of maryland and the deputy chairman is keith ellison of minnesota . related pages . - republican national committee other websites . - official dnc website
