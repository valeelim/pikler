Lanark     lanark is a town in south lanarkshire , scotland . it is close to carluke and biggar . lanark is aproximately 40 miles from edinburgh , the capital of scotland . william wallace once lived there . there is a statue of wallace on the town church . a plaque remembers where he `` first drew sword to free his native land '' in 1296 , when he killed heselrig , the english sheriff of lanark . this event forms the start of the mel gibson film , `` braveheart '' . lanark is also notable for its annual lanimer celebrations , where local children on coloured floats parade through the streets along with marching pipe bands . a lanimer queen is elected from the children , and crowned in a ceremony at the foot of the high street . a lord cornet is chosen from local businessmen , and acts as escort for the queen for the events of lanimer week .
