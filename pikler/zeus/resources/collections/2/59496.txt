Noodle soup     noodle soup is a kind of soup . it contains the normal soup stock , together with noodles , and sometimes other ingredients . this kind of soup is very popular in asia , where it originally came from . there are also many instant soups of this kind . some of the varieties are : - `` beef noodle soup '' - popular in china ; noodles in a beef soup - `` ban mian '' - popular in china , malaysia and singapore ; a noodle soup with flat noodles , egg , and usually vegetables . - `` soba '' - popular in japan ; noodles are made of buckwheat - `` udon '' - popular in japan ; noodles are made of wheat , toppings can be added , broth is usually dashi - `` ramen ''
