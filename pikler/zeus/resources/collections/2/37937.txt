78violet     78violet ( formerly aly & aj ) are an american music group . the members are amanda michalka and her sister , alyson who is two years older . members . - alyson ( nicknamed `` aly '' ) : helps writing the music and songs . she is one of the two guitarists . - amanda ( nicknamed `` aj '' ) : helps writing the songs and constructing the music . aly & aj lived in seattle , washington in the early 1990s , then moved back to southern california . they learned to play the piano at the age of six . they both were in the movie `` my super sweet 16 the movie '' .
