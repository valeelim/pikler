Chastity     chastity is the sexual behavior of a man or woman which is right and proper according to the moral rules of a culture or religion . it has recently begun to mean sexual abstinence ( not having sex at all ) , especially before marriage . however , it can also be used for all kinds of people ( even married people ) with more meaning than simply not having sex . the word chastity came from the latin word `` castitatem '' , meaning `` purity '' . related pages . - sexual abstinence other websites . - catechism of the catholic church ( iii.2.i ) - early shaker writings relating to sexual abstinence
