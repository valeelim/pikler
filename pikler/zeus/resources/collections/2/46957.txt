Legal case     a legal case is a dispute between two parties that is resolved by a court or other legal process . a legal case may be either civil or criminal . the decision of a court is usually published in one form or another for later reference . in countries like the united states which have a common law system , decisions by a higher court are binding on lower courts when the facts and issues are similar . stare decisis , or the concept of precedent , means to follow cases that were decided earlier when judging the current case .
