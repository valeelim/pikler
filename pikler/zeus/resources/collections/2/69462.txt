Bonnaroo Music Festival     the bonnaroo music and arts festival is an annual four-day music festival held in manchester , tennessee . it is held by superfly productions and ac entertainment and was first put together in 2002. the show 's main features are the multiple stages , with the two main stages—the `` what '' stage and the `` which '' stage—providing most of the performances . the music that is heard comes in a wide variety , including world music , hip hop , jazz , americana , bluegrass , country music , folk , gospel , reggae , electronica , and other alternative music .
