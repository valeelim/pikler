T.A.T.u     t.a.t.u. , or known in russian as тату , was a russian pop music duo , made up of lena katina and yulia volkova . the group was formed in 1999 in moscow . their albums include `` 200 russian `` 200 po vstrechnoy '' , `` 200 km/h in the wrong lane '' , `` lyudi invalidy '' , `` dangerous and moving '' and `` waste management '' . they split in 2011. t.a.t.u . released their first single `` all the things she said '' in 2002. it reached number one in the united kingdom . the song is about a relationship between two girls . in the music video lena and yulia kiss each other . itv banned it from their show cd : uk . in 2003 t.a.t.u performed `` ne ver ' , ne boysia '' at the 2003 eurovision song contest for their country russia . they came third . t.a.t.u performed at the sochi winter olympics .
