Livermorium     livermorium is a chemical element . it has the chemical symbol lv . livermorium is number 116 on the periodic table . the element lasts only 47 milliseconds . it was first discovered in 2000 by the joint institute for nuclear research in russia . its name was first `` ununhexium '' ( uuh ) , but it changed on may 30st , 2012 , when the international union of pure and applied chemistry changed it . other websites . - webelements.com - uuh - apsidium - ununhexium - second postcard from the island of stability
