Felicity Huffman     felicity huffman ( born december 9 , 1962 ) is an academy award-nominated and emmy award-winning american movie and television actress . she is well known for her role as lynette scavo , a busy mother on the abc television show `` desperate housewives '' . a year later , her role as a transwoman in the independent film `` transamerica '' was praised by many critics and earned her a golden globe award .
