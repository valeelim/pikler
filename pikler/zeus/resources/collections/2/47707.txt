Champs-Élysées    champs-élysées the champs-élysées is a long and very famous street in paris . it links the arc de triomphe and the place de la concorde . it is a very popular place : there are many famous cinemas , bars , boutiques , and restaurants . it is also where the tour de france ends . on 20 april 2017 , two isis-claimed gunmen opened fired and killed one police officer and injured another at champs-élysées .
