I Have a Dream     `` i have a dream '' is the name of a speech martin luther king , jr. gave on august 28 , 1963 while standing on the steps of the lincoln memorial in washington , d.c.. in his speech , he spoke of his wish for the future . his wish was that people of different races could live together peacefully in the united states . the speech was given to over 200,000 supporters . he spoke of the discrimination that the black men have faced even though they were to be treated as equals after the great abraham lincoln signed the momentous decree ; the emancipation proclamation . the speech is very famous . it has been called by many- the best speech given in the 20th century . other websites . - video of speech on africanamericanhistory.tv - `` i have a dream '' text , audio , video from americanrhetoric.com - text of speech : united states department of state - lyrics of the traditional spiritual `` free at last '' - southcoasttoday.com : a read on a 4th , 5th and 6th graders ' take on martin a simple english video about the `` i have a dream '' speech
