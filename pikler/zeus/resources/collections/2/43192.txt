Ann Arbor, Michigan    ann arbor , michigan ann arbor is a city in the us state of michigan . it is the county seat of washtenaw county . according to the 2010 united states census , ann arbor was the 6th largest city in michigan . in 2010 , there were 113,934 people living there . the city is part of the detroit metropolitan combined statistial area . ann arbor was started in 1824. it is thought that the city is named after the wife , ann , of one of the founders . the university of michigan was moved to ann arbor in 1837 . ( before that , the university had been in detroit . ) the city grew during the 1800s and 1900s . the only time the city did not grow was the depression of 1873. during the 1960s and 1970s , ann arbor was an important place for the civil rights movement , the anti-vietnam war movement , and for left-wing and liberal politics . today , ann arbor is home to the university of michigan . the university is very important to the city because 30,000 people work for it . 12,000 of these workers work for the university of michigan health system , which are hospitals and clinics run by the university . ann arbor 's economy is also very high-tech . high-tech companies come to the area because there are many graduates and a lot of research and development money at the university .
