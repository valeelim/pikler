Messerschmitt Me 262     the me 262 was a german fighter aircraft flown during late world war ii . it was the first jet to be used in war . it began test flights in 1942 , but was not used in combat by the german air force , the luftwaffe , until 1944. very few were built because germany 's enemies were bombing factories . it saw little action because the war was nearly over . the jet engines of the new machine often made problems , that 's why a lot of machines had deathly accidents . other pages . - jet
