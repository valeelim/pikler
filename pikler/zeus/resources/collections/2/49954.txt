Growth-share matrix     the growth-share matrix ( aka b.c.g . analysis , b.c.g.-matrix , boston consulting group analysis ) is a chart that had been created by bruce henderson for the boston consulting group in 1970 to help corporations with analyzing their business units or product lines . this helps the company allocate resources and is used as an analytical tool in brand marketing , product management , strategic management and portfolio-analysis . other pages . - marketing other websites . - software that generates the boston matrix - compares and contrasts bcg with other 2 x 2 matrices like ansoff and 3 x 3 matrices like ge portfolio
