Ban     a ban is an action used to stop people from doing something , either bad or dangerous . ban can also be made on products . for example , singapore , a country , bans chewing gum . ban may sometimes be made on the internet , where an administrator or a moderator with higher powers than other members can stop vandals from causing harm to others . sometimes users of a website say that moderators or administrators have banhammers that they use to ban bad users .
