Hokkaido Wolf     the hokkaido wolf `` ( canis lupus hattai ) '' is a subspecies of gray wolf . scientists believe it became extinct in 1889. there are people who said to have seen some hokkaido wolves after that year , but there is no proof that they actually did . in the meiji restoration in japan , japanese farmers wanted to have american-style ranches for raising livestock . a us farmer named edwin dun started to import livestock to japan . when the wolves began attacking the livestock , farmers started to kill them , using poison . other pages . - werewolf
