Pete Maravich     pete maravich ( june 22 , 1947 - january 5 , 1988 ) , also known as `` pistol pete '' maravich , was an american basketball player . he played for lousisana state university and averaged 44.2 points per game . that is the highest average ever in the history of college basketball . then maravich played for ten seasons in the nba . he played for the atlanta hawks , new orleans jazz , utah jazz , and the boston celtics . he was known for his great dribbling , passing and shooting abilitites . maravich died on january 5,1988. other websites . - espn biography - maravich book ( jackie maravich ) - rare pete maravich film and video clips
