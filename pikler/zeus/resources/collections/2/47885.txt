1996 Summer Olympics     the 1996 summer olympics , officially known as the games of the xxvi olympiad , were held in atlanta , united states from july 19 to august 4. eric rudolph , a christian fundamentalist , bombed the centennial olympic park on july 27. alice hawthorne was killed in the explosion when a nail from the bomb penetrated her skull . melih uzunyol , a turkish cameraman , had a fatal heart attack while running to the scene . the bomb wounded 111 others . these were the top 10 teams at the 1996 summer olympics . - key host nation ( united states )
