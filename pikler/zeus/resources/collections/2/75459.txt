German TV     german tv was a german-speaking television program for viewers in the usa and in canada . it was founded as a joint program from ard and zdf , which formed 40 % each of the broadcast output . the remaining 20 % was from deutsche welle . the channel broadcast 24 hours-a-day . the editorial staff and the management of german tv were in the offices of deutsche welle tv in berlin . german tv sent news and information as well as sport , children 's programs , shows and other entertainment programs as well as various films from different areas . german tv went on the air in april , 2002. it was able to be received in the usa , and in wide parts of canada and latin america , by satellite or cable tv . other websites . - http : //www.german.tv/
