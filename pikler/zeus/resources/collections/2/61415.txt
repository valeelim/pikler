Byron, Wyoming    byron , wyoming byron is a town in the american state of wyoming . it part of big horn county . at the 2010 census , 593 people lived in byron . in high school , students go to rocky mountain high school in byron . middle school students go to rocky mountain middle school in deaver and elementary school students attend rocky mountain elementary school in cowley . the town has an area of 0.9 mi² . 0.1 mi² of it ( 5.62 % ) is water .
