Cebu Province     cebu is the oldest city in the philippines . it is often called the `` queen city of the south . '' cebu is a main center of christianity in the philippines . the city has a lot of interesting history . cebu was a fishing village and busy trading port in 1521. the center of the city has become modern and is well developed . cebu has nine cities : cebu , bogo , carcar , danao , lapu-lapu , mandaue , naga , talisay , and toledo . there are also 44 smaller towns . cebu has recently become the favorite tourist spot of the country .
