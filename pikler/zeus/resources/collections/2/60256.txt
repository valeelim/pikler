Institution     an institution is social structure in which people cooperate and which influences the behavior of people and the way they live . an institution has a purpose . institutions are permanent , which means that they do not end when one person is gone . an institution has rules and can enforce rules of human behavior . the word `` institution '' can be used in two ways . it can mean a very broad idea , or a very `` specific '' ( narrow ) one . for example : - government is an institution in the broad sense . westminster parliament is an institution in the specific sense . - education is an institution in the broad sense . harvard university is an institution in the specific sense . institutions , in the broad sense , are found in every society . the way that each institution works is different in different cultures . some important institutions are : - marriage- this is how society protects itself by controlling the way people live together , have children and care for them . - education- a society controls how young ones are prepared to be useful adult members of society .
