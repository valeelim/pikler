Dachau     dachau is a city in the south of germany , about north-west of munich . it has about 40,000 inhabitants . the town contains a historic town centre with an 18th century castle . dachau was founded in the 8th century . in 1933 , a concentration camp was built in dachau . it was the first camp of the nazis and became the prototype for all other camps . more than 30,000 prisoners died , or were killed . other websites . - town web site
