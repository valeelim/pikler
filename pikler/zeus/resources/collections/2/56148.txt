Scottsdale, Tasmania    scottsdale , tasmania scottsdale is a town in the north-east of tasmania , australia . it is 63 km northeast of launceston and 22 km southeast of town of bridport . it is part of the dorset council . the area was first surveyed in 1855 and was described as , `` '' the best soil on the island ... well watered , with a mild climate '' '' by surveyor james scott . the town was named after him . scottsdale is now a major agricultural centre .
