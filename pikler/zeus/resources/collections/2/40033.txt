Central Powers     the central powers were a group of nations fighting against the allied powers during world war i. the members included germany , austria-hungary , the ottoman empire , bulgaria and their territories . they were also supported by azerbaijan and finland , as well as lithuania in 1918 and co-belligerent jabal shammar . they lost the war . the allied forces included great britain , canada , newfoundland , russia , france , later the united states , greece , portugal , brazil , georgia , armenia , australia and japan .
