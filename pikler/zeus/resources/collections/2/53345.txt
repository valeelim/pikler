Ceará     ceará is one of the 26 states of brazil . it is in the northeastern part of the country , on the atlantic coast . ceará has many beaches , which brings a lot of tourism . ceará has an area of 148,016 km² . it shares borders on the east by the states of rio grande do norte and paraíba , on the south by pernambuco state , and on the west by piauí . its northern border is the atlantic ocean . its capital is fortaleza . other websites . - official government site - tourism guide ceará - fortaleza tourism guide and maps
