James Boswell     james boswell ( october 29 , 1740 - may 19 , 1795 ) was a scottish lawyer and author . he is best known for having written the life of johnson , a biography of his close friend samuel johnson , published in 1791. in the 1920s and 1930s , a large number of his personal papers and journals were discovered . they have since then been published by yale university . other websites . - james boswell - a guide
