Gulf of Finland     the gulf of finland is a body of water that points east from the baltic sea with finland on its north side and estonia on its south side . both finnish capital helsinki and estonian capital tallinn are at shore of gulf of finland . at the bottom of the gulf of finland is russia and its most important coast city st. petersburg . the water is less salty in the eastern side of the gulf . this is because the neva river flows into the gulf in the east .
