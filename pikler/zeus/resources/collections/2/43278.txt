Maitland, New South Wales    maitland , new south wales maitland is a city in new south wales , australia . the city is on the hunter river . maitland is 200 km north of sydney and 30 km northwest of newcastle . maitland is sometimes called the largest city , that is not on the sea in new south wales . the maitland gaol , which closed in 1998 , is now a tourist attraction . other websites . - the maitland city council website - the maitland gaol website
