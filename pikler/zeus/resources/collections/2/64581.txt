Konzum     konzum is croatia 's biggest supermarket chain . konzum was opened in zagreb in 1957 , and today has 230 stores open in croatia . lately , konzum 's popularity has grown a lot in croatia . in 1994 , konzum joined the agrokor group . konzum supermarkets can also be found in croatia 's neighbouring country , bosnia and herzegovina . konzum has relations with the bosnian company , velpro .
