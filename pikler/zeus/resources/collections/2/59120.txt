Puerta del Sol     the puerta del sol ( `` sun gate '' ) is known as 'spain 's belly button ' . in the 1400 's , it was the gate or entrance of the east side of the city . since the sun rises in the east , it got the name puerta del sol . it is now the center of 'kilometer 0 ' of madrid . all roads are measured from this point . it was rebuilt in 1854. the statue of king carlos iii is in the middle of the square . the red brick building there is the former post office . the famous oso y el madroño ( bear eating madroños ) statue can also be found here . the puerta del sol is also famous for its new year 's celebration which has been broadcast on television since december 31 , 1962 .
