Crazy Nights     crazy nights is a studio album by the american hard rock/heavy metal band kiss . it was released on september 18 , 1987 . `` crazy crazy nights '' was the first song released from the album . it reached # 4 in the uk . track listing . - crazy crazy nights - i 'll fight hell to hold you - bang bang you - no , no , no - hell or high water - my way - when your walls come down - reason to live - good girl gone bad - turn on the night - thief in the night
