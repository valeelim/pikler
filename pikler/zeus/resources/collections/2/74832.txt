Las Terrenas, Samaná    las terrenas , samaná las terrenas is a dominican municipality in the samaná province , on the north coast of the peninsula of samaná . the name las terrenas comes from the french `` la terrienne '' ( the `` lady that owns land '' ) . the municipality had , in , a total population of 18,829 : 9,776 men and 9,053 women . the urban population was of the total population . las terrenas has a total area of , about of the total area of the province of samaná . the city is an elevation of above sea level , and at to the northwest of samaná . las terrenas does not have any municipal district . the municipality of las terrenas has the atlantic ocean to the north , the municipality of sánchez to the west and south , and the municipality of samaná to the east . the main economic activities of the municipality are the same as the province : tourism , agriculture and fishing .
