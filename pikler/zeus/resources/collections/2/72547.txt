D'oh!    d'oh ! d'oh ! ( also spelled out as annoyed grunt ) , is a famous catchphrase created by the fictional character homer simpson , from the popular animated television series , `` the simpsons '' . it was also famously accepted into the `` oxford english dictionary '' in 2002. the quote is normally used when homer hurts himself , finds out that he has done something stupid , or when something bad has happened or is about to happen to him . he often gives himself a facepalm at the same time . other characters from the simpsons have also been heard using the chatchprase too , the most common being his son bart . homer has also been known to change this phrase slightly . for example , in the simpsons movie he shouts `` d'oooooome !
