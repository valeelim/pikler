Women and Islam     the complex relationship between women and islam is shown by both islamic texts and the history and culture of the muslim world . islamic scholars maintain that the qur'an , the holy book of islam , affirms women 's religious and moral equality . however , sharia ( islamic law ) shows the marked differences between women 's and men 's roles , rights , and obligations , and many muslim-majority countries give women different rights than men with regard to marriage , divorce , civil rights , legal status , dress code , and education . islamic feminism is a type of feminism that focuses on women in islam . nawal el saadawi is an egyptian feminist who has written many books about women in islam .
