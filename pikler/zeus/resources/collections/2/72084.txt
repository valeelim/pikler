1924 Winter Olympics     the 1924 winter olympics , officially known as the i olympic winter games , were held in 1924 in chamonix , haute-savoie , france between 25 january and 5 february 1924. at first , the games were called `` semaine internationale des sports d'hiver '' ( `` international winter sports week '' ) and were held as part of the 1924 summer olympics . it was later decided by the international olympic committee ( ioc ) that the games would be called the i olympic winter games . athletes from 16 nations competed in the first winter olympic games .
