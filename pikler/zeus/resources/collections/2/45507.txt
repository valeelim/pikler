High School Musical     high school musical is a 2006 disney television movie , starring zac efron , vanessa anne hudgens , ashley tisdale , corbin bleu , monique coleman and lucas grabeel . its sequel is `` high school musical 2 '' . the story is set in new mexico . it is about two high school students , troy bolton , who is captain of his school 's basketball team , and gabriella montez , a shy new student who is good in mathematics and science . together , they try out for the lead parts in their high school musical . despite other students ' attempts to foil their dreams , troy and gabriella persist and inspire others along the way . they also make new friends . other websites . - official website - high school musical news
