Darkness     darkness is when there no light . in practice , out in the open air , there almost always is some light , however slight . the darkness of the night sky is estimated by a nine-point scale known as the bortle scale . darkness is made by blocking light from the sun or some other source of light , which makes shadow . at night the earth blocks the sun , and casts a shadow over one entire side of the world . there is heat radiation even in the dark . in mythology , darkness is often connected with evil . many people are uncomfortable or afraid when in the dark , especially children .
