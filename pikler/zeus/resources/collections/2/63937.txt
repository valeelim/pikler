Chosroid Dynasty     the chosroids ( khosro [ v ] iani , georgian : ხოსრო [ ვ ] იანი ) were a dynasty of the kings and later of the ruling princes of the early georgian state of iberia , natively known as kartli , from the fourth to the ninth centuries . presumably of iranian origin and a branch of the mihranid house , the family accepted christianity as their official religion around the year 337 , and maneuvered between the byzantine empire and sassanid , iran to retain a degree of independence . other pages . - guaramid dynasty
