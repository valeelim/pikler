Manga Kenkanryu     manga kenkanryu ( anti-korea wave ) is a japanese manga book where koreans are described as ugly and stupid . in this manga , the author , sharin yamano , says that japanese colonial rule in korea was a good thing and koreans must say `` thank you '' to japan for the japanese rule of their nation . it was thought to be racism by many people , including japanese . what the author says in the book . - killing korean culture is good , not evil . it is good because it lets koreans be treated as `` equal '' to the japanese . - korea could not modernize by itself . if japan had not ruled korea , korea would be a poor , primitive country now . - koreans begged japan to rule their country . other pages . - `` the truth about nanjing ''
