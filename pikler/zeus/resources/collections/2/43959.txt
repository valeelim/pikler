Thrud     þrúðr , sometimes written as thrúd , is a daughter of the gods sif and thor in norse mythology . although her name is mentioned in the `` prose edda '' , it gives no direct powers or other information about her . thrúd is also one of the valkyries described in `` grímnismál '' ( stanza 36 ) . thrúd is the goddess of the heath , of willows , of trees , flowers and the grass . her name means strength in old norse .
