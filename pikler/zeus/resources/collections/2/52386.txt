Île-de-France    île-de-france île-de-france is a region of france . the capital city is paris . it is also the capital city of france . in 2013 about 12 million people lived in the region . about 2.1 million people live in the city of paris . there are 8 departments in the region . they are : - essonne - hauts-de-seine - paris - seine-et-marne - seine-saint-denis - val-d'oise - val-de-marne - yvelines
