Emo     emo is a genre of music which is short for `` emotive hardcore . '' the genre first formed in the mid-1980s , taking the stylistic formulas of hardcore punk and post-hardcore and combining them with the introspective and sensitive lyricism that emo has become so well known for . in the mid 1990s , emo incorporated indie rock elements . this ultimately made the emo bands of the 90 's much less aggressive and 'punk-ish ' and more melodramatic . once it became mainstream in the 2000s , it became shaped by pop-punk , ( a fusion genre of emo and pop punk exists , and is known as `` emo-pop '' ) alternative rock and melodic hardcore . the birthplace of the emo genre is often referred to as washington , d.c. , mainly because the first known emo band , rites of spring , formed there . today , emo is more often associated with fashion than with music . emo ( sometimes referred to as `` scene '' ) is a teen subculture , though many people use `` emo '' as an insult . emo kids are stereotyped as wearing dark ( especially black ) clothes , mostly wearing tight shirts , skinny jeans and converse shoes . most emo girls and some boys wear thick `` raccoon '' eyeliner . they are also stereotyped as having black dyed hair , or naturally colored with streaks or coon-tails , or unnatural-colored with fringe bangs , of which usually sweep over one eye . the emo culture is also associated with being depressed , introverted , shy and cutting .
