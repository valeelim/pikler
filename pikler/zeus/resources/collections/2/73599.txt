Tánaiste     a tánaiste ( plural : tánaistí ) is the deputy head of the government of the republic of ireland . the taoiseach nominates a member of government to be tánaiste . mary coughlan is the current tánaiste . under the irish free state , the deputy leader was deputy president of the executive council . in 1937 the title was changed to tánaiste in history , the tánaiste was the heir of the chief ( taoiseach ) or king ( rí ) .
