Back Dorm Boys     back dorm boys ( ) are two chinese people who have made several well known spoof music videos in 2006. their name comes from their lip sync video of the backstreet boys ' song `` as long as you love me '' . they are often called the `` back dormitory boys '' . their spoof music videos have been selected by motorola china to promote mobile phones . the two members of the back dorm boys are 韦炜 ( wei wei ) and 黄艺馨 ( huang yi xin ) . they were university students at guangzhou arts institute ( 广州美术学院 ) majoring in sculpture . their videos were very famous in china . the became very famous around the world after the videos were shown on youtube .
