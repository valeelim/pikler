Subaru Impreza     impreza is a japanese automobile . it is made by subaru , which is owned by parent company fuji heavy industries . the car was first made in 1992. the design of the car has been changed 3 times ; the first version was the 1992 model , with it being redesigned in 2000 , 2008 , and 2015. the next redesign is expected to come in 2018 .
