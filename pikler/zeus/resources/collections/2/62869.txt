Stephanie McMahon     stephanie marie mcmahon-levesque ( born september 24 , 1976 ) , better known as stephanie mcmahon , is the `` executive vice president of talent and creative writing '' for world wrestling entertainment ( wwe ) . she is married to paul `` triple h '' levesque . they have three daughters together . she is the daughter of wwe founder , vince mcmahon . she is the sister of shane mcmahon and daughter of linda mcmahon .
