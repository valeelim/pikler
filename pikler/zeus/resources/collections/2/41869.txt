Litter box     a litter box ( sometimes called a sand box , a litter tray or a litter pan ) is a place for cats and some other pets to use as a toilet . litter boxes usually need to be cleaned regularly . cats , for example , wish to have their box cleaned every two to three days . some cats create litterboxes in places like playgrounds and sandpits . litterboxes usually produce unpleasant smells , which can be avoided by special litter .
