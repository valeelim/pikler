Oil field     an oil field is a region with many oil wells extracting petroleum ( oil ) from below ground . because the oil reservoirs usually extend over a large area , possibly several hundred kilometres across , the only way to completely exploit the field is with many wells in different parts of the field . other than wells , there may be pipelines to transport the oil elsewhere , and support buildings for the workers and for repairs . other websites . - interactive oil field map mississippi oil journal - interactive oil and gas maps kansas geological survey
