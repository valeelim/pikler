Continental circuits     the uci protour is the series of races for the top cycling teams . the continental circuits are for teams that are not so good . some teams , such as team milram , have one team on the protour , a team of younger riders on the continental circuit as well as being linked with u23 teams . in this way teams can spot talented riders . there are five circuits , one each for - africa - america - australasia - europe - oceania usually organisers of a protour event can invite one or two teams from the continental circuit to compete . also a protour team might decide to compete in a continental circuit race
