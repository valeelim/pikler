Newton (unit)    newton ( unit ) the newton ( symbol : n ) is the si unit of force . it is named after sir isaac newton because of his work on classical mechanics . a `` newton '' is how much force is required to make a mass of one kilogram accelerate at a rate of one metre per second squared . - 1 n is the force of earth 's gravity on an apple with a mass of about 102 g. - on the earth 's surface , a mass of 1 kg pushes on its support with an average force of 9.8 n .
