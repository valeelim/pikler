Exaggeration     exaggeration is a term for a figure of speech . it means the describing of something and making it more than it really is . the verb is to exaggerate . an example of exaggeration would be : “ i was walking along when suddenly this enormous dog walked along . it was as big as an elephant ” . the dog may have been big , but it was certainly not as big as that . another example of exaggeration would be : “ i caught a fish as big as my house. ” overstatement is another word that means almost the same thing . the opposite of overstatement is understatement . a hyperbole ( ipa : [ haı'pɝ.bə.li ] ) is a type of exaggeration that is used in literature . it is a figure of speech . the opposite of hyperbole is hypobole , which is an understatement . people exaggerate things because they have strong feelings about something . people may exaggerate to make people listen to what they say . they may do it to emphasize something . they may also exaggerate just to sound funny : - ” i ’ ve heard that a million times ” - ” you ’ ve got a head the size of a pin ” .
