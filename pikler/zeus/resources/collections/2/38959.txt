Cost     cost may mean : - the amount of money needed to buy something - something other than money that is used up . example : `` going to jail cost 5 years of my life '' . this usage is a metaphor . - cost function - cost of living , the amount of money it costs just to live in a certain place . it includes food , housing , etc . - opportunity cost , the value of the next best thing you give up whenever you make a decision .
