Oar     an oar is a tool used to move a boat through water . oars have a flat blade at one end . the oarsmen grasp the oar at the other end . the difference between oars and paddles is that paddles are held by the paddler , and are not connected with the vessel . oars generally are fastened to the vessel . the use of oars is called rowing . an oar used at the stern ( rear ) of a boat to steer is called a steering oar .
