MenuetOS     menuetos ( also known as meos ) is an operating system for architecture computers for x86 and x86-64 processors , by ville mikael turjanmaa . it is a very small system , which can be run from a floppy disk . it is written in only assembly code , and works fast because of these differences and because it has less `` code overhead '' . it is available in english , russian , czech , serbian , and chinese .
