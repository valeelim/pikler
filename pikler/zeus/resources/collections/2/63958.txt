Spitz (band)    spitz ( band ) spitz is a japanese rock band that set up in 1987. masamune kusano is a vocalist and plays the guitar , too . tetsuya miwa plays the guitar . akihiro tamura plays the bass . tatsuo sakiyama plays the drums . their songs , especially `` robinson '' , `` cherry '' and `` soramotoberuhazu , '' are very popular . other websites . - spitz official web site - spitz official channel
