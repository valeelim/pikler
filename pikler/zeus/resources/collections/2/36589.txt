Carrion     carrion is the carcass of a dead animal . there are several kinds of animal that feed on such meat , such as vultures , hyenas , condors , or tasmanian devils . carrion is an important source of food for several kinds of carnivores and omnivores . the carrion beetles also feed on it . some plants fake the smell of carrion ( they smell like carrion ) , so that they will attract insects they eat . predators do not normally feed on carrion , they hunt and kill the animals they eat . carrion is sometimes eaten by foxes .
