Promotion     promotion means spreading information about an issue . promotion as part of marketing means spreading information about a product , product line , brand , or company . `` promotion '' includes : - publicity and public relations - advertising `` promotion for marketing '' includes additionally : - personal selling - non-personal selling - sales promotion - tradeshows - direct selling - product placement - for example , arranging that a product is used on a television programme or in a movie so that the viewer can see the brand name or symbol or logo . promotion can also happen when a marketing company is providing discounts and coupons to customers to create brand awareness .
