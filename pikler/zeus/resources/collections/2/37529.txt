Richard Roeper     richard roeper ( born october 17 , 1959 in chicago , illinois ) is an american movie critic , television personality , journalist , and historian . he currently stars on the american television program `` ebert & roeper '' with roger ebert . roeper replaced gene siskel on the program in 2000 ( which used to be called `` siskel & ebert '' ) , shortly after siskel died in 1999. roeper also writes about movies for an american newspaper , the `` chicago sun-times '' . other websites . - biography from tv tome
