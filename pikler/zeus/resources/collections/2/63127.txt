Heating     heating is making something warm . it could mean : - hvac : heating , ventilation and air-conditioning . hvacr `` r '' stand for refrigeration heating devices , or systems : - block heater , or headbolt heater , an electric heater that heats the engine of a car to ease starting in cold weather - boiler - cathode heater , a coil or filament used to heat the cathode in a vacuum tube or cathode ray tube - central heating , method of providing warmth from one point to multiple rooms or apartments of a building - convector heater , a heater which operates by air convection currents circulating through the body of the appliance - dielectric heating , the phenomenon in which radiowave or microwave electromagnetic radiation heats a dielectric material - district heating , a system for distributing heat generated in a centralized location for residential and commercial heating requirements - fan heater , a heater that works by using a fan to pass air over a heating element - feedwater heater , a power plant component used to pre-heat water delivered to the boiler
