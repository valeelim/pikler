Flax     flax ( also known as common flax or linseed ) ( `` linum usitatissimum '' ) is a plant . the fibres of flax are used to make linen . high-quality paper used in banknotes is also made from flax fibres . an oil ( linseed oil ) can be made from the dried ripe flax seeds . flax has been used for a long time in such tasks as making bows and candles .
