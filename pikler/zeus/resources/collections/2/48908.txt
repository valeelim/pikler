Central venous catheter     a central venous catheter ( cvc ) or `` central line '' is a catheter put in a large vein in the neck , chest , or groin when a patient needs close cardiovascular monitoring , for assessment of fluid status , and so that intravenous drugs and fluids can be given to the patient more effectively . the veins most commonly used are the internal jugular vein , the subclavian vein , and the femoral vein .
