Obfuscation     obfuscation means making something harder to understand , usually by complicating sentences needlessly . weasel words are a form of obfuscation . obfuscation is usually used when people either do not know what they are talking about or wish to hide their meaning . some people say that when doctors use difficult medical words to hide unpleasant things from patients , they are using obfuscation . technical term : obfuscation is sometimes used to make source code much harder to read and reverse engineer . this can be a significant concern for technology like java and javascript that require source code to be exposed in order to function .
