Guwahati     guwahati is the largest city in the north eastern region of india . it houses the capital complex of the legislative assembly of assam . the first bridge across the brahmaputra river is situated at the northwest perimeter of the city . guwahati serves as the gateway to the rest of the seven states of arunachal pradesh , manipur , meghalaya , mizoram , nagaland and tripura . other pages . - tezpur
