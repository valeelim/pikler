The Smiths     the smiths were an english rock band from manchester . they formed in 1982 and split up in 1987. they released four studio albums . the band consisted of morrissey ( singing ) , johnny marr ( guitar ) , andy rourke ( bass ) and mike joyce ( drums ) . critics have called them the most important alternative rock band to emerge from the british independent music scene of the 1980s . `` q '' magazine writer simon goddard wrote that the smiths were the one truly important group of the 1980s , `` the most influential british guitar group of the decade '' and the first independent group to become successful in the main music scene . the band was based on the songwriting partnership of morrissey and marr . they signed to the independent record label rough trade records . on this , they released four studio albums : `` the smiths ( 1984 ) , `` meat is murder '' ( 1985 ) , `` the queen is dead '' ( 1986 ) and `` strangeways , here we come '' ( 1987 ) . they have also released several compilations , and many singles that were not released on any of their studio albums .
