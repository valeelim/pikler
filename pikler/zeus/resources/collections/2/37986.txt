Antihero     an antihero is a main character in a story who does not act like a hero . examples of antiheroes are marvel comics the punisher & deadpool , sega 's `` shadow the hedgehog '' , batman , catwoman of batman , sweeney todd and martha-scott benson most of the time it means a character in fiction , television , or movies . sometimes it is used to mean a living person or person from history .
