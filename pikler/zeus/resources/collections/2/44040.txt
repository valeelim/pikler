Anhanguera     anhanguera , which means 'old devil ' , was a pterodactyl from the upper cretaceous . it was found in the santana formation of brazil , and specimens from england are also placed in this genus . it ate fish and grew to 14 feet ( 4.5 meters ) across . the species `` anhanguera piscator '' is now placed in the genus `` coloborhynchus '' .
