Basilisk     basilisks are a type of lizard . they are about 70-75 cm long , when fully grown , and weigh about 80 grams . they can be found in south america . currently , there are four different species . they can be very still and hard to see . the basilisk lizard can walk on water . it runs across the surface of a pond or pool , without sinking . its speed keeps it from sinking . at the same time , its long tail helps it to balance .
