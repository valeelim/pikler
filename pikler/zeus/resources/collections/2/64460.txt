Garnet     a garnet is one kind of silicate mineral . there are many different kinds of garnets , containing different metals . crystals of garnet are often large and pretty . their most common color is red or purple , but they can be found in almost all colors and are often used in jewelry . in the united states , garnet is the birthstone for people born in the month of january . related pages . - list of minerals - list of birthstones further reading . - hurlbut , cornelius s. ; klein , cornelis , 1985 , `` manual of mineralogy '' , 20th ed. , wiley , isbn 0-471-80580-7 - color encyclopedia of gemstones isbn 0-442-20333-0 other websites . - http : //www.gemstonemagnetism.com contains a comprehensive section about garnets and garnet magnetism . - usgs garnet locations â€“ usa - http : //www.gemstone.org/gem-by-gem/english/garnet.html - http : //www.mindat.org/min-10272.html - < a href= '' http % 3a//blogs.loc.gov/law/2012/01/garnets/ '' > blog post on garnets on the law library of congress 's blog < /a >
