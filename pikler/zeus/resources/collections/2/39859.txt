UPN     upn ( which originally stood for the united paramount network ) was a television network in over 200 places in the united states . upn was owned by cbs corporation , which also owns the larger cbs network . upn shut down on september 15 , 2006 , and was replaced with the cw , a joint venture between cbs corporation and the warner bros. division of time warner , which owned the wb before that network was also shut down in 2006 and merged with upn to create the cw .
