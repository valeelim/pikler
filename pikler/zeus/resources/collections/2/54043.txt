Renting     renting ( also called leasing or letting ) is where someone pays money to use something for a definite amount of time . this can be anything from a car or house to a dvd . the document that has all the details written on it is called the `` lease '' . the person who owns the item that will be leased or rented is called the `` lessor '' . the person who is paying the money to use the item is called the `` lessee '' . if it is a house , building , or land that is being leased , the person who pays the money is called the `` tenant '' . for example , a tenant farmer is a person who rents a farm from its owner . in a closed-end lease ( like a car ) , it is best to drive a vehicle with a residual value of at least 55 % .
