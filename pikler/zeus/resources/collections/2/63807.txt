Comanche     comanche peoples are native americans who lived in an area called the `` comancheria '' . this is where eastern new mexico , southern colorado , southern kansas , all of oklahoma and most of northern and southern texas are today . there once were as many as 20,000 comanches . today the comanche nation has 10,000 members , most in oklahoma ( centered at lawton ) , and the rest in texas , california , and new mexico . the comanche speak a language of their own , the comanche language that is related to other native american ones , called `` uto-aztecan language family '' . the name comanche was given by the mexicans , and it was the word the comanche used to call them , `` komantsia '' ( enemy , foreigner ) . but the comanches ' call themselves `` numunuu '' , meaning `` human being '' or `` the people '' . they were very good warriors and fought the white settlers and army for many years until they gave up in 1875. the woman in the family made the teepees . they gathered wild potatoes , fruits , nuts and berries . the woman in the family cooked , cleaned and made their clothing . the clothing was made out either bear , deer , bison , buffalo , wolves or coyotes .
