Robe     a robe is a type of clothing . it is loose-fitting and sometimes ties in the front . there are different types of robes . these include the following : - bathrobe - usually worn at home after taking a bath . they are also sometimes worn at night , or in the morning . - graduation robe - a gown traditionally worn at a high school graduation , or college graduation . - judge 's robe - part of a judge 's clothing . other jobs may call for robes , such as a barrister . - wizard 's robe - in fantasy , wizards are usually shown wearing robes .
