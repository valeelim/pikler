Sunnah     the word sunnah ( arabic : سنة ) is an arabic word meaning `` tradition '' or `` way . '' for muslims , sunnah means `` the way of the prophet '' . the sunnah is made up of the words and actions of muhammad , the prophet of islam . muslims believe muhammad 's life is a good model for them to follow in their own lives . muslim scholars learn about the sunnah by studying thousands of stories about muhammad , his family , and his first followers . these stories are called hadith .
