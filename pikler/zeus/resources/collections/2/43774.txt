Proceratosaurus     proceratosaurus was a small dinosaur from the middle jurassic , about 165 million years ago . it was found in gloucestershire , england , in 1910. it was a carnivorous theropod . `` proceratosaurus '' was first believed to be an early ancestor of `` ceratosaurus '' , but it was actually an ancestor of the coelurosaurs . thus , it was an early ancestor of `` tyrannosaurus '' and its relatives in the upper cretaceous . the animal in life was about 3m long and weighed up to 60kg .
