Crook of Devon     the crook of devon is a perthshire village about 6 miles south west of kinross on the a977 . it was famous in the 17th century for its witch burnings . down the road at the side of the institute ( village hall ) on the right hand side is a field called lamblaires and in the north west corner is the place where the witches were strangled and then burned at the stake . other websites . - overview of crook of devon
