Kuiper belt     the kuiper belt is an area of the solar system beyond the orbit of neptune ( at 30 astronomical units ) to 50 au from the sun . the objects within the kuiper belt together with the members of the `` scattered disk '' beyond , are together called trans-neptunian . many objects such as dwarf planets in the kuiper belt are much bigger than the ones in the asteroid belt . at least some kuiper belt objects are icebound . after the kuiper belt was discovered in 1992 , the number of known kuiper belt objects ( kbos ) has added to over a thousand , and more than 70,000 kbos over 100 km ( 62 mi ) in diameter .
