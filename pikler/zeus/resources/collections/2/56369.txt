Nausea     nausea is a general feeling of unease and discomfort in the stomach , often with the urge to vomit . the word `` nausea '' comes from the latin word for seasickness . nausea is a symptom , rather than an illness or disease . the causes for it very often are not in the stomach itself , but somewhere else in the body . nausea is often caused by a stomach virus . nausea is usually harmless , in the short term . a good treatment for it can be not to take solid food ( and to only drink non-alcoholic drinks , like water ) . nausea can also occur during pregnancy , and is quite normal in that context . people can suffer nausea without vomiting .
