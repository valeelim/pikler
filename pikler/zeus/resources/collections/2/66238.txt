ACID     acid is a concept used with databases . it is an abbreviation . it stands for - `` atomicity '' - something is either done completely , or not done at all . the state of doing it is not visible outside the database . - `` consistency '' - the database is in a legal state at all times . when a transaction occurs , it can not break the rules . these rules are about `` integrity '' , what is allowed and what is not allowed in certain locations of the database . - `` isolation '' - there can be more than one transaction occurring at the same time . a certain transaction will not see changes made by other transactions . - `` durability '' - when a transaction is done , it will be committed . after it is committed , it can no longer be undone .
