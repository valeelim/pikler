Totalitarianism     totalitarianism is a word used to describe modern regimes ( political systems ) where the government controls all public behaviour and as much of private behaviour as they can . it often involves mass participation in events such as parades or rallies . this idea was mainly used in nazi germany and the soviet union . the satire `` nineteen eighty-four '' by george orwell was about such a society taken to extremes . related pages . - dictatorship - fascism - nazism - stalinism
