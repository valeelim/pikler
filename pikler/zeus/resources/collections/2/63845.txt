Serena Williams     serena jameka williams ( born september 26 , 1981 ) is an american professional tennis player who is currently ranked no . 1 in women 's singles tennis . williams has won 23 grand slam singles titles . she is the younger sister of another former world no . 1 professional female tennis player , venus williams . the williams sisters grew up in los angeles and currently live together in palm beach gardens , florida , united states . both are jehovah 's witnesses .
