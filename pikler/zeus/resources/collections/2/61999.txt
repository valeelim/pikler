The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air     the fresh prince of bel-air is an american television series . it is a sitcom that aired on nbc from september 10 , 1990 , to may 20 , 1996 for six seasons . it is set in bel air , los angeles , and stars will smith as the `` fresh prince '' . it was nominated for two golden globe awards . the show was also successful outside of the united states , and was on the uk channel bbc2 from 1991 to 2004 .
