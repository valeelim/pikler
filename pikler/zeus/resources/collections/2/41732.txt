Ipiaú     ipiaú is a city in bahia , brazil . it is at around . the city is in the meeting point of the `` contas river '' and the `` água-branca river '' ( white-water river ) . ipiaú is bordered by ibirataia and jequié to the north , aiquara and jitaúna to the west , ibirataia and barra do rocha to the east and itagibá to the south . the city is a important producer and exporter of cacao in brazil . is the birthplace of many noted brazilian writers and musicians such as euclides neto and luiz caldas .
