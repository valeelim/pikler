Seshat     seshat was the ancient egyptian idea of knowledge , shown as a goddess . she became the goddess of writing , astronomy/astrology , architecture , and mathematics . when thoth became the god of wisdom , seshat was seen as his daughter , or sometimes , as his wife . in art , she was shown as a woman with a stylised papyrus plant above her head . this symbolised writing since the egyptians wrote on a material that came from papyrus . other websites . - thekeep.org - egyptmyths.net - ancientegypt.co.uk
