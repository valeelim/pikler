Addington     addington is a village in the london borough of croydon in south london . addington is home to many historical sites in croydon , like croydon palace and the addington palace . st mary 's church is also in addington and is known for the cross commemorating five archbishops in the foreground . before the tram system was introduced in 2000 connections to the town were very short with only a few buses to croydon . the reputation of addington is not good . people see it as run down and not very nice , meaning that a lot of people from elsewhere in london try to avoid it . new addington is a town close by to addington . once there was only one school in the village and all the children went to it , now that school has been demolished .
