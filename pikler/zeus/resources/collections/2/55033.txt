Mark-Anthony Turnage     mark-anthony turnage ( born grays , essex , 10 june 1960 ) is an english composer . he is one of the most original composers in his country today . turnage studied at the royal college of music and won all the major prizes . he became famous when he wrote an opera called `` greek '' . it is typical of his musical style : quite lyrical but also very dramatic and aggressive . his music is often inspired by jazz . other works include `` kai '' , `` rockaby '' , `` blood on the floor '' , `` dispelling the fears '' , `` drowned out '' and `` three screaming popes '' . he has written music for the english national opera , city of birmingham symphony orchestra and the chicago symphony orchestra . other websites . -
