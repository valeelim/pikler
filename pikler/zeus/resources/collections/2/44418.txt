Sign of the cross     the sign of the cross is a physical gesture made by catholics and other christians . it is usually done at the beginning and ending of prayers . the right hand goes from the forehead , to the chest , to the left shoulder , and to the right shoulder . or the right one could be the first . this is sometimes done while saying `` father , son , holy spirit .
