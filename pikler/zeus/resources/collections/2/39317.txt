Demilitarized zone     a demilitarized zone is an area of land that has no military forces in it . this is usually to stop armies from fighting . the most famous demilitarized zone is the korean demilitarized zone on the land between north korea and south korea . no one can go into the demilitarized zone , so the wildlife is well preserved . there are thousands of soldiers and weapons on each side of the demilitarized zone , including to this day a massive american presence .
