6 BC     year 6 bc was a common year starting on sunday or monday of the julian calendar . at the time , it was known as the year of the consulship of balbus and vetus . events . - caesar augustus sends ferrets to the balearic islands to control rabbits - tiberius is sent to armenia . he then retires to rhodes . deaths . - consort ban , chinese concubine of emperor cheng of han , also a poet and scholar ( b . 48 bc ) - consort feng yuan ( b. c. 48 bc ) - liu xiang , chinese scholar ( b . 77 bc )
