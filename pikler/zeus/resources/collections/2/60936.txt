Yaoundé     yaoundé is the capital city of the republic of cameroon . it is the second largest city in the country after douala . yaoundé was founded in 1888 by german traders . it was created to be used as a base for the ivory trade and an agricultural research station . the city was under the control of belgian troops during world war i. after the war , it became the capital of french cameroons . it has remained the capital of the republic of cameroon until the present day .
