Walter Sutton     walter stanborough sutton ( 5 april 1877 – 10 november 1916 ) was an american biologist and surgeon . sutton was born in utica , new york and raised in russell , kansas . he got bachelors and masters degres from the university of kansas . sutton then went to columbia university and got his doctorate in medicine in 1907. in 1903 sutton suggested that chromosomes , which segregate in a mendelian fashion , are hereditary units . edmund b. wilson ( 1856–1939 ) , sutton 's teacher , and the author of one of the most famous text-books in biology , called this the `` sutton–boveri hypothesis '' .
