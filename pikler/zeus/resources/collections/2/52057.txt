Anarchy in the U.K.     `` anarchy in the u.k. '' is a punk rock song by the sex pistols . it was first released as a single in 1975. in 1976 , it was released as part of the album `` never mind the bollocks here 's the sex pistols '' . the song 's lyrics espouse a nihilistic and violent concept of anarchy . it has been covered by various other bands , including megadeth . this song was in the video game `` '' .
