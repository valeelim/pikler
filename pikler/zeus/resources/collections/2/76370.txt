Lady Mary Grey     lady mary grey ( 1545- 20 april 1578 ) was the sister of lady jane grey and lady katherine grey . her parents were lady frances brandon and henry grey . mary grey 's parents were disappointed with her birth , because she was a girl and a . she was described as `` four foot tall and hunchbacked '' . mary 's life was not as exciting as her sisters , jane and katherine grey . but after their death she was the last surviving grandchild of mary tudor , and therefore some people thought she might be heiress to the english throne . she married the royal gatekeeper thomas keyes in 1565 but this was without queen elizabeth 's permission . but like her sister katherine 's marriage to edward seymour , earl of hertford , queen elizabeth was angry . elizabeth claimed that she wanted `` no bastard keyes '' . elizabeth put mary under house arrest . she was released after the death of her husband in 1572 and was permitted to attend court occasionally . she died childless at age 33. other websites . - britannia.com - life in elizabethan england
