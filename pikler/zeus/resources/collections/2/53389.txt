Final Fantasy VIII     final fantasy viii is a fantasy role-playing video game . it was released for the playstation in 1999 and windows computers in 2000. it was made by square ( now square enix ) . it is the eight title of the `` final fantasy '' series . it removed magic point-based spell-casting . it was the first title to consistently use realistically proportioned characters . the game became available on playstation network as a psone classics title in 2009 .
