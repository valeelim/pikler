Oireachtas     the oireachtas is the national parliament of ireland . there are three parts of the oireachtas : - the president of ireland - dáil éireann - the lower house . it is similar to the house of commons in the united kingdom or the house of representatives in the united states . - seanad éireann - the upper house . it is similar to the house of lords and the senate only the irish names are used , never the english
