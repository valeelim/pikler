Stadium Australia     stadium australia , known for sponsorship reasons as anz stadium , formerly telstra stadium , is a sports stadium in sydney , australia . the 2000 summer olympics took place there . now it is used for many other sports , like rugby and afl . the stadium opened in march 1999. in 2002 , the stadium was named after the sponsor telstra . 1 january 2008 it was renamed the anz stadium . as `` stadium australia '' , the name was sometimes confused with the sydney football stadium , which was formerly known as aussie stadium .
