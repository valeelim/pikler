Funeral Blues     `` funeral blues '' is a poem written in 1936 by w. h. auden . `` funeral blues '' is a simple poem about death , isolation ( loneliness ) , emptiness and longing . the death of a lover may or may not be a literal event ( actually happened ) . the poem is also about the end of a romantic relationship with somebody other than the person 's spouse/partner . in four stanzas it moves from the time before the funeral ( stop all the clocks , silence the pianos ... ) , during the funeral itself ( bring out the coffin , let the mourners come ... ) , after the funeral ( he was my north , my south ... ) , and the loneliness and grief that comes with it .
