Stronghold     stronghold means castle or any other strongly fortified place . the history of fortified buildings reaches from antiquity to modern times . in medieval europe , the castle was the most common type of stronghold and often the residence of the king or territorial lord . the maginot line is an example of a 20th century stronghold . stronghold is also the name of a small video game franchise consisting of several castle-sim games made by firefly studios . related pages . - fortification
