African slave trade     the african slave trade was a trade in slaves , and , like most of the world , has carried on for thousands of years in africa . the first main route passed through the sahara . after the age of exploration , african slaves became part of the atlantic slave trade , from which comes the modern , western type of slavery . despite being against the law , slavery continues in all parts of the world , including africa . in mauritania slavery was not made illegal until august 2007 .
