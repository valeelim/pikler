Labium     labium ( plural `` labia '' ) is a latin-derived term meaning `` lip '' . `` labium '' and its derivatives ( including labia , labial , labrum and `` labellum '' ) are used to describe any lip-like structure , but in the english language , `` labium '' often specifically refers to parts of the vulva . a variant of the word , `` labellum '' ( plural : `` labella '' ) , is used by people studying entomology and botany .
