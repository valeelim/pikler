Native Esperanto speakers     native esperanto speakers are people who learned esperanto as one of their first languages . esperanto is not spoken in any countries as an official language , so they must become bilingual , trilingual , or multilingual . native esperanto speakers can use this language completely and more fluently than non-native esperanto speakers . there are not very many native esperanto speakers . it is thought that no more than one or two thousand people speak esperanto as their first language . other pages . - constructed language
