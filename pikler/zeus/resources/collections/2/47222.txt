Aberdeen, Washington    aberdeen , washington aberdeen is a city in the american state of washington . aberdeen was founded by samuel benn in 1884. in 2000 , 16,461 people lived in aberdeen . aberdeen is sometimes called the `` gateway to the olympic peninsula '' . it is also called the `` birthplace of grunge '' because musician kurt cobain is from aberdeen . other notable musicians from aberdeen include the melvins . aberdeen is also the hometown of professional wrestler bryan danielson , better known as daniel bryan .
