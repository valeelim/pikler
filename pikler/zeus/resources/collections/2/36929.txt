Nocturne     a nocturne is a piece of music , usually for piano , which suggests the night ( “ nocturnal ” means “ of the night ” ) . the irish composer john field was the first person to make nocturnes popular . he was inspired by the slow , gentle tunes of italian operas of his time ( early 19th century ) . his nocturnes are mostly tunes with many long notes in the right hand over . other pages . - nocturnes ( debussy ) - serenade
