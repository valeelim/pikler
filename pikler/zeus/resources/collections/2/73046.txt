Curtis (50 Cent album)    curtis ( 50 cent album ) curtis is the third album by rapper 50 cent . the album was released on september 11 2007. the album features production from dr. dre , eminem , and timbaland , among others . guest appearances include akon , justin timberlake , nicole scherzinger , and other notable musicians . 50 cent divides between `` hard '' songs and `` soft '' songs on the album . `` curtis '' was released and sold 691,000 copies in its first week on the `` billboard '' 200. it sold the most copies in one week for an east coast album since jay-z 's `` kingdom come '' sold 680,000 copies sold . the album 's sales competition with kanye west 's `` graduation '' was considered to be a `` great day for hip hop '' .
