Neurotoxin     a neurotoxin is a toxin that acts specifically on nerve cells – neurons – usually by interacting with membrane proteins and ion channels . many of the venoms and other toxins that organisms use in defense against vertebrates are neurotoxins . a common effect is paralysis , which sets in extremely rapidly . the venom of bees , scorpions , pufferfish , spiders and snakes can contain many different toxins . many neurotoxins act by affecting voltage-dependent ion channels .
