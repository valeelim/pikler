Telesto (moon)    telesto ( moon ) telesto is a tiny moon of saturn . it was discovered by a number of astronomers in 1980 , based from some observations from the earth . it was named after telesto in greek mythology . telesto orbits around saturn together with much larger moon tethys . thus , telesto is also known as a trojan moon . the moon leads at a point 60 degrees in front of tethys in its orbit . another small moon , calypso , orbits with telesto .
