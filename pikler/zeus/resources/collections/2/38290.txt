Choice     a choice is the act of choosing . it is the selection of an optional act , in other words , it has to be an act which allows us a choice . a choice is a decision between alternatives . we all make choices everyday , from what we are going to have for breakfast to whether we drive or walk to work . the field of economics examines how people make choices about how to use scarce resources and goods , such as their money or free time . some ways of thinking such as existentialism argue that people 's choices and decisions are very important .
