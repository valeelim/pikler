Dill     dill ( `` anethum graveolens '' ) is a short-lived annual herb that is often used as a spice , to make food taste better . fresh and dried dill leaves ( sometimes called `` dill weed '' to distinguish it from dill seed ) are used as herbs . like caraway , its fernlike leaves are aromatic , and are used to flavor many foods , such as gravlax ( pickled salmon ) , borscht and other soups , cream cheese and pickles . dill is said to be best when used fresh , as it lose its flavor rapidly if dried ; however , freeze-dried dill leaves preserve their flavor relatively well for a few months . dill seed is used as a spice , with a flavor similar to caraway . dill oil can be extracted from the leaves , stems and seeds of the plant . other websites . - plants for a future : `` anethum graveolens '' - 'a modern herbal ' ( grieves , 1931 )
