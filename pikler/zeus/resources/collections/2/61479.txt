Western United States     the western united states—commonly referred to as the american west or simply the west—traditionally refers to the westernmost states of the united states . since the united states has expanded westward since its founding , the definition of the west has changed over time . in the 18th and early 19th centuries the appalachian mountains were considered to be the boundary . nowadays the mississippi river is often referenced as the easternmost possible boundary of the west . the census bureau defines the western united states as these 13 states : alaska , arizona , california , colorado , hawaii , idaho , montana , nevada , new mexico , oregon , utah , washington , and wyoming . the `` west '' had played a large role in american history ; the american old west is an important part of america 's folklore .
