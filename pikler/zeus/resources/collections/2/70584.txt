Operación Puerto doping case     the operación puerto doping case ( meaning `` operation mountain pass '' ) is a doping investigation in spain . it started in may of 2006. dr. eufemiano fuentes is accused of giving banned doping drugs to 200 professional athletes . fuentes doped a lot of well known tennis and football stars but most attention was on the fairly small number of professional road cyclists that were named in the investigation . by may 2007 , fifteen have been proved not guilty , but three have admitted doping or have had very much evidence of blood doping placed before them .
