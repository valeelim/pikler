Hyperion (moon)    hyperion ( moon ) hyperion is a moon of saturn . it was discovered on september 16 , 1848 by william cranch bond , george phillips bond and william lassell . it is irregular in shape , and has a sponge-like appearance . it is the second largest irregular ( i.e . not spherical ) moon in the solar system , the largest being proteus . its rotation is chaotic , meaning its axis of rotation wobbles , unlike how the earth and the moon rotate , or any other moon in the solar system .
