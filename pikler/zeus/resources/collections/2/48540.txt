Caesarion     ptolemy xv philopator philometor caesar , nicknamed caesarion ( little caesar ) greek : πτολεμαίος ιε ' φιλοπάτωρ φιλομήτωρ καίσαρ , καισαρίων ( june 23 , 47 bc – august , 30 bc ) was the son of cleopatra vii . he ruled egypt as a child along with cleopatra until 30 bc . he was murdered by octavian , who would later become the roman emperor augustus . caesarion is probably the son of julius caesar . if so , he is the only known son of caesar . caesarion was the last king ( pharaoh ) of the ptolemaic dynasty of egypt .
