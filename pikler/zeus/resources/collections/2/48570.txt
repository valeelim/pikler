Republican National Committee     the republican national committee ( the r.n.c . or rnc ) is a group of people who coordinate the activities of the u.s. republican party in all fifty states . the r.n.c . has its headquarters in washington , dc . it also has state republican party headquarters in each of the 50 state capitals . the current chairwoman of the committee is ronna romney mcdaniel of michigan . related pages . - democratic national committee other websites . - official rnc website
