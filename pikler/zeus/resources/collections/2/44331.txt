Coprolalia     coprolalia is a condition that affects about 15 % of people with tourette syndrome . someone with coprolalia will use words that make people feel uncomfortable so often that it is hard to control . this is often in the form of a stream of curse words . people who have tourette syndrome are not the only people who experience coprolalia . for example , it can happen in people who have had a stroke , or who have schizophrenia .
