Phosphate     a phosphate is a salt of phosphoric acid . phosphates are important in biochemistry . phosphates have the formula po and a molar mass of 94.973 g/mol . an example of a phosphate is sodium phosphate . three different types of phosphates are known . they are orthophosphate , po ; metaphosphate , po ; and pyrophosphate , po . phosphates are made of one phosphorus atom surrounded by four oxygen atoms . many phosphates do not dissolve in water .
