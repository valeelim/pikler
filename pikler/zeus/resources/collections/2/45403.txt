Drama     drama is a type of literature . a drama can be in the form of a novel , television show , movie , play or dance . although drama is a greek word meaning `` action '' , most dramas focus more on the relationships between people than on the actions . dramas usually have a serious feel to them . drama is considered the opposite of comedy , but drama and comedy can be used at the same time . drama is full of sensational action , dialogue , incidents etc .
