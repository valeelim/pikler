Curve     a curve is a line that turns or bends . a curved line is never straight , and a curved surface is a surface that is never flat . some curves can be drawn on paper , but some curves , like a helix , can only be seen in 3-d. a circle is a curve that can be described with the formula `` x '' + `` y '' = `` r '' . `` x '' and `` y '' are used as in a cartesian coordinate system , and `` r '' is the radius of the circle ( distance from the center to the outside ) . many other curves can also be described with a formula like that . related pages . - ellipse - parabola - hyperbola
