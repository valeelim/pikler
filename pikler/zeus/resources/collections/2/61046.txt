Shore     a shore or shoreline is the land at the edge of a large body of water , such as an ocean or river . where the water is a sea , the shore is also called a `` coast '' . shores are influenced by the topography of the surrounding landscape , as well as by erosion caused by water , such as waves . the geological makeup of rock and soil controls the type of shore which is created . erosion of sedimentary soils can make a sandy or pebbly shore , usually called a beach . a shore includes the intertidal zone that is alternately exposed and covered by waves and tides . a shore will often have a lighthouse on it to help ships in the sea . related pages . - coast
