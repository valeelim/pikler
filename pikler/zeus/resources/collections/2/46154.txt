Ratchet &amp; Clank (series)    ratchet & amp ; clank ( series ) ratchet & clank is a series of video games . as of 2013 , there are 13 separate video games . in addition to the games , the characters are featured as action figures , in a manga , comic series and as cameos in several other video games . an animated film has been announced for release in 2015. the main characters of the series are ratchet and clank . ratchet is a lombax , a type of alien made up for the game . clank is his robot friend . they travel in space ships through the universe on many adventures . in each game , ratchet and clank must work together to fight evil or bring cruel rulers to justice . the series is noted for using unique and extreme weapons and devices . because of the popularity , the developer insomniac games added this feature to its other video games . the main series has 5 games . `` ratchet and clank '' was released on november 4 , 2002 in north america . - `` ratchet & clank '' - `` '' ( `` ratchet & clank 2 : locked and loaded '' in europe ) - `` '' ( `` ratchet & clank 3 '' in europe )
