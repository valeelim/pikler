Planetary ring     a planetary ring is a ring made of dust and other particles orbiting around a planet in a flat-disc shaped region . the gas giants jupiter , saturn , uranus and neptune are the only planets known to have rings , with the rings of saturn being the most spectacular system of rings . the rings sometimes have `` shepherd satellites '' . these moons orbit either in between ring gaps or the outside edge of a ring . the moons ' gravity maintain the rings current shape with a well defined edge . any material that drifts closer to a moon 's orbit , it can either move back into the body of the ring , deflect out into space , or is added onto the moon 's surface . the rings can be made of silica or water ice . it is not known how any of the rings were formed .
