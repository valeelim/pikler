Evan Lysacek     evan lysacek ( born june 4 , 1985 ) is an american figure skater . he grew up in chicago , illinois . lysacek has trained all over the world , including in switzerland and in moscow . he now trains in el segundo , california under his trainer frank carroll . carroll at one time trained michelle kwan . lysacek won the united states figure skating championships in 2007. he has won two bronze medals at the world figure skating championships . he has also won the four continents figure skating championships twice . lysacek has won three silver medals at the world junior figure skating championships . that , followed immediately by his two bronze medals at the senior world championships , meant that until 2007 , he had won a medal at every world championship he had ever entered . other websites . - figureskates online - evan lysacek - the united states figure skating association page for him - the international skating union page for him
