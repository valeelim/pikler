Marie Picasso     marie picasso ( born 24 april 1979 ) is a swedish reality show star , model and singer who rose to fame in 2002 when she was a housemate on big brother . she later got a job as a game show host on tv4 leading two different game shows . in 2007 she entered idol 2007 where she is currently in the top 10 of the swedish version of pop idol . picasso won pop idol on 7 december 2007 in globen arena in stockholm with 51,3 % of the votes and she won over the first runner up amanda jenssen . her debut single is called `` this moment '' . source . - from big brother to idol 2007
