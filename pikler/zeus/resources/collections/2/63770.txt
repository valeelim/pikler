Puffy AmiYumi     puffy amiyumi was the one of the more popular j-pop groups of the 1990s . the band has two members , ami onuki and yumi yoshimura . it was first formed in 1996. they had some success when they went to the united states to record and perform . they had a short-lived cartoon television series on cartoon network . it can still be seen occasionally on the japanese version of cartoon network .
