Turkish Van     the turkish van is a breed of cat that comes from turkey . it has lived in the lake van area of turkey for hundreds of years . this is why it is named the turkish van . this part of turkey is in very high mountains and has long , cold winters . but the summers are hot . this cat got used to this climate over time by its body changing . it now has a thick , full fur coat in the winter , but sheds in the summer and becomes a short-haired cat . the turkish van swims in the summer to cool itself off and the thin summer coat dries quickly . a turkish van may have blue eyes , amber eyes , or eyes with two different colors . other websites . - cat fanciers ' association : the turkish van breed
