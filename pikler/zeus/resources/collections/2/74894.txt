Santiago Rusiñol     santiago rusiñol ( 1861 - 1931 ) was a catalan naturalist painter , author , and playwright . he was born in barcelona in 1861 , and died in aranjuez in 1931 while painting its famous gardens . he influenced pablo picasso as a modern artist , and also left a lot of modernist buildings in sitges , a town from catalonia very influenced by that artist . he was a modernist artist and there is a street in sant cugat del vallés , a town near barcelona , with his name .
