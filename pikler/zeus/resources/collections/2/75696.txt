Walter Simons     walter simons ( 24 september 1861 in elberfeld - 14 july 1937 in nowawes ) was a german politician . he was president of the german supreme court from 1922-1929. after the death of friedrich ebert in february , 1925 he was acting president of the weimar republic from 12 march 1925 until 12 may 1925 when the next president , paul von hindenburg , was elected and assumed office .
