Braunau am Inn     braunau am inn is a town in the innviertel region of upper austria ( ober√∂sterreich ) , the north-western state of austria . it lies about 90 km west of linz and about 60 km north of salzburg , on the border with the german state of bavaria . it has a continental climate ( `` dfa '' in the koeppen climate classification ) . the population in 2001 was 16,372. a port of entry , it is connected by bridges over the inn river with its bavarian counterpart , simbach am inn . it is the birthplace of adolf hitler .
