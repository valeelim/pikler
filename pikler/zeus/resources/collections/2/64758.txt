Komarno     komarno ( , , ) is a city in the horodok raion ( district ) of the lviv oblast ( province ) in western ukraine . 4,000 people live there . it was founded in 1324. in the center of town is an old polish kostel ( catholic church ) . there is also an old wooden church with ancient icons . the river vereshytsia flows through the town . the hasidic dynasty of komarno comes from this town .
