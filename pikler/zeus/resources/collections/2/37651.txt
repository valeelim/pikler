Paul Allen     paul gardner allen ( born january 21 , 1953 ) is an american who created microsoft with bill gates . he is the fifth richest person in the united states . he lives in mercer island , washington . allen is also the outright owner of two sports teams , and is part-owner of a third . he fully owns the seattle seahawks of the nfl and the portland trail blazers of the nba . he is a minority owner of seattle sounders fc in major league soccer .
