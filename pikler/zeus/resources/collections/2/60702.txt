Gold Coast Art Centre     the gold coast art centre is a place on the gold coast , queensland , australia . the art centre is home to many facilities including the gold coast city art gallery . the art centre holds events like weddings , theatre , parties , corporate functions , seminars and conferences . the art centre is funded by the gold coast city council the gold coast art centre has many facilities , the facilities are listed below : - panorama suite - two cinemas - paradise room - arts theatre - arts restaurant - lobby shop - harlequin room - two art galleries - clubroom - sculpture walk - cafe arts - costume hire department - comedy clubhouse - technical services department - the boardroom - st. margaret 's chapel other websites . - gold coast city art centre webstite
