Rhenium     rhenium is a chemical element . it has the chemical symbol re . it has the atomic number 75. it is a rare metal . it is silver white . in chemistry it is placed in a group of metal elements named the transition metals . the chemistry of rhenium is similar to manganese . it was the last natural element to be discovered . rhenium made as a by-product of molybdenum refinement . rhenium is used in some manganese alloys . rhenium-molybdenum alloys are superconductors .
