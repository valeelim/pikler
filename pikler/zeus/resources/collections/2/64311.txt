Airbus A340     the airbus a340 is a long-range four-engined widebody commercial passenger airplane manufactured by airbus s.a.s . a subsidiary of eads . it is similar in design to the twin-engined a330 . there are 4 variants of this model : the a340-200 ( which is the shortest ) , the a340-300 , the a340-500 and the longest one the a340-600 . the a340-600 is the second longest passenger plane in the world . having four engines , the a340 uses more fuel than many other aircrafts.however , that also mean it does not have to pass the etops ( extended range twin operations ) test to fly long range . the airbus a340 is now not made anymore .
