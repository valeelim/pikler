Matchbox Twenty     matchbox twenty ( or mb20 , mbt , matchbox twenty , originally spelled matchbox 20 ) is an alternative rock band from orlando , florida , united states . the members of the band are rob thomas ( vocals , piano ) , kyle cook ( guitar ) , brian yale ( bass ) , paul doucette ( guitar ) and ryan macmillian ( drums ) . matt beck is a live member , playing several instruments in their shows . adam gaynor was formerly with the band . he left it in 2005. their three studio albums have been popular worldwide , and they have sold more than 29 million copies . they are `` yourself or someone like you '' , `` mad season '' and `` more than you think you are '' . they released recently an album with new material and their biggest hits , called `` exile on mainstream '' . rob thomas , brian yale , and paul doucette were part of the band tabitha 's secret , which was based in orlando , florida , with fellow members jay stanley and john goff , before splitting to start a new band ( what was to become matchbox twenty ) . after recruiting adam gaynor from criteria recording studios in miami and kyle cook from the atlanta institute of music , the band named themselves `` matchbox 20 '' and won a recording contract with atlantic records .
