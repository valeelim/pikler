Never Say Never     never s-a-y never is the second album by american r & b singer brandy , released by atlantic records on june 8 , 1998 in europe and on june 9 , 1998 in north america . chiefly produced by rodney jerkins , helped to make jerkins ' timbaland-inspired production sound popular , which he later used for recordings by whitney houston , destiny 's child , and others . `` never say never '' earned brandy five grammy award nominations and became her biggest-selling album to date .
