Fairy     a fairy or færie ( old english spelling ) is a supposed magical being that flies in the air . they are usually depicted as small girls or women . some færies have certain jobs , such as the tooth fairy , who gives money or treats under the pillow of small children who have had a tooth fall out . a fairy tale is a story with a plot involving fairies . these stories are usually for children . fairies can also be found in folklore , for instance , in ireland and scotland , fairies are still held as creatures that were defeated by the human race thousands of years ago and now live in caverns in the world of faerie . these mystical creatures are believed to be made after a kind of angel . fairies come up in many fictional books such as `` '' peter pan '' and `` the spiderwick chronicles '' '' .
