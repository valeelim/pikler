President of the Republic of China     the president of the republic of china ( taiwan ) is the head of state and commander-in-chief of the democratic island country known officially as the republic of china ( taiwan ) . the republic of china ( taiwan ) government has controlled taiwan , penghu , kinmen , matsu and other islands after the rest of mainland china since 1949. the presidential office was created in 1948 under the 1947 constitution of the republic of china ( taiwan ) . outside of taiwan , the president of the republic of china ( taiwan ) is usually informally referred to as the `` president of taiwan '' ( ) . in the previous kuomintang-controlled administration , the title of `` president of the republic of china '' was used . the current democratic progressive party administration refers to the president as the `` president of the republic of china ( taiwan ) '' ( ) .
