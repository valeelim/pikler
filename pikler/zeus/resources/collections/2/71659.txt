Middle age     middle age is the period of life between youth and before old age . there is no fixed age , but most people think it begins when someone is about 40 years old , until the person is 60 years old . in most countries , people that think that by middle age , people should be mature , perhaps with a good , steady job and a family . due to promotion , middle age is sometimes a time when adults have greater wealth and influence than earlier in their careers , although they may have less disposable income due to having children . however , it is also common for some adults to suffer from mid-life crisis , when they are unsure about their life and sometimes become depressed because of it . women and men often have different experiences of middle age partly depending on whether they choose to work or care for their family full-time . middle-aged people begin to look older , with more wrinkles in their skin , and start to have grey hair . they become less fit and usually gain more weight . middle-aged females find it harder to become pregnant . if they do have a baby , there is a higher chance that it will have a genetic disease ( down syndrome , for example ) . females usually have their menopause in middle age , when they stop bleeding every month . when that happens , they can not have children anymore .
