Hallandale Beach, Florida    hallandale beach , florida hallandale beach is a city in broward county , florida , united states . the city is named after luther halland , a worker for henry flagler 's florida east coast railroad . the population was 37,282 at the 2000 census . sometimes the city is called the `` southernmost canadian city '' . this is because hallandale beach has been a popular vacation spot for years , and most of the tourists come from quebec and the northeast united states . throughout the years , a large number of these tourists eventually move to the area to live there .
