Marvin Hagler     marvin marvelous hagler ( born on may 23 , 1954 , in newark , new jersey ) is an american former boxer . he was the world middleweight champion from 1980 to 1987. hagler fought out of brockton , massachusetts . he won the championship from alan minter , in london , and successfully defended it eleven times before losing in a controversial fight with sugar ray leonard . hagler retired after that fight to live and work in italy .
