Princess Madeleine of Sweden     princess madeleine of sweden , duchess of hälsingland and gästrikland , ( born madeleine thérèse amélie joséphine on 10 june 1982 in drottningholm , sweden ) , is a swedish princess . she is the youngest child and second daughter of king carl xvi gustaf and queen silvia of sweden . madeleine is fourth in line to the swedish throne . she is behind crown princess victoria , princess estelle , and prince carl philip . she is before her niece , princess estelle . princess madeleine is married to british financier christopher o'neill . the couple has two children , a daughter , princess leonore , and a son , prince nicolas . princess madeleine was born on 10 june 1982 at 19.05 central european time in the drottningholm palace in drottningholm , sweden . her fourth name , `` josephine '' honours her ancestor josephine of leuchtenberg . forbes magazine ranked madeleine at 12th place on its `` 20 hottest young royals '' list in 2008. other websites . - the royal court of sweden : princess madeleine
