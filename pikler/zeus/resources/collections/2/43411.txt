Frankfurt U-Bahn     `` the frankfurt u-bahn '' is an underground railway network in the city of frankfurt , germany . it is run by the `` verkehrsgesellschaft frankfurt '' , short vgf . it has 9 lines and a network of 65 kilometers ( 40 miles ) and has exactly 86 stations , apart 27 underground and 59 above ground . two lines , the u2 and the u3 crosses the city border in oberursel and bad homburg .
