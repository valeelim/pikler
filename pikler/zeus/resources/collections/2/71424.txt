Harris Scarfe     harris scarfe is an australian department store . it sells items including clothing , pots and pans , cutlery . the store went bankrupt in 2001 , but has since bounced back . in 2004 , harris scarfe bought the allens group of seven department and five homewares stores . they continued to trade under the allens name in new south wales and the australian capital territory , until 2008 when they got merged with harris scarfe . harris scarfe has stores located in most australian states . in new south wales and the act they used to trade as allens . they were acquired by harris scarfe in 2004 , but continued to trade under the allens name . in late 2008 , the name of these stores was being changed from allens to harris scarfe . the main store for the company is located in rundle mall , adelaide . the store is five stories tall . the main store for tasmania is in hobart and it is three stories tall . harris scarfe sells items in two main groups , `` in style '' ( which includes all clothing ) and `` at home '' ( all other goods , such as homewares & linen ) . the adelaide store sells more goods than the other stores . many of the allens stores sell less goods than other stores .
