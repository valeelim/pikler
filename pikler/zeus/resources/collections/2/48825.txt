Blue box     a blue box is a device that someone could use to make free telephone calls . it worked by playing back the same sounds that the telephone company employees used to program their equipment . the most famous person to use a blue box was john draper , who went by the name captain crunch . draper taught steve wozniak how to build them . wozniak and steve jobs sold blue boxes to fellow students at the university of california , berkeley . one time , wozniak used a blue box to call the pope . other pages . - red box
