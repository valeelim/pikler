Lanark: A Life in Four Books    lanark : a life in four books lanark : a life in four books was the first novel of scottish writer alasdair gray , and is still his best known . the book was written over a period of almost thirty years and was published in 1981. it has real and fantasy descriptions of gray 's home city of glasgow . the book has won the saltire society book of the year and david niven awards , and has become a cult classic . in 2006 , `` the guardian '' said `` lanark '' was `` one of the landmarks of 20th-century fiction .
