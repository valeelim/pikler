Pallava dynasty     the pallavas dynasty rose in tamil nadu ( south india ) during the seventh century under mahendravarman i and his son mamalla narasimhavarman i. the pallavas were not a recognised political power before the second century . after the fall of the `` satavahanas '' , they began to get control over parts of andhra and the tamil country . the pallavas were at their finest during the reigns of narasimhavarman i and pallavamalla nandivarman ii .
