Bora Bora     bora bora is a small island in the leeward group of the society islands of french polynesia , an overseas territory of france in the pacific ocean . vaitape is the largest city on the island with 4,000 people . there is an airport on an islet where residents can fly to other islands in french polynesia . bora bora has one main road which runs around the island . other websites . - bora bora pictures bora bora photos - bora bora entry on worldatlas.com - bora bora islands pictures pictures of the islands
