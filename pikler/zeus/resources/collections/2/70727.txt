1992 Winter Olympics     the 1992 winter olympics , officially known as the xvi olympic winter games , were held in 1992 in albertville , france . other possible host cities were anchorage , usa ; berchtesgaden , germany ; cortina d'ampezzo , italy ; lillehammer , norway ; falun , sweden ; and sofia , bulgaria . these were the last winter games to be held in the same year as the summer games . they were also the first games where the winter paralympics and the winter olympics were held at the same site .
