Aeneid     the aeneid ( latin : `` aeneis '' ) is an epic poem written by virgil between 29 and 19 bc . it tells the story of `` pious aeneas '' . aeneas is a trojan warrior who had to leave his city because the greeks captured troy after a long war . it starts with aeneas carrying his father anchises out of the burning troy . it ends with aeneas landing on the shores of italy , fighting in the region of rome . it can be read as indirect propaganda in favour of augustus , the roman emperor of the day . the story is written in hexameters , a type of verse .
