World Wide Web Consortium     the world wide web consortium ( w3c ) is an organization created by sir tim berners-lee , in order to continue the development of the web , and its languages . tim berners-lee also wants to make standards for the languages , and to improve them . as of 2 april 2010 , the world wide web consortium ( w3c ) has 328 members . as of 29 march 2012 , the world wide web consortium ( w3c ) has 351 members . as of 9 june 2014 , the world wide web consortium ( w3c ) has 384 members . other websites . - w3c - homepage
