Caudipteryx     caudipteryx was a small feathered dinosaur . it was related to `` oviraptor '' but looked more like `` archaeopteryx '' . it was a relative of `` protarchaeopteryx '' . it was found in china . it was a small insectivore , with long thin legs , less than a metre long . it had a fan of tail feathers and a fringe of feathers along the trailing edge of each arm . they are very like bird feathers , with a central stalk , and vanes on either side . its arms were too short for it to fly .
