Language revival     language revival is when people try to make a language that is not spoken , or is spoken very little , spoken more often again . language death is what happens when a language is not used by the people who spoke it before . language revival wants to save a language that is dead or endangered . the hebrew language was once a language that was not used , but because the language was revived , people now speak and use it again . many languages today are now trying to be revived .
