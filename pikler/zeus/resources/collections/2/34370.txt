Elektra     elektra ( or electra ) is the name of several persons in greek mythology . - elektra of mycenae , daughter of agamemnon and klytaimnestra - elektra , an oceanid - elektra , one of the pleiades elektra of mycenae was the daughter of king agamemnon of mycenae and klytaimnestra . she had a brother , orestes , and two sisters , iphigeneia and chrysothemis . after the trojan war , agamemnon was killed by klytaimnestra and her lover aegisthos because agamemnon sacrificed iphigeneia for good winds to sail . elektra helped orestes to kill their mother klytaimnestra and her lover , to avenge their father 's death .
