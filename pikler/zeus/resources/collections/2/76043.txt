Lockheed P-38 Lightning     the p-38 lightning is a world war ii fighter aircraft made by lockheed . it was mainly used by the united states air force . it first flew in 1939. it was different from other fighters because it had two engines instead of one , and was twice the size of other fighters . the plane fought in the pacific and in europe . it could carry 4 machine guns and a cannon . by 1944 , it was replaced by the faster p-51 mustang in europe . the p-38 was the only american fighter being built at the beginning and the end of the war . the germans called it the `` fork tailed devil .
