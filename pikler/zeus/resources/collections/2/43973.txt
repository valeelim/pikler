Runestone     a runestone or rune stone is a large stone , with runes inscribed on it , dating from the early middle ages . currently , about 6000 such stones are known to exist in scandinavia . about half of them date from the tenth and eleventh century , and were found in sweden . most of them are like grave stones . they announce the life and death of a local person . about 10 percent of the known stones tell about the travels and deaths of persons abroad . some of these texts are similar to what scandinavians who travelled to byzantium have written in latin .
