Rhythm and blues     rhythm and blues ( also known as r & b or rnb ) is a popular music genre combining jazz , gospel , and blues influences , first performed by african american artists . it is now performed worldwide by people of many cultures and ethnic groups . during the 1980s , james brown and sly & the family stone had used parts of psychedelic rock and other styles in their music . funk became a big part of disco music . in the early 1980s , funk and soul had become sultry and more sexual with the work of prince and others . the modern style of `` contemporary r & b '' came to be a major part of american popular music . r & b today defines a style of african-american music . it combines elements of soul music , funk music , pop music , and ( after 1986 ) hip hop in what is now called `` contemporary r & b '' . it is sometimes called `` urban contemporary '' or `` urban pop '' . by the 2000s , the only big difference between a record being a hip hop record or an r & b record is whether its vocals are rapped or sung . r & b started to focus more on solo artists than groups . by 2005 , the most famous r & b artists include usher , beyonc√© ( formerly of destiny 's child ) , ashanti , and mariah carey .
