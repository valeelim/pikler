Tracey Ullman     tracey ullman ( born 30 december 1959 ) is a british-american comedian , actress , singer , dancer , screenwriter and author . she is most famous for being the host of her variety television show , `` the tracey ullman show '' . ullman was born in buckinghamshire . she emigrated to the u.s. and ended up having her own network television series , `` the tracey ullman show '' , from which `` the simpsons '' was spun off in 1989. she was the voice of little lulu for a while .
