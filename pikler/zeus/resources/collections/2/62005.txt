Potomac River     the potomac river is a river in the eastern united states . it is 665 kilometers long . it forms part of the borders between maryland , west virginia , washington , d.c. , and virginia . the potomac flows to the chesapeake bay . it has two sources . the north branch starts at fairfax stone in west virginia , and the south branch starts in highland county , virginia . the north branch and the south branch meet in hampshire county , west virginia , and make the potomac river .
