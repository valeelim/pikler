Yellow River     the yellow river ( huáng hé 黄河 ) is the second longest river in china ( after yangtze river ) and the sixth longest in the world . the river is 5464 km long and it drains at the bohai sea , a gulf of the yellow sea . the river is often called the `` mother river of china '' and `` the cradle of the chinese civilization '' in china . other websites . - yellow river delta information at lsu - listen to the `` yellow river ballade '' from the `` yellow river cantata '' - `` a troubled river mirrors china ’ s path to modernity '' `` new york times '' november 19 , 2006 - illustrations of guarding the yellow river is an old book that shows how china protected the cities around the river from flooding . - illustrated work on the storage and drainage activities at the lakes and rivers of the yellow river and the grand canal
