MiG Alley     mig alley was a place during the korean war that had a lot of north korean mig-15 fagots in the area . it was over north west north korea , near china . f-86 sabres mainly fought the migs . some of the pilots were russian fighter pilots from world war ii and were very well trained . they were called `` honchos '' ( japanese for `` big shot '' ) . the top kills were scored by soviet aces , so it was said that the soviets won . related pages . - mikoyan
