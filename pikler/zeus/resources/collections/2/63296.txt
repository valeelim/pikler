Fort Worth, Texas    fort worth , texas fort worth is the fifth largest city in texas . it is the 16th largest city in the united states . it has gotten so big that it has overlapped the next biggest city next to it , dallas , texas . because of this , the area is often referred to as dallas/ft . worth , or dfw . fort worth is the county seat of tarrant county . texas motor speedway is also in fort worth . there are a few interesting landmarks in fort worth . one is sundance square , a popular place to enjoy in downtown . another is west 7th , an entertainment destination . at last , there is the fort worth stockyards , where rodeos are held .
