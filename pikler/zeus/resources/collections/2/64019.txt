Dress     a dress is one of the basic styles of female clothing . it is a garment of a skirt ( bottom half ) with an attached bodice ( top half ) . the colouring and style of the dress gives the effect of a one-piece garment . a dress is also known as a frock or a gown . in europe and the americas , dresses are worn by females of all ages as an alternative to a separate skirt and blouse or trousers . there are many types of dresses . a dress is usually worn at formal occasions , such as a wedding or a dance . it is also worn in the summer to keep cool . younger females tend to prefer shorter skirts than older females . type of dress . - afternoon dress - cocktail dress - evening dress other websites . - stylopedia -- an online dictionary of fashion details - apparelseach glossary of textile and apparel terms - an international dress size converter
