Cushion     a cushion is a soft bag of material , filled with wool , hair , feathers , or even paper in small pieces . it may be used for sitting on to make a chair or couch more comfortable . cushions can be used for body support . cushions and rugs can be used outside , to make hard ground softer . cushions are often for a decorative purpose and can create more character and appeal to a room . cushions can come in many different shapes and sizes . however , the most common shape of a cushion is a square or rectangle , like a pillow .
