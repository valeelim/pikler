Natural person     in jurisprudence , a natural person is a real human being in contrast to an artificial person , meaning an organization that the law treats for some purposes as if it were a person distinct from its members or owner . for example , the right to vote is given to natural persons only . that is the case for most of the human rights . therefore obviously a corporation can not hold public office , but it can file a lawsuit . although science fiction has long fantasized about the possibility of sentient robots ( e.g . androids ) living among humans ( such as robin williams `` bicentennial man '' ) , no court has yet dealt with the question of whether such a being would be considered a `` natural person '' . other websites . - natural person
