Wilhelm Hauff     wilhelm hauff ( november 29 , 1802 - november 18 , 1827 ) was a german poet and novelist . some of his works are very popular in german speaking countries to this day , such as der kleine muck , kalif storch and die geschichte von dem gespensterschiff — all set in the orient and der zwerg nase , das kalte herz and das wirtshaus im spessart , the three are set in germany . other websites . - collection of links with commentary ( german ) - sound samples to download - many fairy tales ( märchen ) available ( german ) - poems - steh ich in finstrer mitternacht - reiters morgenlied - links to hauff 's work by projekt gutenberg-de : - overview of hauff with many of his texts online ( german )
