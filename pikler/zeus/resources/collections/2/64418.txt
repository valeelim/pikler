Marcia Cross     marcia anne cross ( born march 25 , 1962 in marlborough , massachusetts ) is an emmy and golden globe award-nominated american actress . she graduated from juilliard and earned a master 's degree in psychology at antioch university in los angeles . in 2005 , she was nominated for her first emmy for her work as bree hodge , the `` perfect '' housewife on `` desperate housewives '' . in december 2006 , cross earned her third consecutive nomination for the golden globe for best actress in a comedy series with the series .
