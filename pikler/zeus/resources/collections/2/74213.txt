Tadeusz Kościuszko     andrzej tadeusz bonawentura kościuszko ( ; 1746 – 1817 ) was a polish , lithuanian and belarussian national hero , general and a leader of the kościuszko uprising ( which bears his name ) against the russian empire.tadeusz was born in february 1746 on the polish-lithuanian border . he fought in the american revolutionary war as a colonel in the continental army on the side of washington . because of his service he was brevetted by the continental congress to the rank of brigadier general in 1783 , and became a naturalized citizen of the united states that same year . he died in solothurn , switzerland on october 15,1817 due to typhoid fever .
