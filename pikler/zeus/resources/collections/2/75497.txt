Combat sport     a combat sport ( also known as a combative sport ) is a competitive contact sport where two combatants fight against each other using certain rules of contact , with the aim of simulating parts of real hand to hand combat . boxing , kickboxing , wrestling , mixed martial arts and fencing are examples of combat sports . the techniques used can be categorized into three domains : striking , grappling , and weapon usage . some rule-sets specialize in one area , while others allow overlap .
