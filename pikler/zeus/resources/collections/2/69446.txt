Alexios I Komnenos     alexios i komnenos or alexius i comnenus ( ; ; 1048 – august 15 , 1118 ) , byzantine emperor ( 1081–1118 ) , was the son of john komnenos and anna dalassena . he was the nephew of isaac i komnenos ( emperor 1057–1059 ) . the military , financial and territorial recovery of the byzantine empire known as komnenian restoration began in his time .
