Knight (chess)    knight ( chess ) a knight is a piece in the game of chess . each player starts the game with two knights . it moves in an l pattern , two squares in one direction then one square in another . it is the only piece in chess to be able to jump over other pieces . it is also the only piece that can be in position to attack a king , queen , bishop , or rook without being attacked by that piece . because of this , the knight plays forks well in a game of chess . 'knight ' is shortened to kt or n when recording games . in printed game scores it is represented by a figurine .
