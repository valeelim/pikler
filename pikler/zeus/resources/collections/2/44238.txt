Bahá'u'lláh     bahá'u'lláh , also spelled bahaullah , which means `` glory of god '' , was a persian nobleman who founded the religion known as the bahá'í faith . he was born in the city of tehran , in persia , in 1817. his followers consider him a messenger of god . other websites . - the life of bahá'u'lláh – a photographic narrative
