Broad Green     broad green is a small area with a lot of houses at the centre of west croydon in the london borough of croydon , england . the area is mostly filled with a triangular green space which includes shops and large houses . the green was built at the end of the 19th century . most of west croydon is in an electoral ward called `` broad green '' , hence broad green library is some distance from the traditional broad green area . parts of west thornton and selhurst wards are also in west croydon .
