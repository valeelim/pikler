Qiemo     qiemo , also called cherchen , is an inland city in xinjiang , china . about 60,000 people live in the city . it is a river town on the edge of the taklamakan desert . the area has been settled for a long time . a cemetery at least 3,500 years old is near the town , as well as bronze age rock carvings . the town was along the trade route to tibet . marco polo is supposed to have passed through the town in 1273 .
