Gefjun     gefjun ( or gefjon , gefion ) is one of the æsir in norse mythology . this name could be from old norse `` geð fiá '' meaning `` chaste '' is one of the asynjur in norse mythology . she appears only a few times in surviving sources . medieval sources talk of her mainly as a goddess of chastity . however , modern scholarship suggests that she may originally have been a fertility goddess connected with ritual plowing , and even that she was originally the same fertility goddess as freyja . it has also been suggested that she is the origin of grendel 's mother who appears in the epic poem `` beowulf '' .
