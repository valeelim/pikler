Uhlan     uhlan is the name for a type of polish soldier of light cavalry , armed with lance ( a kind of spear ) . the respect for such polish troops caused that in the beginning of the 19th century great part of european armies created similar military units . in most european armies , the units of that kind took the name of lancers . in the russian , austrian , and german armies , the units of polish lancers had kept the original name of uhlans .
