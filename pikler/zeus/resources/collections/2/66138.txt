Schizanthus     schizanthus ( butterfly flower ) is a genus of flowering plants in the nightshade family `` solanaceae '' . the genus is made up of twelve species : `` schizanthus alpestris '' , `` schizanthus candidus '' , `` schizanthus grahamii '' , `` schizanthus hookerii '' , `` schizanthus integrifolius '' , `` schizanthus lacteus '' , `` schizanthus laetus '' , `` schizanthus litoralis '' , `` schizanthus parvulus '' , `` schizanthus pinnatus '' , `` schizanthus porrigens '' and `` schizanthus tricolor '' .
