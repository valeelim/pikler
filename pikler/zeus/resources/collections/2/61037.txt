Dough     dough is a paste made out of flour by mixing it with a small amount of water . dough can be used to make bread , pastries , cookies , muffins , and other foods . in many parts of central india , people use the quick method of making an instant roasted dough ball or baati . flat , unleavened breads known as roti , lavash , yufka , matzo , and tortilla are used in many parts of the world today . leavened dough , made from dry , ground grains mixed with water and yeast , is used all over the world . it includes all kinds of breads made from wheat , maize , rice and other grains or similar crops used today in the world . fried dough foods are common in many cultures . in the united states , `` elephant ears '' is a common name for fried dough . in slang , dough is money .
