Gargoyle     a gargoyle is a grotesque statue that is attached to a building . it is carved out of stone , usually granite . it is used to get water away from the building when it rains . gargoyles are often made to look like animals or people . the collected water comes out of their mouths . most gargoyles were made a long time ago . people used to believe that they scared away evil spirits . many cathedrals have gargoyles . new buildings use rain gutters instead of gargoyles to move water . there are not many gargoyles in america . gargoyles are often confused with grotesques . they are similar , but only gargoyles get rid of rainwater through their mouths what 's in the mouths i do n't know .
