Holguín Province     holguín is one of the provinces of cuba , the second most populous after ciudad de la habana . it lies in the southeast of the country . its major cities include holguín ( the capital ) , banes , antilla , mayarí , and moa . the province has a population of slightly over one million people . its territory extends over more than 9300 km² , 25 percent of which are covered by forest . christopher columbus landed in what is believed to have been today 's holguín province on october 27 , 1492. he declared that it was `` the most beautiful country human eyes had ever seen '' . the holguín province was established in 1978 , when it was split from the `` oriente '' region . general ( 1839-1898 ) , 19th century patriot . wars of independence from spain . commander in chief fidel castro ( 1926 ) , lawyer , revolutionary , ex-president of cuba . army general raúl castro ( 1931 ) , minister of the revolutionary army forces , vice-president and president of cuba . like much of cuba , holguín 's economy is based around sugarcane , though mining , corn and coffee are also large earners for the province .
