Kars Province     kars is a province of turkey . it is in the northeastern part of the country . it shares part of its border with the republic of armenia . from 1878 until 1917 most of the present-day turkish province of kars was part of the russian oblast of kars . in 1919 and 1920 the province came under the control of the democratic republic of armenia as the vanand province ( with the city of kars as its capital ) . its territory was ceded to turkey by the soviet union in the treaty of kars . kars province is divided into 8 districts ( `` ilçe '' ) , each named after the administrative center of the district : - akyaka - arpaçay - digor - kağızman - kars - sarıkamış - selim - susuz there are 383 villages in kars . other websites . - pictures of the capital of this province and of nearby ani
