Staffordshire     staffordshire ( abbreviated staffs ) is a county in the west midlands region of england . the county town is stafford . part of the national forest lies within its borders . it adjoins the counties of cheshire , derbyshire , leicestershire , warwickshire , west midlands , worcestershire and shropshire . the largest city in ceremonial staffordshire is stoke-on-trent . other websites . - staffordshire county council - staffordshire past track - historical archive about the county - the story of a staffordshire home guard battalion , 1940-1944 - staffordshire photographs - staffordshire tourism website
