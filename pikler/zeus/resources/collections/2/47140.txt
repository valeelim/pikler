Raw material     raw materials are materials that have not been processed . they are in the form in which they are found in nature without any thing done to them . raw materials are made into other things . raw materials usually come from : - a plant or tree - an animal - a mine examples of raw materials : - grains such as wheat and rice - vegetables such as carrots and onions - meat such as beef and chicken - wood from a tree - honey from a bee 's nest - minerals or metal from a mine - crude oil things that are not raw materials because they have been processed : - bread and pasta - toothpaste and soap - sausages - furniture such as chairs and tables - machines such as cars - jewelry - clothing and shoes - gasoline - oil that goes in cars
