Ethane     ethane is an organic chemical compound with chemical formula ch . at standard temperature and pressure , ethane is a colourless , odorless gas . it is isolated from natural gas , and as a byproduct of petroleum refining . its main use is in the chemical industry , to make ethylene by steam cracking . at room temperature , the gas is flammable , and it explodes when mixed with air . when ethane is liquid , touching it causes a very serious frostbite .
