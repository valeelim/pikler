Monocotyledon     monocotyledons are a class of flowering plants ( angiosperms ) , whose embryo ( seed ) store only one cotyledon . the apg ii system recognises a clade called `` monocots '' but does not assign it to a taxonomic rank . you can recognize a monocot by its leaves : they have long parallel veins running down the leaf . in contrast , dicot plant leaves have a complex netted vein pattern on their leaves . monocots usually have a fibrous root system . other websites . - apg ii - petrosaviales
