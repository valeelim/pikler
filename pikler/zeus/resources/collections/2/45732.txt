Maloideae     the maloideae ( which is a latin plural form ) is a traditional name for a subfamily of plants in the rose family ( rosaceae ) . these plants are shrubs and small trees . the most well known maloideae are apples and pears . recently it has been shown that the maloideae are a part of the traditional subfamily amygdaloideae , and most botanists no longer use the name maloideae .
