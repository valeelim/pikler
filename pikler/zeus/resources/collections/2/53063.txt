Academy Award for Best Picture (1940s)    academy award for best picture ( 1940s ) the academy award for best picture is one of the academy awards . also called oscars , the academy awards are given to people working in the movie industry by the american academy of motion picture arts and sciences ( ampas ) . in the list below , the winner of the award for each year from 1940 to 1949 is shown first , followed by the other nominees . - 1940 `` rebecca '' – selznick , united artists – david o. selznick - `` all this and heaven too '' – warner bros. – jack l. warner , hal b. wallis , with david lewis - `` foreign correspondent '' – wanger , united artists – walter wanger - `` the grapes of wrath '' – 20th century-fox – darryl f. zanuck with nunnally johnson - `` the great dictator '' – chaplin , united artists – charles chaplin - `` kitty foyle '' – rko radio – david hempstead - `` the letter '' – warner bros. – hal b. wallis - `` the long voyage home '' – argosy wanger , united artists – john ford - `` our town '' – lesser , united artists – sol lesser - `` the philadelphia story '' – metro-goldwyn-mayer – joseph l. mankiewicz
