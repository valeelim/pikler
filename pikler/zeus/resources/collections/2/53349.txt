Maranhão     maranhão is one of the states of brazil . it is in the northeastern region of the country . to the north is the coast of the atlantic ocean . maranhão shares borders with piauí , tocantins and pará . major cities in the state besides the state capital são luís are imperatriz , timon , and caxias . the alcantara spaceport is in the north of the state , not far from são luís .
