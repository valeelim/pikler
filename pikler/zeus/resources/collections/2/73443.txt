Hugo Alvar Henrik Aalto     hugo alvar henrik aalto ( february 3 , 1898 – may 11 , 1976 ) was a finnish architect who made many important contributions to architecture . sometimes called the `` father of modernism '' . alvar aalto was born in kuortane , finland . he studied architecture at the technical university of helsinki from 1916 to 1921. he returned to jyväskylä , where he opened his first architectural office in 1923. alvar aalto died in may 11 , 1976 , in helsinki . other websites . - alvar aalto foundation custodian of aalto 's architectural drawings and writings . - aalto bibliography - from the official site - alvar aalto - design dictionary illustrated article about alvar aalto - alvar aalto 's savoy vase ( 1936 )
