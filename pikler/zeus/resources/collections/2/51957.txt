Armenian Kingdom of Cilicia     the armenian kingdom of cilicia was a state formed in the middle ages by armenian refugees fleeing the seljuk invasion of armenia . it was near the mediterranean sea in what is today southern turkey . the country was independent from around 1078 to 1375. the armenians were the most important of the crusaders ' allies , from the local troops recruited in the crusader states . many armenian mercenaries became available in the early 12th century . thoros ii was a powerful armenian prince who offered the transfer of 30,000 armenian warriors with their families to the kingdom of jerusalem . the king and barons were interested . related pages . - kingdom of armenia - ancient armenia other websites . - history of armenia - armenian history , chapter 6 : armenian kingdom in cilicia
