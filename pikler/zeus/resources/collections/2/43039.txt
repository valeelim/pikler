Hafnium     hafnium is a chemical element . it has the chemical symbol hf . it has the atomic number 72. it is a metal . it is silver gray . in chemistry it is placed in a group of metal elements named the transition metals . the chemistry of hafnium is similar to zirconium . hafnium is found in zirconium minerals . hafnium is used in tungsten alloys in filaments and electrodes . it is also used as a neutron absorber in control rods in nuclear power plants . related pages . - periodic table - list of common elements
