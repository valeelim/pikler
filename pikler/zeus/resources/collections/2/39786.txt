Mily Balakirev     mily alexanyevich balakirev ( born nizhny-novgorod 2 january 1837 ; died st petersburg 29 may 1910 ) was a russian composer . his music sounds very russian , and he was a very important influence on other russian composers such as modest mussorgsky , nikolai rimsky-korsakov , alexander borodin and césar cui . these five composers were known as the “ mighty handful ” . balakirev mainly wrote music for orchestra , choir and piano and solo songs .
