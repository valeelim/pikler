Elena V. Pitjeva     elena vladimirovna pitjeva is a russian theoretical physicist at the institute of applied astronomy , russian academy of sciences , st. petersburg . she is noted for her work on ephemerides and is currently head of the laboratory of ephemeris astronomy at the institute of applied astronomy . pitjeva is a member of the international astronomical union commission 4 : ephemerides . pitjeva earned her undergraduate degree from the faculty of mathematics and mechanics at a. a. zhdanov leningrad state university in 1972 in the specialty of astronomy . she earned an m.a . in theoretical physics in 1994 and a ph.d. in theoretical physics in 2005. she was a student of professor g.a . krasinski . other websites . - laboratory of ephemeris astronomy , institute of applied astronomy
