Miss Universe     miss universe is an international beauty contest . it was started in 1952 and today has over 190 countries taking part . about one billion people watch it on television every year . the pageant is owned by wme and img . until 2015 , it was owned by nbc and donald trump . in 2015 , trump started his campaign for president of the united states and said he wanted to stop illegal immigrants from mexico from coming into the us . nbc then stopped working with him , and so trump bought miss universe out , and sold it to its current owners a few days later . the most recent person to receive the miss universe title is iris mittenaere of france , who did so on january 30 , 2017 in manila , philippines . other websites . - miss universe official homepage
