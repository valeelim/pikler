Torgau     torgau is a town on the banks of the elbe in northwestern saxony , germany . the town is well known as the place where during the second world war , united states army forces met with forces of the soviet union during the invasion of germany on april 25 , 1945. this marked the beginning of the line of contact between soviet and american forces , but not the occcupation zones . in fact the area surrounding torgau at first occupied by u.s. forces was later , in july 1945 , given over to soviet forces . other websites . - http : //www.torgau.de - http : //www.torgau-oschatz.info
