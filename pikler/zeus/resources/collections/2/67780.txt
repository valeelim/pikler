Alliance     an alliance is an agreement between two or more parties . it is made to benefit both parties . the word `` alliance '' is used mainly in war . when two or more countries make an alliance , that means they promise to help each other during war . countries that have an alliance are called `` allies '' . when talking about countries that have an alliance , people say they are `` allied '' . there can be only two countries in an alliance , or there can be more than two countries in an alliance . an example of an alliance with more than two countries is the central powers in world war i. this alliance was made up of germany , austria-hungary , the ottoman empire , and bulgaria .
