Belz Beis HaMedrash HaGadol     the belz beis hamedrash hagadol ( in yiddish : בעלזא בית המדרש הגדול - `` the belz grand synagogue ) is the largest synagogue in jerusalem . it was built by the belz hasidic jews , with help from their supporters and admirers around the world . in the 1980s , rabbi yissachar dov rokeach ( ii ) of belz made plans for the huge synagogue to be built in the kiryat belz part of jerusalem .
