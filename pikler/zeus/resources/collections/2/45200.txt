Fructose     fructose is a simple sugar ( monosaccharide ) found in many foods . it is also one of the three most important blood sugars , the other two being glucose and galactose . honey ; tree fruits ; berries ; melons ; and some root vegetables , such as beets , sweet potatoes , parsnips and onions , contain fructose , usually with sucrose and glucose . other websites . - fructose - carbohydrate metabolism - fructose information center
