LISP     lisp ( used to be called lisp ) is a programming language . it is among the oldest programming languages that are still used today . only fortran is one year older . lisp was designed by john mccarthy in 1958. the best-known versions of lisp are common lisp , scheme and clojure . many concepts that are used in modern programming languages were first created in lisp . linked lists are a very important data structure in lisp . the basic concepts behind lisp are easy to learn . logo is another version of lisp that was made for children . logo can help young children develop skills and become efficient within the programming language .
