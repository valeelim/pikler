Vercingetorix     vercingetorix ( in gaulish ) born 72 bc , died 46 bc ) was a chieftain of the gallic tribe of the arverni . he led the great gallic war against roman imperialism in 52 bc . his name in gaulish means `` over-king of the marching men '' ; the `` marching men '' would now be called `` infantry '' . vercingetorix was probably one of the first to unite some tribes of gaul against a common enemy . this was the last major uprising of the peoples of gaul against the roman invaders .
