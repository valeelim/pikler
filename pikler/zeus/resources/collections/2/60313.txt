Micheline Calmy-Rey     micheline calmy-rey ( born 8 july 1945 ) is a swiss politician . she was switzerland 's foreign minister as head of the federal department of foreign affairs . she was also a member of the swiss federal council from 2003. she was president of the confederation in 2007 and again in 2011. she resigned her office as member of the federal council on 31 december 2011. other websites . - official website |-
