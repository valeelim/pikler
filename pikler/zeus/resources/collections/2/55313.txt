Drinking straw     a drinking straw ( or `` straw '' ) is a utensil used to consume cold drinks such as milk shakes , smoothies , cola , and fruit juice . drinking straws are usually made from plastic that is formed into a tube . to use a drinking straw , a person places the straw in a glass that contains a liquid and then sucks the straw with their mouth . straws are mainly plastic , but some straws are paper or cardboard to protect the environment .
