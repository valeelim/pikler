Fandom     fandom ( from the noun `` fan '' and the affix `` -dom '' , as in `` kingdom '' , `` dukedom '' , etc . ) or fanbase is a name used for a group of people ( called `` fans '' ) who are interested in something . a fandom can be about any interest or activity . fandoms can be for example about hobbies , genres or fashions , book or movie series , or even individual celebrities . related pages . - fan fiction - fictional universe
