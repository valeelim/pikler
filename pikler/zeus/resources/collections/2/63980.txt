Denali     denali , also formerly known as mount mckinley , is the highest mountain peak in both the united states and north america . it is exactly 20,310 feet ( 6,190.5 m ) high . it is in the highest part of the alaska range . denali means `` the high one '' in the athabaskan language . denali is in the middle of the alaska range . it is about 130 miles ( 209 km ) north-northwest of anchorage and 155 miles ( 249 km ) southwest of fairbanks . the summit is about 35 miles ( 56 km ) from the nearest major road , the george parks highway .
