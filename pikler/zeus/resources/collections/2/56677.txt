Concept album     a concept album is a kind of album in which all the songs are about the same thing . most of the time , they tell a story . examples of concept albums are : - `` american idiot '' by green day - all of coheed and cambria 's studio albums - `` thick as a brick '' by jethro tull - most of pink floyd 's albums - `` clockwork angels '' by rush sources . - new york post article about american idiot
