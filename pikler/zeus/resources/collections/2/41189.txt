María Esther de Capovilla     maría esther de capovilla ( september 14 , 1889 – august 27 , 2006 ) was an ecuadorian supercentenarian . she was the world 's oldest living person between may 29 , 2004 , aged 114 years 258 days , and her death from pneumonia on august 27 , 2006 , aged 116 years 347 days . at the time of her death she was verified as the sixth oldest person on record , and later the fifth oldest when the case of shigechiyo izumi was discounted . she remained the world 's fifth oldest verified person until misao okawa surpassed her age in february 2015. following capovilla 's death , elizabeth bolden became the world 's oldest person .
