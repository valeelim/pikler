Yuri Andropov     yuri vladimirovich andropov ( 1914-1984 ) was a soviet politician . he was born in nagutskoye , russian sfsr . andropov was director of the kgb for an unusually long time : 19671982 ( jan-may ) . he was general secretary of the cpsu from november 12 , 1982 until his death fifteen months later . he had diabetes mellitus and died of renal failure in moscow , russian sfsr .
