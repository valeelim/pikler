Yu Suzuki     both of suzuki 's parents were school teachers . when suzuki was growing up , he wanted to be a dentist . but he could not pass the test , so he decided to study computers instead . in 1983 he started working at sega . in 1993 he wrote a famous game called virtua fighter . it was so famous , that it was added to the smithsonian museum in washington dc , as an example of an important game .
