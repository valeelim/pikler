Vought F-8 Crusader     the f-8 crusader was an american fighter plane made by vought . it first flew in 1955 , and went into the navy and marine corps in 1957. it was the only fighter plane in the vietnam war to have both guns and missiles on it . it was slowly replaced by the f-4 phantom ii and was retired in 1999. there was also a different type of it called the rf-8 crusader , meant for spying . it also took the pictures of soviet missiles during the cuban missile crisis . other pages . - a-7 corsair ii
