Gaul     gaul ( ) was the name given in ancient times to the area of western europe that included northern italy , france , belgium , western switzerland and the parts of the netherlands and germany on the west bank of the rhine river . many celtic tribes lived in the region and were known by the romans simply as `` gauls '' . in english , the word `` gaul '' ( ) may also mean a celtic inhabitant of that region , although the expression may be used for all ancient speakers of the gaulish language as well . in this way , `` gaul '' and `` celt '' are sometimes used in the same sense . gauls under brennus attacked rome with the battle of the allia ( 390 bc ) . other websites . - the gallic wars
