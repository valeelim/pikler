Jonas Björkman     jonas lars björkman ( born march 23 , 1972 , alvesta , sweden ) is a swedish professional male tennis player and former world no . 4 in singles and world no . 1 in doubles . he is as of the 2006 season the oldest player in the atp top 100 ( singles ) . he has also played in numerous wimbledon tournaments . other websites . - jonasbjorkman.com - official website
