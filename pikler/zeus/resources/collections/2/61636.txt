Armen Grigoryan     armen grigoryan ( ) is the front man ( and main songwriter ) of `` crematorium '' ( krematorij ) russian rock-band . grigoryan was born on november 24 , 1960 in moscow . his parents were armenian . in 1983 , he formed `` crematorium '' which gained a reputation throughout the former soviet union and began to perform at concerts all over the country . armen grigoryan helped the development of russian rock music . he composes lyrics that frequently deal with the themes of life and death in various religious contexts . discography ( solo ) . - armen grigoryan and `` 3 ' angel '' - `` kitayskij tank '' ( chinese tank ) ( 2006 ) other websites . - official site of crematorium - 3 ' angel
