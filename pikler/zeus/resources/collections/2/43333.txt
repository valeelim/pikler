Worms, Germany    worms , germany worms ( pronounced /vo…êms/ ) is a city in southwestern germany in the federal state of rhineland-palatinate ( rheinland-pfalz ) . it lies on the river rhine . worms is famous for its local liebfraumilch wine . it is an industrial center , producing chemicals and metal goods . at the end of 2004 , it had 85,829 inhabitants . along with cologne and trier , worms is one the oldest cities in germany . but , there is a disagreement which of the three is actually the oldest . the town is mentioned in the `` nibelungenlied '' , an old germanic saga . the town was also very important because of martin luther who defended his case against the emperor charles v in worms . the cathedral of saint peter is a very large church in the town . it is one of only three romanesque cathedrals in germany . the other two are in mainz and speyer . the worms cathedral is one of the most important romanesque buildings in germany . other websites . - the official website of the town of worms `` ( in english ) '' - `` nibelungenmuseum '' website `` ( in english ) ''
