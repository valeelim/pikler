Ali Shariati     ali shariati mazinani ( 1933-1977 ) ( persian : علی شريعتی مزینانی ) or shariati was an iranian muslim philosopher , sociologist , poet and writer . shariati was born in 1933 in mazinan , iran . he studied sociology in the university of paris . he returned to iran in 1964. when he returned to iran , the shah regime arrested and imprisoned him for political reasons . he was released in 1965. shariati began teaching at the university of mashhad . he began lecturing at the hosseiniye ershad institute in 1967. the shah regime arrested him again . when he was freed in 1975 , he was not allowed to teach or publish anything . shariati decided to leave the country for england . three weeks later he died in southampton under suspicious circumstances . he was buried in damascus , syria . shariati 's most important books . 1. `` hajj ( the pilgrimage ) '' 1 . `` where shall we begin ?
