Langnau im Emmental     langnau im emmental ( 'langnau in the emmental ' ) is a small village in the emmental , in the canton of bern . it is by the river ilfis between the cities of bern and luzern . it has a population of about 8800 people . most of the people of the village work in the building trades . langnau has an ice hockey club , called the scl tigers . they have their home stadium in langnau .
