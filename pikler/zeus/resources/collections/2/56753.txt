Tom Brokaw     thomas john brokaw ( born february 6 , 1940 in webster , south dakota ) is a popular american television journalist and is the former anchorman ( host ) of `` nbc nightly news with tom brokaw '' . his last appearance as anchorman was on december 1 , 2004. in the later part of hosting `` nbc nightly news '' , the program became the most-watched news program in the united states . brokaw has received many awards and honors . brokaw was diagnosed with multiple myeloma in august 2013 .
