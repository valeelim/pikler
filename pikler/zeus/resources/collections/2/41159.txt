Xi Shun     xi shun ( bao xishun ) ( china , born in 1951 ) is in the guinness book of world records as the world 's tallest living man . he is 2.36 meters ( 7 ft 9 in ) tall . the last time he was measured was on january 15 of 2005. on august 7 , 2007 leonid stadnyk was measuring and was found to be taller than him . in september of 2005 he made his first trip outside of china to london to visit guinness world records . xi shun served in the people 's republic of china army but got discharged because of rheumatism . he once saved some dolphins ' lives by getting pieces of plastic from their stomachs that veterinarians could not reach .
