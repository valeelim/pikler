Theodoric the Great     theodoric the great ( 454 - august 30 , 526 ) , known to the romans as flavius theodoricus , was king of the ostrogoths ( 488-526 ) , ruler of italy ( 493-526 ) , and regent of the visigoths ( 511-526 ) . he united several german tribes and encouraged arianism . other websites . - theodoric the great at middleages.net - new advent
