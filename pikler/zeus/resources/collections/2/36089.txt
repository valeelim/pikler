Hibernation     hibernation is a time of inactivity . some endothermic ( warm-blooded ) animals hibernate , usually during the winter , when food is short . they fall into a sleep-like state . they regulate their metabolism to consume less energy . they lower their body temperature , slow their breathing , and slow other vital functions . during hibernation , the body uses fat for energy , which the animal has gathered in summer and autumn . mammals like bats , ground squirrels ( like marmots ) , hedgehogs , marsupials and others hibernate . related pages . - torpor - dormancy - aestivation
