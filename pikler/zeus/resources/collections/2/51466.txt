Herefordshire     herefordshire ( known as county of herefordshire ) is a county in the west midlands region of england . it borders the english counties of shropshire to the north , worcestershire to the east , gloucestershire to the south east and the welsh counties of gwent to the south west and powys to the west . it is not to be confused with hertfordshire , a county north of london . it is pronounced ( i.e . first syllable as in `` herring '' , and -e- a separate syllable ) . the county town of herefordshire is the cathedral city of hereford . historically , the rivers wye and lugg were navigable but the wide seasonal variations in water levels mean that few craft larger than canoes and coracles are now used . there are canoe centres at the boat house , glasbury-on-wye , the hereford youth service and kerne bridge ross-on-wye , as well as a rowing club in hereford . the early nineteenth century saw the construction of two canals , the hereford & gloucester canal and the leominster & stourport canal but these were never successful and there are now few remains to be seen .
