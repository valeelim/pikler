Dallas Stars     the dallas stars are a professional ice hockey team based in dallas , texas . they play in the national hockey league ( nhl ) . they have won 1 stanley cup , in 1999. the team began in the autumn of 1967 , as the minnesota north stars . after the 1992-1993 season , the team moved to dallas . texas is known as the `` lone star state '' . the team changed its name from `` north stars '' to just `` stars '' when they moved . other websites . - official website
