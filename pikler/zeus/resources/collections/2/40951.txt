Austria-Hungary     austria-hungary or the austro-hungarian empire was a state in central europe from 1867 to 1918. it was the countries of austria and hungary ruled by a single monarch . the full name of the empire was `` the kingdoms and lands represented in the imperial council and the lands of the crown of st. stephen '' . the dual monarchy replaced the austrian empire ( 1804â€“1867 ) . it started with the compromise between the ruling habsburg dynasty and the hungarians . it was an empire made up of many different peoples and great power . it found its political life full of arguments between the eleven main national groups . it had great economic growth through the age of industrialization . it also saw social changes with many liberal and democratic reforms . the habsburg dynasty ruled as emperors of austria over the western and northern half of the country and as kings of hungary over the kingdom of hungary . the kingdom of hungary had some ability to govern itself . it also had a say in things that affected both it and the rest of the empire . this was mainly foreign relations and defense .
