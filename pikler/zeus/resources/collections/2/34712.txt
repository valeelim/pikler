Bruno Brun     bruno brun ( 1910 — 1978 ) was yugoslavian clarinet player and professor at the music academy in belgrade . he graduated from the belgrade music academy in 1945 and continued his education in paris . he played as a soloist in yugoslavia and other countries . he was one of the founders of the `` association of musical artists of serbia '' . his most famous students are : milenko stefanovic , mihailo zivanovic , ernest ackun , ante grgin and nikola srdic . references • blagojevic , andrija . `` bruno brun ( 1910-1978 ) - founder of the yugoslav clarinet school . `` `` the clarinet '' , vol . 41/3 ( june 2014 ) , pp . 46-51 .
