Quart     a quart is a unit of measurement for volume . it is a quarter of a gallon or two pints . because the imperial system and the american system use different gallons their quarts are different too . in the american system there are two different quarts . there is a wet quart and a dry quart . there is only one imperial quart . one u.s. dry quart is a little smaller than one imperial quart . six u.s. wet pints are about five imperial pints .
