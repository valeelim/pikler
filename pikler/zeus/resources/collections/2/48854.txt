Ferrous     ferrous is an adjective coming from the latin `` ferrum '' ( iron ) . one uses this word to indicate the presence of iron , mostly in metallurgy or related industries . there are also non-ferrous metals , which are metals that have no iron in them at all . ferrous can also refer to iron in its +2 oxidation state . `` ferrous '' is also the name of a song by peter gabriel . related pages . - iron compounds
