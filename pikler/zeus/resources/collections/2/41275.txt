Rodeo     a rodeo is a north american sport . it is a collection of several similar activities that came from the history of the day to day lives of mexican vaqueros ( cowboys ) and american cowboys . it has events that came from the handling of cattle , and the riding and training of horses . rodeos started as a competition between the cowboys to see who was the best . over time it has gone from taking a collection of money for the person who won to today 's large professional competitions such as the national finals rodeo with a lot of money for the winners .
