For a Few Dollars More     for a few dollars more ( 1965 ) is a spaghetti western movie directed by sergio leone and starring clint eastwood . it is the second movie in the dollars trilogy and was released in the united states in 1967. the first movie in the trilogy is `` a fistful of dollars '' ; the third is `` the good , the bad and the ugly '' . other websites . - the spaghetti western database
