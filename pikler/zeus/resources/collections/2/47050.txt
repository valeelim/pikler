Jos Oehlen     jos oehlen , is a dutch sculptor . he was born in 1953 in tegelen , the netherlands . he now lives in bilthoven , the netherlands . jos oehlen studied at the academy of tilburg and the `` rijksakademie voor beeldende kunsten te amsterdam .
