Parque del Buen Retiro     the retiro park is the best known park in madrid . it was planned in 1632 as palace gardens by king felipe iv . 'retiro ' means retreat or a place to rest . when it was first built , it was a little way from the city . now , the city has become so big that it is in the city center . it was opened to the public in 1868. places in the park include a famous rose garden , a museum , a boating lake and the crystal palace . many shows and street theaters can be seen at the park on weekends .
