Crazy Horse (cabaret)    crazy horse ( cabaret ) le crazy horse saloon or le crazy horse paris is a nightclub in paris , founded in 1951. it is famous for its cabaret , which features nude female dancers . it was opened by alain bernardin , who also managed it until his suicide in 1994. today the cabaret is run by bernardin 's children . the franchise includes `` crazy horse paris '' for one in las vegas ( formerly la femme ) , at the mgm grand . other cabarets with similar names are separate enterprises .
