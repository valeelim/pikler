1921 New York Yankees season     the new york yankees during 1921 ( the 1921 season ) was the 19th season for the yankees in new york , and overall it was their 21st season . the season ended in the team having a record ( the number of wins and losses ) of 98-55 ( 98 wins , 55 losses ) . this was the first pennant won in yankees history . they won the american league game pennant , replacing the previous winners , cleveland indians . the manager of their team was miller huggins . the yankees home games were played on the polo grounds . references . - 1921 new york yankees team page at www.baseball-almanac.com other websites . - yankees game log courtesy baseball-almanac.com - 1921 new york yankees
