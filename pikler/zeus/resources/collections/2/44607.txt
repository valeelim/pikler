Budweiser (Anheuser-Busch)    budweiser ( anheuser-busch ) budweiser is a popular american beer made by the anheuser-busch company . it is made with hops , barley , and rice . it is known as the `` king of beers '' . it is brewed in st. louis and other cities around the united states . there are several different types of budweiser beer , such as bud light . the name is similar to a czech beer of the same name , budweiser budvar . websites . - budweiser 's official website
