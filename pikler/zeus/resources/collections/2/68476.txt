Taizu Emperor     taizu emperor ( , 1559 - 1626 ) was the founding father of the qing dynasty . his name was nurhaci and aisin gioro was his surname . the reign name was tian ming . nurhaci was manchurian . he was also the creator of the manchu alphabet . in 1580 , nurhaci became the jian zhou general of the ming dynasty . he unified the manchu tribe and organised the eight banners . in 1616 , nurhaci declared himself khan ( king ) and founded the jin dynasty in liao ling . in 1618 , in order to encourage people to against the ming dynasty . nurhaci wrote an article called the seven great vexations and sent it to the public . form 1618 until 1622 , nurhaci led armies to against the ming dynasty . he successfully occupied 70 cities in the north-eastern part of china . the capital was changed to liao yang after several battles . in 1622 , nurhaci 's armies captured guang ning . in 1626 , nurhaci led armies to attack the ning yuan city . unluckily , nurhaci was wounded by yuan chonghuan 's portuguese cannon and he died 2 days later at a little town called de-a man .
