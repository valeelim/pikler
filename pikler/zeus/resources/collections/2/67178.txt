Chitin     chitin is a semitransparent material that is the main component of the exoskeletons of arthropods , such as crustaceans ( e.g . crab , lobster and shrimp ) and insects ( e.g . ants , beetles and butterflies ) , of the cell walls of fungi , the radula of molluscs and the beaks of cephalopods ( e.g . squid , and octopuses ) . chitin has also proven useful for several medical and industrial purposes . other websites . - chitin product information from china greatvista chemicals - horseshoe crab chitin research
