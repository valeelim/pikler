Kuzcham Dherai     kuzcham dherai ( also sometimes called dherai , dehrai or derai ) is a town in the khyber pakhtunkhwa province , pakistan . it is located on the bank of the river swat and a couple of miles away from swat saidu sharif airport . the population of dherai is about 10,000 people . it is believed that alexander the great once passed through this area .
