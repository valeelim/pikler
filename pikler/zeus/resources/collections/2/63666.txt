Gregory the Illuminator     saint gregory the illuminator or saint gregory the enlightener ( armenian : գրիգոր լուսաւորիչ translit . grigor lusavorich , greek : γρηγόριος φωστήρ or φωτιστής , gregorios phoster or photistes ) , the founder and patron saint of the armenian apostolic church , he was born about 257. he belonged to the royal line of the arsacid dynasty , being the son of a parthian named anak , who assassinated chosroes i of armenia , and thus brought ruin on himself and his family . other websites . - new advent - gregory the illuminator - armenian church website
