Tawaret     in ancient egyptian mythology , tawaret ( also `` taweret ) `` was the demon-wife of apep , the first god of evil . she was often shown as a hippopotamus . because female hippos are aggressive to protect their young , pregnant women wore amulets of tawaret to protect their pregnancies . tawaret was a very popular goddess in the first dynasty . she was , overall , the goddess who protected women during childbirth and pregnancy . in ancient egypt , it was believed that if you wanted to live through childbirth it was necessary to pray to tawaret . other websites . - ancientegypt.co.uk
