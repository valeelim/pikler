Tumulus     a tumulus ( one tumulus , several `` tumuli '' ) is a certain type of grave . the word comes from latin . this way of burying people was common in the stone age , bronze age , and iron age . there are different layouts . sometimes sarcophaguses were used , at other times , urns were placed in the grave . there are layouts with one or with multiple chambers . sometimes the location is privileged , and stone circles can be found nearby .
