Bermuda International Airport     bermuda international airport , also known as l.f. wade international airport , is an airport in bermuda , an overseas territory of the united kingdom in the north atlantic ocean . in 2006 , l.f. wade international airport handled about 900,000 passengers , up 7 % from 2005. it has one passenger terminal , one cargo terminal , and eight aircraft stands . it can support all aircraft sizes up to and including the boeing 747. currently , ten airlines operate services to bermuda airport from canada , the united kingdom , and the united states . the largest aircraft now operating in regularly scheduled service at bda are british airways ' boeing 777-200s .
