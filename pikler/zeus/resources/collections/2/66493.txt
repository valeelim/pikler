Gurkha     gurkhas are nepali citizens . they come from the mountains of nepal and are best known for their courage , honesty , and loyalty . the brigade of gurkhas are a part of the british army . they have fought with the british for the past 200 years . they work in the security forces of brunei , singapore , india and other countries . the gurkha unit in the british army has won 26 victoria cross medals and many other medals .
