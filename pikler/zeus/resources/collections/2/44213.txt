Pterodactylus     pterodactylus was a small pterosaur or flying reptile . it lived in the later jurassic period , at the same time as many dinosaurs . `` pterodactylus '' is typical of the short-tailed pterosaurs , a sub-order which continued to the end of the cretaceous . the first specimens were found at solnhofen in bavaria , germany . these carbonate strata formed in warm lagoons also yielded the famous `` archaeopteryx '' . `` pterodactylus '' may have lived on small islands in the lagoon , or on the coast .
