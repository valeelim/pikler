Player killing     player killing ( pking ) is a part of online gaming with real people . it is when you kill another person 's character . some other terms in pking are : pjing , which means pile jumping , or attacking someone when they are picking up the items dropped by a player they have just killed a player , safer , which means someone who pks safely ( especially when eating or healing ) and does not take risks , making it widely considered 'unfair ' on the people trying to pk them and dd which means death dot , which is when people in a player killing team all stand in the same place as each other , making it appear to other players that there is only one person there . however , `` safing '' varies in how other people perceive it . it can be used to stay alive , however you usually lose a chance to hit when healing .
