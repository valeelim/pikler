Superman Returns (video game)    superman returns ( video game ) superman returns is a video game based on the movie `` superman returns '' . it is available for the playstation 2 , xbox , xbox 360 , nintendo ds and game boy advance consoles . some video game websites said this game would come out for computers ( pc ) , but electronic arts chose not to make a pc version of this game . there was going to be a gamecube version , but it was cancelled because of a 2006 contract with major league baseball as well as to avoid competition with two other movie-licensed games ( `` charlotte 's web '' and `` dreamgirls '' ) .
