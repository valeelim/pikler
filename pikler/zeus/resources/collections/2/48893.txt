Ceramic     ceramic is the name for materials that are formed by the use of heat . the word `` ceramic '' comes from the greek word ( `` '' ) . the term covers inorganic non-metallic materials which are formed by the action of heat . up to the 1950s or so , the most important were the traditional clays , made into pottery , bricks , tiles and the like , also cements and glass . clay-based ceramics are described in the article on pottery . a composite material of ceramic and metal is known as cermet . the word `` ceramic '' can be an adjective , and can also be used as a noun to refer to a ceramic material , or a product of ceramic manufacture . `` ceramics '' may also be used as a singular noun referring to the art of making things out of ceramic materials . the technology of manufacturing and usage of ceramic materials is part of the field of ceramic engineering . many ceramic materials are hard , porous , and brittle . the study and development of ceramics includes methods to deal with these characteristics , to accentuate the strengths of the materials and investigate novel applications .
