Federation     a federation is a union of a number of self-governing states or regions , which are joined together under a central government . the central government is not able to do a lot of things that central government in more centralized states can . these things are done by the states ( or regions ) in a federalized state . the united states of america is a federation . the federal ( central ) government has the highest executive role , but the states have their own special rules in some special cases . australia became a federation in 1901. after the 1971 civil war , pakistan became a federation adopted in the 1973 constitution of pakistan . other examples of federal states are austria , belgium ( since 1993 ) , canada , germany , russia and switzerland .
