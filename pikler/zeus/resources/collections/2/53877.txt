Video game publisher     a video game publisher is a company that publish games that they have made or that they have bought to be made by a video game developer . the publisher pays for the development of the game . because of that , they can tell the developers exactly what kind of game they want . the publisher is responsible for the making and marketing of the game . big publishers also distribute the games that they publish . smaller ones pay for other companies to distribute the game for them . publishers also usually design and make the copies of the games and write the manual .
