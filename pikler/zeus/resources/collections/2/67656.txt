Odawara Castle     odawara castle was destroyed by the meiji government . however , present odawara castle was rebuilt in 1960. it serves as a museum and is designated as an important historical monument . odawara castel premise is a park , and there are zoo and small amusement park in addition to historical site such as castel tower . it is voted the 100 best historic park in japan in october , 2006. other websites . - odawara castle
