Growth     growth means an increase in some quantity over time . the quantity can be physical ( e.g. , growth in height , growth in an amount of money ) or abstract ( e.g. , a system becoming more complex , an organism becoming more mature ) . it can also refer to the mode of growth , i.e . numeric models for describing how much a particular quantity grows over time : - cell growth - individual growth - fungal growth - auxology ( human development ) - human development ( biology ) - growth `` spurt '' ( rapid change in puberty ) - human development ( psychology ) - population growth - bacterial growth - tumours can sometimes be referred to as a growth - growth hormone - economic growth - for financial growth due to simple interest or compound interest see interest - personal growth , i.e. , personal development - mode - linear growth - logistic growth - exponential growth
