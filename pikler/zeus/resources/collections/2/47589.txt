Advice     advice can also mean : - advice ( opinion ) , an opinion or recommendation offered by someone - advice ( constitutional ) , in constitutional law , a frequently binding instruction issued to a constitutional office-holder - advice in aspect-oriented programming , a piece of code executed when a join point is reached - advice ( complexity ) , in complexity theory , a string with extra information used by turing machine or other computing device - pay advice , whereby employees are advised of having received directly deposited pay - advice column , a regular feature in a newspaper or magazine
