Commonwealth of England     the commonwealth of england was the republican government which ruled first england and then ireland and scotland from 1649 to 1660. after the execution of charles i on january 30 , 1649 , the commonwealth was declared ( `` '' ) by the rump parliament on may 19 , 1649. the government during 1653 to 1659 is properly called the protectorate , and took the form of direct personal rule by oliver cromwell and , after his death , his son richard , as lord protector . the term `` commonwealth '' is , however , loosely used to describe the system of government during the whole of 1649 to 1660 , the years of the english interregnum . the protectorate other websites . - british flags of the period - commonwealth of england : heads of state : 1649-1660
