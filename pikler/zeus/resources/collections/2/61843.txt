Lucky Town     lucky town is an album by bruce springsteen . it was produced by bruce springsteen with jon landau and chuck plotkin . the album was recorded by toby scott and mixed by bob clearmountain . it was released in 1992 under the copyright of bruce springsteen and was distributed by sony music . `` lucky town '' has 10 songs : - `` better days '' - `` lucky town '' - `` local hero '' - `` if i should fall behind '' - `` leap of faith '' - `` the big muddy '' - `` living proof '' - `` book of dreams '' - `` souls of the departed '' - `` my beautiful reward '' bruce springsteen plays all the instruments except the drums , which are played by gary mallabar . related pages . - bruce springsteen official website - lucky town official website - sony music usa
