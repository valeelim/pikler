Dubnium     dubnium is a chemical element . it has been named eka-tantalum , hahnium and unnilpentium but is now named dubnium . it has the symbol db . it has the atomic number 105. it is a very radioactive element that does not exist in nature . it has to be made . the isotope that has the longest half-life only has a half life of 32 hours . dubnium is a transactinide element .
