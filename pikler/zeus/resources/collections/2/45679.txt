D'Anjou     d'anjou ( or poire d'anjou ) is a type fruit in the family rosaceae . it is a type of pear that first came from anjou , france . d'anjou pears usually cost less than other types of pears , but are not as sweet . there are two main varieties of d'anjou : - green anjou has a green skin that may turn yellow as the pear ripens . - red anjou has a red skin that darkens as it ripens .
