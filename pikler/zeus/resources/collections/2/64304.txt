Saint Mesrob     saint mesrop mashtots ( also mesrob , mashtotz , , 360 - february 17 , 440 ) was an armenian monk . he invented the armenian alphabet in the year 406. he was a theologian and linguist . that was a basic and important step in strengthening three things : the armenian church , the government of the armenian kingdom , and connections between the armenian kingdom and armenians living in the byzantine and iranian empires . movies . - 1988 : `` mesrop mashtots '' film documentary armenfilm 35mm , color ( ) director levon mkrtchyan , script by artashes martirosyan , the composer sarkis alajajyan , narration by sos sargisyan .
