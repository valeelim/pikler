Lip balm     lip balm is a product which is put on lips so they will not dry when a person is outdoors in the sun and wind . lip balm is usually made from petrolatum . some types of lip balm also include sunscreen to protect the lips from sunburn . lip balm comes in tubes and small pots with screw-on lids . lip balm is often used during the winter , because the cold winter winds can dry out a person 's lips so that they are cracked and hurting ( this is called having `` chapped '' lips ) .
