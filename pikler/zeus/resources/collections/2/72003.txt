1940 Summer Olympics     the 1940 summer olympics , officially known as games of the xii olympiad , were not held . the games were planned for tokyo in japan , but the japanese organizers withdrew in 1938 because of the second sino-japanese war . in 1938 , the international olympic committee decided to hold the games at helsinki in finland . the games were cancelled because of world war ii . related pages . - sports in japan - 1916 summer olympics - 1940 winter olympics - 1944 summer olympics - 1944 winter olympics
