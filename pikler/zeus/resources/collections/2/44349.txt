MIRC     mirc is a popular shareware internet relay chat ( irc ) client for microsoft windows . it was created in 1995 by khaled mardam-bey in c. its most recent update ( 7.25 ) was made in 2012. it is licensed as proprietary . it comes free of charge for 30 days and then you have the choice to buy it for $ 20 usd , or continue using but the program will ask you to buy it at each launch . related pages . - list of irc clients other websites . - official website
