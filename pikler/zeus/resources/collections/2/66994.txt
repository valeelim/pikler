Jennifer Connelly     jennifer lynn connelly ( born december 12 , 1970 ) is an american movie actress . she also worked as child model . although she has been working in the movie industry since she was a teenager and became famous for her roles in movies like `` labyrinth '' and `` career opportunities '' , she did not become a big movie star until the 2000 drama `` requiem for a dream '' , and the 2001 biopic film `` a beautiful mind '' , for which she won an oscar for best supporting actress . other websites . - jennifer connelly interview - jennifer connelly blood diamond interview with leonardo dicaprio
