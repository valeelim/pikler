Thermodynamic equilibrium     in thermodynamics , a thermodynamic system is said to be in thermodynamic equilibrium when it is in thermal equilibrium , mechanical equilibrium , radiative equilibrium , and chemical equilibrium . the local state of a system at thermodynamic equilibrium is determined by the values of its intensive parameters , as pressure , temperature , etc . specifically , thermodynamic equilibrium is characterized by the minimum of a thermodynamic potential .
