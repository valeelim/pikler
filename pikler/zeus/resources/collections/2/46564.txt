Seaborgium     seaborgium is a chemical element . in the past , it has been named eka-tungsten but is now named seaborgium . it has the symbol sg . it has the atomic number 106. seaborgium is a radioactive element that does not exist in nature . it has to be made . the most stable isotope is sg . seaborgium-271 has a half-life of 2.4 minutes . what seaborgium looks like is not known because not enough has been made to see it with human eyesight , but since it is in the same period as tungsten in the periodic table , it 's appearance and may be similar to such . the element is named in honor of glenn seaborg . seaborgium is a transuranium element . this means that it is `` beyond '' ( trans ) the element uranium in the sequence of elements . mendeleev predicted that seaborgium would exist . he called the element eka-tungsten because of its location was near tungsten in the periodic table . the chemistry of seaborgium is like the chemistry of tungsten . other websites . - webelements.com - seaborgium
