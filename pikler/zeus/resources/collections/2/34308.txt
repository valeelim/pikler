Xia dynasty     xia dynasty ( chinese : 夏朝 ; pinyin : xià cháo ; wade-giles : hsia-ch'ao ) was the first dynasty of china . it was founded in 2100 bc and ended in 1600 bc . until archaeologists dug at erlitou in henan province , many scientists were not sure if the xia dynasty was real or a myth . the last king was jie who was said to be cruel and greedy . he was defeated by t'ang , the king of the shang people from the east .
