Clementine     a clementine is a type of citrus fruit . it has a bright orange rind . they have a diameter of about 3 to 5 inches . clementines are easy to peel and have no seeds . they are grown in california , and are usually available from november to january . they were more popular outside the united states than inside , but in 1997 , there were not many florida oranges available because it was very cold . people bought clementines instead , which made them more popular . they are still popular today . clementines are popular in the winter months . they are usually found in wooden crates in the supermarket . a popular clementine brand is called cuties . they are named after a french monk , père clément rodier , who lived in north africa at the beginning of the 20th century . he either found a natural mutation of the mandarin orange , which he grew , or he created a hybrid of the mandarin and the seville oranges . the fruit , however , may have originated long before in asia .
