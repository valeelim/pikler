Clan     a clan is a group of people who – following a tradition – belong to the same kin . the word comes from the gaelic language , and means 'family ' . it is similar in meaning to the more general word tribe . in europe , the best-known clans system is that in scotland , which still has some effects today , especially in the scottish highlands and islands . it existed before the land was unified under a scottish king , and continued long afterwards . clans also existed in ireland
