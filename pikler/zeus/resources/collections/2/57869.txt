Rostov     rostov ( russian : росто́в ; old norse : rostofa ) is one of the oldest towns in russia . it was first mentioned in the year 862 as an already important town . by the 13th century rostov became the capital of an important area . now it is an important tourist centre of the so called golden ring . it is located on the shores of lake nero in yaroslavl oblast . about 35,300 people lived there in 2002. while the official name of the town is rostov , it is better known to russians as rostov veliky , i.e . `` rostov the great '' . this name is used to distinguish it from rostov on don , which is now a much larger city . rostov yaroslavsky is the official name of its railway station ( due to its position in yaroslavl oblast ) , but not of the town itself .
