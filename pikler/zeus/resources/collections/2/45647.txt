Propeller     a propeller makes an aircraft , ship , or submarine in water or air go by making a big wind or a strong stream . it does this by turning two or more wings very quick . the blades of a propeller act as rotating wings , and produce force through application of both bernoulli 's principle and newton 's third law , generating a difference in pressure between the forward and rear surfaces of the airfoil-shaped blades . other websites . - aircraft-info.net - propeller aircraft - titanic 's propellers
