Morning     morning is the time of day from midnight to noon . morning is usually cooler than afternoon . many people say it is not morning until they wake up . many people use the morning as a time to get ready for the day . this includes things like taking a shower , eating a meal ( called breakfast , because you break the fast of the night ) and getting dressed . then many people go to work or school . many people buy a morning newspaper to read about what is going on in the world .
