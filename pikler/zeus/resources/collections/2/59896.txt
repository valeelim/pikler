Cuxhaven     cuxhaven is a town in lower saxony , germany . about 55,000 people live there . it is on the shore of the north sea at the mouth of the elbe river . cuxhaven is the capital of the cuxhaven district . it is a popular vacation spot on the north sea . in 1896 , when the town was an exclave of hamburg , the railway line from bremerhaven hauptbahnhof was built .
