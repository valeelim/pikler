Slackware     slackware is a gnu/linux distribution which is famous for being stable , fast and suitable for older computers . slackware is the oldest linux distribution that is still supported . it does not offer any graphical tools for configuration ; rather , the person must change it on his or her own using the command line interface and by editing text files . the newest version is slackware 14.0. related pages . - list of linux distributions other websites . - http : //www.slackware.com - the official website .
