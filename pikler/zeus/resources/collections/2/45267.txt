Rib cage     the ribcage is a part of the skeleton of humans and some animals . it is made up of curved bones . the rib cage is found in the chest area . it protects a persons internal organs from damage . most humans have 12 pairs of rib bones with one from each pair on each side of the chest . some people may have 11 or 13 pairs of rib bones . the bones are also filled with bone marrow , full of calcium and protein .
