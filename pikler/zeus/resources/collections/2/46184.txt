Botanist     botanists are scientists who study plants , including flowering plants , and plant-like things such as moss and seaweed . botany is a scientific study of plants along with their growth , structure , [ evolution ] ] , and uses . botanists may specialize in certain areas of botany . some important areas of study include : - plant taxonomy - plant ecology - chemical biology - photosynthesis , the process that turns carbon dioxide from the air into food using sunlight - apoptosis , what makes a cell `` decide '' to die . this study area has been important for cancer research .
