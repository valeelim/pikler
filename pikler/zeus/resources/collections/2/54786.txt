Princeton University     princeton university is a private university in princeton , new jersey in the united states . it opened in 1746 with the name `` the college of new jersey '' . it was eventually named `` princeton university '' in 1896. in conversation , it is simply called , `` princeton '' . princeton is one of the 8 ivy league colleges . it was the fourth official college in the united states . princeton 's main library , firestone library , is one of the biggest university libraries in the world . in 2013 forbes put princeton at number three in their list of `` america 's top colleges '' . princeton works with coursera .
