Armenian Premier League     the armenian premier league is the top football competition in armenia . it was founded by the football federation of armenia in 1992. from 1936 to 1991 , the games were held as a regional tournament within the ussr . for the first three years the season ran from spring to fall , but since the 1995-1996 season has run from summer to spring . the league currently consists of six teams , and the worst teams relegates to the armenian first league . over the years , the league has shrunk from a league consisting of only eight teams to six teams . and related pages . - armenian cup - armenian super cup - armenian national team - armenian u-21 national team - sports in armenia other websites . - fifa.com : table
