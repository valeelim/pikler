Bobby Lashley     franklin roberto lashley ( born july 16 , 1976 ) is an american professional wrestler , mixed martial artist and former collegiate amateur wrestler . he is better known as bobby lashley . he is currently signed to total nonstop action wrestling ( tna ) , where he competes under the ring name lashley . he is also known for his time in the world wrestling entertainment ( wwe ) . lashley was released from the wwe on february 4 , 2008. he had not wrestled for several months before that due to injury . he is also an mma fighter and he holds a record of 12 wins and 2 losses . lashley stated that in 2017 he will retire lashley went to missouri valley college . while he was there , he won three national amateur wrestling championships between 1996 and 1998. during 1997 and 1998 he was the naia national wrestling champion . he wrestled at the weight of 177 pounds ( 80 kilograms ) . lashley later joined the united states army , where he continued to wrestle . in november 2003 , lashley started professional wrestling . he began working for world wrestling entertainment ( wwe ) . wwe sent him to ohio valley wrestling ( ovw ) , where he started his training under lance storm . during 2005 , lashley appeared at four live events for both the `` raw '' and `` smackdown ! '' brands . on september 23 2005 , lashley made his televised wwe debut on `` smackdown ! ''
