Henrik Malyan     henrik malyan ( armenian language : հենրիկ մալյան ; september 30 , 1925 in telavi , georgia – march 14 , 1988 in yerevan , armenia ) was an armenian movie director . his 1977 movie `` life triumphs '' ( `` nahapet '' ) is considered to be one of the most important armenian movies to deal with the armenian genocide . it was exhibited in the 1978 cannes film festival . filmography . - 1977 : nahapet - sos sargsyan - 1968 : yerankyunin - 1980 : hayrik - mher mkrtchyan - 1969 : menk enk mer sarare - 1978 : ktor me yerkink
