Roman Abramovich     roman abramovich ( born october 24 , 1966 in saratov , russia ) is a russian-british businessman . he is one of the richest men in the world . according to 2006 forbes magazine , he has a net worth of $ 18.2 billion . he owns chelsea f.c. , an english premiership football club . he has given chelsea a lot money to pay their players , and buy new ones . he is a former governor of a region of russia called chukotka . other websites . - roman-abramovich.com
