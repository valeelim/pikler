Kosovska Mitrovica     kosovska mitrovica is a town in north kosovo , serbia . over 71,601 people live in kosovska mitrovica , mostly in the south . the town used to be called titova mitrovica . originally it was named after saint dimitrije . there is the university of pristina in kosovska mitrovica . other websites . - municipality of mitrovica the official website of municipality of south mitrovica - university of pristina website - jazz & blues festival north city
