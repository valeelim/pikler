Emperor Wu of Jìn     emperor wu of jìn ( 236 - 290 ) was the founder of western jin dynasty . his name was sima yan . he was born in he nei ( now henan province ) . his father was sima zhao and sima yi was his grandfather . they were the imperial officials of wei dynasty and seized control over the imperial court . in 265 ad , sima yan forced emperor cao huan to abdicate . in 280 ad , he conquered eastern wu and unified china .
