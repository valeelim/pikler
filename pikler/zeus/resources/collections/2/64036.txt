Yupik     the yupik ( in the central alaskan yup'ik language , yup'ik , plural yupiit ) people are a group of native people who mainly live in certain parts of alaska , somewhere near fairbanks . most of them were wiped out by a virus or sickness . some of the yupi'k people 's traditions are celebrating the dead . they celebrate by having ceremonies with a shaman , where they connect with the dead . they also name the next child born after the most recent person who has died .
