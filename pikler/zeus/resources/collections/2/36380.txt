Taoyuan City     taoyuan city ( ) is a special municipality of taiwan , republic of china , in the northwestern part of the island , next to new taipei city . taoyuan district is in this municipality and serves as the seat of taoyuan city . `` taoyuan '' means `` peach garden , '' since the area used to have many peach blossoms . taoyuan is the native home to the pingpu tribe of aborigines . it is now an important industrial region of taiwan . taipei taoyuan international airport , which serves taipei , is inside this county . taoyuan city controls 13 districts . education . - chien hsin university other pages . - list of counties of republic of china other websites . - official website ( in chinese ) - official website ( in english )
