Okinawa     okinawa is the name for the biggest island in the ry큰ky큰 islands , far south of japan . it is also the common name for okinawa prefecture , which controls the southern ry큰ky큰 islands . the capital of okinawa is naha . naha is on the island with the most people , okinawa island . okinawa used to be called great lew chew island . many of okinawa 's islands are scenic , and there are many beaches there . the temperature in okinawa is often warm or hot . many animals make their home around the okinawa islands . for example , sea turtles , jellyfish , and many kinds of birds all live around okinawa . an average person in okinawa lives to be older than 90. some people think that is because okinawan food is healthy . fish is very popular , but people also eat sea vegetables and pork . okinawan food is famous for its originality . examples of local specialties are okinawa soba , go-ya champuru , rafute , and ji-mami-doufu . okinawa 's music is popular all over japan . a musical instrument called a `` sanshin '' is often used .
