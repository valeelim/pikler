Eccentricity     eccentricity or extravagance is a word used to describe unusual behaviour in a person . people who have habits which many people think are very strange may be called eccentric . eccentric behaviour is not necessarily good or bad in itself . some clever people ( geniuses ) have behaviour which might be described as eccentric . it is not always clear whether behaviour is just eccentric or whether it is madness . famous people who were eccentric in their different ways include albert einstein , nero , alan turing , howard hughes , ludwig ii of bavaria and lord berners .
