Gesualdo     gesualdo is an italian commune of the region of the campania , province of avellino , with about 3.800 inhabitants . it shares border with sturno , villamaina , frigento , fontanarosa , grottaminarda . carlo gesualdo , the renaissance composer and murderer lived in a castle in gesualdo . other websites . - gesualdo e carlo gesualdo - www.carlogesualdo.eu - www.gesualdo.eu - don carlo gesualdo
