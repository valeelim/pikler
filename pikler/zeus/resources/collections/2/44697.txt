2005 Cronulla riots     the 2005 cronulla riots were a series of violent events between lebanese and white australian mobs . riots started in and around cronulla , a suburb on the beach in sydney , australia 's biggest city . the violence was soon also in other suburbs around sydney . the riots happened on 11 , 12 and 14 december . many people committed crimes there . several people were sent to prison for being involved in the riot .
