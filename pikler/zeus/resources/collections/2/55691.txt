God the Father     in many religions , the deity or god is called the father . in christianity , god the father is the first person of the trinity . he `` is '' god , just like the son and the holy spirit , but as a `` person '' he is `` different '' from the son and the holy spirit . although jesus had st. joseph for his human father on earth , jesus is god , so his father would actually be god the father . related pages . - god - christ - jesus other websites . - god the father at theopedia
