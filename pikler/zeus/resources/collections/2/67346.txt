Tortoise     a tortoise is a reptile of the order testudines which lives on land . like their aquatic cousins , the sea turtles , tortoises are shielded from predators by a shell . they move slowly , and eat vegetation . most are quite small , but island tortoises have several times evolved to a large size : those on the galapagos are famous . darwin made one of his rare mistakes when he did not note which island each of them came from . later it became clear that they were slightly different on different islands . further reading . - chambers , paul . `` a sheltered life : the unexpected history of the giant tortoise '' . john murray ( publishers ) , london . 2004. isbn 0-7195-6528-6 . - gerlach , justin . giant tortoises of the indian ocean . chimiara publishers , frankfurt . 2004 related pages . - turtle - sea turtle other websites . - gulf coast turtle and tortoise society* tortoise world - several types of tortoises - chelonia : conservation and care of turtles . - infotortuga
