Studio     a studio is a place where an artist can work . studios can be used for many things , such as making photos , movies , television shows , cartoons , or music . the word comes from the latin word , `` studium '' , which means to eagerly want something . a music studio is a room or group of rooms that are used for practicing music . most music studios have a piano and a mirror . a recording studio is used for recording music , speech , or other kinds of sound . many recordings become works of art in themselves .
