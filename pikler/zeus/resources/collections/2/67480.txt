Flickr     flickr is a photo sharing website and web services suite . it is also an online community platform , which is considered an early example of a web 2.0 application . users can upload any photo to their website . the user will then be asked to release the photo under many licenses available . some licenses allow other people to reuse it , which makes it possible for wikimedia commons to transfer free files from flickr to their website . any person that has a yahoo ! id can register their flickr account in a few seconds . flickr provides a free service for users to share their photos online with other people . however , free users have a limit of 300 mb of uploads per month , 15 mb per photo and 150 mb per video . however , users can upgrade to the pro account where they can get unlimited uploads at $ 24.95 per month . flickr also has a set of tools for users to upload their photos from their desktops . however , they can only be used on windows and mac computers . other websites . - flickr - flickrblog
