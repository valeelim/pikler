Worcester     worcester is a city in the west midlands region of england . in 2005 , about 94,300 people lived there . worcester is the county town of worcestershire . it is about 30 miles ( 48 km ) southwest of birmingham and 29 miles ( 47 km ) north of gloucester . the river severn runs through the middle of the city . the cathedral overlooks the river . famous people from worcester include edward elgar and cher lloyd . the university of worcester is a high ranking university .
