Billy Cox     billy cox is a bassist , best known for playing with guitarist jimi hendrix . he was born in west virginia . billy first met hendrix in the early part of the 1960s , when they were both in the united states army . cox played with hendrix in an r & b band called the king kasuals . the two played at clubs in nashville , tennessee . while in england , hendrix asked cox to join him in a new band ; cox said no . the spot instead went to noel redding . they met again in 1969 , when hendrix asked on cox to play bass in the experimental band `` band of gypsys '' . billy cox also played at woodstock . in 1971 cox released his album `` nitro function '' . other websites . - billy cox - biography
