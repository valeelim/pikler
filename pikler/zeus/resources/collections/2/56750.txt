Dog sled     a dog sled is a sled that is pulled by sled dogs and can go over ice and snow . dog sleds were first made by first nations people in canada before the english and french got there , because they had no horses so they used dogs to travel . they were also made by people in norway , sweden , finland and russia because it was also easier to travel with dogs in parts of those countries . racing dog sleds is a popular sport which a lot of people enjoy watching or actually going in the sleds to race . it is more popular in places where there is not much horse racing .
