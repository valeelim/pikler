North Hollywood shootout     the north hollywood shootout was a gunfight that happened on february 28 , 1997. emil matasareanu and larry eugene phillips were the bank robbers , and used ak-47s , an ar-15 , an hk-91 , and 9 mm pistols . the police could not kill them because of their heavy body armour , which was made out of kevlar . 20 people were wounded , and the only two killed were the robbers . larry eugene phillips jr ( 1970-1997 ) shot himself in the head with a beretta m92f after being injured multiple times . his body was left in the street for about an hour before ambulances came to take his body away .
