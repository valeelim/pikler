Ming Kipa     ming kipa is a nepalese sherpa girl who ( as recorded in the 2009 guinness book of records ) was the youngest person to climb mount everest . she reached the summit on may 24 , 2003 when she was 15 years old , with her brother mingma gyula and her sister laphka . nepalese law does not allow climbers under 16 to climb everest , so ming kipa sherpa summitted everest from the chinese side .
