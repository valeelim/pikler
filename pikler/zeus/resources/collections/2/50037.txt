Air France     air france is one of the largest airlines in the world . it is based in france . it is owned by air france-klm . before it merged with klm , it employed 71,654 people . its headquarters are at charles de gaulle international airport near paris . the airline can be traced back to 1933 , when four other french airlines were combined to create `` air france '' . air france hired its first flight attendants in 1946. the airline started flying between paris and new york city on july 1 , 1946. in 1957 , the airline bought its first jet aircraft . air france was one of 4 airlines that flew the concorde . it used it from 1976 until 2003. in 1988 , the airline became the first to fly the airbus a320 . in 2003 , klm royal dutch airlines and air france announced they were going to be combining . the combination ended in 2004. as of 2016 , air france is going through many financial problems .
