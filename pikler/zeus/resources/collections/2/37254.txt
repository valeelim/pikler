NBA Finals     the nba finals , previously known as the nba world championship series until 1987 , is the championship series of the national basketball association , played under a best-of-seven playoff format . the team who wins the eastern conference finals earns one of the two places in the championship round , with the other place for the team that wins the western conference finals . this event has been played at the end of every nba and baa season in history . the first was held in 1947 .
