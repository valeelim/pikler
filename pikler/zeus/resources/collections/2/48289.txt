Magdalen College School, Oxford    magdalen college school , oxford magdalen college school is an independent school for boys . it is on the edge of central oxford , england . it was founded as part of magdalen college , oxford by william waynflete in 420. the school was originally founded by william waynflete to educate the sixteen boy choristers of magdalen college , oxford university who sang in the college 's chapel . important old waynfletes include william tyndale , sam mendes , alex lennox , tim hunt , and ben goldacre . other websites . - magdalen college school website - isi inspection report - a history of the choristers of magdalen college school , oxford
