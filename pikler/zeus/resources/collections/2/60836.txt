Ogden Nash     ogden nash ( august 19 , 1902 - may 19 , 1971 ) was an american poet . his poetry is thought by many people to be very funny . nash was born in rye , new york on august 19 , 1902. in 1932 , he joined the staff of `` the new yorker '' . he died of crohn 's disease on may 19 , 1971 in baltimore , maryland .
