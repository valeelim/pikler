Moldavia     moldavia ( romanian : `` moldova '' ) is a region in eastern europe . it existed from the 14th century to 1859 , when it became one with wallachia as the basis of the modern romanian state ; at various times , it included the regions of bessarabia ( with the budjak ) and much of bukovina . the bigger part of bessarabia is currently the independent state of moldova , while the rest of it and the northern part of bukovina are territories of ukraine .
