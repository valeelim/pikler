Stockport     stockport is a large town in greater manchester , north west england . seven miles ( 10 km ) south east of the city of manchester , it is the largest settlement of the metropolitan borough of stockport in greater manchester , and has a population of 136,083 ( 2001 census ) , with the borough as a whole having 281,000. this makes the borough 23rd most populated district in england . the river mersey begins in stockport , at the meeting point of the rivers goyt and tame . the town lies in the metropolitan borough of stockport which is in greater manchester . the town of stockport is twinned with the following towns : - - heilbronn , germany . since 1999 - - beziers , france , since 1972 - - paisley , scotland , since 2006 paisley is not officially twinned with stockport but the entry is probably a mark of friendship . other websites . - stockport council - hatworks museum - stockport county football club - stockport rugby union football club - the stockport express - the national library for the blind
