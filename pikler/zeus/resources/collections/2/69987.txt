Nymphadora Tonks     nymphadora tonks is a fictional character in the `` harry potter '' book series written by j. k. rowling . tonks , as she is known , is the daughter of andromeda black and ted tonks . she is a `` half-blood '' , but both of her parents are actually magical . her mother is a pureblood , and her father is a muggle born , therefore considering that the majority of her father 's family are muggles she is considered a halfblood , even though he is not a muggle himself . tonks is a `` metamorphmagus '' , which means that she can change her appearance when she wants to . tonks is described as being `` seriously clumsy '' in the books . she works as an auror in the ministry of magic . in `` harry potter and the deathly hallows '' , she marries the werewolf remus lupin and they have a son called teddy remus lupin . teddy is a metamorphmagus like tonks . both tonks and lupin die during the battle of hogwarts .
