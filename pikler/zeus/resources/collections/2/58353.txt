PS10 solar power tower     the ps10 solar power plant ( ) is a solar power plant in sanlucar la mayor , spain . the plant is made up of a tower in the center of a large field covered in many mirrors . each of the 624 moveable mirrors has an area of 120 m ( 1290 ft ) . the mirrors reflect the light from the sun to the top of the tower . the heat warms water within pipes in the central tower turning it into steam . the steam drives turbines which , at peak , put out 11 mw of electricity . the planet is really good for providing electricity during the hottest parts of the day when air conditioning demand is the highest . other pages . - solar power - power station - electricity
