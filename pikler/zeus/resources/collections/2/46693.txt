Felix Klein     felix christian klein ( april 25 , 1849 , düsseldorf , ( then ) prussian empire – june 22 , 1925 , göttingen , germany ) was a german mathematician . he became known for his work in group theory , function theory , non-euclidean geometry , and on the connections between geometry and group theory . his 1872 erlangen program , which classified geometries by their underlying symmetry groups , was a hugely influential synthesis of much of the mathematics of the day . the klein bottle is named after him .
