Stage race     a bicycle stage race is a race held over several days . each day 's race is called a `` stage '' . many countries have a racing tour of their country , for example the deutschland tour and the tour of britain . the oldest and most famous stage race is the tour de france . a stage race does not have to be tour of a country . the bayern rundfahrt is a stage race in bavaria , germany . in contrast the eneco tour is a stage race around belgium and the netherlands .
