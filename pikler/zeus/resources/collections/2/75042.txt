B'Day     b'day is the second studio album by american r & b singer beyoncé , released by columbia records on 1 september 2006 outside north america , on september 4 , 2006 worldwide , and on september 5 , 2006 in canada and the united states . the album 's title comes from the fact that september 4 , the date of worldwide release , is beyoncé 's birthday ( 2006 being her 25th ) . `` b'day '' won the award for `` best contemporary r & b album '' at the 49th annual grammy awards . a two-cd deluxe edition of the album , including previously unreleased material in english and spanish , was released on april 3 2007 . 6 singles were released from the album : `` déjà vu '' , `` ring the alarm '' , `` irreplaceable '' , `` beautiful liar '' , `` get me bodied '' , and `` green light '' . `` check on it '' was also a single , but was released from the destiny 's child album # 1 's . track listing . deluxe edition . - north american deluxe edition on digital copies `` world wide woman '' is a non-hidden track placed at track 8 , and other songs are pushed down the track order .
