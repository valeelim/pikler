Woodside, London    woodside , london woodside is a town in the london borough of croydon , england . woodside racecourse opened in 1866 on a park called stroud green farm , it has now closed and is now the site of ashburton learning village , closing in 1890. woodside station opened to serve visitors to the racecourse , but not a lot of people used it when the course closed so it was replaced to make way for a tram line . woodside is close to south norwood and addiscombe .
