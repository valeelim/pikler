False prophet     a false prophet is a person who claims to be a true prophet but really is not . a false prophet is also someone who uses prophecy to do evil . the term is also used outside religion to describe someone who fervently promotes a theory which the speaker thinks is false . in christianity , false prophets are thought of as being inspired by satan , or the devil . false prophets are the opposite of prophets . false prophets try to deceive people and bring them away from god . they use false information to lead people away from god . there are many false prophets in the bible .
