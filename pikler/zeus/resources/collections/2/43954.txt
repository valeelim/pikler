Horn (anatomy)    horn ( anatomy ) horns are part of the body of some animals . they grow on the animal 's head . they are projections ( things that stick out ) made of hard skin . the horn has a lot of keratin in it , the same protein that is in human hair and nails . normally horned animals will have two horns , but the rhinoceros has just one horn in the middle of its head . animals often use their horns for fighting one another . thousands of years ago people used the horns of dead animals for musical instruments ( see horn ( musical instrument ) ) . in later times , they were used for carrying gunpowder . related pages . - antlers , structures similar to horns
