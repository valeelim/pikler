Carolina Gynning     carolina gynning ( born 6 october 1978 ) is a swedish celebrity and model . she also is the winner of the 2004 season of reality television program big brother . gynning has also written the book , `` ego girl '' . it is a biography of her early life . in 2008 it was reported that gynning would write a continuation to `` ego girl '' called `` ego woman '' . sources . - gynning in swedish newspaper
