Barrel (weapons)    barrel ( weapons ) a barrel is a part of a gun . it is a long metal tube that the bullet or projectile goes through after it is fired . guns can have many different sizes of barrels . usually , longer barrels mean that the bullet will hit the target more often . barrels usually have spiral grooves on the inside called rifling . barrels with grooves spin the projectile around faster as it exits the gun . this makes the projectile more stable in flight and more accurate .
