Vaquita     vaquita ( `` phocoena sinus '' ) is a rare type of porpoise . it lives only in the gulf of california . vaquita have a dark coloring around the eyes and mouth . the upper side of the body is medium to dark gray . the underside is off-white to light gray . it has a rather large fin on its back for its size . the vaquita is the smallest type of porpoise in the world , growing up to 5 feet long and weighing up to 120 pounds . vaquita feed on small , bottom-dwelling fish and squid . they live alone or in small groups , usually of about three , but sometimes they live in groups of up to ten .
