Chorlton-on-Medlock     chorlton-on-medlock is a district of manchester in england which is south of the city centre . the northern border is marked by the river medlock . it is sometimes mistaken for chorlton-cum-hardy , a different place in manchester . chorlton-on-medlock contains the main campuses of the university of manchester and the manchester metropolitan university ; also in the district are a group of hospitals including the manchester royal infirmary and hospitals for women , children and the treatment of diseases of the eyes . famous residents of chorlton-on-medlock include lloyd george , emmeline pankhurst and elizabeth gaskell .
