Alba Iulia     alba iulia ( latin : `` apulum '' , german : `` karlsburg '' / '' weißenburg '' , hungarian : `` gyulafehérvár '' , former ) is a city in alba county , transylvania , romania . it has a population of 66,369 people . it is found on the mureş river . sister cities . - arnsberg , germany - székesfehérvár , hungary - aeghio , greece - alcalá de henares , spain - sliven , bulgaria - duzce , turkey - san benedetto del tronto , italy
