Gorton     gorton is a district of manchester in england . it is east of manchester city centre . neighbouring areas include longsight and levenshulme . according to the 2001 uk census , 24,173 lived there . gorton is split into two wards , gorton north and gorton south . the building known as gorton monastery is a former roman catholic church which has been repaired after a period of neglect and is used for other purposes .
