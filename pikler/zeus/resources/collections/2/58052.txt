Deadliest Catch     the deadliest catch is a reality television series on the discovery channel , about alaskan king crab fishing . the series shows people fishing for crabs in the bering sea near alaska . the series is named `` deadliest catch '' because the crew of these boats are at a high risk of death or injury due to the dangerous places and weather of the sea during the seasons they fish , earning the title of most dangerous job in the world .
