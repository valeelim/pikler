Courtly love     courtly love was a special idea of love that people had in europe in the middle ages . the word `` court '' means the courts where princes or dukes lived . courtly love is usually when a young man , who may be a peasant or even a simple king , falls in love with a rich and tries to make himself of her by doing brave things or by singing beautiful love songs . the idea of courtly love can be found in a lot of literature e.g . in works by geoffrey chaucer , dante , gottfried von strassburg , walther von der vogelweide , wolfram von eschenbach , malory and william shakespeare . other pages . - troubadour - minstrel
