Vulnerability     vulnerability means the extent to which changes can hurt or harm a person or a system . in context with natural hazards and natural disasters , `` vulnerability '' is a concept that looks at the relationship that people have with their environment and at social aspects . so it links environment to social forces and institutions and the cultural values that sustain and contest them . “ the concept of vulnerability expresses the multidimensionality of disasters by focusing attention on the totality of relationships in a given social situation which constitute a condition that , in combination with environmental forces , produces a disaster ” ( bankoff et al . 2004 : 11 ) . complex definition : vulnerability is the susceptibility to physical or emotional injury or attack . it also means to have one 's guard down , open to censure or criticism ; assailable . vulnerability refers to a person 's state of being liable to succumb , as to persuasion or temptation ( see thywissen 2006 for a comparison of vulnerability definitions ) .
