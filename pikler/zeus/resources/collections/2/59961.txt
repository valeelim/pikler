Roundhay Garden Scene     roundhay garden scene is a short movie made on october 14 , 1888 in the garden of `` oakwood grange '' , roundhay , leeds , west yorkshire , england . it was made by a french man named louis le prince . it was silent . the movie had adolphe le prince , sarah whitley , joseph whitley , and harriet hartley walking in the garden of oakwood grange . it is the oldest surviving movie . it is 1.66 seconds long . the digitalized version lasts 2.11 seconds . other websites . - louis le prince centre for cinema , photography , and television - youtube video of `` roundhay garden scene ''
