Conversation analysis     conversation analysis ( commonly abbreviated as ca ) is the study of talk in social interaction . ca tries to describe the structure and patterns of interaction , whether this is institutional ( in the school , doctor 's surgery , courts or elsewhere ) or casual conversation . the term “ conversation ” to label this disciplinary movement is misleading if read in a colloquial sense , as many have . other websites . - an introduction to conversation analysis ( by discourse and rhetoric group , loughborough university ) - online bibliography of pre-1990 ca literature - online bibliography of post-1989 ca literature - online clearinghouse for the ca community
