Las Ventas     the plaza de toros de las ventas is a building in madrid , spain used mainly for bullfighting . sometimes it is just called las ventas . it is made of red brick and ceramic tiles . it was built in 1929 by josé espelious and muñoz monasterio . the first bullfight was june 17 , 1931. it is also used for concerts . most fights start at 7 p.m. when the sun is not too hot . tickets are sold for 'sol ' or 'sombra ' . the 'sombra ' tickets for seats not in the sunlight cost more .
