Supreme Leader of Iran     the supreme leader of iran is the head of state of the islamic republic of iran . the supreme leader is also the highest ranking islamic cleric in the country . the position of `` supreme leader '' was created after the islamic revolution of 1979 in iran . the supreme leader is more powerful than the president of iran and chooses the heads of many powerful posts in the military , the government , and the judiciary . imam khomeini and imam khamenei have become supreme leader of the islamic republic so far . president of iran
