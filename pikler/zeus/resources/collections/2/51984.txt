Alex Lifeson     alex lifeson , oc ( born aleksandar živojinović ; august 27 , 1953 ) is a canadian musician . he is best known as the guitarist and co-founder for the canadian rock band rush . lifeson has been a member of the band since 1968. in rush , lifeson plays electric and acoustic guitars . he also plays other instruments including : mandola , mandolin and bouzouki . along with his music career , lifeson is part owner of a toronto restaurant called `` the orbit room . '' he is also a licensed airplane pilot .
