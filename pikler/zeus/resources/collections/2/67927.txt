Wit     wit is a form of intellectual humour . a wit is someone skilled in making witty remarks . a witty remark is an incisive observation , put with humor and said in the perfect moment . forms of wit include : the quip and the repartee . witty remarks may be intentionally cruel , and perhaps more ingenious than funny . a quip is an observation or saying that has some wit but perhaps descends into sarcasm , or otherwise is short of point ; a witticism also suggests the diminutive . repartee is the wit of the quick answer . ( wilde : `` i wish i 'd said that . '' whistler : `` you will , oscar , you will '' . )
