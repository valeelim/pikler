Geddy Lee     geddy lee ( born gary lee weinrib on july 29 , 1953 in toronto , ontario ) is the canadian bass guitarist , singer , and synth player of the rock band rush . he is known for melodic basslines and high pitched singing . in the 1970s , geddy lee mainly used rickenbacker 4001 basses . for the grace under pressure album he used steinberger basses . from the mid-80 's to mid-90 's he used wal basses . currently , he uses fender jazz basses .
