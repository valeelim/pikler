Valence, Drôme    valence , drôme valence is a city in the south of france , along the rhône river . it is in the auvergne-rhône-alpes region in the drôme department in france . valence is twinned with : - asti , italy , since 1966 - biberach an der riss , germany , since 1967 - clacton-on-sea , united kingdom , since 1969 - ijevan , armenia , since 1996 - gedera , israel , since 1997
