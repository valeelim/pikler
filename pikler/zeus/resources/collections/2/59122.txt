Puerta de Alcalá     the puerta de alcalá ( alcalá gate ) is a monument in the plaza de la independencia ( `` independence square '' ) in madrid , spain . francisco sabatini created the monument in 1778 for carlos iii when he arrived in madrid . it is the gateway to the court . it is built of granite and limestone . it has three big arches that have lion 's heads on top made by robert michel . there are two smaller arches on the sides . at the very top of the monument , there are angels , trophies and the coat of arms which were all made by francisco gutiérrez . other websites . - a site with some more information about the monument - puerta de alcalá at google maps - high resolution photos
