500 Series Shinkansen     the 500 series shinkansen is a high-speed train in japan . they run on the shinkansen lines . they were retired from the primary `` nozomi '' service in 2010. they now run the all-stations `` kodama '' service between shin-osaka and hakata . all 500 series have 16 cars . each train cost about 5 billion yen . only nine were built .
