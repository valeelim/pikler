HED meteorite     the hed meteorites are a grouping of achondrite meteorite types , the : - `` howardites '' - `` eucrites '' - `` diogenites '' - `` dunite '' - `` olivine diogenites '' these are all thought to have originated from the crust of the asteroid 4 vesta , their differences being due to different geologic histories of the parent rock . their crystallization ages have been determined to be between 4.43 and 4.55 billion years from radioisotope ratios .
