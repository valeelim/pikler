Delaware River     the delaware river is a large river estuary in the northeast united states . it is about 280 miles ( 450 km ) long . it begins in the catskill mountains in southeast new york state and ends at the atlantic ocean at delaware bay . it forms the border or part of the border between the states of pennsylvania and new york , pennsylvania and new jersey and also between delaware and new jersey . the cities of trenton , new jersey , and philadelphia , pennsylvania are on the delaware river . another name for this river is the south river . it is named for thomas west , 3rd lord de la warr , a colonial governor of virginia . other links . - a historical map made in 1639 of the delaware river
