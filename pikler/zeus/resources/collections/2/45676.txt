Ya pear     the ya pear or chinese white pear is a type of pear . it is found in northern china , where it is grown for food . these pears are very crunchy , juicy , and fragrant . they have a skin that is pale white to light yellow , and the inside is white . ya pears are sold everywhere in china , and can also be found in shops outside china . outside china , they are often called asian pears or chinese pears .
