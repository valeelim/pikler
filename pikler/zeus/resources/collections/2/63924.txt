Carla Garapedian     carla garapedian is an american movie director . she was the anchor of bbc world news . movies directed by garapedian include : - `` children of the secret state '' ( 2000 ) , about north korea - `` lifting the veil '' ( 2002 ) , an emmy award-winning movie about women in afghanistan - `` screamers '' ( 2007 ) , about genocide
