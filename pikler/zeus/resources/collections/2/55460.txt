Destroyer (album)    destroyer ( album ) destroyer is the fourth studio album and fifth album overall by the american hard rock/heavy metal band kiss . it was released on march 15 , 1976. track listing . - `` detroit rock city '' - `` king of the night time world '' - `` god of thunder '' - `` great expectations '' - `` flaming youth '' - `` sweet pain '' - `` shout it out loud '' - `` beth '' - `` do you love me ''
