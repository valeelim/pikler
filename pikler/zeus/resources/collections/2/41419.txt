Jedi     in the fictional `` star wars '' universe , the jedi or jedi knights are members of an old order , who use a power they call the force . the jedi use the force to have magic-like powers . their typical weapon is the lightsaber , a sword-like weapon with an energy blade . the jedi try to lead a good and virtuous life , serving others . they keep and defend the peace of the galaxy . the opposite of the jedi are the sith , who follow the dark side of the force ( using the force for evil ) .
