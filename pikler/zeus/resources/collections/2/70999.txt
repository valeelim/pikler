Anagram     an anagram is a puzzle where the player has to move all the letters in a word to make a new word . an example would be `` the morse code '' and `` here comes dots '' . anagrams are often used in fiction when a character wants to use a different name . for example , the `` harry potter '' character named tom marvolo riddle made an anagram based on his name , `` i am lord voldemort '' , and began calling himself `` lord voldermort '' .
