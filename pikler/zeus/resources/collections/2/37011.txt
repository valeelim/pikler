Radolfzell     radolfzell at the bodensee is northwest a city on the west bank of bodensee of about 18 km from konstanz . it is after konstanz and singen ( hohentwiel ) the third biggest city of the district konstanz . since 1 january 1975 radolfzell is large district town . following cities and municipalities border on the city radolfzell at the bodensee . they are called in the clockwise direction beginning in the southwest : moos ( at the bodensee ) , singen ( hohentwiel ) , steisslingen , orsingen , nenzingen ( all district konstanz ) . other websites . - homepage of radolfszell
