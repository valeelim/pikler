Khaqani Shirvani     afzal ad- din badil ibrahim ibn ali nadjar khaqani shirvani ( 1120-1190 ) ( ) or khaghani shirvani was a persian poet . his most famous poem is `` the arch of madain '' ( ) . he wrote the poem book `` tohfat-ul iraqein '' ( in persian : تحفه العراقين meaning a gift from the two iraqs ) in his journey to mesopotamia . the shirvanshah ordered his officers to imprison him . khaghani was in prison for nearly five years . in prison , he wrote the poems called habsiyye ( , meaning `` the prison book '' ) . after he was freed , he moved to tabriz . khaghani died in tabriz .
