Tackey and Tsubasa     tackey & tsubasa ( タッキー & 翼 ) is a japanese pop duo from johnny & associates . its two members are hideaki takizawa ( japanese : 滝沢秀明 , `` takizawa hideaki '' ) and tsubasa imai ( 今井翼 , `` imai tsubasa '' ) . they are known for their japanese television dramas and they have been successful with numerous hit singles , especially `` kamen '' and `` venus '' . their records are produced by avex trax . discography . singles . 1 .
