Earl Marshal     the earl marshal is a member of the british royal household , and is in charge of organising royal ceremonies and processions . the earl marshal is not a member of the college of arms , but he must give his permission before the college of arms can issue a coat of arms . the earl marshal is always the duke of norfolk , and because he is earl marshal , the duke of norfolk is a member of the house of lords , without being elected by the other hereditary peers .
