Melleruds IF     melleruds if is an association football club in mellerud in sweden , established on 29 july 1908. they are in the swedish 2nd division : västra götaland . they have had some players who have moved into england , most notably wigan athletic f.c . player andreas johansson . johansson played for melleruds between 1993 and 1995. other websites . - official website
