Biosphere     the biosphere includes everything living on earth . currently the biosphere has a biomass ( or amount of living things ) at around 1900 gigatonnes of carbon . it is not certain exactly how thick the biosphere is , though scientists predict that it is around 12,500 meters . the biosphere extends to the upper areas of the atmosphere , including birds and insects . in a general sense , biospheres are any closed , self-regulating systems containing ecosystems . this includes artificial biospheres such as biosphere 2 and bios-3 , and potentially ones on other planets or moons . other page . - biosphere reserve - project by unesco
