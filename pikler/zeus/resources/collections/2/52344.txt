Landsberg Prison     the landsberg prison was built around 1910 on the town 's western edge . its most noted prisoner was adolf hitler . hitler was there in 1924 after being found guilty of treason for his part in the beer hall putsch . it was during his time at landsberg that hitler wrote his book `` mein kampf '' ( `` my struggle '' ) with help from rudolf hess . during the occupation of germany by the allies after world war ii , the us army named the prison as `` war criminal prison no . 1 '' . they held many nazi war criminals there . executions of war criminals were also done at landsberg . the last of these executions happened on june 8 , 1951. these were the last executions in the federal republic of germany , then commonly known as west germany . other pages . - beer hall putsch other websites . - landsberg `` the hitlertown '' : the city of youth
