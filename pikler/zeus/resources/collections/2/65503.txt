High and Mighty Color     high and mighty color ( ハイ・アンド・マイティ・カラー , `` hai ando maiti karā '' ) is a japanese rock band signed to sony music entertainment japan . their single `` ichirinno hana '' was used as an op song for the anime show , `` bleach '' . members . - maki – vocals - yuusuke – vocals - kazuto - guitar - meg – guitar - mackaz – bass - sassy – drums awards . - 47th japanese record grand prix rookie of the year award for 2005 other websites . - official website - smej artist page - high and mighty color at oricon.co.jp
