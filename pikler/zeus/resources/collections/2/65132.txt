Kisai Marvazi     abul hasan abu ishaq kasa'i marvazi ( 953 - 10th century ) ( ) or kasai is a persian poet . he was born in marv . he was a poet in the court of mahmud ghaznavi . he converted to shia islam . he has praised ali ibn abi talib many times in his poems . all of his poems are in persian . his poems are collected in a diwan .
