Punjab region     punjab is a region in south asia . it is divided by the radcliffe line : some parts of it are in islamic republic of pakistan , and others are in the republic of india . it is in a plain , with the river indus flowing through pakistan . the soil is very fertile . it lives from agriculture . main religions in the region are islam , sikhism and hinduism . related pages . - punjab - doab ( india ) other websites . - -citizendium
