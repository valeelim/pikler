Cambuslang railway station     cambuslang railway station is a train station for the scottish town of cambuslang , in south lanarkshire ( near glasgow . ) the station is owned and managed by first scotrail . there is a train every ten minutes . trains come more often at busy times of the day . the station has a ticket office at the top , with a bridge across the track and two ramps down to each platform . other websites . - train times and station information for cambuslang from the national rail website . - map and aerial photo from multimap.com - first scotrail : official website - spt official website
