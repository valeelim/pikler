Megadeth     megadeth is a heavy metal band from the united states . the band was created by dave mustaine in 1983 after he was fired from metallica . megadeth is a member of `` the big four '' in thrash metal along with metallica , slayer and anthrax . megadeth is considered to be one of the creators of thrash metal . they have released 14 albums of music . albums . - 1985 : `` killing is my business ... and business is good !
