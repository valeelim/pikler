Tammy Faye Messner     tamara `` tammy '' faye messner ( march 7 , 1942 - july 20 , 2007 ) was an american christian singer , evangelist , entrepreneur , author , talk show host , and television personality . she was born in international falls , minnesota . messner was a contestant in the second season of `` the surreal life '' . messner died of colon cancer in loch lloyd , missouri .
