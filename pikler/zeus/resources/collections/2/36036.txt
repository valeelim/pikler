Gestation     gestation is the carrying of an embryo or fetus inside a female viviparous animal , until its birth . mammals can have more than one embryo or fetus at the same time . the time between fertilization of an ovum and the birth of the young is called the gestation period . humans have a gestation period of around 37 weeks or about 9 months and 1 week . syrian hamsters have a gestation period of 16 days . that is believed to be the shortest gestation period .
