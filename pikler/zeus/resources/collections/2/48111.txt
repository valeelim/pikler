Old English     the old english language , often called anglo-saxon , was spoken in england from 450 to 1100 . it was spoken by the anglo-saxons who came to england from what is now germany and denmark . old english is very different from modern english ; it has many more germanic words . it was rarely written down , and when written it was in runes . this is an alphabet completely different from the latin alphabet used for english today . old english grammar is difficult , and close to old german . latin was used by churchmen like the venerable bede . old english gradually turned into middle english after the norman conquest of 1066 . `` beowulf '' is written in old english in an alphabetic scrip
