Enkephalin     enkephalins were the first endorphins that were discovered in the brain . they are found in many parts of the brain , specially in the basal ganglia . they act as `` natural pain killers '' . enkephalins are small proteins . they consist of five amino acids in each of their molecules . the last amino acid is either leucine ( leu ) or methionine ( met ) . enkephalin is a brain neurotransmitter that is formed when tyrocine , glycine , glycine , phenylalanine , and methionine through a condensation reaction or dehydration synthesis .
