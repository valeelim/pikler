Krauchthal     krauchthal is a village in the canton of bern . it is about halfway between burgdorf and bern , about 15 km northeast of the city of bern . the commune of krauchthal is made of the villages krauchthal ( with hub and dieterswald ) , and hettiswil . the name of the settlements was mentioned during the middle ages . krauchthal in 1208 , hettiswil in 1107 , hub and dieterswald in 1326 , thorberg in 1196. in 2006 , 2345 people lived there . in thorberg , there is a castle . this has been converted to a prison . other websites . - website of the commune of krauchthal ( german )
