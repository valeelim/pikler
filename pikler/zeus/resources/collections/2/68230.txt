International Society for Krishna Consciousness     the international society for krishna consciousness ( iskcon ) is a newer religious movement based on the original vedic culture of ancient india it is also known as 'the hare krishna ' movement . it was founded in 1966 in new york city by a.c. bhaktivedanta swami prabhupada . the organization 's core beliefs are based on three scriptures , bhagavad gita , srimad bhagavatam and the caitanya-caritamrita .
