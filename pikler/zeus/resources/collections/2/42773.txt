Niobium     niobium is a chemical element . it is sometimes named columbium . it has the chemical symbol nb . it has the atomic number 41. it is a rare metal . niobium is soft and grey . it is ductile . in chemistry it is placed in a group of metal elements named the transition metals . niobium was discovered in a variety of a mineral named columbite ( now called niobite ) . niobite is an ore of niobium . niobium is used in alloys . the most important alloys are used to make special steels and strong welded joints .
