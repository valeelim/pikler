Scottie Pippen     scottie pippen ( born september 25 , 1965 ) is a retired american basketball player . he played for the chicago bulls of the national basketball association ( nba ) for most of his career and won six championships with the bulls , although he also played with charles barkley in 1999 for the houston rockets and the portland trail blazers near the end of his career . pippen went by scott pippen early in his nba career . later on he earned the nicknames pip , robin ( to michael jordan 's batman ) and later batman ( jordan was then called superman ) . other websites . - nba legends profile
