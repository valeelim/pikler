Jèrriais     jèrriais is a language that is spoken in jersey . some people call it jersey french . just over 2000 people speak it . it is a norman language . 3 % of the people in jersey speak it , and around 15 % can understand it . in the 19th century , almost everyone in jersey could speak jèrriais . then , people stopped learning it , and it nearly became extinct . these days , lots of people are trying to get more people to speak the language . now , signposts and road signs on jersey are written in jèrriais and english .
