Greenwich Mean Time     greenwich mean time is an internationally standard time format . it is the main time zone in several countries , including the united kingdom and portugal . it is similar to utc , however there are some small differences between the two . including that the greenwich uses a straight line to signal the time zone , while in the utc you can put which time zone you want , even if it does not need this time zone . one example is china , which uses one same time zone , meaning that in some places like beijing 12:00 pm can be 3:00pm on the western part of the country .
