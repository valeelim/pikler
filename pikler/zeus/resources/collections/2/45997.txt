WWE     world wrestling entertainment , inc. , doing business as wwe , is a big company that produces professional wrestling shows . it is now the most popular company in the wrestling business . vince j. mcmahon started the company in 1963. his son , vince k. mcmahon is currently the chairman and ceo of the company and runs the company along with his daughter ￼￼stephanie￼￼ mcmahon and her husband paul levesque , better known as triple h. the company used to be known as the world wrestling federation or the wwf . they changed their name to world wrestling entertainment after a lawsuit filed by the world wide fund for nature , formerly known as the world wildlife fund , which uses the `` wwf '' initials in the united states . in 2011 , the company rebranded itself as wwe , although its legal name is still world wrestling entertainment . at the top of wwe 's championship hierarchy is the wwe universal championship on wwe raw and the wwe championship on wwe smackdown . there are two championships available for female wrestlers : the raw women 's championship and the smackdown women 's championship .
