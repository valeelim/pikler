ITV     independent television ( generally known as itv but also as itv network or channel 3 ) is a public broadcasting service in the united kingdom . it is a television network of british broadcasters . it was set up in 1955 by the independent television authority ( ita ) to provide competition to the bbc . itv was originally set up as one network of television stations and stayed that way until 2002. then they were split into : itv 1 , itv2 , itv3 and itv4 . these channels are also available in high definition . listed as they were originally named and as they are known now : - anglia television ( now known as 'itv anglia ' , broadcasting to the east of england ) - carlton central ( now known as 'itv central ' , broadcasting to the center of england ) - carlton / london weekend television ( now known as 'itv london ' , broadcasting to london ) - meridian broadcasting ( now known as 'itv meridian ' , broadcasting to the south west of england ) - tynee tees television ( now known as 'itv tynee tees ' , broadcasting to the north east )
