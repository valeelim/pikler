Tony Iommi     frank anthony `` tony '' iommi ( born 19 february 1948 , in birmingham , england ) is an english guitarist and songwriter . he is most famous for starting the heavy metal band black sabbath . he is the only person who has stayed a member of the band since it began in 1968. in 2003 , iommi was listed at number 86 in rolling stone magazine 's `` 100 greatest guitarists of all time '' issue . along with jimmy page of led zeppelin , iommi is oft considered one of the greatest riff writers of all time . iommi used several riffs in black sabbath 's songs . in 2006 tony iommi was inducted into the rock and roll hall of fame .
