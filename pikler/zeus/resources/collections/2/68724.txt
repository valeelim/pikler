Kerry Katona     kerry jayne elizabeth katona ( born 6 september 1980 in warrington , cheshire , england ) is an english television presenter , magazine columnist , actress and former pop singer with girl group atomic kitten . she was a contestant on the sixth series of `` dancing on ice in 2011. she finished in second place in the 2011 uk series of celebrity big brother . in 2006 , katona was diagnosed with bipolar disorder .
