Ceiling     a ceiling is the surface that covers the upper limit of a room . it is not generally considered a structural element , as it does not hold anything up . it simply conceals the underside of the floor or roof above it . there are many different types of ceilings in architecture . in religious buildings , the ceilings are often decorated with frescos , mosaic tiles and other surface treatments . perhaps the most famous example is the ceiling of the sistine chapel , painted by michelangelo . related pages . - hammerbeam roof other websites . - virtual museum treatise on ceiling construction
