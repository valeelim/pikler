Peter, Paul and Mary    peter , paul and mary peter , paul and mary were an american folk music group , most famous during the 1960s . the members are peter yarrow , paul ( noel ) stookey and mary travers . their songs include `` if i had a hammer '' ( also called `` the hammer song '' ) , `` puff , the magic dragon '' , `` leavin ' on a jet plane '' written by john denver , and `` blowin ' in the wind '' , written by bob dylan . they recorded other dylan songs , helping his early career . they made records , toured to perform , and appeared on television shows , including `` the smothers brothers comedy hour '' . peter , paul and mary disbanded in 1970 , but reunited later , made tours and recorded new music . mary travers died on september 16 , 2009 , after chemotherapy treatments for leukemia .
