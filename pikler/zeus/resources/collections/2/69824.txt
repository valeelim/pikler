Fethiye     fethiye is a city and district in the mugla province in turkey . it is on the `` turquoise coast '' . 59,425 people live there . there is a big harbor in the town and a marina . there are no beaches in fethiye town , but there are many nice beaches around oludeniz , a nearby town . there is a number of different places to stay including hotels . the ancient name for the town was telmessos . there are a number of historic sites including rock tombs . these were dug into cliffs by the lycians . the tomb of amnytas was built in the 4th century bc . next to the harbor is a castle built by the knights of rhodes . in nearby villages there are also many historic sites including a temple to apollo at letoon , which is a world heritage site . other websites . - all about fethiye
