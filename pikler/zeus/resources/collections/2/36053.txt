Suva     suva is the capital city of fiji . it is on the southeast coast of the island of viti levu . suva became the capital of fiji in 1877. it was changed from the settlement of kaivalagi on the island of ovalau because that settlement had no space left to grow . kaivalangi had been established by the europeans . in 1996 , 77,366 people lived in suva . including independent suburbs , 167,975 people lived in the greater suva city area . other websites . - suva city council ( official website )
