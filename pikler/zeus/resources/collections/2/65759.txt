Ammonium sulfide     ammonium sulfide , also known as the `` stink bomb '' is made up of a solution of ammonium sulfide in water , sealed inside a glass ampoule ( a small bottle-like container used to store a liquid securely ) . when the ampoule is broken , ammonia and hydrogen sulfide gases are released from the solution . these chemicals have a very strong and unpleasant smell . ammonium sulfide is a very toxic chemical . it is also explosive and highly flammable . it is added to cigarettes .
