Michelle Trachtenberg     michelle christine trachtenberg ( born october 11 , 1985 ) is an american television and movie actress . she is best known for her role as dawn summers from the television series `` buffy the vampire slayer '' . trachtenberg was born in new york city , new york . she is of russian jewish descent on her mother 's side of the family . trachtenberg has been acting since 1988 at the age of four . she has acted in several television shows and many movies .
