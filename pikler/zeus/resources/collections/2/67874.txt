Willow     willows are a family of trees and shrubs which have many differences in size and type of growth , but are very much alike in other respects . there are about 350 species of this plant in all the world , usually found on moist soils in cooler zones in the northern hemisphere . many hybrids are known , both naturally occurring and in cultivation , because willows are very fertile between their own species . some smaller species may also be known by the common names `` osier '' and `` sallow '' ; the last name comes from the same word as the latin `` salix '' . other websites . - salix alba at plants for a future - `` salix purpurea '' at plants for a future - 1911 encyclop√¶dia britannica - `` salix caroliniana '' images at bioimages.vanderbilt.edu - `` salix nigra '' images at bioimages.vanderbilt.edu - `` salix humboldtiana '' or chilean willow images
