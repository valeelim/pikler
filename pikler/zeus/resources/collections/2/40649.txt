Crystal     a crystal is a solid whose molecules ( or atoms ) are arranged in a repeating pattern . crystals are made from fluids by phase change . usually the fluid is a liquid but snow is crystals precipitated from air . crystallization can be by freezing from a simple fluid or from a solution . sometimes heat and pressure needs to be applied to a substance for crystals to form . diamond is a carbon crystal formed under heat and pressure . crystals are found naturally or can be made artificially . different substances form different types of crystals . the study of the various kinds is crystallography . well-known substances that form crystals are table salt ( whose crystals are cubes ) , and quartz . other websites . - crystal -citizendium
