Disc jockey     a disc jockey ( also called a dj or deejay ) is a person who plays recorded pop or dance music for dancers or listeners and introduces the names of the songs using a microphone . there are several types of djs . a radio dj plays music files ( like mp3 ) or recorded cds on a radio station and announces the names of the songs . a club dj plays music files or recorded cds over a pa system ( an amplifier and loudspeakers ) at a nightclub , rave , or disco . a hip hop dj uses turntables and vinyl records to do scratching and make music while other hip hop musicians do rapping ( rhythmic type of singing ) . reggae djs play recordings of rhythm instruments and then rap or `` toast '' ( made-up chanting and boasting ) with a microphone .
