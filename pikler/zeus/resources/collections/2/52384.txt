Rhône-Alpes     rhône-alpes is a former administrative region of france . it is now part of the administrative region of auvergne-rhône-alpes . in 2010 , about six million people lived in the region . the capital was lyon . grenoble and saint-étienne are also important cities in the region . the departments in the region were : - ain - ardèche - drôme - isère - loire - rhône - savoie - haute-savoie the rhône-alpes region hosted the winter olympics three times ; in 1924 at chamonix , 1968 at grenoble , and 1992 at albertville .
