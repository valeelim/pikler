Jejunum     the jejunum is a section of the small intestine between the duodenum and the ileum . it is between 1 and 2 meters long.the term `` jejunum '' derives from the latin `` jejunus , '' which means `` empty of food , '' `` meager , '' or `` hungry . '' the ancient greeks noticed at death that this part of the intestine was always empty of food . hence , the name the jejunum . the latin `` jejunus '' also gave rise to `` jejune '' means lacking in nutritive value and devoid of substance , significance or interest , that is dull . a jejune argument is one that is empty ( like the jejunum ) and totally devoid of interest . famous quote ... '' have you ever been punched in the jejunum ? ''
