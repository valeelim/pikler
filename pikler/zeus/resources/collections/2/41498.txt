Gilbertville, Ontario    gilbertville , ontario gilbertville is a hamlet in norfolk county , ontario , canada that is in between delhi and the historical hamlet of pine grove . there is a small grocery store , a tractor shop , a car repair shop , and farmland to the south . in addition to all this , gilbertville is also the site of a former army base and was founded by the gilbert family in the 19th century as a place to rest horses when travelling to and from the towns of simcoe and delhi .
