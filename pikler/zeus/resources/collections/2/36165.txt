Ichthyology     ichthyology is the scientific study of fish . the term ichthyology which is derieved from following two `` greek '' word 'ichthyus ' means fish and 'logos ' means knowledge . it includes hagfish and lampreys ( jawless fish ) , cartilagenous fish ( such as sharks , skates and rays ) ( chondrichthyes ) and bony fishes ( osteichthyes ) . a person who studies fish is called an `` ichthyologist '' .
