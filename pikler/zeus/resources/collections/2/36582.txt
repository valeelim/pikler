Odd-toed ungulate     odd-toed ungulates are mammals of the order perissodactyla . odd-toed ungulates have an uneven number of toes : horses have only one toe , rhinoceros have three toes , and tapirs have four toes on the front feet and three toes on the hind feet . taxonomy . - order perissodactyla - family rhinocerotidae - family tapiridae - family equidae
