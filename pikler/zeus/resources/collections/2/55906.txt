AWD-Arena     the hdi-arena is a football stadium in hanover , a city in north germany . it is the home stadium of the football club hannover 96. the hdi-arena was built from 1952 to 1954 and called `` niedersachsenstadion '' ( stadium of lower saxony ) and later `` awd-arena '' . lower saxony is a german federal state and hanover is its capital . originally the niedersachsenstadium was large enough to hold about 80.000 people . it was changed for international football championships in 1974 and 1988 , especially changing standing room into seats . in 2003 , it became changed to be used only for football and the name was sold to the german company of awd . today it can hold 49.000 people . in 2006 , the football world championships were played there .
