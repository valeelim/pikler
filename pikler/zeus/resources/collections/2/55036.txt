Guava     guavas are plants in the genus `` psidium '' of the family myrtaceae . there are about 100 species of tropical shrubs and small trees in the genus . they are native to mexico , the caribbean , central america and the northern part of south america . now they are found in all the tropical , and in some subtropical , regions because they are edible fruits . guava are also a kind of berry fruit on those plants . there are many kinds of guavas . the most common guava is the apple guava ( `` psidium guajava '' ) : it is so common that the word 'guava ' usually refers to this species . red guavas are called maroonguava . in 100 g of guava are 200 mg of vitamin c , which means that guavas have five times more vitamin c than oranges .
