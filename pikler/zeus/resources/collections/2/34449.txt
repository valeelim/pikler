Mud     mud is a mixture of soil ( sometimes called dirt ) and water . some animals like worms , frogs , snails , clams , and crayfish live in mud . pigs and elephants use it to cool themselves in hot weather . some people enjoy making mud pies out of mud . mud is a type of soil that is also called `` clay soil '' . others include the sandy soil , or sand , and garden soil . wet mud has a soft wet texture . young children sometimes play in puddles of mud . mud is usually brown .
