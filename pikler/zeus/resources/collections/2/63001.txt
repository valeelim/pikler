Boy band     a boy band is a type of musical group with three or more young male singers . usually , the singers are able to perform as dancers as well . most singers in boy bands do not play musical instruments . boy bands usually perform pop or r & b music . some of the most popular boy bands include the following : - the jackson 5 ( 1960s–70s ) - the temptations ( 1960s–present ) - one direction ( 2010s–present ) - 'n sync ( 1990s–2000s ) - new edition ( 1980s–present ) - new kids on the block ( 1980s–present ) related pages . - girl group
