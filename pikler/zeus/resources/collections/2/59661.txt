Afternoon     afternoon is the time of day from noon to 5:00 pm . the way people use the word is based on the person , and can have many meanings . - `` afternoon '' ( magazine ) ( アフタヌーン ) is a japanese manga magazine published by kodansha . - `` afternoon '' ( newspaper ) , a newspaper published in mumbai , india - `` afternoon '' ( play ) , an 1883 play by ouida - `` afternoon , a story '' , is a hypertext novel by michael joyce - afternoon records , a music record label
