Greater London     greater london is an administrative district . it covers london , england . about 7.5 million people live there . it is also one of the regions of england used by the government for various purposes , including administration and statistics . greater london is a conurbation , a city made from merging many smaller villages , towns and cities into a larger one . `` london '' is technically only the city of london , one of london 's 33 administrative districts . in order to avoid confusion , `` greater london '' is often used to mean the whole modern urban area centred on the city of london , and not just the city of london itself . when most people say london , they are referring to greater london .
