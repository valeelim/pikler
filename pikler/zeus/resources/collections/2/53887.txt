County Cork     county cork is the largest county in ireland in terms of size . almost half a million people live there . the leader of the irish war of independence , michael collins , was born in the town of clonakilty in county cork . he was killed in béal na mbláth in west cork . west cork is known for its beautiful scenery . the main towns in county cork are cork city , youghal , mallow , bandon , clonakilty , kinsale , blarney and cobh . it is nicknamed `` the rebel county '' . parts of the gaeltacht are in the county . other websites . - cork county council - guide to county cork for tourism & business
