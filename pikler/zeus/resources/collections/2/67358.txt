Baikal     baikal is a village in russia , irkutsk oblast , sliudanskyy region . the village is near lake baikal and setted in left coast of river angara . in the right coast is listvianka . the administrative centre irkutsk is 60 kilometers north . there is an automobile ferry to listvianka . in 2005 , there were 400 people living in baikal village . in ussr times there were about 10000 humans .
