François Truffaut     françois roland truffaut ( 6 february 1932 – 21 october 1984 ) was a french movie director , producer , writer , actor and critic . he was very popular in the 1960s for such noted movies as `` the 400 blows '' , `` jules and jim '' , `` stolen kisses '' , `` the wild child '' and others . truffaut was born in paris and died of brain cancer in neuilly-sur-seine , hauts-de-seine .
