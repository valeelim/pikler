Ptah     ptah was the ancient egyptian god of craftsmen and architects . ptah is named in the turin king list as the first of the eight legendary god-kings of egypt . in art , he is shown as a hairless , bearded mummified man , often wearing a skull cap , holding a djed or other large tool ( which he is sometimes shown using during mummification ceremonies ) . he was believed to be married to sekhmet . ptah was the local god of memphis , one of the ancient capitals of egypt . he is the parson of craftsmen , since it is believed that he invented the arts .
