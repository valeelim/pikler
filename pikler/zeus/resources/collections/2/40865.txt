Kissing, Germany    kissing , germany kissing is a municipality in aichach-friedberg district bavaria , germany . it is 10 km south of augsburg and has about 11.500 inhabitants ( as of the year 2003 ) . kissing was first mentioned in 1050 as `` chissingin '' . in even earlier times , around 500 , there was a thing hill nearby , which was later swept away by the floods of the river lech . the surname kissinger ( as in henry kissinger ) means inhabitant of bad kissingen .
