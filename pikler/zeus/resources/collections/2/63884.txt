Pontic Greeks     pontic greeks , pontian greeks , pontians or greeks of pontus ( , ) can refer to greeks specifically from the area of pontus in the region of the former empire of trebizond on the black sea coast of eastern turkey , or in other cases more generally all greeks from the shores of the black sea or the pontus . greeks from trabzon traditionally speak pontic greek . the terms pontic and pontian can be used interchangeably . related pages . - pontic greek genocide
