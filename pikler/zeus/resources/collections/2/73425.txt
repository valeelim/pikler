Melk Abbey     melk abbey or stift melk is a benedictine abbey . it lies on the danube , near the city of melk . it is located in lower austria . it is one of the distinguishing features of a region known as wachau . it is an unesco world heritage site . the abbey was almost totally destroyed in a fire , on 14 august 1297. it was rebuilt in baroque style at the beginning of the 18th century .
