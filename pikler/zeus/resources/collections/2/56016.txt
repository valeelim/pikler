Dirty Deeds Done Dirt Cheap     dirty deeds done dirt cheap is a studio album by the australian hard rock band ac/dc . it was released in november 1976 in europe and delayed for release in usa until 1981. track listing . - dirty deeds done dirt cheap - love at first feel - big balls - rocker - problem child - there 's gon na be some rockin ' - ai n't no fun ( waiting round to be a millionaire ) - ride on - sqeealer
