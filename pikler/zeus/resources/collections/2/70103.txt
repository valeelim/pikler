Fluoxetine     fluoxetine hydrochloride is an antidepressant medication . it is used as a drug against various illnesses , like depression , obsessive-compulsive disorder , panic disorder , and other illnesses . fluoxetine is a one of a group of antidepressants called selective serotonin reuptake inhibitors ( ssris ) . it is commonly known as `` prozac '' . there are newer drugs available to treat these conditions . nevertheless , it remains very popular . over 23.1 million prescriptions for fluoxetine were filled in the united states in 2006. this makes it the third most prescribed antidepressant .
