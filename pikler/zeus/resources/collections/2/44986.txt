Super Nintendo Entertainment System     the super nintendo entertainment system ( often shortened to `` snes '' or `` super nes '' ) is a 16-bit video game console created by nintendo . it was called the super famicom in japan , and the super comboy in south korea . the console can display 2d graphics , with some limited support for 3d graphics with the super fx chip . super nes games are loaded from a cartridge . the major competitor for the super nintendo was the sega genesis/mega drive , but it also competed with the panasonic 3d0 . ... notable games . - `` super mario world '' - `` super castlevania iv '' - `` super metroid '' - `` donkey kong country '' - `` earthbound ''
