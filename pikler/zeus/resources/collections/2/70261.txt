Cable     a communication cable is made of many wires or optical fibers twisted together to make a larger wire . each of these wires may be covered . some cables contain both electrical wires and optical fibers . there are many kinds of cables , including coaxial cables which block out radio or magnetic interference from other wires or cables , by having one wire inside an insulated tube surrounded by a second wire , acting as a metal tube along the same axis . electrical cables are usually made of copper , because electricity moves well through copper , but it is not as expensive as silver or other better conductors . cables are sometimes made of aluminium because it is cheaper than copper . the word `` cable '' is also used as ellipsis , or a short form , for `` cable tv '' . wire ropes are a common type of cable . a cable car is a trolley pulled by a cable . related pages . - cable television
