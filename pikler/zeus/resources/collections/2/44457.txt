Athlete's foot    athlete 's foot athlete 's foot is a condition where the foot is dry and cracked , which can be mildly painful . it was named athlete 's foot because it was common in people who play sports or do exercise . the infection can be spread to other areas of the body , such as the armpits , knees and elbows . athlete 's foot causes scaling , flaking , itching , blisters and cracked skin . it can be cured by using antibiotics .
