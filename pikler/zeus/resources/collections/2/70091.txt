Juan Ignacio Molina     juan ignacio molina . born june 24 1740 ; died september 12 1829 ; chilean naturalist . he is usually referred to as abate molina ( form of abbott molina ) . he wrote `` compendio della storia geografica , naturale e civile del regno del cile '' ( 1776 ) , latter translated in english , french , german and spanish . the journal `` moliniana '' is dedicated in his honour . charles e. ronan : juan ignacio molina . the world 's window on chile .
