Shell (projectile)    shell ( projectile ) a shell is a type of projectile used in weapons . it is filled with explosive or other substances . it is similar to a bomb but is made to be shot out of a gun . shells are usually large caliber projectiles fired by artillery , armored fighting vehicles ( including tanks ) , and warships . shells usually have the shape of a cylinder but taper towards the front end to form a point . however , special shells may use other shapes .
