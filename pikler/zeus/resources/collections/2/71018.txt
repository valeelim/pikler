Tulip     tulip ( `` tulipa '' ) is a potflower plant . there is many cultivars and species of tulips . cultivars are used as ornamental plants . it grows in southern europe , north africa , and asia from anatolia and iran in the east to northeast of china and japan , indo asia . it is the national flower of afghanistan . the tulip is most associated with holland . other websites . - wikispecies - canadian national capital commission : the gift of tulips - greig 's tulip ( tulipa greigii ) in its original habitat in kazakhstan - elegant-tulip-bulbs.com : information about 3700 tulip names - tulip photos - tulip species
