James L. Brooks     james l. brooks ( born may 9 , 1940 ) is a three-time academy award , nineteen-time emmy and golden globe-winning american producer , writer , and movie director . he is best known for creating american television programs such as `` the mary tyler moore show '' , `` the simpsons '' , `` rhoda '' and `` taxi '' . his best-known movie is `` terms of endearment '' , for which he received three academy awards in 1984. other websites . - gracie films website
