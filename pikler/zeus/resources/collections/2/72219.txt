1908 Summer Olympics     the 1908 summer olympics , officially known as the games of the iv olympiad , were held in london , united kingdom . the olympics were originally scheduled to be held in rome ; however , mount vesuvius erupted on 7 april 1906. the natural disaster caused a financial strain on the italian government . then the venue was changed to london . the costs of the games were covered by donations . a total of 22 nations sent athletes to compete at the london games . they were : - argentina - australasia - austria - belgium - bohemia - canada - denmark - finland - france - german empire - great britain - greece - hungary - italy - netherlands - norway - russian empire - union of south africa - sweden - switzerland - ottoman empire - united states related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - london 1908 at olympic.org
