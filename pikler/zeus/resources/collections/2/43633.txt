Dwarf (mythology)    dwarf ( mythology ) a dwarf is a short , humanlike creature from norse and germanic mythologies . they have been used in many fairy tales , fantasy , fiction and role-playing games . in some stories , dwarves are mean , living under bridges , and having a bad reputation for stealing treasure . one example is the norwegian troll . it is an ugly and foolish creature who tells lies . it causes problems everywhere it goes . that is how the internet troll got its name . in robert louis stevenson 's story , `` strange case of dr. jekyll and mr. hyde , '' edward hyde is described as `` a dwarf .
