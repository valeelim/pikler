Amadou Toumani Touré     general ( ret ) amadou toumani touré ( born november 4 , 1948 in mopti , mali ) is the president of mali . he took power from a military ruler , moussa traoré in 1991 , then he gave power to civilian ( non-military ) authorities in 1992. he won the presidential election in 2002. he won the next election easily in 2007 .
