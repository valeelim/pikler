Arroyo Barril, Samaná    arroyo barril , samaná arroyo barril is a municipal district of santa bárbara de samaná , samaná province in the dominican republic . it is on the south coast of the samaná peninsula , on the coast of samaná bay . it was made a municipal district on may 1 , 2003. in the last national census ( 2010 ) , the district had a total population of 11,007 : 5,591 men and 5,416 women . the urban population was 22 % of the total population . the most important economic activities of arroyo barril are the same of the province : agriculture , fishing and tourism . the arroyo barril airport is in this place ; it is a national airport with flights to other airports in the country .
