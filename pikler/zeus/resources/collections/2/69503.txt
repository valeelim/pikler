Toy Story 2     toy story 2 is a 1999 american computer-generated animation movie and a sequel to the 1995 film `` toy story '' . it is the third disney / pixar full-length movie and released to movie theaters by buena vista pictures . it is about toys that come alive when people are not around . woody is stolen by a toy collector so buzz lightyear and his friends save him . a sequel to this film , toy story 3 , released in 2010. soundtrack . 1. woody 's roundup by riders in the sky 1. hold on , i 'm comin ' by the neville brothers 1. wipe out by the surfaris 1. when she loved me by sarah mclachlan 1. pizza planet rocks ! by sum 41 1. you 've got a friend in me by lyle lovette
