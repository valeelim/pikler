Dracula     dracula is a horror novel written by the irish writer bram stoker . bram stoker published the novel in england in 1897. sir henry irving was an actor and friend of bram stoker . the character 'dracula ' may have been based in part on irving , and on vlad iii the impaler . the story in the novel happens in england and transylvania in 1893. the young solicitor ( a type of lawyer ) jonathan harker goes to the country transylvania to sell a house to a nobleman named dracula . he realizes that dracula is a vampire . dracula traps harker in his castle and goes to england , then bites the young woman lucy westenra and turns her into a vampire . then dracula bites jonathan 's wife mina . because of this the other characters in the book , including abraham von helsing and jonathan harker , try to stop dracula . most famous adaptations of dracula . - 1922 , `` '' ( `` nosferatu , a symphony of horror '' ) . first adaptation of the novel . directed by f. w. murnau . max schreck has the role of count orlok . the movie director could not get permission to use the actual story from stoker 's widow , who ordered many copies of the movie to be destroyed after its release .
