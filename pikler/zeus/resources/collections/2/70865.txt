Stoke-on-Trent     stoke-on-trent is a city in staffordshire in the west midlands region of england . it is also a unitary authority . stoke-on-trent is sometimes known to be the home of the pottery industry in the united kingdom and is commonly known as the potteries . the armitage shanks company has its headquarters here . the company makes porcelain products , especially toilets . the city is unusual as it is made-up of 6 towns - longton , fenton , stoke , hanley , burslem , tunstall . stoke on trent is known for - the supermarine spitfire , pottery , oatcakes , spark plug , sir stanley mathews , robbie williams , slash , arnold bennet , captain smith , scfc ( the second oldest professional football club founded in 1863 ) . other websites . - stoke-on-trent city council - bbc stoke website - the potteries - local information from `` the sentinel '' newspaper - staffordshire and stoke-on-trent archive service - exploring the potteries
