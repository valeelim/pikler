Mikoyan-Gurevich MiG-21     the mig-21 fishbed is an airplane made by mikoyan . it is a supersonic fighter , and first flew in 1956. it has a triangle wing , and at least 30 countries use or have used it . more than 10,000 have been made , which is more than any supersonic fighter ever . it fought in the vietnam war and is still being used today .
