Drastamat Kanayan     general drastamat kanayan ( may 31 , 1884 – march 8 , 1956 ) was an armenian politician , revolutionary , and general . he was the commander of the armenian legion of the wehrmacht , the armed forces of nazi germany and part of armenian national liberation movement . drastamat kanayan was born in iğdır , surmalu , russian empire ( present-day turkey ) in 1884 .
