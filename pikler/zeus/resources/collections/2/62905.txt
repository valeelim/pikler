Band Aid (band)    band aid ( band ) band aid was a british and irish charity music supergroup . it was started in 1984 by bob geldof and midge ure . the purpose was to raise money for famine relief in ethiopia by making the record `` do they know it 's christmas ? '' the single was very successful . it was number one on the music charts during christmas . two re-recordings of the song were done . they raised more money and also finished number one on the charts . band aid ii re-recorded the song in 1989 , and band aid 20 did the same in 2004. other websites . - registered charity no . 292199 ( band aid charitable trust )
