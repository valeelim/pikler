The Tracey Ullman Show     the tracey ullman show was a weekly american television variety show , hosted by british comedian and former pop singer tracey ullman . it debuted on april 5 , 1987 as the fox network 's second primetime series ( after `` married ... with children '' ) , and ran until may 26 , 1990. the show featured sketch comedy along with many musical numbers , featuring emmy award-winning choreography by paula abdul . it also produced the hugely successful spin-off , `` the simpsons '' . other websites . - tv.com entry - watch `` simpsons '' shorts - the `` dr. n ! godatu '' web page - the tracey ullman show episode guide
