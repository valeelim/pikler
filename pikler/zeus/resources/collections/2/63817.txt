Thomas Eastoe Abbott     thomas eastoe abbott ( 1786/1787|7 – 18 february 1854 ) was an english poet . he was born in east dereham , norfolk . he lived in darlington , where he worked at the royal free grammar school . he wrote many books like `` peace , a lyric poem '' ( 1814 ) ; `` resignation '' ( 1817 ) ; `` the triumph of christianity '' ( 1819 ) ; and `` the soldier 's friend '' ( 1828 ) . abbott died in darlington , county durham in 1854. other websites . - thompson cooper , `` abbott , thomas eastoe ( 1786/7–1854 ) '' , rev . megan a. stephan , oxford dictionary of national biography , oxford university press , 2004 , accessed 21 march 2007 )
