Mahdi     the mahdi or mehdi ( 'the rightly-guided one ' ) is islam 's messiah or savior . it is said that he and the prophet jesus will change the world for the better , bringing god in all hearts , before yaum al-qiyamah ( `` day of the resurrection '' ) . in particular , the sudanese cleric muhammed ahmed proclaimed himself as the mahdi , appointed by allah to free his country . he defeated the forces of the khedive of egypt and the british , only to die suddenly six months later . other websites . - who are mahdavis ? - the promised mehdi - syed mohammad almahdi almow'ood
