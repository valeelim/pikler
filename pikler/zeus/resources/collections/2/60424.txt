Donkey Kong     donkey kong is a nintendo video game character . he was first in a video game in 1981. he is a large gorilla who lives deep in the jungles of his very own island . since 1994 he has worn a red necktie with his initials `` dk '' in yellow , his only piece of clothing . he appears as both the hero and villain of the `` donkey kong '' games . appearances in video games . - `` donkey kong '' - `` mario kart 64 '' - `` super smash bros.
