Tachycardia     tachycardia is the fast beating of the heart . in adults , it refers to heart rates higher than 100 beats per minute . tachycardia may be a normal physiological response to stress , but depending on the cause and how healthy the patient is , tachycardia could be harmful and need medical treatment . in some cases , tachycardia can cause death . tachycardia can be harmful in two ways . first , when the heart beats too rapidly , it may perform inefficiently . second , the faster the heart beats , the more oxygen and nutrients the heart requires . this can be especially problematic for patients suffering from ischaemic heart disease .
