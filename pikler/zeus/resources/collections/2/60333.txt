Franz Grillparzer     franz grillparzer ( born vienna , 15 january 1791 - died vienna , 21 january 1872 ) was an austrian dramatist and poet . his dramas include three plays ( a “ trilogy ” ) called `` das goldene vliess '' ( `` the golden fleece '' ) . two other famous plays are `` des meeres und der liebe wellen '' ( `` the waves of the sea and of love '' ) and `` der traum , ein leben '' ( `` the dream , a life '' ) . although he said he was not interested in prose his short story `` der arme spielmann '' ( `` the poor minstrel '' ) is probably the best known of his works and one of the best short stories of the 19th century . grillparzer wrote the funeral speech for beethoven 's funeral in 1827. an austrian pastry is named after him : the grillparzertorte .
