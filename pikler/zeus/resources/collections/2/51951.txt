Ilham Aliyev     ilham heydar oglu aliyev ( born december 24 , 1961 ) is the president of azerbaijan and has been since 2003. ilham aliyev succeeded his father heydar aliyev who was president of azerbaijan from 1993 to 2003. on august 4 , 2003 , two months before the presidential elections , he was made prime minister by his father who was very ill and dying . in october he easily won the presidential elections and became president on october 31 , 2003. he also functions as the head of the new azerbaijan party . other websites . - the president of azerbaijan
