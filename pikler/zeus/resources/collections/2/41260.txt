Shampoo     shampoo is a beauty care product . it is used to remove natural oils from hair . it is like liquid soap , but it is made to wash hair . it is sold in stores , and many people use it to make their hair clean . after people use shampoo , they might use a conditioner , which makes the hair soft . together they have a good effect on a person 's hair . the people who make shampoo try to make it smell nice . they also try to make the shampoo healthier . in addition to shampoo for hair , there are body shampoos made to wash an entire body .
