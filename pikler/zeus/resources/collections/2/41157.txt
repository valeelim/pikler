Lee Redmond     lee redmond ( born february 2 , 1941 ) is a woman from the salt lake city , utah , united states . she is famous for having the longest fingernails in the world . she has not cut her fingernails since 1979. the combined length of her fingernails are 7 meters and 51 centimeters long ( 24 feet , 8 inches ) . she has an active life despite of her long fingernails . lee redmond is in the guinness book of world records in the category `` longest fingernails - female '' . on february 10 , 2009 , lee was in a car crash , and was thrown out of the car . she was badly hurt and all her fingernails were broken off . other websites . - guinness world records : longest fingernails - female .
