302 BC     events . - following their agreement to work together to defeat antigonus , seleucus invades asia minor from babylonia , while ptolemy attacks syria and lysimachus moves into the western part of asia minor . - docimus , the regent of phrygia , and phoenix , the strategos of lycia , desert antigonus . - the macedonian general , philetaerus , moves his allegiance from antigonus to antigonus ' rival , lysimachus . in return , lysimachus makes philetaerus guardian of the fortress of pergamum with its treasure of some 9,000 talents .
