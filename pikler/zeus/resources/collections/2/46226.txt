Samuel Alito     samuel anthony alito , jr. ( ; born april 1 , 1950 ) is an associate justice of the supreme court of the united states . he was nominated by president george w. bush and has served on the court since january 31 , 2006. alito was born to italian american parents . he served in the u.s. army reserves years before joining the supreme court . alito often favors a republican view of the law . the vote by the u.s. senate to make alito a judge was one of the closest in u.s. history , because many democrats did not want five republicans on the court . alito is from trenton , new jersey .
