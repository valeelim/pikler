Tsaagan     tsaagan meaning `` white '' is a dinosaur related to `` velociraptor '' . it is in the family dromaeosauridae . it lived in asia about 75 million years ago . it was discovered in the djadokhta formation of the late cretaceous of mongolia . when it was discovered , it was thought to be a species of `` velociraptor '' . after a cat-scan in 1998 , scientists found that it represented a new genus . in 2010 it was discovered that it was closely related to `` linheraptor '' . they are probably in the same genus .
