Human biology     human biology is an area of study that is part of biology , biological anthropology , and medicine which looks at humans ; it is closely related to primate biology , and a number of other areas . human biology research includes : - genetic differences across human populations , present and past - biological differences related to climate and other elements of the natural environment - finding the risk of degenerative disease and infectious disease for different populations of people - human growth and development there are not exact boundaries between human biology and regular medical research . however , human biology usually focuses more on health for an entire group of people , and on human evolution , adaptation , and population genetics rather than individual health . other websites . - human biology association - society for the study of human biology symposium series
