Saale     saale is the name of two rivers in germany : saale in thuringia and saxony-anhalt ( german : `` sächsische saale '' ) and the saale in northern bavaria ( german : `` fränkische saale '' ) . the larger , and better known sächsische saale springs out of the fichtelgebirge at 728 m and joins the elbe in saxony-asshalt . it is 413 km long .
