Jumpin' Jumpin'    jumpin ' jumpin ' `` jumpin ' , jumpin ' '' is a song recorded by destiny 's child . it was the fourth and final single from the group 's second album , `` the writing 's on the wall '' . the single achieved success in various charts . byron rittenhouse 's voice is on the song saying `` uh huh , i ai n't worried 'bout my girl a'ight '' in response to beyonce 's line `` i ai n't thinking 'bout my man tonight '' . two official urban remixes for this song exist . one , known as the nitro remix features rapper nitro , while the other is known as the so so def remix with lil ' bow wow , da brat and jermaine dupri . a video was filmed for the so so def remix later . also , appearing as a second half of the so so def remix on the this is the remix ( destiny 's child album ) , azza 's remix of jumpin ' , jumpin ' . this version was a newly recorded version with slower singing and kelly doing lead on chorus and high notes during break , and one of few remix songs from writing 's on the wall that farrah franklin and michelle are singing ; the other being the wmba version of the song .
