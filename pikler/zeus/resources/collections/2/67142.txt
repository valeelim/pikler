Burrowing parakeet     the burrowing parakeet ( cyanoliseus patagonus ) is a species of parrot in the psittacidae family . it is found in argentina , chile , falkland islands , south georgia and the south sandwich islands , and uruguay . it lives in subtropical or tropical dry shrubland , subtropical or tropical high-altitude shrubland , and pastureland . it is also known as the burrowing parrot . other websites . - birds of chile postage stamps
