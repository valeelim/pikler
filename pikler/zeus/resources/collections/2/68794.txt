Stonemasonry     stonemasonry is the craft of making things out of stone . people who do this are called stonemasons . their crafts have existed since the start of civilization . they have made important buildings and structures out of stone . this stone comes from the earth . stone has been used to build many long-lasting things . these could be monuments , artifacts , cathedrals and cities . these can often be seen in many cultures . some monuments made out of stone include , the egyptian pyramids and the greek parthenon . stonehenge is also a famous stone building .
