Upper Normandy     upper normandy ( called `` haute-normandie '' in french ) is a former region of france . it is now part of the administrative region of normandy . the capital was rouen , which is now the capital of normandy . in 2007 about 1.9 million people lived in the region . upper normandy was formed as a region in 1956. the old region of normandy was split into the current upper normandy and lower normandy . the departments in the region were seine-maritime and eure . the region was the first producer of flax in france . it is also quite industrialized , specialising in petrochemical and pharmaceutical , as well as manufacturing industries .
