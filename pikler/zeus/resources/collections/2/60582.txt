Conrad Lant     conrad `` cronos '' lant ( born 15 january 1963 ) is the vocalist and bass player in black metal band venom . he was born in 1963 in london , england . cronos started to sing in 1978 in the band `` guillotine '' . then he knew jeffrey `` mantas '' dunn and they founded venom . in 1989 cronos left the band to start a solo work . venom could not go on without him and they stopped singing in 1993. then cronos returned in 1995 and rejoined the band . other websites . - official venom website
