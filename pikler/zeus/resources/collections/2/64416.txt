Teri Hatcher     teri lynn hatcher ( born december 8 , 1964 ) is an emmy-nominated and golden globe award-winning american actress and author . she gained attention for her role as lois lane in the television series `` '' co-starring with dean cain . hatcher is also well known for portraying susan mayer , in `` desperate housewives '' , an accident-prone divorcee . she is also a `` bond girl '' , having played paris carver in `` tomorrow never dies '' in 1997 .
