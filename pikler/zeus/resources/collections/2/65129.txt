Conical measure     a conical measure is a piece of laboratory equipment which is used to store and mix liquids . a conical measure is a conical , or in the shape of a cone . it has a wide top so that liquids can be poured in more easily and marks on the side of it to help measure the amount of liquid more easily . they can be made of plastic or glass . a conical measure can be used in any pouring , measuring or mixing of liquids . this means that it is useful in : - pharmacies - schools - laboratories - hospitals they are also useful in homes as a measuring cup for cooking . conical measures are not very accurate , but are easy to use and to mix liquids in . other websites . - a conical measure manufactorer - see for picture - experiment using conical measures
