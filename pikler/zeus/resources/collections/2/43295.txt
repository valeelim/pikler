Zephyrosaurus     zephyrosaurus was a small dinosaur . it was a relative of hypsilophodon . it lived 132 million years ago , in the western part of what is now north america . it was 6 ft ( 2 metres ) long and 3ft ( 1 metre ) high . the remains are bones from various parts of the body . it had a steep face , a raised knob on the upper jaw , and a larger knob on the cheekbone . some of the bones may have allowed movement within the skull ( cranial kinesis ) as well . like other hypsilophodonts , it had beak teeth .
