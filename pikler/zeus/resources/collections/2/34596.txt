70s BC     events . - spartacus leads the slaves against rome . his army was eventually defeated , and spartacus is killed in battle , by roman legionaries under marcus licinius crassus . - in rome , marcus aemilius lepidus becomes the third member of the second triumvirate , with mark antony and octavian . - the city of tigranakert of artsakh is built in armenia . - salome alexandria becomes queen of judea after her husband dies . - hycarnus ii becomes the high priest of jerusalem until 66 bc . - pamplona is built in spain
