Cathay Pacific     cathay pacific limited is an airline from hong kong . it transports passengers and cargo to over 120 cities world-wide . its main base or hub is at hong kong international airport . it is one of the six airlines in the world to get a five star rating from skytrax . it won `` airline of the year '' award from skytrax in 2003 , 2005 and 2009. its sister airline is dragonair which serves destinations in mainland china and asia .
