Urartu     urartu ( assyrian `` urará¹­u '' , urartian `` biainili '' ) was an ancient kingdom in armenia . it was in the mountainous plateau between asia minor , mesopotamia , and caucasus mountains , later known as the armenian highland , and it centered around lake van ( present-day eastern turkey ) . the kingdom existed from ca . 860 bc , emerging from late bronze age nairi polities , until 585 bc . the name corresponds to the biblical ararat . urartu extended from lake van southeastward to mannai , and sometimes even included that land as one of its provinces . related pages . - arzashkun
