International Fight League     the international fight league is an american mixed martial arts ( mma ) league and is the world 's first mma league . instead of the established norm for mma events , where matchups are strictly one-on-one affairs , each ifl card is a showdown between two teams of five fighters , each fighter fighting one match against another on the opposing team . the ifl is shown in the united states on fox sports net and mynetworktv . other websites . - ifl - official website
