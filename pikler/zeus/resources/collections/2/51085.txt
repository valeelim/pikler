Coronation     a coronation is a ceremony that gives a monarch regal or royal power . the power is symbolized by putting a crown upon his or her head . we say that the king or queen is `` crowned '' at the coronation . the ritual may have a religious meaning . coronations are not as common now as in the past , although in some countries such as great britain it is still normal to hold the ceremony to install a new king or queen . related pages . - inauguration
