Battle of Worcester     the battle of worcester was the final battle of the english civil war . oliver cromwell and the parliamentarians defeated the royalist , mainly scottish , forces of king charles ii on september 3 , 1651 at worcester , england . the 16,000 royalist forces were defeated by the 28,000 strong `` new model army '' of cromwell . other websites . - battleground pictures - worcestershire cc : historic environment and archaeology service : the battle of worcester - http : //www.british-civil-wars.co.uk/military/1651-worcester.htm - http : //www.clangarra.com/battle % 20- % 20worcester.htm
