Mesencephalon     the mesencephalon or midbrain is a part of the brain stem . it is associated with vision , hearing , motor control , sleep/wake , arousal ( alertness ) , and temperature regulation . in the anatomy of developing animals , the brain forms from the neural tube , which turns into three vesicles . the mesencephalon ( midbrain ) is the middle vesicle , and becomes part of the brain stem . the mesencephalon is ancient in origin , meaning its general architecture is shared with the most ancient of vertebrates . dopamine produced in the `` substantia nigra '' plays a role in motivation and habituation of species from humans to insects .
