Hot in the Shade     hot in the shade is a studio album by the american hard rock/heavy metal band kiss . it was released on october 17 , 1989. track listing . - rise to it - betrayed - hide your heart - prisoner of love - read my body - love 's a slap in the face - forever - silver spoon - cadillac dreams - king of hearts - the street giveth and the street taketh away - you love me to hate you - somewhere between heaven and hell - little caesar - boomerang
