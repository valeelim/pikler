Hafiz     hafiz or hafez may mean : - hafez or khwajeh shams al-din muhammad hafez-e shirazi , an iranian mystic and poet . sometimes credited as `` hafiz '' or `` hafiz of shiraz '' . - hafiz ( quran ) , a term used by muslims for people who have completely memorized the qur'an - al-hafiz ( d. 1149 ) , the eleventh caliph of the fatimids - hafez al-assad ( 1930-2000 ) , president of syria from 1971 to 2000 - mohamed ali hafez , egyptian federation for scouts and girl guides scouting notable , awardee of the bronze wolf in 1965 - hafiz , a former member of the malaysian rock band one buck short .
