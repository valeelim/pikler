Hanamachi     a hanamachi ( 花街 ) is a japanese word used to call a neighborhood where courtesans and geisha lived and worked . the word 's literal meaning is `` flower town '' . these neighborhoods used to have many `` okiya '' ( `` geisha houses '' ) and `` ochaya '' ( `` tea houses '' , where geishas entertained guests ) . the term `` hanamachi '' is used in modern japan to call the areas where modern-day geisha are still living and working . today , `` hanamachi '' are rare outside of kyoto .
