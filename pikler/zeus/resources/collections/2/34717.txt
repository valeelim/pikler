Faculty of Arts (Priština)    faculty of arts ( priština ) the faculty of arts of priština ( serbian : факултет уметности у приштини or fakultet umetnosti u prištini ) is the faculty of arts of the university of priština in kosovo , serbia . the faculty of arts of pristina was founded in 1973 as the academy of arts , and got its present name in 1986. because of albanian terrorist attacks it was moved in 1999. it first moved to varvarin and later to zvečan , where it is now . it is part of the university of priština temporarily relocated in kosovska mitrovica . divisions . - art - music - theatre degrees offered . acting . - bachelor of arts - master of arts music . - bachelor of music - master of music visual arts . - bachelor of arts - master of arts notable teachers . - jasmina novokmet - milenko stefanovic - sanja stijacic other websites . - official website - infostud - ministry of education of republic of serbia
