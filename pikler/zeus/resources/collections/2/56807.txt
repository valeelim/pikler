Sucrose     sucrose ( common name : table sugar , also called saccharose ) is a disaccharide ( glucose + fructose ) with the molecular formula cho . its systematic name is α-d-glucopyranosyl- ( 1→2 ) -β-d-fructofuranose . it is best known for its role in human nutrition and is formed by plants but not by higher organisms . pure sucrose is most often prepared as a fine , white , odorless crystalline powder with a pleasing , sweet taste . other websites . - computational chemistry wiki - nomenclature of carbohydrates - 3d images of sucrose
