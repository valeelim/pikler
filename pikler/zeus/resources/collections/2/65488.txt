Adaminaby, New South Wales    adaminaby , new south wales adaminaby ( ) is a town in southeast new south wales , australia on the snowy mountains highway . the first town of adaminaby was in the way of a new dam built on the eucumbene river as part of the snowy mountains scheme . in 1957 more than 100 buildings were relocated to a new site 10 kilometres away where they would not be flooded . the name , adaminaby is an aboriginal word meaning a camp , or a resting place . the town first started during the gold rush to kiandra , new south wales|kiandra in 1860. it was first called seymour , but the name was changed to adaminaby in 1886 .
