Freeview (New Zealand)    freeview ( new zealand ) freeview is free television service in the new zealand , it is sent to people 's home using a digital signal by either over the air or by satellite . it is planning to have more channels than analogue television provides . the service was put on air on 2 may 2007. the service was created as a local copy of the british digital television service , also known as freeview . some tvs can receive freeview automatically , but most people have to buy a special box to connect to their old tv or replace their satellite box , if they want to get by satellite .
