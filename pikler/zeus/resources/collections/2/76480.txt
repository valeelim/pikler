Meatball     a meatball is meat that is ground up ( finely cut ) , mixed with other ingredients , then shaped like a ball . most meatballs are made out of beef . meatballs can also be made out of chicken , fish , or other kinds of meat . the other ingredients ( parts ) are usually bread , onion , and eggs . meatballs can be cooked by frying , baking , or steaming them . meatballs are sometimes served with pasta or in a sauce .
