Californication (TV series)    californication ( tv series ) californication is an american television series . the series is shown on the showtime television network . `` californication '' was created by tom kapinos and stars david duchovny as hank moody . it is about a troubled novelist who moves to los angeles , california . the series deals with problems with his job that cause problems with his relationships with his partner karen ( natascha mcelhone ) and daughter becca ( madeleine martin ) . the series was first shown on television on august 13 , 2007. channel five in the uk and network ten in australia have bought the rights to this television series . it was announced that after the seventh season , that californication would end . the seventh season started airing on april 13 , 2014. other websites . - showtime announcement
