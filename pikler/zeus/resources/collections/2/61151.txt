Laksa     laksa is a popular spicy noodle soup from peranakan culture . curry laksa ( sometimes called just `` laksa '' ) is a coconut soup made with prawns , beef , bean sprouts and chili . it is very spicy but can be made milder by leaving out some of the chilis . laksa is a popular food in malaysia . a well-known asam laksa can be found in air itam , penang .
