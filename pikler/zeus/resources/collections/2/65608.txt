Burlington Airpark     burlington airpark is an airport in burlington , ontario , canada . it has two runways . the airport is privately owned by vince rossi . the airport was created by victor and gwen kovachik in 1962. it is a very popular airport for toronto-area pilots . the airport 's first runway is 3,763 ft ( 1,147 m ) and is made of asphalt and turf . the second runway is 2,145 ft ( 654 m ) and is made of turf . other websites . - burlington airpark website - kovachik airport services
