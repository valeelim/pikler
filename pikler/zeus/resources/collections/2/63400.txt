Tucson, Arizona    tucson , arizona tucson is a city in arizona , united states . it is the seat of pima county . it is 118 miles ( 188 km ) southeast of phoenix and 60 miles ( 98 km ) north of the mexican border . it is where the shooting of senator gabrielle giffords took place . arizona has many deserts . it is visited by many people to escape cold weather . tucson has a population of 531,641 as of 2015 and is the second populated city in arizona behind phoenix . the city has a mean elevation of 2,400 feet . the climate of tucson is a arid desert climate with very hot summers and mild winters . the monsoon occurs in mid june and ends around late september in the tucson area . other websites . - official government website
