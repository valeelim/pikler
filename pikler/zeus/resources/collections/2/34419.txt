Polyrhythm     polyrhythm happens when two different rhythms are played at the same time in music . african , latin , and afro-cuban music often use polyrhythms . polyrythm is another word for layering and adding volumes to the piece of music . it happens when one person starts playing a rhythm and then other people start joining in , adding dynamics to it . it happens in africa during festivals and occasions along with a call and response style of playing .
