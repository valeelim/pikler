1900 Summer Olympics     the 1900 summer olympics , officially known as the games of the ii olympiad , were held in paris , france . the games were part of the 1900 exposition universelle ( paris world 's fair ) . the sports events were not well planned ; and the games were not thought to be a success . a total of 24 nations sent athletes to compete at the paris games . related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - paris 1900 at olympic.org
