Florida International University     florida international university ( fiu ) is a public research university in miami , florida in the united states . the university is the largest university in south florida and also the fifth-largest university in florida , and the thirteenth-largest university in the country . fiu 's programs in law , architecture and business are amongst its most well-ranked and most known programs . the mascot of fiu is a panther , named roary , and students and fans of the university 's athletic teams are often called `` panthers '' .
