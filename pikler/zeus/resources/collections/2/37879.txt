Upland, California    upland , california upland is a city in san bernardino county , california . as of the 2000 census , 68,393 people lived in upland . it became a city on may 15 , 1906 , after previously being named north ontario and a part of ontario . upland used to be famous for skateboarding because of its skating park , the pipeline , but the park was demolished in the 1970s . the rapper litefoot was born in upland .
