2001 Pacific hurricane season     the 2001 pacific hurricane season officially started on may 15 , 2001 in the eastern pacific , and on june 1 , 2001 in the central pacific , and lasted until november 30 , 2001. these dates conventionally delimit the period of each year when most tropical cyclones form in the northeastern pacific ocean . the first storm formed on may 25 and the last storm dissipated on november 3. there were no significant storms during the 2001 pacific hurricane season . hurricanes adolph and hurricane juliette both reached category 4 strength , while juliette made landfall in the baja california peninsula as a weak category 1 hurricane , causing two deaths . other websites . - nhc 2001 pacific hurricane season archive - hpc 2001 tropical cyclone rainfall pages - central pacific hurricane center - gary padgett 's monthly storm summaries and best tracks
