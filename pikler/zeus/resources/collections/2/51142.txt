Kinahmi     in finnish mythology , kinahmi is a great whirl in sea . kinahmi is a way to tuonela , the land of dead people . souls of evil people must swim through kinahmi to enter tuonela . swords , axes and needles moves with water and hurts souls of evil people . there is also a different story about water between land of dead and land of living . in that story the water is calm , black river . the whirl is a made by great powers of cosmos . in the middle of disc of earth stands pillar of sky . it holds dome of sky so that dome of sky does not drop on earth . dome of sky goes around itself ; it rotates . stars are a part of dome of sky , and while sky rotates , we see stars moving . one star does not move , it is north star . north star is a nail that connects dome of sky to the pillar of world . the pillar of world rotates with the dome of sky . its bottom side is standing in arctic sea , in the middle of the earth . the rotation of pillar causes water to form a great whirl . tuonela is under the flat earth , but the whirl is so deep that you can go to tuonela through it .
