Bier     a bier is a flat frame , traditionally wooden but sometimes made of other materials , used to carry a corpse for burial in a funeral procession . in antiquity it was often simply a wooden board on which the dead was placed covered with a . in modern times , however , the corpse is almost never carried on the bier without being first placed in a coffin , though the coffin is sometimes kept open . other pages . - catafalque - n.b . `` bier '' is also the german and dutch word for beer .
