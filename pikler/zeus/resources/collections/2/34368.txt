Blue cheese     blue cheese is a type of cheese with a fungus called `` penicillium '' added to it . the fungus makes patches or veins of it look blue . the cheese can be made from cow 's milk , sheep 's milk , or goat 's milk . types of it include roquefort cheese , stilton cheese , and gorgonzola cheese . these names should only be used for cheeses that are made in certain areas and are protected by the european union .
