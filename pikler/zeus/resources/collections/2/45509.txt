Alex Trebek     alex trebek ( george alexander trebek ) is a canadian-american television game show host . he is best known as the host of `` jeopardy ! '' which he has hosted since 1984. he was born july 22 , 1940 in sudbury , ontario , canada . in 2011 , he snapped his achilles tendon while trying to stop a robber trying to rob his san francisco hotel room .
