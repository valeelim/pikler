Solo (music)    solo ( music ) in musical performance , a solo is a piece or a section of a piece that is played or sung by a single performer . this performer is called a `` soloist '' . a soloist can be a singer or an instrumentalist . solos are common in classical music and jazz . in popular music , a `` solo '' is an improvised melody played by a single performer , such as a guitar solo . in the country music genre a solo is known as a `` ride '' . related pages . - concerto - virtuoso
