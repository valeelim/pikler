Johnny Saxton     johnny saxton ( b. july 4 , 1930 in newark , nj , d. october 4 2008 ) was an american boxer who won the welterweight championship . saxton beat kid gavilan in 1954 to become the welterweight champion . the lost the title the following year to tony demarco . in 1956 he won the title again by beating carmen basilio , but lost the title in a rematch with basilio later in the year . he retired in 1958. other websites . - boxing record
