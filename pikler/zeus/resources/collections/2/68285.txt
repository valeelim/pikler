Atharva Veda     the atharva veda is the fourth veda , one of the hindu scriptures . there are a number of upanishads in this veda . ganapathy , narayana , devi , atharva siropanishaths are part of atharv veda . bhavanopanisath is also in atharva veda . sankaracharya established joshimath in kedarnath one of the four popal centres of hinduism to flourish atharva veda .
