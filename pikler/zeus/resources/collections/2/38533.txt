İsmet Özel    ismet özel ismet özel is a turkish poet . he was born in 1944 in kayseri , turkey . he started his university degree at political science faculty of ankara university . he graduated from the french language department at hacettepe university . in 1970 , he published a literature magazine `` halkın dostları '' with ataol behremoğlu , a turkish poet and russian linguist . in fact , ataol behramoğlu purified his poetry by leaving out crude propaganda and naive didacticism .
