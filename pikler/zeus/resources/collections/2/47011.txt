Attac (organization)    attac ( organization ) the association pour la taxation des transactions pour l'aide aux citoyens ( association for the taxation of financial transactions for the aid of citizens , attac ) is an organization that promotes a tax on foreign exchange transactions , the so-called tobin tax . at first attac demanded only the introduction of the so-called tobin tax on currency speculation . now it fights for many issues related to globalisation . therefore , it reports about the decisions of the world trade organisation ( wto ) , the organisation for economic cooperation and development ( oecd ) and the international monetary fund ( imf ) . attac attends the meetings of the g8 with the goal of influencing policymakers ' decisions . though attac criticises the neoliberal ideology dominating economic globalisation , it is not `` anti-globalisation '' . it supports globalisation policies that they characterise as sustainable and socially just . one of attac 's slogans is `` the world is not for sale '' , denouncing the `` merchandisation '' of society .
