Surf's Up (movie)    surf 's up ( movie ) surf 's up is an american computer-animated movie . it was released on june 7 , 2007 by columbia pictures . it was directed by ash brannon ( the co-director of `` toy story 2 '' ) and chris buck ( the director of `` tarzan '' ) . it is a parody of surfing documentaries , like `` the endless summer '' and `` riding giants '' . many actors , including shia labeouf , jeff bridges , zooey deschanel , jon heder , mario cantone , james woods , rebecca honig , diedrich bader , rob machado , kelly slater and sal masekela did the voices of the characters in the movie .
