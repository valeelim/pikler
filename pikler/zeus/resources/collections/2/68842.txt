Ö    ö ö or ö is not a letter used in english , but is used in some other languages , such as german , finnish , estonian , hungarian , turkish and swedish . ö or ö is one of the 4 extra letters used in german . it can be replaced by using the letters oe or oe . in english language newspapers it is often written as o or o but this is not correct . internet addresses are written as `` oe '' because the internet address system can only understand ordinary english letters . german is one of the official languages of switzerland . it has its own dialect ( swiss-german or schweizerdeutsch ) . the ö or ö is used in the swiss dialect as well .
