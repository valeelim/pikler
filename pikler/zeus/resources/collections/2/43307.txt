Dilophosaurus     dilophosaurus ( meaning 'double-crested lizard ' ) was a medium-large dinosaur from 190 million years ago . `` dilophosaurus '' has been examined several times over the years and has been put into no less than nine different theropod groups . it may be related to the smaller `` coelophysis '' . specific details of its life-style are few . some of its fossils were found in terrain which was a low-lying slow-moving river system at the time . the animal was obviously a carnivore . `` dilophosaurus '' was shown in the movie `` when dinosaurs roamed america '' scaring off a pack of `` megapnosaurus '' and eating an `` anchisaurus '' .
