Porno Graffitti     porno graffitti is a japanese j-pop band . the members of the band are akihito okano ( vocals ) and haruichi shindo ( guitar ) . the band is from innoshima in hiroshima . the band first started playing in 1999 with three members . the third member , tama , left the band in 2004. the band has become one of the duos leading the music in japan .
