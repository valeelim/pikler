The Animals     the animals were an english music group , who were most popular in the 1960s . they were known for their bluesy sound , and their heavy-voiced singer eric burdon . one of their first hit songs , `` the house of the rising sun '' , was an old american folk song , which they performed with a rock and roll beat . folk musician bob dylan was impressed by the animals version of the song . other animals hits included `` we got ta get out of this place '' , `` do n't bring me down '' , `` it 's my life '' , `` see see rider '' , `` do n't let me be misunderstood '' , and `` i 'm crying '' . the original animals disbanded , and burdon moved to the united states in 1967 , where he formed a new version of the band . they made more records , like `` san franciscan nights '' , `` sky pilot '' , `` when i was young '' , `` paint it black '' , `` river deep mountain high '' , `` gratefully dead '' and `` monterey '' . the original animals reunited in later years , to perform concerts , and burdon performed animals songs with other bands . bass guitarist chas chandler became a music producer , with artists like jimi hendrix and slade .
