Ballade     a ballade is a medieval french verse or song which tells a story . like the rondeau and the virelai it was very popular in 14th and 15th century france . all these three songs were originally dances . famous composers like guillaume de machaut wrote ballades . ballade verses often had three stanzas which had the same metre and rhyme scheme , and the last line was often the same . they might end with an envoi which meant that it was addressing a king or prince . the great english poet geoffrey chaucer wrote ballades .
