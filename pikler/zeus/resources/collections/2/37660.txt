Hero     a hero ( ) in greek mythology is a half-god/half-human being . herakles , for example , was the son of the god zeus and the mortal woman alkmene . heroes performed extraordinary feats and were worshipped in hero cults . in the modern world , hero has lost its ancient meaning . it now means someone who is courageous ( `` the firefighter who saved my baby is a hero. '' ) . heroes are `` heroic '' , they have `` heroism '' . they help in saving people or a society from bad people , villains , or natural disasters . a hero can also be someone who is helpful , polite , or helping people who need it . the word is used in the sports world to mean an extraordinary player or athlete ( `` football hero '' , `` olympic hero '' , etc. ) . the female equivalent of the male hero is the `` heroine '' . sometimes , the protagonist ( or main character ) of a story is called the `` hero '' of the work ( a book/movie/etc . ) .
