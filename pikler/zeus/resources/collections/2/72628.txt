Kobayr     kobayr ( armenian : կոբայր ) is a 12th century armenian apostolic church monastery . it is located in the village kober within lori marz , armenia . kobayr was built on a shelf of a gorge by the princes of the junior bagratuni branch , kyurikids in 1171. the monastery was later acquired by the zakarids and converted into a chalcedonian monastery . many of the writings in the monastery are written in georgian . the monastery is being fixed and paid for by the government of armenia with the help of the government of italy . the ruins of the main church in the monastery contain frescoes of christ and the twelve apostles as well as the church fathers and other christian figures .
