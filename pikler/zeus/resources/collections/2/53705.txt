Großlangheim     großlangheim is a small village in bavaria , germany . about 1,600 people live in großlangheim . big events in großlangheim are the weinfest ( wineparty ) and the panik-party in the winehill . panik-party is the biggest open air party in unterfranken . over 5,000 people came to this event in 2006 which is organized by teenagers from großlangheim .
