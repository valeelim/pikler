Flag of the United Arab Emirates     the flag of the united arab emirates ( ) is used to represent , or show , the united arab emirates . it contains the pan-arab colors , which are red , green , white , and black , to show arab unity . the green stands for fertility , the white means neutrality , or being neutral , and the black stands for the country 's large amount of oil . some merchants also fly a civil flag , which is red with the regular united arab emirates flag in the top-right .
