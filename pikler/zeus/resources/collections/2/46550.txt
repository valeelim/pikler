Honeysuckle     honeysuckles are types of shrubs or vines in the family caprifoliaceae . they are found in north america , asia and europe . there are about 180 different species of honeysuckle , many of which are climbing plants . over 100 types of honeysuckle are found in china . europe and north america have only about 20 types each . many types of honeysuckles have bell-shaped flowers . these flowers make a sweet nectar that can be eaten . the fruit is a red , blue or black berry that has several seeds . the berries of most honeysuckles are mildly poisonous , but a few have berries that are good to eat .
