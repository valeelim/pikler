Digital     a digital system is a system that stores data in a discrete way . the opposite is an analogue ( us- analog ) system , which stores the data in a continuous way . usually , digital systems store the information in a binary way ; that is , every bit of information can not have a value other than `` zero '' ( off ) or `` one '' ( on ) . larger amounts of data are stored as a string of these bits , which means a set of 0s and 1s together make a meaning to the system . the word `` digital '' is most commonly used in computing and electronics . usage . - compact disc
