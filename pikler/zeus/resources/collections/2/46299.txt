Footballer     a footballer is the athlete who - with amatorial or professional interests - plays football . footballers sign a contract with clubs , playing for them until his expire date . at this point , a footballer can marks an other contract or move to another club . during a game , each team uses 11 players with the compulsory presences of a goalkeeper and a captain . roles are follows : goalkeeper , defender , midfielder and attacker . each of these has more positions , for example : - full-back , centre-back or sweeper ( defenders ) - half-back , central or playmaker ( midfielders ) - centre-forward , winger or striker ( attackers ) despite each role requests different skills and moves , every footballer has a common objective : scoring goals . see also . - football
