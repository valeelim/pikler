Bow     bow can mean : - bow ( weapon ) , `` bows and arrows '' - bow ( music ) , the bow used to play musical instruments - a type of knot which has two large loops . it is used to tie simple things such as apron strings , shoelaces , or ribbons on wrapped-up presents or for decoration in girls ' hair . ( rhyming with `` now '' : ) - bow ( ship ) , the forward part of the hull of a ship or boat - a gesture , see bowing people . - clara bow , an actress
