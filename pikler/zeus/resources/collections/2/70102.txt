Diazepam     diazepam is a drug . it is a benzodiazepine which has a relatively long half-life . it is mostly used to treat anxiety , certain forms of epilepsy , and sleeping problems . like with other benzodiazepines , there is a risk of addiction . for this reason the drug is used to treat acute cases . it should not be taken for longer than a few weeks . according to the world health organization diazepam is an `` essential drug '' . diazepam is marketed under a number of names ; its original trade name was `` valium '' . it is one of the drugs prescribed most often . it is used recreationally to cause a calming effect .
