The Powerpuff Girls     the powerpuff girls is a american children 's animated television series . it airs on cartoon network in 1998-2005 , 2009 , 2012-2014. it is about three girls named blossom , bubbles , and buttercup . they are in kindergarten and have superpowers . the show was created by craig mccracken and produced by hanna-barbera . a reboot was aired on cartoon network in april 2016. other websites . - `` the powerpuff girls '' at cartoon network 's department of cartoons ( archive )
