Welcome to the Black Parade     `` welcome to the black parade '' is the first single and fifth track from my chemical romance 's third studio album , `` the black parade '' . it was released on september 11 , 2006 on itunes and october 9 , 2006 on cd . it is the band 's ninth single . the studio version was available on myspace on september 2 , 2006. the song became the group 's first number one in the uk on october 15 , 2006 , and later rose to number one on the modern rock tracks on october 26 , 2006 , where it would stay for seven weeks . it was originally intended to be called `` the five of us are dying . '' the song ranked at # 17 on rolling stone 's `` the 100 best songs of 2006 '' . other websites . - `` welcome to the black parade '' sound track at `` the black parade '' official website
