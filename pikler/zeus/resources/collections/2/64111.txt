Nassau County, New York    nassau county , new york nassau county is a suburban county in the new york metropolitan area east of new york city and west of suffolk county , in the u.s. state of new york . in 2000 , 1,334,544 people lived there . it and suffolk county are together referred to as long island . until 1899 nassau county was part of queens county . along with suffolk , it was named the safest county in the united states in 2005 by forbes magazine .
