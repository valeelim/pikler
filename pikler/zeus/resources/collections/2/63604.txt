Thomas Cavendish     sir thomas cavendish ( or candish ) ( 1560-1592 ) was an english sea explorer . he was known as `` the navigator '' because he led the third expedition to travel around ( `` circumnavigate '' ) the globe . cavendish was the first who set sail to do this . magellan and francis drake were the first two to circumnavigate the globe , but they did not intend to do so . cavendish died at sea on his second voyage around the world .
