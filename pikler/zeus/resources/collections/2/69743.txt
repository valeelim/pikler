Ron Dennis     ronald `` ron '' dennis cbe ( born 1 june 1947 ) is the executive chairman of mclaren automotive and mclaren group . he was the team principal of the mclaren formula one team until 2009. he was placed at number 648 in the sunday times rich list 2006 with a wealth of £90 million . `` the times '' put his wealth at £200 million in 2009 .
