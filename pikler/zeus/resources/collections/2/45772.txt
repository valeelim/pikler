Beam (structure)    beam ( structure ) a beam is an element that carries load . beams generally carry vertical forces , but can also be used to carry horizontal loads ( those loads can result from wind or an earthquake ) . the loads that a beam carries are led to columns or walls , which lead the force to the next element . beams are characterized by their ( their shape ) , their length , and their material . in constructions of today beams are mostly made of steel , reinforced concrete , or wood .
