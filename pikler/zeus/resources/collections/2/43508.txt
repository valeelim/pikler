Alan Freeman     alan leslie `` fluff '' freeman ( born 6 july 1927 in melbourne , victoria , australia , died 27 november 2006 in london , england ) was an australian radio presenter who worked for most of his career in the united kingdom . in may 2000 he was presented with a lifetime achievement award at the sony radio academy awards . freeman was best known for presenting `` pick of the pops '' on bbc radio 1 throughout the 1960s and 1970s . freeman was openly bisexual .
