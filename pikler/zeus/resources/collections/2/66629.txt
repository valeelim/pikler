International Spiritual Movement Anjuman Serfaroshan-e-Islam     international spiritual movement anjuman serfroshan-e-islam ( asi ) ( ) is a pakistan-based organisation . it was made in 1980 by imam mehdi riaz ahmed gohar shahi . its headquarters are in kotri , hyderabad , sindh and pakistan . the purpose of this movement is to invite all man without any discrimination of caste , creed , nation or religion towards the divine love of god . to serve this purpose , imam mehdi riaz ahmed gohar shahi visited places of worship of different types , including mosques , imam bargahs , churches , hindu temples , sikh temples etc . international spiritual movement anjuman serfroshan-e-islam is divided into two branches one for men only and other is for women only . the ladies wing is led by mrs. gulzar . however , the president of men wing is mr. wasi muhammad qureshi , who was nominated by imam mehdi riaz ahmed gohar shahi as president of international spiritual movement anjuman serfroshan-e-islam . gohar shahi himself is the patrorn-in-chief of international spiritual movement anjuman serfroshan-e-islam .
