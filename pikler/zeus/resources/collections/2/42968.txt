Kenpo     kenpo or kempo is the descriptive name of various japanese martial arts with chinese origins , and also of hybrid martial arts : the japanese ones and the chinese ones . kenpo is similar to karate and includes more punches and kicks than holds or joint locks of wrestling . more known styles of kenpo . - american kenpo - kajukenbo - seishindo kenpo - shorinji kenpo ( kenpo of shaolin ) - kempo tai jutsu - tracy kenpo - kenpo jiu-jitsu - kenpo kai kempo is a japanese martial arts form . however , unlike karate , its origins are linked back to china and still have very strong influences from chinese systems . other websites . - european kenpo kai organization website
