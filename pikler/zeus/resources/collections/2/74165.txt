Joondalup, Western Australia    joondalup , western australia joondalup is a suburb and regional city in perth , western australia . it was established in the 1980s . in 2001 , the suburb had a population of 6,927. the suburb was named after lake joondalup which is located on the eastern side of the suburb . in relation to the other establishments in perth , joondalup is a relatively new settlement , as it was undeveloped until the 1960s .
