Great power     a great power is a nation or state that is able to influence other states in most of the world . that is possible because it has great economic , political and military strength . its opinions are taken into account by other nations before taking diplomatic or military action . characteristically , they have the ability to intervene militarily almost anywhere , and they also have soft , cultural power , often in the form of economic investment in less developed portions of the world . five great powers are permanent members of the united nations security council . the great powers today are : - china - france - germany - japan - russia - united kingdom - united states potential great powers : india
