Ingvar Carlsson     ingvar carlsson ( born 9 november 1934 in borås , västra götaland county ( then älvsborg county ) , sweden ) , is a swedish politician , former prime minister of sweden ( march 1986–october 1991 ; october 1994–march 1996 ) and leader of the social democratic party ( march 1986–march 1996 ) . references . - ingvar carlsson
