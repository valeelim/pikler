Sickle     a sickle is a curved tool used in agriculture for harvesting grain crops . it was the tool used before machines were used for harvesting . it has a curved blade with an attached handle . the inside part of the blade is sharp , so that the user can swing the blade against the crop 's base , catching it in the curve and slicing it . in greek mythology , the greek deities cronus and perseus used the sickle as a weapon . a sickle and a hammer are the symbols of the soviet union .
