Yalta Conference     the yalta conference ( sometimes called the crimea conference ) was held in february 1945 , just before the second world war was over . it was when the prime minister of the united kingdom ( winston churchill ) , the president of the united states ( franklin d. roosevelt ) and the dictator of the ussr ( joseph stalin ) all met in crimea and talked about what to do with germany . the yalta conference was an important part of european history . the allies agreed that : - germany was to be completely disarmed - a new world organisation was to be set up called the united nations ( the un ) - stalin agreed to join the war against japan three months after the defeat of germany - germany would be split up into four different pieces ( occupation zones ) , one occupied by france , one by the ussr , one by the usa and one by the united kingdom - berlin would also be split up into four different pieces - those who committed war crimes would be judged and sentenced - a neutral government would be set up in poland
