The Hershey Company     the hershey company ( ) , until april 2005 hershey foods corporation , commonly called hershey 's , is america 's largest chocolate company . its main office is in hershey , pennsylvania , a town that smells strongly of cocoa on some days , and home to hershey 's chocolate world . the company was founded by milton s. hershey in 1894 as the hershey chocolate company . hershey 's candies and other products are sold around the world .
