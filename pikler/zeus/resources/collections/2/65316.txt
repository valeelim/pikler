Asturian language     the asturian language is the native language of asturias . about 150,000 people speak it in asturias . this language is very similar to leonese language , spoken in other territories that once made up the kingdom of león ( león , western zamora , salamanca and northwestern cáceres where is called extremaduran ) , and to mirandes language , spoken in miranda do douro in portugal . in asturias , primary and secondary students can choose to study it .
