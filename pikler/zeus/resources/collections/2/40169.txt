Taxidermy     taxidermy ( which comes from the greek word for `` the arrangement of skin '' ) is act of taking the skin of an animal and preserving it , by putting it on a fake body . taxidermed animals can be mounted and put on display , sometimes in a museum or a home . people who taxidermy animals are called taxidermists . many hunters and fishermen taxidermine the animals they catch and kill for trophies . ones that are on display in museums are used to make dioramas and for educational purposes . some people taxiderm their pets after they die .
