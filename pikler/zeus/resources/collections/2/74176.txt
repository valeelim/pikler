Silverchair     silverchair is a rock band from newcastle , australia . the band 's members are daniel johns , chris joannou and ben gillies . silverchair is the only artist to score five number-one albums in the australian charts . their studio albums are `` frogstomp '' , `` freak show '' , `` neon ballroom '' , `` diorama '' and `` young modern '' . the band has released many singles , their number-one singles are `` tomorrow '' , `` freak '' and `` straight lines '' .
