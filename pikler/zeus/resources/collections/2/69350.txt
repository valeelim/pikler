Sweater     a sweater is a piece of clothing worn on the upper body to keep the person warm . sweaters are usually knitted or crocheted . other names for sweaters are pullover , jersey , or jumper . sweaters that open in the front are often called `` cardigans '' . they are named after james brudenell , 7th earl of cardigan , a british general during the crimean war , who led the famous charge of the light brigade . sweaters without sleeves are often called `` vests '' . sweaters can be worn all year long for comfort and warmth .
