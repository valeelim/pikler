Particulate     particulates are very tiny pieces of a solid or liquid that are carried floating in a gas . when the tiny pieces are solid , it is called a smoke . when the tiny pieces are liquid , it is called an aerosol . aerosols can be naturally found in clouds and geysers . there are also artificial aerosols such as haze and some air pollutants . smokes can be found naturally in wildfires and volcanoes . artificially , they can be found in cigarettes , factories and power plants . both of them can cause air pollution and climate change .
