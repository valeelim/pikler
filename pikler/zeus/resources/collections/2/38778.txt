Peso     the peso is the monetary unit ( coin ) of some spanish-speaking countries . they were once spanish colonies . they include argentina , chile , colombia , cuba , dominican republic , mexico , philippines , and uruguay . the word means 'weight ' in spanish , or more loosely 'pound ' . it was a coin that originated in spain and became of immense importance internationally . spain brought it to its colonies and minted many pesos in mexico and peru . later , spain stopped using this name , but some of the former colonies still use it . historically , the `` peso '' was eight `` reales '' , the `` real de a ocho '' . this translates to the well-known english term `` pieces of eight '' . it is known in many countries as the `` thaler '' or `` spanish dollar '' . it was used as the model for the original united states dollar and many other coins which were also minted in silver . relared pages . - thaler
