Ouagadougou     ouagadougou is the capital of burkina faso . it is also the country 's biggest city . in ouagadougou , people mainly make clothing and food . ouagadougou 's mayor is chosen every five years . ouagadougou is near the equator . it is always warm . in ouagadougou , people speak french . ouagadougou is one of the poorest cities in africa . almost 70 % of its people can not read or write but the city has ten universities .
