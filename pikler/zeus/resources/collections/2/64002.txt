Riyo Mori     riyo mori ( born 24 december 1986 in aoi-ku , shizuoka , japan ) is a japanese dance instructor . she won the miss universe 2007 title in may 2007. she was the second woman from japan to hold the title . ( the first miss universe winner from japan was akiko kojima in 1959 . ) mori is the eleventh asian woman to win the pageant .
