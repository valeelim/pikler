Wyolica     wyolica is a two person band from japan . the members are azumi ( vocals ) and so-to ( guitar ) . the group is not well known outside of japan . their sound is very similar of many american pop acts but sung in japanese . their first album , `` who said la la ? '' was released in 2000. since then they have released a number of albums and singles , including a six-song ep of acoustic songs . other websites . - official website
