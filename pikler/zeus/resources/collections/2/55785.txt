That '70s Show     that '70s show is an american television series . it is about the lives of a group of teenagers living in point place , wisconsin from may 17 , 1976 to december 31 , 1979. the series first started august 23 , 1998 and its final episode aired may 18 , 2006. the series continues to run in syndication on fx and cw network in the united states and united kingdom , ch in canada , as well as the seven network in australia and star world in asia , including malaysia . it also runs on the ntv for teens .
