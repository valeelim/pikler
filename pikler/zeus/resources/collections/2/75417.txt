Pervez Musharraf     general ( retired ) pervez musharraf ( ) ( born august 11 , 1943 ) was a former military dictator and the tenth president of pakistan . previously , he was chief executive of pakistan as well as former chief of army staff of the pakistan army and he took over power in pakistan in 1999 , by one day removing the then elected government of mr nawaz sharif . he has charged with multiple murders in april 2013 and was given bail in october 2013. then he was arrested again , and his trial is still going on in pakistan .
