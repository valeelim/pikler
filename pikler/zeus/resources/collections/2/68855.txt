O RLY?    o rly ? o rly ? is an internet meme often used in chat rooms or fora . it is short for `` oh , really ? '' . o rly is used as a response to an above post or comment to show ( sarcastically ) that it is obvious , clear , or apparent . o rly ? is usually followed by `` ya rly '' , then `` no wai '' . this sarcasm is not to be confused with trolling , disrupting a forum , or a chat room . the meme has spread on many forums , across the internet .
