Midnight Madness     midnight madness is a 1980 comedy movie from walt disney productions , starring david naughton . the movie is about a group of college students who participate in an all night puzzle solving race . it is michael j. fox 's first film role . the movie did not do well in theaters and many critics did not like it . however , the movie has gained a cult following , and has been the inspiration for many real-life puzzle and alternate reality games . other websites . - fagabeefe : an unofficial fan page devoted to the film - another website about the movie - roger ebert 's 1980 review - comprehensive synopsis/review at 3b theater - dvd review at digitally obsessed - hot springs , arkansas 's 2006 midnight madness - midnight madness brevard county , florida
