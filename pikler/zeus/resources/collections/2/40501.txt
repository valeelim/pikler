Jan Kerouac     jan-michelle kerouac ( born february 16 , 1952 - died june 7 , 1996 ) was the daughter of writer jack kerouac , by his second wife joan haverty . a writer herself , jan 's books included `` baby driver '' and `` trainsong '' . she also appeared in some documentary programs about her father , giving her side of his life story . she died in 1996 of kidney failure . she is buried in nashua , new hampshire , next to her father 's parents .
