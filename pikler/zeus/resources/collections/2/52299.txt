Democratic Republic of Georgia     the democratic republic of georgia ( drg ; georgian : საქართველოს დემოკრატიული რესპუბლიკა , `` sakartvelos demokratiuli respublika '' ) , 1918–1921 , was the first modern establishment of a republic of georgia . georgia 's capital was tbilisi , and its state language was georgian . created on may 26 , 1918 , on the break-up of the transcaucasian federation , it was led by the social democratic menshevik party . facing permanent internal and external problems , the young state was not able to withstand the invasion by the russian sfsr red armies . it collapsed between february and march of 1921 to become a soviet republic .
