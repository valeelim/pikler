Yucca     yucca is a family of shrubs and trees , related to the agaves . there are between 40 and 50 different kinds of yucca . all come from the hot and dry places in north and central america , as well as the caribbean . they have a very special way of pollination . there is an animal , the yucca moth , which does the pollination . it also lays its eggs in the plant . the larvae will eat some of the seeds , but not all of them . in many parts of the world , yuccas are grown as ornamental flowers .
