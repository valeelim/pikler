Armenian Soviet Socialist Republic     the armenian ssr ( armenian : հայկական սովետական սոցիալիստական հանրապետություն , haykakan sovetakan sotsialistakan hanrapetutyun ; russian : армянская советская социалистическая республика ) or soviet socialist republic of armenia came into being when the communist party of armenia proclaimed control of armenia on november 29 , 1920. on december 1 , 1920 , prime minister simon vratsian ceded control of the country . it later changed its name to the armenian soviet socialist republic .
