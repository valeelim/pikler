Nowruz     nowruz ( , meaning `` [ the ] new day '' , ; ; ) it is what the iranians call their new year . it is one of the ancient celebrations which has survived throughout history and is considered to be a day in which new good things start . nowruz is an ancient persian new year holiday , celebrated in early spring 20 - 21 march . khurdish and in middle east and many other countries in central asia including iran , tajikistan , pakistan and afghanistan celebrate this event on 21 - 22 march . it is considered to be one of the most important celebrations of persians .
