Toraja people     the toraja are an ethnic group , indigenous to a part of south sulawesi , indonesia . there are about 650,000 of them around the world . 450,000 of these still live in the regency of tana toraja ( `` land of toraja '' ) . most of them are christian . others of them are muslim or have local animist beliefs known as `` aluk '' ( `` the way '' ) . the indonesian government has recognized this animist belief as `` aluk to dolo '' ( `` way of the ancestors '' ) . other websites . - tana toraja official website . - toraja on the net - news and commentary . - batusura.de - a personal website containing photography and recorded music from toraja . - online toraja information .
