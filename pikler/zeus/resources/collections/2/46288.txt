Split Tours     split tours is a croatian ship travel company . it transports passengers and vehicles by water along the croatian coast . split tours was established in 1992. it became the first private ferry company in croatia . split tours has several ships including the ferry `` split prvi '' , and catamarans `` for '' , `` broc '' and `` komiza '' . these vessels run the coastal lines split-hvar , vis and milna . other websites . - split tours ' web page
