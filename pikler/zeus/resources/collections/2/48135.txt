Descendant     the term descendant or descendent has several meanings , some of which are listed below . as a noun , it must be spelled `` descendant '' . primary meaning : - the progeny of a previous generation ; the product of reproduction . descendants are `` offspring '' . - a later evolutionary type in the same line . it can also mean , by extension : - a type of object that developed from another object existing before it . - descendant ( astrology ) , the point directly opposite from the ascendant , or rising sign
