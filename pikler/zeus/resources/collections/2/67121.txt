Neoprene     neoprene is the dupont performance elastomers trade name for a family of synthetic rubbers based on polychloroprene ( polymer form of chloroprene ) . neoprene was invented by dupont scientists after dr. elmer k. bolton of dupont laboratories . it is used in a wide variety of environments , such as in wetsuits , laptop sleeves , cars , bombs , electrical insulation , and car fan belts . its chemical inertness makes it well suited for industrial applications such as gaskets , hoses , and corrosion-resistant coatings . it can also be used as a base for adhesives . uses . - balls ; diaphragms as in diaphragm valve - sheet : glove - foam : wetsuit ; glove - adhesive : adhesive tape ; liquid adhesive other websites . - information about neoprene at dupont performance elastomers
