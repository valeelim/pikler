Gerbilling     gerbilling is an urban legend . this means it a story that is not true . it is the act of placing a gerbil into the human rectum for sexual pleasure . other small live animals ( usually gerbils but also mice , hamsters , rats and various other rodents ) are used . folklorist jan harold brunvand writes that the urban legend began in 1984. it involved a mouse and an unidentified gay man . in passing years this story changes into a gerbil , and was made to involve to many named male celebrities that were publicly thought to be gay . rumors about various male celebrities engaging in gerbilling have become popular urban legends over the years .
