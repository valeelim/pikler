Goldie Hawn     goldie jeanne hawn ( born november 21 , 1945 ) is an american actress , director and producer . her mother was an american jew of hungarian immigrant parents . one of her early roles was as a cast member of `` laugh-in '' , a comedy series on nbc television . she won the academy award and the golden globe award in 1969 for her work in `` cactus flower '' . she then left `` laugh-in '' to continue her movie career . hawn is famous for her acting in many movie comedies during the 1970s , 1980s and 1990s . her son , oliver hudson , is a television and movie actor . her daughter is kate hudson , who is an actress and has been nominated for an oscar . hawn has another son , wyatt russell , whose father is actor kurt russell , hawn 's longtime partner .
