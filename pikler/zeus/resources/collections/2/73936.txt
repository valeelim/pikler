Karbala     karbala ( arabic : كربلاء ; transliterated : al karbala ; also known as karbala al-muqaddasah , in the west , sometimes spelled kerbela ) is a city in iraq . it is about 100 km southwest of baghdad . it is the capital of karbala province . for shi'a muslims the city is one of the holiest cities , after mekka , medina , jerusalem , and an najaf .
