Princess Taiping     princess taiping was a princess of tang dynasty china . she was the daughter of emperor gaozong and empress wu zetian . she became an important figure during her mother 's rule as empress in the second zhou dynasty 690-705 ad . princess taiping supported saving the tang dynasty and the li family . after the death of empress wu zetian in 705 , princess taiping joined with her nephew li longji ( later emperor xuanzong ) to get power for the tang dynasty again . in 710 , she and li longji stopped empress wei 's plan to gain power . princess taiping was also known for her beauty . in the chinese history ( mishi/秘史 ) , it has been said that , after losing two marriages , princess taiping loved li longji . mishi said that emperor xuanzong told of his love for his aunt in his autobiography . li longji confessed that he had fallen in love with princess taiping at first sight . he thought of her as a goddess-like figure . however , the truth is still unknown . after emperor xuanzong became ruler in 712 ad , princess taiping 's old age and power was seen as a threat to the young emperor , and she was given a death sentence to kill herself in 713 ad .
