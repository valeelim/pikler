IUCN Red List     the iucn red list ( also iucn red list of threatened species ) is a list of species . the list tells for each species how likely it is to become extinct ( or if it is already ) . this is called its conservation status . this list is put together each year by the international union for the conservation of nature ( iucn ) . there are exact criteria to see if a certain species is threatened to become extinct . these criteria are the same for all parts of the world . the purpose of the list is to tell people how threatened a certain species is . politicians can then decide on what needs to be done and the efforts needed to save certain species from becoming extinct . other websites . - iucn red list website - the species list can be found here .
