Bandicoot     bandicoots are marsupials . there are about 20 species of bandicoots . bandicoots are omnivores . they eat bugs , earthworms , larvae , and spiders . despite what they look like , they are not related to rats . they live in parts of australia . they are nocturnal . bandicoots are endangered species . for protection against foxes and wild cats , they retreat to their burrows for safety . there are different species of bandicoot including : - long-nosed bandicoot , `` perameles nasuta ''
