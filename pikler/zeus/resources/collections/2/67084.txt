Shirley MacLaine     shirley maclaine ( born april 24 , 1934 ) is an american movie and theatre actress . she is famous for her acting as well as her strong belief in reincarnation . she is also the writer of a many autobiographical works , some about her new age beliefs , as well as her hollywood career . she is the older sister of warren beatty . she won the academy award for `` best actress '' in 1983 , and has also received many other awards in her career , like the emmy , golden globe and bafta awards . other websites . - maclaine 's official website - shirley maclaine interviewed by ginny dougary ( 2005 )
