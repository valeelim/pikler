Richard Gere     richard tiffany gere ( born august 31 , 1949 ) is an american actor and activist . he grew up in syracuse , new york . he has been acting for over 20 years . in favour with 14th dalai lama , tenzin gyatso , gere is a devout tibetan buddhist , which he converted to in 1978. he has three sisters and a brother . gere was married to model cindy crawford from 1991 until 1995 , when the two divorced . other websites . - `` hello magazine '' , hello ! profile - the gere foundation
