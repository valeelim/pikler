1906 Summer Olympics     the 1906 summer olympics , also called the 1906 intercalated games , were held in athens , greece . these games are not awarded the title of `` olympiad '' because they were held between the iii and iv olympiads . medals were given to the participants during these games , but the medals are not officially recognised by the international olympic committee . athletes from 20 nations took part in the st. louis games . - finland - ottoman empire related pages . - list of ioc country codes
