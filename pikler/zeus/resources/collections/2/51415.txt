Vauxhall Motors     vauxhall is a car manufacturer . it is basically `` opel great britain '' , because it is opel 's subsidiary and vauxhall vehicles are the same as opel vehicles . the company was founded in london in 1903 and was named after the area of the city where it was created . the company is based in luton , bedfordshire . they have 2 factories , 1 in luton for production of the vivaro van and 1 in ellsmere port for production of the astra family car . in march 2017 , vauxhall and parent brand opel were brought by psa , the company who owns peugeot and citroen . other websites . - vauxhall heritage - vauxhall uk website
