Amazons     the amazons ( in greek , ) were a mythical ancient nation of all-women warriors . herodotus placed them in a region around scythia in sarmatia . the histories and legends in greek mythology may be inspired by warrior women among the sarmatians . the amazons are notably featured as part of the labours of hercules , in which the hero defeats them after he steals the magical belt belonging to their queen hippolyta . in more modern times , the comic character wonder woman is an amazon . other websites . - herodotus on the amazons - herodotus via gutenberg - perseus
