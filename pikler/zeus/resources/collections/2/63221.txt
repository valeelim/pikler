Succulent plant     succulent plants are plants that have adapted to very dry climates . they have adapted in such a way that they can store water , either in their leaves , stems or roots . very often , they look different than plants that do not do this . their leaves may look more fleshy . a very good example of succulents are the cacti . almost all cacti are succulents . not all succulents are cacti though . other plant families with this property are the agavaceae ( agave family ) , azioaceae and crassulaceae . other well-known succulents are the aloe and the bottle tree .
