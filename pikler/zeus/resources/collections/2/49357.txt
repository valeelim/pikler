Manufacturing     manufacturing is producing goods out of raw materials . it is a branch of industry . it includes making very big , complicated things that take a lot of steps to finish . often machines are involved in manufacturing . in the past products were made by handicraft workers using tools and manual labor . in modern times , most manufacturing jobs got replaced by automated manufacturing machines , which made the products much faster and in turn - cheaper than humans could . other websites . - how everyday things are made - engineers edge - manufacturers alliance - logistics quarterly - national association of manufacturers
