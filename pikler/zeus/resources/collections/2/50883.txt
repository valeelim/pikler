The Lost World     the lost world is a book written by sir arthur conan doyle in the year 1912. in it , professor challenger and his companions travel to find a large plateau in south america where dinosaurs still live . after a long journey they find the plateau , and witness not only dinosaurs , but two tribes - an ancient indian people and ape-men who resemble the missing link . after many adventures , they return back to london and attempt to describe their adventures to a disbelieving public .
