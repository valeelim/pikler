1977 Pacific hurricane season     the 1977 pacific hurricane season officially started may 15 , 1977 in the eastern pacific , and june 1 , 1977 in the central pacific , and lasted until november 30 , 1977. these dates conventionally delimit the period of each year when most tropical cyclones form in the northeastern pacific ocean . the season was very inactive . this season had a record low level of activity . only eight tropical storms formed , and four hurricanes . zero major hurricanes formed . the next time this happened was during the 2003 season . in addition , there was just one cyclogenesis in each of may , june , july , august , and october , which is also low . activity in the central pacific was zero , as no storms formed there nor moved in from the east . strangely enough it was also not very active in the eastern pacific or the atlantic . other websites . - central pacific hurricane center archive - unisys storm archive
