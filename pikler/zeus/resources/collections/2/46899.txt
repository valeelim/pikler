Employee     an employee is a term for workers and managers working for a company , organisation or community . these people are the staff of the organization . generally speaking , any person hired by an employer to do a particular job is an employee . in most modern economies the word `` employee '' means a person who works for a corporation . the relationship between employer and employee is different from that between the firm and a customer or client . an employee usually has to provide a resume and have an interview before being offered a job . example of resume format other websites . - nber , science and engineering workforce project - uk gov local business link
