Khachkar     a khachkar or khatchkar ( `` խաչքար '' in armenian , meaning `` cross-stone '' , pronounced as ) is a carved memorial stone found in armenia . it has a crucifix or cross , with a rosette or sun circle below it . other parts can be filled with patterns of leaves , grapes or knots . sometimes it has a ledge at the top with saints ' or characters from the bible . much of the time a khachkar is put up for religious reasons such as the salvation of the soul of a living or a dead person . they have also been removed for other reasons , such as to show a military win , building of a new church , or as a protection from natural disasters . most khachkars are in graveyards . but armenian gravestones take many other forms , and only a small are khatchkars . other websites . - khachkar.am - khatchkar collection at armenica.org
