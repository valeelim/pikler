Mallard     the mallard ( `` anas platyrhynchos '' ) is the best-known and most recognizable of all ducks . it can be found throughout most of north america , europe , asia , new zealand and australia . it is the most common and widespread duck . it can be found in almost any area with a wetland habitat , even in urban areas . the mallard is a large and heavy looking duck . it has a body and a long and broad bill . the male has a dark green head , a yellow bill , is mainly purple-brown on the breast and grey on the body . the female is mainly brown with an orange bill . it breeds in all parts of the europe in summer and winter , wherever there are suitable wetland habitats , although it is scarcer in upland areas .
