Aomori, Aomori    aomori , aomori aomori is the capital city of aomori prefecture . it is northest of the town of honsyu . it is a coastal city , and is nearby tsugaru bay . it has been recognized as a core city since 2006. geography . - aomori - hirosaki - hachinohe the nebuta festival is one of the most famous festivals in japan . it takes place at the beginning of august . more than 300,000 people come and see this festival every year . this festival is called both “ nebuta ” and ” neputa ” . in aomori city , they use the word “ nebuta ” , and in hirosaki city , they use the word “ neputa ” .
