Wembley Stadium (1923)    wembley stadium ( 1923 ) wembley stadium was a sports arena in london , england . it was built in 1923 and was first known as the empire stadium , but because the area it was in is named `` wembley , '' it was renamed `` wembley stadium . '' it featured twin towers by the entrance . as well as being a sports arena , it had also been the site for several concerts . the most famous one is the live aid concert held there in july of 1985. in 2000 , wembley stadium was closed . in 2003 , it was demolished , and a brand-new stadium with the same name was put up in its place .
