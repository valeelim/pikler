Cartridge (electronics)    cartridge ( electronics ) a cartridge means an object that adds different functions or content to an electronic device ( like a video game cartridge ) , or that refills something the device needs ( like an ink cartridge for a printer ) . a cartridge is easy to use . for example , an ink cartridge is easier to use than a bottle of ink . this is because all the ink is inside the cartridge . a cassette is a small cartridge . the words have similar meanings .
