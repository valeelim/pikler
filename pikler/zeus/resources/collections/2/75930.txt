Bergen     bergen is the second largest city and municipality in norway . the city has a population of 275,000 in the municipality , and 238,098 in the urban area . bergen was a county until 1972 , when it was merged with hordaland . the old wharf `` bryggen '' is a world heritage site . bergen is on the west coast of norway , and they export a lot of fish . it has a temperate oceanic climate , with high rainfall every month .
