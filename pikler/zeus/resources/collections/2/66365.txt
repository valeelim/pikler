Bill Medley     bill medley ( born september 19 , 1940 in los angeles , california ) is an american singer and songwriter . he was a founding member of the righteous brothers . he also had a solo career . in 1987 , medley had a hit record with a he sang with jennifer warnes , `` ( i 've had ) the time of my life '' . it was the theme to a popular movie , `` dirty dancing '' .
