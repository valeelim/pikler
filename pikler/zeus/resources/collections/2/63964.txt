University of Washington     the university of washington ( uw ) is the main state university of the u.s. state of washington . there are over 40,000 students . it is in seattle , washington . the university of washington 's sports teams ( the huskies ) are part of the pacific-12 conference of the national collegiate athletic association . every year uw has a rivalry game with washington state university . this game is called the apple cup in which the two football teams play each other . as of the year 2010 the university of washington has won 66 apple cups and washington state university only has 31. the longest consecutive wins of the apple cup belongs to u.w . with 8 consecutive wins , with washington state at 2 consecutive wins .
