Mead     mead is a fermented alcoholic drink . it is made from honey . its history is possibly as old as that of beer . it is made by mixing honey , water , and yeast . some kinds of mead also include spices or fruit . mead is sometimes called `` honey wine '' . archaeologists discovered that people made mead 9,000 years ago . mead was described in the rigveda , which is a hindu religious text . mead is popular in central europe and the balkans . mead is also made in ethiopia and south africa .
