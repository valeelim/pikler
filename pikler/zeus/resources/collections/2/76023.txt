Endocrine gland     endocrine glands are glands that their product ( hormones ) directly into the blood . this group contains the glands of the endocrine system . the main endocrine glands include the pituitary gland , the pancreas , the gonads , the thyroid gland and the adrenal glands . other organs which are not so well known for their endocrine activity include the stomach , which produces such hormones as ghrelin .
