Aeneas     aeneas was a trojan prince and hero . his father was anchises , his mother was the greek goddess aphrodite ( venus in the case of roman sources ) . both homer and vergil mention him in their stories . vergil wrote a whole series of books about him . these books are called aeneis in their original latin . the usual english translation of the title is `` aeneid '' . the books are the story of `` '' pious aeneas '' . and about how the city of rome came to be . the tale starts with aeneas carrying his father out of the burning troy , and end with romulus and remus founding rome ( after aeneas landed on the coast , after sailing the mediterranean sea for a long time ) . aeneas also appears in book 5 of the `` iliad '' written by homer . william shakespeare also wrote a story mentioning aeneas . christopher marlowe wrote a play about aeneas and dido , the queen who fell in love with him . there is also an opera about dido and aeneas , written by henry purcell . related pages . - pope pius ii
