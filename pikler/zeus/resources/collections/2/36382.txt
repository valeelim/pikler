Hsinchu County     hsinchu county ( chinese : 新竹縣 ; poj : sin-tek-kōan ) is a county in northwestern taiwan . the population is mainly hakka ; there is a taiwanese aboriginal minority in the southeastern part of the county . hsinchu county controls one township-level city ( 縣轄市 ) , three city-townships ( 鎮 ) , and nine rural townships ( 鄉 ) . related pages . - list of counties of republic of china other websites . - government website | ( english )
