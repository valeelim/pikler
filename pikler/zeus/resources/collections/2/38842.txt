Woodworking     woodworking is a skill . in woodworking , one can cut or sand ( make wood smooth ) . when one is doing woodworking , be careful of being hurt . remember to wear safety goggles or a facemask ( like safety goggles . but to protect ones face ) . do not wear long clothing , because it can get stuck in a tool . there are a lot of woodworking classes , many of them are offered in middle school . in woodworking , the type of wood you get is very important . there are many kinds of softwood and many kinds of hardwood . the words hard and soft wood comes from the type of tree it came from . a hard pice of wood could be softwood . there are a lot of tools that people use during woodworking , such as sandpaper ( used to make wood smooth to touch ) , files , rasps ( all used for trimming wood , cutting , etc . ) , saws ( cutting wood ) , and automatic saws ( saws that use electricity to run ) .
