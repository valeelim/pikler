Public figure     a public figure is a legal term applied in the context of defamation actions ( libel and slander ) , as well as invasion of privacy . a public figure ( such as a politician , celebrity , or business leader ) can not base a lawsuit on incorrect harmful statements unless there is proof that the writer or publisher acted with malice ( knowledge or reckless disregard for the truth ) . the burden of proof is higher in the case of a public figure .
