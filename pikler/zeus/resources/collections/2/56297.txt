Polyhydroxyalkanoate     polyhydroxyalkanoate ( pha ) is a biopolymer that is made by bacteria . it can break down naturally ( it is biodegradable ) . pha has the same qualities of plastic , but it uses renewable raw material such as sugar from crop , instead of finite natural resources such as crude oil , natural gas and coal . some phas share properties with polypropylene , a plastic used to make ropes and packaging . others are similar to rubber .
