Zoo Tycoon 2     zoo tycoon 2 is a 2004 simulation computer game . in the game , the player must create and run a zoo . this is done by managing its finances , employees and animalexhibits . the goal is to become a `` zoo tycoon '' . the game is a sequel the 2001 game `` zoo tycoon '' . six expansion packs have been created for the game . the latest is `` zoo tycoon 2 : extinct animals '' .
