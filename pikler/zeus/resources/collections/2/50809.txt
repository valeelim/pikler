Flip-flop     in footwear and fashion , flip-flops are a kind of flat , backless sandal that consist of a flat sole held on the foot by a u-shaped strap that passes between the first ( big ) and second toes and around either side of the foot , attached to the sole at three points , commonly known as the thong strap . they appear to have been developed out of traditional japanese woven or wooden soled sandals ( `` i.e . `` , zori and setta ) in australia . they are quite trendy nowadays and one of the best alternative for boots in summer .
