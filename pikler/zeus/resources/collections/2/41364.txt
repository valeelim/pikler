Bunsen burner     a bunsen burner is a common piece of laboratory equipment . it is commonly used for heating chemical substances , sterilization , and combustion . it works by burning flammable gas . it is named after robert wilhelm bunsen . bunsen was a german chemist . bunsen did not invent the burner , he made it better . originally , michael faraday developed the bunsen burner . a bunsen burner has different types of flames , with each having a different colour . bunsen burners can be very hot .
