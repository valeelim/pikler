Rodrigo Santoro     rodrigo junqueira dos reis santoro ( born august 22 , 1975 ) is a brazilian actor . he started his career acting in different soap operas in his country . in the last few years , he has become known after his roles in the movies `` love actually '' and `` 300 '' . santoro was born in petrópolis , rio de janeiro state . he is half of italian descent . awards and nominations . - brasília film festival - 2000 – best actor for `` bicho de sete cabeças '' ( won ) - recife film festival - 2001 – best actor for `` bicho de sete cabeças '' ( won ) - brazil quality award - 2001 – best actor for `` bicho de sete cabeças '' ( won ) - santo domingo film festival - 2001 – best actor for `` bicho de sete cabeças '' ( won ) - sesc best film festival - 2001 – best actor for `` bicho de sete cabeças '' ( won ) - cinema brazilian grand prix - 2002 – best actor for `` bicho de sete cabeças '' ( won ) - apca trophy - 2002 – best actor for `` bicho de sete cabeças '' ( won ) - cartagena film festival - 2002 – best actor for `` bicho de sete cabeças '' ( won )
