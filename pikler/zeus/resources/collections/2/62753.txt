Electrology     electrology is an electrical process to remove human hair so that it does not grow back . an electrologist is sometimes called an electrolysist in the united kingdom . the process of removing the hair is referred to as electrolysis . the electrolysist slides a thin metal probe into each hair follicle . this , if done the right way , does not hurt the skin . electricity flows to the follicle through the probe . this causes damage to the areas that grow hair so that it does not grow back . other websites . - american electrology association - british institute and association of electrolysis - the society for clinical & medical hair removal , inc. - international guild of hair removal specialists , inc. - hairfacts - electrolysis medical data - hairtell - electrolysis discussion - electrolysis discussion group
