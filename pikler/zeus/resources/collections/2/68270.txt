Caste     the caste system is a system in india that was once mainly used by hindus . it started as a job system but later became a social class system . there are four castes in this system . - brahmin - priests - kshatriya - warrior , ruler , politician , administrative worker - vaishya - trader , merchant , farmer - shudra - peasant , servant - `` untouchables '' , or dalits - came much later after the other four groups and consisted of people who swept ashes after cremations or who tanned animals ' skins . there are also castes in some countries other than india .
