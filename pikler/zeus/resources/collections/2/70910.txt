Sütterlin     sütterlinschrift , sütterlin or suetterlin script is a form of german handwriting . it was taught in german schools from 1915 to 1941. in the 19th century the common form of german handwriting was german kurrent . it uses very thin lines and sharp edges . many students in primary school found it difficult to learn . so in 1911 the german designer and teacher ludwig sütterlin was asked to design a simpler script . sütterlin script has thicker lines and rounder edges than german kurrent . it can be easily written with any pen . in everyday language some people call any handwritten blackletter script `` sütterlin '' . other pages . - blackletter other websites . - the sütterlin script at omniglot - freeware sütterlin font - learn sütterlin , a lesson , with sample texts
