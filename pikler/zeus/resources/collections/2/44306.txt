Chad Kroeger     chad robert turton ( better known as chad kroeger ) is a canadian singer and guitarist for the rock band , nickelback . he was born on november 15 , 1974 , in hanna , alberta , canada . kroeger is of american , english , german , irish , norwegian , scottish , swiss-german , and welsh descent . kroeger became engaged to canadian singer avril lavigne in 2012. the couple married in 2013 and separated in 2015. nickelback consists of four band members . chad kroeger ( vocals , lead guitar ) , mike kroeger ( bass guitar ) , ryan peake ( rhythm guitar ) and daniel adair ( drums ) .
