1484     events . - july 6 – portuguese sea captain diogo cão finds the mouth of congo river - september 15 – peter arbues , is assassinated while praying in the cathedral at saragossa , spain and he died on september 17. he had been appointed inquisitor of aragon by the inquisitor general , tomás de torquemada , in the campaign against heresy and crypto-judaism . - december 5 – pope innocent viii gives the inquisition a mission to hunt heretics and witches in germany with the lead of heinrich kramer and jacob sprenger . - the first sugar mill becomes operational in the gran canaria . - first cuirassier units ( kyrissers ) formed in austria .
