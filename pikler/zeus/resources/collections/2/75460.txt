List of Presidents of the German Bundesrat     the president of the german bundesrat ( ) is the german bundesrat 's chairperson or speaker . the office rotates among the ministers president of each of the federal `` länder '' annually . the new bundesratspräsident is elected during october and takes office on 1 november . in 1950 the ministers president of the länder agreed to elect the bundesratspräsident on a rota , so that every state had a chance to preside over the bundesrat and to show that the bundesrat represents all of germany , rather than the most successful political party . the rota is based on the state 's population . other pages . - president of the german bundesrat other websites . - `` präsidenten des bundesrates seit 1949 '' ( in german ) - official bundesrat website ( english ) .
