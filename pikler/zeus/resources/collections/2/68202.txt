1960 Winter Olympics     the 1960 winter olympics , officially known as the viii olympic winter games , were held in 1960 in squaw valley , california , united states ( in the lake tahoe basin ) . squaw valley won the bid in 1955. alexander cushing , the creator of the resort , campaigned vigorously to win the games . after being awarded the games , there was a rush to construct roads , hotels , restaurants , and bridges , as well as the ice arena , the speed skating track , ski lifts , and the ski jumping hill .
