Tony Romo     antonio `` tony '' ramiro romo ( born april 22 , 1980 in san diego , california ) is an american football quarterback who plays for the dallas cowboys of the national football league . he played college football at eastern illinois . he is viewed by many as the top quarterback in the nfl . romo was signed by the cowboys as an undrafted free agent in 2003. he has had success with dallas , and is well known by the media . he has the 3rd highest passer rating in nfl history .
