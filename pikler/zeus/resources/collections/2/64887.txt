Ella Enchanted     ella enchanted is a newbery honor book . it was written by gail carson levine . it was published in 1997. it is also the title of the american movie based on the novel , released april 9 , 2004. the movie was directed by tommy o'haver and starred anne hathaway and hugh dancy . the story is a retelling of `` cinderella '' featuring various mythical creatures including elves , ogres , dwarves and giants .
