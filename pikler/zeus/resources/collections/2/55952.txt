Electric power     electric power is defined as the power dissipated by an electric circuit . electric power is a measurement of the rate at which energy is used over a period of time . the si unit for power is the watt , the unit for energy is the joule , and the unit for time is the second , and the unit for electric current is the ampere . when electric current flows through a circuit its slowed down by the resistance in the circuit . devices can convert this current to useful forms of work , such as heat radiation , light emission , mechanical motion , or acoustic vibrations . related pages . - electric power transmission - electric power distribution references . - reports on august 2003 blackout , north american electric reliability council website
