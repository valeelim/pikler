Nezami     nezami ( hakīm nizām ad-dīn abū muhammad ilyās ibn-yusūf ibn-zakī ibn-mu'ayyid nezāmi ganjavi ( 11411209 ) ) was a turkish poet . nezāmi was born in ganja , now azerbaijan . he wrote five poetry books . in 1169/1170 the king of darband sent him the maiden afaq , and nizami married her . in 1174 his son muhammad was born . nizami gencevi wrote five poems . the first of them was `` sirler xezinesi '' . the others are `` xosrov ve shirin '' , `` leyli ve mecnun '' , `` yeddi gozel '' and `` isgendername '' .
