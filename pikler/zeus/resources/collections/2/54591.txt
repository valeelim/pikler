Suwanee, Georgia    suwanee , georgia suwanee is a city in the american state of georgia . it is in gwinnett county . in 2005 , about 12,500 people lived in suwanee . according to the united states census bureau , the city has a total area of 25.6 km² ( 9.9 mi² ) . 25.4 km² ( 9.8 mi² ) of it is land and 0.2 km² ( 0.1 mi² ) of it ( 0.71 % ) is water .
