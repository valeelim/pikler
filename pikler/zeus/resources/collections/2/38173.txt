New Orleans Privateers     the new orleans privateers college sports teams for the university of new orleans ( uno ) . they play in the ncaa division i for intercollegiate athletics . the schools teams are a part of the sun belt conference . the university of new orleans does not currently have a football team . the university has teams in the following sports : - baseball ( men 's ) - basketball ( men 's and women 's teams ) - golf ( men 's ) - swimming and diving - tennis ( men 's and women 's teams ) - volleyball ( women 's ) the school did have a football team from 1965 until 1970. during that time , the team had a record of 12 wins , 10 losses and 1 tie . they also won 3 district . other websites . - unoprivateers.com ( official website )
