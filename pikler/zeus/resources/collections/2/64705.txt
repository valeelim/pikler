Fiqh     fiqh or islamic jurisprudence ( ) is an expansion of sharia law and is meant to be used with fatwas by islamic clerics ( known as 'ulema ' in arabic ) to help muslims not break sharia law . fiqh is a section of islamic law which deals with acts of muslim , that includes both worship and daily life actions . in sunni islam there are four main schools of thought , they are : - hanafi - maliki - shafi'i - hanbali the different schools of thought are not different beliefs but different views . in shia islam there is one main school of thought , it is called jaferi
