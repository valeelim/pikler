Valence (Tarn-et-Garonne)    valence ( tarn-et-garonne ) valence is a village in the midi-pyrénées region of france . it is in the department tarn-et-garonne . in 1999 , about 4800 people lived there . together with 23 other , similar villages , it formed the `` communauté de communes des deux rives '' . in that structure ( that is , in all the villages together ) there are about 15.500 people . valence is located 25 km from agen , 45 km from montauban , 60 km from cahors , 90 km from toulouse and 160 km from bordeaux
