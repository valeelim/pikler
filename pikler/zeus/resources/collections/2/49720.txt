List of plants by common name     the common names of plants often vary from region to region , which is why most plant encyclopedias refer to plants using binomials , or `` latin '' names . common names are also referred to as `` country names '' or `` farmer 's names '' . these names develop over time according to use , appearance , and lore . most plants have several country names , and country names often refer to several distinct plants . this list includes garden plants as well as weeds . this is by no means a complete list . most of the common names here so far are regional names from north america . __notoc__ sources and references . - johnny 's plant profiles
