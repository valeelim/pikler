Hrant Dink     hrant dink ( , ipa : [ həɹɑnt diːnk ] ) ( 15 september 1954 – 19 january 2007 ) was a turkish-armenian editor , journalist and columnist . as editor-in-chief of the bilingual turkish-armenian newspaper `` agos '' ( ակօս ) , dink was a prominent member of the armenian minority in turkey . hrant dink was assassinated in istanbul on 19 january 2007 , by ogün samast , a 17 year old ultra-nationalist turk .
