Spinach     spinach is a green , leafy vegetable . it comes from southwestern and central asia . its flowers are small and yellow . spinach is rich in vitamin k , vitamin a , vitamin c , and folate . spinach , along with other green , leafy vegetables , is rich in iron . here are some perceptions about spinach that come from popular culture : - spinach is traditionally unpopular with children . - the cartoon character popeye eats spinach in order to become strong .
