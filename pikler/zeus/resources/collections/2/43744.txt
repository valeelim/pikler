1936 Summer Olympics     the 1936 summer olympics , officially known as the games of the xi olympiad , were held in berlin , germany . the games were thought to be successful . the costs of the games was mostly paid for by the german government . a total of 49 nations sent athletes to compete at the berlin games . related pages . - list of ioc country codes - olympic football tournament 1936 ( austria ) other websites . - berlin 1936 at olympic.org
