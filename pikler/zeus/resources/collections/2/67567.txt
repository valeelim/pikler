Offering     offering can mean any of the following : - an offering is a donation during religious worship . see also alms , tithe , charity , gift and virtue . - an offering can mean a religious sacrifice of plant , animal or human life ( blood sacrifice ) , or a sacrifice of precious things . an offering may also be in money , or may be of a purely spiritual nature , like offering one 's heart or life to god ( see also oblation ) . - an offering , in buddhism , is a form of prayer .
