Heretic (video game)    heretic ( video game ) heretic is a first-person shooter video game made by id software for computers . it is like doom , but it takes place in a fantasy world . the monsters are mostly not human . the player has the ability to look up and down , which they could not do in doom . the weapons are also fantasy weapons . it uses the same base as doom and doom 2. it has two sequels : hexen and heretic 2. other websites . - official `` heretic '' website from raven - official `` heretic '' website from id software
