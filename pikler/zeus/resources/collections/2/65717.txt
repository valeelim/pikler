Keihan Main Line     the keihan main line ( 京阪本線 , `` -honsen '' ) is a railway line of keihan electric railway . the line starts from sanjo station in kyoto , and ends at yodoyabashi station in osaka , and travels a distance of about 51 km . the line is not `` the main line '' but `` the keihan main line '' . trains go direct to the oto line . stations . - ● : stop , ↓ : pass - ▲ : k-limited express trains bound for yodoyabashi stop at hirakata-shi in the morning . express trains pass through hirakata-koen and moriguchi-shi from the first train until 8:00 a.m. ( jst ) and at midnight , and express trains for demachiyanagi stop at yodo . some express trains stop at yodo during horse racing events . sub express trains pass through moriguchishi from the first train until 8:00 a.m. ( jst ) . - local trains ( 普通 ) stop at all stations .
