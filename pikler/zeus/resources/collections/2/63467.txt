Van Province     van ( ) is a province in eastern turkey , between lake van and the iranian border . it is 19,069 km in area and has a population of 1,035,418 at the end of 2010. kurds form the majority . in the 9th century bc , the van area was the center of the urartian kingdom . for centuries after that , the area was a major armenian population center . after world war i armenian population was forced to leave and was cleansed during the armenian genocide . other pages . - van resistance other websites . - pictures of the capital of this province
