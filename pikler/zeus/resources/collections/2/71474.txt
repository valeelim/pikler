T-Mobile     t-mobile is a mobile ( cell phone ) network operator headquartered in bonn , germany . it was founded in 1990 and is a part of deutsche telekom and belongs to the freemove alliance . t-mobile is a group of mobile phone companies ( all owned by deutsche telekom ) that operate gsm and umts networks in europe and the united states . the `` t '' stands for `` telekom '' . most subsidiaries ( companies ) of deutsche telekom have names beginning with `` t- '' like t-home and t-online . t-mobile also owns parts of mobile operators in eastern europe . globally , t-mobile has 101 million subscribers , making it the world 's sixth largest mobile phone service provider by subscribers and the third largest multinational after the united kingdom 's vodafone and spain 's telefonica . t-mobile international has a large presence in eleven european countries : austria , croatia , czech republic , germany , hungary , macedonia , montenegro , the netherlands , poland , slovakia and the united kingdom as well as in the united states . deutsche telekom recently tried to buy rival mobile network operator o2 , but it was eventually bought by spain 's telefonica .
