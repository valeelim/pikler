Clandestine chemistry     clandestine chemistry is chemistry that occurs in a lab that is used for making illegal drugs , usually using unsafe equipment . the use of this unsafe laboratory equipment will sometimes result in explosions , fires or heavy flows of noxious fumes . meth labs are the main form of clandestine laboratories and are used for making methamphetamine ( crystal meth ) , a kind of illegal drug . `` clandestine '' means `` secret '' .
