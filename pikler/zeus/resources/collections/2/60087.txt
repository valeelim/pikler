Pedal     a pedal is something that is made to work by someone 's foot . the word `` pedal '' comes from the latin word for `` foot '' . many things can be made to work by pedals . these include : - a car ( automobile ) which has a brake pedal , an accelerator pedal and sometimes a clutch ; - a musical instrument such as piano , harp or organ ( see pedal ( music ) ) ; - a spinning wheel - a sewing machine ( in the days before electricity )
