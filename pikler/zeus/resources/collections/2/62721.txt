Samson (band)    samson ( band ) samson was a british heavy metal band between 1977 and 2002. it was considered to be part of the new wave of british heavy metal . its first album , `` survivors '' , received good reviews , making the band more popular . iron maiden lead singer bruce dickinson was in the band before he joined iron maiden . dickinson went by the name `` bruce bruce '' while he was in samson . the band kept making music until 2002 , when guitarist paul samson , who started the band , died of cancer . other websites . - samson on `` allmusic '' - samson on `` billboard ''
