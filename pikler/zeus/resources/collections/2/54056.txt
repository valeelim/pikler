Trenčín     trenčín ( german : `` trentschin '' ; hungarian : `` trencsén '' ; latin : `` laugaritio '' ) is a town in western slovakia ( close to the czech border ) on the váh river . the site is inhabited since time immemorial . trenčín is best known for roman writing below the trenčín castle . in the 13th century , the castle was home to the legendary matthew csák , `` lord of the river váh and the tatra mountains '' . partner towns . - cran-gevrier , france - uherské hradiště , czech republic - zlín , czech republic - tarnów , poland - casalecchio di reno , italy - békéscsaba , hungary - kragujevac , serbia other websites . - official site
