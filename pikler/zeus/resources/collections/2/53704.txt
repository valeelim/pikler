Repperndorf     repperndorf is a village in regierungsbezirk , lower franconia , bavaria , germany . since 1977 it is part of the town of kitzingen . repperndorf has a population of 260 people . the village has a graveyard and two churches , one for catholics and one for protestants . it also has one restaurant and one soccer club . there was the famous `` schnitzel factory '' but now it moved to kitzingen .
