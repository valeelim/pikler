Patti LaBelle     patti labelle ( born patricia louise holt on may 24 , 1944 ) is an american r & b and soul singer . she is known for her high vocal belting ( using the chest to make high pitch sounds ) . she is known for such songs as `` new attitude '' and `` on my own '' . she was the lead singer of patty la belle and the bluebells during the 1960s and 70s . later the group was known as simply `` labell '' . in this later group the style was more laid back , singing hits like the rolling stones ' `` wild horses '' .
