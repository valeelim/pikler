Beam bridge     a beam bridge is a strong , horizontal structure that rests on two end supports , and carries traffic by acting as a beam . beam bridges are often used for heavy cars and trains to pass , and some are also built for people to walk on . beam bridges used to be made from wood and were called `` log bridges '' . modern ones are mostly made of steel . beam bridges are usually placed over highways or gaps in the earth so people or cars can cross over them . a beam bridge can not be as long as more complex kinds . beam bridges can be connected to make one longer bridge .
