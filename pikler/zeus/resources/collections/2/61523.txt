Shaman     a shaman is a kind of medicine man who practices shamanism . shamans were believed to be able to talk to the spirit world , usually animal spirits . the job of the shamans are believed to be is healing , weather control or any other supernatural stuff . in some tribes shamanism is important such as the yanomamis ( a venezuelan tribe in the venezuelan forest ) a shaman was a person who had a special connection to the spiritual world.. related pages . - spiritism
