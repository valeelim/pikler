Demi Moore     demi moore ( born november 11 , 1962 as demi guynes ) is an american actress . she became well-known after a string of 1980s teen-oriented movies , and was one of the best known actresses of 1990s hollywood . she has been married to musician freddy moore and actors bruce willis and ashton kutcher . all her marriages ended in divorce . she has three daughters by willis : rumer , scout and tallulah .
