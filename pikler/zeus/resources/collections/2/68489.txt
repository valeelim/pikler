Huang Taiji     huang taiji ( ; 1592 - 1643 ) was the emperor of qing dynasty . his surname was aisin gioro . his reign names were tian cong and chong de . huang taiji was the eighth son of taizu emperor ( nurhaci ) . he reigned the manchu trible from 1627 until 1643. in 1612 , huang taiji followed his father , nurhaci , to attack wu la . he was awarded `` beile '' , a traditional title of manchu nobility below prince . in 1616 , he was ordered to deal with government affairs with another beili , dai shan . huang became the `` khan '' ( king ) of the manchu trible in n 1626. in 1636 , huang took control of the southern part of mongol , the north-eastern part of china and korea . he started the qing dynasty . huang taiji died in 1643 of apoplexy .
