Ani (god)    ani ( god ) in etruscan mythology , ani is god of the sky . he is shown as living in the highest heaven . he is sometimes shown with two faces , possibly showing a link with the akkadian god anu and the roman god janus . references . - jordan , michael `` encyclopedia of gods : over 2,500 deities of the world '' new york : facts on file 1993 - fischer , richard james `` historical genesis : from adam to abraham '' university press of america , 2007
