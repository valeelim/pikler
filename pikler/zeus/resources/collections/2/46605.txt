Rosales     rosales is an order of flowering plants . it includes nine families . the plants are grouped together because their genetics are similar . some well-known members of rosales include : roses ; strawberries , blackberries and raspberries ; apples and pears ; plums , peaches and apricots ; almonds ; rowan and hawthorn ; elms ; figs ; nettles ; and hops and cannabis . the true roses are rosaceae .
