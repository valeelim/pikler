Jorvik     jórvík was the viking name for the english city of york and the kingdom centred there . york had been founded as the roman legionary fortress of `` eboracum '' and revived as the anglo-saxon trading port of `` eoforwic '' . it was first captured in november 866 by a large army of danish vikings . other websites . - brenda ralph lewis & david nash ford , `` york : viking times '' - timeline of anglo-saxon england - jorvik viking centre - york archaeological trust - welcome to jorvik : links on on-line resources
