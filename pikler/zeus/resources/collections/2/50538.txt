Gioachino Rossini     gioachino rossini ( born pesaro 29 february 1792 ; died passy 13 november 1868 ) was an italian composer . he was the most famous composer of operas in his time . his operas had lots of new ideas . italian operas had become rather unimaginative , with composers such as cimarosa and paisiello writing the same sort of thing each time . rossini made his operas interesting by writing skillfully for the singers , giving them good tunes , as well as giving the orchestra interesting music , and by choosing a variety of stories for his operas . the opera for which he is best known today is the `` il barbiere di siviglia '' ( `` the barber of seville '' ) . many of the overtures to his operas are played at orchestral concerts . the most popular is the overture to his opera `` guillaume tell '' ( `` william tell '' ) with its feature in the british tv show `` the adventures of william tell '' during the last 40 years of his life rossini wrote no more music . his friends begged him to write another opera but he always refused .
