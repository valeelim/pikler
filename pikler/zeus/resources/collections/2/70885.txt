Maya languages     the maya language ( or mayan languages ) is a group of languages spoken in central america . mayan languages are spoken by at least 6 million native maya . most of them are in guatemala , mexico , or belize . in 1996 , guatemala formally recognized 21 mayan languages by name , and mexico recognises eight more . the mayan language family is one of the best documented and most studied in the americas . modern mayan languages come from proto-mayan , a language which was probably spoken at least 5000 years ago . it has been partially reconstructed .
