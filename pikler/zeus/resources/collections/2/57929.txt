Indian Solar Loan Programme     the united nation environment programme 's indian solar loan programme has won the `` world award for sustainability '' for helping to establish a householder loan program for solar home power systems . over the span of three years more than 16,000 solar home systems have been financed through 2,000 bank branches , mainly in areas of south india where the electricity grid does not yet extend . other pages . - energy policy of india other websites . - solar loans light up rural india
