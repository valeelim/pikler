Cyperus papyrus     cyperus papyrus ( also known as papyrus sedge , bulrush or paper reed ) is a plant . it is a sedge . they grow tall . the plant is perennial ( grows every year ) and originally comes from africa . it was used to make a fabric much like paper . this was called papyrus , and served much the same purpose that paper does today . its roots were also used as fuels . also makes rope and sandals too . it is almost extinct along the shores of the river nile where it was originally cultivated .
