Grand Vizier     grand vizier , sadr-ı azam ( sadrazam ) or serdar-ı ekrem ( in ottoman turkish `` صدر اعظم '' or `` وزیر اعظم '' ) , was the greatest minister of the sultan . the grand vizier had absolute power of attorney and could only be removed by the sultan . he held the imperial seal and he could gather all other viziers to attend meetings regarding state affairs . the title is derived originally from the persian word `` vizier '' ( وزير ) . other pages . - list of ottoman grand viziers
