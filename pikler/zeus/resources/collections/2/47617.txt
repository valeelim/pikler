Data migration     data migration is a term computer scientists and economists use when they speak about moving data from one location to another . it can also mean that files , or folders with documents , or databases are moved from one server to another . this might be that because several databases are merged into one , or because the databases are reorganised . it is usually an automated process , supported by data migration tools . if the data structure is complex , the migration may be separated in several phases that are carried out consecutively .
