Morena Baccarin     morena baccarin ( born june 2 , 1979 ) is a brazilian actress . she is best known for her role as inara serra in the sci-fi television series `` firefly '' and the movie `` serenity '' . morena was born in rio de janeiro , brazil . her father is of italian descent . her mother is brazilian stage and television actress vera setta . when she was 10 , baccarin moved with her family to greenwich village , new york , in the united states . she later attended the fiorello h. laguardia high school of music & art and performing arts ( the high school in the movie `` fame '' ) before she studied theater at juilliard . interviews . - when asked in an interview `` if she 's up for the role of `` wonder woman '' , `` baccarin replied : `` i ’ m not going to say no to a superhero but it ’ s not up to me .
