Ariwara no Narihira     ariwara no narihira ( , 825 - 9 july 880 ) was a japanese poet and noble . he was the fifth son of prince abo , a son of emperor heizei . his mother princess ito was a daughter of emperor kammu , so he was family to emperor kammu by both his mother and father . along with his other brothers , he was forced to have a civilian life and did not live in the imperial court , getting a new clan name , ariwara .
