The Internationale     `` the internationale '' ( , ; , , , ) is a very well known french communist , socialist , and social democratic/democratic socialist song . the russian and chinese versions are also very famous . the russian version , written by аркадий яковлевич коц ( arkady yakolevich kots ) , was , before 1944 , the national anthem of the union of soviet socialist republics . the french words , written by eugene pottier , were originally supposed to be sung to the tune of `` la marseillaise '' , but a separate tune was created for it by a belgian named pierre degeyter . the song is known by its tune , and its chorus , `` c'est la lutte finale / groupons-nous , et demain / l'internationale / sera le genre humain '' . the chinese lyrics were written by 瞿秋白 , or qu qiubai . other websites . - downloadable recordings in more than 30 languages - another large collection
