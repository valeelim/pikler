Ferris wheel     ferris wheels ( sometimes called big wheels in the united kingdom ) are large , round , revolving structures with gondolas where people sit . they are popular at amusement parks and fairs . they are named after george washington gale ferris , jr. , who made the first modern wheel for the world 's columbian exposition in 1893. when he built it , it was 264 feet tall . they called it the `` chicago wheel '' after chicago , illinois where it was on display .
