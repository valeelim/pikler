Spyware     spyware is a category of software for computers . spyware is malware that collects some data , usually without the computer users ' knowledge . very often , this data is then sent over the internet to someone else . very often , this is used for marketing . spyware can also be used to steal data from computers . one kind is a keylogger which can see whatever you are typing . keyloggers can steal important information like passwords that you type . the best protection is to get a program that protects against spyware . but be careful , many websites that claim to have them are actually spyware , not something to protect against it . this method of installing spyware is called a trojan horse which is disguised as something useful but when you open it , it can see what keys you press and it can log into your accounts . this makes it easy for the trojan to collect your passwords and important data . spyware tracks your computer and uses some of the things on it . this includes your files , your downloads , and usernames/passwords for websites and programs
