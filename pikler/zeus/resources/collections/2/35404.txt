Lodi     lodi is a city in northern italy . lodi is in the lombardy region with a population of 43,488 people ( as of 2008 ) . the people who live in lodi are called lodigiani . lodi is famous for a cake called tortionata . lodi was built after the destruction of the ancient roman city of laus pompeia during the war with milan . the construction of the new city was aided by the german emperor frederick i barbarossa .
