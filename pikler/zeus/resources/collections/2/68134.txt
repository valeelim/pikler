Meng Tian     meng tian ( ; ? - 210 bc ) was a chinese general of the qin dynasty under emperor qin shi huang . his grandfather , meng ao , and his father , meng wu , were generals as well . after the unification of china , emperor qin shi huang ordered meng tian leading 300,000 armies to attack the xiongnu and build the great wall of china . meng tian was forced by the eunuch , zhao gao , to kill himself by drinking poisonous wine after the death of emperor qin shi haung .
