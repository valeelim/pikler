Salvador Dalí     salvador dalí ( 11 may 1904 – 23 january 1989 ) was a spanish painter who became famous for the unusual images he used in his paintings . he was born in figueres , catalonia , spain . he was a key figure in surrealist art . his most famous work was `` the persistence of memory '' ( 1931 ) , which is now in moma , the museum of modern art in new york . it is a dream-like landscape with a soft , melted pocket-watch . dali died of heart failure in figueras . related pages . - surrealism
