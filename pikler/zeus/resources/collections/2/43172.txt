Raven-Symoné     raven-symone christina pearman ( born december 10 , 1985 ) is an american actress and singer . she was born in atlanta , georgia . she moved to ossining , new york at age three . symone is most well known as the person raven in the tv show `` that 's so raven '' and other parts in `` the cheetah girls '' and `` the cheetah girls 2 '' . she simply goes by the name raven for most of her acting . in august 2013 , raven-symoné , gives no label to her sexuality , commented on legalizing gay marriage , `` i was excited to hear today that more states legalized gay marriage . i , however am not currently getting married , but it is great to know i can now , should i wish to '' . she was dating azmarie livingston since 2012 , and in october 2015 , she announced that she had broken up with her .
