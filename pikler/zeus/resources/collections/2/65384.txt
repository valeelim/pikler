Kyoto Station     kyoto station ( 京都駅 , `` kyōto-eki '' ) is the most important transportation center in kyoto , japan . it has japan 's second-largest train station building ( after nagoya station ) . it is also one of the country 's largest buildings , with a shopping mall , hotel , movie theater , isetan department store , and several local government facilities under one 15-story roof . it also housed the kyoto city air terminal until august 31 , 2002. other websites . - official site
