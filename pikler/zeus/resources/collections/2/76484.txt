Mandé people     mandé is a ethnic group of west africa . these people speak mande languages and they live in gambia , guinea , guinea-bissau , senegal , mali , sierra leone , liberia , burkina faso , and côte d'ivoire . the mande languages are in the niger-congo family . these people made the largest west african empires . some people think that the mandé people are not a racial group , but are a cultural group instead .
