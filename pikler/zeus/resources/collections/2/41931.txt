Strontium     strontium is a chemical element . it has the chemical symbol sr. it has the atomic number 38. it is a metal . the colour of the metal is silver-white or yellow-silver . the metal is soft . in chemistry it is placed in a group of metal elements named the alkaline earth metals . strontium has a high chemical reactivity . the metal turns yellow when exposed to air . strontium has properties similar to those of its two vertical neighbours in the periodic table , calcium and barium . it is found naturally in the minerals celestite and strontianite . the sr isotope is present in radioactive fallout and has a half-life of 28.90 years . strontium forms salts which make a red flame when they burn . they are used in flares for signalling the position of survivors or shipwrecks and to make the red colour in fireworks .
