Sharin Yamano     sharin yamano is a japanese manga artist . he is well known for his books , `` manga kenkanryu '' ( hate korea wave ) and `` manga kenchogokuryu '' ( hate china wave ) . he is thought to be anti-korean and anti-chinese , but the detail of his profile is not known well . in his mangas , yamano says that koreans and chinese must thank the japanese , japanese are very special people in asia . other pages . - doronpa
