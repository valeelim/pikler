Repo Man (movie)    repo man ( movie ) repo man is a 1984 comedy/science fiction movie starring emilio estevez and harry dean stanton . it was written and directed by alex cox . the movie is about the life of a repo man , a person who steals cars legally from people who do not pay for them . the movie is well regarded for several reasons . first , it has talk and events that are both funny and strange . the music played in the movie is also a very good collection of punk rock music that was popular at the time . also , the movie is not similar to most other movies that were made at the same time .
