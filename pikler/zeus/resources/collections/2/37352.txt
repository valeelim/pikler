Bowser (character)    bowser ( character ) bowser is a video game character . he is the evil koopa that takes over the mushroom kingdom . he is a devil version of a normal koopa . he was ranked # 1 in the top 50 video game villains of all time list by the `` guinness world records gamer 's edition 2013 '' . bowser 's first appearance was in `` super mario bros. '' as a final boss . the first seven castles had bowsers , but they are fake . only the eighth bowser is real . bowser 's kids , called the koopalings , were bosses in `` super mario bros. 3 '' , `` super mario world '' , `` new super mario bros. wii '' , `` new super mario bros. 2 '' , and `` new super mario bros. u '' . in `` super mario world '' , he appears in a clown car and throws mecha koopas . when mario moved to the nintendo 64 with `` super mario 64 '' , bowser returned as the main villain . in the remake of this game , super mario 64 ds , he is fought as a boss three times . he also appears in the disney movie `` wreck-it ralph '' . other websites . - bowser on giant bomb
