Base pair     in molecular biology , two nucleotides on opposite complementary dna strands that are connected by hydrogen bonds are called a base pair ( often abbreviated bp ) . in dna pairing , adenine ( a ) forms a base pair with thymine ( t ) , to form an a/t base pair . guanine ( g ) pairs with cytosine ( c ) in dna , to form a g/c base pair . related pages . - dna - nucleobase
