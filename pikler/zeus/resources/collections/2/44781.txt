Kiwa hirsuta     kiwa hirsuta is a crustacean that was first found living on the pacific ocean floor at 7200 feet deep , 900 miles south of easter island in march 2005. it is about 6 inches long , or 15 centimeters . it looks a little like a crab , but its long arms are covered with yellow fur that looks like silk , so its discoverers , from the monterey bay aquarium research insititute , named it the yeti crab or yeti lobster . but it is so different from other crustaceans that it was given its own scientific family , kiwaidae . other websites . - `` furry lobster found in pacific '' at bbc , march 8 2006 .
