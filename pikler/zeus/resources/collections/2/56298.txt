SpVgg Bayreuth     spvgg bayreuth is a german football team in bayreuth , bavaria . it was founded in 1921. the club was banned in 1933 by the nazis but restarted shortly after world war ii . spvgg bayreuth plays in one of the fourths divisions , the bayernliga . in the season 2005/2006 , they played in the third division , the regionalliga . today the play in the fifth division ( bayernliga nord ) .
