Districts of Slovakia     an okres ( in english district ) is an administrative unit of slovakia . several districts form one region . on the other hand , one district is made of several municipalities , which are further divided into cadastral areas . the current districts exist since 1996 , which at first had their own offices , but in 2004 , they were abolished and replaced by the circuit offices , which are usually responsible for several districts ( except for the nové zámky district , which has two circuit offices ) , reducing districts to a little more than statistical units . slovakia currently has 79 districts , with the city of bratislava divided into 5 districts and the city of košice divided into 4 districts . alphabetical list of all slovak districts . - banská bystrica - banská štiavnica - bardejov - bánovce nad bebravou - brezno - bratislava i - bratislava ii , bratislava iii - bratislava iv , bratislava v - bytča - čadca - detva - dolný kubín - dunajská streda - galanta - gelnica - hlohovec - humenné
