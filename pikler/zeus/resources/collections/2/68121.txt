Ba Ðình Square    ba ðình square ba đình square ( vietnamese : quảng trường ba đình ) is a place in the vietnamese capital , hà nội . it is where vietnamese president hồ chí minh declared the independence of the democratic republic of việt nam from french imperialist rule on 2 september 1945 , the same day that world war ii ended . this is now where his mausoleum is .
