Legnica     legnica ( ) is a city in poland . the river katzbach runs through legnica . there live over 100,000 people . the city is in west part of poland ( near germany and czech border ) . legnica is near a bigger city , wrocław . legnica is in the middle of silesia . the area used to be part of prussia , and legnica was the capital of one of the three silesian government areas ( regierungsbezirk ) . silesia , a prussian province , is divided in 3 administrative regions ( regierungsbezirk ) : liegnitz , breslau and oppeln . the regierungsbezirk liegnitz comprised the following districts : - urban districts ( ) 1. glogau 1. görlitz 1. hirschberg 1. liegnitz - rural districts ( ) 1. bunzlau 1. fraustadt 1. freystadt 1. glogau 1. görlitz 1. goldberg 1. grünberg 1. hirschberg 1. hoyerswerda 1. jauer 1. landeshut 1. lauban 1. liegnitz 1. löwenberg 1. lüben 1. rothenburg 1. sprottau education . national colleges . - państwowa wyższa szkoła zawodowa w legnicy - politechnika wrocławska ( affiliate in legnica ) - nauczycielskie kolegium języków obcych w legnicy
