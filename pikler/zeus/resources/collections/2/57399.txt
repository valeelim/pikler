Szczecin - Dąbie Airstrip     szczecin - dąbie airstrip is a small airstrip on right side of the river in szczecin ( poland ) in dąbie . it was built on grass in 1921. during the third reich , there were a lot of domestic flights from this airport . after world war ii to 23 may 1967 , when the airport was moved to goleniów , it was the main airport of szczecin , where lot polish airlines has their flights . now it is a sport airstrip for small airplanes .
