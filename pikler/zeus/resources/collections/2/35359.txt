Humour     humour or humor ( greek : χιούμορ ) is the way some experiences can make people laugh or feel happy . most people are able to be amused ( laugh or smile at something funny ) and have a sense of humor . you can use puns with words that sound similar but have different meanings , or a word that has two meanings . other examples of humor are satire , saying yes or no when it is not expected , and using different kinds of logic . people with different ages and cultures can find different things humorous . for example , adults may like satire , which children could find hard to understand . a comedian is someone who is paid to make people laugh . famous ones include alan carr and harry hill .
