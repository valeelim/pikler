Final Fantasy V     final fantasy v is a medieval fantasy role-playing video game . it is developed and published by square ( now square enix ) . it was first made in 1992 as a part of the `` final fantasy '' series . the game first appeared only in japan on nintendo 's super famicom . it has been remade with little differences to sony 's playstation and nintendo 's game boy advance . the original video animation produced in 1994 called `` '' is a sequel to the game . it was released on the playstation network on april 6 , 2011 in japan . the game was released for iphone and ipad on march 28th , 2013. a version of the game was created for the nintendo game boy advance in 2006. it has many weapons , items , armors and 22 jobs . some jobs are secret and can only be unlocked in the gba version . the game begins when a wanderer named bartz investigates a fallen meteor . he meets several characters . one tells him about the danger the four crystals that control the world 's elements . these crystals act like a seal on exdeath . exdeath is an evil magician . bartz and his friends have to keep the crystals from being used by exdeath .
