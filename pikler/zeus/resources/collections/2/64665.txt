Home Alone     home alone is a 1990 american comedy movie set in chicago , illinois . it was written and produced by john hughes and directed by chris columbus . home alone stars macaulay culkin as kevin mccallister . kevin is mistakenly left home alone when his family goes on a vacation to france , during christmas . harry and marv ( played by joe pesci and daniel stern ) play burglars who rob the house . composer john williams wrote the music for the movie , and some popular songs that already existed were played during the movie as well . in its opening weekend , `` home alone '' grossed $ 17 million in 1,202 theaters . the film was nominated for best original score and for best original song . the film was followed by a commercially successful sequel in 1992 , ' . `` home alone 3 '' ( 1997 ) and ' ( 2012 ) have completely different characters and actors. `` '' ( 2002 ) has some of the main characters from the first two films , but all roles are played by different actors .
