1976 Summer Olympics     the 1976 summer olympics , officially known as the games of the xxi olympiad , were held in 1976 in montreal , quebec , canada . these are the summer olympic games organised by the international olympic committee . montreal was awarded the rights to the 1976 games in may 1970 , over the bid of moscow and los angeles , who later hosted the 1980 and 1984 summer olympic games respectively .
