Family (biology)    family ( biology ) in biology , a family is a taxonomic rank , or a taxon at that rank . each family contains one or more genera . the next important rank is that of order . usually , the name of the family ends with a `` idae '' for animals , and `` aceae '' for plants . sometimes there are also subfamilies and superfamilies . subfamilies end with `` inae '' or `` oideae '' . in latin , family is `` familia '' . for example , walnuts ( genus `` juglans '' ) and hickories ( genus `` carya '' ) both belong to the walnut family ( the `` juglandaceae '' ) .
