Joe Manchin     joseph ( joe ) manchin iii ( born august 24 , 1947 ) is an american politician . he was elected governor of the state of west virginia in the 2004 election . he took office as governor on january 17 , 2005 and remained in that position until 2010. in 2010 , he became a senator for west virginia , after the death of robert byrd . he is one of the least liberal democrats in the senate , having conservative views on social issues , energy , the environment , and the military . he co-sponsored an amendment , a change to a bill , with senator pat toomey ( r-pa ) that would require gun sellers to check whether gun buyers are mentally ill or have a criminal record . this amendment did not become law .
