Twiggy Lawson     twiggy lawson ( born leslie hornby19 september 1949 , in london , england ) is a english actress , fashion model and singer . her childhood nickname twiggy came from her thin legs . in the 1960s , when she first became famous she weighed only . as an actress , she starred in `` the boy friend '' , and had featured roles in `` the blues brothers '' and `` young charlie chaplin '' . she recently appeared as herself , on `` america 's next top model '' , as one of the judges .
