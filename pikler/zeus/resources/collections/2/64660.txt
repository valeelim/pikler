Adhesive tape     adhesive tape is a tape that is coated with adhesive and used to stick objects together . adhesive tape that will stick with application of pressure only ( i.e . without activation by water , solvent or heat ) is known as pressure sensitive tape . it can consist of a wide variety of tape backings and pressure sensitive adhesives . single-sided tapes allow joining of two overlapping or adjoining materials , while double-sided tape ( adhesive on both sides ) allows joining of two items back-to-back . other websites . - pressure sensitive tape council - the history of pressure sensitive tape
