Adenine     adenine is one of the basic molecules which form dna and rna . they are usually called bases in genetics . in dna , adenine sticks to thymine with two hydrogen bonds to help in making the nucleic acid structures stronger . in rna , which is used in the cytoplasm for protein synthesis , adenine sticks to uracil . adenine was sometimes called vitamin b , but no longer . however , two b vitamins , niacin and riboflavin , bind with adenine to form the essential cofactors nad and fad respectively . some think that , at the origin of life on earth , the first adenine was formed by the polymerization ( joining ) of five hydrogen cyanide ( hcn ) molecules . this is not generally accepted . other websites . - computational chemistry wiki
