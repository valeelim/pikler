Naphtha     naphtha is the name for a number of intermediary products that occur when refining crude oil . they are liquid hydrocarbons . they are used to produce high octane gasoline products . the petrochemical industry also uses them to make olefins and solvents . typical naphtas are used for : - solvents in cleaning fluid . - as an ingredient of shoe polish . - as an ingredient for some of the fuels used in lighters . - possibly one of the things in greek fire .
