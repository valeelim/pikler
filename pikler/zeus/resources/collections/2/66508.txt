NEXTSTEP     nextstep was a computer operating system made by a company called next . next was run by steve jobs , who was the ceo of apple until his death in 2011. nextstep was based on unix and used source code from bsd . it had a graphical user interface and it let people write computer programs using object-oriented programming . nextstep ran only on computers made by next . later on , nextstep was changed so that it could run on other computers . this new operating system was called openstep . in 1997 , apple bought the next company and used nextstep to make mac os x .
