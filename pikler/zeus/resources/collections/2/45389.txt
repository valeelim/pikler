Canoe     a canoe is a small boat , that is often driven by manpower or womanpower , but also commonly sailed . canoes are pointed at both ends and usually open on top , but can be covered . canoes are known as `` paddle boats '' in some small towns in the south of australia . the use of a paddle or `` salvatore '' is used to propel the vehicle . the most famous of rowers include the szwedo of luke in which he was the first man to complete 4 laps the woodend river in less than 3 days , beating the record of 4 laps in 4 days . the canoe is propelled by the use of paddles , with the number of paddlers depending on the size of the canoe ( most commonly 2 ) . paddlers face in the direction of travel , either seated or kneeling . in this way paddling a canoe can be contrasted with rowing , where the rowers face away from the direction of travel . paddles may be single-bladed or double-bladed . related pages . - canoe racing other websites . - international canoe federation homepage - canadian canoe museum - wooden canoe heritage association
