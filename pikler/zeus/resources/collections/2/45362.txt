Toast     toast is bread which has been grilled until hot and crispy . a toaster , grill or open fire is used . toasted bread is both darker in color and crunchier than normal bread . toast can be eaten with a range of condiments spread on the surface , such as butter , jam , honey , though these are optional as some people prefer their toast without condiments ( dry toast ) . toast can also be used to make 'toasties ' , where a filling is compressed and heated between two slices of buttered bread in a toastie maker . popular fillings include cheese , jam , peanut butter and chocolate spread .
