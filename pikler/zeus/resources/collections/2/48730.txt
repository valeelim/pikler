Trio (music)    trio ( music ) a trio is a group of three people who are playing musical instruments or singing together . the word `` trio '' may also refer to the piece of music that they are playing . three people who are singing together are a `` vocal trio '' . classical composers writing chamber music very often wrote trios . usually the trio is called after the main instrument in the group . a piano trio is normally a piece for piano , violin and cello . a string trio is a piece for three string instruments , usually violin , viola and cello . many other combinations are possible . brahms wrote a piece which is normally referred to as a `` horn trio '' for french horn , violin and piano . mozart wrote a piece normally called a `` clarinet trio '' which is for clarinet , viola and piano . a piece for three people sitting at one piano is usually called a piece for `` piano 6 hands '' . trios can also apply to popular music , like nickel creek .
