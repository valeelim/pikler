Trdat the Architect     trdat the architect ( c. 950-1020 ) ( armenian : տրդատ ճարտարապետ ) , known in latin as tiridates , was chief architect of the bagratuni dynasty of armenia . after a great earthquake in 989 destroyed the dome of hagia sophia , the byzantine officials sent trdat to byzantium to organize repairs . the restored dome was completed by 994. other websites . - the ani cathedral - cities and sites in armenia
