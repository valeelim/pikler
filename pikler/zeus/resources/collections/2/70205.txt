Glam rock     glam rock is a genre of rock music that was popular in the early 1970s . it was made famous by acts such as david bowie , elton john , t. rex , alice cooper , slade , gary glitter , queen and sweet . glam fans ( usually referred to as the `` glitter people '' ) and performers distinguished themselves from the denim-clad hippie-hordes with sci-fi/mythological/hollywood glamour/ambisexual-inspired costumes , which were perceived as glamorous by the press . they symbolized that they were decadent by wearing platform shoes . the music was characterised by languid , narcotic ballads and raunchy , high-energy rolling stones–influenced rock n ‘ roll stylings . notable artists . - t.rex - david bowie - gary glitter - elton john - roxy music - mot the hoople - alice cooper - slade - sweet - rolling stones
