Academy Award for Best Picture (1920s)    academy award for best picture ( 1920s ) the academy award for best picture is one of the academy awards . also called oscars , the academy awards are given to people working in the movie industry by the american academy of motion picture arts and sciences ( ampas ) . in the list below , the winner of the award for each year between 1927 and 1929 is shown first , followed by the other nominees . outstanding picture - 1927/28 `` wings '' – paramount famous lasky – lucien hubbard - `` the racket '' – caddo , united artists – howard hughes - `` seventh heaven '' – fox – william fox - 1928/29 `` the broadway melody '' – metro-goldwyn-mayer – harry rapt - `` alibi '' – feature productions , united artists – roland west - `` the hollywood revue of 1929 '' – metro-goldwyn-mayer – harry rapt - `` in old arizona '' – fox – winfield sheehan , studio head - `` the patriot '' – paramount – ernst lubitsch outstanding production - 1929/30 `` all quiet on the western front '' – universal – carl laemmle jr. - `` the big house '' – metro-goldwyn-mayer – irving thalberg
