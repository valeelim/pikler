Jean-Pierre Cassel     jean-pierre cassel ( real name jean-pierre crochon , october 27,1932 - april 19 , 2007 ) was a french actor . he was the father of vincent cassel . he became known because of the roles he had in movies in the 1950s and 1960s . he died of cancer . selected filmography . - `` la famille anodin '' ( 1956 ) - `` candide ou l'optimisme au xxe siècle '' ( 1960 ) - `` goodbye again '' ( 1961 ) - `` napoléon ii l'aiglon '' ( 1961 ) - `` the elusive corporal '' ( 1962 ) - `` high infidelity '' ( 1964 ) - `` is paris burning ?
