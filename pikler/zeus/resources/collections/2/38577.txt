Fat-tailed gerbil     the fat-tailed gerbil is `` pachyuromys duprasi '' , a species of gerbil . its tail becomes fatter when it eats something . then it uses it when food becomes scarce . they can live 5 to 7 years in captivity . it is from africa . they can survive a long time without drinking because they are adapted to a dry climate . they bury themselves under the sand until night so that there are not as many predators that will attack them .
