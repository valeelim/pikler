Scarlet     scarlet is a slightly orangish red colour . it is a colour of the rainbow . it is redder than vermilion . the colour 's name comes from the persian `` saqirlat '' . an early recorded use of `` scarlet '' as a color name in the english language dates to 1250. meaning of scarlet . - traditionally , scarlet is the colour of flame . people also call the colour of the blood of a living person scarlet , even though the actual color of blood is crimson . - scarlet may represent sin . - scarlet represents sensual pleasure and fun . scarlet represents partying . other pages . - list of colors
