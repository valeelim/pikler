Pethidine     pethidine or meperidine ( often called demerol in the united states ) is a fully synthetic opioid . it is an analgesic . it is used for the relief of moderate to severe pain . pethidine can also interact with a number of other medications , including muscle relaxants , some antidepressants , benzodiazepines , and alcohol . pethidine is also relatively bad for use when a patient is suffering from liver , or kidney disease , has a history of seizures or epilepsy , has an enlarged prostate or urinary retention problems , or suffers from hyperthyroidism , asthma , or addison 's disease .
