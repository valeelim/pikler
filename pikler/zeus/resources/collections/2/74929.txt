Romanesque architecture     romanesque architecture is a term that describes the style of architecture which was used in europe from the late 10th century until the 12th century when it changed to the gothic style . the romanesque style in england is called norman architecture . buildings in the romanesque style have very thick walls and round arches , castles and churches or cathedrals were built in this style . some of them are still standing . other websites . - círculo románico - visigothic , mozarabic and romanesque art
