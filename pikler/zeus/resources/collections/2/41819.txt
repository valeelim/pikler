Resuscitation     resuscitation is a thing to do in a medical emergency . it is first aid which is given to a person who is unconscious , and where breathing or pulse can not be detected . it is done to make oxygen continues to reach the heart and the brain . that way , a doctor may be able to restart the heart , possibly without damaging the brain . the most common cause for a stopping heart is a heart attack . the person helping must : - make sure there is no danger - get to the patient . talk to them , ask direct questions ( `` where are you hurt ? `` ) , give direct commands ( `` open your eyes ! '' ) . the person helping tells them what he or she is about to do ( `` i am going to touch your shoulder '' ) . - resuscitation is not needed if the patient responds . - call for help
