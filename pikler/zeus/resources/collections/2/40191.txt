Ultramarathon     an ultramarathon is a running race that is longer than a 26.2 mile marathon race . the most common distances for ultramarathons are : - 50 kilometers ( 31 miles ) - 50 miles ( 80.5 kilometers ) - 100 kilometers ( 62 miles ) - 100 miles ( 161 kilometers ) the most famous ultramarathon run in the united states is the western states 100 mile race .
