Golden Globe Award     the golden globe awards are american awards for motion pictures and television programs , given out each year during a formal dinner . run since 1944 by the ( hfpa ) , the awards are a major part of the film industry 's `` awards season '' which ends each year with the oscars and screen actors guild awards . the broadcast of the golden globe awards generally ranks as the third most-watched awards show each year , behind only the oscars and grammys . the golden globes has grown to one of the highest honors for actors and actresses . the golden globes are awarded early in the year , based on votes from ( as of 2005 ) 86 part-time journalists living in hollywood and who work for media based outside of the united states . other websites . - hollywood foreign press association - awards listing at the internet movie database
