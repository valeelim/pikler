Taunus     the taunus is a mountain range in hesse , germany . it is bounded by the river valleys of rhine , main and lahn . on the opposite side of the rhine , the mountains are continued by the hunsrück . the mountains themselves span the districts of hochtaunuskreis , main-taunus , rheingau-taunus , and rhein-lahn . not to be confused with the more famous and high feldberg in black forest , the highest peak is the `` großer feldberg '' ( 878 m above sea level ) . it is followed by the `` kleiner feldberg '' and the `` altkönig '' ( 798 m ) with the remains of a late iron age hill fort ( la-tène a , ca . 400 bc ) near the summit . the roman limes was built across the taunus . the saalburg , a restored roman castellum , now houses a museum . after the fall of the limes ( in 259/260 ad ) , the alamanni settled here . highest mountains . - großer feldberg ( 878 m ) , hochtaunuskreis ( district ) - kleiner feldberg ( 825 m ) , hochtaunuskreis - altkönig ( 798 m ) , hochtaunuskreis
