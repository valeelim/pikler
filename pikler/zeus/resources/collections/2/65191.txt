Tethys (moon)    tethys ( moon ) tethys ( pronounced `` tee-this '' ) is a large moon of the planet saturn . it was discovered by giovanni domenico cassini in 1684. it was one of the first saturnian moons discovered . tethys ' mass is 617,551,805,221,061,000,000 kg , its density is 0.973 g/cm and its volume is 634,264,255 km . it has a very large crater on its surface , called odysseus . it is named after a greek warrior king in homer 's two great works , `` the iliad '' and `` the odyssey '' . there is also a very long valley called ithaca chasma . it is 100 km wide and 2000 km long . tethys takes 45.3 hours to orbit ( go around ) saturn and orbits saturn 294,660 km away . tethys is tidally locked in phase with its parent planet - one side always faces toward saturn .
