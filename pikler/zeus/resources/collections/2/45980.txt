William Penn     william penn ( 14 october 1644-30 july 1718 ) was an english colonial leader . he was given what would become the us state of pennsylvania by king charles ii as a debt to his father . penn was a member of the religious society of friends . penn and his wife were made honorary united states citizens in 1984 by us president ronald reagan . he was friends with george fox . william penn was the leader of the quakers , the quakers were a group like the puritans but the offered freedom of religion
