Glockenspiel     the glockenspiel is a percussion instrument type , in the same musical instruments category as the xylophone and timpani , usually has a range of 2 and a half octaves and is set up in a keyboard instrument format , similar to the xylophone but unlike that wooden instrument , it is a metallophone , is played by hitting it with mallets with a plastic or felt tip , and has a soft sound , but that is very high pitch . if it is hit too hard , it makes a very bad sound , is often used to represent things such as fairies , birds , and butterflies , and it is from germany . categories : percussion instruments , musical instruments , german musical instruments ,
