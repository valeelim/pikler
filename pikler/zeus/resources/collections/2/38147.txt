Coney Island     coney island is a peninsula ( formerly an island ) in brooklyn , new york . it is famous for its beaches , amusements like the cyclone wooden roller coaster , parachute jump , and wonder wheel ferris wheel , boardwalk , and hot dogs . it became a seaside resort in the 19th century . it is unsure how coney island got its name . it may be because when the dutch people arrived on coney island , there was a large population of rabbits , so they named it `` rabbit island '' , which translates to `` coney island '' in english . it also may have been named by an irish pirate , for islands in sligo bay .
