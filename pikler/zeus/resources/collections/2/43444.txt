Wannanosaurus     wannanosaurus is the smallest known ornithischian dinosaur . its length is estimated at just less than a metre . it lived in the upper cretaceous , about 80 million years ago , and was found in anhui , china . it is known from a single partial skeleton , including a partial skull roof and lower jaw , an upper leg and lower leg , part of a rib , and other fragments . it was herbivorous , but may also have eaten insects . wannosaurus means wanno lizard . it is one of several pachycephalosaurs with flat-topped skulls .
