Voting system     the voting system is a set of rules about the way people vote and how the votes are counted . different countries have different voting systems . there are many different types of voting system . one of the first voting systems to be used is called `` first-past-the-post '' . under this system each voter writes an `` x '' next to their chosen candidate . at the end , the votes are counted . the candidate with the most votes wins . some people think that first-past-the-post is unfair because they believe that many votes are wasted . list of voting systems . - additional member system - approval voting - borda count - d'hondt method - instant runoff voting - plurality voting - single transferable vote - schulze method - supplementary vote - first-past-the-post voting related pages . - election
