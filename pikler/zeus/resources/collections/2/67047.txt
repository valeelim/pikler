Gastroenteritis     gastroenteritis is a medical term for inflammation of the stomach and intestines . it causes diarrhea , vomiting and stomach pain . it usually happens because of infection by a virus or bacteria . other common terms for gastroenteritis include infectious diarrhea , stomach bug , and stomach virus . it is sometimes called stomach flu or gastric flu , but it is `` not '' related to the influenza virus ( which is often shortened to 'flu ' ) .
