Weasel     weasels are the genus mustela , part of the mustelidae family . the genus includes the weasels , polecats , stoats , ferrets and minks . weasels are small , active predators , long and slender with short legs . their body shape is adapted to going down burrows after prey such as rabbits . there are 17 species , and they live all over the world except for antarctica and australasia . weasels vary in length from 173 to 217 mm ( 6.8 to 8.5 in ) . they have red or brown upper coats , white underneath . their tails may be from 34 to 52 mm ( 1.3 to 2.0 in ) long . biologically , despite looking a bit like cats , they are in the same suborder as dogs . other websites . - weasel riding on woodpecker .
