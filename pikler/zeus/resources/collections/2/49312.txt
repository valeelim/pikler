Bug     bug can mean many different things : - biology - true bug , a group of insects - bug , a slang word for a pathogen that makes organisms ill - bug , a slang word for an insect , such as an ant or a beetle - technology - software bug , a problem with the code in a computer program - bug , a device for telephone tapping - bug , a nickname for a volkswagen beetle because of its shape - other - western bug and southern bug , two rivers in poland and ukraine - something that irritates somebody , such as `` my loud neighbour really bugs me !
