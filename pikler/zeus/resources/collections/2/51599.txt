Maroon (color)    maroon ( color ) maroon is a dark red color . the word `` maroon '' comes from the french word `` marron '' which means `` chestnut '' . the first recorded use of `` maroon '' as a color name in english was in 1789. meaning of maroon . - vajrayana buddhist monks wear robes that are colored various shades of maroon . related pages . - list of colors
