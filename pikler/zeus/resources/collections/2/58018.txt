Tom Whedon     tom whedon is an american television writer . he has written for many series , for example , `` benson '' , `` alice '' and `` the golden girls '' . whedon also wrote for the children 's series `` the electric company '' . he is the father of deadwood script writer zack whedon . whedon is also the father of writer/producer/director joss whedon , the creator of `` buffy the vampire slayer '' , `` angel '' , and `` firefly '' .
