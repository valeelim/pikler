Frankfurt Airport     frankfurt airport ( `` in german '' : rhein-main-flughafen ) is the biggest airport in germany and the third largest in europe ( in terms of passengers , in cargo second largest ) , it is in frankfurt . in 2005 , 52 million passengers flew from frankfurt and 1.89 million tonnes of cargo . the airport has two train stations , the terminal for the commuter trains is under the terminal and the station for the long-distance trains is five minutes to walk . until 2005 , the rhein-main air base was in the south of the civil airport . frankfurt airport recorded its all-time hottest temperature in july 2015 at 101.8 °f ( 38.8 °c ) .
