If (Beyoncé song)    if ( beyoncé song ) if is an r & b ballad by beyoncé knowles . the song is her final single from her second album `` b'day : deluxe edition '' . the song was most likely chosen due to the fact that originally it was not available in the u.s. as it was an international track , but due to the des'ree law suit , the u.s. version of the album with the track `` still in love ( kissing you ) '' was replaced by a version with `` if '' . a cd single of the song was created for her official fan club and is available for purchase from their online store .
