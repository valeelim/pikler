Ophiuchus     ophiuchus ( οφιούχος : say it 'ofi-youkus ' ) , is one of the 88 listed constellations . in latin , it means `` serpent-holder '' . it is near the celestial equator , and northwest of the center of the milky way . ophiuchus is on the ecliptic , but is generally not considered to be in the zodiac . other websites . - the deep photographic guide to the constellations : ophiuchus - star tales – ophiuchus
