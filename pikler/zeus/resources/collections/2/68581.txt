Brittany Snow     brittany anne snow ( born march 9 , 1986 ) is an american television and movie actress and singer . she is known for her roles as meg pryor in `` american dreams '' , daisy lemay in `` guiding light '' , kate spencer in `` john tucker must die '' , amber von tussle in `` hairspray '' , donna keppel in `` prom night '' and jenna backstrom in the nbc television series `` harry 's law '' .
