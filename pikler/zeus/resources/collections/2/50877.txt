Deutsche Bahn     deutsche bahn ag ( short db ag or often only db ) is europe 's largest railway company . it runs over 30,000 trains a day and owns more than 5,000 stations . the company headquarters are in berlin , germany . it was founded on january 1 , 1994 to replace the deutsche bundesbahn , which was the state railway of west germany , and the deutsche reichsbahn , which was the state railway of east germany . deutsche bahn is also active in the field of logistics and owns companies like schenker . db claim they are the second largest logistics company in the world . other websites . - db corporate home page - db travel portal - db timetable information
