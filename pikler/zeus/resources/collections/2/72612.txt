Suppiluliuma II     suppiluliuma ii , the son of tudhaliya iv , was the last known king of the new kingdom of the hittite empire , ruling 1207 bce – c.1178 bce , contemporary with tukulti-ninurta i of assyira . he is known from two inscriptions in hieroglyphics . they record wars against former vassal tarhuntassa , and against alasiya in cyprus . after the fall , the kaskians were probably in control of hatti . hattusa itself was destroyed , its site only re-occupied by a phrygian fortress some 500 years later . literature . - astour , aja 69 ( 1965 ) . - güterbock , jnes 26 ( 1967 ) , 73-81. other websites . - reign of suppiluliuma ii
