Alive!    alive ! alive is the fourth album from the american hard rock/heavy metal band kiss . it is also their first live album . released on september 10 , 1975. track listing . - deuce - strutter - got to choose - hotter than hell - firehouse - nothin ' to lose - c'mon and love me - parasite - she - watchin ' you - 100,000 years - black diamond - rock bottom - cold gin - rock and roll all nite - let me go , rock 'n ' roll
