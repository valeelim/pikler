Frank Thomas     frank thomas ( born may 27 , 1968 ) is a former american major league baseball player who spent most of his career with the chicago white sox . near the end of his career , he also played with the oakland athletics and toronto blue jays . he hit his 500th career home run on june 28 , 2007. he was the 21st person to hit 500 home runs . thomas was elected to the national baseball hall of fame in 2014 , and will officially enter the hall on july 27 .
