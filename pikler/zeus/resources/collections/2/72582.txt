Armenian Army     the armenian army is the army of armenia . armenia has sent around 1,500 officers to be trained in greece and russia over the past years . since the end of the nagorno-karabakh war , armenia has made the army boost the defense and help defend nagorno-karabakh from a possible new attack by neighboring azerbaijan . related pages . - military history of armenia - armed forces of armenia - armenian air force - armenian air defense - nagorno-karabakh defense army other websites . - military parade in stepanakert , artsakh - armenian military photo gallery
