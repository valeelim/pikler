Muffin     a muffin is a small bread- or cake-like baked food . muffins are similar to cupcakes : they have round bases , rounded tops , and are usually sweet , but there are also savory kinds ( like cornbread muffins ) . a `` muffin '' can also mean a different baked food , the smaller , disk-shaped english muffin , although this is not common outside britain . in britain , the term can mean either type of muffin . muffins can also have extra ingredients to its flavour , such as blueberries or chocolate chips . muffins are usually eaten at breakfast but can be eaten at any other meal . it is usually served with tea .
