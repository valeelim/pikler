Hare Krishna     hare krishna is a mantra . it is also known as maha mantra . the mantra was made well known even outside hinduism by the international society for krishna consciousness . the mantra can be found in many hinduist traditions in india . those who believe in it think that when the mantra is heard , spoken , used for meditation or sung this can lead to a higher state of consciousness . according to gaudiya vaishnava theology , this higher consciousness takes the form of pure love of god ( krishna ) . the mantra is contained in the texts of hinduism known as the upanishads , which are considered by some scholars to have roots in the vedic past .
