Armed Forces of Armenia     the armed forces of the republic of armenia is the army , air defense , and border guard . some parts were formed out of the former soviet forces in the armenian ssr . the commander-in-chief of the military is the president of armenia , currently robert kocharyan . the ministry of defense is in charge of political leadership . it is currently headed by mikael harutyunyan . military command is in the hands of the general staff , headed by the chief of staff , who is lieutenant-general seyran ohanyan . armenia established a ministry of defense on 28 january 1992. the border guards purpose is to patrol armenia 's borders with georgia and azerbaijan , while russian troops continue to watch its borders with iran and turkey . related pages . - military history of armenia - armenian army - armenian air defense - armenian border guard other websites . - armenian ministry of defense
