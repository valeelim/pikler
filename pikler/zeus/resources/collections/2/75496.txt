Tigranakert     tigranakert ( armenian : տիգրանակերտ , transliterated `` tigranakert '' and also spelled `` dikranagerd '' in western armenian ; latin tigranocerta ) was a city near present-day silvan , turkey , east of diyarbakır . it was founded by the armenian emperor tigranes the great in the 1st century bc . tigranakert was founded as the new capital of the armenian empire in order to be in a more central position within the boundaries of the expanding empire . other pages . - tigranes the great - kingdom of armenia - battle of tigranocerta other websites . - ancient and premodern armenia - `` armenia 's 12 capital cities '' exhibition opened in paris
