U.S. Route 1     u.s. route 1 ( sometimes called us 1 ) is a major united states highway ( u.s. route ) in the eastern part of the united states . its northern end is at the canadian border in fort kent , maine and its southern end is at key west , florida . for most of its route , u.s. route 1 runs next to interstate 95. it connects many major cities in the eastern united states , including boston , new york , philadelphia , baltimore , washington , dc , richmond , virginia , raleigh , north carolina , augusta , georgia , jacksonville , florida , and miami . parts of the road were built in the 1800s as the boston post road and in the 1900s as the atlantic highway .
