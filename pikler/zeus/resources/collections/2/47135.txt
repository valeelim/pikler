Pumpkin Studios     pumpkin studios was a company that made computer and video games . it was founded in 1996. it was in bath , england . they made one game : `` warzone 2100 '' . it was a real-time strategy game for pc and playstation . it used a few new ideas such as machines that use radio waves to find other objects ( radar technology ) and machines that shoot objects ( artillery ) . the game also used movies to tell the story to the player . these movies are called full motion videos . the company was closed on 13 march 2000. other websites . - pumpkin studios , mobygames - pumpkin studios website ( no longer used ) - warzone 2100 website
