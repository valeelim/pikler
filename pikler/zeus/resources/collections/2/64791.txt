Limited government     limited government is a form of government with roles and powers given , and limited by law , usually in a written constitution . a limited government has only the powers that the people give it . related to this idea is `` small government '' – a system that put most of its power in smaller groups , such as private businesses and its own citizens . on the other hand , “ big government ” involves a great amount of intervention by the state . in great britain , the idea of limited government is part of the political tradition and is not written into the constitution of the united kingdom . however , in the united states , the idea of limited government is written into the united states constitution .
