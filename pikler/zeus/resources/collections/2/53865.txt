Snuff     snuff is a kind of tobacco which is sniffed instead of smoked . people take a bit of snuff between their fingers and put it in their nostrils to smell it . not many people use snuff now , but a century or more ago it was very common . in the novels of 19th century authors like charles dickens we often read about people taking snuff . they kept it in a `` snuffbox '' . other pages . - tobacco
