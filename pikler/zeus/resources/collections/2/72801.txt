Displacement     displacement is a word . it means that something is moved . more specifically , this movement can be : - a displacement vector , from the original position to the current one . - a special move in fencing to avoid an attack . - forced migration - people having to move to somewhere else , because of violence , war , or another reason . - displacement ( surgery ) - when a bone is broken , this refers to moving around the different parts of the broken bone . - displacement ( psychology ) - a subconscious defense reaction . - displacement ( ship ) - the amount of water a ship displaces when it floats .
