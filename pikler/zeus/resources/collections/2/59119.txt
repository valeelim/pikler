Fountain of Cibeles     cibeles is the name given to the marble fountain found in the plaza de cibeles ( cybele 's square ) in madrid , spain . it is on a very busy road crossing . the statue was made by ventura rodriguez . king carlos iii asked to have it made in 1716. it has changed several times . it was moved to the central position in 1895. the lady is the goddess of fertility . she sits on a chariot pulled by lions . the lions ' names are hippomenes and atalanta . they were people that the goddess turned into lions because they ruined her temple . the lions were made by robert michel .
