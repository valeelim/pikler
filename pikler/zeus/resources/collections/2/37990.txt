Håkan Hardenberger     håkan hardenberger ( born 1961 , malmö ) is a swedish trumpeter . he started playing the trumpet when he was eight . his teacher was bo nilsson , when he left school he studied in paris and los angeles . he is one of the world ’ s most famous trumpeters . he plays well-known pieces of classical music and has also asked living composers like harrison birtwistle , hans werner henze and arvo pärt to write music for him . other websites . - official website
