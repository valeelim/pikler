Gwinnett County, Georgia    gwinnett county , georgia gwinnett county is a county in the american state of georgia . the county seat of gwinnett county is lawrenceville . the county was created on december 15 , 1818. the census bureau says there were 754,104 people living in gwinnett county in 2006. the county was named for button gwinnett , one of the people who signed the united states declaration of independence for georgia . other websites . - official web site of gwinnett county government
