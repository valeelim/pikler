Nymphaeaceae     in botany , nymphaeaceae is the name of a family of plants which grow in water . the family is also called `` water-lily family '' . their flowers suggest they are one of the earliest groups of angiosperms . modern genetic analyses by the angiosperm phylogeny group researchers has confirmed its basal position among flowering plants . also , the nymphaeaceae are more diverse and wdespread than other basal angiosperms . nymphaeaceae is in the order nymphaeales . according to the most widely accepted flowering plant classification system , this is the second diverging group of angiosperms after `` amborella '' . they are happy
