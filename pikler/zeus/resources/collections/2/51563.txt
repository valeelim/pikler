Coral (color)    coral ( color ) coral is a reddish or pinkish shade of orange . the color is named after the sea animal also called corals . the first written use of `` coral '' as a color name in english was in 1513. meaning of coral . - coral and coral pink are popular colors for painting a room to give a calm feeling of warmth . - in some sects of hinduism , coral red instead of red is shown as being the color which represents the first ( `` muladhara '' ) chakra in hindu philosophy . related pages . - list of colors
