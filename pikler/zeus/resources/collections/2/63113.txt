Región Autónoma del Atlántico Sur     the región autónoma del atlántico sur is an independent part of nicaragua . it is often simply known as raas . its capital is bluefields . it was made when new regions were formed in nicaragua in 1989. the old region was turned into atlántico sur and atlántico norte . the capital of atlántico norte is puerto cabezas . there is also a popular tourist place called corn island , which is about 45 km from the capital bluefields
