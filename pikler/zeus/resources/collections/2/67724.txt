Christy Turlington     christy nicole turlington ( born january 2 , 1969 ) is an american supermodel . she grew up in danville , california . her father was british commercial pilot . her mother was a salvadoran flight attendant . turlington is best known for representing calvin klein perfumes . she has done this since 1987. she has also worked on various campaigns for maybelline and giorgio armani . she has appeared in several movies about the fashion industry .
