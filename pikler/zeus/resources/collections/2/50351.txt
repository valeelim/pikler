Mycelium     the mycelium is the part of a fungus that is usually underground ( or inside some another substance ) . it is composed of hyphae , which look like threads , or rootlets . the mat of hyphae may be very thickly woven . the fungus uses it to extract nutritients . the hyphae are usually syncytic , that is , they do not have complete cell walls . fungal colonies composed of mycelium are found in and on soil and many other natural materials . a mycelium may be tiny , forming a colony that is too small to see , or it may be extremely large and cover the floors of a large forest . the part of the fungus which is most visible is the reproductive body . this is often , but not always , the stem and hat of the mushroom .
