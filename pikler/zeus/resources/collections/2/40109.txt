Bad Brains (band)    bad brains ( band ) bad brains is an american band . they play hardcore punk music that is mixed with reggae music . the band formed in 1977 and is still playing now . the members are h.r . ( singer ) , `` dr. know '' ( guitarist ) , darryl jenifer ( bassist ) and earl hudson ( drummer ) . the band originally formed in prince georges county , maryland . it is close to washington , d.c. , the capital of the united states . during most of their time together , bad brains has lived in washington , d.c. the bad brains had a big effect on many hardcore punk bands , including minor threat who are also from washington , d.c. they also had a big effect on the hardcore punk singer henry rollins . rollins was the singer for the hardcore band black flag .
