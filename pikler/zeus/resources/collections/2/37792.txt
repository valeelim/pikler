Spider Riders     while searching a cave , thirteen year old hunter steele finds a strange manacle that connects itself to him . when hunter sees a spider , he falls . hunter falls straight down to the center of the earth , into the underground world of `` arachna '' . here , he finds a group of people fighting to save arachna from the attack of giant insect mutants . the people fighting are kids , just like hunter . each one is fighting with the help of their own ten-foot spiders . they call themselves `` spider riders . '' the anime is based on characters and stories for a series of science fiction books . there were three books in the series . the books in the spider riders series were : - `` spider riders : shards of the oracle '' - `` spider riders : reign of the soul eater '' - `` spider riders : quest of the earthen '' the first book was written by tedd anasti and patsy cameron-anasti . stephen d. sullivan joined them and helped write the second and third books in the series . the books were first published in december 2004. they were published by newmarket press .
