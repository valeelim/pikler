Tsitsernakaberd     tsitsernakaberd ( ) is a memorial for the victims of the armenian genocide . it is on a hill overlooking yerevan , armenia . every year on april 24 , armenians gather here to remember the victims of the 1915 armenian genocide that took place in the ottoman empire carried out by the turkish government . in 1965 to commemorate the 50th anniversary of the genocide after a million people demonstrated for 24 hours ; the demonstrations are now known as the 1965 yerevan demonstrations . in response , the genocide memorial construction began in 1966 and it was completed in 1968. in yerevan , the capital of armenia , crowds of people walk to the tsitsernakaberd genocide memorial to lay flowers at the eternal flame . other pages . - armenian genocide - montebello genocide memorial - marseille genocide memorial - genocide remembrance day - 1965 yerevan demonstrations other websites . - tsitsernakaberd
