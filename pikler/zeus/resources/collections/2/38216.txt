Capra (genus)    capra ( genus ) capra is a genus of the subfamily caprinae and the family bovidae . the domestic goat and other animals like it belong to this genus . taxonomy . - genus capra - wild goat ( `` capra aegagrus '' ) , and the domestic goat - alpine ibex ( `` capra ibex '' ) - walia ibex ( `` capra walie '' ) - west caucasian tur ( `` capra caucasica '' ) - east caucasian tur ( `` capra cylindricornis '' ) - spanish ibex ( `` capra pyrenaica '' ) - markhor ( `` capra falconeri '' ) ( national animal of pakistan )
