Calypso (moon)    calypso ( moon ) calypso is a small moon of saturn . it was discovered by a few astronomers in 1980 , based on observations from the earth . it is named after calypso in greek mythology . calypso is a trojan moon , as it orbits together with larger moon tethys . it orbits behind tethys by 60 degrees in its orbit around saturn . telesto is another moon which shares calypso 's orbit . calypso was named after calypso , a nymph in greek mythology .
