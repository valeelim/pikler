Pogrom     pogrom ( from russian : '' погром '' ( pogrom ) ; from `` громить '' ipa : - to wreak havoc , to demolish violently ) is a form of riot because of antisemitism . this riot is directed against a group of people . these groups may be certain ethnic groups , they may belong to a certain religion , or they may have other marks of distinction . in a pogrom their homes , businesses , and places of worship are destroyed . very often people of the target group are murdered . other pages . - babi yar - `` kristallnacht ''
