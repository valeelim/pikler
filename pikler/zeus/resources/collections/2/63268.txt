Armenian illuminated manuscript     armenian illuminated manuscripts make a different tradition , related to other forms of medieval armenian art , but also to the byzantine tradition . the earliest surviving examples date from the golden age of armenian art and literature in the 5th century . the library at yerevan , has the largest collection of armenian manuscripts , including the mugni gospels and echmiadzin gospel . other websites . - matenadaran site - armenian manuscripts at armenica.org
