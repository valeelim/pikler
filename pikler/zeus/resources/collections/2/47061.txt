Zambezi River     the zambezi river is the fourth largest river in africa . it is also the largest river that enters the indian ocean . it starts in zambia , flows successively through angola , zambia again , borders namibia , botswana and zimbabwe , to mozambique where it enters the ocean . the victoria falls , the most beautiful sight on the river , is listed as one of the unesco world heritage sites . the kariba dam on the zambezi is a major source of hydroelectricity
