Velikiy Novgorod     velikiy novgorod ( russian : вели́кий но́вгород ) is a city in the european part of russia . it is about 200km from st. petersburg . in 2007 about 2170,000 people lived there . `` novgorod '' is the russian word for `` new city '' and `` velikiy '' means `` the great '' . the city is actually one of the most historic in russia . its past can be traced to the 9th century.it was also the only russian province that was `` not '' captured by the golden horde .
