Jeffrey Buttle     jeffrey buttle ( born september 1 , 1982 ) is a figure skater from canada . buttle has won the national championships for canada three times . he won the bronze medal ( third place ) at the 2006 winter olympics . he won the world championships in 2008. he has twice won the four continents figure skating championships , a competition for skaters from countries that are not in europe . buttle retired from competitions in 2008. nowadays , he shows his skating acts in ice shows and makes skating programs for other skaters . buttle is known for very good skating skills . the edges of his skating blades are leaned very deep , making his skating fun to watch even when he does not perform any jumps . he is known for his spread eagle and his ina bauer , skating movements on ice . other websites . - official site - international skating union profile
