Bălţi     bălţi is the third-largest city in the country of moldova . it is the major city in the northern part of the country . during the russian empire ( 1812-1917 ) , and later the soviet era ( 1940-1990 ) the name was also spelled beltsy , from its form in russian . the city is 135 km north of the capital chişinău , on the small river răut , a branch of the dniester ( nistru ) . it is located on a hilly landscape that , in the middle ages , was covered with forest . this forest has since been almost entirely cut down .
