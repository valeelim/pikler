Baker     a baker is somebody who makes mainly bread . some bakers also make cakes and desserts , but a traditional baker only makes bread . the place where a baker works is called a bakehouse or a bakery . the first bread was made by the egyptians in 8000 bc and in the middle ages most landlords had a bakery . they had a public oven and housewives brought the dough to the baker to cook . bread is now much sweeter than it was in the middle ages because a lot of corn syrup or honey is used . they baked lots of breads .
