Anna Jurkiewicz     anna jurkiewicz ( born february 9 , 1984 in oświęcim , poland ) is a polish figure skater . as a young girl , she was one of the best young skaters in the world . then she had some health problems and was not able to skate . she returned in 2006 and has been competing internationally since autumn 2006. other websites . - official homepage - isu official profile
