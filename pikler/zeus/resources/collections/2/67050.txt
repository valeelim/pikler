Television pilot     television pilot , usually called just a `` pilot '' , is a test episode made to sell a television series . it may be broadcast as the first episode if the series is sold to a network or cable channel , and sometimes has different actors or other differences to the real series . there are also many series whose pilot episodes are titled `` pilot '' . while many pilots are made , few make it to the screen , and even fewer go on to become full television series because of big competition between networks and producers .
