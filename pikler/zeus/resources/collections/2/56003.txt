Biological warfare     biological warfare ( or germ warfare ) is when pathogens are used as a weapon . this is called a biological weapon . pathogens are microorganisms that can cause disease . if non-living things ( like toxins ) are used , this is called chemical warfare . biological warfare has been used throughout history . the first form of biological warfare may have been when the plague hit the mongols . during battle , they were said to have thrown plague victims ' bodies at their enemies . before the 20th century , it took some of the following forms : - deliberate poisoning of food or water supplies with infectious materials - use of microorganisms , living or dead animals in a weapon system . - use of fabrics that are contaminated during the second sino-japanese war , biological weapons were used by the japanese army against china . the japanese army usually launched contaminated fleas or food from planes . today , pathogens for diseases like anthrax or smallpox could be used as agents for biological weapons .
