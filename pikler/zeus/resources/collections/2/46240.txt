Liu-bo     liu-bo is an italian martial art . it started in sicily . in sicily , the art of `` paranza '' , moving the stick by hand in order to attack or in order to defend oneself , was being taught to sons by their fathers for centuries . many people who took care of sheep were masters of it . they often were asked to fight in order to decide problems of honor . recently , rules have been created and it was made a sport . this sport has become well-known and has been made official by the national educational sport center ( csen ) . the stick is made from the wood of olive , rowan , or bitter orange tree . it is used as a base for a number of actions . it is held in one or both hands . other websites . - http : //www.liu-bo.it/ - http : //bastone-siciliano.com/
