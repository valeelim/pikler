Hakeem Olajuwon     hakeem olajuwon ( born january 21 , 1963 in lagos , nigeria ) is a retired nigerian-american basketball player . olajuwon travelled all the way from his home country of nigeria to play basketball in the united states . he won two national basketball association ( nba ) championships with the houston rockets . during his career , he was nicknamed `` the dream '' because of his grace both on and off the court . other websites . - hakeem olajuwon 's nba legends profile
