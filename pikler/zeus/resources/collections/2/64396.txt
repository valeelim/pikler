Prosencephalon     the prosencephalon ( or forebrain ) , the mesencephalon ( midbrain ) , and rhombencephalon ( hindbrain ) are the three primary portions of the brain of vertebrates . they develop very early during development of the central nervous system . when the embryonic prosencephalon fails to divide the brain into two lobes , it results in a condition known as holoprosencephaly . other websites . - sylvius : 400+ structure neuroanatomical visual glossary ; used by over half of u.s. medical schools - high-resolution cytoarchitectural primate brain atlases
