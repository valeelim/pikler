Bellinzona     bellinzona ( [ ] in french `` bellinzone '' [ ] and `` bilitio '' [ ] in latin ) is the capital of the swiss canton of ticino . its german name `` bellenz '' [ ] is no longer in use . with about 17.300 people living there , it is the second biggest city of the canton ( after lugano , with about 52.000 people ) . on 2 april 2017 the former municipalities of camorino , claro , giubiasco , gnosca , gorduno , gudo , moleno , monte carasso , pianezzo , preonzo , sant'antonio and sementina merged into the municipality of bellinzona .
