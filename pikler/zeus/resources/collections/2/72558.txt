Marija Gimbutas     marija gimbutas ( lithuanian : marija gimbutienė , born marija birutė alseikaitė ) ( vilnius , lithuania , january 23 , 1921 – los angeles , united states february 2 , 1994 ) , was a lithuanian-american archeologist known for her research into the neolithic and bronze age cultures of `` old europe '' , a new thing she introduced . her works were made between 1946 and 1971 it showed new views by merging traditional spadework with linguistics and mythological info .
