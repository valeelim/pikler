Lake Peipus     lake peipus ( estonian : `` peipsi-pihkva järv '' , german : `` peipussee '' , russian : `` чудско-псковское озеро '' ) is a large fresh water lake . it is between estonia and russia in northern europe . lake peipus is the fourth largest lake in europe . the lake covers 3,500 square kilometres . it has an average depth of 7m . at its deepest point , lake peipus is 15m deep . the lake is used for fishing and recreation .
