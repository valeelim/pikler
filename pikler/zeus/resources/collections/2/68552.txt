Teddy Randazzo     teddy randazzo ( may 13 , 1935 - november 21 , 2003 ) was an american singer , songwriter and record producer , best known for his work in the 1950s and 1960s . one of the groups he worked with was little anthony and the imperials . randazzo appeared as himself in a 1962 movie , `` do n't knock the twist '' , which was about the twist craze of the early 1960s .
