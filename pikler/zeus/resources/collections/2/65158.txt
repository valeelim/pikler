Iraj Mirza     jalalolmamalek pesar-e gholam-hossein mirza ( october 1874 - 14 march 1926 ) ( ) or iraj mirza ( ) was an iranian poet . he was born in tabriz , iran . his most famous poems are satan ( in persian : `` ebleess '' ) , mother ( in persian : `` maadar '' ) , woman 's picture ( in persian `` tassvir-e-zan '' ) , story of the hijab ( in persian `` daastaan-e chaador '' ) and the story of zohreh and manouchehr ( in persian : `` daastaan e zohreh o manouchehr '' ) . he died in tehran on 14 march 1926. his tomb is in shemiran , north of tehran .
