Probability distribution     probability distribution is a term from mathematics . suppose there are many events with random outcomes . a probability distribution is the theoretical counterpart to the frequency distribution . a frequency distribution simply shows how many times a certain event occurred . a probability distribution says how many times it should have occurred . there are different probability distributions . each of them has its use , its benefits , and its drawbacks . common ones are : - binomial distribution - cauchy distribution - chi-square distribution - exponential distribution - gumbel distribution - normal distribution - poisson distribution - student 's t-distribution
