Umbrella term     an umbrella term is a phrase that organises a broad number of related concepts that all fall under a single common category . for example , cryptology is an umbrella term that includes cryptography and cryptanalysis , among other fields . similarly , an is a main body that can represent a number of smaller , separate bodies . the similar phrase blanket term is a used to describe multiple groups of related things . how similar these concepts are to each other may change and they may only have a very small relationship with each other . but the main purpose of blanket terms is that they are easy to say and remember , and so these terms may not necessarily be specific . blanket terms may originate as slang , and eventually become a part of normal speaking .
