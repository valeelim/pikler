Jordin Sparks     jordin brianna sparks ( born december 22 , 1989 in phoenix , arizona ) is an american recording artist , singer and actress . on may 23 , 2007 , she won the 6th season of the reality television show `` american idol '' . she was the youngest winner of the talent show . she has released two studio albums `` jordin sparks '' and `` battlefield '' . she has a vocal range of a mezzo-soprano . other websites . - jordin sparks official webpage - jordin sparks planet
