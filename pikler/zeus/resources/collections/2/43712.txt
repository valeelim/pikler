Battle     a battle is a fight between two or more groups where each group is trying to defeat ( beat ) the others . battles are most often fought during wars , military campaigns , or two or more people fighting . if two armies fight many battles over period of time it is called a war . the ground on which a battle is fought is called a battlefield . battles can very important , for example the 1389 battle of kosovo , which was a draw in military terms , changed the history of the balkans , europe , and the entire world . the outcome changed ottoman politics and had influence in the region for well over 500 years . famous battles . land battles . - battle of hastings — 1066 - battle of kosovo — 1389 - battle of waterloo — 1815 - battle of gettysburg — 1863 - battle of verdun — 1916 naval battles . - battle of trafalgar — 1805 - battle of midway — 1942
