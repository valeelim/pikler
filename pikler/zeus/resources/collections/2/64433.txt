Queen Silvia of Sweden     queen silvia of sweden ( born silvia renate sommerlath ) ( born 23 december 1943 ) and of half german , half brazilian descent is the queen consort of king carl xvi gustaf , sweden 's monarch , and the mother of the heir apparent to the throne , crown princess victoria . their other two children are - prince carl philip of sweden and - princess madeleine of sweden she is styled `` her majesty the queen '' and is in sweden most often referred to as `` queen silvia '' . she also make efforts for charitable organizations , human rights and sign language and opposes child sexual abuse and child pornography .
