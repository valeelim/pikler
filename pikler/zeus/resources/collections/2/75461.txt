Maximilien de Robespierre     maximilian robespierre is one of the best-known leaders of the french revolution . he was born in arras , france and he went to school to become a lawyer and got his degree at law school . he also , got elected to the estates general to help make laws for france and fought against the french monarchy , the death penalty , slavery , for democratic reforms and the people to have more power . he helped gain a reputation for defending the poor society . also he earned a nickname for sticking to his moral values . later was elected president of the powerful jacobin political faction . maximillian led the commitee of public safety during 1793 through it he successfully tried to execute the king through the committee of public safety . even though robespierre got thousands of people executed robespierre cared about the working class . maximillian executed king louis xvi because he was convicted of treason ( treason is the betrayal of ones own country ) under the advice of robespierre the committee of public safety came to control france . the period that the committee of public safety ruled france is known as “ the reign of terror ” and maximilian robespierre was their leader . robespierre was caught and executed with 21 of his followers with a guillotine . the national convention were the people who overthrew maximillian robespierre .
