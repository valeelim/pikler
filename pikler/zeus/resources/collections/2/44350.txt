For Dummies     the ... for dummies is a series of books that try to explain something very simply . the series was created in 1991 by dan gookin . the first book was about the computer operating system ms-dos . dan gookin created the book , because in 1987 a person wanted a book that was simple and easy to read , as the person considered computer books to be dull at that time . after ms-dos for dummies , came pc 's for dummies by gookin , and andy rathbone . other popular ... for dummies books are the internet for dummies by john levine , carol baroudi , and margaret levine young , and beginning programming for dummies by wallace wang . other websites . - for dummies website
