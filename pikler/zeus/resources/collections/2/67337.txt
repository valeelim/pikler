Pasargadae     pasargadae was a city in ancient persia , and is today a historical site in fars , iran . pasargadae was the first capital of the persian empire . the building of the capital city by cyrus the great , begun in 546 bce , but was left unfinished , for cyrus died in 530 bce . pasargade remained the persian capital unti darius i built the persepolis . the tomb of cyrus the great is in this city . pasargadae is a unesco world heritage site .
