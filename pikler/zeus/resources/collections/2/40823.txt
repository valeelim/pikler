St. Lucia Day     st. lucia day is a holiday that is held on december 13 in several countries in europe . those countries include sweden , denmark , norway , and finland . the holiday is in honor of saint lucy . on this day , young girls ( mostly the oldest daughter of the house ) dress like saint lucia in a white robe and a crown of candles . there are parades , pageants , and the girl playing lucia serves cakes . they also sing special songs . here is one of the songs : < poem > the night treads heavily around yards and dwellings in places unreached by sun , the shadows brood into our dark house she comes , bearing lighted candles , saint lucia , saint lucia . < /poem > the lucia celebrations also include ginger snaps and sweet , saffron-flavoured buns ( `` lussekatter '' ) shaped like curled-up cats and with raisin eyes . people eat them with `` gl√∂gg '' or coffee .
