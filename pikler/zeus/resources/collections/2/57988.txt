Melvins     the melvins are an american rock band . they are from the city of aberdeen , washington . the only members who have been in the band for a long time are buzz `` king buzzo '' osborne who plays guitar and dale crover who plays drums . coady willis who plays drums ( in addition to dale ) and jared warren who plays bass guitar are also currently in the band . a variety of terms have been used to describe their style of music , including 'sludge metal ' , 'grunge ' , and 'stoner rock ' . buzz osborne started melvins in early 1983 .
