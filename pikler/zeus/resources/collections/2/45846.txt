Saparmurat Niyazov     saparmurat niyazov ( february 19 , 1940 - december 21 , 2006 ) was the first president of turkmenistan . he served from 1993 to 2006. saparmurat niyazov also called himself türkmenbaşy , which means leader of the turkmen people . he tried to make a cult of personality , which means that he put many pictures and statues of himself around his country to make himself seem important . while he was president , niyazov made doing many things against the law , such as growing a beard or ballet dancing . he died from a heart attack on december 21 , 2006 , and gurbanguly berdimuhammedow became the new president of turkmenistan .
