Frank     the term frank has many meanings : - a germanic people , active during the middle ages were called franks - a person named anne frank ( 1929-1945 ) wrote a diary during the second world war . - frank sinatra ( 1915-1998 ) , american singer who is one of the most highly acclaimed male popular song vocalists of all time - frank zappa ( 1940-1993 ) , american musician - the people who live in franconia ( `` franken '' ) today are calls `` franks '' - what is usually called a hot dog in everyday speech is called a `` frank '' according to the package wrapping .
