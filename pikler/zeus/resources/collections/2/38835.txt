Nightshade     the nightshades ( solanaceae ) are a family of plants . all of them bear flowers . many members of the family are edible , but some are poisonous . very often , only certain parts of the plant are edible or poisonous . well-known members of this family are chili peppers , petunias , deadly nightshade , mandrakes , potatoes , tomatoes , aubergines ( eggplants ) and tobacco . because it is thought that nightshades can cause or increase swelling and pain , many people do not eat them .
