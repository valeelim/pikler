Tropical Storm Delta (2005)    tropical storm delta ( 2005 ) tropical storm delta was a late-forming tropical storm which affected the canary islands and morocco as a strong extratropical storm . it was also the twenty-sixth tropical or subtropical storm of the active 2005 atlantic hurricane season . tropical storm delta formed out from an extratropical cyclone that slowly gained tropical characteristics . it was also a subtropical storm for a very short time before becoming `` tropical storm delta '' on november 22. delta then moved slowly and stubbornly to the canary islands . it became extratropical again on november 29 , just before passing north of the islands . on november 22 , a non-tropical low pressure system in the central atlantic ocean began to gain some tropical characteristics . late that afternoon the low became subtropical storm delta , while located over 800 miles ( 1300 km ) west-southwest of the azores . originally , the national hurricane center thought that the storm had already gained enough tropical characteristics to be classified as a regular tropical storm , but in post-storm analysis this was changed .
