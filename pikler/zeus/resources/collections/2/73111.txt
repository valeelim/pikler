Twister (movie)    twister ( movie ) twister is a 1996 american disaster movie set in oklahoma . it starred helen hunt and bill paxton as `` storm chasers '' , people who follow tornadoes to learn about them . it was directed by jan de bont . the movie was based upon a script by michael crichton and his wife , former actress anne-marie martin . `` twister '' has a famous scene of a flying cow . it is a scene that was the subject of many parodies . according to imdb , `` twister '' was the first movie commercially released on dvd . other websites . - official site for the film
