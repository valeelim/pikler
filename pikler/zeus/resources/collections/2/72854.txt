Hennes &amp; Mauritz    hennes & amp ; mauritz h & m hennes & mauritz ab ( h & m ) , is a swedish clothing company . it is known for offering quality fashion at best prices . the company was created in västerås , sweden , in 1947 by erling persson . at first , the company only sold women 's clothing . at that time , it was called hennes , swedish for `` hers . '' in 1968 , persson got the building and inventory of a stockholm hunting equipment store named mauritz widforss . a supply of men 's clothing was part of the inventory . this caused persson to decide to also sell men 's clothing . he renamed the store hennes & mauritz , later shortened to h & m . h & m has more than 1400 stores in 28 different countries . it has more than 60,000 employees .
