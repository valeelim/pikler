Timbaland     timothy zachery mosley , also known as timbaland , ( born march 10 , 1971 ) is a grammy award-winning american record producer , rapper and songwriter . he is from norfolk , virginia . timbaland owns his own record label , mosley music group , which is under universal music group . timbaland has also taught and nurtured other record producers like danja , polow da don , ryan tedder and jim beanz . other websites . - official site - mosley music group site
