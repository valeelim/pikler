Great Awakening     the great awakenings were a few periods of religious revival in united states history . the term is used sometimes to refer to events like the protestant reformation , as well as to identify general trends within u.s. religious culture . there are four generally accepted great awakenings in u.s. history : - the first great awakening ( 1730s - 1740s ) - the second great awakening ( 1800s - 1830s ) - the third great awakening ( 1880s - 1900s ) - the fourth great awakening ( 1960s - 1970s )
