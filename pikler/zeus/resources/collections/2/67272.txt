Nasal septum     the nasal septum separates the left and right airways in the nose and divides the two nostrils . the fleshy external end of the nasal septum is sometimes also called `` columella '' . the nasal septum is composed of three structures : - ethmoid bone - vomer bone - quadrangular cartilage the nasal septum can depart from the centreline of the nose ; this condition is known as a deviated septum . injury , excessive nose-picking and cocaine usage can cause septum perforations , or holes developing in the septum . an operation to the nasal septum is known as a septoplasty . other websites . - diagram at evmsent.org
