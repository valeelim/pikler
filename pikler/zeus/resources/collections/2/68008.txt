Kitagawa Utamaro     , also called utamaro , was a japanese artist . he was a master of the ukiyo-e style of woodblock prints and painting . utamaro is known for his studies of women ( `` bijinga '' ) . he also made nature studies . his work was popular in europe . he influenced artists in europe who were called impressionists . other websites . - kitagawa utamaro online - utamaro - ( from `` collection of insects in pictures '' ) - kitagawa utamaro at hill-stead museum , farmington , connecticut
