Vic Darchinyan     vic darchinyan ( , born january 7 , 1976 , in vanadzor , armenia ) is the former ibf flyweight champion . he is a left-handed boxer who currently trains under billy hussein , brother of boxers nedal and hussein hussein . he was previously trained by former three-division world champion jeff fenech in sydney , australia . other websites . - official website - vic darchinyan 's career record
