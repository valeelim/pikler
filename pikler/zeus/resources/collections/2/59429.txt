Pocking Solar Park in Germany     the pocking solar park is a 10-megawatt ( mwp ) solar photovoltaic plant which is among the largest in the world . construction of the power plant started in august 2005 and was completed in march 2006. on the former military training area in the lower-bavarian town of pocking , sheep are now grazing under and around the 57,912 photovoltaic modules . related pages . - photovoltaics - renewable energy - solar cell - solar power station in victoria - waldpolenz solar park in germany
