26     26 is a year in the 1st century . it was a common year starting on tuesday of the julian calendar . at the time , it was known as the year of the consulship of lentulus and sabinus . events . - pontius pilate is appointed as prefect of judaea . - the emperor tiberius retires to capri . he leaves the praetorian prefect sejanus in charge of both rome and the empire . - romans stop an uprising of thracian tribesmen . deaths . - claudia pulchra , second cousin and close friend to agrippina the elder . - possible crucifixion of jesus
