Mona Sahlin     mona ingeborg sahlin ( born andersson on march 9 , 1957 in sollefteå , sweden ) is a former leader for the swedish social democratic party , when she replaced göran persson after his ten years as leader , she was the first woman to do so . she was the minister responsible for the handling of the heart 2 art exhibition in stockholm 2002. toblerone scandal or sahlin scandal is the name of a swedish scandal , in which there in october 1995 came to public attention that the socialist politician mona sahlin during her time as minister for employment , 1990-1991 , on several occasions used its credit cards for private expenses . other websites . - social democratic homepage
