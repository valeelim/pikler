Water hemisphere     the water hemisphere is the hemisphere on the earth with the highest area of water . it is centered on , near new zealand . the other half of the earth is the land hemisphere . the water hemisphere has only one-eighth of the world 's land . australia , new zealand , antarctica , a small part of southeast asia and the southern part of south america are in the water hemisphere . most of the pacific ocean and the indian ocean are on the water hemisphere . the area of the oceans of the water hemisphere is much higher than its land area , but the area of the oceans of the land hemisphere is still higher than its land area .
