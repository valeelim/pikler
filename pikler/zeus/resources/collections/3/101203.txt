Jamie Redknapp     jamie redknapp ( born 25 june 1973 ) is an english footballer . he was born in barton on sea , hampshire . he played for liverpool f.c. , tottenham hotspur f.c. , a.f.c . bournemouth and southampton f.c.. his father , harry redknapp , is the manager of portsmouth f.c . redknapp married singer louise nurding in 1998 .
