LNER Class A4     the lner class a4 was a type of steam locomotive made in england in 1935. it was designed by sir nigel gresley for the london north eastern railway . 35 locomotives were built in total . they were designed to be very fast and had a very sleek shape . one of them , 4468 `` mallard '' , is the fastest steam locomotive ever made . it set a world speed record of 125 mph ( 201.16 km/h ) on july 3 , 1938. six a4s still exist today . the rest were scrapped when steam stopped being used to power trains in britain . other websites . - lner encyclopedia page covering the history and development of the lner a4 pacifics
