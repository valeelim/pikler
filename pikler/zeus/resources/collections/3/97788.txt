Durham County, North Carolina    durham county , north carolina durham county is a county in the u.s. state of north carolina . its county seat is durham . the county was made on april 17 , 1881 , from parts of orange county and wake county . in 1911 parts of cedar fork township in wake county were transferred to durham county and became carr township . durham county is part of the regional triangle j council of governments . the only incorporated city in durham county is the city of durham . but , parts of the town of chapel hill in orange county are also in durham county . connected counties . - person county , north carolina - north - granville county , north carolina - northeast - wake county , north carolina - southeast - chatham county , north carolina - south-southwest - orange county , north carolina - west other websites . - durham county government official website - durham convention and visitors bureau
