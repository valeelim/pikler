Motherwell     motherwell is a large town in north lanarkshire , scotland . it is an important town as many people have to come through it to get to places such as lanark , hamilton and glasgow . it has a big train station with four platforms , the biggest in north lanarkshire . the station can take you to places such as london . motherwell is next to wishaw . motherwell has a football team , motherwell f.c.. they play at fir park .
