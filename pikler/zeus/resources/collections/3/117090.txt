Lasbela District     lasbela is a coastal district of balochistan province , pakistan . it became a separate district in the kalat division on 30 june 1954. the name is derived from the words `` las '' which signifies a `` plain '' , the greater part of the area being a flat plain , and `` bela '' which means `` jungle '' and is also the name of the principal town of the district . uthal is the district headquarters . the district is divided into 9 tehsils and 21 union councils . tehsils . - bela tehsil - dureji tehsil - gadani tehsil - hub tehsil - kanraj tehsil - lakhra tehsil - liari tehsil - sonmiani tehsil - uthal tehsil
