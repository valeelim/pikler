Chrysler Building     the chrysler building is a skyscraper in new york city , united states . it is 319 meters ( 1046 feet ) tall and has 77 floors . it was built in 1931 and is one of the tallest buildings in the world . the chrysler building was the tallest building in the world from 1930 to 1931. before it was built , the tallest building in the world was the trump building . the empire state building became the tallest one year later . the chrysler building is still the tallest brick building in the world . the chrysler building was also the tallest structure in the world from 1930 to 1931. before it was built , the tallest structure in the world was eiffel tower . the empire state building became the tallest one year later . other pages . - list of tallest buildings in the world - list of tallest buildings in new york city other websites . - emporis.com - chrysler building - skycraperpage.com â€“ chrysler building
