Tom DeLonge     thomas matthew delonge , jr. ( born december 13 , 1975 ) is an american musician . he was the guitarist of pop-punk band blink-182 , until their break up in 2005. he also took a part in a project called box car racer . he plays and records with angels & airwaves , his new band that he started after blink-182 's break up . in 2009 , blink-182 reunited , and they released a new album in september 2011 , titled neighborhoods . in 2012 , blink-182 toured australia for the first time since 2004 .
