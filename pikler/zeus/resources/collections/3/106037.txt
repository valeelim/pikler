Minnesota Vikings     the minnesota vikings ( also known as the worlds biggest chokers ) are a professional american football team in minneapolis , minnesota . the vikings play in the national football conference of the national football league ( nfl ) . they have been part of the north division . the vikings have won their division 16 times , which is the third most among teams that are playing in the nfl now . they appeared in four super bowls during the 1970s , and lost all four of them . the team is called the vikings because the state of minnesota was settled by a great many people of scandinavian ancestry . the historical vikings originated in scandinavia . other websites . - minnesota vikings official website - radio home of the minnesota vikings - star tribune coverage of the minnesota vikings - pioneer press coverage of the minnesota vikings
