Alan Duncan     alan james carter duncan ( born 31 march 1957 ) is an english politician and a former minister of state in the department for international development . he was born in rickmansworth , hertfordshire . he is the member of parliament for the rutland and melton constituency in the united kingdom . he was first elected in the 1992 general election . he is a member of the conservative party . he is openly gay and in a civil partnership with james dunseath .
