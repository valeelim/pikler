Eurofighter Typhoon     the eurofighter typhoon is a jet fighter aircraft made by eads , bae systems and alenia aeronautica with eurofighter gmbh . it first flew in 1994 and was introduced in 2003. it uses canards , which makes it agile . it uses a delta wing . it is called the `` eurofighter '' because it was made by european countries , italy , the united kingdom , germany , and spain . the typhoon is 11 meters ( 36 feet ) wide , 15 meters ( 49 feet ) long , and 5.3 metres ( 17 ft ) tall . it is 9,750 kilograms ( 21,494 lb ) with nothing in it , and can weigh up to 21,000 kilograms ( 46,300 lb ) with things in it . it is powered by two ej200 engines , which can each make 20,000 pounds ( 9,072 kilograms ) of thrust . it can go 2,125 kilometres per hour ( 1,320 mph ) . it carries a 27mm mauser cannon , and several missiles and or bombs . the countries that designed the typhoon use it , but has also been ordered by austria and saudi arabia .
