Louis-Claude Daquin     louis-claude daquin ( born paris , 4 july 1694 ; died paris 15 june 1772 ) was a french composer . he was writing in the last part of the baroque period and the early part of the classical music period . he was a virtuoso organist and harpsichordist . he wrote a lot of excellent music for organ and for harpsichord . his most popular piece is called `` le coucou '' ( `` the cuckoo '' ) .
