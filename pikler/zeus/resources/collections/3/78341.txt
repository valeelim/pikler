4 (number)    4 ( number ) the number four is a number and a numeral . it comes after the number three , and before the number five . in roman numerals , it is iv . in mathematics , the number four is an even number and the smallest composite number . four is also the second square number after one . a small minority of people have four fingers and four toes , on each hand and foot , respectively . this proves difficult to count on your fingers . four squared is 16 , and four doubled is 8 .
