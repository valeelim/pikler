Münster Government Region     münster is one of the five `` regierungsbezirke '' of north rhine-westphalia , germany , in the north of the state . the `` regierungsbezirk '' was made in 1815. this means it is one of the original 25 `` regierungsbezirke '' made to help govern prussia . the last time the boundaries of the region were changed was 1975. this was when the number of districts was changed from 10 to 5 , and the number of district-free cities ( urban districts ) from six to three . other websites . - official website - the münsterland tourism board
