Province of Trieste     the province of trieste ( italian : provincia di trieste , slovene : tržaška pokrajina ) is a province in the autonomous friuli-venezia giulia region of italy . its capital is the city of trieste . it has an area of 212 km² , and a total population of 242,235 ( 2001 ) . it has a coastal length of 48.1 km . there are 6 communes in the province .
