DIY     diy or `` do it yourself '' is a method of home improvement , where the home owner gets materials and tools for themself , then does the work on their own , without professional help . diy can also mean using any skill to do work or a hobby for fun and not hire help . an example is an activity in arts and crafts . a diy culture is also used in the alternative rock , punk rock , and indie rock music scenes ; and in the zine community .
