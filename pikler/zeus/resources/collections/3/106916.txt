Blood Red Shoes     blood red shoes are an indie music band from brighton , england . blood red shoes 's first album was `` box of secrets '' . there are two members : laura-mary carter and steven ansell . they are often compared to `` the white stripes '' due to the boy-girl drum-guitar setup , but they dislike this comparison and , indeed , the white stripes themselves . laura-mary plays guitar while steven plays drums . discography . compilations . - `` i 'll be your eyes '' - ( v2 records ) 25 june2007
