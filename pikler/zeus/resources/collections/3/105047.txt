Mount Batur     mount batur or gunung batur is a volcano on bali . mount batur last erupted in 2000. it is a volcano inside an older , larger , volcano . the original eruption , about 30,000 years ago , is one of the largest known volcanic events on earth . the volcano is now 1,717 metres ( 5,633 feet ) high . there are many people — whole villages — living inside the caldera .
