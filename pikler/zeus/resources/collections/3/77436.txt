Republic F-105 Thunderchief     the f-105 thunderchief was a bomber during the vietnam war . it first flew in 1955 , and was introduced in 1958. it was used as a bomber , but was later replaced by the f-4 phantom ii and the f-111 raven . but it was still in service for a while as a wild weasel . it could carry an electronic counter-measures pod that could mess up the enemy radar .
