Mitlödi     mitlödi is a municipality of the canton of glarus in switzerland . on 1 january 2011 the former municipalities of mitlödi , betschwanden , braunwald , elm , engi , haslen , linthal , luchsingen , matt , rüti , schwanden , schwändi and sool merged into the new municipality of glarus süd . other websites . - website of mitlödi
