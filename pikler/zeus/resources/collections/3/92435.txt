New York Jets     the new york jets are an american football team . they are a part of the national football league ( nfl ) . even though they are called the new york jets they play in new jersey . they hail from the same stadium as the new york giants . they have many fans in new york and new jersey . the new york jets started playing in 1960. they have only won one championship . the jets were originally called the new york titans . they played their games in the polo grounds . when they moved to shea stadium in 1964 , they were called the jets . that was a more modern-sounding name , and it also rhymed with `` new york mets '' , the other team that played its games at shea . the new york jets have won only one super bowl in 1969 , this victory was guaranteed by their qb , joe namath , in the media in the weeks leading up to the game .
