Müritz Rural District     müritz is a former district in the southern part of mecklenburg-western pomerania , germany . it is named after the lake müritz . lake müritz is the largest lake in germany . lake constance is larger , but part of it is in switzerland and another part in austria . the district was created on june 12 , 1994 by joining the old districts of röbel and waren , and a few municipalities from the districts malchin and neustrelitz . other websites . - official website
