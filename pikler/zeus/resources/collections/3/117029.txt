Rights     a right is something a person has that people think should not be taken away from them . a right is different than a privilege , which is something that must be earned . examples . - many people say people have a `` right to life '' . that means that if one person kills another , the killer would be taking away the victim 's `` right to life '' . - people may also have a `` right to property '' . that means that people can not steal things from each other . - in many countries people have a `` right to free speech '' , which means other people can not stop them from saying what they want to . related pages . - constitution - constitutional economics - rule of law - natural rights
