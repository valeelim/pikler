New Castle, Indiana    new castle , indiana new castle is a city in henry county , indiana , 44 miles ( 71 km ) east of indianapolis , on the big blue river . in 1900 , 3,406 people lived in the town ; in 1910 , 9,446 ; in 1920 , 14,458 ; and in 1940 , 16,620. the population was 17,780 in 2000. the city is the county seat of henry county . new castle is home to new castle fieldhouse , the largest high school in the world . other websites . - city of new castle news & media . - the courier-times - new castle online
