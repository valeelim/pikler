64 (number)    64 ( number ) sixty-four is a number . it comes between sixty-three and sixty-five , and is an even number . it is divisible by 1 , 2 , 4 , 8 , 16 , 32 , and 64 . 64 is the 8th square number after 1 , 4 , 9 , 16 , 25 , 36 , and 49. it is the fourth cube after 1 , 8 , and 27. there are sixty-four 1x1 squares on a chess , checkers and draughts board . it is also a power of two .
