Soanian     the soanian is an archaeological site that is dated to ( comes from ) the early stone age . the name comes from the soan valley in the sivalik hills in pakistan . the people who lived on this site were probably homo erectus , the first people to control fire . hundreds of stone knives were found at the site , as well as hand axes and cleavers . no human skeletons of this age have been found . in the soan river gorge many rocks with fossils can be seen . the 14 million year old fossils of gazelles , rhinoceroses , crocodiles , giraffes and rodents have been found . some of these fossils are on display at the natural history museum of islamabad .
