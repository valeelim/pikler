Thalassa (moon)    thalassa ( moon ) thalassa or neptune iv , is the second closest moon to neptune . it was named after a daughter of aether and hemera from greek mythology . `` thalassa '' is also the greek word for `` sea '' . thalassa was discovered sometime before mid-september , 1989 from the images taken by the `` voyager 2 '' probe . it was given the designation s/1989 n 5. the discovery was said ( iauc 4867 ) on september 29 , 1989 , but the text only talks of `` 25 frames taken over 11 days '' , giving a discovery date of sometime before september 18. the name was given on 16 september 1991. thalassa is not a sphere and shows no sign of any geological changes . unusually for a non-spherical moon , it appears to be disk-shaped . other websites . - thalassa profile by nasa 's solar system exploration
