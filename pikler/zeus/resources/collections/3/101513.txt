Cookstown     cookstown is a small town in northern ireland which is in the united kingdom . 10646 people live in the town . the main street in cookstown is the longest and widest in ireland . on 29 april 2001 the united kingdom government had a census and found out that there were 10,646 people living in cookstown . they also found that : - 26.0 % of the people were less than 16 years old and 15.6 % were over 60 years old - 49.7 % of the people were males and 50.3 % were females - 52.8 % were from a roman catholic background and 45.1 % were from a protestant background - 3.9 % of people aged from 16 to 74 had no job .
