Complicated     complicated was the first and perhaps the most popular song sung by avril lavigne . it was released in 2002. it was on her album `` let go '' . other songs on the same cd single are `` i do n't give '' and additional `` why '' on maxi-cds . it was at one point # 1 in the charts in canada , uk , australia and the us . she was 17 when she sang it and she and her band wrote it together .
