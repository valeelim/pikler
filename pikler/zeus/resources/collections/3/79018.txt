Acanthite     acanthite is a mineral that can be lead gray , gray , or coal in color and is made from crystals . its name comes from the greek word , akanta , meaning `` arrow '' , or the latin word `` argentum '' , meaning `` silver '' . it is made of silver ( i ) sulfide . it is found in freiberg , schneeberg , and annaberg , germany . acanthite is not radioactive .
