Río Limpio, Elías Piña    río limpio , elías piña río limpio ( in english , `` clean river '' ) is a dominican municipal district of pedro santana in the elías piña province . the municipality had , in 2010 , a total population of 3,364 : 1,913 men and 1,451 women . the urban population was of the total population . río limpio became a municipal district of pedro santana by the law 36 of 15 november 1992. río limpio has an area of . it is on the right side of the `` vallecito '' river , a left tributary of the artibonite river . the town is to the west of the `` loma nalga de maco '' mountain , high ( ) , the highest mountain in the western cordillera central mountain range . the limits of the municipality are the dajabón province to the northwest , the santiago rodríguez province to the northeast , the san juan province to the east , the municipality of pedro santana to the south and haiti to the west . the main economic activity of the municipality is farming . the main crops are coffee and rice .
