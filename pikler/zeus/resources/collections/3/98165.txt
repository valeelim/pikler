Gewerkschaft Deutscher Lokomotivführer     gewerkschaft deutscher lokomotivführer ( gdl ) ( ) is a trade union of germany . its president is claus weselsky , who took over from manfred schell in 2008. the gdl has a membership of 34,000. most members live in the old east germany . gdl is a member of the deutscher beamtenbund ( dbb ) . other websites . - gdl homepage
