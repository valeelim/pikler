Sivasspor     sivasspor is a turkish football club founded in 1967. its stadium , the sivas 4 eylül stadium is in sivas city . sivasspor 's colours are red and white . it is called `` yiğidolar '' in turkey . sivasspor became fourth in the turkey turkcell super league 2007/2008 season . sivasspor will play first match in europe with grbalj team to join uefa cup .
