Denis Herron     denis herron ( b. june 18 , 1952 in chambly , quebec ) is a former canadian professional ice hockey goaltender who played for the montreal canadiens , kansas city scouts , and pittsburgh penguins in the national hockey league . denis won the 1981 vezina trophy with montreal along with michel larocque and richard sevigny and in 1982 he won the william m. jennings trophy with rick wamsley . herron finds room at the inn ; ian macdonald . the gazette . montreal , que . : dec 23 , 2004. pg . c.3
