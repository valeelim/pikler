Proteaceae     proteaceae is a family of flowering plants . mainly restricted to the southern hemisphere , it is a fairly large family , with around 80 genera but fewer than 2000 species . together with the platanaceae and nelumbonaceae they make up the order proteales . traditionally divided in two subfamilies : persoonioideae and grevilleoideae . recently , workers have recognized seven subfamilies : - persoonioideae , - bellendenoideae , - eidotheoideae , - proteoideae , - sphalmioideae , - carnarvonioideae - grevilleoideae other websites . - `` protea '' family - toxicosis in dogs - phylogeny , biogeography , and the evolution of life-history traits in leucadendron ( proteaceae )
