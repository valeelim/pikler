Pomo     the pomo were a native american tribe who lived in what is now northern california by russian river . pomo is a language family that includeds seven languages , including northern pomo , northeastern pomo , eastern pomo , southeastern pomo , and kashaya . the pomo tribe were hunters and gatherers . the men hunted meat and fish , while the women and the children gatherd nuts and berries . the pomo villages might have had as few as 100 or moore than 1,500. they wove finely crafted baskets and sometimes decorated them with feathers and shell beads .
