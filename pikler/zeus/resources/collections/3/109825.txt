Bill Moggridge     william ( bill ) moggridge , ( 25 june 1943 - 8 september 2012 ) was a british industrial designer and co-founder of the silicon valley-based design firm ideo . he helped design what was arguably the first laptop computer , the grid compass . he was in favour of user-centered design process in product development cycles and also worked towards popularizing interaction design as a mainstream discipline . moggridge was the author of `` designing interactions '' , published in october 2006. isbn 978-0-262-13474-3 he was also involved in design education in the united kingdom and the united states ; teaching at the royal college of art , the london business school and stanford university . references . - designing interactions - book home page - talk at the innvovationsforum interactiondesign , germany 2007
