Betta     betta is a genus of fish that includes the siamese fighting fish . the males of this type of fish have long flowing fins , whilst the females do not , and the males also have a larger range of color . betas come from southeast asia . many of them can be purchased in a local petstore . male betas can not be in the same tank or bowls because they will fight . betas are known as the fighting fish . males and females are easy to tell apart because males are more colorful than female betas .
