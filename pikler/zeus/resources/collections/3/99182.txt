Gujrat District     gujrat district ( ) is a district in the punjab province of pakistan . the city of gujrat is the capital . gujrat district , had many sikh sardars , ruling villages , islam was a majority , with 10 % sikhs and hindus together in the district . islam had a good influence over the jatts of gujrat district , 98 % of jatts them followed islam , the people of this region are mainly jatts , rajputs , gakhars , balochi , pathans , etc . the district is composed of 3 tehsils : - gujrat tehsil - kharian tehsil - sarai alamgir tehsil
