Andy Burnham     andrew murray burnham ( born 7 january 1970 ) is an english politician . he was a candidate in the 2010 labour leadership election . as of 2012 burnham was shadow secretary of state for health within ed miliband 's shadow cabinet . he is the member of parliament for the leigh constituency in the united kingdom . he was first elected in the 2001 general election . burnham was born in old roan , aintree , lancashire and raised in culcheth , warrington . he is a member of the labour party . on 5 may 2017 , he was elected mayor of greater manchester .
