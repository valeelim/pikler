Naiad (moon)    naiad ( moon ) naiad or neptune iii is the closest moon to neptune named after the naiads of greek legend . it was found sometime before mid-september , 1989 from the images taken by the `` voyager 2 '' probe . the last moon to be discovered during the flyby , it was designated s/1989 n 6. naiad is not a sphere and probably has not been changed by any internal geological processes after its formation . it orbits about 23,500 km above neptune 's cloud tops . since the `` voyager 2 '' flyby , the neptune system has been studied a lot from ground-based observatories and the hubble space telescope as well . in 2002-03 , keck telescope looked at the system using adaptive optics and detected easily the biggest four closer satellites . thalassa was found with some image processing , but naiad was not located . other websites . - naiad profile by nasa 's solar system exploration
