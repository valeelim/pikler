She     the word `` she '' is a third-person singular pronoun used to talk about a female . when the name of a woman has been the subject , then `` she '' takes the place of that name , in the text . while `` she '' is the subject form , the word `` her '' is the object or possessive form ( `` see table below '' ) . the word `` she '' is used for a woman ( or girl ) where the word `` he '' would be used for a man , or `` it '' for a neuter pronoun . in 1999 , the american dialect society chose `` she '' as the word of the past millennium ( 1000-year period ) . the society noted the word was not used before the year 1000 ; `` she '' was first seen in writings from the 12th century , where the word `` heo '' had been used to mean either the feminine or mixed plural as with `` they '' .
