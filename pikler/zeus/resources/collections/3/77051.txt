Damson     damson is a kind of plum . like the plum , it comes from the rosaceae family . originally , the fruit comes from the mediterranean . damsons are thought to be members of the 'european plum ' family , which also includes greengage , mirabelle , quetsche , and italian prune plums . the damson is generally less sweet than other kinds of plum and is used in jams , jellies , and liqueurs .
