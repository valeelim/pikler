Bagh, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa    bagh , khyber pakhtunkhwa bagh is a union council of abbottabad district in the khyber pakhtunkhwa province of pakistan . it has two high schools ghs bagh and ghs kehal . it is in the centre of the district , in the southern part of abbottabad tehsil , it is surrounded by the following union councils , to the north and east by dhamtour , to the north by sarbhana , to the east by bagnotar , namli maira and nagri bala - and to the south by dewal manal . the union council is divided into three areas : bagh , kehial and jaggian kohalian .
