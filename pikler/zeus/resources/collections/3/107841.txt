Native Americans in the United States     native americans in the united states are the indigenous peoples from the areas of north america now part of the continental united states , including parts of alaska . the cherokee were the first native americans to be recognized as us citizens . in the 19th century white colonists called the cherokee one of the five civilized tribes . the others were chicksaw , chocktaw , creek , and seminole . there are about 310 indian reservations in the us . most native americans do not live on a reservation anymore . native americans are affected by some problems more than white americans . for example native americans are alcoholic six times more often than average .
