Forlì     forlì is a city in the region of emilia-romagna in italy . it is the capital of the `` province of forlì-cesena '' . about 110,000 people live in forli . it has a humid subtropical climate ( `` cfa '' in the koeppen climate classification ) . benito mussolini , dictator of italy during world war two , was born near forli , in predappio .
