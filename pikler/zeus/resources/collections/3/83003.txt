Mitsubishi A5M Claude     the a5m claude was a japanese fighter aircraft made by mitsubishi . it first flew in 1935 and was introduced in 1937. it was the first monoplane ( fixed-wing aircraft with one set of wings ) that could work from an aircraft carrier . it was similar to the p-26 peashooter , an american monoplane fighter . it fought in the second sino-japanese war . after the pearl harbor attacks , it was not good enough to fight against american fighters like the curtiss p-40 and the f4f wildcat , so it was replaced by the a6m zero . related pages . - a6m zero
