Book of Ruth     the book of `` ruth '' ( meaning `` pity or compassion '' ) is the eight book of the old testament ( christian ) , and the tanakh ( jewish ) . it is one of the shortest books in both the jewish and christian holy books , consisting of only four chapters . it is unknown who wrote the book . some think that it was written by samuel , but there is no evidence for that . according to many scholars , this book was originally part of the book of judges , and was later taken from that book and made independent . it was probably written around 900 bc . the book tells the story of the family of elimelech , his wife naomi , and their sons mahlon and chilion . because of a famine , they were forced to leave bethlehem and go to the land of moab . once there , elimelech died , and his sons married two moabite women . mahlon married ruth , and chilion married orpah . after some time there , mahlon and chilion both died . naomi was forced to return home because her husband and sons were dead . she then told ruth and orpah to return home to their families and find new husbands . orpah returned home but ruth promised she would follow naomi wherever she went .
