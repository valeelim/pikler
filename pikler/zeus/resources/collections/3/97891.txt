Yekaterinburg     yekaterinburg is a big city in the ural mountains in the asian part of russia . it has a population of 1,293,537 people . the city was started in 1723 by vasily tatischev and named after saint catherine . yekaterinburg is a sister city of - - pilsen , czech republic - - san jose , california , u.s.a. , since 1992 - - guangzhou , the people 's republic of china , since july 10 2002 - - genoa , italy - - ferentino , italy
