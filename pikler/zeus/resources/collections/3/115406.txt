Saint Dominic     saint dominic ( ; 1170 – 6 august 1221 ) was the founder of a catholic order , the friars preachers , often called the dominicans or the order of preachers ( op ) . dominic is the patron saint of astronomers and the dominican republic . he was born in caleruega , half-way between osma and aranda in old castile , spain . he is sometimes called dominic of osma or dominic de guzmán . in paintings , his sign is a star or a dog holding a burning torch in its mouth .
