Muzaffarabad     muzaffarabad ( ) is the capital city of azad kashmir . it is in muzaffarabad district on the banks of the jhelum and neelum rivers . it is very hilly . the district is bounded by khyber pakhtunkhwa in the west , by the kupwara and baramulla districts of the indian jammu and kashmir in the east , and the neelum district of azad kashmir in the north .
