Bergstraße Rural District     kreis bergstraße is a `` landkreis '' ( rural district ) in the south of hesse , germany . the district was created in 1938 , when the two former old districts of bensheim and heppenheim were joined . the district is located in the odenwald mountains , with the 517m high melibokus as the highest elevation . the `` bergstraße '' is a tourist route which leads from darmstadt to wiesloch ; the northern part of this route goes through the district and gave it its name . other websites . - official website ( german )
