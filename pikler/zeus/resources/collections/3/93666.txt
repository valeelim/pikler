Treaty of Troyes     the treaty of troyes ( 1420 ) tried to change the inheritance rule for the kings of france , but did not succeed at doing that . this treaty said that henry v of england and his sons would become kings of france after the death of charles vi of france . the treaty tried to disinherit the future charles vii of france . the treaty did not work because henry v and charles vi died two months apart and joan of arc recaptured land for charles vii .
