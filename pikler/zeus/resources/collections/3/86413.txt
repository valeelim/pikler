Grimoire     grimoire is a book of witchcraft or paranormal magic . the word is pronounced `` grim-warr '' , which rhymes with `` guitar '' . the word is a mistaken way of saying `` grammar '' . it probably started being used in a time when not many people could read . then any book might be thought to contain instructions for magic . in the religion of wicca other forms of neopaganism , such a book is sometimes called a `` book of shadows '' .
