Paul Hindemith     paul hindemith ( pronounce : powl hin-de-mit ) , ( 16 november 1895 in hanau - 28 december 1963 in frankfurt am main ) was a german composer , violist , teacher , music theorist and conductor . he was the most important and influential german composer during the years between the two world wars . his style was at first influenced by schoenberg and expressionism , but gradually it became more neo-classical , and he turned against schoenberg 's whole approach . he was also famous as a viola player and composed a concerto for viola and orchestra .
