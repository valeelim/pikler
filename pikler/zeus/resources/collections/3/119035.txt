Moorslede     moorslede is a municipality in the belgian province of west flanders . moorslede comprises the towns of dadizele and moorslede itself . in 2007 , 10697 people lived there . it is at 50° 53 north , 03° 03 east . constant lievens ( 1856-1893 ) , the apostle of the chota nagpur , was born in moorslede . the 1950 world cycling championshipwas held in moorslede and was won by briek schotte . other websites . - www.moorslede.be
