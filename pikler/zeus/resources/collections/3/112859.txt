Atatürk International Airport     atatürk international airport ( formerly yesilkoy international airport ) is the major international airport in turkey . it is in the european part of istanbul . in 2010 , about 32 million passengers used atatürk airport . it was the 8th busiest airport in europe in 2010. the airport has three terminals – terminal 1 for international passengers , 2 for domestic passengers , and 3 for cargo . atatürk airport has three runways - runways 3000x45m ( 17l/35r & 17r/35l ) and runway 2600x60m ( 05/23 ) . to get to the city center from istanbul atatürk airport ( ist ) , there is a light rail service , airport shuttle service , municipal buses , taxi , or hire cars .
