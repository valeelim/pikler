Spa town     a spa town , or simply spa , is a town that many people go to for health reasons . there is usually a spring there , or several springs . the water that comes from these springs is good for curing many illnesses . when people go to a spa town to get better we say that they go to `` take the waters '' . the word `` spa '' comes from the belgian town spa . in continental europe a spa was called a `` ville d'eau '' ( town of water ) . the term `` spa '' is used for towns or resorts which offer hydrotherapy ( making people better with water ) . this can include cold water or mineral water treatments and hot spring baths .
