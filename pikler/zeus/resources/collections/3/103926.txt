Zettabyte     a zettabyte ( zb ) is a unit of measurement for computers of the future . one zettabyte holds 1000 exabytes ( eb ) or a billion trillion ( 1,000,000,000,000,000,000,000 ) bytes , and 1000 zettabytes make one yottabyte . a zettabyte is so large that it would take about a million supercomputers ( or a billion powerful home computers ) to store this amount of data .
