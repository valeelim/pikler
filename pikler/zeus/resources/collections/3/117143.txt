Jacobabad District     jacobabad district ( ) is a district of sindh , pakistan . according to the 1998 census , 1,425,572 people lived there , of which 24.10 % were urban . the city of jacobabad is the capital of the district . the district is administratively subdivided into the following talukas : - ghari khairo tehsil - khairo tehsil - jacobabad tehsil - thull tehsil
