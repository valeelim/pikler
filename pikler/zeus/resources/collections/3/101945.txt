Skokie, Illinois    skokie , illinois skokie , illinois is a village in the american state of illinois . it was once known as niles center . it is located on the northwest side of chicago in cook county . skokie has an area of 10.1 square miles ( 2.62 square kilometers ) . according the 2000 census , its population was 63,348. the mayor of skokie , as of september 2014 , was george van dusen . skokie was named niles centre in 1888. by 1910 , the spelling of this name was changed to niles center . as a nearby village was named niles , there was confusion , and the name of the village was changed to skokie has in 1940 .
