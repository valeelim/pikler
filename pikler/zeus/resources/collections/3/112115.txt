The Assassination of Jesse James by the Coward Robert Ford     the assassination of jesse james by the coward robert ford is a 2007 western movie . it was based on ron hansen 's 1983 novel of the same name . the movie is directed by andrew dominik , with brad pitt portraying jesse james and casey affleck as robert ford . filming took place in calgary , edmonton and winnipeg . actors . - brad pitt as jesse james - casey affleck as robert ford - sam rockwell as charley ford - paul schneider as dick liddel - jeremy renner as wood hite - sam shepard as frank james - garret dillahunt as edward miller - alison elliott as martha bolton - mary-louise parker as zee james - james carville as governor thomas t. crittenden - zooey deschanel as dorothy evans - hugh ross as narrator other websites . - official site and trailer - official website for the family of jesse james with the james family 's own movie review
