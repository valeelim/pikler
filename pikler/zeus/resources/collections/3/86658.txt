Moshi Moshi Records     moshi moshi records is a small london-based independent record label that has released music by the bands such as the rakes , bloc party , hot chip , hot club de paris , kate nash , junkboy , architecture in helsinki , tilly and the wall , yeti , and its newest release is from a group called au revoir simone . mates of state is the moshi moshi 's 50th release . it is named after the japanese telephone greeting `` moshi moshi .
