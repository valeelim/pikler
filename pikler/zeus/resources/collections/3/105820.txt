John Elway     john albert elway , jr. ( born june 28 , 1960 in port angeles , washington ) is a retired hall of fame american football quarterback . he played his college football at stanford and his whole professional career for the denver broncos football team of the national football league ( nfl ) . he was the nfl most valuable player ( mvp ) in 1987. elway won super bowls with the broncos in 1998 and 1999 , and was the mvp of super bowl xxxiii in 1999 , throwing for one touchdown and rushing for another as the broncos beat the atlanta falcons , 34-19. that ended up being his final game ( other than the 1999 pro bowl ) , as he retired after that season .
