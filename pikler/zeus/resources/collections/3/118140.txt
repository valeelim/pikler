Faline     faline is a fictional female roe deer in felix salten 's novel `` bambi , a life in the woods '' and its sequel , `` bambi 's children '' . she is bambi 's mate . she also appeared in the walt disney movies `` bambi '' and `` bambi ii '' based on the book . her mother is ena . first shown as a fawn and later as an adult doe , faline 's role is as bambi 's friend and later mate . faline was featured as a guest in `` house of mouse '' . she was voiced by cammie king and ann gillis in the original film , and andrea bowen in bambi ii .
