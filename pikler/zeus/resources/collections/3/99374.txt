Baekdu Mountain     baekdu mountain or changbai mountain is a volcano on the border between north korea and china . this mountain is 54.5 % in north korean territory , 45.5 % in china . the korean name is baekdu-san . it is means white head mountain . the china name is changbai-shan . it is means snow piles up on the large mountain . baekdu has large crater lake , it is called heaven lake . koreans call it `` cheon-ji '' lake , chinese called `` tiandi '' lake . it is 2,744 metres above sea level . the beijing olympic torch was lit on baekdu/changbai mountain . heaven lake is the caldera made by a gigantic eruption in 969 ad ( Â± 20 years ) .
