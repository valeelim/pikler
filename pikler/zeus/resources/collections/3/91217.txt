Jan Pieterszoon Sweelinck     jan pieterszoon sweelinck ( born may 1562 in deventer , netherlands ; died 16 october 1621 ) was a dutch composer and organist . sweelinck ( pronounce : zvay-link ) lived at the end of the renaissance and beginning of the baroque periods . he was one of the greatest organists and composers of his day . he was the organist at the oude kerk ( “ old church ” ) in amsterdam for 44 years . many musicians travelled a long way to amsterdam to hear him improvise at the organ . he composed music for the organ and harpsichord as well as vocal music .
