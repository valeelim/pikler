Mausoleum     a mausoleum is a building used to store the remains of people who have died . instead of being buried in soil , like in a cemetery , they are placed in chambers behind walls , or in a vault under the floor . soviet leader lenin is a mausoleum . in 1953 , joseph stalin was buried next to him but his body was removed during destalinization . examples . - the taj mahal - the great pyramids - grant 's tomb
