Hyperion Cantos     the hyperion cantos is a series of science fiction books that were written by dan simmons . `` hyperion '' received the hugo award for best novel in 1990 and `` the fall of hyperion '' was nominated for the nebula award for best novel in 1990. three of the novels received the locus award for best science fiction novel : `` hyperion '' ( 1990 ) , `` the fall of hyperion '' ( 1991 ) and `` the rise of endymion '' ( 1998 ) . the author recently said that a movie might be made about the series . books . - `` hyperion '' ( 1989 ) - `` the fall of hyperion '' ( 1990 ) - `` endymion '' ( 1996 ) - `` the rise of endymion '' ( 1997 ) short stories . - `` orphans of the helix '' - `` remembering siri '' - `` the death of the centaur ''
