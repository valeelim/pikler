Principality of Khachen     the principality of khachen ( ) was a medieval armenian principality , in part of historical artsakh ( present-day nagorno-karabakh ) . the regions of artsakh and utik were attached to the kingdom of armenia in antiquity . from the twelfth century the khachen principality dominated the region . the byzantine emperor constantine vii addressed his letters to the prince of khachen with the inscription `` to prince of khachen , armenia .
