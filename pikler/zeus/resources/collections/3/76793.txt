Rhône     the rhône is a river in france and switzerland . it is 812 kilometers long . the river starts in rhône glacier , which is in switzerland . then , the rhône river joints the saône , in france ( and other rivers ) and ends in the mediterranean sea . the river rhône empties into the mediterranean . only the nile brings more water into the mediterranean . the river drains an are of 95.5500 m. the usual amount of water is about 1.700 m per second ( at tarrascon ) . during floods , this value has reached over 12.000 m per second .
