Yalova     yalova is a turkish city in the northwest , which was affected in the earthquake of 17 august 1999. the city is along the coast of the marmara sea . yalova has a better quality of life from other turkish cities . it has a population of 77,000. the first people settled in this region in the prehistoric period . twin cities of yalova are bonn in germany and mahachkala in russia .
