French Community     the french community ( ) was the body which replaced the french union in 1958. under the 1958 constitution , member territories , former french colonies , had a lot of independence , but france controlled the currency , defence , foreign affairs and national security . when the community was established , french president de gaulle said that any country in the french community could move to complete independence . apart from guinea , which chose by referendum in 1958 not to join the community , all french-ruled territories in sub-saharan africa joined the french community . they obtained independence in 1960. other pages . - colonialism and decolonization other websites . - communauté française
