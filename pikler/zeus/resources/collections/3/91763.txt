Uetersen     uetersen ( ) is a small city in the district of pinneberg , in schleswig-holstein , germany . it is situated approx . 7 km ( 4 miles ) south of elmshorn , and 30 km ( 18 miles ) northwest of hamburg . uetersen is home to the rosarium uetersen , the oldest and largest rose garden in northern germany , created in 1929. mathias rust took off from uetersen for his historic flight in 1987. people . - arthur drews - franz kruckenberg - detlef lienau
