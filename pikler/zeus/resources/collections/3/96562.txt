728 Leonisis     728 leonisis is an asteroid of the flora family , found by johann palisa in 1912 in vienna . its provisional name was 1912 nu . astronomers are not sure what its spectral class is . it has been previously placed in the rare a and l classes . these are generally `` stony '' spectra , but with significant differences from the usual s-type . the unusual spectrum brings leonisis ' membership in the flora family into doubt . other websites . - discovery circumstances : numbered minor planets
