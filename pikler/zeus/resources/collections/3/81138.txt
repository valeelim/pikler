Avenches (district)    avenches ( district ) avenches was a district of the canton of vaud , switzerland . the capital was the town of avenches . as of 2006 it is part of the broye-vully district . it contained the following municipalities : - avenches - bellerive - chabrey - constantine - cudrefin - donatyre - faoug - montmagny - mur - oleyres - vallamand - villars-le-grand
