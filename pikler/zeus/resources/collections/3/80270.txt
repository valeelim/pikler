The Princess Diaries 2     the princess diaries 2 : royal engagement is a 2004 disney movie based on the princess diaries book series by meg cabot . it is a sequel to the 2001 movie `` the princess diaries '' . the movie is a romantic comedy movie about 21-year-old amelia mignomete christos thermopolis grimaldi , princess of genovia , ( mia thermopolis for short ) who has to have an arranged marriage to become queen . the movie stars anne hathaway , julie andrews , and raven . it was directed by garry marshall .
