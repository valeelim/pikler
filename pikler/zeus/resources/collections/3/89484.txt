Silk tree     silk tree albizia julibrissin is a species of genus `` albizia '' from southeast and east asia , from east iran to china and korea . this genus honoring italian noble filippo del albizzi , who introduce it in europe at middle xviii century , sometimes incorrectly named `` albizzia '' . the specific name `` julibrissin '' is a corruption of farsi `` gul-i abrisham '' ( گل ابریشم ) meaning silktree , by its flowers ( `` gul '' گل is `` flower '' and `` abrisham '' ابریشم means `` silk '' ) .
