Ashland, Alabama    ashland , alabama ashland is an american city in the state of alabama . it is the county seat of clay county . the city has a population of about 2,000 people . ashland is the hometown of alabama 's 52nd governor bob riley . supreme court justice hugo black is from ashland . ashland has an area of 7.2 miles . the city is alabama 's highest elevated county seat at 1130 feet .
