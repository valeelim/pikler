Échallens (district)    échallens ( district ) echallens was a district of the canton of vaud , switzerland . the capital was the town of echallens . as of 2006 it is part of the gros-de-vaud district . it contained the following municipalities : - assens - bercher - bioley-orjulaz - bottens - bretigny-sur-morrens - cugy - dommartin - echallens - eclagnens - essertines-sur-yverdon - etagnières - fey - froideville - goumoens-la-ville - goumoens-le-jux - malapalud - morrens - naz - oulens-sous-echallens - pailly - penthéréaz - poliez-le-grand - poliez-pittet - rueyres - saint-barthélemy - sugnens - villars-le-terroir - villars-tiercelin - vuarrens
