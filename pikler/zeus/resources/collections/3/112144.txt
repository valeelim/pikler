George Hainsworth     george hainsworth ( june 26 , 1895 in toronto , ontario - october 9 , 1950 ) was a canadian professional ice hockey goaltender who played for the montreal canadiens and toronto maple leafs in the national hockey league ( nhl ) . records . - he is the all-time leader in professional shutouts with 104 . - his 94 shutouts are third on the nhl 's all-time list behind martin brodeur 's 96 and terry sawchuk 's 103 . - has the lowest career goals against average tied with alex connell . - holds the single-season shutout record with 22 shutouts , a mark likely never to be touched . other websites . - hainsworth 's statistics and bio at hockeygoalies.org
