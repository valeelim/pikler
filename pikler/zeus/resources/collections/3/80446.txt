S/2006 S 3     s/2006 s 3 is an unnamed moon of saturn . its discovery was announced by scott s. sheppard , david c. jewitt , jan kleyna , and brian g. marsden on june 26 , 2006 , from observations taken between january and april 2006. s/2006 s 3 is about 6 kilometres in diameter , and orbits saturn at an average distance of 21,076,300 km in 1142.366 days , at an inclination of 150.8° to the ecliptic ( 128.8° to saturn 's equator ) , with an eccentricity of 0.4710. references . - institute for astronomy saturn satellite data - iauc 8727 : `` satellites of saturn '' 2006 june 30 ( discovery ) - mpec 2006-m45 : `` eight new satellites of saturn '' 2006 june 26 ( discovery and ephemeris )
