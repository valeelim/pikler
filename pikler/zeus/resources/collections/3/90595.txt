Red Army     the red army was the army of the bolsheviks led by leon trotsky in the russian civil war . later it became the army of the union of soviet socialist republics . it was the name used mainly before and during world war ii . after the war it was renamed as the soviet army , although many people still continued to call it the red army . even nowadays some people still nickname the modern russian army as the red army . some later communist organizations such as the red army faction used the name , and anti-communists sometimes used the name for any left-wing group which they said was trying to promote communism and was paid for by the ussr .
