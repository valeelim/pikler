Columbia Records     columbia records is an american record label that formed in 1888. it is the oldest existing record company in the world . its parent company is now sony bmg . over the years , some of the artists and bands that have been signed to the label include aretha franklin , system of a down , johnny cash , bob dylan , bela fleck , janis joplin , paul mccartney , john lennon , willie nelson , dolly parton , and hundreds of other artists . related pages . - list of columbia records artists
