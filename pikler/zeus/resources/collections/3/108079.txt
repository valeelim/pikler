Sergio Castillo     sergio castillo ( 13 may 1925 – 19 august 2010 in santiago , chile ) was a sculptor . he began as an architect . in 1948 he travelled to paris and took painting courses . after returning to chile he attended the faculty of arts of the university of chile , where he studied with two known teachers , lily garafulic and marta colvin . in 1956 he studied at the art student league of new york . from 1967 he taught at the university of chile . he lived and worked for many years at san lorenzo del escorial , spain . in 1985 he joined the faculty of boston university , in the us . he built several large pieces on the campus of boston university . he won the national award in art ( 1997 ) . “ 50 años creando mis animales en acero ” ( 50 years making my steel animals ) is his recent exposition , including things that look a little like animals . other websites . - some of his works
