Stanley, Falkland Islands    stanley , falkland islands stanley ( also known as port stanley ) is the capital city of the falkland islands . it is found on the island of east falkland and has a population of 2,000. stanley was the sight of fighting in the 1982 falklands war , where argentinian forces captured government house , the seat of the falklands government . today , stanley is the biggest town in the falkland islands . it has an airport and a seaport . cruise ships going through the south atlantic often make a stop in stanley , which has a small tourism industry to serve cruise ship passengers and others visiting the islands .
