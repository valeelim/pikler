Celestus warreni     the giant hispaniolan galliwasp ( celestus warreni ) is a kind of lizard of the anguidae family . it can be found in the dominican republic and haiti . its natural habitat is subtropical or tropical moist lowland forests underneath leaf litter and underneath forest debris . giant galliwasps are opportunistic predators that feed on insects , earthworms , small mammals , and other reptiles . it is threatened by habitat loss .
