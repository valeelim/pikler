François Fillon     francois fillon , ( born 4 march 1954 ) is a former prime minister of france . he was born in le mans . nicolas sarkozy gave him the job on 17 may 2007. he resigned on 15 may 2012 , following the defeat of sarkozy in the presidential elections . a new prime minister was appointed following the inauguration of new president françois hollande . the new prime minister is jean-marc ayrault , the mayor of nantes .
