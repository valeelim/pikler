Sonneberg Rural District     sonneberg is a `` landkreis '' ( rural district ) in the south of thuringia , germany . the district was created in 1868 when districts were created in saxe-meiningen . in 1952 part of the district was incorporated into the newly created district neuhaus am rennweg . this district was dissolved again in 1994 and the district sonneberg did regain its old size . other websites . - official website ( german )
