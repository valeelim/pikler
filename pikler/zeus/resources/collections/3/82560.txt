Glosa     glosa is a term that was often used by spanish musicians in the 16th century to mean : a piece of music which was similar to a set of variations . composers at that time often wrote collections of glosas . the term is related to the word “ gloss ” . they were comparing it to gloss ( ornamentation ) that a poet might put on his poem . they were often on religious themes . antonio de cabezón was an important composer of glosas . glosa was also used by spanish musicians to mean : musical ornamentation . this is the meaning in the title of a famous book of music for viola da gamba and harpsichord by diego ortiz called `` trattado de glosas '' ( 1553 ) .
