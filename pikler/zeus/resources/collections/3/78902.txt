Leuk     leuk ( french loèche ) is a city and municipality of switzerland and is the capital city of the district of leuk in the canton of valais in switzerland . since it controls access to the gemmipass , it had some importance from the time of roman raetia . the leukerbad hot baths are just north of leuk , towards the pass . loèche houses the installations of onyx , the swiss interception system for electronic intelligence gathering . on 1 january 2013 the former municipalities of erschmatt merged into the municipality of leuk .
