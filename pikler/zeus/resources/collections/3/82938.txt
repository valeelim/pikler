Magglio Ordóñez     magglio jose `` maggs '' ordóñez ( b. january 28 , 1974 ) is a former major league baseball player from venezuela . he was a right fielder and right-handed batter . ordóñez was born in coro , venezuela . he played for the chicago white sox from 1997 to 2004 and the detroit tigers from 2005 to 2011. after his baseball career , ordóñez became a mayor in venezuela . other websites . - career statistics : - major league baseball - espn - baseball-reference - the baseball cube
