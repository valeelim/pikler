Temple in Jerusalem     the temple in jerusalem or holy temple ( , transliterated `` bet hamikdash '' , which means literally `` the holy house '' ) stood on the temple mount `` ( har habayit ) '' in the old city of jerusalem . according to classical jewish belief , it was the `` footstool '' of god 's presence in the physical world . it was the place where jews brought all the offerings described in the torah . the first temple , also called solomon 's temple , was built by king solomon in seven years during the 10th century bce and has been dated astronomically to 957 bce . it was the center of ancient judaism . this first temple was destroyed by the babylonians in 586 bce . a new temple was built from 535 bce to 516 bce . that was destroyed by the romans in 70 ce . all of the outer walls still stand , although the temple itself has long since been destroyed . the islamic dome of the rock has stood on the site of the temple since the late 7th century ce . the al-aqsa mosque , from roughly the same period , also stands on the temple courtyard .
