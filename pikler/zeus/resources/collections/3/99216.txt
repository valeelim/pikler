Bhakkar     bhakkar ( , seraiki : بھکر ) is the capital city of bhakkar district , punjab , pakistan . it is located at 31°37'60n 71°4'0e at an elevation of 159 metres and lies on the left bank of the indus river . it has a population of almost 300,000 and was given status of district in 1981. it is also the capital of the tehsil of the same name ( bhakkar tehsil ) .
