Multan District     multan district ( ) is a district in the punjab province of pakistan . according to the 1998 census of pakistan it had a population of 3.117 million ( 1.315 million or 42.2 % in urban areas ) . its capital is the city of multan . tehsils of multan . - mumtazabad tehsil - sher shah tehsil - sujaabad tehsil - jalapur pirwala tehsil - bosan tehsil - shah rukn-e-alam tehsil
