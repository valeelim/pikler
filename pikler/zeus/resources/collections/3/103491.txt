M-Tram     m > tram was a tram operator in melbourne , victoria . they got a 12 year contract on 29 august , 1999 to run the swanston trams franchise , which was later renamed to m > train . m > tram stopped running trams in april 2004. yarra trams runs all tram services in melbourne now . m > tram 's slogan was moving melbourne . other pages . - m > train
