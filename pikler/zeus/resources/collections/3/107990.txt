Sanctification     the word sanctification refers to the act of making holy or setting apart as special . the word is used five times in the authorized version of the new testament ( 1 corinthians 1:30 , 1 thessalonians 4:3,1 thessalonians 4:4 , 2 thessalonians 2:13 , and 1 peter 1:2 ) . to sanctify means “ to set apart for special use or purpose , ” and “ to make holy or sacred , ” and etymologically from the latin verb 'sanctificare ' which in turn is from `` sanctus '' “ holy ” and `` facere '' “ to make . ”
