Morges (district)    morges ( district ) morges is a district of the canton of vaud in switzerland . the capital is the city of morges . the population ( as of december 2015 ) is 80,095. it contains the following municipalities : the area of the district is 372.99 square kilometers ( 144.01 sq mi ) . of this area , 205.69 km ( 79.42 sq mi ) or 55.1 % is used for agricultural purposes , while 126.4 km ( 48.8 sq mi ) or 33.9 % is forested.the remaining area , 38.25 km ( 14.77 sq mi ) or 10.3 % is settled ( buildings or roads ) and 2.66 km ( 1.03 sq mi ) or 0.7 % is unproductive land .
