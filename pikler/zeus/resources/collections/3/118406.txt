Sarah Chalke     sarah louise christine chalke ( born august 27 , 1976 ) is a canadian-american actress most famous for her role as `` elliot reid '' on `` scrubs '' and `` becky conner '' on `` roseanne '' and in the cbs comedy `` how i met your mother '' . she is engaged to lawyer jamie afifi , with whom she has a son , charlie rhodes afifi , born december 24 , 2009 .
