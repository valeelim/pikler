Setebos (moon)    setebos ( moon ) setebos is one of the farthest retrograde non-spherical moons of uranus . it was found on 1999-07-18 by john j. kavelaars `` et al . '' and designated s/1999 u 1. confirmed as uranus xix it is named after the god worshipped by caliban and sycorax in william shakespeare 's play `` the tempest '' . the orbital parameters suggest that it may belong , together with sycorax and prospero to the same dynamic cluster , suggesting common origin . however , this suggestion does not appear to be supported by the observed colours . the moon appears grey in the visible light , similar to prospero but different from sycorax ( which is `` light red '' ) . related pages . - list of uranus ' moons references . 1 . < li value= '' 5 '' > ephemeris ( iua ) other websites . - setebos profile by nasa 's solar system exploration - david jewiit pages - scott sheppard pages
