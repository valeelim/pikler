Death Angel     death angel is a thrash metal band from concord , california . they played from 1982 to 1991. in 2001 they started to play again . all of their members are from the philippines . the members are : mark osegueda ( vocals ) rob cavestany ( guitar , vocals ) , ted aguilar ( guitar ) , andy galeon ( drums ) , dennis pepa ( bass , vocals ) discography . - `` kill as one '' ( 1986 ) ( demo ) - `` the ultra-violence '' ( 1987 ) - `` frolic through the park '' ( 1988 ) - `` act iii '' ( 1990 ) - `` fall from grace '' ( 1990 ) - `` the art of dying '' ( 2004 ) - `` killing season '' ( 2008 )
