Mangrio     mangrio ( sindhi مݣڕیو , ) is the sindhi muslim rajput tribe found in sindh in pakistan and rajasthan in india . mangrio are chandarvanshi rajput basically they are royal family of jaisalmer india . in sindh they are found mainly in shikarpur hyderabad tharparkar sanghar umerkot mirpur khas sukkur larkana raipur and khairpur districts of province and in punjab they are mainly found in rahimyar bahawalpur and bahawalnagar they are also live in balochistan and in rajasthan , they are found mainly in jaisalmer and barmer and bikaner disricts in india .
