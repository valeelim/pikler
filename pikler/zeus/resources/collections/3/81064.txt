Vacallo     vacallo is a municipality of the district of mendrisio in the canton of ticino in switzerland . the current population is almost 3,000 people . the coat of arms of vacallo is a v with three bunches of grapes . each bunch of grapes symbolises the three parishes or 'frazione ' of the municipality . other websites . - official website of the municipality of vacallo - page with some pictures - primary school - sav basket team
