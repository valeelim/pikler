Jack Straw     john khoo cheng straw ( born 3 august 1946 in buckhurst hill , essex , england ) is a british politician . he held several cabinet posts in the labour governments of tony blair and gordon brown . he is the member of parliament ( mp ) for the constituency of blackburn . jack straw became a labour party mp at the 1979 general election for the constituency of blackburn . he was promoted to the shadow cabinet in 1987 and became the home secretary when labour returned to power in 1997. his other cabinet posts included : foreign secretary ( 2001-2006 ) , leader of the house of commons ( 2006-2007 ) and justice secretary ( 2007-2010 ) . he did not choose to become a member of ed miliband 's shadow cabinet . straw 's first marriage , in 1968 , to teacher anthea weston ended in divorce in 1977. they had a daughter , rachel , born on 24 february 1976. she died after five days because of a heart defect . on 10 november 1978 he married alice perkins , a senior civil servant . they have two adult children , william and charlotte .
