Srinagar District     srinagar ( ) is at the center of the valley of kashmir . it is the most populous district in the disputed territory of jammu and kashmir , india and is home to the summer capital ( srinagar ) of jammu and kashmir ( the capital moves to jammu city in the winter ) . the largest city is srinagar . it is also home to the scenic dal lake , a popular tourist destination . srinagar district is made up of two administrative tehsils : north srinagar and south srinagar .
