Saalfeld-Rudolstadt Rural District     saalfeld-rudolstadt is a `` landkreis '' ( district ) in the south of thuringia , germany . the district dates back to the `` landratsamt '' saalfeld , which was created in 1868 , and in 1922 it was converted into the `` landkreis '' . also in 1922 the district rudolstadt was created . both districts were merged in the local government changes of 1994. other websites . - official website ( german )
