Department store     a department store is a store that sells many different products . it does not sell the products of only one company , as some other stores do . in a department store a person can usually buy clothing , furniture , electronics , and sometimes paint , hardware , toiletries , cosmetics , photographic equipment , jewellery , toys , and sporting goods . depertment stores are often upmarket places . some department stores sell products cheaper than other stores . they are then called discount department stores .
