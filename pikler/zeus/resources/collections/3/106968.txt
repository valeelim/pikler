Youth for Christ     youth for christ ( yfc ) is an international christian ministry that promotes youth evangelism and biblical christianity . in the early 1940s , during world war ii , many young men , mostly ministers and evangelists , were holding large rallies in canada , the united kingdom and the united states . in an effort to coordinate these programs youth for christ was started in 1944. the organization was co-founded by the rev . billy graham and then-evangelist charles templeton .
