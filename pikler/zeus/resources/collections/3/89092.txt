A-type asteroid     a-type asteroids are fairly uncommon closer asteroids that have a strong , broad 1 µm olivine feature and a very reddish spectrum shortwards of 0.7 µm . they are thought to come from the completely differentiated mantle of an asteroid . a-type asteroids are so rare that as of 2005 only 17 had been found . other websites . - a technical report on a-type asteroids
