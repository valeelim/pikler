The Ultimate Fighter     the ultimate fighter is a reality television series and mixed martial arts ( mma ) contest , in the united states , and made by spike tv and the ultimate fighting championship ( ufc ) . on this show , professional mma fighters that have yet to make a big name for themselves are placed in a house outside of las vegas , nevada and they go against each other for the title of the ultimate fighter , winning a six-figure , multi-fight contract with the ufc . other websites . - official website - spike tv 's ultimate fighter page - ufc official website
