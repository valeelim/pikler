Ypres     ypres ( ) is a municipality in the belgian province of west flanders . the municipality is made up of the city of ypres and the villages of boezinge , brielen , dikkebus , elverdinge , hollebeke , sint-jan , vlamertinge , voormezele , zillebeke , and zuidschote . in 2007 , 34919 people lived in the municipality . it is at 50° 51 north , 02° 53 east . poison gas was first used here , in the second battle of ypres in 1917 .
