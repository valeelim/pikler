Leuven     leuven is a municipality in the belgian province of flemish brabant . in 2007 , 91,942 people lived there . it is at 50° 52 north , 04° 42 east . leuven hosts belgium 's biggest university - katholieke universiteit leuven so , leuven is very young populated city , and there were more than 45000 students in leuven in 2012. leuven also has a long history . the town hall in the city centre dates from the middle ages .
