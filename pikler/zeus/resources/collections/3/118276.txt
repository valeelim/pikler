Trousers     trousers ( or pants in canada , south africa and the united states ) , are a kind of clothing . they are worn on the lower part of the body , to cover both legs apart ( instead of cloth stretching across both as in skirts and dresses ) . historically , in the west , trousers have been the lower-body clothing item for men since the 16th century ; by the late 20th century , they were used sometimes by women as well . trousers are worn at the hips or waist , and may be held up by their own by a belt , or braces ( suspenders ) . related pages . - breeches - jeans
