Kobukuro     kobukuro is a japanese male pop band . the name is a mix of the two members ' family names , kentaro kobuchi and shunsuke kuroda . in 1998 , the two met each other in sakai and formed kobukuro . kobukuro received moderately successful ratings for their first three indie albums - ' ( july 1999 ) , `` root of my mind '' ( march 2001 ) , and 'answer '' '' ( december 2000 ) . kobukuro signed with warner music japan in 2001 , and made their debut with the hit single , `` yell '' which reached # 4 on the oricon charts . their newest album is called `` 5296 '' ( december 2007 ) . kobukuro 's new single is “ stay ” .
