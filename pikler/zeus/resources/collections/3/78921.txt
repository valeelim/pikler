Aspartame     aspartame ( also known as e951 or nutrasweet ) is an artificial sweetener . it was discovered in 1965 by james m. schlatter . it can be used to replace some kinds of sugar . it is not based on sugars . it is not like saccharin . problems . - people with phenylketonuria can not take aspartame - aspartame may cause problems with the skin , such as rashes , or dermatitis - it may cause headache or migraine .
