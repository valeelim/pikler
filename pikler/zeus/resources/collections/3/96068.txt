Bunker     a bunker is a military building for defense . they are usually built below ground . blockhouses are like bunkers , but are built above ground . bunkers were of big importance during world war i and world war ii . bunkers were built during the cold war for important political people and for the general public because of the scare of a nuclear war . there are many different types of bunkers : a trench is a small building made of concrete that is dug into the ground partway . these are usually parts of a big system of trenches . these kinds of bunkers give people fighting better protection than an open trench . they also give top protection against air attack ( grenades , mortar fire and shells ) . they also provide shelter against the weather . a pillbox is a small building made of concrete that also has small windows that people can shoot guns out of . the name came from the fact that they look like the boxes that pills came in . pillboxes were used a lot during world war i when defence in depth was being used . pillboxes are most often found camouflaged so that they are harder to spot . they can also be part of a trench system , where the pillbox is a firing step that has been built to take grenade blasts and smaller mortar fire . pillboxes were also built to help protect strategic structures such as bridges or jetties .
