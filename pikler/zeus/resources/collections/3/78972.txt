Abelsonite     abelsonite ( nickel porphyrin ) is a mineral that can be pinkish-brown , purple , or reddish-brown in colour and is formed from porphyrin crystals . it is platy ( made in plates like mica ) and fragile . porphyrins are organic ring compounds like haeme , the pigment in red blood cells . it is found in the green river formation of utah , in the united states .
