Wildhaus     wildhaus is a village and municipality near st. gallen in the canton of st. gallen in switzerland . about 1,200 people live there . huldrych zwingli was born there in 1484. with `` 9658 '' , wildhaus has the highest zip code in switzerland . on 1 january 2010 the former municipalities of wildhaus and alt st. johann merged into the new municipality of wildhaus-alt st. johann . other websites . - wildhaus online
