Wartburgkreis Rural District     wartburgkreis is a `` landkreis '' ( rural district ) in the west of thuringia , germany . the city of eisenach is nearly completely surrounded by wartburgbreis . the district was set up in 1994 by joining the old districts of eisenach and bad salzungen , and a few municipalities from the district bad langensalza . the city of eisenach left the district in 1998 and became a district-free city . the district is named after the wartburg , a castle near eisenach most famous as martin luther 's refuge in 1521. other websites . - official website ( german )
