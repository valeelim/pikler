Kalasha     kalasha , also spelled as kalash ( sanskrit : , `` kala≈õa '' ; literally `` pitcher , pot '' ) , is a metal ( brass , copper , silver or gold ) pot with a large base and small mouth , large enough to hold a coconut . sometimes `` kalasha '' can mean a pot filled with water and topped with a coronet of mango leaves and a coconut . this combination is often used in hindu rites .
