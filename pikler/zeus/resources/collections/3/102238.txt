Book of Leviticus     leviticus is the third book of both the bible , and the torah , known as wayiqra ' in transliterated hebrew , meaning 'and he will declare . ' it was written by the old testament patriarch moses . leviticus was written to the hebrew people of israel . the book has several main topics , some of which are the jewish laws of sacrificing to god , and the work of the priests . this book probably was written around 1300 b.c . other websites . - leviticus ( kjv ) - leviticus ( niv )
