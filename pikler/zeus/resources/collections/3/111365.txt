Mario Kart: Super Circuit    mario kart : super circuit mario kart : super circuit is a racing game for the game boy advance released in 2001 and later in 2011 for the virtual console on the nintendo 3ds . it has characters from the mario series of video games . the game contains all the courses from `` super mario kart '' along with newcoming courses . it can be played single player ( modes : grand prix , extra [ super mario kart ] , quick run , and time trials ) multiplayer ( modes : grand prix , `` link it up ! `` , v.s. , and battle )
