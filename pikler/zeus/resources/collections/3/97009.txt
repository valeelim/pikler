Alina Kabayeva     alina kabaeva ( , born may 12 , 1983 , tashkent , uzbek ssr ) is a russian former-rhythmic gymnast . she started competing since she was 12 , and stopped when she was 24. she won several medals at world championships , two olympic medals ( one bronze , one gold ) and she came up with some of the flexibility-required moves in the sport . she was trained by irina viner . now she works for state duma .
