Trichotillomania     trichotillomania ( ttm ) ( often called `` trich '' ) is an impulse control disorder . it causes a person to habitually pull out their hair . people with trichotillomania may pull hair from anywhere on the body . sometimes people with trich end up having large areas on the body without hair . trichotillomania is listed in the diagnostic and statistical manual of mental disorders ( dsm ) . the dsm is written by the american psychiatric association . it lists every condition which is officially called a mental illness . the dsm puts trich in the `` impulse control disorder '' category . however , there are questions about what category trich should be in . at different times , it may seem to be a habit , an addiction , a tic disorder , or an obsessive-compulsive disorder .
