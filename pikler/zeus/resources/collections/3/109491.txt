Moldoveanu Peak     moldoveanu peak ( romanian : `` vârful moldoveanu '' ) is the highest mountain in romania . it is in the făgăraş mountains of the southern carpathians . the most-used paths to reach moldoveanu are over the vistea mare peak ( 2527m ) , by paths coming from podragu , sambata , or by the vistea valley . the closest locality is victoria , on the north side . on the south side it is accessible from northwest of câmpulung .
