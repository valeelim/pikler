Divertimento     a divertimento is a piece of music which is lighthearted and easy to listen to . it is often performed on social occasions , e.g . as music to be played while people eat , or as after-dinner music . the divertimento became popular in the 18th century . they were played by a small group of instruments . mozart wrote several pieces called `` divertimento '' . they are similar to a serenade . a divertimento often has several movements , e.g . six , seven or more . there are a few examples from the 20th century of pieces called `` divertimento '' , e.g . works by including works by ferruccio busoni , béla bartók , and igor stravinsky ( from his ballet `` le baiser de la fée '' ) .
