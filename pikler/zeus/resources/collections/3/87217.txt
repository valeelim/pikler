Mudra     in indian religions , a mudra ( sanskrit , literally `` seal '' ) is a gesture made with the hand or fingers . along with `` asanas '' ( body postures ) , they are used in yoga . each mudra has a meaning . they are a central part of hindu iconography . mudras are also used in buddhism . there are hand gestures in both hindu and buddhist iconography . an example would be the outward-facing open palm known as `` abhay '' ( without fear ) mudra , a gesture meant to dispel fear . references . - originally published 1985 , routledge & kegan paul plc , london . other websites . - mudras photo gallery - mudras in buddhism
