Octane     octane is an alkane ( a hydrocarbon ) . it is commonly used in fuel . it is the most important part of gasoline , because it is the part which produces most of the energy that comes from gasoline when it is burned . a `` high octane '' fuel will be more productive than a `` low octane '' fuel . the iupac name for octane is `` 2,2,4-trimethylpentane '' .
