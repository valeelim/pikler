USA Today     usa today is a daily newspaper founded by al neuharth . it has the most readers of any daily newspaper in the united states . about 2.25 million copies are sold on a weekday . it comes second worldwide . `` the times of india '' sells about 2.6 million copies on a weekday . the paper was founded in 1982. it has a much more colorful markup than other national newspapers , like `` the new york times '' or `` wall street journal '' . its use of graphics makes its content rather like a tabloid . it is a middle-ground , middle-class paper . the paper became known for taking national polls before elections . it is connected , through its owners gannet company , to the tv show `` usa today '' . it also has a significant news website .
