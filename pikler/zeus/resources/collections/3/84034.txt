Cape Hatteras     cape hatteras is a cape on the coast of north carolina . it is the point that protrudes the farthest to the southeast along the northeast-to-southwest line of the atlantic coast of north america , making it a key point for navigation along the eastern seaboard . so many ships have been lost around it that the area is known as the `` graveyard of the atlantic '' . the nearby shoals are known as diamond shoals . the cape hatteras lighthouse is a well known feature on cape hatteras . it was built in 1870 and it is the tallest brick lighthouse in the united states . other websites . - national park service : cape hatteras national seashore - graveyard of the atlantic museum
