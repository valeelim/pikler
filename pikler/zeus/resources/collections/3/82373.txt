Home Made Kazoku     home made kazoku is a japanese hip-hop band , formed in 1996. the members of the band are micro , kuro and founding member , dj u-ichi . they were very famous , and some of their famous songs include `` thank you '' bleach 's 2nd ending theme `` nagareboshi shooting star '' 1st naruto shippuden ending theme and `` no rain no rainbow '' .
