Province of Terni     the province of terni ( ) is a province in the umbria region of central italy . its capital is the city of terni . the province of terni was created in 1927 when the old `` province of the umbria '' , created in 1860 , was divided in two provinces , the perugia and terni provinces . the province of terni has an area of , one-third of both the area and population of the umbria region . the province is bordered to the north with the province of perugia , to the east , south and west with the lazio ( the rieti and viterbo provinces ) and to north-west with the tuscany ( province of siena ) . the province is in the southwest of the umbria region , in the last part of the nera river valley near the confluence with the velino river , and the eastern part of the valley of the tiber river . the highest mountain in the province is monte aspra ( ) , in the eastern part of the province , with an altitude of . there are two fluvial parks , to protect two rivers and their areas around them , in the province of terni : `` parco fluviale del tevere '' ( `` fluvial park of the tiber '' ) and `` parco fluviale del nera '' ( `` fluvial park of the nera '' ) .
