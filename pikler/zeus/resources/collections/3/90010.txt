BSA Bantam     the bsa bantam is a small two-stroke motorcycle that was made by the birmingham small arms company from 1949 to 1972. the bike originally came from a german design , the dkw rt 125 , from before world war ii . there were more than 8 different versions of the bike , from the d1 to the b175 , these bikes has engines that range from 125cc to 175cc
