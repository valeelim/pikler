Eulalia!    eulalia ! eulalia ! is a children 's fantasy book . it 's the nineteenth book in the `` redwall '' novel series , and was written by brian jacques in 2007. vizka longtooth , a wicked fox pirate , captures a young badger named gorath . at the same time , the old badger king of the mountain salamandastron tells a young female hare named maudie to go and find a new badger to be king after he dies . at redwall abbey , a young hedgehog named orkwil prink gets kicked out of the abbey because he keeps stealing stuff . what will happen to these three animals and the wicked fox pirate ?
