Cologne Cathedral     cologne cathedral ( officially : '' hohe domkirche st. petrus '' ) is a roman catholic cathedral , in cologne , germany . the cathedral has a height of 157.25 metres . this makes it the second-highest in germany and the third-highest in the world . the cathedral in ulm , the ulm minster at 161 m is the highest in the world . the basilica of our lady of peace of yamoussoukro is also higher , at 158 metres , but it only has the status of a minor basilica , it is not a cathedral . the cathedral is also the third-biggest church , built in gothic architecture . the seville cathedral in seville , spain , and the milan cathedral in milan , italy are bigger . the cologne cathedral was built in gothic style from 1248. it was planned to make the cathedral like the one in amiens . the choir was consecrated in 1322. the first bell could be hung in one of the towers shortly after 1410. around 1510 building was stopped , because people had lost interest . the last payment for its construction was made in 1560 .
