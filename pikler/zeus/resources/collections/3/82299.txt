Mobile Bay     mobile bay is an inlet of the gulf of mexico , lying within the state of alabama in the united states . its mouth is formed by the fort morgan peninsula on the eastern side and dauphin island , a barrier island on the western side . the mobile river and tensaw river empty into the northern end of the bay , making it an estuary . dog river , deer river , and east fowl river empty into the western side of the bay , and fish river is across the bay on the eastern shore .
