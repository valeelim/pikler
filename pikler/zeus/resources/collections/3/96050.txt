University of Hawaii     the university of hawaii is a group of public colleges and universities in hawaii . there are three universities , seven community colleges , an employment training center , four education centers and some research laboratories : - universities - university of hawaii at hilo , doctoral university in hilo - university of hawaii at manoa , doctoral university in honolulu - university of hawaii-west oahu , baccalaureate university in kapolei - community colleges - hawaii community college , hilo - honolulu community college , honolulu - kapiolani community college , honolulu - kauai community college , līhu'e - leeward community college , pearl city - windward community college , kāne'ohe - university of hawaii maui college , kahului
