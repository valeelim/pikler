Bass clarinet     the bass clarinet is a type of clarinet , and is a woodwind single-reed instrument . the earliest record of a bass clarinet is called a 'bass tube ' invented in paris in 1772 by g. lott . it usually has a b-flat pitch . it plays an octave lower than the normal clarinet . this means that when a bass clarinet makes a note , the note that actually sounds is an octave and a whole tone lower . there are some bass clarinets that have an a or c pitch as well as e flat , but there are not very many of these .
