Sola scriptura     sola scriptura ( latin for `` by scripture alone '' ) means `` the bible only '' . protestants believe that they should only read the bible to find out what god wants . they do this instead of listening to the pope or the roman catholic church . `` sola scriptura '' was a principle of the protestant reformation of the reformer martin luther and is a principle of protestants today ( see five solas ) . other pages . - ijtihad , the islamic concept of interpretation of religion and law not limited by tradition - `` sola fide '' other websites . - many articles on `` sola scriptura '' from a protestant perspective - many articles on `` sola scriptura '' from a catholic perspective - `` a critique of the evangelical doctrine of sola scriptura '' , an excerpt from `` the shape of sola scriptura '' ( 2001 ) by keith mathison ( himself a calvinistic evangelical ) - a written debate on `` sola scriptura '' between douglas jones and gerald matatics from `` antithesis magazine '' - a formal written debate on `` sola scriptura '' between julie staples and apolonio latar
