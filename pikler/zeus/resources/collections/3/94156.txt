The Long Patrol     the long patrol is a children 's fantasy book written by brian jacques in 1997. it is the tenth book in the redwall series . `` the long patrol '' has been translated into four different languages including finnish , french , italian , and swedish . a young hare named tammo wants to join the long patrol , the army of fighting hares that serves the ruler of the mountain salamandastron . but meanwhile damug warfang the rat and his evil army of cruel rapscallions are coming .
