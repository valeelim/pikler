Oberon (moon)    oberon ( moon ) oberon is the farthest big moon of the planet uranus . it is made of about half ice and half rock . with a mean radius of about 760 km , oberon is the second biggest moon of uranus 's 27 moons . it orbits uranus about every 13.4 days . it was found on january 11 , 1787 by william herschel , in the same year he found titania . it was named after a oberon , king of the fairies , a character in william shakespeare 's play a midsummer night 's dream . oberon has several large impact craters where it has been hit by meteorites . these were photographed by the voyager 2 spacecraft in 1986 as it flew passed . scientists also believe there is a mountain that could be high on the moon . craters on oberon are named after characters in the plays of william shakespeare . other websites . - information and photos of oberon - usgs : oberon nomenclature
