Hamilton Academical F.C.    hamilton academical f.c . hamilton academical is a football team from hamilton , lanarkshire in scotland . they were first set up in 1874 from hamilton academy . more commonly known as `` hamilton accies '' the team won promotion from sfl division 2 to sfl division 1 in the 2003-04 season . this was followed up by promotion to the scottish premier league in the 2007-08 season . the club have played at new douglas park from 2001 , having previously played at douglas park .
