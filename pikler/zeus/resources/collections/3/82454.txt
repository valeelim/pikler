1986 Formula One season     the 1986 formula one season was the 37th of the championship . the champion was alain prost in a close battle . it started on march 23 , and ended on october 26 with sixteen races . this was the first year that turbocharged were required . formula one had baned naturally aspirated ( atmospheric ) engines . until the planed engine change in 2014 , this was the only year turbos were required . the ban on atmospheric engines was lifted in 1987. in 1989 , all forced induction engines ( turbochargers or supercharge ) were banned . drivers standings . - pole position in bold . - fastest lap in `` italics '' . - drivers did not finish the grand prix , but were classified as they completed over 90 % of the race distance . - numbers without parentheses are championship points . numbers in parentheses are total points scored from the best 11 finishes . championship standings . - pole position in bold . - fastest lap in `` italics '' . - car did not finish the grand prix . car was classified ( ranked ) because it finished over 90 % of the race distance .
