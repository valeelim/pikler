Osroene     osroene ( also spelled osrohene , osrhoene , from the greek ; ' ) , also known by the name of its capital city , edessa ( modern şanlıurfa , turkey ) , was a historic kingdom located on the present-day border of syria and turkey . the kingdom was assyrian and enjoyed semi-autonomy to complete independence from the years of 132 bce to 244 ad . it was a syriac speaking kingdom , and according to an ancient legend king abgar v of edessa was converted to christianity by thaddeus of edessa , or saint addai , one of the seventy-two disciples . by 201 ad or earlier , under king abgar the great , osroene became the first christian state .
