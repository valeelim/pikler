Otogizﾅ行hi     otogizﾅ行hi are a group of short stories with pictures , written in japan , mostly in the period between 1392 and 1573. there are about 350 of these stories . they are about many different things : love , religion , fighting , humor , and other things . the `` otogizﾅ行hi '' were often read to an audience . other websites . - online edition of the `` otogizﾅ行hi '' at kyoto university
