Havelland     havelland is a district in brandenburg , germany . the district was established in 1993 by joining the old districts of nauen and rathenow . havelland is the name for the regions on the banks of the havel river between the city of berlin and the elbe river . this region also includes the cities of brandenburg and potsdam , that are not part of the district . the havelland district mainly consists of the areas north of the havel river . other websites . - official website - touristical website
