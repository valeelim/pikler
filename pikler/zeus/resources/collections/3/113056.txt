Amphisbaenia     amphisbaenia are a suborder of squamata . amphisbaenians have features that link them to both lizards and snakes , but they have enough of their own features to be a separate suborder . amphisbaenians can live both above and below the ground . there are four families , made up of 18 genera , and about 160 species . famililes . - amphisbaenidae - bipedidae - rhineuridae - trogonphidae
