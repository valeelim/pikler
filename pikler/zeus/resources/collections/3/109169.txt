Alvin Journeyman     alvin journeyman ( 1995 ) is an alternate history/fantasy book that was written by orson scott card . it is the fourth book in card 's `` the tales of alvin maker '' series and is about alvin miller , the seventh son of a seventh son . being a seventh son of a seventh son gives him magical powers . other websites . - about the novel `` alvin journeyman '' from card 's website
