2004–05 NHL season     the 2004–05 nhl season would have been the 88th regular season of the national hockey league ( nhl ) . the season was canceled on february 16 , 2005 because of an unfinished lockout that began on september 16 , 2004. the loss of the 2004–05 season made the nhl the first north american professional sports league to cancel an entire season because of a labour issue . it was also the first time since 1919 that the stanley cup was not awarded . in 1919 , the championship was canceled due to the ongoing spanish flu pandemic .
