Aventura, Florida    aventura , florida aventura is a city found in the northeastern part of miami-dade county , florida . the city name is from the spanish word for `` adventure , '' and was named `` aventura '' because that was the name of the original group of condominium developments in the area . the name predates the well known mall ( aventura mall ) built near the condominium developments . according to the u.s census estimates of 2006 , the city had a population of 30,027 .
