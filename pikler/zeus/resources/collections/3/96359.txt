Daytona 500     the daytona 500 is an auto race held in daytona beach , florida . it is the opening event of the nascar sprint cup series . daytona has hosted racing for many years . originally cars raced on the beach , but in 1959 , race officials built the daytona international speedway , where the race has been held ever since . the daytona track hosts many other races as well . the daytona 500 is considered the most important event in nascar .
