Toad     a toad is a type of amphibian . toads look like frogs , and they belong to the same animal group . all toads are frogs , but not all frogs are toads . toads mainly evolved in dry climates , so many toads have rougher , browner skin . many toads also burrow , though that depends on the environment that the toad evolved in . toads lay eggs in strings attached to rocks , unlike true frogs which lay eggs in a jelly-like mass . also , toads generally have shorter legs and fatter bodies . toads have paratoid glands behind their eyes which are used to make poison . the poison is not harmful to humans .
