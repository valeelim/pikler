Ella Koon     ella koon , real name ianna koon , is a chinese singer , actress and model . she was born in tahiti , french polynesia , and was raised in hong kong , where she is currently based . she speaks cantonese , english , french and mandarin languages . after finishing high school in birmingham , england , she began her career as a model in 2000. she debuted into the film industry in the romance movie `` i do '' . in 2004 , she started her singing career and released her debut original ( album ) . in early 2005 , she participated in the tvb show `` hotel situation '' . her second album `` ellacadabra '' was released in late 2005. in mid-2005 , koon starred in the tvb 's series `` revolving doors of vengeance ( 酒店風雲 ) '' , making her a well-known actress as well . she also starred in `` survivor 's law ii '' in 2007. discography . - original ( 2004 ) - ellacadabra ( 2005 ) - lose sanity : 失常 ( 2006 ) - stages ( 2008 ) - being ( 2010 ) - take a shine to ...
