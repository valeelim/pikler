Royal Library of Turin     the royal library of turin is an important museum in turin , italy . it was started by king charles albert of sardinia in 1839. now it has 187,987 print volumes , 4,403 hand-written volumes ( manuscripts ) , 3,000 drawings , 188 books made before 1501 ( incunabula ) , 5,029 books from the sixteenth century , 20,987 pamphlets , and many engravings and maps . these works come mainly from collections from savoy . the most important antique is a self-portrait of leonardo da vinci . this self-portrait is in an underground vault that is resistant to fires , earthquakes , and even airplane crashes .
