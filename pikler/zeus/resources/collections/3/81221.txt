Spice (album)    spice ( album ) spice was the first album by the spice girls . it was released in the united kingdom in 1996 , and in the united states in 1997. it was the most successful album by a girl group ever . it sold 23 million copies around the world . there were five songs that were very successful from this album . the most successful was `` wannabe '' , which went to no . 1 on many music charts around the world .
