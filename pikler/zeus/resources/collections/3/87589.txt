Carluke     the town of carluke is situated in the centre of the scottish countryside in south lanarkshire , scotland , 5.4 miles north-west of lanark and 5 miles south-east of wishaw . many people in carluke are commuters , with a variety of small stores and supermarkets available at the centre of the town . three people who were born in the town have received the victoria cross . the inventor of `` slush puppies '' previously stayed on a street in carluke . the famous ex-motherwell striker dougie arnott currently lives in carluke .
