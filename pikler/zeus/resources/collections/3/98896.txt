Scooter     a scooter is a bicycle or tricycle with very small wheels . some scooters have a motor , some have pedals , and some are pushed with the foot . scooters with motors are called `` motor scooters '' . there are many different kinds of scooters . they include motor scooters and kick scooters . some scooters have four wheels . people who do n't have much balance can drive that kind . in some places , riders of motor scooters need a driver 's license for driving a motorcycle . in other places , they only need a licence for driving a moped . some scooters can hold two people .
