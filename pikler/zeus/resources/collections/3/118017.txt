Michael Weatherly     michael manning weatherly , jr. ( born july 8 , 1968 in new york city ) is an american actor . he is best known for his role as special agent anthony dinozzo on the television series `` ncis '' . after being in over 300 episodes he is leaving ncis at the end of the 2016 season . he also plays dr. jason bull in the cbs drama series `` bull '' . < br >
