Stal Mielec     stal mielec ( ) is a polish football club from mielec . they play in polish fourth league . the club was started april 10 , 1939. their home stadium is stadion stali mielec . their colours are white and blue . stal mielec has won `` i liga '' ( polish first league ) 2 times ( in 1973 and 1976 ) and played in quarterfinals of uefa cup in 1976. they were one of the best teams of poland in 1970s . famous players are grzegorz lato , henryk kasperczak , andrzej szarmach , jan domarski , all of them played in 1970s . other websites . - stalmielec.com - official website
