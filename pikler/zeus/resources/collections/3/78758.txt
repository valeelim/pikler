Armenian National Committee of America     the armenian national committee of america ( anca ) is the largest and most influential armenian american advocacy organization in the united states . working in coordination with a network of offices , chapters , and supporters throughout the country and affiliated organizations around the world , the anca advances the concerns of the armenian american community on a broad range of issues . beyond the central offices of anca are in washington dc , boston and los angeles , anca has more than fifty local chapters and thousands of activists , it is cooperating with a large web of regional armenian national committees ( or `` armenian cause/hay tad '' offices ) in armenia , russia , france , the middle east , canada and australia . the executive director of anca is aram hamparian . other websites . - armenian national committee of america website . - anc of central california official site - an interview with anca executive director aram hamparian
