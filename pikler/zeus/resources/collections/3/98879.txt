Lee Chaolan     lee chaolan is a video game character in `` tekken '' . the first time he was in tekken was the first tekken game . lee chaolan was an orphan before the first game started , but was adopted as a child by heihachi mishima . kazuya mishima , heihachi 's son , was lee 's arch nemesis since they were children . kazuya always won against lee . lee is now in a world of hate because of this experience . he holds a constant grudge against kazuya . lee chaolan 's fighting style uses mostly feet . using his long legs and quick feet , he can take down any foe that comes his way . kazuya uses a balanced force of fist and foot to take down his foes . balance is kazuya 's key to victory over his rival lee . he turned 48 in tekken 5 but the creators made him look younger .
