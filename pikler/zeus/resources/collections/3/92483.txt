Fátima, Portugal    fátima , portugal fátima ( ) is a city in portugal . it is famous for the religious visions that took place there in 1917. the town itself has a population of 10,302 ( 2001 ) . it is in the municipality of ourém , in the centro region and sub region of medio tejo . it is in the district of santarém and included in the urban agglomeration of leiria , in central portugal , south of porto and north of lisbon . related pages . - our lady of fatima other websites . - sanctuary of fátima - pilgrims of fátima
