Karak District     karak district is a district in the khyber pakhtunkhwa province of pakistan . the city of karak is the capital . this district is the second smallest district in khyber pakhtunkhwa after hangu . the area of this district is 3395 km . the district of karak is administratively subdivided into three tehsils . - banda daud shah tehsil - karak tehsil - takht-e-nasrati tehsil
