Notre-Dame de Reims     notre dame de reims is a cathedral in reims . it was built in the 13th century . it is not as old as notre-dame de paris or notre-dame de chartres . the building process started in 1211. most of the works were done in 1275. it was built before the cathedrals of strasbourg , amiens and beauvais , which are also called notre dame . the cathedral is one of the best examples of gothic architecture . it has been a world heritage site since 1991. in 2006 , about 1.5 million people visited it . the kings of france were crowned in this cathedral . the last coronation ceremony was that of charles x of france in 1825 .
