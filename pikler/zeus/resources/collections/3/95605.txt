Pronunciation     pronunciation is a way a word is usually spoken . it can be affected by many different factors : 1. geographical location or dialect 1. presence of speech defect 1. ethnic group 1. education level 1. social class mispronunciation is an actual mistake in speaking . it is not used for how a speaker of a dialect pronounces a word . examples occur in many place names and personal names . for instance , leicester square is actually pronounced `` lester square '' .
