Liquefied petroleum gas     liquefied petroleum gas ( also known as lpg , lp gas or autogas ) is a mixture of hydrocarbon gases . it is commonly used in the household , for example for cooking , or as fuel for heating . lpg is replacing chlorofluorocarbons more and more , also because it is less harmful to the ozone layer . very often , mixes of propane and butane are sold . other websites . - pixie gas lpg supplier based in the united states
