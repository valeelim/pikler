Transport for London     transport for london ( tfl ) is an organisation that runs the public transport network in london . it is held with direct responsibility to the mayor of london . historically , bus routes were grouped by the type of service that they provided . the 1924 london traffic act used the bassom scheme . it was named after a.e . bassom of the metropolitan police who made it . changing and short lines used letter suffixes . also , the numbers were grouped by the company that operated the route . the numbering was changed in 1934 after london transport was formed . `` *except 603 and 607 , which are normal day time routes '' other websites . - `` transport for london '' website
