Red-light district     a red-light district is a part of an urban area where prostitution is concentrated . prostitution in red-light districts is either legal or illegal , but tolerated . in the world there are many red-light districts , the most famous probably being the red-light district in amsterdam , de wallen . in the red-light district , prostitutes are often helped and protected , and every month doctors visit them . condoms are given to the prostitute so they do not catch any sexually transmitted diseases ( std 's ) . in some red-light districts , prostitutes pay taxes , as in the red-light district of amsterdam . in other red light districts prostitutes do not pay taxes , because there they are illegal and often are put on the street with violence . in some parts of africa there are villages of prostitutes , where they live and work , because they are not accepted by the people of other villages . related pages . - list of red-light districts
