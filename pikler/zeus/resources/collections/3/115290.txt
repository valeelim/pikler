Palembang     palembang is a city of 1,286,000 people in the south of the indonesian island of sumatra . it is the capital of the province of south sumatra . the city was the capital of the old , buddhist kingdom of srivijaya that included a large part of malaysia and indonesia . after a 1025 raid by the chola empire of southern india it became less imortant . srivijaya 's capital moved north to jambi . palembang is also the home of parameswara , the ruler of malacca ( a state in malaysia ) who started the most important empire in malaysian history . the battle of palembang was fought near the city during the second world war between february 13-february 15 1942. japanese forces captured the airport and nearby oil refinery after a short but bloody battle . palembang 's economy has grown after it became a venue for national sporting event in 2004. . palembang was also one of hosts of the 2007 afc asian cup . the city is served by sultan mahmud badaruddin ii airport which has many flights to many cities in indonesia and also to singapore and malaysia .
