Aircraft hijacking     aircraft hijacking ( also known as skyjacking and aircraft piracy ) is the takeover of an aircraft , by a person or group , usually armed . in most cases the pilot is forced to fly according to the orders of the hijackers . sometimes the hijackers fly the planes themselves , as believed to have occurred in the september 11 , 2001 attacks . in one case the official pilot hijacked the plane , when he diverted his internal air china flight to taiwan . related pages . - airport security - list of aircraft hijackings - d. b. cooper - september 11 attacks - terrorism
