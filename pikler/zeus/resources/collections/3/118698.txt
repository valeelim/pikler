Mohmand Agency     the mohmand agency ( ) was created in 1951 before which the mohmand tribes were administered by the deputy commissioner based in peshawar , khyber pakhtunkhwa , pakistan . however it was not until 1973 that the headquarters of the mohmand agency were moved from peshawar to ekkagund . now , the permanent agency headquarters is at ghalanai , deep in the mohmand agency . there is scant rainfall and little irrigated land , a factor responsible for the large demographic movements of the mohmands to the fertile lands of charsadda and mardan districts to the east . sher alam mahsud is the `` political agent '' ( or governor ) of mohmand as of july 2007 - halimzai tehsil - pindiali tehsil - pringar tehsil - safi tehsil - upper mohmand tehsil - utman khel ( ambar ) tehsil - yake ghund tehsil
