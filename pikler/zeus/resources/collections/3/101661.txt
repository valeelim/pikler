Kollam     kollam ( malayalam : കൊല്ലം ) ( known to the portuguese as quilon , pronounced `` koy-lon '' ) is a city and a municipal corporation in kollam district in the indian state of kerala . it is north of the state capital thiruvananthapuram ( trivandrum ) . it is also the headquarters of the kollam district , one among the 14 districts in kerala . to the south is thiruvananthapuram district , to the north is pathanamthitta and alappuzha , to the east is tamil nadu and to the west by the arabian sea .
