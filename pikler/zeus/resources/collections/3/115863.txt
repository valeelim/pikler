Svetlana Kuznetsova     svetlana aleksandrovna kuznetsova ( cyrillic : ; born june 27 , 1985 ) is a russian professional tennis player who is currently ranked world no . 4. she won the us open singles title in 2004 and was the runner-up in singles at the french open in 2006 and the us open in 2007 , losing both finals to justine henin in straight sets .
