Drive-through     a drive-thru ( or drive-through ) is for quick service , generally at restaurants . there are also drive-thrus at other places , such as banks or coffee shops . examples . - alcohol at a drive-through liquor store - banking services at a drive-through bank - coffee at a drive-through coffee shop - dairy products at a drive-through dairy store ( notably the skinner dairy shops of north-east florida ) - drugs at a drive-through pharmacy - food or drink at a drive-through restaurant ( typically fast food ) - marriage ( primarily at special drive-through marriage chapels in las vegas in the united states )
