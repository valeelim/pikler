Langur     the gray langur or hanuman langur ( semnopithecus ) is a genus of monkey . they are found in southeast asia . the word `` langur '' means 'having a long tail . ' it is commonly called the leaf monkey . they usually live in small groups of between 2 and 10 individuals . they mainly eat leaves , as well as some fruit . langur spend most of their time in trees . they have white fur and black faces .
