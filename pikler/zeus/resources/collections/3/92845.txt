Waterboarding     waterboarding is a type of torture that has been used for a long time . it was often used to make people confess . the victim is tied onto a table or wooden board . the questioner has a helper , whose job it is to pour water on the victim 's nose and mouth through a towel or cloth , to make it difficult for him to breathe . if and when the victim is supplying answers that the questioner deems acceptable , the victim ( ideally ) is permitted to breathe . in modern times waterboarding has been in use in guantanamo bay and has been a major political argument . its use by american forces was banned by president barack obama in 2009 .
