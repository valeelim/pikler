Houston Rockets     the houston rockets are a team in the national basketball association in houston , texas . they play their home games at the toyota center . they began playing in the nba in 1967 and were originally known as the san diego rockets from 1967 to 1971. hakeem olajuwon was a star player for the rockets . he lead them to their first nba championship in 1994. they repeated as champions in 1995. yao ming was also a star player for the rockets .
