Pac-12 Conference     the pac-12 conference is a group of universities who play each other in football , basketball , and other sports . it has won more national championships than any other conference . this is a list of colleges and universities who play sports in the pac-12 conference : - university of arizona - arizona state university - university of california , berkeley ( `` california '' ) - university of colorado at boulder ( `` colorado '' ) - university of oregon - oregon state university - stanford university - university of california , los angeles ( `` ucla '' ) - university of southern california ( `` usc '' ) - university of utah - university of washington - washington state university
