Tré Cool     tré cool ( born frank edwin wright iii december 9 1972 , in frankfurt , germany ) is a german-born american drummer for the punk band green day . he has red hair and blue eyes . he has two children , ramona , born january 1995 to ex-wife , lisea lyons ; frankito ( `` little frank '' ) , born 2001 to claudia . tré grew up with his father and 2 siblings . he dated the donnas drummer torry castellano in 2003 , they then separated in mid 2005. tre ' cool is currently single .
