Yotsuya Kaidan     yotsuya kaidan , also known as `` tōkaidō yotsuya kaidan '' , is one of the most famous japanese ghost stories of all time . it was written as a play in 1825 by tsuruya nanboku . the tale takes place in the tōkaidō region . the story is a tale of murder and ghostly revenge . it continues to be an influence on japanese horror today .
