Ginny Weasley     ginevra `` ginny '' molly weasley is a fictional character of j.k. rowling 's famous series of fantasy novels , harry potter . she is one year younger than harry potter . she later marries harry and they have three children ; james sirius , albus severus and lily luna potter . ginny is described as a young girl with bright red hair and the youngest child of the weasley family . she is a gryffindor . in the harry potter films she is played by bonnie wright .
