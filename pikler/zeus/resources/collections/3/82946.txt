Hard-Fi     hard-fi are a popular indie rock band from staines an area of london , england . they are named after lee scratch perry 's description of his studio . they have had two hit albums in the uk charts : `` stars of cctv '' and `` once upon a time in the west '' . their most successful songs include `` suburban knights '' and `` hard to beat '' . band members . - richard archer - kai stephens - ross phillips - steve kemp
