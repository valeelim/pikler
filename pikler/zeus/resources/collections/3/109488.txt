Gorj County     gorj ( ) is a county ( judeţ ) of romania , in oltenia . the capital is târgu jiu . gorj county has an area of 5,602 km² . the main river , which collects all the smaller rivers , is the jiu river . gorj county has 2 municipalities , 6 towns and 61 communes . municipalities . - târgu jiu – capital city ; population : over 100,000 in 2004 . - motru in descending order of size - rovinari - bumbeşti-jiu - târgu cărbuneşti - turceni - tismana - novaci - ţicleni
