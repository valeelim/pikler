Boeing 757     the boeing 757 is a short to medium range narrow-body commercial passenger aircraft manufactured by boeing commercial airplanes . it was launched by eastern air lines and british airways to replace the boeing 727 and entered service in 1983. production of the 757 ended on october 28 , 2004 after 1,050 had been built . the final aircraft was delivered to shanghai airlines on november 28 , 2005. as of july 2007 , a total of 1,019 boeing 757 aircraft were in airline service .
