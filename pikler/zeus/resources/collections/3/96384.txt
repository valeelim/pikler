Graphic arts     graphic arts is a term that was used for the art of printmaking and drawing . today it means the skills of a graphic designer or print technician . graphic arts as a trade can be traced back to the first instances of the stamped image or word . graphic art is an artistic process of creating a design where one uses a medium ( like rubber or plexiglas ) and transfers the image to a material ( like paper ) . related pages . - graphic design other websites . - american institute of graphic artists - online design degrees - graphic artists guild
