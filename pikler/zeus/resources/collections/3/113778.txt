Knoppix     knoppix , or knoppix , is a linux distribution based on debian designed to be started directly from a cd / dvd . knoppix is one of the best-known livedistros . it was developed by linux consultant klaus knopper . when starting a program it is loaded from the optical disc and decompressed into a ram drive . the decompression is transparent and on-the-fly . related pages . - list of linux distributions
