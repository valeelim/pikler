Benetton Formula     benetton formula ltd. , commonly referred to simply as benetton , was a formula one constructor that participated from 1986 to 2001. the team was owned by the benetton family who run a worldwide chain of clothing stores of the same name . in 2000 the team was purchased by renault , but competed as benetton for the 2001 season . in 2002 the team became renault f1 . the team won 27 races , as well as the 1994 and 1995 drivers ' championship ( with michael schumacher ) and the 1995 constructors ' championship .
