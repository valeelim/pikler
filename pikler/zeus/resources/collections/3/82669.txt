Notre Dame     notre dame in french is the name give to a number of religious buildings , consecrated to the virgin mary . churches and cathedrals in france . - notre dame de paris , probably the most famous - bayeux cathedral , called notre-dame de bayeux - notre-dame de chartres - notre-dame de la garde in marseille - notre-dame de reims - notre dame du haut in ronchamp - strasbourg cathedral , called notre-dame de strasbourg other . - university of notre dame in the u.s. state of indiana - `` notre dame de paris '' , the original french title of a novel by victor hugo published in 1831. in english , this novel is commonly called `` the hunchback of notre dame '' .
