Lloyds Bank     lloyds tsb is one of world 's largest banks . it is based on gresham street in the city of london and has business all over the world including the us , europe , middle east , asia and latin america . in 2009 lloyds tsb merged with hbos ( halifax bank of scotland ) to make lloyds banking group , this now uses three brands on the high street , lloyds tsb , halifax and bank of scotland . other websites . - lloyds tsb web site - lloyds tsb asia web site - lloyds tsb spain web site
