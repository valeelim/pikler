City of Illusions     city of illusions is a 1967 science fiction book that was written by ursula k. le guin in her hainish cycle . `` city of illusions '' was republished in 1978 with `` rocannon 's world '' and `` planet of exile '' in a set called `` three hainish novels '' and in 1994 with the same books in `` worlds of exile and illusion '' .
