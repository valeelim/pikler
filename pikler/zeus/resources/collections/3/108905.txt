Haltern am See     haltern am see ( `` haltern at the lake '' , before december 2001 only haltern ) is a town and a municipality in the district of recklinghausen , in north rhine-westphalia , germany . it is situated on the lippe river and the wesel-datteln canal , approx . 15 km north of recklinghausen . twin towns . - rochford , essex , england - roost-warendin , france - sankt veit an der glan , austria other websites . - livius.org : roman fortress haltern
