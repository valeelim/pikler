The Master of the Mississippi     `` the master of the mississippi '' is the second chapter of `` the life and times of scrooge mcduck '' . scrooge comes to america . here he begins to work on his uncle pothole mcduck 's boat . scrooge has his first adventure with the beagle boys finding a lost treasure . the beagles are arrested by the police and scrooge buys his uncle 's boat . but the beagles escape and destroy scrooge 's boat .
