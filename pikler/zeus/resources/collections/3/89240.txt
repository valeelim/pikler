Most Haunted     most haunted is a british paranormal television program based on investigating possible paranormal activity . it is shown on the satellite and cable channels living , living2 and virgin 1 ( formerly ftn ) , mainly for the uk market . it is also shown in the united states on the travel channel and on the w channel in australia . it is made by antix productions , and is led by yvette fielding and her husband karl beattie . the show will start their 10th series on february 19 , 2008. the team travel around the uk , and also ireland , the netherlands and the united states , investigating possible paranormal 'hotspots ' for 24 hours . sister shows . - most haunted extra : runs directly after premiere episodes , documenting behind-the-scenes antics of the cast and crew on that night 's location . - most haunted unseen : re-release of complete series one but with added footage bringing the duration of the programme from half an hour to an hour . - most haunted live ! - most haunted live : access all areas : similar to most haunted extra . features behind-the-scenes footage and interviews with the team .
