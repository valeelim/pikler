Hurricane Hazel     hurricane hazel was the worst hurricane of the 1954 atlantic hurricane season and one of the worst hurricanes of the 20th century . hazel killed as many as 1,000 people in haiti before striking the united states just south of wilmington , north carolina as a category 4 hurricane . 19 people were killed in north carolina , and 81 people were killed when it hit toronto , ontario . it is the strongest hurricane ever recorded to strike so far inland .
