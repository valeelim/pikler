Escape the Fate     escape the fate is an american post-hardcore band from las vegas , nevada . the band was formed in 2004 , in 2005 they won a radio competition to open for my chemical romance which led to a signing to epitaph records . in the year 2006 the band released debut album , `` dying is your latest fashion '' . with the new frontman craig mabbitt ( former blessthefall vocalist ) the band released the album `` this war is ours '' in october 2008. in 2010 , the band released the third album , `` escape the fate '' , in november . the lead vocalist ronnie radke left the band in 2008 due to legal issues . radke left the jail in december 2010 and is currently a vocalist in the post-hardcore band falling in reverse . in 2012 , founding member max green was kicked out due to drug abuse and missing shows and he was permanently replaced by thomas `` tj '' bell , formerly of motionless in white and micheal money , brother of bryan `` monte '' money was introduced as official rythmn guitarist . band members . current members . - craig mabbitt ( 2008 - present ) lead vocals
