Hinterrhein (district)    hinterrhein ( district ) hinterrhein is a district of the canton of graubünden , switzerland . it has an area of 617.67 km² and a population of 12,508 people ( as of december 2004 ) . hinterrhein consists of five `` kreise '' ( sub-districts ) and 39 municipalities . on 1 january 2006 , medels im rheinwald became part of splügen . on 1 january 2003 , the municipalities of donath and patzen-fardün became part of donat .
