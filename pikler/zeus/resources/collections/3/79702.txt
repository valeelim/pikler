River delta     a river delta is a landform where the mouth of a river flows into an ocean , sea , desert , estuary , lake or another river . it is formed by sediment carried by the river being deposited in the wider mouth . the flow of water is often slower-moving there . the word `` delta '' comes from the upper-case letter delta , Î´ , in the greek alphabet because a river delta resembles the shape of this letter . other websites . - louisiana state university geology - world deltas
