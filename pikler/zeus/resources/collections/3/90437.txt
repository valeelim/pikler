Bachelor of Arts     bachelor of arts is a college degree . it takes three years to get it in australia , austria , denmark , estonia , england , wales , northern ireland , germany , iceland , india , israel , the netherlands , new zealand , norway , quebec , pakistan , singapore , south africa , and switzerland . it takes four years in canada , republic of ireland , scotland , japan , united states , republic of korea , and the philippines . `` bachelor of arts '' is often written as `` ba '' or `` b.a . '' the latin term is `` artium baccalaureus '' so some colleges use the abbreviation of `` ab '' or `` a.b . ''
