Analog Science Fiction and Fact     analog science fiction and fact is an american science fiction magazine . as of 2007 , it is the longest currently published magazine of that genre . first published in 1930 in the united states with the name astounding stories as a pulp magazine , its name has been changed several times , such as astounding science-fiction in 1938 , and analog science fact & fiction in 1960. references . - analog science fiction and fact official web site - index ( 1930-1960 ) - index ( 1960-present )
