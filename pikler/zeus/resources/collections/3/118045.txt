Camp Rock     camp rock is a 2008 american musical comedy movie made by the disney channel . `` camp rock '' is the soundtrack of the movie . it was released on june 17 , 2008. full songs of the album were made available on camp rock 's official web site for one week , beginning june 10 , 2008. the full soundtrack was first broadcast on radio disney on june 14 , 2008 during `` planet premiere : camp rock '' . the soundtrack was released in the united kingdom on july 14 , 2008. the soundtrack album ( both original and special edition ) is an enhanced cd which contains a clip of an acoustic version of `` this is me '' when played on the computer with internet access . track listing . 1 . `` we rock '' ( 3:12 ) 1 . `` play my music '' ( 3:26 ) 1 . `` got ta find you '' ( 4:05 ) 1 . `` start the party '' ( 3:03 ) 1 . `` who will i be ?
