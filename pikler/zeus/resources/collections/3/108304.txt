Squealer (Animal Farm)    squealer ( animal farm ) squealer is a porker on manor farm in the book `` animal farm '' . squealer is a very fat little piglet , with round cheeks , twinkling eyes , nimble movements , coupled together with a shrill voice . while arguing a difficult point , he skips from side to side and whisks his tail around in a persuasive manner , allowing him to lie with ease . the pig is seen as the propagandist in the book and his ability to use language well makes his speeches believable by the other less intelligent animals . squealer is based on the russia media of stalin 's time , and all the advertisement of stalin .
