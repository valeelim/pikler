Nippes Department     nippes is one of the ten departments ( french : `` départments '' , singular `` départment '' ) of haiti . it is the newest and smallest haitian department ; it was created in 2003 with the eastern part of the grand'anse department . it has an estimated population of 266,379 ( 2003 ) and its capital city is miragoâne . nippes has an area of 1,219 km² . it is bordered to the south by the sud department , to the east by the ouest department , to the west by the grand'anse department and to the north by the gonâve channel , the southern part of the gulf of gonâve . the department is divided into three `` arrondissements '' ( like districts ) and 11 `` communes '' ( like municipalities ) . the `` arrondissements '' and their `` communes '' are : 1. miragoâne 1. miragoâne 1. petite-rivière-de-nippes 1. fonds-des-nègres 1. paillant 1. anse-à-veau 1. anse-à-veau 1. l'asile 1. petit-trou-de-nippes 1. plaisance-du-sud 1. arnaud 1. baradères 1. barradères 1. grand-boucan
