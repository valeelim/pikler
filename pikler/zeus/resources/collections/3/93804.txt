Philip II of Macedon     philip ii was an ancient king of macedon . he ruled from 359 bc to 336 bc . he was the father of the emperor alexander the great , who was born in 356 bc . he united all of greece . he would either bring a city-state into his hegemony with his military or he would talk/bribe the leaders into joining his empire . it was under his reign in 338 bc at chaeronea against athens that alexander first showed his military genius . philip was assassinated by his bodyguard , in a theatre .
