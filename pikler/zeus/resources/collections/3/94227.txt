High Rhulain     high rhulain is a fantasy book written by brian jacques in 2005. it is the eighteenth book in the `` redwall '' series , and was also translated into french and russian . a young female otter named tiria wildlough is living at redwall abbey . when she gets a vision and riddle from the long-dead hero martin the warrior , she sets out with a hare named cuthbert blanedale frunk in search of an island called green isle to fight an evil wildcat and lead a bunch of otters to freedom .
