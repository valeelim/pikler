Bahawalpur District     bahawalpur district ( ) is a district in the punjab province of pakistan . the city of bahawalpur is the capital . the riasti dialect of the language of the punjab is spoken here and the adjoining areas in the bahawalpur area of cholistan . the district is administratively divided into five tehsils and 107 union councils : - ahmedpur sharqia tehsil - bahawalpur tehsil city - hasilpur tehsil - khairpur tamewali tehsil - yazman mandi tehsil - bahawalpur tehsil sadar
