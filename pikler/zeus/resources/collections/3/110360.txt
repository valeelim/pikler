Charley Patton     charlie patton ( also known as charley patton ) ( may 1 , 1891 - april 28 , 1934 ) was an american delta blues musician . he is thought to be the `` father of delta blues '' and was one of the most popular of the early recorded blues musicians . musical releases and his gravestone spell his name “ charley ” even though the musician himself spelled his name `` charlie .
