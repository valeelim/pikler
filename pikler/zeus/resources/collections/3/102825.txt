Kansas City Royals     the kansas city royals are a major league baseball team based in kansas city , missouri . they began play in 1969. the royals won a world series title in 1985. from 1973 to the present , the royals have played in kauffman stadium . they are owned by david glass , managed by ned yost , and their general manager is dayton moore . the old minor league baseball team in kansas city had been called the blues . there was also a famous negro league baseball team called the kansas city monarchs . the royals name and their team color blue are at least partly in honor of those older teams . the kansas city royals won the 2015 world series by the new york mets 4-1. other websites . - kansas city royals official website - royals on radio - around the horn in kc - official mlblog of the kansas city royals front office . - cbs sportsline.com - sports e-cyclopedia
