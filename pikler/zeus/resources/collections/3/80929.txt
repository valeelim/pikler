Small Dark Spot     the small dark spot , sometimes also called dark spot 2 or the wizard 's eye , was a southern cyclonic storm on the planet neptune . it was the second strongest storm on the planet in 1989 , when voyager 2 flew by the planet . when first discovered , it was a dark cyclonic storm . as voyager 2 approached the planet , a bright core of cloud activity developed . it disappeared in 1994 .
