Padmasambhava     padmasambhava is the person who brought buddhism to tibet in the 8th century . the name is in the sanskrit language and means `` born from a lotus flower '' . in bhutan and tibet he is better known as guru rinpoche ( `` precious master '' ) or lopon rinpoche , where followers of the nyingma school regard him as the second buddha . the mantra of padmasambhva is `` om ah hum vajra guru padma siddhi hum '' and is called the `` vajra guru mantra '' . there is also a prayer called `` the seven line prayer '' which is devoted to him . related pages . - lama - rinpoche - yeshe tsogyal other websites . - the seven line prayer : text and commentary
