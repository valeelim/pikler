Émile Durkheim    émile durkheim émile durkheim ( april 15 , 1858 – november 15 , 1917 ) was a french sociologist . he is known for his contributions to sociology and anthropology . he is considered as one of the founding fathers of sociology . he thought that sociology should be scientific . durkheim was born in the eastern french province of lorraine . he studied at école normale supérieure . he made contributions on topics such as education , crime , religion , suicide , and many other topics related to sociology . durkheim died in paris , aged 59. references . - biography of émile durkheim
