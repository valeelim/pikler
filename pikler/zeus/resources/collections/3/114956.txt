Ilium/Olympos     ilium/olympos is a science fiction series that was written by dan simmons . the events in the books are set in motion by beings who have taken on the roles of the greek gods . the first book in the series , `` illium '' received the locus award for best book in 2004. there are two books in the series : - `` ilium '' ( 2003 ) - locus award 2004 - `` olympos '' ( 2005 )
