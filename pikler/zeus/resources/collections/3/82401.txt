2006 Formula One season     the 2006 formula one season was the 57th edition of the championship . the champion was fernando alonso in a close battle . renault won the constructor 's championship . four prominent names in the sport disappeared for this season , with minardi , sauber , bar and jordan withdrawing , and one new team , super aguri entered at the last moment . the sauber name remained , although largely as a sentiment , as bmw owned 80 % of the team to peter sauber 's 20 % . jordan became mf1 racing , as midland started afresh after a disappointing first season under the jordan name . late in the season , the team was bought by spyker . honda , who already owned a 45 % stake in the bar team , completed their takeover of the team and changed its name to honda racing f1 team at the start of the season . super aguri f1 also entered their first season after having problems entering . they received backing from honda racing f1 including technology and engines , due to them running honda driver takuma sato .
