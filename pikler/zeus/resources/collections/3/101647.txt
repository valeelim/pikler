Second Epistle to the Corinthians     the second epistle to the corinthians is one of the books in the new testament . it is an epistle ( letter ) written to the church at corinth by the apostle paul . paul begins by greeting the people in corinth and reminding them that he is serving the lord jesus christ . he then spends a lot of time explaining to the people why he is an apostle . some of the people are mad at him because in 1st corinthians he told them to stop doing bad things . he explains that he told them to stop because he loved them . he also teaches them that it is very important to forgive others .
