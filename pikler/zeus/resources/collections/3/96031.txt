House of Bourbon     the house of bourbon is an important royal house of europe . kings from the house of bourbon ruled france from 1589 to 1792 and again from 1815 to 1848. this is a younger branch of the capetian dynasty . members of the house of bourbon also ruled naples , sicily , and parma in the past . jean , grand duke of luxembourg and king felipe vi of spain are both part of the house of bourbon . all of these branches descended from three legitimate grandsons of louis xiv . - bourbons of france , such as king louis xv . - bourbons of spain , such as the present king . - bourbons of parma , dukes of parma - bourbons of the two sicilies , kings of the two sicilies
