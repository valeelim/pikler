Yogi     a yogi ( sanskrit , feminine root : yogini ) is a term for a male who practices various forms of the path of yoga , maintaining a steadfast mind , the process of transcending the lower self . these designations are mostly reserved for advanced or daily practitioners . in contemporary english `` yogin '' is an alternative rendering for the word `` yogi '' . this word is often used to describe buddhist monks or any lay person or householder who is devoted to meditation . the `` shiva-samhita '' text defines the yogi as someone who knows that the entire cosmos is situated within his own body , and the `` yoga-shikha-upanishad '' distinguishes two kinds of yogins : those who pierce through the `` sun '' ( surya ) by means of the various yogic techniques and those who access the door of the central conduit ( sushumna-nadi ) and drink the nectar . related pages . - yogini 1-2. feuerstein , georg . the shambhala encyclopedia of yoga , shambhala publications , boston , 2000 p. 321 , 350 .
