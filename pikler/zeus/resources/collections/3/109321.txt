New Lanark     new lanark is the unesco world heritage site in the south lanarkshire , scotland . new lanark is a 18th century village and model industrial community with cotton mill buildings , workers ' housing , and school . the small town was built by robert owen as an experiment in utopian idealism . between 1785 and 1968 , some buildings were torn down or burned ; and some new buildings were built . however , the overall appearance of the village stayed much like it was in the first half of the 19th century . in 1975 , the new lanark conservation trust was established to prevent demolition of the village . in 2001 , new lanark was named a world heritage site . related pages . - list of world heritage sites of the united kingdom other websites . - new lanark website
