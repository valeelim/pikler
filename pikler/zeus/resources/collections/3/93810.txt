Nebuchadnezzar II     nebuchadnezzar ii ( about 634 – 562 bc ) was king of the neo-babylonian empire , and reigned ~605 bc – 562 bc . according to the bible , he conquered judah and jerusalem , and sent the jews into exile . he is featured in the book of daniel and is mentioned in several other books of the bible . he ( allegedly ) destroyed solomon 's temple . he is said to have built the hanging gardens of babylon . the king built the hanging gardens in attempt to please his wife . his kingdom had a great flow of trade and prosperity . however , some scholars argue that the gardens may have been constructed by the assyrian king sennacherib in his capital city , nineveh .
