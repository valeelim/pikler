Rosario Dawson     rosario dawson ( born may 9 , 1979 ) is an american actress and singer . her first movie was `` kids '' in 1995. since then , she has become known for her roles in `` men in black ii '' ( 2002 ) , `` 25th hour '' ( 2002 ) , `` the rundown '' ( 2003 ) , `` alexander '' ( 2004 ) , `` sin city '' ( 2005 ) , `` death proof '' ( 2007 ) , `` eagle eye '' ( 2008 ) , `` unstoppable '' ( 2010 ) , `` césar chávez '' ( 2013 ) , `` trance '' ( 2013 ) , `` the lego batman movie '' ( 2017 ) .
