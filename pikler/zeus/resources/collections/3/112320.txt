Lorne Chabot     lorne `` chabotsky '' chabot ( october 5 , 1900 in montreal , quebec - october 10 1946 ) was a canadian ice hockey goaltender . lorne played in the national hockey league from 1926 to 1937. during this time , he played for the new york rangers , toronto maple leafs , montreal canadiens , chicago black hawks , montreal maroons , and new york americans . lorne won the 1935 vezina trophy for the being the league 's best goaltender . he was the first hockey player to appear on the cover of time magazine .
