Sindhi language     sindhi ( arabic script : سنڌي , devanagari script : सिन्धी , `` sindhī '' ) is the language of the sindh region of south asia , which is a province of pakistan . it is an indo-aryan language spoken by about 41 million people in pakistan , and 12 million in the republic of india . other websites . - sindhi tutorial - sindhilanguage.com - sindhi script history and script - daily sindh hyderabad a newspaper in sindhi language
