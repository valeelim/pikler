Ilium (book)    ilium ( book ) ilium is a science fiction book that was written by dan simmons . it is the first part of the `` ilium/olympos '' cycle , and is about the re-creation of the events in the `` iliad '' on mars . in july 2004 , `` ilium '' got the locus award for best science fiction novel of 2003. it was later nominated for the 2004 hugo award for best book .
