Kamer Genç     kamer genç ( february 23 , 1940 – january 22 , 2016 ) was a turkish politician . he is of zaza origin . he was a member of parliament as a member of the republican people 's party in the 18th , 19th , and 23rd election cycles , and in the 20th and 21st as a member of the true path party . he was born in nazımiye , tunceli province . genç died of cancer in istanbul , turkey on january 22 , 2016 at the age of 75 .
