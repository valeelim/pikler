Cynthia Lennon     cynthia lillian lennon ( born cynthia lillian powell 10 september 1939 - 1 april 2015 ) was the first wife of john lennon . cynthia divorced lennon in 1968 after he left her for yoko ono . she is the mother of lennon 's elder son julian . she was born in blackpool , lancashire and raised in hoylake , cheshire . other websites . - the beatles first wives club - living with lennon
