José Figueres Ferrer     josé maría hipólito figueres ferrer ( september 25 , 1906 - june 8 , 1990 ) was president of costa rica three times . he was also the rebel leader during the costa rican civil war . also known as don pepe , josé figueres ferrer was born in san ramón , alajuela , costa rica . his parents were from cataluña , spain : mariano rodríguez borges and francisca ferrer mendiola . figueres entered the primary boys school in san ramón of alajuela in 1913. he got in costa rica ’ s lyceum finishing his secondary studies in 1920. he went to boston , united states to travel and study . he returned to costa rica in 1928 and bought a coffee farm . in 1942 , figueres made a speech on latin america radio . in this speech , he objected to the situation in costa rica under president rafael ángel calderón . because of this speech , he was arrested and forced to leave the country . figueres return to costa rica in 1944. he started a political party , the democratic party . a main goal of the party was to go against calderon 's party , the national republican party . in 1948 , supporters of calderón caused problems with an election for president in which calderón lost to otilio ulate . because of this , the results of the election were not used . this led to the start of the civil war in 1948. in march 11 , 1948 , don pepe began a war to support the peoples decision at the previous election . figueres and his troops won .
