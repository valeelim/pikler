Ob River     the ob is a river in siberia . together with the lena and the jenissei , it is one of the most important rivers of siberia . depending upon how its length is measured , it can be between 3.650 km and 6.210 km long . some important cities on the banks of the river include novosibirsk ( russia 's third largest city ) , barnaul , kolpashevo , nizhnevartovsk , surgut , khanty-mansiysk , beryozovo , labytnangi , and salekhard . the irtysh is the ob river 's main tributary . other tributaries include the omsk , parabel , chaya , tobol and ishim .
