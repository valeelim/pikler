Pidgin     a pidgin is a simplified language . pidgins usually develop because two groups of people need to talk to each other but do not speak the same language . pidgins are not usually as complicated as many other languages . not all simple or `` broken '' forms of language are pidgins . pidgins have rules which a person must learn to speak the pidgin well . countries that use pidgin languages as their official languages include papua new guinea , jamaica and some other caribbean and central american countries .
