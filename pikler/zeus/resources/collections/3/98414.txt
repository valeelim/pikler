Carl von Moers     carl von moers ( 9 december 1871 in neuwied - 26 may 1957 ) was a german horse rider who competed in the 1912 summer olympics . he and his horse `` may-queen '' won the silver medal as members of the german team at the 1912 summer olympics . but he was 15th in the individual eventing competition . he also participated in the individual dressage event at the 1912 summer olympics with his horse `` new bank '' . they finished twelfth . other websites . - data for moers at sports-reference.com - list of german equestrians
