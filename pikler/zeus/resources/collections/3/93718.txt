Ryan Tedder     ryan benjamin tedder ( born june 26 , 1979 ) is an american singer , and grammy nominated songwriter and record producer born in tulsa , oklahoma . he has written and produced for various musicians but , as of 2007 , he is best known as the front man for the `` myspace us rock phenomenon '' , onerepublic . he wrote songs and produced for britney spears , christina aguilera and also , did some work for timbaland . other websites . - ryan tedder for the sound - the official site of onerepublic
