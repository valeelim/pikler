Murrumbidgee River     the murrumbidgee river is a big river in new south wales , australia , and the australian capital territory . it joins in with the murray river . the word `` murrumbidgee '' is from the wiradjuri language ( indigenous australian ) and means `` big water '' , `` track goes down here '' or `` very good place '' . there are many large towns built next to the river , gundagai , wagga wagga , narrandera , hay and balranald .
