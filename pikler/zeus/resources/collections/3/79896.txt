Barbuda     barbuda is an island in the eastern caribbean , and forms part of the state of antigua and barbuda . it has a population of about 1,500 , most of whom live in the town of codrington . barbuda is located north of antigua , in the middle of the leeward islands . to the south are the islands of montserrat and guadaloupe , and to the north and west are nevis , st. kitts , st. barts , and st. martin .
