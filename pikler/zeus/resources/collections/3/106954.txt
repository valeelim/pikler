Hasselt     hasselt is a city in belgium . it is the capital of the belgian provice limburg . it lies about half way between aachen and leuven . the city has city privileges since the mid 13th century , the year 1232 to be exact . today , about 70.000 people live there . most of them are native dutch speakers . well-known people from hasselt include heinrich von veldeke , probably one of the first writers who did no longer write in latin , but rather used the german of the time , in the 12th century , willy claes , who was nato secretary in 1994 and 1995 , and axelle red a contemporary singer-songwriter . in 2007 its population was 70584. it is located at 50° 55 north , 05° 20 east .
