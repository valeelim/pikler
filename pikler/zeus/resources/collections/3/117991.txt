Brooklyn South     brooklyn south is a short-lived american television police drama . it aired from 1997-1998 on cbs for only one season . it was cancelled due to poor ratings . one of the producers of the series was steven bochco , creator of many well-known police dramas such as `` hill street blues '' and `` nypd blue '' . `` brooklyn south '' was also created and produced by david milch . the series tried to create a setting of a gritty , realistic police station similar to that of `` nypd blue '' , but differed by focusing on the uniformed police officers rather than the detectives . the pilot of `` brooklyn south '' was noted as the first tv-ma rated episode on broadcast television .
