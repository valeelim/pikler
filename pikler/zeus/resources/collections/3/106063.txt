Tariq Ramadan     tariq said ramadan ( born 26 september 1962 in geneva , switzerland ) is a swiss muslim academic and theologian . he thinks islamic texts should be studied . he also thinks that islamic society today is different from what it was when the koran was written . he therefore says that muslims living in europe need to adapt and change islam , so they can contribute its values to a european society . this position is often referred to as euroislam .
