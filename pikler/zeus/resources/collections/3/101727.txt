Bangor, County Down    bangor , county down bangor is a town in county down , northern ireland . on 29 april 2001 the united kingdom government had a census and saw that there were 58,388 people living in bangor . they also saw that : - 20.2 % of people in bangor were under 16 years old and 20.3 % were over 60 years old - 47.7 % of people were male and 52.3 % were female - 82.7 % were from a protestant background and 10.6 % were from a roman catholic background
