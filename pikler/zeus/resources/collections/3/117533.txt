Wario Land: The Shake Dimension    wario land : the shake dimension wario land : the shake dimension ( known as wario land : shake it ! in north america ) is a platform video game made by good-feel and to be published by nintendo for the wii game console . `` the shake dimension '' is a sidescrolling platform game that uses 3d cel-shading to make it look hand-drawn . it stars wario , and the player controls him by holding the wii remote like an nes controller . wario can jump , do a shoulder bash , and at certain times , can use the wii remote 's motion features to shake stuff or control stuff .
