Richmond, Kentucky    richmond , kentucky richmond is a city in north-central kentucky . it is a little south of lexington , kentucky and is the county seat of madison county . eastern kentucky university is in richmond . it had 27,152 people in 2000 and an area of 19.3 square miles ( 49.5 square kilometers ) . it has an elevation of 948 feet ( 289 meters ) and a density of 1,420.4/square mile ( 548.4/square kilometer ) . it is the sixth largest city in kentucky when listed by the number of people . other websites . - city of richmond
