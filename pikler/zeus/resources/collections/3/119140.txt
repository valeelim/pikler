Sint-Amands     sint-amands is a municipality in the belgian province of antwerp . it includes the towns of lippelo , oppuurs and sint-amands proper . in 2007 , 7819 people lived there . it is at 51° 03 north , 04° 12 east . related pages . - more info about oppuurs ( page in dutch ) - more info about lippelo ( page in dutch )
