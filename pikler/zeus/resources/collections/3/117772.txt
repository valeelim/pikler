Worf     worf is a ' and ' character . he is played by michael dorn . he serves on the starship enterprise as lieutenant junior grade . worf is the first klingon main character to appear in star trek , and has appeared in more star trek episodes than any other character . worf is the son of mogh . when worf was a child , his parents and family were killed by the romulan attack on the khitomer outpost . worf , his nanny kahlest , and his brother kurn survived the attack . worf was then adopted by a human couple , sergey and helena rozhenko while his brother was raised in the klingon homeworld and was not revealed until they were both adults . although worf never took the rozhenko 's last name , for he preferred to be addressed as `` worf , son of mogh '' . it was during his time on gault that the 13-year-old worf , captain of his school 's soccer team accidentally killed another boy during a championship by throwing himself at him as the two children ran after the ball which flew in the air . this was something worf would never forget .
