Léogâne     léogâne is the name of both a coastal city and a district in ouest department , haiti . the city of léogâne is around . the arrondissement of léogâne contains three communes : petit-goâve , grand-goâve , and the city of léogâne . it is the closest town to the earthquake that struck haiti on 12 january 2010. the port town is about west of the haitian capital port-au-prince . léogâne is the birthplace of the taíno queen anacaona ( the town was originally called the amerindian name `` yaguana '' and the city 's name is a corruption of that ) and of marie-claire heureuse félicité , the wife of the haitian revolutionary jean-jacques dessalines ( 1758 ) .
