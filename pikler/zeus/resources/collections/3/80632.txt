Quraish (sura)    quraish ( sura ) surat quraysh ( arabic : سورة قريش ) ( after the tribe of quraysh ) is the 106th chapter of the qur'an . it is a makkan sura that consists of four ayat . it urges the quraysh tribe who dominated mecca to serve allah , who had protected them , for the sake of their own future . other websites . - quraish , قُرَیش - quraysh at sacred texts - quraysh at altafsir.com
