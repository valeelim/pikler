Bambi, A Life in the Woods    bambi , a life in the woods bambi , a life in the woods ( ) is a novel by austrian author felix salten . it was published in 1923. the story is about a young roe deer named bambi , and his life as he grows up . the book was made into a film called `` bambi '' by walt disney productions . the film is famous and received a midquel titled `` bambi ii '' in 2006. in the disney films , bambi was made a white-tailed deer . other websites . - bambi , the austrian deer , article by paul a. schons published in `` kulturecke '' by the germanic-american institute , september , 2000
