Teltow-Fläming     teltow-fläming is a `` kreis '' ( district ) in the southwestern part of brandenburg , germany . the district was made in december 1993 by joining the old districts of luckenwalde , jüterbog and zossen , but also including small parts from other former districts such as luckau . `` fläming-skate '' is a 160 km long route specially for inline skating , the only such route in germany . curiously , the town of teltow is not a part of the district teltow-fläming , just as the town of dahme is not a part of the district of dahme-spreewald . other websites . - official website - fläming-skate
