Viersen Rural District     viersen is a kreis ( district ) in the west of north rhine-westphalia , germany . in 1816 , the new prussian government created the district of `` kempen '' . in 1929 the district was made a lot bigger and renamed `` kempen-krefeld '' . in 1975 the district again changed its borders and was renamed to `` viersen '' even though kempen remained the capital . viersen city replace kempen as the capital in 1984. since 1983 the district has had been twinned with cambridgeshire in england . other websites . - official website ( german )
