Rudolph of France     rudolph ( also radulf , ralph , or raoul ) ( `` c. '' 890 – 15 january 936 ) was the duke of burgundy between 921 and 923. he was king of france from july 13 , 923 until his death . he married emma of france , daughter of robert i of france and béatrice of vermandois . the nobles elected him king of france after his father-in-law died .
