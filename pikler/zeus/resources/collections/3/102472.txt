Strapping Young Lad     strapping young lad was a metal band from vancouver , british columbia , canada . they played extreme metal . these people played in the band : devin townsend ( guitar , vocals ) , jed simon ( guitar , vocals ) , byron stoud ( bass , vocals ) , gene hoglan ( drums ) . the band went on an extended hiatus and would eventually disband in 2007. discography . - 1995 : `` heavy as a really heavy thing '' - 1997 : `` city '' - 2003 : `` strapping young lad '' - 2005 : `` alien '' - 2006 : `` the new black '' other websites . - official site
