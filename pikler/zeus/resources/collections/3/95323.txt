Quang Binh Province     quang binh ( in vietnamese : `` quảng bình '' ; hán tự : 廣平 ) is a province in the north central coast of vietnam . the province has the world heritage phong nha-ke bang national park . the province is the place of birth of several famous vietnamese persons , including general vo nguyen giap , the family of former south vietnamese president ngo dinh diem , poet han mac tu , and writer bao ninh . during the vietnam war , this province was severely devastated by bombing by u.s. b-52s .
