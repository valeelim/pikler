Virtual Console     virtual console ( vc ) is a wii , nintendo 3ds , and wii u service that lets users download old video games for wii points . the games are from the nintendo entertainment system , super nes , game boy , game boy color , nintendo 64 , sega master system , sega mega drive , turbografx-16 , turbografx-cd , neo geo aes , commodore 64 , and msx ( japan only ) . the service is available in europe , north america , australia , new zealand , japan , and south korea . other websites . - official virtual console site
