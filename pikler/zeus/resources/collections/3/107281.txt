Disney's Aladdin Junior    disney 's aladdin junior disney 's aladdin junior ( `` aladdin jr. '' ) is a musical . it is based on the 1992 disney animated movie , `` aladdin '' . the music was written by alan menken . the lyrics were written by howard ashman and tim rice . the musical is written to be performed by child actors . it is one of the musicals in the broadway junior series by music theatre international . other websites . - disney 's aladdin jr at broadwayjr.com
