Stade     stade is a city in lower saxony , germany . it is the seat of the district which is named the same as the city . the city was founded in 994 when it was called `` stethu '' . a city on the banks of the lower elbe it became an important market town , and a port . it was given a town charter in 1209. from 1645 to 1712 , the stade was controlled by sweden , who made it an important military town . on may 26 , 1659 , the `` great fire '' destroyed more than half the city .
