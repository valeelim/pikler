Smurfette     smurfette is the main protagonist in `` the smurfs '' franchise . is the only female smurf in the smurf village . most of the other smurfs like her because of her beauty . she is not the smartest smurf . often she gets caught in traps while looking at herself in the mirror . she does not seem to have an attraction for many of the other smurfs . later other female smurfs appeared , although it is unclear how they came into being .
