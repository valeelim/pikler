Singer Building     the singer building was a skyscraper in new york city , united states . it was 213 meters ( 700 feet ) tall and has 50 floors . it was built in 1908 and torn down in 1968 to make room for the u.s. steel building ( now one liberty plaza ) . the singer building was the tallest building ever demolished by a company . the singer building was the tallest building in the world from 1908 to 1909. before it was built , the tallest building in the world was philadelphia city hall . the metropolitan life insurance company tower became the tallest one year later . other websites . - emporis.com - singer building
