Bell UH-1 Iroquois     the uh-1 iroquois ( although known mainly as the huey ) was a helicopter made by bell helicopters . it first flew in 1956 and was introduced in 1959. it was known for being in the vietnam war . it could be used for transporting soldiers , cargo , support , helping lost soldiers or pilots , helping hurt people get to hospitals , or attacking enemies . one version , the uh-1v was made just for helping hurt people getting to hospitals . some hueys were given to civilians to help people get to hospitals . now , the uh-60 black hawk is mainly used , but the huey is still in service with countries .
