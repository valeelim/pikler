Adela Florence Nicolson     adela florence nicolson ( née cory ) ( 9 april 1865–4 october 1904 ) was an english poet who lived in india . she wrote under the pseudonym laurence hope . references . - bickley , francis l. and sayoni basu , `` nicolson [ née cory ] , adela florence [ pseud . laurence hope ] ( 1865-1904 ) , '' dictionary of national biography 2004 .
