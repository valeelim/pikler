Nawabshah District     nawabshah district ( ) ( or nawab shah ) is one of the districts in the province of sindh , pakistan , named after syed nawab shah . the syed family is a political power in local politics . the city 's airport has one of the largest runways in pakistan . the climate is dry and hot but sometimes falls to 0 degrees celsius in winter . local government . - daulatpur tehsil - nawabshah tehsil - sakrand tehsil - daur tehsil - qazi ahmed tehsil
