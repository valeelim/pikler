Beat-em up games     beat-em up games are video games where the player beats up bad guys while walking through levels . sometimes the player has to beat up certain bad guys to progress through the game . most of the time , these games have level bosses which are usually at the end of the level . examples of `` beat-em up '' games are arcade games like `` teenage mutant ninja turtles '' and `` the simpsons '' . `` super smash bros. brawl '' also has a `` beat-em up '' part in the game where the player fights bad guys from the subspace emissary and fights other characters as well in order to beat the levels .
