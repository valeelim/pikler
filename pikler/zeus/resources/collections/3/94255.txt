Da Nhay Beach     da nhay beach is a beach on the shore of south china sea . da nhay in vietnamese means `` the rocks that jump '' . the beach was called this because of the geographical appearance of it ; it has thousands rock reaching the sea and surrounding small fine sand beaches . it is found about 40 km north of đồng hới city , quang binh province , vietnam . other websites . - introduction of da nhay beach on official website of vietnam tourism authority
