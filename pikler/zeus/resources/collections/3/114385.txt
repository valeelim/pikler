Rudi Dutschke     rudi dutschke born alfred willi rudi dutschke ( march 7 , 1940 – december 24 , 1979 , århus , denmark ) was a spokesperson of the left-wing german student movement of the 1960s . he famously split from those who went on to form the violent red army faction . instead he wanted to have 'a long march through the institutions ' of power to create radical change from within government and society by becoming an important part of the machinery . this was an idea he took up from antonio gramsci and the frankfurt school of cultural marxism . in 1968 , he survived an assassination attempt which seriously injured him . he had to re-learn how to speak altogether . because of this attempt , he developed epilepsy . he drowned in his bath tub 12 years later . this was caused by an epileptic seizure .
