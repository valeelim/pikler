Patrick McLoughlin     patrick allen mcloughlin ( born november 30 , 1957 ) is an english politician and the current chief whip , which is the same job he had in opposition . he was first elected in 1986 in a by-election as the member of parliament for the west derbyshire constituency in the united kingdom , and since 2010 for its successor , the derbyshire dales constituency . he is a member of the conservative party . in july 2016 , prime minister theresa may made mcloughlin chancellor of the duchy of lancaster and chair of the conservative party .
