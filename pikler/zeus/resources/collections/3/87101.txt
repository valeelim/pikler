Gustav Heinemann     gustav walter heinemann ( born 23 july 1899 in schwelm ; died 7 july 1976 in essen ) was a german politician . he was interior minister under chancellor konrad adenauer . he resigned when adenauer started getting ready to set up the german army . he later resigned from the cdu and joined the spd . he also served as justice minister before he was elected federal president in 1969. he served for one term , and left office in 1974 .
