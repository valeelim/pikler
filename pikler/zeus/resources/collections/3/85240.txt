Ned Flanders     nedward `` ned '' flanders is a character in the animated television series `` the simpsons '' . ned flanders and his son todd first appeared in the season one episode `` simpsons roasting on an open fire '' , which was the series first episode . the first episode in which flanders and his family were seen is season two 's `` dead putting society '' , which also showed the first appearance of maude and rod flanders . flanders was named after flanders st. in portland , oregon , the hometown of `` simpsons '' creator matt groening . groening wanted flanders as `` just a guy who was truly nice , that homer had no justifiable reason to loathe , but then did . '' it was not until after the first few episodes that it was decided flanders would be a faithful christian . mike scully said that flanders is `` everything homer would love to be , although he 'll never admit it . '' flanders had originally been meant to just be a neighbor that homer was jealous of , but harry shearer used `` such a sweet voice '' and flanders was planned to become a christian and a sweet guy that someone would choose to live next to over homer .
