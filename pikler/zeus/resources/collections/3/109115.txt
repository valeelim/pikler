Children of the Mind     children of the mind ( 1996 ) is the fourth , and currently the last , book of orson scott card 's popular `` ender 's game '' series , a series of science fiction novels that focus on the character ender wiggin . although card has written some additional novels that take place with in the ender saga , they take place in the middle of the storyline and therefore are not sequals to `` children of the mind '' . other websites . - about the book `` children of the mind '' from card 's website
