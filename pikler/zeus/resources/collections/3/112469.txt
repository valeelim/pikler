Assembly of Kosovo     the assembly of kosovo ( , `` skup≈°tina kosova '' ) is an institution linked to governing the territory of kosovo . the assembly was created by the united nations to provide for a democratic form of government . the assembly has 120 seats . 100 seats are filled with people directly elected into the assembly . the remaining 20 are always assigned as follows : - 10 seats for the representatives of the serbs . - 4 seats for the representatives of the roma , ashkali and egyptians . - 3 seats for the bosniaks . - 2 seats for the turks . - 1 seat for the gorans .
