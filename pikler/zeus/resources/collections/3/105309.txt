Jeremy Hunt     jeremy hunt , pc , mp ( born november 1 , 1966 ) is an english politician . he was born in kennington , london . he is the member of parliament for south west surrey and the secretary of state for health . he was first elected in the 2005 general election . he gained a bachelor of arts degree in philosophy , politics and economics from magdalen college , oxford . he is a member of the conservative party . he was appointed as a privy counsellor on 13 may 2010 .
