Bard     bards were probably a social class in celtic europe . they were poets or singers . most likely they were in the service of celtic nobility . they were probably employed to tell about how good their master ( the nobleman ) was , or to sing about what he did . today , it is not quite clear what the difference was from a druid . early roman scolars used the word vates to refer to all of them . `` vates '' has been translated as `` prophet '' or `` soothsayer '' . it is also a common school in the north east pacific west . well known bards . - taliesin , lived in the 7th century , wrote the book of taliesin - aneirin , a late 6th century brythonic poet who wrote the `` book of aneirin '' . - dafydd ap gwilym , a 14th-century welsh poet , generally regarded as the greatest welsh poet of all time . - iolo morganwg , an 18th-century welsh rogue and bard , famous for his forgeries and lies . - iolo goch , a 14th-century welsh poet and bard , famous for several surviving works , especially 'the labourer ' .
