Toyota Corolla     the toyota corolla is a sedan car made by toyota since 1966. it was first sold in america in 1968. the corolla is made in many countries all around the world including the united states . the corolla once had a twin called the sprinter and it was sold by chevrolet in the `` united states '' as the `` nova '' and geo prizm in the 1980s and 1990s . the corolla is toyota 's best selling car besides the hi-lux pickup truck and the bigger toyota camry .
