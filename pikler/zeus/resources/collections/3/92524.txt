Märkisch-Oderland     märkisch-oderland is a `` kreis '' ( district ) in the eastern part of brandenburg , germany . much of the district is mainly agricultural land , but there is a swampy area along the oder called the `` oderbruch '' . the oderbruch is about in length and in width . it was partially drained in the 18th century , and people started to live in the new area . the districts of lebus and oberbarnim were both created in 1816. in 1952 the districts were split into three parts , the districts bad freienwalde , seelow and strausberg . in 1993 the three parts were joined together to form the current district . other websites . - official website
