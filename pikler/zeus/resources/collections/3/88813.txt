Epsilon     epsilon ( uppercase/lowercase ε ε ) , is the letter of the greek alphabet , used to represent the `` e '' sound in ancient and modern greek . in the system of greek numerals , it has a value of 5. letters that came from it include the roman e and cyrillic е. it is also used in calculus to represent a very small number . in set theory it can be used to represent the statement `` x is a member of y .
