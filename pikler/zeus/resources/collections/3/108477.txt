Menopause     menopause means the natural and permanent stopping of menstruation . female hormone levels drop , since they were produced by the ovaries which have now stopped working . the menopause starts when the ovaries stop releasing eggs every month . this can occur during old age or because the eggs have had surgery . due to the change in hormone levels , women get hot flushes and palpitations , depression , anxiety , irritability , mood swings and lack of concentration .
