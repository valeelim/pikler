Real Irish Republican Army     the real irish republican army ( or real ira ) is a group from ireland who want northern ireland to become part of the republic of ireland , and want it to leave the united kingdom . the group started after they left the provisional irish republican army , after an argument . on 15 august 1998 they did the omagh bombing , which killed 29 people and hurt over 300 people . they have shot police officers and have also bombed places in england , such as london and birmingham . it is against the law to be a member of the real ira .
