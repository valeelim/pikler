Hard water     hard water is water that has a high mineral content ( contrast with `` soft water '' ) . the main components of these minerals usually are calcium ( ca ) and magnesium ( mg ) ions . but there are also dissolved metals , bicarbonates , and sulfates . calcium usually enters the water as either calcium carbonate ( caco ) in the form of limestone and chalk , or calcium sulfate ( caso ) in the form of several other mineral deposits . the main source of magnesium is dolomite ( camg ( co ) ) . hard water is generally not harmful and some hardness in water is a useful source of calcium for healthy bone development . other websites . - alcoa chemical , langelier saturation index ( lsi ) calculator
