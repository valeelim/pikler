Social status     in sociology or anthropology , social status is the honor or rank of one 's position in society . people earn social status by their own work . this is call `` achieved status '' . or , people can have a place in a social system by birth . this inherited position is called `` ascribed status '' . further reading . - michael marmot ( 2004 ) , `` the status syndrome : how social standing affects our health and longevity '' , times books - botton , alain de ( 2004 ) , `` status anxiety '' , hamish hamilton - social status .
