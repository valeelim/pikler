William H. Macy     william hall macy , jr. ( born march 13 , 1950 ) is an emmy award-winning and academy award-nominated american actor . he was in the movie `` fargo '' . he is also a teacher and director in theatre , movie and television . macy has described his screen persona as `` sort of a middle american , waspy , lutheran kind of guy ... everyman '' . he is now known for his award-winning role as frank gallagher in the television series `` shameless '' . other websites . - william h. macy at tv.com
