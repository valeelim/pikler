Saarlouis District     saarlouis is a kreis ( district ) in the middle of the saarland , germany . the district was part of the holy roman empire . after the napoleonic wars the area went to prussia , which in 1816 created the district as part of its rhineland province . from 1936-1945 it was called `` saarlautern '' , when the nazi government tried to exchange the french name for a german one . the main river in the district is the saar . other websites . - official website ( german )
