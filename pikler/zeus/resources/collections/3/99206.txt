Rawalpindi District     rawalpindi district ( ) is a district in the punjab province of pakistan . the city of rawalpindi is the capital . the district is divided into seven tehsils , these are : - gujar khan tehsil - kahuta tehsil - kallar syedan tehsil - kotli sattian tehsil - murree tehsil - rawalpindi tehsil - taxila tehsil in 1901 punjab census , the sikhs numbered 26,363 , hindus 57,325 , and the muslims being 466,918 83 % . but between 1901-1931 , there was a large number of hindus converting to sikhism . by 1947 , there would have been more than 100,000 sikhs in this district , and also the population growing .
