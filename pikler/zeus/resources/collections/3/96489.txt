Adams, Green County, Wisconsin    adams , green county , wisconsin adams is a town in green county , wisconsin , united states . there were 464 people living in adams as of the 2000 census . the town has an area of 36.2 square miles , thanks to the united states census bureau . the first settlers were cornish lead miners . they were followed by settlers from new england , ireland , switzerland , and norway . the town has been the home of a lutheran and a catholic parish . saint francis catholic church was made by catholic immigrants from ireland . later , the parish joined with saint joseph catholic church in argyle , wisconsin . the church was later torn down . only the cemetery is still there .
