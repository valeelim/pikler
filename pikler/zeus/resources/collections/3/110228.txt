Victoria Island (Canada)    victoria island ( canada ) victoria island is an island in the canadian arctic archipelago and the border between nunavut and the northwest territories runs through the island . it is the 9th biggest island of the world , and at is canada 's second largest island and nearly double the size of newfoundland ( or only slightly bigger than the size of the island of great britain ) . the western third of the island belongs to the inuvik region in the northwest territories and the rest is part of nunavut 's kitikmeot region . as of 2006 , 1,875 live on victoria island . most of the people ( 1,477 ) live in its biggest city , cambridge bay .
