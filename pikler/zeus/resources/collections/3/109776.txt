2008 WWE Draft     the 2008 world wrestling entertainment ( wwe ) draft was the fifth yearly ( and sixth overall ) draft held by wwe . the draft was held on june 23 , 2008 in san antonio , texas from the at & t center . the draft took place live for three hours on wwe 's flagship program , `` raw '' , which aired on the usa network . the draft was announced by wwe chairman , vince mcmahon on the may 26 2008 episode of `` raw '' . during the announcement , he stated that every wwe superstar , diva , and commentators were eligible to be drafted . related pages . - wwe brand extension - wwe draft lottery - wwe raw
