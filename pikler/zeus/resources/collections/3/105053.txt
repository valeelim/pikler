Megawati Sukarnoputri     diah permata megawati setiawati soekarnoputri ( born january 23 , 1947 ) , was president of indonesia from july 23 , 2001 to october 20 , 2004. she was the country 's first female president , and the first indonesian leader born after independence . on september 20 , 2004 , she lost her campaign for re-election in the 2004 indonesian presidential election . she is the daughter of indonesia 's first president , sukarno .
