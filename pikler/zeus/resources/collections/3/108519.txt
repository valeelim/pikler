Province of Enna     enna is a province in the autonomous region of sicily in italy . it is the only sicilian province which is not next to the ocean . it became a province in 1926 thanks to benito mussolini , who was fascinated by the history of the province , and in particular by the legend of proserpina . in the province there are 8 lakes , the most important is pergusa 's lake which is the only natural lake of the sicily ; the other lakes are used for the agriculture .
