Team Lotus     team lotus was the motorsport sister company of english sports car manufacturer lotus cars . the team ran cars in many motorsport series including formula one , formula two , formula ford , formula junior , indycar and sports car racing . more than ten years after its last race team lotus remains one of the most successful racing teams of all time , winning seven formula one constructors ' titles , six drivers ' championships , and the indianapolis 500 in the united states , between 1962 and 1978. under the direction of founder and chief designer colin chapman lotus was responsible for many innovative and experimental developments in motorsport , in both technical and commercial arenas . in 2010 lotus has returned to f1 as lotus racing .
