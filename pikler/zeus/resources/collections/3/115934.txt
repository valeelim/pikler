Deval Patrick     deval patrick ( born july 31 , 1956 ) is an american politician . he was elected to be the governor of massachusetts in 2006. he is the first african-american to be elected governor of that state , and only the third african-american to be elected a governor of any state in the united states . patrick ran as a democrat . he was born in chicago , illinois on 31 july 1956 , is a lawyer , and holds two degrees from harvard university . other websites . - deval patrick for governor official campaign site - national governors association - massachusetts governor deval patrick biography - transcript of deval patrick 's acceptance speech `` the boston globe '' , november 8 , 2006
