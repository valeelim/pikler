Dark Angel (TV series)    dark angel ( tv series ) dark angel is an american science fiction television series created by james cameron and charles h. eglee , which ran from 2000 to 2002 on the fox network . `` dark angel '' tells the story of the character of `` max guevara '' ( x5-452 ) , a genetically enhanced super-soldier , portrayed by jessica alba as an adult , and geneva locke as a child . the program is set in 2019 in seattle , washington , but was filmed in vancouver , british columbia , canada at lions gate studios .
