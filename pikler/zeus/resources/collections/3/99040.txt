Optical disc drive     an optical drive is usually a cd drive or dvd drive . optical means it uses lenses . the drive uses a light called a laser . a laser is the most exact and powerful sort of light but the laser in the drive is very , very small . the cd or dvd disk is similar to a mirror and the laser light reflects off it . the disk has very small ( ) codes written on it . the drive has a very small camera lens beside the laser which can read the codes . another part of the drive is plugged in to the computer by a wire ( inside ) and the code goes through the wire as a signal to the computer . list of optical drives . - cd drive - dvd drive - blu-ray disc drive - hd-dvd drive
