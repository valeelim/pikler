Tunceli Province     tunceli is a province in the eastern part of turkey . the province was named dersim before 1936. the word dersim means silver door in the kurdish language . its capital is also called tunceli . tunceli has one of the highest literacy rates in turkey . in 1979 and 1980 , tunceli has the highest number of students entering to universities . however , after the high schools were converted to military bases , tunceli could not get this achievement again . districts . - nazimiye - hozat - mazgirt - pertek - pülümür - ovacık
