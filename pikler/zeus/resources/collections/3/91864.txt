Jim Root     james `` jim '' root ( born october 7 , 1971 ) is an american musician . he is the guitarist for both slipknot and stone sour . in slipknot he is named # 4. he is the tallest member of the band at 6 foot 6. jim wears a court jester 's mask with a zip-up mouth . over time he has sweated , snotted and puked in it so much that it makes everywhere it goes smell bad . jim enjoys fishing and playing video games .
