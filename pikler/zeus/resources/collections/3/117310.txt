Gwadar City     gwadar is on the southwestern coast of pakistan , on the sea of oman . it is between three increasingly important regions : the oil-rich middle east , heavily populated south asia and the economically emerging and resource-laden region of central asia . the gwadar port is expected to generate billions of dollars in revenues and create at least two million jobs . in 2007 , the government of pakistan handed over port operations to psa singapore for 25 years , and gave it the status of a tax free port for the following 40 years . there is also money invested into the port by the people 's republic of china . the strategic prc plan to be engaged in many places along oil and gas roads is evident . government and ngo links . - real time news and development , central asia economy watch , website sponsored by gda , pak
