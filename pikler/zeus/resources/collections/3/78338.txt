5 (number)    5 ( number ) the number five is a number that comes after four and before the number six . in roman numerals , it is v. five is the third prime number , after two and three , and before seven . the number five is also an odd number . most people have five fingers on each hand and five toes on each foot . it is not known for certain who and how the shape of the number five was created , but most people think it was made by the brahmin indians .
