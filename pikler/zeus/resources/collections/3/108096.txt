Mensa International     mensa international ( or just mensa ) is the largest , oldest and most famous high-iq group of people in the world . the non-profit organization only has members with high iqs . they must score at the 98th percentile or higher on a standardized , supervised intelligence test . in many cases the iq you have to have to join is 130. related pages . - list of mensans - intertel - triple nine society other websites . - mensa education and research foundation - mensa for kids - example of tests similar to mensa tests
