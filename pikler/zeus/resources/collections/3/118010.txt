Awake (movie)    awake ( movie ) awake is a 2007 american crime thriller movie . it was written and directed by joby harold . it stars hayden christensen , jessica alba , lena olin , and terrence howard . the movie was rated r by the mpaa for language , an intense disturbing situation , and brief drug use . the movie was released in the united states and canada on november 30 , 2007 .
