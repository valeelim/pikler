The Icemark Chronicles     the icemark chronicles is a series of books consisting of `` the cry of the icemark '' , followed by `` blade of fire '' , and `` last battle of the icemark '' . the books were written by stuart hill from leicester . fox 2000 pictures have bought the right to create a movie based on the books . the first is planned for 2008. books . - `` the cry of the icemark '' - `` blade of fire '' - `` last battle of the icemark ''
