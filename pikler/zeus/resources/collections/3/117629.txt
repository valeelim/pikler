Wail al-Shehri     wail al-shehri ( وائل الشهري , also transliterated alshehri ) was named by the fbi as one of the hijackers of american airlines flight 11 from boston to los angeles that crashed into the world trade center as part of the september 11 , 2001 attacks . his brother , waleed al-shehri , is also a suspected 9/11 hijacker .
