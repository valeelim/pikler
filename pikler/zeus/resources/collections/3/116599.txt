Le Mans Cathedral     the cathedral of le mans is a cathedral built in the gothic style in the town of le mans , france . it is dedicated to saint julien ; he was the first bishop of le mans ; he is also buried in the cathedral . it is somewhat unusual in the west . it is often compared to those of reims and chartres . its building was influenced a lot by the cathedral of bourges . it is also special because its nave is built in romanesque style , but the choir is built in gothic style .
