Birmingham Small Arms Company     the birmingham small arms company ( bsa ) was a british company that started making guns and bicycles in the 19th century . from 1910 the company started making motorcycles . at one time , it was the most popular maker of motorcycles in the world . over half a million of the company 's most popular model , the bantam , were sold . the company went out of business in the 1970s . it has started again as a gun making company .
