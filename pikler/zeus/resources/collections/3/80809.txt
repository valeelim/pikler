Fibre     is a piece of material that is long , thin and flexible , like a length of thread . plant fibres are the basis of fabric such as cotton . silk and wool fibres come from animals . in the 20th century many artificial fibres were invented like nylon and polyester . they are very important in the structure of plants and animals , because they hold tissues together . there are many uses for fibres . they can be spun together into filaments , thread , string or rope . they can be woven in fabric or in composite materials . they can also be matted into sheets to make products such as paper or felt ( as felt hats ) .synthetic fibres are the cheapest to make . related pages . - textile - optical fiber - tensile strength
