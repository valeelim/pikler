My Trach Massacre     the my trach massacre was the murder of many people in my trach village , my thuy commune , le thuy district , quang binh province , vietnam . the massacre took place on 29 november 1947 between 2:00 a.m. and 12:00 p.m. soldiers of the french army killed nearly half of the 320 people who lived in the village . many of the civilians who were killed were women , old people and children .
