Aircraft registration     an aircraft registration is a unique alphanumeric string that identifies an aircraft , in similar fashion to a licence plate on an automobile . in accordance with the convention on international civil aviation all aircraft must be registered with a national authority ( such as the federal aviation administration or transport canada ) , and furthermore , they must carry proof of this registration in the form of a legal document called a certificate of registration at all times when in operation . most countries also require the aircraft registration to be imprinted on a permanent fireproof plate mounted on the fuselage for the purposes of post-fire/post-crash aircraft accident investigation .
