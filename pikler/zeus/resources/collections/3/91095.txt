Samuel Wesley     samuel wesley ( born bristol , 24 february 1766 ; died london , 11 october 1837 ) was an english organist and composer samuel wesley was the son of methodist hymn-writer charles wesley , the grandson of the poet samuel wesley and the nephew of john wesley , the founder of the methodist church . his illegitimate son samuel sebastian wesley was the greatest english composer of church music of the 19th century . samuel played the violin as well as the organ , and worked as a conductor as well as a music lecturer . he lived at a time when most english composers were writing sentimental music which was not very important . wesley was one of the best composers of his time .
