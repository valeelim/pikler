Tom and Jerry: The Magic Ring    tom and jerry : the magic ring tom and jerry : the magic ring is a 2002 movie that stars tom and jerry . it is the first tom and jerry movie to only be released only on video and never in a movie theatre . also , it was the final cartoon and the last tom and jerry cartoon to be co-executive produced by both , william hanna and joseph barbera , as hanna died in march 22 , 2001 , a few days before the movie 's release .
