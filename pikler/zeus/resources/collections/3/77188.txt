Grumman F4F Wildcat     the f4f wilcat was a fighter aircraft made by grumman . it first flew in 1937 , and was introduced in 1940. it was used in the pacific during the early years of world war ii . it could take off from aircraft carriers . during the war , it mainly fought the a6m zero . because the wildcat was not able to beat the zero , a new airplane was made called the f6f hellcat . the wildcat was retired in 1945 .
