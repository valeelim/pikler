James Kennedy     dennis james kennedy ( or d. james kennedy , november 3 , 1930 - september 5 , 2007 ) was an american televangelist and megachurch pastor . he started coral ridge presbyterian church in fort lauderdale , florida and was the senior pastor there from 1960 until his death in 2007. he began coral ridge ministries in 1974. other websites . - coral ridge presbyterian church website - coral ridge ministries website - d. james kennedy 's memorial tribute website - truths that transform daily radio broadcast on oneplace.com
