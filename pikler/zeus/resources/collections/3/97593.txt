Sonic the Hedgehog 2     sonic the hedgehog 2 , also called sonic 2 , is second video game in `` sonic the hedgehog '' series . it is sequel to `` sonic the hedgehog '' . it was made by sonic team and published by sega for the sega mega drive in 1992. unlike the first game , it has a half-pipe special stage and has seven chaos emeralds ( see `` chaos emerald '' ) instead of six . it also introduces tails .
