Osnabrück (district)    osnabrück ( district ) osnabrück ( ) is a district ( `` landkreis '' ) in lower saxony , germany . it is near the districts of emsland , cloppenburg , vechta and diepholz , the state of north rhine-westphalia ( districts of minden-lübbecke , herford , gütersloh , warendorf and steinfurt ) and the city of osnabrück . the district is almost the same as the prince-bishopric of osnabrück which existed until 1803. when the clerical states of germany were dissolved , osnabrück became part of the kingdom of hanover , which in turn fell to prussia in 1866. the district was established in 1972 by merging four old districts ( osnabrück , melle , bersenbrück , wittlage ) . the landscape is characterised by two mountain chains : the wiehengebirge in the north and the northern foothills of the teutoburg forest in the south . these chains go around the city of osnabrück . other websites . - official website ( german , english , dutch , polish , russian )
