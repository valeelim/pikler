Chuck Rayner     claude earl `` chuck '' rayner ( august 11 , 1920 - october 6 , 2002 ) was a canadian professional ice hockey goaltender . he played nine seasons in the national hockey league ( nhl ) . he played for the brooklyn americans and new york rangers . he was inducted into the hockey hall of fame in 1973. awards & achievements . - turnbull cup mjhl championship ( 1940 ) - ahl second all-star team ( 1941 ) - nhl second all-star team ( 1949 , 1950 , & 1951 ) - hart memorial trophy winner ( 1950 ) - played in the nhl all-star game ( 1949 , 1950 , & 1951 ) - inducted into the hockey hall of fame in 1973 - “ honoured member ” of the manitoba hockey hall of fame other websites . - chuck rayner 's biography at manitoba hockey hall of fame
