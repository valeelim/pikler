Hessischer Rundfunk     the hessischer rundfunk ( hr ) is a german television and radio broadcasting company based in frankfurt am main in the state of hesse . hr is a member of the group of german public-broadcasting institutions that makes up ard . the hr produces the regional television service `` hessenfernsehen '' ( which used to be called hessen3 ) and six regional radio channels . other websites . - official website `` in german ''
