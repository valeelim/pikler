Live and Acoustic at Park Ave.     live and acoustic at park ave. is the second ep by alternative rock band snow patrol . it was released in 2005. all of the songs are played live and are taken from one performance . track listing . 1 . `` spitting games '' 1 . `` how to be dead '' 1 . `` grazed knees '' 1 . `` chocolate '' 1 . `` run ''
