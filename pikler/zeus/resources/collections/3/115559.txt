Toyota Yaris     the toyota yaris is a small car made and sold by toyota since 1999. it is sold as the toyota belta , toyota vios , and toyota vitz . the yaris is sold as a 3-door and 5-door hatchback and 4-door sedan . it is currently in its third generation . the first generation yaris was known as the toyota echo in some markets . a distinctive feature of the first generation in many markets was a digital instrument display which glows out of a black hole in the centre of the dashboard . the second generation model arrived in 2005 and the current third generation has been on sale since 2011 .
