University of Toronto     the university of toronto is a university in toronto , ontario , canada . it was created by the king of the united kingdom in 1827 , many years before canada became a country . the university is made up of 12 different colleges where students live and study . these colleges all have their own buildings , dining halls and libraries . some colleges relate to christian churches , but other colleges are not religious . the university also has hospitals and many places for research . in teaching , the university of toronto is known for its ideas about literature and communication . it is also the place where insulin and stem cells were first found . its people built the first electron microscope , found the first black hole , and began a touchscreen technology called multi-touch . it is the university that has the most money and research work in canada . the university 's sports teams are called the varsity blues , and they have very long histories in american football and ice hockey . the university of toronto has been ranked as one of the top universities in the world .
