Édouard Daladier    édouard daladier édouard daladier ( 18 june 1884 - 10 october 1970 ) was a french socialist politician , and prime minister of france at the start of the second world war . he was one of the politicians who tried to stop the war at the munich conference in 1938 , together with the british prime minister , neville chamberlain and the two dictators of germany and italy , adolf hitler and benito mussolini .
