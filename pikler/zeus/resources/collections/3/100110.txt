Moe Szyslak     moe szyslak is a fictional character on the long running tv show the simpsons . his voice is performed by hank azaria . he is the bartender of moe 's tavern . he speaks with a rough voice . moe is the owner and only worker at moe 's tavern . on some occasions he has had temporary help . the most notable of these occasions being in `` homer the moe '' . his two most loyal customers are barney gumble and homer simpson . other websites . - the moe szyslak file at the simpsons archive
