Francis Maude     francis anthony aylmer maude ( born 4 july 1953 ) is an english politician and the current minister for the cabinet office and paymaster general . he is the member of parliament for the horsham constituency in the united kingdom . he was first elected in the 1983 general election for the north warwickshire constituency but lost in 1992. he returned to parliament in the 1997 general election . maude was born in abingdon-on-thames , berkshire . he is a member of the conservative party .
