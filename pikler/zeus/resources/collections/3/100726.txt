Pskov     pskov ( , ancient russian spelling пльсковъ ( `` pleskov '' ) ) is an old city in the northwest of russia . it is about east of the estonian border , on the velikaya river . pskov is twinned with the following cities : - tartu , estonia - arles , france - gera , germany - kuopio , finland - neuss , germany - nijmegen , netherlands - paderborn , germany - perth , scotland - roanoke , united states other websites . - official website of the city of pskov administration
