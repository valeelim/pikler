Greek Orthodox Church of Alexandria     the orthodox church of alexandria ( greek : ) is one of the eastern orthodox churches . it is sometimes called the greek orthodox patriarchate of alexandria to distinguish it from the coptic orthodox patriarchate of alexandria . in egypt , members of the greek orthodox patriarchate were also known as melkite , because they remained in communion with the ecumenical patriarch of constantinople after the schism that followed the council of chalcedon in 451 .
