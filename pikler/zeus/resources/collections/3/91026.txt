Bad Wörishofen     bad wörishofen is a spa town in the district unterallgäu , bavaria germany . it is known for the water-cure of sebastian kneipp ( 1821 – 1897 ) , a catholic priest , who was living there for 42 years . many hotels and boarding-houses are in the town where guests can be treated using kneipp 's methods . there is a new spa complex out of town , called therme bad wörishofen . cult film director rainer werner fassbinder was a native of bad wörishofen .
