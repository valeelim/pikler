Friedrich Rittelmeyer     friedrich rittelmeyer ( 5 october 1872 – 23 march 1938 ) was a german protestant theologian . he was born in dillingen an der donau , bavaria . from 1895 to 1922 he worked as a lutheran pastor in würzburg , nuremberg and berlin . in 1922 he became the founder ( and leader up to his death ) of the christian community , a christian denomination , inspired by anthroposophy . he died in hamburg .
