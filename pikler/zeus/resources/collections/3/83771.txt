Frank Iero     frank anthony thomas `` frank '' iero , jr. ( born october 31 , 1981 ) is the youngest member of the band my chemical romance . he was born in belleville , new jersey and attended queen of peace high school in north arlington . he attended rutgers university on scholarship but then dropped out for the band after he felt they were beginning to get somewhere . iero is a rhythm guitarist and is one of the two backup vocalists for the alternative rock band my chemical romance . he is also in leathermouth .
