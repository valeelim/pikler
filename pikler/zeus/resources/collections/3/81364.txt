Camptosaurus     camptosaurus ( meaning `` bent lizard '' ) was a plant-eater from the upper jurassic ( about 156 to 145 million years ago ) . it is related to iguanodon . it was a heavy ornithischian dinosaur that was about 16-23 feet ( 5-7 m ) long and 3-4 feet ( 1 m ) high at the hips , weighing roughly 2,200 pounds ( 1000 kg ) . it had a long snout , hundreds of teeth and a horny beak . its legs were longer than its arms ; it had four-toed feet and five-fingered arms , all with hooves . it could walk on two or four legs , it probably went on all four to graze for low-lying plants .
