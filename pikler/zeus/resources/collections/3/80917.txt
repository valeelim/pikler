Helike (moon)    helike ( moon ) helike or ' , is a moon of jupiter . it was found by a team of astronomers from the university of hawaii led by scott s. sheppard in 2003 , and given the designation ' . helike is about 4 kilometres in diameter , and orbits jupiter at an average distance of 20,540,000 km in 601.402 earth days , at an inclination of 155° to the ecliptic ( 156° to jupiter 's equator ) , with an orbital eccentricity of 0.1375. it was named in march 2005 after helike , one of the nymphs that nurtured zeus ( jupiter ) in his infancy on crete . helike belongs to the ananke group , retrograde non-spherical moons which orbit jupiter between 19,300,000 and 22,700,000 km , at inclinations of about 150° .
