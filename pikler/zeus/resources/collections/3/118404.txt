Gerry Anderson     gerry anderson mbe ( 14 april 1929 - 26 december 2012 ) was a british television and movie producer , director and writer of jewish origin . he is famous for the science fiction television programmes he created . the most well known of these was `` thunderbirds '' . most of the programmes he created used marionettes . he also worked with puppeteer christine glanville . he was married to actress and producer sylvia anderson .
