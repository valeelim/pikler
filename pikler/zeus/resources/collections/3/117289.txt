Spartak Tennis Club     the spartak tennis club is a tennis training ground near sokolniki park in moscow , russia . it has 15 outdoor clay courts , which remain closed for half of the year , and only one indoor court . although the facilties are getting old , many top russian tennis players trained there . these include yevgeny kafelnikov , marat safin , elena dementieva , dinara safina , anastasia myskina , and anna kournikova are all graduates of spartak .
