Gunpei Yokoi     , also romanized as gumpei yokoi , was a video game designer for nintendo . he created the game boy and the produced the `` metroid '' series of video games . he was also the mentor to shigeru miyamoto . yokoi 's last invention , the virtual boy , was not a success . he left nintendo and started a company called koto laboratory . his main product idea at this company was the wonderswan , which came out in 1999 , two years after he died .
