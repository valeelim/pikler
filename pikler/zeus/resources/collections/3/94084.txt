Julie Taymor     julie taymor ( born december 15 , 1952 ) is an american director of broadway theatre and movies . taymor 's work has received lots of praise from critics . she has won two tony awards out of four nominations , an emmy award , and an academy award nomination for her work , known for its visual flair and colorful costuming choices . she lives in new york city with her partner elliot goldenthal , who she has been with for over 20 years . filmography . - `` the tempest '' ( 1986 ) - `` fool 's fire '' ( 1992 ) ( tv ) - `` oedipus rex '' ( 1993 ) ( tv ) - `` titus '' ( 1999 ) - `` frida '' ( 2002 ) - `` across the universe '' ( 2007 )
