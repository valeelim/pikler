Saccharide     saccharide can mean different things : - `` saccharides '' are the anions and salts of the sweetener saccharin ( `` sodium saccharide '' , `` denatonium saccharide '' ) . saccharin is sometimes used to replace sugar . - `` saccharide '' is a general synonym for sugars . common kinds of sugar are monosaccharide , disaccharide , and polysaccharide .
