Decision-making     decision making is the mental process that leads to the selection of an action among several alternatives . every decision making process produces a final choice . the output can be an action or an opinion . there is a growing awareness that people often make good decisions rapidly without knowing how they do it . this runs against the older rational decision-making ideas . related pages . - adaptive unconscious - forecasting - risk other websites . - emotional and decision making lab , carnegie mellon , edm lab - the de borda institute - emerson , p j . `` beyond the tyranny of the majority '' , a comparison of the more common voting procedures used in both decision making and elections . - decision analysis in health care - an online course from george mason university providing free lectures and tools for decision making in health care . - decidebetter ! - an online resource to help make better decisions in your everyday lives .
