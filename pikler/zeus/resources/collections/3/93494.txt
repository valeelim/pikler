Freddy Cannon     freddy `` boom boom '' cannon ( born december 4 , 1940 ) is an american singer . he was most famous in the early 1960s . his hit records include `` abigail beecher '' , `` tallahassee lassie '' , and `` way down yonder in new orleans '' . one of cannon 's hits , `` palisades park '' , was written by chuck barris , who later created `` the dating game '' and `` the gong show '' . cannon also appeared in the movies `` just for fun '' and `` village of the giants '' .
