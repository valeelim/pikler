Lord Brocktree     lord brocktree is a fantasy book written by brian jacques in 2000. it is the thirteenth book in the `` redwall '' series . lord brocktree was also translated into french . a badger lord named brocktree is a strong badger with a mighty sword . with the help of friends lord brocktree takes back the mountain fortress of salamandastron from ungatt trunn the wildcat warlord and his vermin horde .
