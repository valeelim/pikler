Layyah District     layyah district ( ) is a district in the punjab province of pakistan . the city of layyah is the capital . layyah district shares borders with the districts of bhakkar ( north ) , muzaffargarh ( south ) , jhang ( east ) and d.g . khan ( west ) . the district is of semi rectangular shape and has sandy land . the total area of the district is 6291 km . it is 88 km from east to west & 75 km from north to south . it is made up of the three tehsils of : 1. layyah tehsil 1. chaubara tehsil 1. karor lal esan tehsil layyah district has an extremely hot climate . the temperature in winter is low because it is near the koh-suleman mountains . the tehsil chaubara is almost barren and is made up of forest and sand dunes . the tehsil of layyah and karor lal esan are better developed agriculturally but they have also large areas of sand dunes and uncultivated land . the river indus passes from north to south on western side of the district and touches d.g . khan . other websites . - map of layyah
