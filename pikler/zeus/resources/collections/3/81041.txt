Haslen     haslen is a municipality of the canton of glarus in switzerland . on july 1 , 2006 , haslen merged with the municipalities of leuggelbach and nidfurn . on 1 january 2011 the former municipalities of haslen , betschwanden , braunwald , elm , engi , linthal , luchsingen , matt , switzerland , mitlödi , rüti , schwanden , schwändi and sool merged into the new municipality of glarus süd . other websites . - official website
