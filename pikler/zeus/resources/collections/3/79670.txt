Pascagoula, Mississippi    pascagoula , mississippi pascagoula is a city in jackson county , mississippi , united states . it is the main city of the pascagoula , mississippi metropolitan statistical area , as a part of the gulfport-biloxi-pascagoula , mississippi combined statistical area . the population was 22,392 at the 2010 census . it is the county seat of jackson county .
