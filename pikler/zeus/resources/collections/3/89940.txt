IG Farben     ig farben ( short for interessen-gemeinschaft farbenindustrie ag ) , was a german conglomerate of companies formed in 1925 – including basf , agfa and hoechst – and even earlier during world war i . `` farben '' is german for `` paints '' , `` dyes '' , or `` colors '' , and initially many of these companies produced dyes , but soon began to embrace more advanced chemistry . the founding of ig farben was a reaction to germany 's defeat in world war i. ig farben held a near total monopoly on chemical production , later during the nazi regime , including manufacturing zyklon b .
