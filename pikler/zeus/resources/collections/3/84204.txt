George Beauchamp     george d. beauchamp ( 1899 - 1941 ) was an inventor of musical instruments and co-founder of national stringed instrument corporation and rickenbacker . born in texas , he played the violin and the lap steel guitar in vaudeville before going into the business of then manufacturing of electric lap steel guitars , electric guitars , electric bass guitars , electric violins and combo instrument amplifiers . he is well known for his creation of the frying pan electric guitar . he died in los angeles , california while deep-sea fishing . other websites . - the earliest days of the electric guitar - which came first- electric guitar or amp ? - digital violin - article examining beauchamp 's electro violins
