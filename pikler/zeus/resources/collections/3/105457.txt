Hull, Florida    hull , florida hull , florida is an unincorporated community in southwest desoto county , florida . about 150 people live there . hull had a creosote tie treating plant for the charlotte harbor and northern railroad . the railroad was bought by the seaboard air line railroad in the 1920s . the seaboard railroad kept using the creosote plant through the 1950s . in the late 1920s , hull became the junction of a new line of the sal to fort myers and naples . passenger service was done on the branch . this line was stopped in 1953 .
