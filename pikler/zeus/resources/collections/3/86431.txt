Illuminati     the illuminati ( the “ enlightened ones ” ) was a secret society . adam weishaupt , a philosopher and lawyer , started the society in 1776. in 1785 it was outlawed in bavaria . originally , they opposed superstition , prejudice , religious influence over public life , and abuses of state power . they supported women ’ s education and gender equality . later , other groups formed using the same name . there is no evidence that these present-day groups have any significant power or influence . they promote themselves using the bavarian illuminati to attract membership .
