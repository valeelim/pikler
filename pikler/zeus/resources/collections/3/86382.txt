Barbelo     barbelo is a goddess in gnosticsm . she is also called thought , providence , forethought , or foreknowledge . she is believed to be the first emanation of bythos . in the apocryphon of john , a writing in the nag hammadi library , barbelo is described as `` the first power , the glory , barbelo , the perfect glory in the aions , the glory of the revelation . '' the apocryphon of john continues : `` this is the first thought , his image ; she became the womb of everything , for it is she who is prior to them all , the mother-father , the first man ( anthropos ) , the holy spirit , the thrice-male , the thrice-powerful , the thrice-named androgynous one , and the eternal aeon among the invisible ones , and the first to come forth . '' the gnostic account of barbelo is quite similar to tantric thought . in kashmir shaivism , shakti is similarly the second tattva or first emanation of shiva . references . - the nag hammadi library . the apocryphon of john retrieved oct. 21 , 2004 .
