1950 FIFA World Cup     the 1950 fifa world cup was a football sporting event that was held in brazil in 1950. this competition was special compared to other fifa world cup tournaments as the winner was decided by winning a mini-league instead of a knock-out game . uruguay won the trophy after finishing higher than brazil , sweden and spain in the final group stage . participants . europe . - england ( eng ) • - italy ( ita ) • - spain ( esp ) • - sweden ( swe ) • - switzerland ( sui ) • - yugoslavia ( yug ) • north and central america . - mexico ( mex ) • - united states ( usa ) • south america . - bolivia ( bol ) • - brazil ( bra ) • - chile ( chi ) • - paraguay ( par ) • - uruguay ( uru ) • references . - fifa
