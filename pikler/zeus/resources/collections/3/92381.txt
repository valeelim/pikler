Space Shuttle Columbia     the space shuttle `` columbia '' ( ov-102 ) was a spacecraft used by nasa to fly into outer space . it was the first space shuttle to fly into space , on april 12 , 1981. it broke apart while re-entering the earth 's atmosphere on february 1 , 2003 , killing all seven people who were on it at the time . the shuttle flew a total of 28 missions . the columbia was named after a us navy ship that circumnavigated the world in 1836. it was also the name of the apollo 11 lunar excursion module . a piece of insulating foam from the external fuel tank peeled off during the launch 16 days earlier and struck the shuttle 's left wing . a hole was punctured in the leading edge of the wing . during the intense heat of re-entry , hot gases entered the interior of the wing , destroying the support structures and causing the rest of the shuttle to break apart . crew . - commander : rick d. husband , a u.s. air force colonel and mechanical engineer , who piloted a previous shuttle during the first docking with the international space station ( sts-96 ) .
