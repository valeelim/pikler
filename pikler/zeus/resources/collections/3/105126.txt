Yvette Cooper     yvette cooper ( born 20 march 1969 ) is an british politician . cooper is the member of parliament ( mp ) for the normanton , pontefract and castleford constituency in the united kingdom . she is a member of the labour party . her husband , ed balls , represents the neighbouring constituency of morley and outwood . she served in the cabinet between 2008 and 2010 under prime minister gordon brown . she was chief secretary to the treasury and then secretary of state for work and pensions . after labour lost the 2010 general election , cooper was appointed shadow foreign secretary , then in 2011 shadow home secretary . other websites . - official site
