Lockheed C-5 Galaxy     the c-5 galaxy is a very big military cargo aircraft made by lockheed ( now lockheed martin ) used by the united states air force . it first flew in 1968 and was introduced in 1970. it is one of the biggest transports in the world . it is 247 feet ( 75 meters ) long , 65 feet ( 19 meters ) high , has a wingspan of 222 feet ( 67 meters ) , and can weigh up to 840,000 pounds ( 378,000 kilograms ) . it is used to fly military equipment on to battlefields , and because of its size , it can even carry tanks and helicopters . five c-5s have crashed .
