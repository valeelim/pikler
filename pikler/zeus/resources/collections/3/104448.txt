Kuta     kuta is a town in southern bali , indonesia . once it was a fishing village . it was one of the first towns on bali to become popular with tourists . it is one of indonesia 's major tourist destinations . it has a long sandy beach , and many restaurants and bars . it is near bali 's ngurah rai airport . on october 12 , 2002 , a series of bombings killed more than 200 people in kuta . the bombings occurred outside two nightclubs .
