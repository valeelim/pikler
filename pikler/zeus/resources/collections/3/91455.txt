Hyper engine     the hyper engine was a possible aircraft engine model that could produce 1 horsepower per cubic inch of engine displacement . the term hyper engine was used only in the united states , where the army air corps gave people money to make a hyper engine that had an area of 1,300 cubic inches . this happened in the 1930s . they thought that this would better help with streamlining and to help get a better range . unfortunately , none of these designs ever made it past the production stage .
