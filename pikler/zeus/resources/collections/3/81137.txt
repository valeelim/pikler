Aubonne (district)    aubonne ( district ) aubonne was a district of the canton of vaud in switzerland . the capital was aubonne . it contained the following municipalities : - apples - aubonne - ballens - berolle - bière - bougy-villars - féchy - gimel - longirod - marchissy - mollens - montherod - pizy - saubraz - saint-george - saint-livres - saint-oyens
