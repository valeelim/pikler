Julio Lugo     julio cesar lugo is a former major league baseball shortstop . he played for the houston astros , tampa bay devil rays , los angeles dodgers , boston red sox , st. louis cardinals , baltimore orioles , and atlanta braves . he batted and threw right-handed . he was born on november 16 , 1975 , in barahona , dominican republic . julio lugo is also the older brother of baseball pitcher ruddy lugo .
