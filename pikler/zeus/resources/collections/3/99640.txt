Seer Sharqi Bhattian     seer sharqi bhattian is a union council of abbottabad district in the khyber pakhtunkhwa province of pakistan . it is in the southeastern part of the province , bordering the rawalpindi district of punjab province . seer sharqi bhattian is named after the two main towns `` seer sharqi '' and `` bhattian '' , seer sharqi means eastern seer , this is because there are two towns called seer near to each other . the other seer is known as seer gharbi ( meaning western seer ) the union council is subdivided into the following areas : bhatian , seer sharqi and taror . other websites . - district government of abbottabad
