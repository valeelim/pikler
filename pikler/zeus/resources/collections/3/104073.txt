Zombie computer     a zombie computer ( often shortened as zombie ) is a computer attached to the internet that has been compromised by a hacker , a computer virus , or a trojan horse . generally , a compromised machine is only one of many in a botnet , and the hacker will make the computer do unwanted things from far away . most owners of zombie computers do not know that their system is being used in this way . because the owner tends to not know , these computers are compared to zombies .
