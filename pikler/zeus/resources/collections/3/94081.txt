Musical movie     the musical movie is a movie genre in which several songs sung by the characters are linked into the story line . the songs are used to advance the plot or develop the movie 's characters . the musical movie was a natural development of the stage musical . typically , the biggest difference between movie and stage musicals is the use of expensive and impressive background scenery which would not be in a theater . musical movies usually contain elements similar of theater ; performers often treat their song and dance numbers as if there is a live audience watching .
