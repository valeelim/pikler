Popcorn     popcorn is a snack food made by heating certain kinds of corn . there is a small amount of water inside the seeds , which causes them to explode . when the seed shell breaks a sharp noise is made . the name `` pop '' is because of this noise . after it pops it is white and fluffy . salt and melted butter is often added to popcorn . sometimes caramel and other flavours are melted and mixed into the popcorn . popcorn is a popular food to eat at the cinema .
