Watchtower     a watchtower or watch tower is a tower used for surveillance . they were used at city walls to see invaders , when they were far away , so the city could prepare for an attack . a watchtower is also used for watching over people ( like a town or a city ) . watchtowers were popular in the middle ages . they are still often used to watch over prisoners and make sure that they do n't escape or leave the area . forestry uses watchtowers to find fires before they become more dangerous .
