Brigham Young University Hawaii     brigham young university hawaii , or `` byu-hawaii '' for short , is an undergraduate institution that educates some 2,400 students from asia , the pacific islands , the united states , and other parts of the world . the campus is located in laie , hawaii . it is owned and operated by the church of jesus christ of latter-day saints . it was founded in 1955 by mormon prophet david o. mckay .
