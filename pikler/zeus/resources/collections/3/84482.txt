Pianosa     pianosa is a small ( about 10 km ) island in italy 's tuscan archipelago . the name is derived from the latin word `` planasia '' ( plain ) because its highest point is some 22 meters over sea level . pianosa has no permanent residents . it is known in italy for a maximum-security prison , where especially dangerous mafia criminals are held . for administration , pianosa is part of the elba island municipality . on clear days , it can be seen from that island as a dark blue line over the lighter blue sea . in the novel `` catch-22 '' , a u.s. army air corps bomber squadron is based on the island during world war ii , there is another , even smaller island called `` pianosa '' off the adriatic coast of italy . other websites . - tourist information
