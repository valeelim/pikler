Dassault Mirage III     the mirage iii is a fighter aircraft made by french airplane company dassault aviation . if first flew in 1956 and was introduced in 1961. it used a triangle wing ( called a delta wing ) . it had 2 cannons and some missiles . because it was an interceptor , an airplane made to shoot down enemy airplanes at high speed , it could go mach 2.2. it was used by israel in the six-day war .
