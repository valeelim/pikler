Daspletosaurus     daspletosaurus ( `` frightful lizard '' ) was a tyrannosaurid theropod dinosaur that was 26-33 feet ( 8-10 m ) long and weighed perhaps 2-3 tons ( 3000 kg ) . it had tiny horns behind its eyes and its arms were a bit longer than those of `` t. rex '' . it may have been an ancestor of `` tyrannosaurus '' . this bipedal meat-eater lived in marshes by streams in what is now alberta , canada , during the upper cretaceous period , 76-72 million years ago . it may have preyed upon triceratops . it was named by d.a . russell in 1970. the type species is `` d. torosus '' . other websites . - it is possible to buy an original skeleton legally . `` dinosaur sanctuary '' .
