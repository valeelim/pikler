Mecklenburg-Strelitz     mecklenburg-strelitz was a duchy and later grand duchy in northern germany . it was about the same as the modern rural district of mecklenburg-strelitz . the duchy also had three exclaves near the town of f√ºrstenberg and the area around ratzeburg in modern schleswig-holstein . the duchy of mecklenburg-strelitz was set up in 1701. the capital was neustrelitz . in 1808 , the duchy joined the confederation of the rhine . the congress of vienna made it a grand duchy and member of the german confederation . mecklenburg-strelitz joined the north german confederation in 1867 , and became a part of the german empire in 1871. on january 1 1934 it was joined with the neighbouring state of mecklenburg-schwerin to form the state of mecklenburg ( today part of the federal state of mecklenburg-western pomerania ) . the us county mecklenburg ( charlotte , north carolina ) is named after mecklenburg-strelitz and strelitz-born princess charlotte ( queen consort of king george iii of the united kingdom ) . the flower `` strelitzia '' ( `` bird of paradise '' ) also is in honor of her home country . queen luise of prussia also was a born princess of mecklenburg-strelitz .
