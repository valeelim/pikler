SpongeBob SquarePants (character)    spongebob squarepants ( character ) spongebob squarepants is one of the main characters in the nickelodeon show `` spongebob squarepants '' . he is a sea sponge who loves his job as a fry cook at the krusty krab . spongebob lives in a pineapple with his pet snail gary . spongebob first appeared on the may 1 , 1999 television pilot of `` spongebob squarepants ''
