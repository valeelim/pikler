Bait     bait is something used to attract prey . people have used it for thousands of years . fishermen use bait to catch fish . there are many different types of bait depending on the kind of animal . frequently the bait is cast using a fishing rod and reel . for a shark , adult squid is often used . when trying to get a salmon , crab is commonly used . some fishermen use plastic bait . recently , fishermen have been using electronic lures . bait can be used to catch most animals .
