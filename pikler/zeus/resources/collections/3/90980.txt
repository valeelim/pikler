Gulf of Gonâve     the gulf of gonâve ( ) is a large gulf along the western coast of haiti . it is at . port-au-prince , the capital city of haiti , is on the coast of the gulf . other cities on the gulf coast include gonaïves , saint-marc , miragoâne , and jérémie . several islands are in the gulf ; the largest is gonâve island , followed by the much smaller cayemites . the artibonite river flows into this gulf . gonâve island splits the gulf in two channels ( a channel is a narrow part of the sea ) : the `` saint-marc channel '' to the north and the `` gonâve channel '' to the south .
