Agamidae     agamidae is a family of iguanian lizards . they are indigenous to africa , asia , australia , and there are a few in southern europe . the family has about 350 species in about 50 genera . agamids usually have scaly bodies , well-developed legs , and a moderately long tail . the average body size ranges from 10 to 15 cm ( 4 to 6 inches ) , and the tail is 20 to 30 cm ( 8 to 12 inches ) long , though the family varies widely .
