Ennepe-Ruhr Rural District     the ennepe-ruhr-kreis is a district in the middle of north rhine-westphalia , germany . the old district was changed in 1970 and 1975. it now includes the city of witten . the district got its name because it is in the valleys of the rivers ruhr and ennepe . in the middle of the coat of arms there is the checked red-white bar of the medieval state of mark , which owned the area in medieval times . the two wavy lines above and below are for the two rivers which gave the district its name , the ruhr and the ennepe . other websites . - official webpage ( german )
