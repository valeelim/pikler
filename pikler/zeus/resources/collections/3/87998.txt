Dolmen     a dolmen is a type of single-chamber megalithic tomb . it usually consists of three or more upright stones ( megaliths ) supporting a large flat horizontal capstone ( table ) . most date from the early neolithic period ( 4000 bc to 3000 bc ) . dolmens were usually covered with earth or smaller stones to form a barrow , though in many cases that covering has weathered away , leaving only the stone `` skeleton '' of the burial mound intact .
