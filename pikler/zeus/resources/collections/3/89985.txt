Screamo     screamo is a musical genre which came from both emo and hardcore punk in the mid 1990s . this genre usually consists of minor chord progressions on guitar with both dissonance and reverb , the time signatures used are usually not in 4/4 instead bands and artists use less used time signatures to create a more tense feel , this genre has both a mellow and chaotic feel to it . the vocals are usually high-pitched screams and lyrics are often poetic or political . an example of some screamo bands include saetia , orchid , circle takes the square , envy , pageninetynine , and thursday . other websites . - screamo at all music guide
