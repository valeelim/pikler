Ajax the Great     ajax the great or aias ( ancient greek : αἴας ) son of telamon , was a well-known hero from the trojan war . he was the cousin of achilles . ajax died by committing suicide . this happened after achilles died and he was in a contest with odysseus to see who would receive achilles 's armour : he lost . related pages . - ajax the lesser other websites . - archaeologist links palace to legendary ajax
