Tift Merritt     tift merritt ( born january 8 , 1975 ) is an american singer , guitarist and songwriter . born in houston , texas , merritt moved to raleigh , north carolina at a young age . she has been compared to songwriters like joni mitchell and emmylou harris . in 2009 , merritt married zeke hutchins , her band 's drummer and her long-time partner . they live in new york city . in addition to austin city limits , merritt has been on the tonight show with jay leno and the late show with david letterman . her music has been in the l word , men in trees , united states of tara and the movie new in town . other websites . - official website - myspace.com site - americana music association
