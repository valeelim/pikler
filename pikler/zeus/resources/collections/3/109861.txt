Boris Berezovsky (pianist)    boris berezovsky ( pianist ) boris berezovsky ( russian : бори́с вади́мович березо́вский ; born moscow , 4 january 1969 ) is a famous russian pianist . berezovsky studied at the moscow conservatory . in 1988 he became well known in the west when he gave a recital in the wigmore hall in london . two years later he won the gold medal at the 1990 international tchaikovsky competition in moscow . boris berezovsky gives concerts all over the world . he plays concertos with many of the great orchestras including the royal concertgebouw orchestra , new york philharmonic , philadelphia orchestra , orchestre de la monnaie , rotterdam philharmonic , danish national radio symphony orchestra , ndr hamburg , hessischer rundfunk , new japan philharmonic , city of birmingham symphony orchestra and with conductors such as kurt masur , wolfgang sawallisch , vladimir ashkenazy , mikhail pletnev and antonio pappano . berezovsky has also made many recordings . berezovsky has a daughter , evelyne berezovsky ( born 1991 ) , who is also a pianist . he now lives in brussels . boris is an excellent jazz pianist and performs with his trio in russian clubs .
