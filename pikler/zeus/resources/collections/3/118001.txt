The Eye (2008 movie)    the eye ( 2008 movie ) the eye is a 2008 movie starring jessica alba . it is a remake of the 2002 hong kong-thai-singaporean movie of the same name . the movie was rated pg-13 by the mpaa for `` violence/terror and disturbing content . '' it was directed by david moreau and xavier palud . it is the story of a young blind girl who has her sight restored . she is able to see dead people , and sets out to find the history of her new eyes .
