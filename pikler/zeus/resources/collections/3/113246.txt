1995-96 NHL season     the 1995–96 nhl season was the 79th regular season of the national hockey league . twenty-six teams each played 82 games . the stanley cup winners were the colorado avalanche . they swept the florida panthers in four games . this was the first year the team was named avalanche . 1995–96 was the first season in denver for the avalanche , who had relocated from quebec city where they were previously known as the quebec nordiques . it was also the last season of existence for the winnipeg jets , as they announced that they would be moving from manitoba to arizona and become the phoenix coyotes at the season 's end . 1995–96 would mark the last season the buffalo sabres would play in the buffalo memorial auditorium , the flyers at the spectrum , and canadiens at the montreal forum . the detroit red wings had a great season , finishing with the second-highest regular-season point total in nhl history ( 131 points ) , yet they fell to the avalanche in the western conference final , the sixth game of which marked the beginning of the heated detroit-colorado rivalry .
