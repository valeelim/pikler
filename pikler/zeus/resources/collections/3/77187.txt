Grumman F6F Hellcat     the f6f hellcat was an american world war ii fighter aircraft made by grumman . it first flew in 1942 and was introduced in 1943. it was supposed to be a little bit better than the f4f wildcat , but became a big improvement . it was more powerful and could turn better . this was supposed to fight the japanese a6m zero . it was able to take off of aircraft carriers . it was also used by the navy as a `` night fighter '' , which means that a radar was put on it so it could fly at night . during the war , it had the most aces fly it . it was retired in 1954 .
