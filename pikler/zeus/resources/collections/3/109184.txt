Caiman     the caiman is a subfamily of the alligators . the alligators are divided into true alligators and caimans . caimans and true alligators develop slower than the rest of the crocodiles . their lifestyle is also slower . because of this , they grow older than the other crocodiles . in total , there are 6 species of caimans . all of them live in south america , the spectacled caiman can also be found in central america . there are three groups : - true caiman ( `` caiman '' ) - musky caiman '' - spectacled caiman ( `` c. crocodilus '' ) - yacare caiman ( `` c. yakare '' ) - broad-snouted caiman ( `` c. latirostris '' ) - paleosuchus ( `` dwarf caimans ?
