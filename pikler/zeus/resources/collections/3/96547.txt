Suzuki     suzuki is a japanese company that makes cars and motorcycles . it had a stake in cami automotive with general motors . after the discontinuation of chevrolet tracker cami started producing the equinox , as well as the suzuki xl-7 suv . suzuki also sold three daewoo models in the u.s. and canada , the kalos , lacetti and magnus . suzuki backed out of the venture in 2010 after the redesigned xl-7 did not match sales demands . in 2012 suzuki stopped making cars for the american market . it will continue to build and sell atvs , motorcycles and army vehicles . other websites . - suzuki global website
