Stollberg Rural District     stollberg rural district is a district in the free state of saxony , germany . chemnitzer land . it was first created in 1910. in 1950 the district was dissolved . in 1952 it was set up again . in the big reform of 1994 the stollberg rural district got bigger , because some smaller towns and villages from the city of zwönitz and districts of chemnitz and aue , were transferred to stollberg . stollberg rural district is on the northwestern slope of the erzgebirge mountains . since 1990 the district has a partnership with the district fürth in bavaria . other websites . - official website ( german )
