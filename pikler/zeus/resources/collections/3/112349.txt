1928-29 NHL season     the 1928–29 nhl season was the twelfth season of the national hockey league . ten teams played 44 games each . this was the first stanley cup playoffs ever that saw two united states based teams compete for the cup . the boston bruins won over the new york rangers two games to none in a best-of-three final . `` note : w = wins , l = losses , t = ties , pts = points , gf = goals for , ga = goals against , pim = penalties in minutes '' `` note : teams that qualified for the playoffs are highlighted in bold '' `` note : gp = games played , g = goals , a = assists , pts = points , pim = penalties in minutes '' the following is a list of players of note who played their first nhl game in 1928–29 ( listed with their first team , stars ( * ) mark start in playoffs ) : - tiny thompson , boston bruins - cooney weiland , boston bruins - george owen , boston bruins - johnny gottselig , chicago black hawks - mush march , chicago black hawks - herbie lewis , detroit cougars - georges mantha , montreal canadiens - armand mondou , montreal canadiens
