Myomorpha     suborder myomorpha has 1,137 species of mouse-like rodents , almost a quarter of all mammals . mice , rats , gerbils , hamsters , lemmings and voles are in the group . they are grouped according to how the jaw looks like and how the molar teeth look like . they are very good at biting and chewing . myomorphs are found all around the world , except antarctica , in almost all land places . they are usually animals who are more active at night and they are seed-eaters . most myomorph species belong to superfamily muroidea : - superfamily muroidea - family platacanthomyidae - family spalacidae - family calomyscidae - family nesomyidae - family cricetidae - family muridae - superfamily dipodoidea - family dipodidae
