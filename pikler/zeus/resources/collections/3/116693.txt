Serial     serial may refer to the presentation of works in sequential segments , e.g . : - serial ( publishing ) : the publication of a journal in separate parts under the same title . example : nature ( journal ) . - serial ( literature ) often a work of narrative fiction published in sequential installments . - serial ( radio and television ) , series of radio and television programs that rely on a continuing plot - serial film , a short subject originally shown in theaters in conjunction with feature films , particularly in the 1930s and 1940s - serialism , in music
