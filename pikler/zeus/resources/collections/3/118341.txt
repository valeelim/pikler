Andre Agassi     andre kirk agassi ( born april 29 , 1970 ) is a retired professional tennis player from the united states . he was the first tennis player to have won all grand slam tournaments on four different surfaces . later , roger federer and rafael nadal did the same . he is one of the most successful tennis players in history and was formerly ranked number 1 in the world . he is married to former women 's number one tennis player steffi graf .
