Tirso de Molina     tirso de molina ( october , 1571 ? - march 12 , 1648 ) was a spanish baroque dramatist and poet . originally gabriel tellez , he was born in madrid . he studied at alcalá de henares , joined the order of the blessed virgin mary of mercy on november 4 1600 , and entered the monastery of san antolín at guadalajara , spain on january 21 1601. he was ordained a priest by 1610 .
