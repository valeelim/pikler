Fibre-reinforced polymer     a fibre-reinforced polymer ( also fibre-reinforced plastic ( `` frp '' ) ) is a composite material that consists of a polymer matrix that is reinforced with fibres . the fibres are usually fibreglass , carbon , or aramid , the polymer is usually an epoxy , vinylester or polyester thermosetting plastic . frps are commonly used in the aerospace , automobile , marine , and construction industries . other pages . - glass-reinforced plastic other websites . - `` business week '' story about frps used to construct bridges
