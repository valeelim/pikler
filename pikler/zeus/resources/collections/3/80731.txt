Mendrisio (district)    mendrisio ( district ) the district of mendrisio is the southernmost district of canton of ticino and of switzerland . the district of mendrisio has 27 municipalities divided in 5 circles ( `` circoli '' ) : - circle of mendrisio : mendrisio , coldrerio , genestrerio , salorino ; - circle of balerna : balerna , casima , castel san pietro , monte , chiasso , pedrinate , morbio inferiore ; - circle of caneggio : caneggio , cabbio , vacallo , sagno , morbio superiore , bruzella , muggio ; - circle of stabio : stabio , novazzano , ligornetto .
