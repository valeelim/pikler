Maiasaura     maiasaura was a large , plant-eating , duck-billed dinosaur . maiasaura was the first dinosaur that was found alongside its young , eggs , and nests . this suggests that maiasaura nurtured its young . maiasaura lived about 80 to 65 million years ago in the late cretaceous period . it was described as a kind and good natured dinosaur who ate plants , leaves and berries . it ate no meat at all since it was a herbivore . its name maiasaura means “ good mother lizard ” . it was named this because scientists discovered that it looked after its young . maiasaura measured about 9 metres long , 2-2.5 metres tall and weighed around 1800 kg . maiasaurs fossils were found in montana , united states .
