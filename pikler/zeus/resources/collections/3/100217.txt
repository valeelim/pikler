Schandmaul     schandmaul is a medieval folk rock band from munich , germany . they formed in 1998. the members right now are : thomas lindner , birgit muggenthaler , anna kränzlein , martin duckstein , stefan brunner , matthias richter . the first song ( teufelsweib ) was recorded in 1998. in the following year they released their very first album `` wahre helden '' . in 2000 the album `` von spitzbuben und anderen halunken '' has been redorded and released . in 2001 the band was discorvered by a record label , which led to the rerelease of `` von spitzbuben und halunken '' . discography . - albums - `` wahre helden '' ( 1999 ) - `` von spitzbuben und anderen halunken '' ( 2000 ) - `` narrenkönig '' ( lit . `` jester 's king '' ) ( 2002 ) - `` wie pech & schwefel '' ( 2004 ) - `` mit leib und seele '' ( 2006 ) - `` anderswelt '' ( 2008 ) - `` traumtänzer '' ( 2011 ) - live albums - `` hexenkessel '' ( 2003 ) - `` kunststück '' ( 2005 ) - `` sinnfonie '' ( 2009 ) - eps - `` bin unterwegs '' ( june 2005 ) - `` kein weg zu weit '' ( february 24 2006 ) - dvds - `` hexenkessel '' ( 2003 ) - `` kunststück '' ( 2005 )
