Burlington, Vermont    burlington , vermont burlington is a city in the american state of vermont . it is the county seat of chittenden county . burlington was first settled in 1783. it became a city in 1865. burlington is vermont 's largest city . it has a population of about 40,000 people . it is the smallest american city that is also the largest in its state . burlington has a metropolitan population of about 210,000. the city is located on the east bank of lake champlain . burlington has an area of about 15.5 sq miles . it sits at an elevation of 200 feet . notable residents . - grace coolidge , wife of calvin coolidge and first lady of the u.s. - howard dean , current chairman of the democratic party , former presidential candidate in 2004 , and former governor of vermont . - bernie sanders , current united states senator of vermont , former mayor of burlington , and 2016 united states presidential election candidate . education . - burlington college , private non-profit liberal arts college . closed in may 2016. sister cities .
