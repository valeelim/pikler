Chronology     chronology is a word meaning 'the study of time ' . it comes from the greek words `` chronos '' ( time ) and `` logos '' ( word ) . the adjective is chronological . putting events in chronological order means : listing them in the order in which they happened . there are many sciences which deal in different ways with chronology . general chronology is sometimes called `` periodization '' . historical chronology can deal with history or with the geological history of the earth . when things are put in chronological order it can sometimes be called a `` timeline '' . related pages . - calendar
