Raisin bread     raisin bread is a type of bread that has raisins in it . most people think of it as sweet bread , and it sometimes has cinnamon in it . it can be eaten as toast or as a dessert . raisin bread is normally sold pre-sliced and often eaten in northern europe and the united states . raisin bread is normally brown in color from the cinnamon . it is normally dryer than normal bread . other pages . - bread - sweet bread - cake - raisins
