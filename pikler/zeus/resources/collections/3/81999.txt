Cabo Engaño     cabo engaño is a cape near punta de agua , the easternmost point of the island of hispaniola , in the dominican republic . the region around the cape is very dry where cacti grow . christopher columbus saw the cape for the first time on 16 january 1493 and named it as cape sant theramo ( or cape san telmo ) . punta cana international airport , one of the busiest airport of the country , is to the south of the cape .
