Dysphemism     dysphemism means using of an harsh or rude word or phrase instead of a polite one . it is more or less the opposite of euphemism . examples of dysphemism are “ dead tree edition ” for the paper version of an online magazine , or the american soldiers calling their common breakfast of creamed chipped beef on toast “ shit on a shingle ” . related pages . - hyperbole - metaphor - euphemism
