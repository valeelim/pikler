William Riker     william thomas riker is a character . he is played by jonathan frakes . he is first officer on the starship enterprise . this means that he is second in command of the starship and can give orders if the captain is away . riker soon becomes very comfortable on the enterprise , many times refusing offers of his own command . on part 1-2 on the episode `` the best of both worlds '' riker takes command on the enterprise and orders jean-luc picard rescued .
