Black-eyed Susan     black-eyed susan `` rudbeckia '' is one of at least four genera within the flowering plant family asteraceae whose members are commonly known as coneflowers . blackeyed susan is a common missouri native wildflower which typically occurs in open woods , prairies , fields , roadsides and waste areas throughout the state . the name was given by carolus linneaus in honor of his teacher at uppsala university , professor olof rudbeck the younger ( 1660-1740 ) , and his father , professor olof rudbeck the elder ( 1630-1702 ) , both of whom were botanists . other websites . - http : //www.mobot.org/gardeninghelp/plantfinder/plant.asp ? code=a109 - http : //plants.usda.gov/java/profile ? symbol=ruhi2
