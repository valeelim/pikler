Beat Generation     the beat generation was a movement of writers , poets , and artists during the 1950s and 1960s , who liked the beat of jazz and bebop music , which became a kind of soundtrack to much of their lives and work . people associated with the movement were called `` beats '' , or sometimes `` beatniks '' , a reference to communism . ( a few beats supported communism , but most did not . ) the term came from novelist jack kerouac , who was sometimes called `` the king of the beats '' . other beat writers were william s. burroughs and allen ginsberg .
