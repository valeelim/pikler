Mayaguana     mayaguana is the most easterly island and district of the bahamas . it is one of only two bahamian islands which retain their arawak names , the other being inagua . the population of mayaguana in the 2000 census was 259 and is currently about 312. it has an area of about 110 square miles . other websites . - islands of the bahamas : mayaguana - bahamas getaway : mayaguana - images of mayaguana
