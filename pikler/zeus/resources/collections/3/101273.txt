Harry Redknapp     harry redknapp ( born 2 march 1947 ) is an english association football manager . he was born in poplar , london . he is currently in charge of english club queens park rangers football club . before he became a manager he used to play football , mainly for west ham . he managed a.f.c . bournemouth , west ham , portsmouth , southampton and portsmouth again before he got an offer to manage tottenham hotspur . redknapp is related to two premier league stars . he is the father of jamie redknapp who played under him at bournemouth and southampton . he is also the uncle by marriage of frank lampard who played under him at west ham united .
