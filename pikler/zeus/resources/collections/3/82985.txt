Rinpoche     rinpoche is a title in tibetan buddhism which may be given to a religious teacher known as a lama . the word `` rinpoche '' literally means `` precious one '' . in tibet and bhutan , when used as `` guru rinpoche '' it refers to padmasambhava , who first brought tibetan buddhism to the himalayas . when used as `` je rinpoche '' , it refers to je tsongkhapa , the founder of the gelug school .
