Lindane     lindane is an insecticide . it can be used to kill insects . the european union allows its use as an insecticide since 2008. this is because it takes a very long time to decay . checks are now getting done to see how bad to human it really is . it can cause diseases such as cancer or parkinson 's disease . the world health organization classifies lindane as `` moderately hazardous '' . international trade with it is restricted and regulated under the rotterdam convention on prior informed consent . over 50 countries have banned it . it might be included in stockholm convention on persistent organic pollutants . this would make it illegal to produce or use it all over the world .
