Cluj County     cluj is a county ( judeţ ) of romania , in transylvania . the capital of cluj county is cluj-napoca . about 755,000 people live in cluj county . cluj county has an area of 6,674 km² . cluj county has 5 municipalities , 1 town and 74 communes . municipalities . - cluj-napoca - turda - dej - câmpia turzii - gherla towns . - huedin
