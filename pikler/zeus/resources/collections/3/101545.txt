United Kingdom Census 2001     on sunday 29 april 2001 the united kingdom government held a census . this was the 19th uk census . united kingdom census 2001 was started by the office for national statistics in england and wales , the general register office for scotland and the northern ireland statistics and research agency . it was the first census to ask people in england , scotland and wales about their religion . other websites . - census 2001 website ( england and wales ) - scotland - northern ireland - the census order 2000 ( england and wales )
