BBC Sport     bbc sport is part of the bbc.co.uk website run by the british broadcasting corporation ( bbc ) . it covers all sports from football and rugby to less high-profile sports such as hockey or badminton . the site includes an discussion board called 606 to allow fans to discuss events and sports-related news . bbc sport also covers sporting events on bbc radio 5 live and bbc television , e.g . the olympics .
