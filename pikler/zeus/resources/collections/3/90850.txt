Dave Matthews Band     dave matthews band ( dmb ) is a band from the united states . in 1991 , dave matthews started the band in charlottesville , virginia . the other band members are stefan lessard , boyd tinsley , and carter beauford . saxophone player leroi moore was in the band until he died in 2008. since 1998 , they usually performed with butch taylor , too . rashawn ross also performed with the band from 2006-2007. dave matthews band has written 235 songs ( included cover songs ) . they have sold over 31 million albums in the united states , and are one of the top 100 highest selling music acts of all time . the dave matthews band has a unique style that stretches across many genres . thus , they have a fan-base that stretches across many generations and types of people .
