Chara (singer)    chara ( singer ) chara ( born as miwa watabiki , on january 13 , 1968 ) is a japanese singer , songwriter , musician and actress . she began performing in 1991 . `` swallowtail butterfly - あいのうた '' is one of her popular songs . chara grew up in kawaguchi , saitama . she started learning the piano from age four , and starting composing songs in elementary school . other websites . - official site - charaweb
