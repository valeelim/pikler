Frank Miller (comics)    frank miller ( comics ) frank miller ( born january 27 , 1957 ) is an american writer , artist and movie director . he best known for his film noir-style comic book stories including ronin , daredevil : born again , the dark knight returns , sin city , and 300. he is one of the most widely-recognized and popular creators in comics , and is one of the most influential comics creators of his generation . miller was born in olney , maryland , on january 27 , 1957 , and raised in montpelier , vermont . because of his interest in comics , miller moved to new york as a young adult to work as a comic book artist . miller released his first graphic novel , ronin , in 1983. miller later wrote other graphic novels including the dark knight returns and 300. miller moved to los angeles and worked in hollywood during the 1980s and 1990s , writing the scripts for robocop ii and iii . not happy with the films , miller stopped working in tv and movies until 2005 , when miller worked with director robert rodgriguez on the film , sin city .
