Datura stramonium     datura stramonium , known by the common names , jimson weed , ditch weed , stink weed , loco weed , korean morning glory , jamestown weed , thorn apple , angel 's trumpet , devil 's trumpet , devil 's snare , devil 's seed , mad hatter , crazy tea , malpitte , the devil 's balls , is an erect annual herb , on average 30 to 150 cm ( 1-5 feet ) tall with erect , forking and purple stems . the leaves are large , 7 to 20 cm ( 3-8 in ) long and have irregular teeth flowers are trumpet-shaped , white to purple , and 2-7 in long.the fruit are walnut-sized , egg-shaped , and covered in prickles , they split into four chambers , each chamber with dozens of small black seeds http : //www.erowid.org/plants/datura/datura_info5.shtml
