Epimetheus     epimetheus is a titan in greek mythology , and is the brother of prometheus , a greek titan who gave all of the creatures something to protect themselves from the elements . by the time he came to the humans , he had run out of feathers and fur to give . he went to his brother , and he gave humans fire . epimetheus literally means `` thinks after '' and prometheus means `` thinks before '' . references . - kerenyi , karl , 1951 . `` the gods of the greeks , pp 209ff . - graves , robert , ( 1955 ) 1960 , `` the greek myths '' 39.a-j
