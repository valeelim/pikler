Drammen     drammen is a city and municipality in the county of buskerud , norway . drammen was established as a municipality january 1 , 1838 ( see formannskapsdistrikt ) . the rural municipality of skoger was merged with drammen january 1 , 1964 . ( and then transferred from the county of vestfold to the county of buskerud . ) drammen is one of the larger cities in norway , and is about 47 km from the capital of norway , oslo . the city centre is at the end of a valley , on both sides of the river drammenselva , and where the river meets the drammensfjord . drammen is also the main city for car and fruit import in norway . the norse form of the name was `` drafn '' , and this was originally the name of the inner part of drammensfjord . the fjord is , however , probably named after the river dramselva ( norse `` dröfn '' ) . famous people . - martinus rørbye , painter ( 1803–1848 ) - marcus thrane , labour movement leader ( 1817–1890 ) - hans peter faye , sugar plantation pioneer ( 1859–1928 ) - johan halvorsen , composer , conductor and musician ( 1864–1935 )
