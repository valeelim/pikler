AAA: Attack All Around    aaa : attack all around aaa ( triple a ) is a japanese music group . the group was formed in 2005 , and were awarded a new face award in the same year . they are signed to `` avex trax '' . the group members were takahiro nisijima , naoya urata , misako uno , mituhiro hidaka , shinjiro atae , shuta sueyoshi and chiaki ito . they play an active role in j-pop .
