Glenn Hall     glenn henry `` mr. goalie '' hall ( born october 3 , 1931 , in humboldt , saskatchewan , canada ) is a canadian retired professional ice hockey goaltender . during his national hockey league career with the detroit red wings , chicago black hawks , and st. louis blues , hall rarely missed a game , winning the vezina trophy three times , and the calder memorial trophy . according to hockey lore , hall threw up before every game and drank a glass of orange juice . other websites . - glenn hall biography
