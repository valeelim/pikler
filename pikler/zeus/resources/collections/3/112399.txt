Simpsons Roasting on an Open Fire     `` simpsons roasting on an open fire '' is an episode of `` the simpsons '' from the first season . it was the first full length episode of `` the simpsons '' to be aired . it was first aired on december 17 , 1989. bart gets a tattoo from a shop in the springfield mall , which marge has to use all of the christmas money to have it removed .
