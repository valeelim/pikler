Marcel Proust     valentin louis georges eugène marcel proust ( french pronounced : maʁsɛl pʁust ) ( 10 july 1871 – 18 november 1922 ) was a french novelist , essayist and critic , best known as the author of `` à la recherche du temps perdu '' ( in english , `` in search of lost time ) '' , a monumental work of twentieth-century fiction published in seven parts from 1913 to 1927. proust was a homosexual . he died of pneumonia . references . - adorno , theodor . `` prisms .
