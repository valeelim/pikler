Blues-rock     blues rock is a type of music which mixes blues with rock and roll , and uses mostly electric guitar as a main instrument . it began in the mid 1960s in england and the united states with bands such as cream and the rolling stones , who experimented with music from the older bluesmen like elmore james , robert johnson , howlin ' wolf and bo diddley . examples of artists . - allman brothers band - mike bloomfield - joe bonamassa - eric clapton - fleetwood mac - rory gallagher - jimi hendrix - john mayall - the rolling stones - carlos santana - lynyrd skynyrd - george thorogood - status quo - stevie ray vaughan - johnny winter - zz top - david wilcox
