Lombok     lombok ( population 2,950,105 in 2005 ) is an island in west nusa tenggara province , indonesia . it is to the east of bali on the other side of the lombok strait . the capital and largest city on the island is mataram . the gili islands are very popular with tourists . there are many places to dive and the beaches are lovely . mount rinjani , 3,726 m ( 12,224 ft ) , is the third-highest volcano in indonesia . other websites . - ny times on lombok - inside lombok
