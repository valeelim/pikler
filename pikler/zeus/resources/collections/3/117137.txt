Badin District     badin district ( ) is a district in the sindh province of pakistan . the district is administratively subdivided into the following talukas : - badin tehsil - matli tehsil - shaheed fazil rahu tehsil - talhar tehsil - tando bago tehsil with the introduction of the devolution system the talukas have been sub-divided into the union councils numbering 49 , tapas 109 and dehs 511 .
