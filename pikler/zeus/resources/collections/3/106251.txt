Baltimore Orioles     the baltimore orioles are a major league baseball team from baltimore , maryland . they compete in the american league 's al east division . they began play in the early 1900s as the st. louis browns , a rival to the st. louis cardinals . they moved to baltimore in 1954 and became the orioles . the `` baltimore oriole '' is an orange-and-black bird which is often seen in maryland . `` orioles '' has been a traditional nickname for baltimore baseball teams . there was a baltimore orioles ( 19th century ) in the major leagues . there was also a minor league baseball team called the orioles which played from the early 1900s until the browns moved to baltimore . the browns were not a very successful team . they only won one league championship , in 1944. the orioles have won several league championships and also won the world series in 1966 , 1970 and 1983 .
