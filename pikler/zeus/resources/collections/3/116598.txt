Rochester Cathedral     rochester cathedral is a norman church in rochester , kent , and the cathedral of the anglican diocese of rochester . it was originally founded in 604 ad , and rebuilt by the normans . the present church was consecrated in 1130. it became a place of pilgrimage after william of perth was murdered nearby in 1201. other websites . - official website of rochester cathedral - a history of the king 's school and of the choristers of rochester cathedral
