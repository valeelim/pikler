Women's Tennis Association    women 's tennis association the women 's tennis association ( wta ) , formed in 1973 by billie jean king , is the principal organizing body of women 's professional tennis . it organizes the wta tour , the worldwide professional tennis tour for women . the wta 's counterpart organization for men 's professional tennis is the association of tennis professionals ( atp ) . the wta was formed in the month of june 1973 , and is able to trace its beginnings back to houston , texas , with the first virginia slims tournament , which was organized by gladys heldman and held in september 1970. the wta gives out awards every year for players who have done well during the season . the wta is based in st. petersburg , florida . wta rankings . - rankings as of 4 august 2014. other websites . - the official women 's tennis association website - the official wta tour facebook page
