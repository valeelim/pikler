Terpsitone     the terpsitone was an electronic musical instrument invented by léon theremin . it consisted of a platform fitted with antennas , through and around which a dancer would control the musical performance . by most accounts , the instrument was nearly impossible to play . theremin only built three of these instruments . only the last one , made by theremin in 1978 for lydia kavina , still exists today . other pages . - theremin references . - mason , c. p.
