Contact lens     a contact lens is a type of soft lens that you put directly into your eye in order to help you see better . they are a different option instead of glasses . they have to be prescribed by your eye doctor . some contact lenses can be used many times , while other types are used only once and then thrown away ( disposable ) . other websites . - consumer guide to contact lenses ( articles ) - consumer guide to contact lenses ( articles ) - contact lens wearing guide - british contact lens association - contact lens history
