Cheryl Gillan     cheryl elise kendall gillan ( born 21 april 1952 in cardiff , wales ) is a british politician . she was the secretary of state for wales from 2010 to 2012. she is a conservative party member of parliament ( mp ) for the chesham and amersham constituency in the house of commons of the united kingdom . she was first elected in the 1992 general election and became the shadow secretary of state for wales in 2005. when the conservative-liberal democrat coalition was formed on 11 may 2010 , she became the secretary of state for wales .
