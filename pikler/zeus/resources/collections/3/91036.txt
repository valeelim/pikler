588 Achilles     588 achilles is an asteroid found on february 22 , 1906 by the german astronomer max wolf . it was the first of the trojan asteroids to be found , and is named after achilles , the fictional hero from the iliad . it orbits in the l lagrangian point of the sun-jupiter system . after a few such asteroids were found , the rule was made that the l point was the `` greek camp '' , while the l point was the `` trojan camp '' , though not before each camp had acquired a `` spy '' ( 624 hektor in the greek camp and 617 patroclus in the trojan camp ) . other websites . - orbital simulation from jpl ( java ) / ephemeris
