Cardiff City F.C.    cardiff city f.c . cardiff city f.c . is a welsh football club who play in cardiff . they play at the cardiff city stadium , ( formerly at ninian park ) and have the nickname `` the bluebirds '' . they are the only non-english side to win the fa cup , in 1926-27. they were also runners up in 1924-25 and 2007-08. name . - 1899-1902 riverside f.c . - 1902-1908 riverside albion f.c . - 1908-present cardiff city f.c .
