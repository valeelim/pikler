Orange, Ohio    orange , ohio orange is a village of cuyahoga county , ohio , united states . it is a suburb of the big city of cleveland . the population was 3,236 people as of 2000. orange was first settled in 1815 , and was founded as a township in 1820. in 1831 , orange became the birthplace of united states president james a. garfield . orange was made into a village in 1929. other websites . - orange village website
