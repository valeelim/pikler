Goodbye (Eurovision song)    goodbye ( eurovision song ) `` goodbye '' was the bosnian and herzegovinian entry in the eurovision song contest 1997. the song was performed in bosnian by alma čardžić . the song is about the singer asking her lover not to end their relationship . she suggests many things that he could do , just as long as he does not `` say the two words , which mean it 's over '' ( the two words being `` goodbye , darling '' – `` '' in bosnian ) .
