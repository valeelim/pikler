I. M. Pei     ieoh ming pei ( born april 26 , 1917 ) is a chinese american architect . pei has been an important name in his field , with his buildings built all over the world . when he was a child , he learned basic knowledge about architecture from his father . pei made innovations in architecture , and won the thomas jefferson memorial medal for architecture . he built the allied bank tower in dallas , texas , as well as the louvre pyramid , which is the main entrance to the louvre museum in paris . in 1983 , pei won the pritzker prize . `` i. m . pei . `` notable asian americans . ed . helen zia and susan b. gall . detroit : gale , 1995. biography in context . web . 29 may 2015 . `` i. m . pei . `` newsmakers . detroit : gale , 1990. biography in context . web . 29 may 2015 .
