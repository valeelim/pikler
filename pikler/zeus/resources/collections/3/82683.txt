Bayeux Cathedral     bayeux cathedral is a cathedral . it is in bayeux , in normandy . the current cathedral was consecrated on july 14 , 1077. it is part of a larger complex , a bishop 's palace . it was built as a replacement for an earlier church which dated from the merovingian era . it was here that harold godwinson signed an oath that later led to the norman conquest . other websites . - les amis de cathedrale de bayeux
