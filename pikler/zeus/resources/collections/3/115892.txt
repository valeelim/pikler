Alejandro Valverde     alejandro valverde belmonte ( born april 25 , 1980 ) is a spanish road racing cyclist . he rides for the caisse d'epargne team . valverde won the liège-bastogne-liège race in 2006 and 2008. he was second in the 2006 vuelta a españa and sixth in the 2007 tour de france . he won the 2008 critérium du dauphiné libéré , an important race before the 2008 tour de france . this means that many people think he will do well in the tour . valverde is a strong climber , sprinter and time trialist . achievements . - 2001 - 2003 - 2004 - 2005 - 2006 - 2007 - 2008 - 2009 - 2010 all results were voided from 31 may , because of the suspension backdated to 1 january . - 2012 - 2013 grand tour general classification results timeline : this table shows valverde 's classics results .
