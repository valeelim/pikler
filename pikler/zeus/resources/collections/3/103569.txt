The Sleepers     the sleepers is a painting by gustave courbet . the painting was made in 1866. in it , two naked women lie on a bed . it is typical of courbet 's later erotic paintings . the painting is done in oil on canvas . it measures 135 cm high and 200 cm wide . it is currently in the museum petit palais in paris , france . the painting is also known as the dream ( french : `` le sommeil '' ) , or lust or the sloth and friends .
