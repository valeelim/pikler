A. Q. Khan     abdul qadeer khan ( ; born april 1 , 1936 in bhopal ) is a pakistani scientist and metallurgical engineer . he is a controversial figure . he is thought by many people to be one of the pioneers of pakistan 's nuclear program . others disagree and claim he is only an opportunist who abused his position to obtain personal benefits and make a lot of money . his middle name is occasionally spelt quadeer , qadir or gadeer . his given names are usually abbreviated to a.q.. khan claims he has pashtun ancestry . however , his ancestors supposedly immigrated to india , during the reign of afghan emperor shahabuddin ghouri from the district of ghour of present afghanistan . he was born in bhopal state in british india in 1936. his father abdul ghafoor khan served in the education department , british india , and after retirement , settled in bhopal . he remained under arrest in pakistan for quite some time , for nuclear proliferation to the evil north korean government and also to iran . other websites .
