Botoşani     botoşani ( pronunciation in romanian : is the capital city of botoşani county , in the northern part of moldavia , romania . it has a population of 115,609 people as of the year 2002 today , it is best known as the birthplace of many celebrated romanians , including mihai eminescu and nicolae iorga . football is represented in botoşani by the team fc botoşani .
