Interstate H-3     interstate h-3 is a highway in the american state of hawaii . it begins at the halawa interchange with interstates h-1 and h-201 . the highway then runs along a viaduct through halawa valley for about 6 miles . it then goes through the tetsuo harano tunnels through the koolau mountains . once on the eastern end of the tunnel , the highway follows a viaduct built along the side of haiku valley until the kaneohe interchange with state route 63 ( likelike highway ) which leads into the town of kaneohe . the highway then continues to the halekou interchange with state route 83 ( kamehameha highway ) . four miles farther , it reaches the main gate of marine corps base hawaii . the route is long .
