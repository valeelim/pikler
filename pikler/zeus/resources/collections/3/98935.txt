Limbourg brothers     the limbourg brothers , or in dutch `` gebroeders van limburg '' ( herman , paul , and johan ; fl . 1385 – 1416 ) , were famous dutch renaissance miniature painters from the city of nijmegen . they were active in the early 15th century in france and burgundy . they created what is certainly the best known late medieval illuminated manuscript , `` les très riches heures du duc de berry '' .
