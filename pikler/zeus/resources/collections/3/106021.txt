Cincinnati Bengals     the cincinnati bengals are a professional american football team in cincinnati , ohio . they are currently members of the north division of the american football conference ( afc ) in the national football league ( nfl ) . their first season , 1968 , was as an american football league franchise , but they joined the nfl as part of the 1970 afl-nfl merger . their current quarterback is andy dalton . the bengals ' stadium is paul brown stadium , in cincinnati . their uniforms are an orange , black , or white jersey with tiger stripes and black or white pants . the team chose its name for the fierce and dangerous big cat called the bengal tiger . other websites . - cincinnati bengals official web site - sports e-cyclopedia.com - bengals history there chant is who dey bengals
