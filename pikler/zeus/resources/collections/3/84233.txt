Foreign Secretary     the foreign secretary is the minister in charge of the united kingdom 's foreign and commonwealth office . in many countries this job is called foreign minister . until the 1960s , the foreign secretary only handled relations with foreign countries and international organisations . there was a separate commonwealth secretary and a colonial secretary , but all three are now joined into one . the foreign secretary 's is one of the four great offices of state . as of 2016 the job is held by boris johnson .
