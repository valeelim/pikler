Lope de Vega     lope de vega ( also félix lope de vega y carpio or lope félix de vega carpio ) ( 25 november 1562 – 27 august 1635 ) was a spanish baroque playwright and poet . his reputation in the world of spanish letters is second only to that of cervantes , while the sheer volume of his literary output is unequalled : he is estimated to have written between 1,500 and 2,500 fully-fledged plays – of which some 425 have survived until the modern day – together with a plethora of shorter dramatic and poetic works . he was `` the phoenix of wits '' and `` monster of nature '' by miguel de cervantes because he did so much work . lope de vega renewed the spanish theatre when people were becoming interested in it . along with calderón de la barca and tirso de molina , he improved spanish theater . he is considered a great dramatist and his plays are often performed . he is also one of the famous spanish poets and authors . lope de vega was born in madrid to a non-notable family , who came to the capital from valle de carriedo in cantabria recently . félix de vega , the bread winner of the family , was an embroiderer .
