Monthey, Switzerland    monthey , switzerland monthey is the capital of the district of monthey in the canton of valais in switzerland . it has a population is of 15,118 ( as of 2004 ) people ( `` montheysans '' ) . monthey is located on the west side of the rhone valley , south to lake geneva . monthey is important to swiss industry , with many important companies including chemical companies . the city is close to the famous winter sports region , the portes du soleil . other websites . - monthey homepage
