County Mayo     county mayo is a county in the province of connacht , in ireland . castlebar and ballina are the two towns with the most people living in them in the county . the highest point in mayo and connacht is mweelrea . the following is a list of the towns and villages in county mayo : - achill island - balla , ballina , ballinrobe , ballintober , ballycastle , ballyhaunis , ballyglass , ballyvary , bangor , belmullet , bohola - castlebar , charlestown , claremorris , cong , crossmolina - derrew - foxford - geesala - keel , kilkelly , killala , kiltimagh , knock - louisburgh - mayo , mulrany - newport - swinford - tourmakeady - westport other websites . - mayo county council 's website - map of mayo
