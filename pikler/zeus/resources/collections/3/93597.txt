Neunkirchen District     neunkirchen is a kreis ( district ) in the middle of the saarland , germany . in 1974 the districts of saarland were reorganized . the capital of the district was moved to neunkirchen . the name of the district was changed to neunkirchen as well . neunkirchen city had first tried to become cspital of the district in 1866. some parts of the administration remained in ottweiler . other websites . - official website ( german )
