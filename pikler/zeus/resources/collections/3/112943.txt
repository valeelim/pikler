Nelson Piquet     nelson piquet souto maior ( born august 17 , 1952 ) , more commonly known as nelson piquet , is a brazilian racing driver who was formula one world champion in 1981 , 1983 , and 1987. he is one of the few men to win at least three world championships in the history of formula one . the others are jack brabham , jackie stewart , niki lauda , ayrton senna ( 3 each ) , alain prost ( 4 ) , juan manuel fangio ( 5 ) , and michael schumacher ( 7 ) .
