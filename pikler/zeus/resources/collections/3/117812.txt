SummerSlam     summerslam is a yearly professional wrestling pay-per-view ( ppv ) event held in august by world wrestling entertainment ( wwe ) . the event is often called `` the biggest party of the summer . '' along with wrestlemania , royal rumble and survivor series , summerslam is one of the `` big four '' pay-per-views and was one of the original four yearly wwe pay-per-views . wwe released a complete dvd box set with the title `` summerslam : the complete anthology '' , which includes all the summerslam events , on august 5 2008. other websites . - official summerslam site - summerslam event history - archived results and trivia
