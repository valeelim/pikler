Clarence E. Anderson     clarence e. `` bud '' anderson ( b. january 13 , 1922 in oakland , california ) was a fighter pilot in the united states air force . he joined the air force in 1942. during world war ii , he flew a p-51 mustang named `` old crow '' . he had 16 kills , and was a triple ace . at the end of world war ii , he was a captain . he flew an f-105 thunderchief in the vietnam war . he retired from the air force in 1972. he was also a test pilot .
