Viviers, Ardèche    viviers , ardèche viviers is a commune in the french `` département '' of ardèche . the commune has a population of about 3,400 people as of the year 1999. viviers became the capital of the gaulish helvii tribe following the falling of nearby alba-la-romaine . related pages . - communes of the ardèche department other websites . - viviers on the site of the institut géographique national - viviers on the site of the i - viviers on the site of the quid - communes closest to viviers - localisation de viviers on a map of france - map of viviers on mapquest
