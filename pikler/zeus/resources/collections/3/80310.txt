Federal Emergency Management Agency     the federal emergency management agency , or fema , is an agency of the united states department of homeland security . the purpose of fema ( begun by presidential order on march 30 , 1979 ) is to coordinate the response to a disaster which has occurred in the united states and which overwhelms the resources of local and state authorities . the governor of the state in which the disaster occurred must declare a state of emergency and formally request from the president that fema and the federal government respond to the disaster . the only exception is when an emergency or disaster occurs on federal property or to a federal asset , for example , the alfred p. murrah federal building in oklahoma city , oklahoma , in the 1995 bombing , or the space shuttle columbia in the 2003 return-flight disaster . other websites . - dhs organization - fema field operations guide including descriptions of markings on evacuated and searched buildings - fema website - a history of fema
