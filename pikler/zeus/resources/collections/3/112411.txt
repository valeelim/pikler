John Tortorella     john robert tortorella ( born june 24 , 1958 in boston , massachusetts ) is an american ice hockey coach . he coached the tampa bay lightning and won both the jack adams award and the stanley cup , with the lightning , in 2004. he most recently coached the new york rangers from february 23 , 2009 to may 29 , 2013. he was fired by the rangers after a disappointing playoff run .
