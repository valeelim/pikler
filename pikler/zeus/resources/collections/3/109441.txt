Cumulonimbus cloud     cumulonimbus ( cb ) is a type of cloud that is tall , dense , and involved in thunderstorms and other intense weather . the clouds can form alone , in clusters , or along a cold front in a squall line , a line of strong winds . cumulonimbus clouds form from cumulus clouds ( namely from cumulus congestus cloud ) and can further develop to a supercell , a severe thunderstorm with special features .
