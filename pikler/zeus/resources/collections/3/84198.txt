Vihuela     vihuela is a name given to two different guitar-like string instruments : one from 16th century spain , usually with 12 paired strings , and the other , the mexican vihuela , from 20th century mexico with five strings and typically played in mariachi bands . other websites . - lutesandguitars.co.uk stephen barber & sandi harris , lutemakers and researchers - vihuelademano.com alexander batov , vihuela maker and researcher - goldberg magazine : `` los dos rinacimintos de la vihuela '' the twentieth-century resuscitation of the vihuela . - several photos of spanish vihuelas can be found among the instrument photos at wayne cripps ' lute pages . - pictures
