Thnks fr th Mmrs     thnks fr th mmrs is a 2007 song by fall out boy . it is the second single from infinity on high , the band 's fourth album . it was released on march 27 , 2007 in the united states . the title is short for `` thanks for the memories '' . it has been certified gold by riaa . it was used by network ten in australia to promote the 2007 afl finals series .
