Euarchontoglires     euarchontoglires ( synonymous with supraprimates ) is a taxonomic superorder , within placentalia . it includes the rodents and the primates . placentalia ( eutheria ) - afrotheria - exafroplacentalia - xenarthra - boreoeutheria - laurasiatheria - euarchontoglires orders . - glires - rodentia - rodents - lagomorpha - rabbits , hares and pikas - euarchonta - dermoptera - colugos - primatomorpha - scandentia - treeshrews - primates - monkeys , apes and humans
