Doge     doge ( plural dogi or doges ) is a word of an italian dialect that comes from the latin `` dux '' ( as does the english `` duke '' and the standard italian `` duce '' ) , that means `` leader '' , especially in a military context . the title of doge was used for the elected head of state in a number of italian `` crowned republics '' . the two best known such republics were venice and genoa , which had maritime , commercial , and territorial mini-empires . sometimes the doge ruled for life ; sometimes for a fixed number of years .
