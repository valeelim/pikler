Tournon-sur-Rhône     tournon-sur-rhône is a commune of the ardèche `` département '' , in france . the commune has a population of 9,960 people as of the year 1999. it can be found on the right bank of the rhône river , across from the tain-l'hermitage . that can be found in the drôme `` département '' . the great bridge of tournon was one of the largest bridge arches in the world before 1600. tournon-sur-rhône is twinned with : - fellbach , germany - erba , italy related pages . - communes of the ardèche department
