Mittlerer Erzgebirgskreis Rural District     the mittlerer erzgebirgskreis is a district in the free state of saxony , germany . the district was formed in 1994 by joining the two previous districts marienberg and zschopau . the district is located in the erzgebirge mountains . the highest point is the high `` hirtstein '' . the lowest point , , is in witzschdorf . 40 % of the district is covered by forests . other websites . - official website ( german ) - tourism website ( german )
