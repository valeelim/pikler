Civilian Conservation Corps     the civilian conservation corps ( ccc ) was started by franklin d. roosevelt during the great depression . the `` ccc '' gave young men jobs such as fighting forest fires , digging ditches , and planting trees . the `` ccc '' paid these men about thirty dollars a month . at first , it was planned to get men out of the cities , but more men came from small towns and rural areas . other websites . - tripleccc museum in st. louis , missouri
