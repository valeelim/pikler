Faido     faido is the capital of the district of leventina in the italian-speaking canton of ticino in southern switzerland . it has about 1,600 people and lies in the leventina valley . the municipality was first noted in 1171 as `` faedo '' . in the spring of 2004 , the people voted for merging the neighboring municipalities of chiggiogna , osco , mairengo , calpiogna , campello , rossura , calonico , anzonico , cavagnago , and sobrio . most of the municipalities did not want to do that , and the new municipality contains only the municipalities of faido , chiggiogna , rossura , and calonico . other websites . - official website
