Saturn Award     the saturn award is an award presented every year by the academy of science fiction , fantasy & horror films to honor the top works in science fiction , fantasy , and horror in movie , television , and home video . the prestigious saturn awards were devised by dr. donald a. reed , who felt that movies within those genres were never given the appreciation they deserved . the physical award is a representation of the planet saturn , surrounded with a ring of film . the award was initially , and is still sometimes loosely referred to as a golden scroll . similar to other awards , like the oscars , the emmys and the grammys , the saturn awards are voted on by members of the presenting academy . there are also special awards for lifetime achievement in the field . the awards were first presented in 1972 .
