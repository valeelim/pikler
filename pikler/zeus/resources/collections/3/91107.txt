Dil Se     dil se ( , , translation : `` from the heart ) '' is a 1998 hindi movie . it was directed by mani ratnam . the movie was also released in tamil as uyire and in telugu as prema tho . it stars shahrukh khan , manisha koirala , and preity zinta . mani ratnam also wrote the screenplay for the movie . it was produced by mani ratnam and ram gopal varma along with shekar kapur . the movie was shot in maqbuza kashmir , assam , delhi and other parts of india and bhutan over a period of 55 days . its cinematography won a national film award for cinematographer santosh sivan . the movie score and soundtrack were composed by a. r. rahman . a. r. rahman received a filmfare award for the music . the movie became the first indian movie to reach the top 10 in the uk box office charts , when released in 1998. cast . - shah rukh khan ... amarkant `` amar '' varma - manisha koirala ... meghna - preity zinta ... preeti the movie has won the following awards since its release : 1999 berlin international film festival ( germany )
