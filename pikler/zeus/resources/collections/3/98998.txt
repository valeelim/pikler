Staples Center     the staples center is a indoor arena in downtown los angeles , california . it is home to the los angeles lakers and los angeles clippers basketball teams , the los angeles kings hockey team , and the los angeles sparks wnba team . they have the grammy awards here every year . the building hosted world wrestling entertainment 's pay-per-view wrestlemania 21 in 2005. home teams . - los angeles kings ( nhl ) - los angeles clippers ( nba ) - los angeles lakers ( nba ) - los angeles sparks ( wnba )
