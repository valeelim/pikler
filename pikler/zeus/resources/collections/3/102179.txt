Bourg-Saint-Andéol     bourg-saint-andéol is a small commune of the ardèche `` département '' in the rhône valley in the southern part of france . it is the capital of a canton in the arrondissement of privas , with a population of about 8,000 as of the year 1999. twin cities . - monschau in germany ( eifel ) since 1975 - albertirsa in hungary since 1998 - gaggiano in italy since 2002 related pages . - communes of the ardèche department
