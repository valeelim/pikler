AboutUs.com     aboutus.com is a wiki internet domain directory . it lists websites along with information about their content . as a wiki , aboutus allows internet users to add entries or modify information . it was created by ray king in 2006. it started in portland , oregon , where the website 's headquarters is today . ray king started aboutus with only five people working with him , but , now he has more than thirty-five employees . aboutus is in two different continents now , and they have an office in the city of lahore , pakistan .
