Martin Luther McCoy     martin luther mccoy is an american musician and actor . he worked with the roots a lot and appears as one of the six lead characters in the 2007 beatles musical `` across the universe '' in the role of `` jojo '' . mccoy goes by the stage name of `` martin luther '' in his music career . for his acting work he is known under his full name . martin luther released his first solo album `` the calling '' in 1999 and followed it with an independent release of his second album `` rebel soul music '' in 2004 on his own label rebel soul records . his third album `` love is the hero '' was supposed to be released in 2011
