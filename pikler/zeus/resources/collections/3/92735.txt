Detmold Government Region     detmold ( ) is one of the five regierungsbezirke of north rhine-westphalia , germany . it is in the northeast of the state . the regierungsbezirk was created in 1947 when the old state of lippe was put into north rhine-westphalia and joined to the old `` regierungsbezirk minden '' . regierungsbezirk minden was one of the original regierungsbezirke , formed in 1815. other websites . - official website
