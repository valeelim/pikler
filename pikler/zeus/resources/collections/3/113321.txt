Gujari language     gojri , also known as 'gujari ' is an indo-aryan language spoken by the gurjars , or gujjars of northern pakistan and the republic of india . it is classified as a form or dialect of rajasthani by the linguists . the language is mainly spoken in the punjab provinces of hindustan and pakistan and also in jammu & kashmir , it is most closely related to marwari and gujarati .
