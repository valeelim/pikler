Wang Xiaobo     wang xiaobo ( ; pinyin : wáng xiǎobō ; may 13 , 1952–april 11 , 1997 ) is a famous writer in mainland china . his most famous and successful literary works is `` golden time '' . xiaobo was born in beijing , china . in 1972 , he became a teacher in the shandong province . in 1978 , he started studying at the renmin university of china . xiaobo 's most famous novel was published in 1994. it was named `` golden time '' .
