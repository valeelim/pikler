Sierre (district)    sierre ( district ) sierre is a district of the canton of valais in switzerland . it contains the following municipalities : - ayer - chalais - chandolin - chermignon - chippis - grimentz - grône - icogne - lens - miège - mollens - montana-vermala - randogne - sierre - saint-jean - saint-léonard - saint-luc - venthône - veyras - vissoie
