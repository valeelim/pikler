Middle way     the middle way or middle path is a teaching about a way of looking at things in buddhism . this teaching advises a path of moderation , taking a middle way between extremes . in particular , the buddha advises against taking a either an overly hard path or an overly easy path . he said that a person should not be either too self-indulgent or self-denying.the middle way is a different way of life . other pages . - buddhism
