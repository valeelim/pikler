Walker (tool)    walker ( tool ) a walker or walking frame is a tool for disabled people who need help to support and keep balance while walking . the rollator/walker is from sweden . these are popular items in the mobility aid field . generally there are three or four wheel rollators . most have some type of pouch or basket to carry items . rollators are often used for long trips or when a person does not want to be slowed down by their mobility equipment . rollators are often viewed as easier to use than a walker due to their ability to just be pushed instead of lifted .
