S-type asteroid     an s-type asteroid is an asteroid that is mainly `` silicaceous '' , or made of a lot of silicon . this is why it is called an `` s-type '' . about 17 % of all asteroids are this type of asteroid , meaning that they are the second most common after c-type asteroids . s-type asteroids are the most common asteroid in the inner main belt , but become very rare farther out in space . the biggest asteroid of this type is 15 eunomia .
