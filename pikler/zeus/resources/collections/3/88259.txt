Der er et yndigt land     `` der er et yndigt land '' ( `` there is a lovely country '' ) is the national anthem of denmark . it is written in 1819 by the danish poet adam oehlensläger . the music was composed by h.e . krøyer , in 1835. the anthem is one of denmark 's two official national anthems . the other one , `` kong christian stod ved højen mast '' , is known as the royal anthem , while this anthem is regarded as the people 's national anthem .
