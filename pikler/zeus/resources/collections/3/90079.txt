Lotus position     the lotus position ( sanskrit : `` padmasanam '' ) is a cross-legged sitting position which is common in meditative practices of hinduism . it is a part of the hindu yoga tradition . the position is said to resemble a lotus plant and to encourage proper breathing and to be helpful for physical stability . other websites . - animation - how to go into a lotus pose - padmasana ( lotus pose ) - crossing the legs - crossing the legs 2 - crossing the legs 3 - crossing the legs 4 - crossing the legs 5
