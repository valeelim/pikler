Szczecin Lagoon     the szczecin lagoon ( polish name ) or stettin lagoon ( german name ) or lagoon of the oder river is an inland water basin found in the southwestern part of the baltic sea . it is shared by germany and poland . these are the towns that can be found at the szczecin lagoon : świnoujście , ueckermünde , wolin ( town ) and nowe warpno . towns , ports and beaches of szczecin lagoon . poland . - towns : - nowe warpno - świnoujście - wolin - trzebież - brzózki - kopice - czarnocin - skoszewo - płocin - sułomino - popielewo - ports : - trzebież - stepnica - nowe warpno - wolin - beaches : - trzebież - popielewo - nowe warpno - stepnica germany . - towns : - ueckermünde - usedom - altwarp - vogelsang-warsin - garz ( uznam ) - kamminke
