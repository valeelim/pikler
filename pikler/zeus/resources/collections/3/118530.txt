War of the Worlds (2005 movie)    war of the worlds ( 2005 movie ) war of the worlds is a 2005 movie that has tom cruise and dakota fanning as the main people . it was directed by steven spielberg . it is based on the h. g. wells novel `` the war of the worlds '' . ray ferrier 's home town is attacked by alien tripods that were buried underneath the ground . he is then forced to flee the city with his two children and make it to boston where his ex-wife is located .
