Gander, Newfoundland and Labrador    gander , newfoundland and labrador gander is a town of almost 10,000 people in the central region of newfoundland , canada . it is home to the gander international airport , which used to be an important point for aircraft to refuel on long flights . most of the streets in gander are named after famous aviators , including amelia earhart , alcock and brown , charles lindbergh , eddie rickenbacker , marc garneau and chuck yeager .
