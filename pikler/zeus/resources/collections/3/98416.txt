Avant-garde     avant-garde ( pronounced ) in french means `` front guard '' , `` advance guard '' , or `` vanguard '' . the term is commonly used in french , english , and german for people or works that are experimental or innovative , particularly in the areas of art , culture , and politics . avant-garde represents a going against what is accepted as the norm , especially in culture . avant-garde art movements . - bauhaus - cubism - expressionism - impressionism - pop art - social realism - surrealism related pages . - bohemianism
