Physical body     in physics , a physical body ( sometimes called simply a body or even an object ) is a collection of masses , when you look at them as one object only . for example , a cricket ball can be understood as an object but the ball also consists of many particles ( pieces of matter ) . a human 's , animal 's or vegetable 's physical body is made up of organs and tissues and is a part of the total living being which in the human being may also include a psychological or spiritual component .
