Abbey library of St. Gallen     the abbey library of st. galllen ( german : `` stiftsbiblothek st.gallen '' ) is a library in st. gallen , switzerland . it was attached to the abbey of st. gallen . this abbey had a great importance in the middle ages . today , there are about 2 100 manuscripts from the middle ages , about 1 650 inucabula ( books and sheets printed before 1501 ) and early printings before 1520 , as well as about 160 000 books . the library is the oldest library in switzerland . the manuscript b of the nibelungenlied is kept here .
