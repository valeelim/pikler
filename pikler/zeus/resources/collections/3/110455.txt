The Other Wind     the other wind is the sixth and last of a series of books that are written by ursula k. le guin . it follows on from `` tehanu '' , the fourth book , and the short story `` dragonfly '' from the fifth book `` tales from earthsea '' . the book won the world fantasy award for best book in 2002 , and was also nominated for the nebula award for best book in the same year . other websites . - a review of the other wind
