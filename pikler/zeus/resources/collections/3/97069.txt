Muhammad ibn Jarir al-Tabari     abi ja'far muhammad ibn jarir al-tabari ( 838-923 أبي جعفر محمد بن جرير الطبري ) was one of the earliest , most important and famous persian historians and explainers of the qur'an , most famous for his `` tarikh al-tabari '' ( `` history of the prophets and kings '' ) and `` tafsir al-tabari '' . tabari ( persian : طبری ) , abu jafar muhammad ibn jarir at-tabari , his name means `` father of jafar , named after the prophet muhammad , son of jarir , from the province of tabaristan '' . he was born in amol , tabaristan , some twenty kilometres south of the caspian sea , in the winter of 838-9. he was precocious . he left home to study in a.h. 236 ( 850-1 ) when he was twelve . he retained close ties to his home town . he did return at least twice , the last time in a.h. 290 ( 903 ) when his outspokenness caused some uneasiness and led to his quick departure . bibliography . - bosworth , c.e. , `` encyclopedia of islam '' , `` al-tabari , abu djafar muhammad b. djarir b. yazid '' - rosenthal , franz , `` the life and works of al-tabari '' pp . 5–134 of volume one of the english translation of the `` history '' .
