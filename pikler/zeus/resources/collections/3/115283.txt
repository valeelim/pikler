Family tree     a family tree is a chart showing family relationships in a tree structure form . the more detailed family trees used in medicine , genealogy , and social work are known as genograms . as normally showed , a family tree `` grows '' down from the top , from the oldest generations at the top to the newer generations at the bottom . a tree showing the descendants of an individual ( illustration right ) will more closely look like a tree in shape ; one showing the ancestors of an individual ( illustration below in `` the family tree of sigmund '' ) will be wider at the top than the bottom . when looking at the closer to the bottom of the tree , it will show more recent generations , and when looking farther up , there will be older generations . the oldest family members go on the right maybe the left . related pages . - ancestor table - genogram
