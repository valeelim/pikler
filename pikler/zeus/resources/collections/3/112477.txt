List of Formula One Grands Prix     the following is a complete list of grands prix which have been a part of the fia world championship since it started in 1950. at the end of the 2008 season , 803 world championship grands prix will have been held including the indy 500 which was a part of the world championship from 1950 until 1960. major auto racing events are usually named `` grands prix '' , a tradition dating back to the first decade of the 20th century and the grand prix motor racing of the 1920s and 1930s . the grands prix races that are on the season calendar are in bold . the initial schedule included 20 races . the abu dhabi grand prix has been postponed because of civil unrest . it has not been decided if the race will be rescheduled or canceled . future races . - grand prix of the americas – scheduled for 2014 - russian states grand prix – scheduled for 2014
