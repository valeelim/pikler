Theatre (warfare)    theatre ( warfare ) in warfare , a theatre or theater is an area where there are many battles . theatres are bordered by peaceful areas . some wars are fought in one theatre . many wars , for example the seven years ' war have been fought in many different theatres . there were many theatres in world war i and world war ii . the napoleonic wars were the first multi-theatre war in modern times . the use of the word `` theatre '' for war was first seen in the book `` on war '' by carl von clausewitz . a `` theater of operations '' ( to ) is a sub-area within a theater of war . the boundary of a to is defined by the commander who is orchestrating or providing support for specific combat operations within the to . it means the territory , including adjoining sea areas and air space , in which a war was conducted . < br >
