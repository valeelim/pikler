Dr. Phil (TV series)    dr. phil ( tv series ) dr. phil is an american talk show hosted by phil mcgraw . it debuted on september 16 , 2002 , after mcgraw had success with the `` tuesdays with dr. phil '' segment on `` the oprah winfrey show '' . on both shows mcgraw offers advice in the form of `` life strategies '' from his experience as a clinical psychologist . the show is in syndication throughout the united states and canada , as well as a number of other countries . also , the show 's syndication contracts specifically state that if `` dr. phil '' is on another station , it can not air at the same time as `` oprah '' . the sixth season premiered on september 10 , 2007. it was announced on august 3 , 2005 that `` dr. phil '' had been renewed through at least 2012-2013 , lasting through the 11th season . in 2007 , dr. phil was nominated for a daytime emmy award for `` outstanding talk show host '' .
