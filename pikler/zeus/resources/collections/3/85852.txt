Bithynia     bithynia was an old kingdom of a roman province in the northwest of asia minor . it was at the coast of the black sea . it was named after the bithyni , a tribe that moved there from thrace . it was an important territory for the roman empire . bithynia was also the site of early christian activities in the first and second centuries ad . further reading . - stanley jonathon storey , `` bithynia : history and administration to the time of pliny the younger '' ( ottawa , national library of canada , 1998 ) isbn 0-612-34324-3
