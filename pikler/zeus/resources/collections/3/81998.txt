Trinculo (moon)    trinculo ( moon ) trinculo is a retrograde non-spherical moon of uranus . it was found by holman , `` et al . '' on 2001-08-13 , and given the designation s/2001 u 1. confirmed as uranus xxi , it was named after the drunken jester trinculo in william shakespeare 's play `` the tempest '' . related pages . - list of uranus ' moons references . - ephemeris iau-nses - mean orbital parameters nasa jpl other websites . - david c. jewitt pages - scott s. sheppard pages
