One Liberty Plaza     one liberty plaza is a skyscraper in new york city , united states . it is 226 meters ( 743 feet ) tall and has 54 floors . it was built in 1973. at first it was named the u.s. steel building , but later the name was changed to one liberty plaza . the singer building , built in 1908 and once the tallest building in the world , was torn down in 1968 to make room for one liberty plaza . other websites . - emporis.com - one liberty plaza - skyscraperpage.com - 1 liberty plaza
