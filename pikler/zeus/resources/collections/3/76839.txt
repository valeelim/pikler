Microform     microform is a name given to special photographic films . microforms contain reduced images of documents . usually , documents stored on microform are reduced about 25 times . microform is mainly used for archival purposes . there are three common formats : - microfilm , which comes in reels of film . - aperture card , this contains additional digital data ( as a punch card ) . - microfiche , a flat sheet with many pages . properly stored ( room temperature , 50 % humidity ) , microform should be able to last up to 500 years .
