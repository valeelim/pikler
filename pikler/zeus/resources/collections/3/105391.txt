Nusa Penida     nusa penida is an island to the southeast of bali , indonesia . the island is a subdistrict of the klungkung regency . there are two small islands nearby : nusa lembongan and nusa ceningan . the badung strait separates the island from bali while the lombok strait separates it from lombok . diving is very popular around the island and there are many macaques living on the island .
