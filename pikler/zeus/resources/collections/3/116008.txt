Rainer Küchl     rainer küchl ( *august 25 1950 in waidhofen an der ybbs ) is an austrian musician . he plays the violin and is a music professor . he started to play the violin at the age of 11. at the age of 14 , he studied music at the university of music and performing arts , vienna . he became the concertmaster of the vienna philharmonic at the age of 20. in 1973 he founded the küchl quartett which is known as the wiener musikverein quartett . other websites . - rainer küchl official website - vienna philharmonic orchestra - vienna musikverein - küchl biography
