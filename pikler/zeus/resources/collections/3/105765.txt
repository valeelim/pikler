National Register of Historic Places     the national register of historic places ( nrhp ) is the united states government 's official list of districts , sites , buildings , structures , and objects ( all called `` properties '' ) worthy of preservation . the list is kept by the national park service . the national historic preservation act of 1966 ( nhpa ) created the national register and the method for adding properties to it . there are more than one million properties on the national register , 80,000 are listed by themselves . the rest of the properties are smaller `` contributing properties '' that are part of historic districts . each year around 30,000 properties are added to the national register as part of districts or through individual listings . a few are national historic landmarks . other websites . - national register of historic places
