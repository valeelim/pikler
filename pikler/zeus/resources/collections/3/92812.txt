Yves Ternon     yves ternon ( born in 1932 in france ) is a french historian specializing on historical research of the jewish holocaust and the armenian genocide . he is a doctor of history of university paris iv sorbonne . he is also an active member of `` doctors without borders '' organization . books . - le massacre des aliénés ( with socrate helman ) , casterman , 1971 . - german doctors and national-socialism ( 1973 ) , - armenians , history of a genocide ( threshold , 1977 and 1996 ) , - the armenian cause ( threshold , 1983 ) , - investigation into the negation of a genocide ( brackets , 1989 ) , - the criminal state ( threshold , 1995 ) . other websites . - lemonde : yves ternon - ternon 's biography
