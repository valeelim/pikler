Rattlesnake     the rattlesnake is a kind of snake , a reptile . about 50 kinds of rattlesnakes are known . they are a kind of pit viper . people of science call the two big groups of rattlesnakes ( each called a genus ) either `` crotalus '' or `` sistrurus '' . all of them have a venomous bite , but some kinds are a much greater danger than others . the name `` rattlesnake '' is used because they have a special tail with several parts on the end that can make a loud `` bzzzz '' sound , or rattle . rattlesnakes live in north and south america , especially , but not always , in dry areas . they eat small animals such as birds and rodents .
