Tyra Banks     tyra banks ( born december 4 , 1973 in los angeles , california ) is an american supermodel and actress . she is hosts her television programs , `` america 's next top model '' and `` the tyra banks show '' . tyra is 5'11 '' tall . she is the ceo of her own company , tyinc . she was the first african american model to appear on the cover of sports illustrated swimsuit edition . tyra is the `` youth correspondent '' for the `` oprah winfrey show '' . she signed a modeling contract with the elite agency when she was 17 years old . before she signed , tyra planned on attending loyola marymount university .
