Sarah     sarah or sara was the wife and half-sister of abraham in the old testament book of genesis ( chapters 12-23 ) . they got married in haran . she was barren for a long period.she had a child with avraham named isaac when she was 90. she lived until she was 127 , and was buried by abraham in hebron in what is now the west bank . her name was sarai but god changed it to sarah . sarah ( also spelled sara ) is also a popular hebrew female name in english . it means 'princess ' . if you lived in spain , the name would mean , `` fairy '' .
