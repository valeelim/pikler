Sergei Fedorov     sergei viktorovich fedorov ( russian : сергей викторович фёдоров , `` sergey viktorovich fyodorov '' ; born december 13 1969 in pskov , soviet union ) is a russian retired professional ice hockey forward and occasional defenseman who plays for the washington capitals of the national hockey league . he gained fame playing for the detroit red wings before tenures with the anaheim mighty ducks and columbus blue jackets .
