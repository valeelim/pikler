University of Portsmouth     the university of portsmouth is a university in portsmouth . it was started as the `` portsmouth and gosport school of science and the arts '' in 1869 , and gained university status in 1992. portsmouth has been rated as the 5th best modern university in the uk by the times good university guide . other websites . - university of portsmouth – the university of portsmouth official website . - university of portsmouth students ' union ( upsu ) – the university of portsmouth students ' union official website . - pure fm – the university of portsmouth student radio official website .
