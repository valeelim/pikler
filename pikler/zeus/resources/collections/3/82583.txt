San Luis Pass     san luis pass is a strait of water at the south-western end of galveston island off the shores of the u.s. state of texas . it connects the sheltered waters of west bay to the open gulf of mexico . there are also two mountain passes of the same name in the usa : - san luis pass , colorado skirting the san luis peak in the san juan mountains - san luis pass , new mexico other websites . - san luis pass fishing pier - a sedimentologic description of a microtidal , flood-tidal delta , san luis pass , texas
