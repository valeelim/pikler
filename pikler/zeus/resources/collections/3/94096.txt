T. V. Carpio     teresa victoria carpio , more commonly known as t. v. carpio , is a chinese / filipina singer , songwriter and actress . she is the daughter of asian singing star teresa carpio , with whom she has performed on stage as a backup singer . carpio played prudence in the 2007 musical movie `` across the universe '' . she has also appeared on television , including a tv movie and episodes of `` the jury '' and `` law & order '' . she made her broadway debut in `` rent '' as alexi darling and others , appearing from december 2006 until april 2007. filmography . - `` limitless '' ( 2011 ) ... valerie - `` across the universe '' ( 2007 ) ... prudence - `` sucker free city '' ( 2004 ) ( tv ) ... angela tsing - `` she hate me '' ( 2004 ) ... gail - `` the jury '' ... esme kingman ( 1 episode , `` mail order mystery , '' 2004 ) - `` law & order '' ... laundry lady ( 1 episode , `` patriot , '' 2002 ) - `` popstars '' ... top 26 finalist ( tv ) ( 2001 )
