Höxter Rural District     höxter ( ) is a kreis ( district ) in the east of north rhine-westphalia , germany . in 1816 the new prussian government created three districts in the area , höxter , brakel and warburg . in 1832 höxter and brakel were joined together as höxter . the present district was created in 1975 when the former districts warburg and höxter were merged . at the same time the municipalities in the district were joined to form the ten cities that exist today . other websites . - official website
