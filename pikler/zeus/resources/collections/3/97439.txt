Xenophilius Lovegood     xenophilius lovegood is a fictional character of the harry potter series . he is the father of luna lovegood ( friend of harry on the story ) , and the writer of `` the quibbler '' , a magazine in which he writes the things that just he believes . he and luna believe harry when he says voldemort has returned . lovegood was played by rhys ifans in `` harry potter and the deathly hallows '' .
