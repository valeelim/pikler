Patriarch Bartholomew I of Constantinople     ecumenical patriarch bartholomew i ( born 29 february 1940 ) is the ecumenical patriarch of constantinople , since 2 november 1991. he is the `` first among equals '' in the eastern orthodox communion , and the spiritual leader of 300 million orthodox christians around the world . bartholomew wrote a chapter with pope john paul ii in “ earth toward our children ’ s future '' . he wrote an essay , `` to commit a crime against the natural world is a sin ” in `` moral ground : ethical action for a planet in peril '' ( trinity university press , 2010 isbn 9781595340665 ) .
