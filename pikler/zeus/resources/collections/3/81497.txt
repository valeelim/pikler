Count Olaf     count olaf is the principal villain , antihero and primary character from lemony snicket 's `` a series of unfortunate events '' . his main goal throughout the series is to inherit or steal the baudelaire fortune . he attempts numerous acts to try to get that fortune , such as trying to marry violet , kidnap sunny , and trip klaus to have him fall and break his glasses ( miserable mill ) etc . he has a tattoo of an eye on his left ankle .
