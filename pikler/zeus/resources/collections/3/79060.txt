Giovanni Guaita     giovanni guaita is an italian historian , researcher of eastern christianity and writer . he is an assistant professor of the moscow linguistic university . guaita was born in 1962 in italy . he studied in different western european and russian ( moscow and st petersburg ) universities . he is the author of several books . three of these books are on the armenian theme . guaita also known as the compilator of `` the vocabulary of orthodox agiology '' . books . - `` 1700 years of faithfulness : history of armenia and its churches '' ( moscow , 2001 , re-published in 2005 ) - `` cry from ararat : armin wegner and armenians ' genocide '' ( moscow , 2005 ) . other websites . - biography
