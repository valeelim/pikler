Bernard Hinault     bernard hinault ( born 14 november 1954 ) in yffiniac is a french cyclist who won the tour de france five times . he is also one of only four cyclists to have won all three grand tours , the tour de france , the giro d'italia , and the vuelta a españa . he is the only cyclist who has won each grand tour more than once . he won the tour de france in 1978 , 1979 , 1981 , 1982 and 1985. he was first in the giro d'italia in 1980 , 1982 and 1985. he was first in the vuelta a españa in 1978 and 1983. hinault had the nickname le blaireau ( the badger ) . hinault says this was a local custom with cyclists when he was young . professional cyclists used the nickname because badgers do not let go of their prey ( animals they want to eat ) easily . people know him as a very independent and strong man . professional highlights . pro team sponsors . - gitane-campagnolo : 1977 - renault-elf-gitane : 1978-1983 - la vie claire : 1984-1986 main victories . - 1977 - 1978 - 1979 - 1980 - 1981 - 1982 - 1983 - 1984 - 1985
