6-1-1     6-1-1 is a special telephone number in the united states and canada . it is the number to call for phone service customers when there is a problem with phones in an area , or when a payphone is not working properly . many mobile phone service providers use 6-1-1 as a customer service number . the federal communications commission did not officially decide that the 6-1-1 number should be used for this . they decided to let it stay because it had already been accepted , or chosen , by the north america numbering plan .
