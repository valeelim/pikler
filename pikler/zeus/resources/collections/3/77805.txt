TAM Airlines     tam airlines ( ) is brazilian airline . tam was an abbreviation of , air taxis of marília . marilia is a town in rural são paulo state . tam was founded in 1961 , but since 2000 it has grown fast . it 's now the biggest airline in brazil and all of latin america . tam airlines began in 1961 , with the name `` taxi aereo marília '' ( marília air taxi ) . in 1971 , one of the airline 's pilot 's buys 33 % of the airline 's shares . in 1976 , the pilot founds an airline with the name `` transportes aereos regionais '' ( regional air transport ) . both `` tam '' and `` tar '' airlines were combined in 2000. this formed the tam airlines that we know of today . in 2010 , lan airlines and tam airlines were also combined . they formed the latam airlines group .
