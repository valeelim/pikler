Nottingham Forest F.C.    nottingham forest f.c . nottingham forest f.c . is an english football club . they are based in nottingham in the county of nottinghamshire and started in 1865. they play their home matches at a stadium called city ground . brian clough was one of their managers and when he was in charge , nottingham forest won two european cups and four league cups . since then they have dropped down to the 3rd division and back up to the second , they have had managers like gary megson , joe kinnear , david platt and frank clark . they have no manager now . their last one was colin calderwood .
