Mattertal     mattertal is a valley in the alps of switzerland which begins on the side of the matterhorn . the `` matterhorn '' is one of the most famous mountains in the world because of its beauty , and because it is hard to climb . the `` mattertal '' is a valley that was made by a glacier ( a slowly-moving river of ice ) moving down from the mountain . this can be seen from the deep u shape of the valley . ( valleys made by a fast-running stream are v-shaped ) . because the sides of the valley are very steep , there are often landslides . one can be seen in the photo taken near randa village . many houses in the villages are called chalets . these can be seen in the picture of täsch village . in the mattertal are villages which include zermatt , täsch , randa , grächen , and st. niklaus . many tourists like to stay in the villages to see the view , to go walking , skiing or mountain climbing . the traditional work of the people is dairy farming , but many people work in the tourist industry .
