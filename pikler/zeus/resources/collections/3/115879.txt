Maria Kirilenko     maria yuryevna kirilenko ( ; born 25 january 1987 in moscow , russian sfsr , soviet union ) is a russian professional tennis player . she won her first wta tour title in 2005 , defeating anna-lena grönefeld 6–3 , 6–4 in the china open . kirilenko reached # 19 , her career-high singles ranking , on the wta tour in june 2008. she is a good friend of maria sharapova . she is affectionately called makiri .
