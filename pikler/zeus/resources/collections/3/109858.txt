Antonio Pappano     antonio pappano ( born december 30 , 1959 in epping ) is a british conductor and pianist of italian origin . he is the conductor of the orchestra of the royal opera house , covent garden in london , and l'orchestra dell'accademia nazionale di santa cecilia in rome . pappano was born in england , but his family had come from italy . when he was 13 years old , he moved with his family to connecticut , in the united states . there he had his musical training , learning to play the piano as well as composition and conducting . he became a rehearsal accompanist at the new york city opera . pappano is the youngest conductor to lead the orchestra of the royal opera house in london , accompanying both the royal opera and royal ballet . he is also an excellent piano accompanist .
