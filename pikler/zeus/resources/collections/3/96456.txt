Eichsfeld Rural District     eichsfeld is a rural district in thuringia , germany , and is part of the historical region of eichsfeld . in medieval times , the district of eichsfeld was much largaer than the present rural district and was owned by the archbishops of mainz . eichsfeld was the only part of thuringia not to accept the protestant reformation , mostly because of the efforts of the archbishop of mainz . in 1801 , the clerical states were dissolved , and prussia took over . the present district was established in 1994 by merging the former districts of worbis and heiligenstadt . other websites . - official website ( german )
