Charles V of France     charles v ( 21 january 1338 – 16 september 1380 ) , called the wise , was the king of france from 1364 to his death and a member of the house of valois . he was born in vincennes , the son of king john ii of france and bona of luxembourg . after his father was captured by the english at the battle of poitiers ( 1356 ) , charles acted as the ruler of france . his reign marked a high point for france during the hundred years ' war . his armies recovered large parts of the territory that had been given to england at the treaty of brétigny .
