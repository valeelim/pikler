Gottfried Silbermann     gottfried silbermann ( born kleinbobritzsch , 14 january 1683 ; died dresden , 4 august 1753 ) was a very important german builder of keyboard instruments . he built harpsichords , clavichords , organs , and pianos . his organs were some of the best instruments in the south of germany , some of them still exist . he had different ideas about organ building from arp schnitger who was an organ builder in the north of germany . silbermann also built some of the earliest pianos . other websites . - www.silbermann.org gottfried-silbermann-society
