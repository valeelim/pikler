Toyota RAV4     the toyota rav4 is a small sized suv made by toyota . toyota has made the rav4 since 1994 , but has sold it in america since 1996. the rav4 is sold in a 4-door with an optional v6 engine , the rav4 is more of a car-like suv than a truck sized one . the rav4 ev was an electric version sold in california from 1996 to 2003. the rav4 shares competition with the similar honda cr-v , subaru forester and ford escape . rav4 stands for recreational active vehicle with 4-wheel-drive but rav4s may just come with 2-wheel drive .
