Alvaneu     alvaneu is a municipality in the district of albula in the canton of graubünden in switzerland . it consists of alvaneu-dorf and alvaneu-bad . the area is 35.68 km² and the population is 421 people . on 1 january 2015 the former municipalities of alvaneu , alvaschein , brienz/brinzauls , mon , stierva , surava and tiefencastel merged into the new municipality of albula/alvra . this table shows the languages of alvaneu : other websites . - gemeinde alvaneu -- official site of the municipality - bad alvaneu -- thermal baths
