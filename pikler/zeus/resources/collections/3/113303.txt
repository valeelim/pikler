Islamgarh     islamgarh ( ) ( or akhalgarh , where `` akhal '' means the immortal and `` garh '' means `` place of '' ) is a small new town near the village of chak haryam , that is close to the mangla dam and one of 25 towns in the union councils of pakistan is the chakswari tehsil , mirpur district of azad jammu and kashmir , pakistan . 10,082 people lived there in 1998 .
