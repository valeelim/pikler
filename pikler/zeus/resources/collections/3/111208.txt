Treaty of Berlin (1889)    treaty of berlin ( 1889 ) the treaty of berlin was signed on june 14 , 1889 between the united states , the british empire , and the german empire . based on the treaty , samoa became independent and was allowed to choose its own king to rule it . however , the king would be advised by the united states , great britain , and germany . in 1899 , the treaty was replaced by the treaty of berlin ( 1899 ) . other websites . - whkmla : history of samoa , 1830-1899
