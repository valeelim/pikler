Troy Aikman     troy kenneth aikman ( born november 21 , 1966 in west covina , california ) is a former american football quarterback for the dallas cowboys of the national football league , and is now a television sportscaster for the fox television network . he is also a shared owner of the nascar nextel cup racing team , hall of fame racing , along with fellow former cowboys quarterback , roger staubach . he was chosen for the pro football hall of fame in 2006 .
