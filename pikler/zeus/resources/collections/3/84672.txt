Limerick     limerick ( ) is a city and the county seat of county limerick in the historical province of munster , in the midwest of ireland . the city lies on the river shannon , with three main crossing points near the city centre and has a 2006 population of 91,000 inhabitants within its urban area . other websites . - limerick city council - limerickpride - architecture of limerick - various online history resources from limerick city library
