Edmund Spenser     edmund spenser ( ; c. 1552 â€“ 13 january 1599 ) was an important poet from england . he is most famous for his epic poem `` the faerie queene '' , which talks about different knights who fight against evil . the poem also praises queen elizabeth i of england . he wrote also many sonnets and poems . the sonnets were collected under the name of amoretti . edmund spenser invented a new rhyme-scheme for sonnet ( abab bcbc cdcd ee ) and a nine-line stanza ( ababbcbcc ) . these forms are today called spenserian sonnet and spenserian stanza . spenserian stanza was later used by many poems , among others by george gordon byron , percy bysshe shelley , john keats and alfred tennyson .
