William R. King     william rufus devane king ( april 7 , 1786 – april 18 , 1853 ) was a u.s. representative from north carolina , a senator from alabama , and the thirteenth vice president of the united states . excluding john tyler and andrew johnson — both of whom ascended to the presidency — he was the shortest-serving person to occupy that office ( 45 days , `` see list of u.s. vice presidents by time in office '' ) .
