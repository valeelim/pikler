Daniel Gonzales Guiza     daniel gonzalez güiza ( born august 17 1980 , jerez de la frontera , cádiz ) is a spanish football player . he started his football career at xerez cd in 1999. he scored 27 goals and he became a top scorer when he was at mallorca in 2007/2008 season . he was then transferred to fenerbahçe . he was called for the first time to spanish national football team in 2008 ; in euro 2008 he scored 2 goals .
