John Mayhew     john mayhew ( 27 march 1947 - 26 march 2009 ) was an english - australian musician . he was the third drummer for the progressive rock band genesis . mayhew was a part of the band from september 1969 until july 1970. he replaced john silver as the drummer for the band in august 1969 . mayhew appears on the album `` trespass '' and the `` genesis archive 1967-75 '' box set . he was replaced in 1970 by phil collins . for many years mayhew 's life after playing for genesis was a mystery to most fans . it was said that his `` trespass '' royaltycheque had gone unclaimed . there were rumors that he was dead or missing . in 1979 , he moved to australia . he eventually found work as a carpenter . he is now an australian citizen . in 2006 , mayhew attended the genesis convention in london along with anthony phillips and steve hackett . he even played drums for a tribute band 's performance of `` the knife '' ( from `` trespass '' ) . he died a day before his birthday from a heart attack .
