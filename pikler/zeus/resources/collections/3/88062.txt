Tara (Devi)    tara ( devi ) in hinduism , the goddess tara ( sanskrit : tārā , devanagari : तारा ) meaning `` star '' is the second of the ten mahavidyas or `` great wisdom ( goddesse ) s '' , tantric manifestations of mahadevi , kali or parvati . as the star is seen as a beautiful but perpetually self-combusting thing , so tara is seen at core as the hunger that propels all life . in the hindu epic the ramayana , tara is the name of bali 's queen . bali is the monkey king who is killed by rama , at the behest of his brother sugriva .
