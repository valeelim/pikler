Jamaica coalition     a jamaica coalition was the name given to a possible coalition government of germany . the government would have been formed by the cdu/csu , the fdp and the greens . their party colours are black , yellow and green , the same colours as the flag of jamaica . a jamaica coalition was most talked about after the 1995 election in germany when the cdu/csu only had a few more seats that the spd , and needed more seats the its usual partner the fdp had in order to have a majority .
