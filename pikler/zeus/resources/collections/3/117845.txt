Mirpur, Kashmir    mirpur , kashmir mirpur ( ) is the largest city in azad jammu and kashmir , and the capital of mirpur district and formerly the mirpur division . although it is now part of kashmir , up until the 1846 treaty of amritsar mirpur was part of the punjab . the population are pahari and the language they speak is potowari . mirpur is located at the extreme south of what is now known as azad kashmir at a elevation of 459 metres ( 1509 ft ) , not far from islamgarh . it is 161 kilometres ( 100 miles ) from islamabad via the grand trunk road and 295 kilometres ( 183 miles ) from muzaffarabad , the capital of azad jammu and kashmir .
