Ghizar District     ghizar district is northernmost part of the northern areas of pakistan . its capital is gahkuch . ghizer is also a contact point between gilgit district and chitral district ( which are connected by shandur pass ) . ghizer is a multi-ethnic district and three major languages are spoken : khowar , shina and burushaski . there are also a few wakhi speakers in ishkoman . tehsils . - gupis tehsil - yasin tehsil - ishkomen tehsil - puniyal tehsil
