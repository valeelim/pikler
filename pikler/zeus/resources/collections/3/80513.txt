Bernese Jura     bernese jura ( german : '' berner jura '' , french : `` jura bernois '' ) is a part of the canton of bern in switzerland . it is the french speaking part of the canton . it was made after the canton of jura split from the canton of bern . the bernese jura is made of three districts , courtelary , la neuveville and moutier . the people living there are mainly protestants , they chose to stay with the canton of bern in 1979 .
