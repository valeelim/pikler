Loamhedge     loamhedge is a fantasy book written by brian jacques in 2003. it is the sixteenth book in the `` redwall '' series . it was translated into french and russian , and was a `` new york times '' bestseller . `` loamhedge '' is about a young hare named martha who can not move her legs , and must use a wheelchair . it is also about the travels of her brother horty , along with friends bragoon , saro , fenna , and springald , who are trying to find a cure for martha .
