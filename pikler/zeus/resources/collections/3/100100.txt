Gospel of Mark     the gospel of mark is a book of the bible . it is the second book of the new testament and comes after the gospel of matthew even though it may have been written before it . the writer is believed to have been john mark , a friend of simon peter , who was one of the twelve apostles of jesus . the four gospels tell the story of the life of jesus . the word `` gospel '' means `` good news '' . the gospel of mark moves quickly , and the story shows how much jesus worked during his short 3-year ministry . mark 's gospel tells many things about jesus which are also found in the gospel of matthew and the gospel of luke . related pages . - jesus - bible - christianity
