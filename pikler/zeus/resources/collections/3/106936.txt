Eurovision Song Contest 2008     the eurovision song contest 2008 was a talent contest hosted in belgrade , serbia . it was won by dima bilan of russia , singing the song `` believe '' . it was a big television event , watched by 250 million people around the world . in 2008 the contest was split into two semifinals , with 19 countries in each semifinal . the first semifinal was held on may 20 , second semifinal on may 22 , and the final on may 24. semi-finals . semi-final 1 . - the first semi-final round was held on 20 may 2008 - the color peach shows which countries qualified for the final - flax shows the entry chosen by the jury to go to the final semi-final 2 . - the second semi-final round was held on 22 may 2008 - the color peach shows which countries qualified for the final - flax shows the entry chosen by the jury to go to the final the final was held on 24 may 2008 .
