Live Session EP     live session ep is an ep released by the rock band panic at the disco on june 13 , 2006. it is available only as a digital download from the itunes store . it has a more acoustic feel to the songs . track listing . 1 . `` i write sins not tragedies '' ( live ) – 3:06 1 . `` lying is the most fun a girl can have without taking her clothes off '' ( live ) – 3:09 1 . `` but it 's better if you do '' ( live ) – 2:50
