Sesame Street     sesame street is an ameircan children 's television series with human actors and jim henson 's muppets . the show deals with issues such as music , song , alphabet , numbers , and teaching children basics in learning . part of the profits , go to an international project for children 's schools . the show has been on tv since november 10 , 1969 , becoming one of the most successful children 's television programs in america . jim henson made the muppets , and a lot of writers and puppeteers worked together to make the show . the muppets were used afterwards in a different show called `` the muppet show '' . `` sesame street '' has been on tv in 120 countries all over the world . more than 4000 episodes have been made over 40 seasons . in 2015 , new episodes started airing on the hbo channel . after five seasons on hbo , the show returns to pbs as its first-run program . the muppets . - big bird is a very tall yellow bird . his best friend is snuffleupagus who looks like a mammoth with no tusks . - kermit the frog is one of the first muppets designed and built by jim henson . borgenicht calls kermit `` funny , ironic , and always the voice of reason amidst the insanity around him ; the calm in the eye of the storm '' .
