Mortadella di Bologna     mortadella di bologna is an italian kind of sausage . it is made of ground pork meat . salt , pepper , sugar as well as small cubes of pork fat are added . it is then made into big sausages . it is then cooked in special ovens at 90°c ( 194° fahrenheit ) . some kinds of mortadella have other spices in them . as a protected geographical status food , mortadella di bologna must come from the bologna province .
