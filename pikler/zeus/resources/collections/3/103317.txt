Michelle Branch     michelle jacquet branch-landau ( born july 2 , 1983 ) is a singer from the united states . she writes songs and plays the guitar . she made her first album , `` broken bracelet '' , in 2000 , and released the platinum-selling albums `` the spirit room '' and `` hotel paper '' in august 2001 and june 2003 , respectively . during this period , she worked with carlos santana on the single `` the game of love '' , which won a grammy award . in 2004 , she formed the musical duo the wreckers with fellow musician jessica harp .
