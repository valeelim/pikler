18 (number)    18 ( number ) eighteen is a number . it comes between seventeen and nineteen , and is an even number . it is divisible by 1 , 2 , 3 , 6 , 9 and 18. in most countries it is the age of majority , age of consent , and voting age . in roman numerals , 18 is written as xviii . the number 18 has many multiples such as : 36 , 54 , 72 , 90 , 108 , and so on . the prime factorization of 18 is 3x3x2 in expanded form , and 3^2x2 in factored form .
