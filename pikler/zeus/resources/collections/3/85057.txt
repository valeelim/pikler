Oak Island, North Carolina    oak island , north carolina oak island , north carolina is a town mostly on oak island ( which also contains the town of caswell beach and the north carolina baptist assembly at fort caswell ) in brunswick county , north carolina ; a small part of the town extends onto the mainland north of the island 's bridge . 6,571 people lived there at the 2000 census . it was founded in 1999 by the consolidation of the towns of long beach and yaupon beach . its main industry is tourism ; the town has about 30,000-40,000 people in the summer . other websites . - oak island , nc city guide - official town site - southport-oak island area chamber of commerce
