Halifax Explosion     the halifax explosion took place on december 6 , 1917 when the ss `` mont-blanc '' , a french cargo ship loaded with explosives collided with the ss `` imo '' , a norwegian ship in the harbour in halifax , nova scotia , canada . 2,000 people were killed and over 9,000 people were injured . this was the largest man-made explosion in history until july , 1945 when the first atomic bomb blew up . other websites . - cbc website about the halifax explosion
