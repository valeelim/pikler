Haan     haan is a town in the district of mettmann in north rhine-westphalia , germany . since 1975 , gruiten belongs to haan . the twin towns of haan are : eu , france , since 1967 berwick-upon-tweed , great britain , since 1982 bad lauchstädt , germany , since 1990 dobrodzień , poland , since 2004 the local gymnasium is also twinned with liceo scientifico of forte dei marmi , italy .
