Sokoto     sokoto is a city in the northwest of nigeria , near to where the sokoto river and rima river meet . the people in sokoto are mostly muslims . sokoto was the capital city of gobir from the 10th century . sokoto was made as a caliphate , an islamic state , in the 19th century . from around 1900 , the british took control of sokoto , which then made up a large part of the north-west corner of nigeria . sokoto became a province ( area ) of the british colony of nigeria .
