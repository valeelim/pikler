Alchevsk     alchevsk ( ) is a town in the luhansk oblast of south-eastern ukraine . alchevsk is one of the largest industrial centers in the donbas , and comprises a quarter of the oblast 's entire production . the city economy depends on ojsc `` alchevsk iron & steel works '' and `` alchevsk coke-chemical plant '' companies . the city was known as `` voroshilovsk '' ( ) from 1931 to 1961 , and then kommunarsk ( ) until 1992 .
