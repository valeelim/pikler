Mühledorf, Solothurn    mühledorf , solothurn mühledorf is a former municipality in the bucheggberg district in the canton of solothurn , switzerland . on 1 january 2014 the former municipalities of aetingen , aetigkofen , bibern , brügglen , gossliwil , hessigkofen , küttigkofen , kyburg-buchegg and tscheppach merged into the new municipality of buchegg .
