Gustatory system     the gustatory system is the sensory system for the sense of taste . the human body can be stimulated by certain things . this stimuli is measured by sensory receptors and the energy is converted to something our brain can understand . there are receptors in our tongue which can measure four main things . this is the simplest receptor found in the mouth . sour tastes are usually received from acidic compounds . bitter tastes are important , since poisons are usually bitter and therefore we need to know whether or not to spit a bitter food out . this is the most complicated receptor found in the mouth . food which has a lot of carbohydrates such as glucose are sweet .
