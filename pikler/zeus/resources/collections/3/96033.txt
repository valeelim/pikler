Camelot     in the legends of king arthur , camelot is arthur 's castle . in fiction about the middle ages , arthur rules scotland from camelot and holds feasts at his round table . the round table , which has no legs or head , symbolized the idea of a good king because every knight who sat at it had an equal place . since camelot 's original place is still uncertain , the truth about it , if it ever existed , is still unknown . camelot once held many great things such as the holy grail and the round table , which brought arthur 's best knights together . the end of camelot was caused by sir lancelot 's and queen guinevere 's love affair , which made camelot plunge into civil-war .
