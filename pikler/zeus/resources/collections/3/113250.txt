Singapore dollar     the singapore dollar is the official currency or money of singapore . the symbol of the singapore dollar when written is ( $ ) . 1 singapore dollar is divided into 100 cents . there are 2 , 5 , 10 , 50 , 100 , 1000 and 10,000 dollars notes and 5c , 10c , 20c , 50c , and $ 1 coins in use . ways of writing . - $ 1 - means one dollar - s $ 1 - means one singapore dollar - sgd 1 - means one singapore dollar without the dollar sign ( used most in foreign exchanges ) the singapore dollar can be used in brunei due to the agreement between the singapore government and the brunei government . both the singapore dollar ( sgd ) and the brunei dollar ( bnd ) are accepted in stores of singapore and brunei .
