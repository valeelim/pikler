Metal leaf     metal leaf is a type of metallic foil used mostly for decoration . gold leaf , a thin layering of gold foil , is the most common form of metal leaf , and the two words are often mixed up . only very malleable metals can be made into leaf . those metals are easily shaped , but at the same time very hard to break . metal leaf is seen in various fields other than in decoration , sometimes used in art and gilding , its purposes in some cultures varies . references . - patricia e. nimocks , `` decoupage '' . 1968 , scribner .
