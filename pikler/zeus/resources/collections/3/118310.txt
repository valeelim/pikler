Requiem (Mozart)    requiem ( mozart ) the requiem mass in d minor k626 is a famous piece of music by wolfgang amadeus mozart which he composed in 1791. this requiem ( a 'requiem ' is a mass ( church service ) to remember people who have died ) , was mozart 's last composition . he wrote it as he lay dying himself . it seems that he did not manage to finish it before he died , and that it was finished by another musician called franz xaver süssmayr . we do not know exactly how much of the music is by mozart , and how much süssmayr had to add . there have been lots of stories and ideas about how the requiem was composed , and it is still a problem that is often discussed by musicians .
