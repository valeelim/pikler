Ringfort     a ring fort is a fortified settlement . ring forts were made to protect villages , mostly in the middle ages . in ireland where many such forts can be found , they are also known as ráth , caiseal , cathair and dún in the early irish sources . a `` ráth '' ( anglicised rath ) , was made of earth , `` caiseal '' ( northwestern ireland , anglicised cashel ) and `` cathair '' ( southwestern ireland ) were built of stone . a `` dun '' is a more prestigious site , the seat of some kind of ruler , the term is applied to promontory forts as well .
