Peeter Volkonski     peeter volkonski peeter ( also piotr andreevich volkonsky ) ( born december 9 , 1954 ) , in tallinn , estonia ) is a estonian actor and rock-musician composer . he is a prince from the rurik dynasty . he is the key art figure in estonia . peeter volkonski is a descendant of an aristocratic princely family , son of prince andrei volkonsky . hsh prince pyotr mikhailovich volkonsky is a great-great-great-great-grandfather of prince peeter volkonski .
