Shadow of the Hegemon     shadow of the hegemon ( 2001 ) is the second book in the `` ender 's shadow '' series ( also called the `` bean quartet '' ) by orson scott card . it is also the sixth book in the ender 's game series . it is told mostly from the point of view of bean , a mostly minor character in the first novel `` ender 's game '' . other webs . - about the novel `` shadow of the hegemon '' from card 's website
