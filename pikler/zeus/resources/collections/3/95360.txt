Hồ Ngọc Hà     hồ ngọc hà ( born 25 november , 1984 ) is a vietnamese singer , model and actress . she became famous when she won 1st prize of super model contest of vietnam in 2003 and 2004 , then she turned to act as singer . hồ ngọc hà was born in đồng hới city , quảng bình province to a banking family . her grandfather was of french descent . she studied piano at military college of arts . she is now living in ho chi minh city . other websites . - official website
