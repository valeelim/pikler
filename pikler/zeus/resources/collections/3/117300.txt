K-Swiss     k-swiss , inc. is an american footwear company . it is based in westlake village , california . it designs , develops , and markets a range of athletic shoes under the k-swiss brand and its wholly owned subsidiary royal elastics , inc. k-swiss was founded in 1966 in los angeles , california , by two swiss brothers who became interested in tennis after emigrating to the united states , where they introduced the first leather tennis shoes .
