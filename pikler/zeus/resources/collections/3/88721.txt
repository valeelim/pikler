Will &amp; Grace    will & amp ; grace will & grace is an emmy winning american comedy television show . the show is set in new york city . it features four best friends , will truman ( eric mccormack ) , grace adler ( debra messing ) , jack mcfarland ( sean hayes ) and karen walker ( megan mullally ) . it was first aired in series beginning 1998 and series finale aired in 2006. it ran for 8 seasons for a total of 194 episodes . cast . - eric mccormack as will truman - debra messing as grace adler - sean hayes as jack mcfarland - megan mullally as karen walker
