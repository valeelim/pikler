Tuscan Archipelago     the tuscan archipelago is a chain of islands between the ligurian sea and tyrrhenian sea , west of tuscany , italy . the archipelago contains the islands of elba ( the largest island of the group ) , pianosa , capraia , montecristo , giglio , gorgona and giannutri , all of which are protected as part of the tuscan archipelago national park . other websites . - tuscan archipelago national park website
