Banana bread     banana bread is a sweet bread made with a batter that is full of mashed bananas , ground cinnamon , and toasted nuts . some may also have chocolate in them . it was first baked in the united states and became very popular because it was so easy to make . it is different from most breads because it is a quick bread , using baking powder to rise . there are many different kinds of banana bread . the dry ingredients are mixed together in one bowl , and the wet ingredients are mixed together in another bowl . the two are then put together , and baked , and then the making of a banana bread is done . other pages . - raisin bread - sweet bread
