Washington Redskins     the washington redskins are a professional american football team based in the washington , d.c. area . the team plays at fedexfield in landover , maryland . the team 's head office and training area are at redskin park in ashburn , virginia . they are part of the eastern division of the national football conference ( nfc ) in the national football league ( nfl ) . the team began in boston . they were first named for the boston braves baseball team . they were soon renamed the `` redskins '' . they kept that nickname after moving to washington . some consider it offensive to name a team after a race or ethnic group , resulting in protests and legal challenges , but the team owners have refused to think about changing the name . on june 18 , 2014 , the united states patent office decided that they were going to cancel the redskins trademarks and called the name `` disparaging to native americans '' . other websites . - washington redskins official web site - sports e-cyclopedia.com - espn 's redskin page
