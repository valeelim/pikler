Ounce     the ounce ( abbreviated : oz , the old italian word `` onza '' , now spelled `` oncia '' ) is a unit of mass in a number of different systems , including various systems of mass that form part of the imperial and united states customary systems . its size can vary from system to system . the most commonly used ounces used today are the international avoirdupois ounce and the international troy ounce . other pages . - fluid ounce other websites . - dictionary of units : ounce
