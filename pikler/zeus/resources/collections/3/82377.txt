Greeeen     greeeen or the green is a japanese j-pop group . the group released their first album in 2007. two of their most successful songs are `` ai uta '' and `` kiseki '' . greeeen is a group of four singers : - hide ( born april 3 , 1980 ) - navi ( born april 30 , 1980 ) - soh ( born february 2 , 1981 ) - 92 ( born march 21 , 1982 )
