Damme     damme is a municipality in the belgian province of west flanders , six kilometres northeast of brugge ( bruges ) . it is made up of the city of damme proper and the towns of hoeke , lapscheure , moerkerke , oostkerke , sijsele , vivenkapelle , and sint-rita . in 2007 , 10853 people lived there . it is at 51° 15 north , 03° 17 east .
