AFL-CIO     the american federation of labor and congress of industrial organizations , called the afl-cio , is a group of labor unions in the united states and canada . there are 54 national different unions in the group , and they speak for over 10 million workers . it was started in 1955 , when the american federation of labor and the congress of industrial organizations joined after a long argument . from 1955 to 2005 , almost all union workers in the u.s. were part of the afl-cio . however , in 2005 , some large unions ( like the teamsters ) left the afl-cio . other websites . - afl-cio official website - list of afl-cio affiliated unions - speeches from the 2005 afl-cio convention - teamsters ' disaffiliation letter to john sweeney , president afl-cio 2005 - workers independent news
