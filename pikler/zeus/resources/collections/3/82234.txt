America's Next Top Model    america 's next top model america 's next top model is a reality television series created and hosted by supermodel tyra banks . it began in 2003 on upn . it moved to the cw for its seventh cycle in 2006 when it replaced upn . after 22 cycles , the show has been canceled with the last episode is going to be aired on december 4 , 2015 a group of contestants are chosen to live together in the top model house and compete to become america 's next top model . each week , one girl is asked to leave ( called 'elimination ' ) because she does not have the standard and ability to become a top model . the elimination is based on a photoshoot or a commercial and the reward challenge is different every week . the last few girls travel to another country ( france , italy , japan , china , thailand etc . ) to continue the competition . near the end , they will do a covergirl commercial . then , the last girls appear in a final runway show against each other . the winner is then chosen by the group of judges that includes tyra banks and others .
