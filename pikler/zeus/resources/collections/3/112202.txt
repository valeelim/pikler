Johnny Bower     john william `` johnny '' bower ( born november 8 , 1924 ) , nicknamed `` the china wall '' , is a hockey hall of fame goaltender . awards . - hap holmes memorial award ( best ahl goaltender , 3 times ) - vezina trophy ( 2 times , at that time awarded for allowing the fewest goals throughout a season ) . - nhl first team all-star in 1961 . - les cunningham award as the ahl 's most valuable player three times consecutively , one of only four players in north american professional hockey history ( bobby orr , wayne gretzky and paul polillo being the others ) to do so at any level of play . other websites . - age never got in bower 's way - ahl hall of fame bio
