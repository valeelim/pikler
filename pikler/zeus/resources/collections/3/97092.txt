Neuendorf-Sachsenbande     neuendorf-sachsenbande is a municipality in wilstermarsch , in the district of steinburg , in schleswig-holstein , germany . the municipality has an area of . on 31 december 2006 , the population was 493. the town is has the lowest point in germany , which is below sea level . the town was made on april 15 , 2003 , when the municipalities of `` neuendorf bei wilster '' , `` sachsenbande '' , and `` bredensee '' were all joined together .
