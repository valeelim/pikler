Flores     flores is one of the lesser sunda islands , an island arc . its area is about . it goes east from the island of java . there are about 1.5 million people in flores . the largest town is maumere . flores is east of sumbawa and komodo . it is west of lembata and the alor archipelago . to the southeast is timor . to the south is the sumba strait . the flores sea is to the north . on december 12 1992 , an earthquake measuring 7.0 on the richter scale occurred . it killed 2,000 people in the town of maumere .
