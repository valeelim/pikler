Sling (weapon)    sling ( weapon ) a sling is a type of weapon which involves swinging a rock round to gain speed before throwing it . the sling is an ancient weapon which was used in the roman empire . a skillful slinger can hit a target 10x10 cm at 20 metres distance . a sling has operational range for some 200 metres . the most famous user of sling was david , the future king of israel , who killed goliath by slinging a stone on his forehead . in the middle ages , spanish peasants used slings against moorish cavalry . a hit on the horse 's head could kill the horse .
