Virginia Polytechnic Institute and State University     virginia polytechnic institute and state university ( or virginia tech ) is a public land-grant university in blacksburg , virginia . the campus was first opened in 1872 as a farming and mechanical college and covers 2,600 acres . it is also one of only a few public universities in the united states to have a corps of cadets . the virginia tech massacre , which happened on the 16th of april 2007 , was a tragedy in the history of the university . a student of the university named cho seung-hui killed 32 people before killing himself . this became the worst school-related massacre in united states history .
