Essen, Belgium    essen , belgium essen is a municipality in the belgian province of antwerp . the municipality includes the towns of essen centrum , heikant , horendonk , wildert , statie en hoek . on january 1 , 2007 essen had a total population of 17,143. the total area is 47.48 km² which gives a population density of 358 inhabitants per km² . the town is near the border with the netherlands . its train station is the last one in belgium , the next station is roosendaal , noord-brabant , netherlands . notable living inhabitants . - raf and mich walschaerts , cabaret duo ( kommil foo ) - frank focketyn , actor - zdeněk štybar , czech cyclist - tom meeusen , cyclist - bart aernouts , cyclist other websites . - official website
