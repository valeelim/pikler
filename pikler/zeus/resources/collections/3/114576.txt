Bohemian Rhapsody     bohemian rhapsody is a song by english rock band queen . it was written by freddie mercury , and was released on their 1975 album `` a night at the opera '' . while , at first , record companies believed the song would not receive radio airplay due to its length ( 5 minutes and 55 seconds ) and its difference to other songs of the time , it became # 1 on many charts . it is now played on the radio in full-length .
