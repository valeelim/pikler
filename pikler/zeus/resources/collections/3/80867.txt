Friedrichshain-Kreuzberg     friedrichshain-kreuzberg is a borough of berlin . it is a new borough , only made in 2001 by joining the old boroughs of friedrichshain and kreuzberg . it occupies and has a population of about 255,200 as of 2003. its current mayor is franz schulz ( green party ) . other websites . - official homepage - xhain.info - private portal ( in german )
