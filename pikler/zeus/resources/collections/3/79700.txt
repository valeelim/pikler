Max Mayfield     britt max mayfield ( born on september 19 , 1948 in oklahoma city , oklahoma ) is a meteorologist who served as the director of the national hurricane center from 2000 to 2007. as director , mayfield became a trusted voice in preparing for weather-related disasters , particularly those involving tropical storms and hurricanes . other websites . - national hurricane center - white house biography of max mayfield - onestorm.org family hurricane preparedness initiative .
