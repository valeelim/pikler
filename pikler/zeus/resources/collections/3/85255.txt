Tress MacNeille     tress macneille ( born june 20 , 1951 ) is an american voice actress best known for doing voices on the animated television series `` the simpsons '' , `` futurama '' , and `` animaniacs '' . her most famous characters on `` the simpsons '' include agnes skinner , brandine spuckler and lindsey naegle , while her performance as mom is her most notable `` futurama '' role . she also been the voice of the penguins in the disney tv series club penguin , and the voice of daisy duck .
