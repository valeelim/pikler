Borneo     borneo is the third largest island in the world . it is in the center of maritime southeast asia . this island is part of three different countries . indonesia , malaysia and brunei each have part of borneo . indonesia 's region is called `` kalimantan '' ( although indonesians use this word for the whole island ) . malaysia 's region of borneo is called east malaysia or malaysian borneo . the independent nation of brunei is a small part of the island . borneo has a rainforest that is in danger because it is valuable . lumber companies cut trees here illegally . borneo is surrounded by the south china sea to the north and northwest , the sulu sea to the northeast , the celebes sea and the makassar strait to the east , and the java sea and karimata strait to the south . it has an area of 743,330 kmÂ² ( 287,000 square miles ) . borneo is home to a few of the oldest rainforests in the world . borneo is home to a vast array of wildlife , including the endangered orangutan , as well as other animals such as monkeys , elephants , snakes , parrots , clouded leopards , hornbills , tarsiers and deer .
