Eta (letter)    eta ( letter ) eta ( uppercase/lowercase η η ) is a letter of the greek alphabet . in very early greek writing it stood for the consonant sound `` h '' , but in classical greek it stood for a long vowel `` e '' . in modern greek , it is pronounced as `` i '' . in the system of greek numerals , it has a value of 8. letters that came from it include the roman h and cyrillic и .
