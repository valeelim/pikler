King of the Klondike     king of klondike is the main chapter of the life and times of scrooge mcduck . scrooge goes to klondike , where he looks for gold at the white agony creek . scrooge goes to dawson where he meets an occasional thief , glittering goldie . goldie and scrooge fall in love but they not admit it to each other . soapy slick , an evil business man , tries to make scrooge to work free for him but scrooge destroys his bar . scrooge returns to white agony creek , where he finds gold and finally becomes rich .
