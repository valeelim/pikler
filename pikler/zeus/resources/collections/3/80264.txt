Skathi (moon)    skathi ( moon ) skathi or saturn xxvii , is a moon of saturn . it was found by brett gladman , kavelaars , et al . in 2000 , and given the designation s/2000 s 8. skathi is about 6.4 kilometres in diameter , and orbits saturn at an average distance of 15,576,000 km in 725.784 days , at an inclination of 149° to the ecliptic ( 150° to saturn 's equator ) with an eccentricity of 0.246. skathi may have been formed from debris knocked off of phoebe by big impacts at some point in the solar system 's history . its name comes from norse mythology , where skaði is a giantess ( female giant ) who is the wife of the vanir god niord .
