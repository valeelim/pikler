Steve Jablonsky     steve jablonsky ( born on october 9 , 1970 ) is an american music composer for movies , television and video games . jablonsky has composed the soundtracks to the movies `` the texas chainsaw massacre '' ( 2003 ) , `` steamboy '' ( 2004 ) , `` the island '' ( 2005 ) , `` transformers '' ( 2007 ) and `` d-war '' ( 2007 ) . he also helped compose some of the music to the video game `` '' , and composed the theme music for the bbc 's `` seven wonders of the industrial world '' in 2003. he continues to write music for the television series `` desperate housewives '' ( 2004-current ) since the fourth episode . jablonsky works in hans zimmer 's studio remote control productions ( formerly known as media ventures ) .
