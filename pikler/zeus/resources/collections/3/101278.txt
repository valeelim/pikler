Cough     a cough , also known as tussis , is an action the body takes to get rid of anything that irritates the lungs or throat . to do this , muscles in the thoracic cavity contract to make air leave the lungs with a lot of force . coughs often happen quickly and more than once , and are usually accompanied by a unique sound , also called a cough . coughs are more likely to happen when a person is sick , because infections often irritate breathing passages . another cause of coughs is a person breathing air that is not clean . causes . - lung cancer - influenza - sarcoidosis - pneumonia - tuberculosis - common cold - smoking
