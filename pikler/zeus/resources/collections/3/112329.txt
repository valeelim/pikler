Johnny Mowers     johnny mowers ( october 29 , 1916 in niagara falls , ontario , canada - december 7 , 1995 ) was an ice hockey goaltender who played for the detroit red wings , winning the vezina trophy in 1943. born in niagara falls , ontario , mowers played goal for the red wings on their way to the stanley cup in 1943 , shutting out the boston bruins twice in the four games .
