Parchim Rural District     parchim is a former district in the southwestern part of mecklenburg-western pomerania , germany . most of the district is now used for farming , but in the easternmost part there are several lakes , the largest of them being the plauer see ( 39 km² ) . the plauer see marks the western end of the müritz lakeland . until 1994 the present-day district was divided into the three districts of parchim , sternberg and lübz . other websites . - official website
