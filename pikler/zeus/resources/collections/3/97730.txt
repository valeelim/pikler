Tekken     tekken is the first game in the tekken series . it was released in arcades on december 9 , 1994. it was released on the playstation on march , 1995 in japan , november , 1995 in north america , and november , 1995 in europe . tekken had two modes , arcade mode and vs mode . extra modes would be added in later tekken games . also see . - tekken ( series )
