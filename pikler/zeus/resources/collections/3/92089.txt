Leipziger Land Rural District     leipziger land is a district in the free state of saxony , germany . it is southwest of the city of leipzig . leipzig city has its own government . the district was formed in 1994 by joining the old districts of leipzig , borna and geithain . the area south of leipzig city is a big lignite mining area . it will be turned lakeland in the near future . the lignite made the leipzig area the biggest industrial part of east germany . other websites . - official website ( german )
