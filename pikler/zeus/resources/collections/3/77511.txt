Die Linke     die linke or the left is a political party in germany . it was formed on 16 june 2007 , when the left party.pds joined with wasg to form a unified party . while the pds was considered by german authorities as extremist , the wasg was not considered extremist . related pages . - electoral alternative for labour and social justice / wasg - socialist unity party of germany other websites . - official website of die linke - the european left site
