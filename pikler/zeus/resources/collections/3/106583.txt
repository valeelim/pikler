Barium chloride     barium chloride is a chemical substance . it is among the most important salts of barium . it is soluble in water . it is a poison . when it is heated , it gives a yellow or green color to the flame . it is hygroscopic , meaning that it attracts water from the air . it is mainly used to purify brine but applications include fireworks . barium chloride is toxic . sodium sulfate may be an antidote .
