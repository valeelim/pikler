Changi Airport     singapore changi airport , or simply changi airport , is a large airport in asia , particularly in the southeast asian region , and is the main airport in singapore . it is located in changi on a site . the airport is operated by the civil aviation authority of singapore ( caas ) and is the home base of singapore airlines , singapore airlines cargo , silkair , tiger airways , jetstar asia airways and scoot . in 2015 , the airport handled a record of 55,448,964 passengers , a 2.5 % increase over the previous year . other websites . - singapore changi airport official site
