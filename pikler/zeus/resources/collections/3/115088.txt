Super Mario Land     super mario land is a platform video game made by nintendo for the game boy game console . it is one of the only two mario games designed by gunpei yokoi , and the first mario game for a handheld console . an evil alien monster called tatanga comes to a kingdom called sarasaland and kidnaps princess daisy . mario comes to save her and has to go through four worlds and defeat tatanga 's four minions to rescue daisy from tatanga .
