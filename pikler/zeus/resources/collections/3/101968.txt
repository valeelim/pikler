Book of Malachi     the book of malachi , is the last of the books of the old testament . the book is made up of four chapters and a total of fifty-five verses . the word malachi means `` my messenger '' . it is thought that the book was written by the prophet isaiah . the date of the book would then be sometime around 740 to 690 bc . other websites . - book of malachi ( kjv ) - book of malachi ( niv ) - theology
