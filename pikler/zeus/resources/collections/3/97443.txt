Bartolomé de Las Casas     bartolomé de las casas ( 1484 – 1566 ) , known as the `` apostle of the indies '' , was a 16th century spanish priest and writer , and the first bishop of chiapas , mexico . bartolome de las casas was born in seville , spain in 1484. he was there when christopher columbus got to seville in 1493 after columbus ' first trip to the americas . his father , pedro de las casas , and his uncle went to the americas in the second voyage of columbus that left in november 1493. with his father , he went to the caribbean island of hispaniola in 1502 together with nicolás de ovando , the new governor of the island . in 1506 he went to rome , italy , where he became a catholic priest . he went back to hispaniola in 1508 and moved to concepción de la vega , in the cibao valley , and got an encomienda ( land with the indigenous people living there ) . then he went in 1513 to cuba and the governor diego velázquez gave him an `` encomienda '' . but soon las casas began to understand that the native people ( taínos in cuba and hispaniola ) were treated as slaves in a very bad way , and he began to defend the native people .
