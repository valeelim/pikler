Bistriţa-Năsăud County     bistriţa-năsăud ( ; hungarian : `` beszterce-naszód '' ) is a county ( judeţ ) of romania , in transylvania . the capital of bistriţa-năsăud county is bistriţa . bistriţa-năsăud county has an area of 5,355 km² . the main river that flows through bistriţa-năsăud county is the someşul mare river . bistriţa-năsăud county has 1 municipality , 3 towns and 56 communes . municipalities . - bistriţa towns . - beclean - năsăud - sângeorz-băi
