Myra     myra is an ancient town in lycia . the small town of kale ( demre ) is situated near it today . it is in antalya province of turkey . it was located on the river myros ( demre çay ) , in the fertile alluvial plain between alaca dağ , the massikytos range and the aegean sea . nicholas of myra ( also known as `` saint nicholas '' ) did many things there .
