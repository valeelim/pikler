Heal's    heal 's heal 's ( `` heal and sons ltd '' ) is a british department store furniture and furnishing store chain with six stores . the stores sell an upmarket range of furniture , lighting , accessories , home and garden-wares . it was founded in 1810 by john harris heal and his son . at first the company made beds . the work of sir ambrose heal made the company famous . he worked there for 60 years , from 1893 to 1953. ambrose heal was one of the group of designers , industrialists and business people who founded the design and industries association in 1915. their slogan was `` nothing need be ugly '' . other websites . - heals website
