Amazing Grace     `` amazing grace '' is a poem written in 1772 by englishman john newton . the poem named , `` faith 's review and expectation '' , was where the song came from . it was set to music . a seventh stanza , by an unknown author , has been added to most versions since 1829 , beginning with the words , `` when we 've been there ten thousand years '' . in some versions , stanzas five and six to the song are cut and go directly to the seventh .
