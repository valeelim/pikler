Güstrow Rural District     güstrow ( ) is a former district in mecklenburg-western pomerania , germany . from 1621 to 1695 the present-day district was the small duchy of mecklenburg-güstrow . afterwards large parts belonged to the duchy of mecklenburg-schwerin , and a small part to the duchy of mecklenburg-strelitz ( see mecklenburg ) . the district of güstrow was made in 1994 by joining the old districts of güstrow , teterow and bützow . other websites . - official website ( german )
