Pashto language     pashto ( pushto or pakhto ) is the official language of afghanistan . it is spoken by the pashtuns living in afghanistan and pakistan . it is the provincial language in khyber pakhtunkhwa , pakistan . pashto belongs to the indo-european languages family . it has two main dialects , western dialect and eastern dialect . the small difference between these two dialects is in the use of sounds . related pages . - languages of afghanistan
