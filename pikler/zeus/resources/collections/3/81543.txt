Medellín, Spain    medellín , spain medellín is a village in the province of badajoz , extremadura , spain . it is notable as the birthplace of hernán cortés in 1485 and the site of the battle of medellín , during the peninsular war . the city was named after the roman general quintus caecilius metellus pius who founded it as a military base for his operations in western iberia . it was originally called metellinum . other websites . - official website of medellín
