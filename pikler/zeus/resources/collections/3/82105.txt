Tomb     a tomb is a place built to bury the dead , usually more elaborate and expensive than a grave . it is often intended to honor the dead , and be visited by the living . a tomb ( from greek `` τύμβος '' - tumbos ) is generally any structurally enclosed space or chamber used for housing the remains of the dead , and sometimes their possessions . the word is used in a broad sense to encompass a number of such types of places of burial .
