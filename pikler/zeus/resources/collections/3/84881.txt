Lava dome     in volcanology , a lava dome or plug dome is a round-shaped mound created by the eruption of viscous lava by a volcano . lava domes are formed when erupting lava is too thick to flow and makes a steep-sided mound as the lava piles up near the volcanic vent . lava domes have formed in mount st. helens and experts say a lava dome is growing inside the shinmoedake volcano in japan .
