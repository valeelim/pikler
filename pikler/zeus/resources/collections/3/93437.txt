Wesel Rural District     wesel ( ) is a `` kreis '' ( district ) in the northwestern part of north rhine-westphalia , germany . the district was created in 1975 by merging old districts of dinslaken , moers and rees , which were all created in 1816 when the area became part of prussia . the main river through the district is the river rhine . other websites . - official website ( german )
