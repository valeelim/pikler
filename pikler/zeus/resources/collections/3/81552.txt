Dsungaripterus     dsungaripterus ( `` wing of junggar basin '' ) was a pterosaur with a wingspan of 10 feet ( 3 m ) . it had an unusual bony crest running along its snout and long , narrow , curved jaws with a pointed tip . the crest may have acted as a rudder when flying or may have been a secondary sex characteristic . `` dsungaripterus '' 's head and neck were together almost a metre long . its most notable feature are its long , narrow , upcurved jaws with a pointed tip , making the animal look like a pair of flying tweezers . it had no teeth in the front part of its jaws , which were probably used to remove shellfish and worms from cracks in rocks or from sandy , muddy beaches . it had knobbly flat teeth more to the back of the jaw that were well suited for crushing shellfish .
