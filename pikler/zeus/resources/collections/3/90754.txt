Jehan Alain     jehan alain ( born saint-germain-en-laye , paris , 3 february , 1911 ; died near saumur , 20 june 1940 ) was a french organist and composer . he learned to play the organ at home on an organ that his father had built . in his short life he composed many works . his most famous organ pieces are `` trois danses '' ( three dances ) and a brilliant piece called `` litanies '' . alain was killed fighting in the world war ii . his youngest sister , marie-claire alain , is an internationally-famous organist and has made several complete recordings of her brother 's organ works .
