Irritator     irritator is a genus of theropod dinosaur which was discovered in the lower cretaceous of the santana formation . the first identifiable dinosaur from brazil and the first non-avian from south america , irritator has teeth similar to spinosaurus . it has a slender jaw with nostrils well back from the front of the snout ( all consistent with fish eating ) . it also had a large crest on the quadrate bone on top of the skull . it was named due to the feelings of the scientists who described it when they found that the snout had been artificially lengthened to resemble a plesiosaur .
