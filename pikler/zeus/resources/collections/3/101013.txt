G-Man     g-man can be any of the following : - g-man ( slang ) , slang for federal bureau of investigation agents , short for `` government man '' - g-man ( `` half-life '' ) , a fictional character in the `` half-life '' computer game series - the g-man , a nickname for g. gordon liddy on his radio show - g-man ( british artist ) , minimal techno artist gez varley
