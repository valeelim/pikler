Amiens Cathedral     cathedral basilica of our lady of amiens is a roman catholic cathedral in amiens , france . it is most commonly called amiens cathedral . the cathedral was built between 1220 and 1270. amiens cathedral was listed as a unesco world heritage site in 1981. it is known for the quality and quantity of early 13th-century gothic sculpture . with the cathedrals of chartres and reims it is considered one of the classical french cathedrals of the middle ages .
