Terabyte     a terabyte ( tb ) is a unit of measurement in computers and similar electronic devices . one terabyte holds 1000 gigabytes ( gb ) or a trillion ( 1,000,000,000,000 ) bytes , and 1000 terabytes make one petabyte . large organizations use terabytes of storage to hold massive amounts of data . people at home might use terabytes for backups . an external hard drive can be several terabytes . usually , one terabyte of space costs $ 50 .
