Calcite     calcite is one of the most widely distributed minerals on the earth 's surface . it is a common constituent of sedimentary rocks , limestone in particular . it is also the primary mineral in metamorphic marble . it is a chemical or biochemical calcium carbonate corresponding to the formula caco and belongs to the carbonate minerals . one can find it as a vein mineral in deposits from hot springs , and in caverns as stalactites and stalagmites .
