West Azarbaijan Province     west azarbaijan or west azerbaijan ( persian : آذربایجان غربی `` āzarbāijān-e gharbī '' ; kurdish : `` azerbaycanî rojawa '' ; azeri : `` qərbi azərbaycan '' ) is one of the 30 provinces of iran . the province of west azarbaijan covers an area of 39,487 km² ( or 43,660 km² including lake urmia ) . the capital city of the province is urmia .
