Baklava     baklava is a traditional ottoman turkish sweet pastry often eaten as a dessert . it is known in many countries of the middle east and western asia . greek and turkish cuisine both developed from the cookery of the byzantine empire . baklava is made of thin pastry layers filled with nuts , pistachios or walnuts . it sweetened with special syrup or honey . baklava can also be served with ice cream or kaymak ( clotted cream ) . its name derives from the name of balkans region . women make baklava for serving in eid ul-fitr and eid al-adha .
