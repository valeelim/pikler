From the Mixed-Up Files of Mrs. Basil E. Frankweiler (1995 movie)    from the mixed-up files of mrs. basil e. frankweiler ( 1995 movie ) from the mixed-up files of mrs. basil e. frankweiler is a 1995 television movie based on e.l. konigsburg 's novel `` from the mixed-up files of mrs. basil e. frankweiler '' . the story is about a girl and her brother who run away from home to live in the new york metropolitan museum of art and find what they think is a lost treasure . the children , claudia and jamie , are amazed with the treasure and would not leave without knowing what its secret is . lauren bacall stars in the title role . before this movie , the book was made into a 1973 feature movie starring ingrid bergman , later released on home video as `` the hideaways '' . cast . - lauren bacall as mrs. basil e. frankweiler - jean marie barnwell as claudia eleanor kincaid - jesse lee soffer as james lincoln `` jamie '' kincaid - miriam flynn as evelyn kincaid - mark l. taylor as ralph kincaid - m. emmet walsh as morris - devon gummersall as steve kincaid - del hunter-white as cora - warren munson as mr. jordan
