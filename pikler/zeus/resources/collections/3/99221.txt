Seer Gharbi     seer gharbi is one of the 51 union councils of abbottabad district in the khyber pakhtunkhwa province of the country of pakistan . it is in the southeastern part of the province , bordering the rawalpindi district of punjab province . seer gharbi means `` western seer '' . this is because there are two towns called `` seer '' and are very near to each other . the other seer is known as `` seer sharqi '' , which means `` eastern seer '' . on october 8 , 2005 , seer gharbi , like much of abbottabad district , was affected by an earthquake . however the number of people killed or injured was low , the damage was largely to buildings and damage to water supplies . the union council of seer gharbi is subdivided into three areas : basbher , malmola and seer gharbi .
