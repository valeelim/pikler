PMMP     pmmp is a finnish pop rock band . the band has two singers : paula vesala and mira luoti and three musicians mikko virta , juho vehmanen and heikki kytölä . the band writes their own songs with jori sjöroos . pmmp became popular in finland in 2003 , with the song `` rusketusraidat '' . their biggest hit is a song called `` oo siellä jossain mun '' . in november 2007 , pmmp released their fourth album `` puuhevonen '' which has songs for children . the members of pmmp . - `` paula vesala '' , `` december 10 , 1981 '' - `` mira luoti '' , `` february 28 , 1978 '' - `` mikko virta '' , `` march 22 , 1982 '' - `` juho vehmanen '' , `` march 31 , 1981 '' - `` heikki kytölä '' , `` october 14 , 1981 '' discography . albums . - `` kuulkaas enot !
