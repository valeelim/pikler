Arabic Wikipedia     the arabic wikipedia ( ) is the arabic language version of wikipedia . it started in september 2001. it was the 23rd largest edition of wikipedia by article count , but grew to 21st largest edition . as of august 2014 , it has over 300,000 articles , over 1,350,000 pages , over 625,000 registered users and over 15,000 files . it has been blocked in syria .
