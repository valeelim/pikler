Adam Dunn     adam troy dunn ( born november 9 , 1979 , in houston , texas ) , nicknamed `` big donkey '' , is an american retired major league baseball first baseman , outfielder , and designated hitter . he played for the cincinnati reds , arizona diamondbacks , washington nationals , chicago white sox and oakland athletics . he batted left-handed and throws right-handed . dunn retired after the 2014 season . other websites . - official facebook - `` jockbio.com '' profile
