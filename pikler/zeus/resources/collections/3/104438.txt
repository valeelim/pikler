Middleweight     in boxing , the term middleweight is used for a specific group of people based on weight . a middleweight is a boxer weight does not exceed 160 pounds ( 73 kg ) . boxing organizations created many divisions so that fighters could fight people of the same size . the first heavyweight champion under modern rules was irish boxer jack ( nonpareil ) dempsey . famous heavyweight champions have include bob fitzsimmons , nino benvenuti , rocky graziano , marvin hagler , bernard hopkins , roy jones jr. , jake lamotta , carlos monzon , sugar ray robinson , tony zale and sugar ray leonard .
