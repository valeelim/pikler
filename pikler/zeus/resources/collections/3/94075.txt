Annie Oliv     annie oliv ( born 1988 in jönköping , sweden ) is a swedish beauty queen , cello player and personal assistant to a disabled person . on september 8 , 2007 , oliv won the `` miss sweden world '' title . she later represented her country at miss world 2007. she placed 4th in the competition . she won the title `` miss world europe 2007 '' . annie oliv played cello during melodifestivalen 2008 in mickey huskics entry in semifinal number three on february 23 .
