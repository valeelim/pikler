Campus novel     a campus novel , also called the academic novel , is a novel that 's story is around or about a campus of a university . the genre is said to have started in the 1950s . `` the groves of academe '' by mary mccarthy , written in 1952 , is often called the first campus novel , but some have also said that novels such as c.p . snow 's `` the masters '' , of the year before , were campus novels .
