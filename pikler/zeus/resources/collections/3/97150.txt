Fulda Rural District     fulda ( ) is a `` kreis '' ( district ) in the north-east of hesse , germany . the district was created in 1821 , when the duchy of fulda became a province of hesse , and was split into four districts . the last change to the district was in 1975 , when the hünfeld district was added to the district . other websites . - official website ( german ) - all around information portal of fulda - 360 degree virtual tour through the hessian rhön
