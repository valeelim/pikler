MTV Movie Awards     the mtv movie awards is a movie awards show presented annually on mtv ( music television ) . it also contains movie parodies that used official movie footage with hosts and other celebrities and music performances . the nominees are decided by a special panel at tenth planet productions , the production company headed by joel gallen , who also created the mtv movie awards . winners are decided by the general public . presently voting is done through mtv 's official website through a special movie awards link .
