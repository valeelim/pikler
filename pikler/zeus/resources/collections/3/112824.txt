Cumhuriyet     cumhuriyet ( english : `` the republic '' ) is a turkish nationwide daily newspaper based in istanbul . it was founded on may 7 , 1924 by yunus nadi abalıoğlu . cumhuriyet newspaper includes columnists that are generally considered to share social democratic , secularist and republican ideas with its readers . cumhuriyet is sold about 60,000 everyday . ilhan selçuk , the editor of cumhuriyet arrested by police after the conservative-islamic government party akp has wanted to closed . related pages . - list of newspapers in turkey other websites . - cumhuriyet 's official website
