Toyota 4Runner     the toyota 4runner is a sports utility vehicle made by toyota . toyota makes the 4runner in tahara , japan as well as the hino factory . the 4runner was first sold in 1984 as a camper version of the toyota hi-lux , sharing the same parts and engines . the 4runner got a bigger v6 engine in 1988. toyota has redesigned the 4runner in many years of its life . the 4runner was sold as a 2-door model alongside the 4-door until 1992. the 4runner 's competition comes from the nissan pathfinder , jeep grand cherokee , and chevrolet blazer . the 4runner was a compact suv until 1996 .
