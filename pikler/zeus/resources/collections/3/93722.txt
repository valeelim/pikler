Shock Value (Timbaland album)    shock value ( timbaland album ) shock value is the second solo studio album by record producer and rapper timbaland . it is his fifth album overall . the album has a long list of guest artists , among them fall out boy , justin timberlake , the hives , keri hilson , nelly furtado , missy elliott , 50 cent , dr. dre , onerepublic , elton john , magoo and nicole scherzinger . the album debuted at # 5 on the u.s. `` billboard '' 200 , with 138,000 copies sold . it debuted at number ten in the u.k. and on the week of july 22 went to number five . the album then reached number two in the uk and number thirteen in france . the album was certified platinum in canada ( 100,000 units ) by the cria in august 2007. on the australian aria albums chart , `` shock value '' was certified 3x platinum with sales of 210,000+ copies . in bbc radio 1 's end of year chart for 2007 , `` shock value '' was the ninth most bought album in the uk .
