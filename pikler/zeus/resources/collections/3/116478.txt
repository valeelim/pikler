Indus river dolphin     indus river dolphin ( `` platanista gangetica minor '' ) are a subspecies of river dolphins . it is only found in the indus river in pakistan . they are very similar to the ganges river dolphin which is mainly found in the ganges and brahmaputra rivers in the republic of india , bangladesh and nepal . in fact , the two subspecies are often described together as the south asian river dolphin these animals are currently endangered species , mainly because of water pollution .
