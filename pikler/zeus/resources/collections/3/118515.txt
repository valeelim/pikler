Stormbreaker (movie)    stormbreaker ( movie ) stormbreaker is a 2005 british movie based on the anthony horowitz novel `` stormbreaker '' , part of the alex rider series of books . the plot is the same as the novel . there are some changes from the novel . for example , the main bad guy , sayle , is changed from lebanese to american , since the actor picked did not have a lebanese accent .
