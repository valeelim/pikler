Privas     privas is a commune of france and a `` préfecture '' ( capital ) of the ardèche `` département '' . with a 1999 census population of only 9,170 people , it is the second smallest prefecture of any department in france . there are four bigger communes in ardèche , called annonay , aubenas , guilherand-granges , and tournon-sur-rhône . the french footballer cyril théréau was born here . privas is twinned with : - tortona , italy - weilburg , germany - wetherby , united kingdom - zevenaar , netherlands - söderköping , sweden related pages . - communes of the ardèche department other websites . - official website
