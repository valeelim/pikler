Arno     the arno is a river in the tuscany region of italy . it is one of the important rivers of central italy . the river begins on mount falterona in the casentino area of the apennines . it first takes a curve to the south . the river turns to the west near arezzo . it passes through florence , empoli and pisa , then flows into the tyrrhenian sea at the marina di pisa . at a length of , it is the longest river in the region . a flood on november 4 , 1966 collapsed the embankment in florence . this killed at least 40 people . it also damaged or destroyed millions of works of art and rare books . 40 years later , hundreds of works have still not been restored .
