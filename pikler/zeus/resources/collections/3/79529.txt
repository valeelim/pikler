Bedouin     bedouins are an arab ethnocultural group . they are descended from nomads who lived in the arabian , syrian and saharan deserts . they call themselves the `` people of the tent '' , because they travelled around , living in tents . bedouin were camel raisers and drivers , sheep and goat nomads , cattle driving nomads , and merchants . today many bedouin have given up the nomadic life and live and work in towns . related pages . - arabian
