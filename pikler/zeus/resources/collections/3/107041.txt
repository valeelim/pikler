Mallacoota, Victoria    mallacoota , victoria mallacoota ( ) is a small town in east gippsland , victoria , australia . less than 1000 people live there . it is 25 kilometres off the princes highway and kilometres from melbourne . it is the last town on victoria 's east coast before the border with new south wales . mallacoota has a small airport ( mallacoota airport ) ymco ( xmc ) for private small planes . the bidawal aborigines lived there before europeans came . europeans came after about 1830. gold was found in 1894 , and was mined for three years . in the 20th century the logging , farming and fishing industries declined , and the tourism and abalone industries grew . the town 's largest business , the abalone co-operative , was started in 1967. other websites . - mallacoota district health and support service - local community radio station , 3mgb - mallacoota arts council
