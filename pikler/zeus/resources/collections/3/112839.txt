Heybeliada     heybeliada is an island in sea of marmara in turkey . it is officially a part of adalar district of istanbul . heybeliada has an old orthodox christian theology school , started in 1844. however this school was closed because of turkish laws on secularism in 1971. these laws on the separation of church and state have also closed all muslim theology schools . turkey and the eurpean union are looking at ways to reopen this school again . however to do this , the government must prepare a new laws that do not conflict with secularism .
