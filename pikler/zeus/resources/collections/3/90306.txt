Bali Nine     the bali nine is the name given to nine australian citizens arrested on april 17 , 2005 , in denpasar on the island of bali , indonesia . they had tried to smuggle 8.3 kg of heroin valued at about aud 4 million from indonesia to australia . andrew chan , si yi chen , michael czugaj , renae lawrence , tach duc thanh nguyen , matthew norman , scott rush , martin stephens and myuran sukumaran , all aged between 18 and 28 at the time of their arrests . after the trial , five of the bali nine received death sentences , and the others got 20 years or life sentences . renae lawrence is currently serving her 20-year sentence in kerobokan prison along with fellow australian schapelle corby . andrew chan and myuran sukumaran were executed by firing squad on 29 april 2015 .
