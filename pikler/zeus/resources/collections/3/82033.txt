St Albans     st albans is the main urban area of the city and district of st albans in hertfordshire , england . it is 22 miles ( 35 km ) north of london . st albans is the former roman town of verulamium . it was originally named verlamion by the celtic catuvellauni tribe , and verulamium by the romans . it was the first major town on the old roman road of watling street for travellers heading north . many of the roman walls were knocked down by william the conqueror and used to build st albans abbey , otherwise known as st albans cathedral . the town had many notable buildings but most were knocked down by henry viii . the only building left is the abbey . though many other buildings have been built nearby ye old fighting cocks , which claims to be the oldest pub in the uk , a roman amphitheatre and hypocaust floor , a disused bear baiting square in verulamium park , and the imposing rats castle at the top of hatfield road as well as roman verulanium muesuem the bones of st. alban , the first english martyr , were kept in the abbey in a shrine , but were stolen during the 19th century.they were smuggled out of the country into munich . a few years ago the german cathedral sent the shoulder bone of st. alban and it is kept in the shrine behind a curtain
