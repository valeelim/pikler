Lectern     a lectern ( from the latin `` lectus '' , past participle of `` legere '' , `` to read '' ) is a reading desk with a slanted top . it is usually placed on a stand some other form of support . documents or books are often put on top of a lectern to make it easier for someone to read them to other people . for example , for scripture reading , lectures , speechs or sermons . the height and slant of a lectern can usually be changed to so the person reading can stand properly and make good eye-contact with an audience .
