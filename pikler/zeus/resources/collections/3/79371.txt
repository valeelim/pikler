Côte-Nord     côte-nord ( french for `` northern coast '' , area 247,627.06 km² , or 95,609.34 sq mi ) is the second largest administrative region by land area in quebec , canada . it covers much of the northern shore of the saint lawrence river 's estuary and the gulf of saint lawrence past tadoussac . at the 2006 census it had a resident population of 95,911 inhabitants . other websites . - regional portal - côte-nord - côte-nord tourism
