LeToya Luckett     letoya nicole luckett ( born march 11 , 1981 ) , known professionally as letoya , is an american r & b singer , actress and songwriter . she is a former member of the r & b female group , destiny 's child with whom she won two grammy awards , but when the group was successfully prompted in the world she was forced to leave . when interviewed by mtv news , luckett said that she and latavia roberson created a new group called anjel . auditions were made , then naty quinones and tiffany beaudoin were selected as members . the group recorded demo songs . their production company which handled the group fell through , and the members of anjel went on to pursue solo projects . in 2004 , capitol records signed luckett , then she started her solo career . her solo debut album , `` letoya '' , topped the u.s. `` billboard '' 200 albums chart in july 2006 and was certified platinum by the riaa in december 2006. the album spawned five singles . two were released with promotional purpose , to see the audience reaction towards her , but `` torn '' would be her most notable song . it achieved records on bet 's top ten countdown show `` 106 & park '' . she was also named `` one of the best new artists of 2006 '' by aol and the `` top songwriter of 2006 '' by ascap . luckett 's second album , `` lady love '' , was scheduled for a spring 2008 release . it was released in 2009. she is now on road with the stage play tour `` rumors '' .
