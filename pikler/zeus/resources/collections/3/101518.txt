Stewartstown     stewartstown is a village in northern ireland , in the united kingdom . it is 5 miles from cookstown and 7 miles from dungannon . on 29 april 2001 the united kingdom government had a census and found that there were 608 people living in stewartstown . they also found that : - 25.3 % of people in stewartstown were over 16 years old and 14.8 % were over 60 years old - 46.7 % were male and 53.3 % were female - 72.9 % were from a roman catholic background and 26.2 % were from a protestant background - 5.1 % of people aged 16-74 were no job
