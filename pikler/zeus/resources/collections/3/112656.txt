Atlanta Hawks     the atlanta hawks are a team in the national basketball association . they play their home games at philips arena in atlanta , georgia . the hawks have played in several cities throughout the team 's history , and have also had other names . the team was first the buffalo bisons in 1946 , then moved to moline , illinois that same year , being called the tri-cities blackhawks until 1951 . ( they did not join the nba until 1949 ) . in 1951 , they changed their name to the hawks and moved to milwaukee , wisconsin , then moved to st. louis , missouri in 1955. while in st. louis , they won an nba championship in 1958. as of 2011 , this is the only championship the team has won . the hawks moved to atlanta in 1968 , and have been there ever since . other websites . - atlanta hawks at the new georgia encyclopedia
