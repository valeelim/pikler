Sofia Rotaru     sofia mihailovna evdokimenko-rotaru ( born in marshintsy , novoselitskiy rayon , chernivtsi oblast , ukraine on august 7 , 1947 ) , simply known as sofia rotaru , is a popular russian , ukrainian , moldavian and formerly soviet singer , actress and producer . rotaru now lives in ukraine . she has made many albums over the years ; some of the more famous ones are `` ballade about violins '' , `` lavender '' ( `` lavanda '' ) , and `` i still love you '' .
