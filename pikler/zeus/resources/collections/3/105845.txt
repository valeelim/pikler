Ronnie O'Sullivan     ronnie o'sullivan ( born 5 december 1975 ) is an english professional snooker player . he was born in wordsley , west midlands . he grew up in chigwell , essex and still lives there . he was world champion in 2001 , 2004 , 2008 , 2012 and 2013. o'sullivan 's first century break made at age 10. he has won the uk championship five times . this was in 1993 , 1997 , 2001 , 2007 , and 2014. he has won the masters seven times , in 1995 , 2005 , 2007 , 2009 , 2014 , 2016 and 2017. other websites . - official website
