Mettingen     mettingen is a municipality in the district of steinfurt , in north rhine-westphalia , germany . mettingen is about 25 km east of rheine and 20 km west of osnabrück . villages close to mettingen are recke in the west , neuenkirchen , ( lower saxony ) in the north , ibbenbüren in the south and westerkappeln in the east . the founders of the textile company c & a , clemens and august brenninkmeyer , were born in mettingen . also annika suthe , participant of the 2004 summer olympics ( javelin throw ) was born there . other websites . - official site
