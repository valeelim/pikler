Prana     prana is the vital air or breath of the human body in hinduism . it is also the vital , life-sustaining force of both the individual body and the universe . its was first written about in the upanishads . prana is in all living forms , but is not itself the soul . the word prana means `` breath '' in sanskrit . in yoga , prana is divided into different type . there are said to be five pranas or vital currents . they are called prana , apana , vyana , udana and samana .
