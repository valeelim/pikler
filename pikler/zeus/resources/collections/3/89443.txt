Audi     audi ag is a german automobile manufacturer with headquarters in ingolstadt , bavaria , and has been an almost wholly owned ( 99.7 % ) subsidiary of the volkswagen group since 1964. audi ag was founded by august horch and the current ceo of audi ag is rupert stadler . audi makes many cars , such as : - audi a1 - audi a2 - audi a3 - audi a4 - audi a5 - audi a6 - audi a7 - audi a8 - audi q3 - audi q5 - audi q7 - audi tt - audi r8 they also used to make cars like the audi a2 or the audi quattro .
