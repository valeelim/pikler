Mark the Evangelist     mark the evangelist ( מרקוס , ) was one of the followers of jesus christ . he is believed to be the writer of the gospel of mark . he is believed by some scholars to be the young man found in chapter 14 , verses 51-52 of the gospel . the copts think mark brought christianity to egypt . his roman name was marcus . it was his house where jesus and his disciples ate the last supper .
