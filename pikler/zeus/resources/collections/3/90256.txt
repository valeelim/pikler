Vergeletto     vergeletto is a municipality of the district locarno in the canton of ticino in switzerland . on 10 april 2016 the former municipalities of vergeletto , gresso , isorno and mosogno merged into the municipality of onsernone . since 1803 , the municipality of vergeletto also contained the current municipality of gresso . both municipalities were moved away from each other in 1882. the coat of arms of the municipality are taken from the garbani , or garban family for its french and northern american branch , starting from the village , who are people who sell things or people of power .
