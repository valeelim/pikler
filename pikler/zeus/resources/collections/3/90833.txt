Billboard (magazine)    billboard ( magazine ) billboard is an american magazine that lists the top albums and singles of the week . it is most famous for the `` billboard '' charts . `` billboard '' was started on november 1 , 1894 in cincinnati , ohio by william h. donaldson and james hennegan . it was called `` billboard advertising '' . it started doing music charts in the 1930s . other websites . - official `` billboard '' website
