Poprad     poprad ( ; , ) is a city in northern slovakia at the foot of the high tatra mountains . it is the biggest town of the spiš region and the tenth largest city in slovakia . as of 2016 , poprad has about 52,000 people . the city has an historic centre , an international airport and is the starting point of the tatranská elektrická železnica ( tatra electric railway ) -- a set of special narrow-gauge trains ( trams ) connecting the resorts in the high tatras with each other and with poprad . main line trains link poprad to other destinations in slovakia and beyond ; in particular , through trains run to prague in the czech republic .
