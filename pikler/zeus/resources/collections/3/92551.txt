Prignitz     prignitz ( ) is a `` kreis '' ( district ) in the northwestern part of brandenburg , germany . the present district was formed in 1993 by joining the old districts of pritzwalk and perleberg and some municipalities from the district of kyritz . the westernmost part of the district was a part of mecklenburg-western pomerania , and changed to be part of brandenburg on august 1 , 1992. other websites . - official website - portal site for prignitz
