Tarqeq (moon)    tarqeq ( moon ) tarqeq or saturn lii ( provisional designation s/2007 s 1 ) is a moon of saturn . its discovery was announced by scott s. sheppard , david c. jewitt , jan kleyna , and brian g. marsden on april 13 , 2007 , from observations taken between january 5 , 2006 and march 22 , 2007. the semi-major axis of tarqeq is 17,910,000 km and it orbits saturn in 894.86 days . the orbit is at an inclination of 49.90° ( to the ecliptic ; 49.77°to saturn 's equator ) , with an eccentricity of 0.1081. the diameter of the moon is 7 km . it is a member of the inuit group of non-spherical moons . tarqeq is named after tarqeq , the inuit moon god .
