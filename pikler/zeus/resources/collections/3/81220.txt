Victoria Beckham     victoria caroline beckham ( born 17 april 1974 ) is an english singer and fashion designer . she used to be a member of a pop group called the spice girls . she receives a great deal of media coverage due to being married to football player david beckham . her nickname is posh spice . she has married david beckham in july 1999. the couple have three sons : brooklyn joseph ( born march 1999 ) , romeo james ( born september 2002 ) , cruz david ( born february 2005 ) ; and a daughter , harper seven ( born july 2011 ) . she had a short solo career after the spice girls in 2001 , unlike her fellow members , she did n't have a solo no.1 . after that , she created clothes and perfumes with her husband . she reunited with the spice girls for a world tour in 2007 .
