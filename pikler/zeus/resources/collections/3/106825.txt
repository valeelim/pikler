Sam Allardyce     sam allardyce ( born : 19 october 1954 ) is an english football manager . he is the manager of crystal palace . he has also been the manager of blackpool , notts county , bolton wanderers , newcastle united , west ham united and the england national football team . he used to play football , and played for bolton wanderers , sunderland , millwall , tampa bay rowdies , coventry city , huddersfield town , preston north end and west bromwich albion .
