Mike Dirnt     mike dirnt ( born pritchard johnton on may 4 , 1972 , in san diego , california ) is the bass guitar player from the punk rock band green day , he also sings back up vocals . mike was adopted . his adoptive parents divorced when he was 7 , and both are now remarried . mike owns a restaurant in california called `` rudy 's ca n't fail cafe '' . mike married his long time girlfriend anastasia in 1996 and their daughter , estella-desiree , `` stella '' , was born in april 1997. mike loves his daughter very much , and his nickname for her is `` hero '' . today , mike and anastasia are separated , but still remain very good friends . he recently was remarried in 2004 , however his wife left him because he spent too much time in the studio recording american idiot . mike is currently married to girlfriend brittney , and resides in oakland , california .
