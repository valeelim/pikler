Urinal deodorizer block     urinal deodorizer blocks are the small disinfectant blocks found in urinals . there are many other words for them , such as : `` piss biscuit '' , `` urinal cake '' , `` urinal mint '' , or `` urinal puck '' . the blocks are used to kill germs and take away the bad smell of urinals in public bathrooms . they are put over the urinal drain and evaporate very slowly . because they do not dissolve or break apart in water , they do not take away the smell of the flushing water or urine . different blocks are made with different chemicals , like paradichlorobenzene or naphthalene . the block is something scented to smell like different things , such as cherries . other pages . - toilet rim block
