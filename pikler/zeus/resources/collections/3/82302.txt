1273     events . europe . - september 29 – rudolph i of germany is elected king of germany over rival candidate king otakar ii of bohemia , ending the interregnum ; otakar refuses to acknowledge rudolph as the new king , leading to the outbreak of war in 1276. rudolph is the first of many habsburgs to hold the throne . - december 6 – thomas aquinas quit writing `` summa theologica '' — a master work of catholic theology — leaving it unfinished after having a mystical experience during mass . - king otakar ii of bohemia captures bratislava from hungary . the middle east . - december – followers of the recently deceased jalal al-din muhammad rumi establish the sufi order of the whirling dervishes in the city of konya ( in present-day turkey ) . - the `` holy redeemer '' khachkar , believed to be one of the finest examples of the art form , is carved in haghpat , armenia , by vahram . - the constantinople suburb of beyoğlu ( then known as pera ) is given to the republic of genoa by the byzantine emperor michael viii palaeologus in return for genoa 's support of the empire after the fourth crusade and the sacking of constantinople .
