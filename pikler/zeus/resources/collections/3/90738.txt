Charles-Marie Widor     charles-marie widor ( born lyons , 21 february , 1844 ; died paris , 12 march , 1937 ) was a french organist and composer . he was one of the greatest organists of his time and he had many pupils who learned a lot from him and became famous . widor is best known for his organ works which he called “ symphonies ” . the `` toccata '' from his `` symphony no 5 '' is one of the best known of all organ pieces , often played at the end of wedding ceremonies .
