Resurrection Ship     the cylon resurrection ship is a fictional type of spacecraft found in the television series `` battlestar galactica '' . the ship is a part of cylon fleet . cylons who are killed have their consciousness downloaded into a nearby resurrection ship . a new body is created for their consciousness . this lets them live again after they die . if a cylon is too far away from the nearest resurrection ship , death will be permanent . with nowhere for the consciousness to download to , they will simply cease to exist .
