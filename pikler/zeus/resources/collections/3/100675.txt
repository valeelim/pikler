Guitar Hero Encore: Rocks the 80s    guitar hero encore : rocks the 80s guitar hero encore : rocks the 80s is a video game for the playstation 2 consoles . it is part of the `` guitar hero '' series of games . it was released on july 24 2007 in the united states and in the european union and on august 1 2007 in australia . the game features songs and music from the 1980s . the game features artists such as quiet riot , ratt , poison and iron maiden
