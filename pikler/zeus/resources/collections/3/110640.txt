Martin Brodeur     martin pierre brodeur ( born may 6 , 1972 , in montreal , quebec ) is a canadian professional ice hockey goaltender . he is currently an unrestricted free agent in the national hockey league ( nhl ) . during his 21-years with the devils , he was a three-time stanley cup champion and holds more than thirty devils team records . on june 7 , 2014 , brodeur told espn that he would test the free agency market for the 2014-15 season . awards . - calder memorial trophy — 1994 - nhl 1st all-star team — 2003 , 2004 , 2007 - nhl 2nd all-star team — 1997 , 1998 , 2006 , 2008 - nhl all-rookie team — 1994 - nhl all-star game — 1996 , 1997 , 1998 , 1999 , 2000 , 2001 , 2003 , 2004 , 2007 , 2008 - olympic gold medal — 2002 , 2010 - primus world'stars challenge bowl — 2004 - qmjhl 2nd all-star team — 1992 - qmjhl all-rookie team — 1990 - stanley cup — 1995 , 2000 , 2003 - vezina trophy — 2003 , 2004 , 2007 , 2008 - william m. jennings trophy — 1997 ( with mike dunham ) , 1998 , 2003 ( tied roman čechmánek & robert esche ) , 2004 , 2010 tied most all time with patrick roy
