Berwick-upon-Tweed     berwick-upon-tweed is a town in northumberland , united kingdom . it is the most northerly town in england , and is just 2.5 miles from scotland . it has around 11,665 people in it . berwick was once a scottish town , and gave its name to berwickshire . often berwick was captured by english armies and later taken back the scottish armies . in the fifteenth century the english took berwick and never lost it again . many people who live in berwick , or who visit berwick because it is their local large market town are scottish . this also used to be a very large port . it was an important commercial town that used to be marked in bold on the maps .
