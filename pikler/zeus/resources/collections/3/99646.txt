Karak     the term karak can mean any of the following : - karak ( pakistan ) is a city in pakistan . - karak district is in the khyber pakhtunkhwa province of pakistan . - karak ( mascot ) , a mascot in form of red-tailed black cockatoo at the 18th commonwealth games at melbourne in 2006 . - karak expressway , a highway in malaysia . - karak , pahang , a town in malaysia .
