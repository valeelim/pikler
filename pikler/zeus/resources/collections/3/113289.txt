Guitar Hero World Tour     guitar hero : world tour is a music game that is part of the `` guitar hero '' franchise . the game was released in north america on october 27 2008. this is the first guitar hero game that allows players to play drums and sing vocals . there are 85 songs that come on the disc . it will also allow players to make their own characters and songs . on the ps3 , xbox 360 , and wii versions , you can download songs online . musicians who guest star in world tour . - hayley williams ( paramore ) - ozzy osbourne ( black sabbath ) - zakk wylde , ( ozzy osbourne and black label society ) - billy corgan ( smashing pumpkins ) - ted nugent - sting ( musician ) ( the police ) - travis barker ( +44 and blink 182 ) - jimi hendrix song list . - band on the run , paul mccartney - misery business , paramore - beat it , michael jackson and van halen - living on a prayer , bon jovi - purple haze , jimi hendrix
