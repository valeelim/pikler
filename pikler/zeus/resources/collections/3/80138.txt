West Highland White Terrier     the west highland white terrier or westie is originally a scottish dog . the westie was first used for hunting foxes and otters . the breed is well known for high stamina , courage and agility . westies have a double white coat which protects them from attacks and cold weather . they have dark black eyes . they are small dogs and are lively , playful and alert . they are loyal to their owner , but can be stubborn at times . they are known to kill small animals , such as moles , squirrels , chipmunks and birds . they like to be outdoors .
