Adamsite-(Y)    adamsite- ( y ) adamsite- ( y ) is a mineral that can be white , pale pink , pale purple , or is colorless in color and is made of crystals . it is named for professor frank dawson adams ( 1859-1942 ) of mcgill university , montreal , quebec and is found in the poudrette quarry in quebec . it 's environment is in a pegmatite dike that is greatly heated . it is barely radioactive , and is easily scratched with calcite and it also breaks easily .
