Twillingate, Newfoundland and Labrador    twillingate , newfoundland and labrador twillingate is a town that can be found on the twillingate islands off the coast of newfoundland . about 2500 people live in the town . it is known for its scenic landscape and friendly hospitality . twillingate is about an hour and a half north of gander and an hour north of lewisporte . twillingate 's economy is based around the fishery .
