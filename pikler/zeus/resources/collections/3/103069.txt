Regent     a regent is a position of government in a country where there is a monarchy ( like a king or queen ) . he ( or she ) is an `` acting king '' , and does the job of a king where the real king is either too young , sick or can not do what he is meant to for some other reason . they may also be voted in if there are no monarchs in line to the throne , for instance , if the current king or queen does not have any relatives when s/he dies . related pages . - regency era
