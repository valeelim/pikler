Leon Czolgosz     leon czolgosz ( may 5 , 1873 - october 29 , 1901 ) was an american assassin and an anarchist best known as the murderer of william mckinley . he was born in alpena , michigan to polish catholic immigrant parents . czolgosz shot president william mckinley in buffalo , new york on 6 september 1901. the president died from his injury on september 14 , 1901 , 8 days later . czolgosz shot the president claiming the president committed war crimes in the american philippines war ( 1899-1913 ) and was an enemy of the people . he was influenced by emma goldman . some people have questioned the sanity of czolgosz . czolgosz was found guilty of murder in a very fast trial . he was executed by electric chair in auburn prison , new york on october 29 , 1901 .
