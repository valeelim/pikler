Choudhary Rahmat Ali     chaudhry rahmat ali or rehmat ali khan ( november 16 , 1897 - february 12 , 1951 ) was an indian muslim who was one of the first people to ask for the creation of the state of pakistan . he is famous for coining the name , or term `` pakistan '' on january 28 , 1933. in pakistan he has considerable importance . his articles are taught in the course books of pakistani schools .
