Defender (football)    defender ( football ) a defender in football is a player , who plays in at the back of the formation . there are many positions classed as a defender , mainly : - left back - central defender - right back - sweeper - full back the defenders are the first number when referring to formations , for example : - 4-3-3 is the formation . this formation shows that there are four defenders , three midfielders and three attackers . - 4-4-2 is the formation . this formation shows that there are four defenders , four midfielders and two attackers . - 3-4-3 is the formation . this formation shows that there are three defenders , four midfielders and three attackers .
