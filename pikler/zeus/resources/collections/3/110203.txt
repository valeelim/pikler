Mountain Brook, Alabama    mountain brook , alabama mountain brook is a city in jefferson county , alabama , united states . the city is a suburb of birmingham , alabama . in 2005 , mountain brook had a population of 20,821 , and is on several lists of the united states ' wealthiest communities . mountain brook was created after an election was held on march 24 , 1942 , with 263 voting in favor of incorporation and 67 opposing . the area was founded by robert jemison , and the city was designed by warren h. manning . mountain brook was built around three villages : english village , mountain brook village , and crestline village . mountain brook is the hometown of `` friends '' actress courteney cox and missing person natalee holloway . other websites . - mtnbrook.org - official city website - mountain brook chamber of commerce
