Rhein-Kreis Neuss Rural District     neuss is a kreis ( district ) in the west of north rhine-westphalia , germany . in 1975 the urban district of neuss lost its independent status and was merged into the grevenbroich district , which was renamed to `` kreis neuss '' . on may 26 2003 it changed its name , and is now officially called `` rhein-kreis neuss '' . other websites . - official website ( german )
