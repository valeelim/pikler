John Edwards     johnny reid `` john '' edwards ( born june 10 , 1953 ) is an american politician . he served one term as u.s. senator from north carolina . before his time in the senate , he was a lawyer . he argued and won several big cases about doctors who gave bad care and caused harm to patients . he also won lawsuits for people who had been injured by negligence of businesses or by using their products . his best known case was for a cary , north carolina three year old girl . she was badly injured by being sucked into an open drain in a swimming pool playpool for toddlers . her family won enough money from the drain company to pay for her medical care for the rest of her life . he ran for president of the united states twice , in 2004 and 2008 , but was defeated by john kerry and barack obama in the primaries . he was also chosen to be the democratic candidate for vice-president in 2004 , but was defeated by dick cheney . in 2008 , he admitted to having an affair while his wife , elizabeth was dying . he has written several books .
