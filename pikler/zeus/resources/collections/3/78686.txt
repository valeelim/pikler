Stud (animal)    stud ( animal ) a stud in animal breeding is a male animal , such as a horse or dog , that is employed for breeding . the terms for the male of a given animal species ( stallion , bull , rooster , etc . ) usually mean that the animal is entire , that is , not castrated , and therefore capable of standing at stud . a specialized vocabulary exists for de-sexed animals . a stud farm , in animal husbandry , is an establishment for selective breeding using stud animals .
