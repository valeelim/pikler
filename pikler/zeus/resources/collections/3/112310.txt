United States Department of the Treasury     the united states department of the treasury is a cabinet department and the treasury of the united states government . it was established by an act of congress in 1789 to manage government revenue . the first secretary of the treasury was alexander hamilton . he almost single-handedly worked out the nation 's early financial system , and for several years was a major presence in washington 's administration as well . his statue still stands outside the treasury building . other websites . - united states department of the treasury website - department of the treasury meeting notices and rule changes from the federal register rss feed references . - `` enough wise men , the story of our constitution '' by forrest mcdonald -- published by the dominion of canada and by longmans canada limited , toronto 1970
