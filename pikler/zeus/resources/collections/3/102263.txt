Epistle to the Galatians     the epistle to the galatians ( or galatians ) is the forty-eighth book in the bible ( and ninth in the new testament ) , written by the apostle paul . the book was written to the people of galatia , with whom paul had , at a previous date , founded the congregation in their area . it is not known for sure , but most believe that the book was written around 57 a.d. the book contains some of the most known bible teachings , such as that all have need of salvation ( galatians 3:28-29 ) , and the fruits of the spirit ( galatians 5:22-23 ) . other websites . - galatians ( kjv ) - galatians ( niv )
