Aleria     aléria ( greek and roman alalia ) is a commune in the haute-corse `` département '' of france , on the island of corsica . it includes the easternmost point in metropolitan france . since 1950 the necropolis of casabianda 's is excavated and have revealed grave goods , from the late archaic period forwards , that include the finest works of art , in jewels , weapons , metalwares , bronze and ceramic plates and dishes in particular , attic cups , and rhytons . as latin alalia , it was the capital of the roman province of corsica . related pages . - battle of alalia , fought between 540 bc and 535 bc other websites . - official website
