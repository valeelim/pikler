Toni Morrison     toni morrison ( born february 18 , 1931 in ohio ) is an african-american author . she is the second child in her working-class family . she normally writes about racial discrimination ( racism , mainly the dislike of blacks ) . she has won awards for writing some books and she has changed african-american history . she is perhaps the most successful mainly story-writing african woman in the world . she was a famous writer and she got her good writing by the people she looked up to . they were b.w.jones and a.i.vinson . her first novel ( `` the bluest eyes '' ) is the story of a girl ruined by a racist society and its violence and she had son named slade who she wrote this book with dreaming emmett . one of her books , `` beloved '' , was made into a movie in 1998. this movie starred oprah winfrey .
