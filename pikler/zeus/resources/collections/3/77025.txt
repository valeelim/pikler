Ashley Massaro     ashley marie massaro ( born may 26 1979 ) , better known simply as ashley , is an american pictorial model and professional wrestler , best known for her time with world wrestling entertainment ( wwe ) while wrestling on its `` raw '' brand . she was the winner of the raw diva search 2005 and was on the cover of the april 2007 issue of `` playboy magazine '' . she was a contestant on the of `` survivor '' in china , but was the second contestant to be eliminated . other websites . - theashleymassaro.com - wwe.com profile - ashley massaro in maxim magazine - ashley 's `` survivor : china '' profile
