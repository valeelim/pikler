Meitnerium     meitnerium is a chemical element in the periodic table . its atomic number is 109. it is not found on earth naturally . because of this , it needs to be made in a science laboratory . its half life , or the amount of time it takes for half of the element to go away is a little over one second . meitnerium was first made in 1982. it is named after the german scientist lise meitner ( 1878-1968 ) . the two letter abbreviation is `` mt '' and is a solid . nobody knows for sure what meitnerium looks like , but scientists think it is a white , gray , or silver color .
