Théodore Géricault     théodore géricault ( september 26 , 1791 – january 26 , 1824 ) was an important french painter , known for `` the raft of the medusa '' and other paintings . he was one of the people who started the romantic movement . born in rouen , france , géricault was schooled in english sporting art by carle vernet and in classical figure composition by pierre-narcisse guérin , who saw that he had talent . his first big work , `` the charging chasseur '' , shown at the paris salon in 1812 , showed the influence of the style of rubens and an interest in contemporary topics .
