Velvet Revolver     velvet revolver ( vr ) , was a grammy award-winning rock supergroup that started in 2002. the members of the band are slash ( guitarist ) , duff mckagan ( bassist , backing vocals ) , matt sorum ( drums ) of guns n ' roses , and dave kushner ( guitarist ) of wasted youth . scott weiland of stone temple pilots was previously the lead singer . he joined the band after stone temple pilots broke up in 2003. the band got success with their first album `` contraband '' and released their second album `` libertad '' in 2007. scott weiland left the band in 2008 , though he did one more one-off show with the band in 2012. velvet revolver have had several searches for a new singer since then , but have yet to announce a replacement . other websites . - velvet revolver official site ( dot com ) / velvet revolver official site ( dot net ) - velvet revolver @ rca records - rca records - velvet revolver vh1 artist page - velvet revolver mtv artist page - velvet revolver aol artist page - velvet revolver billboard artist page
