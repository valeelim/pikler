The Oprah Winfrey Show     the oprah winfrey show was an american talk show which had oprah winfrey as the host . it began on september 8 , 1986 , and has since become one of the most famous talk shows in the world . the show ended on may 25 , 2011 after 25 seasons . it won 47 daytime emmy awards . notable guests on her show were : - gayle king – oprah 's friend ( 140 appearances ) - celine dion – singer ( 27 appearances ) - tom cruise – actor , known for jumping in the show 's couch . he also appeared in the show 's last episode . - nelson mandela – president of south africa .
