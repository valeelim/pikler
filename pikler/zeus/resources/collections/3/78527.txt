Giro Manoyan     giro ( kiro ) manoyan ( ) is an armenian politician , one of the leaders of arf dashnaktsutiun and the head of `` armenian cause '' office . manoyan attended and finished concordia university in montreal , canada . in the 1980s he was the executive director of the armenian national committee of canada . from 1989 to 1999 , he edited the horizon weekly armenian newspaper . since 2000 , he is the director of the international secretariat of the armenian revolutionary federation bureau in yerevan . other websites . - an interview with kiro manoyan
