Latvian lats     the latvian lats ( plural `` lati '' ) was the currency used in latvia in 1922—1940 and in 1993—2013 . it is shortened as '' ls '' and was divided into 100 '' santīmi '' on 1 january 2014 , latvian lats was replaced by the euro . the lats could be used for two weeks alongside euros after the currency was replaced .
