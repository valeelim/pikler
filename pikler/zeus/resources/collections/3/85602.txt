Angus Young     angus mckinnon young ( born 31 march 1955 ) is a scottish-born australian guitarist and songwriter . he is one of the founders of the australian hard rock band ac/dc . he is known for his style of playing the guitar , his very active performance while on stage , and schoolboy-uniform clothing . he was ranked 96th on `` rolling stone '' 's list `` the 100 greatest guitarists of all time '' . young uses gibson sg guitars often but he also owns fender telecasters , firebirds and es-335s . in 2003 , he became a member of the rock and roll hall of fame along with other members of ac/dc .
