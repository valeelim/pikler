Popular Mechanics     popular mechanics is an american magazine devoted to science and technology . it was first published january 11 , 1902 by h.h . windsor , and has been owned since the 1950s by the hearst corporation there is also a latin american edition that has been published for decades and a newly formed south african version available . `` popular mechanics '' features regular sections on cars , trucks , science , technology , home how-to and outdoor adventures . a recurring column is `` jay leno 's garage '' featuring observations by the famed late-night talk show host and vehicle enthusiast . other websites . - popularmechanics.com - popular mechanics south african edition
