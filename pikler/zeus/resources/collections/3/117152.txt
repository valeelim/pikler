Khairpur District     khairpur district ( ) is a district in the sindh province of pakistan . the district has an area of 15,910 square kilometres and is headquartered at the city of khairpur . the district has eight talukas ( counties ) : - khairpur - nara tehsil - kot diji tehsil - sobho dero tehsil - mirwah tehsil - kingri tehsil - faiz ganj tehsil - gambat tehsil
