Anthe (moon)    anthe ( moon ) anthe is a very small moon of saturn between the orbits of mimas and enceladus . it is also known as saturn xlix and its designation was s/2007 s 4. it is named after one of the alkyonides ; the name means `` flowery '' . it was found by the cassini imaging team in images taken on may 30 , 2007. once the find was made , a search of older cassini images showed this small moon in observations from as far back as june 2004. it was first announced on july 18 , 2007 .
