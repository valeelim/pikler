Ganz (toy company)    ganz ( toy company ) ganz is a canadian company . it is a private company with headquarters just north of toronto in woodbridge , ontario . it has many subsidiaries and offices . it is now best known for distributing plush animals and collectables . most recently it is known as the manufacturer of `` webkinz '' , the toy with an internet site . ganz was founded in toronto in 1950 by holocaust survivors samuel ganz and his sons jack ganz and sam ganz . then it was called `` ganz bros. toys limited '' . it is now run by the founder 's grandson , hoard ganz .
