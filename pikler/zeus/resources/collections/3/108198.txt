Spacewar!    spacewar ! spacewar ! is one of the first known video games . it was released in 1962. it was made by steve russel . in this game , the player has to shoot the other spaceships while flying around a star . the ships shoot missiles ( rockets ) which were unaffected ( nothing happened ) by the gravity . each ship had only a few missiles and only a little bit of fuel . the hyperspace could be used to avoid being hit by a missile but the ship would reappear at a random place . the more the player used hyperspace , the bigger the chance that the spaceship would explode .
