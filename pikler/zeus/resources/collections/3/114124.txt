Dwarf Allosaur     dwarf allosaur is a bone from a theropod dinosaur that lived in australia about 106 million years ago in the cretaceous period . it may be related to `` allosaurus '' but is smaller and lived 30 million years after all other allosaurs had gone extinct . as it is known only from a single bone nothing more can be said . besides its poor fossil record , it is famous for appearing in the bbc television series walking with dinosaurs . - it is only known from an ankle bone , making it impossible to figure out its genus . the bone may have belonged to `` australovenator '' . other websites . - walking with dinosaurs - fact file : dwarf allosaur
