Lower Silesian Voivodeship     lower silesian voivodeship is one of the 16 voivodeships of poland . it can be found in the south-west poland and in west silesia ( called lower silesia ) . the capital city is wrocław . it base on 4 former voivodeships : wrocławskie , legnickie , wałbrzyskie and jeleniogórskie . some parts of this area was a territories , which before 1999 belonged to contemporary greater poland voivodeship .
