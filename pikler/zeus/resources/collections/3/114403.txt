Sportswear     sportswear is clothing , including footwear , worn for playing sports or to exercise . sportswear includes shorts , tracksuits , t-shirts , polo shirts and trainers . it also includes some underwear , such as the jockstrap . sportswear is also often worn as casual fashion clothing . sports athletes wear a combination of different items of clothing such as sport shoes , pants and shirts . some athletes wear personal armour such as helmets or american football body armour .
