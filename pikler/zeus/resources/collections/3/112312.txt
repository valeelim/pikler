Loose (album)    loose ( album ) loose is the third studio album by canadian singer-songwriter nelly furtado . it was released on 9 june 2006. it is her most successful album , selling more than 8 million copies worldwide . the hip-hop producer timbaland helped furtado recording this cd . it featured many hits including the u.s. number-one singles `` promiscuous '' and `` say it right '' and the european hit `` all good things ( come to an end ) '' . all lyrics written by nelly furtado , except `` promiscuous '' ( lyrics written by tim `` attitude '' clayton , furtado and tim mosley ) and `` maneater '' ( lyrics written by furtado with additional lyrics by jim beanz ) . - notes - signifies a vocal producer
