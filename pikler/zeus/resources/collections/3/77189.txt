Mitsubishi A6M Zero     the mitsubishi a6m zero , often called just the a6m zero or simply the zero was a long range carrier ship-based japanese fighter aircraft during world war ii . the aircraft was built by mitsubishi , making its first flight in 1939 , and entering naval service in 1940. it was the primary fighter used by japan during the war . it was the primary enemy of us naval aircraft such as the grumman f4f wildcat , vought f4u corsair , and later the grumman f6f hellcat , which was specifically designed to combat the zero . the a6m zero was used throughout the entire war . related pages . - f4f wildcat - f6f hellcat
