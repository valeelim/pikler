L'Île-Rousse    l'île-rousse l'île-rousse ( ) is a commune in the haute-corse department of corsica , france . it has a permanent population of around 2,500. it was founded in 1758 by pasquale paoli to create a port that would not be in the hands of the genoese like calvi . famous people born in the city . - pascal paoli other websites . - map of l'île-rousse on mapquest in french : - l'île-rousse on the site of the institut géographique national - l'île-rousse on the site of insee
