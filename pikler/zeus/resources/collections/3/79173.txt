Adamite     adamite is a mineral that can be either yellow , purple , green , pink , or yellowish-green in color and is made from crystals . it is very easy to break and it can be scratched by a copper penny . adamite is not radioactive . it is named for the french mineralogist gilbert joseph adam ( 1795-1881 ) , who was the person who discovered it . it is mainly found in the atacama desert in chile . adamite is also used for other commercial resources .
