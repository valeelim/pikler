Alfonso Soriano     alfonso guilleard soriano ( born january 7 , 1976 ) is a dominican republican professional major league baseball player . he currently is a free agent . he has used to play with the hiroshima toyo carp , new york yankees , texas rangers and washington nationals before the cubs . he played as a second baseman for the yankees and rangers . he bats right-handed and has batted as a lead-off hitter for most of his career . other websites . - baseball prospectus : alfonso soriano
