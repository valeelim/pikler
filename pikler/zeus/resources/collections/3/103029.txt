Formaldehyde     formaldehyde ( or methanal ) is a chemical compound . it is a colorless gas with a bad smell . it is flammable and toxic . it is the simplest aldehyde . its main application is as a disinfectant . it is a biocide , this means it can kill most bacteria and fungi . it is also used to make dyes , to make textiles wrinkle-free , preservatives , explosives , and certain polymers . about 23 million tonnes ( 50 billion pounds ) of formaldehyde was made worldwide in 2005. on may 21 , 2002 , a worker in a explosive making factory dropped a lighter and the flame immediately spread to the formaldehyde , which in turn caused to fire to blaze abnormally . this caused 17 known deaths and 39 known injuries . these days , the chemical is used less frequently .
