Egyptian Museum     egyptian museum , in cairo , egypt , has a large collection of ancient egyptian antiquities in the world . it has 120,000 items . the museum 's royal mummy room , shows some mummies of new kingdom kings and queens . another large museum of egyptian antiquities is egyptian museum of turin , which is the only museum other than the cairo museum that is dedicated solely to egyptian art and culture . other websites . - egyptian museum in cairo - egyptian museum of turin
