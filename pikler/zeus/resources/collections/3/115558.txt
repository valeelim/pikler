Toyota Highlander     the toyota highlander is a medium-sized crossover sport utility vehicle sold by toyota . the highlander is on the camry platform and shares the camry 's parts including the engine . the highlander was redesigned in 2008 and toyota sells the highlander in australia and japan as the kluger . it has a v6 or v8 engine . the highlander 's competitors are the nissan pathfinder , chevrolet traverse , hyundai santa fe and the ford explorer .
