Zhob District     zhob ( ) is a district in the northwest of balochistan province of pakistan . the zhob district is a provincially administered tribal area ( pata ) . the zhob district is subdivided into three subdistricts : zhob , kakkar and sherani . the population of zhob district was estimated to be over 500,000 in 2005. the zhob river is used for irrigation in the zhob district . tehsils . - ashwat tehsil - qamar din karez tehsil ( qamardin karaiz ) - sambaza tehsil - sherani tehsil - zhob
