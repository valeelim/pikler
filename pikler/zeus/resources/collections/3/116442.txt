Ovis     ovis is a genus of the subfamily caprinae , of the family bovidae . the wild sheep and domestic sheep and other animals like it belong to this genus . there are 5 or more animals in this genus . taxonomy . - genus ovis - `` ovis orientalis '' group - `` ovis orientalis '' - mouflon ( `` ovis orientalis orientalis '' group ) - domestic sheep ( `` ovis aries '' or `` ovis orientalis aries '' ) - urial ( `` ovis orientalis vignei '' group ) - argali or mountain sheep ( `` ovis ammon '' ) - `` ovis canadensis '' group - snow sheep ( `` ovis nivicola '' ) - dall sheep ( `` ovis dalli '' ) - bighorn sheep ( `` ovis canadensis '' ) these are the main species of ovis .
