Leoš Friedl     leoš friedl ( born january 1 1977 in jindřichův hradec ) is a czech professional tennis player best known for his doubles play with františek čermák . he is coached by lubomir gerla . in his career , he has won 15 top-level doubles titles and $ 1,027,625 in prize money . having turned professional in 1997 , friedl won the 2001 wimbledon mixed doubles title with daniela hantuchová , where they beat mike bryan and liezel huber , 4–6 , 6–3 , 6–2 .
