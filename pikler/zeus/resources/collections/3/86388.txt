Bodhicitta     in buddhist thought , bodhicitta is the motivation of a bodhisattva . the word `` bodhicitta '' combines the words for enlightenment `` ( bodhi ) '' and mind `` ( citta ) '' . it is sometime translated as `` mind of enlightenment '' . bodhicitta is taught to be the intent to become enlightened , not for oneself , but for the benefit of `` all '' beings .
