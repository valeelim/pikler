Scientific American     scientific american is a popular science magazine , published ( first weekly and later monthly ) since august 28 , 1845 , making it the oldest continuously published magazine in the united states . it brings articles about new and innovative research to the non-scientific audience . other websites . - online edition of `` scientific american '' with partially free access to the current issue . - online archive ( not free ) of the issues from 1993 to the present . - information about the archive - online archive of `` scientific american '' between 1846 and 1869 .
