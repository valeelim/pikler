De Wallen     de wallen ( `` thin walls '' ) are the biggest and most famous red-light district in amsterdam . it is in the very old ( 14th century ) part of the city around the oude kerk and oudezijds achterburgwal/vorburgwal . in december 2007 , the mayor of amsterdam and his government decided to buy many of brothelhouses within de wallen . by doing this , the area used for prostitution and coffee shops gets smaller and smaller .
