Gamma (letter)    gamma ( letter ) gamma ( uppercase/lowercase γ γ ) , is the letter of the greek alphabet , used to represent the `` g '' sound in ancient and modern greek . in the system of greek numerals , it has a value of 3. letters that came from it include the roman c and cyrillic г. the lowercase gamma ( `` γ '' ) is used in wave motion physics to represent the ratio of specific heat .
