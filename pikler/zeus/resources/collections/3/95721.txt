Snow Patrol     snow patrol is an alternative rock band from scotland and northern ireland . they are based in glasgow and are signed to polydor records . snow patrol was started as an indie rock band , but began to play more alternative rock and pop songs . the band has released six albums , `` songs for polarbears '' , `` final straw '' , `` when it 's all over we still have to clear up '' , `` eyes open '' , `` a hundred million suns '' and `` fallen empires '' . `` eyes open '' won the award for best irish album at the national irish music awards .
