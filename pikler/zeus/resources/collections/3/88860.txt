L'Anse-aux-Meadows     l'anse aux meadows is a historical settlement on the island of newfoundland in eastern canada . it was settled by vikings from greenland , iceland , and norway around the year 1000 , almost 500 years before christopher columbus came to the americas . it is the only known viking place in north america outside of greenland . the remains of the settlement were discovered in 1960 , and were declared a unesco world heritage site in 1978 .
