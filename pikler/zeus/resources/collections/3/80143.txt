Abrasion     in dermatology , an abrasion is a damage to the skin that affects only the surface , generally not deeper than the epidermis . but it can give mild bleeding . mild abrasions , also known as 'grazes ' do not scar , but deep abrasions may lead to the development of scarring tissue . abrasion injuries occur rather often when exposed skin comes into moving contact with a rough surface causing trauma to the upper layers of the epidermis . such injuries , where caused by abrasive contact with textiles or carpet , are sometimes referred to as 'rug burn ' or 'carpet burn ' . related pages . - wound
