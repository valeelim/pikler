Linspire     linspire , also known as lindowsos ( also lin -- -s , pronounced `` lindash '' ) , is a commercial linux distribution based on debian gnu/linux ( ubuntu ) . linspire is sold by linspire , inc. and is focused on ease-of-use for the average person , rather than catering to advanced needs . as of 2007-10-10 , the most recent stable release of linspire is version 6.0 which was released in october of 2007. linspire is available to download and costs us $ 50. based in san diego , california , lindows , inc. was founded in august 2001 by michael robertson with the goal of developing a linux-based operating system capable of running major microsoft windows applications . it based its windows compatibility on the wine api emulation layer . the company later abandoned this approach in favor of attempting to make linux applications easy to download , install and use . to this end a program named `` cnr '' was developed : based on debian 's advanced packaging tool , it provides an easy-to-use graphical user interface and a slightly modified package system for an annual fee . the first public release of linspire was version 1.0 , released in late 2001 .
