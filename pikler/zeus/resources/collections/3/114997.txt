Will Wright (game developer)    will wright ( game developer ) will wright ( born on january 20 , 1960 in atlanta , georgia ) is an american video game developer and is the co-founder of maxis , now a part of electronic arts . he 's most famous for `` simcity '' , `` the sims '' and spore . he is currently working on a new project , and has set up the `` stupid fan club '' .
