Sabana Larga, Elías Piña    sabana larga , elías piña sabana larga ( in english , `` long savanna '' ) is a dominican municipal district of comendador in the elías piña province near the border with haiti . the municipality had , in 2010 , a total population of 2,056 : 1,127 men and 929 women . the urban population was of the total population . sabana larga was made a municipal district of comendador by the law 18 of 20 january 2004 ; a municipal district is a part of a municipality that has its own government . sabana larga is to the northeast of the city of comendador , on the northern side of the `` macasías '' river , the main left tributary of the artibonite river . the main economic activity of sabana larga is farming .
