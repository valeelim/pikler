Jan Howard     jan howard ( born lula grace johnson on march 13 , 1930 in west plains , missouri ) is an american country music singer and grand ole opry star . she was one of country music 's trailblazing female vocalists during the height of her career in the mid-1960s . her last name ( howard ) comes from her ex-husband , legendary singer-songwriter , harlan howard . jan howard 's bigggest hit and signature song is the 1966 classic country hit , `` evil on your mind '' , which has been featured in the book , `` heartaches by the number : the 500 greatest country music singles '' . in the late 60s and early 70s , she dueted with bill anderson , on a number of hits that hit the top 10 , including the no . 1 hit `` for loving you '' .
