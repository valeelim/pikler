Pellet     pellet is a term used for a small particle or grain , typically one created by compressing an original material . the term is used in a variety of specific contexts . - air gun pellets are non-spherical projectiles fired from air guns - airsoft pellets are the spherical plastic projectiles used in airsoft guns - the individual items of `` shot '' fired from a shotgun , are referred to as pellets - in centrifugation , a pellet is the concentrated component of a mixture that had been centrifuged - animal feed pellets are feedstuffs produced from various raw materials and additives - plastic resin pellet , or nurdle - bird pellets are the round balls of undigested matter that some bird species occasionally regurgitate - wood pellets or corn pellets are used in pellet stoves - pelletizing is the industrial process used to create pellets - often using a pellet mill - pellet is an open source reasoner for owl dl
