Veronica Guerin     veronica guerin ( 5 july 1958 - 26 june 1996 ) was an irish journalist who was born in dublin . she was murdered by irish drug dealers . her death made many irish people angry , which brought down many drug dealers on the island . the criminal john gilligan was later convicted of her murder and sentenced to 28 years in prison , though gilligan said in high court that it was his friend john traynor who murdered guerin .
