Land hemisphere     the land hemisphere is the hemisphere on the earth with the highest possible area of land . it is centered on ( near the city of nantes , france ) . the other half of the earth is the water hemisphere . the land hemisphere has seven eighths of the land on the earth . europe , africa , north america , most of asia and most of south america are in the land hemisphere . europe is at the center of the land hemisphere . however , because the area of the oceans of the land hemisphere is still bigger than its land area , the land hemisphere means the hemisphere where most land is , but not the hemisphere where the land area is bigger than the ocean area .
