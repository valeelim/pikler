Chiyoda, Tokyo    chiyoda , tokyo the municipality calls itself `` chiyoda city '' in english . during the edo period , most of the people living in this area were samurai . chiyoda developed around the imperial palace . the imperial palace area occupies about one sixth of the area of chiyoda . there was previously another castle here during the edo period , but it was destroyed . the diet of japan , the supreme court of japan and home of the prime minister of japan are all in chiyoda . chiyoda is in the center of tokyo . it borders chūō and minato on the south . shinjuku is on the ward 's western border . bunkyō and taitō is on the northern border . the ward has an area of . the imperial palace covers about 12 percent of this area . in chiyoda is the hub of the shinkansen high-speed train network , tokyo station . related pages . - list of special wards of tokyo - capital of japan other websites . - chiyoda website , - tokyo metropolitan government , special wards
