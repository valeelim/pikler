Orthodox     orthodox is a word about beliefs . it is made from two ancient greek words , ὀρθός `` orthós '' „correct , straight “ and δόξα `` dóxa '' „teaching , worship '' . orthodoxy means a teaching that is supposed to be close to the original . in particular : in christianity : - eastern orthodox church ( the split of the christian church into the roman catholic church and the eastern orthodox happened in 1054 , in what is known as the great schism ) - oriental orthodox church ( split from the rest in the 5th century , at the council of chalcedon ) - theologically traditional often in all lower case -- `` orthodox '' in judaism : - orthodox judaism - modern orthodox judaism jewish organisations : - orthodox union
