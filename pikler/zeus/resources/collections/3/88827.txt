Coat of Arms of Haiti     the first coat of arms of haiti was created in 1807 , and has appeared in its current form since 1986. it has a palm , with several flags at its sides , and two cannons on a green lawn . on the lawn there are several objects , such as musical instruments ( a snare drum and bugles ) , guns , and ship anchors . above the palm tree , there is a phrygian cap placed as a symbol of freedom . the ribbon on the bottom displays the national motto : `` l'union fait la force '' ( french for `` union makes strength '' ) .
