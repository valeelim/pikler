Alistair Darling     alistair maclean darling ( born november 28 , 1953 ) is a british labour party politician who was the chancellor of the exchequer from june 2007 to may 2010 and also held several posts in tony blair 's government . he is the member of parliament for edinburgh south west . alistair darling was born in london . he was first elected to the house of commons at the 1987 general election . his government posts under tony blair include : chief secretary to the treasury ( 1997-1998 ) , secretary of state for work and pensions ( 1998-2002 ) , transport secretary ( 2002-2006 ) , scottish secretary ( 2003-2006 ) and secretary of state for trade and industry ( 2006-2007 ) .
