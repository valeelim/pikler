Search engine optimisation     search engine optimisation is a way to make websites appear in the search engine 's results pages ( serps ) such as those given by google . many people use search engine optimisation companies to do the work for them because it takes too much time to do themselves . a more common word used to describe search engine optimisation is internet marketing or search engine marketing . search engine companies are careful to watch that spam does not affect their search engine results pages by adding filters such as google 's pagerank . most people do not need to pay for search engine optimisation services because usually a website is supposed to show up on the search engines over time anyway . but some online shopping websites may want to show up sooner , and so they pay for search engine optimisation from companies that provide it . other websites . - google search engine optimisation guidelines - yahoo search content quality guidelines - google search engine optimization pdf for beginners
