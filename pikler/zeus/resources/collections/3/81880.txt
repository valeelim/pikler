Post-hardcore     post-hardcore is a musical genre that evolved from hardcore punk , itself a part of the broader punk rock movement . one of the main influences on this genre is the band fugazi . post-hardcore uses elements of hardcore , along with emo , metal , alternative rock or whatever , to create a more experimental sound . it can be more melodic than normal hardcore , but sometimes is heavier . post-hardcore contributors . 1. fugazi 1. thrice 1. enter shikari 1. underoath 1. silverstein 1. the used 1. les savy fav 1. letter kills 1. rites of spring 1. hawthorne heights 1. pierce the veil 1. escape the fate 1. sleeping with sirens 1. moose blood 1. touche amore
