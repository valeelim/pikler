John Law     john law ( `` baptized '' 21 april 1671 - 21 march 1729 ) was a scottish economist who believed that money was only a means of exchange that did not have wealth in itself , and that national wealth depended on trade . he is said to be the father of finance , responsible for the adoption or use of paper money or bills in the world today . books . - `` millionaire : the philanderer , gambler , and duelist who invented modern finance '' by janet gleeson ( 2000 ) .
