Traffic sign     a traffic sign or a road sign is a sign put at the side of roads to give information to drivers . it can direct people driving where to go or what to do . a road sign is something that gives information by means of a symbol , or in only 1 or 2 words . it helps people in their vehicles on the road . some signs include : give way , stop , keep left , no u-turn , no parking , zebra crossing and much more . signs are very useful . without signs and traffic lights there would be many accidents and arguments on the road .
