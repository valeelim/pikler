Kaioo     kaioo is a social networking website where people can communicate using messages , video and photo uploads , blogs , social groups , and music . users can also create their own networks that are within the kaioo-network . this means they get a network where the user can change the style and their own domain ( such as myschool.kaioo.com or myschool-network.com ) . `` kaioo '' is a non-profit organisation . there is no advertisement in the networks . only in the geographical networks like language , country , city there will be advertisement . kaioo gives all money they earn with these advertisements to poor people . other websites . - kaioo.com - kaioo blog - kaioo wiki - new york times - editors web log - spiegel online
