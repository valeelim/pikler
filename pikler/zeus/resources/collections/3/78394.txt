Miami Heat     the miami heat are a team in the national basketball association in miami , florida . it was started in 1988. they play their home games at the american airlines arena . they won the 2006 nba finals where they defeated the dallas mavericks 4 games to 2 and they also won the 2012 nba finals where they defeated the oklahoma city thunder 4 games to 1. they also defeated the san antonio spurs in 2013 ; 4 games to 3. they lost to the san antonio spurs in 2014 over five games .
