Asiatic salamander     the asiatic salamanders ( family hynobiidae ) are a primitive kind of salamander which can be found throughout asia . unlike other salamanders , hynobiid salamanders do external fertilization , or `` spawning '' . the females lay egg-sacs , and the males pour sperm onto the sacs . they and the giant salamanders ( family cryptobranchidae ) , make up the suborder cryptobranchoidea . about half of hynobiids are unique to japan .
