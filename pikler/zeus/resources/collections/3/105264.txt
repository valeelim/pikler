Adam Sandler     adam sandler ( born september 9 , 1966 ) is an american actor , comedian , musician , screenwriter , and movie producer . his family is jewish and descends from russian and israeli immigrants on both sides . he is best known for his comedic roles , such as in the movies `` billy madison '' , `` happy gilmore '' , `` the wedding singer '' , `` the waterboy '' , `` big daddy '' , `` little nicky '' , `` spanglish '' , `` i now pronounce you chuck and larry '' and `` you do n't mess with the zohan '' . sandler was born in new york city and grew up in new hampshire . references . - adam sandler at biography.com
