Benedictine     the word benedictine usually refers to a follower of the order of saint benedict . these people usually lead a life in an abbey . they follow the rule of benedict of nursia . benedict lived in the 6th century . he made some rules , called the rule of st benedict these people follow . the rules can be summed up by `` pax '' ( `` peace '' ) and `` ora et labora '' ( `` pray and work '' ) . most benedictines see themselves as part of the catholic church . some can also be found in the eastern orthodox church and the anglican church . officially , the order is known as `` ordo sancti benedicti '' ( `` osb '' ) .
