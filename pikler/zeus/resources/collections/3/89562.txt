Judith     judith is a female given name . it comes from the hebrew word `` yəhûḏîṯ '' , meaning either `` praised one '' or `` a woman from judea '' . the name 's popularity goes back to the story told below . in the first half of the 1st millennium bc , an assyrian king , nebuchadnezzar , wanted to conquer israel . an israeli woman , called judith , went to nebuchadnezzar 's general , holofernes , and offered to have sex with him . holofernes used to drink wine before sex . when he was drunk , judith cut off his head and saved israel .
