Welwitschia     welwitschia or `` welwitschia mirabilis '' is known as one of the oldest living plants . it can be called a `` living fossil '' . the plant is mainly found in the deserts of namibia . some of these plants may be more than 5000 years old . this would make them the oldest living things on earth . it is named after the discoverer , dr. friedrich welwitsch . the plant is locally called `` long haired thing '' . its structure consists of a short , woody , top-shaped stem , and never more than two leaves , which are enormously long , ( two or three metres ) and break up at the ends .
