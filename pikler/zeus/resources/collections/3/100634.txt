Albert Fert     professor albert fert is a french physicist who was born on march 7 1938. he discovered giant magnetoresistance which was used to make gigabyte hard disks much easier to build . he is a professor at université paris-sud in orsay and scientific director of a laboratory called unité mixte de recherche . the laboratory is part of the centre national de la recherche scientifique ( national scientific research centre ) and thales group . he was awarded the 2007 nobel prize in physics together with peter grünberg . professor fert graduated in 1962 from the école normale supérieure in paris . he received his master 's degree in 1963 at the university of paris , and earned his phd in 1970 at the université paris-sud . in 1988 fert discovered giant magnetoresistance ( gmr ) in iron and chromium which began spintronics ; gmr was also discovered by peter grünberg from the jülich research centre but peter grünberg did not work with professor fert . since 1988 , albert fert has helped to improve spintronics .
