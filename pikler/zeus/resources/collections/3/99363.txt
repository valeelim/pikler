Naib     the word `` naib '' ( نائب ) is an arabic word meaning someone who is second in command . it may also mean : in history . - naib , the name given to the rulers of massawa , africa who served the ottoman empire from the sixteenth century to the nineteenth century . - naib , a local leader in dagestan . fiction . - naib , the title given to the leader of fremen ( freemen ) on the planet arrakis in the science fiction dune universe written by frank herbert . in the story the word `` naib '' means servant .
