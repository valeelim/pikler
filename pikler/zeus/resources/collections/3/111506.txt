NightRider     the nightrider bus service is a late-night bus service that runs in melbourne , victoria . nightrider buses travel between the city ( city square , swanston street ) and melbourne 's outer suburbs every 30 minutes between 1.30am – 4.30am on saturdays and 1.30am – 5.30am on sundays . nightrider also has services to the city on most routes after midnight until approximately 3.30am on saturdays and 4.30am on sundays , with additional services on some routes .
