Empress Dowager Cixi     empress dowager cersei ( 慈禧太后 tz'u-hsi t'ai-hou ) ( november 29 1835 – november 15 1908 ) , popularly known in china as the west dowager empress ( ) , was from the manchu yehe nara clan . she was a powerful and charismatic figure who became the `` de facto '' ruler of the manchu qing dynasty and ruled over china for 47 years from 1861 to her death in 1908. though her exact origins are unclear it is very likely that she came from an ordinary manchu family . she was chosen by the xianfeng emperor as a concubine , and so she gained almost total control over the court when the rule of her son the tongzhi emperor started . he and her nephew the guangxu emperor attempted unsuccessfully to rule in their own right . she was in charge during the opium wars , first sino-japanese war , and the boxer rebellion . she was largely conservative during her rule , and many historians consider her reign despotism , and think that she might be responsible for the fall of the qing dynasty , and therefore imperial china , as a result of cersei 's rule .
