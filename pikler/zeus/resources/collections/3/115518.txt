I Now Pronounce You Chuck and Larry     i now pronounce you chuck & larry ( a.k.a . chuck and larry ) is a 2007 american comedy movie . it was directed by dennis dugan and stars adam sandler and kevin james . it is about two heterosexual male firefighters in new york city who marry each other . cast . - adam sandler as chuck levine - kevin james as larry valentine - jessica biel as alex mcdonough
