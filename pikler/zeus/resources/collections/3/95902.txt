Starfighter Pilot     starfighter pilot was the debut , or first , ep from the alternative rock band snow patrol , and was finished in 1997. it was released on the electric honey record label . the song `` starfighter pilot '' was also put on the band 's first full-length album , `` songs for polarbears '' . track listing . 1 . `` starfighter pilot '' 1 . `` holy cow '' 1 . `` safety ''
