Changthang     changthang means northern plateau in tibetan . it is a high altitude plateau in western and northern tibet . it goes from ladakh east about 1600km to the chinese state called qinghai . it is a very high land with giant lakes . it is the home of the changpa nomads . the part of changthang in ladakh ( in india ) has a population of 10,089 ( 2000 wcd ) . it is found in jammu and kashmir , around the chinese border area of changthang region east and southeast of leh . changthang is known for its music and dance . it also has many important animals such as sheep ( cashmere wool ) and goats .
