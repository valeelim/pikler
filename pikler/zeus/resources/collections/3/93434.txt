Warendorf Rural District     warendorf is a `` kreis '' ( district ) in the northern part of north rhine-westphalia , germany . in medieval times the region was part of the prince-bishopric of münster . when it became part of the prussian province of westphalia , the new government in 1816 created the districts of warendorf and beckum . in 1975 these two districts were joined to form today 's district . the district is located east of the city of münster . the ems river runs through the district from east to west . the lippe river forms part of the southern border of the district . other websites . - official website ( german )
