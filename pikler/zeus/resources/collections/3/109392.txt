Argeș County     argeș ( ) is a county ( judeţ ) of romania , in wallachia . the capital of argeș county is piteşti . this county has a total area of 6,862 km² . the main river that croses the county is the argeș river . in the south , the main rivers are the vedea river and the teleorman river . the main sights of argeș county include : - the city of piteşti - the city of curtea de argeş , where one of the most beautiful monasteries in romania is located - the câmpulung - rucăr area - the făgăraş mountains - the transfăgărăşan - the leaota mountains - the poienari castle - the cotmeana monastery . argeș county has 3 municipalities , 4 towns and 95 communes . municipalities . - piteşti - câmpulung - curtea de argeş towns . - mioveni - costeşti - topoloveni - ştefăneşti
