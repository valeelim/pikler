Haines City     haines city is a city in polk county , florida , united states . as of 2007 , the economic and business research at the university of florida estimated that the city 's population was 18,748. thus making it the third largest city by population in the county . the city went without a public park as both lake eva park and railroad park were both undergoing some sort renovations . from july 2008 through july 2009. the city has many schools in the area , including haines city high school . notable residents . - wayne gandy - nfl star for the atlanta falcons , born and raised in haines city . other websites . - official city of haines city website
