Aeronautics     aeronautics is the study of how to make and use flying machines like aeroplanes or helicopters . the name comes from the ancient greek language . it means `` air navigating '' . part of aeronautics is aerodynamics , how air moves around things . many books are available on these topics . aeronautics is related to transport and engineering , and aerodynamics is science . related pages . - nasa - spacecraft other websites . - aeronautics citizendium
