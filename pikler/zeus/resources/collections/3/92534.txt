Oder-Spree     oder-spree is a `` kreis '' ( district ) in the eastern part of brandenburg , germany . the district is named after the two major rivers in the district - the spree , and the oder river which is the eastern border . the district was created in 1993 by joining the districts of eisenhüttenstadt , beeskow and fürstenwalde , and the independent city eisenhüttenstadt . other websites . - official website - website beeskow - website eisenhüttenstadt - website fürstenwalde - website schöneiche
