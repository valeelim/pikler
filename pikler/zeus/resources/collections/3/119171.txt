Temse     temse is a municipality in belgium . it is in the province of east flanders , in the region of flanders . there are four towns in the municipality : temse , elversele , steendorp , and tielrode . in 2007 , 26667 people lived there . it is at 51° 07 north , 04° 12 east . the name `` temse '' comes from the gaul ( gallo-roman ) word `` tamisiacum '' or `` tamasiacum '' . until the early 20th century ( 1900s ) , the french name of the city was used : `` tamise '' .
