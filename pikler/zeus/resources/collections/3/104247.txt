Systems theory     systems theory is an interdisciplinary field of science and the study of the nature of systems in nature , society , and science . more specificially systems theory is a framework to analyze or describe any group of objects that work in concert to produce some result . this can be a single organism , any organization or society , or any electronic , mechanical or informational artifact . systems theory as a technical and general academic area of study . it is founded by ludwig von bertalanffy and others in the 1950s . related pages . - chaos theory - fritjof capra
