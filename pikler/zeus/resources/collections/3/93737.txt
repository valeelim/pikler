Los Angeles County     los angeles county is a county in california and is by far the most populous county in the united states . figures from the u.s. census bureau give an estimated 2006 population of 9,948,081 residents , while the california state government 's population bureau lists a january 1 2007 estimate of 10,331,939. the county seat is the city of los angeles . other websites . - los angeles county official website - la county sheriff 's list of unincorporated areas in los angeles county - los angeles area chamber of commerce - lakewood accountability action group a group of residents that lives in lakewood ( within los angeles county ) and often challenges the county board of supervisors on issues that affect lakewood residents .
