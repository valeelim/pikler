GI (military)    gi ( military ) gi or g.i . is a term used to describe a member of the united states armed forces or an item of their equipment . the term is often thought to be an initialism of `` government issue '' but the origin of the term is in fact `` galvanized iron '' after the letters `` gi '' that used for equipment such as metal trash cans made from it in u.s. army inventories and supply records .
