Straffan     straffan ( ) is a village in county kildare , ireland on the banks of the river liffey , 25 km upstream of the irish capital dublin . with a population ( according to the census of 2006 ) of 439 – an increase since 2002 of 32.2 % – straffan is the 31st most populous centre in county kildare . other websites . - development plan - local area plan - straffan steam museum website
