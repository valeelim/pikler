Miami Marlins     the miami marlins are a major league baseball team in miami , florida . the team began play in 1993. they were called the florida marlins through the 2011 baseball season . in november 2011 , after the end of the season , they were renamed the miami marlins . the marlin is a large sport fish that lives in warm parts of the ocean . there was once a minor league baseball team called the miami marlins . the major league team was named the `` marlins '' in honor of the minor league team . the marlins ' first home was sun life stadium . they opened a new stadium in 2012 called marlins park .
