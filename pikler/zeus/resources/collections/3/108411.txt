The Billionaire of Dismal Downs     `` tbe billionaire of dismal downs '' is the ninth episode of `` the life and times of scrooge mcduck '' . after finding gold , scrooge buys the whitehorse bank and begins to build a financial empire . scrooge goes back to scotland where he tries to prove that he is a `` real scotsman '' by playing in the `` scottish games '' . scrooge fails but he decides to install his matrices in duckburg , on the occidental coast . the episode ends with scrooge and his sisters going to duckburg .
