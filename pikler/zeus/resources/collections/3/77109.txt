Australopithecus afarensis     australopithecus afarensis is an extinct hominid . it lived between 3.9 and 2.9 million years ago . `` a. afarensis '' was similar to slightly later `` australopithecus africanus '' . it had a slender build . `` a. afarensis '' may be more closely related to the genus `` homo '' than `` a. africanus '' . `` homo '' includes the modern human species , `` homo sapiens '' . the most famous fossil is the partial skeleton named lucy ( 3.2 million years old ) found by donald johanson and colleagues , who , in celebration of their find , repeatedly played the beatles song `` lucy in the sky with diamonds '' .
