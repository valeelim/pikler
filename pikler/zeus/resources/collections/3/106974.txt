Kølig Kaj     kølig kaj ( born 1971 ) was a danish rapper who gained national fame in 1997 by winning the danish melodi grand prix with his song `` stemmen i mit liv '' . his real name is thomas lægaard . at the eurovision song contest in 1997 , he ended up 16th , with only 25 points . following that , kølig kaj slowly went away , and has never really gained much success . `` kølig kaj '' literally means `` cool kaj '' ( pronounced kai , rhymes with `` high '' ) . he released an album called `` solgt ud !
