Cape Schanck, Victoria    cape schanck , victoria cape schanck is the south end of the mornington peninsula , victoria , australia . it is a piece of land that separates port phillip bay and westernport bay . it is a narrow headland with cliffs 84.7 metres ( 278 feet ) high . it has a lighthouse made from limestone built in 1859. in december 1800 , cape schanck was named by lieutenant james grant after admiral james schank .
