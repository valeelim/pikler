Ariel Rubinstein     ariel rubinstein ( born april 13 , 1951 in jerusalem ) is an israeli economist . he is a professor of economics at tel aviv university and new york university . rubinstein writes about game theory . he has a well-known john nash speech which he gives all around the world . he currently lives in tel-aviv . other websites . - ariel rubinstein 's personal homepage with links to most of his published works - the presentation of ariel rubinstein for the 2004 nemmers prize in economics . - nash lecture - rubinstein 's alternating offers bargaining paper - nash 's home page
