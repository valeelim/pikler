Booker Prize     the man booker prize for fiction , also known in short as the booker prize , is a literary prize awarded each year for the best original full-length novel , written in the english language , by a citizen of either the commonwealth of nations or the republic of ireland . to win the booker prize generally means to gain international success . other websites . - the official website of the man booker prize - the most honoured booker shortlisted books - a primer on the man booker prize and critical review of literature .
