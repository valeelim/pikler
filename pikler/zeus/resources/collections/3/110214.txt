A Wizard of Earthsea     a wizard of earthsea , written in 1968 , is the first of a series of books that are written by ursula k. le guin and set in the fantasy world called earthsea . the series is about a wizard named ged . this book won the 1979 lewis carroll shelf award . a mini-series based on `` a wizard of earthsea '' and `` the tombs of atuan '' was created on the sci fi channel . le guin has said that she was not happy with the mini-series . other websites . - ursula k. le guin 's official website - a part of `` tales from earthsea ''
