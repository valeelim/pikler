Vuze     vuze ( formerly azureus ) is a java-based bittorrent used for file sharing . the program 's logo is the blue poison dart frog ( dendrobates azureus ) . the azureus name was given to the project by co-creator tyler pitchford , who uses the latin names of poison dart frogs as `` nickname '' for his development projects . azureus was first released in june 2003 at sourceforge.net . it is now one of the most popular bittorrent clients since version 2.5.0.4 , azureus was distributed under the gnu general public license ( gpl ) . other pages . - comparison of bittorrent software - vuze other websites . - official website - azureus plugins - azureus wiki
