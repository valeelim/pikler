Jōmon period     the is the time in japanese prehistory which started in roughly 14,000 bc to 10,000 bc . the period ended in roughly 500 bc or 400 bc to 300 bc . `` jōmon '' means `` patterns of plaited cord '' or `` cord-patterned '' . related pages . - ainu people other websites . - university of tokyo , memory of the jomon period - niigata prefectural museum of history , the prehistoric archaeology of japan
