Vung Tau     vung tau ( vietnamese : `` vũng tàu '' ) is a city in ba ria-vung tau province , vietnam . this city has a population of 240.000 and an area of 154.0 km2 . this is a petroleum and tourism centre of vietnam . vung tau is located about 120 km northeast of ho chi minh city . vung tau town was founded by french colonists under the administration of paul doumer , then the governor general of french indochina . this city borders vietnam sea and has several beautiful beaches .
