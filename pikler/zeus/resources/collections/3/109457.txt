Bacău County     bacău ( ) is a county ( judeţ ) of romania , in moldavia . the capital of bacău county is bacău , with a population of about 130,000 people . bacău county has an area of 6,621 km² . bacău county has 3 municipalities , 5 towns and 80 communes . municipalities . - bacău - oneşti - moineşti towns . - comăneşti - buhuşi - dărmăneşti - târgu ocna - slănic moldova
