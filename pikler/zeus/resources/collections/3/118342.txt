Narcissus (genus)    narcissus ( genus ) narcissus ( narcissi ) ( often called a daffodil ) is the botanic name for a genus of mainly hardy , mostly spring-flowering , bulbs in the `` amaryllidaceae '' family . they are native to europe , north africa , and asia . it has pale yellow perianths with a darker central trumpet ( paracorolla or `` corona '' ) . the long , narrow leaves are slightly greyish in colour and rise from the base of the stem . other websites . - american daffodil society - gardening
