Kate Greenaway     catherine `` kate '' greenaway ( london , 17 march 1846-6 november 1901 ) was a children 's book illustrator and writer . her first book , `` under the window '' ( 1879 ) was a best-seller . the kate greenaway medal is awarded every year by the uk chartered institute of library and information professionals to an illustrator of children 's books . greenaway died of breast cancer in 1901 at the age of 55. she is buried in hampstead cemetery , london .
