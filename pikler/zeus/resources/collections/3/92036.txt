Döbeln Rural District     döbeln is a district in the free state of saxony , germany . the region was originally populated by sorbian peoples . the present borders of the district were made in 1952 , when the government of east germany formed the new districts . döbeln is one of the few districts which have not been changed after the german reunification . the district is on the banks of the freiberger mulde in the triangle between the cities of dresden , leipzig and chemnitz . other websites . - official website ( german )
