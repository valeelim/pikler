Brian Burke     brian thomas burke ( born 25 february 1947 ) was the 23rd premier of western australia from 25 february 1983 to 25 february 1988. he was from the labor party . he worked as a journalist before becoming a politician . as premier he was popular at first but later on his government was involved in a number of private companies that failed . he resigned to become an ambassador . during the 1990s he was convicted of committing crimes while he was premier and spent time in prison .
