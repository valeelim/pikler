Showgrounds     the showgrounds is a football stadium in sligo , ireland . it has a capacity of 5,500 . 2,400 of the 5,500 capacity are seated . there are two seated stands a blue stand and a red stand which holds most of the seats . there is also a terrace , there used to be two , but the old one got knocked down along with the shed . there are two training pitches , one of them is astroturf . sometimes there is a carnival in the car park . sligo rovers f.c . play their home games in the showgrounds .
