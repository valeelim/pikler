Haunted house     a haunted house is a house where supernatural events are believed to take place . many haunted houses are believed to contain ghosts . they can also contain the spirits of dead people . the rumour that a house is haunted often starts after something violent or tragic happens in the house , such as a murder or suicide . haunted houses are common in gothic fiction , and are generally seen as old castles or creaky , old , abandoned houses . a haunted house can be a type of attraction in amusements parks too . one scientific explanation of haunted houses is that poisonous gasses such as carbon monoxide from defective gas-burning appliances are what causes the hallucinations that people experience . another possible cause is infrasound . many haunted houses have been investigated and found to contain high levels of carbon monoxide or other poisons , which can cause hallucinations . the carbon monoxide thing pesticides can cause the same types of hallucinations as carbon monoxide . recreational drugs such as lsd and methamphetamine can cause hallucinations . unpleasant hallucinations are known in the recreational drug community as a `` bad trip '' . drug users are usually , but not always , aware that they are experiencing hallucinations related to the drug , at least after they become sober again .
