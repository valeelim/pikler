James R. Schlesinger     james rodney schlesinger ( february 15 , 1929 - march 27 , 2014 ) was united states secretary of defense from 1973 to 1975 under presidents richard nixon and gerald ford . he became america 's first secretary of energy under jimmy carter . on march 27 , 2014 , schlesinger died from complications of pneumonia at a baltimore hospital . he was 85 .
