Virtue     virtue ( latin `` virtus '' ; greek ἀρετή ) is a kind of behaviour which is thought to be good . the adjective is `` virtuous '' . someone who lives a virtuous life is someone who leads a good moral life , doing things that society thinks are good . the opposite of virtue is vice . philosophers throughout the ages have written their ideas about what they think virtue is . the ancient greek philosophers from plato onwards said that virtue consisted of four things : justice , courage , wisdom , and moderation . in the christian religion the three virtues were faith , hope and charity . these are mentioned in the bible in the first epistle to the corinthians chapter 13 verse 13. these virtues are also very important in the judaic and muslim traditions . related pages . - ethics - justice - seven deadly sins
