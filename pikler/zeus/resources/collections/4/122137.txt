Dysnomia (moon)    dysnomia ( moon ) dysnomia is the only known moon of the dwarf planet eris . it was found on september 10 , 2005 by michael e. brown and his team at the w. m. keck observatory . it is probably about 100 km in diameter ( across ) , although it may be up to 250 km in diameter . the moon was named `` dysnomia '' ( from the ancient greek word that means `` lawlessness '' ) after the daughter of the greek goddess eris .
