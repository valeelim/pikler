Semi-automatic firearm     a semi-automatic , or self-loading firearm is a gun that requires only a trigger pull for each round that is fired , unlike a single-action revolver , a pump-action firearm , a bolt-action firearm , or a lever-action firearm , which require the shooter to manually chamber each successive round . also , unlike a double-action revolver , power from one shot is used load the next , instead of using power from pulling the trigger .
