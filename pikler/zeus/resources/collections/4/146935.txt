Anton Reicha     anton reicha ( born 26 february 1770 ; died 28 may 1836 ) was a czech-born composer who became naturalized french . he was a lifelong friend of beethoven . reicha is now best remembered for the large number of quintets that he wrote for wind instruments , and for his work as a music teacher . franz liszt and hector berlioz were both pupils of reicha . reicha wrote several books about music theory and composition .
