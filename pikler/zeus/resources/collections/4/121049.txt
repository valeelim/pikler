Palos Verdes blue     the palos verdes blue butterfly is a small endangered butterfly that can be seen near the palos verdes peninsula in southwest los angeles county , california . so far , it can only be seen here and might be one of the rarest butterflies in the world . this butterfly has a wingspan of only 25–30 mm . the male has a bright silvery-blue dorsal wing outlined in a narrow line of black , while the female ’ s dorsal wing is a more brownish-gray color . both males and females have gray ventral wings with dark spots surrounded by white rings . `` g. lygdamus palosverdesensis '' was listed as an endangered species by the fish and wildlife service on july 2 , 1980. insects are not currently protected by california law . the main threat to the palos verdes blue has been the destruction of their living space by weeds , which has negative effects on the larval foodplants needed .
