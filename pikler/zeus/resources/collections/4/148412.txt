Greenville, Alabama    greenville , alabama greenville is a city in butler county , alabama , united states . at the 2000 census , the population was 7,228. the city is the county seat of butler county . it is called the camellia city , the state flower of alabama . the city of greenville has an average high temperature of 77.30 degrees and an average low temperature of 53.80 degrees fahrenheit . the city averages 4.80 inches of precipitation per month . the local government of greenville is run by the mayor and city council . the city council has five members . the city 's residents were on the first episode of the abc reality/game show series `` my kind of town '' , on sunday , august 14 , 2005. greenville is the location of a robert trent jones-designed golf course , cambrian ridge . the clubhouse for the golf course is on the highest point in butler county . greenville is home to sherling lake park and campground which has 41 campsites and surrounds two lakes . greenville is also the home of the watermelon jubilee , a local arts and crafts exposition held each year in the month of august . every year in september , the city hosts the butler county fair . also , during the fall , greenville hosts old time farm day which features activities such as tractor races , blacksmithing and quilting demonstrations .
