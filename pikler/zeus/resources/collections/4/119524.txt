Don Edwards (ice hockey)    don edwards ( ice hockey ) don edwards ( born september 28 , 1955 in hamilton , ontario , canada ) is a canadian retired professional ice hockey goaltender who played ten seasons in the national hockey league for the buffalo sabres , calgary flames , and toronto maple leafs . he won the vezina trophy along with bob sauve in 1980. he played in the 1980 and 1982 all-star games and backed up mike liut in the 1981 canada cup . on february 24 , 1982 , edwards was a part of history as he gave up wayne gretzky 's 77th goal of the 1981-82 nhl season in a game vs. the edmonton oilers at buffalo memorial auditorium . gretzky broke phil esposito 's former single season record of 76 goals and finished the year with 92 , which still stands as an nhl record .
