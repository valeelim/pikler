Goodnight Goodnight     goodnight goodnight is the fifth single by pop-rock band maroon 5 on the album `` it wo n't be soon before long '' . the song was released august 2008. the song is also the 8th track off the album . the music video was releasd august 21st , 2008. the video deals with a `` before and after '' sequence of a relationship between levine and his girlfriend , bailey . in the `` before '' part she was beautiful and in the `` after '' one they set apart , but he is still in love with her . discography . - `` if i never see your face again '' ( 2008 ) - `` goodnight goodnight '' ( 2008 )
