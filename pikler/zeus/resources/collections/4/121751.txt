Promotion and relegation     promotion and relegation are terms used in many sports . being promoted means moving up a league to one of a higher status . this usually happens when a team wins their league . relegation normally happens to the team ( s ) at the bottom of the league at the end of the competition . they are moved down a league to one of a lower status . for example : - `` team a '' wins the league and is promoted from the `` second division '' to the `` first division '' . - `` team b '' finishes last in the `` first division '' and is relegated to the `` second division '' .
