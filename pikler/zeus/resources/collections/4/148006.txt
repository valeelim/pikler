Hum Ek Hain     hum ek hain is a pakistani urdu movie directed by syed noor which was released across theaters in pakistan in november 2004. it stars shaan , saima , shamyl khan , haidar sultan . the title was meant to be `` 786 '' but the censor board members did not approve of `` 786 '' so it was changed to `` hum ek hain '' .
