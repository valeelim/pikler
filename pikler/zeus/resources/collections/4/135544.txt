Mahmud Shah Durrani     mahmud shah durrani ( 1769 - 1829 ) was the ruler of the durrani empire between 1801 and 1803 , again between 1809 and 1818. he was the son of timur shah durrani and half-brother of his predecessor , zaman shah . on july 25 , 1801 , zaman shah was deposed , and mahmud shah ascended to rulership . he then had a chequered career ; he was deposed in 1803 , restored in 1809 , and finally deposed again in 1818. he died in 1829. between his two stints , the country was ruled by shoja shah , another of his half-brothers .
