EasyJet     easyjet airline company limited , styled as easyjet , is a low cost airline founded in 1995. sir stelios haji-ioannou started the airline in 1995. he was in charge of easyjet until 2002. starting in 2003 , easyjet opened many bases at airports throughout europe . as of 2016 , easyjet is based at london luton airport . easyjet currently has 26 bases and its largest base is at london gatwick airport . it is the second largest low-fare airline in europe . it flies domestic and international scheduled services . it has 387 routes between 104 european and north african airports . easyjet was featured in the television series `` airline '' .
