Game (food)    game ( food ) game is any animal hunted for food or not normally domesticated . small birds , ground game ( such as squirrels and rabbits ) , and big game ( such as deer and moose ) are usually hunted . game animals are also hunted for sport . in britain , `` game birds '' usually means partridge , grouse , and quail . today , most countries have game laws . in some countries hunting is a part of well preserved tradition that is kept alive as a part of national identity .
