Pattan Kalan     pattan kalan is one of the 51 union councils of abbottabad district in the khyber pakhtunkhwa province of pakistan . pattan kalan is in the eastern edge of abbottabad district and is next to muzaffarabad district of kashmir . it is also next the following union councils of abbottabad district , kukmang and boi to the north , kuthwal to the west , beerangali and nambal to the south the union council is subdivided into the following areas : bandi sararha , banota , chamiali , drair , khokhriala , larri , sialkot , maseena kalan and pattan kalan . maseena kalan is a big village .
