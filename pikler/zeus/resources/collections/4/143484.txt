Discordianism     discordianism is a postmodern religion in which disorder is as important as order , existing within chaos . it was founded circa 1958–1959 by malaclypse the younger , who published its most important text : the `` principia discordia '' . the sacred chao and five fingered hand of eris are the most recognized symbols of discordianism . other websites . - principiadiscordia.com – contains the `` principia discordia '' in html .
