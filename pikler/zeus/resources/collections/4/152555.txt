Doral, Florida    doral , florida doral is a city in north miami-dade county , florida , united states . it is a suburb of miami . the population was 39,011 at the 2010 u.s. census . doral is very close to miami international airport . this has encouraged local and big business , and the headquarters of many large companies are in dorel , including carnival corporation , amadeus north america and the dutch airline martinair .
