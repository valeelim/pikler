The Mighty Boosh     the mighty boosh is a british comedy television series that is shown on bbc three . it was also a radio programme . since the television series , there have been live tours of it . the first series is about two zoo keepers called howard and vince . the second series is also about them but is set in a flat . the third is set in a shop . fielding first met barratt after seeing him perform his solo stand-up performance at the hellfire comedy club in the wycombe swan theatre . the pair soon found that they shared comic interests , formed a double act , and `` decided to be the new goodies '' . after their first performance together at a bar in london in april 1998 , barratt and fielding developed their zookeeper characters - howard moon and vince noir , respectively - in a series of sketches for paramount comedy ’ s `` unnatural acts '' . it was here that they also met rich fulcher , who would become bob fossil . fielding ’ s friend dave brown and fielding 's own brother michael also became regular collaborators .
