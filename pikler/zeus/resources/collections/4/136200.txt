Donald Rumsfeld     donald hebb rumsfeld ( born 9 july 1932 ) is a united states businessman and politician . rumsfeld was the 13th united states secretary of defense under president gerald ford from 1975 to 1977 , and the 21st secretary of defense under president george w. bush from 2001 to 2006. he is both the youngest ( 43 years old ) and the oldest ( 74 years old ) person to have held the position , as well as the only person to have held the position for two non-consecutive terms . rumsfeld is also the second longest serving secretary of defense , behind robert mcnamara .
