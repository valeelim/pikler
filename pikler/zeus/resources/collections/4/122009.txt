Dewal Manal     dewal manal is one of the 51 union councils of abbottabad district in the khyber pakhtunkhwa province of pakistan . the main tribe of this union council is the karlal . dewal manal is in the southern part of abbottabad district , it borders the following union councils , nara and seer gharbi ( to the south ) , nathia gali and nagri bala to the east and garhi phulgran to ( to the west and north ) . the union council is made up of the following areas : chehr , dewal manal , makool payeen , nagri tarli and sajkot .
