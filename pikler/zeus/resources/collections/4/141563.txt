RPM (magazine)    rpm ( magazine ) rpm was a canadian music magazine that had song and album charts for canada . it was started by walt grealis in february 1964 and printed with the help of stan klees who owned a record label . `` rpm '' was printed until november 2000 . `` rpm '' stood for `` records , promotion , music '' . sometimes the magazine was also called `` rpm weekly '' and `` rpm magazine '' .
