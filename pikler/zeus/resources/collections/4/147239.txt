British Rail Class 114     the british rail class 114 diesel multiple units were built by br derby from 1956 to 1957. forty-nine 2-car units were built , numbered 50001-49 for driving motors ( later renumbered 53001-49 ) and 56001-49 for driving trailers ( later renumbered 54001-49 ) . the units were used on services in the west midlands , lincolnshire and humberside .
