Cuts Like a Knife     cuts like a knife is the third album by the canadian rock artist bryan adams . released on january , 1983 through a & m records , the album became a big hit in canada when it was released . outside canada and the united states the album did not sell as well . after the release of reckless the album would chart on the british album chart and would later be a silver record by the bpi . it was made at little mountain sound , in vancouver , canada . three singles were released from the album : `` straight from the heart '' , `` cuts like a knife '' and `` this time '' , the three made adams very popular . personnel . - bryan adams - vocals , guitars , piano - jim vallance - guitars , percussion , keyboards - mickey curry - drums - tommy mandel - organ , synthesizer - keith scott - guitars , vocals - alfa anderson - background vocals - lou gramm of foreigner - background vocals
