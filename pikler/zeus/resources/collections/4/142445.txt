Streptococcus pneumoniae     streptococcus pneumoniae , or pneumococcus , is a gram-positive , alpha-hemolytic diplococcus aerotolerant anaerobe and a member of the genus `` streptococcus '' . an important human pathogen , `` s. pneumoniae '' was recognized as a major cause of pneumonia in the late 19th century and is the subject of many humoral immunity studies .
