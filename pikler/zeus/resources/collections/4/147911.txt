Missy Elliott     melissa arnette `` missy '' elliott ( born july 1 , 1971 ) is a five-time grammy award-winning american rapper , singer , songwriter , and record producer . with record sales of over seven million in the united states , she is the only female rapper to have six albums certified platinum by the riaa , including one double platinum ( `` under construction '' ) . elliot has released six studio albums . her seventh album is called block party . she has graves ' disease . discography . - `` supa dupa fly '' ( 1997 ) - `` da real world '' ( 1999 ) - `` miss e ... so addictive '' ( 2001 ) - `` under construction '' ( 2002 ) - `` this is not a test !
