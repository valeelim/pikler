Animator     an animator is an artist who makes drawings for movies . the animator makes many drawings which are called `` frames '' . these are played very quickly , one after another , to create animation . it makes it look as if the pictures are moving . animators can work in many areas including movies , television , video games , and the internet . there are many ways of making animations . animators usually work with other people to form a team . these may be other animators , but can also include layout artists ( who design the backgrounds , lighting , and camera angles ) , storyboard artists ( who draw panels of the action from the script ) , background artists ( who paint the `` scenery '' ) , voice actors who speak the words , and musicians who make the music . related pages . - animation - computer animation - computer graphics - key frame - sweat box other websites . - animation toolworks glossary : who does what in animation - how an animated cartoon is made
