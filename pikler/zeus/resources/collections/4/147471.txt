List of towns and villages in Northern Ireland     this is an alphabetical list of towns and villages in northern ireland . the towns of armagh , lisburn and newry are also classed as cities ( see city status in the united kingdom ) . the northern ireland statistics and research agency ( nisra ) uses these definitions : - town - population of 4,500 or more - small town - population between 4,500 and 10,000 - medium town - population between 10,000 and 18,000 - large town - population between 18,000 and 75,000 - intermediate settlement - population between 2,250 and 4,500 - village - population between 1,000 and 2,250 - small villages or hamlets - population of less than 1,000 towns are listed in bold . acton , aghacommon , aghadowey , aghadrumsee , aghagallon , aghalee , ahoghill , aldergrove , altamuskin , altishane , altmore , annaclone , annaghmore , annahilt , annahugh , annalong , annsborough , antrim , ardboe , ardgarvan , ardglass , ardmore , ardstraw , armoy , arney , articlave , artigarvan , artikelly , atticall , aughafatten , augher , aughnacloy
