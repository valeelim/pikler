Chicago Spire     the chicago spire is a very large skyscraper that was being built in chicago , illinois . because of financial problems , the project has not been finished . as of may 2013 , however , interest in the chicago spire site has risen , drawing in at least a half-dozen offers for the property . the developer is also considering a bid to take back control of the property , reigniting hope that the 2,000 ft. skyscraper may actually be built .
