Butters Stotch     leopold `` butters '' stotch ( voiced by matt stone ) is a popular fictional character in the animated television series `` south park '' . his nickname is a play on the word `` butterscotch '' . the creators have said in more than one dvd commentaries that butters is one of their `` top 3 favorite characters '' . butters is based on eric stough , the animation director of the show .
