Cloth mill Offermann     in the 18th century , the cloth mill offermann was a brass factory in the german city of stolberg . it was later changed to cloth mill and then to a restaurant and apartment building . the changes show the change of economical needs in that city . the building was founded as a brass factory . in the 18th century , it was one of many brass factories in the city . at the end of the 18th century , the brass industry in stolberg lost importance . the building was sold to a clothier of the nearby village of imgenbroich . he produced cloth for more than 60 years . at the beginning of the 19th century , the building was used as a restaurant . some parts of the building were greatly in need of repair . at the end of the 20th century , major repairs were done to the building . currently , it is used as an apartment building . other pages . - factory bauschenberg other websites . - historians are fascinated by the cloth mill offermann
