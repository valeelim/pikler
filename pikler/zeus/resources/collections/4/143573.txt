Exempli gratia     exempli gratia , mostly known in english-speaking countries by its abbreviation e.g. , means `` for example '' in latin . the literal translation is `` for the sake of example '' . gratia means `` for the sake of '' and is used with the genitive case . exempli is the genitive singular form of exemplum meaning example . `` exempli gratia '' is sometimes confused with `` id est '' ( `` i.e. '' ) . '' id est '' means `` that is ( to say ) '' in the sense of `` that means '' , or `` which means '' , or `` in other words '' , or sometimes `` in this case '' .
