Swainsona formosa     swainsona formosa , commonly called `` sturt 's desert pea '' , is the floral emblem of south australia it was named after explorer charles sturt . this plant is a part of the pea family , fabaceae . it is only found in australia . it grows in the drier areas of the country except in victoria . explorer william dampier was the first european to collect a sample of the plant . he found it growing in what is now called the dampier archipelago in western australia in 1699. other websites . - download a line drawing of the plant
