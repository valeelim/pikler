Spoonman     `` spoonman '' is a song by the american grunge band soundgarden , from their fourth studio album `` superunknown '' , which was released in 1994. the song describes artie the spoonman , a real-life musician who makes music with spoons . he was also featured in the song 's music video . `` spoonman '' won the grammy award for best metal performance at the 1995 grammy awards .
