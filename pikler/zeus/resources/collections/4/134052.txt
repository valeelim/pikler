Sulfur dioxide     sulfur dioxide ( also sulphur dioxide ) is the chemical compound with the formula so . it is a gas . it smells like burnt matches . it also smells suffocating . sulfur dioxide is produced by volcanoes and in various industrial processes . it is also used to protect wine from oxygen and bacteria . it can be produced by burning sulfur . it dissolves in water to produce sulfurous acid . it can be oxidized to sulfur trioxide , which is dissolved in sulfuric acid to make more sulfuric acid . it is used to make sulfites .
