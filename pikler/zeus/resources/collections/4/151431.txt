Eredivisie     eredivisie is a football league which is top division in netherlands . it was created in 1956. before 1956 , the teams competed in the netherlands football league championship . the most successful team has been afc ajax , with 33 titles . at the end of the 2014/15 season , the eredivisie was ranked the 10th best league in the europe . clubs 2014/15 . - ado den haag - ajax - az - sc cambuur - fc groningen - fc twente - fc utrecht - go ahead eagles - pec zwolle - feyenoord - heracles almelo - nac breda - excelsior - psv eindhoven - fc dordrecht - willem ii - sc heerenveen - vitesse
