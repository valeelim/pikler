Dianthus     dianthus is a genus of about 300 species of flowering plants in the family caryophyllaceae , found mainly in europe and asia , with a few species found south to north africa , and one species ( `` d. repens '' ) in arctic north america . common names include carnation ( `` d. caryophyllus '' ) , pink ( `` d. plumarius '' and related species ) and sweet william ( `` d. barbatus '' ) . the name `` dianthus '' is from the greek words `` dios '' ( `` god '' ) and `` anthos '' ( `` flower '' ) , and was mentioned by the greek botanist theophrastus .
