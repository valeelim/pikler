List of World Chess Champions     world chess champions are players who have won a match or tournament for the world championship at chess . both men and women can become champion , but no woman has ever been a challenger for the title . there is , however , a separate championship for women . there are also separate championships for specific age groups . before 1886 , there was no official championship held , but some players were thought to be pre-eminent . from 1948 on , the world chess federation fide held the championships . between 1993 and 2006 there were two world champion titles , the fide one and the classical one . the 2013 world championship match took place between anand and magnus carlsen ( challenger ) in chennai , the capital of tamil nadu , where anand grew up . usually , world championships had been played on neutral ground . fischer played spassky in iceland ; alekhine played capablanca in buenos aires . the soviets always played in moscow . however , sponsorship is needed for these matches , and the decision to accept the chennai bid was taken by fide . the challenger was victorious winning 3 games and achieving the necessary 6.5 points to win the title at the end of game 10. magnus carlsen , 22 and from norway is therefore the current world chess champion .
