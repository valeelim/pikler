Hala (clan)    hala ( clan ) hala is a `` gotra '' or clan of the jatt people found in the states of punjab and uttar pradesh in india and in the province of sindh in pakistan . the members of this gotra are hindus , muslims and sikhs . the muslim jatts of the hala gotra are found in sindh , while hindu and sikh jatts belonging to this gotra are found in uttar pradesh and punjab ( india ) . further reading . - ram swarup joon : history of the jats , rohtak , india ( 1938 , 1967 )
