United States Department of Energy     the united states department of energy ( doe ) is a cabinet-level department of the united states government responsible for energy policy and nuclear safety . its responsibilities include the nation 's nuclear weapons program , nuclear reactor production for the united states navy , energy conservation , energy-related research , radioactive waste disposal , and domestic energy production . doe also sponsors more basic and applied scientific research than any other us federal agency ; most of this is funded through its system of united states department of energy national laboratories .
