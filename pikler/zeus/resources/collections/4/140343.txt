Opel Zafira     opel zafira is a car produced by the german carmaker opel since 1999 , and is branded as vauxhall zafira in great britain . it currently in its third generation . the zafira is sometimes said to be the replacement of the sintra , because it was introduced roughly around the same time it was discontinued . also , they were both minivans . however , the zafira was in a different segment , and opel had not intended this .
