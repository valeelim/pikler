Over My Head (Cable Car)    over my head ( cable car ) over my head ( cable car ) is the debut single by rock band the fray off the hit album how to save a life ( album ) . the single was released september of 2005. the video ranked close to number one various times since the release of the single . the music video was actually shot in a high school in denver , colorado . in the brazilian hot 100 , `` cable car '' was ranked # 5 , `` world singles '' ranked the song # 24 , `` u.s. adult top 40 '' ranked it # 2 , and `` u.s. top 40 mainstream '' ranked it # 5. discography . - 2002 - `` movement ep '' - 2003 - `` reason ep '' - 2005 - `` how to save a life ( album ) '' - 2006 - `` live at the electric factory '' - 2007 - `` acoustic in nashville ''
