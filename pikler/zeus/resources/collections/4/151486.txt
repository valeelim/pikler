North American Soccer League     north american soccer league ( nasl ) is a soccer league with teams from united states , canada , and puerto rico . the league was founded in 2009 and began play in 2011. it is the second level of the soccer pyramid in both the u.s. and canada , behind major league soccer . puerto rico has its own soccer league , but it plays at a level below the nasl . the new york cosmos are the most succesful club with 3 titles .
