Bob Holly     robert william `` bob '' howard ( born may 13 , 1963 or 62 ) better known by his ring name hardcore holly , is an american professional wrestler . he is best known for his 15-year job he had with world wrestling entertainment . while in wwf/e , holly has held the wwf/e hardcore championship six times , the wwf/e tag team championship three times ( with the 1-2-3 kid , crash holly and cody rhodes ) , and the nwa world tag team championship once with bart gunn .
