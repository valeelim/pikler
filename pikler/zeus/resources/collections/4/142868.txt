Tiu (pharaoh)    tiu ( pharaoh ) tiu , also teyew etc. , was a ancient egyptian pharaoh who ruled in the nile delta . he ruled in the predynastic period in ancient egyptian history . the predynastic period began around 10,200 bc , and ended between 4,500 and 2,000 bc . tiu is mentioned in the palermo stone inscriptions , along with a small number of kings of lower egypt . nothing else is known of his life or what he did while he was pharaoh .
