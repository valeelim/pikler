Juniperus squamata     juniperus squamata ( flaky juniper or himalayan juniper ) is a species of juniper native to the himalaya and china , from northeastern afghanistan and pakistan east to yunnan in southwestern china , and with disjunct populations north to western gansu and east to fujian . it grows at 1,600-4,900 m altitude . it is the provincial tree of khyber pakhtunkhwa ( unofficial ) .
