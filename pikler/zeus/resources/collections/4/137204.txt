Candela     a candela is the si base unit of luminous intensity . it measures how bright something is . it is one of the basic units of science . a light bulb is bright , but the sun is much brighter , so the sun has more candelas than the light bulb . the frequency chosen for measuring candelas is in the visible spectrum near green . this is a wavelength of about 555 nanometres . the human eye is most sensitive to this frequency , when adapted for bright conditions . a candela sometimes is called 'candle ' .
