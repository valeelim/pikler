Altskeith     altskeith country house , is a traditional scottish country house built in 1898. the property enjoys a magnificent position on the edge of loch ard with spectacular views south over the loch and west to ben lomond . altskeith country house lies in the heart of loch lomond and the trossachs national park . the area where altskeith country house is located has excellent leisure facilities including a sailing club and the macdonald forest hills hotel and spa . there is also the village of aberfoyle at only 4 miles . altskeith featured as glenkirk house in the 1959 adaptation of `` the 39 steps '' .
