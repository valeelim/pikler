Ford Taurus     ford taurus was a car produced by ford motor company from 1986 through 2006. in 2008 ford sold a rebadged ford five hundred as the taurus . they also renamed the ford freestyle crossover cuv as the “ taurus x ” . these cars were produced through the 2015 model year . for 2016 ford showed a new luxury version of the taurus . but this version will only be sold in china . the ford taurus has had several different changes to the way it was built . each different version is called a `` generation '' .
