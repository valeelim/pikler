Hysteria     hysteria is when someone has a very strong emotion that is unmanageable . often , this means a very strong sense of fear . when someone has hysteria , it is called `` hysterics '' . hysteria could be caused by traumatic events in a person 's life . today , many psychiatrists and other doctors do not use the word `` hysteria '' for a patient . they use other words that have the same meaning . some of the words they may use are `` psychosomatic '' , `` functional '' , `` nonorganic '' , `` psychogenic '' , or `` medically unexplained '' . one of the conditions of hysteria may be histrionic personality disorder .
