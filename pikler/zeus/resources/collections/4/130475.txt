Saints' Way    saints ' way the saints ' way ( ) is a long-distance footpath in cornwall , in the united kingdom . it follows the probable route of early christian travellers making their way from ireland to the continent . rather than risk the difficult passage around land 's end they would disembark their ships on the north cornish coast and progress to ports such as fowey on foot . the footpath today between padstow in the north and fowey in the south is 26 miles in length and is well marked . guide books are available . saints ' way information at cornwall county council
