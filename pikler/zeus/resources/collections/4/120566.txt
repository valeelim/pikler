Johann Altfuldisch     johann altfuldisch , ( november 11 , 1911 at brückenau , austria – may 28 , 1947 at landsberg am lech ) was a member of the nsdap and the waffen-ss and one of the guards at the concentration camp mauthausen . he had the second highest rank at mauthausen . after the end of world war ii altfuldisch was charged by a military court for killing innocent people , especially members of ethnic groups and war prisoners . he was found guilty and hanged on may 28 , 1945. other websites . - johann altfuldisch ( pol . ) - johann altfuldisch ( pol . ) - photo of von altfuldisch - documents of the trial ( united states of america against . hans altfuldisch et al . - case no . 000.50.5 )
