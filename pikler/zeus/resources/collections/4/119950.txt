Littlest Pet Shop (video game)    littlest pet shop ( video game ) littlest pet shop is an video game that was made by electronic arts . it is part of the littlest pet shop series . the video game was released on the pc , wii , and nintendo ds . the game was released on october 14 , 2008 in north america . in august 2007 , electronic arts gained the rights to some hasbro brands , like `` monopoly '' , `` littlest pet shop '' , and nerf . in february , ea casual entertainment announced that their ea salt lake studio would develop and release `` littlest pet shop '' and `` nerf n-strike '' . the nintendo ds was 3 separate titles . each version had different pets . it also featured dgamer , the online service from disney . other websites . - littlest pet shop
