Biblical canon     the bible is a collection of texts . these texts are usually called `` books '' . there are different versions of the bible . these differences come from the fact that certain religious communities include those parts they find important and leave out other parts they think are less important . a biblical canon is a set of books of the bible that a certain movement considers scripture ( that is , inspired by god ) . these books are in almost all christian bibles . some bibles contain other books as well . these books were written before the birth of jesus christ . - genesis - exodus - leviticus - numbers - deuteronomy - joshua - judges - ruth - 1 & 2 samuel - 1 & 2 kings - 1 & 2 chronicles - ezra - nehemiah - esther - job - psalms - proverbs - ecclesiastes - song of solomon , sometimes called `` song of songs '' - isaiah - jeremiah - lamentations - ezekiel - daniel - hosea - joel - amos - obadiah - jonah - micah - nahum - habakkuk - zephaniah - haggai - zechariah - malachi these books were written after the birth of jesus christ .
