Deosai Mountains     a range of mountains called deosai stands between the vale of kashmir , gilgit agency and baltistan district . there is a vast plain covering an area of 580 sq miles , bearing the same name deosai . the whole area is uninhabited , surrounded by rocky mountains , with an elevation of 16,000 to 17,000 feet . the road leading to skardu from gurez passes through the deosai plains . tragbal pass and burzil pass , situated in this area , connect the valley of kashmir with baltistan and gilgit .
