Hazro Tehsil     hazro tehsil is one of the six tehsils ( subdivisions ) of attock district in the punjab province of pakistan , it is a newly created tehsil and forms parts of the na-57 constituency of attock . on 26 october 2006 , the minister of state for environment , malik amin aslam announced that rs150 million would be spent on the na 57 area of attock including remote areas of hazro tehsil .
