Interstate 182     interstate 182 ( sometimes called i-182 ) is a 15.19-mile long interstate highway in the state of washington , going into the tri-cities . the road goes from interstate 82 west of richland to u.s. route 12 in pasco . it runs along u.s. route 12 from interstate 82 to u.s. route 12 , washington state route 240 through richland , and u.s. route 395 in pasco .
