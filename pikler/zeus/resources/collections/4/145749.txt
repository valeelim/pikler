Funchal     funchal is the capital city of madeira , part of portugal . it is also the largest city of madeira with 111,892 people . funchal is in a valley . it has even temperatures all year round . there is a rainier and slightly cooler season from october through march . a drier and warmer season lasts from april through september . the harbour of funchal used to be the only major port in madeira . in 2007 cargo trade was moved to the port of caniçal , 12 miles to the east . funchal still serves cruise ships , ferries , and other tourist-related boats and yachts . a highway to câmara de lobos and ribeira brava is to the west . santa cruz , machico and caniçal can be reached to the east . madeira airport is east of the city , in the municipality of santa cruz .
