Live from SoHo (Maroon 5 album)    live from soho ( maroon 5 album ) live from soho is a live album by rock band maroon 5. the songs performed by the band are the first 6 tracks in order from track 1-6 on the album `` it wo n't be soon before long '' . the album was released march , 2008. the band maroon 5 performed `` live from soho '' in apple store soho in new york . track listing . 1 . `` if i never see your face again '' – 4:53 1 . `` makes me wonder '' – 4:17 1 . `` little of your time '' – 2:34 1 . `` wake up call '' – 4:10 1 . `` wo n't go home without you '' – 3:59 1 . `` nothing lasts forever '' – 4:00
