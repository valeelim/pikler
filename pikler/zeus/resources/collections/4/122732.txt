Shea Stadium     william a. shea municipal stadium , or more commonly shea stadium ( often referred to in short as shea ) , was a major league ballpark that was the home for the new york mets 1964 to 2008 when the mets left for their new home citi field . shea was also the home of the new york yankees while yankee stadium was being renovated for the 1974 and 1975 seasons . it was demolished between october 14 , 2008 and february 18 , 2009 .
