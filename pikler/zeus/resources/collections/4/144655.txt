Julia Somerville     julia mary fownes somerville ( born july 14 , 1947 ) is a reporter . she has worked for bbc news and itn . she studied english at sussex university . somerville has been interested in art . in 2001 , she was one of the people who selected the national portrait gallery 's bp portrait of the year . she has also served as a judge for several years on the riba annual architecture award panels . on 18 september 2003 , somerville was appointed chair of the advisory committee of the government art collection . somerville has married three times : to stephen band ( 1970 - 1975 , no children ) , ray gowdridge ( 1984 - 1992 , two children ) . she currently lives in north london with her third husband , the architect sir jeremy dixon . somerville and dixon were interviewed by police in 1996. the people who developed their photograph had raised suspicions , because the couple wanted ot have images of their seven-year-old child developed . these photos showed the child bathing . no caution or charges followed the investigation .
