Kaneez (1965 movie)    kaneez ( 1965 movie ) kaneez is a pakistani urdu black and white movie directed by renown director hassan tariq and produced by hassan tariq and ali sufiyan afaqi . it casted waheed murad , zeba , mohammad ali , sabiha khanam , lehri , saqi , adeeb and talish . it is a musical family relationship movie . waheed murad and zeba played lead roles , and mohammad ali played a complete character role .
