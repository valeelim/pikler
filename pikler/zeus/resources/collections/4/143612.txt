French Beach, Karachi    french beach , karachi french beach of karachi is a small fishing village . it is half way between hawkes bay and paradise point . the local people call it `` haji abdullah goth '' . it is surrounded by a boundary wall . it has 20 huts for rent . the village does not have running water or electric power . its rocky beach and clear waters are ideal for snorkeling and scuba diving . visitors must bring their own equipment as well as food and drink supplies .
