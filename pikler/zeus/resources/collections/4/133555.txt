Equilibrioception     equilibrioception is the sense of balance . this is what prevents a person or animal from falling over while walking or doing other physical activity . in humans , equilibrioception is mainly sensed by the detection of acceleration , which occurs in the vestibular system . other senses play roles as well , e.g . the visual system and proprioception . the importance of visual input for balance is illustrated by its being harder to stand on one foot with eyes closed than with eyes open . the sense of balance often gets worse as a person ages . after a stroke , it may be improved with the help of special training . related pages . - inner ear - calisthenics - training - yoga - benign paroxysmal vertigo of childhood
