Mehrabpur     mehrabpur is a town and a tehsil in the naushahro feroze district of sindh in pakistan . it is on the main railway line between karachi and lahore . it is a junction station with a disused branch line to naushahro feroze . on 19 december 2007 , at 2:30 am an overcrowded express train travelling from karachi to lahore derailed 2 km south of mehrabpur killing over 40 people and injuring hundreds more . derailment seems to have been caused by defective rails .
