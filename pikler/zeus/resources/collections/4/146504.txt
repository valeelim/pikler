18 Days     `` 18 days '' is the second single off the 2008 album by saving abel called `` saving abel '' . the song is also on the previous self-titled album by saving abel . the song was released in late 2008 when it took major airplay . `` 18 days '' by saving abel reached 14 on the u.s. `` billboard '' hot modern rock tracks . the single also peaked at # 8 on the u.s. `` billboard '' hot mainstream rock tracks .
