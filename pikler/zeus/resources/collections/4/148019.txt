British Rail Class 303     the british rail class 303 is a type of electric multiple unit . they are also known as `` blue train '' units . they were introduced in 1960 for the electrification of the north clyde and the cathcart circle lines in strathclyde . at first they were classified as am3 units before the introduction of the tops classification system . they were the dominant emu on the glasgow suburban railway network for over 25 years . the final units were withdrawn from service in 2002 .
