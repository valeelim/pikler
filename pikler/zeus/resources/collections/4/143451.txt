Boaz, Alabama    boaz , alabama boaz is a city in etowah and marshall counties in the u.s. state of alabama . the city is named after the husband of ruth , from the book of ruth in the bible . it is part of the 'gadsden , alabama metropolitan statistical area ' . as of the 2000 census , the population of the city is 7,411. according to the 2005 u.s. census estimates , the city had a population of 7,893 .
