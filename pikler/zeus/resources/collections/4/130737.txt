Gujar Khan     gujar khan ( ) is a city in rawalpindi district , punjab , pakistan . it is the capital of gujar khan tehsil , one of the seven tehsils or subdivisions of rawalpindi district . it is about 55 kilometres southeast of islamabad , the capital of pakistan and 220 km to the northwest of lahore capital of punjab . gujar khan , as well as being tehsil capital , is divided into 3 union councils : - gujar khan-i - gujar khan-ii - gujar khan-iii according to the 1998 census of pakistan , the city had 57,099 people . pothowari is the main language of gujar khan , other languages are urdu , punjabi and pashto . gujar khan has four twin cities , these are :
