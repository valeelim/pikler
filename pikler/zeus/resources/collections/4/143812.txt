Daleville, Alabama    daleville , alabama daleville is a city in dale county , alabama , united states . at the 2000 census , 4,653 people lived there . it is part of the enterprise–ozark micropolitan statistical area . the city 's nickname is `` gateway to fort rucker '' , as this u.s. army post is just north of town . cairns army airfield is to the south of daleville on the road to nearly clayhatchee . notable residents of daleville . - mickey andrews – college football coach - tony richardson – nfl player - robert w. smith – composer - shawn stuckey – nfl player and attorney
