Patnitop     patnitop or patni top , is a hill top tourist location in udhampur district on the national highway 1a , on the way from udhampur to srinagar . it is located in the shiwalik belt of the himalayas , and is in jammu and kashmir , kashmir . the river chenab flows close to this location . origin of the name patnitop is a distortion of the original name of `` patan da talab '' meaning `` pond of the princess '' . in olden times a pond existed in the meadows and the kings princess often used to have her bath there . part of the pond still exists near the youth hostel.the britishers probably could not get the name correctly in their revenue records , and over the years it changed from `` patan da talab '' to patnitop .
