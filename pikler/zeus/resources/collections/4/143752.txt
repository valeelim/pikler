Amfissa     amfissa is a greek city . it is in the center of greece , at the foot of parnassos mountain . near amfissa is delfi . according to the 2001 census , 6,947 lived there . during the middle ages it was named salona , but today is named with its ancient name , amfissa . amfissa is an agriculturist city . the main financial source in amfissa is olive oil . oil-tree is the main cultivation in district of amfissa . the most interest sight in the town is the ancient castle above town .
