Give 'Em Enough Rope     give 'em enough rope is the second album by the clash . it was released in 1978. in the united states , this was their first official album because it was released before the u.s. version of `` the clash '' . the album was well liked and got to number 2 in the uk chart , and number 128 in the u.s. all songs were written by joe strummer and mick jones , except where noted . all songs were arranged by the clash . all lead vocals were by strummer , except `` stay free '' by jones . side one . 1 . `` safe european home '' – 3:50 1 . `` english civil war '' – 2:35 1 . `` tommy gun '' – 3:17 1 . `` julie 's been working for the drug squad '' – 3:03 1 . `` last gang in town '' – 5:14 side two . 1 . `` guns on the roof '' ( strummer , jones , paul simonon , topper headon ) – 3:15 1 . `` drug-stabbing time '' – 3:43 1 . `` stay free '' – 3:40 1 . `` cheapskates '' – 3:25 1 . `` all the young punks ( new boots and contracts ) '' – 4:55
