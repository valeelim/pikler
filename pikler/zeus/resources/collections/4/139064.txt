Scar (The Lion King)    scar ( the lion king ) scar is a fictional character and the main antagonist in walt disney pictures ' animated movie `` the lion king '' . he is the younger brother of mufasa , the great uncle of kion , kopa , and kiara , and the uncle of simba . scar was voiced by jeremy irons with singing vocals by jim cummings . his supervising animator was andreas deja . scar is one of four disney characters to be nominated for `` best villain '' in the mtv movie awards ( the other three being the white witch , captain barbossa and davy jones ) , and is also the only disney animated character to have achieved this .
