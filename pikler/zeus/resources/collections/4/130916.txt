Korba     korba is a city in the central indian state of chhattisgarh . the city is the center of power in the state , meaning that it is the source from which people who live there get their energy supply . it contains major power companies such as ntpc and lenco . apart from power projects , it is the home of the most profiting coal mines in india namely dipka & gevra which are part of secl ( south eastern coalfields limited ) . vedanta group 's presence is also felt through balco aluminium plant .
