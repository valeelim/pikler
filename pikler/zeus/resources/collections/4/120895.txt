Brooklyn Nets     the brooklyn nets are a team in the national basketball association in the new york city community of brooklyn . they moved into their current home arena , barclays center , for the 2012–13 season . the team was founded as the new jersey americans in 1967 , and played in the american basketball association ( aba ) . after one season in that state , they moved to long island , becoming the new york nets . in 1976 , the nets were one of four aba teams that joined the nba , and returned to new jersey a year later , becoming the new jersey nets . after their return to new jersey , they played on the campus of rutgers university . in 1981 , they moved to east rutherford , closer to new york city . in 2010 , they moved to newark , and played there until their new brooklyn arena was built . once they moved to brooklyn , they became the brooklyn nets . home arenas . - teaneck armory ( 1967–1968 ) - long island arena ( 1968–1969 ) - island garden ( 1969–1971 ) - nassau veterans memorial coliseum ( 1971–1977 ) - louis brown athletic center|rutgers athletic center ( 1977–1981 )
