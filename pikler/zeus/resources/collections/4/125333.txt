Dreamgirls     dreamgirls is a musical . tom eyen wrote the book and lyrics . henry krieger wrote the music . the musical was loosely based upon the supremes , the shirelles , and other all-girl groups . the musical premiered on broadway at the imperial theatre on 20 december 1981. it closed on 11 august 1985 after 1,521 performances . it was released as a feature-length movie in 2006 .
