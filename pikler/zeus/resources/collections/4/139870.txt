Derealization     derealization ( dr ) is a change in the way someone views the world so that it seems unreal . derealization can also make someone feel as if their environment does not have emotion or depth . this is a symptom of other problems , such as sleeping disorders , schizophrenia , and other mental disorders . it can also be caused by drug intoxication . similar to derealization is depersonalization , but the two are different . depersonalization is a sense of unreality about oneself . derealization is a sense of unreality of the outside world . derealization often happens during a traumatic or tragic event .
