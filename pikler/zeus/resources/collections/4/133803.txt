Sidewalk     a sidewalk is a path for pedestrians that runs along the side of a road . it is called a sidewalk in american english , but can also be called a pavement ( mainly british english ) and south african english , a footpath ( australian english , irish english , indian english and new zealand english ) or footway ( engineering term ) . pedestrians use sidewalks to keep them safe from vehicles on the road . sidewalks can be made of bricks or from tarmac .
