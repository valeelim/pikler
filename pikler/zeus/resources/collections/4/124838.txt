BBC Online     bbc online ( which used to be called bbc.co.uk ) is the website for the bbc 's uk online services . it is currently the 4th most popular web service in the uk , and includes features such as bbc news , bbc sport , bbc weather , bbc iplayer and learning services such as `` bitesize '' . the bbc has had a website for information about its tv and radio programmes since 1994 , but did not launch officially as bbc online until december 1997 , following government approval to fund it by tv licence fee . the website address was registered in 1991 .
