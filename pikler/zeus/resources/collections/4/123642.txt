Electronic Gaming Monthly     electronic gaming monthly ( abbreviated as egm ) is an american video game magazine published by ziff davis that started in 1989. it 's one of the most popular gaming magazines in america . every april fools ' day , `` electronic gaming monthly '' does an april fools ' day joke . one of the most famous is when they said that sonic and tails can be found in `` super smash bros. melee '' if the player completes a very hard task , and for a long time after that , people thought that they really were in . sonic later appeared in `` super smash bros. brawl '' .
