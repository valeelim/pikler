Rajshahi Division     rajshahi division is one of the six administrative divisions of bangladesh . it has a population of almost 30 million and area of about 34,513 km . rajshahi division consists of 16 districts , 128 upazilas and 1,092 unions ( the lowest administrative tier ) . it has an excellent rail and roads . the divisional city rajshahi is only four hours road journey away from dhaka , the capital city . rajshahi division is in the north western corner of bangladesh . the famous river padma borders rajshahi division on the south and another famous river , jamuna , lies across the eastern border . in the north and west , rajshahi division shares a border with india . references . - banglapedia : rajshahi division
