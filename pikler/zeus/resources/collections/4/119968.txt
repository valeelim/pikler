Fill (music)    fill ( music ) a fill in popular music is a short instrumental passage , between melody lines of a song . ( a longer passage between verses is called a solo . ) fills are usually played with a keyboard instrument or a guitar , but other instruments ( such as a saxophone ) may also be used . drum fills are sometimes called `` drum rolls '' , but a roll is actually a steady beat on a single drum , while a fill may use more than one drum or beat .
