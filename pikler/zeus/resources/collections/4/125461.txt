Paramedic     a paramedic is a health care professional who works in different cases of emergency . they help people who have been in accidents or are having medical emergencies . they use different techniques and protocols to stabilize the patient . then they take the person to a hospital in an ambulance . they evaluate the patient with specific information they have . they try to prevent the condition of the person from getting worse before they reach the hospital , where doctors and nurses will take over care . a paramedic is trusted by the people and can be part of every kind of unstable situations and emergency . videos . - ambulance ( urgences-sant√© ) 911 call other websites . - http : //www.quebecregion.com/fr - http : //www.ville.laval.qc.ca/wlav3/index.php ? pid=1 - http : //ville.montreal.qc.ca/portal/page ? _pageid=4677,10107569 & _dad=portal & _schema=portal - http : //www.urgences-sante.qc.ca - http : //ville.montreal.qc.ca/
