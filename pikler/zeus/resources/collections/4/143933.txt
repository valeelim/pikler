King Naresuan     king naresuan the great . ( another name : `` phra-ong-dam '' or `` naret '' ) . he was born in the year of the rabbit ( refer to chinese zodiac year ) , b.e . 2098 ( a.d 1555 ) at jan palace , phitsanulok . his father was king mahathammaracha and his mother was queen wisutkhasat . king narasuan had one older sister named princess suphan thewi ( supankanlaya ) and one brother named king ekathotsarot ( phra-ong-khao ) . then , the burmese king bayinnong moved his troops to damage ayutthaya and he conquered it . so , he asked king naresuan for his adopted child while naresuan was 9 years old from . when he was 16. king mahathammaracha wanted naresuan to come back to be a viceroy of phitsanulok and gave princess suphan kanla ya instead as the captive from phitsanulok . prestiges/ reputations of king naresuan . b.e . 2113 he fought with khmer 's troop and he won . b.e . 2114 he was established ( set up ) to be a governor of phitsanulok when he was 16. b.e . 2117 he fought at viengjan , and he has a smallpox . so he came back to phitsanulok .
