Wii Sports Resort     wii sports resort ( also known as wii sports 2 ) is a sports video game for the wii . it is a sequel to `` wii sports '' and was released in summer 2009. the game is part of a series of games sometimes referred to as the wii series . the game comes bundled with the motionplus device and another wii remote jacket , both of which are also sold separately . the game is set in beach resort called wuhu island . here are what games can be played on wii sports resort . - swordplay - wakeboarding - frisbee - archery - basketball - table tennis - golf - bowling - power cruising - canoeing - cycling - air sports
