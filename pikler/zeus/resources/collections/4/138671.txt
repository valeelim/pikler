Lissamphibia     the subclass lissamphibia includes all living amphibians . they are classified in three orders : - the anura : frogs , including toads - the caudata or urodela : salamanders , including newts - the gymnophiona or apoda : the limbless caecilians . although the ancestry of each group is controversial , all share certain common characteristics , which indicates they evolved from a common ancestor and so form a clade .
