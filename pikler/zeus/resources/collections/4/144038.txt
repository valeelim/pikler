Charles Barkley     charles wade barkley ( born february 20 , 1963 ) is an american retired professional basketball player and politician . nicknamed `` sir charles '' for his aggressive and outspoken behavior and `` the round mound of rebound , '' for his unusual build and talent as a player , barkley established himself as one of the national basketball association 's most dominating power forwards . often considered the best power forward in the league ( nba ) , people did not agree with his totalitarian views . he is an analyst on the television show `` inside the nba '' . other websites . - charles barkley : nba.com profile - charles barkley : nba.com historical playerfile - charles barkley : nba.com historical biography - charles barkley : hall of fame profile
