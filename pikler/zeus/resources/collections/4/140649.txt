Sukhoi Su-30     sukhoi su-30 is a russian fighter aircraft . it is developed by sukhoi aviation corporation . it is a 4.5-generation jet fighter aircraft . the aircraft is used by the following air forces : - algerian air force - angolan air force - people 's liberation army air force - indian air force - indonesian air force - kazakh air force - royal malaysian air force - russian air force - uganda air force - venezuelan air force - vietnam people 's air force su-30k export version of the basic su-30 su-30mki version for india su-30sm version of the su-30mki for russia
