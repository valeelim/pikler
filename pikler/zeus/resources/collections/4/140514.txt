Guy de Maupassant     guy de maupassant ( 5 august 1850 – 6 july 1893 ) was a popular 19th century french writer . he is considered one of the fathers of the modern short story . he was an apprentice of flaubert . maupassant 's stories have a common economy of style and their efficient , effortless dénouement ( ending ) . many of the stories are set during the franco-prussian war . many more describe the war . he also wrote six short novels .
