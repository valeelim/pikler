Goreeni     goreeni is one of the 51 union councils of abbottabad district in the khyber pakhtunkhwa province of pakistan . goreeni is in the south of abbottabad district and forms part of khyber pakhtunkhwa 's border with punjab province where it lies next to murree tehsil of rawalpindi district . the union council of goreeni is made up of the following areas : bagla , bajarian , banwari , danah , goreeni , kotal , palasi and surna garhage .
