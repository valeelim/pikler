Rick Rubin     frederick jay `` rick '' rubin ( born march 10 1963 in long island , new york ) is an american record producer . he is currently in charge of columbia records . he helped merge the rap and heavy metal genres . mtv called him `` the most important producer of the last 20 years . '' rubin won five grammy awards in 2006 including `` producer of the year , non-classical '' . in 2007 , rubin was listed among `` time magazine '' 's 100 most influential people in the world . rubin is the owner of a mansion in los angeles called the mansion . many albums he produces are recorded there .
