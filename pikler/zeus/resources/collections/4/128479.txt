Woolfardisworthy, Torridge    woolfardisworthy , torridge woolfardisworthy is a village in the torridge district in the english county of devon . the name of the village is a local curiosity , as its pronunciation ( and occasional spelling ) differs to how one may try to pronounce it . on local signs , the village is sometimes marked as woolsery alongside the original name . this is due to the pronunciation of the village 's name being /ˈwʊlzərɨ/ ( `` woolz'ry '' ) . there is another ( smaller ) village of the same name in the mid devon district .
