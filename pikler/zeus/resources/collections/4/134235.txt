American Museum of Natural History     the american museum of natural history ( amnh ) , on the upper west side , manhattan , new york , usa , is one of the largest museums in the world . with its park-like grounds , the museum has 25 interconnected buildings that house 46 permanent exhibition halls , research laboratories , and its renowned library . the collections contain over 32 million specimens , of which only a small fraction can be displayed at any given time . the museum has a scientific staff of more than 200 , and sponsors over 100 special field expeditions each year . other websites . - amnh american museum of natural history main website - history of the amnh - library collection is searchable online .
