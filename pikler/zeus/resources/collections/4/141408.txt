Tehzeeb (1971 movie)    tehzeeb ( 1971 movie ) tehzeeb , is a pakistani urdu movie . it was released on november 20 1971. the makers of this movie have been asked to change the lyrics of a reference to misr might prove detrimental to diplomatic relations with that country . while the line `` laga hai misr ka bazaar dekho '' changed to `` laga hai husn ka bazaar dekho '' in the movie soundtrack , the gramophone records , already sold in good numbers , are beyond redemption .
