Russell T Davies     russell t davies , obe ( born stephen russell davies , 27 april 1963 ) , is a critically acclaimed british television producer and writer from swansea , wales . he is known for television programmes such as `` queer as folk '' and `` the second coming '' , and for bringing back the popular science-fiction television series `` doctor who '' , and creating its spin-off series `` torchwood '' and `` the sarah jane adventures '' . he wrote the book `` doctor who : the writer 's tale '' . in 2015 three new shows by davies , `` cucumber '' , `` banana '' , and `` tofu '' , started . davies is openly gay .
