Bombylius major     bombylius major , the large bee fly , is a fly which is a bee mimic . it is a parasitoid on bees and wasps . the eggs are flicked by the adult female toward the entrance of the underground nests of solitary bees and wasps . after hatching , the larvae find their way into the nests to feed on the grubs . they can be found in april to june in europe and north america and some parts of asia .
