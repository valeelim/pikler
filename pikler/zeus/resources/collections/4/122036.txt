Queen's Park F.C.    queen 's park f.c . queen 's park football club is a famous football club based in glasgow , scotland , and is the oldest football club in scotland , founded in 1867 , and the oldest outside of england and wales . the club has a lot of history and tradition , and is still the only amateur club in the scottish league . the club 's home is one of 29 uefa five-star stadia , the all-seated hampden park in south east glasgow , which is also the home of the scottish national team . queen 's park have won the scottish cup the third most amount of times , behind rangers and celtic . the club 's amateur status is reflected by their motto , `` ludere causa ludendi '' . this means `` to play for the sake of playing '' .
