Hemis National Park     hemis national park ( or hemis high altitude national park ) is a high altitude national park in the eastern ladakh area of the state of jammu and kashmir in india . it is the only national park in india north of the himalayas . the park is home to about 200 snow leopards . hemis is the only refuge in india with the shapu . the tibetan wolf , the eurasian brown bear , and the red fox are also in hemis .
