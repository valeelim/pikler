Insaniyat (1967 movie)    insaniyat ( 1967 movie ) insaniyat ( , ) is a pakistani urdu black and white movie . it was produced by a. hameed . this movie is one of the masterpieces of the director shabab kiranvi . the movie starred waheed murad , zeba , tariq aziz , firdous and nanna . this movie is one of the hit movies of waheed murad .
