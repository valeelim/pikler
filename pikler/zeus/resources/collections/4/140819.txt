Love Parade     the love parade was a big festival centered around techno music in germany . trucks with loudspeakers and deejays played loud music and passersby danced to it . the festival began in berlin in 1989. it had been held most years in different locations . in 2004 and 2005 , there were no love parades . the first love parade was held with about 150 people in 1989. in 1999 , there were about 1.5 million people . in 2007 , there were 1.2 million people and in 2008 , there were 1.6 million people . the last love parade was held in 2010 , at which 21 people were crushed to death . the following are similar events in germany : - union move in munich - generation move in hamburg or kiel - reincarnation in hanover - vision parade in bremen - nachttanzdemo in frankfurt am main - zug der liebe in berlin the following are similar events in other countries : - street parade in zürich - lake parade in geneva - techno parade in paris - lovevolution in san francisco there are also protest parades , like the fuckparade in berlin , and the `` antiparade '' in zürich .
