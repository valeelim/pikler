Sehwan Sharif Tehsil     sehwan sharif ( ) is a city in dadu district , sindh , pakistan . the city is known for its muslim patron saint hazrat lal shahbaz qalandar who lived here in 13th century ce . the mausoleum of hazrat shahbaz qalandar attracts hundred of thousands of faithful muslims every year . sehvan sharif is basically known by the muslim sufi saint , philosopher and poet hazrat syed usman shah marwandi commonly known as lal shahbaz qalandar ( ال شھباز قلندر ) .
