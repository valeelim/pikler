Nara, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa    nara , khyber pakhtunkhwa nara is one of the 51 union councils of abbottabad district in the khyber pakhtunkhwa province of pakistan . nara is in the southern part of the district . the union council of nara takes its name from the village of nara . the main tribe of this union council are the karlal . the union council of nara is subdivided into the following areas : changah , dakhan , gohra , kanger hoter , kehan , maira ulla , mari , mohri-badbhen , nara and satora .
