Gareth David-Lloyd     gareth david-lloyd ( born march 28 1981 ) is a welsh actor who a lot of people know for his role as ianto jones in the british science fiction television programme `` torchwood '' . born gareth david lloyd in bettws , newport . his first acting role was as a robot in a school play . when he was a teenager , gareth joined the gwent young people 's theatre in abergavenny and the dolman youth theatre in newport . while he was there he appeared in several plays , including `` henry v '' , in which he played the most important character . he studied at coleg gwent and then trained at the national youth theatre , before moving to reading .
