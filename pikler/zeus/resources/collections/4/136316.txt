Behemoth     behemoth is a metal band from danzig , poland . their early albums were black metal , but later they switched to a mixture of death metal and black metal . the band has been important in the creation of the polish extreme metal underground . discography . - `` sventevith ( storming near the baltic ) '' ( 1995 ) - `` grom '' ( 1996 ) - `` pandemonic incantations '' ( 1998 ) - `` satanica '' ( 1999 ) - `` thelema.6 '' ( 2000 ) - `` zos kia cultus ( here and beyond ) '' ( 2002 ) - `` demigod '' ( 2004 ) - `` the apostasy '' ( 2007 ) - `` evangelion '' ( 2009 ) - `` the satanist '' ( 2014 )
