Be (Cyrillic)    be ( cyrillic ) be ( б , б ) is the second letter in the cyrillic alphabet . it came from the greek beta , along with the cyrillic ve . its old name was `` buky '' . it stands for the roman b. the б looks like a b with the top curve missing and the lowercase one looks like the number 6. people say it as [ b ] . related pages . - b - beta ( letter )
