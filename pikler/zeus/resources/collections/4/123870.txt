Enter Shikari     enter shikari are a post-hardcore band from st albans , hertfordshire , england . the band is named after the punjabi word `` shikari '' meaning hunter . the band have played at festivals such as reading and leeds . they are known for songs such as `` sorry you 're not a winner '' , `` arguing with thermometers '' , `` juggernauts '' and `` anything can happen in the next half hour '' . they have an album called `` common dreads '' that contains `` common dreads '' , `` solidarity '' , `` step up '' , `` juggernauts '' , `` wall '' , `` zzzonked '' , `` havoc a '' , `` no sleep tonight '' , `` gap in the fence '' , `` havoc b '' , `` antwerpen '' , `` the jester '' , `` halcyon ( intro ) '' , `` hectic '' and `` fanfare for the conscious man '' . the newest album to be released by shikari includes songs such as `` arguing with thermometers '' and `` warm smiles do not make you welcome '' . the band toured this album multiple times . their final tour was named `` flash flood of christmas '' . `` take to the skies '' was the debut studio album by enter shikari . it was released on march 25 , 2007 by christopher bowman records .
