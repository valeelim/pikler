Harlem     harlem is a big neighborhood in the new york city borough of manhattan . it was a village independent of new york city until 1873. most of the people in central harlem are african-american . most of the people in west and east harlem are hispanic . it started as a dutch village called nieuw haarlem . harlem is named after the city of haarlem in the netherlands . there is a lot of crime in harlem , but there is becoming less . the most common cause of death for young black males there is getting murdered . in the 1920s the harlem renaissance happened in harlem .
