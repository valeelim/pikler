Honey to the Bee     `` honey to the bee '' is a single by billie piper released in 1999. it got to # 3 in the uk singles chart . in january 2007 , the song was used by radio 1 dj chris moyles as part of an experiment to test out the new british singles chart rules that came into effect that month , which prompted the song to rise up the itunes music charts , and reached number 17 in the singles chart on 21 january 2007 and number 11 in the download chart . cd single 1 1 . `` honey to the bee '' ( radio edit ) – 3:59 1 . `` call me '' – 3:58 1 . `` what 's missing '' – 3:46 cd single 2 1 . `` honey to the bee '' ( radio edit ) – 3:59 1 . `` honey to the bee '' ( acoustic version ) – 4:24 1 . `` honey to the bee '' ( delakota mix ) – 5:00 1 . `` honey to the bee '' music video
