Inversnaid     inversnaid is a village on the east bank of loch lomond in scotland , near the north end of the loch . it has a pier and a hotel , and the west highland way passes through the village . a small passenger ferry runs from inversnaid to inveruglas on the opposite shore of the loch . to reach inversnaid by road involves a lengthy overland route from aberfoyle . it is also the title of a poem by gerard manley hopkins .
