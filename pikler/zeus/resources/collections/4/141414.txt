Aina (movie)    aina ( movie ) aina , ( ) also known as the mirror , is a 1974 pakistani movie . it was directed by nazr-ul-islam . singers are nayyara noor , mehnaz and alamgir . the movie starred shabnam and nadeem in the lead roles . it is the longest running pakistani movie on record . the movie influenced many indian directors such as dharmesh darshan 's raja hindustani , aziz mirza 's chalte chalte and deepak sareen 's aaina .
