Dunfermline Athletic F.C.    dunfermline athletic f.c . dunfermline athletic football club are a scottish football team based in dunfermline , fife , commonly known as just dunfermline . they play at east end park and are nicknamed `` the pars '' . they are currently coach ( sport ) managed by jim mcintyre who was given the job on a permanent basis on thursday 3 january 2008. he took over from stephen kenny in december of 2007 originally as caretaker boss . the 2008-09 season will be the club 's 2nd season in the second tier of scottish football , after being relegated from the scottish premier league in 2007 .
