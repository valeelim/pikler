Nonviolence     nonviolence is a philosophy that rejects the use of physical violence . people who practice nonviolence when expressing their beliefs may use methods like education , persuasion , civil disobedience , and conscientous objection to military service . a demonstration by a group of people in a public place , to show their support or to protest , is permitted on the condition that it remain nonviolent . sometimes the demonstrators are nonviolent but other people who oppose them will come to the demonstration and behave violently to show their opposition . some religious movements , such as the society of friends ( quakers ) and jainism , are known for the practice of nonviolence . there are people who adopt nonviolence in midlife , such as st. francis of assisi . people who eat a vegetarian or vegan diet , and those who support animal rights , usually oppose any violence by humans against animals . famous leaders who practiced nonviolence . - mahatma gandhi - martin luther king , jr .
