Telecommunication     telecommunication ( from two words , `` tele '' meaning 'from far distances ' and `` communication '' meaning to share information ) is the assisted transmission of signals over a distance for the purpose of communication . in earlier times , this may have involved the use of smoke signals , drums , semaphore , flags , or a mirror to flash sunlight . starting with the telegraph , telecommunication typically involves the use of electronic transmitters such as the telephone , television , radio , optical fiber and computer .
