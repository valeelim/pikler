Assist (ice hockey)    assist ( ice hockey ) in ice hockey , an assist is when up to two players of the scoring team shoots , passes or sends the puck towards the scoring teammate , or touched it in any other way which made the goal . this usually means that they `` assisted '' in the goal . at most , there can only be 2 assists for each goal . other websites . - nhl rulebook , rule # 57 - goals and assists
