Destroy All Humans!    destroy all humans ! destroy all humans ! is a video game developed by pandemic studios and published by thq . it was released for the xbox and playstation 2 game consoles . the game is set in 1950s u.s. and parodies the lifestyles , pop culture , and politics of this time period . the player controls cryptosporidium 137 , a member of the furon race of aliens , who has come to earth to harvest dna from humans to continue the cloning process of his species . destroy all humans has gameplay similar to the `` grand theft auto '' series .
