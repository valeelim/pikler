Q1 (building)    q1 ( building ) q1 ( which means queensland number 1 ) is a skyscraper on the gold coast . it is australia 's tallest skyscraper . it is also the world 's tallest residential tower ( including the spire ) . the building is 322.5 meters high ( 1,058 feet ) including the spire . however without the spire , it 's only 275 meters ( 902 feet ) . only the eureka tower in melbourne is taller without the spire with 300 meters .
