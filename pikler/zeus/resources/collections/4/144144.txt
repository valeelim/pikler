Hurricane Gilbert     hurricane gilbert was a very powerful tropical cyclone which caused much damage to the caribbean , mexico and parts of south texas in the 1988 atlantic hurricane season . before hurricane wilma in 2005 , hurricane gilbert was the strongest hurricane ever recorded in the atlantic in terms of barometric pressure . this hurricane was similar to hurricane beulah and hurricane allen , but hurricane gilbert was unique in many ways . gilbert killed 341 people and left $ 5.5 billion in damage . in addition to beulah and allen , hurricanes charlie ( 1951 ) , emily during 2005 and dean in 2007 had tracks similar to hurricane gilbert . emily and dean were category 5 hurricanes . because gilbert left a powerful impact , the name gilbert was retired . the name `` gordon '' was used in 1994 , 2000 , and 2006 .
