Bénonces     bénonces is a commune . it is found in the auvergne-rhône-alpes region in the ain department in the east of france . in 2009 , 278 people lived in the commune . the inhabitants are the `` bénonçards '' . the most important monuments in the commune are the `` chartreuse de portes '' , a 12th-century monastery listed as a `` monument historique '' , and the saint-pierre church .
