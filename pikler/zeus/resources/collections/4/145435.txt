Flag of Florida     the flag of florida is a white flag with the seal of florida centered on it . behind the seal , it has a red saltire , which resembles the spanish cross of burgundy flag used in florida since the early 1500s . the current flag was adopted in 1900 , however the state seal in the center of the flag was updated in 1985 to reflect the proper historical dress of a seminole maiden , in the previous seal she was dressed in buckskin with a feathered headband .
