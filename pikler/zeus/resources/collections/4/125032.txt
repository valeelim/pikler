British India Command     british india command was the name given to the general staff of the commander-in-chief ( shortened to c-in-c ) of british india . the commander-in-chief of india reported to the civilian governor-general of india . most of the c-in-c 's staff were based at the general headquarters india ( ghq india ) . during world war ii the c-in-c india was also responsible for ceylon and the burma campaign . during this time chinese and american units also came under the operational control of the india command . other websites . - operations in the far east from 17th october , 1940 to 27th december 1941
