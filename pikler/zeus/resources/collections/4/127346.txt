Major League Baseball All-Star Game     in major league baseball , the all-star game is the game between the best players from the american league ( al ) and the national league ( nl ) . the game is held in july of each season . beginning with the 2003 all star game , the winner of the game gets home field advantage in the world series . the national league has won the all-star game forty-two times . the american league has won it thirty-eight times . there have been two ties .
