Boddam, Aberdeenshire    boddam , aberdeenshire boddam is a coastal village in aberdeenshire , scotland . it is north of aberdeen and south of peterhead . sea cliffs rise to , south of the village : a coastal path leads along these to the bullers of buchan . the adjoining settlement , on the aberdeen to peterhead road , was for many years known simply as stirling : in 2004 , it was renamed stirling village , to avoid confusion with the newly granted city of stirling . other websites . - - boddam community website - - buchan ness lighthouse ( northern lighthouse board website ) - - official tourist web site - - peterhead area business directory
