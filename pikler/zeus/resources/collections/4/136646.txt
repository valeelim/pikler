Henry Lefroy     sir henry bruce lefroy ( 24 march 1854 â€“ 19 march 1930 ) was the 11th premier of western australia from 28 june 1917 to 17 april 1919. he was from the nationalist party . he became premier after the australian labor party split over the issue of conscription . labor politicians led by john scaddan agreed to join the nationalist party if lefory was the leader , not frank wilson . he found it diificult to keep control over a government formed by different political parties . when it was discovered that he had used his casting vote in a cabinet meeting to keep the leadership , he was forced to resign . he lost his seat in the 1921 elections and retired back to his farm .
