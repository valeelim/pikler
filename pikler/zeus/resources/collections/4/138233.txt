Santa Maria, Bulacan    santa maria , bulacan santa maria is a municipality ( town ) in the province of bulacan , philippines . it lies 32 kilometers northeast of manila and is located at the eastern portion of the province . it is surrounded by the towns of angat and pandi in the north ; by san jose del monte city in the south ; by norzagaray in the east ; and by marilao and bocaue in the west . according to the 2007 philippine national census ( a total count of the country 's population ) , it has 205,258 people . it is divided into 24 barangays ( villages ) . these barangays are : 1. bagbaguin 1. balasing 1. buenavista 1. bulac 1. camangyanan 1. catmon 1. caypombo 1. caysio 1. guyong 1. lalakhan 1. mag-asawang sapa 1. mahabang parang 1. manggahan 1. parada 1. poblacion ( santa maria town proper ) 1. pulong buhangin 1. san gabriel 1. san jose patag 1. san vicente 1. santa clara 1. santa cruz 1. silangan 1. tabing bakod ( santo tomas ) 1. tumana other websites . - santa maria , my kind of town - official website of the municipality of santa maria , bulacan
