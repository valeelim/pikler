Ole Gunnar Solskjær     ole gunnar solskjær ( born 26 february 1973 in kristiansund ) is a norwegian former football player . he played most of his career for manchester united . he also played for the norway national team . solskjær played for molde fk and clausenlengen before he went to england . solskjær joined manchester united in 1996. he played 366 games for the club and scored 126 goals . in 1999 , he scored four goals in twelve minutes against nottingham forest , `` the fastest scorer of a four-goal haul on record in england '' . with manchester united , solskjær won the premier league six times and the fa cup twice . he scored the winning goal in the 1999 champions league final . as of september 2009 , he is the coach of the manchester united reserve team . in 2011 , he got the job as head coach for molde fk , the club he left to join manchester united . 151||146||||||||||||||151||146 235||91||30||8||11||7||81||20||357||126 286||237||30||8||11||7||81||20||508||272 ! total||67||23
