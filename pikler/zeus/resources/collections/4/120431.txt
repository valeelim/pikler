Dhudial     dhudial is a village and union council , an administrative subdivision , of chakwal district in the punjab province of pakistan , it is part of chakwal tehsil . it is one of the largest villages of chakwal district and is located at 33°3'52n 72°58'24e about 70km south of the capital islamabad . the following are the majority tribe population . - awan ( اعوان ) - quresh ( قریش ) - rana ( انڈین مہاجر ) - ansar ( انصار ) - gondal ( گوندل ) - sadaat ( سادات ) - mughal kasar ( مغل کسر ) - minhas ( منہاس ) - bazigar ( بازی گر ) .
