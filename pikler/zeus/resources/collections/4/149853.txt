Ars-sur-Formans     ars-sur-formans is a commune . it is found in the auvergne-rhône-alpes region in the ain department in the east of france . in 2009 , 1,326 people lived in the commune . the inhabitants are the `` arsois '' . the most important monuments in the commune are the ars basilica , built in 1862 , and the `` presbytère du curé d'ars '' , located at the side of the basilica , and which contains a relic ( his heart ) of jean-marie vianney , `` curé '' of ars in the 19th century . the commune is twinned with freihalden in germany since 1977 .
