Faridpur District     faridpur is a district in central bangladesh . it is a part of the dhaka division . the area of this district is 2072.72 km² and is bordered by rajbari and manikganj districts on the north , gopalganj district on the south , dhaka , munshiganj and madaripur districts on the east . the soil of this region is fertile , although once upon a time the district consisted mainly of marshland . the main rivers are padma , old kumar , arial khan , gorai , chandana , bhubanshwar and lohartek . faridpur district consists of 9 upazilas , 4 municipalities , 79 union parishads , 36 wards , 92 mahallas and 1859 villages . the town consists of 9 wards and 35 mahallas . the area of the town is 20.23 km² . the population of the town is 99634 ; male 51.73 % , female 48.27 % . - rajbari thana - faridpur thana - gopalganj thana - madaripur thana - shariatpur thana the district is named after the sufi saint shah sheikh fariduddin , who preached there in the 12th and 13th centuries . in the early 1800s , hazi shariatullah started the farayzi movement against the british rulers from this district . the movement against the british use of indigo cultivation was also started from faridpur under the leadership of dudu miah .
