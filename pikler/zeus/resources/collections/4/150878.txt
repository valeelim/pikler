Amor asteroid     an amor asteroid is a group of asteroids found near earth that were named after 1221 amor . while some other kinds of asteroids cross over mars 's and earth 's orbits , nearly all of the amor asteroids do not . the best known amor asteroid is 433 eros . it was circled around and landed upon by the near shoemaker . about 1,200 of all the asteroids in space are amor asteroids , with about 200 of them numbered , or given a number . only 50 amor asteroids are named . the following are a few of the most notable amor asteroids , in reverse order of discovery : related pages . - list of named amor asteroids other websites . - list of amor minor planets
