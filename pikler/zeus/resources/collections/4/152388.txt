Bring Me the Horizon     bring me the horizon are a british metalcore band from sheffield , yorkshire , england . the band was formed in 2004 from members of other bands within their local area . they play a style of music that combines rock and metalcore , but they have taken on a more rock style with their latest album . their album in 2006 called `` count your blessings '' was a deathcore album and often people nickname that type of music `` screamo '' . the band 's name comes from a pirates of the caribbean quote . albums . - studio albums - `` count your blessings '' ( 2006 ) - `` suicide season '' ( 2008 ) - `` there is a hell , believe me i 've seen it . there is a heaven , let 's keep it a secret .
