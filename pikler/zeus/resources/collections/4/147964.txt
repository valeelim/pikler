British Rail Class 158     british rail class 158 `` express sprinter '' is a type of diesel multiple unit ( dmu ) train . they were built for british rail between 1989 and 1992 by brel at their derby works . they were built to replace old dmus and locomotive-hauled passenger trains . class 159 trains are almost the same as class 158s . they work many non-electrified services such as exeter st david 's to london waterloo .
