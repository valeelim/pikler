Béligneux     béligneux is a commune . it is found in the auvergne-rhône-alpes region in the ain department in the east of france . in 2010 , 2,905 people lived in the commune . the inhabitants are the `` bélignards '' . the most important monuments in the commune are a neo-gothic church from the 19th century and the saint-andré chapel from the 13th century . the commune is twinned with ostfildern in germany since 1978 .
