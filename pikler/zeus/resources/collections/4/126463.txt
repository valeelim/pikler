Duffy     amiee ann duffy ( born 23 june 1984 ) , usually known as just duffy , is a welsh singer-songwriter . her first album , `` rockferry '' , sold more than 6.5 million copies . `` rockferry '' , and a song from the album , `` mercy '' , both reached number one on the uk music charts . her second album did not sell very well , with only 200,000 sold in the uk . in 2009 she made an advertisement for diet coke . the ad caused problems because she is shown riding a bicycle at night without a helmet or lights . journalist luke lewis described the ad as `` ... blank , sterile and mildly unsettling .
