Badjcinus     badjcinus ( `` expert hunter dog '' ) was an animal that belonged to the extinct marsupial family thylacinidae . it was one of the most primitive members of its group , living 33 to 32 million years ago in the early oligocene . it was quite small , averaging 5.2 lbs ( 2.4 kg ) in weight . its diet probably consisted of small mammals , amphibians , reptiles and insects . although `` badjcinus '' was successful in its period , it probably was pushed out by other , better developed thylacinids . the fossils were found at riversleigh in north-west queensland , australia .
