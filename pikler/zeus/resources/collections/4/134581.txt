Quentin Bryce     quentin alice louise bryce ( born 23 december 1942 ) , ac , was the 25th governor-general of australia , from september 2008 to march 2014. she was the first woman to be the governor-general . before this she was the governor of queensland and she used to be a lawyer . for her community works she was created a dame of grace of the order of st john in 2003. if she were not already a dame , she would have become a dame of justice when she became prior of the australian priory of the order of st john .
