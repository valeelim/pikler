Real time (media)    real time ( media ) real time in a narrative is where the events in the story take the same amount of time to happen as they do for the audience to experience them . if a movie told in real time is two hours long , as in `` rope '' , then the plot of that movie covers two hours of fictional time . for example , in the tv series `` 24 '' , an hour long episode represents an hour of events on the program .
