Namal Lake     namal lake is in one corner of the namal valley in mianwali , punjab , pakistan . this lake was created when namal dam was constructed in 1913. namal dam is 32 km from mianwali city . the lake has a surface area of 5.5 kmÂ² . there are mountains on its western and southern sides . on the other two sides are agricultural areas . namal lake is an ideal abode for the migratory birds in winter season when thousands of water fowls , including russian ducks and siberian cranes , land in the lake water .
