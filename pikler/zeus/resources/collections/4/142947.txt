Ellon, Aberdeenshire    ellon , aberdeenshire ellon is a town in aberdeenshire , scotland , approximately 16 miles north of aberdeen , lying on the river ythan ( pronounced `` eye-than '' ) which has one of the few undeveloped river estuaries on the eastern coast of scotland . it is in the ancient region of formartine . famous people from ellon . - paul sturrock , footballer - teddy scott , footballer , coach and kit man for aberdeen - evelyn glennie , percussionist other websites . - ellon tourism group website - ellon aac website - ellon academy website - ellon webcam , local picture galleries and historic info
