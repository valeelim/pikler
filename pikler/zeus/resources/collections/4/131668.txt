Gregg Gillis     gregg gillis , better known by his stage name girl talk , is an american musian who remixes music . gillis , who lives in pittsburgh , pennsylvania , has released four lps on illegal art and eps on 333 and 12 apostles record labels . he began making music while studying biomedical engineering at case western reserve university . he specializes in mash-up style remixes , in which he uses often a dozen or more unauthorized samples from different songs to create a new song ; `` the new york times magazine '' has called his music `` a lawsuit waiting to happen .
