Ultamatix     ultamatix is a computer program to install other programs on ubuntu and debian-based distributions . since the development of automatix has ended , ultamatix was developed to provide user an easy way to install software on their ubuntu systems . many software/features can be installed with ultamatix , including programs such as the flash plugin , adobe reader , multimedia codecs ( including mp3 , windows media audio and video-dvd support ) , fonts , programming software ( compilers ) and games . related pages . - getdeb other websites . - ubuntu extras remix , a version of ubuntu that already includes ubuntu-restricted-extras
