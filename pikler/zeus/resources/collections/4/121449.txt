Vexilloid     a vexilloid has the same functions as a flag but it looks different . vexilloids are characteristic of traditional societies and ancient empires ( flags did not come into general use until medieval times. ) . a `` vexilloid '' consists of a staff held upright with a cloth banner that hangs down vertically on the staff and on top of the staff is an emblem , such as a carved animal . whitney smith , `` flags through the ages and across the world '' , isbn 0-07-059093-1
