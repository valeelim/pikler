Musikverein     the musikverein in vienna , austria is one of the most famous concert halls in the world . it was opened on 6 january 1870. the musikverein is where the world famous vienna philharmonic orchestra perform . the concert hall was built by the `` gesellschaft der musikfreunde '' ( society of friends of music ) who had been given the land by emperor franz joseph . in 1907 a beautiful organ was put in the konzerthaus by the austrian firm of organ builders rieger . the main auditorium is called the goldener saal ( the golden hall ) . there are seats for 1,744 people and standing room for another 300. every year on 1 january the vienna new year 's concert is held here . millions of people in countries all over the world watch this concert on new year 's day .
