Port Glasgow     port glasgow is a town in inverclyde , scotland , uk . newark castle stands very close to the shore of the clyde . the castle dates to around 1484. it is now a visitor attraction maintained by historic scotland . several acres of the clyde foreshore at parklea are owned by the national trust for scotland . coronation park is in the town centre . the park was opened on the site of the west harbour in 1937 to celebrate the coronation of king george vi .
