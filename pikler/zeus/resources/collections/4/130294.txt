Nintendo 64DD     the nintendo 64dd ( dd meaning disk drive ) was a magnetic disk add-on for the nintendo 64. it was considered too late in the fifth generation and flopped on its release in japan in 1999. when it came out , the dreamcast had already been released and soon for nintendo fans the gamecube was developed with a built-in cd drive . for these reasons , it flopped , and few games were ever released for it . super mario 64 2 ( sequel to nintendo 64 's 1996 super mario 64 was planned , but cancelled due to the commercial failure of the nintendo 64dd . in 2004 , a ninetndo ds remake called super mario 64 ds and was released the same day as the original ds .
