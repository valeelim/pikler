Yulia Barsukova     yulia vladimirovna barsukova ( russian ю́лия влади́мировна барсуко́ва , ) december 31 , 1979 , moscow , russia ) is a russian former rhythmic gymnast . she won the all-around gold medal at the sydney olympics . she left gymnastic to pursue a career in couple figure skating alongside her ex-husband and soon left the sport and gave birth to a child in 2008 .
