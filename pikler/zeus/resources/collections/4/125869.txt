Corsac fox     the corsac fox ( `` vulpes corsac '' ) , also known as the steppe fox , is a medium-sized asiatic fox species . it is spread throughout the central steppes of asia . it is very social and lives in `` corsac cities '' where animals have several dens with connected holes . it prefers some steppe and semi-desert in mountainous areas . it is mainly grey-reddish with silver celadon areas on their coat . the corsac fox is threatened by hunting for the fur trade .
