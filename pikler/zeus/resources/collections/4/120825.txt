Muridke Tehsil     muridke tehsil is an administrative subdivision ( tehsil ) of sheikhupura district , punjab , pakistan . the tehsil was formerly part of ferozewala tehsil until 2005 , when it was separated from ferozewal and became another of sheikhupura 's tehsils . it is headquartered at the city of muridke . besides various villages it includes the town of narang mandi . the 'ravi rayon ' factory complex is very famous industry in muridke . rice reasurch institute is popular place in kala shah kaku .
