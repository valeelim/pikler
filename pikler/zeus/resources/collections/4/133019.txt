Gorgias     gorgias ( , ca . 487-376 bc ) , greek sophist , presocratic philosopher and rhetorician , was a native of leontini in sicily . along with protagoras , he forms the first generation of sophists . several doxographers report that he was a pupil of empedocles , although he would only have been a few years younger . his theory was subjected of critique by plato in his work `` gorgias '' .
