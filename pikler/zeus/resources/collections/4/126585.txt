Eruca sativa     eruca sativa ( syn . `` e. vesicaria '' subsp . `` sativa '' ( miller ) thell. , `` brassica eruca '' l. ) , also known as arugula or rocket , is a plant that can be eaten . it is a leaf vegetable with dark green , spiky , dandelion-like leaves and a strong , spicy , peppery flavor . related pages . - brassicaceae - leaf vegetable
