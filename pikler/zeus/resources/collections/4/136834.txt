Aramid     aramids are polymers . the name stands for `` aromatic polyamide '' . structurally aramids are similar to proteins . stephanie kwolek developed aramids at dupont in 1965. dupont uses the trade name `` kevlar '' for them.. aramids are very present in security helmets , in splinter-resistant glass , in ballistic vests , but also in other domains . they are used as a replacement for asbestos as a building material , for gaskets and other sealings , but also as helpers to make sails , parachutes and similar tools .
