Ghillie suit     a ghillie suit is a special kind of military camouflage suit used by snipers . it is made of netting , with woven threads of burlap , yute , and other fibers in to help hide the sniper 's body outline . ghillie suits are heavy , usually weighing 3-10 pounds or more , and can become very hot . they also can catch on objects when used in the woods and make movement difficult . still , they are one of the best kinds of camouflage a sniper can use . with a properly made ghillie suit that matches the terrain around him , a sniper can be almost invisible , even to a soldier right next to him .
