Mycobacterium leprae     mycobacterium leprae is a bacteria . it causes the disease , leprosy , also known as hanson 's disease . the bacterium was discovered in 1873 by a norwegian doctor named gerhard armauer hansen . `` m. leprae '' is a gram-positive , aerobic rod surrounded by the characteristic waxy coating unique to mycobacteria . in size and shape , it closes resembles m. tuberculosis . due to its thick , waxy coating , stains with carbol-fuchsin are used , rather than with the traditional gram staining method .
