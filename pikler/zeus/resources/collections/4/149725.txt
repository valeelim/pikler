Klawock, Alaska    klawock , alaska klawock is a city in alaska . it is on the west coast of prince of wales island , on klawock inlet , across from klawock island . the population was 854 at the 2000 census . it is 90 km ( 56 mi ) from ketchikan , 11 km ( seven mi ) from craig , and 39 km ( 24 mi ) from hollis .
