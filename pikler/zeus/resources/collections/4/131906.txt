Brantridge Park     brantridge park is a former royal residence in balcombe , west sussex , england . it was owned by princess alice , and her husband alexander cambridge , the first earl of athlone . following the athlone 's departure to canada as viceroy and viceregal consort , it was occupied by princess beatrice , the youngest daughter of queen victoria . beatrice died there in 1945. it is now a hotel operated by timeshare .
