Gold Medal of Military Valor     the gold medal of military valor ( italian language : `` medaglia d'oro al valore militare '' ) is an italian medal established on 21 may 1793 by king victor amadeus iii of sardinia . during world war i , the medal was awarded to military personnel for exceptional valor in the face of the enemy . after the world war ii , the medal was gave to the cities that fought fascism and nazism during the war . other pages . - silver medal of military valor - bronze medal of military valor
