Montecito, California    montecito , california montecito is a census-designated place ( cdp ) in santa barbara county , california . as of the 2000 census , the cdp population was approximately 10,000 , although the boundaries are ill-defined . montecito is among the wealthiest communities in the united states and is home to many celebrities . it is east of , and directly adjacent to the city of santa barbara , occupying the eastern portion of the coastal plain south of the santa ynez mountains . portions of the town are built on the lower foothills of the range . notable roads spanning the length of montecito include mountain drive , sycamore canyon road , and east valley road .
