Futurepop     futurepop is a type of electronic dance music which uses influences from the genre synthpop ( like song structure and vocal style ) , uplifting trance ( grandiose and arpeggiated synthesizer melodies ) , and ebm . the name was created by ronan harris ( of vnv nation ) and stephan groth ( of apoptygma berzerk ) while trying to describe the style of music their bands produced .
