Thee Michelle Gun Elephant     thee michelle gun elephant ( or tmge ) was a japanese punk band . the band was started in 1991 when the members were students at a university near tokyo . they released their first big label album in 1995. tmge has released a total of 16 singles and 8 albums . in october 2003 , the band said they were breaking up . members . - yusuke chiba , singer - july 10 , 1968 - futoshi abe , guitarist - december 16 , 1966 - kouji ueno , bassist - march 27 , 1968 - kazuyuki kuhara , drummer - april 3 , 1969 recordings . singles . 1. world 's end ( 世界の終わり ) 1 feb. 1996 , coda-836 1. candy house ( キャンディ・ハウス ) 1 aug. 1996 , coca-13546 , coka-5 1. lily ( リリィ ) 19 oct. 1996 , coda-1042 , coka-6 1. culture ( カルチャー ) 1 may 1997 , coca-14100 , coka-7 1. get up lucy ( ゲット・アップ・ルーシー ) 1 aug. 1997 , coca-14327 , coja-9189 1. the birdmen ( バードメン ) 21 oct. 1997 , coda-1335 , coka-12 1. vibe on/anta no dorei no mama de ii ( vibe on ! /あんたのどれいのままでいい ) 1 jul . 1998 , terng-008 1. g.w.d 1 aug. 1998 , coda-1632 , coka-14 1. out blues ( アウト・ブルーズ ) 1 sep. 1998 , coda-1633 , coka-15
