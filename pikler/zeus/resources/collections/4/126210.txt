Convention People's Party    convention people 's party the convention people 's party ( cpp ) is a political party in ghana . it was started by kwame nkrumah in 1949. it was formed as a split from the united gold coast convention , which , before the forming of the cpp , nkrumah became general secretary of in 1947. nkrumah split from the ugcc because nkrumah did not agree with the ugcc 's beliefs and ways . the party continues to exist today . other websites . - information on the party
