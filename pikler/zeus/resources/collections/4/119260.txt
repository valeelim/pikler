Sai Baba of Shirdi     sai baba of shirdi ( d. october 15 , 1918 ) was a spiritual master who lived in india . his followers believed that he was a saint or satguru . some say he was an incarnation of lord krishna while others think of him as an incarnation of lord dattatreya . he lived the longest part of his life in a village in india called shirdi . although sai baba died almost 100 years ago , still today large numbers of people follow him or have great respect for him . he is very well respected in india , both by hindus and muslims . there are many temples built all over india to honor sai baba of shirdi .
