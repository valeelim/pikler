Zwentendorf power plant     the zwentendorf power plant is a nuclear power plant in zwentendorf an der donau in austria . it has never been used . this is because the people of austria voted against using it in 1978. today , the plant is used to get replacement parts to other plants of the same type ( mainly in germany ) , and for staff training . it is not possible to visit the power plant . because the nuclear power plant was never in use , a coal power plant was built in 1987 to replace it . the plant is now owned by austrian energy company and used as a solar power plant . related pages . - anti-nuclear movement
