Rutland Boughton     boughton studied with charles villiers stanford and walford davies . he wrote many different kinds of music including symphonies , concertos , part-songs , songs , chamber music and opera . his best known work was the opera `` the immortal hour '' . his carol `` bethlehem '' ( 1915 ) became very popular in england and in many other countries . he was a great admirer of the composer richard wagner who had built his own theatre , the bayreuth festspielhaus , in the german town of bayreuth . boughton wanted to create an `` english bayreuth '' at glastonbury , so he started the series of glastonbury festivals that took place from 1914 until 1926. from 1927 until his death in 1960 , he lived at kilcot near newent , gloucestershire . other websites . - the rutland boughton music trust
