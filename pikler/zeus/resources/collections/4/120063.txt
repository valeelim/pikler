Happy Days     happy days is an american tv show . it was originally aired on abc between january 15 , 1974 and june 12 , 1984. it stars ron howard , and has several spin-offs . it is set in milwaukee , wisconsin in the 1950s and 1960s . the term `` jumping the shark '' , meaning when a good show goes bad , usually because of one or more major mistakes or running out of ideas , originated in a two-part episode of `` happy days '' . the episode was about the fonz jumping a shark . this is thought to be the moment where the show started going downhill .
