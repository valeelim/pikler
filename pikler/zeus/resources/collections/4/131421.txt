Napalm Death     napalm death are a heavy metal band from meriden , west midlands , england . they play a very loud and angry kind of heavy metal called grindcore - a subgenre which they pioneered . the band made the shortest song in the world : 'you suffer ' , which is only 1.316 seconds long - characteristic of grindcore . their debut album , 'scum ' , was revolutionary in the metal underground and was made up of songs , which on average , were about 20-60 seconds long .
