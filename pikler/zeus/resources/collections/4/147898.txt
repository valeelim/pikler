La Silla Observatory     la silla observatory is an observatory in chile with eighteen telescopes that study the stars . the observatory is one of the biggest in the southern hemisphere . la silla is a 2,400 meter high mountain that is found in the far southern part of the atacama desert . it can be found about 160 kilometers north of la serena , 27 kilometers south of the las campanas observatory , and about 100 kilometers north of the cerro tololo observatory . related pages . - very large telescope other websites . - official eso - la silla webpage
