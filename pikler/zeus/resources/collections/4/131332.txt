Louder Than Love     louder than love is the second full-length album by the american grunge band soundgarden . it was released on september 5 , 1989 through a & m records . after touring in support of its first full-length studio album , `` ultramega ok '' , released in 1988 , the band signed with a & m and began work on its first album for a major label . the songs on the album featured a heavy rock sound with some songs featuring unusual or unorthodox time signatures . because of some of the song lyrics the band faced various retail and distribution problems upon the album 's release , including the addition of a parental advisory sticker on the album packaging . `` louder than love '' would become the band 's first album to chart on the `` billboard '' 200. the band supported the album with tours of north america and europe . it was the last soundgarden album to feature the band 's original bassist , hiro yamamoto . all songs written by chris cornell , except where noted : 1 . `` ugly truth '' – 5:26 1 . `` hands all over '' ( cornell , kim thayil ) – 6:00
