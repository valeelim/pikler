A Sound Garden     a sound garden is a public art work on noaa property adjacent to magnuson park in seattle , washington . it was created in 1982 by doug hollis . it is constructed with a number of metal tubes on weathervane-like apparatus that catch the wind and moan eerily . the seattle grunge band soundgarden took their name from this art work . since 9/11 , the facility , including the art installation , is on restricted access under threat level yellow . visitors may check in with the guards and walk onto the facility , but their vehicles are not allowed . photo identification is required in order to view the sound garden and access to the `` art walk '' .
