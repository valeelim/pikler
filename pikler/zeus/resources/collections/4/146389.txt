Kensington, Calgary    kensington , calgary kensington is a neighborhood in calgary , alberta . it is around the intersection of kensington road and 10th st. nw . the neighborhood hosts `` christmas in kensington village '' in december , the sun and salsa festival in july , as well as the `` bizarre bikes & bazaar '' in may . kensington is home to the calgary kookaburras australian rules football club .
