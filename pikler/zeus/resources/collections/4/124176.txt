Olm     the olm , or proteus ( `` proteus anguinus '' ) is an amphibian , native to the underground waters of the dinaric karst that flow through the soča river basin near trieste in italy , through to southern slovenia , southwestern croatia , and herzegovina . it is the only species in the genus `` proteus '' , the only european species of the family proteidae , and the only european cave-dwelling chordate . it is also called the human fish or humanfish , because of its human-like skin ( translated literally from slovenian : `` človeška ribica '' and croatian : `` čovječja ribica '' ) , cave salamander , or white salamander .
