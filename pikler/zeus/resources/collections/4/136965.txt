Hollister Co.     hollister co. is a clothing company owned by abercrombie & fitch . the kind of clothing they sell is `` casual '' , meaning that it is for informal use . it also has a theme based on the surfing cultural movement of southern california . the first hollister store opened in july 2000 in columbus , ohio . even though hollister opened in 2000 , many of their items say it was created in 1922. this is based on a story that is not real . on october 25 , 2008 hollister opened the first store outside the u.s. and canada in brent cross , london .
