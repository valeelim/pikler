British Rail Class 350     the british rail class 350 `` desiro '' is class of electrical multiple unit built by siemens ag from 2004- present . thirty of these units , known as class 350/1s , were built for use by central trains and silverlink on regional express services and central citylink services on the southern section of the west coast main line . a further 37 units known as class 350/2s are currently being delivered to the uk for london midland . the `` desiro '' family also includes units of classes 185 , 360 , 444 and 450. this train is nearly identical to the 450 , as it was originated from that .
