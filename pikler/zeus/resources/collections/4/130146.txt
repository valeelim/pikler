Cellobiose     cellobiose is a kind of sugar . this word is from biochemistry and cellobiose is part of the disaccharide group of sugars . this sugar was called `` cellose '' . cellobiose is made from cellulose , which comes from the cells of plants . things that have a lot of cellulose are good for making cellobiose , like cotton or paper . water and cellulose make cellobiose : this process is called hydrolysis . pure cellobiose is a white powder . people can not metabolize cellobiose .
