Galaga     galaga is a sci-fi arcade game released by namco in 1981. it is the sequel to `` galaxian '' . in this game , the player controls a spaceship and shoots enemies coming down in different directions . the game was published by chunsoft and developed by namco . more reading . - sellers , john ( 2001 ) . arcade fever : the fan 's guide to the golden age of video games . running press , 160 pages . isbn 0-7624-0937-1. other websites . - `` galaga '' at the killer list of videogames - `` galaga '' at the arcade history database
