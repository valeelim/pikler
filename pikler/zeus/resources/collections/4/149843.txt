Andert-et-Condon     andert-et-condon is a commune . it is found in the auvergne-rhône-alpes region in the ain department in the east of france . in 2009 , 336 people lived in the commune . the local economy is mostly based around agriculture and wine . the most important monument in the commune is the `` château d'andert '' , a 15th-century castle that is listed as a `` monument historique '' .
