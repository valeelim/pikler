Belleview, Florida    belleview , florida belleview is a city of florida in the united states . the city had a population of 3,478 people in the 2000 census . in 2009 , the city had a population about 4,500. it is part of the ocala metropolitan statistical area . the name belleview means beautiful view . the name of the city came from one of the first settlers whose daughter was named “ belle ” .
