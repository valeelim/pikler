Take This to Your Grave     take this to your grave is the second studio album by rock band fall out boy . the album was released 2003. credits are patrick stump , pete wentz , joe troham , and andy hurley . singles . - 2003 – `` dead on arrival '' - 2003 – `` grand theft autumn / where is your boy '' - 2004 – `` saturday '' - 2004 – `` tell that mick he just made my list of things to do today ''
