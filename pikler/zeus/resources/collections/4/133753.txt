Billabong (clothing)    billabong ( clothing ) billabong is an australian brand of surf clothing which is popular with young people , in particular the australian surf community , however , more recently of the skate , and other sporting communities . billabong started in the gold coast in 1973 when surfing culture in australia was popular . surfers gordon and rena merchant wanted to create board shorts that were strong enough to withstand the weather conditions of surfing . the name billabong comes from the wiradjuri word 'bilaban ' , which means a creek that runs during the rainy season . billabong is known for their brand mottos `` life 's better in boardshorts '' and `` a bikini kinda life '' .
