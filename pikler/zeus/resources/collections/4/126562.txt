Darren Middleton     darren middleton ( born 4 october 1971 in sydney , australia ) is the guitarist in the band `` powderfinger '' . he is also the lead singer for `` drag '' . middleton began playing guitar in high school where he enjoyed music by bands like twisted sister and ac/dc . he began playing in clubs around his hometown of brisbane with his first band , `` sonic tapestry '' . he formed his second band , `` pirate '' , with friends from high school . he met `` powderfinger '' at a `` pirate '' show and they asked him to play with them . middleton played on all of `` powderfinger 's '' recordings . while playing and recording with them , he was also writing and recording with `` drag '' . they have released an ep and studio album .
