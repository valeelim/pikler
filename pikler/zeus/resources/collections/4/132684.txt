The Little Red Book     the little red book ( official title quotations from chairman mao zedong ) is a book of the sayings of mao zedong . it was put out by the government of the people 's republic of china from april 1964 until about 1976. the book is a collection of quotations taken from mao zedong 's speeches and books . its popular title `` the little red book '' describes its size and appearance : it was specially designed for easy carrying .
