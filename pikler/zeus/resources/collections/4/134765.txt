Armenian Question     the term `` armenian question '' refers to powers of europe 's involvement to the armenian subjects of the ottoman empire beginning with the russo-turkish war of 1877-78. however , in specific terms , the armenian question refers to the protection and the freedoms of armenians from their neighboring communities . the `` armenian question '' , like the `` eastern question '' , remained a factor ( issue ) in international politics , first with great britain and germany ; then russia taking on the role of ottoman armenians protector .
