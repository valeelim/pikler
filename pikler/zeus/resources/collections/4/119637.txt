Mixu Paatelinen     mika-matti `` mixu '' paatelainen ( born february 3 , 1967 in helsinki ) is a retired finnish football player and is the current manager of hibernian . mixu is the oldest of three brothers , all of whom have played professional football . he scored 18 goals in 70 appearances for the finnish national team , which makes him finland 's all time twelfth most capped player and fifth top goalscorer .
