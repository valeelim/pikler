Alex Turner (musician)    alex turner ( musician ) alex turner ( born 6 january 1986 ) is a british musician . he is the lead singer and guitarist of the arctic monkeys . he is also a member of the last shadow puppets . he was born and raised in sheffield . his father was a music teacher and his mother was a german teacher . the arctic monkeys have released 5 studio albums . the first was `` whatever people say i am , that 's what i 'm not '' . the second was `` favourite worst nightmare '' . the third was `` humbug '' . the fourth was `` suck it and see '' , which was released late in 2011. the fifth was `` am '' , which was released in 2013 .
