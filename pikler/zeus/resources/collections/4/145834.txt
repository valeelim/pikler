Monroeville, Alabama    monroeville , alabama monroeville is a city in monroe county , alabama , united states . at the 2000 census , the population was 6,862. the city is the county seat of monroe county . author harper lee was born and raised in monroeville . in her book `` to kill a mockingbird '' , the fictional town of maycomb is modeled on her hometown . the book received the 1961 pulitzer prize for fiction .
