Reşiţa     reşiţa ( german : `` reschitz '' , hungarian : `` resicabánya '' , krashovani : решица or `` rešica '' , czech : `` rešice '' ) is a city in the western part of romania . it is the capital of caraş-severin county , in the banat region . about 84,000 people live in reşiţa as of the year 2004 .
