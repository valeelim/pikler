Swamp blues     swamp blues is a type of blues music . it is based on the louisiana blues , but has a more laid back and relaxed feel . swamp blues is often linked to j. d. `` jay '' miller 's , recording studio in crowley , louisiana . miller released lots of swamp blues recordings on his own labels , as well as on more well known labels . showing the impact of swamp blues on other music , the rolling stones played a version of slim harpo 's `` i 'm a king bee , '' and neil young recorded slim harpo 's `` rainin ' in my heart .
