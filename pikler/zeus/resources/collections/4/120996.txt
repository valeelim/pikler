Carbon-12     carbon-12 is an isotope of carbon . it is the most common isotope of carbon , as 98.89 % of all carbon is this type of carbon . it is one of two stable ( non-radioactive ) isotopes , it can be written c. carbon-12 is important in chemistry as it is the benchmark where all other atomic masses are worked out . its atomic mass is exactly 12. related pages . - avogadro constant
