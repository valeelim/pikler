Fort Gibson, Oklahoma    fort gibson , oklahoma fort gibson is a town in cherokee and muskogee counties in the u.s. state of oklahoma . the population was 4,054 at the 2000 census . it is the oldest town in oklahoma , it was established in 1824. it is the location of fort gibson national cemetery . it is near to the end of the cherokees ' trail of tears at tahlequah .
