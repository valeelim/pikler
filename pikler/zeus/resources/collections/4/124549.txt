Non-fiction     non-fiction is writing about facts . although non-fiction is supposed to be truthful , sometimes it may not be . people normally assume that authors mean to be truthful , but they might make mistakes . writing is normally split into two parts : non-fiction and fiction . as well as non-fiction books , there are also pictures and films . types of non-fiction . - autobiography - biography - diagram - dictionary - documentary - encyclopedia - essay - history - journal - journalism - letter - natural history - philosophy - photograph - textbook other websites . - nonfiction book awards
