Banglapedia     banglapedia , or the `` national encyclopedia of bangladesh '' , is the first bangladeshi encyclopedia . it is available in print , cd-rom format and online , in both bangla and english . the print version comprises has 500-page volumes . the first edition was published in january 2003 by the asiatic society of bangladesh , with a plan to update it every two years . `` banglapedia '' is not a general encyclopaedia but one that covers bangladesh-related topics . although bangladesh is a new country , there is a lot of history going back many , banglapedia uses the word bangladesh to include the territory of ancient eastern india , suba bangla , shahi bangalah , mughal suba bangla , bengal presidency , bengal province , east bengal , east pakistan , and the independent bangladesh .
