Oneonta, Alabama    oneonta , alabama oneonta is a city in blount county , alabama , united states . at the 2000 census the population was 5,576. the city is the county seat of blount county . oneonta is home to the covered bridge festival . oneonta became the county seat in 1889 when it was moved from blountsville . during world war ii , a small pow camp was outside of oneonta . the city government is made up a mayor and a five-person city council .
