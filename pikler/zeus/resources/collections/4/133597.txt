Greenock Morton F.C.    greenock morton f.c . greenock morton football club are a football team , who currently play in the irn-bru sponsored scottish football league first division . the club was founded as morton football club in 1874 , making it one of the oldest senior scottish clubs . greenock morton are popularly known as `` the pride of the clyde '' - a tradition which pre-dates the club 's renaming in 1994 to celebrate the links with its home town of greenock . the club has a traditional local rivalry with st mirren f.c . name . - 1874-1994 morton f.c . - 1994-present greenock morton f.c .
