Asiatic wildcat     the asiatic wildcat ( `` felis silvestris ornata '' ) is also known as the asian steppe wildcat or indian desert cat . it is a subspecies of the wildcat . it lives mainly in the thar desert , and the rann of kutch in india and pakistan . it is about the size of a domestic cat , with a pale yellowish body marked with black spots . other websites . - iucn/ssc cat specialist group : `` asiatic wildcat ''
