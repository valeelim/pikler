Marlborough House     marlborough house is a mansion in westminster , london , england . it was built for a royal favourite , sarah churchill , duchess of marlborough , in 1711. following her death , it passed to the hands of successive dukes of marlborough . in 1817 , it became crown property . it was used by members of the royal family , including queen adelaide . it served as a base for the predecessor of the royal college of art , the `` national art training school '' . in 1861 , marlborough house was made the prince of wales 's london residence . it was used by him for the next 40 years , until he became king in 1901. it continued as a royal residence , and was last used by queen mary until 1953. after her death , her granddaughter , queen elizabeth ii , gave the house to the commonwealth secretariat , which continues to use it today . other websites . - virtual tour
