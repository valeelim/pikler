K-9 and Company     k-9 and company was a 1981 television spin-off of the original series of `` doctor who '' . it featured sarah jane smith , played by elisabeth sladen , and k-9 , a robotic dog . both characters had been companions of the fourth doctor , but they had not appeared together before . a single episode , `` a girl 's best friend '' , was produced , but the series was not taken up .
