Eureka Tower     eureka tower is a 300 metre ( 984 foot ) tall skyscraper in melbourne , australia and is the tallest building in australia when measured to its roof . q1 , on the gold coast , queensland is the tallest building in australia because it has a spire that makes its total height 322.5 metres . eureka tower has 91 storeys and one floor underground . construction of the tower started in 2002 and opened on 11 october 2006. the top 10 floors of the building have windows coated in gold plating . the viewing deck on floor 88 is the tallest public observation deck in the southern hemisphere . it is 285 metres ( 935 feet ) above the ground and people have a large view of the city of melbourne . other websites . - official website - emporis page
