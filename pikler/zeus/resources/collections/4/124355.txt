Rape Me     `` rape me '' is a song by the american grunge band , nirvana . it was the second single from their last album , `` in utero '' . but there was much controversy about the song , so the single was a double-a-side single . the other song was `` all apologies . '' there was much controversy about the title of the song . although many people were shocked about it , kurt cobain , the writer of the song , said that the song is about a man who rapes a woman . he is sent to prison for it , and he is raped there . there was a music video that was planned to be made . it was going to be set in a women 's prison . however , nirvana needed a break from the controversy and did not make it .
