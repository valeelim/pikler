Mink     minks are small , short-legged members of the mustelid family . the mink is an aggressive mammal that is adapted to a semi-aquatic life . mink live in swamps , rivers , marshes , lakes and streams in north america , europe , and the far west of asia . mink have a life span of about 8 to 10 years . minks are carnivores ( meat-eaters ) . they eat small mammals ( like mice , rats and muskrats ) , frogs , crayfish , fish , insects , eggs , and ducks . owls , wolves , coyotes , and bobcats prey on the mink . the breeding season is april to may . it takes 45 to 52 days after mating for birth to take place . a mink litter normally has 6 to 10 cubs ( kittens ) .
