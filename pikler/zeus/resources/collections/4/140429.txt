INSEE     insee ( ) is the french national institute for statistics and economic studies . the insee was created in 1946. it replaced the national statistics service ( sns ) which was created under the `` vichy government '' during world war ii . insee codes ( or cog ) are given to communes in france . they consist of 8 digits ( numbers ) but there is a simple version with 5 digits . the first two digits ( 3 for overseas france ) are the number of the department . the other 2 ( or 3 ) numbers are the number of the commune . the insee code is not the same as the postcode .
