Boulevard of Broken Dreams (Green Day song)    boulevard of broken dreams ( green day song ) `` boulevard of broken dreams '' is a song by the american punk rock band green day , from their seventh studio album , `` american idiot '' . the song depicts a solemn walk of a lonely jesus of suburbia ( the protagonist of the album ) down a road where he walks alone . the song is considered by many to be the best song ever written by green day , and it has received many awards .
