Jelep La Pass     jelep la ( also spelled jelepla ) ( el . 4267 m./14,300 ft. ) is a high mountain pass between sikkim and tibet . etymologically the term jelep la is derived from jelep meaning lonely and la meaning pass in tibetan . the pass is in sikkim and the route connects lhasa to the republic of india . the pass is 46 metres ( 150 feet ) in length .
