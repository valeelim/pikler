Power consumption     in electrical engineering , power consumption often refers to the electrical energy over time supplied to operate an electrical appliance . the energy used by equipment is always more than the energy really needed . this is because no equipment is 100 % efficient . power is wasted as heat , vibrations and/or electromagnetic radiation . power consumption is usually measured in units of kilowatt hours ( kwh ) . more accurately , power is the rate of consumption of energy , measured in watts or horsepower .
