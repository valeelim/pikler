Saiful Muluk Lake     lake saiful muluk ( ) is a lake at the northern end of the kaghan valley ( 34°52′37.34″n , 73°41′37.71″e ) near naran . it is in the northeast of mansehra district of khyber pakhtunkhwa , pakistan . at an altitude of 3,224 m ( 10,578 feet ) above sea level it is one of the highest lakes in pakistan .
