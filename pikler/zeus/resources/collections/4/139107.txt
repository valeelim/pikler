Volvo V40     volvo v40 was a car produced by volvo from 1995 through 2004. it was an estate version of the first generation s40 . it was replaced by the v50 after volvo revamped the s40 and stopped making it . in 2012 volvo introduced a new car called `` volvo v40 '' . it is a totally new designed car and it shares nothing but it 's name with the older car .
