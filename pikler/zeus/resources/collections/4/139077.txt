Fraidy Cat     fraidy cat ( also known as scaredy cat ) is a slang term for a person who is very skittish or fearful . fraidy cat may mean : - `` fraidy cat '' ( movie ) , a 1942 tom and jerry cartoon short - `` fraidy cat '' ( `` garfield and friends '' ) , a 1988 episode of `` garfield and friends '' - `` fraidy cat '' ( tv series ) , a cartoon series - fraidy katt ( band ) , a canadian band - `` fraidy cat '' , a 2008 animated horror short movie from s4 studios
