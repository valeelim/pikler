Oklahoma City bombing     the oklahoma city bombing was a terrorist attack on april 19 , 1995 on the u.s. government in which the alfred p. murrah federal building in oklahoma city , oklahoma was bombed by timothy mcveigh . the bombing destroyed the building . the attack killed 168 people and left over 800 people injured . until the september 11 , 2001 attacks , it was the deadliest act of terrorism in the united states .
