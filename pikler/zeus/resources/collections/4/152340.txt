Samael     samael ( ) ( also sammael and samil ) is an archangel in talmudic and post-talmudic beliefs and stories , as well as christian tradition and demonology . there are also other religions that believe in stories of samael . samael is often thought to be both good and evil . it is said that he was the guardian angel of esau and a patron of the sinful empire of rome . samael is often confused with satan because he often has grim and destructive duties . he is often considered a fallen angel , like satan and the chief of the evil spirits . one of samael 's roles in jewish beliefs is that of the angel of death . in this belief , he is a fallen angel but remains one of the lord 's servants . as a good angel , samael supposedly lives in the seventh heaven , although he is said to be the chief angel of the fifth heaven . the website catholic.org says samael is a `` power '' , which means he is the chief of many warrior angels . powers are warrior angels against evil , who defend the world and people . they fight against evil spirits who attempt to bring chaos through human beings . the chief is said to be either samael or camael , both angels of darkness .
