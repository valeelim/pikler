Fragile Future     fragile future is the third album by hawthorne heights . it was released in 2008. it was their first album without casey calvert , because he died in late 2007. casey was the backing vocalist who used death growls in the songs . this album has no death growls or harsh vocals at all because the band decided not to replace casey with a new person who could do death growls . hawthorne heights did not have a lot of success on the singles , but it was seen as a new start for the band . while much of the album was inspired by things that had been happening to the band , it never says the words `` casey '' or `` victory records '' the album art includes a picture of casey calvert near a lamppost . track listing . 1 . `` the business of paper stars '' - 3:47 1 . `` rescue me '' - 3:08 1 . `` until the judgement day '' - 3:45 1 . `` somewhere in between '' - 3:22 1 . `` sugar in the engine '' - 4:29 1 . `` desperation '' - 3:28 1 . `` four become one '' - 3:55 1 . `` 321 '' - 3:40 1 . `` disaster '' - 3:01 1 . `` let go of everything you know '' - 3:25
