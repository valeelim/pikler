Kukmang     kukmang is one of the 51 union councils of abbottabad district in the khyber pakhtunkhwa province of pakistan . kukmang is located at 34°16'20n 73°25'30e and has a height of 1208 metres ( 3966 feet ) . it is in the northwest of the district , it borders boi to the north and pattan kalan to the east . the union council of kukmang is divided into the following areas : kothiala , kukmang and pattan khurd , as part of the local government reforms of 2000 pattan khurd union council was merged into kukmang . yasir hameed qureshi was the first player of pakistan national cricket team who participated in international cricket with two test centuries . his family migrated to peshawer in 1965 but has family members remaining in kukmang union council .
