Sambrial Tehsil     sambrial is a town and tehsil of sialkot district in the punjab province of pakistan . it is on the bank of upper chenab canal at 32°28'0n 74°20'60e and lies to the west of the district capital sialkot . it is home to sialkot international airport and sialkot dry port . sambrial became the fourth tehsil of sialkot , containing 160 villages with 17 union councils of pakistan and two police stations - sambrial and begowala .
