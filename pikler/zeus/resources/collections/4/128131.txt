Dannii Minogue     dannii minogue is an australian pop singer , actress , presenter and model . she was a judge on the british television programme `` the x factor '' from 2007 to 2010. she was born danielle jane minogue in melbourne on 20 october 1971 to a welsh immigrant mother and irish-australian father . her sister is singer kylie minogue . her most successful hit was `` who do you love now '' . other websites . - official website
