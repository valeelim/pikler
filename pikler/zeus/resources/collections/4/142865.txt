Iry-Hor     iry-hor or ro ( as read by petrie ) was a predynastic pharaoh of ancient egypt , although some archaeologists are doubtful of his existence . he was most likely ka 's immediate predecessor . he ruled over abydos and was buried in its local cemetery at umm el-qa'ab near ka , narmer and the first dynasty kings . if he existed , he would be the earliest documented person . other websites . - king iry-hor
