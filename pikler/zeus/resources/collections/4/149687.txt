Diomede, Alaska    diomede , alaska diomede is a city in alaska , united states . it is on little diomede island . the island is in the middle of the bering strait . it is near the international date line . there are no roads on the island . there also are no banks or restaurants . a general store has basic supplies . diomede is the only place people live on the island . mail is delivered to the island by helicopter . it is the only place in the united states that uses a helicopter for mail delivery .
