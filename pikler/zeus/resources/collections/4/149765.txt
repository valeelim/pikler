New Stuyahok, Alaska    new stuyahok , alaska new stuyahok is a city in alaska on the nushagak river . in 2000 there were 471 people living in new stuyahok . the name `` stuyahok '' means `` going down river place '' . in 2008 a new airport was built at a cost of $ 3,000,000 . the village of new stuyahok has been moved at least three times . it moved down the river from the first site in 1918. in 1942 this site was seen to be still too far up the river and being flooded too often , so the village moved to its current site . a school and a post office were built in 1961 .
