Ouahigouya     ouahigouya is a city in burkina faso . it is the fifth largest city in burkina faso . a census taken in 2006 stated that 70,957 people lived in the city ; 35,017 were male and 35,940 were female . in july 2010 , the us government warned americans that there was a real danger that they might be kidnapped by terrorists in the city .
