Kotzebue, Alaska    kotzebue , alaska kotzebue is a city in the u.s. state of alaska . kotzebue is the largest city in the northwest arctic borough . ralph wien memorial airport is the only airport in the borough . kotzebue is at the end of the baldwin peninsula in the kotzebue sound . it is north of the arctic circle on alaska 's western coast . kotzebue is a gateway to kobuk valley national park .
