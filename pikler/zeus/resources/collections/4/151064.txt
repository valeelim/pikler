X.Org Foundation     the x.org foundation is a group responsible for making x window system . this group was created on 22 january 2004. the x.org foundation is a corporation based in delaware . it has a non-profit status . the x.org server is the most common implementation of x. it is used on linux , unix , and unix-like systems . it is the base technology underlying both gnome and kde desktops and the cde desktop environment . related pages . - x.org server - freedesktop.org other websites . - x.org foundation website
