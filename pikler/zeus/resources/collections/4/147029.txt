British Rail Class 14     the british rail class 14 is a type of small diesel-hydraulic locomotive built in the mid-1960s . twenty-six of these 0-6-0 locomotives were ordered in january 1963 , to be built at british railways swindon works . the anticipated work for this class was yard shunting , trip work ( between local yards ) and short distance freight trains . the order was expanded to 56 in mid-1963 , before work had started on the first order .
