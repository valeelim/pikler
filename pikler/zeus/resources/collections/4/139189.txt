Imam Samudra     abdul aziz aka imam samudra aka qudama ( january 14 , 1970 , serang , banten - november 9 , 2008 , bali , indonesia ) was an indonesian criminal . he was sentenced to execution for his part in the 2002 bali bombings . the execution was expected to be made during early november 2008. it was done on november 9. samudra is one of three in a network of the bali bombers who were sentenced to death .
