Théophile Gautier     pierre jules théophile gautier ( august 30 , 1811 – october 23 , 1872 ) was a french poet , dramatist , novelist , journalist , and literary critic . he was a member of the club des hashischins , or hashish club . this group of french writers experimented with hashish to get ideas . gautier wrote the 1841 ballet story for `` giselle '' with jules-henri vernoy de saint-georges .
