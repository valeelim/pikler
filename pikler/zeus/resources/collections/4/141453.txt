Oriya language     odia ( ଓଡ଼ିଆ oṛiā ) , formerly known as oriya , is an indian dravidian language , mainly spoken in the indian state of odisha . some parts of odisha , mainly the ganjam and gajapati districts , speak the telugu language . the language is also an official language of india . there are about 33 million people who speak odia .
