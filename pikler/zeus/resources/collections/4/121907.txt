Graphic novel     a graphic novel is a type of comic book usually with a long storyline similar to those of novels . the term also encompasses comic short story anthologies , and in some cases bound collections of previously published comic book series ( more commonly referred to as trade paperbacks ) . graphic novels are often bound in a better format than the comic magazines , using the same materials and methods as printed books , and are generally sold in bookstores and specialty comic book shops rather than at newsstands . e.g . the graphic novel road to perdition , which became a screenplay and was filmed in 2002 , starring tom hanks and jude law .
