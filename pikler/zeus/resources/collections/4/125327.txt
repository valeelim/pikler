Okinotorishima     okinotorishima is one of the pacific ocean islands known as ogasawara , tokyo . japan claims the waters around the island as an exclusive economic zone { eez ) ; but this is disputed by china . the japanese government identifies okinotori as japan 's southernmost island . it is 1,700 kilometres south of central tokyo . okinotori is at a militarily strategic point . it is midway between taiwan and guam .
