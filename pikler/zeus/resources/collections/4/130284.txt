Commodore 64 Games System     commodore 64 games system ( often abbreviated c64gs ) was a cartridge based console made by commodore international . it was commodore 's first console and was released in december 1990. it was a commercial failure because commodore could not market the console properly due to its lack of games and the fact it was an 8-bit console in a 16-bit era . other websites . - `` the c64 console !
