Sara Gilbert     sara gilbert ( born sara rebecca abeles on january 29 , 1975 in santa monica , california ) is an american actress most famous for being darlene conner in `` roseanne '' . she has guest-starred on many primetime programs such as `` the simpsons '' and `` will & grace '' . she was in the cast of `` the big bang theory '' . she is a vegetarian . she is openly lesbian and was in a relationship with allison adler from 2002 until their relationship ended in 2011. they have a son born in 2004 , and a daughter born in 2007. she has been in a relationship with musician linda perry since 2011. the couple became engaged in april 2013 .
