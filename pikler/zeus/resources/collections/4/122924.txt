Parsix GNU/Linux     parsix gnu/linux is a linux distribution available as a live cd that can also be installed on a hard disk . it is based on debian `` testing '' and comes from kanotix . opposite to kanotix , its desktop environment is gnome . it is made in iran , and supports many languages . it includes the xfardic multi-language dictionary and also persian fonts . the parsix gnu/linux distribution was first announced on january 25 , 2005 by alan baghumian . other websites . - official site - interview with alan baghumian
