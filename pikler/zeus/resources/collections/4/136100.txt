Karachi Agreement     the karachi agreement was a cease-fire agreement signed by pakistan and india on the 27th of july 1949. it established a cease-fire line which could be monitored by observers from the united nations . the agreement was signed by the following : - major general w. j cawthorn , government of pakistan . - lieutenanat-general s.m shrinagesh , government of india . - hernando samper and m delvoie united nations commission for india and pakistan .
