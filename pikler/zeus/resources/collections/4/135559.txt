Mahmoud an-Nukrashi Pasha     mahmoud fahmi an-nukrashi pasha ( 1888 - 1948 ) ( arabic : محمود فهمي النقراشي باشا ) was an egyptian political figure . he was the prime minister of egypt from 1945 to 1946 and again from 1946 to 1948. he was assassinated by the muslim brotherhood while he was prime minister .
