Underwater     underwater is a term telling what is below the surface of a body of water such as an ocean , sea , lake , pond , or river . most of the planet earth is covered by water . the bottom of the ocean is called the sea-bed . most of the sea-bed is between below the surface of the ocean . just like the earth 's dry land , the sea-bed also has mountains , valleys and flat plains , although they are covered with water . the deepest part of all the world 's oceans is a place called the challenger deep , which is in the mariana trench . it is under the sea . although a number of human activities happen underwater , such as scuba diving for work or recreation , or even warfare with submarines , most of the oceans , especially the deepest parts , have not been explored yet .
