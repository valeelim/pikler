Perineum     the perineum is the area of the human body found between the lower end of the vagina and the anus ( in females ) ; and the scrotum and the anus ( in males ) . when a person reaches puberty , the size of the perineum is about one inch in length . during puberty , hair will grow around and on the perineum to protect it . inside the perineum-area are specialized muscles that perform functions of sexuality , erection and excretion .
