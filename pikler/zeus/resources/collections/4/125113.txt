Bangladesh Awami League     the bangladesh awami league ( bengali : বাংলাদেশ আওয়ামী লীগ ; also translated bangladesh people 's league ) is the mainstream secular political party in bangladesh . the party is now headed by sheikh hasina , the daughter of the late sheikh mujibur rahman . the awami league has been in government twice , about eight and a half years , since bangladesh 's independence in 1971 .
