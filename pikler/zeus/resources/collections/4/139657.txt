Anti-nuclear movement     many people who are anti-nuclear , are against the use of nuclear power for electricity generation , since they think nuclear power is dangerous . they consider the risk of a nuclear accident unacceptable and generally believe that radioactive waste can not be disposed of safely . many also see uranium mining and nuclear reprocessing as bad , because of the environmental risks of these activities . the 2011 japanese nuclear accidents have `` revived anti-nuclear passions worldwide , putting governments on the defensive and undermining the nuclear power industry 's recent renaissance '' . anti-nuclear groups believe that nuclear power is a risk to people and the environment . these include health risks and environmental damage from uranium mining , processing and transport , the risk of nuclear weapons proliferation , the unsolved problem of nuclear waste , and the possibility of further serious accidents . anti-nuclear critics see nuclear power as a dangerous , expensive way to boil water to generate electricity .
