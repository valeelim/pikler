Asiatic Cheetah     the asiatic cheetah acinonyx jubatus venaticus ) is a subspecies of the cheetah . it is also called the iranian cheetah because the only living asiatic cheetahs live in iran . even though it is extinct in india , it is sometimes called the indian cheetah . during british colonial times in india it was famous by the name of hunting-leopard . this name came about because some of these animals were kept captive by indian royalty and used to hunt wild antelopes . related pages . - cheetah
