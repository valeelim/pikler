Kemnay     kemnay ( gaelic : `` ceann a ' mhuigh '' ) is a village 16 miles west of aberdeen in scotland . it has a population of about 7040 ( 2007 ) . the golfer paul lawrie , who famously won the 1999 open championship is a former pupil of kemnay academy , as is aberdeen fc footballer darren mackie . - aberdeenshire cricket association other websites . - kemnay village community web - kemnay academy
