British Rail Class 07     the british rail class 07 diesel locomotive is an off-centre cab dock shunter . they were used in southampton docks , to replace sr usa class steam locomotives . it is a 0-6-0 diesel-electric shunter built by ruston & hornsby in 1962. they were originally numbered d2985-d2998 and later given the tops numbers 07001 ( d2985 ) -07013 ( d2997 ) , d2998 .
