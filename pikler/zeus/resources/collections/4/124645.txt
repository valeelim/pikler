The Feeling     the feeling are a five person band that come from sussex , england . the bands music is classed as pop . they have been nominated for many awards including a brit award in 2006. the first of their four top 10 singles was the song `` sewn '' in march 2006. it was one of 2006 's most played songs . it was first played on bbc radio 2. the feeling 's debut album was named `` twelve stops and home '' , which made it to number two in the uk album chart .
