Arbignieu     arbignieu is a former commune in the ain department in the auvergne-rhône-alpes region of eastern france . on 1 january 2016 , it was merged into the new commune arboys-en-bugey . 471 people lived in the commune . the inhabitants are the `` arbignolans '' . the local economy is mostly based around agriculture and wine . the most important monuments in the commune are the `` boule de gargantua '' , a block of sandstone moved there by the giant `` gargantua '' ( referring to the main character from the novel gargantua by françois rabelais ) , the `` peyzieu '' church and the ruins of the `` château de longecombe '' , a castle from the 12th century . famous people from arbignieu include césaire nivière , an agronomist from the 19th century .
