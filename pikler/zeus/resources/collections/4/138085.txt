Square (cipher)    square ( cipher ) in cryptography , square ( sometimes written s ) is a block cipher invented by joan daemen and vincent rijmen . the design was published in 1997. s is a predecessor to the rijndael algorithm , which has been adopted in 2001 as the advanced encryption standard by the u.s. government . square was introduced together with a new form of cryptanalysis discovered by lars knudsen , called the `` square attack '' . the structure of square is a substitution-permutation network with eight rounds , operating on 128-bit block size and using a 128-bit key size . square is not patented . other websites . - scan 's entry for square
