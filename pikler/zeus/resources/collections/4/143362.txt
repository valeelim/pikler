Ogg     ogg is a free , open standard format for music , video and other multimedia . it is run by the xiph.org foundation . the ogg format is unrestricted by software patents and is designed to provide for efficient internet streaming and for editing of high quality digital multimedia . recently a big library on the internet , the internet archive , announced that they are going to convert many videos and other multimedia to the ogg format as well as other formats like the proprietary adobe flash flv format .
