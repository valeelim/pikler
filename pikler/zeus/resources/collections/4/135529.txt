Mahmud I     mahmud i ( ) ( august 2 , 1696 – december 13 , 1754 ) was the sultan of the ottoman empire from 1730 to 1754. he was the son of mustafa ii ( 1695–1703 ) . his mother emetullah sultan , ( born as evemia ) was of greek ethnic descent . mahmud i was the older brother of osman iii ( 1754–57 ) .
