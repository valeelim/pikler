Attignat     attignat is a commune . it is found in the auvergne-rhône-alpes region in the ain department in the east of france . in 2009 , 2,850 people lived in the commune . the inhabitants are the `` attignatis '' . the local economy is mostly based around commerce . the most important monuments in the commune are the saint-loup d'attignat church , originally from the 12th century , and the ruins of the `` château de jalamonde '' , a feudal castle . famous people from attignat include antoine galléry , a french poet from the 17th century who was born in the commune .
