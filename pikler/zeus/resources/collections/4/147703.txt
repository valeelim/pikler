The Weather Makers     the weather makers : the history and future impact of climate change ( 2005 ) is a book by tim flannery . the book includes 36 short essays predicting the consequences of global warming . the book reviews the evidence about historical climate change and attempts to put our era into context . the book asserts that if atmospheric carbon dioxide levels continue to increase at current rates , the resulting climate change will cause mass species extinctions . the book received much critical acclaim , and has won a major literary prize .
