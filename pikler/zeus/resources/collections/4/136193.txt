Robert Gates     robert michael gates ( born september 25 , 1943 ) was the 22nd united states secretary of defense . he took office on december 18 , 2006 under george w. bush and barack obama 's administrations until his retirement on july 1 , 2011 prior to this , gates served for 26 years in the central intelligence agency and the united states national security council . under president george h. w. bush , gates served as director of central intelligence . he was also president of texas a & m . he is currently chancellor of the college of william and mary .
