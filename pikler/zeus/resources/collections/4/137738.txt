Mac OS X Leopard     mac os x v10.5 `` leopard '' is an operating system made by apple inc. for its macintosh line of computers . it is a version of mac os x , and carries the release number 10.5. it was released on october 27 , 2007 , and came in one release for both intel mac 's , and powerpc processors . according to apple , leopard contains over 300 changes and improvements since mac os x v10.4 `` tiger '' . leopard was the last release for the powerpc processor .
