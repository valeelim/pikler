Physical abuse     physical abuse is a form of abuse which causes feelings of pain , injury , or other physical suffering . physical abuse may lead to low self-esteem and other mental problems . physical abuse has many forms . a few examples of physical abuse are : - striking or hitting - punching - pushing or pulling - slapping - whipping - striking with an object - pinching - kicking - strangling - drowning - sleep deprivation - freezing - burning - electric shock - cutting - exposure to a dangerous animal - poisoning - withholding food or medication - assault - humiliation - neglect - biting
