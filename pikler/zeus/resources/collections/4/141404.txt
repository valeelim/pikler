Zinda Laash     zinda laash is a 1967 pakistani urdu feature movie , directed by khwaja sarfraz . its produced by abdul baqi , a lollywood movie starring by asad bukhari ( dr. aqil harker ) , habib ( aqil 's brother ) , deeba ( shabnam ) , rehan ( vampire ) , nasreen ( vampire bride ) . zinda laash is also known as `` dracula in pakistan '' ( usa title ) and `` the living corpse '' ( international title ) . it is the first movie in pakistan to be x-rated .
