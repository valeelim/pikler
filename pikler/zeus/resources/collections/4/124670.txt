Hyderabad, Sindh    hyderabad , sindh hyderabad ( , ; ) is in the sindh province of pakistan . the city was founded in 1768 by mian ghulam shah kalhoro on the ruins of a mauryan fishing village along the bank of the indus river known as neroon kot ( ) . hyderabad used to be the capital of sindh . now it is the capital of the district of hyderabad . before the starting of pakistan , it was known as the paris of india , for its roads used to be washed with perfumed rose-water every day and sandalwood incense would linger in the air . there are many prominent people who belong to hyderabad sindh . among them are dr. ghulam mustafa , filmstar muhammad ali , dr. amir liaqauat , speaker national assembly fahmida mirza , qazi asad abid , mustafa qureshi , and dr.syed mehboob , a writer and columnist , also born and educated in hyderabad .
