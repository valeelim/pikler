Metropolitan France     metropolitan france ( or `` la métropole '' ) is the part of france that is in europe . it can also be described as mainland france plus the island of corsica . by contrast , overseas france ( `` france d'outre-mer '' ) is the collective name for all of the french overseas departments , territories and collectivities and new caledonia . metropolitan france and overseas france together form the french republic . metropolitan france covers an area of 551,695 km² ( 213,011 sq . miles ) . excluding the territory of adélie land in antarctica , this is 81.8 % of the nation 's territory . as of january 1 , 2011 , 63,136,180 people lived in metropolitan france . this is 95.9 % of the nation 's population . the five overseas departments – martinique , guadeloupe , réunion , french guiana , and mayotte – have the same political status as the departments in metropolitan france . metropolitan france and these five overseas departments together are sometimes called `` france entière '' ( `` entire france '' ) by the french administration , especially by insee . this grouping does not include the french overseas collectivities and territories , which have more autonomy than the overseas departments .
