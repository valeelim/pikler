Iron Maiden (album)    iron maiden ( album ) iron maiden is a first album made by british heavy metal band iron maiden . it was released on 11th april 1980. the main musical type of the album is heavy metal but some songs , like `` sanctuary '' or `` running free '' are like punk rock music . some fans feel that a song from this album , `` phantom of the opera '' is the best song iron maiden have ever made . people who were members of iron maiden when the album was made : - steve harris - bass guitar - paul di'anno – singing - dennis stratton – electric guitar - dave murray - electric guitar - clive burr - percussion
