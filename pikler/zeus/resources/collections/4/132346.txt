Semi-automatic rifle     a semi-automatic rifle is a type of rifle that fires a single bullet each time the trigger is pulled , without the need manually to operate a bolt , lever , pump or other firing or loading mechanism . such a rifle can fire single rounds like this until the firearm 's bullets in the cartridges is gone . on the other hand , a `` fully '' automatic weapon ( a machine gun , for example ) keeps firing until the trigger is released , and can fire hundreds of shots for every trigger press .
