British Rail Class 37     the british rail class 37 is a diesel-electric locomotive . it is also known as the english electric type 3. the class was ordered as part of the british rail modernisation plan . the class 37 became a familiar sight on many parts of the british rail network . they were on inter-city services in east anglia and within scotland . they also performed well on secondary and inter-regional services for many years . the class 37 is known by railway enthusiasts as a `` tractor '' . this nickname came from the similarity of the sound of the locomotive .
