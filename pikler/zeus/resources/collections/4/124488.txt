Basket Case (song)    basket case ( song ) `` basket case '' is a song by the band , green day from its third album `` dookie '' . the song ended up being a huge hit , spending five weeks at the top of the modern rock tracks chart after it was released . the song was written by frontman billie joe armstrong about his struggle with anxiety ; before he was diagnosed with a panic disorder , and he thought he was going crazy . track listing . initial pressing . 1 . `` basket case '' - 3:01 1 . `` on the wagon '' ( non-lp track ) - 2:48 1 . `` tired of waiting for you '' ( non-lp track ) - 2:30 1 . `` 409 in your coffeemaker '' ( unmixed ) - 2:49 alternate pressing . 1 . `` basket case '' - 3:01 1 . `` longview '' ( live ) - 3:30 1 . `` burnout '' ( live ) - 2:11 1 . `` 2,000 light years away '' ( live ) - 2:49 - ( live tracks recorded march 11 , 1994 at jannus landing , st. petersburg , florida ) it should be noted that , while the song was extremely popular in the united states , even reaching hit number 26 on the `` billboard '' hot 100 airplay| '' billboard '' hot 100 airplay ] ] , the lack of a commercial single stopped the song from charting on the `` billboard '' hot 100| '' billboard '' hot 100 ] ] .
