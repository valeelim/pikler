Dromore West     dromore west ( ) is a village in county sligo , republic of ireland . about 300 people live there . it is on the n59 road about halfway between sligo and ballina . dromore west is on the dunneil river . it is between the ox mountains and the atlantic coast . there are four public houses . there are also two shops , a chemist , a chinese take away , a butchers , a post office and florist . other websites . - dromore west - west sligo website - dromore west website
