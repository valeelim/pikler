Bandi Atti Khan     bandi atai khan ( also known as banda atai khan ) is one of the 51 union councils of abbottabad district in the khyber pakhtunkhwa province of pakistan . bandi atai khan is in the south of the district at 34°02'60 '' n , 73°13'0 '' e. it has a height of 1035 metres ( 3398 feet ) .
