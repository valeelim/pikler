Wake Up Call (Maroon 5 song)    wake up call ( maroon 5 song ) `` wake up call '' is the second single off the second studio album by maroon 5 called `` it wo n't be soon before long '' . the song is also the 4th track on the album . the music video deals with the band committing crimes and levine acting as a murderer because of the murder of his girlfriends ex-boyfriend . a remix version of the song was released in 2007 by mark ronson . the remix song was originally released on itunes store on november 13th , 2007. the song later appeared in the hit remix album by maroon 5 called `` '' .
