Tiel     the town is sourrounded by the rivers waal and linge on the south and the north side . the amsterdam-rhine canal is on the east side . the city was founded in the 5th century . the whole municipality has about 41,500 inhabitants . the town 's mascot is a fruit man called `` flipje '' . flipje used to be the mascot for a local jam factory , but is now an icon for the whole of tiel .
