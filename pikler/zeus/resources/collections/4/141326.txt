Teri Yaad (movie)    teri yaad ( movie ) teri yaad is a pakistani urdu movie . it is the first movie to be released in the country after pakistan was created in the separation of india . the movie was released on 7 august 1948 , eid day . it was the first movie released after the creation of pakistan , but not the first to be produced by the people who lived in the land that became pakistan .
