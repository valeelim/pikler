Californication (album)    californication ( album ) californication is the seventh studio album by american alternative rock band red hot chili peppers . it was released on june 8 , 1999 on record label warner bros. records . the producer was rick rubin . on `` californication '' , john frusciante returned to red hot chili peppers . he had appeared on `` mother 's milk '' and `` blood sugar sex magik '' , but dave navarro was the guitarist on the band 's last album , `` one hot minute '' . the band 's sound was very different because of frusciante 's return .
