Heisman Trophy     the heisman memorial trophy award ( often known simply as the heisman trophy or the heisman ) , named after the former college football coach john heisman , is awarded annually by the downtown athletic club of new york city to the most outstanding player in collegiate football . other websites . - the heisman memorial trophy award website - stiffarmtrophy.com ( attempts to project the heisman trophy winner through `` exit polls '' of actual heisman voters ) - heismanpundit.com ( commentary and discussion ) - new home of the heisman - sports museum of america
