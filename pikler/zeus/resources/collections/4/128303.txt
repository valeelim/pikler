Tjako van Schie     tjako van schie ( coevorden , 17 april 1961 ) is a pianist and composer from the netherlands . at the amsterdam conservatory he is professor . even in china , van schie has played piano but he teaches in holland and portugal . van schie was educated in zwolle by pianist ben smits and a student of new york pianist jacob lateiner . he now lives in the dutch province overijssel .
