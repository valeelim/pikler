Menorca     menorca ( spain , catalan : `` menorca '' ) is the most east of the balearic islands . menorca is located east of mallorca . it has an area of . about 88,000 people live there . the main town is mahon , which has about 27,000 people . menorca was influenced by the minons . later it was ruled by the carthageans , after the punic wars by the romans . from the 5th century ad there was a seat of a bishop on menorca . then the vandals invaded it . after they left , the moors took over . later menorca was ruled by the kingdom of mallorca and then it became a part of spain . for a few decades menorca was ruled by the british , but the spanish recovered it again . menorca lives mainly on tourism . there is one airport near the main city . other websites . - menorca facebook page - pictures photographs of menorca - island government of minorca - unesco 's minorca biosphere reserve
