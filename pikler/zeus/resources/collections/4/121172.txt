Malir River     the malir river ( ) is in karachi , sindh , pakistan . it flows through the city of karachi from north east to the centre before flowing into the arabian sea . two smaller rivers flow into the malir , the thadho and the sukhan . the malir river is one of two rivers flowing through karachi , the other is the lyari river . in the rainy season the malir river gets a lot of water and millions of liters of water flows with it into the arabian sea . other pages . - list of rivers of pakistan
