Book of Daniel     the book of daniel ( דניאל ) , originally written in hebrew and aramaic , is a book in both the hebrew bible ( tanakh ) and the christian old testament . the book says that daniel is its writer in several places , such as 9:2 and 10:2. jesus agreed when he spoke about `` 'the abomination that causes desolation ' , spoken of through the prophet daniel '' . the book was probably completed around 200 bce.. this was shortly after cyrus captured babylon in 539 .
