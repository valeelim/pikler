FM Static     fm static is a pop punk band that makes albums with `` tooth & nail records '' . the band was started by thousand foot krutch ( another band ) lead man trevor mcnevan and thousand foot krutch drummer steve augustine . in 2003 , they made their first album called ( `` what are you waiting for ? '' ) . this album has sold over 60,000 copies so far and had the singles `` '' definitely maybe '' and `` crazy mary '' '' . on august 1 , 2006 , fm static started selling their second album called `` critically ashamed '' , which has the single `` waste of time '' on it . their third album was announced in late april 2008. the two piece finished recording `` dear diary '' in early august 2008. it was released on april 7 , 2009. fm static won the `` best website ever made '' award from the `` webgrammys '' , and they have been nominated for the `` gma canada '' `` covenant awards '' . members . - trevor mcnevan - vocals , guitars - steve augustine - drums old members . - justin smith ( bass ) - jeremy smith ( guitar ) - john bunner ( guitar ) albums . - what are you waiting for ?
