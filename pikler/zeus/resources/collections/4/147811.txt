Conspiracy theory     a conspiracy theory is a story that says that a group of people ( `` conspirators '' ) have agreed ( `` conspired '' ) to do illegal or evil things and hide them from the public . conspiracy theories usually have little or no evidence . many conspiracy theories say that certain historical events were actually caused by such conspirators . some conspiracy theories are about the : apollo moon landing , the 9/11/01 terrorist attacks , the assassination of jfk and the death of diana , princess of wales . other examples not relating to specific events are the illuminati , freemasonry-related conspiracy theories , and chemtrails .
