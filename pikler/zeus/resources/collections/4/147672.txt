Angular Recording Corporation     angular recording corporation is an independent record label that was first based in new cross , south east london . it was started in june 2003 by two ex-goldsmiths college students , joe daniel and joe margetts . artists . - crystal stilts - findo gask - gyratory system - je suis animal - tim key - klaxons - the lodger - the long blondes - mitten - navvy - these new puritans - the violets - david westlake - wetdog
