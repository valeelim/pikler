Kasur Tehsil     kasur ( / ) city and urban tehsil ( subdivison ) is the city of the famous punjabi sufi poet bulleh shah and the capital of kasur district . it is 55 km southeast of lahore . it is one of the oldest cities in pakistan . according to legend , it was founded thousands of years ago by kash , or kashava , brother of lav , who were the sons of the deity sri rama chandra , the hero of the epic ramayana .
