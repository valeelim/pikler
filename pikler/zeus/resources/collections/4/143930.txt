People's Alliance for Democracy    people 's alliance for democracy the people 's alliance for democracy ( pad ) is a political pressure group in thailand . it was originally a coalition of protestors against the government of thaksin shinawatra . it called for the resignation of shinawatra and two later prime ministers , samak sundaravej and somchai wongsawat . it is also called the national liberation alliance or the yellow shirts after the colour of the clothes they wear . it started from accusations of fraud against shinawatra 's business deals . in november 2008 , the pad seized donmuang and suvarnabhumi airports to force prime minister somchai wongsawa to resign . the airports had to close . in 2011 they camped outside government house for almost 200 days to protest about government policy . supporters wear yellow shirts with a hat that has a latin message `` liberate '' ( ) . they wear a blue scarf and they use tools that to make the sound like clapping hands ( ) .
