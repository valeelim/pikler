Pesto     pesto is a kind of sauce that originated in liguria , in italy . the uncooked sauce is made of basil which is crushed . other ingredients are garlic , salt , pine nuts olive oil and certain kinds of cheese ( most often parmesan cheese , pecorino sardo or pecorino romano ) . pesto is often used as a sauce for pasta . there is a similar dish in the provence , in france . it contains no nuts or cheese . it is called pistou .
