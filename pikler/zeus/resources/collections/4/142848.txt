Post-grunge     post-grunge is a music genre that is strongly influenced by grunge music , a genre that started in seattle , washington , united states in the mid-1980s . however , it makes it into a more melodic and radio-friendly sound . not by a coincidence , it has become very popular on the radio . examples of post-grunge artists . - foo fighters - matchbox twenty - nickelback - three days grace - daughtry
