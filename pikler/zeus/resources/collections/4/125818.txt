Duttaphrynus melanostictus     the common indian toad or common asiatic/asian toad or black-spined toad ( `` duttaphrynus bufo melanostictus '' ) is a species of toad that is common in south asia . the species grows to almost 20 cm long . the species breeds during the monsoons and the tadpoles are black . young toads may be seen in large numbers after the monsoons .
