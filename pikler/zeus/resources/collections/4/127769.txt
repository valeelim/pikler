Pantropical spotted dolphin     the pantropical spotted dolphin ( `` stenella attenuata '' ) is an oceanic dolphin . it is found in all the world 's temperate and tropical oceans . the species was beginning to come under threat due to the killing of millions of individuals in tuna purse seines ( fishing ) . the 1980s saw the rise of `` dolphin-friendly '' tuna capture methods in order to save millions of the species in the eastern pacific ocean .
