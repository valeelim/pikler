North Ayrshire     north ayrshire ( gaelic : `` siorrachd inbhir Ã ir a tuath '' , ) is one of 32 council areas of scotland . it borders onto the areas of inverclyde to the north , renfrewshire to the north east , and east ayrshire , and south ayrshire to the east and south respectively . the area was created in 1996 as a successor to the district of cunninghame which covered exactly the same boundaries . the mainland portion of the council area has historically been part of a greater county of ayr , however it also contains the islands of arran , great cumbrae and little cumbrae in the lower firth of clyde . all the islands were previously in the county of bute . towns and villages . mainland . - ardrossan - barrmill - benslie - bourtreehill - beith - chapeltoun - cunninghamhead - dalry - dreghorn - fairlie - fergushill - gateside , north ayrshire - girdle toll - hessilhead - irvine - kilbirnie - kilwinning - largs - perceton - portencross - saltcoats - seamill - skelmorlie - springside - stevenston - west kilbride
