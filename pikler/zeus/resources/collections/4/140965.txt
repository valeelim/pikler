Zoo Entertainment     zoo entertainment was an american record label , and a part of bmg . the label was created in the early 1990s by lou maglia , the former president of island records . it is now out of operation . maglia and former bmg executive kevin czinger ran zoo entertainment and its successor company volcano entertainment . when money ran out , volcano 's owners sold the company to zomba records . zomba later entered into a joint venture with bernstein & mensch , the rock management company . zoo is best known for releasing three multi-platinum tool albums .
