Bény     bény is a commune . it is found in the auvergne-rhône-alpes region in the ain department in the east of france . in 2009 , 734 people lived in the commune . the inhabitants are the `` bénéens '' . the local economy is mostly based around agriculture . the most important monument in the commune is the marmont manor house ( `` manoir '' ) , listed as a `` monument historique '' since 1992 .
