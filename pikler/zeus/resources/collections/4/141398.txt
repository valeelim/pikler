Badnaam (1966 movie)    badnaam ( 1966 movie ) badnaam , is a pakistani urdu language movie , the movie was released on september 2 , 1966. badnaam was based on saadat hasan manto 's short story `` jhumkay '' ( `` earrings '' ) . it was adapted for the screen by riaz shahid . the movie was directed and produced by iqbal shehzad , it is his debut movie as a director . `` badnaam '' starred allaudin , nabeela , neelo , ejaz , diljeet mirza , hamid wain and rangela .
