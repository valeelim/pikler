Nirvana (album)    nirvana ( album ) nirvana was a greatest hits album of songs by the popular american grunge band nirvana , who disbanded in 1994 because the lead singer , kurt cobain died . the album was released on october 29 , 2002. it was almost not released because of a lawsuit by cobain 's wife courtney love . this was because of a song on the album , `` you know you 're right '' , that love thought would be wasted on a box-set album . she thought it would work better on one cd . soon after the lawsuit was settled ( unsuccessfully ) the album was released . list of songs . 1 . `` you know you 're right '' 1 . `` about a girl '' 1 . `` been a son '' 1 . `` sliver '' 1 . `` smells like teen spirit '' 1 . `` come as you are '' 1 . `` lithium '' 1 . `` in bloom '' 1 . `` heart-shaped box '' 1 . `` pennyroyal tea '' 1 . `` rape me '' 1 . `` dumb '' 1 . `` all apologies '' ( live at `` mtv unplugged '' ) 1 . `` the man who sold the world '' ( live at `` mtv unplugged '' . david bowie cover ) 1 . `` where did you sleep last night '' ( live at `` mtv unplugged '' . only on vinyl and international versions ) 1 . `` something in the way '' ( live at `` mtv unplugged '' .
