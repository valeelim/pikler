Die Zeit     die zeit is a nationwide weekly newspaper in germany . `` die zeit '' ( the title means : `` the time '' ) is read by about 2 million people . it sells about 480.000 copies every week . it is well known for its good articles which are well-written and often detailed . it is read by well-educated people . it is a liberal newspaper . it includes articles about politics , economics , knowledge , the sciences , art , literature , education , life ( style ) and travel . it has many advertisements for jobs at universities . the first copies of `` die zeit '' were published on the 21 february 1946 in hamburg . since that day it has been published in hamburg every week . weblinks . - die zeit - `` die zeit '' wissen - `` zuender '' das netzmagazin der `` zeit '' - `` zeit '' -studienf√ºhrer - zeit campus online
