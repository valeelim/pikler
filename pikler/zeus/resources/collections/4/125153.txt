Dorothy Bush Koch     dorothy walker bush koch , often called `` doro , '' ( born august 18 , 1959 ) , is the daughter of the 41st president of the united states george h. w. bush and former first lady of the united states barbara bush . her siblings are george w. bush , the 43rd president , jeb bush , former governor of florida , neil bush , marvin bush , and the late robin bush , who died of leukemia in 1953 .
