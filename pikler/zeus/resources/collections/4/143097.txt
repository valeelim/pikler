Whitwell, Isle of Wight    whitwell , isle of wight whitwell is a small village with a population of about 500 on the isle of wight , an island off the south coast of england . it is close to the town of ventnor and the village of niton . the village includes a church , public house and garage . before to 2004 there was also a post office and village shop , but this has now closed . public transport is provided by southern vectis , the local bus company on route 6 .
