Mansehra Tehsil     mansehra tehsil ( ) is a subdivision of the mansehra district , hazara division , khyber pakhtunkhwa , pakistan . the main or central city of mansehra is also located in this area . it is a major stop for tourists on the karakoram highway which leads to china . it is also a major transit point to the northern areas and locations such as the kaghan valley , naran , shogran , lake saiful mulook and babusar top .
