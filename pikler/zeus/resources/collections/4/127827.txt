Ric Flair     richard morgan fliehr ( born february 25 , 1949 , in minneapolis , minnesota ) better known by his ring name , ric flair , is a semi-retired american professional wrestler that is currently signed to total nonstop action wrestling . he wrestled for 36 years . his last wwe match was against shawn michaels at wrestlemania xxiv . he is a two time wwe hall of famer . his nickname is `` '' the nature boy '' ric flair '' .
