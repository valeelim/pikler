Babusar Pass     babusar pass is above sea level . it connects gilgit with kaghan via chilas . prior to construction of karakoram highway , this route was very important , because this was the only route leading to gilgit and baltistan from pakistan . presently a road by the name of mansehra-naran-jalkhad-chilas mnjcroad is being constructed through babusar pass that will shorten the distance between mansehra and chilas by around . work on the route is expected to conclude sometime in mid 2011 .
