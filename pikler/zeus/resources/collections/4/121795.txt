George R. R. Martin     george raymond richard martin ( september 20 , 1948 ) , is an american writer of fantasy , horror , and science fiction . he is best known for his `` a song of ice and fire '' series . hbo made the series into a tv series called `` game of thrones '' . books written . - `` dying of the light '' ( 1977 ) - `` windhaven '' ( 1981 , with lisa tuttle ) - `` fevre dream '' ( 1982 ) - `` a song of ice and fire '' series : - `` a game of thrones '' ( 1996 ) - `` a clash of kings '' ( 1998 ) - `` a storm of swords '' ( 2000 ) - `` a feast for crows '' ( 2005 ) - `` a dance with dragons '' ( 2011 ) - `` the winds of winter '' ( not released ) - `` a dream of spring '' ( not released )
