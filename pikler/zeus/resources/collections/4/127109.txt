Lollapalooza     lollapalooza is a music festival . it was started in 1991 by perry farrell , lead singer of jane 's addiction , as a farewell concert for his band . it happened every year until 1997 , then took a break until 2003. there was no 2004 lollapalooza . in 2005 , the festival started again , and kept going every year . from 2005 , it has played as a weekend show at grant park in chicago . other websites . - lollapalooza website
