BMW 3 Series     bmw 3 series is a car produced by bmw since 1975. it is currently in its fifth generation . this is one of many bmw 's that is tuned by motorsport thus creating the m3 . the 3-series comes in many different body styles and engine sizes . 3-series can be found as sedans , coupes , wagons , and even convertibles . 3-series bmw 's are one of the more common german vehicles purchased over the last 2 decades .
