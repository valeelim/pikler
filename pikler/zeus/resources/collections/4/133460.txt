Dachigam National Park     dachigam national park is a national park in india . it is from srinagar , the capital city of jammu and kashmir . the park covers an area of . the name of the park means `` ten villages '' . the park has been a protected area since 1910. it was first created to ensure a supply of clean drinking water for the city of srinagar . it was declared a national park in 1981 .
