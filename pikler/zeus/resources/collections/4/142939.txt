Gwadar Bay     gwadar bay is near the maritime border of pakistan and iran . the name is from persian , `` khalij-e gavader '' or 'gulf of gwadar ' on arabian sea . it is an inlet of the arabian sea indenting the sandy makran coast at the iranâ€“pakistan border . it is about 20 miles ( 32 km ) long and 10 miles ( 16 km ) wide . the dashtiari river flows into it from the northwest , and the dasht river from the northeast . the town of gwadar , pakistan , is on the arabian sea coast about 30 miles ( 48 km ) east of gwadar bay .
