Walk of Life     walk of life is the second album by pop singer billie piper , released in 2000. piper 's second album did not match the successful start in terms of album sales despite producing three top 30 singles - `` day and night '' went to # 1 and `` something deep inside '' managed to get to # 4 and `` walk of life '' finishing at # 25 on the uk charts . despite good reviews , the album was not released in the us . piper then decided have a break from the music industry and concentrate on acting . track listing . 1 . `` day and night '' 1 . `` something deep inside '' 1 . `` walk of life '' 1 . `` safe with me '' 1 . `` bring it on '' 1 . `` ring my bell '' 1 . `` the tide is high '' 1 . `` run that by me '' 1 . `` promises '' 1 . `` because of you '' 1 . `` what game is this ?
