Lounge Act     `` lounge act '' is a song by the american grunge band nirvana . it was the 9th song on their second album , `` nevermind '' , which made the band very popular . the song is much like the post-grunge songs that were very popular starting in the mid-1990s , after nirvana broke up . the title `` lounge act '' came from when the band thought it sounded like a song they would play in a lounge . it is one of the songs in the soundtrack of the video game `` skate . `` , a skateboarding video game from 2007 for xbox 360 and playstation 3 .
