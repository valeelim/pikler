Yonne     yonne is a department in the centre of france , in the bourgogne-franche-comté region . the largest city in the department , and its prefecture ( capital ) , is auxerre . the department is named after the yonne river , a tributary of the seine , that flows through the department . the celts name of the river was `` ica-onna '' ( ) and from this name come the name of the river and of the people living in the department : `` icaunais '' . during the roman times , the main city in the territory of the present department was sens , the capital of one roman province . yonne is one of the original 83 departments created during the french revolution on 4 march 1790 with auxerre as its capital . it was formed from combined parts of the old provinces of `` burgundy '' , `` champagne '' and `` île-de-france '' . it was divided in seven districts : auxerre , sens , joigny , saint-fargeau , avallon , tonnerre and saint-florentin . in 1800 , with the creation of the `` arrondissements '' in france , the seven districts were changed into five `` arrondissements '' : auxerre , avallon , joigny , sens and tonnerre . on 10 september 1926 , the `` arrondissements '' of joigny and tonnerre were eliminated .
