Economic index     an economic index is a number that is calculated from different economic factors , like prices or income . these numbers are calculated to be able to compare different economies in different countries . an example for such an index would be the gross domestic product ( or gdp ) . usually , the base is given a value of 100 and all later data is expressed in relation to this base . for example , the consumer price index ( cpi ) of a year might stand at 95 ( a fall of 5 percent in the prices ) or 105 ( an increase of 5 percent in the prices ) . indexes ( indices ) also measure up and down movement of industrial production , and of the market prices of bonds , commodities , shares , etc .
