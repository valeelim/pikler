Detritivore     a detritivore ( also known as decomposers ) is an animal that feeds on dead organisms ( living things ) and breaks them down into smaller molecules . detritivores are essential in the ecosystem as they return important materials such as carbon ( see carbon cycle ) back into the environment . examples of common detritivores include earthworms and woodlice , but also includes microscopic ( things that can only be seen using a microscope ) bacteria .
