Tornado Alley     tornado alley is a colloquial term most often used to refer to the area of the united states in which tornadoes are most frequent . the 1974 super tornado outbreak with 148 tornados , took place in georgia , illinois , indiana , kentucky , michigan , mississippi , north carolina , ohio , south carolina , tennessee , virginia , and west virginia . schools in tornado alley often have tornado drills , and many homes may have tornado shelters where people can go for safety .
