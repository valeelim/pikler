Konstantin Tsiolkovsky     konstantin eduardovich tsiolkovsky 5 september 1857 – 19 september 1935 ) was a russian who pioneered spaceflight . when konstantin was young , he had scarlet fever ; because of that he had some problems with hearing and had to study by himself . he became a schoolteacher and , in his spare time , studied and wrote about spaceflight . in 1903 , he wrote `` means of reaction devices '' ( in russian : `` исследование мировых пространств реактивными приборами '' ) , which was the first serious study on how to use rockets to launch spacecraft . he calculated the speed needed to reach orbit around the earth ( at 8 km/s ) . he also wrote that a multi-stage rocket , using liquid fuel could reach that speed . he recommended liquid hydrogen and liquid oxygen , though other fuels could be used . he was correct on these points .
