Cottontail rabbit     cottontail rabbits live in america . they are any of several north , central and south american rabbit species of the genus `` sylvilagus '' . most cottontail rabbits of the genus have a short tail with a white underside that shows when they are running away . this gives them their name , `` cottontails . '' however , this feature is not present in all cottontails . some can be different . the cottontail rabbit lives most across north america , central america , and south america . most species live in nests called `` forms '' . cottontail rabbits do not get the disease myxomatosis as easily as european rabbits . species . - genus `` sylvilagus '' - subgenus `` tapeti '' - swamp rabbit , `` sylvilagus aquaticus '' - tapeti , `` sylvilagus brasiliensis '' - dice 's cottontail , `` sylvilagus dicei '' - omilteme cottontail , `` sylvilagus insonus '' - marsh rabbit , `` sylvilagus palustris '' - lower keys marsh rabbit , `` sylvilagus palustris hefneri '' ( named after hugh hefner ) - upper keys marsh rabbit , `` sylvilagus palustris paludicola ''
