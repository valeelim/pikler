Ka (pharaoh)    ka ( pharaoh ) ka , also sekhem ka , was a predynastic pharaoh of upper egypt . he ruled over abydos in the late 32nd or early 31st century bc and was buried at umm el-qa'ab . he most likely was the immediate successor to iry-hor and was succeeded by narmer . he is the earliest known egyptian king with a `` serekh , '' inscribed on a number of artefacts .
