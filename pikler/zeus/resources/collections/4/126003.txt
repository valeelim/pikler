Colin Delaney     colin matthew delaney ( september 7 , 1986 ) is an american professional wrestler best known for working with world wrestling entertainment ( wwe ) on its ecw brand . delaney made his world wrestling entertainment debut as a jobber , seen wrestling on the ecw show . on august 15 , delaney was released by wwe . colin has an older brother ed who competes as `` hangin ' tough '' freddie midnight , and younger brother brian who competed as mr. bodacious . other websites . - official myspace - online world of wrestling profile
