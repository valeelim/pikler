M postcode area     the m postcode area , also known as the manchester postcode area , is a group of lots of postal districts in greater manchester , england . the code covers three places : manchester , salford , and sale . it covers parts of all greater manchester boroughs except for stockport . all of manchester is covered by the code , except for a small village called ringway , which is in the wa postcode area . the postcode also includes nearly all of salford and most of the borough of trafford . the code covers these districts : other websites . - map of postcodes of greater manchester
