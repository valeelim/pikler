Marshmallow     the marshmallow is a candy that is usually made of sugar or corn syrup , water , gelatin that has been pre-softened in hot water , dextrose , and flavorings , whipped to a spongy consistency . marshmallows are used in several types of candies , on foods , and on some drinks such as hot chocolate . coats can feel like marshmallows because they can have a fluffy inside and feel really warm , just like smores .
