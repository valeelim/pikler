Australian Classification Board     the australian classification board is a government agency in australia that controls censorship and classifications . it rates video games , movies , and publications . it was originally created in 1970 as the office of film and literature classification ( oflc ) . the name was changed in 2006. for most media , there are the following ratings : - g : general - pg : parental guidance - m : mature - ma15+ : mature audiences - r18+ : restricted - x18+ : restricted - rc : refused classification refused classification means that the classification board has banned the item from being released in australia . any movie or video game under this rating can not be shown in australia . rc would be the strongest rating by the australian classification board .
