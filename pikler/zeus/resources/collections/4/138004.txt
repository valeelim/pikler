Amazon Basin     the amazon basin is the part of south america drained by the amazon river and its branches . it is low-lying in relation to sea level . the river has its origins high up in the andes of peru and ecuador . most of this drainage basin ( 54 % ) is in brazil , including the lower part . the river also has tributaries from colombia , venezuela and bolivia . the amazon rainforest is the largest in the world , covering about 8,235,430 km with dense tropical forest . the amazon basin is one of the world 's most important ecosystems .
