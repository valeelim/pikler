Portsoy     portsoy ( gaelic : `` port saoidh '' ) is a burgh in aberdeenshire , scotland , formerly in the county of banffshire . it is known for : - its local jewellery made from `` portsoy marble '' ( which is not marble , but rather serpentine ) - its locally milled oatmeal which is sold around the world - its annual small boat festival . - its two historical harbours
