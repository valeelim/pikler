Girls Just Want to Have Fun     `` girls just wan na have fun '' is a song by cyndi lauper . it was the first single by lauper . the single reached number one in over ten countries . it received a grammy award nomination for record of the year and best female pop vocal performance . cap'n lou albano was in the music video for the song . the song was covered by miley cyrus . the miley cyrus version is off her album `` breakout '' . it has been covered on either an album or in live concert by over 30 other artists .
