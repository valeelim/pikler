Middle Persian     middle persian is the iranian dialect of southwestern khuzestan , lorestan , chaharmahal and bakhtiari provinces in iran . during the sassanid times ( 224-654 ce ) it became a prestige dialect . it came to be spoken in other regions as well . middle persian is classified as western iranian language . it comes from old persian and is the ancestor of modern persian . the native name for middle persian ( and perhaps for old persian also ) was `` pÄrsik '' . the word is the origin of the native name for the modern persian language .
