Evangelical Church in Germany     the protestant evangelical church in germany ( german : evangelische kirche in deutschland ) is a series of 20 regional churches . the 20 are made up of two reformed ( calvinist ) , 9 lutheran and 9 united ( lutheran-reformed ) churches . the moravian church and the federation of evangelical reformed congregations are associate members . the evangelical church in germany is not a church like the church of england or the roman catholic church . this is because it is not one big church which share the same system of beliefs , instead it is a collection of churches which allow its members to share in church services and worship . other websites . - evangelical church in germany ( `` evangelische kirche in deutschland '' )
