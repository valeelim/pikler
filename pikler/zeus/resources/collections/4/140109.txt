Garfield Sobers     sir garfield st aubrun sobers ( born 28 july 1936 in bridgetown , barbados ) is a former west indian cricketer . he is often known as garry . sobers was a very good batsman and highly effective bowler . he was able to bowl both fast and as a spinner . sobers is widely regarded as the greatest all rounder of all time and , with sir donald bradman , as one of the greatest cricketers of any type .
