Ghunghat (movie)    ghunghat ( movie ) ghoongat ( also spelled ghunghat ) ( ) is an urdu movie released in the summer of 1996. it starred mohsin khan , andaleeb , shaan , resham and arbaaz khan . the movie marked shaan 's comeback into the lahore industry and won the actor many plaudits for his performance as the villain . ghoongat was directed by syed noor and produced by shezad rafique .
