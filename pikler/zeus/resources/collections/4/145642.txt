Lincoln City F.C.    lincoln city f.c . lincoln city f.c . is an english football team currently playing in football league two ( the fourth tier of the english football league system ) . the team play at the 10,127 capacity sincil bank in lincoln , lincolnshire , and are nicknamed the imps after the legend of the lincoln imp . more recently , they have also been known as the red imps . traditionally they play in red and white striped shirts with black shorts and red and white socks .
