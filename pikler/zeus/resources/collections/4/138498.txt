St. Anger     st. anger is the 8th studio album by american thrash metal band metallica . it was released on june 5 , 2003. it was their last album released by elektra records . four singles were released from the album . in 2004 , the album 's lead single , `` st. anger '' , won a grammy award for `` best metal performance '' . a lot of people did not like the style of this album and the way the drums sounded . it also has a free dvd that has the band playing the entire album in a studio . track listing . 1. frantic . 1. st. anger 1. some kind of monster 1. dirty window 1. invisible kid 1. my world 1. shoot me again 1. sweet amber 1. the unnamed feeling 1. purify 1. all within my hands
