WrestleMania 2     wrestlemania 2 was the second annual wrestlemania professional wrestling pay-per-view event produced by the world wrestling federation ( although the first wrestlemania was only on pay-per-view in select areas ) . it took place on april 7 , 1986 at three venues : the nassau veterans memorial coliseum in uniondale , new york , the rosemont horizon in rosemont , illinois , and the los angeles memorial sports arena in los angeles , california .
