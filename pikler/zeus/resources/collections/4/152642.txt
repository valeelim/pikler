Miami Beach, Florida    miami beach , florida miami beach is a city of florida in the united states . miami beach is as its name tells , a resort city with many beaches , hotels and restaurants . many celebrities maintain homes in the city , especially in the south beach area . they go to night clubs at night and enjoy the great weather during the day . the city is also home to many hispanics who come through neighboring miami on their way to america . also , a very large part of the population is jewish and many of them are observant . they have built up their own infrastructure with schools , shuls and kosher restaurants . this has made the city draw many observant tourists .
