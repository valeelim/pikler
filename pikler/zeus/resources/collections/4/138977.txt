Belfast Quarters     the central area of belfast has been divided into seven quarters related to its history : - cathedral quarter - gaeltacht quarter - smithfield market and library quarter - linen quarter - market quarter - queen 's quarter - titanic quarter the quarters do not evenly divide the city into four parts , and not all of belfast 's city centre is part of a quarter . instead , each quarter has something important in its history and locale . the `` quarter '' names were given in 2001. other pages . - discover the quarters , official belfast tourism site
