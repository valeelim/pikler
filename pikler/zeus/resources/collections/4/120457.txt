Biblical Mount Sinai     the biblical mount sinai is a mountain that was written about in the old testament . it was written by moses that the ten commandments were given to him by god on mount sinai . the name horeb is sometimes used to describe mount sinai . tradition places it at jebel musa ( mount moses ) in the south of the sinai peninsula , but some see it as being further north at the wadi sudr . related pages . - exodus - deuteronomy other websites . - where is mount sinai - the lost mountain , 1956-12-03 , time
