Hapoel asi gilboa     hapoel asi gilboa is an israeli football club based in prazon on mount gilboa . the club currently plays in the northern division of liga alef . their main rivals are hapoel afula . in 2001 the club won the liga bet north b division and was promoted to liga alef after 18 years in liga bet . honours . - liga bet - north b champions 2000-01 other websites . - hapoel asi gilboa israel football association
