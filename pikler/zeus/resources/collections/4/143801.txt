Tuscumbia, Alabama    tuscumbia , alabama tuscumbia is a city in and the county seat of colbert county , alabama , united states . at the 2000 census , the population was 7,856 and is included in the shoals msa . tuscumbia is the hometown of helen keller and the location of the alabama music hall of fame . notable residents . - helen keller born in tuscumbia , june 27 , 1880. died june 1 , 1968 . - mike cooley , guitarist for the alt-country/rock band , drive-by truckers . born in tuscumbia on september 14 , 1966 . - heinie manush , born in tuscumbia , member of the baseball hall of fame - home of howell thomas heflin , former u.s. senator from alabama ( 1979-97 ) , former alabama supreme court chief justice ( 1971-77 ) . died march 25 , 2005 . - birthplace of mitch mcconnell , u.s. senator from kentucky and the current senate minority leader . born february 20 , 1942 .
