One Flew Over the Cuckoo's Nest (novel)    one flew over the cuckoo 's nest ( novel ) one flew over the cuckoo 's nest is a novel . it was written in 1959 by ken kesey . it was published in 1962. it is set in a mental asylum in oregon . it was made into a movie that was directed by milos forman . the movie stars jack nicholson . it was very popular , and won 5 academy awards . it was a very controversial book . it is still considered a classic .
