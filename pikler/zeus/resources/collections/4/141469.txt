Jo Darr Gya Woh Marr Gya     jo darr gya woh marr gya ( ) is a pakistani urdu movie which starred neeli , javed sheikh , atiqa odho , reema and nadeem . the movie was released in the summer of 1995 and earned major revenue for its production studio . it was directed by iqbal kashmiri and was produced by evernew studios . the english title is `` scared '' .
