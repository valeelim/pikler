Lamiaceae     lamiaceae or labiatae , also known as the mint family , is a family of plants . it includes about 210 genera and about 3,500 species . it has been considered closely related to verbenaceae but 1990s phylogenetic studies have shown that a lot of the genera classified in verbenaceae belong instead in lamiaceae , whereas the core genera of verbenaceae are not closely related to lamiaceae and are more closely related to other members of the lamiales .
