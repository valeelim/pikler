Béon, Ain    béon , ain béon is a commune . it is found in the auvergne-rhône-alpes region in the ain department in the east of france . in 2009 , 352 people lived in the commune . the inhabitants are the `` béonais '' . the most important monuments in the commune are the `` château de béon '' , a 19th-century castle , and the ruins of the `` château de luyrieu '' , a feudal castle . the commune is twinned with béon in the yonne département .
