If Only You Were Lonely     if only you were lonely is the second album released by hawthorne heights . many listeners consider the album more on the dark side , than the first hawthorne heights album . the album includes a few songs where death growls are absent , and others where it is only used in a small part of the song , to make it fit better . previously , they would use these harsh vocals a lot more . it had three singles : `` saying sorry '' , `` this is who we are '' and `` pens and needles '' .
