Ciphertext     in cryptography , ciphertext ( or cyphertext ) refers to the output of the encryption process . plaintext is turned into ciphertext using an algorithm ( cipher ) . this makes it unreadable to anyone except those who own unique information about it , usually referred to as a key . decryption is the reverse process of encryption . it takes the unreadable ciphertext as an input and makes it readable again in the form of plaintext .
