Langra, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa    langra , khyber pakhtunkhwa langra is one of the 51 union councils of abbottabad district in the khyber pakhtunkhwa province of pakistan . langra is at 34°4'0n 73°10'0e at a height of 871 metres ( 2860 feet ) . it is to the south of abbottabad city , neighbouring union councils are , salhad - which is to the north and forms the approach to abbottabad city , rajoya to the east , havelian to the south and kokal to the west ( which forms the approach to haripur district ) . the union council is subdivided into the following areas : banda sahib khan , lari and langra .
