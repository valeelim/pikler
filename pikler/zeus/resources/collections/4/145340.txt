Underwater diving     different types of underwater diving include : - sports diving can be done with air cylinders carried by the diver . this is called scuba diving . it can also be done for shorter dives by holding your breath . this is called freediving . - scuba diving is done with metal cylinders carried by the diver to hold the breathing air . - military diving using air cylinders carried by the diver has existed since world war two . military diving using air supplied through a hose from a pump above the water has existed longer . - commercial diving is diving to do underwater work . the air for breathing is usually supplied through a hose from a pump above the water .
