Mintaka Pass     mintaka pass ( also known as minteke pass ) ( el . 4709 m./15,450 ft. ) or the nearby kilik pass ( 4,827 m or 15,837 ft ( 4,827 m ) high ; 37° 05 ' n ; 74° 41 ' e ) , 30 km to the west , were , in ancient times , the two main access points into the upper hunza valley from the north . the hunza valley is a mountainous valley near gilgit in the northern areas of pakistan .
