It's Only Love    it 's only love `` it 's only love '' is a hit rock duet recorded by grammy award winning singers bryan adams and tina turner . it was included on bryan adams ' album `` reckless '' and tina turner 's live album `` tina live in europe '' , as well as being added to both artists ' greatest hits compilations : adams ' `` anthology '' and turner 's `` all the best '' . it reached number 15 on the billboard hot 100 in the u.s. and number 29 in the uk . the 12 '' single included the 1985 live version that would later appear on the 1988 album `` tina live in europe '' .
