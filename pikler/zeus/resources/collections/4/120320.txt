Abd Manaf ibn Qusai     abd manaf ibn qusai ( arabic : ) is the person , whom the clan of banu abd manaf is named after . his father was qusai ibn kilab . when his father died , he took charge of his work . he too distinguished himself by his ability , and he was famous for his generosity and good judgment . he had five sons : nawfal , abd shams , hashim ( after whom the banu hashim clan was named ) and muttalib . muttalib was younger than hashim , and became his successor . other websites . - al-islam.org
