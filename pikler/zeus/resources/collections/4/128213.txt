New Line Cinema     new line film productions inc. , often called new line cinema , is an american movie studio . it was founded in 1967 by robert shaye and michael lynne . it became a subsidiary of time warner in 1996. it was merged into the bigger studio warner bros. in 2008. unlike other independent studios such as orion pictures , carolco pictures , or cannon films , new line cinema has grown and prospered to become one of hollywood 's major movie studios , culminating in the hit `` lord of the rings '' movie trilogy that brought prestige to the studio . other websites . - official website - new line 40th anniversary interview with michael lynne and robert shaye on charlie rose
