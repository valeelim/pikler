Salhad     salhad is one of the 51 union councils ( sub-divisions ) of abbottabad district in the khyber pakhtunkhwa province of pakistan . the union council of salhad is named after what was the main village of the area , but today has become a part of abbottabad city . the population of the town is now over 21,000. salhad union council is subdivided into two areas salhad and khokar .
