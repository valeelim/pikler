Old Norse language     old norse is a germanic language that was spoken in scandinavia , during the viking age , until about year 1300. it was also spoken in iceland , the faeroe islands , orkney islands and other places , where scandinavians started settlements ( similar to colonies ) . modern icelandic is the modern language , that looks most like old norse , when written . another language , similar to old norse , is elfdalian . other languages that come from old norse , are swedish , danish , faroese , and norwegian .
