John W. Snow     john william snow ( born august 2 , 1939 , in toledo , ohio ) served as the 73rd united states secretary of the treasury . he replaced secretary paul o'neill on february 3 , 2003 and was succeeded by henry paulson on july 3 , 2006 , in a move that had been anticipated for several weeks . snow submitted a letter of resignation on may 30 , 2006 , effective `` after an orderly transition period for my successor . '' to replace him , u.s. president george w. bush nominated henry m. paulson , jr.. snow announced on thursday , june 29 , 2006 that he had completed his last day on the job ; robert kimmitt served as acting secretary until paulson was sworn in .
