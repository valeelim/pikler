Hallux     the hallux , also called the big toe , is the toe innermost ( closest to the middle of the body ) of the foot . it is usually , but not always , the biggest toe in the body . in humans , the hallux is longer than the toes next to it ; this is inherited . the disease fibrodysplasia ossificans progressiva causes people to have a small hallux . in some birds , the hallux is pointed backwards to help with grabbing .
