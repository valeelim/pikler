Sand cat     the sand cat ( `` felis margarita '' ) is a small wild cat in the felinae subfamily . it is distributed over african and asian deserts . the name `` desert cat '' is reserved for a subspecies of the true wildcat , but it would be appropriate for this species . it lives in those arid areas that are too hot and dry even for the desert cat : the sahara , the arabian desert , and the deserts of iran and pakistan . it lives for about 13 years in captivity .
