John Tonkin     john trezise tonkin ( 2 february 1902 – 20 october 1995 ) was the 20th premier of western australia from 3 march 1971 to 8 april 1974. he was from the labor party . he was born in boulder city in 1902. educated in local schools tonkin became a teacher in rural western australia . an active member of the labor party , he was prime minister john curtin 's campaign manager for the 1943 election . he was elected to the western australian parliament for the seat of north east fremantle ( 1933–1950 ) , and for the seat of melville ( 1950–1977 ) . he was a cabinet minister ( 1943–1947 ) , deputy premier ( 1953–1959 ) , leader of the opposition ( 1967–1971 and 1974–1976 ) , and premier ( 1971–1974 ) . he retired from parliament in 1977 and died in 1995 .
