Balochistan forest dormouse     the balochistan forest dormouse ( `` dryomys niethammeri '' ) is a species of rodent in the gliridae family . it is endemic to pakistan . references . - gippoliti s. 2002. dryomys niethammeri . 2006 iucn red list of threatened species . downloaded on 29 july 2007 . - holden m.e . 2005. family gliridae , 819-841 in `` mammal species of the world : a taxonomic and geographic reference '' . d e. wilson and d.m . reeder eds . johns hopkins university press , baltimore .
