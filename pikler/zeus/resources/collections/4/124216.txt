Scarlet macaw     the scarlet macaw ( `` ara macao '' ) is a type of parrot . it is native to humid evergreen forests of tropical south america . its range is from south-eastern mexico to amazonian peru , bolivia , venezuela and brazil in lowlands up to 500 m ( 1,640 ft ) ( at least formerly ) up to 1,000 m ( 3,281 ft ) . it has suffered from local extinction through habitat destruction and capture for the parrot trade , but locally it remains fairly common . it is the national bird of honduras .
