Guin, Alabama    guin , alabama guin is a city in marion county , alabama , united states . at the 2000 census the population was 2,389. on april 3 , 1974 , guin was devastated by an f5 tornado during the super outbreak , the largest tornado outbreak on record . the death toll in guin was 30 , which , at the time , was 11 % of the population .
