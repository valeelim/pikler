George Enescu     george enescu ( august 19 , 1881 , liveni – may 4 , 1955 , paris ) was a romanian composer . he was born in liveni , romania . he studied the violin at the vienna conservatory . he moved to paris in 1895 to study composition at the paris conservatoire with jules massenet and then gabriel fauré . other students at this time were maurice ravel and charles koechlin . in 1927 he taught the violin to yehudi menuhin .
