British Rail Class 128     british rail class 128 was a class of diesel multiple unit , built for british rail . introduced in 1959 , ten of the class were built by british united traction , each with two 230 hp leyland engines . the class was built specifically for parcels , and did not feature any passenger accommodation ; fitted out with parcel racks and bike storages at each end . the last members of the class were withdrawn in 1991 , and none were preserved . other websites . - class info from railcar.com
