American International Group     american international group , inc. ( aig ) ( , , ) is a major american insurance corporation based in new york city . the company 's uk headquarters are in london , its continental europe operations are based in paris and its asian hq is in hong kong . according to the 2008 forbes global 2000 list , aig was the 18th-largest company in the world . it became part of the dow jones industrial average on april 8 , 2004. on september 16 2008 , the u.s. federal reserve bank announced its plan to lend the insurer $ 85 billion , u.s. dollars , in exchange for a 79.9 % equity stake in the company . aig announced on the same day that its board accepted the terms of the federal reserve bank 's loan offer . other websites . - aig website
