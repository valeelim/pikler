Pat Benatar     pat benatar ( born patricia mae andrzejewski on 10 january 1953 ) is a four-time grammy award-winning american rock singer with seven platinum and three gold albums to her credit , as well as 19 top 40 singles . pat benatar was eligible for the rock and roll hall of fame in 2004. benatar is also known for her mezzo-soprano vocal range . other websites . - pat benatar pictures
