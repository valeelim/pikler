Iridaceae     iridaceae are perennial herbs from rhizomes , bulbs or corms comprising about 80 genera and 1,500 species . the leaves are distichous and have a sheathing , equitant base and a generally ensiform or linear blade with parallel venation . the flowers are bisexual , usually showy , and are actinomorphic or zygomorphic . some genera : - `` gladiolus '' - `` ixia '' - `` iris '' - `` libertia ''
