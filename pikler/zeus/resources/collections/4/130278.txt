Sega 32X     the sega 32x is a game console first released by sega in 1994. it was an add on for the sega mega drive and was sega 's first console in the 32-bit games market . in japan , it was called the sega super 32x . in north america , it was called the sega genesis 32x . in europe and australia , it was called the sega mega 32x .
