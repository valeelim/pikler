Anarchist communism     anarchist communism , also known as anarcho-communism , is the belief that no government , private property or capitalism should exist , and that the means of production should be held in common . many anarcho-communists support direct democracy and a network of voluntary associations , workers ' councils and/or a gift economy in which everyone will be free to satisfy their needs . some well-known anarcho-communist writers are peter kropotkin , ricardo flores magnon and nestor makhno .
