Gijsbert Haan     gijsbert haan ( or gysbert haan ) ( january 3 , 1801 - july 27 , 1874 ) was the leader of the 1857 secession of dutch-americans from the reformed church in america . he was the creator of the christian reformed church in the united states and canada . haan was born in hilversum , noord-holland , netherlands and died of liver disease in grand rapids , michigan .
