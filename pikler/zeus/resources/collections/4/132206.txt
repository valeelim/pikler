We Ski     we ski , also known as family ski in japan and europe is a video game for the wii . it is the first third-party game ( and second game behind `` wii fit '' ) released that makes use of the wii balance board . a sequel , `` we ski & snowboard '' , which adds snowboarding to the game , was released in japan november 13 , 2008. other websites . - official website - we ski at ign
