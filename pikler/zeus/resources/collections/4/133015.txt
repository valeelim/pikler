Anaximenes of Miletus     anaximenes ( ) of miletus ( c. 585 bc-c. 525 bc ) was a greek pre-socratic philosopher from the latter half of the 6th century , probably a younger contemporary of anaximander , whose pupil or friend he is said to have been . anaximenes , like others in his school of thought , practised material monism , the idea that underlying reality is one single thing . for him , the primary substance was air . to us , this is obviously wrong , but it was an attempt to find a natural way to explain the world .
