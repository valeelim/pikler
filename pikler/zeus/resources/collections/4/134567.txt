René Adler     rené adler ( born january 15 , 1985 in leipzig ) is a german footballer . he plays on the position of a goalkeeper for bayer leverkusen . leverkusen is a city in germany . the club bayer leverkusen plays in the bundesliga , the first football league in germany . on october 11 , 2008 , adler made his first match in the german national team against russia ( 2-1 ) .
