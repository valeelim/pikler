Nitrous oxide     nitrous oxide is a gas with the chemical formula no . it is found naturally in the air . it is also made artificially , because it has many uses . it is a very strong greenhouse gas ( almost 300 times more so than carbon dioxide ) . because it is a greenhouse gas , people are trying to use it less . nitrous oxide is used by doctors and dentists to reduce pain . in the body , it makes people feel happy . for this reason it is also called `` laughing gas '' . it is sometimes used `` only '' to feel happy , like an illegal drug . nitrous oxide is used in high performance cars to increase the power of their engines . when it is used this way , it is usually called `` nitrous '' or `` nos '' . nitrous oxide can also be used in aerosol spray cans , especially for foods like whipped cream . this is because it has a sweet taste and is not toxic , and also makes the whipped cream more fluffy .
