Songs About Jane     songs about jane is the first album by american pop rock group maroon 5. it was released on june 25 , 2002 under j records in the united states . the album was re-released on october 14 , 2003 under octone records . it was produced by matthew wallace . the album spawned the singles `` harder to breathe '' , `` this love '' , and `` she will be loved . '' the album peaked at number-six on the billboard 200 albums chart selling a round-up of 3 million album sales . by the time 2004 concluded , the album was titled multi-platinum in sales in several different countries . on may 22 , 2012 the band announced on their website that they would be celebrating the ten year anniversary of the album on by releasing a limited edition re-issue . the re-issue was released on june 5 , 2012 . 2 disc cd re-issue of 'songs about jane ' , featuring original demos , unreleased material and videos and a limited edition lithograph incorporating original art inspired by each song and 'songs about jane ' album art . all songs written by jesse carmichael and adam levine , except where noted .
