Genogram     a genogram is a family tree with medical details , such as whether the person is male or female , and whether brothers and sisters are twins . genograms help medics understand a person better . one of the advantages of a genogram is the ability to use colour-coded lines to define different types of relationships such as family relationships , emotional relationships and social relationships . within family relationships , you can illustrate if a couple is married , divorced , common-law , engaged , etc .
