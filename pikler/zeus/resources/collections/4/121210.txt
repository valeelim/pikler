A Perfect Circle     a perfect circle , often called apc by fans , is an alternative rock band . it was put together by guitarist billy howerdel and tool lead singer maynard james keenan . the band is made of jeordie white - the bass player for marilyn manson , james iha - who played guitar for the smashing pumpkins , and josh freese , who used to go on tour and play drums with nine inch nails . although the group never officially broke up , a perfect circle has not performed or written anything since 2006. the band members still keep busy with other projects . on september 17 , 2009 , keenan confirmed that new material from a perfect circle was on the way during a live radio interview with bj shea on seattle rock station kisw . the band 's biggest hits have been `` judith '' , `` weak and powerless '' , and `` the outsider '' . they have released the albums `` mer de noms '' , `` thirteenth step '' , and `` emotive '' . other websites . - a perfect circle.com â€“ official website - interview with billy howerdel - at college crier
