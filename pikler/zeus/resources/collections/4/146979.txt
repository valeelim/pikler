Paul Thompson     paul ivan thompson ( born november 2 , 1906 in calgary , alberta - september 13 , 1991 ) was a canadian ice hockey player . he was a left wing . thompson started playing in the national hockey league ( nhl ) in 1926. he played for the new york rangers and the chicago black hawks . he stopped playing in 1939. he was part of the all-star team two times . he won the stanley cup three times . he won in 1928 with new york and in 1934 and 1938 with chicago . his brother was tiny thompson .
