Gears of War     gears of war , sometimes called gears , is a third-person shooter videogame developed by epic games and published by microsoft . it was released only for the xbox 360 in 2006 but was later released for microsoft windows . `` gears of war '' was very successful . it sold three million copies in ten weeks and was the fastest selling video game of 2006. it was the second most played game on the xbox 360 's online service , xbox live , in 2006 - only beaten by halo 3. a film version of the game is currently being made by new line cinema . the sequel , `` gears of war 2 '' , was released in november 2008. the pc version uses a drm protection scheme . the certificate needed to play the game is invalid since 2009 , jan 1st , making the game unplayable . fortunately to the players , the protection can be worked around by simply changing the system date . other websites . - `` gears of war '' website ( `` warning : contains pictures of in-game violence '' )
