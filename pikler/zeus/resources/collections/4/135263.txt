Concussion     concussion is the caused by an injury to the head . it is the most common form of traumatic brain injury . other terms include mild brain injury , mild traumatic brain injury ( mtbi ) , mild head injury ( mhi ) , and minor head trauma . people suffering from it are usually monitored in hospital . the main cause of this is that hemorrhage in the brain is common ; this can be life-threatening . common symptoms include headache , dizziness , fatigue , problems with vision , and losing consciousness . symptoms last three to seven days in minor cases , but they usually take ten to 25 days to go away . the main cause of concussions are accidents ; in some cases , wearing a helmet can help prevent major damage .
