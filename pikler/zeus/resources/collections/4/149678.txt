Circle Hot Springs, Alaska    circle hot springs , alaska circle hot springs , alaska is a holiday resort in alaska . it is 137 miles northeast of fairbanks . the hotel was built in 1930. the athabasca people were the first to use the hot springs . in 1893 , william greats discovered the springs . in 1905 the land around the springs was sold for farming and a resort was opened in 1909. the main feature of circle hot springs is a very large hot pool . there is a story that the hotel is haunted by a ghost .
