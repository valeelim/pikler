Shawn Johnson     shawn machel johnson ( born january 19 , 1992 ) is an american artistic gymnast . she is the 2008 olympic women 's balance beam gold medalist . johnson is the daughter of doug and teri johnson . her parents put her in a gymnastics class after they saw her climbing up cabinets and jumping down from tables . when she was six , liang chow opened a gymnastics school in her city . she was one of chow 's first students when he opened the school .
