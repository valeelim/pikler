Yoshio Sakamoto     yoshio sakamoto is a video game developer for nintendo who works in nintendo r & d 1. he was the co-creator of the `` metroid '' series along with gunpei yokoi , and is in charge of most of the games released after yokoi 's death . he also works on `` wario '' games , including games in the `` wario land '' series and the `` warioware '' series . he founded `` team shikamaru '' , which is dedicated to script writing .
