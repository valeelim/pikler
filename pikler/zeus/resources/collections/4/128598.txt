Appliance     an appliance is a machine that only has limited functionality . a prosthesis is an example . many appliances need electricity to work . - `` household appliances '' can be used for cleaning ( like the vacuum cleaner ) or cooking . if they are easy to carry around , they are `` small appliances . '' - `` major appliances '' are big machines like dishwashers , refrigerators and ice makers . - a `` fire appliance '' is the name of a fire engine the firefighters use to extinguish a fire
