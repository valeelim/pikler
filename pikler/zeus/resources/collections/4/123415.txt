Pangong Tso (Lake)    pangong tso ( lake ) pangong tso ( or pangong lake ; tso : ladakhi for lake ) is a lake in the himalayas situated at a height of about 4,250 m ( 13,900 ft ) . it is 134 km ( 83.3 mi ) long and extends from ladakh to tibet . two thirds of the length of this lake lies in tibet . the lake is 5 km ( 3 mi ) wide at its broadest point . in winter , the lake surface freezes completely despite being salt water .
