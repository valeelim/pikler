Flag of Sri Lanka     the flag of sri lanka , also called the lion flag , consists of gold lion passant , holding a sword in its right fore paw , in front of a crimson background with four golden bo leaves in each corner . around the background is a yellow border , and to its left are two vertical stripes of equal size in saffron and green , with the saffron stripe closest to the lion . it became the flag in 1950 following the recommendations of a committee appointed by the 1st prime minister of sri lanka d.s . senanayake .
