Jamie Noble     james gibson ( born december 23 , 1976 ) better known by his ring name jamie noble , is an american professional wrestler . he is best known for his time wrestling with world wrestling entertainment ( wwe ) and as a road agent/producer to smackdown 's cruiserweight division . he is also known for his appearances with ring of honor , where he won its world championship , and world championship wrestling in its cruiserweight division and is a one time cruiserweight champion .
