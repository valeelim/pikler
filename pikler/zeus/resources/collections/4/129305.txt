Lucy Lawless     lucille frances ryan ( born march 29 , 1968 in auckland ) is a new zealand actress . when she was twenty years old , she got her first acting job in the tv program `` funny business '' . in 1995 , she starred in her first television series , `` '' , becoming in an international star in this role . lawless is also known for her role as `` d'anna biers '' in the tv series `` battlestar galactica '' between 2005 and 2008. during a break in 1997 between filming seasons on xena , lawless made her onstage debut singing songs on broadway as rizzo in the remake of the musical play grease . since her role as xena , lawless has pursued a new career in singing after being a contestant on `` celebrity duets '' . she sang at the roxy in hollywood in 2007 , and has since performed regularly in concerts and released several cds . she has a contralto singing range . she was born in auckland on 29 march , 1968. lawless ' mother , julie , was teacher , and her father , frank , was mayor . she has 6 brothers . she had bulimia and depression .
