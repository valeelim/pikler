Biafo Glacier     the biafo glacier is a 63 km long glacier in the karakoram mountains of the northern areas , pakistan which meets the 49 km long hispar glacier at an altitude of 5,128 m ( 16,824 feet ) at hispar la ( pass ) to create the world 's longest glacial system outside of the polar regions . this highway of ice connects two ancient mountain kingdoms , nagar ( immediately south of hunza ) in the west with baltistan in the east . the traverse uses 51 of the biafo glacier 's 63 km and all of the hispar glacier to form a 100 km glacial route .
