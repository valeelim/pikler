Lund Khwar     lund khwar , is a village and union council of mardan district in the khyber pakhtunkhwa province of pakistan . it is located at 34°23'22n 71°58'51e with a height of 371 metres ( 1220 feet ) . lund khwar means `` the ever flowing stream ( or brook ) '' . the village was founded in the 12th century by yousafzai and khattak tribesmen .
