United States Secretary of Education     the united states secretary of education is the head of the department of education . the secretary is a member of the president 's cabinet , and 16th in line of united states presidential line of succession . the united states secretary of education deals with education . in 1979 , president jimmy carter established the department of education as a cabinet-level agency . previously , education had been handled by the secretary of health , education , and welfare , henceforth known as the secretary of health and human services . the current acting secretary of education is betsy devos .
