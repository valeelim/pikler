Nubra Valley     nubra ( ) is a valley in the region of ladakh , india . diskit , the capital of the nubra area , is about north from leh , the capital of ladakh . its name is derived from ldumra ( the valley of flowers ) . the nubra river is a tributary of the shyok river , which flows into the indus river . it flows in the ladakh area of jammu and kashmir .
