Scorpion I     scorpion i was the first of two kings so-named of upper egypt during the protodynastic period . his name may refer to the scorpion goddess serket . he is believed to have lived in thinis one or two centuries before the rule of the better known king scorpion of nekhen . to him belongs the u-j tomb found in the royal cemetery of abydos where thinite kings were buried . in contemporary fiction . - william golding 's novel `` the scorpion god '' is loosely based upon this period of egyptian history . - the 2001 adventure film `` the mummy returns '' features a fictionalized account of the `` scorpion king '' of egypt , a character who went on to have his own spin-off in the 2002 film , `` the scorpion king '' .
