WQED (TV)    wqed ( tv ) wqed ( channel 13 , digital channel 38 ) is a pbs television station based in pittsburgh , pennsylvania . established 1 april 1954 , it was the first community-sponsored television station in the united states as well as the fifth public tv station . wqed also became the first station to telecast classes to elementary classrooms when pittsburgh launched the metropolitan school service in 1955. wqed has produced many shows for pbs , such as mister rogers ' neighborhood ( which aired first run episodes from 19 february 1968 to 31 august 2001 ) . pittsburgh magazine is also a publication of wqed .
