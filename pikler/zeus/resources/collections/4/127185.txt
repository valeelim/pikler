Marat Safin     marat safin is a retired professional tennis player from russia . he was once ranked number 1 in the world . in 2000 and 2005 , safin won the us open and the australian open , respectively . in 2002 and 2004 , safin played in the last round of the australian open , but lost . in 2008 , safin became the first russian man to play in the semifinals in the wimbledon championships . born to a tatar family , he learned tennis in spain when he was a teenager .
