Imran Khan     imran ahmad khan niazi ( ) ( born 25 november 1952 ) is a retired pakistani cricketer who played international cricket for two decades in the late 20th century . he became a politician in the mid-1990s . currently , besides running his own political party , khan is also a charity worker and cricket commentator . he made his political party pakistan tehreek-e-insaaf ( pti ) . he made cancer hospital after his mother died because of this disease .
