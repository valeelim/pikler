Salicaceae     salicaceae is a family of flowering plants . each plant has either staminate or pistillate catkins . the flowers have no petals or sepals . each has a small scale-like bract below it . these developing catkins on willows form the well-known `` pussy-willow '' branches . except for two species of `` populas '' ( cottonwood ) , all the members of `` salicaceae '' in the bay area are of the genera `` salix '' ( willows ) .
