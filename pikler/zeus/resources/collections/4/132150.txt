Anantnag District     anantnag ( ) is a district in the state of jammu and kashmir in northwestern india . it is one of six districts which make up the kashmir valley . the district headquarters is anantnag . tehsils . - kokernag tehsil - shangus tehsil - anantnag tehsil - bijbehara tehsil - doru tehsil - pahalgam tehsil - qazigund tehsil - kulgam tehsil
