Death Magnetic     death magnetic is the ninth studio album by heavy metal band metallica . the album was released september 12 , 2008 with the hits `` the day that never comes '' , `` cyanide '' , `` my apocalypse '' , `` the unforgiven iii '' , and `` the judas kiss '' . track listing . 1. that was just your life 1. the end of the line 1. broken , beat & scarred 1. the day that never comes 1. all nightmare long 1. cyanide 1. the unforgiven iii 1. the judas kiss 1. suicide & redemption 1. my apocalypse `` death magnetic '' was nominated for four grammy awards . rick rubin won `` producer of the year , non-classical '' for his work on `` death magnetic '' , as well as other albums this past year . - best rock album - best metal performance ( `` my apocalypse '' ) - best rock instrumental performance ( `` suicide & redemption '' ) - best recording package the album won two grammys for `` best metal performance '' and `` best recording package '' at the 51st grammy awards on february 8 , 2009 .
