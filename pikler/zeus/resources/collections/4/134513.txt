Hesperomannia arbuscula     the maui hesperomannia ( hesperomannia arbuscula ) is a species of flowering plant in the asteraceae family . it is found only in the united states . its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical wet low lying land forests and subtropical or tropical dry shrubland ( an area with mostly bushes and few trees ) . it is in danger due to the loss of its natural area of habitat . source . - bruegmann , m.m . & caraway , v. 2003. hesperomannia arbuscula . 2006 iucn red list of threatened species . downloaded on 20 july 2007 .
