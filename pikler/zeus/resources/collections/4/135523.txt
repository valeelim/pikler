Discovering the Waterfront     discovering the waterfront is the second full-length album by the band silverstein . the album has three singles ; `` smile in your sleep '' , `` discovering the waterfront '' , and `` my heroine '' . all of the singles had music videos . all songs written by shane told and neil boshart , except for where noted . personnel . - band - shane told – lead vocals , rhythm and other guitars , keyboards - neil boshart – lead guitar - josh bradford – rhythm guitar - billy hamilton – bass , backing vocals - paul koehler – drums , percussion charts . - billboard ( north america )
