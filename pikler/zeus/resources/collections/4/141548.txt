Ithaca     ithaca ( ithaka or ithaki ; greek : ιθάκη ) is one of the ionian islands on the west coast of greece . it is about long and wide . in 2001 there were about 3,000 people living there . odysseus was said to be the king of ithaca in the epic poem the `` odyssey '' , which was written in the ancient greek language . it is possible that this ancient island is in a place different from the modern island .
