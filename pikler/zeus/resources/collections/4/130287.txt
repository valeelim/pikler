Almondvale Stadium     almondvale stadium is a football stadium in livingston , west lothian . it is the home of scottish first division club livingston f.c.. the stadium , which used to be called the `` city stadium '' because of a deal with the city group , was built in 1995. it had also previously been called the `` west lothian courier stadium '' . since the club was taken over by the lionheart consortium , it was changed back to its original name , almondvale stadium . it is hired by livingston fc from west lothian council every year . as livingston moved up the divisions into the top flight of scottish football , they expanded the stadium to meet premier league standards .
