Apiaceae     the apiaceae or umbelliferae is a family of usually aromatic plants with hollow stems , commonly known as umbellifers . it includes cumin , parsley , carrot , coriander/cilantro , dill , caraway , fennel , parsnip , celery , anise , queen anne 's lace and other relatives . it is a large family with about 300 genera and more than 3,000 species . the family includes a number of food plants , and a number of poisonous plants . several poisonous plants go by the name of hemlock , and have rather similar paralysing effects .
