Bahawalpur Tehsil     bahawalpur tehsil ( also bhawalpur or bhawulpore ) ( ) is a city of ( 1998 pop . 403,408 ) in bahawalpur district , punjab pakistan . seraiki is the local language of the area . urdu and english are also spoken by the people . bahawalpur is located south of the sutlej river and it lies in the cholistan region . it is situated 90 km from multan , 420 km from lahore and about 700 km from the national capital islamabad . it was the capital of the former princely state of bahawalpur .
