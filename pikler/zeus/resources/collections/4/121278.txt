The Hundred and One Dalmatians     the hundred and one dalmatians , or the great dog robbery is a 1956 children 's novel by dodie smith . the story is about cruella de vil and her plans to skin 101 dalmatian puppies to make a fur coat . the puppies are rescued by other animals . disney released an animated movie in 1961 based on the book as `` one hundred and one dalmatians '' . the book has been published as `` 101 dalmatians '' . smith wrote a sequel to the book called `` the starlight barking '' .
