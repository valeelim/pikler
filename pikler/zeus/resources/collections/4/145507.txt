Duncan II of Scotland     donnchad mac maíl coluim ( modern gaelic : `` donnchadh mac mhaoil chaluim '' ) renamed as duncan ii ( before c. 1060 – 12 november 1094 ) was king of scots . he was son of malcolm iii ( máel coluim mac donnchada ) and his first wife ingibiorg finnsdottir , widow of thorfinn sigurdsson .
