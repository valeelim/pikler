Good Morning (song)    good morning ( song ) `` good morning '' is a single on kanye west 's 3rd studio album `` graduation '' . the song was released in late august 2007 on itunes . `` good morning '' is the 1st track on the album . it has samples of `` someone saved my life tonight '' as sung by elton john . additional vocals were provided by connie mitchell , tony `` penafire '' williams , and jay-z .
