Foz do Iguaçu     foz do iguaçu ( fɔs do igwa'su ) is the 4th largest city of paraná state , in the south region of brazil . 309,000 people live there . it is about west of curitiba , parana 's capital city , on the border with argentina and paraguay . the people who live there are known as `` iguaçuenses '' . the city is known for the iguaçu falls and the itaipu dam , the largest dam of the world was built .
