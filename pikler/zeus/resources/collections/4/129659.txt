Phineas and Ferb     phineas and ferb is an american animated television program produced by disney . it was first on tv on february 1 , 2008. it has been nominated for an emmy award . it airs on the disney channel and related channels . the program is about two brothers from a blended family with a pet platypus , who do impossible plans . in every episode , phineas and ferb create big machines in their backyard , like a roller coaster or a huge waterslide . their sister , candace , tries to show her mother the machines . but always when the mother is in the backyard , the machines have disappeared . almost all episodes have a song which the characters sing . the creators of the show are dan povenmire and jeff `` swampy '' marsh . they also worked together on the nickelodeon series `` rocko 's modern life '' . `` phineas and ferb the movie : across the 2nd dimension '' was on the disney channel on august 5 , 2011. in the movie the characters discover perry the platypus is an agent . they go to another dimension and utilize many inventions .
