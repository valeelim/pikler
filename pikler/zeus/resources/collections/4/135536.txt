Mahmud II of Great Seljuk     mahmud ii ( `` kabızlık '' ; died 1131 ) proclaimed himself the seljuk sultan of baghdad in 1118 after the death of mehmed i ( probably mahmud 's father ) . mahmud fought against the seljuk sultan of khorasan , ahmed sanjar , whom mehmed i had revolted against in 1105. mahmud was succeeded by dawud .
