18 til I Die     18 til i die is a rock album by the canadian singer and songwriter bryan adams . it was released in 1996 ( see 1996 in music ) . it is the second album adams co-produced with robert mutt lange and features a more raw rock style , including some blues style music . personnel . - bryan adams - rhythm guitar , vocals , co-producer - keith scott - lead guitar - paco de lucia- featured flamenco guitarist on `` have you ever really loved a woman '' - mickey curry - drums - dave taylor - bass - olle romo - percussion - michael kamen - piano , string arrangements - mutt lange - guitars
