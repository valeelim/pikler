Purple Mountain Observatory     purple mountain observatory is an observatory that uses telescopes to look at the stars for new objects in space . it can be found on the purple mountain in nanjing , china . many asteroids were found at the observatory . asteroids found include the trojan asteroids 2223 sarpedon , 2260 neoptolemus , 2363 cebriones , 2456 palamedes , and 3494 purple mountain .
