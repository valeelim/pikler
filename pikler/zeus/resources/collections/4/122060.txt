Nagri Totial     nagri tutial ( also known as nagri totial ) is one of the 51 union councils of abbottabad district in the khyber pakhtunkhwa province of pakistan . nagri tutial which means `` the town of the tutials '' is named after abassi chief toota khan ( a son of doomat khan ) . most of the people of nagri tutial are from the tutial branch of the dhund abbasi tribe . nagri tutial a famous and beautiful village in pakistan . it is in the south east of the district , next to the punjab . nagri tutial shares a borders with the other union councils in abbottabad district - to the north and east with seer gharbi , to the west with nara , and to the south with lora . nagri tutial is 1243 metres ( 4081 feet ) above the level of the sea . the people or nagri tutial are hardworking and dedicated and work far away in various places , throughout pakistan , to earn their living . they are politically conscious and careful in electing their local representatives . in the past , members of parliament from this area have done little or nothing to help in development and this has made the majority of people wary .
