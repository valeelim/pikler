Act     act has several meanings : - an act is a deed or thing done . for example , 'patting the dog is a kind act ' . - an act can also mean a law passed by a government , for example , the health and safety at work act 1974 . - an act is a part of a piece written to be performed in a theatre . - in the military , acting is when someone temporarily holds a higher rank . - the language code for achterhooks , a dutch dialect .
