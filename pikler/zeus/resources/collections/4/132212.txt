Nepeta     nepeta is a genus of about 250 species of flowering plants in the family lamiaceae . some members of this group are known as catnip or catmint because of their effect on house cats . the oil in the leaves of some `` nepeta '' species gives cats euphoria , that is , it makes them feel good . they get a `` high '' , roll around , and may cry out , jump and behave in an unusual way .
