Loudest Love     loudest love is an ep by the grunge band soundgarden . it was released in october 1990 through a & m records . the ep was first released in japan , and later released in the united states . the track listing is the same as that for the `` loud love '' single , but with the addition of `` hands all over '' , `` heretic '' and `` come together '' . track listing . 1 . `` loud love '' ( chris cornell ) – 5:26 - appeared on the album `` louder than love '' and released as a single 1 . `` hands all over '' ( cornell , kim thayil ) – 6:00 - appeared on the album `` louder than love '' and released as a single 1 . `` get on the snake '' ( cornell , thayil ) – 3:44 - appeared on the album `` louder than love '' 1 . `` heretic '' ( thayil , hiro yamamoto ) – 3:48 - originally written and recorded in 1985 and appeared on the `` deep six '' compilation . however , the version on this ep is the re-recorded version ( with matt cameron rather than scott sundquist on drums ) . 1 . `` come together '' ( john lennon , paul mccartney ) – 5:52 - a cover of the beatles song `` come together '' , originally written by john lennon and paul mccartney . it features soundgarden 's producer jack endino on backing vocals .
