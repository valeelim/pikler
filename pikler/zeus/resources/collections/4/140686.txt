Languages of Iran     there are dozens of languages spoken in iran . the following are the languages with the greatest number of speakers ( data from the cia world factbook ) : - persian , luri , gilaki and mazandarani 58 % - azeri and other turkic languages 26 % - kurdish 9 % - balochi 1 % - arabic 1 % - the remaining 5 % include a variety of other languages . related pages . - indo-iranian languages other websites . - ethnologue report for languages of iran - britannica - cia factbook
