Brunello di Montalcino     brunello di montalcino is a red italian wine . it is made in the vineyards surrounding the town of montalcino about southwest of florence , tuscany , italy . brunello , roughly translated as `` nice dark one '' in the local dialect , is the unofficial name of the clone of sangiovese ( also known as `` sangiovese grosso '' ) grown in the montalcino region . in 1980 , the brunello di montalcino was awarded the first `` denominazione di origine controllata e garantita '' ( docg ) designation and today is one of the italy 's best-known and most expensive wines . other websites . - rosso and brunello di montalcino from tuscany - map highlighting the montalcino region - above 90 montalcino wine tour
