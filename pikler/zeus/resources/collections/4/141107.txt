Gears of War 2     gears of war 2 is a third-person shooter video game made by epic games and published by microsoft . it can only be played on microsoft 's own xbox 360 console . it is the sequel to the popular xbox 360 game `` gears of war '' . the game was released around the world on november 7 2008. by november 12 it had already sold over 2 million copies .
