Apologetic proverb     apologetic proverbs ( also known as wellerisms ) , make fun of proverbs by showing that they are wrong , in some situations . they have : - a proverb ( or saying ) , and - an explanation ( often humorous ) . they often have sardonic humor . examples . - a body can get used to anything , even to being hanged , as the irishman said . ( lucy maud montgomery -- '' anne of green gables '' )
