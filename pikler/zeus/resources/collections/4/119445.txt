Mastung Tehsil     mastung , the capital of mastung district , is a town in the baluchistan province of pakistan . it is located at 29°48'0n 66°50'60e and has an altitude of 1701 metres ( 5583 feet ) . the town is also the administrative centre of mastung tehsil , an administrative subdivision of the district - the town itself is administratively subdivided into two union councils of pakistan
