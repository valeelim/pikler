Black Stone Cherry     black stone cherry are a rock band from kentucky , usa . they are signed to roadrunner records and have released two albums , called `` black stone cherry '' and `` folklore and superstition '' . the band has had several songs reach the top 30 of billboard 's top mainstream rock songs , including `` lonely train '' , which reached # 14 on that chart in 2006. members of the band . - chris robertson ( lead vocals , guitar ) - ben wells ( guitar , backing vocals ) - jon lawhon ( bass guitar , backing vocals ) - john fred young ( drums , backing vocals ) other websites . - black stone cherry 's official website
