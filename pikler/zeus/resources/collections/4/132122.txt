Circuit de Monaco     circuit de monaco is the name given to a motor racing circuit laid out on the city streets of monte carlo and la condamine around the harbor of the principality of monaco . it is commonly referred to as `` monte carlo '' because it is largely inside the monte carlo neighborhood of monaco . deaths from crashes . - 1952 luigi fagioli - 1967 lorenzo bandini other websites . - monaco grand prix - circuit de monaco history and statistics - statistics from official f1 site
