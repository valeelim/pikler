Chitral National Park     chitral national park is one of the national parks of pakistan which owes its own individuality due to its unique features . it is located in the khyber pakhtunkhwa province of pakistan at the side of chitral river , just at a distance of two hours drive from the chitral ( ) , city . this park is also known as , chitral gol national park . the word , gol in local language means ' the valley ' . this park constitutes many valleys thereby owing the name , chitral gol national park .
