Alan Stephenson Boyd     alan stephenson boyd ( born july 20 , 1922 ) was the first united states secretary of transportation , appointed by lyndon johnson in november 1966. he was appointed to the civil aeronautics board in 1959 by dwight eisenhower and promoted to chairman by john f. kennedy . when the richard m. nixon administration took power , boyd left the government and became the president of the illinois central railroad , a position he held from 1969 to 1972 .
