British Rail Class 121     sixteen class 121 single-car driving motor vehicles were built from 1960 , numbered 55020-55035. these were supplemented by ten trailer vehicles , numbered 56280-289 ( later renumbered 54280-289 ) . they had a top speed of 70 mph , with slam-doors , and vacuum brakes . the driving motor vehicles were nicknamed `` bubble cars '' by some enthusiasts .
