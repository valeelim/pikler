Chamhad     chamad ( also known as chamhad ) is one of the 51 union councils ( subdivisions ) of abbottabad district in the khyber pakhtunkhwa province of pakistan . chamad is located at 34°7'0n 73°4'60e , in the west of the district where it borders haripur district , it has a height of 870 metres ( 2857 feet ) . the union council is made up of areas : khani thathiara , shadial , chamhad , fateh bandi , sarbangala , chamak mera , bagh darah , bermgali , bisala , mohal , baln seri , bain noora , bain gojra , bothiala , jattal , baghati , thana , kasaki khurd , kasaki kalan , daroparh and bhoraj .
