Steely Dan     steely dan is an american jazz rock band . they are from annandale-on-hudson , new york . they were active from 1971 to 1981. they reunited in 1993 to play their music live at concerts . discography . - studio albums - `` ca n't buy a thrill '' ( 1972 ) - `` countdown to ecstasy '' ( 1973 ) - `` pretzel logic '' ( 1974 ) - `` katy lied '' ( 1975 ) - `` the royal scam '' ( 1976 ) - `` aja '' ( 1977 ) - `` gaucho '' ( 1980 ) - `` everything must go '' ( 2003 )
