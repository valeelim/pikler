Pantanal     the pantanal is the world 's largest wetland or swamp . it covers a region of 140,000 km , larger than the area covered by the country greece . the pantanal is located in south america and shared by brazil , bolivia and paraguay . there are many threats to the pantanal such as pollution , mercury from gold mining and ranching . although the pantanal can wash some of the chemicals away , the increased amount is becoming a concern . in winter nearly 80 % of the area is flooded .
