Network computing     network computing is when computers work together over a network . network computing is `` storing and/or running applications in servers in a network '' this means that the network may use the power from all the networked computers to perform complex tasks . the network could also be for sharing files so that they can be looked at on any computer in the network easily . network computing is the opposite to `` stand-alone computing '' . this is where computers are not connect to a network . most laptops and home computers do their jobs alone and are stand-alone computers . references . - network computing at network-computing.net
