Palmerston Park     palmerston park is a multi-use stadium on terregles street in dumfries , south west scotland . the site of the ground was formerly a farm called palmers toun . this is on the maxwelltown side of the river nith in dumfries . it is currently used mostly for football matches and is the home ground of queen of the south f.c . the stadium holds 6,412 people . it also holds the record of having the tallest free standing floodlights in scottish football , standing at 85 feet .
