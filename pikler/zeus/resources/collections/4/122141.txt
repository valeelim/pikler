Phalkot     phalkot is one of the 51 union councils of abbottabad district in the khyber pakhtunkhwa province of pakistan . most of the people are awans with some dhund abbasis . most of the people are poor and lead a simple life . phalkot malsa is located at 34° 9 ' 40n 73° 22 ' 30e and has a height of 1747 metres ( 5734 feet ) . the union council is subdivided into two areas phalkot and malsa , that include other settlements including kutli sehana jandar bari buni gali and upri kutli . the hindko language is widely spoken throughout the area , urdu being national language also understand and spoken here . english and pashto is spoken and understood by some people
