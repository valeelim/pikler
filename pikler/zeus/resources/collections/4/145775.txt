Karditsa     karditsa is a greek city . it is in the center of greece in the district of thessaly . it had 35,971 people according to the 2001 census . karditsa is build on the large plain which is named thessaly plain . it is the second largest plain in greece . so it is an agricultural city . near karditsa is the city of trikala.in the west of karditsa is the agrafa range . agrafa is a part of pindus range .
