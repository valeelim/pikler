Losing Grip     losing grip is a 2003 single by avril lavigne . it is the fourth single from her first studio album `` let go '' . on the `` billboard '' charts , `` losing grip '' was able to peak at # 17 on the mainstream top 40 , # 33 on the adult top 40 , and at # 64 on the hot 100 charts . at the 2004 grammy awards , `` losing grip '' was nominated for best female rock vocal performance .
