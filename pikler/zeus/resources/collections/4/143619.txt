Sandspit Beach     sandspit beach is a beach southwest of karachi , pakistan . it is a famous tourist spot . the sea at sandspit is calm and quiet from october to march but it is rough during the monsoon season . a variety of marine life ( algae and crabs ) is found there . the shallow water here is good for swimming and sunbathing . it has an unusual rocky formation . sandspit beach is a popular relaxation spot in karachi . facilities at the sandspit beach include horseback and camel riding .
