McMahon Line     the mcmahon line is a demarcation line drawn on map referred to in the simla convention , a treaty between britain and tibet signed in 1914. although its legal status is disputed , it is currently the effective boundary between china and india . this dispute was used a pretext in a war between china and india in 1962. related pages . - arunachal pradesh - durand line - radcliffe line further reading . - globalsecurity.org , india-china border dispute - kawaguchi , yuki . `` arunachal pradesh territorial dispute between india and china , '' `` ice case studies '' ( us ) . no . 161 , november , 2005 .
