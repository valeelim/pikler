Khanpur Tehsil     khanpur ( ) is a city in punjab , pakistan . khanpur is a centre for sugarcane and cotton crops . the tehsil of khanpur is one of the largest area-wise of rahim yar khan district . the famous cholistan desert is mainly located in the khanpur area . khanpur is also known as khanpur katora ; katora means bowl . khanpur had a long history of producing clay bowls.c khanpur is a nice and historical city of punjab , pakistan .
