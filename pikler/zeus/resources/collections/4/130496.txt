Pete Wentz     peter lewis kingston wentz iii ( born on june 5 , 1979 ) better known as pete wentz , is an american musician and songwriter . he is most famous for his work with the chicago-based band fall out boy . in recent years , he has also become an entrepreneur and the host of the mtv program , fnmtv . wentz has been diagnosed with bipolar disorder . ashlee simpson divorced him . fall out boy came off hiatus in 2013 , releasing a new single `` my songs know what you did in the dark ( light 'em up ) '' and an album `` save rock and roll '' , along with a supporting spring tour . they will be going on the save rock and roll arena fall tour this september with pop punk group panic ! at the disco and rapper big sean in support of the album as well .
