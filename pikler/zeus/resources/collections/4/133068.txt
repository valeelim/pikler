Pythagoreanism     pythagoreanism is a term used for the esoteric and metaphysical beliefs held by pythagoras and his followers , the pythagoreans , who were much influenced by mathematics . later resurgence of ideas similar to those held by the early pythagoreans are collected under the term neopythagoreanism . pythagorean symbols . - monad - dyad - triad - tetrad - pentad - decad - tetractys - vesica piscis pythagoras was scared of beans
