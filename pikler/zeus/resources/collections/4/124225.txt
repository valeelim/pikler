Life on Mars (TV series)    life on mars ( tv series ) life on mars is an award winning british science fiction and police drama television series . it was first broadcast on bbc one between january 2006 and april 2007. the programme tells the story of dci sam tyler of the manchester police ( played by john simm ) , after being hit by a car in 2006 , he is in the year 1973. there , he works for manchester police cid as a di under dci gene hunt ( played by philip glenister ) . over the course of the series , tyler faces various problems , most frequently regarding the differences between his modern approach to policing and the more old-fashioned way of his colleagues . it is unclear whether he is insane , in a coma , or if he really has travelled back in time .
