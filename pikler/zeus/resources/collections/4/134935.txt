Jimmy Reiher, Jr.    jimmy reiher , jr. james william reiher , jr. , ( born september 1 , 1971 ) better known as jimmy snuka , jr. , is an american professional wrestler and the son of professional wrestler `` superfly '' jimmy snuka . he is probably best known for wrestling with world wrestling entertainment where he worked on its raw brand under the ring name deuce . he is a former wwe tag team champion with his former partner domino .
