Flag of Libya     the flag of libya has three horizontal stripes of red , black , and green , with a white crescent and star in the center of the black stripe . it has been the official flag of libya since 3 august 2011. the flag is the same as the flag of the earlier kingdom of libya , used from 1951 to 1969. the previous flag of libya is still used by supporters of muammar al-gaddafi . it was just a plain field of green . it was unique for being the only national flag in the world with just one color and no design , emblems , coat of arms , or any other details . it was adopted on 11 november 1977 .
