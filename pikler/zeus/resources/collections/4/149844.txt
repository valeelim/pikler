Anglefort     anglefort is a commune . it is found in the auvergne-rhône-alpes region in the ain department in the east of france . in 2009 , 960 people lived in the commune . the inhabitants are the `` clafordans '' . the local economy is mostly based around a silicon factory and an edf dam used for hydroelectricity . the most important monuments in the commune are the `` château d'anglefort '' , a 13th-century castle , and the ruins of the `` château de la rochette '' . the `` château d'anglefort '' is listed as a `` monument historique '' . famous people from anglefort include jacques-françois besson , bishop of metz in the 19th century .
