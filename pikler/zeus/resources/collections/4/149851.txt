Argis     argis is a commune . it is in the auvergne-rhône-alpes region of the ain department ; in the east of france . in 2009 , 421 people lived in the commune . the inhabitants are the `` argissiens '' . in the middle ages there was a castle in argis ; but it does not exist anymore . there are also two cascades in the commune , the `` cascade de la côte '' and the `` cascade de la pissoire '' .
