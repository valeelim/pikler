St. Johnstone F.C.    st. johnstone f.c . st. johnstone football club is a scottish football club based in perth . although it is officially recorded as being formed in 1884 , research has shown that they did not play their first game until february 1885. the club plays its home games at mcdiarmid park . st. johnstone will now compete in the scottish premier league after finishing first and being promoted in the 2008-09 season .
