Messolonghi     messolonghi is a greek city . it is the capital of the aitoloakarnania prefecture . it is build on the bank of a lagoon . its population is 14,209 inhabitants . messolonghi is known because of the greek war of independence . during that time the siege of messolonghi took place . during the siege the residents of messolonghi fought with the turks and the majority of them were killed there . during the siege of messolonghi lord byron died , in 1824 .
