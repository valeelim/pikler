Emile Heskey     emile william heskey ( born 11 january 1978 ) is an english footballer . he currently plays for bolton wanderers in the football league championship . at the pinnacle of his career , he played for leicester city , liverpool and birmingham city . he later he played for wigan athletic and aston villa , then newcastle jets in the australian a-league . for a while he was a regular member of the england national team , striking up a good partnership with fellow forward michael owen , where he was often deployed as a 'target man ' due to his immense strength , good first touch and passing skills .
