Bangladesh Standard Time     bangladesh standard time ( bst ) is the time zone for bangladesh . it is six hours ahead of utc ( utc+6 ) . bangladesh does not observe any daylight saving time . bangladesh used summer time , making it bangladesh daylight time ( bdt ) . this was used from june 19 until december 31 , 2009. the measure has not been used since . other websites . - the current time in bangladesh - current local time in dhaka
