Nerf N-Strike     nerf n-strike is an video game that was made by electronic arts . it is part of the nerf series . it was released on the wii and nintendo ds . the game was released in north america on october 28 , 2008. in august 2007 , electronic arts gained the rights to some hasbro brands , like `` monopoly '' , `` littlest pet shop '' , and nerf . in february , ea casual entertainment announced that their ea salt lake studio would develop and release video games for `` littlest pet shop '' and `` nerf n-strike '' . other websites . - nerf n-strike
