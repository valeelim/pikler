SMS     an sms , or short messaging service is a type of communication used in mobile phones . they are more commonly called 'text messages ' . with sms , someone can send text to another person . sms can be slow , as normal calls take priority over sending text messages on a network ( which mean that they come first ) . there is also a limit of 160 characters that a text message can send . longer messages than this will be sent as multiple messages , where the sender has to pay for each individual message . text messages are often sent between phone users when an actual phone call is not wanted or needed . for example , they may be used in a situation where silence is needed , or to send something private that one person does not want people near them to hear . message price depends on the phone network of the user . some mobile phone plans allow for unlimited texting . hindi sms
