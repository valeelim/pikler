A Song of Ice and Fire     a song of ice and fire is an award-winning series of fantasy books that were written by an american author , george r. r. martin . martin started writing the series in 1991 , and the first book came out in 1996. now there are five books in the series , and two more are planned . the series has been translated into more than twenty languages and is a best-seller , with the fourth volume reaching the top of the `` new york times '' bestseller lists when it came out . a fictional language called `` dothraki '' was created for the series . it was adapted and improved when the hbo series `` game of thrones '' was created . books . - `` a game of thrones '' ( 1996 ) - `` a clash of kings '' ( 1998 ) - `` a storm of swords '' ( 2000 ) - `` a feast for crows '' ( 2005 ) - `` a dance with dragons '' ( 2011 ) - `` the winds of winter '' ( forthcoming ) - `` a dream of spring '' ( forthcoming )
