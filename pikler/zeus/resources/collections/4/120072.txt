Rayman Raving Rabbids     rayman raving rabbids is a party video game made by ubisoft for the wii , playstation 2 , xbox 360 , microsoft windows , game boy advance , and nintendo ds game consoles . it 's a spin-off of the `` rayman '' series where rayman has to thwart the rabbids , the bad guys of the game , playing mini-games to do this . the game boy advance and ds versions are platform games like older `` rayman '' titles with less mini-games .
