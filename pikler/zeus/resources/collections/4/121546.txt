Saint-Étienne    saint-étienne saint-étienne is a city in france , located southwest of lyon . it is the prefecture of the loire department in the auvergne-rhône-alpes region . it was first mentioned in 1258 as `` sancti stephani de furanum '' . from about the 14th century , the importance of the city grew due to the arms that were made there . jules massenet was born near saint-étienne . a.s. saint-étienne is a famous football club . education . - emlyon business school saint-étienne is twinned with : - annaba ( عنّابة ) , algeria — since 1981 - banska bystrica , slovakia — since 2006 - ben arous ( بن عروس ) , tunisia — since 1994 - coventry , united kingdom — since 1955 - des moines , iowa , united states — since 1984 - ferrara , emilia-romagna , italy — since 1960 - fes , morocco — since 2006 - geltendorf , bavaria , germany — since 1966 - granby , quebec , canada — since 1960 - katowice , poland — since 1994 - luhansk ( луга́нськ ) , ukraine — since 1959 - nazareth illit ( ) , israel — since 1974 - oeiras , portugal — since 1995 - patras , greece — since 1990
