Mumia     mumia ( also pulvis mumiae , mumia vera aegyptiaca ) was a powder made from ground mummies . it was used as a drug , for good health , and as a coloring agent ( a medium to dark brown ) until the 1920s . eating ground mumified corpses is a form of cannibalism . the powder was said to work against almost any disease . to mummify a corpse , tar was used . it was said this tar had magical powers . it was also said to be an aphrodisiac .
