Queer as Folk (U.S.)    queer as folk ( u.s. ) queer as folk is an american-canadian drama television series that aired from 2000-2005 on showtime . it is a remake of a british program with the same name . the series follows the lives of several gay men living in pittsburgh , pennsylvania . due to the cost of making the series , it was actually filmed in toronto , canada . following its first episode , the series quickly became the most-viewed program on the showtime network in the united states , despite its somewhat controversial and graphic depictions of gay life .
