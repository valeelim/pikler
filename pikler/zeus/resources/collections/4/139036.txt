Churro     churros , sometimes called spanish doughnuts , are fried-dough pastry-based snacks , sometimes made from potato dough , that originated in portugal . they are popular in latin america , france , portugal , the united states , and spanish-speaking caribbean islands . there are two types of churros in spain . one is long and fat ( `` porra '' ) , and the other , extra popular in madrid , is thin and has knots . they are both eaten for breakfast or as a treat with chocolate or sugar .
