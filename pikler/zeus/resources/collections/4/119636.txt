Csaba László     csaba lászló ( born 13 february , 1964 in odorheiu secuiesc , harghita county , romania ) is a romanian-hungarian football manager and former player . in 2012 he was appointed as a head coach of lithuanian national football team . he was previously manager of scottish premier league club hearts and also had spells at ferencvárosi tc , fc sopron and the ugandan national team .
