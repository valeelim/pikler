Dysprosium     dysprosium ( ) is a chemical element with the symbol dy and atomic number 66. it has a very high melting temperature of 1,407°c . it can also absorb neutrons . this makes it very suitable for use with nuclear reactors and lasers . dysprosium is a rare earth element that has a metallic , bright silver luster . dysprosium was first identified in paris in 1886 by french chemist paul émile lecoq de boisbaudran . compounds . - fluorides : dyf - chlorides : dycl , dycl - bromides : dybr , dybr - iodides : dyi , dyi - oxides : dyo - sulfides : dys - nitrides : dyn other websites . - webelements.com – dysprosium - los alamos national laboratory – dysprosium
