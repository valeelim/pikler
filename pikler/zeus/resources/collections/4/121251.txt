Federal Information Processing Standard     federal information processing standards ( fips ) are standards made by the united states federal government for non-military government agencies and by government contractors to use , and they are announced to the public . many fips standards are modified versions of standards used in the wider community ( ansi , ieee , iso , etc . ) some fips standards were originally developed by the u.s. government . for instance , standards for encoding data ( e.g . country codes ) , but more significantly some encryption standards , such as the data encryption standard ( fips 46 ) and the advanced encryption standard ( fips 197 ) . other websites . - fips homepage - fips resource center - computer security division
