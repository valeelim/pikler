Volvo C30     volvo c30 is a car produced by volvo since 2006. it was officially unveiled at the 2006 paris motor show . it went on sale in mid-2006 in europe as a 2006 model . then it went on sale in canada in march 2007 as a 2007 model , before finally going on sale in the united states in october 2007 as a 2008 model .
