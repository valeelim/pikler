Horn of Africa     the horn of africa ( alternatively northeast africa , and sometimes somali peninsula ) is a peninsula in east africa that protrudes for hundreds of kilometres into the arabian sea , and lies along the southern side of the gulf of aden . horn of africa can also mean the greater region containing the countries of eritrea , djibouti , ethiopia and somalia . the greater region covers approximately 2,000,000 kmÂ² ( or 772,200 square miles ) and is inhabited by about 94.2 million people ( ethiopia : 79 million , somalia : 10 million , eritrea : 4.5 million , and djibouti : 0.7 million ) .
