Passer rating     passer rating ( known as passing efficiency or pass efficiency in ncaa football ) is a measure of how well a quarterback , or any other passer in american and canadian football , played . math is used to get the passer rating . to get a good passer rating , a quarterback needs to have a high number of good passes and touchdowns . a quarterback can get a bad passer rating if the quarterback has many interceptions . other websites . - nfl.com qb rating page - online qb rating calculator . nfl formula . - online passer rating calculator for nfl/cfl , ncaa and afl formulas
