Countdown to Extinction     countdown to extinction is the fifth studio album by american thrash metal band megadeth . `` countdown to extinction '' was released on july 6 , 1992. it was released through capitol records . the album debuted at # 2 on the billboard 200 and sold 128,000 copies in the first week . the album was nominated for best metal performance at the 1993 grammy awards . the title track won the humane society 's genesis award for raising awareness for animal rights issues .
