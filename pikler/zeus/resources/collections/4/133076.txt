Tooth fairy     the tooth fairy is a mythical figure found in modern folklore . around the world , families have many different traditions to celebrate the loss of a child 's tooth , especially a first tooth . many of the traditions involve throwing a tooth on a roof , under a house , burying it under a tree , or leaving it for a rodent . one of the most famous traditions is leaving a tooth under a pillow for the tooth fairy . this tradition is very popular in the united states as well as other countries . the tooth fairy is said to take the tooth away while the child is sleeping and leave some money in its place . sometimes the tooth fairy may also leave a note . if the child does not go to sleep the tooth fairy will not come . some people tell the story that the tooth fairy is named fatina and lives in neverland . the belief is that fatina creates a magic powder from children 's teeth that protects the fairies from hawks who might otherwise eat them .
