The Blister Exists     `` the blister exists '' is the final single from slipknot 's `` vol . 3 : ( the subliminal verses ) '' album . a music video for the song was released may 21st 2004 . `` the blister exists '' is known among fans for its snare drum solo . this song was used to open concerts from the subliminal verses tour , following a staged announcement where the audience was told that `` due to unforeseen circumstances , slipknot will not be performing this evening . '' the song appeared in the video game `` prince of persia : warrior within '' . the music video displays the band performing the song live , with clips of their fans with much similarity to `` the nameless '' music video . other websites . - official site
