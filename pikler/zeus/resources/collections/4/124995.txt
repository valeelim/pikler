Patuakhali District     patuakhali is a district in south-western bangladesh . it is a part of the barisal division . this district is called `` sagorkonna '' , which means daughter of sea . it is watered by the bay of bengal . there are 6 upazilas/thanas in this district . they are : 1. bauphal upazila 1. dasmina upazila 1. dumki upazila 1. galachipa upazila 1. kalapara upazila 1. mirzaganj upazila 1. patuakhali sadar upazila . agriculture is the profession of the most of the people . fishing is also a prominent profession of this district . thousands of boats go into the deep sea to fish and come back with tons of fish . paddy , jute , and different types of vegetables are the main product of agriculture sector .
