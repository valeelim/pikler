Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie     chimamanda ngozi adichie ( born september 15 , 1977 ) is an award-winning nigerian writer . she comes from abba in anambra state , southeast nigeria . her book half of a yellow sun won the 2007 orange prize for fiction . adichie was born in enugu into an igbo family . she married ivara alistair esege , a nigerian doctor . in december 2013 beyoncé released her fifth album `` beyoncé '' . the eleventh song on the album `` flawless '' has a sample from adichie 's ted talk `` why we should all be feminists '' . adichie 's sample on the album caused her novel `` americanah '' go up around 600 places in the amazon chart .
