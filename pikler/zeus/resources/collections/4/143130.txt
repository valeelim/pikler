Gatcombe     gatcombe is a village and civil parish on the isle of wight . it is four kilometres from newport in the centre of the island . st. olave 's church is a gatcombe church dedicated in 1292 , serving as chapel to gatcombe manor . the gatcombe parish was established in 1560. a carved wooden effigy of edward estur , a local knight who fought in the crusades , is in the church .
