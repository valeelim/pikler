Anhedonia     in psychology , anhedonia ( < greek αν- an- , `` without '' + ηδονή hēdonē , `` pleasure '' ) is not being able to feel pleasure . this may include not enjoying things like eating , exercise , watching movies , meeting friends , social activities , or sexual interaction and activity . in most cases , anhedonia is caused by mental disorders . it happens in some severe cases of depression and it is a common symptom of schizophrenia , schizoid personality disorder and major depressive disorder . other websites . - no pleasure , no reward - discussion of male orgasmic dysfunction ( ejaculation without orgasm ) on the sexual health forums of hisandherhealth.com
