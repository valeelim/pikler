Baccara     baccara was a spanish pop group . they were formed in 1977. it had two members : mayte mateos and maría mendiola . they were successful with their first song , `` yes sir , i can boogie '' . baccara represented luxembourg in the eurovision song contest in 1978. they performed `` parlez-vous français ? '' . in 1981 , baccara split . both mateos and mendiola then began work on music by themselves .
