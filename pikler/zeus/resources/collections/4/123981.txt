North Tyrol     north tyrol , or north tirol ( german : nordtirol ) is the main part of the austrian state of tyrol , located in the western part of the country . the other part of the state is east tyrol , which also belongs to austria , but does not share a border with north tyrol . besides those two regions , the historical region of tyrol for many centuries also included the areas today known as bolzano-bozen and trento , which were annexed by italy after world war i. by that , north tyrol and east tyrol were effectively cut of from each other . north tyrol borders salzburg in the east , the german state of bavaria in the north , vorarlberg in the west , the swiss canton of graub√ºnden in the southwest and bolzano-bozen in the south . its capital is innsbruck .
