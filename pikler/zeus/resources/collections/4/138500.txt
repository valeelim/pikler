Killing Is My Business... and Business Is Good!    killing is my business ... and business is good ! killing is my business ... and business is good ! is the first studio album by american thrash metal band megadeth . the album was released on june 12 , 1985. it was released through combat records . early in 1985 , the band was given $ 8,000 by combat records to record and produce the band 's first album . the money proved not to be enough and the band was given $ 4,000 more . most of the budget was spent on drugs , alcohol , and food , which forced the group to fire their original producer and produce the record by themselves .
