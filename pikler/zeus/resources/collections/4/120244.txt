Klaus Barbie     klaus barbie ( 25 october 1913 – 25 september 1991 ) was an ss- '' hauptsturmführer '' , soldier , and gestapo member . he is known as the `` butcher of lyon '' because of the war crimes he committed in france during world war ii . he lived in bolivia from 1951 until he was extradited ( moved by government order ) to france in 1983. from 1984-1987 , he was tried and convicted of , and sentenced to life imprisonment for , crimes against humanity . he remained in prison until he died of leukemia in lyon .
