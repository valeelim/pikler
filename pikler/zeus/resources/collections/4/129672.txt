Tourcoing     tourcoing is a town in the nord department and nord-pas de calais region in france . it is lille and roubaix and the belgian border ; its population is 94,204 ( 1999 ) . there is a napoleon iii-style `` hôtel de ville '' ( town hall ) built in 1885. twin towns . - berlin , germany - biella , italy - bottrop , germany - guimarães , portugal - jastrzebie zdroj , poland - mouscron , belgium - mühlhausen , germany - rochdale , united kingdom
