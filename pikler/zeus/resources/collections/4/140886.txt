Merrie Melodies     merrie melodies is the name of a series of animated cartoons distributed by warner bros. pictures between 1931 and 1969. the sister series to warner 's `` looney tunes '' , `` merrie melodies '' were originally one-shot musical cartoon shorts before gradually featuring recurring characters . by 1944 , no distinctions existed between the two series . originally produced by harman-ising pictures , `` merrie melodies '' were produced by leon schlesinger productions from 1933 to 1944. schlesinger sold his studio to warner bros. in 1944 , and the newly renamed warner bros. cartoons continued production until 1963. other websites . - the big cartoon database entry for `` merrie melodies ''
