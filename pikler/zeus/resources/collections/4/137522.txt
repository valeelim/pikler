Truncated differential cryptanalysis     in cryptography , truncated differential cryptanalysis is a generalization of differential cryptanalysis , an attack model against block ciphers . in 1994 lars knudsen designed the technique , during this period ordinary differential cryptanalysis analyzes the full difference between two texts , the truncated version considers differences that are only determined to some extent . it has been applied to safer , idea , skipjack , e2 , twofish , camellia , crypton , and the stream cipher salsa20 as well .
