Greenock     greenock ( gaelic `` grianaig , '' is a large town in the inverclyde council area of western scotland . greenock 's population was recorded as being 45,467 in the 2001 census , a decrease from about 78,000 in 1966. it lies on the south bank of the clyde at the `` tail of the bank '' where the river clyde expands into the firth of clyde , and is in what was the county of renfrewshire .
