Tomis Team Dobrogea Wind Farm     the tomis team dobrogea wind farm is a wind farm under construction in the dobrogea region of romania . it will consist of an individual wind farm with 300 individual wind turbines with a nominal output of around 2 mw which will deliver up to 600 mw of power , enough to power over 393,000 homes , with a capital investment required of approximately us $ 750 million . other pages . - list of large wind farms
