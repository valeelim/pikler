British Rail Class 04     the british rail class 04 0-6-0 diesel-mechanical shunting locomotive class was built between 1952 and 1962. it was the basis for the later class 03 built in the british railways workshops . the class 04 locomotives were supplied by the drewry car co. , which at the time ( and for most of its existence ) had no manufacturing capability . drewry sub-contracted the construction work to two builders . both of these built other locomotives under the same arrangement . early locomotives were built by vulcan foundry and later examples were built by robert stephenson and hawthorns .
