Yaoi (manga)    yaoi ( manga ) yaoi ( やおい ) is a term for fiction that shows homosexual male relationships but is usually created by females ( can called fujoshi too ) . most yaoi fans are female . yaoi can also show a girls relationship . this is called `` yuri '' . it is mostly created by men . some yaoi are real but most of are the yaoi 's imagination . one example of yaoi anime is `` sekaiichi hatsukoi '' .
