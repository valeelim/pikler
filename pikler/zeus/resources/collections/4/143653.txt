Mathew Brady     mathew b. brady ( 1822 – january 15 , 1896 ) was one of the most celebrated 19th century american photographers , best known for his portraits of celebrities and the documentation of the american civil war . he is credited with being the father of photojournalism . other websites . - mathewbrady.com - mathew brady biography at american memory of the library of congress - mathew brady photographs more than 6,000 photographs available in the archival research catalog of the national archives and records administration - mathew brady 's world – biography , timeline and analysis of brady 's work at the smithsonian institution
