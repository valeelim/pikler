Lanzarote     lanzarote is one of the canary islands . lanzarote is one of the two islands in the east of the canary islands . it is located 100 km west of the western african coast line and 11 km north of the neighbouring island fuerteventura . its dimensions are from east to west coast 16.5 km , from north to south 60 km . the island was created by volcano eruptions . there is only limited natural vegetation on the island . about 130,000 people live on lanzarote . the largest town on the island is arrecife . lanzarote has one international airport at arreceife ( airport code ace ) . lanzarote 's economy is mainly supported by tourism . many tourist come to the island for sunbathing , swimming , windsurfing , walking or snorkeling . there are a few agricultural producers on the island , and in particular there are some vineyards producing local wines . other websites . - web centers of art , culture and tourism of lanzarote - official lanzarote website - pictures of lanzarote
