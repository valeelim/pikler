Prahova County     prahova ( ) is a county ( judeţ ) of romania , in the historical region of muntenia . the capital of prahova county is ploieşti . prahova county has an area of 4,716 square kilometers . the main river in prahova county is the prahova river . prahova county has 2 municipalities , 12 towns and 90 communes . municipalities . - ploieşti - câmpina towns . - azuga - băicoi - boldeşti-scăeni - breaza - buşteni - comarnic - mizil - plopeni - sinaia - slǎnic - urlaţi - vălenii de munte related pages . - prahova river - prahova valley
