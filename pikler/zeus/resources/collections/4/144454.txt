Piggy bank     a piggy bank is used to collect coins . it is shaped like pig and comes in various colors and sizes . it is often used by parents to teach their children how to save money . it is not certain about why it is a called piggy bank and how it was started . some people say that `` piggy '' was `` pygg '' at first . pygg is a clay that was used to make a bowl or vase to store salt or money .
