Halesowen     halesowen is a town in west midlands of england . historically , it is part of worcestershire . the population of the town in the 2001 census was 55,273. the centre of halesowen has a norman church , st john 's church . it was built in 1083 on the site of an anglo-saxon church . it was founded by earl rodger de montgomery . the building of the spire at st john 's church started at the end of the 1st century and is shows evidence of norman origin even though there have been many alterations to it .
