Kashmiri people     the kashmiri people ( , `` koshur '' ) are a dardic ethnic group living in the central valley of kashmir between pakistan and india . they speak the kashmiri language . kashmiri is an indo-aryan language . the kashmiri are also known as koshur . there are about 5.6 million people speaking the kashmiri language . about 5.5 million of them live in jammu and kashmir , and about 100,000 in azad jammu and kashmir and the federally administered northern areas ( fana ) in pakistan .
