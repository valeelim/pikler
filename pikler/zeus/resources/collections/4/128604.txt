Mayenne (river)    mayenne ( river ) `` for other uses , see '' mayenne ( disambiguation ) la mayenne is a river in the northwest of france . its source in the orne , about 15 km west of alençon . it joins the sarthe and loire river north of angers to form the maine . it is 200 km long and its surface area is about 5 820 km² . it has an average discharge of 50 m³/s .
