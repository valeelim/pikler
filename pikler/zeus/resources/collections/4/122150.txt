Tajwal     tajwal is one of the 51 union councils of abbottabad district in the khyber pakhtunkhwa province of pakistan . tajwal is located in the south of the district and is in the north western part of havelian tehsil , it is next to the following union councils , nathia gali to the north , palak to the east , seer gharbi and nara to the south , as well as dewal manal and nara to the west . tajwal is a beautiful and scenic valley , near by changla gali en-route from murree to abbottabad . the union council of tajwal is subdivided into the following areas : nowshera , pirkot , tajwal and topla .
