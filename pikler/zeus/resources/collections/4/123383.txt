Kanchenjunga     kangchenjunga ( nepali : कञ्चनजङ्घा kanchanjaŋghā ) sewalungma ( limbu language ) is the third highest mountain in the world ( after mount everest and k2 ) . kangchenjunga has an altitude of 8,586 metres ( 28,169 ft ) . kangchenjunga translated means `` the five treasures of snows '' , as it has five peaks , four of them over 8,450 metres . it is one of the mountains in the range of the himalayas .
