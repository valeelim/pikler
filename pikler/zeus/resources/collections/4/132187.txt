Fagales     the fagales are an order of flowering plants , including some of the best known trees . the order name is derived from genus `` fagus '' , beeches . they belong among the rosid group of dicotyledons . the families currently included are as follows : - betulaceae - birch family - casuarinaceae - she-oak family - fagaceae - beech family - juglandaceae - walnut family - myricaceae - bayberry family - nothofagaceae - southern beech family - rhoipteleaceae - rhoiptelea family - ticodendraceae - ticodendron family
