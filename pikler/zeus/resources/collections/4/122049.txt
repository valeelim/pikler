Langrial     langrial is one of the 51 union councils of abbottabad district in the khyber pakhtunkhwa province of pakistan . it is in the south west of the district and borders haripur district . the union council is subdivided into the following areas : basgora , chotala , dabran , danna nooral , faqir mohammad , gali batgran , karkaki , langrial , lassan and manjia . other villages are kialapain and basara .
