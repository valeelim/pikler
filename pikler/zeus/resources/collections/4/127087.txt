Council-manager     the council-manager government is one of two main variations of representative municipal government in the united states that began in staunton , virginia in 1908. this system of government is used in the majority of american cities with populations over 12,000. when a city has a `` council-manager '' form of government , the elected city council usually hires a city manager to oversee the day-to-day management of the city employees . in these cities , the position of mayor is either a ceremonial position , or does not exist .
