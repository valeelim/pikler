Igoumenitsa     igoumenitsa is a small greek city . it has a population of about 9,644 people . it is found in the north-western part of greece in the ipiros periphery . it is a city built along the coast of the ionian sea . it has a big port which links igoumenitsa with the italian cities of bari , ancona and brintezi . it is the second important port of greece along the ionian sea , after the port in the city of patras .
