Picross DS     picross ds is a puzzle video game made by jupiter and published by nintendo for the nintendo ds game console . `` picross ds '' got many great reviews . ign gave it a 9.0/10 and an editor 's choice award . the magazine said the game was one of the most addictive games for the nintendo ds . they liked it for its downloadable puzzles , create-a-puzzle mode , and the number of puzzles to play . they said that the game 's biggest mistake was that it penalized the player for missing by adding time , but does n't count how many times the player misses .
