Argali     the argali or mountain sheep ( `` ovis ammon '' ) is a wild sheep . it lives in the highlands of central asia ( himalaya , tibet , altay ) . it is the biggest wild sheep . it stands as high as 120 cm . it weights as much as 140 kg . the pamir argali ( also called marco polo sheep , for they were first described by that traveller ) may be more than 6 feet in length . it is globally endangered .
