Dokri     dokri ( ) is a town in larkana district , sindh province of pakistan and is the capital town of the dokri taluka . moenjodaro is found 7 km away in east-south of the dokri town . the town has been the point of several communist movements in the past . dokri was once called `` lab-e-darya '' or a `` lip of the indus '' , the name is believed to have been taken from `` dhaatki '' , or `` ‘ old woman ’ '' .
