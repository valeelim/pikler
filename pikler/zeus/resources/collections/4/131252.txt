Anemoi     in greek mythology , the anemoi ( in greek , άνεμοι – `` winds '' ) were wind gods . boreas was the greek god of the cold north wind and the bringer of winter . notus was the greek god of the south wind . he was had hot wind and was thought to bring the storms of late summer and autumn . he was feared as a destroyer of crops . eurus was the greek god of the unlucky east wind . zephyrus , or just zephyr , is the greek god of the west wind . this is the gentlest of the winds .
