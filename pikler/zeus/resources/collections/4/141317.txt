Tollywood     telugu cinema ( telugu : తెలుగు సినీపరిశ్రమ ) , refers to the cinema in telugu language , sometimes referred as tollywood , a portmanteau of `` telugu '' and `` hollywood '' . tollywood ( telugu movies ) refers to the movies produced in andhra pradesh and telengana states based on telugu film industry in the republic of india : the origins of that name , tollywood a portmanteau of the words tollygunge and hollywood are disputed .
