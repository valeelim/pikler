Walter Jones     walter junior jones ( born january 19 , 1974 ) is an american football offensive tackle for the seattle seahawks team in the national football league . he was selected by the seattle seahawks with the sixth overall pick in the first round of the 1997 nfl draft , a pick obtained from the tampa bay buccaneers in a trade . jones left florida state university prior to his senior season after being selected to the second team college football all-america team . on april 29 , 2010 , he announced on his twitter account that he was retiring . other websites . - walter jones on twitter
