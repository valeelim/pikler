Bay leaf     bay leaf ( plural bay leaves ) , ( greek `` daphni '' , romanian `` foi de dafin '' ) is the aromatic leaf of several species of the laurel family ( lauraceae ) . fresh or dried bay leaves are used in cooking for their distinctive flavor and fragrance . - `` laurus nobilis '' , is a culinary herb often used to flavor soups , stews , and braises and pâtés in mediterranean cuisine . the fresh leaves are very mild and do not develop their full flavor until several weeks after picking and drying . - california bay leaf - `` indian bay leaf '' ( also `` tej pat '' , `` tejpat '' , `` tejpata '' तेजपत्ता or `` tamalpatra '' तमालपत्र )
