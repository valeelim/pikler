British Rail Class 309     the british rail class 309 `` clacton express '' electric multiple units ( emus ) were built by british rail ( br ) york works from 1962-1963. they were initially classified as class am9 before the introduction of tops . these units were the first express 25 kv alternating current ( ac ) units to be built by british rail , and british rail 's first emus to be capable of 100 mph .
