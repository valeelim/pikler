Miep Gies     miep gies , née hermine santrouschitz ( born 15 february 1909 ; died 11 january 2010 ) , was one of the dutch citizens who hid anne frank and her family from the nazis during world war ii . she found and kept anne 's diary after anne frank was found and sent away by the nazis . although gies did not read the diary ( as she gave it to otto frank immediately after recovering it from the annexe and thought it would be rude to read ) , it has become very successful worldwide .
