Superb lyrebird     the superb lyrebird ( `` menura novaehollandiae '' ) is a pheasant-sized songbird . it is an australian endemic species , about 100cm long . it has brown upper body plumage , grayish-brown below , rounded wings and strong legs . it is the longest and third heaviest of all songbirds . they are capable of mimicking a wide variety of sounds , including other bird 's songs and man-made sounds like car alarms and chainsaws .
