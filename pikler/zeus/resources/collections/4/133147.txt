The Used     the used are an american post hardcore band . they formed in the year 2000 and have released a number of albums since then . their first album was called `` the used '' . their latest album is called `` vulnerable '' , and was released in 2012. they have always been signed to one record label named reprise . in 2004 , singer bert mccracken 's girlfriend at the time was pregnant with their child and she died from a drug overdose . for that reason , their second album was named `` in love and death '' . around that time , the band also took on a new drummer .
