Furius ISO Mount     furius iso mount is a program to mount and burn iso files . features . - ability to mount iso , img , bin , mdf and nrg image files - automatically creates a mount point in the users home directory . - automatically saves the history of the last 10 images mounted . - ability to burn iso and img files to optical disks - ability to generate md5 and sha1 checksums - automatically retrieves any previously unmounted images . - automatically generates a log file of all commands needed to mount and unmount images manually .
