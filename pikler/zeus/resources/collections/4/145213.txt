Manta ray     the manta ray ( `` manta birostris '' ) is the largest species of the rays . the largest known specimen was more than across , with a weight of about . it ranges throughout tropical waters of the world , typically around coral reefs . they have the largest brain-to-body ratio of the sharks , rays and skates ( elasmobranchii ) , a brain which is kept warm during lengthy dives to as deep as in cold water . mantas may be at least two different species , the giant manta ( `` manta birostris '' ) , which migrates , and another smaller one called the reef manta ( `` manta alfredi '' ) , which does not . the genus may need revising . manta rays are probably at the top of the food chain . some shark species , such as the tiger shark may hunt them .
