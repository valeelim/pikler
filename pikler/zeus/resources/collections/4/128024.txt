Woolly Flying Squirrel     the woolly flying squirrel ( `` eupetaurus cinereus '' ) is a squirrel . it is the only species of the genus `` eupetaurus '' . until recently scientific knowledge of this rare species was limited to 11 skins collected in the late nineteenth century . however , recent research has confirmed that it is still extant in pakistan-administered kashmir ( p.a.k ) . it is the longest member of the family sciuridae and the most massive gliding animal known , but observations confirm that despite its size , it does glide effectively like other flying squirrels .
