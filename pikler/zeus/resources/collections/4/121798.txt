Windhaven     windhaven is a science-fiction and fantasy book that was written by george r.r . martin and lisa tuttle . the book is about events that take place in a fictional space colony called windhaven . the novel is a collection of short stories . simon & schuster collected the stories and published them for the first time in 1981. bantam spectra published it again in hardcover in 2001 and in mass market paperback in 2003 .
