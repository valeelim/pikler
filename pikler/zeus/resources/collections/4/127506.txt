Isaac Slade     isaac slade is an american singer-songwriter . he is a member of the rock band called the fray . he was born in may 1981. he has released several albums with the band `` the fray '' including `` how to save a life '' and `` live at the electric factory '' . he records and performs more of a piano rock and acoustic . singles include `` how to save a life '' , `` look after you '' , and `` cable car '' .
