Martung     martung , mārtung or martoong is a highland village and sub tehsil of shangla district , khyber pakhtunkhwa province ( former ) , pakistan . the center of martung ( koozkalai ) is 982 meters above sea level . some parts of martung are more than 1800 meters above mean sea level . some of martung 's important villages are kabalgram , pishlor , titwalan , koozkalai , manzkalai , shaga and karindara .
