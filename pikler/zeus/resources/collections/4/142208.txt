Galago     galagos , also known as bushbabies or ( meaning `` little night monkeys '' in afrikaans ) , are small , nocturnal primates native to continental africa . there are 21 species , and they form the family galagidae . range varies with species but includes most of the forested and bush regions of africa south of the sahara including some of the nearby islands such as zanzibar . their range does not extend to madagascar . they are quite successful . in both variety and abundance , the bush babies are the most successful prosimian primates in africa . galagos are all arboreal and known for their ability to leap great distances between branches . galagos are omnivorous but primarily insectivorous . their favorite food is grasshoppers , but they also eat small birds , eggs , fruits , seeds and flowers . euoticus has specialized front teeth for removing tree bark so it can feed on tree gum .
