Johnny Marr     johnny marr ( born john martin maher on 31 october 1963 is an english guitarist , keyboardist , harmonica player , and singer . marr was born in ardwick , manchester to irish immigrants . he wanted to be a football player . he rose to fame in the 1980s as the guitarist in the smiths , where he formed an influential songwriting partnership with morrissey . in 2013 he was given nme 's godlike genius award . he released his first solo album , `` the messenger , '' in 2013 .
