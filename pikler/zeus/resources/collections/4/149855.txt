Asnières-sur-Saône     asnières-sur-saône is a commune . it is found in the auvergne-rhône-alpes region in the ain department in the east of france . in 2009 , 73 people lived in the commune . the inhabitants are the `` asniérois '' . famous people from asnières-sur-saône include josé mingret , a french painter in the 20th century who was inhumated there . the commune has been twinned with bad waldsee in germany since 1991 .
