International Bluegrass Music Association     the international bluegrass music association , or ibma , is a trade association to promote bluegrass music . formed in 1985 , ibma established its first headquarters in owensboro , kentucky . in 1988 they announced plans to create the international bluegrass music museum as a joint venture with riverpark center in owensboro . in 1990 ibma established the world of bluegrass , a combination trade show , concert , and awards presentation . this was originally set in owensboro , before moving to louisville , kentucky in 1997. nashville , tennessee hosted this event in 2005 and 2006 , and will continue as the host location in the future . in 1991 ibma established the international bluegrass music hall of honor at the international bluegrass music museum to recognize lifetime contributions to bluegrass , both by performers and non-performers . in 2003 ibma relocated its offices to nashville , tennessee . other websites . - ibma : http : //www.ibma.org
