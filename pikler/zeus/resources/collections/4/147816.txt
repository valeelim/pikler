WWE Backlash     backlash was a professional wrestling pay-per-view event made by world wrestling entertainment , which started in 1999 and was held in april ( but in 2005 , it was held in may ) . from 2004 to 2006 , only wwe raw superstars appeared at backlash . the 2007 event was the first wwe ppv event since judgment day 2003 ( other than wrestlemania , summerslam , royal rumble , and survivor series ) to feature all three brands ( raw , smackdown ! and ecw ) after wwe stopped with brand-only events . the last backlash ppv was in 2009. it was replaced by extreme rules in 2010. other websites . - official backlash website - backlash results at prowrestlinghistory.com
