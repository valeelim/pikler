Laurencekirk     laurencekirk is a small town in the ancient county of kincardineshire , modern county of aberdeenshire , scotland , just off the a90 dundee to aberdeen main road . it is the largest settlement in the howe o ' the mearns area and houses the local secondary school ; mearns academy , which was awarded the charter mark in 2003. the 2004 population is estimated at 3,596. other websites . - laurencekirk
