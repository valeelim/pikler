Ratodero     ratodero is the capital of ratodero taluka a `` sub-division '' of larkana district in the sindh province of pakistan . it is located some 30 km away from the district capital larkana . ratodero taluka is administratively subdivided into 9 union councils two of which form the town of ratodero these are : - ratodero-i - ratodero-ii there have been many people from ratodero including two former prime ministers - zulfikar and benazir bhutto .
