Mike Adamle     michael david adamle ( born october 4 , 1949 ; pronounced ) is a sports personality and former national football league player . in college he became a big ten most valuable player . he is still thought of as one of the great players for northwestern university . after a successful career with the national football league , adamle began working for nbc sports . he then worked for a variety of programs . he was host of `` american gladiators '' and worked for world wrestling entertainment . he has worked for wmaq-tv in chicago since 2004. in february 2017 , adamle was diagnosed with dementia .
