Bakerhill, Alabama    bakerhill , alabama bakerhill or baker hill is a city in barbour county , alabama , united states , near eufaula . according to the 2005 u.s. census estimates , the city had a population of 321. although it existed as an unincorporated village since before 1860 , the city was officially incorporated in 2000. bakerhill is located at 31.88 degrees north , 85.15 degrees west ( 31.88495 , -85.15390 ) .
