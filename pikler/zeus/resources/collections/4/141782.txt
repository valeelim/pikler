Red deer     the red deer , `` cervus elaphus '' , is one of the largest deer species . the red deer lives in most of europe , the caucasus mountains region , asia minor and parts of western and central asia , in kashmir as the state animal . they can also be found in the atlas mountains region between algeria and tunisia in northwestern africa . they are the only species of deer in africa . red deer have been introduced to other areas including australia , new zealand and argentina . in many parts of the world the meat ( venison ) from red deer is widely used as a food source .
