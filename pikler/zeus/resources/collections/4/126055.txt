She Will Be Loved     `` she will be loved '' is the third single off the debut album by rock band maroon 5 called `` songs about jane '' . the song is also the fourth track off the album . the song was released in june 2004. the video features adam levine ( lead singer of maroon 5 ) who falls in love with his girlfriend 's mother . she is played by kelly preston and is being victimized by her husband . the band is briefly shown in the video . track list . 1 . `` she will be loved '' 1 . `` this love '' ( kanye west remix ) 1 . `` closer '' ( live acoustic version ) `` she will be loved '' ranked no . 4 on the u.s. `` billboard '' hot adult contemporary tracks and no . 5 on the u.s. `` billboard '' hot 100 .
