Sanghar     sanghar ( ) is a city in the sindh province of pakistan . the city is headquarters of sanghar district and sanghar taluka ( a subdivision of the district ) . it is an agricultural town about 265km from karachi . unfortunately , there is no railway station and airport in the city . but the city does have road links with hyderabad , nawabshah , mirpur khas and other major cities of sindh . other websites . - district government sanghar
