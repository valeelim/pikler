Macron Stadium     macron stadium is a football stadium in the town of horwich , near bolton , in the county of greater manchester , england . it is the current home stadium of bolton wanderers f.c.. it opened in 1997 as reebok stadium , named after the club 's former sponsor , the sportswear company reebok . in 2014 , after the sponsorship deal with reebok ended , a new deal was signed with italian sportswear company macron , and the stadium was renamed accordingly . on the stadium , there is the 188 from their current sponsor , 188bet . the team is in the football league championship .
