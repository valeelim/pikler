Iowa (Slipknot album)    iowa ( slipknot album ) iowa is the second studio album by american heavy metal band slipknot . it was released on august 28 , 2001. the producers were ross robinson and the band itself . the album 's title came from the band 's home state iowa . singles include `` left behind '' and `` my plague '' . singles of `` iowa '' . - 2001 - `` left behind '' - 2002 - `` my plague ''
