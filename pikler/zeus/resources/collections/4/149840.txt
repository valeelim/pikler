Ambléon     ambléon is a commune in the auvergne-rhône-alpes region in the ain department in the east of france . in 2009 , 120 people lived in the commune . the inhabitants are the `` ambléonais '' . the local economy is mostly based around agriculture and wine . the most important monuments in the commune are a 13th-century chapel and two private castles from the 15th and 16th centuries . famous people from ambléon include théodore chavanton , author of `` l'oasis perdue '' ( 1945 ) . he was a school teacher in the commune .
