750     events . by geography . asia . - after the defeat of his army in the battle of the zab the last umayyad caliph , marwan ii , is overthrown and killed . al-saffah is proclaimed the first abbasid caliph . the capital of the caliphate will later moved from damascus to baghdad , within the territory of the former persian empire ; this transfer will prove to be a momentous event for baghdad , which develops into a centre of international trade and culture . africa . - the ghana empire begins .
