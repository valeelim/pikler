Key distribution center     in cryptography , a key distribution center ( kdc ) is part of a cryptosystem intended to minimize the risks of exchanging keys and shared secrets . kdcs often operate in systems when some users may have permission to use certain resources at some times but not at any times . - benefits : easier key distribution and scalability . - drawbacks : a kdc can become a single point of failure and every user must trust the kdc administrators other websites . - kerberos 5 kdc operation
