Hispar Pass     hispar pass ( or hispar la ) ( el . 5,128 m./16,824 ft. ) is a high-altitude , non-technical mountain pass in the karakoram range in pakistan . at the pass , the biafo glacier ( 63 km . long ) and hispar glacier ( 49 km long ) meet to form the world 's longest glacial traverse outside of the polar regions , 100 kilometers in length .
