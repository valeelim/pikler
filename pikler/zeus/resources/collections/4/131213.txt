Alternative metal     alternative metal is a type of heavy metal in the alternative music genre . alternative metal became popular during the 1990s . characteristics of alternative metal music are heavy guitar riffs , syncopation , uncommon time signatures , and unusual techniques . alternative metal is like regular heavy music , but it can be more experimental . this is what makes it `` alternative '' . popular alternative metal bands are jane 's addiction , faith no more , primus , helmet , alice in chains , nine inch nails , tool , rage against the machine and system of a down .
