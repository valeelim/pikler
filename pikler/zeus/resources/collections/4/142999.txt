Portlethen     portlethen is a town 7 mi south of aberdeen , scotland along the a90 . it has expanded rapidly over the last 30 years . portlethen has a population of about 7000 , and more than 800 houses are , as of 2007 , were being built on nearby farmland . to the east of portlethen lie three fishing villages : findon , downies and old portlethen ( now often referred to as `` portlethen village '' ) . although portlethen is a town in terms of population ( and it has been granted official town status ) , it resembles a residential suburb without a clear 'town centre ' or focal point .
