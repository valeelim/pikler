Bargeddie     bargeddie ( ) is a small town in north lanarkshire , scotland , east of the junction of the m73 and m8 motorways , west of coatbridge town centre . a location in the same area is named in timothy pont 's map of 1595 , but it appears to be originally spelt as balgedy . former scottish football player and arsenal manager , george graham was born at bargeddie on 30 november 1944 .
