Muhammad Rafiq Tarar     muhammad rafiq tarar ( , born november 2 , 1929 ) was president of pakistan from january 1 , 1998 until june 20 , 2001. during pakistan 's independence in 1947 , rafiq tarar worked as a relief worker in camps set up by muslim students federation for refugees , migrating from british india to pakistan . he belongs to the tarar jatt clan of pakistani punjab .
