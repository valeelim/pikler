Secret Diary of a Call Girl     secret diary of a call girl ( also known as secret diary ) is a british television drama broadcast on itv2 starring billie piper as `` belle '' , a high-class london-based call girl . the story is based on the blogs of belle de jour . the first 8 part series began on 27 september 2007 at 10pm . the second 8 part series began on 11 september 2008 at 10pm .
