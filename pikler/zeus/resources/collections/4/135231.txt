Asparagales     asparagales is an order of flowering plants . the order must include the family asparagaceae , but other families included in the order have varied markedly between different classifications . no one is sure , but it is supposed that this group of plants evolved between the late and early cretaceous . but because of the difficult classification of the families , it is not entirely certain when they evolved . asparagales have a large number of families , including ashphodelaceae ( xanthorrhoeaceae ) , which includes daylilies and new zealand flax .
