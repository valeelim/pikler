Wii Chess     wii chess is a video game for the wii console . it was made by nintendo . the game was released on january 18 , 2008 in europe . under the name tsuushin taikyoku : world chess , it was released as a downloadable wiiware title in japan on september 30 , 2008. the game is played using the wii remote . the game features an option that gives players new to chess instructions on how each piece should move across the board . players are also able to replay their games at a later time . players can also play online against other players with the nintendo wi-fi connection , with wii chess players being able to compete against players of world chess and wii chess players play against world chess players .
