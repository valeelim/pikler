Ubayd Allah al-Mahdi Billah     ubayd allah al-mahdi billah a.k.a said ibn husayn ( arabic : عبيد الله بن الحسين المهدي ) is considered the founder of the fatimid dynasty , the only major shi'ite caliphate in islam . he established fatimid rule throughout much of north africa . he ruled from 909 to 934. after establishing himself as the first imam of the fatimid dynasty he made claim to genealogic origins dating as far back as fatimah , the daughter of the prophet of islam , muhammad , through husayn , fatimah 's son , and ismail . it was at this time as well that he changed his name to ubaydallah al-mahdi . links and references . - imam al-mahdi , the 11th fatimi imam . - institute of ismaili studies london .
