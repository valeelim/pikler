Quincy, Massachusetts    quincy , massachusetts quincy is a city in norfolk county in the u.s. state of massachusetts . it is part of metropolitan boston . it is named after colonel john quincy , who was abigail adams ' mother 's father . quincy also had adams ' son , john quincy adams , named after him . it is the birthplace of john adams , the second u.s. president , and adams ' son , john quincy adams , the sixth u.s. president . it was settled in 1625 and officially founded in 1792 .
