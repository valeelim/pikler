Graveson     graveson is a commune of 3,836 people ( 2008 ) . it is found in the region provence-alpes-côte d'azur in the bouches-du-rhône department in the south of france . the village is very attractive for holidaymakers , because it is located between arles and avignon . neighbour villages are châteaurenard , maillane and rognonas . graveson is birthplace of the painter auguste chabaud .
