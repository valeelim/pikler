Santiago de Compostela     santiago de compostela ( also saint james of compostela ) is the capital of the autonomous community of galicia . it is a united nations educational , scientific and cultural organization ( unesco ) world heritage site . the city is in the northwest of spain in the province of a coruña . the city 's cathedral is the destination of an important pilgrimage route , the way of st. james ( galician : `` camiño de santiago '' , spanish : `` camino de santiago '' ) . pilgrims have been following this route since the ninth century . the cathedral is believed to be where james , son of zebedee is buried . saint james was one of the apostles of jesus christ .
