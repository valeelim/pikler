Harmukh Mountain     harmukh is a branch of great himalayan range which is between the valleys of jhelum and neelum in the north eastern part of kashmir valley to the north of srinagar . excepting the mornings and evening , the mountain is always covered by clouds . name of the 16980 feet high peak is also harmukh . the peak is square shaped and can be viewed from all the sides therefore the name `` harmukh '' . sarbalnag spring is on the top of harmukh mountain .
