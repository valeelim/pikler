Letterkenny     letterkenny ( ) is a town in county donegal of the ireland . it is on the river swilly and has a population of 17,586. in 1824 , when the first description of letterkenny as a modern town was written , it was stated that : `` within half a mile is the port of ballyraine , whither vessels of 100 tons bring iron , salt and colonial produce and whence they export hides and butter '' . nothing is left of the port now except the warehouses . letterkenny achieved town status in the early 1920s following the partition of ireland .
