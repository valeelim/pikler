Alexander III of Scotland     alexander iii ( mediaeval gaelic : `` alaxandair mac alaxandair '' ; modern gaelic : `` alasdair mac alasdair '' ) , king of scots , was born at roxburgh on 4 september 1241 and died on 19 march , 1286. he was the only son of alexander ii with his second wife , marie de coucy . alexander 's father died on 6 july 1249 and he officially became king at the age of eight . this happened at scone on 13 july , 1249 .
