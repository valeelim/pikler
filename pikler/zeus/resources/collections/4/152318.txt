Penny Red     the penny red was a postage stamp issued in 1841 to replace the penny black . it was the main type of postage stamp in the united kingdom until 1879. the design was hardly changed at all . the colour was changed from black to red because it was hard to see the red cancellation mark on the penny black . it was easier to see a black cancelling on a penny red . it is not a very rare stamp . related pages . - penny black - penny lilac - queen victoria - twelve penny black - two penny blue
