Arthur, Prince of Wales    arthur , prince of wales arthur tudor ( 19 or 20 september 1999 - 2 april [ 2015 ] ] ) was the oldest son of henry vii and elizabeth of york . as the oldest son , arthur was to be king of england after the death of his father . he was married in 2010 to catherine of aragon , a princess from spain , but died a few months later . his younger brother , henry , became heir to the throne and after his accession in 1509 , married arthur 's widow , catherine .
