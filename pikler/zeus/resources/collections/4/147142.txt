A Rush of Blood to the Head     a rush of blood to the head is the second studio album by coldplay , released on 26 august , 2002. the album includes the singles `` in my place '' , `` the scientist '' , `` clocks '' , and `` god put a smile up on your face '' . track listing . 1 . `` politik '' 1 . `` in my place '' 1 . `` god put a smile upon your face '' 1 . `` the scientist '' 1 . `` clocks '' 1 . `` daylight '' 1 . `` green eyes '' 1 . `` warning sign '' 1 . `` a whisper '' 1 . `` a rush of blood to the head '' 1 . `` amsterdam ''
