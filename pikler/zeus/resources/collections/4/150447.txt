Walnut Ridge, Arkansas    walnut ridge , arkansas walnut ridge is a city of the state of arkansas in the united states of america . it has a population of 4,925. in 1964 , the beatles visited the city at the height of their popularity . it was a brief stop on the beatles ' american tour . they landed at the city 's airport to spend the weekend at a local dude ranch . the city has built a sculpture to commemorate the visit . the city also holds a festival to remember the beatles ' visit . johnny cash and elvis presley performed in the city early in their careers . the town is located on u.s. route 67 which is named `` rock 'n roll highway .
