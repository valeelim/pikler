4 Baruch     4 baruch , also called `` paraleipomena of jeremiah '' ( `` paralipomena jeremiae '' ) , contains the rest of the words of baruch and the epistle of jeremy . this religious text is said to be a part of the old testament by the ethiopian orthodox church . other christian denominations do not include in the bible . related pages . - book of baruch - 2 baruch - 3 baruch
