George Clinton (musician)    george clinton ( musician ) george clinton ( born july 22 , 1941 ) is an american musician . he was involved in funk music and created a genre called p-funk . he also worked as a record producer for bands including red hot chili peppers . he was also part of the bands parliament and funkadelic during the 1970s and early 1980s . from 1981 , he performed by himself as a solo artist . he has been called one of the most important innovators of funk music , next to james brown and sly stone . clinton is a member of the rock and roll hall of fame , inducted in 1997 with fifteen other members of parliament-funkadelic .
