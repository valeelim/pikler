Savory (herb)    savory ( herb ) satureja is a genus of aromatic plants of the family lamiaceae , related to rosemary and thyme . there are about 30 species called savories , of which summer savory and winter savory are the most important in cultivation . selected species . - `` satureja acinos '' - `` satureja alpina '' - `` satureja coerulea '' - `` satureja cuneifolia '' - `` satureja douglasii '' – yerba buena ( syn . `` s. chamissonis '' ) - `` satureja hortensis '' – summer savory - `` satureja mexicana '' - `` satureja montana '' – winter savory - `` satureja multiflora '' – chilean shrub mint - `` satureja palmeri '' ( believed extinct ; rediscovered 2001 ) - `` satureja rumelica '' - `` satureja spicigera '' - `` satureja thymbra '' - `` satureja viminea '' – serpentine savory - `` satureja viminea '' - `` satureja vulgaris '' – wild basil
