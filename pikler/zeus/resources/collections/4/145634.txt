Live at Wembley '86     live at wembley '86 is a set of two discs recorded by english rock band queen . it was recorded live on saturday july 12 , 1986 during the `` magic tour '' at wembley stadium in london , england and was released on may 26 , 1992. a dvd was released in june 2003. the album was re-sold in a new cd with bonus tracks in august 2003 in the united states as live at wembley stadium after the dvd .
