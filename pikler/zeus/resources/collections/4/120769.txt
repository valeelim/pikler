Felipe Massa     felipe massa ( born april 25 1981 in são paulo ) is a brazilian formula one racing driver . in the 2008 racing season he was working for the ferrari team . his contract allows him to continue driving for that racing team until 2010. in 2008 he is a co-driver of kimi räikkönen . massa was hurt in the 2009 grand prix of hungary qualifying session . he missed the remainder of the 2009 season , but was back for the season . season in progress .
