Khamosh Pani     khamosh pani ( , , ) is a 2003 french/german production about a widowed mother and her young son set in a late 1970s village in punjab , pakistan which is coming under radical influence . the movie was released in india too . it was shot in a village in pakistan and won 7 awards , including best film , best actress and best direction at the 56th locarno international film festival , switzerland , august 18 , 2003 .
