Felching     felching is a human sexual practice in which one person sucks semen or other fluids out of the vagina or anus of a another person . the acts of sucking the semen and then passing it from the mouth of one person to a third person is called snowballing . however , snowballing usually relates to semen ejaculated in the mouth following fellatio . felching can also mean the licking or sucking of another person 's anus , similar to the act of a rimjob ( analingus ) .
