Hearth     a hearth ( har-th ) is a brick or stone lined fireplace or oven that is used for cooking and heating . historically , the hearth was the center of the home , as it provided heat for the house . its latin name is `` focus '' . that is why `` focus '' is used for many kinds of central point . in more modern times , the hearth may be section of the fireplace that can be seen . this often includes tiles around the base of the fireplace and the floor .
