Emily Browning     emily jane browning ( born 7 december 1988 ) is an australian film actress and singer . browning made her film start in the australian television film `` the echo of thunder , '' later , she played recurring roles in australian television shows `` blue heelers '' and `` something in the air . '' her breakthrough role was in the 2002 horror film `` ghost ship , '' which introduced her to a wider audience . in 2005 , browning won the australian film institute international award for best actress for her portrayal of violet baudelaire in the comedy film `` lemony snicket 's a series of unfortunate events '' ( 2004 ) . browning is also known for her roles in the horror film `` the uninvited '' ( 2009 ) , the action film `` sucker punch '' ( 2011 ) , and the independent film `` sleeping beauty '' ( 2011 ) . her role in `` sleeping beauty '' was highly praised and in 2011 was named the breakthrough performer of the year by hamptons international film festival .
