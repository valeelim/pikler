Richard Branson     sir richard charles nicholas branson ( born 18 july 1950 in surrey , england ) is an english businessman . he is the chairman of the virgin group , a group of companies including virgin atlantic airways and virgin records . branson started virgin galactic , a private company that is trying to get people in space . according to forbes , branson was the 245th richest person in the world in 2008. branson has been diagnosed with attention-deficit hyperactivity disorder . he lives on an island he owns in the caribbean .
