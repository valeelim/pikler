Bakot     bakot is one of the 51 union councils of abbottabad district in the khyber pakhtunkhwa province of pakistan . bakot was badly affected by the 2005 pakistan earthquake , as the place where the earthquake happened was nearby . bakot is in the east of abbottabad district and is next to the muzaffarabad and bagh districts of kashmir . it is also next the following union councils within abbottabad district : to the north by nambal , to the south by berote kalan and to the west by phalkot , namli maira , nagri bala . the union council of bakote is made up of three areas these are : bakot , moolia and sagnal .
