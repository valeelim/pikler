Jaal (1973 movie)    jaal ( 1973 movie ) jaal is a 1973 pakistani urdu colour movie . it is a musical and romantic movie . it was produced by waheed murad and directed by iftikhar khan . it was the first colour movie that was produced by waheed murad . the movie was released on 31 august 1973. it starred waheed murad , nisho , husna , shaista qaiser and nanna . it was the second movie of waheed murad with nisho , first being mulaqat ( released during the same year ) .
