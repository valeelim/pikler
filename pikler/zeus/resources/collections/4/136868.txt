John Ashcroft     john david ashcroft ( born may 9 , 1942 ) is an american politician . he was the 79th united states attorney general . he served during the first term of president george w. bush from 2001 until 2005. ashcroft was previously the governor of missouri ( 1985–1993 ) and a u.s. senator from missouri ( 1995–2001 ) . ashcroft was born in chicago , illinois .
