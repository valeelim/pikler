Brassicaceae     brassicaceae is a family of plants including mustard , wasabi , and horseradish . they are also called the mustard family , or cabbage family . some plants in this family are hot when you eat them , so they are used as spices . all of them disturb the nose more than the tongue . another group includea the cabbages , where there are broccoli , cauliflower , and cabbage . another name for this family is cruciferae . the flowers of this family have four petals and look like a cross . the name comes from the genus in it , brassica .
