GNU Parted     gnu parted is a free computer program for creating , destroying , resizing , checking , and copying partitions , and the file systems on them . this is useful for creating space for new operating systems , reorganising hard disk usage , copying data between hard disks , and disk imaging . it was written by andrew clausen and lennert buytenhek . gparted is a graphical program using the parted libraries . it is adapted for gtk+ . it is often included as utility on many live cd distributions to make partitioning easier .
