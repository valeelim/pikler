Santa Cruz, Bolivia    santa cruz , bolivia santa cruz de la sierra , also known as santa cruz , is the largest city in bolivia , capital city of the santa cruz department ( in the eastern region of the country ) . the municipality 's population was 1,528,683 inhabitants , and the urban population was 1,545,161. santa cruz is not only the most populated city in bolivia , but the department is also the richest , with over 30 % of the national gdp . the city is a major center of petrochemical production in bolivia , especially because the production of natural gas that exports to neighboring countries . the main sectors that drive the economy are the oil , forestry and agrobusiness firms .
