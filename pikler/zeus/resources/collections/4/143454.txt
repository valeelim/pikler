Brewton, Alabama    brewton , alabama brewton is a city in escambia county , alabama , united states . at the 2000 census the population was 5,498. the city is the county seat of escambia county . the city government consists of mayor who is elected at large and five member city council who are each elected from a district . schools in the brewton city school system : - brewton elementary school , on douglas ave. - brewton middle school , on liles blvd . - t. r. miller high school , on douglas ave. brewton is home to jefferson davis community college , a small postsecondary school that primarily gives associate 's degrees . the annual alabama blueberry festival is held at burnt corn creek park and downtown brewton . the thomas e. mcmillan museum is on the campus of the jefferson davis community college . notable natives or residents . - wayne frazier , pro football player , auburn university , - teddy keaton , football coach , stillman college - kevin sumlin , football coach , texas a & m aggies football
