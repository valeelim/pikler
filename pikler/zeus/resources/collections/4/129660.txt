TobyMac     kevin michael `` toby '' mckeehan ( born october 22 , 1964 in fairfax , virginia ) , better known as tobymac , is a well known christian singer from the band dc talk . the band separated in 2000 to pursue solo careers . some of his most notable songs are `` made to love '' , `` burn for you '' , `` boomin '' , `` city on our knees '' , `` me without you '' , and `` feel it '' . he has also released a live album , `` alive and transported '' .
