Takht-e-Nasrati Tehsil     takht-e-nasarati is an administrative subdivision ( tehsil ) of karak district in khyber pakhtunkhwa , pakistan . the town of takht-e-nasrati is the headquarters of the tehsil . the tehsil is administratively divided into 7 union councils - one of which makes up the tehsil capital . these are : - ahmad abad - chowkara - jehangiri - mianki banda - shanawa godi khel - siraj khel - takht-e-nasrati - shaheedan banda
