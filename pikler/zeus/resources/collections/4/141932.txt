White-crested Kalij pheasant     the white-crested kalij pheasant `` ( lophura leucomelanos hamiltonii ) '' is one sub-species of the kalij pheasant ( lophura leucomelanos ) , western most subspecies of lophura leucomelanos ; western himalayas in parts of northern india , as well some areas of western nepal and northern-western pakistan , where it is the provincial/regional bird of the afghania province .
