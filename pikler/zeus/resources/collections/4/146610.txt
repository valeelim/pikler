Angus     angus is a county on the north sea and on the northern shore of the firth of tay in scotland , united kingdom . angus is also the name of a council area . the county town of angus is forfar , but its largest town is dundee . dundee is not part of the council area but is a council area on its own . angus is also well known for its breed of cattle much used in beef production . the angus cattle were developed from cattle native to the counties of aberdeenshire and angus , and are now found in most parts of the world .
