Amritsar     amritsar ( , meaning : `` the lake of the holy nectar '' , ) is the capital of the amritsar district in the state of punjab , india . according to the 2001 indian census , over 1,500,000 people lived in the city . amritsar is in the state of punjab , india , 32 km east of lahore , pakistan . the harimandir sahib , also known as the golden temple , is there . the temple is the centre of the sikh religion . the city has been famous for many events in history . in the jallianwala bagh massacre of 1919 under british rule a large number of people were killed by british troops . the city is also famous for operation bluestar , a military operation in 1984 that was ordered by the prime minister of india of the time , indira gandhi . many sikhs were angered , as the holy golden temple of the sikhs was damaged .
