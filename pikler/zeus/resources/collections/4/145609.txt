James Skinner (soldier)    james skinner ( soldier ) james skinner cb ( 1778 – december 4 , 1841 ) was an anglo-indian military adventurer in india . skinner was born in india , son of lieutenant-colonel hercules skinner and a rajput lady . because of his indian heritage , skinner was unable to serve as an officer in the east india company army and , at the age of eighteen , he entered the mahratta army under benoît de boigne , where he soon showed military talent . he remained in the same service under pierre cuillier-perron until 1803 , when , on the outbreak of the second anglo-maratha war all anglo-indians were dismissed from mahratta service .
