Heuriger     heuriger is a word that describes the wine of the current year , and the places which serve it . according to a law of 1784 , the winemakers may serve their own current wine , without a special license . these restaurants are licensed according to laws of the bundesland . in vienna these laws date from 1975. very often , these establishments also serve cheese and sausages , other meat products , certain kinds of fish , mixed pickles , eggs , various spreads ( to put on breads ) etc . usually they only serve cold dishes .
