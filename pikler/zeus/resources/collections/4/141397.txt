Armaan (1966 movie)    armaan ( 1966 movie ) armaan ( ) , the pakistani urdu melodramatic black and white movie . it was produced by waheed murad and directed by pervaiz malik . it was the first pakistani movie to complete 75 weeks in cinemas and , thus , became the first pakistani 'platinum jubilee ' movie . the movie was picturized in black and white prints and in beautiful locations of karachi .
