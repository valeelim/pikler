Impala     an impala ( `` aepyceros melampus '' ) is a medium-sized african antelope . the name `` impala '' comes from the zulu language . the impala is reddish-brown with white hair inside the ears , over each eye and on the chin , upper throat , underparts and buttocks . a narrow black line runs along the middle of the lower back to the tail , and a vertical black stripe appears on the back of each thigh . impalas have unique brushlike tufts of black hair that cover a scent gland located just above the heel on each hind leg . the impala can jump more than 10 meters distance and 3 meters high , and can reach running speeds of about 80 to 90 km/h , to escape from predators . impalas are found at grassland and woodland edges , usually very close by water .
