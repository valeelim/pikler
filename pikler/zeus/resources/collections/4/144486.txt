Lamia     lamia is a greek city in the center of greece . according to the 2001 census , 50,551 people lived there . lamia is build at the foot of the hill on the top of which there is a castle . during the middle ages lamia was named zitouni but today is named with its ancient name , lamia . around lamia there is a big plain , so lamia is an agricultural city . at the side of plain there are many tall mountains . they are named timphristos , oiti and othris .
