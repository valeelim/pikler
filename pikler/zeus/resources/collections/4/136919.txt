Porter Goss     porter johnston goss ( born december 10 , 1938 ) is an american politician . he was the last director of central intelligence and the first director of the central intelligence agency following the passage of the 2004 intelligence reform and terrorism prevention act , which abolished the dci position . a cia officer in latin america during the cold war , he served as a republican member of the u.s. house of representatives from 1989 until he took up his post at the agency .
