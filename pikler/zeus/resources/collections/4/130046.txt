Upton Park     upton park , or the boleyn ground , was previously the home stadium of west ham united in the east end of london . the club started playing here in 1904. recently they moved to the olympic stadium because the boleyn ground was to be made into flats . many west ham fans were devastated but many debate that the olympic stadium is better . on the 10th may 2016 , the final game was played between west ham united f.c . and manchester united f.c . because the ground was sold for demolition . the game finished with west ham winning 3-2. by july 2016 , the seats were removed and it was handed over to the developers for demolition .
