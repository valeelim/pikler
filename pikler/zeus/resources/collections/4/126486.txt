Stable     a stable is a building for livestock animals such as horses or cows to live in . a stable often has a loft where food such as hay can be kept to feed the animals in the winter . the stable may be divided into enclosures or stalls to keep the animals separate . a stable may be built near a farmhouse . according to the christian bible , the baby jesus was born in a stable . he was wrapped up and put to sleep on the straw in the feed bin ( the `` manger '' ) .
