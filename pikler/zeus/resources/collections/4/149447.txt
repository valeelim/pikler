M40 motorway     the m40 motorway is a motorway between denham , near london and solihull near birmingham in the united kingdom . it was built in stages between 1967 and 1991 and is 89 miles long . places served . - starting in denham - beaconsfield - high wycombe - stokenchurch - oxford - bicester - banbury - leamington spa - warwick - ending in solihull
