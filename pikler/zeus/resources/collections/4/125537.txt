Malam Jabba     malam jabba ( also maalam jabba , ) is a hill station in the karakoram mountain range nearly 40 km from saidu sharif in swat valley , sarhad , pakistan . it is 314 km from islamabad and 51 km from saidu sharif airport . malam jabba is home to the largest ski resort in pakistan . the area also contains two buddhist stupas and six monasteries that are scattered around the resort . the presence of the monuments at such a height indicates that the area has been inhabited for over 2000 years .
