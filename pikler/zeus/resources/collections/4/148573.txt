Christian rock     christian rock is a type of rock music that is performed by christians . it often has lyrics and themes dealing with christianity . christian rock started as `` jesus music '' and piano in church but has become a form of modern music that has become more popular . some christian rock bands such as switchfoot and p.o.d . have become popular even with fans who are not christians . some christians do not like the idea of this type of music and think rock in any form is a work of the devil . other bands in this genre include skillet , fireflight and red . there are many more .
