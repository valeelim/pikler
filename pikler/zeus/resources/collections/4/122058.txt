2 BC     year 2 bc was a common year starting on thursday or friday of the julian calendar . at the time , it was known as the year of the consulship of augustus and silvanus . events . - emperor augustus is made `` pater patriae '' , or `` father of the country '' by the roman senate - julia the elder is exiled on charges of treason and adultery - phraates v becomes king of the parthian empire births . - jesus ( approximate date , according to eusebius of caesarea and jehovah 's witnesses ) deaths . - iullus antonius , son of mark antony and consul in 10 bc - phraates iv , king of parthia
