Asnières-sur-Seine     asnières-sur-seine is a town in france near paris . it is in the region île-de-france and the department hauts-de-seine . about 76,000 people live there . it is one of the most densely populated municipalities in europe ( its density is 17,178/km² ) . `` asnières-sur-seine '' used to just be called asnières . the name asnières was first mentionned in 1158 as `` asnerias '' , from medieval latin `` asinaria '' , meaning `` donkey farm '' . the soil of asnières was poor , where heather grew in medieval times , was probably only good enough for breeding donkeys .
