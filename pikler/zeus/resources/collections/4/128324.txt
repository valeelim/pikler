Gwynt y Môr Wind Farm     gwynt y môr is a 576 mw offshore wind farm off the coast of north wales . permission for the project was given in 2008 , and building began in 2013. the wind farm will begin working in 2014. it will be wales ' largest wind farm . the wind farm is near the existing north hoyle offshore wind farm and the rhyl flats offshore wind farm . all of these projects are owned by rwe npower . related pages . - list of large wind farms references . - gwynt y môr , rwe innogy
