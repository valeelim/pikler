Turkish alphabet     the turkish alphabet is used for writing , speaking and reading in turkish . this alphabet is modified from the latin alphabet . there are 29 letters in the turkish alphabet of turkey . the english letters , `` q '' , `` w '' and `` x '' are not in turkish alphabet . seven turkish letters ( ç , ğ , i , i , ö , ş , and ü ) are in the turkish alphabet . there are 8 vowels in turkish alphabet : a , e , i , i , o , ö , u , ü there are 21 consonants in turkish alphabet : b , c , ç , d , f , g , ğ , h , j , k , l , m , n , p , r , s , ş , t , v , y , z .
