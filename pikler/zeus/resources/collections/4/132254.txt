Faboideae     faboideae is a subfamily of the flowering plant family fabaceae or leguminosae . an acceptable alternative name for the subfamily is papilionoideae , or papilionaceae when this group of plants is treated as a family . this subfamily is widely distributed and members are adapted to a wide variety of environments . faboideae may be trees , shrubs or herbs . the flowers are usually papilionaceous and root nodulation is very common . vetch , laburnum , and sweet pea are all members of faboideae .
