International Alliance of Research Universities     the international alliance of research universities ( iaru ) was a program launched in january 2006 as a leading co-operative network of 10 international research-intensive universities . the president of the iaru is australian national university vice-chancellor professor ian chubb . its members are : - australian national university - university of cambridge - university of oxford - university of california , berkeley - yale university - peking university - national university of singapore - university of tokyo - university of copenhagen - eth zurich
