Argenteuil     argenteuil is a town northwest of paris , france . argenteuil is a sub-prefecture of the val-d'oise department . argenteuil is the second most populated town in the suburbs of paris ( after boulogne-billancourt ) and the most populated commune in the val-d'oise department ; however it is not the prefecture . its population is about 94,000 ( 1999 ) . claude monet painted things in argenteuil many times . twin towns . - dessau ( germany ) ; - clydebank ( scotland ) ; - alessandria ( italy ) ; - hunedoara ( romania )
