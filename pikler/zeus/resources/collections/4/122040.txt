Jhangi     jhangi is one of the 51 union councils of abbottabad district in the khyber pakhtunkhwa province of pakistan . it is also known as jhangi syedan to the local people . it is to the north of abbottabad city ( the district capital ) . it includes the northwestern parts of the city , its suburbs , and outlying villages . jhangi is at 34°12'0n 73°13'60e with a height of 1201 metres ( 3943 feet ) . to the south is the city of abbottabad . the union councils is administratively subdivided into the following areas : - banda amlok - banda batang - banda falzullah - banda khair khan - banda lamba - banda noor ahmed - banda qazi - banda sher khan - bandi gasan - dheri - dobather - jhangi - phagwarhan other villages are : - banda buraj - banda jalal khan - banda laman - boi di gali - laman mera
