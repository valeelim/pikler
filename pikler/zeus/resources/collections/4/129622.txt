Hunter Valley     the hunter valley , is an area of new south wales . the hunter river flows through the valley . the hunter valley is one of the largest river valleys on the nsw coast . it is known for its wineries and coal mines . it is about to and less than 2 hours drive from north of sydney , australia . about 590,000 people live in the valley . more than half of the people live in the coastal cities of newcastle and lake macquarie . there are also other towns and villages across the valley . there are 11 local government areas that look after the area . these eleven lgas are : - cessnock city council - dungog shire council - gloucester shire council - great lakes council - lake macquarie city council - maitland city council - muswellbrook shire council - newcastle city council - port stephens council - singleton shire council - upper hunter shire council
