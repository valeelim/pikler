Bellegarde-sur-Valserine     bellegarde-sur-valserine is a commune . it is found in the auvergne-rhône-alpes region in the ain department in the east of france . in 2010 , 11,790 people lived in the commune . the inhabitants are the `` bellegardiens '' . the local economy is mostly based around commerce . monuments in the commune are numerous . there are many castles including the `` châteaux de musinens '' , `` mussel '' , `` arlod '' , `` vanchy '' and `` crêts '' . there are also many churches including the `` saint-vicent-de-paul '' , `` notre-dame-de-l'assomption '' , `` saint-nicolas d'arlod '' and `` saint-claude de vanchy '' churches . famous people from bellegarde-sur-valserine include robert barrier , a politician , david venditti , a french rugby player , younès kaboul , a french football player , nelly moenne loccoz , a french snowboarder and yves amoureux , an author and movie producer .
