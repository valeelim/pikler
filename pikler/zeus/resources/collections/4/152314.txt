Penny Black     the penny black was the world 's first adhesive postage stamp . it was issued by the united kingdom on the 1 may 1840 and used from the 6 may the same year . all london post offices received official issues of the new stamps , but other offices in the country did not . some post offices like in bath , offered the stamp unofficially after 2 may . once the stamps were used , they had a red cancellation . however , it was hard to see , and it could easily be taken off . it is not very rare . the total print run was 286,700 sheets with 68,808,000 stamps . a mint condition stamp might fetch £3–4,000 . related pages . - penny lilac - penny red - queen victoria - twelve penny black - two penny blue
