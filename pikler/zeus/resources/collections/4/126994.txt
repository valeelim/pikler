Melon-headed whale     the melon-headed whale ( `` peponocephala electra '' ) is a mammal of the oceanic dolphin family . it is closely related to the pygmy killer whale and the pilot whale . these dolphin species are known by the common name `` blackfish '' . the melon-headed whale is widespread throughout the world 's tropical waters , although it is not often seen by humans because it prefers to live in deep water .
