Barisal District     barisal is a district in southern bangladesh . it is also the headquarters of barisal division . barisal was a semi-independent area in the mughal period . later it came under the control of british india . after british rule it was part of east bengal ( later east pakistan ) and finally , bangladesh . barisal district is divided into the following upazilas ( formerly called thanas ) : 1. agailjhara upazila 1. babuganj upazila 1. bakerganj upazila 1. banaripara upazila 1. gaurnadi upazila 1. hizla upazila 1. barisal sadar upazila 1. mehendiganj upazila 1. muladi upazila 1. wazirpur upazila
