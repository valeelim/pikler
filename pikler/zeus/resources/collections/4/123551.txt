Chesapeake Energy Arena     chesapeake energy arena is a multipurpose indoor sports/concert arena in downtown oklahoma city . it is the home of the national basketball association 's oklahoma city thunder . the arena was also home to the new orleans/oklahoma city hornets from 2005 to 2007. the arena was formerly named the ford center from 2002 to 2010 and the oklahoma city arena from 2010 to 2011. other websites . - official website
