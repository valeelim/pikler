Trade secret     a trade secret is a formula , practice , process , design , instrument , pattern , or a collection of information which is not generally known , invented or created , by which a business can obtain an economic advantage over competitors or customers . in some jurisdictions , such secrets are referred to as `` confidential information '' or `` classified information '' . there are three factors that are common to all definitions of a trade secret , a trade secret is information that : - is not generally known to the public ; - grants some sort of economic benefit on its holder ( where this benefit must derive `` specifically '' from its not being generally known , not just from the value of the information itself ) ; - is the subject of reasonable efforts to maintain its secrecy .
