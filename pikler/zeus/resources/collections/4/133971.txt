Punching bag     a punching bag is a tough bag which is supposed to be punched a lot . it is used in exercise or stress relief to improve one of three areas : physical strength , aerobic fitness or punching technique . punching bags have been used in martial arts for as long as there has been a written history of military training . similar apparatus in asian martial arts include the okinawan `` makiwara '' and the chinese `` mook jong '' , which may have padded striking surfaces attached to them . related pages . - boxing - jujutsu - karate - kung fu - taekwondo
