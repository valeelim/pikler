Suru Valley     the suru valley is a valley in the ladakh region of jammu and kashmir , which is drained by the suru river ( indus ) , a tributary of the indus river . it is at a height of . the towns of kargil and rangdum are here . the penzella pass at panikhar , a hill resort in zanskar , divides the suru valley with zanskar . willow and poplar are grown in the suru valley . suru valley has 25,000 by people of dard and tibetan descent . the majority of the people are followers of shi'a islam , having converted from tibetan buddhism in the 16th century under the direction of thi-namgyal . buddhists in the suru valley live throughout the valley , but are mainly in rangdum .
