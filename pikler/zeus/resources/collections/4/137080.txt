Pete Geren     preston m. geren , iii ( born january 29 , 1952 in fort worth , texas ) was the 20th united states secretary of the army from 16 july 2007 to 16 september 2009. he had already been acting united states secretary of the air force , between 29 july 2005 and position he served in until the confirmation of his successor michael wynne on 4 november 2005. geren was also a member of the united states house of representatives from texas .
