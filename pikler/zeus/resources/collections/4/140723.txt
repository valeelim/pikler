Samekh     samekh or simketh is the fifteenth letter in many semitic alphabets , including phoenician , hebrew , and aramaic , representing . the phoenician letter is where the greek xi ( ξ , ξ ) comes from . samekh represents , a voiceless alveolar fricative . unlike most semitic consonants , the pronunciation of remains constant between vowels and before voiced consonants . samekh in gematria has the value 60. samekh and mem form the abbreviation for the angel of death , whose name in hebrew is `` samael '' . it also stands for centimetre .
