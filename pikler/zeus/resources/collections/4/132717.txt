Oral sex     oral sex is when one partner uses the tongue , mouth or throat to excite the other partner 's sex organs . for males it can include placing the penis in one 's mouth and for females licking the vaginal area . sometimes oral sex is performed as a way of getting ready for intercourse , but it may be done as an alternative to intercourse . if someone is performing oral sex on a male , he or she may decide to swallow the semen if the male ejaculates in the mouth . there is no risk of pregnancy from swallowing semen . one can get sexually transmitted diseases from oral sex , such as herpes simplex ( which can be passed between mouth and groin or the other way ) . some stds , like hiv , are much harder to pass through oral sex , but it is still possible . the technical term for oral sex is fellatio if performed on a male and cunnilingus if performed on a female . slang for oral sex is common in western cultures , for example : `` going down on '' or `` giving a blowjob '' or `` giving head .
