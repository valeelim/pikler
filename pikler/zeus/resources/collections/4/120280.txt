Ned Kelly (2003 movie)    ned kelly ( 2003 movie ) ned kelly is a 2003 australian movie directed by gregor jordan . the movie shows the life of ned kelly who was a famous bushranger in australia . the movie is based on robert drewe 's book `` our sunshine '' . most of the filming was done at the little river earth sanctuary , mount rothwell , near geelong . street scenes were filmed in clunes and ballarat . other locations include broadford , glenfern , hepburn springs and melbourne . related pages . - ned kelly - ned kelly ( 1970 movie ) - the story of the kelly gang other websites . - official site - boxoffice information
