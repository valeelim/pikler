Ben Shepherd     ben shepherd ( born hunter benedict shepherd on september 20 , 1968 ) is an american musician , best known as the bass player in the grunge band soundgarden from 1990 until the band 's 1997 break-up . ben shepherd first auditioned for the role of bassist in soundgarden in 1989 , to replace hiro yamamoto , who had left the band . he was turned down because he could not play the songs well enough . but after jason everman was let go , shepherd was then hired . chris cornell said that shepherd brought a `` fresh and creative '' approach to the `` badmotorfinger '' recording sessions . all the band members said that his knowledge of music and writing skills would be a help to the band . before joining , soundgarden had been shepherd 's favorite band .
