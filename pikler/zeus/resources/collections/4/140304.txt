Dominion of Pakistan     the dominion of pakistan was an independent federal dominion in south asia that was created in 1947 in the partition of british india into two sovereign dominions ( the other being the union of india ) . the dominion included modern-day pakistan ( called west pakistan ) and bangladesh ( east pakistan ) . it was supposed to be a homeland for the muslims of the indian sub-continent . it became the islamic republic of pakistan in 1956. the eastern part of the country became independent as the people 's republic of bangladesh in 1971 .
