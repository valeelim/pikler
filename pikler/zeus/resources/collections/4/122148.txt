Sheikh-ul-Bandi     sheikhul bandi is one of the 51 union council ( sub-divisions ) of abbottabad district in the khyber pakhtunkhwa province of pakistan . it is in the valley toward the north-west of the sarban hills . it is located at 34°8'60n , 73°13'60e most people of sheikhul bandi are the jadoons . the main language is hindko although urdu is also spoken and understood . the union council is subdivided into the following areas : cantonment , qasba abbottabad , sheikhulbandi and rakh civil military .
