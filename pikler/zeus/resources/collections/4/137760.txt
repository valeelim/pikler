Frankfurter Allgemeine Zeitung     the frankfurter allgemeine zeitung is a conserative newspaper in germany . the frankfurter allgemeine zeitung is the leading conservative newspaper in germany . there are 6 newspapers a week and supplementary the frankfurter allgemeine sonntagszeitung on sundays . there are about 360,000 newspapers sold every day . it is the german newspaper with the widest circulation abroad . it is sold to 148 countries every day . the first edition of the faz published on november 1 , 1949. its founding editor was erich welter . some editors had worked for the frankfurter zeitung , which was banned in 1943. other websites . - the faz online edition ( in german ; most articles are free ) - ketupa.net - frankfurter zeitung and faz media profile
