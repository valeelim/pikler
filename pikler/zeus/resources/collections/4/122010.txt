Ghari Phulgran     garhi phulgran is one of the 51 union councils ( administrative subdivisions ) of abbottabad district in the khyber pakhtunkhwa province of pakistan . the union council is made up of the following areas : garhi phulgran , takia sheikhan , ghora bazgram , harnara , kiala , mannan , rajoya and thith ochar . until the local government reforms of 2000 , rajoya was separate union council .
