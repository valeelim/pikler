The Mummy: Tomb of the Dragon Emperor    the mummy : tomb of the dragon emperor the mummy : tomb of the dragon emperor is a 2008 american adventure movie . it is a sequel to `` the mummy '' and `` the mummy returns '' . it was released on august 1 , 2008 in the united states . jet li is in the movie also . the movie opened in los angeles on july 27 , 2008. most critics said the movie is bad . roger ebert of the `` chicago sun-times '' liked it as `` plain dumb fun .
