Kulachi Tehsil     kulachi is a city and the headquarters of kulachi tehsil ( an administrative subdivision ) of dera ismail khan district in khyber pakhtunkhwa , pakistan . it is situated 50 km from dera ismail khan in the damaan valley of the sulaiman range , and lies on the banks of the gomal river ( local name `` luni '' or `` khuarha '' ) , a tributary of the indus river .
