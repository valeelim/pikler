So Far So Good (album)    so far so good ( album ) so far so good is a compilation album by bryan adams , released by a & m records in november 1993. the album reached number six on the `` billboard '' 200 charts in 1993. the album contains hits from between `` cuts like a knife '' ( 1983 ) and `` waking up the neighbours '' ( 1991 ) , and a new single , `` please forgive me '' . the only song on the album that has never been released as a single is `` kids wan na rock '' from `` reckless '' ( 1984 ) . originally the song `` so far so good '' was going to be included on the album so the album started and finished with a new song but it was dropped . the song was included on disc two of `` anthology '' .
