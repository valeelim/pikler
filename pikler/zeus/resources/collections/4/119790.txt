Patricia Heaton     patricia helen heaton ( born march 4 , 1958 in bay village , ohio ) is an emmy award-winning american actress best known for playing lead character and ray barone 's wife debra barone on the cbs television sitcom `` everybody loves raymond '' . she currently stars as frankie heck on the abc sitcom `` the middle '' . she received a star on the hollywood walk of fame on may 22 , 2012. heaton won two outstanding lead actress in a comedy series emmy awards for her work in `` everybody loves raymond '' : - for : `` bad moon rising '' ( 2000 ) - for : `` the canister '' ( 2001 ) she has been nominated for five others for the same series : - for : `` be nice '' ( 1999 ) - for : `` a vote for debra '' ( 2002 ) - `` baggage '' ( 2003 ) - `` fun with debra '' ( 2004 ) - for : `` finale '' ( 2005 )
