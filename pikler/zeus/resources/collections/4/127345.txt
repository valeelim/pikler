National League East     the national league east is a division in the mlb . all of the teams are on the east coast of the u.s. in 2007 , 2008 , and 2009 , the phillies won the division ; they also won the 2008 world series . the nl east is known as one of the toughest divisions in the mlb . teams in the national league east division . - philadelphia phillies - new york mets - miami marlins - atlanta braves - washington nationals
