Lodhran Tehsil     lodhran is the chief city of lodhran district in the punjab province of pakistan . it is at 29°31'60n 71°37'60e . it is on the northern side of river sutlej . as well as being district capital , lodhran is the administrative centre of lodhran tehsil , which is subdivided into 28 union councils of pakistan . lodhran tehsil , has a total of 325 schools , 9 colleges , 30 hospitals , 10 police stations , 11 railway stations , 52 post offices , one radio station and 20 banks .
