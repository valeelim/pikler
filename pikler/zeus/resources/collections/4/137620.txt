Ontario Lacus     ontario lacus is a lake filled with methane and ethane near the south pole of saturn 's moon titan . it was identified as a hydrocarbon lake and confirmed by data from the cassini spacecraft . this discovery was announced in the 31 july 2008 edition of `` nature '' . ontario lacus is about 20,000 km in area , and is named after lake ontario in north america , which is a similar size and shape .
