British Rail Class 143     the british rail class 143 is a diesel multiple unit , part of the pacer family of trains introduced between 1985 & 1986. they originally worked around north east england but were later transferred to wales . the coaches are based on an alexander bus body mounted on a chassis from a freight or goods truck . twenty-five units were built , numbered 143001-025 but later re-numbered 143601 - 143625 .
