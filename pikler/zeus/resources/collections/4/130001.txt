Anesthesiologist     an anesthesiologist ( also anaesthesiologist ) is a doctor of medicine.an anesthesiologist ( american english ) or anaesthetist ( british english ) is a physician trained in anesthesia and perioperative medicine . terminology varies between countries . the main jobs of an anesthesiologist are : - providing anesthesia to a patient undergoing surgery - intensive care medicine - medical emergency medicine - palliative care the first anesthesiologist was john snow ( 18131858 ) .
