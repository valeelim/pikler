8 Eyes     8 eyes is a 1988 video game created by thinking rabbit for the nintendo entertainment system . the game has eight levels in it , and can be played by one or two players . in the game , a soundtrack , composed by kenzou kumei , had three songs for each of the eight levels , each set in a different part of the world . it has almost the same gameplay and graphics to `` castlevania '' . the name of the main character of the game is orin , and he looks like simon belmont from the castlevania games .
