Sligo     sligo is a town in county sligo , republic of ireland . it is the largest town in the county and the second largest rural area in connacht after galway city . it is home to sligo rovers f.c . who play in the league of ireland premier division and won the league in 2012. sligo has a population of around 20,000 and has many tourist attractions . sligo is at the end of the railway line from dublin . lakes : lough gara , lough arrow and gara vogue
