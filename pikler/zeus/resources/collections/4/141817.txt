Salwar kameez     salwar kameez ( also spelled shalwar kameez or shalwar qameez ) is a traditional dress worn by both women and men in southern asia . salvars or shalvars are loose pajama-like trousers . the legs are wide at the top , and narrow at the bottom . the kameez is a long shirt or tunic . the side seams ( known as the chaak ) are left open below the waist-line , which gives the wearer greater freedom of movement . it is the most common dress for men in pakistan and afghanistan .
