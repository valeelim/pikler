Mark McGhee     mark edward mcghee ( born 25 may , 1957 in glasgow , scotland ) is a former professional footballer and current manager of english club bristol rovers . mcghee played for many scottish and english clubs , before he had to retire because of injury . he started his playing career at greenock morton before moving on to newcastle united , aberdeen , hamburg , celtic , ik brage and reading and won the scottish pfa players ' player of the year in 1982 as well as four caps and two goals for the scotland national football team . his greatest career moment came in 1983 when he was in the aberdeen side that defeated real madrid 2-1 to lift the european cup winner 's cup .
