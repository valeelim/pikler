Uttoxeter     uttoxeter is a small market town and civil parish in east staffordshire , staffordshire , england . in 2001 , 12,023 people lived there . this number was expected to grow when new developments in the town finished . uttoxeter is close to the river dove . it is near the cities of stoke-on-trent , derby and lichfield . it is twinned with raisdorf in germany ( with whom close links are still maintained ) , and fumel in france .
