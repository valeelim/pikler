Shut Up and Let Me Go     shut up and let me go ( aka : shut up & let me go ) is the third single off the studio album `` we started nothing '' by the ting tings . the song was released in the united kingdom on july 21st of 2008 , and was the follow-up to the number one hit single `` that 's not my name '' . the song was added to the coveted a-list of bbc radio 1 's playlist , and has received a substantial amount of airplay during the day on the station . the single peaked at number six in both the uk singles chart and the uk download chart .
