Asunción     asunción ( full name : nuestra señora santa maría de la asunción ) , is the capital city and largest city of paraguay . the city has more than 1.2 million inhabitants , and the metropolitan area has more than 1.8 million inhabitants . it is on the left bank of the paraguay river and is the main seaport of paraguay . the spanish word `` asunción '' means `` assumption '' in english . it refers to the assumption of mary ; the full name means `` our lady , holy mary of the assumption '' .
