Flag of Texas     the texas flag is known as the `` lone star flag '' . this flag was introduced to the republic of texas on december 28 , 1838 , by senator william h. wharton . it became its national flag on january 24 , 1839. when texas became the 28th state of the united states of america on december 29 , 1845 , its national flag became the state flag . each color means a special symbol on the flag : blue stands for loyalty , white for purity , and red for bravery .
