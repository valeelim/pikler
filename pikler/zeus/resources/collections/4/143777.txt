Heraclion     heraclion is the biggest city of crete . its population is 132,000 inhabitants , according to the 2001 census . heraclion is located in the north of crete . heraclion is near knossos , the ancient capital of crete and the center of minoan civilization . heraclion has a big harbor which it connects heraclion with continental greece . heraclion has a hot-summer mediterranean climate . the most interest sight of heraclion is the venetian square with the fountain from marble . the venetian rule , between the 13th and 16th century , has left many important monuments . the medieval fortification of the town is another important sight . the fortification had been constructed by arabians , byzantines and venetians which they had passed from heraclion in the past .
