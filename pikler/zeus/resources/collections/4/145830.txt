Astro (satellite TV)    astro ( satellite tv ) astro is a subscription-based direct broadcast satellite service based in bukit jalil , kuala lumpur , malaysia . it transmits digital satellite television and radio to households in malaysia , brunei , indonesia and singapore . astro is owned by measat broadcast network systems , which is a subsidiary of astro all asia networks plc . related pages . - astro corporate web site - astro all asia networks plc corporate web site - astro 's indonesia service website - astro all asia networks plc annual report fy2006 6 channel m
