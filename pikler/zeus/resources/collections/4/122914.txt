Shandur Pass     the height of this pass is 3738 m above sea level , it connects gupis , gilgit with chitral . the top is flat , a plateau and can be crossed between late april and early november . the grade is very gradual , and the area is crossed by small streams of trout . grazing in summer is plentiful . every year there is a polo match played on shandur top between a team from chitral and a team from gilgit . shandur top is one of the four major mountain passes to enter chitral . the others are dorah pass from badakshan in afghanistan , lowari pass from dir , and broghol pass from the wakhan corridor in afghanistan . the people who live on both sides of shandur top speak the khowar language .
