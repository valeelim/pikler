Stormbreaker     stormbreaker is a book by british writer anthony horowitz . it is the first book in the `` alex rider '' series . it was released on 4 september 2000 in the united kingdom and on 21 may 2001 in the united states . a movie based on the book was released on 21 july 2006. it starred alex pettyfer as the book 's main character , alex rider .
