Nocera Inferiore     nocera inferiore , is a town and comune in campania , italy , in the province of salerno , at the foot of monte albino , 20 km east-south-east of naples by rail , . above sea-level . nuceria alfaterna appears to have been the chief town in the valley of the sarnus , herculaneum , pompeii , stabiae and surrentum all being dependent upon it . hannibal destroyed the town in 216. in 1385 pope urban vi was besieged in the castle of charles of durazzo . at nocera had sped a big part of they life the painter francesco solimena , and the writers jacopo sannazzaro and domenico rea . is the birthplace of the footballers simone barone and raffaele de martino . originally from nocera are the parents of italian american warren cuccurullo and mario cuomo .
