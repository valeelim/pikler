Talagang Tehsil     talagang tehsil is a subdivision ( tehsil ) of chakwal district in the punjab province of pakistan . the tehsil , which is headquartered at the city of talagang , is administratively subdivided into 23 union councils . talagang tehsil is one of the largest tehsils in south asia as it contains 102 villages . some of the villages near talagang town are cheenji , adhlaka , jatla , lawa , dhurnal , pachnand , mogla , malakwal , tamman , akwal , chowkhandi , dhook goria , perha fathial , and dhook kund .
