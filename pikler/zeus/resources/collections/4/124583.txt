Mick Foley     michael francis `` mick '' foley , sr ( born march 7 , 1965 ) is an american author , actor and professional wrestler . he has wrestled under the names dude love , cactus jack , and mankind . he is currently signed to a wwe on smackdown brand as mankind . a hardcore specialist , foley is known as `` the king of hardcore wrestling '' . he is a former ecw , wcw and wwe tag team champion and was a part of the `` rock and sock connection '' with wrestler the rock . he has held the wwe championship 3 times . he has held the tna world heavyweight championship after defeating sting at lockdown 2008. in wwe he has won a total of 17 championships . he was announced as the first inductee into the wwe hall of fame class of 2013. memoirs - ( 1999 ) `` .
