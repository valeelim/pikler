Artemare     artemare is a commune . it is found in the auvergne-rhône-alpes region in the ain department in the east of france . in 2009 , 1,112 people lived in the commune . the inhabitants are the `` artemariens '' . the local economy is mostly based around commerce . the most important monuments in the commune are the `` château de mâchuraz '' , a castle from the 16th century close to the commune ( administratively in the commune of vieu ) , the `` château d'antioche '' , and the ruins of `` châteauneuf '' ( also on the land of the commune of vieu ) . famous people from artemare include adophe appian , a french painter from the 19th century .
