Atari Jaguar     the atari jaguar was a video game machine released by atari in 1993. it was not a successful product and was atari 's last console . it also had a cd-based accessory called the atari jaguar cd . the atari jaguar was sold at about the same time as the playstation , sega saturn and nintendo 64 , but it was not very popular as it did not have as many popular games as these other consoles . the graphics of atari jaguar games look a lot like the sega genesis , sega mega drive or snes games as it did not run 3d games as well as the sega saturn or playstation .
