Dead Man Walking     dead man walking is a 1995 american drama movie . the subjects represented in this movie are capital punishment and murder . this was shot near new orleans , louisiana . susan sarandon plays sister helen prejean and sean penn plays a louisiana inmate on death row . `` dead man walking '' was well received by the movie critics . the budget was $ 11 million ; it got back $ 83 million in the box office . this movie was released in december 1995 in the american theaters .
