The Garfield Show     the garfield show is a french/american cgi animated television show . it started in france on france 3 on december 22 , 2008. english-language episodes started in the united kingdom on bommerang on may 5 , 2009. the show started airing in the united states on cartoon network on november 2 , 2009. it is based on the comic strip `` garfield '' . it is the second `` garfield '' television series , after `` garfield and friends '' . other websites . - official website
