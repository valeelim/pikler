Shrimp toast     shrimp toast or prawn toast is a chinese dim sum dish . it is small triangles of bread , brushed with egg and topped with chopped shrimp and water chestnuts . it is baked or deep fried . it is often served as an appetizer in chinese restaurants in australia and the united states . in some countries , such as the united kingdom and australia , sesame seeds are sometimes sprinkled on the dish before it is baked or deep fried .
