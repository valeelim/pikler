Alexa Nikolas     alexa helen nikolas ( born april 4 , 1992 in chicago ) is an american actress . she is best known for her role as `` nicole bristow '' on the nickelodeon television series `` zoey 101 '' . nikolas is the only child of alexandra nikolas , a real estate salesman . her grandmother was a beauty pageant winner in texas . she was to see in the musicvideo of vanessa anne hudgens ' single `` come back to me '' . since february 22 , 2012 , she is married with mike milosh .
