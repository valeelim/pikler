Iyengar Yoga     iyengar yoga is a type of yoga named after and developed by b. k. s. iyengar ( 1918-2014 ) . it is a form of hatha yoga . it emphasizes detail , precision and alignment . it focuses performing postures ( poses ) and breath control . this type of yoga helps to develop strength , mobility and stability . b. k. s. iyengar has created over 200 yoga poses . students start with simple poses and move on to more complex ones . iyengar yoga often uses belts , blocks , and blankets to help people get into the postures . by using belts , blocks and blankets , people are less likely to hurt themselves .
