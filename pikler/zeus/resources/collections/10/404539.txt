Waterloo International railway station     waterloo international was the first london station for eurostar trains to france and belgium . it was treated as a separate station to the main station at london waterloo , but its 5 platforms were numbered 20 to 24. in 2007 it closed and all eurostar trains now run from st pancras international railway station . the platforms are no longer used , but south west trains now plans to use at least one of the platforms when it starts running trains with 10 carriages in late 2013 .
