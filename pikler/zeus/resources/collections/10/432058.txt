Benton County, Indiana    benton county , indiana benton county is a county in indiana . its county seat is fowler . about 93 % of the land in the county is used for growing crops , mainly corn and soybeans . there are no cities in this county . the largest town , fowler , in 2010 had a population of 2,317. towns . - ambia - boswell - earl park - fowler - otterbein - oxford there are three large wind farms in the county : - benton county wind farm - 135 turbines - fowler ridge wind farm - 460 turbines - hoosier wind farm - 53 turbines
