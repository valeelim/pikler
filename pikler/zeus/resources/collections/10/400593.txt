Mika (singer)    mika ( singer ) mika ( born michael holbrook penniman , jr. ; 18 august 1983 ) is a british singer . mika was born in beirut , lebanon to a syrian-lebanese mother and american father . he moved to paris at age one . he moved to london at age nine . he is openly gay and some of his songs have lgbt themes . albums . - life in cartoon motion ( released february 5th , 2007 ) - the boy who knew too much ( released september 21st , 2009 ) - the origin of love ( released october 8th , 2012 ) - no place in heaven ( released june 15th , 2015 ) songs . - elle me dit - grace kelly - happy ending - we are golden
