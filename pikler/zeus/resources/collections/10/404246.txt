Water for Elephants     water for elephants is a novel by sara gruen . it was published in 2006 by algonquin books . the story is about a 23 year old polish american orphan who joins a circus during the great depression . themes are love and romance , murder , and cruelty to animals . the story was made into a 2011 movie starring robert pattinson , reese witherspoon , christoph waltz , jim norton , and hal holbrook .
