Stan (song)    stan ( song ) `` stan '' is the third single from `` the marshall mathers lp '' , eminem 's third album . it was no . 1 in the uk and australia . it is about an eminem fan who kills himself after eminem does not respond to his letters . devon sawa plays the fan in the music video . eminem performed the song at the 2001 grammy awards . elton john played the piano at the performance . the album version of the song is 6 minutes and 39 seconds long . the video is 8 minutes and 15 seconds . on `` the marshall mathers lp 2 '' , the song `` bad guy '' is a sequel to `` stan '' ; it is about stan 's brother , matthew , taking revenge on eminem .
