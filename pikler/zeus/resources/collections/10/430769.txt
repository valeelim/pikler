Diomedes Díaz     diomedes de jesús díaz maestre ( may 26 , 1956 – december 22 , 2013 ) was a colombian vallenato singer , songwriter and composer . his nickname was `` el cacique de la junta '' ( the chieftain of junta ) . it was given to him early in his career by another vallenato singer , rafael orozco maestre . díaz died in valledupar at the age of 56. he was resting in his bed when his heart failed and he stopped breathing .
