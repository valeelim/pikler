Death and funeral of Hugo Chávez     hugo chávez , the president of venezuela , died on 5 march 2013. he was aged 58. because of his death , the venezuelan constitution required an election for a new president to be held within 30 days . vice president nicolás maduro became temporary president . on 14 april , he was officially elected . chávez ' funeral was held on 8 march . maduro originally said that chávez ' body would be embalmed and permanently displayed in a glass coffin at a military museum . however , on 13 march maduro announced that this would not happen . this was because the government could not get prepared in the short time that had passed . maduro extended the national mourning for four days more , to eleven days . by the end of this , chávez ' body was moved to the museum of the revolution at fort montana . this had been where chávez had tried to overthrow the government in 1992. chávez 's body was never embalmed . he was buried at a cemetery in venezuela .
