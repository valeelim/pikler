Sex (book)    sex ( book ) the book sex is a book by the singer madonna . it was released on 21 october 1992. it covers topics like sadomasochism , sex , pornography , adult content and sexual simulation . madonna wrote the book as a character named mistress dita . `` sex '' had 128 pages plus a warning . others involved in the book include naomi campbell , vanilla ice and isabella rossellini . the book was controversial for a lot of reasons . one was that the book was not allowed for sale to anyone under the age of eighteen . the book became a very major hit .
