Oceanus Hopkins     oceanus hopkins ( 1620 ? ) was born at sea during the voyage of the `` mayflower '' to the new world in 1620. he was the son of stephen hopkins and his wife , elizabeth ( fisher ) hopkins . he was born sometime between 6 september and 9 november 1620. he died before 1627. his body and grave are lost . oceanus is the latin word for ocean . other websites . - oceanus hopkins on mayflowerhistory.com
