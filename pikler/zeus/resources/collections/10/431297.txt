Charlemagne Prize     the charlemagne prize ( ) is a famous prize awarded each year . the prize has been awarded since 1950 by the german city of aachen . it is awarded to persons who have done outstanding work towards the unification of europe . the award is named after charlemagne , emperor of the frankish empire , and founder of what became the holy roman empire . charlemagne lived and was buried in aachen . other websites . - charlemagne prize
