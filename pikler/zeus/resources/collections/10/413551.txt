Escape from New York     escape from new york is a 1981 american science fiction movie . it was released on july 10 , 1981. it was directed by john carpenter . the film 's total budget was estimated to be us $ 6 million . it stars kurt russell . the cast includes donald pleasence ( as the president of the united states ) , isaac hayes , adrienne barbeau , harry dean stanton , lee van cleef , ernest borgnine , tom atkins , charles cyphers , ox baker , and jamie lee curtis as introduction narrator ( uncredited ) . a following movie was released called , `` escape from l.a.
