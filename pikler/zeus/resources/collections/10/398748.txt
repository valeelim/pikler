A Christmas Carol (2009 movie)    a christmas carol ( 2009 movie ) a christmas carol is a 2009 american 3d computer animated motion-capture holiday fantasy comedy-drama movie . it was written and directed by robert zemeckis . it stars jim carrey , gary oldman , colin firth , robin wright , and bob hoskins . the movie was released on november 6 , 2009. it was based on the charles dickens book `` a christmas carol '' . roger ebert gave it 3/4 stars .
