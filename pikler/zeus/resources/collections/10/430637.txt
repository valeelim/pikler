David Hobbs     david wishart hobbs ( born 9 june 1939 ) is a british retired racing driver . hobbs was a commentator for the speed channel . he now works as a commentator for nbc and nbc sports network . hobbs was born in royal leamington spa , england . in 1969 hobbs was included in the fia list of graded drivers . this is an elite group of 27 drivers who were rated the best in the world by their achievements . other websites . - speedtv bio - david hobbs honda - stats from david hobbs ' iroc and nascar careers on racing-reference.info - david hobbs - test driver jaguar xj13 - building the legend
