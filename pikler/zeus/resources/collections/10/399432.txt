Discipline     discipline is an important idea in the family , the military , and in teaching . it is related to the word disciple . to discipline a child is to punish the child for bad behavior . spanking is or was considered a form of discipline . self-discipline is controlling your own actions . military discipline is training people to obey rules and keep order . a disciplined soldier is one who can act even while afraid for his life . a disciplined person is one who does what must be done to achieve his goals , even if it is difficult . related pages . - discipline ( study ) - disciple references . - solving discipline problems - wolfgang and glickman published by allyn and bacon 1986
