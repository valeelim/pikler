Rolandz     rolandz is a dansband from sweden . it was established in 2008 by robert gustafsson . they first became successful during a dansbandskampen break appearance in 2008. in 2009 , the band participated at `` allsång på skansen '' on 7 july 2009. the singer and leader of rolandz is the character roland järverup , whom robert gustafsson played in the 1999 movie `` screwed in tallinn '' by killinggänget . the idea to create rolandz using the roland järverup character was developed later . dvds . - 2009 : `` rolandz – the movie '' - 2010 : `` rolandz danzar igen '' - 2011 : `` rolandz – fadersjakten '' other websites . - official website
