Henrique Rosa     henrique pereira rosa ( 16 january 1946 – 15 may 2013 ) was a politician from guinea-bissau who served as interim president of guinea-bissau from 2003 to 2005. rosa was born on 16 january 1946 in bafatá , guinea-bissau . rosa handed over power to vieira on 1 october 2005. rosa stood as an independent candidate in the guinea-bissau presidential election , 2009 , finishing in third place . on 15 may 2013 , rosa died at a hospital in porto , in northern portugal , after losing a nine month battle with lung cancer . he was 67 years old .
