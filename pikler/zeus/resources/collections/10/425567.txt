Behind the Mask (song)    behind the mask ( song ) `` behind the mask '' is a synthpop song by the band yellow magic orchestra . it was written by the band 's keyboardist , ryuichi sakamoto . it was first produced in 1978 for a seiko advertisement . it was later released in 1979 on the band 's album `` solid state survivor '' . english lyrics were added by chris mosdell . michael jackson sang his own version of it . it was released in 2010 , a year after jackson 's death on the album `` michael '' . jackson 's version was released as a single in france .
