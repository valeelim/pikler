Haim     haim ( which is said like 'time ' with a 'h ' instead of a 't ' ) is an american indie pop band . the members are sisters alana , danielle , and este . they released their first album `` days are gone '' on september 27 2013. the british newspaper the guardian put the album at number twelve on their list of 2013 's 40 best albums . they are often compared to fleetwood mac . the sisters grew up in the san fernando valley in los angeles . in 2006 they started haim . for the next five years the band performed where they lived . on february 10 , 2012 haim released their first ep `` forever '' . it was on their website to download for free for a short amount of time . in january 2013 haim won bbc 's sound of 2013 poll . on september 22 , 2013 haim released their fourth single from their album `` days are gone '' , `` the wire '' . it reached number sixteen in the uk singles chart . it was their highest charting single in the uk yet . on november 23 2013 haim performed their singles `` the wire '' and `` do n't save me '' on saturday night live .
