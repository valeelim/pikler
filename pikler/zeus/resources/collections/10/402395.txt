Mary Carlisle     mary carlisle ( born february 3 , 1914 ) is a retired american actress and singer . she was known for her roles in `` long live the king '' , `` madame satan '' , `` college humor '' , `` double or nothing '' , and `` doctor rhythm '' . carlisle was born on february 3 , 1914 in boston , massachusetts . she was married to james blakeley from 1942 until his death in 2007. they had one child . in 2010 , aged 98 , she received a star on the hollywood walk of fame . carlisle became the last known sole surviving `` wampas baby star '' . other websites . - photographs of mary carlisle
