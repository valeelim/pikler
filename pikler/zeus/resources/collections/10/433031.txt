Captain EO     captain eo is an american short 3d science fiction movie . it is 17 minutes long . it is about an astronaut called captain eo . he is played by michael jackson . george lucas was the executive producer . `` captain eo '' is one of the first 4d films . the film played at disneyland and epcot center . in 1996 `` captain eo '' was shown on mtv . this is the only time it was shown on tv . it has never been released on dvd or video .
