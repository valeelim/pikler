Richard Karn     richard karn ( born february 17 , 1956 ) is an american actor and game show host . he is best known for acting as al borland in the 1990s abc sitcom `` home improvement '' , and for hosting `` family feud '' during the 2000s . he was born in seattle , washington . karn became host of `` family feud '' in september 2002. he left in 2006 and was replaced by john o'hurley . he also hosted gsn 's `` bingo america '' in 2008. he replaced patrick duffy , who hosted the show for the first season .
