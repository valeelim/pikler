Aziz Ansari     aziz ansari ( born february 23 , 1983 ) is an american actor and comedian . he is best known for his role as tom haverford on the nbc television program `` parks and recreation '' . he is also a movie actor , appearing in `` funny people '' ( 2009 ) , `` i love you , man '' ( 2009 ) , `` observe and report '' ( 2009 ) , and `` 30 minutes or less '' ( 2011 ) . ansari was born in columbia , south carolina . he is the son of an indian tamil muslim family . he started acting after graduating from the new york university in 2004. he lives in los angeles , california and is an atheist .
