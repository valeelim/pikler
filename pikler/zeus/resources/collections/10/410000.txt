José Ramos Castillo     josé ramos castillo ( born december 24 , 1974 in castellon de la plana , castellón ) is a swimmer from spain . he has a physical disability : he is an s7 type swimmer . in 2004 , he lived in artesa-onda , castellón . he raced at the 2004 summer paralympics . he finished third in the 4 x 50 meter 20 points medley relay .
