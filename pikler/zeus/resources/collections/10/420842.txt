Arianna Huffington     arianna huffington ( née stassinopoulos ; born july 15 , 1950 ) is a greek-american author and syndicated columnist . she is best known for her news website `` the huffington post '' . at one time a liberal democrat she later became a popular conservative commentator in the mid-1990s , after which she adopted liberal political beliefs in the late 1990s . she is the former spouse of former republican congressman michael huffington . in 2003 , she ran as an independent candidate for governor in the california recall election . huffington was born ariánna stasinopoúlou in athens , greece on july 15 , 1950. she studied economics at girton college , cambridge . huffington was married to michael huffington from 1986 until they divorced in 1997. she became an american citizen in 1990. other websites . - column archive at `` the huffington post '' - column archive at `` alternet '' - campaign contributions made by arianna huffington
