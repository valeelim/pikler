Taiki Morii     taiki morii ( 森井 大輝 morii taiki ? ) ( born july 9 , 1980 ) is a skier from japan . he has a disability and uses a wheelchair . he skied at the 2011 ipc alpine skiing world championships . he was the first skier to finish in the giant slalom race , and was the second skier to finish in the sitting men 's downhill race and the super-g race .
