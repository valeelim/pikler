Polly Scattergood     polly scattergood ( born 18 october 1986 , colchester , essex , england ) , is a british singer-songwriter . she went to the brit school . her first album , `` polly scattergood '' , was released by mute records in 2009. her second album `` arrows '' was released in october 2013. it took three and a half years to make . the first single from `` arrows '' , `` wanderlust '' , was released in april 2013. on january 21 2014 the video for `` subsequently lost '' was posted on vimeo . she has remixed `` reunion '' by m83 and `` you will find a way '' by maps .
