Michel Phaneuf     michel phaneuf is a canadian columnist who writes about wine . since 1981 , 30 editions of his `` guide du vin '' have been published and have sold almost a million copies . he holds a bachelor in communication from the university of quebec in montreal . the 2010 edition of his guide won a gourmand world cookbook awards . his guide is now copublished with nadia fournier . since 2007 , michel phaneuf has suffered health issues : heart problems , cancer and parkinson 's disease .
