The Beatles: Rock Band    the beatles : rock band the beatles : rock band is a music video game . it was developed by harmonix music systems , published by mtv games , and distributed by ea distribution . it is the third game in the `` rock band '' series . it was released for the playstation 3 , wii , and xbox 360 on september 9 , 2009. the game centers on the popular english rock group the beatles . the game comes with a guitar controller , a drum set , and a microphone . up to six people can play at a time , playing drums , vocals , bass , and guitar . as with previous games in the series , players get points by hitting the right notes at the right time . earning points earn the players stars , which show how well the player did on a song . the game has 45 beatles songs . more songs are available as download content ( content that can be downloaded from the internet from the game 's publisher ) .
