Tim Waterstone     tim waterstone ( born 1939 in glasgow , scotland ) is the founder of waterstones . waterstones sells books in 288 stores and on the waterstones website . there are waterstones shops in the united kingdom , the netherlands , belgium and ireland . in 1973 , waterstone started working at w.h . smith . he was fired in 1981. he started waterstone 's in 1982. the name was later changed to waterstones .
