This Film Is Not Yet Rated     this film is not yet rated is a documentary movie . it is about discrepancies in the mpaa rating systems . this movie was originally rated nc-17 ( no children 17 or under admitted ) because of `` some graphic sexual content '' . however , it was released without a rating . one discrepancy covered in this movie is between the way violence and sexual behaviors are treated . another is between how homosexual and heterosexual materials are treated in independent movies vs. hollywood movies . according to some interviewed in this movie , the mpaa often treats homosexual issues in movies much harder than heterosexual content .
