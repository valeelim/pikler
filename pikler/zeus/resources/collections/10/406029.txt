Bijou Theater     the bijou theater is a gay adult theater and sex club in chicago , illinois . it was established in 1970 , and is the oldest such theater and club in the united states of america . it is located at 1349 n. wells st. in chicago 's old town neighborhood . in january 1970 , the bijou opened on the first floor of a four story victorian . the first movie shown was richard nixon 's checkers speech , named after nixon 's dog checkers . the movie that followed was a gay porn movie . in 1980 , the bijou expanded to the second floor , where a sexual playground was established . on this floor are booths , glory holes , a dungeon with slings , and a saint andrew 's cross . the same year , the back yard was overhauled for summer frolicking . the bijou was opened 24 hours a day , 7 days a week . in 1984 , the bijou expanded to the third floor .
