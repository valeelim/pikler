Australian Academy of Science     the australian academy of science is the highest institution for sciences in australia . it was established in 1954. the academy is based in canberra in the shine dome building beside the australian national university campus . the academy is modelled after the royal society and operates under a royal charter . it is independent from the government . the purpose of the academy is to promote science and science education in australia . other websites . - australian academy of science - australian academy of science building
