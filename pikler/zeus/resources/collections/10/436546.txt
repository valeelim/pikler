Eduardo Coutinho     eduardo coutinho ( may 11 , 1933 - february 2 , 2014 ) was a brazilian movie director , screenwriter , and actor . he directed and wrote the script to the 1967 popular brazilian movie , `` abc do amor '' . it was made near the beginning of his career . the movie was entered into the 17th berlin international film festival . he died on february 2 , 2014 in a suspected murder in rio de janeiro , aged 80 .
