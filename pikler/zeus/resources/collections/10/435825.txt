Lars Andreas Larssen     lars andreas larssen ( 27 march 1935 – 29 january 2014 ) was a norwegian stage , movie and television actor . his career began in 1957 when he starred in the movie `` nine lives '' . in 1959 , larssen starred in a bigger role in the movie `` the master and his servants '' . larssen was born on 27 march 1935 in hadsel , norway . he studied acting at the folketeatret . larssen was diagnosed with alzheimer 's disease in 2003 and was forced to retire due to his illness . he lived in oslo during his last years with his wife monna tandberg . larssen died on 29 january 2014 in oslo from the disease , aged 78. other websites . - lars andreas larssen på sokkel . larssen 's bust unveiled in melbu ( 2008 ) .
