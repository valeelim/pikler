Michael Anderson     michael joseph anderson , sr. ( born 30 january 1920 ) is a british movie director . he is best known for directing `` the dam busters '' ( 1955 ) , `` around the world in 80 days '' ( 1956 ) and `` logan 's run '' ( 1976 ) . he is the step-grandfather of laurie holden . other websites . - michael anderson biography at bfi screenonline
