Sri Lankan Civil War     the sri lankan civil war was a war fought in the island of sri lanka . it started on 23 july 1983. there was a revolt against the government by the terrorist organization liberation tigers of tamil eelam ( also called the tamil tigers ) . they are a separatist militant organisation which fought to create an independent state for tamil people called tamil eelam in the north and the east of the island . the war lasted for 26 years . the sri lankan military defeated the tamil tigers in may 2009 .
