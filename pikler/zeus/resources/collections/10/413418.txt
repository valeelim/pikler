Sophie Germain prime     a sophie germain prime is a type of prime number . if a prime number is still prime after being multiplied by 2 and adding 1 to the answer , then the original prime is a sophie germain prime . a mathematical definition is : a prime number , denoted by `` p '' , is a sophie germain prime if `` 2p+1 '' is also a prime number . `` 2p+1 '' is called a safe prime if it is a prime number . sophie germain primes are named after the french mathematician sophie germain . many mathematicians believe that there are an infinite amount of sophie germain primes , but this has not been proven . 11 is a sophie germain prime because 23 ( ( 2 × 11 ) + 1 = 23 ) is also a prime number . 13 is a prime number but not a sophie germain prime because 27 ( ( 2 × 13 ) + 1 = 27 ) is not a prime number .
