Amber Tamblyn     amber rose tamblyn ( born may 14 , 1983 ) is an american movie and television series actress . she first became known through `` general hospital '' , an abc soap opera . from 2003 to 2005 , she starred in `` joan of arcadia '' . she also acted in the movies `` the sisterhood of the traveling pants '' and `` django unchained '' . her father is golden globe award-winning actor russ tamblyn .
