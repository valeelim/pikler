Flash Gordon (1996 television series)    flash gordon ( 1996 television series ) flash gordon is a 1996 animated science fiction television series . it is based on the flash gordon comic strip created by alex raymond in 1934. flash has appeared in several movie serials , a 1980 flash gordon movie , two live action television series , and two earlier cartoon series , `` the new adventures of flash gordon '' and `` defenders of the earth '' .
