Ranil Wickremasinghe     ranil shriyan wickremesinghe ( , ; born 24 march 1949 ) is a sri lankan politician and leader of the opposition in the sri lankan parliament . he was prime minister of sri lanka two times , from 7 may 1993 to 19 august 1994 and from 9 december 2001 to 6 april 2004. a member of the united national party he was nominated as party leader in november 1994. he is also the leader of the united national front having been nominated head of the organization in october 2009 .
