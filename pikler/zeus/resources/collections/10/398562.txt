Jakub Krako     jakub krako ( born july 7 , 1990 ) is a skier from slovakia . he has a disability : he can not see things . he skied at the 2011 ipc alpine skiing world championships . he was the second skier to finish in the men 's visually impaired super combined race and the giant slalom race . he was the first skier to finish in the slalom race . because he can not see , dusan simo was his guide and told him where to when going down the mountain .
