Rockwell B-1 Lancer     the rockwell b-1 lancer is a strategic bomber in the service of the us air force . it is a four-turbojet aircraft that uses a variable-sweep wing . it can carry nuclear weapons or classic bombs . it is only used by the usa . variants . - rockwell b-1a : four examples , only used as prototype and test planes . - rockwell b-1b : one hundred examples , used as bombers .
