Mallika Sherawat     mallika sherawat ( born on october 24 , 1976 as reema lamba ) is an indian actress . she was born in rohtak to a jatt family . the first movie she acted in was the 2002 bollywood movie `` jeena sirf merre liye '' . she acted in the 2005 chinese movie `` the myth '' . in 2010 , `` hisss '' was released . it was her first english movie . sherawat played the main character , naagin . in 2011 , she was in another english movie , `` the politics of love '' . other websites . - official website
