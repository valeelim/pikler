Manuel Angel Martin Perez     manuel angel martin perez ( born january 30 , 1980 in portugos ) is a boccia player from spain . he has a physical disability : he has cerebral palsy and is a bc2 type athlete . he competed at the 2008 summer paralympics . he played in the one person bc2 game and he finished thir . he competed at the 2012 summer paralympics in london , england .
