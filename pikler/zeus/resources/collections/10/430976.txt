Havard Nielsen     håvard nielsen ( born 15 july 1993 ) is a norwegian football player . he plays as a forward for eintracht braunschweig in the german bundesliga . nielsen grew up in the oslo suburb of oppsal . he began his career with the local club , oppsal if . in 2007 he played for vålerenga oslo . on 5 october 2009 he played his first match in the norwegian premier league against viking . he was the youngest ever vålerenga player . in january 2011 , he signed a three-year professional contract with vålerenga . by the age of 18 , nielsen was an experienced player . on 11 july 2012 , he scored his first hat-trick for vålerenga in the match against odd grenland . in july 2012 he signed with red bull salzburg together with his friend valon berisha . in salzburg he scored a hat-trick in the match against admira wacker mödling during a 4-4 draw . in the following matches he often played only as substitute . after only a few matches he went to eintracht braunschweig on loan . nielsen played in all of norway 's national football teams up to the under-21 team . he played in the u21 european championship in israel where the squad reached the semifinals . in november 2012 he played his first match in the national team in a friendly match against hungary .
