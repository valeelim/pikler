Ed Wood (movie)    ed wood ( movie ) ed wood is a 1994 american comedy-drama movie directed and produced by tim burton , and starring johnny depp . the movie is about the movie director ed wood . the movie also stars martin landau , sarah jessica parker , patricia arquette , jeffrey jones , lisa marie , george steele and bill murray . it won two academy awards : best supporting actor for landau and best makeup for rick baker ( who designed landau 's prosthetic makeup ) . roger ebert gave the movie four stars out of four .
