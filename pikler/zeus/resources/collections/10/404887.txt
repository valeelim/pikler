Lotta Engberg     lotta engberg ( born 5 march 1963 in överkalix , ) is a swedish dansband and schlager singer . she participated at the eurovision song contest in 1987. discography . solo albums . - `` fyra bugg & en coca cola '' ( 1987 ) - `` fyra bugg & en coca cola och andra hits '' ( 2003 ) - `` kvinna & man '' ( lotta engberg & jarl carlsson , 2005 ) - `` världens bästa lotta '' ( 2006 ) - `` jul hos mig '' ( 2009 ) - `` lotta & christer '' ( 2012 , lotta engberg & christer sjögren )
