Feather River     the feather river is a river in northern california in the united states . it is a tributary of the sacramento river . the river originates in the sierra nevada mountains and is long , flowing in a general southward direction . the river is an important source of drinking and irrigation water for california . oroville dam is located on the river , near the city of oroville . tributaries . - bear river - yuba river - middle fork feather river - north fork feather river related pages . - american river - california state water project
