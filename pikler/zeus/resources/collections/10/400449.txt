Purificación Santamarta Bravo     purificación santamarta bravo ( born may 25 , 1962 in burgos ) is a track and field athlete from spain . she has a disability : she is blind and a t11/b1 type athlete . she raced at the 1996 summer paralympics . she finished first in the 400 meter t11 race , in the 200 meter t11 race and in the 100 meter t11 race . she raced at the 2000 summer paralympics . she finished first in the 400 meter t11 race . she finished second in the 100 meter t11 race . she raced at the 2004 summer paralympics . she finished third in the 200 meter t11 race .
