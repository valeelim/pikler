Korean Air     korean air is the flag carrier and largest airline of south korea . its headquarters are in seoul . it has two hubs at both airports in seoul , the major international airport incheon international airport and the smaller domestic airport seoul gimpo . korean air has planes from both boeing and airbus , including the boeing 747 and airbus 380. these fly to destinations all over the world , including places in asia , africa , europe , south america , and north america . other websites . - official website of korean air
