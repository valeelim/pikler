The Simpsons Sing the Blues     the simpsons sing the blues is an album of songs sung by the characters from `` the simpsons '' . the songs are not on the television series , except for `` moaning lisa blues '' . the album was released in early december 1990. it included many songs , including `` do the bartman '' and `` deep deep trouble '' . the most notable , however , was lisa simpson 's version of `` god bless the child '' ( originally by billie holiday ) . that song was a major hit during early 1991 .
