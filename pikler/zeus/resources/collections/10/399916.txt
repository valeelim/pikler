Atari Lynx     the atari lynx is a handheld video game console made by atari . it was released in september 1989. the atari lynx was the first handheld video game system to have color graphics . it was also the first to have a backlit display , which allows it to be played in a dark room . the system failed due to low sales . it did not sell as well as nintendo 's game boy and sega 's game gear . the atari lynx had a new design model called the atari lynx 2 which was a smaller version of the atari lynx .
