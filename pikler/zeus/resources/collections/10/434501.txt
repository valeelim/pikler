Gross (unit)    gross ( unit ) 1 gross is a unit of measurement , for countable items . it is equal to 12 dozen , or 144 pieces . in the same manner , a dozen gross , or 1728 items is called great gross . small gross or great hundred is used to refer to ten dozen items . if a shop is advertizing to sell a product '' en gros '' , this means that it will sell larger quantities .
