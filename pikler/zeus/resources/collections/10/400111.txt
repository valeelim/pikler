Mafioso rap     mafioso rap is a form of hardcore hip hop music that originated in the east coast hip hop scene of the 1980s . the genere began to die down but then it was bought back to the mainstream in the early 2000s . the themes of mafioso rap are known for organized crime , mobsters and expensive champagne . lil ' kim 's 2003 mafioso album `` la bella mafia '' sold over two million copies .
