Zine     a zine ( a short way of referring to a fanzine ) is a photocopied newspaper or magazine about music , politics , or another issue . zines are usually photocopied by hand , and made by amateurs who do not have training in journalism . only a small number of copies of a zine are usually made . zines are often sold at record stores . a music zine might include reviews of line music shows , interviews with bands , and advertisements for cds . zines were an important part of the punk rock and hardcore punk subcultures .
