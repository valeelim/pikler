Peter Schulz     peter schulz ( 25 april 1930 – 17 may 2013 ) was a german politician , member of the social democratic party ( spd ) and first mayor of hamburg ( 1971 – 1974 ) . schulz was born in rostock . he studied law at the university of hamburg and after his graduation in 1958 schulz founded his own office . schulz died on 17 may 2013 in hamburg , germany , aged 83. other websites . - schulz noack bärwinkel – lawyers office homepage
