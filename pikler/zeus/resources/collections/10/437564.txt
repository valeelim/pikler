Alison Brie     alison brie schermerhorn ( born december 29 , 1982 ) , better known by her stage name alison brie , is an american actress . she is best known for her role as `` annie edison '' on the nbc sitcom `` community '' and trudy campbell on the amc drama `` mad men '' . she has also been in movies such as `` scream 4 '' ( 2011 ) , `` the five-year engagement '' ( 2012 ) , and `` the lego movie '' ( 2014 ) . brie was born in hollywood , california . her mother is jewish and her father is of dutch , irish , and scottish origin . she started acting in 2004 at the jewish community center in southern california . she lives in los angeles . she received her bachelor of arts degree from the california institute of the arts in 2005. before she was a movie actress , brie worked as a clown at birthday parties . she also performed in theater in california . brie also studied at the royal scottish academy of music and drama in scotland .
