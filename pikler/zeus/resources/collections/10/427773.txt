Heinrich Boere     heinrich boere ( 27 september 1921 – 1 december 2013 ) was a convicted german-dutch war criminal and former member of the waffen-ss . he was one of the most wanted nazis during the 1940s . boere was born in eschweiler , germany to a dutch father and german mother . boere began serving his life sentence on 16 december 2011 at the age of 90. he died on 1 december 2013 in prison custody in fröndenberg from natural causes , aged 92. other websites . - germany : trial opens of former ss member heinrich boere , wsws.org 2 november 2009 - life imprisonment for ss man heinrich boere , wsws.org 3 april 2010
