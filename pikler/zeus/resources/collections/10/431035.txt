Frédéric Back     frédéric back ( april 8 , 1924 – december 24 , 2013 ) was a canadian artist and movie director of short animated movies . he won two academy awards . his first was for his movie `` tout rein '' and his second for `` crac ! '' . back was born in saarbrücken , saar . he moved to canada in 1948. he worked as a teacher at école des beaux-arts de montréal . back died of cancer in montreal on december 24 , 2013. other websites . - frédéric back , agir ensemble | caring together - frédéric back at the canadian encyclopedia
