Political geography     political geography is the study of the spaces of politics and the politics of space . it is the field of geography which is concerned with the relationships between people , the state , and territory . political geography is organized or studied in three scaled groups . - international - national - within nations the british academic halford mackinder created the study of `` political geography '' . other websites . - `` political geography '' at elsevier.com - royal society of geographers ( rgs ) - political geography research group ( polgrg )
