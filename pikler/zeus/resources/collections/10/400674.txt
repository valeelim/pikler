Orosirian     the orosirian is the third geological period in the palaeoproterozoic . it began 2.05 billion years ago ( bya ) and ended at 1.8 bya . the latter half of the period was a time of mountain-building on nearly all continents . it was the time when the supercontinent columbia began to form . two of the largest known impact events on earth happened during the orosirian . at the beginning of the period , 2023 million years ago ( mya ) , a large asteroid hit the earth and created the vredefort impact structure . the event that created the sudbury basin structure happened near the end of the period about 1850 mya .
