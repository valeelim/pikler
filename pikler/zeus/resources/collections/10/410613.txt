Eurocopter AS-565 Panther     the eurocopter as-565 panther is a military variant of the as-365 dauphin 2 's helicopter . it is specially designed for naval missions . but some of them are used by army aviations , like in brazil . as-565 panther are used in angola , brazil , bulgaria , france , israel , mexico , morocco , saudi arabia , and uae . bibliography . - jackson , robert , `` helicopters : military , civilian , and rescue rotorcraft '' ( the aviation factfile ) . london : grange books ltd , 2005. isbn 1-84013-812-2 . - gaillard , pierre , `` avions et hélicoptères militaires d'aujourd'hui '' paris , france : larivière éditions , 1999. isbn 2-907051-24-5 . - frawley , gerard , `` the international directory of military aircraft 2002/2003 '' . london : aerospace publication , 2002. isbn 1-875671-55-2 .
