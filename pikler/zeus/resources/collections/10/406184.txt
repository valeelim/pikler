Miguel Ángel Deniz Mendez    miguel ángel deniz mendez miguel ángel deniz mendez ( born november 17 , 1982 in las palmas , canary islands ) is a swimmer from spain . he has a disability : he is blind and is an b1/s11 type swimmer . he raced at the 1996 summer paralympics . he raced at the 2000 summer paralympics . he finished third in the 4 x 100 meter medley relay 49 points race and third in the 100 meter backstroke event . he raced at the 2004 summer paralympics .
