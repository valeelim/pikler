Narcissistic parent     the term narcissistic parent refers to parents who are affected by narcissism . they are almost exclusively close to and possessive about their children . the parents are especially envious regarding their child growing to independence . in other words , narcissistic parents often feel senses of losses when their children grow to adulthood . the parents carry self-esteem problems and extreme needs regarding how others may look at or regard them . children are often punished very strongly if narcissistic parents are not responded to in adequacy . the behavior of such parents is not flexible , and they have an inability to admit to their own fault or feel sympathy and empathy .
