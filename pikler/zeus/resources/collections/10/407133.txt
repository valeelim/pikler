Kamba people     the kamba ( akamba in the plural ) are a bantu ethnic group . they live in the semi-arid eastern province of kenya , from nairobi east to tsavo and north up to embu , kenya . this land is called `` ukambani '' . they are one of the largest ethnic group in kenya . they speak the bantu kikamba language as a mother tongue . the kamba mostly live in the machakos district of kenya . the total population of the kamba is about 4 million . the kamba are also called kikamba , kekamba , masaku , ukamba , kitui and mumoni .
