Mariano Suárez Veintimilla     mariano suárez veintimilla ( 8 june 1897 – 23 october 1980 ) was ecuadorian politician . he served as velasco 's vice president from 1946 until he was ousted in 1947 by carlos mancheno cajas . soon mancheno was ousted and suárez served briefly as president of ecuador from 2 september 1947 to 15 september 1947. veintimilla died on 23 october 1980 in quito , ecuador from natural causes , aged 83 .
