Bob McDonnell     robert francis `` bob '' mcdonnell ( born june 15 , 1954 ) is an american politician . he was the 71st governor of virginia from 2010 to 2014. he was on the executive committee of the republican governors association . he succeeded rick perry of texas . mcdonnell was a lieutenant colonel in the united states army . he served in the virginia house of delegates from 1993 to 2006. mcdonnell was the attorney general of virginia from 2006 to 2009. mcdonnell was born on june 15 , 1954 in philadelphia , pennsylvania . he studied at notre dame university , at boston university , and at regent university . mcdonnell is married to maureen gardner . they have five children . on january 21 , 2014 , mcdonnell and his wife , maureen , were accused on federal corruption charges . they were convicted of most charges by a federal jury on september 4 , 2014. mcdonnell is the first virginia governor to be indicted â€“ and the first virginia governor to be convicted of a felony . mcdonnell was sentenced on january 6 , 2015 to two years in prison , followed by two years of supervised release .
