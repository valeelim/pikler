Juan Viedma Castillo     juan viedma castillo ( born february 6 , 1970 in barcelona ) is a track and field athlete from spain . he has a disability : he is blind and a t12/f12/b2 type athlete . he competed at the 1992 summer paralympics in the 100 meter b2 race . he competed at the 1996 summer paralympics . he finished first in the f12 triple jump . he finished third in the long jump . he competed at the 2000 summer paralympics . he finished third in the 4 x 100 meter t11-t13 race . he competed at the 2004 summer paralympics .
