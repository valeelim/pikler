Michael Jackson: The Immortal World Tour    michael jackson : the immortal world tour michael jackson : the immortal world tour is the first of two theatrical productions by cirque du soleil . it uses music by michael jackson . the show was written and directed by jamie king . the first show was in october 2011. by december 2011 the show had sold out over $ 100 million in tickets . on november 21 , 2011 `` immortal '' , the soundtrack album to the show , was released by epic records . there are 20 songs on the album . it sold 43,000 copies in its first week .
