Lawrenceville School     lawrenceville school is a boarding prep school in the u.s. state of new jersey . it was started in 1810 by isaac van arsdale brown , as the maidenhead academy . until 1987 it did not let girls be students . tuition for boarding students coses $ 53,320 plus a medical fee of $ 755 , a technology fee of $ 465 , and tuition refund insurance costing $ 453 .
