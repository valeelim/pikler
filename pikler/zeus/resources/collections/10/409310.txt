GameRankings     gamerankings is a gaming website . the website receives reviews from other publications and gives an average rating . it has over 315,000 articles relating to more than 14,500 video games . gamerankings is owned by cbs interactive . similar websites include gamestats , metacritic , mobygames , and toptenreviews . other websites . - gamerankings - `` high scores matter to game makers , too '' , `` the wall street journal '' , september 20 , 2007 .
