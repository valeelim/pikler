The Well-Tempered Clavier     the well-tempered clavier is the title of two collections of preludes and fugues by johann sebastian bach . the music was written in all 24 major and minor keys for solo keyboard . the first collection was written in 1722 and the second was written in 1742. they were not published until 1801. related websites . - well-tempered clavier preludes and fugues , fugues on piano , organ , and harpsichord ( flash interactive )
