Star Alliance     star alliance is the world 's first and largest airline alliance . its headquarters are in frankfurt , hesse , germany . it was founded in 1999 by five airlines : scandinavian airlines , thai airways international , air canada , lufthansa , and united airlines . varig brazilian airlines was the first airline to join , in 1997. in 2013 , the alliance now has 28 member airlines with more than 21,100 daily departures combined . these flights fly to 1,329 airports in more than 150 countries , carrying 678.5 million passengers . founding member . past members . - ansett australia - blue1 - bmi - continental airlines - mexicana de aviacion - shanghai airlines - spanair - taca airlines - tam airlines - us airways - varig brazilian airlines
