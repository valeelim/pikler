To Venus and Back     to venus and back is the fifth album by tori amos . it was released on 20 september 1999 in the united kingdom and on september 21 , 1999 in the united states . the album has two discs . the first disc is called `` venus : orbiting '' . it has eleven new songs . the second disc is called `` venus live : still orbiting '' . it is thirteen recordings of song performances amos made in her plugged '98 tour . the album was nominated for best alternative music album and amos was nominated for best female rock vocal performance for the song `` bliss '' at the 2000 grammy awards . in 1999 , amos did a tour for the album , the `` five and a half weeks tour '' .
