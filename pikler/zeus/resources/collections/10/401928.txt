Ruth Gordon     ruth gordon jones ( or just simply ruth gordon ; october 30 , 1896 - august 28 , 1985 ) was an american writer and actress who was extremely known by movie roles that she performed past age 70. she played minnie castevet in the movie `` rosemary 's baby '' . she played maude in `` harold and maude '' . she received an academy award , two golden globes and an emmy award for her acting . ruth gordon was born in quincy , massachusetts . she died from a stroke in edgartown .
