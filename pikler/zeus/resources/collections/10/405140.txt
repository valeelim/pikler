Sheree J. Wilson     sheree julienne wilson ( born december 12 , 1958 ) is an american actress and producer . she was born in rochester , minnesota . she was married ( now divorced ) to paul derobbio , and has two sons : luke ( b . 1990 ) and nicolas ( b . 1997 ) . she is best known for two television roles : april stevens on `` dallas '' ( 1986 to 1991 ) , and alexandra `` alex '' cahill-walker , on `` walker , texas ranger '' ( 1993-2001 ) .
