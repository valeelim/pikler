Saarijärvi     saarijärvi is a city in central finland . the municipalities next to it are kannonkoski , karstula , multia , soini , uurainen , ähtäri and äänekoski . saarijärvi has been city since 1986. however , there has been people living here for 6,000 years . in 1749 , `` vanha saarijärvi '' had populatoin of 1,380. the largest population was in 1870 when it was 14,000. other websites . - saarijärvi pages
