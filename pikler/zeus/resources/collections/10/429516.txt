Jang Sung-taek     jang sung-taek ( alternatively jang song thaek or chang sung-taek ; 2 february 1946 - 13 december 2013 ) was a leading figure in the government of north korea . he was married to kim kyong-hui , aunt of kim jong-un , the supreme leader of north korea . on 13 december , north korean state media announced he had been executed . other websites . - north korean leadership watch
