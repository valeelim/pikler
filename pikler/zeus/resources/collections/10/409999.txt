Tomas Piñas Bermudez     tomas piñas bermudez ( born july 8 , 1982 in granada ) is a table tennis athlete from spain . he has a physical disability : he is class 3 player and competes while using a wheelchair . in 2012 , he lived in ogijares , granada . he played table tennis at the 2004 summer paralympics , 2008 summer paralympics , 2012 summer paralympics and the 2012 summer paralympics . in 2008 , he finished third in the class 3 singles table tennis game..
