Endless Love (2014 movie)    endless love ( 2014 movie ) endless love is an american romantic drama movie . it was released february 2014 in the united states and the united kingdom . the movie is about a charismatic boy and wealthy girl . her parents want them separated . this movie was rated pg-13 in the united states , for language , sexual content and brief nudity . it is a remake of the movie of the same name from 1981. they are both based on the novel of the same name .
