Mario Kart 8     mario kart 8 is a racing video game for the wii u game console . it is part of the `` mario kart '' series . it was released on may 30 , 2014. the game is like other `` mario kart '' games , where characters from the `` mario '' series race each other in go-karts . players are able to use items from item boxes to keep their opponents from winning . along with go-karts , the game also has motorcycles . there are 12 players in a race . like in `` mario kart 7 '' , karts have hang-gliding attachments , and players are able to drive underwater . for the first time in the series , players are able to drive on walls and ceilings . there are two new types of vehicles : ufos and four-wheel buggies . there is local and online multiplayer for up to 12 players . players include mario , luigi , princess peach , etc . the first dlc track pack was revealed in october 2014 .
