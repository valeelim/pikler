Zafer Önen    zafer önen zafer önen ( 1 january 1921 çorum – 13 december 2013 istanbul ) was a turkish movie actor . he was known for his roles in `` curcuna '' ( 1955 ) , `` my wife 's lover '' ( 1959 ) and `` flying saucers over istanbul '' ( 1955 ) . önen died in istanbul , turkey from heart failure , aged 92 .
