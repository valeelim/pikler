Reckendorf     reckendorf is a village of 2000 ( 2012 ) in the upper franconian region of northern bavaria . it has a documented history going back more than 1000 years . it is one of the few villages which retains its synagogue structure , which was recently purchased by the village and renovated to be a community center . some elements of its jewish heritage were preserved in the renovation . jews were invited to settle in the village after the decimation of the population of the thirty years war . further reading . - `` kultur und kultus '' by adlheide wauscha
