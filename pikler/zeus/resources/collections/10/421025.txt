Captain Phillips (movie)    captain phillips ( movie ) captain phillips is a 2013 action thriller movie based on the true events of the maersk alabama hijacking in 2009. it stars tom hanks , faysal ahmed and barkhad abdi . the movie is based on the book by captain richard phillips `` '' ( 2010 ) . it was released to positive reviews on october 11 , 2013. barkhad abdi received an oscar nomination .
