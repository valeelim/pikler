Pedro Cordero Martin     pedro cordero martin ( born january 28 , 1972 in barcelona ) is a boccia player from spain . he has a physical disability : he has cerebral palsy and is a bc2 type athlete . he competed at the 2004 summer paralympics . he competed at the 2008 summer paralympics . he was part of the spain team in the bc1-bc2 boccia team game . his team finished third . he competed at the 2012 summer paralympics .
