Striptease (movie)    striptease ( movie ) striptease is a 1996 comedy-drama movie . `` striptease '' is based on a novel by the same name , which was released three years prior to the movie . the stories in this movie are about an exotic dancer who gets involved in serious child custody matters and corrupt politics . demi moore is exotic dancer erin grant , who was earlier a federal bureau of investigation secretary . also in the movie is burt reynolds as congressman david dildeck and rumer willis ( moore 's real-life daughter ) as erin 's daughter angela . `` striptease '' got negative reviews similar to `` showgirls '' the previous year . unlike `` striptease '' , however , `` showgirls '' became a strong cult favorite within one decade after its release .
