ESTCube-1     estcube-1 is the first satellite from estonia . it was made as part of the estonian student satellite program , and was launched on 7 may 2013 on a vega rocket . the satellite is a project in which university and high-school students took part . the cubesat standard for nanosatellites was followed while it was built , resulting in a 10x10x11.35 cm cube , with a volume of 1 liter and a mass of 1.048 kg . estcube-1 was put into orbit by arianespace , using a vega rocket which took off from ela-1 at kourou at 02:06:31 utc on 7 may 2013 at a height of 670 kilometres .
