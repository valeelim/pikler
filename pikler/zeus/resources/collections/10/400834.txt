Outlaw biker movie     outlaw biker movie is a movie genre . such movies are about motorcycle riding rebels . the characters are typically members of an outlaw motorcycle club . the first such movie was `` the wild one '' ( 1954 ) . marlon brando and lee marvin starred in this movie as rival biker gang leaders . harvey lembeck parodied brando in the aip `` beach party '' movies of the early 1960s starring annette funicello and frankie avalon . biker movies were aimed at teenage audiences and the drive-in crowd . the genre took off in the 1960s when the real world hells angels received much media attention . hollywood cranked out dozens of low-budget exploitation outlaw biker movies during this decade . novelty movies of the genre featured all-female gangs , horror and zombie crossovers , and one featured a gay gang . the biker outlaw genre played itself out in the 1970s .
