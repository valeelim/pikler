Kellogg College, Oxford    kellogg college , oxford kellogg college is one of the colleges of the university of oxford . it was founded in 1990 as `` rewley house '' . it was renamed as kellogg college in 1994 to honor large financial donations from the w.k . kellogg foundation . kellogg college only admits graduate students . it does not have any undergraduate students . it was the first college at the university to allow part-time students .
