Sub-Saharan Africa     sub-saharan africa is the part of africa which is south of the sahara . it includes of all the countries of africa that are fully or partly located south of the sahara desert ( except sudan ) . it contrasts with north africa , which is considered a part of the arab world . somalia , djibouti , comoros and mauritania are geographically part of sub-saharan africa , but also part of the arab world .
