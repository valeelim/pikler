Can't Hold Us    ca n't hold us `` ca n't hold us '' is a song by american rapper macklemore and producer ryan lewis . it is from their album `` the heist '' ( 2012 ) . the music video is nominated for best music video at the 56th annual grammy awards in 2014. it was released on august 16 , 2011. the song also features actress susannah wetzel , who sings portions of the chorus alongside ray dalton .
