Puss in Boots (2011 movie)    puss in boots ( 2011 movie ) puss in boots is a 2011 animated movie . it is a spin-off prequel in the `` shrek '' series of animated movies . it follows the character puss in boots , who first appeared in `` shrek 2 '' . it is about his adventures before appearing in `` shrek 2 '' . a sequel titled puss in boots 2 : 9 lives and 40 thieves will be released on november 2 , 2018. the movie was directed by chris miller , produced by guillermo del toro , and written by brian lynch . it stars antonio banderas as puss in boots , zach galifianakis as humpty dumpty , salma hayek as kitty softpaws , and billy bob thornton and amy sedaris as jack and jill . like the `` shrek '' movies , this movie is a parody of fairy tales . `` puss in boots '' was released on dvd , blu-ray and blu-ray 3d on february 24 , 2012. the movie has a short animation called `` puss in boots : the three diablos '' . 7.2 million home entertainment units have been sold worldwide . another featured extra short is `` klepto kitty '' ; a three-minute profile of dusty the klepto kitty , a notorious cat in california who steals items from neighbors ' yards . some of it was filmed on a night vision kitty-cam , hung around dusty 's neck by the animal planet network for their own documentary .
