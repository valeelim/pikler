Gösta Gahm     gösta fredrik gahm ( born 7 july 1942 ) is a swedish astronomer . gahm was born in stockholm . he is the professor emeritus of astronomy at stockholm university . until 2010 , he was the chairman of the swedish astronomical society . he is the main initiator of the popular science project sweden solar system . 10997 gahm , a main-belt asteroid discovered on 2 september 1978 , is named in honor of gahm .
