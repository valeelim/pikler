Jean-Luc Godard     jean-luc godard ( born 3 december 1930 ) is a french-swiss movie director , producer , editor , cinematographer , writer and actor . he was born in paris to a french mother and swiss father . the english film magazine `` sight and sound '' called him one of the top three film directors of all time . `` breathless '' , based on a story by francois truffaut , is one of his most famous movies . other websites . - godard entry at the criterion collection
