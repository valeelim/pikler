Natillas     natillas is a type of food . natillas refers to a variety of dishes in spanish-speaking countries . in spain natillas is a custard dish . in colombia the dish called natilla is often served at christmas . it is like a flan or pudding . in peru dulce de leche is called natillas . mexico uses the term for a thicker version of the dessert drink called `` atole leche '' . in costa rica it is a sour cream-like dairy product used as a condiment .
