Quail     quail is a collective name for several genera of mid-sized birds in the order galliformes . the birds birds in this group are often referred to as `` fowl '' . old world quail are found in the family phasianidae , and new world quail are found in the family odontophoridae . many of the common larger species are farm-raised for table food or egg consumption , and are hunted on game farms or in the wild , where they may be released to supplement the wild population , or extend into areas outside their natural range . the collective noun for a group of quail is a flock , covey or bevy . other websites . - quail forever - recipes
