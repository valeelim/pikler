Piers Morgan Live     piers morgan live ( previously known as piers morgan tonight ) is a television talk show on cnn . it is hosted by piers morgan . it started on january 17 , 2011. it took over the timeslot that `` larry king live '' was in before larry king retired . on the show , morgan interviews guests such as politicians , celebrities , and members of the public . his first guest was oprah winfrey . the show was cancelled on february 23 , 2014 and the final episode was aired on march 28 , 2014 .
