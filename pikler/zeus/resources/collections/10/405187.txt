Frances Ellen Watkins Harper     frances ellen watkins harper ( 1825–1911 ) was an african american lecturer , suffragist , and writer . her book of antislavery verse , `` poems on miscellaneous subjects '' ( 1854 ) , sold thousands of copies . her `` the two offers '' ( 1859 ) was the first short story published by an african american . she was born in baltimore , maryland . she married fenton harper in 1860. she headed the african american department of the woman ’ s christian temperance union . she had a hand in organizing the national association of colored women 's clubs , and became the organization 's vice president . she died in philadelphia , pennsylvania .
