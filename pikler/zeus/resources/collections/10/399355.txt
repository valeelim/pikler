Tarzan (television series)    tarzan ( television series ) tarzan is a television series . it aired on nbc from 1966 to 1968. tarzan was played by ron ely . the character is depicted as a well-educated man who has tired of civilization and returns to the jungle where he was raised . the show kept many of the elements of the classic movie series , such as `` cheeta '' . other elements , such as `` jane '' , were dropped . this is because the producer sy weintraub wanted a `` new look '' for the story . cbs aired reruns during the summer of 1969 .
