Avranches     avranches is a commune in the manche department . it is in the lower normandy region in northwestern france . it is a subprefecture of the department . the inhabitants are called `` avranchinais '' . avranches is at the south of the cotentin peninsula . the town was built on high ground . it overlooks the dunes and coastal marshes along the bay . from avranches , it is possible to see the mont-saint-michel . twin towns . - saint-gaudens , france , since 1944 . - korbach , germany , since 1963 - saint helier , jersey , since 1982 - crediton , united kingdom , since 1993 other websites . - insee commune file
