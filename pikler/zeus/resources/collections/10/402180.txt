Munenori Kawasaki     munenori kawasaki ( 川﨑 宗則 , kawasaki munenori ) ( born june 3 , 1981 ) is a japanese baseball player . as of august 2016 , he plays as an infielder for the chicago cubs of major league baseball . he has also played for the fukuoka softbank hawks of nippon professional baseball , the seattle mariners , and the toronto blue jays . he also played for the japanese national baseball team in the 2008 summer olympics and both the 2006 and 2009 world baseball classics . other websites . - official website ( in japanese ) - munenori kawasaki 's mlb player profile
