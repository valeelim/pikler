Calaisa     calaisa is a quartet from the town of malmö in sweden . it consists of the systers , caisa and lisa troedsson and the sisters malin and anna törnquist . the band participated at melodifestivalen 2008 with the song `` if i could '' , which was knocked out during the karlskrona competition on 1 march 2008. calaisa has released several albums . discography . albums . - calaisa - 2006 - grafton street - 2009 - up to us - 2012 other websites . - calaisa - trelleborgs allehanda 2009 , från kulturgymnasiet heleneholm , malmö
