Valerie Perrine     valerie perrine ( september 3 , 1943 ) is an american model and actress . she has been nominated for academy awards and golden globe awards . she played marcy , the wife of a corrupt policeman , in the 1982 movie `` the border '' . she played miss eve teschmacher in certain superman movies , and mona in `` boiling point '' , a 1993 thriller movie . perrine was born in galveston , texas .
