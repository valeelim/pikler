Alexey Pajitnov     alexéy leonídovich pájitnov ( born 14 march 1956 ) is a russian computer engineer who lives in the united states . he is best known for creating the popular video game `` tetris '' . he was born in moscow , soviet union . pajitnov created `` tetris '' in 1985. the game was first sold in the soviet union . in 1986 , the game began being sold in the west . since then , it has become a very successful series . in 1996 , he started the tetris company , which owns the trademark to `` tetris '' . in 1996 , gamespot named him the fourth most influential computer game developer of all time . other websites . - alexey pajitnov profile at mobygames - `` tetris - from russia with love '' , bbc documentary
