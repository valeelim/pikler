The Fir Tree     `` the fir tree '' is a literary fairy tale by hans christian andersen . it was published with `` the snow queen '' in copenhagen , denmark on 21 december 1844. the story tells of a fir tree unhappy with his life in the forest . he is cut and taken to the city where he becomes a decorated christmas tree . he is thrilled , but the excitement is shortlived . one day he is discarded . other websites . - `` the fir tree '' english translation by jean hersholt
