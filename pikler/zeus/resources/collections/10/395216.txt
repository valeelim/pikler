Memorial for the Jewish Soldiers in the Polish army in World War II     the memorial for the jewish soldiers in the polish army in world war ii is a memorial dedicated to the jews who served in the polish army and died in world war ii . the memorial is in the national military and police cemetery in mount herzl in jerusalem . the memorial was built in 1998 next to the memorial for those who served in the russian army . the memorial was built with pink stones with the symbols of the polish army and the inscription of the years 1939-1945 next to stairs going to ceremonial plaza . it is inscribed with a bible verse from 2 samuel 1:23 : `` they were swifter than eagles , they were stronger than lions .
