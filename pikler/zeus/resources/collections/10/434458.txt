Hideyo Noguchi     he was born in inawashiro , fukushima . he worked at the institute of infectious diseases as a doctor . when he was 24 , he went to the united states . in 1928 , he moved to ecuador , where there were many cases of yellow fever . he went to find the bacteria that caused it . after arriving there , he was able to identify the bacteria as yellow fever , and developed a vaccine . this quickly reduced the number of people dying of yellow fever in south america . in 1928 , while researching yellow fever in accra , he was infected by yellow fever and died .
