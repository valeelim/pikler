Smoki Whitfield     robert `` smoki '' whitfield ( august 3 , 1918 - november 11 , 1967 ) was an african american television and movie actor . he played porters , servants , and african chieftains and witch doctors . he appeared in the 12 bomba , the jungle boy movies starring johnny sheffield from 1949 to 1955 as eli , a friendly native guide . he was born in pittsburgh , pennsylvania , and died in los angeles , california . he is buried in pierce brothers valhalla memorial park , hollywood , california .
