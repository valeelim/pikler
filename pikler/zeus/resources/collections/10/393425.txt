Chris Cooper     christopher w. `` chris '' cooper ( born july 9 , 1951 ) is an american actor . he is known for his role as john laroche in the 2002 movie `` adaptation '' for which he won an academy award and an golden globe award for best supporting actor . cooper was born on july 9 , 1951 in kansas city , missouri . he was raised in houston , texas . cooper studied at stephens college and at the university of missouri . he was married to marianne leone since 1983. they had one son , jesse cooper who died in 2005 from sudep . cooper now lives in kingston , massachusetts .
