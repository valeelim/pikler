Kanta-Häme     kanta-häme is a region in finland . kanta-häme has many features that are similar to the finnish lakelands . neighbouring regions are uusimaa , päijänne tavastia , pirkanmaa and southwest finland . torronsuo national park and liesjärvi national park are both located in kanta-häme , near tammela . cities marked bold : - forssa - hattula - hausjärvi - humppila - hämeenlinna - janakkala - jokioinen - loppi - riihimäki - tammela - ypäjä traditional food . - lammin sahti - mämmi other websites . - hämeen liitto
