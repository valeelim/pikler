Gossip (band)    gossip ( band ) gossip is an american indie rock band . they started in 1999 in olympia , washington as the gossip . the main singer is beth ditto . brace paine is the guitarist and hannah blilie is the drummer . the band became popular with their third album `` standing in the way of control '' ( 2006 ) . three singles were released from the album . the first single , `` standing in the way of control '' , reached number seven in the uk singles chart . gossip 's fourth album `` music for men '' was released in 2009 by columbia records . their 2009 single `` heavy cross '' reached number two in germany 's chart . other websites . - official website
