Snow skin mooncake     snow skin mooncake ( also known as snowy mooncake , ice skin mooncake or crystal mooncake ) is a chinese food which is often eaten during the mid-autumn festival . a bakery in hong kong created snow skin mooncakes in 1989. snow skin mooncake is not baked . traditional mooncakes are made with egg yolks and lotus seed paste . therefore , there is a lot of sugar and oil in them . because some customers think traditional mooncakes are oily , the bakery used fruit for filling . also , less oil is used to make a healthier mooncake .
