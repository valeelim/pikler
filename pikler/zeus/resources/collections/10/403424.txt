Beyond: Two Souls    beyond : two souls beyond : two souls is a video game made by quantic dream . the game was announced at sony 's press conference during the electronic entertainment expo 2012 by the ceo of quantic dream david cage . canadian actress ellen page was confirmed to star in the game . the game is the final soundtrack that was composed by normand corbeil , who died of pancreatic cancer on 25 january 2013. lorne balfe took over as composer after corbeil 's death . the player controls jodie holmes ( ellen page ) through 15 years of her life from age eight to age 23 as she goes on a journey to discover the true meaning of the entity aiden , who has been alongside her throughout her life . other websites . - official website
