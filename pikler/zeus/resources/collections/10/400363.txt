Egoz (ship)    egoz ( ship ) the egoz ( , literally : `` walnut '' , originally : `` pisces '' ) was a ship that sank in the mediterranean sea on 10 january 1961. it was carrying 44 jewish emigrants sailing from morocco . it also carried one israeli that was deputy of the israeli government , and two spanish crew members . only 22 bodies were found . they were buried at a jewish cemetery in morocco . in 1992 , the 22 bodies were buried in the national civil cemetery of the state of israel at mount herzl in jerusalem . an empty grave was built to honor the people who were never found . a memorial for all of the victims was built beside the graves . other websites . - this week in haaretz : 1961 / the egoz sinks , killing moroccan jewish immigrants to israel - the ship egoz - state medal - fifty years since the egoz disaster
