Tomáš Plekanec     tomáš plekanec ( born october 31 , 1982 ) is a czech professional ice hockey centre . he currently plays for the montreal canadiens of the national hockey league ( nhl ) . he also played for hc kladno of the czech extraliga . plekanec was born in kladno , czechoslovakia . before playing the nhl , plekanec played two seasons with hc kladno of the czech extraliga . he was drafted by the montreal canadiens with the 71st overall pick in the 2001 nhl entry draft . he played two seasons with their affiliate in the american hockey league ( ahl ) , hamilton bulldogs . he played two games and then stayed in hamilton for a third ahl season because of the 2004–05 nhl lockout . since the 2005-06 nhl season , he has been part of the canadiens . on june 22 , 2010 , he signed a contract to play with the canadiens for six years . he will be paid ca $ 30 million . during the 2012–13 nhl lockout , he played for his former team hc kladno .
