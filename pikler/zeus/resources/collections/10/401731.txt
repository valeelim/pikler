Knockout     knockout , also known as the knockout game and knockout king , is a violent activity played by teenagers . the object of the game is to render unconscious ( knockout ) an innocent pedestrian with one punch . the game can result in serious injuries or death for its victims . players face criminal charges . in 1992 , a teen stabbed an mit student through the heart , killing him . in another case , a st. louis , missouri teenager allegedly attacked a pedestrian who hit his head on the ground and died in hospital . in may 2013 , a 51 year-old man died from his injuries after he was attacked in syracuse , new york , by two teenagers .
