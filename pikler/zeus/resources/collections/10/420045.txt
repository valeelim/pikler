Redback spider     the redback spider is a venomous spider that lives in australia . the female is larger than the male . the female 's body is about 1 centimetre long and the male 's body is about 3 millimetres long . the adult female spider is usually black and usually has a red stripe on its back . the female likes to live in warm sheltered places and makes a web to catch insects and other small creatures . the spider 's bite can be very painful and dangerous to people , because it can inject a venom through its fangs . an antivenom injection is available , which can help to restore the health of people who have become very ill after being bitten by the spider .
