High fructose corn syrup     high fructose corn syrup ( alternate names : glucose-fructose in canada ) is corn syrup whose glucose has been converted to fructose . the conversion produces sweetness of desired types . the material is used in many breads , cereal , sodas , yogurt and breakfast bars . high fructose corn syrup was first introduced during the late 1950s . the material did not come to the united states until the late 1970s into the 1980s . high fructose corn syrup has been linked to obesity , diabetes and certain other health matters .
