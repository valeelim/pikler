Bridget Sloan     bridget elizabeth sloan ( born june 23 , 1992 ) is an american gymnast . she is the 2009 all-around world champion and the 2009 all-around u.s. national champion . bridget was a member of the silver medal u.s. women 's gymnastics team at the 2008 summer olympics in beijing . as of 2013 , sloan is a member of the ncaa university of florida team , and the ncaa all-around and beam champion .
