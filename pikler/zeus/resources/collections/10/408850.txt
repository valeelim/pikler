Four Rooms     four rooms is a 1995 american comedy movie set in a hotel on new year 's eve . it has four different stories : - honeymoon suite - `` the missing ingredient '' - room 404 - `` the wrong man '' - room 309 - `` the misbehavers '' - penthouse - `` the man from hollywood '' the cast includes tim roth , jennifer beals , madonna , marisa tomei , antonio banderas , quentin tarantino , and others . `` four rooms '' got negative reviews from the critics . however , the movie earned back just beyond its actual budget .
