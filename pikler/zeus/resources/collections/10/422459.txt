Spiritual warfare     spiritual warfare is a christian belief of fighting against bad supernatural forces ( demons ) . the existence of bad spirits which can affect humans is the main idea for this belief system . many christian groups have used ideas of this movement to hold back these bad forces . these ideas mainly come from christian demonology . a way to fight these forces is by using prayer . exorcisms ( removing demons ) , laying-on of hands , fasting , or anointing with oil are also used . famous believers in spiritual warfare . - billy graham - pope john paul ii - gabriele amorth - d. l. moody - billy sunday - john livingstone nevius - don basham - derek prince - c. peter wagner - merrill unger some well known people , such as bill ellis , elliot miller ( christian research institute ) , and gretchen passantino have claimed that ideas in spiritual warfare are based off of christian stories that are not from the bible . these stories are not considered true by christians who believe that only the bible is fact .
