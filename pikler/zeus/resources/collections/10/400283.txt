Marcos Francisco Dueñas Jimeno     marcos francisco dueñas jimeno ( born april 9 , 1978 in granada ) is a track and field athlete from spain . he has a disability : he is missing part of a leg and is a t44 type athlete . he is a physiotherapist . he raced at the 1996 summer paralympics . he raced at the 2000 summer paralympics . he finished second in the 4 x 400 meter t42-t46 race .
