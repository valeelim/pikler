Airbus A350 XWB     the airbus a350 xwb ( xwb standing for extra wide body ) is a type of airplane built by european aerospace company airbus . it is a long-ranged aircraft with two engines and a wide body . it is predicted that the plane will be able to hold and transport 250-350 passengers in a first class , business class , and economy class or a normal 3-class setting . in january 2015 , the a350 entered service with qatar airways .
