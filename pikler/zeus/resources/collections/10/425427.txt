The Great Gatsby (2013 movie)    the great gatsby ( 2013 movie ) the great gatsby is a drama movie released in 2013. it is based on the novel `` the great gatsby '' by f. scott fitzgerald . the movie was an australian-american production . it was directed by baz luhrmann . the screenplay was written by luhrmann and craig pearce . the main actors in it are leonardo dicaprio , tobey maguire , carey mulligan , joel edgerton and isla fisher .
