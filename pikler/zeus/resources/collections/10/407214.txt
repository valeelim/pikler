Thespis     thespis , or the gods grown old is an operetta by gilbert and sullivan . it is the first of their fourteen operettas . it was commissioned in 1871 by john hollingshead , manager and owner of the gaiety theatre in the strand . it was not well rehearsed , and lasted only 65 performances . it was generally well received by the critics . `` thespis '' takes place on mount olympus and tells of a theatrical company which takes over the duties of the ancient greek gods . most of the music has been lost , making it impossible to reconstruct the operetta . references . - bradley , ian . 2001 . `` the complete annotated gilbert and sullivan '' . oxford university press . p. 3 .
