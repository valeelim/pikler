Bulbul     bulbuls are a family of birds living in africa , the middle east and southern asia . they are passerine songbirds . there are about 130 species in 24 genera . many live in forests : the greenbuls , brownbuls , leafloves , or bristlebills . most african species are found in rainforests whilst in asia they live in more open areas . related pages . - red-vented bulbul - flame-throated bulbul - red-whiskered bulbul these birds are colorful on their eyes and on their tail .
