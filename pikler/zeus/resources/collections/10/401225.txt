Michelle Alonso Morales     michelle alonso morales ( born september 15 , 1994 , tenerife ) is a swimmer from spain . she has an intellectual disability and is an s14 type swimmer . as of 2012 , she lived in tenerife . she raced at the 2012 summer paralympics in london , england . she finished first in the 100 meter breaststroke . she has been dubbed by the media as `` the little mermaid of the canary islands '' . she is also considered one of the most important personalities of spanish sport .
