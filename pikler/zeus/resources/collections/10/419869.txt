Arrondissement of Montbard     the arrondissement of montbard is an arrondissement of france , in the côte-d'or department , bourgogne-franche-comté region . its capital is the city of montbard . when the côte-d'or department was created on 17 february 1800 , montbard was not one of the original `` arrondissements '' of that department . the `` arrondissement '' of montbard was made in 1926 with the merging of the arrondissements of châtillon-sur-seine and semur-en-auxois . it is the largest in area of the `` arrondissements '' of the department , with an area of , but the one with the lowest population with 60,699 inhabitants and a density of inhabitants/km² . the `` arrondissement '' of montbard is bordered to the north by the aube and haute-marne departments , to the east by the `` arrondissement '' of dijon , to the south by the `` arrondissement '' of beaune , to the southwest by the nièvre department and to the west by the yonne department . after the reorganisation of the cantons in france , cantons are not subdivisions of the `` arrondissements '' so they could have `` communes '' that belong to different `` arrondissements '' .
