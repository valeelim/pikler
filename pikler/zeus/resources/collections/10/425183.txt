Chaff (countermeasure)    chaff ( countermeasure ) in the military , chaff are small thin pieces of metal which are shot out of an airplane or warship . these thin strips of metal make it harder for enemy radar to detect where the plane or ship is . the thin strips of metal also make it harder for radar-guided missiles to hit the plane or warship . the chaff tricks the enemy and helps to keep the plane or warship safe .
