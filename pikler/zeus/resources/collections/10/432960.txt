Swedish Alliance Mission     the swedish alliance mission ( `` sam '' , ) is a christian denomination in sweden . it is mainly based in the town of jönköping . in 2009 the denomination had 13,448 members . within 167 congregations both inside and outside sweden . the denomination was created in 1919 from a merger of jönköping mission society , the jönköping circle christian youth association and the scandinavian alliance mission in sweden . other websites . - svenska alliansmissionen - jönköpings allianskyrka
