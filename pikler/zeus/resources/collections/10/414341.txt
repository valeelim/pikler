Coburger Hütte     the coburger hütte is a mountain refuge in tyrol , austria . it is in the mieminger chain in tyrol . it is a few meters above the drachensee and 200 meters from seebensee . tourist accommodation is usually open from june to early october . a winter room is available . its altitude is 1917 meters . from there , mountain climbers can make a few other climbs ( including sonnenspizte , taja-kopf , hinterer ) .
