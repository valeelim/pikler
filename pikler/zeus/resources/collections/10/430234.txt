Hyrule Warriors     hyrule warriors is a video game for the wii u console . it is a crossover between `` the legend of zelda '' and `` dynasty warriors '' series . it was developed by tecmo koei and nintendo . it is a spin-off game in `` the legend of zelda '' series . it was released on august 14 , 2014. the game plays like the `` dynasty warriors '' series , using the setting and characters from `` the legend of zelda '' . the player controls link to take on a large number of enemies from `` the legend of zelda '' series . the player can use weapons such as a sword and bombs , as well as link 's spin attack . like in other `` the legend of zelda '' games , the player can get new items by finding and opening chests .
