Home Alone: The Holiday Heist    home alone : the holiday heist home alone : the holiday heist ( or home alone 5 ) is an american children 's criminal comedy television movie . it is set in maine . it is the fifth movie in the successful `` home alone '' series . it stars malcolm mcdowell , debi mazar , eddie steeples and christian martyn . the movie was first shown on abc family on november 25 , 2012. like `` home alone 3 '' , it does not focus on any of the characters from the first two films . home alone 4 : taking back the house has some of the main characters from the first two films but all roles have different actors .
