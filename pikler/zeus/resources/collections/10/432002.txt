Wim Kok     willem `` wim '' kok ( born september 29 , 1938 ) is a retired politician and former trade unionist from the netherlands . he represented the labour party ( pvda ) . he served as prime minister of the netherlands from august 22 , 1994 until july 22 , 2002. wim kok was born in bergambacht in south holland . other websites . - w.
