Mary Kom     mangte chungneijang mary kom ( b . 1 march 1983 ) is an indian boxer . kom is a member of the kom tribe of north-east india . she was born in kangathei , churachanpur , manipur . since childhood , kom has liked athletics and was good at them . she became interested in boxing after watching dingko singh in 2000. that same year , she began training with m. narjit singh ( manipur state boxing coach ) at khuman lampak , imphal . kom has won the world boxing championship five times . she is the only woman to get a medal in all six world championships . she is also the only indian woman to qualify for boxing in the 2012 london olympics . at the 2013 games , she competed in the flyweight category 51kg and won a bronze medal . , she was ranked as the number 3 in the aiba world women 's ranking flyweight category kom is married to k onler kom . they have twin sons named rechungvar and khupneivar . she lives with her family in manipur .
