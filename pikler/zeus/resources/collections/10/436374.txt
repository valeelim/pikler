Knesset Menorah     the knesset menorah ( , `` menorat haknesset '' ) is a monument in front of the knesset . it was built by benno elkan in 1956. it was a gift from the parliament of the united kingdom . the knesset menorah is in the southern part of the wohl rose park called the menorah garden . the statue was first put in the horse garden in central jerusalem and moved when the new knesset building opened in 1966. other websites . - the knesset menorah in the `` shalom israel tours '' website
