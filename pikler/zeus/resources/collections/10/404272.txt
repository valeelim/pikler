The Story of a Bad Boy     the story of a bad boy is a book by thomas bailey aldrich . it is based in part on his life as a boy in new hampshire . the book is considered the first in the `` bad boy '' genre of literature . the story was first published in parts ( serialization ) in 1869 in the magazine `` our young folks '' . it was published in book form in 1870. other websites . - `` the story of a bad boy '' at project gutenberg - `` the story of a bad boy '' at internet archive - aldrich house at strawbery banke ,
