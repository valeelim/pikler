Elegy for Brahms     elegy for brahms is a short orchestral work . it was written by hubert parry in 1897. it is in the key of a minor , and is marked `` maestoso espressivo - largamente - tempo primo '' . the work quotes brahms 's music in several places . it was first performed after parry 's death in october 1918 at a memorial concert for him at the royal college of music on 8 november 1918. it received its second performance in 1977. it has been recorded by sir adrian boult and matthias bamert .
