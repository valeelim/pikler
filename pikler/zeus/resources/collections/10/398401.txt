Tirunelveli district     tirunelveli district is a district of tamil nadu state in southern india . the city of tirunelveli is the district headquarters . tirunelveli district was formed on 1 september 1790 by the east india company ( on behalf of the british government ) , and comprised the present tirunelveli and thoothukudi districts and parts of virudhunagar district and ramanathapuram districts . it is the second-largest district ( as of october 2008 ) , after villupuram district . as of 2011 , the district had a population of 3,077,233 .
