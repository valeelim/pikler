Diana (movie)    diana ( movie ) the movie diana is a 2013 british-french-belgian-swedish biographical movie . it is on the life of princess diana . in particular , the movie is about the last two years of her life after her divorce from prince charles . naomi watts plays diana . the movie was released on 20 september 2013 in the uk . it was condemned almost immediately by british press . in the united states , reviews were negative .
