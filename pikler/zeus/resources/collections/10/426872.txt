It's Classy, Not Classic    it 's classy , not classic it 's classy , not classic is the debut studio album by electronicore band breathe carolina . it was released on september 16 , 2008 in the united states . it was released by rise records . the single `` diamonds '' was released in february , 2009. the album charted at no . 186 on the u.s. billboard 200 albums chart . the album has been compared to other artists such as jeffree star and blood on the dancefloor .
