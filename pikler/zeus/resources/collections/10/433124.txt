Arnold Klein     arnold william klein ( born february 27 , 1945 ) is an american dermatologist . in 1983 michael jackson became one of klein 's patients . he is a member of the advisory board of the aids services foundation orange county . he has published over 150 scientific papers . klein was born in michigan . his father was a jewish orthodox rabbi . in 1971 he got his doctorate in medicine from the university of pennsylvania school of medicine . klein is gay . other websites . - official website
