Hannah (given name)    hannah ( given name ) the name hannah ( or hanna ) is a feminine given name . in hebrew , it means `` gracious '' . as `` hannah '' , this name was used by the puritans in the 16th and 17th centuries . there are many celebrities who have the name . one fictional character with the name is hannah montana . `` hannah '' is very popular for girls . as `` hanna '' , the name is now being used on tropical storms or hurricanes over the atlantic basin .
