Under the Pink     under the pink is the second solo album by singer-songwriter tori amos . it was released january 31 , 1994. four singles were released from it . the third single , `` cornflake girl '' , reached number 4 on the uk singles chart . the album went straight to number 1 in the uk . in australia and new zealand a special version of `` under the pink '' was released called `` more pink : the b-sides '' . it had a second disc with the album 's fourteen b-sides .
