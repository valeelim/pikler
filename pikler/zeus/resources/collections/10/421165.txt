LSD: Dream Emulator    lsd : dream emulator lsd : dream emulator is a video game made for the first playstation . the video game was released in japan during the year 1998 and it is a hard to get video game with a small number of copies made . the game was made by osamu sato in the year 1998. he also made other video games , but they are also hard to get . the game was inspired by a dream diary that was written by a woman called hiroko nishikawa , she worked in a company called asmik ace entertainment . this game can only be played by one person .
