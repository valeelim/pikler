Alex Cross     alex cross is an american crime thriller movie . it was released in october 2012. it is about a police detective and psychologist who lives in detroit with his family . he would like to leave detroit for washington , d.c.. but when his wife is killed , alex goes looking for evidence against the killer . tyler perry plays the title character . cross was previously played by morgan freeman in `` kiss the girls '' and `` along came a spider '' . the movie critics did not give good reviews for `` alex cross '' . the movie did not do well at the box office .
