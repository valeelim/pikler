Enontekiö     enontekiö is a municipality in lapland , finland . as of 2013 , about 1,890 people lived there . neighbouring municipalities are inari , kittilä and muonio . enontekiö shares a border with norway and sweden . the point where the borders of finland , norway and sweden meet is treriksröset . this is located about 10 km west of kilpisjärvi village . a monument was built at treriksröset in 1926. it replaced an earlier monument built of stone in 1897. sights . - saana mountain - halti mountain - pihtsusköngäs , 17 meters higs waterfall other websites . - community website
