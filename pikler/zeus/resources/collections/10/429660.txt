François Bozizé     françois bozizé yangouvonda ( born 14 october 1946 ) is a central african politician . he was elected president of the central african republic in 2003. his predecessor was ange-félix patassé . his term ended in 2013. he was succeeded by michel djotodia . other websites . - jean-marc aractingi , la politique à mes trousses ( politics at my heels ) , editions l'harmattan , paris , 2006 , central africa chapter ( isbn 978-2-296-00469-6 ) .
