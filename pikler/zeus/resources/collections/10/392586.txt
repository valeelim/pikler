Seattle Metropolitans     the seattle metropolitans were an american professional ice hockey team in the pacific coast hockey association ( pchl ) , based in seattle , washington from 1915 to 1924. in 1917 , the metropolitans became the first american team to win the stanley cup when they defeated the montreal canadiens 3 game to 1. they played their home game at the seattle ice arena . other websites . - metropolitans on seattlehockey.net - results on hockeydb.com
