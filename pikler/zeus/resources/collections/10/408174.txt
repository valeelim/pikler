Patricia Routledge     patricia routledge ( born 17 february 1929 ) is an english retired character comedy actress and singer . she has received several awards . she received a tony award in 1968 and an olivier award in 1988 , both for best actress in a musical . she played the character hyacinth bucket in the british sitcom `` keeping up appearances '' . she also performed on stage , including the characters hippolyta in `` a midsummer night 's dream '' and the title character for `` little mary sunshine '' . routledge was born in tranmere , cheshire , england .
