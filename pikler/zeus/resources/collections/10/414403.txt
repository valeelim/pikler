Tommy Chong     thomas b. kin `` tommy '' chong ( born may 24 , 1938 ) is a canadian-american comedian , actor , writer , director , activist , and musician . he is well known for his marijuana-themed cheech & chong comedy movies with cheech marin , as well as playing the character leo on fox 's `` that '70s show '' . chong was born on may 24 , 1938 in edmonton , alberta , canada . he was raised in calgary , alberta . chong studied at crescent heights high school . chong first married maxine sneed , from 1960 until their divorce in 1970. they had two daughters , robbi and rae dawn . his second marriage was to shelby fiddis in 1975. they had three children . he announced on june 9 , 2012 , that he has prostate cancer . he described the cancer `` as a slow stage one [ that i 've ] had for a long time . '' he said that he was drug-free for about three years , during which time he began having prostate-related problems . on july 15 , 2012 chong tweeted that the hemp oil he has been using to treat his cancer is working and that `` he is 99 % cancer free '' . however , on june 16 , 2015 , chong stated he has rectal cancer and was `` using cannabis like crazy '' in order to endure the treatment .
