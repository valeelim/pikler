Leksands IF     leksan leksands if is an ice hockey club in leksand in sweden . it was established in 1919. the club adopted the game of ice hockey in 1938. it won the swedish national championship in 1969 , 1973 , 1974 and 1975. it lost the swedish national finals in 1959 , 1964 , 1971 , 1972 , 1975 and 1989. other websites . - official website
