Man in the Mirror     `` man in the mirror '' is a song by michael jackson . it was written by siedah garrett and glen ballard . it was released as the fourth single from jackson 's seventh album , `` bad '' . it was nominated for record of the year at the grammy awards . it reached number one in the `` billboard '' hot 100. in 2009 michael jackson died and the song entered the charts again . it reached number two in the uk singles chart . this was much higher than when the song was first released in 1988 .
