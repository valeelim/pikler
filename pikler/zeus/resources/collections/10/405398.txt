Chips (band)    chips ( band ) chips was a country and schlager group from sweden . it was active from 1979 to 1983. when consisting of elisabeth andreassen and kikki danielsson , the group represented sweden in the eurovision song contest 1982 with the song `` dag efter dag '' , finishing eighth . members . - britta johansson ( 1979-1981 ) - elisabeth andreassen ( 1980-1982 ) - kikki danielsson ( 1979-1982 ) - lasse holm ( 1979-1981 , songwriter from 1982 ) discography . albums . - chips ( 1980 ) - having a party ( 1982 ) - 20 bästa låtar ( 1997 compilation album ) singles . - `` mycke ' mycke ' mer/ca n't get over you ( 1980 ) '' - `` god morgon/it takes more than a minute ( 1981 ) '' - `` dag efter dag/här kommer solen ( 1982 ) ''
