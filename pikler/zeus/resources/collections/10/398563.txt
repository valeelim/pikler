Takeshi Suzuki     takeshi suzuki ( 鈴木 猛史 suzuki takeshi ? ) ( born may 1 , 1988 ) is a skier from japan . he has a disability and uses a wheelchair . he skied at the 2011 ipc alpine skiing world championships and was the first skier to finish in the sitting men 's super combined race and in the slalom race . he was the second skier to finish in the giant slalom .
