Robyn     robin miriam carlsson ( or robyn ; june 12 , 1979 ) is a swedish recording artist and musician . she first became well-known worldwide during the late 1990s . she had a quite a few hit songs , including `` do you know ( what it takes ) '' from her first record `` robyn is here '' . other singles include `` dancing on my own '' , `` be mine ! '' and `` crash and burn girl '' . robyn was born in stockholm , sweden . she has been nominated for four grammy awards . robyn is engaged to max vitali .
