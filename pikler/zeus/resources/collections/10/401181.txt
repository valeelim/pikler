Light Years (Kylie Minogue album)    light years ( kylie minogue album ) light years is the seventh studio album by australian singer kylie minogue . it was released on 25 september 2000 by parlophone records . the album contains five singles , `` spinning around '' , `` on a night like this '' , the duet `` kids '' with robbie williams , `` please stay '' and `` your disco needs you '' . the album reached number one on the australian charts . other websites . - `` light years '' on kylie minogue 's official website
