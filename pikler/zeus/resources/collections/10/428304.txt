Don't Wanna Fall in Love    do n't wan na fall in love `` do n't wan na fall in love '' ( also titled `` do n't want to fall in love '' ) is a 1990 pop and romantic dance song . it peaked at # 2 across the `` billboard '' in spring of 1990. a new jack swing remix for the song was released later that year . the single is by jane child . this is the only top 40 charting single from child . in the united kingdom , however , it peaked at only # 22 due to child not wanting to appear on a popular british television show for fear of being classified a sellout .
