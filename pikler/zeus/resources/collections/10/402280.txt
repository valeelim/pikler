Harpoon     a harpoon is a long spear-like instrument used in fishing to catch fish or large marine mammals such as whales . it does this task by impaling the target animal , allowing the fishermen to use a rope or chain attached to the butt of the projectile to catch the animal . a harpoon can also be used as a weapon . typically , a harpoon for fishing has backwards-pointing barbs which stop the fish getting free .
