Anxiety disorder     the term anxiety disorder is a term for a number of psychiatric disorders . these disorders can be classified into two very broad groups : 1. disorders that are marked by extreme worry about the past , present or future . 1. disorders that are marked by fear of a given object or situation . anxiety conditions include phobia , social anxiety disorder and generalized anxiety disorder . other anxiety disorders include agoraphobia and panic disorder . stress from life events , including past abuse , being intimidated or health worries , can trigger anxiety problems for many people . symptoms include headache , spasms in the muscle , trembling , fatigue and exhaustion . there are various manners for treating anxiety disorders . these include medication and therapy . there is some recent research .
