Jessie Ware     jessica lois `` jessie '' ware ( born 15 october 1985 ) is a british singer-songwriter . she signed a record agreement with pmr records . she is best known for her 2012 single `` wildest moments '' . she was a backing vocalist for joker and sbtrkt . `` clash '' magazine called ware `` the missing link between adele , sbtrkt and sade '' . the editor also gave a positive review on her debut album , `` devotion '' . the website , idolator , named it one of best albums of 2012. on the 17th june 2013 ware was named as the skiddle artist of the week .
