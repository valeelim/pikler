Românii au talent     românii au talent ( ) is a tv show which started on 18 february 2011. the project is a franchise of got talent , developed by simco limited . the hosts of the show are smiley and pavel bartoş . the judges are the romanian singer andra , pro tv star and presenter andi moisescu and a well known romanian dancer , mihai petre . it started on the a romanian tv channel , protv .
