We Are the World     `` we are the world '' is a song written by michael jackson and lionel richie . it was first performed by the band usa for africa in 1985. it was produced by quincy jones . it was meant to raise funds and awareness for the poor in africa . it sold over 20 million copies . 800,000 records were sent out when the single was released . they were all sold in three days . it won three grammy awards . at the 1985 mtv video music awards it won best group video and viewer 's choice . the video showed the song being recorded at a & m recording studios . it was sold as a video cassette . the group artists for haiti released their own version of the song in 2010. it was titled `` we are the world 25 for haiti '' . it was a charity song to help victims of the earthquake in haiti .
