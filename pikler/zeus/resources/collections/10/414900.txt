Dauberval     jean bercher ( known as dauberval or d'auberval ; montpellier , august 19 , 1742 - tours , february 14 , 1802 ) was a french ballet dancer and choreographer . he was a pupil of jean-georges noverre and made his debut at the paris opéra on june 12 , 1762. he was a superb mime . he choreographed few ballets , but is credited with creating the comedy ballet . his best known work is `` la fille mal gardée '' , which is one of the oldest ballets still performed . references . - beaumont , cyril w. 1935 . `` the complete book of ballets '' . grosset & dunlap . pp . 1-5 .
