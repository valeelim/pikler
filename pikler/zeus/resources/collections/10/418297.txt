St Paul's Suite    st paul 's suite st paul 's suite is a piece of music by gustav holst . it was written in 1912. it was published in 1922 after some revisions . it is written for string orchestra with the addition of some wind instruments . it was written for st paul 's girls ' school where holst was director of music for many years . the suite has four movements : jig , ostinato , intermezzo , and finale .
