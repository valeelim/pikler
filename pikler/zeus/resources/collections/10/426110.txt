Mohammed Karim Lamrani     mohammed karim lamrani ( born 1 may 1919 ) was the prime minister of morocco three times . lamrani is one of the oldest former living world leaders who are still alive today . lamrani was born in fes on 1 may 1919. lamrani served as the prime minister three times : from 6 august 1971 to 2 november 1972 , then from 30 november 1983 and 30 september 1986 and lastly from 11 august 1992 to 25 may 1994 .
