Ted Lange     theodore william `` ted '' lange ( born january 5 , 1948 ) is an american actor , director , producer , and screenwriter . he is best known for his role as the bartender , isaac washington , in the 1970s tv series `` the love boat '' . lange was born on january 5 , 1948 in oakland , california . he studied at the royal academy of the dramatic art . lange has been married to mary ley since 2001 .
