Serse     serse ( english : xerxes ) is an opera seria in three acts with music by george frideric handel . the italian libretto was written by silvio stampiglia . the opera was first performed in london on 15 april 1738. the opera was a failure . it closed after only five performances . the first aria in the opera , `` ombra mai fu '' , is very famous . it is sometimes known as `` handel 's largo '' .
