Sheba Medical Center     the sheba medical center is a hospital in israel . it is located at tel hashomer region in ramat gan . the medical center was established by the american army in 1941. it operated until 1943. in 1944 the hospital was opened again by the british army . in 1948 it was turned into the israeli army medical center until 1953. today it is one of the biggest hospitals in israel . it does surgery and treatments for patients from other countries . other websites . - sheba medical center
