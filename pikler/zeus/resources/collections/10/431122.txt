NHL 13     nhl 13 is an ice hockey video game . it was developed by ea canada and published by ea sports . it was released on september 11 , 2012 in north america , september 13 , 2012 in australasia , and on september 14 , 2012 in europe . the game was the 22nd in the `` nhl '' video game series and is the sequel to `` nhl 12 '' . philadelphia flyers forward claude giroux is featured on the cover of the game . for the first time in the series , there were two playable female characters , hayley wickenheiser and angela ruggiero that were added . ign rated the game a 8.5 out of 10. they said , `` for a full-price title , gamers get so much more than mere roster and tuner updates ( which come for free and with regularity , by the way ) . you also get robust online and offline modes that could potentially keep you busy for hundreds of hours . '' gamespot gave the game a 8 out of 10. they said , `` nhl 13 adds some great new skating physics and smarter ai , although you 've already seen a lot of what the game has to offer . ''
