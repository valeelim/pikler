Kelly Chen     kelly chen wai-lam ( born 13 september 1972 ) is a hong kong cantopop singer and actress . she sings in cantonese , english , japanese , and mandarin . she has released thirty-two studio albums . in 2009 the world economic forum chose her as one of the year 's `` young global leaders '' . she has appeared in many tv commercials . she is signed to the record label go east entertainment ltd , hk . in 2002 she started the kelly chen children education fund . other websites . - official website
