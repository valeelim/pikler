Division of Northern Melbourne     the division of northern melbourne was an australian electoral division in the state of victoria . it was one of the first 75 divisions set up for the first federal election in 1901. it was located in the inner northern suburbs of melbourne , including the suburbs of carlton , north melbourne and fitzroy . it was abolished in 1906 when the boundaries were redrawn and mostly replaced by the division of batman . the division was held by one member , h. b. higgins . higgins was attorney general from 1904 to 1905. he was appointed a justice of the high court of australia in 1906. isaac selby , who was an unsuccessful candidate in the 1901 election , became famous in 1905 when he tried to shoot a judge during a court case in san francisco .
