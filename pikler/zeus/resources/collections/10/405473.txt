Malmö Redhawks     malmö redhawks is an ice hockey club in malmö , sweden . the club started on 28 february 1972 , out of the malmö ff ice hockey section . the original name was malmö if . the name was changed in 1996. the club won the swedish national championship in 1992 and 1994. it also won the iihf european cup in 1992. other websites . - official website
