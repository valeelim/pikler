Edward II (play)    edward ii ( play ) the troublesome reign and lamentable death of edward the second , king of england , with the tragical fall of proud mortimer is a play by christopher marlowe . it is a history play . it was probably written no earlier than 1590. marlowe 's source was probably `` holinshed 's chronicles '' . the play is about king edward ii 's downfall and his homosexual passion for piers gaveston .
