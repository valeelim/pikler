Turpan     turpan also referred to turfan or tulufan in chinese , is an oasis county-level city in xinjiang uyghur autonomous region of china . historically , turpan is an important ancient stop on the silk road in china . turpan means 'the lowest place ' in uygur language and 'the fertile land ' in turki . now the city is a tourist spot with unique landscape , abundant historical relics and fertile farmland . other websites . - photos of turpan attractions
