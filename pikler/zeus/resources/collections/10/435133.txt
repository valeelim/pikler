Jean-Pierre Thiollet     jean-pierre thiollet ( born 9 december 1956 in poitiers ) is a french author and journalist . in 2010 , he was among the petition 's signatures for roman polanski when the film director was temporarily arrested by swiss police at the request of u.s authorities . since 2007 , he has been a member of the world grand family of lebanon ( rj lebanon club ) . he was , with alain decaux , frédéric beigbeder and richard millet , one of the guest writers at the 2005 beirut book fair ( biel ) for `` je m'appelle byblos '' ( my name is byblos ) . in 1994 , he was the author allowed to interview gérard mulliez , one of the wealthiest and powerful people in france , for the book `` the customer driven company — moving from talk to action '' ( translated in french as `` la dynamique du client '' ) by richard c. whiteley . from 1988 to 1994 , he was editor-in-chief for `` le quotidien de paris '' ( daily press group ) . bibliography . - `` hallier , l'edernel jeune homme '' , neva editions , 2016. isbn 978-2-35055-217-0 - `` 88 notes pour piano solo '' , neva editions , 2015. isbn 978-2-3505-5192-0
