The Shangri-Las     the shangri-las were an american pop girl group of the 1960s . they released their first album `` leader of the pack '' in 1965. their 1964 single `` leader of the pack '' was number one in the united states . the members of the group were sisters betty and mary weiss , with twins , margie and mary-ann ganser . their first hit recording was `` remember ( walking in the sand ) '' ( 1964 ) which reached number 5 in the us and number 14 in the uk .
