Little Orphan Annie     little orphan annie was a daily comic strip created by harold gray . the strip made its first appearance on august 5 , 1924 in the `` new york daily news '' . the main characters in the strip were little orphan annie , oliver `` daddy '' warbucks , and annie 's dog sandy . the strip followed annie 's many adventures around the world . it was cancelled on june 13 , 2010. strip spin-offs over the years included a 1930 radio series ; two movies in 1932 and 1938 ; an assortment of chapter books , games , comic books and other juvenile paraphernalia ; the broadway musical , `` annie '' ; and a movie adaptation of the musical .
