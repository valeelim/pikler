Uto-Aztecan languages     uto-aztecan or uto-aztekan is a language family of the americas . it consists of over 30 languages . almost all of the uto-aztecan languages are found in the western united states and mexico . the name of the language family was given to show that it links the ute language of utah and the aztecan languages of mexico . the language of the aztecs and its modern relatives are part of the uto-aztecan family .
