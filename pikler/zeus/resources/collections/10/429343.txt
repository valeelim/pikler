Nadir Afonso     nadir afonso , ( december 4 , 1920 – december 11 , 2013 ) was a portuguese geometric painter . he painted abstract art . he wanted to be in architecture , which he practiced early in his career with le corbusier and oscar niemeyer . afonso later studied painting in paris and became one of the experts in kinetic art . he worked alongside victor vasarely , fernand léger , auguste herbin , and andré bloc . other websites . - nadir afonso foundation website
