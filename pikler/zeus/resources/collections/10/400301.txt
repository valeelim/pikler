José Manuel González Santamaria     jose manuel gonzalez santamaria ( born september 19 , 1970 in asturias ) is a track and field athlete from spain . he has a disability : he has cerebral palsy and is a t36 type athlete . he raced at the 1996 summer paralympics , the 2000 summer paralympics , the 2004 summer paralympics , the 2008 summer paralympics and the 2012 summer paralympics . he was the third runner to finish in the 400 meter t37 race .
