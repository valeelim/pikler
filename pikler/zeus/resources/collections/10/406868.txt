Mthatha     mthatha ( formerly umtata ) , is the main town in eastern cape province of south africa . the town has an airport , last known by the name k. d. matanzima airport . after former leader kaiser matanzima . former south african president nelson mandela was born in mvezo ( a village in mthatha ) . on 2 march 2004 umtata was officially renamed to its current name mthatha . walter sisulu , bantu holomisa , and buyisiwe yonela mhlana were also born in mthatha . other websites . - mthatha town website - the ecological health of the mthatha river
