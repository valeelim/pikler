Henryk Baranowski     henryk baranowski ( 9 february 1943 – 27 july 2013 ) was a polish theatre director and actor . he starred in two episodes of `` the decalogue '' series directed by krzysztof kieślowski . baranowski was born on 9 february 1943 in tarnopol , poland ( now ternopil , ukraine ) . he studied at the university of warsaw . his career began in 1969. he retired in 2006. baranowski died on 27 july 2013 in warsaw , poland from an illness , aged 70 .
