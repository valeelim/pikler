Soufrière Hills     the soufrière hills volcano is an active stratovolcano on the caribbean island of montserrat . after being dormant for a long time , it became active in 1995. it has been active since then . because of its eruptions , no one can live on the south half of montserrat . the capital city , plymouth , was destroyed . about two thirds of the population had to leave montserrat . it had been showing signs of erupting for a long time before it did . when it did erupt , 19 out of the 23 people that remained burned to death because of the pyroclastic flow which was .
