Black Panties (R. Kelly album)    black panties ( r. kelly album ) black panties is the twelfth solo studio album by r & b singer r. kelly . it was going to be released on 11 november 2013 , by rca music group . but some changes were made to the album . it was released on december 6 2013. kelly said that this album is different from his previous albums `` love letter '' and `` write me back '' . the first single `` my story '' was released on 28 july 2013. genius got released a month later on itunes and r. kelly 's vevo page . on december 10 , 2013 , the music video was released for `` cookie '' . the album featured guest appearances by 2 chainz , future , young jeezy , twista , kelly rowland , alicia keys and ludacris . but only ludacris , kelly rowland and young jeezy made the final cut of the album . music critics gave `` black panties '' good reviews . kelly said that he made 362 songs for `` black panties '' . `` black panties '' entered the billboard 200 at number four .
