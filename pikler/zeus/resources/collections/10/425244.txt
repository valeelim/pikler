M-60 machine gun     the m60 is a type of american-made machine gun . the m60 fires 7.62mm nato cartridges . the cartridges are fed into the gun using a belt . the united states army started to use the m60 in 1957. it has been used by all the branches of the u.s. military . the united states army used the m60 during the vietnam war , gulf war , afghanistan war and the iraq war . recently , the u.s. military has started to replace the m60 with the m240 machine gun .
