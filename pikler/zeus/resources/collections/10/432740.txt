St Peter's College, Oxford    st peter 's college , oxford st peter 's college is one of the colleges of the university of oxford . the college was founded in 1929. it became a full college of the university in 1961. the college is located on the site of the former new hall inn . its oldest buildings are linden house ( 1794 ) and canal house ( 1828 ) . both were former headquarters of the oxford canal company .
