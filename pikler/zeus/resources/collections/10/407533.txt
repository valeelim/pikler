Black Dog     black dog is a 1998 action thriller movie . patrick swayze plays jack crews , brenda strong plays melanie crews . randy travis plays earl . the movie is about a trucker who at first refuses a job , but then learns that his house will be repossessed unless he pays his debt . this movie was rated pg-13 when it was released in the united states in may 1998. it earned back more than usd $ 12 million in the box office . rotten tomatoes gave `` black dog '' an 11 % rating , meaning rotten .
