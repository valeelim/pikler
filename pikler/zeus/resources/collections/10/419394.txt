Caius (jurist)    caius ( jurist ) caius or gaius was a lawyer who lived in the 2nd century . very little is known about his life , but he left a scholarly work on roman law , called institutiones . the law of citations , introduced in the year 426 made him one of the authorities of roman law , together with ulpian , paulus , papianus and modestinus . his works were written between ad 130 and 180 .
