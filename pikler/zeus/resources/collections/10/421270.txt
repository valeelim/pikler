Tata Institute of Fundamental Research     the tata institute of fundamental research ( tifr ) is a research organization in india . it is for studies in mathematics and science . it is located in mumbai . tifr was started by jenhangir ratanji dadabhoy tata . it is now government funded . this institute has various departments like physics , chemistry , astronomy , and mathematics . one of its founding members was physicist homi bhabha . it was inaugurated by prime minister sri jawaharlal nehru . professors in this organization are recruited from institutes including the illinois institute of technology .
