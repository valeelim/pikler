Ali Zeidan     ali zeidan ( sometimes written as zidan ; ; born 1950 ) is a former prime minister of libya . he was appointed by the general national congress on 14 october 2012. zeidan took office on 14 november after congress approved his cabinet nominees . before the libyan civil war , zeidan was a human rights lawyer in geneva . according to the bbc , he is considered by some local observers as a strong-minded liberal . it was reported on 10 october 2013 that zeidan was kidnapped by armed men in tripoli , during the early hours in the morning . he was freed hours later .
