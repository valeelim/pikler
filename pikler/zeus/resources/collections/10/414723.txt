Yvain     sir yvain ( also called owain , ywain , ewain or uwain ) is a knight of the round table in the arthurian legend . he is purported to be the son of king urien . the character is thought to be based on owain mab urien , who was the king of rheged in great britain during the late 6th century . `` yvain , the knight of the lion '' is a well known and documented story of a sir yvain in king arthur 's court . before being known as the knight of the lion , sir yvain went on a mission to revenge the brother-in-law of king arthur—and to win fame for himself . this trip lead to several more and , in the course of his travels , he jousted various knights , fought a few monsters , fell in love with a widow queen , lost his sanity—albeit shortly—and saved helpless women .
