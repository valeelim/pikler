Dominic Chianese     dominic chianese ( ; born february 24 , 1931 in the bronx , new york ) is an american actor , singer , musician , activist , and philanthropist . he is best known for his role as corrado `` junior '' soprano on the hbo tv series `` the sopranos '' . in 2010 , chianese received the ellis island medal of honor in recognition of his humanitarian efforts . other websites . - `` a conversation with dominic chianese , the sopranos ' uncle junior '' in - dominic chianese - `` downstage center '' interview at american theatre wing.org
