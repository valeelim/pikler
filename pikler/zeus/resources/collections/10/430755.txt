Christine Ebersole     christine ebersole ( born 21 february , 1953 ) is an american singer , actress and musician . she played evelyn tracy in `` black sheep '' ( 1996 ) and muffin in the comedy `` the big wedding '' ( 2013 ) . in 2007 she won the tony award for best actress in a musical for her work in `` grey gardens '' . ebersole was born in winnetka , illinois . she now lives near maplewood , new jersey .
