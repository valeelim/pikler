University of Rhode Island     the university of rhode island ( uri ) is the main public research university in the u.s. state of rhode island . it was established in 1892 as rhode island college of agriculture and mechanic arts . the name was changed to rhode island state college around 1909 , and officially to university of rhode island during 1951. uri is in kingston , rhode island . in 1888 the university was chartered as rhode island 's agricultural school and became the rhode island college of agriculture and mechanic arts in 1892. the first class of 17 students was graduated two years later .
