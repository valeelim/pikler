Characters in Romeo and Juliet     this is a list of characters in william shakespeare 's `` romeo and juliet '' . - romeo montague is the son of lord and lady montague . romeo falls in love with juliet capulet , and secretly marries her . juliet fakes her death to rid herself of a parental marriage arrangement with count paris . romeo is not aware of the fake death . he believes juliet is truly dead and kills himself . leonardo dicaprio , leonard whiting and leslie howard have played romeo in movie adaptations of the play . romeo is also a character in opera and ballet adaptions of the play . - juliet capulet is barely a young adult when she meets and falls in love with a family enemy , romeo montague . the two marry secretly . when juliet 's parents arrange a marriage for juliet with count paris , she takes a potion that will make her appear to be dead . her body is taken to a crypt . she later comes back to life . when she discovers romeo has killed himself , she kills herself . norma shearer and olivia hussey have played juliet in the movies . the play was adapted to opera and ballet . rudolph nureyev and margot fonteyn have danced the `` star-crossed lovers '' in the ballet adaptation .
