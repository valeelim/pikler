Boinpalle     boinpalle or boinpalli is a village and a mandal in india . it is in karimnagar district in the state of andhra pradesh . it is 28 kilometres from the district capital . drought is common in this area . many people from this area migrate to gulf countries to find jobs as unskilled labourers . agriculture is the primary industry in this area . main crops are cotton and maize . rice is the secondary crop . the mandal of boinpalle has 18 village panchayats . 1. ralla ramannapeta 1. rathnampet ( population 1200 ) 1. deshai palli 1. vardavelli 1. kodurupaka 1. vilasagar 1. korem 1. marlapeta 1. tadagonda 1. venkatrao palli 1. anantha pally 1. burgu palli 1. mallapur 1. manvada 1. neelogipalli 1. rajannapeta 1. kothapeta 1. narsingapur
