Death anniversary     a death anniversary is the custom of commemorating the date of someone 's death . it is observed in several asian cultures , including among chinese , japanese , korean , jewish and vietnamese people . the tradition has also been associated with confucianism , buddhism , hinduism and judaism . like a birthday , it is celebrated each year . instead of on the date of birth of the individual being celebrated , it is celebrated on the day on which a family member or other loved one died .
