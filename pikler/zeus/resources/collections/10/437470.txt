Kal Penn     kal penn ( born kalpen suresh modi ; april 23 , 1977 ) is an american actor , producer , and civil servant . he is best known for his role as lawrence kutner on the fox television program `` house '' . he is also well known for appearing in the movies `` harold & kumar go to white castle '' ( 2004 ) , `` harold & kumar escape from guantanamo bay '' ( 2008 ) , and `` a very harold & kumar 3d christmas '' ( 2011 ) . penn was born in montclair , new jersey . he is the son of an indian family . he started acting after graduating from the university of california , los angeles in 1998. he lives in los angeles , california . penn was the associate director in the white house office of public engagement for barack obama from 2009 through 2011 .
