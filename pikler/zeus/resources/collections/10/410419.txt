Division of Oxley (1901–34)    division of oxley ( 1901–34 ) the division of oxley was an australian electoral division in the state of queensland . it covered the inner southern suburbs of brisbane , including south brisbane , woolloongabba , coorparoo , bulimba and maree . oxley was one of the 75 divisions set up for the first federal election in 1901. it was named after explorer john oxley . it was replaced by the division of griffith at the redistribution of 1 august 1934. in 1949 , a new division of oxley was created in the south-western suburbs of brisbane , mainly around ipswich .
