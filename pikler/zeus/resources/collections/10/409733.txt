Jeanette     jeanette ( also jeannette ) is a female given name . it comes from the name jeanne . other variations are jennette , janette and janet . the name means `` god is gracious '' in hebrew . people named jeannette include jennette mccurdy . fictional characters include jeanette miller of the chippettes . people named `` janet '' include janet jackson , janet leigh and janet reno . the related name `` janet '' was used for a hurricane , which in 1955 , caused major devastation in eastern mexico .
