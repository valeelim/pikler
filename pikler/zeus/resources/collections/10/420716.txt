Bambi Meets Godzilla     bambi meets godzilla is a two-minute short cartoon created by marv newland in 1969. it features the characters bambi and godzilla . it is ranked as # 38 in the book `` the 50 greatest cartoons '' , written in 1994. two sequels were later made that were not created by newland : `` son of bambi meets godzilla '' and `` bambi 's revenge '' . in the opening credits , bambi is seen grazing while `` call to the dairy cows '' from gioachino rossini 's opera `` william tell '' ( 1829 ) plays in the background . after the credits , bambi looks up to see godzilla 's foot coming down on him , crushing him ( set to `` a day in the life '' by the beatles ) . after this , the closing credits play over an image of godzilla 's foot .
