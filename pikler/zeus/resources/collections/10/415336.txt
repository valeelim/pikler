So Long, Frank Lloyd Wright    so long , frank lloyd wright `` so long , frank lloyd wright '' is a song written by paul simon that was originally released on simon & garfunkel 's 1970 album `` bridge over troubled water '' . it was released on several simon & garfunkel compilation albums . it has also been recorded by the london pops orchestra and joe chindamo trio . art garfunkel said that the origin of the song came from his request that paul simon write a song about the famous architect frank lloyd wright . simon has stated that he wrote the song despite not knowing who frank lloyd wright was .
