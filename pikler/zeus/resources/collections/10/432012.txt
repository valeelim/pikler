Ginnifer Goodwin     jennifer michelle goodwin ( or ginnifer goodwin ; born may 22 , 1978 ) is an american television and movie actress . she is best known for her role as margene heffman on `` big love '' . she also appeared on `` mona lisa smile '' , `` something borrowed '' , `` walk the line '' , `` he 's just not that into you '' , and `` zootopia '' .
