Daylight (video game)    daylight ( video game ) daylight is an upcoming survival horror video game for the playstation 4 and microsoft windows . it was developed by zombie studios . it is powered by unreal engine 4. it was released in north america on april 29 , 2014. it was released in the pal region on april 30 , 2014. it was released in japan on august 28 , 2014. the story is centered around a woman who regains consciousness in an abandoned hospital . she has no memory of how she got there . with her mobile phone as her only source of light , she must explore the haunted hospital and its criminal past in order to escape . the terrain outside the hospital is randomly generated , so that every time you play it is different .
