Leas Cliff Hall     leas cliff hall is an entertainment and function venue in folkestone , on the kent coast of england . the leas shelter was built in 1894. in 1924 , it was decided that a larger hall was needed . 28 months later , the building was finished . it was opened on 13 july 1927 by prince henry , duke of gloucester . the opening concert was performed by the folkestone municipal orchestra . one thousand people attended . it was closed for modernisation and refurbishment on 16 september 1980. it re-opened on 6 may 1981 in the form that it is today .
