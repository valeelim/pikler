Huun-Huur-Tu     huun-huur-tu ( tuvan : `` хүн хүртү '' , `` khün khürtü '' ) is a music group from tuva , a russian federation republic on the mongolian border . huun huur tu performs throat singing , in which the singers sing more than one note at a time . one of the notes sometimes sounds like a flute , whistle or bird , but is actually a person 's voice . the group mostly uses native tuvan instruments such as the `` igil '' , `` khomus '' ( tuvan jaw harp ) , `` doshpuluur '' , and `` dünggür '' ( shaman drum ) . however , in recent years , the group has started using western instruments , such as the guitar . huun huur tu 's music is mostly tuvan folk music , but they also experiment with western instruments and electronic music .
