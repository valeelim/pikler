Kidnapping of Jaycee Lee Dugard     the kidnapping of jaycee lee dugard happened june 10 , 1991 near south lake tahoe , california . dugard ( born may 3 , 1980 ) was 11 years old at that time and was abducted from a street while walking home from a school bus stop . she remained missing for over eighteen years . she was held prisoner by a married couple . she was finally rescued in august 2009. the kidnappers pleaded guilty to kidnapping and other charges and were sentenced to many years in prison .
