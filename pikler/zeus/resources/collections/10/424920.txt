Karla Álvarez    karla álvarez karla mercedes álvarez báez ( 15 october 1972 – 15 november 2013 ) was a mexican actress . she was best known for her roles including `` maria mercedes '' and `` alma rebelde '' and as a contestant for `` big brother vip '' in 2003. álvarez was born on 15 october 1972 in mexico city , mexico . she was married to alexis ayala until they divorced . on 15 november 2013 , álvarez was found dead at her apartment in mexico city due to respiratory failure . she was rumored to have suffered from alcoholism and allegedly had an eating disorder .
