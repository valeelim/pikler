Bob Gunton     robert patrick `` bob '' gunton , jr. ( born november 15 , 1945 ) is an american actor . he is known for his role as warden samuel norton in the 1994 movie `` the shawshank redemption '' . gunton was born on november 15 , 1945 in santa monica , california . he studied at mater dei high school and at university of california , irvine . gunton was married to annie mcgreevey from 1980 until they divorced in 2006. then he was married to carey pitts since 2006. he has one child with mcgreevey . further reading . - voisin , scott . `` character kings : hollywood 's familiar faces discuss the art & business of acting '' . bearmanor media , 2009. isbn 978-1-59393-342-5 .
