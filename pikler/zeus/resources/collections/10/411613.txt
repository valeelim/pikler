Nokia, Finland    nokia , finland nokia is a town in pirkanmaa , finland . about 32,700 people lived there as of january 2014. nearby municipalities include hämeenkyrö , pirkkala , sastamala , tampere , vesilahti and ylöjärvi . nokia is an industrial city and where products of many finnish brands are made . the most notable is nokia , which makes information technology products . in 2013 , nokia reported that it would sell its mobile phone division to microsoft . other websites . - community website
