Rise of the Planet of the Apes     rise of the planet of the apes is a 2011 american science fiction movie . it stars james franco , john lithgow , brian cox , tom felton , and andy serkis . it was released on august 5 , 2011 to positive reviews . a sequel to the film `` dawn of the planet of the apes '' was released on july 11 , 2014. a third film is scheduled for release in july 14 , 2017. roger ebert gave the movie 3 out of 4 stars . he liked how andy serkis played caesar by saying it was a `` wonderfully executed character '' and `` one never knows exactly where the human ends and the effects begin , but serkis or caesar gives the best performance in the movie . ''
