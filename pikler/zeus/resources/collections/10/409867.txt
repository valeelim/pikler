Newcastle Falcons     newcastle falcons ( formerly gosforth/newcastle gosforth ) are a rugby club based in newcastle , england who play in the aviva premiership . the club was formed in 1877 as gosforth football club and were well known for their successes in cup competition . the advent of league rugby saw their first name change to newcastle gosforth as well as a move to the kingston park stadium . at the start of the professional era the club adopted its current name of newcastle falcons and in 1998 they won the premiership within a year of winning promotion from the second tier , a feat that has yet to be repeated .
