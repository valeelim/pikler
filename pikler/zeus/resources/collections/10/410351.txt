Diocese of Mariestad     the diocese of mariestad ( ) was a diocese of the church of sweden , established in 1580 , and disestablished in 1646. it was disbanded following the establishment of the diocese of karlstad . spanning across the swedish historical provinces of värmland and northern västergötland , it used the mariestad cathedral as its seat . superintendents . - jesper marci 1580–1592 - mattias marci molitaeus 1592–1603 - petrus henrici melartopaeus 1605–1610 - jonas oriensulanus 1612–1646
