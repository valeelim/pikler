Battle of Vimy Ridge     the battle of vimy ridge was a world war i battle fought in 1917 near arras in northern france . from the fall of 1914 until 1917 the german army held this eight kilometre-long ridge . from there they could see the allied for miles in all directions . several times the allies tried to take it from the germans without success . on 9 april , 1917 they attacked again . four divisions of the canadian attacked three divisions of the german 6th army defending the ridge . the battle was won by the canadians on easter monday , 1917. it was the first time that canadians had attacked as a national unit . however it cost the canadians 10,602 casualties .
