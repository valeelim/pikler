Frankenweenie (2012 movie)    frankenweenie ( 2012 movie ) frankenweenie is a 2012 american 3d stop motion animated comedy-horror family movie directed by tim burton . it stars martin short , winona ryder , catherine o'hara , and martin landau . it was nominated for many awards including the academy award for best animated feature . it was released on october 5 , 2012. roger ebert gave the movie 3/4 stars . it is a remake of a 1984 short film by burton . it was released by walt disney pictures .
