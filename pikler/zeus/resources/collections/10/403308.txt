Manuel Buendia Navarrete     manuel buendia navarrete ( born march 28 , 1957 in girona ) is a skier from spain . he has a disability : he is blind and is a type b2 skier . he raced at the 1992 winter paralympics in para-alpine skiing . he was the number 2 skier in the super giant . he raced at the 1994 winter paralympics in para-alpine skiing . he was the number 2 skier in the super giant , giant slalom and slalom . he raced at the 1998 winter paralympics in para-alpine skiing .
