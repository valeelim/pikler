1971 San Fernando earthquake     the 1971 san fernando earthquake ( also known as the sylmar earthquake ) happened in the early morning of february 9 in the san gabriel mountains . it was at the northern edge of the san fernando valley in southern california . the quake measured 6.6 on the richter scale . the damage was severe near the san fernando valley . the quake happened at 6am pacific standard time . sixty-four people died from the earthquake .
