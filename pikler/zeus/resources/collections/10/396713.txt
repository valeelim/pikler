Hanne Sørvaag     hanne margrethe sørvaag ( december 27 , 1979 ) is a norwegian singer and songwriter . she later moved to stockholm , sweden . she released her first studio record `` you know me '' under the name paris in 2002. that gave her three hits in norway . her second record , `` talk of the town '' , was released in 2006 under her own name . she also writes songs for other musicians .
