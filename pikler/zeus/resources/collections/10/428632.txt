Édouard Molinaro    édouard molinaro édouard molinaro ( 13 may 1928 – 7 december 2013 ) was a french movie director and screenwriter . he was born in bordeaux , gironde . he was nominated for an academy award . molinaro was known for his movie `` la cage aux folles '' . molinaro died on 7 december 2013 in paris . his cause of death was of a long-illness . he was 85 years old .
