Cortex     cortex is a word that comes from latin language , and that is usually translated as `` shell '' or `` bark '' . the term is used for different concepts : - cortex ( botany ) , the outer layer of a stem or bark . - cortex ( archaeology ) , the outer layer of rock - cell cortex , directly below the cell membrane - cortex ( anatomy ) , the outermost layer of an organ - renal cortex , the outermost layer of the kidney - adrenal cortex , the outermost layer of the adrenal gland - cerebral cortex , a region of the brain
