A&amp;W Restaurants    a & amp ; w restaurants a & w restaurants is a chain of fast food restaurants known for its draft root beer and root beer floats . a & w was the first successful franchise company : the franchises began in the 1920s in california . the company 's name was taken from the surnames of partners roy w. allen and frank wright . yum ! brands purchased the company in 2002. in 2011 the company was sold back to a group of franchise owners . kevin bazner returned as the ceo . the menu of a & w includes hamburgers , french fries , hot dogs and other fast food style meals . a & w has restaurants across the united states . a & w exists in canada , but it has been owned by a different company since 1972. as of 2017 there were 634 restaurants in the united states and 328 international restaurants . a & w is known for `` frosty mugs '' . this is when the mug is kept in the freezer . it is removed , filled with a & w root beer , and then served to the customer . a number of a & w 's are drive-in restaurants with carhops .
