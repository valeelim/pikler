Doctor Faustus     the tragical history of the life and death of doctor faustus is a play by christopher marlowe . it is a tragedy . marlowe 's source was `` the english faust book '' of 1592. the play was first performed about 1592. it was first published in 1604. it tells of a scholar who sells his soul to the devil in exchange for youth , power , knowledge , and wisdom . the play contains the well known line , `` was this the face that launch 'd a thousand ships ? '' the play was released as a movie in 1967 starring richard burton .
