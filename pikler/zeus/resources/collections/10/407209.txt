Iolanthe     iolanthe , or the peer and the peri is an operetta by gilbert and sullivan . it is the seventh of the team 's fourteen operettas . it opened at the savoy theatre on 25 november 1882. it had a run of 398 performances . it is the first of the operettas to have its first performance at the savoy . the story is about a group of immortal fairies who find themselves on a different side as the house of peers . the opera makes fun of many parts of british government and law .
