Derek McLane     derek mclane ( born june 14 , 1958 , in london ) is an american set designer for broadway plays . he did a master of fine arts at yale school of drama in set and costume design . he also graduated from harvard university . he designed many broadway shows in the last decade , including `` the heiress '' , `` 33 variations '' , `` macbeth '' , `` follies '' , `` anything goes '' , and `` ragtime '' .
