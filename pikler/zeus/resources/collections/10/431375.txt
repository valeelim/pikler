Fernando Luján     fernando luján ( born fernando ciangherotti díaz on august 23 , 1938 ) is a mexican movie and television actor . he is known for his many roles on telenovelas . his career began in 1952 and since then has appeared in over 50 movies and hundreds of television shows . luján was born on august 23 , 1938 in mexico city . other websites . - fernando luján at the telenovela database . - `` el coronel no tiene quien le escriba '' review on the new york times
