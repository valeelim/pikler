Daily Star     the daily star is a tabloid newspaper in the united kingdom . it was first published on 2 november 1978. in 2011 , it made 706,757 copies every day . it is quite similar to `` the sun '' . it tries to be cheaper so more people buy it than `` the sun '' . although other newspapers have their favourite political party , the `` daily star '' does not . however , it was against labour prime minister gordon brown in 2009. to attract women and teenage girls the `` daily star '' has a horoscope column .
