Prince of Conti     the title of prince of conti was an old title at the old french court . the prince held the prestigious rank of `` prince of the blood '' at the court and was also entitled to the style of serene highness . the eldest son of the prince usually used the title of `` prince of la roche-sur-yon '' and at other times used the title `` count of la marche '' marquess and princes of conti . - 1558-1614 : marquis , from 1581 onwards 1st prince françois , prince of conti . `` at his death , the title was not passed on , because of the lack of direct descendants . the title was bestowed in 1629 upon : '' - 1629-1666 : 2nd prince armand - 1666-1685 : 3rd prince louis armand - 1685-1709 : 4th prince françois louis called `` le grand conti '' . - 1709-1727 : 5th prince louis armand - 1727-1776 : 6th prince louis françois - 1776-1814 : 7th prince louis françois joseph , prince of conti `` at his death , the title was not passed on , because of the lack of legitimate descendants .
