Donkey Kong Barrel Blast     donkey kong barrel blast , known as donkey kong jet race in europe and australia and as donkey kong barrel jet race in japan , is a racing video game for the wii game console . it was released in japan on june 28 , 2007 , in north america on october 8 , 2007 , in europe on january 25 , 2008 , and in australia on february 7 , 2008. it was originally going to be released on the nintendo gamecube and was going to use the dk bongos , but was moved to the wii . `` donkey kong barrel blast '' is a racing game where the player controls their character with the wii remote and nunchuk . to move their character , the player has to shake the wii remote and nunchuk , lift them at the same time to jump , and use the controller 's motion sensor to beat their opponents and use items . the main mode of play is a grand prix like the one seen in the `` mario kart '' series . there is also a time trial mode where players can race on one track without opponents . other websites . - official webite - `` donkey kong : barrel blast '' at nintendo
