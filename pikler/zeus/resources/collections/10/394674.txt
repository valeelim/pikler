Ürümqi    ürümqi ürümqi is the capital city of xinjiang uygur autonomous region in northwest china . it is at the foot of the tianshan mountains . with a population of two million people , this prairie city is a major hub on the ancient silk road , leading to central asia and even as far as europe . it developed its reputation as a leading cultural and commercial center during the qing dynasty . ürümqi , whose name means `` beautiful pasture '' in the mongolian language of the dzungar people , is the largest city in china 's western interior . since the 1990s ürümqi has developed economically and is now a regional transport node , cultural , and commercial centre . other websites . - images & photos of urumqi sights
