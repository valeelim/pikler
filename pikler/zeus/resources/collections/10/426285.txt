James Thurber     james grover thurber ( december 8 , 1894 – november 2 , 1961 ) was an american author , journalist , and cartoonist . thurber was best known for his cartoons and short stories . he published his stories mainly in the new yorker magazine . one of the most popular humorists of his time , thurber celebrated the comic frustrations and eccentricities of ordinary people . thurber died on november 2 , 1961 from complications from pneumonia and a stroke , aged 66. his last words , aside from the repeated word `` god , '' were `` god bless ... god damn , '' according to his wife . other websites . - the `` paris review '' interview - the thurber house website - `` thurber 's world ( and welcome to it ) '' by bill ervolino , `` the record '' ( bergen county , nj ) , december 17 , 1995 - pathfinder : james grover thurber – thurber links portal - `` the last flower '' – ballet after an idea by james thurber ; 1975 - james thurber biography , `` encyclopedia of world biography ''
