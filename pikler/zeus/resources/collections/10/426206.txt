Spencer, South Dakota    spencer , south dakota spencer is a city in the eastern part of the u.s. state of south dakota . it is located in mccook county , and 154 people lived there at the 2010 census . spencer became a city in 1917. on the night of may 30 , 1998 , a tornado rated f4 on the fujita scale struck the city . it killed six people and destroyed most of the buildings in the city .
