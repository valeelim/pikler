Tricia Helfer     tricia janine helfer ( born april 11 , 1974 ) is a canadian model and actress . she is best known for playing the cylon `` number 6 '' in `` battlestar galactica '' . in `` supernatural '' , she played molly mcnamara . she also played alex rice in `` dark blue '' . she also played in other television series . helfer was born in donalda , alberta .
