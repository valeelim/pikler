Body Language (Kylie Minogue album)    body language ( kylie minogue album ) body language is the ninth studio album by australian singer kylie minogue . it was recorded during the summer of 2003 in the united kingdom , ireland and spain , and released on 20 november 2003 by parlophone . the album contains three official singles , `` slow '' , `` red blooded woman '' and `` chocolate '' . other websites . - `` body language '' on kylie minogue 's official website
