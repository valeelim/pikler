Nostalgia, Ultra    nostalgia , ultra nostalgia , ultra is the debut mixtape album by recording artist frank ocean . it was released in february 2011 as a free digital download on ocean 's website . it featured the successful singles `` novacane '' and `` swim good '' . it was supposed to be reissued as an extended play , but this was cancelled . the mixtape caught the attention of rappers kanye west and jay-z , who got ocean as a featured artist on their `` watch the throne '' album . the mixtape caused controversy in march 2012 because of the track `` american wedding '' , which is a remake of `` hotel california '' by the eagles .
