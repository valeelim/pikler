Division of Cook     the division of cook is an australian electoral division in new south wales . it was set up in 1969 and is named for james cook , who mapped the east coast of australia in 1770. it is located in the southern suburbs of sydney , including caringbah , cronulla , miranda and sylvania . in 2007 there were claims that the liberal candidate michael towke had broken party rules and not told the truth about himself . these claims were later found to be not true . towke lost his liberal party position and was replaced with scott morrison , former director of the new south wales liberal party . in 2013 morrison became the minister for immigration and border protection . election results . - 2004 election results - 2007 election results - 2010 election results - 2013 election results other websites . - division of cook - australian electoral commission - scott morrison 's website
