John Cornyn     john cornyn iii ( born february 2 , 1952 ) is the senior united states senator for texas , serving since 2002. he is a member of the republican party . he is the current senate minority whip for the 113th congress . cornyn was chairman of the national republican senatorial committee from 2007-2011. cornyn was born in houston , texas on february 2 , 1952. his family moved to tokyo , japan . he studied at trinity university and st. mary 's university school of law . he received his ll.m . from the university of virginia school of law . cornyn and his wife sandy have two daughters . other websites . - senator john cornyn `` official u.s. senate site '' - john cornyn for u.s. senate `` official campaign site '' - cornyn profile at sourcewatch
