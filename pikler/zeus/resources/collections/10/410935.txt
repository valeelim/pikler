Virginia E. Johnson     virginia eshelman johnson ( february 11 , 1925 – july 24 , 2013 ) was an american sexologist and psychologist , best known as the junior member of the masters and johnson sexuality research team . johnson was born on february 11 , 1925 in springfield , missouri . she studied at drury university . johnson divorced her first husband , with whom she had had two children – scott forstall and lisa evans – in 1956. then she was married to william h. masters from 1971 until they divorced in 1992. johnson died on july 24 , 2013 in st. louis , missouri from an illness , aged 88 .
