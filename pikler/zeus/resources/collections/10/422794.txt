Brian Doyle-Murray     brian doyle-murray ( born brian murray ; october 31 , 1945 ) is an american comedian , screenwriter , actor and voice actor . he is the older brother of actor/comedian bill murray and the two have acted together in some movies . murray appeared on `` caddyshack '' , `` scrooged '' , `` ghostbusters ii '' , `` the razor 's edge '' and `` groundhog day '' .
