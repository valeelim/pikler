Cartoon Network (Europe)    cartoon network ( europe ) cartoon network europe is a television channel that broadcasts across europe . it shows children 's cartoons . it is based on the original american version of the channel . the channel was created by turner broadcasting , which is a part of time warner . it was launched on 17 september 1993. the network broadcasts to countries with no cartoon network channel of their own . this includes republic of macedonia , czech republic , slovakia , lithuania , latvia , estonia , belarus , ukraine , moldova and africa . most european countries received the european version of the channel until at least 2000 , when the versions for each country were started to replace it . other websites . - official pan-european feed site - other official pan-european feed site - official uk site
