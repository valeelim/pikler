Absurd     absurd can refer to several different things : - absurdity , very poor reasoning ; ridiculous , or nonsense - absurdism , a philosophy about how the human search some meaning in the universe and the impossibility of finding that meaning - absurd or surreal humour - absurd ( band ) , a german heavy-metal band - absurd , a term used in logic to describe a contradiction - `` reductio ad absurdum '' , a type of logical argument
