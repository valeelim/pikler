Diego De Paz Pazo     diego de paz pazo ( born september 25 , 1971 in sevilla ) is a wheelchair basketball athlete from spain . he has a physical disability : he is 4 point wheelchair basketball player . he played wheelchair basketball at the 1996 summer paralympics . his team was third . they beat the united states 66-60 to finish third . he played wheelchair basketball at the 2012 summer paralympics . his team was fifth . they beat germany 67-48 to finish fifth .
