Fernandel     fernand joseph désiré contandin was a french actor and singer . he is usually called fernandel . he was born on 8 may 1903 in marseille , france . he acted in comedies for vaudeville and operettas . he was best known for his role in the `` don camillo '' series of motion pictures . his first hollywood movie was `` around the world in eighty days '' . fernandel died 26 february 1971 from lung cancer . he is buried in the cimetière de passy , paris , france .
