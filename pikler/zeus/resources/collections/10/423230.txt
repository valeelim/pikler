The Interesting Narrative of the Life of Olaudah Equiano     the interesting narrative of the life of olaudah equiano , or gustavus vassa , the african , is a book that was first published in 1789. it is the autobiography of olaudah equiano . the book describes equiano 's life in slavery . it records his efforts to be free by studying the bible . the book goes on to tell about his gaining his freedom and his success in business . it was published in 1789 in great britain to inform the british public what the life of slaves was like .
