Water-tube boiler     a water-tube boiler is a type of boiler in which water circulates in tubes heated by fire from outside . the fire is produced by the fuel being burned inside the furnace for the boiler . design types . - babcock and wilcox boiler - a/d/m/o-type boiler - flex-tube boiler - forced-circulation boiler - low water content - stirling boiler - thornycroft - white-forster - yarrow boiler use . - marine - railway - stationary
