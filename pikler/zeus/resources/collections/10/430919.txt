Elisa's    elisa 's elisa 's is a swedish dansband from skaraborg . it was formed in 2009. the band is named after its lead singer elisa lindström . the band won the 2010 `` dansbandskampen '' competition . the band was launched by young musicians in musikgymnasiet i skövde ( the music college in skövde ) . during 2010 , the band 's fame spread in sweden . their first single , `` hey go so long '' , was recorded in february 2010. in 2011 the band went to the top of the swedish albums chart with their first studio album `` det här är bara början '' . elisa 's consists of : - elisa lindström – lead vocals , trumpet - markus frykén – guitar - petter ferneman – bass , accordion - robert lundh – piano - daniel wallin – drums discography . svensktoppen songs . - 2011 : `` det här är bara början '' - 2011 : `` en stjärna föll för oss '' - 2011 : `` jag säger som det är '' other websites . - official website
