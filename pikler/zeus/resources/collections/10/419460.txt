Gaius     gaius , sometimes spelled caius , was a common given name in latin . when written , it is often abbreviated c. ; the abbreviation goes back to before the roman alphabet distinguished between the letters c and g. some people with this name : - caligula , the roman emperor - gaius caesar , adopted son of emperor augustus - gaius ( jurist ) , a lawyer of the 2nd century ; one of the authories on roman law - gaius the platonist , a 2nd century philosopher - pope caius , a bishop of rome in the 3rd century - gaius cassius longinus , senator - gaius cornelius tacitus , historian - gaius terentius varro , senator ; commander in the battle of cannae in the second punic war - gaius julius caesar , the roman dictator - gaius gracchus - gaius iulius vindex - pliny the elder - pliny the younger - galerius - diocletian
