Bruce Almighty     bruce almighty is a 2003 american comedy movie starring jim carrey , jennifer aniston , and morgan freeman . it is about a news journalist , bruce nolan , complaining about his life to god , so nolan himself is offered the chance to become god himself and see his responsibilities . it was released on may 23 , 2003 to mixed reviews . a spin-off movie was released entitled `` evan almighty '' and a sequel is in the works .
