Monica Potter     monica potter ( born june 30 , 1971 ) is an american actress . she works in both movies and television programs . she played alison gordon in the 2004 movie `` saw '' , tricia poe in the thriller movie `` con air '' and kristina braverman in the television show `` parenthood '' . she also played lori colson in `` boston legal '' . she was born in cleveland , ohio .
