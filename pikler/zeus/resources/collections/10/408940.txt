William Riley Dunham     william riley dunham ( 1856–1921 ) wan an american politician . he was a member of the indiana general assembly from 1913 to 1915. he represented hamilton and tipton counties . an issue of the `` kempton courier '' in 1912 called dunham `` the man known by name by more men , women and children than any other one man in tipton county '' . notable family members . - stanley armour dunham ( 1918–1992 ) , maternal grandfather of barack obama , great nephew of william riley dunham - ann dunham ( 1942–1995 ) , american anthropologist and mother of barack obama , great-great niece of william riley dunham - barack obama ( 1961- ) , 44th president of the united states , great-great-great nephew of william riley dunham
