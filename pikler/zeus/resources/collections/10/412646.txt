Johor Bahru     johor bahru ( also spelled johor baharu , johor baru , or johore bahru ; shortly as jb ) is the capital city of johor , malaysia . johor bahru is the southernmost city of the eurasian mainland . pasir pelangi , the royal village , is located in johor bahru . the city in itself has a population of 1,334,188 as of 2010. the metropolitan area of johor bahru , also known as iskandar malaysia , has a population of 1,730,000. other websites . - official site of johor bahru city council - johor bahru community portal - johor bahru events
