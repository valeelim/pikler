2010 Winter Paralympics     the 2010 winter paralympics were the 10th winter paralympics . they were held in vancouver and whistler , british columbia , canada from 12 to 21 march 2010. the opening ceremony was held in bc place stadium in vancouver . the closing ceremony was in whistler . the 2010 winter paralympics para-alpine skiing races were at whistler creekside . the types of races at whistler included downhill , super-combined , super-g , slalom and giant slalom . the united states came in first in sledge hockey .
