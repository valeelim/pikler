Asparagus officinalis     asparagus officinalis is a perennial from the genus `` asparagus '' of the asparagaceae family . it is commonly grown as a vegetable , eaten in spring . when people talk about asparagus as a vegetable , they usually mean this plant . it was once classified in the lily family , like its `` allium '' cousins , onions and garlic , but the liliaceae have been split and the onion-like plants are now in the family amaryllidaceae and asparagus in the asparagaceae . `` asparagus officinalis '' is native to most of europe , northern africa and western asia , a. officinalis is widely cultivated as a vegetable crop .
