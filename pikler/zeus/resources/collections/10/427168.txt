Julianna Barwick     julianna barwick is an american singer . she was born in louisiana . she has released three albums . in 2006 barwick released `` sanguine '' . her first album `` the magic place '' was released by asthmatic kitty records on february 21 , 2011. it got good reviews . pitchfork gave it 8.5 out of 10. on june 14 2011 , `` frkwys vol . 6 '' was released . it is an album that barwick made with ikue mori . on august 20 , 2013 , `` nepenthe '' was released by dead oceans records . amiina play string instruments in it . there is also a choir of teenage girls in it . `` pitchfork '' said that it 'sounds like nobody else ' . they gave it 8.5 out of 10. barwick is currently doing a tour for the album . other websites . - official website
