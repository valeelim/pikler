Jokichi Ikarashi     jokichi ikarashi ( 五十嵐 丈吉 jōkichi ikarashi ; 26 january 1902 – 23 july 2013 ) was a japanese supercentenarian who was the world 's oldest verified living man after the death of 111-year-old james mccoubrey on july 5 , 2013. he remained as the oldest living man until his own death 18 days later at the age of 111 years , 178 days . however , two days after ikarashi 's death , the gerontology research group confirmed the longevity of new york man salustiano sanchez , who was born on 8 june 1901 , making ikarashi the second-oldest man at the time of his death . ikarashi also became the oldest living man in japan upon the death of 116-year-old jiroemon kimura on june 12 , 2013. ikarashi had four children , eleven grandchildren , 22 great-grandchildren , and one great-great grandchild .
