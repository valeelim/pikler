Division of Farrer     the division of farrer is an australian electoral division in new south wales . it was set up in 1949 and is named for william farrer , an agricultural scientist . it is in the southern rural area of the state and includes albury , balranald , broken hill , deniliquin and jerilderie . it was held by three cabinet ministers in succession , david fairbairn , wal fife and deputy prime minister tim fischer . election results . - 2004 election results - 2007 election results - 2010 election results - 2013 election results other websites . - division of farrer - australian electoral commission
