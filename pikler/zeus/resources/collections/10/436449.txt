Tor Milde     tor milde ( 14 january 1953 – 15 january 2014 ) was a norwegian music journalist and writer . he was a judge on the music competition `` idol '' in 2005 and 2006. milde was born in oslo . he started working in journalism in 1984. he was best known as a music journalist and critic for `` verdens gang '' ( `` vg '' ) . milde died after a short illness on the morning of 15 january 2014 , aged 61 , at the ullevål university hospital in his hometown of oslo . he was survived by his two sons and his girlfriend , irja anthi .
