Tabouleh     tabouleh ( `` tabÅ«lah '' ; also tabbouleh or tab ( b ) ouli ) is a salad . it is usually made of bulgur , tomatoes , cucumbers , parsley , mint , onion , and garlic , and seasoned with olive oil , lemon juice , and salt . there are other ways to make it , such as using couscous instead of bulgur . the salad originally came from the mountains of syria and lebanon . it has become popular throughout the middle east , and has spread to other countries . it is a popular ethnic food in western countries . in the arab world , particularly syria , lebanon , jordan and palestine , it is usually served as part of a meze .
