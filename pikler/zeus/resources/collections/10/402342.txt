St. George, New Brunswick    st. george , new brunswick st. george is a canadian town in charlotte county , new brunswick . st. george is nicknamed the `` granite town '' . it is located on the magaguadavic river between passamaquoddy bay and lake utopia . it is 70 km west of saint john . the magaguadavic river flows through the town and into a large gorge and the st. george dam at st. george , with caves in the cliffs on either side . the town was founded by peter clinch , a united empire loyalist who emigrated from the united states in 1784. several granite quarries operated in the 19th and early 20th century . the town is centered mainly between route 1 to the north , route 172 to the south-east along the most southern portion of route 770. other websites . - town of st. george - charlotte county guide
