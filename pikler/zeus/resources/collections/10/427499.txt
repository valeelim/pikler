No Ordinary Love     `` no ordinary love '' is a 1992 ballad . it is from the album `` love deluxe '' by singer sade . it was in the 1993 movie `` indecent proposal '' . the song hit # 26 in ireland , united kingdom and scotland . in june 1993 , the song became a hit across the world . sade won a grammy award for this ballad in early 1994. the full ballad is seven minutes and 21 seconds in length . the single edit is five minutes and 22 seconds .
