Human Nature (Madonna song)    human nature ( madonna song ) `` human nature '' is a single from american singer-songwriter madonna . it is from her album `` bedtime stories '' , which was released in autumn 1994. the song was written and produced by madonna and released in june 1995. the song is about humor , sex and sarcasm . it was written in response to the huge backlash to her 1992 record `` erotica '' and her coffee table book `` sex '' . this single got mixed reviews by the critics , although it was a moderate hit in america , australia and the united kingdom . there are many versions of this song .
