Boiler     a boiler is a device used to heat water or other fluids until it boils or before boiling point . the heated or vaporized fluid is then used for various process applications , such as central heating , power generation , cooking , and sanitation . the source of the heat for boiler can be from coal , oil , wood , rejected heat or natural gas . types . - fire-tube boiler - flash boiler - flued boiler - water-tube boiler
