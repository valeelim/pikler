Anne Jeffreys     anne jeffreys ( born january 26 , 1923 ) is an american actress and singer . she was known for her works in `` topper '' , `` street scene '' , and the musicals `` kiss me , kate '' ( 1948 ) and the musical `` three wishes for jamie '' ( 1952 ) . she replaced patricia morison in the musical `` kiss me , kate '' . jeffreys was born annie carmichael in goldsboro , north carolina on january 26 , 1923. other websites . - anne jeffreys profiled in book - huell howser interview of anne jeffreys and ann rutherford
