The Open Road     the open road was a 20th-century american monthly magazine for teen boys . it focused on outdoor life , sports , and other subjects of interest to teen males . it was published from november 1919 to 1954. the magazine went through several title changes : `` the open road '' ( november 1919 ) ; `` the open road for boys '' ( october 1925 ) ; `` open road : the young people 's magazine '' ( april 1950 ) ; and `` american boy and open road '' ( july 1953 ) .
