D. M. Jayaratne     disanayaka mudiyanselage jayaratne ( , ; born 4 june 1931 ) known as d. m. `` di mu '' jayaratne is a sri lankan politician . he was 20th prime minister of sri lanka from 2010 to 2015. a founding member of the sri lanka freedom party , jayaratne was first elected to parliament in 1970 .
