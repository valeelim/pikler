Beginners     beginners is a 2010 american romantic-comedy-drama movie directed by mike mills . it is about the story of oliver , a man remembering the life and death from cancer of his widowed gay father , hal , while trying to work a new romantic relationship with a frenchwoman , anna . it stars christopher plummer and ewan mcgregor . it was released to positive reviews on june 3 , 2011. this movie lead to plummer 's first academy award win .
