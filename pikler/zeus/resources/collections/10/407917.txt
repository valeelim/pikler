Devinda Kalupahana     major general devinda kalupahana , rsp , usp , psc , slac , is a sri lankan general . he was the former general officer commanding of 3rd division , and 2nd division . he was director of operations , general staff and commandant , at the sri lanka military academy . kalupahana was educated at royal college , colombo . he joined the army in 1966 , training to becoming an officer , a cadet officer training at the royal military academy sandhurst .
