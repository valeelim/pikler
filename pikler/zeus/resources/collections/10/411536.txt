The Cabin in the Woods     the cabin in the woods is a 2012 american horror movie directed by drew goddard and produced by joss whedon . it stars kristen connolly , chris hemsworth , anna hutchison , fran kranz , jesse williams , bradley whitford , richard jenkins , and sigourney weaver . it was released on april 13 , 2012 to positive reviews . other websites . - the cabin in the woods wiki
