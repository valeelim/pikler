Francisco José Urrutia Holguín     francisco josé urrutia holguín ( 28 may 1910 – 19 october 1981 ) was a colombian-ecuadorian lawyer and diplomat . he served as 7th permanent representative of colombia to the united nations , 11th colombia ambassador to united states , as well as ambassador to venezuela and argentina . holguín was born on 28 may 1910 in quito , ecuador . he studied at national university of columbia . holguín was married genoveva montoya williamson on 18 july 1934 in bogotá . together had five children : francisco , maría lourdes , jorge , miguel , and jaime . holguín died on 19 october 1981 in bogotá , colombia , aged 71 .
