Santiago Pesquera Blanco     santiago pesquera blanco ( born april 21 , 1973 in la rioja ) is a boccia player from spain . he has a physical disability : he has cerebral palsy and is a bc3 type athlete . he had cerebral palsy when he was born . his job is working with computers . he started playing sport when he was 17 years old . he also plays wheelchair track and field . he competed at the 1996 summer paralympics in boccia . he competed at the 2000 summer paralympics . he finished second in the bc3 two person event event . he competed at the 2004 summer paralympics . he finished second in the bc3 two person event event . he finished second in the bc3 one person event . he competed at the 2008 summer paralympics . he finished first in the bc3 one person event .
