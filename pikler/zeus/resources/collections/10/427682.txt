Transmission tower     transmission tower or pylon is a tall tower used to carry the high-voltage cable used in electric power transmission . the tower is high for a safe distance for people , animals or things below the high-voltage cable . most big towers are built as suspension towers . they only hold up the cables , not pull them . tension towers pull on the cables . they work as terminal towers at the end of a transmission line , or on sharp turns , or as transposition towers . most big towers are lattice structures . smaller ones may be wood , concrete or steel poles . related pages . - underground cable
