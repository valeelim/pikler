Julian Galilea Martinez de Pinillos     julian galilea martinez de pinillos ( born september 17 , 1966 in la rioja ) is a 7-per-team football player from spain . he has a disability : he has cerebral palsy and is a c7 type player . he played 7-per-team football at the 1996 summer paralympics . his team , spain , played the united states national 7-per-team football team and won 2-1. his team finished third .
