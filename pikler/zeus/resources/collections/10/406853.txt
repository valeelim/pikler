The Help (movie)    the help ( movie ) the help is a 2011 american drama movie from director tate taylor . the drama is based on the novel `` the help '' by kathryn stockett . the actresses in this movie are emma stone , viola davis , octavia spencer , bryce dallas howard and jessica chastain . the young skeeter ( emma stone ) returns to her hometown . she has finished her study at the university of mississippi and now she wants to be a novelist . she begins to interview african-american women who are housemaids working for white people . she writes a book about the lives of the housemaids . first of all , the housemaids are against an interview . however , after an incident , many housemaids tell about their lives . each shift begins with a whisper , but the voices get louder and many people have many things to tell . `` the help '' won an oscar and a golden globe for octavia spencer in the category best actress in a supporting role . viola davis was nominated for an oscar in the category best actress in a leading role .
