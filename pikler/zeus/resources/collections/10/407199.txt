Pericles, Prince of Tyre    pericles , prince of tyre pericles , prince of tyre is a play by william shakespeare . it is a comedy . shakespeare 's source was `` the patterne of painefull adventures '' ( 1576 ) by lawrence twine . `` pericles , prince of tyre '' was probably first acted between 1608 and 1609. it was probably first printed in quarto form in 1609 .
