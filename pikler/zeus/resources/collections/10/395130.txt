Sam Raimi     samuel marshall `` sam '' raimi ( born october 23 , 1959 ) is an american movie director , producer , writer , and actor . he directed the `` spider-man '' movie trilogy . he also directed `` oz the great and powerful '' . raimi was born on october 23 , 1959 in royal oak , michigan to a hungarian-russian jewish family . he was raised in birmingham , michigan . he studied at groves high school and at michigan state university . raimi has been married to gillian dania greene since 1993. they have five children .
