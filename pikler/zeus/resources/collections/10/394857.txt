Jocelyn Burdick     jocelyn birch burdick ( born february 6 , 1922 ) is an american politician . she was the senator of north dakota from september 1992 through december 1992 for the democratic party . burdick was born on february 6 , 1922 in fargo , north dakota . she studied at principia college and at northwestern university . burdick was married to quentin n. burdick from 1960 until his death in 1992 .
