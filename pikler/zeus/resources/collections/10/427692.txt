Laverne Cox     laverne cox is an american actress . she is best known for playing sophia burset in orange is the new black , a netflix series . burset is a transgender woman in a women 's prison . she was born in mobile , alabama . she is a transgender woman . she has a twin brother , m lamar . cox is making a documentary about cece mcdonald , an african american transgender woman who was put in prison for killing a man who attacked her . on january 13 2014 cox met cece to drive her home after she was released from prison . cox spoke at the start of the 2014 national gay and lesbian task force conference creating change . cox was chosen as one of the 2014 grand marshalls for the new york city pride march . cox came fifth in the time 100 reader poll in 2014. after this happened cox was put on the cover of `` time '' magazine . she was the first transgender person to be on the cover . cox was nominated for the primetime emmy award for outstanding guest actress in a comedy series for her role as sophie in orange is the new black .
