Harry Lennix     harry joseph lennix iii ( born november 16 , 1964 ) or harry j. lennix is an american actor . he is best known for his roles as terrence `` dresser '' williams in `` the five heartbeats '' and as boyd langton in the television series `` dollhouse '' . he played general swanwick in the 2013 movie `` man of steel '' . lennix was born on november 16 , 1964 in chicago , illinois . he studied at quigley preparatory seminary south and at northwestern university .
