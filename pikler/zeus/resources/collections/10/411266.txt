Padasjoki     padasjoki is a municipality in päijänne tavastia , finland . about 3,319 people lived there as of 31 may 2013. nearby municipalities include asikkala , hämeenlinna , kangasala , kuhmoinen , pälkäne and sysmä . people lived in the area of padasjoki during the stone age . there is hill fort around the town . other websites . - community website
