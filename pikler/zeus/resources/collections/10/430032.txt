Vinny Griffin     vinny griffin is a fictional character in the television comedy show `` family guy '' . vinny is replaced with brian after his death in the episode `` life of brian '' . on the episode `` christmas guy '' , stewie meets another version of himself and steals his time pad . he goes back in time to save brian , causing stewie not to meet vinny . vinny has an italian accent and is kind to the griffins . he last appeared in `` christmas guy '' . he was voiced by tony sirico .
