Tirol-Choco     tirol-choco ( ) is a type of chocolate made in japan . it has been made since 2004. tyrol is an area of austria . when tirol-choco 's president visited austria , he decided to use the name to give his brand a fresh image . tirol has almost 383 flavors ; strawberry , milk , vanilla , coffee , cookies , and so on . the most popular flavor is `` kinako '' ( japanese soy bean 's rice cake ) . tirol-choco is cheap . it costs about 10 yen ( 10 cents ) to 30 yen ( 30 cents ) .
