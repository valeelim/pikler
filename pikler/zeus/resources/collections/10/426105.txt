Patricio Aylwin     patricio aylwin azócar ( november 26 , 1918 – april 19 , 2016 ) was a chilean retired politician . he served as president of chile from march 11 , 1990 through march 11 , 1994. he was the first president of chile after democracy was restored in 1990. he was also a lawyer and former senator . aylwin was born in viña del mar , chile on november 26 , 1918. he married leonor oyarzún in 1948. they had five children . aylwin died on april 19 , 2016 at his home in santiago from heart failure , at the age of 97. other websites . - official biography in section `` galería de presidentes '' ( president 's gallery )
