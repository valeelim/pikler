Division of Mitchell     the division of mitchell is an australian electoral division in the state of new south wales . it is named after major thomas mitchell , surveyor and explorer who was the first european to explore large areas of new south wales and victoria . it was set up in 1949. the division covers the north-west suburbs of sydney . it includes baulkham hills , beaumont hills , bella vista , box hill , kellyville , nelson and winston hills . it also includes parts of castle hill , north rocks , northmead , maraylya , rouse hill and west pennant hills . after the 2013 federal election , mitchell became the safest liberal seat in metropolitan australia . the alp would need a 22 % swing to win it . election results . - 2004 election results - 2007 election results - 2010 election results - 2013 election results other websites . - division of mitchell - australian electoral commission
