Marple Hall School     marple hall school is a specialist language college in marple , greater manchester , england . the headteacher is mr. joseph barker . the school is on the outskirts of greater manchester , near the river goyt . marple hall was a manor house once owned by john bradshaw . born at marple hall , bradshaw was first a clerk , then a lawyer . after the english civil war he was the first to sign the death warrant of charles i of england . the house was later owned by the isherwood family . the ruins of the house are still near the grounds of the school . two of the modern buildings are named for these two . in 1960 a grammar school was built on the grounds of the manor . in 1965 the school had separate schools for boy and girls . in 1974 it became marple hall county high school . this school is for both boys and girls .
