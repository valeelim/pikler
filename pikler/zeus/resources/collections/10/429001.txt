Orix Buffaloes     orix buffaloes is a japanese baseball team . this team was formed by the orix bluewave and the osaka kintetsu buffaloes after the 2004 npb season . the team is based in osaka and kobe and play home games at kyocera dome and kobe hotto motto stadium . the team has not had good results in recent seasons . a former player , ichiro suzuki , now plays for the new york yankees .
