Abbie Cornish     abbie cornish ( born august 7 , 1982 ) is an australian actress who has performed in some notable movies . cornish was born in new south wales , and grew up on a farm . her movies include `` somersault '' , `` candy '' and `` bright star '' . she played emma matisse in `` one perfect day '' . she plays ellen murphy in the 2014 remake of robocop .
