The Boy in the Striped Pyjamas     the boy in the striped pyjamas is a novel written by john boyne . bruno 's father is a soldier in world war ii and the family moves house so he can work closer to concentration camps . bruno makes friends with a jewish boy named shmuel in the camp . they meet up on their side of the fence each day . one day , he accidentally enters a gas chamber while attempting to look for shmuel 's father and dies by the side of the jewish boy . there is a movie of the same name based on it .
