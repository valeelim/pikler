Antonov An-2     the antonov an-2 is a soviet airliner & cargo aircraft . it was designed and built after world war ii . it is a biplane . it can carry between nine and eleven passengers . nato calls it `` colt '' . it was built under licence in china as y-5 . it is a single-engine aircraft . an-3 is a turboprop . an-6 ( `` meteo '' for nato ) is for military meteorologic reconnaissance .
