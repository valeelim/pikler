Flash Gordon (movie serial)    flash gordon ( movie serial ) flash gordon is a 1936 13-chapter science fiction flash gordon movie serial . it is the first movie adventure for the alex raymond comic strip character . it is the first science fiction movie serial . it tells the story of flash gordon 's first visit to the planet mongo where he confronts the evil emperor ming the merciless . buster crabbe plays the space adventurer flash gordon , jean rogers plays his love dale arden , frank shannon plays the scientist dr. zarkov , charles b. middleton plays ming the merciless , and priscilla lawson plays ming 's daughter princess aura . in 1996 , the serial was deemed `` culturally , historically , or aesthetically significant '' , and was selected for preservation in the united states national film registry by the library of congress . `` flash gordon 's trip to mars '' , a sequel to this movie , was released in 1938 .
