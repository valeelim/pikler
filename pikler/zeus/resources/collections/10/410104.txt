Francisco Segarra Simon     francisco segarra simon ( born february 27 , 1976 in granollers , barcelona ) is a swimmer from spain . he has a vision problem and is an s12 type swimmer . he raced at the 1996 summer paralympics . he finished second in the 400 meter freestyle race and the 100 meter backstroke race . he raced at the 2000 summer paralympics . he finished third in the 4 x 100 meter relay medley 49 points race and the 100 meter backstroke race .
