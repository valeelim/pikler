Azealia Banks     azealia amanda banks ( born may 31 , 1991 ) is an american rapper , singer , and songwriter . she was born in harlem , new york city . on november 9 , 2008 she made a myspace account and released her first song , `` gim me a chance '' on it as `` miss bank $ '' . later that year she was signed by xl recordings . in 2011 banks was chosen by nme as no.1 of their cool list of the year . her first album `` broke with expensive taste '' is going to be released in january 2014. her second album , fantasea ii : the second wave is going to be released july 2013. on december 6 2011 banks ' first single `` 212 '' was released . on may 28 , 2012 her first ep `` 1991 '' was released in the uk . it was released in the us the next day . a video for the song `` van vogue '' was released . on july 11 , 2012 a 19-song mixtape called `` fantasea '' was released for free download . banks ' second single `` esta noche '' was going to be released september 25 , 2012 , but a day before this was cancelled because the producer munchi said she `` stole '' the track .
