Musée de l'Air et de l'Espace     the musée de l'air et de l'espace ( `` air & space museum '' ) is the main french aviation and space museum . it is inside paris-le bourget international airport . the musée de l'air et de l'espace has two main sites : the museum itself , located in the old terminal of the airport , and reserves set up outside on the other side of the runway . the collections of the museum include : boeing 727 , boeing 747 , concorde , dassault mirage iii , dassault mirage iv , dassault mirage 2000a , dassault rafale a , douglas c-47 skytrain , focke-wulf fw 190 , republic p-47 thunderbolt , sncan n.2501 noratlas , supermarine spitfire ... websites . - official website . - reserves of the museum in 2009 .
