Musopen     musopen is an online library of public domain music recordings and sheet music , launched by aaron dunn in 2005. it aims to `` set music free '' through providing music to the public free of charge , without copyright restrictions . in 2008 , musopen released newly-commissioned recordings of the 32 beethoven piano sonatas into the public domain . other websites . - musopen homepage - musopen kickstarter-funded recordings on archive.org
