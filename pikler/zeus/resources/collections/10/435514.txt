Hugo Stiglitz     hugo stiglitz ( born august 28 , 1940 ) is an mexican actor who has appeared in over 100 movies . he was known for his roles in `` tintorera '' and `` la noche de los mil gatos '' . he is known in the united states for his roles in `` under the volcano '' . quentin tarantino named a character `` hugo stiglitz '' ( played by actor til schweiger ) in the movie `` inglourious basterds '' in his honor . in 2013 , stiglitz played juan `` johnny '' bravo in eugenio derbez comedy movie `` instructions not included '' . stiglitz was born on august 27 , 1940 in mexico city , mexico . other websites . - profile on cinefania site . - biography on yahoo ! movies site .
