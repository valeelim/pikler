The Amazing Spider-Man 2     the amazing spider-man 2 is an 2014 superhero-action movie directed by marc webb . it is a sequel to the 2012 spider-man movie `` the amazing spider-man '' . andrew garfield , emma stone , jamie foxx , dane dehaan , paul giamatti , martin sheen , denis leary , colm feore , chris cooper , sally field , chris zylka , marton csokas , and felicity jones are in the cast . hans zimmer composed the music for the movie . spider-man co-creator stan lee once again had a cameo appearance as a graduation guest . it was released on may 2 , 2014. the film starts with richard parker uploading a video explaining why he is disapearing . while on the plane , an assassin comes & attempts to kill richard & mary , the plane the crashes . after the events of the first film , spider-man is trying to stop alexi syetsevich hijacking an oscorp truck filled with plutonium .
