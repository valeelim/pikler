Jenůfa     jenůfa is a three-act opera with words and music by leoš janáček . it is based on the play `` její pastorkyňa '' by gabriela preissová . the opera was first performed in 21 january 1904 in brno , ( now in the czech republic ) . it was a great success when it was performed in prague in 1916. the opera is about the murder of a baby ( infanticide ) .
