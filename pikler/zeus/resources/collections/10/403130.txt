Sacrifice     a sacrifice is giving something up . in many religions , it usually is about giving something to god or gods . many pagan peoples made human sacrifices and animal sacrifices . the greek play `` iphigenia '' by euripides contains an example of this . in the old testament , jewish people followed the laws about animal sacrifices in the book of leviticus . cows and pigeons were killed to make atonement with god . in the new testament , christ 's death on the cross is seen as substitutionary atonement for sin . related pages . - epistle to the hebrews other websites . - sacrifice at catholic encyclopaedia
