Bell P-59 Airacomet     the bell p-59 airacomet is an american military aircraft . it was the first fighter jet designed and built in the united states . it was used in world war ii against nazi germany 's luftwaffe fighters , but it was far below the best enemy aircraft of the time . it was finally replaced in end of 1946 by the lockheed p-80 shooting star . the last one was retired in 1949 .
