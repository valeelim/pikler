Wii Party U     wii party u is a party video game for the wii u game console . it is the sequel to `` wii party '' . it was released in north america and europe on october 25 , 2013 , in australia on october 26 , 2013 , and in japan on october 31 , 2013 . `` wii party u '' is a party video game which takes elements from the `` mario party '' series . it has over 80 minigames . players are able to play minigames with the wii u gamepad .
