Arke (mythology)    arke ( mythology ) in greek mythology , arke is the daughter of thaumas . her sister is iris , the rainbow goddess . arke and her sister iris had wings and were the of the olympian gods . when the gods were fighting the titans , arke betrayed the gods and joined the titans against them . when the olympians won , zeus took arke 's wings and cast her into tartarus . arke 's wings were later given to thetis as a gift on her wedding day . thetis later gave them to her son achilles . she tied them to his ankles . which is where his surname podarces ( literally `` swift-footed '' ) came from .
