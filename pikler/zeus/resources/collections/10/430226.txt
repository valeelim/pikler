Anyang, Gyeonggi    anyang , gyeonggi anyang is a city in south korea . it is in gyeonggi province . anyang is located south east of seoul . the city has developed along anyang stream , which is 32.2 km long . the city is divided into two halves ; the manan district and dongan districts . dongan district has about 356,566 people and manan district has about 216,714 people . anyang is famous for anyang yuwonji and the anyang art park . it has seven mountains . mt . suri is the most famous one . it has produced many celebrities like rain , kim jong kook , kang min , niel .
