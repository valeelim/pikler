The End of the World     `` the end of the world '' is a song about feeling sad and breakups . the song was released in late 1962 , though it did not become a hit until spring of the following year . the song was first performed by skeeter davis . it was later recorded by other artists including nancy sinatra , brenda lee , johnny mathis , loretta lynn and anne murray . in several cases the song had a strong chart play and performance in the united states , united kingdom and several other areas .
