Vladimír Guma Kulhánek     vladimír guma kulhánek ( born 4 september 1944 in prague ) is a czech rock bass guitarist . he studied at the institute of chemical technology in prague . he was member of flamengo , etc ... , stromboli , bohemia and many other bands . he was also a sought-after session musician who has played with dežo ursiny , eva pilarová , jana kratochvílová , anna k and many others . now , he is member of t4 , november 2nd , energit and the supergroup called `` kulhánek/holeček/razím/kowacz '' .
