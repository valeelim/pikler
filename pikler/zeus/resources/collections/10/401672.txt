Christy Mathewson     christopher mathewson ( august 12 , 1880 - october 7 , 1925 ) was an american major league baseball player . he was known as `` big six '' , `` the christian gentleman '' , and `` matty '' . he played for the new york giants and the cincinnati reds , and also managed the reds . he became a member of the baseball hall of fame in 1936 .
