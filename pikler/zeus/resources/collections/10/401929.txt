Isabel Sanford     eloise gwendolyn sanford ( also known as isabel sanford ; august 29 , 1917 - july 9 , 2004 ) was an american movie , television and stage actress . she acted in several notable sitcoms . she played louise `` weezy '' jefferson in `` the jeffersons '' ( january 1975 - june 1985 ) . she also had many movie roles . sanford was born in harlem , new york . she died in los angeles from natural causes at age eighty six .
