Division of Chisholm     the division of chisholm is an australian electoral division in victoria . it was set up in 1949 and is named for caroline chisholm , a social worker and supporter of women 's immigration . it is covers an area of 65 km2 in the eastern suburbs of melbourne . in the 1980 's it was based on the suburb of camberwell , but later changes have moved it south-east to box hill , burwood and parts of clayton . the first member for chisholm , sir wilfrid kent hughes , was one of australia 's most important soldiers , who held the seat until his death on 31 july 1970. anna burke was the speaker of the house of representatives during 2013. election results . - 2004 election results - 2007 election results - 2010 election results - 2013 election results other websites . - division of chisolm - australian electoral commission - anna burke 's website
