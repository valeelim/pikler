John L. Harmer     john l. harmer ( born april 28 , 1934 ) is an american politician . harmer served in the california state senate from 1966 to 1974. he was a member of the republican party . he served as the 40th lieutenant governor of california from 1974 to 1975. he was given the job by governor ronald reagan , who was leaving office at the time . harmer wrote the book `` reagan : man of principle '' .
