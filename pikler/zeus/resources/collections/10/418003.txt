Old MacDonald Had a Farm     `` old macdonald had a farm '' is a children 's song . it tells of the different animals found on a farm and the sounds they make . animals include a cow , a duck , a sheep , and any other animals the singer cares to imitate . a typical verse : < poem > `` old macdonald had a farm , e-i-e-i-o ! '' `` and on this farm he had a cow , e-i-e-i-o ! '' `` with a moo-moo here , '' `` and a moo-moo there , '' `` here a moo , there a moo , '' `` everywhere a moo-moo , '' `` old macdonald had a farm e-i-e-i-o ! '' < /poem >
