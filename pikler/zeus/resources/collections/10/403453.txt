Elysée Palace     the élysée palace ( , ) is the official residence of the french president . it is in paris , in the 8th `` arrondissement '' , near the champs-élysées . the building is under protection of a unit of the famous republican guard . it was built between 1718 and 1722. bibliography . - de moncan , patrice , `` le palais de l'élysée : visite guidée '' , paris : éditions du mécène , 2011 .
