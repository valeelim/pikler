Pope Eleuterus     pope eleuterus ( ? ? ? − 189 ) was thirteenth pope , the official title is the bishop of rome of the catholic church from c. 174 to his death in 189. he was born in nicopolis , epirus , greece . according to `` liber pontificalis '' , they ascribe a decree to pope eleutherius that no kind of food should be despised by christians ( et hoc iterum firmavit ut nulla esca a christianis repudiaretur , maxime fidelibus , quod deus creavit , quæ tamen rationalis et humana est ) .
