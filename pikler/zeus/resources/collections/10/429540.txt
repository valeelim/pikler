Nicole Beharie     nicole beharie ( born january 3 , 1985 ) is an american actress . the first movie beharie acted in was `` american violet '' . beharie was nominated for best actress at the 2010 black reel awards for her acting in `` american violet '' . beharie played rachel robinson in the 2013 movie `` 42 '' . in 2013 , beharie started playing lt. abbie mills in the television series `` sleepy hollow '' .
