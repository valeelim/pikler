Rafael Moreno López     rafael moreno lopez ( born august 25 , 1966 in málaga ) is a judo athlete from spain . he has a disability : he is blind and is a b2 type athlete . he competed at the 1996 summer paralympics in judo . he finished 6 in the + 95 kg group . he competed at the 2000 summer paralympics in judo . he finished second in the open group . he competed at the 2004 summer paralympics in judo . he competed at the 2008 summer paralympics in judo .
