Weymouth, Massachusetts    weymouth , massachusetts weymouth is a city in norfolk county , massachusetts , united states . as of the 2010 census , weymouth had a total population of 53,743. despite its city status , it is formally known as the town of weymouth . it is named after weymouth , dorset , a coastal town in england . it is the second oldest european settlement in massachusetts . actor hal holbrook and architect frank lloyd wright are from weymouth . other websites . - town of weymouth - the weymouth news - weymouth history - answer book/weymouth : `` everything you need to know ''
