Tromsø     tromsø is a city in norway . it is in tromsø municipality and troms county . most of the city is on a small island called tromsøya . tromsø is the largest city in northern norway . ships , each with hundreds of tourists , came to tromsø , already around 1850 ; the largest tourist attraction there , was the sami camp in tromsdalen . living polar bears were being sold in tromsø , author cora sandel wrote in 1904. the university of tromsø was opened in tromsø in 1972 .
