Nonverbal learning disorder     a nonverbal learning disorder ( or nonverbal learning disability ) , or nvld , is a neurological problem . it can cause lower verbal skills , lower motor , social skills and other areas . a person with nvld also has problems in non-verbal problem solving , understanding humor and rote learning ( remembering something by repeating it ) . nvld is linked with autism spectrum disorders , mostly autism or asperger 's . most people with either high-functioning autism or asperger fit this actual type . this condition , though , is also tied to depression and anxiety . nonverbal learning disabilities affect one in ten learning disabled children .
