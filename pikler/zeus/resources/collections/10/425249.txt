Ms. Marvel (Kamala Khan)    ms. marvel ( kamala khan ) kamala khan is a fictional character appearing in comic books published by marvel comics . she first appeared in captain marvel # 17 ( november 2013 ) . khan is a 16-year-old pakistani-american muslim . she is from the suburbs of new jersey . she can shapeshift . she was made by sana amanat , steve wacker , and g. willow wilson .
