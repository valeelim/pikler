Battle of Majuba Hill     the battle of majuba hill was the main battle of the first boer war . it happened near volksrust , south africa on 27 february 1881. while the battle was small it was important in history . it led to the signing of a peace treaty . the treaty was between the british and the south african republic . it ended the first boer war . also the fire and movement tactics used by the boers were years ahead of their time . this battle and two before it convinced the british of the boers strength . during the second anglo-boer war `` remember majuba '' became a rallying cry . some important british historians say that this defeat marked the beginning of the decline of the british empire .
