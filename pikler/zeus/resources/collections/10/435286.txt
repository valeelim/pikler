Dieter Borst     dieter borst , ( born 12 may 1950 ) is a german painter and sculptor of 20th century art . borst was born on 12 may 1950 in schramberg , germany . as a painter , he specialized in informal painting . the basic element in his paintings is the line , which is carried through colored segments . in 1969 he studied at the american school of art in munich . in 1972 he took a half year study trip to west africa . in 1973 he started painting . in 1997 he moved to gran canaria . in 2007 his atelier burned in an island fire on gran canaria . hundreds of paintings of all phases of his working life were destroyed . borst 's works were exhibited in new york , fort lauderdale / miami , cologne and gran canaria . literature . - author : diana neubauer : `` dieter borst â€“ art informel 2013 '' , verlag : bod , isbn 978-3-7322-5015-8 museum / gallery . - broadway gallery , new york - gallery colonial house inn , new york - the grand , fort lauderdale , florida - galerie tempestas , cologne - galeria de arte , spain/gc references .
