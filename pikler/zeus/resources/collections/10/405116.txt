Hobomok: A Tale of Early Times    hobomok : a tale of early times hobomok : a tale of early times is a book by lydia maria child . it was published anonymously in boston , massachusetts , in 1824 by cummings , hilliard & co. the story is about the marriage between a white woman and a native american man . they have a son . the heroine later remarries , and rejoins puritan society with her child . the theme of miscegenation crearted a scandal . the book was not a critical success .
