Boris Pahor     boris pahor ( born 26 august 1913 ) is a slovenian writer . he is known for his holocaust experience described in the novel `` necropolis '' the novel has been translated twice into english . his first book was under the title `` pilgrim among the shadows '' in 1995 , and the second time under the title `` necropolis '' in 2010. other websites . - writer boris pahor receives top french honour ( slovenian government page ) - boris pahor on natzweiler-struthof
