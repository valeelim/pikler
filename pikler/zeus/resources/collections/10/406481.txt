Shakespeare's Sonnets    shakespeare 's sonnets shakespeare 's sonnets is the title of a collection of 154 sonnets written by william shakespeare . the collection was first published in 1609 by thomas thorpe . in general , each sonnet is in iambic pentameter , and consists of three quatrains followed by a concluding couplet . the sonnets are about love , death , jealousy , procreation , beauty , etc .
