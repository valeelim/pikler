PSR B1257+12 B     psr b1257+12 b is an extrasolar planet in the constellation of virgo . it is around 980 light years away from earth . it was the first planet ever discovered outside of the solar system . it is over four times bigger than earth . it orbits the pulsar psr b1257+12 , going around it approximately every 66 days at a distance of 0.36 au . other websites . - extrasolarplanet.net data - pulsar planets
