Arrondissement of Bagnères-de-Bigorre     the arrondissement of bagnères-de-bigorre is an arrondissement of france . it is part of the hautes-pyrénées `` département '' in the occitanie region . its capital is the city of bagnères-de-bigorre . when the `` arrondissements '' were created in 1800 , bagnères-de-bigorre was one of them in the hautes-pyrénées department . it is the largest of the `` arrondissements '' of the department , with an area of , but the second in population with 46,384 inhabitants ; its population density is of inhabitants/km² . the `` arrondissement '' of bagnères-de-bigorre is bordered to the north by the `` arrondissement '' of tarbes , to the east by the haute-garonne department , to the south by spain and to west by the `` arrondissement '' of argelès-gazost . after the reorganisation of the cantons in france , cantons are not subdivisions of the `` arrondissements '' so they could have `` communes '' that belong to different `` arrondissements '' . in the `` arrondissement '' of bagnères-de-bigorre , there is only 1 canton with all its `` communes '' in the `` arrondissement '' , the canton of neste , aure et louron with 62 `` communes '' . the following table shows the distribution of the `` communes '' in the cantons and `` arrondissements '' :
