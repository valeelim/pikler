Battle of Berlin (air)    battle of berlin ( air ) the battle of berlin was the british bombing raid on berlin during world war ii . the bombings were done from november 1943 to march 1944. british bombers had new navigation aids to help them , such as the new h2s radar . between november 1943 and march 1944 , bomber command made 16 attacks on berlin . although the bombings were mostly done by british bombers , australian and canadian bombers also flew to germany . the battle of berlin was considered a failure for the royal air force ( raf ) . they lost 1,047 bombers , with 1,682 more damaged . the raf also lost over 7,000 air crew members . another reason that the battle is seen as a defeat for the british is that the bombings did not cause the germans to give up . although it left 450,000 germans homeless .
