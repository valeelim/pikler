Stan Freberg     stanley victor `` stan '' freberg ( august 7 , 1926 – april 7 , 2015 ) was an american author , recording artist , animation voice actor , comedian , radio personality , puppeteer and advertising creative director . his career began in 1944. he was still active in the industry in his mid-80s , nearly 70 years after entering it . freberg was born on august 7 , 1926 in pasadena , california . he studied at alhambra high school . freberg was married to donna freberg from 1959 until her death in 2000. he had been married to betty hunter since 2001. he had two daughters with donna . in 1985 , paul mccartney said that freberg was an influence to the beatles ’ `` anarchic humor '' . freberg died on april 7 , 2015 , at the age of 88 at a hospital in santa monica , california from pneumonia . other websites . - stan freberg discography - `` stan the man '' in `` time '' ( july 29 , 1957 ) - `` mark thomas presents : stan freberg '' bbc radio 4 ( 2005 ) - cosmik debris interview - `` oregon ! oregon !
