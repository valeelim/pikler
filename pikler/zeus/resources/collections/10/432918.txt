Josep Maria Castellet     josep maria castellet díaz de cossío ( 15 december 1926 – 9 january 2014 ) , also known as josé maría castellet , was an spanish writer , poet , critic , publisher and editor . castellet was born in barcelona on 15 december 1926. he began his career in writing in 1955. he wrote novels and poetry in both catalan and spanish . his last novel was published in 2012. castellet died on 9 january 2014 from pneumonia . he was aged 87. he died in his hometown of barcelona .
