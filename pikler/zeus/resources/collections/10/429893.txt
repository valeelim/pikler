Antonis Samaras     antonis samaras ( ; born 23 may 1951 ) is a greek politician . he became prime minister of greece and leader of new democracy . he last served as the minister of finance in 1989 , then minister of foreign affairs from 1989 to 1990 , and again from 1990 to 1992. his term as prime minister ended on 26 january 2015 when he was succeeded by alexis tsipras .
