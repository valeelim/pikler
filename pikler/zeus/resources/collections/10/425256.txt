Robert Peston     robert james kenneth peston ( born 25 april 1960 ) is a british journalist . in 2005 his biography of gordon brown , `` brown 's britain '' was published . in 2013 , `` how do we fix this mess ? : the economic price of having it all , and the route to lasting prosperity '' was published . on 17 october 2013 he was made the economics editor for bbc news .
