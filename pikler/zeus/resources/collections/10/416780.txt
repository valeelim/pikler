Theo Peckham     theo `` teddy '' peckham ( born november 10 , 1987 in richmond hill , ontario ) is a canadian professional ice hockey defenceman currently playing for the chicago blackhawks of the nhl . he started his hockey career with the north york rangers of the ontario junior hockey league and played a season with them . he then moved on to play for the owen sound attack of the ontario hockey league for three seasons . he was drafted by the edmonton oilers with the 75th overall pick in the 2006 nhl entry draft . he debuted for the oilers during a march 7 , 2008 game against the columbus blue jackets and scored his first goal during a november 11 , 2010 game against the detroit red wings . on july 19 , 2013 , he was signed as a free agent to a one-year contract by the chicago blackhawks .
