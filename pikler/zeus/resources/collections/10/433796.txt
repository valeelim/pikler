Ivar Giaever     ivar giaever ( ; born april 5 , 1929 ) is a norwegian-american physicist . he shared the nobel prize in physics in 1973 with leo esaki and brian josephson . they were awarded it for their discoveries about the tunnelling phenomena in solids . he is a professor at the university of oslo . other websites . - interview with professor ivar giaever , from the official nobel prize website - biography and bibliographic resources , from the office of scientific and technical information , united states department of energy - university of oslo website about ivar giaever - applied biophysics website - family genealogy
