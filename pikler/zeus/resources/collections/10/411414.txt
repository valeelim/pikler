Kitee     kitee is a city in north karelia , finland . as of january 2014 over 11,200 people lived there . nearby municipalities include parikkala , rääkkylä , savonlinna and tohmajärvi . kitee was established 1631 and it came to be a city in 1992. sights . - aquapark , water park - model railway , over 250 metres long . orivesi , karjalan pyhäjärvi , kiteenjärvi , ätäskö , heinäjärvi , pieniheinäjärvi , särkijärvi , hyypii , pitkäjärvi , säynejärvi , lautakko , paasselkä ( paasivesi ) and piimäjärvi . other websites . - community website
