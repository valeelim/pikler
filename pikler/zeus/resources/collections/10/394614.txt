Jessica McClure     jessica mcclure morales ( march 26 , 1986 ) , better called baby jessica , became famous at eighteen months because of an ordeal in midland , texas in which she tumbled into a well on wednesday , october 14 , 1987. between then and october 16 , workers spent almost 58 hours to free her from the 8 inch wide well casing 22 feet below the ground . this story gained worldwide attention , and became the subject of a television movie two years following the incident .
