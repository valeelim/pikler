The Pearl Fishers     the pearl fishers is an opera by georges bizet . the libretto was written by eugène cormon and michel carré . it was first performed in paris on 30 september 1863. the opera is set in ancient ceylon , which is now called sri lanka . it tells the story of two men who are best friends , but love the same woman , who is a priestess . it was generally disliked at the time , especially by the newspapers . after his death , it has been more popular and is now regularly played across the world .
