Francisco Robles     francisco robles garcía ( may 5 , 1811 – march 2 , 1893 ) was an ecuadorian politician . he was president of ecuador from october 16 , 1856 to september 17 , 1859. robles was born on may 5 , 1811 in guayaquil , royal audience of quito , ecuador . he was married to carmen santistevan avilés . robles died on march 2 , 1893 in guayaquil , ecuador from heart failure , aged 81. other websites . - official website of the ecuadorian government about the country president 's history
