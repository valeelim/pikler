Into Eternity     into eternity are a canadian progressive metal band from regina , saskatchewan . into eternity was formed in 1997 by band members tim roth , scott krall and jim austin . they released their eponymous debut studio album `` into eternity '' in 1999. they re-released their debut album when they signed to century media records a year later . their second album `` dead or dreaming '' was released in 2001. the album saw the band move more into a progressive and death metal style . their third studio album `` buried in oblivion '' was released on february 10 , 2004 and their fourth studio album `` the scattering of ashes '' was released on october 3 , 2006. the band then went on tour from 2006 to 2008 and released their fifth studio album `` the incurable tragedy '' on august 25 , 2008. drummer bryan newbury joined the band on march 9 , 2011 and stu block joined iced earth in 2011. amanda kiernan joined the band in february 2012. band members . - current - tim roth − guitar , backing vocals ( 1997− '' present '' ) - troy bleich − bass , backing vocals ( 2004− '' present '' )
