Horrible Bosses 2     horrible bosses 2 is an american comedy movie . it is the sequel to 2011 's `` horrible bosses '' . it stars jason bateman , jason sudeikis , charlie day , kevin spacey , jennifer aniston , chris pine , jamie foxx , and christoph waltz . the movie was released on november 26 , 2014. it was produced by new line cinema and distributed by warner bros. pictures .
