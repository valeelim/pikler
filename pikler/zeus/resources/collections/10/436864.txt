Marquinhos     marquinhos ( real name marcos aoás corrêa , born 14 may 1994 in são paulo ) is a brazilian footballer . he plays as a defender for paris saint-germain f.c . in france . `` the guardian '' newspaper think that he is one of the 10 best young football players in the world . he was bought by his current club from a.s. roma for €32 million in 2013 .
