Kandyan jewellery     kandyan jewellery comes from ceylon or sri lanka . the kandyan kingdom disappeared in 1815 but some jewellery and designs are still being used by kandyan families today . kandyan jewellery is handmade and was originally designed for the royal families . it is a symbol of wealth , solidarity and strength . it is worn by women of royal or noble births and not women of lower castes . bridal pieces are the most important pieces of kandyan jewellery . they are handmade and consist of 26 pieces of jewellery that will be worn by the bride .
