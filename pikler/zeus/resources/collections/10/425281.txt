Garretson, South Dakota    garretson , south dakota garretson is a city in the eastern part of the u.s. state of south dakota . it is located in minnehaha county , and 1,166 people lived there at the 2010 census . garretson became a city in 1891. a popular place in the city is devil 's gulch , the place where jesse james jumped over a wide cliff on his horse and got away from the police after trying to rob a bank in northfield , minnesota , in 1876. garretson gets its name from a banker named a.s. garretson . other websites . - city of garretson website
