Rhythm section     a rhythm section is the group of musicians in a rock music , jazz , or country music group who play their instruments to set out the pulse or beat of the music . this is played in accompaniment to the rest of the band . some of the rhythm section instruments , such as the guitar and the piano , also play the chord progression ( sequence of chords ) that a song is based upon . some rhythm section instruments , such as the drums and percussion , are just used to set out the beat . most rhythm sections include a bass instrument , such as the bass guitar or the double bass , which plays the deep , low notes of the chord progression .
