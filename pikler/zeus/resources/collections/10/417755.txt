Heated shot     heated shot is the heating of round shot before firing from muzzle-loading cannons . the purpose is for setting fire to enemy warships , buildings , or equipment . the use of hot shot dates back centuries . it was stopped when vessels armored with iron replaced wooden warships in the world 's navies . it was a powerful weapon against wooden warships , where fire was always a hazard . it was mainly used on shore batteries and forts , due to the need for a special furnace to heat the shot . the use of heated shot from a ship was so dangerous , that it was against royal navy regulations . the american ship uss `` constitution '' had a shot furnace installed , for hot shot to be fired .
