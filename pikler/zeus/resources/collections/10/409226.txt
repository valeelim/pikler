Irene     irene is a female given name . it is greek for `` peace '' . this was the name of several saints , and an 8th century empress . the name was sixth most popular for girls in spain in 2006. around that same time , in the united states , the name ranked at 592 and fell to near 632 the following year . the name was used for several atlantic tropical storms or hurricanes . hurricane irene in 2011 was especially destructive to the east coast of the united states . this caused `` irene '' to be retired in spring of 2012 .
