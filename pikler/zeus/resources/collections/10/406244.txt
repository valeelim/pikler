Sarai Gascon Moreno     sarai gascon moreno ( born november 16 , 1992 in terrassa , barcelona ) is a swimmer from spain . she has a disability : she is an s9 type swimmer . she raced at the 2008 summer paralympics . she was the number two swimmer in the 100 meter breaststroke race . she raced at the 2012 summer paralympics . she was the number two swimmer in the 100 meter butterfly race . she was the number three swimmer in the 100 meter freestyle race .
