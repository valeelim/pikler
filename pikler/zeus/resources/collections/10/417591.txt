Funk Volume     funk volume is an independent record label and production company . it produces hip hop music . it was founded in 2009 by american rapper hopsin and damien `` dame '' ritter . so far funk volume has signed artists such as dizzy wright , jarren and hopsin has realeased 2 albums under funk volume . funk volume began in 2009 after hopsin left ruthless records . before he launched it , he made a mixtape to promote the label .
