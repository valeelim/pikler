Alexandra Frantseva     alexandra frantseva ( born 24 april 1987 ) is a skier from russia . she has a disability : she can not see everything . she skied at the 2011 ipc alpine skiing world championships . she was the second skier to finish in the women 's visually impaired super-g race , giant slalom race and super combined race , and the third skier to finish the women 's visually impaired downhill race and slalom race . because she can not see , evgenia kolosovskaya was her guide and told her where to go when going down the mountain .
