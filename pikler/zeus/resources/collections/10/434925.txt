The Fox and the Grapes     `` the fox and the grapes '' is a fable by aesop . it is about a fox who sees some grapes hanging high on a vine . he tries to reach the grapes , but he ca n't . since he ca n't get them , he tells himself that the grapes are probably sour anyway . this fable shows the idea of cognitive dissonance . the english expression `` sour grapes '' comes from this fable .
