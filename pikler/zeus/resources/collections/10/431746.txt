Malin Åkerman    malin åkerman malin maria åkerman ( born 12 may 1978 ) is a swedish-canadian actress . she acted in `` 27 dresses '' in 2008 and in `` watchmen '' in 2009. she also does singing and modeling . she played kate harrison in the abc comedy `` trophy wife '' . åkerman was born in stockholm . she moved to canada at age two and was raised partly in sweden and partly in ontario .
