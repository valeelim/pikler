Reform School Girl     reform school girl is a 1957 movie directed by edward bernds . it was produced by american international pictures ( aip ) and distributed by rko radio pictures . it stars gloria castillo , ross ford , and edd byrnes . the movie is about a young woman who is sent to reform school after she refuses to disclose what she knows about a traffic accident in which a pedestrian was killed . it was remade in 1994 with the same title .
