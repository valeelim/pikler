About Last Night (1986 movie)    about last night ( 1986 movie ) about last night is a 1986 american romance-comedy-drama movie . two people begin a relationship from a one-night stand after meeting in a chicago bar . demi moore plays debbie . rob lowe plays danny . megan mullally plays pat . this movie was released on july 2 , 1986. it got very good reviews . a remake was released in 2014 .
