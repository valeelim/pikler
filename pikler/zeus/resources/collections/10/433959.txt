Arvid Carlsson     arvid carlsson ( born 25 january 1923 ) is a swedish scientist . he is best known for his work with the neurotransmitter dopamine and its effects on parkinson 's disease . for his work on dopamine , carlsson was awarded the nobel prize in physiology or medicine in 2000. he won the award alongside eric kandel and paul greengard . other websites . - nobel prize biography - the sahlgrenska academy at the university of gothenburg
