Scott Sanders     scott gerald sanders is a former major league baseball player . he played as a right-handed pitcher . in 1993 sanders began playing for the san diego padres . this was his first major league team . he also played for the san diego padres , seattle mariners , detroit tigers , and chicago cubs . sanders also started one game for the cleveland indians in 2000. he pitched poorly . after a few innings , rain caused the game to be cancelled . all stats from the game were removed . sanders did not play for the indians after that game . in 2001 , sanders pitched in japan for the nippon ham fighters . in his career , sanders was in 34 winning games and 45 losing games . he pitched 632 strikeouts and had a 4.86 era .
