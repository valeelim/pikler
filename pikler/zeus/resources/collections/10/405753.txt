Videotape     videotape is magnetic tape used to record moving images and ( usually ) sound . videotape was invented in the 1950s for recording television shows for later broadcasting . in the 1970s it entered use in low-cost filmmaking . in the 1980s analog tape became commonplace at home , and replaced the use of photographic film for home movies . better digital videotape systems started replacing motion picture film at the end of the 20th century . 21st century video mostly uses digital recording media such as memory cards and hard drives rather than videotape .
