Santa Fe University of Art and Design     the santa fe university of art and design ( sometimes called sfuad ) is a private 4-year university in santa fe , new mexico . it was founded in 1859 with the name st. michael 's college . the government gave it a charter for higher education in 1874. this meant that the college had the right to give degrees . in 1966 it was renamed college of santa fe . in 2010 it was renamed again to santa fe university of art and design . the school has a large movie studio where many movies have been made . in 2013 tuition was $ 27,996 a year .
