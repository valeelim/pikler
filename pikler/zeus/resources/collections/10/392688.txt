Erythema ab igne     erythema ab igne is a rash of the skin , which is usually caused by the long-term exposure to heat or infrared radiation , and which is not a burn . usually the name is abbreviated `` eai '' . other names include hot water bottle rash fire stains , laptop thigh , granny 's tartan and toasted skin syndrome . some people may complain of mild itchiness and a burning sensation . when a change in skin pigmentation can not be seen , it is often not noticed at all .
