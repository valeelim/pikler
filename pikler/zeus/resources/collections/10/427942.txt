TD Garden     td garden is a sports arena in boston , massachusetts . the arena opened on september 30 , 1995 , and is the current home to the boston bruins of the national hockey league ( nhl ) and the boston celtics of the national basketball association ( nba ) . the arena was originally known as fleetcenter from 1995 to 2005. td banknorth acquired the naming rights for the arena on march 3 , 2005. the name was changed to td banknorth garden on july 1 , 2005. td garden has held many wwe events as well including : wrestlemania xiv in 1998 , king of the ring in 2000 , royal rumble in 2003 , summerslam in 2006 , survivor series in 2008 , royal rumble in 2011 , night of champions in 2012 , and survivor series in 2013. other websites . - official website
