Chaplin (movie)    chaplin ( movie ) chaplin is a 1992 american biographical comedy-drama movie . it is about the life of british comedian charlie chaplin . it was directed by richard attenborough . it stars robert downey , jr. , moira kelly , dan aykroyd , geraldine chaplin , penelope ann miller , anthony hopkins , james woods , and kevin kline . it 's soundtrack was composed by john barry . it was released on december 25 , 1992 to mixed reviews .
