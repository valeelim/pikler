Tigrosa     spiders in the genus tigrosa were formerly included in the genus `` hogna '' , and before that to the genus `` lycosa '' . as people who study spiders make more progress they divide things in new ways . among the species included in this genus are : - t. annexa - t. aspersa - t. grandis - t. helluo - t. georgicola references . - brady , a.r . 2012 : nearctic species of the new genus `` tigrosa '' ( araneae : lycosidae ) . `` journal of arachnology '' , 40 ( 2 ) : 182-208. issn 0161-8202
