Dealul Mitropoliei     dealul mitropoliei ( ) is a small hill in bucharest , romania.. it is also called or `` patriarchate hill '' . it is an important historic , cultural , architectural , religious and touristic point in the national capital . the romanian patriarchy and the residence of the patriarch are here . next to the patriarchy there is a house ( nr . 19 ) which is in the `` national architectural patrimonial of romania '' .
