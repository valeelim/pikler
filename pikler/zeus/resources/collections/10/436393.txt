Rutherford B. Hayes Presidential Center     the rutherford b. hayes presidential center is a group of several buildings dedicated to president of the united states rutherford b. hayes . it is in fremont , ohio . it was started in 1916. the rutherford b. hayes center library was the first presidential library and one of only three for a 19th-century president . the library holds the 12,000 volume personal library of rutherford b. hayes . other websites . - `` life portrait of rutherford b. hayes '' , broadcast from the rutherford b. hayes presidential center from c-span 's `` ''
