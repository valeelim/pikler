H. Robert Horvitz     howard robert horvitz ( born may 8 , 1947 in chicago , illinois ) is an american biologist . he is best known for the research he did on the nematode worm caenorhabditis elegans and he won the nobel prize in physiology or medicine in 2002 for `` discoveries concerning genetic regulation of organ development and programmed cell death '' . he shared the prize with sydney brenner and john e. sulston . he earned a phd in biology from harvard university in 1974 .
