Tainan Mosque     the tainan mosque ( ) is a mosque in tainan , taiwan . it is the sixth mosque in taiwan , and the first and only mosque in tainan . the mosque was built in 1996. a land was initially donated by an chinese muslim living in the united states to taipei grand mosque . it was then transferred to kaohsiung mosque which managed the construction of the tainan mosque . starting in 1983 , they collected money for 10 years for the mosque construction , and the construction finally started in 1993 and the mosque was opened in 1996. the mosque has four floors , which consists of prayer hall , shop , meeting room , ablution room and office .
