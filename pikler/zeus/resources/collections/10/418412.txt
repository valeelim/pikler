Interstate 787     interstate 787 ( i-787 ) is an interstate highway in the u.s. state of new york . it goes through downtown albany . the southern end is at the toll plaza for new york state thruway ( i-87 ) exit 23. the location of the northern end is unclear . some sources say it is at 8th street in troy . other sources say it is at its interchange with ny 7 and ny 787 in the town of colonie . the route is long if extended to troy , or without the ny 7 overlap . north of ny 7 , the highway continues as ny 787 to the city of cohoes .
