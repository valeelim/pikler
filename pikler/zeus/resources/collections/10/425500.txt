Isaiah Wohlgemuth     isaiah wohlgemuth wrote a widely read book , `` a guide to jewish prayer '' . he was from germany and came to the united states after world war ii . he studied at a religious school in berlin . he was ordained as a rabbi . his synagogue was destroyed . the building was burned on kristallnacht . he was imprisoned in a concentration camp . as soon as the war was over , he came to the united states and lived in boston . in boston he went back to school . he studied for many years and received a doctorate ( an advanced degree ) in education . he taught students in boston for more than 50 years . because he was a rabbi and had a doctorate , he was called `` rabbi dr . wohlegemuth . '' rabbi wohlgemuth , died january 6 , 2008. he had parkinson 's disease .
