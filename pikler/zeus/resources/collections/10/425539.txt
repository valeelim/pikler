Another Part of Me     `` another part of me '' is a song by michael jackson . it was in jackson 's 1986 3-d film `` captain eo '' . it was released as the sixth single from jackson 's seventh album `` bad '' on july 11 , 1988 . `` another part of me '' was not going to be on `` bad '' . but after jackson 's manager frank dileo danced along to the song , it was decided that the song would be featured on the album . the song reached number eleven in the `` billboard '' hot 100 .
