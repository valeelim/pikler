Pye Engström     pye ester agneta engström ( née nanneson ; born 8 june 1928 ) is a swedish sculptor . she was born in danderyd municipality , sweden . engström studied at the konstfack ( 1945–48 ) , royal institute of art ( 1945–48 ) and royal danish academy of fine arts ( 1948–53 ) . she has been married to writer clas engström since 1951 .
