Savannah monitor     the savannah monitor ( or bosc 's monitor ) is a medium-sized monitor lizard from africa . its scientific name is `` varanus exanthematicus '' . its range extends throughout sub-saharan africa from senegal east to sudan and south almost to the congo river and great rift valley . it is a ground-dwelling species which shelters in burrows , although it is sometimes found in bushes or low trees . information about the diet of savannah monitors in the wild has been recorded in senegal and ghana . it feeds almost exclusively on arthropods and molluscs .
