Elinor Donahue     mary eleanor donahue ( born april 19 , 1937 ) , best known as elinor donahue , is an american actress . she was known for her role in the 1950s sitcom , `` father knows best '' . she was born in tacoma , washington donahue was married for nearly 30 years to producer harry ackerman . they met each other on the set of `` father knows best '' . she and ackerman had three children together : peter , james , and christopher ackerman . other websites . - elinor donahue interview at archive of american television
