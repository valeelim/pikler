Skellefteå AIK     skellefteå aik is an ice hockey club in the town of skellefteå in sweden . it was established in 1921. the club adopted the game of ice hockey in 1943. it won the swedish national championship in 1978 , 2013 and 2014. the team also lost the swedish national finals in 2011 , 2012 and 2015. other websites . - official website
