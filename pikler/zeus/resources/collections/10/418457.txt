Hurricane Ingrid     hurricane ingrid was a strong category 1 hurricane which struck eastern mexico at the same time another storm struck the western parts . the storm claimed the lives of 32 people in mexico . the winds of ingrid reached at its peak . the storm , however , weakened before heading into mexico as a severe tropical storm in september 2013. the name `` ingrid '' was later retired . it was replaced with `` imelda '' for 2019 .
