Rubén Bonifaz Nuño     rubén bonifaz nuño ( 12 november 1923 – 31 january 2013 ) was a mexican poet and classical scholar . nuño was born on 12 november 1923 in córdoba , veracruz , mexico . he studied at national autonomous university of mexico . nuño died on 31 january 2013 in mexico city , mexico from natural causes , aged 89. other websites . - rubén bonifaz nuño ( el colegio nacional ) - rubén bonifaz nuño : brief biography - rubén bonifaz nuño : interview , biography , criticism , poetry
