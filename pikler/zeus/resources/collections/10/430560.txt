Nae Lăzărescu     nae lăzărescu ( 8 september 1941 – 19 december 2013 ) was a romanian television , stage and movie actor . he was also a comedian . he was born in bucharest . his career began in 1963. lăzărescu died of chronic liver disease , in bucharest . he was aged 72. he will be buried on 21 december in focșani , vrancea county .
