Louan Gideon     louan gideon ( november 12 , 1955 - february 3 , 2014 ) was an american actress . she is best known for her role as danielle atron in `` the secret world of alex mack '' . she was also in `` search for tomorrow '' . gideon died on february 3 , 2014 in asheville , north carolina from cancer , aged 58 .
