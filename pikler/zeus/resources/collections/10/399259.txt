Lee Weaver     lee weaver ( born april 10 , 1930 ) is an american movie and television actor . he is known for his role in `` o brother , where art thou ? '' . he also appeared in `` the fresh prince of bel-air '' . weaver was born on april 10 , 1930 in fort lauderdale , florida . he has been married to ta-tanisha since 1971. they have one child . weaver now lives in the bronx , new york .
