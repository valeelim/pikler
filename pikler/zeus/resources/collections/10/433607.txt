Mishon Ratliff     mishon ratliff ( born february 3 , 1993 ) , also simply called mishon , is an american singer , dancer and actor . ratliff is best known for his role as taylor `` tay '' sutton on the abc family television series `` lincoln heights '' . he has appeared on music television broadcast such as `` soul train '' , `` america 's most talented kid '' , `` the mo'nique show '' and `` 106 & park '' . as well as being an actor , mishon is a musician who plays the keyboard and both acoustic and electric guitar . filmography . - films - television - theatre other websites . - official website - mishon 's youtube
