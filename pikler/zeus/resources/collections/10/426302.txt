Mulatu Teshome     mulatu teshome wirtu ( born 1955 or 1956 ) is a ethiopian politician and the current president of ethiopia . mulatu teshome was born in 1955 or 1956 , in arjo , ethiopia . he went to school in china at peking university where he received a bachelor 's degree in philosophy of political economy and doctorate in international law . he served as the deputy minister of economic development and cooperation under girma birru , and was the minister of agriculture in 2001. he also served as the speaker of the house of federation from 2002 to 2005 and was ethiopia 's ambassador to china , japan , and turkey . he was elected as president of ethiopia by a unanimous parliamentary vote on 7 october 2013 .
