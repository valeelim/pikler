Jorge Cardona Marquez     jorge cardona marquez ( born november 2 , 1987 in zaragoza ) is a table tennis athlete from spain . he has a physical disability : he is class 10 player . he played table tennis at the 2008 summer paralympics and the 2012 summer paralympics . in 2008 , he finished second in team table tennis . in 2012 , he finished third in team class 9-10 .
