Rafael Carvajal     rafael carvajal guzmán ( 1818–1881 ) was an ecuadorian politician . he served as vice president of ecuador from 1864 to 1865 and acting president from 31 august 1865 to 7 september 1865. he was the vice president of gabriel garcía moreno . he succeed him after he was assassinated . other websites . - official website of the ecuadorian government about the country president 's history
