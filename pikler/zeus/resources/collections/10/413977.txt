Alfredo Baquerizo     alfredo baquerizo moreno ( 28 september 1859 , in guayaquil – 20 march 1951 ) was an ecuadorian politician . he served as vice president of ecuador of leónidas plaza and lizardo garcía from 1903 to 1906 and as president of ecuador three times in august - september 1912 , september 1916 – august 1920 and october 1931 - august 1932. he was a member of the ecuadorian radical liberal party . other websites . - official website of the ecuadorian government about the country president 's history
