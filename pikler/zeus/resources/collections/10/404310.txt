Consumer Electronics Show     the consumer electronics show , also known as just ces , is a yearly conference for technology held every january at the las vegas convention center , las vegas , nevada , united states . technology companies come here to host previews of their products and announce new products . it is not open to the public . products that were first introduced to the public at a consumer electronics show include the videocassette recorder ( in 1970 ) , the camcorder ( in 1981 ) , high-definition television ( in 1998 ) , the xbox ( in 2001 ) , and the blu-ray disc ( in 2003 ) . it was first held in june 1967 in new york city . in 2009 , `` jeopardy !
