Philip Baker Hall     philip baker hall ( born september 10 , 1931 ) is an american actor . he is known for his roles in `` midnight run '' , `` say anything ... '' , `` air force one '' , `` the rock '' , `` the truman show '' , `` the talented mr. ripley '' , `` bruce almighty '' , `` rush hour '' , `` you kill me '' , `` in good company '' , `` dogville '' , `` the amityville horror '' , `` the matador '' , `` the sum of all fears '' , `` the zodiac '' , and `` zodiac '' . hall was born on september 10 , 1931 in toledo , ohio . he studied at the university of toledo . hall was married twice , both ended in divorce . hall had two children .
