Flower pot     a flower pot is a container in which flowers and other plants are grown . they are traditionally made from terracotta , but are now often also made from plastic , wood or stone . the flower pot is filled with soil and have a plant planted in them . often there are holes in the bottom , to allow water to flow out , sometimes into a saucer that is placed under the flowerpot . if the plant becomes too big for the pot , it is moved to a bigger one .
