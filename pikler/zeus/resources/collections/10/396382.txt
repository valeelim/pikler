Anke Engelke     anke christina fischer ( née engelke ; born 21 december 1965 in montréal ) is a german comedian , actress and voice-over actress born . engelke moved to cologne in 1971 with her parents . early on , she exhibited singing talent , and was discovered in 1978 by georg bossert at a performance of her school 's choir . in may 2011 , she hosted the eurovision song contest 2011 in düsseldorf alongside stefan raab and judith rakers . the following year , engelke served as the spokesperson for germany at the eurovision song contest 2012 held in baku , azerbaijan . before presenting the votes , she also gave a live statement on the human-rights issues in the hosting country , saying : `` tonight nobody could vote for their own country . but it is good to be able to vote . and it is good to have a choice . good luck on your journey , azerbaijan . europe is watching you . '' she was the only spokesperson to articulately address human rights during the event . she had also served as one of germany 's national juries for the contest during that year .
