Maurice Vachon     maurice vachon ( september 14 , 1929 - november 21 , 2013 ) was a canadian professional wrestler . he is best known under his ring name , `` mad dog '' vachon he was known for competing in american wrestling association ( awa ) where he played one of the all-time great heels . he spent four decades wrestling and retired in 1986. he is a member of the vachon wrestling family with his brother paul and sister vivian vachon , and the uncle of wrestler luna vachon . he died on november 21 , 2013. he was inducted into the wwe hall of fame by pat patterson in 2010. championships . - american wrestling association - awa midwest tag team championship ( three times ) ( with bob orton ( two ) and paul vachon ( one ) ) - awa world heavyweight championship ( five times ) - awa world tag team championship ( two times ) ( with paul vachon ( one ) and verne gagne ( one ) ) - cauliflower alley club - iron mike mazurki award ( 2003 ) - central states wrestling - nwa world tag team championship ( central states version ) ( one time ) ( with baron von raschke )
