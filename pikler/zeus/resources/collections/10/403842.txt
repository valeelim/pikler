SNCASE SE-313 Alouette II     the sncase se-313 alouette ii ( , `` lark '' ) is a french helicopter built between 1956 and 1975 by société nationale de construction aéronautique du sud-est and later by aérospatiale . it was the first helicopter in the world that has a gas turbine for engine . it was the basis of two majors variants : sa-315 lama and sa-316 alouette iii . bibliography . - jackson , robert , `` helicopters : military , civilian , and rescue rotorcraft '' ( the aviation factfile ) . london : grange books ltd , 2005. isbn 1-84013-812-2 . - gaillard , pierre , `` avions et hélicoptères militaires d'aujourd'hui '' paris , france : larivière éditions , 1999. isbn 2-907051-24-5 . - boulay , pierre , `` les hélicoptères français '' , paris , france : larivière éditions , 1998. isbn 2-907051-17-2
