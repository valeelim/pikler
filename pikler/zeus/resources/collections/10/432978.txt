Myliobatiformes     myliobatiformes is one of the four groups of batoids . it is made up of cartilaginous fishes . they are related to the shark family . they were recently re-classified as part of the monophyletic group , after being part of the rajiformes group . another relative to myliobatiformes is the common form of skates . genera . - platyrhinidae - zanobatidae - myliobatoidei - hexatrygonidae - plesiobatidae - urolophidae - urotrygonidae - dasyatidae
