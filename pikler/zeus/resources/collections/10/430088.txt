Marie Dressler     leila marie koerber ( or marie dressler ; november 9 , 1868 - july 28 , 1934 ) was a canadian and american actress . she acted in lots of movies and on stage . her many movies include `` tillie 's punctured romance '' ( 1914 ) , `` min and bill '' ( 1930 ) . she won an academy award for best supporting actress for the latter . she was born in ontario . dressler died of cancer at age 65 .
