Eliza Doolittle     eliza sophie caird ( born 17 april 1988 ) , best known as eliza doolittle , is a british singer and songwriter . her first album `` eliza doolittle '' was released on 12 july 2010. eliza doolittle was born in london in 1988. her father is john caird . her mother is frances ruffelle . doolittle grew up in camden . she went to channing school . on 29 november 2009 doolittle released her first ep `` eliza doolittle '' on itunes . on 11 april 2010 she released her first single `` skinny genes '' . on 5 july 2010 her second single `` pack up '' was released . it entered the uk singles chart at number twelve . it reached number five on 11 july 2010. the two singles were from her album `` eliza doolittle '' , which was released on 12 july 2010. in 2011 doolittle was signed to select models . on 28 april 2013 `` you & me '' , a song by disclosure with doolittle singing on it , was released . it reached number ten in the uk singles chart . on 7 june 2013 doolittle released the first single from her second album , `` big when i was little '' .
