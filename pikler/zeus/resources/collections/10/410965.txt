Luis Armand Garcia     luis armand garcia , ( born march 9 , 1992 ) is an american actor . he is best known for his role as max lopez on the sitcom `` george lopez '' . garcia was born on march 9 , 1992 in lagrange , illinois . he moved to los angeles , california . he lives in valencia , california . he graduated from high school in 2010. other websites . - luis armand garcia at tvguide.com
