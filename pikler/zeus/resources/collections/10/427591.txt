Astraphobia     astraphobia , also called astrapophobia , brontophobia , keraunophobia , or tonitrophobia , is a fear of thunder and lightning . it is a type of specific phobia . both humans and animals can have this type of phobia . a person with astraphobia often feels fear during a thunderstorm even if there is a low chance that they will be hurt during the storm . they will sometimes look for better shelter during the storm . they might hide under a bed , under blankets , in a closet , in a basement , or anywhere else where they feel safe . a sign that a person has astraphobia is if they are very interested in weather forecasts . a person with astraphobia will want to hear news of storms that are coming by watching the weather on television or watching the storms on the internet .
