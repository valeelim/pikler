Armie Hammer     armand douglas `` armie '' hammer ( born august 28 , 1986 ) is an american actor . he is best known for his roles as the winklevoss twins in the 2010 movie `` the social network '' , the lone ranger in the 2013 movie `` the lone ranger '' and as jackson storm in the 2017 pixar movie `` cars 3 '' . hammer was born in los angeles , california and is of russian jewish , russian , english , scotch-irish , and cherokee origin . he took courses while at the university of california , los angeles , before he started acting in 2004. he has been married to television host and actress elizabeth chambers since may 10 , 2010. they live together in los angeles .
