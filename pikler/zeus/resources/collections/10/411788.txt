Ayalon Highway     ayalon highway ( , pronounced : `` netivei ayalon '' , lit . `` ayalon routes '' ) is a highway system in the tel aviv metropolitan area . the main road goes through tel aviv and is called highway 20. the road starts in hama'apilim interchange near the cities kfar shmaryahu , herzliya , ramat hasharon . it ends at holot interchange in gan sorek . it goes through the cities of bat yam , holon and rishon lezion . it meets highway 4. a railway line runs along the road with a major train station inside the gates road . in central tel aviv , the road passes a canal of the ayalon river . other websites . - ayalon highways ltd. includes up to the minute traffic reports and live traffic camera views . - transport today and tomorrow
