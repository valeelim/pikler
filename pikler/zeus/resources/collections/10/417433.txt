Vampire Weekend     vampire weekend is an indie rock band . they have released three albums . their 2011 album `` contra '' was nominated for best alternative music album at the grammy awards . their third studio album , `` modern vampires of the city '' , was released on may 14 , 2013. it was nominated for a grammy for best alternative music album . pitchfork gave the album a score of 9.3 out of 10. rolling stone put it at number one on their list of 50 best albums of 2013. members . - ezra koenig – lead vocals , guitar , lyrics - rostam batmanglij – producer , keyboards , guitar , backing vocals , lyrics - chris tomson – drums , percussion - chris baio – bass guitar , backing vocals
