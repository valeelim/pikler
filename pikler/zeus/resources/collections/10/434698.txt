Miuccia Prada     miuccia prada ( born maria bianchi ; 10 may 1949 ) is an italian fashion designer . she designs for prada . in 1992 she started a second fashion label , miu miu . prada is the youngest granddaughter of mario prada . she was born in milan . prada met patrizio bertelli in 1977. in 1978 she married him .
