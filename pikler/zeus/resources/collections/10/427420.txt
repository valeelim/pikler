Melissa Harris-Perry     melissa victoria harris-perry ( born october 2 , 1973 ) is an american television host and writer . she hosts a television programme on msnbc called `` melissa harris-perry '' . she got a ph.d. in political science from duke university in 1999. that year she married dennis lacewell . she divorced him in 2005. she is a professor of political science at tulane university . in 2004 her first book `` barbershops , bibles , and bet : everyday talk and black political thought '' was published . in 2011 yale press published perry 's book `` sister citizen : shame , stereotypes , and black women in america '' . other websites . - official website
