Clare Balding     clare balding obe ( born 29 january 1971 ) is a british television presenter . she was born in kingsclere , hampshire . she is openly lesbian . in 2009 , she was diagnosed with thyroid cancer . she recovered later that year . in 2012 , a book she wrote about her life was published . it was called `` my animals and other family '' . in february 2013 woman 's hour , a programme on bbc radio 4 , put her on their list of the most powerful women in the uk . balding presents `` britain 's brightest '' .
