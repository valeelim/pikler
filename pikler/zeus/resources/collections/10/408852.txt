Cedric the Forester     cedric the forester is a long story for children and young adults . it was written by bernard marshall . it was a newbery medal honor book in 1922. dickon mountjoy tells the story . he is a norman boy who lives in a castle in england in the 13th century . his best friend is cedric , a boy who lives in a cottage in the forest . cedric is very good at shooting with a bow and arrow . dickon and cedric grow up together and have many adventures . in the end , they help to get king john of england to sign the magna carta .
