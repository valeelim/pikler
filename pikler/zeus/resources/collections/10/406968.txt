Lizzy Caplan     elizabeth anne `` lizzy '' caplan ( born 30 june 1982 ) is an american television and movie actress . she has played many roles . she played janis ian in `` mean girls '' , marlena diamond in `` cloverfield '' and gena in `` bachelorette '' . she also played amy burley in the show `` true blood '' . since 2013 she has played virginia e. johnson in `` masters of sex '' . she was born in los angeles to a jewish family .
