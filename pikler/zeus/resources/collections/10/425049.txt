Primarily Obsessional OCD     primarily obsessional obsessive-compulsive disorder ( also called purely obsessional ocd ) is a lesser-known form of obsessive-compulsive disorder ( ocd ) . for people with primarily obsessional ocd , there are less easy-to-see compulsions , compared to the compulsions that people with regular ocd have ( like checking , counting , hand-washing ) . while people with primarily obsessional ocd may do rituals , they mostly take place in their mind . primarily obsessional ocd often takes the form of horrific thoughts that pop into the person 's head . these thoughts may be scary or violent .
