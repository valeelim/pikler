Đỗ Mười    đỗ mười đỗ mười ( born 2 february 1917 ) is a vietnamese communist politician . he served as the general secretary of the central committee of the communist party of vietnam from 27 june 1991 through 29 december 1997. muoi was born on 2 february 1917 in dong phu , thanh trì , hanoi as nguyễn duy cống . he turned 100 in 2017 .
