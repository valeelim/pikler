Middle Angles     the middle angles were an important ethnic group in the kingdom of mercia during the anglo-saxon period . it is probable that angles moved into the midlands from east anglia early in the 6th century . the area they occupied is now centred around modern leicestershire and northamptonshire . they were in place before the reign of penda ( c.626-655 ) . he made his eldest son , peada , king of the middle angles in 653. for some time they kept their own identity in mercia . the middle angles had their own bishop based in leicester . other websites . - the middle angles , anglo-saxon kingdoms , `` the history files ''
