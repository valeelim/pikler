Kenneth Welsh     kenneth welsh , cm ( born march 30 , 1942 in edmonton , alberta , canada ) is a canadian-american movie and television actor ( sometimes credited as ken welsh ) . he is known for his roles in `` twin peaks '' and in `` the aviator '' . he also appeared in `` the day reagan was shot '' and in `` the day after tomorrow '' . other websites . - kenneth welsh at northernstars.ca - excerpts from an interview with kenneth welsh , conducted by charles prepolec on september 8 , 2000 .
