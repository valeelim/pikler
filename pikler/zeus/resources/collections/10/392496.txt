Commodore BASIC     commodore basic is also known as pet basic . it is a basic programming language . it was used in commodore international 's 8-bit home computers . it started with the pet computer in 1977. the last computer to use commodore basic was commodore 128 of 1985. the core was based on 6502 microsoft basic . it shares many characteristics with other 6502 basics like applesoft basic . commodore licensed basic from microsoft on a `` pay once with no royalties '' basis after jack tramiel turned down bill gates ' offer of a $ 3 per unit fee stating `` i 'm already married '' , and would pay no more than $ 25,000 for a perpetual license .
