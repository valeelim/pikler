Pequot War     the pequot war ( 1634-1638 ) was fought between the pequot tribe and settlers of the massachusetts bay , plymouth , and saybrook colonies . the settlers were helped by native american groups including the narragansett and mohegan tribes . hundreds of people were killed , and hundreds more were sold as slaves in the west indies . at the end of the war , about 700 pequots had been killed or captured . the others were forced off their lands and into other areas .
