The Hangover Part III     the hangover part iii is an 2013 american comedy movie . it is the last movie of `` the hangover trilogy '' , following `` the hangover '' and `` the hangover part ii '' . it stars bradley cooper , ed helms , zach galifianakis , heather graham , ken jeong , and john goodman . the movie was released on may 23 , 2013 .
