Ignition (Remix)    ignition ( remix ) `` ignition ( remix ) '' is a song by r & b singer r. kelly . it was released as a single in 2003. it peaked at number two in the u.s. `` billboard '' hot 100 and number one in the uk singles chart . it also peaked at number one in some other countries . the song has been received quite well and was a commercial success . it is ranked as one of the best songs ever by `` rolling stone '' .
