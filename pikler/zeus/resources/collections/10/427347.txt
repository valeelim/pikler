Troublemaker (Olly Murs song)    troublemaker ( olly murs song ) `` troublemaker '' is a song by olly murs . he also wrote the song . it was released on october 12 , 2012. the song features flo rida . `` troublemaker '' was a major commercial success . it was a success across the united kingdom and the united states . it also served as the closing song at murs 's 2013 arena tour .
