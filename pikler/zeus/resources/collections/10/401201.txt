Rosalía Arteaga     lupe rosalía arteaga serrano de fernández de córdova ( born december 5 , 1956 ) is an ecuadorian politician who served as the country 's first female president for only two days in 1997. arteaga became president when abdalá bucaram left office . she was born in cuenca , ecuador . other websites . - encyclopædia britannica - about the editorial board - short biography
