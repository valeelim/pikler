Juan Manuel Lebrero Morillo     juan manuel lebrero morillo ( born october 22 , 1969 in sevilla ) is a track and field athlete from spain . he has a physical disability : he is a t44 type athlete . he competed the 1996 summer paralympics in the shot put . he was the third best person in the shot put . he finished 9th in the javelin and he finished 5th in the discus .
