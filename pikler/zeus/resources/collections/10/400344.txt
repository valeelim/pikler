Jorge Nuñez Piris     jorge nuñez piris ( born jue 18 , 1972 in madrid ) is a track and field athlete from spain . he has a disability : he is blind and a t12/b2 type athlete . he raced at the 1996 summer paralympics . he finished first in the 4 x 100 meter t11-t13 race . he finished third in the 100 meter t12 race .
