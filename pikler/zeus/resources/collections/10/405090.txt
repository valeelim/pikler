Luxor     luxor ( ) is a city in lower egypt . in 2012 , about 506,588 people lived there . to tourists , it is mostly known because of karnak , as well as other tourist sites nearby . because the ancient egyptian city of thebes is located in luxor , the city has been called the `` world 's greatest open-air museum .
