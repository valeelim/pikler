1556 Shaanxi earthquake     the 1556 shaanxi earthquake was a very bad earthquake that occurred on the morning of 23 january 1556 in shaanxi , during the ming dynasty . it killed around 830,000 people , making it the worst earthquake in human history by number of deaths . more than 97 counties in the provinces of shaanxi , shanxi , henan , gansu , hebei , shandong , hubei , hunan , jiangsu and anhui were affected . the earthquake destroyed a area . in some counties , 60 % of the population was killed . it had a magnitude of around 8.0. at the time , many people in that area lived in homes they made from artificial caves . loess , the material that made these caves , is known to be very weak and can cause landslides . scholars believe that is why so many people had died . other websites . - ruins of hua county earthquake
