Carlos Ghosn     carlos ghosn is a french-lebanese-brazilian businessman . he is currently the chairman and chief executive officer of renault , which is based in paris . he holds the same positions at nissan , based in japan . together , these companies produce more than 10 % of cars sold around the world . ghosn is also chairman and ceo of the renault-nissan alliance , the strategic partnership overseeing the two companies through a unique cross-shareholding agreement . he was born in brazil .
