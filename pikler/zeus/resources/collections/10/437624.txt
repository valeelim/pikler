Tuusniemi     tuusniemi is a municipality in finland . more than 2,800 people lived there in august 2013. the municipalities next to it are heinävesi , juankoski , kaavi , kuopio , leppävirta and outokumpu . between 1904 and 1975 there was an asbestos mine in paakkila village . it was closed because workers died earlier than others . other websites . - the official tuusniemi page
