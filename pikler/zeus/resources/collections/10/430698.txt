Burlesque (movie)    burlesque ( movie ) burlesque is a 2010 musical movie . it is about a woman losing her waitress job and then moving to los angeles . she gets a job dancing in a burlesque show . cher plays tess , christina aguilera plays ali and julianne hough plays georgia . the movie was sent to north american theaters just before thanksgiving 2010. the ratings of the movie were mixed to negative . its box office performance , however , was close to $ 90 million . related pages . - burlesque ( soundtrack ) , the soundtrack from the movie
