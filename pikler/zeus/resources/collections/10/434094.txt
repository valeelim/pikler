Childlessness     childlessness is not having a child . since the 1970s , there are more childless people . there are two kinds of childlessness , voluntary and involuntary . voluntary childlessness is when people choose to not have children . people do this for several reasons , which include : - celibacy ( not having sexual intercourse ) - not wanting to have a child ( see childfree ) - not having enough money to take care of a child - being too old to have a child voluntary childlessness is sometimes called childfree . there are also several reason for involuntary childlessness , including : - infertility , which means they are unable to biologically have children . - the death of an infant or child either before , during , or shortly after birth . sometimes a couple can have a child through artificial insemination , when a sperm is place in a woman 's uterus immediately after ovulation . another option is in vitro fertilization ( or ivf ) . in this process , a sperm and an egg are placed together until fertilization happens , then the egg is put into the uterus .
