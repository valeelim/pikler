Josh Ryan Evans     joshua ryan evans ( 10 january 1982 - 5 august 2002 ) was an american actor . he was best known for acting as timmy in the television series `` passions '' . he also acted in movies . due to dwarfism , josh was only 3 feet 2 inches tall and had the voice of a small child . evans died from complications due to a congenital heart condition . he was born in hayward , california and died in san diego , california .
