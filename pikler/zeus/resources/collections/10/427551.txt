River Tyne     the river tyne is a river in north east england . it is used for water sport in the summer . more than 43,500 people come and visit it every year . there is a famous bridge that crosses it called the `` tyne bridge '' . another bridge that crosses it is the millennium bridge . the bridges connect gateshead to newcastle upon tyne . other websites . - bridges on the tyne - online charts of the tyne , to newcastle - tyne rivers trust
