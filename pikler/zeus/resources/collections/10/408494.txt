Les Misérables (musical)    les misérables ( musical ) les misérables is a musical . it is based on the 1862 novel of the same name by victor hugo . the book of the musical was written by claude-michel schönberg and alain boublil . the lyrics were written by alain boublil and jean-marc natel . the music was written by claude-michel schönberg . herbert kretzmer adapted the lyrics and text to english . the musical opened in 1980 in paris . in 1985 , it opened in london 's west end . in 1987 , it opened on broadway . it won a tony award for best musical , and a drama desk award for outstanding musical . in 2012 , it was adapted to a movie starring hugh jackman , russell crowe , and anne hathaway .
