Álvaro Galán Floria    álvaro galán floria alvaro galan floria ( born january 28 , 1977 in madrid ) is a boccia player from spain . he has a physical disability : he has cerebral palsy and is a bc2 type athlete . he competed at the 2000 summer paralympics . he was on the bc1/bc2 team from spain . his team finished second . in the first - second finish game , south korea finished first by a score of 7-6 .
