Noah (movie)    noah ( movie ) noah is a biblical epic movie . it is directed by darren aronofsky and written by aronofsky and ari handel . it stars russell crowe , anthony hopkins , emma watson , jennifer connelly , douglas booth , logan lerman , and ray winstone . the movie is based on the story of noah 's ark . the movie was released in north american cinemas on march 28 , 2014 .
