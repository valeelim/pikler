Reflektor     reflektor is the fourth album by arcade fire . it was released on october 28 , 2013. the album has two discs . the first disc has seven songs . the second disc has six songs . it got good reviews from music critics . pitchfork gave it a score of 9.2 out of 10. the first single from the album , `` reflektor '' , was released on september 9 , 2013. the album was number one in several countries including the united states , canada and the united kingdom .
