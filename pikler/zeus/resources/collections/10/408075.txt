Solar eclipse of July 16, 2186    solar eclipse of july 16 , 2186 a total solar eclipse will happen on july 16 , 2186. it will pass over the southern galápagos islands ( with a maximum eclipse of 4 minutes occurring over the southern tip of española island ) , northern south america ( ecuador including bogota for 4 minutes and 50 seconds ) , central venezuela , and northern guyana ( with a maximum eclipse of 7 minutes 4 seconds just north of anna regina ) . this will be the longest total solar eclipse between the dates of 4000 bc and 600 ad . it will last a maximum of 7 minutes , 29 seconds . the longest historical total eclipse lasted 7 minutes 28 seconds on june 15 , 744bc . other websites . - nasa solar eclipses : 2101 to 2200 - besselian elements - nasa googlemap of eclipse path
