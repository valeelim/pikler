Manfred Bleskin     manfred bleskin ( 13 december 1949 – 21 january 2014 ) was a german journalist and television presenter . bleskin was born in tribsees , east germany . he began working after receiving a doctorate from the akademie für gesellschaftswissenschaften . he spoke five languages . bleskin died on 21 january 2014 , aged 64 , in berlin , germany .
