Division of Greenway     the division of greenway is an australian electoral division in new south wales . it was set up in 1984 and is named for francis greenway , an ex-convict who became an important architect in colonial sydney . it is in the west and north-west suburbs of sydney . it includes acacia gardens , girraween , glenwood , kellyville ridge , kings langley , kings park , lalor park , parklea , seven hills , stanhope gardens , the ponds , toongabbie and parts of blacktown , pendle hill , prospect , quakers hill , riverstone , rouse hill , schofields and vineyard . election results . - 2004 election results - 2007 election results - 2010 election results - 2013 election results other websites . - division of greenway - australian electoral commission - michelle rowland 's website
