The Octonauts     the octonauts is a american-british television series is an disney junior from february 14 , 2011 until november 25 , 2014. it is produced by silvergate media for the bbc channel cbeebies . its first episode ran on 10 october 2010. the show is broadcast in the united states through disney junior . it was first shown there in 2012. it is like `` star trek '' and `` thunderbirds '' along with the adventures of jacques cousteau .
