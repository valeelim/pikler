The Pipettes     the pipettes are an english girl group . they were formed in brighton by robert `` monster bobby '' barry in 2003 at a seaside party . the members were julia clarke-lowes , rose elinor dougall and rebecca stephens . the band has had a lot of changes of members . the members are now gwenno and ani saunders . they have released two albums and two eps . their single `` pull shapes '' reached number 26 in the uk singles chart . ani saunders joined the band in 2008. she is also a member of the lovely wars .
