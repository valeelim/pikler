Christy Hemme     christina lee `` christy '' hemme ( born october 28 , 1980 ) is an american ring announcer , professional wrestling valet , singer , former model and retired professional wrestler . she currently works for total nonstop action wrestling as an interviewer and ring announcer . she is also known for her time in world wrestling entertainment where she won the 2004 diva search . she stayed with them from 2004 to 2005 and was released from her wwe contract on december 5 , 2005. she signed with total nonstop action wrestling ( tna ) in april 2006. championships . - pro wrestling illustrated - she was ranked # 42 of the best 50 female singles wrestlers in the pwi female 50 in 2008 . - total nonstop action wrestling - knockout of the year ( 2006 ) - world wrestling entertainment - wwe diva search winner ( 2004 ) other websites . - tna profile
