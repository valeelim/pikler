Elon Gold     elon gold ( born september 14 , 1970 ) is an american comedian , television actor , writer and producer . gold was a guest judge on the celebrity impersonation series , `` the next big thing '' . he has been in multiple movies such as `` cheaper by the dozen '' . he was also in the sitcom `` in-laws '' . gold is from new york . in his younger years , gold attended the westchester day school , in westchester , and the marsha stern talmudical academy , in manhattan . gold has also stated that he is an observing jew .
