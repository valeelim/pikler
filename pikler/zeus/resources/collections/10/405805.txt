Hugh Baily     hugh baily ( 1935- ? ) was an american sideshow attraction . his stage name was the pretzel man . he also appeared as spider boy or crawfish boy . he was born with twisted and deformed limbs . his shins were bent at 90 degree angles . he may have been afflicted with parastremmatic dwarfism . references . - hartzman , marc . 2006 . `` american sideshow '' . penguin . p. 115 .
