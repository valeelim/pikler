VFW-Fokker 614     the vfw-fokker 614 is a west german airliner . it is a twin-turbojets aircraft . its uniqueness lies in the implementation of its two turbojets above the wing . vfw-fokker 614 can carry between 40 and 44 passengers . it was used by airlines in denmark , france , and germany until 2007. german air force , the `` luftwaffe '' , also used this aircraft for speed liaisons and vip transport .
