Céline (brand)    céline ( brand ) céline is a french luxury fashion house . it was founded in 1945 by céline vipiana . today , it is an international brand owned by lvmh . it was sold to lvmh in july 1988 for 2.7 billion francs ( us $ 540 million ) . céline started as a children 's shoe business . it later began selling women 's shoes and accessories . it began selling ready-to-wear shoes in the 1960s . celine vipiana remained the designer until 1997. american fashion designer michael kors then took over as creative director for the brand . kors turned the fashion house into a highly successful brand . the current creative director of céline is phoebe philo , since october 2008. chief designers . - céline vipiana ( 1945–1997 ) - michael kors ( 1997–2004 ) - roberto menichetti ( 2004–2005 ) - ivana omazic ( 2005–2008 ) - phoebe philo ( 2008–present )
