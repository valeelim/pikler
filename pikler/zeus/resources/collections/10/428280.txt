Marianne Muellerleile     marianne muellerleile ( born november 26 , 1948 ) is an american actress . she played norma bates ( 2000-2008 ) in the nbc soap opera `` passions '' . in `` the terminator '' in 1984 , she also played a woman with a name similar to arnold schwarzenegger 's confidant and love interest . muellerleile was born in st. louis , missouri .
