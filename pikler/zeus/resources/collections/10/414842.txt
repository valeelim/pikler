Holland, Michigan    holland , michigan holland , michigan is a city in western michigan , united states . about 33,279 people live in holland as of 2012. holland is famous for its tulip time festival , held every may . the festival was named best small town festival by reader 's digest . holland was started in 1847 by dutch calvinist separatists , led by dr. albertus van raalte . they had left the netherlands because they had been persecuted ( were not treated nicely ) there because of their religious beliefs .
