Hürtgen Forest     the hürtgen forest ( also : huertgen forest ; ) is along the border between belgium and germany . the forest is in the southwest corner of the german federal state of north rhine-westphalia . the rur river runs along the eastern edge of the forest . during world war ii , the u.s. and german armies took part in the battle of hürtgen forest . in 1998 , hbo made `` when trumpets fade '' , which is a movie about the battle .
