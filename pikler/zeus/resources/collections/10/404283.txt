The Shaggy Dog     the shaggy dog is a 1959 walt disney movie . it stars fred macmurray , tommy kirk , kevin corcoran , and annette funicello . it is based on the story , `` the hound of florence '' by felix salten . it was disney 's first live-action movie . a sequel was made in 1976 called `` the shaggy d.a . '' which stars dean jones , tim conway , and suzanne pleshette .
