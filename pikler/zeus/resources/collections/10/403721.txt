Donkey Kong Country: Tropical Freeze    donkey kong country : tropical freeze donkey kong country : tropical freeze is a platform video game for the wii u game console . it was developed by retro studios and published by nintendo . it is the seventh game in the `` donkey kong country '' series . it is the sequel to `` donkey kong country returns '' . it was released on february 21 , 2014. the story is about a group of viking animals that invade donkey kong island . players are able to play as donkey kong , diddy kong , dixie kong , and cranky kong . this game is the first game to have dixie kong as a playable character since `` '' . it has two-player multiplayer . the music for this game was composed by david wise . david wise wrote the music for the original super nintendo `` donkey kong country '' games a very long time ago . he was not involved in `` donkey kong country returns '' , which was released in 2010 .
