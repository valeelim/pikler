Kaira     kaira ( ; altai : `` кайра '' , ) is the king of the gods and ruler of the universe in turkic and altai mythologies . he is the god of creation and the creator of everything . kaira sits on the 17th floor of sky and determines the fate of the universe . he created the other gods . he existed before the creation of the universe . he planted a tree with nine boughes . this tree connects the sky and earth and nine human races are descended from the tree . his sons are `` ulgan '' , `` kyzaghan '' and `` mergen '' . in ancient turkic beliefs , kaira is a white goose that flies over an endless water . ak ana ( white mother ) appears and tells him to create . kaira is the biggest and the strongest of all the gods . so he creates the earth . the word ( kaira ) means gracious or benevolent . other pages . - ulgan - son of ulgan . god of benevolence . other websites . - türk mitolojisi ( türk mitleri ve destanları ) kayra han
