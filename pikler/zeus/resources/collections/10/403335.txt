Vicente Aguilar Carmona     vicente aguilar carmona ( born april 10 , 1970 in valencia ) is a 5-per-team football player from spain . he has a disability : he is blind . he played 5-per-team football at the 2004 summer paralympics . his team finished third after they played greece and , won 2-0. he was a member of the national team in 2013 and competed in the european championships .
