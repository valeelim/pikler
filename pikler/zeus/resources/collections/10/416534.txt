Centre for Conflict and Peace Studies     the centre for conflict and peace studies ( abbreviated caps ) is an independent research organization , which started in july 2006. its office is in kabul , afghanistan . this office is a research centre , which means that it is an important place where people can do independent research . most of caps ' research is about governance , security , and developmental challenges ( difficulties ) in afghanistan . most research centres in afghanistan have foreign - non-afghan - workers , but the people who work at caps are from afghanistan . caps is a place where people can discuss ways to change afghanistan . the workers from caps tell other afghan people about their research . they also discuss their research with people from other countries . when people from other countries come to afghanistan to work , they can learn how to live in afghanistan from caps . other websites . - official website
