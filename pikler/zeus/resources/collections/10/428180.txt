Ming-Na Wen     ming-na wen ( born november 20 , 1963 ) is a chinese actress . she is known for doing the voice of fa mulan in the disney film 1998 `` mulan '' and `` mulan ii '' . she played jing-mei chen in `` er '' . wen plays agent melinda may in `` agents of s.h.i.e.l.d '' . wen was born in hong kong . her name means `` enlightenment '' . when she was a child she moved to the united states . in 1995 she married eric michael zee . she lives with him in california . other websites . - official website
