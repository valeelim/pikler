Douglas A-3 Skywarrior     the douglas a-3 skywarrior is an american twin-turbojet military aircraft . its first flight was in 1952. it was first used in 1956. it was originally designed and built as an attack aircraft . later it was optimized for aerial refueling and electronic warfare as ka-3 and ea-3 . the us navy used it from aircraft carriers for the vietnam war . the b-66 destroyer is a variant built for the us air force . the douglas a-3 skywarrior has been out of service since 1991 .
