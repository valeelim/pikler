Cleat     cleats or studs are spikes or studs that are attached to the bottom of a shoe to provide more traction ( grip ) on a soft or slippery surface . shoes specially designed for several kinds of sports have cleats , so that the players do not slip on the ground so easily . ice cleats ( or crampons ) are a type of spikes that usually are attachable to the soles of shoes to prevent slipping on ice .
