Sassarese language     sassarese ( local name `` sassaresu '' or `` turritanu '' ) is a romance language . it is related to the corsican language and the sardinian language . it is also related to gallurese . the latter is regarded as a corsican dialect despite the geographic location , although this attribution is a matter of controversy . it can be considered a transitional language between italo-dalmatian languages sassarese is spoken by approximately 120,000 people in the northwest coastal areas of sardinia , italy .
