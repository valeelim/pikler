Margarita Henríquez     margarita esther henríquez rodríguez ( born april 17 , 1991 , panama ) is the winner of the third season of `` latin american idol '' , which she won on october 9 , 2008. henríquez became the youngest contestant to win and the third one since carlos peña . henríquez beat the runner up maría josé castillo for the title . she released her first album under the sony bmg label on december 1 , 2008. it has been very successful in panama . it contains six songs that she performed during the tv show . her song `` vuela '' has been successful on panamanian radio stations .
