The Windy Hill     the windy hill is a long book for children . it was written by cornelia meigs . it tells a story about a boy and girl who live in new england . they meet a man who tells them stories . the stories are about other people who used to live in new england . the people in these stories are part of the boy 's and girl 's family . between these stories , the main story of the book happens . the main story is about a bad man who wants to take away the house where the boy and girl live . the bad man is part of the boy 's and girl 's family , too . this book was first printed in 1921. berta and elmer hader drew the pictures . in 1922 , this book was made a newbery medal honor book . other websites . - `` the windy hill '' at a celebration of women writers
