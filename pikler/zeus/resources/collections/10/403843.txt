Sud Aviation SA-316 Alouette III     the sud aviation sa-316 alouette iii is a french helicopter built between 1961 and 1985 by sud aviation and later by aérospatiale . it is a modernized variant of alouette ii helicopter . alouette iii was built for civil and military users for light liaison duties , light transport , search and rescue , and sometimes for air attack . most famous users were indian air force , french gendarmerie , and french sécurité civile . some were used by ireland air force . bibliography . - jackson , robert , `` helicopters : military , civilian , and rescue rotorcraft '' ( the aviation factfile ) . london : grange books ltd , 2005. isbn 1-84013-812-2 . - boulay , pierre , `` les hélicoptères français '' , paris , france : larivière éditions , 1998. isbn 2-907051-17-2
