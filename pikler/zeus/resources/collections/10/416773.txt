Flight of the Bumble Bee     `` flight of the bumble bee '' is a musical composition by nikolai rimsky-korsakov for solo violin and orchestra . it was written as an interlude for his opera , `` the tale of tsar saltan '' ( 1899-1900 ) . the music depicts the buzzing of a bumble bee in its flight . it is a popular piece , especially with children , and is often played outside the opera as a concert piece .
