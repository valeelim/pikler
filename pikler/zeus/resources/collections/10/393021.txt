Cannabis drug testing     cannabis drug testing describes many drug testing methods for the use of cannabis in medicine , sport , and law . cannabis use is easy to detect using urine testing , hair analysis and breath . there are some ways to cheat such a test using synthetic urine or human hair - making blood testing the most effective . unlike alcohol , cannabis testing is time consuming , expensive and can not give accurate time of consuming and level of intoxication . the lack of good testing methods and universal intoxication level scale is an issue in the legality of cannabis debate , especially regarding intoxicated driving .
