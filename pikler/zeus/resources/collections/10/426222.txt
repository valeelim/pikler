Kelowna     kelowna is a city in british columbia , canada . it is on okanagan lake in the okanagan valley . the name `` kelowna '' comes from the okanagan word for `` grizzly bear '' . kelowna is the 22nd largest metropolitan area in canada . in 2011 , it had a population of 179,839. the communities nearby include the district municipality of west kelowna ( also called westbank , westside ) to the west across okanagan lake . lake country and vernon are to the north . peachland is to the southwest and summerland and penticton further south . kelowna is also sometimes called `` ktown '' for short .
