Awasa     awasa ( sometimes spelled or awassa or hawassa ) is a city in ethiopia . it is on the shores of lake awasa in the great rift valley . the city is the capital of the southern nations , nationalities , and peoples region . it is on the trans-african highway 4 to cairo-cape town . it has 165,275 people ( 2012 ) . the city was once the capital of the sidamo province from 1978 to 1995 ; when the 1995 constitution was adopted and the province was abolished . the city also has hawassa university , which was created in 1999 . 194 members of the sidama liberation movement were arrested and held in awasa prison in september 1994. the chairman fled to the united kingdom after someone tried to assassinate him . other websites . - official city website
