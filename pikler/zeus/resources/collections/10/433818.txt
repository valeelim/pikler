Sue Ane Langdon     sue ane langdon ( previously sue lookhoff ; born 8 march 1936 ) is a retired american actress . she began her career with singing and theatre . on the television series `` perry mason '' , she played rowena leach . she also acted in lots of movies . her character was opal ann in the 1970 movie `` the cheyenne social club '' . langdon was born in paterson , new jersey .
