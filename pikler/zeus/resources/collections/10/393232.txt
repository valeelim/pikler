National Sun Yat-sen University     national sun yat-sen university ( nsysu ) is a university in taiwan , named in honor of dr. sun yat-sen , the founding father of the republic of china . the main campus is in kaohsiung city . it was founded in 1980. nsysu is among the four universities of the taiwan comprehensive university system . other websites . - nsysu - study in taiwan national sun yat-sen university ( nsysu ) - national sun yat-sen university reviews - kaohsiung , taiwan attractions - tripadvisor
