Kymenlaakso     kymenlaakso is a region in finland . as of november 2013 , there were over 180,000 people living there . neighbouring regions are uusimaa , päijänne tavastia , southern savonia and south karelia . municipalities in the region are hamina , iitti , kotka , kouvola , miehikkälä , pyhtää and virolahti . kymenlaakso was one most earliest industrialized areas in finland , since around the 1800s . other websites . - webpages
