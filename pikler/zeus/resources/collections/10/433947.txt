7.92 x 57mm Mauser     the 7.92x57mm mauser , aka 8mm mauser round is a german bullet first used in the g98 mauser rifle . other german rifles chambered ( size of the bullet the gun can take ) in 7.92x57mm include weapons such as the k98 , mg34 , mg42 , fg42 , g41 , and g43 . a variant ( different style ) of the 7.92x57mm , the 7.92x33 mm `` 8mm kurz '' , was created as an intermediate cartridge intended to be used in urban warfare , as the german army learned from fighting in cities , mainly in russia after operation barbarossa . the 8mm kurz was used in the stg44 assault rifle . this round , weapon , and concept was picked up and expanded on by the russians after the war , and this led up to the development of the 7.62x39mm cartridge used in the sks45 , ak47 ( and variants ) , and the rpd light machine gun .
