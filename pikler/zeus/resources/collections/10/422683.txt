Holiness movement     the holiness movement is about certain of beliefs and practices that came from methodism in the 19th-century . it also refers to some groups of evangelical christianity who believe that these ideas are central doctrine . the movement is distinguished by its focus on john wesley 's teaching of `` christian perfection '' . it says that it is possible to live free of sin , and specifically by the belief that this can be done instantly by preforming a second work of grace . other websites . - how they entered canaan : a collection of holiness experience accounts by b. g. paddock - holiness history from the global anabaptist mennonite encyclopedia online - a look at holiness and perfectionism theology by armin j. panning
