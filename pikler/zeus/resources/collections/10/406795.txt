Joan Caulfield     joan caulfield ( june 1 , 1922 - june 18 , 1991 ) was an american former model and actress . she started acting during the early to mid 1940s . she was in the 1947 movie `` the unsuspected '' . she appeared mostly on television in her later life , including `` murder , she wrote '' . caulfield was born in new jersey . she later moved to los angeles . she died from cancer at age sixty-nine .
