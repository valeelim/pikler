Kacey Musgraves     kacey lee musgraves ( born august 21 , 1988 ) is an american country singer . she released her first three albums herself before being signed to mercury records in 2012. her first album on the label , `` same trailer different park '' , was released on march 19 , 2013. it went straight to number two in the `` billboard '' 200. musgraves won new artist of the year at the 2013 academy of country music awards . she also performed her single `` follow your arrow '' . `` same trailer different park '' was nominated for best country album at the 2014 grammy awards . in 2009 , she sang on three songs on mark mckinney 's album `` middle america '' . musgraves was born in mineola , texas . she wrote her first song , `` notice me '' , at the age of 9. other websites . - official website
