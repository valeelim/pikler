Pays de Bray     the pays de bray is a small natural region of france . it is northeast of rouen and is in the french départements of the seine-maritime and oise . it was in the historic of normandy and picardy since 911. the landscape is mainly bocage , a land use which has to do with its clay soil . this makes it suitable for pasture for the raising of dairy cattle . it produces famous butters and cheeses such as neuchâtel .
