German Colony, Jerusalem    german colony , jerusalem the german colony in jerusalem is a neighbourhood in jerusalem . it began as a colony established by german templars in 1873. the colony was built in the valley of rephaim on the way to bethlehem , next to the greek colony . the main street of the neighbourhood today is emek refaim street . the rephaim river passes through the valley . it is also known as `` white valley '' . other websites . - the german colony in jerusalem from the jerusalem webshots site
