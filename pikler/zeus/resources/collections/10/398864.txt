Ruben Alvarez Serrano     rubén álvarez serrano ( born june 19 , 1975 in barcelona ) is a t42 track and field athlete from spain . he has a disability : he is missing part of one of his arms or legs . he jumped at the 1992 summer paralympics . he was not one of the best races . he jumped at the 1996 summer paralympics . he finished third in the triple jump . he finished first in the long jump . he jumped at the 2000 summer paralympics . he finished third in the triple jump .
