2013 El Reno tornado     the 2013 el reno tornado occurred on may 31 , 2013. it happened close to the city of el reno , oklahoma in central oklahoma . it was the widest tornado ever recorded . this tornado also was found to have some of the strongest winds ever found . one mobile doppler radar found very high winds of 296 miles per hour . these winds are slightly less than the 301 mile per hour winds detected in a tornado on may 3 , 1999 while it was close to bridge creek , oklahoma in the oklahoma city metropolitan area . < br >
