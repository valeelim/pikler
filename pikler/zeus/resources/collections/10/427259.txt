Jamia Mosque     the jamia mosque or shelley street mosque ( ) is a mosque in hong kong , people 's republic of china . it is the first and oldest mosque in hong kong . the original building of the mosque was built in 1890 during the british hong kong government as the mohammedan mosque on a piece of land rented by the government in 1850. in 1915 , the building of the mosque was expanded and it was renamed to jamia mosque after world war ii . the mosque building is included into the grade i building in which it is considered as a historical building which needs to be protected and conserved .
