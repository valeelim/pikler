The Browning Version     the browning version is a british drama movie from 1994. the movie is about a teacher , andrew , having a failed life . people hate him and do not respect him . his wife , laura , is not faithful . andrew is forced to retire , and may not even receive a pension . greta scacchi plays laura . albert finney plays andrew . the runtime is almost 97 minutes . this was released 8 june 1994 .
