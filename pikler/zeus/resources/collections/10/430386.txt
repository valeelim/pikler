Hammer Bro.    hammer bro .  , also called hammer brothers or hammer bros. , is an enemy from the `` mario '' series of video games . it is a type of koopa troopa that walks on two feet and attacks by throwing hammers at the player character . there are different types of hammer bro . that are named after the attack that they use , such as the boomerang bro . and the fire bro . the hammer bro . has appeared in many `` mario '' games since its first appearance in `` super mario bros.
