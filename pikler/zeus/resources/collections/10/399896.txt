Tarzan and the Golden Lion     tarzan and the golden lion is a 1927 jungle movie . it is a tarzan silent movie . it is based on a book of the same name by edgar rice burroughs . the movie was directed by j. p. mcgowan . it stars james pierce as tarzan . boris karloff plays owaza . after searching for a city of diamonds , tarzan races with his pet lion jad-bal-ja to save a young woman from being sacrificed to a lion-god .
