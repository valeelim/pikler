Bluebeard's Castle    bluebeard 's castle bluebeard 's castle is a one-act opera by béla bartók . the words are written in hungarian . the opera is based on the 1697 french fairy tale `` bluebeard '' by charles perrault . there are only two characters in the opera : bluebeard and his wife judith . the opera was written in 1911. it was first performed in budapest in 1918 .
