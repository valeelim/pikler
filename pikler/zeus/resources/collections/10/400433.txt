Julio Requena Hernandez     julio requena hernandez ( born september 9 , 1969 in leon ) is a track and field athlete from spain . he has a disability : he is blind and a t11/b1 type athlete . he raced at the 1992 summer paralympics . he finished first in the 100 meter b1 race . he raced at the 1996 summer paralympics . he finished first in the 4 x 100 meter t11-t13 race , the 100 meter t11 race and the 200 meter t11 race . he raced at the 2000 summer paralympics . he finished third in the 4 x 100 meter t11-t13 race and the 200 meter t11 race .
