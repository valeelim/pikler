Malcolm X (movie)    malcolm x ( movie ) malcolm x is a 1992 american biographical movie directed by spike lee . its stars denzel washington as malcolm x. it also stars angela bassett , albert hall , al freeman , jr. , and delroy . al sharpton and nelson mandela also appear in this movie . it received positive reviews . it was released on november 18 , 1992. in 2010 , the movie was selected for preservation in the united states national film registry by the library of congress as being `` culturally , historically , or aesthetically significant '' .
