Jane Goldman     jane loretta anne goldman ( born 11 june 1970 ) is an english screenwriter and television producer . she was born in hammersmith , west london . she wrote the screenplay for the 2012 movie `` the woman in black '' . she married jonathan ross in 1988. she has two daughters and a son with him . she lives with ross in hampstead garden suburb . she produces `` the big fat quiz of the year '' .
