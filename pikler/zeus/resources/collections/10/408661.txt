Hamilton Tiger-Cats     the hamilton tiger-cats are a canadian football league team in hamilton , ontario . they won 8 grey cups . they play at tim hortons field in hamilton , which opened in 2014 at the site of their former home of ivor wynne stadium . the tiger-cats were founded in 1950 when the hamilton tigers , founded in 1869 , merged with the hamilton wildcats , formed in 1941. other websites . - official website
