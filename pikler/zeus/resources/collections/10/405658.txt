Helicopter parent     the term helicopter parent means when a parent pays closer than usual attention to experiences from their children . the terminology began appearing around 1969. it was not until late 2002 or 2003 , however , that the term became very popular via media and cell phone use . this was at exactly the same time the millennials begin turning 18 and moving into college . the rising of mobile phones is often blamed for the rise of helicopter parent-style teaching over the years .
