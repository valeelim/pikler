Taichung Mosque     the taichung mosque ( ) is a mosque in taichung , taiwan . it is the fourth mosque in taiwan . the original building of the mosque was built in 1951. after fleeing mainland china at the end of chinese civil war in 1949 , some of the nationalist muslim army resided in changhua . most of them did their prayers in some residential houses . to accommodate the growing number of muslims in taiwan , a mosque was planned to be built in 1951. the first location of the mosque was at zhongxiao road in taichung city . when the number of muslims grew , the new taichung mosque was constructed and reopened in 1990 at dadun south road . taichung mosque is a building with three floors . it has shop , restaurant , dormitories and classroom . the library at the mosque is used as a place to spread information about islam to the public .
