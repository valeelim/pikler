Claude Vilgrain     claude vilgrain ( born april 17 , 1979 ) is a canadian retired professional ice hockey right winger . he played a total of 89 games in the national hockey league ( nhl ) . vilgrain was born in haiti . he is the first haitian-born player in nhl history . before playing in the nhl , vilgrain played three seasons in the quebec major junior hockey league ( qmjhl ) with the laval voisins . he also played hockey at the universit√© de moncton for three years . he was drafted with the 107th overall pick by the detroit red wings in the 1982 nhl entry draft . he never played a game for the red wings . he signed a contract with the vancouver canucks in june 1987. he played six games with them , scoring one goal and making one assist . in march 1989 , the canucks traded vilgrain to the new jersey devils in exchange for tim lenardon . during his time with the devils , he played 81 games and made 20 goals and 31 assists . the philadelphia flyers signed him to a contract in 1993. he only played 2 games with them . he retired in 2002 .
