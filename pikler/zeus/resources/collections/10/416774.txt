Scheherazade (Rimsky-Korsakov)    scheherazade ( rimsky-korsakov ) scheherazade is a piece of music by nikolai rimsky-korsakov . it was written for orchestra in 1888. the music was inspired by the arabian nights stories . there are four movements : `` sinbad the sailor '' ; `` the kalendar prince '' ; `` the young prince and the young princess '' ; and `` festival at baghdad '' . fokine designed a ballet in 1910 for the ballets russes using the music .
