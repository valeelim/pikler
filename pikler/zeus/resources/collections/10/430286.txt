Bruce Reynolds     bruce richard reynolds ( 7 september 1931 – 28 february 2013 ) was a british criminal . he led the great train robbery of 1963. at the time it was britain 's largest robbery . he stole an amount equal to £41 million today ( 2013 ) . reynolds spent five years on the run from police . he was imprisoned for 25 years in 1969. he was released in 1978. he wrote three books and performed with the band alabama 3 , for whom his son , nick , plays . reynolds was born in strand , london . he also had convictions for assault and supplying drugs . he died in croydon , london , at the age of 81 .
