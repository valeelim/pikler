Chocolat     chocolat is a british-american romance and drama movie . it was released december 15 , 2000. it is about events in a fictional dull french village during the autumn-winter of 1959. a woman opens a chocolate shop . one customer , armande , is diabetic . she , however , continues eating chocolate . armande later dies from her illness . juliette binoche plays vianne , carrie-anne moss plays caroline clairmont and judi dench plays armande ( the diabetic woman ) . the reviews from critics were very positive . it was nominated for several academy awards . it did not win any .
