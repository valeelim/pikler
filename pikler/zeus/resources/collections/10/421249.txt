The Apprentice (UK TV series)    the apprentice ( uk tv series ) the apprentice is an english reality television program . twenty young entrepreneurs compete to win an investment from multi-millionaire businessman alan sugar . sugar and his two assistants , nick hewer and karren brady , `` fire '' a different person each week . the highlight is a comical scene in the boardroom where all the contestants try to impress alan . alan sugar is the chairman of tottenham hotspur f.c . and is worth billions of pounds . brady had a rich upbringing and is known as the `` first woman of football '' . hewer did television presenting .
