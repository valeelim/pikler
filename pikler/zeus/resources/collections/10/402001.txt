Sujata Trivedi     sujata trivedi ( ) is an indian singer whose music is played in movie soundtracks . she has made music for over 25 bollywood movies . she began singing when she was seven years old . trivdei 's training in classical music began under prabhakar dhakde in nagpur , padmashree ustaad ghulaam mustafa khan , pt . ajay pohankar , and ustad asad ali khan . she has learned various aspects of light music under pt . satyanarayan mishra , gautam mukherji and shri kalyanjibhai . she recorded with a. r. rahman songs like the title track of `` dil ne jise apna kaha '' , `` boondon se baatein '' , and classical-based `` yeh jo zindagi hai '' from `` 1947 : earth '' . she also recorded `` in saahab ko zara dekho '' for `` calcutta mail '' with viju shah . trivdei has sung in several languages , including marathi , bhojpuri , manipuri , bengali , gujarati , punjabi , tamil , malayalam , nepali , rajasthani and oriya . she has performed more than 1600 stage shows all over india and overseas . she performed individually and in association with other playback singers , such as nitin mukesh , sonu nigam , manhar udhas , kumar sanu , vinod rathod , babul supriyo , kavita krishnamurthi , ravindra jain , abhijit , shaan and sunidhi .
