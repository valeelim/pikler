Playaz Circle     playaz circle were an american hip hop group from georgia . the members were rappers 2 chainz and dolla boy . the group was established in 1997 but did not began recording until 2007 with their best selling album . they were discovered by ludacris and he signed them to his record label dtp . they are best known for their hit single `` duffle bag boy '' that features lil wayne . in 2013 they broke up to work individually .
