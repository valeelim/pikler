Victims of Terrorist Attack on the Pentagon Memorial     the victims of terrorist attack on the pentagon memorial is a memorial dedicated to the victims of the september 11 attacks 2001 on the pentagon . the memorial is shaped as a pentagon . it has the names of the victims of the attack by the american airlines flight 77 and the victims who died in the building . the memorial was opened in 2002 in the section of the victims graves at the arlington national cemetery . other websites . - arlington national cemetery
