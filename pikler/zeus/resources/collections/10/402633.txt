Division of Cowper     the division of cowper is an australian electoral division in new south wales . it was one of 75 divisions set up for the first federal election in 1901. it is named for charles cowper , an early premier of new south wales . it is on the north coast of new south wales , and includes the towns of coffs harbour , kempsey , macksville , nambucca heads . its most famous member was sir earle page , leader of the country party and prime minister of australia in 1939. election results . - 2004 election result - 2007 election result - 2010 election result - 2013 election result other websites . - - australian electoral commission - luke hartsuyker 's website
