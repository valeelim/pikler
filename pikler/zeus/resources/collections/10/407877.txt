Brynäs IF     brynäs if is an ice hockey club in the town of gävle in sweden . it was established in 1912. the club adopted the game of ice hockey in 1939. the club won the swedish national ice hockey championship in 1964 , 1966 , 1967 , 1968 , 1970 , 1971 , 1972 , 1976 , 1977 , 1980 , 1993 , 1999 and 2012. it also lost the swedish national finals in 1975 , 1995 and 2017. other websites . - official website
