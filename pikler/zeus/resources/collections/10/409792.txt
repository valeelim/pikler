Raul García Gutierrez     raul garcía gutierrez ( born december 3 , 1971 in garrovillas , cáceres ) is a goalball athlete from spain . he has a disability : he is blind and is a b2 type athlete . his job is working as a coupon seller . he played goalball at the 2000 summer paralympics . his team was third . he played goalball at the 2008 summer paralympics . his team was 12 .
