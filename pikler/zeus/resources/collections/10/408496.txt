James Taylor (planter)    james taylor ( planter ) james taylor ( born 29 march 1835 in kincardineshire - died 2 may 1892 in kandy ) was a scottish man who brought tea plantations to sri lanka ( ceylon ) . he arrived in ceylon in 1852 and landed in loolecondera . he lived in sri lanka until his death ( more than half of his lifetime ) . he worked with thomas lipton , another scottish immigrant , to grow the tea industry in sri lanka .
