Marta Arce Payno     marta arce payno ( born july 27 , 1977 in valladolid ) is a judo athlete from spain . she has a disability : she is blind and a b3 type athlete . she competed in judo at the 2004 summer paralympics , 2008 summer paralympics and 2012 summer paralympics . she finished second at the 2004 games in the up to 57 kg group . she finished second at the 2008 games in the up to 63 kg group . she finished third in the 2012 games up to 63 kg group . she got a job with help from the employment hpod proad program , which is helped by once .
