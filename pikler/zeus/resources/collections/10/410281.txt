Grand Theft Auto V     grand theft auto v is a action-adventure video game that was made and released by rockstar games . it is the fifteenth game in the `` grand theft auto '' video game series . it was also the first main game since 2008 when grand theft auto iv was released . the game was released all around the world on september 17 , 2013 on the playstation 3 and xbox 360. in 2014 rockstar games released gta v on playstation 4 , xbox one . in 2015 , the game was released for microsoft windows . the game sold 54 million copies , becoming the fourth best selling game of all time .
