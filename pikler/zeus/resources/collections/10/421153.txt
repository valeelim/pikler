Emilia Plater     emilia plater was a polish-lithuanian noblewoman and revolutionary . plater led soldiers in several engagements during the november uprising in the polish-russian wars of 1830-1831. she became ill toward the end of the uprising and died . though she did not fight in any large battles , her story inspired many works of art and literature . she is a national heroine in poland , lithuania and belarus , all formerly parts of the polish-lithuanian commonwealth .
