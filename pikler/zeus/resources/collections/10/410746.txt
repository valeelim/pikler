Hiroto Kōmoto     hiroto kōmoto ( / , born 17 march 1963 ) is a japanese rock singer . he was born in the city of okayama in okayama prefecture . he dropped out of the department of economics in housei university . he is an executive vice-president of a corporation ( happy song ) . he is married . he had sung in many famous rock bands . he sings in the cro-magnons these days .
