Star Fox (video game)    star fox ( video game ) , known as starwing in europe , is a video game that was developed by nintendo ead and published by nintendo . it was released for the super nintendo entertainment system . it is the first game in the `` star fox '' series . it was the first three-dimensional nintendo video game . it was released in japan on february 21 , 1993 , in north america on march 23 , 1993 , and in europe on june 3 , 1993. it uses the super fx chip , which improves the graphics display . the story involves fox mccloud and his star fox team defending their home of corneria from andross , the game 's main villain . the gameplay involves guiding fox 's spacecraft , an arwing , through stages while destroying enemies . power-ups are placed in stages to help the player . the player must also defend their teammates from enemies . at the end of each stage is a boss . after completing a stage , the player is given a score based on how many enemies they destroyed and how well they defended their teammates .
