Abu Nidal     abu nidal ( ) ( may 1937 – 16 august 2002 ) , born sabri khalil al-banna ( ) , was the founder of fatah – the revolutionary council ( ) , a militant palestinian separatist group also known as the abu nidal organization ( ano ) . at the height of his power in the 1970s and 1980s , abu nidal was mostly known as the most merciless of the palestinian political leaders . he told `` der spiegel '' in a rare interview in 1985 : `` i am the evil spirit which moves around only at night causing ... nightmares .
