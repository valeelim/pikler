André Gide     andré paul guillaume gide ( 22 november 1869 – 19 february 1951 ) was a french writer . he won the nobel prize in literature in 1947. gide was born in paris into a middle class protestant family . during a trip to alegeria gide realized that he was gay . his first novel `` the notebooks of andre walter '' was published in 1891. gide met oscar wilde in 1894. in october 1895 gide married his cousin madeleine . when gide was 47 15-year-old marc allégret became his lover .
