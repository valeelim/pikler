2010–11 Chicago Blackhawks season     the 2010–11 chicago blackhawks season was the team 's 85th season in the national hockey league ( nhl ) . the team suffered from salary cap restrictions and lost several players due to it . they also had to recover from winning their first stanley cup in 40 years . the team was defeated in the first round of the stanley cup playoffs by the presidents ' trophy-winning vancouver canucks .
