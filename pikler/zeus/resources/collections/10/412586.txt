Oona O'Neill     oona , lady chaplin ( née o'neill ) ( may 14 , 1925 – september 27 , 1991 ) was the fourth wife and later widow of british comic and filmmaker sir charles `` charlie '' chaplin . she was the daughter of nobel and pulitzer prize-winning playwright eugene o'neill and writer agnes boulton . in 1954 , she gave up her american citizenship and became a british citizen .
