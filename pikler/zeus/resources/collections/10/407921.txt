Sepala Attygalle     general deshamanya don sepala attygalle , lvo , adc , idc , psc , slac ( 1922 — january 2001 ) was a sri lankan military leader , a civil servant and a diplomat . he became the longest-serving commander of the sri lankan army ( 1967–1977 ) . he went on to serve as the permanent secretary to the ministry of defense and sri lankan high commissioner to the united kingdom .
