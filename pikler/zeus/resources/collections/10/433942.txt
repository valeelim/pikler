Juicy J     jordan michael houston ( born april 5 , 1975 ) , better known by his stage name juicy j , is an american rapper , songwriter and record producer . he is from memphis , tennessee . he is a founding member of the southern hip hop group three 6 mafia . they formed in 1991. discography . - `` chronicles of the juice man '' ( 2002 ) - `` hustle till i die '' ( 2009 ) - `` stay trippy '' ( 2013 ) other websites . - three 6 mafia official website
