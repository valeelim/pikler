Artemisia (movie)    artemisia ( movie ) artemisia is a 1997 french and italian erotic and biographical movie . it is about artemisia gentileschi , a 17th-century italian painter . she is as talented as her father , who is also a painter . at that time in italy , women were not allowed to paint humans in the nude . artemisia , though , is extremely determined . the movie got very positive reviews . the mpaa originally gave `` artemisia '' an nc-17 rating . on appeal , the rating was changed to r .
