Lalande 21185     lalande 21185 is a red dwarf in the constellation of ursa major . although relatively close by , it is only magnitude 7 in visible light and thus is too dim to see with the unaided eye . the star is visible through a small telescope . at approximately away this star is the fifth closest stellar system to the sun ; only the alpha centauri system , barnard 's star , wise 1049-5319 and wolf 359 are closer . because of its proximity it is a frequent subject for astronomical surveys and other research . it is known by other designations , such as bd+36 2147 , gliese 411 , and hd 95735 .
