NXT Tag Team Championship     the nxt tag team championship is a professional wrestling championship in the wwe . the title is a nxt-only championship . the title was introduced by special guest shawn michaels on the january 23 , 2013 edition of nxt . he announced that there would be a tournament held to crown the inaugural champions . the current champions are # diy ( johnny gargano and tommaso ciampa ) . they are in their first reign , both as a team and individually . other websites . - nxt website
