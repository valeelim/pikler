Babyface     kenneth brian `` babyface '' edmonds ( born april 10 , 1959 ) is an american r & b musician . he released his first album `` lovers '' in 1986. he has won ten grammy awards . he started a record label called soda pop records in 2009. babyface will release an album with toni braxton called love marriage & divorce in 2014. other websites . - official website
