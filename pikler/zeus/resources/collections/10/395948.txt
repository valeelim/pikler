Inclosure Act 1773     the inclosure act 1773 ( 13 geo 3. c. 81 ) ( also known as the enclosure act 1773 ) is an act of the parliament of the kingdom of great britain . the act is still in force in the united kingdom . it created a law that allowed landowners to enclose land and remove the right of commoners to use the land . this act was a big part of the agrarian revolution .
