Hope Springs (2012 movie)    hope springs ( 2012 movie ) hope springs is a 2012 american romantic comedy-drama movie directed by david frankel and written by vanessa taylor . it stars meryl streep , tommy lee jones , mimi rogers , jean smart , and steve carell . the movie was released on august 8 , 2012. it received generally positive reviews and the cast was praised for their performances . it was nominated for a golden globe award , and won a people 's choice award .
