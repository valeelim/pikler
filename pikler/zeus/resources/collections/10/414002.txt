Pedro José Cevallos     pedro josé cevallos salvador ( 1830–1892 ) was president of ecuador from 1 july 1888 to 17 august 1888 and vice president from 1886 to 1890. from april to august 1891 the presidency of antonio flores jijón he was minister of public instruction , interior affairs and foreign affairs . shortly before his death he became a member of the ecuadoran academy of literature . other websites . - official website of the ecuadorian government about the country president 's history
