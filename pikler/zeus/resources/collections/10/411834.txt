Caroline Aigle     caroline aigle ( 12 september 1974 – 21 august 2007 ) was a french fighter jet pilot . she was born in montauban . she was the first in france trained to fly a mirage 2000. she died of a withering melanoma . she was mother of two boys . president of the french republic nicolas sarkozy awarded her the medal of aeronautics by posthumous recognition . a few weeks before her death , she was selected by the european space agency for training to be an astronaut . caroline aigle graduates `` école polytechnique '' and `` école de l'air '' .
