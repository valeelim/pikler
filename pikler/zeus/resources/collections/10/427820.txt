Cable tray     a cable tray is a system to put insulated cables and other wiring ( communication , network etc . ) in building structures , such as in office buildings , residential , industries etc . a cable tray system gives easy access . this is useful when adding new cables or replacing the old cables on the tray . cable trays can be made out of galvanized steel , stainless steel , aluminum or plastic . the choice depends on how it will be used or where it will be placed .
