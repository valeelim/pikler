Ben Casey     ben casey is an american medical drama television series . it ran on abc from 1961 to 1966. the series starred vince edwards and sam jaffe . ben casey is a young , intense but idealistic surgeon at county general hospital . his mentor is doctor david zorba , at the beginning of the 1965 season , jaffe left the show and franchot tone replaced him as new chief of surgery , doctor daniel niles freeland . the show began running multi-episode stories and casey developed a romantic relationship with a patient who had just emerged from a coma after thirteen years . in 1988 , the television movie , `` the return of ben casey '' , with vince edwards in his role as casey , aired in television syndication . the pilot was not picked up by the major networks to bring the series back . other websites . - i love comix archive : `` ben casey ''
