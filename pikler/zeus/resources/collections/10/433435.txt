Puebloan peoples     the pueblo people are a group of native american people who live in the southwestern part of the united states . they speak several different native languages . they are split into two major cultures , based on different systems of kinship . when the spanish arrived in the 16th century , they were living in communities that the spanish called pueblos , meaning `` towns '' . today there are 21 pueblos remaining . the main pueblos are located in the states of new mexico and arizona .
