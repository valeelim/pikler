Microsoft Studios     microsoft studios is the video game production wing for microsoft , responsible for the development and publishing of games for the xbox , xbox 360 , games for windows and windows phone platforms . they were established in 2002 as microsoft game studios to coincide with the release of the xbox , before being re-branded in 2011. microsoft studios develops and publishes games in conjunction with first and third party development studios under their publishing label . other wbesites . - microsoft 's official xbox website
