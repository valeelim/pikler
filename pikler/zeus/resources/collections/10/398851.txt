Goalball     goalball is a sport for people who can not see . it is played by men and women . each team has three people on it . a team throws the ball at a goal behind the people on the different team . they get a point if the ball goes in the goal . the ball has a bell in it . players can not see the ball : they can hear the bell . they use their body to stop the ball . people wear glasses that make it so player can see anything .
