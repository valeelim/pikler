Bubblegum pop     bubblegum pop , is a style of pop music . it derives from synthpop . it is much like its predecessors , although it adds traditional nursery rhymes to a synth background . the archies created this genre of music . `` sugar , sugar '' is the first song which the archies made that is bubblegum pop . bubblegum pop also can be called `` bubblegum music '' , or just simply `` bubblegum '' .
