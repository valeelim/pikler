Billy The Kid (book)    billy the kid ( book ) billy the kid is the story of a boy who has a dream to become a professional footballer . the book was published in 2000 and was written by michael morpurgo . it is illustrated by michael foreman . most of the book is set during world war ii . throughout the story , billy , the main character , has to cope with a lot of sadness and loss . whilst playing football in the local park , he is scouted by the club of his dreams : chelsea fc . but his mood quickly changes when his brother dies in the war . this persuades billy to join the army . the idea for the book came about when michael foreman took michael morpurgo to a chelsea game and pointed out the chelsea pensioners , who get free tickets for every game . morpurgo was fascinated to learn that one pensioner chose to pay for his ticket still so he could sit with the same fans he did during his childhood .
