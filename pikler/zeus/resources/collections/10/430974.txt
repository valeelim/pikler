Rodnei     rodnei francisco de lima ( born 11 september 1985 in são paulo ) , is a brazilian football player . his player name is rodnei . he is playing for red bull salzburg in the austrian bundesliga as a defender . rodnei started his career in brazil for c.a . juventus . he then moved to lithuania . he played for fc vilnius . in 2007 he moved to pão de açúcar ec from rio de janeiro but was loaned to jagiellonia białystok . his next teams were hertha bsc and 1. fc kaiserslautern in the german bundesliga . in 2012 he went on to fc red bull salzburg . he played 18 matches . in the 2013/14 season he only played 3 matches because he is injured .
