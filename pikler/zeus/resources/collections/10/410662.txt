Casey Fossum     casey paul fossum ( born january 6 , 1978 ) is an american former professional baseball player . he played for the boston red sox ( 2001–2003 ) , arizona diamondbacks ( 2004 ) , tampa bay devil rays ( 2005–2007 ) , detroit tigers ( 2008 ) , and new york mets ( 2009 ) of major league baseball . he batted and threw left-handed .
