1919 Florida Keys hurricane     the florida keys hurricane of 1919 was a strong hurricane in september 1919. it killed more than 770 people . it moved through the florida keys and southern texas . it was the first such storm to cause a lot of damage in corpus christi , texas . this storm did $ 22 million in damage . in texas alone , the official number of deaths was 286 , but it may have been closer to 600. the winds were around category 3 level between brownsville and corpus christi . they were at category 4 levels over the florida keys .
