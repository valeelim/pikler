Ray Frederick     ray frederick ( july 31 , 1929 - august 23 , 2001 ) was a canadian professional ice hockey goaltender . frederick played a total of 5 games in the nhl with the chicago black hawks during the 1954-55 nhl season . he started his ice hockey career with the fort frances canadians of the northern hockey league . he then played 2 seasons in the manitoba junior hockey league where he played for the brandon wheat kings . after his time with the wheat kings was done , he moved on to play in the western canada senior hockey league with the edmonton flyers for 2 seasons . he then played 1 season with the chicoutimi sagueneens of the quebec senior hockey league , 1 season with the charlottetown islanders of the maritime major hockey league and 3 seasons with the ottawa senators of the quebec senior hockey league . he played a seasons in the american hockey league with the buffalo bisons and 5 games with the chicago blackhawks of the national hockey league . he retired from professional ice hockey in 1958 .
