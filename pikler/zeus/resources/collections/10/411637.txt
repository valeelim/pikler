The Other Woman (2014 movie)    the other woman ( 2014 movie ) the other woman is a 2014 american comedy movie . cameron diaz , leslie mann and kate upton star in the movie . it is the first film nicki minaj has acted in . it was released in north america on april 25 , 2014 by 20th century fox . it is about a woman ( played by diaz ) who finds out that her boyfriend is married to another woman ( played by mann ) . both women later find out that he is also having an affair with a younger woman ( played by upton ) .
