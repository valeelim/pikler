Soliloquy     a soliloquy is a special kind of speech in a play . in a soliloquy , the character speaks to himself or herself . other characters of the play are not aware of what is said . that way , the character can share certain thoughts or feelings with the audience . there are many famous soliloquies in shakespeare 's plays such as the `` to be or not to be '' soliloquy in `` hamlet '' , the `` is this a dagger '' and the `` tomorrow , and tomorrow , and tomorrow '' soliloquies in `` macbeth '' , and the `` bottom 's dream '' soliloquy in `` a midsummer night 's dream '' . plays changed towards realism in the 18th century . soliloquies became less common in plays .
