Herzliya Hebrew Gymnasium     the herzliya hebrew gymnasium ( , ' , also known as `` gymnasia herzliya '' ) , originally known as ( lit . hebrew high school ) is the first hebrew high school in the world . it opened in 1905 in jaffa . it moved between several buildings in jaffa until 1909 , when it moved to tel aviv . in 1955 , the building was demolished and moved to a new site to build the shalom meir tower . other websites . - school website ( in hebrew )
