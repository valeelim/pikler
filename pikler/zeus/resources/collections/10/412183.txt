Yakovlev Yak-3     the yakovlev yak-3 is a soviet single-engine fighter aircraft . it was one of the main military aircraft used in ussr for world war ii . the first flight was in 1941. it started being used in 1943. some were used by french pilots of the `` groupe normandie-niemen '' . after the war , some were used in poland and yugoslavia . more than 4,800 were built .
