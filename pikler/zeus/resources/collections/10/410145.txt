Álvaro Valera Muñoz-Vargas    álvaro valera muñoz-vargas alvaro valera muñoz-vargas ( born october 16 , 1982 in seville ) is a table tennis athlete from spain . he has a physical disability : he is class 3 player and competes while using a wheelchair . in 2012 , he lived in madrid . he played table tennis at the 2004 summer paralympics , 2008 summer paralympics , 2012 summer paralympics and the 2012 summer paralympics . in 2008 , he finished third in the class 3 singles table tennis game . in 2008 , he finished third in the class 7 men 's singles . in 2012 , he finished second in the class 6 men 's singles and in the team class 6-8 game .
