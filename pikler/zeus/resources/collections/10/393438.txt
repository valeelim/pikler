Kermit Gosnell     kermit gosnell ( 1941- ) is a physician with a clinic in philadelphia , pennsylvania . he has been charged with murdering babies born alive . a patient also died in his care . he is facing 8 charges of murder and a possible death penalty . state officials , despite awareness of bad things going on , did nothing to stop him from 1993 until the fbi raided his clinic in 2010 on an unrelated matter .
