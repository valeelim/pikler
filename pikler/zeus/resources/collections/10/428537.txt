Scarling.    scarling . scarling . is an american noise pop band . the members are jessicka and her husband christian hejnal-addams . they have released two albums . jessicka fodera started scarling . in los angeles in 1999 after her band jack off jill broke up . their first single , `` band aid covers the bullethole '' was released in 2003. the b-side was a cover of `` creep '' by radiohead . they released their first album `` sweet heart dealer '' on february 17 , 2004. it was produced by chris vrenna . in the summer of 2004 scarling . performed at curiosa , a tour organized by robert smith . they released their second album `` so long , scarecrow '' on october 25 , 2005. scarling . produced the album with rob campanella . scarling . did their first uk tour in december 2005. in 2006 they released an ep , `` staring to the sun '' . it has a cover of `` wave of mutilation '' by pixies on it . on october 29 , 2013 they released a single , `` who wants to die for art '' ? their next album will be released in 2014 by the committee to keep music evil . other websites .
