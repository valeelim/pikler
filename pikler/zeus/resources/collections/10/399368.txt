King of the Jungle     king of the jungle is a 1933 paramount pictures movie . it was directed by h. bruce humberstone and max marcin . it stars buster crabbe and frances dee . kaspa , a man raised in the african jungles by lions is captured by circus people and taken to new york city with his lion friends . the man escapes as the ship nears new york . dressed in nothing but a loin cloth , he encounters a pretty girl on the streets who teaches him english . they fall in love . when a fire breaks out in the brooklyn zoo , panic stricken animals begin running wild in the streets . kaspa saves the day .
