Corpse Bride     corpse bride , also known as tim burton 's corpse bride , is a 2005 stop-motion-animated fantasy movie directed by tim burton and mike johnson . it stars johnny depp , helena bonham carter , emily watson , albert finney , michael gough , richard e. grant , deep roy , paul whitehouse , and christopher lee . it was released on september 23 , 2005. roger ebert gave the movie three out of four stars . it is now aired on spacetoon plus .
