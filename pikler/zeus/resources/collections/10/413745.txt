Marmaduke (movie)    marmaduke ( movie ) marmaduke is a 2010 live action movie . it is adapted from brad anderson 's comic strip of the same name . the movie is about a rural kansas family and their pets , a great dane named marmaduke and a balinese cat named carlos . the family moves to california because of business . the movie was released on june 4 , 2010. it had negative reviews .
