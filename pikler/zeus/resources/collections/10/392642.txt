Yuki people     the yuki are a native american group of california . their traditional territory is around round valley , in what is now mendocino county . the yuki call themselves the ukomno 'm , meaning `` valley people . '' european americans learned and adopted the name `` yuki '' from the their neighbours , the nomlaki , who called them `` enemy '' in the wintu language . `` yuki '' is an exonym , a name by another group .
