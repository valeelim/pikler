Asa Butterfield     asa bopp farr butterfield ( born 1 april 1997 ) is an english actor . he starred in the movie `` the boy in the striped pyjamas '' . he played young mordred in the bbc tv series `` merlin '' . he played norman in the 2010 movie `` nanny mcphee and the big bang '' . he played the title role in martin scorsese 's 2011 movie `` hugo '' . he played ender wiggin in the movie `` ender 's game '' based on the science fiction novel of the same title by orson scott card .
