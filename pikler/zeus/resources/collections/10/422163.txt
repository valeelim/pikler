Clay Aiken     clay aiken ( born november 30 , 1978 ) is an american singer-songwriter , author and activist . aiken was born and raised in raleigh , north carolina . he was a contestant on the second season of `` american idol '' in 2003. he is openly gay . in 2014 , aiken announced his intentions to run for congress in north carolina 's 2nd congressional district against republican incumbent renee ellmers . he officially filed to run on february 27 , 2014. studio albums . - `` measure of a man '' ( 2003 ) - `` merry christmas with love '' ( 2004 ) - `` a thousand different ways '' ( 2006 ) - `` on my way here `` ( 2008 ) - `` tried and true '' ( 2010 ) - `` steadfast '' ( 2012 )
