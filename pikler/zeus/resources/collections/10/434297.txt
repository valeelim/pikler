Pete Burns     pete burns ( 5 august 1959 - 23 october 2016 ) was an english singer . he started the band dead or alive in 1980. their 1985 single `` you spin me round ( like a record ) '' was number one . he has since become known for having had a lot of cosmetic surgery . he also became known for being on celebrity big brother in 2006. burns was born in wirral to a german mother , eva . he started school when he was seven . in 1979 burns was the singer of nightmares on wax . in 1980 he started dead or alive . in april 1984 dead or alive released their first album `` sophisticated boom boom '' . dead or alive 's second album `` youthquake '' was released in may 1985. it reached number nine in the uk albums chart . in 2004 burns released a single with the pet shop boys . in 2006 `` you spin me round ( like a record ) '' was released as a single for the fourth time . it reached number five in the uk singles chart . in 2011 burns was on `` celebrity come dine with me '' . burns used to have `` really bad depression '' . in 2006 he was arrested for attacking his fiancee . in 2007 burns married michael simpson .
