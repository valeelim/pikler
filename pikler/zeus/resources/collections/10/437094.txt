Carlos Borges     carlos ariel borges ( 14 january 1932 – 5 february 2014 ) was an uruguayan footballer . borges is best known for scoring the first goal ever in a copa libertadores . he earned 35 caps and scored 10 goals for the uruguay national football team from 1954 to 1959. he played in the 1954 fifa world cup when uruguay finished fourth , as he scored four goals including a hat-trick against scotland . during his club career he played for c.a . peñarol . he died on february 5 , 2014 , aged 82 .
