Tony! Toni! Toné!    tony ! toni ! toné ! tony ! toni ! toné ! are an american rhythm and blues club who originated out of oakland , california . they were extremely popular in the late 1980s-early 1990s . their debut record `` who ? '' was released in 1988. their hit singles included `` little walter '' and `` let 's get down '' . they disbanded around 1997 , although they returned six years later .
