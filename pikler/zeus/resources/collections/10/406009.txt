Outerbridge Crossing     the outerbridge crossing is a cantilever bridge that connects perth amboy , new jersey and staten island , new york . it was designed by john alexander low waddell and built by the port of new york authority ( now the port authority of new york and new jersey ) . it opened on june 29 , 1928 , the same day that the goethals bridge to elizabeth , new jersey opened . it was named after eugenius harvey outerbridge , the first chairman of the port of new york authority . the bridge of one of three bridges for cars that connect staten island with new jersey . the other bridges are the goethals bridge and the bayonne bridge to bayonne , new jersey .
