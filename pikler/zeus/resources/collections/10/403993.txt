Blohm &amp; Voss Bv 142    blohm & amp ; voss bv 142 the blohm & voss bv 142 was a german reconnaissance aircraft of world war ii . its maiden flight was in october 1938. it was built at the beginning like an airliner . but just one was used by lufthansa . the others were used by luftwaffe for far reconnaissance . fragile and unwieldy it was stopped in 1942 , just two years after it commissioned . only four examples were built .
