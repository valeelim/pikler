Catalan independence     the catalan independence or separatism is a political movement that supports the independence of catalonia or the catalan countries from spain and france . the catalan independence movement began in the 19th century . the independentists believe that catalonia is a nation . catalonia has its own language ( catalan language ) and culture ( catalan culture ) . during the francisco franco dictatorship , catalans and the catalan language were oppressed . recently , the number of people who support catalan independence has increased . in 2012 there was a demonstration in barcelona with 1.500.000 people . the majority of the parliament in catalunya wants to have a referendum on independence , and 55 % identify as separatists . the estelada flag is the symbol of the catalan independence movement .
