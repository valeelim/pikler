Great Central Fair     the great central fair was a fair that happened in june 1864. it took place in philadelphia , pennsylvania . it was held to raise funds for the united states sanitary commission . the great central fair took place from june 7 until june 28 , 1864. it happened in a park called logan circle in philadelphia . other sanitary fairs took place in chicago , cincinnati , and new york city . the people in philadelphia thought they should do one to raise money for the united states sanitary commission . two architects named samuel honeyman kneass and william stickland designed the buildings for the fair . the biggest building was 200,000 square feet in size . inside , visitors could visit different theme areas . the areas included information about new jersey , delaware , sewing , corn , weapons , art and children . there was also a restaurant . abraham lincoln visited the fair on june 16. he donated 48 copies of his emancipation proclamation . they were sold for $ 10 each . he autographed all of them . the festival raised over $ 1 million .
