Mr. Peabody &amp; Sherman    mr. peabody & amp ; sherman mr. peabody & sherman is a 2014 american-british 3d-computer animated comedy adventure movie directed by rob minkoff . it stars ty burrell , ariel winter , max charles , stephen colbert , and leslie mann . it has been released on february 7 , 2014 in the united kingdom and on march 7 , 2014 in the united states .
