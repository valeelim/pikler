Thoroughly Modern Millie     thoroughly modern millie is an american 1967 musical comedy movie . julie andrews stars as millie dillmount . mary tyler moore plays the role of miss dorothy brown . carol channing plays muzzy van hossmere . the story is about incidents in june 1922 and about young women who find themselves in the midst of adventures . this movie was released in march 1967. the reviews of the movie were very positive . the box office was also positive . this movie got nominations for many academy awards .
