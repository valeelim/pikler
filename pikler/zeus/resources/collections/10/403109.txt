Dani Harmer     dani harmer ( born 8 february 1989 ) is an english actress , best known for her portrayal as tracy beaker in programmes like : the story of tracy beaker and tracy beaker return she was an extra in `` harry potter and the philosopher 's stone '' . she is also a singer-songwriter . she released a single called `` free '' . harmer also appeared on appeared on `` strictly come dancing '' in 2012 .
