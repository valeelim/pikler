British Aircraft Corporation One-Eleven     the british aircraft corporation one-eleven or sometime british aircraft corporation 111 or also bac 111 was a british airliner . it was a twin-turbojet that could carry around one hundred passengers . some were built under licence in romania as rombac 111. this aircraft is similar to the french sud aviation caravelle and to the us douglas dc-9 . the one-eleven was retired from service in the 1990s because of noise restrictions . it was one of the more successful british designed airliners .
