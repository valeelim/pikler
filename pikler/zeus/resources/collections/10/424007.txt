A.J. Styles    a.j . styles allen neal jones ( born march 2 , 1977 ) better known by his ring name aj styles or the phenomenal one , is an american professional wrestler . he is currently wrestling for world wrestling entertainment ( wwe ) . he is best known for working for total nonstop action wrestling ( tna ) where he along with james storm and jeff jarrett were the only wrestlers to have worked continuously for the promotion since its first broadcast on june 19 , 2002. he was the first wrestler to complete the tna triple crown championship when he won the tna x division , nwa world tag team , and nwa world heavyweight championships . he is also the first wrestler to complete it more than once when he completed it five more times . on december 8 , 2013 , it was announced by tna that styles ' contract negotiations had failed . he was later released from the company and will be a free agent on december 17. on march 27 , 2014 , styles signed a contract with new japan pro wrestling . he debuted at invasion attack 2014 on april 6 where he attacked the iwgp heavyweight champion kazuchika okada and hit his finishing move , the `` styles clash '' on him . styles then said that he was the new leader of bullet club . this came after prince devitt , the former leader of bullet club , had to leave the company after losing a loser leaves town match against his rival ryusuke taguchi .
