Chrissy Amphlett     christine joy `` chrissy '' amphlett ( october 25 , 1959 - april 21 , 2013 ) was an australian singer-songwriter who was the lead singer of the rock band divinyls . she had many hits with the group and in solo . amphlett was born in geelong , victoria , australia . she died from breast cancer . she also had multiple sclerosis . she died in new york city at age 53 .
