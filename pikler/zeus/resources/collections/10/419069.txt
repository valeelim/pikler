Juliet (Shakespeare)    juliet ( shakespeare ) juliet capulet is a character in shakespeare 's `` romeo and juliet '' . she is barely a young adult when she meets and falls in love with a family enemy , romeo montague . the two marry secretly . when juliet 's parents arrange a marriage with count paris for her , she takes a potion that will make her appear to be dead . her body is taken to a crypt . she later comes back to life . when she discovers romeo has killed himself , she kills herself . there have been countless adaptations . norma shearer and olivia hussey have played juliet in the movies . the play was adapted to opera and ballet . rudolph nureyev and margot fonteyn have danced the `` star-crossed lovers '' in the ballet adaptation .
