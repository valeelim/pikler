Struga     struga is a small city in the southwest of the republic of macedonia at the lake ohrid . the black drin river runs through the town , dividing it into a western and an eastern part . about 15,000 people live in struga . they are a mix of albanians , ( slavic ) macedonians , turks and romani . struga is known for his tourism , mostly in the summer , when people come to take a bath at the shores of the lake ohrid and of the river black drin . struga is also the capital of the municipality with the same name including the town itself as well as nearby villages .
