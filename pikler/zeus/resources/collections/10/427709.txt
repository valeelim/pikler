Maria Bello     maria elena bello ( born april 18 , 1967 ) is an american actress and singer . she has acted in the movies `` permanent midnight '' ( 1998 ) , `` payback '' ( 1999 ) , `` coyote ugly '' ( 2000 ) , `` the cooler '' ( 2003 ) , `` a history of violence '' ( 2005 ) , `` thank you for smoking '' ( 2006 ) , and `` the jane austen book club '' ( 2007 ) . in television , she acted in the medical drama `` er '' from 1997 to 1998. she also acted in `` touch '' in 2013. she was born in pennsylvania . in november 2013 , bello wrote about having a same-sex relationship with her partner clare munn . other websites . - official website
