Ryukyuan languages     ryukyuan languages are a group of languages in the japonic languages , spoken in the ryūkyū islands , south of japan . those languages are amami , okinawan , miyako , yaeyama and yonaguni . in japan , some believe that the ryūkyūan languages are dialects of japanese , however this is not true . further reading . - sanseido ( 1997 ) . `` 言語学大辞典セレクション：日本列島の言語 '' ( `` selection from the encyclopædia of linguistics : the languages of the japanese archipelago '' ) . `` 琉球列島の言語 '' ( `` the languages of the ryukyu islands '' ) . - ashworth , d. e.
