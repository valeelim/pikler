Konzertstück in F minor     concert piece ( konzertstück ) in f minor for piano and orchestra , op . 79 , j . 282 is a piece of music for piano and orchestra . it was written by carl maria von weber , and first performed on june 25 , 1821 in berlin , germany . it is one unbroken piece of music with four sections in different tempos . weber wrote a program for the piece . it tells of a lady and her knight . they are separated by war , but are happily reunited by the end of the music . mendelssohn played the piece in a concert when he was 18 years old . liszt and stravinsky were both influenced by the piece when writing similar works of their own .
