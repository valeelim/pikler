Rovaniemi     rovaniemi is a town in lapland , in northern finland . rovaniemi is very close to the arctic circle . the population was over 61,000 in january 2014. the area is huge in typical european scale . neigbouring municipalities are kittilä , sodankylä , kolari , pello , ylitornio , tervola , ranua , posio , kemijärvi and pelkosenniemi . other websites . - community website
