Prince Edward Islands     the prince edward islands are two islands in the subantarctic indian ocean . they are part of south africa . the two islands are called marion island and prince edward island . the only people on the islands are meteorologists and biologists . the islands were discovered in 1663 by a dutch explorer . different people visited the islands over the years but no one settled there . the british claimed the islands in 1908 , then gave the islands to south africa in 1948 .
