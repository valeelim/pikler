UFC 152     ufc 152 : jones vs. belfort was a mixed martial arts pay-per-view event held by the ultimate fighting championship on september 22 , 2012 at the air canada centre in toronto , ontario , canada . rory macdonald was expected to face former two-time champion b.j . penn at the event , but macdonald pulled out of the bout after he cut his forehead while training . the bout was rescheduled for ufc on fox 5 on december 8 , 2012. ufc newcomer roger hollett was expected to face matt hamill , who returned from retirement , at the event . however , hollett was forced out of the bout due to a contract dispute and replaced by vladimir matyushenko . then , on september 11 , 2012 , matyushenko pulled out of the fight after suffering a torn achilles tendon while training , and hamill vs. hollett was rebooked by ufc officials . a bout between evan dunham and tj grant was first supposed to be contested on the main card but was instead fought on the fx prelims , for unknown reasons . as a result of the cancellation of ufc 151 , a bout between kyle noke and charlie brenneman was rescheduled for this event .
