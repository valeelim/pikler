Marimekko     marimekko is a textile design company . it was started in 1951 in finland . it is famous for its original prints , for instance , prints of red and blue flowers . the company makes high-quality items , not only furnishing fabrics to tableware but also clothes , bags and other accessories . its fabrics have been introduced inside the airplanes of finnair . other websites . - marimekko | we as a company
