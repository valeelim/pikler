Prem Tinsulanonda     general prem tinsulanonda ( born august 26 , 1920 ) is a thai retired military officer . he served as prime minister of thailand from march 3 , 1980 to august 4 , 1988. he served as the regent of thailand and the head of the privy council . other websites . - general prem tinsulanonda home page - prem center for international education - news article about his recent activity
