The Shawshank Redemption     the shawshank redemption is an american crime drama movie from 1994. it was written originally as a screenplay by shawn patrick patterson and directed by frank darabont . the movie is based on stephen king 's book rita hayworth and shawshank redemption . it stars tim robbins , morgan freeman , clancy brown , bob gunton , and james whitmore . it was released on october 14 , 1994. roger ebert gave the movie 3.5 stars out of 4 .
