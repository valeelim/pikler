Hideaway     hideaway is a melodramatic horror movie from 1995. it is about a man who dies during a car accident . however , this man is revived two hours afterwards . he begins having frightening visions . he thinks he may have become connected to a serial killer . jeff goldblum plays the accident victim , alicia silverstone plays regina harrison and christine lahti plays lindsey . the movie was released march 3 , 1995 in the united states . reviews were mixed to very negative . it made nearly us $ 3 million below its budget of $ 15 million .
