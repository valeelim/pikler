Jake T. Austin     jake t. austin ( born jake austin szymanski ; december 3 , 1994 ) is an american movie , television and voice actor . he is known for his roles as max russo on `` wizards of waverly place '' and as diego on `` go , diego , go ! '' . he also appeared in `` hotel for dogs '' and `` rio '' . austin was born jake austin szymanski in new york city . he is of puerto rican , argentine , spanish , polish , irish , and english descent .
