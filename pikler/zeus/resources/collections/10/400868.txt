Blind Melon     blind melon are an american alternative rock band from los angeles , california . the band is best known for their 1993 song `` no rain '' . blind melon was formed in march 1990 by band members rogers stevens , brad smith and shannon hoon . they released their eponymous debut studio album `` blind melon '' in september 22 , 1992. their second album `` soup '' was released on august 15 , 1995. on october 21 , 1995 , lead singer shannon hoon was found dead on the band 's tour bus of a heart attack caused by a cocaine overdose . the band looked for a new singer after hoon 's death but broke up on march 4 , 1999. the band reunited on september 15 , 2006 with new lead singer travis warren . they released their fourth studio album `` for my friends '' on april 22 , 2008. the band went on hiatus in 2009 and reunited again in 2010. band members . - current - rogers stevens – lead guitar , piano , keyboards ( 1990–1999 , 2006– '' present '' ) - brad smith – bass , flute , backing vocals ( 1990–1999 , 2006– '' present '' ) - christopher thorn – rhythm guitar , steel guitar , mandolin , harmonica ( 1990–1999 , 2006– '' present '' )
