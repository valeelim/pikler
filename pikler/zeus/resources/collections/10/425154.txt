Carbine     a carbine is a lighter , shorter type of rifle . the smaller size and lighter weight of carbines makes them easier to handle . they are often issued to high-mobility troops such as special-ops soldiers and paratroopers . also they can be used by mounted or other non-infantry personnel who do not need full-sized rifles . the u.s. m4 carbine is a classic example . continuous-fire versions may also be described as submachine guns
