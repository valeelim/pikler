Henry VI, Pt. 2    henry vi , pt . 2 henry vi , pt . 2 is a play by william shakespeare . it is a history play . shakespeare 's source was `` holinshed 's chronicles '' . the play was first performed between 1594 and 1595. it was probably first printed in 1597. it was printed in the first folio of 1623. references . - henry vi , pt . 2
