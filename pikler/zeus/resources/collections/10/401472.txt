Night of the Ghouls     night of the ghouls is a 1959 horror movie written , directed , and produced by ed wood . it is a sequel of sorts to wood 's 1955 horror movie , `` bride of the monster '' . the story is about a crook , dr. acula , who pretends to be able to contact the dead . he charges people large amounts of money to speak to their dead relatives . he summons a group of real ghosts who bury him alive . the movie stars kenne duncan as dr. karl acula , duke moore as lt. dan bradford , tor johnson as lobo , valda hansen as sheila , paul marco as patrolman kelton , and criswell as himself .
