Division of Wilmot     the division of wilmot was an australian electoral division in the state of tasmania . it was located in central tasmania . at various times it included the towns of deloraine , beaconsfield , devonport , latrobe and new norfolk . the division was named after sir john eardley-wilmot , the sixth lieutenant-governor of tasmania . it was set up for the 1903 election . the division was abolished in 1984 , and new boundaries were created . much of the area is now included in the division of lyons . the most famous member was joseph lyons , a premier of tasmania , who later became the 10th prime minister of australia . lyons died while still in office , and a by-election was held for the division in 1939 .
