Luis Antonio Arevalo Espadas     luis antonio arevalo espadas ( born september 10 , 1982 in miguelturra ) is a swimmer from spain . he is also a student . he has a disability : he is blind and is an s13/sb13/sm13/b3 type swimmer . he raced at the 2000 summer paralympics . he came in third in 4 x 100 meter relay 49 points swimming race . he raced at the 2004 summer paralympics . he raced at the 2008 summer paralympics .
