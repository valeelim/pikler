Miklós Jancsó     miklós jancsó ( 27 september 192131 january 2014 ) was a hungarian movie director and screenwriter . jancsó became famous during the mid-1960s . his famous works were `` the round up '' ( `` szegénylegények '' , 1965 ) , `` the red and the white '' ( `` csillagosok , katonák '' , 1967 ) and `` red psalm '' ( `` még kér a nép '' , 1971 ) . jancsó died on 31 january 2014 , aged 92. other websites . - jancsó 's biography at hungary.hu - -
