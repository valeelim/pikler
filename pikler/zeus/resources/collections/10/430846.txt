Anal cancer     anal cancer is a type of cancer in the anus . it is different from the much more common colorectal cancer . the etiology , risk factors , clinical progression , staging , and treatment are all different . anal cancer is mostly a squamous cell carcinoma that rises near the squamocolumnar area . other types of anal carcinoma are adenocarcinoma , lymphoma , sarcoma or melanoma . in the united states , it is estimated that in 2014 about 7,060 new cases of anal cancer would be diagnosed in the united states ( 4500 in women and 2,500 in men ) . it is typically found in adults , average age early 60s .
