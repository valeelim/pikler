Shelly-Ann Fraser-Pryce     shelly-ann fraser-pryce , od ( born december 27 , 1986 ) is a jamaican sprinter . she was unknown until 2008 when she became the first caribbean woman to win 100 m gold at the olympics at the 2008 olympic games in bejiing . at the 2012 london olympic games she won 100 m gold . she also won silver in 200 metres and 4 x 100 metres relay . she is five feet tall . she is the fourth fastest 100 m female sprinter of all time . she is a christian .
