Kylie (album)    kylie ( album ) kylie is the first album by australian singer kylie minogue , released on 4 july 1988. the album was produced by stock , aitken and waterman , who also wrote nine of the ten songs . mostly a dance-pop album , it also contains many bubblegum pop elements . all songs written and produced by mike stock , matt aitken and pete waterman , except where noted . other websites . - kylie minogue official website
