Baku Stock Exchange     the baku stock exchange , bse ( ) is the main stock exchange of azerbaijan which is situated in the capital baku . the baku stock exchange was established in 2000 and it began its operations the same year on february 15 with the first transaction . the stock exchange is owned by its own members , who are shareholders . the current head of the stock exchange is emin aliyev , who is the chairperson of the board of directors . related pages . - economy of azerbaijan - azerbaijani manat - central bank of azerbaijan - agriculture in azerbaijan
