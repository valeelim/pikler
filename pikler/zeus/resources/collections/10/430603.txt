Dr. Luigi     dr. luigi is an upcoming puzzle video game for the wii u console . it was developed by nintendo and arika . it was released in north america on december 31 , 2013 , and will be released in europe on january 15 , 2014. the gameplay of `` dr. luigi '' is to make sure vitamins of the same color are lined up together . there are three colors of vitamins : red , blue , and yellow . the player has to clear the field of viruses by dropping capsules with two colors on them . the player moves the capsules as they fall by moving them left or right and rotating them . the player gets points by killing viruses . the game ends if the bottle fills up with capsules . there are four different modes . `` retro remedy '' is played like other `` dr. mario '' games . in `` operation l '' , capsules that are shaped like an l are dropped into the playing field . in `` virus buster '' , players hold the wii u gamepad vertically , and use the controller 's touch screen to drop the capsules onto the playing field . in `` online battle '' , players can play against other people through online multiplayer .
