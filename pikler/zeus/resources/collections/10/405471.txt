HV71     hv71 is an ice hockey club that plays its home games in the kinnarps arena in the town of jönköping in sweden . the club was established on 24 may 1971 when the two local clubs of husqvarna if and vätterstads ik merged their ice hockey sections . hv71 won the swedish national championship in 1995 , 2004 , 2008 , 2010 and 2017. they also lost the finals of 2009. other websites . - official website
