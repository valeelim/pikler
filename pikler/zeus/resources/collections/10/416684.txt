Jeux d'enfants     jeux d'enfants ( ) is a set of 12 piano duets by georges bizet . the set was published in 1871. the pieces describe children 's toys and games . the set was later orchestrated . george balanchine made two ballets based on the music . he used the whole set for a ballet in 1955. he used a few pieces for `` the steadfast tin soldier '' in 1975 .
