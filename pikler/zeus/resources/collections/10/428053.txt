4.3.2.1     4.3.2.1 is a british 2010 crime-thriller movie . the movie is about four friends , all 19 , during the late 2000s decade . one of them ( shannon ) has one bad day after another . she gets mugged , assaulted and rejected . another , joanne ( or simply jo , played by emma roberts ) , works inside a 24-hour retail store . the reviews mixed . it was released in june 2010 .
