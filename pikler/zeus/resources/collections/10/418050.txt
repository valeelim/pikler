Scènes de la vie de bohème     scènes de la vie de bohème `` ( scenes from the bohemian life ) '' is a book by henri murger . it was published in parts between 1847 and 1849 , and in book form in 1851. the book is about the lives of the poor poets , artists , and musicians in paris about 1830. it was the basis for `` la bohème '' , an opera by giacomo puccini in 1896 , and another opera of the same name by ruggero leoncavallo in 1897 .
