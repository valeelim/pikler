The Smurfs (movie)    the smurfs ( movie ) the smurfs is a 2011 animated comedy movie that is based on the comics `` the smurfs '' by peyo . it stars neil patrick harris , katy perry , and jonathan winters . the movie was released on july 29 , 2011 to mixed reviews . a sequel entitled `` the smurfs 2 '' was released on july 31 , 2013 .
