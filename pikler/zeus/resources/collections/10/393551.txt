Angélica Rivera     angélica rivera hurtado ( born august 2 , 1969 ) is a mexican telenovela actress and the first lady of mexico . she is the wife of the president of mexico , enrique peña nieto . she is known for her roles on `` la dueña '' ( mexico ) ( 1995 ) , `` ángela '' ( 1998 ) , `` sin pecado concebido '' ( 2001 ) , `` mariana de la noche '' ( 2003 ) , and in `` destilando amor '' ( 2007 ) . rivera was born on august 2 , 1969 in mexico city , mexico . she was married to josé alberto castro from 1994 until they divorced in 2008. then she was married to enrique peña nieto since 2010. she has three children . other websites . - angélica rivera on the internet movie database - biography and galleries - esmas biography and career source - enelbrasero ( fotos de boda ) wedding images
