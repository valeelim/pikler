Jesús González Margaride     jesus gonzalez margaride ( born september 28 , 1967 in a pontenova ) is a track and field athlete from spain . he has a physical disability : he is a t36 type athlete . he raced at the 1996 summer paralympics . he race and finished in 7 in the 5,000 meter t34-t37 race . he raced at the 2000 summer paralympics in sydney , australia . he finished third in the 1,500 meter t36 race .
