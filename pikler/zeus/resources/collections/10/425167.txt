Ingrid Newkirk     ingrid newkirk ( born june 11 , 1949 ) is an animal rights activist . she started people for the ethical treatment of animals ( peta ) in 1980 with alex pacheco . it is the world 's biggest animal rights organization . in 2007 hbo showed a documentary called `` i am an animal : the story of ingrid newkirk and peta '' . in 2010 jezebel called newkirk 'the worst person in the world ' . other websites . - official website
