Adam Munro     adam munro ( born november 12 , 1982 ) is a canadian ice hockey goaltender that currently plays for hc twk innsbruck of the ebel . before playing in the nhl , munro played 4 years with the erie otters and 1 season with the sault ste . marie greyhounds of the ohl . he was drafted with the 29th overall pick in the 2001 nhl entry draft by the chicago blackhawks . he played 17 games with the blackhawks and spent most of his time with their-then ahl affiliate team , norfolk admirals . in 2006 , munro was signed as a free agent by hc fribourg-gottéron of the national league a. he played a season with them and returned to north america to play for the columbus blue jackets ' ahl affiliate , syracuse crunch . he played 25 games for them and then played for the toronto maple leafs ' ahl affiliate , toronto marlies . after playing 40 games with the marlies between two seasons , munro left north america again to play for hc sibir novosibirsk of the khl . munro would play 4 games in the khl and 1 season in the elite.a with sg cortina before heading to the ebel . in the ebel , he has also played 2 seasons with alba volán székesfehérvár .
