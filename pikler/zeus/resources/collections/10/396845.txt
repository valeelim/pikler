Abu Dhabi (emirate)    abu dhabi ( emirate ) abu dhabi ( , `` ʼabū ẓaby '' ) is one of the emirates that make up the united arab emirates . it is the largest of the seven . the capital city of uae , abu dhabi , is in this emirate . al ain is abu dhabi 's second largest urban area with a population of 348,000 ( as of the 2003 census estimate ) .
