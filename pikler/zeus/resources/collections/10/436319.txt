Crocin     crocin is a chemical compound that produces a type of natural pigment . it is found in the flowers of crocus and gardenia plants . it has a deep red colour . crocin is what is mainly responsible for the colour of saffron . it is also called saffronin or polychroite . crocin was used as a dye for textiles . because it is unstable it is no longer used for that purpose..
