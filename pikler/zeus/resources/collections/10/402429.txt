Tasman Island     tasman island is a small island in south-eastern australia . it is part of tasmania . it has an area of , and consists of a plateau surrounded by steep cliffs . the plateau is about above sea level , the highest point on the island is about above sea level . in 1905 , a lighthouse was built on the island ; there is also a weather station . since 1977 , the lighthouse is automatic , and no longer requires people to be on the island . the island is named after abel tasman . it is part of tasman national park today .
