Millie Jackson     mildred `` millie '' jackson ( born july 15 , 1944 ) is an american r & b singer and songwriter . she released her first album in 1972. in 1974 jackson was nominated for a grammy award . in 2000 she sung on some songs for the soundtrack of a french movie called `` comme un aimant '' . in 2008 she played claudette in a movie called `` soul men '' . jackson has a daughter called keisha jackson . she is also a singer .
