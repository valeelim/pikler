Juan Antonio Saavedra Reinaldo     juan antonio saavedra reinaldo ( born november 21 , 1973 in pontevedra ) is a shooter from spain . he has a disability and is an sh1-sh2 type shooter . he competed at the 2000 summer paralympics , 2004 summer paralympics and 2012 summer paralympics . in 2012 , he finished second in the free rifle lying r6 , mixed gender sh1 event .
