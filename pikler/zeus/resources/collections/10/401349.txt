Fabián Alarcón     fabián ernesto alarcón rivera ( born april 14 , 1947 ) was president of ecuador from 6 february 1997 to 9 february 1997 and from february 11 , 1997 to august 10 , 1998. alarcón was born in quito . he was head of the congress from 1991 to 1992 and then again from august 1995 to february 1997 , when he was made acting president due to the impeachment of president abdalá bucaram . other websites . - official website of the ecuadorian government about the country president 's history
