SoulDecision     souldecision was a canadian pop band from vancouver , british columbia . souldecision was formed in 1993 under the original name , indecision . they changed their name to souldecision because of naming conflicts . even though the band started during the boy band era , they were not a boy band , as they played their own instruments and wrote their own music . the band released their debut full-length album `` no one does it better '' on august 22 , 2000 in canada . the album featured their hit single `` faded '' which reached # 1 on canada 's singles chart as well as # 22 in the united states . the album also featured the single `` ooh it 's kinda crazy '' which reached # 1 in canada and was the number one request on mtv 's total request live at the time . the band toured with destiny 's child , christina aguilera and 'n sync . lead singer trevor guthrie left the band in 2005 to write and record his first solo record . the group split up after . discography . albums . - `` no one does it better '' ( 2000 ) - `` shady satin drug '' ( 2004 )
