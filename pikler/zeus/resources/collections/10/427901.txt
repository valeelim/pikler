Uninterruptible power supply     an uninterruptible power supply ( or uninterruptible power source ; ups ) is an apparatus that provides emergency power supply to a load when there is a problem with the normal electricity supply . it provides an almost instantaneous supply of electricity during any power failure . it is used normally to protect any sensitive hardware ( computer , data center , telecommunication ) in which if there is any disruption of power supply , it might easily damage that equipment .
