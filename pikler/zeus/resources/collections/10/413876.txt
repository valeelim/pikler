Vicente Ramón Roca     vicente ramón roca rodríguez ( 2 september 1792 – 23 february 1858 ) was president of ecuador from 8 december 1845 to 15 october 1849. he was a member of the liberal party . he led the revolution that overthrew juan josé flores , along with josé joaquín de olmedo and diego noboa . he ruled under the constitution of 1845. roca was born on 2 september 1792 in guayaquil , ecuador . he was married to juana andrade . roca died on 23 february 1858 from complications of pneumonia , aged 65. other websites . - official website of the ecuadorian government about the country president 's history
