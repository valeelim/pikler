Heinola     heinola is a town in finland . about 19,997 people lived there as of 31 may 2013. nearby municipalities include asikkala , hartola , iitti , kouvola , mäntyharju , nastola , pertunmaa and sysmä . the newspaper `` itä-häme '' is published in heinola and neighbouring towns . heinola is founded 1776. tuusjärvi , hirvisalo , hujansalo , härkälä , imjärvi , komeankylä , korkee , lauhjoki , lusi , läpiä , kausa , marjoniemi , myllykylä , onali , paaso , paistjärvi , pääsinniemi , rihu , taipale , vaippilainen ( vaippila ) , vierumäki and jyränkö . other websites . - community website
