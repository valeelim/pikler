Selçuk Uluergüven     selçuk uluergüven ( 1 january 1941 – 8 january 2014 ) was a turkish actor . he appeared in over 80 movies and many television and stage roles . uluergüven began his theatre career in 1962 , at meydan stage in ankara . uluergüven died on 8 january 2014 in aydın , turkey . he was 73. other websites . - selçuk uluergüven at sinematürk website
