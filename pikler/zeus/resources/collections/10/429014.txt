Claire Martin     claire martin ( april 18 , 1914 – june 18 , 2014 ) was a canadian writer . `` claire martin '' was her pseudonym . her real name was claire montreuil . martin was born in quebec city , quebec . she studied at the ursuline convent and by the dames de la congrégation . from 1945 to 1972 , she lived in ottawa and was a writer in residence at the university of ottawa . from 1972 to 1982 , she lived in france , before returning to quebec . martin died in quebec city , aged 100. other websites . - claire martin at the canadian encyclopedia
