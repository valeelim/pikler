Peter O'Sullevan     sir peter john o'sullevan ( 3 march 1918 – 29 july 2015 ) was an irish retired horse racing commentator . he worked for the bbc , the press association , `` daily express '' and `` today '' . he was the bbc 's leading horse-racing commentator from 1947 to 1997. o'sullevan was born in kenmare , ireland . he studied at hawtreys , charterhouse school , and later at collège alpin international beau soleil in switzerland . o'sullevan died of cancer in london , aged 97. other websites . - 2002 bbc sports article on royal ascot - 2005 interview in the independent ( london )
