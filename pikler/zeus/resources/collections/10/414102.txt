Whina Cooper     dame whina cooper ( 9 december 1895 , te karaka , hokianga , new zealand – march 1994 , panguru , new zealand ) fought for maori land . she was elected first president of the new maori womens welfare league . cooper was appointed a member of the order of the british empire for the services to the māori people in the 1953 coronation honours . in 1974 , she was promoted to commander . in 1981 , she was promoted again to dame commander .
