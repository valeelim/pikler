Pistacia lentiscus     pistacia lentiscus ( also mastic ; `` '' ) is a shrub or small tree of the pistacio genus . they grow in the mediterranean sea region , mainly on the greek island of chios in the aegean sea . the resin of the tree is called : `` mastic '' . it was famous in the southern region of the island of chios , called mastichochoria . the tree is used for food , medicine and industrial purposes . other websites . - chios mastic growers association ( in greek )
