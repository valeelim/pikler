Tor Johnson     tor johnson ( born tor johansson , 19 october 1903 â€“ 12 may 1971 ) was a swedish professional wrestler and movie and television actor . during his wrestling career , he was billed as the super-swedish angel . johnson was born in sweden , the son of karl j. johansson and lovisa petersson . he began appearing in movies in 1934 , usually as boxers or wrestlers . in the 1940s , he played lumbering thugs and scowling plug-uglies . he appeared with bob hope and bing crosby in `` road to rio '' ( 1947 ) , in `` abbott and costello in the foreign legion '' ( 1950 ) , and with shirley jones in the movie version of `` carousel '' ( 1956 ) . he is best known though for his appearances in the low budget movies directed by ed wood . he played lobo in `` bride of the monster '' and `` night of the ghouls '' , and the zombified inspector clay in `` plan 9 from outer space '' . in the early 1960s , he had guest appearances as wrestlers , thugs , and circus strongmen in various television series including `` peter gunn '' , `` bonanza '' , and `` the shirley temple show '' . johnson died in san fernando , california , from heart failure in 1971. he was buried at the eternal valley memorial park in newhall , california .
