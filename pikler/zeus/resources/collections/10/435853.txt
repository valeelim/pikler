Perelman School of Medicine at the University of Pennsylvania     the perelman school of medicine ( penn med ) , is the medical school of the university of pennsylvania . it is an ivy league school located in philadelphia , pennsylvania . opened in 1765 , penn med is the first and oldest medical school in the united states . today , the perelman school of medicine is a chief place of biological and medical research and education . it is one of the country 's top medical schools . the school ranks among the highest recipients of the national institutes of health research awards . john morgan earned a bachelor of arts degree from the college of philadelphia ( now called the university of pennsylvania . he then earned doctor of medicine ( md ) from the university of edinburgh medical school in scotland . in 1765 , after five years training in edinburgh and touring europe , dr. morgan returned to philadelphia . there he opened the united states ' first medical school at the university of pennsylvania . he planned for the school to be one of the best medical schools in the country and the world . dr. morgan copied many teaching methods from his medical school .
