Pablo Saavedra Reinado     pablo saavedra reinado ( born january 29 , 1975 in pontevedra ) is a swimmer from spain . he has a disability and is an s9 type swimmer . he raced at the 1996 summer paralympics . he finished third in 4 x 100 meter 34 point freestyle relay race . he raced at the 2000 summer paralympics .
