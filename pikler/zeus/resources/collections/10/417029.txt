Rodeo (ballet)    rodeo ( ballet ) rodeo is a ballet . the story and dances were designed by agnes de mille . the music was written by aaron copland . the ballet was first performed on october 16 , 1942 at the metropolitan opera house in new york city . it was a great success . the ballet consists of five sections : `` buckaroo holiday '' , `` ranch house party '' , `` corral nocturne '' , `` saturday night waltz '' , and `` hoe-down '' . the ballet tells the story of an awkward cowgirl 's romance .
