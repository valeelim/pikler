My Cousin Vinny     my cousin vinny is a 1992 american criminal comedy movie . joe pesci and marisa tomei star . the subject of the movie is two young new yorkers traveling across rural alabama . they are put on trial for a murder they never committed . tomei plays mona lisa . pesci plays vincent ( `` vinny '' for short ) . this comedy movie was released in march 1992 in the united states . it received positive reviews from the critics .
