Nok culture     the nok culture was an ancient civilisation in what is now nigeria . it appeared around 1000 bc and vanished around 300 ce . this civilisation lived in northern and central nigeria . its social system is thought to have been very advanced for its time . the nok are considered to be the earliest producers of life-sized terracotta sculptures in sub-saharan africa . it has been suggested that the nok culture eventually evolved into the yoruba . the use of iron , in smelting and forging tools , appears in the nok culture at least by 550 bc and possibly earlier .
