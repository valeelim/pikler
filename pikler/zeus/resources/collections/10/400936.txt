Jim Thornton     jim thornton ( born april 21 , 1965 ) is an american radio and television personality . he is currently an afternoon anchor on all-news radio station knx 1070 in los angeles , california . he is also the announcer on television game show `` wheel of fortune '' . he was born in huntington , west virginia , united states . thornton became announcer of `` wheel of fortune '' in september 2011. he auditioned for the role in december 2010 after previous announcer charlie o'donnell 's death . he was confirmed as the show 's new announcer on june 13 , 2011. he was also a guest announcer on `` the price is right '' after previous announcer rod roddy 's death in 2003 .
