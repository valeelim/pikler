Melissa Perrine     henrieta farkašová ( born 21 february 1988 ) is a skier from australia . she has a disability : she can not see everything . she skied at the 2011 ipc alpine skiing world championships . she was the second skier to finish the women 's visually impaired downhill race , and the third skier to finish in the super-g race and the super combined race . because she can not see , andrew bor was her guide and told her where to go when going down the mountain .
