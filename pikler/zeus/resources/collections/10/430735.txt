Emma Darwin     emma darwin ( née wedgwood ; 2 may 1808–7 october 1896 ) was an english woman and the wife of charles darwin . charles darwin was a very important biologist . emma was the daughter of josiah wedgwood ii . josiah ii was the son of josiah wedgwood . josiah was a potter and founder of the wedgwood company . charles darwin was her cousin . he was also a grandchild of josiah . they married on january 29 , 1839. emma had 10 children with charles . 2 of these died when they were babies . another child , annie , died when she was 10. emma helped charles with his work , mostly by writing out his works onto manuscripts .
