Jamelia     jamelia niela davis ( born 11 january 1981 ) , best known as jamelia , is a british singer-songwriter . she has released three albums . jamelia was born to jamaican parents in birmingham . she was raised there on a council estate by her single mother . when she was 15 she signed with capitol records . in 1999 she released her first single , `` so high '' . it did n't sell many copies , so jamelia left capitol records and signed to parlophone . they released her first album `` drama '' in 2000. she released her second album `` thank you '' on september 29 , 2003. seven singles were released from the album . the second single `` superstar '' reached number three in the uk singles chart . it reached number one in australia and the netherlands . the third single `` thank you '' reached number two in the uk . it was nominated for best british single at the 2005 brit awards . the fourth single `` see it in a boy 's eyes '' was written by jamelia with chris martin . it reached number five in the uk singles chart . the album is jamelia 's best-selling in the uk .
