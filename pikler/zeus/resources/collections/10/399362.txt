Tarzan Escapes     tarzan escapes is a 1936 jungle adventure tarzan movie . it is based on the fictional characters and concepts created by edgar rice burroughs . it is the third of the six mgm `` tarzan '' movies starring johnny weissmuller and maureen o'sullivan . captain fry ( buckler ) , a hunter , captures tarzan to put him on public display . fry makes a deal with an unfriendly native tribe to give him food , canoes , and protection for the journey back to civilization . fry 's plan goes wrong when the natives capture tarzan in his cage . tarzan escapes with the help of elephants and cheeta . fry falls into a quicksand bog and dies . tarzan and jane are reunited at their treehouse . related pages . - tarzan and his mate , prequel to this movie - tarzan finds a son ! , sequel to this movie
