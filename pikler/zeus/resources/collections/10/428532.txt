Qunu     qunu [ ˈk͡ǃuːnu ] is a small rural village in south africa 's eastern cape province . it is south-west of mthatha on the road between butterworth and mthatha . nelson mandela spent his childhood here and had a retirement home there . on 15 december 2013 , mandela was buried in qunu after a state funeral . related pages . - more about the nelson mandela museum at qunu
