Lulu Roman     lulu roman ( or bertha louise hable ; born 6 may 1946 ) is an american comedian and singer . she acted in the television comedy `` hee haw '' . she sings mostly gospel music . in 1984 , roman received a dove award from the gospel music association for her album `` you were loving me '' . in 2003 she was the author of a book titled `` kitchen komforts '' . roman was born in dallas , texas .
