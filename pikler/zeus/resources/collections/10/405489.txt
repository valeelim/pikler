Modo Hockey     modo hockey is an ice hockey club in the town of örnsköldsvik in sweden . the club was established in 1921 as alfredshems ik before becoming modo aik in 1963 and modo hk ( or modo hockey ) in 1987. modo hockey won the swedish national championship in 1979 and 2007. they also played in the finals and lost in 1994 , 1999 and the year 2000. other websites . - official website
