Escape from L.A.     escape from l.a. is a 1996 american science fiction movie directed by john carpenter . it stars kurt russell , cliff robertson , steve buscemi , peter fonda , stacy keach , bruce campbell , and pam grier . robertson replaced donald pleasence as president due to his death in 1995. it was sequel for `` escape from new york '' . it was released on august 9 , 1996 .
