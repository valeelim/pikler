Pål Skjønberg     pål eugen bucher skjønberg ( 20 october 1919 – 3 february 2014 ) was a norwegian actor . he was born in stavanger . he was the son of actors eugen and henny skjønberg . he started acting in 1946. he retired in 1991. skjønberg died on 3 february 2014 , aged 94. he was outlived by his two children with actress , elisabeth bang .
