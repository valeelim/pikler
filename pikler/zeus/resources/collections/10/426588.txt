What's Eating Gilbert Grape    what 's eating gilbert grape the movie what 's eating gilbert grape is a 1993 romantic comedy-drama . this movie is about several men in iowa , one of whom is mentally retarded . the title character , gilbert ( johnny depp ) , cares for his brother arnie ( leonardo dicaprio ) because of his issue , and also for his mother , bonnie , who is very overweight ( darlene cates ) . juliette lewis plays becky . the release date of `` what 's eating gilbert grape '' was december 17 , 1993 , in limited release . it was released elsewhere in february-march 1994. the reviews were very positive . its box office , however , was low .
