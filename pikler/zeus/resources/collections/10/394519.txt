The Phoenix (song)    the phoenix ( song ) `` the phoenix '' is a promotional single by american pop rock band fall out boy . the single was released on march 24 , 2013 in the united states . it was accompanied by a music video , released on the same day . it peaked and debuted at number-eighty on the billboard hot 100 singles chart and at number-fourteen on the billboard rock songs chart . the music video 's plot follows the story line of fall out boy 's capture in the video for `` my songs know what you did in the dark ( light 'em up ) '' , which was released on february 4 , 2013. the song was succeeded by the second promotional single from `` save rock and roll '' titled `` young volcanoes '' , released on april 8 , 2013 on soundcloud . the music video for the song was released on april 18 on youtube . the song sampled a various amount of genres and sounds . the introduction to the track samples an orchestral like symphonic build up leading to the song 's high-nrg sound . overall , it is an alternative dance pop , punk rock song composed by producer butch walker .
