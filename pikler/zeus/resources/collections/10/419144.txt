Coming Soon (1999 movie)    coming soon ( 1999 movie ) coming soon is a 1999 romance-comedy movie . it is about three rich high school seniors . yasmine bleeth plays mimi , mia farrow is jody , ashton kutcher is louie and ellen pompeo is the upset girl . this movie was released on april 17 , 1999 in the united states . `` coming soon '' was controversial because officials of the mpaa marked it with an nc-17 . the movie , however , was trimmed and re-rated r .
