Greyfriars Kirkyard     greyfriars kirkyard is a cemetery in edinburgh , scotland . it surrounds greyfriars kirk in the old town . burials have been held since the late 16th century . the kirk and its monuments are protected as a category a-listed building . the kirkyard is said to be haunted . many notable scots are buried in the kirkyard including sir george mackenzie , lord advocate mary erskine , regent of scotland george buchanan , and john douglas , 4th earl of morton . the kirkyard is the resting place of greyfriars bobby , a skye terrier who stayed by his master 's grave in the kirkyard for 14 years .
