Texas City disaster     the texas city disaster of 1947 was a major and very deadly incident . it happened on wednesday , april 16 , 1947 , and was one of the largest non-nuclear explosions to hit the united states . the incident began as a late morning fire on the french vessel ss `` grandcamp '' which had been docked next to texas city and nearby galveston . but then , its almost twenty three hundred ton cargo of ammonium nitrate detonated , creating several massive explosions that caused serious damage . the incident killed 581 people .
