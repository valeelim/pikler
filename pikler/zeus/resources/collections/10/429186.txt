Don Lund     donald andrew lund ( may 18 , 1923 – december 10 , 2013 ) was a backup outfielder in major league baseball . he played for the brooklyn dodgers ( 1945 , 1947 , 1948 [ `` start '' ] ) , st. louis browns ( 1948 [ `` end '' ] ) and detroit tigers ( 1949–1954 ) . he batted and threw right-handed . he died on december 10 , 2013 at the age of 90 in ann arbor , michigan at his home from natural causes . other websites . - baseball library entry for don lund - vintage card traders 1954 series don lund card
