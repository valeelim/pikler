Men in Black 3     men in black 3 is a 2012 american 3d science fiction comedy movie . it is about stopping aliens from changing the past in 1969 , so agent j travels back in time to stop that from happening . the movie stars will smith , tommy lee jones , and josh brolin . movie critic roger ebert gave the movie 3 out of 4 stars . it is a sequel to men in black and men in black ii .
