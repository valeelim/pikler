Aesculus indica     aesculus indica ( or the indian or himalayan horse chestnut ) is a tree . it grows to about with a spread of about . it best grows in -15°c ( 5°f ) , usda zones 7-9. it is in flower from june to july . the seeds ripen in october . the flowers are hermaphroditic . there are many white blossoms during may and june . the plant is pollinated by bees . its leaves are 10–20 cm long by 2–6 cm wide . the mature tree forms a round canopy .
