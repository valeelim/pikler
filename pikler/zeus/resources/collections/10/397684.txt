Constantino Romero     constantino romero garcía ( may 29 , 1947 – may 12 , 2013 ) was a spanish tv and radio host and actor . he was known for dubbing in spanish as darth vader in `` star wars '' , as james t. kirk in `` star trek '' , as james bond in several `` james bond '' movies , as harry callahan in `` dirty harry '' , and as the terminator in `` terminator '' . romero was born on may 29 , 1947 in alcalá de henares , spain . he was raised in chinchilla de monte-aragón . romero retired in 2012 after 47 years of acting . he was married to joana vila until his death . romero died on may 12 , 2013 from als in his home in barcelona , spain , aged 65 .
