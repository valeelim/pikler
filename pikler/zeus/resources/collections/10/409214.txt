Son of Flubber     son of flubber is a 1963 disney movie directed by robert stevenson . it stars fred macmurray , nancy olsen , keenan wynn , and tommy kirk . it is a science fiction , family-oriented comedy , and a sequel to disney 's 1961 comedy , `` the absent-minded professor '' . `` flubber '' tells of the professor 's further experiments with his anti-gravity substance called `` flubber '' .
