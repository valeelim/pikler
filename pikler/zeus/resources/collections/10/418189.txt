Daisy Miller     daisy miller is a novella by henry james . it was published in 1879. it is about a rich and flirtatious young american woman who offends the sensibilities of the europeans she is moving among . she is an outsider and dies after catching a fever on a visit to the colosseum . it was a huge success and is still one of james 's most popular works . the story was made into a movie starring cybil shepherd .
