Cooking show     a cooking show ( also called a cookery programme ) is a television program where one or more hosts show people how to cook food dishes in a kitchen studio set . the show 's host , often a celebrity chef , will make one or more food dishes in an episode . they will show the inspiration for the dish , how to make it , and the stages of cooking . these types of shows are often meant to be educational , although some shows are competitions that are meant to entertain the viewer . the cable television network food network airs these types of shows .
