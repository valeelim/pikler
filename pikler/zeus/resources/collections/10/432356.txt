Alma Muriel     alma muriel ( 20 october 1951 – 5 january 2014 ) was a mexican actress . she was known for playing villain roles on telenovelas . muriel was born in mexico city on 20 october 1951. she was partners with actor joaquín cordero . muriel died from a heart attack on 5 january 2014 at her apartment in playa del carmen , quintana roo . she was aged 62 .
