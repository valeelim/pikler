The Thing (1982 movie)    the thing ( 1982 movie ) the thing ( also known as john carpenter 's the thing ) is a 1982 american science fiction horror movie directed by john carpenter . it stars kurt russell and wilford brimley . the film 's title refers to the main antagonist : a parasitic extraterrestrial lifeform that violently assimilates other organisms and in turn changes into their shape . the thing enters an antarctic research station , taking the appearance of the researchers that it absorbs , and paranoia develops within the group . it was released on june 25 , 1982. other websites . - `` the thing '' at theofficialjohncarpenter.com - film critic jonathan rosenbaum 's set visit
