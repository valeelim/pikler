Marrakesh     marrakesh , or marrakech ( berber : ; ) is an important city in the african country of morocco . it is the fourth largest city in the country . marrakesh is the capital of the economic region of marrakesh-tensift-el haouz . marrakesh is the most important city out of all of morocco 's cities that used to be imperial cities . berber farmers lived in marrakesh during neolithic times . abu bakr ibn umar , the chieftain and cousin of the king of the almoravids yusuf ibn tashfin , founded marrakesh in the year 1062. the almoravids built many schools and mosques in marrakesh . marrakesh has the name `` red city '' because a lot of it is made out of red sandstone . marrakesh got bigger very quickly and became a cultural , religious , and trading center for sub-saharan africa . the mayor of marrakesh fatima zahra mansouri is the second woman mayor in morocco . tourism is important to marrakesh , and the city has about 400 hotels , some very grand indeed .
