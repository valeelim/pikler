Färjestads BK     färjestads bk is an ice hockey club in the town of karlstad in sweden . the club was established on 10 november 1932 a bandy club . it adopting the game of ice hockey in 1956. färjestads bk won the swedish national championship in 1981 , 1986 , 1988 , 1997 , 1998 , 2002 , 2006 , 2009 and 2011. they also lost the national finals in 1976 , 1977 , 1983 , 1985 , 1987 , 1990 , 1991 , 2001 , 2002 , 2004 , 2005 and 2014. other websites . - official website
