Paul Greengard     paul greengard ( born december 11 , 1925 ) was an american neuroscientist . he is best known for his work on the molecular and cellular function of neurons . in 2000 , greengard , arvid carlsson and eric kandel were awarded the nobel prize for physiology or medicine for their discoveries concerning signal transduction in the nervous system . other websites . - nobel prize biography - the greengard lab at the rockefeller university - paul greengard us patents
