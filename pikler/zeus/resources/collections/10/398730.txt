Kelly Gallagher     kelly gallagher ( born 24 april 1987 ) is a skier from northern ireland . she has a disability : she can not see everything . she skied at the 2011 ipc alpine skiing world championships . she was the second skier to finish in the women 's visually impaired slalom race . she was the third skier to finish in the women 's slalom race . because she can not see , charlotte evans was her guide and told her where to go when going down the mountain .
