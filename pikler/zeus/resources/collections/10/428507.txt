Martel, Lot    martel , lot martel is a commune in the lot department in south-western france . the town has a distinctive sky-line with medieval towers . there is a museum containing items from puy d'issolud , a local gallic archaeological site which has been identified as uxellodunum , besieged by julius caesar in 51 bc . henry the young king died here in 1183. related pages . - communes of the lot department
