Israel Railway Museum     israel railway museum ( ) is the national trains museum of israel . the museum is located in the east train station of haifa . the station was established in 1892 by the ottoman government for the hejaz train to mecca . it was the first train station in palestine and the land of israel . in the station there is a turkish monument for the train . other websites . - israel railways : about the israel railway museum - ilmuseums.com : israel railway museum
