Miguel Galindo Garces     miguel galindo garces ( born 1981 ) is a skier from spain . he helps skiers who can not see by telling them where to go on a mountain . he skied at the 2011 ipc alpine skiing world championships with jon santacana maiztegui . they were the second skiers to finish in the visually impaired men 's downhill race . they were the first skiers to finish in the men 's visually impaired super-g race , the giant slalom race and the super combined race . they were the second skiers to finish in the slalom race .
