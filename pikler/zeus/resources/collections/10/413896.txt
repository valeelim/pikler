Antonio Borrero     antonio maría vicente narciso borrero y cortázar ( 29 october 1827 – 9 october 1911 ) was vice president of ecuador from 1863 to 1864 and president from 9 december 1875 to 18 december 1876. borrero was born in cuenca , ecuador on 29 october 1827. he died on 9 october 1911 in quito , ecuador from natural causes , aged 83. other websites . - official website of the ecuadorian government about the country president 's history
