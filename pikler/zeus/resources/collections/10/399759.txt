Ecuadorian American     an ecuadorian american is an american who is of ecuadorian ancestry . ecuadorian americans can be mestizo , white , afro-ecuadorian , indigenous , mulato , or zambo . american singer christina aguilera is of ecuadorian descent . former ecuadorian presidents sixto durán ballén and galo plaza were born in the united states , but their parents were from ecuador . voice actor mike judge was born in ecuador to american parents .
