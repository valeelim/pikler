Bonnie and Clyde     bonnie elizabeth parker ( october 1 , 1910 – may 23 , 1934 ) and clyde chestnut barrow ( march 24 , 1909 – may 23 , 1934 ) were american outlaws and robbers . they were from the area around dallas . they traveled the central united states with their gang during the great depression . clyde is known today for his bank robberies . however , he preferred to rob small stores or rural gas stations . the gang is believed to have killed at least nine police officers and several civilians . the couple were eventually surprised and killed by police in bienville parish , louisiana . a 1967 movie about them , `` bonnie and clyde '' , stars faye dunaway and warren beatty . other websites . - fbi files on bonnie and clyde , covering 1933-1944 - the poems of bonnie parker - < a href= '' http % 3a//www.jeffreysward.com/gallery/gallerybonnieclyde/pages/r3c2.htm '' > unauthenticated barrow letter to henry ford < /a > - the clyde barrow gang collection from the dallas police department archives
