Shmuley Boteach     shmuel `` shmuley '' boteach ( born november 19 , 1966 ) is an american orthodox rabbi and writer . he hosted a reality television series called `` shalom in the home '' on the tlc network . boteach is married to debbie boteach . he became friends with michael jackson in the 1990s . he was jackson 's rabbi for two years . other websites . - official website
