TR-069     tr-069 is a protocol that is mainly used for the communication with end-user devices , such as dsl modems . the number of such devices is rising steadily , and they are often are very difficult to configure . for that reason , tr-069 allows to configure these modems remotely . this also poses problems , because it allows to change the configuation the managed device as well as other devices , which may be behind a firewall . since the users are not aware of the configuration changes , tr-069 can be used to introduce a backdoor . this backdoor might allow a `` remote search '' by police or legal authorities of a country . tr069 open source implementations . - easycwmp opensource implementation of the tr069 cwmp client developed with c/shell
