Panchayat town     a panchayat town or town panchayat is a type of town in india . it refers to any small town which is formed under the panchayati raj administrative system . it will have under 25,000 people living there . in census data , the abbreviation t.p . is used to indicate a `` town panchayat '' . tamil nadu was the first state to introduce the panchayat town as an intermediate step between rural villages and urban local bodies ( ulb ) . each state of india has an organisation that manages panchayat towns . - karnataka : directorate of municipal administration - kerala : local self government department - tamil nadu : directorate of town panchayats
