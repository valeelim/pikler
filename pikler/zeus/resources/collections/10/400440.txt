Sergio Sanchez Hernandez     sergio sanchez hernandez ( born may 16 , 1968 in barcelona ) is a track and field athlete from spain . he has a disability : he is blind and a t12/b2 type athlete . he raced at the 1996 summer paralympics . he finished first in the 4 x 400 meter t11-t13 race . he finished second in the 400 meter t12 race .
