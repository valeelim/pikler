Raquel Saavedra Salvador     raquel saavedra salvador ( born may 11 , 1981 in barcelona ) is a swimmer from spain . she has a vision impairment and is an s11/b1 type swimmer . she raced at the 1996 summer paralympics . she finished first in the 100 meter backstroke . she finished third in the 4 x 100 meter 49 points medley relay race . she raced at the 2000 summer paralympics . she finished third in the 100 meter backstroke .
