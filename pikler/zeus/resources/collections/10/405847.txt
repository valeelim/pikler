Staten Island Zoo     the staten island zoo is a zoo in staten island , new york . it has over 443 animals of over 206 species . it opened on june 10 , 1936. at the time of its opening , it was considered the first `` educational zoo '' in the united states . the zoo is known for its large collection of rattlesnakes . the zoo 's collection is one of the largest rattlesnake collections in north america . it is the home of staten island chuck , a groundhog who is new york city 's official groundhog day forecaster . other websites . - staten island zoo website
