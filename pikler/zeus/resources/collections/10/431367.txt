Saul Williams     saul stacey williams ( born february 29 , 1972 ) is an american singer , poet , and actor . he played the main character in the 1998 movie `` slam '' . williams was born in newburgh , new york . for high school he went to newburgh free academy . he became an open mic poet in 1995. in 1998 he played ray joshua , the main character in the movie `` slam '' . he also helped write the script . in 2001 he released his first album `` amethyst rock star '' . his second album `` saul williams '' was released in 2004. his third album `` the inevitable rise and liberation of niggytardust ! '' was released on november 1 , 2007. it was produced by trent reznor . williams is vegan . he lives in paris .
