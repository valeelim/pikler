Jason Sudeikis     daniel jason sudeikis ( ; born september 18 , 1975 ) is an american actor , voice actor , writer and comedian . sudeikis was born on september 18 , 1975 in fairfax , virginia . in 2003 , he was hired as a sketch writer for `` saturday night live '' and became a cast member from 2005 to 2013. he has appeared on television in `` 30 rock '' , `` the cleveland show '' , `` eastbound & down '' , and other shows . he played `` schmitty '' on `` it 's always sunny in philadelphia '' . he is also known for his roles in the films `` hall pass '' , `` horrible bosses '' , `` epic '' and `` we 're the millers '' .
