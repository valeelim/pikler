Mutiny on the Bounty (1935 movie)    mutiny on the bounty ( 1935 movie ) mutiny on the bounty is an mgm movie from 1935 directed by frank lloyd and produced by irving thalberg . it was directed by frank lloyd . it is based on the novel of the same name by charles nordoff and james hall . the movie is about the mutiny on the ship `` bounty '' . it stars charles laughton as captain bligh , clark gable as fletcher christian , franchot tone as roger byam , and movita castaneda as a tahitian girl . all three were nominated for best actor academy awards . the movie won the 1935 best picture academy award .
