The Farmer's Daughter    the farmer 's daughter the farmer 's daughter is a political based 1947 movie . the movie is about a farmgirl who ends up working as a maid for a congressman and his very powerful mother . loretta young stars as katie holstrom . anna q. nillson plays as mrs. holstrom . loretta young received the academy award for best actress for her performance in this movei . the movie was released in march and april 1947 in the united states .
