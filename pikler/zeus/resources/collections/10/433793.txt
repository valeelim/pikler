Jon Bing     jon bing ( 30 april 1944 – 15 january 2014 ) was a norwegian writer and law professor . he was a professor at the norwegian research center for computers and law ( nrccl ) , and the faculty of law at the university of oslo . bing was born on 30 april 1944 in tønsberg . he died on 15 january 2014 , at the age of 69 , in oslo . he died from a stroke .
