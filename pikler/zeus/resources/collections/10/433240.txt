Corey Feldman     corey scott feldman ( born july 16 , 1971 ) is an american actor and singer . he was born in los angeles . he is the singer of the ska band truth movement . he first acted when he was 3 years old in a mcdonald 's advert . he was friends with michael jackson . feldman was a contestant in the first season of `` the surreal life '' . on october 28 , 2013 `` coreyography '' was published . in the book feldman wrote about how he started taking drugs when he was 14 years old . when he was 17 , joe dante and carrie fisher told him to stop taking drugs . he did not listen to them .
