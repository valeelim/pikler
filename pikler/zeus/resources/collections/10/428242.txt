Blindfold     a blindfold is a piece of cloth tied over a person 's eyes . sometimes a blindfold is made of material other than cloth . blindfolds make it impossible to see . they are used for many different reasons : - to cover the eyes of a person who is blind . - they are used in some games played by children on a playground . - as sleep masks to help a person sleep when there is too much light . - in a ransom so that a person can not see the kidnappers . - in magic tricks . - for erotic play . in art , justice is sometimes made to look like a woman wearing a blindfold . related pages . - eyeglasses - mask
