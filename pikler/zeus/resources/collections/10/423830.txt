Santa Ana winds     the santa ana winds are a kind of wind that happen in southern california and northern baja california . they are strong and extremely dry . they start in the mountains and blow towards the pacific ocean . they often happen during the autumn and winter . they cause wildfires to become worse . these winds are a kind of katabatic wind , which means they come from the mountains . many people think the name `` santa ana '' comes from the santa ana canyon in orange county , california .
