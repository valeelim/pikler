Buenos Aires Convention     the buenos aires convention ( or convention on literary and artistic copyright ) is an international copyright treaty . it was signed at buenos aires on 11 august 1910. the countries which signed it agreed to respect the copyrights of the other countries . for a document to be covered by this convention , its copyright statement must use the proper wording . often , the phrase `` all rights reserved '' ( , ) was used . however , the united states only required the name of the writer and the year that the work was published . the following countries signed the buenos aires convention : other websites . - text of the convention
