Fernand Léger     fernand léger ( joseph fernand henri léger , 4 february 1881 – 17 august 1955 ) was a french painter , sculptor , and filmmaker . in his early works he created a personal form of cubism which he gradually changed into a more popular figurative style . his boldly simplified treatment of modern subject matter made him a forerunner of pop art . his personal style was called `` tubism '' because it was influenced by cubism , but used tube-like forms .
