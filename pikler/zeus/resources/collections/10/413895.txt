José Javier Eguiguren     josé javier eguiguren ríofrío ( 3 december 1816 – 1884 ) was acting president of ecuador from 6 october 1875 to 9 december 1875. eguiguren was born in 3 december 1816 in ibarra , ecuador . he died in 1884 in quito , ecuador . other websites . - official website of the ecuadorian government about the country president 's history
