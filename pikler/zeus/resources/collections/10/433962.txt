Bảo Đại    bảo đại bảo đại ( 22 october 1913 - 30 july 1997 ) was the last king of vietnam . he was the 13th and last king of the nguyễn dynasty . he was the leader of the country during world war ii . bảo đại ruled as the king of annam from 1926 to 1945. japan briefly took control of vietnam in 1945 , and bảo đại was made emperor of vietnam . france took back control of vietnam in 1949 , as the state of vietnam . bảo đại served as chief of state from then until 1955 .
