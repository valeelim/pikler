Cirrhosis     cirrhosis is a result of disease of the human liver . it is marked by replacement of liver tissue with scar tissues and fibrosis , leading to a loss of function in the liver . cirrhosis is caused mostly by alcoholism , hepatitis b or hepatitis c , although there are other causes . ascites ( retention of fluid in the abdomen ) is the most common complication for cirrhosis . it is linked to poor qualities of life and poor outcomes . cirrhosis is generally not reversible .
