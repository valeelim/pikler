Diana Rigg     diana elizabeth rigg dbe ( born 20 july 1938 ) is an english actress . rigg played emma peel in the television thriller `` the avengers '' ( 1965-68 ) . she played helena in a `` midsummer night 's dream '' and arlene marshall in `` evil under the sun '' . she has also performed on stage . rigg was born in doncaster , west riding of yorkshire , england . rigg was made a commander of the order of the british empire ( cbe ) in 1988. she was made a dame commander of the order of the british empire ( dbe ) in 1994 .
