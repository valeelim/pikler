Dark Shadows (movie)    dark shadows ( movie ) dark shadows is an american-british comedic horror movie . it is based on the gothic television soap opera that aired in the united states from 1966 until 1971. it was directed by tim burton and produced by richard d. zanuck . it stars johnny depp , michelle pfeiffer , helena bonham carter , jackie earle haley , eva green , and christopher lee . jonathan frid had a cameo role . it was composed by danny elfman . it was released on may 11 , 2012 .
