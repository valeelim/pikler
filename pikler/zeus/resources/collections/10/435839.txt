Columbian mammoth     the columbian mammoth ( `` mammuthus columbi '' ) , or imperial mammoth , was a species of mammoth which lived late in the pleistocene period . its remains have been found in canada , the united states , nicaragua and mexico . the columbian mammoth went extinct at the end of the ice ages about 11,000 years ago . it shared the fate of many other large mammals , whose extinction is attributed to hunting and climate change .
