Say You'll Be There    say you 'll be there `` say you 'll be there '' is a pop single by the spice girls . it was recorded in sometime in 1995 though it was not released until the middle of october 1996. the song is about relationships and setting conditions for staying together . the song has various mixes , one of them eight minutes and thirty seconds . the song peaked at # 3 for the `` billboard '' hot 100. it hit # 1 over western europe . the song got mixed reviews from critics . the music video was filmed in the mojave desert and spent 2 weeks at no.1 in the uk .
