İstiklâl Marşı    istiklâl marşı the istiklâl marşı ( english : independence march ) is now the national anthem of the republic of turkey and turkish republic of northern cyprus . it was adopted in 12 march 1921. penned by mehmet âkif ersoy , ultimately composed by osman zeki üngör . the lyrics of the turkish national anthem consist of a long poem with 41 lines of verse . only the first 8 lines are performed in ceremonies .
