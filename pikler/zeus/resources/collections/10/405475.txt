IF Björklöven     if björklöven is an ice hockey club in the town of umeå in sweden . the club was established on 15 may 1970 as ifk/ssk , umeå when the clubs of ifk umeå and sandåkerns sk merged their ice hockey sections . the name was changed in 1971. if björklöven won the swedish national championship in 1987. they also lost the finals of 1982 and 1988. other websites . - official website
