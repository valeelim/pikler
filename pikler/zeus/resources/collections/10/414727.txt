Mud (movie)    mud ( movie ) mud is a 2012 american drama movie directed by jeff nichols . it is nichols first movie that he ever directed . it stars matthew mcconaughey , tye sheridan , sam shepard , and reese witherspoon . the movie is set in arkansas . it was released for a limited time on april 26 , 2013 and later on may 10 , 2013. it received very positive reviews . it currently has a 98 % on rotten tomatoes .
