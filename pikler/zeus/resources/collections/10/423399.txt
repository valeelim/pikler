The Indelicates     the indelicates are an indie rock band from sussex , england . it was started in late 2005 by julia clark-lowes , who left the pipettes six months before , and simon clayton ( under their stage names , julia and simon indelicate ) . they have released four albums . julia and simon are a couple . the indelicates released their first album `` american demo '' in 2008. in 2010 they released their second album , `` songs for swinging lovers '' . in 2011 the band 's third album `` david koresh superstar '' was released . it is a concept album about david koresh . in 2013 the indelicates released their fourth album `` diseases of england '' . official website
