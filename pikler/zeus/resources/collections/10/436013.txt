Riedlingen     riedlingen is a town in the german federal state of baden-württemberg . about 10,000 people live there . it is near the danube . riedlingen is over 1,000 years old . its foundation is dated back to the 13th century . documents mention it as early as 835. in 1806 , it became part of the kingdom of württemberg . in 1973 it became part of the district of biberach . riedlingens current mayor is marcus schafft ( cdu ) .
