Fernando Gómez Doblas     fernando gómez doblas ( born october 16 , 1965 in málaga ) is a track and field athlete from spain . he has a disability : he has cerebral palsy and a t36 type athlete . he raced at the 1996 summer paralympics in atlanta , georgia . he finished second in the 400 meter t34-t35 race , the 200 meter t34-t35 race and the 100 meter t35 race . he raced at the 2000 summer paralympics in sydney , australia . he did not finish 1 , 2 or 3 in any race he raced in at the 2000 games .
