Central Bank of Azerbaijan     the central bank of azerbaijan ( ) is the central bank of azerbaijan . the headquarters of the bank is in the capital city baku . the bank has regional branch offices in nakhchivan , ganja , yevlakh , khachmaz , goychay and bilasuvar . the bank was first established on september 16 , 1919 shortly after the establishment of the azerbaijan democratic republic on may 28 , 1918. it was closed when azerbaijan was occupied and annexed by the soviet union , and was established again in february 11 , 1992 after the soviet union ended and azerbaijan became independent again on october 18 , 1991. the main tasks of the central bank of azerbaijan are : 1. to control the supply of money and its exchange with other currencies 1. to control banks and make sure the economy is stable 1. to regularly determine and announce an official exchange rate of the national currency , the azerbaijani manat 1. to print banknotes and coins , and to introduce and withdraw them from circulation 1. to make money
