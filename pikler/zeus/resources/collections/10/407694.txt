Wildest Moments     `` wildest moments '' is a song by british singer-songwriter jessie ware . it was for her debut album , `` devotion '' . it was released in the united kingdom on 29 june 2012. it was only available as a digital download . since its release , `` wildest moments '' has received positive reviews and honors from music critics . the website , pitchfork media , gave the song its `` best new music '' award . in belgium , the song was certified gold for sales of 15,000 units .
