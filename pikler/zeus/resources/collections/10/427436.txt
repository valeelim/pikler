Enrico Letta     enrico letta ( born 20 august 1966 ) was the prime minister of italy ( head of government ) from 2013 to 2014. previously , he had also been a member of several italian governments , as of 1998. in april 2015 he announced that he was not going to be a professional politician anymore , and that he wanted to concentrate on academia . he was born in pisa , tuscany . he is married to gianna fregonara and has 3 children .
