Jorge Mendoza     jorge mendoza parrado ( born june 15 , 1972 in barcelona ) is a goalball athlete and track and field athlete from spain . he has a disability : he is blind and is a type b1 goalball player . he played goalball at the 1996 summer paralympics . his team was third . he was as the 2000 summer paralympics in sydney , australia . he threw the discus and javelin .
