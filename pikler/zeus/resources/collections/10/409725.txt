Sandra Gomez Perez     sandra gomez perez ( born may 22 , 1986 in pamplona , navarra ) is a swimmer from spain . she has a disability : she is blind and is an b2/s12 type swimmer . she raced at the 2004 summer paralympics and 2008 summer paralympics . she finished first at the 2004 games in the women 's sb12 100 meter breaststroke race . she finished second in the women 's sb12 100 meter breaststroke at the 2008 games .
