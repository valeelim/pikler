Johan van Hulst     johan wilhelm van hulst ( born january 28 , 1911 ) is a dutch retired politician . he served as member of the senate from july 3 , 1956 until june 10 , 1981. he was the parliamentary leader of the christian historical union in the senate from december 10 , 1968 until june 8 , 1977 when the christian historical union merged into the christian democratic appeal . van hulst is also a chess player and a former academic . other websites . - dr. j.w .
