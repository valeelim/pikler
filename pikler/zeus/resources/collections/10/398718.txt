P. J. Carlesimo     peter john `` p . j . '' carlesimo ( born may 30 , 1949 ) is an american basketball coach . most recently he was the interim head coach of the nba 's brooklyn nets . carlesimo was born on may 30 , 1949 in scranton , pennsylvania . he is the son of college basketball coach and athletic director peter a. carlesimo . carlesimo studied at fordham university . he is married and has two sons .
