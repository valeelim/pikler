Salivary gland neoplasm     salivary gland cancer is a cancer that forms in tissues of a salivary gland . the salivary glands can be called major and minor . the major salivary glands consist of the parotid , submandibular , and sublingual glands . the minor glands include small mucus-secreting glands located throughout the palate , nasal and oral cavity . salivary gland cancer is rare , with 2 % of head and neck tumors forming in the salivary glands , the majority in the parotid .
