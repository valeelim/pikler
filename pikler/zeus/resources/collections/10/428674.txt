John C. McGinley     john christopher mcginley ( born august 3 , 1959 ) is an american actor , comedian , spokesperson , and writer . he is known for his roles as perry cox in `` scrubs '' , bob slydell in `` office space '' , sergeant red o'neill in oliver stone 's `` platoon '' and marv in stone 's `` wall street '' . he has also written and produced for television and movies . mcginley is also an author and a spokesperson for the national down syndrome society .
