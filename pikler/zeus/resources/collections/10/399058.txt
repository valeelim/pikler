Cesar Carlavilla Cubillo     cesar carlavilla cubillo ( born march 9 , 1977 in madrid ) is a track and field athlete from spain . he has a disability : he can not see . he is b2/t12 type athlete . he raced at the 1996 summer paralympics . he finished second in the 1500 metre race . he raced at the 2000 summer paralympics in sydney , australia . he finished first in the 800 metre race and the 1500 metre race . he finished second in the 4 x 400 metre t11 - t13 race .
