Blood on the Dance Floor: HIStory in the Mix    blood on the dance floor : history in the mix blood on the dance floor : history in the mix is a remix album by michael jackson . it was released on may 20 , 1997 by epic records . it has sold six million copies . three singles were released from the album . it is the best-selling remix album ever . the album has five new songs . tracks six to thirteen are remixes of songs from jackson 's album `` history '' . `` blood on the dance floor '' was released as the album 's first single . it reached number one in the uk , spain , and denmark . `` history/ghosts '' was released as the second single . `` is it scary '' was released as the third single . jackson wrote the song for the 1993 film `` addams family values '' . the second song on the album , `` morphine '' , includes jackson saying 'demerol ' many times . demerol is a drug that was found in jackson 's body after he died .
