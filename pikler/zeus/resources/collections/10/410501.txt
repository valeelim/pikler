Visiting card     a visiting card is a small paper card with a person 's name on it . they were used to let someone know that another person had come to their home for a visit . some visiting cards had just a name on them . some also had images . visiting cards are not used as much as they used to be . business cards are now used the way visiting cards used to be used . before the industrialization visiting cards were hand made . since the end of the 19th century production has become more and more automated .
