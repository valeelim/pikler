Deep Throat (movie)    deep throat ( movie ) deep throat is a 1972 american pornographic film . in it linda lovelace plays herself . she was paid $ 1250 for acting in the movie . the movie was directed by gerard damiano . in 1974 `` deep throat part ii '' was released . the movie was made in new york . a documentary about `` deep throat '' called `` inside deep throat '' was released in 2004 .
