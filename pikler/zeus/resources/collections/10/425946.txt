Kearney, Nebraska    kearney , nebraska kearney is a city in nebraska , united states . it is in the south-central part of nebraska . the city began in the late 19th century . the previous name of the city was dobytown , but the area was later renamed `` kearney '' . the 2010 census recorded the population of kearney at about 30,800. the city has grown in recent years .
