Division of Solomon     the division of solomon is an australian electoral division in the northern territory . it covers the cities of darwin ( including the surrounding suburbs ) and palmerston . the only other division in the territory , the division of lingiari , covers the remainder of the territory . the division was created in 2000. it is named for vaiben solomon , a premier of south australia , and member of the first australian parliament . he represented the northern territory in the south australian house of assembly , when it was still part of that state . election results . - 2004 election results - 2007 election results - 2010 election results - 2013 election results other websites . - division of solomon â€“ australian electoral commission - natasha grigg 's website
