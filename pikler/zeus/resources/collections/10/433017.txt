Relish     a relish is a cooked , chopped , or pickled vegetable or fruit . relish originated in india , and has spread worldwide . although it is used as an ingredient in many different dishes in india , it is mainly used as a condiment in most other countries . this use has made it one of the world 's largest selling condiments . it is mainly used on hot dogs and hamburgers . related pages . - condiment - ketchup - mustard
