Kate Upton     katherine `` kate '' upton ( born 10 june 1992 ) is an american model and actress . she was on the covers of `` cosmopolitan '' , `` gq '' , `` vogue '' and `` sports illustrated '' . she starred as sister bernice in the 2011 adaptation of `` the three stooges '' and mistress for mr. hightower in the movie `` tower heist '' , also in 2011. she was born in st. joseph , michigan , but was raised in florida .
