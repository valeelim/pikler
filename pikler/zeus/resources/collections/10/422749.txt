Goetia     goetia is a practice which involves invoking ( `` calling '' ) angels and demons . the word came from the 17th-century grimoire `` the lesser key of solomon '' . it describes the calling of seventy-two demons . the book was edited by aleister crowley in 1904. it was then called `` the book of the goetia of solomon the king '' . the word comes from the , meaning `` sorcery '' . more reading . - aleister crowley ( ed . ) , samuel liddell mathers ( trans . ) , `` the goetia : the lesser key of solomon the king '' . york beach , me : samuel weiser ( 1995 ) isbn 0-87728-847-x . - stephen skinner , & david rankine , `` the goetia of dr rudd : the angels and demons of liber malorum spirituum seu goetia ( sourceworks of ceremonial magic ) '' . golden hoard press , 2007. isbn 978-0-9547639-2-3
