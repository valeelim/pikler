Neon Jungle     neon jungle were a british girl group . they started in early 2013. the members were shereen cutkelvin , jess plummer , asami zdrenka and amira mccarthy . on 8 august 2013 , the band put a cover they sung of `` we ca n't stop '' by miley cyrus on their youtube channel . they released their first single `` trouble '' on 1 september 2013. the song was written and produced by cocknbullkid and benjamin berry . it reached number twelve in the uk singles chart and four in the scottish chart . in november 2013 , the band performed at the 2013 victoria 's secret fashion show . they released their second single `` braveheart '' on itunes in january 2014. it reached number four in the uk singles chart . the group separated in 2015. two of the band 's members were chosen for their strong looks . jess is from london . she has a jamaican father and english mother . asami lives in scotland but was born in japan . shereen has a scottish mother and belizean father . amira has a gambian mother and jamaican father .
