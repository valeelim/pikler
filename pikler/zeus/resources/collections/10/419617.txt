The Road to Wellville     the road to wellville is a 1994 comedy-drama movie . this movie is about the doctor and clean-living advocate john harvey kellogg and his unusual methods for maintaining health . anthony hopkins plays doctor kellogg . lara flynn boyle plays ida . camryn manheim plays virginia . the movie was released october 28 , 1994 in the united states . the reviews of `` the road to wellville '' were not good . the movie performed poorly in the box office .
