Opposing Viewpoints series     opposing viewpoints is a series of books on current events . it tries to explore the many different opinions of each event in the style of a debate . the series tries to encourage critical thinking and public awareness by providing opposing views on issues that have been debated . books in the series . - `` abortion : opposing viewpoints '' ( 2002 ) edited by mary e. williams ( isbn 0-7377-0777-1 , isbn 0-7377-0778-x ) - `` addiction : opposing viewpoints '' ( 2005 ) edited by louise i. gerdes ( isbn 0-7377-2216-9 , isbn 0-7377-2217-7 ) - `` adoption : opposing viewpoints '' ( 2002 ) edited by roman espejo ( isbn 0-7377-0789-5 , isbn 0-7377-0790-9 ) - `` africa : opposing viewpoints '' ( 2005 ) edited by laura k. egendorf ( isbn 0-7377-2218-5 , isbn 0-7377-2219-3 ) - `` an aging population : opposing viewpoints '' ( 2002 ) edited by laura k. egendorf ( isbn 0-7377-0782-8 , isbn 0-7377-0781-x ) - `` aids : opposing viewpoints '' ( 2003 ) edited by tamara l. roleff ( isbn 0-7377-1136-1 , isbn 0-7377-1135-3 ) - `` alcohol : opposing viewpoints '' ( 2004 ) edited by karen balkin ( isbn 0-7377-1216-3 , isbn 0-7377-1215-5 )
