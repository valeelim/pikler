Ty Burrell     tyler gerald `` ty '' burrell ( born august 22 , 1967 ) is an american actor . he is known for his role as phil dunphy in the abc comedy `` modern family '' . he has won a primetime emmy award for outstanding supporting actor in a comedy series twice , in 2011 and 2014 , and has been nominated another three times ( in 2010 , 2012 and 2013 ) for his role as phil dunphy . burrell was born on grants pass , oregon on august 22 , 1967. other websites . - ty burrell at emmys.com
