Mireia Riera Casanovas     mireia riera casanovas ( born march 14 , 1975 in lloret de mar , barcelona ) is a swimmer from spain . she has a physical disability : she has cerebral palsy and is an s7 type swimmer . she raced at the 1996 summer paralympics . she finished second in the 100 meter freestyle race . she finished third in the 400 meter freestyle race .
