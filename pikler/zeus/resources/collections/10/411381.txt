Family Four     family four was a band from sweden . they represented sweden in the eurovision song contest 1971 with `` vita vidder '' and at eurovision song contest 1972 with `` härliga sommardag '' . discography . - `` 1971 '' ( 1971 ) - `` family four 's jul '' ( 1971 ) - `` picknick '' ( 1972 ) - `` family four på berns '' ( 1973 ) - `` family four : guldkorn '' ( 2000 )
