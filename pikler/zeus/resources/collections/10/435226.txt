John McHugh     john michael mchugh ( born september 29 , 1948 ) is an american politician and diplomat . he is the 21st united states secretary of the army . he was a former new york representative in the united states house of representatives . on june 2 , 2009 , president barack obama nominated him to the position of united states secretary of the army . the united states senate confirmed mchugh in a voice vote on september 16 , 2009. he was sworn in as the 21st secretary of the army at a pentagon ceremony on september 21 , 2009. he resigned on november 1 , 2015. other websites . - secretary of the army , united states army website - statemens by john m. mchugh
