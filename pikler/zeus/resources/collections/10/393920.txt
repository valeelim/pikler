Otfried Preußler     otfried preußler or otfried preussler ( october 20 , 1923 – february 18 , 2013 ) was a german children 's book author . he wrote over 100 books . some of them are `` the robber hotzenplotz '' and `` the satanic mill '' ( krabat ) . his books sold 50 million copies . they have been translated into over 55 languages . preußler was born on october 20 , 1923 in liberec , czechoslovakia to german parents . he was married to annelies kind from 1949 until his death in 2013. he won the deutscher jugendliteraturpreis in 1972 for krabat . preußler died on february 18 , 2013 in bavaria , germany from natural causes , aged 89. other websites . - preußler 's official website - www.worldcat.org : list of preußler 's books that have been translated into english
