Julie Christmas     julie christmas is an american musician . she was the lead singer of the rock band made out of babies and the metal band battle of mice . christmas released three albums with made out of babies . they broke up in 2012. christmas sung on `` generation of ghosts '' on mouth of the architect 's 2008 album `` quietly '' . in 2010 , christmas released her first solo album `` the bad wife '' on cd and vinyl .
