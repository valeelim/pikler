Striking Distance     striking distance is an american melodrama thriller movie from 1993. it is about a police sergeant , tom hardy , who rats out his partner for using excessive force on a suspect . the movie is also about abduction . bruce willis plays tom hardy . sarah jessica parker plays jo christman and emily harper . this movie was released in september 1993 in united states theaters . the reviews were very bad . `` striking distance '' barely earned back its own $ 30 million budget .
