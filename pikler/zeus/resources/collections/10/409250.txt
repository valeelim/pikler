Eva     the name eva is a female given name . it is the latin form of the english name eve . the name `` eve '' comes from a hebrew name , meaning `` life '' or `` the living one '' . in the 1990 united states census , the name `` eva '' was very popular . its popularity peaked one year earlier at # 31. in 2006 , the name ranked at # 124 . `` eva '' is more common across england and wales . notable people with the name include eva gabor , eva marie saint and eva longoria .
