Madame Bovary     madame bovary is a novel by gustave flaubert . it was published in 1856. it is the story of an unhappy middle class french housewife . she longs for the kind of romance and glamor that life in the country does not provide . after a few unfulfilling adulteries , she kills herself . the story was first published in parts ( serialization ) in a magazine . it was thought obscene . flaubert and his publisher were charged with immorality , but were acquitted . the story was a great success when published in book form in 1857. it is thought one of the greatest novels ever written . it has been adapted to different media . an american movie starring jennifer jones is perhaps the best known adaptation to americans .
