Amber Coffman     amber dawn coffman ( born june 15 , 1984 in austin , texas ) is an american singer and guitarist . between 2005 and 2007 , she was member of instrumental band sleeping people . in 2007 , she joined dirty projectors and released three studio albums with this band . she also worked together with rusko ( `` hold on '' ) , j. cole ( `` she knows '' ) , major lazer ( `` get free '' ) and snoop lion ( `` no regrets '' ) . since 2013 she has worked on her first solo album . her debut solo album `` city of no reply '' was released in june 2017 .
