Feliciano Canaverys Denis     feliciano canaverys ( 8 june 1813 - 1840 ) was an argentine military officer . he was born in buenos aires . he was the son of josé canaverys and agustina denis . in 1830 he graduated as a second lieutenant of artillery . in 1940 canaverys was suspected of belonging to the unitarian party . he was discharged from the army , and the government quickly ordered his detention . feliciano decided to escape to uruguay and enlist in the army of general juan lavalle . he traveled to tacuarembó , but was ambushed by federales troops . after being arrested , general manuel oribe ordered that feliciano be executed . the denis family were among the first irish settlers in buenos aires . they arrived to the río de la plata in the 17th century . other websites . - http : //lanormal-isfd95.com.ar/biblioteca/historia/barba % 20enrique % 20m. , % 20unitarismo % 20federalismo % 20rosismo.pdf
