Cécile La Grenade     dame cécile ellen fleurette la grenade , gcmg , obe , phd ( born 30 december 1952 ) is a grenadian food scientist and politician . she became governor-general of grenada in may 2013. la grenade was sworn-in as governor-general of grenada on 7 may 2013 as the nation 's first female governor-general . other websites . - grenada names first female governor general , cecile la grenade - convention on the elimination of all forms of discrimination against women
