Julie Benz     julie benz ( born may 1 , 1972 in pittsburgh , pennsylvania ) is an american actress . she works mostly on television programs . she played darla in the series `` buffy the vampire slayer '' . benz also acted in some movies , including `` jawbreaker '' ( 1999 ) , `` rambo '' ( 2008 ) and `` bedrooms '' in 2010 .
