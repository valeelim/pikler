François Deguelt     françois deguelt ( born louis deghelt , 4 december 1932 – 22 january 2014 ) was a french singer . he was best known for his performance on behalf of monaco in the eurovision song contests of 1960 and 1962. deguelt died on 22 january 2014 in var , provence-alpes-côte d'azur , france from heart failure , age 81. other websites . - discography and cover art at encyclopédisque.fr
