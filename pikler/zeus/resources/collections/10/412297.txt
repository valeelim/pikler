Tower of Saviors     tower of saviors is a video game which is developed in hong kong . since july 2013 , this game has been downloaded over 5,000,000 times . seventy-five percent of players are taiwanese . the gameplay and characters are similar to `` puzzle & dragons '' , a japanese game . the person in charge said that the game has referenced other games , such as `` puzzle & dragons '' . other websites . - official website of `` tower of saviors ''
