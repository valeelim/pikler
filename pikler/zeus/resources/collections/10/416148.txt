Roman Dragoun     roman dragoun ( born 8 april 1956 ) is a czech singer , songwriter and keyboardist . he is a member of the band progres 2. he was a member between 1980 and 1983 , and has rejoined the band since 2007. he was also member of stromboli , t4 and futurum . he was a session musician for a number of other musicians and he was singer for musical theatre . in 2012 , dragoun was inducted into beatová síň slávy ( the beat hall of fame ) . he is the son of painter františek roman dragoun . other websites . - oficial website
