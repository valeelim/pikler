Mäntsälä     mäntsälä is a municipality in finland . about 21,000 people lived there in 2015. the municipalities next to it are askola , hausjärvi , hyvinkää , järvenpää , tuusula , kärkölä , orimattila , pornainen , pukkila and sipoo . mäntsälä was probably founded in 1585. other websites . - mäntsälä pages
