Bitsie Tulloch     elizabeth tulloch ( born january 19 , 1981 ) is an american actress . she acts in movies and television programs . she starred as jackie in the independent movie `` caroline and jackie '' . tulloch also played as nadine in `` lakeview terrace '' , a crime movie from 2009. she plays juliet in `` grimm '' . tulloch was born in san diego . she grew up in spain and south america .
