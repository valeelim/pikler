Richard Thorp     richard thorp ( 2 january 1932 – 22 may 2013 ) was an english actor . he was best known for playing the part of alan turner in `` emmerdale '' from 1982–2013 . he also appeared in several movies , including `` the dam busters '' ( 1955 ) and `` the barrets of wimpole street '' ( 1957 ) . thorp was born on 2 january 1932 in croydon , surrey , england . he took a break from `` emmerdale '' in july 2009 to have his right knee replaced . he never married . he died on 22 may 2013 in leeds , west yorkshire , england , aged 81 .
