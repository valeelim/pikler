Stamford Bridge, East Riding of Yorkshire    stamford bridge , east riding of yorkshire stamford bridge is a village on the river derwent , in the east riding of yorkshire , in great britain . it is about east of york and north of selby . the village sits on an old river crossing over the derwent river . the romans established a fort here about 70 ad . later a settlement of the same name was here . the battle of stamford bridge was fought here on 25 september 1066. the battle marked the end of the viking era in england .
