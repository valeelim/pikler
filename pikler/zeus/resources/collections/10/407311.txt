Order of Jamaica     the order of jamaica is an honor given to any jamaican citizen or to anyone from any other country . the order was made in 1969. members and honorary members must do : - wear the insignia of the order as a decoration - be named `` the honourable '' - use the post nominal letters `` oj '' ( members ) or `` oj ( hon . ) '' ( honorary members ) . the motto of the order is `` for a covenant of the people '' . other websites . - order of jamaica . office of the prime minister . - jamaica national awards
