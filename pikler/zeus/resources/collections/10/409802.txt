Jordi Gordillo Brunet     jordi gordillo brunet ( born august 16 , 1983 in barcelona ) is a swimmer from spain . he has a disability : he has cerebral palsy and is an s5 type swimmer . he raced at the 2000 summer paralympics in sydney , australia . he raced at the 2004 summer paralympics in athens , greece . he finished third in the 4 x 50 meter 20 point medley relay . he raced at the 2004 summer paralympics in beijing , china . he finished second in the 4 x 50 meter 20 point medley relay .
