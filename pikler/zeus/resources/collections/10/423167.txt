Juan Carlos Calabró     juan carlos calabró ( 3 february 1934 – 5 november 2013 ) was an argentine actor and comedian . he was known for his work `` johnny tolengo , el majestuoso '' . calabró was born on 3 february 1934 in buenos aires , argentina . calabró died on 5 november 2013 in buenos aires , argentina from kidney disease , aged 79. other websites . - cine nacional.com
