Civil rights movement     the civil rights movement was a series of worldwide political movements for equal civil and political rights . many times in history , people have used nonviolence to show that they are equal without hurting anybody . other times were more violent with people who began to rebel against others . many of these movements did not fully achieve their goals . however , many of them helped make steps toward equality . the main goal of the civil rights movement is that all people 's rights are equally protected by the law , including the rights of minorities . civil rights movements are different from each country . the lgbt rights movement , women 's rights movement , and many racial minority rights movements are still continuing to fight for equal rights . the angolan war of independence was fought from 1961 to 1975. angola fought against portugal . portugal was making people in angola farm cotton . three different groups in angola were against portugal . millions of people died during the war .
