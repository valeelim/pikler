Christian Benítez     christian rogelio benítez betancourt ( may 1 , 1986 – july 29 , 2013 ) , also known as chucho , was an ecuadorian footballer who played as a forward for el jaish of the qatar stars league at the time of his death . on july 29 , 2013 , benítez entered a hospital in doha , qatar because of a `` strong pain '' in his abdomen . he later went into respiratory failure . he died from complications leading to cardiac arrest at the age of 27. other websites . - transfermarkt profile - player details at ecuadorian football federation - espn profile - profile at birmingham city f.c . website
