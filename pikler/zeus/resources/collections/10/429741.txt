Miloš Zeman     miloš zeman ( ; born 28 september 1944 ) is a czech politician . he became the third president of the czech republic on 8 march 2013. before he was president , zeman served as the prime minister of the czech republic from 1998 to 2002. he describes himself as a `` tolerant atheist '' . other websites . - curriculum vitae at the website of the prague castle - miloš zeman at the website of the government of the czech republic
