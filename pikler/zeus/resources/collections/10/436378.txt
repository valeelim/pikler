Judgment at Nuremberg     judgment at nuremberg is a 1961 american drama movie directed by stanley kramer . the movie was about crimes being wrongly committed by citizens during the nuremberg trials by the nazis . the was set during world war ii . it stars spencer tracy , burt lancaster , richard widmark , maximilian schell , werner klemperer , marlene dietrich , judy garland , william shatner , and montgomery clift . it was released to very positive reviews on december 19 , 1961 .
