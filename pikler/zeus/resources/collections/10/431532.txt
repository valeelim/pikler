Rapper's Delight    rapper 's delight `` rapper 's delight '' is a song by the sugarhill gang . it was their first single . it is fourteen minutes and thirty-five seconds long . the single reached number one in the netherlands and canada . the song made hip hop popular around the world . `` rolling stone '' magazine put `` rapper 's delight '' at number 248 in their 500 greatest songs of all time list .
