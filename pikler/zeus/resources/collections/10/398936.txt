María Begoña Redal Giraldos     maria begoña redal giraldos ( born august 3 , 1975 in navarra ) is a goalball player from spain . she is blind and is a type b2 goalball player . she played goalball at the 1996 summer paralympics . her team was third . she played goalball at the 2000 summer paralympics in sydney , australia . her team was second .
