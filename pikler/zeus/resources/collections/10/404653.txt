House of Usher     house of usher is a 1960 american international pictures horror movie . it stars vincent price , myrna fahey , mark damon , and harry ellerbee . the movie was directed by roger corman . the screenplay was written by richard matheson . it is based on the short story `` the fall of the house of usher '' by edgar allan poe . `` house of usher '' was the first of eight corman movies based on poe 's stories . the movie is also known as `` fall of the house of usher '' and `` the mysterious house of usher '' . in 2005 , the movie was listed with the united states national film registry as being deemed `` culturally , historically , or aesthetically significant .
