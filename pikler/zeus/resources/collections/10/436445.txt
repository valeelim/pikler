Jóhann Gunnar Sigurðsson     jóhann gunnar sigurðsson ( 2 february 1882 – 20 may 1906 ) was an icelandic poet . sigurðsson was a new new romantic-style writer . he was born in reykjavík . he studied at the menntaskólinn í reykjavík until 1903. in november 1905 , sigurðsson fell ill and was hospitalized . he died on 20 may 1906 , aged 24 , in reykjavík .
