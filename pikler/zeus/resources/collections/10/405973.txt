Paris-Saint-Lazare railway station     the paris-saint-lazare railway station is one of the most important railway stations in paris . it is in the eighth arrondissement of paris . it was built between 1842 and 1853. it serves the major cities of normandy , but also the western suburbs and northwest of paris . it is served by metro lines 3 , 9 , 12 , 13 , and 14 .
