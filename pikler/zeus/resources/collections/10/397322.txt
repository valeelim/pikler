Joe Astroth     joseph henry astroth ( september 1 , 1922 – may 3 , 2013 ) was an american professional baseball player . he was a catcher for the philadelphia/kansas city athletics ( 1945–46 and 1949–56 ) . astroth was born on september 1 , 1922 in east alton , illinois . he studied at the university of illinois at urbana-champaign . astroth was married to marjorie astroth from 1947 until his death in 2013. they had four children . astroth died on may 3 , 2013 in boca raton , florida at the age of 90 from natural causes .
