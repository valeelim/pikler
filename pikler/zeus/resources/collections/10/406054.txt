Chicken fingers     chicken fingers ( also known as chicken tenders , chicken tendies or chicken strips ) are chicken meat dipped in breading and then deep-fried , grilled or baked . in the northeastern united states or the midwest , chicken strips are often made by way of egg batter and have smooth texture . elsewhere , however , chicken strips may lack egg batter and the texture can be rather coarse . chicken strips are provided to customers in certain restaurants , including kfc , mcdonald 's and wendy 's fast food networks .
