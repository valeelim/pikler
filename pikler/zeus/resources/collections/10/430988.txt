Kathryn Hahn     kathryn hahn ( born 23 july 1973 ) is an american actress . she played lily for six years in the television program `` crossing jordan '' on nbc . she played a hippie in the sitcom `` our idiot brother '' , also on nbc . she also played karen in the movie `` wanderlust '' . hahn was born in westchester , illinois , near chicago . she was raised near cleveland , ohio .
