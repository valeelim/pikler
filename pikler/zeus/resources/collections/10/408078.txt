Information systems     information systems ( is ) is the study of how people put data into computers to create useful information . there are five things you need to make an information system work : - people - people that are using these computers to do their jobs . - procedures - the things people have to do to do their jobs . - hardware - the actual computers that people use to store facts . - software - the instructions on these computer screens that tell you how to enter facts into the computer . - data - facts , facts , and more facts in the computer . related pages . - information technology
