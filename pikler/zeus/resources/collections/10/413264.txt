University of Paris     the university of paris ( ) was a famous university in paris , france . it was one of the first universities to be established in europe . it was founded in the middle of the 12th century . the university is often referred to as the sorbonne or la sorbonne , after the creation of the college ( collège de sorbonne ) founded around 1257 by robert de sorbon . in 1971 , the university was split into thirteen independent universities . other websites . - study abroad in france - academy of paris - sorbonne - universités paris i , iii , iv and v ( official homepage )
