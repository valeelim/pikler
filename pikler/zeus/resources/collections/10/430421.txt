Iman (model)    iman ( model ) iman mohamed abdulmajid ( born july 25 , 1955 ) , known as iman , is a somali-american supermodel . in 1992 , she played a queen in michael jackson 's music video `` remember the time '' . iman was born in mogadishu , the capital city of somalia . she went to high school in egypt . iman then studied political science at the university of nairobi in kenya . photographer peter beard saw her on a street in nairobi and asked if she had even been photographed . when he said he would pay her she asked if he would give her $ 8,000 and he said yes . that day he took photographs of her . he then took them back to new york . iman was signed to the modeling agency wilhelmina . in 1994 iman started `` iman cosmetics '' . iman cosmetics makes makeup for women with dark skin . iman 's first book , `` i am iman '' , was published in 2001. in 1978 , iman married american basketball player spencer haywood . she had a daughter with him called zulekha . she works for iman 's cosmetics company . iman divorced haywood in 1987. in 1992 , iman married david bowie . in 2000 , she gave birth to their daughter alexandria zahra jones . iman and bowie stayed together until his death on january 10 , 2016 .
