Mono-ski     a mono-ski is a sled used by a skier with paraplegia , spina bifida , muscular dystrophy , or cerebral palsy . mono-skis are used only in para-alpine skiing . people who use mono-skis need good balance to stop themselves from falling over . the mono-ski has a chair with one ski attached to the bottom . people first used mono-skis in 1985 in the united states . mono-skis allow skiers to ski using the same way that people who stand ski down a hill . mono-skis have a hydraulic lift . this makes it easier for a skier to get in and out of the mono-ski .
