Dani Filth     dani filth ( born daniel lloyd davey , born 1 july 1970 ) is an english heavy metal singer . he is the founder and lead singer of the extreme metal band cradle of filth . he co-wrote a book with gavin baddeley titled `` the gospel of filth '' which documented the history of the band . he also appeared in the movie `` cradle of fear '' where he portrayed the man . he has been accused of being a satanist , but denounced the claims and said that he was `` more of a luciferian '' . he was ranked at # 95 in the `` top 100 heavy metal vocalists '' by hit parader . other websites . - cradle of filth website
