Scott Wilson (actor)    scott wilson ( actor ) scott wilson ( born march 29 , 1942 ) is an american actor of television and movies . he is known for his roles in `` in the heat of the night '' , `` in cold blood '' , `` the great gatsby '' , `` dead man walking '' , `` pearl harbor '' , and `` junebug '' . wilson was born on march 29 , 1942 in atlanta , georgia . he has been married to heavenly wilson since 1977 .
