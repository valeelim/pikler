Baneh County     baneh county ( ) is a county in kurdistan province in iran . the capital of the county is baneh . at the 2006 census , its population was 116,773 , in 24,709 families . its people speak kurdish . they are followers of islam . the county is subdivided into four districts : the central district , alut district , namshir district , and nanur district . the county has four cities : baneh , armardeh , kani sur , and buin-e sofla . references . - `` gitashenasi province atlas of iran ''
