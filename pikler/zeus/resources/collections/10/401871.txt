Goliath and the Barbarians     il terrore dei barbari ( english : goliath and the barbarians ) is a 1959 peplum movie . it was directed by carlo campogalliani . the movie stars steve reeves as `` goliath '' . it is about the barbarian invasions of italy in ad 568. american international pictures released the movie in the us with new music by les baxter . aip invested $ 20,000 in the movie to help the producers complete it , and were rewarded when it became a big hit . the movie earned $ 1.6 million in north america during its initial release . barbarians attack and destroy an italian village . one man , emiliano ( reeves ) survives . he swears revenge and wages a one man war against the tribes . he is helped by landa ( chelo alonso ) , the daughter of the barbarian leader . alboino is played by bruce cabot .
