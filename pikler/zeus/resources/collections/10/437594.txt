President of Kenya     the president of the republic of kenya ( ) is the head of state and head of government of kenya . the president leads the executive branch of the government of kenya . the president is the commander-in-chief of the kenya defence forces . the first president of kenya was jomo kenyatta . his son , uhuru is now president . list of presidents . - 1. jomo kenyatta ( 12 december 1964 - 22 august 1978 ) - 2. daniel arap moi ( 22 august 1978 - 30 december 2002 ) - 3. mwai kibaki ( 30 december 2002 - 9 april 2013 ) - 4. uhuru kenyatta ( 9 april 2013 - )
