Flame-throated bulbul     the flame-throated bulbul is a bird found in south western india . it eats fruit and insects , and lives in thick bushes and trees . it is a part of the bulbul family of birds and is yellow with a black head and red neck . sources . - grimmett , inskipp and inskipp `` birds of india '' . isbn 0-691-04910-6 - rasmussen p.c. , and j.c. anderton 2005 . `` birds of south asia '' . the ripley guide . volume 2 : attributes and status . smithsonian institution and lynx edicions , washington d.c. and barcelona .
