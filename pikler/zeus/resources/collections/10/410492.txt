Boxing Helena     boxing helena is a 1993 american romantic drama movie . it is about a lonely surgeon from atlanta who gets obsessed with an accident victim named helena . sherilyn fenn plays helena . this movie was originally marked nc-17 ( no children 17 or under admitted ) . it was later re-rated r on appeal . the movie performed poorly in the box office . the reviews were very negative . this movie was released in september 1993. there was controversy with the casting of this movie . madonna and kim basinger were each cast as helena , but backed out . basinger was taken to court for leaving the role . she lost , and was ordered to pay 8.1 million dollars.the verdict was later set aside on appeal in 1994 , but the actress later settled for $ 3.8 million .
