Yaracuy     yaracuy ( ) is one of the 23 states of venezuela . yaracuy is divided into fourteen municipalities : the state capital is san felipe de yaracuy . other important towns are aroa , chivacoa , cocorote , marín , nirgua , sabana de parra , yaritagua , and urachiche . yaracuy state covers a total surface area of 7,100 km² . population : 597,700 inhabitants ( 2007 est ) .
