Hal Linden     hal linden ( born harold lipshitz ; march 20 , 1931 in new york city , new york ) is an american stage and screen actor , television director and musician . linden is known for his role as cpt . barney miller in `` barney miller '' . he won two special daytime emmy awards and a tony award . linden is the spokesman for the jewish national fund , a position he has held since 1997 .
