Rick Ross     william roberts leonard ii ( born january 28 , 1976 ) , better known by the stage name rick ross , is an american rapper and former civil servant . he is the founder of maybach music group . this group has helped release three of ross ' albums , including `` deeper than rap '' ( 2009 ) , `` teflon don '' ( 2010 ) , and `` god forgives , i do n't '' ( 2013 ) . mtv named him the `` hottest mc in the game '' in 2012. in july 2008 , `` the smoking gun '' claimed that ross used to work in a prison . they also released a photograph of him in prison officer clothes . at first , ross said that that was a lie ; but after more proof was published in the media , ross admitted to having worked as a correctional officer in florida before .
