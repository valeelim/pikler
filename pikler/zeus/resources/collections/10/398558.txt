Robin Femy     robin femy ( born 19 october 1989 ) is a skier from canada . he helps skiers who can not see by telling them where to go on a mountain . he skied at the 2011 ipc alpine skiing world championships with chris williamson . they were the third skiers to finish in the visually impaired men 's downhill race . they were the second skiers to finish in the men 's visually impaired super-g race . they were the third skiers to finish in the men 's visually impaired super combined race . they were the third skiers to finish in the slalom race .
