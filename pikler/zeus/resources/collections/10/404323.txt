Aripiprazole     aripiprazole ( also called abilify ) is an antipsychotic medicine used for treatment of schizophrenia , bipolar disorder , autism and an adjuvant for major depression , which was first approved by food and drug administration of the united states in november 2002. the first approval was for the treatment of schizophrenia . later approvals were for bipolar disorder and depression . common side effects include increase of blood sugar , vomiting , nausea and dizziness .
