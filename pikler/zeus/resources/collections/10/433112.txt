David Oistrakh     david oistrakh ( 30 september 1908 - 24 october 1974 ) was a classical violinist . he is considered one of the most important violinists of the 20th century . oistrakh worked with major orchestras and musicians from many parts of the world , including the soviet union , europe , and the united states . many musical pieces for the violin have been dedicated to oistrakh . this includes both of dmitri shostakovich 's violin concerti , and the violin concerto by adam khachaturian .
