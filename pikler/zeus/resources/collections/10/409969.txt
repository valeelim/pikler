Antonio Martorell Sastre     daniel llambrich gabriel ( born december 8 , 1978 in inca , balearic islands ) is a swimmer from spain . he has a disability : he is an s9 type swimmer . he raced at the 1996 summer paralympics . he finished third in the 4 x 100 freestyle 34 points relay . he raced at the 2000 summer paralympics .
