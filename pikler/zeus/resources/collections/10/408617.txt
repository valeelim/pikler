Ten Days of Repentance     the ten days of repentance ( , `` aseret yemay teshuva ) '' is a time of year when jews focus on prayer and repentance . the ten days begin with the holiday of rosh hashanah , the jewish new year , and end with the fast of yom kippur , the day of atonement . because of the importance of repentance , the two holidays of rosh hashanah and yom kippur are often called the high holy days or high holidays ( ימים נוראים , `` yamim nora'im , '' `` days of `` ) . < br > < br >
