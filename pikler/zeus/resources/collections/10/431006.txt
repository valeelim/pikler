Rap God     `` rap god '' is a 2013 hip hop song by the american rapper eminem . it is the third single from his eighth studio album `` the marshall mathers lp 2 '' which was released on november 5 , 2013. eminem raps 97 words in 15 seconds ( an average of 6.5 words per second ) in a verse that starts 4:26 into the song . he described this as `` supersonic speed '' : the song has been praised for eminem 's rapping speed and his lyrical ability . it was ranked # 14 on complex 's list of the 50 best songs of 2013 and it was ranked # 15 on rolling stone 's list of the 100 best songs of 2013. other websites . - rap god on youtube
