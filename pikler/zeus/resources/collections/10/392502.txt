Pruners     pruners are a cutting tool for pruning plants . a common form is held in one hand ; it is also called `` snippers '' or `` secateurs '' . it has two handles , one connected to a blade and the other to a blunt surface . like the word `` scissors '' , `` pruners '' is used as a plural . a pole pruner is like a pair of pruners , but at the end of a pole . it is operated by pulling on a rope . pruners are used for cutting plants and small branches , such as roses and grapevines . stems thicker than a pencil will need to be cut with loppers or a saw instead .
