Home theater     a home theater or home theatre is a theater built in a home . home theaters are designed to be like watching a movie at a movie theatre . - home cinema , commonly referred to as home theater or home theatre , are home entertainment systems that try to give you the feeling that you are watching a movie at a theater - backyard theater often uses a foldable screen , a projector and speakers . some people build a permanent screens and speakers . - home theater in a box , is a home theater package which includes a dvd-video or blu-ray disc player , amplifier , speakers , a radio , speaker wires , connection cables , a remote control , and a subwoofer - av receiver , often called a home entertainment system - front video projector and projector screen - home theater pc , using a computer and a media center software - media center , refers either to a computer or to computer software which can play music or movies , photos etc - a switchable projection window turns a window into a tv screen
