All Through the Night     `` all through the night '' is a song by cyndi lauper . it is off her first album `` she 's so unusual '' . this was the only single by lauper released worldwide which did not have a music video . it peaked at # 5 on the `` billboard '' hot 100. it became her fourth top five song in the united states . the song received mostly positive reviews from critics . it was released in september 1984 in the united states .
