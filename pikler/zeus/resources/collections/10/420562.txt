Call of Duty: Ghosts    call of duty : ghosts call of duty : ghosts is infinity ward 's 5th `` call of duty '' video game . it was first sold for microsoft windows , playstation 3 , wii u , and the xbox 360 on november 5 , 2013. it was released for the playstation 4 on november 15 , 2013 and for the xbox one on november 22 , 2013 .
