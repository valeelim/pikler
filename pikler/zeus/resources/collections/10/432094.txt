Laurel Hill Cemetery     laurel hill cemetery is a cemetery in the city of philadelphia in pennsylvania . it is a national historic landmark . it is near the schuylkill river . it is 3.5 miles north of philadelphia . a group of wealthy people from philadelphia wanted a cemetery outside the city . they found a place for it in laurel hill . it used to be owned by a businessman named joseph sims . building of the cemetery started in 1836. it was finished in 1839. the scottish architect john notman designed the cemetery . it was his first commission . there is a chapel . it is made in the gothic architecture style . louis antoine godey , sarah josepha hale , george g. meade , john c. pemberton , and many other famous people , are buried there . other websites . - general view of laurel hill cemetery , ca . 1847 - laurel hill cemetery , philadelphia , 1840
