Douglas A-1 Skyraider     the douglas a-1 skyraider is an attack aircraft designed and built in the united states . its first flight was in 1945 , but it was too late to be flown in world war ii . originally designed for the us navy , it was also used by the us air force , fleet air arm and french air force . it was used by us pilots in the korean war and vietnam war , and by the french in the algeria war . some were modified for reconnaissance or electronic warfare . before 1962 us navy 's a-1 were called ad .
