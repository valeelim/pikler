Mister Donut     mister donut is a worldwide food franchise which sells pastries , soda , coffee , muffins and doughnuts . the company was established in the united states in 1956 , though it is now based from japan . the company was acquired in 1990 , following which every north american outlet was renamed dunkin ' donuts except for fewer than six by the start of 2010. only one illinois location continues using the `` mister donut '' corporate name .
