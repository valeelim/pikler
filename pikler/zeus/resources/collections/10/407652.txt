Joanne Linville     joanne linville ( born january 15 , 1928 ) is an american jewish actress . she acted in a lot of television series and movies . her television work includes : `` gunsmoke '' , `` have gun will travel '' and `` alfred hitchcock presents '' . her movies include `` the goddess '' and `` gable and lombard '' . linville was born in bakersfield , california . she was married to director mark rydell from 1962 until they divorced in 1973 .
