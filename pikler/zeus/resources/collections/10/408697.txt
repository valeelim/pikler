Carlit     pic carlit ( ; ) is a mountain of the french pyrenees and has an elevation of above sea level . it is the highest point of the department of pyrénées-orientales and of the region languedoc-roussillon , france . pic carlit is in the nortwestern part of the regional natural park of the catalan pyrenees ( ; , often shortened as `` parc del pirineu català '' ) . there are many small lakes in the region around the mountain . one of them is the man-made lake of bouillouses , which is one of the point to begin hiking to get to the top of pic carlit . the source of têt ( the largest river of the department pyrénées-orientales ) is on the northeastern side of the mountain . the first ascent of this peak was made by henry russell in 1864. other websites . - pic carlit : climbing , hiking & mountaineering - pic carlit - altituderando - pic carlit - world 66 travel guide - pic carlit - languedoc-roussilon , france - peakery
