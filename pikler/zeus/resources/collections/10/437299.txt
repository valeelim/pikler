Simon Baker     simon baker ( born 30 july 1969 ) is an australian actor and director . he is best known for his role as patrick jane on the cbs television program `` the mentalist '' . baker was born in launceston , tasmania . he was first raised in new guinea and then ballina , new south wales . he began a career in acting in 1987. baker married actress rebecca rigg in 1998. they live together in los angeles , california and have three children . baker was nominated for an emmy award for `` the mentalist '' in 2009 .
