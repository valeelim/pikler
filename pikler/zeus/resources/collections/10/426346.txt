Flen     flen is an urban area in the county of örebro in sweden . it is the seat of flen municipality . other things which made flen famous is `` violen från flen '' , a song written by ulf peder olrog in the 1940s . the town also plays an important role in `` du ringde från flen '' , 1992 dansband song by grönwalls . the town is also mentioned in the kent songs `` flen/paris '' on the 2005 ep `` the hjärta & smärta ep '' and `` ensam lång väg hem '' on the 2010 album `` en plats i solen '' .
