Thomas Reed (architect)    thomas reed ( architect ) thomas reed ( 1817–1878 ) , was a danish architect . he was born in the 19th century in the saint croix island ( in the time when it was a dependent territory of the kingdom of denmark ) in 1817. he studied in germany and england , and arrived to colombia in 1847. in bogotá he was contracted to build the colombian national capitol by the president tomás cipriano de mosquera . he also designed the panopticon prison which was adapted in 1975 for the colombian national museum . reed died in 1878 in guayaquil , ecuador , aged 61. other websites . - biography of thomas reed , banco de la república .
