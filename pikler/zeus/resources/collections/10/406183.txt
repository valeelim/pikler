Philadelphia International Airport     the philadelphia international airport ( code : phl ) is an important airport in philadelphia , pennsylvania . it is the biggest airport in the state and in the delaware valley . starting around 1924 or 1925 , the pennsylvania national guard began using what is now known as phl ( also called hog island ) for a practice airfield . the site was later named the philadelphia municipal airport , though it had no proper terminal building until around 1940. the name changed to philadelphia international near the end of world war ii . the airport has grown a lot over the decades .
