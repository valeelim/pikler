Vaginal cancer     vaginal cancer is a type of cancer that forms in the tissues of the vagina . primary vaginal cancer is rare in the general population of women . metastases are more common . vaginal cancer occurs more often in women over age 50 , but can occur at any age , even in when they 're pregnant . it often can be cured if found and treated in its early stages . surgery alone or surgery combined with pelvic radiation is typically used to treat vaginal cancer . there are two primary types of vaginal cancer : squamous-cell carcinoma and adenocarcinoma .
