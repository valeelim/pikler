Pacific Rim (movie)    pacific rim ( movie ) pacific rim is a 2013 american science fiction monster movie . it was directed by guillermo del toro , and written by toro and travis beacham . it was produced by legendary pictures and distributed by warner bros.. it was released on july 12 , 2013. critics gave it good reviews . they liked its special effects and action scenes . it made more than $ 384 million worldwide . in 2013 , the earth 's cities are attacked by kaiju , very large monsters who come from a portal on the floor of the pacific ocean . to battle them , the nations in the pacific rim build the jaegars : very large robots piloted by two people whose brains are linked to the robots . this allows the pilots to control the robots using their mind , and the robots do the same actions that the pilots do . although the jaegars work well in killing the kaiju , the attacks happen more often and are more powerful . by 2025 , the governments of the pacific rim have ended the jaegar project and build very large walls to protect humans from the kaiju . most of the film is set in this period , focusing on the war 's later days .
