Katy B     kathleen anne brien ( born 8 may 1989 ) , better known as katy b , is an english singer and songwriter . she released her first album `` on a mission '' on april 1 , 2011. it got good reviews from music critics . it was nominated for the 2011 mercury prize . katy was born in peckham , london . she went to the brit school . her next album `` little red '' will be released in february 2014. she released the second single from it , `` 5 am '' , on 4 november 2013. it reached number fourteen in the uk singles chart .
