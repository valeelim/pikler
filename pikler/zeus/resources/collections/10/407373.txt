Angela Cartwright     angela cartwright ( 9 september 1952 ) is an english born and american actress who stars in both movies and television programs . she acted as brigitta in the 1965 movie '' the sound of music '' . she also acted as penny robinson in the 1960s television series `` lost in space '' . she is the sister of veronica cartwright , who is also a television and movie actress . angela cartwright was born in cheshire , england . < br >
