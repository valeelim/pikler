Smooth Criminal     `` smooth criminal '' is a song written by michael jackson . it is the theme song of jackson 's 1988 movie `` moonwalker '' . it reached number 1 in the netherlands , flanders , and spain . on april 10 , 2006 it was released again as a single from the `` visionary : the video singles '' box-set . it was number 1 in spain again . in 2001 , alien ant farm released a cover version of the song . it was their first single . it was a number-one hit .
