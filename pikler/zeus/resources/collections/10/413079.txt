On Human Nature     on human nature is a 1979 pulitzer prize-winning book by harvard biologist e.o . wilson . the book tries to explain how aspects of humans and society can be explained from the point of view of evolution . he explains how evolution has left its traces on the characteristics which are the specialty of the human species . examples are generosity , self-sacrifice , worship and the use of sex for pleasure . the book was an attempt to complete the darwinian revolution by bringing biological thought into social sciences and humanities .
