César Évora    césar évora césar évora ( born 'césar évora y díaz ; november 4 , 1953 ) is a cuban-mexican actor who is known for his roles on mexican telenovelas . he is the cousin of cuban writer daína chaviano . évora was born on november 4 , 1953 in havana , cuba . he is divorced from his first wife . évora is currently married to vivian domínguez and has three children . évora acquired mexican citizenship in 1999 .
