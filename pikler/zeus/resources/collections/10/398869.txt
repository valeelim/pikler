Magda Amo Rius     magda amo rius ( born july 23 , 1973 in barcelona ) is a track and field athlete and a ski athlete from spain . she has a disability : she can not see . she is a b2 type skier . she ski raced at the 1992 winter paralympics . she came in third in the giant slalom race . she ski raced at the 1994 winter paralympics . she came in second in the downhill race . she jumped at the 1996 summer paralympics . she jumped the most long in the long jump . she ski raced at the 1998 winter paralympics . she finished first in the giant , super giant , slalom and downhill races .
