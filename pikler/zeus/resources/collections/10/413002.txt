R. Hunter Middleton     robert hunter middleton ( may 6 , 1898 – august 3 , 1985 ) was an scottish-american book designer , painter , and type designer . middleton was born in glasgow , scotland on may 6 , 1898. he came to chicago in 1908 where he studied at the school of the art institute . he joined the design department of the ludlow typograph company in 1923 and served as director of the department of typeface design from 1933–71 . in 1944 he began operating a private press , the cherryburn press . he died in chicago . middleton died on august 3 , 1985 from natural causes in his home in chicago , illinois , aged 87 .
