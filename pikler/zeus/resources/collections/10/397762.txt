Banquo     banquo is a character in william shakespeare 's 1606 play `` macbeth '' . he was a friend of the play 's villain macbeth . banquo was the thane of lochaber . he was killed by murderers hired by macbeth so that he does n't get in the way of macbeth 's reign as king . his son , fleance was with him during the attack and lives .
