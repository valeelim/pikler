Marcos Manuel Llados     marcos manuel llados ( born march 26 , 1962 in madrid ) is a ski athlete from spain . he has a disability : he can not see . he is a b2 type skier . he ski raced at the 1992 winter paralympics . he was the number three skier in the giant slalom race . he ski raced at the 1994 winter paralympics . he was the number three skier in the super giant race .
