Jessica Oyelowo     jessica oyelowo ( born jessica watson in 1978 ) is an english actress . she is known for her roles in `` murphy 's law '' , `` mayo '' , and in `` hex '' . she studied at woodbridge school . she was a member of the national youth music theatre . she lives in los angeles with her husband , actor david oyelowo , and their three sons . other websites . - bbc mayo - tv.com
