Milan Michálek     milan michálek ( born 7 december 1984 , in jindřichův hradec , czechoslovakia ) is a czech professional ice hockey left winger . he currently plays for the ottawa senators of the national hockey league ( nhl ) . he has also played for the san jose sharks . before playing in the nhl , michálek played 2 seasons with hc české budějovice of the czech extraliga . he was drafted by the san jose sharks with the 6th overall pick in the 2003 nhl daft . he played 4 seasons with the sharks and was then on september 12 , 2009 , he was traded to the senators along with jonathan cheechoo and a second round pick for dany heatley and a fifth round draft pick in 2010. his older brother zbyněk plays in the nhl for the arizona coyotes .
