Palfrey     a palfrey is a type of horse that was valued as a riding horse in the middle ages . it is not a breed . the term 'palfrey ' usually meant a more expensive and well bred type of riding horse popular during the middle ages , sometimes equalling the knight 's destrier in price . it was popular with nobles , ladies and knights for riding , hunting and ceremonial use during the medieval period . a palfrey had a smooth , ambling gait . it was an ideal horse for traveling long distances comfortably .
