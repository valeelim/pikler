Cat Power     charlyn marie marshall ( better known by her stage identity cat power ; 21 january , 1972 ) is an american singer-songwriter and musician . she has been releasing albums since 1992. her cover of `` sea of love '' is played in the 2007 movie `` juno '' . her album `` sun '' , released in 2012 , is her best and highest-charting record as of 2013. she was born in atlanta , and has lived in several other states .
