Martha Layne Collins     martha layne collins ( born martha layne hall ; december 7 , 1936 ) is a former american businesswoman and politician from kentucky . as of 2014 , she is the only female governor kentucky has had . she was the fifty-sixth governor ( 1983-87 ) after replacing john y. brown jr.. collins was considered as a running mate for democratic presidential nominee walter mondale . mondale , however , chose geraldine ferraro for his mate . layne collins was born in bagdad , kentucky .
