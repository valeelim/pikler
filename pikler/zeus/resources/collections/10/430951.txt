Practical Magic     practical magic is a romantic comedy drama movie . it was released october 1998. the story is based on a novel from the 1990s . it is about sisters who are witches . after the death of their parents , they are raised by aunts who are also witches . sandra bullock and nicole kidman play the sisters . stockard channing and dianne wiest play the aunts . `` practical magic '' received very bad reviews . its overall box office intake was very low .
