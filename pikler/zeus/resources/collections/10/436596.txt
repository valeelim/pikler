Ice Train (wrestler)    ice train ( wrestler ) harold fitzgerald hogue , sr. ( born november 13 , 1967 in atlanta , georgia ) is an american retired professional wrestler . he is known for his time when he wrestled in world championship wrestling ( wcw ) from 1990 to 2001. he competed in wcw under the ring names ice train and m.i . smooth . hogue retired from professional wrestling in 2001 soon after the wcw was bought by the world wrestling federation ( wwf ) . championships . - catch wrestling association - catch cup ( 1995 ) - pro wrestling illustrated - he was ranked # 221 of the best 500 singles wrestlers in the pwi 500 in 1993. other websites . - ice train 's profile on the internet wrestling database
