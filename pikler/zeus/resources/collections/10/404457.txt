2013 Santa Monica shooting     on june 7 , 2013 , a killing spree by a lone gunman occurred in santa monica , california , starting with a domestic dispute and subsequent fire at a home , followed by a series of shootings near and on the campus of santa monica college . six people were killed , including the suspect , and four people were injured in the incident . the gunman , 23-year-old john zawahri , was killed by police officers when he exchanged gunfire with them at the santa monica college library .
