South African Army     the south african army is the army of the south african national defence force , which is the military of south africa . the south african military traditions evolved from the frontier warfare fought by the commando forces , because the afrikaners did not trust standing armies . after the union of south africa was formed in 1910 , general jan smuts , south africa 's first minister of defence , placed a high priority on creating a unified military from the four separate armies of the provinces . this lead to the creation of the union defence force ( udf ) . the modern day south african army is made up of professional soldiers of all races . the south african army has been with peacekeeping operations with the united nations and the african union in countries such as burundi and the democratic republic of the congo .
