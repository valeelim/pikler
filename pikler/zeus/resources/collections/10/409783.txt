Paloma     paloma is a female given name . it comes from the latin word `` palumba '' , which means `` dove '' . this name is often understood to mean a christian type of symbol of the holy spirit in this bird . the name is common in italy , spain and hungary . in november 2008 , hurricane paloma caused heavy destruction over the cayman islands and cuba . the name `` paloma '' was then retired and replaced with `` paulette '' for 2014 .
