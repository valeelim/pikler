Southern hip hop     southern hip hop is a type of hip hop music that comes from the southern united states . a well-known southern hip hop musician is lil ' wayne . some other well known artists include : ludacris , gucci mane , birdman , 2 chainz , vanilla ice , t.i. , young buck , and three six mafia . in 2003 a documentary movie called `` dirty states of america : the untold story of southern hip-hop '' was released .
