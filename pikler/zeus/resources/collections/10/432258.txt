Rookie (magazine)    rookie ( magazine ) rookie is an american online magazine for teenage girls . it was started by the fashion blogger tavi gevinson in september 2011 with anaheed alani as the editorial director . gevinson was going to make the magazine with jane pratt . pratt decided not to be part of the magazine . however , gevinson said that she is the magazine 's `` fairy godmother . '' rookie has published interviews with famous people including lorde , emma watson , and kathleen hanna . rookie posts three times every weekday . each month it has a theme . rookie has released two yearbooks .
