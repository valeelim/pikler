Andhrímnir     andhrímnir is the chef of the gods in norse mythology . he is the god that killed the mighty boar sæhrímnir . he cooks sæhrímnir in his cauldron named eldhrímnir . the cooked beast is then fed to the gods and fallen warriors in valhalla . at night , the boar comes back to life so andhrímnir can kill him and cook him again . this way , the gods and fallen warriors eat well until ragnarok .
