Harlequin F.C.    harlequin f.c . harlequins are a rugby club based in london , england who play in the aviva premiership . they are perhaps most well known for their distinctive multi-coloured shirts . the official colours are listed as : light blue , magenta , chocolate , french grey , light green and black . their ground is not far from twickenham stadium ( the home of the rfu ) which was also the venue where they became aviva premiership champions in 2012 .
