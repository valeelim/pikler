Knack     knack is a platform video game . it is published by sony computer entertainment and developed by japan studio working with game director mark cerny . the game was made for the playstation 4 , and was released as a launch title for that video game console . it was officially announced by cerny on february 20 , 2013 during playstation meeting 2013 in new york city . the game was released on november 15 , 2013 in north america and november 29 , 2013 in europe . it was released in japan on february 22 , 2014. the game received generally low scores from most reviewers like ign , but received an eight-out-of-ten from destructiod . knack discovers that he has a skill ( `` knack '' ) that allows him to incorporate ice , metal and other substances into his body , giving him new abilities . he then uses these powers to try to end the war between the humans and goblins .
