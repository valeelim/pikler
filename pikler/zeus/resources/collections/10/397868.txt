Division of Fraser     the division of fraser is an australian electoral division in the australian capital territory and the jervis bay territory . it was set up in 1974 when the division of australian capital territory was changed . it is named for jim fraser , who was member for australian capital territory 1951-70. it includes the area north of the molonglo river and lake burley griffin , belconnen , gungahlin , and north canberra . it also includes jervis bay territory . election results . - 2004 election results - 2007 election results - 2010 election results - 2013 election results other websites . - division of fraser - australian electoral commission - andrew leigh 's website
