4 (Beyoncé album)    4 ( beyoncé album ) 4 is the fourth studio album by american singer and songwriter beyoncé knowles . it was released june 24 , 2011. seven singles were released from the album . `` run the world ( girls ) '' was the first single from the album . pitchfork gave the album a score of 8 out of 10. the single `` love on top '' won the best traditional r & b performance award at the grammy awards . the album has sold three million copies . the third song on the album `` i miss you '' was written by frank ocean .
