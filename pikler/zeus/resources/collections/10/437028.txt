Chevrolet Celebrity     the chevrolet celebrity was a mid sized automobile . it was made for years 1982 to 1990. the celebrity was best selling for 1986 in north america . the vehicle lasted one generation . the sedan was stopped for 1989. the station wagon was last produced for 1990. chevrolet replaced the sedan with the lumina , and the station was succeeded by chevrolet lumina apv ( a minivan ) .
