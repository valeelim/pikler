New South Wales Legislative Assembly     the new south wales legislative assembly is the lower house of the parliament of new south wales in australia . the upper house is the legislative council . both sit at parliament house in the state capital , sydney . the assembly has 93 members , each elected to a single-member constituency , known as seats . voting is by the optional preferential system . members of the legislative assembly have the acronym mp after their names . until 1990 , the acronym `` mla '' ( member of the legislative assembly ) was used .
