Bugsy Malone     bugsy malone is a british criminal comedy musical movie from 1976. it was directed by alan parker . it is set in 1929 in new york city . the story is very loosely based on events in new york city from the early 1920s to 1931 in the prohibition era . it is about gangsters like al capone and bugs moran . the movie feature only child actors . the singing voices are provided by adults . it was aimed at an audience of children .
