Team Dresch     team dresch is an american punk band . donna dresch started the band in 1993. in 1995 they released their first album `` personal best '' . in 1996 they released their second album `` captain my captain '' . after this kaia wilson and melissa york left the band to start another band called the butchies . the band broke up in 1998. in 2004 they got back together . in may 2010 the band played two shows at ladyfest brazil . all of the members have been openly lesbian since the band started .
