Iñigo García Ruiz     iñigo garcia ruiz ( born july 3 , 1974 in san sebastian ) is a track and field athlete from spain . he has a disability : he is blind and a t12/b2 type athlete . he was at the 1996 summer paralympics and competed in the f12 shot put and discus . he finished 4 in the shot put and 8 in the discus . he was at the 2000 summer paralympics in sydney , australia and competed in the f12 shot put and discus . he finished 2 in the shot put and 7 in the discus . he was at the 2004 summer paralympics in athens , greece and competed in the f12 shot put and discus . he was 6 in the shoput and 9 in the discus .
