Nia Vardalos     antonia eugenia `` nia '' vardalos ( born september 24 , 1962 ) is a canadian actress and screenwriter . she has performed in several movies . she starred in comedies `` my big fat greek wedding '' in 2002 , and `` connie and carla '' in 2004. she later began acting in more independent movies . she starred in my big fat greek life and `` my big fat greek wedding 2 '' . vardalos was born in winnipeg , manitoba . her parents are greeks . she got united states citizenship in 1999 .
