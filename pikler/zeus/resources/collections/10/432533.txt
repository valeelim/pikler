It's My Party    it 's my party it 's my party is a romance and drama movie . it was released march 1996. the story is about a two-day-long party given by nick stark , a man with aids . instead of dying slowly , he decides to kill himself using seconal . eric roberts plays nick , marlee matlin plays daphne , margaret cho plays charlene lee , sally kellerman plays sara , olivia newton-john plays lina . the movie had mixed to positive reviews . its running time was 110 minutes .
