Sylvie and Bruno     sylvie and bruno is a novel by lewis carroll . it was published in 1889 by macmillan . its companion novel sylvie and bruno concluded was published in 1893 by macmillan . the first volume is set in the real world , but the second volume is set in fairyland . the books were not so well received as the `` alice '' books . related pages . - `` alice in wonderland '' - `` through the looking-glass ''
