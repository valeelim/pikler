Comedy hip hop     comedy hip hop ( or comedy rap ) is a musical genre that combines hip hop and rapping with humor . while gangsta rap came with its dark and violent themes in the 1990s , comedy rap came with its very light and very humorous themes in the 1980s . some notable artists include eminem and weird al yankovic . while very few rappers make comedy rap their focus , it still keeps its royalties with hip-hop . notable artists . - eminem - hopsin - eamon
