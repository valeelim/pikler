Free Nelson Mandela     `` nelson mandela '' ( known in some versions as `` free nelson mandela '' ) is a song written by british musician jerry dammers and performed by band the special a.k.a . - with lead vocal by stan campbell . it was released on the single `` nelson mandela / break down the door '' in 1984. it was created as a protest against the imprisonment of nelson mandela by the apartheid south african government .
