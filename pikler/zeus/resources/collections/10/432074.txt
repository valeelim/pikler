Cicely Tyson     cicely tyson ( born 19 december 1933 ) is an american actress . she played sipsey in the comedic-drama movie `` fried green tomatoes '' ( 1991 ) , and played mamma kay in the movie `` the haunting in georgia '' ( 2013 ) . she also played carrie grace battle in the television series `` sweet justice '' . tyson was born in new york city .
