WBZ-TV     wbz-tv is a television station in boston , massachusetts in the united states . it is owned by cbs . it is received on channel 4 in eastern massachusetts , rhode island , and southern new hampshire . wbz-tv first went on the air on june 9 , 1948. it is the oldest television station in boston . it is also the oldest television station in the new england states . it was originally owned by westinghouse electric corporation . the station showed programs from the nbc network when it first went on the air . on january 2 , 1995 , wbz-tv stopped showing nbc programs and started showing cbs programs . before then , cbs programs aired on whdh-tv , which now airs nbc programs . the reason channel 4 switched networks is because westinghouse electric agreed to merge with cbs , and all westinghouse channels became cbs stations .
