Dušan Dragosavac     dušan dragosavac ( ; born 1 december 1919 - died 21 december 2014 ) was a former yugoslav politician . he was a serb from croatia . he was the chairman of the league of communists of yugoslavia from 20 october 1981 until 20 june 1982. he died on 21 december 2014 aged 95. other websites . - dušan dragosavac information
