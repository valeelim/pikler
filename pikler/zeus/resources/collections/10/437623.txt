Heinävesi     heinävesi is a municipality in north karelia , finland . it had almost 3,710 living there in august 2013. the municipalities next to it are savonlinna , varkaus , leppävirta , tuusniemi , outokumpu and liperi . the most notable lakes in the area are kermajärvi and juojärvi . both are very clean and bright . other websites . - the official heinävesi page
