Trace Cyrus     trace dempsey cyrus ( born february 24 , 1989 ) is an american musician . he was the former guitarist , songwriter and vocalist of metro station . he is the adopted son of billy ray cyrus . cyrus was born in ashland , kentucky to billy ray cyrus and leticia finley . his parents are of cherokee native american , dutch , english , french , german , irish , scottish and welsh descent .
