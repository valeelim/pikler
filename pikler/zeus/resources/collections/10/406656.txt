2001 (Dr. Dre album)    2001 ( dr. dre album ) 2001 is the second studio album by american hip hop recording artist dr. dre . it was released on november 16 , 1999 by interscope records . `` 2001 '' is the follow-up to dr. dre 's first album `` the chronic '' ( 1992 ) . the album features several guest rappers such as hittman , snoop dogg , kurupt , xzibit , nate dogg , and eminem . `` 2001 '' has been certified sextuple platinum in sales by the riaa after sales of six million copies in the united states .
