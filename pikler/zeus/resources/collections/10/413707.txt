Penn's Creek Massacre    penn 's creek massacre the penn 's creek massacre was a massacre committed by delaware indians on 16 october 1755. it happened near what is present day selinsgrove , pennsylvania . made bold by braddock 's defeat on july 9 , indians killed and pillaged along the pennsylvania frontier . fourteen settlers were killed at penn 's creek . eleven were taken captive . one was wounded , but escaped . a posse sent to investigate was attacked , and both posse members and indians were killed . some of those taken captive in the penn 's creek massacre eventually returned to civilization alive .
