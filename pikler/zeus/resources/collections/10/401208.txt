Alfredo Palacio     luis alfredo palacio gonzález ( born january 22 , 1939 ) served as president of ecuador from april 2005 to january 2007. from january 15 , 2003 to april 20 , 2005 , he served as vice president , after which he was appointed to the presidency when the ecuadorian congress removed president lucio gutiérrez from power following a week of growing unrest with his government . palacio was born on january 22 , 1939 in guayaquil , ecuador . he studied at case western reserve university . palacio was married to maría beatriz paret . other websites . - presidencia de la república – presidente biography as president - bbc mundo | américa latina | ¿quién es alfredo palacio ?
