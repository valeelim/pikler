John Fortune     john fortune ( 30 june 1939 – 31 december 2013 ) was a british satirist , comedian , writer and actor . he was best known for his work with john bird and rory bremner on the television series `` bremner , bird and fortune '' . he studied at bristol cathedral school and king 's college , cambridge , where he met john bird . fortune died from leukemia , at the age of 74. other websites . - john fortune at vivienne clore - bremner , bird and fortune , `` best political satire -television , 2003 '' – political studies association awards 2003 - bird and fortune : a life in television bafta filmed event , march 2009
