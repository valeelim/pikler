Codell, Kansas    codell , kansas codell is an unincorporated community in rooks county , kansas , united states . it is south of the city of stockton ( the county seat of rooks county ) . it is near the southeastern corner of rooks county . the nearest city , plainville , is about northwest by road . on may 20 in 1916 , 1917 , and 1918 , codell was hit by tornadoes . the 1918 tornado was the worst . it killed ten people and did a lot of damage . other websites . - pictures of codell - codell history - newspaper article about the 1918 tornado
