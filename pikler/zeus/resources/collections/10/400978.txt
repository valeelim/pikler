Huey, Dewey and Louie    huey , dewey and louie huey , dewey , and louie are disney characters . they are donald duck 's nephews and triplets . the trio first appeared in a newspaper comic strip on october 17 , 1937. their first animated appearance was in the short film `` donald 's nephews '' , released april 15 , 1938. they starred in the 1987 television series ducktales . in the ducktales stories they appeared in adventures with their great-uncle , scrooge mcduck . in the 1990s series `` quack pack '' the boys were shown as teenagers .
