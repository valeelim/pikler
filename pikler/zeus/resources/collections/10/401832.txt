Chum Bucket     the chum bucket a fictional , slow and empty restaurant in the television series , `` '' spongebob squarepants '' '' . it is in the city of bikini bottom . the restaurant is run by a plankton , sheldon j. plankton . one main worker at the restaurant is carin the computer ( gives plankton ideas about how to get the restaurant running ) , and squidward tentacles ( in one episode ) . the chum bucket is mainly an empty restaurant in bikini bottom , plankton tries to sell `` chum '' but it 's inedible .
