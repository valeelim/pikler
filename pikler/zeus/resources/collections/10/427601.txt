Whitehouse.gov     whitehouse.gov is the official website of the white house . it is owned by the united states government . it was started in october 1994. it has information about events in american history , as well as current news about the president , news conferences , announcements made by the president , executive orders , and speeches that the president has made on radio . the website is the responsibility of the current administration . it is changed for each new president . the content of the website is in the public domain . it is licensed under the creative commons attribution license . other websites . - the white house - archived versions of the site during the bush administration - archived versions of the site during the clinton administration
