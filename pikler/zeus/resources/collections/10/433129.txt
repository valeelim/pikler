We Be Clubbin'    we be clubbin ' `` we be clubbin ' '' is the first single from ice cube 's soundtrack , `` the player 's club '' . it was released in 1998. it reached number 32 in the rhythmic top 40. a remix of the song called `` we be ballin ' '' was made . michael jackson sung in it . unlike `` we be clubbin ' '' , there is no swearing .
