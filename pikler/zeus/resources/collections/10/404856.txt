Kirby Puckett     anthony kirby puckett ( more often known simply as kirby puckett ; march 14 , 1960 - march 6 , 2006 ) was an american baseball player . he was born in chicago . he played for the minnesota twins from 1984 to 1995. he had many home run hits and doubles . he retired because of bad vision in one eye . kirby died on march 6 , 2006 in phoenix , arizona from a hemorrhagic stroke .
