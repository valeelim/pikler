Chandler Riggs     chandler riggs is an american child actor . he is most well known for his role on the amc original series `` the walking dead '' , as carl grimes . he was born in june 27 , 1999 in atlanta , georgia . along with his role in `` the walking dead '' , he has starred in several other movies such as `` get low '' and `` mercy '' . chandler has been nominated for `` best child actor '' in both 2012 and 2013 .
