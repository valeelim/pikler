Mega Man Anniversary Collection     mega man anniversary collection is a compilation video game for the nintendo gamecube , playstation 2 , and xbox . it was released on june 23 , 2004 for the gamecube and playstation 2 , and on march 15 , 2005 for the xbox . it contains the first eight `` mega man '' games , as well as two spin-off fighting games in the series . it also contains extras such as original artwork and remixed music . for the first six games , a `` navi mode '' is available . this allows players to get hints from helpers if they are in need of help . it also changes the look of the health and weapon meters , as well as the weapon screen , for the first three games . list of games . - `` mega man '' - `` mega man 2 '' - `` mega man 3 '' - `` mega man 4 '' - `` mega man 5 '' - `` mega man 6 '' - `` mega man 7 '' - `` mega man 8 '' - `` mega man : the power battle '' - `` mega man 2 : the power fighters ''
