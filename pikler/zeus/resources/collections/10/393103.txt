Macklemore     ben haggerty ( born june 19 , 1983 in seattle , washington ) , known by his stage name macklemore , is an american rapper , musician , and singer-songwriter . the music video for his song `` thrift shop '' has been viewed more than 210 million times on youtube . it also reached # 1 on the u.s. billboard hot 100 chart . he said he was influenced by freestyle fellowship , aceyalone , living legends , wu-tang clan , nas , and talib kweli during his earlier years . macklemore is in support of gay rights , including same-sex marriage , and sang about it in the song `` same love '' which he also sings against homophobia in mass media , hip hop , and society . macklemore was nominated for seven grammy awards at the 2014 grammys . macklemore and lewis won best new artist , best rap album ( `` the heist '' ) , best rap song and best rap performance ( `` thrift shop '' ) . he was taught at garfield high school and then nathen hale high school . his parents are namend bill haggerty and julie haggerty . he has one brother named tim haggerty . tim haggerty sings on few of macklemore 's songs like : `` and we danced '' and `` american '' .
