Pedro José de Arteta     pedro josé de arteta y calisto ( 1797 – 24 august 1873 ) was vice president of ecuador from 1865 to 1869 and served briefly as president from 6 november 1867 to 20 january 1868. he was the brother of nicolás joaquín de arteta y calisto , first archbishop of quito . he was a conservative . arteta was born in 1787 in quito , ecuador . he died from a heart attack in his home in quito , ecuador , aged 66 .
