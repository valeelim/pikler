Jacques Cloutier     jacques cloutier ( born january 3 , 1960 in noranda , quebec ) is a canadian retired ice hockey goaltender . he currently works as the associate coach for the calgary flames . before playing in the nhl , cloutier played 4 seasons in the qmjhl with the trois rivieres draveurs . he was drafted by the buffalo sabres with the 55th overall pick in the 1979 nhl entry draft . he played 144 games with sabres and 136 games with their ahl affiliate , rochester americans . after his time with sabres , he played 2 seasons with the chicago blackhawks and 58 games with the quebec nordiques . he retired from playing professional ice hockey in 1994. after retiring , cloutier became the assistant coach of the cornwall aces for 2 seasons , the assistant coach for the goaltending coach for the quebec nordiques/colorado avalanche for 12 seasons and is the current associate coach of the calgary flames .
