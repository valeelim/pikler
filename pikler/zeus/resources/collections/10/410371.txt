That Evening Sun (movie)    that evening sun ( movie ) that evening sun is a 2009 movie based on a 2002 short story `` i hate to see that evening sun go down '' by william gay . the movie is produced by dogwood entertainment . it stars hal holbrook as abner meecham and is directed by scott teems who also wrote the screenplay . dixie carter and mia wasikowska are also in the cast . the movie received positive reviews and many enjoyed holbrook 's performance .
