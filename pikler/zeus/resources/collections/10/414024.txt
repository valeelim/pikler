Gaya College of Engineering     gaya college of engineering ( gce ; ) is a college in the indian state of bihar . it was established in 2008. it was formerly named magadh engineering college when it was a private engineering institution . it is now a public institute . it is approved by aicte . it was inaugurated by the chief minister of bihar mr. nitish kumar on 19 november 2008. it is government engineering college managed by the government of bihar . it is under the administrative control of the department of science and technology . it is affiliated with aryabhatta knowledge university , patna . gce offers undergraduate courses in four streams of engineering .
