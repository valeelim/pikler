David H. Burr     david h. burr ( born 1803-died 1875 ) was an american map maker and worked for the surveyor general of the united states . he was also surveyor general of utah . he stopped being a lawyer and became a surveyor . he worked for the state of new york . he made an atlas in 1829. he was born in bridgeport , connecticut . he lived in new york in 1822. he became a lawyer . he then joined the militia for new york . he would make many more , including atlases of america . he became the official geographer of the house of representatives in the united states . he moved to utah and became their surveyor general . he stopped doing surveying and became a full-time map maker in 1857 .
