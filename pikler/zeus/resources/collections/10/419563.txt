The Chew     the chew is an american television talk and cooking show . it airs on abc . it started on september 26 , 2011. it replaced the soap opera `` all my children '' . the show 's name was inspired by talk show `` the view '' . it focuses on food-related and lifestyle topics . it is hosted by mario batali , michael symon , carla hall , clinton kelly , and daphne oz . on april 11 , 2012 , abc announced that the show would air for a second season .
