Jackie Jackson     sigmund esco `` jackie '' jackson ( born may 4 , 1951 ) is an american singer . he is the second child of katherine and joe jackson . he was a member of the jackson 5. the band has sold 150 million records around the world . they had four number one singles in the united states . the group released their first album `` diana ross presents the jackson 5 '' in 1969. the group released nine more studio albums on motown . in 1976 they left the label . they were signed to epic records . the jackson 5 's name changed to the jacksons . they released six albums . he released two solo albums , `` jackie jackson `` ( 1973 ) and `` be the one `` ( 1989 ) . he lives in las vegas . he runs two record companies , jesco records and futurist entertainment there . he has two children with his first wife enid adren spann . she divorced him after catching him cheating with paula abdul . . in 2001 jackson married his second wife victoria triggs .
