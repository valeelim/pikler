Australia's Next Top Model    australia 's next top model australia 's next top model ( also known as auntm or ausntm ) is an australian reality television show that is produced and broadcasted by fox8 . the series is a spin-off of the popular `` america 's next top model '' . the show has currently 7 `` cycles '' , but an eight is announced in july 9 , 2013. the show is/was hosted by sarah murdoch , jodhi meares , erika heynatz and for the upcoming cycle the beauty queen , model and television personality jennifer hawkins .
