5 Seconds of Summer     5 seconds of summer ( abbreviated 5sos ) is a pop-rock band from sydney , australia . the band has 4 members : calum hood ( bass guitar , vocals , poet ) , michael clifford ( guitar , vocals ) , luke hemmings ( lead vocals , guitar ) , and ashton irwin ( drums , vocals ) . the members all went to school with each other except for ashton . they supported hot chelle rae on their 2012 `` whatever '' tour . they toured with one direction for their tmh and wwa tours and gained international fame . they now have their own tour `` rock out with your socks out '' ( rowyso ) . their second headline tour started in february 2016 , `` sounds live feels live '' ( slfl ) . 5 seconds of summer started out with 3 friends ( luke hemmings , calum hood , and michael clifford ) making covers on youtube and quickly getting recognition . later on , they were in need of a drummer which led them to ashton irwin on facebook from a mutual friend , and officially became a band on december 3,2011. little by little , with all members singing and playing an instrument and getting serious by writing their own songs , louis tomlinson from one direction ( 1d ) , directed his attention toward the 4 boys and tweeted them causing the rage of many fans to fall in love with 5 seconds of summer . over the years 5sos released ep 's like `` somewhere new ( 2012 ) , unplugged ( 2012 ) , do n't stop ( 2014 ) , good girls ( 2014 ) , amnesia ( 2014 ) , and she 's kinda hot ( 2015 ) . '' they proceeded to release their first album `` 5 seconds of summer '' on june 27 , 2014 which got recognition from fans worldwide and resulted in their very first headlining tour `` rock out with your socks out '' . their second album `` sounds good feels good '' was released on october 23 , 2015 , followed by their second headlining world tour `` sounds live feels live '' . 5 seconds of summer are currently working on their third album .
