Jura, Scotland    jura , scotland jura is an island in the inner hebrides of scotland , just north-east of islay . compared with its fertile and more populous neighbour , jura is mountainous , bare and infertile . it is covered largely by vast areas of blanket bog , hence its small population . in a list of the islands of scotland ranked by size , jura comes eighth , whereas ranked by population it comes thirty-first . it is in the council area of argyll and bute .
