Kirby's Pinball Land    kirby 's pinball land kirby 's pinball land is a pinball video game that was developed by hal laboratory and published by nintendo in 1993. it is for the game boy game console . it is the first spin-off game in the `` kirby '' series . it was later released for the virtual console service on the nintendo 3ds in 2012. the game features three pinball tables . each table has three floors . the player earns points by bouncing off of walls and bumpers , and by defeating enemies . players can also earn maxim tomatoes , which stops the player from losing a ball for a short amount of time . on the middle and top floors is a warp star . if the player takes the warp star on the middle floor , the player can play a minigame to earn extra points and multipliers . the warp star on the top floor takes the player to the boss of the table . after defeating a boss , the player can earn another life by spelling out the word `` extra '' with the letters that appear on the table selection screen . after the player plays all three tables , they face against king dedede . defeating king dedede allows the player to play the tables again for a higher score .
