The Little Pilgrim     the little pilgrim was a 19th-century american monthly children 's magazine . it was published between october 1853 and december 1868 in philadelphia , pennsylvania by leander k. lippincott . it was edited by his wife , sara jane lippincott , under the pen name , `` grace greenwood '' . `` the little pilgrim '' was intended to replace the discontinued children 's magazine , `` friend of youth '' . f. o. c. darley drew the picture of the boy pilgrim on the cover . in january 1857 , the page size was reduced to about a third of its original height because `` it was found that our paper was too long to fit on the book-shelves '' . the number of pages increased from 8 to 12 with the height reduction . `` the little pilgrim '' was absorbed by `` the little corporal '' in 1869 .
