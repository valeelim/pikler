Tropical Storm Debby (1994)    tropical storm debby ( 1994 ) tropical storm debby was a severe storm in some areas of the caribbean region in september 1994. the highest winds of debby were 70 mph . the storm did between $ 100 million to $ 130 million overall in damage . nine people died because of this storm . puerto rico , montserrat and the dominican republic were affected especially bad . despite the severity , the name debby was not retired the following spring .
