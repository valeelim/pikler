Racketeer Influenced and Corrupt Organizations Act     the racketeer influenced and corrupt organizations act ( rico ) is an american federal law . this law allows extended criminal penalties and civil actions for criminal networks . it allows leaders of any group to be tried for crimes in which they forced others into going along with them . the rico act covers these crimes : - embezzlement - bankruptcy fraud or other fraud - obstruction of justice - copyright infringing - bribery rico was established in 1970. various groups have been threatened with lawsuits or sued under this act .
