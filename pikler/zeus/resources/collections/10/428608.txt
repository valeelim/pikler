Deap Vally     deap vally is a pair of americans , lindsey troy and julie edwards , who make music together . they started their band in los angeles in 2011. lindsey sings and plays the guitar . julie plays the drums . they met at a needlework class that julie edwards was teaching . lindsey troy was performing music on her own at the time . edwards was playing drums and keyboards and singing in a band called the pity party in july 2012 they released their first single `` gon na make my own money '' on ark recordings . the next month the band was signed to island records . their first album `` sistrionix '' was released in june 2013 .
