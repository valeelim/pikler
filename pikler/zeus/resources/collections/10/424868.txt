Dornier Do 17     the dornier do 17 is a nazi german military aircraft . it was used for the world war ii as a bomber , night fighter , and reconnaissance plane . it 's a twin-engines aircraft . it was used by `` luftwaffe '' for battle of france and after battle of britain . maiden flight in 1934. more than 2100 examples were built . do 217 and do 317 are modern variants of it .
