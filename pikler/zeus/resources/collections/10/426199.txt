Manuel &quot;Flaco&quot; Ibáñez    manuel & quot ; flaco & quot ; ibáñez manuel `` flaco '' ibañez ( born manuel ibáñez martínez ; on october 17 , 1946 , in acatlán de pérez figueroa , oaxaca , mexico ) is a mexican actor and comedian . he has appeared in over 140 movies and television shows including `` la red '' .
