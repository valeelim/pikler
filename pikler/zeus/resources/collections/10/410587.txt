TaeTiSeo     girls ' generation-tts ( also known as girls ' generation-taetiseo and taetiseo ) is a pop girl group from south korea . it is a sub-group of girls ' generation . it was formed by s. m. entertainment in may 2012. it is made up of three girls ' generation members , taeyeon , tiffany and seohyun . the name taetiseo is a portmanteau of the girls ' names : taeyeon , tiffany and seohyun .
