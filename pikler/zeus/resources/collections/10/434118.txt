Erotic thriller     an erotic thriller is a form of the thriller genre that involves sexuality . many people liked this from around 1984-85 until the early 1990s . after that , not as many people liked it . it is , however , still popular . an erotic thriller can be a book , a movie , or a television program . examples of erotic thriller movies are `` color of night '' , `` single white female '' ( 1992 ) , `` body of evidence '' and `` crash '' ( 1996 ) .
