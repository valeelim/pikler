Acun Ilıcalı     acun ilıcalı ( born 29 may 1969 , edirne ) is a turkish producer and presenter . he is also the founder and owner of acun medya . acun ilıcalı was born in may 29 , 1969 in edirne . he completed his basic education in edirne . he went to high school in istanbul at kadıköy maarif college and anatolian high school . he enrolled in elt department at istanbul university but he dropped out and decided to work on television . programs . - `` fear factor '' - `` survivor '' - `` var mısın yok musun '' - `` yetenek sizsiniz '' - `` yok böyle dans '' - `` o ses türkiye ''
