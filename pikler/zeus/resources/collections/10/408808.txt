Henry VI, Pt. 3    henry vi , pt . 3 henry vi , pt . 3 is a play by william shakespeare . it is a history play . shakespeare 's source was `` holinshed 's chronicles '' . the play was first performed between 1592 and 1593. it was probably first printed in 1594. it was printed in the first folio of 1623. references . - henry vi , pt . 3
