Issa Rae     issa rae is an american actress and writer . in 2011 she started a youtube comedy series called `` awkward black girl '' . in 2013 it won the shorty award for best web show . forbes put her in their 30 under 30 list in 2013 and 2014. rae was born jo-issa diop . in the third grade , rae entered a program for gifted and talented students . she went to stanford university .
