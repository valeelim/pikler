Applause (Lady Gaga song)    applause ( lady gaga song ) `` applause '' is a 2013 song performed by american singer lady gaga . it was released for her album `` artpop '' . it was released on august 12 , 2013 , a week ahead of its original release date , because it was leaked . `` applause '' is an electropop and eurodance song . it is about being famous and applauded by fans . `` applause '' reached the top five in the united states , canada , and the uk , while reaching the top ten in several other countries . it was certified triple-platinum by the recording industry association of america on may 29 , 2015 , for sales and streams equal to three million copies across the united states .
