Curtis Axel     joseph curtis `` joe '' hennig ( born october 1 , 1979 ) is an american professional wrestler . he is better known by his ring name curtis axel and previous ring name michael mcgillicutty . he currently works for the wwe . he is a former intercontinental champion and former wwe tag team champion with david otunga . he was also a member of the new nexus which was led by cm punk . axel was born in champlin , minnesota . his father was `` mr. perfect '' curt hennig and his grandfather is larry `` the axe '' hennig . he chose his ring name curtis axel as a tribute to them . championships . - florida championship wrestling - fcw florida heavyweight championship ( one time ) - fcw florida tag team championship ( three times ) ( with heath miller ( one ) , brett dibiase ( one ) and kaval ( one ) ) - pro wrestling illustrated - pwi rookie of the year ( 2008 ) - pwi feud of the year ( 2010 ) ( the nexus vs. wwe ) - pwi most hated wrestler of the year ( 2010 ) ( as part of the nexus ) - he was ranked # 47 of the top 500 singles wrestlers in the pwi 500 in 2013 .
