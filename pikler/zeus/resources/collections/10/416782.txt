William Tell Overture     the `` william tell overture '' is a piece of music by gioachino rossini . he wrote it for his opera , `` william tell '' . the opera was first performed in 1829. the overture has four sections : - dawn ; - storm ; - call to the cows ; often used in animated cartoons to signify daybreak - march of the swiss soldiers ; famous for being the theme music for the `` the lone ranger '' .
