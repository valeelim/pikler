Leicester Tigers     leicester tigers are a rugby union club based in leicester , england who play in the aviva premiership . they are the most successful english club with a record 10 championships and are the current champions . the 'tigers ' are one of only four teams ( along with gloucester , bath and wasps ) never to have been relegated from the top flight and are also the most successful english side in europe having made five heineken cup finals , winning back to back titles in 2001 and 2002 .
