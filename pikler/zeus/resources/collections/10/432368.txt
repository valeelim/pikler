Ovo-lacto vegetarianism     ovo-lacto vegetarianism ( alternate : lacto-ovo ) is a diet in which people do not eat the flesh of animals . they do , though , eat eggs and dairy . ovo-lacto vegetarians are the most prevalent types in the western world . such people are well served in restaurants , especially in certain parts of europe and in north america . ovo-lacto vegetarians use this diet sometimes for religious reasons . others use it for health .
