Oz the Great and Powerful     oz the great and powerful is an american fantasy adventure movie from 2013. it is directed by sam raimi , produced by joe roth , and written by david lindsay-abaire and mitchell kapner . it stars james franco as oscar diggs , mila kunis as theodora , rachel weisz as evanora , michelle williams as glinda , zach braff as finley , bill cobbs as master tinker , and joey king as china girl .
