Mandela Day     nelson mandela international day ( or mandela day ) is an international holiday in honor of nelson mandela who has contributed for world peace and conflict resolution . it is celebrated each year on 18 july ( mandela 's birthday ) . the day was officially declared by the united nations general assembly in november 2009. the first un mandela day was held on 18 july 2010. however , other groups had celebrated mandela day the previous year , on 18 july 2009. this was mandela 's 91st birthday . a series of concerts art exhibitions , fundraising and educational events were held . they led up to a concert at radio city music hall .
