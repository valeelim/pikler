OFC Nations Cup     the ofc nations cup is an international football ( soccer ) tournament held among member nations of the oceania football confederation ( ofc ) . it was first held in 1973 , under the name oceania nations cup . it was held every two years from 1996 to 2004 ; since then it has been held every four years . results . - 1973 : new zealand - 1980 : australia - 1996 : australia - 1998 : new zealand - 2000 : australia - 2002 : new zealand - 2004 : australia - 2008 : new zealand - 2012 : tahiti
