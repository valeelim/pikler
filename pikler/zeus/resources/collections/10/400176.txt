Billy Crudup     william gaither `` billy '' crudup ( born july 8 , 1968 ) is an american actor . he is known for his roles in `` almost famous '' , `` big fish '' , `` princess mononoke '' , `` watchmen '' , and `` public enemies '' . crudup was born on july 8 , 1968 in manhasset , new york . he studied at saint thomas aquinas high school , at university of north carolina at chapel hill , and at new york university . he has one son with his ex-girlfriend mary-louise parker .
