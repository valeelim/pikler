Sarah Roemer     sarah roemer ( born august 28 , 1984 ) is an american model and actress . she acts mostly in independent movies . she played lacey kimble in `` the grudge 2 '' , a 2006 movie . she played ashley carlson the following year in `` disturbia '' . she also acted as leila buchanan in the nbc series `` the event '' . roemer was born in san diego , california .
