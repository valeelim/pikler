Television movie     a television movie ( also called a tv film , television film , tv movie , telefilm , telemovie , made-for-television film , direct-to-tv film , movie of the week , feature-length drama , single drama , or original movie ) is a movie that is made for and first shown on a television network . unlike theatrical movies , which are first seen in movie theaters , television movies are first seen on television . some are first shown on television in one country , and in theaters in other countries . one of the most well-known television movies is `` the day after '' , which was seen by over 100 million people when it was first shown on abc on november 20 , 1983 .
