Funeral march     a funeral march ( `` marche funèbre '' , `` trauermarsch '' ) is a march , usually in a minor key , in a slow `` simple duple '' metre , creating the solemn pace of a funeral procession . some such marches are often considered appropriate for use during funerals and other occasions , the most well-known being the third movement of piano sonata no . 2 by frédéric chopin . handel uses the name dead march , also used for marches played by a military band at military funerals and executions . other websites . - chopin 's iconic funeral march
