Juan Javier Espinosa     juan javier espinosa ( 1815 – 4 september 1870 ) was president of ecuador , from 20 january 1868 to 19 january 1869. he succeed pedro josé de arteta as president of ecuador . gabriel garcía moreno would soon succeed espinosa as president . espinosa died on 4 september 1870 in quito , ecuador from a heart attack , aged 55. other websites . - official website of the ecuadorian government about the country president 's history
