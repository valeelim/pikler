Yegor Ligachev     yegor kuzmich ligachev ( , born 29 november 1920 ) is a soviet politician . he was a high-ranking official in the communist party of the soviet union ( cpsu ) . he was the second secretary of the party when mikhail gorbachev was serving as general secretary . ligachev was born on 29 november 1920 in dubinkino . it is close to novosibirsk . more reading . - `` inside gorbachev 's kremlin : the memoirs of yegor ligachev '' . pantheon books : 1993 ( isbn 0-679-41392-8 ) - `` ligachev on glasnost and perestroika '' . `` carl beck papers in russian and east european studies '' , no . 706 : 1989 .
