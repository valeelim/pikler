Duke of Norfolk     the duke of norfolk is an aristocrat or peer in the united kingdom . he is earl marshal in the house of lords . he has a family home at arundel castle in west sussex . his family have long been catholic . they paid for the building of arundel cathedral . the title dates to 1397. as of 2013 the holder of the title is the 18th duke , edward fitzalan-howard . he took the title in 2002 .
