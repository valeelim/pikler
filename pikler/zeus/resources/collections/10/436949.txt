Věra Tichánková     věra tichánková ( 7 december 1920 – 9 january 2014 ) was a czech actress . she was born in zarnovická hut , nová bana , czechoslovakia ( now slovakia ) . tichánková studied at the institute of chemical technology in prague . she began her acting career in 1940 which lasted over seventy years . věra tichánková died on 9 january 2014 , aged 93 , in prague , czech republic . she was survived by her husband , actor and playwright jan skopeček .
