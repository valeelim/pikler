Roger Squires     roger squires ( born 22 february 1932 ) is a british crossword maker . he lives in shropshire with his wife anna . he has made more crosswords than anyone else in the world . he makes the monday crossword for `` the telegraph '' . on 22 february 2012 , it was squires ' 80th birthday . `` the guardian '' and `` financial times '' held a party for him at `` the guardian '' 's offices in london . `` the telegraph '' held a lunch party for him in birmingham . john galbraith graham wrote him a sonnet . squires has two sons . he is married to anna squires .
