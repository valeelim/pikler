Jesus Iglesias Cortes     jesus iglesias cortes ( born december 25 , 1968 in barcelona ) is a swimmer from spain . he had a disability : he is an s7 type swimmer . he raced at the 1996 summer paralympics . he finished first in the 100 meter breaststroke . he finished second in the 4 x 50 meter 20 points freestyle relay race . he finished third in the 50 meter s7 freestyle race and the 4 x 50 meter 20 points medley relay race .
