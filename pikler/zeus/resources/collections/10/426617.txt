Little Earthquakes     little earthquakes is the first solo album by tori amos . five singles were released from it . the album got good reviews from music critics . it is the only tori amos album in the book `` 1001 albums you must hear before you die '' . `` me and a gun '' was released as the first single from `` little earthquakes '' . it is a song about when amos was raped in los angeles when she was 21 . `` silent all these years '' was released as the second single . it reached 21 in the uk singles chart . `` china '' was released as the third single from the album . the album 's fourth single `` winter '' was released march 9 , 1992 in the united kingdom . it reached 25 in the uk singles chart . `` crucify '' was the album 's last single . it reached 15 in the uk singles chart .
