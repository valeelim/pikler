Eugenio Andres Santana Santana     eugenio andres santana santana ( born december 2 , 1959 in las palmas ) is a judo athlete from spain . he has a disability : he is blind and is a b2 type athlete . he competed at the 1996 summer paralympics in judo . he finished third in the 78 kg group . he competed at the 2000 summer paralympics in judo . he finished 5 in the up to open group .
