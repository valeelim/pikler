Money plant     money plant is the trivial name of several plants : - `` epipremnum aureum '' - a tropical vining plant from the solomon islands - `` lunaria annua '' - a herbaceous biennial , grown for its attractive spring flowering and dried silver seed pod middles - `` crassula ovata '' - a small plant with fleshy leaves , also known as a jade plant or a friendship tree - `` pilea peperomioides '' - a small plant with very round , dark green leafs , also known as chinese money plant , lefse plant , or missionary plant and is from the south of china
