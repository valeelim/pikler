Alice Babs     alice babs ( born hildur alice nilsson ; 26 january 1924 – 11 february 2014 ) was a sweedish singer and actress from kalmar , sweden . while she worked in a wide number of genres – swedish folklore , elizabethan songs and opera . she was best known internationally as a jazz singer , and for being swedens first entrant in eurovision singing the song `` lilla stjärna '' in the eurovision song contest 1958. babs died of complications from alzheimer 's disease on 11 february 2014. she was 90. other websites . - jazzbrat : alice babs
