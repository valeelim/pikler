Erik Solbakken     erik solbakken ( born 17 november 1984 in hemsedal ) is a norwegian television presenter . solbakken hosted the eurovision song contest 2010 together with haddy jatou n'jie and nadia hasnaoui . he has hosted multiple children 's shows including `` barne-tv '' , `` julemorgen '' , '' krem nasjonal '' , and `` superkviss '' , as well as `` barnetimen for de minste '' on nrk p2 . erik solbakken hosted the prestigious idrettsgallaen with haddy n'jie 2010 from hamar . in 2010 he is ready for one more golden moment - `` popstokk '' , the norwegian version of `` battlepop '' , airing spring prime time friday nights at nrk . in 2011 , he hosted the eurovision young dancers competition 2011 .
