Goldfrapp     goldfrapp is alison goldfrapp and will gregory , two electronic musicians . they have released six studio albums . in 2005 their single `` ooh la la '' reached number four in the uk singles chart . in 1999 goldfrapp were signed to mute records . in 2000 they released their first album `` felt mountain '' . pitchfork gave the album a score of 8 out of 10. it reached number 57 in the uk albums chart . in 2003 goldfrapp 's second album `` black cherry '' was released . it entered the uk albums chart at number 19. in 2005 their third album `` supernature '' was released . the album went straight to number two in the uk . it was nominated for best electronic/dance album at the 2007 grammy awards . in februar 2012 goldfrapp released `` the singles '' . the album has twelve of goldfrapp 's singles from earlier albums and two new songs . in 2014 goldfrapp will nine concerts in the uk . discography . - `` felt mountain '' ( 2000 ) - `` black cherry '' ( 2003 ) - `` supernature '' ( 2005 ) - `` seventh tree '' ( 2008 ) - `` head first '' ( 2010 ) - `` tales of us '' ( 2013 )
