I, Frankenstein    i , frankenstein i , frankenstein is a 2014 australian-american fantasy action movie written and directed by stuart beattie . it is based on the graphic novel and original screenplay by kevin grevioux . it stars aaron eckhart , bill nighy , yvonne strahovski , miranda otto , socratis otto , jai courtney and kevin grevioux . it was released on january 24 , 2014 .
