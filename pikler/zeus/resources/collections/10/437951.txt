Pelkosenniemi     pelkosenniemi is a municipality in finnish lapland , finland . it had almost 1,000 people living there in august 2013. it has the smallest population among municipalities in lapland . the municipalities next to it are kemijärvi , rovaniemi , salla , savukoski , and sodankylä . aapajärvi , arvospuoli , kairala , kiemunkivaara , luiro , pelkosenniemi , pyhäjärvi , saunavaara and suvanto . other websites . - the official pelkosenniemi page
