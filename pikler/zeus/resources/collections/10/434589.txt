Binyavanga Wainaina     binyavanga wainaina ( born 18 january 1971 ) is a kenyan writer . in july 2002 he won the caine prize for african writing for his short story `` discovering home '' . he started `` kwani ? '' magazine . his first book , `` one day i will write about this place '' , was published in 2011. wainaina was born in nakuru . in 2014 he tweeted that he is gay .
