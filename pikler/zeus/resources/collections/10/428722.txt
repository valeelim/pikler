Mansfield College, Oxford    mansfield college , oxford mansfield college is one of the colleges of the university of oxford . it was founded in 1838 as spring hill college in birmingham . spring hill college moved to oxford in 1886. at that time it was renamed mansfield college , in honor of its largest donors , george and elizabeth mansfield . the college 's main building was designed by basil champneys , and was completed in 1889. the first female student at mansfield was admitted in 1913. in 1955 , mansfield was named a permanent private hall at oxford university . in 1995 , mansfield was granted a royal charter making it a full member of the university .
