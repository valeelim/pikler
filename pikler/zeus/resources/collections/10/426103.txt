Constantine Mitsotakis     constantine mitsotakis or konstantinos mitsotakis ( 18 october 1918 – 29 may 2017 ) was a greek politician . he is the former prime minister of greece from 11 april 1990 through 13 october 1993. he was born in chania , crete , to a political family : his father and grandfathers were members of parliament , and the great liberal leader eleftherios venizelos was his uncle . he graduated in law and economics from the university of athens . he was married to marika mitsotakis ( née giannoukou ) from 1953 until her death on 6 may 2012. they had four children . in january 2004 mitsotakis announced that he would retire from parliament at the 7 march election , 56 years after his first election . mitsotakis died on 29 may 2017 in athens , aged 98. other websites . - the constantine k. mitsotakis foundation/ίδρυμα κωνσταντίνος μητσοτάκης
