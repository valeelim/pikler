God's Gonna Cut You Down    god 's gon na cut you down `` god 's gon na cut you down '' , also known as `` run on '' and `` run on for a long time '' , is a traditional folk song which has been recorded by many artists representing many genres . the lyrics are a warning to sinners that no matter how hard they try , they will not avoid god 's judgment . johnny cash sang this song for an album called `` '' ( 2006 ) .
