Flan     a flan is a kind of tart found in british cuisine . it consists of an open pastry or sponge case holding a sweet or savoury filling . a typical flan is round , with shortcrust pastry . flan brillat-savarin , a savoury flan named in honour of the french gastronome jean anthelme brillat-savarin : 1. bake blind a savoury flan case 1. fill the cooked flan case with a mixture of scrambled eggs and truffles 1. slice more truffles , heat in butter , seson , and arrange on the egg mixture . 1. sprinkle with grated parmesan cheese , and put in an oven or under a grill ( broiler ) to brown well . related pages . - quiche
