The Client     the client is a 1994 american melodramatic legal crime drama movie . it was directed by joel schumacher . it is based on the novel of the same name . this is about murder , suicide and mafia activities in new orleans . brad renfro plays mark sway . tommy lee jones plays reverend foltrigg . susan sarandon plays regina love . mary-louise parker plays dianne . the movie was released on july 20 , 1994. it got positive reviews from most critics . it made a lot of money .
