Armide     armide is an opera . the music was written by jean-baptiste lully . the words were written by philippe quinault . the story of the opera is based on `` jerusalem delivered '' by torquato tasso . it was first performed february 15 , 1686 by the paris opéra at the théâtre du palais-royal . it was revived several times in the 18th century . the story is about an enchantress named armide and her love for a knight named renaud .
