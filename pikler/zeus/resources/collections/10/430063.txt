Ray Price     noble ray price ( january 12 , 1926 – december 16 , 2013 ) was an american country music singer , songwriter and guitarist . some of his famous songs were `` release me '' , `` crazy arms '' , `` heartaches by the number '' , `` for the good times '' , `` night life '' , and `` you 're the best thing that ever happened to me '' . he was elected to the country music hall of fame in 1996 and—even into his late 80s—continued to record and tour . price died at his home in mt . pleasant , texas on december 16 , 2013 from pancreatic cancer . other websites . - the `` ray price shuffle '' with audio examples - at the country music hall of fame - allmusic ray price with biography , discography , charts
