Natascha Kampusch     natascha maria kampusch ( born 17 february 1988 ) is an austrian woman who was kidnapped on 2 march 1998. she was held in a secret cellar by her kidnapper for over eight years until she escaped on 23 august 2006. media attention then led to natascha giving certain interviews and writing an autobiography . her abductor later killed himself when he jumped in front of a suburban train in vienna ; he had apparently planned the killing of himself rather than be caught by police , and had told kampusch he would not be arrested alive .
