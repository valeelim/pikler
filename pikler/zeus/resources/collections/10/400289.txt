José Manuel Fernández Barranquero     jose manuel fernandez barranquero ( born march 19 , 1975 in málaga ) is a track and field athlete from spain . he has a physical disability : he a t46 type athlete . he raced at the 1992 summer paralympics in barcelona . he raced at the 1996 summer paralympics in atlanta , georgia , united states . he raced at the 2000 summer paralympics in sydney , australia . he finished second in the 4 x 400 meter t42-t46 race and finished third in the 800 meter t46 race .
