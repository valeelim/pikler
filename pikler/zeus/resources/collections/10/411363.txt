Parliament of Sweden     the parliament of sweden ( ) is the legislative government of sweden . the parliament building is on the stockholm island of helgeandsholmen . the parliament has 349 seats since 1976 ( 350 between 1971 and 1976 ) . between 1867 and 1971 , the parliament had two chambers , but has been reduced to one . elections are held in september every fourth year ( third year between 1970-1994 ) . other websites . - parliament of sweden
