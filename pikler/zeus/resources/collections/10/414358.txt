Ardennes (forest)    ardennes ( forest ) the ardennes ( also known as ardennes forest ) is a mountain range in eastern belgium ( eastern wallonia ) , northern luxembourg ( the oesling ) and bordering france ( ardennes ( department ) ) . it consist of hills of an altitude up to nearly 700 meters . it has many forests and few people . the ardennes are primarily made of slate , and borders in the east at the german eifel . important river is the meuse .
