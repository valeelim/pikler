ClariS     claris is a japanese pop music duo and idol unit . the members are high-school girls clara and alice from hokkaido . claris was originally known as `` alice clara '' . claris began singing covers of songs mostly featured in anime and songs originally sung by vocaloids . they posted their cover song in nico nico douga video sharing website between october 2009 and june 2010. in september 2010 , claris was signed to sme records . they released their first single `` irony '' in october 2010. that song was used as the opening theme to the 2010 anime `` oreimo '' . claris released six more singles between 2011 and 2013. their debut album `` birthday '' was released in april 2012. other websites . - official site - official website as alice clara
