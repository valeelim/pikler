Manuel Robles Aguila     manuel robles aguila ( born march 6 , 1959 in monachil , granada ) is a table tennis athlete from spain . he has a physical disability : he is class 5 player . he played table tennis at the 1996 summer paralympics , 2000 summer paralympics and 2004 summer paralympics . in 2000 , he finished third in the open 1-5 singles table tennis game .
