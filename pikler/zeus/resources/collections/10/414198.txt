Rindr     in norse mythology , rindr is a jotun . she is the daughter of billing , king of the ruthenians ; mate of the god odin ; mother of vali . she appears only once in the existing norse manuscripts , as the temporary wife of odin . rinda is a personification of the hard , frozen rind of the earth . at first , she resists the woohooing of odin ( the sun ) but finally succumbs to his warmth and gives birth to vali , the light god of spring . and bous , who would later grow up to balderus .
