Tarzan Finds a Son!    tarzan finds a son ! tarzan finds a son ! is a 1939 jungle adventure tarzan movie . it is based on the fictional characters and concepts created by edgar rice burroughs . it is the fourth of the six mgm tarzan movies starring johnny weissmuller and maureen o'sullivan . johnny sheffield makes his debut as boy in this movie . an airplane flying to cape town crashes in the jungle . everyone is killed , except a baby . cheeta rescues the baby . tarzan and jane adopt him . they name him `` boy '' . five years later , a search party comes to the jungle looking for boy . he is the heir to a fortune worth millions . jane wants boy to go back to civilization . in the end though , boy stays with tarzan and jane in the jungle . related pages . - tarzan escapes , prequel to this movie - tarzan 's secret treasure , sequel to this movie
