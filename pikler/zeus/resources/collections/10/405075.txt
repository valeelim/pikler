Avenue Franklin Roosevelt     avenue franklin roosevelt is a street in brussels , ixelles . it is next parallel to bois de la cambre , a forest in brussels . many houses there were built in the second world war . it is known as one of the most beautiful boulevards in brussels . the ixelles community opposes that people build shops or hotels and apartment buildings have to be no bigger than 16 meters high . there are many embassies and residences of ambasadors located on avenue franklin roosevelt . the avenue was named `` avenue des nations '' in 1922 , after leopold ii came the visit de universal exposition from 1910. he said it should be a residential area . in 1945 , de `` avenue des nations '' becomes avenue franklin roosevelt , in honor of the president of america . notable buildings . - the villa empain is notable as the boghossian foundation is taking place there with different exhibitions . - the villa bernheim was occupied by émile bernheim , former president of the group à l'innovation and now is an art gallery .
