Division of Canberra     the division of canberra is an australian electoral division in the australian capital territory . it was created in 1974 from the old division of australian capital territory . it is named for the city of canberra , which is australia 's national capital . it includes the areas of tuggeranong , fyshwick , weston creek and woden valley , along with the rest of the act south of the molonglo river and lake burley griffin . it also includes people living on norfolk island . election results . - 2004 election results - 2007 election results - 2010 election results - 2013 election results other websites . - division of canberra â€“ australian electoral commission - gai brodtmann 's web site
