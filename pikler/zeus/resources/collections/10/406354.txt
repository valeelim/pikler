The Slave's Friend    the slave 's friend the slave 's friend was a 19th-century american monthly children 's magazine . it was published in new york city , new york by the american anti-slavery society beginning in 1835 and ending some time before 1839. thirty-eight issues were published . single copies cost 1 cent , but the magazine was made available in public places for free . the 16-page , tiny magazine ( 4.5 '' h x 2.75 '' ) was considered dangerous reading by anti-abolitionists . copies were destroyed in charleston , south carolina and philadelphia , pennsylvania .
