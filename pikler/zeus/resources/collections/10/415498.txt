Ian &quot;H&quot; Watkins    ian & quot ; h & quot ; watkins ian `` h '' watkins ( born 8 may 1976 ) is a welsh singer , dancer and actor . he was born in cwmparc . he is best known for being a member of the british pop group steps . he has said that `` h '' stands for `` hyperactive '' , describing his character . after steps broke up in 2001 , he formed a singing duo with claire richards , who was also a steps member . they released their music under the name h & claire . in january 2007 he came out as gay . in the same month , he was a housemate in `` celebrity big brother '' .
