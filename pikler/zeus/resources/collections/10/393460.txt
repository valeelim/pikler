Assumption College     assumption college ( ) , is a private catholic school for boys in thailand . it was founded in 1885 by father emile august colombet , and is the third-oldest school in thailand . assumption college is in bang rak , bangkok . the school is one of the four schools in jaturamitr samakkee , a traditional football match by four oldest boys ' schools in thailand including suankularb wittayalai school , debsirin school and bangkok christian college . the list of graduates includes four former prime ministers of thailand , several businessmen , fifteens privy counselors , etc . a lot of students are chinese thais and the first student of school name mr. siew meng tek is also chinese-thai . assumption college is the one of most famous schools in thailand . as of today , more than 50,000 students have been educated at assumption college . other websites . - assumption college official website
