Mars Attacks!    mars attacks ! mars attacks ! is a 1996 movie directed by tim burton . it stars jack nicholson , glenn close , natalie portman , annette bening , sarah jessica parker , martin short , pierce brosnan , rod steiger , danny devito , and sylvia sidney . it was released on december 13 , 1996 to mixed reviews . it was nominated for many saturn awards . the movie was based on playing cards about martians taking over earth .
