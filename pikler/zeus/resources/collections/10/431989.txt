Warren Skorodenski     warren skorodenski ( born march 22 , 1960 in winnipeg , manitoba ) is a canadian retired professional ice hockey goaltender . frederick played a total of 35 games in the national hockey league ( nhl ) with the chicago black hawks and edmonton oilers . he played for the new brunswick hawks , the ahl affiliate of the black hawks . during that time , he was able to win the harry `` hap '' holmes memorial award for being the goalie with the lowest goals against average and shared the award with his teammate bob janecyk . he was called up by the black hawks during the 1981-82 nhl season . he played 1 game and was sent back down to the ahl where he played 4 seasons and 1 season in the chl with the birmingham south stars . during the 1984-85 nhl season , he was called up and played as murray bannerman backup . he recorded the league 's highest save percentage , at 0.903. in 1987 , he signed a contract with the edmonton oilers and played 3 games with them . he retired from playing professional ice hockey in 1990 .
