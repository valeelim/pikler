Snuff (song)    snuff ( song ) snuff is the fifth single from the album `` all hope is gone '' by metal band slipknot . it was released on september 28 , 2009. the song reached # 2 on billboard 's hot mainstream rock tracks . it was the last single that was released from the band before the death of their bassist , paul gray . vocalist corey taylor performs an acoustic version of the song as a tribute to paul gray during stone sour shows . the song was nominated for best single at the kerrang ! awards 2010 , but it lost to `` liquid confidence '' by you me at six .
