Lightsaber     a lightsaber is a weapon used in the fictional star wars universe . lightsabers are usually only used by the good jedi and the evil sith , and not really anyone else . a lightsaber is kind of like a sword , but instead of having a blade made out of metal , lightsabers have something kind of like a laser beam for the blade . this beam is very powerful , and can cut through anything . the lightsaber 's blade can be red , blue , green , or purple . the sith usually have red lightsabers , and the jedi have the other three colors . in some of the expanded universe books , there are lightsabers that are other colors too .
