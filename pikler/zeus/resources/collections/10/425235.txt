Shed     a shed is a simple , single-storey structure in a garden which is used for storage . some people use their shed to do hobbies or as a workshop . there are many types of sheds . some sheds are small open-sided structures with tin rooves . some sheds are large structures framed by wood . some sheds are made out of plastic . sheds used on farms can be large and tall structures . sheds can be made with metal panels attached to a metal frame , plastic or vinyl panels attached to a frame , all-wood construction .
