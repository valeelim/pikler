Commission     commission can refer to several things : - commission ( remuneration ) , a form of payment to somebody for a job they have done - commission ( art ) , when somebody orders the creation of a piece of art - a type of contract for performance or creation of a specific work - the document appointing someone as an officer - a group of officials - statutory commission , a government authority created by acts of the legislature ( for which , see statutory authority ) - constitutional commission , a group of people who are ordered to review or write a constitution - european commission , the executive body of the european union - royal commission , a public inquiry
