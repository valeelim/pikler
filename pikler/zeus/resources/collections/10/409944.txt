José Antonio Mari Alcaraz     josé antonio mari alcaraz ( born october 23 , 1988 in valencia ) is a swimmer from spain . he has a physical disability : he is an s10 type swimmer . in 2012 , he lived in san cugat del vallés , barcelona . he raced at the 2008 summer paralympics . he raced at the 2012 summer paralympics . he finished third in the 50 meter s10 freestyle race .
