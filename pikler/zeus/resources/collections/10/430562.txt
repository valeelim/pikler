Gottfried Böhm     gottfried böhm ( born january 23 , 1920 ) is a german architect . he is currently the only german architect to be honored with a pritzker prize . famous buildings . - st. columba church , in cologne , germany ( 1947-1950 ) - pilgrimage church , in neviges , germany ( 1968-1972 ) - christi auferstehung ( church of resurrection ) , in cologne ( 1968-1970 ) - bensburg town hall , germany ( 1964-1969 ) other websites . - pritzker prize page on gottfried böhm
