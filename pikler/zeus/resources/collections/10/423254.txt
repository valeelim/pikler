Postage stamps and postal history of Macau     this is a survey of the postage stamps and postal history of macau . the first stamps issued for macau by the portuguese was in 1884. the common `` portuguese crown '' design was used and nine values ranging from 5 to 300 reis were made . when macau became a special administrative region of the people 's republic of china , all stamps with `` macau , portuguese republic '' ( ) were no longer used . instead , `` macau , china '' was printed on the stamp . further reading . - `` yang 's catalog of the postage stamps and postal stationery of macau '' ( 1998 ) . other websites . - encyclopaedia of postal authorities , macao ( macau ) .
