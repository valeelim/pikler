Dave Lee Travis     dave lee travis ( born 25 may 1945 ) , also known as dlt , is a british radio and television presenter . he was born in buxton , derbyshire . he started being a presenter for bbc radio 1 in 1967. from 1981 to 2001 , travis presented `` a jolly good show '' . travis married his wife marianne in 1971. he said he has cheated on her `` once or twice '' . travis was arrested on 15 november 2012 as part of operation yewtree , an investigation into sexual abuse by jimmy savile and other people . travis is currently on trial for 14 counts of indecent assault and one count of sexual assault . he has pleaded not guilty to all charges . travis told police that if he had known savile was a pedophile he would have reported him . travis was cleared of all but two charges on 13 february 2014. he was retried on those two charges and another charge . on 23 september 2014 , he was convicted of one count of indecent assault .
