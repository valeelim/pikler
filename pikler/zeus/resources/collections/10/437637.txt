I Believe I Can Fly     `` i believe i can fly '' is a song by r & b singer r. kelly . it was originally released on november 26 , 1996. it was also featured on the soundtrack to the movie `` space jam '' in 1996. it later appeared in kelly 's album `` r. '' in 1998. the song peaked at number one in many countries around the world . the song won three grammy awards out of five nominations .
