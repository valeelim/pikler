Pixie Geldof     little pixie geldof ( born 17 september 1990 ) is a british model and singer-songwriter . she was born in london to paula yates . she is the youngest daughter of bob geldof and the second youngest of yates ' four daughters . when she was 17 she was on the cover of `` tatler '' magazine . this was the first magazine cover she was on . she has a band called violet . she is the singer and writes the songs . they released their first single in 2012 .
