Stenian     the stenian is the last geological period in the mesoproterozoic . it began 1.2 billion years ago , and ended at 1 billion years ago . before it was the ectasian . it is followed by the tonian . the supercontinent rodinia began forming in this period . fossils of the first known sexually-reproducing eukaryotes are found at the end of the previous period in the hunting formation on somerset island , nunavut , canada . this period includes the keweenawan age at about 1100 mya .
