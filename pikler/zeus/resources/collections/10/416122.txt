Abell 2029     abell 2029 or a2029 is a large cluster of galaxies 315 billion light years away in the constellation virgo . its central galaxy , ic 1101 , is the largest galaxy known . estimates ranging from 5.6 to over 6 million light years across . contrast this with the milky way , which is 100,000 light years across . a2029 is also about 81 times bigger than the milky way , with a luminosity of 2 l. this type of galaxy may have grown to its large size by accreting nearby galaxies . other websites . - the scale of the universe ( astronomy picture of the day 2012 march 12 ) - curious about astronomy ? , `` what is the largest galaxy ? `` , cornell university
