Carmel-by-the-Sea, California    carmel-by-the-sea , california carmel-by-the-sea , often called simply carmel , is a small city in monterey county , california , united states . it was founded in 1902 and incorporated on october 31 , 1916. carmel is on the monterey peninsula . it is known for its natural scenery and rich artistic history . actor clint eastwood was mayor of carmel-by-the-sea for two years .
