Agoura High School     agoura high school is a public high school in agoura hills , california , united states . it is the largest school in the las virgenes unified school district , which covers the western section of los angeles county . in `` newsweek '' 's 2007 poll of the best high schools in the united states , agoura high school ranked 1173th . the current principal of the school is brian mercer . other websites . - official website
