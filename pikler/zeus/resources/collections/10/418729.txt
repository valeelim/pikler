Arrondissement of Toulouse     the arrondissement of toulouse is an arrondissement of france . it is part of the haute-garonne `` département '' in the occitanie region . its capital , and préfecture of the department , is the city of toulouse . when the haute-garonne department was created on 17 february 1800 , the `` arrondissement '' of toulouse was part of that original department and was made the capital ( préfecture ) of it . it is the most northern of the `` arrondissements '' of the department , with an area of . it is the most populated `` arrondissement '' with 1,024,517 inhabitants and a density of inhabitants/km² the `` arrondissement '' is bordered to the north by the tarn-et-garonne department , to the northeast by the tarn department , to the southeast by the aude department , to the south by the ariège department , to the southwest by the `` arrondissement '' of muret and to the west by the gers department . after the reorganisation of the cantons in france , cantons are not subdivisions of the `` arrondissements '' so they could have `` communes '' that belong to different `` arrondissements '' .
