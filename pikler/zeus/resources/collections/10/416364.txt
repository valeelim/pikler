Guide skier     a guide skier is a person who helps skiers who can not see by telling them where to go . they communicate by using a radio or talking to them . the guide skier can see . skiers can use more than one guide during a race type . the guide can only win a medal if they raced with the same skier for the whole race type . like the skier , the guide has to have an ipcas licence in order to compete in races . guide skiers need to follow to anti-doping rules . skiers follow the guide skier in the biathlon .
