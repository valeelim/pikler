Kabsa     kabsa is the national dish of saudi arabia . it is a dish made of rice and meat , cooked either separately or together . it can be made with spices and camel meat , lamb , chicken or fish . it is usually eaten for lunch and sometimes for dinner . kabsa is often a dish for a group of people . the kabsa is normally served on a round metal dish placed on the floor and people eat with their right hand , although a spoon may be used . in saudi arabia there are many different ways to make kabsa . kabsa is also eaten in other middle eastern countries such as kuwait , bahrain and other gulf countries .
