Cyclic compound     in chemistry a cyclic compound is a molecule where some atoms are linked together forming a loop or ring . most of the time , these molecules are organic . there are also many examples of inorganic molecule forming rings . cyclic compounds can be aromatic : in this case they are flat . benzene is a simple aromatic cyclic compound . when they are not aromatic , rings tend not to be flat to avoid steric effects . cyclic compounds are very important . many drugs are based on them , like aspirin . there are many reactions that can make or break cyclic compounds . one important class of these is pericyclic reactions .
