Amitabh Bachchan     amitabh harivansh bachchan is an indian movie actor . he has been in over 180 indian movies in more than forty years . bachchan is widely thought of as one of the greatest and most influential actors in the history of indian cinema . bachchan has won many major awards in his career . his awards include three national film awards as best actor and fourteen filmfare awards . bachchan made his hollywood debut in 2013 with `` the great gatsby '' .
