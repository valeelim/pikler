Pope Anacletus     pope anacletus , also sometimes referred to as pope cletus , was the third pope of the catholic church , ( the official title is bishop of rome ) . he served from 76-88 c.e . he established a christian community in ruvo , apulia , where he baptized christian followers in secret . after the death of the second pope , linus , anacletus returned to rome . he divided the area into what would today be called parishes or small groups . he established a small chapel near the burial sites of peter and linus which is the site of today 's basilica of st. peter . he was killed during the reign of the emperor domitian for being a christian leader . he was also buried near peter and linus .
