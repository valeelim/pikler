Kamala Harris     kamala devi harris ( born october 20 , 1964 ) is an american lawyer and politician . she is the current united states senator from california . she was the attorney general of california from 2011 to 2017. she was elected in 2010. harris ran for the united states senate seat of retiring barbara boxer , in the 2016 california senate elections . she won the election with 63 % of the vote . she was born in oakland , california . harris is the state of california 's first african-american female attorney general . her mother is indian and her father is jamaican american . other websites . - official website
