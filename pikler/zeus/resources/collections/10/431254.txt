Peppered corydoras     the peppered corydoras ( `` corydoras paleatus '' ) is a freshwater catfish . the peppered corydoras live at the bottom of the fish tank . they are peaceful community fish . they like to live in a temperature of around 25–28°c . they should be kept in groups of other corydoras . they grow to about 7 centimetres . the peppered corydoras are scavengers . they eat the fish food after it sinks to the bottom of the tank . other websites . - http : //www.aquaticcommunity.com/fish/pepperedcory.php - http : //www.seriouslyfish.com/species/corydoras-paleatus/
