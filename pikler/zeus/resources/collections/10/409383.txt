Translocation     translocation may mean : - translocation ( genetics ) , an abnormality of chromosomes - robertsonian translocation , a chromosomal rearrangement in pairs 13 , 14 , 15 , 21 , and 22 - nonreciprocal translocation , transfer of genes from one chromosome to another - pep group translocation , a method used by bacteria for absorbing sugar - translocation ( botany ) , the transport of food through the phloem in plants - protein translocation , a process in protein biosynthesis - species translocation , movement of a species , by people , from one area to another
