Passbook (application)    passbook ( application ) passbook is an application in ios that lets users to keep coupons , boarding passes , event tickets , store cards , 'generic ' cards , and other forms of mobile payment . it was made by apple and announced at the 2012 apple worldwide developers conference on june 11 , 2012. the application was released with ios 6 on september 19 , 2012. the passbook application shows the following kinds of 2d barcodes : aztec , pdf417 , and qr . each digital coupon or ticket is called a `` pass '' . when the user starts passbook for the first time , a short introduction screen will appear with a button inviting users to find applications on the app store that integrate passbook . passes can also be distributed with the internet via safari or sent to another user using e-mail . passes are synced between ios devices using icloud . os x 10.8.2 and newer also support opening passes to be sent to users ' ios devices . the application is available in ios 6 or later . it is currently only available on iphone and ipod touch , but not on ipad .
