Rossana Podestà     rossana podestà ( 20 june 1934 – 10 december 2013 ) was an italian actress . she was known for her roles in `` ulysses '' and `` helen of troy '' . she was born in tripoli , in the italian colony of libya on 20 june 1934. she was married to walter bonatti until his death in 2011. podestà died from brain cancer in rome , italy , aged 79. some movies . - 1950 : `` strano appuntamento '' - 1951 : `` domani è un altro giorno '' - 1951 : `` the seven dwarfs to the rescue '' - 1952 : `` il moschettiere fantasma '' - 1955 : `` ulysses '' - 1958 : `` helen of troy '' - 1964 : `` le ore nude '' - 1974 : `` the sensual man '' - 1985 : `` segreti , segreti ''
