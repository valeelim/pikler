María Beatriz Mendoza Rivera     maria beatriz mendoza rivera ( born november 18 , 1975 in santa maría de guía ) is a track and field athlete from spain . she has a disability : she is blind and a t12/b2 type athlete . she raced at the 1996 summer paralympics . she finished first in the 100 meter t12 race and she finished first in the 200 meter t12 race . she raced at the 2004 summer paralympics and finished second in the 200 meter race and third in the 100 meter race .
