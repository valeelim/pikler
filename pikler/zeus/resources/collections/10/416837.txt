Moro National Liberation Front     the moro national liberation front ( mnlf ) is a rebel group in the philippines . it was started by nur misuari in 1971. the mnlf fights against the philippine government for the independence of the bangsamoro land . over the years there have been several attempts to achieve peace between the mnlf and the government . in 1976 a treaty was arranged by the organization of islamic cooperation but this failed . in 1986 president corazon aquino met with misuari , and in 1989 the area was given a level of autonomy . president fidel ramos arranged a peace deal in 1996 which allowed misuari to be elected as regional governor . misuari led an uprising against the federal government in 2001 , but this failed and he was sent to prison . the mnlf says that the territory of bangsamoro land covers sulu , mindanao , palawan and sabah ( in malaysia ) . the mnlf is an observer member of the organisation of islamic cooperation . other websites . - the long struggle to silence the guns of rebellion : a review of the long and winding trail to the elusive peace agreements by the censei report
