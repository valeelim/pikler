Mulholland Falls     mulholland falls is a 1996 american neo-noir crime drama movie . john malkovich plays thomas timms , melanie griffith stars as katherine hoover and jennifer connelly plays allison pond . the movie is set in the 1950s in los angeles . it is about different types of organized crimes happening in the area . this movie was released april 1996 for the united states . the reviews were excellent . the box office intake was rather poor : the movie only got back almost $ 12 million , roughly half its budget of $ 29 million .
