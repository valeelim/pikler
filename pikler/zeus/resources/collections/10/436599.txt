Luis Raúl     luis raúl martínez ( march 6 , 1962 – february 2 , 2014 ) , better known by his stage name luis raúl , was a puerto rican actor , comedian , and host . born in ponce , he started working in 1983. he most often worked as a stand-up comic . raúl died from bilateral pneumonia on february 2 , 2014 , aged 51 , in san juan . he arrived at hospital on january 13 and his condition worsened two weeks later .
