The Kane Chronicles     the kane chronicles are a series of fantasy novels by rick riordan . the characters are based on egyptian mythology . the story is set in the same universe as riordan 's `` percy jackson & the olympians and the heroes of olympus '' series . characters . - carter kane : one of the heroes of the series . is one of the two most powerful magicians that ever lived . sadie kane 's brother , amos kane 's nephew and julius and ruby kane 's son - sadie kane : one of the heroes in the series . carter kane 's sister , amos kane 's niece and julius and ruby kane 's daughter . is one of the two most powerful magicians that ever lived . - amos kane : magician of the house of life . julius kane 's brother and carter and sadie kane 's uncle .
