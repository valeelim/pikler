Ricky Jay     richard jay potash ( born 1948 ) is an american stage magician , actor , and writer . he is known for his card tricks , card throwing , memory feats , and stage patter . he acted in many movies including `` boogie nights '' , `` the prestige '' , `` heist '' , and `` magnolia '' . jay was born in 1948 in brooklyn , new york to a jewish family . he is married to chrisann verges . other websites . - ricky jay archive at the magic newswire website - san francisco chronicle review of `` extraordinary exhibitions : broadsides from the collection of ricky jay '' during 2005 exhibition - `` secrets of magus '' 1993 `` new yorker '' profile
