Adventures of Captain Marvel     adventures of captain marvel is a 12-chapter movie serial released in 1941 by rko radio pictures . it was produced by republic studios . it stars tom tyler as captain marvel and frank coughlan , jr. as billy batson . it was directed by william witney and john english . references . - weiss , ken and ed goodgold . 1972 . `` to be continued ...
