It's My Party (song)    it 's my party ( song ) `` it 's my party '' is a song by lesley gore from 1963. the song is about a teenage girl during a party on her birthday . her boyfriend , johnny , goes missing during the party . he comes back with another girl , judy , who is wearing his ring . the girl johnny left becomes so unhappy that she cries . the song was written in 1962. the single peaked near # 1 for the pop and rhythm and blues musical charts . other musicians have recorded the song .
