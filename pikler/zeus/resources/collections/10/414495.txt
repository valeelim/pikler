The World's End (movie)    the world 's end ( movie ) the world 's end is a 2013 british science-fiction comedy movie directed by edgar wright . it stars simon pegg , nick frost , paddy considine , martin freeman , and eddie marsan . it is the third in the `` three flavours cornetto trilogy '' , following `` shaun of the dead '' ( 2004 ) and `` hot fuzz '' ( 2007 ) . it was released on 19 july 2013 in the united kingdom and on 23 august 2013 in the united states . it received positive reviews .
