Industrial and Commercial Bank of China     industrial and commercial bank of china ltd. ( icbc ) ( ; ; , more commonly just ) is the largest bank in the world by profit and market capitalization . it is one of china 's 'big four ' state-owned commercial banks . the other three banks are bank of china , agricultural bank of china , and china construction bank . it was founded on january 1 , 1984. at first it was a private company . it had assets of rmb 12.55 trillion ( us $ 1.9 trillion ) . it also had over 18,000 outlets including 106 overseas branches and agents globally . in 2013 , it was ranked number 1 on `` forbes '' global 2000 list of world 's biggest public companies . other websites . - financial reports - industrial and commercial bank of china - industrial and commercial bank of china
