Gustavo Noboa     gustavo josé joaquín noboa bejarano ( born 21 august 1937 in guayaquil , ecuador ) is an ecuadorian politician , former president of ecuador ( from 22 january 2000 to 15 january 2003 ) and vice president during jamil mahuad 's government . noboa was born on 21 august 1937 in guayaquil , ecuador . he was married to maría isabel baquerizo . since march 2006 , noboa is under house arrest for mishandling ecuador 's foreign debt .
