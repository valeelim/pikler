Freeform     freeform ( formerly abc family ) is an american basic cable and satellite television channel . it is owned by abc family worldwide inc. this is part of the disney-abc television group division of the walt disney company . the channel usually shows modern family programs which are aimed for everyone . however , their shows and movies are aimed at teenage girls and young women ( age 15-30 ) . freeform also offers other shows from other channels . freeform was founded in 1977. it was first an extension of pat robertson 's christian television ministry . it later became the family channel in 1990. in 1998 , it was sold to fox kids . they renamed the channel fox family . on october 24 , 2001 , fox family channel and fox family worldwide were sold to the walt disney company . network slogans . - `` stay with us '' ( 1977–1985 ; as cbn satellite service and cbn cable ) - `` just watch us '' ( 1985–1988 ; as cbn cable ) - `` together , we 're family '' ( 1988–1991 ; as the cbn family channel ) - `` the greatest in the family '' ( 1991–1995 ; as the family channel )
