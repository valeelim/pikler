Miguel Ángel Perez Tello    miguel ángel perez tello miguel ángel perez tello ( born october 2 , 1957 in granada ) is a skier and bicyclist from spain . he has a disability : he can not use part of one leg . he is a type lc3 bicycle athlete . he raced at the 1988 winter paralympics in para-nordic skiing . he was the second skier in the 10 km race and the second skier in the 2.5 km race . he raced at the 1992 winter paralympics in para-nordic skiing . he was the second skier in the 2.5 km race . he raced at the 1996 summer paralympics on a bicycle . he was the fastest to go omnium track race .
