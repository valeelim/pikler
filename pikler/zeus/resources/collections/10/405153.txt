English Defence League     the english defence league , or in short edl , is a street protest group in the united kingdom which is against sharia law and islamism . the group was founded in 2009 in luton by tommy robinson . groups including unite against fascism ( uaf ) and anonymous are against the edl . edl can also stand for the `` european defence league '' , also founded by tommy robinson . tommy robinson left the edl in october 2013. tim ablitt became the new leader . a report found that 81 % of edl supporters are male .
