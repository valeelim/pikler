Vermillion, South Dakota    vermillion , south dakota vermillion is a city in the southeastern part of the u.s. state of south dakota . it is the county seat of clay county , and it became a city in 1877. vermillion is the tenth largest city in south dakota ; 10,571 people lived there at the 2010 census . the city is home to the university of south dakota , a major college in the state . other websites . - city of vermillion website
