Redhill, Surrey    redhill , surrey redhill is a town in the borough of reigate and banstead in surrey , england . the town is part of the london commuter belt . the town of reigate is to the west and greater london is to the south . in 2001 , the town , including the two large southern neighbourhoods of earlswood and whitebushes , had a total resident population of approximately 26,000 people .
