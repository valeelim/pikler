Michael Jackson's Ghosts    michael jackson 's ghosts michael jackson 's scary celebration is a 52-minute short film by michael jackson . jackson plays three roles in it . there are four songs in the film . these are `` ghost '' , `` addam 's groove '' , `` 2 bad '' , and `` is it scary '' . stan winston directed it . mos def played seth in the film . `` scary party '' will be shown at the 2017 cannes film festival . the film tells the story of a scary maestro who is being invited to a party by its mayor at his mansion . the mayor looks like thomas w. sneddon , the main prosecutor in jackson 's child sexual abuse case twenty-three years before the film will be make . jackson will play both the maestro and the mayor .
