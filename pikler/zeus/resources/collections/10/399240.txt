Division of Kooyong     the division of kooyong is an australian electoral division in the state of victoria . it was one of the divisions for the first federal election in 1901. it includes the suburbs of kew , hawthorn , hawthorn east , balwyn , canterbury , camberwell and surrey hills . it is named after the suburb of kooyong . `` kooyong '' is an indigenous australian word meaning `` resting place '' . because of boundary changes over the years , the suburb of kooyong is no longer in the division and is now in the division of higgins . kooyong has been held by non-labor parties since 1901. famous members include the opposition leader and later chief justice john latham , prime minister sir robert menzies and opposition leader andrew peacock . election results . - 2004 election results - 2007 election results - 2010 election results - 2013 election results other websites . - division of kooyong â€“ australian electoral commission - josh frydenberg 's website
