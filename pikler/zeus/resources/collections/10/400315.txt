Rosalia Lazaro Calleja     rosalia lazaro calleja ( born january 25 , 1975 in madrid ) is a track and field athlete from spain . she has a disability : she is blind and a t12/p12/f12/b2 type athlete . she was at the 1996 summer paralympics and competed in the long jump and pentathlon . she finished second in the long jump and 9 in the pentathlon . she was at the 2000 summer paralympics and competed in the long jump . she finished first . she was at the 2004 summer paralympics and competed in the long jump and finished second . she was at the 2008 summer paralympics and competed in the long jump 100 meter race .
