Norman Schwarzkopf, Jr.    norman schwarzkopf , jr. herbert norman schwarzkopf , jr. ( ; august 22 , 1934 – december 27 , 2012 ) , better known as norman schwarzkopf , jr. , was a united states army general who was the commander of the u.s. central command and the coalition forces in the gulf war . he was born in trenton , new jersey , the son of ruth alice ( née bowman ) and norman schwarzkopf , sr. , both americans of german origin . schwarzkopf died from pneumonia on december 27 , 2012 , aged 78 , in tampa , florida .
