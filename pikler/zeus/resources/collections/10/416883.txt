Looper (movie)    looper ( movie ) looper is a 2012 american science fiction action-thriller movie directed and written by rian johnson . it stars bruce willis , joseph gordon-levitt , emily blunt , and jeff daniels . it was released to positive reviews on september 28 , 2012. critic roger ebert gave the movie 3.5/4 stars . it won nine awards and was nominated for twenty-six .
