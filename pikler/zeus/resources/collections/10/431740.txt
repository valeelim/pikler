Leopard danio     the leopard danio ( `` brachydanio frankei '' ) is a freshwater fish . they are native to india . the leopard danio is an active fish that likes to live in shoals ( groups ) . they are peaceful fish and are often kept in community fish tanks . they grow to about 6 cm and like to live in a water temperature of about 18–24°c . leopard danios are omnivores and will eat bloodworms , brine shrimp and flake foods . references . - http : //www.aquaticcommunity.com/fish/leoparddanio.php - http : //www.aquaticstoyourdoor.co.uk/leopard-danio.html
