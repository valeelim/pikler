Walter Huddleston     walter darlington `` dee '' huddleston ( born april 15 , 1926 ) is a retired american politician . he is a democrat from the state of kentucky . he represented kentucky in the united states senate from 1973 until 1985. huddleston was born on april 15 , 1926 in burkesville , kentucky . he studied at the university of kentucky . huddleston was married to jean huddleston from 1947 until her death in 2003. huddleston was the ceo of the first financial service corporation . in 2012 , huddleston announced he was stepping down as ceo of first financial service corporation for health reasons .
