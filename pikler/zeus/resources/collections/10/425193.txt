Flare (countermeasure)    flare ( countermeasure ) in the military , a flare countermeasure is a very hot , burning object which is shot out from a warplane or helicopter . warplanes and helicopters shoot out flares if the enemy has fired a heat-seeking missile at them . the flare may trick the missile into hitting into the hot , burning flare . this helps to protect warplanes and helicopters from enemy missiles . if a warplane or helicopter has had a radar-guided missile fired at them , they may shoot out chaff . chaff is thin strips or fibres of metal which make if hard for the enemy missile to target the plane or helicopter .
