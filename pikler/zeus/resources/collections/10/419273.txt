Big Time Rush     big time rush ( btr ) is an american television series created by scott fellows . it originally aired on nickelodeon . it stars the boy band of the same name , which includes kendall schmidt , james maslow , carlos pena jr. , and logan henderson . the show centers on four hockey players from minnesota who were selected to form a boy band in los angeles . aired on november 28 , 2009 , the series continued for four seasons . it ended with an hour-long finale special called `` big time dreams '' .
