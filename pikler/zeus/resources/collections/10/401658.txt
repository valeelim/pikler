Mikkeli     mikkeli is a town in southern savonia , finland . as of january 2014 , the population was almost 54,700. in the finnish point of view , mikkeli is a city , not a town . neighbouring municipalities are hirvensalmi , juva , kangasniemi , mäntyharju , pieksämäki , puumala and savitaipale . mikkeli was established in 1838. distances . - ristiina 20 km - hirvensalmi 34 km - juva 44 km - kangasniemi 52 km - helsinki 230 km - kuopio 163 km - lappeenranta 106 km - savonlinna 104 km other websites . - the official page
