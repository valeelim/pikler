Waistline (clothing)    waistline ( clothing ) the waistline is the part of a piece of clothing that sits on the wearer 's waist . the actual position of the waitline can be different depending on the fashion or how the person chooses to wear the garment , but it is normally just above the hips . waistlines can be secured with a variety of methods , such as buttons , knots and zippers . a waistband is a strip of elastic rubber that adjusts to keep the item tight against the body .
