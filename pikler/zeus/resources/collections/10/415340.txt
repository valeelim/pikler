Larkin Administration Building     the larkin building was designed in 1904 by frank lloyd wright and built in 1906 for the larkin soap company of buffalo , new york . the five story dark red brick building used pink tinted mortar and utilized steel frame construction . it was noted for many innovations , including air conditioning , stained glass windows , built-in desk furniture , and suspended toilet bowls . though this was an office building , it still caught the essence of frank lloyd wright 's type of architecture . sculptor richard bock provided ornamentation for the building . it was located at 680 seneca street , the larkin building was demolished in 1950. other websites . - the larkin building , buffalo , ny : history of the demolition by jerome puma , 1978 - work of art : demolition of larkin building was key loss for preservation advocates - great buildings : larkin building - larkin company : the people , products and premiums
