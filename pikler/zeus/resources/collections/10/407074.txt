Deborah Kara Unger     deborah kara unger ( born may 12 ) is a canadian actress who has been mostly in independent movies . she acted as catherine ballard in the 1996 movie `` crash '' and as carol lynn johnson for the 2007 movie `` 88 minutes '' . she was born in vancouver , british columbia . some sources cite 1964 for her birth year , although other sources mention 1966 .
