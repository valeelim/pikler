Anne Veski     anne veski ( born anne vaarmann ; 27 february 1956 ) is an estonian pop singer . she has sung in estonian , russian , and polish . veski is married to her second husband , benno beltšikov , who also is her manager . veski was previously married to jaak veski ( 1956–1994 ) from 1977 tae 1981. she has a daughter , kerli . kerli is a diplomat an has worked at the estonian consulate in moscow . veski also has two grandchildren via kerli . discography . - `` ingleid ei ...
