Michael Parkinson     sir michael parkinson , cbe , also known as parky , ( born 28 march 1935 ) is an english broadcaster , journalist , and writer . he is best known for his chat show `` parkinson '' . he was born in cudworth , a village in south yorkshire . he went to barnsley grammar school . he started his journalism career writing for local newspapers . his book `` parky : my autobiography '' was published on 2 october 2008. parkinson lives with his wife in berkshire . other websites . - official website
