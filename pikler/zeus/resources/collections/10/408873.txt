Missing in action     missing in action ( mia ) refers to a soldier who is reported to have gone missing during active service . the soldier may have been killed , injured , captured or deserted . it is not known what happened to them . if they are dead , neither their body nor grave can be found or identified . going missing in action has been a risk for people in armed forces for as long as there has been war . according to the us dept of defense as of august 17 , 2017 : - mias-north america : 1,165 - mias-south america : 998 - mias-europe : 20,952 - mias-middle east : 17 - mias-africa : 972 - mias-asia/pacific : 48,552 - total of mia us servicemen/civilians : 73,029 according to the us dept of defense as of august 17 , 2017 : note : '' * reflects actual number still unaccounted-for . pmkor database count is slightly higher due to several entries pending administrative review .
