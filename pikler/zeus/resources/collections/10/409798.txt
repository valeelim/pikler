Aranzazu González Muñoz     aranzazu gonzalez muñoz ( born june 5 , 1975 in san sebastian , guipúzcoa ) is a swimmer from spain . she has a disability and is an s4 type swimmer . she raced at the 1996 summer paralympics in atlanta , georgia . she finished first in the 50 meter breaststroke , 50 meter freestyle and 100 meter freestyle races .
