Physalis     the physalis is a genus of plants that is part of the nightshade family . there are between 75 and 90 species . all except one are native to the americas . the plants grow small orange fruit encased in a lantern-like husk that comes from the calyx . they are similar to the tomato . since it has a mild , refreshing acidity similar to the tomato , it can be used in many of the same ways . many `` physalis '' species are called groundcherries .
