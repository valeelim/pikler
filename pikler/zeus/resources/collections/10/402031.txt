David Barrallo Fuertes     david barrallo fuertes ( born june 4 , 1973 in barcelona ) is a track and field athlete from spain . he has a physical disability and is t44 type athlete . he raced at the 2000 summer paralympics . he finished second in the 4 x 400 meter t42-t46 race . he finished fourth in the 200 meter race . he finished fifth in the 400 meter race .
