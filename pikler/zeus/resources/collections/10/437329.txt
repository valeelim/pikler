Eritherium     eritherium is an extinct genus of early proboscidean . it was discovered in morocco . it was first named by emmanuel gheerbrant in 2009 and the type species is `` eritherium azzouzorum '' . eritherium is the oldest , smallest and most primitive known elephant relative . the phosphate bed where the remains were found places the animal 's age as 61.1 to 57.8 million years ago . `` eritherium '' is the oldest known representative of the proboscideans . it has not been assigned to any family in the order . `` eritherium '' is the sister clade to `` phosphatherium '' , `` numidotherium '' and `` moeritherium '' . this is one of the most complete evolutionary sequences of early mammalian radiations after the k/t extinction event . thus , the elephants are one of the oldest `` modern '' mammal orders to have survived .
