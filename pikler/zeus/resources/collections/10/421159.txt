F.K. Bačka    f.k . bačka f.k . bačka is a football club which plays in bačka palanka , serbia . the club was founded in 1945. its stadium , vava slavko maletin , can hold 5,500 people . it opened on 7 july 1951. the first time bačka was ranked in the second federal league was in the 1959/1960 season , and again in 1988/1989 . one of their best seasons was when they were in the yugoslav cup quarterfinal in 1969 , losing to hadjuk split 2:1. the club has also taken part in the national finals , the most recent time being in 1993/1994 when they lost to nis workers .
