Luis Cordero Crespo     luis benjamín cordero y crespo ( 6 april 1833 – 30 january 1912 ) was president of ecuador 1 july 1892 to 16 april 1895. his parents were gregorio cordero and josefa crespo . he was a member of the progresistas , a liberal catholic political party , and was a member of the provisional governing junta which led the progresistas to power in 1883. luis cordero wrote poems in spanish and kichwa and published the first kichwa-spanish-kichwa dictionary in ecuador . other websites . - official website of the ecuadorian government about the country president 's history - ¡rinimi , llacta ! - poems of luis cordero crespo in quichua , with spanish translation
