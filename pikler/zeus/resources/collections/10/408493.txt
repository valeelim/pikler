Phil Bryant     dewey phillip `` phil '' bryant ( born december 9 , 1954 ) is an american politician from mississippi . bryant is the 63rd and current governor of mississippi , going against the democratic party candidate , johnny dupree in the 2011 general election . he used to serve as the 31st lieutenant governor of mississippi , going against the democratic party candidate , jamie franks , in the 2007 general election . on november 8 , 2011 , he was elected governor of mississippi and took office on january 10 , 2012 .
