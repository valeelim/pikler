Arrondissement of Muret     the arrondissement of muret is an arrondissement of france . it is part of the haute-garonne `` département '' in the occitanie region . its capital is the city of muret . when the haute-garonne department was created in 1790 , muret was one of the districts of the department and , with the creation of `` arrondissements '' in 1800 , the `` arrondissement '' of muret was part of that original department , but it was eliminated in 1803 and , in 1806 , became again an `` arrondissement '' . in 1926 , the `` arrondissement '' of muret was eliminated again but , in 1942 , it was made again an `` arrondissement '' . it is the smallest of the `` arrondissements '' of the department , with an area of , but it is the second in population with 215,606 inhabitants and a density of inhabitants/km² the `` arrondissement '' is bordered to the northeast by the `` arrondissement '' of toulouse , to the south by the ariège ( occitanie ) department , to the southwest by the `` arrondissement '' of saint-gaudens and to the northwest by the gers ( occitanie ) department .
