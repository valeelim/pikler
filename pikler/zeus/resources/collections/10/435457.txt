Mad Max (video game)    mad max ( video game ) mad max is an upcoming video game . it is set in an open world environment after civilization has been destroyed . it is based on the `` mad max '' film series . it is published by warner bros. interactive entertainment and developed by avalanche studios . the game was officially revealed at `` e3 '' on june 10 , 2013 during a sony press conference . it is set to be released for microsoft windows , playstation 3 , playstation 4 , xbox 360 and xbox one in 2015 .
