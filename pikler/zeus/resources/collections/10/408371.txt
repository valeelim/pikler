Skara     skara is a town in the county of västra götaland in sweden . it is the seat of skara municipality . skara is the home of skara cathedral , and swedish record label mariann grammofon as well . founding dates back to 988 ( traditional date ) . from 1634 and until 1660 , skara was the seat of skaraborg county . famous people . - bert karlsson , swedish record label manager and politician
