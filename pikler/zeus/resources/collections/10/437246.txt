Gomphothere     the gomphotheres were a varied family of extinct elephant-like animals ( proboscidea ) the gomphotheriidae . they had a wide distribution . they were common in north america during the miocene and pliocene epochs , 121.6 million years ago . some lived in parts of eurasia , beringia and , after the great american interchange , south america . beginning about 5 million years ago , they were gradually replaced by modern elephants . the last two south american species , in the genus `` cuvieronius '' , did not become extinct until about 9000 years ago . `` stegomastodon '' remains have been dated as recently as 6,000 years ago in the valle del magdalena , colombia . gomphotheres also survived in mexico and central america until the end of the pleistocene .
