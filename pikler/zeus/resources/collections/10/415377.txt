Giuseppina Bozzacchi     giuseppina bozzacchi ( november 23 , 1853 – november 23 , 1870 ) was a ballerina . she was born in milan , italy and died in paris , france . she was the first to dance the part of swanhilda in `` coppélia '' in may 1870. she was a great success . she died a few months later on her birthday in november 1870 .
