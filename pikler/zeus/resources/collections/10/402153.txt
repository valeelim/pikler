Charlie and the Great Glass Elevator     charlie and the great glass elevator ( 1972 ) is a children 's book by british author roald dahl . the book continues directly from the events of `` charlie and the chocolate factory '' . willy wonka has just given his factory to charlie . in a flying machine known as the great glass elevator , they crash through the roof of charlie 's house and tell his family the good news . on their way to the factory , the elevator makes a noise . charlie and his family find themselves in orbit around the earth . an adventure begins with willy wonka leading the way . other websites . - official roald dahl website
