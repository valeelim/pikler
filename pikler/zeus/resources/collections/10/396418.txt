Eublepharinae     the eublepharinae are one of the five subfamilies of the gekkonidae , or geckos . the eublepharinae subfamily has 27 species in five genera . also called eyelid geckos , the eublepharines have more simple features than other geckos . for example , they do n't have the ability to climb surfaces . unlike other geckos , they also have movable eyelids . they are nocturnal lizards , often preying on insects and similar creatures . they lay a pair of leathery eggs , and , in most species , the gender of the young is determined by the temperature of the egg ; just like crocodiles . the subfamily contains the following genera : - `` coleonyx '' ( seven species ) - `` eublepharis '' ( five species ) - `` goniurosaurus '' ( 11 species ) - `` hemitheconyx '' ( two species ) - `` holodactylus '' ( two species )
