Division of Dunkley     the division of dunkley is an australian electoral division in victoria . it was set up in 1984 and is named for louisa margaret dunkley , a trade unionist and campaigner for equal pay for women . it covers an area of 140 km2 in the southern suburbs of melbourne . it includes frankston , mornington and mount eliza . in 2013 bruce billson became the minister for small business in the abbott government . election results . - 2004 election results - 2007 election results - 2010 election results - 2013 election results other websites . - division of dunkley â€“ australian electoral commission - bruce billson 's website
