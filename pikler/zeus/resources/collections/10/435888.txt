Inline skating     inline skating is a form of skating . it involves a person moving across land using shoes that have wheels on the bottom . it is similar to roller skating , except that the wheels are smaller and arranged in a single line . it is also called rollerblading after the brand of skates , `` rollerblade '' . inline skates usually have between 2 to 5 polyurethane wheels . they are arranged in a single line . this allows for faster speeds than roller skates .
