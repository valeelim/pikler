Ruben Delgado Martin     ruben delgado martin ( born augsut 27 , 1976 in santa cruz de tenerife ) is a track and field athlete from spain . he has a disability : he is blind and a t12/b2 type athlete . he raced at the 1996 summer paralympics . he race and finished in third in 800 meter t12 race . he raced at the 2000 summer paralympics . he finished fifth in the 1,500 meter t12 race .
