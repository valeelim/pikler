Beachbody     beachbody llc is an american multinational corporation . it sells fitness , weight loss and muscle-building home-exercise dvds . the company 's best-known product is tony horton 's p90x . its television advertisements have become some of the most frequently shown infomercials in the united states . american fitness coach , shaun t. was signed by beachbody to create the `` most intense and insane workout ever put on dvd '' . this led to the creation of insanity , becoming one of the best-selling exercise dvds in the united states . other websites . - official website - team beachbody - carl daikeler 's blog
