Eva Ngui Nchama     eva ngui nchama ( born on june 9 , 1985 in malabo , equatorial guinea ) is a athlete from spain . she has a disability : she can not see and is a t12/b2 type athlete . she competed in the 2004 summer paralympics in athens , greece in the women 's 100 metres - t12 event . she competed at the 2008 summer paralympics in beijing , china and finished third in the women 's 100 metres - t12 event and thir in the women 's 200 metres - t12 event . she raced at the 2012 summer paralympics and was the 14th runner to finish . in 2012 , she had a €18,000 athlete scholarship with a €3,000 reserve and a €2,500 coaching scholarship .
