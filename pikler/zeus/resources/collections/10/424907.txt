Merton College, Oxford    merton college , oxford merton college is one of the colleges of the university of oxford . it was founded in 1264 by walter de merton , who was the chancellor to henry iii and edward i. it claims to be the oldest college in oxford , although balliol college and university college also claim to be the oldest . construction of the college at its present location began in the 1270s . its buildings are among the oldest in oxford . mob quad at merton college was built in the 14th century . it is often said to be the oldest quadrangle at oxford or cambridge . other quadrangles at oxford and cambridge , however , may be older . j.r.r . tolkien was a professor of english language and literature at merton college .
