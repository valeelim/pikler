Jorge Pedreros     pedreros jorge arnaldo aviles ( august 8 , 1942 – september 14 , 2013 ) was a chilean musician , record producer , actor , comedian . he is one of the creators of the iconic comedy show `` jappening with ja '' . pedreros was born on august 8 , 1942 in santiago , chile . he was married to gladys del río from 1967 until his death in 2013. pedreros died on september 14 , 2013 from complications of pneumonia , aged 71 .
