Luisa Regina Cachan Muñoz     luisa regina cachan muñoz ( born january 21 , 1979 in aviles ) is a swimmer from spain . she has a disability where she can not use her legs and can only use some of her arms and hands , and is an s4 type swimmer . she raced at the 1992 summer paralympics . she did not finish as the first , second or third swimmer . she raced at the 1996 summer paralympics . she did not finish as the first , second or third swimmer . she raced at the 2000 summer paralympics . she did not finish as the first , second or third swimmer . she raced at the 2004 summer paralympics . she finished second in the 4 x 50 meter 20pts relay .
