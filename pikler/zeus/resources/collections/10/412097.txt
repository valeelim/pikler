Dassault Étendard IV    dassault étendard iv the dassault étendard iv is a french fighter jet . it is a single-turbojet aircraft . its first flight was in 1958. it was first used by the french navy 's squadrons in 1962. it was used in the french aircraft carriers `` clemenceau '' and `` foch '' . the last one was retired in 2000. the super-étendard is a modernized variant .
