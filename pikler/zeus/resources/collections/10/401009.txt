NRK1     nrk1 is the main television channel of norwegian broadcasting corporation ( nrk ) . test broadcasts started on 12 january 1954 , regular test broadcasts started on 13 april 1958 and regular broadcasts started on 20 august 1960. it is norway 's oldest and largest television channel . previously known as simply as `` nrk '' , the channel now known as `` nrk1 '' , to distinguish itself from sister channel `` nrk2 '' , which started broadcasting in 1996 .
