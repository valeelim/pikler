Zirkel     zirkel may refer to : - ferdinand zirkel ( 1838–1912 ) , german geologist and petrographer - uss `` zirkel '' ( id-3407 ) , a cargo ship that served in the united states navy from 1918 to 1919 - zirkel ( studentenverbindung ) , a symbol used in european studentenverbindungen - albert zirkel ( 1885– ? ) , american wrestler who competed in the 1904 summer olympics - mount zirkel - dorsum zirkel , a wrinkle ridge in mare imbrium on the moon . it is 193 km long and was named after ferdinand zirkel in 1976
