The Spanish Tragedy     the spanish tragedy , or hieronimo is mad again is a play by thomas kyd . it is a tragedy . the exact date the play was written is uncertain . it was probably written in the 1580s . ben jonson referred to the play in `` bartholomew fair '' . this suggests that `` the spanish tragedy '' was staged between 1584 and 1589. a date earlier than 1588 is very likely . the play is about the horrific murders of the heirs to the spanish throne . it had great influence on elizabethan playwrights . shakespeare may have adopted elements of the play for `` hamlet '' . other websites . - complete play
