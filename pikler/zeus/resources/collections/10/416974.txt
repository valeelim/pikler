The Saint of Bleecker Street     the saint of bleecker street is a three-act opera with words and music by gian carlo menotti . it was first performed at the broadway theatre in new york city on december 27 , 1954. the opera won the pulitzer prize for music in 1955 and the new york drama critics circle award for best musical . the opera is about a young woman in new york city 's little italy who receives the stigmata .
