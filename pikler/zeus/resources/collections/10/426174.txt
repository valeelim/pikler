Lacuna Coil     lacuna coil is an italian gothic metal band . they started in 1994 in milan with the name sleep of right . then they changed their name to ethereal in 1997. they found that there was already a band with that name . so they changed their name to lacuna coil that year . they are known for making music with male and female vocal harmonies . cristina scabbia joined the band in 1996. she does the main vocals with andrea ferro . the band has released seven albums . other websites . - official website
