Innocent Blood     innocent blood is a horror-crime movie released in autumn 1992. its cast members are angela bassett as attorney sinclair , linnea quigley as nurse and robert loggia as salvatore macelli . the movie is set in pittsburgh , pennsylvania . it is about vampires and mafia activities . this movie was released in september 1992. it got mixed reviews , though it took in almost $ 5 million in the box office .
