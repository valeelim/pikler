Magdalen College, Oxford    magdalen college , oxford magdalen college is one of the colleges of the university of oxford . the bishop of winchester , william of waynflete , founded the college in 1458. the full name of the college is `` the president and fellows of the college of st. mary magdalene in the university of oxford '' . at dawn on may day , the college choir sings hymns from the top of the magdalen tower . large crowds gather each year on magdalen bridge to hear the choir .
