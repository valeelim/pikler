FC Biel/Bienne     fc biel-bienne is the most successful football club in the city of biel/bienne , switzerland . the club was founded on 13 november 1896. the club played in a stadium called ‘ jardin anglais ’ at the beginning . they went to the gurzelen stadium in the year 1913. there they still play today . the gurzelen stadium has a capacity of 15,000. the club got the name fc biel-bienne in 1911. before they were called ‘ football-club de bienne ’ . during world war i they didn ’ t play in the league but they did a lot of charity projects . fc biel-bienne plays in the challenge league today . the second best football league in switzerland . they moved to the first league in the season 1989/90 . this is the best league of amateur-football in switzerland . they went to the challenge league again in 2008. because the gurzelen stadium is very old , fc biel/bienne has to play on the ‘ maladière stadium ’ in neuenburg . but the club can come back in 2014 because the new stadium is going to be built . it will be opened in 2015 .
