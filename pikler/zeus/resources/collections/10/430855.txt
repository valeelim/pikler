Eye neoplasm     ocular oncology is the branch of medicine dealing with tumors relating to the eye and its adnexa . eye cancer can affect all parts of the eye . eye cancers might start in the eye and it can metastatic cancer ( spread to the eye from another organ ) . the two most common cancers that spread to the eye from another organ are breast cancer and lung cancer . other less common sites of origin include the prostate , kidney , thyroid , skin , colon and blood or bone marrow . this cancer is most common in children . there is a 95 % of finding a cure for this cancer .
