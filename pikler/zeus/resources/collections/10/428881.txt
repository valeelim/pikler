Division of McMahon     the division of mcmahon is an australian electoral division in the state of new south wales . it was set up in 2010 and is named for australian prime minister sir william mcmahon . it replaced the division of prospect . the division covers the western suburbs of sydney . it includes the suburbs of abbotsbury , blacktown ( part ) , bossley park , canley vale , cecil park , eastern creek , edensor park , erskine park , fairfield , fairfield heights , fairfield west , greenfield park , greystanes , horsley park , kemps creek , merrylands ( part ) , merrylands west , mount vernon , orchard hills , pemulwuy , prairiewood , prospect , smithfield , south wentworthville ( part ) , st clair and wetherill park . election results . - 2010 election results - 2010 election results other websites . - division of mcmahon - australian electoral commission
