Natsume Soseki     natsume soseki ( february 9 , 1867 – december 9 , 1916 ) , was a japanese novelist and specialist of english literature . he is best known for his novel `` i am a cat '' . he was born in tokyo , japan . natsume soseki graduated from the university of tokyo , and he was to become a school teacher . also he was a master of ryūnosuke akutagawa . other websites . - works by natsume sōseki – project gutenberg
