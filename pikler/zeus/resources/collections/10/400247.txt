A Midsummer Nights Dream (1959 movie)    a midsummer nights dream ( 1959 movie ) a midsummer night 's dream is a 1959 czechoslovak animated puppet movie directed by jiří trnka . it is based on the shakespeare play of the same name . it was entered into the 1959 cannes film festival . an english language version was made with narration by richard burton . the movie won several awards including the prize of the technical committee in cannes and an honourable medal at the venice film festival .
