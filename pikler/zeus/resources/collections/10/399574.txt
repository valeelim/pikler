Heinrich Rohrer     heinrich rohrer ( 6 june 1933 – 16 may 2013 ) was a swiss physicist . he shared half of the 1986 nobel prize in physics with gerd binnig for the design of the scanning tunneling microscope ( stm ) . ( the other half of the prize was awarded to ernst ruska . ) rohrer was born on 6 june 1933 in buchs , st. gallen , switzerland . he was raised in zürich , switzerland . rohrer studied at swiss federal institute of technology . he was never married . rohrer died on 16 may 2013 in wollerau , switzerland after battling a disease , aged 79 .
