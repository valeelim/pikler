Neil Simon     neil simon ( born july 4 , 1927 ) is an american playwright and screenwriter . he has written over thirty plays and nearly the same number of movie screenplays , most adapted from his plays . he has received more oscar and tony nominations than any other writer . simon was born on july 4 , 1927 in the bronx , new york city to a jewish family . he studied at new york university and at university of denver . simon was married five times . the first four ended in divorce . he is currently married to elaine joyce since 1999. simon has two children and one adopted child . other websites . - video : `` neil simon 's broadway '' , 6 minutes - neil simon theatre festival - pbs article , american masters
