Lê Đức Anh    lê đức anh lê đức anh ( born 1 december 1920 ) is an vietnamese general and politician . he was president of vietnam from 1992 until 1997. he became president after leading the vietnamese forces in cambodia throughout the 1980s . he was regarded as a conservative . he supported maintaining strict party control over local policies . he was born in phú lộc district , thừa thiên–huế province on 1 december 1920 .
