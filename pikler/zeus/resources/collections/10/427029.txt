John C. Pemberton     john clifford pemberton ( august 10 , 1814 – july 13 , 1881 ) , was a career officer in the united states army . he fought in the seminole wars and the mexican–american war . he was commended ( rewarded ) for his performance in the mexican–american war . he also fought in the american civil war . he commanded the confederate army in the siege of vicksburg . he surrendered on july 4th , 1863 .
