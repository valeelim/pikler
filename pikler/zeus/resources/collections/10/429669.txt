Ange-Félix Patassé     ange-félix patassé ( january 25 , 1937 – april 5 , 2011 ) was a central african politician . he was president of the central african republic from 1993 until 2003. in 2003 , he was forced out of office by the rebel leader françois bozizé . patassé died on april 5 , 2011 from complications of diabetes .
