Human Nature (Michael Jackson song)    human nature ( michael jackson song ) `` human nature '' is a song by the american singer-songwriter michael jackson . it was written by steve porcaro and john bettis . it was the fifth song from his sixth solo record `` thriller '' . `` human nature '' was released on july 3 , 1983. it was a moderate hit in america and other countries . this song has been covered , performed and sampled by other musicians .
