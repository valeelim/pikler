Mooreana trichoneura     mooreana trichoneura is commonly known as the 'yellow flat ' . it is a butterfly belonging to the family hesperiidae . it has a wingspan of 32-36mm . butterflies like this are called 'flats ' because they fly quickly and usually rest on the underside of a leaf with their wings open flat . its host plant is `` mallotus paniculatus '' , in the spurge family euphorbiaceae . the yellow flat is usually found in lowland forests up to 833m ( 2500ft ) . the yellow flat has a wide distribution . it ranges from india to thailand to malaysia and can even be found in the philippines . in the 4th quarter of 2012 , it was discovered in singapore .
