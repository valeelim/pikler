Westgate shopping mall shooting     on 21 september 2013 , ten men with guns attacked a shopping mall in nairobi , kenya . the attack lasted until 24 september . the attackers held hostages and later fought in gun battles with kenyan security forces . a total of 72 people died , including 61 civilians , 6 kenyan soldiers and 5 attackers . over 200 people were injured . the shopping mall where the attack took place was westgate . the islamist group al-shabaab claimed responsibility for the attack . it said that it was in retaliation for the kenyan military 's role in the somali civil war . newly released intelligence report named kenyan commander and chief in attack .
