Gurdaspur District     gurdaspur district is a district in the state of punjab in india . gurdaspur is the headquarters of the district . it borders narowal district of the pakistani punjab , kathua district of jammu and kashmir , the districts of amritsar and hoshiarpur in punjab , and chamba and kangra districts of himachal pradesh . two main rivers , beas and ravi , pass through the district . the mughal emperor akbar is said to have been enthroned in a garden near kalanaur , a historically important town in the district . the district is in the foothills of the himalayas .
