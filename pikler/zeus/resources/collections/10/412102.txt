Douglas A-4 Skyhawk     the douglas a-4 skyhawk is an american military jet aircraft . its first flight was in 1954. it was first used by the us navy was in 1956. the skyhawk was one of the main attack aircraft of the vietnam war . many foreign countries used it , such as argentina , israel , kuwait , and singapore . the argentina air force used theirs in the falklands war for attacks against british forces .
