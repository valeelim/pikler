Mrs. Parker and the Vicious Circle     mrs. parker and the vicious circle is a 1994 biographical and drama movie . this movie is about dorothy parker and related writers . the movie takes place in the 1920s . jennifer jason leigh plays dorothy parker . also acting in this movie is martha plimpton : she plays jane grant . the movie was an official selection in the 1994 cannes film festival . the movie was given very positive reviews from many critics . the box office performance , however , was not good .
