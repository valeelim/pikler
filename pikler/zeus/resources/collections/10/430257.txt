Chris Furrh     chris furrh ( born 18 december 1974 ) is an american actor . he was born in san marcos , texas . he is most famous for starring as jack in the 1990 movie version of `` lord of the flies '' . after `` lord of the flies '' , he played the role of nick bankston in the 1990 television movie `` a family for joe '' . he played another castaway teenager in `` exile '' . furrh has since retired from acting .
