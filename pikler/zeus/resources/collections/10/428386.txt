Birdman (rapper)    birdman ( rapper ) brian williams ( called birdman ) is an american rapper . he is the co-founder of cash money records . he began his career as an oil company executive . in 1997 , he became part of a rap group called big tymers along with mannie fresh . he has collaborated for a long time with lil wayne and is currently one of the richest rappers . birdman has a reported net worth of $ 160 million in 2014 .
