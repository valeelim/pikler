Wii Zapper     the wii zapper is a light gun add-on for the wii remote made for the wii game console . it was first released in japan on october 25 , 2007 with `` ghost squad '' . it was later released in a package with `` link 's crossbow training '' , a shooter video game based on `` '' . it was released in north america on november 19 , 2007 , in europe on december 7 , 2007 , and in australia on december 13 , 2007. it allows players to aim at the screen and hit targets . to hit targets , players have to point the device at the television and pull the trigger . it is mainly used for shooter games , including first-person shooters and third-person shooters . the name is a reference to the nes zapper , a light gun accessory released for the nintendo entertainment system .
