Arlene Golonka     arlene golonka ( born january 23 , 1936 ) is an american actress who works mostly on television . she is known for playing bubbly and eccentric blondes . she played millie swanson in `` the andy griffith show '' . she also did `` the rockford files '' , `` murder , she wrote '' and `` all in the family '' . golonka was born in chicago .
