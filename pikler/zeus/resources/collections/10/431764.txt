Seal of Louisiana     the great seal of the state of louisiana is an emblem that represents the u.s. state of louisiana . it was adopted in 1902. it has four pelicans ( one adult , the other three are little ) . mother pelican wounds her breast to feed her young . `` union , justice , confidence '' are the words of the motto surrounding these four pelicans . the wording `` state of louisiana '' is on the outer edge of the seal .
