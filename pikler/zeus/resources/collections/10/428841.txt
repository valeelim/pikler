Sándor Szokolay     sándor szokolay ( 30 march 1931 – 8 december 2013 ) was an hungarian composer and a professor of the liszt ferenc academy , budapest . szokolay was born on 30 march 1931 in kunágota , hungary . he was chairman of the hungarian kodály society ( 1978 ) and the hungarian music camera ( 1991–92 ) , and he is member of the hungarian széchenyi art academy ( 1992 ) . szokolay died on 8 december 2013 in sopron from pneumonia , hungary , aged 82. other websites . - szokolay sándor : sopron years ( accessed at the wayback machine ) - evangelic church life - fidelio - sopron walker ’ s book - pearlhymn vivat academia 2005 .
