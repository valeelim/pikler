Azerbaijani diaspora     the azerbaijani diaspora is a term used to describe the communities of ethnic azerbaijanis living outside of places of their ethnic origin : republic of azerbaijan and iranian azerbaijan . of the total azerbaijani population living worldwide which is estimated to be 30,000,000 , only about 9,000,000 live in azerbaijan and about 16,000,000 live in iran . other websites . - azerbaijani diaspora population
