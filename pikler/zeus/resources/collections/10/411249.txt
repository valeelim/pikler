Luhanka     luhanka ( ) is a municipality in central finland , finland . about 765 people lived there in february 2014. the municipalities located next to luhanka are hartola , joutsa , jyväskylä , jämsä , kuhmoinen and sysmä . originally luhanka was part of sysmä . later it was made into its own municipality in 1864. luhanka is a popular place for summer cottages . famouse people . - composer toni edelmann lives in luhanka . - writer kreetta onkeli spent her childhood at luhanka . other websites . - community website
