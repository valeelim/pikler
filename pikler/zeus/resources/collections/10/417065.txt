Invitation to the Dance     `` invitation to the dance '' is a piece of music for solo piano written by carl maria von weber in 1819. it is written in d-flat major in rondo form . it was the first concert waltz . this means it was meant to be listened to rather than danced to . in 1841 , hector berlioz made an arrangement for orchestra . in 1911 , the berlioz version was used for a short ballet called `` le spectre de la rose '' . this ballet starred nijinsky and karsavina . it became very well known .
