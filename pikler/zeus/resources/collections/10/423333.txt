Division of Grayndler     the division of grayndler is an australian electoral division in sydney , new south wales . it is one of australia 's smallest electorates , and includes the suburbs of annandale , ashfield , dulwich hill , enmore , haberfield , hurlstone park , leichhardt , marrickville , newtown , petersham , stanmore and summer hill . it was set up in 1949 and is named for edward grayndler ( 1867–1943 ) , a member of the new south wales legislative council and general secretary of the australian workers ' union from 1912 to 1941. fred daly was a minister in the whitlam government . leo mcleay was speaker of the house 1989–93 . he moved to watson during an electoral boundary change . tony whitlam , gough 's son , served only one term but then lost preselection after division boundaries were changed in 1977. election results . - 2004 election results - 2007 election results - 2010 election results - 2013 election results there was a by-election in grayndler in 1979 after the death of the sitting member , frank stewart .
