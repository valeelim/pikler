Brandon Pirri     brandon pirri ( born april 10 , 1991 , in toronto , ontario ) is a canadian professional ice hockey centre . he currently plays the florida panthers of the national hockey league ( nhl ) . before he played in the nhl , pirri played in the ontario junior hockey league for the streetsville derbys and the georgetown raiders . he also played at the rensselaer polytechnic institute in the ecac hockey conference . he was drafted by the blackhawks with the 59th overall pick in the 2009 nhl entry draft . he spends most of his time with the blackhawks ahl farm team , the rockford icehogs . on march 2 , 2014 , the blackhawks traded pirri to the florida panthers for a 2014 3rd round pick and a 2016 5th round pick .
