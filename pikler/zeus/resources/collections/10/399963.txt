John Kasich     john kasich ( born may 13 , 1952 ) is an american politician . he is the incumbent governor of ohio , in office since 2011. he is a republican . before being governor , he served in the u.s. house of representatives and in the ohio senate . kasich was born in mckees rocks , pennsylvania . his mother was of croatian ancestry while his father was czech . kasich attened the ohio state university and graduated with a degree in political science . while he was in college , he met us president richard nixon in the oval office after he wrote to the president . he was first elected to the united states house of representatives in 1982. kasich became known for his role in the balanced budget of the 1990s . he was an advocate of cutting spending to make government work better for less money . in 1998 , he was the main force behind the balanced budget that was signed by president bill clinton . after a short lived campaign to be president , he left politics in 2001 and hosted a tv show on fox news for a time . he also worked in banking .
