Arnoldo Foà     arnoldo foà ( 24 january 1916 – 11 january 2014 ) was an italian movie actor . he has appeared in more than 130 movie since 1938. he was born in ferrara , italy . foà died on the morning of 11 january 2014 , 13 days before his 98th birthday , from respiratory failure in his home in rome , italy .
