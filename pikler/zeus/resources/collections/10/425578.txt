Too Young (popular song)    too young ( popular song ) `` too young '' is a popular song . the music was written by sidney lippman . the lyrics were written by sylvia dee . the song was published in 1951. many people have sung their own versions of it . one of the best-known versions of the song was recorded by nat king cole in 1951. it reached the number 1 position on the `` billboard '' charts . a version by donny osmond in 1972 was also successful . it spent eight weeks on the record charts , with a peak position of # 13. michael jackson also recorded a version of the song .
