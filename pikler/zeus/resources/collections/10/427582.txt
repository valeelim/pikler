Wu Chinese     wu is a group of chinese dialects spoken in the area around shanghai in southern china . wu dialects have been spoken by some important people such as chiang kai-shek . however , wu speakers are similar to speakers of spanish and french because they can not always understand each other . in fact , the name wu is mainly used by people who study languages . like other types of chinese , the wu dialects are starting to be replaced by mandarin as time goes on .
