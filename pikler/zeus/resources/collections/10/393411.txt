Kirby's Dream Collection    kirby 's dream collection kirby 's dream collection is a 2012 compilation video game for the wii game console . it was developed and published by nintendo . it was made to honor the 20th anniversary of the `` kirby '' series . it includes six games in the `` kirby '' series , along with a booklet about the history of `` kirby '' games and a soundtrack . it was released in japan on july 19 , 2012 and in north america on september 16 , 2012. it includes the following games : - `` kirby 's dream land '' - `` kirby 's adventure '' - `` kirby 's dream land 2 '' - `` kirby super star '' - `` kirby 's dream land 3 '' the game includes challenge stages based off of those seen in `` kirby 's return to dream land '' . it also includes a museum that shows every title in the `` kirby '' series and has three episodes from `` kirby : right back at ya ! `` , an anime based off of the `` kirby '' series .
