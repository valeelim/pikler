A Hazy Shade of Winter     `` a hazy shade of winter '' is a hit song performed by simon and garfunkel in the 1960s . it was covered by the bangles two decades later . the song was released by itself at first , but was later released as part of the album `` bookends '' , released in 1968. this single is about sadness during winter . the bangles version was released in november 1987. both versions became major hit singles . in the late 1980s , the bangles ' version became more popular than the first version .
