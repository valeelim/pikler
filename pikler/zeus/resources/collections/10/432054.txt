Look Who's Talking Too    look who 's talking too the movie look who 's talking too is a comedy and drama movie . it was released december 14 , 1990 in the united states . the movie is about childbirth and a couple wanting to be an average family . kirstie alley plays mollie , roseanne barr voices julie , john travolta plays james and bruce willis voices mikey . the movie received very negative reviews . its overall box office , however , was moderately strong .
