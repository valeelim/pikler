Tribute in Light     the tribute in light is a memorial with two beams of light shooting straight up into the sky done to remember the september 11 attacks . it is lit up at dusk every september 11 , lasting until dawn the next day . it is done by the municipal art society of new york . it was first done on march 11 , 2002 , six months after the attacks happened , and lasted until april 14 , 2002. it was done again on the second anniversary of the attacks , on september 11 , 2003. since then , it has been done on every september 11. on clear nights , the lights can be seen from over 60 miles away . it can be seen in all of new york city , as well as most of northern new jersey and long island , fairfield county , connecticut , westchester county , new york , orange county , new york and rockland county , new york . other websites . - the municipal art society of new york , producers of the tribute in light - tribute in light video - creativetime.org : tribute in light - `` from 88 searchlights , an ethereal tribute '' , `` the new york times '' , march 4 , 2002
