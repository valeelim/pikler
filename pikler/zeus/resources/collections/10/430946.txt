Zekes     zekes was a dansband from skara , sweden . it was formed in january 2009. the band participated at dansbandskampen in 2009 , where they reached the final . on 27 december 2009 , the song `` i ett fönster '' entered svensktoppen , the swedish record chart . in june 2012 , sr skaraborg announced that the band would split up after its summer tour of 2012. their final appearance was at matfestivalen in skövde in august that year . members . - rickard carlsson ( vocals , guitar ) - mathias johansson ( bass ) - jakob stenseke ( saxophone , guitar , vocals ) - anton johansson ( keyboard , saxophone , vocals ) - simon warnskog ( drums ) svensktoppen songs . - i ett fönster- 2009 svensktoppen test ( failed to enter chart ) . - vi lyfter igen-2011 - samma sak- 2012
