Roger Corman     roger corman ( born april 5 , 1926 ) is an american movie director , producer , and actor . he made dozens of low-budget b-movies such as `` swamp women '' ( 1955 ) , `` the little shop of horrors '' ( 1960 ) , and `` the saga of the viking women and their voyage to the waters of the great sea serpent '' . he is best known for six movies based on the short stories of edgar allan poe that include `` house of usher '' ( 1960 ) , `` the pit and the pendulum '' ( 1961 ) , and `` the masque of the red death '' ( 1964 ) .
