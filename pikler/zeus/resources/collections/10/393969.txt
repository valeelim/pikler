Moccasin     a moccasin is a shoe made of deerskin or any other soft leather . it has a sole and sides made out of a single piece of leather . it is stitched together toward the top , sometimes with vamps ( which are additional panels of leather ) . the soles are usually soft and flexible . moccasins are usually worn outside . the shoes protect the foot while allowing whomever wears them to feel the ground . in modern times , moccasins are worn almost exclusively by women , although sometimes by men .
