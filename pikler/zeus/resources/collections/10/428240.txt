Felicity Jones     felicity rose hadley jones ( born 17 october 1983 ) is a british television , stage and movie actress . she starred in movies including `` brideshead revisited '' ( 2008 ) , `` cemetery junction '' ( 2009 ) and `` rogue one '' ( 2016 ) . jones was born in birmingham . sources differ on her year of birth , with some saying 1983 and others saying 1984 .
