Lưu Hữu Phước     lưu hữu phước ( 12 september 1921 - 8 june 1989 ) was a vietnamese composer . he composed `` thanh niên hành khúc '' ( english : `` march of the students '' ) , the national anthem of south vietnam . after he became a communist , he composed `` giải phóng miền nam '' ( english : release the south ) , the national anthem of provisional revolutionary government of the republic of south vietnam .
