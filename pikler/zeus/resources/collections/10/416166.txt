Padmé Amidala     padmé amidala is a character in the fictional `` star wars '' universe . she is the secret wife of anakin skywalker and the mother of luke skywalker and leia organa . in `` the phantom menace '' , padmé is the fourteen-year-old queen of the planet naboo . in the movie , amidala gets captured by the evil trade federation , meets the young slave boy anakin skywalker , and takes her captured planet back from the trade federation . in `` attack of the clones '' , padmé is not the queen anymore ; she is now an adult , and a senator . she and anakin skywalker fall in love though she said it would make them live a lie . even though anakin is a jedi , and jedi are n't supposed to get married , padmé and anakin marry anyway . `` revenge of the sith '' is the last movie padmé is in . she is going to have a baby , and her husband anakin dreams that padmé will die when she has the baby . palpatine , a man that anakin trusts , tells anakin that he can save padmé with the evil dark side of the force . so anakin goes to the dark side but little does he know that the power to save people from death was achieved by a jedi not a sith . he goes to a lava planet called mustafar , and his master obi-wan kenobi , along with padmé , follow him there . anakin , who is completely evil because of the dark side , thinks that padmé had gotten obi-wan to come with her ( she had n't ) . anakin chokes padmé using the force and she blacks out and crumples to the floor .
