Miller's Crossing    miller 's crossing miller 's crossing is a 1990 mystery crime drama thriller movie . marcia gay harden plays verna bernbaum . frances mcdormand plays a secretary . the subject in the movie is mafia related issues and crimes . the movie was released in september 1990 in the united states . the budget was over $ 14 million . `` miller 's crossing '' earned back only $ 5 million . however , the movie was critically praised .
