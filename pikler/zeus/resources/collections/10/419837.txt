Mutya Keisha Siobhan     mutya keisha siobhan ( mks ) is an english girl group . it formed in london in 2011. its members are siobhán donaghy , mutya buena and keisha buchanan . they were the original members of sugababes , but siobhán left in 2001 , mutya left in 2005 , and keisha left in 2009. in 2013 , they released their first single `` flatline '' . it was unsuccessful in the uk but reached 14 in the irish chart . their first album will be released by polydor in 2014. they started a uk tour in november 2013 .
