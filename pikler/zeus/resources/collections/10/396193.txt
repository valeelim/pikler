Anglo-Zulu War     the anglo-zulu war was a war fought from 1879 to 1887 between the zulu people and the british empire . there was no declaration of war . the war was fought in south africa , mostly . the zulu were victorious at first , and won the battle of isandlwana . later their luck turned , and the british empire won the battle of ulundi . the zulu lost the war , and the zulu kingdom ceased to exist as an independent state . today , the area is part of south africa , and is called kwazulu-natal .
