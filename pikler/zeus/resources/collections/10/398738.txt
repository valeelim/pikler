Andrea Rothfuss     andrea rothfuss ( born 20 october 1989 in freudenstadt ) is a skier from germany . she has a disability : she was born without a left hand . she skied at the 2011 ipc alpine skiing world championships . she was the first skier to finish in the standing women 's downhill race and the slalom race . she was the second skier to finish in the super combined . she was the third skier to finish in the super g race and the giant slalom race . other websites . - official website
