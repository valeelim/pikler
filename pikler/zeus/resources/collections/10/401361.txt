Ayelet Zurer     ayelet zurer ( born 28 june 1969 ) is an israeli actress . she is known for her roles in `` angels & demons '' , `` fugitive pieces '' , and in `` man of steel '' . zurer was born on 28 june 1969 in tel aviv , israel to a jewish family . she has been married to gilad londovski since 2003. they had one child . other websites . - ayelet zurer at theiapolis.com - ayelet zurer at e-online
