Fort Minor     fort minor was an american hip hop music act from los angeles . it was a small side project of mike shinoda , who is better known as the rapper and co-founder of the band linkin park . shinoda was the only member . he wanted to make this side-project because he only had limited vocal credited in linkin park and he could not always use hip-hop style . fort minor 's first album was `` the rising tied '' , which was released 22 november 2005 .
