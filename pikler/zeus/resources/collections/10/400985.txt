My So-Called Life     my so-called life is an american teen drama television series . it takes place at the fictional liberty high school , in pittsburgh , pennsylvania . the main character is angela chase . she is played by claire danes . the last show was a cliffhanger . the people who made the program thought it would be on television for another season . it was canceled on may 15 , 1995. many people enjoyed the program because the characters seemed like real teens . in 2007 , it was listed as one of `` time '' magazine 's `` 100 best tv shows of all- '' time '' '' . `` tv guide '' ranked the series number 16 on its 25 top cult shows ever list . atlantic records released a soundtrack of the show , which originally released on august 25 , 1994 , then re-released on january 24 , 1995 . 1 . `` make it home '' by juliana hatfield – 4:44 1 . `` soda jerk '' by buffalo tom – 4:26 1 . `` genetic '' by sonic youth – 3:46 1 . `` petty core '' by further – 3:46 1 . `` drop a bomb '' by madder rose – 2:11 1 . `` fountain and fairfax '' by afghan whigs – 4:21 1 . `` south carolina '' by archers of loaf – 3:30
