The Ice Storm     the ice storm is a 1997 american drama movie . the movie is about two families in new canaan , connecticut who are dealing with politics and serious social matters in november 1973. these two families do that using adultery , alcohol and sexual experimenting . christina ricci , elijah wood and kevin kline are in the cast . `` the ice storm '' received very positive reviews from the critics . its performance at the box office , however was terrible : the movie only got back close to half its $ 18 million budget . this movie was released in the united states in autumn 1997 .
