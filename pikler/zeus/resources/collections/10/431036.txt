Allan McKeown     allan john mckeown ( 21 may 1946 – 24 december 2013 ) was a british television producer . he was known for producing `` tracey takes on ... '' . this show starred his wife tracey ullman . he won an emmy award . mckeown was born in london . he married ullman in 1983. they had two children . their marriage would last until mckeown 's death . he lived in los angeles . his net worth was £75 million . mckeown died in los angeles , after suffering from prostate cancer .
