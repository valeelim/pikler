Hurricane Gert (1993)    hurricane gert ( 1993 ) hurricane gert was a destructive and very deadly tropical cyclone in september 1993. it struck central america and mexico . the storm affected almost the same region devastated by hurricane diana three years prior , except winds were a little stronger than for diana . winds for gert were 105 miles per hour ; ninety lives were lost , and forty three other people were missing due to hurricane gert . despite the devastation , the name gert was not retired in spring of 1994 .
