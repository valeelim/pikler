Australian of the Year     the australian of the year is an award presented on australia day ( 26 january ) every year to an important australian . the awards began in 1960 and are supported by the australian government and several major companies . the awards are given by the national australia day council . over the years it has grown to include the young australian of the year and the senior australian of the year . an award to honor people at a local community level has also been developed as the asutralian local hero . the first australian of the year was nobel prize winner , sir macfarlane burnet .
