Jeanne Cooper     wilma jeanne cooper ( or just jeanne cooper ; october 25 , 1928 - may 8 , 2013 ) was an american actress . she was best known for her emmy award-winning role as katherine chancellor on cbs ' soap opera `` the young and the restless '' . she was a long serving television actress . cooper was born in taft , california on october 25 , 1928. she was raised in bakersfield , california . cooper was married to harry bernsen , jr. from 1954 until they divorced in 1977. they had three children , including actor corbin bernsen . cooper died on may 8 , 2013 from complications from copd in los angeles , california , aged 84. other websites . - jeanne cooper bio on `` the young and the restless '' cbs website - jeanne cooper obituary
