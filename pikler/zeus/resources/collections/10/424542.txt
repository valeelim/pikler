No Way Out of Texas: In Your House    no way out of texas : in your house no way out was a professional wrestling pay-per-view show made by world wrestling federation ( wwf ) . it was held on february 15 , 1998 at the compaq center in houston , texas . it was the twentieth in your house event . no way out later became the title of annual ppv held by the wwf . other websites . - official no way out of texas : in your house website
