Ed Shaughnessy     edwin thomas `` ed '' shaughnessy ( january 9 , 1929 - may 24 , 2013 ) was an american singer for swing music and bebop drummer who was associated with the tonight show band that appeared on `` the tonight show starring johnny carson '' . he was born in jersey city , new jersey and grew up in new york city . shaughnessy was married to ilene woods . he died on may 24 , 2013 , age eighty-four .
