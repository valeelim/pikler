Of Thee I Sing (book)    of thee i sing ( book ) of thee i sing : a letter to my daughters is a children 's book by united states president barack obama . it came out on november 16 , 2010. it is about the lives of thirteen americans : george washington , abraham lincoln , martin luther king , neil armstrong , sitting bull , cesar chavez , billie holiday , maya lin , albert einstein ( who was born in germany ) , georgia o'keeffe , jackie robinson , helen keller , and jane addams . obama finished writing the book in 2008 , after he was elected but before he took office .
