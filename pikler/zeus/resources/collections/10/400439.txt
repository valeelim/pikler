José Manuel Rodríguez Ibañez     jose manuel rodriguez ibañez ( born may 20 , 1966 in madrid ) is a track and field athlete from spain . he has a disability : he is blind and a t11/f11/b1 type athlete . he competed at the 1996 summer paralympics . he finished first in the triple jump and long jump . he finished second in the 100 meter race . he raced at the 2000 summer paralympics . he finished first in the triple jump and he finished second in the long jump . he competed at the 1996 summer paralympics . he finished 6 in the triple jump and 11 in the long jump .
