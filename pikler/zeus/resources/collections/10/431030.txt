Surge     surge was a soft drink manufactured by the coca-cola company . the product was made mostly in the united states beginning in early 1997. the soda was marketed as a substitute to mountain dew and to take customers from pepsi . the brand was very popular at first , but that did n't last . surge stopped being made in 2003. surge was re-released by coca-cola as an amazon exclusive in packs of 12 16oz can because of the success of the `` surge movement '' campaign on september 15 , 2014 .
