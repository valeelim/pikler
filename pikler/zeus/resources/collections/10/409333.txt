Elena     the name elena is a female given name . it is a variation of helen , which dates to the 12th century . `` elena '' was among the most popular female names given to girls in russia in 2004 , and third most popular in the republic of macedonia in 2006. notable people who have the name include elena kagan , a politician in the united states . hurricane elena went across the gulf coast of the united states in late august/early september 1985. the name `` elena '' was then retired and replaced by `` erika '' .
