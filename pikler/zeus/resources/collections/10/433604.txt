Scary Movie     scary movie is an american comedy movie . it is a parody of a horror movie . it is directed by keenan ivory wayans . it stars anna faris , jon abrahams , shannon elizabeth , regina hall , marlon wayans , shawn wayans and carmen electra . it was released to mixed reviews on july 7 , 2000. it was followed by four sequels : scary movie 2 , scary movie 3 , scary movie 4 and scary movie 5 .
