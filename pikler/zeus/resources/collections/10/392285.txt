Aaron Downey     aaron downey ( born august 27 , 1974 in shelburne , ontario ) is a canadian retired professional ice hockey right winger that played 10 seasons in national hockey league . he was one of few people that was not drafted by an nhl team or a major junior team . downey played in the nhl for six teams , the boston bruins for 1 game , chicago blackhawks for 39 games , dallas stars for 2 seasons , st. louis blues for 17 games , montreal canadiens for 2 seasons , and the detroit red wings for 2 seasons . since his retirement , he returned to detroit to become a part-time strength and conditioning coach for the red wings and grand rapids griffins .
