Combustor     a combustor is a part or area of a gas turbine , ramjet , or scramjet engine where combustion takes place . it is also known as a burner , combustion chamber or flame holder . in a gas turbine engine , the `` combustor '' or combustion chamber is fed high pressure air by the compression system . the combustor then heats this air at constant pressure . after heating , air passes from the combustor through the to the turbine . in the case of a ramjet or scramjet engines , the air is directly fed to a nozzle . components . - casing - diffuser - liner - snout - dome - fuel injector - igniter
