Jordi Morales García     jordi morales garcía ( born november 17 , 1985 in esparreguera , barcelona ) is a table tennis athlete from spain . he has a physical disability : he is class 7 player . he played table tennis at the 2000 summer paralympics , 2004 summer paralympics , 2008 summer paralympics and the 2012 summer paralympics . in 2004 , he finished third in the class 7 singles table tennis game . in 2012 , he finished second in the team class 6-8 game .
