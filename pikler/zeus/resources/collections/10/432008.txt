Dick Cheney hunting incident     the dick cheney hunting incident happened on february 11 , 2006 , when then u.s. vice president dick cheney shot harry whittington , a 78-year-old texas attorney , while participating in a quail hunt on a ranch in kenedy county , texas . both cheney and whittington called the incident an accident . on february 14 , 2006 , whittington suffered a non-fatal heart attack and atrial fibrillation due to at least one lead-shot pellet lodged in or near his heart . cheney did not speak publicly about the incident until february 15 in an interview with fox news . early reports indicated that cheney and whittington were friends and that the injuries were minor . whittington has since told the `` washington post '' that he and cheney were not close friends but acquaintances and has not said if cheney apologized .
