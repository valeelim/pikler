The Taming of the Shrew     the taming of the shrew is a play by william shakespeare . it is a comedy . shakespeare 's source was `` suppositi '' ( 1509 ) by ludovico ariosto . george gascoigne 's play `` supposes '' ( 1566 ) may have also been used . the play was probably first acted between 1593 and y first printed in 1623 in the first folio . the musical , `` kiss me kate '' , was based on the play . elizabeth taylor and richard burton made a movie of the play .
