Quvenzhané Wallis     quvenzhané wallis ( born august 28 , 2003 ) is an american actress . wallis ' first movie was `` beasts of the southern wild '' . she got nominated for the academy award for best actress . this made her the youngest person ever to be nominated for the award . in january 2013 , `` boneshaker '' , a short film , was released . wallis plays blessing in it . wallis played margaret northup in the 2013 movie `` 12 years a slave '' . wallis is going to play annie in a remake of the musical `` annie '' . it is being filmed currently . it is going to be released in 2014. wallis does the voice of almitra in the animated movie `` the prophet '' . it will be released in 2014. wallis was born in houma , louisiana .
