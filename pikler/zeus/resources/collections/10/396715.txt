Lousado     lousado is a freguesia in portugal in the vila nova de famalicão concelho . it covers 5.61 km² ( 2.17 sq . mi. ) . it has a 3,725 people ( 2001 ) . its population density is 664.0 people per km² ( 1,720 per sq . mi. ) . other websites . - lousado on freguesiasdeportugal.com - civil government and administrative information
