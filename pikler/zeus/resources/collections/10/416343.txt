Mobility aid     mobility aids ( or orthopedic aids ) are devices that are designed to help people walk or move better . they can make people hurt less and feel less pain . an example is a wheelchair . there are two types of orthopedic aids . one type means people can move part of the body less . the other type means people put less weight on their feet . splints , knee braces , shoe adjustments , position aids and abduction casts are orthopedic aids . children with a disability are two times more likely than children who do not have a disability to use an orthopedic aid . in croatia , health insurance pays for orthopedic aids for people less than 18 years old , or people who got hurt while working . related pages . - prosthesis
