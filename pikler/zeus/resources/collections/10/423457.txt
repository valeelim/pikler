History of bus transport in Hong Kong     the history of bus transport in hong kong started in 1920 when the first bus route started providing service . while there was the japanese occupation of hong kong , the japanese government had tried to turn bus service to the status before the war . however , it was n't successful since there has run out of petrol . after wwii , the bus companies temporarily used vans to carry the passages . they used back buses after about 100 buses were bought from the uk .
