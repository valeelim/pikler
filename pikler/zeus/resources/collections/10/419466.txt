Willa Ford     amanda lee modano ( earlier last name : williford , also called willa ford ; born 22 january 1981 ) is an american singer , songwriter , actress and television personality . she released her very first studio record , `` willa was were '' , in july 2001. ford hosted several reality television shows , posed for `` playboy '' and competed on the show `` dancing with the stars '' . the actress was born in florida .
