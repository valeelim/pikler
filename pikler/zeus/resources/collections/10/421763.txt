M61 Vulcan     m61 vulcan is a hydraulic and pneumatic rotary cannon . it has six barrels . it is cooled by air and fires by the power of electricity . it is a gatling-style rotary cannon . the size of its caliber is 20 mm . it fires at an very very high speed . it has been the main cannon armament of united states military fixed-wing aircraft for over fifty years . it was made by general electric at first , but today it is made by general dynamics .
