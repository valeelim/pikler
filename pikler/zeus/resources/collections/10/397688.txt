Gerd Langguth     gerd langguth ( 18 may 1946 in wertheim – 12 may 2013 in cologne ) was a german professor of political science at the university of bonn and the author of biographies of angela merkel and horst köhler . langguth studied at dietrich bonhoeffer school . he died in 2013 , aged 66. from 1976 to 1980 he was a member of the bundestag on behalf of the cdu . other websites . - personal web site
