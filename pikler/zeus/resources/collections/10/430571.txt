Pro Wrestling Illustrated     pro wrestling illustrated is the monthly american professional wrestling magazine published by the kappa publishing group . the pwi has also ranked a list of the top 500 professional wrestlers each year since 1991. as of 2013 , only jushin liger has appeared in every edition of the pwi 500. the first woman to be ranked in the list was jacqueline moore who was ranked # 249 in 1993. pro wrestling illustrated gives out many different awards every year . the awards they currently give out are : - wrestler of the year - tag team of the year - match of the year - feud of the year - most popular wrestler of the year - most hated wrestler of the year - most improved wrestler of the year - most inspirational wrestler of the year - rookie of the year - stanley weston award ( lifetime achievement ) - comeback of the year - woman of the year other websites . - official website
