Restless legs syndrome     restless legs syndrome is a serious neurological disorder . it involves an irresistible urge to move certain regions of the human body to stop odd or uncomfortable sensation . this condition often involves the legs , torso , the head or the arms . moving an affected part of the body modulates sensations , which may provide temporary relief . around 75 % of people who have rls have problems involving iron . other possible issues include varicose veins , sleep troubles , conditions involving blood sugar , adhd or fibromyalgia .
