Kwame Kilpatrick     kwame malik kilpatrick ( 8 june , 1970 ) is an american politician . he was a michigan state representative and mayor of detroit . he is a democrat . kilpatrick was mayor of detroit from 2002 until he resigned in 2008. in spring 2013 , kilpatrick was sentenced to almost 28 years in prison after being declared guilty on many criminal charges , including fraud . he went to federal prison in eastern michigan .
