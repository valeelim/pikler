Mary Had a Little Lamb     `` mary had a little lamb '' ( originally , `` mary 's lamb '' ) is a poem by sarah josepha hale . it was published in her `` poetry for children '' in 1823. it tells of a lamb who follows his little mistress to school . in the 1830s , lowell mason set the poem to music . in 1877 , thomas edison recited `` mary had a little lamb '' for the first recording of the human voice .
