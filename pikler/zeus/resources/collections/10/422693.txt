David di Donatello     the david di donatello award is a film award . it is presented each year by l'accademia del cinema italiano ( aci ) . there are 24 categories as of 2006. the trophy is a gold david statuette like donatello 's famous sculpture . it is on a square malachite base . a gold plaque shows the award category , year and winner . the 1956 `` david '' awarded to gina lollobrigida for `` beautiful but dangerous '' was auctioned at sothebys in 2013. other websites . - official website - david di donatello awards at internet movie database
