Atoms for Peace (band)    atoms for peace ( band ) atoms for peace is a band from california . they started in 2009. they did not have a name until february 2010. the members are thom yorke , flea , nigel godrich , joey waronker , and mauro refosco . in february 2013 , they released their first album , `` amok '' on xl recordings . the album charted at number 1 in nine countries . `` pitchfork '' scored it 6.9/10 . other websites . - official website
