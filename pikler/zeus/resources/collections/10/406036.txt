Rumination     rumination is the compulsively focused attention by one particular person toward their distresses , misery or worries and on possible causes or consequences . rumination is dwelling on or having thoughts about earlier events , failures , abuse or hardship . the tendencies to ruminate often serve for factors for clinical depression . habitual ruminators are more likely to become depressed and to carry major mental troubles , such as generalized anxiety disorder and suicidal behavior . one type of rumination , the state rumination , is connected with pessimism and serious neurosis .
