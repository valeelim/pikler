Nick Offerman     nick offerman ( born june 26 , 1970 ) is an american actor , writer , and carpenter . he is best known for his breakout role as ron swanson in the nbc sitcom `` parks and recreation '' . offerman was born on june 26 , 1970 in minooka , illinois . he was raised in chicago , illinois . offerman studied at the university of illinois at urbana-champaign . he has been married to megan mullally since 2003 .
