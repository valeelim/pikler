Vanesa Capo Perez     vanesa capo perez ( born october 3 , 1982 in balearic islands ) is a swimmer from spain . she has a disability : she has cerebral palsy and is an s6 type swimmer . she raced at the 2000 summer paralympics . she raced at the 2004 summer paralympics . she finished second in the 4 x 50 meter relay 20pts race .
