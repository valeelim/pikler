Neutral Milk Hotel     neutral milk hotel is an american indie rock band . they started in 1989. they were formed by singer , songwriter , and guitarist jeff mangum . he was the only member at first . then robert schneide joined the band for its first album , `` on avery island '' . it was released on merge records . after `` on avery island '' was released , julian koster , scott spillane and jeremy barnes joined the band . they then released an album called `` in the aeroplane over the sea '' in 1998. pitchfork scored their second album 10/10 when it was released again in 2005. in 2009 , amazon.com 's editors put `` in the aeroplane over the sea '' at number two on their list of `` the 100 greatest indie rock albums of all time '' . on december 11 , 2011 , the band released their first release since 1998. it was an ep called `` ferris wheel on fire '' . they are going to perform at the 2014 primavera sound festival in barcelona . in `` will grayson , will grayson '' , a 2010 novel by john green and david levithan , will grayson 1 is a neutral milk hotel fan .
