St Anne's College, Oxford    st anne 's college , oxford st. anne 's college is one one of the colleges of the university of oxford . it was founded in 1879 as the association for the education for women . it was the first institution in oxford for the education of female students . in 1942 , it was renamed as the st. anne society . in 1952 , it received a royal charter as a college , for female students only .
