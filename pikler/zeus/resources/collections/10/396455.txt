Shontelle     shontelle layne ( born october 4 , 1985 ) , or just shontelle , is a barbadian singer-songwriter . she mainly performs r & b , pop and reggae music . she released her debut album `` shontelligence '' in 2008 and her second album `` no gravity '' in september 2010. some if her recent songs achieved only modest success . she co-wrote `` man down '' by r & b singer rihanna in 2010. discography . - `` shontelligence '' ( 2008 ) - `` no gravity '' ( 2010 )
