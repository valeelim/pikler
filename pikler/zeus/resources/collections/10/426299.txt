The Book Thief (movie)    the book thief ( movie ) the book thief is a 2013 german-american war drama movie . it is based on the novel of the same name by markus zusak . it is directed by brian percival . the screenplay was written by michael petroni . the movie 's score is composed by john williams . the movie stars emily watson , geoffrey rush , sophie nélisse , ben schnetzer , roger allam , nico liersch , and joachim paul assböck . it was on november 15 , 2013 .
