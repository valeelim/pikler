Jessica Walter     jessica walter ( b. january 31 , 1941 near brooklyn , new york ) is an american actress . she played lucille bluth in the television sitcom `` arrested development '' . she also played tabitha wilson in the series `` 90210 '' . walter received an emmy award for outstanding lead actress in a miniseries or tv movie . she won that award for playing amy prentiss in the police drama television series of the same name . walter was born in brooklyn , new york .
