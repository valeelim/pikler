Hatfield, Herefordshire    hatfield , herefordshire hatfield is a small village near leominster in herefordshire . in the village is a caravan site called fair view caravan site . it has a small church called hatfield st. leonard , built . it is of norman design . it is built of local sandstone . other nearby and towns include bockleton , bromyard and leominister . the city of hereford is south of hatfield . the herefordshire trail ( walking trail ) runs through the village .
