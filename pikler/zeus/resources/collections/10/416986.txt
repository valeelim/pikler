Romeo and Juliet (ballet)    romeo and juliet ( ballet ) romeo and juliet is a ballet . the music was written by sergei prokofiev . the ballet is based on shakespeare 's play , `` romeo and juliet '' . the ballet was first performed in brno ( now in the czech republic ) in 1938. it has been re-choreographed many times , notably by sir frederick ashton in 1955 , john cranko in 1962 , kenneth macmillan in 1965 , and rudolph nureyev in 1977 .
