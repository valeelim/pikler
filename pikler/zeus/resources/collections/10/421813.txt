The Simpsons: Road Rage    the simpsons : road rage the simpsons : road rage is a 2001 action-adventure video game based on the animated television show `` the simpsons '' . it was released for the nintendo gamecube , xbox , playstation 2 and game boy advance . it is similar to the game `` crazy taxi '' , in that the player has to pick up people and drive them to where they want to go as fast as they can .
