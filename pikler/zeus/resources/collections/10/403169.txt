María Ángeles Fernández Lebrato    maría ángeles fernández lebrato maría angeles fernandez lebrato ( born september 12 , 1970 in valladolid ) is a cyclist and swimmer from spain . she has a disability : she is blind and is an b2/s12 type athlete . she competed at the 1996 summer paralympics in swimming . she was the second swimmer to finish in the 200 meter one person medley and the 100 meter freestyle . she was the third swimmer to finish in the 50 meter freestyle race , 400 meter freestyle race and the 4 x 100 medley relay 49 points race . she competed at the 2000 summer paralympics in cycling .
