PBR&amp;B    pbr & amp ; b pbr & b ( also called r-neg-b hipster r & b or alternative r & b ) is a musical genre which stylistic origins are derived from r & b , alternative hip hop , hip hop soul , rock and sometimes electronic dance music . it emerged in the late 2000s and did not have an official name to it , so people called it alternative r & b until 2011 when the term pbr & b ( an abbreviation of pabst blue ribbon ) came on twitter . some notable artist include the weeknd , frank ocean , miguel and drake
