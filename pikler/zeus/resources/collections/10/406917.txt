The Equalizer     the equalizer is a 2014 american action crime thriller movie . it is set in boston , massachusetts . it stars denzel washington and melissa leo . it is based on the the tv series of the same name . this movie is about retired intelligence officer robert mccall ( washington ) . he becomes a vigilante on behalf of helpless people , though he gets heat from the russian mafia . the movie was released worldwide on september 26 , 2014 .
