Sonic Lost World     sonic lost world is an action-adventure platforming video game for the nintendo 3ds and wii u game consoles . it is part of the `` sonic the hedgehog '' series . it was developed by sonic team and published by sega . it was released on october 18 , 2013 in europe , on october 19 , 2013 in australia , on october 24 , 2013 in japan , and on october 29 , 2013 in north america . the game takes its inspiration from the genesis-era `` sonic '' games . it has both side-scrolling 2d levels and 3d spherical levels like the ones seen in `` super mario galaxy '' . the nintendo 3ds and wii u versions are separate games with different level designs . the nintendo 3ds version has local and online multiplayer . it is able to connect with the wii u version .
