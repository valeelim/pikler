Nina Andrycz     nina andrycz ( 11 november 1912 – 31 january 2014 ) was a polish actress . her last movie was in the 2009 movie `` before twilight '' . andrycz was the wife of józef cyrankiewicz . she earned the name `` the great dame of polish theatre '' because of her long and successful career . andrycz died on 31 january 2014 in warsaw , poland from natural causes , aged 101 .
