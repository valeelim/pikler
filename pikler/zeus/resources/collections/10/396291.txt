Sony Pictures Studios     the sony pictures studios is a television and movie studio complex in culver city , california . it is owned by sony pictures entertainment . many movies and television shows are taped there , including the popular television game shows `` wheel of fortune '' and `` jeopardy ! '' . it houses the movie companies columbia pictures and tristar pictures . the complex started in 1915 as the triangle studios ( 1915-1918 ) , then paramount culver studios ( 1918-1921 ) , then goldwyn studios ( 1921-1924 ) it was acquired by american film and television company metro-goldwyn-mayer and renamed it mgm studios in 1924. many famous mgm movies such as `` the wizard of oz '' and `` singin ' in the rain '' were produced there . between 1927 and 1963 , 52 movies a year were shot there . in 1986 , mgm sold it 's studio lot to lorimar , later purchased by sony and redone as the sony pictures studios in 1989. in 2016 while all the sony and columbia titles are later named after tristar , it was purchased by pinewood and restored as the pinewood studios hollywood
