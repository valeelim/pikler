Sexless marriage     a sexless marriage is a marriage in which the partners do not have sex very often or at all . the u.s. national health and social life survey in 1994 ( laumann et al . 1994 ) found that 2 percent of the married people who answered said they did not have sex in the past year . the definition of a sexless marriage is often broadened , to include marriages where sex occurs less than ten times per year . using this definition , 20 percent of the couples in the national health and social life survey would be in the category . `` newsweek '' magazine says that 15 to 20 percent of couples in the u.s. are in a sexless relationship .
