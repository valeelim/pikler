Fred Durst     william frederick `` fred '' durst ( born august 20 , 1970 in gastonia , north carolina ) is an american musician and movie director known for being the lead singer of the nu metal band , limp bizkit . he is also known for his work in independent movies and made his directorial debut in with the 2007 movie `` the education of charlie banks '' . the second movie he directed was the 2008 movie `` the longshots '' . he was ranked at # 71 in the `` top 100 heavy metal vocalists '' by hit parader .
