The Holt School     the holt school is a secondary school for girls in wokingham , england . it has over 1200 girls from ages 11-18. boys are admitted to the sixth form . the school was founded in the 1930s . it is a specialized language and science college . it is in the top 200 highest achieving schools in gcse , as and a level exam results . ofsted judged the school to be 'outstanding ' and the sixth form to be 'good ' . in 2013 88.3 % of students got 5 a*-cs in their gcses .
