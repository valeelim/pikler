Goody Two-Shoes     the history of little goody two-shoes is a children 's book . it was published in london in 1765 by john newbery . the story is about a poor orphan girl who has only one shoe . a rich man gives her a pair of shoes . she is then known as goody two shoes . she becomes a teacher and marries a rich widower . the name 'goody two shoes ' has come to mean anyone who is virtuous . it is not known who wrote the book . some say oliver goldsmith , some say john newbery himself . other websites . - text of an 1881 reprint of the original version on project gutenberg - image of an 1787 edition - 18th century children 's book collections at the british museum - 1888 chromolithographed pictorial edition at the internet archive
