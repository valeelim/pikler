Lewiston, Maine    lewiston , maine lewiston is a city in androscoggin county in maine , and the second-largest city in the state . the population was 36,592 at the 2010 census . it is one of two principal cities of and included within the lewiston-auburn , maine metropolitan new england city and town area and the lewiston-auburn maine metropolitan statistical area . as of 2006 census estimates has a combined population of 107,702. it is also part of the extended portland-lewiston-south portland , maine combined statistical area , which has a combined population 621,219 as of 2006 estimates . other websites . - city of lewiston , maine - lewiston public library - `` lewiston sun-journal online '' - l/a arts - lewiston , maine detailed profile - the public theatre
