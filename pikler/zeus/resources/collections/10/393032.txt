Composite decking     composite decking ( also called wood-plastic ) is an alternative to timber . it is made with recycled plastic and wood . this type of decking is becoming more and more popular as it is eco-friendly in nature and helps to save forests as it can be used as an alternative to wood . it is being used in many environments projects including outdoor dining , leisure areas , balconies and caravan decks , in addition to many decks of all shapes and sizes .
