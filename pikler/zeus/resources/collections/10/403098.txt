Rancho Mirage, California    rancho mirage , california rancho mirage is a resort city in riverside county , california , united states . the population was 17,218 at the 2010 census , up from 13,249 at the 2000 census . the seasonal ( part-time ) population can surpass 20,000. many famous people lived here including frank sinatra , bob hope , fred astaire , ginger rogers , richard nixon , gerald ford , ronald reagan , queen elizabeth ii , and tom bosley .
