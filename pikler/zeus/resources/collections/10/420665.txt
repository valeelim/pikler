Evapotranspiration     evapotranspiration is the movement of water from the earth 's surface to the atmosphere by evaporation and transpiration . evaporation is the movement of water to the air from the ground or bodies of water ( such as lakes ) . transpiration is a process that happens in plants , in which water is lost through stomata in the leaves and becomes vapour in the air . together , these form an important process in the water cycle .
