The 40-Year-Old Virgin     the 40-year-old virgin is a 2005 american romantic comedy movie . it was written , directed and co-produced by judd apatow . steve carell plays the title character , andy stitzer , a 40 year old virgin who lives alone and is involuntarily celibate . he works in the stockroom of an electronics store and is socially inept . steve carell also helped to write the movie script . the movie got mostly good reviews from critics .
