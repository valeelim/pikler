City of London School for Girls     city of london school for girls ( clsg ) is a private school for girls aged 7-18 in london , england . it was started by william ward in 1894. it is associated with the city of london school ( a boys day school ) and the city of london freemen 's school ( a boys and girls day and boarding school ) . 97 % of a level results are a*-b grades . over 50 % study maths at a level . 30 % get into oxford or cambridge . famous women who went there include dido , tasmin lucia-khan , hermione lee , and romola garai . charlotte barbour-condini is currently a student , who was a finalist for bbc young musician of the year 2012 .
