The Campaign (movie)    the campaign ( movie ) the campaign is a 2012 american political comedy movie . it was directed by jay roach . it stars will ferrell , zach galifianakis , john lithgow , brian cox , and dan aykroyd . the movie was about two southerners from north carolina fighting for a seat in congress . wolf blitzer , piers morgan , bill maher , dennis miller , and chris matthews have cameo roles . it was released on august 10 , 2012 to positive reviews .
