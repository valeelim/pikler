NHL 14     nhl 14 is an ice hockey video game . it was developed by ea canada and published by ea sports . it was released on september 10 , 2013 in north america , september 12 , 2013 in australasia , and on september 13 , 2013 in europe . the game was the 23rd in the `` nhl '' video game series and is the sequel to `` nhl 13 '' . new jersey devils goaltender martin brodeur is shown on the cover of the game . this is the first time since `` nhl 97 '' that a goaltender has been featured on the cover . ign rated the game a 8.3 out of 10. they said , `` with much better collisions and a vastly improved fighting minigame , nhl 14 is becoming more gamey and less of a simulation , but that gives it a distinct and interesting flavor compared to its predecessors . '' gamespot gave the game a 6 out of 10. they said , `` nhl 14 adds little of note to nhl 13 aside from better hitting , more fighting , and a disappointing nhl 94 retro mode . ''
