Hurt (Nine Inch Nails song)    hurt ( nine inch nails song ) `` hurt '' is a song written by trent reznor . it was first released on nine inch nails ' 1994 album `` the downward spiral '' . it received a grammy award nomination for best rock song in 1996 but lost to alanis morissette 's `` you oughta know '' . originally recorded by nine inch nails , the song `` hurt '' has been adapted and covered by several artists , including johnny cash and leona lewis . the song clearly includes references to self-harm and heroin addiction , although the overall meaning of the song is unknown . in 2002 , johnny cash covered the song for his album , `` '' . the line `` crown of shit '' was changed to `` crown of thorns '' , not only removing profanity from the lyrics , but also more directly referencing christ and cash 's devout christianity . its accompanying video , featuring images from cash 's life and directed by mark romanek , was named the best video of the year by the grammy awards and cma awards , and the best video of all time by `` nme '' in july 2011. the cover was released on a single with the b-side 'personal jesus ' , a cover of the depeche mode single .
