Doug Mohns     douglas allen `` diesel '' mohns ( december 13 , 1933 – february 7 , 2014 ) was an canadian-born american professional ice hockey player . he played 22 seasons in the national hockey league from 1953–54 until 1974–75 . mohns twice won the most coveted prize in junior hockey , the memorial cup . he played on the 1951 and 1953 barrie flyers teams . he died on february 7 , 2014 at the age of 80 .
