Judith Butler     judith butler ( born february 24 , 1956 ) is an american philosopher . she was born in ohio . she got her ph.d. in philosophy from yale university in 1984. she lives in berkeley , california , with her partner wendy brown . in 1990 `` gender trouble '' was published by routledge . it is considered one of the key works of contemporary feminism . in the book butler talks about her theory of gender performativity , which is that gender , along with sex and sexuality , is something you `` perform '' rather than an expression of human biology . butler was born in ohio . she went to hebrew school . she studied philosophy at yale university .
