Raquel Garcia Borreguero     raquel garcia borreguero ( born august 13 , 1986 in toledo , spain ) is a skier from spain . she helps skiers who can not see by telling them where to go on a mountain . skiing with anna cohí fornell , she has race in the european cup , 2010 winter paralympics , and ipc alpine skiing world cup . garcia and cohí skied with the spanish national team at a training camp .
