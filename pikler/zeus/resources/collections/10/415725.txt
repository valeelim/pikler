University of Fort Hare     the university of fort hare is a public university in alice , eastern cape , south africa . it was an important university for black africans from 1916 to 1959. it offered a western-style , academically excellent education to students from across sub-saharan africa , creating a black african elite . nelson mandela studied at the university during his early years before leading a group of students on a political strike and getting expelled . other websites . - official website
