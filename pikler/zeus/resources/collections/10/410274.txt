Skaraborg County     skaraborg county ( ) was a county in southern sweden . the seat was first in skara , then in mariestad from 1660. it was established in 1634 , and disestablished on 31 december 1997 , following the establishment of västra götaland county . governors . - harald strömfelt ( 1695–1707 ) - carl gustaf soop ( 1707–1711 ) - germund cederhielm d.ä .
