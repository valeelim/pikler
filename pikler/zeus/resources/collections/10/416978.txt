Māori Wikipedia     māori wikipedia is wikipedia in the maori language . it is a free and publicly editable online encyclopaedia . was created february 28 , 2004. as of may 13 , 2012 the mongolian wikipedia contains 7599 articles . the number of active participants is small , about 104 people ( august 2011 ) , while the number of registered users is 17,763 people . separate wikipedias have been created for several maori dialects , including buryat ( ) and kalmyk ( ) . other websites . - maori wikipedia - wikipedia statistics maori — maori wikipedia
