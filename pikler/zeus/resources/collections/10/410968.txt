The Conjuring (movie)    the conjuring ( movie ) the conjuring is a 2013 american supernatural horror movie . the movie is based on the real life events of ed and lorraine warren . it is set in rhode island in 1971. it stars patrick wilson , vera farmiga , ron livingston , lili taylor , mackenzie foy , and joey king . `` the conjuring '' was released in the united states and canada on july 19 , 2013 , and in the united kingdom on august 2 , 2013. a prequel titled `` annabelle '' was released in october 2014. a sequel , `` conjuring 2 '' will be released in june 2016.pranav
