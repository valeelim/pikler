Aphrodite (album)    aphrodite ( album ) aphrodite is the eleventh studio album by australian singer kylie minogue . it was released worldwide in july 2010 by parlophone records . the album contains four singles , `` all the lovers '' , `` get outta my way '' , `` better than today '' and `` put your hands up ( if you feel love ) '' . the four singles have all reached number one on the billboard hot dance club songs chart . other websites . - official website of kylie minogue
