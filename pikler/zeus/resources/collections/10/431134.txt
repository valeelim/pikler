Elvira Quintillá     elvira quintillá ( 19 september 1928 – 27 december 2013 ) was a spanish actress . her career has lasted for nearly 60 years . quintillá was born in barcelona . her career began in 1941. in 1947 , she married josé maría rodero . they were married until rodero 's death in may 1991. quintillá died at the age of 85 , in madrid . she died from a heart attack .
