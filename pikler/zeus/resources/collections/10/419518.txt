Mamie Gummer     mary willa `` mamie '' gummer ( born august 3 , 1983 ) is an american actress . she has been in many movies and television programs . gummer was born in new york city , new york . she is the daughter of actress meryl streep and sculptor don gummer . she grew up in salisbury , connecticut . she also spent five years in los angeles , california with her older brother , henry wolfe gummer , and younger sisters , actress grace gummer and louisa .
