Afroman     joseph edgar foreman ( born july 28 , 1974 in palmdale , california ) is an american rapper . he is better known by his stage name afroman . he was nominated for a grammy award for `` best rap solo performance '' for his song `` because i got high '' in 2002. he released his first studio album `` because i got high '' on june 20 , 2000. the album featured the song `` because i got high '' . his second studio album `` sell your dope '' was released on september 12 , 2000. it featured the song `` crazy rap '' which is also called `` colt 45 and 2 zig zags '' . other websites . - official website
