Songs of a Wayfarer     songs of a wayfarer is a set of four songs for low voice and orchestra by gustav mahler . mahler started writing the music in 1884. the music was first performed on march 16 , 1896. there are four songs : `` when my sweetheart is married '' , `` i went this morning over the field '' , `` i have a gleaming knife '' , and `` the two blue eyes of my beloved '' .
