Neoarchaean     the neoarchaean ( neoarchean ) is the last geological era in the archaeaneon . it began 2.8 billion years ago and ended at 2.5 billion years ago . before it was the mesoarchaean and before the palaeoproterozoic . oxygenic photosynthesis first evolved in this era . this led to an oxygen catastrophe in the palaeoproterozoic . it was caused by a poisonous buildup of oxygen in the atmosphere . this was created by oxygen-producing photoautotrophs that evolved in the neoarchaean . the supercontinent kenorland formed during this period , about 2.7 billion years ago .
