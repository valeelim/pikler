Brunhilde     brunhilde was a mighty female warrior , one of the valkyries . she was also a heroine from the german epics , especially in the nibelungen saga , in which she is an icelandic princess . she defied odin and in punishment he imprisoned her within a ring of fire on earth . she had to stay there until someone rescued her . siegfied ( sigurd ) braved the fire , woke her , and fell in love with her . he gave her the ring andvarinaut , unaware of its curse . eventually she killed herself when she learned that sigurd had betrayed her with another woman ( gudrun ) , not knowing he had been bewitched into doing so by grimhild . sources . - http : //www.pantheon.org/articles/b/brunhilde.html
