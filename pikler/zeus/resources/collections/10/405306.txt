George Becwar     george jerome becwar ( september 16 , 1917 - july 9 , 1970 ) was an actor . he was born in berwyn , illinois . he played prof. strowski in ed wood 's `` bride of the monster '' ( 1955 ) . he appeared in 1950s-early 1960s television series like `` sky king '' , `` fury '' , `` sea hunt '' , and `` the rebel '' . he died in santa monica , california .
