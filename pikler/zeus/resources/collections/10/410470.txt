The Ark     the ark was a rock band from rottne in sweden , active between 1991 and 2011. started up by vocalist and priest-son ola salo , the band participated at eurovision song contest 2007 with the song `` the worrying kind '' . discography . albums . - 2000 – `` we are the ark '' - 2002 – `` in lust we trust '' - 2004 – `` state of the ark '' - 2007 – `` prayer for the weekend '' - 2010 – `` in full regalia '' - 2011 – `` arkeology ''
