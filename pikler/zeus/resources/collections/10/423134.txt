Division of Cunningham     the division of cunningham is an australian electoral division in new south wales . it was set up in 1949 and is named for allan cunningham , a 19th century explorer of new south wales and queensland . it is on the coast of new south wales and includes parts of the city of wollongong , corrimal , figtree and unanderra . it also includes several suburbs of sydney , including heathcote and bundeena . rex connor was the minister for minerals and energy in the whitlam government . stephen martin was speaker of the house of representatives from 1993-1996. sharon bird held several ministerial positions in the gillard and rudd governments . election results . - 2004 election results - 2007 election results - 2010 election results - 2013 election results there have been two by-elections in cunningham . the first was in 1956 when the member , billy davis , died . there was only one nomination , victor kearney , who was elected unopposed . the second was in 2002 when stephen martin resigned . michael organ for the greens was elected , the first time the greens had won a seat in the house of representatives .
