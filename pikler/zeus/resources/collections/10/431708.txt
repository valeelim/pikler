Insider trading     insider trading means the trading of public companies ' stocks and other assets on stock markets using secret information . this is done by getting people who have confidential or non-public facts about how much a stock is really worth to tell traders so they can buy or sell . a stock could be worth more or less than the public knows , depending on how well a company is doing in making money . in many countries , insider trading with secret information is against the law and thought to be a bad thing to do . some economists think insider trading should be allowed .
