Sonian Forest     the sonian forest ( , ) is a large forest in the south-eastern part of brussels , belgium . the forest is in the flemish parts of sint-genesius-rode , hoeilaart , overijse and tervuren , uccle , watermael-boitsfort , auderghem and woluwe-saint-pierre in the brussels-capitale region and in the walloon towns of la hulpe and waterloo . thus it stretches out over the three belgian regions . it used to be connected with bois de la cambre and hallerbos ( blue forest in belgium ) but they split up in 1900. it is maintained by flanders ( 56 % ) , the brussels-capitale region ( 38 % ) and wallonia ( 6 % ) . there are some parts of the forest privately held forest and the kapucijnenbos , the `` capuchin wood '' , which belongs to the royal trust . auguste rodin went to the forest while living in brussels in the 1870s . he made several paintings of the forest during this time .
