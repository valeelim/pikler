Samuelsons     samuelsons was a country and gospel group from sweden , between the 1960s and 80 's . it consisted of the brothers rolf ( 1939–1981 ) , kjell ( born 942 ) , olle ( född 1950 ) and jard samuelson ( born 1952 ) . in december 1966 the first album was released in the usa , and was awarded three stars by billboard . they soon became popular throughout europe , canada and the usa .
