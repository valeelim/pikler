Covert operation     a covert operation is an operation that is done in a way that keeps it secret . the term suggests some kind of important reason . examples might be : 1. a kind of surveillance to collect information . 1. to gather information for future arrest and prosecution 1. to get information which is needed for government security 1. an attempt to rescue or to capture a person without it being known . covert operations may be done by police . they can also be done by security agencies such as the central intelligence agency ( us ) or mi5 ( uk ) .
