Juan António Prieto Cardenas     juan antonio prieto cardenas ( born march 6 , 1969 in jaén ) is a track and field athlete from spain . he has a disability : he is blind and a t12/b2 type athlete . he raced at the 1996 summer paralympics . he raced and finished in finished first in the 4 x 400 meter t11-t13 race and in the 100 meter t12 race . he finished second in the 200 meter t12 race . he raced at the 2000 summer paralympics . he finished third in the 4 x 100 meter t11-t13 race .
