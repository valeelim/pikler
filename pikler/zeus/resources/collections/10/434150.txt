Mr. Lordi     mr. lordi ( born 15 february 1974 ) is a finnish musician , painter and graphic designer . he is the singer and songwriter for the hard rock band lordi . he also makes all the costumes for the band . his real name is tomi petteri putaansuu . putaansuu was born in rovaniemi , finland . he studied filmmaking in tornio . while there , he created lordi 's first music video , `` inferno '' . he formed the band lordi in 1992. all publicity photographs of mr. lordi have his face hidden behind his mask and stage makeup . he does not talk about his private life or his real name for the media .
