Dr. Seuss' The Lorax    dr. seuss ' the lorax dr. seuss ' the lorax is an american animated movie . it is based on the book `` the lorax '' by dr. seuss . it is a family comedy . it is directed by chris renaud . it stars danny devito , ed helms , zac efron , taylor swift , betty white , rob riggle and jenny slate . it was released on march 2 , 2012. the movie received positive reviews . it has songs like let it grow and how bad can i be .
