Jackie Joseph     sammie jacqueline joseph ( or jackie joseph ; born november 7 , 1934 ) is an american actress . she plays the voices of characters in certain movies . she played sheila futterman in both `` gremlins '' movies in 1984 and 1990. she played jackie on `` the dick van dyke show '' . she was a regular on `` the doris day show '' , playing jackie parker . the actress was born in los angeles .
