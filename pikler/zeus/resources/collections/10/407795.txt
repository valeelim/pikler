Mrs. Miniver     mrs. miniver is a 1942 british drama movie . it won six academy awards , including the best picture and best director . the movie is about a housewife during world war ii who finds herself confronting a german pilot , and about relationship disagreement . walter pidgeon and greer garson play mr. and mrs. miniver . may whitty plays lady beldon . `` mrs. miniver '' was released in june 1942. it got very positive reviews from critics . it made a lot of money .
