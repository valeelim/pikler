Dewoitine D.520     the dewoitine d.520 is a french fighter aircraft . its first flight was in 1938. it was , with the morane saulnier ms.406 , the main fighter aircraft used by the french air force at the beginning of world war ii . it was used in the battle of france against nazi germany luftwaffe fighters like messerschmitt bf 109. after the 1940s french defeat , some of there were delivered to bulgaria and luftwaffe .
