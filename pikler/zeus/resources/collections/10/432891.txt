Steven Yeun     steven yeun ( born december 21 , 1983 ) is a korean-american actor . he is most well known for his role as `` glenn '' in the amc original series `` the walking dead '' . yeun was born in south korea and grew up in troy , michigan . along with `` the walking dead '' , he has acted in multiple movies and television series , such as `` the big bang theory '' and `` the lemons show '' .
