Despicable Me     despicable me is a 2010 american family/animated/comedy/crime movie directed by pierre coffin . it stars steve carell , will arnett , kristen wiig , russell brand , jason segel , julie andrews , ken jeong , and miranda cosgrove . it was released on july 9 , 2010. the movie received positive reviews . a sequel `` despicable me 2 '' was released on july 3 , 2013 .
