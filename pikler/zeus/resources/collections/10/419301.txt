Clonazepam     clonazepam is an anticonvulsant . this means it can be used to stop seizures . it is also used to lessen states of fear . it is an anxiolytic . it is a benzodiazepine . benzodiazepines are very addictive . clonazepam is used to treat certain forms of epilepsy . it is also used to treat certain disorders of sleep , such as sleepwalking . in certain cases it is used as an off-label drug in psychiatry . it is also used for panic disorders .
