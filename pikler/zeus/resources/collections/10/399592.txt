2013 Moore tornado     the 2013 moore tornado was a very violent ef5 tornado that went through moore , oklahoma on the afternoon of may 20 , 2013. it killed at least 23 people and hurt 377 others , and caused very heavy damage . it had winds of up to 210 miles per hour ( 340 km/h ) . this tornado reminded people of the similar tornado that struck moore fourteen years before , in 1999. other websites . - moore , oklahoma tornadoes ( 1890-present ) from the national oceanic and atmospheric administration
