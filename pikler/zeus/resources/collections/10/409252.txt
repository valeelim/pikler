Jeanne     the name jeanne is a female given name which is from french . the name is the equivalent for joan and jane . there are many celebrities with the name , including jeanne cooper , jeanne moreau and jeanne calment . `` jeanne '' was used for three hurricanes in the atlantic . hurricane jeanne in september 2004 killed over three thousand people in the caribbean islands and caused heavy damage in eastern florida . the name `` jeanne '' was then retired and replaced with `` julia '' .
