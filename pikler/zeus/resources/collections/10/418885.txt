Pihtipudas     pihtipudas is a municipality in finland . in august 2013 , 4,386 people lived there . it is in central finland . it is next to the municipalities of haapajärvi , keitele , kinnula , pielavesi , pyhäjärvi , reisjärvi and viitasaari . it contains the villages of alvajärvi , elämäjärvi , ilosjoki , korppinen , kärväskylä , muurasjärvi , pihtipudas , peninki , rönny , saani , kojola and seläntaus . the largest lakes nearby are kolima , alvajärvi and muurasjärvi . other websites . - municipal website
