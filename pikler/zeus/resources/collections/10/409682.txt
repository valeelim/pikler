Ron Arad (industrial designer)    ron arad ( industrial designer ) ron arad ( ; born 1951 in tel aviv ) is an israeli industrial designer , artist , and architect . arad became famous for his design of the design museum holon that opened in 2010. it was written about by travel magazine `` conde nast traveler '' as one of the new world wonders . he also designed the tel aviv bauhaus museum in 2008. other websites . - ron arad associates
