Oriented strand board     oriented strand board , commonly called osb , is a type of particle board which is made of carefully positioned strands of wood . aspen and other similar woods are cut into long strands parallel to the . it is manufactured into sheets made up of wood strands , wax , and resin adhesives . sheets are formed under pressure and heat . the finished sheet size varies by country . osb is very strong making it ideal for building construction . its most common uses in construction are for covering walls , floors and roofs before the finish products are applied . - osb-2 is specially designed osb for purposes . it is for use in dry areas . - osb-3 is designed the same , but is for use in wet areas . osb can also be made from trees from areas burned over by forest fires . related pages . - plywood - engineered wood - particle board - waferboard other websites . - apa - the engineered wood association : osb - kronospan worldwide : osb
