Julia Ormond     julia karin ormond ( january 4 , 1965 ) is an english actress . she became famous in the mid 1990s via the movies `` nostradamus '' , `` first knight '' , `` legends of the fall '' and `` sabrina '' . she also performed on television shows including `` nurse jackie '' and the television movie `` temple grandin '' . julia was born in surrey , england .
