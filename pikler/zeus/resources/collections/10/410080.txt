José Manuel Ruiz Reyes     josé manuel ruiz reyes ( born july 16 , 1978 in granada ) is a table tennis athlete from spain . he has a physical disability : he is class 10 player . he played table tennis at the 1996 summer paralympics , 2000 summer paralympics , 2004 summer paralympics , 2008 summer paralympics and the 2012 summer paralympics . in 2000 , he finished second in the men 's class 6-10 singles open and third in the class 10 team . in 2008 , he finished second in the team class 9-10 game . in 2012 , he finished third in the team class 9-10 game .
