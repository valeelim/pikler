Kevin Thorn     kevin matthew fertig ( born january 17 , 1977 ) is an american professional wrestler . he is best known for working with world wrestling entertainment ( wwe ) under the ring names , mordecai and kevin thorn . under the mordecai name , he portrayed a religious zealot and under his kevin thorn name , he portrayed a vampire character along with his manager ariel . in january 2009 , he was offered a new contract with wwe , but turned it down . wwe announced that he was released from contract by request on january 9 , even though his contract did not end until april 5 which prevented him from wrestling for other promotions . championships . - frontier elite wrestling - few heavyweight championship ( five times , current ) - few deadman 's heavyweight championship ( three times ) - memphis championship wrestling - mcw hardcore championship ( one time ) - mcw southern heavyweight championship ( one time ) - mcw southern tag team championship ( one time ) ( with trash ) - memphis wrestling - memphis wrestling southern heavyweight championship ( two times )
