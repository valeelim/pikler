Backlash (2002)    backlash ( 2002 ) backlash ( 2002 ) was a professional wrestling pay-per-view show made by world wrestling federation ( wwf ) . it was held on april 29 , 2007 at the kemper arena in kansas city , missouri . it was the fourth backlash event held by the wwf . this was the first time a wwf pay-per-view was held at kemper arena since wwf over the edge 1999 where owen hart , competing as the blue blazer at the time , lost his life after he fell 78 feet from a harness to the ring during an entrance stunt and landed chest-first on the top rope , approximately a foot from the nearest turnbuckle . other websites . - official backlash 2002 website
