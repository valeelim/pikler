Urðarbrunnr     urðarbrunnr ( old norse for the `` well of urðr '' ) is a well in norse mythology . it is situated under the yggdrasil or the world tree . norse mythology is a version of the older northern germanic mythology . the gods would travel via the bifrost with the exception of thor as it was thought that he would break the bridge which took the form of a rainbow , to urðarbrunnr . here they would meet and decide things , presumably laws of nature and magic and love and war .
