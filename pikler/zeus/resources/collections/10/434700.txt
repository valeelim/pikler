Georgi Slavkov     georgi slavkov ( ; april 11 , 1958 – january 21 , 2014 ) was a bulgarian football player . slavkov scored 61 goals in 112 games for trakia between 1976 and 1982. with cska sofia he played between 1982 and 1986 , scoring 48 goals . then he was transferred to the french club as saint-étienne . he finished his career in portugal in 1992. between 1978 and 1983 , he won 37 caps for bulgaria and scored 11 goals . georgi slavkov died suddenly on jan 21 , 2014 in plovdiv after suffering a heart attack at the age of 55. other websites . - profile at national-football-teams
