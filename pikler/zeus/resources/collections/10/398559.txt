Gregory Nouhaud     gregory nouhaud ( born 26 august 1982 ) is a skier from france . he helps skiers who can not see by telling them where to go on a mountain . he skied at the 2011 ipc alpine skiing world championships with nicolas berejny . they were the first skiers to finish in the visually impaired men 's downhill race . they were the third skiers to finish in the men 's visually impaired super-g race .
