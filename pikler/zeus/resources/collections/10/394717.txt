Burning of Jaffna library     the burning of the jaffna library ( ) was an attack at the central library in the city of jaffna , sri lanka . it was one of the events that led up to the sri lankan civil war . the first attack on the library building was on 31 may 1981. a group of sinhalese burned the library that was one of the important libraries in sri lanka and one of the biggest in asia . the event was one of the worst book burning events in the 20th century . after the event , the terror organization of `` black july '' burned the building several times until 1998 when the president of sri lanka tried to make peace between the two sides until the last opening in 2003. other websites . - about jaffna library in tamil - official website of the jaffna library - jaffna library vandalized 2010 - documentary on the burning of the library by someetharan - history of the jaffna public library - burning books - reported by kevin sites
