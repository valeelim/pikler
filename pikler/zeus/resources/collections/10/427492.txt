Taiwan Province, People's Republic of China    taiwan province , people 's republic of china taiwan ( ) is the claimed province by the people 's republic of china ( prc ) government to be part of prc , although officially it is currently under the control of the republic of china ( roc ) government . the recognized capital of the country is taipei city . taiwan province of the prc does n't include kinmen county and lienchiang county ( as currently under the control of roc government ) because those countries are considered to be part of the fujian province of the prc .
