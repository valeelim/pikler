Bad Moon     bad moon is a 1996 american horror and thriller movie . the movie is about strange events , full moons and werewolves . mariel hemingway plays attorney janet harrison . the movie was distributed by warner bros.. the movie was rated r for gore , horror , violence and some language . they had earlier given a higher rating based on graphic violence , which was then cut from the movie . when released , the movie received terrible reviews . `` bad moon '' was a huge box office flop .
