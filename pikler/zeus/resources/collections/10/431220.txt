Lovesexy     lovesexy is the tenth studio album by prince . it was released on may 10 , 1988. three singles were released from the album . in july 1988 prince started the lovesexy tour to promote the album . prince is naked on the cover of the album . some stores would not sell the album because of this and if they did they would cover it up in a black sleeve . the album went straight to number one in the uk .
