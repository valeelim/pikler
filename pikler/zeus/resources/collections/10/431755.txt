Seal of Missouri     the great seal of the state of missouri is an emblem that represents the u.s. state of missouri . it was officially adopted on january 11 , 1822. the center of the seal has two bears with the motto : `` united we stand , divided we fall '' . the outer circle has the words `` the great seal of the state of missouri '' . the year 1820 is shown in roman numerals . missouri , however , did not become a state until august 1821 .
