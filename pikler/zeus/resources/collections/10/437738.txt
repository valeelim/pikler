Morning in America     `` morning in america '' is the common name of a political campaign television commercial . the ad was part of the 1984 u.s. presidential campaign of republican party candidate ronald reagan . it was narrated by hal riney . it shows a montage of pictures of americans going to work and a calm , optimistic narration that suggested the improvements to the u.s. economy since his 1980. the term was used many times during reagan 's presidency .
