Strood     strood is a town in medway , north kent . it has a retail park which has the following stores : - next - argos - poundworld - carpet right - poundland - matalan - kfc in 2016 the b & q store at strood retail park was demolished , and new shops were built which include m & s foodhall , maplin , b & m bargains , starbucks and a gym . it has a railway station , strood railway station . it also has a leisure centre , strood leisure centre . strood includes several named areas , notably earl estate and marlowe park .
