Zbyněk Michálek     zbyněk michálek ( born 23 december 1982 , in jindřichův hradec , czechoslovakia ) is a czech nhl ice hockey defenceman that currently plays for the arizona coyotes . he was undrafted but started his career in the nhl with the minnesota wild . he played for the wild for a season and mainly stayed with their farm team , houston aeros whom he won the calder cup with in 2003. he was traded to the phoenix coyotes for erik westrum and dustin wood in 2005. he played 5 seasons with the coyotes and lead the league in blocked shots during the 2008–09 nhl season . he signed a 5 year , $ 20 million contract with the pittsburgh penguins on july 1 , 2010. he played 2 seasons with them and was traded back to the coyotes during draft day 2012 for harrison ruopp , marc cheverie and a 2012 third-round draft pick ( 81st overall ) . his younger brother milan plays in the nhl for the ottawa senators .
