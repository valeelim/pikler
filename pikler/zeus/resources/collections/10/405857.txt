Pablo Cimadevila Alvarez     pablo cimadevila alvarez ( born december 12 , 1978 in pontevedra ) is a swimmer from spain . he has a disability : he is an s6 type swimmer . he raced at the 2000 summer paralympics . he finished first in the 200 meter individual medley . he raced at the 2004 summer paralympics . he finished third in the 4×50 meter medley relay 20 pts race . he raced at the 2008 summer paralympics . he finished third in the 200 meter individual medley race and third in the 4×50 m medley 20 points race . he raced at the 2012 summer paralympics . in 2010 , he raced at the tenerife international open .
