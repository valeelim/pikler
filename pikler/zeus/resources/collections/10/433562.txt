Ronan Farrow     ronan farrow ( born satchel ronan o'sullivan farrow ; december 19 , 1987 ) is an american activist . he is the son of actress mia farrow . it is not known who his father is - it may be filmmaker woody allen or singer frank sinatra . he was born in new york city . he has written for `` the guardian '' , `` the wall street journal '' , and other newspapers . his book , `` pandora ’ s box : how american military aid creates america ’ s enemies '' , will be published in 2015 .
