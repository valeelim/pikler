Who's Afraid of the Big Bad Wolf?    who 's afraid of the big bad wolf ? who 's afraid of the big bad wolf ? is a song from the walt disney animated short movie `` three little pigs '' ( 1933 ) . it was written by frank churchill and ann ronell . the song buoyed spirits during the great depression . it inspired the title of edward albee 's `` who 's afraid of virginia woolf ? '' ( 1963 ) .
