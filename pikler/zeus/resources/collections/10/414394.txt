Eileen Essell     eileen essell ( 8 october 1922 - 15 february 2015 ) was an english actress . she was known for her roles in `` charlie and the chocolate factory '' and in `` duplex '' . she also starred in `` the bill '' , `` doc martin '' , `` holby city '' , `` hustle '' , `` casualty '' , `` ideal '' and `` sensitive skin '' . she guest starred as christine in the episode `` out of the rain '' in season 2 of the bbc spin-off show `` torchwood '' . essell was born on 8 october 1922 in london , england and died on 15 february 2015 in london , england .
