Kate Bornstein     katherine vandam `` kate '' bornstein ( born march 15 , 1948 ) is an american writer . she lives in new york city with her partner barbara carrellas . in 2012 she made a video for dan savage 's it gets better project . in august 2012 she was diagnosed with lung cancer . bornstein was born in new jersey to a jewish family . in 1970 bornstein joined the church of scientology . she left in 1981. bornstein does not feel male or female . she was born male . in 1986 she had sex reassignment surgery . she has written books about gender . the most well-known one is `` gender outlaw '' . in 2013 a documentary about bornstein called `` kate bornstein is a queer & pleasant danger '' was made .
