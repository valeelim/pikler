The Killers (1964 movie)    the killers ( 1964 movie ) the killers is an american crime-drama movie . it is directed by don siegel . the story is based on a book by ernest hemingway . it stars lee marvin , angie dickinson , clu gulager , john cassavetes , and ronald reagan . this was reagan 's last movie . it was composed by john williams . it was released on july 7 , 1964 .
