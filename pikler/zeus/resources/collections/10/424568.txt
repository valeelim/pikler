Scotts (band)    scotts ( band ) scotts is a modern dansband from lidköping , sweden . established in 1992 , scotts competed at dansbandskampen in 2008 , finishing up second behind larz-kristerz , starting to aim at their ambition becoming a full-time band . the band also competed at melodifestivalen in 2009 , with the song `` jag tror på oss '' . members . - henrik strömberg - vocal , guitar - roberto mårdstam - bass - claes linder - keyboard - per-erik `` lillen '' tagesson - drums svensktoppen songs . - `` cassandra '' - 1999 - `` marias kärlek '' - 2001 - `` en blick , en dans , en kyss '' ( one dance , one rose , one kiss ) - 2001 - `` om igen '' - 2009 - `` underbar '' - 2009 - `` jag ångrar ingenting '' - 2010 missade svensktoppslistan . - `` leker med elden '' - 2002 - `` jag tror på oss '' - 2009 other websites . - official webesite
