Bío Bío River     the biobío river ( also known as bío bío or bio-bio ) is a river in chile . it is the second largest river in chile . the river starts at icalma and galletué in the andes , araucanía region . the river flows 380 km to the gulf of arauco on the pacific ocean . other websites . - evaluacion de los recursos hidricos superficiales en la cuenca del rio bio bio ( source )
