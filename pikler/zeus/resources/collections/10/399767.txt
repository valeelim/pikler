Alameda High School     alameda high school is a high school in alameda , california . alameda high was founded in 1873 with the first class graduating in 1874. varsity sports include : - badminton - baseball - basketball ( men 's and women 's ) - football - diving - golf - cheerleading - cross country - soccer ( men 's and women 's ) - softball - swimming ( men 's and women 's ) - tennis ( men 's and women 's ) - track & field - volleyball ( men 's and women 's ) - water polo ( men 's and women 's )
