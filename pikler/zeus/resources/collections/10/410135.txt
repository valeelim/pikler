Laura Tramuns Tripiana     laura tramuns tripiana ( born february 19 , 1970 in badalona , barcelona ) is a swimmer and track and field athlete from spain . she has a physical disability and is a s8/t46 type athlete . she competed at the 1996 summer paralympics and 2000 summer paralympics in swimming . in 1996 , she finished second in the 100 meter breaststroke race .
