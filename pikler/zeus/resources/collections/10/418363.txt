Ulgan     ulgan ( , khakass : `` ӱлген '' , , , ) is a creator god in turkic mythology . he is ruler of the sky . he lives in a golden palace on altyn dagh ( `` '' , `` golden mountain '' ) . he has a golden throne and a lightning arc . his father is kaira khan . ulgan lives on 16th floor of the sky , and manages the stars and other objects in the sky . it was believed that at the beginning of the universe there was only ulgan and erlik . ulgan has seven sons and seven daughters . ulgan has seven sons , named akoghlanlar ( white boys ) . they are `` karakush khan , karshyt khan , pura khan , burcha kan , yashyl khan , er-kanym khan , bakty khan '' . and he has nine daughters , that named akkyzlar . no one knows the names of the daughters . they are the sons of ulgan . 1. karshyt khan : the god of purity . 1. pura khan : the god of horses . 1. burcha khan : the god of prosperity . 1. yashyl khan : the god of nature . 1. karakush khan : the god of birds . 1. kanym khan : the god of confidence . 1. bakhty khan : the god of blessing . related pages . - kaira - father of ulgan . sky god .
