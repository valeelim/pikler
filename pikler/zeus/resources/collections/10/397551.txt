Right upper quadrant (abdomen)    right upper quadrant ( abdomen ) the right upper quadrant ( ruq ) , is an anatomical area of the human body , this area may also be called the `` epigastrium '' . the ruq extends from the median plane to the right of the patient , and from the umbilical plane to the right ribcage . pain localized in the ruq may be a sign associated with various medical conditions : - hepatomegaly - enlarged liver - splenomegaly - enlarged spleen - hepatosplenamegaly both liver and spleen enlarged - gallstones - pyloric stenosis internal organs of the ruq the rightupper quadrant contains all or part of the following internal organs : - liver - gall bladder - duodenum - pancreas - colon - stomach
