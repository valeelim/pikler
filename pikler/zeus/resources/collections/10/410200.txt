Uğur Yücel     uğur yücel ( born 26 may 1957 ) is a turkish actor , director , producer and screenwriter . uğur yücel was born in istanbul . he graduated from istanbul municipality conservatory department of theatre . he worked in private theatres between 1975 and 1984. his first movie role is in `` aşık oldum '' ( 1985 ) . he is married with actress derya alabora and has a son . other websites . - uğur yücel at sinematurk
