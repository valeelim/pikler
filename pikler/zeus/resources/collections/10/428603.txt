Raman     sir chandrasekhara venkata raman , frs ( 7 november 1888 â€“ 21 november 1970 ) was an indian physicist . he studied light scattering . he won the nobel prize in physics in 1930 for his work in this subject . he discovered that , when light passes through a transparent material , some of the deflected light changes in wavelength . this phenomenon is now called raman scattering and is the result of the raman effect . he died of natural causes in his institute garden surrounded by flowers . he was awarded the bharat ratna in 1954 .
