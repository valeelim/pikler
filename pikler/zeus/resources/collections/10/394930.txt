Drew LeBlanc     drew leblanc ( born june 29 , 1989 , in duluth , minnesota ) is an american nhl ice hockey center that currently plays for the chicago blackhawks . he was not drafted by a team but he signed a 1-year contract with the blackhawks on april 12 , 2013. he played college hockey at st. cloud state university and won the hobey baker award as the top national collegiate athletic association ( ncaa ) men 's ice hockey player in 2013 .
