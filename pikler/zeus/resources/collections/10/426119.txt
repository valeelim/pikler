Daniel (mount)    daniel ( mount ) daniel is the highest peak of the ammergau alps . it is above sea level . it is in tyrol , austria above the villages ehrwald and leermoos . it is near two other peaks , upsspitze and hochschrutte . the climb to mount daniel first goes to tuftalm , a small lodge . from there , there are two paths to mount daniel , a easier one , and a more difficult one . tuftalm is above lermoos . to get there there is a path from erwhald or from lermoos .
