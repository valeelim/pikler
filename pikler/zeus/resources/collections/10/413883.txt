Jerónimo Carrión     jerónimo carrión y palacio ( 1804 – 5 may 1873 ) was president of ecuador between september 7 , 1865 and november 6 , 1867. he also served as vice president of ecuador and led a brief rebellion in 1859. he was a member of the ecuadorian conservative party . carrión died on 5 may 1873 in quito , ecuador from pneumonia , aged 69. other websites . - official website of the ecuadorian government about the country president 's history
