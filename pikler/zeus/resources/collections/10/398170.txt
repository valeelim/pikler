British North America     british north america was what the british-owned part of the continent of north america was called , mostly before the american revolution . `` see history of the united states # colonial america '' . after the battle of the plains of abraham the colony of new france was also under british control . after the american revolution , some people still used the term to refer to what is now the country of canada , and in fact the canadian constitution was first called the `` british north america act 1867 '' ( that being the year in which it first became a law ) .
