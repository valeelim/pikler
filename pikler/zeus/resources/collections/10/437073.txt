Maggie Hassan     margaret `` maggie '' hassan ( before that : margaret wood ; born february 27 , 1958 ) is an american politician from new hampshire . she is the u.s. senator from new hampshire . she previously served as the 81st governor of new hampshire from 2013 to 2017. she was elected governor in november 2012 and reelected in november 2014. in 2016 , she ran for the u.s. senate and unseated kelly ayotte , the republican incumbent in new hampshire . she narrowly won the election by less than 720 votes . hassan was born in boston , massachusetts .
