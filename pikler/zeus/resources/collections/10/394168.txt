Pikmin 3     pikmin 3 is a real-time strategy video game that was released by nintendo for the wii u game console . it is the sequel to `` pikmin 2 '' and the third game in the `` pikmin '' series . it was released in japan on july 13 , 2013 , in europe on july 26 , 2013 , and in north america on august 4 , 2013 . `` pikmin 3 '' has the same gameplay as the last 2 `` pikmin '' games . players control 100 plant-like creatures called pikmin . the player uses them to pick up object , reach areas , and fight enemies . there are three characters that players can play as . in addition to the five types of pikmin from `` pikmin 2 '' ( red pikmin , yellow pikmin , blue pikmin , purple pikmin , and white pikmin ) , there is two new types of pikmin . the rock pikmin can destroy tough barriers , while the flying pink `` winged '' pikmin can attack airborne enemies and carry items in the air . the player is able to control the game with the wii u gamepad or the wii remote and nunchuk combination . the wii u gamepad gives the player a map of the game environment , which is visible on its touch screen .
