Franco Bracardi     franco bracardi ( 16 may 1937 – 27 february 2005 ) was an italian actor , composer , pianist and stand-up comedian . he was born in rome and began working at night clubs by the early 1950s . later , in the mid-1960s , he started composing songs for mina , mirelle mathieu , raffaella carrà . during those same years , he started work as an actor and comedian .
