Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2010     the junior eurovision song contest 2010 was the eighth edition of junior eurovision song contest . it took place in minsk , belarus on 20 november 2010. the contest was won by vladimir arzumanyan of armenia with the song `` mama '' . this gave armenia its first win at junior eurovision and its first win in any eurovision contest , even though sweden had been the betting favourite . each country gave votes . half of the votes were cast by a jury , while the other half came from the public who voted via telephone and text messages . the voters decided their top ten songs using the points 12 , 10 , 8 , 7 , 6 , 5 , 4 , 3 , 2 , and 1 . - the rules stated that the participants must sing in one of their national languages , however they were permitted to have a few lines in a different language , as seen in some entries . - this was the first time since 2000 that the maltese language is used in a eurovision event since `` desire '' by claudette pace , the maltese entry for the 2000 contest .
