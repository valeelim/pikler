Juice (movie)    juice ( movie ) juice is a 1992 american crime drama movie . it is about four young african-american men in harlem who are being having trouble with police and gangs every day . they begin as friends , but the friendship goes bad after a robbery . one of the men shoots several others , killing two of them near a building , including his friend raheem . the murderer later dies from falling off a building . the movie got very positive reviews . it was filmed and completed in late 1991 .
