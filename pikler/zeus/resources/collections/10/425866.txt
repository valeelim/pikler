HiiiPoWeR     `` hiiipower '' is the debut single by kendrick lamar . it is a conscious hip hop song . it was released in april 2011 through top dawg entertainment . it was a promotional single that was produced by j.cole . its lyrics make references to african-american activists such as martin luther king and are about racism and the media . hiiipower was the only single realeased out of the album `` section.80 '' . the reason why the title has 3 ‘ i ’ s is because it stands for honor , respect and heart according to lamar . lamar was influenced by thug life , a hip hop group led by tupac shakur .
