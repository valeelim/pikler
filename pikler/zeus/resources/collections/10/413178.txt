Skin graft     skin grafts are transplants of skin . most common is to transplant skin from one part of a person to another place on the same person . the technique was developed in world war ii by surgeons who had to deal with people who had suffered severe burns . transplants of skin from one person to another is limited by tissue rejection caused by the immune system . as with organ transplants , tests are done to find a donor who is `` compatible '' with the host . identical twins make the perfect donors for each other because they are genetically identical .
