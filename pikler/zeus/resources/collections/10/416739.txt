Mamungkukumpurangkuntjunya Hill     mamungkukumpurangkuntjunya hill is a hill in south australia . it is in the everard ranges , about 22 km south-west of mimili . the name consists of several pitjantjatjara words made into one . it roughly means `` where a devil urinates '' ( a `` '' is a bad spirit ) . it is the longest place name in australia . other websites . - mamungkukumpurangkuntjunya hill on google maps
