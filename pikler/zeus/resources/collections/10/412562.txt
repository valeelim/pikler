Arrondissements of the Ariège department     there are 3 arrondissements in the ariège department . the french departments , and in other countries , are divided into `` arrondissements '' , which may be translated into english as districts ( in some cases , as boroughs ) . the capital of an arrondissement is called a subprefecture . if the prefecture ( capital ) of the department is in an arrondissement , that prefecture is the capital of the arrondissement , acting both as a prefecture and as a subprefecture . arrondissements are further divided into communes . the arrondissements of ariège are : since its creation , the ariège department has had few changes : - 1790 : creation of the department with three districts : mirepoix , saint-girons , tarascon - 1800 : creation of the arrondissements : foix , pamiers , saint-girons - 1926 : the arrondissement of pamiers is eliminated - 1942 : restoration of the arrondissement of pamiers related pages . - list of arrondissements of france
