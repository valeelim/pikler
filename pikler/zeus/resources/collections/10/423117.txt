Anna Favella     anna favella ( september 21 , 1983 ) is an italian actress . she works in television and movies . she is known for roles in the tv series `` terra ribelle '' and in the movie `` mr. america '' . anna favella was born and raised in rome . favella started acting when she was very young . her first minor roles were in programs such as `` enrico mattei - l'uomo che guardava al future '' and `` don matteo 7 '' . her first major role was in a tv series called `` terra ribelle '' , where she played the role of the main character , elena marsili . in 2013 , favella was the main character in the movie `` mr. america '' .
