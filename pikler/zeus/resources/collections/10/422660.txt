Jersey Girl (2004 movie)    jersey girl ( 2004 movie ) jersey girl is a 2004 american drama-comedy movie . it was written and directed by kevin smith . it was produced by scott mosier . it stars ben affleck , liv tyler , raquel castro , george carlin , jason biggs , jennifer lopez , stephen root , and will smith . jason lee , matt damon , and betty aberlin had cameo roles . it was released on march 26 , 2004 to negative reviews . critic roger ebert thought the movie was good because he gave it 3.5 out of 4 stars .
