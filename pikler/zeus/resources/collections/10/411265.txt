Hämeenlinna     hämeenlinna is a town in finland . as of january 2014 , about 67,750 people lived there . nearby municipalities include akaa , asikkala , hattula , hausjärvi , hämeenkoski , janakkala , kärkölä , loppi , padasjoki , pälkäne , tammela , urjala and valkeakoski . other websites . - community website
