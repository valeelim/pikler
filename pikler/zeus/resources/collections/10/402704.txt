Of Plymouth Plantation     of plymouth plantation is a book written by william bradford between 1630 and 1651. it tells of the pilgrims , of their years in holland , their voyage on the `` mayflower '' , the founding of plymouth colony in massachusetts , and the colony 's early years . the book ends with a list written in 1651 of mayflower passengers and what happened to them . other websites . - new england religious history at gordon college includes `` of plymouth plantation '' and other works in a variety of formats . - full text `` of plymouth plantation '' provided by google book search
