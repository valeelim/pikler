Pygmalion (1983 movie)    pygmalion ( 1983 movie ) pygmalion is a television movie from 1983. it was produced by margot kidder . kidder also stars in the movie , which also stars peter o'toole as professor henry higgins . it received many positive reviews . it was based on george bernard shaw 's play `` pygmalion '' . it was released on july 14 , 1983 .
