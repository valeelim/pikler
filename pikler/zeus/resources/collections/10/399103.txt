Chinese Rail DF4     chinese rail df4 is a kind of diesel locomotive used in china . it was built by dalian locomotive & rolling stock works . it has been in production since 1969 and is still produced as of 1984. the first df4 was built in 1969 in dalian . the first 108 locomotives used the 16v240zj engine . it took seven years to build all 108 locomotives of this type . but the first ever is a permanent exhibition at the beijing railway museum , and the second `` mao zedong '' is a permanent exhibition at the fengtai yard , beijing railway bureau . the df4a used an improved engine , the 16v240zja . production started in 1976 and lasted until 1984. df4s have been manufactured by several companies : - csr ziyang locomotive works ( df4a:3001~3042 ) - dalian locomotive & rolling stock works ( df4a:0001~0771,2001~2031 )
