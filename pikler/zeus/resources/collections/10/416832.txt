Flag of the Philippines     the flag of the philippines has two horizontal bands colored royal blue and scarlet . it also has a triangle on its left side with an 8-rayed sun and three stars . the three stars symbolizes the island groups of luzon , visayas and mindanao . the 8 rays of the sun of the flag symbolizes the provinces of manila , cavite , bulacan , pampanga , nueva ecija , tarlac , laguna and batangas . these provinces started the philippine revolution . the flag of the philippines can be also hoisted upside-down to show that the country is at war . the flag was designed by emilio aguinaldo .
