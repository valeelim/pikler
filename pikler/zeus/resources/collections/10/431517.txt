Samuel Rhoads     samuel rhoads ( 1711 - april 7 , 1784 ) was an american architect and politician . as an architect , he made drawings of what the pennsylvania hospital should look like . his designs were used and continued to be used after he died . he was the mayor of philadelphia in 1774. he also was a delegate in the first continental congress . he was friends with benjamin franklin . he was director of the library company of philadelphia . he was also involved in the american philosophical society , where he was vice president . other websites . - biographical sketch at philadelphia architects and buildings - brief biography at virtualology.com
