Bayonne Bridge     the bayonne bridge is a steel arch bridge that connects bayonne , new jersey and staten island , new york . it is the fifth-longest steel arch bridge in the world . it was the longest in the world when it was opened . it was designed by bridge-builder othmar ammann and architect cass gilbert . it was built by the port of new york authority ( now the port authority of new york and new jersey ) . it opened on november 15 , 1931. the bridge of one of three bridges that connect staten island with new jersey . the other bridges are the goethals bridge in elizabeth , new jersey and the outerbridge crossing in perth amboy , new jersey .
