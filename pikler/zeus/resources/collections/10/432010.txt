Mercedes Sosa     haydée mercedes sosa ( 9 july 1935 – 4 october 2009 ) was an argentine singer . she was popular throughout latin america and many countries outside the continent . sosa became one of the important examples of nueva canción . she gave voice to songs written by both brazilians and cubans . she was best known as the `` voice of the voiceless ones '' . sosa suffered from breathing problems in later years . sosa went into hospital in buenos aires on september 18 , 2009. she died from multiple organ failure on october 4 , 2009. she was aged 74. other websites . - tribute to mercedes sosa ( in portuguese br ) - mercedes sosa 's website ( in spanish ) - mercedes sosa 's news ( in spanish )
