Yeongwol County     yeongwol county ( `` yeongwol-gun '' ) is a county in gangwon province , south korea . it is well known as the place where king danjong , the sixth king of joseon dynasty , was exiled to , when he was forced to abdicate by his uncle , who became sejo of joseon . it is also where he is buried after he was murdered in 1457 , because he was perceived to be a continuing threat to the rule of his uncle . it is also where joseon dynasty poet kim sat-gat is buried . a tourist attraction in this area is the yeongwol insectarium ( ) , a private insect museum . it has a collection featuring a large quantity of insects from korea and all around the world . it was founded by professor lee dae-am who is professional architect and an amateur entomologist , and was opened may 5 , 2002. the building had been a school unti lee dae-am turned it into the insectarium . the aim of the museum is to create interest in insects , and to educate people on the importance of bugs to the world ecological systems . the yeongwol insectarium has been recognized as one of the best visitor attractions .
