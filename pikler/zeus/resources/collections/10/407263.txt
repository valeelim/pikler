Dudley Manlove     dudley manlove ( june 11 , 1914 - april 17 , 1996 ) was an american radio announcer , movie actor , and television actor . he had a few parts in movies that included the space alien , eros , in ed wood 's 1959 science fiction classic , `` plan 9 from outer space '' . on television , he appeared in many series including `` dragnet '' and `` alfred hitchcock presents '' .
