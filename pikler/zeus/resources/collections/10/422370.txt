Night of Champions (2013)    night of champions ( 2013 ) night of champions ( 2013 ) was a professional wrestling pay-per-view show made by world wrestling entertainment ( wwe ) . it was held on september 15 , 2013 at the joe louis arena in detroit , michigan . it was the seventh yearly event with the `` night of champions '' name . similar to the last five events , the concept of the show was that every championship in the company was defended at the event . other websites . - official night of champions 2013 website
