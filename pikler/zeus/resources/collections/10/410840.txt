Richard Erdman     richard `` dick '' erdman ( born june 1 , 1925 ) is an american actor , movie director and movie producer . he was known for his roles in `` stalag 17 '' , `` cry danger '' , `` the men '' , and in `` anything goes '' . he also appeared as leonard rodriguez in `` community '' . erdman was born on june 1 , 1925 in enid , oklahoma . he was married to leza holland from 1948 until they divorced in 1950. he has been married to sharon randall since 1953. they had one daughter , erica who died in 2010 .
