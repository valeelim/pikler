Jesús María Visitacion Villan     jesus maria visitacion villan ( born may 17 , 1966 in san sebastian ) is a 7-per-team football player from spain . he has a disability : he has cerebral palsy and is a c7 type player . he played 7-per-team football at the 1996 summer paralympics . his team finished third after they played the united states and won 2-1. he played 7-per-team football at the 2000 summer paralympics . his team finished 6 .
