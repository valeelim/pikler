Kika     kika is a 1994 comedy movie . the title character gets involved in relationships with different men and is assaulted several times . one of these assaults is by a pornographic movie actor with a very low iq . he assaults kika with serious force and is then pulled from her by two unknown people . the movie originally received an nc-17 just before its release in the united states half a year after completion . the rating , though , was surrendered , and `` kika '' was then released unrated .
