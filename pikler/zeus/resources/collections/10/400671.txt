Ectasian     the ectasian is the second geological period in the mesoproterozoic . it began 1.4 billion years ago and ended at 1.2 billion years ago . the ectasian came after the calymmian and before the stenian . this period shows for the first evidence of sexual reproduction . the 1.2 billion-year-old hunting formation on somerset island , canada , is from the end of the ectasian . it has the microfossils of the multicellular filaments of `` bangiomorpha pubescens '' ( a type of red algae ) . this was the first organism that had sexual reproduction .
