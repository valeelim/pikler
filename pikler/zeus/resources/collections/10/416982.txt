Night on Bald Mountain     night on bald mountain or night on bare mountain is a piece of music ( tone poem ) for orchestra by modest mussorgsky . he completed it in 1867. it was first performed in 1886 in a version by rimsky-korsakov after mussorgsky 's 1881 death . the music depicts a witches ' sabbath on st. john 's night . the music was used in walt disney 's animated movie , `` fantasia '' in 1940 .
