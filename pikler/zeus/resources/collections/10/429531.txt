Oriel College, Oxford    oriel college , oxford oriel college is one of the colleges of the university of oxford . it claims to be the oldest college at the university . university college , balliol college and merton college also claim to be the oldest . oriel college traces its origins to 1324. the full name of the college is `` the provost and scholars of the house of the blessed mary the virgin in oxford , commonly called oriel college , of the foundation of edward the second of famous memory , sometime king of england '' . the common name of the college refers to one of its earliest buildings that building , which no longer exists , featured a prominent bay window , known as an oriel .
