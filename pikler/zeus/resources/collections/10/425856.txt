Yonkers (song)    yonkers ( song ) `` yonkers '' is a song by odd future 's frontman tyler , the creator . it is from his debut studio album `` goblin '' . it features hardcore lyrics and also contains insults directed at singers hayley williams of the band paramore , bruno mars and rapper b.o.b.. this song is the one that odd future gained mainstream success with . it was high on the billboard charts , but lost to katy perry 's firework . in response to the insults , b.o.b . made a song entitled `` no future '' . other websites . - music video on youtube
