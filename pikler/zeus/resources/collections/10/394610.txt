Gingerbread Man (fairy tale)    gingerbread man ( fairy tale ) `` the gingerbread man '' ( sometimes , `` the gingerbread boy '' ) is an american fairy tale . it is a variant of the european runaway pancake story . the american version first appeared in the may 1875 issue of '' st. nicholas magazine '' . the story tells of a gingerbread man who runs away from the old woman who baked him . he is chased by several people and animals . a crafty fox finally eats him . the tale is known for the title character 's chant : `` run , run , run as fast as you can ! / you ca n't catch me , i 'm the gingerbread man ! '' gingerbread men were popular characters in children 's stories of the late 19th century . the most notable gingerbread character was l. frank baum 's john dough .
