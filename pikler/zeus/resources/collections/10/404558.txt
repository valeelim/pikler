Popeye (movie)    popeye ( movie ) popeye is a 1980 musical movie based on the characters and concepts created in e. c. segar 's `` thimble theatre '' comic strip . the movie starred robin williams ( his movie debut ) as popeye , paul dooley as wimpy , paul l. smith as bluto , ray walston as poopdeck pappy , and shelley duvall as olive oyl . it received mixed reviews .
