Main course     the main course is food that is served as the main dish in a multi-course meal . it is often served after the entrée . in the united states , the main course may be called the `` entrée '' . the main course is usually the biggest dish on a menu . the main ingredient is often meat or fish . it most often follows an appetizer , soup , or salad , and is often followed by a dessert . other websites . - wikibooks cookbook
