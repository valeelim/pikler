Enough Said (movie)    enough said ( movie ) enough said is a 2013 american comedy movie directed and written by nicole holofcener . it is a romantic comedy with julia louis-dreyfus , james gandolfini ( in one of his final film roles ) , toni collette , catherine keener , ben falcone , and toby huss . the movie was released to very positive reviews on september 18 , 2013 .
