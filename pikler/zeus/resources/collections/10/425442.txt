Battle of Guam (1941)    battle of guam ( 1941 ) the first battle of guam was a battle fought during the pacific war in world war ii . it took place on 8 december 1941 on guam in the mariana islands . the battle was between japan and the united states . the american forces on guam were defeated by japanese forces . the japanese forces then occupied the island until the second battle of guam in 1944 .
