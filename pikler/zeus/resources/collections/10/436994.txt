Rajendran Mani     rajendran mani is a bodybuilder from chennai , tamil nadu , india . mani was in the indian air force for 15 years . after he retired , he began weightlifting . he has won both the mr. india and champion of champion titles in india 8 times . he won the mr. world title at the fifth world bodybuilding and physique sports championships . the event was held in budaors , hungary on 13-17 november 2013. rajendran mani is a very popular bodybuilder in south india . he ranks no 4 in the top 10 indian bodybuilder list .
