Caro Emerald     caroline esmeralda van der leeuw ( born 26 april 1981 ) , best known by her stage name of caro emerald , is a dutch pop and jazz singer . she released her first song `` back it up '' on 6 july 2009. her single `` a night like this '' in 2009 reached the number 1 position in the netherlands . her second and latest album , `` the shocking miss emerald '' , reached the # 1 peak in the uk and the netherlands .
