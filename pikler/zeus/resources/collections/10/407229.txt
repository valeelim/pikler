Javier Porras Santana     javier porras santana ( born on august 22 , 1981 in olot , girona ) is a athlete from spain . he has a disability : he can not see and is a t11/b1 type athlete . he competed in the 2004 summer paralympics in athens , greece . he competed at the 2008 summer paralympics in beijing , china and was the third best athlete in the triple jump . he competed at the 2012 summer paralympics .
