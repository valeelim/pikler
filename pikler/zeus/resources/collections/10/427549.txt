Nils Petter Molvær     nils petter molvær ( born september 18 , 1960 ) is a norwegian jazz trumpeter . he plays a kind of jazz called future jazz . he has won spellemannprisen in the open class three times . in 1983 he joined jazzpunkensemblet , a norwegian jazz orchestra . he released his first solo album `` khmer '' in 1998 in the united states on ecm records . it has sold more copies than any of his later albums . molvær played trumpet on the 2008 hector zazou album `` corps electriques '' . molvær was a member of a post-rock band called jaga jazzist . other websites . - official website
