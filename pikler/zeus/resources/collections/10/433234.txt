Evan Chandler     evan chandler ( born evan robert charmatz ) ( january 25 , 1944 – november 5 , 2009 ) was an american screenwriter and dentist . he is best known for accusing michael jackson of sexually abusing his son jordan chandler in 1993. chandler was born in the bronx to jewish parents . he changed his name from charmatz to chandler because he thought charmatz was “ too jewish-sounding ” . chandler wrote the 1993 movie `` robin hood : men in tights '' . in her book `` shockaholic '' , carrie fisher said that chandler was her dentist . in 1993 , chandler accused michael jackson of molesting his son jordan . jordan had made the allegations after taking sodium amytal . the 1993 molestation case ended in 1994 after jackson reached a settlement with chandler for a reported u.s. $ 20 million . an article in `` usa today '' said that chandler tried to sue jackson again in 1996. in 1996 jordan took out a restraining order against evan . chandler shot himself in the head on november 5 2009 .
