47     47 is a year in the 1st century . it was a common year starting on sunday of the julian calendar . at the time , it was known as the year of the consulship of caesar and vitellius . events . - gnaeus domitius corbulo is made commander of the roman army in germania inferior . he conquers the chauci . - publius ostorius scapula becomes governor of britain , in place of aulus plautius . - musselburgh is founded in roman britain - a canal is built between the rhine and meuse in the netherlands deaths . - vardanes i of parthia
