Leónidas Plaza     leónidas plaza gutiérrez ( april 19 , 1865 – november 17 , 1932 ) was president of ecuador for two separate occasions : from september 1 , 1901 to august 31 , 1905 and from september 1 , 1912 to august 31 , 1916. he was the son of josé buenaventura plaza , a school teacher and , alegría gutiérrez de caviedes sevillano , an attractive tall woman with arostocratic features and exquisite social manners of colombian descent . he married maría avelina lasso ascázubi , with whom he had eight children . one of them , galo plaza , was also president of ecuador . other websites . - rodolfo pérez pimentel , `` diccionario biográfico del ecuador '' , guayaquil , 1987. sin isbn .
