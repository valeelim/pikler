Thief (video game)    thief ( video game ) thief is a stealth video game . it was developed by eidos montreal . it was published by square enix . it is a remake of the `` thief '' series . the game is the fourth game in its collection . it was released for the microsoft windows , playstation 3 , playstation 4 , xbox 360 and xbox one consoles on february 25 , 2014. the game was first announced in 2009 under the working title of `` thief 4 '' , but was revealed again in march 2013 as just `` thief '' .
