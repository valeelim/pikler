Evan Almighty     evan almighty is a 2007 american comedy movie and a spin-off sequel to `` bruce almighty '' . it stars steve carell , morgan freeman , molly shannon , john goodman , wanda sykes , and jonah hill . it is about congressman , evan baxter , trying to save many animals before a massive flood wipes-out an entire forest after he was sent by god . it was released on june 22 , 2007 to negative reviews .
