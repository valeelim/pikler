Giulietta Guicciardi     julie ( `` giulietta '' ) guicciardi ( 23 november 1782 – 22 march 1856 ) was an austrian countess . for a short time , she was a piano student of ludwig van beethoven . he dedicated his piano sonata no . 14 to her , which is now commonly known as the `` moonlight sonata '' . julie guicciardi , as she was called by her family , was born in przemyśl , galicia in 1782 .
