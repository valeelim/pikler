Shirley Manson     shirley ann manson ( born 26 august 1966 ) is a scottish singer , songwriter , musician and actress . she was born in edinburgh . she is best known for being the main singer of the rock band garbage . they have released five albums . between 1992 and 1994 manson was a member of a rock band called angelfish . in 1994 manson joined garbage . they released their first album , `` garbage '' , the next year . the first single released from the album was `` vow '' . the single `` stupid girl '' reached number four in the uk . it was their best selling single in the united states . in 1998 garbage 's second album `` version 2.0 '' was released . it was number one in the united kingdom , france , belgium , and new zealand . six singles were released from the album . `` beautifulgarbage '' was released in 2001. in 2005 `` bleed like me '' was released . `` not your kind of people '' , garbage 's fifth album , was released in 2013. in 2013 manson made a cover of `` because the night '' with screaming females . manson acted in eighteen episodes of `` '' .
