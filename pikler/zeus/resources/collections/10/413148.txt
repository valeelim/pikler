Hip hop soul     hip hop soul is a genre of music that fuses hip hop , r & b , soul , and new jack swing . some notable artist include r. kelly , mary j. blige , ashanti , keyshia cole , frank ocean , mariah carey , ray j and akon . hip hop soul first started as a fusion genre that fused hip hop with soul music , some of the best songs of hip hop soul include `` i 'll make love to you '' , `` lonely '' , `` swim good '' and `` ca n't be without you '' . it was very popular at first , but then it began to lose commercial success because of its late sexual lyrics and dark themes .
