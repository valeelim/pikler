The Monolith Monsters     the monolith monsters is a 1957 sci fi horror movie . it was directed by john sherwood and stars grant williams and lola albright . it was based on a short story by jack arnold and robert m. fresco . fresco and norman jolley wrote the screenplay . troy donahue and `` the donna reed show '' regular paul petersen have uncredited roles . a meteor crashes near the desert town of san angelo . fragments are scattered everywhere . they grow into skyscraper-sized monoliths . these monsters fall and shatter into thousands of pieces that grow into monoliths themselves . people are crushed or turned into statues . other websites . - movie review at rotten tomatoes - review of `` the monolith monsters ''
