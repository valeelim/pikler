Connie Booth     connie booth ( born january 31 , 1944 , indianapolis , indiana ) is an american actress , writer and psychotherapist . she has appeared on british and american television . she played polly sherman in the popular 1970s sitcom `` fawlty towers '' . booth ended her acting career 1995. she began working as a psychotherapist in the late 1990s . booth married john cleese in 1968. their daughter , actress cynthia cleese , was born in 1971. the couple divorced in 1978. booth has been married to critic john lahr since 2000 .
