Evelyn Kozak     evelyn kozak ( née jacobson ) ( august 14 , 1899 – june 11 , 2013 ) was an american supercentenarian who was the world 's oldest living jewish person , until her death at the age of 113 years , 301 days . she was also the oldest verified jewish person in history after surpassing fellow american adelheid kirschbaum 's age of 113 years and 83 days on november 6 , 2012. kozak was born on august 14 , 1899 in manhattan , new york city . she died from a heart attack on june 11 , 2013 in new york city , aged 113 .
