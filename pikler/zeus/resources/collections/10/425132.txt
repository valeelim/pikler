Titanium alloy     titanium alloys are metals which contain a mixture of titanium and other chemical elements . they have very high tensile strength and toughness . they are light in weight , resist corrosion very strongly and can withstand extreme temperatures . however , the cost of both raw materials and processing is very high . therefore , their use is limited to military applications , aircraft , spacecraft , medical devices , connecting rods on expensive sports cars and some high quality sports equipment and consumer electronics . other websites . - titanium and titanium alloys - general titanium alloys property data - properties of alpha/beta titanium alloy
