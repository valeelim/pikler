Division of Menzies     the division of menzies is an australian electoral division in the state of victoria . it is covers the suburbs of bulleen , croydon hills , doncaster , doncaster east , donvale , park orchards , templestowe , templestowe lower , warrandyte and warrandyte south . there are also parts of ringwood north , warranwood and wonga park in the division . the division was set up in 1984. it was named after rt hon sir robert menzies , the longest serving prime minister of australia . kevin andrews became the minister for social services in the abbott government . election results . - 2013 election results - 2010 election results - 2007 election results - 2004 election results other websites . - division of menzies - australian electoral commission
