Ron Reagan     ronald prescott `` ron '' reagan ( born may 20 , 1958 in los angeles , california ) is an american political commentator . he presents his commentary on the radio . he currently works for msnbc as a political analysist . reagan is a democrat and an atheist despite being the son of republican president ronald reagan . his mother is nancy reagan . he is often incorrectly called ronald reagan jr. or ronald reagan ii . in 2011 he wrote a book on his father called my father at 100 .
