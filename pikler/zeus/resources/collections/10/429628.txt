Abdelmalek Sellal     abdelmalek sellal ( عبد الملك سلال ) ( born 1 august 1948 ) is an algerian politician . he was the prime minister of algeria from 29 april 2014 to 25 may 2017. he previously served as the prime minister of algeria from 3 september 2012 to 13 march 2014. his predecessor was ahmed ouyahia .
