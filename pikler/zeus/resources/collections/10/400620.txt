Commonwealth War Graves Commission     commonwealth war graves commission ( cwgc ) is a british international organisation taking care of cemeteries and memorials of the british army across countries of the commonwealth of nations . the organisation was established in 1916 , as an idea of the british army to take care of and maintain the military cemeteries . other websites . - cwgc official website - maple leaf legacy project - south africa war graves project - united kingdom national inventory of war memorials - new zealand `` memorials register , '' ministry of culture & heritage
