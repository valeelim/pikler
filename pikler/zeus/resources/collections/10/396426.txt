1980 eruption of Mount St. Helens     on may 18 , 1980 , a major volcanic eruption occurred from mount st. helens , a volcano in washington , in the united states . the eruption was recorded as a 5 on the volcanic explosivity index . throughout the two months before the eruption , there was a series of earthquakes and steam was vented from the mouth of the volcano . a total of 57 people died in the eruption . including harry r. truman , who ran spirit lake .
