Nymphomaniac (movie)    nymphomaniac ( movie ) nymphomaniac is a 2013 two-part erotic drama movie directed by lars von trier . it is about a nymphomaniac who meets an asexual virgin and tells him about her sexual adventures . in the movie are charlotte gainsbourg , stacy martin , stellan skarsgard , shia labeouf , uma thurman and christian slater . the movie was released on december 25 , 2013 in denmark . it was released in early 2014 in some other countries . it was filmed in germany and belgium in 2012 . `` the guardian '' gave the movie four out of five stars . links . - official website
