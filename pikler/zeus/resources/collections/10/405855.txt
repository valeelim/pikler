Tania Cerda Meseguer     tania cerda meseguer ( born september 30 , 1977 in badalona ) is a swimmer from spain . she has a physical disability : she is an s9 type swimmer . she raced at the 1996 summer paralympics . she finished third in the 4 x 100 meter relay 34 points race . she raced at the 2000 summer paralympics .
