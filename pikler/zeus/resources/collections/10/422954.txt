WrestleMania X8     wrestlemania x8 was a professional wrestling pay-per-view show made by world wrestling federation ( wwf ) . it was held on march 17 , 2002 at the skydome in toronto , ontario . it was the eighteenth wrestlemania event held by the wwf and was the second wrestlemania to take place in toronto with the first being wrestlemania vi in 1990. this was the final wrestlemania under wwf name . other websites . - official wrestlemania x8 website
