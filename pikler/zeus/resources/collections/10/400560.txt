Gema Victoria Hassen-Bey González     gema victoria hassen-bey gonzález ( born may 5 , 1967 in madrid ) is a wheelchair fencing athlete from spain and journalist . she is a category b fencer . she has a disability : she uses a wheelchair . she became a paraplegic when she and her family were in a car accident . she is a journalist . she fenced at the 1996 summer paralympics . she finished third in the women 's sword . she fenced at the 2000 summer paralympics in sydney , australia . she fenced at the 2004 summer paralympics in athens , greece . she fenced at the 2008 summer paralympics in beijing , china . oc .
