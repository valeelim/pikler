Westerly, Rhode Island    westerly , rhode island westerly is a town in southwestern rhode island , near the state line of connecticut . the town was established in 1669. in 2010 , the population was about 23,000 by the census . westerly might be named because of where it is in rhode island . but , the town may have also gotten the name `` westerly '' from the english village westerleigh . during summer , westerly becomes a tourist attraction .
