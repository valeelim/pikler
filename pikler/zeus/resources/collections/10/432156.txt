Bianca Ryan     bianca taylor ryan ( born september 1 , 1994 ) is an american singer . she was the winner of nbc 's `` america 's got talent '' in its first season . her first album , `` bianca ryan '' , was released in autumn 2006. in 2012 , ryan joined the cast of the independent movie `` we be kings '' . she played a teenaged musician who is guided by an angel to an ancient blues musician frozen in a blizzard . ryan was born and raised in philadelphia .
