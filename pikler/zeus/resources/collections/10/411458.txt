Lohja     lohja ( ) is a city in uusimaa , finland . as of january 2014 almost 48,000 people live there . nearby municipalities are inkoo , karkkila , raasepori , siuntio , vihti , salo , somero and tammela . a large cave is located in lohja . the people in lohja speak two languages : finnish and swedish . other websites . - community website
