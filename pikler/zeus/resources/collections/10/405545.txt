Milo Đukanović    milo đukanović milo đukanović ( ; born 15 february 1962 ) is a montenegrin politician . he has been the country 's prime minister since 2012. đukanović has previously served as prime minister three times , from 1991 to 1998 , from 2003 to 2006 and from 2008 to 2010. he also served as president from 1998 to 2002. đukanović is the leader of the democratic party of socialists of montenegro .
