Shifty Adventures in Nookie Wood     shifty adventures in nookie wood is the fifteenth studio album by multi-instrumentalist john cale . it was released in october 2012. it was his first full-length album on double six records label . personnel . - john cale − vocals , keyboards , piano , organ , synthesizer , electric guitar , acoustic guitar , electric viola , bass guitar , percusions , programming - dustin boyer − guitars , synthesizer , background vocals - michael jerome moore − drums , percusions , cajón - joey maramba − bass guitar - danger mouse − bass guitar , synthesizer and programming on `` i wan na talk 2 u '' - erik sanko − bass guitar on `` scotland yard '' - eden cale − background vocals on `` hemingway ''
