Isaac Vorsah     isaac vorsah ( born 21 june 1988 ) is a ghanaian football player . he plays for fc red bull salzburg in the austrian bundesliga . he began his career at oscar fc in kpando , ghana and fc maamobi . he transferred to gamba all blacks f.c . in 2005. in january 2007 asante kotoko bought vorsah . the german team tsg 1899 hoffenheim offered him a trial . after the trial he was loaned until 30 june 2008. on 1 april 2008 tsg 1899 hoffenheim bought him and he signed until 30 june 2011. for hoffenheim he played 106 times and shot 4 goals . he moved to red bull salzburg in august 2012. after he came back from the africa cup 2013 he was injured badly and missed the 2013/14 season . vorsah played his first match for ghana in 2007 against senegal . for ghana he played the africa cup 2010 and 2013. he was also part of the team for the fifa world championship 2010. in total he played 16 matches for ghana .
