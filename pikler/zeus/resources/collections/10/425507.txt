Invincible     invincible is the tenth and last studio album by michel jackson . it was released in october 2001. it was the last studio album by jackson to be released while jackson was alive . it reached number one in eleven countries . three singles were released from the album . the first single was `` you rock my world '' . `` cry '' was released as the second single . it was written by r. kelly . the notorious b.i.g . rapped on the first song on the album , `` unbreakable '' . the album was unsuccessful compared to jackson 's previous albums . it sold thirteen million copies . `` rolling stone '' gave the album a rating of three out of five stars . `` you rock my world '' was nominated for best pop vocal performance - male .
