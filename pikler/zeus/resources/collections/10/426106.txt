Leonor Oyarzún     leonor oyarzún ivanovic ( born 10 march 1919 ) was the first lady of chile . she was the wife of former president patricio aylwin . oyarzún was born on 1919 in temuco . she married aylwin in 1948 and they have five children . they have 14 grandchildren ( among them , telenovela actress paz bascuñán ) . aylwin died in april 2016 .
