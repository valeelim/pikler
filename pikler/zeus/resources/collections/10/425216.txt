Trevor McDonald     sir trevor mcdonald obe ( born george mcdonald on 16 august 1939 ) is a british journalist . he was itn 's first black journalist . in 1992 , mcdonald became the presenter of the itv news at ten . in 1999 the programme ended . in 1999 `` tonight with trevor mcdonald '' started on itv . in 2007 mcdonald stopped presenting the programme . it is now simply called `` tonight '' . he was born in trinidad . in 1969 he started working for the bbc .
