Kinmen     kinmen ( or quemoy ) is a small group of islands close to the coast of china . it is governed by the republic of china ( taiwan ) . it forms a county , kinmen county ( ) , of fujian province . the capital is jincheng township . the county is claimed by the people 's republic of china ( prc ) as part of its own fujian province . it is very close to xiamen , no more than 2 kilometres .
