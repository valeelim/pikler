Gravity (movie)    gravity ( movie ) gravity is a 2013 american space drama science fiction thriller movie . it is a 3d movie . it was co-written , co-produced , co-edited and directed by alfonso cuarón . the movie stars sandra bullock and george clooney as surviving astronauts from a space shuttle destroyed by debris . it was released on october 4 , 2013. it received positive reviews . the movie has gotten many awards , and won seven awards in the 86th academy awards , with alfonso cuarón receiving best director .
