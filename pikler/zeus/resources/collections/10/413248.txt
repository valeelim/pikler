Public Enemies (2009 movie)    public enemies ( 2009 movie ) public enemies is a 2009 biographical-crime-drama movie set in chicago during the 1930s . it was directed by michael mann . it stars johnny depp as john dillinger . christian bale , marion cotillard , stephen lang , leelee sobieski , channing tatum , and billy crudup also star . it was released on july 1 , 2009. it was released to positive reviews . roger ebert , peter travers , rex reed , and michael phillips gave it positive reviews .
