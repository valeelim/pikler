Elmo Lincoln     elmo lincoln ( otto elmo linkenhelt , february 6 , 1889june 27 , 1952 ) was an american movie actor . he played the first movie tarzan in `` tarzan of the apes '' ( 1918 ) . he played tarzan in two other movies , `` the romance of tarzan '' ( 1918 ) and the movie serial `` the adventures of tarzan '' ( 1921 ) . he appeared , uncredited , in two tarzan movies in the 1940s . he was a circus roustabout in `` tarzan 's new york adventure '' ( 1942 ) , and a fisherman repairing his net in `` tarzan 's magic fountain '' ( 1949 ) . his last movie was `` carrie '' in 1952. he has a star on the hollywood walk of fame at 7042 hollywood boulevard .
