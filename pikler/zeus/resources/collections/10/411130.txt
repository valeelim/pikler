Evangelical Free Church in Sweden     the evangelical free church in sweden ( ) , `` efk '' , is a christian protestant denomination in sweden . it was established in 1997 as a merger out of örebro mission ( `` örebromissionen '' , founded in 1892 as örebro missionary society ) , the free baptist union ( `` fribaptistsamfundet '' , founded in 1872 ) and the sanctification union ( `` helgelseförbundet '' , founded in 1887 ) . other websites . - official website
