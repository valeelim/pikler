Vitruvius     vitruvius ( probably marcus vitruvius pollio ) was an ancient roman architect and engineer who lived in the 1st century bc . neither his name , `` marcus '' , nor his cognomen `` pollio '' are certain . his main work is called `` de architectura '' ( about architecture ) . there are ten books in it . he was probably born between 80 and 70 bc in campania , and died after 15 bc .
