Neon tetra     the neon tetra ( `` paracheirodon innesi '' ) is a freshwater fish . neon tetras are native to south america and mainly in the amazon . in captivity they grow to about 2.2 cm . neon tetras are sturdy , cheap and easy to look after . they are schooling fish and like to be kept with other neon tetras . they can also be kept in community tanks with other peaceful fish . they like to live in a water temperature of around 20–26°c . neon tetras are omnivores so will happily eat most flake foods . they should also be fed live food like brine shrimp or daphnia supplement their diets . references . - http : //www.wikihow.com/care-for-neon-tetra - http : //www.aquaticcommunity.com/tetrafish/neontetra.php - http : //www.keepingtropicalfish.co.uk/fish-database/neon-tetra/
