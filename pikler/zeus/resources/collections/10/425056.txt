Worthing, South Dakota    worthing , south dakota worthing is a city in the southeastern part of the u.s. state of south dakota . it is in lincoln county , about ten miles south of sioux falls . it became a city in 1879 , and 877 people lived there at the 2010 census . since 2010 , worthing has been the home of the lifelight music festival , the largest free music festival in the world . other websites . - city of worthing website
