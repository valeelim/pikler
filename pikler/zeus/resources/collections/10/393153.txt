Fu Jen Catholic University     fu jen catholic university ( fju ; ) is a well known university in taiwan . the main campus is in new taipei city . it was first started in 1925. its first name was catholic university of peking ( currently beijing normal university ) . it was started again in 1961. fu jen is the wade–giles romanization of the chinese characters 輔仁 ( furen in pinyin ) , literally meaning `` assistance '' and `` benevolence '' . related pages . - roman curia - society of jesus - benedictine other websites . - fu jen
