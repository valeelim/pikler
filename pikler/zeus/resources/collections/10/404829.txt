Carter Hutton     carter hutton ( born december 19 , 1985 , in thunder bay , ontario ) is a canadian ice hockey goaltender that is currently signed to the nashville predators of the national hockey league . before he played in the ahl and nhl , hutton played 4 seasons in the university of massachusetts lowell with the river hawks . he has also played in the ahl with the adirondack phantoms for 4 games and the worcester sharks for 22 games . on august 1 , 2011 , the rockford icehogs signed hutton as an unrestricted free agent . he was called up by the blackhawks to replace ray emery who was out with an illness and played his first game in the nhl on march 26 , 2012. on july 5 , 2013 , hutton signed a one-year , two-way contract worth $ 550,000 with the nashville predators .
