Arturo Licata     arturo licata ( 2 may 1902 – 24 april 2014 ) was an italian supercentenarian , who at the age of 102 , was italy 's oldest living man since the death of giuseppe mirabella on 30 march 2012 and europe 's oldest living man since the death of 111-year-old spanish man francisco fernández fernández on 7 september 2012. licata was also the world 's oldest living man since the death of salustiano sanchez on 13 september 2013. licata was born on 2 may 1902 in enna , sicily , italy . he was born into a family of four brothers and two sisters . licata lived in enna , sicily , italy . aside from poor hearing and eyesight , he remained in good health until may 2013 when his health began to rapidly decline . since september 2013 , he was bedridden . licata died at his home in enna , italy from natural causes , aged 111 .
