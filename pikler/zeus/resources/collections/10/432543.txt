Krip Hop     krip-hop is a movement that draws attention to hip-hop artists with disabilities . leroy moore 's krip hop nation has released two mix tape cds . the 'krip ' in krip hop is short for 'cripple ' . the word is used by some disabled people to describe themselves . moore chose to change the 'c ' in 'crip ' to a 'k ' because there is a gang called the crips . krip hop nation performed at the happening weekend at dadafest 2010. krip-hop musicians include miss money and kalyn heffernan . heffernan has brittle bone disease . she is the frontwoman of a group called wheelchair sports camp .
