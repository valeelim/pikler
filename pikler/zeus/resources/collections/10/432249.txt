(You Drive Me) Crazy    ( you drive me ) crazy `` ( you drive me ) crazy '' is a popular romance song by britney spears . it was released in late august 1999. the song charted at # 10 on the `` billboard '' hot 100 and # 9 in the rhythmic top 40. it performed better in united kingdom . the original version on the album `` ... baby one more time '' is different from the single version usually heard .
