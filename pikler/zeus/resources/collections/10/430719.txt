Mercedes-Benz Vario     the mercedes-benz vario is a series of full-size commercial heavy vans , trucks and step-entrance buses . the vario series was originally called the mercedes-benz t2 ( transporter 2 ) . they have been produced by mercedes-benz since 1995. this series was restyled in 1996 and given the current name vario . the vario is available with a 'om904 ' diesel engine . this is the same engine shared with the mercedes-benz atego . the vario has proved a popular chassis for minibuses and mini-coaches . a model with a is now available .
