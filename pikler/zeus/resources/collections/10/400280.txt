Raquel Diaz Caro     raquel diaz caro ( born may 13 , 1974 in barcelona ) is a track and field athlete from spain . she has a disability : she is blind and a t11/b1 type athlete . she raced at the 1996 summer paralympics . she finished third in the 100 meter t11 race and fourth in 200 meter t11 race . she raced at the 2000 summer paralympics .
