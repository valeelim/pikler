Christine Pascal     christine pascal ( november 29 , 1953 — august 30 , 1996 ) was a french actress and director . she played sylvie in `` round midnight '' ( 1986 ) . she also acted in other movies . most of those movies were french . pascal was also a writer . pascal was born , lyon , rhône . she died after jumping from the window of a psychiatric ward near paris . she was aged 42 .
