Södertälje SK     sdertälje sk is an ice hockey club in the town of södertälje in sweden . it was established in 1902 and adopted the game of ice hockey in 1925. the club won the swedish national championship in the years of 1925 , 1931 , 1941 , 1944 , 1953 , 1956 and 1995. the club also lost the national finals in the years of 1928 , 1929 , 1932 , 1937 , 1942 , 1946 , 1951 and 1986. other websites . - official website
