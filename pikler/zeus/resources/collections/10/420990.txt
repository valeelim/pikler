Philip Short     philip short ( born on 17 april 1945 ) is an english journalist . he studied at queens ' college , cambridge . after his graduation , he spent from 1967 to 1973 as a freelance journalist , first in malawi , then in uganda . he then joined the bbc ( british broadcasting corporation ) as a foreign correspondent . he is an author of many books , such as biographies of mao zedong , `` mao : a life '' , and of pol pot , `` pol pot : anatomy of a nightmare '' .
