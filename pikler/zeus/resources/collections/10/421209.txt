The Exorcist (movie)    the exorcist ( movie ) the exorcist is a 1973 american horror movie directed by william friedkin . it is based on the 1971 book by william peter blatty and is also based on the true events by 1949 exorcism case of ronald doe . it stars max von sydow , ellen burstyn , and linda blair . it was released to positive reviews on december 26 , 1973 .
