Darren Hayes     darren stanley hayes ( born 8 may 1972 ) is an australian singer-songwriter . he was born in brisbane . he was the singer of savage garden . savage garden released two albums before they broke up in 2001. in january 2002 hayes released his first solo single `` insatiable '' . his first solo album , `` spin '' , was released in april 2002. it has sold over 5 million copies . in 1993 hayes joined savage garden . in 1997 the band released `` savage garden '' . eight singles were released from the album . in 1999 savage garden 's second and last studio album , `` affirmation '' , was released . in 2002 hayes ' first solo album `` spin '' was released . in 2004 `` the tension and the spark '' was released . in 2007 hayes released his third album `` this delicate thing we 've made '' on his own record label , powdered sugar . in 2009 he released his fourth album `` we are smug '' . in 2011 `` secret codes and battleships '' was released . in 2006 hayes came out as gay . other websites . - tumblr
