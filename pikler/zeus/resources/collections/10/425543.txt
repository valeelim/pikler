Remember the Time     `` remember the time '' is a song by michael jackson . it was released as the second single from his album `` dangerous '' on january 14 , 1992. it was written by teddy riley , jackson , and bernard belle . when the single was released jackson dedicated the song with love to diana ross . the music video is nine minutes long . john singleton directed it . in 2006 the song was released again . it reached number 2 in the spanish chart .
