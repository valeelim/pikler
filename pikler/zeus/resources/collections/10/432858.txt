Ezra Miller     ezra matthew miller ( 30 , september 1992 ) is an american actor . he played patrick in the 2012 movie `` the perks of being a wallflower '' . for the role he won the boston society of film critics award for best supporting actor . he will play leon in the 2014 movie `` madame bovary '' . he has said that he is queer , not gay . he appeared in the 2016 movie `` '' as the flash . he will reprise his role in the upcoming `` suicide squad '' and the 2017 movie `` justice league '' . he portrayed credence barebone in the 2016 movie `` fantastic beasts and where to find them '' .
