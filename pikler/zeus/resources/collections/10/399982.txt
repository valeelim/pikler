B-movie     a b-movie is usually a low-budget movie crafted by second rate talents and starring lesser-known actors . in the past , these movies were the second feature on a double-bill . some actors ( such as bela lugosi ) appeared mostly in b-movies . today , many of these movies are highly regarded . `` detour '' starring tom neal and ann savage , for example , has come to be considered as one of the greatest of film noirs .
