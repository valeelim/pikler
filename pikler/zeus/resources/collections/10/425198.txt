Traffic calming     traffic calming measures are changes to a road to make car drivers slow down and to make the road safer for people who have to walk across the road . some traffic calming techniques include installing speed bumps and narrowing roads , which make drivers slow down . another way to make drivers slow down is to set speed limits . these speed limits can be enforced with automatic speed cameras . these cameras take photographs of people who are driving too fast . another way to slow drivers down is to make curbs wider .
