Jungle Man     jungle man is a 1941 american jungle adventure movie directed by harry l. fraser . it stars buster crabbe in his first movie for producers releasing corporation . he is reunited with charles b. middleton from the flash gordon movie serials . the story is about an african expedition that battles thick jungle , hostile natives , wild animals , and a deadly epidemic in its search for the fabled city of the dead . in 1951 , the movie was retitled `` drums of africa '' and sold to television .
