The Way You Make Me Feel     `` the way you make me feel '' is a song by michael jackson . it was the third single from jackson 's seventh album `` bad '' . it was produced by quincy jones . like the two singles before it , it reached number 1 in the `` billboard '' hot 100. it was also number 1 in spain . he performed the song at the 1988 grammy awards . after jackson died in 2009 the song entered the charts again . in the video jackson follows a girl called tatiana thumbtzen around and sings `` the way you make me feel '' . la toya jackson appears as one of the girl 's friends . cover versions . - in february 2010 , whitney houston performed `` the way you make me feel '' at her `` i look to you '' tour .
