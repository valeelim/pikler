Vito Corleone     vito andolini corleone ( born vito andolini in corleone , province of palermo , sicily , italy ) is a fictional character in mario puzo 's novel `` the godfather '' and in francis ford coppola 's first two movies , where he was portrayed by marlon brando in `` the godfather '' and , as a younger man , by robert de niro in `` the godfather part ii '' . other websites . - vito corleone on the godfather wiki
