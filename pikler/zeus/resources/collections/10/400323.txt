Alicia Martinez Gragera     alicia martinez gragera ( born september 24 , 1975 in badajoz ) is a track and field athlete from spain . she has a disability : she has cerebral palsy and a t37 type athlete . she raced at the 1996 summer paralympics . she finished third in the 200 meter t34-t37 race . she raced at the 2000 summer paralympics in sydney , australia .
