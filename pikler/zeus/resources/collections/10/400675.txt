Rhyacian     the rhyacian is the second geological period in the palaeoproterozoic that began 2.3 billion years ago , and ended at 2.05 billion years ago . before it was the siderian it is followed by the orosirian . the huronian ( makganyene ) global glaciation began at the start of the rhyacian . it lasted until 2200 million years ago . the first known eukaryotes began to evolve in the rhyacian period . the multicellular francevillian group fossils , at 2.1-gyr-old are from the rhyacian period .
