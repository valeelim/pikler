Vincenzo Sangermano     vincenzo sangermano ( 1758-1819 ) was a roman catholic priest from italy . he was a barnabite . he was a missionary in burma from 1783-1806. further reading . - sangermano , vincenzo and nicholas patrick wiseman . `` the burmese empire a hundred years ago '' . westminister : a. constable and company ( 1893 ) . isbn 9748299384 - sangermano , vincenzo . `` a description of the burmese empire '' . london : oriental translation fund of geat britain and ireland ( 1833 ) .
