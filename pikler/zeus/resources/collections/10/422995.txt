Sune (book series)    sune ( book series ) the sune series is a series of children's/youth books by swedish writers anders jacobsson and sören olsson . the books have been published since 1984. the stories are originally a radio series , produced as totally 90 episodes for swedish radio between the years of 1983 and 1993. the sune books are set in fictional town of `` glimmerdagg '' in sweden , which just like berts hometown öreskoga is located somewhere between karlskoga-örebro . there is söderskolan , for the grades 1–6 . the sune books are told in the third person perspective and using the present tense and uses imperfect during flashback scenes , while bert is told as a first person narrative . instead of being a typical `` tough guy '' who uses to fight or play soccer , sune is more interested into girls . books . - 1984 – `` sagan om sune '' ( `` the tale about sune '' ) - 1985 – `` sune börjar tvåan '' ( `` sune starts the second grade '' ) - 1986 – `` självklart , sune '' ( `` of course , sune '' ) - 1989 – `` sune och svarta mannen '' ( `` sune and the dark man '' )
