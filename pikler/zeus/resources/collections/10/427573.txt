Elf (movie)    elf ( movie ) elf is an american 2003 christmas comedy movie , directed by jon favreau . it stars will ferrell , james caan , bob newhart , zooey deschanel , peter dinklage , mary steenburgen , jon favreau , andy richter , ray harryhausen , leon redbone , and ed asner . will ferrell plays a human named buddy who is raised by elves in the north pole . he lives the way the elves do and helps in santa 's workshop . he travels to new york city to find his father . he is used to how elves live , which is very different from how humans live , with new york being a perfectly opposite way of living compared to the north pole . the movie was released to very positive reviews on november 7 , 2003 by warner bros. the film inspired the 2010 broadway musical ' and nbc 's 2014 stop-motion animated television special ' .
