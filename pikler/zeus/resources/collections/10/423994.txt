Ertharin Cousin     ertharin cousin ( born 1957 ) is , since 2012 , the twelfth executive director of the united nations world food programme . on june 9 , 2009 , cousin was nominated by president barack obama to become united states ambassador to the united nations agencies for food and agriculture . in november 2013 she was in `` time '' magazine 's list of `` the 13 gods of food '' . she was born in chicago . other websites . - unwfp page
