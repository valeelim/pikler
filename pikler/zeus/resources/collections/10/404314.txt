Here Comes Honey Boo Boo     here comes honey boo boo is a reality television program . it is about a 7-year-old beauty contestant named alana thompson in the u.s. state of georgia . it is a spin-off of the reality television program `` toddlers & tiaras '' . the show has gotten mixed reviews . it began in august 2012. the second season of it started july 17 , 2013 and ended on september 11 , 2013 .
