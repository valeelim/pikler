Knock Madness     knock madness is the third studio album by american rapper hopsin . it was released on november 24 , 2013 through funk volume . it has so far been supported by the singles `` old friend '' , `` hop is back '' , and `` rip your heart out '' featuring tech n9ne . the album debuted at # 132 on the billboard 200 and sold 4,000 copies in the united states . a week after it was released , the album rose to # 76 on the billboard 200 and sold 12,000 more copies .
