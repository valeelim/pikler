Mandela: The Authorised Biography    mandela : the authorised biography mandela : the authorized biography is a biography of nelson mandela , former president of south africa . it was written by journalist anthony sampson . it was published in 1999. sampson 's book was one of the first to talk about such issues such as winnie mandela 's crimes , and state president frederik willem de klerk 's suspected attempts to use the security forces to derail peace talks .
