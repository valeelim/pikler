Chloe     the name chloe is a female given name . it is especially popular in united kingdom . the name comes from the greek word `` khloē '' , one of many names of the greek goddess demeter . the name refers to young , green foliage . the name appears in new testament . in northern ireland , `` chloe '' was among the most popular names for babies between 1997 and 2002. people named `` chloe '' include chloë moretz , chloë sevigny and khloé kardashian . fictional chloes include chloe sweeney , played by amanda seyfried in the 2009 movie `` chloe '' .
