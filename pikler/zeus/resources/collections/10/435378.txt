Ellen Vogel     ellen marie elze anthing vogel ( january 26 , 1922 – august 5 , 2015 ) was a dutch television , movie , and stage actress . vogel was born on january 26 , 1922 in the hague , netherlands . she was raised in amsterdam . vogel married hans jürgen tobi in 1942. they divorced in 1949. she was in a relationship with fons rademakers from 1950 to 1955. vogel then married james münninghoff in 1976. their marriage lasted until his death in 2012. vogel had a son with hans , peter paul . vogel died in amsterdam at the age of 93 .
