New City, New York    new city , new york new city is a town in rockland county , new york . it is about north of new york city . the town is classified as a hamlet of clarkstown , meaning it is a small settlement inside of a larger town . the town is the county seat of rockland county , because it is the location of the rockland county courthouse and government . because of how close it is to new york city , many people go there to work .
