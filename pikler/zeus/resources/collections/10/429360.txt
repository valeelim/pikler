Choco pie     a choco pie is a small cake made up of two small round layers of cake with marshmallow filling and a chocolate covering . the origin of choco pie goes back to 1917 in the american south . chattanooga bakery in chattanooga , tennessee created the moon pie with graham crackers and marshmallow filling which is similar to the choco pie of nowadays . tongyang r & d , the snack company of south korea , began selling a similar type of chocolate cake with marshmallow filling named 'orion choco pie ' in 1974. a member of the company was inspired by a chocolate-coated cake in a restaurant in a hotel in the u.s. state of georgia on a business trip . after he got back to korea , he made many experiment to make that kind of chocolate cake . finally , he created the choco pie as it is known to koreans now . later tongyang r & d changed its name into orion confectionery because of the success of 'orion choco pie ' . in addition , similar versions of choco pie were produced by lotte confectionary and crown confectionery .
