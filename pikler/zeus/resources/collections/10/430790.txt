I Will Always Love You     `` i will always love you '' is a song by american singer-songwriter dolly parton . it is a country music song . it was released on june 6 , 1974. it was the second single from parton 's thirteenth solo album , `` jolene '' ( 1974 ) . whitney houston recorded a version of the song for the movie `` the bodyguard '' in 1992. that version is one of the best selling singles of all time .
