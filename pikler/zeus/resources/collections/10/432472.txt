Mustafa Ben Halim     mustafa ahmed ben halim ( ; born 29 january 1921 ) is an libyan politician . he served as the prime minister of libya from 12 april 1954 to 25 may 1957. ben halim is the last surviving of the kingdom of libya 's premiers . he is the only one of them who survived the removal of muammar gaddafi in 2011. other websites . - `` libya 's hidden pages of history : a memoir - صفحات مطوية من تاريخ ليبيا السياسي '' rimal publications , 2011 ( arabic edition ) isbn 9789963610761 - `` libya 's hidden pages of history : a memoir '' rimal publications , 2013 ( english edition )
