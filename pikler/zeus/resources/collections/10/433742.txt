Thomas Mesereau     thomas arthur mesereau , jr. ( born 1950 ) is an american lawyer . he is best known for defending michael jackson in his child molestation trial during 2005. he works with another lawyer called susan yu . mesereau lives in los angeles . he was born in new york . mesereau started a free legal clinic in the morningside united church of christ in los angeles . he wrote the foreword for aphrodite jones ' book `` michael jackson conspiracy '' ( 2007 ) . in 2005 the magazine `` gq '' put him on their list of `` men of the year '' .
