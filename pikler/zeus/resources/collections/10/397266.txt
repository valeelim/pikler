Rap rock     rap rock ( also called rockrap or rock hip hop ) is a genre of music that combines the vocals and compositions of hip hop with various forms of rock . rap rock has gained popularity with its sub-genres rap metal and rapcore . some of the most famous rap rock bands and musicans include linkin park , hollywood undead , p.o.d. , vanilla ice , kid rock , eminem , deuce , bizzy bone and roscoe dash . some artists have done more rapping rather than singing . it began during the 1980s and it is still a popular genre today .
