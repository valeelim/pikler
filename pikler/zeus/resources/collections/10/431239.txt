Chiwetel Ejiofor     chiwetelu umeadi `` chiwetel '' ejiofor obe ( born 10 july 1977 ) is a british actor . he played solomon northup in the 2013 movie `` 12 years a slave '' . he was nominated for the award for best actor in a motion picture , drama at the 2014 golden globe awards for his acting in the movie . his first movie role was in the 1996 movie `` voyage '' . in 2008 , ejiofor was made an officer of the order of the british empire . ejiofor played the band leader louis lester in the tv series `` dancing on the edge '' . it had six episodes . in 1996 ejiofor acted in his first film , `` deadly voyage '' . in 2002 he played the main character in a movie for the first time in `` dirty pretty things '' . he won the 2003 british independent film award for best actor for the film . in 2005 ejiofor played lola in `` kinky boots '' . ejiofor was born in london 's forest gate , to nigerian parents . he started acting in school plays when he was 13 .
