Constitution of Japan     the constitution of japan is the legal document that lays out the rules of how japan is governed . it dates 3 november 1946 on creation and from 3 may 1947 it entered into force , when it replaced the earlier meiji constitution , by legal process of earlier constitution . this constitution consists preamble , affirming that japanese people should contribute for all human being to live in peace and tolerance , free from fear and want and 11 chapters , and contrary to former constitution , it ensure all human rights as unalienable , both civil rights and economic , social and cultural rights ( at chapter iii ) , article 98 affirms that any laws or orders against the constitution are invalid and international law must be earnestly observed , article 99 says that empire and all public sector have duty to respect and observe the constitution and the article 9 of chapter ii prohibits all war and army for purpose of making war . jiminto wants to change it . other websites . - text in japanese and english
