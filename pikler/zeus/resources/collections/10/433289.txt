Árpád Göncz    árpád göncz árpád göncz ( `` göncz árpád '' ; february 10 , 1922 – october 6 , 2015 ) was a hungarian liberal politician . he was president of hungary from may 2 , 1990 to august 4 , 2000. he was the first democratically elected president of hungary . göncz played a role in the hungarian revolution of 1956. he was also founding member and vice chairman of alliance of free democrats ( szdsz ) and speaker of the national assembly before becoming president . he was a member of the international advisory council of the victims of communism memorial foundation . other websites . - his biography on the office of the president of the republic of hungary site
