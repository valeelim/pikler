Semi-vegetarianism     semi-vegetarianism means eating seafood or poultry , or both , but no red meat . people who eat red meat on rare occasion are `` flexitarians '' . real or semi-vegetarians , though , usually resent `` flexitarian '' , looking down on this term and calling it cheating . a `` pollotarian '' is a person who eats chicken . they do not eat red meat , seafood or fish . `` pollo-pescetarians '' eat seafood , shrimp and poultry but no red meat . people who have semi-vegetarian diets are usually doing so for health reasons , including avoiding heart disease , diabetes and strokes .
