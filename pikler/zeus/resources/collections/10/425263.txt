Joseph Paul Franklin     joseph paul franklin ( april 13 , 1950 – november 20 , 2013 ) was an american serial killer . he tried to kill larry flynt , a magazine publisher , in 1978 and vernon jordan , jr. , the civil rights activist , in 1980. he has been given six life sentences . he lived at potosi correctional center on death row . on november 20 , 2013 he was executed at 8:30am with propofol , a lethal drug , for killing a jewish man outside a synagogue . he was diagnosed with paranoid schizophrenia . franklin said that he thought he deserved to die for his crimes . larry flynt wrote in an essay published in the hollywood reporter , “ i do not want to see him die. ” franklin was executed on november 20 , 2013 by lethal injection .
