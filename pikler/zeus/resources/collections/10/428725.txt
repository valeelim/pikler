Chlöe Howl     chlöe howl ( born march 4 1995 ) is an english singer . on 4 march 2013 , her first extended play `` rumor '' was released for free download . in june 2013 , she released the video for her first single `` no strings '' . she released her second extended play `` no strings '' on august 23 . `` no strings '' was played in the movie `` kick-ass 2 '' . howl was nominated for bbc 's sound of 2014 prize on 3 december 2013. on 5 december 2013 , was nominated for the 2014 brits critics ' choice award . on 9 december 2013 , her second single `` paper heart '' was released .
