Slammiversary     slammiversary is a yearly pay-per-view made by total nonstop action wrestling ( tna ) . the event is usually produced in june . the first slammiversary event took place on june 19 , 2005 at the impact zone in orlando , florida . the name of the pay-per-view is a reference to the anniversary of the first tna event which took place on june 19 , 2002. other links . - tna website
