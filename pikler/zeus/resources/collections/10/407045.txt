Jean Marsh     jean marsh ( b. london , 1 july 1934 ) is an english actress and screenwriter who appeared mostly on television . marsh co-created and starred in the television series `` upstairs , downstairs '' and created `` the house of elliot '' . she also starred in `` doctor who '' , and appeared in many other series , and in films . she got an emmy award for the outstanding lead actress for the role of the parlourmaid rose buck in `` upstairs , downstairs '' .
