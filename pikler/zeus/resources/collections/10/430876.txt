Richard Drew     richard drew is an associated press photo-journalist . he is best known for his photograph `` the falling man '' , which shows a man falling from the world trade center towers during the september 11 attacks . a british documentary about his photograph , `` the falling man '' , aired on channel 4 on march 16 , 2006. drew was one of the press photographers at the assassination of robert f. kennedy . he has worked as an associated press photographer for over 40 years .
