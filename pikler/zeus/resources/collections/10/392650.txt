The Devil Put Dinosaurs Here     the devil put dinosaurs here is the fifth studio album by american rock band , alice in chains . `` the devil put dinosaurs here '' was released on may 28 , 2013. the album features the singles `` hollow '' , `` stone '' , and `` voices '' . guitarist jerry cantrell said about the album , `` i do n't think you 'll be surprised by anything you hear . ... it 's us . but it 's also really unique . it 's got all the elements of any record we 've put out , but it 's unlike any record we 've put out . basically , it 's the next chapter in the alice in chains book , and it 's going to be a big one .
