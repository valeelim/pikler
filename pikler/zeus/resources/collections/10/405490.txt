Sweden men's national ice hockey team    sweden men 's national ice hockey team the sweden men 's national ice hockey team ( ) represents the country of sweden in men 's ice hockey . the team started in 1920. it won the world championships in 1953 , 1957 , 1962 , 1987 , 1991 , 1992 , 1998 , 2006 , 2013 and 2017. they also became olympic champions in 1994 and 2006 .
