Tyrant flycatcher     the tyrant flycatchers ( tyrannidae ) are a clade of passerine birds from north and south america . they are the largest family of birds on earth , with more than 400 species . they are the most diverse family of birds in every country in the americas , except for the united states and canada . the family is named after the old world flycatchers . some members have similar features to the old world flycatchers , but they are not related to them . they are members of suborder tyranni , which do not have the vocal capabilities of most other songbirds . other websites . - tyrant flycatcher videos on the internet bird collection
