Tarzan's Secret Treasure    tarzan 's secret treasure tarzan 's secret treasure is a 1941 jungle adventure tarzan movie . it is based on the fictional characters and concepts created by edgar rice burroughs . the movie is the fifth of the six mgm tarzan movies starring johnny weissmuller and maureen o'sullivan . johnny sheffield plays boy . two crooks find out that there is gold in the jungle . they kidnap jane and boy in order to make tarzan show them the location of the treasure . everyone is captured by natives . tarzan comes to their rescue . related pages . - tarzan finds a son ! , prequel to this movie - tarzan 's new york adventure , sequel to this movie
