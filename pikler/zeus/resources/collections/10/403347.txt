José López Ramírez     josé lopez ramirez ( born december 17 , 1975 in málaga ) is a 5-per-team football player from spain . he has a disability : he is blind and is a b1 type sportperson . he played 5-per-team football at the 2004 summer paralympics . his team finished third after they played greece and , won 2-0. he played 5-per-team football at the 2012 summer paralympics . his team finished third after they played argentina and , won 1-0 .
