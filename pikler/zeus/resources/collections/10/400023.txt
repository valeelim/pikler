Hamad bin Khalifa Al Thani     sheikh hamad bin khalifa al thani ( , born 1 january 1952 ) is the former emir of the state of qatar . he was the emir from 1995 to 2013. sheikh hamad was the heir apparent of qatar between 1977 and 1995. he was lso the minister of defense at that time . in the early 1980s , he led the supreme planning council . it sets the economic and social policies for qatar . in 1992 , hamad worked more on the day-to-day running of the country . this included the development of qatar 's oil and natural gas resources . on 27 june 1995 , he took control of the country from his father in a palace coup . sheikh hamad was crowned on 20 june 2000. the emir is famous for helping to create the first arab international news network , al jazeera , when he gave a loan of qar 500 million ( $ 137 million ) to keep al jazeera running for its first five years . in late 2012 and early 2013 , it was said that the emir gave money and items to terrorist groups such as hamas , ansar dine , and the al-nusra front .
