The Frozen Ground     the frozen ground is a 2013 american crime movie based on a true story . it tells the story of a woman who helped to catch serial killer robert hansen in 1983. hansen murdered 17 young women , and kidnapped and raped at least 30 others , in alaska during the 1970s and early 1980s . the movie stars vanessa hudgens as cindy paulson , nicolas cage as sergeant jack halcombe , and john cusack as hansen . the movie was released august 23 , 2013 in north america . the reviews of the movie were mixed .
