Marly Marley     marly marley ( 5 april 1938 – 10 january 2014 ) was a brazilian actress . she was known for her roles in `` chega de saudade '' ( 2007 ) , `` o puritano da rua augusta '' ( 1965 ) and `` casinha pequenina '' ( 1963 ) . she also appeared on many brazilian sitcoms and telenovelas . marley died from pancreatic cancer on 10 january 2014. she was aged 75. she died in são paulo . her widower is the actor and comedian ary toledo .
