Black cockatoo     black cockatoo is general term for cockatoos that are mainly black . it may include : - palm cockatoo , `` probosciger aterrimus '' , also called the great black cockatoo - species of the genus `` calyptorhynchus '' : - red-tailed black cockatoo , `` calyptorhynchus banksii '' , has several alternative common names including black cockatoo . the red black cockatoos live in the drier parts of australia . they are widespread across the northern half of australia . they leave all areas with high humidity in the summer wet season . when cockatoos start moving they tend to follow food sources . - glossy black cockatoo , `` calyptorhynchus lathami '' - yellow-tailed black cockatoo , `` calyptorhynchus funereus '' - short-billed black cockatoo , `` calyptorhynchus latirostris '' - long-billed black cockatoo , `` calyptorhynchus baudinii ''
