The Boy in the Striped Pyjamas (movie)    the boy in the striped pyjamas ( movie ) the boy in the striped pyjamas is a 2008 british-american historical drama movie . it is based on the book of the same name by john boyne . the movie is directed by mark herman and produced by david heyman . it stars asa butterfield , jack scanlon , david thewlis , vera farmiga , amber beattie , richard johnson and rupert friend . the movie is about a boy whose father is a soldier during the world war ii . they decided to move to a new house so that the father can work near the concentration camps . the son likes to explore and he comes to meet a jewish boy who lives in the concetration camp . one day , he decides to go into the camp and accidentally enters a gas chamber and dies along with all prisoners .
