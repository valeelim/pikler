Gangawati     gangawati ( or gangavati , ) is a town , municipality and taluk in the indian state of karnataka . it is part of the koppal district . in the 2001 census of india , gangawati had a population of 93,249. near gangawati is hampi , a unesco world heritage site . it is south-west of the town . villages in gangawati include : - varankhed - vipra - virupapuragaddi - yemmigudda
