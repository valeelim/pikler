Gambarogno     gambarogno is a municipality in the district of locarno in the canton of ticino in switzerland . on 25 april 2010 the former municipalities of caviano , contone , gerra ( gambarogno ) , indemini , magadino , piazzogna , san nazzaro , sant'abbondio and vira ( gambarogno ) merged into the new municipality of gambarogno .
