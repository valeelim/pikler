Luigi Mastrangelo     luigi mastrangelo ( born august 17 , 1975 in mottola , italy ) is an italian men 's volleyball player , a member of italy national volleyball team , silver and bronze medalist of the olympic games , multiple winner of the european championship and world league . he competed in the 2000 , 2004 , 2008 and 2012 summer olympics . he won a bronze medal in the 2000 and 2012 games . he won a silver medal in the 2004 games . references . - nbc olympics profile - sports reference profile
