Yabby     yabby or yabbie is a name given in australia to two different kinds of crustacean . 1. a freshwater yabby is a crayfish of the genus `` cherax '' . they are often caught for food . they are found in dams , ponds , lakes and rivers in australia 's eastern states . 1. a marine yabby is a ghost shrimp , which lives in deep burrows in the intertidal zone . they are used as bait for fishing , especially in queensland and northern new south wales . a common species in south-eastern australia is the bass yabby , `` trypaea australiensis '' . hello ! ! !
