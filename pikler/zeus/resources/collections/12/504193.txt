John Mahaffy     john mahaffy ( july 18 , 1918 – may 2 , 2015 ) was a canadian professional ice hockey centre . he played 37 games in the national hockey league for the new york rangers and montreal canadiens . mahaffy was born in montreal , quebec . he was married to angie péloquin . he died on may 2 , 2015 , aged 96 .
