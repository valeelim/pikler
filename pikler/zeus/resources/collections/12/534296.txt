Arjun Bijlani     arjun bijlani ( born 31 october 1982 ) is an indian television actor . he played aalekh in `` right left '' and mayank sharma in `` miley jab hum tum '' . his other work includes `` jo biwi se kare pyaar '' . in april 2015 , bijlani played shikhar mehra in `` meri aashiqui tumse hi '' . he plays the lead role of hritikh in the television drama `` naagin ( 2015 tv series ) '' . bijlani was born on 31 october 1982. he has appeared in many advertisements , including lg , samsung and bpl mobile . he has been in many music videos . he was in `` kartika '' , a disney channel show , with jennifer winget . he has been in many serials , including `` miley jab hum tum , pardes mein mila koi apna '' . he also hosted a show named `` road diaries '' . other websites . - arjun bijlani official website
