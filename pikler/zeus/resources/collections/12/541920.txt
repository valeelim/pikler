Lasse Mårtenson     lars anders fredrik `` lasse '' mårtenson ( 24 september 1934 – 14 may 2016 ) was a finnish singer , composer , actor and theater conductor . he was born in helsinki . he performed at the eurovision song contest in 1964 with the song `` laiskotellen '' ( `` idling '' ) . mårtenson died of a cerebral haemorrhage , aged 81 .
