Zenit Kazan     vc zenit-kazan ( russian : зенит-казань ) ( formerly `` dinamo tattransgaz kazan '' ) is a professional volleyball team based in kazan , russia . it plays in the russian volleyball super league ( 7-time champion ) . the team won the 2007–08 , 2011–12 and 2014–15 cev champions league titles . the club was founded on 13 may 2000. other websites . - web site
