Bryan Robinson     bryan keith robinson , nicknamed `` big dog '' , ( june 22 , 1974june 11 , 2016 ) was an american football defensive tackle . he was originally signed by the st. louis rams as an undrafted free agent in 1997. robinson also played for the chicago bears , the miami dolphins , the cincinnati bengals , and the arizona cardinals . on june 11 , 2016 , robinson was found dead in a milwaukee motel room , aged 41 .
