Samara (fruit)    samara ( fruit ) a samara is an achene , a type of dry winged botanical fruit . the shape of a samara enables the wind to carry the seed farther away than regular seeds from the parent tree : - the seed can be in the centre of the wing , like the elms and the hoptree . - the seed can be on one side , with the wing extending to the other side , making the seed self rotate it falls . further reading . - ralph lorenz 2006 . `` spinning flight : dynamics of frisbees , boomerangs , samaras and skipping stones '' , , copernicus new york . isbn 0-387-30779-6
