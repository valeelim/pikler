Get Your Heart On!    get your heart on ! get your heart on ! is the fourth studio album by canadian pop punk band , simple plan . `` get your heart on ! '' was released on june 21 , 2011 through both atlantic and lava . it was also released on 17 june 2011 in countries like australia and the netherlands . the album peaked at # 52 on the `` billboard '' 200 and at # 2 on the canadian albums chart . the album featured the singles `` ca n't keep my hands off you '' , `` jet lag '' , `` astronaut '' , and `` summer paradise '' .
