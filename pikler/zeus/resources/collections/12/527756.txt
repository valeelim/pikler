École de l'Air    école de l'air the école de l'air is a military school and grande école training line officers in the french air force . it is located at salon-de-provence air base in salon-de-provence , france . its different curricula lead to the following french & european degrees : - `` ingénieur école de l'air '' ( `` école de l'air '' graduate engineer masters level program ) ; - `` mastères spécialisés '' ( in partnership with the french civil aviation university and the `` institut supérieur de l'aéronautique et de l'espace '' . - massive online open course in air defense . it trains fighter pilots , military engineers and technicians . famous former students . - caroline aigle , french fighter pilot . - michel tognini , french test pilot , astronaut , educator , writer and administrator . other websites . - official website
