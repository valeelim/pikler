The Anthem     `` the anthem '' is the second single off american pop punk band good charlotte 's second studio album , `` the young and the hopeless '' . the song was released in january 2003. on the `` billboard '' charts , the song was able to peak at # 43 on the hot 100 and # 10 on the hot modern rock tracks . in 2004 , the song won `` best rock video '' at the mtv video music awards japan . the song has appeared on the soundtracks to the video games `` madden nfl 2003 '' , `` project gotham racing '' , `` elite beat agents '' , and the north american release of `` donkey konga 2 '' . other websites . - `` the anthem '' on youtube
