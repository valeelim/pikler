Simone Veil     simone veil , dbe ( ; 13 july 1927 – 30 june 2017 ) was a french lawyer and politician . she served as minister of health under valéry giscard d'estaing , president of the european parliament and member of the constitutional council of france . she was awarded the grand cross of the légion d'honneur in 2012. she of jewish descent and survived the holocaust . veil died in paris on 30 june 2017 , aged 89 .
