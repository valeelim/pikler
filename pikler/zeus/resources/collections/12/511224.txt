Becky G     rebbeca marie gomez ( born march 2 , 1997 ) , better known by her stage name becky g , is a mexican-american singer , songwriter and rapper . she became popular in 2011 , after she posted several videos of herself performing remixes of popular songs on to youtube . in 2014 , gomez had her first billboard hot 100 entry , with `` shower '' , peaking at 16 .
