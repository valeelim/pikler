Andrzej Niemczyk     andrzej niemczyk ( 16 january 1944 – 2 june 2016 ) was a polish volleyball player and coach . he was the coach of the poland women 's national volleyball team that competed at the 2005 women 's european volleyball championship . he was born in łódź . niemczyk died in warsaw , poland from cancer on 2 june 2016 , aged 72 .
