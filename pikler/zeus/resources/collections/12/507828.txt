Rhynia     rhynia is a genus of early devonian fossil land plants . only one species is known , `` r. gwynne-vaughanii '' . the fossils are the sporophyte generation of a vascular plant . `` rhynia '' had anatomical features more advanced than those of the bryophytes . related pages . - `` horneophyton '' , a fossil non-vascular plant similar to `` rhynia ''
