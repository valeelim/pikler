Traffic (movie)    traffic ( movie ) traffic is a 2000 american crime drama movie . it contains three stories of the illegal drug trade , one in mexico , one in san diego , one in ohio . catherine zeta-jones plays helena . erika christensen plays caroline . dennis quaid plays arnie . `` traffic '' got very positive reviews from movie critics . the movie got back over $ 207 million at the box office . it also earned four academy awards , including best director and best film editing .
