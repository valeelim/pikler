Survey of India     survey of india ( ) is the national indian institute of geodesy and cartography . it was established in 1767. it is in new delhi . the survey of india is also responsible to others organizations such as : - survey of india service - archaeological survey of india ( asi ) - botanical survey of india ( bsi ) - cartography of india - geological survey of india ( gsi ) - maintains 2 fossil parks currently - linguistic survey of india - zoological survey of india ( zsi ) other websites . - official website of the survey of india - directory of indian government websites
