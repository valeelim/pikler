Michal Hornstein     michal hornstein ( september 17 , 1920 – april 25 , 2016 ) was a polish-born canadian businessman , art collector and philanthropist . he was born in kraków , poland . he moved to canada after surviving the holocaust . he became a citizen in 1957. he was known for donating money to art museums and schools in quebec . in 1984 he was made a member of the order of canada . in 1993 , he was made a knight of the national order of quebec . he was made officer in 2002 and grand officer in 2013. hornstein died at his home in montreal , quebec on april 25 , 2016 , aged 95 .
