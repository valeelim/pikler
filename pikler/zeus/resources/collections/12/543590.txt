Galvarino     galvarino was a famous mapuche warrior . in 1557 , he fought in the battle of lagunillas against the an army of spanish conquistadors . the battle took place in chile near the bio bio river . he was taken prisoner along with one hundred and fifty other mapuche . some prisoners were condemned to amputation of their right hand and nose . others like galvarino had both hands cut off . he was released and told to tell the mapuche chief caupolic√°n to surrender to stop more bloodshed . he did just the opposite . he urged the mapuche to continue fighting against the spanish invaders . less than a month later , on november 30 , 1557 , with knives fastened to both of his wrists he fought at the battle of millarupe . it was supposed to be a surprise attack but the mapuche sprang the trap too early . in the battle , over 3,000 mapuche warriors were killed . galvarino was one of several hundred who were captured . he was sentenced to be hung . some say he was executed by being thrown to the dogs . others claim he was hanged . still others say he killed himself before he could be executed .
