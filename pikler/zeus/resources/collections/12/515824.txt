Ahmed Mohamed clock incident     ahmed mohamed is a 14-year-old student who was arrested in irving , texas in september 2015. the arrest happened after a teacher , and later the police mistook his home-made clock for a hoax bomb . his arrest caused a huge controversy . us president barack obama invited him to the white house . president obama and others complimented mohamed for his inventive nature . facebook co-founder mark zuckerberg and hillary rodham clinton also gave him support . his father , mohamed elhassan mohamed , ran in the election for president of sudan in 2010 and 2015. the elder mohamed is the director of the islamic sufi center in texas .
