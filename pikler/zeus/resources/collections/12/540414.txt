Basseri     the basseri are a persian nomadic and pastoral tribe of the fars province in iran.their migratory area is around shiraz.the basseri are a tribe of khamseh confederation . basseries are persian.they are the survivors of pasargadean tribe , the biggest tribe of persia in achaemenid empire period.they speak persian with a different accent.they are shia muslims . sources . - ehsan yousefi , nomads of persia : the basseri tribe ; 2014 ; shiraz
