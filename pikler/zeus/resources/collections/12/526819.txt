Dal Richards     dallas murray `` dal '' richards , cm , obc ( 5 january 1918 - 31 december 2015 ) was a canadian big band leader . richards led his band for many years in a weekly cbc radio show broadcast nationally from the panorama roof ballroom of the hotel vancouver . , richards also hosts a weekly one-hour show on radio station cisl . richards is commonly thought to be the lyricist of `` roar you lions roar '' , the fight song of the bc lions football club set to the music of `` i love the sunshine of your smile '' .
