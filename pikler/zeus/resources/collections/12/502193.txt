Extreme Rules (2015)    extreme rules ( 2015 ) extreme rules ( 2015 ) was a professional wrestling pay-per-view show made by wwe . it was held on april 26 , 2015 at allstate arena in rosemont , illinois . it was the seventh extreme rules event held by the wwe . the main event saw seth rollins defeat randy orton in a steel cage match to retain the wwe world heavyweight championship . in the match , orton 's rko finisher was banned and kane served as the gatekeeper for the cage . other websites . - official extreme rules 2015 website
