William Zinsser     william knowlton zinsser ( october 7 , 1922 - may 12 , 2015 ) was an american writer , editor , literary critic , and teacher . he began his career as a journalist for the `` new york herald tribune '' , where he worked as a feature writer , drama editor , film critic , and editorial writer . he was known for writing the book `` on writing well '' . zinsser was born in new york city , new york . he studied at deerfield academy . zinsser died in new york city at the age of 92 .
