The Truth Seekers     the truth seekers ( chinese : 真探 ) is a singaporean drama television series . it is produced and shown on mediacorp channel 8. it began its run from 28 april 2016. there are 23 episodes . it stars chen hanwei , rebecca lim , desmond tan and yusuke fukuchi as the main characters . they play a team of 'cold case ' private investigators , who look into unsolved cases from years ago and break new ground with the help of scientific and technological advancements . during their investigations , members of the crime-busting team fall in – and out – of love as they learn other hidden truths about themselves and their comrades . cast . - chen hanwei as bai qingxiong - rebecca lim as huang yuyang - desmond tan as hong junyan - yusuke fukuchi as lan haifeng - lynn poh as yamamoto atsuki trivia . - lynn poh 's 2016 drama after `` your hand in mine '' .
