Cyclone Winston     severe tropical cyclone winston was a tropical cyclone in february 2016. it was the first recorded category 5 storm to hit fiji . it was also one of the strongest tropical cyclones in the southern hemisphere . before the storm got to fiji , many shelters were opened . a curfew was called across the country on 20 february . the cyclone struck fiji as a category 5 tropical cyclone . winston caused a lot of damage in the islands . it killed twenty-eight people .
