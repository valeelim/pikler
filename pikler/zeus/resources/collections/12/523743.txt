Romantic orientation     romantic orientation , also called affectional orientation , indicates the sex or gender that a person is most likely to have a romantic relationship with . it is compared to the term `` sexual orientation '' . romantic orientation is based on the idea that sexual attraction is one component of a larger picture . for example , although a pansexual person may feel sexually attracted to multiple genders , they may be predisposed to romantic intimacy with females . for asexual people , romantic orientation is often considered a more useful measure of attraction than sexual orientation .
