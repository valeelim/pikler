Alexandre Astruc     alexandre astruc ( 13 july 1923 – 19 may 2016 ) was a french movie critic and movie director . he was known for being part of the french new wave . he was born in paris . astruc was known for his movies `` the crimson curtain '' , `` la putain respectueuse '' , `` les mauvaises rencontres '' , `` amour de poche '' , `` une vie '' , and `` la proie pour l'ombre '' . in 1994 , he was given the rené clair award . astruc died in paris on 19 may 2016 , aged 92. other websites . - alexandre astruc on newwavefilm.com - foco - revista de cinema , special edition devoted to alexandre astruc
