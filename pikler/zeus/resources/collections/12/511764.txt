Mi querida España     `` mi querida españa '' ( `` my dearest spain '' or `` my lovely spain '' ) is a song . it was written and sung by the spanish songwriter cecilia in 1975. it was included on her album `` un ramito de violetas '' `` ( a bouquet of violets ) .
