Tommy Shane Steiner     tommy shane steiner ( born october 9 , 1973 in austin , texas ) is an american country music artist . he made his in 2001 with the single `` what if she 's an angel '' . the song reached no . 2 on the `` billboard '' hot country singles & tracks charts . it was the first of three chart singles from his singular album `` then came the night '' which was released in 2002. the album peaked at no . 6 on the country albums chart . discography . - studio albums - `` then came the night '' ( 2002 )
