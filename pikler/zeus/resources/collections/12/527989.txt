Prohibition Party     the prohibition party ( pro ) is a political party in the united states . the party is known for its historic opposition to the sale or consumption of alcoholic beverages . it is the oldest existing third party in the united states . the party was an important part of the temperance movement . while never one of the leading parties in the united states , it was once an important force in the third party system during the late 19th and early 20th centuries . it declined dramatically after the repeal of prohibition in 1933. the party earned only 519 votes in the 2012 presidential election with jack fellure as the candidate . james hedges is the candidate in the upcoming 2016 presidential election . other websites . - ( party ) - ( people )
