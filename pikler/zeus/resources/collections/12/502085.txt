Ronald Senator     ronald senator ( 17 april 1926 – 30 april 2015 ) was a british composer . he was best known for `` holocaust requiem '' , for which he was nominated for a pulitzer prize . he had concerts in new york ( 1990 , 1998 , 2001 ) , london ( 1989 , 1997 ) , sydney ( 1978 ) , moscow ( 1993 ) , and st. petersburg ( 1991 ) . senator studied at oxford university ( 1944–1947 ) with austrian-born british composer egon wellesz . he was jewish and married to miriam brickman . he lived in london and new york , united states . senator and his 81-year-old wife died in a fire on 30 april 2015 at their home in yonkers , new york . he was 89 .
