Pam Ferris     pamela e. `` pam '' ferris ( born 11 may 1948 ) is a british actress . she was born in hanover to welsh parents . she starred on television as ma larkin in `` the darling buds of may '' , and as laura thyme in `` rosemary & thyme '' . on movies , she is known for her roles as miss trunchbull in `` matilda '' and as aunt marge in `` harry potter and the prisoner of azkaban '' .
