Peter Lustig     peter fritz willi lustig ( october 27 , 1937 – february 23 , 2016 ) was a german television presenter and author of children 's books . he was known as leading actor in the weekly children 's television show `` löwenzahn '' . he hosted the show from 1979 up until 2006 ; during its first year the show was still called `` pusteblume '' ) . he also appeared on the shows `` mittendrin '' ( 1987–1995 ) and `` gordos reise ans ende der welt '' ( 2007 ) . lustig died on february 23 , 2016 at his home in bohmstedt , germany . he died from stomach cancer . he was aged 78. he was surrounded by his family . he is survived by four children and nine grandchildren .
