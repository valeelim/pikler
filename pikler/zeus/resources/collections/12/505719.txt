Frequent-flyer program     a frequent-flyer program is offered by an airline to reward its customers for loyalty . people who are part of the program usually earn miles ( frequent-flyer points ) . these miles can be redeemed ( exchanged ) for free flights or other products and services . many airline alliances allow customers to earn and redeem miles with a number of airlines . this may also work with a codeshare agreement . the first airline to launch a frequent-flyer program was american airlines .
