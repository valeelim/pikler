Chevrolet Tahoe     the chevrolet tahoe ( and its sibling gmc yukon ) is a full sized suv sold by general motors since 1995. it replaced the chevrolet k5 blazer and gmc s15 jimmy . it is available in three trims ls , lt , and ltz . the tahoe comes in either rear-wheel drive or four-wheel drive . it also offers flexfuel ( can burn gasoline or mixes of gasoline and ethanol up to e85 ) . the tahoe is available with a choice of automatic or manual transmission and has a v8 engine . the tahoe is built on the same chassis used by full-sized chevrolet and gmc pickup trucks . it is also offered to police fleets as the `` chevrolet tahoe police package '' . < br >
