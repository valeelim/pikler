Jim Gilmore presidential campaign, 2016    jim gilmore presidential campaign , 2016 the 2016 presidential campaign of jim gilmore , the 68th governor of virginia , was filed with the fec on july 29 , 2015. he made an official launch of his campaign on july 30 , 2015. it was headquartered in henrico , virginia . he withdrew on february 12 , 2016 due to poor polling numbers .
