Chris O'Dowd     christopher `` chris '' o'dowd ( born 9 october 1979 ) is an irish actor and comedian . he was best known for his role as roy trenneman in the channel 4 comedy `` the it crowd '' . o'dowd created and starred in the sky 1 television series `` moone boy '' , which aired between 2012 and 2015. he had a recurring role on the drama series `` girls '' and starred in the television series `` family tree '' . o'dowd is also known for his movies , most notably `` bridesmaids '' ( 2011 ) , `` this is 40 '' ( 2012 ) , `` the sapphires '' ( 2012 ) , `` '' ( 2013 ) , `` calvary '' ( 2014 ) and `` st. vincent '' ( 2014 ) .
