Jade (American band)    jade ( american band ) jade is an american hip hop-soul group . the group began in 1991. they split in 1997 and reformed in 2014. the group 's members are joi marshall , tonya kelly and myracle holloway . the group is from chicago . the group had hit songs with `` i wan na love you down '' and `` do n't walk away '' in 1992 and 1993. their second album , `` mind , body & song '' , was released in 1994 .
