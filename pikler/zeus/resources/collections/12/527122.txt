Out of the Woods (song)    out of the woods ( song ) `` out of the woods '' is a song co-written and performed by american singer-songwriter taylor swift . it is the sixth single from her album , `` 1989 '' . it was written by swift and jack antonoff from the american alternative band fun . the two produced the song along with swedish songwriter and producer max martin . the song is the fourth track on the album . it became her sixth top-20 hit in a row on the us `` billboard '' hot 100. the music video for the song was released on december 31 , 2015 , live on `` dick clark 's new year 's rockin ' eve with ryan seacrest '' on abc stations in the united states .
