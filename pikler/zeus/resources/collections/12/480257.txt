Rena Owen     rena owen ( born 22 july 1962 in bay of islands , northland in new zealand ) is a new zealand actress . she works in movies , television and theatre . she played beth heke in `` once were warriors '' . she also played anna in `` ocean of pearls '' ( 2007 ) . the awards owen has won include : - montreal world film festival winner ( 1994 ) : best actress- `` once were warriors '' - san diego international film festival ( 1994 ) : best actress- `` once were warriors ''
