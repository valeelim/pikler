Toulouse Business School     toulouse business school is a european higher studies establishment . the school is in 5 places , in paris and toulouse , france , in the united kingdom ( london ) , in spain ( barcelona ) and in morocco ( casablanca ) , where it is a university . it is one of the oldest business school in the world established in 1903 and therefore presents itself as `` think and create '' . tbs is well-known for its aviation management degrees in partnership with the french civil aviation university . other websites . - toulouse business school
