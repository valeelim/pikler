I Will Never Let You Down     `` i will never let you down '' is a song by british recording artist rita ora . it was first planned as lead single for her upcoming second studio album . but it was later released as a buzz single . it was written and produced by calvin harris . the single was released on 31 march 2014. the song debuted at number one on the uk singles chart making it ora 's fourth overall uk number-one single . the music video features twin dancers polly duniam and sophie duniam .
