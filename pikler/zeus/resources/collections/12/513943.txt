Giya Kancheli     giya kancheli ( born august 10 , 1935 ) is an georgian composer . after the fall of the soviet union he became famous as one of the composers who resisted pressure to conform to official soviet styles of music . kancheli has composed many soundtracks for movies such as `` mimino '' , `` kin-dza-dza ! `` , `` passport '' , `` blue mountains '' , `` the eccentrics '' , and `` extraordinary exhibition '' .
