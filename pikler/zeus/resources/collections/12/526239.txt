Carpetbagger     carpetbagger is a term from the united states political history . a carpetbagger was a northerner who moved to the south after the american civil war . this period is called the `` reconstruction era '' . white southerners feared they would loot the defeated south . sixty carpetbaggers were elected to congress . among them were most republican governors in the south during reconstruction . a historian said : `` carpetbagger '' referred to the cheap carpet bags ( a form of luggage ) which many of the newcomers carried . the term came to be associated with opportunism and exploitation by outsiders . it is used today for a `` parachute candidate '' : an outsider who runs for public office in an area where they have not lived , and are not personally known .
