Kings Dominion     kings dominion is a theme park in doswell , virginia . it is north of richmond and south of washington , d.c. , off interstate 95. the park opened on may 3 , 1975. it is owned by cedar fair . kings dominion offers over 60 rides , shows and attractions including 21 roller coasters and water park . the name of the park comes from the name of its sister park , kings island , and the nickname for the state of virginia , `` old dominion '' .
