Bolt     a bolt is a form of grooved fastener with an external male thread . bolts are thus closely related to , and often confused with , screws . a screw is different from a bolt , because it does not take a nut , and its helical thread is used to penetrate ( cut into ) a softer material ( such as wood ) . this distinction is not always clear , and sometimes screws are regarded as bolts without a nut . related pages . - wrench - screw
