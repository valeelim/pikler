Greg Garcia     gregory thomas garcia ( born april 4 , 1970 ) is a american television producer and screenwriter . he was born in arlington county , virginia . he was the creator and producer of the comedy series , yes , dear . he was the creator and executive producer of `` my name is earl '' . he also worked for the series `` family matters '' . he was the consultant in `` family guy '' . he produced the fox series `` raising hope '' . he left that series in 2013 .
