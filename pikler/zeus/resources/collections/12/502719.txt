Ruud Lubbers     rudolphus franciscus marie `` ruud '' lubbers ( ; born 7 may 1939 ) is a dutch retired politician of the christian democratic appeal ( cda ) . he served as prime minister of the netherlands from 4 november 1982 until 22 august 1994. other websites . - dr. r.f.m .
