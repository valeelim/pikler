Red Fisher     red fisher ( born 22 august 1926 ) is a canadian retired sports journalist . his columns focused on the national hockey league and its montreal canadiens team . he was born in montreal , quebec . he began his hockey coverage in `` the montreal star '' newspaper in 1954 where he remained as writer and sports editor until that paper 's demise in 1979. he retired in 2012. other websites . - `` montreal gazette '' : red fisher , accessed 11 november 2006
