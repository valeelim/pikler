Crystal City, Texas    crystal city , texas crystal city is a city in the state of texas in the united states . it is the county seat of zavala county . about 7,446 people lived there in 2013. the `` san antonio , uvalde and gulf railroad '' stopped in crystal city . crystal city was home to a large internment camp in world war ii . it was the largest internment camp for germans and those of german descent in the us . almost all the children and many of the wives interned here were american citizens . most were held here for four years .
