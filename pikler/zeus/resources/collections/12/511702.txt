The Four Horsemen of the Apocalypse (1921 movie)    the four horsemen of the apocalypse ( 1921 movie ) the four horsemen of the apocalypse is a 1921 american silent movie set during world war i. its story was written in a book by vicente blasco ibáñez , and it was made into the movie by june mathis . when the film was released in 1921 , it became very popular . people bought `` gaucho pants '' to dress like the movie 's main character , julio desnoyers . julio desnoyers was played by rudolph valentino . after starring in this film , he became famous . june mathis became one of the most successful women in hollywood . `` the four horsemen of the apocalypse '' is considered an important film . because of this , the library of congress 's national film registry is preserving it and making sure it does n't get damaged . the film is also in the public domain because it is so old . ( it was released 94 years ago . ) `` the four horsemen of the apocalypse '' can be downloaded for free on the internet archive .
