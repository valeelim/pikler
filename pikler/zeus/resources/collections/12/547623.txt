Hekatonkheires     the hekatonkherires were uranus ' and gaia 's children . after they were born , they were thrown in a prison in tartarus guarded by kampê because their father thought they were hideous . many years later zeus , with his brothers and sisters , saved the hundred-handed hekatonkheires and the cyclops from kampê 's prison to fight against the titans . the hekatonkheires threw rocks at mount othrys in the titan war and destroyed the mountain . after the titan war , there were not many stories about the hekatonkheires .
