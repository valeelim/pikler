Mike Huckabee presidential campaign, 2016    mike huckabee presidential campaign , 2016 the mike huckabee presidential campaign of 2016 began on may 5 , when huckabee announced his intention to run for the republican nomination in the 2016 presidential election . huckabee made the announcement in his hometown , hope , arkansas . he suspended his campaign on february 1 , 2016 .
