Arrondissements of the Charente department     there are 3 arrondissements in the charente department . the french departments , and in other countries , are divided into `` arrondissements '' , which may be translated into english as districts ( in some cases , as boroughs ) . the capital of an arrondissement is called a subprefecture . if the prefecture ( capital ) of the department is in an arrondissement , that prefecture is the capital of the arrondissement , acting both as a prefecture and as a subprefecture . arrondissements are further divided into communes . the 3 arrondissements of charente are : 1. arrondissement of angoulême , ( prefecture of the charente department : angoulême ) , with 148 communes . 1. arrondissement of cognac , ( subprefecture of the charente department : cognac ) , with 108 communes . 1. arrondissement of confolens , ( subprefecture of the charente department : confolens ) , with 138 communes . since its creation , the charente department has had some changes : - 1790 : creation of the charente department with six districts : ruffec , confolens , la rochefoucauld , angoulême , barbezieux and cognac . angoulême was the capital of the department .
