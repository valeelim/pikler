Kyle Jean-Baptiste     kyle jean-baptiste ( december 3 , 1993 – august 28 , 2015 ) was an american broadway actor . he was the youngest actor ever to play the role of jean valjean in `` les misérables '' on broadway . he was also the first african-american to play that role on broadway . jean-baptiste died at age 21 , after falling off a fire escape staircase at his mother 's home in new york city .
