Battlefield Hardline     battlefield hardline is a first-person shooter video game . it is published by electronic arts . it was released in march 2015 for microsoft windows , playstation 3 , playstation 4 , xbox 360 and xbox one . unlike the earlier games in the `` battlefield '' series , `` hardline '' is about crime , heist and police force , instead of military warfare . battlefield hardline focuses on a different aspect of the first person shooter than the other games in its series while battlefield 2,3 , and 4 focus on the american and russian military , battlefield hardline now focuses on the war on crime in america . the new game now features two different teams with cops and criminals . both sides now receive guns and gadgets specific to their team . so while the criminal would get an akm the cops will get a m16a3 . the game features a bunch of new game modes and a bunch of different maps . a small hillbilly-like town focused around one big house in the middle of the map that is secretly a meth lab . a big open downtown city of miami ; this map has many open streets and tall skyscrapers .
