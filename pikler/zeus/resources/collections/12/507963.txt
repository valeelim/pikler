Bob Corker     bob corker ( born august 24 , 1952 ) is an american politician . on january 3 , 2007 , he became the junior u.s. senator from tennessee . from 2001 to 2005 , he was mayor of chattanooga . corker was born on august 24 , 1952 in orangeburg , south carolina . he graduated from the university of tennessee . he and his wife elizabeth have two daughters .
