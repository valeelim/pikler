Matthias Kuhle     matthias kuhle ( 20 april 1948 – 25 april 2015 ) was a german geographer . he was also a professor at the university of göttingen in göttingen . he edited the book series `` geography international '' published by shaker verlag . kuhle was born in berlin . he died in an earthquake on 25 april 2015 in the village of saurpani , gorkha , nepal . he was 67 .
