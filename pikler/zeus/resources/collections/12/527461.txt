Cubic inch     the cubic inch is a unit of measurement for volume in the imperial units and united states customary units systems . it is the volume of a cube with each of its three dimensions ( length , width , and depth ) being one inch long . the cubic inch and the cubic foot are still used as units of volume in the united states . one popular use is for engine capacity , also called cubic inch displacement . the common si units of volume , the liter , milliliter , and cubic meter , are also used , especially in manufacturing and high technology . one cubic foot is equal to exactly 1,728 cubic inches because 12 = 1,728. one cubic inch equals 16.39 cubic centimeters .
