Momoko Kōchi     momoko kōchi ( born momoko okouchi march 7 , 1932 - november 5 , 1998 ) was a japanese actress . she is best known for her roles in the original `` godzilla '' , playing the character of emiko yamane . kōchi later played the same role in `` godzilla vs. destoroyah '' in 1995. she starred in `` the mysterians '' , playing hiroko iwamoto . kōchi was discovered in 1953 in toho 's new face contest . by the late 1950s she found herself typecast into science fiction and horror movies . she decided to pursue formal acting . in the 1960s she was on stage doing shakespeare . she also did occasional television commercials . she did n't act in movies again until 1995. kōchi died at age 66 in tokyo japan on november 5 , 1998 from intestinal cancer .
