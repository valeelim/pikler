Joan Sebastian     josé manuel figueroa sr. ( april 8 , 1951 – july 13 , 2015 ) , better known as joan sebastian ( ) , was a mexican singer-songwriter . he has written over a thousand songs including compositions for vicente fernández , lucero , pepe aguilar , and rocío dúrcal . his music is a mixture of latin pop , ranchera and grupera music . on july 13 , 2015 , sebastian died at his ranch in cruz de la sierra , teacalco , guerrero , mexico , from bone cancer , aged 64. other websites . - biography at musicamania - starpulse.com – discography - biography at chalino.com
