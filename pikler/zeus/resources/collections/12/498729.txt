Helmut Dietl     helmut dietl ( 22 june 1944 – 30 march 2015 ) was a german movie director and author . he was born in bad wiessee , but raised in munich , germany . he was known for directing the popular television series `` monaco franze – der ewige stenz '' . dietl died of lung cancer in munich , germany , aged 70. awards . - 1996 bavarian film awards , best director - 2013 deutscher filmpreis , lifetime achievement award - 2014 bambi award , lifetime achievement award
