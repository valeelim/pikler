Skyros     skyros is an island of the east coast of greece . it is the southernmost island of the sporades , an archipelago in the aegean sea . around the 2nd millennium bc and slightly later , the island was known as `` the island of the magnetes '' . later it was called `` pelasgia '' and `` dolopia '' . finally it got the name skyros . at it is the largest of the sporades . the island has a population of about 3,000 ( in 2011 ) . the hellenic air force has a major base in skyros , because of the island 's strategic location in the middle of the aegean . it is a popular tourist location . skyros is the resting place of rupert brooke , the british poet . < br >
