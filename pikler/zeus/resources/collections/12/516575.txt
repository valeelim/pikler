2008 Detroit Lions season     the 2008 detroit lions season was the 79th football season of the detroit lions of the national football league ( nfl ) . it was their third year under head coach rod marinelli . in 2008 , the lions had one of the worst seasons of any major american pro sports team . they lost all 16 of their regular season games . they were the first nfl team to finish a season with no wins and 16 losses after their loss to the green bay packers . they were the second team with no wins after the 1976 tampa bay buccaneers . matt millen was fired during the bye week of the season and the rest of the staff were fired after the season was over . color key and notes : season facts . - three people who were on the 2008 lions played in super bowl 48. they were manny ramirez and paris lenon of the denver broncos and cliff avril for the seattle seahawks , who won the super bowl . - first 0-16 team in nfl history after loss to the green bay packers - the team won all 4 preseason games . - after losing to the new orleans saints in week 16 ( 7-42 ) , they became the first 0-15 team . they also became the first team to lose 15 straight games since the 2001 panthers
