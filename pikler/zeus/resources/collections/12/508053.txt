Vermont's at-large congressional district    vermont 's at-large congressional district vermont has been represented in the united states house of representatives by a single at-large congressional district since the 1930 census , when the state lost its second seat . there were once six districts in vermont . bernie sanders ( independent ) held the seat from 1991 until 2007 , when he became a u.s. senator . democrat peter welch has represented the state since 2007 .
