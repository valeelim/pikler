Sabah Al-Ahmad Al-Jaber Al-Sabah     sabah iv ahmad al-jaber al-sabah gcb ( hon ) ( arabic : الشيخ صباح الأحمد الجابر الصباح born 16 june 1929 ) is the 5th emir of kuwait and the commander of the military of kuwait . he was sworn in on 29 january 2006 after confirmation by the national assembly of kuwait .
