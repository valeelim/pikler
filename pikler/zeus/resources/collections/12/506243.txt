Kenya Airways     kenya airways is the flag carrier of kenya . it began in 1977 after east african airways stopped flying . its main hub is at nairobi airport . in 1995 , klm and kenya airways signed a codeshare agreement . klm purchased 26 % of kenya airline 's shares . kenya airways joined the skyteam alliance in 2010 as a full member . as of 2015 , the airline has 43 airplanes . they fly to africa , europe and asia . < br >
