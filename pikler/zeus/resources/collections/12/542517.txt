The Today Show     the today show is a morning talk/news show . it airs on national broadcasting company . as of 2016 , the anchors were matt lauer and savannah guthrie . natalie morales reads the news and al roker forecasts the weather . carson daly contributes to the program on a regular basis as well . the show also has a third and fourth hour . the third hour is hosted by morales , roker , tamron hall , and willie geist . the fourth hour is anchored by kathie lee gifford and hoda kotb . former anchors include bryant gumbell , katie couric , meredith vieira , and ann curry
