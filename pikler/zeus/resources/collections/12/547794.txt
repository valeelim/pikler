Gloria de Piero     gloria de piero ( born 21 december 1972 ) is a british labour party politician and journalist . she has been the member of parliament ( mp ) for ashfield since 2010. she was last known for her work on `` gmtv '' . after serving as a shadow minister from october 2010 , de piero was promoted to the shadow cabinet in 2013 as shadow minister for women and equalities . in 2015 she was appointed shadow minister for young people and voter registration . she resigned that position on 26 june 2016 .
