Rubén Mendoza Ayala     rubén mendoza ayala ( 2 february 1961 – 16 april 2016 ) was a mexican politician . he was a member of the party of the democratic revolution . he served as deputy of the lvii and lix legislatures of the mexican congress . he served from 2014 until his death in 2016. he represented the state of mexico . ayala was born in tlalnepantla de baz , mexico . he studied at national autonomous university of mexico . ayala died of cardiac arrest on 16 april 2016 in ciudad del carmen , aged 55 .
