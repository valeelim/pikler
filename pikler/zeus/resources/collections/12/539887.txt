Sergio Onofre Jarpa     sergio onofre jarpa reyes ( born 8 march 1921 ) is a chilean politician . he was born in rengo , chile . jarpa served as a member of the cabinet during the government of augusto pinochet . he served as minister of the interior from 1983 through 1985. before becoming minister , jarpa was a member of the senate from may 1973 through september 1973 and again from 1990 through 1994 .
