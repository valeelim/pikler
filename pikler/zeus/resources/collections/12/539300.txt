Ahmed Brahim     ahmed brahim ( , `` ʾaḥmad ibrāhīm '' ; 14 june 1946 – 14 april 2016 ) was a tunisian politician . he was the first secretary of ettajdid movement and the leader of the democratic modernist pole until april 2012 , when his party merged into the social democratic path of which he became the president . he was the ettajdid movement 's candidate for president of tunisia in the 2009 presidential election . he was a professor of french at the university of tunis ; his area of study was comparative linguistics .
