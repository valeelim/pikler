Åge Nordkild    åge nordkild åge nordkild ( 19 september 1951 – 28 december 2015 ) was a norwegian sami politician . he was a member of the norwegian sami association . he held many places in the sami parliament from 2001 to 2005 and 2009 to 2013. he was born in narvik . nordkild died on 28 december 2015 at the age of 64 .
