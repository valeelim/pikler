At sign     the at-sign ( @ ) is a symbol . it is used in bills , emails and programming . in email , it is used to show a specific domain of an email address . in bills , it used to mean `` at a rate of '' ; for example , `` 7 widgets @ £2 = £14 '' . in programming , the at-sign has been used in a wide range of programming languages . it was invented by ray tomlinson . the sign is one of several special symbols on the standard english keyboard .
