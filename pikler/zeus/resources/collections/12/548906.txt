Ashdod     ashdod ( hebrew : אַשְׁדּוֹד ; arabic : اشدود‎‎ , إسدود ) is the sixth-largest city in israel . ashdod is israel 's largest port , since about 60 % of the country 's imported goods come through the city . ashdod has a mediterranean climate with hot summers , pleasant spring and fall , and cool , rainy winters . the humidity is usually high many times year round , and rain occurs mainly from november to march . ashdod had a population of about 220,000 in 2015 , making it the sixth-largest city in israel . the ratio of women to men is about 1.05 to 1.00. also , 19.7 % of the population was under the age of 10 , 15.7 % from age 10 to 19 , 14.9 % from 20 to 29 , 19.1 % from 30 to 44 , 19.1 % from 45 to 64 , and 11.3 % were 65 or older . the population of ashdod is a lot younger than the israeli average because there are a lot of young couples in the city . over 95 % of ashdod 's population is jewish , even though only about 30 % follow their religion . the city is also home to the world 's largest karaite community , with about five thousand members .
