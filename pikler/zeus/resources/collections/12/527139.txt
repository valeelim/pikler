Michel Galabru     michel louis edmond galabru ( 27 october 1922 – 4 january 2016 ) was a french actor . he was known for his roles in `` subway '' ( 1985 ) , `` belle époque '' ( 1992 ) and as abraracourcix in `` asterix & obelix take on caesar '' ( 1999 ) . galabru was born in safi , morocco to french parents . he was raised near paris , france . he appeared in over 250 movies mostly directed by bertrand blier , jean-luc godard , luc besson and costa-gavras . galabru died on 4 january 2016 in his home in paris , france at the age of 93 .
