Lovely Professional University     lovely professional university ( lpu ) is a private university in north india created under the punjab state private university act 2005 and recognized by ugc under section 2 ( f ) of ugc act 1956. lpu has been offering more than 200 programs under 40+ disciplines . lpu is spread across more than 600 acres complete with a state-of-the-art infrastructure . the campus is free from the use of drugs , alcohol and smoking . even ragging is an offense in the campus . situated at the entrance of jalandhar city on the national highway-1 , the campus itself look like a planned city with wide roads , gardens and lawns surrounding the residential complexes as well as the buildings for academic purposes . the university campus includes its own backup electricity and water supply , shopping complexes , eating joints , transportation facilities and banking and postal services . for healthcare assistance , the campus has a hospital that operates 24*7. the campus has four major faculties
