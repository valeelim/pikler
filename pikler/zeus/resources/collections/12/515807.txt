Yoram Gross     yoram jerzy gross ( 18 october 1926 – 21 september 2015 ) was an australian producer of children 's and family entertainment . his company , flying bark productions , is best known for producing the movies `` dot and the kangaroo '' ( 1977 ) and `` blinky bill '' ( 1992 ) . gross was born in kraków , poland to jewish parents . his brother was screenwriter , director and producer natan gross ( 1919–2005 ) . in 1950 , he moved to israel . he later moved to australia in 1968 with his wife and young family . gross died in 21 september 2015 in sydney , new south wales , aged 88 .
