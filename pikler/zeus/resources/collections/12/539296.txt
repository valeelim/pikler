Boris Hybner     boris hybner ( august 5 , 1941 — april 2 , 2016 ) was a czech actor and mime artist . in 2010 , hybner won the thalia award . he was known for his movies `` jára cimrman lying , sleeping '' ( 1983 ) , `` dissolved and effused '' ( 1985 ) , `` cosy dens '' ( 1999 ) , `` i 'm all good '' ( 2008 ) , `` toys in the attic '' ( 2009 ) and in `` marguerite '' ( 2015 ) . hybner died on april 2 , 2016 in prague at the age of 74 from an illness .
