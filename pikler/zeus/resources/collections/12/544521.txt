Luis Salom     luis jaime salom horrach ( 7 august 1991 – 3 june 2016 ) was a spanish grand prix motorcycle racer . he was born in palma , majorca . salom won his first grand prix in indianapolis in 2012 , beating sandro cortese and maverick viñales in a last-lap fight . he also won at aragon . he finished the championship in second behind cortese . salom died on 3 june 2016 while racing in the 2016 catalan moto2 grand prix in barcelona after crashing against a wall , aged 24. other websites . - profile on motogp.com
