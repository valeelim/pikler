Good Girl Gone Bad: Reloaded    good girl gone bad : reloaded good girl gone bad : reloaded is the reissue of barbadian recording artist rihanna 's third studio album `` good girl gone bad '' ( 2007 ) . it was released june 2 , 2008 , by def jam recordings and srp records . the album has three new songs to go with the `` good girl gone bad '' album .
