Finnhorse     the finnhorse or finnish horse is a horse breed developed in finland . the finnhorse is the national horse breed of finland . it is suitable both for riding and to pull vehicles in races and as a workhorse . studbook of finnhorses was founded 1907. the horse is 156 cm high . it has many colours , but the main colours are brown and black . the finnhorse has often white markings on the face and the legs .
