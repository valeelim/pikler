Viktor Andersson     viktor andersson , born 28 september 1982 , is a swedish floorball player . he is a defender , he has represented several club teams . he has played in the ssl . his first club is hagaboda sk . throughout the years he has represented the following clubs : - 1995/1996 - fagerhult habo ibk ungdom , sweden - 1995/96 - 1998/99 - fagerhult habo ibk , sweden - 1999/00-2001/02 - mullsjö ais , sweden - 2001/2002-2003/04 - jönköpings ik , sweden - 2003/2004-2004/2005 - fagerhult habo ibk , sweden - 2005/2006-2006/07 - jönköpings ik , sweden - 2007/08 - basel magic , switzerland - 2008/09 - 2009/2010 - mullsjö ais , sweden - 2010/11 - fagerhult habo ibk , sweden - 2011/2012-2013/2014 - ibk landskrona , sweden
