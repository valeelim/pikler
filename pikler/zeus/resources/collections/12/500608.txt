Brive     brive-la-gaillarde , commonly called brive , is the subprefecture of the corrèze departement . until 1919 , the name of the city was brive ; since then it has been known as brive-la-gaillarde . about 50.000 people live in the city , and close to 100.000 in its urban area . the city goes back to antiquity ; its name simply means bridge . in this case , the corrèze was easy to cross at this place . gundoald was elected king of aquitaine in brive , in the year 584 .
