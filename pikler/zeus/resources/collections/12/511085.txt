Governor of Wisconsin     the governor of wisconsin is the highest executive authority in the government of the u.s. state of wisconsin . the position was first filled by nelson dewey on june 7 , 1848 , the year wisconsin became a state . prior to statehood , there were four governors of wisconsin territory . , there are five former u.s. governors of wisconsin who are currently living at this time , the oldest u.s. governor of wisconsin being anthony s. earl ( 1983–1987 , born 1936 ) . the most recent death of a former u.s. governor of wisconsin was that of patrick lucey ( 1971–1977 ) on may 10 , 2014 .
