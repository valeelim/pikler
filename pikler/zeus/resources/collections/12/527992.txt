Gloria La Riva     gloria estela la riva ( born august 13 , 1954 , in albuquerque , new mexico ) is an american politician . she works with the party for socialism and liberation and in california with the peace and freedom party , and previously with the workers world party . she was the psl 's 2008 presidential candidate , and was also vying for the nomination of the peace and freedom party in california , but lost the bid to ralph nader . la riva is running as the psl 's presidential candidate in the 2016 race , with eugene puryear as her running mate . other websites . - 2016 campaign website - `` standing for castro at the us election '' , a brief report on gloria la riva by france 24 , october 29 , 2008 - `` meet gloria '' , profile on votepsl.org
