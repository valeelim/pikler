Leonid Plyushch     leonid plyushch ( ukrainian : плющ леонід іванович ; 26 april 1938 – 4 june 2015 ) was a ukrainian mathematician and soviet dissident . plyushch became a dissident by taking a public stance on political hot topics of the time . he was arrested in january 1972 on charges of anti-soviet activity , and was jailed for a year before his trial began . plyushch was born into a ukrainian working-class family in 1938 in naryn , kirghizia . he died on 4 june 2015 in france , aged 77. other websites . - plyushch , leonid ivanovych at the dissident movement in ukraine virtual museum
