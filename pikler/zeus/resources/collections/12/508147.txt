Chałupy     chałupy ( kashubian : chalëpë , german : ceynowa ) is a village and a popular polish seaside resort , in gmina władysławowo . it has very good conditions for windsurfing and kitesurfing . it is between władysławowo and kuźnica on the hel peninsula on the southern baltic sea in puck county , pomeranian voivodeship , northern poland . it had 376 inhabitants as of 2009. prior to january 1 , 2015 , chałupy was a part of the town władysławowo .
