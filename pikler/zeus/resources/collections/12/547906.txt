Jim Metzen     james p. `` jim '' metzen ( october 26 , 1943 – july 11 , 2016 ) was a american politician and member of the minnesota senate . he is a member of the minnesota democratic–farmer–labor party ( dfl ) . he represents district 52 , which includes portions of dakota county in the southeastern twin cities metropolitan area . metzen died on july 11 , 2016 from lung cancer in south st. paul , minnesota , aged 72 .
