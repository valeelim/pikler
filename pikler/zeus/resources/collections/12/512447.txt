Palomar College     palomar college is a community college that is in san diego county , california , united states . the main campus is in san marcos , and the others are in escondido , camp pendleton , mt . carmel high school , fallbrook high school , pauma indian reservation and at ramona high school . a proposal in 2006 allowed millions of dollars to be given to the school from the state of california . there are currently 26,606 students at the college ( 2014 ) . palomar college has more than 250 associate 's degree and certificate programs , and also offers students transfer programs , which would allow them to go to different four-year universities . below is a list of the programs that are being offered at the college - arts , media , business , and computer science - business administration - communications department - computer science and information systems department - graphic communications department - performing arts department - career , technical , and extended education - cooperative education department
