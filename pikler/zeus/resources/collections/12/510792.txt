Arrondissement of Béziers     the arrondissement of béziers is an arrondissement of france . it is part of the hérault `` département '' in the occitanie region . its capital is the city of béziers . when the hérault department was created on 17 february 1800 , the `` arrondissement '' of béziers was part of that original department . it is the southernmost and largest of the `` arrondissements '' of the department , with an area of . it has 312,443 inhabitants and a density of inhabitants/km² . the `` arrondissement '' of béziers is bordered to the north by the aveyron department and the `` arrondissement '' of lodève , to the northeast by the `` arrondissement '' of montpellier , to the east by the mediterranean sea , to the south by the aude department and to the west by the tarn department . after the reorganisation of the cantons in france , cantons are not subdivisions of the `` arrondissements '' so they could have `` communes '' that belong to different `` arrondissements '' . in the `` arrondissement '' of béziers , there are only two cantons where not all their `` communes '' are in the `` arrondissement '' ; the other cantons are completely within the `` arrondissement '' . the following table shows the distribution of the `` communes '' in the cantons and `` arrondissements '' :
