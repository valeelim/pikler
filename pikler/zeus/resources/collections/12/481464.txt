Bryan Henderson     bryan henderson is an american wikipedia editor . he is known for changing `` comprised of '' on the english wikipedia . on wikipedia , he uses a user name `` giraffedata '' . in february 2015 , his edits for changing `` comprised of '' became national news . most media coverage liked his edits as `` uniquely effort for correctness '' , while others criticized it as being wrong . by august 2010 , henderson removed all – around 18,000 – occurrences of the term . he has since monitors any edits using `` comprised of '' and removes them . as per wikipedia logs , he has made over 47,000 edits to the english wikipedia .
