Netherlands women's national football team    netherlands women 's national football team the netherlands women 's national football team ( dutch : nederlands vrouwenvoetbalelftal ) is the women 's association football team that represents the european country of the netherlands . they qualified for their first fifa women 's world cup in 2015 , and two uefa women 's championships . as of 27 march 2015 , their fifa ranking is 12 . `` as of 8 june 2015 ''
