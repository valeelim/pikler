Andrew Lesnie     andrew lesnie ( 1956 – 27 april 2015 ) was an australian movie cinematographer . he was best known as the cinematographer for `` the lord of the rings '' trilogy ( 2001–2003 ) and its prequel `` the hobbit '' trilogy ( 2012–2014 ) , both directed by new zealand director peter jackson . he received the academy award for best cinematography for his work on `` '' in 2002. he also worked on `` i am legend '' ( 2007 ) and `` the water diviner '' ( 2014 ) . lesnie was born in sydney , new south wales . he died from a heart attack on 27 april 2015 in sydney , aged 59 .
