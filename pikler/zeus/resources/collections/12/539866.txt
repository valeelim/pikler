Petr Nečas     petr nečas ( born 19 november 1964 ) is a czech former politician . he served as 9th prime minister of czech republic and leader of the civic democratic party from 2010 to 2013. he was former ministry of defence ( 21 december 2012 – 19 march 2013 ) and minister of labour and social affairs ( 4 september 2006 – 8 may 2009 ) .
