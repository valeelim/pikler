Paul Émile Chabas    paul émile chabas paul émile chabas ( 1869-1937 ) was a french painter . he was born in nantes . he studied with william-adolphe bouguereau . he often travelled , to norway , algeria and greece . during his lifetime , he was known for the portraits he painted . he liked to paint naked girls , in natural settings . probably his best known work is `` september morning '' , dated 1912 .
