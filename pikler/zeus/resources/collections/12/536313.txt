Allergic rhinitis     allergic rhinitis ( also known as hay fever or pollinosis ) is a type of inflammation in the nose . it occurs when the immune system overreacts to allergens in the air . symptoms include sneezing , red , itchy and watery eyes , and swelling around the eyes . hay fever is typically caused by allergens , for example pollen , pet hair and mold . hay fever is most common between ages twenty and forty .
