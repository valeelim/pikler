Meessen De Clercq     meessen de clercq is a contemporary art gallery in brussels , belgium . the gallery is in an old building with three floors . in 2007 , art historian olivier meessen and art lover jan de clercq began to work to start a contemporary art gallery in brussels . in april 2008 the gallery opened , with a group show of works by seven artists . after this first show , the gallery closed to rebuild it . from september 2008 the gallery re-opened with group and solo shows by both older and new artists . artists who show their work include : - ignasi aballì - susan collis - jordi colomer - hreinn friðfinnsson - filip gilissen - ellen harvey - adam henry - nicolas lamas - benoît maire - jorge méndez blake - claudio parmiggiani - tania pérez cordova - evariste richer - fabrice samyn - kelly schacht - josé maría sicilia - thu van tran - maarten vanden eynde - leon vranken other websites . - official website
