Riddick Bowe     riddick bowe ( born august 10 , 1967 ) is an american amateur boxer . he won a silver medal at the 1988 summer olympics . he later became a heavyweight professional boxer . he defeated other boxers like evander holyfield and andrew golota . the latter was disqualified for giving too many low blows to bowe in 1996. bowe was born in brooklyn , new york city .
