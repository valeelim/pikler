Bound for Glory (2012)    bound for glory ( 2012 ) bound for glory ( 2012 ) was a professional wrestling pay-per-view show made by total nonstop action wrestling ( tna ) . it was held on october 14 , 2012 at the grand canyon university arena in phoenix , arizona . it was the eighth bound for glory event that tna held . this was the first time that tna held an event in the state of arizona . there were eight matches on the card . the main event saw jeff hardy defeat austin aries to win the tna world heavyweight championship .
