Bedok MRT Station     bedok mrt station ( ew5 ) is an mrt station in singapore . it is along the east west mrt line . underpasses connect the station to both sides of the road . the station also links to bedok mall and the bedok bus interchange . timeline . - 4 november 1989 : station opened previous : tanah merah ( ew4 ) < br > next : kembangan ( ew6 ) formerly the code was : e8 ( 1989 - 2001 )
