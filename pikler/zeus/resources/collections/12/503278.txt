British post offices in Crete     british post offices in crete provided the postal service in the territory of the island of crete . they were established by the military administration in the area under the control of great britain . it functioned in the period from 25 november 1898 to the end of 1899. in 1898 crete obtained autonomy under turkish suzerainty . but it was garrisoned by an international military force from britain , france , italy and russia . britain issued 5 postage stamps inscribed in greek . related pages . - british army - crete
