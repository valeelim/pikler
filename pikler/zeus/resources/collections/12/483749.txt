Still Not Getting Any...    still not getting any ... still not getting any ... is the second studio album by canadian pop punk band , simple plan . `` still not getting any ... '' was released on october 26 , 2004 through both atlantic and lava . the album peaked at # 3 on the `` billboard '' 200 and at # 2 on the canadian albums chart . the album was certified platinum by the riaa , selling one million copies . the album featured the singles `` welcome to my life '' , `` shut up ! `` , `` untitled ( how could this happen to me ? ) '' , and `` crazy '' .
