Juan Carlos Maccarone     juan carlos maccarone ( 19 october 1940 – 29 march 2015 ) was an argentine roman catholic retired bishop . in 1996 , he became bishop in roman catholic diocese of chascomús . on 18 february 1999 , maccarone was nominated as bishop in roman catholic diocese of santiago del estero . in 2005 , a video was published , which showed maccarone having sex together with a 23-year-old man .
