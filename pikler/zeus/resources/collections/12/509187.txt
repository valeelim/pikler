Mike Enzi     mike enzi ( born february 1 , 1944 ) is the senior united states senator from wyoming . he is a member of the u.s. republican party and has been in senate since january 3 , 1997. enzi was born on february 1 , 1944 in bremerton , washington . he studied at george washington university and at university of denver . he is married to diana buckley . they have three children . other websites . - mike enzi , u.s. senator for wyoming , home page
