Krampus (movie)    krampus ( movie ) krampus is a 2015 american fantasy comedy horror movie based on krampus from alpine folklore . it was directed by michael dougherty and written by dougherty , todd casey , and zach shields . the movie stars adam scott , toni collette , david koechner , allison tolman , conchata ferrell , emjay anthony , stefania lavie owen , and krista stadler . it was released on december 4 , 2015 , by universal pictures to mixed reviews .
