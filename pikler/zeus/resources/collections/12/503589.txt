Millerites     millerites was the name for the followers of the teachings of william miller . in 1833 , miller said that he had done some calculations , and that the second coming of christ would happen about the year 1843. this turned out to be false , and there was a great disappointment . miller had documented how he did bible interpretation , so anyone could do it . after the disappointment , some movements emerged that were generally called `` adventist '' .
