Anne Jules de Noailles, Duke of Noailles    anne jules de noailles , duke of noailles anne jules de noailles , duke of noailles ( 5 february 1650 - 2 october 1708 ) was a french nobleman from the house of noailles . a well regarded military man of his day , he was a general under king louis xiv of france and was later the subject of a painting by the famous court painter hyacinthe rigaud . son of anne de noailles and his wife , louise boyer , he acceded to the title of duke of noailles on his father 's death in 1678. he was married to marie françoise de bournonville , with whom he had children . one of the chief generals of france towards the end of the reign of louis xiv , and , after raising the regiment of noailles in 1689 , he commanded in spain during both the nine years ' war and the war of the spanish succession . he was made a marshal of france in 1693. he died on october 2 , 1708. anne jules de noailles had many children 1. marie christine de noailles ( 1672–1748 ) , married ( 1687 ) antoine de gramont , duke of gramont ; , `` marshal of france '' ( 1671–1725 )
