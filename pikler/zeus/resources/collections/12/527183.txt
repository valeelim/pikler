Stephen W. Bosworth     stephen warren bosworth ( december 4 , 1939 – january 3 , 2016 ) was an american academic and diplomat . he served as u.s. ambassador three times : tunisia ( 1979–1981 ) , philippines ( 1984–1987 ) , and south korea ( 1997–2001 ) . bosworth was born in grand rapids , michigan . he was married and had four children . bosworth died from prostate cancer on january 3 , 2016 in boston , massachusetts . he was 77 .
