Bill Nelson     clarence william `` bill '' nelson ii ( born september 29 , 1942 ) is an american politician and a member of the democratic party . nelson was born in miami , florida . he was raised in melbourne , florida . nelson studied at the university of florida , yale university and at the university of virginia . he serves as the senior united states senator from florida , in office since 2001. nelson began his career in the florida house of representatives , where he served from 1973 to 1979. he then served in the united states house of representatives from 1979 to 1991. in january 1986 , nelson became the second sitting member of the united states congress to fly in space along with john glenn . he flew as a payload specialist on the space shuttle `` columbia '' . in 1972 , nelson married grace cavert . the couple have two adult children : charles william `` bill jr.
