Yusuf Zuayyin     yusuf zuayyin ( ‎ ; 1931 – 10 january 2016 ) was a syrian politician . he was a member of the ba'ath party . he served as prime minister from 1965 and , again , from 1966 to 1968. he was born in al-bukamal . zuayyin died after a long illness on 10 january 2016 in stockholm , sweden . he was 84 .
