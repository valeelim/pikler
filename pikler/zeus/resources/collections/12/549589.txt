Express Yourself (Madonna song)    express yourself ( madonna song ) `` express yourself '' is a 1989 dance-pop song by madonna . the lyrics are about female empowerment and inspire women to go for the best . the song was a tribute to american r & b-funk band sly and the family stone . the song peaked at # 2 on the american `` billboard '' hot 100. it was certified gold by the recording industry association of america . there are many versions of the song . one is almost eleven minutes long .
