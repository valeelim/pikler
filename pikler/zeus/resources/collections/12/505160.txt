United Counties of Prescott and Russell     the united counties of prescott and russell are two consolidated counties in ontario , canada . the county seat is l'orignal . it was created by a merger between russell county and prescott county in 1820. it is in eastern ontario . the county covers an area of . the alfred bog is in the county . many species of birds , plants , and butterflies live in the bog . according to the canada 2011 census , prescott and russell has a population of 85,381 people . many franco-ontarian people live in prescott and russell . two thirds of the people speak french as their first language . there are four townships , one town , two municipalities , and one city in prescott and russell : - township of alfred and plantagenet - township of champlain - township of east hawkesbury - township of russell - municipality of casselman - municipality of the nation - town of hawkesbury - city of clarence-rockland
