José Napoleón Duarte     josé napoleón duarte fuentes ( november 23 , 1925 – february 23 , 1990 ) was a salvadoran politician . he served as president of el salvador from 1984 to 1989. he was mayor of san salvador before running for president in 1972. duarte died of stomach cancer in san salvador at the age of 64. other websites . - josé napoleón duarte fuentes : president of el salvador cnn cold war profile - article about herbert sanabria 's murder in 1987 .
