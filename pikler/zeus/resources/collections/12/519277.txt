France Bučar     france bučar ( 2 february 1923 – 21 october 2015 ) was a slovenian politician , legal expert and author . from 1990 through 1992 , he served as the first chairman of the freely elected slovenian parliament . he was the one to formally declare independence for slovenia on june 25 , 1991. he was thought to be one of the founding fathers of slovenian democracy and independence . he was also thought , together with peter jambrek , the main author of the current slovenian constitution .
