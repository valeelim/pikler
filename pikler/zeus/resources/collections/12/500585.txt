Tom McCabe     thomas `` tom '' mccabe ( 28 april 1954 – 19 april 2015 ) was a scottish labour party politician . he was minister for finance in the scottish executive , and was member of the scottish parliament for hamilton south until may 2011. he became an msp in the first elections to the scottish parliament in 1999 , later holding the positions of minister for parliament ( 1999–2001 ) , minister for health and community care ( 2003–2004 ) , and minister for finance . mccabe died in april 2015 after a short illness , aged 60 .
