Rita Renoir     rita renoir ( 19 january 1934 – 4 may 2016 ) was a french striptease entertainer and actress . she was born in paris . she was one of the most famous european strip-teasers . in 1964 , renoir was chosen by michelangelo antonioni to play the role of emilia in `` the red desert '' renoir died on 4 may 2016 at the age of 82. other websites . - jean-michel palmier , « rita renoir : les voyeurs attrapés par la queue » , in `` politique hebdo '' .
