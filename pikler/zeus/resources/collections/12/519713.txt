Dick Bavetta     richard w. `` dick '' bavetta ( born december 10 , 1939 in brooklyn , new york ) is a retired american professional basketball referee for the national basketball association ( nba ) . since starting in 1975 , he had never missed an assigned game . he holds the league record for most officiated games . his game on april 12 , 2013 in washington was his 2,600th consecutive game as an nba official . he retired in 2014. other websites . - professional basketball referees association
