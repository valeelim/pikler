Marcel Barbeau     marcel christian barbeau ( february 18 , 1925 – january 2 , 2016 ) was a canadian artist . his best known works are `` fenêtre sur l'avenir '' and `` fenêtre sur l'avenir '' , both in montreal . barbeau was born in montreal , quebec . he studied with paul-émile borduas . in 1995 , he was made an officer of the order of canada . he was a member of the royal canadian academy of arts . he had two children . barbeau died on january 2 , 2016 at the age of 90 .
