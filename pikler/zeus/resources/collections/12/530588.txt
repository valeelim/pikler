Eric Garcetti     eric michael garcetti ( born february 4 , 1971 ) is an american politician . he has served as mayor of los angeles since 2013. he is a former member of the los angeles city council , representing the 13th district . he served as council president from 2006 to 2012. he is a member of the democratic party . garcetti won an election to become mayor on may 21 , 2013/ he defeated city controller wendy greuel . garcetti is the city 's first elected jewish mayor and its youngest in more than a hundred years .
