F. Lee Bailey     francis lee bailey jr. , commonly referred to as f. lee bailey , ( born june 10 , 1933 ) is an american former attorney . bailey was born in waltham , massachusetts . he worked in massachusetts and florida . he was a criminal defense attorney . he served as the lawyer in the re-trial of physician sam sheppard . he was also the supervisory attorney over attorney mark j. kadish in the court martial of captain ernest medina for the my lai massacre . he was one of the lawyers for the defense in the o. j. simpson murder case . in 2014 he was denied a law license by the maine state bar association and the maine supreme judicial court .
