Anger Management (movie)    anger management ( movie ) anger management is a 2003 american slapstick comedy movie directed by peter segal . it was written by david s. dorfman . it stars adam sandler , jack nicholson , marisa tomei , john turturro , and woody harrelson . it was produced by revolution studios . it has cameos from rudy giuliani , bob sheppard , and derek jeter . it was released to positive reviews on april 11 , 2003 .
