Richard Arvine Overton     richard arvine overton ( born may 11 , 1906 ) is an american supercentenarian . he is the oldest verified surviving american world war ii veteran . he was born in bastrop county , texas . he lives in austin , texas . when he was 82 years old , his wife , wilma , died in 1988. in november 2015 , overton was hospitalized for pneumonia at the age of 109 in austin . on may 11 , 2016 , overton became a supercentenarian .
