Jon Landau (movie producer)    jon landau ( movie producer ) jon landau ( born july 23 , 1960 ) is an american movie producer . he is best known for his work with james cameron : `` titanic '' ( 1997 ) , `` solaris '' ( 2002 ) and `` avatar '' ( 2009 ) . he also produced `` campus man '' ( 1987 ) , `` honey , i shrunk the kids '' ( 1989 ) and `` dick tracy '' ( 1990 ) . landau was born in new york city , new york . he was the son of edie , a producer , and ely a. landau , a studio executive and producer . he is jewish .
