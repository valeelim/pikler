Super Bass     `` super bass '' is a song by trinidadian-american rapper nicki minaj , taken from her 2010 debut album `` pink friday '' . the song is listed as a bonus track on the deluxe edition of the album . it was released as a single from `` pink friday '' in 2011. the song was able to cross over into many genres on radio , such as pop , urban , and rhythmic . `` super bass '' was written by minaj , daniel johnson , and ester dean , who also helps write songs for other recording artists such as rihanna . the song was produced by kane beatz . the song reached number three on the u.s. `` billboard '' hot 100 in 2011 , as well as on the mainstream top 40/pop songs airplay chart . it topped the us rhythmic songs chart and reached number two on the rap songs chart . in australia and canada , the song reached number six , while it peaked at number eight in the united kingdom . `` super bass '' finished as the eighth-best performing song of 2011 in the united states .
