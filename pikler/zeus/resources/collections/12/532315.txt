Hu Chunhua     hu chunhua ( ; born april 1963 ) is a chinese politician . he is a member of the communist party 's politburo . he is the communist party secretary of guangdong province , the province 's top political office . hu was then promoted to party secretary of inner mongolia in 2009. as one of the youngest provincial leaders , hu is seen as a promising candidate in the 'sixth generation ' of chinese leadership .
