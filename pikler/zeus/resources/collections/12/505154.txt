Bill O'Herlihy     bill o'herlihy ( 26 september 1938 – 25 may 2015 ) was an irish television broadcaster and public relations executive . his career began in 1965. he worked for raidió teilifís éireann ( rté ) , and was best known for his work on rté sport . he presented for 10 fifa world cups and 10 summer olympic games before retiring in 2014. he also appeared in current affairs programs `` newsbeat '' and `` 7 days '' . o'herlihy was born in glasheen , cork . he was married to hilary and had two daughters , jill and sally . he had an operation in 2007 because of a colon cancer tumour . o'herlihy died on 25 may 2015 in dublin , aged 76 .
