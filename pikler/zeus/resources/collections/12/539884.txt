Vincent Darius     vincent matthew darius , op ( 6 september 1955 – 26 april 2016 ) was grenadian roman catholic official . he was the bishop of the roman catholic diocese of saint george 's in grenada from 2002 until his death in 2016. he was born in the village of crochu . he was ordained on 28 june 1987. darius died from cancer in bellevue , new york on 26 april 2016 , aged 60. other websites . - bishop vincent darius , `` catholic diocese of saint george 's in grenada '' - rinuncie e nomine , 10.07.2002 ( b0362 ) , `` press office of the holy see '' ( in italian ) - bishop vincent matthew darius , o.p . † , `` hierarchy of the catholic church ( catholic-hierarchy.org ) '' - coat of arms ( of the bishop ) , `` catholic diocese of saint george 's in grenada '' - death of bishop vincent darius op , `` now grenada '' , 26 april - caribbean bishops meet , `` catholic news ( trinidad and tobago ) '' , 10 april ( front page )
