Corgémont     corgémont is a municipality in the administrative district of jura bernois in the canton of bern in switzerland . it is located in the french-speaking part of the canton . corgémont is in the jura mountains . corgémont has a population of 1,650 as of 2014. there are 689 families living in corgémont . males make up 50.1 % of the population while females make up 49.9 % . the average age is 41.6 years old .
