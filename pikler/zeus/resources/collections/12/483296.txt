Joshua Fishman     joshua aaron fishman , ( yiddish : שיקל פֿישמאַן — shikl fishman ; july 18 , 1926 – march 1 , 2015 ) was an american linguist . he specialized in the sociology of language , language planning , bilingual education , and language and ethnicity . fishman was born in philadelphia , pennsylvania . he died in the bronx , new york , aged 88 .
