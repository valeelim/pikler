Jimmy Fitzmorris     james edward `` jimmy '' fitzmorris , jr. ( born november 15 , 1921 ) is an american businessman and civic leader . he was the democratic lieutenant governor of louisiana from 1972 to 1980. he was the first full-time lieutenant governor in state history . he was the last lieutenant governor whose duties included presiding over the louisiana state senate . in 1979 , he ran third in the nonpartisan blanket primary for governor . in 1983 , he was unsuccessful is his effort to regain the lieutenant governor 's office .
