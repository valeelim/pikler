Donald Crisp     donald crisp ( born george william crisp , 27 july 188225 may 1974 ) was an english-born american actor , director , producer and screenwriter . he began his career as a silent movie actor . he won an academy award for best supporting actor in 1942 for his role in `` how green was my valley '' . crisp was born in london , england . he studied at the university of oxford . crisp died in los angeles , california from a stroke at the age of 91 .
