Negev tortoise     the negev tortoise ( scientific name : '' testudo werneri '' ) is a tortoise found mainly in the negev desert in southern israel . it was once present in the sinai peninsula but disappeared due to loss of habitat . in israel it is protected by law and several conservation programs . it is currently considered a critically endangered species . the negev is one of the world 's smallest tortoises . unlike other tortoises it does not hibernate in winter . instead , winter is the breeding season . there is some disagreement over whether the negev tortoise is the same species as the kleinmann 's tortoise . in 2010 the iucn recommended that israel take additional steps to protect the negev tortoise including creating a wildlife preserve . < br >
