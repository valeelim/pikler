FFC Frankfurt     1. ffc frankfurt is a german women 's association football team that represents frankfurt and plays in the frauen bundesliga . the team is the most successful german team , both in the league and in europe . frankfurt has won 7 bundesliga titles , 4 uefa women 's champions league titles and 9 dfb-pokal titles . some notable players include birgit prinz , fatmire alushi , melanie behringer , célia šašić and simone laudehr . other websites . - 1. ffc frankfurt official website
