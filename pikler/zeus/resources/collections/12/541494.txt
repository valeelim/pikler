Philippe Beaussant     philippe beaussant ( 6 may 1930 – 8 may 2016 ) was a french musicologist and novelist . he was expert on french baroque music . he was born in bordeaux , france . he was the founder of the centre de musique baroque de versailles . he was the artistic adviser of 1987 to 1996. he has also been a producer of musical programs for radio france since 1974. he was known for writing the biography of jean-baptiste lully , `` lully ou le musicien du soleil '' ( éditions gallimard , 1992 ) . beaussant won the 1993 grand prix du roman de l'académie française for his novel `` héloïse '' . he was elected to seat 36 of the académie française on 15 november 2007. he died in paris on 8 may 2016 , aged 86. other websites . - biography of beaussant at the french-language publisher librairie arthème fayard
