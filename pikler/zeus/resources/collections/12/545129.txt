Bob Uecker     robert george `` bob '' uecker ( ; born january 26 , 1934 ) is a retired american major league baseball player , later a sportscaster , comedian and actor . uecker is also known as `` mr. baseball '' . since 1971 , uecker has served as a play-by-play announcer for milwaukee brewers radio broadcasts . uecker is a member of the national baseball hall of fame as a 2003 recipient of the ford c. frick award for his broadcasting career . other websites . - wwe hall of fame profile - baseball hall of fame - frick award recipient - bob uecker quotes - bob uecker interview on onmilwaukee.com
