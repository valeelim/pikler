Strelka Institute for Media, Architecture and Design    strelka institute for media , architecture and design strelka institute for media , architecture and design is an international school for architecture located in moscow , russia . the institute works with students from all around the world to solve problems relating to urbanism and urban development . the institute is a nonprofit project by the russian government . it began in 2009. in 2014 the institute won a prize from the domus magazine for the top 100 best schools of architecture of that year . other websites . - strelka institut
