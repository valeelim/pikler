Joe Scarborough     charles joseph `` joe '' scarborough ( born april 9 , 1963 ) is the host of morning joe on national broadcasting company 's ( nbc ) sister network msnbc ( microsoft nbc ) . he previously hosted scarborough country on the same network . he is a former member of the house of representatives . on july 11 , 2017 , scarborough annouced on the late show with stephen colbert that he was leaving the republican party to become an independent .
