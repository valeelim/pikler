John Sergeant Cram     john sergeant cram , sr. ( 1852-1936 ) was head of the head of the new york public service commission . cram was born in 1852 in new york to henry a. cram and catherine sergeant . he attended the st. paul school in concord , new hampshire and then harvard university . he was appointed as the head of the new york public service commission by charles seymour whitman from 1911 to 1916. in 1916 he was replaced by travis h. whitney . he married edith claire bryce . he died in 1936. his widow died in 1960 .
