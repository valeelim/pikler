Khamseh     the khamseh is a tribal confederation in the province of fars in southwestern iran . it consists of five tribes.it was created in 1861 by naser al-din shah qajar to decrease strength of the qashqai , who were gaining so much power . the khamseh tribes were a mixture of persians , turks , and arabs . tribes . - the basseri ( persian ) - the arab ( arabic ) - tha baharlu ( turkish ) - tha inalu ( turkish ) - tha nafar ( turkish ) sources . - ali mohammad najafi.great men of arab tribe and the tribes - ali mohammad najafi.khamseh tribes happenings - ehsan yousefi , nomads of persia : the basseri tribe ; 2014 ; shiraz
