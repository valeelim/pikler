Alexander Schalck-Golodkowski     alexander schalck-golodkowski ( july 3 , 1932 – june 21 , 2015 ) was a east german politician and trader . he was born in berlin . he was director of a main department ( 'hauptverwaltungsleiter ' ) in the ministry for foreign trade and german domestic trade ( 1956–62 ) , the deputy minister for external trade ( 1967–75 ) , and head of the gdr 's `` kommerzielle koordinierung '' ( koko , 1966–86 ) . other websites . - biography
