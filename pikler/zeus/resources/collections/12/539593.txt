Martin Gray (Holocaust survivor)    martin gray ( holocaust survivor ) martin gray ( born mieczysław grajewski ; 27 april 1922 – 25 april 2016 ) was a polish holocaust survivor . he was born in warsaw . he emigrated to the west , and has published books in french about his experiences during world war ii in which his family was killed in poland . gray 's first book , `` for those i loved '' ( `` au nom de tous les miens '' ) , became a bestseller . he moved to belgium in 2001. on 25 april 2016 , he was found dead in his swimming pool at his home in ciney , belgium . he died of a suspected heart attack . he was aged 93. other websites . - martin gray official website - `` a biography of martin gray ''
