Maryam Zakaria     maryam zakaria ( persian : مریم زکریا ; born on 27 september 1985 in tehran ) swedish-iranian actress . she has worked in bollywood and the south indian cinema . she is best known for her work in the 2012 film `` agent vinod '' . in sweden , zakaria worked as model , dance teacher , choreographer and established a bollywood dance school called the indisk dans studio . she moved to mumbai in 2009 and began to model in ads for set wet , layz and coke . she performed an item number in the film `` nagaram '' ( 2010 ) , after the director saw a video of her dancing on youtube . the film was a big breakthrough for zakaria .
