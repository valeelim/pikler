Hand in My Pocket     `` hand in my pocket '' is a rock song by alanis morissette . it was from her third studio album `` jagged little pill '' . the album peaked at number one in canada . in the united states it hit number one on the u.s. billboard 200 albums chart . the song was released as a single on 31 october 1995 in the united states . it became her second song to hit number one on the billboard modern rock tracks chart . a music video in black and white was released for the single showing morissette at a festival driving her car .
