Peter Howell (actor)    peter howell ( actor ) peter howell ( 1919 – 20 april 2015 ) was an english actor . he was known for his appearances on `` emergency – ward 10 '' , `` the avengers '' , `` the prisoner '' , `` doctor who '' , `` the lord of the rings '' , and `` yes minister '' . howell was born in london . he died after a short illness on 20 april 2015 in northwood , aged 95/96 .
