Getatchew Mekurya     gétatchèw mèkurya ( amharic : ጌታቸው መኩሪያ ) ( 14 march 1935 – 4 april 2016 ) was an ethiopian jazz saxophonist . mekurya reached an international audience when his album `` negus of ethiopian sax '' was re-released as part of the ethiopiques cd series . mekurya 's playing style has been compared to free jazz . mekurya died in addis ababa , ethiopia from a leg infection caused by diabetes on 4 april 2016 , aged 81 .
