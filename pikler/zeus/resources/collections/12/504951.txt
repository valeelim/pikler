Little Big Man     little big man is a 1970 western-comedy movie . it is about native americans and american pioneers in the 19th century . also in 19th century , a white male child is raised by the cheyenne nation . dustin hoffman plays jack crabb . faye dunaway plays louise pendrake . the movie got very positive reviews from critics . roger ebert gave the movie four stars . it was rated pg-13 by the mpaa .
