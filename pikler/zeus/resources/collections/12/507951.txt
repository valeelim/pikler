Pat Toomey     pat toomey ( born november 17 , 1961 ) is an american politician . on january 3 , 2011 , he became the junior u.s. senator from pennsylvania . he was in the united states house of representatives from 1999 to 2005. toomey was born on november 17 , 1961 in providence , rhode island . he graduated from the harvard university . he is married to kris toomey . they have three children .
