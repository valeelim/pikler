Rise (Katy Perry song)    rise ( katy perry song ) `` rise '' is a song co-written and recorded by american singer-songwriter katy perry . the song was released exclusively on itunes and apple music on july 14 , 2016. the song is set to be used in the 2016 summer olympics in rio de janeiro , brazil . she released the song because `` [ she ] was inspired to finish it now , rather than save it for [ her ] next album , because now more than ever , there is a need for our world to unite . '' `` rise '' has topped the itunes charts of over 25 countries worldwide , including the u.s. , uk , canada , and australia . the song entered the australian aria singles chart at number 1 and reached number 3 in scotland . the song reached the top 20 in the us and canada . one music video for the song features olympic athletes practicing for the games . however , an official video was released and shows perry climbing through different terrains with a red parachute . critics have said that the song was powerful and uplifting but received mixed reviews . daniel kreps from `` rolling stone '' called producer max martin an `` extraordinaire , '' while brandon caldwell from the `` houston press '' said that the song `` just doesn ’ t fit the template of inspirational , fiery and get america ready to beat the rest of the world in sporting competitions . it just doesn ’ t fit the olympics . now , in terms of beating world issues ? 'rise ' may have something there . ''
