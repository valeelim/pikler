Planomicrobium stackebrandtii     planomicrobium stackwebrandtii is a species of bacteria . it was named after german microbiologist erko stackebrandt . it belongs to the firmicutes . it is gram-positive and was first found in 2005 at the himalayas . the cells are cocci in pairs or clumps . it is capable of aerobic respiration using oxygen . the bacterium does not form endospores . it has one or two flagella .
