Franco Citti     franco citti ( 23 april 1935 – 14 january 2016 ) was an italian actor . he acted in over fifty movies between the early 1960s and the late 1990s . he was best known to non-italian audiences as calò in `` the godfather '' ( 1972 ) and `` the godfather part iii '' ( 1990 ) . he also appeared in the movies `` accattone '' ( 1961 ) and `` oedipus rex '' ( 1967 ) . citti was born in fiumicino , rome . his brother was the director and screenwriter sergio citti ( 1933–2005 ) . citti died on 14 january 2016 in rome , aged 80 .
