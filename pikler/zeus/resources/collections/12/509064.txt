Javier Krahe     javier krahe de salas ( march 30 1944 – july 12 , 2015 ) was a spanish singer-songwriter . he met joaquín sabina and alberto pérez after his services in the military . as a trio , they recorded `` la mandrágora '' . he also worked at tve on the television show `` si yo fuera presidente '' ( `` if i were president '' ) . krahe died of a heart attack on july 12 , 2015 in zahara de los atunes , cádiz .
