Papa Wemba     jules shungu wembadio pene kikumba , or commonly know as papa wemba ( june 14 , 1949 – april 24 , 2016 ) was a congolese rumba ( later known as soukous ) singer and musician . he was one of africa 's most popular musicians , and known in world music . he was born in lubefu , sankuru district , belgian congo . he worked with zaiko langa langa and viva la musica . wemba , who suffered from epilepsy , died after suffering a seizure on stage in abidjan , côte d'ivoire on april 24 , 2016 at the age of 66. other websites . - papa wemba official web site - biography of papa wemba , soukous radio
