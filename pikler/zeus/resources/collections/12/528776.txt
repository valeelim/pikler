S7 Airlines     s7 airlines is a russian airline . it is the second largest airline . it was created in may 1992. originally , it was named `` siberia airlines '' . in 1994 , the airline joined iata . the airline 's name was changed to `` s7 airlines '' in 2005. in 2010 , the airline joined oneworld . s7 airlines stopped flying soviet planes in 2008. as of 2015 , the airline has airbus a319s , airbus a320s , airbus a321s , boeing 737s and boeing 767s .
