Stratford Festival     the stratford festival , formerly called the stratford shakespeare festival , is an annual theatre festival held in stratford , ontario , canada . it runs from april to october . about a third of the plays are by william shakespeare . the remainder range from greek tragedy to contemporary theatre . the theme for the 2015 season is called `` eureka moments '' . featured plays will include shakespeare 's `` hamlet '' , `` the taming of the shrew '' and `` love 's labour 's lost '' .
