Goosebumps (movie)    goosebumps ( movie ) goosebumps is a 2015 american fantasy horror comedy movie . it was based on the children 's book series of the same name by r.l . stine . it is directed by rob letterman and produced by deborah forte and neal h. moritz . it was written by darren lemke with music by danny elfman . it was made by columbia pictures and sony animated pictures . the movie stars jack black , dylan minnette , odeya rush , amy ryan , halston sage , ryan lee , and jillian bell . it was filmed in atlanta , georgia . the movie is set in present-day delaware . it was released on october 16 , 2015 to mostly positive reviews . it earned $ 156 million . it received nominations for the las vegas film critics society for best family film and the visual effects society for outstanding visual effects in a special venue project . on september 2 , 2015 , it was reported that a sequel is in the works . other websites . - `` goosebumps '' - `` the goosebumps movie ''
