Madhur Jaffrey     madhur jaffrey , cbe ( born 13 august 1933 ) is an indian cook and writer . she has been recognized in many magazine articles . sixteen of her books are cookbooks . the other four are stories . one of her cookbooks is `` madhur jaffrey 's world vegetarian : more than 750 meatless recipes from around the globe '' . one of her stories is `` robi dobi : the marvelous adventures of an indian elephant .
