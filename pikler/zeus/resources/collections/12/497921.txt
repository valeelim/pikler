Tierra de reyes     tierra de reyes is an american telenovela . it is produced by telemundo . cast . - aarón díaz as arturo `` rey '' gallardo - ana lorena sánchez as sofía del junco - kimberly dos ramos as irina del junco - gonzalo garcía vivanco as flavio `` rey '' gallardo - scarlet gruber as andrea del junco - christian de la campa as samuel `` rey '' gallardo - sonya smith as cayetana belmonte del junco - fabián ríos as leonardo montalvo - daniela navarro as patricia rubio
