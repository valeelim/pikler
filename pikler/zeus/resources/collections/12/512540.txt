Black Friday (Lil' Kim mixtape)    black friday ( lil ' kim mixtape ) black friday is the second mixtape by american rapper lil ' kim , released on february 14 , 2011. the mixtape is a parody of fellow female rapper nicki minajs 2010 album `` pink friday '' in which minaj takes shots at kim on several songs such as `` roman 's revenge '' and `` did it on 'em '' . kim 's artwork features a picture of kim with a sword that had just been used to behead minaj . the title track also is a diss song aimed at minaj as kim imitates minaj talking about kim on her song `` roman 's revenge '' in an interview . the album also includes vocals from minaj and r & b singers rihanna and keri hilson .
