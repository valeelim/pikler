Quarter Pounder     the quarter pounder is a hamburger product sold by international fast food chain mcdonald 's . the burger contains a patty with a precooked weight of a quarter of a pound ( 113.4 g ) , two buns , two slices of american cheese , pickles and onions , as well as ketchup and mustard . the quarter pounder was created by al bernardin , a franchise owner , in fremont , california , in 1971. other websites . - quarter pounder at mcdonalds.com
