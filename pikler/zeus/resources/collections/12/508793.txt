Ghulam Sughra     ghulam sughra is a human rights activist in pakistan . she is the head of the `` marvi rural development organization '' ( mrdo ) . in 2011 she received the international women of courage award from the u.s. department of state . sughra was born in sindh province in pakistan . she was married at the age of 12. six years later , she was the first woman in her village to divorce . when she tried to go to school , her brothers beat her . so she studied at home . sughra became the first girl in her village to finish secondary school . later she was the first teacher in the first school for girls . ghulam sughra started the marvi rural development organization ( mrdo ) in her village . the mrdo works for education , health , and human rights . it also loans money for women to start small businesses . awards . - international women of courage award other websites . - 2011 international women of courage awards , u.s. state department photostream on flickr - secretary 's international women of courage award ( u.s. department of state )
