The Stooges     the stooges are an american protopunk band . they were formed in 1967 in ann arbor , michigan and began to record music two years later . their first album was released in august 1969. it was produced by john cale from the velvet underground . they released two more albums before the first members broke up in 1974. discography . - `` the stooges '' ( 1969 ) - `` fun house '' ( 1970 ) - `` raw power '' ( 1973 ) - `` the weirdness '' ( 2007 ) - `` ready to die '' ( 2013 )
