2014 Major League Soccer season     the 2014 major league soccer season was the 19th major league soccer season . the season had 19 teams ( 16 from the united states , and 3 from canada ) . the seattle sounders won the supporters ' shield , and los angeles galaxy won the mls cup . the top scorer was bradley wright-phillips , with 27 goals . the champion of the overall table , which combines the eastern and western conference , is the champion of the supporters shield . this table also will show what team will qualify for next years concacaf champions league .
