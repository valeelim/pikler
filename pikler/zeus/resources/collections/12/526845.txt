Raoul Walsh     raoul a. walsh ( march 11 , 1887december 31 , 1980 ) was an american film director and actor . he also founded the academy of motion picture arts and sciences . he was known for playing the part of john wilkes booth in the movie `` the birth of a nation '' ( 1915 ) . he also directed films like `` the big trail '' ( 1930 ) starring john wayne , `` high sierra '' ( 1941 ) starring ida lupino and humphrey bogart , and `` white heat '' ( 1949 ) with james cagney and edmond o'brien . walsh died on december 31,1980 and as buried in ventura county , california .
