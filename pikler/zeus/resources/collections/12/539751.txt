Éverton Ribeiro    éverton ribeiro éverton augusto de barros ribeiro ( born 10 april 1989 ) , known as éverton ribeiro , is a brazilian footballer who plays as an attacking midfielder or winger for emirati club al-ahli , and the brazil national football team . formed at corinthians , after a loan at são caetano he was sold to coritiba in 2011 , winning the campeonato paranaense in both of his seasons . he also won série a twice at cruzeiro , being voted the best player of each season , and joined al-ahli for €15 million in 2014. a full international since 2014 , ribeiro repesented brazil at the 2015 copa américa .
