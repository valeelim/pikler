Tampines MRT Station     tampines mrt station ( ew2/dt32 ) is an mrt station in singapore . it is an interchange station along east west mrt line . as of 2017 , it is planned to also be an interchange for the downtown mrt line . timeline . - 16 december 1989 : station opened along east west line - 10 january 2008 : train disruption at tampines - july 2011 : downtown line started construction - 2017 : downtown line will begin operation following stations . - east west line previous : pasir ris ( ew1 ) < br > next : simei ( ew3 ) - downtown line previous : tampines west ( dt31 ) < br > next : tampines east ( dt33 ) former codes . - east west line : e11 - downtown line : n/a
