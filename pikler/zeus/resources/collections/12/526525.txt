Antony Booth     anthony george booth ( born 9 october 1931 , later known as tony and antony ) is a retired english actor . he is best known for his role as mike rawlins in the bbc series `` till death us do part '' . booth is the father-in-law of former prime minister of the united kingdom tony blair . blair is married to booth 's daughter cherie booth . booth suffered a stroke in 2010. he was diagnosed with alzheimer 's disease in 2004 .
