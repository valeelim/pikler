Kinnickinnic River (St. Croix River)    kinnickinnic river ( st. croix river ) the kinnickinnic river , called the kinni for short , is a river in northwestern wisconsin in the united states . the kinni is a cold water fishery supporting a population of native brook trout and naturally reproducing brown trout . the kinnickinnic river is officially designated as a class i trout stream by the wisconsin department of natural resources . this means it is a `` high quality '' trout water that has populations of wild trout . also , these streams do not need stocking with hatchery trout . the main recreational activity on the upper part of the kinnickinnic river is trout fishing . nearer to river falls downtown , walking , hiking , running , mountain biking along the trails are popular activities . the city is also a bird city and attracts a number of birders along the river . kayaking along the lower parts of the river has become popular .
