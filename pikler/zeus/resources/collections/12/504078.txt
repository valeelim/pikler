Wind of Change     `` wind of change '' is a 1990 rock ballad by the scorpions . the song is about the ending of the cold war and hope when tense conditions came up . the song was released in january 1991. it became a worldwide hit single during 1991. the song has sold millions of copies worldwide since then . it hit # 4 on the american `` billboard '' hot 100. there is also a russian language version of the song .
