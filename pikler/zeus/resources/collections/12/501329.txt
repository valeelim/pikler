Arrondissement of Lesparre-Médoc     the arrondissement of lesparre-médoc is an arrondissement of france . it is part of the gironde `` département '' in the nouvelle-aquitaine region . its capital is the city of lesparre-médoc . when the gironde department was created on 17 february 1800 , the `` arrondissement '' of lesparre-médoc was part of that original department , with the name of `` arrondissement of lesparre '' . the `` arrondissement '' was dissolved on september 1926 but it was again an `` arrondissement '' on june 1942 but with the name of `` arrondissement of lesparre-médoc '' . the `` arrondissement '' of lesparre-médoc is in the northwest of gironde , with an area of , the second largest , after the `` arrondissement '' of langon , of the `` arrondissements '' of the department . it has 86,560 inhabitants and a population density of inhabitants/km² . the `` arrondissement '' is bordered to the north by the charente-maritime department , to the east by the the `` arrondissements '' of blaye , to the southeast by the `` arrondissement '' of bordeaux , to the south by the `` arrondissement '' of arcachon and to the west by the atlantic ocean ( `` gulf of biscay '' ) .
