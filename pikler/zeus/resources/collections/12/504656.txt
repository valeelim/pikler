Abby Elliott     abigail `` abby '' elliott ( born june 16 , 1987 ) is an american actress and comedian . she does impressions of celebrities on television . she played jeanette peterson in `` how i met your mother '' . she also played taylor in the 2014 remake movie `` teenage mutant ninja turtles '' . elliott was born in new york city . her father , chris elliott , was on `` saturday night live '' in 1994-1995. her grandfather is comedian bob elliott .
