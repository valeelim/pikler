Tom Ellis (actor)    tom ellis ( actor ) tom ellis ( born 17 november 1978 ) is a welsh actor . he is known for playing dr. oliver cousins in the bbc television soap opera `` eastenders '' and detective sergeant sam speed in a `` life on mars '' parody on `` the catherine tate show '' . he starred as sam in the bbc comedy `` pulling '' and he played gary in the bbc2/bbc1 comedy `` miranda '' . he is best known in the united states for two american tv shows in which he played the title character , `` rush '' and `` lucifer '' .
