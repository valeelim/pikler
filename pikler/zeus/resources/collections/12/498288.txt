Fabian Bredlow     fabian bredlow ( born 2 march 1995 ) is a football player from germany . he plays for f. c. liefering , the second team of f.c . red bull salzburg , in the sky go erste liga ( 2nd level in austrian football ) as goalkeeper . he started playing football for fc hertha 03 zehlendorf . in 2012 he went on to rasenballsport leipzig u17 . 2014 he went on loan to fc liefering , where he is playing as goalkeeper in the sky go 1. liga , which is the 2nd level in austrian football on 11 october 2014 he played for the first time in the german u20 national team versus turkey .
