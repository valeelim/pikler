Russell M. Nelson     russell marion nelson ( born september 9 , 1924 ) is a member of the quorum of the twelve apostles of the church of jesus christ of latter-day saints ( lds church ) . he is also a known cardiothoracic surgeon . currently , he is the third most senior and the oldest living apostle among the ranks of the church . in july 3 , 2015 , following the death of president boyd k. packer , nelson became president of the quorum .
