Katie Ledecky     kathleen genevieve ledecky ( born march 17 , 1997 ) is an american competition swimmer . at the 2012 olympics in london , she won the gold medal in the 800-meter freestyle . at the 2016 olympics in rio de janeiro , she won four gold medals . at the world championships in 2013 in barcelona , spain , and the 2015 world championships in kazan , russia , she won 9 gold medals in total . she has broken a world record 13 times , in the 400-meter , 800-meter , and 1500-meter freestyle . ledecky was born in washington , d.c .
