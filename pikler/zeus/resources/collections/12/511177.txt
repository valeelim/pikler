2015–16 Premier League     the 2015–16 premier league is the 24th premier league that is taking place from 8 august 2015 to 17 may 2016. it was won by leicester city who , guided by claudio ranieri , upset bookmakers odds of 5000/1 at the start of the season by finishing 10 points clear of 2nd place arsenal to win their 1st ever top flight english league title . the season was supposed to end on sunday 15th may , but instead ended on tuesday 17th may due to the abandonment and subsequent rearrangement of manchester united 's last home game against a.f.c . bournemouth after a bomb scare .
