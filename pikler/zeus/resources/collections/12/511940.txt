Hotter Than July     hotter than july is the nineteenth album by american recording artist stevie wonder . it was released on motown 's tamla label on september 29 , 1980. it was certified platinum , which means it sold more than 1,000,000 copies in the us . it reached number three on the us billboard chart . this was stevie wonder 's most successful album in the uk , where it reached number two on the sales charts , and produced four top ten singles . `` hotter than july '' was nominated for favorite soul/r & b album at the american music awards in 1982. all songs written , produced and arranged by stevie wonder except where noted . - side one 1 . `` did i hear you say you love me '' – 4:07 1 . `` all i do '' ( music : wonder ; lyrics : wonder , clarence paul , morris broadnax ) – 5:06 1 . `` rocket love '' – 4:39 1 . `` i ai n't gon na stand for it '' – 4:39 1 . `` as if you read my mind '' – 3:37 - side two 1 . < li value=6 > '' master blaster ( jammin ' ) '' – 5:07 1 . `` do like you '' – 4:25 1 . `` cash in your face '' – 3:59 1 . `` lately '' – 4:05 1 . `` happy birthday '' – 5:57
