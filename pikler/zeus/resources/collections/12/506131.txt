Deepin     deepin is a free operating system that uses the linux kernel . it is one of the most popular chinese linux distributions and it is based on debian . the goal with deepin is to make it easy to use and install onto a computer . deepin can be used on all types of personal computers . deepin is downloaded as a dvd , which is free to download on the deepin website . it can be installed or tested by running the dvd . started in 2004 , deepin has been developed at a fast rate by wuhan deepin technology co. , ltd , a chinese company . the release cycle has followed various schedules but currently aims for four releases per year . releases are delayed if the work of development and testing has not been completed . deepin is based on debian and both distributions have a lot in common . both distributions use the same software repositories . most differences are on the desktop . debian has a vastly larger support community than deepin . however , the majority of debian help and advice is also applicable to deepin . debian and deepin support also x86-64 cpu architecture .
