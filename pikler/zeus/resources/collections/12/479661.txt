Joe Rígoli     jorge rígoli ( jorge alberto rípoli ; 5 november 1936 – 27 january 2015 ) was an argentine comedian and actor . he was known for starring in the popular argentine animated series `` un , dos , tres ... responda otra vez '' . rígoli was born in buenos aires , argentina . rígoli died in mar del plata , argentina from heart failure after being in a medically induced coma after having high blood pressure . he was 78 years old .
