Johan Jüngling     johan jüngling , born 1977 , is a swedish former squash player . he represented växjö sk and lived in the town of jönköping . he represented sweden at several european championships and world championships . he played for the swedish national team for 10 years and was ranked number-one in sweden . he won the swedish national championship eight times as a junior . as a senior he bcame swedish national champion four times . he also won the andalucian team championship in 2005 .
