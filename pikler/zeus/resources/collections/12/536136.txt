United States Reports     united states reports are the official record of all the cases that the united states supreme court has heard . the united states reports include many types of information , like : - the court 's decisions and orders on each case - this includes majority opinions , concurring opinions , and dissenting opinions - lists of every case the court decides in the united states , when lawyers want to talk or write about a supreme court decision , they cite the `` united states reports '' . for example , the case `` brown , et al. , v. board of education of topeka , kansas '' , is cited this way : this tells the lawyer that the decision of the court in the case entitled `` brown v. board of education '' ( as properly abbreviated ) , decided in 1954 , can be found in volume 347 of the united states reports , starting on page 483 .
