Bill Saluga     bill saluga ( born william saluga ; september 16 , 1937 ) is an american comedian . he was born in youngstown , ohio . he was a founding member , along with fred willard , michael mislove , george memmoli , and patti deutsch of the improvisational comedy troupe ace trucking company . saluga is best known for his cigar-smoking , zoot-suit-wearing television character “ raymond j. johnson , jr. ” . he is famous for his catchphrase `` you can call me ray , or you can call me jay , or you can call me raymond j. johnson , but you does n't hasta call me johnson !
