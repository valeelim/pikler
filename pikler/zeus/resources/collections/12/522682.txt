A Head Full of Dreams     a head full of dreams is the seventh hit studio album by rock-pop band coldplay . coldplay recorded `` a head full of dreams '' from summer 2014 - autumn 2015. the single `` adventure of a lifetime '' is featured in the album . the album was released in december 4 , 2015. track listings . original track list for `` a head full of dreams '' . 1 . `` a head full of dreams '' 1 . `` birds '' 1 . `` hymn for the weekend '' 1 . `` everglow '' 1 . `` adventure of a lifetime '' 1 . `` fun '' 1 . `` kaleidoscope '' 1 . `` army of one '' - `` x marks the spot '' ( hidden track ) 1 . `` amazing day '' 1 . `` colour spectrum '' 1 . `` up & up '' `` japanese version bonus track '' . 1 . `` miracles ''
