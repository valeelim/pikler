Piotr Domaradzki     piotr krystian domaradzki ( june 21 , 1946 – november 4 , 2015 ) was a polish-american journalist , essayist and historian . he had a longtime association with chicago 's polish community . he worked for 30 years at `` dziennik związkowy '' ( `` alliance daily '' ) , the oldest and largest polish language newspaper in the united states . from october 2009 to march 2013 , he served as the paper 's editor-in-chief . he emigrated from poland in 1984 and became a u.s. citizen in 1996. domaradzki died in chicago , illinois from complications following injuries sustained in a house fire , aged 69 .
