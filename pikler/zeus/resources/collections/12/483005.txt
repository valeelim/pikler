Alejandro González Iñárritu     alejandro gonzález iñárritu ( born august 15 , 1963 ) is a mexican movie director , screenwriter , movie producer , and composer . his five feature movies – `` amores perros '' ( 2000 ) , `` 21 grams '' ( 2003 ) , `` babel '' ( 2006 ) , `` biutiful '' ( 2010 ) , `` birdman or ( the unexpected virtue of ignorance ) '' ( 2014 ) , and `` the revenant '' ( 2015 ) . other websites . - alejandro gonzález iñarritu at biosstars international - `` moving pictures magazine '' interview - `` guardian '' interview , discussing `` amores perros '' - `` indiewire '' interview , discussing `` amores perros '' - `` indiewire '' interview , interview discussing `` 21 grams '' - `` sounds and colours '' interview , discussing `` biutiful ''
