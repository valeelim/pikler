Chevrolet Blazer     chevrolet blazer ( and gmc jimmy ) can refer to one of several suv models from general motors : - chevrolet k5 blazer , based on the c/k pickup chassis and built from 1969 to 1994. it was replaced by the chevrolet tahoe in 1995 . - chevrolet s-10 blazer , based on the s-10 pickup and built from 1983 to 2005 . - chevrolet trailblazer , originally 200cm and a trim package on the s-10 blazer until 2002 , when it became its own , larger model . - chevrolet tahoe , replaced the k5 blazer , also sold in argentina , brazil , ecuador and venezuela as the `` grand blazer '' from 1990 to 2000 .
