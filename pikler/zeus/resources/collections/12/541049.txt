2016 Fort McMurray wildfire     the 2016 fort mcmurray wildfire is a natural disaster that is affecting alberta , canada . the wildfire damaged or destroyed 1,600 structures in early may 2016. almost 90,000 people evacuated ( left ) the area as the wildfire spread through the community of fort mcmurray . a local state of emergency was put in effect on sunday , may 1 , 2016 at 9:57 pm . several areas were put under a mandatory evacuation . more than 90,000 people people left the area . fort mcmurray was expecting high winds in addition to the wildfire .
