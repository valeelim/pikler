Eva de Goede     eva de goede ( born 23 march 1989 in zeist , netherlands ) is a dutch field hockey midfielder . she currently plays for the netherlands women 's national field hockey team . with the netherlands , she won gold medals in the 2008 and 2012 summer olympics , as well as in various other tournaments . along with her teammate , ellen hoog , de goede has modeled with `` sports illustrated '' .
