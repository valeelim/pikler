Dirk Kempthorne     dirk kempthorne ( born october 29 , 1951 ) was the united states secretary of the interior . kempthorne was the 30th governor of idaho . he is a member of the u.s. republican party . kempthorne was born on october 29 , 1951 in san diego , california . he graduated from the university of idaho . he is married to patricia kempthorne . they have two children .
