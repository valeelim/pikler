Colin Friels     colin friels ( born 25 september 1952 ) is a scottish-born australian actor . he is best known for his role in the television series `` water rats '' which earned him a logie award . he also appeared in the movies `` malcolm '' ( 1986 ) , `` dark city '' ( 1998 ) and `` tomorrow , when the war began '' ( 2010 ) . friels was born in kilwinning , ayrshire , scotland . he moved to australia in 1963 , first living in darwin , northern territory before moving to melbourne , victoria . he is married to actress judy davis . they have two children , son jack and daughter charlotte . friels is a pancreatic cancer survivor .
