Suchitra Bhattacharya     suchitra bhattacharya ( 10 january 1950 - 12 may 2015 ) was an indian novelist . over the past two decades , suchitra had written about 24 novels and a large number of short stories in different leading bengali literary magazines . bhattacharya died on 12 may 2015 from cardiac arrest at her home in dhakuria , kolkata , aged 65 .
