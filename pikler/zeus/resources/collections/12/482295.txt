G.U.Y.    g.u.y . `` g.u.y . '' is a song by american pop singer-songwriter lady gaga . it is from her third studio album , `` artpop '' . it was released on march 28 , 2014 ( gaga 's 28 birthday ) . it was the third single from the album , after `` applause '' and `` do what u want '' . the single charted at number 76 on the us `` billboard '' hot 100. it charted at number 115 on the uk singles chart .
