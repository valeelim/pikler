Octocorallia     octocorallia is a subclass of the anthozoa in the phylum cnidaria . there are over 3.000 different species . these animals have an eight-fold symmetry . most are colonial sessile polyps . octocorallia look like the stony corals . stony corals have a six-fold symmetry , and are classified in the group hexacorallia . unlike the stony corals , most octocoralllia lack a lasting skeleton . for this reason , they do not build coral reefs . an older name for this group is alcyonaria . many species form symbioses with unicellular algae . octocorallia have many enemies : for this reason , many are toxic , or they taste bad . many species of the octocorallia can reproduce both sexually and asexually : it is common to find a colony of octocorallia , where most polyps have the same sex .
