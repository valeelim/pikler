Paul Reeves     sir paul alfred reeves ( 6 december 193214 august 2011 ) was a new zealand politician and archbishop . he was the archbishop and primate of new zealand from 1980 to 1985 and the 15th governor-general of new zealand from 22 november 1985 to 20 november 1990. he was the first chancellor of auckland university of technology . in july 2011 , reeves announced that he had been diagnosed with cancer . he died of the disease in auckland , new zealand , aged 78. other websites . - biography at holy trinity cathedral website - official biographies of former governorsâ€“general of new zealand - radio nz interview , 8 may 2011 sir paul talks extensively about his life and work with interviewer chris laidlaw .
