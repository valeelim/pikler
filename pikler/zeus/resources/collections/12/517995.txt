Lynn Westmoreland     leon acton `` lynn '' westmoreland ( born april 2 , 1950 ) is an american politician . he was the u.s. representative for georgia , serving from 2007 through 2017. he is a member of the republican party . he previously represented georgia 's 8th congressional district from 2005 to 2007. other websites . - congressman lynn a. westmoreland official u.s. house site - lynn westmoreland for congress
