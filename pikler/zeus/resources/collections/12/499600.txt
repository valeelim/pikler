Jirair Sefilian     jirair sefilian ( ժիրայր սեֆիլյան ; born 10 july 1967 ) is an armenian military commander and political activist . he is one of the leaders of armenian opposition and the head of founding parliament opposition group . since april 8 , 2015 he is under arrest and recognized as a political prisoner . in 1990 sefilian arrived in armenia and participated in the karabakh war . sefilian became the commander of the `` shushi '' special military commando , playing a significant role during the battle of shusha . sefilian was awarded with the first degree order of the combat cross . other websites . - biography
