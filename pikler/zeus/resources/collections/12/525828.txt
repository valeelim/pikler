In the Line of Duty: The FBI Murders    in the line of duty : the fbi murders in the line of duty : the f.b.i . murders is a 1988 television crime movie starring david soul . it is about a real event that happened in april 1986 and has since become known as `` bloody friday '' . two former army buddies , mike platt ( played by david soul ) and bill matix ( michael gross ) , commit a series of murders and bank robberies in miami , florida . a group of f.b.i . agents led by benjamin grogan ( ronny cox ) , was assigned to the investigation . the story ends with one of the bloodiest clashes in fbi history , the 1986 fbi miami shootout . it took place in the middle of a residential suburb in broad daylight . the movie was released on november 27 , 1988 .
