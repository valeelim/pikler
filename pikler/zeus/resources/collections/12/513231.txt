List of Russian Monarchs     this is a list of all reigning monarchs in the history of russia . it begins with the prince of novgorod rurik in the year 862. it ends with the emperor of russia nicholas ii who abdicated the throne in 1917 , and was shot dead with his family in 1918. since 1331 the title of the grand princes of vladimir was assigned to the princes of moscow . `` dates are listed in the old style , which continued to be used in russia .
