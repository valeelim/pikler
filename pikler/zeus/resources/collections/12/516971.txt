Rose Livingston     rose livingston ( circa 1885 - after 1937 ) was a suffragist . she was known as the `` angel of chinatown '' . she worked with prostitutes and victims of sexual slavery . in 1912 while trying to rescue a prostitute she was severely beaten . in 1914 she participated in one of the suffrage hikes from manhattan to albany , new york . in 1929 , she was given a gold medal by the national institute of social science . in 1934 she was found living in poverty . a retirement fund was established for her . in 1937 she was awarded a silver cup by edith claire bryce ( 1880-1960 ) of the peace house for her `` deeds of courage without violence '' .
