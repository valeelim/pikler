Jeff Flake     jeff flake ( born december 31 , 1962 ) is the junior united states senator from arizona . he is a member of the u.s. republican party and has been in senate since january 3 , 2013. he was in the united states house of representatives from 2001 to 2013. flake was born on december 31 , 1962 in snowflake , arizona . he graduated from the brigham young university . he is married to cheryl . they have five children . other websites . - jeff flake , u.s. senator for arizona , home page
