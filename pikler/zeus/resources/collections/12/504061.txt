The Thrill Is Gone     `` the thrill is gone '' is a 1969 slow minor-key blues song written by west coast blues musician roy hawkins and rick darnell in 1951. hawkins ' recording of the song reached number six in the billboard r & b chart in 1951. in 1970 , `` the thrill is gone '' became a major hit for b.b . king . afterwards , many blues and other artists have recorded their versions of the song .
