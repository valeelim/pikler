Alexandru Lăpușan     alexandru lăpușan ( 1 february 1955 – 17 may 2016 ) was a romanian politician . he served as minister of agriculture from 1992 through 1996. he was member of the chamber of deputies from 1990 through 1992. he was born in țăndărei , romania . lăpușan died on 17 may 2016 , aged 61. other websites . - alexandru lăpușan on chamber of deputies official site .
