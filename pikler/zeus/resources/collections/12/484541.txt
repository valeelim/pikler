Anthony Minichiello     anthony minichiello ( born 24 may 1980 ) is an australian former professional rugby league footballer . he played his entire career ( 2000–14 ) for the sydney roosters of the national rugby league , captaining them for his final two seasons . he played more games and scored the most tries for the club than any other player in its history . he played in six grand finals , winning two in 2002 and 2013. minichiello was born in liverpool , in south-west sydney , new south wales . he is of italian ancestry . his younger brother , mark , also plays rugby league . he has been married to shoe designer terry biviano since january 2012. in december 2013 , their daughter , azura trésor , was born . other websites . - anthony minichiello at the rugby league project
