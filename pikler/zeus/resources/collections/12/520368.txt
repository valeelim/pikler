Superstition (song)    superstition ( song ) `` superstition '' is a funk and r & b ballad from stevie wonder . the song hit # 1 on `` billboard '' hot 100 in january 1973. it also hit # 1 on the u.s. soul singles chart . the song advises not to believe in theories such as superstitions and the song references how adults blame children for the bad luck they have faced - `` thirteen month old baby broke the looking glass '' . the song became stevie 's signature song and is his most performed live . on the charts it was succeeded by timmy thomas 's `` why ca n't we live together ''
