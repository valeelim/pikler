Jasmine Tookes     jasmine tookes ( born february 1 , 1991 ) is an american model . tookes was born and raised in huntington beach , california . she did gymnastics for ten years and was active in volleyball and softball before she became a model at about the age of 15. her mother is a celebrity fashion stylist . tookes was discovered at one of her mother 's showroom appointments . tookes is of mixed ancestry . african , west indian and her maternal grandmother is caucasian . she is in a relationship with fellow calvin klein model tobias sorensen . tookes ' first commercial ad campaigns were in 2010 for ugg boots and gap . before breaking out on the international scene , she booked a spring 2011 dkny campaign and a layout in `` vogue italia '' . in 2015 , she became a victoria 's secret angel .
