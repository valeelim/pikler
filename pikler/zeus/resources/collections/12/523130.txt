Charles Thone     charles thone ( born january 4 , 1924 ) is an american politician . he was a member of the republican party . he served as the 34th governor of nebraska serving from january 4 , 1979 through january 6 , 1983. he lost re-election to bob kerrey . before being governor , thone represented nebraska 's 1st congressional district in the united states house of representatives from january 3 , 1971 through january 3 , 1979. thone was born in hartington , nebraska . he studied at the university of nebraska , lincoln . other websites . - retrieved on 2008-01-24 - national governors association - the political graveyard
