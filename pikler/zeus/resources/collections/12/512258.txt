Straight Outta Compton (movie)    straight outta compton ( movie ) straight outta compton is a 2015 american biographical movie . it was directed by f. gary gray and it was produced by ice cube , tomica woods-wright , matt alvarez , f. gary gray , scott bernstein , and dr. dre . `` straight outta compton '' was released on august 14 , 2015. the movie is about the compton hip hop group n.w.a and it follows the group through their rise and fall . the movie got positive reviews from critics . it has a 89 % rating on rotten tomatoes . movie critic richard roeper of the chicago sun-times gave the movie 3.5 stars out of 4 and called it `` one of the better musical biopics of the last 20 years . '' roeper said that the movie was `` enthralling '' and `` energized '' and he praised the cast for delivering `` strong , memorable work that transcends mere imitation .
