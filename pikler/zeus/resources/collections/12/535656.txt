Samuel H. Young     samuel hollingsworth young ( born december 26 , 1922 ) is an american politician . he was a u.s. representative from illinois . he represented illinois 's 10 congressional district as a republican . he served as a representative from january 3 , 1973 through january 3 , 1975. he was replaced by abner j. mikva . young was born in casey , illinois . he studied at the university of illinois .
