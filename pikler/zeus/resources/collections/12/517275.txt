Emilia Clarke     emilia clarke ( born may 1 , 1987 ) is an english actress . she played sarah connor in `` terminator genisys '' . she also played holly golightly in the stage play of `` breakfast at tiffany 's '' . she was in `` game of thrones '' . clarke is best known for her role as daenerys targaryen in the hbo series `` game of thrones '' . for this role she received an emmy award nomination for outstanding supporting actress in a drama series in 2013. she was nominated again for the same role in 2015. emilia clarke was born in london , england .
