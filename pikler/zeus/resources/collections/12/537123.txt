David Snellgrove     david llewellyn snellgrove ( 29 june 192025 march 2016 ) was a british tibetologist . he was known for his pioneering work on buddhism in tibet as well as his many travelogues . he was born in portsmouth , hampshire . in 1950 , after having finishing his studies at cambridge , he was invited to teach a course in elementary tibetan at the school of oriental and african studies university of london . he was professor of tibetan at soas until his retirement in 1982 .
