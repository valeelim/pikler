Mary King     mary king ( born 8 june 1961 ) is a british horse rider . she won the european championships and the badminton horse trials . she won four gold medals at the world and european championships . she was the british champion a record four times . her sixth olympic appearance was in london at the 2012 summer olympics . king and her daughter emily were the first british mother and daughter team to represent britain in their respective age groups , mary at senior and emily ( 15 years old ) at junior level . king was born in newark-on-trent . she has lived in salcome regis since she was 3 years old . at 6 years old , she was riding the vicar 's pony . she had three jobs as an adult to raise money to buy her own pony . she has two children .
