How to Eat Fried Worms (movie)    how to eat fried worms ( movie ) how to eat fried worms is an american comedy movie . it was based on the children 's book of the same name written by thomas rockwell . parents were warned the movie was `` gross '' but had a worthwhile moral . the movie was rated pg . the movie centers around an 11-year-old male friends dare . billy forrester has to eat 15 worms in 15 days for $ 50 . rotten tomatoes gave the film a mild 59 % . they said only 47 % of audiences liked the movie . gregory kirschling of entertainment weekly gave it a score of 75. he commented `` worms goes light ( but not too light ) on the gore and the goo . '' joanne kaufman of the wall street journal said `` after the first bit of fish bait is consumed , actually even before , this one-trick movie is a tough slog .
