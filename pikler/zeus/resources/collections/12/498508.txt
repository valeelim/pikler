Man of Constant Sorrow     `` man of constant sorrow '' ( also known as `` i am a man of constant sorrow '' ) is a traditional american folk song first recorded by dick burnett , a partially blind fiddler from kentucky . the song was originally recorded by burnett as `` farewell song '' printed in a richard burnett songbook , about 1913. an early version was recorded by emry arthur in 1928 ( vocalion vo 5208 ) . it became popular again when it was played in the 2000 movie `` o brother , where art thou ? `` .
