Haron Amin     haron amin ( dari : هارون امین‎ ; june 19 , 1969 – february 15 , 2015 ) was the afghan ambassador to japan and non-resident ambassador to thailand , the philippines and singapore from 2004 to 2009. amin was born in kabul , afghanistan . amin died of cancer at a u.s. hospital , aged 47 .
