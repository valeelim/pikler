József Verebes     józsef verebes ( 23 march 1941 – 13 march 2016 ) was a hungarian football manager and player . he was born in budapest . verebes played for ferencvárosi tc from 1961 through 1962. he managed the hungary national football team in 1987. on 15 october 2010 , verebes became the coach of nagytétény se , competing in the budapest championship . verebes became the advisor of his former club rába eto in 2011. verebes died in budapest from complications of a stroke at the age of 74 .
