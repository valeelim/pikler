Plumcot     the plumcot is a hybrid fruit . a plumcot is part plum and part apricot . there are many different types of plumcot . some of them are about half plum and half apricot , while others are mostly plum . one well-known type of plumcot is called the pluot , which is a trademarked name for plumcots that were developed in the 1980s by floyd zaiger . the first plumcots were bred in the late 1800s by luther burbank .
