Lisa Murkowski     lisa murkowski ( born may 22 , 1957 ) is an american politician . she is a member of the u.s. republican party . on december 20 , 2002 , she became a united states senator from alaska . in 2009 , she became the senior senator from alaska . she was in the alaska house of representatives from 1999 to 2002. murkowski was born on may 22 , 1957 in ketchikan , alaska . she studied at georgetown university and at willamette university . she is married to verne martell . they have two children .
