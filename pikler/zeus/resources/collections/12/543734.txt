Volleyball at the 2016 Summer Olympics     the volleyball tournaments at the 2016 summer olympics in rio de janeiro were played between 6 and 21 august . twenty-four volleyball teams and 48 beach volleyball teams , totalling 386 athletes , participated in the tournament . the indoor volleyball competition was held at ginásio do maracanãzinho in maracanã , and the beach volleyball tournament took place at copacabana beach . other websites . - volleyball qualification process - beach volleyball qualification process - volleyball qualification website - beach volleyball qualification website
