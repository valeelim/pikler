Stockholm Arlanda Airport     stockholm arlanda airport ( arn ) is an international airport between uppsala and stockholm . it was opened in 1962. it 's the largest hub for scandinavian airlines . it is about 40 kilometers ( 25 miles ) north of stockholm . arlanda has four terminals . terminals 2 and 5 are for international flights . terminal 3 is for regional airlines . terminal 4 is for in-country or domestic flights . < br >
