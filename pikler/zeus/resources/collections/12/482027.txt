Eslövs IK     eslövs ik is a sports club in eslöv in sweden . it was established in 1961. nowadays the club mostly runs handball activity . the women 's team won the swedish national indoor championship titles in 2002 and 2003. the women 's team plays under the name eslövs ik ( up to 2002 team skåne eik ) . the club also runs the women 's division 2 team 'eslövstjejerna ' . other websites . - official website
