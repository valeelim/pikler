Zoantharia     zoantharia is an order of anthozoa . unlike the stony corals , these species do not have a hard skeleton . most are very small . some species form colonies . zoanthids can be distinguished from other colonial anthozoans and soft corals . most of them ( but not the family zoanthidae ) include sand and other bits of material in their tissue to help build their structure . the main characteristic of the order is that their tentacles are organised in two distinct rows .
