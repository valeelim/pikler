King Kong (character)    king kong ( character ) king kong is a giant movie monster , resembling a huge gorilla . the character first appeared in the 1933 film `` king kong '' . the movie is a classic adventure-fantasy . the film was remade in 1976 and once again in 2005. the character has become one of the world 's most famous movie icons . kong has inspired countless spin-offs , comic books , team ups with other monsters and more . there was even a stage play . his role in the different movies varies , ranging from a rampaging monster to a vegetarian who only wants to be left alone . < br >
