Time in North Korea     time in north korea is given pyongyang time ( pyt ) which is 8.5 hours ahead of coordinated universal time ( utc ) . if it is 4 p.m. utc , it is 12:30 a.m. the next day in north korea . it has never observed summer time . north korea was originally 9 hours ahead of utc ( ) from 1912 until 2015 , just like it 's neighbours south korea and japan . in august 2015 , the government announced it will be switching to utc+8.5 affective at midnight on 15 august .
