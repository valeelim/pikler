Renato Corona     renato tirso antonio coronado corona ( october 15 , 1948 â€“ april 29 , 2016 ) was a filipino jurist . he was the 23rd chief justice of the supreme court of the philippines . he served as chief fro 2010 until his resignation in 2012. corona was born on october 15 , 1948 in the santa ana area of manila . he studied at ateneo de manila university , at harvard university and at the university of santo tomas . he served as an associate justice after being appointed by president gloria macapagal-arroyo on april 9 , 2002. he was appointed as chief justice on may 12 , 2010 after the retirement of chief justice reynato puno . corona was a law professor and member of the cabinet under two presidents , fidel v. ramos and gloria macapagal-arroyo , before being appointed to the court . on december 12 , 2011 , he was impeached by the house of representatives . on may 29 , 2012 , he was found guilty by the senate of violating some laws of the constitution of the philippines . he resigned as a result . corona died on april 29 , 2016 at the medical city in pasig due to complications of a heart attack , aged 67. he suffered from kidney disease .
