James Abdnor     james abdnor ( february 13 , 1923 – may 16 , 2012 ) ) was a republican u.s. senator from south dakota . he served in the united states senate from 1981 to 1987. he was in the united states house of representatives from 1973 to 1981. abdnor was born on february 13 , 1923 in kennebec , south dakota . he graduated from the university of nebraska–lincoln in 1945. abdnor died on may 16 , 2012 , at the age of 89 .
