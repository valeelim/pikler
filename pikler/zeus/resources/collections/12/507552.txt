Thé Lau     matheus j . `` thé '' lau ( 17 july 1952 – 23 june 2015 ) was a dutch musician and writer . besides his solo career , he was the lead singer of the dutch band `` the scene '' . he was born in bergen , north holland . lau was diagnosed with lung cancer in august 2013. he was told he could not be cured in april 2014. he died on 23 june 2015 in amsterdam , aged 62 .
