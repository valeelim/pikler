Frederic Morton     frederic morton was an austrian writer . originally , he was named fritz mandelbaum . he was born in vienna , on 5th of october 1924 , and died there on 20th april 2015. in 1939 , the family emigrated to london , and later to new york . in new york , morton 's father changed the family name to morton . in 1951 , morton came back to vienna , where he wrote as a correspondent for the us press .
