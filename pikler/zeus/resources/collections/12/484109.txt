Airport lounge     an airport lounge is a members-only rest area usually owned by an airline ( or operated by several airlines ) . anyone who pays the membership fee can join . airport lounges are designed mainly for business travelers . most offer private meeting rooms and other business services and conveniences . they offer comfortable seating , food , beverages ( often an open bar ) and free wi-fi . most major airlines have one or more lounges in the major airports they serve . the first airport lounge was owned by american airlines . it opened in 1939 at laguardia airport .
