Jack Ely     jack ely ( september 11 , 1943 – april 27 , 2015 ) was an american guitarist and singer . he was best known for singing the kingsmen 's version of `` louie louie '' . ely was born in portland , oregon . ely died at his oregon farm residence on april 27 , 2015 age 71 , having long suffered from an unknown illness . `` because of his religious beliefs , we ’ re not even sure what it [ the illness ] was , '' his son sean ely said .
