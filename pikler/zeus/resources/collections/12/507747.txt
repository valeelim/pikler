Premier of Manitoba     the premier of manitoba is the head of government for the province of manitoba , canada . the premier is appointed by the lieutenant governor of manitoba , who represents the queen . the premier is usually the head of the largest party in the legislative assembly of manitoba . the premier then appoints the cabinet of manitoba . the current premier of manitoba is brian pallister of the progressive conservative party . he was sworn in on may 3 , 2016. other websites . - premier of manitoba official site
