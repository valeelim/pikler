Veijo Meri     veijo väinö valvo meri ( 31 december 1928 – 21 june 2015 ) was a finnish writer . his work was mainly about world war ii and the cold war . the work is anti-war and has dark humor . he was born in viipuri , finland . he wrote novels , short stories , poetry , and essays . he was known for his works ; `` manila rope : a novel '' , `` private jokinen ’ s marriage leave '' and `` beneath the polar star : glimpses of finnish history '' . he won the nordic council 's literature prize in 1973. meri died in helsinki , finland at the age of 86 .
