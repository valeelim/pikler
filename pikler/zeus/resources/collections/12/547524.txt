Rahul Dravid     rahul dravid is a retired cricketer and former captain of the indian cricket team . he was widely renowned as one of the best cricketers in cricket history . he is the fourth highest run scorer after sachin tendulkar , ricky ponting and jacques kallis . he was born in 11 january 1973 in indore , madhya pradesh of india . he is the only player in the world till date to score a century against all ten test playing countries . rahul sharad dravid played his first debut test match vs england . he met sri lanka in his odi debut match . he played only a single international t20 match vs england . dravid played 164 test cricket matches and 344 odi matches . rahul dravid scored 13288 runs in test cricket and 10899 runs in odis.he picked up 4 wickets in odis and 1 wicket in test cricket . rahul dravid made 36 centuries and 63 fifties in test matches , 12 centuries and 83 fifties in odis . rahul dravid is one of the best wicket keepers of his time.his performances in wicket keeping are : catches\stumpings in test cricket : 210\0 , odis : 196\14 .
