Cardiovascular disease     cardiovascular disease ( cvd ) is a kind of disease that involves the heart or blood vessels . cardiovascular disease includes coronary artery diseases ( cad ) such as angina and myocardial infarction ( commonly known as a heart attack ) . other cvds are stroke , hypertensive heart disease , rheumatic heart disease , cardiomyopathy , atrial fibrillation , congenital heart disease , endocarditis , aortic aneurysms , and peripheral artery disease .
