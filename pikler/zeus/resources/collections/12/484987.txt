TNT (TV channel)    tnt ( tv channel ) turner network television ( tnt ) ( stylized as tnt drama ) is an american cable and satellite television network . it is owned by the turner broadcasting system division of time warner . the channel 's programming consists of television series and feature movies . it broadcasts mostly dramatic programming , along with some professional sports events . the sports events include nba basketball games and pga golf . as of february 2015 , tnt was available to about 95.3 million households ( 81.9 % of households with at least one television set ) in the united states .
