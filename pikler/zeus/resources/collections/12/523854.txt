Kaye Revil     jo kristine `` kaye '' celera-revil ( born april 6 , 1982 ) is a filipino politician . she is the current acting vice governor of masbate province in the philippines . prior to that , she was elected as a board member of the second district of masbate in the 2013 elections . she was the regional chairperson of provincial board members ' league of the philippines . revil is a graduate of the university of the philippines ( up ) diliman . she received her juris doctor at the ateneo de manila university school of law .
