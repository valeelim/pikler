Robert P. Casey     robert patrick `` bob '' casey , sr. ( january 9 , 1932 – may 30 , 2000 ) was an american politician from pennsylvania . a member of the democratic party , he served as the 42nd governor of pennsylvania from 1987 to 1995. he previously served as a state senator ( 1963–68 ) and auditor general of pennsylvania ( 1969–77 ) . casey died at the age of 68 from a rare disease called amyloidosis in scranton , pennsylvania .
