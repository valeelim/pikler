Lola Novaković     zorana `` lola '' novaković ( 25 april 1935 – 3 april 2016 ) was a serbian singer . she was popular during the 1960s and the 1970s . she was born in belgrade , yugoslavia . she was known for having represented fpr yugoslavia at eurovision song contest in 1962 where she finished fourth . in 1962 , she starred in `` šeki snima , pazi se '' , a full-length comedy movie . she died a few weeks before her 81st birthday on 3 april 2016 .
