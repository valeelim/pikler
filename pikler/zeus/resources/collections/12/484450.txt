Peachtree Road     peachtree road is the twenty-eighth studio album by elton john . the album was released on november 9 , 2004. it lacked any big hit singles or music videos . the los angeles times gave it three stars and a moderate review . they wrote `` forget about new melodies and themes : they must have trouble just thinking of new song titles . '' entertainment weekly gave it a b- . their review said “ peachtree 's few attempts to rock out are tame and hokey. ” overall , however the album got a score of 70. in sales it got a gold record .
