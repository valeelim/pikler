Sunvära SK     sunvära sk is a sports club in sunvära in sweden . it was established on 4 january 1943. the club runs bandy . it plays its home games on sjöaremossen 's artificial ice rink . the activity originally consisted of bandy , racewalking , skiing and track and field athletics . since the 1950s the club only runs bandy . the women 's bandy team has played three seasons in the swedish top division . other websites . - official website
