Jamala     jamala born susana dzjamaladinova born 27 august 1983 is a ukrainian singer that won the eurovision song contest 2016 with the song `` 1944 '' . on 14 may 2016 , jamala won the competition with 534 points . her song , `` 1944 '' is about the deportation of the crimean tatars in 1944. it is also about her great-grandmother , who lost her daughter while being deported to central asia .
