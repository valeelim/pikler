Suicide Squad (movie)    suicide squad ( movie ) suicide squad is an american superhero movie based on the dc comics team of the same name . the movie is written and directed by david ayer . it stars a cast featuring will smith , jared leto , margot robbie , joel kinnaman , jai courtney , cara delevingne , adewale akinnuoye-agbaje , karen fukuhara , jay hernandez , adam beach , and viola davis with ben affleck as batman and ezra miller as the flash . by february 2009 , a `` suicide squad '' movie was being made at warner bros. pictures . david ayer signed on to write and direct in september 2014 and by october the casting process had begun . principal photography began on april 13 , 2015 in toronto , ontario , canada and in chicago , illinois , and ended in august that year . the film is scheduled to be released on august 5 , 2016 in 2d , 3d , and imax 3d .
