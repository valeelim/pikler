Dominant (music)    dominant ( music ) the dominant in music is the fifth note of a major or minor scale . in a c scale , the fifth and dominant note will be g. chords built on this note are called dominant chords . in the key of c , the dominant chord will be g-b-d. keys have the same relationship as notes , so the dominant key will be the key of the fifth note . so in c , the key of g is the dominant key . in musical theory , the dominant is written with the roman numeral `` v '' .
