Magda Szubanski     magdalene mary `` magda '' szubanski ( born 12 april 1961 ) is an english-born australian actress , comedian , television presenter , radio host and author . she is best known for her as sharon strzelecki in the television comedy show `` kath & kim '' . she also portrayed esme hoggett in the movie `` babe '' ( 1995 ) and its sequel `` '' ( 1998 ) . szubanski was born in liverpool , lancashire . she is of polish , irish and scottish ancestry . she moved to melbourne , victoria with her family in 1965. she came out as lesbian in 2012 .
