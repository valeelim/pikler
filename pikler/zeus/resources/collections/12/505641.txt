Marissa Mayer     marissa mayer is the president and ceo of yahoo ! . she started as the head of the company in 2012. mayer went to college at stanford university . she studied computer science there . after college , she went to google in 1999 as the first female engineer when she was 24 years old . she worked at google until 2012. when she went to yahoo ! , the company was losing money . she helped them improve . during her first year , their stock prices increased by 75 % . references . - hanson , jarice . `` mayer , marissa .
