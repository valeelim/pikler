Álvarez    luis h. álvarez luis héctor álvarez álvarez ( october 25 , 1919 – may 18 , 2016 ) was a mexican industrialist and politician . álvarez was a member of the national action party . in 1958 he was a candidate for the presidency of mexico . he has also served as mayor of chihuahua . he served as president of the national action party from 1987 through 1993. other websites . - biography of luis h. álvarez
