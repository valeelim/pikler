Albertus Magnus College     albertus magnus college is a catholic private liberal arts college in new haven , connecticut , united states . it was ed in 1925 by the dominican sisters of st. mary of the springs ( now dominican sisters of peace ) . it is located in the prospect hill neighborhood of new haven , near the border with hamden . albertus magnus has associate , bachelor 's and master 's degree programs . its ranking in the 2015 edition of best colleges , regional universities ( north ) , is 115th . other websites . - visual tour of albertus magnus college < br >
