Hadi Norouzi     hadi norouzi ( ‎ ; 19 july 1985 – 1 october 2015 ) was an iranian footballer . he played as a striker and winger . he was best known for playing for persepolis who he captained . he also had spells with clubs pasargad tehran , fajr sepah tehran , gahar zagros and naft tehran . norouzi was born in babol , mazandaran province . norouzi died from a heart attack on 1 october 2015 in tehran . he was 30. other websites . - hadi norouzi at persianleague.com - hadi norouzi at teammelli.com
