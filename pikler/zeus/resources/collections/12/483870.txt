IC 342/Maffei Group     the ic 342/maffei group ( also known as the `` ic 342 group '' or the `` maffei 1 group '' ) is the nearest group of galaxies to the local group . the group is basically a binary group . the member galaxies are mostly concentrated around either ic 342 or maffei 1 , which are the brightest galaxies in the group . the group , and the local group , are in the virgo supercluster ( i.e . the local supercluster ) .
