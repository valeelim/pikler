Alpine, Texas    alpine , texas alpine is a city in the state of texas in the united states . it is the county seat of brewster county . about 6,054 people lived there in 2013. alpine was first named osborn for the part of the railroad that it was near . from 1883 to 1888 , alpine was named murphyville , for the owners of the land where the spring was that the railroad used to get water for its steam locomotives . sul ross state university opened in alpine in summer of 1920. big bend national park was started near alpine on june 12 , 1944. both of these things forced people to move to alpine .
