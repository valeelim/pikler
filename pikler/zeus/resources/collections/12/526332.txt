Trumbo (movie)    trumbo ( movie ) trumbo is a 2015 american biographical-drama movie directed by jay roach . it was written by michael london . the movie is about when screenwriter dalton trumbo is blacklisted as a communist for his support of the communist party of the usa during the 1950s . it stars bryan cranston as trumbo with diane lane , helen mirren , louis c.k. , elle fanning , michael stuhlbarg , and john goodman . it was released to positive reviews on november 6 , 2015 .
