Stephan Beckenbauer     stephan beckenbauer ( 1 december 1968 – 1 august 2015 ) was a german footballer , born in munich . he was a son of franz beckenbauer . he played with fc bayern munich . he also played for 1. fc saarbrücken . beckenbauer was latterly a scout and coach with bayern munich . beckenbauer died in munich on 1 august 2015 after a long illness , aged 46 .
