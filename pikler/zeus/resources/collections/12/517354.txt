The Soul Sessions Vol. 2    the soul sessions vol . 2 the soul sessions vol . 2 is the sixth studio album by english singer-songwriter joss stone , released on 20 july 2012 by s-curve records and her own label , stone 'd records . like her debut album `` the soul sessions '' ( 2003 ) where the songs were covers of classic soul songs , `` the soul sessions vol . 2 '' s songs are covers of soul and r & b songs that were originally made during the 1970s . `` the soul sessions vol . 2 '' received positive reviews from music critics .
