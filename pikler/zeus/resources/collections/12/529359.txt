Ray Reardon     ray reardon ( born 8 october 1932 ) is a former welsh snooker player who dominated his sport in the seventies of the 20th century . he has won the world championship six times . the former policeman and miner started his professional snooker career in 1967 , which ended in 1992. as the world 's best player , he was replaced by steve davis . other websites . - ray reardon at world snooker
