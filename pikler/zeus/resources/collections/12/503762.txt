Vera Miles     vera miles ( born august 23 , 1929 ) is an american actress . she worked closely with alfred hitchcock . she is known for her role as lila crane in the classic masterpiece `` psycho '' , and in the sequel `` psycho ii '' . her other popular movie include `` the wrong man '' , `` the searchers '' , `` follow me boys !
