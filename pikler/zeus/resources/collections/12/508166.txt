Laura, Saskatchewan    laura , saskatchewan laura is a hamlet in the rural municipality of montrose no . 315 , saskatchewan , canada . it previously held the status of village until december 31 , 1954 and again at an unknown date but was restructured back to hamlet status on december 28 , 1978. the town used to have a bank , a school and a grain elevator . now , no businesses are left . even the post office closed . the grain elevator was burned down by its owner . except for a few people who still live there , it is a now ghost town .
