Victoria, Hong Kong    victoria , hong kong victoria city was one of the first urban settlements in hong kong after it became a british colony in 1842. it was named after queen victoria , the queen of the united kingdom . in 1857 , the british government expanded victoria city and divided it into seven districts , including sai ying pun , sheung wan , tai ping shan and central . these were located in the area that is now the central , western and wanchai districts . victoria city is technically the capital of hong kong . however , the name is rarely used today . other websites . - historical and statistical abstract of the colony of hongkong ( 1907 )
