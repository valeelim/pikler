Manitoba Liberal Party     the manitoba liberal party is a political party in manitoba , canada . the party developed in the 1880s from the provincial rights party . the liberal party won the 1915 general elections winning 40 seats out of 57 in the legislative assembly . this was the largest victory in manitoba history . however , in the 1922 general elections , the liberals lost power to the united farmers of manitoba . support for the liberal party declined in the decades after this election . from 1932 to 1961 , the party merged with the progressive party of manitoba as the liberal-progressive party . in 1961 it changed its name back to the liberal party . in the 1981 general elections , the party failed to win any seats at all for the first time in its history .
