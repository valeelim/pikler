Philip Hart     philip aloysius hart ( december 10 , 1912december 26 , 1976 ) was an american lawyer and politician . he was a democrat . he served as a united states senator from michigan from 1959 until his death in 1976. he was known as the `` conscience of the senate . '' hart died of skin cancer on december 26 , 1976 in washington , d.c. at the age of 64 .
