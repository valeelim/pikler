Geraldine McEwan     geraldine mcewan ( née mckeown ; 9 may 1932 – 30 january 2015 ) was an english actress . mcewan was nominated for the tony award for best actress in a play in 1998 for her performance in `` the chairs '' . she also won a bafta award for her performance in the television serial `` oranges are not the only fruit '' ( 1990 ) . from 2004 to 2009 she appeared as miss marple , the agatha christie sleuth , for the series `` marple '' . other websites . - official website
