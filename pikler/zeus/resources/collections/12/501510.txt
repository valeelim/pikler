Émile Bouchard    émile bouchard joseph émile alcide `` butch '' bouchard , cm , cq ( september 4 , 1919 – april 14 , 2012 ) was a canadian ice hockey player who played defence with the montreal canadiens in the national hockey league from 1941 to 1956. he is a member of the hockey hall of fame , won four stanley cups , was captain of the canadiens for eight years and was voted to the nhl all-star team four times .
