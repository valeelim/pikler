Bruno Zuppiger     bruno zuppiger ( 24 february 1952 – 19 february 2016 ) was a swiss management consultant and politician . he was a member of the conservative swiss people 's party . he served on the swiss national council representing the canton of zürich . in 1999 , he was elected to the national council . he was chairman of the national council 's finance committee and also served on the security policy committee . zuppiger was born in st. gallen , switzerland . he graduated from the university of st. gallen and the zürich teachers ' seminary in 1976. zuppiger died in zürich on 19 february 2016 from cancer . he was aged 63 .
