Cinna Lomnitz     cinna lomnitz aronsfrau ( 4 may 1925 – 7 july 2016 ) was a german-born chilean-mexican geophysicist of jewish descent . he known for his work in the fields of rock mechanics and seismology . lomnitz was born in cologne , germany . he graduated as engineer from the university of chile in 1948. he then studied with karl von terzaghi in harvard university and obtained a master 's degree in soil mechanics there . he became editor of the journal `` geofísica internacional '' in 1990. he is the father of claudio lomnitz . lomnitz died in 2016 at the age of 91 .
