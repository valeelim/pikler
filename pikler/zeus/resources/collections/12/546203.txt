Muhammad Azam Shah     muhammad azam shah is the successor of the last great mughal emperor aurangzeb after his death in 1707. he is also the elder son of aurangzeb . he was at throne from 14 march 1707 to 8 june 1707. he was born on 28 june 1657 to aurangzeb and his first wife and chief consort dilras banu begum . he was also a timurid from father 's blood and a persian safavid by mother 's blood . he was the grandson of shah nawaz khan safavi , the safavid emperor of persia and father of azam shah 's mother . after his father emperor aurangzeb 's death , he ruled the empire . then a succession war took place between the three sons of aurangzeb . azam shah and his son were killed in the battle of jajau on 8 june 1707 by his step brother bahadur shah i , who ruled the empire by killing his both brothers in war .
