Japandroids     japandroids is a canadian rock band from vancouver , british columbia . the band was formed in 2006 by brian king and david prowse . king and prowse met each other in 2000 while they were attending the university of victoria in victoria . they started going to live shows together regularly in victoria and vancouver after they found out that they had a mutual interest in music . in 2003 , prowse moved to vancouver and transferred to simon fraser university . after king graduated in 2005 , he moved to vancouver to form the band with prowse . the band was influenced by the sonics and they started writing music in 2006. the band got their name from a combination of two other band name ideas : japanese scream and pleasure droids . the band self-released two eps : `` all lies '' in 2007 and `` lullaby death jams '' in 2008. the eps were only limited to 500 copies each but were re-released as a compilation titled `` no singles '' in 2010. their first studio album titled `` post-nothing '' was released on april 28 , 2009 through unfamiliar records and re-released on august 4 , 2009 on polyvinyl record co. in march 2009 , the website pitchfork media named the song `` young hearts spark fire '' as a 'best new track ' . this helped the band gain more recognition outside of canada .
