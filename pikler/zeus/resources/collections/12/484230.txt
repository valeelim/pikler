Erol Büyükburç     erol büyükburç ( 22 march 1936 - 12 march 2015 ) was a turkish pop music composer and singer . in 1961 , he composed his best known hit `` little lucy '' . he also wrote the lyrics of this melody . he was born in adana . büyükburç was found dead in his home in istanbul , turkey . he was 78 years old . he died of a heart attack .
