Algarve Cup     the algarve cup is a yearly women 's association football tournament that has international teams from around the world . it takes place in the algarve area of portugal . the team with the most titles is the united states , with 10. norway has also won the tournament four times , germany three times , sweden three times and china twice . even though the algarve cup is a very important tournament in women 's football , there is no male version of the tournament . other websites . - all results of the algarve cup - algarve cup on womenssoccerunited.com
