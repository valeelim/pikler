Roberto Rossellini     roberto gastone zeffiro rossellini , ( 8 may 1906 – 3 june 1977 ) , was an italian movie director , producer and screenwriter . he was born in rome . he was best known for his movies `` rome , open city '' ( 1945 ) , `` paisà '' ( 1946 ) and `` germany , year zero '' ( 1948 ) . rossellini died from a heart attack in rome , italy , aged 71. other websites . - biography , filmography and more on roberto rossellini
