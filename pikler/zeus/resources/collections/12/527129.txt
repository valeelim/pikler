Point Break     point break is a 1991 american action crime thriller movie . it was directed by kathryn bigelow and it was produced by peter abrams and robert l. levy . `` point break '' was released on july 12 , 1991. the movie got mixed and positive reviews from critics . it has a 68 % rating on rotten tomatoes . movie critic roger ebert of the chicago sun-times gave the film three-and-a-half stars out of four . in his review , he wrote that `` bigelow is an interesting director for this material . she is interested in the ways her characters live dangerously for philosophical reasons . they are n't men of action , but men of thought who choose action as a way of expressing their beliefs . '' a remake was released on december 25 , 2015 and received negative reviews . cast . - patrick swayze as bodhi - keanu reeves as fbi agent johnny utah - gary busey as fbi agent angelo pappas - lori petty as tyler endicott - john c. mcginley as fbi agent ben harp - james legros as roach - john philbin as nathaniel - bojesse christopher as grommet
