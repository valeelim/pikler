Amartya Sen     amartya kumar sen ( born 3 november 1933 ) is an indian economist and philosopher of bengali origin . since 1972 has taught and worked in the united kingdom and the united states . he worked in welfare economics , social choice theory , economic and social justice , economic theories of famines , and indexes of the measure of well-being of citizens of developing countries . he was awarded the nobel prize in economics in 1998 and bharat ratna in 1999 for his work in welfare economics . he was also awarded the inaugural charleston-efg john maynard keynes prize in recognition of his work on welfare economics in february 2015 during a reception at the royal academy in the uk . other websites . - amartya sen at harvard university - the possibility of social choice 1998 lecture at nobelprize.org - profile and papers at research papers in economics/repec
