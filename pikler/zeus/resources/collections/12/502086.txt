François Michelin     françois michelin ( 15 june 1926 – 29 april 2015 ) was a french heir and business executive . he served as the ceo of michelin from 1955 to 1999. michelin was born in clermont-ferrand , auvergne . his grandfather , édouard michelin ( 1859–1940 ) , was the founder of michelin . he became an orphan at the age of ten . he was a roman catholic . he had five children , including édouard ( 1963–2006 ) . michelin died on 29 april 2015 in clermont-ferrand , aged 88 .
