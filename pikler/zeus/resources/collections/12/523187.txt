G. Wayne Clough     gerald wayne clough ( born september 24 , 1941 ) is an american educator and engineer . he was the president emeritus of the georgia institute of technology ( georgia tech ) and secretary of the smithsonian institution ( 2008-2015 ) . he retired in october 2014. a graduate of georgia tech in civil engineering , he was the first alumnus to serve as president of the institute . other websites . - g. wayne clough speeches collection from georgia tech archives - biography of g. wayne clough on the smithsonian website - g. wayne clough from the smithsonian institution archives
