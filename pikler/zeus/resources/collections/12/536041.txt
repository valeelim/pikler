Defence Intelligence     defence intelligence ( di ) is part of the united kingdom `` intelligence community '' , with the sis , gchq , and the security service . it is not a stand-alone organisation , but a part of the ministry of defence ( mod ) . the organisation employs civilian and military staff . the organisation was formerly known as the defence intelligence staff or dis , but changed its name in 2009. defence intelligence gets information from many sources . this information is used to support military operations , planning , and defence policy . it notes politico-military , scientific and technical developments which may affect the uk . di 's assessments support the work of the joint intelligence committee ( jic ) , other government departments , and international partners such as nato and the european union . it is this 'all-source ' information which distinguishes defence intelligence from other organisations such as sis and gchq . they focus on the collection of 'single-source ' human intelligence ( humint ) and signals intelligence ( sigint ) respectively .
