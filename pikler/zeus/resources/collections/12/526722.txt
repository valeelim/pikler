Pierre-Marie Rudelle     pierre-marie rudelle ( 18 june 1932 – 28 december 2015 ) was a french trompe-l'œil painter . he painted first lady of the united states jacqueline kennedy a pair of doors in her dressing room in the white house . he also worked for mohammad reza pahlavi and pamela harriman . he was born in paris . rudelle died on 28 december 2015 in paris , aged 83 .
