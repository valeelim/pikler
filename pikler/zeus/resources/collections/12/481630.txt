Wayne Allwine     wayne anthony allwine ( february 7 , 1947 - may 18 , 2009 ) was an american voice actor , sound effects editor and foley artist for the walt disney company . he was best known for his role as the voice of mickey mouse from 1977 to 2009. he was married to voice actress russi taylor , who was the voice of minnie mouse . he died on may 18 , 2009 of complications of diabetes . he received an emmy award for sound editing in 1986. filmography . park attractions . - `` mickey 's philharmagic '' - mickey mouse
