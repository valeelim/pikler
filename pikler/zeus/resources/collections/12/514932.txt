Hamadryas baboon     the hamadryas baboon ( `` papio hamadryas '' ) is a species of baboon from the old world monkey family . it is the northernmost of all the baboons . it lives in the horn of africa and the southwestern tip of the arabian peninsula . these regions have fewer natural predators than central or southern africa , where other baboons live . the hamadryas baboon was a sacred animal in ancient egypt .
