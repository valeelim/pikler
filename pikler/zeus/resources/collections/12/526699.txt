Prunella Scales     prunella scales ( born prunella margaret rumney illingworth ; 22 june 1932 ) is an english actress . she is best known for her role as basil fawlty 's wife sybil in the bbc comedy `` fawlty towers '' and her bafta award-nominated role as queen elizabeth ii in `` a question of attribution '' ( `` screen one '' , bbc 1991 ) by alan bennett . in march 2014 , her husband timothy west revealed to the `` the guardian '' that scales was living with alzheimer 's disease .
