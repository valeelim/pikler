It's Too Late (Carole King song)    it 's too late ( carole king song ) `` it 's too late '' is a soft rock ballad by carole king . the song is about a woman leaving a relationship . it is on king 's studio album `` tapestry '' . this song was released as a single in april 1971. it hit # 1 on the united states `` billboard '' hot 100. sales were later certified gold by the riaa . the song was later covered by gloria estefan and the isley brothers . the isley brothers version was released in 1973. estefan released her version in 1995 .
