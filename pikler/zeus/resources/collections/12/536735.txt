Sher e Bangla Medical College     sher-e-bangla medical college ( sbmc ) , is a government medical school in bangladesh , established in 1968. it is located in barisal . construction began on the college on 6 november 1964 , at that time it was known as barisal medical college . there are three hostels for male students and two for the female students . both male and female intern doctors have each one hostel for them .
