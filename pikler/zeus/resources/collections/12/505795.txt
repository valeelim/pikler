Miguel-Ángel Cárdenas    miguel-ángel cárdenas miguel-ángel cárdenas ( october 3 , 1934 - june 2 , 2015 ) , also known as michel cardena , was a colombian-dutch , new realism and pop art painter . he was known for his video art in the netherlands . his works covered many artistic media , including painting , drawing , video , photography , object assemblages and digital art . he was born in espinal , tolima , colombia . other websites . - miguel-ángel cárdenas official website - collection of video works by miguel-ángel cárdenas
