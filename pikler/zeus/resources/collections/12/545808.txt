Hotel for Dogs     hotel for dogs ( 1971 ) is a children 's novel by lois duncan . it was made into a movie of the same name by nickelodeon movies for dreamworks pictures . when the book was originally released in 1971 , andi 's name was liz , and friday 's name was sadie . the book was re-released december 1 , 2008 , to promote the movie with the names changed . two sequels were released : `` news for dogs '' in 2009 and `` movie for dogs '' in 2010 .
