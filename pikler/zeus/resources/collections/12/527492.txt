Kitty Kallen     kitty kallen ( born katherine kalinsky ; may 25 , 1921 – january 7 , 2016 ) was an american popular singer whose career spanned from the 1930s to the 1960s—to include the swing era of the big band years . she was best known for her 1954 solo recording ' '' little things mean a lot '' . kallen died on january 7 , 2016 at her home in cuernavaca , mexico at the age of 94. other websites . - profile , discogs.com - profile , artistdirect.com
