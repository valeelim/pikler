Lieutenant Governor of Manitoba     the lieutenant governor of manitoba is the representative of the canadian monarch , queen elizabeth ii in the government of manitoba . the manitoba lieutenant governor is appointed in the same way as the other canadian lieutenant governors . most of the lieutenant governor 's roles are ceremonial , such as giving provincial awards and decorations . the office of lieutenant governor of manitoba was created in 1870 , when manitoba became a province . the first lieutenant governor was adams george archibald , from 1870 to 1872 .
