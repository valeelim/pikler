Gertrude Lawrence     gertrude lawrence ( 4 july 1898 – 6 september 1952 ) was an english actress , singer , dancer and musical theater performer . she appeared on stage in broadway , new york city and west end , london . lawrence was in the children 's play `` where the rainbow ends '' . she also acted in `` the king and i '' . she won a tony award for that performance . in 1928–29 she starred in the stage play `` treasure girl '' . her best-known american movie role was in `` the glass menagerie '' . she played amanda wingfield , who had a southern american accent . on 16 august 1952 , lawrence fainted backstage . the doctors said she was suffering from hepatitis . lawrence was born in london , england . she died from abdominal and liver cancer in new york city at age 54 .
