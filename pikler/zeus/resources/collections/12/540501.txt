Simi     simisola bolatito ogunleye ( born ) , also known as simi , is a nigerian singer and songwriter best known for her hit singles `` tiff '' and `` jamb question '' . she is signed to x3m music since 2014 simi was born in surulere , lagos state . she graduated from covenant university where she majored in mass communication . in 2006 , simi released her first project titled `` ogaju '' , a gospel music studio album that was entirely produced by samklef . in 2014 , simi released an ep tited `` restless '' , a 5-track cover of popular international songs which earned her a record deal with x3m music in april 2014 .
