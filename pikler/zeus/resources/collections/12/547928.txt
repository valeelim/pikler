Alexander Chizhevsky     alexander chizhevsky ( also aleksandr leonidovich tchijevsky ) ( 7 february 1897 – 20 december 1964 ) was a soviet-era interdisciplinary scientist , a biophysicist . he also was noted for his work in `` cosmo-biology '' and hematology '' . he may be most notable for his use of historical research techniques to link the 11-year solar cycle , earth ’ s climate and the mass activity of peoples . related pages . - victor skumin
