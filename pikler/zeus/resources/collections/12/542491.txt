Alan Zweibel     alan zweibel ( born may 20 , 1950 ) is an american producer and writer . he was one of the original writers on `` saturday night live '' . he and garry shandling were the co-creators of showtime 's `` it 's garry shandling 's show '' . zweibel was born to a jewish family in brooklyn and grew up in suburbs of wantagh and woodmere on long island . zweibel graduated from george w. hewlett high school and the university at buffalo , the state university of new york . in 2006 , zweibel won the thurber prize for american humor for his book `` the other shulman '' . other websites . - official website - alan zweibel on twitter - alan zweibel on facebook
