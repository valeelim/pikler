Cristiano Araújo     cristiano melo araújo ( january 24 , 1986 – june 24 , 2015 ) was a brazilian singer and songwriter . araújo was famous for the singles `` efeitos '' ( 2011 ) , `` você mudou '' ( 2012 ) , `` maus bocados '' ( 2013 ) , `` cê que sabe '' ( 2014 ) and `` é com ela que eu estou '' ( 2014 ) araújo and his girlfriend , allana moares , 19 , died early on 24 june 2015 in a car accident on the br-153 at km 614 , between morrinhos and pontalina in goiás .
