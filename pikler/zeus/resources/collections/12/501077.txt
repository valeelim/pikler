Macbeth (character)    macbeth ( character ) macbeth is a main character and antagonist in the play `` macbeth '' by william shakespeare . he was the thane of cawdor . he would eventually kill the then-king of scotland duncan so that he can become king . his wife , lady macbeth makes him kill the king . he then eventually kills his friend banquo and macduff 's family . he is eventually killed by macduff .
