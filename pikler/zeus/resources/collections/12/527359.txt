Frances E. Allen     frances elizabeth `` fran '' allen ( born august 4 , 1932 , peru , new york ) is an american computer scientist and pioneer in the field of optimizing compilers . her achievements include seminal work in compilers , code optimization , and parallelization . she also had a role in intelligence work on programming languages and security codes for the national security agency . allen was the first female ibm fellow and in 2006 became the first woman to win the turing award . other websites . - frances allen via ibm women in witi hall of fame . - frances allen : 2000 fellow awards recipient via computer history museum - fran allen on compilers and parallel computing systems notes from her 2008 organick memorial lecture
