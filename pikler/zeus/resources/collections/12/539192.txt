Branch MRT Line     the branch mrt line was the third line of the mass rapid transit ( mrt ) network in singapore . the 6.5-km line and three of the four stations on it opened in march 1990. trains ran as a branch line of the east west line . the trip from one end of this line to the other took about ten minutes . the line used six-car kawasaki heavy industries c151 and siemens c651 trains as its rolling stock . in february 1996 , the north south line was extended to woodlands . the branch mrt line was merged into the north south mrt line .
