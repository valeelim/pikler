LGBT rights in the United Kingdom     lesbian , gay , bisexual and transgender ( lgbt ) rights in the united kingdom have improved a lot in recent years . in 2005 civil partnerships for same sex couples was legalised across the uk . in 2014 same sex marriages was legalised in england , scotland and wales . gay people can not get married in northern ireland because the northern ireland assembly voted against legalising it . homosexuality was illegal in the uk . in 1967 it was legalised in england and wales . in 1981 it was legalised in scotland and in 1982 it was legalised in northern ireland .
