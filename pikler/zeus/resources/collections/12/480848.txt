St. John's Park    st. john 's park st. john 's park used to be a park in lower manhattan , new york city . it was square-shaped . to the west , north , east , and south were varick , laight , hudson , and beach streets . the park is now a traffic circle at the manhattan end of the holland tunnel to new jersey . it used to be a farm belonging to a new netherland farmer . then , the country of england gave the park to trinity church . the church built st. john 's chapel , as well as some townhouses , around the park . by 1827 , people living in the area were very rich . in the 1850s , many rich people moved out . in 1866 , cornelius vanderbilt bought the park for a railroad terminal . the terminal was destroyed in 1927. the port authority of new york and new jersey 's holland tunnel was built in 1927 .
