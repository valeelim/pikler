Wajid Khan     wajid khan ( b. march 10 , 1981 ) is an indian painter , nail artist . he is the first ever artist to acquire the patent for nail art . wajid was named in the guinness book of world records , golden book of world records , limca book of records , asia book of records and india book of records . his nail portraits include mary ( mother of jesus ) , jesus , dhirubhai ambani and mahatma gandhi . other websites . - wajid khan 's website
