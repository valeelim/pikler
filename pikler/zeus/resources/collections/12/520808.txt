Matt Bevin     matthew griswold `` matt '' bevin ( born january 9 , 1967 ) is an american businessman . he is the governor of kentucky , serving since december 8 , 2015. he has served as the president of bevin brothers manufacturing company since 2011. bevin was a republican candidate and primary challenger to then senate minority leader mitch mcconnell in kentucky 's 2014 senate election . on november 3 , 2015 , bevin was elected governor of kentucky in the state 's election for governor . other websites . - waycross partners business website - matt bevin for governor
