David Morgenthaler     david turner morgenthaler ( august 5 , 1919 – june 17 , 2016 ) was an american businessman and capitalist . he founded the venture capital firm morgenthaler ventures . he was also important in helping change the u.s. capital gains tax rate from 49 % to 28 % in 1978 and amending erisa legislation to allow pension funds to invest in venture capital in 1979. he was born in chester , south carolina . morgenthaler died on june 17 , 2016 in cleveland , ohio , aged 96. other websites . - david morgenthaler biography - david morgenthaler , march 23 , 2006 - smart leaders : david morgenthaler - “ the smart vc : he ’ s seen it all ” , “ red herring ” `` the smart vc : he 's seen it all '' , `` red herring , '' - national academies ' board on science , technology and economic policy
