Flame fougasse     a flame fougasse ( fougasse or foo gas ) is a type of mine or improvised explosive device . it uses an explosive charge to put burning liquid onto a target . the flame fougasse was developed in britain as an anti-tank weapon in the world war ii invasion crisis of 1940. during that period , about 50,000 flame fougasse barrels were put in some 7,000 batteries . most were in southern england and some in scotland . although never used in combat in britain , the design saw action later in greece .
