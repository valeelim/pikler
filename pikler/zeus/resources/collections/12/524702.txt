Bad Rats: The Rats' Revenge    bad rats : the rats ' revenge bad rats : the rats ' revenge is a 2009 physics puzzle video game for windows pcs . it was made by invent4 entertainment . it was published by strategy first . the mission of the game is to sole physics-based puzzles to take revenge on cats . the game is known for its popularity , and terrible reputation , among steam users the goal of each stage is to use rats to activate an object to kill a cat . the player must place objects within a time limit . the player 's score is based on the objects used and time taken . the game did not receive professional reviews on metecritic or gamerankings . according to kotaku , it is a `` nearly universally reviled '' game . it attracted a community of followers still active in 2015 , six years after its release . other websites . - http : //www.invent4.com/rats/
