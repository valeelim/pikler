Fab Morvan     fabrice `` fab '' morvan ( born 14 may 1966 ) is a french singer-songwriter , dancer and model . he was half of pop duo milli vanilli , with rob pilatus . the duo sold multi-platinum albums around the world . but in the early 1990s , he and pilatus were involved in one of the largest scandals in the history of pop music . it was revealed that neither he nor pilatus really sang on any of milli vanilli 's music whatsoever . after the scandal , the duo got themselves back together as rob & fab , though with limited success . morvan had a solo comeback in the 2000s , with his album `` love revolution '' . morvan was born in paris , france .
