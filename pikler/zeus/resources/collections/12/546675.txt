Howards End     howards end is a novel by e. m. forster , first published in 1910. it is about social conventions , personal relationships and conflicting values in edwardian england . some people think `` howards end '' was forster 's best piece of work . in 1998 , the modern library ranked `` howards end '' 38th on its list of the 100 best english-language novels of the 20th century . it was adapted into a film in 1992 starring anthony hopkins , vanessa redgrave , emma thompson , and helena bonham carter . it was directed by james ivory . it was nominated for 9 academy awards and won 3 academy awards .
