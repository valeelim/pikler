Halmstads BTK     halmstads btk is a table tennis club in halmstad , sweden . it was established on 27 october 1937. the club won the swedish national men 's team championship in 1993 and 2003 and the swedish women 's national team championship in 1991 , 1992 , 1995 and 1996. jörgen persson and åsa svensson have played for the club . other websites . - official website
