Pakistan Super League     pakistan super league ( psl ) is a professional twenty20 cricket tournament in pakistan . organised by the pakistan cricket board , the first season of psl took place between february 4 and 24 in 2016. the tournament was held in the united arab emirates . the tournament has five franchise teams : islamabad united , karachi kings , lahore qalandars , peshawar zalmi and quetta gladiators . each playing side has a mixture of seven pakistani players and four foreign players from around the world . other websites . - official website - pakistan cricket board
