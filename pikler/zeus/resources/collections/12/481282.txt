VietJet Air     vietjet air is a vietnamese budget airline . the headquarters are in hanoi . the center of activity is from tan son nhat international airport in ho chi minh city . the company was founded in 2007. vietjet air began operation in 2011. it has flights to most of airports in vietnam and some international airports . from its first flight to 31 december 2014 , the airline has served 10 million passenger . in february 2014 , it signed a purchase order with airbus to buy 63 aircraft . it has plans to purchase 30 more and to lease seven more .
