Linezolid     linezolid is an antibiotic . it can heal serious infections caused by gram-positive bacteria that other antibiotics are do not heal . linezolid is active against most gram-positive bacteria which cause disease , like streptococci and mrsa . it is mainly used to treat infections of the skin and pneumonia . it is relatively safe to take linezolid for short periods . it can be used in people of all ages and in people with liver disease or poor kidney function . common harmful effects of short-term use include headache , diarrhea , and nausea . long-term use , however , has been associated with more serious harmful effects . linezolid was discovered in the 1990s by a team at pharmacia & upjohn company . it was first approved for use in 2000. it is on the world health organization 's list of essential medicines , which is a list of medications needed in a basic health system . linezolid costs approximately us $ 100 per tablet in the united states . linezolid is marketed by pfizer under the trade names zyvox ( in the united states , united kingdom , australia , and several other countries ) , zyvoxid ( in europe ) , and zyvoxam ( in canada and mexico ) . generics are also available , such as linospan/lizomed ( in india ) , nezocin ( in pakistan ) , and linzolid ( in bangladesh , by incepta ) .
