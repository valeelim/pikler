Magic Kingdom for Sale–SOLD!    magic kingdom for sale–sold ! magic kingdom for sale–sold ! is the first book in a series written by terry brooks . it is the story of a trial lawyer named ben holiday who gets a catalog from rosen 's , ltd. that is selling a fantasy kingdom named landover . he buys the kingdom but learns that it is more challenging than he thought it would be . ben holiday is a lawyer from chicago who works in a law office with his partner miles bennett . he is depressed after the death of his wife , annie . he becomes king of landover , and is crowned after joining the people together and killing the iron mark . he learns that he can become the paladin when he is in danger .
