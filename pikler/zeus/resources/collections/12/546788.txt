Mama     mama ( stylized as mama ) is the first mini-album of south korean band exo , produced and released by s.m . entertainment on april 9 , 2012. it has two editions : by exo-k korean version and the chinese version by exo-m. the album made its debut at position number one on gaon chart . the album was produced by lee soo-man , who is also the main producer of the group . the title of the album is a term that is commonly used in korea in one person in the family , similar to `` your highness '' or `` your majesty '' . the main single was composed by yoo young-jin , who is also the co-author of other singles of the mini album , `` what is love '' and `` history '' other websites . - about `` mama '' ( korean ver . ) - about `` mama '' ( chinese ver . )
