Jim O'Toole     james jerome `` jim '' o'toole ( january 10 , 1937 – december 26 , 2015 ) was an american professional baseball pitcher . he played in major league baseball ( mlb ) for the cincinnati reds ( 1958–1966 ) and chicago white sox ( 1967 ) . he was left-handed . o'toole was born in chicago , illinois . he married betty wall on july 2 , 1960. they had 11 children . o'toole died from cancer on december 26 , 2015 in cincinnati , ohio . he was 78 .
