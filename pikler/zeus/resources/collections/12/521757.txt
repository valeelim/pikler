Savings account     saving accounts are a type of deposit account kept by banks that pay interest . they let a person keep some of their money in the bank for immediate use . they also earn money through interest . having a savings account in a bank allows a person to have money available for an emergency . savings accounts may be opened at most banks , credit unions and trust companies . the other major types of deposit account are the transactional account ( usually known as a `` checking '' or `` current '' account ) , money market account and time deposit .
