Jean-Marie Girault     jean-marie girault ( 9 february 1926 – 1 may 2016 ) was a french politician . he served as mayor of caen from 1970 through 2001. he was a member of the union for french democracy . girault was born in pont-l'évêque , france . girault died in caen , france on 1 may 2016 , aged 90 .
