Pete Domenici     pete domenici ( born may 7 , 1932 ) was a republican u.s. senator from new mexico . he served in the united states senate from 1973 to 2009. he was mayor of albuquerque from 1967 to 1970. domenici was born on may 7 , 1932 in albuquerque , new mexico . he studied at university of new mexico and at university of denver . he is married to nancy burk domenici . they have nine children .
