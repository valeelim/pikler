Arrondissement of Confolens     the arrondissement of confolens is an arrondissement of france , in the charente department , nouvelle-aquitaine region . its capital is the city of confolens . when the charente department was created on 1800 , the `` arrondissement '' of confolens was part of that original department . the `` arrondissement '' of confolens is the largest `` arrondissement '' of the department with an area of . it has 64,252 inhabitants and a density of inhabitants/km² . the `` arrondissement '' of confolens is in northern charente and is bordered to the northwest by the deux-sèvres department , to the north by the vienne department , to the east and southeast by the haute-vienne department , to the south by the `` arrondissement '' of angoulême , to the southwest by the `` arrondissement '' of cognac , to the west by the charente-maritime department . it has a small border with the dordogne department to the southeast . after the reorganisation of the cantons in france , cantons are not subdivisions of the `` arrondissements '' so they could have `` communes '' that belong to different `` arrondissements '' .
