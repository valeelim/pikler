2015–16 Liga I     the 2016–17 liga i was the 98th season of liga i , which is the top division football league in romania . the season began on 10 july 2015 and ended in may 2016. it was won by astra giurgiu . teams start the championship round with their points from the regular season halved , rounded upwards , and no other records carried over from the regular season . teams start the relegation round with their points from the regular season halved , rounded upwards , and no other records carried over from the regular season . uta arad , from the liga ii , qualifies to the second round . voluntari wins second round and stays in the liga i. other websites . - official website
