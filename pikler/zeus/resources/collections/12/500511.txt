Ontario Highway 7     ontario highway 7 is a provincial highway in ontario , canada . it runs from west to east and is divided into two parts . the western part starts at highway 4 north of london and ends near georgetown . this part is 154.1 kilometers ( 95.8 miles ) long . the eastern part starts at the donald cousens parkway in markham and ends at highway 417 in ottawa . this part is 381.6 kilometers ( 237.1 miles ) long .
