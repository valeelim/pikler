Andreas Lubitz     andreas günter lubitz ( 18 december 1987 – 24 march 2015 ) was a german pilot for the international airline lufthansa . marseille prosecutor brice robin , french and german authorities , as well as executives of lufthansa , have issued statements that they believe that lubitz crashed the airbus a320 of germanwings flight 9525 ( gwi18g & d-aipx ) into the french alps , killing himself along with 149 others in a mass murder-suicide on 24 march 2015 .
