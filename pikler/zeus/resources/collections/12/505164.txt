Manitoba Highway 2     manitoba highway 2 is a provincial highway in manitoba , canada . it is long . it runs from east to west . it starts at the border with saskatchewan , where it is called saskatchewan highway 13. it ends southwest of winnipeg where it meets highway 100. it does n't pass through any major cities . it passes through several rural municipalities , including oakland-wawanesa , glenboro-south cypress , and norfolk treherne . the highway is the manitoba part of the red coat trail .
