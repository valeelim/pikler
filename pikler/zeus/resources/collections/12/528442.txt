P. J. Mara     patrick james `` p . j . '' mara ( 1942 – 15 january 2016 ) was an irish public affairs consultant and former senator . he was best known as a political adviser to former taoiseach charles haughey . he served briefly in the seanad on two separate occasions , in 1981 and 1982. he was nominated by the taoiseach charles haughey . mara served as press secretary to fianna fáil between 1983 and 1987. in 1987 he was appointed government press secretary , and he held this position until february 1992 , when he left the public service to move into the private sector . he later returned to serve as fianna fáil director of elections for the 1997 , 2002 , and 2007 general elections . he was regularly lampooned on the satirical rté radio sketch show `` scrap saturday '' , which ran from 1989 to 1991. mara died on 15 january 2016 at a hospital after a long-illness in dublin , ireland at the age of 73 .
