Lo Bosworth     lauren ogilvie `` lo '' bosworth ( born september 29 , 1986 ) is a american television personality and writer . she was born and raised in laguna beach , california . there she attended laguna beach high school . in 2004 , bosworth became famous when she appeared on the reality series `` laguna beach : the real orange county '' . she appeared later in the series spin-off `` the hills '' .
