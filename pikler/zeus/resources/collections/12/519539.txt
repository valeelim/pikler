Lateral sulcus     the lateral sulcus ( also called sylvian fissure or lateral fissure ) is one of the most prominent structures of the human brain . it divides the frontal lobe and parietal lobe above from the temporal lobe below . it is in both hemispheres of the brain . a sulcus is a depression or groove in the cerebral cortex . the lateral sulcus first appears around the fourteenth week of gestation .
