Alfredo &quot;Chocolate&quot; Armenteros    alfredo & quot ; chocolate & quot ; armenteros alfredo `` chocolate '' armenteros ( 4 april 1928 – 6 january 2016 ) was a cuban trumpeter . he has played with artists such as arsenio rodríguez , generoso jiménez , chico o'farrill , orchestra harlow , eddie palmieri and cachao . he was known for making afro-cuban jazz , latin jazz and salsa more popular in the united states where he lived . he was sometimes called `` cuban louis armstrong '' . armenteros was born in santa clara , las villas . he was married eight times , and had nine children , one son and eight daughters . armenteros died from prostate cancer on 6 january 2016 in new york city . he was 87 .
