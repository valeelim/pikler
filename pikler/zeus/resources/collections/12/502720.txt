Dries van Agt     andreas antonius maria `` dries '' van agt ( ; born 2 february 1931 ) is a retired dutch politician of the christian democratic appeal ( cda ) . he served as prime minister of the netherlands from 19 december 1977 , until 4 november 1982. other websites . - mr. a.a.m .
