Flight of the Navigator     flight of the navigator is a science fiction movie from 1986. it was directed by randal kleiser and written by mark h. baker and michael burton . it is about david freeman , a 12-year-old boy who is taken by an alien spaceship and finds himself caught in a world that has changed around him . the producers of the movie at first sent it to walt disney pictures in 1984. however , the studio was not able to approve it . it was sent to producers sales organization . that studio made a deal with disney to distribute the movie in the united states . the movie was partially filmed in fort lauderdale , florida and norway . it was a co-production with the norwegian company `` viking film '' . cast . - joey cramer as david freeman - paul reubens ( credited as `` paul mall '' ) as the voice of max - cliff deyoung as bill freeman - veronica cartwright as helen freeman - matt adler as jeff freeman - sarah jessica parker as carolyn mcadams - howard hesseman as dr. louis faraday - robert small as troy
