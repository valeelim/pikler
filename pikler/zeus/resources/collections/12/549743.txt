RPK machine gun     the rpk is a soviet machine gun that was designed by mikhail kalashnikov . the rpk is a replacement of the older rpd . the rpk production began in 1961 and ended in 1978. the rpk-74 began in 1974 replacing the older rpk . the rpk was based on the ak-47 . the rpk was used by the nva and viet congs in the vietnam war .
