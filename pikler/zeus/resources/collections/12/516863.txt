James D. Watkins     james david watkins ( march 7 , 1927july 26 , 2012 ) was an american politician . he was a united states navy admiral and former chief of naval operations . he served as the u.s. secretary of energy during the george h. w. bush administration , also chairing u.s. government commissions on hiv/aids and ocean policy . watkins also served on the boards of various companies and other nongovernmental organizations and as the co-chair of the joint ocean commission initiative . watkins died of congestive heart failure on july 26 , 2012 in alexandria , virginia at the age of 85 .
