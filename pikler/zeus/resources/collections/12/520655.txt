Black Mark Productions     black mark productions ( founded in 1991 ) is an independent record label company from sweden . it has offices in berlin , toronto , stockholm , and new york . the label is known for working with extreme metal bands , including bathory and edge of sanity . the name of the label came from bathory 's album , `` under the sign of the black mark '' . other websites . - official site - official bathory website : the history archive - recording the debut album - by quorthon - tartarean desire : quorthon r.i.p . - by fjordi
