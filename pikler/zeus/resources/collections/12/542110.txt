Thank You (Meghan Trainor album)    thank you ( meghan trainor album ) thank you is the second major-label studio album by american singer-songwriter meghan trainor . the album was released exclusively on apple music on may 6 , 2016 , and was fully released on may 13 , 2016 , through epic records . track listing . - notes - `` woman up '' is a cover of the song of the same name by ashley roberts , with different lyrics .
