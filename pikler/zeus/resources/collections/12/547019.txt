Cecilia Bowes-Lyon, Countess of Strathmore and Kinghorne    cecilia bowes-lyon , countess of strathmore and kinghorne cecilia nina bowes-lyon , countess of strathmore and kinghorne , née cavendish-bentinck , gcvo ( 11 september 1862 – 23 june 1938 ) was the mother of queen elizabeth the queen mother and maternal grandmother and godmother of elizabeth ii .
