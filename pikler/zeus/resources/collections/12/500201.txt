Solidarity (Polish trade union)    solidarity ( polish trade union ) solidarity ( , pronounced ; full name : independent self-governing trade union `` solidarity '' – `` niezależny samorządny związek zawodowy `` solidarność '' '' ) is a polish trade union that began on 17 september 1980 at the gdańsk shipyard under the leadership of lech wałęsa . it was the first trade union in a warsaw pact country that was not controlled by the communist party . it had 10 million members which was one in three of the total working-age population of poland .
