Tamarillo     tamarillo is a shrub or tree in the flowering species solanaceae . it bears an egg-shaped edible fruit , also called a tamarillo . the fruit is about 4-10 centimeters long . the taste is similar to that of a tomato , but with less sweet and more of a salty flavour . the tamarillo is native to the andes of ecuador , colombia , peru , chile , and bolivia .
