Firooz Bahram High School     firooz bahram high school ( ) is one of tehran 's oldest high schools . the architecture of the building belongs to qajar era of iran . it is a historical place of tehran . this school was built in 1932 , on zoroastrian land . the school was named after an iranian zoroastrian named firooz . he died in the mediterranean sea in world war i. the school 's establishment was supported by keikhosrow shahrokh , the zoroastrian representative in iranian parliament .
