Kenne Fant     kenne fant ( born carl-henrik fant ; 1 january 1923 – 30 may 2016 ) was a swedish actor , director and writer . he was born in strängnäs . he was known for his roles as göran thome `` the most beautiful on the earth '' ( 1948 ) and as arne in `` prison '' ( 1949 ) . he directed the movie `` adventures of nils holgersson '' ( 1962 ) . fant died on 30 may 2016 in stockholm , sweden at the age of 93 .
