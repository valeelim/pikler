Behaviour therapy     behaviour therapy is used for a number of methods of psychotherapy . ivan pavlov found that it is possible to `` learn '' how to react to a stimulus . his method is known as classical conditioning today . behaviour therapy tries to use this knowledge to change the behaviour of people . in that way , people `` learn '' how to cope with their fears , for example . in a similar manner , people can be helped to cope with a number of mental disorders . one well known kind of behaviour therapy is exposure therapy : people with fears are `` exposed '' to them , and learn how to cope . as an example , someone who is afraid of spiders will be exposed to spiders .
