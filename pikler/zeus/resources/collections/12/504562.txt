Radomir Naumov     radomir naumov ( ; 20 december 1946 – 22 may 2015 ) was a serbian politician and engineer . he was the minister of mining and energy from 3 march 2004 until 15 may 2007. he was also minister of religion from 15 may 2007 until 7 july 2008. naumov was born in čoka . he graduated from the university of belgrade . he was married to marija . naumov died after an illness on 22 may 2015 in belgrade . he was 68 .
