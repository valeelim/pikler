Born to Be Wild     `` born to be wild '' is the third single off canadian-american rock group steppenwolf 's self-titled first studio album . the song was released in june 1968. on the `` billboard '' charts , the song was able to peak at # 2 on the hot 100. in 2004 , the song was ranked # 130 on the 500 greatest songs of all time list by `` rolling stone '' . it has also been ranked at # 53 on the 100 hard rock songs list by vh1 . the song was first featured in the 1969 movie `` easy rider '' and has since been featured in many movies , trailers , tv shows and commercials . it was ranked # 29 on afi 's 100 years ... 100 songs survey of the top songs in american cinema .
