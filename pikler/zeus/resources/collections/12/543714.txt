Claire Shulman     claire k. shulman ( born february 23 , 1926 in brooklyn , new york ) is an american politician . she was the borough president of queens , new york city from 1986 until 2002. she is the first woman to hold this position in queens history . other websites . - `` city hall news '' ( 03/12/2007 ) - `` new york times '' 1996 article
