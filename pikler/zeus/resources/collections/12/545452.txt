Farhiyo Farah Ibrahim     farhiyo farah ibrahim ( arabic language : فرحيو فرح إبراهيم ) is a somali refugee and women ’ s rights activist . she fights against female genital mutilation and forced marriage in the dadaab refugee camp in north-east kenya . in 2008 , she received the international women of courage award . in 2002 farah went to work for the national council of churches in kenya , teaching people about reproductive health . after she was attacked , she took a job as an interpreter in dadaab . in 2005 farah went to a leadership program in the town of naivasha in western kenya . then she worked for care , giving people information about reproductive health . care is an international ngo that helps refugees .
