Southern Sotho     southern sotho ( `` sesotho '' in southern sotho ) is an african language mainly spoken by people living in lesotho and the free state province of south africa . southern sotho is one of the eleven official languages of south africa . it is spoken by almost 6,024,000 people , or 7 % of south africans at home ( 2011 census ) . southern sotho is part of the sotho language family .
