Narayani Zone     narayani ( is one of the fourteen zones of nepal , located in the central south of the country . it is named after the narayani river which is situated on the western border of the zone . narayani occupies parts of the terai , inner terai , and hill regions of nepal , but it does n't cover any of the mountain or himalayan region . narayani is rich in flora and fauna . narayani river and rapti river are the main rivers of narayani zone . bisharari and garuda laked are some of the few lakes in narayani . narayani is divided into five districts : narayani is the third largest industrial area of the country . some important cities and towns are birganj , parwanipur , jitpur , alau , hetauda , bharatpur , narayanghat , kalaiya , and gaur . related pages . - development regions of nepal - zones of nepal - districts of nepal
