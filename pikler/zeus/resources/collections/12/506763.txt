Rick Santorum presidential campaign, 2016    rick santorum presidential campaign , 2016 the 2016 presidential campaign of rick santorum , former united states senator from pennsylvania , was formally announced at a rally in pittsburgh on may 27 , 2015. his campaign for the republican nomination for president of the united states in 2016 is his second bid for the office , after having been a candidate in 2012 , where he received the second most delegates after 2012 nominee mitt romney .
