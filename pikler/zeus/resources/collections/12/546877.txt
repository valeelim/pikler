Ragnar Sigurðsson     ragnar sigurðsson ( born 19 june 1986 in reykjavík , iceland ) is an icelandic professional footballer who plays as centre back . he currently plays for the russian premier league club krasnodar and the iceland national football team . he has previously played for fylkir , ifk göteborg and f.c . copenhagen . he was included in iceland 's uefa euro 2016 squad . honours . - ifk göteborg - allsvenskan : 2007 - svenska cupen : 2008 - svenska supercupen : 2008 - copenhagen - danish superliga : 2012–13 - danish cup : 2011–12 other websites . - profile at fc copenhagen
