Alsike     alsike is a locality in knivsta municipality in uppsala county in sweden . in 2010 , 2,681 people lived there . alsike is located circa 50 kilometers north of stockholm and 25 kilometers away from the arlanda airport . alsike is located on the ingegerdsleden , which is a historic pilgrimage route between the uppsala cathedral and the stockholm cathedral . the alsike abbey is also located here . alsike clover gets its common name from the settlement .
