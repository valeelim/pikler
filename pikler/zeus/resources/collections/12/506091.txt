David Rotem     david rotem ( ‎ ; 11 january 1949 – 8 june 2015 ) was an israeli politician . he served as a member of the knesset for yisrael beiteinu between 2007 and 2015. he was born in bnei brak to a orthodox jewish family , and grew up in jerusalem . rotem died from cardiac arrest on 8 june 2015 in efrat , west bank , aged 66 .
