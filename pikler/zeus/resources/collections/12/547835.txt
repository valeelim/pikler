Jusztin Nándor Takács     jusztin nándor takács ( 15 january 1927 – 11 july 2016 ) was a hungarian roman catholic prelate . he was born in rábacsanak , hungary . he served as the bishop of székesfehérvár from 1991 until his retirement in 2003. takács died on 11 july 2016 from complications of pneumonia in székesfehérvár , hungary , aged 89. other websites . - bishop jusztin nándor takács , o.c.d .
