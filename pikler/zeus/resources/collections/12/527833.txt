Philippe Petit     philippe petit ( ; born 13 august 1949 ) is a french high-wire artist . he became famous for his high-wire walk between the world trade center in new york city , on the morning of august 7 , 1974. the 2015 movie `` the walk '' is based on his high-wire walk in new york . other websites . - msa - the man who walked between the towers . co-produced by michael sporn animation and weston woods studios - philippe petit signature visible in the 1980s - photos of philippe petit crossing the twin towers along with others - philippe petit : the journey across the high wire , ted2012 , filmed mar 2012 , posted may 2012 .
