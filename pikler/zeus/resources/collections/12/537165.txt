Ureteral cancer     ureteral cancer is cancer of the ureters , muscular tubes that propel urine from the kidneys to the urinary bladder . it is also known as ureter cancer , renal pelvic cancer , and rarely ureteric cancer or uretal cancer . this kind of cancer is very rare . ureteral cancer is usually transitional cell carcinoma . transitional cell carcinoma is `` a common cause of ureter cancer and other urinary ( renal pelvic ) tract cancers .
