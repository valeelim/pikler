Karpathos     karpathos is a greek island in the aegean sea . it is one of the greek dodecanese islands , in the southeastern aegean . together with the neighboring smaller saria island it forms the municipality of karpathos . this in turn is part of the karpathos regional unit . because of its remote location , karpathos has preserved many peculiarities of clothing , customs and dialect . the dialect is very similar to that of crete and cyprus . the island has also been called carpathus in latin , scarpanto in italian and kerpe in turkish .
