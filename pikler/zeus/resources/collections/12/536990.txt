Selkirk, Manitoba    selkirk , manitoba selkirk is a city in manitoba . it is west of winnipeg . it was named after the 5th earl of selkirk . it was incorporated as a town in 1882 and as a city in 1998. at the 2016 census , selkirk 's population was 10,278. the city of selkirk has 2 taxi services : wills taxi and first rate taxi . it also has selkirk transit and selkirk mobility . selkirk has many businesses . these include mcdonald 's , dairy queen , and a & w . it also has sterling press and international pipe . andrew murray lives in selkirk . selkirk is home to the mjhl selkirk steelers and the kjhl selkirk fishermen .
