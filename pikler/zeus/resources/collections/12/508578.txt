Love Me like You Do     `` love me like you do '' is a song recorded by british recording artist ellie goulding . it was recorded for the movie `` fifty shades of grey '' . it reached number three on the us `` billboard '' hot 100 and on the canadian hot 100. it was # 1 on the uk singles chart and the charts of several other countries . the song is an electropop song . it is the fifth song on the movie 's soundtrack .
