Vivienne Tam     vivienne tam is the first chinese-born fashion designer . her goal was to make clothing that balanced both chinese and american cultures . this was important because previously most of the successful fashion designers were either american or french . her company earns over $ 40,000,000 annually . her courage and success inspire other people of color to follow their dreams . she continues to explore fashion . sources . - `` vivienne tam . `` encyclopedia of world biography . vol . 24. detroit : gale , 2005. biography in context . web . 29 may 2015 . - `` vivienne tam . `` contemporary fashion . gale , 2002. biography in context . web . 29 may 2015 .
