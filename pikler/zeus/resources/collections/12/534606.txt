Julia Holter     julia holter ( born december 18 , 1984 ) is an american singer , songwriter , record producer , composer and artist . her first studio album , `` tragedy '' , was released in 2011. she released a second album , `` ekstasis '' , in march 2012. this album was compared to works by such artists as laurie anderson , kate bush , joanna newsom and several others . `` ekstasis '' received many positive reviews . holter joined domino recording company . she then released her 3rd album `` loud city song '' . the album was a critical success . she released `` have you in my wilderness '' in 2015. the album was very successful .
