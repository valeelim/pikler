Pushyamitra Sunga     pushyamitra sunga is the founder of the sunga dynasty in 180bce by defeating the mauryan army . he was the general of mauryan emperor brihadratha maurya . later defeated and killed his master in a war . he proclaimed himself king . his dynasty replaced the mauryan empire . he performed ashvamedha yaga and brought most of the north india under him . he ruled until his death in 149 bce .
