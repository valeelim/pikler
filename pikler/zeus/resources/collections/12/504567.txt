Ontario Liberal Party     the ontario liberal party is a provincial political party in ontario , canada . the party has an ideology similar to the liberal party of canada but the two parties are organizationally independent . the party is descended from the reform movement in the 1830s and 1840s . the party was organized in 1847 by george brown . it won the provincial elections of 1871 and remained in power until the general election of 1905. the ontario liberal party has won every general election since 2003 .
