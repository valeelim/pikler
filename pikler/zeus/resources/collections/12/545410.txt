Secunderabad     secunderabad , popularly called the twin city of hyderabad , is a big city in the state of telangana , india . secunderabad was founded in the year 1806 as a british cantonment . secunderabad and hyderabad together form the sixth biggest metropolis in india . it is one of the biggest cantonments in india . it has a large presence of army and air force . the population of secunderabad is 204,182. the literacy rate of secunderabad is 73 % . the most popular sport is cricket .
