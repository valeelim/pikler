Noble orchid     the noble orchid ( `` cymbidium goeringii '' ) is a cold hardy evergreen orchid . it is a plant that blooms in the spring . its petals are green , yellow-green or brown . it has pseudobulbs found below ground . the leaves are long and grass-like with serrated s. it is found growing as far north as okushiri island ( 42 degrees north latitude ) . it can stand freezing temperatures over the winter months . it has been grown in china for over 2500 years , dating back before the time of confucius . in japan it has been cultivated for several hundred years . in europe the earliest records date it to 1780 .
