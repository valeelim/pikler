Marcin Wrona     marcin wrona ( 25 march 1973 – 19 september 2015 ) was a polish director and screenwriter . his movies `` the christening '' and `` demon '' were both shown at the toronto international film festival ( tiff ) in 2010 and 2015 , respectively . wrona was born in tarnów . his wife , olga szymanska , served as a producer for `` demon '' . wrona was found dead hanging on 19 september 2015 at a hotel room in gdynia . he was 42. his death was ruled a suicide .
