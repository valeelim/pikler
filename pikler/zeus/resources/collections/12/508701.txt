Saud bin Faisal     saud bin faisal bin abdulaziz al saud ( ) , also known as saud al faisal ( ‎ ; 2 january 1940 – 9 july 2015 ) , was a saudi diplomat and statesman . he served as saudi arabia 's foreign minister from 1975 to 2015. by the time of his retirement , he was the world 's longest-serving foreign minister . he was a member of the saudi royal family . saud died on 9 july 2015 from complications from parkinson 's disease after going through surgery in los angeles , california at the age of 75 .
