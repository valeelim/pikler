Spencer Abraham     spencer abraham ( born june 12 , 1952 ) is an american politician . he represented michigan in the united states senate from 1995 until 2001. he was the 10th united states secretary of energy . he is a republican . abraham was born on june 12 , 1952 in east lansing , michigan . he graduated from michigan state university with a bachelor of arts degree in 1974. abraham received his law degree from harvard law school in 1979. he is married to jane abraham . other websites . - biography ngr - spencer abraham chairman & ceo
