Lucjan Avgustini     lucjan avgustini ( august 28 , 1963 – may 22 , 2016 ) was an albanian prelate of the roman catholic church . he was the bishop of the roman catholic diocese of sapë . avgustini was born in ferizaj , sfr yugoslavia . avgustini was a priest of the roman catholic diocese of prizren from 1989 to 2006. in 2006 , he was made the bishop of the roman catholic diocese of sapë in albania . avgustini died on may 22 , 2016 from a brain tumor in vau i dejës , albania , aged 52 .
