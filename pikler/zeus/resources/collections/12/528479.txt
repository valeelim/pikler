Lycra     lycra is a type of synthetic fabric that is very elastic . it is also called spandex or elastane . the name `` spandex '' is an anagram of the word `` expands '' . lycra was invented in 1958 in waynesboro , virginia , united states . it was invented by joseph shivers , a chemist working for dupont . it is made from a combination of polyester and polyurethane . it was introduced in 1962. because of its elasticity and strength , lycra is often used to make clothing . it is commonly mixed with cotton to make things like tights , bras and underwear . lycra is especially common in sports clothing , such as wrestling singlets . it is also often used to make swimsuits and wetsuits , because lycra dries more quickly than many other fabrics . related pages . - nylon
