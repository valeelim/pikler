Blönduós     blönduós is a town and municipality in northwest iceland . it has a population of 881 people . it is located next to route 1 at the end of the blanda river . on a hill above the town is a church with striking architecture that is supposed to look like a volcanic crater . blönduós does not have a good harbour . it is mainly used as dairy service centre for the nearby farm areas . many people in the city work in agriculture , industry and fishing . related pages . - list of settlements in iceland other websites . - official website
