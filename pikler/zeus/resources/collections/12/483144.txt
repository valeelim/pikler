Jaden Smith     jaden christopher syre smith ( born july 8 , 1998 ) is an american actor and rapper . he is the son of actors will smith and jada pinkett smith . he is the older brother of singer willow smith and younger half-brother of trey smith . his first movie role was in the 2006 movie `` the pursuit of happyness '' . he has also appeared in `` the day the earth stood still ( 2008 ) '' and `` the karate kid ( 2010 ) '' .
