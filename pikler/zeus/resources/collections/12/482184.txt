Linköpings AIK     linköpings aik is a sports club in the town of linköping in sweden . it was established on 22 october 1908. the club runs orienteering , earlier even bandy and soccer . the men 's bandy team played in the swedish top division in 1931. the men 's bandy team reached three semifinals when the swedish national championship was played as a knockout-tournament back in the 1920 's . the men 's soccer team also played in the swedish third division during the seasons of 1928-1929 and 1929-1930. other websites . - official website
