Bertrand Blier     bertrand blier ( born 14 march 1939 ) is a french screenwriter and movie director . his 1978 movie `` get out your handkerchiefs '' won the academy award for best foreign language film at the 51st academy awards . he is the son of famous french actor bernard blier . his 1996 movie `` mon homme '' was entered into the 46th berlin international film festival . his 2005 film `` how much do you love me ? '' was entered into the 28th moscow international film festival where he won the silver george for best director .
