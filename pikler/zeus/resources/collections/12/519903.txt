Eyedentify     eyedentify is a playstation 3 video game where the player is the controller of a team of game characters using voice recognition via the playstation eye . the game was first announced at e3 2005 in a trailer . a release date has n't been announced and no further news on development has been released . matthew rorie of gamespot was guarded about the title being able to achieve the impressive interaction shown in the promotional video , and was unsure if it should be classed as a game or a technology demo .
