Hylte VBK     hylte vbk is a volleyball club in hyltebruk , sweden . it was established in 1980. the club won the swedish men 's national championship in the years of 1995 , 1996 2000 , 2001 , 2005 , 2006 and 2013. the women 's team won the swedish national championship in 2014. other websites . - official website
