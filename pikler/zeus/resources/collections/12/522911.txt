Cavit Şadi Pehlivanoğlu    cavit şadi pehlivanoğlu cavit şadi pehlivanoğlu ( 1927 – 21 november 2015 ) was a turkish statesman . he served as a legislator of the grand national assembly of turkey for three terms between 1961 through 1996. he was the first as a member of justice party ( turkey ) and then as the vice president of motherland party ( turkey ) , that he founded together with president turgut özal and others . pehlivanoğlu died on november 21 , 2015 in ordu , turkey from complications of pneumonia , aged 88 .
