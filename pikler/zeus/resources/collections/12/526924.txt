Storyboard     a storyboard is a way of planning animated films , movies , or television shows . a storyboard shows examples of what the artist wants to make before they are animated . it also lets artists organize their stories before they start to make the animation . storyboarding , in the form it is known today , was developed at walt disney productions . it was first used in the 1928 animated short film , `` plane crazy '' .
