David Vitter     david vitter ( born may 3 , 1961 ) is an american politician . he was the united states senator from louisiana from 2005 to 2017. he is a member of the u.s. republican party and has been in senate since january 3 , 2005. he was in the united states house of representatives from 1999 to 2005. vitter was born on november 17 , 1961 in new orleans , louisiana . he studied at harvard university , at magdalen college , oxford , and at tulane university . he is married to wendy baldwin . they have four children .
