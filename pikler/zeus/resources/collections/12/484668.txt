13 Ghosts     13 ghosts is a 1960 horror movie . this movie was directed by william castle . it was written by robb white . it was released in 10 july , 1960. cast . - charles herbert as arthur `` buck '' zorba - jo morrow as medea zorba - rosemary decamp as hilda zorba - martin milner as benjamin rush - donald woods as cyrus zorba - margaret hamilton as elaine zacharides - john van dreelen as van allen - william castle as himself - david hoffman as messenger - roy jenson as dr. plato zorba 's ghost
