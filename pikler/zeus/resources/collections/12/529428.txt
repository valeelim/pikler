John Parrott     john parrott ( born 11 may 1964 in liverpool ) is an english former professional snooker player . he was twice in the finals of world snooker championship . in 1989 , he lost to steve davis , in 1991 he defeated jimmy white . parrott started his professional career in 1989 , in the season 2010/11 he lost his place in the world rankings . now he is snooker commentator . other websites . - john parrott homepage
