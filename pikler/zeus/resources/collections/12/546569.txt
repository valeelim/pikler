Himeros     himeros ( ancient greek : ἱμερος `` uncontrollable desire '' ) is the god of sexual desire or unrequited love . he is one of the erotes . himeros is shown to carry a bow and arrow . he created desire and passion in mortals . he is often depicted as a young man or child . his parents are ares and aphrodite and he 's brothers with eros , his twin brother . his other siblings include pothos , anteros , hermaphroditus , hedylogos , phobos , deimos and harmonia . sources . - `` himeros .
