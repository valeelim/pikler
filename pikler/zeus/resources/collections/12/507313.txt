Maqil     the maqil ( ) were an arabian nomadic tribe . in the 11th century they migrated from yemen into what is now the sahara desert and morocco . they mainly settled near forests and oases . despite their originally small number ( less than 200 ) , they were later involved in fighting a dynasty of sunni muslims . they also brought change to the culture of these regions , and a new language . they descend from son of , father of the hebrews and coming from the line of seth , son of adam . the exact origin of the maqil tribe is unknown . they claimed for themselves a prestigious banu hashim descent from jaafar ibn abi talib , son of abu talib and brother of ali ibn abi talib . arabian genealogists categorized them as hilalians . ibn khaldun wrote that both of these versions are false , since the banu hashim lived in urban cities and were n't nomadic nor ever wandered in desert wolds . he added that the maqil is a name only found in yemen . ibn khaldun speculated that they were likely an arab nomadic group from yemen , and this is supported by bn al-kalbi and ibn said .
