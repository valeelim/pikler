Paris Square (Jerusalem)    paris square ( jerusalem ) paris square ( , `` kikar pariz '' , also called : france square = `` kikar tzarfat ) '' is a square or plaza in jerusalem . the name `` france square '' was established in 1959. in 2007 the city of paris gave jerusalem a `` french fountain '' that was built in the middle of the square . from that time the square has been called : `` paris square '' .
