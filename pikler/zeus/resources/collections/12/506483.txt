Ozona, Texas    ozona , texas ozona is a census-designated place in the state of texas . it is the county seat of crockett county . it is the only settlement in the county that is recognized by the census bureau . about 3,225 people lived there in 2010. first named powell well for e.m. powell , a surveyor who made a well and a windmill in 1891. its name was changed to ozona because it has much open air or ozone .
