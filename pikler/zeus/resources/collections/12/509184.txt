Wayne Allard     wayne allard ( born december 2 , 1943 ) was a republican u.s. senator from colorado . he served in the united states senate from 1997 to 2009. he was in the united states house of representatives from 1991 to 1997. allard was born on december 2 , 1943 in fort collins , colorado . he graduated from the colorado state university . he is married to joan malcolm . they have two children .
