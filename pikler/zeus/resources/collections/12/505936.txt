The Shuffles     the shuffles were a dutch pop band formed in rosmalen , north brabant . the group , fronted by the late albert west , were active for ten years from 1963 until 1973. discography . singles . - 1969 – `` cha-la-la , i need you '' - 1970 – `` believe now in tomorrow '' - 1970 – `` bitter tears '' - 1970 – `` teardrop on teardrop '' - 1970 – `` the way the music goes '' - 1970 – `` without you '' - 1971 – `` glory , glory '' - 1971 – `` i give you my love ''
