Fernando Cardenal     fernando cardenal martínez ( 26 january 1934 – 20 february 2016 ) was a nicaraguan jesuit and liberation theologian . he served as minister of education from 1984 to 1990 , during the sandinista era . his brother , poet ernesto cardenal , also a catholic priest , served as minister of culture from 1979 to 1987. he was born in granada , nicaragua . cardenal died in managua from heart failure on 20 february 2016. he was aged 82. other websites . - fernando cardenal as a jesuit priest - interview with fernando cardenal from georgetown university
