Ruzizi River     the ruzizi river , or rusizi river , is a river , long . it flows from lake kivu to lake tanganyika in east africa . a notorious man-eating crocodile , gustave , roams the banks of the ruzizi river and the northern shores of lake tanganyika . gustave is a nile crocodile and is about long and weighs about . he is said to have killed and eaten many people . though the actual number is difficult to verify , he is a near-mythical creature and is greatly feared by people in the region .
