Lucien Bouchard     lucien bouchard , ( ; born december 22 , 1938 ) is a canada lawyer , diplomat , politician and former minister of the environment of the canadian federal government . he was the founder of the bloc québécois , leader of the opposition in the canadian house of commons from 1993 to 1996. he served as the 27th premier of quebec from january 29 , 1996 , to march 8 , 2001. he became a central figure for the `` yes '' side in the 1995 quebec referendum . he is a recipient of the title of `` commander '' of the légion d'honneur . bouchard was born in saint-cœur-de-marie , québec . he was the son of alice ( née simard ) and philippe bouchard . he studied at université laval . bouchard married solange dugas in may 2013 .
