Northern Sotho     northern sotho ( `` sesotho sa leboa '' in northern sotho ) is an african language mainly spoken by people living in the limpopo province of south africa . northern sotho is one of the eleven official languages of south africa . it is spoken by almost 4 618 500 people , or 8.4 % of south africans at home ( 2011-census ) . northern sotho is part of the sotho language family .
