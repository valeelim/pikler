Renata Notni     renata notni ( born january 2 , 1995 in cuernavaca , morelos , mexico ) is a mexican actress and model . notni was born in cuernavaca , morelos , mexico . as a child , she attended acting lessons at centro de educación artística , the children 's version of drama school . centro de educación artística is run by televisa in mexico city . notni had a relationship with diego amozurrutia . the relationship ended but they remain friends . she currently resides in mexico city . < br >
