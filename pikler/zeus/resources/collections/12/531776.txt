André van den Heuvel     andré van den heuvel ( 4 june 1927 – 9 february 2016 ) was a dutch actor . he appeared in more than 55 movies and television shows between 1957 and 2008. he was born in tegelen , netherlands . he was known for his roles in `` lifespan '' ( 1976 ) , `` rubens '' ( 1977 ) , `` dag dokter ! '' ( 1978 ) and in `` de rode zwaan '' ( 1999 ) . van den heuvel died in amsterdam on 9 february 2016 from complications from a stroke . he was aged 88 .
