Drakensberg     drakensberg ( derived from afrikaans drakensberge , literally : dragon mountains ) is the highest mountain range in southern africa . the highest peak is in lesotho , where they are called maloti . the drakensberg are also known for their many waterfalls , and cave paintings done by the san people.ukhahlamba-drakensberg park , which was created in 2000 , is an unesco world heritage site which covers part of the range .
