Gorgoderidae     the gorgoderidae are a family of flatworms ( platyhelminthes ) . they belong to the ordo plagiorchiida . the species are parasites of fish , amphibians and reptiles . they have distome , which means they got two suckers , one at the mouth ( oral ) and one at the waist ( ventral ) . there are five genera : - `` gorgorodera '' - `` gorgoderina '' - `` phyllodistomum '' - `` probolitrema '' - `` staphylorchis ''
