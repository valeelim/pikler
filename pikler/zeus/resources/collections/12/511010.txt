Mafia III     mafia iii is an open world third-person action-adventure video game . it was developed by hangar 13. it was published by 2k games . it was announced on july 29 , 2015 and revealed at gamescom on august 5 , 2015. it was released on microsoft windows , playstation 4 , and xbox one on 7 october 2016. the game is set in 1968 in new orleans , louisiana in the united states . the protagonist is lincoln clay , a mixed race orphan and vietnam war veteran . vito scaletta , `` mafia ii '' protagonist , returns .
