Non-denominational Muslim     just a muslim or just muslim are a group of muslims who do n't follow any particular sect . the country with the highest proportion of such muslims is kazakhstan at 74 % . other countries where they are a majority includes albania ( 65 % ) , kyrgyzstan ( 64 % ) , kosovo ( 58 % ) , indonesia ( 56 % ) , mali ( 55 % ) , bosnia and herzegovina ( 54 % ) , uzbekistan ( 54 % ) .
