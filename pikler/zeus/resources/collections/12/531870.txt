AAAA record     in the domain name system , an aaaa record is something that changes a domain name into a 128-bit ip address ( or `` ipv6 address '' ) . a domain can have many aaaa records , for example , the domain `` example.com '' could have an aaaa record for `` www.example.com '' that points to the ip `` 2001:0db8:0000:0000:0000:0000:3257:9652 '' , and another aaaa record for `` mail.example.com '' that points to the ip `` 2001:0db8:0000:0000:0000:0000:3257:9653 '' . related pages . - a record - mx record the domain name rfc
