Benchmark     a benchmark is a standard , or set of standards , that a product can have to show a level of quality . it can also be a test that a person makes a product like a computer do to see how good it can do a task , or set of tasks . some computer benchmarks are to test the cpu or the gpu . people use benchmarks to see if their computer can run some kinds of software or games . people also use benchmarks to see if one piece of hardware is better than another .
