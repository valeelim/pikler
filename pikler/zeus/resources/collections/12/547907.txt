Tamarugal     tamarugal ( ) is one of two provinces in the northern chilean region of tarapacá ( i ) . the capital is the city of pozo almonte . tamarugal means `` a place where there are many `` tamarugos '' '' ; tamarugo ( `` prosopis tamarugo '' ) is a tree endemic to the arid lands of northern chile . the tamarugal province has an area of . tamarugal is bordered on the north by the arica and parinacota region , on the east by bolivia , on the south by the antofagasta region and on the west by the iquique province and the pacific ocean .
