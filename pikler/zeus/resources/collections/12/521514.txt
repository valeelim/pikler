Ernst Fuchs     ernst fuchs ( february 13 , 1930 – november 9 , 2015 ) was an austrian painter , draftsman , printmaker , sculptor , architect , stage designer , composer , poet , and singer . he was born in vienna . he was one of the founders of the vienna school of fantastic realism . in 1972 , he bought the otto wagner villa in hütteldorf , which he restored and transformed . the villa was inaugurated as the ernst fuchs museum in 1988. fuchs died at the age of 85 on november 9 , 2015 in vienna .
