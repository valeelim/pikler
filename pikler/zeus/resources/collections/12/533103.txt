Ramón Castro Ruz     ramón eusebio castro ruz ( october 14 , 1924 – february 23 , 2016 ) was a cuban politician . he was the older brother of fidel and raúl castro . he was a key figure of the cuban revolution . castro was born in birán , oriente province . after the revolution , castro left his family farm as it became legal property of the state . he continued to dedicate himself to the study of agriculture . castro died on february 23 , 2016 in havana . he was aged 91. other websites . - braga , michael . the other castro brother in the `` sarasota herald-tribune '' , june 15 , 2004
