ABC Dunhill Records     abc records purchased dunhill records in 1966 , forming abc dunhill records . they purchased don robey 's record labels on may 23 , 1973. these included duke records , peacock records , back beat records and song bird records . afterwards , they also purchased the famous music record labels from gulf and western in 1974. these included dot records and blue thumb records . with the famous acquisition , abc gained a distribution deal with sire records . abc distributed ( through sire ) the first releases by punk band the ramones . sire switched to warner bros. distribution in 1977 , being sold to wb a year later .
