A record     in the domain name system , an a record is something that changes a domain name into a 32-bit ip address ( or `` ipv4 address '' ) . a domain can have many a records , for example , the domain `` example.com '' could have an a record for `` www.example.com '' that points to the ip `` 127.0.0.1 '' , and another a record for `` mail.example.com '' that points to the ip `` 127.0.0.2 '' . related pages . - aaaa record - mx record the domain name rfc
