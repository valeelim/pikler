Russi Taylor     russell `` russi '' taylor ( born may 4 , 1944 ) is an american voice actress . she is best known for being the voice of disney 's minnie mouse since 1986. she also voices numerous other disney characters . taylor is the widow of the late voice actor wayne allwine . he was the third voice of mickey mouse from 1991 until his death in 2009. in 2008 she received a disney legends award . filmography . park attractions . - `` mickey 's philharmagic '' - minnie mouse
