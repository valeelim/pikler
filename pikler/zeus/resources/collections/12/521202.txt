Skeletal muscle     skeletal muscle is a form of `` striated '' ( striped ) muscle tissue . it is under the voluntary control of the somatic nervous system . most skeletal muscles are attached to bones by bundles of collagen fibers known as tendons . it is one of three muscle types , the others being cardiac muscle and smooth muscle . skeletal muscle is made up of individual muscle cells or myocytes , known as `` muscle fibres '' . muscle fibres do the work when muscles contract . a great deal is known about their structure and how they work . they contract when they get a nerve impulse .
