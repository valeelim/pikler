Foreign national     a foreign national is a person who is visiting or living in a country where they are not a citizen . the european union has a different definition of `` foreign national . '' in the european union , a person is only thought of as a foreign national if they are not a citizen of any country in the european union . these people are called `` third party nationals , '' not `` foreign nationals . '' the united states department of homeland security says that a foreign national as a person `` who is a citizen of any country other than the united states . '' in other words , a foreign national is a person who : - was born outside the united states ; and - is a citizen of another country ; and - has not applied to become a united states citizen through naturalization .
