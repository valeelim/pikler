Tiffany &amp; Co.    tiffany & amp ; co. tiffany & company ( better known as tiffany or tiffany 's ) is an american luxury goods company . their main store is in new york city . tiffany sells jewelry , sterling silver , china , crystal , perfumes , watches and personal accessories , as well as some leather goods . many of these goods are sold through direct-mail as well as at tiffany stores . tiffany is particularly known for its diamond jewelry . tiffany markets itself as an judge of taste and style . founded in new york city in 1837 , the store sold a wide variety of items as `` tiffany , young and ellis '' in manhattan . the name was shortened to tiffany & company in 1853 when charles tiffany took control . he established the firm 's emphasis on jewelry . related pages . - tiffany lamp other websites . - tiffany & company official website
