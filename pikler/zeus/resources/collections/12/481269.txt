Big Eyes     big eyes is a 2014 american biographical drama movie directed by tim burton and produced by burton and scott alexander and larry karaszewski . it is based on the careers of american artists margaret and her husband walter keane . it is set in san francisco , california during the 1950s . they are played by amy adams and christoph waltz . it also stars jason schwartzman and terence stamp . the music was composed by danny elfman . it was released to very positive reviews on december 25 , 2014 .
