Hugo Blanco (musician)    hugo blanco ( musician ) hugo blanco ( september 25 , 1940 - june 14 , 2015 ) was a popular venezuelan musician . he was best known as the author of `` moliendo café '' and other songs like `` el burrito de belén '' ( also known as `` el burrito sabanero '' ) , `` leche condensada '' , `` luces de caracas '' , `` sierra nevada '' , `` mañanita zuliana '' , and others . `` moliendo café '' , has become one of the most recognized venezuelan songs in all the world . blanco was born in caracas , venezuela .
