Karan Brar     karan brar `` ( born january 18 , 1999 ) '' is a teenage american actor . he is known from his role as `` shirag gupta '' in the movie series `` diary of a wimpy kid '' . he is also known for co-starring role as `` ravi ross '' on the disney channel original series `` jessie '' and its spinoff series `` bunk 'd '' .
