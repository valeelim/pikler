Santa Clarita, California    santa clarita , california santa clarita , officially the city of santa clarita , is the third largest city in los angeles county , california , united states . it is the eighteenth largest city in the state of california . the city has annexed unincorporated areas , which has made the population higher . santa clarita is about 35 miles ( 56 km ) northwest of downtown los angeles . it takes up most of santa clarita valley . it is a good example of a u.s. edge city or boomburb . santa clarita was ranked by `` money '' magazine in 2006 as 18th of the top 100 places to live .
