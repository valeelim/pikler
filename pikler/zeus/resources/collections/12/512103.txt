Point Grey Secondary School     point grey secondary school is a public secondary school in vancouver , british columbia , canada . it was built in 1929. it was designed in the collegiate gothic style by townley & matheson . it is on heritage vancouver society ’ s top 10 most-endangered sites list for 2015. the school is at risk of being damaged by an earthquake . the fraser institute gives the school a ranking of 52 out of 289 schools for 2013–2104 . < br >
