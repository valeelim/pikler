Orizuru     an orizuru ( 折鶴 `` ori- '' `` folded , '' `` tsuru '' `` crane '' ) , or paper crane , is a classically designed paper japanese origami . red paper cranes is a representation of the japanese red-crowned crane which is special in japanese culture . the orizuru is often is used restaurant table decoration . `` senbazuru '' ( 千羽鶴 ) , which means `` thousand cranes , '' are one thousand paper cranes strung together .
