Earth-like planet     earth-like planet is used as a synonym for one 0r more of the following : - earth analog : another world that is very similar to earth in size , make up , and distance from its star . - earth-size planet : meaning , it is not a gas giant . - terrestrial planet : a planet made of the same materials as earth , i.e. , primarily of silicate rocks and an iron core . terrestrial planets are often contrasted with gas giants .
