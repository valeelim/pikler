FC Rapid București     fotbal club rapid bucurești is a romanian association football club from bucharest , romania . it was founded in 1923 by a group of workers . rapid won the romanian championship three times ( 1967 , 1999 and 2003 ) and the romanian cup 13 times . in the 2015–16 season , they were promoted to liga i. liga i liga ii cupa eroilor ( heroes cup ) cupa româniei supercupa româniei other websites . - official website of the club
