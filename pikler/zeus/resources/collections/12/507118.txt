Eva Abu Halaweh     eva abu halaweh ( arabic : إيفا أبو حلاوة ) , born 1975 , is a jordanian lawyer and human rights activist . in 2011 she received the international women of courage award from the u.s. department of state . eva works against honor killings . she also works against torture and abuse in the jordanian prisons and police stations . eva has a bachelor 's degree in law and a master ’ s degree in diplomacy . eva had her own private law practice . she also worked for the united nations high commissioner for refugees as a legal advisor . eva is the executive director of the mizan law group for human rights . she helped start the organization . awards . - international women of courage award , 2011 other websites . - mizan law group ميزان القانون ( in arabic ) see also . - 2011 international women of courage awards , u.s. state department photostream on flickr - secretary 's international women of courage award ( u.s. department of state )
