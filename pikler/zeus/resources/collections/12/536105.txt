Niš     niš is the city of southern serbia . it is the third-largest city in serbia ( after belgrade and novi sad ) . it is the administrative center of the nišava district . according to the 2011 census , the city has population of 183,164. the city center has 187,544 people living there and the administrative area has a population of 260,237. other websites . - regional chamber of economy niš official website
