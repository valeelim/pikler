Ralph Torres (politician)    ralph torres ( politician ) ralph deleon guerrero torres ( born august 6 , 1979 ) is an american republican politician from saipan , northern mariana islands . he is serving as the 9th governor of the northern mariana islands upon the death of governor eloy inos on december 28 , 2015. he previously served as the 10th lieutenant governor . he was elected to this position in the 2014 election .
