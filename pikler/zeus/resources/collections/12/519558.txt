Krunoslav Hulak     krunoslav hulak ( 25 may 1951 – 23 october 2015 ) was a croatian chess master . he was awarded the international master title in 1974 , and the grandmaster title in 1976. hulak was born in osijek , croatia . hulak died in zagreb , croatia , aged 64. other websites . - tužna vijest za hrvatski šah : bio je omiljen ...
