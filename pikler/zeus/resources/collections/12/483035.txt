Chungking Express     chungking express is a 1994 drama movie made in hong kong . it was directed by wong kar-wai . the movie has two stories , one after the other . each story is about a policeman who is unlucky in love . the main actors in the first half were takeshi kaneshiro and brigitte lin . the second half of the movie stars tony leung as a different policeman . valerie chow plays the flight attendant . the movie features the american song `` california dreamin ' '' and other songs by faye wong . the movie received good reviews .
