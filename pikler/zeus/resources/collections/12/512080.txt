Bagmati Zone     bagmati ( `` bāgmatī añcal '' ) , is one of the fourteen zones of nepal . it is named after the bagmati river . bagmati is in the central development region of nepal . the zone contains the kathmandu valley . the capital is kathmandu . in 2011 the total population was 3,843,596. limestone , talc , zinc , lead and silver are mined and exported from bagmati . brown , white and pink marble are also exported . the hill districts around kathmandu are notorious for human trafficking in young girls . the girls are taken to india where they are sold to brothels . bagmati is divided into eight districts : other websites . - development regions of nepal - zones of nepal - districts of nepal
