2015 San Bernardino shooting     the 2015 san bernardino shooting was a mass shooting in san bernardino , california . it started at 10:59 a.m. pacific standard time ( pst ) on december 2 , 2015. at least fourteen people were killed and seventeen were wounded . it happened at the inland regional center . the shooters may have targeted a banquet hosted by san bernardino county department of public health . the shooters left in an suv . the two suspected shooters , one man and one woman , were killed by police during a gunfight .
