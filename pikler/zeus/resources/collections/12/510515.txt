Jurassic Park: The Ride (Universal Studios Hollywood)    jurassic park : the ride ( universal studios hollywood ) jurassic park : the ride is a water-based amusement ride . it is based on the steven spielberg movie `` jurassic park '' and michael crichton 's novel . it is at universal studios hollywood . the ride was researched and built while the movie was in its production phase . the ride opened on june 21 , 1996. duplicates of the ride have since been built at islands of adventure and universal studios japan . a river rapids ride version of the attraction , jurassic park rapids adventure , opened in universal studios singapore in 2010 .
