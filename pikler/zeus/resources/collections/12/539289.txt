Ilija Ivezić     ilija ivezić ( 20 july 1926 – 14 april 2016 ) was a croatian movie actor . he was born in ričice , gračac , croatia . in a career that spanned more than fifty years , ivezić worked with directors fadil hadžić , krsto papić , vatroslav mimica , antun vrdoljak , veljko bulajić , and antun vrdoljak , among many others . he was known for his roles in `` last of the renegades '' ( 1964 ) , `` die rechnung - eiskalt serviert '' ( 1966 ) , `` acting hamlet in the village of mrdusa donja '' ( 1974 ) , `` the golden years '' ( 1993 ) and in `` marshal tito 's spirit '' ( 1999 ) ivezić died on 14 april 2016 in zagreb , croatia , aged 89 .
