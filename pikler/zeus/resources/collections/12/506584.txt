Elsa &amp; Fred (2014 movie)    elsa & amp ; fred ( 2014 movie ) elsa & fred is a 2014 american comedy-drama movie directed by michael radford . it stars shirley maclaine and christopher plummer . the movie , set and filmed in new orleans . it is an english-language remake of the 2005 argentinian movie of the same name . it was released on november 7 , 2014 .
