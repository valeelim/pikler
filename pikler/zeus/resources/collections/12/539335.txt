Eunos MRT Station     eunos mrt station ( ew7 ) is an mrt station in singapore . it is along the east west mrt line . the station is located in geylang east which is sometimes called eunos . eunos mrt station opened on 4 november 1989. the station features a malay style roof . this style roof can also be found at chinese garden ( ew25 ) and lakeside ( ew26 ) . timeline . - 4 november 1989 : station opened - 2010 : platform screen doors installed previous : kembangan ( ew6 ) < br > next : paya lebar ( ew8 ) formerly the code was : e6 ( 1989 - 2001 ) relating pages . - east west mrt line
