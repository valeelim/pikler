Manuel Valdés     manuel `` el loco '' valdés ( born 29 january 1931 ) is a mexican actor and comedian . he is member of the valdés family . he is the brother of ramón valdés ( aka `` don ramón '' ) , from the sitcom `` el chavo '' , and germán valdés ( aka `` tin tan '' ) . he is also the father of singer cristian castro and marcos valdés . he is known for his roles in `` la carabina de ambrosio '' , `` la pulquería '' , `` la leyenda de la nahuala '' , `` caperucita y pulgarcito contra los monstruos '' and `` calabacitas tiernas '' . he was in three telenovelas : `` amy , la niña de la mochila azúl '' ( 2004 ) , `` entre el amor y el odio '' ( 2002 ) and `` siempre te amaré '' ( 2000 ) .
