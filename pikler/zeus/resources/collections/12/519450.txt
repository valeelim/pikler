Alfredo di Stéfano Stadium     the alfredo di stéfano stadium is the stadium of real madrid castilla . they are the reserve team of real madrid . it was built in 2006. it has a capacity of 9,000. future expansions are planning it to be able to seat 24,000. the stadium is named after club legend alfredo di stéfano . there is a statue of him at the entrance created by pedro montes . other pages . - real madrid - real madrid castilla - santiago bernabéu stadium - alfredo di stéfano
