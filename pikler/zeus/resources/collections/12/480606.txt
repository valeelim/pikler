Relitahallen     relitahallen is an indoor bandy venue the town of uppsala in sweden . it was opened on 11 september 2011 with the game ik sirius-hk jenisej krasnojarsk 5-4. on 6 and 7 september 2013 , double international games between sweden and finland were played inside . both a- and u23 international games were played . other websites . - relitahallen
