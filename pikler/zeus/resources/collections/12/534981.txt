Beechcraft King Air     the beechcraft king air is a twin-engines aircraft designed and built in usa . it built in two majors series : king air 90 and king air 100. it used as light business aircraft and light transport aircraft . beechcraft super king air is a modern variant . beechcraft king air are known in the us army as u-21 ute , for light transport duties . in us navy it known as t-44 pegasus , a trainer .
