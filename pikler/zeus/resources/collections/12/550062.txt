Sirkazhi     sirkazhi is a town in the nagapattinam district , tamil nadu , india . the town covers an area of 13.21sq.km . the population according to 2011 census is 34,927. this town is the part of cauvery delta region . sirkazhi is the motherland of the shaiva saint thirugnanasambandhar , world renowned actor sivaji ganesan and many famous people . the literacy rate of sirkazhi population is 81.5 % .87 % of the population is hindus , 9.5 % are muslim , 2.1 % are christians and 1 % are others . famous temples here are the bhrammapureeshwarar temple , also known as sattainathar temple , and kazheesirama vinnagaram temple .
