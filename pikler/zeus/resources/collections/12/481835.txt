Perro de Presa Canario     the perro de presa canario is a large mastiff-type dog breed from the canary islands . it was bred for working livestock . also to run off or kill other dogs . they are often on the list of outlawed dogs in places that ban dangerous breeds . they are a working breed that can weigh or more . if trained and socialized early they can make a good family pet . they make excellent guard dogs . the name of the breed is spanish and means `` canarian catch dog '' . the name is often shortened to `` presa canario '' or simply `` presa '' . the breed is sometimes also called dogo canario or canary dog .
