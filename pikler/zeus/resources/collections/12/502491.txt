Austin St. John     austin st. john ( born jason geiger ; september 17 , 1974 ) is a american emt and former actor and martial artist . he is best known for his role as jason lee scott , the original red ranger and first leader of the power rangers on the television series `` mighty morphin power rangers '' . st. john holds a second-degree black belt in taekwondo , a first-degree black belt in judo , and is also a practitioner of kenpo . st. john has also worked as an emergency medical technician and firefighter in washington , d.c. and on the east coast . he graduated from concordia university . filmography . television . - `` mighty morphin power rangers '' - jason lee scott/red power ranger ( 1993–1994 ) - `` power rangers zeo '' - jason lee scott/gold zeo ranger ( 1996 ) - `` exposé '' - detective anderson ( 1998 ) ( tv movie ) - `` power rangers : the lost episode '' - himself/jason lee scott/red power ranger ( 1999 ) - `` power rangers wild force '' - jason lee scott/red power ranger ( 2002 ) - `` footsteps '' - police investigator ( 2003 ) ( tv movie )
