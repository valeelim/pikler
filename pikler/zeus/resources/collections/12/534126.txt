Emergency room     an emergency room ( also called an emergency department ) is a medical treatment facility . it specializes in emergency medicine . it also specializes in the acute care of patients who come in without telling anyone they were coming . a 2005 patient study found an average emergency room wait time from 2 hours in iowa to more than 5 hours in arizona . the midwest had the highest number of emergency room visits ( 460 per 1,000 people ) . the west had the lowest number .
