George Baker (actor)    george baker ( actor ) george baker , mbe ( 1 april 19317 october 2011 ) was an english actor and writer . he was best known for playing tiberius in `` i , claudius '' , and inspector wexford in `` the ruth rendell mysteries '' . he was born in varna , bulgaria . baker died on 7 october 2011 in west lavington , wiltshire from pneumonia after suffering a stroke , aged 80 .
