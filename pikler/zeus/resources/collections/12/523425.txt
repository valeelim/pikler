Ray     robert dolph ray ( born september 26 , 1928 ) is an american politician . he served as the 38th governor of iowa serving from january 16 , 1969 to january 14 , 1983. he was a member of the republican party . ray was born in des moines , iowa . other websites . - robert d. ray collection - personal papers and official documents 1987-1999 , at cowles library , drake university
