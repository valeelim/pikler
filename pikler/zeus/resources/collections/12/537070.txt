Miguel Hernández Agosto     miguel hernández agosto ( april 5 , 1927 – march 18 , 2016 ) was a puerto rican politician . he worked with the popular democratic party ( ppd ) . he started his political career as a cabinet member , but eventually became a senator at-large . hernández agosto served as president of the senate of puerto rico for 12 years ( 1981-1993 ) . agosto died in san juan , puerto rico on march 18 , 2016 at the age of 88. other websites . - official biography from the senate of puerto rico
