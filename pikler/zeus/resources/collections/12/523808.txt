St Denys' Church, Sleaford    st denys ' church , sleaford st denys ' church , sleaford is a medieval church located in sleaford , lincolnshire , england . the oldest parts of the present building are the tower and spire . the spire stands 144 feet ( 44 metres ) tall . a window tracery is dated to about the late 13th century . the gothic nave , aisles and north transept were built in the 14th century . the church is a grade i listed building , a term given to `` buildings of exceptional interest '' .
