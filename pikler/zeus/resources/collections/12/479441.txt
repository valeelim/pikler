Wilfred Agbonavbare     wilfred agbonavbare ( 5 october 1966 – 27 january 2015 ) was a nigerian footballer who played as a goalkeeper . in his country , lagos-born agbonavbare played for new nigeria bank f.c . and bcc lions fc . in 1990 he moved to spain where he would spend the rest of his career , starting with rayo vallecano in segunda división . agbonavbare died at the age of 48 from cancer in alcalá de henares , spain .
