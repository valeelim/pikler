Graeae     the graeae , alternatively known as the grey sisters , are characters from greek myth . they are a trio of hags , called deino , enyo and pemphredo , and are so named because they were born with grey skin and hair . they also share one tooth and eye between them and they took it in turns to use them . they are affiliated with the gorgons and the hero perseus forced them to reveal the whereabouts of medusa by taking their eye and ransoming it for information .
