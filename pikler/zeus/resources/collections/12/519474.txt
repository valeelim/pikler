Marshawn Lynch     marshawn lynch ( born april 22 , 1986 ) is a former american football running back . he played in the national football league ( nfl ) for the buffalo bills and seattle seahawks . he was drafted by the buffalo bills in the first round of the 2007 nfl draft . his aggressive running style has earned him the name `` beast mode '' . marshawn grew up in oakland , california with his three siblings and his mother . his mother had to work multiple jobs in order to keep the family moving . lynch 's family had a history of being athletes , such as his mother , who had been a very good sprinter at the high school that marshawn was attending at the time ( oakland technical high school ) . lynch played many different positions during high school , including defensive back , quarterback , wide receiver , and linebacker . references . - http : //www.jockbio.com/bios/m_lynch/m_lynch_bio.html
