I'm Your Baby Tonight (song)    i 'm your baby tonight ( song ) `` i 'm your baby tonight '' was the lead single and title track from whitney houston 's album of the same name . the song became a major hit for houston . it reached the top 10 in many countries . it hit # 1 on the u.s. `` billboard '' hot 100. after her death in 2012 , the song was ranked at number 18 by `` entertainment weekly '' .
