Woodleigh MRT Station     woodleigh mrt station ( ne11 ) is an mrt station in singapore . it is along the north east mrt line . the station is built on part of the former bidadari cemetery . the station was built in 2003. it remained closed for 8 years , with the train passing through . timeline . - 20 june 2003 : station built - 20 june 2011 : station opened along with damai ( pe7 ) formerly the code was : p5 ( before opening )
