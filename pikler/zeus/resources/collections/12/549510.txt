Lincoln Red Imps F.C.    lincoln red imps f.c . the lincoln red imps football club is a semi-professional association football club from gibraltar . they currently play in the gibraltar premier division and share the victoria stadium with all other clubs in the territory . they have won 22 league titles and 17 cup titles . the football club was first formed in 1976 by charles polson and charles head . honours . - gibraltar football league : 22 - rock cup : 17 - gibraltar league senior cup : 18 other websites . - official website - lincoln red imps f.c . twitter account - uefa profile
