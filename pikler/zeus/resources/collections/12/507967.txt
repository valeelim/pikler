Tim Scott     tim scott ( born september 19 , 1965 ) is an american politician . on january 3 , 2013 , he became the junior u.s. senator from south carolina . he was in the u.s. house of representatives from 2009 to 2013. scott was born on september 19 , 1965 in north charleston , south carolina . he attended presbyterian college . scott graduated from charleston southern university . he is unmarried .
