Uri Orbach     uri shraga orbach , hebrew : אורי שרגא אורבך , ( march 28 , 1960 – february 16 , 2015 ) was an israeli religious zionist writer , journalist and politician . he served as a member of knesset for the jewish home party and as pensioner affairs minister . orbach was born in petah tikva and died of blood disease in jerusalem , aged 54. other websites . - bio at the knesset website
