Michael Burgess (singer)    michael burgess ( singer ) michael burgess ( july 22 , 1945 – september 28 , 2015 ) was an canadian actor and singer ( tenor ) . he was best known for his portrayal of jean valjean in the toronto production of `` les misérables '' . in 2013 , he was made a member of the order of ontario . burgess was born in regina , saskatchewan . he grew up in toronto , ontario . he studied at the university of ottawa . he married fellow `` les misérables '' performer susan gilmour on october 8 , 1994. burgess died from cancer on september 28 , 2015 in toronto , aged 70 .
