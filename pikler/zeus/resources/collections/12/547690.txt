FreeThe20 campaign     freethe20 is a campaign to call attention to women who are political prisoners around the world . the campaign names twenty women who are imprisoned unjustly . samantha power , the u.s. ambassador to the united nations , started the campaign for the twentieth anniversary of the beijing declaration . the reason for the campaign was to tell governments to stop putting women in prison , and to tell the women and their families that they are not alone . there were twenty names of women in twenty days , one for every year since the beijing declaration . in september 2015 , a bipartisan `` # freethe20 '' bill was introduced to the u.s senate . all twenty women senators of the two political parties supported the bill . they said , for status of the women updated to the end of the powers ' term as secretary of state , see # freethe20 women political prisoners and prisoners of concern campaign . - day 20 : rasha chorbaji , syria - day 19 : ta phong tan , vietnam - day 18 : women political prisoners , north korea
