Thom Tillis     thom tillis ( born august 30 , 1960 ) is the junior united states senator from north carolina . he is a member of the u.s. republican party and has been in senate since january 3 , 2015. he was in the north carolina house of representatives from 2007 to 2015. tillis was born on august 30 , 1960 in jacksonville , florida . he graduated from the university of maryland university college . he is married to susan tillis . they have two children . other websites . - thom tillis , u.s. senator for north carolina , home page
