10th Army (German Empire)    10th army ( german empire ) the 10th army ( ) was an army level command of the german empire in world war i. the 10th army was formed 1 january 1915. the army was stationed on the eastern front where it fought against the russian empire . it was dissolved in january 1919. commanders . - hermann von eichhorn ( 26 january 1915 - 18 december 1917 ) - erich von falkenhayn ( 5 march 1918 - 6 january 1919 )
