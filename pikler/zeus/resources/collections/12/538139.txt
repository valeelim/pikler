Kollam temple fire     on 10 april 2016 at around 3:30 am indian standard time , there was an explosion and fire at the puttingal temple in paravur , kollam , kerala , india . the incident happened when firework celebrations went out of control . more than 100 people were killed . more than 350 were injured , some with severe burns . the temple did not have permission from kerala government authorities to do a competitive fireworks display .
