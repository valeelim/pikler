Linköping Arena     the linköping arena is an association football stadium in the town of linköping in sweden . it was opened in 2013 and has a capacity of 8 500 and hosted four games during the uefa women 's euro 2013 tournament . the stadium became home to linköpings fc women 's association football team after that tournament . the name was discussed for a while and the project was named the arena linköping before the name was changed to linköping arena . other websites . - linköping arena
