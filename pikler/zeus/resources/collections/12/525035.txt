Jiří Paďour     jiří paďour ( 4 april 1943 – 11 december 2015 ) was a czech auxiliary bishop in the archdiocese of prague from 1996 to 2001 , and the bishop of české budějovice from 2002–2014 . pad'our was born in vraclav , czechoslovakia . on 1 march 2014 , pope francis accepted paďour 's resignation for health reasons . pad'our died in prachatice , czech republic at the age of 72 .
