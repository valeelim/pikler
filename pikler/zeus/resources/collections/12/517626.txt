Dominique Dropsy     dominique dropsy ( 9 december 1951 – 7 october 2015 ) was a french footballer . he played as a goalkeeper . he earned seventeen international caps for the french national team during the late 1970s , early 1980s . he was a player of rc strasbourg ( 1973–1984 ) , he was a member of the french team in the 1978 fifa world cup . he played in a total of 596 ligue 1 matches between 1972-1989 , a record at that time . dropsy died in leuze , france from leukemia , aged 63. other websites . - french football federation profile - profile
