African fish eagle     the african fish eagle ( `` haliaeetus vocifer '' ) or the african sea eagle is a large species of eagle . it is found throughout sub-saharan africa where there are large bodies of open water with plenty of fish . it is the national bird of zimbabwe , zambia and south sudan . as a result of its large range , it is known in many languages . this species looks a bit like the bald eagle . they are related , but live on different continents . the bald eagle lives in north america .
