Nature reserve     a nature reserve ( preserve or ( national/nature ) conserve ) is a protected area where wildlife , flora ( plants ) and fauna ( animals ) , are left undisturbed . they may also protect geological features or other special interests . reverves can also provide special opportunities for study or research . nature reserves may be run by government institutions in some countries . they can also be created or run by private landowners , such as charities and research institutions , regardless of nationality . nature reserves fall into different iucn categories depending on the level of protection given by local laws . < br >
