2015 Chapel Hill shooting     the 2015 chapel hill shooting was an incident that happened on february 10 , 2015 in chapel hill , north carolina . three muslim college students were shot dead . their neighbor craig stephen hicks was arrested for it . families of the victims called it a hate crime . police do not know why hicks might have done it . police in chapel hill say hicks had argued with the victims over parking spaces before . neighbors said hicks was obsessed over people parking in his space . his facebook page says he supports “ atheists for equality ” and blamed “ radical christians and radical muslims '' for causing problems in the world . police are still investigating . the fbi has been called in to help the local police . they have also started an investigation to see if any federal laws have been broken . so far federal officials do not have any evidence it is a hate crime .
