My Immortal     `` my immortal '' is the third single off american rock band evanescence 's first studio album `` fallen '' . the song was released in december 8 , 2003. the song was written by guitarist ben moody except for the bridge . it is a piano and power ballad . the lyrics of the song are about a spirit that haunts the memory of a grieving loved one . it is based on a short story that moody had written before . on the `` billboard '' charts , `` my immortal '' was able to peak at # 7 on hot 100 , # 2 on the mainstream top 40 , # 1 on the adult top 40 , and # 19 on the adult contemporary charts . in 2005 , the song was nominated for a grammy award in the category of best pop performance by a duo or group with vocals . other websites . - `` my immortal '' on youtube
