Alex Rocco     alex rocco ( born alexander federico petricone ; ( february 29 , 1936 - july 18 , 2015 ) was an american actor . he was known for his roles as moe greene in `` the godfather '' . he was also known for his roles in `` facts of life '' , `` the famous teddy z '' , `` the simpsons '' , `` a bug 's life '' , and `` get shorty '' . he won an emmy award in 1990 .
