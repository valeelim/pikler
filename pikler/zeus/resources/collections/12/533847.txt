Alan Grayson     alan mark grayson ( born march 13 , 1958 ) is an american politician . he is the united states representative for . he is a member of the democratic party . he last served as representative for from 2009 to 2011. he was defeated for re-election in 2010 by republican daniel webster . he was then elected in 2012 for another term in the house of representatives , defeating republican todd long . in 2014 he defeated republican challenger carol platt to win re-election . on july 9 , 2015 , grayson announced his intentions to run in the united states senate election in florida , 2016 to replace marco rubio . in 2016 , grayson said he supported bernie sanders to be the democratic nominee for president of the united states .
