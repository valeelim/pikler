Markiplier     mark edward fischbach ( born june 28 , 1989 ) , known by his youtube username markiplier , is an american internet personality , video game commentator , and charity fundraiser . he was born in o'ahu , hawaii . fischbach began his career in cincinnati , ohio . he now lives in los angeles , california . he studied civil engineering at the university of cincinnati . he later changed to biomedical engineering . he then left college and started his youtube career . as of may 2017 , his youtube channel has over 17 million subscribers , he is known for his let 's play gaming style and for his video game commentary on horror or survival games .
