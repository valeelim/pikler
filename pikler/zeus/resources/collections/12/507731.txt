Manitoba general election, 2011    manitoba general election , 2011 the 40th general election of manitoba was on october 4 , 2011. it was to elect members of the legislative assembly of manitoba . the new democratic party ( ndp ) won 37 out of 57 seats , the progressive conservative party ( pcp ) won 19 seats . the liberal party won one seat . the green party and the communist party both ran candidates but none were elected . since they had a majority of seats in the legislative assembly , the ndp formed the government , with greg selinger as premier . the pcp formed the official opposition .
