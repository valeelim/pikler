Ali Abu Mukhammad     ali abu mukhammad ( ) , also known as ali abu muhammad al-dagestani , born aliaskhab alibulatovich kebekov ( ) , was a dagestani militant islamist in russia and the leader of the caucasus emirate following the death of inaugural leader dokka umarov . the united states department of state added kebekov to its list of specially designated global terrorists on march 25 , 2015. on 19 april 2015 , ali abu mukhammad was killed by russian security forces during special operations near buynaksk .
