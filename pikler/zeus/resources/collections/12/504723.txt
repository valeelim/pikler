Volleyball World League     the fivb volleyball world league is an international men 's volleyball competition held annually . it was created in 1990. it is the longest and richest of all the international events organized by the fédération internationale de volleyball ( fivb ) . there are millions in prize money and worldwide tv coverage . the 2015 event is the 26th edition with a record 32 teams competing . other websites . - fédération internationale de volleyball ( fivb ) - fivb world league 2015
