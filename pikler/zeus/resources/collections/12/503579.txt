Victor Fleming     victor lonzo fleming ( february 23 , 1889 – january 6 , 1949 ) was an american movie director , cinematographer , and producer . his most popular movie were `` the wizard of oz '' ( 1939 ) , and `` gone with the wind '' ( 1939 ) . he won an academy award for best directing in 1939. fleming died of a heart attack while on his way to the hospital in cottonwood , arizona , aged 59. other websites . - the real rhett butler – david denby on victor fleming ( `` the new yorker '' )
