Moscow Time     moscow time is utc+3 , the same time as eastern european summer time , east africa time , and arabia standard time . on 28 march 2010 , moscow time became the time zone for the samara olabast in russia , leaving samara time ( utc+4 ) unused . on 8 february 2011 , the russian government announced the abandonment of winter time in all time zones across russia . moscovians setted their clocks forward on 27 march 2011 , and then utc+4 became its standard time permanently . in july 2014 , the law to observe summer time year round was abandoned , and the clock has been set to utc+3 starting from 26 october 2014. the time zone database is europe/moscow .
