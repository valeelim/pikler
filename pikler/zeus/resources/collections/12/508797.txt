Vanda     vanda is a genus in the orchid family ( `` orchidaceae '' ) . it is one of the most important orchids , with about fifty species . the genus is very highly prized in horticulture for its showy , fragrant , long lasting , and intensely colorful flowers . vanda is widespread across east asia , southeast asia , and new guinea , with a few species extending into queensland and some of the islands of the western pacific . the genus is sometimes abbreviated as v. in the flower trade . it belongs to angiosperm . references . - grove , d. l. 1995. vandas and ascocendas . timber press , portland , oregon . 241 pp . - motes , martin r. , and alan l. hoffman . 1997 vandas , their botany , history and culture . isbn 0-88192-376-1 other websites . - breeding in vanda breeding in vanda
