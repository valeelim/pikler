Mario Puzo     mario gianluigi puzo ( ; ; october 15 , 1920 – july 2 , 1999 ) was an american author and screenwriter . he was born in manhattan , new york city . he is known for his novels about the mafia , including `` the godfather '' ( 1969 ) , which was later made into a movie by francis ford coppola . he won the academy award for best adapted screenplay in both 1972 and 1974. puzo died of heart failure in west bay shore , new york , aged 78. other websites . - freshair interview – audio interview from fresh air . originally broadcast july 25 , 1996 . - mario puzo at the internet movie database - mario puzo books - the official mario puzo library - `` saying goodbye to mario puzo '' , an affectionate recollection of mario puzo written by his friend jules siegel on being notified of his death .
