2016–17 Liga I     the 2016–17 liga i will be the 99th season of liga i , which is the top division football league in romania . the season will begin on 23 july 2016 and will end in may 2017. teams start the championship round with their points from the regular season halved , rounded upwards , and no other records carried over from the regular season . teams start the relegation round with their points from the regular season halved , rounded upwards , and no other records carried over from the regular season . other websites . - official website
