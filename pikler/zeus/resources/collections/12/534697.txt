Reading Rainbow     reading rainbow is an american children 's television series . it aired on pbs kids from june 6 , 1983 until november 10 , 2006. it encouraged children to read . in 2012 , an ipad and kindle fire educational interactive book was launched with the `` reading rainbow '' name . the public television series won over 200 broadcast awards , including a peabody award and 26 emmy awards . eleven of those awards were in the `` outstanding children 's series '' category . when the show was canceled in 2006 , reruns ran until august 28 , 2009. the original team included lynne brenner ganek , ellen schecter and levar burton .
