Johan Ludvig Runeberg     johan ludvig runeberg ( ; 5 february 1804 – 6 may 1877 ) was a poet from finland . he is the national poet of finland . he wrote in the swedish language . runeberg 's most famous work is `` fänrik ståls sägner '' ( the tales of ensign stål , `` vänrikki stoolin tarinat '' in finnish ) . he wrote it between 1848 and 1860. the book consists of 36 poems . the tales are about finnish war of 1808–09 . in the war russia got finland from sweden . in the tales runeberg tries to tell us that deep down inside people are similar on both sides of the war . in that book , small people from finland become heroes in their own life . the first poem `` vårt land '' ( `` our land '' , `` maamme '' in finnish ) became the finnish national anthem .
