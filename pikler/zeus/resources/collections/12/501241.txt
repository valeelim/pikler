High Rock Lake     high rock lake is a reservoir lake located in davidson and rowan counties of north carolina . it serves as a border between them . it is a reservoir for hydroelectric power generation . it is the northernmost of the uwharrie lakes . high rock is the second largest lake in north carolina after lake norman . its water surface covers and there are of shoreline . high rock lake begins at the confluence of the yadkin river and the south yadkin river . the lake 's name comes from nearby `` high rock mountain '' , the tallest mountain in the uwharrie mountains . high rock lake is currently managed and operated by the alcoa company .
