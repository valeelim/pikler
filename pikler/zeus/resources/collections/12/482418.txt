Here Without You     `` here without you '' is the third single off american rock band 3 doors down 's second studio album `` away from the sun '' . the song was released on august 11 , 2003. on the `` billboard '' charts , `` here without you '' was able to peak at # 5 on the hot 100 chart . lead singer brad arnold said that his inspiration for this song was his now ex-wife . arnold said `` the song 's about being away from someone , or missing them . and it really does n't matter if you 're here without them for all day or all month . it 's about the loneliness and missing of somebody '' . the song was used by the wwe in a tribute video to professional wrestler eddie guerrero after he died from a heart attack in november 2005. other websites . - `` here without you '' on youtube
