Léonie Geisendorf     léonie geisendorf , née kaplan ( 8 april 1914 – 18 march 2016 ) , was a polish-born swedish architect . she lived and worked in stockholm , sweden most of her life . geisendorf was born in warsaw , poland . she studied architecture at eidgenössische technische hochschule zürich . in 1938 , after her internship , geisendorf moved to sweden and was hired by sven ivar lind , kfai and paul hedqvist . in 1950 geisendorf and her husband , charles edouard started their own office in stockholm . she was known for creating the s : t göran high school . she turned 100 in april 2014. geisendorf died in paris , france at the age of 101 .
