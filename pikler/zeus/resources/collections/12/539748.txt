Mei Baojiu     mei baojiu ( ) ( 29 march , 1934 – 25 april , 2016 ) was a chinese contemporary peking opera artist . he was also a performer of the dan role type in peking opera and kunqu opera . he was the leader of mei lanfang peking opera troupe in beijing peking opera theatre . mei ’ s regular performances of opera include `` farewell my concubine '' , `` guifei intoxicated '' ( 貴妃醉酒 ) , `` lady general mu takes command '' ( 穆桂英挂帅 ) , `` the story of yang guifei '' ( 太真外传 ) , `` luo shen '' ( 洛神 ) , and `` xi shi '' ( 西施 ) . on 31 march 2016 , mei was hospitalized in beijing because of bronchospasm . he died on 25 april 2016 , at the age of 82 .
