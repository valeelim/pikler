The Departed     the departed is a 2006 american crime drama thriller movie . it is directed by martin scorsese and written by william monahan . the movie stars leonardo dicaprio , matt damon , jack nicholson , mark wahlberg , martin sheen , ray winstone , vera farmiga , anthony anderson and alec baldwin . it was very successful , getting positive reviews and $ 289.8 million in the box office as well as winning four academy awards . it is a remake of the 2002 hong kong movie `` infernal affairs '' . the movie is set in boston , massachusetts . irish mob boss francis `` frank '' costello ( jack nicholson ) plants colin sullivan ( matt damon ) as a mole within the police force . at the same time , the police get william `` billy '' costigan ( leonardo dicaprio ) to gain access into costello 's crew . this leads to sullivan and costigan each trying to find the other 's identity before his own cover is blown .
