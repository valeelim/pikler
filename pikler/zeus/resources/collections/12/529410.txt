Sandhikharka     sandhikharka ( ) is a municipality in nepal . it is the headquarters of arghakhanchi district , in the lumbini zone of nepal . on 18 may 2014 , it was converted into a municipality by merging sandhikharka , bangla , narapani , khanchikot , kimadada , argha and dibharna vdcs . related pages . - development regions of nepal - zones of nepal
