Thomas Perez     thomas edward perez ( born october 7 , 1961 in buffalo , new york ) is an american politician , consumer advocate and civil rights lawyer . on july 23 , 2013 , he was sworn in as the united states secretary of labor . perez is a member of the democratic party . he previously was the assistant attorney general for the civil rights division of the united states department of justice . perez announced his candidacy for chair of the democratic national committee in the 2017 election on december 15 , 2016. on february 25 , 2017 , perez was elected chairman on a second ballot . minutes after being elected , perez named keith ellison as deputy chair .
