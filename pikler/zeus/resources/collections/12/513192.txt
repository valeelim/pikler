Neville Marriner     sir neville marriner ( born 15 april 1924-2 october 2016 ) is an english conductor and violinist . he was the first music director of the los angeles chamber orchestra , from 1969 to 1978. from 1979 to 1986 , he was music director of the minnesota orchestra . he was principal conductor of the stuttgart radio symphony orchestra from 1986 to 1989. marriner was born in lincoln , england , and studied at the royal college of music and the paris conservatoire .
