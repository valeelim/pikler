FCR 2001 Duisburg     fcr 2001 duisburg ( officially known as fußballclub rumeln 2001 duisburg ) was a german women 's association football club that was dissolved in 2013 because of a financial crisis . after the club disbanded , it merged with msv duisburg . some notable players who played for the club include alexandra popp , ashlyn harris , sara däbritz , simone laudehr , lieke martens and annike krahn . honours . - bundesliga 1 : 2000 - dfb-pokal 3 : 1998 , 2009 , 2010 - uefa cup 1 : 2009
