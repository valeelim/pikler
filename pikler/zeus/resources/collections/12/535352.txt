Sergio Arellano Stark     sergio arellano stark ( 10 june 1921 – 9 march 2016 ) was a chilean military officer . he led the so-called “ caravan of death '' . it killed many chileans from helicopters and established augusto pinochet ’ s hold on power . he was born in santiago , chile . stark died in santiago , chile on 9 march 2016 from complications of alzheimer 's disease . he was aged 94 .
