María Dolores Pradera     maría dolores fernández pradera ( born 29 august 1924 in madrid ) is a spanish melodic singer and actress . she one of the most famous singers in spain and latin america . she is also known as maría dolores or la pradera . she started her career as an actress , working in the theatre and in some of the most famous spanish movies from the 1940s : “ ¡a mí la legión ! ” ( 1942 ) , “ la lola se va a los puertos ” ( 1947 ) , or “ agustina de aragón ” ( 1950 ) .
