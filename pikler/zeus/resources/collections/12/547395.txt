Stanley Mandelstam     stanley mandelstam ( 12 december 1928 – 23 june 2016 ) was a south african-born american theoretical physicist . he introduced the mandelstam variables into particle physics in 1958. it was a coordinate system for creating his double dispersion relations . he was the professor of mathematical physics at the university of birmingham ( 1960-63 ) , the professeur associé at université de paris-sud ( 1979–80 ; 1984–85 ) and the professor of physics at the university of california , berkeley from 1963 until his death and was also the professor emeritus from 1994 until his death . other websites . - web page at berkeley
