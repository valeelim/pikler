Misako Uno     , known as akane ( 茜 ) ( born july 16 , 1986 ) is a japanese actress , product designer and talent agent . she is also a singer and dancer in the band `` '' . she was born in endokawa-ku , tokyo , japan . uno grew up in there . her father is a copywriter . she has been active since the 1994. in 2006 uno appeared in her first movie , hollywood 's `` the grudge 2 '' as a japanese student . in 2008 she played keiko endo ( 遠藤 恵子 ) in `` hitomi '' ( 瞳 ) of the nhk television fiction show . in 2010 uno appeared in her second film `` rendez-vous ( ランデブー！ ) '' as the leading character . in 2011 her early life was into the english textbooks of japan 's ministry of science . in 2012 she played akane yabuki ( 矢吹 茜 ) in `` ume-chan sensei '' ( 梅ちゃん先生 ) of the nhk television fiction show . in 2013 she played hoshino tsukiyama ( 月山 星乃 ) in `` tokyo toy box ( 東京トイボックス ) '' of the tv tokyo . tsukiyama is the heroine . works . music . - 2005 : slow dance original sound track - groovin ' ( with mayu ) - good time ( with mayu , eco ) - now is the time - 2007 : the grudge 2 original sound track
