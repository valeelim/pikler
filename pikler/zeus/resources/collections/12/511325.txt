Uhaa Nation     sesugh uhaa ( born august 25 , 1987 ) is an american professional wrestler . he is better known by his ring name uhaa nation . he is currently signed to the wwe and works in their developmental territory nxt under the ring name apollo crews . uhaa is best known for his work in dragon gate usa , as well as the japanese promotion dragon gate . during his time in dragon gate , he won the open the twin gate championship with bxb hulk . he was also named the best newcomer by dragon gate usa in 2011. in october 2014 , uhaa entered a wwe tryout camp and he was offered a developmental contract . on april 6 , 2015 , he reported to the wwe performance center , the home of their nxt . in a press release on april 13 , the company announced that uhaa was a member of their new nxt recruits . uhaa made his first televised appearance on the may 6 episode of nxt and signed his nxt contract during a segment with william regal . on august 5 , it was announced that uhaa would make his televised nxt debut on august 22 at the ' event . it was also announced that his new ring name would be apollo crews .
