Prayer in C     `` prayer in c '' is a song by the band lilly wood & the prick . it was originally released as a folk-pop song . the song was later remixed by dj robin schulz in 2014. schulz ' remix peaked at number one on `` billboard '' 's hot dance/electronic songs chart . it hit number one in many european countries including the uk and belgium . it reached the top twenty in canada . the remix is a dance and house song . it was included on schulz ' album , `` prayer '' .
