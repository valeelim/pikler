Cob     cob , or clom in wales , is a natural building material . cob is made from `` subsoil '' ( soil below the top layer ) , water , some kind of fibrous organic material ( usually straw ) , and sometimes lime . the contents of subsoil varies and if it does not contain the right mixture is can be modified with sand or clay . cob is fireproof , resists seismic activity , and inexpensive . it can be used to create artistic , sculptural forms and has been revived in recent years by the natural building and sustainability movements . cob material is known by many names including adobe , lump clay , puddled clay , chalk mud , wichert , clay daubins , swish ( african ) , torchis ( french ) , bauge ( french ) , bousille ( french mud with moss ) . the material has a long life-span even in rainy climates , provided a tall foundation and large roof overhang are present .
