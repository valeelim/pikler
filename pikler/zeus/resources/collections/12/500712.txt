Ben Powers     alton `` ben '' powers ( july 5 , 1950 – april 6 , 2015 ) was an american actor . he was known for his role as thelma evans ' husband , keith anderson , during the sixth and final season of the 1970s tv show `` good times '' ( 1978–1979 ) . during the 1970s , he was a cast member of the tv comedy series , `` laugh-in '' .
