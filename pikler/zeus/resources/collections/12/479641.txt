Anohana: The Flower We Saw That Day    anohana : the flower we saw that day the anime aired 11 episodes . it began airing on april 14 , 2011 and ended on june 23 , 2011. it was shown on fuji television 's noitamina block . it started as a serial novel by mari okada and appeared in media factory 's `` da vinci '' magazine from march to july 2011. it was later adapted into a manga and it was illustrated by mitsu izumi . a visual novel for the playstation portable was released in august 2012. on august 31 , 2013 , an anime movie was released in japanese theatres . a second anime movie is set to be released in 2015. other websites . - official anime website - official visual novel website
