HarbourFront MRT Station     harbourfront station ( ne1/cc29 ) is an mrt station in singapore . it is an interchange station along the north east mrt line and circle mrt line . it is the only double terminus in singapore 's mrt line . the station is located next to vivocity and harbourfront centre . timeline . - 20 june 2003 : station opened - 8 october 2011 : circle mrt line opened former codes . - north east mrt line : h1 ( before opening ) - circle mrt line : n/a
