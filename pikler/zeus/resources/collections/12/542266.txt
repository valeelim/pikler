Modena Volleyball Club     modena volley is a professional volleyball team based in modena , italy . it has played in the highest level of the italian volleyball league since 1968. it is the most successful italian club , having won the national league twelve times and the national cup as well : modena is the current holder of both titles . the club is one of the most prominent and prestigious in europe . it achieved thirteen european trophies including four cev champions league . the main sponsor of the club is dhl express . references . - official website
