Lady Saw     marion hall ( born 12 july 1972 ) , better known as lady saw , is a grammy award-winning jamaican reggae singer . hall was born in galina , saint mary , jamaica on 12 july 1972 and began performing with local sound systems at the age of fifteen . she went on to work with the `` stereo one system '' in kingston . her performances brought her to the attention of record producers . she recorded her first single for producer piper , and `` love me or lef me '' , produced by olive shaw received significant airplay . she had her first hit with `` if him lef '' and she topped the jamaican record charts with `` good man '' . awards . - lady saw received a gold certification from the r.i.a.a . for `` smile '' with vitamin c. - lady saw received a triple-platinum certification from the r.i.a.a . for `` underneath it all '' , her 2003 collaboration with no doubt . - in 2004 , lady saw earned a grammy award for best performance by a duo or group for her featured performance on no doubt 's single `` underneath it all '' .
