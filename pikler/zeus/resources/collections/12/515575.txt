Anoka, Minnesota    anoka , minnesota anoka ( ) is a city in anoka county , minnesota . the population was 17,142 at the 2010 census . it is the county seat . anoka calls itself the `` halloween capital of the world '' . this is because it hosted one of the first halloween parades in 1920. it continues to celebrate the holiday each year with several parades . anoka is a northern suburb of the twin cities .
