Adams' Grammar School    adams ' grammar school adams ' grammar school ( often abbreviated as ags ) is a selective boys ' grammar school in england . it is located in newport , shropshire . it was founded in 1656 by william adams , a wealthy member of the worshipful company of haberdashers ( one of the great twelve livery companies of the city of london ) . it is now one of the country 's best-performing schools . it was rated by the ofsted as a grade 1 outstanding school .
