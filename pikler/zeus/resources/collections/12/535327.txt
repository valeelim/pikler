Suranimala Rajapaksha     jayasinghe rajapakse mohottige don suranimala rajapaksha ( sinhala : සුරනිමල රාජපක්ෂ ) ( 5 january 1949 – 14 march 2016 ) was a sri lankan politician . he was first elected to the parliament of sri lanka in 1994. he was the minister of school education in republic socialist of sri lanka in 2001 to 2004. he was a member of the united national party ( unp ) and a member of unp working committee . he was appointed as the coordinating secretary to the prime minister ranil wickremasinghe on 27 january 2015. rajapaksha died on 14 march 2016 at a hospital in colombo , sri lanka from renal failure . he was aged 67 .
