Chivalric romance     chivalric romances are stories , poems or songs . they were written about marvel-filled adventures . knights having heroic qualities went on quests . it is the emphasis on love and courtly manners which distinguishes it from other kinds of epic . other types of epic are about masculine military heroism . romances were written in european languages of the middle ages . the best known romance was king arthur and the knights of the round table . `` sir gawain and the green knight '' is another example .
