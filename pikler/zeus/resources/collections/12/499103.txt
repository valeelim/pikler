Lance-Corporal Jones     lance corporal jack jones is a fictional home guard platoon lance-corporal , veteran of the british empire and butcher . he was played by clive dunn in the bbc television sitcom `` dad 's army '' and by tom courtenay in the upcoming 2015 movie `` dad 's army '' . his catchphrases are `` do n't panic ! `` , `` permission to speak , sir ? '' and `` they do n't like it up 'em ! `` , and sometimes `` you saved my life ! '' ( usually to captain mainwaring ) .
