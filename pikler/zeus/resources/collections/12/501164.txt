Francis Tsai     francis tsai ( 1968 – april 23 , 2015 ) was an american comic book artist , illustrator , author and conceptual artist . he was of taiwanese and japanese ancestry . tsai also contributes covers and interior art for marvel comics . tsai was from honolulu , hawaii . tsai provided interior artwork for a number of `` dungeons & dragons '' manuals for wizards of the coast , including `` '' , `` monster manual iii '' , `` d20 future '' , `` races of eberron '' , `` lost empires of faerûn '' , `` explorer 's handbook '' , `` tome of magic : pact , shadow , and truename magic '' , `` power of faerûn '' , and `` d20 future tech '' . he also worked on movies such as `` sucker punch '' and `` tmnt - teenage mutant ninja turtles '' . in 2010 , at the age of 42 , tsai was diagnosed with amyotrophic lateral sclerosis ( als ) , also known as lou gehrig 's disease . he died april 23 , 2015. other websites . - francis tsai official website
