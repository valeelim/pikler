Sean Hayes (actor)    sean hayes ( actor ) sean patrick hayes ( born june 26 , 1970 ) is an american actor , comedian , and producer . he is best known for his role as jack mcfarland on the nbc sitcom `` will & grace '' . during the series he won an emmy award , four sag awards , and one american comedy award , and earned six golden globe nominations . after `` will & grace '' he guest starred in programs such as `` 30 rock '' , `` portlandia '' and `` parks and recreation '' .
