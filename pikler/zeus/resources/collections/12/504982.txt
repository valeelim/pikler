Kate Quilton     kate quilton is a television presenter best known for her work on food shows like `` food unwrapped '' .. quilton began working in television while still a student . she had jobs with itv and the bbc . she made programmes for channel 4 from 2010 until 2014 including the short lived documentary series `` seven days '' . she resigned from channel 4 in 2014 to work full time on `` food unwrapped '' . in 2014 quilton appeared with `` food unwrapped '' co-presenter jimmy doherty in `` the world 's best diet '' , a one-off show .
