Joey Essex     joey don essex ( born 29 july 1990 ) is an english television personality and dj for sugar hut , a nightclub in brentwood , essex . he rose to fame in 2011 after appearing in the itv2 television series `` the only way is essex '' . essex and his sister frankie left the show in 2013 , although his cousin chloe remains on it . in 2013 , essex took part in the first series of the celebrity diving show `` splash ! '' and the thirteenth series of `` i 'm a celebrity ... get me out of here ! `` , where he finished in fourth place . in 2015 , he won the channel 4 reality series `` the jump '' . essex is at least an ex-social smoker . this picture was taken , and published on the metro website on 3rd november 2011 .
