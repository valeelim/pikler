Spotswood, New Jersey    spotswood , new jersey spotswood is a town in middlesex county , new jersey , united states . its first settler james johnston originally called the place `` spotteswoode '' , named for his old place of residence in scotland . the settlement dates back to 1685. spotswood was originally part of east brunswick township , but decided to become its own town on april 15 , 1908. the town had a total area of 2.472 square miles ( 6.402 km ) . as of the 2000 united states census there were 7,880 people , 3,099 households , and 2,163 families residing in the borough .
