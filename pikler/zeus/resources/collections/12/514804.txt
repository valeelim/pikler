Jarryd Hayne     jarryd lee hayne ( born 15 february 1988 ) is an australian american football player and former rugby league player . he currently plays for the san francisco 49ers in the national football league ( nfl ) . during his time playing rugby league , he played for the parramatta eels of the national rugby league ( nrl ) from 2006 until 2014. hayne was born in sydney , new south wales . his father is manoa thompson , a fijian rugby league player , and an australian mother , jodie . he has three siblings .
