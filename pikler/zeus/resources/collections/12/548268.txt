Blen Mesfin     blen mesfin ( amharic : ብሌን መስፍን ) is an official in ethiopia ’ s opposition semayawi ( blue ) party . in 2016 she was named one of twenty women political prisoners in the freethe20 campaign by samantha power , the u.s. ambassador to the united nations . mesfin was arrested at a protest in addis ababa in april 2015. the protest was for 26 ethiopians killed by the a group called islamic state in libya . more than 30 members of the semayawi party were arrested before an april 27 political rally . two other members of the semayawi party , meron alemayehu ( ሜሮን አለማየሁ ) and nigist wondifraw ( ንግስት ወንዲፍራው ) , were also arrested . they were # 5 and # 6 in the freethe20 campaign . they are now free . mesfin was released in december 2016 , along with a mass release of 9,800 people detained under the state of emergency . according to ethiopian journalist tesfalem waldyes‏ she was re-arrested during a wave of arrests in october 2016.‏ ethiopia : semayawi party ( blue party ) , including origin , mandate , leadership .
