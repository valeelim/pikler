Austin Clarke     austin ardinel chesterfield `` tom '' clarke ( july 26 , 1934 – june 26 , 2016 ) was a barbadian-canadian novelist , essayist and short story writer . he worked in toronto , ontario . clarke was a reporter in the ontario communities of timmins and kirkland lake before joining the canadian broadcasting corporation as a freelance journalist . he taught subsequently at several american universities , including yale university ( hoyt fellow , 1968–70 ) , duke university ( 1971–72 ) , and the university of texas ( visiting professor , 1973 ) .
