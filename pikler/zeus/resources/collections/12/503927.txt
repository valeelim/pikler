The Longest Ride     the longest ride is a 2015 romantic-drama movie . it is about luke collins , a bull rider who is seriously injured one night while riding . one year later , he meets sophia . lolita davidovich plays linda collins . gloria reuben plays adrienne . cast . - scott eastwood as luke collins - britt robertson as sophia danko - alan alda as ira levinson - jack huston as young ira - oona chaplin as ruth - melissa benoist as marcia - lolita davidovich as linda collins - barry ratcliffe as the auctioneer - gloria reuben as adrienne francis - brett edwards as jared midelton - hunter burke as david stein - alina lia as brooke in april 2014 , fox 2000 pictures set a movie adaptation to be released on april 10 , 2015. principal photography began on june 16 , 2014 in wilmington and winston-salem , north carolina . on july 28 , filming began in jacksonville . the movie was released on april 10 , 2015. critics gave negative reviews to the movie . rotten tomatoes has given a rating of 29 % to `` the longest ride '' . rogerebert.com gave the movie one and a half stars in its reviews . variety magazine gave mixed reviews for this movie . metacritic rates the movie 29 % , meaning `` generally unfavorable '' reviews .
