The Black Unicorn     the black unicorn is a fantasy book written by terry brooks . it is the second book in the `` magic kingdom of landover '' series . the book continues the stories of ben holiday , willow , and questor thews . each of them had a dream about something important to them . they each left sterling silver ( the castle they live in ) to look for what they dreamed about . the dreams led ben holiday to chicago , questor thews to find the books of magic , and willow to hunt for the black unicorn .
