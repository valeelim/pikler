Francisco Rubio Llorente     francisco rubio llorente ( 25 february 1930 – 23 february 2016 ) was a spanish judge and law professor . he was president of the spanish council of state between 2004 to 2012. his predecessor and successor was josé manuel romay beccaría . he was judge on the constitutional court of spain between 1980 to 1992. he spent the last three of those years as vice president . llorente died in madrid on 23 february 2016 from heart failure . he was aged 85 .
