Mike Porcaro     michael joseph porcaro ( may 29 , 1955 – march 15 , 2015 ) was an american bass player known for his work with toto . he retired from touring in 2007 as a result of being diagnosed with amyotrophic lateral sclerosis ( als ) , also known as lou gehrig ’ s disease . he was the middle son of joe porcaro . his elder brother was jeff porcaro . his younger brother is steve porcaro . porcaro died from complications of als in his sleep at his home in los angeles , california , aged 59 .
