Triceps brachii muscle     the triceps brachii muscle is a muscle on the back of the upper arm . there are three parts that each come from different places at the top of the arm and join together at the elbow . it straightens the arm . the name is latin for `` three-headed muscle in the arm '' . the triceps brachii has three heads . they are : the long head , medial ( inside ) head , and the lateral ( outside ) head.the long head comes from the shoulderblade . it goes down the arm between teres minor muscle and teres major muscle . the medial head and lateral heads comes from the humerus ( upper arm bone ) . the medial head is mostly made of small , slow muscle fibers and motor units . the lateral head is mostly made of large , fast fibers and motor units . the long head is made of a mixture of fiber types and motor units . some scientists think that each of the three parts can be thought of as separate muscles . the fibers join to a single tendon to attach to the ulna bone . some research found that there might be more than one tendon .
