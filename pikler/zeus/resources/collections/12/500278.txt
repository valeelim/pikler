Alexander Nadson     alexander nadson ( ; 8 august 1926 – 15 april 2015 ) was a belarusian émigré social and religious leader . he was the apostolic visitor for belarusian greek catholic faithfuls abroad from 1986 until his death . he became a priest in 1958 and worked in the united kingdom for most of his life . nadson was born in the town of haradzeya , nesvizh raion of the minsk oblast . nadson died on 15 april 2015 in london , aged 88 .
