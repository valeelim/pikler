Charlie Beasley     charles p. `` charlie '' beasley ( september 23 , 1945 – april 11 , 2015 ) was an american basketball player . he played for the dallas chaparrals ( 1967–70 ) , texas chaparrals and the floridians ( 1970–71 ) in the aba for 281 games . beasley was born in shawnee , oklahoma . he was married and had a son . beasley died on april 11 , 2015 in mckinney , texas , aged 69 .
