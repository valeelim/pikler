Antoine Demoitié     antoine demoitié ( 16 october 1990 – 27 march 2016 ) was a belgian cyclist . he last rode for wanty–groupe gobert in 2016. he joined the team in 2016 after leaving wallonie-bruxelles at the end of the 2015 season . he was born in liège , wallonia , belgium . demoitié died on 27 march 2016 after he crashed and went down in a pile-up of several cyclists . he was then hit by a motorcycle in the gent–wevelgem road race . he died that evening in hospital in lille , hauts-de-france , france , aged 25 .
