Dangerous Game (movie)    dangerous game ( movie ) dangerous game is a 1993 erotic movie . harvey keitel plays eddie israel . madonna plays sarah jennings . the movie is about a movie director based in new york city . the director has sarah jennings acting in a hollywood marital-crisis drama movie . this movie was released on november 19 , 1993 in the united states . it got mixed reviews by movie critics . rotten tomatoes gave it a score of 29 % based on 14 critic 's reviews . it was rated r by the motion picture association of america ( mmpa ) . it contains violence , profanity , sexual situations and nudity .
