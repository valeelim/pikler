Curt Lundmark     curt `` curre '' lundmark , born 9 september 1944 in byske , sweden is a swedish former ice hockey player and manager . he acted as the for the swedish men 's national team when it won the gold medal during the 1994 olympic winter games in lillehammer . earlier he had also assisted conny evensson when sweden won the 1991 iihf world championship in turku and the 1992 iihf world championship in prague . curt lundmark also led the to a silver medal during the 1995 iihf world championship in stockholm . the swedes lost the final game against finland ( 1-4 ) during that tournament . curt lundmark resigned after that tournament . he was replaced by kent forsberg . he has also coached club teams like västerås ik , hv 71 , jokerit and leksands if . for some years he was also the svt sport expert commentator . as an expert commentator he often worked with åsa e. jönsson or staffan lindeborg .
