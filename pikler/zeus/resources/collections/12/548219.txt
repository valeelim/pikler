Arrondissement of Cognac     the arrondissement of cognac is an arrondissement of france , in the charente department , nouvelle-aquitaine region . its capital is the city of cognac . when the charente department was created on 1800 , the `` arrondissement '' of cognac was part of that original department . the `` arrondissement '' of cognac is the smallest `` arrondissement '' of the department with an area of . it has 92,224 inhabitants and a density of inhabitants/km² . the `` arrondissement '' of cognac is in western charente and is bordered to the north by the `` arrondissement '' of confolens , to the east by the `` arrondissement '' of angoulême and to the south and west by the charente-maritime department . after the reorganisation of the cantons in france , cantons are not subdivisions of the `` arrondissements '' so they could have `` communes '' that belong to different `` arrondissements '' . in the `` arrondissement '' of cognac , there are three cantons where not all their `` communes '' are in the `` arrondissement '' : charente-sud , tude-et-lavalette and val de nouère . the following table shows the distribution of the `` communes '' in the cantons and `` arrondissements '' :
