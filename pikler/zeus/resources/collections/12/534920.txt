Jon English     jonathan james `` jon '' english ( 26 march 1949 â€“ 9 march 2016 ) was an english-born australian singer , songwriter , musician and actor . he was an early member of the band sebastian hardie . he played the role of judas iscariot in the australian production of the stage musical `` jesus christ superstar '' in 1972. he also performed in gilbert and sullivan 's operettas `` the pirates of penzance '' , `` the mikado '' and `` h.m.s . pinafore '' from 1984. he also acted in a number of television series . as a solo singer , his best known songs were `` turn the page '' , `` hollywood seven '' , `` words are not enough '' , `` six ribbons '' and `` hot town '' . english was born in hampstead , london . he moved to sydney as a child in 1961. he had four children . he was a fan of the parramatta eels . english died from an aortic aneurysm caused by surgery problems on 9 march 2016 in newcastle , new south wales . he was 66 .
