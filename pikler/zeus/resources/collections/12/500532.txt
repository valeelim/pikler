West Perth, Ontario    west perth , ontario west perth is a municipality in ontario , canada , located in perth county . it was started in 1998 , when the town of mitchell and the townships of logan , hibbert , and fullarton amalgamated . west perth has an area of 589.42 square kilometers ( 223.72 square miles ) . according to the canada 2011 census , it has a population of 8,919 .
