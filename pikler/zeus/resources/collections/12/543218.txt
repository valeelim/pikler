Clash of Clans     clash of clans is a freemium mobile mmo strategy video game made by supercell . the game was made in finland . it was released for ios platforms on 2 august , 2012 , and on google play for android on 7 october , 2013 it is an online multiplayer game . players build a community ( called a clan ) , train troops , and attack other players to earn gold , elixir and dark elixir . these can be used to build defenses that protect the player from attacks by other players attacks .
