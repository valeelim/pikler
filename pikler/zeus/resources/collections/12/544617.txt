This Is What the Truth Feels Like     this is what the truth feels like is the third studio album by american recording artist gwen stefani . it was released on march 18 , 2016 by interscope records , and is her first solo album in ten years . `` this is what the truth feels like '' has received favorable reviews from music critics . on april 18 , 2016 ; stefani announced that she will tour with american rapper eve to promote the album , the tour was mainly around america and canada and started at mansfield , massachusetts on july 12 , 2016 .
