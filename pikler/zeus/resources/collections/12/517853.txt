Lake County, California    lake county , california lake county is a county in the u.s. state of california . as of the 2010 census , the population was 64,665. the county seat is lakeport . the county takes its name from clear lake . clear lake is the largest natural lake entirely within california . lake county is surrounded by 6 counties . napa county is to the south . sonoma county is to the south west . mendocino county is to the north west . glenn county is to the north east . colusa county is to the east . yolo county is to the south east . lake county is north of the san francisco bay area . lake county was formed in 1861 from parts of napa and mendocino counties . the u.s. census bureau estimates that the population was 64,184 in 2014. in september and october 2015 , a wildfire called the valley fire burned more than 76,000 acres of land in lake county , napa county , and sonoma county . the fire destroyed almost 2,000 buildings and threatened almost 7,500 buildings . the fire killed 4 people and left 3,000 people homeless . the damage is estimated at `` hundred of millions of dollars '' .
