Mark DeSaulnier     mark james desaulnier ( born march 31 , 1952 ) is an american politician . he is a democratic member of the united states house of representatives . he has served california 's 11th congressional district since 2015. in 2015 , desaulnier was diagnosed with leukemia . in may 2016 , it was announced the leukemia was incurable and that desaulnier would still seek re-election in 2016. other websites . - http : //www.markdesaulnier.com
