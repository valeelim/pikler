Sea Life Minnesota Aquarium     sea life minnesota aquarium is a public aquarium located in the mall of america in bloomington , minnesota , usa . it holds over 10,000 aquatic creatures . this includes a large collection of sharks , sea turtles , stingrays and fresh water fish . the aquarium also features sea turtles , octopuses , and lobsters . freshwater exhibits include game fish from the northern united states . there are several giant catfish and a alligator gar . the aquarium has 30 tanks . the aquarium includes a clear acrylic tunnel . it has the `` world 's largest jellyfish collection '' and the `` world 's largest underground shark exhibit '' .
