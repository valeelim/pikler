Gaëlle Enganamouit     gaëlle deborah enganamouit ( born on 9 june 1992 in yaoundé , cameroon ) is a cameroonian association football midfielder and forward who currently plays for eskilstuna united dff and the cameroon women 's national football team . she has also played for lorema yaoundé and žfk spartak subotica . she can play a variety of positions , but normally she plays as a midfielder or forward .
