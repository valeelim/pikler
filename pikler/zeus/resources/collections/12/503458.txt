Levelland, Texas    levelland , texas levelland is a city in the state of texas in the united states . it is the county seat of hockley county . about 13,929 people lived there in 2013. in november 1957 , some drivers in levelland saw an unidentified flying object that caused automobiles to stop until it lifted . two of the drivers were weir clem , levelland 's sheriff , and ray jones , a firefighter .
