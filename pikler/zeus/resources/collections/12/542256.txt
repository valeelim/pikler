Oya Aydoğan     oya aydoğan ( 10 february 1957 – 15 may 2016 ) was a turkish actress , model and television presenter . she was the winner of the ses magazine movie actress contest in 1976. her career began in 1976 with the movie `` deli şahin '' . she was born in erzincan , turkey . she was a alevi islam . she retired in 2015. she suffered an aortic aneurysm on 7 may 2016 , before the mother 's day . she died on 15 may 2016 in a hospital in istanbul , aged 59 .
