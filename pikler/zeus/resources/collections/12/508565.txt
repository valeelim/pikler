Jane Morgan     jane morgan ( born may 3 , 1924 ) is an american popular music singer . morgan was popular in france and the uk before becoming popular in the united states . she received six gold records and a star on the hollywood walk of fame . she retired in 1973. morgan married movie producer and talent agent , jerry weintraub in 1965. they had four children . weintraub died in 2015. morgan performed for french president charles de gaulle . she also sang for five u.s. presidents : john f. kennedy , richard nixon , gerald ford , jimmy carter , and george w. bush . she toured with jack benny and john raitt , and sang at the grand ole opry . two of her rca singles hit the billboard country charts in 1970 .
