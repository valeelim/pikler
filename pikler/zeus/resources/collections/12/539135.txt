Purple Rain (song)    purple rain ( song ) `` purple rain '' is a pop and rock song by the musician prince . the song is from the 1984 album of the same name . it was the third single off that album . on the `` billboard '' hot 100 , the album reached # 2 for two weeks . it was certified as gold by the recording industry association of america . one million copies were shipped in the united states . prince won an academy award for best original song score for `` purple rain '' . the original version is eight minutes/forty five seconds long . the song was shortened to four minutes before it was released as a single .
