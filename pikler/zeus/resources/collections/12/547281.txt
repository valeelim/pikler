Dan Patrick     daniel patrick pugh ( born may 15 , 1956 ) , known professionally as dan patrick , is an american sportscaster , radio personality , and actor . he currently hosts `` the dan patrick show '' broadcast on radio and on television . he also currently co-hosts nbc 's `` football night in america '' and serves as a senior writer for `` sports illustrated '' . he previously worked at espn for 18 years , where he often anchored the weeknight and sunday 11 p.m. edition of `` sportscenter '' . other websites . - dan patrick online official website on cnn.com - dan patrick biography
