Echo (Leona Lewis album)    echo ( leona lewis album ) echo is the second studio album by british recording artist leona lewis . it was released in november 2009 by syco . the music on `` echo '' is a mixture of europop and r & b . it also has a cover version of the oasis song `` stop crying your heart out '' two singles were released from the album . one of them was the r & b ballad `` happy '' . the album also features a duet with american pop rock group onerepublic called `` lost then found '' . `` echo '' met mixed to favourable reviews by critics .
