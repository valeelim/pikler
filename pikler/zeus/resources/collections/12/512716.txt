Sharon Case     sharon case ( born february 9 , 1971 ) is an american actress and a former model . at age 17 , she began working as a model before getting into acting . she is best known for her roles on daytime soap operas . she played dawn winthrop in `` general hospital '' . she also played debbie simon in `` as the world turns '' . she played sharon newman on `` the young and the restless '' . case was born in detroit , michigan .
