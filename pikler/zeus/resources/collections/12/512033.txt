Koshi Zone     koshi ( , is one of the fourteen zones of nepal . the headquarters of koshi zone is biratnagar . it is also its largest city . other cities of koshi zone are inaruwa , dharan , dhankuta and itahari . its main rivers are the arun , tamar and sapta koshi . koshi has six districts . these are bhojpur , dhankuta , morang , sankhuwasabha , sunsari and terhathum . famous religious places . - pindeswari temple , dharan - buda subba , dharan - panchakanya temple , dharan - bishnupaduka , dharan - kali mandir , biratnagar related pages . - zones of nepal - districts of nepal - development regions of nepal
