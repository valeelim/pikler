Everton Weekes     sir everton decourcy weekes , kcmg , gcm , obe ( born 26 february 1925 ) is a retired cricket player for the west indies . along with frank worrell and clyde walcott , he formed what was known as `` the three ws '' of west indian cricket . he was born in saint michael , barbados . weekes began his career in 1948. he scored 88 and 117 runs ( retired ) and was selected for the tour . he played his first match for barbados on 24 february 1945. he was aged 19 years ; it was one day before his 20th birthday . the match was against trinidad and tobago at queen 's park oval , port of spain . batting at number six , he scored 0 and eight runs . barbados lost by ten wickets . he retired in 1958 .
