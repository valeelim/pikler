Gordon Peters     gordon peter wilkinson , known professionally as gordon peters ( born 29 november 1926 in durham ) is a british television actor . he starred in a bbc tv comedy series in 1973 called `` the gordon peters show '' . he is known for his roles in `` hi-de-hi ! `` , `` dad 's army '' , `` are you being served ? `` , `` grace & favour '' , `` one foot in the grave '' , `` never the twain '' , `` little britain '' , `` keeping up appearances '' and `` hbo 's tales from the crypt '' . other websites . - official website
