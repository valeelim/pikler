Wait Your Turn     `` wait your turn '' is a song recorded by barbadian singer rihanna for her fourth studio album , `` rated r '' ( 2009 ) . it was written by mikkel s. eriksen , tor erik hermansen , saul milton , will kennard , james fauntleroy ii , takura tendayi , and rihanna herself . stargate ( eriksen and hermansen ) and chase & status ( milton and kennard ) produced the song .
