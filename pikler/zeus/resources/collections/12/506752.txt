Lindsey Graham presidential campaign, 2016    lindsey graham presidential campaign , 2016 the 2016 presidential campaign of lindsey graham , the senior united states senator from south carolina , and former member of the house of representatives , was announced on june 1 , 2015. graham sought the republican nomination for president of the united states in the 2016 election ; a close personal friend of senator john mccain of arizona , graham was a top staffer during mccain 's 2008 campaign . after low polling numbers , graham withdrew from the race on december 21 , 2015 .
