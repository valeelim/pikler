John E. Sununu     john e. sununu ( born september 10 , 1964 ) is a former united states senator from new hampshire and a member of the republican party . he was in the united states house of representatives from 1997 to 2003. sununu served in the united states senate from january 3 , 2003 , to january 3 , 2009. sununu was born on september 10 , 1964 in boston , massachusetts to former governor of new hampshire john h. sununu . he studied at massachusetts institute of technology and at harvard business school . he is married to kitty sununu . they have three children . his brother , chris sununu is the governor-elect of new hampshire .
