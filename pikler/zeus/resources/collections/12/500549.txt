Central Ontario     central ontario is a subregion of southern ontario in the canadian province of ontario . it is between georgian bay and the eastern end of lake ontario . it covers . it has a population of 1,070,644 according to the canada 2011 census . much of the subregion is rural . the southern part is more densely populated than the northern part . some of the largest cities include peterborough , barrie , and belleville . there are five counties in the subregion : - dufferin county - haliburton county - hastings county - northumberland county - peterborough county - simcoe county - prince edward county is a single-tier municipality . ten towns , villages and townships merged with the county . the county government provides all services .
