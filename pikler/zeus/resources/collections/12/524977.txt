SS Daniel J. Morrell     ss `` daniel j. morrell '' was an american great lakes ore carrier . it was 603 feet long . the ship usually carried iron ore. the `` morrell '' sank during a severe storm on november 29 , 1966. the ship broke in two before sinking . twenty-eight of the `` morrell '' 's 29 crewmen died during the sinking . only one crewman survived .
