Walter Tull     walter daniel john tull ( 28 april 1888 â€“ 25 march 1918 ) was an english professional footballer . he played as an inside forward for tottenham hotspur and northampton town . he was the second person of mixed race to play in the top division of the football league . he was the first mixed-race british outfielder in the top division of english football . his professional football career began after he was spotted while playing for his local amateur club , clapton f.c.. he began playing for clapton in 1908. within a few months he had won winners ' medals in the fa amateur cup , london county amateur cup and london senior cup . in march 1909 the `` football star '' called him `` the catch of the season '' . tull was brought up in a national children 's home orphanage in bethnal green , london , along with his brother . they were placed there following the death of their parents . he joined tottenham in 1909 and transferred to northampton town in 1911 , where he made 111 first-team appearances . during the first world war , tull served in the footballers ' battalion of the middlesex regiment . he fought in the battle of the somme in 1916. tull was commissioned as a second lieutenant on 30 may 1917. this was in spite of the fact there was a ban on black people being given that status .
