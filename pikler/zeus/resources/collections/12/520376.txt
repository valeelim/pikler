Västerstrands AIK     västerstrands aik is a sports club in the town of karlstad in sweden . the club was founded on 11 october 1940. it currently only runs a women 's bandy team . the women 's bandy team has won the swedish national championship in the years of 1991 , 1992 , 1994 , 1997 , 2001 , and 2002. it has also ended up as runner-up several times . the team colours are black and yellow . other websites . - västerstrands aik
