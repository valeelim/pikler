Drush     drush is a free and open source software application that allows people to install and change drupal websites . the name `` drush '' means `` '' dru '' pal `` sh '' ell '' . people who install drush can do all these things using the command line , or terminal of a computer . this makes managing a website that uses drupal faster and more efficient . according to github , drush has been downloaded more than 788,000 times as of march , 2016 .
