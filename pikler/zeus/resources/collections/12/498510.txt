Josef Sommer     josef sommer ( born june 26 , 1934 ) is a german-american stage and movie actor . he was born in greifswald , germany , and raised in north carolina . he appeared as 38th president gerald ford opposite gena rowlands in the made-for-tv movie `` the betty ford story '' ( 1987 ) . he is known for his role as dr. eaton in the 1998 movie `` patch adams '' .
