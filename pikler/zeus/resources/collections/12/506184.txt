Hana El Hebshi     hana elhebshi or hana el hebshi is a libyan activist and architect . in 2012 she received the international women of courage award . during the libyan revolution , hana advised nato on strikes . she also made public how many people gaddafi 's government killed during the war . she did this under the name `` numidia '' . she also fought for women 's rights in libya . other websites . - 2012 international women of courage awards , u.s. state department photostream on flickr - secretary 's international women of courage award ( u.s. department of state )
