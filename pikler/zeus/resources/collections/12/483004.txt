Moonrise Kingdom     moonrise kingdom is a 2012 american comedy-drama adventure movie . it was directed by wes anderson and written by anderson and roman coppola . it stars jared gilman , kara hayward , bruce willis , edward norton , bill murray , frances mcdormand , jason schwartzman , bob balaban and harvey keitel . it is set in new england in 1965. it is about a 12-year-old boy and 12-year-old girl who run away together . it received very positive reviews when it was released on may 25 , 2012 .
