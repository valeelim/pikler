Barbro Hiort af Ornäs     barbro hiort af ornäs ( 28 august 1921 – 28 november 2015 ) was a swedish actress . she was born in gothenburg , sweden . she won the best actress award at the 1958 cannes film festival for the movie `` brink of life '' . she was also known for her roles in `` while the city sleeps '' , `` all these women '' , `` the passion of anna '' , and in `` the sense of marriage '' . af ornäs died on november 28 , 2015 in gothenburg from natural causes , aged 94 .
