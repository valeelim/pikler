Pedro de Felipe     pedro eugenio de felipe cortés ( 18 july 1944 – 12 april 2016 ) was a spanish footballer who played as a central defender . he was born in madrid . de felipe arrived at real madrid in 1964 at the age of 20. he won the 1965–66 european cup while playing in seven complete matches . de felipe gained one cap for spain , playing the full 90 minutes in a 0–0 friendly with turkey on 17 october 1973 , in istanbul .
