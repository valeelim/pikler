Scion FR-S     the scion fr-s is a sports car developed by toyota and subaru . it is built solely . it features a rear wheel drive drivetrain and 2+2 seating . it is sold worldwide under three different brands with respective model names : - toyota 86 in asia , south africa , north america ( from august 2016 ) , south america and australia ; gt86 in europe ; new zealand ; ft86 in nicaragua and jamaica ; - subaru brz worldwide ; - scion fr-s in the us and canada . after august 2016 , the scion fr-s will be renamed as the toyota 86 .
