Akhtar Mansour     mullah akhtar mohammad mansoor ( `` akhtar muḥammad manṣūr '' ; pronounced or ; born probably in 1968 ) was the emir ( leader ) of the taliban , an islamic fundamentalist political movement in afghanistan also known as the islamic emirate of afghanistan ( a recently created title ) . on 21 may 2016 , it was reported by a united states department of defense official that mansour had been killed in a u.s. drone strike in pakistan . other websites . - t.a.c . – link published november 2009 - p. tomsen – text published by `` publicaffairs '' , november 25 , 2013 , 912 pages , isbn 1610394127
