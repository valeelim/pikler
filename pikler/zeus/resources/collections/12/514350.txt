The Late Show with Stephen Colbert     the late show with stephen colbert is an american late-night talk show hosted by stephen colbert on cbs . it is the second version of the `` late show '' franchise , following the retirement of original `` late show '' host david letterman . jonathan batiste is the bandleader . the show premiered on september 8 , 2015. colbert had previously hosted `` the colbert report '' on comedy central . other websites . - official website
