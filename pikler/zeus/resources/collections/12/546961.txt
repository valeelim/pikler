Ouroboros     the ouroboros or oroboros is an ancient symbol of a serpent or dragon eating its own tail . the ouroboros symbolizes eternity or cyclicality . it is the sense of something constantly re-creating itself . like the phoenix , it symbolises cycles that begin again as soon as they end . the first known appearance of the ouroboros motif is in kv62 , the tomb of tutankhamun , in the 14th century bc . the text is about the god ra and his union with osiris in the underworld . in an illustration , two serpents , with their tails in their mouths , coil around the head and feet of an enormous god ( this the unified ra-osiris ) . the whole figure represents the beginning and the end of time .
