Góra     góra is a town in lower silesian voivodeship , in south-western poland . the town is approximately north-west of the regional capital wrocław . the population was 12,574 in 2006. the settlement was first named `` góra '' in an 1155 deed by pope adrian iv . it gave the property to a diocese of the roman catholic church . the name of the town means `` hill '' or `` mountain '' in the old polish language . beginning in the 14th century , the town became a centre of cloth manufacturing . góra became part of the kingdom of prussia during a war in 1742. in world war ii it was occupied by the red army in 1945. in the potsdam agreement , the town fell to the republic of poland , and the remaining german population was thrown out . the town became a county seat again in 1946. twin towns . - herzberg am harz , germany ( since 1993 ) - maasdonk , netherlands - trakai , lithuania other websites . - official website - jewish community in góra on virtual shtetl - góra pictures gallery
