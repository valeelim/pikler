Mitchell, Ontario    mitchell , ontario mitchell is a community in perth county , ontario , canada . it is 20 kilometers north of stratford and 60 kilometers north of london . the mitchell hawks of the southern ontario junior hockey league is based in the town . mitchell was founded in 1836 by william johnston and john hicks . it was incorporated as a village in 1857 and became a town in 1874. in 1998 , mitchell amalgamated with the townships of logan , fullarton , and hibbert to form the municipality of west perth .
