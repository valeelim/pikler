Rocka Rolla     rocka rolla is the first studio album by british heavy metal band , judas priest . `` rocka rolla '' was released on 6 september 1974 through both gull records . the album was produced by rodger bain , who had produced the first three albums for black sabbath . when the album was released , it was met with a small reception and sold `` only a few thousand copies '' . due to the flop of the album , judas priest had found themselves financially in dire straits . the band said that there were nights where they were starving and that they did n't know when they were going to get their next meal . they tried to make an agreement with gull to pay them 50 pounds a week , but the record label was also not doing well financially and they did not agree . the album featured the single `` rocka rolla '' .
