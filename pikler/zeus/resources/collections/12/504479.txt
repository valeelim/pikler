Terry Sue-Patt     terence anthony `` terry '' sue-patt ( 29 september 1964 – april/may 2015 ) was a british actor . he was best known for the role of benny green in the bbc television series `` grange hill '' ( 1978–1982 ) . he also appeared in `` jackanory '' . he was born in islington , london to british jamaican parents . sue-patt was found dead on 22 may 2015 in walthamstow , london . he was 50 and is believed to have been dead for about a month .
