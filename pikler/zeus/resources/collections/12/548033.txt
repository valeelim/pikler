El Lebrijano     juan peña fernández ( 8 august 1941 – 13 july 2016 ) , also known as juan peña `` el lebrijano '' or simply el lebrijano , was a spanish flamenco singer . he was born in lebrija , spain . in 1964 , he won the championship at the competition of mairena del alcor , one of the most important flamenco music festivals . he was known for working with famous flamenco musician paco de lucía . his most famous albums were `` casablanca '' , `` open door '' and `` encuentros '' . in 1997 the spanish ministry of culture awarded him the medalla de oro al trabajo ( gold medal working labour ) his most recent song was a tribute to his friend , writer gabriel garcía márquez in 2014. peña died at his home in seville , spain on july 13 , 2016 , aged 75. other websites . - el lebrijano biography at esflamenco.com
