Jack's Big Music Show    jack 's big music show jack 's big music show is a children 's musical television series that is shown on nick jr. it involves a guitarist named jack , his best friend mary , and his dog mel . each of the characters play music together , as well as music guests who are willing to join in . the schwartzman quartet , however , also appear in various episodes . main characters . - jack : the main character of the series . - mary : jack 's best friend . - mel : jack 's dog . - the schwartzman quartet : a look-alike singing group .
