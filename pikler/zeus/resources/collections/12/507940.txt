Chris Christie presidential campaign, 2016    chris christie presidential campaign , 2016 the 2016 presidential campaign of chris christie , the 55th governor of new jersey , began on june 30 , 2015 at an event in his hometown of livingston , new jersey . christie was seen by many as a possible presidential candidate in the 2016 u.s. presidential race . he withdrew on february 10 , 2016. in january 2015 , christie formed a pac to see if running for president was a good idea . the campaign 's headquarters is in trenton , new jersey .
