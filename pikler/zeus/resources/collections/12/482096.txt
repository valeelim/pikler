Umeå BBK     umeå bbk is a basketball club in umeå , sweden . the representative teams play under the name `` udominate basket '' . the club was established in 2012 out of the kfum umeå women 's basketball section `` umeå comets '' . the women 's team debuted in basketligan dam during the 2012-2013 season . they ended up fifth and reached the semifinals . they lost there to the norrköping dolphins . the club also plays in the bwbl . during the 2013-2014 season the men 's team qualified for the swedish basketball league . other websites . - official website
