Andrzej Ciechanowiecki     andrew stanislaus ( andrzej stanisław ) ciechanowiecki ( 28 september 1924 – 2 november 2015 ) was a polish art historian , philanthropist , art collector , and antique dealer . he is the founder of the ciechanowiecki foundation at the royal castle in warsaw ( 1986 ) . he is an honorary professor of the academy of fine arts in warsaw and a member of the polish academy of sciences . he was honored with the order of the white eagle in 1998 , grand cross of polonia restituta , war medals , gloria artis gold medal and other awards . ciechanowiecki died in london at the age of 91 .
