Real Muthaphuckkin G's    real muthaphuckkin g 's `` real muthaphuckkin g 's '' ( also known as `` real compton city g 's '' ) is a song off american rapper eazy-e 's second ep , `` it 's on ( dr. dre ) 187um killa '' . the song was released on august 26 , 1993. the song was a diss song towards former n.w.a member dr. dre and his protégé snoop doggy dogg . on the `` billboard '' charts , the song was able to peak at # 42 on the hot 100 .
