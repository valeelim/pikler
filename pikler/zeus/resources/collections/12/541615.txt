Gene Gutowski     gene gutowski , born as witold bardach , ( july 26 , 1925 in lwów , poland , now ukraine – may 10 , 2016 warsaw ) was a polish-born american movie producer . he produced many of roman polanski 's movies . he was known for producing `` the pianist '' , `` the fearless vampire killers '' , `` cul-de-sac '' . gutowski died of pneumonia in warsaw , poland on may 10 , 2016 , aged 90 .
