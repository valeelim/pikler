Tiffany lamp     a tiffany lamp is a type of lamp with a glass shade . they are made with glass designed by louis comfort tiffany and his design studio , tiffany & co.. the most famous was the stained leaded glass lamp . tiffany lamps are considered part of the art nouveau movement . due to tiffany 's dominant influence on the style , the term 'tiffany lamp ' or 'tiffany style lamp ' often refers to stained leaded glass lamps . even those not made by louis comfort tiffany 's company .
