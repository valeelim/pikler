Seven Days Battles     the seven days battles ( june 25 , 1862 – july 1 , 1862 ) were a series of six major battles fought near richmond , virginia during the american civil war . union army . in early 1862 , major general george b. mcclellan ’ s army of the potomac landed at hampton , virginia and began fighting its way up the virginia peninsula . by mid-may his army of over 100,000 soldiers were on the outskirts of richmond , virginia , the confederate capital . if mcclellan could capture richmond , it could end the war . the city was defended by the confederate army of northern virginia . the two armies had just fought the battle of seven pines in which the confederate general , joseph e. johnston , had just been wounded . jefferson davis appointed general robert e. lee to take over command . the seven days battles proved to be the most decisive engagements of the peninsula campaign .
