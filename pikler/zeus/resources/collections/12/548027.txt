MGM Studios Home Entertainemnt     mgm studios home entertainment is a home video company was created in 2008 by metro-goldwyn-mayer and restructured mgm home entertainment as it 's in-name-only subsidiary . it 's mgm and ua movies , and tv shows are available on dvd and blu-ray . in 2011 ( a year that mgm emerged from bankruptcy ) , mgm studios home entertainment is reincarnated as a joint venture partnership between metro-goldwyn-mayer , amblin partners , studiocanal and bbc . now through 20th century fox home entertainment , mgm handles home entertainment distribution rights to theatrical films and television shows , following the latter 's emergence from bankruptcy and financial troubles . in 2016 , mgm studios home entertainment formed an alliance with universal pictures home entertainment to release films that are produced by amblin partners under the amblin entertainment and dreamworks pictures brands , with mgm handles distribution of the film and television library and universal handles distribution rights to the amblin films , since universal studios bought the minority stake in amblin partners , strengthening the relationship between universal and amblin , and reuniting a minority percentage of the dreamworks pictures label with dreamworks animation .
