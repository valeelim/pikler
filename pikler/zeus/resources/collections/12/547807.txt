Charles Falconer, Baron Falconer of Thoroton    charles falconer , baron falconer of thoroton charles leslie `` charlie '' falconer , baron falconer of thoroton , pc , qc , ( born 19 november 1951 ) is a british labour peer and barrister . falconer became the lord chancellor and the first secretary of state for constitutional affairs in 2003 under prime minister tony blair . he is the first secretary of state for justice in a 2007 reorganization and enlargement of the portfolio of the department for constitutional affairs . he held this role for over a month until gordon brown became prime minister in june 2007. falconer was replaced by jack straw . he was named shadow justice secretary under the acting leadership of harriet harman , and continued in this role after the election of jeremy corbyn as leader of the party , until he resigned on 26 june 2016 .
