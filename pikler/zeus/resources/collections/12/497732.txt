Wild Turkey (bourbon)    wild turkey ( bourbon ) wild turkey is an american brand of kentucky straight bourbon whiskey . wild turkey is bottled and distilled by campari group 's austin nichols division . the first distillery was created in tyrone , kentucky by the ripy brothers in 1869. in 1905 , the ripy brothers distillery was opened by the sons of t.b . ripy . they started distilling again after prohibition ended . in 1940 , the 'wild turkey ' brand name was created when thomas mcarthey , a distillery executive , took a some of the bourbon on his wild turkey hunting trip . on april 8 , 2009 , the brand and distillery were acquired by the campari group . other websites . - official website
