Lake Chungará     lake chungará ( from aymara : `` chunkara '' `` moss on the stone '' ) is a lake in the extreme north of chile , in the altiplano of the arica y parinacota region . it is the 29th highest lake in the world ( and the 10th highest in south america ) . the lake is near the volcanos parinacota ( ) and pomerape ( ) . it is part of the lauca national park , in the putre comuna . the lake is at about to the east of the town of putre and about to the west of the border with bolivia . in the lake and streams next to it there some small fishes that are endemic to the lake basin : a pencil catfish `` trichomycterus chungaraensis '' , and `` orestias chungarensis '' . related pages . - arica y parinacota region
