The Destruction of Sodom and Gomorrah     the destruction of sodom and gomorrah is a painting by john martin ( 1789-1854 ) . martin painted it in 1852. john martin was famous for painting landscapes in the style of romanticism . he painted pictures of the end of the world and events from the bible . `` the destruction of sodom and gomorrah '' was the last painting that martin finished . he completed it in 1852 and sent it to the royal academy . in 1853 he started to paint `` the last judgment '' , `` the great day of his wrath '' , and `` the plains of heaven '' , but he became sick . he died the next year . the painting shows the destruction of the cities of sodom and gomorrah from the bible . the picture shows lot and his two daughters running from the city . lot 's wife looked back and became a pillar of salt . the painting has a red color and a storm in the sky , typical of martin 's work . `` the destruction of sodom and gomorrah '' is in the laing art gallery in newcastle , england .
