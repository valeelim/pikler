Dragan Holcer     dragan holcer ( 19 january 1945 – 23 september 2015 ) was a croatian football defender . he played for fk radnički niš , hnk hajduk split , vfb stuttgart , fc schalke 04 and the yugoslavia national team . holcer was born in a nazi prison camp in zwiesel , bavaria , germany . his mother was austrian-italian and his father was slovenian . holcer died on 23 september 2015 in split , aged 70 .
