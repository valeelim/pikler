Paul Sorvino     paul sorvino ( born april 13 , 1939 ) is an american actor . he often plays authority figures on both sides of the law . he played paulie cicero in the 1990 gangster movie `` goodfellas '' . he also played nypd sergeant phil cerreta in `` law & order '' . he is the father of actress mira sorvino . sorvino was born in brooklyn , new york .
