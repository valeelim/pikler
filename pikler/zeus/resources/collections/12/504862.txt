Twelve Penny Black     twelve penny black is a rare canada stamp . it was issued in 1851. the 12-pence stamp showed the portrait of queen victoria . in 1851 , the province of canada issued the stamps of queen victoria . for the stamp image , the queen 's portrait by alfred edward chalon ( 1780–1860 ) was used . this design is called the `` chalon head '' . the stamps were printed in new york . in fine and unused condition , these stamps are very priced . in february 2006 , a twelve penny black was sold £116,000 . related pages . - penny black - penny lilac - penny red - queen victoria - two penny blue other websites . - canadian stamps ( 1851–1875 ) on the site of library and archives canada .
