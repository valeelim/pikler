Mark Everson     mark whitty everson ( born september 10 , 1954 ) is an american politician . he served as the 46th commissioner of internal revenue from 2003 until 2007. on march 5 , 2015 , everson announced his intention to seek the republican party 's nomination for president of the united states in the 2016 presidential election . he launched his bid with a video and sixteen page open letter in which he laid out six primary issues . he withdrew from the race on november 5 , 2015 .
