Public interest     public interest is a term meaning something that benefits all the people . this is compared to a personal interest that only benefits one person . something that is in the public interest is something that concerns the general public . it is something governments recognize , protect and serve . thomas aquinas stated the purpose of government and the law was to serve the “ common good ” ( ) , meaning the public interest . the term can also be vague in its use . for example , 'public interest ' can be used by governments for the purpose of state secrecy . it can also be misused to mean something that actually serves private interests .
