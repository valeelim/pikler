Pokémon Colosseum     the concept for `` pokémon colosseum '' was influenced by rpgs like `` final fantasy vii '' and `` '' . during an interview with prima games , `` pokémon '' director junichi masuda was asked why the gameplay did not mirror that of the other games in the series . masuda said `` how players communicate with each other has been key to the `` pokémon '' games – it is the backbone of all `` pokémon '' game designs . i feel that the handheld systems work better than the home-based consoles . it 's certainly possible to come up with concepts for home-based consoles , but we might then have to change the core of the game . '' the game was first shown at e3 2003. ign rated the game a 7.5 out of 10. craig harris of ign said that the `` pokémon '' rpg 's move to 3d `` does a decent enough job '' and is `` a bit more linear and straightforward '' . in 2006 , `` nintendo power '' ranked the game as the 121st greatest video game to appear on a nintendo console .
