Institut aéronautique Jean Mermoz     the institut aéronautique jean mermoz ( iajm ) is a french airline pilot school created in 1957. it is in rungis , ile-de-france the school is famous for its aviation books , published in french and also in english since 2016 , thanks to a cooperation with the french civil aviation university . the school is member of the international association of aviation personnel schools and is in partnership with the aerospace university ipsa to offer a double-degree msc in aviation / airline pilot.. further reading . - official
