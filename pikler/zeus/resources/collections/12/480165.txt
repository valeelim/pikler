Sergio Mattarella     sergio mattarella ( ; born 23 july 1941 ) is a italian politician . he became the 12th president of italy on 3 february 2015. he was a member of parliament from 1983 to 2008 , serving as minister of education from 1989 to 1990. he served as minister of defence from 1999 to 2001. later , in 2011 , he became a judge on the constitutional court .
