Bill Farmer     william `` bill '' farmer ( born november 14 , 1952 ) is an american voice actor , impressionist and comedian . he is best known for being the current voice of the disney character goofy , since 1986. he also voices pluto , horace horsecollar and many other disney characters . he was born in pratt , kansas . at age 10 he was already doing voice impressions . while in college he worked in radio and tv . he worked as a stand-up comedian . in late 1986 he was one of hundreds of voice actors competing for a few roles at disney studios . he got the voice acting job for goofy , one of his favorite characters growing up . he has been the voice of goofy ever since . in 2009 farmer was named a disney legend .
