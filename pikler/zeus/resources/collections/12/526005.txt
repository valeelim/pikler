Red Star OS     red star os is the operating system used by computers in north korea . the latest version of the operating system is red star 3 and it looks almost exactly like macos . before red star 3 came out , red star looked more like windows instead of macos . red star os also has its very own web browser called naenara which means `` my country '' in korean . it has a modified version of mozilla firefox and it has the north korean internet called kwangmyong .
