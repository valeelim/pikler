Marco Vicario     marco vicario ( born 20 september 1925 ) is an italian movie actor , screenwriter , movie producer and director . he appeared in 23 movies between 1950 and 1958. he is known for his roles in `` 7 uomini d'oro '' ( 1965 ) , `` il grande colpo dei 7 uomini d'oro '' ( 1966 ) and `` wifemistress '' ( 1977 ) .
