Planomicrobium     planomicrobium is a bacterium . the cells are coccoid or rod shaped . they can move with one or two flagella . they are aerobic , they need oxygen . there are ten species : - `` planomicrobium alkanoclasticum '' - `` planomicrobium chinense '' - `` planomicrobium flavidum '' - `` planomicrobium glaciei '' - `` planomicrobium koreense '' - `` planomicrobium mcmeekinii '' - `` planomicrobium okeanokoites '' - `` planomicrobium psychrophilum '' - `` planomicrobium soli - `` planomicrobium stackebrandtii ''
