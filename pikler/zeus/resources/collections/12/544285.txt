Erika Berger     erika berger ( august 13 , 1939 – may 15 , 2016 ) was a german television presenter and author . she was born in munich , germany . berger worked as television presenter on german television broadcaster rtl . as writer , she wrote several books over sexuality . berger has two children . berger died in cologne , germany on may 15 , 2016 , aged 76. other websites . - spiegel.de : interview : `` worüber soll man denn sonst reden ?
