László Bárczay     lászló bárczay ( 21 february 1936 – 7 april 2016 ) was a hungarian chess grandmaster . in 1966 he was awarded the fide international master title . at the 1966 olympiad in havana , he scored 11/12 ( ten wins and two draws ) as second reserve for the bronze medal winning hungarian team , winning the individual gold medal for sixth board . in 1967 he was awarded the grandmaster title .
