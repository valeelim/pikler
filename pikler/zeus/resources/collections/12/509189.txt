Jim Risch     jim risch ( born may 3 , 1943 ) is the junior united states senator from idaho . he is a member of the u.s. republican party and has been in senate since january 3 , 2009. risch was born on may 3 , 1943 in milwaukee , wisconsin . he graduated from the university of idaho . he is married to vicki risch . they have three children . other websites . - jim risch , u.s. senator for idaho , home page
