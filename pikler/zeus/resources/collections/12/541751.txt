Muhammad Abdul Wahhab     haji muhammad abdul wahhab ( , `` ḥājī muḥammad ‘ abdul-wahhāb '' ; born january 1 , 1923 ) is an islam preacher and the `` ameer '' of tablighi jamaat in pakistan . he played an important role in the tablighi jamaat movement . wahhab was born in delhi , india . he studied at islamia college in lahore . in october 2013 it was reported that the name of haji abdul wahhab was suggested to head a loya jirga in preparation for peace talks with the pakistani taliban .
