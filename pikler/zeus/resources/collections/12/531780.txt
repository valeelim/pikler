New York metropolitan area     the new york metropolitan area is the city and suburbs of new york city . it includes long island and the mid- and lower hudson valley in the state of new york . it also includes north and central new jersey , three counties in western connecticut and five counties in northeastern pennsylvania . the new york metropolitan area is the most populous in the united states . this includes the numbers defined by both the metropolitan statistical area ( 20.1 million residents ) and the combined statistical area ( 23.6 million residents ) . it is also one of the most populous built-up areas in the world , and the single largest in north america .
