String Quartet No. 14 (Beethoven)    string quartet no . 14 ( beethoven ) the string quartet no . 14 in c minor , opus 131 , is a string quartet by ludwig van beethoven . he wrote it between november 1825 and july 1826. it was published in mainz , germany in april 1827 ( after beethoven 's death ) with a dedication to baron joseph von stutterheim . the work consists of seven movements played without a break : 1. adagio ma non troppo e molto espressivo 1. allegro molto vivace 1. allegro moderato 1. andante ma non troppo e molto cantabile 1. presto 1. adagio quasi un poco andante 1. allegro it is about 40 minutes in length .
