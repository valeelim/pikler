Iran men's national volleyball team    iran men 's national volleyball team the iran men 's national volleyball team is the national men 's volleyball team of iran . it is governed by the islamic republic of iran volleyball federation ( i.r.i.v.f. ) . it takes part in international volleyball competitions . they are currently the strongest team in asia . they won the 2011 and 2013 asian volleyball championships . they rank 10th in the fivb world rankings ( as of september 2014 ) . the team 's home venues are azadi indoor stadium and azadi volleyball hall . other websites . - official website - fivb profile
