Valentin Rasputin     valentin grigoriyevich rasputin ( ; 15 march 193714 march 2015 ) was a russian writer . he was born and lived much of his life in the irkutsk oblast in eastern siberia . rasputin 's works show rootless urban characters and the fight for survival of centuries-old traditional rural ways of life . rasputin 's work are about the hard questions of ethics and spiritual revival . rasputin died a day short of his 78th birthday on 14 march 2015 in moscow .
