Öxabäcks IF    öxabäcks if öxabäcks if is a sports club in öxabäck in sweden . it was established in 1931. the women 's soccer section was established in 1966 as one of the earliest women 's soccer teams in sweden . the club adopted the name `` öxabäck/marks if '' in 1991 to show up more of its connections with mark municipality . some years later it adopted the previous name . the women 's soccer team won the swedish national championship in the years of 1972 ( unofficial ) , 1973 , 1975 , 1978 , 1983 , 1987 and 1988. and the swedish cup in the years of 1985 , 1986 , 1987 , 1988 , 1989 and 1991. the club was relegated from damallsvenskan during the 1998 season . the floorball section merged with örby if and berghems if in 1999 to create `` team tygriket 99 '' .team tygriket 99 later became ibk tygriket 99. other websites . - official website
