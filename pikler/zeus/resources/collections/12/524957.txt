Rusty Jones     isham russell `` rusty '' jones ii ( april 13 , 1942 – december 9 , 2015 ) , better known as rusty jones , was an american jazz drummer . he was born in cedar rapids , iowa , but was raised in chicago , illinois . jones was known for performing with marian mcpartland and stéphane grappelli . jones died in chicago , illinois from cancer , aged 73 .
