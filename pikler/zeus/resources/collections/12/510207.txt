Bubba Franks     daniel lamont `` bubba '' franks ( born january 6 , 1978 ) is a former american football tight end . he played nine seasons in the national football league ( nfl ) . he played college football for the university of miami . he was drafted by the green bay packers 14th overall in the 2000 nfl draft . bubba attended big spring high school in big spring , texas . he was an all state tight end there .
