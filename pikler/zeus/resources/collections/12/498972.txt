Philip A. Potter     philip alford potter ( 19 august 1921 – 31 march 2015 ) was a dominican religious leader . he was a leader in the methodist church . he was the third general secretary of the world council of churches ( 1972–1984 ) . potter was born at roseau , dominica , west indies into a christian family with a protestant mother and a catholic father . he died on 31 march 2015 in lübeck , germany , aged 93 .
