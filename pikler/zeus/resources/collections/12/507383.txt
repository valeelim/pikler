Shere Hite     shere hite ( born november 2 , 1942 ) is a german feminist . she is known for her research on women 's orgasms . `` the hite report : a nationwide study of female sexuality '' was published in 1976. the report is hite 's most famous work . hite was born in saint joseph , missouri . she received her masters degree in history from the university of florida in 1967. she then moved to new york city . in 1985 hite married friedrich horicke , a german concert pianist . the couple divorced in 1999 .
