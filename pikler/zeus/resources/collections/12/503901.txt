Roger Mudd     roger mudd ( born february 9 , 1928 ) is an american broadcast journalist . he worked as the primary anchor for the history channel . mudd was the weekend and weekday substitute anchor for the `` cbs evening news '' , the co-anchor of the weekday `` nbc nightly news '' , and the host of the nbc-tv `` meet the press , '' and `` american almanac '' tv programs . mudd is the winner of the peabody award , the joan shorenstein award for distinguished washington reporting , and five emmy awards . other websites . - official web site - `` booknotes '' interview with mudd on `` great minds of history '' , june 6 , 1999 . - c-span `` q & a '' interview with mudd , about `` the place to be : washington , cbs , and the glory days of television news '' , march 30 and april 6 , 2008
