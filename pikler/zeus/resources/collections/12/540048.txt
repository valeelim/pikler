Punggol MRT/LRT Station     punggol mrt/lrt station ( ne17/ptc ) is an mrt and lrt station in singapore . it is an interchange station between north east mrt line and punggol lrt line . these are connected with mrt/lrt stations around the punggol area . the station opened on 20 june 2003. it is the terminus station along the north east mrt line . waterway point is located nearby .
