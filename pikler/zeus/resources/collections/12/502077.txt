Stand by Me (song)    stand by me ( song ) `` stand by me '' is a r & b ballad which was originally performed by ben e. king . the song was inspired by the spiritual single `` lord stand by me '' . the song is on the soundtrack of the 1986 movie `` stand by me '' . musicians who have covered the song include prince royce and john lennon . in 1999 , bmi named it the fourth most performed song of the 20th century .
