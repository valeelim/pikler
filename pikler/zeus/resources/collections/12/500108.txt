ILOVEYOU     iloveyou was a computer worm that first appeared on may 5 , 2000. the worm was sent by email using the subject `` iloveyou '' , with the worm attached . the worm would look like a text file , until it was opened . the file would be called `` love-letter-for-you.txt.vbs '' . because of microsoft windows ' default options , the user would not see the `` .vbs '' extension . most of the files on the computer would be replaced by the worm . depending on what version was run , the payload would be different , because the worm could be changed so easily . the worm was written in visual basic , and used microsoft outlook to spread . it infected over 45 million computers and caused $ 5.5 billion in damages .
