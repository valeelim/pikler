Governor of New Jersey     the office of the governor of new jersey is head of the executive branch of new jersey 's state government . the office of governor is an elected position , for which elected officials serve four-year terms . governors can not be elected to more than two `` consecutive '' terms , but there is no limit on the total number of terms they may serve . the official residence for the governor is drumthwacket , a mansion located in princeton , new jersey ; the office of the governor is at the new jersey state house in trenton . the first governor of new jersey was william livingston , who served from august 31 , 1776 to july 25 , 1790. the current governor is chris christie , who assumed office on january 19 , 2010 , and was elected for his second term on november 5 , 2013 .
