Jadranka Stojaković     jadranka stojaković ( 24 july 1950 – 3 may 2016 ) was a sarajevo-born yugoslav singer-songwriter . she was popular in the former yugoslavia . she was known for her unique voice . her best known hits are `` sve smo mogli mi '' , `` što te nema '' and `` bistre vode bosnom teku '' . stojaković died on 3 may 2016 from motor neurone disease at a nursing home in banjaluka , bosnia and herzegovina , aged 65 .
