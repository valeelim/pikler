TOP 09     top 09 is a political party in the czech republic . it is a right-wing , conservative party . the chairman of the party is former finance minister miroslav kalousek and the honorary chairman is former foreign minister karel schwarzenberg . top 09 have 26 members in the chamber of deputies and 4 members in the european parliament . the party 's policies are based on conservatism and pro-europeanism . other websites . - official website
