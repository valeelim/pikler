Joe Kenda     joseph perry kenda ( born november 14 , 1946 ) is an american television presenter and retired police detective . he is the former colorado springs police department detective who solved nearly 400 murders in his career . he is featured on the investigation discovery television show `` homicide hunter '' , where he recounts stories of homicides he solved . kenda was born in herminie , pennsylvania . he studied at the university of pittsburgh . other websites . - joe kenda at the internet movie database - homicide hunter on investigation discovery
