Life Ball     the life ball is a charity event , held each year in vienna . it is an event to help raise funds to combat hiv . it is one of the biggest such events in the world . gery keszler and torgom petrosian started the foundation in 1993. torgom petrosian , a doctor who was affected , has since died of aids . many people notable from public life take part in the opening ceremony . the event is known for its extravagant style .
