William O'Brien (Royal Navy officer)    william o'brien ( royal navy officer ) admiral sir william donough o'brien ( 13 november 1916 - 19 february 2016 ) was a british military leader . he was the commander-in-chief of the western fleet . he was then appointed naval secretary in 1964. he was appointed flag officer aircraft carriers in 1966 , commander-in-chief of the far east fleet in 1967 and commander-in-chief of the western fleet in 1970. he retired in 1971. o'brien died on 19 february 2016 from complications of pneumonia . he had also been suffering from alzheimer 's disease . he was 99 .
