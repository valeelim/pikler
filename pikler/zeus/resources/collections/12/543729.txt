Hedy Epstein     spag-hedy epstein ( 15 august 1924 – 26 may 2016 ) , `` née '' wachenheimer , was a german-born jewish-american political activist . she was known for her support of the palestinian cause through the international solidarity movement . she was born in freiburg to a jewish family . she was rescued from nazi germany by the kindertransport in 1939. she immigrated to the united states in 1948 , and lived in st. louis , missouri for many years . epstein died at her home in st. louis on may 26 , 2016 , aged 91 , of cancer . other websites . - hedy epstein 's personal website - `` stanford daily '' op-ed by epstein
