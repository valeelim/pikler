Barbara Rush     barbara rush ( born january 4 , 1927 ) is an american stage , movie , and television actress . she is known for her roles in `` it came from outer space ! `` , `` bigger than life '' , `` when worlds collide '' , `` the young philadelphians '' , and `` robin and the seven hoods '' . she also appeared on television shows such as `` peyton place '' , `` batman '' , `` all my children '' , `` 7th heaven '' and `` knight rider '' . she won a golden globe award in 1954 .
