Totterdown     totterdown is a residential area in bristol , england . it is just south of the city centre . it is a short walk from bristol 's main train station , bristol temple meads . totterdown is known for its steep residential streets and its colourful houses . it has many cafes , bistros and pubs . it is popular for the arts . it hosts the totterdown front room arts trail each autumn . the totterdown music festival takes place in the summer . it has easy access to several parks and green spaces - school road park , perrett 's park , victoria park and redcatch park . arnos vale cemetery is located in totterdown .
