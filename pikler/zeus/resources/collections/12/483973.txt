A La Carte Communications     a la carte communications is a television production company founded in 1990 by natan katzman and geoffrey drummond . a la carte produced jeff smith 's `` the frugal gourmet '' cooking show . other shows produced by the company included `` julia child : cooking with master chefs '' and `` in julia 's kitchen with master chefs '' . a la carte and maryland public television co-produced the emmy award-winning series `` baking with julia '' .
