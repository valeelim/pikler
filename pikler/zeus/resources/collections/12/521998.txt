Cortisone     cortisone is a 21-carbon steroid hormone . its chemical name is 17-hydroxy-11-dehydrocorticosterone . it is one of the main hormones released by the adrenal gland in response to stress . in chemical structure , it is closely related to cortisol . it is used to treat a variety of ailments and can be administered in various ways . cortisone suppresses the immune system . this reduces inflammation , pain and swelling at the site of the injury . long-term use of cortisone has risks . tadeus reichstein , e.c . kendall and p.s . hench were awarded the nobel prize in physiology or medicine in 1950 for their discovery of cortisone .
