Jaime Nebot     jaime josé nebot saadi ( born october 22 , 1946 ) is an ecuadorian politician . he serves as mayor of guayaquil , which is ecuador 's largest city . although he is a member of the social christian party , he has distanced himself from it and now is more on running the city . nebot twice ran unsuccessfully for president of ecuador . he is considered as the protégé of former president león febres-cordero .
