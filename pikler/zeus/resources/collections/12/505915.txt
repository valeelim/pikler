Adria Airways     adria airways is the national airline of slovenia . it began in march 1961. in 1969 , it bought its first jet aircraft , the douglas dc-9 . in december 1981 , an adria airplane crashed in corsica . the crash killed 180 people . adria bought its first airbus a320 in 1989. as of june 2015 , adria has 12 airplanes .
