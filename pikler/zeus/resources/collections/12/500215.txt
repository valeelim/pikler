Stanislav Gross     stanislav gross ( 30 october 1969 – 16 april 2015 ) was a czech lawyer and politician . he served as the prime minister of the czech republic from 4 august 2004 to 25 april 2005. he was a member of the czech social democratic party ( čssd ) , which he lead from 26 june 2004 to 26 april 2005. gross was born in prague . he was married to šárka grossová and had two daughters . he was a christian . gross died from amyotrophic lateral sclerosis ( als ) on 16 april 2015 in prague , aged 45 .
