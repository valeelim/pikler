Dobri Dobrev     dobri dimitrov dobrev ( ) ( born 20 july 1914 ) , better known as grandpa dobri , elder dobri ( ) , or the saint of bailovo is a bulgarian philanthropist . he walks several kilometers each day to sit or stand in front of the cathedral of alexander nevsky in sofia to collect money for charitable causes . dobrev donates all the money he collects to charities , orphanages , churches and monasteries . he turned 100 in july 2014 .
