Usnija Redžepova     usnija redžepova ( ; 4 february 1946 – 1 october 2015 ) was a macedonian actress and singer . she appeared in the movies `` zenidba nosaca samuela '' ( 1973 ) , `` the dervish and death '' ( 1974 ) and `` kostana '' ( 1980 ) . she was given a national pension from the serbian government on 29 december 2011. redžepova was born in skopje . she was of romani and turkish ancestry . she died from lung cancer in belgrade , aged 69 .
