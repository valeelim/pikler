2015 Baltimore riots     the 2015 baltimore riots were riots that began on april 25 , 2015 in baltimore , maryland . the riots happened in response to the death of an african american man named freddie gray . gray was 25 years old . he lived in baltimore . he had been arrested for having a switchblade . he was in good health when he was arrested . later , he had injuries to his spine and larynx . he fell into a coma on april 12. he died the following week . over 200 people were arrested . a state of emergency was declared in baltimore . the city was later put under curfew .
