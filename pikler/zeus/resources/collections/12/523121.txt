Heinz Oberhummer     heinz oberhummer ( 19 may 1941 – 24 november 2015 ) was an austrian physicist and skeptic . he was born in bischofshofen and raised in obertauern , austria . oberhummer was professor emeritus of theoretical physics at the atominstitut of the vienna university of technology . his main research area was nucleosynthesis . oberhummer died of pneumonia in vienna , austria , aged 74. other websites . - articles of heinz oberhummer in arxiv.org - kann das alles zufall sein ? - geheimnisvolles universum , ecowin-verlag , 2008 ; award for `` best popular science book in austria 2009 '' - science busters - cinema and science ( cisci ) - neue erkenntnisse zur entstehung der grundlagen für leben
