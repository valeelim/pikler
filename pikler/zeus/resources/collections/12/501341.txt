Bill Valentine     william terry `` bill '' valentine , jr. ( november 21 , 1932 - april 26 , 2015 ) was an american professional baseball umpire . he worked in the american league from 1963 to 1968. valentine was an umpire in the 1965 major league baseball all-star game . in his career , he umpired 947 major league games . valentine died on april 26 , 2015 in little rock , arkansas , aged 82 .
