Carol Burns     carol burns ( 22 february 1947 – 21 december 2015 ) was an australian actress . she was born in brisbane , queensland . she worked in australia and in the united kingdom . she began her career in 1967. burns was known for her role as franky doyle in the soap opera `` prisoner '' . burns died of cancer at her home in brisbane at the age of 68. other websites . - queensland theatre company – the glass menagerie
