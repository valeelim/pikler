Mil Mi-8     the mil mi-8 is one of the most famous helicopter built in ussr during the cold war . it 's used for transport and attack by more than 50 countries in the world . it 's the basis of mil mi-14 and mil mi-17 helicopters . bibliography . - jackson , robert , `` helicopters : military , civilian , and rescue rotorcraft '' ( the aviation factfile ) . london : grange books ltd , 2005. isbn 1-84013-812-2 . - gaillard , pierre , `` avions et hélicoptères militaires d'aujourd'hui '' paris , france : larivière éditions , 1999. isbn 2-907051-24-5 .
