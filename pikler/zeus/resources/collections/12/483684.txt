Legislative Assembly of Ontario     the legislative assembly of ontario is the legislature of the province of ontario , canada . it has the second largest number of members out of the provincial legislatures of canada . it meets at the ontario legislative building in toronto , the provincial capital . the assembly was created in 1867 by the british north america act . the assembly is unicameral , meaning there is no upper house . there are 107 members of the assembly . each member is elected from one electoral district . the party with the most members gets to choose the premier of ontario , who leads the government , and the executive council of ontario , which is the cabinet . the leader of the second largest party in the assembly becomes the leader of the opposition .
