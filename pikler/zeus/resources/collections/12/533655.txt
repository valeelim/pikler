Title IX     title ix ( pronounced `` title nine '' ) is a portion of the united states education amendments of 1972. it was co-authored and introduced by senator birch bayh . it was renamed the patsy mink equal opportunity in education act in 2002 , after its late house co-author and sponsor . title ix is a federal law that makes it illegal to discriminate against a person on the basis of sex in any federally funded activity . but even after title ix was passed by congress , women 's athletic and academic opportunities in american schools still did not equal men 's programs .
