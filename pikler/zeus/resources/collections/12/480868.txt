IFK Jönköping     ifk jönköping was a sports club in the town of jönköping in sweden . its women 's soccer team played in the swedish second division during the 1990 's . the club was established in 1989 out of ik tord 's women 's soccer section . the club won the women 's småland district championship in the years of 1994 , 1995 and 1997. the women 's soccer team was dissolved before the 1997. the activity was then transferred over to ifk öxnehaga .
