Nut (hardware)    nut ( hardware ) a nut is a type of fastener with a threaded hole . nuts are almost always used with a mating bolt to fasten parts together . thus `` nut and bolt '' or `` nuts and bolts '' are term which recognise their pairing . like bolt heads , their shape is usually hexagonal , because this is best for gripping with tools . nuts are graded with strength ratings like their respective bolts . for example , an iso property class 10 nut can support the bolt proof strength load of an iso property class 10.9 bolt without stripping its thread ( the inside spiral groove ) .
