José Eduardo Dutra     josé eduardo dutra ( 11 april 1957 – 4 october 2015 ) was a brazilian businessman and politician . he was born in rio de janeiro . he joined petrobras in 1983 , and served as ceo from 2003 to 2005. he returned to the company in 2012 , after a two year stint as the president of the workers ' party . he was elected to the federal senate in 1994 and served until his death in 2015. dutra died of cancer in belo horizonte , aged 58 .
