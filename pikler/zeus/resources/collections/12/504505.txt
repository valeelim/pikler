Reversal of Fortune     reversal of fortune is a 1990 drama movie . it was adapted off a 1985 novel . jeremy irons acts as claus . glenn close plays sunny . the movie is based on the true story of the unexplained coma of socialite sunny von bülow . it is about the attempted murder trial and the acquittal of her husband claus von bülow . the movie was released on october 17 , 1990 in the united states . its reviews were mostly positive .
