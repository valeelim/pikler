Kurup     ottaplakkal neelakandan velu kurup ( 27 may 1931 â€“ 13 february 2016 ) , popularly known as o. n. v. kurup or simply o. n. v. , was a indian poet and lyricist . he won jnanpith award , the highest literary award in india in 2007. he was born in chavara , kollam . kurup wrote lyrics for music used in malayalam cinema . he has written a lot of lyrics for dramas and television serials also . he received the awards padma shri in 1998 and padma vibhushan in 2011 , the fourth and second highest civilian honours from the government of india . in 2007 he was given an honorary doctorate by university of kerala , trivandrum . o. n. v. was known for his leftist political views . he was the left democratic front ( ldf ) candidate in the thiruvananthapuram constituency for the lok sabha elections in 1989. kurup died of respiratory failure on 13 february 2016 in trivandrum , kerala . he was aged 84 .
