Västerås BK30     västerås bk30 is a sports club in the town of västerås in sweden . it was established on 29 november 1929 as a merger out of ik city and ik sture . it was named after 1930 . 1930 was the year it joined the swedish sports confederation . the club nowadays mostly runs soccer . it earlier also ran bandy , handball , ice hockey , table tennis and track and field athletics . the soccer activity has mostly used ringvallens ip as home ground . a women 's soccer team was established in 1969. the women 's soccer team played in the swedish top division in 1994. bandy was a major sport up to the mid 1980s . the club played in the swedish top division in 1932 and 1936. other websites . - official website
