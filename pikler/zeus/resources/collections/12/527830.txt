The Walk (movie)    the walk ( movie ) the walk is a 2015 american 3d biographical drama movie directed by robert zemeckis and written by christopher browne and zemeckis . it is based on the story of french high-wire artist philippe petit 's walk between the world trade center on august 7 , 1974. the movie stars joseph gordon-levitt , ben kingsley , charlotte le bon , james badge dale , ben schwartz , and steve valentine . the movie was released by tristar pictures on september 30 , 2015 , in the united states in imax 3d , and on october 9 in regular 2d and 3d . the film was dedicated to the victims of the september 11 attacks in 2001 .
