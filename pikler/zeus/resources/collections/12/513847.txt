Aleksander Mandziara     aleksander mandziara ( 16 august 1940 – 2 september 2015 ) , also known as alfons mandziara , was a polish football player and coach . he played for łts łabędy , flocie gdynia , szombierki bytom , nac breda , and gks tychy . he managed gks tychy , pogoń szczecin , rot-weiss essen , bsc young boys , fc biel/bienne , lask linz , fc stahl linz , sv darmstadt 98 , and fc bern . he was born in gliwice . mandziara died on 2 september 2015 , aged 75 .
