Cheshmeh-Ali (Rey, Iran)    cheshmeh-ali ( rey , iran ) cheshmeh-ali ( meaning : ali 's spring ) is a natural and swimming spring in rey in iran . the spring is located in south of tehran . cheshmeh-ali is run from a big rock and flown to south-east . cheshmeh-ali dates back 8000 years ago and is placed under rey castle . inscription of fath ali shah is between rey castle and cheshmeh-ali from top to down . resources . - čašma ( -ye ) ʿalī `` iranicaonline.org ''
