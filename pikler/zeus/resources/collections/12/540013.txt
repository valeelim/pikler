Jenny Diski     jenny diski frsl ( née simmonds ; 8 july 1947 – 28 april 2016 ) was an english writer . she was known for her works `` nothing natural '' and `` rainforest '' . diski was born in london . she lived with author doris lessing for four years after being abused by her parents . diski studied at university college london . she worked as a teacher during the 1970s and early 1980s . diski was a regular contributor to the `` london review of books '' ; the collections `` do n't '' and `` a view from the bed '' . she won the 2003 thomas cook travel book award for `` stranger on a train : daydreaming and smoking around america with interruptions '' . in june 2014 , diski was told that she had at three years to live . in september 2014 , she revealed that she had been diagnosed with lung cancer . she died on 28 april 2016 in london from the disease , aged 68 .
