The Amazing Criswell     jeron criswell king ( august 18 , 1907 – october 4 , 1982 ) , born jeron criswell konig , and known by his stage-name the amazing criswell , was an american psychic known for making wrong predictions . in person , he went by charles criswell king , and was sometimes credited as jeron king criswell . he was born in princeton , indiana . criswell was a friend of director ed wood and narrated in his famous movie `` plan 9 from outer space '' . criswell died of cardiac arrest in burbank , california , aged 75. other websites . - `` criswell predicts '' — `` the onion '' a.v . club , august 11 , 1999 - photos of criswell 's grave at findagrave - `` the amazing criswell '' tribute page
