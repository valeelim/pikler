Bayfront MRT Station     bayfront mrt station ( ce1/dt16 ) mrt station in singapore . it is an interchange station between circle mrt line and downtown mrt line . bayfront was the third station with cross platform interchange . timeline . - 14 january 2012 : station opened along circle line , under circle line extension - 22 december 2013 : downtown line opened platform directions . - platform a : towards chinatown ( dt19/ne4 ) - platform b : towards marina bay ( ce2/ns27/te20 ) - platform c : towards bukit panjang ( dt1/bp6 ) - platform d : towards stadium ( cc6 ) /harbourfront ( cc29/ne1 )
