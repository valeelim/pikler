Gloria Jones     gloria jones ( born october 19 , 1945 ) is an american singer and songwriter . she recorded the 1965 song `` tainted love '' . she also had other songs . on september 16 , 1977 , jones was driving a car . marc bolan of the group t. rex was a passenger . the car crashed and killed bolan . jones was born in cincinnati , ohio . she later moved to los angeles , california .
