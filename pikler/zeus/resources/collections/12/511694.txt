Evan Prodromou     evan prodromou ( born 14 october 1948 ) is an american software developer and open source advocate . his major contributions have been wikitravel ( with his wife michele ann jenkins ) , identi.ca and statusnet . he heads a social media software called pump.io . prodromou was born in cincinnati , ohio . he was raised in california . he has lived in amsterdam , san francisco , and lisbon . he currently lives in montreal . in 1990 , he graduated from university of california , berkeley . he worked for microsoft and many website development companies . in 2003 , he started wikitravel , a travel guide similar to wikivoyage . other websites . - official website
