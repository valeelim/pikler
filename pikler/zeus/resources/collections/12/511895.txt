Jacob Bekenstein     jacob david bekenstein ( hebrew : יעקב בקנשטיין ; may 1 , 1947 – august 16 , 2015 ) was a mexican-born israeli-american theoretical physicist . he made fundamental contributions to the foundation of black hole thermodynamics and to other aspects of the connections between information and gravitation . he was of jewish descent . other websites . - bekenstein 's papers list at arxiv with links to the full papers - israel prize official site - cv of jacob bekenstein
