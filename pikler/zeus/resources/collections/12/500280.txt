Juan Carlos Cáceres     juan carlos cáceres ( 4 september 1936 – 5 april 2015 ) was an argentine jazz and tango musician , singer-songwriter , and painter . he began his career in 1977 and created `` tango negro '' . for most of his career , he worked and lived in france . he released 12 albums between 1993 and 2011. cáceres was born in buenos aires . he died from cancer on 5 april 2015 in perigny , near paris , aged 78 .
