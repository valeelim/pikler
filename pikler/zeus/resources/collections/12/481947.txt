Bengt Ohlson     bengt ohlson , born 10 march 1943 , is a swedish former ice hockey player and manager . he acted as the head coach for the swedish national team during the 1980-1981 season . during that season sweden won the silver medal during the 1981 iihf world championship . otherwise that season had been less successful for the swedes . he resigned after being threatened to death . when sweden won the silver medal he became popular known as `` silverfisken '' by the swedes . he had been called `` torsken '' after the swedish fiasco during the 1980 izvestia tournament . other websites . - eliteprospects
