Rutger Gunnarsson     rutger gunnarsson ( 12 february 1946 – 8 may 2015 ) was a swedish musician , bass guitarist , arranger and record producer . he was best known as a bassist for abba on tours and their albums , serving as a kapellmeister . he also worked with artists including celine dion , westlife , elton john , adam ant , bobbysocks , gwen stefani , elin lanto , joyride and alla pugacheva . gunnarsson was born in linköping municipality , östergötland county . he died in stockholm , aged 69 .
