Jeep Renegade     the jeep renegade is a subcompact crossover suv . it is built in melfi , italy on the same assembly line as the fiat 500x , which it shares its platform with . the renegade started production in 2014 as a 2015 model . it is the first jeep model built only outside of north america . it is sold in south africa , europe , north america , and brazil . in the us , the renegade is available in the following trims : sport , latitude , limited , and trailhawk . all trims except trailhawk are available with front and four-wheel-drive . the trailhawk features four-wheel-drive as standard . the renegade has two four-wheel drive systems : active drive i and active drive low . both are mated to jeep 's selec-terrain system . in the us , the renegade comes powered by the following engines : - 1.4 l inline-4 multiair turbo with mated to a 6-speed manual transmission - 2.4 l inline-4 tigershark multiair2 with mated to a 9-speed automatic transmission other websites . - official jeep renegade site - latest jeep renegade overview & pictures
