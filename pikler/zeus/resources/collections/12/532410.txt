Ludwig     ludwig is german name . it can be a given name for males or a surname . it means `` famous warrior '' . - ludwig mies van der rohe , a german american architect - ludwig von mises , an austrian economist - ludwig i of bavaria , king of bavaria ( 1825–1848 ) - ludwig ii of bavaria , king of bavaria ( 1864–1886 ) - ludwig iii of bavaria , last king of bavaria ( 1913–1918 ) - ludwig van beethoven ( 1770-1827 ) , german composer and pianist - ludwig wittgenstein , austrian philosopher - ludwig erhard , a west german chancellor - ludwig beck , a general in nazi germany - ludwig leichhardt , a prussian explorer - alexander ludwig ( born 1992 ) , canadian actor
