Harry Rowohlt     harry rowohlt ( 27 march 1945 – 15 june 2015 ) was a german writer and translator . he was born in hamburg . he also played the role of a derelict in the famous german weekly-soap lindenstraße . in 1999 he was awarded the johann-heinrich-voß-preis für übersetzung . and in 2005 , he received the deutscher jugendliteraturpreis for his translation work . he died of lung cancer in hamburg .
