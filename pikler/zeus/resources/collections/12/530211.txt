University of North London     the university of north london was a university in london , england , from 1992 to 2002. it was merged with london guildhall university to form london metropolitan university . its former campus are now the university 's north campus , on holloway road and highbury grove , islington . in 1996 , the university celebrated its centenary year . other websites . - internet archive ( university of north london , 17 january 2004 )
