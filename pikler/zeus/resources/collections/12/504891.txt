Kenneth Bloomfield     sir kenneth percy bloomfield ( born 15 april 1931 ) is an irish politician . he was the former head of the northern ireland civil service . he was later a member of the independent commission for the location of victims ' remains and for a time northern ireland victims commissioner . in addition to this , he has held a variety of public sector posts in northern ireland and elsewhere . bloomfield was born in belfast , northern ireland . other websites . - independent commission for the location of victims remains official website
