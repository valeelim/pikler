Cyclone Pam     cyclone pam is a 2015 pacific ocean tropical cyclone . the storm caused major devastation in vanuatu . it is the second most intense storm according to pressure , after cyclone zoe in 2002. the storm has killed many people and caused heavy damage . pam formed march 6 east of the solomon islands . it reached category 5 on saffir-simpson scale . it caused damaging storm surges in certain areas .
