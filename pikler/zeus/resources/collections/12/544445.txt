Maratha dynasty     maratha dynasty is one of the dynasties that was against the mughal rule during the reign of aurangzeb . it was founded by the great emperor chhatrapati shivaji in the 17th century . this maratha dynastywas the biggest enemy for the mughal emperor aurangzeb . shivaji is recognized as one of the heroes on the side of maharashtra . after shivaji 's death his son sambhaji then his children 's fought against the mughals . this led to the decline of the mughal empire in the later 18th century . the maratha dynasty in the 18th to 19th century ruled from maharashtra , gujarat in the west to orissa in the east and from delhi in the north to karnataka in the south .
