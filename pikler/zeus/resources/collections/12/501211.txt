Uherský Brod shooting     on 24 february 2015 , a mass shooting happened at the restaurant in the town of uherský brod , czech republic . the gunman , 63-year-old zdeněk kovář , killed eight people and injured another . he was in the restaurant for around two hours ( 12:30 p.m.–14:23 p.m. ) before the tactical team went into the building . after they entered the building , the gunman committed suicide ( killed himself ) .
