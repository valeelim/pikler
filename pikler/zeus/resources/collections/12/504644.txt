In Cold Blood     in cold blood is a 1967 american crime-drama movie . the movie is based on the novel of the same name . about richard hickock and perry smith . in 1959 , the two men break into a kansas home and kill four people . they drive to las vegas , then to mexico . they are later caught by police , tried for the murders , then executed . robert blake plays perry smith . scott wilson plays richard hickock . the movie was released on 14 december 1967. it was officially rated r by the mpaa for violence .
