Eerste Divisie     the eerste divisie ( english : first division ) is an association football league in the netherlands . it is the second-highest league in the country . when a team wins the league , they get promoted to the eredivisie . until 2015 , when a team did bad in the league , they would get sent to the topklasse . recently , the team would get sent to the tweede klasse instead . the league has been around since 1956. other websites . - official website
