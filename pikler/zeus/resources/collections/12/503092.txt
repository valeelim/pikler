The Pale Emperor     the pale emperor is the ninth studio album by american rock band , marilyn manson . `` the pale emperor '' was released on january 15 , 2015 through hell , etc.. manson dedicated the album to his late mother , barbara warner , who died on may 13 , 2014 after an eight-year battle with alzheimer 's disease and dementia . the album peaked at # 8 on the `` billboard '' 200 , # 6 on the top album sales , # 3 on the top rock albums , # 3 on the top alternative albums , and at # 1 on the top hard rock albums .
