Janusz Bolonek     januariusz mikołaj `` janusz '' bolonek ( 6 december 1938 – 2 march 2016 ) was a polish roman catholic bishop . he was born in huta dłutowska , poland . he worked in diplomacy for the pope . among other positions , has served as nuntius in uruguay , bulgaria , romania , and in the republic of macedonia . bolonek was made a priest in 1961 in łódź , poland . he retired as apostolic nuncio in 2013. he died in łódź on 2 march 2016 of cancer . he was aged 77 .
