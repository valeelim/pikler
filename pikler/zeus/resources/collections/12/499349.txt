Jeralean Talley     jeralean talley ( née kurtz ; may 23 , 1899 – june 17 , 2015 ) was an american supercentenarian who was , at the age of 116 years , 25 days , the verified world 's oldest living person . she was previously thought to be the oldest living american , from the death of elsie thompson on march 21 , 2013 until gertrude weaver was verified to be older in july 2014. upon weaver 's death on april 6 , 2015 , talley became the world 's oldest living person evidence of their age .
