Poe Dameron     poe dameron is a fictional character in the `` star wars '' franchise . he was introduced in the 2015 movie `` '' . he was played by oscar isaac . poe is an x-wing fighter pilot for the resistance who brings renegade stormtrooper finn and jakku scavenger rey into the fight against—and eventually a victory over—the sinister first order . he is the master of the loving android bb-8 . he is featured in `` the force awakens '' media and merchandising , and will appear in the movie 's forthcoming sequel , `` '' . isaac and the character have received positive reviews , with poe being compared to the characterization of han solo ( harrison ford ) in the original `` star wars '' movie trilogy . other websites . - poe dameron in the official starwars.com encyclopedia
