Armagnac (brandy)    armagnac ( brandy ) armagnac ( ; ) is a kind of brandy produced in the armagnac region of france . the region lies between the adour and garonne rivers in the foothills of the pyrenees . the area contains of grape-producing vines . it was given an aoc in 1936. column stills rather than pot stills are used in the production of armagnac . it is then aged in oak barrels . armagnac is the oldest brandy distilled in france . in the past was consumed for its therapeutic benefits . in the 14th century , prior vital du four , a cardinal , claimed it had 40 virtues :
