Snapchat     snapchat is a photo sharing app . using the app , people can take pictures , add a text or drawing and send them to a list of contacts . the service started in september 2011. at first it was intended to send selfies that would disappear quickly but people learned how to save the pictures . as of june 24th , 2017 snapchat ceo , has stated that the service was under attack apparently hackers have been logging in and changing users passwords and stealing photos . users are advised to update the app , and change their passwords . the '' for your eyes '' only section of the `` memories '' feature is not at risk , however the password data may be leaked to prying eyes .
