Laser pointer     a laser pointer ( or laser pen ) is a small device that shines a low-powered laser light . the pointer is usually powered by a battery . it contains a laser diode that emits a very narrow laser beam of visible light . when pointed at an object , it shines a small bright spot of colored light . the pointer is held in a hand . it is used to highlight something of interest . power is restricted in most jurisdictions not to exceed 5 milliwatts .
