Eustace Mullins     eustace mullins ( 1923-2010 ) was a right-wing political activist . he wrote many biographies and history books . the best known is `` secrets of the federal reserve . '' he also wrote books opposing the one world government movement . david randall calls him `` one of the world 's leading conspiracy theorists '' . he was born in roanoke , virginia . he died of a stroke in hockley , texas .
