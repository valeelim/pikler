Frank Kelly     frank kelly ( né francis o'kelly ; 28 december 1938 – 28 february 2016 ) was an irish actor , singer and writer . he worked in television , radio , theatre , music , screenwriting and movies . he played father jack hackett in the channel four sitcom `` father ted '' . he also played dermont in `` emmerdale '' . kelly died on 28 february 2016 from a heart attack . the heart attack was a complication of parkinson 's disease . he was 77. he was recovering from bowel cancer and survived skin cancer .
