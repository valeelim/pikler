Flora MacNeil     flora macneil , mbe ( 1928 – 16 may 2015 ) was a scottish gaelic singer . macneil was born in 1928 on the island of barra . she was known for signing `` mo run geal og '' ( “ my fair young love ” ) , one of the greatest of the `` orain mor '' , or “ big songs ” . she was known as the `` queen of gaelic singers '' . macneil died after a short-illness , aged 86 .
