Hezar Dastan     hezar dastan or hezār dastān ( ) was a very popular iranian television series that ran from 1979 through 1987. it is considered toto be one of the greatest television series in iran . it is set in a fictional town in iran called `` hezar dastan '' . it was directed by ali hatami . it stars ezzatolah entezami , jamshid mashayekhi , mohammad-ali keshavarz and ali nassirian . other websites . - hezar dastan at the internet movie database
