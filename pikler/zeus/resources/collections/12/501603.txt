Princess Gabriella, Countess of Carladès    princess gabriella , countess of carladès princess gabriella of monaco , countess of carladès ( `` gabriella thérèse marie '' ; born 10 december 2014 ) , is the twin daughter of prince albert ii and princess charlene . she is second in line to the throne of monaco . she was born at the princess grace hospital centre in monaco . she was born two minutes before her twin brother , hereditary prince jacques . she was granted the title countess of carladès by her father . titles and styles . - `` '' her serene highness '' the countess of carladès '' .
