Imre Pozsgay     imre andrás pozsgay ( 26 november 1933 – 25 march 2016 ) was a hungarian communist politician . he played a key role in hungary 's fight for democracy after 1988. he served as minister of culture ( 1976–1980 ) , minister of education ( 1980–1982 ) and minister of state ( 1988–1990 ) . he was also a member of parliament from 1983 to 1994 .
