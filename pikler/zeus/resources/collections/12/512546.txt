Hard Core 2     hard core 2 ( also known as hard core 2k14 and hard core ) is the third mixtape by american rapper lil ' kim ; it was released independently by kim on her international rock star ( i.r.s . ) label ; which was originally called `` queen bee entertainment '' before the release of 2011 's `` black friday mixtape . '' it is the first mixtape that kim released after giving birth to her daughter royal reign on 9 june 2014. the name of the mixtape pays homage to kim 's debut album `` hard core '' which was released in 1996. the mixtape also includes two songs that are diss tracks aimed at nicki minaj ; a reggae type song called `` dead gal walking '' and a hip hop and r & b sounding song called `` identity theft '' .
