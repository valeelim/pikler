Atari, Inc.    atari , inc. atari , inc. is an american video game and home computer company . it was founded in 1972 by nolan bushnell and ted dabney . atari was the first company to make a video arcade game in a cabinet . the company made famous games , such as pong . atari helped define the gaming entertainment industry and home computers from the 1970s to the mid-1980s . they made home computers until 1993. other websites . - http : //atari.com
