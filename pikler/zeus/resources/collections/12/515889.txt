Unwell     unwell is the second single from matchbox twenty 's third album , `` more than you think you are '' . it was written by matchbox twenty lead singer rob thomas . it was very successful on the radio , and spent 20 weeks at the top of the u.s. adult top 40 charts . the song is about a person who claims to not be crazy even though they 're suffering from various occurrences that could lead them to be labeled as such . rob thomas states on the live dvd `` show '' that he wrote the song to be a metaphor for humanity in general , a song for people who are `` fucked up and feel alone like that . we all feel a little fucked up sometimes ... you 're not alone . '' this song is a staple of adult contemporary stations even years after its release . the song also was the second most played song in the united states for 2003 according to billboard .
