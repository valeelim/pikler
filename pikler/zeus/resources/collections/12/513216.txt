Nödinge Church     nödinge church ( ) is a church building in nödinge-nol in västergötland in sweden . it is located north of the town of gothenburg . the current church was built in 1727 when it replaced an older romanesque church from the 1100s or 1200s . the church has a baroque interior . its ceiling has paintings by painter alexander fox from 1734. the pulpit was added in 1741 by sculptor johan petter weber . the church underwent renovation in 1981-1982. the church was also used during recording of the 2013 film `` 'the anderssons hit the road '' .
