Aaron Ekblad     aaron ekblad ( born february 7 , 1996 ) is a canadian professional ice hockey defenceman . he currently plays for the florida panthers of the national hockey league ( nhl ) . he has also played for the team canada junior team . he won a gold medal with them during the 2015 world junior ice hockey championships . he suffered a concussion in an exhibition game against the czech republic in december 2014. before playing in the nhl , ekblad played 3 seasons with the barrie colts of the ontario hockey league ( ohl ) . he was drafted with the 1st overall pick by the florida panthers in the 2014 nhl entry draft . on september 3 , the panthers signed ekblad to a three-year , entry-level contract . ekblad made the panthers ' opening night roster for the 2014–15 season . he made his nhl debut on october 9 in a game against the tampa bay lightning . during the game , ekblad scored his first career nhl point , an assist on a jonathan huberdeau goal in a 3–2 overtime loss . on november 1 , he scored his first nhl goal against steve mason of the philadelphia flyers in a 2–1 panthers win .
