David Ellefson     david warren `` dave '' ellefson ( born on 12 november 1964 in jackson , minnesota , united states ) is an american born bassist who currently plays for the band megadeth . along with dave mustaine , he is one of the founding members of the band . he currently lives in scottsdale , arizona . he has also played with soulfly , avian , temple of brutality , f5 , killing machine , ministry , hail ! and altitudes & attitudes . other websites . - official website - david ellefson on loudwire.com
