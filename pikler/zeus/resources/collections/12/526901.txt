Roland Alpha Juno     roland alpha juno is a series of synthesizer keyboards . they were made by the roland corporation from 1985 to 1986. two main models were released : the alpha juno 1 ( ju-1 ) ( 1985–1986 ) and the alpha juno 2 ( ju-2 ) ( 1985–1986 ) . the ju-2 added one octave of notes and a slot for cartridges .
