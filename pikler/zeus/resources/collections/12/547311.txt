Kuk (mythology)    kuk ( mythology ) kuk ( also spelled as kek or keku ) is basically the ancient egyptian deity of darkness . as a concept , kuk was viewed as having male and female qualities . his female form was known as kauket ( also spelled as keket ) , which is simply the female form of the word `` kuk '' . `` kuk '' 's male form was seen as a frog , or as a frog-headed man . the female form was seen as a snake , or a snake-headed woman . he also symbolized the unknown and chaos .
