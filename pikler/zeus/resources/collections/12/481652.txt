University of the Humanities     the university of the humanities is a private university in ulaanbaatar , mongolia . the mongolian name is хүмүүнлэгийн ухааны их сургууль . it has undergraduate and graduate programs . fields of study include foreign languages , translation , education , journalism , computer science , economics and business . the school is in downtown ulaanbaatar at grand chinggis khaan square . the university is accredited by the mongolian national council for education accreditation . other websites . - in english
