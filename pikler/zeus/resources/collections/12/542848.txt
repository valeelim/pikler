Boris Schnaiderman     boris solomonovitch schnaiderman ( russian борис соломонович шнайдерман ; 17 may 1917 – 18 may 2016 ) was a brazilian translator , writer and essayist . he was of jewish descent . he became a naturalized citizen of brazil in 1941 and fought in world war ii with the brazilian expeditionary force . in 2003 he received the prize of translation by the academia brasileira de letras . he was the first person who translated to portuguese classical russian books directly from the russian language .
