Eva Burrows     eva evelyn burrows , ac ( 15 september 192920 march 2015 ) was an australian community welfare organiser . from 1986 to 1993 , burrows served as the 13th general of the salvation army . she served as an officer of the salvation army from 1951 to her retirement in 1993. burrows died on 20 march 2015 at a hospital in melbourne , victoria , aged 85. other websites . - biography and interview from australian biography online - general eva burrows , biography at women 's international center - `` why help the poor ?
