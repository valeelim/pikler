Jacobo Zabludovsky     jacobo zabludovsky kraveski ( may 24 , 1928 – july 2 , 2015 ) was a mexican journalist . he was the first anchorman in mexican television and his tv news program , `` 24 horas '' ( `` 24 hours '' ) was for decades the most important in the country . zabludovsky died of a stroke in mexico city , aged 87. other websites . - video of jacobo zabludovsky and salvador dalí ( 1971 )
