Killing Reagan     killing reagan : a violent assault that changed a presidency is a book written by bill o'reilly and martin dugard about the attempted assassination of u.s president ronald reagan . the book is the fifth in the `` killing '' series , following `` killing lincoln '' , `` killing kennedy '' , `` killing jesus '' , and `` killing patton '' . the book was released on september 22 , 2015. on september 26 , 2015 , about a week after the novel 's release national geographic announced that a television movie version is in the works without a specific release date or cast . in may 2016 , it was announced that tim matheson and cynthia nixon would play ronald and nancy reagan respectively in the movie . filming will begin in late may and will be released sometime in the fall of 2016 .
