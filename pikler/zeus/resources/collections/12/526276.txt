Jacques Santer     jacques santer ( born 18 may 1937 in wasserbillig ) is a luxembourgian politician . he was finance minister of luxembourg from 1979 until 1989. he was the 22nd prime minister of luxembourg serving from 1984 to 1995. he was a member of the christian social people 's party , which has been the leading party in the luxembourg government since 1979. as prime minister of luxembourg he also led the negotiations on the single european act , which effectively set aside the 20-year-old luxembourg compromise . he was the president of the european commission from 1995 to 1999 .
