Prince Johann Georg of Hohenzollern     johann georg , prince von hohenzollern ( `` johann georg carl leopold eitel-friedrich meinrad maria hubertus michael '' ; 31 july 1932 – 2 march 2016 ) was a german prince . through his marriage to princess birgitta of sweden , he was brother-in-law of king carl xvi gustaf of sweden . prince johann georg was the sixth child of frederick , prince of hohenzollern ( 30 august 1891 – 6 february 1965 ) and his wife princess margarete karola of saxony ( 24 january 1900 – 16 october 1962 ) . goerg died on 2 march 2016 in munich . he was 83 years old . other websites . - prince johann georg of hohenzollern , thepeerage.com
