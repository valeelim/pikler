Divine Intervention     divine intervention is the sixth studio album by american thrash metal band , slayer . `` divine intervention '' was released on september 27 , 1994 through american recordings . many of the song were inspired by television shows . the songs were also inspired by other things , like rush limbaugh , serial killer jeffrey dahmer , and vocalist tom araya 's wife . araya said that the album `` came out of the past four years of hating life . '' the album peaked at # 8 on the `` billboard '' 200. the album was certified gold by the riaa . it is the first album to feature drummer paul bostaph , who replaced slayer 's original drummer dave lombardo .
