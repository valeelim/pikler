José Artetxe     josé luis artetxe muguire ( 26 june 1930 – 19 march 2016 ) was a spanish footballer who played as a striker . he was born in algorta , spain . his career began in 1947. artetxe played for getxo from 1949 through 1950 , for athletic bilbao from 1950 through 1965 and for spain national football team in 1953 and again from 1954 through 1959. he retired in the 1960s . artetxe earned six caps for spain , during five years . his debut came on 17 march 1954 in a 2–2 draw against turkey for the 1954 fifa world cup qualifiers . he scored the game 's first goal in rome . artetxe died in bilbao , spain from complications of alzheimer 's disease at the age of 85. other websites . - spain stats at eu-football
