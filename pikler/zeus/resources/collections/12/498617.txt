American Beauty/American Psycho     american beauty/american psycho ( sometimes abbreviated as ab/ap ) is a studio album by punk rock group fall out boy . it was released on january 20 , 2015. this was the second album the band recorded since getting back together in 2013. the band wrote the music for the album while on tour with paramore . the band released a single ( `` centuries '' ) on september 9th , 2014. this was before the album itself was complete . `` centuries '' reached no . 10 on the billboard hot 100. it reached first position on the uk rock and metal chart . the album debuted at no . 1 on the us billboard 200 album chart . it sold the equivalent of 218,000 copies in its first week . it became the band 's third no . 1 album . track listing . 1 . `` irresistible '' 1 . `` american beauty/american psycho '' 1 . `` centuries '' 1 . `` the kids are n't alright '' 1 . `` uma thurman '' 1 . `` jet pack blues '' 1 . `` novocaine '' 1 . `` fourth of july '' 1 . `` favorite record '' 1 . `` immortals '' 1 . `` twin skeleton 's ( hotel in nyc )
