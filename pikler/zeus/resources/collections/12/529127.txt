Edmonde Charles-Roux     edmonde charles-roux ( 17 april 1920 – 20 january 2016 ) was a french writer . she was born in neuilly-sur-seine , hauts-de-seine . in 1946 , she joined the staff of a magazine being created , a women 's weekly : `` elle '' , where she spent two years . from 1948 , she worked for the french edition of `` vogue '' , becoming the magazine ’ s editor-in-chief in 1954. charles-roux died on 20 january 2016 , in marseille , france , at the age of 95. other websites . - `` edmonde charles-roux '' , `` academie goncourt ''
