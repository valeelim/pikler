United Nations Integrated Mission in East Timor     the united nations integrated mission in east timor ( unmit ) is a peacekeeping operation that was created in august 25 , 2006 by un security council resolution 1704. its objectives are `` to support the government in consolidating stability , enhancing a culture of democratic governance , and facilitating political dialogue among timorese stakeholders , in their efforts to bring about a process of national reconciliation and to foster social cohesion '' . the operation ended on december 31 , 2012 .
