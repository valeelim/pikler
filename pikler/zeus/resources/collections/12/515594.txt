Aaron B. Rollins     aaron b. rollins ( 1818 - december 4 , 1878 ) was a politician connected to the tammany hall political machine . he served in new york 's legislature in 1852. he was later elected coroner for new york city from 1867 to 1870. rollins was born in 1818. he died on december 4 , 1878 at the union-place hotel in manhattan .
