Safi     safi ( berber : asfi , ⴰⵙⴼⵉ ; , portuguese : `` safim '' ) is a city in western morocco on the atlantic ocean . the capital of safi province , it has a population of 282,227 ( 2004 census ) . safi is the main fishing port for the country 's sardine industry , and also exports phosphates , textiles and ceramics . other websites . - entry in lexicorient
