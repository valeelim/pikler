Philip Bailey     philip bailey ( born may 8 , 1951 ) is an american singer , songwriter , percussionist and actor . he was one of the lead singers ( along with group founder maurice white ) of the band earth , wind & fire . he has won seven grammy awards . he has released ten albums , including `` chinese wall '' . he also performed the duet `` easy lover '' with phil collins . bailey was born in denver , colorado . he has been married twice and has six children .
