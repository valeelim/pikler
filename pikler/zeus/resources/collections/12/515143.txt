Alpha Phi Alpha     alpha phi alpha is the first black , inter-collegiate greek-lettered fraternity in the united states . it was founded on december 4 , 1906 at cornell university in ithaca , new york . it uses an icon from ancient egypt , the great sphinx of giza , as its symbol . its aims are `` manly deeds , scholarship , and love for all mankind . '' its motto is `` first of all , servants of all , we shall transcend all . '' its archives are preserved at the moorland-spingarn research center . chapters were chartered at howard university and virginia union university in 1907. the fraternity has over 290,000 members . since 1940 it has been open to men of all races . currently , there are more than 730 active chapters worldwide . < br >
