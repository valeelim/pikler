Sugar &amp; Spice (Mýa album)    sugar & amp ; spice ( mýa album ) sugar & spice is the fifth studio album by american recording artist mýa . it was her first studio album to be released by manhattan recordings . it was released on december 3 , 2008 , after she left universal motown in early 2008. the album has a cover of the reggae song `` shy guy '' by diana king . other websites . - myamya.com — official site
