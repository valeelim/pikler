Lofsöngur     lofsöngur ( icelandic : `` hymn '' ; also called ó guð vors lands which means `` o , god of our land '' in english ) is the national anthem of the european country of iceland . it was written for the celebration of the 1000th anniversary of the first norse settlement of iceland . but it was n't considered a nation anthem for another 40 years . the anthem contains three stanzas , but only the first one is usually sung . other websites . - the icelandic national anthem - audio of the national anthem of iceland , with information and lyrics
