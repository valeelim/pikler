Miguel Picazo     miguel picazo ( 27 march 1927 – 23 april 2016 ) was a spanish movie director , screenwriter and actor . he is best known for directing his first movie `` la tía tula '' `` ( aunt tula ) '' ( 1964 ) . he was also an actor . he was in `` the spirit of the beehive '' ( 1973 ) and `` tesis '' ( 1996 ) . he retired in 1997. picazo died in madrid , spain on 23 april 2016 , aged 89 .
