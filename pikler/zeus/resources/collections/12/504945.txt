Vézère Valley     the vézère valley is known for its prehistoric cave systems , containing numerous cave paintings and hominid remains . they are found in the valley of the vézère river between the `` communes '' of les eyzies-de-tayac-sireuil and montignac in the dordogne department , nouvelle-aquitaine region . unesco designated the systems as a world heritage site in 1979. among the archaeological sites with remarkable caves is lascaux . unesco description is : related pages . - lascaux - vézère other websites . - hominides.com website - office de tourisme de la vallée de la vézère
