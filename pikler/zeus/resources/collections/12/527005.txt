Oscar Isaac     oscar isaac ( born óscar isaac hernández estrada ; march 9 , 1979 ) is a guatemalan american actor and musician . he is known for his lead film roles in the comedy-drama `` inside llewyn davis '' ( 2013 ) , for which he received a golden globe award nomination , the crime drama `` a most violent year '' ( 2014 ) and the science fiction thriller `` ex machina '' ( 2015 ) . he had a role in the 2015 film `` '' . he has also recorded music in guatemala . he won a golden globe award in 2016 .
