Yemanzhelinsk     yemanzhelinsk ( russian : еманжели́нск ) is a town and administrative centre in the yemanzhelinsky district in chelyabinsk oblast , russia . it is located near the kazakhstan border , and is on the eastern slopes of the southern ural mountains . yemanzhelinsk was founded in 1770 by the cossacks . the population was 30,216 at the 2010 census . yemanzhelinsk was founded in 1770 as a cossack village and was named yemanzhelinskaya ( ) since 1866. it became a coal mining settlement in the early 1930s , and was granted town status on september 25 , 1951. the chelyabinsk meteor landed close to yemanzhelinsk .
