Tainted Love     `` tainted love '' is a pop rock single . it was first sung by gloria jones . the lyrics are about leaving a relationship due to being abused . the song was first released in may 1965. in 1981 , soft cell covered the song . their version hit # 1 on some charts . in summer 1982 , it reached # 8 on the `` billboard '' hot 100. there are many versions of the song . it has been covered by many musicians .
