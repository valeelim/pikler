Antônio Pompêo     antônio pompêo ( 23 february 1953 – 5 january 2016 ) was a brazilian actor . he appeared in the television shows `` balacobaco '' , `` rebelde '' , `` a casa das sete mulheres '' , `` o rei do gado '' , `` mulheres de areia '' and `` pedra sobre pedra '' . he also appeared in the movies `` xica '' ( 1976 ) , `` a samba for sherlock '' ( 2001 ) and `` que sera , sera '' ( 2002 ) . he was born in são josé do rio preto or são josé do rio pardo , são paulo . pompêo was found dead on 5 january 2016 in rio de janeiro , aged 62 .
