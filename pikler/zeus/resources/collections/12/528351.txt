Kalevi Lehtovirta     kalevi valdemar lehtovirta ( 20 february 1928 – 10 january 2016 ) was a finnish footballer . he played as a striker . he played for tuwe turku , pyrkivä turku , red star , tps , and the finland national football team . he competed in the 1952 summer olympics . he was born in turku . lehtovirta died on 10 january 2016 in turku , aged 87 .
