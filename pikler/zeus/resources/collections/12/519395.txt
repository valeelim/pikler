Garfield (character)    garfield ( character ) garfield is a fictional character . he is from the comic strip `` garfield '' created by jim davis . garfield is portrayed as a lazy , fat and cynical orange cat . he loves lasagna and coffee . he hates mondays , nermal or raisins . garfield was born on june 19 , 1978. from the moment he was born , he has loved lasagna . in almost every cartoon appearance , garfield causes mischief . he did that in almost every episode of `` garfield and friends '' ( 1988-1995 ) . garfield was named after jim davis ' grandfather , james garfield davis , who in turn was named after us president james a. garfield . < br >
