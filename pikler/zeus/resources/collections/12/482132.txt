Nynäshamns IF     nynäshamns if is a sports club in the town of nynäshamn in sweden . it was established on 4 february 1917. it became an alliance club on 1 january 1989. the men 's bandy team played in the swedish top division during the season of 1972-1972. the men 's soccer team played in the swedish second division during the seasons of 1939-1940 and 1940-1941 .
