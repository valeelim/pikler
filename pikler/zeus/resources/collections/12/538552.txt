Dunellen, New Jersey    dunellen , new jersey dunellen is a borough in middlesex county , new jersey , united states . dunellen started in 1867. it grew around a railroad station . the station was originally called new market station . it served the nearby community of that name in piscataway . according to the united states census bureau , the borough has a total area of 1.048 square miles ( 2.715 km ) . all of it is land . dunellen is in the raritan valley , a line of communities in central new jersey .
