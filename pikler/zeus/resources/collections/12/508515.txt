Rebel Heart     rebel heart is the thirteenth studio album by american singer-songwriter madonna . it was her second album to be released under interscope records , after her previous album , `` mdna '' . it was released on march 10 , 2015. rebel heart debuted at number two on the us `` billboard '' 200. it contained the singles `` living for love '' and `` bitch i 'm madonna '' . the second song , `` bitch i 'm madonna '' , features trinidadian-american rapper nicki minaj . american disc jockey diplo was one of the producers of the album , along with madonna herself .
