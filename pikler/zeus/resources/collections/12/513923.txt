Leon Gorman     leon a. gorman ( december 20 , 1934 – september 3 , 2015 ) was an american businessman and philanthropist . he was the president ( 1967–2001 ) and chairman ( 2001–2013 ) of the bard for the clothing company l.l.bean . he had a reported net worth of $ 860 million , and , until his death , was the richest person in maine . he was born in nashua , new hampshire . gorman died from cancer on september 3 , 2015 in yarmouth , maine , aged 80 .
