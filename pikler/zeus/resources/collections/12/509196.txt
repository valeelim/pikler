John Barrasso     john barrasso ( born july 21 , 1952 ) is the junior united states senator from wyoming . he is a member of the u.s. republican party and has been in senate since june 25 , 2007. he was in the wyoming senate from 2003 to 2007. barrasso was born on july 21 , 1952 in reading , pennsylvania . he graduated from the georgetown university . he is married to bobbi brown . they have three children . other websites . - john barrasso , u.s. senator for wyoming , home page
