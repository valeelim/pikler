Oberdiessbach     oberdiessbach is a municipality in the administrative district of bern-mittelland in the canton of berne in switzerland . on 1 january 2010 the former municipalities of aeschlen bei oberdiessbach merged into the municipality of oberdiessbach and on 1 january 2014 the former municipalities of bleiken bei oberdiessbach merged into the municipality of oberdiessbach . other websites . - official website
