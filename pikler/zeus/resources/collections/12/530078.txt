Bosler, Wyoming    bosler , wyoming bosler is an unincorporated community in albany county , wyoming , united states , along the laramie river . it lies along the concurrent u.s. routes 30 and 287 north of laramie . its elevation is 7,080 feet ( 2,158 m ) . although bosler is unincorporated , it has a post office , with the zip code of 82051 .
