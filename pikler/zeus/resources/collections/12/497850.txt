Gerda van der Kade-Koudijs     gerda johanna marie van der kade-koudijs ( 29 october 1923 – 19 march 2015 ) was a dutch athlete who competed at the 1948 summer olympics . she won a gold medal in the 4×100 m relay . two years earlier she won european titles in the 4×100 m relay and long jump and finished sixth in the individual 100 m race . she was born in rotterdam and died in almelo .
