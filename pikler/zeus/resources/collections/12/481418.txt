Kingdom of Navarre     the kingodom of navarre was a small kingdom that existed from 841 to 1820. the kingdom was located roughly in the basque country on both sides of the pyrenees mountains . in 1512 , it was split into a spanish part , and a french part . in 1620 , the french part , called lower navarre was merged with france . the spanish part existed until 1820. the capital of the kingdom was pamplona .
