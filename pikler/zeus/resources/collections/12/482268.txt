Kyla Rae Kowalewski     kyla rae kowalewski ( born april 8 , 2001 ) is an american child voice actress . she is known for her role as anais watterson in `` the amazing world of gumball '' . she was also the voice of fufu in `` ni hao , kai-lan '' . filmography . - `` ni hao , kai-lan '' - fufu ( 2008-2011 ) - `` the amazing world of gumball '' - anais watterson ( 2011-present ) - `` adventure time '' - memow ( guest voice ) ( 2011 )
