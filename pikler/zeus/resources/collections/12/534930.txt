Oprichnina     oprichnina ( `` опричнина '' ) is a state policy used by russian tsar ivan the terrible between 1565 and 1572. the origin of its name is old russian `` опричь '' , what means 'apart ' , 'except ' , or 'special ' . oprichnina included secret police , mass repressions , public executions , and confiscation ( taking somebody 's property ) of land from the boyars . it was created because ivan did n't trust the boyars ( russian feudal aristocrats ) anymore .
