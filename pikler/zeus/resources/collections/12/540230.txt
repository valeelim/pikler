LACSA     lacsa was/is the flag carrier of costa rica . pan am founded the airline on october 17 , 1945. the first flight was on june 1 , 1946. in 1992 , taca airlines bought 86 % of lacsa . most of lacsa 's planes were repainted in the `` taca airlines '' paint-job . the `` original lacsa '' stopped flying in 2014. however , the `` new lacsa '' is now a subsidiary of avianca .
