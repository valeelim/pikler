Mario Benjamín Menéndez     mario benjamín menéndez ( 3 april 1930 – 18 september 2015 ) was an argentine military officer . he was a brigadier general of the argentine army . after the 1982 argentine occupation , he served as governor of the falkland islands from 3 april until 14 june . he surrendered to british forces during the falkland wars . menéndez was born in buenos aires . his brother is luciano benjamín menéndez ( born 1927 ) , a general of the dirty war . menéndez died on 18 september 2015 at the age of 85 .
