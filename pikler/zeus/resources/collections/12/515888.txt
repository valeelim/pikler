Bright Lights (Matchbox Twenty song)    bright lights ( matchbox twenty song ) bright lights is the third single from matchbox twenty 's third album , more than you think you are . the song proved to be a moderate hit for the band . it peaked at 23 on the billboard hot 100. the song is written by lead singer rob thomas . it is about a girl who leaves her hometown looking for . throughout the song , he lets the girl know she can come home if things do n't work out .
