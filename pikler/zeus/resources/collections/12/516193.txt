Steve Scalise     stephen joseph `` steve '' scalise ( born october 6 , 1965 ) is an american politician . he is the united states house of representatives majority whip and representative for , serving since 2008. he is a member of the republican party and the chairman of the 170-member conservative house republican study committee . on june 14 , 2017 , scalise was shot at a practice session for the congressional baseball team in virginia , by james t. hodgkinson . later that day scalise was reported to be in critical condition . other websites . - congressman steve scalise official u.s. house site - steve scalise for congress
