Middlesex County, Ontario    middlesex county , ontario middlesex county is a rural county in southwestern ontario , canada . according to the canada 2011 census , it had a population of 70,796. the county seat is in the city of london , even though london is politically independent of the county . middlesex county has eight incorporated municipalites : adelaide metcalfe , lucan biddulph , middlesex centre , newbury , north middlesex , southwest middlesex , strathroy-caradoc , and thames centre . there are also three reservations of the first nations in the county : chippewas of the thames 42 , munsee-delaware 1 , and onedia 41 .
