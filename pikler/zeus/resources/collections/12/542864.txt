Agostinho Neto     antónio agostinho neto ( 17 september 1922 – 10 september 1979 ) was an angolan politician and poet . he was the first president of angola , serving from 1975–1979 after he leading the popular movement for the liberation of angola ( mpla ) in the war for independence that lasted from 1961–1974 . he died of inoperable cancer on 10 september 1979 in a hospital in moscow , russia , aged 56. other websites . - agostinho neto on encyclopedia
