William Waad     sir william waad ( also wade or wadd ) ( 1546 - 1623 ) was an english diplomat and administrator . he served as a special diplomatic agent for elizabeth i of england . she sent him to france to collect papers which were used to convict her sister , queen mary . waad also carried out the questioning and interrogations of political prisoners , often using torture to get the answers . he was involved in questioning sir walter raleigh , members of the gunpowder plot , and the essex rebellion among others .
