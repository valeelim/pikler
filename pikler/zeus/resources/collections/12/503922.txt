Poltergeist (2015 movie)    poltergeist ( 2015 movie ) poltergeist is a 2015 american horror , fantasy and thriller movie . it is a remake of steven spielberg 's 1982 movie with the same name . this new version is produced by sam raimi . it stars sam rockwell , rosemarie dewitt , jared harris , and jane adams . the movie was released on may 22 , 2015 in north america . it will be screened in 3d . this movie is about a family whose suburban home has an invasion of angry spirits . these frightening ghosts attack the family who live in a quiet wealthy neighborhood . they kidnap the youngest daughter . the family has to make a plan to rescue her . the first trailer for the new movie was released on february 5 , 2015. a `` slate magazine '' writer said that the trailer made the movie look too much like the first one . james hibberd of `` entertainment weekly '' said that the trailer `` retains and amplifies several elements from the original '' . he also gave praise that `` the modernizing doesn ’ t result in , say , the family ’ s daughter being kidnapped by ghosts in snapchat '' . brad miska of bloody disgusting said that `` while every fiber of my being wants to reject it , [ the film ] actually looks pretty insane '' , and praised the trailer 's last scene . a `` polygon '' writer said that the trailer looked similar to the first movie , but that it `` looks great , as a horror movie '' .
