Professional Air Traffic Controllers Organization (1968)    professional air traffic controllers organization ( 1968 ) the professional air traffic controllers organization or patco was a united states trade union . it ran from 1968 until its decertification in 1981 following an illegal strike that was broken by the ronald reagan administration . the 1981 strike and defeat of patco was called `` one of the most important events in late twentieth century u.s. labor history '' by labor historian joseph a. mccartin .
