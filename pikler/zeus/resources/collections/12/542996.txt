English National League     the national league is a league for association football teams in england ( though some teams come from wales ) . the league was created in 1979 from the northern premier league , southern league and isthmian league . in 1987 , teams began to be promoted to football league two , and from 2002 onwards , a second team was also promoted . the league was renamed to the national league in 2015 as a rebranding . in 2004 , two divisions were created below the main league , known as the national league north and south .
