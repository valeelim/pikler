Prince Alexander of Yugoslavia (born 1924)    prince alexander of yugoslavia ( born 1924 ) prince alexander of yugoslavia ( `` aleksandar pavlov karađorđević '' ; 13 august 1924 – 12 may 2016 ) was the elder son of prince paul . his father served as regent of yugoslavia in the 1930s , and his wife , princess olga of greece and denmark . he was born in white lodge , richmond park , surrey . alexander married princess maria pia of savoy , daughter of king umberto ii of italy in 1955. alexander and maria pia divorced in 1967. on 17 february 2008 , alexander said that he did not support the declaration of independence by kosovo . alexander died on 12 may 2016 in paris , aged 91 .
