Mohamed Hassanein Heikal     mohamed hassanein heikal ( 23 september 1923 – 17 february 2016 ) was an egyptian journalist . for 17 years ( 1957–1974 ) , he was editor-in-chief of the cairo newspaper `` al-ahram '' . he was a commentator on arab affairs for more than 50 years . he was born in cairo , egypt . heikal died in cairo , egypt from renal failure on 17 february 2016. he was aged 92. other websites . - various articles , `` al-ahram weekly ''
