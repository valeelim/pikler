Chest x-ray     a chest x-ray or chest film an x-ray of the chest used to diagnose diseases of the chest . x-rays are among the most common medical images . doctors use them to diagnose problems . like all radiography methods , chest x-rays use ionizing radiation . the chest x-ray process makes a chest x-ray photo . the photo shows the inside of the chest . chest x-rays are used to find many diseases inside the chest . a doctor can use an x-ray to examine bones , lungs , heart , and great vessels . pneumonia and congestive heart failure are commonly diagnosed by chest radiograph . chest x-rays are used to screen for job-related lung disease , for example in mining where workers breathe dust .
