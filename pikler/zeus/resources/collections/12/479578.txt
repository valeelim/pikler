Cartier (jeweller)    cartier ( jeweller ) société cartier makes and sells jewellery and watches . it was started in paris , france in 1847 by louis-françois cartier.. the company is based in paris . cartier has sold items to celebrities and royalty for many years . edward vii said that cartier is `` the jeweller of kings and the king of jewellers '' . for his coronation in 1902 , edward vii ordered 27 tiaras from cartier . he chose cartier as a main provider to the royal family in 1904 .
