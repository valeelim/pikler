United States Secretary of the Air Force     the secretary of the air force ( secaf , or saf/os ) is the head of the department of the air force . it is an organization within the department of defense of the united states of america . the secretary of the air force is appointed from civilian life by the president , by and with the advice and consent of the senate . the secretary reports to the secretary of defense and/or the deputy secretary of defense , and is by statute responsible for and has the authority to conduct all the affairs of the department of the air force .
