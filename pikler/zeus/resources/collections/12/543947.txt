Georges Sesia     georges sesia ( 8 july 1924 – 12 may 2016 ) was a french footballer who played as a striker . he was born in villerupt . he made one appearance for the french national team in 1948. sesia played for nancy-lorraine , fc nancy , stade français paris , roubaix-tourcoing , strasbourg , béziers and merlebach . sesia died on 12 may 2016 in france , aged 91 .
