Arrondissements of the Ain department     there are 4 arrondissements in the ain department . the french departments , and in other countries , are divided into `` arrondissements '' , which may be translated into english as districts ( in some cases , as boroughs ) . the capital of an arrondissement is called a subprefecture . if the prefecture ( capital ) of the department is in an arrondissement , that prefecture is the capital of the arrondissement , acting both as a prefecture and as a subprefecture . arrondissements are further divided into communes . the 4 arrondissements of ain are : 1. arrondissement of belley , ( subprefecture of the ain department : belley ) , with 104 communes . 1. arrondissement of bourg-en-bresse , ( prefecture of the ain department : bourg-en-bresse ) , with 218 communes . 1. arrondissement of gex , ( subprefecture of the ain department : gex ) , with 29 communes . 1. arrondissement of nantua , ( subprefecture of the ain department : nantua ) , with 59 communes . since its creation , the ain department has had some changes :
