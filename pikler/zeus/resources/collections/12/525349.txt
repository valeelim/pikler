Roland D-50     the roland d-50 is a linear/polyphonic 61-key synthesizer made by roland . it was released in 1987. its features include linear arithmetic synthesis , on-board effects , a joystick for data manipulation , and an analog synthesis-styled keyboard layout . the external roland pg-1000 programmer could also be attached to the d-50 for more complex manipulation of sounds . it was also produced in a rack-mount version . this was called the d-550 and had almost 450 user-adjustable parameters .
