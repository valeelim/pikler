Manhattan Bridge     the manhattan bridge is a suspension bridge across the east river in new york city . it connects the new york city boroughs of manhattan and brooklyn . the bridge is long . the bridge opened on december 31 , 1909. it was designed by leon moisseiff . its design was new for bridges at the time . many longer suspension bridges are based on this bridge 's design . the bridge is officially landmarked . it was made a national historic civil engineering landmark in 2009. other websites . - manhattan bridge at new york city department of transportation
