Húsavík     húsavík is a town in northern iceland . it has a population of 2,237 people . most of the industry in the city comes from tourism and fishing . about 850 ad , a swedish viking named garðar svavarsson arrived on the northern coast of iceland . there he built a settlement and prepared to stay for the winter . when spring arrived he departed leaving two slaves behind . they made a farm there . other websites . - official tourist information website - official website - whalewatching in húsavík
