Michelle (song)    michelle ( song ) `` michelle '' is a 1965 love ballad from the beatles . the song is on their `` rubber soul '' album . the song is the only one from the beatles that has some lyrics in french . the song won a grammy award for song of the year in 1967. it is one of the best known and most often recorded beatles songs . in 2009 , paul mccartney dedicated the song to michelle obama .
