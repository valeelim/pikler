Bernardo Ribeiro     bernardo salim ribeiro ( 9 october 1989 – 7 may 2016 ) was a brazilian footballer . he played as an attacking midfielder . on february 2011 , he completed move to skënderbeu korçë . he officially became part of the club in july 2011. inaugust 2012 he signed for a-league club newcastle jets . on 8 may 2016 , ribeiro died of a heart attack during a match in brazil , aged 26 .
