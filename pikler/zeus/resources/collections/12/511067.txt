Bump n' Grind    bump n ' grind `` bump & grind '' is an r & b ballad by the singer r. kelly . the song is about rough sexual activity . it comes from kelly 's debut record `` 12 play '' . the song was released in january 1994. it became a # 1 single on the `` billboard '' hot 100. it also spent twelve weeks at # 1 on us hot r & b songs . the song became the longest-running number one song of 1994. the song was released again in 2014 .
