IF Verdandi     if verdandi is a sports club in the town of eskilstuna in sweden . it was established in 1900. the club runs soccer . it earlier even ran amateur wrestling , bandy and table tennis . the men 's bandy team played in the swedish top division in 1932 , 1933 and 1941. the men 's soccer team has played the swedish second division during the seasons of 1942-1943. , 1943-1944 and 1944-1945 . kjell johansson has played for the club . other websites . - official website
