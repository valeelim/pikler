Jan Góra     jan wojciech góra op ( 8 february 1948 – 21 december 2015 ) was a polish dominican roman catholic priest , doctor of theology , academic and chaplain . since 1997 , he had been the organiser of the polish youth meeting lednica 2000 at the lednica fields . he was the animator for the academic chaplaincy centre `` respublica dominicana '' in jamna . he was the author of numerous books and articles . he was born in prudnik . góra died at the age of 67 in poznań , poland from a heart attack .
