Jane Briggs Hart     jane briggs ( “ janey ” ) hart ( october 21 , 1921 - june 5 , 2015 ) was an american aviator . she was the widow of senator philip a. hart . she was born in detroit , michigan . she was a member of the nasa 's mercury 13. hart was also an avid sailor and has sailed in the port huron to mackinac boat race 15 times as part of an all-women crew . hart died on june 5 , 2015 in west hartford , connecticut from alzheimer 's disease , aged 93 .
