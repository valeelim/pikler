Orma Slack     orma slack ( née snider ; february 19 , 1903 – april 13 , 2015 ) was a canadian supercentenarian who , at the time of her death aged 112 years , 53 days , was the oldest living person in canada since the death of colombe benoît-leclerc on 14 january 2015. slack was born in bracebridge , ontario . her husband , william george slack , died in 1988. they had two children , rosemary ( –1991 ) and ted ( ) . slack died on april 13 , 2015 in belleville , ontario , aged 112 years and 53 days .
