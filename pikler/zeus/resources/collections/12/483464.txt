Maureen Dowd     maureen dowd ( born january 14 , 1952 ) is an american columnist for `` the new york times '' . she is also a best-selling author . she worked for `` time '' magazine in the 1970s and 1980s . she worked for the `` washington star '' . she first joined `` the new york times '' in 1983. in 1999 , dowd received a pulitzer prize . it was for her series of columns on the monica lewinsky scandal in the clinton administration . she was born in washington , d.c..
