Dan Green     james hadley `` jay '' snyder ( born february 7 , 1975 ) also known by his stage name as dan green , is an american voice actor , voice director and script adapter who is known for his work with 4kids entertainment , duart film and video , nyav post and central park media . he is best known for his voice work as yugi mutou in the `` yu–gi–oh ! '' franchise and as knuckles the echidna in `` sonic x '' and in the `` sonic the hedgehog '' video game franchise from 2005 to 2010. he married his wife michal lura friedman , a singer and fellow voice actress in 2008. they were married for four years until her death in 2011 , from complications during childbirth . they have two children , jackson james snyder and reverie vivian snyder .
