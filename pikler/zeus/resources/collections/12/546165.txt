2003 European heat wave     the 2003 european heat wave was a natural disaster in europe in the summer of 2003. the heat led to the hottest summer on record since at least 1540. france was especially hard hit . the heat led to health crises in several countries . it combined with droughts to reduce crops in southern europe . more than 70,000 people died in europe . almost 15,000 people died in france . people also died in the netherlands , portugal and spain .
