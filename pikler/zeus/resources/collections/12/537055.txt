Leila de Lima     leila de lima ( born august 27 , 1959 ) is a filipino lawyer . she was appointed by president gloria macapagal arroyo as chairperson of the commission on human rights on may 2008 and served until june 30 , 2010 when she resigned and appointed by president benigno aquino iii as secretary of the philippine department of justice . she resigned october 12 , 2015 to focus on her candidacy for the senate in the 2016 election .
