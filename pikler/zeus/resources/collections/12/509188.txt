Mike Crapo     mike crapo ( born may 20 , 1951 ) is the senior united states senator from idaho . he is a member of the u.s. republican party and has been in senate since january 3 , 1999. he was in the united states house of representatives from 1993 to 1999. crapo was born on may 20 , 1951 in idaho falls , idaho . he studied at brigham young university and at harvard law school . he is married to susan hasleton . they have five children . other websites . - mike crapo , u.s. senator for idaho , home page
