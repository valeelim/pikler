Tom Latham     thomas paul `` tom '' latham ( born july 14 , 1948 ) is an american politician . he was the u.s. representative for serving from 2013 through 2015. he is a member of the republican party . on december 17 , 2013 , latham announced he would not seek reelection next term , becoming the third member of the house to announce his retirement on the same day ( along with rep. frank wolf from virginia and utah rep. jim matheson ) .
