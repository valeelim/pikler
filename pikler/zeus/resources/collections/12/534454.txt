Berta Cáceres     berta isabel cáceres flores ( 4 march 1973 – 3 march 2016 ) was a honduran environmental activist and indigenous leader of the lenca people . she was co-founder and coordinator of the council of popular and indigenous organizations of honduras ( copinh ) . she won the goldman environmental prize in 2015. cáceres was killed in her home in la esperanza , honduras . she was killed by armed intruders , after years of threats against her life . she was aged 42 .
