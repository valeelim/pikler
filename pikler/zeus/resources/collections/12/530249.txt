Newport, Shropshire    newport , shropshire newport is a market town in the ceremonial county of shropshire , england . it is in the borough of telford and wrekin . it is located about north of telford and about west of stafford , on the shropshire/staffordshire border . the 2001 census recorded 10,814 people living in the town 's parish . this makes it the second-largest town in telford and wrekin and the fifth-largest in the ceremonial county of shropshire . the population in 2008 was between 12,444 and 15,632 .
