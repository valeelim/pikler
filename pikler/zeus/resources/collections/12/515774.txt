Black Mass (movie)    black mass ( movie ) black mass is a 2015 american crime movie directed by scott cooper and written by mark mallouk and jez butterworth . it is based on the 2001 book `` black mass : the true story of an unholy alliance between the fbi and the irish mob '' by dick lehr and gerard o'neill . the movie stars johnny depp , joel edgerton , benedict cumberbatch , kevin bacon , jesse plemons , peter sarsgaard , rory cochrane , adam scott , dakota johnson , and corey stoll . the movie is about the criminal career of infamous irish-american mobster whitey bulger ( depp ) . the movie was released to positive reviews on september 18 , 2015 .
