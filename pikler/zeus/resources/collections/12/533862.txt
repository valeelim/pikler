Repeating decimal     repeating decimal ( or recurring decimal ) is a decimal where the number or pattern repeats forever . repeating decimal is resulted when dividing a number by a number that is not divisible except for certain cases like dividing any number by 5. for example , dividing 7 by 3 would result in a repeating decimal , which is 2.333 ... or 2.. the number or group of numbers that repeats is called a repetend or reptend and the number of numbers in the repetend is called the period length . for 2. , 3 is the repetend and the period length is 1. related pages . - 0.999 ... - rational number
