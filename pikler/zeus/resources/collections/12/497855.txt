Thomas Hopko     thomas hopko ( march 28 , 1939 – march 18 , 2015 ) was an eastern orthodox christian priest and theologian . he was the dean of saint vladimir ’ s orthodox theological seminary from september 1992 until july 1 , 2002 and taught dogmatic theology there from 1968 until 2002. in retirement , he carried the honorary title of `` dean emeritus '' . hopko died of complications from congestive heart failure on march 18 , 2015 , in wexford , pennsylvania , aged 75 .
