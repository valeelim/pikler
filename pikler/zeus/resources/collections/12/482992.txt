Boris Nemtsov     boris yefimovich nemtsov ( ; 9 october 195927 february 2015 ) was a russian scientist , statesman and liberal politician . he had a successful political career during the 1990s under president boris yeltsin . since 2000 had been an outspoken critic of vladimir putin . he was shot and killed in february 2015 for his pro-democracy views on russia , probably by loyalists of vladimir putin , on a bridge near the kremlin and red square in moscow . nemtsov studied physics , and held the equivalent of a phd in physics and mathematics , from the university of nizhny novgorod , which was called gorki at the time .
