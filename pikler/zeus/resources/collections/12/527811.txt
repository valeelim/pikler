Demmus Hentze     demmus hentze ( 4 december 1923 – 3 january 2016 ) was a faroese politician and lawyer . he was a member of the people 's party . he served as finance minister from 1975 to 1981. he was born in sandur , sandoy . he graduated from the university of copenhagen . hentze died on 3 january 2016 in tórshavn , aged 92 .
