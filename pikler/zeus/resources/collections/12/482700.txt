Ulf Georgsson     ulf georg georgsson , born 22 april 1962 in kinna parish in former älvsborg county , sweden is a swedish songwriter . as a songwriter he has participated at melodifestivalen . he has also written songs recorded by acts like lasse stefanz , vikingarna , playtones , drifters , zekes , cc & lee , black jack , barbados , friends and frøya . as a musician he plays the drums in flamingokvintetten since june 2013. before that he played the drums 30 years in bhonus . he became the second most successful svensktoppen composer in the year 2000. he was also appointed svensktoppen `` composer of the year '' in 2001 .
