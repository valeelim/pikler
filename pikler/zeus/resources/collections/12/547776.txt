Robert E. Cooper, Sr.    robert e. cooper , sr. robert e. cooper , sr. ( october 14 , 1920 july 11 , 2016 ) was an american judge . he was a justice on the tennessee supreme court from 1974 until his retirement in 1990. cooper was born in chattanooga , tennessee . he was a former chairman of the tennessee judicial council . he has served on the sixth judicial circuit ( 1953–1960 ) and tennessee court of appeals ( 1960–1974 ) . cooper , sr. died in chattanooga , tennessee on july 11 , 2016 , aged 95 .
