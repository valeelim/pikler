Antoine Valois-Fortier     antoine valois-fortier ( born february 6 , 1985 ) is a canadian athlete . he is best known as a judoka . he was the winner of the canadian national judo championship in the -81 kg category in 2010. valois-fortier won a bronze medal at the 2012 summer olympics in london , defeating travis stevens of the united states in the men 's 81 kg half-middleweight judo . it was the canada 's first medal in olympic judo since 2000 when his coach nicolas gill won a silver medal at the 2000 summer olympics . other websites . - antoine valois-fortier at the judoinside.com - canadian olympic team profile
