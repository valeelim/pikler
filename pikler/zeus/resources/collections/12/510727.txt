Microsoft Edge     microsoft edge is a web browser . it was developed by microsoft . it is included in the company 's windows operating system . it was released on july 29 , 2015. it replaced internet explorer as the default browser on windows 10. it is also the default browser on windows 10 for mobile devices . edge connects with microsoft 's online platforms . it features cortana , a digital assistant . cortana provides voice control , search functionality , and personalized information related to searches in the address bar . users can make annotations to web pages . the annotations can be stored to and shared with onedrive . it also has a `` reading list '' function to sync content between devices . it has a `` reading mode '' that makes reading websites easier . internet explorer 11 will remain available alongside edge on windows 10 for compatibility purposes .
