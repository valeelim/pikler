Andrew Heyward     andrew john heyward is an independent media consultant in manhattan , new york . heyward is a principal in marketspacenext llc and in his own practice , heyward advisory llc . he is a senior advisor to monitor deloitte . he was formerly president of cbs news from january 1996 to november 2005. before that he was an executive producer for the cbs evening news ( october 1994-january 1996 ) . he has won 12 national emmy awards . heyward was born in new york in 1950. his parents were e. j. r. and elisabeth heyward . in 1972 he graduated magna cum laude from harvard university . he earned a bachelor of arts degree in history and literature . heyward is a member of phi beta kappa . he was going to go to law school but took a job as a production assistant at wnyw-tv in new york . he became a producer . he then was hired by wcbs and became the news producer within a year . in 1981 he took a job as a field producer at cbs . in 1984 he was promoted to senior producer of the cbs evening news . then in 1986 he was senior broadcast producer of the cbs evening news with dan rather . in 1994 he became the vice president of cbs news and executive producer of the cbs evening news . he became president of cbs news in january 1996 .
