Kit Bond     kit bond ( born march 6 , 1939 ) is a former united states senator from missouri and a member of the republican party . before his career in the u.s. senate , bond served two non-consecutive terms as governor of missouri , from 1973 to 1977 and from 1981 to 1985. he was previously state auditor of missouri from 1971 to 1973. bond was born on march 6 , 1939 in st. louis , missouri . in 1960 he graduated from princeton university . he received a law degree from the university of virginia school of law . he is married to linda bond . other websites . - kit bond strategies llp
