Burt Kwouk     herbert w. `` burt '' kwouk , obe ( pron . `` kwok '' ; ; 18 july 1930 â€“ 24 may 2016 ) was a british actor . kwouk was born in warrington to chinese parents . he was raised in shanghai . kwouk was known for his role as cato ( spelled `` kato '' in the 1964 release `` a shot in the dark '' ) in the `` pink panther '' movies . he was also known for his role of major yamauchi in the british drama series `` tenko '' and , later , as entwistle in `` last of the summer wine '' . in the 2011 new year honours list , burt kwouk was appointed an officer of the order of the british empire ( obe ) for services to drama . kwouk died on 24 may 2016 in london , england of cancer , aged 85 .
