Razem     partia razem ( , `` together party '' ) is a left-wing polish political party created in may 2015. it is one of the many parties that were involved in the polish parliamentary election of 2015. the party supports labor rights and is against deregulation . it is socially liberal . during the 2015 elections it has achieved 3.62 % of the votes , but that was not enough to gain a seat in the parliament . the colour of the party is red-violet .
