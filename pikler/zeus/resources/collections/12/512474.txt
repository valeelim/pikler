Exclusive (album)    exclusive ( album ) exclusive is the second studio album by american recording artist chris brown . it was released on november 6 , 2007 , by his own record label cbe , with jive records . in 2008 the album was re-released under the name `` exclusive : the forever edition '' . much like his first album `` chris brown '' released in 2005 , the album features a hip hop and r & b sound and features a wide range of guest appearances from different acts from the hip hop and r & b world like t-pain , keri hilson , will.i.am , lil wayne and big boi . track listing . - uk/japanese edition bonus tracks : 1 . < li value=15 > '' get at ya '' – 3:18 1 . `` mama '' – 4:24 1 . `` nice '' – 4:32 1 . `` down '' – 4:17 1 . `` fallen angel '' - 5:34 - limited edition dvd 1. journey to south africa – 15:00 1. behind the scenes of `` wall to wall '' – 15:00 1 . `` wall to wall '' ( video ) – 5:20 1. behind the scenes of `` kiss kiss '' – 12:37 1 . `` kiss kiss '' ( video ) – 4:25 - special edition dvd chris brown on tour ( documentary ) < br > - japanese limited edition dvd 1 . < li value=1 > wall to wall ( video )
