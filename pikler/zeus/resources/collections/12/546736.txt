Birkir Bjarnason     birkir bjarnason ( born on 27 may 1988 in akureyri , iceland ) is an icelandic professional footballer who plays as a central midfielder . he currently plays for the swiss club of fc basel and the iceland national team . in his homeland he is nicknamed thor . he has also played for viking fk , fk bodø/glimt , standard liège , delfino pescara 1936 and sampdoria . he won the swiss super league with basel in 2016. he was included in iceland 's squad for the uefa euro 2016. on 14 june 2016 , he scored iceland 's first goal at a major tournament . it was against portugal , and the game ended in a draw . other websites . - ksi profile - profile on the swiss football league homepage - profile at viking
