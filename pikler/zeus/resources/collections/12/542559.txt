Saheed Osupa     saheed akorede okunola ( born 1969 ) , popularly known as saheed osupa or king saheed osupa is a nigerian musician , performer and film actor . his genre of music is majorly fuji . saheed osupa was born in ibadan , oyo state but grew up in lagos state where he completed his early education . in 1983 , saheed osupa chose music as his profession and went on to release his first record album titled `` fuji fa disco '' which earned him fame . he has released over 25 record albums .
