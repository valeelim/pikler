May Sabai Phyu     may sabai phyu ( also may sabe phyu ) is an activist from kachin state in burma ( myanmar ) . she works for human rights , freedom of speech , peace , justice for myanmar ’ s ethnic people . she works to stop violence against women . in 2015 , she won the u.s. state department 's international women of courage award . in 2008 , may sabai phyu started the women ’ s protection technical working group during cyclone nargis . after the storm , 14 out of every 100 families were headed by women , many of them widows . because of this , more women were in the sex trade for money or food . the working group helped women to find housing , sex education , and jobs . after the problems of the storm were ended , the working group became the gender equality network . may sabai phyu was the senior coordinator . in 2012 , this group helped the government write the national strategic plan for the advancement of women . the organization also works for gender equality and makes plans to stop violence against women and girls .
