Kayahan     kayahan açar ( 29 march 1949 – 3 april 2015 ) , better known by his stage name kayahan , was a turkish pop music singer-songwriter . his career lasted forty years . he composed all of his own material and released more than eight best-selling albums . kayahan was born in izmir . he was married three times and had two daughters . kayahan died from multiple organ dysfunction syndrome due to lung cancer on 3 april 2015 at a hospital in istanbul . he was 66 .
