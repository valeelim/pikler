Harry Landers     harry landers ( born harry sorokin ; september 3 , 1921 ) is an american character actor . he was born in new york city , new york . he was known for his roles in `` up periscope '' ( 1959 ) , `` the gallant hours '' ( 1960 ) , and the elvis presley western `` charro !
