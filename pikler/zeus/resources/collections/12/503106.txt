Excise stamps of Ukraine     excise stamps of ukraine are a kind of ukrainian revenue stamps to collect excise tax . they are used in accordance with the ukraine 's presidential decree of 18 september 1995 `` on approval of the excise duty on alcoholic beverages and tobacco products '' . on 24 october 1996 , there was another decree of the cabinet of ministers of ukraine , `` on approval of the production , storage and sale of excise stamps and marking of alcoholic beverages and tobacco products , and the sale or destruction of the confiscated alcoholic beverages and tobacco products '' . it introduced excise stamps into circulation in ukraine . the law was amended by later decrees of the ukrainian government . related pages . - customs - excise stamps of russia - stamp act - tax
