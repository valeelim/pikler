Koyashskoye Salt Lake     koyashskoye or koyashs'ke is a salt lake on the coast of the kerch peninsula in crimea . it is separated from the black sea by a strip of land . it is and deep . the water color varies from pink to the scarlet , depending on the light , because there are microscopic algae living in the water . when the water evaporates , it leaves a deposit of salt on the stones and the shores . the salt becomes crystal , producing crystal stones and the scent of viola . this lake is considered healing and is appreciated by some local people . it is not widely known to tourists .
