Princess Sofia, Duchess of Värmland    princess sofia , duchess of värmland princess sofia of sweden , duchess of värmland ( born sofia kristina hellqvist on 6 december 1984 ) is the wife of prince carl philip , duke of värmland . she began dating prince carl philip in 2010. she has also been a glamour model and reality television contestant . hellqvist has appeared on the reality show `` paradise hotel '' . she married prince carl philip at slottskyrkan in stockholm on 13 june 2015. after the marriage she got the titel princess of sweden , duchess of värmland . thier first child prince alexander , was born on 19 april 2016. in march 2017 , it was announced that the couple 's second child is expected in september 2017. titles , styles and honours . - 6 december 1984 – 13 june 2015 : miss sofia kristina hellqvist - 13 june 2015 – present : `` her royal highness '' princess sofia of sweden , duchess of värmland ( `` swedish '' : hennes kungliga höghet prinsessan sofia av sverige hertiginna av värmland )
