Jingle All The Way     jingle all the way is an american christmas comedy family movie . tvt records released the film 's soundtrack album on audio cd on november 26 , 1996. it features only two of composer david newman 's pieces from `` jingle all the way '' , but features many of the songs by other artists included in the film , as well as other christmas songs and new tracks by the brian setzer orchestra . intrada music group released a special collection limited edition of newman 's full 23-track score on november 3 , 2008 .
