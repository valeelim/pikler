Bernard Lamarre     bernard lamarre ( 6 august 1931 – 30 march 2016 ) was a canadian engineer and businessman . he was born in chicoutimi , quebec in 1931. lamarre received a bachelor of appled sciences in civil engineering from the école polytechnique de montréal in 1952. he received a master of science degree from imperial college of science and technology in 1955. he started work as a soil mechanics engineer with the firm of lalonde & valois eventually becoming president and chief executive officer of the firm now called lavalin group . he was president of `` ordre des ingénieurs du québec '' from 1993 to 1997. other websites . - bernard lamarre at the canadian encyclopedia
