Rómulo Macció     romulo macció ( 1931 – 11 march 2016 ) was an argentine painter . he worked with the local avant-garde art movement which took shape during the 1960s . he was born in buenos aires . macció described much of the genteel portrait and landscape art available at that time as `` pink chocolate . '' he was awarded the de ridder prize in 1959 and the torcuato di tella institute international prize in 1962. he helped create the neo-figurative movement that swept latin american art during the 1960s . macció died in buenos aires on 11 march 2016. he was aged 84. other websites . - artnet - the legacy project
