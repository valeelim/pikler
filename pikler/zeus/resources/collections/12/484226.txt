2010 (movie)    2010 ( movie ) 2010 ( known also as 2010 : the year we make contact ) is a 1984 science fiction movie . it was both directed and produced by peter hyams . `` 2010 '' was released on december 7 , 1984. it is based on the arthur c. clarke novel ' and is the sequel to the 1968 movie ' . the movie got mixed to positive reviews from critics . it has a 66 % rating on rotten tomatoes . movie critic roger ebert rated the movie three out of four stars and said that it `` does n't match the poetry and the mystery of the original film '' and that it `` has an ending that is infuriating , not only in its simplicity , but in its inadequacy to fulfill the sense of anticipation , the sense of wonder we felt at the end of 2001 '' . he finished his review by `` and yet the truth must be told : this is a good movie . once we 've drawn our lines , once we 've made it absolutely clear that 2001 continues to stand absolutely alone as one of the greatest movies ever made , once we have freed 2010 of the comparisons with kubrick 's masterpiece , what we are left with is a good-looking , sharp-edged , entertaining , exciting space opera '' .
