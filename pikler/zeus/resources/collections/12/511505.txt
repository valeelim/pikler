Agustín Cejas     agustín mario cejas ( 22 march 1945 – 14 august 2015 ) was a former argentine football goalkeeper . he played for a number of clubs in argentina and brazil and has the all-time record number of appearances for racing club de avellaneda . he was born in buenos aires . cejas played for argentina at the 1964 summer olympics . cejas died of alzheimer 's disease on august 14 , 2015 in brazil , aged 70 .
