Arrondissement of Villeneuve-sur-Lot     the arrondissement of villeneuve-sur-lot is an arrondissement of france . it is part of the lot-et-garonne `` département '' in the nouvelle-aquitaine region . its capital , and subpréfecture of the department , is the city of villeneuve-sur-lot . when the lot-et-garonne department was created on 17 february 1800 , the `` arrondissement '' of villeneuve-sur-lot was part of that original department . the `` arrondissement '' of villeneuve-sur-lot is in the northeast of lot-et-garonne , with an area of , the largest `` arrondissement '' of the department . it has 90,527 inhabitants and a population density of inhabitants/km² . the `` arrondissement '' is bordered to the north by the dordogne department , to the east by the lot department , to the southeast by the tarn-et-garonne department , to the south by the `` arrondissement '' of agen and to the west by the `` arrondissement '' of marmande . after the reorganisation of the cantons in france , cantons are not subdivisions of the `` arrondissements '' so they could have `` communes '' that belong to different `` arrondissements '' .
