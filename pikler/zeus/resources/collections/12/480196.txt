Sajida Mubarak Atrous al-Rishawi     sajida mubarak atrous al-rishawi ( born c. 1970-4 february 2015 ) was a iraqi failed suicide bomber . she has been convicted in the november 9 , 2005 amman bombings in jordan but survived when her explosive belt failed to detonate . al-qaida in iraq claimed responsibility for the triple bombings that hit three hotels . she was sentenced to death as a result of her involvement in the hotel suicide bombings . jordan executed her on february 4 , 2015 in response for the execution of jordanian pilot muath al-kasasbeh by the isis terrorist organization . jordan had expressed willingness to swap al-rishawi in exchange for the pilot 's safe return , however the pilot had already been killed .
