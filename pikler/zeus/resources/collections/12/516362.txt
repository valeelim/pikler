The Burlington Free Press     burlington free press is a daily newspaper based in burlington , vermont , in the united states . with a circulation of about 20,166 daily and 27,830 on sundays , it is the largest daily newspaper in vermont . the newspaper has been hit by waves of layoffs over the past several years and employs 272 full-time employees in its burlington headquarters . international news is usually reprinted stories supplied by the associated press and reuters news services .
