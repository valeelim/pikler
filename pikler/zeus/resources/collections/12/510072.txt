2014–15 Eredivisie     the 2014–15 eredivisie was the 59th eredivisie tournament . psv eindhoven won the tournament , ending afc ajax 's winning streak that was going since 2011. because vitesse won the play-offs , they then qualified for the 2015–16 uefa europa league third qualifying round . two teams from the 2014–15 eredivisie and eight from the 2014–15 eerste divisie participated in these play-offs . in the end , de graafschap and roda jc kerkrade won the play-offs , meaning that they move on to the 2015–16 eredivisie , the two teams from the eredivisie are relegated , and the six other teams remain in the eerste divisie .
