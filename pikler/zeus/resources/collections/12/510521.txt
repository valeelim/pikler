Stjarnan     stjarnan f.c . ( stjarnan knattspyrnudeild ; english : star f.c . ) is an icelandic sports club from garðabær . it was founded in 1960. it is best known for their association football team . the men 's football team has won the úrvalsdeild once . the women 's team has won it three times . the volleyball team has also won the icelandic championship five times . other websites . - official football club website
