Legionnaires' disease    legionnaires ' disease legionnaires ' disease is a form of pneumonia caused by any type of `` legionella '' bacteria . over 90 % of cases are caused by `` legionella pneumophila '' . the length of time between exposure to the bacteria and the appearance of symptoms is generally two to ten days . it can be as much as 20 days . people with legionnaires ' usually have fever , chills , and a cough . some also have muscle aches , headache , tiredness , loss of appetite , diarrhea or vomiting . people of any age may have legionnaires ' disease . it is most common in middle aged and older people .
