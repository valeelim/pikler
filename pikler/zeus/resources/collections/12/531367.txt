Bad Aibling rail accident     the bad aibling rail accident was an accident that happened on the railway line in bad aibling in bavaria , germany , on 9 february 2016. eleven people were killed and many more injured when two trains collided head-on , along a single-track line . the accident happened on a section of the line with only one track . the line is equipped with a security system which should have prevented the accident . it is currently unclear if the accident occurred because of a system malfunction or because of human error . at the current state of research , an error of the train drivers or a signal malfunction look unlikely .
