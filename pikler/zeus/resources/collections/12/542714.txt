François Maistre     françois maistre ( 14 may 1925 – 16 may 2016 ) was a french , television and theatre actor . he was born in demigny , saône-et-loire , france . he appeared in nearly 100 movies between 1960 until his retirement in 2003. he was known for his roles in `` angélique , marquise des anges '' and `` the discreet charm of the bourgeoisie '' . maistre died in france on 17 may 2016 , aged 91 .
