Rodrigo Duterte     rodrigo `` rody '' roa duterte ( born march 28 , 1945 ) , nicknamed digong , is a filipino lawyer and politician who is the current president of the philippines . because of the urge of most filipinos , he announced that he would run as a candidate for president of the philippines on november 21 , 2015. on may 9 , 2016 , duterte was elected president . he was among the longest-serving mayors in the philippines . he had previously been a long-time mayor of davao city from 1988 through 1998 , from 2000 through 2010 , and from 2013 through 2016 .
