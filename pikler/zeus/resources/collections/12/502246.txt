Grace Lee Whitney     grace lee whitney ( born mary ann chase ; april 1 , 1930 - may 1 , 2015 ) was an american actress and entertainer . she was best known as janice rand on the `` star trek '' television series . whitney died at her home in coarsegold , california on may 1 , 2015 from natural causes , aged 85. other websites . - grace lee whitney official autograph site - janice rand history - grace lee whitney- notable names database
