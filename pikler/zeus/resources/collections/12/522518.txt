Don't Disturb This Groove (album)    do n't disturb this groove ( album ) do n't disturb this groove is the fourth studio album recorded by american r & b/electropop band the system . released by atlantic records in 1987 , the album became a highlight of the system 's career , as it has been the most commercially successful album , surpassing their hugely successful debut , `` sweat '' . it reached no . 62 on the `` billboard '' 200 and no . 1 on the r & b albums chart . the album was produced by its band members , david frank and mic murphy . successful singles from this album include the band 's first ( and to date , only ) top 10 hit on the pop charts with the contemporary r & b ballad title track , `` do n't disturb this groove '' and urban hit `` nighttime lover '' . doug e. fresh is featured on the track `` house of rhythm '' . personnel . - audrey wheeler , dolette mcdonald , fonzi thornton , michelle cobbs , phillip ballou – backing vocals - omar hakim – drums on `` save me '' - ira siegel , steve stevens – electric guitar - paul pesco – electric guitar - jimmy maelen – percussion
