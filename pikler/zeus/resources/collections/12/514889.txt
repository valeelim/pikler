Southern Zazaki     southern zazaki ( in zaza langauge known as : `` zazakiyê veroci '' , `` dımılki '' ) is one of main dialects of the zaza language . this dialect is spoken in southeastern provinces of republic of turkey such as şanlıurfa ( mainly in siverek ) , diyarbakır , bitlis and adıyaman . southern zazaki is spoken by more than 1,500,000 people in places where southern zazas ( known as dimlis ) living .
