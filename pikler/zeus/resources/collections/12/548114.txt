2016 Nice attack     the 2016 nice attack happened on 14 july 2016 in nice , france . a truck drove into people celebrating bastille day , the national day of france . more than eighty people were killed during the incident . gunfire was reported ; at first , people thought it was fireworks . at around 22:45 local time , a truck was driven into a large crowd of people at promenade des anglais . later , guns were fired . the driver , tunisian truck driver mohamed lahouaiej-bouhlel , was killed by police .
