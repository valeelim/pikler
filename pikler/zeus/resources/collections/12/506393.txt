Alex Morgan     alexandra patricia morgan ( born 2 july 1989 in san dimas , california , united states ) is an american football forward . she currently plays for the orlando pride in the national women 's soccer league , the top tier of women 's soccer in the u.s. she is also a member of the united states women 's national soccer team ( uswnt ) . morgan was the first pick in the 2011 fifa women 's world cup . she was an olympic gold medalist in the 2012 summer olympics , which was the uswnt 's 4th olympic gold medal . she also played in the 2015 fifa women 's world cup , and her home country , the united states , won their third world cup . she is known for being one of the greatest american forwards of all time .
