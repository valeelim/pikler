Lina Morgan     maría de los ángeles lópez segovia ( 20 march 1937 – 20 august 2015 ) , better known as lina morgan , was a spanish actress and showgirl . she performed in movie , theatre , and television . she was known for her roles in `` hostal royal manzanares '' ( 1996 ) , `` una de dos '' ( 1998 ) and `` academia de baile gloria '' ( 2001 ) .
