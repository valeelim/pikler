Fetty Wap     willie maxwell ii ( born june 7 , 1991 ) , better known by his stage name , fetty wap , is an american rapper . he became known for his 2014 single `` trap queen '' which was able to peak at # 2 on the us `` billboard '' hot 100 chart in may 2015 and helped him gain a record deal with 300 entertainment . wap released his first self-titled studio album on september 25 , 2015. the album featured the single `` trap queen '' , `` 679 '' , `` my way '' , and `` again '' . the album debuted at # 1 on the us `` billboard '' 200. wap was nominated for two grammy awards in the categories of best rap performance and best rap song at the 2016 grammy awards . maxwell had glaucoma in both eyes as a child . in a 2015 interview , he said that doctors were only able to save his right eye and gave him a glass eye . other websites . - official website
