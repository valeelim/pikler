Boom Clap     `` boom clap '' is an electropop song by charli xcx . this song was released as the first single from `` the fault in our stars '' soundtrack . it was also on xcx 's studio album `` sucker '' . the single was released on june 15 , 2014. it peaked at # 6 on the uk singles chart . it was also # 8 on the `` billboard '' hot 100 chart . it got positive reviews by music critics .
