Walter Benjamin     walter bendix schönflies benjamin ( ; 15 july 1892 – 26 september 1940 ) was a german philosopher and cultural critic . benjamin was born to a jewish family in berlin , then-german empire . he worked in many subjects such as german idealism , romanticism , historical materialism , and jewish mysticism . he helped aesthetic theory and western marxism grow . benjamin committed suicide by taking an overdose of morphine in portbou at the french–spanish border while attempting to escape from the nazis at the age of 48. other websites . - walter benjamin , at the stanford encyclopedia of philosophy - the internationale walter benjamin gesellschaft . `` in english and german .
