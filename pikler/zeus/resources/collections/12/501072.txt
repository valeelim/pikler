Cindy Yang     peng hsin-yi ( ; 4 december 1990 – 21 april 2015 ) , better known by her stage name cindy yang ( chinese : 楊又穎 ) , was a taiwanese actress and model . she was known for her appearances on `` 100 % entertainment '' and the movie `` first of may '' ( 2015 ) . yang was born in taichung . she was the daughter of politician peng tso-kwei . she committed suicide by helium inhalation on 21 april 2015 , aged 24. her funeral was held on 24 april .
