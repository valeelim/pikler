West Frisian language     west frisian ( ) is a language that is mainly spoken in friesland , netherlands . as of 2005 , 94 % of the people in friesland understand west frisian , 74 % can speak it , 75 % can read it and 27 % can write it . west frisian uses the latin alphabet . a , e , o and u can sometimes have unique accents added to them . in western frisian , there are two types of genders that nouns can have . one is the `` common '' gender ( where both the male and female genders are put together ) . the other type is a neutral gender noun . like many other languages , west frisian has singular and plural nouns . the two main suffixes used in the language , are `` '' - ( e ) n '' and `` -s '' . '' west frisian also used three types of verbs : weak , strong and irregular . after a series of riots by pro-frisian activists , west frisian became an official language in the netherlands in 1956. west frisian is a required subject in schools in friesland . courts in friesland speak west frisian . people in court are allowed to go under oath speaking west frisian .
