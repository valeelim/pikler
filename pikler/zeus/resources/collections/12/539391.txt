Isan language     isan is the name for a number of closely related dialects which are spoken in the northeast of thailand and neighbouring countries . isan is mostly used for informal communication . isan consists of a number of dialects , there is no official language . people who speak one dialect usually understand the others . when isan is written down , the thai alphabet is used most of the time . very rarely , the lao alphabet , which is also used to write the laotian language is used . about 20 milllion people speak the isan language as their first language . about a third of the population of thailand , and 80 % of the speakers of laotian understand it .
