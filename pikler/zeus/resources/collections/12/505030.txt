Style (Taylor Swift song)    style ( taylor swift song ) `` style '' is a song from taylor swift . it is the third track from her fifth studio album , `` 1989 '' . it was released as the third single from the album on february 9 , 2015. it reached number six on the us `` billboard '' hot 100. it topped the mainstream top 40/pop songs chart in the united states . the song contains elements of disco , funk-pop , and pop rock .
