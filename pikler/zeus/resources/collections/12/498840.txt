Måns Zelmerlöw     måns petter albert sahlén zelmerlöw ( born 13 june 1986 ) is a swedish singer . he participated in the talent singing competition `` idol 2005 '' . he won `` let 's dance '' 2006 , both broadcasted on tv4 . he won melodifestivalen 2015. he represented sweden in the eurovision song contest 2015 in vienna , austria . in the eurovision final zelmerlöw won with 365 pointsahead of russia and italy . he will host the eurovision song contest 2016 to be held in stockholm along with petra mede .
