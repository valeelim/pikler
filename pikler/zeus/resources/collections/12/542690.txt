Sammy Ellis     samuel joseph ellis ( february 11 , 1941 – may 13 , 2016 ) was an american professional baseball pitcher . he played in major league baseball ( mlb ) for the cincinnati reds ( 1962 – 1967 ) , california angels ( 1968 ) , and chicago white sox ( 1969 ) . he was born in youngstown , ohio . his career began in the early 1960s . he retired in 1970. ellis was an mlb all-star in 1965. ellis died of cancer at his home in temple terrace , florida on may 13 , 2016 , aged 75. or baseball almanac or retrosheet or venezuelan professional baseball league
