Peter Roskam     peter james roskam ( born september 13 , 1961 ) is the u.s. representative for , serving since 2007. he is a member of the republican party . he served as the chief deputy majority whip from 2011 to 2014 , ranking fourth among house republican leaders . he has also served in the illinois senate and the illinois house of representatives . he now serves as chairman of the house ways and means subcommittee on oversight . other websites . - congressman peter roskam official u.s. house website - peter roskam for congress
