Heliobatis     heliobatis is an extinct genus of stingray . at present the genus contains the single species heliobatis radians . the fossil is found in the early eocene , wasatchian stage , fossil lake deposits . fossil lake is part of the green river formation in southwest wyoming . `` heliobatis '' is one of only two known rays to have been found in the green river formation . the other species , `` asterotrygon maloneyi '' , was recognized and described in 2004 .
