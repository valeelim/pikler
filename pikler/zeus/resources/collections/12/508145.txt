Kratos (God of War character)    kratos ( god of war character ) kratos , the `` ghost of sparta '' , is a fictional video game character from sony santa monica 's god of war series , which is loosely based on greek mythology . kratos first appeared in the 2005 video game god of war , which led to the development of six additional games featuring the character as the protagonist . kratos also appears as the protagonist of the god of war comic series and novels . he has been consistently voiced by terrence c. carson , and antony del rio voiced the character as a child in god of war : ghost of sparta .
