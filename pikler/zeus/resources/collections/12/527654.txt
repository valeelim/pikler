Brad Fittler     bradley scott `` brad '' fittler ( born 5 february 1972 ) is an australian former professional rugby league player and coach . he played mostly as a five-eighth . his career lasted from 1989 to 2004. he played for the penrith panthers until 1995 and later for the sydney roosters . he also coached the roosters from 2007 to 2009. fittler was born in auburn , new south wales . he is of german ancestry . he has three children ; two of whom with his partner marie liarris . other websites . - brad fittler stats at rugby league project
