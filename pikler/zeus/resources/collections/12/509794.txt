Tove Lo     ebba tove elsa nilsson ( born 29 october 1987 ) , better known by her stage name tove lo , is a swedish singer and songwriter . she was born and raised in stockholm , sweden . she graduated from musical magnet school rytmus musikergymnasiet . early in her life she started writing poetry and short stories . she wrote songs for other singers including icona pop and girls aloud . lo then began writing songs for herself . she self-released her single `` love ballad '' in 2012. her 2013 single `` habits '' reached # 3 on the billboard hot 100. in 2014 she released her debut album `` queen of the clouds '' . it peaked at # 14 on the billboard hot 200 . < br >
