This Used to Be My Playground     `` this used to be my playground '' is a soft rock ballad from american singer madonna . the song is about memories of childhood dreams . the song was featured in the credits of the movie `` a league of their own '' in 1992. it is , however , not on the soundtrack album . the song spent one week at # 1 on `` billboard '' hot 100 in summer 1992. the longest version of the song is just over six minutes in length .
