Muş Province     muş province ( ) is a province in eastern turkey . it is 8,196 km² in area , and has a population of 406,886 according to a 2010 estimate . the population was 453,654 in 2000. the provincial capital is the city of muş . another town in muş province , malazgirt ( `` manzikert '' ) , is famous for the battle of manzikert of 1071. the majority of the province 's population is kurdish . muş province is divided into 6 districts ( capital district in bold ) : - bulanık - hasköy - korkut - malazgirt - varto other websites . - governor 's web site . - muş weather forecast information
