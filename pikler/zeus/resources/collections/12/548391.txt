Janez Bernik     janez bernik ( 6 september 1933 – 15 july 2016 ) was a slovenian painter and academic . he was born in gunclje , now part of ljubljana . his works include painting , sculpture , graphic design , illustration , tapestry , book design , and poetry . he won the prešeren fund award in 1963. bernik died on 15 july 2016 in the municipality of prebold , slovenia , aged 82 .
