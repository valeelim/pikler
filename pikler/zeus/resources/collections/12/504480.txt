Joaquim Durão     joaquim manuel durão ( 25 october 1930 – 21 may 2015 ) was a portuguese chess player . he was an international master from 1975 until his death . he was 13 times portuguese champion , and represented his country in ten chess olympiads . he was also president of the portuguese chess federation . durão was born in lisbon . he died on 21 may 2015 , aged 84 .
