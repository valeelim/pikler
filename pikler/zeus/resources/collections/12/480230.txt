Prague Manifesto (Esperanto)    prague manifesto ( esperanto ) prague manifesto ( ) is a manifesto in which are seven principles for esperanto movement . it was published during world esperanto congress in prague , czech republic in 1996 by the representative of unesco and participants of the meeting . because the manifesto shows advantages of accepting of esperanto as international auxiliary language , people consider it as the modern manifesto of finvenkism ( the movement which wants esperanto to be an official international auxiliary language ) . other websites . - text of prague manifesto in many languages
