Abortion in Azerbaijan     abortion in azerbaijan is legal upon request within the first twenty-eight weeks of pregnancy . after twenty-eight weeks , it is legal when : - the abortion will save the woman 's life or preserve her health ; - the father died during the pregnancy ; - either parent was imprisoned ; - the pregnancy is a result of rape ; - there is a disability in the family ; or - the parents have divorced . additionally , a physician may approve any other reason to carry out an abortion .
