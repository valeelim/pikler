Metropolitan Community Church     the metropolitan community church ( mcc ) is an international protestant christian denomination . it is based in los angeles , california . it has 222 member congregations in 37 countries . it has a specific outreach to lesbian , gay , bisexual and transgender families and communities . the mcc has property worth $ 50 million . it has 40,000 members . they want to double in size in the next ten years . they also want acceptance by other christian organizations . the mcc currently has `` official observer '' status with the world council of churches . mcc supports same-sex marriage . the mcc has been denied membership in the us national council of churches . many local mcc congregations are members of local ecumenical partnerships around the world . mcc also belongs to several statewide councils of churches in the united states .
