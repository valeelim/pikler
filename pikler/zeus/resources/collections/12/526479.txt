Buick Rendezvous     buick rendezvous was a midsize crossover suv . it was built by general motors from 2002 to 2007. the rendezvous was built on a short wheelbase of the gm u/minivan platform similar to the pontiac aztek . it slotted between the rainier suv and the terraza minivan . from 2002 to 2006 it offered the option of front wheel-drive or all-wheel drive , this was dropped for the final model year . the rendezvous was later discontinued due to a drop in sales and replaced by the slightly larger buick enclave cuv which also replaced the rainier and terraza .
