Rita Gam     rita gam ( april 2 , 1927march 22 , 2016 ) was an american and television actress and documentary filmmaker . she was nominated for a golden globe and won the silver bear for best actress . she was known for her roles in `` the thief '' and `` king of kings '' . she was also known for being sidney lumet 's first wife . gam shared the silver bear for best actress award with viveca lindfors at the 1962 berlin film festival , for their performances in tad danielewski 's `` no exit '' . gam died on march 22 , 2016 at the cedars-sinai medical center in los angeles , california from respiratory failure , aged 88. other websites . - rita gam at the university of wisconsin 's actors studio audio collection
