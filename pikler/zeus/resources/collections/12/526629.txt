Pilot Butte, Saskatchewan    pilot butte , saskatchewan pilot butte is a town in saskatchewan , canada . it has over 2,100 people living in it . most people live in town but drive into the nearby city regina to work . the town got its name after the hill ( or butte ) that it surrounds . pilot butte is sometimes known as the `` sand capital of canada '' because of all of the sand and gravel it has . the pilot butte storm are a junior ice hockey team that play in pilot butte .
