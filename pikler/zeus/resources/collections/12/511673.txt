IRT Broadway - Seventh Avenue Line     the irt broadway - seventh avenue line is one of the lines of the new york city subway . there are three services on the line , colored tomato red and numbered 1 , 2 and 3. services . - 1 : this is a local service from 242nd street to south ferry . - 2 : this is an express service from 241st street to flatbush avenue ( limited to new lots avenue during rush hours ) . - 3 : this is an express service from 148th street to new lots avenue ( limited to 42nd street / times square during late nights ) .
