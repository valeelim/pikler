Benny Banks (rapper)    benny banks ( rapper ) benjamen o'leary better known by his stage name benny banks is a british rapper . he was born and raised in islington , north london . he is popular for releasing his remix of tyga 's `` rack city '' . he is also popular for featuring on rapper yungen 's track `` ai n't on nuttin '' . he was recently signed to warner music . as of 2012 he had over three million hits on youtube . his hit “ bada bing ” was a commercial success . it debuted 18 february 2012 at # 93 on the british itunes charts . discography . - bada bing !
