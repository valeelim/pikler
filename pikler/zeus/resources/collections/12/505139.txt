Wabasha, Minnesota    wabasha , minnesota wabasha is a city in wabasha county , minnesota , usa . it is also the county seat.the population was 2,495 in 2013. wabasha is located in southeastern minnesota on the mississippi river . established in 1830 , wabasha is the oldest town in minnesota . it is also the oldest continuously occupied city in the upper mississippi river valley . wabasha is named after chief wa-pa-shaw of the sioux nation . wabasha was the setting of the movies `` grumpy old men '' and `` grumpier old men '' . < br >
