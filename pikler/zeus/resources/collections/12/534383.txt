Sophie Dessus     sophie dessus ( 24 september 1955 – 3 march 2016 ) was a french socialist politician . she was born in suresnes , france . she represented corrèze 's first constituency in the national assembly , the first woman to represent corrèze in the national assembly . she served from 20 june 2012 until her death in 3 march 2016. dessus died in limoges , france of cancer on 3 march 2016. she was aged 60. other websites . - dessus 's official website
