Bacillus (shape)    bacillus ( shape ) a bacillus ( plural bacilli ) is a rod-shaped cylindrical bacteria . bacilli are found in many different taxonomic groups of bacteria . - when the name `` bacillus '' is capitalized and italicized , it refers to a specific genus of bacteria . - if it is not capitalized or italicized the name refers to any rod-shaped bacterium . there is no connection between the shape of a bacterium and its colors in the gram staining . in other words , some of them are gram negative and some are gram positive .
