Rey (Star Wars)    rey ( star wars ) rey is a fictional character in the `` star wars '' franchise , played by daisy ridley . she is the main protagonist of the `` star wars '' sequel trilogy . she first appeared as one of the lead characters in `` '' . rey is a scavenger who was left behind ( for protection ) on the planet jakku when she was a child , and soon becomes involved with the resistance 's conflict with the first order after meeting finn , a former stormtrooper , and bb-8 , the droid of ace resistance pilot poe dameron . although her background is unknown , her last name was not said as a decision by j. j. abrams . this led to fan theories by saying that she is the daughter of han solo , princess leia or luke skywalker or even obi-wan kenobi 's granddaughter . other websites . - rey in the official starwars.com encyclopedia
