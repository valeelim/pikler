Subnautica     subnautica is an open world survival game made by unknown worlds entertainment . `` subnautica '' lets the player explore the sea of an alien planet by scuba diving , or using vehicles . they must collect resources and food to survive.. it came out on steam early access on december 2014 , and is currently being made for xbox one . the full version is planned to come out for august 2016. the player in a first-person view controls the lone survivor of a crashed ship . the objective is to explore the planet and collect resources to survive , the player must make sure to not run out of oxygen and keep fed and hydrated . the player can create bases and build vehicles . the game uses a day and night cycle . `` subnautica '' was announced by unknown worlds entertainment on december 17 , 2013. they used unity rather then spark which they used for natural selection 2. the development team chose to not include lethal weapons in the game . charlie cleveland , the game 's director , described subnautica as `` one vote towards a world with less guns , '' .
