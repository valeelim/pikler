Dalian     dalian ( ) is an important port city in the liaoning province of china . it is located on the liaodong peninsula . it is the northernmost warm water port in china as well as the southernmost city in northeast china . the name of the city translates into `` great link '' , due to its strategic location on the coast of the east china sea since it is used for trade with countries like russia , the north and south korea , and japan .
