El Cerrito, California    el cerrito , california el cerrito is a city in contra costa county , california , united states and it also lies in the san francisco bay area . it was founded by refugees after the 1906 san francisco earthquake . el cerrito was incorporated on august 23 , 1917. el cerrito has a cool mediterranean climate . this means that el cerrito has generally mild weather , with warm summers and cool winters . it rains an average of 23 inches . it snowed in el cerrito in 1976 , and snow is sometimes on the forecast as well . the 2010 united states census said that el cerrito had a population of 23,549. the racial makeup was : - 12,543 ( 53.3 % ) white , - 6,439 ( 27.3 % ) asian , - 2,621 ( 11.1 % ) hispanic or latino , - 1,819 ( 7.7 % ) african american , - 107 ( 0.5 % ) native american , - 37 ( 0.2 % ) pacific islander , - 1,079 ( 4.6 % ) from other races , and 1,525 ( 6.5 % ) from two or more races . the 2000 united states census said that el cerrito had a population of 23,171. the racial makeup was : - 13,390 ( 57.79 % ) white , - 5,649 ( 24.38 % ) asian ,
