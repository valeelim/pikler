Judd Gregg     judd gregg ( born february 14 , 1947 ) is an american politician . he was a united states senator from the state of new hampshire . before becoming a u.s. senator , he was governor of new hampshire from 1989 to 1993. gregg was born on february 14 , 1947 in nashua , new hampshire . he studied at columbia university and at boston university school of law . he is married to kathleen maclellan gregg . they have two children .
