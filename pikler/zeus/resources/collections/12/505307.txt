Robert D. Hales     robert dean hales ( born august 24 , 1932 ) is a member of the quorum of the twelve apostles of the church of jesus christ of latter-day saints ( lds church ) . as a member of the quorum of the twelve , hales is accepted by the church as a prophet , seer , and revelator . currently , he is the seventh most senior apostle among the ranks of the church . references . - `` elder robert dean hales : assistant to the council of the twelve '' , `` ensign '' , may 1975 other websites . - `` general authorities : elder robert d. hales '' , lds.org - grampa bill 's g.a . pages : robert d. hales - hales on `` special witnesses of christ ''
