Tetraplegia     tetraplegia ( or quadriplegia ) is a condition in which a human loses use of all limbs and the torso . it usually affects both the ability to move and the use of the senses . `` quadriplegia '' is the common term in north america . `` tetraplegia '' is the term in europe . tetraplegia is caused by damage either to the brain or to the spinal cord at the level of the cervical vertebrae . people with an injury at the c1 vertebra may lose functions from the neck down . they may need a machine to breathe for them . people with injuries at the c7 vertebra may still be able to use their arms and hands .
