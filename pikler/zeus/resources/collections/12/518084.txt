Gareth     gareth was one of the knights of the round table in king arthur 's court . in sir thomas mallory 's classic story , `` le morte d'arthur '' ( french for `` the death of arthur '' ) , gareth is given the nickname of `` beaumains '' by sir kay . after one year , gareth revealed his identity as sir gawain 's brother and went on a quest as a gift from arthur . he was named a knight by sir lancelot after a jousting and sword fighting challenge . he dies in the same story , which is the last in the series .
