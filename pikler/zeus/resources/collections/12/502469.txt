Frits Bolkestein     frederik `` frits '' bolkestein ( ; born 4 april 1933 ) is a retired dutch politician of the people 's party for freedom and democracy ( vvd ) . he served as a member of the house of representatives from 16 january 1978 , until 5 november 1982. other websites . - prof.dr.mr . f.
