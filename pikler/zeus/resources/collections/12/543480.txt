Viktor Kulikov     viktor kulikov ( born july 5 , 1921 – died may 28 , 2013 in moscow ) was a marshal of the soviet union and commander-in-chief of the warsaw pact from 1977 until 1989. kulikov first served in the military during the great patriotic war ( 1941–1945 ) . after that , he was given the title of hero of the soviet union . in 1983 , he was honored with the lenin prize .
