CIH     cih ( also known as chernobyl or spacefiller ) is a windows 9x computer virus which first appeared in 1998. it was created by chen ing-hau , a chinese student . the name it was given is based on his initials . after the virus is run on april 26 , it will start adding random data to files on the hard drive . this will leave the computer unable to operate . it does not infect windows nt computers . it resulted in over 1 billion usd worth in damages . in 1999 , ibm shipped some of their aptiva computers with the virus already on the computer . some games , like activision 's sin and electronic arts ' wing commander came with the virus .
