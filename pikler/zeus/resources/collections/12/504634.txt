Payback (2015)    payback ( 2015 ) payback ( 2015 ) was a professional wrestling pay-per-view produced by wwe that was held on may 17 , 2015 at royal farms arena in baltimore , maryland . it was the third payback event . the event was free to new subscribers on wwe 's monthly subscription service , the wwe network . the main event saw seth rollins defeat roman reigns , dean ambrose and randy orton in a fatal 4-way match to retain the wwe world heavyweight championship . rollins won by pinfall after a pedigree to orton . other websites . - official payback 2015 website
