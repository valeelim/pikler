Sason     sason ( `` sasun '' ; from ; formerly known as sasun or sassoun ) is a district in the batman province of turkey . it was formerly part of the sanjak of siirt , which was in diyarbakır vilayet until 1880. it was in bitlis vilayet in 1892. later it became part of muş sanjak in bitlis vilayet . sason remained part of muş until 1927. it was one of the districts of siirt province until 1993. the boundaries of the district varied considerably in time . the current borders are not the same as in the 19th century . this is when the district of sasun was situated more to the north ( mostly territory now included in the central district of muş ) . sasun , as it is called by armenians , holds a prominent role in armenian culture and history . it is the setting of `` daredevils of sassoun '' , armenia 's national epic . in the late 19th and early 20th centuries it was a major location of armenian `` fedayi '' activities , who staged two uprisings against the ottoman authorities . they rebelled against the kurdish tribes in 1894 and 1904 .
