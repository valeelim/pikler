Lakmé     lakmé is a french opera in three acts by léo delibes , first performed at the opéra-comique in paris , on april 14 , 1883 , libretto by edmond gondinet and philippe gille , after pierre loti rarahu 's `` le mariage de loti '' . an immediate success , it has remained one of the most popular of french operas . principal roles and voice types . - lakmé - soprano - gérald - tenor - mallika - mezzo-soprano - nilakantha - bass discography . - 1940 : lily pons ( lakmé ) , armand tokatyan ( gérald ) , ira petina ( mallika ) , ezio pinza ( nilakantha ) ; new york metropolitan opera chorus and orchestra , wilfrid pelletier - ( the golden age ; live ) - 1952 : mado robin ( lakmé ) , libero de luca ( gérald ) , agnés disney ( mallika ) , jean borthayre ( nilakantha ) ; chœurs et orchestre du théâtre national de l'opéra-comique , georges sébastian - ( decca ) - 1967 : joan sutherland ( lakmé ) , alain vanzo ( gérald ) , jane berbié ( mallika ) , gabriel bacquier ( nilakantha ) ; chœurs et orchestre national de l'opéra de monte carlo , richard bonynge - ( decca )
