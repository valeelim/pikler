Blood Fire Death     blood fire death is the fourth studio album by the swedish extreme metal band bathory . it was released on 8 october 1988 , through the music label of under one flag . the album is mainly black metal , but it is sometimes called the first viking metal album . in 2009 , ign included `` blood fire death '' in their `` 10 great black metal albums '' list . people . - quorthon – guitar and vocals - vvornth – drums - kothaar – bass - peter nicolai arbo – album cover painting , `` the wild hunt of odin ( åsgårdsreien ) '' - pelle matteus – sleeve photography
