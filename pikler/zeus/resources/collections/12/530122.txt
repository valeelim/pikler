Michel Legrand     michel legrand ( born 24 february 1932 ) is a french musical composer , arranger , conductor , and pianist . he was born courbevoie , in present-day france . legrand is a well-known composer . he has written over 200 movie and television scores , as well as many memorable songs . he is best known for his movie music , such as `` the umbrellas of cherbourg '' ( 1964 ) and `` the thomas crown affair '' ( 1968 ) . `` the thomas crown affair '' featured the song `` the windmills of your mind '' . legrand won his first academy award for this song . he would win another academy award in 1971 for `` summer of '42 '' . other websites . - biography of michel legrand , from radio france internationale - non official website of michel legrand +
