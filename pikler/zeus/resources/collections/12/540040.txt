Pasir Ris MRT Station     pasir ris mrt station ( ew1 ) is an mrt station in singapore . it is part of the east west mrt line . the station serves as a terminus for the east west mrt line . it was the first station to be fitted with platform screen doors . timelime . - 16 december 1989 : station opened - 2009 : half-height platform screen doors installed previous : n/a < br > next : tampines ( ew2/dt32 ) formerly the code was : e12 ( 1989 - 2001 )
