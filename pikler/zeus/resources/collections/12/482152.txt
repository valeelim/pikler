IK Heros     ik heros is a sports club in smedjebacken in sweden . it was established in 1915. the club has bandy and track and field athletics sections . it earlier even played soccer . the men 's bandy team has played 11 seasons in the swedish top division . between 1940 and 1965-1966. the men 's soccer team has played five seasons in the swedish third division.. other websites . - bandy - track and field athletics
