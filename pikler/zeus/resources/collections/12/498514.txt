Jack Higgins     jack higgins ( born 27 july 1929 ) is a british writer . he is one of the best-selling authors of popular thrillers and espionage novels . his breakthrough novel `` the eagle has landed '' ( 1975 ) . he is also known for his books ; `` a prayer for the dying '' ( 1987 ) , `` the eagle has flown '' ( 1991 ) , `` thunder point '' ( 1993 ) , `` angel of death '' ( 1995 ) , `` flight of eagles '' ( 1998 ) , and `` day of reckoning '' ( 2000 ) . other websites . - the unofficial jack higgins homepage - jack higgins blog - author 's page at harpercollins publishers
