Muiderslot     the muiderslot is a castle-museum in the netherlands , situated in muiden . it is southeast of amsterdam . it is one of the better known castles in the netherlands . the muiderslot is part of the defense line of amsterdam . it is on the world heritage list . the castle was built in 1280 by floris v. pieter corneliszoon hooft used to live there . references . - amsterdam muiderslot castel
