Oʻtkir Sultonov     oʻtkir toʻxtamurodovich sultonov ( russian : уткир тухтамурадович султанов , `` utkir tukhtamuradovich sultanov '' ) ( 14 july 1939 – 29 november 2015 ) was a uzbek politician . he was the prime minister of uzbekistan from 21 december 1995 until 12 december 2003. sultonov died in tashkent , uzbekistan at the age of 76 .
