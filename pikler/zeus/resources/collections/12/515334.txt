Guy Béart     guy béhart-hasson ( 16 july 1930 – 16 september 2015 ) , better known as guy béart , was a french singer and songwriter . he released over 240 songs and 25 albums during his sixty-plus year career . béart was born in cairo , egypt to a jewish family . he also grew up in lebanon and later moved to paris . his daughter is actress emmanuelle béart ( born 1963 ) . béart died from a heart attack on 16 september 2015 in garches , aged 85 .
