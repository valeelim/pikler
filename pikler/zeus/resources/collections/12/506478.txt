Rupert Davies     rupert davies ( 22 may 191622 november 1976 ) was a british actor . he is best known for playing the title role in the bbc 's 1960s television adaptation of `` maigret '' , based on georges simenon 's `` maigret '' novels . davies was born in liverpool . he died in london from cancer , aged 60. other websites . - rupert davies 's appearance on this is your life
