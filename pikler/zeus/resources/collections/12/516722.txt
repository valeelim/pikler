Bathory (band)    bathory ( band ) bathory was an extreme metal band from vällingby , sweden . the band formed in 1983. bathory broke up in 2004. the band 's main songwriter was quorthon . bathory released 12 studio albums and 5 compilation albums . bathory 's first four albums were `` the blueprint for scandinavian black metal '' . the band first played black metal but moved on to viking metal in its later years . band members . - quorthon ( thomas börje forsberg ) – vocals , guitar , bass guitar , drums - freddan/hanoi ( frederick melander ) – bass guitar - vans mcburger ( jonas åkerlund ) – drums - ribban – bass guitar - stefan larsson – drums - adde – bass guitar - paul pålle lundburg – drums - kothaar – bass guitar - vvornth – drums discography . studio albums . - `` bathory '' ( 1984 ) - `` the return '' ( 1985 ) - `` under the sign of the black mark '' ( 1987 ) - `` blood fire death '' ( 1988 ) - `` hammerheart '' ( 1990 ) - `` twilight of the gods '' ( 1991 ) - `` requiem '' ( 1994 ) - `` octagon '' ( 1995 ) - `` blood on ice '' ( 1996 ) – recorded in 1989 - `` destroyer of worlds '' ( 2001 )
