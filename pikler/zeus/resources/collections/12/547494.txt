Benoîte Groult     benoîte groult ( january 31 , 1920 , in paris – june 20 , 2016 in hyères ) was a french journalist , writer and feminist activist . her novel `` les vaisseaux du cœur '' , published in 1988 , was called pornographic by some because of its explicit sexual depictions . it was filmed by andrew birkin in 1992 as `` salt on our skin '' . in april 2010 , she became commander of the légion d'honneur .
