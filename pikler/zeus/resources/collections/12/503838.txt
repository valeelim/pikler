Pontiac G6     pontiac g6 was a mid-sized car sold by the pontiac division of general motors . it was available as a coupe , sedan or convertible . the g6 was introduced in 2005 to replace the grand am . the last model year for the g6 was 2010. it was unchanged from the 2009 model . general motors declared bankruptcy in 2009 and pontiac division was discontinued . the 2010 g6 was the very last u.s. built pontiac .
