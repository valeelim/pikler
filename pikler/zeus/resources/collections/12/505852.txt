Alan M. Lovelace     alan mathieson lovelace ( born september 4 , 1929 ) is an american civil servant . he was the deputy administrator of nasa from july 2 , 1976 to july 10 , 1981. lovelace was born in st. petersburg , florida . he graduated with a bachelor of science in chemistry from the university of florida in gainesville in 1951. he received a master of science in organic chemistry in 1952. he became a doctor of philosophy in 1954 , also in organic chemistry . in june 1981 , lovelace was presented with the presidential citizens medal by president ronald reagan . nasa - `` goddard news ''
