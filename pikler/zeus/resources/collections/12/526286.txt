Italy national volleyball team     the italy men 's national volleyball team is the volleyball national team of italy . it dominated international volleyball competitions in the 1990s and early 2000s , by winning three world championships in a row ( 1990 , 1994 and 1998 ) , six european championships , one world cup ( 1995 ) and eight world league ( 1990 , 1991 , 1992 , 1994 , 1995 , 1997 , 1999 and 2000 ) . other websites . - official website - fivb profile
