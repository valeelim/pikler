Fernvale LRT Station     fernvale lrt station ( sw5 ) is an lrt station singapore . it is a station along sengkang lrt line . it is on the west loop . fernvale is the only station along the sengkang lrt line which uses side platforms . timeline . - 29 january 2005 : station opened previous : thanggam ( sw4 ) < br > next : layar ( sw6 )
