Karolína Kurková     karolína kurková ( born 28 february 1984 ) , is a czech model . she is best known as a former victoria 's secret angel . kurková was discovered by miuccia prada in 1999. she became the face of louis vuitton in 2001 , chanel in 2003. in 2002 , kurková was the youngest model to be elected the model of the year at the vh1/vogue fashion awards . after walking for victoria 's secret since 2001 , she was an angel from 2005 to 2009. in 2002 and 2006 , kurková wore the fantasy bra , a multimillion dollar bra , during the victoria 's secret fashion show . in 2008 , e ! named her the sexiest woman of the year .
