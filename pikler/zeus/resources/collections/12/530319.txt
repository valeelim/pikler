Francisco Flores Pérez     francisco guillermo flores ( october 17 , 1959 – january 30 , 2016 ) was a salvadoran politician . he served as president of el salvador serving from 1999 to 2004 as a member of the conservative nationalist republican alliance ( arena ) . he was born in santa ana , el salvador . at the time of his death , flores was being investigated for corruption charges . flores died on january 30 , 2016 , at the age of 56. he died in a hospital in san salvador from a stroke after having surgery to treat it . other websites . - biography and tenure , by cidob foundation ( in spanish )
