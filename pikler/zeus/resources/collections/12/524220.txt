Dag Skogheim     dag skogheim ( 17 may 1928 – 4 december 2015 ) was a norwegian teacher , poet , novelist , short story writer , biographer and non-fiction writer . he was born in sømna . he made his literary debut in 1970 with the poetry collection `` ... gagns menneske '' . he was known for his 2001 novel `` sanatorieliv '' . the book was awarded the `` sverre steen prize '' from the norwegian historical association in 2002. skogheim died in oslo , norway from complications following a stroke , aged 87 .
