Water for Your Soul     water for your soul is the seventh studio album by english singer-songwriter joss stone . it was released on 31 july 2015 by her own label , stone 'd records . `` water for your soul '' received mixed to positive reviews from music critics . track listing . - sample credits - `` harry 's symphony '' contains samples from `` here i come '' by barrington levy , `` young lover '' by cocoa tea , `` be careful '' by matthew mcanuff and `` bad boys '' by inner circle
