Steve Davis     steve davis ( born 22 august 1957 ) is an english former professional snooker player . he was born in plumstead , london . he has won the world championship six times and dominated snooker in the eighties of the 20th century . davis started his professional career in 1978. ten years later he became the first player to complete snooker 's `` triple crown '' by winning the uk championship , masters , and world championship in the same season . other websites . - steve davis at snooker.org
