Bill Davis     william grenville `` bill '' davis , ( born july 30 , 1929 ) is a canadian politician . he was born in toronto . he was the 18th premier of ontario , canada , from 1971 to 1985. in a 2012 edition , the institute for research on public policy 's magazine , `` policy options '' , named davis the second-best canadian premier of the last forty years , beaten only by peter lougheed .
