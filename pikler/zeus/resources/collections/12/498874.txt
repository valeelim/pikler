Leroy Jethro Gibbs     leroy jethro gibbs is a fictional character from the cbs television series `` ncis '' , played by mark harmon . he is an ex-scout sniper for the united states marines turned special agent . he commands a team for the naval criminal investigative service . gibbs is the most skilled marksman on the team . he is the most skilled at handling violent situations . he is also a skilled interrogator . gibbs is a natural leader who always accomplishes his mission .
