Xavier Becerra     xavier becerra ( ; born january 26 , 1958 ) is an american politician and the attorney general of california . he previously served in the united states house of representatives for . he had represented downtown los angeles in congress from 1993 to 2017. becerra , a member of the democratic party , was chairman of the house democratic caucus . he was chosen to become attorney general of california after kamala harris was elected to the united states senate . he is the first latino to serve as attorney general of california . other websites . - california department of justice official website of the attorney general
