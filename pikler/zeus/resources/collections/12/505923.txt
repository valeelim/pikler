Anti-revisionism     anti-revisionism is opposition to attempts to modify or reject the fundamentals of communist revolutionary theory and practice . reformism is seen as a concession to communism 's adversaries . revisionism comes from within the party , towards whatever is the standard policy of the party . after stalin 's death , krushchev 's style and policies were thought by stalinists to be revisinist . the people 's republic of china under mao zedong and the people 's republic of albania under enver hoxha proclaimed themselves to be taking an anti-revisionist line and denounced khrushchev 's policies .
