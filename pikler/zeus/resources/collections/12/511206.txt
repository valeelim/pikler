Oceanside Municipal Airport     oceanside municipal airport ( also called the bob maxwell field ) is a public airport that is in oceanside , san diego county , california , united states . the airport covers 43 acres ( 170,000 m2 ) and has one runway . the airport is mostly used for general aviation , which means that no airlines operate at this airport . the airport is owned by airport property ventures and the city of oceanside . other websites . - official website - airport property ventures
