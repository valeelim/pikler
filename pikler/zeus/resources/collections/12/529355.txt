John Higgins     john higgins ( born 18 may 1975 ) is a scottish professional snooker player . he has won the world championship four times . in the modern snooker era only stephen hendry ( 7 ) , steve davis ( 6 ) , ray reardon ( 6 ) and ronnie o'sullivan ( 5 ) were more often world champion . higgins started his professional career in 1992. because a betting scandal he was 2010 banned for six months , but successfully returned to the world snooker tour . other websites . - john higgins at snooker.org
