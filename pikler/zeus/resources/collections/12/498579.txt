Eurovision Song Contest 2015     the eurovision song contest 2015 was the 60th edition of the contest and took place in vienna , austria , after conchita wurst won for austria in copenhagen , denmark in 2014. a total of 40 countries participated in that year , while australia participated for the first time ever as a special-guest due the 60th anniversery . ukraine quit because of politics , while cyprus , czech republic and serbia returned to the contest . the first semi-final took place on tuesday , the 19th may 2015 . 16 countries participated and 10 qualified for the final ( fed marked ) . the `` big-five '' members austria , france and spain voted in this semi-final . the second semi-final took place on thursday , the 21th may 2015 . 17 countries participated and 10 qualified for the final ( fed marked ) . the `` big-five '' members germany , italy and the united kingdom voted in this semi-final . the grand final took place on saturday , the 23th may 2015. sweden won with 365 points , while austria and germany finished both last with zero points .
