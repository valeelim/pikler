Willy Stähle     willy juana stähle ( 16 june 1954 – august 2015 ) was a dutch world water skiing champion . she won a gold for women 's figure skiing and a silver medal for women 's salom at the 1972 summer olympics in munich , germany . she was the dutch sportswoman of the year in 1971. stähle was born in amsterdam . her death was announced on 1 september 2015 .
