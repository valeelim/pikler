Thomas Bibb     thomas bibb ( may 8 , 1783 â€“ september 20 , 1839 ) was the second governor of alabama . he was born in amelia county , virginia in 1783. bibb was president of the alabama senate when his brother , governor william wyatt bibb , died in office on july 10 , 1820. the new state constitution specified the president of the senate was next in line as governor . so he took over the rest of his brother 's term of office . bibb served as acting governor from july 25 , 1820 to november 25 , 1821. he decided not to run for the governorship again . bibb was elected to the alabama house of representatives in 1828 and 1829. he was the director of the huntsville branch of the alabama state bank . bibb was married to parmelia thompson .
