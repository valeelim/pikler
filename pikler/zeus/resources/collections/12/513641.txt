Sucka Free     sucka free is the second mixtape by trinidadian-american rapper nicki minaj . the album was released on april 12 , 2008 through young money entertainment and dirty money records . `` sucka free '' features guest appearances from fellow rappers lil wayne , gucci mane , jadakiss , lil ' kim ; a person who minaj would argue with later on in her career and ransom . just after minaj 's previous mixtape , `` playtime is over '' , was released in 2007 , minaj released `` sucka free '' with label mate lil wayne after he saw her on the queens-made dvd series called `` the come up '' . < ref name= '' billboard - nicki/wayne tour '' > < /ref > lil ' wayne helped minaj with making the mixtape .
