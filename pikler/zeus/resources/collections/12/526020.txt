IPhone 6S     the iphone 6s and iphone 6s plus are two smartphones designed by apple inc. they were released on september 25 , 2015. they replace the iphone 6 and iphone 6 plus , respectively . these `` s '' models have a rose gold finish . they have cameras on the front ( 5 megapixels ) and back ( 12 megapixels ) . the screens are for the 6s and for the 6s plus . the storage capacities are 16 , 64 , and 128 gigabytes . a new feature , force touch , is on these models and is also on the apple watch . live photos is a new feature that allows users to take moving photos .
