We Are Never Ever Getting Back Together     `` we are never ever getting back together '' is a 2012 dance-pop , bubblegum pop , pop rock , pop song from taylor swift . the song is about swift being frustrated at her ex-lover wanting to fix their relationship . the song was the lead single from her album `` red '' . `` rolling stone '' named this song the second best song of 2012. the song hit # 1 on the `` billboard '' hot 100 .
