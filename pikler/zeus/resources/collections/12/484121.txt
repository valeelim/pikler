The Ballad of Bilbo Baggins     `` the ballad of bilbo baggins '' is a song composed by charles randolph grean and performed by leonard nimoy , telling the story of bilbo baggins and his adventures in j. r. r. tolkien 's novel `` the hobbit '' . the recording was featured on `` two sides of leonard nimoy , '' the second of nimoy 's albums on dot records . it was also released as a single ( dot records cat . # 45-17028 ) in july 1967 .
