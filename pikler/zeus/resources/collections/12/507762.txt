Manitoba general election, 1981    manitoba general election , 1981 the manitoba general election of november 17 , 1981 was held to elect members to the legislative assembly of manitoba , canada . the new democratic party won the election . they won 34 out of 57 seats . the progressive conservative party won the remaining 23 seats . the manitoba liberal party did n't win any seats for the first time in its history . the progressive party ( which ran for the first time ) , the communist party , and western canada concept party also ran candidates in the election . none of these parties won any seats . howard pawley of the new democratic party became premier .
