S-train     the s-train ( , ) is an urban rail network which serves copenhagen , denmark and the surrounding area . it connects the city center to its suburbs . the railway they use is called s-banen ( the s-track ) . the s-train is operated by dsb ( ) . the s-track has a length of about . there are 84 railway stations , where 32 of the stations are in the central area . on weekdays all stations are served at least every 10 minutes until the evening . there are six main lines . there is one peak hour support line . since most lines use the same path through the city core , train departures every second minute . on most suburb lines trains depart every five minutes . on sundays these time intervals are doubled . most trains have 8 cars but more can be added as needed . there are a total of 120 8-car trains .
