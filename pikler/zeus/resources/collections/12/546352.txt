Azzedine Alaia     azzedine alaïa is a tunisian haute couture fashion designer based in paris . in 2008 , he was awarded the legion of honour by the french government . in 2011 , he started showing his collections on the catwalk again after an eight-years pause . in 2015 , he was celebrated at the galleria borghese in rome with an exhibition called `` couture/sculpture : azzedine alaïa in the history of fashion '' .
