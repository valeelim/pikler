Yo no creo en los hombres     yo no creo en los hombres is a mexican telenovela produced by giselle gonzález for televisa . it is based on `` yo no creo en los hombres '' ( 1991 ) , produced in méxico . alejandro camacho , adriana louvier and gabriel soto star in the series . flavio medina , azela robinson and rosa maría bianchi also star .
