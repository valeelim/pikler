War tax due stamp     war tax due stamp is a kind of war tax and postage due stamps that was used for mail when the war tax has not been paid by the sender . they were issued in romania between 1915 and 1921. during war the sender of a letter paid a war tax . then they placed a war tax stamp on the letter showing the tax was paid . if the sender did not do that , a war tax due stamp was applied by the postal service . when the letter was delivered the person receiving the letter paid double the normal rate . related pages . - postage stamp - stamp act - tax
