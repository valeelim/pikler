Zim, Minnesota    zim , minnesota zim is an unincorporated place in mcdavitt township , st. louis county , minnesota , united states . in 2010 the population of zim was 5,228 people according to the u.s. census . zim makes up 2.6 % of the entire population of saint louis county . zim got its name from a nearby lumber camp of a man named zimmerman . nearby is the `` sax-zim bog '' , one of the world 's best birdwatching places . it was named for the two nearby , mostly uninhabited , towns of zim and sax . every february the habitat hosts a three-day festival for birdwatchers .
