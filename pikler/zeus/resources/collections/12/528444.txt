George Carroll (judge)    george carroll ( judge ) george carroll ( january 6 , 1922 – january 14 , 2016 ) was an american lawyer . he was an important civic figure in contra costa county , california and the city of richmond . he was born in brooklyn , new york . he was the first black lawyer in richmond , california . in 1961 carroll became the first african american elected to the city council ( 1961–1964 ) and later became the first black mayor of richmond ( 1964–1965 ) or any large american city . carroll died in his sleep on january 14 , 2015 in richmond , california from respiratory failure at the age of 94 .
