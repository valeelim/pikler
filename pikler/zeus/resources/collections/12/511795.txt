Arsen Dedić     arsen dedić ( 28 july 1938 – 17 august 2015 ) was a croatian singer-songwriter . he was known in the croatian as well as former yugoslav music scene . dedić wrote and performed chansons as well as movie music . he was also an award-winning poet , and was one of the best-selling poets of former yugoslavia . other websites . - compositions by arsen dedić
