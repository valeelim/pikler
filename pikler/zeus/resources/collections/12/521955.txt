Harrods     harrods is an upmarket department store on brompton road in knightsbridge in the royal borough of kensington and chelsea , london . the harrods brand also applies to harrods enterprises , including harrods bank , harrods estates , harrods aviation and air harrods . the store has a site . it has 330 departments covering one million square feet ( ) of retail space . it is said to be the biggest department store in europe . harrods was founded by charles henry harrod in 1834. in the 20th century they bought other stores in the uk . in 1959 , the british department store holding company , house of fraser , bought harrods . later , in 1985 , the egyptian mohamed al-fayed bought harrods . in 2010 , the store was sold to qatar investment authority ( qia ) . this company invests the wealth of the qatar royal family . harrods is also known for their annual christmas bears which are very popular .
