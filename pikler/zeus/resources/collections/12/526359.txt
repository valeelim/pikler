Dami Im     dami im ( , born 17 october 1988 ) is a south korean-born australian singer . she is best known for winning the fifth season of `` the x factor '' in 2013. her first album was an aria number one . she has three studio albums , two extended plays , nine singles , two album appearances , and four music videos . she represented australia in the 2016 eurovision song contest , coming second with `` sound of silence '' . im was born in incheon . she grew up in brisbane , queensland . she is married and lives in daisy hill , a suburb of logan city , queensland . she is a christian .
