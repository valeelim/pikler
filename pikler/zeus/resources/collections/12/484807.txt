Grant Gustin     thomas grant gustin ( born january 14 , 1990 ) is an american actor and singer . he is known for portraying the roles of sebastian smythe on `` glee '' and barry allen/the flash on `` the flash '' . he was born norfolk , virginia to his mother tina haney , a pediatric nurse , and his father tom gustin . he attended the governor 's school for the arts program in norfolk for musical theatre . he has also been to hurrah players incorporated and graduated from granby high school . he would then go to the bfa music theatre program at elon university in north carolina for two years . from september 30 , 2010 to september 23 , 2011 , gustin played the role of baby john in the broadway revival tour of `` west side story '' . filmography . movies . - `` kid fitness jungle adventure exercise video '' - club fit kid ( 2003 ) ( direct-to-dvd ) - `` a mother 's nightmare '' - chris stewart ( 2012 ) ( tv movie ) - `` affluenza '' - todd goodman ( 2014 ) television . - `` a haunting '' - stacey 's boyfriend ( 2006 ) - `` glee '' - sebastian smythe ( 2011â€“13 )
