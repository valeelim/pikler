Božo Bakota     božo bakota ( 5 october 1950 – 1 october 2015 ) was a croatian footballer . he played as a forward and midfielder professionally between 1971 and 1986. he played for nk zagreb and sk sturm graz . he also played for the yugoslavia national team in 1978. he was born in zagreb . bakota died on 1 october 2015 in graz , austria . he was 64 .
