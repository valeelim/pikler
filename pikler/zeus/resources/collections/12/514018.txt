Il Guarany     il guarany is an italian opera in four acts , music by antônio carlos gomes and libretto by antonio scalvini and carlo d'ormeville , after josé de alencar 's novel `` o guarani '' . it was first performed in milan , on march 19 , 1870. by far gomes 's most successful work and his only opera still regularly performed . principal characters . - peri - tenor - cecilia - soprano - gonzales - baritone - il cacico - bass discography . - 1930 : aureliano pertile ( peri ) , giannina arangi-lombardi ( cecilia ) , carlo galeffi ( gonzales ) , nazzareno de angelis ( il cacico ) ; coro e orchestra del teatro alla scala di milano , lorenzo molajoli - ( emi classics ) - 1951 : giacinto prandelli ( peri ) , renata tebaldi ( cecilia ) , enzo mascherini ( gonzales ) , dario caselli ( il cacico ) ; orchestra e coro dell'accademia nazionale di santa cecilia , alberto erede - ( decca ) - 1958 : giuseppe di stefano ( peri ) , zinka milanov ( cecilia ) , leonard warren ( gonzales ) , ezio flagello ( il cacico ) ; coro e orchestra dell'accademia di santa cecilia , fernando previtali - ( rca victor , later decca/london )
