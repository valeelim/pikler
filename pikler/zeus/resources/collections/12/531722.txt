Mabel Matiz     mabel matiz ( born fatih karaca , august 31 , 1985 in mersin ) is a turkish singer , writer and dentist . he chose mabel matiz as stage name . mabel is a character in the novel kumral ada mavi tuna . matiz means `` drunk '' in old greek language . he has released 3 studio albums . he studied dentistry at the university of istanbul , and graduated in 2008. he briefly worked in a health care clinic until starting a music career . he is an lgbt supporter and lgbt rights activist .
