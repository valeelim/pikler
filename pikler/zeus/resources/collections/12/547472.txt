Acute myeloid leukemia     acute myeloid leukemia ( aml ) , also known as acute myelogenous leukemia or acute nonlymphocytic leukemia ( anll ) , is a cancer of the myeloid line of blood cells . it is the rapid growth of uncommon white blood cells that build up in the bone marrow and harm the the production of normal blood cells . aml is the most common acute leukemia affecting adults .
