Cley Marshes     cley marshes is a nature reserve on the north sea coast of england . it is just outside the village of cley next the sea , norfolk . it has been a reserve since 1926. it is the oldest of the reserves belonging to the norfolk wildlife trust ( nwt ) . cley marshes protects an area of reed beds , freshwater marsh , pools and wet meadows . it has been designated as a site of special scientific interest ( sssi ) , special area of conservation ( sac ) , special protection area ( spa ) and ramsar site because of the large numbers of birds it attracts .
