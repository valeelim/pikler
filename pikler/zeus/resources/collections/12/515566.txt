Fired Up (Alesha Dixon album)    fired up ( alesha dixon album ) fired up is the debut studio album by british recording artist alesha dixon . the album was recorded after dixon 's group mis-teeq had split up . dixon signed a deal with polydor records . her single `` lipstick '' reached number 14 on the uk chart . her next single with polydor , `` knockdown '' failed to reach the top 40 ( it reached number 45 ) . dixon left polydor afterwards but kept the rights to her songs from the album . `` fired up '' was finally released as a cd in japan in 2008. the album was billed as just alesha instead of using dixon 's full name . for the album , dixon worked with many known uk songwriters including richard x and xenomania .
