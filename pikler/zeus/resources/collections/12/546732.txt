Neil Taylor (footballer)    neil taylor ( footballer ) neil john taylor ( born 7 february 1989 in st asaph , clwyd , wales ) is a welsh professional footballer who plays as a defender . he currently plays for the premier league club swansea city and the wales national football team . he has also played for manchester city and wrexham . he is mother is from india , and he is one of the few british asians in professional football . he was included in wale 's squad for the uefa euro 2016. other websites . - neil taylor profile at swansea city a.f.c .
