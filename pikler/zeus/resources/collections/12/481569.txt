Jonathan Klein     jonathan klein was president of cable news network ( cnn ) for 6 years . he was fired in 2010. he was replaced by ken jautz , leader of cnn 's sister network , cnn headline news . klein hired eliot spitzer as a talk show host . he fired larry king and replaced him with piers morgan . while at cnn , he tried to hire keith olbermann away from nbc 's cable news channel msnbc . cnn exceutive jim walton did not want that , and it did not happen .
