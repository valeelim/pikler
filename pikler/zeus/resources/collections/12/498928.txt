Jesse Metcalfe     jesse eden metcalfe ( born december 9 , 1978 ) is an american actor . he was born in carmel valley village , california . metcalfe mostly known for his role as john rowland in the series `` desperate housewives '' . metcalfe has also had roles on the soap opera `` passions '' and in the movie `` john tucker must die '' . he played christopher ewing in the continuation of `` dallas '' , based on the 1978 series of the same name .
