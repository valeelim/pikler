Chris Isaak     chris isaak ( born june 26 , 1956 ) is an american rock musician and actor . he is known for his 1950s rock and roll style . his songs are usually about love , loss and heartbreak . his has been singing for four decades . he has had twelve studio albums . in 1990 , his song `` wicked game '' was released . it became a top 10 hit in 1991. isaak was born in stockton , california .
