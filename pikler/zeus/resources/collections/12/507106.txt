François Delapierre     françois delapierre ( 4 november 1970 – 20 june 2015 ) was a french politician . he served as the national secretary of the left party from december 2010 until his death . he was also the regional councillor of île-de-france from february 2009 until his death . he was also a member of the socialist party from 1986 to 2008. he was born in paris . delapierre died from brain cancer on 20 june 2015 in paris , aged 44 .
