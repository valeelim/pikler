Princess Charlotte of Cambridge     princess charlotte of cambridge ( charlotte elizabeth diana ; born 2 may 2015 ) is the daughter of prince william , duke of cambridge , and catherine , duchess of cambridge , the granddaughter of charles , prince of wales , and the great-granddaughter of queen elizabeth ii . she was born at st mary 's hospital in paddington , london . catherine 's pregnancy was announced on 8 september 2014 by clarence house . from birth , she is the fourth in line to succeed the queen , elizabeth ii , after her grandfather , father , and older brother , prince george of cambridge ( born 2013 ) .
