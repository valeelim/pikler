Vladimir Shainsky     vladimir yakovlevich shainsky ( ) ( born december 12 , 1925 ) is a soviet and russian composer . in the 1950s shainsky played in leonid utyosov 's orchestra . he taught young people how to conduct music . he worked as a composer and musical leader at different dance orchestras . he studied in baku conservatory , the composers faculty . shainsky was born in kiev . shainsky has been awarded many awards , including the ussr state prize ( 1981 ) , people 's artist of the russian sfsr title ( 1986 ) , order of friendship ( 1996 ) , multiple laureate of the russian ( formerly soviet ) `` song of the year '' festival ( since 1971 ) . other websites . - shainskiy 's biography - another biography
