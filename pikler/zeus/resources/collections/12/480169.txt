Hugo Banzer     hugo banzer suárez ( may 10 , 1926 – may 5 , 2002 ) was a bolivian politician , military general , and dictator . he was the president of bolivia . he held the bolivian presidency twice : from 1971 to 1978 , as a dictator ; and then again from 1997 to his resignation in 2001. banzer was born in concepción , santa cruz . he studied at the school of the americas . banzer died on may 5 , 2002 in santa cruz , bolivia from lung cancer , aged 75. other websites . - on the crisis of december 1977 ( video )
