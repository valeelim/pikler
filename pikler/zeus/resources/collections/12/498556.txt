Tim Omaji     tim omaji ( born 9 september 1987 ) , also known by his former stage name timomatic , is an australian singer-songwriter and dancer . he is best known for his single `` set it off '' . he was a contestant on `` so you think you can dance australia '' in 2009 , finishing seventh in the competition . he also appeared as a contestant on `` australia 's got talent '' and placed third in the competition . later that year , he signed with sony music australia . omaji was born in nigeria . he moved to australia when he was ten months old . he lived in many places all over the nation before settling in canberra .
