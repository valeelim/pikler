Donald W. Riegle, Jr.    donald w. riegle , jr. donald wayne riegle , jr. ( born february 4 , 1938 ) is an american politician . he was born in flint , michigan . he served for five terms as a representative from 1967 through 1976. he served for three terms as a senator in the u.s. congress from 1976 through 1995. he is a member of the democratic party . on march 6 , 2016 , riegle endorsed bernie sanders for the democratic nomination for president of the united states .
