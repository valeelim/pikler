Benoît Lacroix     benoît lacroix ( 8 september 1915 – 2 march 2016 ) was a canadian theologian , philosopher , and dominican priest . he was a professor in medieval studies and historian of the mediaeval period . he was the author of almost 50 works and a great number of articles . he was born in saint-michel-de-bellechasse , quebec . lacroix died in quebec on 2 march 2016 from complications of pneumonia . he was 100 years old .
