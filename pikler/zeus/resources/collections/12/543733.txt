Lindy Hop     lindy hop is an american dance . it started in harlem , new york city in the 1920s and 1930s . it was originally associated with jazz and tap dance . it was popular from the 1920s to the 1940s . the lindy hop was a mixture of many dances before it , like the charleston , tap and breakaway dances . frankie manning helped develop the dance . lindy hop today is danced as a social , competitive or performance dance . it is also sometimes called a street dance .
