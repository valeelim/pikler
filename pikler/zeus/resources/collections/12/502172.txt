Vafa Guluzade     vafa guluzade ( ; 21 december 1940 – 1 may 2015 ) was an azerbaijani diplomat , political scientist and specialist in conflict resolution . he worked as foreign policy state advisor for azerbaijan president heydar aliyev between 1990 and 1999. guluzade was born in baku to a shia muslim family . he was married to leyla ahmadova . they had a daughter ( 1974–2010 ) . guluzade died from heart failure after surgery on 1 may 2015 in baku . he was 74 .
