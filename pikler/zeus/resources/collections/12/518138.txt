Auxin     auxins are a class of plant hormones ( or plant growth substances ) with some morphogen-like characteristics . auxins have a main role in coordination of many growth and behavioral processes in the plant 's life cycle . they are essential for plant body development . auxins and their role in plant growth were first described by the dutch scientist frits warmolt went . kenneth v. thimann isolated auxin and found its chemical structure to be indole-3-acetic acid ( iaa ) . went and thimann co-authored a book on plant hormones , `` phytohormones '' , in 1937. there are synthetic auxins , and in big doses they can be used as herbicides . agent orange is a mixture of synthetic auxins .
