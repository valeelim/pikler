Macarena     `` macarena '' is a spanish dance song by los del rio . the single is about a woman named macarena . it was on the 1994 album `` a mi me gusta '' . the song was an international hit in 1995 , 1996 and 1997. the song was recorded in 1992. it was released on august 15 , 1995 in the united states . a reworked version peaked at # 1 on the u.s. `` billboard '' hot 100 in 1996. it stayed there for 14 weeks . the song was also performed by the bayside boys . the original version was released in spanish language in 1993 .
