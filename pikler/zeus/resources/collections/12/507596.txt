Undercover    k.c . undercover k.c . undercover is an american sitcom , created by corinne marshall . rob lotterstein is the executive producer . the series stars zendaya as k.c . cooper . k.c . undercover was released on january 1 , 2015 on watch disney channel . it was followed by the television premiere on january 18. the first season has 27 episodes . on may 15 , 2015 , tvline reported that the show will have a second season.. in washington d.c. , there is k.c . cooper , a high school math genius . her parents , craig & kira , are secretly undercover spies . she becomes an undercover spy too . her brother , ernie , tried to help because he feels left out . eventually he joins the team . ernie 's desire led craig & kira to get a robot child named judy . each episode centers on k.c . and her family . they have family issues but go on missions to save the country . cast and characters . - zendaya as k.c . cooper , a high school teenage girl training to become an undercover spy . she is very good at mathematics . - as marisa , k.c .
