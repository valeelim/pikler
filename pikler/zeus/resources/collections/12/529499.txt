Josip Friščić     josip friščić ( 15 august 1949 – 23 january 2016 ) was a croatian politician . he was a leader of the croatian peasant party ( hss ) from 17 december 2005 until 28 january 2012. he was also vice president of croatian parliament from 2008 to 2011. he was born in subotica podravska near rasinja , sfr yugoslavia . friščić died from cancer on 23 january 2016 in koprivnica , aged 66 .
