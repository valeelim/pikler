George Washington University Hospital     the george washington university hospital is the hub of a major medical center in washington , d.c. in the united states . it is affiliated with the george washington university school of medicine and health sciences . the current building was opened on august 23 , 2002. since 1997 , the george washington university hospital has been jointly owned and operated by a parnership between a subsidiary of universal health services and the george washington university . in 1981 after being shot , ronald reagan was taken to the hospital .
