Arturo Paoli     arturo paoli ( 30 november 1912 – 13 july 2015 ) was an italian priest and a missionary . he was a member of the congregation of the little brothers of the gospel . paoli helped saved jewish italians during world war ii . he was born in lucca , italy . paoli died of natural causes in lucca , italy , aged 102. other websites . - il fondo documentazione arturo paoli aperto presso la sede della fondazione banca del monte di lucca - piccoli fratelli del vangelo - fraternità di spello - servizio su rai 3
