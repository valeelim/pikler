Sylvie Joly     sylvie joly ( 18 october 1934 – 4 september 2015 ) was a french actress and comedian . she was best known for her roles in the movies `` going places '' ( 1974 ) and `` get out your handkerchiefs '' ( 1978 ) . joly was born in paris . she had two daughters , mathilde vitry and stéphane joly . in october 2010 , she revealed she had parkinson 's disease . joly died from a heart attack on 4 september 2015 in paris , aged 80 .
