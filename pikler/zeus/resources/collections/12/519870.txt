Ann Blyth     ann marie blyth ( born august 16 , 1928 ) is an american actress and singer . her performance as veda pierce in the 1945 movie `` mildred pierce '' , she was nominated for an academy award for best supporting actress . blyth also appeared in `` our very own '' ( with farley granger ) , `` the great caruso '' ( with mario lanza ) , `` one minute to zero '' ( with robert mitchum ) , `` the world in his arms '' ( with gregory peck ) , `` rose marie '' , `` the student prince '' , `` kismet '' , `` the buster keaton story '' , and `` the helen morgan story '' ( with paul newman ) . other websites . - ann blyth `` women 's international centre '' biography and more recent photograph - photographs of ann blyth
