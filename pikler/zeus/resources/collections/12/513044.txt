Charles Evers     james charles evers ( born september 11 , 1922 ) is an american civil rights activist . in 1969 , he became the first african american since the reconstruction era to have been elected as mayor in a mississippi city . he served as mayor of fayette in jefferson county . thereafter , he ran for governor in 1971 and the united states senate in 1978. he ran both times as an independent candidate . other websites . - `` the rise and fall of jim crow '' , pbs - 90.1 wmpr , jackson mississippi , charles evers station manager : blues , urban contemporary gospel , talk , variety - oral history interview with charles evers , from the lyndon baines johnson library
