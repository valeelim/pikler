Debt bondage     bonded labour or debt bondage ( or debt slavery ) is a term used to refer to a situation where someone ( a debtor ) has to work for the person who lent him money . generally , the conditions of this form of labour are close to slavery . bonded labour is one of the most common forms of modern-day slavery-like conditions . usually , the person working gets paid very little money in return for very hard work . very often , the exact amount to be re-paid is not defined clearly . the person doing the bonded labour will never be able to pay off the debt through work . a regular work contract will also give rights to the person being employed , and the amount of work . in a normal working contract , difficult , dangrous or exhausting work will usually get paid better . this is not the case with debt bondage . debt bondage is banned by the convention of united nation about slavery in 1956. related pages . - child labour - slavery
