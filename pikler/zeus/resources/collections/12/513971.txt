Unbreakable (Janet Jackson album)    unbreakable ( janet jackson album ) unbreakable is the eleventh studio album by janet jackson . it was released on october 2 , 2015. as of september 2015 , two r & b tinged singles had been released from the album . one was `` no sleeep '' which features american rapper j-cole . the other was the title track . this will be jackson 's first album with newly recorded material since 2008 's `` discipline '' .
