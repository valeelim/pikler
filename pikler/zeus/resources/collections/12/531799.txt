Charles C. Campbell     charles c. `` hondo '' campbell ( 1948 – february 8 , 2016 ) was an american general . he was the 17th commanding general in the united states army forces command ( forscom ) . he previously served as forscom ’ s deputy commanding general and chief of staff from april 26 , 2006 to january 8 , 2007. he assumed his commanding general assignment january 9 , 2007 , and completed it june 3 , 2010. he was born in shreveport , louisiana . campbell died in shreveport on february 8 , 2016 , from a heart attack . he was aged 68 .
