Nimr al-Nimr     nimr baqr al-nimr ( ‎ ; 1959 – 2 january 2016 ) , better known as sheikh nimr , was a saudi shia sheikh and human rights activist . he was a sheikh from around 2008 until his death . he was one of the best known shia clerics in saudi arabia . in july 2012 , he was arrested by saudi police and shot in the leg . he was put on trial for terrorism charges and sentenced to death in october 2014. al-nimr was born in al-awamiyah , qatif . he studied for about ten years in tehran , iran and also studied in syria . al-nimr was among the prisoners killed by beheading in a mass execution on 2 january 2016. he was 56 .
