Washington Metro     the washington metro is a rapid transit system that serves the washington , d.c. area in the united states . it has the second busiest rapid transit system in the united states . the new york city subway is the busiest . there are six lines in this system : - red line - blue line - orange line - green line - yellow line - silver line the first part of the metro system opened on march 27 , 1976. the rail system was completed on january 13 , 2001 .
