Monarchy of Norway     before the unification in 872 a.d , norway was made up of 22 small kingdoms . they merged into larger kingdoms by the different kings ( chiefs ) ; harald gullskjegg , gandalv alvgerisson , kjøte den rike . vigbrand fra agder , vikar haraldsson , øystein halvdansson in addition to halvdan svarte . harald fairhair ( harald den hårfagre ) the son of halfdan the black ( halvdan svarte ) unified norway once and for all into one kingdom at the battle of hafrsfjord . where he fought against the five remaining kings in the west of the country ( vestlandet ) .
