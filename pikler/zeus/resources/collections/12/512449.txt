Jentina (album)    jentina ( album ) jentina is the self-titled debut studio album by english rapper jentina , it was released by virgin on september 9 , 2005 in italy . as of 2015 it is her only album that she has ever released , it features `` bad ass strippa '' and `` french kisses '' both which reached the top 30 in the uk and `` mysterious '' which was only released in italy . track listing . 1 . `` mysterious '' 1 . `` bad ass strippa '' 1 . `` wonderful day '' 1 . `` french kisses '' 1 . `` feels good '' 1 . `` gone '' 1 . `` here i come '' 1 . `` sneakers '' 1 . `` eenie meenie '' 1 . `` smoke '' 1 . `` baby '' 1 . `` hard times '' other websites . - `` jentina '' at ibs store
