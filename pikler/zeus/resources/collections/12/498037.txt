Jackie Joyner-Kersee     jacqueline `` jackie '' joyner-kersee ( born march 3 , 1962 ) is a retired american athlete . she is one of the greatest athletes in the women 's heptathlon as well as in the women 's long jump . joyner-kersee represented the united states at four olympic games ( 1984-96 ) . she won a total of three gold , one silver , and two bronze olympic medals . joyner-kersee was voted `` the greatest female athlete of the 20th century '' by `` sports illustrated '' magazine . she won four world outdoor championships gold medals . joyner-kersee holds the current world record of 7,291 points in the women 's heptathlon .
