Michael Brandner     michael brandner ( born 13 february 1995 ) is a football player from austria . he plays for f. c. liefering , the second team of f.c . red bull salzburg , in the sky go erste liga ( 2nd level in austrian football ) as midfielder . he started playing football for ask salzburg . then he went on to the red bull youth academy , where he played in all teams . for the 2013/14 season he was transferred to fc liefering , where he is still playing . michael brandner also played for the autrian football national youth teams from u16 up to u20 . with the u19 team he reached the 3rd place in the european championship .
