Marina Beach     marina beach is a natural beach in chennai , tamil nadu along the bay of bengal sea . the beach is the first longest beach in india and the second longest in the world . the length of the beach is 13 km . it is a renowned beach because the memorial of the two chief ministers m.g.ramachandran and c.n.annadurai . it is the most crowded beach in the country . more than 10,000 to 25,000 visitors visit the beach on weekdays while more than 50,000 visitors visit the beach during holidays . there are many statues erected in the beach like the statues of triumph of labour , robert caldwell , kambar , ilango adigal , kannagi , bharathiyar , bharathidasan etc .
