Soma     soma is a term with two different origins : - soma ( drink ) : a ritual drink of early indo-iranians , continued in vedic and persian sources . - a word of ancient greek origin meaning `` body '' . - somatic cell : a basic term in biology , describing the body cells as opposed to the reproductive cells - soma ( nerve cell ) , the body ( `` perikaryon '' ) of a nerve cell as opposed to its dendrites or axon - soma ( fiction ) , a hallucinogenic drug in aldous huxley 's novels `` brave new world '' and `` island '' .
