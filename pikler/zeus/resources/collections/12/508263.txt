Florac     florac is a former french commune , a `` subprefecture '' of the lozère department , in the occitanie region . it is also the capital of the `` arrondissement '' of florac . on 1 january 2016 , it was merged into the new commune of florac trois rivières . the inhabitants of this city are known , in french , as `` floracois '' ( women : `` floracoises '' ) . florac is in the valley of the tarnon river , a small river that flows through the lozère department before joining the tarn river . the `` commune '' is found at the start of the gorges du tarn ( a canyon formed by the tarn river ) . the city and the tarnon valley are within the cévennes national park but they are not part of the park . the `` commune '' of florac has an area of and its average altitude is ; at the city hall , the altitude is . the `` commune '' is surrounded by the `` communes '' quézac , bédouès , la salle-prunet , saint-laurent-de-trèves , vebron and montbrun . the climate in prades in the köppen climate classification is cfb - oceanic climate with template summers .
