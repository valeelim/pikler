Ali Nassirian     ali nassirian ( , born 4 february 1935 ) is an iranian movie and theater actor . he was born in tehran , iran . his career began in 1947 and would last until his retirement in 2008. nassirian was known for his role in the popular iranian television series `` hezar dastan '' ( 1979-1987 ) . he also appeared in movies such as `` the mandrake '' ( 1975 ) , `` kamalolmolk '' ( 1983 ) , `` captain khorshid '' ( 1987 ) , and `` iron island '' ( 2005 ) .
