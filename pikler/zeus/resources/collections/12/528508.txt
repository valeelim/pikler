Superphylum     a superphylum is an unofficial grade in taxonomy , above the phylum and below the subregnum , used to describe a group of phyla which probably evolved from a common ancestor . it is used especially in the animal kingdom . examples : - ecdysozoa - lophotrochozoa - radiata - deuterostomia - bilateria - planctomycetes-verrucomicrobia-chlamydiae ( pvc )
