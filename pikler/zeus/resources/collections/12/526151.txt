Dieter Laser     dieter laser ( born 17 february 1942 ) is a german actor . he is known to english speaking audiences for his roles as mantrid in `` lexx '' , prof. otto blaettchen in `` the ogre '' , dr. josef heiter in `` the human centipede ( first sequence ) '' and bill boss in `` the human centipede 3 ( final sequence ) '' . other websites . - official facebook site of dieter laser
