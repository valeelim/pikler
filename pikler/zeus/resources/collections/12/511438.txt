The Pleasure Principle (song)    the pleasure principle ( song ) `` the pleasure principle '' is a new jack swing and pop song by the singer janet jackson . it is about her not wanting a loveless relationship and not settling for materialism . the song is from her album `` control '' . the song hit # 14 on the `` billboard '' hot 100. this was the only song from jackson to not chart in the top 10. it was released in may 1987 .
