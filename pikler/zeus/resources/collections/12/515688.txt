Jack Larson     jack edward larson ( february 8 , 1928 – september 20 , 2015 ) was an american actor , librettist , screenwriter and producer . he is best known for his role as jimmy olsen in the television series `` adventures of superman '' ( 1952–1958 ) . he also appeared in ' and ' and the movie `` superman returns '' ( 2006 ) . he retired in 2011. larson was born in los angeles , california . he grew up in pasadena , california . he was of swedish and russian ancestry . he was gay and his partner of 35 years , director and screenwriter james bridges , died in 1993. larson died on september 20 , 2015 in brentwood , california , aged 87 .
