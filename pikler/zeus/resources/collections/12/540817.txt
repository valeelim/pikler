Carl Fredrik Reuterswärd     carl fredrik reuterswärd ( 4 june 1934 – 3 may 2016 ) was a swedish painter and sculptor . he was a professor of painting at the academy of fine arts in stockholm from 1965 until 1969. in 1974 , he was a guest professor at minneapolis school of art in minneapolis , minnesota . in 1986 he was awarded the prince eugen medal for painting . reuterswärd was known for his sculpture showing a revolver tied in a knot , called `` non violence '' . it is outside of the united nations headquarters in new york city . reuterswärd died of pneumonia at a hospital in landskrona , sweden on 3 may 2016 , aged 81 .
