Manuel Velázquez     manuel velázquez villaverde ( 24 january 1943 – 15 january 2016 ) was a spanish footballer . he played as a striker . he played for plus ultra , rayo vallecano , málaga , real madrid , and toronto metros-croatia . he also played for the spain national football team . he was born in madrid . velázquez died on 15 january 2016 in madrid , aged 72 .
