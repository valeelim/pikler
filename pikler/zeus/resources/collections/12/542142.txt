Sea Islands     the sea islands are a chain of tidal and barrier islands on the southeastern atlantic ocean coast of the united states . there are over a hundred of them . they are between the mouths of the santee and st. johns rivers along the coast of the us states of south carolina , georgia , and florida . barrier islands like these may change during storms . they absorb energy and protect the coastlines . indirectly , they may help to create areas of protected waters where wetlands may flourish .
