Madison De La Garza     madison lee de la garza ( dallas , texas ; december 28 , 2001 ) is an american actress , maternal half-sister of the singer and actress demi lovato . she is best known by her role of juanita solís in `` desperate housewives '' . madison de la garza was born in dallas , texas , daughter of dianna de la garza and eddie de la garza . de la garza plays the recurrent role of juanita solís , in `` desperate housewives '' of abc . juanita is the daughter of gabrielle solís ( eva longoria ) and carlos solís ( ricardo chavira ) . de la garza has been bullied at school , and her sister demi talked about her and that she has been intimidated . in december 2010 , de la garza announced through twitter that she was doing a christmas present for his sister demi , who was receiving treatment for `` physical and emotional problems ''
