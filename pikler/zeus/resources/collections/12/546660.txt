2016 Stanley Cup Finals     the 2016 stanley cup finals is the 123rd stanley cup trophy presentations as well as the champions of the 2015-16 nhl season . it featured the pittsburgh penguins and the san jose sharks . this was the sharks first ever appearance in a stanley cup final and the penguins 5th time in a stanley cup finals . the penguins beat the sharks winning the series in game 6 at sap center , 3-1. years in boldface under the `` finals appearance '' column signify the player won the stanley cup in the given year .
