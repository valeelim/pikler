Irena Sendler     irena sendler ( 15 february 1910 – 12 may 2008 ) was a nurse and social worker in warsaw , poland . during world war ii , when poland was occupied by nazis , she took about 2,500 jewish children out of poland with help from other members of the żegota ( the polish council to aid jews ) . she was caught , and the nazis tortured her . the nazis were planning on killing her , but she avoided death .
