Earl Lloyd     he didn ’ t enter the draft he was just randomly picked . he was walking down campus when someone told him he was picked in the draft earl francis lloyd ( april 3 , 1928 – february 26 , 2015 ) was an american professional basketball player . he was the first black person to play in the national basketball association , in the 1950–51 nba season . three other african americans played in the same season : chuck cooper , nathaniel clifton , and hank dezonie .
