Gustavo Endres     gustavo endres ( born august 23 , 1975 in passo fundo , brazil ) is a retired brazilian volleyball player . endres played for brazil 's men 's national volleyball team in 1997-2008. the team won the gold medal at the 2004 summer olympics in athens , greece . the team won the silver medal at the 2008 summer olympics in beijing , china . endres was also a member of the 2002 and 2006 world championship teams . he won several medals in the world league , south american championship , world cup and the grand champions cup . other websites . - legavolley profile
