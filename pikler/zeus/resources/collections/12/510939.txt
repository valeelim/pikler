Buddy Roemer presidential campaign, 2012    buddy roemer presidential campaign , 2012 former governor of louisiana and former u.s. representative buddy roemer of louisiana began a movement for the 2012 republican party nomination for president of the united states shortly following the 2010 midterm elections . after he was not added to the nationally-televised republican debate , roemer announced on february 22 , 2012 that he would instead run on a third-party ticket , specifically the reform party and americans elect nominations . shortly after americans elect announced they would not be fielding a candidate , roemer 's campaign announced on may 31 , 2012 that he was ending his 2012 presidential campaign altogether . other websites . - buddy roemer 2012 `` official campaign site ''
