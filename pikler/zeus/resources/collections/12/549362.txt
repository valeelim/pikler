Arrondissement of Poitiers     the arrondissement of poitiers is an arrondissement of france , in the vienne department , nouvelle-aquitaine region . its capital is the city of poitiers . when the vienne department was created on 1800 , the `` arrondissement '' of poitiers was part of that original department . the `` arrondissement '' of poitiers is in western vienne and is bordered to the north and northeast by the `` arrondissement '' châtellerault , to east and south by the `` arrondissement '' montmorillon , and to the west by the deux-sèvres department . it has an area of . it has 246,038 inhabitants and a density of inhabitants/km² . it is the `` arrondissement '' of vienne with more people living in it but is the one with the smallest area . after the reorganisation of the cantons in france , cantons are not subdivisions of the `` arrondissements '' so they could have `` communes '' that belong to different `` arrondissements '' . in the `` arrondissement '' of poitiers , there are 8 cantons whose `` communes '' are in the `` arrondissement '' : chasseneuil-du-poitou , migné-auxances , poitiers-1 , poitiers-2 , poitiers-3 , poitiers-4 , poitiers-5 , vivonne and vouneuil-sous-biard . the following table shows the distribution of the `` communes '' in the cantons and `` arrondissements '' :
