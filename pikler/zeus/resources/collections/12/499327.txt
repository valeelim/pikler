Arrondissement of Bergerac     the arrondissement of bergerac is an arrondissement of france . it is part of the dordogne `` département '' in the nouvelle-aquitaine region . its capital is the city of bergerac . when the dordogne department was created on 17 february 1800 , the `` arrondissement '' of bergerac was part of that original department . the `` arrondissement '' of bergerac is in southwestern dordogne , with an area of . it has 111,875 inhabitants and a population density of inhabitants/km² . the `` arrondissement '' is bordered to the north by the `` arrondissement '' of périgueux , to the east by the `` arrondissement '' of sarlat-la-canéda , to the south by the lot-et-garonne department and to the west by the gironde department . the dordogne river flows , from east to west , through this `` arrondissement '' . after the reorganisation of the cantons in france , cantons are not subdivisions of the `` arrondissements '' so they could have `` communes '' that belong to different `` arrondissements '' . in the `` arrondissement '' of bergerac , there is only one canton where not all its `` communes '' are in the `` arrondissement '' : périgord central . the following table shows the distribution of the `` communes '' in the cantons and `` arrondissements '' :
