Jean-Luc Préel     jean-luc préel ( 30 october 1940 – 3 september 2015 ) was a french politician . he was a member of the national assembly from 1988 until 2012. he represented the vendée department , and was a member of the new centre . he was born in caen . préel died on 3 september 2015 in la roche-sur-yon , aged 74 .
