Howard Schultz     howard d. schultz ( born july 19 , 1953 ) is an american businessman . he is best known as the chairman and ceo of starbucks and a former owner of the seattle supersonics . he was a member of the board of directors at square , inc.. in 1998 , schultz co-founded maveron , an investment group , with dan levitan . in 2012 , `` forbes '' magazine ranked schultz as the 354th richest person in the united states , with a net worth of $ 1.5 billion .
