Albany, Texas    albany , texas albany is a city in the state of texas in the united states . it is the county seat of shackelford county . about 2,032 people lived there in 2010. the city was named for albany , georgia . the cook oilfields were discovered in 1926. they became a large part of the local economy . ranching is the main occupation , raising sheep and cattle . the town slogan is `` albany , the home of the hereford .
