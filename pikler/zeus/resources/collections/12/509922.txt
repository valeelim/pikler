Atlanta United FC     atlanta united football club is an american soccer team from atlanta , georgia that plays in major league soccer as a member of the western conference since the 2017 season . the team was founded on april 16 , 2014 , and it is owned by the owner of the atlanta falcons of the nfl , arthur blank . the team 's manager is gerardo `` tata '' martino . the team 's colors are black , red and gold . the team plays their home games at bobby dodd stadium and will later move to mercedes-benz stadium , which is currently under construction and expected to be completed during late 2017 .
