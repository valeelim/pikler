Premier of the People's Republic of China    premier of the people 's republic of china the premier of china , officially `` premier of the state council of the people 's republic of china '' , is the leader of the state council of china , who is the head of government and holds the highest rank ( level 1 ) in the civil service . the premier is formally approved by the national people 's congress upon the nomination of the president . in practice , the candidate is chosen through an informal process within the communist party of china . both the president and the premier are selected once every five years . the premier has always been a member of the powerful politburo standing committee . the current premier is li keqiang , who took office on 15 march 2013 .
