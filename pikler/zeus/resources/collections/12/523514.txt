Property law     property law covers two types of property in the common law legal system , real property and personal property . in the civil law system , there is a division between movable and immovable property . movable property is more or less the same as personal property . it includes money , stocks , notes , copyrights and patents . the idea behind all property law is property . in some jurisdictions , historically all property was owned by the monarch . land ownership was through land tenure in feudal systems . the first legal right of absolute ownership was under roman law and was called `` dominion '' . the napoleonic code was among the first government acts of modern times to introduce the notion of absolute ownership into statute . but protection of personal property rights was present in medieval islamic law .
