Günter Schabowski     günter schabowski ( 4 january 1929 – 1 november 2015 ) was an east german journalist and politician . he was an official of the socialist unity party of germany ( sed ) , the ruling party during most of the existence of the german democratic republic . schabowski gained worldwide fame in november 1989 when he improvised a slightly mistaken answer to a press conference question , raising popular expectations so rapidly that massive crowds gathered the same night at the berlin wall , forcing its opening after 28 years . schabowski died from complications of a stroke in berlin , aged 86 .
