Matey Kaziyski     matey kaziyski ( ; born 23 september 1984 ) is a bulgarian volleyball player . he was a member of bulgaria men 's national volleyball team in 2003-2012 and japanese club panasonic panthers , a participant of the olympic games beijing 2008 , bronze medalist of the world championship 2006 , world cup 2007 and european championship 2009. he is a multiple winner of the cev champions league and fivb club world championship with the italian club trentino volley . other websites . - fivb profile
