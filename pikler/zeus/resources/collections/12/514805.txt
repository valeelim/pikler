Martin Feldstein     martin stuart `` marty '' feldstein ( born november 25 , 1939 ) is an american economist . he is the george f. baker professor of economics at harvard university , and the president emeritus of the national bureau of economic research ( nber ) . he served as president and chief executive officer of the nber from 1978 through 2008. from 1982 to 1984 , feldstein served as chairman of the council of economic advisers and as chief economic advisor to president ronald reagan ( where his deficit hawk views clashed with reagan administration economic policies ) . he has also been a member of the washington-based financial advisory body the group of thirty since 2003. other websites . - faculty profile at harvard university - profile at the national bureau of economic research - profile and papers at research papers in economics/repec - column archive at project syndicate - feldstein says u.s. dollar needs to depreciate , jan zilinsky , `` the harvard crimson '' , january 12 , 2007
