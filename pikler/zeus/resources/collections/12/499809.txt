Xavier Bosch i Sancho     xavier bosch i sancho ( born july 21 , 1967 in barcelona ) is a catalan writer and journalist . bosch and antoni bassas created the program `` alguna pregunta més ? '' at catalunya ràdio . he received an ondas award for that program . he worked at `` la vanguardia '' , `` tv3 '' and `` rac 1 '' in sports . he was the director of the newspaper `` avui '' from 2007 to 2008. in 2010 , he founded a newspaper in catalan , `` diari ara '' . he wrote a trilogy whose first book ( `` se sabrà tot '' ) received a sant jordi award in 2009. his second and third novels were `` homes d'honor '' ( 2012 ) and `` eufòria '' . his last novel , `` algú com tu '' ( someone like you , 2015 ) , is winner of the ramon llull 2015 award . works . short stories . - `` jo , el simolses '' , barcelona : la magrana , 1992 . - `` estimat diari '' ( with lloll bertran , antoni bassas ) , barcelona : la magrana , 1996 . - `` vicis domèstics '' , barcelona : la magrana , 1998. novel . - `` la màgia dels reis '' , barcelona : columna - la galera , 1994 . - `` se sabrà tot '' , 2009 . - `` homes d'honor '' , barcelona : proa , 2012 .
