Curt Simmons     curtis thomas `` curt '' simmons ( born may 19 , 1929 ) is a former left-handed pitcher in major league baseball from 1947 to 1950 and 1952 to 1967. he was born in whitehall township , pennsylvania . he played for the philadelphia phillies from 1947 through 1960. he played for the st. louis cardinals from 1960 through 1966. then he played for the chicago cubs from 1966 through 1967. and then he played for the california angels in 1967 until his retirement later that year .
