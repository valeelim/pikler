Rowland Evans     rowland evans ( april 28 , 1921-march 23,2001 ) was a conservative `` chicago sun-times '' columnist and co-host of the cable news network 's ( cnn ) `` evans & novak '' , later `` evans , novak , hunt , & shields '' . he was also a political analyst for cnn , along with his partner on `` evans and novak '' and at the `` chicago sun times '' , robert novak .
