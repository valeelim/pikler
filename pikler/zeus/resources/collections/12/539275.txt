Dene Smuts     muldene `` dene '' smuts ( july 13 , 1949 – april 21 , 2016 ) was a south african politician . she was a member of parliament for the democratic alliance , serving in many capacities , including as shadow minister of justice and constitutional development . smuts was editor of `` fair lady '' magazine , managing editor of `` leadership '' , and was a prize-winning author . smuts was broadcasting and telecoms spokesperson for both the democratic party ( dp ) and democratic alliance ( da ) from 1994 and 1996. in 1992 , smuts became south africa ’ s first female whip in parliament and served as dp party chairperson from 1994 to 1997 .
