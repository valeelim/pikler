Splatoon     nintendo announced that a series of splatoon amiibo figures would be released along with the game , boy and girl inklings are sold separately and a squid figure is sold only as a bundle with either the game or the two other amiibos . people who pre-ordered `` splatoon '' at gamestop were given a download voucher that gave them splatoon-themed mii costumes to use in `` super smash bros. for wii u '' . `` splatoon '' was met with positive reviews . gamesradar rated the game a 3.5 out of 5 stars . they said that the game was a refreshing take on the shooter genre but criticized some of the features like lobbies and amiibo-locked content . destructoid rated the game 8.5. chris carter of destructoid , said that `` most of ( the game 's ) shortcomings can be forgiven in my mind because of how damn fun it is .
