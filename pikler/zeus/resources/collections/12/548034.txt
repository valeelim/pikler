Armando León Bejarano     armando león bejarano valadez ( april 11 , 1916 – july 6 , 2016 ) was a mexican orthopedic surgeon , physician , and politician . he was a member of the institutional revolutionary party ( pri ) . he served as the governor of morelos from 1972 until 1982. bejarano died on july 6 , 2016 in cuernavaca , morelos from pneumonia , aged 100 .
