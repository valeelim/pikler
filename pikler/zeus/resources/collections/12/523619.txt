Overcome     overcome is the debut studio album by british singer alexandra burke , released 19 october 2009 on syco music . it was released a year after burke won the british talent show , `` the x factor '' . it was also re-released in 2010 with a new artwork and three extra songs . `` overcome '' has received mainly positive reviews . information taken from `` overcome '' liner notes .
