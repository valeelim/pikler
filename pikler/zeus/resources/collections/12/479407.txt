Funeral viewing     a funeral viewing or body viewing is the time family and friends come to see the dead after the body has been prepared by a funeral home . the body is embalmed in order to keep the body looking as natural as possible . a viewing may take place at a funeral parlor , in a family home , or at a church . a funeral viewing may also be important to grieving process . other websites . - important etiquette tips for wakes , viewings , and visitations - funeral home traditions < br >
