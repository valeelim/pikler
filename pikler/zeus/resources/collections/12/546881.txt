Birkir Már Sævarsson     birkir már sævarsson ( born 11 november 1984 in reykjavik , iceland ) is an icelandic footballer who can play as a right fullback , right winger , left fullback or center back . he currently plays for the swedish club hammarby fotboll and the iceland national football team . he was included in iceland 's uefa euro 2016 squad . honours . - icelandic premier league : 2007 - icelandic cup : 2005 - icelandic league cup : 2008 birkir már sævarsson at national-football-teams.com
