Buddy Elias     bernhard `` buddy '' elias ( 2 june 1925 – 16 march 2015 ) was a swiss actor and president of the anne frank fonds . he was born in frankfurt am main , germany . he was the cousin of anne frank . elias was known for his role as agent ute nicolai in the 2014 movie `` the monuments men '' . elias died in basel , switzerland at the age of 89. other websites . - buddy elias at his agency - „erinnerungen vom dachboden “ , interview , einestages , 28. februar 2012 - biography - obituaries : faz , spiegel.de , sueddeutsche.de
