Rhododendron niveum     rhododendron niveum is a `` rhododendron '' species . it is native to northeastern india ( including sikkim ) , bhutan , and southern tibet . it grows at altitudes of 2600–3500 meters . it is a shrub or small tree that grows to 2–6 m in height , with leathery leaves 8.5–11 by 3.6–4.6 cm in size . flowers are magenta to lilac .
