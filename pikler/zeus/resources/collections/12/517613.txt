Elimination Chamber (2015)    elimination chamber ( 2015 ) elimination chamber ( 2015 ) ( called no escape ( 2015 ) in germany ) was a professional wrestling pay-per-view show made by wwe . it was held on may 31 , 2015 at american bank center in corpus christi , texas . it was the sixth elimination chamber event held by the wwe . 20,000 fans bought tickets for the event . the main event saw dean ambrose defeat wwe world heavyweight champion seth rollins by disqualification . this was also the first event to have a tag team elimination chamber match . in the match , the new day retained their wwe tag team championships . other websites . - official elimination chamber 2015 website
