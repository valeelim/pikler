IFK Växjö     ifk växjö is a sports club in the town of växjö in sweden . it was established on 2 june 1919. the club runs track and field athletics and disabled sports . it earlier also ran bandy and soccer . the men 's bandy team played in the swedish top division in 1941. the women 's soccer activity was transferred to östers if in 1983. that led to the establishments of östers if dam . carolina klüft has competed for the club . other websites . - official website
