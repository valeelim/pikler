2014–15 UEFA Champions League     the 2014–15 uefa champions league was the 23rd uefa champions league , and the 60th season of a european club tournament being played . the defending champions were real madrid , however , barcelona won their 5th title over juventus . the draw for the round of 16 was held on 15 december 2014. the first legs were played on 17 , 18 , 24 and 25 february , and the second legs were played on 10 , 11 , 17 and 18 march 2015. the draw for the quarter-finals was held on 20 march 2015. the first legs were played on 14 and 15 april , and the second legs were played on 21 and 22 april 2015. the draw for the semi-finals and final ( to come up with the `` home '' team ) was held on 24 april 2015. the first legs were played on 5 and 6 may , and the second legs were played on 12 and 13 may 2015. other websites . - 2014–15 uefa champions league on uefa.com
