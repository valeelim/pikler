Bonnie Lou     mary joan okum ( née kath ; october 27 , 1924 – december 8 , 2015 ) , known by her performing name bonnie lou , was an american musical pioneer . she was one of first female rock and roll singers . she was also one of the first artists to gain crossover success from country music to rock and roll . she was the `` top name '' on the first country music program regularly broadcast on a national tv network . lou was one of the first female co-hosts of a successful television talk show , and a regular musical performer on popular shows in the 1960s and 1970s . she `` was a prime mover in the first days of rockabilly , '' and is a member of the rockabilly hall of fame . lou died from complications of dementia under hospice care in cincinnati , ohio , aged 91 .
