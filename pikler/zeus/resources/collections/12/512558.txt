UK garage     uk garage ( sometimes shortened to ukg and simply garage ) is a genre of electronic music originating from england in the early 1990s . it is made up of elements from garage house , r & b , drum & bass , dance , reggae and hip hop music . it was very popular during the beginning of the 21st century with teenagers and young adults listening to it . some of its most popular musicians to perform uk garage are mis-teeq , sweet female attitude , so solid crew , shola ama , tinie tempah , dj pied piper , craig david and daniel bedingfield . it is also a genre that blends with grime .
