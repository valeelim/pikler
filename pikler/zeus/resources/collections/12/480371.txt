Show Me the Meaning of Being Lonely     `` show me the meaning of being lonely '' is a pop ballad from the backstreet boys . the song is about missing someone you love and asking the meaning of living a lonely life . on the u.s. `` billboard '' hot 100 , the song hit # 6. it hit # 2 on the hot adult contemporary chart . the song was released on december 21 , 1999 .
