Christian Louboutin     christian louboutin ( ; born 7 january 1963 ) is a french footwear designer . his signature is the red soles on the shoes he designs . louboutin 's shoes were named the `` most prestigious women 's shoes '' in 2007 , 2008 , and 2009. in 2011 , louboutin became the most searched-for shoe brand online .
