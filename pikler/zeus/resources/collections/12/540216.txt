Papanasam, Tirunelveli    papanasam , tirunelveli papanasam is a town in tirunelveli district . this town is where the thamirabarani river rises . in one of papanasam 's temples , a ritual is held to wash away sins . people throw the clothes they wear into the thamirabarani river . the movie acted by kamalahaasan was shot in and named papanasam .
