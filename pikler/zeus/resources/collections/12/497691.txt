Arrondissement of Marmande     the arrondissement of marmande is an arrondissement of france . it is part of the lot-et-garonne `` département '' in the nouvelle-aquitaine region . its capital , and subpréfecture of the department , is the city of marmande . when the lot-et-garonne department was created on 17 february 1800 , the `` arrondissement '' of marmande was part of that original department . the `` arrondissement '' of marmande has an area of . it has 83,245 inhabitants and a population density of inhabitants/km² . the `` arrondissement '' is in the northwest of lot-et-garonne and is bordered to the north by the dordogne department , to the east by the `` arrondissement '' of villeneuve-sur-lot , to the southeast by the `` arrondissement '' of agen , to the south by the `` arrondissement '' of nérac and to the west by the gironde department . after the reorganisation of the cantons in france , cantons are not subdivisions of the `` arrondissements '' so they could have `` communes '' that belong to different `` arrondissements '' . in the `` arrondissement '' of marmande , there are only two cantons where not all their `` communes '' are in the `` arrondissement '' : les forêts de gascogne and le val du dropt . the following table shows the distribution of the `` communes '' in the cantons and `` arrondissements '' :
