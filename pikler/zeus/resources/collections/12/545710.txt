Gil Bartosh     gilbert c. bartosh , sr. ( may 21 , 1930 – june 4 , 2016 ) was an american football player and coach . he served as the head football coach at the university of texas at el paso ( utep ) from 1974 to 1975 , with a record of 6–28 . he was born in granger , texas . he died in liberty hill , texas , aged 86 .
