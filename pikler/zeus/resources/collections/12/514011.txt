Brentor     brentor is a small village about north of tavistock within west devon . the village is famous for it 's church , `` church of st michel de rupe '' ( saint michael of the rock ) . the church which dates back to the 13th century sits high upon a tor overlooking the village . it can be seen from miles around and is visited by many people each year . the rock it sits on is what is left of a volcano and is high . it was once used as a beacon tower to warn people of approaching enemy ships .
