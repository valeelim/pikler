Karel Pečko     karel pečko ( september 29 , 1920 – may 2 , 2016 ) was a slovenian artist . in 1957 , he created an arts pavilion ( art school ) . in 1963 , he became the school 's principal . from 1975 to 1991 , he was in charge of a cultural institute in slovenj gradec . he also led the slovenj gradec art gallery ( ) until 1997. in 1996 , he was honored with the honorary medal of freedom of the republic of slovenia . pečko died in slovenj gradec on may 2 , 2016 at the age of 95 .
