Michèle Morgan     michèle morgan ( ; 29 february 1920 – 20 december 2016 ) was a french movie actress . morgan was born simone renée roussel in neuilly-sur-seine , hauts-de-seine , a wealthy western suburb of paris . morgan was known for her roles in `` port of shadows '' , `` passage to marseille '' ( alongside humphrey bogart ) , `` stormy waters '' , `` fabiola '' , `` the proud and the beautiful '' , and in `` the grand maneuver '' . morgan died on 20 december 2016 at her home in paris , aged 96. other websites . - michèle morgan at filmsdefrance.com - photographs of michèle morgan
