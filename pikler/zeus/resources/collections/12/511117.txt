Reform Party of the United States of America     the reform party of the united states of america ( rpusa ) , generally known as the reform party usa or the reform party , is a political party in the united states , founded in 1995 by ross perot . it can be seen as a third party to the republican and democratic parties . the party had nominated several notable candidates over the years , such as founder ross perot , pat buchanan , and ralph nader . the party 's most significant victory came when jesse ventura was elected governor of minnesota in 1998 .
