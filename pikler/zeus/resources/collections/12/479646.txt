José Manuel Lara Bosch     josé manuel lara bosch ( 8 march 1946 – 31 january 2015 ) was a spanish media executive and businessman . he was the ceo of grupo planeta since 2003 and atresmedia since 2012. bosch was born in barcelona , spain . bosch died in barcelona , spain from pancreatic cancer , aged 68. he is survived by his wife of 40 years , consuelo garcía píriz and his four children .
