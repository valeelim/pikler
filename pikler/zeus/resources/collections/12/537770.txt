Aérocentre     aérocentre is a french group of aerospace engineering companies and research centres . it is in the region of centre-val de loire in the middle of france . there are over 321 companies . about 20.000 people work there in the aviation and space flight industries . the headquarters of aérocentre is in châteauroux . the chairman of the group is jean-michel sanchez . partners . - agence régionale pour l'innovation - banque populaire - airemploi - centre d'études supérieures industrielles of orléans - électrcité de france - institut polytechnique des sciences avancées other websites . - website of aérocentre
