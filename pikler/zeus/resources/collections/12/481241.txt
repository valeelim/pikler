Alexander Cools     alexander ( `` lex '' ) rudolf cools ( the hague , 1941 â€“ nijmegen , 7 september 2013 ) was a dutch neuroscientist . he was interested in the pharmacology of behavior . cools got his doctor of philosophy in 1973 at the radboud university nijmegen . he was a professor in nijmegen from 1985 until his retirement in 2006. a special issue of the scientific journal `` behavioural pharmacology '' was dedicated to his memory in 2014. cools was one of the founders of the european behavioural pharmacology society . he became its second president . he received that society 's distinguished achievement award in 2003. cools was the first to propose ( 1976 ) the existence of different types of dopamine receptors . this claim is correct but was initially not believed . cools is also known for his work on the basal ganglia .
