Five Nights at Freddy's 3    five nights at freddy 's 3 five nights at freddy 's 3 is a 2015 indie point-and-click survival horror video game developed by scott cawthon . it is the third entry in the `` five nights at freddy 's '' series . the game was released on steam on march 2 , 2015 , then for android devices on march 7 , 2015. the game is set 30 years after `` five nights at freddy 's '' . the player assumes the role of a newly hired employee at fazbear 's fright , a horror-themed amusement park attraction . in addition , the player must monitor the status of three operating systems - cameras , audio , and ventilation - and reboot them whenever they begin to malfunction . they have to defend from springtrap and the 'phantoms ' , which are ghost animatronics that ca n't hurt you .
