Penza Oblast     penza oblast ( , `` penzenskaya oblast '' ) is a administrative division of russia . penza oblast has over 3000 rivers ; the overall length is 15,458 km . in the waters of the oblast , there are about 50 species of fish . the regional center of penza was built in 1663 as a russian fortress . penza oblast is a part of the volga economic region . population of this oblast : 1,386,186 ( 2010 ) . ethnic composition ( 2010 ) : - russians - 86.8 % - tatars - 6.4 % - others - 6.8 % notable people . - aristarkh lentulov , avant-garde artist - victor skumin , philosopher , writer , psychologist , psychiatrist , psychotherapist , and medical doctor . related pages . - russia
