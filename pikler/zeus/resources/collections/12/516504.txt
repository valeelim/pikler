Frank Martinus Arion     frank martinus arion ( born frank efraim martinus ; 17 december 1936 – 28 september 2015 ) was a dutch antillean author , poet , and linguist . he wrote over ten books in dutch and papiamento . his best known works were `` dubbelspel '' , `` afscheid van de koningin '' , `` nobele wilden '' , `` de laatste vrijheid '' and `` de deserteurs '' . he also worked in netherlands and suriname . he was born in curaçao . arion died on 28 september 2015 in curaçao , aged 78 .
