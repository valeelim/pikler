Department for Social Security     the department of social security ( dss ) was a government department in the united kingdom . the old abbreviation is still often used informally . the department was created in 1988. the department of health and social security was divided into two departments , one of which was the dss . during 2001 , the department was largely replaced by the department for work and pensions . the other responsibilities of the department were assumed by the treasury and the ministry of defence .
