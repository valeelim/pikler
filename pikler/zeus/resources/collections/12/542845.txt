Wendy Davis (politician)    wendy davis ( politician ) wendy russell davis ( born wendy jean russell ; may 16 , 1963 ) is an american lawyer and democratic politician . she was born in west warwick , rhode island , but was raised in fort worth , texas . davis represented district 10 in the texas senate from 2009 to 2015. she previously served on the fort worth city council . davis ran for the office of governor of texas in the 2014 election but lost to greg abbott with 59-38 percent .
