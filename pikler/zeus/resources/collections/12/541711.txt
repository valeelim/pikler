Automatic Identification System     automatic identification system ( ais ) is a way for a ship to use a radio to tell other ships about it 's position . it sends messages every few seconds . these are received other ships and by stations on land and on some satellites . a ship knows its position by using global positioning system ( gps ) . since 2004 , all ships above 300 tons and all passenger ships have to use ais . ais was made to avoid ships colliding .
