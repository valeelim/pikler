Grand Theft Auto VI     grand theft auto vi is a confirmed video game in the grand theft auto series ( rockstar games ) , set to be released sometime between 2016-2020. the game has been predicted to feature one of the following locations in its map : the entire united states , bogota ( capital of columbia ) , las vegas nevada , or the previous grand theft auto games ' maps combined into one enormous setting . rumors have it that the game 's story mode may feature a female protagonist . actors who are rumored to have been cast as voices for the game include eva mendes and ryan gosling .
