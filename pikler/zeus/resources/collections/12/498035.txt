Sporosarcina ureae     sporosarcina ureae is a species of bacteria . it is the type species of its genus and is closely related to `` bacillus '' species . it lives in the soil . it is able to break down urea . therefore , the name ( latin : `` urea '' ) . the cells are coccoid . it has some flagella . `` sporosarcina ureae '' is heterotrophic . it is aerobic , it needs oxygen . `` sporosarcina ureae '' is one of the bacteria which can make use of urea , it has the enzyme urease . it is often found in soils where are large amounts of urine like meadows where cows are kept . it is also found in manure .
