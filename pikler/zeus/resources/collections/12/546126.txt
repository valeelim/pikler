Heatwave (band)    heatwave ( band ) heatwave is an international disco and funk band . the group was formed around 1975. its most popular lineup was americans johnnie wilder , jr and keith wilder for vocals , englishman rod temperton for the keyboards , jamaican eric johns and briton roy carter for guitar . the group had hit singles , like `` boogie nights '' and `` always and forever '' . the group broke up in 1984. however , heatwave got back together around 1988 and resumed releasing r & b music . in 2002 the group released an extended remix of `` boogie nights '' .
