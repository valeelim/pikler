Norman Stansfield     norman stansfield ( also known as stansfield ) is a fictional character and the main villain of luc besson 's 1994 movie `` '' . he is played by gary oldman . he is a corrupt and mentally unstable drug enforcement administration ( dea ) agent . he has been named as one of cinema 's greatest villains . in honor of its influence , noah walden of msn movies described the stansfield character as `` the role that launched a thousand villains '' .
