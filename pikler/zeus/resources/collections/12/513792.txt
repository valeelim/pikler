Dave Foley     david scott `` dave '' foley ( born january 4 , 1963 ) is a canadian actor , stand-up comedian , director , producer and writer . he is best known for his work in `` the kids in the hall '' and `` newsradio '' and for voicing flik in `` a bug 's life '' . foley was born in etobicoke , ontario . his mother , mary , was a homemaker . his father , michael , was a steam-fitter . his father is of irish descent and his mother is of english , irish , scottish and welsh descent .
