Peter Varghese     peter joseph noozhumurry varghese ( born 1956 ) is an australian diplomat and public servant . he has been the secretary of the department of foreign affairs and trade since 3 december 2012. varghese was born in kenya to nasrani parents from kerala , india . he moved to australia as a child in 1964. he studied history and graduated from the university of queensland . he is married and has a son .
