Murray Weidenbaum     murray lew weidenbaum ( february 10 , 1927 – march 20 , 2014 ) , was an american economist . he was the edward mallinckrodt distinguished university professor and honorary chairman of the murray weidenbaum center on the economy , government , and public policy at washington university in st. louis . he has served as assistant secretary of the treasury for economic policy ( 19691971 ) . he was chairman of president ronald reagan 's first council of economic advisors from 1981–1982 .
