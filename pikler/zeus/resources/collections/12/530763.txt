Kim Yong-nam     kim yong-nam ( ; born 4 february 1928 ) is a north korean politician . he serves as president of the presidium of the supreme people 's assembly . he has held the position since 1998. previously , he served as minister of foreign affairs from 1983 to 1998. he was elected a member of the presidium of the politburo of the central committee of the workers ' party of korea ( wpk ) in 2010 .
