Combat boot     combat boots are military boots designed to be worn by soldiers during combat or combat training . they are not intended for wear during parades and other ceremonial duties . modern combat boots are designed to provide a combination of grip , ankle stability , and foot protection suitable for a rugged environment . they are traditionally made of hardened and sometimes waterproofed leather . today , many combat boots incorporate technologies originating in civilian hiking boots , such as gore-tex nylon side panels , which improve ventilation and comfort . they are also often specialized for certain climates and conditions , such as jungle boots , desert boots , and cold weather boots as well as specific uses , such as tanker boots and jump boots .
