Regulate (song)    regulate ( song ) `` regulate '' is a hip hop song performed by nate dogg and warren g. the song was released in the summer of 1994. it appears on the soundtrack of `` above the rim '' , and later `` regulate ... g funk era '' . the song reached # 2 on the `` billboard '' hot 100.. it also hit # 8 on the r & b/hip hop chart . the song samples `` i keep forgettin ' ( every time you 're near ) '' by michael mcdonald .
