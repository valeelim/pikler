2011 Campeonato Brasileiro Série A     the 2011 campeonato brasileiro série a ( officially the brasileirão petrobras 2011 for sponsorship reasons ) was the 55th edition of the campeonato brasileiro série a , the top-level of professional football in brazil . it began on may 21 and was scheduled to end on december 4. fluminense comes in as the defending champion having won the 2010 season . results . - 2011 campeonato brasileiro série a - results of first round - 2011 campeonato brasileiro série a - results of second round
