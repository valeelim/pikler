Lubomír Lipský     lubomír lipský ( april 19 , 1923 – october 2 , 2015 ) was a czech actor . he was known for his roles in `` four murders are enough , darling '' , `` grapes '' , and in `` six bears and a clown '' . he was born in pelhřimov . his brother was director oldřich lipský . he died in prague , aged 92 .
