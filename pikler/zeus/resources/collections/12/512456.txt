Ain't It Funny (Murder Remix)    ai n't it funny ( murder remix ) `` ai n't it funny ( murder remix ) '' is a song by american entertainer jennifer lopez . the song , a remix of `` ai n't it funny '' , features additional vocals from ja rule ( jeffery atkins ) and cadillac tah as well as backing vocals from fellow r & b singer ashanti . the song is about a relationship where a man plays around and cheats on a woman .
