Charles Gibson     charles dewolf `` charlie '' gibson ( born march 9 , 1943 ) is a united states former broadcast television anchor and journalist . he was born in evanston , illinois . he grew up in washington , d.c. he was a host of `` good morning america '' from 1987 to 1998 , and 1999 to 2006. he was the anchor of `` world news with charles gibson '' from 2006 to 2009. in 2008 he was a moderator for the us presidential debates . he graduated in 1965 with a degree in history from princeton university .
