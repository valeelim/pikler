Daan Myngheer     daan myngheer ( 13 april 1993 – 28 march 2016 ) was a belgian cyclist . in 2015 , he was a member of the verandas willems cycling team and in 2016 he was a member of the roubaix–lille métropole . he was born in roeselare , belgium . myngheer died in a hospital in ajaccio , france after suffering a heart attack during the criterium international , aged 22 .
