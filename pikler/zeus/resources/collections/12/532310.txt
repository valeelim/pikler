State Council of China     the state council is the cabinet of the people 's republic of china . currently , the council has 35 members : the premier , four vice premiers , five state councilors ( of whom two are also ministers ) , and 25 additional ministers and chairs of major agencies . constitutionally , the state council is the same as the central people 's government since 1954 ( particularly in relation to local governments ) . in the politics of china , the central people 's government forms one of three interlocking branches of power , the others being the communist party of china and the people 's liberation army . the state council directly oversees the various subordinate people 's governments in the provinces , and in practice maintains membership with the top levels of the communist party of china .
