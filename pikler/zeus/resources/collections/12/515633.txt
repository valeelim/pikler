Chief Wiggum     chief clarence `` clancy '' wiggum is a fictional character on the long running tv show the simpsons . his voice is performed by hank azaria . he is the police chief of springfield . his surname `` wiggum '' is matt groening 's mother 's maiden name . clancy wiggum is the police chief of springfield . he is the father of ralph wiggum and the husband of sarah wiggum . he is often shown as an incompetent and lazy policeman .
