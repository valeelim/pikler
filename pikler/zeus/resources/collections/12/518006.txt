Shaggy (singer)    shaggy ( singer ) orville richard burrell cd ( born october 22 , 1968 , kingston ) , best known by his stage name shaggy , is a jamaican-canadian reggae fusion singer and deejay . he is best known for his hit singles `` boombastic '' , `` it was n't me '' , `` hey sexy lady '' , and `` angel '' . he is said to have got his stage name from his shaggy hair . he is also known in canada as `` manitoba 's first son. '' . burrell was born in manitoba , canada . at the age of 18 , he and his family moved to the flatbush area of brooklyn , new york . in 1987 , he took singing courses in brooklyn and was discovered a year later , while singing in the streets with friends . shaggy was also in the united states marine corps and got the mos of 0811 ( field artillery cannon crewman ) . shaggy served with a firing battery from the 10th marine regiment during the gulf war . shaggy worked on his signature singing voice in the marine corps and it is also where he got the idea for his song `` boombastic '' . discography . - `` pure pleasure '' ( 1993 )
