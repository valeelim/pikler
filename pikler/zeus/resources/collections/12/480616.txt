Brit Hume     alexander britton `` brit '' hume ( born june 22 , 1943 ) is a political commentator and television journalist . hume had a 23-year career with abc news . there he contributed to `` world news tonight '' , `` nightline '' and `` this week '' . he served as abc 's chief white house correspondent from 1989 through 1996. he then spent 12 years as the washington , d.c. , managing editor of the fox news channel . he was also the anchor of `` special report with brit hume '' . since 2008 , he has been the senior political analyst for fox news and a regular public-affairs panelist for the television program `` fox news sunday '' .
