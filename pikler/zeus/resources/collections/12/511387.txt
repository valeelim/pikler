Fred Eckhardt     fred eckhardt ( 1926 – august 10 , 2015 ) was an american brewer , homebrewing advocate and publicist . he wrote about brewed beverages—beer and sake , and wrote the 1989 book , `` the essentials of beer style '' . he called himself a `` beer writer , '' a `` beer historian , '' and a `` beer critic . '' he was a local celebrity in portland , oregon , which eckhardt described as `` the brewing capital of the world . ''
