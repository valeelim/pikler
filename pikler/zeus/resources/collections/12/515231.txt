Suvigya Sharma     suvigya sharma ( b. july 28 , 1983 ) is an award winning indian artist and painter based in mumbai , india . he is known for creating miniature paintings , tanjore painting , fresco work and lifelike portraits . he was awarded with `` bharat gaurav award '' in 2012 for his contribution in filed of arts . his latest collection `` an art collector 's paradise '' featuring colonial stamp paper paintings embellished with 24 carat gold were inaugurated by actress rani mukherji in september , 2014. other websites . - official website
