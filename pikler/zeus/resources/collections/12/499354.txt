Kermit, Texas    kermit , texas kermit is a city in the state of texas in the united states . it is the county seat of winkler county . about 5,690 people lived there in 2010. after theodore roosevelt came to the city , it was named for his son kermit roosevelt . on july 16 , 1926 , after discovery of oil in the hendrick oil field near kermit , the city grew quickly .
