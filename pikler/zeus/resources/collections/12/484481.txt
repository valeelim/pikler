Schwerte     schwerte is a city in north rhine-westphalia , germany . about 48,000 people live there . schwerte is in the ruhr valley just north of the mountainous sauerland region . the ruhr river runs through the center of schwerte . schwerte is a popular cycling destination for tourists . schwerte received civic rights in the 12th century . it was a member of the medieval hanseatic league . during world war ii schwerte housed a branch of the buchenwald concentration camp . the camp held 710 prisoners . in 2015 schwerte officials have proposed housing refugees at the site of the former concentration camp . the plan has been criticized as a bad idea by many germans including the premier of north rhine-westphalia . officials say the buildings were built after the war and should not be a problem . refugees coming to germany include those from syria , afghanistan , eritrea , kosovo and serbia . other websites . - official site - heimatverein ergste internet site of the local history society of ergste
