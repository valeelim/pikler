École Centrale de Lyon    école centrale de lyon école centrale de lyon is a graduate school in engineering in france . it is in a campus of the écully ( east of france ) . its different curricula lead to the following french & european degrees : - ingénieur centralien de lyon ( centralien graduate engineer master 's degree level program ) - masters & phd ( doctorate studies ) - mastères spécialisés ( ms ) . education and research are performed mainly in french and english languages . students from a dozen of nationalities participate to the different classes at école centrale de lyon . most of the 1500 engineering graduate students at école centrale de lyon live in residential buildings near the research labs on campus . these are also shared with 6,000 students from emlyon business school . the school is member of the french aerospace network france aerotech . notable graduates . - hervé renaudeau , french aerospace engineer other websites . - official website
