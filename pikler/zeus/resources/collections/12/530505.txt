Benoît Violier     benoît violier ( 22 august 1971 – 31 january 2016 ) was a french-swiss chef . he owned the three michelin star restaurant de l'hôtel de ville from 2012 until his death . the restaurant was first on la liste in december 2015. violier was born in saintes , charente-maritime . he studied in paris . he moved to switzerland in 1996 to work for philippe rochat . he became a swiss citizen in 2014. violier was found dead from a gunshot wound on 31 january 2016 in crissier . he was 44. his death was reported as a suicide .
