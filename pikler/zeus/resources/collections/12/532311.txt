National People's Congress    national people 's congress the national people 's congress ( usually abbreviated npc ) is the national legislature of the people 's republic of china . it had 2,987 members in 2013. it is the largest parliament in the world . under china 's current constitution , the npc is unicameral ; it is not divided into a lower house and an upper house . it the power to legislate , to oversee the operations of the government , and the power to elect the major officers of state .
