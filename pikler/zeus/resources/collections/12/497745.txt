2015 Sana'a mosque bombings     the 2015 sana'a mosque bombings were a series of three suicide attacks that happened during midday prayers on march 20 , 2015 in sana'a , yemen . in the attack 142 people were killed . it left 357 people injured . the mosques were mainly attended by houthis supporters . the houthis are the ruling faction in sana'a . islamic state later claimed that they were behind the bombings .
