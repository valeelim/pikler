All Night Long (1981 movie)    all night long ( 1981 movie ) all night long is a 1981 comedy movie starring barbra streisand , gene hackman , diane ladd , dennis quaid , kevin dobson , and william daniels . it was written by w. d. richter and directed by jean-claude tramont . the movie was generally a failure . it had an opening weekend of about $ 1.4 million and grossed around $ 10 million worldwide . this is a little over $ 26 million in 2016 dollars . it was nominated for worst actress at the golden raspberry awards in 1982 .
