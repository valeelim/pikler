Jimmy Wales Foundation     the jimmy wales foundation is a non-profit organization started by jimmy wales to promote internet freedom . the organization was established with prize money from the “ knowledge award ” , which was presented to jimmy and tim berners-lee at the first knowledge conference in dubai . the organization talks about individual human rights cases of real people who were persecuted . one of the people featured is bassel khartabil , formerly from creative commons syria . bassel was imprisoned in syria in 2012 , and disappeared completely a few years later . bassel khartabil has been killed in 2015. another featured case is roya saberinejad , who went to visit her family in iran , and never returned . the purpose of the jimmy wales foundation is to stop government censorship and promote freedom of expression on the internet . the goals of the jimmy wales foundation are to : 1. research the problem of freedom of expression and provide public information about the international problem 1. put political pressure on governments that control freedom of speech
