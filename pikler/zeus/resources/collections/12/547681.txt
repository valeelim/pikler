Geneviève Castrée     geneviève castrée elverum ( 1981 – 9 july 2016 ) was a canadian comics artist , illustrator , and musician . she was from loretteville , quebec . she once recorded under the name woelv and later switched to ô paon . castrée died on 9 july 2016 from pancreatic cancer in anacortes , washington , aged 34 .
