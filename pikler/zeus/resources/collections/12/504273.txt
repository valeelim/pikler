Sudha Shivpuri     sudha shivpuri ( 14 july 1937 – 20 may 2015 ) was an indian stage , movie and television actress . she was known for her role as `` baa '' in the hindi tv serial `` kyunki saas bhi kabhi bahu thi '' ( 2000–2008 ) . shivpuri died from multiple organ failure on 20 may 2015 in mumbai , india , aged 78 .
