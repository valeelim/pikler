Marit Nybakk     marit nybakk ( born 14 february 1947 ) is a norwegian labour party politician . she has been a member of parliament for oslo since 1986. she is the first vice president of the norwegian parliament since 2013 , and is also the woman who has served the longest time in the parliament during its entire history . she is also president of the norwegian association for women 's rights since 2016 .
