Premier of Ontario     the premier of ontario is the head of the government of ontario , canada . the premier is appointed by the lieutenant governor of ontario . he or she is the head of the executive council of ontario . the premier is usually the leader of the largest party in the legislative assembly of ontario . the first premier of ontario was john sandfield macdonald . he was premier from 1867 to 1871. the current premier is kathleen wynne . she is the first female premier of ontario . related pages . - list of premiers of ontario - premier ( canada ) - leader of the opposition ( ontario )
