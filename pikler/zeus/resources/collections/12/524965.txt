Ron Bouchard     ron bouchard ( november 23 , 1948december 10 , 2015 ) was an american nascar driver . he was the 1981 nascar winston cup rookie of the year . he was born in fitchburg , massachusetts . bouchard began his nascar career in 1981 in the # 47 j.d . stacy buick for owner jack beebe ( race hill farm team ) . in 1982 bouchard finished a career-high eighth in the final points standing with 15 top 10 finishes in 30 races . he ran full-time in 1983 , 1984 , and 1985. bouchard died of cancer in fitchburg , massachusetts at the age of 67. other websites . - ron 's biography
