Aldo Ciccolini     aldo ciccolini ( , 15 august 1925 – 1 february 2015 ) , was an italian-french pianist . he was born in naples , italy . he taught at the conservatoire de paris from 1970 to 1988. his students included akiko ebi , géry moutier , jean-yves thibaudet , artur pizarro , nicholas angelich , andré sayasov and jean-luc kandyoti . ciccolini died at the age of 89 on 1 february 2015 at his home in paris .
