Eagle nebula     the eagle nebula is a young open cluster of stars in the constellation serpens . the nebula is catalogued as messier 16 ( m16 ) , and as ngc 6611. it is also called the star queen nebula and the spire . these last names refer to visual impressions of the dark patch near the center of the nebula . this is called the `` pillars of creation '' . it was photographed by the hubble space telescope . the nebula contains several active star-forming gas and dust regions , including the pillars of creation .
