Berge Furre     berge ragnar furre ( 13 april 1937 – 11 january 2016 ) was a norwegian historian , theologian and politician . he was a member of the socialist people 's party ( sf ) and later socialist left party ( sv ) . he was the leader of the socialist left party from 1976 until 1983. he was also known for work on 1900s norwegian history . he was born in sjernarøy , finnøy . furre died after an illness on 11 january 2016 , aged 78 .
