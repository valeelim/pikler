Jay Roach     matthew jay roach ( born june 14 , 1957 ) is an american movie director , producer and screenwriter . he is best known for directing the `` austin powers '' movies and `` meet the parents '' . he also directed the comedy movie `` dinner for schmucks '' ( 2010 ) , the hbo political dramas `` recount '' ( 2008 ) and `` game change '' ( 2012 ) , and the political comedy `` the campaign '' ( 2012 ) . his twelfth movie , the biographical drama `` trumbo '' , was released on november 6 , 2015 to positive reviews . he won two emmy awards ( 2008 , 2012 ) .
