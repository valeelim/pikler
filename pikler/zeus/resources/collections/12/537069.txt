Shozo Awazu     shozo awazu ( 18 april 1923 – 17 march 2016 ) was a japanese master of judo . he won the rank of kōdōkan 9th dan . he led the development of judo in france . he was born in kyoto , japan . awazu was considered to be one of the top experts in ne waza ( grappling techniques ) , kata and tandoku-renshu . awazu died in paris , france on 17 march 2016 at the age of 92 .
