Balázs Birtalan     balázs birtalan ( october 12 , 1969 . – may 14 , 2016 ) was a hungarian author , poet , publicist , psychotherapist and activist . he was mostly known for his activism in the gay christian movement in hungary . he was the son of the poet ferenc birtalan . he was born in budapest . he was gay . birtalan died on may 14 , 2016 , aged 46 .
