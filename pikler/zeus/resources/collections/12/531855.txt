The Elements of Style     the elements of style is a american english how-to-do-it writing style guide . it lists brief guidelines for writing simple english . the book ran to many reprints . the original was composed by william strunk jr. in 1918 , and was published by harcourt in 1920. it had eight `` elementary rules of usage '' , ten `` elementary principles of composition '' , `` a few matters of form '' , a list of 49 `` words and expressions commonly misused '' , and a list of 57 `` words often misspelled '' . e. b. white much enlarged and revised the book for publication by macmillan , in 1959. that was the first edition of the so-called `` strunk & white '' , which `` time '' called in 2011 `` one of the 100 best and most influential books written in english since 1923 '' . the first printing of the 1959 edition sold about two million copies . in the following four decades , more than ten million copies of three printings have been sold .
