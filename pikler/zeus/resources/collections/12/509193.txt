Mike Rounds     mike rounds ( born october 24 , 1954 ) is the junior united states senator from south dakota . he is a member of the u.s. republican party and has been in senate since january 3 , 2015. rounds was born on october 24 , 1954 in huron , south dakota . he graduated from the south dakota state university . he is married to jean rounds . they have four children . other websites . - mike rounds , u.s. senator for south dakota , home page
