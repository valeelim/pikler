Eddy Wally     eddy wally ( born eduard rené van de walle ; 12 july 1932 – 6 february 2016 ) was a belgian singer . he was nicknamed the `` voice of europe '' . he was best known for his hits `` chérie '' ( 1966 ) , `` als marktkramer ben ik geboren '' ( 1969 ) and `` ik spring uit een vliegmachien '' ( 1996 ) . he was also a popular meme in 2014. wally was born in zelzate , east flanders . he was married to mariëtte roegiers until her death in october 2012. they had a daughter , marina wally ( born 1957 ) , who became a singer . he retired in 2012 after poor health . wally died from a brain haemorrhage on 6 february 2016 in zelzate . he was aged 83. other websites . - official website
