Prince Antoine, Marquis of Baux    prince antoine , marquis of baux prince antoine of monaco , marquis of baux ( 16 december 1717 – 4 february 1718 ) , was a monégasque prince and member of the house of grimaldi . he was known as the marquis of baux as the hier to the throne of monaco . he was also the and count of matignon . he died before succeeding his mother . born in monaco , he was the eldest son of the then-reigning princess louise hippolyte of monaco and her husband , jacques i. he was given the title of marquis of baux , the traditional title for an heir to the monégasque throne .
