Olga Korbut     olga valentinovna korbut ( born may 16 , 1955 in grodno , belarus ) , also known as the `` sparrow from minsk '' , is a retired belarusian gymnast . she won four gold medals and two silver medals at the summer olympic games , in which she competed in 1972 and 1976 for the soviet team . she could do backwards flips from the balance beam and uneven parallel bars , when no one else could . after her retirement from gymnastics , she became a teacher .
