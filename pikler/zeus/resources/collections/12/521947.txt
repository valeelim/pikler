Chelsea, London    chelsea , london chelsea is a well-known area in london . it was a borough , but now it is in the royal borough of kensington and chelsea . it is an area where the property values are very high , and the shops and facilities cater to wealthy people . it is the home of sloane square , the king 's road and chelsea f.c . it was the centre of swinging london in the 1960s , and is still a centre of the fashion trade . sloane street is one of london 's premier shopping destinations , with high-end fashion or jewellery boutiques such as cartier , tiffany & co. , dolce & gabbana , prada , gucci , harrods , dior , louis vuitton , jimmy choo , giorgio armani , yves saint laurent , chanel , valentino , bvlgari , gianni versace and graff .
