Magali Noël     magali noëlle guiffray ( 27 june 1930 – 23 june 2015 ) , better known as magali noël , was a french actress and singer . she was known for her song `` fais-moi mal , johnny '' ( `` hurt me johnny '' ) . her best known movie was `` la dolce vita '' . noël died on 23 june 2015 , aged 84. other websites . - official site
