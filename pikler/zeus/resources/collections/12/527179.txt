Sabri Yirmibeşoğlu     sabri yirmibeşoğlu ( 1 september 1928 – 2 january 2016 ) was a turkish army general . he was secretary-general of the national security council from 1988 to 1990. he was said to be behind a failed killing of president turgut özal in 1988. he was born in foça , izmir . yirmibeşoğlu died from kidney failure on 2 january 2016 in istanbul , aged 87 .
