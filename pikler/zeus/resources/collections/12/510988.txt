Manuel Contreras     juan manuel guillermo contreras sepúlveda ( 4 may 1929 – 7 august 2015 ) was a chilean military officer and the former head of dina , chile 's secret police during the dictatorship of general augusto pinochet . as head of dina he was the most powerful and feared man in the country , after pinochet . until his death in august 2015 of multiple organ failure , he was serving 58 sentences totaling 498 years in prison for kidnapping , forced disappearance and assassination .
