The Human Centipede 3 (Final Sequence)    the human centipede 3 ( final sequence ) the human centipede 3 ( final sequence ) is a 2015 english-language dutch body horror movie written and directed by tom six . it is the third and final installment in six 's `` human centipede '' trilogy . it was confirmed to be in production in may 2013. starring dieter laser and laurence r. harvey , the leading actors from the first two movies , in new roles . it also stars eric roberts as the governor . `` the human centipede 3 '' was released both theatrically and on video on demand on 22 may 2015 .
