David G. Hartwell     david geddes hartwell ( july 10 , 1941 january 20 , 2016 ) was an american critic , publisher and editor of science fiction and fantasy . he was best known for work with signet , pocket and tor books publishers . he was also known as an award-winning editor of anthologies . he was born in salem , massachusetts . hartwell died in pleasantville , new york from complications of a fall on january 20 , 2016 at the age of 74. other websites . - excerpt from the locus online interview with david hartwell , 2004 . - interview with david g. hartwell , spacewesterns.com , 2007 - profile of david hartwell by michael swanwick , 2009
