O Death     o death — also known as o , death , oh death , and conversations with death — is a traditional american folk song . country blues banjo player moran lee `` dock '' boggs ( 1898-1971 ) recorded the song in the late 1920s . a recording from the 1938 national folk festival held in washington , d.c. is on file with the library of congress . various folk music artists included `` oh , death '' on a variety of musical collections throughout the 1970s and '80s .
