Pierre Aubert     pierre aubert ( 3 march 1927 – 8 june 2016 ) was a swiss politician , lawyer and former member of the swiss federal council ( 1978–1987 ) . he was elected to the swiss federal council on 7 december 1977 as member of the social democratic party from the canton of neuchâtel . he handed over office on 31 december 1987. he was born in la chaux-de-fonds , canton of neuchâtel . aubert died on 8 june 2016 in neuchâtel , switzerland at the age of 89 .
