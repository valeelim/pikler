Chevrolet Traverse     chevrolet traverse is a full-sized crossover ( cuv ) made by general motors ' chevrolet division . it was introduced for the 2009 model year . chevrolet markets the traverse as a minivan ( without sliding side doors ) . the design for the traverse is loosely based off the concept vehicle the chevrolet sequel ( a hybrid crossover suv ) . it replaced the uplander ( a minivan ) and the trailblazer ( an suv ) in north america . after the uplander and its siblings failed to match the sales quota of toyota sienna and honda odyssey gm announced it would target crossovers . in south america it replaced the grand blazer . this vehicle uses a unibody platform called the gm lambda platform . in 2013 traverse was updated with a brand new grille , fascia and camaro inspired tail lights .
