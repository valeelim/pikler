Fred Brooks     frederick phillips brooks , jr. ( born april 19 , 1931 ) is an american computer architect , software engineer , and computer scientist . he is best known for managing the development of ibm 's system/360 family of computers alongside gerrit blaauw and gene amdahl and the os/360 software support package , then later writing candidly about the process in his seminal book `` the mythical man-month '' . brooks has received many awards , including the national medal of technology in 1985 and the turing award in 1999. brooks was born in durham , north carolina . he studied duke university , graduating in 1953 with a bachelor of science degree in physics , and he received a ph.d. in applied mathematics ( computer science ) from harvard university in 1956 , supervised by howard aiken .
