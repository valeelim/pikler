Hans Jansen     johannes juliaan gijsbert `` hans '' jansen ( 17 november 1942 - 5 may 2015 ) was a dutch scholar of contemporary islam and politician in the netherlands . he was born in amsterdam . until his death he was a member of the european parliament for the political party party for freedom . jansen died of a stroke at the age of 72. other websites . - his website , partially in english contains a list of publications and a cv . - interview at religioscope site
