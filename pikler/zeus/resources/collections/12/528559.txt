Tower defense     tower defense is a type of game where players use strategy to build towers to help defeat an enemy . in many tower defence games the player 's towers must stop an enemy from invading a base . if the towers can not stop the enemy , the player loses the game . the first game that was thought to be a tower defense game was `` rampart '' . other games started to be used for tower defense by using tools in the games to change them . those games were ones like `` starcraft '' , `` age of empires ii '' , and `` warcraft iii '' . tower defense games were made by independent developers also . the first game made in flash was `` flash element tower defense '' . the next game was made quickly after and was called `` desktop tower defense '' . desktop tower defense won an award from the independent games festival .
