Giant Bible of Echternach     the giant bible of echternach , echternacher riesenbibel or gothaer riesenbibel is an illuminated bible from the 11th century . it is written in the latin language . it was made for the abbot of echternach sometime between 1051 and 1081. the bible is in the national library of luxembourg ( formerly gotha ) . the giant bible of echternach was made at the abbey echernach . the abbey is famous for its scriptorium and its illuminated manuscripts from the 11th century . other websites . - luxembourg postage stamp with initials from the giant bible of echternach abbey ( 1 ) - luxembourg postage stamp with initials from the giant bible of echternach abbey ( 2 )
