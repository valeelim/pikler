Jean-Noël Rey     jean-noël rey ( 23 december 1949 – 16 january 2016 ) was a swiss socialist politician . he was the secretary of the socialist group of the federal assembly of switzerland . he was a member of the national council of switzerland between 2003 and 2007. he was also the ceo of the national postal service swiss post . rey was born in sierre . he was married twice and had two children . rey was shot and killed by terrorists on 16 january 2016 in ouagadougou , burkina faso . he was 66 .
