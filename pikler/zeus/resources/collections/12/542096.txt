Monteagle Stearns     monteagle `` monty '' stearns ( december 5 , 1924 – may 14 , 2016 ) was an american diplomat . he was the former ambassador to côte d'ivoire ( ivory coast ) ( 1976–79 ) and greece ( 1981–85 ) . he worked for presidents gerald ford , jimmy carter and ronald reagan . stearns was born in cambridge , massachusetts . he was a member of the american academy of diplomacy and council on foreign relations . stearns died on may 14 , 2016 in belmont , massachusetts at the age of 91 .
