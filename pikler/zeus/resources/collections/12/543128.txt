Aar de Goede     arie `` aar '' de goede ( 21 may 1928 – 16 may 2016 ) was a dutch politician . he was a member of the democrats 66. he served in the house of representatives ( 1967–1973 ) , european parliament ( 1979–1984 ) and senate ( 1986–1987 ) . as part of the executive he was state secretary of finance between 1973 and 1977. de goede died in zoeterwoude on 16 may 2016 , aged 87 .
