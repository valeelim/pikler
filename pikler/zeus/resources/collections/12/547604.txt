Münsingen     münsingen ( ) is a town and municipality in the administrative district of bern-mittelland in the canton of berne in switzerland . on 1 january 2013 the former municipalities of trimstein merged into the municipality of münsingen and on 1 january 2017 the former municipalities of tägertschi merged into the municipality of münsingen . the municipality lies on the river aare between the cities of bern and thun . other websites . - official website
