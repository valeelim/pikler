Charleston church shooting     on the evening of june 17 , 2015 , a mass shooting took place at emanuel african methodist episcopal church , that was the first church for black people ever in america , in charleston , south carolina , united states . nine people at the church were killed and another was injured . among the dead was minister and state senator , clementa c. pinckney . police arrested a white youth , later identified as 21-year-old dylann roof , in shelby , north carolina , the morning after the attack . dylann storm roof was named by the federal bureau of investigation ( fbi ) as the suspected killer after his father and uncle got in touch with police . they identified roof after seeing security photos of him in the news . roof was born in columbia , south carolina . he was living in a mostly african-american neighborhood near columbia at the time of the shooting . roof already had a police record of two arrests , both made in the months leading up to the attack .
