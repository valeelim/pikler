Government of Newfoundland and Labrador     the government of newfoundland and labrador is the provincial government of newfoundland and labrador , canada . until 1949 , newfoundland was a separate dominion in the british empire . with the newfoundland act , newfoundland became a canadian province . the legislature of newfoundland and labrador has two parts : the lieutenant governor of newfoundland and labrador and the general assembly of newfoundland and labrador . the general assembly is a unicameral body with 48 members . the party with the most members in the assembly elect the premier , who is the head of the cabinet . the head of the second largest party in the assembly is the leader of the opposition .
