Brighton hotel bombing     the brighton hotel bombing was an attack at the grand hotel in brighton , england . it happened on 12 october 1984. a time bomb was planted in the hotel by the provisional irish republican army ( pira ) member patrick magee . the purpose was to kill prime minister margaret thatcher and her ministers , who were staying at the hotel for the conservative party conference . thatcher narrowly escaped injury . five people were killed , including two high-profile members of the conservative party , and 31 were injured .
