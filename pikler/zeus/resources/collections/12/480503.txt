Pedro León Zapata     pedro león zapata ( february 27 , 1929 – february 6 , 2015 ) was a prominent venezuelan artist , humorist and cartoonist . he was born in la grita , táchira . zapata died in caracas , venezuela at the age of 85. other websites . - interview with zapata `` this revolution does not make me laugh '' , from the newspaper `` tal cual '' ( 2003 ) .
