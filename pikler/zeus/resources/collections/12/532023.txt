Wiesław Rudkowski     wiesław rudkowski ( 17 november 1946 – 14 february 2016 ) was a polish boxer . he was born in łódź . he competed in the 1968 summer olympics . he won a silver medal at the 1972 summer olympics in munich , losing in the final against dieter kottysch . rudkowski died from a heart attack on 14 february 2016 in warsaw . he was 69. other websites . - wiesław rudkowski olympic results
