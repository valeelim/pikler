Pincer     pincers are a hand tool used in many situations where a mechanical advantage is required . they are used to pinch , cut or pull an object . pincers are first-class levers . pincers differ from pliers in that the concentration of force is either to a point , or to an edge at a right angle to the handle of the tool . this allows pincers to be brought close to a surface . this is often helpful when removing nails from wood . pincers are often called end-nippers or end-cutters . carpenter 's pincers are very well suited to these tasks . pincers are primarily used for removing objects out of a material . pincers , often red-hot , have been used as an instrument of torture since the middle ages or earlier . pliers are a similar tool with a different type of head used for squeezing , rather than cutting and pulling . < br >
