Margot Honecker     margot honecker ( née feist ; 17 april 1927 – 6 may 2016 ) was an east german politician . she was a member of the east german communist party and the east german regime until 1989. she was born in halle , saxony-anhalt . from 1963 through 1989 , she was `` minister of people 's education '' ( `` ministerin für volksbildung '' ) of the gdr . she was married to erich honecker , the dictator of east germany ( 1971 – 1989 ) from 1953 until his death of liver cancer in 1994. she and her husband were exiled in chile during the 1990s . honecker died at her home while in exile in santiago , chile on 6 may 2016 , aged 89 .
