Henk Hofland     hendrik johannes adrianus `` henk '' hofland ( ; 20 july 1927 – 21 june 2016 ) was a dutch journalist , commentator , essayist and columnist . h.j.a . hofland , as he is also commonly known , is often referred to as the éminence grise of dutch journalism . in 1999 he was named dutch `` journalist of the century '' in a nationwide poll among his fans . he once called himself as belonging to the `` anarcho-liberal community '' , though his political orientation is that of the secular center of society .
