Liberty Heights     liberty heights is a 1999 american comedy-drama movie . it was directed by barry levinson and was produced by both barry levinson and paula weinstein . `` liberty heights '' was released on november 17 , 1999. the movie was written by levinson and it is semi-autobiographical account of his time growing up in baltimore during the 1950s . the movie gained positive reviews from critics and holds a 87 % rating on rotten tomatoes . cast . - adrien brody as van - ben foster as ben - orlando jones as little melvin - bebe neuwirth as ada - joe mantegna as nate - rebekah johnson as sylvia - david krumholtz as yussel - justin chambers as trey - carolyn murphy as dubbie - kevin sussman as alan - shane west as ted - anthony anderson as scribbles - elizabeth ann bennett as mary - carlton j smith as james brown
