Sebastiano Castagna     sebastiano castagna was an italian engineer . he was born in 1868 in aidone , sicily . he was murdered in ethiopia during a peace mission in 1938. castagna designed st george 's cathedral , an ethiopian orthodox cathedral in addis ababa , ethiopia . it is known for its octagonal shape . it is in the northern part of the city . he built it with the work of italian prisoners of war who were defeated at the battle of adwa in 1896. it is named for saint george .
