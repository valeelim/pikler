Roy Blunt     roy blunt ( born january 10 , 1950 ) is the junior united states senator from missouri . he is a member of the u.s. republican party and has been in senate since january 3 , 2011. he was in the united states house of representatives from 1997 to 2011. blunt was born on january 10 , 1950 in niangua , missouri . he studied at southwest baptist university and at missouri state university , springfield . he is married to abigail perlman . other websites . - roy blunt , u.s. senator for missouri , home page
