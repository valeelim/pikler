Saving All My Love for You     `` saving all my love for you '' is a 1985 song written by michael masser and gerry goffin with arrangement by gene page . it was originally a minor hit for marilyn mccoo and billy davis jr. in 1978 on their album `` marilyn & billy '' . a cover of the song was done by american recording artist whitney houston , for her debut , self-titled album , which was released on february 14 , 1985 , by arista records . the song was the second single from the album in the united states and third worldwide .
