Joe Arpaio     joseph m. `` joe '' arpaio ( born june 14 , 1932 ) is the six-time elected sheriff of maricopa county , arizona . a controversial figure , arpaio has been accused of abuse of power , misuse of funds , failure to investigate sex crimes , improper clearance of cases , unlawful enforcement of immigration laws , and election law violations , amongst others . he has been found guilty of racial profiling in federal court . other websites . - official website
