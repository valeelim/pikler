Joaquín Castro     joaquín castro ( born september 16 , 1974 ) is an american politician . he has served in the united states house of representatives for texas 's 20th congressional district since 2013. julián castro , his identical twin brother , served as mayor of san antonio from 2009 to 2014 and is now the 16th united states secretary of housing and urban development in the cabinet of president barack obama . other websites . - congressman joaquin castro official u.s. house website - joaquin castro for congress
