Alfonso Portugal     alfonso portugal díaz ( 21 january 1934 – 12 june 2016 ) was a mexican football . he played as defender for mexico in the 1958 fifa world cup . he was born in mexico city . portugal captained club américa to the 1965–66 mexican primera división title . he also played for club necaxa . in 1967 he appeared in one match for the chicago spurs of the npsl . portugal died on 12 june 2016 in atlixco , puebla , aged 82. other websites . - fifa profile - profile at mediotiempo
