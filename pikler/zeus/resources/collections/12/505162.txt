National Capital Region (Canada)    national capital region ( canada ) the national capital region is the official federal name for the area around ottawa , ontario . ottawa is the national capital of canada . the region includes the city of gatineau in quebec and the census metropolitan area ( cma ) which surrounds the two cities . some people think the national capital region should be formed into a separate capital district like other countries . the national capital region covers an area of . it has a population of 1,235,324. the ottawa part of the region is part of the eastern subregion of ontario . the ottawa-gatineau cma is the only cma in canada to include parts of two provinces .
