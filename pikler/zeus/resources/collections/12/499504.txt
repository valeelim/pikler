Old Calendarists     old calendarists is the name for two groups of eastern orthodox churches : - some churches have always used the julian calendar to determine holidays and church festivities . this means that from 1900 to about 2100 , the date of a given occasion is about thirteen days later , than if the gregorian calendar had been used . - some churches ( and splinter groups ) have switched back from the new calendar to the old one in the 20th century . in addition , other christian churches use the julian calendar : - a few oriental orthodox churches - the assyrian church of the east
