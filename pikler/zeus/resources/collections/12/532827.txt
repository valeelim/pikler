Ove Verner Hansen     ove verner hansen ( 20 july 1932 – 20 february 2016 ) was a danish opera singer and actor . he played the character `` bøffen '' ( or `` biffen '' in norwegian , literally meaning `` the steak '' in both languages ) in several of the olsen gang ( olsenbanden ) movies . he played a tall large slow-moving henchman , often seen lifting egon olsen up over his head , carrying him under one arm , and trying to kill him . hansen died in gentofte , denmark from a heart attack on 20 february 2016. he was aged 83. other websites . - ove verner hansen at danskefilm.dk
