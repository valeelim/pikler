Breckland District     breckland district is a local government district in norfolk , england . about 135,000 people live there . it was created on 1 april 1974. its council is based in east dereham . breckland district gets its name from the breckland landscape region . that is a gorse-covered sandy heath of south norfolk and north suffolk . the term `` breckland '' is ancient . it is found as early as the 13th century in the form brakelond .
