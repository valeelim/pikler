Karaikal district     karaikal district is one of the 4 districts of puducherry union territory . it is the 2nd biggest district of the union territory after the puducherry district . it has the population of more than 2lakhs . the capital is karaikal port city . the district is made of communes kottucherry , nedungadu , thirunallar , neravy and thirumalairajanpattinam . the karaikal district formed an integral part of the pallava kingdom in eighth century . poet sekkizhar describes the town as `` vanga malik kadar karaikal '' .the famous thirunallar saniswaran temple , karaikal ammaiyar temple are in this district . the rivers arasalar , veerasolan river , kodamurutti river , kaveri river 's other tributaries reach the sea here . the karaikal port is one of the biggest in south india . the karaikal airport is being constructed . it will be the first airport builted entirely with private capital .
