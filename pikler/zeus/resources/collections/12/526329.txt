Daddy's Home (movie)    daddy 's home ( movie ) daddy 's home is a 2015 american comedy movie directed and written by sean anders . it was produced by will ferrell and adam mckay . it stars ferrell , mark wahlberg , linda cardellini , thomas haden church with john cena . it was filmed in new orleans , louisiana , but the movie takes place in california . it was released to mixed reviews on december 25 , 2015 .
