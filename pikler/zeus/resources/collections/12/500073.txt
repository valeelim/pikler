When a Man Loves a Woman     `` when a man loves a woman '' is a 1966 r & b song by percy sledge . the song hit # 1 on the u.s. `` billboard '' hot 100 chart . it also was # 1 on the r & b singles chart . the song was later performed by michael bolton in 1991. his version won a grammy award . barbara mandrell recorded a country music version of this song , also in 1991 .
