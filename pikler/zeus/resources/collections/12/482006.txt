Simborgarmärket     simborgarmärket is an award given to any person who can swimg at least 200 meter . he or she herself can chose which stroke to use . the award was first awarded on 8 july 1934. it was established on the initiative of david jonason . the ambition was increase the number of people able to swim . it was originally only awarded to people aged 16 and older . girls and women originally only needed to swim 150 meters .
