Reus     reus is a city in catalonia , spain . it is the capital of baix camp , in the province of tarragona . the area has always been an important producer of wines and spirits . it was famous at one time for its brandy . today reus is a popular center for rock-climbing . as of 2014 , 104,962 people live in the city . the famous architect , antoni gaudí , was born in reus . the poet and playwright joaquín bartrina ( 1850–1880 ) was born in reus . < br >
