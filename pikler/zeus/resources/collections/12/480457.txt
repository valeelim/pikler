Lili Taylor     lili anne taylor ( born february 20 , 1967 ) is an american actress . she appeared in many independent movies . she was also in big budget movies including `` ransom '' ( 1996 ) and `` the conjuring '' ( 2013 ) . she played grace stalker in `` arizona dream '' . she played jojo barbosa in `` mystic pizza '' in 1988. taylor was born in glencoe , illinois .
