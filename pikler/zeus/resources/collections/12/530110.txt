Aleš Debeljak     aleš debeljak ( 25 december 1961 – 28 january 2016 ) was a slovenian cultural critic , poet , and essayist . he was born in ljubljana , slovenia , yugoslavia . from the mid-1980s onwards , debeljak took an active part in civil society movements . he was one of the co-editors of popular journal `` nova revija '' . debeljak was killed in a car crash on 28 january 2016. he was aged 54. other websites . - personal page ( faculty for social sciences , ljubljana ) - biography and bibliography - aleš debeljak 's `` without anesthesia '' in `` blackbird : an online journal of literature and the arts ''
