Chevrolet Trax     chevrolet trax is a subcompact cuv/mini suv ( europe ) sold by the chevrolet division by general motors . it was introduced in 2013. it replaced both the chevrolet hhr station wagon and the suzuki based tracker subcompact suv . it is a mere rebadged version of opel mokka , which is also offered in the u.s. as buick encore . it is built on gm 's gamma platform . the gamma platform is also used to built the sonic and the spark . in 2014 it replaced the chevrolet captiva sport as gm 's fleet car . the car is sold under the name tracker in russia due to the word trax loosely . translating to a sexual connotation . gm has thought of selling a gmc version of this vehicle due to sales success in the u.s .
