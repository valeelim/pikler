Bill Nye the Science Guy     bill nye the science guy was a live action educational television program . it originally aired from september 10 , 1993 to june 20 , 1998. the program was hosted by bill nye . it was produced by buena vista television . the show aired on pbs kids and was also syndicated to local stations . each of the 100 half-hour episodes aims to teach a specific topic in a natural science to a preteen audience . in 1999 the program received a public service award from the national science board `` for its masterful presentations of the sciences to the younger generation ... '' the show won a total of 19 emmy awards during the five years it was broadcast . nye won seven emmys for his performing , writing and producing the program .
