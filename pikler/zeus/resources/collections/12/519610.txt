Ján Chryzostom Korec     ján chryzostom korec , sj ( 22 january 1924 – 24 october 2015 ) was a slovakian jesuit cardinal and bishop emeritus of the diocese of nitra . he served in the church from 1990 through 2005. he was in jail for eight years ( 1960-1968 ) because of his activism against communism . korec was born in bošany , czechoslovakia . he died in nitra , slovakia at the age of 91 .
