Adriana Benetti     adriana benetti ( 12 december 1919 – 24 february 2016 ) was an italian actress . she was known for her roles in `` quattro passi fra le nuvole '' , `` c'è sempre un ma ! `` , `` uno tra la folla '' and `` avanti c'è posto ... '' . she was born in ferrara , italy . benetti died in rome on 24 february 2016 from complications of pneumonia . she was 96 years old .
