Jess Harnell     jess q. harnell ( born december 23 , 1963 ) is an american voice actor and singer . he is best known for his voice roles as wakko warner in `` animaniacs '' , ironhide in the `` transformers '' movie series , chilly in `` doc mcstuffins '' and crash bandicoot in the video game franchise of the same name . he has been the voice of br'er rabbit in various disney projects since 1989. he has been the announcer on `` america 's funniest home videos '' since 1998. other websites . - jess harnell at behind the voice actors
