Tamerlan Aguzarov     tamerlan kimovich aguzarov ( ; 14 june 1963 – 19 february 2016 ) was a russian politician . he was the head of north ossetia-alania from june 5 , 2015 until his death in february 19 , 2016. aguzarov died on 19 february 2016 in moscow from complications of pneumonia . he was aged 52. other websites . - официальный сайт правительства республики северная осетия — алания
