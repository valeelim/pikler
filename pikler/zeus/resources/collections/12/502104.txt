Ruth Rendell     ruth barbara rendell , baroness rendell of babergh ( née grasemann ; 17 february 1930 – 2 may 2015 ) , also known as barbara vine , was an english writer . she wrote mostly psychological thrillers and murder mysteries . she released at least 90 books and was best known for her character inspector wexford . rendell was born in south woodford , essex ( now london ) . her mother was born in sweden and grew up in denmark , while her father was english . she was married to don rendell until his death from prostate cancer in 1999. they had a son , simon ( born 1953 ) . rendell had a stroke in january 2015. she died on 2 may 2015 in london , aged 85 .
