Arrondissement of Langon     the arrondissement of langon is an arrondissement of france . it is part of the gironde `` département '' in the nouvelle-aquitaine region . its capital is the city of langon . when the gironde department was created on 17 february 1800 , the `` arrondissement '' of langon was part of that original department , with the name of `` arrondissement of bazas '' . when the subprefecture was moved in 1926 from bazas to langon , the name of the arrondissement was changed to `` langon '' . the `` arrondissement '' of langon is in the southeast of gironde , with an area of , the largest of the `` arrondissements '' of the department . it has 130,911 inhabitants and a population density of inhabitants/km² . the `` arrondissement '' is bordered to the north by the `` arrondissement '' of libourne , to the east by the lot-et-garonne department , to the south by the landes department , to the west by the `` arrondissements '' of arcachon and bordeaux . after the reorganisation of the cantons in france , cantons are not subdivisions of the `` arrondissements '' so they could have `` communes '' that belong to different `` arrondissements '' .
