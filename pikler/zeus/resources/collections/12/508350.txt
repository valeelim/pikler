PBS NewsHour     the pbs newshour is an american evening television news program that is broadcast seven nights a week on more than 300 pbs stations . the weekday program is one hour-long . it is co-anchored by gwen ifill and judy woodruff . they are the only two women leading a national nightly news program on broadcast currentlly . it is produced by weta-tv . it is the flagship public media station for washington , d.c.. the pbs newshour weekend is 30 minutes long . it is anchored by hari sreenivasan and produced by new york pbs member station wnet . as the nation 's first hour-long broadcast of nightly news , the pbs newshour is known for its in-depth coverage of issues and current events . < br >
