See You Again (Wiz Khalifa song)    see you again ( wiz khalifa song ) `` see you again '' is a song by american rapper wiz khalifa featuring the vocals and piano playing of american singer charlie puth . the song is featured in the 2015 action movie `` furious 7 '' . it was created as a final tribute to paul walker , who died in a car crash in november 2013. the single was released on march 17 , 2015 in the us , followed by an international release on april 3 , and a uk release on april 12 .
