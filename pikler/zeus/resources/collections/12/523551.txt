Hugh Hewitt     hugh hewitt ( born february 22 , 1956 ) is an american radio talk show host . he works with the salem radio network . he is also a lawyer , academic , and author . hewitt is a conservative and a catholic , he comments on society , politics , and media bias in the united states . hewitt is also a law professor at chapman university school of law . other websites . - hewitt 's columns at townhall.com
