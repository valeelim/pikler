Stadionul Municipal (Botoșani)    stadionul municipal ( botoșani ) the stadionul municipal is a multi-use stadium in botoşani , romania . it is usually used for football matches with fc botoșani ( since 2001 ) . the stadium holds 12,000 people . it was renovated in 2008 , 2009 and 2013. other websites . - stadiumdb profile
