Joey Haro     joey haro is an american actor best known for playing clark stevenson on the mtv teen comedy `` awkward '' . he was also a member of the warblers on `` glee '' . haro was born in miami . he went to a performing arts high school . he attended florida state university . he left there after his sophomore year to move to new york city for an acting career . in 2009 he appeared on broadway as chino in the `` west side story '' . he was part of the casts of `` hairspray '' and `` altar boyz .
