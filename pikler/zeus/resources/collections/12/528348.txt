ASAS-SN-15lh     asas-sn-15lh is a supernova detected on 14 june 2015. it is the most luminous supernova ever detected . at its brightest it was 50 times brighter than the whole milky way . the supernova is some 3.8 billion light-years from earth . it was found by the all sky automated survey for supernovae ( asas-sn ) .
