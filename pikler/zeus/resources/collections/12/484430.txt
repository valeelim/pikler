Marinilabiliaceae     `` marinilabiliaceae '' is a family of bacteria . the cells are flexible rods and thin . mostly species are able to move smoothly . some genera : - `` alkaliflexus '' - `` alkalitalea '' - `` anaerophaga '' - `` carboxylicivirga '' - `` geofilum '' - `` mangroviflexus '' - `` marinilabilia '' - `` natronoflexus '' - `` thermophagus ''
