Jerry Collins     jerry collins ( 4 november 1980 – 5 june 2015 ) was a professional new zealand rugby union player . he played for the all blacks between 2001 and 2007. at the time of his death , he was playing for rc narbonne . he also played for the hurricanes , wellington , toulon , ospreys , yamaha júbilo . collins was born in apia , samoa . he grew up in porirua , wellington . he and his partner , alana , had a daughter , ayla . collins and his partner were killed in a car accident on 5 june 2015 near béziers , hérault in southern france . he was 34. his three-month-old daughter was badly injured and was flown to a montpellier hospital . other websites . - jerry collins at allblacks.com
