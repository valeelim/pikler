Rudolf E. Kálmán     rudolf ( rudi ) emil kálmán ( ; may 19 , 1930 – july 2 , 2016 ) was a hungarian-born american electrical engineer , mathematician , and inventor . he was known for his co-invention and development of the kalman filter . it was a mathematical algorithm that is widely used in signal processing , control systems , and guidance , navigation and control . for this work , u.s. president barack obama awarded kálmán the national medal of science on october 7 , 2009 .
