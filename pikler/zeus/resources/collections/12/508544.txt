Chris     chris is a nickname or short form of various given names . these include christopher , christian , christina , christine , and christos . the nickname is used by both males and females . most names chris is based on have meanings regarding christ . for example christopher means `` bearer of christ '' . it is also a name used by individuals . chris columbus and chris penn are two well known examples . it is also a name given to tropical storms , hurricanes , trademarks such as chris-craft boats and other places and things . < br >
