High River     high river is a town in the calgary region of alberta , canada . as of 2011 , the town had a population of 12,920 people . it is 37 kilometres ( 23 miles ) south of calgary . the name of the town comes from the nearby highwood river . the town was incorporated as a village on 6 december 1901. it was incorporated as a town on 12 february 1906. high river has an elevation of 1,040 m ( 3,410 ft ) . it has an area of 14.27 km2 ( 5.51 sq mi ) . other websites . - official website
