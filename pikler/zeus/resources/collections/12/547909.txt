Terence Hill     terence hill ( born mario girotti ; 29 march 1939 ) is an italian actor . he was known for his roles in `` lo chiamavano trinità '' ( `` they call me trinity '' , 1971 ) and `` il mio nome è nessuno '' ( `` my name is nobody '' , 1973 ) , co-starring henry fonda . his movie `` django , prepare a coffin '' , shot in 1968 by director ferdinando baldi , and co-starring horst frank and george eastman , was featured at the 64th venice film festival in 2007 .
