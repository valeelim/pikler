Codex     a codex ( plural `` codices '' ) is a book-like object with writing on it . on a codex there are separate pages , whereas a scroll is one continuous long document . the term is used for book-type objects written by hand , and includes books written on vellum or parchment . the codex transformed the shape of the book itself . as an objects , books can last for centuries , and many codices are in the great modern libraries . the codex was first described by the 1st-century ad roman poet martial , who praised it . the codex was as common as scrolls by about ad 300. it had completely replaced scrolls in the greco-roman world by the 6th century . related pages . - codex huamantla - codex buranus
