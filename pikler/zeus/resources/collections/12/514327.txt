Pieter van den Hoogenband     pieter van den hoogenband was born on 14 march , 1978. he is a former dutch swimmer . he won three gold medals at the olympics . he swam at the olympics four times , from 1996 to 2008. he held the world record in the 100 meter and 200 meter freestyle . van den hoogenband is well known for his races in the 200 meter freestyle against ian thorpe from australia .
