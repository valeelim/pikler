Rhythmic Songs Chart     the rhythmic songs chart is a monitored airplay chart in billboard magazine . the chart tracks the 40 most popular singles being played on 68 monitored rhythmic radio stations in the united states . arbitron sometimes calls it rhythmic contemporary hit radio the chart was introduced on october 3 , 1992 , with `` end of the road '' by boyz ii men as the first number-one single . other websites . - current bds rhythmic top 40 chart ( updated weekly )
