Netatmo     founded in 2011 , netatmo is a french company specializing in connected objects . their four products ( weather station , thermostat and camera ) have been awarded by the `` ces innovations design and engineering awards '' in 2013 , 2014 and 2015 . in 2015 , welcome , won 4 awards in the following categories : `` smart home '' , `` home appliances '' , `` digital imaging '' and `` tech for a better world '' .
