CBS Evening News     the cbs evening news is a daily evening newscast on cbs . it is the `` flagship '' program of the cbs news division . the program began in 1948 as `` douglas edwards with the news . '' they were sponsored by general motors . in 1963 the name was changed to the `` cbs evening news '' . it is currently anchored by scott pelley . he became the anchor and managing editor of cbs evening news in 2011 .
