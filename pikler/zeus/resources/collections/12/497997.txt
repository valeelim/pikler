Filibacter limicola     filibacter limicola is a bacterium . `` filibacter limicola '' is the only species of the genus `` filibacter '' . it is gram-positive . the species is aerob : it needs oxygen . it is filamentous and composed of more than one cells . the cells are curved or straight . it is a gliding bacterium . other websites . - list of prokaryotic names with standing in nomenclature ( lpsn )
