John Kasich presidential campaign, 2016    john kasich presidential campaign , 2016 the 2016 presidential campaign of john kasich , the 69th governor of ohio , began on july 21 , 2015. kasich , the governor of ohio , announced his candidacy at the ohio state university in the ohio union . he suspended his campaign on may 4 , 2016. he won 154 delegates , coming fourth in the republican primaries . he won his home state , ohio . he was the last challenger to donald trump , and the last to drop out .
