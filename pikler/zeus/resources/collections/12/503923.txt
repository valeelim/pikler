Get Hard     get hard is an american comedy movie . alison brie plays alissa . kevin hart plays darnell . cast . - will ferrell as james king - kevin hart as darnell lewis - alison brie as alissa barrow - edwina findley as rita lewis - craig t. nelson as martin barrow - t.i . as russell - ariana neal as makayla lewis - greg germann as peter penny - paul ben-victor as gayle - dan bakkedahl as leo - erick chavarria as cecelio - jon eyez as big mike - nito larioza as jaoa - ron funches as jojo - john mayer as himself - jimmy fallon as himself on december 7 , 2012 , it was announced that warner bros. was in talks to acquire the `` get hard '' comedy movie script . on september 17 , 2013 , etan cohen was set to direct . on february 24 , 2014 , warner bros. set the release date for 27 march 2015. the movie was released on 27 march 2015 in the united states . it got negative reviews by movie critics . rotten tomatoes gave the movie 29 % . metacritic rates the movie 34 out of 100 , meaning `` generally unfavorable reviews '' .
