Jacques Rivette     jacques rivette ( ; 1 march 1928 – 29 january 2016 ) was a french movie director , screenwriter and movie critic . his best-known movies include `` celine and julie go boating '' , `` la belle noiseuse '' and the rare thirteen-hour `` out 1 '' . rivette died at his home in paris , france from complications of alzheimer 's disease on 29 january 2016 at the age of 87. other websites . - jacques-rivette.com- a website devoted to rivette 's film and journalism careers - biography on newwavefilm.com - sensesofcinema.com great directors article - jonathan rosenbaum : jacques rivette [ chapter from film : the front line 1983 ] - edition de « de l'abjection » ( 1961 ) par jacques rivette , sur le site d'analyse l'observatoire ( simple appareil ) .
