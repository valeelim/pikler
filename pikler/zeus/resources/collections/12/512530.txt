White-tailed eagle     the white-tailed eagle ( `` haliaeetus albicilla '' ) — also called the sea eagle , erne , and white-tailed sea-eagle — is a large bird of prey . it is in the family accipitridae , which includes other raptors , such as hawks and kites . it is a close cousin of the bald eagle but it lives in eurasia . the white-tailed eagle is a very large bird . it measures in length with a wingspan . the wingspan averages about 7 feet . this is the largest of any eagle .
