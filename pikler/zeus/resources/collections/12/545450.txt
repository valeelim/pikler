Bogdan Stancu     bogdan sorin stancu ( born on 28 june 1987 in pitești , romania ) is a romanian association football forward and left winger who currently plays for the turkish club gençlerbirliği and the romania national football team . his nickname is `` motanul '' ( meaning tomcat ) . he also previously played for argeș pitești , cs mioveni , unirea urziceni , steaua bucurești , galatasaray and orduspor . these goals are current since the game against switzerland . other websites . - official fcsb profile - bogdan stancu 's profile at romaniansoccer.ro - bogdan stancu 's profile uefa.com
