Joe Kittinger     joseph william kittinger ii ( born july 27 , 1928 ) is a retired colonel in the united states air force and a usaf command pilot . he was born in tampa , florida . he set a world record for the longest skydive from a height more than . he was also the first man to cross the atlantic ocean , alone , in a hot air balloon . he was a fighter pilot in the vietnam war and was a prisoner of war in a north vietnamese prison .
