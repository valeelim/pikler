Härnösands AIK     härnösands aik is a sports club in the town of härnösand in sweden . it was established in 1945 and originally thought to be called bk virgo . however the club was required to change name because the other name was already in use . the club plays bandy . earlier the club also ran soccer , track and field athletics , skiing and table tennis . the men 's bandy team played in the swedish top division during the season of 1984-1985. the women 's bandy team has played several seasons in the swedish top division . other websites . - official website
