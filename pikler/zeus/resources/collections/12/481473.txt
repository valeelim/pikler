Lou Dobbs     louis carl `` lou '' dobbs ( born september 24 , 1945 ) is a television journalist , business writer and news anchor . he is the host of the program `` lou dobbs tonight '' on the fox business network . before fox , dobbs was an anchor , managing editor , an executive vice president at cnn . dobbs is an award-winning journalist . he has won an emmy , a peabody award and a luminary award ( from the business journalism review ) .
