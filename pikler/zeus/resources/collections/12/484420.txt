Maggie Roswell     maggie roswell ( born november 14 , 1952 ) is an american actress . she got both an annie award and an emmy award . she voiced the characters maude flanders , miss hoover and helen lovejoy in the animated series `` the simpsons '' . she got her acting break during the 1980s in movies including `` lost in america '' and `` pretty in pink '' . the actress was born in los angeles , california .
