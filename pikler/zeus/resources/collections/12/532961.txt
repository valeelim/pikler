Toyota Avalon     the 'toyota avalon is a car made by toyota . it is a full-size car with four doors . as of 2013 , the toyota avalon is available in the united states , canada , puerto rico , south korea , and the middle east . it is named after avalon , a legendary island from celtic legends . the car was first introduced as a 1995 model . it has sold over 1 million copies since it was introduced .
