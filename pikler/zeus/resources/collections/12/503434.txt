Garden fork     a garden fork , also called a spading fork or digging fork , is a tool for digging . it is used for loosening soil in gardening and farming . it is used similarly to a spade , but it can be pushed more easily into the ground . it does not cut through plant roots . garden forks were made of wood , but now most are made of steel . garden forks are slightly different from pitchforks . their tines are usually shorter and thicker and closer together .
