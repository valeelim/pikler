Thermophile     a thermophile is an organism – a type of extremophile – that thrives at relatively high temperatures . that means between about . many thermophiles are archaea . thermophilic eubacteria may have been among the earliest bacteria . thermophiles are found in various geothermally heated parts of the earth 's surface . examples are hot springs like those in yellowstone national park ( see image ) and deep sea hydrothermal vents . also decaying plant matter , such as peat bogs and compost , makes higher temperatures .
