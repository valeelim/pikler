Love?    love ? love ? is the seventh studio album by jennifer lopez . it was released on april 29 , 2011 by island records . it was the only album that lopez released on that label . this was lopez ' third consecutive album to have all singles reach number one on the `` billboard '' hot dance club songs . that made eleven number ones for her . lopez also worked with many entertainers including pitbull , lil wayne , lady gaga , and the-dream . the album 's first single was `` on the floor '' . pitbull sings on it . the song reached number three on the `` billboard '' hot 100. it reached number one on more than eighteen international charts . personnel . - performance - david angell on `` starting over '' - monisa angell – viola on `` starting over '' - anesha birchett – background vocals on `` ( what is ) love ? `` , `` run the world '' and `` one love '' - john catchings – cello on `` starting over '' - tami chynn – additional vocals on `` hypnotico '' - david davidson – violin - alli davis – background vocals on `` good hit '' - lauren evans – background vocals on `` run the world '' and `` villain ''
