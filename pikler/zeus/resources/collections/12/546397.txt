FC Viitorul Constanța     fotbal club viitorul constanța is a professional association football club from ovidiu , constanța county , romania . they currently play in the liga i. the club was founded in the summer of 2009 and began to play in the liga iii . it was founded by famous romanian footballer gheorghe hagi . the club currently possesses the top youth facilities of any club in the country . they won the liga iii in the 2009-10 season . also , their team colours are blue , white and black . liga iii - winners ( 1 ) : 2009-10
