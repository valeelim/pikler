Bard's Oath    bard 's oath the bard 's oath is the third book in the dragonlords series written by joanne bertin . it starts as two stories that merge in the middle of the book . one story is about raven redhawkson and why he is at the balyaranna fair ( a horse festival ) . the other story is about bard leet and his plot for revenge over the death of a family member .
