Virisila Buadromo     virisila buadromo is a human rights activist from fiji . in 2008 she received the international women of courage award . in 2001 she became the executive director of the fiji women ’ s rights movement . this organization helps women by changing laws and policies . after the fiji government changed in december 2006 , buadromo had difficulty with the new government . buadromo made the program larger by starting the emerging leaders forum , a program for young women . the emerging leaders forum is a one-year program . it works with fiji ’ s family law . buadromo is also chairperson of the fiji ngo coalition on human rights . awards . - women have wings courage award , oxfam , 2012 . - international women of courage award , u.s state department , 2008 .
