Chiune Sugihara     sugihara was born in yaotsu , gifu . he was first married to klaudia semionovna apollonova until their divorce in 1935. he was later married to yukiko sugihara . they had four sons . sugihara died on 31 july 1986 at a hospital in kamakura . he was 86. other websites . - chiune sugihara at the jewish virtual library
