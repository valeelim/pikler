Adi Parashakti     in hindu mythology , adi para shakti is the supreme being . she is the goddess , devi and recognized as para brahman ( the highest brahman ) . in many shakta texts she is known as `` parma '' meaning supreme , and `` satya '' meaning truth . the devi bhagawata mahapurana says that adi parashakti is the original creator , observer and destroyer of the whole universe .
