Joseph Mercieca     joseph mercieca ( , 11 november 1928 – 21 march 2016 ) was a maltese prelate . he served as archbishop of malta from 1976 to 2006. he was born in gozo , malta . he was known for restoring stability in the maltese church following his predecessor mikiel gonzi 's dispute with the malta labour party . mercieca died in his home in żejtun , malta after a long-illness on 21 march 2016. he was aged 87 .
