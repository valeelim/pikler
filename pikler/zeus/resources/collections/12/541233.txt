Isao Tomita     , often known simply as tomita , was a japanese music composer . he was born in tokyo . he is called as one of the pioneers of electronic music and space music . he is one of the most famous producers of analog synthesizer arrangements . many of his albums are electronic versions and adaptations of famous classical music pieces and he received four grammy award nominations for his 1974 album `` snowflakes are dancing '' . isao died of heart failure in tokyo on 5 may 2016 , aged 84 .
