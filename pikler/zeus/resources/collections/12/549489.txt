STG-58     the stg-58 , is a austrian battle rifle that was based on the famous belgian fal which was adopted in early and mid 1950s . the stg-58 was used by the austrian army from 1958 until 1977. the stg-58 stands for sturmgewehr 1958 which the 1958 is the year when austrians adopted the rifle . the stg-58 shoots a 7.62 nato that is more powerful than the aug that shoots a 5.56 nato . in 1978 the austrian army replaced the stg-58 with the aug .
