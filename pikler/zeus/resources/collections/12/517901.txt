Chop suey     chop suey is a dish in american-chinese cuisine . it is made with meat , often chicken , fish , pork or beef . there are also eggs and vegetables in it . it is usually served with rice or noodles . there are many myths about chop suey . one is that in the 19th century it was invented by chinese-american cooks working for the transcontinental railroad . chop suey appears in an 1884 article of the `` brooklyn eagle '' by wong chin foo .
