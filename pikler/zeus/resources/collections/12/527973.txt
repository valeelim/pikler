Reginaldo Araújo     antónio reginaldo matias de araújo ( 2 october 1977 – 11 january 2016 ) was a brazilian footballer . he played as a defender . he played for coritiba , são paulo , santos , flamengo , santa cruz and criciúma . he was born in presidente prudente , são paulo . araújo died from a heart attack on 11 january 2016 in cornélio procópio , paraná . he was 38 .
