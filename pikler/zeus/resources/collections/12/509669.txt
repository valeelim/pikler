Dik-dik     a dik-dik is a small antelope in the genus madoqua that lives in the bushlands of eastern and southern africa . dik-diks stand about 30–40 cm ( 12–16 in ) at the shoulder , are 50–70 cm ( 20–28 in ) long , weigh 3–6 kg ( 7–16 lb ) and can live for up to 10 years . dik-diks are named after the alarm calls of the females . in addition to the females ' alarm call , both males and females make a shrill , whistling sound . these calls may alert other animals to predators . the dik-dik is a favourite prey of the cheetah . there are four species : - günther 's dik-dik `` madoqua guentheri '' - kirk 's dik-dik `` madoqua kirkii '' - silver dik-dik `` madoqua piacentinii '' - salt 's dik-dik `` madoqua saltiana ''
