James Douglas (actor)    james douglas ( actor ) james douglas ( may 20 , 1929 – march 5 , 2016 ) was an american actor . he best known for his role as grant colman on `` as the world turns '' ( 1974–81 , with return appearances in 1986 and 1989 ) . douglas was born in los angeles , california . he died on march 5 , 2016 at the age of 86. his first major role came in 1964 when he joined the cast of the primetime soap `` peyton place '' . he played the role of steven cord . he stayed with the series until it left the air in 1969. he then turned mainly to daytime roles , appearing as elliott carrington on `` another world '' from 1972 to 1974. he then moved to `` as the world turns '' . he also appeared on `` the edge of night '' in 1984 and on `` one life to live '' as dr. marcus polk ( 1985–87 ) . movies . - `` g.i . blues '' ( 1960 ) as rick - `` sweet bird of youth '' ( 1962 ) as leroy
