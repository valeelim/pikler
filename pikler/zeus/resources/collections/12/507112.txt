Timofey Mozgov     timofey pavlovich mozgov ( born july 16 , 1986 ) is a russian professional basketball player . he currently plays center for the cleveland cavaliers of the national basketball association ( nba ) . mozgov is from st. petersburg , russia . he is 7 foot 1 inches tall . mozgov also played for the new york knicks and the denver nuggets . mozgov played in the 2012 london olympics . his team won the bronze medal . he first came into the nba on october 27 , 2010. his jersey number is 20. mozgov also played for khimki moscow region . on june 4 , 2015 , he became the first russian to play in the nba finals . < br >
