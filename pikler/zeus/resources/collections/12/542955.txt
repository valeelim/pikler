Ashure     ashure ( ) is a traditional turkish pudding . ashure is made from grains , dried fruits and nuts . there are no animal products ( butter , milk , cheese , etc . ) and ashure is vegan . specially , turkish people make ashure during muharram , the first month of the islamic calendar . ashure ( ) means tenth in arabic and the 10th day of every lunar year is the day of ashure . related pages . - day of ashure
