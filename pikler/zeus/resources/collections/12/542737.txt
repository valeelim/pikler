President of Nigeria     the president of nigeria is the head of state and head of the executive of nigeria . the first president of nigeria was nnamdi azikiwe . the current president is muhammadu buhari , since 29 may 2015. the 1999 constitution of nigeria of nigeria sets the requirement . a president must : - be a citizen of nigeria ; - be at least 40 years old ; - be a member of a political party and is sponsored by that political party . these powers include : - appointing ambassadors and diplomatic and consular representatives - approving and signing of bills - conferring honours - receiving and recognising foreign diplomatic and consular representatives - referring a bill to the constitutional court for a decision on the bill 's constitutionality - summoning the national assembly , or parliament to an extraordinary sitting to conduct special business other websites . - state house abuja official website
