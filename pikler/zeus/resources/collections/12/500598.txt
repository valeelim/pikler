State Archive of the Russian Federation     the state archive of the russian federation ( commonly known as garf ) is a major national archive of russia . it was established in moscow in 1992. it acquired collections of the central state archive of the october revolution . it also houses the central state archive of the russian soviet federative socialist republic . the archives contain collections covering the period from 1800 to 1993. other websites . - official website - page about garf at the dutch international institute of social history
