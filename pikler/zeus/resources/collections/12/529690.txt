Mexican Cession     the mexican cession of 1848 refers to the lands ceded or surrendered to the united states by mexico at the end of the mexican–american war . the territory became the states of california , nevada , utah and arizona . it was agreed to in the treaty of guadalupe hidalgo , which formally ended the war ( 1846–1848 ) between mexico and the united states . the treaty was signed on 2 february 1848. the cession added of territory to the southwestern united states . it also contributed to the united states emerging as a world power in the late the 19th century .
