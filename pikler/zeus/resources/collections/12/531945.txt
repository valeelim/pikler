Latrobe, Pennsylvania    latrobe , pennsylvania latrobe is a city in westmoreland county , pennsylvania in the united states and part of the pittsburgh metropolitan area . the city population was 8,338 as of the 2010 census ( 9,265 in 1990 ) . latrobe was incorporated as a borough in 1854 , and as a city in 1999. the current mayor is rosemarie m. wolford . other websites . - city of latrobe website - greater latrobe community network
