José Ángel Espinoza    josé ángel espinoza josé ángel espinoza aragón ( october 2 , 1919 – november 6 , 2015 ) , also known as ferrusquilla , was a mexican singer-songwriter and movie actor . he was the father of actress angélica aragón . he also was a composer working with the sacm ( society of authors and composers of mexico ) he was known for his roles in `` house of evil '' and in `` two mules for sister sara '' . he was born in mazatlán , sinaloa . espinoza died in mexico city , mexico from pneumonia , aged 96 .
