BMT Broadway Line     the bmt broadway line is a line of the new york city subway . there are four services , and about 748,900 people ride on them every day . services using this line are colored sunflower yellow . services . - n : this is the broadway express , operating from ditmars blvd to coney island . - q : this is the broadway express , which currently operates from 57th street-7th avenue to coney island-stillwell avenue at all times . - r : this is a local service , operating from 71st avenue to 95th street ( but only from whitehall street-south ferry at late nights ) . - w : this train is a local service , operating from astoria-ditmars boulevard in queens to whitehall street-south ferry in manhattan . the w runs on weekdays from 6:00 a.m to 11:30 p.m
