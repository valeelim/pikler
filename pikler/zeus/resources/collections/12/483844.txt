Burlington Coat Factory     burlington coat factory is a coat factory/store/warehouse established in 1924. it was called `` burlington coat/fine clothing factory warehouse for the entire family '' in 1924-1984 and `` the original burlington coat factory '' in 1984-2011. it is an american national off price retailer , with 523 stores in 44 states and puerto rico , with its corporate headquarters in burlington township , new jersey .
