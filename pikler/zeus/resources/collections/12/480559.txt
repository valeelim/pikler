Caracara     caracara are birds of prey in the family falconidae . they live in south and central america , and the southern usa . mostly they are not fast fliers , and are scavengers . one species , the red-throated caracara , eats insects and the larvae of bees and wasps . their classification is somewhat unclear , but some sources say there are five living genera and 11 species . they were all originally put within the genus `` polyborus '' . extinct species have been found in the la brea tar pits . - genus `` daptrius '' - genus `` ibycter '' - genus `` phalcoboenus '' - genus `` caracara '' - genus `` milvago ''
