Gay bar     gay bars are drinking establishments that cater to lesbian , gay , bisexual or transgender people . the term `` gay '' is used as an inclusive concept for lgbt communities . gay bars once served as the centre of gay culture . they were one of the few places people with same-sex orientations and gender-variant identities could openly socialize . other names include `` boy bar '' , `` girl bar '' , `` lesbian bar '' or `` dyke bar '' . the stonewall inn in new york city is one example of a gay/lesbian bar . the gay rights movement was born there .
