Tom Cotton     tom cotton ( born may 13 , 1977 ) is the junior united states senator from arkansas . he is a member of the u.s. republican party . cotton has been in senate since january 3 , 2015. he was in the united states house of representatives from 2013 to 2015. cotton was born on may 13 , 1977 in dardanelle , arkansas . he graduated from the harvard university . he is married to jaime gardner . they have one child . in january 2016 , cotton became the first sitting united states senator to support bernie sanders for president of the united states . other websites . - tom cotton , u.s. senator for arkansas , home page
