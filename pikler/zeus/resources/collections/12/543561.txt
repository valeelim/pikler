Zdeněk Mézl     zdeněk mézl ( 31 october 1934 – 23 may 2016 ) was an czech print-maker and writer . he was known for his illustrations . he was born in prague . he studied at the college of applied arts between 1949–1953 and later graduated from the academy of fine arts , prague . mézl has illustrated some books using traditional woodcut engraving . his published books are `` a battle for cathedral '' or `` hey , slavs ! '' . mézl died on 23 may 2016 in prague from a heart attack , aged 81 .
