María Luisa Alcalá     maría luisa alcalá ( march 26 , 1943 – february 21 , 2016 ) , also known as ma . luisa alcalá , was a mexican actress . she was known for her roles as socorrito in `` esmeralda '' , filomena in `` la usurpadora '' and malicha in `` el chavo del ocho '' . alcalá was born in mexico city . alcalá died in her sleep at her home in mexico city from a heart attack on february 21 , 2016. she was aged 72 .
