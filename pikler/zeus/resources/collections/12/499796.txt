Borderlands 2     borderlands 2 is a first-person shooter video game that was developed by gearbox software and published by 2k games . it is the sequel to the 2009 video game borderlands . the game follows the story of `` vault hunters '' on the planet pandora , as one of four classes : `` gunzerker '' , `` assassin '' , `` siren '' or `` commando '' . downloadable content was also released for the game , later being included in a game of the year edition . a version for the playstation 4 and xbox one was released with . as of march 25 , 2015 , the game has sold 12 million copies . a sequel , called was released on october 14 , 2014 .
