Sjöstads IF     sjöstads if is a floorball club in the town of karlstad in sweden . it was established in 1977 as a soccer club . in 1984 the floorball section was started . the soccer section was later disestablished . during the 1990 's , both the men 's and the women 's teams played in the swedish top division . the women 's team even won the 1993-1994 swedish national championship . from the 2001-2002 season , the club became inactive . instead , the club played floorball together with ibf nb 87 under the name karlstads ibf . from the 2005-2006 , sjöstads if as a club returned . other websites . - men 's floorball team - women 's floorball team
