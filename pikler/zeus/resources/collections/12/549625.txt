Sex hormone     sex hormones , also called sex steroids , are steroid hormones that interact with androgen or estrogen receptors . the non-steroid luteinizing hormone , follicle-stimulating hormone and gonadotropin-releasing hormone are usually not regarded as sex hormones , although they play major sex-related roles . natural sex steroids are made by the gonads ( ovaries or testes ) , by adrenal glands , or by conversion from other sex steroids in tissues such as liver or fat .
