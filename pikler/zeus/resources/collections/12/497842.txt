Jørgen Ingmann     jørgen ingmann ( 26 april 1925 – 21 march 2015 ) was a danish musician from copenhagen . he worked as a member of the duet , grethe og jørgen ingmann , together with his wife grethe ingmann . after winning the dansk melodi grand prix competition in 1963 , they went on to represent denmark at the eurovision song contest 1963 where they won with the song `` dansevise '' ( dance ballad ) , music by otto francker and lyrics by sejr volmer-sørensen . he and grethe met in 1955 , married in 1956 , and divorced in 1975. jørgen ingmann died on 21 march 2015 , aged 89 .
