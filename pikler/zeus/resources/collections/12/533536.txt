Gianni Infantino     gianni infantino ( ; born 23 march 1970 ) is a swiss-italian football administrator . he has been the president of fifa since 26 february 2016. he was the general secretary of uefa from 2009 through 2016. on 26 october 2015 , infantino received the full support of the uefa executive committee to stand for the position of president in fifa 's upcoming extraordinary congress . on the same day , he confirmed his candidacy . he was elected president at the congress on 26 february 2016. the congress was held with the purpose of reforming fifa .
