Martin Nag     martin nag ( 30 july 1926 – 2 may 2015 ) was a norwegian literary historian , literary critic , poet , essayist , translator and biographer . he was born in stavanger . nag was known for his books `` uro , rød lørdag '' ( 1976 ) and `` steingjerde '' ( 1991 ) . nag died of a severe stroke in oslo at the age of 88 .
