Saulius Sondeckis     saulius sondeckis ( 11 october 1928 – 3 february 2016 ) was a lithuanian violinist , conductor , orchestra leader and professor . he founded the lithuanian chamber orchestra in 1960 and was its artistic director and principal conductor until 2004. sondeckis was born in šiauliai , lithuania . he studied at lithuanian academy of music and theatre . sondeckis died in vilnius from a stroke on 3 february 2016. he was aged 87 .
