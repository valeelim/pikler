Ellen Hoog     ellen hoog ( born 26 march 1986 in bloemendaal , north holland , netherlands ) is a dutch field hockey midfielder . she currently plays for the amsterdamsche hockey & bandy club and the netherlands women 's national field hockey team . she first played for the netherlands in 2004. she has since played in 124 matches and has scored 32 goals . hoog is sponsored by ritual hockey . along with teammate eva de goede , she modeled with `` sports illustrated '' in 2013. hoog played for the netherlands field hockey team in the 2008 beijing and 2012 london olympics . the netherlands team won the gold medal for each year . other websites . - ritual hockey
