Marion Maréchal-Le Pen     marion maréchal-le pen ( born 10 december 1989 ) is a french politician . she is the granddaughter of jean-marie le pen and the niece of marine le pen . she is a member of front national and an mp for vaucluse 's 3rd constituency since 2012. twenty-two years old at the time of her election , she became france 's youngest mp in modern political history .
