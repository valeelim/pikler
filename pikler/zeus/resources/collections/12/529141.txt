Larry Sanders (politician)    larry sanders ( politician ) lawrence `` larry '' sanders ( born april 25 , 1935 ) is an american-born british academic and politician . he is a member of the green party . sanders was born in brooklyn , new york . he is the brother of united states senator and presidential candidate bernie sanders . sanders moved to the united kingdom in 1968. he was active in the labour party in oxford in the 1980s . he split labour party in 2001 because he felt that it had moved too far to the right under tony blair , and joined the green party . sanders was a green party county councillor representing the east oxford division on oxfordshire county council , until he retired from the council in 2013. he is currently running for to be the member of parliament for witney as the candidate for the green party .
