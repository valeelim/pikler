Catadioptric sensor     a catadioptric sensor is a visual sensor that contains mirrors and lenses . video cameras are limited as to how much they can see in one view . they can lose sight of what they are recording if something causes them to move suddenly . mirrors can be carefully arranged with lenses to allow the camera lens to see a much wider field of view . they are often used in robotics . one example is a catadioptric sensor used to inspect the inside of a cylindrical pipe .
