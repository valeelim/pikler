Ramón Castro Jijón     admiral ramón castro jijón ( 15 november 1915 – 1 november 1984 ) was an ecuadorian politician . he was the president of ecuador from 11 july 1963 to 29 march 1966. he was appointed commander of the ecuadorian navy by carlos julio arosemena monroy . he later overthrew arosemena in a military coup . for his entire term as president he was chairman of a military junta . other websites . - official website of the ecuadorian government about the country president 's history
