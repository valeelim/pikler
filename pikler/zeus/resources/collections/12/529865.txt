Sick leave     sick leave ( or paid sick days or sick pay ) is time off from work that workers can use to stay home because they are sick and they do not loose pay . paid sick leave is a requirement in many nations around the world . most european , many latin american , a few african and a few asian countries have legal requirements for paid sick leave . already in 1500 bce , at least some of the workers who built the tombs of egyptian pharaohs received paid sick leave as well as state-supported health care .
