Lauren Collins     lauren felice collins ( born august 29 , 1986 ) is a canadian actress . she acts in both movies and television . she started acting in 1998. between 2001 and 2008 , she played paige michalchuk on `` '' . she played a host in `` 1 girl 5 gays '' . in 2002 , she won a young artist award for her performance in `` degrassi : the next generation '' . collins was born in the thornhill area of the greater toronto area , ontario .
