April Winchell     april winchell foley ( born january 4 , 1960 ) is an american voice actress , writer , talk radio host , commentator and comedian . she is best known for voicing clarabelle cow in `` mickey mouse clubhouse '' and various other disney projects . she is the daughter of comedian and ventriloquist , paul winchell . she was married mick kuisel until they divorced . she has been married to john foley since 2013 .
