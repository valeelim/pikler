Tomás Bulat     tomás ariel bulat ( 12 september 1964 – 31 january 2015 ) was an argentine economist , journalist , writer , and professor . he was known for his report on channel a24 . he wrote some books about the economy , some of those books are `` la economía de tu vida '' and `` la economía descubierta '' . bulat was born in buenos aires . bulat died in buenos aires , argentina after being killed in a car crash , aged 50 .
