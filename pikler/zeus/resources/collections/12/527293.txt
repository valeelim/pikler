National Archive Department of Azerbaijan     the national archive department of the republic azerbaijan ( ) is the national archive of azerbaijan . it is a governmental agency within the government of azerbaijan in charge of managing , maintaining , protecting and updating national archives of the azerbaijani nation state . the archive houses documents , maps , stamps , audio tapes , video clips , photographs and special publications . it was established on december 2 , 2002. it is in the capital city baku . related pages . - archive - preservation
