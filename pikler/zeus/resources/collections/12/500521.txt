Ontario Highway 24     ontario highway 24 is a provincial highway in ontario , canada . it runs from south to north . it starts at highway 3 in norfolk county and ends in collingwood , near the southern part of cambridge . there are many accidents on the part of the highway between brantford and collingwood , where highway 403 connects with highway 24. this area can become extremely busy at times . this problem has become so bad that the ministry of transportation of ontario is trying to decide how to improve highway 24 at this part .
