Peter Kelamis     peter kelamis ( born december 11 , 1967 ) is an australian-canadian actor , voice actor and comedian . he was born in sydney and raised in vancouver . he is best known for playing dr. adam brody in `` stargate universe '' , goku in the ocean group english dub of the animated series `` dragon ball z '' and rolf from `` ed , edd n eddy '' . kelamis has been married to canadian actress alannah stewartt since 2004 .
