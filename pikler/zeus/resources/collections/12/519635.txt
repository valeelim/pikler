Walk with Me (Jamelia album)    walk with me ( jamelia album ) walk with me is the third studio album by british singer-songwriter jamelia . it was released by parlophone records on 25 september 2006 in the uk and europe . it was released on 17 march 2007 in australia . track listing . - sample credits - `` no more '' contains a sample of `` golden brown '' by the stranglers . - `` ai n't a love '' contains a drum sample from `` jungle jazz '' by kool & the gang . - notes - australian edition of the album does not feature the track `` get up , get out '' .
