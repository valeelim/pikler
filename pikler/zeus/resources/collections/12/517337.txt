The Soul Sessions     the soul sessions is the debut studio album by english singer-songwriter joss stone . it was released on 16 september 2003 by s-curve records and emi/virgin records . when `` the soul sessions '' was released on 16 september 2003. at the time stone was just 16 years old . the album took four days to record . the songs on the album are cover versions of classic soul songs.. `` the soul sessions '' received positive reviews from music critics .
