Marvin Panch     marvin panch ( may 28 , 1926 – december 31 , 2015 ) was a american stock car racing driver . panch won the 1961 daytona 500. he also won seventeen nascar grand national - now sprint cup series - events during a 17-year career . he was born in menomonie , wisconsin . panch died of natural causes after he was found in his car in port orange , florida at the age of 89. other websites . - réplica del histórico auto
