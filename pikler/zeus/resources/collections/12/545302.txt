Vasili Berezutski     vasili vladimirovich berezutski ( russiann : василий владимирович березуцкий ; born on born 20 june 1982 in moscow , russian sfsr , soviet union ) is a russian association football defender who currently plays for the russian club pfc cska moscow and the russia national football team . he played for torpedo-zil from 1999 to 2001 as well . he sometimes plays as a defensive winger or midfielder . he has an identical twin who also plays for cska moscow , aleksei . other websites . - vasili berezutski at cska moscow official website - vasili berezutski profile on soccerway
