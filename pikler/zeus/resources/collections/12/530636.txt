Word of mouth     word of mouth is the passing of information from a person to one or more people in conversation . in the past , it only referred to speech , but now , it can sometimes include online communication . it can be as simple as telling someone what time it is , or it can involve longer stories and news . it is a good way to share information about things that are of interest to a local community , such as businesses , other people , and things that happened in the past . oral tradition ( also called `` oral culture '' or `` oral lore '' ) is when cultural material is transmitted orally from older people to younger people . this material may include folktales , sayings , songs , or chants . it is a way for things like history , literature , and law to be passed down from generation to generation without a writing system . oral history is history that is given by people who lived through historical eras or events . often , they are interviewed , and it is recorded . usually , they talk about things that are not part of written history , such as what everyday life was like .
