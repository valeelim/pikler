Arizona tree frog     these species of tree frogs in the hylidae family are commonly referred to as the arizona tree frog : - canyon tree frog , `` hyla arenicolor '' - mountain tree frog , `` hyla eximia '' is listed as the official arizona state amphibian , but it does not occur in arizona . - arizona tree frog , `` hyla wrightorum '' , was previously a synonym of `` hyla eximia '' .
