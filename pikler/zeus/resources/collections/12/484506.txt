Ana María Giunta     ana maría giunta ( 1 march 1943 – 14 march 2015 ) was an argentine actress . she was known for her roles in `` s.o.s gulubú '' ( 1986 ) , `` a king and his movie '' ( 1986 ) , and in `` eversmile , new jersey '' ( 1989 ) . giunta was born in concepción del uruguay , argentina . giunta died in buenos aires , argentina from respiratory failure , aged 72 .
