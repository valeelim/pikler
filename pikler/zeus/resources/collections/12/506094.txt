Iceland women's national football team    iceland women 's national football team the iceland women 's national football team is a women 's association football team that represents iceland . although the team has not qualified for a fifa women 's world cup , it has been at two uefa women 's championship tournaments . as of 8 june 2015 , the top scorer is margrét lára viðarsdóttir with 70 goals , and the woman with the most appearances is katrín jónsdóttir with 132 caps . official website
