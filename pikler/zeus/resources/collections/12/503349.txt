Comminges     the comminges is a natural region in southern france , near the pyrenees . it contains the upper valley of the garonne , one of france 's four great rivers . it is one of the oldest inhabited places in france . before the french revolution it was a county of the historical region of gascony . it was named for the ancient tribe the `` convenae '' . they were a celtic tribe living in and around saint-bertrand-de-comminges by at least the 1st century bc .
