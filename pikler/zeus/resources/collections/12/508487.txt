Trap music     trap music is a subgenre that originated during the 1990s from reggeaton in puerto rico . it is typified by its ominous lyrics and sound that incorporate double or triple-time division hi-hats , heavy kick drums from a roland tr-808 synthesizer , layered synthesizers , and `` cinematic '' strings . the term `` trap '' initially referred to places where drug deals take place . in recent years it has been incorporated with electronic dance music ( edm ) by artists who have remixed and made trap songs with more edm-like aspects .
