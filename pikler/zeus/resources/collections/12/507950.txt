Skate punk     skate punk is a music genre related to punk rock music . it began on the west coast . it is a popular music with skateboarders and their culture . skate punk means the sound of melodic hardcore bands from the 1990s with an aggressive sound , and similar sounding modern bands . many bands classified as skate punk also fit into genres such as pop punk , melodic hardcore , thrashcore , metalcore , surf punk and crossover thrash . skate videos use this aggressive style of punk rock for their background music . this played a big part in the coining of the term `` skate punk '' . skate punk is now popular in other parts of the world , including a large punk group in oxnard , california .
