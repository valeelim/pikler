Yahrzeit     yahrzeit ( yiddish , from german , `` yahr , '' year , plus `` zeit , '' time ) in judaism is the yearly observance of the death of a close family member . there are a number of common customs for jews observing a yahrzeit . these include remembering the relative , lighting memorial candles , saying special prayers , giving money to charity , and visiting the grave of the relative . the date of the yahrzeit is fixed by the hebrew calendar . other websites . - ( may not be simple english ) yahrzeit full article
