Gabi Calleja     gabi calleja is a gay rights activist in malta . in 2012 , she received the international women of courage award from the u.s. state department . calleja studied youth and community studies in a master 's degree program at the university of malta . in 2010 , calleja became head of the malta gay rights movement . in 2014 , she became co-chair of the executive board of ilga europe , a lesbian and gay group that works for human rights . in 2014 she worked for changes in danish law . calleja also works with teaching , drug education , community development , and fundraising . in 2005 , she helped write the “ school attendance improvement ” report . other websites . - 2012 international women of courage awards , u.s. state department photostream on flickr - secretary 's international women of courage award ( u.s. department of state )
