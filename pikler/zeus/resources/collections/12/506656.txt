Georg Ritter     georg ritter ( december 22 , 1897 in dachau - october 17 , 1941 in jastrebovka ) was a german major in the wehrmacht during world war ii . he was killed in action on october 17 , 1941 in jastrebovka , soviet union . after his death , he was awarded the knight 's cross of the iron cross on november 15 , 1941. awards . - ritterkreuz des eisernen kreuzes on november 15th , 1941 as `` major '' and `` kommandeur '' of the ii . bataillon , infanterie-regiment 62 , 7. infanterie-division , heer ( posthumously ) - eisernes kreuz 2. klasse ( 1914 ) ? - dienstauszeichnungen der nsdap ? - ehrenkreuz für frontkämpfer references . - fellgiebel , w.p . `` elite of the third reich , the recipients of the knight 's cross of the iron cross 1939-1945 : a reference '' . helion & company limited , solihull , 2003 , isbn 1-874622-46-9
