Lugo (province)    lugo ( province ) lugo is a province of northwestern spain , in the northeastern part of the autonomous community of galicia . it is bordered by the provinces of ourense , pontevedra , and a coruña , the principality of asturias , león , and in the north by the cantabrian sea ( bay of biscay ) . the population is 356,595 ( 2006 ) , of whom a quarter live in the capital lugo . the capital city was an ancient celtic settlement named in honour of the god lugh ( see lyon ) , later latinised as `` lucus augusti '' , and which became one of the three main important galician-roman centres alongside braccara augusta and asturica augusta ( modern braga and astorga respectively ) . the province has 67 municipalities .
