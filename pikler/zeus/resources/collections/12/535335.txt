Davy Walsh     david joseph walsh ( 28 april 1923 – 14 march 2016 ) , commonly referred to as davy walsh or dave walsh , was an irish footballer . he was born in waterford , ireland . he played as a centre forward for , among others , linfield , west bromwich albion and aston villa . walsh was a dual international . he played for both ireland teams – the fai xi and the ifa xi . in 1949 , he was a member of the fai xi that defeated england 2–0 at goodison park , becoming the first team to beat england at home . walsh died on 14 march 2016 from pneumonia in dublin , ireland . he was aged 92. other websites . - northern ireland ’ s footballing greats
