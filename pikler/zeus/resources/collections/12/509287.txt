Arrondissement of Carcassonne     the arrondissement of carcassonne is an arrondissement of france in the occitanie region . it is part of the aude `` département '' . its capital , and prefecture of the department , is the city of carcassonne . when the aude department was created on 17 february 1800 , the `` arrondissement '' of carcassonne was part of that original department . it is the largest in area of the `` arrondissements '' of the department , with an area of , and the one with more people living in it with 161,442 inhabitants and a density of inhabitants/km² . the `` arrondissement '' of carcassonne covers the northwestern part of the aude department ; it is surrounded by departments of the occitanie region and is bordered to the north by the tarn department , to the northeast by the hérault department , to the east by the `` arrondissement '' of narbonne , to the south by the pyrénées-orientales department , to the southwest by the `` arrondissement '' of limoux , to the west by the ariège department and to the northwest by the haute-garonne department .
