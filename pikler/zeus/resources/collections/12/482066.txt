Mastiff     the mastiff , also called the english mastiff , is a large breed of dog . they have a rectangular body , thick muscles and a massive head with a wrinkled forehead . they stand from to at the shoulder and typically weigh between and . the mastiff is one of the largest breeds of dogs who can outweigh many full-grown men . they can be gentle with family members but are also excellent guard dogs . the early mastiff was called a molosser or molossus . genetics and breeding dogs to get certain characteristics was unknown then . in 350 bc , aristotle wrote that the ancestor of the mastiff was the molosser . these were the war dogs and guard dogs kept by the molossoi people , an ancient greek tribe . today the two names are often used to mean the same group or family of dogs . for example , the modern st. bernard is sometimes described as a mastiff and sometimes as a molosser type . while the mastiff is a recognized breed , there are also a number of working dog varieties that are closely related to the mastiff . many prefer to class them as molosser breeds so as not to confuse them with the mastiff dog breed . currently there are about 14 different breeds in the molosser/mastiff family of dogs that are bred from or have a common ancestor with the mastiff . these include the :
