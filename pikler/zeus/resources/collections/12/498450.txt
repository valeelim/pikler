Mi corazón es tuyo     mi corazón es tuyo ( english : `` my heart is yours '' ) is a mexican telenovela . it is produced by juan osorio for televisa . it airs weeknights on el canal de las estrellas . the telenovela is an adaptation of the spanish telenovela `` ana y los 7 '' . it was adapted in mexico by alejandro pohlenz , marcia del río , and pablo ferrer . silvia navarro and jorge salinas are the protagonists . mayrín villanueva stars as the main antagonist .
