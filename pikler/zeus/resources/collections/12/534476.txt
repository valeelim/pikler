Model airplane     a model airplane is a small sized unmanned aerial vehicle . it may be a replica of an existing airplane or an imaginary aircraft . model aircraft are generally divided into two basic types , flying and non-flying . non-flying models are also called static , display , or shelf models . flying models range from simple toy gliders to powered scale models . some can be very large , especially when used to simulate the conditions of a real flight . static models range from mass produced toys to highly accurate models used for museum display . aircraft manufacturers and researchers use wind tunnel models . they are used for testing new designs and development of new ones . sometimes only part of the aircraft is modeled .
