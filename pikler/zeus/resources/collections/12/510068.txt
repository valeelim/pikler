1957–58 Eredivisie     the 1956–57 eredivisie was the 2nd eredivisie tournament . door oefening sterk ( dos ) won the tournament . dos defeated sc enschede 1–0 in extra time to win their first title . lastly , elinkwijk defeated gvav 2–1 to stay in the eredivisie , while gvav were sent to the eerste divisie . related pages . - 1957–58 eerste divisie
