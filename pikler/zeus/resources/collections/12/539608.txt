Jory Prum     jory prum ( january 31 , 1975 – april 22 , 2016 ) , also known as jory k. prum , was an american audio engineer . he was best known for his work in movie and video games . he was the owner of a recording studio located in the san francisco bay area . he worked many games but most famously `` the walking dead '' , `` the wolf among us '' , and `` brütal legend '' . prum was killed in a motorcycle accident on april 22 , 2016 in san francisco , california , aged 41. other websites . - official site
