Austrian Airlines     austrian airlines is the largest airline of austria . its main hub is the vienna international airport . it was founded on may 3 , 1923. austrian bought its first jet aircraft in 1963. austrian joined the star alliance on march 26 , 2000. the government of austria sold the airline in 2008. lufthansa bought austrian airlines in 2009. in april 2015 , austrian airlines was combined with tyrolean airways .
