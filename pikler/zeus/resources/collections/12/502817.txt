Kava     kava is a plant of the pepper family . it comes from polynesia and other lands in the pacific islands . the roots of the plant are used to make a drink with sedative and anesthetic properties . kava is used in many cultures in the pacific ocean , besides its native polynesia . these include hawaii , vanuatu , melanesia and some parts of micronesia . kava is pain-relieving , calming . and is usually taken as a drug to relax without losing mental clarity in thinking and remembering . evidence collected in studies show it is likely to be more effective than a placebo at helping calm some mild anxiety .
