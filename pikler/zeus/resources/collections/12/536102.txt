Dušan Čkrebić    dušan čkrebić dušan čkrebić ( born 7 august 1927 ) is a serbian politician . he was the prime minister of serbia . he served as minister from 5 may 1984 through 5 may 1986. he was a member of the league of communists of yugoslavia . čkrebić was born in niš , serbia . other websites . - biography of prime minister dušan čkrebić
