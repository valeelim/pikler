Berugo Carámbula     heber hugo carámbula , ( 31 october 1945 – 14 november 2015 ) , also known under the stage name of berugo carámbula , was a uruguayan actor , comedian and tv host . he was born in las piedras in 1945. he was known for his role in the argentine telenovela `` son amores '' from which he starred in from 2002 through 2003. carámbula died in montevideo , uruguay from complications of parkinson 's disease at the age of 70. other websites . - berugo carámbula en cinenacional ( spanish ) - berugo carámbula ( spanish ) - un único : berugo carámbula recordado en buenos aires ( spanish )
