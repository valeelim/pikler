Riccardo III     riccardo iii is an italian opera in four acts , music by luigi canepa and libretto by fulvio fulgonio , after william shakespeare 's play `` richard iii '' . it was first performed in milan , on november 10 , 1879. principal characters . - riccardo iii - bass - rismondo - tenor - scroop - baritone - rutlando - comprimary tenor - elisabetta , queen - soprano or mezzo-soprano - elisabetta , daughter - soprano discography . - 1962 : nicola rossi-lemeni ( riccardo iii ) , franco bonisolli ( rismondo ) , walter alberti ( scroop ) , tommaso frascati ( rutlando ) , lucilla udovich ( regina ) , antonietta pastori ( elisabetta ) ; corale “ luigi canepa ” sassari , orchestra dell ’ ente concerti “ marialisa de carolis ” , nino bonavolontà - ( opera d'oro , later decca ) sources . - `` the complete dictionary of opera & operetta '' , james anderson , wings books , 1993 .
