Janis Paige     janis paige ( born september 16 , 1922 ) is an american movie , musical theatre and television actress . she was born in tacoma , washington . her career began in 1944. she retired in 2001. paige made her live dramatic tv debut june 27 , 1957 , in `` the latch key '' on `` lux video theatre '' . paige appeared in many television shows during her career such as `` 87th precinct ( tv series ) '' , '' the pat boone chevy showroom '' , `` trapper john , m.d . `` , `` all in the family '' , `` columbo '' and `` caroline in the city '' , and in the 1975 television movie `` john o'hara 's gibbsville '' ( also known as `` the turning point of jim malloy '' ) .
