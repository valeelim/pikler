Robert Davi     robert john davi ( born june 26 , 1953 ) is an american actor , singer , writer , and producer . davi has performed in more than 130 movies . he has played the roles of main villain and drug lord franz sanchez in the 1989 james bond film `` licence to kill '' . he was fbi special agent bailey malone in the nbc television series `` profiler '' . he played a vietnam veteran and fbi special agent big johnson in `` die hard '' . davi played the opera-singing heavy jake fratelli in `` the goonies '' , hans zarba in `` son of the pink panther '' and al torres in `` showgirls '' . davi was trained as a singer . he launched his professional singing career in 2011. his first album , `` davi sings sinatra -- on the road to romance , '' hit # 6 on the `` billboard '' jazz charts . praised for his voice , davi ed as a headliner at the venetian , in las vegas , nevada , six months after the record was released .
