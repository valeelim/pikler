Wikitravel     wikitravel is a project dedicated to creating a travel guide reliable , current , complete and open content . although it is based on wiki technology , the project is intended to be used to create printed guides . wikitravel is created with the help of wikitravellers in worldwide . wikitravel was created in july 2003 by evan prodromou and michele ann jenkins . it is inspired in part by wikipedia . the project uses mediawiki , like the wikipedia , but it is not a project of the wikimedia foundation . wikitravel formerly used a creative commons attribution-sharealike 1.0 , but since january 1 , 2010 uses a creative commons attribution-sharealike 3.0. among other things , this facilitates the reprinting of pages . related pages . - wikivoyage other websites . - official website
