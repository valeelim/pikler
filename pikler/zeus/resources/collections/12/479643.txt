Emelie Öhrstig    emelie öhrstig emelie öhrstig , born 27 february 1978 in borås , sweden , is a swedish cross-country skier and road racing cyclist . as a country skier she who won a gold medal during the 2005 nordic world ski championships in oberstdorf , germany . she also won eleven additional victories up to 15 kilometers between 2002-2005. her best individual finish in turin during the 2006 winter olympics was 22nd in the women 's individual sprint race . in april 2006 she resigned as a member of the piteå elit sk team .
