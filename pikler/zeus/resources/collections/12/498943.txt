24 (2001 movie)    24 ( 2001 movie ) 24 is a 2001 czech thriller movie . it was directed by david beránek and stars barbora seidlová and martin trnavský . it happens during 24 hours and follows a man and a woman on the run in the czech republic . the movie was released through bontonfilm on 11 january 2001. cast . - pavel trnavský as martin - barbora seidlová as dívka - jiří tomek as grandfather
