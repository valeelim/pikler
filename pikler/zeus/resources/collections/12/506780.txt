Sal Mineo     salvatore `` sal '' mineo , jr. ( january 10 , 1939february 12 , 1976 ) , was an american movie and theatre actor . mineo was best known for his performance as john `` plato '' crawford opposite james dean in the movie `` rebel without a cause '' . he was twice nominated for the academy award for best supporting actor . once for his roles in `` rebel without a cause '' and once for `` exodus '' . in 1976 he played the role of a bisexual burglar in a series of stage performances of the comedy , `` p.s . your cat is dead '' . the play moved from san francisco to los angeles and mineo moved along with the play . he was arriving home after a rehearsal on february 12 , 1976 , when he was stabbed to death in the alley behind his apartment building . this happened near the sunset strip in west hollywood , california . he was buried in hawthorne , new york .
