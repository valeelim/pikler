Mariano Gago     josé mariano rebelo pires gago ( 16 may 1948 – 17 april 2015 ) was a portuguese electrical engineer and politician for the socialist party . he served as the minister for science and technology from 28 october 1995 to 6 april 2002. he also served as the ministry of science , technology and higher education from 21 march 2005 to 21 june 2011. gago was born in lisbon . he studied at universities in lisbon and paris . he was married and had a daughter . gago died a sudden cardiac death on 17 april 2015 in lisbon . he was 66 .
