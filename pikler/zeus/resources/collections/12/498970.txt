Carlos Gaviria Díaz     carlos emilio gaviria díaz ( 8 may 1937 – 31 march 2015 ) was a colombian lawyer , professor and politician . he served as the 5th chief magistrate of the constitutional court of colombia , where he served as a magistrate from 1993 to 2001. after retiring from the court , he went into politics becoming a senator of colombia in 2002. he ran for president as an alternative democratic pole candidate in the 2006 presidential election , ultimately losing to ex-president álvaro uribe vélez , who was seeking his second term in office .
