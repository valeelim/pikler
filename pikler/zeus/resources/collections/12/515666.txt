The Monkey's Paw    the monkey 's paw the monkey 's paw is a short story by william wymark jacobs . it was published in 1902 , in the book `` the lady of the barge '' . it is one of the most famous horror short stories of english literature . it is based on the idea that the holder of a talisman has three wishes . in this case , the talisman is the mummified paw of a monkey . in the story , the wishes are granted , but the price to be paid is very high . the story has been the source for many movies , and a theatre play . other websites . - `` the monkey 's paw '' ; full short story text - `` gaslight '' edition of the story - monkey 's paw radio play - podcast of `` the monkey 's paw '' as read by john lithgow - books that grow leveled book
