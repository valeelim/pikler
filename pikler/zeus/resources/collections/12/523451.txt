Elliot Richardson     elliot lee richardson ( july 20 , 1920 december 31 , 1999 ) was an american lawyer and politician . he was a member of the cabinet of presidents richard nixon and gerald ford . as u.s. attorney general , he was a known figure in the watergate scandal , and resigned rather than obey president nixon 's order to fire special prosecutor archibald cox . richardson served as secretary of health , education , and welfare from 1970 to 1973 , secretary of defense from january to may 1973 , attorney general from may to october 1973 , and secretary of commerce from 1976 to 1977. that makes him one of only two individuals to have held four cabinet positions within the united states government ( the other such individual being george shultz ) . on december 31 , 1999 , richardson died of a cerebral hemorrhage in boston , massachusetts , at the age of 79. other websites . - social security administration biography - elliot l. richardson
