Maiduguri     maiduguri , also called yerwa , is the capital and the largest city of borno state in north-eastern nigeria . as of 2007 , 1,197,497 live in the city . maiduguri was founded in 1907 as a military outpost by the british . the islamist group boko haram was founded in maiduguri in 2002. since then , the city has been target of many terrorist attacks by the group .
