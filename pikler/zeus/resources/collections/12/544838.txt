Handshaking (computing)    handshaking ( computing ) handshaking is the establishment of a connection between the computer and a peripheral device , such as a mouse or a keyboard . when the computer requests an action from a peripheral device , such as a printer , it `` asks '' the peripheral device to do something , and then gives a connection with each other . in the case of the printer , once the computer gains connection with the printer ( `` handshakes '' ) , the printer will print out the file from the computer .
