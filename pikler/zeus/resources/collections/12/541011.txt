Martin de Porres     saint martin de porres ( december 9 , 1579 – november 3 , 1639 ) is a christian saint of dominican order . he is the patron saint of social justice . he is also the patron saint of race relations , african-americans , barbers and hairdressers . he was born in lima , peru where he is venerated in the large basilica of santo domingo . on his feast day , november 3rd , many churches have a ceremony . saint martin was born to juan de porras , a spanish hidalgo , and ana velásquez , a black woman . he was originally named martín de porres velázquez . other websites . - about st. martin de porres - st. martin de porres
