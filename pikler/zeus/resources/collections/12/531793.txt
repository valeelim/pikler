La Paz, State of Mexico    la paz , state of mexico la paz is a municipality in the state of mexico , mexico . its municipal seat is the town of los reyes acaquilpan . it is located on the dividing line between mexico state and the eastern edge of the federal district and is part of the greater mexico city area . the area was part of a region called `` atlicpac '' which in nahuatl means 'above or at the edge of water .
