Robert Shapiro (lawyer)    robert shapiro ( lawyer ) robert leslie shapiro ( born september 2 , 1942 ) is an american civil lawyer . he is a senior partner in the law firm glaser weil fink jacobs howard avchen & shapiro , llp , which is based in los angeles . shapiro was born in plainfield , new jersey . he studied at the university of california , los angeles and loyola law school . shapiro is known for being part of the team which successfully defended o. j. simpson in 1995 from the charges that he murdered his ex-wife nicole brown and ronald goldman in 1994. shortly after the o. j. simpson trial , shapiro moved his practice away from criminal defense toward the civil litigation arena . he is also the voice of , and pictured in , the television commercials for the legalzoom company he co-founded .
