James Lankford     james lankford ( born march 4 , 1968 ) is the junior united states senator from oklahoma . he is a member of the u.s. republican party and has been in senate since january 3 , 2015. he was in the united states house of representatives from 2011 to 2015. lankford was born on march 4 , 1968 in dallas , texas . he studied at university of texas , austin and at southwestern baptist theological seminary . he is married to cindy lankford . they have two children . other websites . - james lankford , u.s. senator for oklahoma , home page
