Siemens C651     the siemens c651 train are a type of rolling stock . they can be found on the north south line and east west line in singapore . the siemens c651 began service on 20 september 1994. upgrades are being made between 2016 and 2018 to replace ageing parts and improve the appearance of the trains . there are 19 trainsets with 114 cars in total . see also . - mass rapid transit ( singapore ) - kawasaki heavy industries c151 - kawasaki heavy industries & nippon sharyo c751b - kawasaki heavy industries & csr qingdao sifang c151a - kawasaki heavy industries & csr qingdao sifang c151b
