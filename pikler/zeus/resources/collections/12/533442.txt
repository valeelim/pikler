Martin Sherman (actor)    martin sherman ( actor ) martin t. sherman ( born november 28 , 1966 ) is an american actor , voice actor , voice director and writer who has starred in various movies and television shows as well as providing the voice for several video games . one of his most well-known roles was as pac-man in `` pac-man world 3 '' . he also voiced thomas the tank engine from 2009 to 2015 in the british children 's television series `` thomas & friends '' . he also appeared on `` doctor who '' . sherman lives in the united kingdom .
