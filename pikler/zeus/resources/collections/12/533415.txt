François Dupeyron     françois dupeyron ( 14 august 195025 february 2016 ) was a french movie director and screenwriter . he directed 17 movies since his career began in 1977. his movie `` la chambre des officiers '' was entered into the 2001 cannes film festival . he was born in tartas , landes , france . dupeyron died in paris from a heart attack on 25 february 2016. he was aged 65 .
