Margaret Keane     margaret d. h. keane ( born peggy doris hawkins ; 1927 ) is an american artist . she mainly paints women , children , and animals in oil or mixed media . keane 's works are recognizable by the big , doe-like eyes of her subjects . in the 1960s , her artwork was sold under the name of her husband , walter keane , who claimed credit for it . they went to court and margaret won the case .
