The Place Beyond the Pines     the place beyond the pines is a 2012 american crime drama movie . it was directed by derek cianfrance and it was produced by lynette howell , sidney kimmel , alex orlovsky , and jamie patricof . the movie got positive reviews from critics . it has a 80 % rating on rotten tomatoes . kevin jagernauth of indiewire gave a positive review and said that it was a `` ambitious epic that is cut from some of the same thematic tissue as cianfrance 's previous film , but expands the scope into a wondrously widescreen tale of fathers , sons and the legacy of sins that are passed down through the generations '' . cast . - ryan gosling as luke glanton - bradley cooper as avery cross - eva mendes as romina gutierrez - ray liotta as peter deluca - ben mendelsohn as robin van der zee - rose byrne as jennifer cross - mahershala ali as kofi - bruce greenwood as bill killcullen - dane dehaan as jason cankam - emory cohen as a. j. cross - harris yulin as al cross - robert clohessy as chief weirzbowski
