Stein Eriksen     stein eriksen ( 11 december 1927 – 27 december 2015 ) is a norwegian-american alpine skier . he won gold and two silvers at the 1952 winter olympics in oslo . he retired in 1954. eriksen was born in oslo . his parents were marius eriksen ( 1886–1950 ) and birgit heien ( 1900–1996 ) . he lived in the united states for six decades . eriksen died on 27 december 2015 in park city , utah . he was 88. other websites . - stein eriksen at the international ski federation
