Shawn Mendes     shawn peter raul mendes ( born august 8 , 1998 ) is a canadian singer-songwriter . he became known in 2013 , when he began posting cover versions of songs by artists such as justin bieber on the video sharing application vine . in 2014 , he signed a recording contract with island records . mendes released his first single , `` life of the party '' . the song peaked at number 1 on the real-time `` billboard '' twitter trending 140 chart . mendes also released a four-track ep , `` the shawn mendes ep '' . it charted at number 5 on the `` billboard '' charts . he is known for the songs `` life of the party '' , `` stitches '' ( his biggest hit and a uk number-one ) , and `` treat you better '' , and more recently , there 's nothing holding me back . mendes has now had two world tours for his albums handwritten and illuminate and is very commonly known .
