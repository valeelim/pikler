Gurgen Dalibaltayan     gurgen dalibaltayan ( ; 5 june 1926 – 1 september 2015 ) was an armenian military commander . he was the chief of general staff of armenian armed forces during the battle of shusha on 8–9 may 1992. the battle took place during the nagorno-karabakh war . dalibaltayan was born in bogdanovka , javakheti , georgian ssr ( present-day ninotsminda , samtskhe-javakheti , georgia ) . he was married to shushanik dalibaltayan ( née khachatryan ) . they have two children , a daughter varduhi ( b . 1952 ) and a son varazdat ( b . 1954 ) . dalibaltayan died on 1 september 2015 in yerevan , aged 89 .
