Wheelspin     wheelspin is the practice of making wheels lose traction on a road surface . this can occur in manual transmission cars when the clutch is released too quickly while accelerating . this can happen when pulling away from stop . how to do a wheelspin in a manual car - press the clutch full down - select 1st gear - push down the gas pedal - release the clutch pedal as fast as possible this should result in a wheelspin
