2016 Ecuador earthquake     on april 16 , 2016 , a 7.8 magnitude struck ecuador at 18:58 local time . it was centered about 17 miles from the town of muisne and about 110 miles from the capital quito . there was widespread damage , so that most infrastructures are destroyed . structures hundreds of miles from the epicenter collapsed . over 272 people died . over 2,500 people were injured . president rafael correa declared a state of emergency after the earthquake .
