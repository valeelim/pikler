John David Jackson     john david jackson ( january 19 , 1925 – may 20 , 2016 ) was a canadian–american physics . he was the professor emeritus at the university of california , berkeley and a faculty senior scientist emeritus at lawrence berkeley national laboratory . he was a theoretical physicist . he was a member of the national academy of sciences . he was well known for his many publications and summer-school lectures in nuclear and particle physics , as well as his widely used graduate text on classical electromagnetism .
