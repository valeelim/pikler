Wasted username     a wasted username or wasted account is a user account on a website that was not used very much ( or at all ) after it was made . a person who makes an account like this is called a namewaster or a name waster . this word came from youtube , where many people would post both funny and hateful comments on namewasters ' youtube channels . this was an internet phenomenon until 2012 , when youtube deleted a lot of these wasted accounts .
