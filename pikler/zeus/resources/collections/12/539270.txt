Yuri Bychkov     yuri alexandrovich bychkov ( ; september 1 , 1931 , lopasnya – april 18 , 2016 , moscow ) was a russian art historian . he was a member of the union of artists and the union of theatre workers of the russian federation . he was director of writer 's house museum in melikhovo from 1994 through 2004. he graduated from the moscow aviation institute . he was one of the founders voopik ( all-russian society for historic preservation and cultural organization ) . bychkov died in moscow on april 18 , 2016 , aged 84. other websites . - биография - бычков
