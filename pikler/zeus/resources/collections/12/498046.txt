Cryptic Writings     cryptic writings is the seventh studio album by american thrash metal band , megadeth . `` cryptic writings '' was released on june 17 , 1997 through capitol records . it is megadeth 's last album to feature drummer nick menza . the album peaked at # 10 on the `` billboard '' 200 and at # 17 on the canadian albums chart . in 1998 , the song `` trust '' was nominated for a grammy award in the category of best metal performance .
