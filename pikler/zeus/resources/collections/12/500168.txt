Zalman Shoval     zalman shoval ( , born 28 april 1930 ) is an israeli politician and diplomat . he is also active in israel 's economic life . he was the israeli ambassador to the united states in the years 1990–1993 and 1998–2000 , and an active member of the knesset in the rafi party of ben gurion , the state list , and the likud party . shoval was born in the free city of danzig ( now gdańsk , poland ) .
