Frank Watkins     frank watkins ( february 19 , 1968 – october 18 , 2015 ) was an american heavy metal musician . he was best known as the bass player for the death metal band obituary . he also played for the norwegian black metal band gorgoroth from 2007 until his death in 2015. he was known by the stage name `` bøddel '' during his time with gorgoroth . he started playing music when he was 12 years old . he joined obituary in 1989. other than music , watkins decided to form a management company called back from the dead productions which tries to help bands create a positive career in the music business . watkins joined gorgoroth in december 2007. he debuted with gorgoroth at the hole in the sky festival on august 29 , 2009. in 2012 , watkins was diagnosed with primary central nervous system lymphoma . on october 18 , 2015 , watkins died after a long battle with cancer .
