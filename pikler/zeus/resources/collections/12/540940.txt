Janusz Tazbir     janusz tazbir ( august 5 , 1927 – may 3 , 2016 ) was a polish historian . he worked in the subject of the culture and religion of poland in the 16th and 17th centuries . he was born in kałuszyn . he wrote over 1,000 books and essays about the history of poland . tazbir died on may 3 , 2016 in warsaw , aged 88. other websites . - professor janusz tazbir – historian of old polish culture
