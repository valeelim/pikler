Clocks (song)    clocks ( song ) `` clocks '' is the third single off british alternative rock band coldplay 's second studio album , `` a rush of blood to the head '' . the song was released on 10 december 2002. on the `` billboard '' charts , `` clocks '' was able to peak at # 4 on the adult top 40 , # 9 on the modern rock tracks , # 21 on the mainstream top 40 , and # 29 on the hot 100 charts . in 2004 , `` clocks '' won a grammy award for record of the year . it was also nominated in the category of best single at the 2003 q awards . pitchfork media ranked the song # 68 on their top 100 singles of 2000-04 list and at # 155 on their 500 greatest songs of the 2000s . other websites . - `` clocks '' on youtube
