Madeleine LeBeau     marie madeleine berthe lebeau ( 10 june 1923 – 1 may 2016 ) was a french movie actress . she was known for her roles in `` hold back the dawn '' , `` gentleman jim '' , `` casablanca '' , `` paris after dark '' , and `` 8½ '' . lebeau died on 1 may 2016 in estepona , spain , after breaking her femur in a fall , aged 92 .
