Pickling     pickling is a method of making food last longer . in pickling , the food is put either into vinegar , or in salt water ( called brine ) . pickling also changes the flavour of the food . vegetables are often preserved like this . the key to pickling is that it produces low ph , 4.6 or lower . this kills most bacteria . pickling can preserve perishable foods for months . anti-microbial herbs and spices , such as mustard seed , garlic , cinnamon or cloves , are often added .
