Aida (musical)    aida ( musical ) aida is a musical by sir elton john and tim rice , first opened in 1998. it was based on the verdi opera of the same name it was nominated for tony awards . act i the musical opens in a modern-day museum . in the egyptian wing , a man and woman meet each other as a statue of a female pharaoh , amneris , awakens ( `` every story is a love story '' ) and transports them to ancient egypt . ramades , who is leader of the egyptian army , return from an expedition through the lands of the nubians , the longtime enemies of egypt ( `` fortune favors the brave '' ) . when the soldiers capture a group of women , he is instantly drawn to one of them , aida , who tries freeing herself by fighting one of his soldiers . after ramades forces aida to wash his back , she refuses , telling him that although the egyptians took everything from the nubians , their spirit will never be taken ( `` the past is another land '' ) . ramades saves aida 's companions from working in the copper mines ( and certain death ) by sending them to work in the palace grounds instead . he also sends aida as a handmaiden to amneris , to whom he is betrothed .
