Peter Barry     peter barry ( 10 august 1928 – 26 august 2016 ) was a irish politician and businessman . he was from cork city . he was a member of fine gael . he was a teachta dála ( td ; member of parliament ) from 1969 to 1997 , and minister for foreign affairs from 1982 to 1987. he helped negotiate the anglo-irish agreement . in 1987 , he served for a short time as tánaiste ( deputy prime minister ) .
