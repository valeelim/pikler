Walter Isaacson     walter isaacson ( born may 20 , 1952 ) is an american businessman and journalist . in 2001 he became chairman and chief executive officer ( ceo ) of cnn . in 2003 he became the president and ceo of the aspen institute . he also has been the editor of time magazine . isaacson has written biographies of benjamin franklin , albert einstein , steve jobs and henry kissinger . he also co-authored the book `` the wise men : six friends and the world they made '' . in 2012 time magazine named him one of the top 100 most influential people in the world . isaacson was born on may 20 , 1952 in new orleans , louisiana . his father irwin was an engineer . his mother betsy was a realtor . isaacson grew up in new orleans . he graduated from harvard university in 1974. isaacson attended pembroke college , oxford as a rhodes scholar . he started as a journalist at `` the sunday times of london '' . he returned home to write for `` new orleans times-picayune/states-item . '' in 1978 he started as a political correspondent for time magazine . he became the editor of time in 1996. after 23 years at the magazine he joined cnn as ceo in 2001. he left in 2001 to head up the washington dc think tank , the aspen institute .
