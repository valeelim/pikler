Keytar     a keytar is a musical instrument . it is shaped and held like a guitar , but has keys like a keyboard . keytars are lightweight keyboards that have a strap that is put around the neck and shoulders . the instrument has a keyboard for playing musical notes and sounds . the earliest keytars were created around the 1970 's . this picture of a keytar is one of the most popular keytars . most popular keytars are seen with a red guitar neck and played in 1980 movies .
