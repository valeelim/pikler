Mark Cuban     mark cuban ( born july 31 , 1958 ) is an american businessman , investor , movie producer , author , television personality and philanthropist . he is the owner of the nba 's dallas mavericks , landmark theatres , and magnolia pictures , and the chairman of the hdtv cable network axs tv . as of september 2015 , cuban has publicly expressed interest in running for president in the 2016 u.s. presidential election , citing that he `` could beat both trump and clinton '' . he later decided not to run . on october 9 , 2015 , cuban announced his intentions to run for speaker of the house to replace john boehner .
