Héctor Babenco     héctor eduardo babenco ( february 7 , 1946 – july 13 , 2016 ) was an argentine-born brazilian movie director , screenwriter , producer and actor . he worked in several countries including argentina , brazil and the united states . his best known movies are `` pixote '' ( 1980 ) , `` kiss of the spider woman '' ( 1985 ) , `` ironweed '' ( 1987 ) , `` at play in the fields of the lord '' ( 1990 ) and `` carandiru '' ( 2003 ) . babenco died on july 13 , 2016 from a heart attack in são paulo , brazil , aged 70 .
