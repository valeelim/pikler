Ann Wedgeworth     ann wedgeworth ( born january 21 , 1934 ) is an american actress . she is best known for her roles as lana shields on `` three 's company '' and merleen elridge on `` evening shade '' . on `` three 's company '' , wedgeworth 's character pursued the younger jack tripper . wedgeworth was written out of `` three 's company '' due to producers thinking that was too much for the show . however , wedgeworth said that she asked to be released from her contract when she noticed her role became smaller .
