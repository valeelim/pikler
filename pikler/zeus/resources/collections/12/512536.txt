Dance Remixes     dance remixes is the first remix album by american rapper lil ' kim . it was released on june 6 , 2006 by atlantic records . the album has dance remixes of the two singles `` lighters up '' and `` whoa '' from kim 's fourth studio album `` the naked truth '' ( 2005 ) . it also has two remixes of `` big momma thang '' and a remix of `` queen bitch '' from kim 's debut album `` hard core '' ( 1996 ) . the official track listing for the album .
