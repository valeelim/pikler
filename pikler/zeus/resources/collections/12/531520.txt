Portland, Missouri    portland , missouri portland is an unincorporated community in callaway county , missouri , united states . it is part of the jefferson city , missouri . it is located at ( 38.710161 , -91.717166 ) on route 94 about twenty miles northeast of jefferson city . the village was laid out in 1831. its location on the missouri river made it a popular shipping point for the farms and plantations of callaway county . the name portland stems from that activity . < br >
