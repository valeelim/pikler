Fatimata Touré     fatimata touré is a women 's rights activist and the head of the regional forum on reconciliation and peace in gao , mali . she is also the head of women ’ s action , research , study and training group , which works against fistula . in 2014 , she received the international women of courage award . during the 2012 and 2013 fighting in mali , a hospital in gao was attacked . touré helped the fistula patients find a new place to live , and find medical help . touré also gave care and shelter to those forced into marriages or raped . she also spoke against violence against women . she made a record of what happened , even as her own home was attacked . awards . - in 2014 , fatimata touré received the international women of courage award . - in 2014 , the united states ambassador to the united nations samantha power , mentioned touré in a speech in mali . other websites . - 2015 international women of courage awards , u.s. state department photostream on flickr - secretary 's international women of courage award ( u.s. department of state )
