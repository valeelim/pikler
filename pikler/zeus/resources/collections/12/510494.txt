Saint Petersburg Stadium     the saint petersburg stadium russian : стадион санкт-петербург ) is an association football stadium in saint petersburg , russia that will be completed in 2016. the stadium will have a capacity of 66,881 people , and it costs $ 1.1 billion to $ 1.4 billion . it will eventually be the home of fc zenit st. petersburg , and it will be used in the 2017 fifa confederations cup , 2018 fifa world cup and the uefa euro 2020 .
