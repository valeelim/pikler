Princess Leonore, Duchess of Gotland    princess leonore , duchess of gotland princess leonore , duchess of gotland ( leonore lilian maria ; born 20 february 2014 ) , is the first child of princess madeleine of sweden and christopher o'neill . she is the youngest member of the swedish royal family . she is fifth in the line of succession to the swedish throne . she was christened on 8 june 2014 .
