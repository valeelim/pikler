Lothar Späth     lothar späth ( 16 november 1937 – 18 march 2016 ) was a german politician of the cdu . from 30 august 1978 to 13 january 1991 , späth was the 5th minister president of baden-württemberg and chairman of the cdu baden-württemberg . he served as the 36th president of the bundesrat in the 1980s .
