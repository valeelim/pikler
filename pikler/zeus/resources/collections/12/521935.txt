Eagles of Death Metal     eagles of death metal is an american rock band from palm desert , california . the band released their debut studio album , `` peace , love , death metal '' on march 23 , 2004 through antacidaudio . the song `` i only want you '' was featured in the 2004 racing game `` gran turismo 4 '' , the television series `` queer as folk '' , an advertisement for windows 8 , and was a downloadable song for the `` rock band '' series . their second studio album `` death by sexy '' was released on april 11 , 2006 in the united states and on july 3 , 2006 in europe through downtown records and columbia records . in the fall of 2006 , they shared an opening support slot with guns n ' roses , but it only lasted one show . axl rose of guns n ' roses called the band `` pigeons of shit metal '' from the stage that night and eagles of death metal used the phrase on a t-shirt . the band performed a live concert for the launch of the 2008 video game at the hollywood & highland virgin megastore in los angeles . the band 's third studio album `` heart on '' was released on october 28 , 2008 .
