Over and Over Again (song)    over and over again ( song ) `` over and over again '' is a song by english recording artist nathan sykes . the song was written by sykes and was released in the united kingdom on 20 november 2015 as the second single from his debut studio album . the song peaked to number 8 on the uk singles chart . sykes performed the song live on `` the x factor '' on 22 november 2015 .
