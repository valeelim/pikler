Vätterstads IK     vätterstads ik was an ice hockey club in jönköping-huskvarna in sweden . the club was established in-mid 1967 whenik stefa and vättersnäs if merged . the club played its home games in rosenlundshallen . the club played in the swedish second division during the seasons of 1967-1968 and 1968-1969 in 1971 the club merged with husqvarna if 's ice hockey section . this led to the establishment of hv71 .
