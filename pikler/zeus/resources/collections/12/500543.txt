Stevie McCrorie     stevie mccrorie is a scottish singer , musician and firefighter . he is best known for winning the fourth series of `` the voice uk '' in april 2015. mccrorie 's first song was called `` lost stars '' . it was released on 5 april 2015. he used to be a singer from 2010 until 2012 and again has had a new career since january 2015 .
