Pelle Gudmundsen-Holmgreen     pelle gudmundsen-holmgreen ( 21 november 1932 – 27 june 2016 ) was a danish composer . gudmundsen-holmgreen was born in copenhagen , denmark . he was the son of the sculptor jørgen gudmundsen-holmgreen . he studied at the royal danish academy of music in copenhagen , with finn høffding , svend westergaard , bjørn hjelmborg , and vagn holmboe ( instrumentation ) , graduating in 1958. amongst other works , he composed fourteen string quartets and a concerto grosso for string quartet and orchestra , written for the kronos quartet , which he referred to as `` vivaldi on safari '' . he won the nordic council music prize in 1980 for his `` symfoni/antifoni '' . gudmundsen-holmgreen died of cancer on 26 june 2016 , aged 83 .
