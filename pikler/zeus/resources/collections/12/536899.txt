Swati language     swati ( also known as swazi ) is part of the nguni group of bantu languages . it is spoken by about 1.5 million people in swaziland and south africa . it is one of the eleven official languages in south africa . it is taught in the schools of swaziland , and in some south african schools . swati is closely related to phuthi , a language spoken in lesotho , and to zulu , ndebele and xhosa . swati has four main varietes : shiselweni , hhohho , manzini and shiselweni . they correspond with the four administrative regions of swaziland .
