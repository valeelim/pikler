Nancy Olson     nancy olson ( born april 14 , 1928 ) is an american actress . she got an academy award nomination for best supporting actress for her role as betty schaefer in `` sunset boulevard '' ( 1950 ) . she played betsy carlisle in `` the absent-minded professor '' . she played mrs. abbott in `` airport 1975 '' ( 1974 ) . she has mostly retired from acting . olson was born in milwaukee , wisconsin .
