André Mba Obame     andré mba obame ( 15 june 1957 – 12 april 2015 ) was a gabonese politician . after serving as an adviser to president omar bongo in the 1980s , he was a minister in the government of gabon from 1990 to 1991 and again from 1997 to 2009. obame died in yaoundé , cameroon , on 12 april 2015 from a long-illness , aged 57 .
