Hermann Schweppenhäuser     hermann schweppenhäuser ( 12 march 1928 – 8 april 2015 ) was a german philosopher and publisher . he was a professor of philosophy at the johann-wolfgang-goethe uinversity in frankfurt am main , germany and the university of lüneburg , germany . he was the autohor of books on heidegger , kierkegaard and hegel and numerous esaays and articles on philosphical and aesthetical topics . together with rolf tiedemann , he was the editor of the collected works of walter benjamin . he was born in frankfurt am main an died in veitshöchheim , germany at the age of 87. other websites . - biography
