Zin Mar Aung     zin mar aung is a burmese activist . she received the international women of courage award from the u.s state department in 2012. zin mar aung studied botany in the university . she received a degree from the university of distance education in yangon . zin mar aung was part of the burma democracy movement . because of this , she was a political prisoner for eleven years . she started a group for asian culture and democracy . she also started a group to help women who were political prisoners . she also helped start a group for women 's empowerment . in 2012 , she became the leader of an organization for ethnic minorities in war areas . in 2013 she started to work with the international foundation for electoral systems on women ’ s political empowerment under the global women ’ s leadership fund . other websites . - 2012 international women of courage awards , u.s. state department photostream on flickr - secretary 's international women of courage award ( u.s. department of state )
