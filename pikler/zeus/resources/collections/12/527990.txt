Doping in sport     in sport , doping is the use of a drug that athletes or competitors are not allowed to use . certain drugs , such as steroids , are not allowed ( or banned ) in professional sports . this is because they can help a competitor 's performance , by making them stronger or faster . this gives them an unfair advantage over other competitors . the drugs may be legal , but competitors will not be allowed to use them . many sports make competitors take a drug test to prove that they are not using any drugs . related pages . - blood doping
