Binali Yıldırım     binali yıldırım ( born 20 december 1954 ) is the 27th and current prime minister of turkey and the leader of the justice and development party ( akp ) since may 2016. he was the minister of transport , maritime and communication from 2002 to 2013 and again between 2015 and 2016. between 2014 and 2015 , he served as senior advisor to president recep tayyip erdoğan .
