Eva Henning     eva henning ( born eva wetlesen ; 10 may 1920 – 18 april 2016 ) was a swedish stage and movie actress . she was known for her roles in `` elvira madigan '' ( 1943 ) , `` flickan från tredje raden '' ( 1949 ) , and in `` the firebird '' ( 1952 ) . she was born in newark , new york to swedish parents . henning died in oslo , norway on 18 april 2016 , aged 95 .
