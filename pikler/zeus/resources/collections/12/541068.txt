Abel Fernandez     abel gonzalez fernandez ( july 14 , 1930 – may 3 , 2016 ) was an american actor . he was known for his role as federal agent william `` bill '' youngfellow on the 1959 – 1963 abc television series `` the untouchables '' . fernandez died on may 3 , 2016 from lung cancer at a hospital in whittier , california , aged 85. other websites . - `` abel fernandez '' bio from the `` native american celebrities '' website - abel fernandez at `` tv.com '' - obituary
