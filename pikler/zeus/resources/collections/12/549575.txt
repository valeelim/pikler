C96     the c96 , is a german semi automatic pistol that has a broomhandle pistol grip . the german army used c96s in world war i and world war ii . during world war ii the c96 got replaced by the p38 . c96s are used by many countries including germany , spain , china , and the soviet union . the c96 was produced by germany from 1896 and ended in 1937. the chinese army nicknamed the c96 called boxcannon .
