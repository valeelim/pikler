Alena Vrzáňová     alena `` ája '' vrzáňová ( ) ( also zanová , married name : steindler ) ( 16 may 1931 – 30 july 2015 ) , was a czech figure skater who represented czechoslovakia in competition . vrzáňová is the 1949 & 1950 world champion and 1950 european champion . she was born in prague . vrzáňová died suddenly on 30 july 2015 in new york city at the age of 84 while in exile . other websites . - sports-reference profile
