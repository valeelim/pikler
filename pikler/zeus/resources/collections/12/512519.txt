Jörg Schneider     jörg schneider ( 7 february 1935 – 22 august 2015 ) was a swiss stage and movie actor . he usually starred in swiss german language cinema and television and stage productions . he was known for his `` kasperle '' , `` pumuckl '' and fairytale-radio plays records and adapted plays for the swiss german language . schneider died in wetzikon from cancer , aged 80. other websites . - `` jörg schneider '' on the website of the swiss television srf
