Raúl Lozano     raúl lucio lozano ( born september 3 , 1956 in la plata , argentina ) is a former argentine volleyball player . he first coached for estudiantes de la plata . in 2005-2008 lozano was the head coach of poland 's national volleyball team . he has also coached other top-level teams in europe including teams from italy , greece , and spain . he led the polish national team to win a silver medal at the 2006 world championship held in japan . his polish team lost to italy in the quarter final round of the 2008 summer olympics at beijing . in 2009 he coached the german team for two years . he was then the head coach of czarni radom in poland . on november 17 , 2015 , it was announced that raúl lucio will coach the iranian national volleyball team . he replaced slobodan kovac who is the former coach of the iranian team .
