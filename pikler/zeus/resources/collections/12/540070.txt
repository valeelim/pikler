Sengkang MRT/LRT Station     sengkang mrt/lrt station ( ne16/stc ) is an railway station in the north-east part of singapore . it is an interchange station between the north east mrt line and sengkang lrt line . the station is located in sengkang and is integrated with compass one . timeline . - 18 january 2003 : station opened along sengkang lrt line east loop - 20 june 2003 : north east line opens - 29 january 2005 : sengkang lrt line west loop opens in unidirectional manner - 1 january 2013 : sengkang lrt line west loop becomes full-day bidirectional former codes . - north east line : p10 ( before opening ) - sengkang lrt line : n/a
