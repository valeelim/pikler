Vanessa Paradis     vanessa paradis ( born 22 december 1972 ) is a french singer , model and actress . she became a child star at age of 14 with the great worldwide success of her single `` joe le taxi '' . since 1991 , she has been a spokesmodel for chanel . she was in a relationship with american actor johnny depp from 1998 to 2011 , and they had two children : lily-rose and jack . she is also the older sister of alysson paradis , who is also an actress . other websites . - vanessa paradis at universal music
