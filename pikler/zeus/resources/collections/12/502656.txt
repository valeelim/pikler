Salting (food)    salting ( food ) salting is a treatment for food to make it last longer . salting means that the food is treated with table salt . salting is similar to pickling . in pickling , food is treated with brine . the main types of food that are salted are fish and meat . bacon is an example of salted meat . it was discovered in the 19th century that salt mixed with nitrites ( saltpeter ) would color meats red , rather than grey , and consumers at that time then strongly preferred the red-colored meat . the food hence preserved stays healthy and fresh for days avoiding bacterial decay . salting is one of the ancient ways to preserve food .
