Óscar Marcelino Álvarez    óscar marcelino álvarez óscar marcelino álvarez ( 25 may 1948 – 28 april 2016 ) was an argentine former professional football player . he played in argentina for banfield and newell 's old boys between 1968 and 1970 before reappearing in greece . in 1980 he was transferred to atromitos where he played until his retirement in 1981. álvarez died on 28 april 2016 in athens , greece at the age of 67 .
