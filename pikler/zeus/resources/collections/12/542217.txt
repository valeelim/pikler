Michał Kubiak     michał kubiak ( born 23 february 1988 ) is a polish volleyball player , a member of polish national team and turkish club halkbank ankara , a participant of the olympic games ( london 2012 ) , 2014 world champion , a gold medalist of the 2012 world league , silver medalist of the 2011 world cup , bronze medalist of the 2011 european championship and the 2011 world league , 2016 turkish champion . other websites . - fivb profile - plusliga player profile
