Sri Srinivasan     padmanabhan srikanth `` sri '' srinivasan ( born february 23 , 1967 ) is an american jurist . he is the united states circuit judge of the united states court of appeals for the district of columbia circuit since may 2013. he was confirmed by the united states senate by a vote of 97â€“0 on may 23 , 2013. before being confirmed as a judge he was the principal deputy solicitor general of the united states . he has argued 25 cases before the u.s. supreme court . he was also a lecturer at harvard law school . srinivasan also is known for having represented former enron executive jeffrey skilling in his appeal before the u.s. supreme court , `` skilling v. united states '' ( 2010 ) . srinivasan was born in chandigarh , india on february 23 , 1967. he was raised in lawrence , kansas . he studied at stanford university . on june 11 , 2012 , president barack obama nominated srinivasan to the seat on the d.c. circuit . he was nominated again on january 3 , 2013 , after the senate adjourned . his senate confirmation hearing on april 10 , 2013 , was uneventful . his nomination was reported to the floor of the senate on may 16 , 2013 , by a unanimous vote of 18 for and 0 against . a final vote on his nomination took place on may 23 , 2013 , where he was confirmed 97-0. he received his commission on may 24 , 2013. he received the oath of office from chief judge merrick garland on june 18 , 2013 .
