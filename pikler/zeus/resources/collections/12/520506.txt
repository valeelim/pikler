Jaws (novel)    jaws ( novel ) jaws is a 1974 american novel by peter benchley . it is about a great white shark that eats several people in the ocean near a small town in long island , new york . three men set out on a boat to hunt and kill the shark . benchley was partly inspired by the adventures of a shark fisherman named frank mundus . millions of copies of the novel were sold . in 1975 , a movie based on the book , also called `` jaws '' , was released . the movie was directed by steven spielberg .
