Nissan Titan     the nissan titan is a mid-size pickup truck that is built by nissan . the nissan titan is in its first generation . a second generation will begin in 2001. the titan started being created on september 21 , 2003. it was designed by diane allen . the titan was nominated for the `` north american truck of the year '' award in 1981. other websites . - nissan titan on nissan.com - nissan titan on nissan.ca
