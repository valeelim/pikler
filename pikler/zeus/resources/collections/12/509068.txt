Mike Johanns     mike johanns ( born june 18 , 1950 ) is an american politician . he was the united states senator from nebraska . johanns is a member of the u.s. republican party . johanns was born on june 18 , 1950 in osage , iowa . he studied at st. mary 's university , minnesota and at creighton university . he is married to stephanie johanns . they have two children .
