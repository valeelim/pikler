Lincoln County, South Dakota    lincoln county , south dakota lincoln county is a county in the u.s. state of south dakota . as of the 2010 census , the population was 44,828. this makes it the third-most populated county in the state . its county seat is canton . the first resident of the area was a. i. linderman in 1866. he was the only resident until the summer of 1867. lincoln county was incorporated on december 30 , 1867. the county was named for abraham lincoln , 16th president of the united states .
