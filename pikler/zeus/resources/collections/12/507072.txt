José Gaspar Rodríguez de Francia y Velasco     josé gaspar rodríguez de francia y velasco ( 17661840 ) was the dictator of paraguay . he helped paraguay become independent from spain . paraguay became independent from spain in 1811. francia y velasco reigned from 1814 until his death in 1840. francia y velasco believed that paraquay should be self-sufficient . during his reign , almost no goods were imported , and only yerba mate was exported . leaves and twigs of this herb , `` ilex paraguariensis '' , are the basis of the drink called `` mate '' .
