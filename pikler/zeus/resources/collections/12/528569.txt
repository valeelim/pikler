Mel Tillis     lonnie melvin `` mel '' tillis ( born august 8 , 1932 ) is an american country music singer and songwriter . he recorded songs since the late 1950s . his biggest success occurred in the 1970s , with a long list of top 10 hits . his biggest hits include `` i ai n't never '' , `` good woman blues '' , and `` coca-cola cowboy '' . on february 13 , 2012 , president barack obama awarded tillis the national medal of arts for his contributions to country music . he also has won the cma awards ' most coveted award , entertainer of the year . he is also known for his stutter , which does not affect his singing voice . his daughter is country music singer pam tillis . other websites . - mel tillis at the country music hall of fame and museum
