Doomsday (comics)    doomsday ( comics ) doomsday is a fictional super villain appearing in comics by dc comics . he is a superman villain . doomsday was first shown in 1992. he made his first full appearance in `` '' that same year . the character was created by writer-artist dan jurgens , who wanted to create a villain who could be a physical challenge for superman . ign 's list of the top 100 comic book villains of all time ranked doomsday as # 46. he is best known as the character who `` killed '' superman . the character appears in the 2016 movie `` '' , voiced and performed by robin atkin downes through motion-capture .
