Red Fort     the red fort ( lal qila ) is a monument built in 1638 that rises above old delhi . it was built by the mughal emperor shah jahan . the fort is located in what is now the centre of delhi , india . it is made of red stone and marble . it was built on the right bank of the yamuna river . the red fort was originally referred to as `` qila-i-mubarak '' ( the blessed fort ) , because it was the residence of the royal family . the layout of the red fort was organised to keep and integrate this site with the salimgarh fort . the planning and aesthetics of the red fort represent the high point in mughal creativity which happened during the reign of emperor shah jahan . this fort has had many additions after its construction by emperor shah jahan . it is a world heritage site . suiip
