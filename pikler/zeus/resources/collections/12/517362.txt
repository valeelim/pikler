Jos Vandeloo     josephus albertus `` jos '' vandeloo ( zonhoven , 5 september 1925 - 5 october 2015 ) was a belgian writer and poet . his career began in the 1950s . he became well known with the stories `` de muur '' ( e : the wall ) en `` het gevaar '' ( e : the danger ) . other websites . - jos vandeloo - jos vandeloo - g.j . van bork en p.j . verkruijsse , de nederlandse en vlaamse auteurs ( 1985 )
