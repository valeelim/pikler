Assia Djebar     assia djebar ( ) is the pen name of fatima-zohra imalayen ( 30 june 1936 – 6 february 2015 ) was an algerian novelist , translator and filmmaker . she was known for writing books about feminism . she was elected to the académie française on 16 june 2005 , the first writer from the maghreb to achieve such recognition . for the entire body of her work she was awarded the 1996 neustadt international prize for literature . djebar died in paris , aged 78 .
