Bodil Malmsten     bodil malmsten ( 19 august 1944 – 5 february 2016 ) was a swedish poet and novelist . she wrote over fifteen books from 1977 to her death . her best known book was the english translation of `` priset på vatten i finistère '' ( `` the price of water in finistère '' ) which was awarded book of the week by bbc radio 4. malmsten was born in bjärme , jämtland . her grandfather was designer and architect carl malmsten ( 1888–1972 ) . malmsten died from cancer on 5 february 2016 in stockholm . she was aged 71 .
