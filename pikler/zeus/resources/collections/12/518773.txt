Dodge Caliber     dodge caliber is a compact hatchback sold from 2006-2011 by chrysler . it was often categorized as a station wagon or a crossover ( due to its limited awd ) . the caliber replaced the neon . it was built on the chrysler pm/mk platform which was also used on the compass , patriot , and mitsubishi models as well . it was later replaced by the dart ( a badge engineered alfa romeo model ) .
