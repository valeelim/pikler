World Painted Blood     world painted blood is the tenth studio album by american thrash metal band , slayer . `` world painted blood '' was released on november 3 , 2009 through american recordings and sony music entertainment . it is slayer 's last album to feature drummer dave lombardo who was fired and guitarist jeff hanneman who died of liver failure , both in 2013. the album peaked at # 12 on the `` billboard '' 200 , # 18 on the digital albums , # 2 on the top hard rock albums , # 4 on the top rock albums , and at # 1 on the top tastemaker albums charts . in 2010 , the song `` hate worldwide '' was nominated for a grammy award in the category of best metal performance . the next year , the song `` world painted blood '' was nominated for a grammy award in the same category .
