Rickard Fagerlund     rickard fagerlund , born 25 january 1937 in södertälje , sweden , dead 31 december 2009 , was a swedish ice hockey player and manager . he played as a defenseman for södertälje sk . he won the swedish national championship in 1956. he also played for gais , ifk norrköping and ik oskarshamn . he later coached hc vita hästen . he was the chairman of the swedish ice hockey association between 17 september 1983 and 15 june 2002 .
