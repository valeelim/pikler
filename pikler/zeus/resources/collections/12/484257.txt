Capsule     a pharmaceutical capsule is an easy way to take medication . capsules may contain powder , liquid or oil . the outer shell is made of hard or soft gelatin . capsules come in different shapes and colors to identify dose or what company made them . they are also available as timed release which work over a period of time . capsules should usually be taken whole . always consult a pharmacist or desk reference before opening a capsule . capsules should also not be crushed without first checking to see if it is safe .
