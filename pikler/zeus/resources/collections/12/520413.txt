Law of the land     the phrase law of the land ( latin lex terrae ) is a legal term . it means all of the laws in force within a country or region . the term was first used in the magna carta . it was used to mean the laws of the kingdom . this was distinct from roman law or civil law . in the united states , the constitution declares it is the `` supreme law of the land .
