Turgay Şeren    turgay şeren turgay sabit şeren ( 15 may 1932 – 6 july 2016 ) was a turkish footballer . he was the former goalkeeper of galatasaray . he played at galatasaray between 1947 and 1966 and was capped 52 times for turkey , including two matches at the 1954 fifa world cup . şeren was awarded a testimonial match by the club in 1967 in istanbul .
