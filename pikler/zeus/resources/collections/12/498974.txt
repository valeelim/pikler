Gertrude Weaver     gertrude weaver ( july 4 , 1898 â€“ april 6 , 2015 ) was an american supercentenarian . she was born in arkansas near the border with texas . weaver was the verified oldest living person in the world from april 1 to april 6 , 2015. she became the oldest living person in the united states upon the death of dina manfredini on december 17 , 2012 , and became the oldest living person in the world on april 1 , 2015 following the death of japanese woman misao okawa . at the time of her death in april 2015 , she was the seventh verified oldest person ever , and the third oldest person ever from the united states , after sarah knauss and lucy hannah . on april 6 , 2015 , gertrude weaver died in silver oaks health and rehabilitation center at the age of 116 from pneumonia .
