Ángel de Andrés López    ángel de andrés lópez ángel de andrés lópez ( 23 october 1951 – 4 may 2016 ) was a spanish actor . he appeared in more than 60 movies and television shows between 1977 and 2015. he was known for his roles as antonio in `` what have i done to deserve this ? '' ( 1984 ) and as cheyenne in `` 800 bullets ( 2002 ) . on 4 may 2016 , andrés lópez died at his home in miraflores de la sierra , madrid , spain from a heart attack , aged 64 .
