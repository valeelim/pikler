Sporosarcina     sporosarcina is a genus of bacteria . the cells of `` sporosarcina '' are either shaped like a rod or like a bowl ( coccoid ) . most species are able to move ( motile ) . all species of `` sporosarcina '' are capable of aerobic respiration using oxygen . some species have the enzyme urease and can break down urea . the species `` s. ureae '' is common in the soil where cattle live .
