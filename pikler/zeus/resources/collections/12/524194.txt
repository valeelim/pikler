Casualty (TV series)    casualty ( tv series ) casualty is a british television show aired on the bbc since 6 september 1986. it is the longest-running emergency medical drama television series in the world . it is filmed in cardiff . the 1000th episode of `` casualty '' aired on 25 june 2016 , episode 39 of series 30. the series was created by jeremy brock and paul unwin , as a response to the margaret thatcher era . `` casualty '' is set in the fictional holby city hospital , in the fictional county of wyvern , in the south-west of england .
