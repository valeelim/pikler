Vlada Divljan     vladimir `` vlada '' divljan ( serbian cyrillic : владимир дивљан ; 10 may 1958 – 4 march 2015 ) , was a serbian singer and songwriter . he was known as the frontman for the serbian and former yugoslav rock band idoli . he died from appendix cancer . other websites . - `` ludvig i steva '' , interview , vreme weekly news magazine - youtube - vis idoli at youtube
