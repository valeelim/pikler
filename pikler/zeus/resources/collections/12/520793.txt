Ahmed Chalabi     ahmed abdel hadi chalabi ( ‎ ; 30 october 1944 – 3 november 2015 ) was an iraqi politician . he was interim minister of oil in iraq serving from 16 april 2005 to 1 january 2006. he served as deputy prime minister from 1 may 2005 to 20 may 2006. chalabi failed to win a seat in parliament in the december 2005 elections . chalabi died on 3 november 2015 from a heart attack at his home in kadhimiya , baghdad , aged 71 .
