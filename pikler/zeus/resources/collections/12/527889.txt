Rūsiņš Mārtiņš Freivalds     rūsiņš mārtiņš freivalds ( 10 november 1942 – 4 january 2016 ) was a latvian computer scientist and mathematician . he was a member of latvian academy of sciences from 1992. he discovered freivalds ' algorithm . he also taught at the university of latvia with students including daina taimina and andris ambainis . he was born in cesvaine and studied at moscow state university ( msu ) . freivalds died from a heart attack on 4 january 2016 in riga , aged 73 .
