CFA franc     the west african cfa franc is the currency of eight countries in west africa : benin , burkina faso , guinea-bissau , ivory coast , mali , niger , sénégal and togo . cfa means communauté financière d'afrique ( `` financial community of africa '' ) or communauté financière africaine ( `` african financial community '' ) . the central african cfa franc has the same value as the west african cfa franc .
