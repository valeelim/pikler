Allan MacEachen     allan joseph maceachen , oc ( born july 6 , 1921 in inverness , nova scotia ) is a retired canadian politician . he was the first deputy prime minister of canada , serving from 1977 to 1979 and 1980 to 1984 during pierre trudeau tenure as prime minister . in 2008 , he was made an officer of the order of canada . other websites . - allan maceachen
