Émile Marie Laurent    émile marie laurent emile marie laurent ( 1 october 1852 - october 1930 ) was the prefecture of police of paris from september 1914 to june 1917. laurent was born on 1 october 1852 in brest , france . he served in the military from from 25 october 1875 to 5 october 1877. he was appointed as the prefecture of police of paris on 2 september 1914. he retired on 5 june 1917. he died in october 1930 in paris .
