World Esperanto Association     world esperanto association ( in esperanto , uea ) is the largest organization of esperanto speakers . its main office is in rotterdam , netherlands and it also has an office at the united nations building in new york city . swiss journalist hector hodler with other people founded the world esperanto association on 28 april in 1908. today it has got around 6.000 individual members and 70 country organizations . other websites . - official website
