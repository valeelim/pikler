Military Cross     the military cross ( mc ) is the third-level military award given to officers and ( since 1993 ) other ranks of the british armed forces . previously it was also awarded to officers of other commonwealth countries . the mc is granted in recognition of `` an act or acts of exemplary gallantry during active operations against the enemy on land to all members , of any rank in our armed forces '' . in 1979 , the queen approved a proposal that some awards , including the military cross , could be awarded posthumously .
