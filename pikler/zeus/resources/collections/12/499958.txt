Eduardo Galeano     eduardo hughes galeano ( 3 september 1940 – 13 april 2015 ) was a uruguayan journalist , writer and novelist . his best-known works are `` las venas abiertas de américa latina '' ( open veins of latin america , 1971 ) and `` memoria del fuego '' ( memory of fire trilogy , 19826 ) . galeano died in montevideo , uruguay from lung cancer , aged 74 .
