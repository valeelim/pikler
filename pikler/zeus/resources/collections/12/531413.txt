Planet Asia     jason green ( better known as planet asia ; born october 24 , 1976 ) is an american rapper . he is known for being part of the hip hop duo cali agents . he is also well known for producing many mixtapes . he was with interscope records . however , he was not promoted like more popular interscope rappers 50 cent and eminem . he was nominated for a grammy award in 2002 for the song `` w '' by mystic . he was born jason green in fresno , california .
