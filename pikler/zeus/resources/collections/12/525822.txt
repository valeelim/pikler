Meyerasaurus     meyerasaurus is an extinct genus of plesiosaur . an almost complete fossil was found in holzmaden , baden-württemberg , southeastern germany . it was a medium-sized , 3.35 m ( 11.0 ft ) long , pliosaur . the specimen was collected from shale over 150 years ago . the shale dates to the early jurassic , about 183–180 million years ago ( mya ) . the specimen was described as a species of `` plesiosaurus '' a hundred years ago . it got its new name in 2010. most members of its family , the rhomaleosauridae , have been found in england . they were found in the lower blue lias deposits which date to the earliest jurassic , just above the boundary with the triassic .
