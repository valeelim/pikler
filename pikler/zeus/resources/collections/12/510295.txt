Trey Gowdy     trey gowdy ( born august 22 , 1964 ) is the u.s. representative for south carolina 's 4th congressional district since 2011. he currently chairs the house benghazi committee . gowdy is a member of the republican party . gowdy was born on august 22 , 1964 in greenville , south carolina . in 1982 he graduated from spartanburg high school , spartanburg , south carolina . in 1986 he graduated from baylor university with a bachelor of arts degree . gowdy graduated from university of south carolina school of law . he was an assistant u.s. attorney from 1994 to 2000. gowdy was then elected state district attorney in south carolina . he was elected to the united states house of representatives in 2010. on may 4 , 2015 he was named to head the special house panel to investigate the 2012 benghazi attack . he is married to terri gowdy . they have two children . other websites . - trey gowdy , u.s. representative , home page
