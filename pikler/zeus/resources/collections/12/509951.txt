Canadian Championship     the canadian championship ( also called the amway canadian championship ) is an association football tournament for professional canadian teams . it was created in 2008. the winner of the tournament qualifies for the concacaf champions league , and gets the voyageurs cup . as of 2015 , the tournament was with toronto fc , vancouver whitecaps fc , montreal impact , fc edmonton and ottawa fury fc . toronto fc has won 4 titles , which is the best performance from any team in the tournament . other websites . - official website
