Emma Cohen     emma cohen ( born emmanuela beltrán rahola ; november 21 , 1946 – july 11 , 2016 ) was a spanish actress , director , producer and writer . she appeared in many spanish language movies . she was known for roles in `` the glass ceiling '' , `` voyage to nowhere '' , and in `` the grandfather '' . cohen was married to actor fernando fernán gómez until his death in 2007. cohen died on july 11 , 2016 of cancer in madrid at the age of 69 .
