Music (Madonna song)    music ( madonna song ) `` music '' is a dance-pop and electropop ballad by singer madonna . its lyrics are about how music can bring people together . `` music '' was released in august 2000. it peaked at # 1 on `` billboard '' hot 100. it also hit # 1 in australia , canada , new zealand and italy . the song received many positive reviews from critics . it was nominated at the grammy awards for record of the year and best female pop vocal performance .
