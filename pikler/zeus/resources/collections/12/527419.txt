Agathemera     agathemera ( the chinchemolle ) is a phasmid insect from chile and argentina . it is famous for its intense odor , which is its main defence . it is notable for its sexual dimorphism . females are much bigger than males , at 75mm long and 16mm wide in the thorax . this phasmid is an evolutionary isolate . it is alone in its suborder , and there are few publications about it .
