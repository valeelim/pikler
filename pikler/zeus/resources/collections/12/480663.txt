Lönsboda GoIF     lönsboda goif is a sports club in lönsboda in sweden . it was established on 24 september 1930 as a merger out of lönsboda if , lönsboda gf , lönsboda ck and lönsboda pingpongklubb . the women 's soccer team played five seasons in the swedish top division between 1978-1982. other websites . - official website
