Black Magic (Little Mix song)    black magic ( little mix song ) `` black magic '' is a dance pop song by the girl group little mix . the song is of using magic to promote love . it was released as the lead single from their third studio album `` get weird '' . the song has many versions . one of them is four minutes long . upon its release , `` black magic '' was liked by music critics . the song was influenced by 1980s music . on the uk singles chart , the song reached # 1. it also reached the top 10 in some other countries . however , the song only reached # 68 on `` billboard '' hot 100 .
