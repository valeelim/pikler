Köniz     köniz ( ) is a city in the administrative district of bern-mittelland in the canton of berne in switzerland . it has population of 38,793 inhabitants ( 2008 ) , the municipality is one of the 15 most populous cities in switzerland . the official language of köniz is ( the swiss variety of standard ) german , but the main spoken language is the local variant of the alemmanic swiss german dialect . other websites . - official website
