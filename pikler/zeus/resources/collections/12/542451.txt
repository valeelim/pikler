British Petroleum     british petroleum , now called `` bp plc '' , is one of the world 's seven biggest oil and gas companies . its headquarters are in london . the company grew out of the old anglo-persian oil company , which was founded in 1908. it was privatised by the british government in stages between 1979 and 1987. references . - bamberg , james h 1994/2000 . `` the history of the british petroleum company '' . vols 2 & 3 of 3 vols , cambridge university press . isbn 9780521785150
