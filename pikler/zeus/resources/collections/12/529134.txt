Bogusław Kaczyński     bogusław kaczyński ( may 2 , 1942 – january 21 , 2016 ) was a polish classical music journalist . he was born in biała podlaska , poland . he was the author of a classical recording series called `` the golden collection '' . he also hosted musical competitions , including the frederic chopin piano competition , the henryk wieniawski violin competition , and the new year 's concerts in vienna . he has been ranked among the top 10 polish television personalities by the polityka magazine . kaczyński died in warsaw , poland of a stroke on january 21 , 2016 , aged 73. other websites . - bogusław kaczyński – homepage - warszawa . bogusław kaczyński kończy 65 lat – e-teatr.pl
