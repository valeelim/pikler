Mircea Albulescu     mircea albulescu ( ; real name iorgu constantin albulescu ; 4 october 1934 – 8 april 2016 ) was a romanian actor , university professor ( doctor of arts ) , journalist , poet , and writer . he was a member of the writers ' union of romania ( `` uniunea scriitorilor '' ) . he was known for his roles in `` michael the brave '' , `` capcana mercenarilor '' , and `` semne în pustiu '' . he was born in bucharest . he graduated from the secondary school of architecture in 1952 , and in 1956 he graduated from the institute of theatrical and cinematographic art in bucharest . albulescu died on 8 april 2016 in bucharest , romania from heart failure , aged 81 .
