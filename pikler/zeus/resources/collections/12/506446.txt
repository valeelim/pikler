Pedro Zerolo     pedro gonzález zerolo ( 20 july 1960 – 9 june 2015 ) was a spanish lawyer , politician , activist and town councillor . he was a member of the spanish socialist workers ' party . zerolo , who was gay , helped spain become the first country to recognise same-sex marriage . zerolo was born in caracas , venezuela . he married jesús santos , his partner of ten years , in a civil ceremony in october 2005. zerolo died from pancreatic cancer on 9 june 2015 in madrid . he was 55 .
