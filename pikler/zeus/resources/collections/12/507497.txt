Dakhla-Oued ad Dahab     the region of dakhla-oued ad dahab is one of the 12 new regions of morocco established by the territorial division of 2015. its perimeter is the same as the former region of oued ad dahab ( ) . its capital is dakhla . this region lies on the disputed territory of western sahara . oued ad dahab has 2 provinces : - aousserd province - oued ad dahab province other websites . - official website
