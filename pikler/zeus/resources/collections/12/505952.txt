2015 FIFA Women's World Cup    2015 fifa women 's world cup the 2015 fifa women 's world cup was the seventh fifa women 's world cup , hosted by canada . the tournament began on 6 june and ended on 5 july . in a rematch of the 2011 final , the defending champions japan met the united states . the usa scored four goals in the first 16 minutes , with three by carli lloyd , on their way to a 5–2 win and their third women 's world cup title . the cities of vancouver , edmonton , winnipeg , ottawa , montreal and moncton hosted matches . some believe that artificial turf causes more injuries than normal grass . more than 50 players have protested the use of artificial turf , saying that it is sexist . they filed a lawsuit against fifa , however , they dropped the lawsuit in january of 2015. statistics . top scorers . - 6 goals - célia šašić - carli lloyd - 5 goals - anja mittag - 3 goals - kyah simon - gaëlle enganamouit - fara williams - marie-laure delie - eugénie le sommer - ada hegerberg - ramona bachmann - fabienne humm - 2 goals - lisa de vanna
