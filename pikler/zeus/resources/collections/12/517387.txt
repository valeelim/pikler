Nordland II     nordland ii is the eighth studio albumby the swedish extreme metal band bathory . it was released on 31 march 2003 through black mark productions . the album is mainly viking metal , so the songs have lyrics about norse mythology . the album was the second in a planned four-album long set . quorthon only lived long enough to complete two albums in the set . people . - quorthon – all instruments - kristian wåhlin – album cover artwork
