Uwe Friedrichsen     uwe friedrichsen ( 27 may 1934 – 30 april 2016 ) was a german movie and television actor . he was born in hamburg , germany . he was known for his roles in `` faust '' , `` john kling '' , `` maximilian von mexiko '' , `` sesamstraße '' , and `` schwarz rot gold '' . friedrichsen died in hamburg , germany from complications of pneumonia on 30 april 2016 , aged 81. other websites . - scherf agency berlin
