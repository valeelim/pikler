Abadia dos Dourados     abadia dos dourados is a brazilian city in the state of minas gerais . its estimated that the population in 2007 was 6,556 people living in a total area of 894 km² . the city is located in the region of the alto paranaíba , south of the paranaíba river and west of the emborcação reservoir . it is in the valley of the upper reaches of the dourados river . neighboring cities are : catalão ( n ) , monte carmelo ( e ) , coromandel ( s ) , and douradoquara ( w ) . distances . - coromandel : 24 km - monte carmelo : 20 km - patrocínio : 71 km - uberlândia : 160 km - belo horizonte : 547 km
