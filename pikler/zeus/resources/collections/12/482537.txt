Ibrahim Biogradlić     ibrahim `` ibro '' biogradlić ( 8 march 1931 – 20 february 2015 ) was a bosnian footballer . he was born in sarajevo , kingdom of yugoslavia . he played at 1956 summer olympics for sfr yugoslavia , winning a silver medal . he was capped 646 times for fk sarajevo , and is the club 's record holder with the most appearances . he died on 20 february 2015 in sarajevo , bosnia and herzegovina after a long illness , aged 83 .
