Walther Leisler Kiep     walther gottlieb louis leisler kiep ( 5 january 1926 – 9 may 2016 ) was a german politician . he was a member of the christian democratic union ( cdu ) . he was a member of the bundestag between 1965 and 1976 and again from 1980 to 1982. after switching to state-level politics , he served as minister of economy ( 1976–77 ) and minister of finance ( 1976–80 ) in lower saxony under ernst albrecht . in 1982 , kiep was the leading candidate for the cdu in two state elections in hamburg . from 1971 until 1992 , he was treasurer of his party at the federal level . kiep died on 9 may 2016 in his home in kronberg im taunus in hesse of pneumonia , aged 90 .
