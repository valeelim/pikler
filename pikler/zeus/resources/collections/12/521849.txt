Frankee     nicole francine aiello ( born june 9 , 1983 ) is an american r & b singer-songwriter and model known by her stage name , frankee . she grew up with her parents , matthew and francine , and her two siblings , elder brother matthew jr. and younger sister monique . nicole was given the nickname `` frankee , '' which came from her middle name ( and is also her grandfather 's nickname ) . she is best known for her debut single `` f.u.r.b . ( fuck you right back ) '' which is a answer back to fellow american r & b singer-songwriter eamons debut single `` fuck it ( i do n't want you back ) '' . `` f.u.r.b . ( fuck you right back ) '' reached number one in the united kingdom in may 2004 , and australia in june 2004. she then released her debut studio album later on that year . she then left her label in 2006 and signed with big management she has also modelled for `` fhm '' and `` maxim '' magazines . discography . albums . - 2004 : `` the good , the bad , the ugly '' - 2006 : watch me
