Slither.io     slither.io is an online multiplayer video game . it is played on a web browser or through an app . the game was started by steve howse in 2016. the game is played in the role of a snake eating 2-dimensional dots on a grid . each snake is another online player . when any snake dies it turns into dots equal to their score before death . it is based on the 2015 game `` agar.io '' the main difference is that snakes die whether their head or body collides with another snake . players can also die by falling off the edge , however , their score will not be released into dots .
