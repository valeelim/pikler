Peter Lundblad     gustaf peter lundblad ( 26 august 1950 - 22 december 2015 ) was a swedish singer and songwriter . he was known for his 1986 song `` ta mig till havet '' . lundblad started his career in the band 'the most remarkable nailband ' . lundblad died of prostate cancer on 22 december 2015 in stockholm at the age of 65. other websites . - aftonbladet.se - official website
