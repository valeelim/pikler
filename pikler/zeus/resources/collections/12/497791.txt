Puttin' On the Ritz    puttin ' on the ritz `` puttin ' on the ritz '' is a popular ballad . it was written by irving berlin . he wrote this song in may 1927. it was first published in december 1929. the song is about poor people spending money for a wonderful time . fred astaire first sang the song . in the early 1980s , taco released a synthpop version of `` puttin ' on the ritz '' . his version had a tap dance inside it . it hit # 4 on the u.s. `` billboard '' hot 100 .
