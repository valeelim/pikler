Rodrigo Espíndola     rodrigo federico espíndola ( 29 july 1989 – 13 may 2016 ) was an argentine professional footballer . he played for chacarita juniors , racing club and most notably nueva chicago . he played as a defender . he was born in monte grande , argentina . he began his career in 2009. espíndola died on 13 may 2016 after being shot during a robbery in monte grande , argentina , aged 26 .
