Putrefaction     putrefaction is a natural process of decay . microorganisms break down organic materials . usually it occurs after the death of a plant or an animal . the process consists of many steps . the total time it takes is dependent on various factors , such as the availability of oxygen . putrefaction involves fermentation . putrefaction is important , because elements such as nitrogen , phosphorus and sulfur which are bound in the dead matter are converted into a form that is usable by plants .
