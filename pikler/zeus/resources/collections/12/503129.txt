Jef Geeraerts     jef geeraerts ( jozef adriaan anna geeraerts , 23 february 1930 - 11 may 2015 ) was a belgian writer . he was born in antwerp . he decided to become a writer . he went to the university of brussels to study germanic languages . he was known for his books `` gangreen 1 '' ( `` black venus '' ) , `` gangreen 2 '' ( `` de goede moordenaar '' ) , `` gangreen 3 '' ( `` het teken van de hond '' ) and `` gangreen 4 '' ( `` het zevende zegel '' ) . geeraerts died of a heart attack in ghent at the age of 85. other websites . - jef geeraerts
