Naan     naan bread is a type of bread made with flour . it is a flatbread that is baked in the oven . naan bread often looks like a tear drop . it is often covered in herbs and spices to change the taste . naan bread is made from basic bread ingredients like wheat flour , yeast , salt , and butter or ghee . it also is made with yogurt and milk . the yogurt is what makes naan bread taste so much different than other kinds of bread .
