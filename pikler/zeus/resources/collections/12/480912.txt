Roosevelt Island     roosevelt island is a narrow island in the east river in new york city . to the west is manhattan island , and to the east is the new york city borough of queens . roosevelt island is part of the borough of manhattan . a vertical lift bridge stands there known as the roosevelt island bridge . the island is about long . the widest point on this island is about wide . the island 's total area is . according to the us census of 2000 , 9,520 people lived on the island . the roosevelt island operating corporation guessed that about 12,000 lived here in 2007. roosevelt island was called minnehanonck by the lenape native americans . later , it was called varkens eylandt ( hog island ) by people who lived in new netherland . during britain 's colonization of the united states , the island was named blackwells island . it was known as welfare island when hospitals were opened on the island between 1921 to 1971. it was renamed roosevelt island in 1971 , after u.s. president franklin d. roosevelt .
