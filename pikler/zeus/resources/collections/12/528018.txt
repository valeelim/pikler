Rescue 911     rescue 911 was an american reality-based television series . the show premiered on cbs on april 18 , 1989 and ended on august 27 , 1996. the series was hosted by william shatner . it carried reenactments . it also included footage of real emergencies . it was never intended as a teaching tool . however some viewers used the knowledge they got watching the show . there were two specials : `` 100 lives saved '' and `` 200 lives saved '' . they were dedicated to viewers who wrote cbs with their stories about watching `` rescue 911 '' . the show carried two episodes on hurricane hugo . it also had episodes on the 1986 new year 's eve fire in puerto rico . it covered a june 1990 tornado and a 1987 amtrak train wreck .
