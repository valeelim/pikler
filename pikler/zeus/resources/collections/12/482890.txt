Holly Smale     holly smale ( born 7 december 1981 ) is the a british writer . she wrote the best-selling , award-winning series `` geek girl '' . smale won the 2014 waterstones children 's book prize . she was shortlisted for the roald dahl funny prize . she was recruited by a modelling agency at the age of 15. she modeled for 2 years before deciding to become a writer .
