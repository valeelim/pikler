Football Association of Iceland     the football association of iceland ( icelandic : `` knattspyrnusamband íslands '' ; or the ksí ) is the main association football body in iceland . it was founded on 26 march 1947 , and joined fifa on the same year . it also joined uefa in 1954. the football association of iceland organizes and controls the úrvalsdeild , iceland national football team and iceland women 's national football team . their headquarters are in reykjavík . other websites . - official website
