The Crazy Ones     the crazy ones is an american sitcom created by david e. kelley . it starred robin williams and sarah michelle gellar . the single-camera series aired on cbs on september 26 , 2013. it was based in chicago , illinois . it was about simon roberts ( williams ) who is an advertising executive and his daughter , sydney roberts ( gellar ) also an executive working together . it was cancelled on april 17 , 2014 .
