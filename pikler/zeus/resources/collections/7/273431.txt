Dolomite     dolomite is the name of a sedimentary carbonate rock and a mineral , both composed of calcium magnesium carbonate camg ( co ) as a mineral , it is found in crystals . dolomite rock ( or 'dolostone ' ) is mostly made of the mineral dolomite . limestone that is partially replaced by dolomite is referred to as `` dolomitic limestone '' . in the old u.s. geologic literature it is called `` magnesian limestone '' . dolomite was first described in 1791 for exposures in the dolomite alps of northern italy .
