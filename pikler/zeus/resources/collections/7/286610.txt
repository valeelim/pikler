Courtney Thorne-Smith     courtney thorne-smith ( born 8 november 1967 ) is an american actress . she is best known for her television work . her television roles include alison parker on `` melrose place '' , georgia thomas on the sitcom `` ally mcbeal '' and lyndsey mcelroy on `` two and a half men '' . she has acted in many movies and other television programs . thorne-smith was born in san francisco , california .
