Dale Jarrett     dale arnold jarrett ( born november 26 , 1956 ) is a retired american racecar driver . jarret used to drive in the nascar sprint cup series . he won the 1999 sprint cup championship and the 1993 daytona 500. jarrett has been called one of nascar 's 50 greatest drivers . dale jarrett retired in 2008 and now does commentary for espn .
