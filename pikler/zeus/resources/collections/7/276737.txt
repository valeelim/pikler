Dave Freudenthal     david duane `` dave '' freudenthal ( born october 12 , 1950 in thermopolis , wyoming ) is an american politician . he was the 31st governor of wyoming . freudenthal was re-elected to his second term on november 7 , 2006 , and announced on march 4 , 2010 , that he would not attempt to seek a third term as governor of wyoming . freudenthal is married to nancy d. freudenthal who currently serves as a judge on the united states district court for the district of wyoming and they have four children : donald , hillary , bret and katie .
