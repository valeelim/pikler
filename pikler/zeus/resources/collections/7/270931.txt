Ann Widdecombe     ann noreen widdecombe ( born 4 october 1947 in bath , somerset , england ) is a british former politician . she was the conservative party member of parliament ( mp ) for the constituency of maidstone and the weald from 1987 to 2010. widdecombe is the daughter of a ministry of defence civil servant james murray widdecombe and rita n plummer . she attended the royal navy school in singapore , and a convent school in bath . she then read latin at birmingham university and later attended lady margaret hall , oxford , to read philosophy , politics and economics ( ppe ) . she worked for unilever ( 1973–75 ) . she was then an administrator at the university of london ( 1975–87 ) before entering parliament . she was first elected to the house of commons at the 1987 general election . from 1995 to 1997 , she was the minister of state for prisons . ann widdecombe served in william hague 's shadow cabinet from 1997 to 2001. widdecombe retired from politics at the 2010 general election . throughout her political career , widdecombe was socially conservative and converted from the church of england to roman catholicism in 1992 after the church of england voted to allow female priests .
