Bismuth(III) chloride    bismuth ( iii ) chloride bismuth ( iii ) chloride , also known as bismuth trichloride , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is bicl . it has bismuth and chloride ions in it . the bismuth is in its +3 oxidation state . bismuth ( iii ) chloride is a pale yellow solid . it reacts with water to make bismuth oxychloride and hydrochloric acid . it dissolves in acids like hydrochloric acid . it reacts with reducing agents to make bismuth metal . it is made by reacting bismuth with chlorine . it can also be made by dissolving bismuth ( iii ) oxide in hydrochloric acid . bismuth can be dissolved in nitric acid to make bismuth ( iii ) nitrate , which reacts with sodium chloride to make bismuth ( iii ) chloride . it is used as a catalyst in organic chemistry . it is a good source of bismuth and is used to make other bismuth compounds . bismuth ( iii ) chloride can cause burns on skin because it makes hydrochloric acid when it reacts with water . it irritates the gastrointestinal tract . related pages . - bismuth ( iii ) bromide - bismuthine
