Ubar     ubar is a ruined city in oman . english adventurer ranulph fiennes discovered the city in 1992. according to nicolas clapp , this probably is the lost city iram of the pillars the qu'ran talks about . today , ruins are left , which can be reached in a four-wheel drive . part of the area forms the ceiling to underground vaults , which have collapsed . these vaults hold water supplies for a nearby settlement , which houses a small museum .
