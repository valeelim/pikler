Jesus Christ Pose     `` jesus christ pose '' is a 1991 heavy metal song by the american grunge band soundgarden , from their third studio album `` badmotorfinger '' , which was released in 1991. the song was written by matt cameron , chris cornell , ben shepherd and kim thayil and is a downloadable song in the video game , rock band . the music video for jesus christ pose received a lot of controversy from christians since many of the band 's listeners were christians and they felt that the band was showing the song and music video as anti-christian .
