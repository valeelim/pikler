Amphora     in ancient greece and ancient rome , amphoras were used to transport liquids or grain . amphores had two handles , used for carrying . amphores existed in different sizes , between 5 litres and 50 litres . they were made of clay , or metal . cargo ships for liquids were full of amphorae . most of the amphoras that now exist were made in old times of ancient greece and ancient rome . however , many were made long before then . some amphoras are as old as 4500 bce . some varieties have been found in ancient china .
