Castro     castro is a chilean city with 39.366 inhabitants ( 2002 census ) . castro was discovered by the spanish conqueror martín ruiz de gamboa on 1567 castro is well known for its `` palafitos '' , traditional wooden stilt houses that are common on the chiloé island . some of them are preserved in in the west of the town in a bay named `` fiordo de castro '' . in gamboa , there is also an interesting wooden chapel . other websites . - castro municipality - history & legends of castro
