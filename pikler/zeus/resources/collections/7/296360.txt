Madame de Lafayette     marie-madeleine pioche de la vergne , countess of la fayette ( or lafayette ) was a french writer . she is commonly known as madame de lafayette . she was born 18th march 1634 in paris , and died there , on 26 may 1693. in 1678 , she published the novel `` la princesse de clèves '' ( the princess of clèves ) , which is regarded as one of the first psychological novels .
