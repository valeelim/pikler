Andy &amp; Lucas    andy & amp ; lucas andy & lucas are a spanish flamenco band formed by andrés morales and lucas gonzález . they introduced their first cd in 2003. andrés was born on april 4 , 1982 and lucas gonzález on september 28 , 1982. they lived in cádiz ( la laguna ) and they went to the same school . on may 26 , 2003 they edited their first album which had 13 songs . the single `` son de amores '' became the summer song in spain . they sold more than 500,000 copies around the world . their second album was called `` desde mi barrio '' and it was edited on 2004. their third album was `` ganas de vivir '' . it had 12 songs . andy wrote seven of the songs , and lucas wrote five .
