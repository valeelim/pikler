Sewage     sewage is the liquid waste produced by us all when using the toilet , having a bath or shower or washing clothes and dishes . in some places it also includes rain-water falling on houses and roads . in big towns and cities it can also have liquid waste from factories in it . it flows in pipes called sewers to be cleaned up at a sewage treatment works . sewage is mostly water but it also has many microbes some of which can be harmful . it can have many chemicals in it which can also cause harm to animals and humans .
