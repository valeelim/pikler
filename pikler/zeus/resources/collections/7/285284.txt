Alborz Mountains     alborz ( also written alburz , elburz or elborz ) , is a mountain range in northern iran . its borders start at azerbaijan and armenia in the northwest of iran and reach to the southern end of the caspian sea . the mountain range ends in the east at the borders of turkmenistan and afghanistan . tehran , the capital of iran is at the foot of the alborz mountain range . mount damavand , the tallest mountain in the middle east , is located in this mountain range .
