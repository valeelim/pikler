Sailing     sailing is the craft of controlling a boat that uses the force of the wind as a source of movement . sailing boats were very important to move cargo and people . starting at the end of the 19th century they were gradually replaced by the boats that used steam to generate motion . today , sailing is a hobby some people have . in some regions of the world , sailing vessels still keep their importance . many military forces also have sailing vessels for the purpose of training . sailing is also a competitive sport . it is one of the sports in the olympic games .
