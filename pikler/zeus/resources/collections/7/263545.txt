Manganese(IV) fluoride    manganese ( iv ) fluoride manganese ( iv ) fluoride is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is mnf . it contains manganese and fluoride ions . the manganese ions are in the +4 oxidation state . it is a light blue-green solid . it is very reactive . it can form very reactive fluorine gas easily . it can be made by reacting fluorine or strong oxidizing agents containing fluorine with several manganese compounds . it was used to make fluorine without using electrolysis . it is much more reactive than manganese ( iv ) oxide . related pages . - manganese ( iii ) oxide - manganese ( iii ) fluoride - manganese ( vii ) oxide
