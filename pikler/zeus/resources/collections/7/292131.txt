Tom Glavine     tom glavine ( born march 25 , 1966 ) is a former baseball player who played pitcher . he won more than 300 games in his career and has won the second-most games in national league history ( behind his former teammate greg maddux ) . he won one world series with the atlanta braves in 1995 , and was the world series most valuable player ( mvp ) . most of his career was with the braves ( from 1987 to 2002 and in 2008 ) , but he also played with the new york mets from 2003 to 2007. as well as being the mvp of the 1995 world series , glavine also won the national league ( nl ) cy young award in 1991 and 1998 .
