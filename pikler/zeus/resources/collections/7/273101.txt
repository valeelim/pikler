Maria Feodorovna (Dagmar of Denmark)    maria feodorovna ( dagmar of denmark ) maria feodorovna ( 26 november 1847 – 13 october 1928 ) , born princess dagmar of schleswig-holstein-sonderburg-glücksburg and later princess dagmar of denmark , was the empress consort of russia . her son , nicholas ii of russia , was the last tsar of russia . she was born in copenhagen , denmark .
