New York Herald     the new york herald was a daily newspaper based in new york city . the paper was circulated from 1835 to 1924. the first issue of the paper was published by james gordon bennett , sr. on may 6 , 1835. by 1845 , it was the most popular and most bought daily newspaper in the united states . in 1861 , it had sold about 84,000 copies and called itself `` the most largely circulated journal in the world . '' bennett said that the point of newspapers `` is not to instruct but to startle . '' bennett 's politics influenced the articles in the `` herald '' . he tended to be anti-catholic , but not particularly anti-immigrant like most people who did not like the catholics in new york at the time . during the american civil war , it was a staunch supporter of the democratic party . when the `` herald '' was still controlled by bennett , it was considered to be the most sensationalist of the leading new york papers at the time . related pages . - the new york zoo hoax
