Andrew Cuomo     andrew mark cuomo ( born december 6 , 1957 ) is the 56th and current governor of new york state . he was 64th new york state attorney general and the 11th united states secretary of housing and urban development under bill clinton . he was born in queens . his father mario cuomo was also governor of new york and andrew worked for him . cuomo was able to make same-sex marriage legal in new york , made a law that was tough on guns , and proposed a law that would allow businesses not to pay taxes for some time in some parts of the state . he also said he would not end the moratorium ( delay ) on hydrofracking , a special process that extracts ( takes out ) oil and natural gas so that they can be used for fuel and other things . he was reelected governor in 2014 .
