No. 427 Squadron RCAF    no . 427 squadron rcaf the 427 special operations aviation squadron ( `` 427soas '' ' ) ( french:427 escadron d'opérations spéciales d'aviation ) is a helicopter squadron of canada responsible to provide support to the joint task force 2 and the canadian special operations regiment . it operates the ch-146 griffon . the squadron is part of the canadian special operations forces command of the canadian forces .
