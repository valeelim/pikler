Madge Adam     madge gertrude adam ( born : march 6 , 1912 ; died : august 25 , 2001 ) was an english solar astronomer . she was `` internationally known for her work on the nature of sunspots and on their magnetic fields . '' she worked at the university of oxford in the department of astrophysics from 1937-1979. she is a member of the royal astronomical society .
