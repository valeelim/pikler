Copper(II) bromide    copper ( ii ) bromide copper ( ii ) bromide , also known as cupric bromide , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is cubr . it has copper and bromide ions in it . the copper is in its +2 oxidation state . copper ( ii ) bromide is a grayish-black solid . most copper ( ii ) compounds , except for the oxide , are bright blue or green . it can be reduced to copper ( i ) bromide . it can be made by dissolving copper ( ii ) oxide in hydrobromic acid . it can also be made by reacting copper and bromine . copper ( ii ) bromide is used in lasers . it is also used to add bromide ions to other chemical compounds in organic chemistry . related pages . - copper ( ii ) chloride - copper ( i ) iodide
