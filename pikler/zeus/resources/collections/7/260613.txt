Shaun Toub     shaun toub is an iranian-american movie and television actor . he is perhaps best known for his role as farhad in the 2004 movie `` crash '' , as rahim khan in the movie `` the kite runner '' , as yinsen in the movie `` iron man '' and as `` iroh '' in the movie `` the last airbender '' . other websites . - shaun toub cover story in ocpc magazine
