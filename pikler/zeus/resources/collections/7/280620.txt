Hell's Pit    hell 's pit hell 's pit is the ninth studio album by american horrorcore band , insane clown posse . it was released on august 31 , 2004. the producer of the album was mike puwal . the album was the final of the `` joker cards '' chapter of the dark carnival mythology . the album peaked at number 12 on the billboard 200 , peaked number 1 on the top independent albums and peaked at number 1 on the top internet albums .
