Manganese carbonate     manganese carbonate , also known as manganese ( ii ) carbonate , is a chemical compound . it is a brownish powder with a chemical formula of mnco . it can be heated to produce manganese ( ii ) oxide and carbon dioxide . it is made by reacting a soluble manganese compound such as manganese ( ii ) chloride with sodium carbonate . related pages . - manganese - manganese ( iv ) oxide - potassium carbonate
