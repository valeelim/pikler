Caper     the caper ( `` capparis spinosa '' ) , is a perennial winter-deciduous species that bears rounded , fleshy leaves and big white to pinkish-white flowers . a caper is also the pickled bud of this plant . caper bush is present in almost all the countries and is included in the floristic composition of most of them but whether it is indigenous to this region is uncertain . although the flora of the mediterranean region has considerable endemism , the caper bush could have originated in the tropics , and later been naturalized to the mediterranean basin .
