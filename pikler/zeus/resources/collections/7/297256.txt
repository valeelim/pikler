Holiday Inn     holiday inn is a brand of hotels . holiday inn is one of the world 's most recognized hotel brands . it is part of the intercontinental hotels group ( ihg ) . kemmons wilson had the idea for the business after a family road trip to washington , d.c.. during the trip , he was disappointed by the quality of roadside motels at that time . mr. wilson partnered with wallace e. johnson to build the first four motels on roads entering memphis , tennessee . eddie bluestein was the architect who designed these first hotels . he named the first one `` holiday inn '' after a bing crosby movie as a joke . other websites . - official website - holiday inn hotels review
