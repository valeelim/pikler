Choi Soo-young     choi soo-young ( ; hanja : ; japanese : チェ・スヨン ; born february 10 , 1990 ) is a south korean singer-songwriter , actress and tv presenter . she is a former member of the korean-japanese duo group `` route θ '' and a current member of the south korean girl group girls ' generation . sooyoung is the only asian woman in `` complex 's '' hottest '90s babies .
