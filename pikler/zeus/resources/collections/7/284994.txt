Maxime Vachier-Lagrave     maxime vachier-lagrave ( born 21 october 1990 ) is a french chess grandmaster and the 2009 world junior chess champion . mvl , as he is sometimes called , is at present their strongest player . he completed his final grandmaster norm in 2005 , at 14 years , 4 months . he got his norms in these tournaments : - paris championship 2004 : finished 3rd with 6½ points from nine rounds and a rating performance of 2703 - nao gm tournament 2004 : won the tournament with 6/9 and got a performance of 2605 - gm tournament of évry : from 21 to 27 february 2005 , finished second with 7½ and a performance of 2712
