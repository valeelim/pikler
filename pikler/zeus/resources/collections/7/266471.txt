Selenium monochloride     selenium monochloride , also known as selenium ( i ) chloride or diselenium dichloride , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is secl . it contains selenium in its +1 oxidation state . it also contains chloride ions . it is a reddish brown oily liquid . it can be made by reacting selenium , selenium dioxide , and hydrochloric acid . it can be made though by just reacting chlorine with selenium . another way to make it is reacting oleum and hydrochloric acid with selenium . it can be used to add selenium to other chemical compounds . related pages . - selenium ( iv ) chloride
