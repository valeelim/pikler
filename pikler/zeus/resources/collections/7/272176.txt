U.S. Route 2     u.s. route 2 is a highway that has two parts . the western part begins in everett , washington and ends in st. ignace , michigan . the eastern part begins in rouses point , new york and ends in houlton , maine . for much of its route , route 2 runs near the u.s.-canadian border . part of it follows the theodore roosevelt highway . it goes through glacier national park
