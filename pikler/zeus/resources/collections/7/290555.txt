Mark Boal     mark boal ( born 1973 ) is an american journalist , screenwriter and film producer . boal won 2 academy awards in 2009 for the hurt locker in the categories of best writing ( original screenplay ) and best picture . filmography . - `` the hurt locker '' ( screenplay , producer ) ( 2009 ) - `` in the valley of elah '' ( based on story by boal and paul haggis ) ( 2007 )
