Idaho State University     idaho state university is a public university in pocatello , idaho . the university has about 15,553 students . it was founded in 1901. the current president of the university is arthur c. vailas , ph.d. . the men 's athletics teams of the university are called the bengals . they have 15 varsity teams and they compete in the big sky conference . other websites . - idaho state university 's official website - idaho state bengals ' official athletics website
