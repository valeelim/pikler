Social justice     social justice is an idea of justice that affirms social equality against any kind of discrimination or abuse out of social class , social status or any other reasons . this idea is discussed by modern philosophers such as john rawls . it is a fundamental idea for international labour organization for universal and lasting peace ; and vienna declaration also affirm social justice as a subject and a purpose of education for human rights . social justice has been criticized by many people . related pages . - international labour organization - vienna declaration
