Strong Bad's Cool Game for Attractive People    strong bad 's cool game for attractive people strong bad 's cool game for attractive people ( abbreviated sbcg4ap ) is an episodic series of point-and-click adventure games developed by telltale games based on the homestar runner web cartoon and starring strong bad . the five episodes include homestar ruiner , strong badia the free , baddest of the bands , dangeresque 3 : the criminal projective , and 8-bit is enough .
