Freshwater Biological Association     the freshwater biological association , often shortened to the fba , is a british charity started in 1929. it aims are to promote freshwater biology . it publishes identification keys to freshwater plants and animals throughout the world , and also maintains a library of freshwater science . membership is open to anyone who is interested in freshwater science . the association 's main offices are in far sawrey , on the banks of windermere in cumbria . other websites . - the fba web-site - fba web-cam at far sawrey ferry landing stage
