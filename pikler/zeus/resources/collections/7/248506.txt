Bob Bodak     robert peter bodak ( born may 28 , 1961 in thunder bay , ontario ) is a canadian retired professional ice hockey player who played in the national hockey league for the calgary flames and hartford whalers . a career minor-leaguer , bodak played only three games with the flames in 1987–88 and one more with the whalers in 1989–90 . he did not record a point , but had 29 penalty minutes in his four games . other websites . - bodak 's legends of hockey profile
