Rob Klinkhammer     robert klinkhammer ( born august 12 , 1986 in lethbridge , alberta ) is a canadian professional ice hockey left winger . he currently plays for the edmonton oilers of the national hockey league ( nhl ) . he signed as a free agent with the rockford icehogs nhl affiliate , the chicago blackhawks , to a two-year entry level contract on june 8 , 2009. he was called up by the blackhawks on december 10 , 2010 to replace an injured patrick kane and debuted in a home game against the dallas stars which they won . he was traded to the ottawa senators on december 2 , 2011. he was signed by the coyotes as a free agent on july 3 , 2012 and played with their ahl farm team , the portland pirates during the 2012 nhl lockout .
