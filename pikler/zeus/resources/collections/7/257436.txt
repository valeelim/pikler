RZA     robert diggs ( born july 5 , 1969 ) is an american rapper and producer , as well as a member of the hip-hop group wu-tang clan , better known by his stage name , rza . he was a member of gravediggaz . he was known as rzarector . this is a pun on 'resurrector ' . rza has written two books . rza started his music career in 1991 when he released the ep `` ooh i love you rakeem '' . discography . - `` bobby digital in stereo '' ( 1998 ) - `` digital bullet '' ( 2001 ) - `` birth of a prince '' ( 2003 ) - `` digi snacks '' ( 2008 )
