Reticulated python     the reticulated python ( python reticulatus ) is a species of python found in southeast asia . adults can grow to 22.8 feet ( 6.95 m ) in length but normally grow to an average of 9.8-19.7 feet . they are the world 's longest snakes and longest reptile , but are not the most heavily built . like all pythons , they are non-venomous constrictors ( they squeeze their prey ) and normally not considered dangerous to humans . although large ones are powerful enough to kill an adult human , attacks are only sometimes reported .
