Randy Couture     randy duane couture ( born june 22 , 1963 in everett , washington ) is an american mixed martial artist , greco-roman wrestler and actor fighting for the ufc . couture has won the ufc heavyweight championship 3 times , the ufc light heavyweight championship 2 times and is a ufc hall of famer . couture also acted in the movie the expendables as `` toll road '' . other websites . - randy couture 's official website
