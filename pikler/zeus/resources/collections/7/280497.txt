Exposé (group)    exposé ( group ) exposé is an american pop-rock group . the group was formed in 1984. members of the group included ann curless , jeanette jurado and gioia bruno . they had success in the late 1980s and early 1990s . exposé are the first music group to have four top 10 hit songs on the billboard hot 100. the group have had over 25 million records worldwide .
