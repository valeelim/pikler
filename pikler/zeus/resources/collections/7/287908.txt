Edelweiss     edelweiss is a well-known europe mountain flower . leaves and flowers are covered with white hairs and look woolly . each edelweiss bloom has five to six small yellow flower heads surrounded by leaflets in a star shape . the flowers are in bloom between july and september . the edelweiss prefers rocky limestone places . it has been used traditionally in folk medicine . the hair on the flower protect the edelweiss from cold .
