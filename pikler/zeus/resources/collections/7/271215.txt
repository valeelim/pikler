Monocalcium phosphate     monocalcium phosphate is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is ca ( hpo ) . it contains phosphate , calcium , and hydrogen ions . monocalcium phosphate is a colorless solid . it dissolves a little in water to make an acidic solution . it is made by replacing one of the hydrogen ions in phosphoric acid with a calcium ion . it is made by reacting calcium phosphate rock with sulfuric acid . this makes monocalcium phosphate . it is used in baking powder . when it is dissolved in water , it makes an acidic solution which reacts with the sodium bicarbonate to make carbon dioxide . the carbon dioxide makes it rise . it is also used in fertilizers . related pages . - dicalcium phosphate - sodium phosphate
