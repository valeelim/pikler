Book frontispiece     a frontispiece is a picture . it is in a book , on one of its front title pages . it is usually a big scene of the book , or a portrait of the writer . in older times , the picture of the writer was more often used . this was because it was less costly , and could be used again for the writer 's other books . frontispieces were often used in scholarly books and bibles , and many are fine works of art .
