University of North Carolina at Charlotte     the university of north carolina at charlotte is a public , coeducational university in charlotte , north carolina . the university has about 24,700 students . it was founded in 1946. the current chancellor of the university is dr. phillip l. dubois . the athletics teams of the university are called the charlotte 49ers . they have 16 varsity teams , most of which compete in conference usa . the football team , which only started play in 2013 , will not join c-usa until 2015. other websites . - university of north carolina at charlotte 's official website - charlotte 49ers ' official athletics website
