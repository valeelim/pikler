Sgt. Slaughter    sgt . slaughter robert remus ( born august 27 , 1948 , in parris island , south carolina ) better known by his ring name , sgt . slaughter , is a retired american professional wrestler that is best known for wrestling for wwf/e under the ring name sgt . slaughter . he is a one-time wwf champion and was inducted into the wwe hall of fame in 2004 by pat patterson . his finishing move was the camel clutch and then the cobra clutch . other websites . - sgt . slaughter 's official website - sgt . slaughter 's wwe hall of fame profile at wwe.com
