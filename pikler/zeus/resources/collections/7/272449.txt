Andray Blatche     andray blatche ( born august 22 , 1986 in syracuse , new york ) is an filipino-american basketball player that currently plays for the washington wizards of the nba . on september 25 , 2005 , blatche was shot in a carjacking in alexandria , virginia and recovered successfully . blatche has played for the washington wizards for 5 seasons . blatche became a naturalized filipino in 2014. he plays for the philippine national basketball team .
