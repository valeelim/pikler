Regurgitation     regurgitation is the process of a human or animal letting out the food it had been digesting . sometimes , blood can come out as well . regurgitation can be very important to some animals , such as birds for example . it is used by a number of species to feed their young . this is especially when the young is in its nest or home and the parent hunts for food . or some birds can occasionally regurgitate pellets of food that is not digestable , such as bones and feathers . owls are known to do this .
