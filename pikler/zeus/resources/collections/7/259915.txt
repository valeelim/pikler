Roraiman Barbtail     the roraiman barbtail ( `` roraimia adusta '' ) is a species of bird in the furnariidae family . it is placed in the monotypic genus roraimia . it is found in brazil , guyana , and venezuela . its natural habitat is subtropical or tropical moist montane forests . references . - birdlife international 2004. roraimia adusta . 2006 iucn red list of threatened species . downloaded on 27 july 2007 .
