Seventh Son of a Seventh Son     seventh son of a seventh son is a studio album by iron maiden . it went on sale on 11 april 1988. it is a concept album , and talks about the idea that a seventh son of a seventh son has special powers . the album was the most popular one in th eunited kingdom when it went on sale . the album got good reviews . one person who reviewed it said that `` ranks among [ iron maiden 's ] best work .
