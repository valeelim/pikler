Kopan Monastery     kopan monastery is a tibetan buddhist monastery near boudhanath , on the outskirts of kathmandu , nepal . it belongs to the fpmt , an international network of gelugpa dharma centers , and once served as its headquarters . the monastery was established by the fpmt founders , lamas thubten yeshe and thubten zopa rinpoche , who bought the property from nepal 's royal astrologer in 1969. its name comes from the name of the hill on which it was built . kopan has become famous for teaching buddhism to visiting western foreigners . the first of what would become annual month-long ( november-december ) meditation courses was held in 1971. these courses generally combine traditional lam rim teachings with informal discussion , several periods of guided meditation , and a vegetarian diet .
