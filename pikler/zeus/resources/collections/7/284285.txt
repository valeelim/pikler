Místico     luis ignacio urive alvirde ( born december 22 , 1982 ) is a mexican luchador enmascarado . he currently works for asistencia asesoría y administración ( aaa ) and world wrestling league ( wwl ) . urive is best known for his time working with consejo mundial de lucha libre ( cmll ) where he competed under the ring name místico . during his time in cmll , he was the biggest técnico ( face ) and was the biggest box office draw in all of mexico . urive also worked for the wwe where he competed under the ring name sin cara ( spanish for `` without face '' or `` faceless '' ) . during his time in the wwe , urive had a hard time getting used to the wwe style of wrestling and would get injured a lot . he was released from his wwe contract on march 27 , 2014. after his release , he continued to use the sin cara name in mexican independent promotions before adopting the ring name myzteziz after signing with aaa in february 2014. in 2007 , pro fteenwrestling illustrated ranked místico # 3 of the pwi 500. championships . - baja star 's wrestling
