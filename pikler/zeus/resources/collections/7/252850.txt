Addictive personality     people who have a personality with many traits that make them prone to addiction are said to have an addictive personality . an addiction is a physical or psychological dependency that influences the life of that person negatively . very often , such people use illegal drugs , but this does not have to be the case : many can also be addicted to smoking , gambling , food , exercise , work , video games , pornography or relationships . this is called codependency . people with an addictive personality tend to plan their lives around the addiction . addictive personality is difficult to treat .
