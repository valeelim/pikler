Kemal Kılıçdaroğlu     kemal kılıçdaroğlu ( born 17 december 1948 ) is a turkish politician . he is the currently leader of the republican people 's party ( chp ) and the leader of the main opposition in turkey . he has been the member of parliament for istanbul since 2002. he was nominated by the chp as candidate for mayor of istanbul in 2009 local elections , in which he gained 36.8 % of all votes . other websites . - chp official website
