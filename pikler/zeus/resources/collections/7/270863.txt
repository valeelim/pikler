Dryptosaurus     dryptosaurus ( `` tearing lizard '' ) was a theropod dinosaur of the tyrannosauroidea type . it was found in upper cretaceous strata of eastern north america . it was formerly known as `` laelaps '' . it was given its name because it had 8-inch ( 21-cm ) long finger claws . fossils have been found in new jersey . it is known only from a partial skeleton .
