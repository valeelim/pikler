David Trimble     william david trimble , baron trimble , pc ( born 15 october 1944 , in belfast ) , is a politician from northern ireland . he was leader of the ulster unionist party ( uup ; 1995–2005 ) , the first first minister of northern ireland ( 1998–2002 ) , and was a member of the house of commons ( 1990–2005 ) . he is a life peer for the conservative party . trimble was awarded the 1998 nobel peace prize , along with john hume bacause of effort toward belfast agreement .
