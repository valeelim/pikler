Wuerhosaurus     wuerhosaurus was a stegosaurid dinosaur ( related to stegosaurus ) from the lower cretaceous of china . many details of the skeleton are lacking . it had bony , rounded plates along its back and probably 4 bony spikes at the tip of its tail . it had a shorter body and wider hip bones than other stegosaurids . it was one of the last genera of stegosaur known to survive , since most others died out by the end of the jurassic .
