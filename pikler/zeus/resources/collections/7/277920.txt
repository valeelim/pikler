Tammany Hall     tammany hall , ( also called the society of st. tammany , the sons of st. tammany , or the columbian order ) , named for tamanend ( meaning `` '' affable '' '' ) , a native american leader of the lenni lenape , was started in 1786 and was declared on may 12 , 1789 as the tammany society . it was the democratic political machine that played a major role in controlling new york city politics . it also helped many immigrants , especially the irish , rise up in american politics from the 1790s to the 1960s .
