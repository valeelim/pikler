Bowery     bowery , ( commonly called the bowery ) is the name of a street and a small neighborhood in the southern manhattan . the name comes from the dutch `` bouwerij '' , which is an old word for `` farm . '' the neighborhood is surrounded by the east village to the north , canal street and chinatown to the south , allen street and the lower east side to the east and little italy to the west . the area was home to many irish immigrants in the early 19th century after the great irish famine .
