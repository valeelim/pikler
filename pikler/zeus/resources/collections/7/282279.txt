Fernando Pisani     fernando antonio pisani ( born december 27 , 1976 in edmonton , alberta ) is a canadian professional ice hockey right winger who is currently a free agent . he was picked by the edmonton oilers with the 195th overall pick in the 1996 nhl entry draft . he formerly played for the edmonton oilers from 2002 to 2010 , the chicago blackhawks in 2011 and also played for the scl tigers of the national league a and the asiago hockey a.s. of the serie a during the 2004-05 nhl lockout . other websites . - fernando pisani 's nhl player page
