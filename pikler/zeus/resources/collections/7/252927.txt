El Mercurio de Valparaíso     el mercurio de valparaíso is the oldest newspaper of chile , and the oldest one in the world in spanish language that is still being published –the first newspaper in chile was `` la aurora de chile '' . it is from valparaíso , and it was founded in 1827 by typographers thomas g. wells , ignacio silva medina and pedro félix vicuña . the first edition was published in september 12 , 1827. it is claimed that newspaper `` el peruano '' is the oldest one in spanish language . other links . - el mercurio de valparaíso homepage
