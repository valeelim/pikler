Rufous Hornero     the rufous hornero is a kind of bird that lives around south america . it builds its nests on high places such as telephone poles , or trees . when it makes a nest , it looks like a cup that has fallen over to the side , or like an old-fashioned baker 's oven . to make it , the rufous hornero mixes grass and animal hair and mud , and then leaves it to dry . inside there is a grassy room where it lays its eggs .
