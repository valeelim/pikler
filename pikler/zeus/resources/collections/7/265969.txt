Capparaceae     capparaceae commonly known the caper family , are trees , shrubs , or lianas , sometimes herbs , that are usually found in the tropics . the family may contain up to 16 genera and 480 species , although some genera currently included may not belong there . the largest genera are `` capparis '' ( about 150 species ) , `` maerua '' ( 100 species ) , `` boscia '' ( 37 species ) and `` cadaba '' ( 30 species ) .
