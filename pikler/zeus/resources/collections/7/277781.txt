Asclepiadoideae     the asclepiadaceae is a former plant family now treated as a subfamily ( subfamily asclepiadoideae ) in the apocynaceae . they form a group of perennial herbs , twining shrubs , lianas or rarely trees but notably also contain a significant number of leafless stem succulents , all belonging to the order gentianales . the name comes from the type genus `` asclepias '' ( milkweeds ) .
