Arizona State University     arizona state university is a public university in tempe , arizona . the university has about 70,440 students . it was founded in 1885. the current president of the university is michael m. crow . the sports teams of the university are called the sun devils . asu has 20 varsity teams , almost all of which compete in the pacific-12 conference . other websites . - arizona state university 's official website - arizona state sun devils ' official athletics website
