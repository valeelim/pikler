Loyola University Chicago     loyola university chicago is a private , jesuit university in chicago , illinois . the university has about 15,951 students . it was founded in 1870. the current president of the university is michael j. garanzini , s.j . . the athletics teams of the university are called the ramblers . they have 13 varsity teams , most of which compete in the missouri valley conference . the men 's volleyball team is a member of the midwestern intercollegiate volleyball association . other websites . - loyola university chicago 's official website - loyola ramblers ' official athletics website
