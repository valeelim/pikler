Florida Gulf Coast University     florida gulf coast university is a public , coeducational university in fort myers , florida . the university has about 12,024 students . it was founded in 1991. the current president of the university is wilson g. bradshaw . the athletics teams of the university are called the eagles and they have 15 varsity teams . they compete in the atlantic sun conference . other websites . - florida gulf coast university 's official website - fgcu eagles ' official athletics website
