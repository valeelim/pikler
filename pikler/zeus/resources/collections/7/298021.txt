St Ives, Cornwall    st ives , cornwall st ives is a seaside town and port in west cornwall , england , united kingdom . the town lies north of penzance and west of camborne on the coast of the atlantic ocean . it was once commercially dependent on fishing but fishing has declined and the town is now primarily a holiday resort . st ives has become renowned for the large number of artists who work there . it was named best seaside town of 2007 by the `` guardian '' newspaper .
