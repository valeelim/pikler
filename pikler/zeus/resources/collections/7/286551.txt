Brendon Ayanbadejo     oladele brendon ayanbadejo ( born september 6 , 1976 in chicago , illinois ) is an american football linebacker and special teamer for the baltimore ravens of the national football league ( nfl ) . he has played for 8 nfl teams as well as 3 cfl teams . he played college football at ucla . he won super bowl xlvii with the ravens when they defeated the san francisco 49ers 34-31. other websites . - brendon ayanbadejo at nfl.com
