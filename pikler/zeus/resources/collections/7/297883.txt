Like a Boss     `` like a boss ' '' is a 2009 comedy hip hop song by the american comedy trio the lonely island and was featured on their album incredibad . the song is a parody of slim thug 's first single off his debut album , `` already platinum '' which was also called `` like a boss '' . the song is about a man ( played by andy samberg ) who is having a performance review at a law firm . the person who is reviewing samberg is played by canadian actor seth rogen .
