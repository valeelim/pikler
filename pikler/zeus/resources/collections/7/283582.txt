Holy Week     holy week ( latin : `` hebdomas sancta '' or `` hebdomas maior '' , `` greater week '' ; greek : `` μεγάλη ἑβδομάς '' , `` megale hebdomas '' ) is the commemoration of the last week of jesus christ 's ministry on earth . holy week is the last week of lent . it begins with palm sunday and leads up to easter sunday . other websites . - calculate easter dates as well as other main variable feasts during and after passover - the days of holy week at the christian resource institute - holy week and easter fast facts from aol research & learn - holy week from the catholic encyclopedia
