En Vogue     en vouge are an american female r & b and pop quartet . four members-cindy herron , terry ellis , dawn robinson and maxine jones-make up this female group . en vogue released their first record `` born to sing '' during 1990. they won many awards in the 1990s and 2000s , including seven mtv video music awards and four soul train awards . discography . - studio albums - 1990 : `` born to sing '' - 1992 : `` funky divas '' - 1997 : `` ev3 '' - 2000 : `` masterpiece theatre '' - 2002 : `` the gift of christmas '' - 2004 : `` soul flower '' - eps - 1991 : `` remix to sing '' - 1993 : `` runaway love ''
