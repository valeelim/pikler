Craig Ferguson     craig ferguson ( born 17 may 1962 in glasgow , scotland ) is an scottish-american late night television host , comedian and actor . he hosted the the late late show with craig ferguson on cbs . he also hosted shark week in 2010 on the discovery channel . he became an american citizen on february 1 , 2008. he currently hosts celebrity name game on american broadcasting company . he also hosts join or die on history ( tv channel ) .
