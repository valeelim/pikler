Valeri Zelepukin     valeri mikhailovich zelepukin ( born september 17 , 1968 in voskresensk , urs ) is a russian retired professional ice hockey left winger . he played a total of 11 seasons in the nhl ; 7 with the new jersey devils , 1 with the edmonton oilers , 2 with the philadelphia flyers and 1 with the chicago blackhawks . he moved back to russia in 2002 and retired . he won the stanley cup with the new jersey devils in 1995 .
