Puntland     puntland is a province of somalia . its capital is garowe , but the economy is centered around boosasso . the province covers about a third of somalia , and about a third of the population of somalia lives in the province . puntland is next to somaliland , which wanted to become an independent state . puntlant also wants some degree of autonomy , but the people of puntland want to stay within somalia , and find a solution for the whole country .
