Clumsy (Our Lady Peace song)    clumsy ( our lady peace song ) `` 'clumsy ' '' is a 1997 alternative rock song by the canadian alternative rock band our lady peace and is the second single from their second studio album `` clumsy '' , which was released in 1997. the song was written by raine maida and mike turner and was produced by arnold lanni . the song was featured on the soundtrack to the movie , i know what you did last summer .
