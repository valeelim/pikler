Sodium periodate     sodium periodate is a chemical compound . its chemical formula can be naio ( sodium metaperiodate ) or naio ( sodium orthoiodate ) . it has sodium and periodate ions in it . sodium periodate is a colorless solid . it reacts with acids to make periodic acid . it breaks down when heated to make sodium iodate and oxygen gas . it is a strong oxidizing agent . it can be made by oxidation of sodium iodide with sodium hypochlorite in a nitric acid solution . sodium periodate can oxidize cellulose . it is also used as a biodegradable chemical in the human body . it is also used in organic chemistry reactions . related pages . - potassium iodate - perbromic acid - potassium perchlorate
