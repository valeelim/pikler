Socorro dove     the socorro dove ( zenaida graysoni ) is a dove that now can not be found in the wild . in captivity , there are not more than some 200 and probably fewer than 100 purebred birds . because of this , a reintroduction program is being prepared . it is a close relative of the mourning and eared doves , particularly the former . it was at one time considered a subspecies of the mourning dove .
