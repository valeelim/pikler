Bircham International University     bircham international university is a private independent institution of higher education via distance education . it offers adult degree programs at professional , undergraduate and graduate levels . it is registered in spain and delaware . in the past it operated from the bahamas or the united kingdom . it has no recognized educational accreditation in any of these places . bircham is not accredited by any accreditation agency recognized by united states authorities . it is listed as a member of the european foundation for quality in elearning , a membership organization that does not engage in educational accreditation . it also is listed as a member of the international accreditation and recognition council , an unrecognized accreditation agency in the united states . bircham states that it was accredited by the american association for higher education and accreditation in 2010. this organization is not a recognized accreditation body . because bircham is not accredited by a recognized accreditor in the countries where it operates , employers or other institutions might not accept bircham 's degrees and credits .
