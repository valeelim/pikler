Ian Curtis     ian kevin curtis ( 15 july 1956 - 18 may 1980 ) was the singer of the post-punk band joy division . he was born in stretford , lancashire and grew up in macclesfield , cheshire . he suffered with epilepsy and committed suicide by hanging in macclesfield in 1980 after the illness became severe . after this joy division broke up . in 2007 `` control '' , a film about curtis ' life , was released . it made $ 8,159,508 and won five british independent film awards .
