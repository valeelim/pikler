Surfing with the Alien (song)    surfing with the alien ( song ) `` surfing with the alien '' is the first song in joe satriani 's second album surfing with the alien . the song refers to the comic book character silver surfer . the song was named the 30th greatest guitar solo ever by guitar world magazine readers and is one of his faster shred guitar style songs . the song was also the theme song for the nintendo 64 game nascar 99 .
