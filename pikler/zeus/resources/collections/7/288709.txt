Ilene Woods     jacqueline ruth `` ilene '' woods ( may 5 , 1929 â€“ july 1 , 2010 ) was an american singer and actress who voiced cinderella in the 1950 disney movie . woods was born on may 5 , 1929 in portsmouth , new hampshire . as a child woods was led into singing by her mother . by the age of 11 , woods had her own radio show . in 1948 , at the age of 18 , woods recorded several songs for friends mack david and jerry livingston . the songs ( `` bibbidi bobbidi boo '' , `` so this is love '' , and `` a dream is a wish your heart makes '' ) were then played for walt disney . he chose woods out of 300 other girls to play the part of cinderella for the disney movie `` cinderella '' . recording the part took about 2 years , working off and on . woods was first married to stephen steck jr. they had one child , a daughter named stephanie . after a divorce , in 1963 she married ed shaughnessy . they had two sons , james and daniel . in 2003 she was recognized as a disney legend for her work in `` cinderella '' . woods died on july 1 , 2010 from causes related to alzheimer 's disease in canoga park , los angeles , california . she was 81 .
