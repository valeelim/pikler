Bodmin     bodmin is a town on the a30 road , in cornwall , united kingdom . it is situated in the centre of the county southwest of bodmin moor . the neighbouring parishes are cardinham , lanhydrock , lanivet and helland . in 2001 there were 12,881 people living in bodmin . in the middle ages bodmin was one of the most important towns in cornwall and the site of a monastery founded by saint petroc . the parish church of bodmin was built in the 15th century and is the second largest church in the county after truro cathedral .
