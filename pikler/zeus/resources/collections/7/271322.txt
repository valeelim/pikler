Grand Duchy of Tuscany     the grand duchy of tuscany ( , ) was a monarchy in tuscany , an area in the north-west of the italian peninsula . it existed , with interruptions , from 1569 to 1859. it replaced the duchy of florence . it was ruled by the grand duke of tuscany . the capital of the grand duchy was florence . until the house of lorraine took over from the medici , tuscany was a state of the holy roman empire until the peace of westphalia in 1648. under the medici the heir to the grand ducal throne was called the `` grand prince of tuscany '' whereas the lorrainers used various titles for the heir apparent .
