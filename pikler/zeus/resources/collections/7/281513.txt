The Hawthorns     the hawthorns is a football stadium in west bromwich , sandwell , england . it is the current home stadium of the west bromwich albion f.c.. the stadium was built in 1900 and opened in that same year . the total seating capacity of the stadium is approximately 26,500. the stadium is also near the hawthorns station which provides transportation to the stadium . it is the highest ground above sea level in england . the four stands at the ground are called the east stand , the birmingham road , the west stand ( formerly halfords lane ) and the smethwick end .
