Conjure One     conjure one is an electronic music project from vancouver , british columbia . the project 's most well known song is `` sleep '' . the only member of the project is rhys fulber . it started in early 1997 when fulber decided that he was going to pursue a solo career . he has released three albums under conjure one . singer sandra covered `` sleep '' on her single `` the way i am '' . other websites . - official website
