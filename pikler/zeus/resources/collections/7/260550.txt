Chris Kuper     christopher kuper ( born december 19 , 1982 in anchorage , alaska ) is an american football offensive guard for the denver broncos of the national football league ( nfl ) . kuper played college football at the university of north dakota and was drafted by the denver broncos with the 161st pick in the fifth round of the 2006 nfl draft . websites . - chris kuper at denverbroncos.com
