Be Here     be here is the forth studio album by new zealand-born australian country music singer keith urban . it was released on september 21 , 2004 in the united states and november 30 , 2004 in australia . the producer of the album was dan huff and keith urban . the songs `` days go by '' , `` making memories of us '' and `` better life '' all reached # 1 on the hot country songs chart `` you 're my better half '' and `` tonight i wan na cry '' peaked at # 2 and `` live to love another day '' peaked at # 48 and the album peaked at # 1 on the billboard hot country albums chart and # 3 on the billboard 200. the album is certified triple platinum by the riaa and is urban 's best selling record selling 3,639,000 units in the united states . singles from `` be here '' . - 2004 - `` days go by '' - 2004 - `` you 're my better half '' - 2005 - `` making memorie of us '' - 2005 - `` better life '' - 2005 - `` tonight i wan na cry ''
