Vladimir Malakhov     vladimir malakhov ( born 27 november 1980 ) is a chess grandmaster from russia . he was a member of the gold-medal-winning russian team at the 2009 world team chess championship in bursa . malakhov 's career choices are interesting . qualified as a nuclear physicist , he used to be an amateur chess player . now he 's a professional chess player who works part-time as a nuclear physicist .
