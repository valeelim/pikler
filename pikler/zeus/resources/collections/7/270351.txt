Pagani     pagani automobili s.p.a. is an italian car manufacturer . it was founded in 1992 by horacio pagani . horacio pagani used to work with lamborghini on cars like the lamborghini diablo . in the late 1980s , he started to design his own cars . in 1992 , he started to work on a concept-car for his very own car . in 1994 , he made a deal with mercedes-benz . that gave him a v12 engine for his car . in 1999 , he showed his first production car , the zonda , at the geneva motor show . the zonda was the first production car made by pagani . it has a v12 engine by mercedes-benz . mercedes-benz produced in the very first version , called the zonda c12 , 290 kw . after several new versions , the very last zonda , the zonda revoluci√≥n , was released in 2013. it is a very light version with a 597 kw v12 engine . the huayra is the second production model by pagani . it also has a v12 engine by mercedes-benz . it also has a turbocharger . the engine makes 539 kw . its top speed is about 372 km/h ( 231 mph ) . the official debut of the huayra was at the 2011 geneva motor show .
