SpongeBob SquigglePants     spongebob squigglepants is a video game based on the television series `` spongebob squarepants '' . it is the fourth game in the udraw series . it was published by thq and developed by wayforward technologies . the game was released for wii on april 12 , 2011. it was released for nintendo 3ds on may 17 , 2011. it contains over 100 mini-games that require using the udraw gametablet . it allows players to draw their own paintings or color in them .
