Trypanosomiasis     trypanosomiasis is the name for several diseases , caused by parasites of the genus `` tryposoma '' : - african typanosomiasis or sleeping sickness , caused by `` 'trypanosoma brucei '' and spread by the tsetse fly - american typanosomiasis or chagas disease , caused by `` trypanosoma cruzi '' and spread by various bloodsucking bugs of the subfamily triatominae there are also various diseases that only affect animals , caused by such parasites , amongst others : - nagana , or animal african trypanosomiasis ; the animal counterpart of sleeping sickness - surra - covering sickness or douring ( which affects horses )
