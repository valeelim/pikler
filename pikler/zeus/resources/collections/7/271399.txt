Tuner (car)    tuner ( car ) when talking about cars , a tuner is a car with many parts changed so the car can go very fast . the word `` tune '' here means `` to adjust '' or `` to change '' . typically , people call japanese fast cars `` tuners '' , comparing to american fast cars . it is because people call american fast cars `` muscles '' . when people want to drive fast cars but do n't want to buy a very expansive car , we can change some parts for an ordinary car to make it go fast . for basic tuning , we can work on intake and exhaust , which means the process that air goes into the engine and out of the engine . we can also make the frame of the car stronger . related pages . - street racing - car customizing - cruising ( driving ) - import scene - sleeper ( car ) - drifting - virtual tuning
