Albufera Sauce     albufera sauce ( fr . sauce albuféra ) is a minor sauce whose mother sauce isn velouté . probably created by antonin careme rather than his trainee adolphe dugléré ( who was only ten when albufera fell out of favour ) , to accompany poularde albufera , which he also created . it was named in honour of the duke of albufera . escoffier gives a recipe in `` le guide culinaire '' which consists of a base of suprême sauce to which is added meat glaze in order to lend the latter an ivory-white tint which characterizes it . it is served chiefly with poultry and sweetbreads .
