Amide     amide can refer to two different things . an amide ion is different from an amide in organic chemistry . the amide ion is made by deprotonating ammonia . this is ammonia acting as an acid , although a very weak one . amides made by deprotonating ammonia are very strong bases . sodium amide is an example . amides are also chemical compounds . they are made by reacting carboxylic acids like acetic acid with amines . there are many ways to make them . they are also used in many chemical reactions . related pages . - sodium azide - azide - nitrate
