Carnival of Carnage     carnival of carnage is the first studio album by american hip hop band , insane clown posse . it was released on october 18 , 1992. the producers of the album were joseph bruce , mike clark , esham and chuck miller . the album was the first of the `` joker cards '' in the dark carnival mythology . kid rock and esham sing guest vocals on the album , the album 's lyrics are centered around the controversies , violence and politics in the ghetto . track listing . - explicit version shaggy dope sings vocals on `` night of the axe '' and `` wizard of the hood '' . - clean version - the clean version of the album was released in 1999 and : - edits out profanity with beeps - does not include the songs `` night of the axe '' and blackin , your eyes '' - the buzzing chainsaw sound at the beginning of the song `` pychopathic is not heard . - the track list is slightly different violent j- vocals shaggy dope- occasional vocals special musical guests kid rock and esham .
