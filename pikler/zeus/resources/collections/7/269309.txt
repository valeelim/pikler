Semisolid     a semisolid thing is something that is not really a solid or a liquid . mayonnaise is an example . mayonnaise does not hold its shape like a solid but does not flow like a liquid . normally semisolids are covalent things . covalent substances get softer and softer until they melt . ionic substances melt at one temperature and do not get softer . butter can be a semisolid when it is warm . butter does not hold its shape but it does not flow like a liquid . paint is also a semisolid . related pages . - thixotropy
