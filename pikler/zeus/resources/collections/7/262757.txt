Br'er Rabbit     br'er rabbit ( also spelled bre'r rabbit or brer rabbit or bruh rabbit , with the title `` br'er '' ) is an important character in the uncle remus stories in the southern united states . he is a tricky character , and usually wins or escapes because he is clever , not because he is strong . br'er rabbit came from both african and cherokee cultures . disney later used the character for their `` the song of the south '' . in a cherokee story , `` the fox and the wolf throw the trickster rabbit into a thicket from which the rabbit quickly escapes . '' there was a `` melding ( mixing ) of the cherokee rabbit-trickster ... into the culture of african slaves .
