Selenium trioxide     selenium trioxide , also known as selenium ( vi ) oxide and selenic oxide , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is seo . it contains selenium in its +6 oxidation state . it also contains oxide ions . selenium trioxide is a white solid . it evaporates easily . it dissolves easily in water . it is not stable and breaks down to make selenium dioxide and oxygen when it is heated . it is a powerful oxidizing agent like all selenates . it dissolves in water to make selenic acid . it can be made by reacting potassium selenate with sulfur dioxide . it can also be made by dehydrating ( removing the water from ) selenic acid with a strong dessicant , like phosphorus ( v ) oxide . related pages . - selenium ( i ) chloride
