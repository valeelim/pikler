Jacksonville University     jacksonville university is a private university in jacksonville , florida , united states . the university has about 3,600 students . it was founded in 1934. the current president of the university is kerry d. romesburg . the athletics teams of the university are called the dolphins and they have 20 varsity teams . they compete in the atlantic sun conference . other websites . - jacksonville university 's official website - jacksonville dolphins ' official athletics website
