Weber State University     weber state university is a public university in ogden , utah . the university has about 25,130 students . it was founded in 1889. the current president of the university is f. ann millner . the men 's athletics teams of the university are called the wildcats . they have 15 varsity teams and they compete in the big sky conference . other websites . - weber state university 's official website - weber state wildcats ' official athletics website
