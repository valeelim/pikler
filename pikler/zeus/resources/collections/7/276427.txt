New Taipei City     new taipei city ( ) is a city in northern taiwan . it circles taipei city . it is south of keelung , north of taoyuan county and west of yilan . it is a part of the republic of china and the largest city in taiwan . - mayor : eric chu ( 朱立倫 ) - total area : 2,052.5667 km² - total people : 3,743,553 ( april,2006 ) new taipei city controls 29 districts ( 區 ) . it has 1,017 villages ( 里 ) . the villages are divided into 21,683 neighborhoods ( 鄰 ) . related pages . - list of counties of republic of china
