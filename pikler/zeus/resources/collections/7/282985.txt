Sissy Spacek     mary elizabeth `` sissy '' spacek ( born december 25 , 1949 ) is an american actress and singer . she played carrie white in the 1976 movie `` carrie '' . she also played country singer loretta lynn in the 1980 movie `` coal miner 's daughter '' . spacek is known mainly as a dramatic actress , but she has acted in comedies . she was born in quitman , texas .
