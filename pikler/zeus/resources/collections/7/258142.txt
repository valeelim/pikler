Ammonium chloride     ammonium chloride is a chemical compound composed of ammonium and chloride ions . it is a colorless crystalline compound . it is used in soldering . it is also used as an electrolyte in the leclanche cell , a type of primary cell . ammonium chloride can be deprotonated by strong bases such as sodium hydroxide to produce ammonia gas . related pages . - ammonium sulfide - ammonium - ammonia
