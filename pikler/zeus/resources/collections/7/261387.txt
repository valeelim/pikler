Sodium bisulfate     sodium bisulfate , also known as sodium hydrogen sulfate , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is nahso . it is made of sodium and bisulfate ions . it is a white crystalline solid . it is an acid , though not as strong as sulfuric acid . it can be made by reacting smaller amounts ofsodium hydroxide and sulfuric acid . if too much sodium hydroxide is used , sodium sulfate is made instead . it is also made by reacting sodium chloride and sulfuric acid when heated . this reaction also makes hydrogen chloride . it is used to lower ph ( made things more acidic ) . other pages . - sulfite - sodium sulfite - sulfate
