Dean     a dean is the title of a high official in a church or college . at one time all ancient colleges were entirely run by priests . in england , you could not even be a student unless you were a member of a church of england congregation . in those days , a dean was a senior person who looked after the people and finance , and that is usually so today . but now there are two kinds of dean : 1. a dean is a priest who is the head of the chapter of a cathedral , which meets in a chapter house . he is responsible for managing the cathedral and its affairs . in some cathedrals he is also the local parish priest , called a rector . 1. a dean is the official who usually looks after an educational college . sometimes he is also the head of the college , sometimes not . in modern colleges , the dean may take charge of discipline , or be the chief of a faculty of a university .
