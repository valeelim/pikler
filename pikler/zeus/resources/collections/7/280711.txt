Eduardo Nájera     eduardo alonso nájera pérez ( born july 11 , 1976 ) is a mexican basketball player that currently plays for the charlotte bobcats of the nba . he also played for the dallas mavericks for five seasons , the golden state warriors for one season , the denver nuggets for 4 seasons and the new jersey nets for two seasons . he went to university of oklahoma for college . other websites . - eduardo nájera 's nba profile
