Mandi     mandi ( ) is a major city in the indian state of himachal pradesh . it is the capital of the mandi district . it was formerly called mandav nagar , and is also known as sahor . it is located north of state capital , shimla . it is in the north-west himalayas . the average altitude is about 1,044 metres ( 3,425 ft ) , other websites . - official site of the municipal council of mandi
