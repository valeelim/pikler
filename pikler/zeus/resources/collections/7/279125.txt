Goodbye Lullaby     goodbye lullaby is the fourth studio album by canadian avril lavigne . it was released on march 2 , 2011 through rca records . her first single off the album was `` what the hell '' which was released on january 10 , 2011. the second single , `` smile '' , was released on april 11 , 2011. the album is a lot more personal and softer than her three previous albums , `` let go '' , `` under my skin '' and `` the best damn thing '' .
