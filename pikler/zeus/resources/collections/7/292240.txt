Parable of the budding fig tree     the parable of the budding fig tree is a parable of jesus . it is written in the new testament in matthew 24:32-35 , mark 13:28-31 and luke 21:29-33. the parable compares the kingdom of god with a budding fig tree , the opposite of the barren fig tree . he told them this parable : this eschatological parable follows the olivet discourse in luke 21:5-28 ( and matthew 24 and mark 13 ) . new buds appear on the branches of trees when winter ends . the buds open into blossoms and green leaves in spring . fruit starts to appear as summer arrives . when the signs of the end of the age start to happen , it means jesus will return soon . related pages . - figs in the bible
