Provincial Assembly of Balochistan     the provincial assembly of balochistan is one of the four provincial assemblies of pakistan . the balochistan assembly is the unicameral ( single chamber ) legislature of the province of balochistan . it was set up by article 106 of the constitution of the pakistan . the assembly has 51 directly elected members of the provincial assembly , representing constituencies from each district , as well as 11 seats reserved for women and 3 for non-muslims .
