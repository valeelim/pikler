Erzincan     erzincan is the capital of erzincan province in the eastern anatolian region of turkey . nearby cities include erzurum , sivas , tunceli , bingöl , elazığ , malatya , gumushane , bayburt , and giresun . the city is at 39° 45 ' 12 '' north and 39° 20 ' 28 '' east , with an altitude of 1185 meters . the population is 90,100 ( 2009 census ) . its climate produces snowy winters and cool summers . other websites . - governor 's office - municipality
