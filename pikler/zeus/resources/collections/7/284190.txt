List of Premiers of the Soviet Union     the premier of the soviet union was the head of government . most western countries use the term `` prime minister '' . in the former soviet union the job had many names : 1. chairman of the council of people 's commissars ( 1917–1946 ) 1. chairman of the council of ministers ( 1946–1991 ) 1. prime minister of the soviet union ( 1991 ) 1. chairman of the interstate economic committee of the ussr—prime minister of the economic community ( 1991 )
