Three Choirs Festival     the three choirs festival is a festival of classical music which is held every year in august . the choirs from three cathedrals take part in many of the concerts . the three cathedrals are : hereford cathedral , gloucester cathedral and worcester cathedral . each year the festival is held in one of those three cathedrals in turn . the three choirs festival is one of the oldest festivals of classical music . it has its roots in the 18th century . many famous musicians have performed in the festival . concerts have been conducted by samuel sebastian wesley , edward elgar , ralph vaughan williams and many others . music has been composed for the festival by frederick delius , zoltan kodaly holst , arthur sullivan , herbert howells , gerald finzi , william walton , arthur bliss and benjamin britten , lennox berkeley , john mccabe , william mathias , paul patterson and james macmillan . as well as the cathedral choirs there is also a choir called the festival chorus which takes part , usually singing large-scale works with orchestra . many ensembles and international soloists perform at the festival .
