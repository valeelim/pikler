1989 Loma Prieta Earthquake     the 1989 loma prieta earthquake was an earthquake that happened in the san francisco bay area . it happened on october 17 , 1989 , just after 5pm local time . the earthquake caused over 60 deaths . it caused billions of dollars worth of property damage . the earthquake happened during one of the games of the 1989 world series . because of that , it is sometimes called the `` world series quake '' .
