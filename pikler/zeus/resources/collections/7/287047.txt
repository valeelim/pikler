Brian Elliott     brian elliott ( born april 9 , 1985 in newmarket , ontario ) is a canadian professional ice hockey goaltender that currently plays for the colorado avalanche of the national hockey league ( nhl ) . he was drafted by the ottawa senators with the 291st overall pick in the 2003 nhl draft and played 3 seasons with them before being traded to the colorado avalanche on february 18 , 2011 for craig anderson .
