Sodium permanganate     sodium permanganate is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is namno . it contains sodium and permanganate ions . it is a purple-black crystalline solid . it can dissolve in water to make a bright pink solution . it dissolves in water more than potassium permanganate . it is more expensive than potassium permanganate . it also absorbs water from the air . it is a strong oxidizing agent . it can not be made in the way that potassium permanganate is made because sodium manganate is not made . it can be made by reacting pure manganese ( iv ) oxide with sodium hypochlorite . it can also be made by reacting potassium permanganate with certain sodium compounds . it can be used to make circuit boards . permanganates are toxic . they are also strong oxidizing agents and should be kept away from things that can burn . related pages . - manganate - manganese ( ii ) oxide - manganese ( vii ) oxide
