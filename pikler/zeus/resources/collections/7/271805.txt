Relic     a relic is an object , especially a piece of the body or a personal item of someone of religious importance , that was carefully preserved with an air of veneration as a memorial that you can touch . relics are an important aspect of buddhism , some denominations of christianity , hinduism , shamanism , and many other personal belief systems . the word relic comes from the latin `` reliquiae '' ( 'remains ' ) . a reliquary is a shrine that houses one or more relics . some well-known religious relics include the tooth of buddha , the holy grail , the crown of thorns , the true cross of jesus , the staff of moses , and the sword of mohammed . other websites . - relics article from the catholic encyclopedia - relics - keeping relics in perspective - a place for relics - the veneration of the relics of saints - from the summa theologiae - `` an inventory of relics '' by john calvin
