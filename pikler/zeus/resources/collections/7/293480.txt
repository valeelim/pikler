Poison Ivy (musician)    poison ivy ( musician ) kristy marlana wallace ( born february 20 , 1953 ) is an american guitarist , songwriter , arranger , producer , and sometimes a vocalist . wallace is better known by the stage names of poison ivy and sometimes poison ivy rorschach . she was a co-founder of the american garage punk band the cramps . she was married to lux interior for 37 years until he died on february 4 , 2009 .
