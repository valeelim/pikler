Jeff Bezos     jeffrey preston `` jeff '' bezos ( born january 12 , 1964 ) is the founder of amazon.com . he is also the chairman , president , and chief executive officer ( ceo ) of amazon.com . bezos graduated at princeton university as a under the tau beta pi association , was hired at d. e. shaw & co. as a financial analyst for before founding amazon.com in 1994. when markets opened on july 27 , 2017 , bezos briefly became the world 's richest person , surpassing bill gates with a net worth of just over $ 90 billion . he lost the title later in the day when amazon 's stock dropped , returning him to second place with a net worth just below $ 90 billion .
