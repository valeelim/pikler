Eastern Michigan University     eastern michigan university is a public , coeducational university in ypsilanti , michigan . the university has about 22,974 students . it was founded in 1849. the current president of the university is susan martin . the athletics teams of the university are called the eagles . they have 21 varsity teams , almost all of which compete in the mid-american conference . in women 's rowing , a sport that the mac does not sponsor , the eagles compete in the colonial athletic association . other websites . - eastern michigan university 's official website - eastern michigan eagles ' official athletics website
