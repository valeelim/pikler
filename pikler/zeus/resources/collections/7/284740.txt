University of North Texas     the university of north texas is a public university in denton , texas . the university has about 36,206 students . it was founded in 1890. the current president of the university is v. lane rawlins . the athletics teams of the university are called the mean green . they have 18 varsity teams and they compete in conference usa . other websites . - university of north texas ' official website - north texas mean green 's official athletics website
