Kurt Thomas (basketball)    kurt thomas ( basketball ) kurt vincent thomas ( born october 4 , 1972 in dallas , texas ) is an american basketball player . he currently plays for the chicago bulls as a power forward and center . he was drafted out of texas christian university in the 1995 nba draft in the 1st round with the 10th pick by the miami heat . he has also played with the miami heat from 1995 to 1997 , dallas mavericks from 1997 to 1998 , new york knicks from 1998 to 2005 , phoenix suns from 2005 to 2007 , seattle supersonics from 2007 to 2008 , san antonio spurs from 2008 to 2009 and the milwaukee bucks from 2009 to 2010. other websites . - kurt thomas ' nba player profile
