David Yelldell     david yelldell ( born october 1 , 1981 , in stuttgart ) is a german-american goalkeeper who is currently playing for bayer 04 leverkusen . yeldell has dual citizenship . his mother is german ; his father is african-american . at march 29 , 2011 , he had his first match for the united states national team . he is allowed to join the team because his father is american .
