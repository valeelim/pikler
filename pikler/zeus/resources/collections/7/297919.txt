Burke and Wills expedition     the burke and wills expedition , officially called the victorian exploring expedition , was the largest and best equipped in australian history . led by robert o'hara burke and william john wills , they crossed the continent of australia from south to north . the group left melbourne on 20 august 1860 and reached the gulf of carpentaria on 9 february 1861. however , because of poor planning and leadership , burke and wills died from starvation at cooper creek on their return journey .
