Christie Brinkley     christie brinkley ( born february 2 , 1954 ) is an american model , actress , writer and designer . she was born christie lee hudson in monroe , michigan . she moved to los angleles , california and changed her surname to that of her stepfather , writer don brinkley ( 1921-2012 ) . she has appeared on magazine covers many times and has a long contract with covergirl ( the longest of any model in history ) . all her four marriages ended in divorce . her second marriage was to singer billy joel and lasted from 1985-1994. their daughter alexa ray joel ( born 1985 ) is her eldest child and is a musician . brinkley also has a son by her third husband and a daughter by her fourth .
