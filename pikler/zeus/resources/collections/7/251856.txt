Martin Niemöller     emil gustav friedrich martin niemöller ( 14 january 1892 - 6 march 1984 ) was a german pastor who opposed the nazis . at first he supported hitler , but he changed his mind when the nazis tried to control the churches . because of his beliefs , he was imprisoned in concentration camps , and almost got executed . niemöller is best known because he wrote the poem `` first they came ... '' . he was born in lippstadt . he died in wiesbaden , aged 92. references . - martin niemöller : biography on united states holocaust memorial museum
