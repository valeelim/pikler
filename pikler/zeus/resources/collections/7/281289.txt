Lenticular cloud     a lenticular cloud ( `` altocumulus lenticularis '' ) is a type of cloud that forms at high altitudes . they are shaped like discs , and remain roughly in the same position . because of the way they are shaped , they have been offered as an explanation for some ufo sightings . lenticular clouds are formed when the wind forces the air up a mountain ridge to where the air is cooler and water vapor condenses .
