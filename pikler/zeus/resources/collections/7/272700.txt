Albany International Airport     albany international airport is a public use airport six nautical miles ( 11 km ) northwest of the central business district of albany , in albany county , new york , united states . it is owned by the albany county airport authority . it is an airport of entry serving albany . it is in the town of colonie . it was built on the site of the shaker settlement about 6 miles ( 10 km ) north of albany and stretching north to the hamlet of verdoy . the airport is the seat of a class c airspace . source :
