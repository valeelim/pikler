Taylor Pyatt     taylor pyatt ( born august 19 , 1981 in thunder bay , ontario ) is a canadian professional ice hockey player who currently plays for the new york rangers . drafted out of the ontario hockey league ( ohl ) , he played major-junior with the sudbury wolves . he has previously played for the new york islanders , buffalo sabres , vancouver canucks and phoenix coyotes in the nhl . awards . - named to the ohl first all-star team in 2000 .
