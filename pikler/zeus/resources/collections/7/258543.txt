Noel Gallagher     noel gallagher ( born 29 may 1967 ) is an english musician and singer-songwriter . gallagher was the former lead guitarist and main songwriter for the band oasis . he left the band in 2009. in 2011 he released his first solo album `` noel gallagher 's high flying birds '' . the band 's second album `` chasing yesterday '' was released 2 march 2015 .
