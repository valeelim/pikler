Spyro: Enter the Dragonfly    spyro : enter the dragonfly spyro : enter the dragonfly ( also called spyro 4 ) is a platform video game that was published by universal interactive . the video game was released for the playstation 2 video game system . the video game was released on november 3 , 2002 in north america , november 29 , 2002 in europe and on december 13 , 2002 in australia for the playstation 2 and was released for the gamecube on november 8 , 2002 in north america , november 29 , 2002 in europe and on december 13 , 2002 in australia . ratings . - playstation - 2.8 out of 10 ( gamespot ) - 6.0 out of 10 ( ign ) - gamecube - 3.2 out of 10 ( gamespot ) - 6.0 out of 10 ( ign ) even though the game got bad reviews , it sold enough copies that it was named as a greatest hits ( platinum edition in europe ) status on the playstation 2 and a player 's choice status on the gamecube . it is the fourth game in the `` spyro '' video game series and also the first `` spyro '' game that was not made by insomniac games .
