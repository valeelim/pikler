Perbromate     perbromate is an ion . its chemical formula is bro . it contains bromine in its +7 oxidation state . it is a strong oxidizing agent . it is very unstable and turns into bromate and oxygen easily . it was first made by decay of a radioactive selenate . later it was gotten by electrolysis of a bromate . it can also be made by oxidation of bromate with fluorine or xenon difluoride . related pages . - bromite - hypobromite
