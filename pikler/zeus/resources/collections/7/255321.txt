Anthony Norris     anthony `` tony '' norris ( born june 6 , 1970 ) , better known by his ring name , ahmed johnson , is an american former pro wrestler . he is best known for his career in the world wrestling federation , which lasted from october 1995 to february 1998. he is a one time intercontinental champion and is the first african american to ever hold the title . he was also the winner of the wwf 's first ever kuwait cup .
