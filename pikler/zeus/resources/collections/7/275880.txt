Antimony trichloride     antimony trichloride , also known as antimony ( iii ) chloride , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is sbcl . it has antimony and chloride ions in it . the antimony is in its +3 oxidation state . antimony trichloride is a white or pale yellow solid . it has a strong odor and absorbs water from the air . it reacts with water to make antimony trioxide and hydrochloric acid or hydrogen chloride . it is made by reacting chlorine with antimony , antimony trioxide , or antimony trisulfide . it is used to detect vitamin a. it is used to get horns off calves . it is also used as a catalyst . related pages . - antimony pentachloride - phosphorus trichloride - bismuth trichloride - antimony trifluoride
