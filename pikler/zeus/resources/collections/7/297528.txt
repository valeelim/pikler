Cahors     cahors ( ) is a commune in southwestern france . located in the region of occitanie , it is the `` prefecture '' ( capital ) of the lot department . it is also the capital of 3 `` cantons '' : cahors-1 , cahors-2 and cahors-3 . cahors was the capital of the old province of quercy and the people of this city are known , in french , as `` cadurciens '' ( women : `` cadurciennes '' ) ; this name comes from the old name of the city , `` carduca '' . cahors is an ancient town . the people that inhabited the area at the time of the roman conquest were called `` carduci '' and so the romans named the town as divona or `` divona cadurcorum '' , `` divona of the cadurci '' ; divona was a source of water , now called `` fontaine des chartreux '' . the word `` cahors '' comes from `` cadurcorum '' . cahors is located in the southwest of france , in the old province of quercy , in the valley of the lot river , and is found in a peninsula ( ) formed by an u-shaped meander of that river which gives its name to the department . it has an area of and its average altitude is ; at the city hall , the altitude is .
