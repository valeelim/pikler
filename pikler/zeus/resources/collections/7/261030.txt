Brent Bowden     brent michael bowden ( born may 21 , 1987 in huntsville , alabama ) is an american football punter that is currently a free agent in the national football league ( nfl ) . bowden was drafted out of the virginia tech university with the 172nd pick in the sixth round of the 2010 nfl draft by the tampa bay buccaneers . other websites . - brent bowden at nfl.com
