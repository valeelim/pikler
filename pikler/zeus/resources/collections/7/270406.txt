Unenlagia     unenlagia was a theropod from the mid-cretaceous , living about 90 million years ago . the first birds appeared 150 million years ago , evolving from dinosaurs long before `` unenlagia '' lived . therefore , it is not an ancestor of modern-day birds , but was an evolutionary dead-end . the most bird-like dinosaur ever discovered is the 90-million-year-old `` unenlagia comahuensis '' , a flightless , 4-foot tall , 7.5-foot-long carnivore .
