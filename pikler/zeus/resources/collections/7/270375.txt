Le Plateau-Mont-Royal     the plateau is a neighborhood in montreal just northeast of downtown . originally it was a middle class neighborhood containing large québécois , portuguese and jewish population . in the 1980s , many artists moved into the plateau attracted by low rent and the beautiful architecture . today the plateau is a very expensive area to live in with many attractive shops and restaurants . the neighborhood maintains an old world charm with its beautiful architecture . it is certainly one of the most beautiful places in montreal .
