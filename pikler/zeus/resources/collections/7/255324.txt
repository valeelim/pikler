Cryme Tyme     cryme tyme was a professional wrestling tag team that was signed to world wrestling entertainment ( wwe ) and wrestled on their smackdown brand . the team wrestled together for 4 years until they broke up on april 2 , 2010. the team consisted of shad gaspard and jtg . championship . - ohio valley wrestling - ovw southern tag team championship ( two times ) other websites . - jtg on wwe.com - shad gaspard on online world of wrestling
