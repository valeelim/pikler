Ghee     ghee is a product made from butter . it is used in indian and pakistani cookings as a form of oil or cooking fat . it is made by boiling butter until all the water has evaporated out and the milk solids have settled to the bottom . ghee can be made out of many different kinds of milk like cow , sheep , and buffalo milk . ghee aged 10 to 100 years , called kumbhiighrta , is believed to have medicinal properties .
