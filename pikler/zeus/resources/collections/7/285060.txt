Dennis Pitta     dennis pitta ( born june 29 , 1985 in fresno , california ) is an american football tight end for the baltimore ravens of the national football league ( nfl ) . pitta was drafted out of the brigham young university by the baltimore ravens with the 114th pick in the fourth round of the 2010 nfl draft . he won super bowl xlvii with the ravens when they defeated the san francisco 49ers 34-31. other websites . - dennis pitta at nfl.com
