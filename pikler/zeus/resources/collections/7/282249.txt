Maratha, Santalaris and Aloda massacre    maratha , santalaris and aloda massacre maratha , santalaris and aloda massacre ( turkish : muratağa , sandallar ve atlılar katliamı ) was a massacre of totally 126 turkish cypriots in the villages of maratha ( muratağa ) , santalaris ( sandallar ) and aloda ( atlılar ) in cyprus . it happened on 14-16 august 1974. the massacre was committed by eoka-b . 89 ( or 84 ) people were killed in maratha and santalaris . another 37 people were killed in aloda . old people and children were also killed . only three people could escape from the massacre in aloda . a bulldozer buried people into mass graves . people from maratha and santalaris were buried in the same grave .
