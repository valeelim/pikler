Frank Oz     frank oz ( born richard frank oznowicz on may 25 , 1944 ) is an american puppeteer and filmmaker . he is known for his work on `` the muppets '' , `` star wars '' , and `` sesame street '' . he also directed some popular films , such as `` little shop of horrors '' and `` dirty rotten scoundrels '' . his most popular puppets are yoda and cookie monster .
