Nissan Juke     the nissan juke is a mini suv that is built by the nissan motor company . the juke has had 1 generation during its time . the assembly for the nissan juke is in oppama , japan and sunderland , united kingdom . the juke made its debut at the 2010 geneva motor show in march , and made its north america debut at the 2010 new york international auto show . other websites . - nissan juke at nissan.ca
