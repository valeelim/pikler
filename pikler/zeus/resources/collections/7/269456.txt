Martin Luther (1953 movie)    martin luther ( 1953 movie ) martin luther is a 1953 movie biography of martin luther . it was directed by irving pichel , who also plays a supporting role . the movie starred niall macginnis as luther . the movie begins in the year 1505. luther goes into a monastery in erfurt , but he is in fear of god . one day he finds god 's mercy in the new testament ( in romans 1:17 ) . later he published his ninety-five theses . in the year 1521 , in the view of the pope , he should recant on the diet of worms . but there he does it not . instead he says `` here i stand '' . the movie ends with the presentation of the augsburg confession . related pages . - luther film
