Book of Optics     the book of optics is a book that was written by ibn al haytham ( 965â€“1040 ce ) , an arabic scientist and polymath . it was a seven-volume treatise on optics , physics , mathematics , anatomy and psychology , from 1011 to 1021. it was originally written in arabic and was later translated into persian , latin and italian within the next several centuries . the book had an important influence on the development of optics and on science in general because it introduced the experimental scientific method . ibn al-haytham has been called the `` father of modern optics '' . ibn al-haytham wrote more than 200 works on a wide range of subjects , of which at least 96 of his scientific works are known .
