Renault 5     the renault 5 is a automobile produced by renault from 1972 to 1996. it was sold in two generations with the second arriving in 1985. it was replaced by the renault clio in 1990 but it remained on sale until 1996 as the budget campus model . it is available as hatchback with three or five doors , and with 4-cylinder petrol and diesel engines from 35 to 94 hp , and as the `` renault 5 turbo '' with 110 or 160 hp .
