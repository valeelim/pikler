Baba O'Riley     `` baba o'riley '' is a song by the english rock band the who , written by pete townshend . roger daltrey sings most of the song , with pete townshend singing the middle eight : `` do n't cry/do n't raise your eye/it 's only teenage wasteland '' . the title of the song is comes from this combination of the song 's philosophical and musical influences : meher baba and terry riley .
