The Gaslight Anthem     the gaslight anthem is an american punk band from new brunswick , new jersey . the members are brian fallon ( lead vocals , guitar ) , alex rosamilia ( guitar , backing vocals ) , alex levine ( bass guitar , backing vocals ) and benny horowitz ( drums , percussion , tambourine ) . they released their first album , `` sink or swim '' , on xoxo records in may 2007 , and their second album , `` the '59 sound '' , on sideonedummy records in august 2008. the band 's third album , `` american slang '' , was released on june 15 , 2010 .
