Frances McDormand     frances mcdormand ( born june 23 , 1957 ) is an american movie , television and stage actress . she won the academy award for `` best actress '' for her portrayal of marge in 1996 's `` fargo '' . she was nominated three times for `` best supporting actress '' . mcdormand was born in chicago , illinois . she is married to joel coen since 1984 and has two adopted sons . they currently live in new york city .
