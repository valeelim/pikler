Serum amyloid A     serum amyloid a ( saa ) is a group of proteins which flow in the blood . they are related to `` good cholesterol '' . almost all animals have saa 's . saa 's are made in and controlled by the liver . they move cholesterol to the liver . saas increase during inflammation . doctors and scientists think that saas may be part of amyloidosis , heart disease , and rheumatoid arthritis .
