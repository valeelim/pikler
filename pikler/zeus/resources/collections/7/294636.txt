Unsportsmanlike conduct     unsportsmanlike conduct ( or unsporting behaviour , or ungentlemanly conduct ) describes a kind of action by professional sportspeople . it is when a player or team during the game that is not professional or is offensive . it is not cheating or breaking the rules . unsportsmanlike conduct is something most players and fans dislike . the official rules of many sports include a rule so that a referee can call unsportsmanlike conduct on a team or player . there may be a fine or penalty .
