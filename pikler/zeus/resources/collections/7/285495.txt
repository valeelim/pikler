University of Montana – Missoula     university of montana – missoula is a public university in missoula , montana . the university has about 15,642 students . it was founded in 1893. the current president of the university is royce engstrom . the athletics teams of the university are called the grizzlies except for the women 's basketball team , which is called the lady griz . they have 14 varsity teams and they compete in the big sky conference . other websites . - university of montana – missoula 's official website - montana grizzlies ' official athletics website
