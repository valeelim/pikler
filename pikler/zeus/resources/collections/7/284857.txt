My Darkest Days     my darkest days is a canadian rock band from norwood , ontario . my darkest days has five members in the band ; the current band members are matt walst , sal costa , brendan mcmillan , doug oliver and reid henry . the band was formed in 2006 by lead singer matt walst . they released their self-titled debut album `` '' my darkest days '' '' on september 21 , 2010. they are currently signed to 604 records . they released their second studio album `` '' sick and twisted affair '' '' on march 26 , 2012. band members . - current - matt walst – lead vocals , rhythm guitar - sal costa – lead guitar , vocals - brendan mcmillan – bass guitar - doug oliver – drums , percussion - reid henry – keyboards - former - paulo neta – lead guitar - chris macmillan - lead guitar so far as of 2012 , my darkest days have released 2 studio album : - `` my darkest days '' ( 2010 ) - `` sick and twisted affair '' ( 2012 ) singles . - `` porn star dancing '' ( 2010 ) - `` move your body '' ( 2011 ) - `` every lie '' ( 2011 ) - `` casual sex '' ( 2012 ) - `` sick and twisted affair '' ( 2012 )
