Mockumentary     a mockumentary is a movie that pretends to be a documentary . it pretends to tell a true story , but is really a work of fiction . the word is a combination ( a portmanteau word ) of `` mock '' ( meaning fake or false ) and `` documentary '' . other websites . - websters dictionary definition - lists of mockumentary at uc berkeley mockumentary collection - mockumentary - reflexivity , satire and a call to play
