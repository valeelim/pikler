Australidelphia     the australidelphia is a superorder of australasian marsupial mammals . it consists in five extant ( living ) orders , which contain most of the world 's marsupials . - superorder australodelphia - order microbiotheria - order dasyuromorphia - order peramelemorphia - order notoryctemorphia - order diprotodontia - order yalkaparidontia ( extinct )
