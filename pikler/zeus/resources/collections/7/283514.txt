Bobby Hull     robert marvin `` bobby '' hull , oc ( born january 3 , 1939 in pointe anne , ontario ) is a canadian retired ice hockey left winger . hull is highly regarded as one of the greatest ice hockey players of all time and perhaps the greatest left winger to ever play the game of hockey . hull was known for his blonde hair , blinding skating speed , and having the fastest shot in the game which also earned him the nickname , `` the golden jet '' . hull played 17 seasons in the nhl with 3 teams , the chicago black hawks for 15 seasons , the winnipeg jets for 1 season and the hartford whalers for 1 season . hull was inducted in the hockey hall of fame in 1983. other websites . - bobby hull 's nhl player profile - bobby hull at the internet hockey database - bobby hull 's legend 's of hockey profile
