XPilot     xpilot is multiplayer 2d space action video game . it is an open source game , and its first version was released in 1991 . `` xpilot '' was created in 1991 by bjørn stabell and ken ronny schouten , students in computer science at the tromsø university ( norway ) , as a multiplayer game similar to `` thrust '' . they received a lot of patches and feedback when they released the first public version . the game has been improved a lot by players from all the world and has many contributors . originally only available on unix , it was ported on the mac os and microsoft windows . it has been forked and new versions appeared : xpilot 5 and xpilot ng ( next generation ) . in 2009 a version was released for iphone and itouch . several projects are currently in progress : a java version of xpilot , an optimisate version for the `` blood 's music '' map and a project to improve the artificial intelligence . other websites . - official page of the original `` xpilot '' - page of `` xpilot 5 '' and `` xpilot nt '' - page of `` xpilot ng ''
