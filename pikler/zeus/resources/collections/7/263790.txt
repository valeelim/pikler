Tetrasulfur tetranitride     tetrasulfur tetranitride , also known as tetrasulphur tetranitride , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is sn . it contains sulfur in its +3 oxidation state . it also contains nitride ions . it is a gold solid . it is an explosive , releasing sulfur and nitrogen when detonated . the purer the substance is , the more easily it explodes . it is pale yellow when cold , orange at normal temperature , and red when hot . it reacts with bases . it can be split into disulfur dinitride when it is reacted with silver . it does not dissolve in water . it can be made by reacting disulfur dichloride with ammonia . it can also be made by reacting ammonium chloride with disulfur dichloride . it can be used to react with many organic compounds . it is an explosive , so it should not be ground . explosion releases harmless nitrogen and sulfur . related pages . - nitrate - sulfate
