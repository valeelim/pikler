Drew Brees     drew brees ( ; born january 15 , 1979 ) is the starting quarterback for the new orleans saints . a native of austin , texas , he played college football for purdue and was drafted by the san diego chargers in 2001 , where he was their starting quarterback until 2006. in 2009 , he led the saints to a super bowl championship and was named mvp of the game . other websites . - the brees dream foundation - new orleans saints bio
