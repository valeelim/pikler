Superbad (movie)    superbad ( movie ) superbad is a 2007 american teen sex comedy movie . it was produced by judd apatow , evan goldberg , seth rogen and shauna robertson and was directed by greg mottola . superbad was released on august 17 , 2007 in north america . the movie received positive reviews with a 87 % rating on rotten tomatoes and a 76 out of 100 from metacritic . cast . - jonah hill as seth - michael cera as evan - christopher mintz-plasse as fogell - bill hader as officer slater - seth rogen as officer michaels - martha macisaac as becca - emma stone as jules - aviva farber as nicola - joe lo truglio as francis the driver - kevin corrigan as mark - danny mcbride as buddy at ( 5th and paysview ) party - clark duke as teen at the party other websites . - superbad 's official website
