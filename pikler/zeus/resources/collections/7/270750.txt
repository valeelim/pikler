Constructivism     constructivism was an artistic and architectural movement that originated in russia from 1913 by vladimar tatlin onward which rejected the idea of `` art for art 's sake '' in favour of art for social purposes . it heavily influenced graphic and industrial designers . constructivism as an active force lasted until around 1934 , having a great deal of effect on the art of the weimar republic and elsewhere , before being replaced by socialist realism . its motifs have sporadically recurred in other art movements since .
