Somewhere in Time (album)    somewhere in time ( album ) somewhere in time is a studio album by iron maiden . it went on sale on 29 june 1986. it was the third most popular album in england when it started being sold . the album got average reviews ; one reviewer said that it was `` less inspired '' than iron maiden 's earlier albums , but that the first half of it was still good . musicians . - bruce dickinson – vocals - dave murray – guitar , guitar synth - adrian smith – guitar , backing vocals - steve harris – bass guitar , backing vocals , bass synth - nicko mcbrain – drums
