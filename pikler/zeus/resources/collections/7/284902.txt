Dynasty (TV series)    dynasty ( tv series ) dynasty was an american primetime soap opera which aired on abc beginning in january 1981 and ending in may 1989. starring in the series were john forsythe , heather locklear , linda evans , pamela sue martin and joan collins . this show was focused on the rich carrington family from denver , colorado. < br > between 1985 and 1987 a spin-off show `` the colbys '' was being made .
