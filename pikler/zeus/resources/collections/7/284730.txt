University of New Mexico     the university of new mexico is a public university in albuquerque , new mexico . the university has about 34,674 students . it was founded in 1889. the current president of the university is david j. schmidly . the athletics teams of the university are called the lobos . they have 16 varsity teams , most of which compete in the mountain west conference ( mw ) . new mexico has separate conference memberships in two sports that the mw does not sponsor . in skiing , the lobos are members of the rocky mountain intercollegiate ski association . in men 's soccer , new mexico is a member of conference usa . other websites . - university of new mexico 's official website - new mexico lobos ' official athletics website
