Telluric acid     telluric acid is a chemical compound . it is an acid . its chemical formula is hteo . it contains hydrogen and tellurate ions . it contains the teo form of the tellurate ion . telluric acid is a weak acid . it reacts with strong bases to make tellurates . it is a powerful oxidizing agents . it can be dehydrated to make tellurium trioxide . the metatelluric acid , hteo , does not exist . telluric acid can be made by reacting tellurium dioxide with chromium trioxide or hydrogen peroxide . it is used to make other tellurates such as sodium tellurate . related pages . - tellurous acid - sodium tellurite
