Air door     an air door or air curtain is a machine used for separating two spaces from each other . the machine is mainly used at doors into buildings . the most common setup for air curtains is a downward-facing blower fan placed over an opening . normally this opening is an door to a building the air curtain keeps flying insects out by making turbulence . it also helps keep outside air out , avoiding cold winds by mixing-in warm air from the air curtain . the fan must be powerful enough to generate a jet of air that can reach the floor .
