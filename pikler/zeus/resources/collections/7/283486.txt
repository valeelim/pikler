Shawinigan     shawinigan is a city on the saint-maurice river in quebec , canada . in 2011 , 50060 people lived there . michel angers was elected as mayor in the 2009 municipal election . the name of the city has also been spelled different over periods of time : chaouinigane , oshaouinigane , assaouinigane , achawénégan , chawinigame , shawenigane , chaouénigane and could either mean `` portage '' , `` portage of beeches '' , `` angular portage '' or `` summit '' or `` crest '' . famous residents . - jean chrétien , 20th prime minister of canada - jacques plante , former ice hockey goaltender other websites . - city of shawinigan - official city website
