Ulster Unionist Party     the ulster unionist party or uup for short is a centre-right political party in northern ireland . it wants northern ireland to stay in the united kingdom and is also moderately against the european union . it has 18 out of 108 seats in the northern ireland assembly and 1 of 3 northern ireland seats in the european parliament but has no seats in the house of commons of the united kingdom . it 's current leader is tom elliott . during the 2010 general election and the 2009 european parliament election , the party allied itself to the uk conservative party and called itself ulster conservatives and unionists - new force .
