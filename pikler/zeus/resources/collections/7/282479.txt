Q fever     q fever is a disease caused by `` coxiella burnetii '' , bacteria that affect humans and other animals . it is not common but can be found in cattle , sheep , goats , and other household animals , including cats and dogs . people can get infected if they inhale endospores or touch the milk , urine , feces , vaginal mucus , or semen of infected animals . the disease is rarely carried by ticks . a human can be infected by a single bacterium . other sites . - q fever at the cdc
