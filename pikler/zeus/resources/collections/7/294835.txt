Computer-aided technologies     the term cad/cam is a shortening of computer-aided design ( cad ) and computer-aided manufacturing ( cam ) . the term cad/nc ( numerical control ) is equivalent in some industries . most cad/cam software was developed for product development and the design and manufacturing of components and molds , but they are being used by architects with greater frequency . cad/cam software uses cad drawing tools to describe geometries used by the cam portion of the program to define a toolpath that will direct the motion of a machine tool to machine the exact shape that was drawn .
