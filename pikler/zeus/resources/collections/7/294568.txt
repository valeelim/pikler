Concertina     a concertina is a musical instrument that makes sounds using air pushed and pulled through reeds using a bellows . the fingers push buttons at both ends of the instrument . the anglo concertina has the bass notes at the left side and makes other tones pushing than pulling a button . the english concertina makes the same tone pushing and pulling a button . the duet concertinas have the bass notes at the left side and make the same tone pushing and pulling a button . the hayden duet concertina has the same fingering for all scales . it has a shifted 6-plus-6 keyboard layout . related pages . - bandoneon
