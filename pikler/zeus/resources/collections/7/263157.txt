Man of the match     man of the match is an award given to one player from each fifa world cup match ( game ) . this award was created and first awarded for the 2010 fifa world cup tournament . the award is sponsored by budweiser , a large world-wide beer company . the winner of the award is decided by a fan vote . the fan vote during the match . voting closed when the match is over . the budweiser man of the match trophy will be awarded to the winning player at the end of the game .
