Teddy Bears' Picnic    teddy bears ' picnic `` teddy bears ' picnic '' is a very popular song about teddy bears . the tune was composed by john walter bratton in 1907. the words were added by irish songwriter jimmy kennedy in 1932. since then it has always been extremely popular as a children 's song . many musicians have made recordings of it . a particularly famous recording was made in 1932 by henry hall . for more than 30 years this recording was used by bbc audio engineers to test audio equipment . other websites . - lyrics and audio from the bbc - - the original bbc recording along with information on val rosing , vocalist -
