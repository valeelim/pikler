Jiří Tlustý     jiří tlustý ( born march 16 , 1988 in slaný , czechoslovakia ) is a czech nhl ice hockey left winger . he currently plays for the carolina hurricanes . before playing in the nhl , tlustý played 1 season with hc kladno of the czech extraliga and 1 season with the sault ste . marie greyhounds of the ontario hockey league ( ohl ) . he was drafted by the toronto maple leafs with the 13th overall pick in the 2006 nhl entry draft . he played 3 seasons with the maple leafs . the maple leafs traded tlustý to the carolina hurricanes in exchange for philippe paradis .
