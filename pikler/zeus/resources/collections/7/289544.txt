Sundae     the sundae is an ice cream dessert . it typically consists of a scoop of ice cream topped with sauce or syrup , and in some cases other toppings including chopped nuts , sprinkles , whipped cream , or maraschino cherries . the first historically documented sundae was created on sunday , april 3 , 1892. other websites . - wisconsin historical society ice cream marker - historical documents supporting ithaca as the birthplace of the sundae
