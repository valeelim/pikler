Wheel loader     a wheel loader or a shovel uses a controlled arm to put materials into a dumptruck , onto a conveyor belt or a feed hopper . possible materials are : asphalt , demolition debris , dirt , feed , gravel , logs , raw minerals , recycled material , rock , sand , wood chips , etc . there are many types and brands of wheel loaders . the most popular brands are caterpillar , volvo , case , ahlmann and komatsu . the type most used is a front end loader .
