RCA Records     rca records ( originally the victor talking machine company , then rca victor ) is one of the flagship labels of sony music entertainment . the rca initials stand for radio corporation of america ( later renamed rca corporation ) , which was the parent corporation from 1929 to 1985 and a partner from 1985 to 1986. rca 's canadian unit the sony 's oldest label in canada , because it was only one of two canadian record labels to survive the great depression .
