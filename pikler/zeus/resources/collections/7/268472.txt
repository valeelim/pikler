Diamagnetism     diamagnetism is a type of magnetism . they very weakly repel magnets . most substances are diamagnetic . it was discovered in 1778 when someone saw that bismuth and antimony repelled a magnet . diamagnetic things can be levitated . a frog was levitated in a very strong magnet . water can be repelled by a magnet because it is diamagnetic . it is too weak to be noticed normally , though .
