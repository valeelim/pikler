Dallas (TV series)    dallas ( tv series ) dallas is an american soap opera . it is about the ewings , a family from texas involved in oil and cattle ranching . the show began as a television miniseries during april 1978. it was broadcast until may 3 , 1991 on cbs . the cast included charlene tilton , victoria principal , priscilla presley , patrick duffy , and larry hagman as j.r. ewing . the series lasted for fourteen seasons . the show was revived on tnt in june 2012 .
