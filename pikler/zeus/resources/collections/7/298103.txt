Grillz     `` grillz ' '' is a 2005 hip hop song by american rapper nelly . the song features rapper paul wall , rap duo ali & gipp and singer brandi williams . it was the 13th track from his first compilation album sweatsuit . the song was released on november 8 , 2005. the song reached # 1 on the u.s. billboard hot 100 , # 11 on the australia ( aria ) and # 24 on the uk singles chart .
