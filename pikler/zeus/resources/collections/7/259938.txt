Toyota Sienna     the toyota sienna is a family-sized minivan that is built by toyota motor corporation in princeton , indiana . three generations of the toyota sienna have been produced since 1997. both the second and third generations received an overall `` good '' score from the iihs and a 4-star rating for frontal and side impacts from the nhtsa . the second generation of the sienna was sold for the 2003 - 2010 model years with seating for 7 passengers and featured a 3.3l v6 from the start , which was later replaced by a 3.5l v6 . the third generation has been sold since the 2011 model year and is powered by the 3.5l v6 with 265 hp and still seats seven passengers . websites . - toyota sienna reviews & pictures - toyota sienna at toyota usa - toyota sienna at toyota canada
