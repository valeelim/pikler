University of South Dakota     university of south dakota is a public university in vermillion , south dakota . the university has about 10,515 students . it was founded in 1862. the current president of the university is james w. abbott . the athletics teams of the university are called the coyotes . they have 15 varsity teams , most of which compete in the summit league . the football team plays in the missouri valley football conference . other websites . - university of south dakota 's official website - south dakota coyotes ' official athletics website
