Masashi Kishimoto     masashi kishimoto ( , `` kishimoto masashi '' ) is a japanese manga artist . he is best known for creating the hugely popular manga series known as `` naruto '' . he has been drawing from a very early age . two of his former assistants , osamu kajisa ( `` tattoo hearts '' ) and yuuichi itakura ( `` hand 's '' ) , have also had success following their work on `` naruto '' . kishimoto was born 8 november 1974 in the katsuta county of okayama , japan . he has a twin-brother named seishi kishimoto . they started watching `` dragon ball '' and `` kunnikuman '' .
