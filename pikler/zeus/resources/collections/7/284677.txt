Andy Panda     'andrew `` andy '' panda ' is a fictional panda . he was created by walter lantz for universal studios in the 1930s -1940s from 1939-1949. his girlfriend was miranda panda . he introduced woody woodpecker in 1940 's knock knock . he was one of walter lantz 's staples ( the others being woody woodpecker , oswald rabbit , chilly willy ) . his last appearance was in 1949 's `` scrappy birthday '' . in comics he was often paired with charlie chicken . related pages . - woody woodpecker - winnie woodpecker - chilly willy - miranda panda - knothead and splinter
