Prem Nazir     prem nazir ( born abdul khader ; 7 april 1926 - 16 january 1989 ) was an indian actor . he acted mainly in movies made in the malayalam language . he started his career in the 1950s . he was one of the biggest stars in indian cinema and is fondly remembered as evergreen hero . he acted in lead roles in more than 600 movies , which is a world record . he also holds three more guinness world records . he got the padma bhushan , padma shri and kerala state film awards .
