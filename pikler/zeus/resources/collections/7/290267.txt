Sex shop     a sex shop is a shop that sells adult products including sex toys , pornography , lingerie and fetish goods . most countries make it so that children can not enter sex shops . some require shops to have warnings outside the shop . in the united kingdom , sex shops must not show the products on sale inside the shop so that people on the street can see them . sex shops in the united kingdom are the only place where you can buy videos rated as `` r18 '' by the british board of film classification .
