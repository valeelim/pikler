Elena Kagan     elena kagan ( born april 28 , 1960 ) is an american lawyer and judge . she is a united states supreme court justice . she was confirmed by the united states senate on august 5 , 2010. she is one of three women on the court . kagan is the only supreme court justice who had never been a judge before becoming a justice . before being a justice , she was solicitor general and dean of harvard law school . kagan was born and raised in new york city and is jewish .
