Mary Jane Bennett     mary jane bennett ( 1816 to 6 july 1885 ) was the first official lighthouse keeper in new zealand , and the only woman to have the position . mary moved to new zealand in 1840. she soon married george white bennett . in 1852 george took the position of lighthouse keeper at pencarrow head . following the death of her husband in 1855 , mary worked at the lighthouse . in january 1859 a good lighthouse building was made . she was selected as keeper . pencarrow lighthouse was the first permanent lighthouse in new zealand .
