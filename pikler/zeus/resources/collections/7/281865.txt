2009 Pacific typhoon season     the 2009 philippine region typhoon season was the period that tropical cyclones formed in the western pacific ocean . the season ran throughout the year during 2009 , with most tropical cyclones forming between may and november.the philippine atmospheric , geophysical and astronomical services administration ( pagasa ) assigns names to tropical cyclones ( including tropical depressions ) that enter or form in the philippine area of responsibility . these names , however , are not in common use outside of the philippines . during the season , 39 tropical depressions developed within the western pacific , whilst 2 formed outside the region before moving into the western pacific . storms . philippines . - tropical depression auring - tropical depression bising - typhoon crising - typhoon dante - typhoon emong - tropical storm feria - tropical depression gorio - tropical depression huaning - typhoon isang - tropical depression jolina - tropical storm kiko - tropical storm labuyo
