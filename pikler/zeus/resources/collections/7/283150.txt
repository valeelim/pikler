Mike Fisher     michael andrew fisher ( born june 5 , 1980 in peterborough , ontario ) is a canadian professional ice hockey centre . he currently plays for the nashville predators of the nhl ( nhl ) . fisher also played for the ottawa senators and played 10 seasons also becoming the assistant captain for them . on july 10 , 2010 , fisher married country music singer , carrie underwood after proposing to her on december 20 , 2009. he is a devout christian . in 2016 after the former nashville predators captain , shea weber , was traded , he was made captain of the team . other websites . - mike fisher 's official website - mike fisher 's nhl profile
