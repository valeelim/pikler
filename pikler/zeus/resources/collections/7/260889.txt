Border Collie     the border collie is a breed of dog that originated along the english-scottish border . it is one of the most intelligent dog breeds . they have been used to herd livestock for over 300 years . it is also famous for a pet or show-dog . the border collie is about 20 inches ( 51 cm ) in height and weighs 31 to 50 pounds ( 14 to 23 kg ) . it is usually a long-haired dog . its coat is often black and white in color .
