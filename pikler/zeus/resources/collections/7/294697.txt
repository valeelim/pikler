Denis Diderot     denis diderot ( ; 5 october 1713 – 31 july 1784 ) was a french philosopher , art critic and writer . he was a known figure during the enlightenment . he is best known for serving as co-founder , chief editor and contributor to the `` encyclopédie '' along with jean le rond d'alembert . diderot died from pulmonary thrombosis in paris , aged 70 .
