Saratoga Trunk     saratoga trunk is a 1945 american romance movie directed by sam wood and written by edna ferber and casey robinson . flora robson who played ingrid bergman 's servant in the movie was nominated for a academy award for best supporting actress . cast . - gary cooper as colonel clint maroon - ingrid bergman as clio dulaine - flora robson as angelique buiton - jerry austin as cupidon - john warburton as bartholomew van steed - florence bates as sophie bellop - curt bois as augustin haussy - john abbott as roscoe bean other websites . - saratoga trunk at tcm movie database
