Scottish Liberal Democrats     the scottish liberal democrats is a political party in scotland that is part of the british liberal democrats . the party 's policies usually promote liberalism and progressivism as well as the continuing of scotland as part of the united kingdom . in the scottish parliament , the party 's leader is willie rennie and the party is an opposition party with 5 out of 129 seats . the party has 1 seat out of 59 scottish seats in the house of commons of the united kingdom and 1 out of 6 scottish seats in the european parliament . other websites . - official website
