International Commission of Jurists     international commission of jurists ( icj ) is a non-governmental organization created at berlin in 1951 by specialists of law , lawyers or judges for the protection of human rights and rule of law . right now , the headquarters are situated in geneva and has relationships with united nations , council of europe and african union . this commission has also played an important role in the creation of the international criminal court and the yogyakarta principles . from 2008 to 2010 , mary robinson was the president of this commission .
