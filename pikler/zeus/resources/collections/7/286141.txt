Tim Russert     tim russert ( may 7 , 1950 - june 13 , 2008 ) was an american television journalist and lawyer . he was the longest serving moderator for nbc 's `` meet the press '' beginning in 1991. he was the senior vice president of nbc news and a frequent correspondent and guest on nbc 's `` the today show '' and `` hardball '' . he was born in buffalo , new york . russert died from a heart attack in washington , d.c .
