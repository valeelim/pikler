Serena Deeb     serena deeb ( born june 29 , 1986 in oakton , virginia ) is an american female professional wrestler . she is best known for wrestling with world wrestling entertainment on their smackdown brand under the ring name , serena , where she was part of the wrestling stable straight edge society . while part of ohio valley wrestling , serena was won the ovw women 's championship six times and was named queen of fcw one time while part of florida championship wrestling . serena was released from her wwe contract for not portraying her straight edge society gimmick in real life , but because smackdown was pre-recorded , her release was not revealed immediately , and gave her one last appearance on wwe programming which aired on august 27 2010. deeb works as a yoga teacher . other websites . - serena 's wwe profile
