Nimatullah Kassab Al-Hardini     nimatullah kassab al-hardini ( 1808-1858 ) is a catholic saint . he was a lebanese maronite father who living in kfifane , lebanon . he died in kfifane on december 14 , 1858. nimatullah was canonized on may 16 , 2004 by pope john paul ii . he is remembered on december 14 , the date of his death . other websites . - vatican biography of nimattullah kassab al-hardini
