All Saints (group)    all saints ( group ) the all saints are an english-canadian pop group . they were founded in 1993. the members of all saints are british melanie blatt , shaznay lewis and canadian sisters natalie and nicole appleton . the group were one of the most successful pop groups in the uk in the 1990s with nine top 10 singles , two platinum albums and two gold albums . albums . - 1997 : `` all saints '' - 1998 : `` the remix album '' - 2000 : `` saints & sinners '' - 2001 : `` all hits '' - 2006 : `` studio 1 '' - 2016 : red flag
