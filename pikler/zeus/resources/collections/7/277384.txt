Bernie Morris     bernard patrick morris ( b. august 21 , 1890 - d. may ? 1963 ) - was an canadian ice hockey player for the old seattle metropolitans of the pacific coast hockey association . when the metropolitans became the first u.s.-based team to win the stanley cup in 1917 , morris scored 14 of seattle 's goals ( in a best-of-five series ) . born in brandon , manitoba , morris played for several senior teams in manitoba and saskatchewan before joining the victoria aristocrats of the pacific coast hockey association ( pcha ) in 1914. he moved over to seattle the following season , and played eight seasons for seattle , and was a member of the stanley cup champion 1917 team . he played in two other finals , the ill-fated 1919 series , and the 1923 series . he had an outstanding 1917 series , scoring 14 goals in four games . in 1923 , he joined the calgary tigers and played a season and a half before joining the boston bruins of the national hockey league ( nhl ) . from 1925 unil 1930 , he finished his career with various teams in the minor california pro league , the can-pro and international league .
