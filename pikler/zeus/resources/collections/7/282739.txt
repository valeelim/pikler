Amblypygi     amblypygi is an order of arachnids also known as whip spiders or tailless whip scorpions . the name 'amblypygid ' means `` blunt rump '' , a reference to a lack of the telson ( tail ) . they are harmless to humans . the amblypygs are in the subphylum chelicerata of the phylum arthropoda . the order has 5 families , 17 genera and about 155 species . they are found in tropical and subtropical regions worldwide . some species are subterranean ; many are nocturnal . during the day , they may hide under logs , bark , stones , or leaves . they prefer a humid environment .
