Hypochlorous acid     hypochlorous acid is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is hclo or hocl . it is not stable . it contains chlorine in its +1 oxidation state . it is a powerful oxidizing agent . it is made by reacting sodium hypochlorite with an acid that can not be oxidized , such as phosphoric acid . if the acid can be oxidized , the hypochlorous acid will be destroyed . it is used to make some chemicals . it breaks down into hydrochloric acid and oxygen in sunlight . it reacts with hydrochloric acid to make chlorine gas . it is a powerful disinfectant . related pages . - perchlorate - chlorate - hypochlorite
