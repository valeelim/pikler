Fort Nelson, British Columbia    fort nelson , british columbia fort nelson is a canadian town in british columbia , canada . fort nelson is in the newly formed northern rockies regional municipality and is also in the northeastern corner of british columbia . the population of fort nelson was 4,514 at the 2006 census . around fort nelson is fort nelson river , muskwa river and prophet river . the forests in fort nelson are part of canada 's boreal forest . other websites . - town of fort nelson 's official website
