Sphalerite     sphalerite is a mineral . its is a mixed zinc and iron sulfide . the chemical formula is ( zn , fe ) s. it can be brown , yellow , red , green , and black . it is normally found with galena , a lead ore. it is also known as zinc blende . it has a mohs hardness of 3.5 to 4. it is the main ore of zinc . it has a non-metalic sheen .
