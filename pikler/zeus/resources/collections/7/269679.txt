Year of the Four Emperors     the year of the four emperors was a year in the history of the roman empire , ad 69 , in which four emperors ruled in a remarkable turnover . the four emperors were galba , otho , vitellius , and vespasian . the suicide of emperor nero , in 68 , was followed by a brief period of civil war , the first roman civil war since mark antony 's death in 30 bc . between june of 68 and december of 69 , rome witnessed the successive rise and fall of galba , otho and vitellius until the final accession of vespasian , the first ruler of the flavian dynasty . this was an example of the political disturbances which occurred in the history of the roman empire . the military and political anarchy created by this civil war had serious repercussions , such as the outbreak of the batavian rebellion . the jewish revolt was already ongoing . by june of 68 , the senate took the initiative to rid itself of nero , declaring him a public enemy and galba emperor . the praetorian guard were bribed to betray nero , and nero committed suicide . galba was recognized as emperor and welcomed into the city at the head of his legions
