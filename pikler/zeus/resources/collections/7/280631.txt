Beverly Kills 50187     beverly kills 50187 is the first ep by american horrorcore band , insane clown posse . it was released on july 16 , 1993. the producers of the ep were mike e. clark , insane clown posse , esham and chris conley . the ep featured the only appearance of member greez-e , who appears on the songs `` in the haughhh ! '' and `` 17 dead '' . the ep 's name is a parody of beverly hills 90210 .
