Moses Mabhida Stadium     moses mabhida stadium is a sports stadium in durban , south africa . it was built in 2009. the stadium hosted the 2010 fifa world cup . is one of the 10 stadiums where world cup games were played . fifa calls this stadium `` durban stadium '' . 2010 world cup matches all times are given in south african standard time ( utc+2 ) . other websites . - official moses mabhida stadium site - fédération internationale de football association ( fifa )
