Lead(II) chloride    lead ( ii ) chloride lead ( ii ) chloride , also known as plumbous chloride and lead dichloride , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is pbcl . it contains lead and chloride ions . the lead is in the +2 oxidation state . its use may lead to lead poisoning . lead ( ii ) chloride is a colorless crystalline solid . it does not dissolve well in water . it can dissolve in solutions that have chloride ions in them . it reacts with molten sodium nitrite to make lead ( ii ) oxide . lead ( ii ) chloride is made by reacting sodium chloride or hydrochloric acid with lead nitrate . it can also be made by reacting lead ( iv ) oxide with hydrochloric acid . it can also be made by reacting chlorine with lead . it is used to make ceramics . a basic chloride is used in pigments . related pages . - lead ( ii , iv ) oxide - lead ( ii ) acetate - lead ( ii ) carbonate
