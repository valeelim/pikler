St. John's University    st. john 's university st. john 's university is a private , coeducational , roman catholic university in queens , new york . the university has about 20,069 students . it was founded in 1870. the current president of the university is rev . donald j. harrington . there is also a campus in paris , france and rome , italy . the athletics teams of the university are called the red storm . they have 17 varsity teams and they compete in the big east conference . other websites . - st. john 's university 's official website - st. john 's red storm 's official athletics website
