Bromine trifluoride     bromine trifluoride is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is brf . it contains bromine and fluoride ions . the bromine is in its +3 oxidation state . it is a yellow liquid . it absorbs water . it reacts when it is placed in water . it can be dissolved in concentrated sulfuric acid . it conducts electricity . it can dissolve other fluorides . it is made by reacting cold fluorine with bromine . hot fluorine makes bromine pentafluoride . it is used to make uranium hexafluoride for uranium making . this makes the uranium able to explode in an atomic bomb . related pages . - bromine monochloride - bromine dioxide
