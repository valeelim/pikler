Weeds (TV series)    weeds ( tv series ) weeds is an american comedy television series which was on showtime from august 8 , 2005 - september 16 , 2012. the series is about a widow ( who is played by mary-louise parker ) who is a mother of two sons silas ( who is played by hunter parrish ) and shane ( who is played by alexander gould ) and she turns to selling marijuana to support her family after her husband , judah ( who was played by jeffrey dean morgan ) suddenly dies . other websites . - weeds on showcase
