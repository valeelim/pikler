Mira Sorvino     mira sorvino ( born september 28 , 1967 ) is an american actress . she received an academy award for `` best supporting actress '' for her role in the 1995 movie `` mighty aphrodite '' . she starred in many other hollywood movies . more recently she has been starring in low budget or independent movies . sorvino and actor christopher backus were married june 11 , 2004. the couple have four children . their daughters are mattea angel ( born 2004 ) and lucia ( born 2012 ) . their sons are johnny christopher king ( born 2006 ) and holden paul terry backus ( born 2009 ) .
