Peoria Rivermen     the peoria rivermen were an american professional ice hockey team that played in the american hockey league from 2005 to 2013. they play in peoria , illinois at the carver arena . the ahl announced that on june 14 , 2013 the rivermen franchise would relocate to utica , new york and be known as the utica comets for the 2013-14 ahl season . other websites . - peoria rivermen official site
