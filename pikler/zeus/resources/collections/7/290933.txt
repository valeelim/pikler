Jaclyn Linetsky     jaclyn linetsky ( january 8 , 1986 – september 8 , 2003 ) was a canadian teenage actress who has voiced many different people . she was best known for voicing caillou in the animated television show between 2000–2002 . she later played as megan in the television show `` 15/love '' . she died in a car crash on september 8 , 2003 at the age of 17 .
