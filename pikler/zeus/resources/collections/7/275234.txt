Daddy G     daddy g , ( born grantley evan marshall , , bristol , uk ) , is an english musician and a founding member of the band , massive attack . marshall joined the bristol music scene as a member of the dj group the wild bunch in the 1980s , which included two other massive attack members , robert del naja and andrew vowles . in 1986 , the wild bunch dissolved . del naja , vowles , and marshall then formed the trip-hop group massive attack , which is considered to have pioneered the `` bristol sound '' along with portishead and tricky . in addition to appearing on massive attack 's albums , marshall has also mixed a for the `` dj-kicks '' mix series .
