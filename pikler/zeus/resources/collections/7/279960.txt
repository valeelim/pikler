Shuvuuia     shuvuuia is a genus of bird-like theropod dinosaur from the later cretaceous of mongolia . it is a member of the family alvarezsauridae , small coelurosaurian dinosaurs which have short but powerful forelimbs specialized for digging . the name `` shuvuuia '' is derived from the mongolian word `` shuvuu '' meaning `` bird '' .
