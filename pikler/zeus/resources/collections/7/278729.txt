America's Got Talent    america 's got talent america 's got talent ( sometimes abbreviated as agt ) is an american reality television series on the nbc television network , and part of the global british `` got talent '' franchise . it began in june 21 , 2006. it features singers , dancers , magicians and other talents from america to compete for a contract and $ 1,000,000 and as a headliner on the las vegas strip since season 3 .
