2010 Formula One season     the 2010 formula one season was the 61st formula one season . red bull racing won its first constructors ' championship . sebastian vettel won his first drivers ' championship . he only took the championship by winning the final race of the season . vettel became the youngest world champion . the following teams and drivers will compete in the 2010 fia formula one world championship . on 8 july , 2010 , the thursday before the british grand prix , the hrt f1 team ( hrt ) announced that bruno senna would be replaced by test driver sakon yamamoto . after the british race , hrt announced that senna would return to his seat and race the number 21 car . hrt also announced that yamamoto would replace karun chandhok at the german grand prix in hockenheim . in august , mercedes release nick heidfeld from his contract . heidfeld was released so he could join pirelli and test of the new tyre for the season . in september , sauber replaced pedro de la rosa with nick heidfeld for the remainder of the season , starting with the singapore grand prix .
