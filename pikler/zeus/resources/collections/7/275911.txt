Flaviviridae     the flaviviridae are a family of viruses that are primarily spread through arthropod vectors ( mainly ticks and mosquitoes ) . the family gets its name from yellow fever virus , a type virus of flaviviridae ; `` flavus '' means `` yellow '' in latin . they include the following genera : - genus `` flavivirus '' ( type species yellow fever virus , others include west nile virus and dengue fever ) - genus `` hepacivirus '' - genus `` pestivirus ''
