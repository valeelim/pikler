Upper Volta     upper volta was a territory belonging to france from 1919 to 1932 and later from 1947 to 1958. in 1958 , upper volta became a republic . until 1960 , it was still part of the french community . in 1960 , upper volta gained independence from france . in 1984 , it became the country now known as burkina faso . references . - burkina faso ( upper volta ) at nations encyclopedia
