Mount Rowan (Victoria)    mount rowan ( victoria ) mount rowan is a small extinct volcano on the edge of the city of ballarat , victoria , australia . it was also known as waldies hill . it is 518 metres above sea level . the lava flows from the volcano are thought to be from 2.1 million to 2.9 million years ago . it is one of the 123 volcanoes in the ballarat and daylesford area . these are part of the western victorian volcanic plains . in 2009 , a geologist , professor bernie joyce warned that the volcanoes around ballarat could become active again .
