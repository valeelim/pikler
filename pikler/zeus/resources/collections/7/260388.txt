Boy with the Blues (EP)    boy with the blues ( ep ) boy with the blues is an album by the rock band oasis . it was released in early july 2009 as an itunes exclusive ep . it contained two songs not previously available on general release and also a remix of `` ( get off your ) high horse lady '' from their album `` dig out your soul '' . track listing . 1 . `` boy with the blues '' 1 . `` i believe in all '' 1 . `` ( get off your ) high horse lady '' - the may 29th devendra banhart mix the first two tracks were only available on the `` dig out your soul '' deluxe boxset bonus disc , while the third track had only been released as a listen only track on oasisinet 's radio supernova .
