Richard Holbrooke     richard charles albert holbrooke ( april 24 , 1941 - december 13 , 2010 ) was an american diplomat . he became well-known when he helped broker the dayton peace agreement , together with carl bildt . this agreement of 1995 helped end the war in bosnia-herzegovina . he died of a torn aorta on december 13 , 2010. holbrooke was born to dan and trudi holbrooke on april 24 , 1941 in manhattan , new york . he had a brother , andrew , who he attended scarsdale high school , before going to brown university and the princeton university .
