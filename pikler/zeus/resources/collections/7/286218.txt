Miami University     `` this is the university in ohio . for the university in florida , see university of miami . '' miami university is a public , coeducational university in oxford , ohio . the university has about 20,126 students . it was founded in 1809. the current president of the university is david c. hodge . the athletics teams of the university are called the redhawks . they have 8 varsity teams for men and 10 for women , most of which play in the mid-american conference . the men 's ice hockey team plays in the national collegiate hockey conference . other websites . - miami university 's official website - miami redhawks ' official athletics website
