Acetone     acetone is the organic compound with the chemical formula ( ch3 ) 2co . this clear , mobile , easy-to-burn liquid is the simplest example of the ketones . acetone can be mixed with water . it is an important solvent , often to clean things in the laboratory . common uses of acetone in the home are as the active ingredient in nail polish remover and as paint thinner . it is a common building block in organic chemistry .
