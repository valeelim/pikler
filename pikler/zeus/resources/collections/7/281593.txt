Tim Masthay     tim masthay ( born march 16 , 1987 in pittsburgh , pennsylvania ) is an american football punter for the green bay packers of the national football league ( nfl ) . masthay was not drafted but started his football career in 2009 with the indianapolis colts when he was part of their practice squad . masthay 's father , dr. mark masthay is a chairman of the chemistry department at the university of dayton and his mother is the executive director of tristate habitat for humanity in cincinnati , ohio . other websites . - tim masthay 's profile at nfl.com
