New World vulture     new world vultures are the group of vultures that are found in the americas . they form the family cathartidae . there are seven species in five genera . four of the genera contain only one species each , so they are monotypic . these are large birds of prey that feed on the meat of dead or dying animals . the name `` cathartidae '' comes from the greek word `` katharsis '' meaning to purge or make clean . unlike other birds of prey , they have a good sense of smell . the opening of their nostrils has a hole through from one side of the beak to the other . some other vultures hop to move on the ground , but the new world vultures walk . the two largest species are called condors . species . - genus `` coragyps '' - black vulture `` coragyps atratus '' in south america and north to us - genus `` cathartes '' - turkey vulture `` cathartes aura '' throughout the americas to southern canada - lesser yellow-headed vulture `` cathartes burrovianus '' in south america and north to mexico - greater yellow-headed vulture `` cathartes melambrotus '' in the amazon basin of tropical south america
