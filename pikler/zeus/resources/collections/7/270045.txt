Least Concern     least concern is a term used by the international union for the conservation of nature ( iucn ) to talk about animals that are not endangered . they do not need to be worried about as there are a lot of them found at the time of classification . examples of species and subspecies that are of least concern include : - rock pigeon - cockroach - asian tiger mosquito - common juniper - minke whale - snail kite - sacred kingfisher - house mouse - humans - pike
