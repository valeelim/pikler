The Huffington Post     the huffington post is an american news website and political blog founded in 2005. on february 7 , 2011 , aol announced it would acquire `` the huffington post '' for us $ 315 million . the huffington post prints news stories and columns ( called blogs ) by celebrities , politicians , academics , and other well known people who have been invited to write for them . the huffington post has around 25 million visitors each month and is the most well known political blog in the world . der spiegel calls the huffington post the most important source of information for millions of americans . `` the huffington post '' has both staff writers , and other individuals they have invited to write for them , from politicians and celebrities to academics and policy experts , who contribute on a wide range of topics . the term `` blog '' is used for columns written by both staff and invited columnists . - alec baldwin - lawrence bender - edgar bronfman , sr. - deepak chopra - marc cooper - bruce cohen - richard dawkins
