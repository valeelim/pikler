Canary     the canary ( `` serinus canaria '' ) , also called the island canary , atlantic canary or common canary , is a small passerine bird . it belongs to the genus `` serinus '' in the finch family . it is native to the canary islands , the azores , and madeira . wild birds are mostly yellow-green , with brownish streaking on the back . the species is common in captivity . different colour varieties have been bred . canaries are domestic birds . they are popular pets .
