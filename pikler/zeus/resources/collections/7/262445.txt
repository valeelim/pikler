Methyl iodide     methyl iodide is a chemical compound . it is made of methyl and iodide ions . its chemical formula is chi . it is a liquid that evaporates easily . when it is exposed to light , it turns purple because of iodine . this can be stopped by keeping it with copper . it breaks down when heated to make carbon dioxide , carbon monoxide , and hydrogen iodide . it is made by reacting iodine , red phosphorus , and methanol all at the same time . phosphorus ( iii ) iodide is formed during the reaction . another way to make it is to react methanol with potassium iodide and some sulfuric acid . it is used to add methyl to some organic compounds . it can also be used as a pesticide . it is used to make dimethylmercury and other chemicals . it causes nausea and dizziness when breathed . it can cause burns when put on skin . related pages . - methane - ethanol - potassium iodide
