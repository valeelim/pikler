Omniverse     the omniverse is a set or number of supposedly co-existing universes . one can imagine the stucture of an `` omniverse '' to be like that of a tree : the omniverse is the trunk , each multiverse is a branch , and each universe is a leaf . a ominiverse is closely related to the idea of parallel universe 's . in term usage universes are refereed to as the level 1 multiverse
