Antimony triiodide     antimony triiodide is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is sbi . it has antimony and iodide ions in it . the antimony is in its +3 oxidation state . antimony triiodide is a red solid . it is made by reacting antimony with iodine or by reacting concentrated hydriodic acid with antimony trioxide . it is used as a dopant . related pages . - antimony trifluoride - antimony trichloride - antimony tribromide
