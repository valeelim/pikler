Dorudon     dorudon ( `` spear-tooth '' ) was a genus of ancient cetacean . it lived alongside `` basilosaurus '' , 41 to 33 million years ago , in the eocene . they were about five meters ( 16 ft ) long and were most likely carnivorous , feeding on small fish and molluscs . `` dorudon '' lived in warm seas around the world. , fossils have been found along the former shorelines of the tethys sea in present-day western sahara , egypt , pakistan , the united states , and new zealand . most finds have been in egypt .
