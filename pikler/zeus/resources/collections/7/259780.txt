Head-Smashed-In Buffalo Jump     head-smashed-in buffalo jump is a buffalo jump in the foothills of the rocky mountains about 18 km northwest of fort macleod , alberta , canada . the plains indians used head-smashed-in buffalo jump for over 5,500 years to kill buffalo . head-smashed-in buffalo jump is a unesco world heritage site and became one in 1981. other websites . - head-smashed-in buffalo jump 's official website
