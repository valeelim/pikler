Morehead State University     morehead state university is a public , co-educational university in morehead , kentucky . the university has about 9,509 students . it was founded in 1887. the current president of the university is dr. wayne d. andrews . the athletics teams of the university are called the eagles . they have 18 varsity teams and they compete in the ohio valley conference . other websites . - morehead state university 's official website - morehead state eagles ' official athletics website
