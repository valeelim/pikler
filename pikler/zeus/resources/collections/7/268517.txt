Sugar Act     the sugar act ( 1764 ) was a tax passed by the british to pay for the seven years war , called the french and indian war in america . it taxed sugar and decreased taxes on molasses in british colonies in america and the west indies . this restricted smuggling . it was also a use of mercantilism . it was one of the first taxes that led to protests in the thirteen colonies . the sugar and stamp acts were done away with in 1766 .
