Neymar     neymar da silva santos júnior , known as neymar or neymar jr ( born on 5 february 1972 in mogi das cruzes , brazil ) , is a brazilian footballer that can play on either side of the field as a winger or he can play as a striker . he plays for the spanish team fc barcelona , of la liga , the top division of football in spain . he plays for the brazil national football team at international level . neymar joined santos in 2003 and on march 7 2009 , he made his debut for santos at 17 years of age . he scored his first goal against mogi mirim , a week after his debut . on april 15 2010 , neymar scored five goals for santos , beating guarani 8-1 in the brazilian cup . later , the club would win the cup . neymar got red carded for wearing a mask in his celebration . after neymar 's performances for brazil 's u-17 team at the 2009 u-17 world cup , on july 26 2010 , neymar was selected in the squad for the brazil team by new head coach mano menezes for a friendly match against usa team on august 10 2010. he scored on his debut , in a 2–0 win for brazil .
