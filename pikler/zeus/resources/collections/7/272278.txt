Tin(IV) sulfide    tin ( iv ) sulfide tin ( iv ) sulfide , also known as stannic sulfide , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is sns . it has tin and sulfide ions in it . the tin is in its +4 oxidation state . tin ( iv ) sulfide is a gold-colored or brown solid . it reacts with strong acids to make hydrogen sulfide . tin ( iv ) sulfide is made by reacting hydrogen sulfide with tin ( iv ) compounds such as tin ( iv ) chloride . it is used as a decorative coating because of its color . this coating is known as mosaic gold . related pages . - tin ( ii ) sulfide - tin ( iv ) oxide
