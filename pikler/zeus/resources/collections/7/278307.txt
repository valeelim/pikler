Nancy Kerrigan     nancy kerrigan ( born october 13 , 1969 ) is an american silver medalist and figure skater . during the winter olympics of 1994 , kerrigan was clubbed by the knee by an attacker . as a result , tonya harding , her figure skating rival , was banned from professional skating . kerrigan married her agent , jerry solomon , on september 9 , 1995. the couple have three children together , matthew , brian , and nicole .
