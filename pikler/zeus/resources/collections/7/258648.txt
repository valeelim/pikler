Not Afraid     `` not afraid '' is a 2010 hip hop song by the american rapper eminem , and is the first single from his seventh studio album `` recovery '' , which was released in 2010. the song was written by eminem , boi-1da , luis resto , j. evans and m. burnett . the song is about how eminem tried to overcome the troubles in his life from the past few years and mainly his drug addiction and rehabilitation .
