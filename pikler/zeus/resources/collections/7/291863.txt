Johnny Galecki     john mark `` johnny '' galecki ( born april 30 , 1975 ) is an belgian-born american actor . galecki is best known for playing the role of david healy on `` roseanne '' and leonard hofstadter on `` the big bang theory '' . galecki was born in bree , belgium to american parents . he was raised in oak park , illinois . galecki dated kaley cuoco who is his co-star on `` the big bang theory '' . they dated secretly for two years before they broke up . in february 2012 , it was revealed that galecki was dating actress kelli garner since 2011 .
