KT Tunstall     katherine victoria `` katie '' tunstall , also known as kt tunstall ( born 23 june 1975 ) , is a scottish singer and songwriter . she was born in st andrews , fife . her father is irish ; her mother is half-chinese , half-scottish . she was adopted when she was 18 days old . she has had many hit songs , including , `` black horse and the cherry tree '' ' .
