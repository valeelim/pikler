Aucasaurus     aucasaurus was a medium-sized theropod dinosaur from argentina that lived during the upper cretaceous . it was smaller than its relative `` carnotaurus '' . it had extremely short arms . its skeleton is the most complete abelisaurid skeleton discovered so far . however , the skull is damaged , causing some paleontologists to speculate that it was involved in a fight shortly before it died . this dinosaur was featured in the discovery channel series `` dinosaur planet '' , where it was depicted as a pack hunter and ate the sauropod `` saltasaurus '' . in the episode , the head injury discovered in the type specimen was interpreted as being crushed by a stumbling `` saltasaurus '' .
