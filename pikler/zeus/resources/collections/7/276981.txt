Massive Attack discography     massive attack is a british trip-hop group . it was started in 1988 by robert del naja , grantley marshall , and andrew vowles . they are in bristol , england . they made 5 studio albums , 2 extended play ( ep ) , 3 compliations , and 16 singles . i the `` massive attack '' ep and `` splitting the atom '' ep charted on the uk singles chart and swiss singles top 100 , despite being extended-play length releases .
