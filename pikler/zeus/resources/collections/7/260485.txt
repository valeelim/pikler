Steve Yzerman     stephen gregory `` steve '' yzerman ( born may 9 , 1965 ) is a canadian retired professional ice hockey centre and current general manager of the tampa bay lightning of the national hockey league . yzerman played his entire national hockey league ( nhl ) career with the detroit red wings and is a member of the hockey hall of fame . he has three daughters , the youngest being sophia yzerman , middle is maria yzerman , and the eldest is isabella yzerman . all three of them attended academy of the sacred heart in bloomfield hills , mi , with the isabella at university of michigan . he won an gold medal with team canada at the 2002 winter olympics . this made yzerman one of few players to win an olympic gold medal and the stanley cup in the same year . yzerman retired as the longest serving captain in north american sports history .
