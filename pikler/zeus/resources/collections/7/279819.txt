Pisanosaurus     pisanosaurus ( meaning `` pisano lizard '' ) is a genus of primitive ornithischian dinosaur from the upper triassic of what is now south america . it was a bipedal herbivore described by argentine paleontologist rodolfo casamiquela in 1967. only one species the type , `` pisanosaurus mertii '' , is known , based on a single partial skeleton . it is the oldest known ornithischian .
