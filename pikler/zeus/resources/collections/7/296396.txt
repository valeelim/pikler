Brief psychotic disorder     brief psychotic disorder is a short illness of less than one month that has psychotic symptoms . this could be like having schizophrenia symptoms for only an hour , but it can be any psychotic disorder , such as delusions or hallucinations . doctors describe three types of bpd . the first type is caused by some kind of traumatic stress . the second type has no known cause . some psychologists believe it could be from stimulants such as caffeine , morphine or any type of drug . there is no known cure but staying away from caffeinated drinking may lower the risk of brief psychotic disorder . the third type is caused by childbirth and usually affects mothers about four weeks after having a baby .
