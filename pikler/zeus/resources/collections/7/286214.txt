Kent State University     kent state university is a public university in kent , ohio . the university has about 41,365 students . it was founded in 1910. the current president of the university is lester lefton . the athletics teams of the university are called the golden flashes . they have 17 varsity teams and they compete in the mid-american conference . other websites . - kent state university 's official website - kent state golden flashes ' official athletics website
