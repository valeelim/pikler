Haley Joel Osment     haley joel osment ( born april 10 , 1988 ) is an american actor and voice artist . he was born in los angeles , california . he is the brother of emily osment . he had many television and movie roles throughout the 1990s . he is best known for starring in movies including `` forrest gump '' ( 1994 ) and `` the sixth sense '' ( 1999 ) .
