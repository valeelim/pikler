Mathias Grünewald     mathias grünewald ( born between 1475 and 1480 in würzburg , died 31 august 1528 ) was a german painter and artist . today he is seen as one of the important german artists of the renaissance . his real name was `` mathias gothart '' or `` mathias neidhardt '' , his first name is also spelled `` mathis '' . sometimes he is called mathias of aschaffenburg . today , only ten paintings and 35 drawings survive . at first many of his works were attributed to albrecht dürer , even though dürer 's style of paining is completely different . all the surviving works have religious motifs .
