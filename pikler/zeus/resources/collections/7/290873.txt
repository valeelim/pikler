Caillou     caillou is an american/canadian children 's tv show based on the series of books by author christine l'heureux and illustrator hélène desputeaux . it is about a young 4-year-old who lives with his dad named boris , his mom named doris , and his 2-year-old sister named rosie . caillou lives in a blue house on 17 pine street . caillou was first voiced by bryn mcauley , then jaclyn linetsky , but due to her death in 2003 , jaclyn was replaced with annie bovaird . the show is about a young boy named caillou who lives with his mom , doris ; his dad , boris ; his little sister , rosie ; and his pet cat , gilbert . mr. hinkle lives next door to caillou 's house and caillou has a babysitter named julie . in the first season , caillou was 2–3 years old and wore a gray shirt , but later , he wore a yellow shirt . in season 4 , caillou started going to preschool . the series originally aired on pbs kids in the united states and teletoon in canada .
