Janusz Palikot     janusz palikot , born in biłgoraj on october 26 , 1964 , is a polish politician , activist and businessman known for his controversial remarks . he was elected to the sejm twice—on september 25 , 2005 , when he got over 26,000 votes in 6 lublin district , running on the platforma obywatelska ( po ) list and getting over 44,000 votes in 2007. he left the civic platform on october 6 , 2010 and started his own party , modern poland on october 22 .
