David Moss (ice hockey)    david moss ( ice hockey ) david moss ( born december 28 , 1981 in livonia , michigan ) is an nhl ice hockey right winger for the calgary flames . he was picked by the calgary flames with the 220th overall pick in the 2001 nhl entry draft . he played college hockey at the university of michigan with the michigan wolverines ice hockey team . moss is the cousin of current toronto maple leaf , phil kessel .
