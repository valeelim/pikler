Tarawih     tarawih ( تراويح ) also spelled taraweeh , are extra prayers which muslims read at night in the month of ramadan . the details of how the tarawih are to be said is different between the different branches of islam . tarawih prayers are made up of a number of prayer phrases and actions , raka ‘ āt , said in pairs . hanafis and shafi'i say there are 20 raka ‘ āt , some hanabalis say it is only eight , while others say 20. malikis say there are 36 , and the ahl al-hadeeth say it is 8 .
