California State University, Sacramento    california state university , sacramento california state university , sacramento , also known as sacramento state university , is a public university in sacramento , california . the university has about 29,241 students . it was founded in 1947. the current president of the university is alexander gonzalez . the men 's and women 's sports teams of the university are called the hornets . they have 21 varsity teams , almost all of which play in the big sky conference . the baseball team plays in the western athletic conference , and the women 's rowing team is in the american athletic conference . other websites . - california state university , sacramento 's official website - sacramento state hornets ' official athletics website
