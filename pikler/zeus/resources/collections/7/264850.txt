The Lesser Polish Way of Saint James     the lesser polish way of saint james is a popular pilgrimage route in eastern europe . this particular route was created in poland . it starts in the city of sandomierz , and end in the city of krakow began in medieval times when many pilgrims travelled through europe to santiago de compostela . the other places on the lesser polish way are : klimontów , szczaworyż , wiślica , and więcławice . other websites . - website of parish of st. james in więcławice with description of the route in polish - possible accommodation - facebook funpage
