Lesser Poland Voivodeship     lesser poland voivodeship , or małopolska province , is a voivodeship , or province , in southern poland . it is , and as of 2006 , has 3,267,731 residents . it was created on 1 january 1999. the capital of the province is kraków . the province 's natural borders are świętokrzyskie mountains ( `` góry świętokrzyskie '' ) on the north , `` jura krakowsko-częstochowska '' ( a broad range of hills stretching from kraków to częstochowa ) on the west , and the tatra , pieniny and beskidy mountains on the south . the provinces and countries it is bordered by are the silesian voivodeship to the west , świętokrzyskie voivodeship to the north , subcarpathian voivodeship to the east , and the country of slovakia to the south .
