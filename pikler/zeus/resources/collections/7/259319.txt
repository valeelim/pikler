The Cheeky Girls     the cheeky girls are a female bubblegum pop/europop duo based in great britain . they are identical twins from transylvania , romania . they have been active since 2002 when they auditioned in ' . their songs include `` the cheeky song ( touch my bum ) '' , `` take your shoes off '' , `` ( hooray , hooray ! ) it 's a cheeky holiday ! `` , `` have a cheeky christmas '' , `` cheeky flamenco '' and `` boys and girls ( xmas time love ) '' . they released another single named `` the farmyard hokey '' , but it did not chart . they also have one album , `` party time as in my mind ( is a different world - a cheeky one ) '' . this album did not chart in the official albums chart . they are part of multiply record label . their real names are gabriela and monica irimia .
