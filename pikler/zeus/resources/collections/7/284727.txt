University of Wyoming     the university of wyoming is a public university in laramie , wyoming . the university has about 13,657 students . it was founded in 1886. the current president of the university is tom buchanan . dick cheney went to the university of wyoming . the athletics teams of the university are called the cowboys for the men and the cowgirls for the women . they are commonly known as the pokes . they have 17 varsity teams and they compete in the mountain west conference . other websites . - university of wyoming 's official website - wyoming cowboys ' official athletics website
