Mercury battery     a mercury battery is a primary cell . it has similar performance to , but is much cheaper than , silver oxide batteries . its disadvantage is that it contains toxic mercury , which pollutes the environment . this battery uses the oxidation of zinc by mercury ( ii ) oxide to generate electricity . it has either sodium hydroxide or potassium hydroxide as the electrolyte . mercury batteries used to be common but have become increasingly restricted . some advantages of mercury batteries are their very long life and steady voltage . other pages . - battery - primary cell - table of batteries - dry cell
