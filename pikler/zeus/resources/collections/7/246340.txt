Institution of Structural Engineers     the institution of structural engineers ( istructe ) is an organisation for structural engineering based in the united kingdom . it has 23,600 members in 105 countries . the institution is a learned body and provider of professional accreditation for structural engineers . the institution publishes a monthly journal , `` the structural engineer '' . other websites . - www.istructe.org – official website - www.istructe.ie – republic of ireland branch website - structural awards 07
