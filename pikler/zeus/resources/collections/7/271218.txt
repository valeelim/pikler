Dicalcium phosphate     dicalcium phosphate is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is cahpo . it contains calcium , hydrogen , and phosphate ions . dicalcium phosphate is a white solid . it does not dissolve in water . it can be thought of as phosphoric acid where two of the hydrogen ions are replaced with calcium ions . it can be made by reacting phosphoric acid with calcium hydroxide . it can also be made by reacting sulfuric acid with a certain amount of tricalcium phosphate . it is used in fertilizers . it is also used in vitamin tablets . it is used to make sure chickens get calcium . related pages . - monocalcium phosphate - tricalcium phosphate
