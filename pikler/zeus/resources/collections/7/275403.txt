Thallium(I) sulfide    thallium ( i ) sulfide thallium ( i ) sulfide , also known as thallous sulfide , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is tls . it has thallium and sulfide ions in it . the thallium is in its +1 oxidation state . thallium ( i ) sulfide is a black solid . it reacts with acids to make hydrogen sulfide and a thallium salt . it is found as a mineral called carlinite , but it is rare . it is the only thallium sulfide mineral that does not have another metal in it . it is made in the laboratory by reacting thallium ( i ) sulfate with hydrogen sulfide or by heating thallium and sulfur together . it was used in some of the first infrared detectors . one of the first reliable infrared detectors used thallium sulfide and it was made in world war ii . related pages . - thallium ( i ) chloride - thallium ( iii ) oxide - thallium ( i ) oxide
