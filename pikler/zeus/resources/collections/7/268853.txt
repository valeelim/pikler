Polacanthus     polacanthus was an armoured , spiked , plant-eating dinosaur from the lower cretaceous , about 132 to 100 million years ago . `` polacanthus '' means `` many spines . '' some paleontologists think that `` polacanthus '' is the same genus as `` hylaeosaurus '' . fossils of this ankylosaurid have been found in england . `` polacanthus '' was about 12 feet ( 4 m ) long and about 4.8 feet ( 1.5 m ) tall . it was a bulky quadruped that relied on its many rows of bony spikes for protection . no head is known for this genus .
