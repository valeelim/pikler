Unwritten (song)    unwritten ( song ) `` unwritten '' is a 2004 single by singer natasha bedingfield , although the song was not released in the united states until 2006. the song is from her debut record , also titled `` unwritten '' . this song hit number five on the `` billboard '' hot 100 and became bedingfield 's first top ten hit single in the united states . this single and `` pocketful of sunshine '' are among bedingfield 's most successful singles .
