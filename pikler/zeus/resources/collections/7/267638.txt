Model rocket     a model rocket is a miniature rocket . they are not launched by the government or a space program . they are used by people interested in rockets . they are normally made of light ( not heavy ) materials . even though it seems dangerous , it is quite safe and is a way many people start getting interested in rockets and space exploration . rocket motors come in many different sizes , and they are indicated by a code on the motor . there are also many ways for the rocket to fall back down after it is ignited . some of them use parachutes . other websites . - faa launch regulations for the united states
