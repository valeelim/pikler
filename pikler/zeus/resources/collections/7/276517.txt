Armidale, New South Wales    armidale , new south wales armidale is a city in new south wales , australia . as of 2006 it had a population of 19,485. it is found half-way between brisbane and sydney . it has an average altitude of 980 meters . it has a cool climate . it also has four seasons , unlike other parts of australia . armidale sometimes gets hailstorms . it was proclaimed a city in 1885. it has passenger trains to and from sydney . there are bicycling areas in some parts of the city .
