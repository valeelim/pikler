The Usos     the usos ( born august 22 , 1985 ) are a professional wrestling tag team consisting of identical twin brothers jimmy and jey uso . they are signed to the wwe jimmy ( real name jonathan solofa fatu ) and jey ( real name joshua samuel fatu ) are the twin sons of solofa fatu , better known as rikishi , and are part of the anoa'i family . in wrestling . - finishing moves - double diving splash - signature moves - assisted samoan drop - managers - tamina snuka - naomi championships and accomplishments . - florida championship wrestling - fcw florida tag team championship ( 1 time ) - wwe - wwe tag team championship ( 2 times ) - slammy award for wwe tag team of the year ( 2014 ) - pro wrestling illustrated - pwi tag team of the year ( 2014 ) - pwi ranked jimmy # 25 of the top 500 singles wrestlers in the pwi 500 in 2014 . - pwi ranked jey # 26 of the top 500 singles wrestlers in the pwi 500 in 2014 .
