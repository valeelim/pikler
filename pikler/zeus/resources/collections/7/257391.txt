Leominster     leominster is a small town in the english county of herefordshire . the name is said as `` lemster '' . leominster is about 12 miles north of hereford and 11 miles south of ludlow . there are about 11,000 people living in leominster . it is a historic market town going back to the seventh century . markets are still held every friday in corn square . the leominster priory , originally a monastery , began in 663 ad .
