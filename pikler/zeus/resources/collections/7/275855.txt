Flame retardant     a flame retardant is a chemical substance that makes things harder to burn . some flame retardants are minerals or inorganic compounds like antimony trioxide or asbestos . other flame retardants are special types of plastics and polymers . there are several ways flame retardants work . some absorb heat when they are heated , stopping the thing from igniting . others stop the heat from the fire from going into the thing . flame retardants are used in textiles and bedding to prevent them from burning easily .
