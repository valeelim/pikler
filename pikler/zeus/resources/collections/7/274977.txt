Tony Hrkac     anthony j . `` tony '' hrkac ( ) ( born july 7 , 1966 in thunder bay , ontario ) is a canadian professional ice hockey centre currently playing for the houston aeros of the ahl . awards and achievements . - hobey baker award ( top player in ncaa ) : 1986–1987 season - led the ihl in points scored ( 132 ) and was named league mvp as a member of the indianapolis ice in 1992–93 . - hrkac 's # 26 was retired by the ahl 's milwaukee admirals on march 14 , 2008. other websites . - profile at hockeydraftcentral.com
