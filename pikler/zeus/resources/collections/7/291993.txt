Suspension (chemistry)    suspension ( chemistry ) in chemistry , a suspension is a mixture of two or more components . in a suspension , very small pieces of solid are spread through a liquid but do not dissolve . if left still , the solid pieces will separate from the liquid and either fall to the bottom or rise to the top . sand in water is an example of suspension . suspensions may separate quickly or stay suspended for a long time , depending on what they contain . if a liquid is suspended in another liquid , it is called an emulsion . milk is an emulsion . if a liquid is suspended in a gas it is an aerosol . mist is an aerosol .
