Abbotsford Heat     the abbotsford heat were a canadian professional ice hockey team that began playing in 2009. they played for 5 seasons in the american hockey league and were the farm team of the calgary flames of the nhl . they played their home games in abbotsford , british columbia at the abbotsford entertainment & sports centre . the team struggled with financial problems and had low attendance near the end . the city of abbotsford terminated their contract with the heat on april 15 , 2014. on may 5 , 2014 , the ahl 's board of governors said at their spring meeting in chicago that they had approved the relocation of the team to glens falls , new york for the 2014-15 ahl season . other websites . - official website
