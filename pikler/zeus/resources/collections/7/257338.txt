Brad Paisley     bradley douglas paisley ( born october 28 , 1972 ) is an american country music singer-songwriter and guitarist . paisley has won three grammy awards : one in 2008 for `` best country instrumental performance '' for `` '' throttleneck '' , one in 2009 again for `` best country instrumental performance '' this time for `` cluster pluck '' and one in 2009 for `` best male country vocal performance '' for `` letter to me '' '' paisley was born in glen dale , west virginia . paisley is married to american actress , kimberly williams-paisley and they were wed on march 15 , 2003 , at stauffer chapel on the campus of pepperdine university . other websites . - brad paisley 's official website
