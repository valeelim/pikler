Elimination Chamber     elimination chamber is a professional wrestling pay-per-view ( ppv ) event held by world wrestling entertainment ( wwe ) . the event replaced the no way out pay-per-view except in germany where it is still called , no way out . wwe released a dvd with the title `` satan 's prison : the anthology of the elimination chamber '' ( `` iron will '' in europe ) , which included every elimination chamber match up until elimination chamber 2010 , in july of 2010. the dvd is very edited mostly because of chris benoit . other websites . - elimination chamber description at wwe.com
