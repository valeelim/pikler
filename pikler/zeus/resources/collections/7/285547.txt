University of South Carolina Upstate     university of south carolina upstate is a public university in spartanburg , south carolina . the university has about 4,608 students . it was founded in 1967. the current chancellor of the university is dr. john c. stockwell . the athletics teams of the university are called the spartans . they have 13 varsity teams . they compete in the atlantic sun conference . other websites . - university of south carolina upstate 's official website - south carolina upstate spartans ' official athletics website
