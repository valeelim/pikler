Soulmate (song)    soulmate ( song ) `` soulmate '' is a 2007 song by the english singer and songwriter natasha bedingfield . the song was released during july 2007 in united kingdom . it was not until late 2008 that this song was released in the united states . it got mixed to negative review from critics within the united kingdom , though soulmate did become commercial success . this song gained popularity in the u.s. after being played on an episode of mtv 's `` the hills '' .
