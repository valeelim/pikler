Tomáš Kaberle     tomáš kaberle ( born march 2 , 1978 in rakovník , czechoslovakia ) is a professional czech ice hockey defenceman that currently plays for the montreal canadiens of the national hockey league ( nhl ) . he also played for the toronto maple leafs from 1998 to 2011 , the boston bruins in 2011 and the carolina hurricanes in 2011. he was drafted by the toronto maple leafs with the 204th overall pick in the 2003 nhl draft . he played in the czech extraliga for the hc kladno where he played four seasons . he won the stanley cup with the bruins on june 15 , 2011 when the bruins defeated the vancouver canucks in game 7 by a score of 4-0. websites . - tomáš kaberle at nhl.com
