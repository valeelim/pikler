Adam Foote     adam david vernon foote ( born july 10 , 1971 in whitby , ontario ) is a canadian retired professional ice hockey defenceman that played 20 seasons in the national hockey league ( nhl ) most notably with the quebec nordiques/colorado avalanche organizations . foote played his last nhl game on april 10 , 2011 with the avalanche in a 4-3 ot win and was awarded the first star of the game . he won a gold medal with team canada in the 2002 winter olympics .
