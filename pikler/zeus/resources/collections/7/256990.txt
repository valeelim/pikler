Burn Burn (album)    burn burn ( album ) burn burn is the seventh studio album by canadian rock band our lady peace . it was released on july 21 , 2009. the producer of the album was lead singer raine maida . the peaked at # 3 on the canadian albums chart and also peaked at # 41 on the u.s. `` billboard '' 200. singles from `` burn burn '' . - 2009 - `` all you did was save my life '' - 2009 - `` the end is where we begin '' - 2010 - `` dreamland ''
