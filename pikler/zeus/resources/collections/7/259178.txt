Fuck the World (Insane Clown Posse song)    fuck the world ( insane clown posse song ) `` fuck the world ' '' is a 1999 midwest hip hop song by the american horrorcore band insane clown posse and is the second single from their fifth studio album `` the amazing jeckel brothers '' , which was released in 1999. the song was produced by insane clown posse and mike clark and also written by mike e. clark and icp . the song is notable for using the curse word , `` fuck '' 95 times even though the song claims to have said it 93 times . the people they cursed in the song were : beastie boys , the dalai lama , forrest gump , oprah winfrey , kyle petty , tom petty , fred bear , ted nugent , the count of monte cristo , cisco , jack brisco , jerry brisco , rms titanic , celine dion , dionne warwick , berlin wall , lyle lovett ( claiming `` whoever the fuck that is '' ) , jumpsteady , rocky maivia , spin , rolling stone , vibe , and violent j .
