Bryan Bickell     bryan bickell ( born march 9 , 1986 in orono , ontario ) is a canadian professional ice hockey left winger . he currently plays for the chicago blackhawks of the national hockey league ( nhl ) . he was chosen 41st overall by the chicago blackhawks in the 2004 nhl entry draft . he earned a stanley cup ring from the team but his name was not engraved on the cup . on june 24 , 2013 , bickell won his second stanley cup with the blackhawks after they defeated the boston bruins 4 games to 2 in the 2013 stanley cup finals .
