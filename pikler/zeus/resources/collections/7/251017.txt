Isoform     a protein isoform is any of several different forms of the same protein . different forms of a protein may be produced from related genes , or may be made from the same gene by alternative splicing . a large number of isoforms are caused by single-nucleotide polymorphisms or snps , small genetic differences between alleles of the same gene . these occur at specific individual nucleotide ( building block of dna-strand ) positions within a gene .
