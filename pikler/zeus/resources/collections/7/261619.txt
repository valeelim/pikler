Alvarezsaurus     alvarezsaurus is a small dinosaur from the upper cretaceous of argentina , about 86-83 million years ago . it measured about 2 meters in length and weighed 20 kilograms . it was named by paleontologist jose bonaparte in 1991. it walked on two legs , had a long tail , and its leg structure suggests that it was a fast runner . it may have eaten insects . it was more primitive than other , better-known members of its family , such as `` mononykus '' and `` shuvuuia '' . `` alvarezsaurus '' was featured in the discovery channel television series `` dinosaur planet '' , which speculated that it was a predator of young `` saltasaurus '' , although these species did not exist at the same time , `` alvarezsaurus '' being about 5 million years older than `` saltasaurus '' .
