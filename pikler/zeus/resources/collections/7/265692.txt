Peroxide     peroxide is an ion . its chemical formula is o. peroxide can be a reducing agent or an oxidizing agent . more often it is an oxidizing agent . it can be reduced to oxide or oxidized to oxygen . hydrogen peroxide is the most common peroxide . barium peroxide is another peroxide . there are organic peroxides . these are chemical compounds where peroxide is attached to an organic molecule . some of them are explosives . related pages . - perchlorate
