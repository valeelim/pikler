Sriharikota     sriharikota is a barrier island off the coast of the southern state of andhra pradesh in india . it houses india 's only satellite launch centre in the satish dhawan space centre ( also known as shar ) and is used by the indian space research organisation to launch satellites using multi-stage rockets such as the polar satellite launch vehicle and the geosynchronous satellite launch vehicle . sriharikota separates the pulicat lake from the bay of bengal , and is home to the town of pulicat . it is in sri amarajeevi potti sri ramulu nellore district in andhra pradesh . the nearest town is sullurpeta , which has the nearest railway station . the nearest business city is sri city . it is also connected by expressways to chennai .
