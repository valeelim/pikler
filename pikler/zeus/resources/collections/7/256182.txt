The Gadget Show     the gadget show is a british television series . it focuses on technology and is shown on channel 5. it has progressed from 30 minutes to 1 hour long . each week there is a challenge , usually starring main presenter suzi perry and jason bradbury , and numerous tests , as well as some new features such as top five , the wall of fame and focus group . also , there is a huge prize fund available to win if viewers phone in and answer a multiple-choice question . the show tries to have enough information but not too much so that it bores people . it is hosted in a studio . series 20 began on 14 october 2013 .
