Story of the Year     story of the year is an american alternative rock band . they formed in 1995 in st. louis . the members of the group are dan marsal , the singer , ryan phillips , who plays the guitar , philip sneed , who plays the guitar , adam russell , who plays the bass guitar and josh wills , who plays the drums . the band 's original name was 67 north but was then changed to big blue monkey and was again changed to story of the year . discography . studio albums . - 2003 : `` page avenue '' - 2005 : `` in the wake of determination '' - 2008 : `` the black swan '' - 2010 : `` the constant '' live and compilation albums . - 2005 : `` live in the lou/bassassins '' - 2008 : `` our time is now '' other websites . - story of the year 's official website
