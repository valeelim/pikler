NHL 11     nhl 11 is a ice hockey video game that was developed by ea canada and published by ea sports . it was released on september 7 , 2010 in north america for the xbox 360 and playstation 3. jonathan toews was the cover athlete for the north american version of `` nhl 11 '' making it the first time players from the same team were cover athletes in consecutive years ( patrick kane was on the cover of nhl 10 ) . new features in nhl 11 include : broken sticks , new goal celebrations , play without a stick , all the hits are different , new physics-based engine , a new face-off system , new passing system , hockey ultimate team , where a player can build a dream team from packs of cards bought in-game , new hustle button which is like the speed burst in previous games and restricted and unrestricted free agents in the be a pro and be a gm modes , disallowed goals , playoff beards and players can now jump over over other player that are lying down . nhl 11 also included the chl ( which includes the qmjhl , the whl and the ohl ) , and a couple of european leagues .
