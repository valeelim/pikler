Thundersnow     thundersnow is a thunderstorm with snow instead of rain . this rare kind of weather is less rare around the midwestern united states , the great lakes and rocky mountains , the sea of japan , and mount everest . thundersnow mostly happens in march . how it is made . - a thunderstorm at the front of a wave of cold air or warm air ( cold front or warm front ) - the edge of a big ocean storm ( or cyclone ) where lower warm and higher cold air mix . - thunderstorms going over a warm lake while the air is cold ( or lake-effect ) . thunder can be heard many miles away , but thundersnow thunder can only be heard a few miles away . the snow stops sounds of thunder more than rain does .
