Taipei Taoyuan International Airport     taiwan taoyuan international airport ( ) is the largest international airport in republic of china where most international flights depart and arrive everyday . taipei songshan airport is in taipei . it used to be the international airport of taiwan . however as the number of people grew and a bigger airport was needed , there was no room to build more . in order to solve the problem , construction for new airport that can run 24 hours and meet the growing needs started . taoyuan international airport is in taoyuan city .
