Klinefelter syndrome     klinefelter syndrome , 47 , xxy , or xxy syndrome is when people have an extra number of x chromosomes . people who have klinefelter 's syndrome are often called `` xxy males '' , or `` 47 , xxy males '' . other males can have klinefelter 's syndrome as well ( for example , mice ) . males who have this condition are almost always infertile . this means that they can not father babies .
