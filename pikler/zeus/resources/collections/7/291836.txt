Denmark-Norway     the kingdom of denmark-norway was a union between the two kingdoms denmark and norway , which lasted from 1536 to 1814. during this time , the capital of both countries was copenhagen , and they shared the same military . the kingdom also possessed colonies in greenland , iceland , africa , the caribbean and india . monarchs . - 1524–1533 : frederick i - 1588–1648 : christian iv - 1648–1670 : frederick iii - 1808–1814 : frederick vi
