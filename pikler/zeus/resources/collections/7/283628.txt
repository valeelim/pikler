Culinary name     culinary name is the name of an ingredient when used in the kitchen for preparing food . sometimes the kitchen name is used because it sounds more attractive than the real name , or because a cheaper ingredient can be linked with a more expensive one . the culinary name may also refer to a way of cooking or to a region , or using a particular ingredient examples . - sweetbreads , used because it sounds nicer than thymus or pancreas . - lumpfish caviar , used give lumpfish eggs the sound of the more expensive sturgeon caviar . - cassia bark is a cheaper substitute , but is called cinnamon in north america . - florentine , meaning from the region of florence , means the dish will include spinach .
