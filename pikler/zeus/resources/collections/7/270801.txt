Werner Faymann     werner faymann ( born 4 may 1960 in vienna ) is an austrian politician . he belongs to the social democratic party of austria . he was transport , innovation and technology minister from 2007 to 2008. his party won the elections of the national assembly in 2008. as a result he was the federal chancellor of austria from 2008 to 2016. he was succeeded by christian kern . other websites . - homepage of the austrian chancellor
