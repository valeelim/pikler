Hofstra University     hofstra university is a private university in hempstead , new york . the university has about 12,400 students . it was founded in 1935. the current president of the university is stuart rabinowitz . the athletics teams of the university are called the pride . they have 15 varsity teams and they compete in the colonial athletic association . other websites . - hofstra university 's official website - hofstra pride 's official athletics website
