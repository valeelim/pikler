Sodium sulfite     sodium sulfite is a chemical compound . it is made of sodium and sulfite ions . it is one of the sulfites used to preserve dried food . it is used to remove chlorine from pools . it reacts with acids to produce sulfur dioxide . it is a weak reducing agent . when it is oxidized , it is converted to sodium sulfate . its chemical formula is naso . related pages . - sulfate - sulfuric acid - sulfurous acid
