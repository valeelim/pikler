Lead(II) sulfate    lead ( ii ) sulfate lead ( ii ) sulfate , also known as plumbous sulfate , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is pbso . it contains lead and sulfate ions . the lead is in its +2 oxidation state . lead ( ii ) sulfate is a white solid . it does not dissolve in water . it is toxic because it has lead in it . it does not dissolve in sulfuric acid . lead ( ii ) sulfate is made in a lead acid battery by normal usage . it can be made by reacting lead ( ii ) acetate or lead ( ii ) nitrate with any sulfate . it is found as a mineral named anglesite . this mineral is made when galena is oxidized . related pages . - lead ( ii ) sulfide - lead ( iv ) acetate
