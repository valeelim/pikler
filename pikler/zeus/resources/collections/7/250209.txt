Walled In     walled in is a 2009 horror/thriller movie that was produced by kevin dewalt and directed by gilles paquet-brenner . the plot of the movie is based on the best-selling novel `` les emmures '' and is about a woman ( mischa barton ) who discovers past residents who were entombed within the walls of a building by a vicious murderer . other websites . - `` walled in '' 's official website
