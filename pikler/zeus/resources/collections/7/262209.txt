Alum     alum is both a specific chemical compound and a class of chemical compounds . 1. it means potassium aluminium sulfate , a chemical compound . 1. it also means a double sulfate salt . examples : - potash alum is potassium aluminum sulfate - sodia alum is sodium aluminum sulfate - ammonium alum is ammonium aluminum sulfate words like `` alum '' and `` potash '' were used before modern chemistry developed . the word 'alumen ' occurs in pliny 's `` natural history '' ( 2379 ad ) . in the 52nd chapter of his 35th book , he gives a detailed description . different substances were distinguished by the name of 'alumen ' , but they were all astringents , and were all used in dyeing and medicine .
