Green River, Wyoming    green river , wyoming green river is an american city in the state of wyoming . it is in sweetwater county where it is the county seat . the city has about 11,808 people . the city was started in 1868 in what used to be the dakota territory . every year , a festival is held there called the flaming gorge days where acts will perform music . acts like poison and reo speedwagon have played there . other websites . - official city of green river website
