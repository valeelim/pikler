Selenium hexafluoride     selenium hexafluoride , also known as selenium ( vi ) fluoride , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is sef . it contains selenium in its +6 oxidation state . it also contains fluoride ions . selenium hexafluoride is a colorless gas . it is much more reactive and toxic than sulfur hexafluoride . it has a bad odor . selenium hexafluoride is made by heating selenium and fluorine together . this chemical compound has no uses . related pages . - selenium tetrafluoride - selenium tetrachloride
