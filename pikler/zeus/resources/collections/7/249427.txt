Midshipman     a midshipman is an officer cadet , or a commissioned officer . they are the lowest rank of officer in the royal navy , united states navy , and many commonwealth navies . commonwealth countries which use this rank are australia , new zealand , south africa , pakistan , india , singapore , sri lanka and kenya . before 1968 , it was used by the royal canadian navy . but it was replaced with that of naval cadet upon the making of the canadian forces .
