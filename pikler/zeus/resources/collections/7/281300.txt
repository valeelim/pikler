Patric Hörnqvist     patric gösta hörnqvist ( born january 1 , 1987 ) is a swedish professional ice hockey right winger . he currently plays for the pittsburgh penguins of the national hockey league ( nhl ) . he has also played for the nashville predators of the nhl and djurgårdens if of the sel . hörnqvist was selected 230th overall in the 2005 nhl entry draft by the nashville predators . on june 28 , 2014 , the predators traded hörnqvist along with nick spaling to the pittsburgh penguins in exchange for james neal .
