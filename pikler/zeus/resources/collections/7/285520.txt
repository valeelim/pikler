Towson University     towson university ( tu ) is a public university in towson , maryland . the university has about 21,117 students . it was founded in 1866. the current president of the university is robert l. caret . it started in baltimore as state norman school in 1866. tu moved to towson in 1915. in 1935 , the name was changed to state teachers college at towson because maryland opened more colleges . in 1963 , tu became towson state college . in 1976 the name changed to towson state university . finally , in 1997 , it became towson university . the athletics teams of the university are called the tigers . they have 18 varsity teams and they compete in the colonial athletic association . other websites . - towson university 's official website - towson tigers ' official athletics website
