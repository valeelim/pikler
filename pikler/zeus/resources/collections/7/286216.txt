St. Cloud State University     the st. cloud state university is a university in st. cloud , minnesota . it was established in 1869. it has over 18,000 students . it is the largest member of the minnesota state colleges and universities system . st. cloud state has teams in 20 ncaa sports , plus the non-ncaa sport of nordic skiing , nicknamed huskies . of the ncaa teams , 18 play in the northern sun intercollegiate conference , a division ii conference . the men 's and women 's ice hockey teams compete in the top-level ncaa division i , with the men playing in the national collegiate hockey conference and the women in the western collegiate hickey association .
