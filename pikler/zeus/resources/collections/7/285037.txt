Ali Abdullah Saleh     ali abdullah saleh ( ; born march 21 , 1946 ) is the first and former president of the republic of yemen . before that time , saleh served as the president of the yemen arab republic ( north yemen ) from 1978 until 1990. in 1990 , he assumed the office of chairman of the presidential council of the republic of yemen ( north & south yemen ) . he was the longest-serving president of yemen , ruling since 1978. on february 2 , 2011 , he announced that he would step down in 2013 .
