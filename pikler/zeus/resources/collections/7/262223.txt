Potassium aluminium sulfate     potassium aluminium sulfate , or potash alum , or potassium alum , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is kal ( so ) . it contains potassium , aluminium , and sulfate ions . it is a white acidic solid . it reacts with sodium bicarbonate to make carbon dioxide gas . it does exist naturally . it can often be found as a mineral . it is made by reacting aluminium sulfate with potassium sulfate . it is used to purify water ( ho ) . it is also used in deodorants . it is an antiseptic . it had several uses in the past , such as in dying clothes . it is used to grow big crystals . related pages . - alum - aluminium sulphate
