Amos Arbour     joseph hermas `` amos , butch '' arbour ( january 26 , 1895 in waubaushene , ontario – november 1 , 1943 ) was a canadian professional ice hockey left winger who played 7 seasons in the national hockey association and 6 seasons in the national hockey league for the montreal canadiens , hamilton tigers and toronto st. pats . he was a member of the original canadiens team in the 1909–10 nha season .
