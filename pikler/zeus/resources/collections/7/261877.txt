2006 Copiapó mining accident     on january 20 , 2006 , an explosion occurred in the underground copper mine of carola-agustina in copiapó , chile , shortly after two trucks collided , covering the only escape route for the miners inside the mine . the explosion caused two deaths , two injured and 70 miners stayed trapped for 7 hours . at the nearby mine , `` punta de cobre '' , miners constructed a tunnel to allow evacuations in case of emergencies , after the explosion .
