Semiramide     semiramide is an italian opera written by gioachino rossini . it has two acts . it was first performed at the teatro la fenice in venice , on february 3 , 1823 , libretto by gaetano rossi . this was after voltaire 's `` semiramis '' . this opera was successful from the start and is still performed today . principal roles . - semiramide - soprano - arsace - mezzo-soprano - assur - bass - idreno - tenor - oroe - bass - azema - soprano sources . - `` the complete dictionnary of opera & operetta '' , james anderson , vikings books , 1989 .
