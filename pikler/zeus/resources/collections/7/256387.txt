Dual carriageway     a dual carriageway is a type of road . it is an important route that usually carries long-distance traffic . a dual carriageway has four lanes ( 2 lanes each side ) , and is always separated by a man-made barrier or strip of land . dual carriageways have no hard shoulder . a hard shoulder is an area at the side of a road where drivers can stop if there is a serious problem , a breakdown for example .
