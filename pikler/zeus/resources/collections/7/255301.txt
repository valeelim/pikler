Hook-handed man     the hook-handed man is a villain and antihero from `` a series of unfortunate events '' , a series of children 's books written by lemony snicket . he is part of count olaf 's acting group , and he has hooks on both of his hands . he is first seen in `` the bad beginning '' and last seen in `` the grim grotto '' . he is captain widdershins ' stepson and has a sister called fiona .
