Suez     suez ( , egyptian pronunciation `` es suwais '' ) is a town in northern egypt , on the coast of the gulf of suez . almost 500.000 people live in the city . the city is the southern terminus of the suez canal . it has two harbours , and a large seaport . an oil refinery is in suez . highways and railroad tracks connect suez to the cities of cairo , port said and ismailia .
