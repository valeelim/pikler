Villanova University     the villanova university is a private university in villanova , pennsylvania . the university has about 9,535 students . it was founded in 1842. the current president of the university is rev . peter m. donohue , o.s.a . . the athletics teams of the university are called the wildcats . they have 24 varsity teams , most of which compete in the big east conference . the football team plays in the colonial athletic association , and the women 's rowing team is a member of the american athletic conference . other websites . - villanova university 's official website - villanova wildcats ' official athletics website
