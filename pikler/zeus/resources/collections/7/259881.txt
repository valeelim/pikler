Dig It (The Beatles song)    dig it ( the beatles song ) `` dig it '' is a song by the beatles from their album `` let it be '' . the song is credited to lennon/mccartney/harrison/starkey . it is one of the few songs to be credited to all of the beatles ; the others are `` flying '' ( `` magical mystery tour '' ) , `` suzy parker '' ( `` let it be '' movie ) , `` 12-bar original '' ( `` anthology 2 '' ) , `` los paranoias '' ( `` anthology 3 '' ) and the beatles ' version of `` free as a bird '' . personnel . - john lennon – vocal and 6 string bass - paul mccartney – piano - george harrison – lead guitar - ringo starr – drums - billy preston – hammond organ
