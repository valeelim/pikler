Gateway (computer networking)    gateway ( computer networking ) in computer networking and telecommunications , a gateway is a component that is part of two networks , which use different protocols . the gateway will translate one protocol into the other . a router is a special case of a gateway . gateways , also called protocol converters , can operate at any network layer . the activities of a gateway are more complex than that of the router or switch as it communicates using more than one protocol . both the computers of internet users and the computers that serve pages to users are `` host nodes '' . the nodes that connect the networks in between are `` gateways '' . these are are gateway nodes : - the computers that control traffic between company networks - the computers used by internet service providers ( isps ) to connect users to the internet
