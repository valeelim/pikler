Mike Bossy     michael dean bossy ( born january 22 , 1957 in montreal , quebec ) is a canadian retired professional ice hockey right winger . he played 10 seasons in the national hockey league ( nhl ) all with the new york islanders organization . he won the stanley cup 4 straight times from the 1979-80 to 1982-83 nhl seasons . he was inducted into the hockey hall of fame in 1991 .
