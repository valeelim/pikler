Heo Jeong     heo jeong ( hangul : 허정 , hanja : 許政 , april 8 , 1896 – september 18 , 1988 ) was a south korean politician and independence activist . in 1948 he was the second transportation minister of south korea . in 1950 he was social affairs minister , in 1951 prime minister ( acting ) of south korea , and in 1952 government services minister . in 1957 he was the eighth mayor of seoul city until 1959. on april 19 , 1960 , he became foreign affairs minister . on april 25 his ministries involved being acting president of south korea . in june 1960 he became the sixth prime minister of south korea . his nickname was yooyang ( 우양 ) . related pages . - rhee syng-man - kim kyu-sik - yun poson - chang myon
