Space opera     some people have a negative opinion of space opera . they use the name as an insult . they do not like these stories because they have several problems . the action may be impossible to believe . the science may be silly . the characters may be simple and boring . but , other people have a feeling of nostalgia for space opera . they think these stories from the golden age of science fiction have a special feeling of wonder and excitement . edward e. ( doc ) smith may be the first important writer of this type of sf . he wrote two series called `` skylark '' and `` lensman '' . these are important early examples of space opera novels . flash gordon is an early example of this genre from radio , tv , and film . modern examples include : alastair reynolds ' `` revelation space '' series , peter f. hamilton 's `` the dreaming void '' , `` the night 's dawn '' , and `` pandora 's star '' series . the very popular star wars movies and books are also examples of this sort of sf .
