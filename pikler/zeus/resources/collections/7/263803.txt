Disulfur dinitride     disulfur dinitride is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is sn . it contains sulfur in its +3 oxidation state . it also contains nitride ions . disulfur dinitride is a colorless crystalline solid . it is explosive , similar to tetrasulfur tetranitride . it easily evaporates . it does not dissolve in water . it turns back into tetrasulfur tetranitride when exposed to water . disulfur dinitride is made by reacting silver with tetrasulfur tetranitride . this makes some silver ( i ) sulfide , which is a catalyst . this reaction happens in a vacuum ( not the one used to clean floors ) . it reacts with some chemical compounds that are acids . related pages . - sulfur nitride - sulfur hexafluoride
