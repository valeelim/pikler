Pedobear     pedobear is an internet meme that gained popularity on 4chan . pedobear is usually posted as a way to mock users requesting or posting sexualized images of children on 4chan . pedobear first appeared on 2channel , a popular bulletin board system in japan and was originally called kumā ( interjection of kuma which means bear in japanese ) or safety bear . pedobear has also been featured as istockphoto wallpaper and was in the background of a cover of the auto magazine import tuner . on july 3 , 2009 , michael r. barrick , a canadian artist had created two pictures showing pedobear together with the mascots of 2010 winter olympic and paralympic games . gazeta olsztyńska , a polish newspaper mistakenly used one of the pictures for a february 4 , 2010 front page story about the olympics in vancouver . other websites . - pedobear at knowyourmeme.com
