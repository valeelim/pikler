Smokey and the Bandit     smokey and the bandit is a 1977 action-comedy movie that is produced by mort engelberg , directed by hal needham and starred burt reynolds , sally field , jackie gleason , jerry reed and mike henry . smokey and the bandit spawned two sequels : `` smokey and the bandit ii '' and `` smokey and the bandit 3 '' . cast . - burt reynolds - bo darville ( `` bandit '' ) - sally field - carrie ( `` frog '' ) - jerry reed as cledus snow ( `` snowman '' ) - jackie gleason as sheriff buford t. justice ( `` smokey bear '' )
