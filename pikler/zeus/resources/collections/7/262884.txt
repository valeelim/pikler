Rick Bragnalo     richard james bragnalo ( born december 1 , 1951 in fort william , ontario ) is a retired canadian ice hockey centre . signed by the washington capitals as a free agent in 1976 , bragnalo played parts of four seasons for the capitals . after playing a few years in the minors , bragnalo moved onto playing hockey in italy with the team of the val pusteria wolves , former ev bruneck . he signed for hc milano saima in 1990 , scoring the goal that permitted milano to win the league in 1990/91 . he retired in 1992. other websites . - val pusteria wolves
