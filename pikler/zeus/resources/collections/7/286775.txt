Glenn Ruth     glenn ruth ( born june 13 , 1969 in camden , new jersey ) is a retired american professional wrestler who was best known for wrestling with the world wrestling federation ( wwf ) under the ring name thrasher . ruth was best known as one half of the tag team the headbangers , along with mosh . ruth held many championships during his professional wrestling career : - blackburn wrestling alliance - heartland wrestling association - insane championship wrestling - main event championship wrestling - maryland championship wrestling - nwa southwest - new england wrestling federation - pro wrestling illustrated - pwi ranked him no . 110 of the top 500 singles wrestlers in the pwi 500 in 1997 . - texas wrestling alliance - world wrestling federation
