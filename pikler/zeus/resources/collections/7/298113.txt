Embioptera     embioptera are order of insects , commonly known as webspinners , are a small group of mostly tropical and subtropical insects , classified under the subclass pterygota . the group probably first appeared during the jurassic and is well represented in cretaceous amber . the common name webspinner comes from the insects ' ability to spin silk from structures on their front legs . they use the silk to make a web-like pouch or gallery in which they live .
