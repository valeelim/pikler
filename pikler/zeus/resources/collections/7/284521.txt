Soul Punk     soul punk is the first studio album by american musician `` patrick stump '' . the album was released on october 18 , 2011. stump 's solo project was officially revealed in january 2010 and was later titled `` soul punk '' . it is the first major musical project undertaken by stump since fall out boy 's hiatus . the album is scheduled for release on october 18 , 2011 through island , with stump officially announcing the release on july 25 via twitter and the internet . the track `` explode '' from the album was leaked on may 6 , 2011 for promotion purposes . the album 's first single `` this city '' was released on july 26 , 2011. the music video for the single will be released on september 20 , 2011. the track features rapper lupe fiasco .
