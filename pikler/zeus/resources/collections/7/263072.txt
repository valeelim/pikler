Nitrogen oxide     nitrogen oxide may refer to any of these chemical compounds : - nitrous oxide , +1 oxidation state , sweet-smelling anesthetic sometimes called `` laughing gas '' - nitric oxide , +2 oxidation state , used in the body as stimulant - nitrogen dioxide , +4 oxidation state , brown choking gas - dinitrogen tetroxide , +4 oxidation state , two nitrogen dioxide molecules bonded together - dinitrogen pentoxide , +5 oxidation state , made from nitric acid
