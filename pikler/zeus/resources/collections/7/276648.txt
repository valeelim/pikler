Keith Ballard     keith ballard ( born november 26 , 1982 in baudette , minnesota ) is an american nhl ice hockey defenceman for the minnesota wild . he was picked by the buffalo sabres with the eleventh overall pick in the 2002 nhl entry draft . he played hockey in college for the university of minnesota 's golden gophers . ballard has also played for the phoenix coyotes for 3 seasons and the florida panthers for 2 seasons before being traded to the vancouver canucks in 2010 during the 2010 nhl entry draft for michael grabner , steve bernier , and vancouver 's first round pick and 25th overall selection in that draft . on july 3 , 2013 , he was placed on waivers and his contract was bought out by the canucks . on july 4th , 2013 , ballard signed a two-year $ 3 million contract with the minnesota wild . ballard is married and had a daughter with his wife in may 2010 .
