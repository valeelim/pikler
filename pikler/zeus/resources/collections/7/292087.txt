Ilya Bryzgalov     ilya nikolayevich bryzgalov ( илья николаевич брызгалов in russian ; born june 22 , 1980 in togliatti , russian sfsr , urs ) is a russian professional ice hockey goaltender . he currently plays for the minnesota wild of the national hockey league ( nhl ) . bryzgalov has also played for the anaheim ducks/mighty ducks of anaheim for 5 seasons , the phoenix coyotes for 4 seasons , the philadelphia flyers for 2 seasons , and the edmonton oilers for 20 games . he was drafted by the mighty ducks of anaheim with the 44th overall pick in the 2000 nhl draft . he won the stanley cup with the anaheim ducks on june 6 , 2007. on march 4 , 2014 , the oilers traded bryzgalov to the minnesota wild for a fourth-round draft pick .
