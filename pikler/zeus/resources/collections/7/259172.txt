Love, Pain &amp; the Whole Crazy Thing    love , pain & amp ; the whole crazy thing love , pain & the whole crazy thing is the fifth studio album by new zealand-born australian country music singer keith urban . it was released on november 7 , 2006. the producers of the album was dan huff and keith urban . the album is certified double multi-platinum by the riaa and platinum by the riaa . the album sold over 2,000,000 units in the united states and over 100,000 in canada . singles from `` love , pain & the whole crazy thing '' . - 2006 - `` once in a lifetime '' - 2006 - `` stupid boy '' - 2007 - `` i told you so '' - 2007 - `` everybody ''
