Hands All Over (song)    hands all over ( song ) `` hands all over '' is a single released under a & m/octone records by pop/rock band `` maroon 5 '' . it is the 3rd single released off of the band 's 3rd studio album called `` hands all over '' . it was released on december 22nd , 2010. the music video for the song was leaked on youtube and yahoo ! music on december 23rd , 2010. the music video for `` hands all over '' was leaked on maroon 5 's home youtube page on december 23rd , 2010. however , the music video was leaked on youtube for only if a user had the link to the video . the band released the link for anyone on facebook and twitter . the facebook page `` hands all over ( maroon 5 album ) 9.21.10 '' leaked the leak along with the band 's official facebook page . the music video shows a somewhat animated feature showing the band mates performing in separate areas with `` hands '' on the bodies of the band members .
