Three Days Grace     three days grace is a canadian alternative rock band . they formed in 1992 in norwood , ontario as a post-grunge band and were originally called `` groundswell '' . there are four members in the band : adam gontier , barry stock , neil sanderson and brad walst . the band was named `` number one rock artist of the year '' by billboard . gontier resigned from the band on january 9 , 2013 due to a `` non-life threatening '' health issue and was replaced by my darkest days lead singer matt walst . discography . studio albums . - 2003 : `` three days grace '' - 2006 : `` one-x '' - 2009 : `` life starts now '' - 2012 : `` transit of venus '' - 2015 : `` human '' other websites . - three days grace 's official website
