Telluride     telluride is an ion . chemical compounds containing this ion are called tellurides . examples are sodium telluride and hydrogen telluride . the chemical formula is te . tellurides are strong reducing agents . some tellurides are impure , making them weaker reducing agents . tellurides are the main source of tellurium in the ground . it only exists at very high ph . at lower ph 's , hydrogen telluride , hte , is made . when it is acidic ( very low ph ) , hydrogen telluride gas is made . related pages . - tellurite - tellurate
