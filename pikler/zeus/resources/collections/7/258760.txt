Jetix     jetix was a tv brand owned by walt disney . it existed from 2004 to 2010 worldwide . the brand was started in the united states in february 2004. it was retired in most countries in 2009. in the netherlands , it was retired in january . in russia , it was retired in august 2010. jetix play started on various european channels replacing fox kids play . it aired mainly pre-school shows . the brand was retired in most countries in august 2010. it was not rebranded in romania until march 2011. in most countries it was replaced by playhouse disney . in the united kingdom and russia , it had no replacement . jetix action block started in spain in 2007 as a block on star one . the block aired mainly shows which aired on the main jetix channel . the block was discontinued in both regions in 2010. jetix +1 was a timeshift service in the united kingdom and italy . it aired shows which aired an hour earlier on the main channel .
