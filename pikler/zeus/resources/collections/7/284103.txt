Passions (soap opera)    passions ( soap opera ) passions was a television soap opera which aired starting july 5 , 1999 until september 7 , 2007 on nbc and on directv from september 17 , 2007 to august 7 , 2008. this show focused toward the romantic and paranormal issues of harmony , a fictional new england town . the storylines centered around multi racial families ( the white cranes and bennetts , the black russells , the hispanic lopez-fitzgeralds ) . it also focused on tabitha lenox , a town witch .
