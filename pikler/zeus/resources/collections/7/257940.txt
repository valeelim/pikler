Reducing agent     reducing agent is a chemical that gives away electrons to another chemical compound , the oxidizing agent . for example , zinc is a reducing agent . when it reacts with an oxidizing agent , it gives up two electrons , changing its oxidation state from 0 to +2 . all chemical elements have an oxidation state of 0. examples . - tin ( ii ) chloride - zinc - aluminum - alkali metal - sulfur - hydrogen related pages . - oxidation - reduction - oxidizing agent
