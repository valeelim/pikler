Pediococcus     pediococcus is a genus of gram-positive lactic acid bacteria . they usually occur in pairs or tetrads , and divide along two planes of symmetry , as do the other lactic acid cocci genera . they are purely homofermentative . `` pediococcus '' strains are found in foods , on plants , and as beer-spoilage agents . they produce inactive lactic acid and are used mainly for making fermented vegetables , mashes , beer , and wort .
