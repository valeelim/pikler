Kyle Boller     kyle bryan boller ( born june 17 , 1981 in burbank , california ) is an american football quarterback for the oakland raiders of the national football league . boller was drafted out of the university of california by the baltimore ravens with the 19th pick in the first round of the 2003 nfl draft . he also played for the st. louis rams in 2009. boller has been considered a professional bust , as his success with any of his nfl teams was very limited , unlike his college football career . boller is married to former miss california , carrie prejean . other websites . - kyle boller at oaklandraiders.com
