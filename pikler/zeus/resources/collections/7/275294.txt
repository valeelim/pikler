Poxviridae     poxviridae are family of large complex dna viruses , including the vaccinia and variola viruses , that are pathogenic to humans and animals and have an affinity for skin tissue . four genera of poxviruses may infect humans : orthopox , parapox , yatapox , molluscipox . orthopox : variola virus , vaccinia virus , cowpox virus , monkeypox virus , smallpox ( eradicated ) ; parapox : orf virus , pseudocowpox , bovine papular stomatitis virus ; yatapox : tanapox virus , yaba monkey tumor virus ; molluscipox : molluscum contagiosum virus ( mcv ) .
