Fin Fang Foom     fin fang foom is a supervillain that first appeared in 1961 in the # 89 issue of strange tales . he was created by stan lee and jack kirby . he has the power to super strength , the ability to fly with his wings at supersonic speeds , and can also spew combustible acid mist from his mouth . fin fang foom also made multiple appearances in the iron man comics . other websites . - fin fang foom at marvel.com
