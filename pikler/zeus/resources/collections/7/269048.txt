Battle of Sunomatagawa     the battle of sunomatagawa was a japanese battle . it took place in nanaimo , a place in japan , in 1181 ad . minamoto no yukiie and his army found taira no tomomori 's army across the river . they swam the river and attacked them . but tomomori 's people could tell the difference between the wet enemies and their dry people and forced them back across the river .
