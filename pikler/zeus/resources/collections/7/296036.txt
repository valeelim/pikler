Gnetophyta     the gnetophytes are a group of seed plants with three genera . their evolutionary relationships are unclear , but it is now clear that they are related to each other . the three genera are : - `` gnetum '' - `` welwitschia '' - `` ephedra '' most morphological and molecular studies confirm that `` gnetum '' and `` welwitschia '' diverged from each other more recently than from `` ephedra '' .
