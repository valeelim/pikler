Sleepwalker (song)    sleepwalker ( song ) `` sleepwalker '' is a song by adam lambert released off his debut album `` for your entertainment '' . the song was written by frontman of onerepublic , ryan tedder , as well as aimee mayo , and chris lindsey . it was produced by tedder and was released in canada and new zealand for radio mainstream on june 15 , 2010 .
