University of Louisville     the university of louisville is a public university in louisville , kentucky . the university has about 19,743 students . it was founded in 1798. the current president of the university is james r. ramsey . the sports teams of the university are called the cardinals . they have 21 varsity teams that compete in the atlantic coast conference . other websites . - university of louisville 's official website - louisville cardinals ' official website
