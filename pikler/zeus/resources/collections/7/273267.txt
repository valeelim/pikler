Potassium periodate     potassium periodate is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is kio . it has potassium and periodate ions in it . potassium periodate is a white solid . it is an oxidizing agent . it is similar to potassium perchlorate . it does not dissolve good in water . it makes potassium iodate and oxygen when heated . it is made by reacting potassium iodide with a strong oxidizing agent . it is used to find out how much potassium is in something because it is one of the few potassium compounds that do not dissolve easily in water . related pages . - sodium periodate - periodic acid - iodic acid
