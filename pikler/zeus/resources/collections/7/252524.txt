Wednesday Morning, 3 AM    wednesday morning , 3 am wednesday morning , 3 am is the name of the first album by simon and garfunkel . it came out in 1964. some of the songs on the album were written by paul simon , but others were written by other people . the songs `` wednesday morning , 3 am '' , `` the sounds of silence '' , `` bleecker street '' and bob dylan 's `` the times they are a-changin ' '' are on the album . the album did not sell well , though the song `` the sounds of silence '' was later released as a single .
