WWE SmackDown vs. Raw 2007     wwe smackdown vs. raw 2007 is a wrestling video game that was published by thq . the video game was released for the playstation 2 , playstation portable and xbox 360 systems . the video game was released on january 25 , 2007 in japan , november 14 , 2006 in north america , november 10 , 2006 in europe and on november 16 , 2006 in australia for the playstation 2 & xbox 360. and the game was released on february 22 , 2007 in japan , december 6 , 2006 in north america and on december 15 , 2006 in europe for the psp . the video game received a 8.1 for both the ps2 and xbox 360 and a 7.9 for the psp from gamespot . it is part of the wwe smackdown vs. raw video game series . other websites . - official wwe smackdown vs. raw 2007 website
