Herbert Gasser     herbert gasser ( herbert spencer gasser , july 5 , 1888 may 11 , 1963 ) was an american doctor . he won the 1944 nobel prize in physiology or medicine , with joseph erlanger . gasser discovered the different conductivity rates of different groups of nerve fibres . the work led to advances in our knowledge of the mechanism of pain and of reflex action and has inspired a large school of neurophysiologists . the nobel foundation described their work as `` for their discoveries relating to the highly differentiated functions of single nerve fibres '' which probably means 'different fibres do different things ' .
