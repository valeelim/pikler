Villain     a villain is a bad person . villains are usually found in fiction , such as books and movies . the audience do not usually like the villain and they support the hero or heroine . in some movies and books ( such as the movie ocean 's eleven ) , the villain is a good person and the hero/heroine is bad ( such as a robber ) . when used in comic books , they are depicted as super-villains . super-villains usually hold some kind of unnatural force .
