Northwest Passage     the northwest passage is a sea passage along the north coast of north america , through the arctic ocean . because of ice , the passage is usually closed to ships . the spanish empire wanted to reach asia by a western route , because portugal controlled the eastern route around the cape of good hope . spain wanted to have a route that it could control . this caused most european countries to search for a possible route . roald amundsen was the first person to navigate the passage between 1903 and 1906. global warming is increasing the melting for the passage . other websites . - northwest passage citizendium
