William Gibson     william gibson ( born march 17 , 1948 ) writes science fiction . he is one of the most important people in the part of science fiction called cyberpunk . his most famous book is `` neuromancer '' . william ford gibson was born in conway , south carolina on the atlantic coast . he spent most of his childhood in wytheville , virginia . whytheville is a small town in the appalachians . his parents had been born and raised there . gibson was shy and awkward as a teenager . he grew up in a place and time where most people thought and acted alike . this was a problem for him . he chose not to believe in religion . he read science fiction and writers such as william s. burroughs and henry miller . gibson was not doing well in school and his mother was frustrated . she threatened to send him to a boarding school . she was very surprised when he liked this idea very much . gibson 's mother died when he was eighteen years old . he left school and did not graduate . he traveled a lot . he became very interested in the counterculture . he left the united states and moved to canada during the vietnam war draft .
