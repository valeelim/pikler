Monoclonius     monoclonius was a herbivore ( plant-eater ) ceratopsid dinosaur . it lived in north america during the upper cretaceous , about 76 to 73 million years ago . `` monoclonius '' was about 16 feet ( 5 meters ) long , and probably weighed about 2 tons . `` monoclonius '' was named by edward drinker cope in 1876 , soon after the first fossil specimen ( a near complete skeleton including skull material and the pbase of the nasal horn , with only the feet missing ) had been founded in montana . its name means `` single stem '' and refers to its teeth , which have a single root .
