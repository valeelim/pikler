Lead nitrate     lead nitrate is a chemical compound . it has lead and nitrate ions in it . it was known long ago as plumb dulcis . the lead is in the +2 oxidation state . lead nitrate is a white solid . it is one of the few lead compounds that can dissolve in water . lead ( ii ) acetate is the other lead compound that can dissolve . it is an oxidizing agent . it crackles when it is heated . it is toxic because it has lead in it . it is made by dissolving lead , lead ( ii ) oxide , or lead ( ii ) carbonate in nitric acid . it is used to make other lead compounds . related pages . - lead ( ii ) oxide - lead carbonate
