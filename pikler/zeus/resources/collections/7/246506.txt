Seating capacity     seating capacity is the highest number of people who can sit in a specific space . this limit can be either from the amount of space available , or from a law saying how many people are allowed to be there at a time . seating capacity can be for different kinds of things . it can be the number of people who can fit in a vehicle , for example an automobile or an airplane . it can also be for a stadium . the largest sports building in the world , the indianapolis motor speedway , has a permanent seating capacity of more than 257,000 people . seats can be added on the field to raise that number to about 400,000 .
