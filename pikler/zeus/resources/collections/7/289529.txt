Misery (Maroon 5 song)    misery ( maroon 5 song ) misery is a song by american band maroon 5. it was released on june 22 , 2010 by a & m/octone records via the itunes store . the song was released in advance of the band 's mid-2010 tour , and is the first single from their third studio album , hands all over , which was released on september 21 , 2010 .
