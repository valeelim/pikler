Phosphorus(V) chloride    phosphorus ( v ) chloride phosphorus ( v ) chloride , also known as phosphorus pentachloride , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is pcl . it contains phosphorus and chloride ions . the phosphorus is in the +5 oxidation state . phosphorus ( v ) chloride is a colorless solid . it is one of the most important phosphorus chlorides . sometimes it is yellowish . it reacts with hot water to make phosphoric acid and hydrochloric acid . it is made by reacting a lot of chlorine with phosphorus . it can also be made by reacting chlorine with phosphorus ( iii ) chloride . phosphorus ( v ) chloride is used to add chloride to other chemical compounds . phosphorus ( v ) chloride is acidic and toxic . it puts off toxic gases when reacted with water . related pages . - phosphorus ( iii ) iodide - phosphorus ( v ) oxide - phosphorus ( iii ) oxide
