Box Car Racer     box car racer was an american alternative rock/post-hardcore band from san diego , california that were active from 2001 to 2003. box car racer was a side-project for blink-182 members tom delonge and travis barker and lead guitarist of hazen street , david kennedy and touring bassist anthony celestino . when they were active they were signed to the record label mca records . discography . albums . - box car racer ( 2002 ) singles . - `` i feel so '' ( 2009 ) - `` there is '' ( 2003 ) websites . - box car racer at interscope records
