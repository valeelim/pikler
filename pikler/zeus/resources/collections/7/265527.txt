Barium bromide     barium bromide is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is babr . it contains barium and bromide ions . barium bromide is a colorless solid . it dissolves easily in water . it is very toxic because of the barium ions . it reacts with the sulfate ion to make insoluble ( does not dissolve in water ) barium sulfate . barium bromide can be made by reacting barium carbonate with hydrobromic acid . it can also be made by a violent reaction between barium and bromine . it can be used to make zinc bromide . it also is used to purify radium . related pages . - barium chloride - barium phosphate
