Holes     holes is a novel written by louis sachar . it was published on august 20 , 1998 by farrar , straus and giroux , with cover art by vladimir radunsky . it won the newbery medal in 1999. in 2003 , disney released a movie which was based on this novel , which was directed by andrew davis and written by the author of `` holes '' himself , louis sachar . in 2006 , sachar also published a companion novel called `` small steps '' , which is about one of the characters from `` holes '' .
