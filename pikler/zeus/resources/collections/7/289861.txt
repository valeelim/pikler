Microsoft Publisher     microsoft publisher is a desktop publishing application ( program ) . it is made by microsoft and it is part of microsoft office . publisher is similar to word except that it places the emphasis on page layout and design ( rather than writing text and proof reading ) . with publisher , you can create a variety of publication types . for example , you can make brochures , newsletters , postcards and greeting cards as well as normal , 'word-like ' documents . publisher 's file extension is `` .pub '' . the current version is 2013 for windows ; there is no version for macintosh .
