Rice University     william marsh rice university , also called rice university , is a private university in houston , texas . rice was started in 1912. it is ranked one of the best universities in the world , especially for undergraduate education . rice is named after the houston businessman william marsh rice who provided the money in his will to begin the university . rice is organized into eleven residential colleges and eight schools of study , for natural sciences , engineering , humanities , business , architecture , and music . the students at rice abide by an honor code that is strictly enforced .
