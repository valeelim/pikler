Fruitadens     fruitadens is a genus of heterodontosaur dinosaur . the name means `` fruita tooth '' , in reference to fruita , colorado , where its fossils were first found . it is known from partial skulls and skeletons from at least four individuals from rocks of the morrison formation in colorado . `` fruitadens '' is a small ornithischian dinosaur , with young adults estimated at 65 to 75 cm ( 26 to 30 in ) in length and 0.5 to 0.75 kg ( 1.1 to 1.7 lb ) in weight . the lower jaws had an enlarged canine-like tooth , opposite a gap in the upper jaw ( a 'diastema ' ) . unlike `` echinodon '' , there was n't an enlarged tooth in the upper jaw . a small peg-like tooth was present in front of the canine-like tooth . replacement teeth were present in the jaws , unlike most other heterodontosaurids . `` fruitadens '' was an omnivore .
