Star anise     illicium verum , or star anise is a spice that tastes like anise . the star shaped fruits are harvested before they start getting ripe . star anise has been used in a tea , and the seeds are sometimes chewed after meals for digestion . star anise is grown in four places in china and harvested between march and may . it is also found in the south of new south wales . japanese star anise ( `` illicium anisatum '' ) , a similar tree , is not edible because it is `` highly toxic '' ; instead , it has been burned as incense in japan .
