Sealed lead acid battery     a sealed lead acid battery or gel cell is a lead acid battery that has the sulfuric acid electrolyte coagulated ( thickened ) so it can not spill out . they are partially sealed , but have vents in case gases are accidentally released for example by overcharging . they can be used for smaller applications where they are turned up side down . they are more expensive than normal lead acid batteries , but they are also safer . they use different chemicals than dry cells , so they are rechargeable . related pages . - battery - lead acid battery - table of batteries
