Sinornithosaurus     sinornithosaurus ( derived from latin and greek , meaning 'chinese bird-lizard ' ) was a feathered dinosaur from the lower cretaceous , a dromaeosaur . it was found in the yixian formation in what is now china . this new fossil supports the idea that these dinosaurs did not have scaly , reptile-like skin , but had a softer , downy coat . no fossil skin impressions of these dinosaurs had been found previously .
