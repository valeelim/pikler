STS-1     sts-1 was the first flight of the space shuttle . it launched on april 12 , 1981 and landed on april 14. it was launched 20 years after the first manned space flight . the space shuttle used was `` columbia '' . space shuttle columbia orbited the earth 37 times in this mission . sts-1 was the first of two missions with a painted external tank . the paint was put to protect the external tank from ultraviolet light when it was standing on the launch pad . after sts-2 , the paint was removed
