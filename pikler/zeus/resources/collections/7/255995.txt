Canteen (bottle)    canteen ( bottle ) a canteen is a bottle which can be re-used . canteens are most used for carrying water to places where water is hard to find . many canteens can be worn on a belt , or held with a strap , like a purse . they are carried often by soldiers , hikers , and campers . old canteens were mostly made of metal but newer ones can be made of plastic . the idea of a canteen comes from ancient times when water and wine were carried in bags made from animal skins .
