Iowa State University     iowa state university of science and technology or as it is better known , iowa state university is a public university in ames , iowa . the university has about 33,000 students . it was founded in 1858. the president of the university is steven leath . george washington carver was a student at iowa state . the athletics teams of the university are called the cyclones . they have 16 varsity teams and they compete in the big 12 conference . other websites . - iowa state university 's official website
