Waco, Texas    waco , texas waco ( ) is a city in mclennan county , texas . the city has an estimated total population of 126,217. major waco attractions include : - armstrong browning library - baylor university - cameron park zoo - dr pepper museum - lake waco - grand lodge of texas - mayborn museum complex - the ranger wing of the commemorative air force - texas ranger hall of fame and museum - texas sports hall of fame - texas state technical college - waco mammoth site - waco suspension bridge
