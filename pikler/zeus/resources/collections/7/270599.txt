Michael Portillo     michael denzil xavier portillo ( born 26 may 1953 ) is a british television presenter and former conservative party politician . michael portillo was born in bushey , hertfordshire , to a scottish mother and spanish father . portillo first became a member of parliament ( mp ) in a by-election in 1984 , representing the constituency of enfield southgate . he very much liked margaret thatchers policies and was quite eurosceptic , meaning that he was against the european union . he became a cabinet member in 1992 , holding the following positions : chief secretary to the treasury ( april 1992 - july 1994 ) , secretary of state for employment ( july 1994 - july 1995 ) and defence secretary ( july 1995 - may 1997 ) . many people thought he would try to become the leader of the conservative party in 1995 but he chose to stay loyal to john major . at the 1997 general election , portillo lost his seat in the house of commons to the labour party . portillo returned to the house of commons after winning a by-election in 1999 for the constituency of kensington and chelsea . he then started to re-think many of his ideas and began to argue for a more socially liberal conservative party . he became the shadow chancellor of the exchequer in february 2000 but he did not have a good relationship with party leader william hague . he was a candidate in the 2001 conservative party leadership contest ; he lost to iain duncan smith . portillo left the house of commons at the 2005 general election and has since hosted several television and radio programmes .
