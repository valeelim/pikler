Western Grebe     the western grebe is a species of grebe , or bird . they are well known for their dancing on the top of the water in their early nesting season after mating . the two birds will hold long strands of grass or straw in their bills and dance a dance called `` weed dancing '' . then , at the climax , they rise and make a pose , which is called rushing , and run across the water 's surface for a little bit before they dive . males also used this dance when they wanted to protect their territories or fight over the female .
