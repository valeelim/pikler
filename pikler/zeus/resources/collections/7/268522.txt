Jay Treaty     the jay treaty was a treaty between the united states and britain in the years following the american revolution . the main american involved in the treaty was john jay , who was also chief justice of the united states . it was passed by congress in 1795. it allowed for increased trade with britain in exchange for the british giving up their forts on the great lakes . many people thought that the jay treaty was good for the british and bad for the americans , which led to them burning effigies of jay .
