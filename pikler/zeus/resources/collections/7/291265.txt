Torsten Wiesel     torsten nils wiesel ( born june 3 , 1924 ) is a swedish neuroscientist . he and david hubel won the 1981 nobel prize in physiology or medicine , for their discoveries of information processing in the visual system . the prize was shared with roger sperry for his independent research on the cerebral hemispheres . after serving as president of the rockefeller university , he is now director of the shelby white and leon levy center for mind , brain and behavior at the same institution . he is also an advisor to several countries and organizations , mainly helping young scientists do research . he is a member of the president 's council of university of the people . torsten was married to american editor jean stein from 1995 until they divorced in 2007 .
