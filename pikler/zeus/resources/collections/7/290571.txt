Sidney Lumet     sidney lumet ( june 25 , 1924 - april 9 , 2011 ) was an american movie director , producer and screenwriter . he was born in philadelphia , pennsylvania . he directed more than 200 plays for live television . he also directed more than 40 movies . his movies include `` serpico '' , `` dog day afternoon '' , `` 12 angry men '' , `` network '' , `` prince of the city '' and `` q & a '' . he died of lymphoma in new york city .
