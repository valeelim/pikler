Google Sidewiki     google sidewiki is an online service from google launched on september 23 , 2009. google sidewiki is an extension for web browser . you have sidewiki for internet explorer and firefox through google toolbar . you have sidewiki for google chrome browser through an add-on . you have sidewiki for other browsers like safari as a bookmarklet . when you visit a website with google sidewiki you can press on sidewiki button . it opens a window where you can read others ' comments or write your own comments . the owner of the website can not stop you from doing this .
