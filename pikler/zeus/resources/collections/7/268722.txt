Nodosaurus     nodosaurus ( meaning `` knobbed lizard '' ) was a large , armored , quadrupedal ( it walked on four legs ) , tank-like dinosaur . it lived in the first part of the upper cretaceous about 113â€“98 million years ago . three very incomplete `` nodosaurus '' fossils have been found in wyoming and kansas ; no skulls have been found yet . it was named by paleontologist othniel c. marsh in 1889 . `` nodosaurus '' was about 13 to 20 ft ( 4-6 m ) long . it was an ornithischian ( bird-hipped ) dinosaur with bony dermal plates covering the top of its body . it had four short legs , five-toed feet , a short neck , and a long , clubless tail . the nodosaurids had no tail club ; it is not known how they defended themselves .
