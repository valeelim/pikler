Sodium sulfate     sodium sulfate , also known as glauber 's salt , is a chemical compound made of sodium and sulfate ions . it is a bitter-tasting white solid . it is made by a neutralization reaction between sodium hydroxide with sulfuric acid . the sodium ions are in the oxidation state of +1 . it is used to make detergents and paper making . its ph is neutral . related pages . - sulfite - acid - base
