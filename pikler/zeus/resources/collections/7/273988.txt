Maebashi, Gunma    maebashi , gunma it has been recognized as a core city since 2009. hagiwara sakutaro is famous poet who was born in gunma . his most famous work `` tsuki ni hoeru '' was published in 1917 . `` sakutaro kinen kan '' is a memorial museuum in maebashi . rinkokaku is a wooden building which was built in 1884. it is an important cultural property . `` misopan '' , meaning “ bread with miso paste ” originates from yakimanju . friand , numata , is famous for misopan . the buns are made from gunma prefecture ’ s high quality wheat . azuki bean paste is not used , but rather , a spread of miso with sugar , which is then grilled until brown . the prefectural office building was formed from maebashi castle . it was renovated in 1999 , and became the tallest building in maebashi . other websites . - maebashi city website
