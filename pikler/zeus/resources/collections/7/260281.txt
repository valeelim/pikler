Chromyl chloride     chromyl chloride , also known as chromium oxychloride is a chemical compound . it has the formula crocl . it is an red liquid . it is made by reacting hydrochloric acid with chromium ( vi ) oxide or potassium chromate . it is a strong oxidizing agent . when it is mixed with sulfur it oxidizes it . it is the result of a test for chloride . it is toxic because it contains chromium in its +6 oxidation state . it also contains some hydrochloric acid . it reacts with grease and some plastics . related pages . - chromic acid - chromate - dichromate
