Hypobromite     hypobromite is an ion . its chemical formula is bro . it contains bromine in its +1 oxidation state . it is an oxidizing agent . it is similar to hypochlorite . hypobromites are the salts of hypobromous acid . hypobromite is a disinfectant . some white blood cells oxidize bromide to hypobromite with peroxide and use the hypobromite to kill germs . bromides can be added to pools along with chlorine to make hypobromite , which disinfects the pools . hypobromites are made by dissolving bromine in a basic solution . hypobromite disproportionates to bromate and bromide when heated . related pages . - bromite - perbromate
