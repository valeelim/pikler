Walleye     walleye ( `` sander vitreus '' ) is a freshwater perciform fish native to most of canada and to the northern united states . it is a north american close relative of the european pikeperch . the walleye is sometimes also called the yellow walleye to distinguish it from the blue walleye , which is an extinct subspecies formerly found in the southern great lakes . references . - boyd huppert ( december 7 , 2004 ) . `` walleye or zander ? what are you really eating ?
