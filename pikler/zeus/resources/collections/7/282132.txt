'' with a then unknown michael bolton . in 1981 branigan appeared on german television in `` an american girl in berlin '' . she was a guest on the american tv show , `` chips '' . she starred in the movie `` mugsy 's girls '' in 1984. she also appeared in the 1988 movie `` backstage '' . in 2002 she starred in the off-broadway production of `` love , janis '' , playing janis joplin . she received rave ( very good ) reviews for her performance .
