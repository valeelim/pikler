Western Kentucky University     western kentucky university is a public university in bowling green , kentucky . the university has about 20,712 students . it was founded in 1906. the current president of the university is dr. gary a. ransdell . the athletics teams of the university for men are called the hilltoppers and the athletics teams for the women are called the lady toppers . they have 17 varsity teams , all of which play in conference usa . other websites . - western kentucky university 's official website - western kentucky hilltoppers ' official athletics website
