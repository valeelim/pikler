Awista Ayub     awista ayub ( c. 1979 ) is an afghan writer . she formed the afghan youth sports exchange in the united states . in her novel `` kabul girls soccer club '' , first called `` however tall the mountain '' , ayub writes of eight girls brought to the us to learn soccer . the afghan youth sports exchange has grown from the first 8 young women to hundreds playing through the afghanistan football federation . other websites . - home page
