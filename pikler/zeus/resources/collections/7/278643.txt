Ruslan Fedotenko     ruslan viktorovych fedotenko ( born january 18 , 1979 ) is an national hockey league ice hockey left winger for the new york rangers . fedotenko has also played for the philadelphia flyers for two seasons , the tampa bay lightning for four seasons , the new york islanders for one season and the pittsburgh penguins for two seasons before being signed to a 1-year-deal worth $ 1 million after accepting a tryout with the new york rangers .
