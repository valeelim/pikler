Non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug     non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs ( often shortened to nsaids ) are drugs that have analgesic or fever-reducing properties , but that are not based on steroids . higher doses of such drugs can also be used to fight inflammation . such drugs are special as they are not narcotic , that is they do n't induce sleep . well-known examples of such drugs are aspirin , diclofenac and ibuprofen .
