East Caribbean dollar     the east caribbean dollar ( sign : $ ; code : xcd ) is the currency of eight of the nine members of the organisation of eastern caribbean states ( the exception is the british virgin islands ) . it has existed since 1965 , when it replaced the british west indies dollar , and it is normally abbreviated with the dollar sign `` $ '' or , alternatively , `` ec $ '' to distinguish it from other dollar-denominated currencies . the ec $ is subdivided into 100 cents . it has been pegged to the united states dollar since 1976 and the exchange rate is currently at us $ 1 = ec $ 2.70 . six of the eight countries which use the ec $ are independent ( antigua and barbuda , dominica , grenada , saint kitts and nevis , saint lucia , and saint vincent and the grenadines ) . the other two are british overseas territories : anguilla and montserrat . all of the countries except dominica are commonwealth realms .
