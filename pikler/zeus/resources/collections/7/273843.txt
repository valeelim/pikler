Light beam     a light beam or beam of light is a small projection of light energy coming from a source into a beam . sunlight is a natural example of a light beam when it crosses through various objects ( clouds , foliage , windows , etc. ) . to produce fake light , a lamp and a parabolic reflector is used in many lighting devices such as spotlights , car headlights . light from certain types of lasers has the smallest possible beam width , so it produces the most parallel light beams possible .
