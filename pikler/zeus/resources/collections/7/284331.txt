Faye Dunaway     dorothy faye dunaway ( born january 14 , 1941 in bascom , florida , usa ) is an american actress . she has starred in `` bonnie and clyde '' , `` chinatown '' , and the 1968 and 1999 film versions of `` the thomas crown affair '' . she won an academy award for best actress for her role in `` network '' . duanaway is a convert to roman catholicism .
