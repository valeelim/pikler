Croissant     the croissant is a type of bread . it is shaped like a crescent moon . it is usually a breakfast food . it is also called a crescent . it can also be called a crescent roll , although a crescent roll may be smaller . it is not a quick bread . it is made of a variant of puff pastry . breads shaped like crescents have been around since medieval times . croissants used to be high-class food . now they can be bought frozen which can be cooked with only a little work .
