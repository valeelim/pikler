Cuban Solenodon     the cuban solenodon or almiqui ( `` solenodon cubanus '' ) , is a species of soricomorph that is endemic to cuba . it belongs to the family solenodontidae along with a similar species , the hispaniolan solenodon ( `` solenodon paradoxus '' ) . the solenodon is unusual among mammals in that its saliva is venomous . since its discovery in 1861 by the german naturalist wilhelm peters .
