Ammonium sulfate     ammonium sulfate is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is ( nh ) so . it contains ammonium and sulfate ions . it is slightly acidic because ammonia is a weak base . it is made by reacting ammonia with sulfuric acid . it breaks down when heated . it is used in fertilizers to provide both sulfur and nitrogen to plants . it is banned in some parts of the middle east because it was being used to make explosives . it rarely occurs in the ground . related pages . - sodium sulfate - sulfite - potassium sulfate
