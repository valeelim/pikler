Eddie Colón     edwin `` eddie '' carlos colón ( born december 21 , 1982 ) is a puerto rican pro wrestler . he is currently working in the wwe where competes under the ring name primo colon . he is the son of carlos colón , sr. and the younger brother of pro wrestlers stacy and carly colón , who used to wrestle as carlito in wwe . the brothers , known as the colóns , were the first team to win the wwe tag team championship and the world tag team championship at the same time , making them the first unified wwe tag team champions . other websites . - wwe profile
