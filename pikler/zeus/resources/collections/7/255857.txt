Billy Butler     billy butler ( born april 18 , 1986 ) is an american baseball player . he plays first base for the kansas city royals of major league baseball ( mlb ) . the royals chose butler in the 2004 first-year player draft . butler had played baseball in high school in jacksonville , florida , and entered the draft instead of playing in college . he played his first mlb game on may 1 , 2007 .
