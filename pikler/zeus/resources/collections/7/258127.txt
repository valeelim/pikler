Glycerine (song)    glycerine ( song ) `` glycerine '' is the fourth single by the post-grunge band bush . `` glycerine '' reached number one for two weeks in december of 1996. it was bush 's biggest pop hit , peaking at number 28 on the billboard hot 100 on february 24 , 1996 and the hot 100 airplay on january 27 , 1996. the song is notable for not featuring any drums . references . - http : //www.rockonthenet.com/archive/1996/mtvvmas.htm
