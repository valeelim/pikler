University of Northern Colorado     the university of northern colorado is a public , coeducational university in greeley , colorado . the university has about 12,358 students . it was founded in 1889. the current president of the university is kay norton . the men 's athletics teams of the university are called the bears . they have 15 varsity teams and they compete in the big sky conference . other websites . - university of northern colorado 's official website - northern colorado bears ' official athletics website
