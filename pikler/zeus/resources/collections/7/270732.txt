Hypobromous acid     hypobromous acid is a chemical compound . it is also an acid . its chemical formula is hbro . it contains hydrogen and hypobromite ions . it is a weak and unstable acid . it is similar to hypochlorous acid . it disproportionates into bromate and bromide when heated or left to dry . it is made when bromine is added to warm water . it can kill many bacteria and deodorize . it works very well when it is with hypochlorous acid . related pages . - bromous acid - bromic acid - perbromic acid
