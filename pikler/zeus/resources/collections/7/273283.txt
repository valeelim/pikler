Silver iodate     silver iodate is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is agio . it has silver and iodate ions in it . the silver is in the +1 oxidation state . silver iodate is a colorless solid . it does not dissolve in water . it is an oxidizing agent . it is made by reacting silver nitrate with sodium iodate . related pages . - potassium iodate - sodium periodate - sodium bromate
