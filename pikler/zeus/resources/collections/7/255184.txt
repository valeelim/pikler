Clumsy (album)    clumsy ( album ) clumsy is the second studio album by canadian rock band our lady peace . it was released on january 23 , 1997. the producer of the album was arnold lanni . it sold over 1,000,000 copies making it diamond status in canada . it peaked # 33 on the top 102 new rock albums of all time by 102.1 the edge in 2009. singles from `` clumsy '' . - 1996 - `` superman 's dead '' - 1997 - `` automatic flowers '' - 1997 - `` carnival '' - 1998 - `` 4am '' - 1998 - `` clumsy ''
