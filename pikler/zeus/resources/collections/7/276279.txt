Fear of the Dark (Iron Maiden album)    fear of the dark ( iron maiden album ) fear of the dark is a studio album by iron maiden . it went on sale on 11 may 1992. it was the most popular album in the united kingdom when it started being sold . the album got mixed reviews . one person who reviewed it said that it was a `` return to form , '' but had too many songs that were boring on it . musicians . - bruce dickinson – vocals , bass guitar - dave murray – guitar - janick gers – guitar - steve harris – bass guitar , backing vocals - nicko mcbrain – drums
