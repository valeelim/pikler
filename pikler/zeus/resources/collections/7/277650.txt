Claire Danes     claire catherine danes ( born april 12 , 1979 ) is an american actress . she was born in new york city , new york . danes starred as angela chase in `` my so-called life '' in the 1994-1995 television season . she also acted in many movies , including the 1996 movie `` romeo + juliet '' and the 2010 movie `` temple grandin '' . she plays carrie mathison in `` homeland '' .
