Crystal Castles II     crystal castles , also known as crystal castles ii , is the second album by electronic music group crystal castles . the album was released by fiction records on 24 may 2010 . `` crystal castles '' has been met with mostly good reviews from people notes . - ethan kath sings `` not in love '' , which is a cover of a song by 1980s new wave band platinum blonde . - `` year of silence '' samples the vocal track from the sigur ros song called `` inní mér syngur vitleysingur '' . - both `` violent dreams '' and `` vietnam '' contain parts of the song `` a walk in the park '' by stina nordenstam . - the cd sleeve notes state that the album cover is a photo of x tecumseh clark taken by his father , avant-garde composer and poet todd tamanend clark .
