What the Hell     `` what the hell '' is a 2011 single from canadian singer avril lavigne 's album `` goodbye lullaby '' . it was released on january 11 , 2011. the song received a 4 out of 5 from about.com , a 5 out of 5 from digital spy , a 3.5 out of 5 from rolling stone and was called positive by blogcritics , `` frost magazine '' , idolator , mtv and was classified as a mixed reaction by stereoboard.com .
