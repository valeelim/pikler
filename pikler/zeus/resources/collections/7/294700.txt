2 Baruch     2 baruch is a text that was written in the late first century , or the early second century ce . it was probably written after the destruction of the temple in jerusalem , in 70 ce , and before the second roman-jewish war , 132-135 ce . it is attributed to baruch ben neriah and is included in some editions of the old testament and the peshitta , but neither jews nor christians regard it as scripture . the syrian orthodox church includes it in the bible . the text has 87 sections and is also known as syriac apocalypse of baruch and letter of baruch to the nine and a half tribes .
