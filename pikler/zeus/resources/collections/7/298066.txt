Najib Ullah     najib ullah ( february 14 , 1914 to august 2 , 1965 ) was an afghan writer and ambassador . ullah worked for the foreign office of afghanistan for many years . he was also a representative to india , england and the united states . he worked as the head of the afghan group at unesco general assembly in beirut , lebanon in 1948. books . - `` political history of afghanistan '' , 1942-1944 - `` strabo and aryana '' , 1945 - `` negotiations with pakistan '' , 1948 - `` islamic literature '' , 1963
