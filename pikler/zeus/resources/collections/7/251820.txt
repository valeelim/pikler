Ubaldo Jiménez     ubaldo jiménez ( born january 22 , 1984 ) is a pitcher for the baltimore orioles . he became the first pitcher for the colorado rockies to throw a no-hitter on april 17 , 2010. he is from the dominican republic . in 2010 , jiménez was the starting national league pitcher at the mlb all-star game , which the national league won .
