Superstar (movie)    superstar ( movie ) superstar is a 1999 comedy movie and `` saturday night live '' spin-off about a quirky , socially incompetent girl called mary katherine gallagher . the character was created by the `` snl '' star , molly shannon and appeared as a recurring character on `` snl '' in many skits . the story tells about mary katherine trying to find her place in her catholic high school . the movie was directed by a former `` kids in the hall '' member , bruce mcculloch . the movie stars the following people : - molly shannon - will ferrell - harland williams - elaine hendrix `` snl '' and `` kids in the hall '' alum , mark mckinney , who appeared in many of the mary katherine gallagher `` snl '' skits on television , also has a minor role as a priest . molly shannon got a nomination for blockbuster entertainment award `` favorite actress - comedy '' , but lost to heather graham in `` austin powers : the spy who shagged me '' . mary katherine gallagher ( shannon ) is an irish-american catholic school girl and social outcast in a catholic high school . she wants to be a superstar , as she wants to get a kiss from sky corrigan ( ferrell ) . when mary is sent to special education , she makes a new best friend , the tomboyish and equally socially-awkward helen lewengrub ( emmy laybourne ) .
