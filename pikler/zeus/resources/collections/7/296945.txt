Emme river     the emme is a river in switzerland . the emmental ( literally : valley of the emme ) is named after it . the river is about 80 km long , and verses itself into the aare near solothurn . on 13th august 1837 there was the biggest known flood of the river . jeremias gotthelf describes this in a story . this story , as well as other flooods led to the river being canalised in the 19th century .
