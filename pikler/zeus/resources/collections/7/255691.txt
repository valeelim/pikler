Steve Brookstein     steve brookstein ( born 10 november 1968 ) is a british soul and jazz singer from london . he has been active since he won the 2004 uk series of talent show `` the x factor '' . he has had 1 hit single to date , number 1 hit , against all odds a cover of the 1984 phil collins original his version was number 11 in ireland . he was part of the syco music record label . his first album `` heart & soul '' reached number 1 in the united kingdom album charts . his second album `` 40,000 things `` only reached number 165 .
