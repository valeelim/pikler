Youngstown State University     youngstown state university is a public university in youngstown , ohio . the university has about 15,194 students . it was founded in 1908. the current president of the university is jim tressel , better known as a former football coach both at youngstown state and ohio state university . the athletics teams of the university are called the penguins . they have 16 varsity teams and they compete in the horizon league and the missouri valley football conference for football . other websites . - youngstown state university 's official website - youngstown state penguins ' official athletics website
