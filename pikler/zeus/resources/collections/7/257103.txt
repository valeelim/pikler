Nunc dimittis     the nunc dimittis is a hymn from the bible . it was sung by simeon when he saw the baby jesus . the story told in the book of luke was that simeon , who was a jew , had been promised by the holy ghost that he would not die until he had seen jesus . when mary and joseph brought the baby jesus to the temple in jerusalem for the ceremony of consecration of the firstborn son , simeon was there , and he took jesus into his arms and said the words which are now known as the nunc dimittis . he was saying to god that he could now die happily because he had seen the saviour . the words of the nunc dimittis have been set to music by many composers . in the anglican church they are traditionally sung at evensong . the english words as found in the `` book of common prayer '' , 1662 ) : other pages . - magnificat - service ( music )
