Luis Gonzalez (outfielder)    luis gonzalez ( outfielder ) luis emilio gonzalez ( born september 3 , 1967 in tampa , florida ) is a retired american major league baseball ( mlb ) outfielder . he had his greatest success with the arizona diamondbacks , becoming a star in 2001. he got the hit that won the world series for the diamondbacks that year . in 2010 , it was announced that gonzalez would be the first diamondbacks player to have his jersey number ( 20 ) retired . awards . - selected for the mlb all-star game in 1999 , 2001 , 2002 , 2003 and 2005 - home run derby winner in 2001 - silver slugger award winner in 2001 - branch rickey award in 2005 - world series champion in 2001
