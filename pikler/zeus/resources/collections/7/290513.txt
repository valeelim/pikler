Melinoe     in greek mythology , melinoe is the goddess of ghosts . she is the daughter of persephone and hades . she lives in the underworld . she has a pale complexion and a short temper and takes after her father in almost every way , except from her beauty that she gets from her mother persephone . melinoe has the powers to turn any mortal insane by her phantoms . little is known about melinoe , but ancient greeks used to conduct rituals and acts of praises to her as a sign of devotion and to garner protection from ghosts .
