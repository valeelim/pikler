Group 15 element     the group 15 elements , also called the pnictogens , group v elements , or group vb elements , are a group on the periodic table . they have many different properties . nitrogen is a colorless unreactive gas . phosphorus is a reactive white or red solid nonmetal . arsenic is a highly toxic semimetal . antimony is also a semimetal . bismuth is a metal . they all have 5 valence electrons . most of them form +5 or -3 chemical compounds .
