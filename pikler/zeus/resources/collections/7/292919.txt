Pasadena Star-News     the pasadena star-news is the local daily newspaper for pasadena , california . the `` star-news '' is a member of the los angeles newspaper group , since 1996. it is also part of the san gabriel valley newspaper group , along with the `` san gabriel valley tribune '' and the `` whittier daily news '' . ridder newspapers bought the `` star-news '' in 1956 ; bernard j. ridder took over as publisher . ridder merged with knight to form knight ridder in 1974. the paper was sold in 1989 to a company owned by william dean singleton ; the thomson corporation bought majority control of the paper a year later . thomson sold the `` star-news '' to singleton 's medianews group in 1996. the pasadena star-news was first published in 1884. the paper was in a building at the corner of colorado blvd . and oakland avenue for years . this building is now being used by technique at le cordon bleu college of culinary arts and 24 hour fitness . the newspaper also published the `` rose magazine '' . other websites . - pasadena star-news website
