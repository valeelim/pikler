Arsenic tribromide     arsenic tribromide , also known as arsenic ( iii ) bromide , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is asbr . it has arsenic and bromide ions in it . the arsenic is in its +3 oxidation state . arsenic tribromide is a white or pale yellow solid . it is highly toxic . it reacts with water to make arsenic trioxide and hydrobromic acid . it melts at a low temperature . it is made by reacting arsenic and bromine . related pages . - arsenic trichloride - antimony tribromide - bismuth tribromide
