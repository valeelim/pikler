Engelbert Humperdinck     engelbert humperdinck ( 1 september 1854 – 27 september 1921 ) was a german composer . today , he is seen as part of the romantic movement . most people remember him for his opera , `` hänsel und gretel '' which was first performed in 1893. british singer , arnold george dorsey ( born 1936 ) , uses the name `` engelbert humperdinck '' as his stage name . he wanted the name to make him seem more cultured and refined .
