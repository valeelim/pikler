Reggie Jackson     reginald martinez `` reggie '' jackson ( born may 18 , 1946 ) was a baseball player for the oakland athletics , the new york yankees , and the california angels . he was named mr. october in the 1977 series . in 1973 and 77 he was named the most valuable player of the world series . he played right fielder . jackson played 21 seasons and reached the post-season in 11 of them , winning six pennants and four world series . jackson was also voted to the baseball hall of fame in 1993 on the first ballot . his number was retired by the athletics and the yankees . in 2007 , espn aired a mini-series called `` the bronx is burning '' . it was about the 1977 yankees . the conflicts and controversies around jackson are a central part of the storyline . jackson is portrayed by daniel sunjata .
