Antiseptic     an antiseptic is a substance that is applied to lesions or wounds of the skin to kill germs . this helps prevent the wound becoming infected . commonly used antiseptics include alcohol , iodine and mercury compounds . antiseptics are different from antibiotics . antiseptics can only be used on the skin , antibiotics can also be used internally , through the blood and lymphatic systems . they are also different from disinfectants : disinfectants are used on non-living objects only , because they are dangerous to the living .
