Mike Bibby     michael `` mike '' bibby ( born may 13 , 1978 in cherry hill , new jersey ) is a retired american basketball player who played with the sacramento kings of the nba for most of his career . he attended college at university of arizona where he played basketball with the wildcats . mike bibby started his nba career when he was drafted by the vancouver grizzlies in the 1998 nba draft with the 2nd pick in the 1st round . bibby played 3 seasons with the vancouver grizzlies , 7 seasons with the sacramento kings , 3 seasons with the atlanta hawks and two games with the washington wizards before having his contract bought out by the wizards on february 28 , 2011. other websites . - mike bibby 's official website - mike bibby 's nba player profile
