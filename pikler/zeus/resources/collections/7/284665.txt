Honda Civic     the honda civic is a subcompact and compact car that is built by the honda company . the civic has had 9 generations . the honda civic has been built and sold since 1972. the 8th and 9th generation civics have a digital speedometer . the civic lx is available with an automatic or manual transmission . the civic ex is only available with an automatic transmission . the top-of-the-line civic is called the civic si , and is only available with a manual transmission . other websites . - honda civic coupe at honda.ca - honda civic sedan at honda.ca
