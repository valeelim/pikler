Lipscomb University     lipscomb university is a private , coeducational university in nashville , tennessee . the university has about 3,742 students . it was founded in 1891. the current president of the university is l. randolph lowry iii . the athletics teams of the university are called the bisons and they compete in the atlantic sun conference . other websites . - lipscomb university 's official website - lipscomb bisons ' official athletics website
