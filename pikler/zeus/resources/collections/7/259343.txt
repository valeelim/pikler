Tin(II) sulfate    tin ( ii ) sulfate tin ( ii ) sulfate is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is snso . it contains tin and sulfate ions . the tin is in its +2 oxidation state . it is a white crystalline solid . it is not a reducing agent like other compounds containing tin ( ii ) , like tin ( ii ) chloride . it absorbs water from the atmosphere and dissolves in the water it absorbed . it is made by dissolving tin in sulfuric acid . it is also made by reacting tin with copper ( ii ) sulfate . it is used as a source of tin ( ii ) ions without any tin ( iv ) ions contaminating them . related pages . - tin ( ii ) sulfide - tin ( iv ) sulfide
