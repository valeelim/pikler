Astro Boy     astro boy ( 鉄腕アトム , `` tetsuwan atomu '' , lit . `` iron arm atom '' ) is a japanese manga series first published in 1952 and television program first broadcast in japan in 1963. the story follows the adventures of a robot named `` astro boy '' and a selection of other characters along the way . `` astro boy '' is the first , most popular japanese television series that embodied the aesthetic that later became familiar worldwide as anime . it originated as a manga in 1952 by osamu tezuka , revered in japan as the `` god of manga '' .
