Benedict Arnold     benedict arnold v ( â€“ 14 june 1801 ) was a general during the american revolutionary war . he began the war in the continental army but later changed to the british army . while a general on the american side he became commander of the fort at west point , new york , and plotted to surrender it to the british forces . after the plot was exposed in september 1780 , he was made a brigadier general in the british army . born in connecticut , arnold was a merchant operating ships on the atlantic ocean when the war broke out in 1775. after joining the growing army outside boston , he distinguished himself through acts of cunning and bravery . his actions included 1 . 1775 : the capture of fort ticonderoga 1 . 1776 : defensive and delaying tactics after losing the battle of valcour island on lake champlain 1. the battle of ridgefield , connecticut ( when was promoted to major general ) , 1. relieving the siege of fort stanwix , and 1 . 1777 : actions in the battles of saratoga , in which he suffered leg injuries that ended his combat career for several years .
