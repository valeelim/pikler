Land Transport Authority     the land transport authority ( abbreviation : lta ; chinese : 陆路交通管理局 ( shortform : 陆交局 ) ; malay : penguasa pengangkutan darat ; ) of singapore is a statutory board of the ministry of transport . it was founded in 1 september 1995 , by merging four agencies , which are registry of vehicles , mass rapid transit corporation , roads & transportation division of the public works department of singapore and land transport division of the then-ministry of communications . lta leads the development of land transport in singapore . other websites . - official website
