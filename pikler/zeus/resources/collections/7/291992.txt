Innocent (Our Lady Peace song)    innocent ( our lady peace song ) `` 'innocent ' '' is a 2002 alternative rock song by the canadian alternative rock band our lady peace and is the second single from their fifth studio album `` gravity '' , which was released in 2002. the song was written by raine maida and was produced by bob rock . the song is about people that are suffering which also includes a girl with cancer . maida stated in 2002 that innocent was his favorite song on the gravity album and said that `` i wrote this song over a year ago and i was originally hesitant to play it for the band , but once we got into the studio and began working with bob we were able to make it an our lady peace song . `` .
