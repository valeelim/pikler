Attila the Hun     attila , or attila the hun ( 406-453 ) , was a leader of the huns from 434 ce until he died in 453 ce . he was one of the most common ruler . attila led the hunnic empire , and was known as a feared military commander . he invaded gaul , and nearly captured the city of aurelianum . in 451 ce , he lost the battle of the catalaunian plains , the only one he ever lost . he also invaded italy and attacked and looted several towns . attila died in 453. the traditional account of his death is that it happened the night after his marriage to a woman named ildiko . the huns declined in the years immediately after attila 's death .
