Gloria Macapagal-Arroyo     maria gloria macapagal-arroyo ( born 5 april 1947 ) is a filipino politician who was the president of the philippines from 2001 to 2010 and the vice-president from 1998 to 2001. she is currently a member of the house of representatives . she was the country 's second female president , after corazon aquino , who was the first . she is also the daughter of former president diosdado macapagal .
