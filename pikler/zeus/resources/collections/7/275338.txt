Dana Delany     dana welles delany ( born march 13 , 1956 ) is an emmy award-winning american movie and television actress . she is well known for her role as colleen mcmurphy on the abc television show `` china beach '' ( 1988–1991 ) , `` katherine mayfair '' on `` desperate housewives '' ( 2007–2010 ) and `` megan hunt '' on `` body of proof '' ( 2011–present ) .
