Fashion accessory     a fashion accessory is a decorative item that supplements one 's outfit . items such as such as jewellery , gloves , handbags , hats , belts , scarves , watches , sunglasses , pins , stockings , bow ties , leggings , ties , suspenders , and tights . accessories add color , style and class to an outfit , and create a certain look , but they may also have practical functions . handbags are for carrying small necessary items , hats protect the face from weather , laptops and mobile phones communicate , and gloves keep the hands warm . accessories may be used to show religious or cultural affiliation : crucifixes , star of david , islamic headscarves , skullcaps and turbans are common examples . designer labels on accessories are perceived as an indicator of social status . accessories are also available in the form of bracelets , necklaces , and earrings .
