Oakwood tube station     oakwood is a london underground station on the piccadilly line . it is the second most northerly station on the line , between southgate and cockfosters stations , and is in travelcard zone 5. the station is on the edge of the oakwood area of enfield ( n14 ) and is at the junction of bramley road ( a110 ) and chase road ( the other end of chase road is close to southgate tube station ) . other pages . - list of london underground stations other websites . - london transport museum photographic archive < br >
