Ghostwriter     a ghostwriter is a professional writer whose published work is credited to another person . one famous ghostwriter is andrew neiderman . he was hired by the estate of v. c. andrews , who died in 1986. niederman completed the `` casteel '' series of books and continues writing books credited to andrews . ghostwriters are paid a fee to help write the autobiographies of celebrities , athletes and politicians . they also help write letters , song lyrics , magazine and news articles that get published under someone else 's name .
