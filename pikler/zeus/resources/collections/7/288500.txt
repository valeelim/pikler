Altricial     altricial , meaning `` requiring care '' , refers to a group or kind of creature that is incapable of moving around on its own soon after hatching or being born . the word is derived from the latin root `` alere '' meaning `` to nurse , to rear , or to nourish '' , which means that young has to be taken care of for a long time before it can overcome its helplessness . birds or mammals which need much less care after birth are called precocial , meaning they are precocious . other websites . - the altricial-precocial spectrum in birds
