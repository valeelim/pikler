Manon Lescaut     manon lescaut is an italian opera in four acts by giacomo puccini , first performed in turin , on february 1 , 1893 , libretto by luigi illica , giuseppe giacosa , giulio ricordi , marco praga and domenico oliva . puccini 's third opera and the work that established his reputation . principal roles . - manon - soprano - des grieux - tenor - lescaut - baritone - geronte - bass - edmondo - tenor reference . - `` the complete dictionary of opera & operetta '' , james anderson , wings books , 1993 .
