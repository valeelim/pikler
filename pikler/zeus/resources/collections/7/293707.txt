Newcastle-under-Lyme     newcastle-under-lyme is a town near stoke-on-trent in staffordshire , england . it is part of the potteries , an area of the west midlands which was important in the industrial revolution . in the 2001 census the town had a population of 73,944. newcastle-under-lyme was chosen for the campus of university college of north staffordshire , established in 1949 two miles from the town centre . it was granted full university status as keele university in 1962. its medical school is based in the grounds of the university hospital of north staffordshire at hartshill in stoke-on-trent , about a mile from the centre of newcastle-under-lyme . newcastle-under-lyme also has a city college called newcastle-under-lyme college . hi : )
