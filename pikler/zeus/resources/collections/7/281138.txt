The New York Times Best Seller list     `` the new york times '' best seller list is a list of best-selling books in the united states that has been published weekly on sundays by the `` new york times '' , a new york city based newspaper since 9 april 1942. it is also released a stand-alone publication , meaning it is sometimes published separately outside of the newspaper , and can also be found online . the list is usually split into fiction and non-fiction sections ; each contain ten to twenty titles .
