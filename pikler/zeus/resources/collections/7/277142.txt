Brian Boucher     brian boucher ( born january 2 , 1977 in woonsocket , rhode island ) is a professional american ice hockey goaltender currently playing for the philadelphia flyers of the nhl . boucher was drafted into the nhl by the philadelphia flyers with the twenty-second overall pick in the 1995 nhl entry draft . boucher has also played for many other teams in the nhl : phoenix coyotes for 3 seasons , calgary flames for 1 season , chicago blackhawks for 1 season , columbus blue jackets for 1 season and the san jose sharks for 2 seasons .
