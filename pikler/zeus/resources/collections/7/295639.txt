Hubert Cecil Booth     hubert cecil booth ( 4 july 1871 - 14 january 1955 ) was a british engineer . he invented the vacuum cleaner . he was born in gloucester , england . he died in croydon , surrey . in 1900 , booth saw that if you put a handkerchief between your mouth and the floor , you can inhale dust from the carpet . when he saw this , he wanted to invent something get out the dust . finally in 1901 he patented the vacuum , with a mechanical system .
