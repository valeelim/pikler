Sodium chlorate     sodium chlorate is a chemical compound . it is a crystalline solid . it is a strong oxidizing agent . its chemical formula is naclo . it contains sodium and chlorate ions . it is made by electrolysis of a hot sodium chloride solution . it is used as a herbicide . it is toxic because of its oxidizing properties . it is mostly known to be used as non-selective herbicide . related pages . - potassium chlorate - perchlorate - chlorate
