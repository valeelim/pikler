Space Shuttle Discovery     space shuttle discovery ( ov-103 ) is the third space shuttle run by nasa . it has flew more missions than any orbiter . it is named after `` rrs discovery '' . it was retired on 9 march 2011. it has spent one year ( 365 days ) in space . it is now on display at the national air and space museum in washington d.c. space shuttle overview : discovery ( ov-103 )
