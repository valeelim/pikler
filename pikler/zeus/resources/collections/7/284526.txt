Ken Starr     ken starr ( born july 21 , 1946 ) is a u.s. lawyer , a former federal u.s. court of appeals judge and a solicitor general . he was independent counsel ( which was a special office that investigated government officials ) while bill clinton was the president of the united states . starr filed the starr report , which accused president clinton of lying in court about having an affair with an intern at the white house . other websites . - the starr report
