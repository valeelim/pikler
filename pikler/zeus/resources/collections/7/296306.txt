Matt Mead     matthew hansen `` matt '' mead ( born march 11 , 1962 in teton county , wyoming ) is an american politician and is the current 32nd governor of wyoming . he easily won against former chairman of the wyoming democratic party , leslie petersen in the election and became the governor . mead is married to carol l. mintzer and they have 2 children together . other websites . - governor of wyoming - official website of the governor of wyoming - dave for governor - official website of mead for governor
