Sulfur dichloride     sulfur dichloride , also known as sulfur ( ii ) chloride , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is scl . it contains sulfur in its +2 oxidation state . it also contains chloride ions . sulfur dichloride is a red liquid . it has a strong odor . it is a common sulfur chloride . it reacts with water to make hydrochloric acid and sulfur dioxide . when it is old , it might have some chlorine in it . it reacts with fluorides to make sulfur tetrafluoride . it is made by reacting chlorine with sulfur . disulfur dichloride is an intermediate . it is also a common impurity in sulfur dichloride . related pages . - sulfur hexafluoride - disulfur decafluoride
