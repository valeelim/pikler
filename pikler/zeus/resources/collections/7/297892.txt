Air Force Ones (song)    air force ones ( song ) `` air force ones ' '' is a 2002 rap song by american rapper nelly . the song features the hip hop group st. lunatics . it was the fourth single from his second album nellyville . the song was released on november 7 , 2002. the song reached # 3 on the u.s. billboard hot 100 , # 1 on the u.s. billboard hot r & b/hip-hop singles & tracks and # 1 on the u.s. billboard hot rap tracks .
