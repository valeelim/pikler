Alex Rider     alex rider is a series of spy books . it is written by british author anthony horowitz . the series is about a teenage spy named alex rider . the series is aimed at young adults . there are ten books in the series , but short stories and other books have been published too . the novels are published by walker books in the united kingdom . the first novel , `` stormbreaker '' , was published in the united kingdom on 4 september 2000 and was adapted into a movie in 2006. over 10 million books in the alex rider series have been sold worldwide .
