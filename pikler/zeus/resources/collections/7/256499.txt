Viktor Stålberg     viktor stålberg ( born january 17 , 1986 in gothenburg , sweden ) is a professional swedish ice hockey right winger that currently signed to the nashville predators of the national hockey league ( nhl ) . stålberg formerly played in the nhl with the toronto maple leafs for 1 season and the chicago blackhawks for 3 seasons . during his time with the blackhawks , he won the stanley cup with them after they defeated the boston bruins 4 games to 2 in the 2013 stanley cup finals . on july 5 , 2013 , stålberg signed a $ 12 million , 4 year deal with the nashville predators . he has also played for the j20 superelit league team , frölunda hc and played collegiately for the university of vermont catamounts .
