Space Shuttle Endeavour     space shuttle endeavour ( ov-105 ) is the fifth space shuttle run by nasa . it replaced the space shuttle challenger . endeavour was named after captain james cooks 's ship , hms endeavour . it made 25 trips into space , travelling about 123,000,000 miles . it is now on display at the california science center . references . - space shuttle overview : endeavour ( ov-105 ) - `` shuttle orbiter endeavour ( ov-105 ) ''
