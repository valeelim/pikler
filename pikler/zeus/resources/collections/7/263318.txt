Food and Drug Administration     the food and drug administration ( fda or usfda ) is a united states government agency that protects and promotes public health , by regulating food safety , tobacco , prescription drugs , over-the-counter drugs , vaccines , and cosmetics . the fda enforces federal laws along with other agencies , such as the dea . the agency was made in 1906 , and is led by the commissioner of food and drugs , currently dr. margaret a. hamburg . other websites . - fda home page
