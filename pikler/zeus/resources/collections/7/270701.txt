Afrovenator     afrovenator was a theropod dinosaur from the middle jurassic of northern africa . it was a bipedal meat-eating predator with long bladelike teeth . fossils have been found in abaka , niger in the southern sahara desert . a nearly complete `` afrovenator '' was found in 1993. it is one of the most complete dinosaurs found in africa so far . the rocks where it was found were redescribed as `` mid-jurassic '' after the discovery had been announced . it is in a group of theropods which are known as the megalosaurs .
