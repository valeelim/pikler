Holocene extinction     the holocene extinction is , first of all , the extinction of nearly all large mammal species after the end of the ice age . this is attributed to hunting by humans , and climate change . habitat destruction and over-harvesting are two of the primary causes which drive species extinct in more recent times . the fundamental cause of this is the huge increase in human population since the agricultural revolution . forests and natural habitats have been greatly reduced throughout the world , and this has been the cause of many extinctions .
