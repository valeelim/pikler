Hashemite University     the hashemite university ( الجامعة الهاشمية ) is a university in zarqa , jordan . it is named after the jordanian royal family the hashemites and was established by royal decree in 1992. there are about 18,000 students in the university . the university is a public one ( meaning it is owned by the state ) . other websites . - hashemite university home - hashemite university admissions page - colleges of the university publications . - jordan journal of biological sciences - jordan journal of mechanical and industrial engineering - the jordan journal of earth and environmental sciences
