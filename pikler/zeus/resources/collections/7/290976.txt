Slingshot     a slingshot is a kind of projectile weapon which is used to launch small things . it is small and made to be handheld . it is made of a y-shaped body with an elastic band such as rubber tied to the two top ends of the y. the projectile is placed in the band , which is pulled back and let go of . how strong and dangerous the slingshot is depends on how it is made and especially the elastic band . although a slingshot is a type of weapon and a strong one can hurt people , they are not often used in modern war because there are many better weapons . strong slingshots may be used to hunt small animals , or weaker ones may be used as toys . in some places , a slingshot may be illegal because it can be a weapon .
