Beipiaosaurus     beipiaosaurus is a therizinosaur theropod dinosaur . it lived in the lower cretaceous , about 125 million years ago . its fossil was found in the yixian formation near beipiao , liaoning province of china . the theroziosaurs were a group of theropods which became herbivorous . `` beipiaosaurus '' had a toothless beak , cheek teeth , and protofeathers .
