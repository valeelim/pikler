The Great Milenko     the great milenko is the fourth studio album by american horrorcore band insane clown posse . it was released on june 25 , 1997 by hollywood records . the producers of the album were insane clown posse and mike e. clark . the album was the fourth of the `` joker cards '' in the band 's dark carnival series of concept albums . three singles were released from the album . most of the reviews of the album were bad .
