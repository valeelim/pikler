Tellurous acid     tellurous acid is a chemical compound . it is also an acid . its chemical formula is hteo . it contains hydrogen and tellurite ions . it can be made by reacting tellurium dioxide with water . it is not made as easily as telluric acid . it is a weak oxidizing agent and a weak reducing agent . it easily turns back into tellurium dioxide and water . it is a weak acid . it reacts with bases to make tellurites . related pages . - telluric acid - sodium tellurite
