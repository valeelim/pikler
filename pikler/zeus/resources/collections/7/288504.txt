Glen Matlock     glen matlock ( greenford , august 27 1956 ) is the first bassist of the british punk group sex pistols . he left the group in 1977 , because he `` liked the beatles '' , legend being `` thrown out '' . matlock was replaced by john ritchie , also known as sid vicious . in may 2010 it was announced that matlock would be a member of a reunion of the faces , scheduled to play at the vintage at goodwood festival in london in august 2010 .
