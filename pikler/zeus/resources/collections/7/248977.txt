Kangwŏn-do (North Korea)    kangwŏn-do ( north korea ) kangwŏn-do ( 강원도 ) is a province of north korea . its capital is at wŏnsan . before korea was divided in 1945 , kangwŏn and its south korean neighbor gangwon-do ( also spelled `` kangwon-do '' ) was a single province . the `` diamond mountain '' is its highest part .
