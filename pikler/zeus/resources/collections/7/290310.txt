Bird bath     a birdbath is an artificial `` puddle '' or small shallow pond , created with a water-filled basin , for birds to bathe in , cool off , and drink from . a birdbath can be a garden decoration , small reflecting pool , or outdoor sculpture . a birdbath is an attraction for many different species of birds to visit gardens , especially during the summer and drought periods . birdbaths that provide clean and continual water all year makes more birds come . related pages . - birdwatching - drought other websites . - birdbaths and birdwatching - birdbath styles - beautiful stone birdbaths
