Sean Avery     sean christopher avery ( born april 10 , 1980 in pickering , ontario ) is a canadian retired professional ice hockey left winger . he played 12 years in the national hockey league ( nhl ) . he played with the detroit red wings , los angeles kings , new york rangers , and dallas stars . avery was known for his agitating playing style , as well as his controversial behavior both on and off the ice . he has also worked as an intern for vogue , a male model and a restaurateur . avery has been featured on mtv cribs , punk 'd and trl and has also opened a sports bar called , warren 77 in manhattan .
