Swiss Family Robinson     swiss family robinson is a 1960 walt disney movie . it is based on an 1812 book of the same name by johann david wyss . the movie stars john mills , dorothy mcguire , james macarthur , tommy kirk , kevin corcoran , janet munro , and sessue hayakawa as a pirate chief . the movie is about a shipwrecked family building an island home . it was filmed on tobago ( with great difficulties ) and in england . it was released just before christmas 1960. it was a huge box office hit , and is one of the top movie moneymakers of all time .
