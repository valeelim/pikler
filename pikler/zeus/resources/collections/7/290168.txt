Safeway Inc.     safeway , inc. is the third-largest supermarket chain in north america . the company has around 198,000 employees worldwide . in some of the safeway stores , they have fuel stations where they allow customers to use a phone number or club ( loyalty ) card to receive fuel savings . safeway stores also have a safeway atm network . the name `` safeway '' came from a contest that was held in 1925 to develop a name for the store and safeway was chosen . safeway has stores in the united states , canada , united kingdom , australia , west germany , mexico , saudi arabia and kuwait . other websites . - safeway 's official website
