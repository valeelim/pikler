Chicago Tribune     chicago tribune is a major newspaper that serves chicago , illinois . it was founded on june 10 , 1847 by james kelly , john e. wheeler and joseph k. c. forrest . the tribune was also known for running the wrong result in its headline , `` dewey defeats truman '' during the 1948 united states presidential election which it was expected thomas e. dewey would defeat harry s. truman . other websites . - official website
