Sinner (Drowning Pool album)    sinner ( drowning pool album ) sinner is the first album by nu metal band drowning pool . the album is the band 's most popular , reaching platinum only six weeks after it came out . this was thought to be because of the song `` bodies '' , often known as `` let the bodies hit the floor '' , which remains the band 's most well-known song . this was the only album to have lead singer dave williams . while touring in support of `` sinner '' , he died on august 14 , 2002 from heart failure . the album debuted at # 14 on the billboard 200 chart .
