Iron(II) oxide    iron ( ii ) oxide iron ( ii ) oxide is an chemical compound composed of iron in the +2 oxidation state bonded to oxygen atoms . it easily converts to a mixture of iron and iron ( iii ) oxide at room temperature . it is stable though when it is very hot . it is a black powder . if it gets hydrated ( bonded to extra water molecules ) , it turns green . the green chemical , named iron ( ii ) hydroxide , gets oxidized by oxygen to a reddish color of iron ( iii ) oxide .
