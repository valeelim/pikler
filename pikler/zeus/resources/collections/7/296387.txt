Summer Palace     the summer palace is a palace in beijing , china . it is mainly dominated by longevity hill ( 60 meters high ) and the kunming lake . it covers an expanse of 2.9 square kilometers , three quarters of which is water . the palace is a unesco world heritage site . between 1750 and 1764 the qing emperor qianlong created the garden of clear ripples ( summer palace ) , extending the area of the lake and carrying out other improvements based on the hill and its landscape . during the second opium war ( 1856-60 ) the garden and its buildings were destroyed by the allied forces . between 1886 and 1895 it was reconstructed by emperor guangxu and renamed the summer palace , for use by empress dowager cixi . it was damaged in 1900 by the international expeditionary force during the suppression of the boxer rising and 24 years later , it became a public park in 1924. there are many buildings in the summer palace including the cloud-dispelling hall , the temple of buddhist virtue and the sea of wisdom temple .
