Rey, Iran    rey , iran rey , rayy , ray , rhages or rages ( ; ; ) also known as shahr-e-rey ( ) , is the oldest city in the state of tehran , iran . rey today has been added into the greater tehran city . it is linked by the tehran metro to the rest of tehran and has many industries and factories in use . a settlement began here c 6,000 bce as part of the central plateau culture . the settlement was used as a capital by the medes called rhaga . in classical roman geography it was called rhagae . its name dates back to the pre-median . some historians point its building to very old storybook royal family , and some others believe that rey was the seat of a house of zoroastrian leader .
