Nanaimo     nanaimo is a city in british columbia with a population of 78,692. the mayor of nanaimo is john ruttan . nanaimo is located on vancouver island and is the second largest city on vancouver island . nanaimo is ranked 62nd overall in the 100 largest municipalities in canada and is nicknamed the `` bathtub racing capital of the world '' and the `` harbour city '' . other websites . - official city website
