Aluminium oxide     aluminium oxide , also known as aluminum oxide , is a chemical compound made from aluminium and oxygen . its chemical formula is alo . it is a normally white , powdery solid . it does not exist in a particular form . it can dissolve in acids and bases . it is used as an abrasive ( sandpaper is an abrasive ) . it comes in bauxite and corundum . it does not melt easily . it is dissolved in a cryolite melt and electrolyzed to make aluminium . related pages . - aluminium chloride - calcium oxide - corundum
