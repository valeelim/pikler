Japanese Grand Prix     the japanese grand prix is a race the fia formula one world championship calendar . usually , this race is one of the last races of the season . the japanese grand prix has been hosted by both the fuji speedway and the suzuka circuit . fuji speedway is owned by toyota and suzuka circuit is owned by their rival honda . in july 2009 , toyota announced it would not host the race at fuji speedway in 2010 and beyond due to a downturn in the global economy . the first formula 1 japanese grand prix , in , was held at the fuji speedway , west of yokohama . the race was to become famous for the battle between james hunt and niki lauda for the championship . the race was held during monsoon rain conditions . lauda had survived a near-fatal crash at the german grand prix earlier in the season . he withdrew from the japanese grand prix fearing for his safety . hunt finished third , and ended up winning championship by a margin of one point . hunt returned to win the second japanese grand prix . there was a collision between gilles villeneuve and ronnie peterson in the race . villeneuve 's ferrari flew off the track and killed two spectators . the japanese grand prix did not return to formula one for another decade .
