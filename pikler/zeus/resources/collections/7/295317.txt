Randy Johnson     randall david `` randy '' johnson ( born september 10 , 1963 ) was a pitcher in major league baseball ( mlb ) . he pitched for the montreal expos , seattle mariners , houston astros , arizona diamondbacks , new york yankees , and san francisco giants . he was chosen to 10 all star teams , and won five cy young awards . he became a member of the baseball hall of fame in 2015 .
