Freshwater biology     freshwater biology is the study of plants and animals that live in fresh water . this includes rivers , lakes , ponds , marshes and other bodies of water . it is part of the branch of science called limnology . much freshwater biology takes place in man-made places such as sewage treatment works and water treatment works . in these places water is purified using plants and animals . keeping them working as hard as possible helps to make the water as cleaner . in recent times freshwater biologists have been looking at how plants and animals change in the autumn and spring . this is helping us to understand more about the effects of climate change .
