Reign in Blood     reign in blood is the third album and major label debut by the american thrash metal band slayer . released on october 7 , 1986 , `` reign in blood '' was very well received by both critics and fans , and was responsible for bringing slayer to the attention of a mainstream metal audience . `` kerrang ! '' magazine said the record was `` the heaviest album of all time , '' and a breakthrough in thrash metal and speed metal . alongside metallica 's `` master of puppets '' , `` reign in blood '' is considered one of the most influential thrash metal albums of all time .
