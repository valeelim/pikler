Zinc iodide     zinc iodide is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is zni . it contains zinc and iodide ions . zinc iodide is a colorless solid . it dissolves in water . it reacts with bases to make zinc hydroxide . it is a reducing agent . when it is oxidized it makes iodine . it is made by reacting zinc with iodine . it is opaque ( not clear ) to x-rays . it can be used as a stain in microscope slides . related pages . - zinc fluoride - zinc bromide - zinc chloride
