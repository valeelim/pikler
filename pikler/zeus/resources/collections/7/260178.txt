Prescott Burgess     prescott ennis burgess ( born march 6 , 1984 in warren , ohio ) is an american football linebacker . he is currently a free agent in the national football league ( nfl ) . burgess was drafted out of the university of michigan by the baltimore ravens with the 207th pick in the sixth round of the 2007 nfl draft . burgess has played for the baltimore ravens from 2007 to 2009 , the new england patriots in 2009 , and the ravens again from 2009 to 2011. he was released by the ravens in 2011. other websites . - prescott burgess at nfl.com
