The Sin of Madelon Claudet     the sin of madelon claudet is a 1931 american drama movie . the screenplay was adapted from the play `` the lullaby '' by edward knoblock . it tells the story of a woman who was put in jail for the crime she did not commit and later had to turn to theft and prostitution to support her son . in 1932 helen hayes won an academy award for her leading role in it and was named a favorite actress at the first venice film festival .
