Iodine monochloride     iodine monochloride , also known as iodine ( i ) chloride , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is icl . it has iodine and chloride ions . the iodine is in its +1 oxidation state . iodine monochloride is a red or brown solid . it easily melts . it has two forms ; one melts at 27.2°c and the other melts at 13.9°c . it is an oxidizing agent . it can vaporize easily , too to make a brown vapor . it reacts with water . it is the main source of i. it is an interhalogen . it is made by mixing equal moles of iodine and chlorine . more chlorine would make iodine trichloride . it is used to add iodine atoms to some organic compounds . related pages . - iodine trifluoride - iodine pentoxide - iodine pentafluoride
