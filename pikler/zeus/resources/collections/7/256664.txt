For Whom the Bell Tolls (Metallica song)    for whom the bell tolls ( metallica song ) for whom the bell tolls is a 1985 thrash metal song by rock band metallica from their second album ride the lightning . the song 's name comes from ernest hemingway 's 1940 novel of the same name . in pop culture . - the song was featured in the opening credits of the movie `` zombieland '' . - it is played when the nfl 's chicago bears score a touchdown . - the song is used in the trailer for the video game gaia : honor and vengeance .
