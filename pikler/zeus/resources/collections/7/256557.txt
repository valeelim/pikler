John Madden (ice hockey)    john madden ( ice hockey ) john madden ( born may 4 , 1973 in barrie , ontario ) is a canadian retired professional ice hockey player that played a total of 13 seasons in the nhl . during his time in the nhl he played 10 seasons with the new jersey devils where he won the stanley cup 2 times , 1 season with the chicago blackhawks where he also won the stanley cup , 1 season with the minnesota wild and 1 season with the florida panthers . on september 4 , 2012 , madden retired from the nhl . he currently works with the montreal canadiens as a scout and evaluating amateur free agent talent in united states college hockey . awards . - stanley cup ( 2000 ) - frank j. selke trophy ( 2001 ) . - stanley cup ( 2003 ) - stanley cup ( 2010 )
