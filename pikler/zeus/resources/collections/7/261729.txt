Fumigation     fumigation is the making of toxic fumes . it may be done for several reasons . the most common use is to kill insects that hide in various places in a house or other building . there are a few steps to fumigate . first , the house or room is sealed . then the fumigant ( substance making fumes ) is placed in the area . then the fumigant is left to flow through the area . finally , the fumigant is released before the people move back in . the gases are very toxic and the person fumigating has to make sure no one goes into the fumigation area or they might get poisoned . related pages . - pest control - pesticide - herbicide
