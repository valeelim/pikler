Sylvester (Looney Tunes)    sylvester ( looney tunes ) sylvester is a cartoon character from the warner bros. cartoons . sylvester is a tuxedo cat ( bicolor ) and is the main rival of tweety bird . he first appeared in life with feathers on march 24 , 1945. he was created by friz freleng and is currently voiced by joe alaskey . sylvester 's name is a play on the word , `` felis silvestris '' which is a scientific name for the wild cat species although sylvester is a domestic cat . he was voted as # 33 on tv guide 's list of 50 best cartoon characters along with tweety . he has a son named sylvester junior who first appeared in pop 'im pop on 1949. sylvester junior is useually seen with sylvester in the cartoons when sylvester is trying to catch a baby kangaroo named hippety hopper who he often mistakes for a mouse to prove a point to his son .
