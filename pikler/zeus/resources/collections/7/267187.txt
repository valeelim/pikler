Slag     slag is a liquid residue from making iron . it consists of calcium silicate . the silicon dioxide in iron has to be taken away . the calcium oxide reacts with the silicon dioxide solid to make liquid calcium silicate , which is lighter than iron . it is drained out of the blast furnace . slag can be used to make concrete . air can be blown through molten slag to make a fluffy insulation made mineral wool . concrete made with blast furnace slag is almost pure white compared to ordinary concrete which is greyish . related pages . - steel
