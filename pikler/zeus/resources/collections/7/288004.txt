What's My Name?    what 's my name ? `` what 's my name ? '' is a 2010 song from barbadian singer rihanna and canadian rapper drake . this song was released in october 2010 and was praised by the music critics . it was a commercial success , topping the us `` billboard '' hot 100 and becoming her third number-one song during 2010. the song was rihanna 's seventh number-one hit in the us and drake 's first . it also reached number one on the uk singles chart and the top five in canada , ireland , and new zealand . in the video for the song , rihanna and drake are seen walking in a city until they meet in a grocery store . rihanna dances throughout the video , with the two becoming romantic with one another .
