Lenape     the lenape , lenappe , lenapi or lenni lenape ( meaning `` '' the people '' or `` true people '' '' ) are a group of several bands of native american people who share cultural and linguistic traits . they are also known as the delaware indians . they spoke two similar languages known as the delaware languages : `` unami '' and `` munsee '' . the lenape lived in the area called lenapehoking , roughly the area around and between the delaware and lower hudson rivers . these areas are known today as the u.s. states of new jersey , eastern pennsylvania around the delaware and lehigh valleys . it is also the north shore of delaware and much of southeastern new york , mostly the lower hudson valley and upper new york bay . they had several games .
