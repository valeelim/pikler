Masood Farivar     masood farivar ( born 1969 ) is an afghanistan-born writer and journalist and author of confessions of a mullah warrior ( atlantic monthly press , 2009 ) , which describes two teenage years while a member of the anti-soviet mujahideen resistance movement . farivar moved with his family to pakistan in 1983. he obtained a degree from harvard university in 1994 and subsequently worked as a business reporter for dow jones newswires . according his own `` linkedin '' resume , he was as of 2013 , chief of the afghanistan service at voice of america and based in washington , d.c. previously he was with internews network and spent the 2011-2012 academic year as a knight fellow at stanford university .
