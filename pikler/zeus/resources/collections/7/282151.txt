Aalten     aalten is a village and municipality in the eastern netherlands . the former municipalities of bredevoort ( 1818 ) and dinxperlo ( 2005 ) have been merged with aalten . aalten village has 12,900 inhabitants , the municipality has 27.593 inhabitants . aalten , barlo , bredevoort , dale , dinxperlo , haart , heurne , ijzerlo , 't klooster and lintelo . other websites . - official website aalten
