Michael Tarver     tyrone evans ( born march 8 , 1977 in akron , ohio ) is a american professional wrestler who is best known for wrestling with world wrestling entertainment ( wwe ) under the ring name michael tarver . during his time with wwe he was a part of the first season of nxt . he was eliminated along with daniel bryan in the first round . tarver debuted on raw as a part of the professional wrestling stable , the nexus . on june 13 , 2011 , he was released from his wwe contract . he currently wrestles for florida underground wrestling . pro wrestling illustrated ranked tarver # 343 of the top 500 singles wrestlers in the pwi 500 in 2010. tarver 's finishing move is the tarver 's lightning which is a reverse thrown scoop powerslam . other websites . - michael tarver at wwe.com
