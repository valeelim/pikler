Contactless smart card     a contactless smart card is in a card pocket-sized ( usually as big as a credit card ) card with integrated circuits that can process and save data . they can receive information which is processed and delivered through radio . there are two main kinds of contactless smart cards . many people believe contactless smart cards have a read-only rfid inside it . this is not true , but they do have a re-writable smart card microchip inside them . contactless smart cards can be used for identification , authentication , and saving up data .
