Téa Leoni     elizabeth téa pantaleoni ( born february 25 , 1966 ) , better known by her stage name téa leoni , is an american actress . she has starred many movies . these include : `` bad boys '' , `` deep impact '' , `` the family man '' , `` jurassic park iii '' , `` fun with dick and jane '' , `` spanglish '' , `` ghost town '' and `` tower heist '' . leoni separated from her husband , david duchovny , in 2011 .
