Taylor Lautner     taylor daniel lautner ( ; born february 11 1992 ) is an american actor . he is best known for playing jacob black in the `` twilight saga '' movie series , though he has also been in family movies and done voiceover work for animated cartoons . lautner was born in grand rapids , michigan . his parents are deborah , who works for a software development company , and daniel lautner , a commercial airline pilot . lautner was raised as a roman catholic in hudsonville , michigan . he went to jamestown elementary school until the age of eleven , when the family moved to the santa clarita area . lautner is of mostly dutch , french , and german ancestry , and claims some native american ancestry through his mother . he has a younger sister , makena . he has karate since the age of six . filmography : 1. shadow fury ( 2001 ) . 1. summerland ( tv series ) : to thine self be true ( 2004 ) 1. the bernie mac show ( tv series ) bernie mac rope a dope ( 2003 ) and being bernie mac ( 2004 ) 1. my wife & kids ( tv series ) class reunion ( 2004 )
