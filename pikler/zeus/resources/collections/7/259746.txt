M. Night Shyamalan     manoj nelliyattu shyamalan ( ; മനോജ്‌ നെല്ലിയാട്ടു ശ്യാമളന്‍ , mitur ben es-derti , latin english : `` maṉōj nelliyāṯṯu ṣiyāmaḷaṉ '' , born 6 august 1970 ) , known professionally as m. night shyamalan , is an indian-american filmmaker and screenwriter who resides and works primarily in the united states , known for making movies with contemporary supernatural plots that climax with a twist ending including `` the sixth sense '' ( 1999 ) , `` unbreakable '' ( 2000 ) , `` signs '' ( 2002 ) , `` the village '' ( 2004 ) , `` lady in the water '' ( 2006 ) , `` the happening '' ( 2008 ) and `` the last airbender '' ( 2010 ) . he is also known for filming his movies ( and staging his plots ) in and around philadelphia , pennsylvania , where he was raised . shyamalan was born in a hindu household in mahé , puducherry , india . his father , nelliate c. shyamalan is a malayali traditional physician ( vaidyar ) from mahé . his mother , jayalakshmi is tamil and is an obstetrician and gynecologist by profession . movies . other projects . - indiana jones and the kingdom of the crystal skull
