Derrick Rose     derrick martell rose ( born october 4 , 1988 in chicago , illinois ) is an american basketball player . he currently plays for the new york knicks . he plays at the position of point guard . he attended college at university of memphis where he played basketball with the memphis tigers . rose was drafted out of college by the chicago bulls with the 1st overall pick in the 1st round of the 2008 nba draft . rose was named the 2011 nba most valuable player . he is the youngest person to win an nba mvp . on october 9 , 2012 , rose 's girlfriend , mieka reese , gave birth to their son , derrick rose , jr. on june 22 , 2016 , rose was traded to the new york knicks
