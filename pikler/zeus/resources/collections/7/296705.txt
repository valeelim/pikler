Reto Von Arx     reto von arx ( born 13 september , 1976 in egerkingen , switzerland ) is a swiss professional ice hockey left winger that currently plays for the hc davos of the swiss national league a. von arx also played in the nhl for 1 season with the chicago blackhawks and also for their farm team at the time , the norfolk admirals . he was drafted by the chicago blackhawks with the 271st overall pick in the 2000 nhl draft . other websites . - reto von arx 's nhl profile
