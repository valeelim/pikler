Fianna Fáil     fianna fáil is an irish political party . it was founded in 1926 by éamon devalera . its leader is michéal martin . it is a faction of the sinn fein , another irish political party alongside the fine gael . its name is translated from irish as 'soldiers of destiny ' but literally means 'warriors of fál ' ( fál was the name of ireland in legends ) . fianna fáil does not support the anglo-irish treaty , unlike the fine gael . there have been eight leaders of the fianna fáil since its creation in 1926. they are : 1. éamon de valera , ( 1926 - 1959 ) 1. seán lemass , ( 1959 - 1966 ) 1. jack lynch , ( 1966 - 1979 ) 1. charles haughey , ( 1979 - 1992 ) 1. albert reynolds , ( 1992 - 1994 ) 1. bertie ahern , ( 1994 - 2008 ) 1. brian cowen , ( 2008 - 2011 ) 1. micháel martin ( 2011 onwards ) the youth wing of fianna fáil is the ógra fianna fáil , which was created in 1975 to get younger people to vote and support the party . other websites . - official party website - the official youth wing 'ógra fianna fáil 's ' website
