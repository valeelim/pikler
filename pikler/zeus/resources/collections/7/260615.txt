Nicktoons Network     nicktoons is an american digital cable and satellite television network owned by viacom . it is a sister service of the nickelodeon cable channel . the network broadcasts nickelodeon 's animated series , nicktoons , along with other original animated series and foreign animation from other nickelodeon networks overseas 24 hours a day . the slogan for the network is `` big stars , animated .
