Tube worm     a tube worm is a worm-like sessile invertebrate which anchors its tail to an underwater surface . it creates a mineral tube around its body , and can withdraw the body into it . tube worms are found in these groups : - the phylum annelida ( segmented worms ) - the class polychaetea ( bristle worms ) - the order canalipalpata ( bristle-footed annelids or fan-head worms ) - the family siboglinidae ( beard worms ) - the genus lamellibrachia - the species `` riftia pachyptila '' ( giant tube worm ) - the family serpulidae - the family sabellidae ( feather duster worms ) - the phylum phoronida ( horseshoe worms ) - the order microconchida ( extinct tubeworms )
