University of Denver     university of denver is a private university in denver , colorado . the university has about 11,644 students . it was founded in 1864. the current chancellor of the university is robert coombe . the athletics teams of the university are called the pioneers . they have 17 varsity teams , most of which compete in the summit league . some pioneers teams play in other conferences . the men 's ice hockey team plays in the national collegiate hockey conference . the men 's and women 's lacrosse teams play in the big east conference . the skiing team , made up of both men and women , competes in the rocky mountain intercollegiate ski association . the women 's gymnastics team is a member of the big 12 conference . other websites . - university of denver 's official website - denver pioneers ' official athletics website
