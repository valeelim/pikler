The Medical Journal of Australia     the medical journal of australia is a peer-reviewed medical journal . it is the official journal of the australian medical association and began in 1914. it is published by the australasian medical publishing company proprietary limited ( ampco ) . the current ( 2008 ) editor-in-chief is martin van der weyden . the journal publishes editorials , articles , case reports , and letters . the full text of every issue since january 2002 is available on their website . related pages . - `` british medical journal ''
