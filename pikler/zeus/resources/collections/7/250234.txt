Order of Australia     the order of australia was created by elizabeth ii , queen of australia on 14 february 1975. it was created to show the very best people of australia . the award is given to notable australian citizens who make big changes to australia or internationally with their work . sometimes the award is given to people who are not citizens of australia . the order has many groups . the awarded person will join one of these groups : - knight or dame ( ak or ad ) - companion ( ac ) - officer ( ao ) - member ( am ) - medal of the order of australia ( oam )
