Horace Andy     horace andy ( born horace hinds , february 19 , 1951 , kingston , jamaica ) , is a roots reggae songwriter and singer , known for his distinctive vocals and hit songs such as `` government land '' , `` angel '' , `` five man army '' and a cover version of `` ai n't no sunshine '' . in recent years he has mostly worked as a guest singer for trip hop band massive attack .
