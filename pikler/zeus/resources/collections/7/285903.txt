Steaua Bucureşti    f.c . steaua bucureşti f.c . steaua bucureşti is a football team from bucharest , romania . it was founded on june 7 , 1947 as `` asa bucureşti '' ( `` asa bucharest '' ) . it has won the most championships in romania , 23 , and it is the only team from romania to win a uefa champions league title . its chairman is george becali ( usually `` gigi '' becali ) . other websites . - official website - fans site
