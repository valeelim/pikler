Trace element     a trace element is present in only a small amount . the amount depends on context . - in analytical chemistry , it is an element in a sample that has an average concentration of less than 100 parts per million ( ppm ) measured in atomic count , or less than 100 micrograms per gram . - in biochemistry , it is one that is needed in minute quantities for the proper growth , development , and physiology of the organism . in biochemistry , a trace element is also referred to as a `` micronutrient '' . see cofactor . in geology , its concentration is less than 1000 ppm or 0.1 % of a rock 's composition . the term is used mainly in igneous petrology .
