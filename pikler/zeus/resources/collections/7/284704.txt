Hunter Cantwell     hunter cantwell ( born december 30 , 1985 in chattanooga , tennessee ) is a retired american football quarterback who played for on the practice squad for the carolina panthers and the baltimore ravens of the national football league ( nfl ) . cantwell played college football at the university of louisville . he was undrafted and started his nfl career in 2009 and ended his nfl career in 2011. he is currently the quarterbacks coach for campbellsville university . other websites . - hunter cantwell at carolinapanthers.com
