Spiderbait     spiderbait is an australian rock band who have had two top ten albums and three albums reach the australian top 40. their song `` buy me a pony '' was a defining song of australian rock in the 90 's and was # 1 on the triple j hottest 100 for 1996. the group had success with their 2004 # 1 australian hit cover of `` black betty '' . other websites . - spiderbait home page - howlspace spiderbait page - spiderbait at the dwarf
