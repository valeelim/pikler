Phosphorus acid     there are several phosphorus acids . they are acids that have phosphorus in them . three of them are common . - hypophosphorous acid , phosphorus in oxidation state of +1 , salts are called hypophosphites , reducing agent - phosphorous acid , phosphorus in oxidation state of +3 , salts are called phosphites , reducing agent - phosphoric acid , most common , phosphorus in oxidation state of +5 , salts are called phosphates
