Ford Model A (1927–1931)    ford model a ( 1927–1931 ) the ford model a was a car made by the ford motor company . it had a four-cylinder engine . it replaced the ford model t , and was replaced by the ford model b and ford model 18. though it was only made for five years , 4,849,340 model as were made and sold . the ford model a was made from 1928 through 1931. in 1932 ford made the model b which had a fuel pump attached to the motor .
