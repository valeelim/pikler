Silver bromide     silver bromide is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is agbr . it contains silver in its +1 oxidation state . it also contains bromide ions . it is a light yellow solid . it does not dissolve in water . it darkens when light shines on it . the darkening is because it turns into silver metal and bromine gas . it reacts with ammonia to dissolve . it is made by reacting a bromide such as potassium bromide or sodium bromide with silver nitrate . it is used in photographic film . many science studies are on this . scientists are figuring out how it changes color when light shines on it . related pages . - silver chloride - silver fluoride - silver iodide
