Paul (movie)    paul ( movie ) paul is a 2011 british-american science fiction comedy movie . it was produced by nira park , tim bevan and eric fellner . it was directed by greg mottola . the movie was released on february 14 , 2011 in the united kingdom and was released in the united states on march 18 , 2011. it is set in nevada and was filmed in new mexico . the movie received positive reviews with a 66 % on rotten tomatoes which is fresh . cast . - simon pegg as graeme willy - nick frost as clive gollings - seth rogen as the voice of paul - jason bateman as special agent lorenzo zoil - kristen wiig as ruth - bill hader as agent haggard - gregg turkington as neil hamburger - jane lynch as pat stevenson - sigourney weaver as `` the big guy '' - blythe danner as tara walton - mia stallard as young tara - joe lo truglio as o'reilly - john carroll lynch as moses buggs - david koechner as gus - jesse plemons as jake - jeffrey tambor as adam shadowchild - luke jackson as ford - paula labaredas as princess leia - chris bentley as highway patrolman ( `` uncredited '' )
