Objectivism     objectivism may mean : - objectivity ( philosophy ) : a philosophical opinion or doctrine . it holds that reality exists outside ( independent of ) the human mind . - moral objectivism : that some acts are right or wrong in themselves , and are not matters of opinion . - objectivism ( ayn rand ) . the objectivist philosophy created by novelist ayn rand , which endorses productive achievement and logical reasoning . - the objectivist poets , a group of modernist writers who emerged in the 1930s .
