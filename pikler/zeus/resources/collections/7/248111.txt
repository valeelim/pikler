Pat Patterson     pierre clermont ( born january 19 , 1941 ) is a canadian retired professional wrestler . he is better known by the ring name pat patterson . he currently works as a creative consultant for the wwe . his career highlights are 2 matches against sgt . slaughter at madison square garden in 1981. the rematch was called an `` alley fight '' ( with no referee ) and remains the wildest and one of the greatest matches in wrestling history . patterson was the very first man to hold the intercontinental heavyweight championship . patterson was inducted into the wwe hall of fame in 1996. patterson invented the wwe royal rumble , which is one of the most popular type of matches and is a hit with fans every year since the first one in 1988. patterson is openly gay . other websites . - wwe profile
