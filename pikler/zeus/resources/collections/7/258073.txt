Chromium(III) oxide    chromium ( iii ) oxide chromium ( iii ) oxide is a dark green chemical that has a formula of cro . it is composed of chromium in its +3 oxidation state and oxide ions . it can react with other metal oxides in basic conditions to make chromates . it is made from chromium ore and is part of the process for making chromium metal . it was used as a pigment . it dissolves in acid to produce chromium ( iii ) salts , such as chromium ( iii ) chloride . stainless steel 's corrosion resistance is based on this compound . a thin layer of this compound forms on the surface of the stainless steel . it prevents the rest of the steel from oxidizing . related pages . - chromium - chromium ( iii ) chloride - chromate - dichromate
