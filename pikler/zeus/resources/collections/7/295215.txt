Sineu     `` sineu '' is a mountainous municipality in central majorca , one of the balearic islands , spain . sineu is situated in the middle of mallorca´s island . sineu has a population of more than 3000 residents . sineu is the capital of spring fairs . sineu fair is the oldest on the island and has more visitors than any other fair in mallorca.sineu ’ s weekly market takes place on wednesdays . it is quite an event . it is the only livestock market of mallorca.nowadays the market is part of the visit to sineu and many visitors come to see the market .
