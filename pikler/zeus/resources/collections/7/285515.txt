James Madison University     james madison university is a public university in harrisonburg , virginia . the university has about 18,971 students . it was founded in 1908. the current president of the university is dr. linwood h. rose . the athletics teams of the university are called the dukes . they have 7 varsity teams and they compete in the colonial athletic association . other websites . - james madison university 's official website - james madison dukes ' official athletics website
