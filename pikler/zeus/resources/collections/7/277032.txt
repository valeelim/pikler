Bedrock Anthem     `` bedrock anthem '' is a song by `` weird al '' yankovic . it is a parody of `` under the bridge '' and `` give it away '' by the red hot chili peppers . it is about the narrator who desires to live in bedrock , the setting of the cartoon `` the flintstones '' . it states several reasons referring to the show . it was released on the 1993 album `` alpalooza '' .
