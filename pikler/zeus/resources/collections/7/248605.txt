Isle Royale National Park     isle royale national park is a wildlife reserve on more than 100 islands in northwest lake superior , in northern michigan in the united states . this national park was opened in 1940. the wildlife includes moose , wolves and many kinds of birds . one of the islands is called isle royale . it has an area of 229 square miles . native americans mined copper on this island in pre-columbian times ( before 1492 ) . the mines can still be seen here . isle royale national park - national park service website
