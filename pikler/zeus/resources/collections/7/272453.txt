Drew Doughty     drew doughty ( born december 8 , 1989 ) is a canadian professional ice hockey defenceman . he plays for the los angeles kings of the national hockey league ( nhl ) . he was selected 2nd overall by the kings in the 2008 nhl entry draft . he plays on team canada in the olympics where he won a gold medal in the 2010 winter olympics . doughty was named to the all-rookie team in the 2008-09 nhl season . he won the stanley cup with the los angeles kings in 2012 .
