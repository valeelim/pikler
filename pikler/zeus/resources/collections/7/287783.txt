Cubeb     cubeb , or tailed pepper , is a plant in genus `` piper '' is used for its fruit and essential oil . it is usually found in java , and so it is sometimes called java pepper . the fruits are gathered before they are ripe , and dried carefully . the seed of the plant is white and hard oily . in europe , cubeb was one of the valuable spices during the middle ages . it was used in sauces . an old recipe includes cubeb in making `` sauce sarcenes '' , which consists of almond milk and a few spices . as a good-smelling snack , cubeb was often candied and eaten .
