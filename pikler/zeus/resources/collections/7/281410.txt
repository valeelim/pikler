Pitfall!    pitfall ! pitfall ! is a 1982 platforming video game made by activision for atari 2600 , atari 5200 , atari 8-bit , colecovision , commodore 64 , intellivision and the sega sg-1000 . the game was a best seller for the atari 2600 , with over 4 million copies sold . in this game , the player has to move a character who is known as harry through a maze-like jungle where he has to attempt to recover 32 treasures in a 20-minute time period . the player has to avoid many hazards including pits , quicksand , rolling logs , fire , rattlesnakes , scorpions , and crocodiles .
