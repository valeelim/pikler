Nickel(II) oxide    nickel ( ii ) oxide nickel ( ii ) oxide , also known as nickelous oxide , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is nio . it contains nickel in its +2 oxidation state . it also contains oxide ions . nickel ( ii ) oxide is a light green solid . it does not dissolve in water . sometimes it is gray because all of the nickel is not in the +2 oxidation state . it reacts with acids to make nickel ( ii ) salts . for example , it reacts with hydrochloric acid to make nickel ( ii ) chloride . it does not react with bases easily . nickel ( ii ) oxide can be made by heating nickel powder in air at . this normally makes a black nickel oxide . it also can be made by reacting nickel with water at . the simplest way to make it is to heat nickel ( ii ) hydroxide , nickel ( ii ) carbonate , or nickel ( ii ) nitrate . it is used to make alloys . it is also used in ceramics . it is used to make other nickel compounds . nickel ( ii ) oxide can irritate skin and make problems when breathed in . related pages . - nickel ( ii ) sulfate
