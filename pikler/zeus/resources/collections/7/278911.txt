Droseraceae     droseraceae is a family of flowering plants . the family is also known under its common name , the sundew family . it consists of carnivorous plants : besides the sundews , the genus `` drosera '' , it also contains the even more famous venus flytrap ( `` dionaea muscipula '' ) . the sundews produce sticky substances on their leaves that traps prey , and the venus fly trap has leaves that form traps that close when disturbed .
