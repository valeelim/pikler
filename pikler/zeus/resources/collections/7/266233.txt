Prince of Persia     prince of persia is the title of a series of video games and a movie that follow the adventures of a persian prince . they were created by jordan menchner . prince of persia has been developed and published by several different companies . prince of persia and prince of persia 2 : the shadow and the flame , were developed by brøderbund . later ubisoft took over creating the development starting with the series prince of persia : the sands of time . the game platform has developed over the years from 2d animations to 3d worlds . the movie `` prince of persia : the sands of time '' was released by disney in 2010. games . - `` prince of persia '' created by brøderbund . - `` prince of persia 2 : the shadow and the flame '' created by brøderbund . - `` prince of persia : the sands of time '' created by ubisoft . - `` prince of persia : warrior within '' created by ubisoft . - `` prince of persia : the two thrones '' created by ubisoft . - `` prince of persia : the forgotten sands '' created by ubisoft .
