L'Étoile du Nord    l'étoile du nord l'étoile du nord is a french phrase meaning `` the star of the north '' . it is the motto of the u.s. state of minnesota . it was chosen by the state 's first governor , henry hastings sibley , and was adopted in 1861 , three years after minnesota became a state . because of this motto , one of minnesota 's nicknames is `` the north star state '' . the minnesota north stars chose the english translation for their name .
