Private equity     private equity is investment in shares outside a stock exchange . investors , often from institutions like funds , give a company money , and in turn buy part of that company . the most common types of private equity are : leveraged buyouts , venture capital , growth capital , distressed investments and mezzanine capital . in leveraged buyouts , investors buy the majority control of a mature company . in venture capital or growth capital investment , investors give money to start-up companies .
