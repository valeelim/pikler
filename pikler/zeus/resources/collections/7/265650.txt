Deekshabhoomi     deekshabhoomi is a historical place in india where about 380,000 people were converted from hinduism to buddhism at a single moment on october 14 , 1956. these people were people of lower classes who converted under the leadership of dr. babasaheb ambedkar . ambedkar felt that their human rights could only be protected as bhuddists . deekshabhoomi is in the city of nagpur , maharashtra in central india . it is an important tourist attraction .
