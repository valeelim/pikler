The Prodigy     the prodigy are an english electronic dance music group formed by liam howlett in 1990 in braintree , essex . along with fatboy slim , the chemical brothers and the crystal method , as well as other acts , members of the prodigy have been said as founders of the big beat genre which achieved mainstream popularity in the 1990s and 2000s . they have sold over 25 million records worldwide . they named the band after the synthesizer , moog prodigy .
