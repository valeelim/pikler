Demonic possession     demonic possession is a term used to describe a situation when a demon ( a devil ) controls a human being . in many cultures and religions people often used to believe that a devil could enter the body of a person and control them . they often thought this was happening when a person seemed to be mad , or when they had a fit , or when they fainted . related pages . - demon - demonology - umbrella ( song ) , a song that has been claimed to be about demonic possession .
