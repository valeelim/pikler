Ligand     a ligand is a biochemical substance . its function is to bind to a biological molecule , and make the molecule perform a function . ligand binding alters the shape of the receptor protein . this changes the protein 's behaviour . in a narrower sense , it is a signal triggering molecule , binding to a site on a target protein . the binding occurs by intermolecular forces , such as ionic bonds , hydrogen bonds and van der waals forces . the docking ( association ) is usually reversible ( dissociation ) . ligands include substrates , inhibitors , activators , and neurotransmitters . the tendency or strength of binding is called affinity . related pages . - signal transduction - cytokine - agonist - receptor antagonist
