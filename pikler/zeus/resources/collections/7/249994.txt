National Park Service     the national park service is part of the united states department of the interior . the national park service looks after many natural landscapes and historic and archaeological places and buildings . the office opened in 1916. the united states secretary of the interior a director to head the office in washington , d.c. there are more than 400 areas in the national park system . of these , 59 are national parks . the service also keeps the national register of historic places . other websites . - national park service
