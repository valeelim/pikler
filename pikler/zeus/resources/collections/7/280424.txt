Eastern wallaroo     the eastern wallaroo , `` macropus robustus '' , sometimes called the , common wallaroo , the red wallaroo , or just wallaroo lives on the sides of the great dividing range . this runs for more than 3,000 km along the eastern and south-eastern coast of australia . there are four subspecies of eastern wallaroo : - the eastern wallaroo found across australia - the euro , also widespread . - one found only on barrow island , `` macropus robustus isabellinus '' . - one found only in the kimberley area of western australia .
