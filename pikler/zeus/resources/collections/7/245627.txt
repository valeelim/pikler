Air India Express Flight 812     air india express flight ix-812 was a passenger flight of air india from dubai international airport that crashed on 22 may 2010 at mangalore international airport . the time of the crash was utc+5:30 local time . the airplane went beyond the area of the runway and crashed . the accident killed about 160 people . the aircraft was a boeing 737-800. the first flight was on 20 december 2007 .
