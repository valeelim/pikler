Negro spiritual     - `` for the religious meaning of `` spiritual '' see spirituality '' spirituals ( or negro spirituals ) are the songs which were sung by the black slaves in the united states in the 18th and 19th centuries . the words to many negro spirituals have christian themes . this is because black slaves in the united states turned to religion , as a way to cope with the pain of slavery . negro spirituals combine traditional west african musical style with the style of christian hymns from europe . related pages . - gospel music
