Barred owl     the barred owl is a large owl commonly found in the eastern united states . the barred owl grow 43 to 61 centimeters ( 17 to 21 in ) with a wingspan of 102 to 127 centimeters ( 40 to 50 in ) at full length . the average male weigh 630 grams ( 22 oz ) and female weigh 800 grams ( 28 oz ) . it has a round head with dark brown eyes while most owls in the eastern united states have yellow eyes . the barred owl lives about 10 to 12 years in the wild . the barred owl lives in the woods across the eastern united states , southern canada and alaska . they sometimes migrate to central america in the winter . the great horned owl is the only predator of the barred owl . the barred owl makes nests on tree limbs and are usually near ponds or lakes . they may use nests that have been left by other animals . they can lay 1 to 5 eggs . eggs hatch in 28 days and the young begin to fly 42 days later . the barred owl eats rabbits , squirrels , bats , snakes , and small insects . they are able to find food from a far distance from its great hearing sense . they can also hunt fish .
