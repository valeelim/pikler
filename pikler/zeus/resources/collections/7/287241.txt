Spyro the Dragon (character)    spyro the dragon ( character ) spyro the dragon is a fictional character from the video game series with the same name , where he is the main hero . he is a purple dragon who goes on missions to help protect his home world which is the dragon realms . he is usually followed by a yellow dragonfly named sparx and in the earlier games sparx would be spyro 's health bar . his main attack in the games are ramming enemies with his horns . he also has 5 breath abilities : fire , water , bubbles , electricity and ice . other websites . - spyro the dragon on giant bomb
