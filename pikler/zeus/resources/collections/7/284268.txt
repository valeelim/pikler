Alcides Escobar     alicedes escobar ( born december 16 , 1986 in la sabana , venezuela ) is a venezuelan baseball player . he currently plays as an infielder for the kansas city royals of major league baseball ( mlb ) . escobar formerly played for the milwaukee brewers from 2008-2010 before being traded along with lorenzo cain , jeremy jeffress , jake odorizzi for zack greinke and yuniesky betancourt . other websites . - alcides escobar at mlb.com
