Nathanael Greene     nathanael greene ( august 7 , 1742-june 19 , 1786 ) was a general in the continental army during the american revolution . he was from rhode island and was a quaker . before the war , he was a blacksmith and a delegate to the rhode island general assembly . during the war , he commanded a fort in boston and a part of washington 's army during the new york , new jersey and philadelphia campaigns . late in the war , greene commanded an army in the south . after the war , he moved to georgia , where he died of sunstroke .
