Vitaly Petrov     vitaly aleksandrovich petrov ( born september 8 , 1984 in vyborg ) is a russian formula one driver for the renault f1 team . he is known as `` vyborg rocket '' in russia . by racing in the bahrain grand prix , he is the first russian driver to take part in the formula one world championship . he currently resides in valencia , spain . season in progress . season in progress . other websites . - vitalypetrov.com - официальный виталий петров сайт - vitaly petrov career statistics at driver database
