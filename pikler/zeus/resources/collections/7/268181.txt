Lithium nitrate     lithium nitrate is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is lino . it contains lithium and nitrate ions . lithium nitrate is a colorless solid . it makes a red flame when heated . it is an oxidizing agent . it breaks down when heated into lithium oxide , nitrogen dioxide , and oxygen . this metal can be made by reacting lithium , lithium hydroxide , lithium oxide , or lithium carbonate with nitric acid . lithium nitrate is used in fireworks to make a red color . it is also used in flares . related pages . - lithium nitride
