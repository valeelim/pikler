Zinc fluoride     zinc fluoride is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is znf . it contains zinc and fluoride ions . zinc fluoride is a colorless solid . it only dissolves a little in water . it reacts with bases to make zinc hydroxide . zinc fluoride is made by reacting zinc chloride with a fluoride . it can also be made by reacting zinc with fluorine . it also can be made by reacting zinc with hydrofluoric acid to make hydrogen and zinc fluoride . related pages . - zinc bromide - zinc iodide
