A Matter of Life and Death     a matter of life and death is an album by iron maiden . it is their fourteenth album and went on sale on 5 september 2006. the album talks a lot about war and death , as well as other parts of life . it was produced by kevin shirley . it is a popular album , and sold within the top ten most popular album in several countries when it was released . it was the first iron maiden album to do this in the united states . musicians . - bruce dickinson - vocals - janick gers - guitar - steve harris - bass guitar - nicko mcbrain - percussion - dave murray - guitar - adrian smith - guitar track listing . - bonus track ( itunes store edition )
