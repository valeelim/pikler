Neve Campbell     neve adrianne campbell ( born october 3 , 1973 ) is a canadian actress . she was born in guelph , ontario and is 5'7 '' ( 1.70 m ) tall . she became famous after playing one of the main roles in 1990s television series `` party of five '' . she has had leading roles in several hollywood films , including scream 1 - 4 and the lion king 2 : simba 's pride .
