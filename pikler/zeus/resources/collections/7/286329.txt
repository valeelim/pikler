Beyti (meat dish)    beyti ( meat dish ) the beyti is a type of turkish meat dish . during a visit to switzerland in 1961 , beyti güler was inspired by the renowned butcher möller 's way of preparing meat . when he returned home , he introduced a dish consisting of roasted lamb fillets wrapped in strips of lamb cutlet fat . it came to be named after him when the kebap 's fame spread all over the world .
