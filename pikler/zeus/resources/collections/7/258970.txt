Universal design     universal design is a design of any products and informations that all the persons can use , even the person may be a woman or girl , of old age , or with disability of any kind . the principle of universal design is to be simple , and easy to accesse to any public institutions and informations . it also include the useful information technology and internet . this concept is also uesd in the convention on the rights of persons with disabilities as a condition of the common human rights . related pages . - accessibility - convention on the rights of persons with disabilities
