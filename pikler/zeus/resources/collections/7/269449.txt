Exorcism     exorcism is the practice of evicting demons or other spiritual beings . this practice is quite ancient and part of the belief system of many cultures and religions . there are different types of exorcisms . exorcisms can be performed on people , animals and places . these rituals are done by people called `` exorcists '' . exorcisms are usually done for religious reasons . the reasons vary , and the exorcisms can be different based on religions and which exorcists does them .
