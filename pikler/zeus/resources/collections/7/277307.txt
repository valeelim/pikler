Kratos     kratos is a minor ancient greek god or demon . his name comes from the greek word for `` power '' or `` strength '' . he was a guardian of the throne of zeus . kratos was the second son of pallus and styx , and also the brother of nike , zelus , and bia . he was one of the few people who dragged prometheus to the rock where he was chained . his name is used as part of other words about power , including democracy , theocracy , plutocracy , aristocracy and dictator . this kratos is not to be confused with the protagonist of the video game `` god of war . '' that kratos is a spartan warrior and son of zeus , who is enslaved by ares , the greek god of war . over the course of the original god of war game , kratos escapes bondage and becomes the new god of war by slaying ares . however , that title is stripped from kratos by zeus at the beginning of god of war ii .
