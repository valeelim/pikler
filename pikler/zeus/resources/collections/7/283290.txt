Winton, Victoria    winton , victoria winton is a small town nine kilometres north east of benalla , victoria . about 2,800 people live in the town and the other small towns in the area . it is home to the winton motor raceway , a three kilometre motor racing track . winton was first settled in 1853 , and the town was surveyed in 1857. the hume highway from melbourne to sydney passed through the town , but the hume freeway bypassed winton in 1987 .
