Barbara Walters     barbara walters ( born september 25 , 1929 ) is an author and broadcast journalist . walters was the news anchor at nbc 's `` today '' . for 25 years walters also hosted the abc show `` 20/20 '' . she co-hosted the morning television show `` the view '' from 1997 to 2014. walter 's has interviewed every u.s. president since richard nixon . walters has received 3 daytime emmy awards . she has received lifetime achievement awards for news and documentary and daytime programs . she was inducted into the television hall of fame in 1989. on june 15 , 2007 , walters received a star on the hollywood walk of fame . in 1992 walters received a courage in journalism award for reporting in dangerous areas . other websites . - barbara walters archive of american television interview - barbara walters video produced by `` ''
