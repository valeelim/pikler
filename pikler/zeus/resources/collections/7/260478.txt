Hypophosphite     hypophosphite , also known as phosphonate , is an ion . its chemical formula is hpo . it contains phosphorus in its +1 oxidation state . it is made by disproportionation of phosphorus in warm alkali . phosphine is also produced . it is a strong reducing agent . chemical compounds that contain the hypophosphite ions are called hypophosphites . they are poisonous . hypophosphites are salts of hypophosphorous acid . related pages . - phosphite - phosphate - sodium hypophosphite
