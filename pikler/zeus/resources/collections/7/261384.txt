Hydrogen sulfate     hydrogen sulfate , also known as bisulfate , is an ion . its chemical formula is hso . it is formed as part of sulphuric acid , hso . chemical compounds containing this ion are known as bisulfates or hydrogen sulfates . an example would be sodium bisulfate . hydrogen sulfates are acidic . they can be used as a weaker form of acid than sulfuric acid . it is a salt of sulfuric acid . in these compounds , sulfuric acid is deprotonated once . in the sulfate ion , it is deprotonated twice . related pages . - sulfur - sulfite - sulfurous acid
