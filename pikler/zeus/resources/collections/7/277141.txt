Lamborghini Gallardo     the lamborghini gallardo is a sportscar that has been built by lamborghini since 2003 and is lamborghini 's most-produced model to date . the gallardo is named after a type of fighting bull . later , at the geneva motor show 2014 , the gallardo was replaced by the lamborghini huracán , which is set to release in spring 2014. there are also certain police car models of the gallardo for certain countries like italy and england . the gallardo 's superior model is the lamborghini murciélago . the lamborghini v10 engine was so well made that audi took the engine and put the engine into their sportscar the audi r8 . other websites . - http : //www.lamborghini.com/en/masterpieces/gallardo/
