Kaihin-Makuhari Station     kaihin-makuhari station ( 海浜幕張駅 , かいひんまくはりえき ) is a railway station of east japan railway company ( jr east ) in mihama-ku , chiba city , chiba prefecture , japan . this is the nearest station to makuhari messe , chiba marine stadium , and aeon headquarters . adjacent stations . - jr east see also . - jr east station information - kaihimmakuhari station bus terminal map - keisei bus makuhari new city route map
