3 Doors Down     3 doors down is an american alternative rock band . they formed in 1994 in escatawpa , mississippi . the members of the group are brad arnold , the singer , matt roberts , who plays the lead guitar and backing vocals , chris henderson , who plays the rhythm guitar , todd harrell who plays the bass guitar and greg upchurch , who plays the drums . discography . studio albums . - 2000 : `` the better life '' - 2002 : `` away from the sun '' - 2005 : `` seventeen days '' - 2008 : `` 3 doors down '' - 2011 : `` time of my life '' other websites . - 3 doors down 's official website
