9x19mm Parabellum     a 9x19mm parabellum is a nine millimeter wide bullet . it is usually used in handguns or submachine guns . the nine millimeter is often referred to as a `` nine '' , `` 9mm '' , or `` 9x19 '' . it is one of the most widely used bullets in history originating with the 9x19 luger , invented in germany . many guns fire the 9mm , most notably the glock 18. it is the standaard handgun bullet for all nato countries .
