Classical liberalism     classical liberalism is a type of liberalism . it is a belief in economics and a political ideology . it emphasizes civil and economic freedom . classical liberals believe in a small government , and believe that people will be ruled mostly by natural law . they believe that people have inalienable rights . they also believe in a free market and property rights . notable people who developed classical liberalism during the 18th and 19th century are adam smith , thomas malthus and thomas jefferson . more recently , in the mid-20th century , ludwig von mises , friedrich hayek and milton friedman were in favor . many of the beliefs of classical liberalism are similar to those of libertarianism .
