( what 's the story ) morning glory ? is the second album by the rock band oasis . it was released in october of 1995. the album was oasis ' highest selling album , charting at number one in the uk ( staying there for 10 weeks ) and number four in the u.s. the album sold 346,000 copies in its first week in the united kingdom and has sold around 23 million copies worldwide , but several sources say that it has sold over 30 million . it is the third biggest-selling album in uk chart history ( with 4.4 million copies sold ) . the album was the second biggest selling album for both 1995 and 1996 in the uk . it went on to become the biggest selling album of the decade . the album has gone 4Ã— platinum ( 3.9 million copies ) in the united states . album singles `` some might say '' , `` roll with it '' , `` wonderwall '' and `` do n't look back in anger '' were big hits in the uk , with `` some might say '' and `` do n't look back in anger '' both reaching the number one spot in the uk singles chart . `` wonderwall '' and `` champagne supernova '' both went # 1 on the u.s. modern rock tracks chart . they would go on to be certified gold in the united states . whilst `` wonderwall '' also topped the australian aria charts . the album won best british album at the 1996 brit awards , and won the brit award for the best british album of the last 30 years at the 2010 brit awards .
