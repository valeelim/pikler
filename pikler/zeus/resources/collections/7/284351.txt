Jochen Hecht     jochen hecht ( born june 21 , 1977 in mannheim , west germany ) is a retired ice hockey centre that played a combined 13 seasons in the nhl . he was picked by the st. louis blues with the 49th overall pick in the 1995 nhl entry draft . hecht played 2 seasons with the st. louis blues , 1 season with the edmonton oilers and 10 seasons with the buffalo sabres . he also played 5 seasons in the del with adler mannheim . on april 26 , 2013 , he announced that he was retiring from professional ice hockey to reporters in the locker room after the sabres final regular season game against the new york islanders .
