Quinoa     quinoa ( , from ) is a species of goosefoot ( `` chenopodium '' ) . it is a grain-like crop grown primarily for its edible seeds . quinoa is not a real cereal , or grain , as it is not a member of the grass family . as a chenopod , quinoa is closely related to species such as beets , spinach , and tumbleweeds . its leaves are also eaten as a leaf vegetable , much like amaranth . quinoa greens are not widely available for purchase . quinoa gives high quality protein and other nutrients . it has been called a 'superfood ' .
