Natalya (wrestler)    natalya ( wrestler ) natalie katherine neidhart ( born may 27 , 1982 in calgary , alberta ) is a canadian female professional wrestler . she is currently signed to the wwe . she competes under the ring name natalya . she is a former wwe divas champion . she is a third generation superstar , her parents are former professional wrestler jim `` the anvil '' neidhart and ellie hart , the daughter of stu hart . in june 2013 , neidhart married long time boyfriend and fellow professional wrestler tyson kidd . she currently appears on the reality television show total divas where she is a part of the main cast . championships . - nwa : extreme canadian championship wrestling - supergirls championship ( one time ) - pro wrestling illustrated - she was ranked # 4 of the best 50 female singles wrestlers in the pwi female 50 in 2011 . - stampede wrestling - stampede women 's pacific championship ( two times ) - women 's wrestler of the year ( 2005 ) - world wrestling entertainment - wwe divas championship ( one time ) - wrestling observer newsletter
