Ramon Harewood     ramon harewood ( born february 3 , 1987 in st. michael , barbados ) is an barbadian-american football offensive tackle for the baltimore ravens of the national football league ( nfl ) . harewood was drafted out of morehouse college by the baltimore ravens with the 194th overall pick in the 6th round of the 2010 nfl draft . websites . - ramon harewood at baltimoreravens.com
