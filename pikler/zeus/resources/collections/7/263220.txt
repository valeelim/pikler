Popish Plot     the popish plot was a story made up by titus oates that caused people to hate the catholics in england between 1678 and 1681. oates said that there was a large catholic conspiracy to kill charles ii . his story led to the execution of at least 15 men and caused the exclusion bill crisis . after a while oates ' complicated story fell apart , and he was arrested and sent to prison for perjury .
