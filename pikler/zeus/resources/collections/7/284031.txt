Greensboro, North Carolina    greensboro , north carolina greensboro is a city in the u.s. state of north carolina . it has about 260,000 people and is growing . greensboro is near winston salem and high point in an area called the triad . it is in guilford county . greensboro was started in 1808. it was built in the middle of guilford county . it grew slowly , but was rich because of cotton . in the 1840s , a railroad was built going through greensboro , making it grow faster . the civil war did not hurt greensboro much . in the early 1900s , greensboro grew a lot because of textiles . they also became rich . a lot of stuff happened during the african-american civil rights movement in the 1960s in greensboro , including a sit-in at a counter in a restaurant . now , greensboro continues to grow , and is located at the intersection of two big freeways , interstate 40 and i-85 .
