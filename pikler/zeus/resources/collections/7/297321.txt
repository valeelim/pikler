Element Skateboards     element skateboards ( sometimes shortened to element ) is an american company . it is known for making skateboards and swimwear . it was founded in 1992 by johnny schillereff . the company is based out of irvine , california . the company was originally known as underworld element skateboards but schillereff decided to drop the `` underworld '' part due to the company being absorbed because of financial problems . the element company also sponsors professional skateboarders like bucky lasek , bam margera , chad muska , ray barbee , vanessa torres , mark appleyard , nyjah huston and madars apse
