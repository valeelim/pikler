Folic acid     folic acid ( also known as vitamin b , vitamin bc or folacin ) and folate , as well as pteroyl-l-glutamic acid , pteroyl-l-glutamate , and pteroylmonoglutamic acid are forms of the water-soluble vitamin b9 . folic acid is itself not biologically active , but its biological importance is due to tetrahydrofolate and other derivatives after its conversion to dihydrofolic acid in the liver .
