Radiated tortoise     the radiated tortoise is a type of tortoise . it has vibrant yellow streaks down its bumpy black shell . it lives in southern and hotter parts of madagascar . it can stay alive up to about 50 years in the wild and about 100 years in captivity . the radiated tortoise has a black patch on its head . its scaly legs are yellow . its shell is its most impressive feature . the shell is black and bumpy with a pattern of yellow stripes on it . the radiated tortoise can weigh up to 35 lbs . the radiated tortoise likes to use the same food source many times before choosing a new one . they eat seeds , fruit , succulent plants and shrubs . they get their water from water pools on rocks . it is very hot so they have to stay hydrated . references . - -
