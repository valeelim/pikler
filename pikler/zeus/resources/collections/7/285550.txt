Stetson University     stetson university is a private , co-educational university in deland , florida . the university has about 2,200 students . it was founded in 1883. the current president of the university is wendy b. libby . the athletics teams of the university are called the hatters . they compete in the atlantic sun conference . other websites . - stetson university 's official website - stetson hatters ' official athletics website
