2011 Daytona 500     the 2011 daytona 500 took place on february 20th 2011 at the daytona international speedway . it was the first race of the 2011 nascar sprint cup series . dale earnhardt , jr. won the pole in qualifying , however he did not start in 1st place because he crashed his car during practice . the race was won by trevor bayne . at 20 years old , he is the youngest driver to win the race .
