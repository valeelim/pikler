Nolan Baumgartner     nolan baumgartner ( born march 23 , 1976 in calgary , alberta ) is a canadian retired ice hockey defenceman . he played a total of 12 seasons in the national hockey league ( nhl ) . he played for the washington capitals for 4 seasons , the chicago blackhawks for 1 season , the vancouver canucks for 4 seasons , the pittsburgh penguins for 1 season , the philadelphia flyers for 1 season and the dallas stars for 1 season . he was drafted with the 10th overall pick in the 1994 nhl draft by the washington capitals . he also played with the kamloops blazers of the whl .
