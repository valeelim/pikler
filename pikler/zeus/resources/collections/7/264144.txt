South African Grand Prix     the south african grand prix was first run as a grand prix motor racing handicap race in 1934 at the prince george circuit at east london , eastern cape province , south africa . the races were stopped because of world war ii . it was started again in 1962 as part of the formula one circuit . it was a popular f1 event . it was stopped again after the 1985 race . some nations began boycotting ( not participating ) south african events because of racial segregation policy of apartheid . after apartheid was ended in 1991 , there were two more races held in 1992 and 1993. active drivers are in bold . `` events that were not part of the formula one world championship have a '' pink background . active formula one constructors are in bold . `` events that were not part of the formula one world championship have a '' pink background . `` events that were not part of the formula one world championship have a '' pink background . resource . - chicanef1
