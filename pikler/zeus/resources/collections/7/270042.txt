Covenant     a covenant is a formal agreement , a promise made between two people . it is often used as a religious promise . it can also be an item which is a symbol of keeping the promise . some covenants include stone tablets , written documents , and other sacred items . in judaism , the ark of the covenant was a wooden box which held the stone tablets with the ten commandments written on them . the jewish people saw the box as a symbol of god being with them and leading them .
