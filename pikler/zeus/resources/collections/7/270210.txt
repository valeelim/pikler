Antihydrogen     antihydrogen is an atom made of antimatter . like normal matter , antihydrogen can bond and form molecules with other antimatter . ( it would annihilate any normal matter that it touches ) . antihydrogen has one positron–which is positively charged–orbiting an antiproton , which is negatively charged . this is opposite normal hydrogen , which has one negatively charged electron orbiting a positively charged proton . `` antihydrogen '' can also refer to the molecule called antihydrogen , which is made of two bonded antiprotons .
