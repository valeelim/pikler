NCAA Basketball 09     ncaa basketball 09 is a college basketball video game that was published by ea sports . the video game was released for the playstation 3 , playstation 2 and xbox 360 systems . the video game was released on november 17 , 2008. the athlete on the cover of the game is kevin love who currently plays for the minnesota timberwolves . the video game received a 7.0 for both the ps3 and xbox 360 from gamespot and also received a 7.1 for both the xbox 360 and the ps3 and a 5.1 for the ps2 from ign . the game was the 13th installment in the ncaa basketball video game series . other websites . - official ncaa basketball 09 website
