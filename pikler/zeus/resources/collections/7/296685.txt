Tanzanite     tanzanite is a blue-purple gemstone found in tanzania , in the mererani hills near the city of arusha and mount kilimanjaro . it is named after the country of tanzania , where it is found . it is a kind of a mineral called zoisite . when it is first mined , tanzanite is usually a red-brown colour . it needs to be heated to 600 degrees celsius in a special oven to bring out its purple colour .
