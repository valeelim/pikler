University of Colorado Boulder     the university of colorado boulder is a public university in boulder , colorado . the university has about 30,196 students . it was founded in 1876. the athletics teams of the university are called the buffaloes . they have 14 varsity teams and they compete in the pacific-12 conference , which they joined in july 2011. the law school at university of colorado , boulder , is ranked # 40 by u.s. news & world report . this is for the 2016 rankings and is out of 196 law schools . other websites . - the university of colorado boulder
