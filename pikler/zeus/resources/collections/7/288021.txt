Hoja santa     hoja santa ( piper auritum ) is a good-smelling herb with a heart-shaped velvety leaf . the name `` hoja santa '' means `` sacred leaf '' in spanish language . it is also known as yerba santa , hierba santa , mexican pepperleaf , root beer plant , and sacred pepper . the difficult taste of hoja santa is not easy to describe . it has been compared to eucalyptus , licorice , sassafras , anise , nutmeg , mint , tarragon , and black pepper . the flavor is stronger in the young stems and veins .
