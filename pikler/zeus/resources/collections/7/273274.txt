Hypoiodite     hypoiodite is an ion . its chemical formula is io . it is very unstable . it easily disproportionates to iodide and iodate . it is made by reacting iodine with a base . the iodine is in its +1 oxidation state . it is the salt of hypoiodous acid . related pages . - iodine monochloride - iodine trifluoride
