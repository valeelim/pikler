Onon River     the onon gol ( mongolian : онон гол , russian : онон ) is a river in mongolia and russia . it is 818 km long . its watershed is 94,010 km . it starts at the khentii mountains , on the eastern slopes . 298 km of the river is in mongolia . it has a confluence ( the place where two rivers join together ) with the ingoda river . when they meet , it makes the shilka river . people believe that genghis khan was born near the upper onon and he grew up there . the rivers onon—shilka—amur makes one of the world 's ten longest rivers ( their lengths , in order : 818 km , 560 km , 2,874 km ) .
