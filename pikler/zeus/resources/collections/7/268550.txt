Gulf of Salerno     the gulf of salerno is a gulf of the tyrrhenian sea in the coast of the province of salerno in south-western italy . the northern part of this coast is the costiera amalfitana . this includes towns like amalfi , maiori , positano and the city of salerno itself . the gulf of salerno is separated from the gulf of naples ( on the north ) by the sorrentine peninsula , while from the south it is bounded by the cilento coast .
