Ali Baba     ali baba ( , `` ʿali bāba '' ) is a fictional character , who is described in the adventure tale of `` ali baba and the forty thieves '' . it is believed by some critics that this story was added to `` one thousand and one nights '' by one of its european translators , antoine galland , who is an 18th-century french orientalist who may have heard it in oral form from a middle eastern story-teller from aleppo . however , another writer and scholar sir richard francis burton said it was a part of the original `` one thousand and one nights '' . most arab and persian ( irani ) scholars seem to also agree with burton . texts . - pitt education
