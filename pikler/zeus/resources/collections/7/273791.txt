River Ystwyth     the river ystwyth ( ; ) is a river in west wales . it starts in the hills between rhayader and ysbyty ystwyth and flow into the sea at aberystwyth . although the river looks very clean , it has high levels of some metals such as lead , zinc and silver . these metals poison a lot of the wildlife so that only a few things live in some of the river . these metals get into the river from old lead mines . nobody uses the mines any more but the waste material left behind still has lots of metal in it and this dissolves in rain and gets into the river . other reading . - green , john ( 2006 ) , `` afon ystwyth : the story of a river '' , artery publications 2006. isbn 0-9513909-7-x
