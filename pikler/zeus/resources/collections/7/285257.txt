Sacred Heart University     sacred heart university is a private , roman catholic university in fairfield , connecticut . the university has about 6,023 students . it was founded in 1963. the current president of the university is john j. petillo . the athletics teams of the university are called the pioneers . they have 31 varsity teams , most of which compete in the northeast conference . the men 's ice hockey team competes in atlantic hockey . other websites . - sacred heart university 's official website - sacred heart pioneers ' official athletics website
