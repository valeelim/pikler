Sodium phosphide     sodium phosphide is a chemical compound . it contains sodium and phosphide ions . its chemical formula is nap . sodium phosphide is a black solid . it is ionic . there are several other sodium phosphides that do not have the formula nap . it reacts with acids to make phosphine gas . it is a reducing agent . it can be made by reacting liquid sodium with phosphorus ( v ) chloride . it can also be made by reacting sodium with phosphorus . it is used to make semiconductors . it can also be used as a catalyst . sodium phosphide is flammable , making toxic gases when burned . it also makes toxic flammable gases when reacted with acids . related pages . - sodium phosphate - sodium phosphite - sodium hypophosphite
