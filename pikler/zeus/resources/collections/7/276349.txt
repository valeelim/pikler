Gwangaeto the Great     gwanggaeto the great ( , , 374 - 413 ) was an ancient korean monarch . he was the 19th king of goguryo and reigned from 393 to 413 ce . during his reign the goguryeo territory widened with the conquests of suksin , mohe , khitan , buyeo . his era name was `` yuungrak '' ( , ) . king gwanggaeto died from a sickness in the year 413. references . - les coréens dans l'histoire , gwanggaeto le grand , l ’ esprit de goguryeo , kbsworld , le 27 aout 2010 .
