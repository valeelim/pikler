Steve Berry (novelist)    steve berry ( novelist ) steve berry ( born in 1955 ) is an american author , professor and former attorney currently living in st. augustine , florida . steve berry first appeared in print with his historical books `` the amber room '' and `` the romanov prophecy '' in 2003 and 2004. berry had been writing since 1990 , and it took him 12 years and 85 rejections ( over five different editions ) to finally sell a manuscript to ballantine books . berry credits the nuns who taught him in catholic school about how to write novels and contact publishers . today , berry has nearly 11 million books in print , which have been translated into 37 languages and sold in 50 countries . berry focuses his time on writing or planning new novels , teaching writing to people all over the world , golfing or spending time on the beach .
