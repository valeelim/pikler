Zinc sulfate     zinc sulfate is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is znso . it contains zinc and sulfate ions . zinc sulfate is a colorless solid . it dissolves in water . it can react with bases to make zinc hydroxide . zinc sulfate can be made by reacting zinc or zinc oxide with sulfuric acid . it can also be made by reacting copper sulfate with zinc . zinc sulfate can be used to add zinc to the soil . it is also used to add zinc to animal feed . it was once used to remove acne . related pages . - zinc fluoride - zinc chloride
