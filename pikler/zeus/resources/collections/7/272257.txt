Tin(IV) iodide    tin ( iv ) iodide tin ( iv ) iodide , also known as stannic iodide and tin tetraiodide , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is sni . it contains tin and iodide ions . the tin is in its +4 oxidation state . tin ( iv ) iodide is a red-orange solid . it reacts with water to make hydriodic acid and tin ( iv ) oxide . it dissolves in organic solvents . it reacts with hydriodic acid to make the sni ion . it is normally made by reacting iodine with tin . it can also be made by reacting tin ( iv ) oxide with hydriodic acid . related pages . - tin ( ii ) iodide - tin ( iv ) chloride
