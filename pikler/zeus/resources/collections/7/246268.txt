Ginebra San Miguel     ginebra san miguel , inc. ( gsmi ) is a philippines-based alcoholic drink company . it is owned by san miguel corporation , then called la tondeňa distillers , inc. ( ltdi ) from 1987 to 2003. founded originally by the late carlos palanca , sr. as la tondeña , inc. ( lti ) in 1902 in the district of tondo , manila ( where its name come from ) , it manufactures a range of products and carries packaging innovations that satisfy the needs of its target consumers . its main product is hard liquor . other websites . - ginebra san miguel official website - san miguel corporation
