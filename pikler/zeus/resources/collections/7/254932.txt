Dragunov sniper rifle     the dragunov sniper rifle is an sniper rifle that was originally developed by soviet union . it is used by twenty-six countries . the dragunov uses the 7.62x54mmr cartridge , with a muzzle velocity ( the velocity of bullet leaving the rifle ) of over 830 meters per second and has a maximum effective range of 1,300 meters with the scope and 1,200 meters with the iron sights .
