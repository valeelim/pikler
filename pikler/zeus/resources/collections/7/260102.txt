Heath Slater     heath miller ( born july 15 , 1983 in pineville , west virginia ) is an american professional wrestler . he currently works with the wwe where he wrestles under the ring name heath slater . he was a member of the stables 3mb and the nexus . slater has won the wwe tag team championship three times with justin gabriel . he also won a slammy award in 2010 for the debut of the nexus . slater 's finishing move is the `` smash hit '' which is a spinning lifting ddt . championships . - florida championship wrestling - fcw florida heavyweight championship ( one time ) - fcw southern heavyweight championship ( one time ) - fcw florida tag team championship ( one time ) ( with joe hennig ) - georgia championship wrestling - nwa/gcw columbus heavyweight championship ( one time ) - pro wrestling illustrated - pwi feud of the year ( 2010 ) ( the nexus vs. wwe ) - pwi most hated wrestler of the year ( 2010 ) ( as part of the nexus ) - he was ranked # 66 of the top 500 singles wrestlers in the pwi 500 in 2011 . - world wrestling entertainment/wwe
