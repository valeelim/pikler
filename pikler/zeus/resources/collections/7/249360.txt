Soccer City     soccer city is a sports stadium in johannesburg , south africa . it was first built in 1987. at that time , it was named the fnb stadium . in 2009 , the stadium was improved . more seats were added . new locker ( dressing ) rooms and new floodlights were built . the stadium hosted the 2010 fifa world cup . is one of the 10 stadiums where world cup games will be played . 2010 world cup matches all times are given in south african standard time ( utc+2 ) . on june 29 , the south african government announced that a memorial service for nelson mandela would be held 10 to 14 days after his death at soccer city . mandela died on december 5 , 2013 and a memorial service was held at soccer city . fnb stadium is mostly used for concerts and local 5-a-side tournaments . when south africa 's most popular soccer team , the [ kaizer chiefs ] play in the stadium , they attract approximately 15,000 fans when they play there among the rotating venues that they use . other websites . - official soccer city site - fédération internationale de football association ( fifa )
