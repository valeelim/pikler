David Reed (American football)    david reed ( american football ) brandon david reed ( born march 22 , 1987 in dubuque , iowa ) is an american football wide receiver who currently plays for the baltimore ravens of the national football league ( nfl ) . reed was drafted out of the university of utah by the baltimore ravens with the 156th pick in the fifth round of the 2010 nfl draft . he won super bowl xlvii with the ravens when they defeated the san francisco 49ers 34-31. other websites . - david reed at nfl.com
