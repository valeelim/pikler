Christian Schwegler     christian schwegler ( born 6 june 1984 in ettiswil ) is a swiss footballer . he plays for red bull salzburg in the austrian bundesliga as defender . he started with fc luzern in the swiss challenge league ( 2nd division ) and went on to arminia bielefeld in germany but after one half of the season he went back to switzerland . there he played for bsc young boys bern . in 2009 he signed with fc red bull salzburg where he played regularly as right defender . he is known for his long throw-ins ( up to 35 meters ) .during the match versus sv mattersburg on 27th march 2010 he shot his first goal for salzburg . in the first half of the 2010/11 season he was injured and could not play the rest of the year . christian schwegler has a younger brother who is also a professional footballer playing for eintracht frankfurt in the german bundesliga . honours . - austrian champion - 1x with fc red bull salzburg ( 2010 ) other websites . - red bull salzburg profile - football.ch profile - guardian football stats
