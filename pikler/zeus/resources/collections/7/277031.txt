Homer, Alaska    homer , alaska homer is a city on the kenai peninsula in alaska . it is known for being `` the end of the road . '' there are signs that native american people lived in the area as early as 6000 bc , there are now about 5,500 people living in homer . the most notable feature of homer is the homer spit , a long finger of land sticking out into kachemak bay . the town 's harbor and many of its fishing and tourism related businesses are on the spit .
