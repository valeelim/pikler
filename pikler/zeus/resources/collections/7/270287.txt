Leptoceratops     leptoceratops ( meaning `` slender horned face '' ) was a stocky ceratopsian dinosaur that had a large skull are there was a horn on its beaked snout . it is known from five skulls and some skeletal material . `` leptoceratops '' are during in the upper cretaceous , about 6865 million years ago . fossils have been found in alberta , canada , and wyoming , in north america .
