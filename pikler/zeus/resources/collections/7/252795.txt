Chico Slimani     chico slimani ( born 2 april 1971 ) is a moroccan-welsh pop singer based in the uk and morocco . he has been active since 2005 when he reached the quarter-finals of the 2005 uk series of talent show `` the x factor '' . he has had 3 hit singles as of 2010 , number 1 hit `` it 's chico time '' , which was number 3 in ireland , number 24 hit `` disco '' which was number 39 in ireland and number 45 hit `` curvy cola bottle body '' which failed to chart in ireland he charted at number 85 with `` it 's england time '' . he is part of sony bmg record label . his album is `` lights , camera , action '' . he is a contestant in the seventh series of `` dancing on ice '' , which began in january 2012. he is also known as chico .
