Roberto Devereux     roberto devereux is an italian opera in three acts by gaetano donizetti , libretto by salvatore cammarano , after françois ancelot 's `` elisabeth d'angleterre '' , it was first performed in naples , on october 29 , 1837. it is a powerful and sweeping work and is sometimes considered one of the most `` verdian '' of donizetti 's operas . after almost a century of neglect , it was revived in naples by soprano leyla gencer in 1964 , and has since then been regularly performed , notably by sopranos montserrat caballé and beverly sills . the plot is about the love between queen elizabeth i of england and her favorite robert devereux , earl of essex . principal roles . - elisabetta i - soprano - roberto devereux - tenor - sara , duchess of nottingham - mezzo-soprano - duke of nottingham - baritone sources . - `` the complete dictionnary of opera & operetta '' , james anderson , wings books , 1993 .
