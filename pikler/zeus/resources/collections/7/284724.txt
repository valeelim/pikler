Providence College     providence college is a private , roman catholic university in providence , rhode island . the university has about 4,585 students . it was founded in 1917. the current president of the university is fr . brian j. shanley , o.p . . the athletics teams of the university are called the friars . they have 19 varsity teams and they compete in the big east conference . other websites . - providence college 's official website - providence frairs ' official athletics website
