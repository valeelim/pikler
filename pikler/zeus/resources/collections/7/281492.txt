Debbie Gibson     deborah ann gibson ( born august 31 , 1970 in brooklyn , new york ) is an american singer-songwriter and actress . she was very popular in the late 1980s and early 1990s and had many hit songs . in 1988 she became the youngest artist to write , produce , and perform a # 1 single on the billboard hot 100 , with her song `` foolish beat '' . she remains the youngest female to write , record , and perform a # 1 single . although never married , gibson was once engaged to jonathan kanterman . on january 6 , 2008 gibson was on `` deal or no deal '' along with corey feldman as part of an 1980s special . gibson co-starred with actor lorenzo lamas in the low-budget movie `` mega shark versus giant octopus '' . it was released on may 19 , 2009. this movie had 2 million viewers on syfy in 2009. the movie premiered at the 2009 cannes film festival . gibson 's former music rival tiffany had her movie `` necrosis '' ( a.k.a . `` blood snow '' ) premiere at the cannes as well . tiffany and debbie gibson starred in a syfy original movie called `` mega python vs. gatoroid '' . it aired on january 29 , 2011. the pairing was at the suggestion of tiffany , who wanted to play off their supposed rivalry .
