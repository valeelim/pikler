Toni Collette     antonia `` toni '' collette ( born 1 november 1972 ) is an australian actress and singer . she was born in blacktown , new south wales , a suburb of sydney . collette 's acting career began during the early 1990s . she starred in several australian movies , including : `` muriel 's wedding '' , `` in her shoes '' , `` little miss sunshine '' and `` the black balloon '' . in 2013 she had a convincing starring role as an american mother in the nat faxon and jim rash hollywood coming of age comedy `` the way , way back '' . she is the lead singer of rock band toni collette & the finish .
