Cape Town Stadium     cape town stadium is a sports stadium in cape town , south africa . it was first built in 1987. at that time , it was named the fnb stadium . the stadium will host the 2010 fifa world cup . is one of the 10 stadiums where world cup games will be played . fifa calls this stadium `` green point stadium '' . 2010 world cup matches all times are given in south african standard time ( utc+2 ) . other websites . - fédération internationale de football association ( fifa )
