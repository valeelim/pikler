Fairuz Fauzy     mohamed fairuz fauzy ( born october 24 , 1982 in kuala lumpur , malaysia ) is a race car driver . fauzy started competing in karting in 1994. in 1999 , he moved to british formula ford . he moved to british formula renault in 2000 , and to british formula three in 2002. he moved to the gp2 series for 2005 and 2006. he did not score any point in gp2 . fauzy raced in the a1 grand prix series for team malaysia . he then raced in the formula renault 3.5 series in 2007. fauzy returned to gp2 in 2008 , driving for the super nova team in the gp2 asia series . in 2009 , he raced in the formula renault 3.5 series for mofaz racing . he finish in second place in the championship standings . fauzy was one of spyker f1 's test and reserve drivers for , he was announced as the third driver for lotus for the 2010 season . fauzy drove in the first free practice sessions at the malaysian grand prix , the british grand prix , the german grand prix and the abu dhabi grand prix . at the end of the 2010 season , fauzy left lotus racing . he joined renault as a test driver for the 2011 season .
