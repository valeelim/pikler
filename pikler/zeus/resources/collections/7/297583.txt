Saint-Lô     saint-lô is a commune . it is found in the manche department in the west of france . saint-lô is twinned with : - saint-ghislain , belgium , since 1961 - aalen , germany , since 1979 - christchurch , uk , since 1985 - kervénanec , lorient , france , since 1988 - roanoke , usa , since 1999
