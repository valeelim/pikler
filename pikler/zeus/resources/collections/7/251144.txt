Stygimoloch     stygimoloch was a plant-eating dinosaur that had bony spikes and knobs on its skull . this bipedal dinosaur lived during the late cretaceous period , about 68 million to 65 million years ago . `` stygimoloch '' are ornithischian dinosaurs that had thick , domed , spiked skulls . pachycephalosaurs probably engaged in head-butting both as a defense and in intra-species rivalry .
