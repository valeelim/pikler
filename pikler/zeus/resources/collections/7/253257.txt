Hail! Minnesota    hail ! minnesota `` hail ! minnesota '' ( also simply called `` minnesota '' in early years ) is the state song of minnesota . it began at the university of minnesota in the early 1900s when some students decided to honor their graduating class with a new song . in 1945 , the minnesota state legislature approved the tune as the state song . other websites . - minnesota secretary of state with sheet music and recording .
