Wiki markup     wikitext language , or wiki markup , is a markup language used to write pages in wiki websites , such as wikipedia . it is a simplified alternative to html . it is converted by wiki software ( like mediawiki ) into regular html , which the user 's web browser will render . the main purpose of having wikitext in wikis instead of html is because html is more complicated and not many people know how to use it . therefore , it is easier to use wikitext rather than html and thus makes editors contributing to a wiki easier to create content . related pages . - html - wysiwyg editor other websites . - help on editing on meta wiki
