Bill Fairbairn     william john `` magnet '' fairbairn ( born january 7 , 1947 in brandon , manitoba ) is a retired professional hockey player . he played in the national hockey league with the new york rangers , minnesota north stars and st. louis blues . awards and achievements . - mjhl second all-star team ( 1967 ) - chl second all-star team ( 1969 ) - inducted into the manitoba sports hall of fame and museum in 2007 - `` honoured member '' of the manitoba hockey hall of fame other websites . - bill fairbairn 's biography at manitoba hockey hall of fame - bill fairbairn 's biography at manitoba sports hall of fame
