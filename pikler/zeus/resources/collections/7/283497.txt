It Was a Good Day     `` it was a good day '' is a 1993 single by american rapper ice cube . it was released from the album `` the predator '' . `` it was a good day '' peaked at number seven on the `` billboard '' hot r & b/hip-hop songs and number fifteen on the `` billboard '' hot 100. the song also peaked at number twenty-seven on the uk singles chart . uk single 1 . `` it was a good day '' ( radio edit ) 1 . `` it was a good day '' ( instrumental )
