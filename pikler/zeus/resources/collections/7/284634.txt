Kevin Dineen     kevin william dineen ( born october 28 , 1963 in quebec city , quebec ) is a canadian retired professional ice hockey right winger . he lived in cape elizabeth , maine and moved florida in 2010. he played a career total of played 21 seasons in the national hockey league ( nhl ) . he played for the hartford whalers , philadelphia flyers , carolina hurricanes , ottawa senators , and columbus blue jackets . he is currently the assistant coach of the chicago blackhawks . he was the head coach of the florida panthers from 2011–2013 . dineen also scored the last goal ever for the hartford whalers in a 2–1 win against the tampa bay lightning . on january 6 , 2006 , he had his playing number 11 retired by the american hockey league 's hartford wolf pack .
