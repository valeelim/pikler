Plumbaginaceae     plumbaginaceae is a family of flowering plants are about 560 species in 10 genera found all over the world . they are annual or perennial herbs , shrubs and climbers . thrift ( `` armeria '' ) and sea lavender ( `` limonium '' ) , have small flowers in dense clusters . they are generally grown for garden decoration or as cut flowers , either fresh or dried , and medicinal extracts are obtained from some species .
