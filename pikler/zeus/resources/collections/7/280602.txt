The Wraith: Shangri-La    the wraith : shangri-la the wraith : shangri-la is the eighth studio album by american horrorcore band , insane clown posse . it was released on november 5 , 2002. the producer of the album was mike puwal . the album was the sixth and final of the `` joker cards '' in the dark carnival mythology . the album peaked at number 15 on the billboard 200 and peaked number 1 on the top independent albums in both 2002 and 2003 .
