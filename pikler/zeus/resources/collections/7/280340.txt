Zingiberales     zingiberales is an order of flowering plants . the order has been widely recognised by the taxonomists , at least for the past few decades . this order includes many familiar plants like ginger , cardamom , turmeric , galangal and myoga of the zingiberaceae or ginger family , and bananas and plantains of the musaceae or banana family , along with arrowroot of the marantaceae or arrowroot family .
