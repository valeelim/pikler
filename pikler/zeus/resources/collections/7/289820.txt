Warkany syndrome 2     warkany syndrome 2 , also known as chromosome 8 trisomy syndrome or trisomy 8 , is a condition where chromosome 8 in humans has three sets of chromosomes , as opposed to the normal two . this condition is called `` trisomy '' , and is where warkany syndrome 2 derives its alternative names . people born with warkany syndrome 2 typically have mental retardation and renal or skeletal malformations .
