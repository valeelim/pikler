University of North Carolina at Greensboro     the university of north carolina at greensboro is a public university in greensboro , north carolina . the university has about 18,502 students . it was founded in 1891. the current chancellor of the university is linda p. brady . the athletics teams of the university are called the spartans . they have 16 varsity teams and they compete in the southern conference . other websites . - university of north carolina at greensboro 's official website - unc greensboro spartans ' official athletics website
