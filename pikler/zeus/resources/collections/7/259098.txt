Happiness (movie)    happiness ( movie ) happiness is a 1998 american comedy drama movie . it stars jane adams , jon lovitz , philip seymour hoffman , dylan baker and lara flynn boyle . it was directed by todd solondz . the movie was first rated nc-17 . this caused problems with the advertising and distribution of the movie . because of this , they chose to release the movie without any rating . the movie was nominated for a golden globe award for `` best screenplay '' ( todd solondz ) .
