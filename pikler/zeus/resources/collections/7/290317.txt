Jared Carter     jared carter ( born january 10 , 1939 ) is an american poet . carter studied at yale university . after military service and travel abroad , he made his home in indianapolis , where he has lived since 1969. carter writes in free verse and in traditional forms . much of his early work is set in mississinewa county , an imaginary place that includes the actual mississinewa river . in recent years , as carter began to publish his poems on the web , his poetry has ranged farther . his first collection , `` work , for the night is coming '' , won the walt whitman award . his second , `` after the rain '' , received the poets ' prize . his poems have appeared in literary journals in the u.s. and in the `` twentieth-century american poetry , '' `` contemporary american poetry , '' and `` writing poems .
