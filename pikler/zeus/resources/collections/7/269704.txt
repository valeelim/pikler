Scottish Green Party     the scottish green party is a political party in scotland that has close relations to the green party of england and wales . the party 's policies usually promote environmentalism , socialism and the creation of an independent scotland free from the united kingdom but within the european union . in the scottish parliament the party is an opposition party with 2 out of 129 seats . it does not have any seats in the house of commons of the united kingdom or the european parliament . the co-conveners ( leaders ) of the party are patrick harvie msp and maggie chapman . other websites . - official website
