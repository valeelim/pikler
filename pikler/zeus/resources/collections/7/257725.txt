Mur de la peste     the mur de la peste or plague wall was built in the vaucluse mountains to stop the spread of the plague . between 1720 and 1722 , there was an epidemic in the provence and in marseille . the wall is built of dry stone , and was about 27 km long . today , a part of the wall still stands , near murs . along the wall , there were watchtowers for guardposts .
