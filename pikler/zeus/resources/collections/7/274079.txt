Sodium bismuthate     sodium bismuthate is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is nabio . it has sodium and bismuthate ions in it . sodium bismuthate is a light brown solid . it does not dissolve in water . it is one of the only sodium compounds that do not dissolve in water . it reacts with hot water to make sodium hydroxide , bismuth ( iii ) oxide , and oxygen gas . it reacts with acids to make bismuth ( iii ) salts , sodium salts , and oxygen gas . it is a strong oxidizing agent . it is made by reacting a basic solution of bismuth ( iii ) oxide and sodium hydroxide with a strong oxidizing agent . sodium bismuthate is used in a test for manganese . related pages . - bismuth ( v ) oxide - bismuth ( v ) fluoride - bismuth ( iii ) bromide
