Lead(II) bromide    lead ( ii ) bromide lead ( ii ) bromide , also known as plumbous bromide , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is pbbr . it contains lead and bromide ions . the lead is in its +2 oxidation state . lead ( ii ) bromide is a white solid . it only dissolves a little in water . it melts to make a liquid that conducts a little electricity . it is toxic because it has lead in it . it can be made by reacting lead ( ii ) nitrate or lead ( ii ) acetate with hydrobromic acid or any other bromide . related pages . - lead ( ii ) chloride - lead ( ii ) sulfate
