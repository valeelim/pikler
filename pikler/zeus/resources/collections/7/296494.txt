Jejomar Binay     jejomar `` jojo '' cabauatan binay ( born november 11 , 1942 ) , also known as jojo binay or vnay , is a filipino politician . he became the 15th vice president of the philippines on june 30 , 2010. previously , he was mayor of makati city from 1986 to 1998 and again from 2001 to 2010 .
