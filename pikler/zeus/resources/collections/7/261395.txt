Potassium sulfate     potassium sulfate , also known as arcanite , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is kso . it contains potassium and sulfate ions . it is a white crystalline solid . it dissolves in water easily . it does not dissolve in potassium hydroxide or concentrated ethanol . it can react with additional sulfuric acid to make potassium bisulfate . it is made by reacting potassium hydroxide and sulfuric acid . it can also be made by reacting potassium chloride with sulfuric acid . it can also be made by reacting sulfur dioxide , oxygen , and potassium chloride with some water . it is used in fertilizer . it is sometimes used to make glass . arcanite is the mineral form of potassium sulfate . related pages . - sodium sulfate - potassium sulfite
