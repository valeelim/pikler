Potassium bromate     potassium bromate is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is kbro . it contains potassium and bromate ions . potassium bromate is a white solid . it is a strong oxidizing agent . it might be a carcinogen . it can be made by dissolving bromine in hot potassium hydroxide . it can also be made by electrolysis of potassium bromide solution . potassium bromate is used to make wheat flour better . some places have banned it from being added to flour because it might be a carcinogen . normally when the bread is baked the potassium bromate is broken down . if there is too much , then some might be left over in the bread , which is bad . related pages . - sodium bromate - calcium bromate
