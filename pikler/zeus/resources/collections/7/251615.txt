Thescelosaurus     thescelosaurus was a plant-eating dinosaur that lived in forests during the late cretaceous period in north america , about 77 to 65 million years ago . a thescelosaurus fossilized four-chambered heart was recently found near buffalo , south dakota in usa . one complete specimen and 8 incomplete `` thescelosaurus '' skeletons , including skin impressions , have been found in wyoming , montana , and south dakota in the usa and in alberta and saskatchewan in canada .
