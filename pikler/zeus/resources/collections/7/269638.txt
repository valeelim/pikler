Old Schwamb Mill     the old schwamb mill is the oldest continuously operating mill site in the united states . there has been a mill operating at this site since c.1650 . the current mill is a 19th-century woodworking factory . the mill was built in 1861 , and purchased by brothers charles and frederick schwamb in 1864 for the manufacture of picture frames . it remained within the family 's ownership and continued to operate , more or less unchanged in both equipment and processes , until 1969. two wings were added during this time , however : one by 1881 , and the other between that date and 1898. the mill still contains operational equipment and four preserved power systems . references . - historic american engineering record ( haer ) mass , 9-arl , 4- , `` old schwamb mill '' , stuart campbell ( historian ) and richard k. anderson , jr. , ( photographer ) , library of congress online collection . http : //hdl.loc.gov/loc.pnp/hhh.ma1024
