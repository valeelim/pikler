Luther (1974 movie)    luther ( 1974 movie ) luther is a 1974 movie about the life of martin luther . the movie starred stacy keach as luther . the movie is a version of john osborne 's biographical play , presenting the life of martin luther . the movie begins in the year 1506 and ends in the year 1526. cast . - peter cellier as prior - leonard rossiter as brother weinand - stacy keach as martin luther - patrick magee as luther 's father - thomas heathcote as [ lucas , a ] friend [ of luther 's father ] - julian glover as knight - matthew guinness as reading monk - hugh griffith as john tetzel - maurice denham as johann von staupitz , vicar general - alan badel as cardinal cajetan de vio - robert stephens as johann von eck - bruce carstairs as duke frederick , elector of saxony - malcolm stoddard as emperor charles the fifth - judi dench as luther 's wife related pages . - martin luther - luther film
