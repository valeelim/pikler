Petri Kontiola     petri kontiola ( born october 4 , 1984 in seinajoki , finland ) is a professional finnish ice hockey center that currently plays in the kontinental hockey league for the traktor chelyabinsk . kontiola also played in the nhl for a season with the chicago blackhawks . he was drafted by the blackhawks with the 196th pick in the 2004 nhl draft .
