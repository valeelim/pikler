J.P. Manoux     jean-paul christophe manoux ( born june 8 , 1969 ) is an american actor . he is best known for his role in the disney channel series `` phil of the future '' as curtis/vice principal . he is also famous for playing kuzco in `` the emperor 's new school '' . manoux was also a contestant on `` jeopardy ! `` , `` family feud '' and `` wheel of fortune '' . he has authored and read two pieces for npr 's `` all things considered '' . back in 2000 , he provided commentary on the screen actors guild strike . he was a striking actor and refused to do work .
