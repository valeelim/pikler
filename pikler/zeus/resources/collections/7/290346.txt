About.com     about.com is an online source for information and advice . it is written in english , and is aimed mostly at north americans . it is owned by the new york times company . about.com is divided into topic sites , which are grouped into channels and subjects such as home repair , pediatrics , and weather . the content is written by a network of writers , referred to as guides , who have experience in the subject they look after . each guide looks after one subject only . guides receive money of about $ 100,000 per year , although the exact number is not disclosed .
