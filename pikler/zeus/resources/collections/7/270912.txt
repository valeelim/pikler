Islamic Azad University     islamic azad university ( , `` dāneshgāh-e āzād-e eslāmi '' ) is a private university system based in iran . the university was founded in 1982. it is the world 's third largest university . 1.5 million students study there . the university has over 100 branches across iran and also in other countries such as the emirate of dubai and the united kingdom . over the years , the university has become richer . it is estimated to be worth between $ 20 and $ 25 billion .
