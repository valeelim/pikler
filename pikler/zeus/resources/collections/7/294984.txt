Austrian Cup     the austrian cup ( german : öfb-cup ) is an football ( soccer ) competition held by the austrian football association , the öfb . it was played by male teams . for women there is the öfb-ladiescup . although it has a long tradition the cup never played an important role in austrian football . the austrian cup emerged from the lower austria cup ( niederösterreichischen cup , held 1915–1918 ) . the first cup was played 1919 and was held since then each year . only from 1938 to 1945 it was suspended , because austria was a part of nazigermany . also from 1950 till 1958 there was no competition because the öfb was not interested in . in 2008 the clubs of the bundesliga and the first division did not take part due to the uefa euro 2008. finals . - 1919 : sk rapid wien 3-0 wiener sport-club - 1920 : sk rapid wien 5-2 amateur-sv - 1921 : amateur-sv 2-1 wiener sport-club - 1922 : wiener af 2-1 amateur-sv - 1923 : wiener sport-club 3-1 sc wacker wien - 1924 : amateur-sv 8-6 sk slovan wien ( aet ) - 1925 : amateur-sv 3-1 first vienna fc
