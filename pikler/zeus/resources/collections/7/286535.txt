Dev (singer)    dev ( singer ) devin star tailes ( born july 2 , 1989 ) , best known by her stage name dev , is an american singer . her associated acts include far east movement . her song `` booty bounce '' was sampled in the far east movement 's `` like a g6 '' . her most successful single was `` in the dark '' . dev was born in tracy , california . she later moved herself to los angeles .
