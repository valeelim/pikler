Debut (album)    debut ( album ) debut is an album by björk . it was released in 1993. it has sold 4.7 million copies around the world . five singles were released from it . these were `` human behaviour '' , `` venus as a boy '' , `` play dead '' , `` big time sensuality '' and `` violently happy '' . most of the reviews of debut said that it is a good album . the video for `` human behaviour '' was nominated for best short form music video at the 1994 grammy awards . track listing . 1 . `` human behaviour '' ( björk , nellee hooper ) – 4:10 1 . `` crying '' ( björk , hooper ) – 4:52 1 . `` venus as a boy '' – 4:42 1 . `` there 's more to life than this '' ( björk , hooper ) – 3:21 1 . `` like someone in love '' ( johnny burke , james van heusen ) – 4:33 1 . `` big time sensuality '' ( björk , hooper ) – 3:57 1 . `` one day '' – 5:24 1 . `` aeroplane '' – 3:54 1 . `` come to me '' – 4:55 1 . `` violently happy '' ( björk , hooper ) – 4:59 1 . `` the anchor song '' – 3:32
