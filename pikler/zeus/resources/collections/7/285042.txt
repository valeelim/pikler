Lamborghini Jalpa     the lamborghini jalpa is a sport car that was built by lamborghini from 1981 to 1988. the jalpa is named after the type of fighting bull . the jalpa was replaced by the lamborghini gallardo . the designer of the jalpa was carrozzeria bertone . the production of the jalpa ended in 1988 due to failing sales of the car . other websites . - lamborghini 's official website
