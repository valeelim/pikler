Perilla     perilla is a genus of annual herb which is a member of the mint family . in mild climates the plant can reseed itself . there are both green leafed and purple leafed versions of this plant . the plant 's leaves resemble stinging nettle leaves , though slightly rounder in the shape . - koreans use `` perilla frutescens '' leaves and seeds in cooking . - japanese use `` perilla frutescens '' var . `` crispa '' leaves in cooking .
