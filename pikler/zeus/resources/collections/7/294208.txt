Binghamton, New York    binghamton , new york binghamton is a city in the u.s. state of new york . it is near pennsylvania , and sits along the susquehanna river . in 2010 , 47,376 people lived in the city , while 251,725 people lived in the metropolitan area . in the past , binghamton was known for making cigars , shoes , and computers . ibm started in the binghamton area . today , the area is home to binghamton university . binghamton is home to the binghamton senators hockey team and the binghamton mets baseball team .
