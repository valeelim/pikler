Hollerado     hollerado is a canadian indie rock band from manotick , ontario . hollerado has four members in the band ; the current band members are dean baxter , jake boyd , nixon boyd and menno versteeg . their name is a parody of the united states state , colorado . band members . - current - dean baxter - bass guitar & vocals - jake boyd - drums & vocals - nixon boyd - guitar & vocals - menno versteeg - guitar & lead vocals so far as of 2011 , hollerado has released 7 albums : - `` demo in a bag one '' ( 2007 ) - `` demo in a bag two '' ( 2007 ) - `` demo in a bag three '' ( 2007 ) - `` demo in a bag four '' ( 2008 ) - `` demo in a bag five '' ( 2009 ) - `` record in a bag '' ( 2010 ) - `` margaritaville ii : the reckoning '' ( 2011 ) singles . - `` americanarama '' ( 2010 ) - `` juliette '' ( 2010 ) - `` got to lose '' ( 2011 ) - `` good day at the races '' ( 2011 ) other websites . - hollerado 's official website
