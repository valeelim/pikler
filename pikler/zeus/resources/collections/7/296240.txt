Nmap     nmap is a computer program that is used for `` port scanning '' ( finding out all the ways a computer communicates with other computers on a network ) . it was written by gordon lyon . it can also do other kinds of scanning . this is because nmap is open source , so many people can help write it to make it better . nmap works on most operating systems . it works on linux , microsoft windows , mac os x , solaris , and bsd . most people use it on linux , but many people use it on windows . here are some things nmap can do : - find computers on a network - find open ports on those computers - find out what services are using those ports - find out what operating system is on the computers - let a programmer choose what to do with the services it finds . the programmer can write `` nmap scripting engine '' programs in the lua programming language . then nmap will use those programs to decide what to do . nmap also finds out other things about computers . it can find out dns names , mac addresses , and the kind of computer it is .
