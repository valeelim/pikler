Chromium(II) chloride    chromium ( ii ) chloride chromium ( ii ) chloride is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is crcl . it is a white crystalline chemical . it dissolves in water to produce a bright blue solution . it is a strong reducing agent . if the water has a little acid in it , hydrogen is produced . if the water has a little base in it , chromium ( ii ) oxide is produced . the water needs to be very pure . it is made by reacting chromium ( iii ) chloride and hydrogen at a high temperature . it can also be made by reduction of chromium ( iii ) chloride with zinc . it is used in science research . related pages . - chromium ( iii ) oxide - chromium ( vi ) oxide
