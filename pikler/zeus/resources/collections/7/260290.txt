Sodium iodide     sodium iodide is a chemical compound . it is composed of sodium and iodide ions . the chemical formula is nai . it is a white crystalline solid . it is a weak reducing agent . when it is exposed to oxygen in the air , it turns yellow-brown . it does this because some of the iodide was oxidized to iodine . it is used to supply iodine to the human body . related pages . - sodium fluoride - sodium bromide - sodium chloride
