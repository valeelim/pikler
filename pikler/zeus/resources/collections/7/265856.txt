Empire State of Mind     `` empire state of mind '' is a song by jay-z and alicia keys . it was released as the third single from jay-z 's 11th music album `` the blueprint 3 '' ( 2009 ) . the song is about new york city , and has a music sample of `` love on a two-way street '' ( 1970 ) by the moments . the title of the song takes its name from billy joel 's `` new york state of mind '' ( 1976 ) .
