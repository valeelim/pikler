Lua     lua is an open source programming language . it was created in 1993 by roberto ierusalimschy , luiz henrique de figueiredo , and waldemar celes . lua is used for many different things , especially in video games such as world of warcraft and simcity 4. it is also used in the popular virtual world sharing website roblox . in june 2010 , apple inc. changed the terms of use for the software development kit of its ios operating system to allow the use of lua . this led to lua being used to make iphone applications such as angry birds . in june 2011 , lua was ranked the tenth most popular programming language by the tiobe index . an example hello world program in lua : an example of setting a value , then printing it : other websites . - lua.org - lua-users.org
