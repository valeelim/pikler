Karate gi     a karate gi is a standardized piece of karate clothing that acts as a uniform . whether or not a student is ranked , one must bring a gi to the academy . a karate gi includes a martial arts jacket , as well as a martial arts pant . the gi is white . the belt can be : 1. white 1. yellow 1. purple 1. orange 1. green 1. blue 1. brown 1. black ( sub-divided by dan )
