San Gabriel Valley Tribune     the san gabriel valley tribune is the daily newspaper in west covina , california . it serves the san gabriel valley . the `` tribune '' is a member of the los angeles newspaper group , a division of medianews group . it is also part of the san gabriel valley newspaper group , along with the `` pasadena star-news '' and the `` whittier daily news '' . the newspaper chain brush-moore bought the `` tribune '' in 1960. thomson newspapers purchased brush-moore in 1967. thomson sold the `` tribune '' to singleton 's medianews group in 1996. other websites . - official website
