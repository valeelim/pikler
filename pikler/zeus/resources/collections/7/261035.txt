The Amazing Jeckel Brothers     the amazing jeckel brothers is the fifth studio album by american horrorcore band , insane clown posse . it was released on may 25 , 1999. the producers of the album were insane clown posse and mike e. clark . the album was the fifth of the `` joker cards '' in the dark carnival mythology . the album featured guest vocals such as ol ' dirty bastard , snoop dogg , the jerky boys and twiztid .
