Tuomas Grönman     tuomas oskar grönman ( born 22 march 1974 in viitasaari , finland ) is a finnish retired professional ice hockey defenceman . he played 2 seasons in the national hockey league ( nhl ) with the chicago blackhawks and pittsburgh penguins organizations . he also played in the sm-liiga for tps , lukko and jokerit . he won a bronze medal with team finland at the 1998 winter olympics .
