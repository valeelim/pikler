Canine tooth     canine teeth are found in mammals . they are the eye teeth , fangs or dogteeth . they are on either side of the incisors . their main function is to hold food firmly in order to tear it apart , and also attack and defence . in some mammals they are weapons of attack and defence . examples are : wolves and dogs ; the cat family ; walrus . baboons and the great apes have large canines , especially the adult males . in humans they are much reduced , and sit in line with the other teeth .
