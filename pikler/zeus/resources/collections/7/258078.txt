Mike Tomlak     michael richard tomlak ( born october 17 , 1965 in thunder bay , ontario ) is a former professional ice hockey centre who played four seasons for the hartford whalers of the national hockey league . tomlak was drafted 208th overall by the toronto maple leafs in the 1983 nhl entry draft . he played 141 career nhl games , scoring 15 goals and 22 assists for 37 points .
