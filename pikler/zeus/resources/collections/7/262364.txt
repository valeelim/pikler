Tropical Storm Alberto (1994)    tropical storm alberto ( 1994 ) tropical storm alberto was a catastrophic storm in the 1994 atlantic hurricane season . the cyclone was responsible for between 22 and 38 deaths and between $ 500 million and $ 1 billion in destruction ( 1994 usd ) . the storm struck northwest florida , southwest georgia and alabama . in spite of the destruction , the name `` alberto '' was not retired .
