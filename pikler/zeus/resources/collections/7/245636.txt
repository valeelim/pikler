Captain (association football)    captain ( association football ) the team captain of a football team is a team member who is chosen to be the leader during a football match . mostly a captain is an older or more experienced player . a team captain often wears an armband . the captain has to participate in the coin toss before the match and before a penalty shootout . referees mostly talk to the captain about the behaviour of him and his team mates . when the team wins a trophy it is given to the captain .
