Chevrolet Malibu     the chevrolet malibu is a mid-size car that is manufactured by general motors . there have been eight generations of the malibu . the malibu is named after the city in california , malibu , california . the malibu is sold in the united states , canada , mexico , brazil and the middle east . the malibu sucessed the 1997 chevrolet corsica/beretta and was succeeded by the 1983 chevrolet celebrity . the eighth generation malibu is a rebadged daewoo tosca , due to gm phasing out the brand . a ninth generation is being prepared for the 2016 market , it will be slightly larger and have a look similar to the impala . websites . - chevrolet malibu 's official website
