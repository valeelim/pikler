Hypoiodous acid     hypoiodous acid is a chemical compound . it is also an acid . its chemical formula is hio . it has hydrogen and hypoiodite ions in it . hypoiodous acid is very unstable . it easily disproportionates to iodine and iodic acid . it is a disinfectant and oxidizing agent . it is made by reacting mercury ( ii ) oxide with iodine . it is too unstable to be used , although people think that this is what makes iodine a disinfectant . related pages . - iodic acid - periodic acid
