St. Bonaventure University     st. bonaventure university is a private , roman catholic university in allegany , new york . the university has about 2,500 students . it was founded in 1858. the current president of the university is sr. margaret carney , osf std . the athletics teams of the university are called the bonnies . they have 14 varsity teams and they compete in the atlantic 10 conference . other websites . - st. bonaventure university 's official website - st. bonaventure bonnies ' official athletics website
