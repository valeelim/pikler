Natural rights     natural rights are rights that believe it is important for all people or even living being to have out of natural law . these rights are often viewed as unalienable , meaning they can almost never be taken away . the concept of what are natural rights has varied throughout history . the idea first came up in ancient times , but was discussed most famously by english philosopher john locke . locke said that the most important natural rights are `` life , liberty , and property '' . in the united states declaration of independence , the natural rights mentioned are `` life , liberty , and the pursuit of happiness '' . the idea was also found in the declaration of the rights of man . it is viewed by locke , jefferson , and others that the purpose of government is to protect peoples ' natural rights through a social contract .
