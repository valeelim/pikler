Cobalt(II) hydroxide    cobalt ( ii ) hydroxide cobalt ( ii ) hydroxide is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is co ( oh ) . it contains cobalt in its +2 oxidation state as an ion . it also contains hydroxide ions . it is either reddish or bluish . it is made by reacting a strong base with a cobalt ( ii ) salt . cobalt ( ii ) chloride is normally used . this makes the red form . the blue form is made by reacting ammonium hydroxide with cobalt ( ii ) hydroxide . it turns into cobalt ( ii ) oxide when heated . it is used to dry paints , to make other cobalt compounds , and as a catalyst . related pages . - cobalt ( ii ) sulfate - cobalt ( iii ) oxide - cobalt
