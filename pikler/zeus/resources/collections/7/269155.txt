Googie     googie is the term given to a type of architecture and art in the united states in the 20th century . it is also called doo wop , atomic age , or populuxe . it was very futuristic , echoing themes of the 1950s as a whole . some of googie architecture was based on what spaceships look like . it started in california soon after world war ii and spread to the rest of the united states in the 1950s . many of the buildings built in the googie style were coffee shops , bowling alleys and motels . here are a few famous googie buildings : - the space needle - los angeles international airport - the first tomorrowland at disneyland - the first mcdonald 's - many of the early casinos in las vegas
