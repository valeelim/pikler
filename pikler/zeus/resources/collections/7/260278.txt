Chromic acid     chromic acid is a chemical that comes in several forms . it is made by reacting chromates or dichromates with acids or by reacting chromium ( vi ) oxide with sulfuric acid . as its name shows , it is an acid . it is a strong oxidizing agent . when it is reduced , it turns green . it is used to oxidize many chemicals , such as alcohol . it is very toxic because it contains the chromate ion . it is either a reddish solution or red flakes . it is used to make chromyl chloride . other pages . - chromium - chromium ( vi ) oxide - chromium ( iii ) oxide , one of the chemicals it is reduced to
