Mike Krzyzewski     michael william `` mike '' krzyzewski ( , born february , 13 , 1947 ) is an american college basketball coach . he is sometimes referred to as coach k. he was born in chicago and went to school at the united states military academy , where he played guard . he served in the army as a basketball coach , and was later the coach of the u.s. military academy 's basketball team . in 1980 , krzyzewski became the head coach at duke university , where he has been for 31 years . his teams have been to the ncaa tournament 27 times , been to the final four 11 times , and won 4 national championships . krzyzewski has also been the coach of the united states national basketball team since 2006. he was elected to the basketball hall of fame in 2001 .
