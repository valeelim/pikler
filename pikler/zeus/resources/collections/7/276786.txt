Guilder     guilder is the english translation of the dutch gulden , meaning `` golden '' . the guilder used to be a gold coin , but has been a used for silver or metal coins for some centuries . the name is also called florin . the guilder was used most in the netherlands ( as the dutch guilder ) , until it was replaced by the euro on 1 january 2002. the netherlands antillean guilder is currently the only guilder officially in use , though the netherlands no longer accepts the currency in their banks .
