Chow Chow     the chow chow is a breed of dog that originated in china and is believed to be one of the oldest dog breed . it has a large head , a curly tail and a blue tongue . it has a thick coat of fur and can be either rough or smooth . most chow chows are a reddish brown or black color . it stands about 17–20 inches ( 43–51 cm ) and weighs 45–70 pounds ( 20–32 kg ) .
