Cornellà     cornellà de llobregat is a municipality in the comarca of the baix llobregat in catalonia , spain . it is situated on the left of the llobregat river . the history of cornellà de llobregat is defined by three principal factors : its proximity to the city of barcelona , its being an area of passage from the capital of catalonia , and the presence of the llobregat river . its name is of roman origin ( cornelianus ) and the first written reference to the city dates from 980 ad , although the city was incorporated into barcelona 's territory in the thirteenth century .
