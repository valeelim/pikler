Belmont University     belmont university is a private , coeducational university in nashville , tennessee . the university has about 5,936 students . it was founded in 1890. the current president of the university is robert fisher . the athletics teams of the university , called the bruins , mostly play in the ohio valley conference ( ovc ) . the men 's soccer team plays in the horizon league because the ovc does not run a men 's soccer league . other websites . - belmont university 's official website - belmont bruins official athletics website
