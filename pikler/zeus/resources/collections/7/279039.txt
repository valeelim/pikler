International Species Information System     the international species information system ( isis ) is an international non-profit organization that helps zoos and aquariums around the world . isis gives its members software to collect and share information about animals . the isis database has information on 2.4 million animals of 10,000 species . isis members use basic information ( age , sex , parents , place of birth , reports of deaths , etc . ) collected in the isis system to care for and manage their animals . other websites . - official site
