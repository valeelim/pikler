Sirenomelia     sirenomelia , also known as mermaid syndrome , is a very rare disorder in which the legs are stuck together , making them look like a mermaid 's tail . this , however , is as rare as conjoined twins , and only happens to one out of every 100,000 live births . it is usually very bad for the kidney and bladder , and only about four survivors were known to be alive as of july 2003 .
