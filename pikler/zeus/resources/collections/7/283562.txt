Circuit of the Americas     the circuit of the americas is a motor racing circuit in austin , texas . hermann tilke designed the circuit in the middle of 2010. construction was started in early 2011. it is planned to open in 2012. the circuit will run counter-clockwise . the circuit will host the united states grand prix . starting in 2013 , it will host motogp races for 10 years . it will also host a round of v8 supercars , an australia-based touring car racing series , starting in 2013 .
