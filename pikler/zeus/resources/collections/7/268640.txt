Xiaosaurus     xiaosaurus ( means `` dawn lizard ) '' was a small , plant-eating dinosaur . it walked on two long legs , had four-toed feet , five-fingered hands , short arms , a long , stiff , pointed tail , a flexible neck , and a small head with large eyes . this early dinosaur ornithischian lived during the mid-jurassic period , about 169–163 million years ago . it was small and lizard-like , about four feet ( 1.2 m ) long – smaller than an adult human . fossils of this dinosaur were found in china .
