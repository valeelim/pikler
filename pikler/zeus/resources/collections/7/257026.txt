Lawrence, Kansas    lawrence , kansas lawrence is a city in the state of kansas in the united states . it is in the northeastern part of the state , near the kansas city area , and is in douglas county . in 2009 , about 92,000 people lived there . the university of kansas and haskell indian nations university are in lawrence . in 1854 , a group of people who were against slavery started lawrence . ( this was before kansas became a state . kansas did not become a state until january 29 , 1861 . ) many people had moved to kansas because a law called the kansas-nebraska act said that people could vote on whether slavery would be allowed there or not . these people moved there so they could vote . a newspaper was started in 1854 called the `` kansas pioneer '' . the writers of the newspapers wrote about their beliefs that slavery was wrong . on august 21 , 1863 , a man named william quantrill , who wanted slavery to be legal , rode into lawrence with some men and destroyed much of the town . they killed every adult man they saw . more than 150 men died .
