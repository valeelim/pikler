Frigate     a frigate ( ) is a warship . the term has been used for warships of many sizes and roles for a few centuries . during the age of sail they were small , agile ships that were extensively used in the american war of independence , and later against the british . their main use was shore defense . in the modern day the term `` frigate '' covers a broad range of ships . this is primarily for political reasons . the generally accepted term is of a warship less that 6 , 000 dwt and greater that around 2 , 000 dwt . its primary purpose is usually anti-submarine warfare . it can also be a long range patrol vessel or in smaller navies an anti-air vessel .
