Cosmological argument     the cosmological argument is an attempt to prove the existence of god by the fact that things exist . it assumes that things must have a cause , and that the chain of causes can only end by a supernatural event . other names for the argument are argument from universal causation , argument from first cause , causal argument and argument from existence . the universe exists , so there must be something that caused the universe . the first cause is claimed to be god . thomas aquinas said that god is the only thing that was not caused by something else , and that god created the cause of existence . the idea has been popular with many theologians and philosophers . other websites . - theopedia
