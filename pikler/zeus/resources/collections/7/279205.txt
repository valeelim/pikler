Dodge Caravan     the dodge caravan is an american minivan manufactured by fca us , llc . and sold by dodge . the caravan was introduced around 1983 , and grand caravan was introduced around 1987. the original caravan was dropped after the 2007 model year and replaced by the dodge journey . grand caravan has been redesigned many times through the years . chrysler announced that 2015 will be the last model year for the grand caravan , as part of the plan to refocus dodge as a performance brand . however the grand caravan name might make a comeback for a later successor .
