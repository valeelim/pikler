Barry Humphries     john barry humphries , ao , cbe ( born 17 february 1934 ) is an australian comedian , satirist , dadaist , artist , author and character actor . his is probably best known for making up and playing the characters of dame edna everage , a melbourne housewife and `` gigastar '' , and sir les patterson , who is an really rude `` australian diplomat '' . he is also a movie producer and script writer , a star of london 's west end musical theatre , an award-winning writer and a landscape painter . he has been on tv , in movie and on stage . he became popular in austtralia in the 1950s , then britain from the 1960s and finally in america in the 1990s . he has been called the most important comedian since charlie chaplin .
