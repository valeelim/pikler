Montana State University - Bozeman     montana state university – bozeman is a public university in bozeman , montana . the university has about 13,559 students . it was founded in 1893. the current president of the university is waded cruzado . the men 's athletics teams of the university are called the bobcats . they have 15 varsity teams and they compete in the big sky conference . other websites . - montana state university – bozeman 's official website - montana state bobcats ' official athletics website
