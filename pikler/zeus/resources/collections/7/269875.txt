Janenschia     janenschia ( named after werner janensch ) was a huge , plant-eating dinosaur with an elongated neck and tail . it was a sauropod from the late jurassic period , about 156 million to 150 million years ago . `` janenschia '' had a small head , blunt teeth , and a short , thick tail ; it was about 80 feet ( 24 m ) long . this titanosaurid back may have been covered with bony , armored plates . fossils have been found in tanzania , africa .
