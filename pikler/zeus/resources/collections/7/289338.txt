Hurricane Igor     hurricane igor was a destructive tropical cyclone which passed by bermuda and struck the island of newfoundland . this storm was a powerful category four hurricane with winds as high as 155 miles per hour . igor brushed bermuda as a minor hurricane with minimal damage . in newfoundland , however , the storm caused $ 200 million in damage , making igor the most destructive atlantic tropical cyclone on record to strike the region . the name igor was retired and replaced by ian for the 2016 atlantic season .
