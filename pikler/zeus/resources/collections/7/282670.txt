Rajon Rondo     rajon pierre rondo ( born february 22 , 1986 in louisville , kentucky ) is an american basketball player who currently plays for the dallas mavericks of the nba as a point guard . rondo was selected with the 21st overall pick in the first round of the 2006 nba draft by the phoenix suns . rondo played college basketball for the university of kentucky . other websites . - rajon rondo 's nba profile page
