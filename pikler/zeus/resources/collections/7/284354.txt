Lima, Ohio    lima , ohio lima is a city in allen county , ohio , united states . the population of lima was 40,081 in 2000. the mayor of lima is david berger . lima was founded in 1831 as a county seat which was mandated by the ohio legislature . it got it 's name from patrick g. goode who was a judge . he insisted on the spanish pronunciation `` lee-mah '' after the capital city of peru , but the pronunciation — `` lye-mah '' — won . professional wrestler , al snow was born in lima . actor and movie critic donald richie was also born here . other websites . - the city hall of lima
