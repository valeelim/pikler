Hydrogen telluride     hydrogen telluride , also known as hydrotelluric acid , tellane , or tellurium hydride , is a chemical compound . it is also an acid . its chemical formula is hte . it contains hydrogen and telluride ions . hydrogen telluride is a toxic reactive gas . it easily decomposes to hydrogen and tellurium . it also burns in air to make tellurium dioxide and water . it has a very bad smell of decayed garlic . it is almost as acidic as phosphoric acid . it reacts with some metals and metal oxides to make tellurides . it is made by adding a strong acid to a telluride , such as sodium telluride . hydrogen telluride does not have many uses because it is very toxic and unstable . related pages . - aluminium telluride - sodium tellurite
