Prohibition Era     the prohibition era was a period in united states history when alcohol was outlawed . police would arrest anyone who was found making or selling alcohol illegally . the women 's christian temperance union and other reformist organizations agitated for this change . prohibition lasted from 1920 to 1933. it was established by the eighteenth amendment to the united states constitution . it was ratified ( approved by the states ) by january 16 , 1919. it came into effect at january 16 , 1920. prohibition was good as the consumption of alcohol was reduced , but it was bad in that the mafia and other underground organizations took up rum-running . they became big and powerful and made trouble . prohibition was repealed december 5 , 1933 by the twenty-first amendment . now states can regulate the selling of liquor by themselves .
