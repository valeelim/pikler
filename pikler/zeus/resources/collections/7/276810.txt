Ice lolly     an ice lolly , ice lollipop , or ice pop , is a frozen snack of flavoured ice . it is made by freezing flavoured liquid ( such as fruit juice ) around a wooden stick . once the liquid freezes solid , the stick can be used as a handle to hold the ice pop . in the united states , the snack also referred to as a popsicle after the brand . term usage in uk , usa and canada the term ice lolly is used primarily in the united kingdom , with variations used in other english speaking countries . in the united states and canada the same frozen snack is often termed a 'popcicle ' or 'ice pop ' . the term has also been used as branding for the tourism website ice lolly .
