Dan Hamhuis     daniel hamhuis ( born december 13 , 1982 in smithers , british columbia , canada ) is a canadian professional ice hockey defencemen . he currently plays for the vancouver canucks of the national hockey league ( nhl ) . hamhuis formerly played for the nashville predators for 6 seasons . hamhuis played in the whl for the prince george cougars for 4 seasons and played for the milwaukee admirals in the ahl .
