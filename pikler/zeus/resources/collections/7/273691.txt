New Mexico State University     new mexico state university is a four-year public university in the united states . it has five campuses , but its main campus is in las cruces , new mexico . in 2010 , it had about 18,000 undergraduate students . the school started in 1888. it was first called the las cruces college . soon after , it changed its name to new mexico college of agriculture and mechanical arts . in 1960 , its name was changed to new mexico state university . new mexico state university 's sports teams are called the aggies . other websites . - new mexico state university
