Varkala     varkala ( ) is a city in the indian state of kerala . it is on the west coast of india near the far south of the mainland . it is the only region in the southern kerala that has cliffs . geologists call these cliffs the varkala formation . varkala is a famous tourist place . the town has great telecommunication facilities , an average-rated water supply system , fire station , several post offices and a police station . the town has a government-run modern medicine , ayurveda , and nature cure hospital . also there are about 10 private hospitals , clinics and some dental clinics .
