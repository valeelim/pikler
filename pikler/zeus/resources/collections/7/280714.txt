Samuel Dalembert     samuel davis dalembert ( born may 10 , 1981 ) is an haitian-canadian basketball player that currently plays for the milwaukee bucks of the nba . he has also played for the philadelphia 76ers from 2001 to 2010 , the sacramento kings from 2010 to 2011 and the houston rockets from 2011 to 2012. he played college basketball at seton hall university . dalembert pledged $ 100,000 to unicef and traveled to haiti to help with the relief efforts . he won the 2009-10 j. walter kennedy citizenship award for helping with the relief efforts in haiti . other websites . - samuel dalembert 's nba profile
