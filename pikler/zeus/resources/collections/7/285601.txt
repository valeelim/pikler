Shawn Hernandez     shawn hernandez ( born february 11 , 1973 in houston , texas ) is an american professional wrestler who is better known by the ring name , hernandez and is currently signed to total nonstop action wrestling . hernandez is also known for forming the tag team stable , the latin american xchange with homicide which ended when he was betrayed by homicide and was attacked by him on the september 10 edition of tna impact ! . he has been a two time nwa world tag team champion with homicide and a two time tna world tag team champion with homicide ( once ) and matt morgan ( once ) . he also won the deuces wild tournament in 2008 with homicide . his finishing move is called the `` border toss '' which is a throwing crucifix powerbomb . other websites . - shawn hernandez 's official website - shawn hernandez 's tna profile
