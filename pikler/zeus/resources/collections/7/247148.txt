Neoclassicism     neoclassicism is the name given to movements in the arts that draw upon western classical art and culture ( usually that of ancient greece or ancient rome ) . traditionally , classicism is about the art made in antiquity ( very long ago , in ancient times ) or later art inspired by that of antiquity . but neoclassicism is always about the art made later but inspired by antiquity . so , classicism and neoclassicism are often used together . it often means clearness , elegance , harmony , and rest made by careful attention to traditional forms . examples of neoclassicism in architecture include the white house and neue wache . other websites . - *
