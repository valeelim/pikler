Daniel Sedin     daniel sedin ( born september 26 , 1980 ) is a swedish professional ice hockey winger with the vancouver canucks of the national hockey league ( nhl ) , and serves as an alternate captain for the canucks during home games . his identical twin brother henrik also plays for the canucks , and is the team captain . born and raised in örnsköldsvik , sweden , they have played together throughout their careers ; the pair are known for their effectiveness playing off one another . daniel is known as a goal-scorer , while henrik is known as a playmaker .
