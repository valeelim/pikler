Slav defence     the slav defence is an important reply to 1d4 . it is popular with top players . the slav usually begins : other fourth moves for white are 4.e3 , 4.cxd5 and 4.qb3 . now 4 ... dxc4 is the slav : this line was explored in two world championship matches between alekhine and euwe in 1935 and 1937. sometimes called the botvinnik variation . references . - matthew sadler 1997 . `` the slav '' . chess press , brighton - matthew sadler 1998 . `` the semi-slav '' . chess press , brighton .
