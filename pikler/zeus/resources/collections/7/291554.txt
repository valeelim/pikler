How Do You Know (movie)    how do you know ( movie ) how do you know is a 2010 american romantic comedy-drama movie . it was produced by julie ansell , james l. brooks and paula weinstein and was written and directed by james l. brooks . `` how do you know '' was released on december 17 , 2010 in north america . the movie received negative reviews with a 30 % rating on rotten tomatoes and a 47 out of 100 from metacritic . the movie also did poorly at the box office with only $ 7.6 million made in the united states and canada , which made it eighth in the box office at its first weekend . it fell off the chart by its third weekend . the movie only grossed a total of $ 47,707,151 worldwide , which failed to recoup for its $ 120 million budget and as a result , made the movie a massive box-office bomb . cast . - reese witherspoon as lisa jorgenson - paul rudd as george madison - owen wilson as matty reynolds - jack nicholson as charles madison - dean norris as tom - kathryn hahn as annie - yuki matsuzaki as tori - andrew wilson as matty 's teammate
