It's On (Dr. Dre) 187um Killa    it 's on ( dr. dre ) 187um killa it 's on ( < del > dr . dre < /del > ) 187 killa is the second ep by american rapper , eazy-e. it was released on october 25 , 1993. the producers of the album were eazy-e , dj yella , rhythum d , madness 4 real , dr. jam and cold 187um . the ep was certified 2x platinum in late 1994. the album is a reply to then rival/former n.w.a bandmate dr dre . the songs `` real muthaphuckkin g 's '' and `` it 's on '' are diss songs meant to insult dr dre and his then new protegee snoop doggy dogg . other tracks such as `` down 2 tha last roach '' , `` any last werdz '' , `` still a nigga '' and `` exxtra special thankz '' are about the gangster lifestyle . the song `` gim me that nutt '' tells a rather sexually vulgar story that was banned from being played on radio stations .
