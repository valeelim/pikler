I Lost on Jeopardy     `` i lost on jeopardy '' is a song by `` weird al '' yankovic . it is a parody of `` jeopardy '' by the greg kihn band . it was about the singer 's experience losing on the game show `` jeopardy ! '' . it was released for the album `` weird al '' yankovic in 3-d '' . the music video featured yankovic and original `` jeopardy ! '' host art fleming and announcer don pardo . it also featured `` jeopardy '' artist kihn and yankovic 's mentor dr. demento .
