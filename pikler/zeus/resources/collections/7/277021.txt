You Don't Love Me Anymore    you do n't love me anymore `` you do n't love me anymore '' is an original song by `` weird al '' yankovic . it was released for `` off the deep end '' in 1992. it is written as a soft ballad , but describes yankovic and an unnamed girl 's relationship . the couple were in love , but the girl has tried to kill yankovic many times . she had done things like poison his coffee , leaving him for dead in a ditch , and put a cobra in his underwear drawer . the humor in this song is the lyrics and the delivery .
