Boraginaceae     boraginaceae is a family of around 2000 species , occurring mainly in europe and asia , especially in the mediterranean region . most of them are herbs , although there are some woody plants . most of them are grown as ornamental plants , although some are a source of dye or have medicinal uses . the most well-known members of the family include forget-me-not ( `` myosotis '' ) , heliotrope ( `` heliotropium '' ) , the comfreys ( `` symphytum '' ) , borage ( `` borago '' ) , and hound 's tongue ( `` cynoglossum '' ) .
