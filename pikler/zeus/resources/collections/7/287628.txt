Brett DiBiase     brett dibiase ( born march 16 , 1988 ) is an american professional wrestler . he currently has a contract with world wrestling entertainment . he wrestles in their farm team in florida championship wrestling , where he gains experience before going national . he was a fcw florida tag team championship . dibiase 's stepgrandfather michael dibiase , his grandmother helen hild , and his father ted dibiase were professional wrestlers . his older half brother mike dibiase ii and his older brother ted dibiase , jr. are also professional wrestlers . on march 27 , 2010 , dibiase and his brother ted jr. induct their father into the wwe hall of fame . on june 9 , 2009 , dibiase married his high school sweetheart leah nicole may . other websites . - florida championship wrestling profile - online world of wrestling profile
