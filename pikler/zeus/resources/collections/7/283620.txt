Bulletproof (La Roux song)    bulletproof ( la roux song ) bulletproof is a 2009 song by the english electropop duo la roux off their self-titled album . it was released in the united kingdom in june 2009 and was highly received by the critics . this song debuted at # 1 on the uk singles chart . it was a sleeper hit in the united states , peaking at number eight .
