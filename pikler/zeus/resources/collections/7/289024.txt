Merritt, British Columbia    merritt , british columbia merritt is a canadian city in british columbia , canada . marritt is in the south-central interior of british columbia and also in the nicola valley . as of 2006 , marritt had 6,998 people . the current mayor of merritt is susan roline . merritt is home to a bchl ( british columbia hockey league ) team called the merritt centennials which is the oldest ice hockey team in the league . in 2008 , the country music hall of fame relocated from calgary to merritt . other websites . - city of merritt 's official website
