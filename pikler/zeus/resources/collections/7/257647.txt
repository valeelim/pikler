Armor for Sleep     armor for sleep was an american alternative rock band . they formed in 2001 in new jersey and disbanded in 2009 because of being inactive and not undertaking a tour for over a year . the members of the group were ben jorgensen , the singer , who also played the rhythm guitar , pj decicco , who played the guitar , anthony diionno , who played the bass guitar and nash breen , who played the guitar . discography . studio albums . - 2003 : `` dream to make believe '' - 2005 : `` what to do when you are dead '' - 2007 : `` smile for them '' eps . - 2008 : `` the way out is broken '' other websites . - armor for sleep 's official website
