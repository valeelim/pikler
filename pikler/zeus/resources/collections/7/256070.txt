The Flash     the flash is the name of several popular comic book characters from the dc comics universe . all versions of the flash have the ability to travel at a very high speed and defy the laws of physics . jay garrick was the first character to take on the alter-ego `` the flash '' . barry allen is currently the youngest flash . he calls himself `` kid flash '' . other characters who have been the flash are barry allen and wally west . ezra miller played the flash in `` '' and will again in `` justice league '' ( 2017 ) .
