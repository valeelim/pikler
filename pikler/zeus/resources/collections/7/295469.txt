Douglas da Silva     douglas da silva ( born 7 march 1984 in florianópolis , brazil ) is a brazilian football player who is playing for f.c . red bull salzburg in austria as defender . 2005 he went from brazil to israel , where he started playing for hapoel kfar saba and soon became one of the best defenders in the league . after four years with this club he went one to an israelian top club hapoel tel aviv , because his old club was relegated to the second league . with tel aviv he became cup winner and champion in 2010. they also played in the uefa champions league 2010/11 after eliminating red bull salzburg in the play off round . he played in the champions league group stage , but in december 2010 he signed with f.c . red bull salzburg.in a preliminary match he was injured badly , so he missed the whole second part of the 2010/11 season . honours . - israel state cup ( 1 ) : - 2010 - israeli premier league ( 1 ) : - 2009–10
