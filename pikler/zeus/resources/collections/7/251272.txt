HiT Entertainment     hit entertainment is a british-american tv entertainment company owned by mattel . they have tv characters such as angelina ballerina , barney & friends , bob the builder , fifi and the flowertots , mopatop 's shop , roary the racing car , fireman sam , fraggle rock , kipper , pingu , rainbow magic and thomas & friends . hit movies is a division of hit entertainment . they have movies for the whole family to enjoy , such as film franchises based on thomas & friends , bob the builder , and new characters and stories . hit entertainment was founded in 1989. it has headquarters in london , united kingdom & new york city , united states .
