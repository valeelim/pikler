Zinc nitrate     zinc nitrate is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is zn ( no ) . it contains zinc and nitrate ions . zinc nitrate is a colorless solid . it dissolves in water . it reacts with bases to make zinc hydroxide . it reacts with sodium carbonate to make zinc carbonate . it can be made by reacting zinc metal or zinc oxide with nitric acid . it can be used as a mordant . it is also used as a source of zinc ions . related pages . - zinc sulfate - zinc sulfide
