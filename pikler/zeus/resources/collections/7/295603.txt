Fernando Llorente     fernando llorente ( born 26 february 1985 in pamplona ) is a spanish footballer who plays for athletic bilbao and the spanish national team , as a striker . he won the fifa world cup with the spanish national team . llorente has scored 90 goals in his career . he said no to offers from important teams such as real madrid . he has been selected by the spanish national team 21 times and has scored 7 goals .
