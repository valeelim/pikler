Flag of Slovenia     the national flag of slovenia has three equal horizontal bands of white ( top ) , blue , and red , with the slovenian coat of arms on the upper left side of the flag centered in the white and blue bands . the coat of arms is a shield with the image of mount triglav , slovenia 's highest mountain , in white against a blue background ; below it are two blue lines that stand for the adriatic sea and local rivers , and above it are three six-pointed stars arranged in an triangle which are taken from the coat of arms of the counts of celje , an important slovenian family .
