Nate Marquardt     nathan joel marquardt ( born april 20 , 1979 in lander , wyoming ) is an american mixed martial artist . he currently fights for the ufc in the middleweight division and fights out of denver , colorado . marquardt holds a second black belt in both brazilian jiu-jitsu and jujutsu . his current record is 32 wins , 10 losses and 2 draws . other websites . - nate marquardt 's official website
