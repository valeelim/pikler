Werner Arber     werner arber ( born 3 june 1929 ) is a swiss microbiologist and geneticist . he won the nobel prize in physiology or medicine in 1978 for his discovery of restriction endonucleases which he shared with daniel nathans and hamilton o. smith . arber as one of 20 nobel laureates who signed the `` stockholm memorandum '' at the 3rd nobel laureate symposium on global sustainability in stockholm , sweden on 18 may 2011 .
