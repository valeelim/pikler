Gaur     the gaur is a bovine species , similar to the bison . bos gaurus ( or indian bison ) is the largest living bovine . it is native to south asia and southeast asia . the species is listed as vulnerable on the iucn red list since 1986. population trends are stable in well-protected areas , and are building up in a few areas which had been neglected . the gaur is the tallest species of wild cattle . the malayan gaur is called `` seladang '' , and the burmese gaur is called `` pyoung '' . the domesticated form of the gaur `` bos frontalis '' is called 'gayal ' or 'mithun ' .
