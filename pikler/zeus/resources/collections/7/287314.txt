Go West     go west is an english pop duo . they were formed in 1982 by lead singer and drummer peter cox ( born 17 november 1955 , kingston upon thames , surrey , england ) and guitarist and singer richard drummie ( born 20 march 1959 , twickenham , middlesex , england ) . while they have had many successful singles in the united kingdom , they are generally best known in the united states for their hits `` king of wishful thinking '' and `` faithful '' . on 16 october 2009 , cox was announced as the new lead singer of manfred mann 's earth band .
