2011–12 Juventus F.C. season    2011–12 juventus f.c . season the 2011–12 season is juventus football club 's 114th in serie a. juventus starts the season with a new manager , antonio conte . on june 15 , former juventus director manager luciano moggi has been banned for life from all italian football . on july 8 , juventus start pre-season training in turin . on october , president andrea agnelli said that alessandro del piero will leave juventus at the end of the season .
