Valerie Harper     valerie harper ( b. august 22 , 1939 ) is an american movie and television actress . she is best known for playing rhoda morgenstern on `` the mary tyler moore show '' and starring in the sitcom `` valerie '' . she also does stage acting . she began her career as a dancer and chorus girl on broadway in the late 1950s and early 1960s . she was born in suffern , new york . harper is wendy schaal 's former step-mother . she was married to richard schaal from 1964 until they divorced in 1978. she has been married to tony cacciotti since 1987. on march 11 , 2013 , harper announced that she has an incurable brain tumor . doctors say that she has less than three months to live . although the disease is incurable , her doctors said they were treating her with chemotherapy in an effort to slow its progress . on july 30 , 2015 , harper was hospitalized in maine after falling unconscious . later on the 30th she was taken via medevac to a larger hospital for further treatment . she was later released . other websites .
