Liam Gallagher     william john paul `` liam '' gallagher ( born 21 september 1972 ) is an english musician and songwriter . gallagher is best known as a singer in the rock band oasis . the band split up in 2009. gallagher formed a new band called beady eye . former oasis members gem archer , andy bell and chris sharrock were also in the new band . beady eye disbanded in 2014. in 2010 gallagher was selected as `` best frontman '' by reader 's of q magazine .
