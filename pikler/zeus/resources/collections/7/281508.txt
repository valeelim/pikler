Ibis     the ibises ( collective plural ibis ; classical plurals ibides and ibes ) are a group of long-legged wading birds in the family threskiornithidae . they all have long down curved bills , and usually feed as a group , probing mud for food items , usually crustaceans . most species nest in trees , often with spoonbills or herons . the word ibis comes from greek and latin , and probably from the ancient egyptian . according to josephus , moses employed ibes against serpents during a desert campaign into ethiopia in his early life . pliny the elder also recounted , â€œ the egyptians invoked ibes against the serpents '' .
