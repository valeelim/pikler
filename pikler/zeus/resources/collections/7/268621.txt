Wheat flour     wheat flour is a type of flour made from wheat kernels . there are two main types of wheat flour : whole wheat flour and white flour . whole wheat flour contains the whole grain of wheat , making it more nutritious . white flour only contains the endosperm of the wheat grain , making it less nutritious . white flour is preferred by many people . white flours are normally enriched . wheat flour can be sifted to make it finer and smoother .
