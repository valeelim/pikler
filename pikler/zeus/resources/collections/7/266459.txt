Selenide     selenide is an ion . its chemical formula is se . selenides are similar to sulfides . selenides are reducing agents when pure . they are stronger reducing agents than sulfides . selenide ores are the source of selenium . selenides are normally mixed with sulfides . the selenide ion is unstable except in very basic conditions . normally hydroselenide is made , which is hse . in acidic conditions , hydrogen selenide gas is made . related pages . - selenite - selenate
