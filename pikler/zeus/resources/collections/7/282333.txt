Alejandro (song)    alejandro ( song ) `` alejandro '' is a single from lady gaga 's album `` the fame monster '' . the single is composed as a midtempo ( medium speed ) and europop-influenced song with an uplifting melody . critics portrayed the song as heavily influenced by pop groups such as ace of base . they gave this song mostly positive reviews . this was the seventh song by lady gaga to hit the top ten on the `` billboard '' hot 100. the song was released in 2010. it reached the top five in the us and canada , while reaching the top ten in the uk .
