Ball State University     ball state university is a public , coeducational university in muncie , indiana . the university has about 21,401 students . it was founded in 1918. the current president of the university is jo ann m. gora . the athletics teams of the university are called the cardinals . they have 18 varsity teams and they compete in the mid-american conference . other websites . - ball state university 's official website - ball state cardinals ' official athletics website
