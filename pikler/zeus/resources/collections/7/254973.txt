Botanical garden     a botanical garden is generally a well taken care of park which contains many different kinds of plants labeled with their botanical names . they can contain collections of special types of plants , such as cacti , herb gardens , plants of certain parts of the world . some of these collections need to be kept in greenhouses , such as tropical plants , alpine plants or other exotic plants . in botanic gardens , seeds or cuttings are collected from species in the wild and then used to build up a population of plants from which , one day , some plants may be reintroduced to their natural habitats . tissue culture can often help to produce many plants from just a few specimens .
