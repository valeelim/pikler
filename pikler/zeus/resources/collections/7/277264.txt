Gata Kamsky     gata kamsky ( born june 2 , 1974 ) is a soviet-born american chess grandmaster , and the current world rapid chess champion . he is rated # 2 in the united states . kamsky was a prodigy who reached the final of the fide world chess championship 1996 at the age of 22 , and reached a ranking of fourth in the world in 1996. he played almost no fide-rated games between 1997 and late 2004. he was studying medicine at the time . kamsky won the chess world cup 2007. this earned him a match against veselin topalov for the right to challenge for the world chess championship 2010 , which he lost . kamsky also played in the candidates tournament to determine the challenger for the world chess championship 2012. he advanced to the semifinals before losing to boris gelfand .
