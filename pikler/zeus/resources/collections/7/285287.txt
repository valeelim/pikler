American Gangster (movie)    american gangster ( movie ) american gangster is a 2007 american biographical crime drama movie . it is set in new york city , new jersey , thailand and vietnam . denzel washington stars as real life african american drug smuggler frank lucas . russell crowe stars as the detective determined to stop the drug empire controlled by lucas . the movie was released in november 2007. despite being set in the late 1960s and the 1970s , the movie has many anachronisms .
