Namco Museum Remix     namco museum remix is a video game released for the wii . this game features 14 classic arcade games which are `` cutie q '' , `` dig dug '' , `` galaxian '' , `` gaplus '' , `` mappy '' , `` pac & pal '' , `` pac-mania '' , `` super pac-man '' , and `` xevious '' . there are a few remixed versions of games which are `` pac'n roll '' , `` galaga '' , `` motos '' , `` rally x '' , and `` gator panic '' .
