National Recovery Administration     the national recovery administration was an agency in the government of the united states in the 1930s , during the new deal . it was formed by the national recovery act during the first `` hundred days '' of president franklin d. roosevelt . it regulated the economy . it was the first agencies that could control minimum wages . it was headed by hugh s. johnson , a former general . the nra lasted until 1935 , when the united states supreme court said it was unconstitutional .
