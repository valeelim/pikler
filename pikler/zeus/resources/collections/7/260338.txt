Life After Life     life after life is a 1975 book written by psychiatrist raymond moody . the book presents the author 's account of what it is like to die . `` life after life '' sold more than 13 million copies and became an international best seller which made the subject of near death experiences popular , and opened the way for many other studies . moody wrote a second book , `` reflections on life after life '' , in 1977. related pages . - bruce greyson - ian stevenson - parapsychology - life before life
