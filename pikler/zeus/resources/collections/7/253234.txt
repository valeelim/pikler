Okutama, Tokyo    okutama , tokyo okutama has an area of 225.63 km² . mount kumotori , tokyo 's highest peak at 2017 m , divides okutama from the okuchichibu region of the neighboring saitama and yamanashi prefectures . tokyo 's northernmost and westernmost points lie in okutama , as does lake okutama , an important source of water for tokyo , located above the ogōchi dam in the town . other websites . - okutama website ;
