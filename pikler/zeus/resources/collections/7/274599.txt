Robert Bortuzzo     robert bortuzzo ( born 18 march 1989 ) is a canadian ice hockey defenceman . he currently plays for the st. louis blues of the national hockey league ( nhl ) . bortuzzo was picked up by the kitchener rangers of the ontario hockey league in 2006 after leading the fort william north stars of the superior international junior hockey league to a league championship , a dudley hewitt cup , and an overtime away from playing in the 2006 royal bank cup final . bortuzzo was drafted by the pittsburgh penguins in the 3rd round ( 78th overall ) of the 2007 nhl entry draft . he played junior hockey in the ontario hockey league for the kitchener rangers . in his three years with the rangers he recorded 49 points and 152 penalty minutes in 138 games . bortuzzo won the hamilton spectator trophy in his second year as a member of the 2007â€“08 kitchener rangers .
