Love the Way You Lie     `` love the way you lie '' is a 2010 hip hop song by the american rapper eminem and featured barbadian singer rihanna , and is the second single from his seventh studio album `` recovery '' , which was released in 2010. the song was written by eminem , a. grant , h. hafferman , m. riddick . h. hafferman is holly brook hafermann who now writes and records as skylar grey . the song 's music video stars megan fox and dominic monaghan as a couple in a violent abusive love-hate relationship .
