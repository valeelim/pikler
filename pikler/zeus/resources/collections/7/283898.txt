All My Children     all my children was an american soap opera set in a fictional suburb of philadelphia . susan lucci plays erica kane . other famous people have starred , including sarah michelle gellar . the show aired on abc for 43 years since its beginning until september 23 , 2011 when cancellation was announced on april 2011. it was canceled on april 14 , 2011 along with `` one life to live '' due to low ratings and production costs . another series was shown in 2013 .
