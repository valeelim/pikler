Tiffany (Korean singer)    tiffany ( korean singer ) stephanie hwang ( korean name : hwang mi-young ; hangul : 황미영 ; hanja : 黃美英 ; born august 1 , 1989 ) , best known simply as tiffany , is an american singer and dancer . on august 15th , 2016 , she was heavily criticized by korean social media because she posted a confederate japanese flag on the korean liberation day .
