Simone Rethel     simone rethel ( born 1949 in germany ) is a german actress , painter and photographer . she is the widow of actor and singer johannes heesters . rethel came from a large , artistic family . her grandfather was a painter . rethel married heesters , 46 years her senior , in 1991. in december 2010 , the 107-year-old heesters said that he had quit smoking for his then 61-year-old wife : `` she should have me as long as possible .
