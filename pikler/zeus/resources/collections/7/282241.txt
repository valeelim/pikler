Ramekin     a ramekin or is a small glazed ceramic or glass serving bowl used for preparating and serving various food dishes . the word is from french ( as `` ramequin '' ) , and before that middle dutch and middle low german . ramekins usually have a volume of , and are dishes commonly used for serving a variety of dishes such as crème brûlée , french onion soup , molten chocolate cake , moimoi , cheese or egg dishes , poi , potted shrimps , ice cream , soufflé , baked `` cocottes '' , crumbles , or scallops or used to serve side garnishes and condiments alongside an entree . traditionally circular with a fluted exterior , ramekins can also be found in novelty shapes , such as flowers , hearts or stars . ramekins are built to withstand high temperatures , as they are often used in ovens , or in the case of crème brûlée , exposed to the flame of a cooking torch .
