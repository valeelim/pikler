Lead(II,IV) oxide    lead ( ii , iv ) oxide lead ( ii , iv ) oxide , also known as lead tetroxide , minium , red lead , or triplumbic tetroxide , is a chemical compound . it contains some lead atoms in the +2 oxidation state and some in the +4 oxidation state . it is used in batteries , paints , and glass . it dissolves in acids . it is made by heating lead ( ii ) oxide in air . it is toxic because it contains lead . its formula is pb3o4 . it is commonly said `` sandur '' . related pages . - lead ( iv ) oxide - lead ( ii ) oxide - lead oxide
