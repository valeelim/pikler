John Bercow     john simon bercow ( surname pronounce `` ber-coe '' ; born 19 january 1963 in edgware , middlesex , england ) is a british politician who is the speaker of the house of commons of the united kingdom . he is the member of parliament ( mp ) for the constituency of buckingham . john bercow was active in the conservative party from a young age and was a conservative councillor , for lambeth , from 1986 to 1990. he stood as a candidate to become an mp at the 1987 and 1992 general elections but was not successful . at the 1997 general election , bercow was elected the conservative mp for buckingham . he served in the shadow cabinets of iain duncan smith and michael howard . after the speaker michael martin resigned on 22 june 2009 , bercow was elected as the speaker . bercow is the first jewish speaker . he is the first speaker not to wear traditional court robes while presiding over the house of commons . but , along with tradition , bercow does now display his coat of arms at speaker 's house .
