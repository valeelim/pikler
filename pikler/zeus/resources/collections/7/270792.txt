Nick Young     nick young ( born june 1 , 1985 in los angeles , california ) is an american basketball player that currently plays for the los angeles lakers of the nba . he was selected by the washington wizards with the 16th pick in the 1st round of the 2007 nba draft . young played four seasons with the wizards before being traded to the los angeles clippers for a future second-round pick on march 15th , 2012. he went to university of southern california for college and played basketball with the usc trojans . other websites . - nick young 's nba profile
