Jeanne Moreau     jeanne moreau ( 23 january 1928 – 31 july 2017 ) was a french actress particularly associated with the french `` new wave '' cinema . moreau was born in paris , france , the daughter of an english mother , a dancer at the folies bergères , and a french father , a restaurateur . she studied at the conservatoire de paris . she began her career as a stage actress and joined the celebrated `` comédie-française '' in 1948 , aged only 20 , one the youngest ever member of the company . during the same time , she was in small roles in movies . she got her first major break when she met french director louis malle . he cast her in his movies `` l'ascenceur pour l'échafaud '' ( 1957 ) and `` les amants '' ( 1958 ) , which established her as a movie star in france . she also appeared in roger vadim 's `` les liaisons dangereuses '' ( 1959 ) and peter brooks 's `` moderato cantabile '' ( 1960 ) , but it is with françois truffaut 's iconic movie `` jules et jim '' ( 1961 ) that she became an international star . she then went on to work with directors such as michelangelo antonioni ( `` la notte '' ) , orson welles ( `` le procès '' ) , luis bunuel ( `` the diary of a chambermaid '' ) , etc . in the 1970s , she attempted directing with `` lumières '' ( 1976 ) and `` l'adolescente '' ( 1979 ) with mixed success .
