Northern Arizona University     northern arizona university is a public university in flagstaff , arizona . the university has about 22,204 students . it was founded in 1899. the current president of the university is john d. haeger . the men 's athletics teams of the university are called the lumberjacks . they have 15 varsity teams and they compete in the big sky conference . other websites . - northern arizona university 's official website - northern arizona lumberjacks ' official athletics website
