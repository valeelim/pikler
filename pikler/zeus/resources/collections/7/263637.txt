Hypomanganate     hypomanganate is an ion . its chemical formula is mno . it is bright blue . it disproportionates into manganates and manganese ( iv ) oxide easily . this can be stopped by adding a lot of a base . they are both reducing agents and oxidizing agents . they are made by reducing manganates with sulfites and hydrogen peroxide . related pages . - permanganate - potassium hypomanganate - potassium manganate
