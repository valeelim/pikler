Brigadier general     brigadier general is a rank in the army . he is normally in command of a brigade , about 3,000 men . it is between the rank of major general and colonel . in the united states , brigadier general is also a rank in the air force and marines and is also called o-7 . brigadier in the british army is of-6 , and also in nato . the difference is that nato classes both first and second lieutenants as of-1 .
