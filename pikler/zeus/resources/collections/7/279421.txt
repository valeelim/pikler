Honda Odyssey     the honda odyssey is a minivan that has been made by honda since 1994. from 1994-1999 it was also sold as the isuzu oasis . a larger version of the minivan has been available since 1999 in north america . it was redesigned in 2005 and 2011. the 2015 model was awarded the insurance institute for highway safety 's top rated pick because of it 's collision avoidance system , hardly any injuries .
