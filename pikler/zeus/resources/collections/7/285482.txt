Eared dove     the eared dove , zenaida auriculata , is a new world tropical dove . it is seen throughout south america from colombia to southern argentina and chile , and on the offshore islands from the grenadines southwards . it appears to be partly migratory , but it may only be when it needs more food . when it lays its eggs , it builds a small stick nest several meters up in a tree . it is a close relative of the north american mourning dove .
