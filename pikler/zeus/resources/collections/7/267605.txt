Tellurate     tellurate is an ion . its chemical formula is teo or teo . chemical compounds having the tellurate ion in them are called tellurates . tellurates are strong oxidizing agents . they are similar to selenates . tellurates react with acids to make telluric acid . an example is sodium tellurate ( nateo ) . it is made by reacting telluric acid with sodium hydroxide . it can also be made by oxidizing sodium tellurite . it is similar to sodium selenate . related pages . - tellurite - telluride - tellurium
