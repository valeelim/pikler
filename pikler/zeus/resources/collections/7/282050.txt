Fran Drescher     francine joy `` fran '' drescher ( born september 30 , 1957 in queens , new york ) is an american television , movie , musical theatre and voice actress , television director and producer , comedian , screenwriter , model , health , lgbt rights and political activist . she is of jewish descent . her acting career began around 1977 when she acted in the movie `` saturday night fever '' . she is best known for playing fran fine from the sitcom `` the nanny '' . drescher wrote her memoirs and is a universal life church minister .
