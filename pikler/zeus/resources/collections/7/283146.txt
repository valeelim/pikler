Buddh International Circuit     the buddh international circuit ( or bic ) is a track ( circuit ) that had opened in 2011 , which is used for motor racing . the main event is the indian grand prix , the first to be held in that country . the track was designed by hermann tilke , the fia 's most commonly used track designer . the track was originally known as the jaypee group circuit , after the owners , the jaypee group . the track was renamed in april 11 , 2011
