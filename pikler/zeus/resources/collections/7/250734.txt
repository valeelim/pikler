Alfred Macalik     alfred ( alfréd ) macalik ( 10 september 1888 in vienna - 20 september 1979 in oradea ) was an austrian-romanian landscape painter , sculptor and composer . at the suggestion of his former art teacher , szőke gyula , he studied art at the art university in craiova ( 1910-1911 ) . later macalik studied at the art university in budapest ( 1914-1918 ) . macalik influenced drastic the cultural life in oradea .
