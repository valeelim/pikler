One Sweet Day     `` one sweet day '' is a song by american singer mariah carey . it is a duet with american boy-group boyz ii men . the song was written by mariah carey , walter afanasieff and the members of boyz ii men . `` one sweet day '' was produced by carey and afanasieff for her sixth album `` daydream '' . it was released as the album 's second single in late 1995. the song speaks about losing a loved one , and one day seeing them in heaven once again . both carey and boyz ii men wrote the song about specific people in their lives . `` one sweet day '' was positively reviewed by music critcs . the song became a hit in many countries around the world , especially in the united states . it spent sixteen weeks at number one on the `` billboard '' hot 100. it also became number one in canada and new zealand , and reached the top-ten in australia , belgium , france , ireland , the netherlands , norway , sweden and the united kingdom . the music video for `` one sweet day '' featured clips of carey and boyz ii men in the studio recording the song .
