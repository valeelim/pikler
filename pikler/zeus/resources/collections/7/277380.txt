Brad Maxwell     brad robert maxwell ( born july 8 , 1957 in brandon , manitoba ) is a canadian retired professional ice hockey defenceman . during his junior career he won the memorial cup in 1977 as a member of the new westminster bruins . following that season maxwell was selected seventh overall in the 1977 nhl amateur draft by the minnesota north stars . he spent time playing for minnesota , the quebec nordiques , the toronto maple leafs , the vancouver canucks and the new york rangers before retiring in 1987 .
