Bobby Brown     robert barisford brown ( born february 5 , 1969 ) is an american r & b singer-songwriter and a member of new edition . he left the group in 1986 and has many hits , including `` do n't be cruel '' , `` every little step '' and `` roni '' . brown is an african-american and was born in roxbury , boston , massachusetts . brown married whitney houston in 1992. they had a daughter , bobbi kristina houston brown , in 1993. the couple separated in 2006 ; houston divorced him in 2007. brown has been diagnosed with attention-deficit hyperactivity disorder and bipolar disorder .
