Mercury(II) bromide    mercury ( ii ) bromide mercury ( ii ) bromide , also known as mercuric bromide , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is hgbr . it has mercury and bromide ions in it . the mercury is in its +2 oxidation state . mercury ( ii ) bromide is a colorless solid . it is very toxic and similar to mercury ( ii ) chloride . it can form explosive mixtures with potassium . it also reacts violently with indium at high temperatures . it is corrosive . it can be made by reacting mercury ( ii ) oxide with hydrobromic acid or by disproportionation of mercury ( i ) bromide . it is used as a reagent in a reaction in organic chemistry . it is also used to test for arsenic . the arsenic is reacted with hydrogen to make arsine , which reacts with mercury ( ii ) bromide to turn it yellow or brown . related pages . - sodium bromide - mercury ( ii ) sulfate
