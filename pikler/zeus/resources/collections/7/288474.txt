Ellen Page     ellen philpotts-page ( born february 21 , 1987 ) , who goes by ellen page , is a canadian actress . she was nominated for an academy award for best actress for her role in `` juno '' , in which she played a pregnant teenager . she has also starred in movies including `` hard candy '' , `` whip it '' , and `` inception '' . page is from halifax , nova scotia . she was nominated for the orange rising star award in 2008. on february 14 , 2014 , page came out as gay .
