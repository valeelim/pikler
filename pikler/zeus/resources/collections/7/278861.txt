Gabrielle Giffords     gabrielle giffords is an american politician . she was born june 8 , 1970. she is a democratic member of the united states house of representatives . she was badly injured january 8 , 2011 , during a shooting in tucson , arizona . giffords was shot in the head at very close range , but survived . she was holding a meeting around the parking lot of a supermarket when the shooting happened .
