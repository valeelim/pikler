Beverly Sills     beverly sills ( may 25 , 1929 â€“ july 2 , 2007 ) is an american operatic soprano particularly associated with italian bel canto and french operas . born belle silverman , in brooklyn , new york , as a child prodigy , she began singing on radio shows at the age of three and began formal vocal studies at the age of seven with estelle liebling . she began her professional career in 1947 with touring companies and later joined the new york city opera in 1955. after singing a wide variety of roles , she obtained her first great success with douglas moore 's `` the ballad of baby doe '' in 1959 , and finally achieved international recognition in 1966 , with the role of cleopatra in handel 's `` giulio cesare '' . this was followed by the much acclaimed donizetti 's tudor trilogy `` anna bolena '' , `` maria stuarda '' and `` roberto devereux '' . this led to her being invited to sing at the vienna state opera ( 1968 ) , la scala in milan ( 1969 ) , the royal opera house in london ( 1970 ) , and finally at the metropolitan opera in new york in 1975 .
