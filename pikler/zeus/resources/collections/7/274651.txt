Theo Ratliff     theophilus curtis ratliff ( born april 17 , 1973 in demopolis , alabama ) is a retired american basketball player that played a total of 20 seasons in the nba . he went to university of wyoming for college where he played basketball with the cowboys . during his nba career , ratliff played for the detroit pistons , philadelphia 76ers , atlanta hawks , portland trail blazers , boston celtics , minnesota timberwolves , san antonio spurs , charlotte bobcats and the los angeles lakers . other websites . - theo ratliff at nba.com
