Wilby, Suffolk    wilby , suffolk wilby is a village and civil parish in the mid suffolk part of suffolk in eastern england . it is around nine miles south-east of diss , in 2001 there was 231 people living in wilby . it has a primary school , village hall , church , trucking company ( tg askews ) and a phone box ( cards only ) . between 1908 and 1952 the village had a railway station on the mid-suffolk light railway .
