Desert sand (color)    desert sand ( color ) desert sand is a color which is like the color of desert sand . it is a deep tone of beige . in 1998 , `` desert sand '' was made into a crayola crayon color . the color shown at right matches the palest of the three colors in the 3-color `` desert camouflage uniform '' of united states army.in 1990 , it began to replace the 6-color `` desert battle dress uniform '' . meaning of desert sand . - in interior design , the tones of `` desert sand '' are called `` desert colors '' . this is because they suggest the colors of the landscape and the design of the native american cultures of the southwestern united states . the `` desert colors '' are used in many places ( with both tones of sky blue or turquoise and tones of maroon to complement them ) in `` southwest design '' . - the colors `` desert sand '' , `` earth yellow '' , `` sand '' , and `` field drab '' are all on the list of the twelve standard camouflage colors used by the united states army . other pages . - list of colors
