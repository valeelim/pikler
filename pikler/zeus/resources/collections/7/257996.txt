Pontiac Sunfire     the pontiac sunfire was a car produced by pontiac from 1995 through 2005. the sunfire succeeded the pontiac sunbird and was succeeded by the pontiac g5 ( a rebadged chevrolet cobalt ) . the sunfire was also purchased in a sedan , convertible or coupe . much like its twin the cavalier the sunfire also fared poor in the offset test by the insurance institute for highway safety . the first pontiac sunfire became the successor of the pontiac sunbird after it got cancelled in 1990s . the pontiac received a minor facelift in the early 2000s .
