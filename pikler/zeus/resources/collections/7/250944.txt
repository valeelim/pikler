Barosaurus     barosaurus was a large , long-necked , plant-eating dinosaur which lived about 156 to 145 million years ago . the name `` barosaurus '' means `` heavy lizard '' ; this giant was named by paleontologist othniel c. marsh in 1890 . `` barosaurus '' fossils have been found in western north america and east africa . `` barosaurus '' was differently proportioned to its close relative `` diplodocus '' . it had a longer neck and shorter tail , but was about the same length overall . it was longer than `` apatosaurus '' , but its skeleton was less robust . sauropod skulls are rarely preserved , and scientists have yet to discover a `` barosaurus '' skull .
