Barium sulfide     barium sulfide is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is bas . it contains barium and sulfide ions . barium sulfide is colorless when pure . it can get colored when it is not pure . it reacts slowly with water to make barium hydroxide and hydrogen sulfide . it reacts with acids to make a barium salt and hydrogen sulfide , too . it is made by reduction of barium sulfate with carbon at a high temperature . it is used to make barium compounds from barium ore. barium sulfide looks very similar to barium sulfate . barium sulfate is not toxic , while barium sulfide is very toxic . barium sulfide dissolves in water to make toxic hydrogen sulfide and toxic barium hydroxide . if someone is fed barium sulfide , it would kill them . related pages . - barium chloride - barium carbonate
