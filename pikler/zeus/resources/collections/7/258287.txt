Hypochlorite     hypochlorite is an ion . it can be found in household bleach , sodium hypochlorite . it is unstable , meaning that it breaks down easily . hypochlorites react with acids to produce chlorine . their chemical formula is clo . it is a strong oxidizing agent . it contains chlorine in its +1 oxidation state . they are the salts of hypochlorous acid . related pages . - chlorate - sodium hypochlorite - potassium chlorate
