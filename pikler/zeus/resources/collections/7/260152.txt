Minotaur     the minotaur ( ) is a creature in greek mythology . he is shown as half-bull , half-man , normally with the head of a bull and body of a man . he lived at the center of the cretan labyrinth ( maze ) . the labyrinth was built for king minos by daedalus . the minotaur was killed by theseus . the term `` minotaur '' now refers to a race of bull-headed creatures created for the modern fantasy fiction genre . other websites . - minotaur in greek myth source greek texts and art .
