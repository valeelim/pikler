International Institute of Social History     the international institute of social history ( dutch : `` internationaal instituut voor sociale geschiedenis '' ; abbreviation : `` iisg '' ) is a historical research institute in amsterdam . it was founded in 1935 by nicolaas posthumus . it has collections of the most works and manuscripts by socialist including karl marx , friedrich engels and rosa luxemburg . it is now part of the royal netherlands academy of arts and sciences . other websites . - international institute of social history , amsterdam , netherlands : the official website
