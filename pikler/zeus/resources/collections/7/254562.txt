Renault Mégane     the renault mégane is a automobile produced by renault in three generations since 1995. the first mégane arrived in late 1995 to replace the 19. it came in many bodystyles and engines , the model formed the basis for the scenic , europe 's first compact mpv which arrived in late 1996. the range was facelifted in april 1999 and an estate/wagon version appeared but was n't sold in the uk . although a new mégane arrived in late 2002 , the original was sold into 2003 until all bodystyles were redesigned . engines . petrol engines . - `` 1.4 '' 51 kw ( 69 hp ) and 55 kw ( 75 hp ) - `` 1.4 16v '' 70 kw ( 95 hp ) - `` 1.6 '' 55 kw ( 75 hp ) and 66 kw ( 90 hp ) - `` 1.6 16v '' 79 kw ( 107 hp ) - `` 1.8 16v '' 85 kw ( 115 hp ) - `` 2.0 '' 84 kw ( 114 hp ) - `` 2.0 16v '' 110 kw ( 150 hp ) - `` 2.0 ide '' 103 kw ( 140 hp ) diesel engines . - `` 1.9 d '' 47 kw ( 64 hp ) - `` 1.9 dt '' 66 kw ( 90 hp ) - `` 1.9 dti '' 59 kw ( 80 hp ) and 72 kw ( 98 hp ) - `` 1.9 dci '' 75 kw ( 102 hp ) the second mégane arrived in late 2002 with the hatchback models , other versions appeared during 2003. the hatchback models became known for their distinctive rear end styling which resulted in renault doing an advert for it based on that . it was facelifted in early 2006 .
