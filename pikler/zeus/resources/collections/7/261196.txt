SoundtrackNet     soundtrack.net is a website dedicated to movie and television music . the website was founded in 1997 by david a. koran and dan goldwasser at carnegie mellon university . in november 2005 , `` time magazine '' listed soundtracknet as one of the `` top 20 music websites of 2005 '' . in october 2011 , the site was acquired by box office mojo cofounder sean saulsbury . other websites . - soundtrack.net
