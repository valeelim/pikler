Poularde Albufera     poularde albufera , ( chicken albufera ) is a chicken dish named in honour of the duke of albufera . it sometimes credited to french chef adolphe dugléré , who invented several other dishes . however , as dugléré was only ten years old when albufera fell out of favour with the king , and had his french title of nobility taken away , it is more likely that poularde albufera was created by antonin carême , who trained dugléré it consists of poached chicken with a garnish of vol-au-vents filled with quenelles , cocks ' kidneys , mushrooms and truffles in albufera sauce .
