Spiritual Machines     spiritual machines is the fourth studio album by canadian rock band our lady peace . it was released on december 12 , 2000. the producers of the album was arnold lanni and lead singer raine maida and it was the last our lady peace album produced by long time producer arnold lanni . it is certified double platinum in canada , peaking # 5. singles from `` spiritual machines '' . - 2000 - `` in repair '' - 2000 - `` life '' - 2001 - `` right behind you ( mafia ) ''
