Pepper spray     pepper spray ( also called oc spray , from oleoresin capsicum ) is a spray used for self-defence , by spraying it into an attacker 's face . it makes eyes water and causes intense pain , sometimes even leading to temporary blindness . pepper spray is an inflammatory agent . when someone is sprayed with pepper spray , their eyes will close and they will have difficulty breathing , a runny nose , and cough . the stronger the spray is , the longer the effects last , but the average full effect lasts around thirty to forty-five minutes , with longer effects lasting for hours . pepper spray is mostly used by police forces for riot control , but in some countries people can use it for self-defence . the active ingredient in pepper spray is capsaicin , a chemical found in capsicums , chillis and other members of the pepper family . pepper spray usually comes in canisters and can be sprayed up to twenty-five metres . alternate names . - oc spray ( from oleoresin capsicum ) - capsicum spray ( australia )
