M&amp;T Bank Stadium    m & amp ; t bank stadium m & t bank stadium is the home football stadium of the baltimore ravens . it is located in downtown baltimore , maryland . the ravens moved into m & t bank stadium in 1998 after spending a two years in memorial stadium . m & t bank stadium has also hosted the ncaa lacrosse final four in 2003 , 2004 and 2007. other websites . - stadium site on baltimoreravens.com
