Black Hole Sun     `` black hole sun '' is a 1994 grunge song by the american grunge band soundgarden , from their fourth studio album `` superunknown '' , which was released in 1994. the song was written by chris cornell . it is a playable song in the video game , rock band . the song won the grammy award for best hard rock performance and it was also nominated for best rock song . the music video for black hole sun won a mtv video music award for best metal/hard rock video in 1994 .
