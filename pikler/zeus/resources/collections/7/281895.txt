Patty Loveless     patty lee ramey ( or patty loveless ; born january 4 , 1957 ) is an american country singer and songwriter . she emerged onto the country music scene around 1986. she has also sung bluegrass and country pop . she was born in pikeville , kentucky . academy of country music - 1996 top female vocalist - 1997 top female vocalist american music awards - 1989 favorite new country artist country music association - 1995 album of the year – `` when fallen angels fly '' - 1996 female vocalist of the year - 1998 vocal event of the year – `` you do n't seem to miss me '' grand ole opry - inducted in 1988 georgia music hall of fame - inducted in 2005 grammy awards - best country collaboration with vocals – `` same old train '' ( 1998 ) - best bluegrass album – `` mountain soul ii '' ( 2011 ) kentucky music hall of fame - inducted in 2011 ( announced 17 march 2010 ) discography . - studio albums - `` patty loveless '' ( 1986 ) - `` if my heart had windows '' ( 1988 ) - `` honky tonk angel '' ( 1988 ) - `` on down the line '' ( 1990 ) - `` up against my heart '' ( 1991 )
