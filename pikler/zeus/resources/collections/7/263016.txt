Hydrazoic acid     hydrazoic acid is a chemical compound . ( it is similar to hydrogen azide , which is the gas form . hydrazoic acid is hydrogen azide dissolved in water . ) its chemical formula is hn . it contains hydrogen and azide ions . hydrazoic acid is very explosive . it is a powerful reducing agent . it is used to make other azides . it has an unpleasant smell . it can be made by reacting sodium azide with an acid . it can be made pure by distillation . it does not have many uses , because it is very explosive and toxic . hydrazoic acid is extremely toxic , even more toxic than sodium azide . it can make very bad headaches . it has a bad odor . it does not accumulate in the body . once again , it is dangerous to drink ! related pages . - hydrazine - ammonia
