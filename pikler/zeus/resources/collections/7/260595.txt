Constitutional Union Party     the constitutional union party was an american political party . it was one of four major parties to have candidates in the 1860 election , right before the american civil war . it was made up of former whigs and know-nothings who had n't joined the republican party , as well as people from the south who wanted the union to stay together . it had one idea - keeping the united states together . it ran john bell for president and edward everett for vice-president . most of its vote came from the south and the border states .
