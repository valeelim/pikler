Alexander Mogilny     alexander gennadevitch mogilny ( born february 18 , 1969 ) is a russian retired professional ice hockey right winger . he played 17 seasons in the national hockey league ( nhl ) with the buffalo sabres , new jersey devils , toronto maple leafs and vancouver canucks organizations . he won a gold medal at the 1988 winter olympics with the soviet union hockey team . he won a stanley cup in 2000 with the devils . he was named a member of the buffalo sabres hall of fame on january 1 , 2011 .
