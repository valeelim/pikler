Capri pants     capri pants are a type of pants . they are shorter than normal pants ; they end between the knee and lower leg . they were first made in 1948 by sonja de lennart , a clothes designer from prussia . they were popular in the 1950s and 1960s , but went out of style until the 2000s , when they became popular again . some pants have a zipper at the knee , allowing them to be converted into shorts . this design allows people to change between shorts and pants in variable weather conditions .
