Windows on the World     windows on the world was a complex of venues at the top floors ( 106th and 107th ) of the north tower of the world trade center and it included windows on the world , a smaller restaurant which was called wild blue , and a bar which was called the greatest bar on earth . the restaurant was destroyed in the september 11 attacks and it has also been speculated that `` the falling man '' was an employee at windows on the world but his identity was never released . websites . - windows on the world ( archive )
