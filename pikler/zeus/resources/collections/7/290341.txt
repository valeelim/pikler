Music download     a music download is the act of downloading a song from the internet to a user 's computer . this term can mean both legal downloads ( by paying to download the music ) and downloads of copyright material without permission or paying . as of 2006 , digital music sales are estimated to have reached approximately us $ 2 billion . around the world in 2006 , an estimated 509 million songs were purchased online . there are many websites that let you download music . some of these sites are itunes , google play , windows store , and amazon.com . , the biggest of these stores is itunes , which is run by apple , and had an income of $ 1.1 billion .
