Silver(I) carbonate    silver ( i ) carbonate silver ( i ) carbonate is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is agco . it contains silver in its +1 oxidation state . it also contains carbonate ions . it is a yellow solid . it reacts with acids to make carbon dioxide and a silver ( i ) salt . it does not dissolve in water . it is made by reacting sodium carbonate with silver nitrate . it is used as a catalyst . silver compounds can irritate skin . if consumed , they may turn the skin blue . related pages . - silver oxide - silver chloride
