Zinc phosphate     zinc phosphate is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is zn ( po ) . it contains zinc and phosphate ions . zinc phosphate is a white solid . it does not dissolve in water . it can react with strong acids to make phosphoric acid and a zinc salt . it can be made by reacting zinc with phosphoric acid . it is normally made by reacting zinc oxide and magnesium oxide with a mixture of phosphoric acid , water , and a buffer . it is used as a cement in dentistry . it rarely is found as a mineral . related pages . - zinc sulfate
