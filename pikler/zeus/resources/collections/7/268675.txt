Olympiacos F.C.    olympiacos f.c . p.a.e . olympiacos is a football club which plays in greece . the club based in piraeus , greece . the colours of olympiacos is white and red and its emblem is an adolescent with a crown made of laurel . it is the most successful greek team . it has won thirty-seven league titles , twenty-four greek cups and three greek super cups , more titles than any other greek team and it is one of four teams that have never been relegated from the first division . the club 's stadium is the newly rebuilt karaiskakis stadium in piraeus . olympiacos is the most popular greek club with around two and a half million fans in greece and was placed ninth on the list with the most paid up members in the world in 2006 , having 83,000 registered members as of april 2006. they share a great and long-standing rivalry with panathinaikos .
