Another Love Song (Insane Clown Posse song)    another love song ( insane clown posse song ) `` another love song ' '' is a 1999 midwest hip hop song by the american horrorcore band insane clown posse and is the first single from their fifth studio album `` the amazing jeckel brothers '' , which was released in 1999. the song was produced by insane clown posse and mike clark . the main music for `` another love song '' comes from the 1996 single `` jack-ass '' from beck .
