Alliance Party of Northern Ireland     the alliance party of northern ireland is a liberal and non-sectarian political party in northern ireland . it is neutral on the issue of whether northern ireland should stay in or leave the united kingdom . the party has 1 out of 18 northern ireland seats in the house of commons and 7 out of 108 seats in the northern ireland assembly . its leader david ford is the justice secretary in northern ireland 's all-party government . the party was founded in 1970 and gained its first elected member of parliament ( mp ) at the 2010 uk general election . it is close to the british liberal democrats .
