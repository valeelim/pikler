Adelaide Football Club     the adelaide football club , the crows , is an australian rules football team in the australian football league ( afl ) based in adelaide , south australia . the club was formed by the south australian national football league as a joint team to represent south australia in the afl . the crows played their first game on march 22 1991 , against the hawthorn football club which they won 155 to 69 , in front of a crowd of 44,902 people . its home ground is the aami stadium in adelaide , which it shares with the port adelaide football club . the crows have won two afl premierships , in 1997 against st kilda football club , and in 1998 against the north melbourne football club . other websites . - adelaide football club website
