Chris Kluwe     christopher james kluwe ( born december 24 , 1981 in philadelphia , pennsylvania ) is an american football punter for the minnesota vikings of the national football league ( nfl ) . kluwe was undrafted but played college football at the university of california . he is a supporter of same-sex marriage and released a letter on deadspin that he sent to maryland state assembly delegate emmett burns . in the letter he defended the opinions of baltimore ravens linebacker brendon ayanbadejo who is a supporter of same-sex marriage and criticized burns for sending a letter to the ravens owners to `` inhibit such expressions '' by their employee and trying to stifle ayanbadejo 's freedom of speech . other websites . - chris kluwe at nfl.com
