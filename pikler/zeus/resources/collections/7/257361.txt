Yoko Kanno     yoko kanno ( 菅野 よう子 `` kanno yōko '' , born march 19 , 1964 in miyagi prefecture , japan ) is a famous composer and keyboardist known for her musical achievements in anime titles . some of her most famous pieces include `` voices '' ( `` macross plus '' ) , `` tank ! '' ( `` cowboy bebop '' ) , `` yakusoku wa iranai '' ( `` escaflowne '' ) , `` gravity '' ( `` wolf 's rain '' ) , and `` inner universe '' ( `` '' ) . she was the singer of a band called `` the seatbelts '' , which was created just to make the `` cowboy bebop '' soundtrack . she has worked for many koei games sold from the late 1980s to 1990s and even for a dreamcast game . because she worked a lot in the `` cowboy bebop '' anime , the game sold by bandai also features her work . apart from anime and games , kanno also makes pieces for live-action films and cms ( television advertisements or commercials ) for all sorts of brands . grand funk inc. is her recording studio of choice in producing for these two media . contributions to films started in the 90s but only since 2002 has there been a trend towards the medium . most of the latter were shown in international film festivals .
