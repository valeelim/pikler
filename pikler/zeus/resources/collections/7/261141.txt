Twinkie the Kid     twinkie the kid is the mascot for twinkies , a cream-filled snack cake . he is a trademark of interstate bakeries corporation and has appeared on twinkie packaging , in commercials , and as collectible related merchandise . twinkie the kid is an anthropomorphized twinkie , which means he is a twinkie that has been given human-like features . he looks like a wrangler and wears boots , gloves , a kerchief , and a ten-gallon hat . related pages . - captain cupcake - fruit pie the magician
