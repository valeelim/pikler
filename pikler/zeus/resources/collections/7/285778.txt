South Dakota State University     south dakota state university is a public university in brookings , south dakota . the university has about 12,816 students . it was founded in 1881. the current president of the university is david chicoine . the athletics teams of the university are called the jackrabbits . they have 19 varsity teams , most of which compete in the summit league . the football team plays in the missouri valley football conference . other websites . - south dakota state university 's official website - south dakota state jackrabbits ' official athletics website
