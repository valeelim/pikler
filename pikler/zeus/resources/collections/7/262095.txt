Aluminium sulfate     aluminium sulfate , sometimes spelled aluminum or sulphate , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is al ( so ) . it is a white crystalline solid . it can be made by dissolving aluminium oxide in sulfuric acid . it is used to remove particles from water when it is processed for drinking . it is also used in making paper . it is used in baking powder , deodorants , slug killer , and to make garden soils more acidic . related pages . - aluminium chloride - sulfate - aluminium
