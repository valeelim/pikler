Fred Brathwaite     fredrick brathwaite ( born november 24 , 1972 ) is a canadian professional ice hockey goaltender currently playing for adler mannheim of the deutsche eishockey liga . he previously played in the national hockey league ( nhl ) for the edmonton oilers , calgary flames , st. louis blues , columbus blue jackets and atlanta thrashers . brathwaite was named deutsche eishockey liga mvp in 2009 .
