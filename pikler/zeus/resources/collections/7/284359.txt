Degrassi: The Next Generation    degrassi : the next generation degrassi : the next generation is a canadian teen drama created by yan moore and linda schuyler . it was shown on ctv , muchmusic and mtv canada . the series has had fourteen seasons and had made 385 episodes . the tv series spawned two movies : `` degrassi goes hollywood '' in 2009 and `` degrassi takes manhattan '' in 2010. tv.com rated the series a 8.8. in june 2014 , it was announced that degrassi would end . the last episode will air on july 31 , 2015. degrassi will be replaced by degrassi : next class . the show will air on netflix instead of teennick . other websites . - degrassi : the next generation at muchmusic.com
