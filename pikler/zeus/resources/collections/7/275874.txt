Antimony tribromide     antimony tribromide , also known as antimony ( iii ) bromide , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is sbbr . it has antimony and bromide ions in it . the antimony is in its +3 oxidation state . antimony tribromide is a colorless solid . it absorbs water from the air . it reacts with water to make antimony trioxide and hydrobromic acid . it has covalent bonds in it and melts easily . it is made by reacting antimony and bromine or by reacting concentrated hydrobromic acid with antimony trioxide . it is used as a fire retardant . it is also used in dyeing , and analytical chemistry . related pages . - antimony trichloride - antimony sulfide
