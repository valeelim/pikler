Kim Yu-sin     kim yu-sin ( 김유신 , 金庾信 , 595 – july 673 ) was a general and politician of the silla kingdom in ancient korea . he belonged to the royal family of gaya , and served as a soldier of silla . he conquered the kingdoms of goguryeo and baekje . after his death , he was given the title of heungmu the great ( 흥무대왕 ) . other websites . - naver-cast kim yu-sin ( korean )
