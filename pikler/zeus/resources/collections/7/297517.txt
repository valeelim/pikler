G20 developing nations     the g20 ( group of 20 , also variously g21 , g22 and g20+ ) is a bloc of developing nations established on 20 august 2003. distinct and separate from the g-20 major economies , the group emerged at the 5th ministerial wto conference , held in cancún , mexico , from 10 september to 14 september 2003. the g-20 accounts for 60 % of the world 's population , 70 % of its farmers and 26 % of world ’ s agricultural exports .
