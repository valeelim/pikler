Perbromic acid     perbromic acid is a chemical compound . it is an acid . its chemical formula is hbro . it contains hydrogen and perbromate ions . perbromic acid is a very unstable acid . it easily breaks down . it can be made by reacting perbromates with an acid . it releases oxygen and turns into bromic acid . it can also totally break down , releasing bromine gas . related pages . - bromous acid - hypobromous acid
