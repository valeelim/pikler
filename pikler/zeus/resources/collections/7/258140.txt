Deprotonation     deprotonation is the removal of a hydrogen ion from a substance . a hydrogen atom normally contains 1 proton and 1 electron . when it is oxidized , it loses the electron , turning it into a hydrogen ion . a hydrogen cation is the same as a proton . substances are deprotonated by other substances more basic than themselves . the base itself is protonated . related pages . - proton - hydrogen - protonation
