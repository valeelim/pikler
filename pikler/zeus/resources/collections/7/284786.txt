Marie Osmond     olive marie osmond , known simply as marie osmond ( born october 13 , 1959 ) is an american singer , actress and doll designer . she was born in ogden , utah . she is the sister of donny osmond . marie was not a part of her family 's singing group the osmonds . she had some success as a solo country artist in the 1970s and 1980s . she also hosted several talk shows with her brother donny .
