Chicken Huntin'    chicken huntin ' `` chicken huntin ' '' is a 1995 midwest hip hop song by the american horrorcore band insane clown posse and is the first single from their third studio album `` riddle box '' , which was released in 1995. the song was produced by insane clown posse and mike clark . the song is about meeting and killing multiple racist rednecks , who the band referred to as 'chickens ' .
