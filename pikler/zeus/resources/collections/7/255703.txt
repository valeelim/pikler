Wolfgang Petersen     wolfgang petersen ( born 14 march 1941 ) is a german movie director and screenwriter . his work includes `` the neverending story '' , `` enemy mine '' , `` outbreak '' , `` in the line of fire '' , `` air force one '' , `` das boot '' , `` the perfect storm '' , `` troy '' , and `` poseidon '' . petersen was born in emden , lower saxony , germany on 14 march 1941. one of his first efforts was the 1977 `` die konsequenz '' he is currently working on a live-action adaptation of the 2006 anime movie paprika .
