Kathleen Sebelius     kathleen sebelius ( born may 15 , 1948 ) is an american politician . she sered as the united states secretary of health and human services from 2009 to 2014. before becoming the secretary of health and human services , sebelius was a governor from kansas . `` time magazine '' listed sebelius as one of america 's five best governors in 2005. sebelius is a democrat . she resigned on april 10 , 2014 .
