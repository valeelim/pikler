Mark Rutte     mark rutte ( born 14 february 1967 ) is a dutch politician . he has been prime minister of the netherlands since 14 october 2010. he is the leader of the liberal party vvd . in 2010 , rutte won the elections with his party . he did not gain a majority in parliament , he won 31 out of 150 seats . before he could form his government , there were four weeks of negotiations with the christian-democratic cda and the right-wing pvv of geert wilders . wilders is not in the government , but he will support the government on several issues . these issues were discussed in those four weeks . when he was sworn in on 14 october 2010 , he became the first liberal prime minister in the netherlands in 92 years . rutte is also a teacher at a school in the hague .
