Kijiji     kijiji ( ; , swahili for `` village '' ) is a centralized network of online groups of people for writing local online classified advertisements . it is a subsidiary of ebay launched in march 2005. kijiji is currently available for more than 300 cities in germany . further kijiji countries are france , italy , canada , china , india , taiwan , belgium , switzerland , austria , and japan . kijiji was made available to selected cities in the united states on june 29 , 2007 , under the name of `` ebay classifieds '' . kijiji offers similar services and is seen as a competitor to craigslist with the biggest exception being that kijiji has quite an extensive pets section . kijiji 's owner ebay is also a minority shareholder in craigslist . in april 2008 ebay launched a lawsuit against craigslist claiming that their executives were attempting to weaken ebay 's investment , while in may of the same year , craigslist filed a countersuit claiming kijiji had stolen trade secrets and that ebay used misleading tactics to promote the service .
