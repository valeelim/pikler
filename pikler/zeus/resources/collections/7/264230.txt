Luxembourg Grand Prix     the luxembourg grand prix ( großer preis von luxemburg ) was a formula one motor race for two years in the 1990s . the fia created the luxembourg gp to keep a race at nürburgring , and allow the european gp to be held in another country . the fia only allowed one formula one per country . they would allow a second race within a country if it had a different grand prix name . for several years , the german grand prix had been held at hockenheimring , and the european grand prix at nürburgring . there was unease that the nürburgring was being given a monopoly for the european gp . the border of luxembourg lies about 50 miles away from the nürburgring track .
