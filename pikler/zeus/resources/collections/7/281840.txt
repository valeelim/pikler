Aedes     aedes is a genus of mosquito originally found in tropical and subtropical zones , but has spread by human activity to all continents excluding antarctica . several of the species transmit important human diseases and one species , `` aedes albopictus '' , is the most invasive mosquito in the world . the name comes from the greek `` aēdēs '' , meaning `` unpleasant '' or `` odious '' , so called because of the diseases this type of mosquito transmits , including dengue fever and yellow fever .
