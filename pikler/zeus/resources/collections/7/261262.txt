Snog Marry Avoid?    snog marry avoid ? snog marry avoid ? is a british reality tv show . the first four series were hosted by jenny frost . from the fifth series ellie taylor has presented it . its format is related to the fashion industry . it is shown on bbc three . it has been on air since 2008 and stars pod ( the `` personal overhaul device '' ) who gives make-unders to people to try to show their natural beauty without makeup or fake tan .
