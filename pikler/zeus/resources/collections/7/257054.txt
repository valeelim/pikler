Lennon/McCartney     the songwriting duo of john lennon and paul mccartney is one of the best-known and most successful musical partnerships in history . between 1962 and 1969 , they wrote about 180 credited songs . most of the songs were recorded by the beatles . other songs credited to lennon–mccartney were originally released not by the beatles but by other artists , especially those managed by brian epstein . recording a lennon–mccartney song helped start new artists ' careers . for example , one of the rolling stones ' first singles , `` i wan na be your man '' , was a lennon–mccartney song .
