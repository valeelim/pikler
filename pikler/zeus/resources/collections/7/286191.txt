Creighton University     creighton university is a private , coeducational , jesuit , roman catholic university in omaha , nebraska . the university has about 7,385 students . it was founded in 1878. the athletics teams of the university are called the blue jays . they have 14 varsity teams , 13 of which compete in the big east conference . in women 's rowing , a sport that the big east does not sponsor , creighton competes in the west coast conference . other websites . - creighton university 's official website - creighton bluejays ' official athletics website
