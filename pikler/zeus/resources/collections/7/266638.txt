Calcium nitrate     calcium nitrate , also known as norwegian saltpeter , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is ca ( no ) . it contains calcium and nitrate ions . calcium nitrate is a colorless solid . it is an oxidizing agent . it absorbs water from the air . it decomposes to make nitrogen dioxide and calcium oxide when heated . calcium nitrate is made by reacting calcium hydroxide or calcium carbonate with nitric acid . it is made when nitric acid reacts with calcium phosphate . it can be made by reacting ammonium nitrate and calcium hydroxide . calcium nitrate is used in fertilizers to add nitrogen to the soil . it can be complexed with urea to make a different nitrogen fertilizer . related pages . - calcium nitride
