Rubidium nitrate     rubidium nitrate is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is rbno . it is a colorless crystalline solid . it is an oxidizing agent . it is made by reacting rubidium or rubidium hydroxide with nitric acid . the reaction with rubidium is very violent . it is used to make other rubidium compounds . related pages . - rubidium oxide - rubidium hydride - potassium nitrate
