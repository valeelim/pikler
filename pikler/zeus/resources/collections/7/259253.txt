Annecy International Animated Film Festival     the annecy international animation film festival ( festival international du film d'animation d'annecy ) was created in 1960. it takes place at the beginning of june in the town of annecy , france . at first , it took place every two years . the festival became annualin 1998. it is one of the four international animated movie festivals sponsored by the `` association d'international du film d'animation '' ( or asifa , the international animated film association ) short movies awards . - 1960 - `` lev a pisnicka '' ( grand prix ) - 1962 - `` the flying man '' ( grand prix ) - 1963 - `` spatne namalovana slepice '' ( grand prix ) - 1965 - `` la demoiselle et le violoncelliste '' ( grand prix ) - 1967 - `` ar√®s contre atlas '' ( grand prix ) - 1967 - `` klatki '' ( grand prix ) - 1967 - `` krotitelj divljih konja '' ( grand prix ) - 1967 - `` the breath '' ( grand prix ) - 1971 - `` apel '' ( grand prix ) - 1971 - `` nevesta '' ( grand prix ) - 1971 - `` the further adventures of uncle sam '' ( grand prix ) - 1973 - `` frank movie '' ( grand prix )
