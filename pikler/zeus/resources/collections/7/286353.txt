Michael Kort     michael kort ( born 1944 ) is an american historian . after studying at johns hopkins university and new york university , he eventually became a professor at boston university . he specializes in soviet history . kort 's books include biographies of nikita krushchev and mikhail gorbachev , as well textbooks about the soviet union . other nonfiction books include `` the rise and fall of the soviet union '' and `` marxism in power '' .
