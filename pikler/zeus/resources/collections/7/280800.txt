Powerslave     powerslave is a studio album by iron maiden . it went on sale on 3 september 1984. it was the second most popular album in england when it started being sold . the album got good reviews ; one person said it was `` classic and essential . '' one of the songs , `` rime of the ancient mariner , '' was adapted from a poem of the same name by samuel taylor coleridge . it is iron maiden 's longest song ( almost 14 minutes ) . musicians . - bruce dickinson – lead vocals - dave murray – guitar - adrian smith – guitar , backing vocals - steve harris – bass , backing vocals - nicko mcbrain – drums
