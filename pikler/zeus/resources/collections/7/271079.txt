Philip V of Spain     philip v of spain ( 19 december 1683 - 9 july 1746 ) was the second son of louis , grand dauphin and maria anna victoria of bavaria . he was a grandson of louis xiv and marie therese of austria and was king of spain from 1700 – 1724 and again from 1724 – 1746. he succeeded charles ii of spain who was childless and mad . he was born at the palace of versailles , france . he lost the kingdom of naples to charles vi , holy roman emperor and the kingdom of sicily to victor amadeus ii . he died at his the royal palace of madrid . philip was the first bourbon king of spain . he died in madrid , spain . his reign of 45 years and 21 days is the longest in modern spanish history . he was also the father of three kings of spain . he married maria luisa of savoy in 1701 and had four children : 1. louis i of spain ( 25 august 1707 – 31 august 1724 ) married louise élisabeth d'orléans , no children . 1. felipe of spain ( 2 july 1709 – 18 july 1709 ) 1. felipe pedro gabriel of spain ( 7 june 1712 – 29 december 1719 ) died in childhood .
