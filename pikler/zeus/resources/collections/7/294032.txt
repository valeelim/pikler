Ishinomaki, Miyagi    ishinomaki , miyagi as of january 1 , 2010 , about 164,294 people lived there . that was 295.83 people per km² . the total area is 555.35 km² . ishinomaki was one the cities most seriously affected by the 2011 tōhoku earthquake and tsunami . between 1 march and 31 august 2011 , many people left ishinomaki . 6,500 fewer people , or 4 % , lived there .
