Bugatti Veyron     the bugatti veyron is a 2-door coupé that is built by the bugatti brand . the veyron can also be bought as a roadster . the veyron is in its 1st generation . the car is built in france . the sport series of the veyron is currently the fastest production car in the world with a top speed of 431.072 km/h ( 267.856 mph ) , though the new koenigsegg agera aims to beat the record . the car costs about £1.6 million to buy . the tyres cost over £20,000 for a set . the car has a monocoque construction . the car has two v8 engines joined together to make a w16 engine . other websites . - official website
