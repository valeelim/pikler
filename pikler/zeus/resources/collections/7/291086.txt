Daniel Catán     daniel catán porteny ( 3 april 1949 in mexico city - 9 april 2011 in austin , texas ) was a mexican opera composer of jewish descent . he is best known for turning the italian movie , `` il postino '' , into an opera . he was working at the university of texas at austin on a new opera based on the frank capra movie , `` meet john doe '' . other websites . - official website of daniel catán - official facebook page of daniel catán - g. schirmer inc. daniel catán 's music publisher
