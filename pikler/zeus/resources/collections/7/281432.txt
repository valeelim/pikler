The Terror Wheel     the terror wheel is the second ep by the american horrorcore band , insane clown posse . it was released on august 5 , 1994. the producer of the ep was mike e. clark . if you called the number featured in the song `` amy 's in the attic '' at 6:06 into the song , it would tell you the name of the next joker 's card , riddle box .
