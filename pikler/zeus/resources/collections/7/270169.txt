Cult of domesticity     the cult of domesticity , also known as the cult of true womanhood , is an opinion about women in the 1800s . they believed that women should stay at home and should not do any work outside of the home . there were four things they believed that women should be : 1. more religious than men 1. pure in heart , mind , and body 1. submissive to their husbands 1. staying at home these were very popular in the 1800s and in the 1950s . this ideology would strongly discourage women from obtaining education . this ideology was thought to elevate the moral status of women , and be beneficial for them in ways such as living lives of higher material comfort . it made the roles of wife and mother more important in society .
