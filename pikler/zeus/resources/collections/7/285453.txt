Arthur Christmas     arthur christmas ( originally called operation rudolph ) is a 2011 computer-animated comedy-drama movie that was produced by steve pegram and was directed by sarah smith and barry cook . `` arthur christmas '' was released on november 11 , 2011 in the united kingdom and on november 23 , 2011 in north america . cast . - james mcavoy as arthur - hugh laurie as steve - jim broadbent as santa - bill nighy as grandsanta - imelda staunton as mrs. santa - ashley jensen as byrony other websites . - arthur christmas ' official website
