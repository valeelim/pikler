ESCP Europe     escp europe business school is a european higher studies establishment . the school is in 6 places , in paris , france , in the united kingdom ( london ) , in spain ( madrid ) , in italy ( torino ) , in poland ( warsaw ) , and in germany ( berlin ) , where it is a university . it is the oldest business school in the world established in 1819. and therefore presents itself as `` the world 's first business school '' . escp europe is specialist in the fields of economics , social sciences and management . further reading . - escp website
