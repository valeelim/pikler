The Mike Douglas Show     the mike douglas show was an american daytime television talk show . the host mike douglas . the show was broadcast in syndication from 1961 to 1982. westinghouse broadcasting distributed the show . many of the programs were made in studios of two of the company 's tv stations . the show featured the first television appearance of then 2 year old tiger woods who showed off his swing for bob hope and james stewart . others who appeared on the show over the years include malcolm x , martin luther king , jr. , mother teresa , jerry rubin , angela davis ,
