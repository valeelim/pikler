Amar Ramasar     amar ramasar ( born c. 1981 , the bronx ) is a principal dancer of the new york city ballet . in 2010 , `` dance magazine '' reported that ramasar was one of the few asian american professional ballet dancers . he took his first dance lessons at the henry street settlement house on the lower east side . he went there every day by subway , from his home in the south bronx .
