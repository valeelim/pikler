Flag of New Mexico     the flag of the state of new mexico has the red sun symbol of the zia in the middle and has a yellow background . in 1920 , dr. harry mera , an archaeologist won a contest to design a new state flag and chose the zia sun symbol because he was familiar with the one that was found in zia pueblo on a 19th century pot . there is four because four is a sacred number to the zia which symbolizes the circle of life : four winds , four seasons , four directions , and four sacred obligations . new mexico was the 48th state and it was founded in 1912 .
