Rupert Bottenberg     rupert bottenberg ( born : 19 july 1971 in wolfville , nova scotia ) is a comic artist and writer . he works for the `` montreal mirror '' newsweekly . bottenberg grew up in montreal . he started `` comix jams , public events centered around collaborative , improvised comic art , '' a concept other north american cities copy . his way of drawing is called `` clean-line '' and full of `` technical precision .
