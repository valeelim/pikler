Titan stick insect     the titan stick insect is one of the largest stick insects in australia . it has a brown body with spikes on its neck . the titan stick insect can reach up to 27 cm in length , and takes the form of a stick as a disguise to predators . the titan stick insect can be found in two states of australia ; queensland and new south wales . the titan stick insect reproduces through sexual reproduction and lay eggs that hatch .
