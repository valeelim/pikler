University of Cincinnati     the university of cincinnati is a public university in cincinnati , ohio . the university has about 41,357 students . it was founded in 1819. the current president of the university is gregory h. williams . the athletics teams of the university are called the bearcats . they have 18 varsity teams and they compete in the american athletic conference . other websites . - university of cincinnati 's official website - cincinnati bearcats ' official athletics website
