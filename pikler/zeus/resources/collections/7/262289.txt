Headlamp     a headlamp is a light bulb mounted on the front of a machine . normally it is mounted on a car . it shines forward . it can be turned on and off . it is used to see ahead of the machine so it would n't drive into trees or telephone poles . incandescent bulbs and a variant known as halogen bulbs are the most common types . they are normally bright . related pages . - fluorescent bulb - mercury-vapor lamp - sodium vapor lamp
