Keith Urban     keith lionel urban ( born october 26 , 1967 in whangarei , new zealand ) is a new zealand-born australian grammy award winning country music singer and songwriter . urban is married to actress nicole kidman . they met at g'day la , a hollywood event honoring australians . the couple have two daughters : sunday rose kidman urban ( born july 7 , 2008 ) and faith margaret kidman urban ( born december 28 , 2010 ) . other websites . - keith urban 's official website
