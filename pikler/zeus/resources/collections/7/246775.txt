Saraiki dialect     the saraiki dialect ( perso-arabic : سرائیکی sometimes spelled siraiki and seraiki ) is closely related to punjabi . it is a dialect of punjabi as agreed by majority of local and foreign linguists since late 90 's . it is spoken by approximately 18 million pakistanis , mostly in the southern part of punjab province and in adjacent parts of sindh , balochistan and khyber pakhtunkhwas . saraiki belongs to the indo-aryan branch of indo-european .
