Fish pie     fish pie is a savory pie that has fish in it . it is normally eaten by british people , and made with white fish ( cod , haddock , or halibut ) in a white sauce . hard boiled eggs are sometimes added as well , and the pie is made in a deep dish . the fish is not covered by a pie crust , but there are mashed potatos over it instead . the pie is sometimes also called the `` fisherman 's pie '' because it is similar to the shepherd 's pie . related pages . - pot pie - sweet potato pie other websites . - recipes for luxury fish pie and fisherman 's pie from bbc food .
