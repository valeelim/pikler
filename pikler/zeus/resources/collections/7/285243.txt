University of Wisconsin–Green Bay     the university of wisconsin–green bay is a public university in green bay , wisconsin , and a part of the university of wisconsin system . the university has about 6,549 students . it was founded in 1965. the current chancellor of the university is dr. thomas k. harden . the university calls itself `` green bay '' for sports purposes , and its teams use the nickname of phoenix . the school has 16 varsity teams that compete in the horizon league . other websites . - university of wisconsin–green bay 's official website - green bay phoenix official athletics website
