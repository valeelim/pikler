Disulfur decafluoride     disulfur decafluoride , also known as sulfur pentafluoride and sulfur ( v ) fluoride , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is sf . it contains sulfur in its +5 oxidation state . disulfur decafluoride is a colorless liquid . it has no odor . it is very toxic . it disproportionates to sulfur hexafluoride and sulfur tetrafluoride when heated . it reacts with sulfur dioxide . it also can react with chlorine , bromine , and ammonia . it is more toxic than phosgene . it was thought of as a poison gas in world war ii , but it was never used . it has almost no uses . related pages . - sulfur trioxide - phosphorus pentoxide
