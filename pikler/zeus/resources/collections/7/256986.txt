Gravity (Our Lady Peace album)    gravity ( our lady peace album ) gravity is the fifth studio album by canadian rock band our lady peace . it was released on june 18 , 2002. the producer of the album was bob rock . this album featured new guitarist steve mazur who replaced mike turner when he left the band in 2001. singles from `` gravity '' . - 2002 - `` somewhere out there '' - 2002 - `` innocent '' - 2003 - `` made of steel ''
