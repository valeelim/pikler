S Club     s club , formerly s club 7 and currently touring under the name s club 3 and a bit are a british pop group . they formed in 1998 in london and split up in 2003. the group reunited in 2008 with jo o'meara , bradley mcintosh and paul cattermole and was renamed s club . discography . studio albums . - 1994 : `` s club '' - 1997 : `` '7 ' '' - 1999 : `` sunshine '' - 2000 : `` seeing double ''
