Nickel(II) hydroxide    nickel ( ii ) hydroxide nickel ( ii ) hydroxide , also known as nickelous hydroxide , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is ni ( oh ) . it contains nickel in its +2 oxidation state . it also contains hydroxide ions . it is a light green solid . it does not dissolve in water . it dissolves in acids to make other nickel ( ii ) salts . it is made by reacting a base containing hydroxide ions with a nickel ( ii ) salt , such as nickel ( ii ) chloride . nickel ( ii ) hydroxide is used in the nickel cadmium battery and the nickel-metal hydride battery . related pages . - nickel ( ii ) nitrate - nickel ( ii ) sulfate
