Tellurium(II) bromide    tellurium ( ii ) bromide tellurium ( ii ) bromide , also known as tellurium dibromide , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is tebr . it contains tellurium and bromide ions . the tellurium is in the +2 oxidation state . tellurium ( ii ) bromide is a green brown crystalline solid . it absorbs water from the air . it is made when tellurium ( iv ) bromide is heated in air . it can also be made by careful reduction of tellurium ( iv ) bromide . related pages . - tellurium ( iv ) chloride - tellurium ( ii ) chloride - tellurium ( iv ) oxide
