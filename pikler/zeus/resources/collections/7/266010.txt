Barium chlorate     barium chlorate is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is ba ( clo ) . it contains barium and chlorate ions . barium chlorate is a colorless solid . it is very toxic because it has barium ions in it . it also is an oxidizing agent . it makes oxygen gas when it is heated . barium chlorate can be made by reacting sodium chlorate and barium chloride . it can also be made by electrolytic oxidation of barium chloride , similar to the chloralkali process . it can also be made by reacting ammonium chlorate , an unstable compound , with barium carbonate . this mixture is boiled , converting the ammonium carbonate to ammonia and carbon dioxide . barium chlorate is used in fireworks to make a green color . it is also used in matches to make oxygen and make the match burn faster . related pages . - barium nitrate
