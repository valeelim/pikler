Lead azide     lead azide , also known as lead ( ii ) azide , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is pb ( n ) . it contains lead and azide ions . it contains lead in its +2 oxidation state . lead azide is an explosive . most other azides just burn rapidly . it is used as a detonator to detonate other explosives . it is made by reacting lead ( ii ) nitrate and sodium azide . it can be destroyed by sodium dichromate . it can react with other metals such as copper to make even more sensitive azide explosives . it does not become less of an explosive when wet . it will explode if it is dropped . related pages . - lead ( ii ) oxide - sodium amide
