Golden Road (album)    golden road ( album ) golden road is the third studio album by australian country music singer keith urban . it was released on october 8 , 2002. the producers of the album were dan huff and keith urban . the album stayed on the billboard hot country albums chart for 108 weeks peaking at # 2 and peaked # 11 on the billboard 200. the album reached double platinum in canada selling 200,000 units . singles from `` golden road '' . - 2002 - `` somebody like you '' - 2002 - `` raining on sunday '' - 2003 - `` who would n't wan na be me '' - 2004 - `` you 'll think of me ''
