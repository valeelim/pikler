Zinc antimonide     zinc antimonide is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is znsb . it has zinc and antimonide ions in it . zinc antimonide is a gray solid . its properties are between an alloy and a salt . it reacts with water to make stibine . it is a reducing agent . it is a semiconductor . it is made by heating zinc and antimony . it is used in transistors and infrared detectors . related pages . - aluminium antimonide - zinc sulfate - zinc chloride
