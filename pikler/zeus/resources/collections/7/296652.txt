Mikhail Yakubov     mikhail yakubov ( born february 16 , 1982 in barnaul , russian sfsr ) is a russian professional ice hockey centre . he currently plays for the severstal cherepovets of the kontinental hockey league . yakubov also played in the nhl for a total of 3 seasons ; 2 with the chicago blackhawks and 1 with the florida panthers . he was drafted by the chicago blackhawks with the 10th overall pick in the 2000 nhl draft . other websites . - mikhail yakubov 's nhl profile
