Dodge Journey     the dodge journey is a mid-size crossover suv that is built by chrysler 's dodge brand . the journey has had one generation during its time . the vehicle has replaced the dodge caravan . the journey has been built since 2008 and made its debut in 2007 at the frankfurt motor show . the journey was designed by ryan nagode . the journey can seat five or seven people depending on the wheelbase length . other websites . - dodge journey at dodge.ca
