Tom Araya     tom araya ( born tomás enrique araya june 6 , 1961 in viña del mar , chile ) is an chilean-american musician , best known as the bassist and vocalist of the american thrash metal band slayer . araya is ranked fifty-eighth by `` hit parader '' on their list of the 100 greatest metal vocalists of all time . araya was employed as a respiratory therapist in the early 1980s and used his earnings to finance slayer 's debut album `` show no mercy '' . much of araya 's lyrical content is about serial killers , a subject he finds interesting , first making his lyrical contribution to 1985 's `` hell awaits '' with the track `` at dawn they sleep '' .
