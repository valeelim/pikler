Operation Sea Lion     operation sea lion ( ) was the plan to invade the united kingdom by the nazis during world war ii . the plan began in 1940. however , germany first had to control the sky and sea of the english channel before a land invasion . with the german air force defeated in the battle of britain , the operation was put off on 17 september 1940 and never happened . the invasion force would have used a varied amount of weapons , developing underwater tanks , the first jet powered airplane , and a massive underwater army armed with old an innovative scuba suits .
