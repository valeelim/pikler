Knots Landing     knots landing was an american soap opera which began december 27 , 1979 and ended may 13 , 1993. it is a spin-off of `` dallas '' . the show aired on cbs throughout its run . it was set around a fictional coastal suburb of los angeles and focuses on four married couples living in a cul-de-sac . by the time of the show 's end , it had become one of the longest running primetime drama shows on television in the us .
