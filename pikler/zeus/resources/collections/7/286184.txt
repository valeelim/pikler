Bethune-Cookman University     bethune-cookman university is a private university in daytona beach , florida . the university has about 4,000 students . it was founded in 1904. the current president of the university is trudie kibbe reed . it is a historically black university , which means that it was founded to educate african american students . the athletics teams of the university are called the wildcats . they have 7 varsity teams and they compete in the mid-eastern athletic conference . other websites . - bethune-cookman university 's official website - bethune-cookman wildcats ' official athletics website
