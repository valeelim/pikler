Ed Litzenberger     edward c. j . `` eddie '' litzenberger ( born july 15 , 1932 in neudorf , saskatchewan : died november 1 , 2010 ) was a canadian professional ice hockey right winger . he played for the chicago black hawks , montreal canadiens , detroit red wings and toronto maple leafs of the national hockey league ( nhl ) . litzenberger won the stanley cup 4 times one time in 1961 with the chicago black hawks and three times with the toronto maple leafs one in 1962 , one in 1963 and one in 1964 .
