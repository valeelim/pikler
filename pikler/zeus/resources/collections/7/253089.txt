International Civil Aviation Organization airport code     an icao code is the code used by pilots and air traffic controllers to identify airports simply and easily . the code is made up of four numbers or letters . these are not the same as iata codes , which are often simpler , and are made to sound like the airport 's name . for example , people who fly from heathrow airport , are likely to know it best as either heathrow , or by its iata code : lhr . they are less likely to know its icao code : egll .
