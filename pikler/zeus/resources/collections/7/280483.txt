Bay laurel     the bay laurel ( `` laurus nobilis '' , of the lauraceae family ) is also known as 'sweet bay ' , 'bay tree ' , 'true laurel ' , 'grecian laurel ' , 'laurel tree ' , or simply laurel . it is an aromatic evergreen tree or large shrub with green , glossy leaves , native to the mediterranean region . it is the source of the bay leaf used in cooking .
