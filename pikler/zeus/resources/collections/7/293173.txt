Foundation for the Preservation of the Mahayana Tradition     the fpmt ( foundation for the preservation of the mahayana tradition ) is a group of buddhist centers focusing on the gelugpa tradition of tibet . founded in 1975 by lama thubten yeshe and lama thubten zopa rinpoche , who began teaching buddhism to western students in nepal , the fpmt has grown to encompass 150 teaching centers , projects , and social services in 33 countries . since the death of lama yeshe in 1984 , the fpmt 's spiritual director has been lama zopa rinpoche . other pages . - dalai lama - lam rim
