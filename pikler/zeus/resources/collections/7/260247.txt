Helm of Darkness     the helm of darkness or cap of invisibility . in greek mythology , is a helmet that makes the person wearing it invisible . it is also called the cap of hades or helm of hades . it was said to be used by the goddess of wisdom , athena , the messenger god , hermes , and perseus . according to mythology , it was made by the cyclops . they had made a lightning bolt for zeus , a trident for poseidon , and the helm of darkness for hades . hades is the god of the underworld and jewelry .
