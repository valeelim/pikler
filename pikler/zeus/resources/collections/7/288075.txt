The Sims Medieval     the sims medieval is a video game developed by the sims studio . it is based on `` the sims '' game series , and was released on march 22 , 2011 by electronic arts . it is available for microsoft windows and mac os x. the game is different from the previous versions of `` the sims '' , in the sense that it is set in medieval times . players can build a kingdom , through a quest-driven gameplay . the game has received mixed reviews from critics .
