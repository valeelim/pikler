Slasher movie     a slasher movie is a horror or thriller movie in which there is a killer hunting after a group of people , picking them off one by one . often the killer has a powerful weapon , is crazy or insane , and is either unstoppable or can not die . common examples of slashers include : a nightmare on elm street , friday the 13th ( 1980 movie ) , and psycho ( movie ) . stephen king has written several slasher movies .
