Nickel silver     nickel silver , sometimes called german silver , is an alloy . it normally has copper , nickel , and zinc in it . despite the name , it does not contain any real silver . it is common in utensils . it is also used in cymbals , keys , zippers , and musical instruments . some coins use nickel silver . it is sometimes used to trick people into thinking it is silver . a german industrial chemist named e.a . geitner discovered nickel silver in the early 19th century .
