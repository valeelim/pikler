University of Dayton     the university of dayton is a private , roman catholic university in dayton , ohio . the university has about 10,569 students . it was founded in 1850. the current president of the university is daniel curran . the athletics teams of the university are called the flyers . they have 16 varsity teams and they compete in the atlantic 10 conference and the pioneer football league for football . other websites . - university of dayton 's official website - dayton flyers ' official athletics website
