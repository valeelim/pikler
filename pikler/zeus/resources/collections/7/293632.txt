Skegness     skegness is a town and civil parish on the a52 road and a158 road in east lindsey , lincolnshire , england . in 2001 there were 18,910 people living in skegness . it is known for being bracing , which means that it often has high winds . this is due to its exposed position on the north sea . skegness is known as the home of butlins , a theme park .
