Bob Clampett     robert ( bob ) clampett ( may 8 , 1913 - may 4 , 1984 ) was an american puppeteer , animator , and director and producer . he was one of the important people in the history of american animation . he claimed to have invented the characters of porky pig , daffy duck and bugs bunny . born in san diego , california , in 1913 clampett held a number of jobs including a newspaper cartoonist , and at disney , before becoming an animator for harman-ising . he died in detroit , michigan , in 1984 .
