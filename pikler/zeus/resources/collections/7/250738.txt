StarHub TV     starhub tv is a cable television service in singapore . it is owned by starhub limited . the cable network has been operating since 1991. it was then operated by singapore cable vision ( scv ) . by 1996 , scv had built a s $ 600 million broadband cable network across the country . in 2002 , scv merged with starhub , and was renamed starhub cable vision . in 2007 , starhub cable television was rebranded as starhub tv . it is the only cable television service in singapore . it was previously also the only pay television service in the country until 2007 , when mio tv was launched . other websites . - starhub tv 's official website - starhub tv 's online programme guide
