Tony Danza     tony danza ( born anthony salvatore iadanza ; april 21 , 1950 in brooklyn , new york ) is an american actor , producer and boxer . his mother was an immigrant from sicily ; his father was a sanitation worker . danza made his big screen debut in the 1981 movie going ape ! . danza was also the host of the espy awards in 1996 and miss america in 2002. filmography . - `` going ape !
