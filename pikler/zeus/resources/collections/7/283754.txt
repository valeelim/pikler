Cul-de-sac     a cul-de-sac ( literally `` bottom of bag '' ) is a word that came from the french language and means a small street without a way through at the far end . it is also used in catalan and occitan , its meaning being equivalent to a dead end , close , or no through road ( united kingdom , australian and canadian english ) . while culs-de-sac were built in history for other reasons , its modern use is to calm down the traffic .
