Industrial metal     industrial metal is a heavy metal musical genre that draws from industrial music and many different types of heavy metal , using repeating metal guitar riffs , sampling , synthesizer or sequencer lines , and distorted vocals . founding industrial metal acts include ministry , godflesh , and kmfdm . industrial metal 's popularity led to some criticism from other artists associated with the industrial scene . subsequently , it is most well known in various european permutations . industrial metal groups have produced many acclaimed music videos .
