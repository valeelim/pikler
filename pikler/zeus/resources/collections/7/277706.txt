Milk substitute     a milk substitute is a product that resembles milk . milk substitutes have a similar look and taste to milk produced by the mammary glands of a mammal . in milk substitutes many of the ingredients of milk are replaced : a milk substitute may contain fat and proteins obtained from plants . people with a lactose intolerance or a milk allergy often replace milk by a substitute . the two most common categories of milk substitutes are grain milk and plant milk . the most common grain milk is rice milk , while the most common plant milk is soy milk . other examples include coconut milk , almond milk , and peanut milk .
