Vampirates     vampirates is a series of young adult fantasy books . it is written by british author justin somper . the series is about two twin children , connor and grace tempest , who get separated at sea . they are saved by two very different ships . connor is saved by a normal pirate ship called the diablo , grace by the mythical vampirate ship called the nocturne . the word `` vampirate '' refers to a vampire living on the nocturne .
