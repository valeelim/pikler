Bull Durham     bull durham is an 1988 american romantic comedy baseball movie set in durham , north carolina . it was produced by thom mount and mark burg and was directed by ron shelton . bull durham was released on june 15 , 1988 in north america . the movie received positive reviews with a 98 % rating on rotten tomatoes and a 73 out of 100 from metacritic . cast . - kevin costner as `` crash '' davis - susan sarandon as annie savoy - tim robbins as ebby calvin `` nuke '' laloosh - trey wilson as joe riggins - robert wuhl as larry hockett - william o'leary as jimmy - david neidorf as bobby - samuel veraldi as a second baseman - stephen ware as a umpire other websites . - bull durham 's official website
