Cherith Baldry     cherith baldry ( born on january 21 , 1947 , in lancaster , england ) is an author . she grew up on a farm , and first worked as a teacher , but became a full-time writer . in addition to books that she wrote , such as the `` eaglesmount '' series , she works with kate cary , tui sutherland , and victoria holmes under the pen name of erin hunter , writing the `` warriors '' and `` seekers '' series . she owns two cats , bramble and sorrel , and lives in england .
