Zinc bromide     zinc bromide is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is znbr . it contains zinc and bromide ions . zinc bromide is a colorless solid . it dissolves in water . it makes an acidic solution . it can be made by reacting barium bromide with zinc sulfate . this forms barium sulfate which can be filtered out . it can also be made by reacting zinc with bromine . it can also be made by reacting zinc with hydrogen bromide or hydrobromic acid . it can be used in organic chemistry . it can be used as a shield against radiation . there is a battery that uses zinc bromide . related pages . - zinc iodide - zinc chloride - zinc fluoride
