Country Grammar (Hot Shit)    country grammar ( hot shit ) `` country grammar ( hot shit ) ' '' is a 2000 midwestern hip hop song by american rapper nelly . the song was the debut single from his debut album country grammar . the song was released on february 29 , 2000. the song was parodied on madtv where it was turned into a rap-duet which was titled `` country slammer '' and was done by 2000 presidential and vice presidential candidates george w. bush and dick cheney .
