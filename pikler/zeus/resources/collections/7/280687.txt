King vulture     the king vulture ( `` sarcoramphus papa '' ) , is a large bird found in central and south america . this vulture lives predominantly in tropical lowland forests stretching from southern mexico to northern argentina . it is the only surviving member of the genus sarcoramphus , although fossil members are known . large and predominantly white , the king vulture has gray to black ruff , flight , and tail feathers . the head and neck are bald , with the skin color varying , including yellow , orange , blue , purple , and red . the king vulture has a very noticeable yellow fleshy caruncle on its beak .
