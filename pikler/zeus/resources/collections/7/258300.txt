Perchloric acid     perchloric acid is an acid that contains the perchlorate ion . it is produced by the oxidation of chlorate by electrolysis . the chlorate came from the chloralkali process . it is used to make ammonium perchlorate , which is used in rockets . it is a strong oxidizing agent and very corrosive . it is a colorless liquid . related pages . - perchlorate - potassium chlorate - ammonium
