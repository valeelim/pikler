Norbulingka Institute     the norbulingka institute was founded in 1988 , by the present 14th dalai lama at sidhpur , near dharamsala , india . the institute helps to preserve the tibetan language and culture . the institute is named after norbulingka , the traditional summer residence of the dalai lamas , in lhasa , tibet . the main building is a replica of the norbulingka palace in lhasa
