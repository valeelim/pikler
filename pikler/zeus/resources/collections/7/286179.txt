Hampton University     hampton university is a private university in hampton , virginia . the university has about 5,000 students . it was founded in 1868. the current president of the university is william r. harvey . it is a historically black university . the athletics teams of the university are called the pirates . they have 16 varsity teams and they compete in the mid-eastern athletic conference . other websites . - hampton university 's official website - hampton pirates ' official athletics website
