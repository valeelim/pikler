Central Connecticut State University     central connecticut state university is a public university in new britain , connecticut . the university has about 12,315 students . it was founded in 1849. the current president of the university is jack miller . the athletics teams of the university are called the blue devils . they have 14 varsity teams and they compete in the northeast conference . other websites . - central connecticut state university 's official website - central connecticut blue devils ' official athletics website
