Jackie Gayda     jacquelyn suzanne gayda-haas ( born november 3 , 1981 in strongsville , ohio ) is a semi-retired american professional wrestler who was best known for wrestling with world wrestling entertainment and total nonstop action wrestling ( tna ) . she won the second season of tough enough . she is married to charlie haas who she married in 2005. gayda won many things during her professional wrestling career : - world wrestling entertainment - tough enough ii ( with linda miles ) - total nonstop action wrestling - knockout of the year in 2005 - wrestling observer newsletter awards - worst worked match of the year in 2002 ( with christopher nowinski vs. bradshaw and trish stratus on `` raw '' on july 7 ) .
