Dave Creighton     david theodore creighton ( born june 24 , 1930 in port arthur , ontario ) is a canadian retired professional ice hockey forward . creighton started his national hockey league career with the boston bruins in 1948. he would also play with the toronto maple leafs , chicago black hawks , and new york rangers . he left the nhl after the 1960 season . he played several more seasons in the ahl before retiring after the 1969 season . after this , he coached the providence reds from 1969 to 1970 .
