Volkswagen Transporter     the volkswagen transporter is a range of vans and minibuses produced by volkswagen since 1950. before 1990 they were air-cooled with the engine in the rear , after 1990 the engine was moved to the front and water-cooled . related pages . - volkswagen type 2 officially known as the type 2 while air-cooled . - volkswagen caravelle - volkswagen multivan
