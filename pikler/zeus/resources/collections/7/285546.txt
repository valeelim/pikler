University of North Florida     university of north florida is a public university in jacksonville , florida . the university has about 16,509 students . it was founded in 1969. the current president of the university is john delaney . the athletics teams of the university are called the ospreys . they have 10 varsity teams . they compete in the atlantic sun conference . other websites . - university of north florida 's official website - north florida ospreys ' official athletics website
