Henry IV Probus     henry iv probus ( latin for `` the righteous '' ) ( ; ) ( c. 1258 – 23 june 1290 ) was a duke of the silesian territory of wrocław ( lower silesia ) starting in 1266 , and starting in 1288 high duke of the seniorate province of kraków until his death in 1290. he was the only son of henry iii the white , duke of wrocław , by his first wife judith , daughter of duke konrad i of masovia .
