Ankylosauridae     the ankylosauridae ) are a family of armoured dinosaurs which evolved 125 million years ago and became extinct 65 million years ago during the cretaceous-tertiary extinction event . ankylosaurids and their sister group , the nodosauridae make up the infraorder ankylosauria . they have been found in western north america , europe and east asia . good specimens are rare ; most are known only from fragments , and bits of their armour . taxonomy . - infraorder ankylosauria - family ankylosauridae - `` aletopelta '' - `` cedarpelta '' - `` gobisaurus '' - `` minotaurasaurus '' - `` shamosaurus '' - `` tatankacephalus '' - subfamily ankylosaurinae - `` ankylosaurus '' - `` euoplocephalus '' - `` nodocephalosaurus '' - `` pinacosaurus '' - `` saichania '' - `` shanxia '' - `` talarurus '' - `` tarchia '' - `` tianzhenosaurus '' - `` tsagantegia '' - subfamily polacanthinae - `` gargoyleosaurus '' - `` gastonia '' - `` hoplitosaurus '' - `` hylaeosaurus '' - `` mymoorapelta '' - `` polacanthus ''
