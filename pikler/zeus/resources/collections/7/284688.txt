Wilkes-Barre/Scranton Penguins     the wilkes-barre/scranton penguins are an american professional ice hockey team that began playing in 1999 they currently play in the american hockey league and are the farm team of the pittsburgh penguins of the nhl . they play their home games in wilkes-barre , pennsylvania at the mohegan sun arena at casey plaza . their echl affiliate team is the wheeling nailers . other websites . - wilkes-barre/scranton penguins ' official site
