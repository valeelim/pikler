University of Wisconsin–Milwaukee     the university of wisconsin–milwaukee is a public university in milwaukee , wisconsin , and part of the university of wisconsin system . the university has about 30,455 students . it was founded in 1956. the current chancellor of the university is michael lovell . the university calls itself `` milwaukee '' for athletic purposes , and its teams are nicknamed panthers . it has 15 varsity teams that compete in the horizon league . other websites . - university of wisconsin–milwaukee 's official website - milwaukee panthers ' official athletics website
