Firework (song)    firework ( song ) `` firework '' is a 2010 song by katy perry on her second album `` teenage dream '' . it was released on october 16 , 2010. the song received a good review by music critics and claimed the top spot on `` billboard '' hot 100. the single hit top 5 on twenty charts worldwide . at the 2012 grammy awards , `` firework '' was nominated for record of the year and best pop solo performance . `` firework '' was written by perry , mikkel s. eriksen , tor erik hermansen , sandy wilhelm , and ester dean while produced by stargate and sandy vee at soapbox studios in atlanta , georgia . it was recorded at roc the mic studios in new york city . it was mixed at the bunker studios by vee . audio engineering was done by carlos oyandel and damien lewis . all instruments were done by stargate and vee . lead and background vocals were provided by perry . perry said that `` firework '' is her favorite song on `` teenage dream '' . other websites . - `` firework '' on youtube
