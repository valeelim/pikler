Gingerbread man     gingerbread men are cookies made of gingerbread shaped like people . in america , they are most popular at christmastime . ingredients include ginger , molasses , spices , flour , butter , eggs , etc . the dough is rolled out on a flat surface , and the gingerbread man is made with a cookie cutter . the cookies can be frosted and decorated with various trims such as raisins and silver dragees .
