Mason Ryan     barri griffiths ( born january 13 , 1982 in tremadog , wales ) better known by his ring name mason ryan , is a welsh professional wrestler currently signed to world wrestling entertainment ( wwe ) and to the raw brand where he used to be part of the stable , the nexus . he also wrestled for florida championship wrestling where was a one time fcw florida heavyweight championship . griffiths is a former gladiator where he was known by the name , goliath . he has also wrestled in several wrestling tournaments throughout his home country wales before joining the wwe .
