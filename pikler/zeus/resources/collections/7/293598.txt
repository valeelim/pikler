Horse fly     a horse fly is a type of insect and a member of the family tabanidae ( order diptera ) , but more specifically are any member of the genus `` tabanus '' . these stout flies , as small as a housefly or as large as a bumblebee , are sometimes known as greenheaded monsters ; their metallic or iridescent eyes meet dorsally in the male and are separate in the female . gad fly , a nickname , may refer either to the fly ’ s roving habits or to its mouthparts , which resemble a wedge-shaped miner ’ s tool .
