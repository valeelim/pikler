2011 Myanmar earthquake     the 2011 myanmar earthquake was a 6.8 magnitude earthquake on march 24 in shan state , myanmar . its hypocenter was deep . it had two aftershocks , one of magnitude 4.8 , another at magnitude 5.4. the quake 's epicenter was from the northern thai city of chiang rai , north of mae sai and southeast of kentung . `` the bangkok post '' reported on 27 march 2011 , that the death toll was `` over 150 '' , which is double the official figure of 75 .
