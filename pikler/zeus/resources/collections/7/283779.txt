Calhoun County     calhoun county is the name of many counties in the united states , mostly named after senator john c. calhoun : - calhoun county , alabama - calhoun county , arkansas - calhoun county , florida - calhoun county , georgia - calhoun county , illinois - calhoun county , iowa - calhoun county , michigan - calhoun county , mississippi - calhoun county , south carolina - calhoun county , texas - calhoun county , west virginia
