Eric Nickulas     eric nickulas ( born march 25 , 1975 in hyannis , massachusetts ) is a american professional ice hockey right winger who last played for the erc ingolstadt of the deutsche eishockey liga ( del ) . nickulas has also played a total of 7 seasons in the nhl ; 4 with the boston bruins , 2 with the st. louis blues and 1 with the chicago blackhawks . he won the calder cup in 1998 while he was apart of the boston bruins farm team , the providence bruins . other websites . - eric nickulas at the internet hockey database
