Brandon Jones (American football)    brandon jones ( american football ) brandon jones ( born october 6 , 1982 in texarkana , texas ) is an american football wide receiver in the national football league who is currently a free agent . he was drafted out of the university of oklahoma by the tennessee titans with the 96th pick in the third round of the 2005 nfl draft . he also played for the tennessee titans from 2005 to 2008 , san francisco 49ers in 2009 and the seattle seahawks in 2010. he was also drafted by the new york yankees of major league baseball but he did n't sign a contract with them . in 2011 , the baltimore ravens signed jones . jones caught the game-winning touchdown from tyrod taylor in a preseason game against the washington redskins , but was released by the ravens . other websites . - brendon jones ' nfl player profile
