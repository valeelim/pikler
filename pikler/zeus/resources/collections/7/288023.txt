Brandon McKinney     brandon mckinney ( born august 24 , 1983 in dayton , ohio ) is an american football nose tackle for the indianapolis colts of the national football league ( nfl ) . mckinney did n't get drafted but played college football at michigan state university . mckinney started his nfl career with the san diego chargers in 2006 and played 3 seasons with them before signing a contract with the baltimore ravens on october 8 , 2008. he played four seasons with the ravens before signing with the indianapolis colts on april 4 , 2012. other websites . - brandon mckinney at nfl.com
