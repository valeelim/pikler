Volbeat     volbeat is an danish heavy metal band from copenhagen . volbeat formed in 2000 when singer michael poulsen formed a death metal band called dominus . he became tired of the death metal genre and left the band . he then formed a new band called volbeat with other members of dominus . discography . albums . - the strength / the sound / the songs ( 2005 ) - rock the rebel / metal the devil ( 2007 ) - guitar gangsters & cadillac blood ( 2008 ) - beyond hell/above heaven ( 2010 ) other websites . - volbeat 's official website
