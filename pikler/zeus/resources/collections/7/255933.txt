Khloé Kardashian     khloé alexandra kardashian ( born june 27 , 1984 ) is an american television personality . she is the sister of kim and kourtney kardashian . she was married to los angeles lakers basketball player lamar odom from 2009 to 2016. kardashian is the daughter of kris and robert kardashian sr. , who is known for representing o. j. simpson during his infamous 1995 trial . she has one brother , rob kardashian jr. she has had her own reality television series , `` kourtney and khloé take miami '' and `` khloé & lamar '' . she and her sisters own the fashion store dash and formerly owned the children 's store smooch , both run by her family . in 2017 , calling herself the `` fat , funny sister '' during her childhood , kardashian introduced the new e ! tv series `` revenge body with khloé kardashian '' , focused on helping contestants lose weight and improve physically and mentally over 12 weeks before making a reveal of their new body . the show was picked up for a second season .
