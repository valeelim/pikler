Halls of Illusions     `` halls of illusions ' '' is a 1997 midwest hip hop song by the american horrorcore band insane clown posse . it is the first single from their fourth studio album `` the great milenko '' ( 1997 ) . the song was produced by insane clown posse and mike clark . it featured slash on the guitar . the song is about the band 's hatred towards child abuse and domestic violence . the song has swearing but is loved by many juggalos .
