Woody Woodpecker     wooddrow `` woody `` woodpecker is an animated cartoon character , an anthropomorphic woodpecker who appeared in theatrical short movies produced by walter lantz , and distributed by universal pictures . woody woodpecker cartoon was created in 1940 by ben `` bugs '' hardaway . he also created the cartoon characters of bugs bunny and daffy duck when he worked at the schlesinger/warner bros. studio in the late 1930s . his niece and nephew are knothead and splinter . his girlfriend is winnie woodpecker . in `` the new woody woodpecker show '' , winnie is voiced by b. j. ward . related pages . - knothead and splinter - andy panda - chilly willy
