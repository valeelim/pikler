One-X     one-x is the second studio album by canadian rock band , three days grace . it was released on june 13 , 2006. the producer of the album was howard benson . the album peaked at # 5 on the u.s. `` billboard '' 200 and # 2 on the canadian albums chart . the songs `` riot '' and `` animal i have become '' were featured in the video game , `` wwe smackdown vs. raw 2010 '' .
