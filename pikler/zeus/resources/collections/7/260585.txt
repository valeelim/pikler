Kevin Harvick     kevin michael harvick ( born december 8 , 1975 ) is an american racecar driver . he drives the no . 4 budweiser/jimmy john 's chevrolet ss for stewart-haas racing in the nascar sprint cup series . harvick won the nascar nationwide series championship and the sprint cup rookie of the year award in 2001. in 2007 harvick beat mark martin to win the daytona 500. in 2014 harvick began racing for stewart-hass racing . over the year he was able to win a total of five races , and at the end of the year he was named the nascar sprint cup champion .
