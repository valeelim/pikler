Imperial Japanese Army     , also known as the ija , was the army of imperial japan from 1871 to 1945. the ija was dissolved at the end of world war ii . article 9 of the constitution of japan prohibits the use of aggressive force as a means for settling international disputes . in order to defend japan , if necessary , the japan self-defense forces ( jsdf ) was formed . the ground-reach of jsdf is the japan ground self-defense force ( jgsdf ) . related pages . - imperial japanese navy other websites . - axishistory.com , japanese army
