Ammonium perchlorate     ammonium perchlorate is a chemical compound composed of ammonium and perchlorate ions . it is a colorless crystalline chemical . it is a strong oxidizing agent and contains chlorine in its +7 oxidation state . it breaks down when heated slowly and detonates when heated strongly . it is made by the reaction of ammonia with perchloric acid . it is not very toxic . it is used in rockets . related pages . - potassium chlorate - sodium hypochlorite
