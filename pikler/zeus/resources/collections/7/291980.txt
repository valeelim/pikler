Life (Our Lady Peace song)    life ( our lady peace song ) `` 'life ' '' is a 2000 alternative rock song by the canadian alternative rock band our lady peace and is the second single from their fourth studio album `` spiritual machines '' , which was released in 2000. the song was written by raine maida and was produced by both arnold lanni and raine maida . the song was nominated for `` best single '' at the 2002 juno awards .
