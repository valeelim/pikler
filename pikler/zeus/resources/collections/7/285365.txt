Adventureland (movie)    adventureland ( movie ) adventureland is a 2009 american romantic comedy-drama movie . it is set in pittsburgh , pennsylvania in summer 1987. it was produced by sidney kimmel , anne carey and ted hope . it was written and directed by greg mottola . adventureland was released on april 3 , 2009 in north america . the movie received positive reviews with a 88 % rating on rotten tomatoes and a 76 out of 100 from metacritic . cast . - jesse eisenberg as james brennan - kristen stewart as emily `` em '' lewin - ryan reynolds as mike connell - martin starr as joel - matt bush as tommy frigo - margarita levieva as lisa p. - bill hader as bobby - kristen wiig as paulette - mary birdsong-covey as francy - josh pais as mr. lewin - jack gilpin as mr. brennan - wendie malick as mrs. brennan other websites . - adventureland 's official website
