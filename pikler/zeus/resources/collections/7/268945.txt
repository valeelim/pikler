Works Progress Administration     the works progress administration ( or wpa ) was an agency in the united states during the new deal . it was formed in 1935 , during the `` second new deal '' . it employed more workers than any government agency had before . it built thousands of roads , schools , and government buildings . part of it was the federal art project , which created , such as murals by diego rivera and the play `` the cradle will rock '' . the agency was led by harry hopkins . it lasted until world war ii . this program gave millions of people jobs during the great depression .
