4319 Jackierobinson     4319 jackierobinson ( 1981 er14 ) is a main-belt asteroid discovered on march 1 , 1981 by schelte j. bus at siding spring observatory . it was found during the u.k. schmidt-caltech asteroid survey . it was named after jackie robinson , the first african-american major league baseball player of the modern era . other websites . - jpl small-body database browser on 4319 jackierobinson
