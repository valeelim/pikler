Bromic acid     bromic acid is a chemical compound . it is also an acid . its chemical formula is hbro . it contains hydrogen and bromate ions . bromic acid can not be found pure . it can be found in a solution though . it is very corrosive and a strong oxidizing agent . it is similar to chloric acid . bromic acid is made by adding a strong acid to a bromate such as sodium bromate . bromic acid can be used to make bromates , although it is not normally used . related pages . - bromous acid - perbromic acid - hypobromous acid
