New Found Glory     new found glory ( used to be `` a new found glory '' ) is an american rock band from coral springs , florida . they formed in 1997. founding members were lead vocalist jordan pundik , guitarists chad gilbert and steve klein , bassist ian grushka and drummer joe moreno . current drummer cyrus bolooki replaced moreno after only a handful of shows and the lineup has since remained unchanged . their kind of music is said to have come from bands such as bands green day and nofx , to hardcore acts like hatebreed and madball . the band are known for their mix of pop melodies with the energy and fast tempos of classic punk rock . because of this , they are now widely known as a key pioneer in the fusion genre of pop punk . they are also noted for their wild live performances .
