Great Fire of New York (1776)    great fire of new york ( 1776 ) the great fire of new york was a large fire in new york city . it burned through the night of september 21 , 1776 on the west side of the former boundaries of new york city at the southern end of the island of manhattan . it started when the british army took control of the city during the american revolutionary war . the fire destroyed 10 to 25 % of the city . some of the unburned parts of the city were ransacked . many people thought that someone started the fire on purpose , for different reasons . british leaders accused american rebels , who had recently lost the battle of long island , of starting the fire . most people thought that one side or the other had started it .
