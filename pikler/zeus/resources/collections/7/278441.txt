Off the Wall     off the wall is an album by michael jackson . it was released in 1979 . 5 of the 10 songs on the album were released as singles : `` do n't stop 'til you get enough '' , `` rock with you '' , `` off the wall '' , `` she 's out of my life '' , and `` girlfriend '' . three of them had videos . it sold over 8 million copies in the united states and 20 million copies around the world . the album got good reviews . `` rolling stone '' compared his vocals to the `` breathless , dreamy stutter '' of stevie wonder and described his tenor as `` extraordinary beautiful '' . the first single , `` do n't stop 'til you get enough '' , reached no.1 . it won a grammy award for best male r & b vocal performance in 1980. however , jackson was disappointed at this . he said , `` it was totally unfair that it did n't get record of the year and it can never happen again '' . `` off the wall '' was jackson 's first album produced by quincy jones . in 2001 a special edition of the album was released by sony records with 9 extra tracks . `` rolling stone '' put the album at number 68 on their list of the 500 greatest albums of all time in 2003 .
