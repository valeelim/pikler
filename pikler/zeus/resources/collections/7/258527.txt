Full Circle (Drowning Pool album)    full circle ( drowning pool album ) full circle is the third album from hard rock band drowning pool . it is their first release with former soil singer ryan mccombs , who joined the band after the departure of jason 'gong ' jones in 2005. it was released on august 7 , 2007. this album has more of a post-grunge sound compared to the last two releases by the band and is less heavy as well . the first single off the album , `` soldiers , '' is dedicated to all the men and women of the united states army .
