Flame test     a flame test is a test for chemicals . normally it is used to see whether there are certain metals in a chemical compound . the chemical compound is heated in a flame and a colored flame is made . the colored flame can be turned into its spectrum to analyze it . many metals produce flame colors . the colors are made by electrons jumping up to higher orbits in the atom when it is heated . they fall down soon , putting out light as energy . related pages . - chemical reaction - ion
