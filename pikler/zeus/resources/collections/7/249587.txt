NGO Monitor     ngo monitor ( non-governmental organization monitor ) is a non-governmental organization based in jerusalem . ngo monitor produces and distributes statements and information about the international ngo community , all of which are carefully and fully checked out . this information is used by government policy makers , journalists , charity and philanthropic organizations and the general public . ngo monitor is an independent organization , started in 2001 , but only registered in 2007. ngo monitor has an online directory of ngos from around the world , which generally includes a description , a quote from the organization itself , who gives money to it , and relevant quotes from publications and officials . other websites . - ngo monitor home page
