Lamborghini Countach     the lamborghini countach is a sport car that was built by lamborghini from 1974 to 1990. the countach is named after the type of fighting bull . the countach was replaced by the lamborghini diablo . the designer of the countach was gruppo bertone . the production of the countach ended in 1990. top gear named the countach # 1 on their list of `` 100 sexiest supercars of all time '' . other websites . - lamborghini 's official website
