2010 Israel–Lebanon border clash     the 2010 israeli–lebanese clash occurred on august 3 , 2010. israel defence forces and lebanese armed forces fought along the border between israel and lebanon . early reports said three lebanese soldiers and one civilian were killed while five soldiers were wounded ; reports also said 1 israeli soldier was killed and 2 were wounded . this was the most serious fighting on the border since the 2006 lebanon war .
