Kite surfing     kite surfing or kite boarding is a kind of water sports that uses the wind to pull a rider on the water surface on a small surfboard or a kiteboard . there are a number of different styles of kiteboarding . a `` kitesurfer '' or `` kiteboarder '' uses a board with or without foot-straps or bindings , combined with the power of a large controllable kite to propel the rider and the board across the water . in 2006 , the number of kitesurfers has been estimated at around 150,000 to 210,000 , with 114,465 inflatable kites sold that same year . various surfing styles have appeared to suit both kitesurfers and conditions , such as `` waveriding '' , `` freestyle '' , `` freeride '' , `` jumping '' , and `` cruising '' .
