Demigod     demigods are people in mythology ( usually greek or roman ) who are half-god and half-human . they are special because of their godly parent . however , they normally have no great powers because of their human parent . demigods are usually mortal but not always . an example of one is hercules . his father , jupiter ( zeus ) , made him a god after hercules died . other demigods from classical mythology include achilles , heracles and romulus and remus .
