Melendi     ramón melendi espinosa ( born 21 january 1979 in oviedo , asturias ) is a spanish singer . he studied in the school , leopoldo alas `` clarín '' with the formula 1 pilot , fernando alonso . he wrote a song for him called `` magic alonso '' . in 2003 he made his first album `` sin noticias de holanda '' which had 12 songs . in december , the same year he made two new songs `` asturias '' and `` moratalá '' which were very successful . in 2004 one of his songs `` con la luna llena '' was chosen to represent `` la vuelta ciclista españa '' . one year later he made his second single album `` que el cielo espere sentao '' . he sold more than 200,000 copies of it . some years later he made three albums more . nowadays he plays in concerts around spain and south america .
