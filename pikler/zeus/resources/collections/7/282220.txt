Classics     classics refers to the branch of the humanities that includes languages , literature , philosophy , history , art , archaeology and other culture of the ancient mediterranean world ( bronze age ca . bc 3000 – late antiquity ca . ad 300–600 ) ; especially ancient greece and ancient rome during classical antiquity ( ca . bc 600 – ad 600 ) . initially , study of the classics ( the period 's literature ) was the principal study in the humanities . humanism also means the `` classics '' . it also involved civic humanists is the modern term for the moral , social and political philosophy that in the course of the fourteenth and fifteenth centuries began to be articulated in italian city-states and most notably in florence .
