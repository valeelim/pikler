Crystal Castles (album)    crystal castles ( album ) crystal castles is the first studio album by toronto-based duo crystal castles . the album was selected for the `` nme '' magazine 's list of the 50 greatest albums of the decade , and gained # 39. personnel . - alice glass ( vocals/lyrics ) - ethan kath ( music/samples/production ) - matthew von wagner ( vocal production for 3 tracks : `` alice practice '' , `` love and caring '' , `` xxzxcuzx me '' )
