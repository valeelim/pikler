Meg Whitman     margaret cushing whitman ( born august 4 , 1956 ) is an american billionaire . she is the president and chief executive officer of hewlett-packard . she was ceo of the online auction site ebay from 1998 to 2008. she also worked for dreamworks , procter & gamble , and hasbro . in 2010 , she ran for governor of california as a republican . her campaign was paid for mostly by millions of her own money . even though she spent over $ 100 million , she still lost to jerry brown .
