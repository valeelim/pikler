Bullshit     bullshit ( also bullcrap , bullplop , bullbutter , bovine stool ) is a common american english expletive . it can be used also as an interjection or as many other parts of speech . usually , it is a noun , verb or interjection that describes a lie . it shows that someone is trying to deceive , e.g . `` stop bullshitting me '' ( `` do n't try to deceive me . i know what you are saying is a lie . `` ) .
