Sodium carbonate     sodium carbonate is a chemical compound . it is composed of sodium and carbonate ions . its chemical formula is naco . it is a base . it reacts with acids to produce carbon dioxide . it is made by reacting sodium hydroxide with carbon dioxide or by heating sodium bicarbonate . it is produced industrially by the solvay process . it is used to make glass . it is also used in pools and cooking . it is used as an electrolyte . related pages . - sodium compounds - sodium chlorate - potassium carbonate
