John Bellamy Foster     john bellamy foster ( born august 19 , 1953 ) is editor of the independent socialist magazine `` monthly review '' and professor of sociology at the university of oregon in eugene . his writings have focused on political economy , environmental sociology , and marxist theory . foster 's most recent book , `` the ecological revolution : making peace with the planet '' , examines the ecological crisis , and includes essays on global warming , peak oil , species extinction , world water shortages , global hunger , alternative energy sources , sustainable development , and environmental justice . foster says that any attempt to solve our problems merely by technological , industrial or free market means , divorced from fundamental social relations , can not succeed .
