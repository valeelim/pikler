Ávila Province    ávila province ávila province ( ) is a province of central-western spain , in the southern part of the autonomous community of castile and león . it is bordered on the south by the provinces of toledo and cáceres , on the west by salamanca , on the north by valladolid , and on the east by segovia and madrid . ávila has a population of 165,138 ( 2002 ) . its capital is ávila .
