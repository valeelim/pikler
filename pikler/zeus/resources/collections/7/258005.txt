Dangerous (Kardinal Offishall song)    dangerous ( kardinal offishall song ) dangerous is a 2008 r & b song by kardinal offishall and featured r & b singer akon . the song was released march 2008 on the radio and april 1 , 2008 on itunes . the song is from kardinal offishall 's 2008 album , not 4 sale . the song won single of the year at the 2009 juno awards and was certified 3x platinum by the cria .
