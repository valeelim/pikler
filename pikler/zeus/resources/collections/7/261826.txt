Malcolm in the Middle     malcolm in the middle is a fox television series . it ran from january 9 , 2000 to may 14 , 2006 . `` malcolm in the middle '' won 9 emmy awards , 1 peabody award and 1 grammy award . cast . - frankie muniz as malcolm - jane kaczmarek as lois - bryan cranston as hal - christopher masterson as francis - justin berfield as reese - erik per sullivan as dewey other websites . - `` malcolm in the middle '' on fox.com
