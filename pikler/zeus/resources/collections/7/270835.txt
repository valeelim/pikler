The Final Frontier     the final frontier is an album by iron maiden . it was announced on 4 march 2010 , along a concert tour . it was recorded in the bahamas and produced by kevin shirley . the album began being sold on 17 august 2010. it sold very well , being the most popular album in 21 countries when it went on sale , as well as being one of the top five most popular albums in eight other countries . musicians . - bruce dickinson - vocals - janick gers - guitar - steve harris - bass guitar - nicko mcbrain - percussion - dave murray - guitar - adrian smith - guitar
