Faneuil Hall     faneuil hall is an historic place near the waterfront in boston , massachusetts . it was built in 1742. it has been a marketplace and a meeting hall . today it is the city 's government center . it was the site of several speeches by samuel adams and others encouraging independence from great britain . it is now part of boston national historical park and a stop on the freedom trail . it is sometimes called `` the cradle of liberty '' .
