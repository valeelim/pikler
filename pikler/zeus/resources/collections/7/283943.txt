Inclusive education     inclusive education is an idea in the field of education . it means that persons with disabilities or persons who need special care can learn in schools together with people who do not have special needs . this idea comes from the idea of social justice , which demands that all people have human rights without any discriminations . the convention on the rights of persons with disabilities also says that access to education is a right . however , inclusive education may make special aid , care or knowledge for persons with disabilities difficult if enough reasonable accommodation are not given . other pages . - disability - education - special education - convention on the rights of persons with disabilities
