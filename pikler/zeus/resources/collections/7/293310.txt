Hydrosphere     the hydrosphere is the total mass of water on , under , and over the surface of a planet . it is part of physical geography . the total mass of the earth ’ s hydrosphere is about 1.4 × 10 grams , which is about 0.023 % of the earth ’ s total mass . around 2 × 10 grams of this is the earth ’ s atmosphere . in addition , 71 % of the earth ’ s surface , an area of 361 million square kilometers , is covered by ocean .
