Tim Lincecum     timothy leroy lincecum ( born june 15 , 1984 in bellevue , washington ) is a filipino american baseball player . he is currently a free agent of major league baseball ( mlb ) . lincecum was the nl cy young award winner in 2009 and 2008 , won the golden spikes award in 2006 and led major league baseball with 265 strikeouts in 2008. other websites . - tim lincecum at mlb.com
