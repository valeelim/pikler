Zack Ryder     matthew cardona ( born may 14 , 1985 in merrick , new york ) better known by his ring name zack ryder , is an american professional wrestler currently signed to world wrestling entertainment ( wwe ) and to the raw brand . ryder was also known for being part of a tag team with curt hawkins . ryder is a one time wwe tag team champion with curt hawkins and has also won a slammy award for most annoying catchphrase in 2010 .
