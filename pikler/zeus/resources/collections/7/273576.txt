Nikolai Tsukanov     nikolai nikolaevich tsukanov ( russian : николай николаевич цуканов ) ( born march 22 , 1965 is a russian politician . he is from the village lipovo , kaliningrad oblast , russia ) . in september 2010 , he became governor of the kaliningrad oblast . he became secretary of the kaliningrad regional council of the party `` united russia '' in 2010. he was also the mayor of the municipality `` gusev city district '' in 2005-2010 .
