Medal of Honor (2010 video game)    medal of honor ( 2010 video game ) medal of honor is a first-person shooter video game . it was developed by danger close and ea digital illusions ce ( dice ) . it is the twelfth game in the `` medal of honor '' series . the game was released for microsoft windows , playstation 3 and xbox 360 on october 12 , 2010. the game is set in the ongoing war in afghanistan .
