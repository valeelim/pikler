Meresankh I     meresankh i was ancient egyptian queen of the 3rd dynasty of egypt . she was a lesser ranked wife of pharaoh huni , mother of pharaoh sneferu , grandmother of pharaoh khufu . one of her great-granddaughters was named meresankh ii . name of meresankh appears on a fragment of the palermo stone . she is also mentioned at meidum . title of meresankh was `` king 's wife '' . queens ' name means `` she loves life '' . here is a name of this queen written in hieroglyphs : u6-s29-s34
