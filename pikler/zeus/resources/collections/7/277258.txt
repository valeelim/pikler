Ian Nepomniachtchi     ian nepomniachtchi ( born 14 july 1990 ) is a russian chess grandmaster and former russian chess champion . he won the european youth chess championship three times , in 2000 in the u10 class and in 2001 and 2002 in the u12 class . in 2002 he also won the world youth chess championship in the u12 class . by winning the aeroflot open in moscow in february 2008 , he qualified for the 2008 dortmund sparkassen chess meeting . in 2010 , in rijeka , he won the european individual chess championship with 9/11 . in the same year , in moscow , he won the russian chess championship ; he defeated sergey karjakin in a playoff .
