Dr. Dolittle (movie)    dr. dolittle ( movie ) dr. dolittle is a comedy movie released in 1998 by davis entertainment/20th century fox . it stars eddie murphy who plays dr. dolittle . he is a doctor who lives in san francisco . he discovers that he has the ability to talk to ( and understand ) animals . it was marketed as a family movie and earned $ 294,456,605 . it had 1 sequel of the same format and 3 direct-to-video sequels . there was and is a video game based on the movie which was and is simulation game `` dr. dolittle '' by blast ! entertainment ltd/aqua pacific which was released in 2006 .
