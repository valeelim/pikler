Sundre, Alberta    sundre , alberta sundre , in alberta , is a rural town of about 2,000 in population . it is a half hour drive east of the rocky mountains . the mayor of sundre is annette clews . sundre is home to the pro rodeo . sundre has one public kindergarten to grade 8 school called river valley school with chinooks edge school division . and there is one public high school called sundre high school . sundre became a village in 1950 and a town in 1956 .
