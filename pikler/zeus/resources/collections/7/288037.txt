Cranbrook, British Columbia    cranbrook , british columbia cranbrook is a canadian city in british columbia , canada . cranbrook is in the southeastern and the east kootenay region of british columbia . at the 2006 census , 18,947 people lived in cranbrook . it was named a city in 1905. cranbrook is also home to an whl team called the kootenay ice . other websites . - city of cranbrook 's official website
