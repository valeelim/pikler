Kristján Eldjárn     dr. kristján eldjárn ( december 6 , 1916 – september 14 , 1982 ) was the 3rd president of iceland . he was president from 1968 to 1980. he won as the underdog in the 1968 presidential election , winning 65.6 % of the vote on a 92.2 % voter turnout running against gunnar thoroddsen . his son is þórarinn eldjárn who is a popular author in iceland and his daughter sigrún eldjárn is also an author .
