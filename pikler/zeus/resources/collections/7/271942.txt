Ryan Caldwell     ryan caldwell ( born june 15 , 1981 in brandon , manitoba ) is a canadian ice hockey defenceman . he currently plays for the schwenninger wild wings of the deutsche eishockey liga ( del ) . he has also played for the new york islanders and phoenix coyotes of the national hockey league ( nhl ) , the deg metro stars , eisbären berlin , and thomas sabo ice tigers of the del , and ässät and lukko of the sm-liiga . as an amateur he was an all-american defenceman and captained the university of denver pioneers to the 2004 ncaa championship .
