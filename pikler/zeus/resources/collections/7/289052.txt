Glenn Gilbertti     glenn gilbertti ( born november 12 , 1968 in brooklyn , new york ) is an american professional wrestler who was best known for wrestling with world championship wrestling ( wcw ) and total nonstop action wrestling ( tna ) under the ring name , disco inferno . he currently works as a host at the sapphire gentlemen 's club , which is a strip club in las vegas , nevada . gilbertti won many championships during his professional wrestling career : - great championship wrestling - gcw heavyweight championship ( one time ) - gcw tag team championship ( one time ) with johnny swinger - gcw television championship ( three times ) - gcw united states junior heavyweight championship ( one time ) - impact pro wrestling ( new zealand ) - armageddon cup in 2008 - mid-eastern wrestling federation - mewf heavyweight championship ( one time ) - north georgia wrestling association - ngwa tag championship ( one time ) ( with ashley clark ) - palmetto pride championship wrestling - ppcw heavyweight championship ( one time )
