International Day Against Homophobia     international day against homophobia is a day for campaigning against homophobia and transphobia held every may 17. on this day in 1990 , the world health organization removed homosexuality from the international classification of diseases , where it was listed as a mental illness . the declaration of montreal demands all countries to recognize this day , and the european union and brazil have already recognized formally this memorial day . other pages . - homophobia - transphobia - transgender day of remembrance - declaration of montreal
