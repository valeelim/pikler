Calcium phosphate     calcium phosphate is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is ca ( po ) , although there are other formulas , like cahpo ( dicalcium phosphate ) and ca ( hpo ) ( monocalcium phosphate ) . it is made of calcium and phosphate ions . it is a white or gray solid . it becomes more insoluble when heated ; most chemicals become more soluble ( more easily dissolved ) . it is found in teeth , bones , and milk . it is found in the ground , too . it is used to make phosphoric acid . it is used to make fertilizer . it is also used in bovine milk related pages . - calcium carbonate - calcium chloride - phosphite
