Zhytomyr     zhytomyr ( ) is a city in ukraine , the administrative center of zhytomyr oblast . nearly 271 000 people live here ( ) . zhytomyr is a big transportation center . the city lies on a historic way that connects the city of kiev with the west through brest . today it connects warsaw with kiev , minsk with izmail , and several major cities of ukraine . zhytomyr was also the location of ozerne , an important cold war strategic plane base that was situated southeast of the city . zhytomyr is divided into two districts . it is located on the teteriv river . as a legend says , zhytomyr was founded about 884 by zhytomyr , prince of a slavic tribe of drevlians . this date , 884 , is cut in the large stone of the ice age times , that stands on the hill where it was founded .
