Jake Dowell     jacob dowell ( born march 4 , 1985 in eau claire , wisconsin ) is a american professional ice hockey center that currently plays for the dallas stars of the national hockey league ( nhl ) . he was selected by the chicago blackhawks with the 140th pick in the 2004 nhl draft and played 4 seasons with them before being signed to the dallas stars on july 1 , 2011. dowell also played for the chicago blackhawks ahl farm team , the rockford icehogs .
