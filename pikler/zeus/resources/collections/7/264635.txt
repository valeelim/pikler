Macintosh Classic     the macintosh classic is a computer made by apple inc. in late october 1990 and sales ended september 1992. it has a motorola 68000 at 8mhz ( how fast the cpu is ) . the macintosh 128k , originally released as the apple macintosh , is the original apple macintosh personal computer . it had an base retail price of $ 2,495 ( close to $ 5,692 in 2016 ) . as the release of its successor , the macintosh 512k , it was renamed the macintosh 128k .
