Nissan Altima     the nissan altima is a vehicle made by nissan motor company . it has been around since 1993 and has had four generations . the car won the 2002 north american car of the year and the 2010 altima received the nhtsa 's highest front- impact crash safety rating ( five stars ) . there is a hybrid version of the altima available which was first made in 2007. the car comes in a 4-door sedan and a 2-door coupe . other websites . - altima at nissanusa.com
