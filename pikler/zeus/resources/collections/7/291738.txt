A Rugrats Passover     `` a rugrats passover '' ( possibly known as `` passover '' in the opening title ) is the sixty-fifth episode of the nickelodeon show `` rugrats '' . it aired april 13 , 1995. while attending a passover seder at didi 's parents ' house , boris and the kids ( and later , most of the adults ) , get locked in the attic because the door does n't open from the inside . to pass the time , boris tells the kids the story of why jews celecrate passover , and how moses saved his people from slavery . cast . - tommy pickles ( voiced by e.g . daily ) - chuckie finster ( voiced by christine cavanaugh ) - angelica pickles ( voiced by cheryl chase ) - didi pickles ( voiced by melanie chartoff ) - grandpa boris ( voiced by david doyle ) - grandma minka ( voiced by melanie chartoff ) `` a rugrats passover '' was released on the vhs `` rugrats : a rugrats passover '' , and will be released on the dvd `` rugrats : the complete series '' .
