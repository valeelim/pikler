Commelinids     the name commelinids is a plant taxonomy ( plural , not capitalised ) is used by the apg ii system for a clade within the monocots , which in its turn is a clade within the angiosperms . the commelinids are the only clade that the apg ii has named within the monocots , the remaining monocots are a paraphyletic unit , occasionally referred to as the `` basal monocots '' .
