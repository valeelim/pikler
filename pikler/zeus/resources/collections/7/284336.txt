Leukodystrophy     leukodystrophies are disorders which affect the white matter of the brain and the nervous system . nerves are surrounded by fatty cover of a substance called myelin . in a leukodystrophy , there are problems with the growth or the development of this cover . this affects the function of the respective nerve . myelin is made of at least ten different chemicals . leukodystrophy is a gene defect . as a result of this defect , one of the molecules that make up myelin can not be synthesized as it should .
