Meet the Parents     meet the parents is a 2000 american comedy movie . it was produced by robert de niro , jay roach , jane rosenthal and nancy tenenbaum and was directed by jay roach . `` meet the parents '' is a remake of the 1992 movie of the same name . there were two sequels to `` meet the parents '' . the first sequel was `` meet the fockers '' , which was released in 2004. the second sequel was `` little fockers '' , which was released in 2010. cast . - ben stiller as gaylord `` greg '' focker - robert de niro as jack byrnes - teri polo as pam byrnes - blythe danner as dina byrnes - nicole dehuff as deborah byrnes - jon abrahams as denny byrnes - owen wilson as kevin rawley other websites . - meet the parents ' official website
