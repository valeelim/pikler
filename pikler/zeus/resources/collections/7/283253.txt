Bad Romance     `` bad romance '' is a song by american recording artist lady gaga . it was released as the lead single from her third extended play ( ep ) , and second major release `` the fame monster '' on october 26 , 2009. gaga has said that the song is about being in love with a best friend . there are some lines sung in french in the song . the song reached # 1 in many countries including canada , the uk , germany , france , italy , and spain , while reaching # 2 in the us and australia . it was certified diamond in america and has sold over 5.5 million downloads in that country . `` bad romance '' won gaga the 2011 grammy awards for best pop vocal performance and for best short form music video .
