Kate Cary     kate cary ( born 4 november 1967 in england ) is one of the authors who write the `` warriors '' book series using the name erin hunter . she works with cherith baldry , victoria holmes , and tui sutherland while writing these books . as well , she has written books on her own , such as `` bloodline '' and `` reckoning '' . kate cary owns three cats , which she revealed on the warriors website . she also has a son , and has lived in scotland , but now lives in england . other websites . - warriors website - kate cary 's blog
