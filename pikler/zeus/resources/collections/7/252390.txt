Summer of 4 Ft. 2     `` summer of 4 ft. 2 '' is the season finale of `` the simpsons '' 7th season . it originally aired on may 19 , 1996. in this episode , ned flanders has to do jury duty , and therefore ca n't go to his beach house . he allows the simpsons to use it for 2 weeks . lisa , realizing that she is unpopular in springfield , decides to try to make friends in this new place . after getting a new wardrobe , she meets some new kids and befriends them by not being herself . bart , jealous of lisa 's new popularity , shows her new friends that lisa is not cool . lisa is furious at bart , but gets happier when her friends accept her for who she is .
