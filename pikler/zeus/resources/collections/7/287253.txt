Biophilia     biophilia is the musical project and seventh studio album from icelandic singer björk . the album was released in 2011. the album is `` partly recorded '' on an ipad and was released in the form of a series of apps . the project is directed by michel gondry . björk has described the project as a multimedia collection `` encompassing music , apps , internet , installations and live shows '' . material from the album debuted in summer 2011 during a series of concerts at the manchester international festival . tracklist . 1. moon 1. thunderbolt 1. crystalline 1. cosmogony 1. dark matter 1. hollow 1. virus 1. sacrifice 1. mutual core 1. solstice
