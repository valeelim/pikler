Neoptera     neoptera is a classification group that has almost all the winged insects , mostly those that can flex their wings over their abdomens . this is in contrast with the more basal orders of winged insects , which are not able to flex their wings in this way . the neoptera can be contrasted with the palaeoptera , the 'ancient-winged ' insects , which include the odonata , the ephemeroptera and extinct groups such as the palaeodictyoptera .
