Homininae     homininae is a subfamily of hominidae , which includes humans , gorillas and chimpanzees . it also includes some extinct human relatives such as ardipithecus and australopithecus . it comprises all those hominids , such as `` australopithecus '' , that arose after the split from the other great apes . taxonomic classification . - tribe gorillini - genus gorilla - western gorilla , ( `` gorilla gorilla '' ) - western lowland gorilla , ( `` gorilla gorilla gorilla '' ) - cross river gorilla , ( `` gorilla gorilla diehli '' ) - eastern gorilla , ( `` gorilla beringei '' ) - mountain gorilla , ( `` gorilla beringei beringei '' ) - eastern lowland gorilla , ( `` gorilla beringei graueri '' ) - tribe hominini - genus pan - common chimpanzee , ( `` pan troglodytes '' ) - central chimpanzee , ( `` pan troglodytes troglodytes '' ) - west african chimpanzee , ( `` pan troglodytes verus '' ) - nigeria-cameroon chimpanzee , ( `` pan troglodytes ellioti '' ) - eastern chimpanzee , ( `` pan troglodytes schweinfurthii '' ) - bonobo ( pygmy chimpanzee ) , ( `` pan paniscus '' )
