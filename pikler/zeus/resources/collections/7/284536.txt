David Baltimore     david baltimore ( born march 7 , 1938 in new york city ) is an american biologist . he won the nobel prize in physiology or medicine in 1975 for discovering reverse transcriptase . he shared the prize with howard temin and renato dulbecco . the discovery of reverse transcriptase was made at the same time as howard temin . it overturned the central dogma of molecular biology because it showed that genetic information could traffic both ways between dna and rna . they published these findings in back-to-back papers in the journal , `` nature '' .
