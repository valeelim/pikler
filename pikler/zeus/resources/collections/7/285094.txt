American Beauty (movie)    american beauty ( movie ) american beauty is a 1999 american comedy-drama movie that won five academy awards , including best picture . it was directed by sam mendes and written by alan ball . it starred kevin spacey , annette bening , thora birch and mena suvari . the movie centres on a dysfunctional middle-class suburban family and their neighbors . it centers on the father 's midlife crisis and his infatuation with his daughter 's friend .
