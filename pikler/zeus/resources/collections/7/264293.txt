United States Grand Prix West     the united states grand prix west was a formula one motor race held at street circuit in long beach , california . it was a formula 5000 race in 1975 , and a f1 world championship event from 1976 to 1983. after 1983 the event became the long beach grand prix of the cart series . `` no driver has won the united states grand prix west more than once .
