Soyuz (spacecraft)    soyuz ( spacecraft ) soyuz ( , ) is a series of spacecraft designed for the soviet space programme . it was first used in the 1960s . the soyuz spacecraft is launched on a soyuz rocket , the most frequently used and most reliable launch vehicle in the world to date . all soyuz spacecraft are launched from the baikonur cosmodrome in kazakhstan . the first soyuz flight had no people aboard . it launched on november 28 , 1966. the first soyuz mission with a crew , soyuz 1 , launched on 23 april 1967 but crashed , killing cosmonaut vladimir komarov . soyuz 3 ( october 26 , 1968 ) was the first successful manned mission . now , the soyuz is widely considered the world 's safest , most cost-effective human spaceflight vehicle . soyuz spacecraft were used to carry cosmonauts to and from salyut and later mir soviet space stations , and are now used for transport to and from the international space station ( iss ) . at least one soyuz spacecraft is docked at the iss at all times for use as an escape craft in the event of an emergency .
