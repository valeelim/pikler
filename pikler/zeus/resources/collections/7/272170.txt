Group 14 element     the group 14 elements , also known as the carbon group and the group iv elements , is a group of elements on the periodic table . each element has 4 valence electrons . only carbon makes anions . silicon and germanium are semimetals . tin and lead are poor metals . ununquadium , a radioactive element , is not stable enough to see its properties . all of them can form +4 oxidation state compounds . tin and lead can form +2 compounds , too .
