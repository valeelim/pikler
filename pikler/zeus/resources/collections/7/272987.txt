LG Corp.     lg corp. is the second largest korean multi-industry company that produces electronics , chemicals , and telecommunications products and operates subsidiaries like lg electronics , lg display , lg telecom , lg boring , and lg chem in over 80 countries . lg corp. established as lak-hui chemical industrial corp. in 1947. in 1952 , lak-hui became the first korean company to enter the plastics industry . while the company expanded its plastic business , it established goldstar co. ltd. ( currently lg electronics inc. ) in 1958. both companies lucky and goldstar merged and formed lucky goldstar . in 1995 , to compete better in the western market , the lucky-goldstar corporation was renamed `` lg '' . the company also associates the letters lg with the company 's tagline `` life 's good '' .
