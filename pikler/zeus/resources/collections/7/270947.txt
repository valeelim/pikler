Sodium bromate     sodium bromate is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is nabro , which means that one molecule of it contains one atom of sodium and one ion of bromate . sodium bromate is a colorless solid . it is a strong oxidizing agent . sodium bromate is made by reacting sodium hydroxide with bromic acid . it can also be made by electrolysis of a sodium bromide solution . it may be made by dissolving bromine in hot sodium hydroxide solution . this also makes sodium bromide . it is used to dye hair . it is also used to dye other things . it can be used to dissolve gold . related pages . - potassium bromate - calcium bromate
