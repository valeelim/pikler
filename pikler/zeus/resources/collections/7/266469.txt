Sodium selenide     sodium selenide is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is nase . it contains sodium and selenide ions . sodium selenide is a solid . it reacts with water to make a mixture of sodium hydroxide and sodium hydroselenide . it is a reducing agent . it is oxidized to a mixture of selenide and selenium , making an offwhite color . it is eventually oxidized to selenium dioxide . it reacts with acids to make hydrogen selenide gas . it is made by reacting selenium with a solution of sodium in liquid ammonia . sodium selenide is used to react with some organic compounds . related pages . - sodium selenate - sodium selenite
