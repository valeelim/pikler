Annette Bening     annette bening ( born may 29 , 1958 ) is an american movie and television actress . she was born near topeka , kansas . she has been nominated four times for an academy award during her career . bening is a four-time oscar nominee for her roles in `` the grifters '' , `` american beauty '' , `` being julia '' , and `` the kids are all right '' , winning golden globe awards for the latter two films . she won a bafta and a sag award for her performance in `` american beauty '' . in 1995 , bening appeared in `` the american president '' , playing sydney wade , a pr ( public relations ) consultant who dates the president , played by michael douglas . from 1984 to 1991 , annette was married to j. steven white . on march 3 , 1992 , she married warren beatty . they have four children .
