List of Nazi concentration camps     this article lists some of concentration camps set up by the third reich , and includes camps such as dachau which was set up at first to help the nazis keep power and control political opponents , and other camps such as auschwitz which was set up to help fulfil the final solution . the german ministry of justice , in 1967 , named about 1200 camps and subcamps in countries occupied by nazi germany , while the jewish virtual library writes `` it is estimated that the nazis established 15,000 camps in the occupied countries .
