Vernon, British Columbia    vernon , british columbia vernon is a canadian city in british columbia , canada . vernon is in the south-central region of british columbia and in the north okanagan region . at the 2006 consensus , 33,846 people were living there . vernon was named one of the top six most desirable communities to retire to in north america by consumer reports in 2005. vernon has a bchl ( british columbia hockey league ) ice hockey team called the vernon vipers . other websites . - town of vernon 's official website
