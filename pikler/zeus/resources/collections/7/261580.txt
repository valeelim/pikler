Yamatai     the yamatai was the strongest country in wa , beginning from the early 2nd century to the end of the 3rd century . wa is the oldest written name of japan . the wei zhi worenshuan is a chinese text that tells about this country . yamatai came into being when about 30 smaller parts of japan of that time united under a queen named himiko . yamatai is also famous for trading with wei , which is a chinese dynasty . no one knows where the yamatai can be found . some archaeologists dispute whether the yamatai kingdom was located in northern kyushu or in the central kinki region .
