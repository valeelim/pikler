J.J. Redick     jonathan clay `` j.j. '' redick ( born june 24 , 1984 in cookeville , tennessee ) is an american professional basketball player . he is currently a shooting guard for the los angeles clippers of the national basketball association ( nba ) . he has also played for the orlando magic from 2006 until 2013 and for the milwaukee bucks in 2013. redick was born in cookeville , tenneesee , and played college basketball for four years at duke university . on july 10 , 2013 , the clippers picked up redick in a sign-and-trade , three-team deal which also involved the milwaukee bucks and phoenix suns . redick was reported to have signed a four-year , $ 27 million contract .
