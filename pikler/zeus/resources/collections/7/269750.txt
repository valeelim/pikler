Galba     servius sulpicius galba ( lucius livius ocella sulpicius galba , 24 december 3 bc15 january ad 69 ) was a roman emperor for seven months from ad 68 to 69. galba was an governor of hispania tarraconensis , which was the province near the pyrenees . he was the first emperor of year of the four emperors . he was born near terracina , `` on the left as you travel towards fundi '' in the words of suetonius . galba was adopted by livia ocellina and took her family name until becoming emperor .
