L-DOPA     levodopa , or -dopa is an amino acid . in the body of mammals , it is made from tyrosine in a process named synthesis . it also occurs in different plants . as such it can be used as a dietary supplement . l-dopa is the precursor to the neurotransmitters dopamine , norepiphedrine and epiphedrine , which are part of a group known as catecholamines . l-dopa can also be used as a drug for diseases such as parkinson 's disease . the substance used in the drug is usually called `` levodopa '' , and the naturally occurring substance is called `` l-dopa '' .
