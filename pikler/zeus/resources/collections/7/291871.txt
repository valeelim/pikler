Litefoot     gary paul davis ( born march 1 , 1969 in upland , california ) , known by his stage name litefoot , is an american rapper and actor . he is of cherokee and chichimeca descent . he was the first native american person to rap and he called his style of music , `` tribalistic funk '' . david is also an actor and acted in the movies the indian in the cupboard in 1995 , in 1997 , kull the conqueror in 1997 and adaptation in 2002. websites . - litefoot 's official website
