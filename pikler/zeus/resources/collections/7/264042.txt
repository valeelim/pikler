Hurricane Bob     hurricane bob struck north carolina and southeast new england during august 1991. it was caused by a tropical depression in the bahamas , which came inland and turned into a hurricane . bob took 18 lives , injured 190 and created $ 1.5 billion in damage . the storm was one of the worst hurricanes to impact the new england during the 20th century , the 1938 hurricane being the worst of them . bob was strong for a category 3 hurricane .
