Ipswich, Queensland    ipswich , queensland ipswich is a large city in queensland , australia . it is about 40 minutes drive west of the state capital , brisbane . it has a population of about 155,000 people . ipswich is the oldest provincial city it queensland , and has more than 6000 heritage listed houses , shops , public buildings and historic sites . it is also the site of the amberley air base , the royal australian air force 's largest operating base .
