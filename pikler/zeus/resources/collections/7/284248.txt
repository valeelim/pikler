Paul Hermann Müller     paul hermann müller ( january 12 , 1899 – october 12 , 1965 ) was an swiss chemist . he won the nobel prize in physiology or medicine in 1948 for his discovery of insecticidal qualities and use of ddt in the control of vector diseases . he was born on january 12 , 1899 in olten , switzerland and died on october 12 , 1965 in basel , switzerland .
