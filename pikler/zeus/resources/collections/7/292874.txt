R2-D2     r2-d2 ( phonetically spelled artoo-detoo , and called `` r2 '' for short ) , is a fictional character in the star wars universe . an astromech droid , r2-d2 is a major character throughout all seven star wars films . along with his droid companion c-3po , he joins or supports anakin skywalker , luke skywalker , and obi-wan kenobi in various points in the saga . r2-d2 was played by kenny baker .
