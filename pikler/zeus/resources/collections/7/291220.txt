Traveler's cheque    traveler 's cheque a traveler 's cheque ( also traveller 's cheque , travellers cheque , traveller 's check or traveler 's check ) is a preprinted cheque for a fixed amount . the person who buys it must sign it and then sign it again when he `` cashes '' it . it is a good way for a traveller to take money when going on holiday . if a traveler 's cheque is stolen it can be replaced so long as the person still has a receipt to prove that he bought it . traveler 's cheques were a large part of the business of american express . since 2005 the american express have been issuing `` american express travelers cheque cards '' . these can be used in stores like a credit card . other websites . - american express traveler 's cheques merchant site
