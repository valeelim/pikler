Chrome alum     chrome alum , also known as chromium potassium sulfate , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is kcr ( so ) . it contains chromium , potassium , and sulfate ions . the chromium is in its +3 oxidation state . it is a dark purple solid . it is normally attached to extra water molecules . it dissolves in water . it is made by reducing potassium dichromate with sulfur dioxide . it was used in making leather ; now chromium ( iii ) sulfate is used instead . related pages . - alum - aluminium sulfate
