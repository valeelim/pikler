Southcentral Alaska     southcentral alaska is a part of the u.s. state of alaska . it is around the gulf of alaska . most of the people of the state live in this part of the state . anchorage , the largest city in alaska , is here . 40 % of the people in alaska live in or near anchorage . the area includes cook inlet , the matanuska-susitna valley , the kenai peninsula , prince william sound , and the copper river valley . tourism , fishing , and making petroleum are the biggest businesses . other towns include palmer , wasilla , kenai/soldotna , homer , seward , valdez , and cordova .
