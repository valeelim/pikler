Alexander Sulzer     alexander sulzer ( born may 30 , 1984 in kaufbeuren , germany ) is an nhl ice hockey defenceman that currently plays for the florida panthers . sulzer has also played in the nhl for the nashville predators for 3 seasons and the del for the hamburg freezers and deg metro stars . he was traded to the florida panthers on february 25 , 2011 for a conditional draft pick in the 2011 nhl draft .
