Thallium(III) oxide    thallium ( iii ) oxide thallium ( iii ) oxide , also known as thallic oxide , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is tlo . it has thallium and oxide ions in it . the thallium is in its +3 oxidation state . thallium ( iii ) oxide is a white powder . it is very heavy and very toxic . it is a semiconductor . it reacts with acids to make other toxic thallium compounds . it does not dissolve in water . it is made by oxidation of thallium ( i ) oxide . it may be used in solar cells . related pages . - thallium ( i ) chloride - silver ( i ) oxide
