Aeolus     aeolus or eolus was the king of winds in greek mythology . although he varied from story to story , he was sometimes said to have had all the winds in a bag . in the odyssey , he gave that bag to odysseus to help him get home . aeolus was the son of hellen and a nymph named orseis . aeolus ' brothers were dorus , xuthus and amphictyon .
