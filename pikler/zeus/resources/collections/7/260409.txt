Chamomile     chamomile ( `` kam-ə-meel or kam-ə-myl '' ) , also known as camomile , is a plant from the genus `` anthemis '' . its name comes from the greek word `` chamaimelon '' , meaning `` ground apple '' , because of its smell . it has white or yellow flowers , and over 100 species . it can be used as a medicine or as tea . it has been shown to make rodents feel calmer , and helps make people less stressful . it is the national flower of russia .
