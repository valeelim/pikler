Liberty University     liberty university is a private , christian university in lynchburg , virginia . the university has about 69,214 students . it was founded in 1971 by jerry falwell . the current chancellor of the university is jerry falwell , jr. . the athletics teams of the university are called the flames . they have 18 varsity teams and they compete in the big south conference . other websites . - liberty university 's official website - liberty flames ' official athletics website
