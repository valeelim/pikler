Royal Oak, Michigan    royal oak , michigan royal oak is a city in the state of michigan . it has a population of 60,062. it is in oakland county . the mayor of royal oak is jim ellison . the racial makeup is 94.80 % white , 1.54 % african american , 0.26 % native american , 1.56 % asian , 0.05 % pacific islander , and 0.38 % from other races .
