Wanda Sykes     wanda sykes ( born march 7 , 1964 ) is an american writer , comedienne and actress . she was born in portsmouth , virginia and raised in washington , d.c. she starred in the sitcom `` curb your enthusiasm '' on the hbo network . in november 2009 her late night talk show `` the wanda sykes show '' premiered . sykes is lesbian . she married her wife , alex , in 2006 .
