Affix     an affix is a piece added to a word , or root , which signals or changes its role in a sentence . it is part of grammar . affixes may occur in various positions in a word : - prefix : before the word/root , for instance : anti-climax ; re-write . - suffix : after the word/root , for instance : climactic ; walked . most plurals . - infix : inside the word/root . rare in english , example : the m in recumbent . - interfix : between words , to connect them : the reduced `` and '' in sun 'n sand ; editor-in-chief .
