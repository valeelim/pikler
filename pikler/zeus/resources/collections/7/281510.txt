Molineux Stadium     molineux stadium is a football stadium in wolverhampton , england . it is the current home stadium of the wolverhampton wanderers f.c.. the stadium was built in 1889 and was opened that same year . the total seating capacity of the stadium is approximately 28,525. the stadium has also hosted the england internationals and , more recently , the england under-21 internationals , and the uefa cup final in 1972. other websites . - molineux stadium 's official website
