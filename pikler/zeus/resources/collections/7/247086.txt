Waiting for Guffman     waiting for guffman is a musical mockumentary movie made in 1997. it was directed by christopher guest , who also stars in the movie . the movie is set in the small town of blaine , missouri , which is not a real town . the town decides to perform a musical called `` red , white and blaine '' to celebrate the 150th anniversary of the town , and invites a famous theater critic named guffman to see it , hoping that he will make the play famous . the name of the movie is a reference to the play `` waiting for godot '' by samuel beckett . in that play , the two main characters are waiting for a man named godot , who never shows up . in `` waiting for guffman '' , guffman never shows up , either .
