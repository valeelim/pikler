Juno (movie)    juno ( movie ) juno is a 2007 american teen comedy-drama movie set in minnesota . it was written by diablo cody and directed by jason reitman . it starred ellen page as juno macguff , a teenage girl who becomes pregnant by her best friend ( who is played by michael cera ) . the movie made $ 231 million around the world . in the movie , juno macguff thinks about getting an abortion and goes to an abortion clinic , but chooses not to . both people who think abortion should be legal and people who think it should not be legal thought that the movie supported their views . reitman said that he did not try to put a political message in the movie . `` juno '' got good reviews from most movie critics who saw it . 93 % of the critics listed on rottentomatoes.com gave the movie a good review . `` juno '' won an academy award for best original screenplay .
