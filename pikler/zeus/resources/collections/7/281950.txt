Linas Kleiza     linas kleiza ( born january 3 , 1985 in kaunas , lithuania ) is a lithuanian basketball player who currently plays for the toronto raptors . kleiza played college basketball with the university of missouri tigers . he also played with the denver nuggets from 2005 to 2009. he plays for the lithuanian national basketball team . he signed a four-year , $ 20 million dollar gross income offer sheet with the toronto rapors on july 7 , 2010. other websites . - linas kleiza 's nba profile
