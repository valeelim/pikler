Her Majesty (song)    her majesty ( song ) `` her majesty '' is a song written by paul mccartney ( although credited to lennon/mccartney ) that appears on the beatles ' album `` abbey road '' . `` her majesty '' is the final track of the album and appears fourteen seconds after the song `` the end '' , but was not listed on the original sleeve . as such , it is considered one of the first examples of a hidden track in rock music . the song was recorded in three takes on july 2 , 1969 , before the beatles started work on `` golden slumbers '' / '' carry that weight '' . mccartney sang and played an acoustic guitar . the decision take it from the abbey road medley was made on july 30 .
