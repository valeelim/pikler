Evan Bourne     matthew joseph korklan ( born march 19 , 1983 in st. louis , missouri ) , is an american professional wrestler . he is best known for his time working with the wwe where he competed under the ring name evan bourne . he also wrestled for tna wrestling from 2004 to 2005. he won a slammy award in 2008 for best finishing maneuver ( air borne ( shooting star press ) ) . on june 12 , 2014 , bourne was released from his wwe contract . other websites . - wwe profile
