Lead glass     lead glass , also known as lead crystal , is a type of glass that has lead in it . normal glass has calcium oxide in it , while lead glass changes the calcium oxide with lead ( ii ) oxide . it looks nice because the lead is very large and heavy and changes how the glass looks . lead glass rings when tapped while ordinary glass does not . this glass can absorb some x-rays . it is very insulating . when food is in lead glass , lead might come out of the glass and into the food . acidic foods such as lemons leach lead much more easily .
