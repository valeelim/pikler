Xcel Energy Center     xcel energy center ( the x ) is a multi-purpose arena in saint paul , minnesota , sponsored by xcel energy . it is home of the minnesota wild of the national hockey league and the minnesota swarm of the national lacrosse league . it is owned by the city of saint paul and operated by minnesota sports & entertainment . it is on the same block as the rivercentre convention facility , roy wilkins auditorium , and ordway center for the performing arts . other websites . - xcel energy center official website - arena map and directions
