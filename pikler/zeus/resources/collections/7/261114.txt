Rubidium oxide     rubidium oxide is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is rbo . it is a yellow solid . it reacts vigorously with water to produce rubidium hydroxide , a very strong base . it is made by reduction of rubidium nitrate using rubidium metal . it can also be made by reduction of rubidium hydroxide with rubidium metal . it reacts with hydrogen to disproportionate it into rubidium hydroxide and rubidium hydride . other pages . - lithium oxide - calcium oxide - chemistry
