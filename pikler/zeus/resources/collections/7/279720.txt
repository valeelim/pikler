Loris     the lorises are solitary mammals that live in tropical rainforests in southeast asia . the loris is arboreal , living in trees . it is nocturnal , most active at night . in captivity , the loris lives for up to 13 years . the loris is a group of primates . they are closely related to galagos and slightly less closely related to lemurs . the loris has enormous eyes , grasping fingers , and toes with opposable digits . the loris ranges from 7 to 15 inches ( 18 to 38 cm ) long . it has a stubby tail , only about 1 inch ( 3 cm ) long . the loris is an omnivore ( eating plants and animals ) ; it eats mostly insects , lizards , fruits , and some plant shoots .
