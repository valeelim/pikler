Charles, Duke of Berry    charles , duke of berry charles of france , duke of berry , ( 31 july 1686 – 5 may 1714 ) was a grandson of louis xiv of france . the duke of berry was for seven years heir presumptive to the throne of spain ( 1700–1707 ) when his brother philip v of spain was childless . born at the palace of versailles , charles was the youngest son of louis , dauphin of france , `` le grand dauphin '' , and of maria anna victoria of bavaria . the duchess of orléans proposed he marry her daughter marie louise élisabeth d'orléans . all agreed , the marriage with marie louise élisabeth took place on 6 july 1710. it was an unhappy marriage , and after a miscarriage which ended her first pregnancy , marie louise élisabeth never gave birth to a child that lived for more than a few days . once a widow , madame de berry became ill-famous for her debauched lifeways . she hid several pregnancies and died at 23 a few weeks after suffering a harrowing clandestine childbirth . elisabeth charlotte , duchess dowager of orléans and the grandmother of berry 's wife , nicknamed him `` berry-bon cœur '' ( `` berry good heart '' ) .
