Fluoride     fluoride is the reduced form of fluorine . when fluorine gains an electron by taking it from another substance ( oxidizing it ) , it converts into fluoride . fluoride is found in toothpaste and helps prevent tooth decay . fluoride is added artificially to drinking water in some places and has caused a controversy over the harmful effects versus the benefits of fluoridation . related pages . - fluorine - fluorite , a natural fluoride - fluoridation
