Rock of Ages Corporation     rock of ages corporation is a company in vermont . it mines granite , which it sells largely for use in memorials , though it also makes smaller products . it was started in 1885 , and has its headquarters in graniteville , vermont , which is close to barre . there are around 230 people who work for the company . rock of ages had a profit of about $ 800,000 in 2008. in october 2010 , another mining company , swenson granite company , said it would buy rock of ages for $ 39 million . rock of ages runs the largest granite quarry in the world . it is about deep , and covers about 50 acres .
