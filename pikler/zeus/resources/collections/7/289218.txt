Arma-Goddamn-Motherfuckin-Geddon     `` arma-goddamn-motherfuckin-geddon ' '' is a industrial rock song by the american rock band marilyn manson and is the first single from their seventh studio album `` the high end of low '' , which was released in 2009. there is a remix of the song by the swedish rock band , teddybears . the song reached number 37 on the u.s. billboard mainstream rock tracks .
