Austrobaileyales     austrobaileyales is the botanical name for an order of flowering plants . there are about 100 species of woody plants , perhaps the most famous of which is the spice star anise , from `` illicium verum '' . the apg system , of 1998 , did not recognize such an order . however , the apg ii system , of 2003 , did accept this order and placed it among the basal angiosperms .
