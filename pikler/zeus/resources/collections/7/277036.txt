Conservative Party of Canada     the conservative party of canada ( french : parti conservateur du canada ) is a political party in canada . the party is considered to be on the right of the political spectrum , unlike the liberal party of canada , bloc québécois or the new democratic party . the current party leader is stephen harper , who also has been prime minister of canada since 2006. the conservative party was elected to form a minority government in 2006 and in 2008. the party was elected to form a majority government in 2011. other websites . - official website
