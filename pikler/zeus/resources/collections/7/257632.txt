Twinkie     a twinkie is a golden sponge cake which has a creamy filling . the twinkie was created by james a. dewar in 1930 and is made by hostess . twinkies can also be deep-fried and this was inspired by the deep-fried mars bar . the twinkie was originally filled with banana cream inside but was forced to switch to the vanilla cream flavour during the second world war because the bananas were rationed . twinkies have around 150 calories in each bar . twinkies have a cookbook and it has over 50 recipes . twinkies also have a mascot called `` twinkie the kid '' which is an anthropomorphized twinkie . the twinkie is known to be very tasty .
