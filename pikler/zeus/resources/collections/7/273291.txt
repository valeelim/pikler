Sodium iodate     sodium iodate is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is naio . it has sodium and iodate ions in it . sodium iodate is a colorless solid . it is similar to sodium chlorate . it is a strong oxidizing agent . it reacts with sodium hypochlorite to make sodium periodate . it can catch things on fire . it is made by dissolving iodic acid or iodine in sodium hydroxide solution . iodine dissolving also makes sodium iodide . it is used to make sodium periodate . related pages . - potassium iodate - potassium periodate - potassium chlorate
