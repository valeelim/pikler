Khaba     khaba was a pharaoh of the 3rd dynasty of ancient egypt . his predecessor was sekhemkhet , and his successor was huni . khaba 's reign is dated from 2640 bc to 2637 bc . we do not know who khaba 's parents were , but it is most likely that he was a son of sekhemkhet and his wife djeseretnebti . it is possible that khaba was a father of huni and grandfather of sneferu . khaba 's name means `` the soul appears '' .
