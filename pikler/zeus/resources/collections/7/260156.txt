Bruno Senna     bruno senna lalli ( born 15 october 1983 in são paulo ) is a brazilian formula one auto racing driver . he is the nephew of the late three-time formula one world champion ayrton senna . his mother is ayrton 's sister , viviane . his father , flávio , died in a motorcycle crash in 1996. bruno senna wears a slightly modified version of his uncle 's helmet . on 25 august , 2011 , renault announced that senna would replace nick heidfeld as the race driver . season in progress . season in progress . other websites . - official site
