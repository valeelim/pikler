Ultratop 40     ultratop 40 singles , often called ultratop 40 , is the weekly chart of best-selling singles in wallonia , belgium . its equivalent covering the flanders region is ultratop 50. both charts are made and published by the ultratop organization . like the uk singles chart , ultratop creates charts based on retail record sales of around 500 retail outlets and legal digital downloads . currently gfk is the market observer of the charts . the chart broadcasts on radio contact on saturdays from 12:00 to 14:00. the combined number of ultratop chart listeners on radio or tv stations exceeds two million each week . to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the charts in 2005 a jubilee book was published . it covered all the 15,282 singles from 5,882 artists thus far . related pages . - ultratop 50
