Mercury(I) sulfate    mercury ( i ) sulfate mercury ( i ) sulfate , also known as mercurous sulfate , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is hgso . it has mercury and sulfate ions in it . the mercury is in its +1 oxidation state . mercury ( i ) sulfate is a whitish-yellow solid . it is not soluble in water . it does dissolve in dilute nitric acid . it is made by dissolving a large amount of mercury in concentrated sulfuric acid . it can also be made by reacting mercury ( i ) nitrate with any sulfate or sulfuric acid . related pages . - mercury ( ii ) sulfate - mercury ( i ) chloride
