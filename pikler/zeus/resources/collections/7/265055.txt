Polygonaceae     polygonaceae is a family of flowering plants are herbs , shrubs , or rarely trees comprising about 30 genera and 1,000 species . the leaves are simple , alternate or rarely opposite , and usually stipulate . some well known members include buckwheat ( `` fagopyrum '' ) , sorrel ( `` rumex '' ) , rhubarb ( `` rheum '' ) , and knotweed ( `` polygonum '' ) .
