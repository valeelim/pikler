Sinoceratops     sinoceratops , whose name means 'horned face from china ' , was a very large ceratopsian dinosaur . the dinosaur used to live in what is now china during the upper cretaceous . `` sinoceratops '' is an ancestor of `` styracosaurus '' and `` centrosaurus '' . the head of a full-grown `` sinoceratops '' was probably about long .
