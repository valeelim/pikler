Gregory Helms     gregory shane helms ( born july 12 , 1974 in smithfield , north carolina ) , is an american professional wrestler . helms is best known for working with world wrestling entertainment ( wwe ) where he worked under the ring names the hurricane and gregory helms . he was also known for working with world championship wrestling ( wcw ) where he wrestled under the ring name `` sugar '' shane helms . he is a one-time wwf european champion , two-time wwe cruiserweight champion , two-time wwe world tag team champion - with kane and with rosey and one-time wwf hardcore champion . other websites . - wwe profile
