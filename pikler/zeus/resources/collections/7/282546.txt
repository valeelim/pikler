Yip Man     yip man ( 18931972 ) , also known as ip man , was the first teacher to teach openly the martial art wing chun . he started teaching in hong kong . his students spread wing chun around the world . later , ip man was the teacher of bruce lee . after ip man died , the teacher duncan leung taught wing chun in hong kong from 1945 to 1969. nowadays , wing chun is taught around the world . many children do wing chun .
