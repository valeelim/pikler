University of Connecticut     the university of connecticut or sometimes referred to as uconn for short is a public university in storrs and mansfield , connecticut . the university has about 28,677 students . it was founded in 1881. the current president of the university is dr. philip e. austin . the athletics teams of the university are called the huskies . they have 20 varsity teams , most of which compete in the american athletic conference . the uconn ice hockey teams play in other conferences—men in atlantic hockey , and women in hockey east . the men 's hockey team will join the women in hockey east starting in 2014–15 . other websites . - university of connecticut 's official website - connecticut huskies ' official athletics website
