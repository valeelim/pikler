Magnus Johansson     magnus johansson ( born september 4 , 1973 in linköping , sweden ) is an swedish ice hockey defenceman for the linköpings hc of the shl where he is also the captain . he has also played in the nhl for the chicago blackhawks for 1 season and the florida panthers for 1 season before being signing a contract with the atlant moscow oblast of the khl and then retured back to linköpings hc where he was named the team 's captain . other websites . - magnus johansson 's nhl player page
