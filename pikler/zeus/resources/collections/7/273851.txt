SOS (song)    sos ( song ) `` sos '' is a song by barbadian singer rihanna . it is a dance-pop and r & b song that samples the 1981 song `` tainted love '' by soft cell . it was released on february 14 2006 off of the album `` a girl like me '' and went to number one on the `` billboard '' hot 100 , her first song to do so . the song was originally written for christina milian .
