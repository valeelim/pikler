Spyro: Year of the Dragon    spyro : year of the dragon spyro : year of the dragon ( also called spyro 3 : year of the dragon or spyro 3 ) is a platform video game that was published by sony computer entertainment . it was released for the playstation video game system on october 25 , 2000 in north america , on october 23 , 2000 in the united kingdom and on november 10 , 2000 in the pal region . the game got a 8.4 out of 10 from gamespot and a 9.1 out of 10 from ign . it is the third game in the `` spyro '' video game series and also the last `` spyro '' game that was made by insomniac games .
