Speed of Sound (roller coaster)    speed of sound ( roller coaster ) speed of sound is a boomerang coaster in walibi holland in the netherlands . it is manufactured by vekoma and opened in 2000 as `` la via volta '' . it operated until 2007 when the park announced it would be shut down . in the same year , the train was sold to pleasurewood hills to be operating on wipeout , also a boomerang coaster . on january 30 , 2011 , walibi world announced that the roller coaster will reopen in 2011 , with a new style train from vekoma . the coaster was renamed to speed of sound and the track was painted orange . the coaster also has a unique tunnel on the lift hill where the train go through before starting the ride . speed of sound on the walibi holland website
