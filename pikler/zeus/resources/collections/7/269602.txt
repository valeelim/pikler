Singapore National Pledge     the singapore 's national pledge was written by sinnathamby rajaratnam in 1966 , not long after singapore 's independence . a draft was submitted to the then prime minister lee kuan yew , before he made it better and passed it to the cabinet . these are the words of the pledge : these are the rules for using the pledge . they are set by the government . 1. we will recite the pledge in schools , on saf day and during the national day parade ( ndp ) . 1. everyone should put their right fist at their left part of the chest to be loyal to singapore . 1. we can not use the pledge for what it should not be used as .
