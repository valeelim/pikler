Minute Maid     minute maid is a drink company owned by the coca-cola company . minute maid makes juices , lemonade and other soft drinks . minute maid was the first company to make orange juice from concentrate . it makes an orange drink called 'minute maid pulpy ' . it contains juice with a lot of orange pulp . now 'minute maid pulpy ' came in four variants , orange , tropical , o'mango ( orange mango ) and lemon . now 'minute maid pulpy aloe vera ' contains juice with a lot of aloe vera pieces . it came in white grape .
