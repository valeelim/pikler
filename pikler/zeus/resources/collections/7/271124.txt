Gian Gastone de' Medici, Grand Duke of Tuscany    gian gastone de ' medici , grand duke of tuscany gian gastone de ' medici ( 24 may 1671 – 9 july 1737 ) was the last medici grand duke of tuscany . he was the second son of cosimo iii de ' medici , grand duke of tuscany , and marguerite louise d'orléans . his sister , anna maria luisa de ' medici , the electress palatine , married him to anna maria franziska of saxe-lauenburg in 1697. the two remained childless and it was common knowledge that gian gastone was homosexual . gian gastone succeeded his father as grand duke in 1723. in his reign he reversed all his father 's laws and stopped taxes for poorer people , repealed ( got rid of ) the anti-jewish laws and stopped public executions . his father , cosimo iii , wanted the his sister to succeed him but spain , great britain , austria and the dutch republic disregarded and made charles iii of spain his heir . charles changed his claim to francis iii of lorraine . francis then became the grand duke on 9 july 1737 ending almost 300 years of medici rule over florence .
