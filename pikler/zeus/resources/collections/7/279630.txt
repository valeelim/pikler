Baird's tapir    baird 's tapir baird ’ s tapir ( tapirus bairdii ) is a species of tapir that is native to central america and northern south america . it is one of four latin american species of tapir . baird ’ s tapir is found in the dense jungles of central america , including southeastern mexico , belize , guatemala , honduras , costa rica , nicaragua , panama , colombia , and ecuador . the animal can be found at altitudes as high as 3,350 metres ( 10,990 ft ) .
