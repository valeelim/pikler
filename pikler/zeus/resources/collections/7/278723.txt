Candida albicans     candida albicans is a yeast which causes oral and genital infections in humans . the infection is candidiasis , better known as `` thrush '' . it is treated with antimycotics ( anti-fungal drugs ) . `` c. albicans '' biofilms readily form on the surface of medical devices . hospital-related infections in patients are a major health concern . `` c. albicans '' is among the gut flora , the many organisms that live in the human mouth and gastrointestinal tract . under normal circumstances , it lives in 80 % of the human population with no harmful effects , although overgrowth results in candidiasis .
