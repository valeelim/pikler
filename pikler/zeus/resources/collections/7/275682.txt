Avialae     avialae is a term for birds and their immediate extinct ancestors . it is a clade of dinosaurs containing their only living representatives , birds , and whatever coelurosaurs are regarded as their ancestors . avialae is defined as clade based on physical characteristics . jacques gauthier named avialae in 1986 , and first defined it in 2001 as all dinosaurs that possessed feathered wings used in flapping flight , and the birds that descended from them . several other authors use a similar , but branch-based , definition : all theropods closer to birds than to `` deinonychus '' .
