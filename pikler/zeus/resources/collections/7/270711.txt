Æsir–Vanir War    æsir–vanir war in norse mythology , the æsir–vanir war was a war that occurred between the æsir and the vanir , two groups of gods . the war ultimately resulted in the unification of the two tribes into a single tribe of gods . the war is an important event in norse mythology . the two side eventually came to a truce by spitting into a bowl ; the spit was mixed , and the god kvasir was born . kvasir was killed by dwarves for his blood .
