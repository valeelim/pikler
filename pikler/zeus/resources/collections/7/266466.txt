Aluminium selenide     aluminium selenide , also known as aluminum selenide or aluminium ( iii ) selenide , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is alse . it contains aluminium in its +3 oxidation state . it contains aluminium and selenide ions . it reacts with water to make hydrogen selenide . it is a reducing agent and can burn to make aluminium oxide and selenium dioxide . it is made by reacting selenium with aluminium powder at a high temperature . it is used to make hydrogen selenide . related pages . - sodium selenide
