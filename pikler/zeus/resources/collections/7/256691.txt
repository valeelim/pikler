Imagine (album)    imagine ( album ) imagine is john lennon 's second solo album . it was recorded and released in 1971 and has remained popular . in 2012 , `` imagine '' was voted 80th on `` rolling stone '' magazine 's list of the `` 500 greatest albums of all time '' . this album had songs that were gentler , more commercial and less avant-garde than the ones he released on his previous album , `` john lennon/plastic ono band '' . in 2003 `` rolling stone '' magazine named `` imagine '' # 76 on its list of the 500 greatest albums of all time .
