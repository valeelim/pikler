Perry Saturn     perry arthur satullo ( born october 25 , 1966 ) is a american professional wrestler . he is best known for wrestling with world wrestling federation ( wwf ) and extreme championship wrestling ( ecw ) under the ring name , perry saturn . in an interview , saturn revealed that he had been shot with a .25 caliber handgun in the back of the neck while he was trying to save a woman who was being raped by two men . after he was shot , satullo became addicted to crystal meth and was homeless for two and a half years . many friends and fans believed that he was dead until he made a surprise appearance at a wwe event . championships . - extreme championship wrestling - ecw world tag team championship ( three times ) with john kronus - independent wrestling federation - iwf light heavyweight championship ( two times ) - pro wrestling illustrated - pwi ranked him # 47 of the 500 best singles wrestlers of the `` pwi 500 '' in 1999 . - pwi ranked him # 227 of the 500 best singles wrestlers of the `` pwi years '' in 2003 .
