Curt Hawkins     brian joseph myers ( born april 20 , 1985 in glen cove , new york ) is an american professional wrestler . he is best known for his time working with the wwe . he made his debut in 2004 and is now better known by his ring name curt hawkins . hawkins was also known for being part of many tag teams with zack ryder ( the major brothers , curt hawkins and zack ryder and the edgeheads along with edge ) . hawkins is a one-time wwe tag team champion with zack ryder . on june 12 , 2014 , hawkins was released from his wwe contract . other websites . - wwe profile
