Northern green frog     the northern green frog is a subspecies of the north american green frog . its name is either `` rana clamitans melanota '' , or `` lithobates clamitans melanota '' . it is moderately large . it is found in the northeast united states and some of eastern canada . its call sounds like a plucked banjo . it has a fold running from the top of its eyes down the side of its back . the bullfrog does not have these folds . it is normally found in swamps , marshes , and muddy streams . related websites . - pickerel frog
