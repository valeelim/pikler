Who's Next    who 's next who 's next is the fifth album by the english rock band the who . it was released on july 31 , 1971 by decca and mca in the united states and august 25 , 1971 in the united kingdom through track and polydor . `` who 's next '' was a huge success when it was released , and has been certified 3x platinum by the riaa . all songs written by pete townshend except where noted .
