Georg Philipp Schmidt von Lübeck     georg philipp schmidt von lübeck ( january 1 , 1766 – october 28 , 1849 ) was a german poet . he was born in lübeck as member of a merchant family with long tradition . he studied law in jena and göttingen 1786 bis 1790 , then he changed to theology and in the end to medicine . in jena he made friends with the writers sophie mereau and johann gottfried herder . after some journeys through germany he worked as civil servant for the danish government till 1829. his most famous poem is „der wanderer “ . it was composed by franz schubert .
