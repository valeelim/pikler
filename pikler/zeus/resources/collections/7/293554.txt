Hartlepool     hartlepool is a town near the a689 road in county durham , england . there is a legend that during the napoleonic wars , a french ship was wrecked off the coast near hartlepool . the captain 's pet , a monkey , was washed ashore . the people from hartlepool thought it was a person and tried to ask it questions . when they were unable to understand the monkey they assumed it was a french spy . a trial was held on the beach and the monkey was sentenced to death and hanged . this event is remembered in several songs . the local football team , hartlepool united f.c. , used to greeted with cries of `` who hung the monkey ? '' . their mascot is a monkey called `` h'angus the monkey '' . the rugby football team , the hartlepool rovers , are known as the monkeyhangers .
