Monster Energy     monster energy is the name of a brand of energy drinks manufactured by hansen natural and distributed by anheuser-busch ( united states ) , pepsico canada ( canada ) , pepsi ( germany ) , grupo jumex ( mexico ) and schweppes ( australia ) . monster energy has sponsored many sporting events like the x games and pays many sportpeople to wear their logo . monster energy has 100 calories in a can . websites . - monster energy 's official website - monster energy 's official canadian website
