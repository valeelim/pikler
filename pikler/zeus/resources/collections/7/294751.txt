Merlin     merlin is a legendary character . he is best known as the wizard in the arthurian legends . he was king arthur 's guide . merlin is always a mysterious figure . he rarely gives his reasons for the things he does . merlin was probably first written about in welsh legend . his story is based on parts of several different real and fictional characters.merlin is usually said to be one with nature , being the source of his enormous power.the lady of the lake enchanted him .
