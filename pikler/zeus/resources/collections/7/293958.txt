Ralph Darling     general sir ralph darling ( 1772—1858 ) was an officer in the british army and the governor of new south wales from 1825—1831 . darling was the first governor to work with an executive council , made up of the lieutenant-governor , chief justice , colonial secretary and the archdeacon . these four people and another three people that darling could select would make up the legislative council . darling was expected to follow their advice in governing new south wales .
