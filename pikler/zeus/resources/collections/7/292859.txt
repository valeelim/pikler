Stamford, Lincolnshire    stamford , lincolnshire stamford is a town in the county of lincolnshire , england . it is about to the north of london . it has a long history , and has many interesting old buildings . the old town has been classed as an outstanding area of architectural or historic interest of national importance . there are two private boarding schools , stamford school and stamford high school . in 2013 a group of parents worried about education in stamford started a campaign to make a free school there .
