United States Tennis Association     `` united states tennis association '' ' ( usta ) is the organization that controls tennis at the national level in the united states . it is a non-profit private organization formed in 1881 to standardize the rules of tennis . it is affiliated with the international tennis federation , which is the international governing body of tennis . at present usta has more than 700,000 members . its aim is to promote and develop the game of tennis and to develop better infrastructure for tennis . the usta runs the billie jean king national tennis center which hosts the u.s. open , the last grand slam of the year .
