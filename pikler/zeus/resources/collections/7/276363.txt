Cadmium iodide     cadmium iodide is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is cdi . it has cadmium and iodide ions in it . cadmium iodide is a white or pale yellow solid . it is known for its crystal structure . many other chemical compounds have a cadmium iodide crystal structure . it easily dissolves in water . it is toxic . it is made by reacting cadmium with iodine . it can also be made by reacting cadmium , or cadmium oxide with hydriodic acid . cadmium iodide is used to make phosphors ( things that light when struck by uv light ) , photography , and electroplating . related pages . - cadmium chloride - cadmium fluoride - cadmium bromide - mercury iodide
