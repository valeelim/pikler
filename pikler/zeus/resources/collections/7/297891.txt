Hot in Herre     `` hot in herre ' '' is a 2002 pop/hip hop song by american rapper nelly . the song was the second single from his second album nellyville . the song was released on april 16 , 2002. the song is very popular and reached # 1 on the canadian singles chart , # 1 on the u.s. billboard hot 100 and # 1 on the u.s. billboard hot r & b/hip hop songs .
