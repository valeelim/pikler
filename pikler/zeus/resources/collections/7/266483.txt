Sodium selenate     sodium selenate is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is naseo . it contains sodium and selenate ions . sodium selenate is a white solid . it is a strong oxidizing agent . it dissolves in water easily . sodium selenate is made by reacting sodium hydroxide with selenic acid . sodium selenate is used as a mineral supplement . sodium selenate is very toxic in amounts any more than a very tiny amount . related pages . - sodium selenite
