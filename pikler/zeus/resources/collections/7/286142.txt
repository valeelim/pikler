Kangaroo Jack     kangaroo jack is an 2003 american-australian adventure comedy movie set in new york city and australia . it was produced by jerry bruckheimer and was directed by david mcnally . `` kangaroo jack '' was released on january 17 , 2003 in north america . the movie received negative reviews with a 8 % rating on rotten tomatoes and a 16 out of 100 from metacritic . cast . - jerry o'connell as charlie carbone - anthony anderson as louis booker - estella warren as jessie - michael shannon as frankie lombardo - christopher walken as salvatore `` sal '' maggio - dyan cannon as anna carbone - marton csokas as mr. smith - bill hunter as blue - tony nikolakopoulos as sal 's capo - david ngoombujarra as james `` mr. jimmy '' inkamala - adam garcia as kangaroo `` jackie legs '' jack ( voice ) ( he was uncredited )
