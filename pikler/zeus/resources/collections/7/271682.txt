Tinie Tempah     patrick chukwuemeka okogwu ( born 7 november 1988 ) is a british grime mc and rapper . he was born in plumstead , london and is of nigerian origin . he is better known by his stage name tinie tempah . he also founded the record label disturbing london in 2006. some of his more famous songs are `` pass out '' , `` written in the stars '' and `` earthquake '' . tempah released his first album `` disc-overy '' in 2010. it went straight to number one in the uk albums chart . seven singles were released from the album . on 4 august 2013 , the first single from `` demonstration '' , `` trampoline '' , was released . 2 chainz rapped in it . it reached number three in the uk singles chart . his second album `` demonstration '' was released 4 november 2013. twelve of the album 's fifteen songs feature other musicians . tempah is doing a uk tour for the album in 2014. tempah won best uk hip hop/grime act at the 2013 music of black origin awards . other websites . - official website
