Clastic rock     clastic rocks are composed of fragments , or clasts , of pre-existing rock . although , in principle , almost any rock made of eroded former rock is clastic ( such as sandstone or mudstone ) , in practice the term is used for clasts of at least gravel size upwards . that means clasts are at least visible to the naked eye , and may be huge . there are two types of clastic rock , by shape . conglomerates have rounded clasts , and breccias have jagged clasts . in a clastic rock , the clasts are often of a different make-up from the ground rock or matrix
