Brecqhou     brecqhou is one of the channel islands . it is part of the bailiwick of guernsey . it is just west of sark and has a surface area of about 200 acres ( 0.81 km ; 0.31 sq mi ) . it is outside of the united kingdom and not part of the european union . brecqhou has a castle that belongs to the barclay brothers , owners of london 's `` the daily telegraph '' . the castle is sometimes used for meetings and filing of papers outside of the united kingdom .
