Abyssinia     abyssinia , also known as the empire of ethiopia , was an old empire . it included ethiopia and eritrea . when it was biggest , it had some of egypt , sudan , yemen , and saudi arabia . it ruled from 980 b.c . to 1974 a.d. , where it was overthrown by communists . at the time of its overthrow , it was the longest-lasting government in the world and one of the only two nations that did not get colonized in africa .
