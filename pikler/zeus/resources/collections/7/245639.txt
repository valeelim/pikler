Rik van den Boog     rik van den boog ( born 1 september 1959 in amsterdam ) is the managing director of dutch football club afc ajax since october 2008. on 22 may 2010 several media reported that stock holder of the entertainment group , unico glorie , has reported rik van den boog to police for fraud and theft . unico glorie says that van den boog made a payment of euro 2.000.000 without permission .
