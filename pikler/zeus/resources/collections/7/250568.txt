The Dirty Energy Dilemma     the dirty energy dilemma is a 2008 book by academic benjamin k. sovacool . in the book sovacool discusses some of the `` big energy challenges '' facing the united states , and suggests that the fix for these problems is 'clean energy ' in the form of energy efficiency and renewable energy . sovacool sees no role for nuclear power in a clean energy transition . `` the dirty energy dilemma '' won a 2009 nautilus award . related pages . - an inconvenient truth - winning the oil endgame
