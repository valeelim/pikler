Mercury(I) iodide    mercury ( i ) iodide mercury ( i ) iodide , also known as mercurous iodide , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is hgi . it has mercury and iodide ions in it . the mercury is in its +1 oxidation state . it is a yellow solid . when light is shined on it , it breaks down ( by disproportionation ) to mercury ( ii ) iodide and iodine . it is toxic , as are all mercury compounds . it is made by reacting mercury and iodine . it was once used as a medicine . related pages . - mercury ( i ) chloride - mercury ( ii ) oxide - mercury ( ii ) chloride
