Swallow     the swallows and martins are a group of passerine birds in the family hirundinidae which are characterised by their adaptation to aerial feeding . swallow is used colloquially in europe as a synonym for the barn swallow . within the swallow , the name `` martin '' tends to be used for the squarer-tailed species , and the name `` swallow '' for the more fork-tailed species ; however , there is no scientific distinction between these two groups . the family contains around 83 species in 19 genera .
