Jacob Riis     jacob august riis ( may 3 , 1849 - may 26 , 1914 ) was a danish-american photographer and journalist . riis came to the united states in 1861 and worked in various odd jobs before going into journalism . riis is most known for his photographs and writings on slums in new york city . riis is usually thought of as one of the muckrakers , a group of people who wanted to improve conditions in cities .
