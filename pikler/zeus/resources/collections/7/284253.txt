José Bautista     josé antonio bautista ( born october 19 , 1980 in santo domingo , dominican republic ) is a dominican major league baseball player . he has played for the baltimore orioles in 2004 , the tampa bay devil rays in 2004 , kansas city royals in 2004 , the pittsburgh pirates from 2004 to 2008 and the toronto blue jays since 2008. bautista plays as both an outfielder and third baseman . most often , he plays right field . he is also one of 26 players in the 50 home run club . in both the 2010 and 2011 seasons , he hit more home runs than any other player . he hit 54 home runs in 2010 and 43 home runs in 2011. other websites . - jose bautista 's mlb player profile
