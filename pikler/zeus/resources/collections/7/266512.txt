Eydie Gormé     eydie gormé , ( edith gormezano , august 16 , 1928 – august 10 , 2013 ) was an american singer . her husband , steve lawrence , sang with her . they were known as the duo `` steve and eydie '' . gormé herself has had some hit songs . one was `` blame it on the bossa nova '' . gormé was born on august 16 , 1928 in the bronx , new york city to sephardi jewish parents . her father was from sicily ; her mother was from turkey . she studied at william howard taft high school in new york . gormé was married to steve lawrence from 1957 until her death in 2013. they had two sons . gormé died on august 10 , 2013 , six days before her 85th birthday , following a brief illness , in las vegas , nevada .
