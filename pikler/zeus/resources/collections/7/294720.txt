Flinders Ranges     the flinders ranges are a group of mountains in south australia . they have been listed as one of australia 's outstanding national landscapes . the were named `` flinders range '' in 1839 by explorer edward john eyre who was searching for an overland route from adelaide to port lincoln . the ranges were home to the adnyamathanha , an indigenous australian people , for thousands of years . their paintings and rock carvings can be found throughout the area . a large area is protected by the flinders ranges national park . important towns in the flinders ranges include hawker , quorn , wilpena pound and leigh creek .
