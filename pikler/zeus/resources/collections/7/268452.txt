Periodic table block     a block in the periodic table is an area of chemical elements that has the same electron arrangement . there are four blocks ; the s-block , the p-block , the d-block , and the f-block . the alkali metals and the alkaline earth metals are in the s-block . the group of elements after the transition metals are the p-block . the transition metals are the d-block , as well as the last lanthanide and actinide . the rest of the inner transition metals are in the f-block .
