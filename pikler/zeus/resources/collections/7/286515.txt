Mark Appleyard     mark appleyard ( born november 11 , 1982 in oakville , ontario ) is a canadian professional skateboarder . he is currently sponsored by element skateboards , thunder , autobahn , globe shoes , volcom , ccs , arnette , and vivo . he was the thrasher skater of the year in 2003. other websites . - mark appleyard 's profile at grindtv.com
