Conan (TV series)    conan ( tv series ) conan is a late-night talk show on tbs . it is hosted by conan o'brien , who used to host `` the tonight show '' and `` late night with conan o'brien '' . his sidekick and announcer is andy richter , and his band is jimmy vivino and the basic cable band . the show started in november of 2010. a show has two celebrity guests and a monologue . in addition , there is either a musical guest or a stand-up comedian . rounding out the show are comedy bits , which include spoofs of local newscasts , recurring characters , and ridiculous things happening to trombone player labamba rosenberg .
