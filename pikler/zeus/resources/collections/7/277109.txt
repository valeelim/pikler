Luke Ridnour     lucas robin `` luke '' ridnour ( born february 13 , 1981 in coeur d'alene , idaho ) is an american basketball player that currently plays for the minnesota timberwolves of the nba . he went to university of oregon for college where he played for the oregon ducks basketball team . ridnour has also played with the seattle supersonics from 2003-2007 and the milwaukee bucks from 2008-2010. other websites . - luke ridnour at nba.com
