Barium oxide     barium oxide is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is bao . it contains barium and oxide ions . barium oxide is a white solid . it dissolves in water to make barium hydroxide . it reacts with acids to make barium salts . barium oxide is made by heating barium carbonate with carbon . this prevents barium peroxide from forming when it is heated . it can also be made by heating barium nitrate . barium oxide can be added to glass instead of lead ( ii ) oxide . it is also used in cathode ray tubes . it can be used as a catalyst . barium oxide is very toxic when eaten . related pages . - barium chloride - barium sulfate
