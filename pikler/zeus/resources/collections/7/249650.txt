Life Before Life     life before life is a book written by psychiatrist jim b. tucker . it presents an overview of more than 40 years of reincarnation research at the university of virginia into children 's reports of past life memories . the book suggests that consciousness can be considered separately from the brain , which provides a basis for claims of reincarnation . `` life before life '' has been translated into ten languages and the foreword to the book is written by ian stevenson .
