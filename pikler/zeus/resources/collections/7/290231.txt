Lyoto Machida     lyoto carvalho machida ( born may 30 , 1978 ) , nicknamed `` the dragon '' , is a mixed martial arts fighter from salvador , brazil . machida fights in the light heavyweight division . he is a former ultimate fighting championship light heavyweight champion . he is a third degree black belt in machida karate . he is a first degree black belt in brazilian jiu-jitsu . he is known to have great counter-fighting ability . other websites . - official website - ufc profile
