University of North Carolina Wilmington     the university of north carolina wilmington is a public , coeducational university in wilmington , north carolina . the university has about 13,401 students . it was founded in 1947. the current chancellor of the university is rosemary depaolo . the athletics teams of the university are called the seahawks . they have 19 varsity teams and they compete in the colonial athletic association . other websites . - university of north carolina wilmington 's official website - uncw seahawks ' official athletics website
