Provincial Assembly of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa     the provincial assembly of khyber pakhtunkhwa is the unicameral legislative body of the khyber pakhtunkhwa province in pakistan . it was established under article 106 of the constitution of the pakistan . the assembly has 124 elected members . there are 99 regular seats , with 22 seats reserved for women and 3 seats for non-muslims . the assembly elects a chief minister of the province . he selects a cabinet of ministers to run the various government departments .
