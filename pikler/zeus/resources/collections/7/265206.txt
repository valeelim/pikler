Nickel(II) carbonate    nickel ( ii ) carbonate nickel ( ii ) carbonate , also known as nickelous carbonate , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is nico . it contains nickel in its +2 oxidation state . it also contains carbonate ions . nickel ( ii ) carbonate is a pale green solid . it does not dissolve in water . it reacts with acids to make carbon dioxide . it turns into carbon dioxide and nickel ( ii ) oxide when heated . it is made by reacting sodium carbonate with nickel ( ii ) chloride or nickel ( ii ) sulfate . it is used in pigments and catalysts that have nickel in them . related pages . - nickel ( ii ) hydroxide - nickel ( ii ) nitrate
