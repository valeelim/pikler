John Riggins     robert john riggins ( born august 4 , 1949 in seneca , kansas ) is a retired american football running back who used to play for the new york jets and washington redskins of the nfl . he was nicknamed `` the diesel '' . he won super bowl xvii with the washington redskins where he was also the mvp and is a member of the redskins ring of fame . he was inducted into the pro football hall of fame in 1992 .
