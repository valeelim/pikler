Mattress     a mattress is a mat or pad , usually placed on top of a bed , where somebody sleeps or lies on . the word `` mattress '' came from arabic words meaning `` to throw '' and `` place where something is thrown '' or `` mat , cushion . '' during the crusades , europeans slept on cushions thrown on the floor , and the word `` materas '' eventually became middle english through the romance languages . commonly used by militaries to build forts .
