Scotty 2 Hotty     scott ronald garland ( born july 2 , 1970 in westbrook , maine ) is an american firefighter and professional wrestler who is best known for working with wwf/e and extreme championship wrestling ( ecw ) under the ring name , scotty 2 hotty . he was one-half of the tag-team too cool with grandmaster sexay and sometimes rikishi . during his time in too cool , garland won the wwe tag team championship 1 time with rikishi and won the wwf world tag team championship with grandmaster sexay . he also defeated dean malenko to win the wwf light heavyweight championship on april 17 , 2000 but he held the title for only 10 days before he dropped it back to malenko on april 27 , 2000. in february 2013 , he began training as a firefighter and graduated from the lake tech fire academy in tavares , florida in june 2013. garland has won many belts with different wrestling promotions : - coastal championship wrestling - ccw heavyweight championship ( one time ) - eastern pro wrestling - epw heavyweight championship ( one time )
