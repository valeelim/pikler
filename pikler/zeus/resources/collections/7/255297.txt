Perez Hilton     mario armando lavandeira , jr. ( born march 23 , 1978 ) , known as perez hilton , is an american blogger and television presenter . he writes and gossips about musicians , actors and celebrities . his name is a play on the name `` paris hilton '' . hilton was born in miami to cuban parents . he was a housemate in the january 2015 series of celebrity big brother ( uk ) . hilton is openly gay . hilton is a democrat and attended the 2012 democratic national convention in support of president obama 's re-election . other websites . - perez hilton 's official website
