Iodine pentoxide     iodine pentoxide , also known as iodine ( v ) oxide and diiodine pentoxide is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is io . it has iodine and oxide ions . the iodine is in its +5 oxidation state . iodine pentoxide is a white solid . it breaks down to iodine and oxygen when heated . it reacts with carbon monoxide to make carbon dioxide and iodine . it is a strong oxidizing agent . it reacts with water to make iodic acid . iodine pentoxide is made by heating iodic acid to in dry air . it is used to see how much carbon monoxide is in a sample of gas . related pages . - iodine pentafluoride - iodine trifluoride - iodine monochloride
