Phosphorus(V) oxide    phosphorus ( v ) oxide phosphorus ( v ) oxide , also known as phosphorus pentoxide , is a chemical compound . its chemical formula is po . it contains phosphorus and oxide ions . it contains phosphorus in its +5 oxidation state . phosphorus ( v ) oxide is a colorless solid . it has a strong odor . it dissolves in water to produce phosphoric acid . it can corrode metals . it is irritating to the skin . it can make bad burns . it is made by burning phosphorus into high amounts of air . it is used to dry things out because it absorbs water . it dries things out from acids like nitric acid to organic compounds like carboxylic acids . phosphorus ( v ) oxide is corrosive . its vapors are toxic and burn the eyes and skin . it also irritates skin and eyes . related pages . - phosphorus ( iii ) oxide - hypophosphite - phosphite
