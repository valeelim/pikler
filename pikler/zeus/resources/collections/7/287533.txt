Tsunami warning system     tsunami warning system or tws detects tsunamis and issues warnings to prevent loss of life and property . it consists of two equally important components : a `` network of sensors '' to detect tsunamis and a `` communications infrastructure '' to issue timely alarms to start evacuation before the big waves reach the land to let the people to rush to safety . there are two distinct types of tsunami warning systems : `` international '' and `` regional '' . both of those systems depend on the fact that `` tsunami waves '' travel , approximately , 15-30 times slower than `` seismic waves '' .
