Larry Cahan     lawrence louis henry `` hank '' cahan ( december 25 , 1933 - june 25 , 1992 ) , nicknamed hank , was a canadian professional ice hockey player who played in several canadian and u.s. teams , including fort william hurricanes , toronto maple leafs , pittsburgh hornets , new york rangers , vancouver canucks , baltimore clippers , los angeles kings and chicago cougars . cahan was born in fort william , ontario . his career as a player began in 1949 and ended in 1974. on january 13 , 1968 , cahan , playing with the oakland seals against the minnesota north stars , was involved in the accident that caused the death of bill masterton .
