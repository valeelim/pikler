Union Kleinmünchen Linz     union kleinmünchen linz is a women soccer club from linz , upper austria . it was founded in 1980 and was the first club , which is a pure women soccer club . the coulors of the team are red and white . the club was founded in 1980 and started playing in the highest league . from 1980 till 2008 they played each season in the highest class . the season 2008/09 they played for the first time in the 2. league but next season ( 2009/10 ) they could reach the championship and were promoted to the öfb-frauenliga . honours . - austrian champion : 1990 , 1991 , 1992 , 1993 , 1994 , 1996 , 1998 , 1999 - austrian cup : 1991 , 1993 , 1995 , 1996 , 1998 , 1999 - upper austrian champion : 1991 , 1992 , 1997 , 1998 , 1999 , 2000 , ( second squad ) - upper austrian cup : 2001 ( second squad ) - öfb-frauenliga runner-up : 1987 , 1988 , 1989 , 1995 , 1997 , 2000 , 2005 - austrian cupfinals : 1997 , 2001 , 2003 other links . - union kleinmünchen official homepage with statistics about austrian women soccer ( german )
