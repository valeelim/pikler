Heterokont     the heterokonts or stramenopiles are a major line of eukaryotes with more than 100,000 known species , most of them diatoms . heterokonts are mostly algae . in one stage of their life cycle they have two unequal flagella . they include both single-celled types and brown algae ( seaweeds such as kelp and `` sargassum '' ) . they are members of the kingdom chromalveolata .
