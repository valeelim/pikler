Arsenite     arsenite is an ion . its chemical formula can be either aso or aso . it has arsenic in its +3 oxidation state . all arsenites are highly toxic . sodium arsenite is an example . arsenites are salts of arsenous acid . sometimes arsenite means arsenic trioxide , especially in groundwater chemistry ( chemistry dealing with the properties of water in the ground ) related pages . - arsenate - arsenide - phosphite - nitrite
