Loving County, Texas    loving county , texas loving county is a county in the state of texas in the united states . it is the united states ' smallest county by number of people . in 2000 , only 67 people lived there . the county seat is mentone . the county is named for oliver loving , a cattle farmer who , along with charles goodnight , developed the goodnight-loving trail . he died of wounds from comanches while on a cattle drive in 1867 near the county .
