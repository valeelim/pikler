Pomeroy, Iowa    pomeroy , iowa pomeroy is a city in calhoun county , iowa , united states . the population of pomeroy was 710 in 2000. pomeroy was hit by a tornado on july 6 , 1893 , that was estimated to be f5 on the fujita scale . there was a damage path of around 500 yards ( 460 m ) wide and 55 miles ( 89 km ) long and the tornado destroyed about 80 % of the homes in pomeroy . it killed 71 people and injured 200 others . other websites . - official city of pomeroy website
