Canadian Security Intelligence Service     the canadian security intelligence service ( csis ) ( french : service canadien du renseignement de sécurité ) ( scrs ) is the main intelligence agency of the canadian government . it is responsible for collecting , analyzing and reporting intelligence on threats to canada 's national security , and conducting operations , covert and overt , within canada and abroad . its headquarters are located at 1941 ogilvie road , in ottawa , ontario , in a purpose-built facility completed in 1995. csis ( pronounced /ˈsiːsɪs/ ) is responsible to parliament through the minister of public safety , but is also overseen by the federal court system , the inspector general of canada , and the security intelligence review committee . csis is a spy agency , similar to the cia in the united states .
