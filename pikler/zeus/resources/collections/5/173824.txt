Camellia sinensis     camellia sinensis , the tea plant , is the species of plant whose leaves and leaf buds are used to produce tea . it is in the genus `` camellia '' . white tea , green tea , oolong , pu-erh tea and black tea are all got from this species . they are processed differently , so they have different levels of oxidation . kukicha ( twig tea ) is also harvested from `` camellia sinensis '' , but uses twigs and stems rather than leaves .
