2101 Adonis     2101 adonis was one of the first near-earth asteroids to be found . it was found by eugene delporte in 1936 and named after adonis , the beautiful youth with whom the goddess venus fell in love . adonis is thought to measure about 1 km in diameter . in the close approach that led to the first time it was found , not enough sightings could be made to find out it 's orbit , and adonis was lost until 1977 when it was found once again by charles t. kowal . adonis was the second apollo asteroid to be found ( after 1862 apollo itself ) . it may be a dead comet , and may be the source of some meteor showers . it comes within 30 gm of the earth six times in the 21st century , the nearest being 5.3 gm in 2036. other websites . - long-lost planet 1936 ca ( `` adonis '' ) recovered
