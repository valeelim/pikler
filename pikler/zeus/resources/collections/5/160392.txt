Turbostar     turbostar is the name given to a series of diesel multiple units ( dmu ) built by bombardier transportation ( previously adtranz ) at their litchurch lane works in derby , england . the turbostar was the first new train type to be introduced after the privatisation of british rail , and is the most common type of diesel multiple unit ( its electrical variant , the electrostar , is the most common electric multiple unit ) . the first units were ordered by chiltern railways in 1996 and were designated class 168 ( also known as `` clubman '' ) . since then the family has grown with the addition of the class 170 , class 171 , and ( in development ) the class 172 .
