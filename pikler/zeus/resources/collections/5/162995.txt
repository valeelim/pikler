Anthony McGill     anthony mcgill is an american clarinetist . he is the principal clarinetist for the metropolitan opera . mcgill grew up in chicago , illinois . he went to the curtis institute of music in philadelphia , pennsylvania . he teaches at the peabody institute at johns hopkins university in baltimore , maryland . together with itzhak perlman ( violin ) , yo-yo ma ( cello ) , and gabriela montero ( piano ) , he recorded and performed `` air and simple gifts '' by john williams , for barack obama 's inauguration ceremony on 20 january 2009. other websites . - anthony mcgill biography at the peabody institute
