Otto Meißner     otto meißner ( 13 march 1880 in bischwiller – 27 may 1953 in munich ) was a german politician . he is well known for his work as head of the office of the presidents of germany during the weimar republic , under friedrich ebert and paul von hindenburg . he was also a member of hitler 's cabinet . meißner was tried at the ministries trial . he was acquitted in april 1949 , but in may 1949 was accused , and found to be a mitläufer
