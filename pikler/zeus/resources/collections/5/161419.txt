Rudolf II, Holy Roman Emperor    rudolf ii , holy roman emperor rudolf ii ( july 18 , 1552 - january 20 , 1612 ) was king of hungary ( as rudolf , 1572-1608 ) , king of bohemia ( as rudolf ii , 1575-1608/1611 ) , archduke of austria ( as rudolf v , 1576-1608 ) , and holy roman emperor ( as rudolf ii , 1576-1612 ) . he was a member of the habsburg family .
