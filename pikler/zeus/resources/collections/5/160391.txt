Sprinter (train)    sprinter ( train ) the sprinter is a family of diesel multiple unit trains in use on the uk railway system . they were built in the 1980s and early 1990s by brel , metro cammell and leyland . all are based around a cummins engine with voith hydraulic transmission . sprinters can be seen operating in almost every part of the uk , from rural branch lines to commuter expresses into major cities .
