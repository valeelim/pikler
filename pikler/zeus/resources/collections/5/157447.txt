Sphinginae     the sphinginae are a subfamily of the hawkmoths ( sphingidae ) , moths of the order lepidoptera . notable taxa include the pink-spotted hawkmoth , `` agrius cingulata '' , being a very common and easily spotted species , the death's-head hawkmoths ( `` acherontia '' spp . ) of `` silence of the lambs '' fame , and `` xanthopan morgani '' with its huge proboscis .
