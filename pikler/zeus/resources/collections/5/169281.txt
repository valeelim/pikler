Bruce Campbell     bruce lorne campbell ( born june 22 , 1958 ) is an american actor , producer , screenwriter and director . he is known for his starring role in the 1981 horror movie `` evil dead '' . he has since become a b-movie icon . he starred as sam axe in `` burn notice '' on the usa network . he played ronald reagan in season 2 of the fx original series `` fargo '' in late 2015 . < br >
