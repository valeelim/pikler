Iguaçu Falls     iguaçu falls are a series of massive waterfalls that lie on the border of argentina 80 % and brazil 20 % . it is fed by the iguaçu river , which flows into the paraná river . 275 individual waterfalls side by side , some as tall as , make up the falls , though most are around . the falls divide the river into the upper and lower iguaçu . it is one of the largest and most famous waterfalls in the world , receiving thousands of visitors each year .
