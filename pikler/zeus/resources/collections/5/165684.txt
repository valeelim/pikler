Three Cheers for Sweet Revenge     three cheers for sweet revenge is the second studio album by band my chemical romance . it was released on june 8 , 2004 through reprise records . four singles were released from this album : `` thank you for the venom '' , `` i 'm not okay ( i promise ) '' , `` helena '' and `` the ghost of you '' . it has sold over one million copies in the united states . the 11th track it 's not a fashion statement it 's a deathwish has the word 'fucking ' in the title on the cd itself but it is not shown as having that title on the back of the album . band - frank iero — guitar - matt pelissier — drums - ray toro — guitar - gerard way — vocals - mikey way — bass additional musicians - bert mccracken - additional vocals on `` you know what they do to guys like us in prison '' - keith morris - additional vocals on `` hang 'em high '' - rinat - additional vocals on `` the ghost of you '' - howard benson - 1958 hammond b3 production - howard benson - producer - rich costey - mixing - howard benson - mixing - mike plotnikoff - recording
