Geena Davis     virginia elizabeth `` geena '' davis ( born january 21 , 1956 ) is an academy award- , golden globe-winning and emmy-nominated american actress , film producer , writer , former fashion model . geena is also a member of mensa international . since 1982 until 1983 geena was married to richard emmolo . since 1987 until 1990 she was married to jeff goldblum . since 1993 until 1998 she was married to renny harlin . since september 1 , 2001 she married to reza jarrahy . they has three children , daughter alizeh keshvar davis jarrahy ( born on april 10 , 2002 ) and twin sons kian william davis jarrahy and kaiis steven davis jerrahy ( born on may 6 , 2004 ) .
