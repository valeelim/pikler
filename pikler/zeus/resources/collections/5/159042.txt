Abson     abson is a hamlet in south gloucestershire , england . it is in the united kingdom . the center of the village is a small village green and a church . this church is dedicated to st james the great , and is very important to abson . its belltower has six bells which are still rung by hand . blue lodge , one of the houses , was once the home of anna sewell . anne sewell is the writer of black beauty . while living there she saw a man killed by a cart . this was put into the novel .
