Alec Baldwin     alexander rae `` alec '' baldwin iii ( born april 3 , 1958 ) is an american actor , comedian and film producer , the oldest and probably the most well-known of the baldwin brothers , a family in movie and television . his career has brought him academy award , emmy , and golden globe nominations . he is noted for having hosted `` saturday night live '' several times . he plays the television executive jack donaghy in the nbc series `` 30 rock '' . he is also known for portraying president donald trump on `` saturday night live '' since 2016 .
