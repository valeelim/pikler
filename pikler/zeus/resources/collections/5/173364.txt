Glenn Ljungström     glenn ljungström was the guitarist of the swedish melodic death metal band in flames with jesper strömblad . he was a member of in flames from 1993 until 1997 , when he quit to find another job . glenn was also a guitarist of the swedish power metal band hammerfall from 1995 until 1997. album appearances . in flames . - `` lunar strain '' ( 1994 ) - `` subterranean '' ( 1995 , ep ) - `` the jester race '' ( 1996 ) - `` black-ash inheritance '' ( 1997 , ep ) - `` whoracle '' ( 1997 ) hammerfall . - `` glory to the brave '' ( 1997 ) dimension zero . - `` penetrations from the lost world '' ( 1997 , ep ) - `` silent night fever '' ( 2001 ) - `` this is hell '' ( 2003 ) - `` he who shall not bleed '' ( 2008 )
