East Germany national football team     east germany national football team was the national football team of the german democratic republic ( gdr or east germany ) from 1952 to 1990. the east germany team is now the germany team . east germany only qualified for one world cup , and they only made it to round 2 . - 1950 - `` did not enter '' - 1954 - `` did not enter '' - 1958 to 1970 - `` did not qualify '' - 1974 - round 2 - 1978 to 1990 - `` did not qualify '' east germany never qualified for a uefa european football championship tournament . - 1960 to 1988 - `` did not qualify '' - 1992 - `` left to qualification round ''
