Curry Tree     the curry tree ( also called karivepallai , karivembu or kadipatta ) is a kind of tree . it is found mostly in hot climates , and is a native plant of india . the leaves of the curry tree are called curry leaves or sweet neem leaves . usually , curry trees grow to 4-6 metres tall , and their trunk grows to 40 centimetres in diameter . small black , shiny berries that grow on the tree can be eaten , but their seeds are poisonous . the scientific name of the curry tree , `` murraya koenegii '' , was chosen to celebrate the botanist johann koenig .
