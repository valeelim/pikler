Salat al Eid     salat al eid ( ) also known as salat al eidain ( ) is the special prayer offered to commemorate two islamic festivals . both the eids are collectively known as eidain ( عيدين ) in arabic language . the two islamic festivals are : - eid al fitr , ( ) is celebrated on the 1st day of shawwal , the 10th month of islamic calendar . - eid al adha , ( ) is celebrated on the 10th day of dhu al-hijjah , the 12th month of islamic calendar .
