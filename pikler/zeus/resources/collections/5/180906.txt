Moeller High School     archbishop moeller high school is an all-boys , catholic school near cincinnati , ohio , united states . it opened in 1960 and is run by the society of mary ( marianist order ) . the school is famous for good sports teams : in its first 25 years , they won seven state football championships and five national football championships with gerry faust as head coach . other pages . - elder high school - la salle high school - st. xavier high school - elder high school
