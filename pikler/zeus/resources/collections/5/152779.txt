Multialphabetism     multialphabetism is where a mix of more than one alphabet is used . this can have different reasons : - cretain languages can be written using more than one alphabet . examples for this are japanese . it is possible to use kanji , which is based on logograms to write japanese , but also hiragana or katakana which are syllabic writing systems . - moldovan is officially written in the roman alphabet , because it is almost identical to the romanian language . in parts of moldovia , the cyrillic script is used though . the written languages are identical but use different scripts . - multialphabetism can also be the result of speaking many languages , and using the respective script for each . - only certain names are spelled in the original language . these use special characters . other pages . - multilingualism
