City of London School     the city of london school ( cls ) is a private school for boys , aged 10 to 18 , next to the river thames in the city of london . it was established in 1442. it is the brother school of the city of london school for girls ( a girls ' school within the city ) and of the city of london freemen 's school ( a day and boarding school in surrey ) . most boys enter the school at 11 and fewer enter at 10 and 13. there is a small intake at 16 into the sixth form .
