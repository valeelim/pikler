Zwickau Rural District     the rural district zwickau is a district in west saxony . it was founded at august 1 , 2008. it consists of the ( former ) chemnitzer land rural district and zwickauer land rural district . it is the smallest district of saxony but also the district with the highest population density of saxony . the district was formed on august 1 , 2008. this was the result of an administration reform in saxony . the rural district zwickau was built up by the chemnitzer land rural district , zwickauer land rural district and the town of zwickau . the county seat of the district is the biggest city zwickau . however , the former county seats of the different districts were kept as outpost . those are namely glauchau , limbach-oberfrohna , werdau and hohenstein-ernstthal . in july 2008 , there were 25,026 people jobless . that means there was a rate of unemployment of 11.1 percent . it was the lowest jobless rate of the regierungsbezirk chemnitz . because of the end of the schoolyear the unemployment rate of pupils rose up .
