Sidney Crosby     sidney patrick crosby , ons ( born august 7 , 1987 in cole harbour , nova scotia ) is a canadian professional ice hockey centre . he currently plays for the pittsburgh penguins of the national hockey league ( nhl ) where he serves as their captain . he was the top pick in the 2005 draft . in his second season , he won the art ross trophy . his nickname is `` sid the kid '' . sidney is a stanley cup champion , winning it in 2009 with the pittsburgh penguins . he got the game-winning goal for canada in the 2010 winter olympics in overtime against the usa and in doing so won the team the gold medal . the maurice 'rocket ' richard trophy ( tied with steven stamkos ) in 2010 other websites . - official website
