Corrientes     corrientes is a city in argentina . is the capital city of the province of corrientes , on the eastern shore of the paraná river , and about 1,000 km from buenos aires . according to the census of 2001 , the city had 328,689 people . as the provincial capital , corrientes is the most important city in the province , being an economical center , and holds the authorities and governmental institutions . the annual average temperature is 20 °c , with maximum and minimum averages of 33 °c and 10 °c , respectively . the annual rainfall is around 1,200 mm .
