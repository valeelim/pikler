Race to Witch Mountain     race to witch mountain is a 2009 movie starring dwayne johnson as jack bruno , a las vegas cabbie enlists the help of a ufo expert to protect two siblings , named sarah and seth played by annasophia robb and alexander ludwig , with paranormal powers from the clutches of an organization that wants to use the kids for their nefarious plans . the movie stars dwayne johnson , annasophia robb , alexander ludwig and carla gugino .
