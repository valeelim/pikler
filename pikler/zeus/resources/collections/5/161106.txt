Bloc Party (EP)    bloc party ( ep ) bloc party is an ep of songs in bloc party 's first two ( uk ) singles : `` she 's hearing voices '' and `` banquet/staying fat '' . the ep was released on may 24 , 2004 throughout europe . it was bloc party 's first v2 records ep . this ep was released later in japan in august , and then in the united states in september of the same year . track listing . 1 . `` banquet '' – 3:19 1 . `` staying fat '' – 2:23 1 . `` she 's hearing voices '' – 3:30 1 . `` the marshals are dead '' – 3:52 1 . `` the answer '' – 4:04 1 . `` banquet '' ( phones disco edit ) – 5:26
