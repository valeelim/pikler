Wilsthorpe Community School     wilsthorpe business and enterprise college is a school for children in the town of long eaton in south east derbyshire . the school is close to nottinghamshire . the school delivers free education to local children when they are eleven until they leave school . wilsthorpe was the first school in england to have a policeman on site in 2003. in their 2013 report , ofsted said that the school 'requires improvement ' .
