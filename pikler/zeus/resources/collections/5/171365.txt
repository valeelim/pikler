Woodstock Palace     woodstock palace was a royal residence in the english town of woodstock , oxfordshire . the title of `` palace '' was first used to refer to it during the twelfth century , when it was favoured by king henry i of england . in about 1120 he created a zoo in the grounds . his grandson , henry ii was also fond of woodstock , and spent time here with his mistress , rosamund clifford . woodstock palace was destroyed during the english civil war .
