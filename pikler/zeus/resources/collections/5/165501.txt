Sherbino Wind Farm     the sherbino wind farm is in pecos county in west texas . the first 150 megawatts ( mw ) of the project , which will be able to create 750 mw , is in operation . phase i uses 50 vestas v-90 wind turbine generators , each with a rated capacity of 3 mw . bp will operate phase i of the project . other pages . - list of large wind farms - wind farms - wind power - wind power in the united states - wind power in texas
