Manuel Pino     manuel pino is a professor at scottsdale community college in arizona . opposition to uranium mining has played a big role in pino 's life . he has written about `` the destructive impact of uranium mining on native american culture '' . he has spoken out at many international conferences about victims of uranium mining , depleted uranium , and associated cancer deaths . in 2008 , manuel pino received the nuclear-free future award . other pages . - uranium in the environment - anti-nuclear movement in the united states - jacqui katona other websites . - uranium mining and indigenous people - indigenous leaders call for end to uranium mining
