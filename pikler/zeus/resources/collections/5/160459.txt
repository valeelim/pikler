I (Cyrillic)    i ( cyrillic ) i ( и , и ) is the ninth letter in the cyrillic alphabet . it sounds like [ i ] . it looks like a backwards n. the lowercase и 's italic looks like a `` и '' . its old name was `` izhe '' . it came from the greek eta and stands for the roman i. there is another letter , the decimal i ( `` і '' , `` і '' ) , that sounds the same as и. russian , macedonian , serbian and bulgarian languages use only и ; and church slavonic and ukrainian uses both и and і. to tell the letters apart , и is sometimes called `` octal i '' because was eighth in the cyrillic numeral system . related pages . - eta ( letter ) - i - decimal i
