Tropical Storm Arthur (2008)    tropical storm arthur ( 2008 ) tropical storm arthur was the first tropical storm to form in may since 1981. arthur was a weak tropical storm that affected central america . arthur was the first tropical storm of the 2008 atlantic hurricane season . arthur formed from the remains of dissipating tropical storm alma which crossed central america from the pacific ocean . arthur caused nine deaths in belize and caused $ 78 million in damage .
