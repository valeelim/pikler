Cupressus macrocarpa     cupressus macrocarpa ( monterey cypress , macrocarpa ; syn . `` callitropsis macrocarpa '' ( hartw . ) d.p.little ) is a species of cypress endemic to the central coast of california . in the wild , the species is confined to two small populations , near monterey and carmel . these two small populations represent what was once a very large forest on the west coast . the surviving trees from this forest are as old as 2000 years .
