Ziaur Rahman     lieutenant-general ziaur rahman , bir uttam , hilal-i-jurat ( ziaur rôhman ; january 19 , 1936–may 30 , 1981 ) was a bangladeshi war hero , politician and statesman . he was the president of bangladesh from 1976 until 1981 and founded the bangladesh nationalist party ( bnp ) , one of the two largest political parties in the country . his widow , begum khaleda zia , has been prime minister of bangladesh three times .
