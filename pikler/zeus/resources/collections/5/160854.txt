Stagecoach Group     stagecoach group plc ( ) is a leading international transport group operating buses , trains , trams , express coaches and ferries . the group was founded in 1980 by the current chairman , brian souter , his sister , ann gloag , and her former husband robin . the group is based in perth , scotland , and has operations in the united kingdom and north america . other websites . - stagecoach group official site
