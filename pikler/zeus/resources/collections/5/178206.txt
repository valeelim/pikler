Masjid al-Haram     al-masjid al-ḥarām ( `` the sacred mosque '' ) , is the largest mosque in the world . it is in the city of mecca . it surrounds the kaaba ( the place which muslims face while offering daily prayers ) . it is considered the holiest place on earth by muslims . the mosque is also known as the grand mosque . this mosque has nine minarets . like masjid al-nabawi in medina , it also has undergone many renovations and expansions . the latest expansion was during king fahd 's reign . further expansion was planned by the former ruler , king abdullah .
