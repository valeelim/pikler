Mount Redoubt     mount redoubt , also called redoubt volcano , is a volcano in the u.s. state of alaska . it rises above sea level and above the valleys around it . it is about from the city of anchorage . on march 22 , 2009 , mount redoubt erupted , sending ash high in the air and covering the mountains around with ash . no people live near mount redoubt , but the ash spread into towns and cities and the airport of anchorage was closed shortly . other years when it erupted include 1902 , 1922 , 1966 , and 1989 .
