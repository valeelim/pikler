Roma     a.s. roma is a football club which plays in italy . the club formed from a merger of three clubs ; alba-audace roma , fortitudo-pro roma and roman in 1927. the current captain of the club is francesco totti . league title . - serie a : 3 - 1941/42 , 1982/83 , 2000/01 - serie b : 1 - 1951/52
