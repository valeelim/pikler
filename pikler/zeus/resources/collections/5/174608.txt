Khaleda Zia     begum khaleda zia ( ; born august 15 , 1945 ) is a former prime minister of bangladesh . she was prime minister from 1991 to 1996 , and also from 2001 to 2006. she was the first woman prime minister in bangladesh . zia is the widow of assassinated president of bangladesh ziaur rehman . she leads his old party , the bangladesh nationalist party ( bnp ) .
