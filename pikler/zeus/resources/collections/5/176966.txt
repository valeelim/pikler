Hillsborough Stadium     hillsborough stadium is the home of sheffield wednesday football club in sheffield , england . it was opened on 2 september 1899. it is now a 39,814 capacity all-seater stadium . a lot of the seats are covered . it is located in the sheffield suburb of owlerton . it takes its name from the parliamentary constituency in which it is . on 15 april 1989 , the ground was the scene of one of the worst sporting tragedies of all time . 94 liverpool fans were crushed to death in an fa cup semi-final . this was called the hillsborough disaster . the final death toll was 96. this meant that extra security measures were added there and in other stadiums around the country .
