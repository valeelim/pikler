Interstitial fluid     interstitial fluid is a type of fluid in the body . it is found in the interstitial spaces or tissue spaces - the fluid-filled areas that surround cells . it makes up about 3/4 of all extracellular fluid . it 's main purpose is provide cells with nutrients and assist the cells in waste removal . some components of interstitial fluid are plasmatic , lymphatic , intraocular , synovial , pericardial , intrapleural , peritoneal , and digestive fluids .
