Bruce Smeaton     bruce smeaton ( born 5 march 1938 ) is a composer , well known for writing music for australian movies and television . he has written music for movies , short films , television , documentaries and advertisements . he was born in brighton , victoria and currently lives in binalong , new south wales . he has written music for movies including `` picnic at hanging rock '' , `` seven little australians '' , `` the cars that ate paris '' and `` the devil 's playground .
