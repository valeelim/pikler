Salmon (color)    salmon ( color ) salmon ( pronounced sa-mon , as the l is silent ) is a range of pale pinkish-orange to light pink colors , named after the color of salmon flesh . the first recorded use of `` salmon '' as a color name in english was in 1776. meaning of salmon . - the color salmon is know to provide good health because salmon contains omega-3 fatty acids , which are good for you . - the color `` pale salmon '' ( shown below ) represents finance because it is used as the color of the paper on which some economic newspapers like the `` financial times '' are printed ( it is also the color of logo of the `` financial times '' website ) . hence the economic press is sometimes termed the `` salmon press '' as opposed to regular general newspapers published on off-white colored newsprint . other pages . - list of colors
