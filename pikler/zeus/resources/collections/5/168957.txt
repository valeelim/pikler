Ageism     ageism is when a person is treated badly only because of their age . ageism can be discrimination by one person or by a part of society as a group . discrimination is when a person is left out or abused by somebody who does not like something about them . for example , their facial features , their height or their skin . there have been many issues at the british broadcasting company , bbc , dealing with people saying that they have issues with ageism . ageism is often common in the entertainment industry where what a person looks like is felt by many to be important . there are two targets for age discrimination : old people ( also called senior citizens ) or very young people . discrimination against old people might happen , for example , at job interviews . a company might hire a younger worker who is about 30 years old instead of someone who is about 50 years old .
