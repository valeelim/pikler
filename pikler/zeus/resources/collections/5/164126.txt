Condé Nast Building     the condé nast building , also known as 4 times square , is a skyscraper in new york city , united states . it is 247 meters ( 809 feet ) tall and has 48 floors . it was built in 1999 and is one of the tallest buildings in the world . other pages . - list of tallest buildings in the world - list of tallest buildings in new york city other websites . - emporis.com - 4 times square - skycraperpage.com – condé nast building
