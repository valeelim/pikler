Knowth     knowth is the name given to a number of passage graves , in the boyne valley in ireland . it consists of a bigger tumulus with two passages , and of 20 smaller ones . of the smaller graves , 17 remain , 3 have been destroyed . knowth is approximately 1 km west of newgrange . it is probably older than newgrange , which has been dated to about 3.150 bce , but not as old as dowth . the main tumulus is one of the biggest to be found in ireland .
