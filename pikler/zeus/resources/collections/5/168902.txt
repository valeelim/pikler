Oppression     oppression is when a person or group of people who have power use it in a way that is not fair , unjust or cruel . it can also describe the feeling of people who are oppressed . oppressive governments can lead to a rebellion . racism , sexism and other prejudices can cause oppression , especially if laws are made based on them . anarchists say that government , the police and laws are oppressive . this is because they stop people from doing whatever they want to . the universal declaration of human rights , and the idea of human rights in general , is meant to stop oppression . related pages . - persecution
