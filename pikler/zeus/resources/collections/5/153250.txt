Courcelles-sur-Vesle     courcelles-sur-vesle is a commune . it is found in the region picardie in the aisne department in the north of france . its population is 295 ( 1999 census ) . its density is 32 inhabitants/km² . it 's in this village that napoléon met marie-louise , in front of the church , on march 27 1810. she became empress of the french at just 19 years of age . at the time napoléon was 41. the mayor since 2001 has been luc tordeux . he was re-elected in 2008. the next elections are in 2014. the remains of the choir of the old church have had listed building status since 1927. the chapel called `` le calvaire '' , has been a listed building since 1929. the courcelles sur vesle castle was built in 1694. it was ordered by the baron jacques de la grange . in 1988 , it became a hotel and a restaurant .
