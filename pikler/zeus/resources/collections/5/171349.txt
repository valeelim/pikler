Kensington Palace     kensington palace is a royal residence set in kensington gardens , in the royal borough of kensington and chelsea in london , england . it has been a residence of the british royal family since the 17th century . today , it is the main residence of the duke and duchess of gloucester ; the duke and duchess of kent ; and prince and princess michael of kent . kensington palace is also used on an unofficial basis by prince henry , as well as his cousin , zara phillips .
