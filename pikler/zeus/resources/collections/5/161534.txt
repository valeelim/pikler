Kero Kero Keroppi     kero kero keroppi is a japanese character of a frog . it was made in 1987 by a japanese design company called sanrio . sales of the first goods were in june , 1988. he got the first place in popularity vote of character in 1990. kero kero keroppi was made into a game . that game was released on the nintendo famicom console in japan in 1991. he lives in doughnut pond with his brother and sister , koroppi and picky , father and mother . his father is a doctor and mother runs a small restaurant . keroppi likes rice balls that his mother made . he has girlfriend . her name is keroleen . his birthday is july 10. he likes adventures and he is good at swimming and singing . he is a popular frog in the doughnut pond .
