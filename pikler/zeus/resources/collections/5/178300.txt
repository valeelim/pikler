Gymnasium     a gymnasium ( often called a gym ) is a place used for physical activity . the word `` γυμνάσιον '' ( gymnasion ) was used in ancient greece . it meant a place for both physical and intellectual education of young men ( see gymnasium ( ancient greece ) ) . the intellectual education meaning persisted in german and other languages to mean a certain type of school providing secondary education , the gymnasium , whereas in english the meaning of physical education was pertained in the word gym . however , the word `` gymnasium '' is not used very often in english nowadays . the term favoured in english is `` sports centre '' , which perhaps seems more natural . some gyms offer memberships where people can train and improve their physical appearance . commercial gyms offer sometimes help from qualified trainer . again , another term is more used today : `` fitness centre '' . sports such as basketball and volleyball are often played in gymnasia .
