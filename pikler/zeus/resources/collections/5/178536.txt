Mickey's House of Villains    mickey 's house of villains mickey 's house of villains is a direct-to-video animated movie created by disney . it is the movie adaptation of the disney channel animated television series `` disney 's house of mouse '' , starring mickey mouse , donald duck , minnie mouse , goofy , daisy duck , and characters and villains that have appeared in past disney productions . it was released on both vhs and dvd by walt disney home video on september 3 , 2002. voice cast . - wayne allwine as mickey mouse - russi taylor as minnie mouse - bill farmer as goofy - tony anselmo as donald duck - tress macneille as daisy duck / queen of hearts / si / am / the fates - jonathan freeman as jafar - gilbert gottfried as iago - corey burton as captain hook / chernabog - susanne blakeslee as cruella de vil - pat carroll as ursula - james woods as hades - rob paulsen as hades ( singing ) - lois nettleton as maleficent - jim cummings as big bad wolf / kaa / ed the hyena
