Quid pro quo     `` quid pro quo '' is a latin phrase . it means roughly `` this for that . '' in other words , the phrase describes the context when something is given in return for something else . the phrase means the same thing as the english saying `` a tit for a tat '' , or trading something for something else . it can also mean the same thing as `` an eye for an eye '' . lawyers sometimes use this saying to mean `` an even trade '' . - quid pro quo means reciprocity related pages . - golden rule
