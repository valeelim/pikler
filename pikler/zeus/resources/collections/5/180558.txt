Tim Walberg     tim walberg ( born 12th april 1951 ) is a republican politician . he is a social and economic conservative . he won his campaigns with clear support for president bush 's war in iraq , tax cuts , and opposition to `` pork barrel '' spending . he was defeated in the 2008 election by mark schauer . he lives in tipton , michigan with his wife sue and their children matthew , heido and caleb . he was elected back to congress in november 2010 , this time defeating schauer , and thus will be going back in january 2011 .
