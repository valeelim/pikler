Åland Islands    åland islands the åland islands ( swedish : `` åland '' ( pronounced ) , finnish : `` ahvenanmaa '' ) form an archipelago in the baltic sea . it is situated at the entrance to the gulf of bothnia and forms an autonomous , demilitarized , monolingually swedish-speaking administrative province , region and historical province of finland . it is the smallest province of finland , comprising 0.5 % of finland 's population and 0.49 % of land area . residents of åland make up .9 % of finland 's population . other websites . - official website
