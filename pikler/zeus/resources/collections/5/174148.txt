Duke of Devonshire     duke of devonshire is a title in the peerage of england held by members of the aristocratic cavendish family . this branch of the cavendish family has been one of the richest and most influential aristocratic families in england since the 16th century , and have been rivalled in political influence perhaps only by the earls of derby and the marquesses of salisbury . further reading . - pearson , john . `` the serpent and the stag '' . holt , rinehart , & winston , 1984. references . - kidd , charles , williamson , david ( editors ) . `` debrett 's peerage and baronetage '' ( 1990 edition ) . new york : st martin 's press , 1990 . - www.thepeerage.com other websites . - an online gotha - devonshire - chiswick 's local search engine
