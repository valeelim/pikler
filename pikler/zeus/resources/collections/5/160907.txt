Euston Square tube station     euston square is a london underground station at the corner of euston road and gower street , just north of university college london ( not to be confused with nearby euston tube station ) . the station , which is within walking distance of euston railway station , is on the circle , hammersmith & city and metropolitan lines , between great portland street and king 's cross st. pancras . it is in zone 1 of the underground . the postcode is nw1 2lu .
