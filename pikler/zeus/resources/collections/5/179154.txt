MythBusters     mythbusters is an american scientific television show , which began on january 23 , 2003. the hosts , adam savage and jamie hyneman along with kari byron , tory belleci and grant imahara attempt to test whether certain myths are `` true or false '' , referred to on the show as `` confirmed or busted '' , respectively . the show has had a total of 267 episodes as of september 5 , 2015. two more episodes are to come in january 2016 .
