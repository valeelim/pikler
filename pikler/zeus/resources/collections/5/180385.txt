Waterfowl     waterfowl are any type of bird that spends a lot of its life in or near water . however , it mostly refers to the family anatidae , which are ducks , geese and swans . the term can be used more widely to include birds such as coots , grebes , moorhens , shorebirds and seabirds . a very similar term is `` water bird '' , which is used in contrast to waders , which walk about in shallow water .
