Chinese Wikipedia     the chinese wikipedia ( in chinese : / ) is the chinese-language edition of wikipedia . this edition was started in october 24 , 2002. the chinese wikipedia can show the same article in many different scripts , in both simplified and traditional script . it is the 15th largest edition by article count . as of july 14 , 2016 , it had 889,003 articles . in the english language , `` wikipedia '' is a portmanteau word made by `` wiki '' ( a technology that allows many people working together ) and `` encyclopedia '' . at first , `` wikipedia '' did not have a chinese name . the site was called `` zhongwen wikipedia '' ( 中文wikipedia , chinese wikipedia ) . on october 21 , 2003 , wikipedia 's chinese name `` weiji baike '' ( 维基百科/維基百科 , meaning weiji encyclopedia ) was decided by the chinese wiki community ( 13 people voted ) . the pronunciation of `` weiji '' is like the pronunciation of `` wiki '' . `` wei '' ( 维/維 ) means rope that can link things , or net . `` ji '' ( 基 ) means basic , or base . so `` weiji baike '' means `` the encyclopedia on the internet that covers human 's all basic knowledge '' .
