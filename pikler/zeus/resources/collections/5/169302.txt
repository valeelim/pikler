Chulabhorn Dam     chulabhorn dam ( เขื่อนจุฬาภรณ์ ) is named after a princess , chulabhorn walailak , of thailand . the dam is in tambon thung lui lai , amphur kon san , chaiyaphum province on the nam phrom river . the dam is 700 metres long and 70 metres high . it holds a reservoir of 31 square kilometres . other websites . - chulabhorn dam in chaiyaphum
