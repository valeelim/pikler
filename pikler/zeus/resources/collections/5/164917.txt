Fourcès     fourcès is a commune in southwestern france . it is more precisely in gascony , in the gers department , in the region midi-pyrénées . this graph indicates the population of fourcès over time ( source : insee ) . in 2005 there were 295 inhabitants ( 2005 ) . fourcès is a typical medieval bastide , a little village where the houses are built in a circle to allow a proper defence . because of its landscapes and its vineyards the gers is now and then called by some the `` tuscany of france '' . the summers are long and warm ; the winters are soft and short . in the neighbourhood are numerous old castles , little medieval walled villages , small cities built around castles and `` sacred '' places , because the via podiensis and the way of st. james of compostela are going through the montréal and left their traces .
