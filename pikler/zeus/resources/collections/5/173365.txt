Magnus Rosén     klas magnus rosén is a swedish musician , born 1963 in gothenburg , and was the bassist of swedish band hammerfall . magnus rosén started to play the bass when he was 15 years old . when he had played bass for three years he went on his first tour with the band kung sune . after the tour he moved to los angeles . he became a member in the swedish heavy metal band hammerfall in 1997 .
