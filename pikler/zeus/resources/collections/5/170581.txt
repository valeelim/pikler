Donkey Kong 64     donkey kong 64 ( also called dk 64 ) , is a platform video game made by rare and published by nintendo for the nintendo 64 video game system . it was first out on november 24 , 1999 in north america . donkey kong 64 is one of the first games for the nintendo 64 to use the expansion pak . the gameplay of donkey kong 64 is about getting different types of items . some of those items include coins , bananas , weapons , and keys . these items are needed in order to move on to new levels and fighting bosses . donkey kong 64 can be played on multiplayer for up to 4 players at a time . the multiplayer mode has three arenas , and five characters to pick from the single player mode . donkey kong 64 got good scores from reviewers , which had a 88 % average on gamerankings .
