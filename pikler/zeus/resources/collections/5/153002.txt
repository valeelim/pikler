Crinkle-collared manucode     the crinkle-collared manucode ( `` manucodia chalybatus '' ) is a medium-sized bird of paradise , that grows up to 36cm long . it looks glossy greenish blue , black and purple with long tail , red iris and iridescent green breast feathers . this species looks a little like the jobi manucode in appearance . it is found in lowlands and hill forests in mainland new guinea and misool island of west papua . they eat mainly of fruits and figs . references . - database entry includes a lengthy justification of why this species is of least concern
