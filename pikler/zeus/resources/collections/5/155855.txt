Joliet, Illinois    joliet , illinois joliet is a city of illinois in the united states . joliet is 40 miles southwest of chicago , and is the county seat of will county . about 106,000 people lived in joliet as of the year 2000 , and about 146,000 people lived in joliet as of the year 2007. joliet is one of illinois ' quickest growing cities , and one of the quickest growing cities in the united states . it is also home to the chicagoland speedway used by nascar annually .
