Mark McGwire     mark david mcgwire ( born october 1 , 1963 in pomona , california ) is a former right-handed first baseman in major league baseball who played for the oakland athletics ( 1986–1997 ) and the st. louis cardinals ( 1997–2001 ) . he had a career .263 batting average , and 583 home runs . although mcgwire has never admitted to or been convicted of any steroid use , many of his accomplishments , particularly his historic home run surge late in his career , have come into question due to his connection to the steroid scandal in major league baseball .
