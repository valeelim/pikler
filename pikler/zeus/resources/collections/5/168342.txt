Slip End     slip end is a medium sized village in south bedfordshire , england . there is one school in the village - slip end lower school serving the community and surrounding areas from reception to year 4 children . there are two community venues in the village : - the village hall is at the centre of the village . - peter edwards hall and playing fields is adjacent to the church , st andrews , next to the m1 motorway . other websites . - slip end official website
