Duchy of Courland and Semigallia     the duchy of courland and semigallia was a duchy in the baltic region . it existed from 1562 through 1795. it was a vassal state of grand duchy of lithuania and later of poland-lithuania . it became independent in 1791 , but was annexed by russian empire in 1795 during third partition of poland . it had its own empire . one of its colonies was modern day trinidad and tobago . predecessor . - livonian confederation successor . - courland governorate
