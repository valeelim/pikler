Lee Soo Young     lee soo young ( born lee ji yeon ( ) april 12 , 1979 ) is a korean ballad singer . she started her singing career in 1999 in korea with `` i believe '' . she is well known for her lyrical and powerful voice . she has earned much popularity in korea . she sang the song , `` 얼마나 좋을까 '' ( `` eolmana joheulkka '' ) which is added to the original soundtrack of final fantasy x in 2001. lee has released 8 studio albums . she is one of the most popular ballad singer in korea . lee was born in seoul , south korea . she has a younger brother and a younger sister . her father died from car accident when she was nine . after this , her mother earned money by working for restaurants and delivering milk bottles . she went to a church and she said it helped her a lot to maintain her life . after her mother died from car accident , she began her career as a singer . she started to sell her albums in japan in 2004. she released one studio album , and two single album . with these albums , she got only 125th position in oricon chart .
