10370 Hylonome     10370 hylonome is an asteroid orbiting in the farther part of the solar system . it belongs to the group of icy minor planets called `` centaurs '' , with an orbit that crosses the orbits of neptune and uranus . it was found on february 27 , 1995. sightings with the spitzer space telescope show that it 's diameter is about 70 km ( 43 miles ) plus or minus 20 km ( 50 to 90 km in diameter ) , or a 35 km radius . related pages . - centaur ( planetoid ) - minor planet - solar system other websites . - list of centaurs and sdos .
