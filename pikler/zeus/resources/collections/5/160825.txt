British Rail APT-E     the apt-e ( meaning advanced passenger train experimental ) tilting train was the prototype advanced passenger train unit . it was powered by gas turbines , the only multiple unit so powered that was used by british rail . it was formed of two power cars ( numbers pc1 and pc2 ) , with two trailer cars ( tc1 and tc2 ) in between . the cars were made of aluminium and were articulated to reduce the weight of the unit . other pages . - advanced passenger train - british rail class 370
