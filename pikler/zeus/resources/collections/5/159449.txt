British Rail Class 415     british rail class 415 ( or 4epb ) was a suburban 750v dc third rail electric multiple unit commissioned by the southern region of british railways . built between 1951 and 1957 , it became the most numerous class on the region after the withdrawal of the 4subs . the final trains were withdrawn in the 1990s , replaced by class 455 , 456 , 465 and 466
