Castor oil plant     the castor oil plant ( `` ricinus communis '' ) , is a plant species of the euphorbiaceae , the sole member of the genus ricinus and of the subtribe ricininae . its seed is the castor bean which is not a true bean . the castor oil plant is used as an ornamental plant in gardens and to create castor oil . its oil is used in the chemical industry , e.g . for polyurethane , for adhesives , dyes and lubricants . as a drug , it can be used as a laxative .
