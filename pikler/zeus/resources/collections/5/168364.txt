David Gilmour     david jon gilmour cbe ( born 6 march 1946 ) is an english musician . he was born in cambridge . he is most famous for being a guitarist , singer and songwriter in the rock band pink floyd . along with being in pink floyd , he has worked as a record producer for other musicians . gilmour has also recorded and released albums as a solo performer . gilmour has always been active with many different charities . he was appointed cbe in 2003 for services to both music and for his charity work . in 1996 , gilmour was inducted into the rock and roll hall of fame as a member of pink floyd . in 2003 , gilmour was listed at number 82 in `` rolling stone magazine 's list of the `` 100 greatest guitarists of all time '' discography . - `` david gilmour '' ( 1978 ) - `` about face '' ( 1984 ) - `` on an island '' ( 2006 ) - `` live in gda≈Ñsk '' ( 2008 )
