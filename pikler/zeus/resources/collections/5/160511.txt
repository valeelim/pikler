Michael I of Romania     michael , king of the romanians , prince of hohenzollern ( born 25 october 1921 ) , reigned as king of the romanians for two periods . he was king from 20 july 1927 to 8 june 1930 , and again from 6 september 1940 , until forced to abdicate by the communist party of romania . the communists were backed up by orders of stalin to the soviet armies of occupation on 30 december 1947. a great-great-grandson of queen victoria and a third cousin of queen elizabeth ii , he is one of the last surviving heads of state from world war ii , the other one is simeon ii of bulgaria .
