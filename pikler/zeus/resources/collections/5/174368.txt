Hysterectomy     a hysterectomy ( from greek `` hystera '' `` womb '' and `` ektomia '' `` a cutting out of '' ) is surgery to remove the womb . this is usually done by a gynecologist . women who undergo a hysterectomy can no longer bear children . since the womb also produces hormones their hormone levels change . hysterectomies are done for only a few , very specific conditions , including : - certain forms of cancer of the reproductive system ( affecting womb , cervix , ovaries ) , or tumors - for transsexual men , as part of the gender transformation
