Camp Nou     camp nou ( also known as the nou camp ) is a football stadium used by fc barcelona that was opened on 1957. it can hold 99,354 people , making it the largest stadium in europe and second largest football stadium in the world . the stadium was used to host the 1982 fifa world cup and the 1992 summer olympics . it also hosted the 1999 uefa champions league final between manchester united and bayern munich . renovations are to be made for the stadium that would increase the capacity by 13,000 people ( which would be about 112,000 capacity ) . if the renovations will be made , the stadium would be the second largest stadium in the world .
