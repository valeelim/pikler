WrestleMania X     wrestlemania x was the tenth wrestlemania professional wrestling pay-per-view event . it was produced by the world wrestling federation . it happened at madison square garden in new york , new york on march 20 , 1994. the main part of the pay-per-view was the wwf championship , which lasted two matches . lex luger challenged the champion yokozuna first . luger was later for pushing the referee . bret hart faced yokozuna later in the event . he won the championship by beating yokozuna . this led to a long worked feud between bret and his brother owen . owen beat bret in the opening match of the pay-per-view .
