Border Gateway Protocol     the border gateway protocol ( bgp ) is the main routing protocol of the internet . it decides how data gets from one place to another on the internet . it was made to get rid of the egp routing protocol to make the internet more independent . most internet users do n't use bgp . it is the isps who use it to talk to other isps so they can send and get data from each other . it is one of the most important protocols of the internet . related pages . - shortest path bridging - intermediate system to intermediate system
