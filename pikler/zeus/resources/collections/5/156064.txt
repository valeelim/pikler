Greencastle, Indiana    greencastle , indiana greencastle is a city in the u.s. state of indiana . before greencastle became a city in 1861 , it was a town . greencastle , indiana was founded in year 1821 by a man named ephraim dukes after he purchased and later sold some land to the local government . this type of purchase is called a land grant . ephraim duke named the settlement for his hometown of greencastle , pennsylvania .
