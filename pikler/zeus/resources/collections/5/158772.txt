Tool     tools are things used to make tasks easier . tools have changed over time . the first tools were made in the stone age and bronze age . throughout history , people have made many new and more complicated tools . some examples of tools that are often used today are the hammer , the wrench ( also called a spanner ) , saws , shovel , telephone , and the computer . very basic things like knives , pens , and pencils are also tools . the bludgeon ( a stick or rock used as a weapon to smash things ) was one of the first tools made by humans . the knife is also one of the first tools humans made .
