Paranthropus boisei     paranthropus boisei was originally called zinjanthropus boisei and then australopithecus boisei until recently . it was an australopithecine , the largest of the `` paranthropus '' species . it lived from about 2.6 until about 1.2 million years ago during the pliocene and pleistocene epochs in eastern africa . it was 4 ' 6 '' tall for a male and 4 ' 1 '' for a female .
