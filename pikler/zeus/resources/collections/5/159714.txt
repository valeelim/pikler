Juniper (train)    juniper ( train ) the coradia juniper series is a family of electric multiple unit trains built by alstomtransport birmingham , subcontractor : ganz hunslet , hungary ( project for 120 bodyshells in 1998-2000 , project manager at ganz hunslet ; gabor honthy ) for use on the railway network in great britain . the family is related to the coradia 1000 series of diesel multiple unit . there are currently three types in service with various tocs .
