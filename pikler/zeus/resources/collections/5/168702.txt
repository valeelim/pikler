Cheesesteak     a cheesesteak is a sandwich invented in philadelphia . it is made of a long roll , cut in half and filled with thin slices of steak and covered with melted cheese . some cheesesteaks also have onions or green peppers . pat 's king of steaks and geno 's are two famous places in philadelphia that make cheesesteaks . pat 's is considered the place that invented the cheesesteak .
