Lacres     lacres is a commune . it is found in the region nord-pas-de-calais in the pas-de-calais department in the north of france . lacres is about 12 miles or 19 kilometers south east of boulogne , where the d901 meets with the d125 and d125e roads . some places to visit there are the church of st. martin , from the 1400 's and a farm and manor house from the 1600 's .
