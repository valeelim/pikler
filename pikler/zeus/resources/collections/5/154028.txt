Rexburg, Idaho    rexburg , idaho rexburg is a city in idaho in the united states . it is the county seat of madison county . there were 28650 people living in rexburg in 2009. brigham young university-idaho , is based in the city . the town was settled in 1883 by a group of young mormon men who had been sent into the upper snake river valley . they were looking for places to start mormon communities . the town was first called ricksburg , after one of the men , thomas e. rick , but later changed to his original german family name of rex .
