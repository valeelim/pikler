British Rail Class 423     the british rail class 423 ( or 4vep ) electrical multiple units were built by br at york works from 1967 to 1974 , although the mbsos and tsos of the first 20 , 7701-7720 , were constructed at derby works . they feature manually opening doors next to every seating row . they were mostly found working outer suburban services in south london , and rural services in kent and sussex , up to 2005 when they were finally replaced by electrostar and desiro units .
