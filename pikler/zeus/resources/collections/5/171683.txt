Perforation     perforation means lines of punching holes made in any material . it is utilised in producing paper-made objects ( like stamps ) , films , etc. ) . in philately , there are three types of the cutting of postage stamps that are used for stamp separation , including : 1 . `` perforation '' , or cutting rows and columns of small holes . 1 . `` rouletting '' , or small horizontal and vertical cuts . 1 . `` diecutting '' , or cutting paper to shape using a metal die ( used for self-adhesive stamps ) . related pages . - postage stamp
