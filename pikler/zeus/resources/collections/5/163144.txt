Short I     short i ( й , й ) is a letter in the cyrillic alphabet . it sounds like [ j ] . it is an и with a breve on top . it is used in the russian alphabet , where it is called `` и краткое '' ( `` i kratkoye '' or `` short i '' ) , in the ukrainian alphabet , where it is called `` йот '' , ( yot ) or `` ий '' ( `` yi '' , sounded like [ j ] ) , in the bulgarian alphabet , where it is called `` и кратко '' ( `` i kratko '' or `` short i '' ) , and in the belarusian alphabet . in serbian , macedonian and azeri , ј is used instead of й. related pages . - i ( cyrillic ) - je ( cyrillic )
