Hannah     hannah ( meaning `` grace of god '' ) is a woman in the bible , in the book of 1 samuel chapters 1 and 2. she was the wife of elkanah . after she begged god for a child , she gave birth to samuel . samuel became a prophet of god ( a voice for god in israel ) . when samuel was a young child , she took him to live in the temple ( as she had promised god ) , to be an apprentice of eli the priest .
