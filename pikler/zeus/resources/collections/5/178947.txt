Cryptosporidium     cryptosporidium is a protozoan parasite which causes outbreaks of diarrhea . a number of species of `` cryptosporidium '' can infect mammals . the most common species to infect man is `` cryptosporidium parvum '' . the diarrhea can become severe in children , and in people whose immune system is weakened . it is often found in hiv patients who have diarrhea . in healthy adults , the infection is usually brief . it is difficult to identify in the laboratory . the oocytes can be seen under a microscope , but they may be confused . most cryptosporidia are 3-6 Î¼m in size , but some reports have described larger cells . new real-time monitoring can detect `` cryptosporidium '' better than the older spot testing and batch testing methods . if `` cryptosporidium '' is suspected in the water , the safest option is to boil water used for drinking .
