Erie Canal     the erie canal is a canal in new york state . it runs for 365 miles from the hudson river to lake erie . it connects the great lakes with the atlantic ocean . it was first thought of in 1808 , and it was built between 1818 and 1825. it was the first route between the eastern coast of the united states and the great lakes . the canal caused a burst of population in western new york . it also helped new york city become a large port . today , the canal is part of the `` new york state canal system '' . other websites . - `` the erie canal ''
