Regiment     a regiment is a military unit , made up of a number of battalions , run by a colonel . a modern regiment can also be similar to a brigade , in that both can be from a few hundred soldiers to 5,000 soldiers ( from 3 to 7 battalions ) . the modern regiment 's size changes in number , purpose , and role from country to country ( and might not exist in some military forces ) and sometimes even within the military of the same nations .
