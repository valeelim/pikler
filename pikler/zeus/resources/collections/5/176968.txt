Liverpool Street station     liverpool street station , ( or london liverpool street ) is a major railway station in the city of london . it is the southern terminus of the great eastern main line ( geml ) , and is connected to a london underground station of the same name . liverpool street the main departure point from london to the east of england . it serves those places not served by the east coast main line from king 's cross . it has commuter service to parts of east london and essex , such as chelmsford , clacton-on-sea , witham and norwich . it serves the london stansted airport via the stansted express . it is one of the busiest stations in the united kingdom , the third busiest in london after waterloo and victoria . over 123 million people use the station each year . it is one of 17 managed by network rail . the station has exits to bishopsgate , liverpool street and the broadgate development . it is in travelcard zone 1. other websites . - station information on liverpool street station from network rail
