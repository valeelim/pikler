Raccoon Dog     the raccoon dog ( `` nyctereutes procyonoides '' ) is a member of the canid family and is from east asia . despite being hunted by many people ( thousands of people ) , a small island in japan has been designated a protected area for raccoon dogs . raccoon dogs are fox-like in build , but with shorter legs and tail . they have a dark patch on the side of the face , similar to a raccoon 's facial markings .
