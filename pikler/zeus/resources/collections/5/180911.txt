No man's land    no man 's land no man 's land is land that is not part of any country . it may be unclaimed territory , or an area that is under dispute and not occupied because of fear or uncertainty . during war ( especially world war i ) , it is a term used for the area of land between two enemy trenches . usually nobody was alive there . it was full of shell holes , dead bodies , and barbed wire , and raked by machine gun fire . attacks were made across no man 's land but few attacking soldiers survived . related pages . - terra nullius
