Dracaena (plant)    dracaena ( plant ) dracaena is a genus of about 40 trees and succulent shrubs . the name comes from ancient greek `` drakaina '' , meaning female dragon . most of them are native to tropical africa , some occur in southeast asia , and tropical south america . they are usually classified into `` dragon trees '' and `` shrubby dracenas '' according to how they grow ; the latter are also often grown as house plants .
