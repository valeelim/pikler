Anaxandridas II     anaxandridas ii was a king of sparta between 560 and 525 bc , and was the father of leonidas i , king of sparta . anaxandridas was a son of leon of sparta and also his successor . it is not known who anaxandridas ' mother is . his first wife was his niece . having a barren wife whom he would not divorce , the ephors , we are told , made him take with her a second . by her he had cleomenes i , and after this , by his first wife he had dorieus ( father of euryanas ) , famous leonidas i , and cleombrotus . several sayings are ascribed to him according to plutarch .
