Mihai Eminescu     mihai eminescu ( pronunciation in romanian : ) ( january 15 1850 – june 15 1889 ) , born `` mihail eminovici '' , was a late romantic poet , the best-known and most influential romanian poet . famous poems include `` luceafărul '' ( `` evening star '' ) , `` odă în metru antic '' ( `` ode in ancient meter '' ) , and the five `` scrisori '' ( `` epistles/satires '' ) . eminescu was active in the `` junimea '' literary society , and served as editor of `` timpul '' , the official newspaper of the conservative party .
