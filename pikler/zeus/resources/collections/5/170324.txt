Kim Yoon-ok     kim yoon-ok , also called kim yun-ok , ( , born 26 march 1947 ) is the wife of lee myung-bak , president of the republic of korea . her family runs a construction company in daegu . she and her husband lee myung-bak met at the college of health education at ewha womans university . on december 19 , 1970 , after graduation from the university , kim was engaged to lee myung-bak against her family 's will . lee was then director of the hyundai engineering corporation . they have 1 son and 3 daughters together . kim yoon-ok enjoys cooking .
