Johan Larsson     johan larsson ( b . 8 december 1974 ) is a swedish guitarist . he was the first bassist of the swedish melodic death metal band in flames . johan was also the first bassist of the swedish power metal band hammerfall , but quit before the band recorded a studio album . album appearances . - `` lunar strain '' ( 1994 ) - `` subterranean '' ( 1995 , ep ) - `` the jester race '' ( 1996 ) - `` black-ash inheritance '' ( 1997 , ep ) - `` whoracle '' ( 1997 )
