Amy MacDonald     amy macdonald is a scottish singer and songwriter . she became famous in 2007 with her first album `` this is the life '' and her first single `` poison prince '' . she has become even more successful in europe since her single `` this is the life '' charted at number 1 in many european countries . discography . - `` this is the life '' ( 2007 ) - `` a curious thing '' ( 2010 ) - `` life in a beautiful light '' ( 2012 )
