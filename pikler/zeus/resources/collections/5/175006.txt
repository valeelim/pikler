Johnstown Flood     the johnstown flood , otherwise known as the great flood of 1889 , was a major flood that happened in a city called johnstown , pennsylvania , in the eastern united states . it was on a river called the `` little conemaugh river '' . it was caused by the collapse of a dam called the `` south fork dam '' , upstream , after several days of heavy rain . the date it happened was may 31 , 1889 . 2,209 people were killed by the 20 million tons of water that flooded the town . the flood also resulted in $ 17 million in damages .
