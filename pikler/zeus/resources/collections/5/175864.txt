Yosemite National Park     yosemite national park is a national park in the state of california , u.s.a.. created in 1890 , it was the second national park , after yellowstone . it is a unesco world heritage site . yosemite covers 1,189 square miles ( 3,081 square kilometers ) on the sierra nevada . over 3.5 million people visit the park each year . despite the park 's great size , most people spend time in yosemite valley , one of the most beautiful places in the park . almost 95 percent of the park is wilderness . yosemite has many waterfalls . the most famous of these is the world-famous yosemite falls , which at tall , is one of the tallest waterfalls in the world . in the northern area of the park lies the hetch hetchy reservoir , a debated dam project . the reservoir floods . many people supporting environmentalism are trying to get the dam removed . the eighth major operating system of os x by apple inc. is named after the yosemite national park , called os x yosemite . related pages . - list of world heritage sites in the united states
