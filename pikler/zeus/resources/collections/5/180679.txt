Claves     claves ( spanish word : second e is sounded ) are percussion instruments . they are small wooden sticks made of tropical hardwood . they are used in pairs . they are traditionally heard in cuban music , e.g . salsa . in cuban music they often repeat a special rhythm called `` clave '' ( pronounce : `` clah-ve '' ) . the usual way of playing claves is to hold one clave lightly between the thumb and the fingertips with the palm of the hand facing upwards , or to cradle the clave in the hand . the other , held with the other hand , hits it .
