San Luis Rey River     the san luis rey river is a river in the southern part of the u.s. state of california . it begins in the cleveland national forest , near a mountain called palomar mountain , in several forks . it flows south into a reservoir , called `` lake henshaw '' . it then turns west and flows to its mouth at the pacific ocean . altogether , it is about long . its watershed covers an area of 562 square miles . the lower part of the river is polluted , but the water in the upper part of the river can be used for drinking water . nearby rivers . north along coast . - santa margarita river south along coast . - san diego river
