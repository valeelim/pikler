Gilan Province     guilan is a small province in iran . it covers about 1 % of the total area of iran ( 14711 kms ) . guilan has a population of 2,410,523 and the population density is 172 people per square kilometer . there are many cities in guilan , including rasht ( at its center ) , lahidjan , anzali , and rudsar . the caspian sea borders the north of guilan . foods . - rice - orange - roe castles . - bandebon castle - rudkhan castle
