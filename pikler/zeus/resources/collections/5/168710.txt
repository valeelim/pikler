Juglandaceae     the juglandaceae , also known as the walnut family , is a family of trees , or sometimes shrubs , in the order fagales . the leaves are pinnately compound , or ternate , and usually 20-100 cm long . there are eight genera in the family , including the commercially important nut-producing trees walnut ( juglans ) , pecan ( carya illinoinensis ) , and hickory ( carya ) .
