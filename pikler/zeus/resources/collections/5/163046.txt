Red algae     red algae are members of the phylum rhodophyta , a large group of aquatic algae with approximately 6000 species . the red algae are characterized by reddish phycobilin pigments—phycoerythrin and phycocyanin—that mask the color of the chlorophylls . most species grow near tropical and subtropical shores below the low-tide mark . a few are found in fresh water . red algae is also used to make nori .
