Doaba     doaba ( ) is the region of indian punjab surrounded by the rivers beas and sutlej . the name `` doaba '' literally translates to `` land of two rivers '' ( `` '' do '' two , `` ab '' '' river ; farsi ) . it is one of the most fertile regions of the world , and was the centre of the green revolution in india . to this day , it remains one of the largest per capita producers of wheat in the world . the people of doaba started out as agriculturalists , but have now diversified into education , sports and entrepreneurship .
