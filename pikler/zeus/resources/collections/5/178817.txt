Thai Airways     thai airways international ( การบินไทย ) is the national airline of thailand . `` thai airways international '' began in 1960. in 1988 , it was combine with `` thai airways company '' . this created the current thai airways . it joined star alliance in 1997. its headquarters are at 89 vibhavadi rangsit road , chatuchak , bangkok 10900. currently it has 82 aircraft . it goes to 78 cities all over the world , except africa and america .
