Pulp (band)    pulp ( band ) pulp are an english band from sheffield , south yorkshire . the lead singer , jarvis cocker , started the band in 1978. for a long time , cocker found life as a musician hard . however , in 1994 , he and his band became famous when their album called his `` his and hers '' became popular in the united kingdom . they had three more albums and a number of singles before the band split up in 2001 . 1994 - his 'n ' hers ( number 9 in the uk music charts ) 1995 - different class ( number 1 in the uk music charts ) 1996 - countdown 1992-1983 ( number 10 in the uk music charts ) 1998 - this is hardcore ( number 1 in the uk music charts ) 2001 - we love life ( number 6 in the uk music charts )
