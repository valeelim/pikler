Tottenham Hale station     tottenham hale , is a national rail and london underground victoria line station in tottenham , north london . it is on hale road near the tottenham hale one-way system . the station is in travelcard zone 3. the station opened on 15 september 1840 as tottenham . it was on the northern & eastern railway ( n & er ) line from stratford in east london to broxbourne in hertfordshire . it was renamed tottenham hale on 1 september 1968 when it became an interchange station with london underground on the opening of the first stage of the victoria line . other websites . - london 's transport museum photographic archive
