FreeOTFE     freeotfe is a free , open source , computer program used to automatically encrypt and decrypt information stored on a hard disk . freeotfe was originally written for microsoft windows and was later copied to windows mobile ( freeotfe4pda ) . it can encrypt information using a large number algorithms including aes ( aka rijndael ) , blowfish , cast6 , mars , rc6 , serpent and twofish . sarah dean is the developer of freeotfe . other pages . - encryption - pretty good privacy ( pgp ) - email encryption - putty - ssh encryption other websites . - official www site
