Classicism     the word classicism , in the arts , is used when talking about art from the ancient greeks and romans and their influence . the art of classicism often involves a thoughtful approach to the arts in which beauty and form is admired , rather than passion or any kind of exaggeration . classicism influenced several periods in european history , especially the italian renaissance , age of reason , the age of enlightenment and some movements in modernism . classicism can apply to painting , sculpture , architecture , music , dance , literature or philosophy . related pages . - neoclassicism - classical music - classical music period other websites . - , explains the difference between classicism and baroque
