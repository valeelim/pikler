Severus Snape     severus snape is a character from j.k. rowling 's fictional book series harry potter . snape is the potions master at hogwarts in the series . he is also known to be a death eater but after voldemort started hunting lily evans , the woman snape had loved since he was a child , he asked albus dumbledore to find a safe place for the potters because he knew voldemort wanted to kill their son , harry potter . as dumbledore was not able to save the potters , snape 's sorrow was real and extremely deep , he gained dumbledore 's trust . snape was loyal to dumbledore after this event . snape killed dumbledore in the sixth book on dumbledore 's request . voldemort believed snape killed him because voldemort had planned snape to do that . snape had let it slip in the third book that remus lupin was a werewolf . snape did n't kill himself after lily 's death only because of the green eyes he had loved that lily 's son had inherited from her . just before snape 's death in the seventh book snape asks harry to do the last thing for him .
