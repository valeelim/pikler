Paranthropus aethiopicus     paranthropus aethiopicus is an extinct species of hominid . the fossil was discovered in 1985 in lake turkana , kenya . knm wt 17000 is known as the `` black skull '' due to the dark colouration of the bone , caused by high levels of manganese . it is one of the earliest examples of robust australopithecines from the pliocene . the skull is dated to 2.5 million years ago , before the other forms of robust australopithecines .
