Duvet     a duvet is a type of bedding used as a blanket on a bed . they are normally made of a cover , filled with feathers or man made fibres such as polyester and hollow fibre . they are soft and comfortable and because of the material inside the bed cloth , it makes the user feel warm at night . the thickness of the duvet is measured by a tog rating . the higher the tog , the warmer the duvet will be . the warmest duvet would be a 13.5 tog . there is an all season duvet where you get two duvets with different togs , usually a 4.5 and a 9.5 .
