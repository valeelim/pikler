Teton Dam     teton dam was a dam on the teton river in idaho , in the usa . when it was built , it was long and tall , making it one of the largest dams in the united states . it is most known for collapsing during spring runoff on june 5 , 1976 a few months after it was completed . the resulting flood destroyed 10,000 acres of land and five cities . the dam was never rebuilt . although the u.s. government paid $ 100 million to build the dam , it had to pay $ 300 million more dollars to pay for flood damages .
