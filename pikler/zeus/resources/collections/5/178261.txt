Peaceful coexistence     peaceful coexistence was a theory developed by the soviet union during the cold war that said that capitalist states could 'accept ' each other . the idea of peaceful coexistence came from before the communist leader stalin ’ s death , as georgy malenkov called for it in 1952 but it was more clear after stalin ’ s death in 1953 where destalinization ( removing the personality and politics of stalin ) began .
