Secretary of State for Culture, Media and Sport    secretary of state for culture , media and sport the secretary of state for culture , media and sport is a uk cabinet position with responsibility for the department for culture , media and sport . the role was created in 1992 by john major as secretary of state for national heritage . it took on its current title on 14 july 1997. the first to fill the role , david mellor dubbed it `` minister for fun '' .
