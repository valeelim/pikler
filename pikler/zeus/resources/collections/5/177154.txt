Dol2day     dol2day ( `` democracy online today '' ) is a social networking platform for politically interested german speaking people launched on mai 15 , 2000. the site is a combination of internet forum and multi-user dungeon with - personal pages , - discussions , - virtual parties , - groups , - chat-function , - e-mail-function , - internet radio , - chats with politicians and other celebrities and - an election of an `` internet chancellor '' for a period of four months . other websites . - official website - dora community radio website
