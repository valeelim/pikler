San Diego Creek     san diego creek is a small northwest-flowing river in orange county , california . it is about long and its watershed covers 112.2 square miles ( 291 square kilometers ) in three cities . its source is in the coast range , but several of its tributaries begin in the santa ana mountains and flow west to the creek . san diego creek flows into upper newport bay , a branch of newport bay and eventually the pacific ocean . nearby rivers . north along coast . - santa ana river south along coast . - aliso creek
