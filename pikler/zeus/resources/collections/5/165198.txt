1914 Atlantic hurricane season     the 1914 atlantic hurricane season was the shortest and least active hurricane season on record . this season had only one storm with two other possible storms . this season was also one of two to not have a hurricane with the other being 1907. this system form as a tropical wave just to the east of florida on september 13. it became a tropical depression on the 14th , the storm made landfall in georgia with 70mph winds . it moved westward and dissipated over texas on the 19th .
