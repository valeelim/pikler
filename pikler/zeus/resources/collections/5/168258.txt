Puget Sound     puget sound is a sound in western washington . it is a large area of salt water that is partially cut off from the rest of the pacific ocean . many of washington 's cities , like seattle and tacoma are next to the sound . many boats travel on the sound . some of the boats are ferries which take people from the main part of washington to the islands in the sound . many boats come from across the ocean from places like japan , and china to trade in washington .
