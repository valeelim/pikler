Excitebike 64     excitebike 64 is a video game published by nintendo and developed by left field productions . it was released in north america on april 30 , 2000 for the nintendo 64. it is the series ' first 3d game . `` excitebike 64 '' is the sequel to the acclaimed nes game `` excitebike '' and is the second title in the series . it was later succeeded by the wii game `` excite truck '' . the game received an average 88 out of 100 from the review compilation site metacritic.. review scores : - gamespot : 8.8 - `` gamepro '' : 80 - `` electronic gaming monthly '' : 9.0 - ign : 9.7 - `` reviews on the run '' : 9/10
