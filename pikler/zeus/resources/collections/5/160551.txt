Paris Peace Treaties, 1947    paris peace treaties , 1947 the paris peace conference ( july 29 to october 15 , 1946 ) resulted in the paris peace treaties signed on february 10 , 1947. the victorious wartime allied powers ( principally the united states , united kingdom , france and the soviet union ) negotiated the details of treaties with italy , romania , hungary , bulgaria , and finland . the treaties allowed italy , romania , hungary , bulgaria , and finland to reassume their responsibilities as sovereign states in international affairs and to qualify for membership in the united nations .
