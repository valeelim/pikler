Lycée Thiers     the lycée thiers is a public secondary school in the city center of marseille and obtains the best results of the academy of aix-marseille . it is considered one of the most demanding in france . the law of may 1 , 1802 decided the creation of 45 secondary schools in france . the `` lycée de marseille '' was opened on december 22 , 1802. under napoleon iii , it was called `` lycée impérial '' . in 1930 , it took the name of `` lycée thiers .
