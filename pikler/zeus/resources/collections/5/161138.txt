Matt Smith (actor)    matt smith ( actor ) matthew robert smith ( born 28 october 1982 ) is an english stage and television actor . he is best known as the eleventh form of the doctor on the british television series `` doctor who '' . he got a bafta award nomination for the role in 2011. he wanted to be a professional footballer . a back injury made him stop playing the sport . he joined the national youth theatre and studied drama and creative writing at the university of east anglia . smith became an actor in 2003. he performed in plays like `` murder in the cathedral '' , `` fresh kills '' , `` the history boys '' and `` on the shore of the wide world '' in london theatres . on west end , he has performed in `` swimming with sharks '' with christian slater , and `` that face '' . smith 's first television role was in 2006. he played jim taylor in the bbc version of phillip pullman 's `` the ruby in the smoke '' and `` the shadow in the north '' . his his first major role in television came as danny in the 2007 bbc series `` party animals '' . smith is the youngest person to play the character in the british television series .
