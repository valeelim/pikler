Lohengrin     lohengrin is a character in some german stories of arthurian literature . he is the son of parzival ( percival ) , he is a knight of the holy grail who is sent in a boat pulled by swans to rescue a girl who is falsely accused of murder . he must never tell anyone his name his story is a version of the knight of the swan legend . it is particularly famous now as an opera , `` lohengrin '' by richard wagner ( 1849 ) .
