Shoot (professional wrestling)    shoot ( professional wrestling ) a shoot is something in professional wrestling that is completely real and is not scripted . in other words , broken kayfabe . a shoot rarely happens . on the scripted level , a worked shoot is a scripted segment that takes place in a show with elements of reality being exposed , such as an off-screen incident between wrestlers being used as fuel for an on-screen rivalry between them . it can also be a segment that fans are meant to believe is a shoot , but is not .
