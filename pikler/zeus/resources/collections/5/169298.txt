Chaiyaphum Province     chaiyaphum ( thai : ชัยภูมิ ) is one of the isan ( `` north-eastern '' ) provinces ( `` changwat '' ) of thailand . as of 2000 , 1 million people live there , and has an area of 12,778.3 km² . the governor is thawon phrommichai . geography . - chulabhorn dam the province has 16 districts ( amphoe ) ; 124 subdistricts ( tambon ) and 1393 villages ( muban ) .
