Strontium Dog     strontium dog is a science fiction comic strip that was first published in a british comic called starlord in 1978. the same year it was moved to a comic called 2000 ad . it is set in a future where a war ended long ago and it disfigured people . the main character is one of the disfigured mutants called johnny alpha . in the future where the story is set , there are many mutants , who are discriminated and in order to live , the mutants either must live on the streets , or become a bounty hunter . johnny alpha is a mutant bounty hunter who was given a special gift when he was disfigured . his eyes glow white , and they can let him see through walls , except lead , because radioactive waves can not pass through lead . they also let him control peoples minds , which helps him in some instances .
