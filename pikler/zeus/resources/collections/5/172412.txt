1974 Atlantic hurricane season     the 1974 atlantic hurricane season began on june 1 , 1974 and ended on november 30 , 1974. this season was about average in activity 11 storms , 7 of which got named . this season was one of the deadliest on record , most deaths were caused by hurricane fifi . this season also had the most subtropical storms ( 4 ) . storms . - subtropical storm one - subtropical storm two - subtropical storm three - tropical storm alma - hurricane becky - hurricane carmen - tropical storm dolly - tropical storm elaine - hurricane fifi - hurricane gertrude - subtropical storm four in 1975 the names carmen and fifi were retired . other websites . - hpc rainfall page for 1974 tropical cyclones - monthly weather review
