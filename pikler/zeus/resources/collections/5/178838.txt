Christ myth theory     the christ myth theory ( also called the jesus myth theory , jesus myth , and christ myth ) is the idea that the person named jesus in the bible 's gospels is a myth . however , there is a big difference between what the words `` myth , '' `` historical , '' and `` fiction '' mean . because of this , there are many different opinions about `` how '' the jesus in the gospels is a myth . other websites . sites promoting some variant of the theory . - `` the jesus puzzle : was there no historical jesus ?
