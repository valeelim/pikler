Stade du 4-Août     stade du 4-août is a stadium in ouagadougou , burkina faso . it is used mostly for football games and it also has an athletics track . the stadium can hold of 35,000 people . etoile filante ouagadougou play their homes games at the stadium . other websites . - photo at worldstadiums.com - photos at fussballtempel.net - stadium at openstreetmap
