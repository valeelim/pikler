New York City Housing Authority Police Department     the new york city housing authority police department was a police unit in new york city that existed from 1952 to 1995. the housing police , as well as new york city transit police , became part of the new york city police department in 1995 by order of new york city mayor rudolph w. giuliani and is now called the housing bureau . other pages . - new york city transit police - color of the day ( police ) other websites . - new york city police department other websites . - nypd homepage
