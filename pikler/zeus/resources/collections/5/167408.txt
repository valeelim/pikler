Abitur     the abitur ( from latin `` abire '' = go away ) is a final exam in germany , finland and the czech republic at the end for the secondary education . the abitur takes usually 12 or 13 years . the official term in germany for this certificate is `` allgemeine hochschulreife '' . the `` abiturzeugnis '' get pupils when they passed the final exams . it formally enables the possibility to attend a university .
