Israel Philharmonic Orchestra     the israel philharmonic orchestra in tel aviv is the most famous orchestra in israel . it is usually called the israel philharmonic orchestra , or abbreviated ipo . in hebrew it is written : , pronounced : `` ha-tizmoret ha-filharmonit ha-yisre'elit '' . it was started in 1936 by the violinist bronis≈Çaw huberman when it was known as the palestine orchestra . at this time the nazis were in power in germany and jews were being persecuted . many european orchestras were getting rid of their jewish musicians . the orchestra gave their first concert in tel aviv on 26 december 1936 , and was conducted by arturo toscanini . the orchestra tours to many parts of the world . in 1971 they visited germany . some of the world 's greatest conductors have conducted the orchestra . leonard bernstein and zubin mehta in particular have had a close relationship with them . they did not have a main conductor until 1977 when mehta was made music director . the ipo have made many recordings with mehta , and also with bernstein .
