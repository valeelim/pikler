Norfolk House     norfolk house , at 31 st james 's square , london , was built in 1722 for the duke of norfolk . it was a royal residence for a short time only , when frederick , prince of wales , father of king george iii of the united kingdom , lived there 1737-1741 , after his marriage in 1736 to princess augusta of saxe-gotha , daughter of frederick ii , duke of saxe-gotha . king george iii was born in the house , which was offered to the royal couple by the 9th duke of norfolk .
