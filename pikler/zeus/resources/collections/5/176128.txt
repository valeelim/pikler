Twelvers     twelvers , or twelver shia 's ( arabic : اثنا عشرية ‎شيعة ) are shia muslims that believe that god appointed twelve guides after prophet muhammad . these are : - ali ibn abi talib ( ameerul mu'mineen ) - hasan ibn ali ( al-mujtabaa ) - husayn ibn ali ( ash-shaheed ) - ali ibn husayn ( zaynul aabideen ) - muhammad al-baaqir - ja'far as-saadiq - musa al-kaadhim - ali ar-ridha - muhammad at-taqi , al-jawaad - ali al-haadi , an-naqi - hasan al-askari - muhammad al-mahdi about 85 % of shia muslims are twelvers . most of them can be found in iran ( 90 % ) , iraq ( 65 % ) , azerbaijan ( 85 % ) , lebanon ( 35 % ) , kuwait ( 35 % ) , saudi arabia ( 10-15 % ) , and bahrain ( 80 % ) . there are large minorities in pakistan ( 20 % ) and afghanistan ( 18 % ) .
