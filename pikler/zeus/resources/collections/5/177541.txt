US Airways Flight 1549     us airways flight 1549 was a commercial airplane flight that landed in the hudson river because a flock of birds hit its engines . the plane took off from la guardia in new york . the event was remarkable because no one died . flight 1549 is also known as the miracle in the hudson . the aircraft had a pilot named sully sulleburger . in 2016 a film was made recreating the crash . the film is named `` sully '' .
