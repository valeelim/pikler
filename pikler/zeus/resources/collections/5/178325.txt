Laylat al-Qadr     laylat al-qadr ( ) ( also known as shab-e-qadr ) , basically the night of decree or night of measures , is the anniversary of two very important dates in islam . they occurred in the month of ramadan . it is the anniversary of the night muslims believe the first verses of the qur'an were revealed to the islamic prophet muhammad . other websites . - an old book from 1683 that talks about the night of destiny
