Sika deer     the sika deer ( `` cervus nippon '' ) is a member of the deer family cervidae . it lives in east asia . it is found in mixed deciduous forests to the north , and mixed subtropical deciduous and evergreen forests to the south . sika deer are native to taiwan to japan , but they have been introduced to many parks in europe . in the uk , there are large populations in ireland and scotland and there are patchy populations scattered across england .
