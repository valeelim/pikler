Santa Ana River     the santa ana river is a major river in southern california , in the united states . it begins in san bernardino county and flows west about to the pacific ocean , through riverside and orange counties . it has a watershed of 2,650 square miles ( 6,863 square kilometers ) . its biggest tributaries include lytle , temescal , and santiago creeks . like the los angeles and san gabriel rivers to the north , it is lined with concrete in many places . a notable feature on the river is the seven oaks dam , which , at tall , is the 6th tallest dam in the united states . nearby rivers . north along coast . - san gabriel river south along coast . - san diego creek references . - santa ana river watershed - oc watershed
