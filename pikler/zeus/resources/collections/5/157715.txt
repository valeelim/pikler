Dorsal     in anatomy , the dorsal is the upper side of animals that can run , fly , or swim in a forwards and backwards direction , and the back side of animals ( like humans ) that walk upright . in vertebrates the dorsum contains the backbone . the term dorsal means the parts that are either located toward or grow off that side of an animal . the opposite side of the animal is described with the terms ventrum and ventral . in humans , the top of the foot is the dorsal part . examples of the use of `` dorsal '' are the dorsal fins of fish .
