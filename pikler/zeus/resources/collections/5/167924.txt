Laurel     the lauraceae or laurel family is a group of flowering plants in the order laurales . it includes the bay laurel and its closest relatives . the family has over 3000 species of flowering plants in over 50 genera worldwide . they mostly live in warm temperate and tropical regions , especially southeast asia and south america . most are aromatic evergreen trees or shrubs , but one or two genera such as `` sassafras '' are deciduous , and `` cassytha '' is a genus of parasitic vines .
