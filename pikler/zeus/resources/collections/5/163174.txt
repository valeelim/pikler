Jade Ewen     jade ewen ( born 24 january 1988 ) is an english singer and actress . ewen was born in plaistow , newham , london to a jamaican mother and scottish-sicilian father . she started her career when she got the role as nala in the west end production of `` the lion king '' , she has then been seen in many tv-series in both the united kingdom and australia . in 2005 she joined r & b group trinity stone which reached moderate success in the uk , ireland and russia . in early 2009 she was selected by andrew lloyd webber to participate in the uk national selection for eurovision song contest 2009 in moscow , russia . a selection she won and represented the country at the eurovision that same year placing fifth . since september 2009 , ewen is a member of the girl group sugababes . filmography . - the bill - casualty - out there - mr harvey lights candles - myths
