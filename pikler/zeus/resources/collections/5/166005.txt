Black Light Burns     black light burns is an industrial rock band with wes borland ( formerly of limp bizkit , big dumb face , eat the day ) as the front man . their first album , `` cruel melody '' , was released on june 5 , 2007. the band released a covers album+dvd package in summer of 2008 titled `` cover your heart and the anvil pants odyssey '' , and is currently working on its second album . band members . - wes borland - lead vocals , guitar , bass guitar , keyboard , programming , production ( 2005 - present ) - nick annis - guitar ( 2007 - present ) - dennis sanders - bass guitar ( 2008 - present ) - marshall kilpatric - drums , percussion ( 2007 - present ) discography . studio albums . - 2007 : `` cruel melody '' compilation albums . - 2008 : `` cover your heart and the anvil pants odyssey '' singles . - 2007 : `` lie '' - 2007 : `` 4 walls '' other websites . - black light burns official myspace - black light burns article for billboard.com
