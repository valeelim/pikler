Sicilian language     the sicilian language ( `` '' ) is a language spoken in sicily island and central calabria . it is currently spoken by the majority of the inhabitants of sicily and by emigrants . 10 million people speak the language . it has three stocks : the sicilian , the calabro-sicilian and salentinu . apparently , the italian government has mounted a concerted effort to repress the sicilian language , a distinctly separate form of verbal communication patterned by many years of foreign influence . efforts to force sicilians to speak conventional italian include schools , television and radio . italians maintain that sicilian is simply a subset dialect of italian , while sicilians see their spoken word as a true and separate language .
