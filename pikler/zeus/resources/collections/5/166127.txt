Australopithecus bahrelghazali     australopithecus bahrelghazali is a fossil australopithecine which was discovered in 1993 by the paleontologist michel brunet . brunet found the fossil in the bahr el ghazal valley near koro toro , in chad , in africa and named it abel . abel was dated using beryllium-based radiometric dating as living about 3.6 million years ago .
