You Nazty Spy!    you nazty spy ! you nazty spy ! is the 44th short movie by the three stooges . it was the first hollywood movie to make fun of adolf hitler . in the movie , germany is replaced by the pretend country of moronica and the hitler-type character is called moe hailstone , and is played by stooge moe howard . this made howard the first us actor to play hitler . it was directed by jules white .
