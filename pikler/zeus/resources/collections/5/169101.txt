Massively multiplayer online game     massively multiplayer online games ( mmogs ) are video games where players play together over the internet . these games are different from other online video games because they have very many players all playing with each other at the same time . other online video games have fewer players playing with each other at the same time . the most popular kind of mmog is the massively multiplayer online role-playing game ( mmorpg ) . examples are `` runescape '' , `` world of warcraft '' ( wow ) and `` roblox '' .
