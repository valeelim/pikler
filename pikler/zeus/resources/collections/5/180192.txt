Pitchfork Media     pitchfork media is a daily music webzine . it is based in chicago , united states . its focus is on independent music , especially indie rock . pitchfork was made in 1995. it talks about new music , but journalists there also review albums that have been released again . each year pitchfork chooses an album of the year . since 2003 they have chose a track of the year each year too .
