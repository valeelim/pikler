7066 Nessus     7066 nessus is a centaur ( a type of icy planetoid ) that was found by david l. rabinowitz , working with spacewatch , at kitt peak on 26 april , 1993. it was the second centaur found by him ( 5145 pholus being his first ) , and the third centaur to be found ( 2060 chiron was the first ) . it was officially announced on may 13 , 1993 in iauc 5789 with designation . nessus finishes one orbit around the sun in 122.4 years , an eccentricity of 0.52 and an inclination to the ecliptic of 15.6 degrees . at perihelion ( closest approach to the sun ) , it moves closer to the sun than uranus , while at aphelion ( farthest approach to the sun ) it goes even farther thanneptune . the orbits of centaurs change from time to time because of interactions with the giant planets . nessus is thought to have a fairly long orbital half-life of about 4.9 myr . other websites . - orbital simulation from jpl ( java ) / ephemeris - spacewatch - asteroid/comet connection - the centaur research project - an account of the naming
