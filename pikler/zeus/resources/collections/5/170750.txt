Typographical error     a typographical error or typo is a word that has been spelt wrong by accident . there are a few ways to make a typo : 1. hitting the wrong key while typing . example : `` tom amd jane are friends . '' 1. pressing the keys in the wrong order . example : `` tom nad jane are friends . '' 1. forgetting to press a key . example `` tom nd jane are friends . '' 1. holding a key too long , causing a letting to be typed twice . example `` tom andd jane are friends . ''
