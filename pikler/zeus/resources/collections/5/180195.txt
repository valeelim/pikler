Paste (magazine)    paste ( magazine ) paste is a monthly music and entertainment magazine . it is published in the united states . in 2005 , `` paste '' was listed at # 21 on the chicago tribune 's list of `` 50 best magazines '' . `` paste '' appeared on that newspaper 's list again in 2007 . `` paste '' was also named `` magazine of the year '' by the plug independent music awards in 2006 , 2007 and 2008. in 2008 , `` paste '' was nominated for a national magazine award in the category of general .
