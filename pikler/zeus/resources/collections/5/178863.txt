Hibernate (OS feature)    hibernate ( os feature ) hibernation is a technology used in certain operating systems . it allows to save the contents of the system memory onto a hard disk or other permanent storage . the computer can then be powered off . when it is powered up again , this information can be read again . that way the system will start up much faster . the amount of free space on the hard disk needs to be greater than the amount of ram in order for hibernation to work .
