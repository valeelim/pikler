Ultratop     ultratop is the name of the official belgian record charts . due to the two different regions ( flanders and wallonia ) there are two different record charts . in flanders the most important charts are the `` ultratop 50 singles '' and the `` ultratop 200 albums '' . in wallonia the most important charts are the `` ultratop 40 singles '' and the `` ultratop 200 albums '' . the rankings in these charts are based on record sales .
