Tributary state     a tributary state is a state that is subordinate to a more powerful neighbour . it was a form of subordination in pre-modern times . the tributary sent a regular token of submission to the superior power . this was a tribute , a substantial gift of wealth , such as the gold , produce , or slaves . the tribute might best be seen as the payment of protection money . but there were other ways to show submission as well . for modern forms of state subordination , see puppet state , satellite state and client state .
