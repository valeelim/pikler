Beacon, New York    beacon , new york beacon is a city that is found in dutchess county , new york , in the united states . the 2000 united states census says that the city is home to 13,808 people . beacon is part of the poughkeepsie–newburgh–middletown , ny metropolitan statistical area . beacon was named in memory of fires that burned from the summit of the fishkill mountains to warn the continental army about british troop .
