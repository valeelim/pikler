Skydiving     skydiving is kind of a sport involving a skydiver to jump down from an airplane while it is flying and parachuting to the ground . skydiving can be done individually and with lots of people . training is required to do it alone . the jump can also be made from a helicopter or the bottom of a hot air balloon or a plane from 4000m or 13,000 ft. skydiving can be a very exciting sport . related pages . - base jumping - hang gliding
