Ilya Mechnikov     ilya ilyich mechnikov ( 16 may 1845 â€“ 16 july 1916 ) was a russian zoologist . he won the 1908 nobel prize in physiology or medicine , with paul ehrlich , for finding out how immunity fights disease . mechnikov was a protozoologist who became better known for the discovery of macrophages in 1882. he discovered how they dealt with germs by phagocytosis . macrophages are found in virtually all tissues , and patrol for potential pathogens by amoeboid movement . he is also credited with coining the term gerontology in 1903 , for the emerging study of ageing and longevity .
