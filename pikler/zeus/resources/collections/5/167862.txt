Royal Rumble (2008)    royal rumble ( 2008 ) royal rumble ( 2008 ) was a wrestling ppv that took place in new york city , new york at madison square garden . it was the 21 royal rumble event . the event had the twenty-first yearly royal rumble match as the main event , which john cena won . one of the matches was randy orton defending his wwe championship against jeff hardy . another was , edge defending his world heavyweight championship against rey mysterio . another match was , john `` bradshaw '' layfield wrestling against chris jericho . the last match was ric flair wrestling against m.v.p .
