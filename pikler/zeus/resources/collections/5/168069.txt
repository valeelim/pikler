Palm Sunday     palm sunday is a christian festival day . it is always happens on a sunday but the date each year changes . it is a day to celebrate the day that jesus entered the city of jerusalem in israel as was written about in the bible in mark 11:1-11 , matthew 21:1-11 , luke 19:28-44 , and in john 12:12-19. during palm sunday many christians celebrate with crosses made out of palm leaves and by going to church . related pages . - roman catholicism - eastern orthodox other websites . - bbc - palm sunday - christian calendar for 2009
