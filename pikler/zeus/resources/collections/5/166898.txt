Alismatales     alismatales is an order of flowering plants with 14 families . they are monocots which live in marshy and aquatic ( water ) habitats . classification . - order alismatales - family alismataceae ( `` including '' limnocharitaceae ) - family aponogetonaceae - family araceae - family butomaceae - family cymodoceaceae - family hydrocharitaceae - family juncaginaceae - family posidoniaceae - family potamogetonaceae - family ruppiaceae - family scheuchzeriaceae - family tofieldiaceae - family zosteraceae
