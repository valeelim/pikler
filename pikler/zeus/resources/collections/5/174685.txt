Javan hawk-eagle     the javan hawk-eagle , `` nizaetus bartelsi '' is a medium-sized ( about 61 cm long ) dark brown raptor in the family accipitridae . the javan hawk-eagle is the national bird of indonesia . the javan hawk-eagle is believed to be monogamous . the female usually lays one egg in a nest high on top of a forest tree . the diet consists mainly of birds , lizards , fruit bats and mammals .
