Trapdoor spider     trapdoor spiders are members of the spider family ctenizidae . they dig a small burrow in the forest floor , and covers it with a dirt trapdoor.the spider lurks in its burrow with the door half-open , until something to eat walks by without knowing that the spider is watching . then the trapdoor spider pounces , drags the prey into its trap , and slams the trapdoor shut .
