Tariff     a tariff is a tax charged on goods as they pass between one country and another . a tariff can be placed on goods being brought into the country ( imports ) , and goods being exported from the country to another . it is usually done to make money for the government . it may also be done for protectionism . protectionism makes it easier for local products to sell by making products from foreign countries more expensive . often , one government will have an agreement with another not to place any tariffs on goods that are traded between them . this kind of agreement is called free trade . related pages . - customs - indirect tax - international trade
