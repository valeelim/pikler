Wind farm     a wind farm is a group of wind turbines in the same area . these turbines turn moving air into electricity . a large wind farm has several hundred wind turbines , and covers an area of hundreds of square miles . a wind farm may also be offshore . many of the biggest onshore wind farms are in the usa . the roscoe wind farm is the largest onshore wind farm in the world , producing 781.5 mw of power , followed by the horse hollow wind energy center ( 735.5 mw ) . the thanet offshore wind project in united kingdom is the largest offshore wind farm in the world at 300 mw , followed by horns rev ii ( 209 mw ) in denmark .
