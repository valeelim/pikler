Dianetics     dianetics is a practice created by science fiction writer l. ron hubbard . it is about the way people think . hubbard also started the religion of scientology , which uses dianetics . the photo at the right shows a person learning about dianetics by doing a stress test . the idea of the stress test is to show people something in their own minds . most psychologists think that dianetics is a pseudoscience . that means that it is not really a science , and it only looks like one .
