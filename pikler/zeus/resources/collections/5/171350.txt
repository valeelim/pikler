Sandringham House     sandringham house is in norfolk , england . it has been in the british royal family since albert , prince consort bought it for edward vii of the united kingdom . like balmoral , it is the private property of the monarch of the united kingdom , not an official , government owned , building . this meant that when edward viii abdicated in 1936 the new king , george vi , had to buy the properties from his brother . york cottage is in the grounds of sandringham estate .
