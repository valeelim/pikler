Na Mee     na mee ( korean : 나미 ) is a south korean singer . she was born in 1957. in the 1980 's she became famous because of songs like `` bingeulbingeul '' and `` like an indian doll '' . in 2007 she made a comeback with the special single `` if i could '' . references . - ( in korean ) segye ilbo , 2007.01.04
