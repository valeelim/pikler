Pecan     the pecan ( `` carya illinoinensis '' ) is a species of hickory nut , native to south-central north america , in the united states from southern iowa , illinois and indiana east to western kentucky , north carolina and western tennessee , south through georgia , alabama , mississippi , louisiana , texas , oklahoma and arkansas ; and in mexico from coahuila south to jalisco and veracruz . `` pecan '' is from an algonquian word , meaning a nut requiring a stone to crack . pecans are often used in cooking in the southern united states ; a common dessert there is pecan pie .
