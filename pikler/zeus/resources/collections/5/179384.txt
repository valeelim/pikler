Austin City Limits Music Festival     the austin city limits music festival is an annual three-day music and art festival in austin , texas ' zilker park . the festival brings together more than 130 bands on eight stages , including rock , country , folk , indie , americana , hip-hop , reggae , and bluegrass , and attracts a crowd of about 65,000 visitors each day . named after the legendary pbs concert series , the festival is produced by c3 presents , who also co-produce the lollapalooza and big state festivals .
