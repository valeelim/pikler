Little Sioux, Iowa    little sioux , iowa little sioux is a city of iowa in the united states . the lewis and clark expedition went through the area in 1804 , and named it little sioux river . the first european settlers arrived in the 1850s . the first post office in little sioux opened in july , 1857. this was the same year that the first school opened . a two storey brick school was in use by 1885. in 2008 , a boy scout camp near little sioux was hit by a tornado . four boys were killed , and another 48 were injured . other websites . - a list of students at little sioux school in 1885
