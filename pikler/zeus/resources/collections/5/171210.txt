Lesotho national football team     lesotho national football team is the national football team of lesotho . they are nicknamed the `` likuena ( crocodiles ) '' . the country has never qualified for the final stages of the world cup or the africa cup of nations . world cup record . - 1930 to 1970 - `` did not enter '' - 1974 - `` did not qualify '' - 1978 - `` did not enter '' - 1982 - `` did not qualify '' - 1986 to 1990 - `` withdrew '' - 1994 to 1998 - `` did not enter '' - 2002 to 2010 - `` did not qualify '' african nations cup record . - 1957 to 1972 - `` did not enter '' - 1974 - `` did not qualify '' - 1976 - `` withdrew '' - 1978 - `` did not enter '' - 1980 to 1982 - `` did not qualify '' - 1984 - `` withdrew '' - 1986 - `` did not enter '' - 1988 - `` withdrew '' - 1990 to 1992 - `` did not enter '' - 1994 - `` did not qualify '' - 1996 - `` withdrew during qualifying '' - 1998 - `` banned for withdrawing in 1996 '' - 2000 to 2010 - `` did not qualify '' - 2012 - `` did not enter '' - 2013 - `` did not qualify ''
