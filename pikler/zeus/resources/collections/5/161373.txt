Cosmopolitan     cosmopolitan is a word considered first made by diogenes of sinope and is made of two parts , `` cosmos '' and `` politics '' . it can be used for : - a place or person that is proud of its multicultural heritage - world citizens think that all the people of the world should have and follow the same ideals or international law , especially on human rights and that the idea of nation-states will be therefore outdated , before international organizations . - `` cosmopolitan ( magazine ) '' , an american women 's magazine .
