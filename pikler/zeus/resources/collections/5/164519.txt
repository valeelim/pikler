St John Passion     the st john passion ( ) is a musical composition by johann sebastian bach . the words tell the story of jesus ’ s death on the cross as told in the book of st john in the bible . the work is for a choir , solo voices and orchestra . the `` st. john passion '' was first performed in 1724 in the st nicholas church , leipzig . the words are from three sources : the bible ( telling the story ) , by two poets from hamburg , mostly by barthold heinrich brockes , and from chorales which were well-known to german church goers at the time . there were other composers who also wrote works called `` st john passion '' , the most famous being heinrich schütz . however , bach ’ s `` st john passion `` is thought by many musicians to be one of the greatest choral works ever written . two other very great choral works by bach are his `` st matthew passion `` and `` mass in b minor `` .
