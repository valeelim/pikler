Gabriel Agbonlahor     gabriel agbonlahor ( born 13 october 1986 ) is an english footballer . he plays for aston villa . he is of nigerian and scottish descent . gabriel agbonlahor 's professional football career started in 2005. he played his first game for watford on loan from aston villa . this means that he was still an aston villa player , but he was allowed to play for watford to get experience . he then played for sheffield wednesday , again on loan . he played his first english premier league game in 2006 for aston villa . gabriel agbonlahor played 16 games for the england under 21 team . he has also played for the england national football team .
