Storm drain     a storm drain , storm sewer ( u.s. ) , stormwater drain ( australia and new zealand ) or surface water system ( uk ) is designed to drain extra rain and ground water from paved streets , parking lots , sidewalks , and roofs . storm drains vary in design from small residential dry wells to large municipal systems . they are fed by street gutters on most motorways , freeways and other busy roads , as well as towns in areas which experience heavy rainfall , flooding and coastal towns which experience regular storms .
