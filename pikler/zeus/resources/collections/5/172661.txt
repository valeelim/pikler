Fritz Sauckel     ernst friedrich christoph `` fritz '' sauckel ( 27 october 1894 – 16 october 1946 ) was a nazi war criminal . he organized the systematic enslavement of millions from lands occupied by nazi germany . he was general plenipotentiary for labour deployment from 1942 until the end of the war . he was tried and executed . sauckel was also honorary obergruppenführer of the sa .
