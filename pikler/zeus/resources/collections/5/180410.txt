Luís Mesquita de Oliveira     luís mesquita de oliveira ( born 29 march , 1911 ) is a former brazilian football player . he has played for brazil national team.he was son of ernesto luiz de oliveira and cecilia mesquita de oliveira , and was the fifth of seven brothers and one sister . one brother , carlos , was football player , but for short time , because become a doctor . luiz mesquita de oliveira was a lawyer for many years for the são paulo government
