Lucas (movie)    lucas ( movie ) lucas is a 1986 american romantic comedy-drama movie directed by david seltzer , it was released on march 28 , 1986. the movie was a geared toward a teen audience . this movie ranked number 16 on entertainment weekly 's list of the 50 best high school movies . the main character of the movie `` lucas '' is a shy and quiet , fourteen year old nerd , who is in love with a girl . this leads to heartbreak when his older friend and the girl fall in love .
