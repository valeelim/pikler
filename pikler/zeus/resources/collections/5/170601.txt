Lymph node     a lymph node ( ) is an organ consisting of many types of cells , and is a part of the lymphatic system . lymph nodes are found all through the body , and remove foreign ( `` not of the body '' ) objects from the body . lymph nodes contain white blood cells . they are important for the correct use of the immune system . in the human body , the most likely place where lymph nodes can be felt are the neck , underarms , and groin area .
