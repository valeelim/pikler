Dr. Mario (video game)    dr. mario ( video game ) the gameplay of `` dr. mario '' is to make sure vitamins of the same color are lined-up together . there are three colors of vitamins ( red , blue , yellow ) . the goal is to clear all the capsules without going to the top . the player can pick any default level from 0-20 , with a set number of viruses to clear depending what level the player is on . the virus count goes as high as 84 , and does not increase after you pass level 20. there are over 40 levels in the game . the player can also set up the skill level and music ( fever , chill ) . `` dr. mario '' was rated the 134th best game made on a nintendo system in nintendo power 's top 200 games list . `` dr. mario '' was also rated by screwattack as the seventh best `` mario '' game of all time . overall , dr. mario has scored a press average of 7.6 on gamestats .
