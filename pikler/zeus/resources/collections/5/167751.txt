German National People's Party    german national people 's party the german national people 's party ( german : `` deutschnationale volkspartei '' and short : `` dnvp '' ) was national-conservative party of the time of the weimar republic and the third reich . this party was founded in 1918 , after world war i. in june 1933 , the dnvp merged with the nsdap . chairman . - 1918 to 1924 oskar hergt ( 1869-1967 ) - 1924 to 1928 kuno graf von westarp ( 1864-1945 ) - 1928 to 1933 alfred hugenberg ( 1865-1951 )
