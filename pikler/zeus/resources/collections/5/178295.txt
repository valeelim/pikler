Iftar     iftar ( ) , refers to the evening meal for breaking ( fasting ) during the islamic month of ramadan . muslims fast in the holy month of ramadan . at the time of maghrib they eat and drink . dua for iftar is اللَّهُمَّ اِنِّى لَكَ صُمْتُ وَبِكَ امنْتُ [ وَعَلَيْكَ تَوَكَّلْتُ ] وَعَلَى رِزْقِكَ اَفْطَرْتُ.o allah ! i fasted for you and i believe in you [ and i put my trust in you ] ...
