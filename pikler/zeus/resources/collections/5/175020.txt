Predestination (Calvinism)    predestination ( calvinism ) predestination is a central belief in calvinism . calvinists believe that god picked those who he will save and bring with him to heaven before the world was created . they also believe that people who are not saved by god will go to hell . john calvin thought people who were saved could never lose their salvation and the `` elected '' ( those god saved ) would know they were saved because of their actions . related pages . - predestination
