Hjalmar Schacht     hjalmar horace greeley schacht ( 22 january 1877 in tingleff – 3 june 1970 in münchen ) was a german politician and minister for economics during the time of the third reich . in the weimar republic he was the president of the reichsbank . he was also one of the 24 defendants at the nuremberg trials . his middle names come from the american politician horace greeley .
