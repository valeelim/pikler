Whitehall, Wisconsin    whitehall , wisconsin whitehall is a city of trempealeau county in the state of wisconsin , united states . it can be found along the trempealeau river . about 1,650 people were living in whitehall as of 2000. the cities of arcadia , independence , and blair can be found nearby whitehall . it is the county seat of trempealeau county . other websites . - whitehall chamber of commerce
