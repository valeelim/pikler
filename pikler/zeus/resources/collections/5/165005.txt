Purple mangosteen     purple mangosteen is a tropical fruit which comes from the rainforests of south east asia . it grows on evergreen trees . it is one of the most popular tropical fruits . it is popular because it has such a sweet , juicy , acidic taste . it has a hard purple shell . inside the shell , there are white segments , just like an orange . it has to be grown in temperatures of above 4 °c ( 40 °f ) . if it is grown in temperatures below that , the plants die .
