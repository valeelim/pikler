Boulogne-sur-Mer     boulogne-sur-mer is a commune . it is found in the region nord-pas-de-calais in the pas-de-calais department in the north of france . boulogne-sur-mer set its record high temperature ( 35.4°c or 95.7°f ) in july 2015 surpassing their previous record from aug. 11 , 2003 of 34.8°c .
