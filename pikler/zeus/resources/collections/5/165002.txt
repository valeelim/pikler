Thammasat University     thammasat university ( tu ) is the second oldest university in thailand . it was established on june 27 , 1934. the university was initially named , by dr. pridi banomyong , ‘ vichar thammasat lae karn muang ’ – ‘ the university of moral science and politics ’ . at that time , there was only chulalongkorn university in thailand , so he thought that the university should have people who have knowledge of law , administration and social . it had started out as an open university , with 7,094 people for the first academic year . the main goal of the university ’ s foundation was `` to teach students to love and cherish democracy '' . only in the year 1952 was the name ‘ vichar thammasat lae karn muang ’ changed to the present day ‘ thammasat university ’ . other websites . - thammasat university
