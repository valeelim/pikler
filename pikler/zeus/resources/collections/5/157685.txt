Huntsman spider     huntsman spider is a name given to the family sparassidae ( formerly `` heteropodidae '' ) . they are also known as the giant crab spiders , due to the way they look . larger ones , are also sometimes referred to as wood spiders , because they live in woody places ( forest , mine shafts , woodpiles ) . they are found in australia , new zealand , south africa , southeast asia , the mediterranean , florida , and hawaii , and possibly in many other tropical and semi-tropical regions . they can be found as far north as england , sweden and wales . huntsman spiders do not build webs , but hunt for food . they eat insects and other invertebrates . the average huntsman spider is about 1 inch long with a leg span of up to 5 inches . the giant huntsman spider , however , has a leg span of up to 12 inches , making it the largest spider by diameter ; it is often described as being `` the size of a dinner plate .
