University of Pittsburgh     the university of pittsburgh is a public university in the city of pittsburgh in the united states of america . it is also called `` pitt '' . pitt was started in 1787. pitt has 27,562 students . pitt is famous for philosophy , studying other countries , and its hospital called the `` university of pittsburgh medical center '' ( upmc ) . pitt does a lot of research . pitt is also famous for its tall building named the `` cathedral of learning '' . pitt is also famous for sports . pitt 's best sports are american football and basketball . other websites . - pitt 's webpage - pitt sports
