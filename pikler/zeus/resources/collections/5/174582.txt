Manager (baseball)    manager ( baseball ) a manager is the head coach of a baseball team . his job is to tell the players on the team what to do and make decisions about how the team should play , like who will play in what position . in the past , managers were usually players . since they were players , these managers wore the same uniform as the rest of the team . managers who were not players , such as connie mack , would not be dressed in uniform . today , managers dress in the same uniform as the team , even though most managers today are not players . managers in major league baseball and for many other teams as well are usually former players themselves . their playing experience may give them more knowledge on the game that they can use to help their team .
