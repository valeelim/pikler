Democratic Party (Serbia)    democratic party ( serbia ) the democratic party ( , `` demokratska stranka '' , ) is the main center-left political party in serbia . it claims continuity of the historical democratic party . it is the biggest political party in serbia in terms of sitting members of parliament , and in what respects the international arena , the democratic party is a member of the socialist international and party of european socialists . both current president of serbia boris tadić and current prime minister mirko cvetković are members of and also members of party government of the democratic party .
