Howitzer     a howitzer is a special type of big gun used by an army . it has a short barrel , and is used fire a shell over a short distance . the barrel length of a howitzer is 15 to 25 times of the caliber . howitzers are special because they 're made to fire higher than a cannon but lower than a mortar . howitzers are almost always used to shoot at targets on the ground , but they can be used for targets on water , or in the air . the name `` howitzer '' comes from an old czech word , `` haufnice '' , which was a catapult that could fire many stones . related pages . - list of howitzers
