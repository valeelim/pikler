Geoff Capes     geoff capes ( born 23 august 1949 ) is an english shotput champion , two times world 's strongest man and a champion budgerigar breeder ( now president of the british budgerigar breeding association . he has a beard . he was born in 1949 in holbeach , lincolnshire . he is 6 foot 6 inches tall , which is unusually tall . he weighed 139 kg or 306 stone . he used to be a policeman but retired when he became a shotputter in the 1970s . his best throw was in 1980 and was 20.5 metres . this record stood until 2003 , over twenty years . capes once advertised caravans in south yorkshire .
