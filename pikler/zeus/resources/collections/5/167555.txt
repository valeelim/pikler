28978 Ixion     28978 ixion is a kuiper belt object that was found on may 22 , 2001. ixion is a plutino ( an object that has a 2:3 orbital resonance with neptune ) and could be a dwarf planet . astronomers think that it has a diameter of about 800 km , which makes it the third biggest plutino . it is named after ixion , a figure from greek mythology . before it was named ixion , it had the provisional designation .
