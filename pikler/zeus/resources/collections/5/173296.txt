Sweet potato     the sweet potato ( `` ipomoea batatas '' ) is a plant that is a member of the morning glory family , grown for its sweet , edible , tuberous roots . sweet potatoes contain a lot of fiber and beta-carotene . they are eaten around the world . in peru , the use of sweet potatoes dates to 8000 bc . sweet potatoes were introduced a food crop in japan in 1735 and in korea in 1764 .
