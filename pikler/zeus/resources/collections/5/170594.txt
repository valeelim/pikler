Froot Loops     froot loops is a many colored , fruit flavored breakfast cereal made by the cereal maker kellogg 's . it is sold worldwide . when it started in 1963 , the cereal had red , orange , and yellow loops . in the 1990s , blue , green , and purple loops were added to the cereal . the cereal 's mascot is a toucan called `` toucan sam '' . froot loops website
