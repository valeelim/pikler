Boro-ichi     the boro-ichi is a market in setagaya , tokyo , japan . there is also a traditional boro-ichi festival or `` matsuri '' which happens during the market . the market started in 1578. these days the market happens every year on january 15 and 16 , and on december 15 and 16. there are over 700 stalls . more than 200,000 people come to the market . most of the things people sell in the market are old and cheap . they sell things like antiques , books , plants and food . other websites . - from rags to riches setagaya 's long-running boroichi market offers bargains and bites japan times , sunday , jan. 11 , 2009
