Nell Gwyn     eleanor `` nell '' gwyn ( or gwynn or gwynne ) ( 1650 - 14 november 1687 ) , was one of the earliest english actresses to receive prominent recognition , and a long-time mistress of king charles ii . called `` pretty , witty nell '' by samuel pepys , she has been called a living embodiment of the spirit of restoration england and has come to be considered a folk heroine , with a story echoing the rags-to-royalty tale of cinderella .
