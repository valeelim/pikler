Royal Australian Air Force     the royal australian air force ( raaf ) is the air force of the australian defence force . it started in 1912 as the australian flying corps and saw its first action in world war i. the first plan for an australian air force was given to the military board in 1910. the plan was approved on 22 october , 1912 , and five planes were ordered . a flying school was set up on of land at point cook , victoria , with the first students starting on 17 august , 1914 .
