Veslud     veslud is a commune . it is in the picardie region in the aisne department in the north of france . in 2012 , 253 people lived there . the motto « vetus lucus semper virens » ( `` old sacred wood still green '' ) suggests it might have been a place of cult during antiquity . a german cemetery was built at the end of world war i , where 1,704 soldiers are buried .
