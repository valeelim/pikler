British Rail Class 432     the british rail 4-rep electric multiple units were built by br at york works from 1966-1967 and 1974. the units were built to power the 4-tc trailer units on services on the south western main line . fifteen four-car units were built . the motor coaches were new build , but the trailers were converted from mk1 hauled stock . they were initially classified as class 441 and numbered 3001-3015. this was later changed to class 430 , under which they spent the majority of their working lives . shortly before withdrawal they were reclassified class 432 and the units were renumbered to 2001-2015 .
