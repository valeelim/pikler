Rio Hondo (California)    rio hondo ( california ) the rio hondo ( spanish for `` deep river '' ) is a river in los angeles county , california . its official beginning is at a reservoir called the whittier narrows reservoir ; and it flows southwest to the los angeles river . notably , the whittier narrows reservoir is also fed by the san gabriel river , and water can be released into either river . therefore , the watersheds of the los angeles river and san gabriel river are connected by the rio hondo . most of the rio hondo is lined with concrete , but there are two places the river is free-flowing . these are the `` peck road water conservation park '' and the `` whittier narrows recreation area '' . eaton wash is a tributary to the rio hondo .
