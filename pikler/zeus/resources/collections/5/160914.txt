Singapore Airlines     singapore airlines is the national airline of singapore . it carries people from singapore to 5 continents in the world . it is a major airline in southeast asia as well as on the `` kangaroo route '' from australia to the uk . the company was the first airline to buy the airbus a380 `` superjumbo '' . it used to fly the longest planned flight in the world , from singapore to newark . singapore airlines was given `` airline of the year '' by skytrax in 2008. its base , singapore changi airport , was given `` airport of the year '' in 2006 .
