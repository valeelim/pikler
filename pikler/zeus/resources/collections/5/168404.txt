Heat sink     a heat sink is an object that cools another part that is too hot . the heat sink is in contact with the part that should be cooled . it takes heat away and dissipates it , for example into the air around it . heat sinks are found in most computers . the chips inside a computer can get very hot . therefore they need to be cooled , so that they do n't break . this is usually done with a heat sink . heat sinks are also found in most high fidelity audio amplifiers .
