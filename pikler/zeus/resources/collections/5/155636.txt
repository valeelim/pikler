Mineral, Virginia    mineral , virginia mineral , virginia is a town of virginia in the united states . in the 2010 census , mineral had a 467 population . at 1:51 p.m. on august 23 , 2011 , a 5.8 magnitude earthquake was centered 5 miles ( 8 kilometers ) south-southwest of mineral , at a depth of 3.7 miles ( 6 kilometers ) . according to the associated press , it `` forced evacuations of all the monuments on the national mall in washington and rattled nerves from florida to canada '' . the roof of mineral 's town hall collapsed , and three of the six schools in the county 's school system suffered heavy damage . no one died , and some people had only minor injuries .
