Le Grand, Iowa    le grand , iowa le grand is a city of iowa in the united states . in 2008 , there were 975 people living in le grand . the town was first settled in 1847. it was named after legrand byington ( 1845-1909 ? ) , a politician from iowa city , who had helped several early settlers . the first post office started in 1853. in 1874 , a number of fossil crinoids were found in the local quarry . these are small sea animals that look like flowers . many of these have been sent to museums around the world .
