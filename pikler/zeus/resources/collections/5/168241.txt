Dipstick     a dipstick is a tool which can be dipped into liquid to measure how much of it there is , or to test it for things . a testing dipstick is usually made of paper or cardboard . it contains substances which change colour when something is found in a liquid . measuring dipsticks show the amount of liquid in a container . these containers are usually hard to access . measuring dipsticks are used by inserting and removing the stick and then checking how much of the stick is covered by the liquid . the oil level dipstick is an example of a measuring dipstick found on most internal combustion engines .
