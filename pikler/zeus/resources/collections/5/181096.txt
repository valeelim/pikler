Liz Cohen     liz cohen ( born 1973 ) is a performance artist . her best known project is called `` bodywork '' . she changed an old east german small car ( a trabant ) , so that it can transform into a larger american car ( a chevrolet ) . she hired a personal trainer and dieted so she could appear in a bikini to be the model for the car . she currently teaches at cranbrook academy of art and lives in detroit , michigan . references . - creative capital listing - wired magazine - fast company magazine
