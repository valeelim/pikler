Shelley, Idaho    shelley , idaho shelley is a city in idaho in the united states . it is in bingham county . shelley is on the eastern side of the snake river . it faces the blackfoot mountains . since 1927 shelley has had the `` idaho annual spud day '' . it is on the 3rd saturday of september . it has a parade , live bands , games ( such as a tug of war into a pit of mashed potatoes ) and free baked potatoes .
