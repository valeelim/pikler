Hurricane Irene (1999)    hurricane irene ( 1999 ) hurricane irene was an atlantic tropical cyclone . the storm caused heavy damage across florida and north carolina in october 1999. irene developed in the western caribbean sea on october 13 from a tropical wave . irene was a wet hurricane for florida , similar to many hurricanes of the 1930s and 1940s . in north carolina and virginia , the storm produced heavy rainfall of more than 10 inches . irene caused 18 deaths and $ 800 million in damage in the united states .
