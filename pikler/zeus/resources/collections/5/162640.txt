Paris Saint-Germain F.C.    paris saint-germain f.c .  , also abbreviated as psg , is a football club in france . it is based in paris , the capital city and most populated city of france . they have been champions of ligue 1 6 times ( 1985/1986 , 1993/1994 ) including each of the last four seasons ( 2012/13 , 2013/14 , 2014/15 and 2015/16 ) . they have been champions of ligue 2 once ( 1970/1971 ) . they play their home games at the parc des princes . league titles . - ligue 1 : 6 - 1985/86 , 1993/94 , 2012/13 , 2013/14 , 2014/15 , 2015/16 - ligue 2 : 1 - 1970/71
