Boldo     boldo is a kind of tree that can be found in the central part of chile . it is a big part of the sclerophyllous forests found in the central part of chile . boldo has also been introduced to europe and north africa , though it is not often seen outside botanical gardens . its leaves are used for culinary purposes , mostly in latin america . they have a strong , woody and slightly bitter flavor and camphor-like aroma . they are used in a similar manner to bay leaves and also used as an herbal tea .
