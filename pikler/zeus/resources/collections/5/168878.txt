Pay per click     pay per click advertising on search engines such as google and bing . on google the adverts are displayed either at the very top of the search results with an orange background , on the right hand side or in both these places . advertisers pay google each time someone clicks on their advert . it is often referred to by the letters ppc . many pay per click providers exist , google adwords , yahoo ! search marketing , and microsoft adcenter are the three largest operators .
