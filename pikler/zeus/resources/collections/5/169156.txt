Pane sciocco     `` pane sciocco '' ( also called in tuscany `` pane toscano '' ) is a variety of bread very common in tuscany and in umbria , two regions in italy . differently from the others kinds of italian bread , pane sciocco does n't have salt . probably the reason why there is no salt in the pane sciocco comes from the 1600s when salt was a rare product with a high cost . pane sciocco is often eaten with typically tuscan ham or sausages , famous for being salty . related pages . - tuscan food
