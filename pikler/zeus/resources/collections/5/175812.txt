Lilo Pelekai     lilo pelekai ( first name lee-lo , last name peh-leh-kai ) is one of the two characters of the disney animated movie `` lilo & stitch '' , its sequels , and . lilo ( literally , `` lost '' in hawaiian , and also in mandarin ) . lilo is a young hawaiian girl who lives on the island of kauai with her older sister nani and her new family of alien visitors that live on earth .
