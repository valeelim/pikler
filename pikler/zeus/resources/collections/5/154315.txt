Framecourt     framecourt is a small commune . it is found in the region nord-pas-de-calais in the pas-de-calais department in the north of france . a small farming village situated west of arras , at the junction of the d916 and the d102e roads . the church of st.vulgan in the village dates from the seventeenth century . there is also an 18th century priory . references . - insee commune file other websites . - framecourt on the quid website
