Comparison (grammar)    comparison ( grammar ) comparison , in grammar , is a property of adjectives and adverbs . adjectives and adverbs take three degrees , such as `` kind '' , `` kinder '' , `` kindest '' . the central degree is comparative , since for example bill is `` kinder than '' joe . whereas the first and third degrees make no comparison . likewise the use of `` more '' as in `` more quickly '' is comparative , where `` quick '' and `` quickest '' are not .
