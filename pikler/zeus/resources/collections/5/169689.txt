19521 Chaos     19521 chaos is a kuiper belt object . chaos was found in 1998 by the deep ecliptic survey , with kitt peak 's 4 m telescope . it is big at about 560 km , and bright at h=4.755 . it is named after the primeval state of existence in greek mythology , from which the first gods appeared . a jazz-fusion/progressive-rock band from connecticut got their name from this minor planet . references . - original mpec circular 1998-x08 and the revision , 1999-v03 - astdys
