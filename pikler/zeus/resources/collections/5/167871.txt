Mike Knox     michael shawn hettinga ( born on july 17 , 1978 ) is an american professional wrestler that is currently working for total nonstop action wrestling ( tna ) . he is best known for working with wwe on all three brands ( raw , smackdown and ecw ) from 2006 to 2010. he was released from his wwe contract on april 23 , 2010. he had a dark match in total nonstop action wrestling in may , 2012 .
