Annapurna     annapurna is an 8091 m ( 26,545 foot ) mountain in the himalayas , in nepal . it is the 10th highest mountain in the world . in the native language , the word `` annapurna '' means `` full of food '' or `` goddess of the harvests '' . the first people to climb annapurna were maurice herzog and louis lachenal , in 1950 .
