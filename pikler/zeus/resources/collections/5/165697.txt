Plug and play     plug and play is the name for a number of technologies used in computing . technologies that support plug and play allow to connect new devices to a computer . after a short while , these devices can simply be used . when they are no longer needed , they can be disconnected again . plug and play does not require the user to load a specific driver to access the device . plug and play is used to refer to both the configuration of devices at boot time , as well as to system that support adding a device later on . examples for systems that support plug and play are usb and firewire .
