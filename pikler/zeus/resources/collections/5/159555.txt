British Rail Class 508     the british rail class 508 electric multiple units ( `` emus '' ) were built by brel at york works in 1979-80. they were the fourth variety of br 's then-standard 1972 design for suburban emus , eventually encompassing 755 vehicles and five classes ( 313/314/315/507/508 ) . they have mostly worked on the merseyrail network since 1983 , and continue to do so , now refurbished by alstom 's eastleigh works . related pages . - british rail class 507 - merseyrail
