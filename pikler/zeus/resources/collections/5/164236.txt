IRCd     an ircd , meaning internet relay chat server program , is a program , server software that uses the irc protocol . this allows people to talk to each other on the internet . they exchange textual messages in real time . the server accepts connections from irc clients on a group of tcp ports . when the server is part of an irc network , it also keeps one or more connections with other servers/daemons . the word `` ircd '' once referred to only one piece of software . other pages . - comparison of internet relay chat daemons other websites . - irc.org - irc resources - technical comparison of ts and nickname delay mechanisms - darkfire irc manual ( network specific ) - ircd network provider - undernet k-line and g-line faq with reasons for them , amongst other things - efnet faq with several -line terms explained - quakenet general faq g/k-line - gline , kline , qline and eline syntax
