Audrey Hepburn (semi-postal)    audrey hepburn ( semi-postal ) the audrey hepburn semi-postal was a semi-postal postage stamp issued in germany in 2001 and showing audrey hepburn . it was forbidden by the family of the actress . the deutsche post ( `` german post '' ) authorities changed the stamp for one depicting a film roll . the complete series included other famous actors such as charlie chaplin , marilyn monroe , greta garbo and jean gabin .
