British Rail Class 460     the british rail class 460 ( or 8gat ) electrical multiple units were built by alstom at washwood heath from 2000-01. these units are part of alstom 's `` juniper '' family of units , which also includes classes 334 and 458. the eight class 460 units are ( up to 2009 ) the last to be classified under the tops `` 4xx '' series for dc 3rd rail emus on the southern region .
