Holyrood Palace     the palace of holyroodhouse , also known as holyrood palace , founded as a monastery by david i of scotland in 1128 , has served as the main home of the kings and queens of scotland since the fifteenth century . the palace stands in edinburgh , at the bottom of the royal mile . the palace of holyroodhouse is the official home in scotland of queen elizabeth ii , who spends time at the palace at the beginning of the summer . holyrood is an anglicisation of the scots `` haly ruid '' ( holy cross ) . pope benedict xvi visited queen elizabeth ii on 16th september 2010 on his first day of his official papal visit to the united kingdom . he then went on to say mass at glasgow 's beallahouston park .
