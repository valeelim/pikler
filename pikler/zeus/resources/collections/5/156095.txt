Mishawaka, Indiana    mishawaka , indiana mishawaka is a city in the state of indiana , in the united states . it is built on the banks of the st.joseph river . first settled in the 1830 's , in 2000 there were 44,657 people living in the city . the city is named after a native american girl , princess mishawaka , the daughter of a shawnee chief , elkhart . the first european settlers came to the area after bog iron was found there in the 1830 's . being near the river , it was a good site for industry which could use river transport . the town was first called st.joseph iron works , after the works which started there in 1833. shortly after , three other small towns were joined together as the city of mishawaka .
