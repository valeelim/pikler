Paris, Ontario    paris , ontario paris is a town in ontario , canada . it is where grand and nith rivers come together . it is west of toronto . it has a population of over 10,000 people . these people normally work in the nearby cities such as kitchener , brantford and london . paris has a strong agricultural background . there are many farms around the center of the town . paris used to have large clothing and furniture factories . most of the manufacturing sector has disappeared in recent year . the town has a strong community spirit . there are many business association that try to find ways to make the town successful and make people want to live there . several new building projects have brought hundreds of families to the community to live in a town with a strong school system , sports activities and jobs . other websites . - paris downtown - paris historical society
