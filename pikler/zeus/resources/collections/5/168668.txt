Building material     building material is any material which is used for a construction purpose . many naturally occurring substances , such as clay , sand , wood and rocks , even twigs and leaves have been used to construct buildings . apart from naturally occurring materials , many man-made products are in use , some more and some less synthetic . bricks are man-made , and so is plaster , concrete , stucco and many other materials . however , they are usually made out of natural materials , bricks being a good example . they is based on clay . steel is based on natural types of iron . one example of a man-made imitation is scagliola , which can imitate more expensive natural materials such as marble .
