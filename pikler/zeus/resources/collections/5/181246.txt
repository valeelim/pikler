George Victor, Prince of Waldeck and Pyrmont    george victor , prince of waldeck and pyrmont george victor ( 14 january 1831 - 12 may 1893 ) was the 3rd sovereign prince of the german state of waldeck and pyrmont . he was the father of emma of waldeck and pyrmont , the consort of william iii of the netherlands . george victor is the great-great grandfather of beatrix of the netherlands .
