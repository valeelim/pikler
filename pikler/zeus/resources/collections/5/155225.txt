Hazard, Kentucky    hazard , kentucky hazard is a city of kentucky in the united states . it is built on the north fork of the kentucky river . the first european settler was elijah combs who built a house near the river in 1795. in 1821 , the village became the seat of the local government area called perry county . the village was first called perry county court house . the town was called hazard in early records , and the post office , built in 1854 was called hazard . the name became official in 1884. the name hazard came from oliver hazard perry , a naval hero from the revolutionary war of 1812. other websites . - historic photos of hazard .
