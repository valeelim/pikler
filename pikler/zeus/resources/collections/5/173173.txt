HTC     htc corporation is a manufacturer of smartphones in taiwan . it used to be called high tech computer corporation and now is called htc by lots of people . the company first made smartphones based on microsoft 's windows mobile operating system , but in 2009 it started to make smartphones based on android operating system . htc is also a member of the open handset alliance , a group of manufacturers and mobile network operators helping to improve the android operating system . the htc dream , sold by t-mobile in many countries as the t-mobile g1 , was the first phone to use this . other websites . - htc website
