Falkenfelsen     the falkenfelsen ( falcons rock ) is a cliff in the northern black forest in baden-württemberg , germany . the granite rock formation is about high . from the schwarzwaldhochstraße ( black forest highway ) , two hiking trails lead through the formation and end at the shelter `` herta-hut '' . from the platform there is a view over the northern black forest with the hornisgrinde in the south , bühlerhöhe in the north east and the valley of the upper rhine in the west side . other websites . - falkenfelsen at environment ministry of baden-württemberg ( german )
