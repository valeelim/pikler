San Pietro in Montorio     san pietro in montorio is a church in rome , which includes in its courtyard `` the tempietto '' ( a small commemorative martyrium ) built by donato bramante . the `` tempietto of san pietro in montorio '' is a small commemorative martyrium built by donato bramante , possibly as early as 1502 , in the courtyard of `` san pietro in montorio '' . it is considered a masterpiece of high renaissance architecture .
