Ragdoll Productions     ragdoll productions ( or ragdoll ltd. ) ( and ragdoll productions animations . ) is a british company that produces children 's television shows . it was founded by anne wood in 1984 , who later made many of their successful television series , seen ( in the case of `` teletubbies '' ) in 111 countries and translated into 41 languages . other websites . - ragdoll 's web site
