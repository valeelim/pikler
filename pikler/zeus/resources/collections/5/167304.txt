Theodor Eicke     theodor eike ( october 17 , 1892 in hampont – february 26 , 1943 near kharkov ) was an obergruppenführer of the ss and general of the waffen-ss . he was the commander of the concentration camp of dachau . during the world war ii was he the leader of the totenkopf division . eike was on behalf of the nazi party a member of the reichstag . he was also a member of the sa .
