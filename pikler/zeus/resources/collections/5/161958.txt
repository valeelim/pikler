Mashed potato     mashed potatoes are a way to serve potatoes . mashed potatoes are made by boiling potatoes , and then mixing them with milk and butter until they are smooth . vegetable oil , cheese , sour cream , parsley , bacon bits and many other things can be added according to the maker 's taste . dehydrated instant mashed potato mixes are available in many different flavors . sometimes gravy is poured on top of mashed potatoes . mashed potatoes and gravy are usually served with roasts or meat loaf . mashed potatoes are part of the traditional american thanksgiving dinner . mashed potato was a running joke in the popular bbc children 's series bodger & badger which ran from 1989 to 1999 .
