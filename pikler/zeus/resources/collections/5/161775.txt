Ditrysia     the ditrysia are a natural group or clade of insects in the lepidopteran order containing both butterflies and moths . they are so named because the female has two distinct sexual openings : one for mating , and the other for laying eggs . about 98 % of described species of lepidoptera belong to ditrysia . the group can be divided into basal or `` incertae sedis '' `` micromoths '' and the apoditrysia which includes mostly larger moths as well as the butterflies .
