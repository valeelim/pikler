Erich Raeder     erich johann albert raeder ( 24 april 1876 in wandsbek - 6 november 1960 in kiel ) was a naval leader in germany before and during the second world war . he was made a `` großadmiral '' ( , equivalent to admiral of the fleet ) in 1939 , which is the highest naval rank . in 1943 he was replaced by karl dönitz in his position as head of the german navy . raeder was also one of the 24 charged at the nuremberg trials , and was sent to prison .
