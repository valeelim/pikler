Mach number     a mach number is a number that describes speed . it is a measure of the speed of an object compared to the speed of sound . mach number is given as a percentage compared to the local speed of sound . for example , half the speed of sound = mach 0.5. formula_1 where v is the speed of the object , and a is the speed of sound . it is named after the austrian physicist ernst mach . when writing a speed as a mach number , the number comes after `` mach .
