Flaming Gorge Dam     the flaming gorge dam is a concrete-arch dam on the green river in utah , in the united states . built in 1958-1964 , the dam is tall , long , and thick . it is one of the biggest dams in the united states . its reservoir , the flaming gorge reservoir , is about long , and covers 42,000 acres ( 170 square kilometers ) . the dam was built for water storage and hydroelectric power , and it produces about 152 megawatts of electricity .
