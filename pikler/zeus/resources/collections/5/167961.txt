Chepstow     chepstow ( ) is a town in monmouthshire , wales . it is next to the border with england . it is built beside the river wye , close to where it joins with the river severn . it is near the western end of the severn bridge on the m48 motorway . it is east of newport and west of london . chepstow is famous for its castle , the oldest stone castle still standing in britain , and for chepstow racecourse which is home to the welsh grand national . the town is on the west bank of the wye ; villages on the east bank of the wye , tutshill and sedbury , are in england .
