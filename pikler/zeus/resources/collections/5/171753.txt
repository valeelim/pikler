Antivirus software     antivirus software , if properly installed on a computer system , can prevent access to computer systems by unwanted computer programs . viruses , worms or trojan horses can be used by criminals or mischievous people ( called 'crackers ' ) . they can be used to steal information or damage computer systems . if no antivirus software is installed , hackers may be able to access the information in the computer . most tests and experts claim that antivirus software is unable to prevent some attacks . there are many different types of antivirus software . many antivirus programs can be downloaded for free . these versions usually have some features missing . the missing features are only available to those who buy the `` full '' version . antivirus software uses many ways to protect the computer . they often search for signs of viruses in every website that is visited . most also do a regular scan of all the data and files on the computer 's hard disk . installing more than one antivirus is not a good idea . the 2 different antivirus software can interfere with each other .
