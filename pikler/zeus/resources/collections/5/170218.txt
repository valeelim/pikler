Sabrina, the Teenage Witch (TV series)    sabrina , the teenage witch ( tv series ) sabrina the teenage witch is a comedy american television show based on the `` archie '' comic book series of the same name . it is set in massachusetts . it stars melissa joan hart in the title role . its first four seasons aired on abc from september 27 , 1996 to may 5 , 2000 ; the final three seasons ran on the wb television network from september 22 , 2000 to april 24 , 2003 .
