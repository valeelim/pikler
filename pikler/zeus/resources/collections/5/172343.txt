Burj al-Arab     the burj al arab is a hotel in dubai , united arab emirates . it is the fourth largest hotel in the world , after rose tower , which is also in dubai . it is very expensive to stay at the burj al arab . it is known as the world 's only 7 star hotel . on the top of the burj al-arab there is helipad which is sometimes used as a tennis court for high paying guests . -caleb
