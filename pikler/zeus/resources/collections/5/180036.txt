Bože Pravde     `` bože pravde '' ( , `` lord of justice '' or `` lord , give us justice '' ) is the official national anthem of serbia , as defined by the article 7 of the constitution of serbia . `` bože pravde '' was the anthem ( hymn ) of the principality of serbia and kingdom of serbia all of time except 1918 when kingdom of serbs , croats and slovenes was formed . it was `` reborn '' by the parliament of serbia on august 17 , 2004. it was written in 1872 with music by davorin jenko and lyrics by jovan đorđević . original source was a piece for the theater play `` marko kazuje na kome je carstvo '' ( marko names the emperor ) , and it 's famous popularity with public wanted bože pravde as the serbian national anthem . it was also used as an anthem of republika srpska , an entity of bosnia and herzegovina , in the period of 1991 to 2006 , when it was ruled down by the country 's constitutional court . other websites . - parliament of serbia a page featuring sheet music for brass band , and for orchestra with mixed choir , as well as an instrumental file
