Charlottesville, Virginia    charlottesville , virginia charlottesville is a city of virginia in the united states . it is named for charlotte of mecklenburg-strelitz , king george iii 's queen . 45,049 people live there . in 2004 , charlottesville was named the best place to live in the united states in a book named `` cities ranked and rated . '' three american presidents have lived in charlottesville , including thomas jefferson , james madison , and james monroe . the university of virginia is in charlottesville . president jefferson 's home , monticello , is also in charlottesville . both are popular places for people to visit .
