Dragoş     dragoş ( `` drágfi of béltek '' ) was a maramureş voivode ruling over the lands of what was to become moldavia ( between 1351 and 1353 ) . he left maramureş by orders from the hungarian king louis i , in order to establish a defense line against the golden horde . he was succeeded by his son , sas ( `` szász '' or `` sas of béltek '' ) ( ruled 1354-1358 ) .
