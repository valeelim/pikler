Moscow Symphony Orchestra     the moscow symphony orchestra is an orchestra based in moscow , russia . it was started in 1989. it was the first orchestra that was started in modern russia with private money , not with money from the government . the orchestra have played under famous russian conductors and with famous soloists , including yuri bashmet and vadim repin . the orchestra 's main conductor is vladimir ziva , and its main guest conductor is ignat solzhenitsyn . the orchestra have given concerts in united states , japan , south korea and western europe . other websites . - mso official site
