Vrancea County     vrancea ( ) is a county ( judeţ ) in romania . the capital of vrancea county is focşani . it is mostly in the historical region of moldavia . the southern part of vrancea county is not in moldavia . about 387,630 people were living in vrancea county as of the year 2002. vrancea county has 2 municipalities , 3 towns , and 67 communes . municipalities . - focşani -- county seat , population : 103,219 - adjud towns . - mărăşeşti - odobeşti - panciu other websites . - portal of vrancea county - vrancea online - vrancea county 's start page on the internet
