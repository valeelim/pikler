Hurricane Iris     hurricane iris was the third major hurricane of the 2001 atlantic hurricane season . hurricane iris was also the second storm of 2001 to strike belize . iris was a category 4 hurricane when it struck belize , but damage was rather low . it is likely because iris weakened very quickly over land . people were thinking that iris would cross central america without dying and would keep the name iris . this did not happen , iris weakened over land very fast and died . iris did however reformed in the pacific ocean , it was given a new name , manuel . iris left $ 150 million in damage and killed 49 people . the name iris was retired anyway . in 2007 the name ingrid was used instead .
