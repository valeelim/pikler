World Poverty and Human Rights     world poverty and human rights is a 2002 book by thomas pogge . in the book , pogge says that the poorest 46 percent of people have 1.2 percent of global income , and 826 million of them do not have enough to eat . one-third of all human deaths are from poverty-related causes : 18 million annually , including 12 million children under five . at the other end of the spectrum , the 15 percent of people in the developed countries have 80 percent of global income . pogge says that shifting 1 or 2 percent of our share toward poverty eradication seems morally compelling . yet most of the rich people believe that they have no such responsibility .
