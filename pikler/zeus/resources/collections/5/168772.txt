Drill     a drill is a type of electrical tool that has a spinning tip . it can be used for many different jobs depending on the attachment . if a drill bit is attached , the drill can be used to make holes in material . a driver bit can also be attached , allowing the drill to put screws into the desired material . a drill can run on a battery or be plugged into the wall , depending on the drill . other websites . - the different types of drill - introduction to the corded drill
