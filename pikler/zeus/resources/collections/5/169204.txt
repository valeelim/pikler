Paraglider     a paraglider is a free-flying , foot-launched aircraft . in mountain areas , pilots usually take off from great height to fly down to the valley . in flat areas , a winch is used to tow the paraglider to a height of up to 400 meters before it is released . it is also possible to soar on the coast in laminar sea breeze . some more advanced pilots also launch from hot air balloons or helicopters .
