Mos Def     dante terrell smith ( born december 11 , 1973 ) is an emmy- and golden globe-nominated american actor and a grammy-nominated rapper known by the stage name mos def . mos def started his hip hop career in a group called urban thermo dynamics , after which he appeared on albums by da bush babees and de la soul . with talib kweli , he formed the duo black star , who released the well-received album `` black star '' in 1998. he was a major force in the late 1990s underground hip hop explosion spearheaded by rawkus records . as a solo artist he has released the albums `` black on both sides '' in 1999 , `` the new danger '' in 2004 , and `` true magic '' , 2006. other websites . - mos def 's official website . - mos def / dante smith discographies at discogs . - mos def at geffen records .
