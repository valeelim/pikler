University of Waterloo     the `` university of waterloo '' is a school that you attend after high school . it is in the southwest of ontario . this university is marked the 5th best university in canada . this university is very large . it was founded in 1957. the university of waterloo holds many contests in mathematics for students in high school . some contests let you choose the correct answer out of many wrong answers ( multiple choice ) , and for other contests one must write the whole answer and how it was found . anybody in canada can take these contests . each contest is named after famous mathematicians , such as galois or fermat . references . - cemc.uwaterloo.ca - http : //www.4icu.org/ca/ - http : //www.4icu.org/reviews/618.htm
