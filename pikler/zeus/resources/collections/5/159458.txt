British Rail Class 421     the british rail class 421 ( or 4cig ) electrical multiple units were built at br york works from 1964-1972. units were built in two batches , and were initially introduced on services on the brighton main line . later units were introduced on services to portsmouth . these units replaced older southern railway-designed units , such as the 5bel `` brighton belle '' units , and 4cor units . these trains were known commonly as `` slam-doors '' .
