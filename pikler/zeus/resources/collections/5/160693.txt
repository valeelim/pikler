Gatwick Express     gatwick express is the name of a train that runs between victoria station in london and gatwick airport in south east england . it is fast because it does not stop at other stations . the train comes every 30 minutes . before june 2008 , gatwick express was a train operating company ( toc ) and was a franchise owned by southern , another toc that has trains on other routes . on 22 june 2008 , the gatwick express toc was closed . now the gatwick express is directly operated by southern , but it is still called gatwick express . related pages . - heathrow express - stansted express - airport rail link < br >
