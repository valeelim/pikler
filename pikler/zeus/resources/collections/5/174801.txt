The Lone Gunmen (TV series)    the lone gunmen ( tv series ) the lone gunmen was a short-lived television show based on the show `` the x-files '' . it focused on the three geeks that occasionally helped agents mulder and scully . their names were john byers , played by bruce harwood , ringo langly , played by dean haglund , and melvin frohike , played by tom braidwood ( who used to work behind the scenes on `` the x-files '' until he was talked into taking the part of frohike ) . the series expanded the line-up to include a character named jimmy bond , played by stephen snedden , and yves adele harlow , played by zuleikha robinson . it is best known for predicting the events of 9/11 in its first episode .
