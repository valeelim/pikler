Baduhenna     in germanic paganism , baduhenna is a goddess of war . she is only referred to by tacitus in the `` annals , '' where he tells of a grove in frisia that was dedicated to her , near where 900 roman prisoners were killed in 28 ce . references . - simek , rudolf ( 2007 ) translated by angela hall . `` dictionary of northern mythology '' . d.s . brewer . isbn 0-85991-513-1
