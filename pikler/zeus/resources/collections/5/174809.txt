Top-level domain     a top-level domain , or tld , are the letters that follow the final dot of the domain names . there are several kinds of tlds : - infrastructure : one tld , the .arpa - countries : usually they are codes with two letters according to iso 3166 . - sponsored : they are promoted by collectives , organizations , the us government or territories with its own language and culture . - generic : they are .com , .net , .org and .info . - generic restricted : .edu , .gov , .mil related pages . - country code top-level domain ( includes a list )
