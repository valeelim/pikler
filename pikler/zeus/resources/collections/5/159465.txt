British Rail Class 438     the british rail 4-tc multiple units were unpowered fixed formation 4 carriage set ( fitted with driving cabs at each end ) converted by br at york works from locomotive-hauled mark 1 carriages in 1966-1967 and 1974. the units built on experience gained from the prototype 6tc unit . they were initially classified as class 442 and numbered in the 300 and 400 series . this was later changed to class 491 , under which they spent the majority of their working lives . shortly before withdrawal they were reclassified class 438 and the units were renumbered to 8001-8034 .
