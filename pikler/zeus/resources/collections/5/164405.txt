Goby     the gobies are teleost fish of the family gobiidae . this is one of the largest families of fish , with more than 2,000 species in more than 200 genera . most are relatively small , less than 10 cm ( 4 in ) in length . although few are important as food for humans , they are of great significance as prey species for commercially important fish like cod , haddock , sea bass , and flatfish .
