Whopper     whopper is a kind of hamburger sold at the fast-food chain burger king . it is made using a beef patty , lettuce , tomato , sliced onion , pickles , ketchup , and mayonnaise served on a sesame seed bun . also , cheese can be added at the customer 's request . there is also a double whopper available . a `` whopper jr. '' is the same as the whopper , but in a smaller size .
