Coconut crab     the coconut crab , birgus latro , is a large edible land crab related to the hermit crab . coconut crabs are found in the tropical indian and pacific oceans . even though they are named the coconut crab , they do n't eat coconuts that often . they mainly eat certain fruits and nuts . coconut crabs can climb trees . coconut crab meat has been considered a local delicacy . coconut crabs are extremely high priced in the national food market .
