Just Say No     `` just say no '' was an advertising campaign , part of the u.s. `` war on drugs '' , dominant during the 1980s and early 1990s , to discourage children from engaging in illegal recreational drug use by offering various ways of saying no . this also expanded the realm of `` just say no '' to violence and premarital sex . the slogan was created by first lady of the united states nancy reagan during her husband , ronald reagan , presidency .
