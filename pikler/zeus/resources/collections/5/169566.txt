Tim Flannery     professor tim flannery ( born 28 january 1956 ) is an australian mammalogist , palaeontologist and global warming activist . flannery was australian of the year in 2007 and previously was a professor at macquarie university . he left macquarie university in mid-2013 . flannery is also the chairman of the copenhagen climate council , an international climate change awareness group.he also has a wife/spouse named alexandra szalay who is also a scientist and italian related pages . - the weather makers
