Michail Leontievich Bulatov     michail leontievich bulatov ( михаил леонтьевич булатов ) , ( 1760 in rjazan , 2 may 1825 in omsk ) , was a russian military who fought during the russo-turkish war ( 1787-1992 ) and became major general in 1799 during the napoleonic wars and lieutenant-general in 1823 .
