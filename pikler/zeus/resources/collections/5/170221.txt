Breakaway (song)    breakaway ( song ) `` breakaway '' is a 2004 song written by avril lavigne , bridget benenate and matthew gerrard and produced by john shanks . it was recorded by pop rock singer kelly clarkson for the soundtrack to the film `` the princess diaries 2 : royal engagement '' by disney records . it was also included on clarkson 's second album , `` breakaway '' and was released as the album 's first single .
