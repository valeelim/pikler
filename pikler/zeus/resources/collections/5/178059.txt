Ludwig von Brenner     ludwig von brenner ( born leipzig , 19 september 1833 ; died 9 february 1902 ) was a german conductor and composer . he studied at the leipzig conservatory . he went to saint petersburg to play in the saint petersburg philharmonic orchestra . in 1872 he returned to germany and conducted an orchestra called the `` berlin symphony orchestra '' . he then started an orchestra called `` neue berliner symphoniekapelle '' . in 1882 he became the first conductor of a new orchestra , the berlin philharmonic orchestra , which was soon to become one of the world 's best orchestras .
