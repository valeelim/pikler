Silent Alarm     silent alarm is an album by bloc party . it was released on 14 february 2005 in the uk . in the usa , it was released on 22 march . track listing . 1 . `` like eating glass '' – 4:22 1 . `` helicopter '' – 3:40 1 . `` positive tension '' – 3:55 1 . `` banquet '' – 3:21 1 . `` blue light '' – 2:47 1 . `` she 's hearing voices '' – 3:29 1 . `` this modern love '' – 4:25 1 . `` the pioneers '' – 3:35 1 . `` price of gasoline '' – 4:19 1 . `` so here we are '' – 3:52 1 . `` luno '' – 3:57 1 . `` plans '' – 4:10 1 . `` compliments '' – 4:43 the albums tracks were written by lead singer kele okereke and guitarist russell lissack . the album was produced by paul epworth who also produced `` a certain trigger '' by maximo park and `` made of bricks '' by kate nash .
