First Canadian Place     first canadian place is a skyscraper in toronto , ontario , canada . at 298 metres , or 978 feet ( 355 metres with antenna included ) , it is canada 's tallest skyscraper and the eleventh tallest building in north america . it is the third tallest free-standing structure in canada , after cn tower , also in toronto , and the inco superstack in sudbury , ontario . first canadian place is located in toronto 's financial district at the northwest corner of king and bay streets , the centre of canadian financial industry . it is home to the toronto headquarters of the bank of montreal , the oldest canadian bank ( hence the 'first ' ) .
