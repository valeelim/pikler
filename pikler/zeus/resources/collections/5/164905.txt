Villefranche-de-Rouergue     villefranche-de-rouergue ( ) is a commune in southern france in the aveyron department , in the region of occitanie . it is the `` sous-prefecture '' ( capital ) of the `` arrondissement '' of the same name in the aveyron department . in 1252 , alphonse de poitiers , count of toulouse , sent jean d'arcis , seneschal of rouergue , to build a city along the aveyron as a new capital for rouergue ( an old french province ) : villefranche-de-rouergue . from 1369 to the french revolution , villefranche-de-rouergue was the capital of rouergue . villefranche-de-rouergue is in the valley of the aveyron river along the line that separates the `` causses '' ( limestone plateaus ) from the `` ségala '' geographical region . there are mines of copper , lead , tin , iron and silver . the alzou river joins in the city , as a right tributary , the aveyron river . villefranche-de-rouergue has an area of , and its average altitude in the city is ; at the city hall , the altitude is .. villefranche-de-rouergue is surrounded by the `` communes '' saint-rémy , maleville , la bastide-l'évêque , morlhon-le-haut , sanvensa , la rouquette , savignac and toulonjac .
