Blacksburg, Virginia    blacksburg , virginia blacksburg is a town in montgomery county , in western virginia . in 2000 , 39,284 people lived there . blacksburg was first explored by henry batt and john fallam who were sent by abraham wood in 1671. the town is named after samuel black , who used to own land there . on october 2 , 1872 , virginia tech was opened with three teachers . on april 26 , 2007 , seung-hui cho shot 32 people in the virginia tech massacre .
