Kankakee, Illinois    kankakee , illinois kankakee is a city in illinois , united states . it is also the county seat of kankakee county . u.s. house of representatives member adam kinzinger was born in kankakee . the potawatami lived in the area beginning sometime in the 18th century . in 1833 the potawatami signed a treaty with the united states government . they agreed to leave the region and move west . kankakee was founded in 1854 .
