Beehive     a beehive is a structure in which some species of honey bees ( genus `` apis '' ) live and raise their young . natural beehives ( or `` nests '' ) are made by honey bee colonies , while domesticated honey bees are kept in man-made beehives in a location known as an apiary . the beehives made by people are usually the things referred to by the word `` beehive . '' beehives have hexagonal cells in them , packed together . this is called a honeycomb .
