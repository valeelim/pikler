Dumbbell nebula     the dumbbell nebula ( sometimes called messier 27 , m 27 , or ngc 6853 ) is a planetary nebula in the constellation vulpecula . it is about 1,360 light years away from earth . the dumbbell nebula was the first planetary nebula to be discovered ; by charles messier in 1764. other websites . - seds messier page on m27 - m27 dumbbell nebula - m27 image taken by stargazer observatory
