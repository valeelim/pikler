Solar cooker     a solar cooker is a device that uses sunlight for energy . they use no fuel and cost nothing to run . they also help slow deforestation and desertification . another benefit to solar cooking is that unlike cooking with fire , solar cookers do n't pollute the air . solar cookers are also sometimes used for cooking outside , especially when using fire is risky or there is no fuel . it is very useful . it is a renewable source of energy solar box cookers can be made of materials or be manufactured in a factory for sale . there are many kinds of solar box cookers , from small cardboard boxes , that can be used to cook a single meal , to wood and glass boxes built into the sunny side of a house . although the solar cooker was invented by horace de saussure , a swiss naturalist , as early as 1767 , solar box cookers have only become popular since the 1970s . they are found in almost every country of the world .
