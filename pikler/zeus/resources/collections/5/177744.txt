San Gabriel Mountains     the san gabriel mountains are a mountain range in southern california , in the united states . they are in both los angeles county and san bernardino county . mount san antonio is the highest point in the mountains range , standing 10,064 feet ( 3,067 m ) tall . the mountains separate the basin of los angeles in the south from the mojave desert in the north . the san gabriel river and los angeles river are the major rivers that receive water from the mountains . california state route 2 is the major highway that runs through the san gabriel mountains .
