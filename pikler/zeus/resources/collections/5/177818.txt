True parrots     the true parrots are about 330 species of bird belonging to the psittacoidae superfamily , one of the three superfamilies in the biological order psittaciformes ( parrots ) . the other superfamilies are the cacatuoidea ( cockatoos ) , and strigopoidea ( new zealand parrots ) . true parrots are more widespread than cockatoos , with species in the americas , africa , asia , australia and eastwards across the pacific ocean as far as polynesia .
