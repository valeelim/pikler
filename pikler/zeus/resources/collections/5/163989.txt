Lee Keun-Ho     lee keun-ho ( , born : 11 april 1985 ) is a south korean professional athlete . he is best known as an association football player . he was a member of the korean national team . lee plays in forward position , and usually wing-forward ( at the side ) . he was born on april 5th , 1985. he is now playing for daegu fc . his height is 176cm , and his weight is 71kg . he graduated from bu-pyeong high school . his nickname is 'eagle ' . he joined incheon united in 2005 , but he stayed in the second team despite his potential ability . however , after he moved to dae-gu fc , he played at his best . after that , many people became interested in him , and he was able to join the korean national team . he got his first goal for the national team in june 29th , 2007. that game was against iraq . it was his first game for the national team . after that game , he perform a meritorious deed , also in his club team . on october 11th , 2008 , he scored 2 goals in the game agains uzbekistan . because of this , it was much easier for the korean national team to get to the world cup .
