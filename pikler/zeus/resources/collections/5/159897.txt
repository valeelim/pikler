Isigny-sur-Mer     isigny-sur-mer is a commune . it is found in the region basse-normandie in the calvados department in the northwest of france . walt disney 's surname derives from the commune of isigny . the earliest known ancestor of walt disney , with a similar name , was jean-christophe d'isigny ( `` of isigny '' ) . `` d'isigny '' was anglicised to disney . `` d'isigny '' in french means ( `` from isigny '' ) .
