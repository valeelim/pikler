Burglary     burglary is a crime . a person who does a burglary is called a burglar . in a burglary , the burglar breaks into a place ( like another person 's house or car ) with the goal of stealing something . burglary happens more often in poor communities than rich communities . normally they do it at night time and when nobody is in the house . people can also do it in stores and banks . the word `` burglar '' comes from the old english words `` burg '' ( `` house '' ) and `` laron '' ( `` thief '' ) .
