Alonso de Ercilla     alonso de ercilla y zúñiga ( august 7 , 1533–november 29 , 1594 ) , was a spanish nobleman , soldier and epic poet . while in chile ( 1556–63 ) he fought against the araucanian indians , and there he began the epic poem `` la araucana '' , said to be the greatest spanish historical poem . this heroic work in 37 cantos is divided into three parts , published in 1569 , 1578 , and 1589. it tells of the brave uprising of the araucanians and also tells the history of chile and of contemporary spain .
