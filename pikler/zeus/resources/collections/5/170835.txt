Ganz (tramcars)    ganz ( tramcars ) the ganz ( ganz vállalatok , `` ganz companies '' ) electric works in budapest is best known for making tramcars . it was also one of the first to use three-phase alternating current on electric railways . ganz also made / makes : ships ( ganz danubius ) , steel bridges ( ganz acélszerkezet ) , high voltage electrical equipment ( ganz transelektro ) . famous engineers employed by ganz were kálmán kandó and ottó bláthy . the company is named after ábrahám ganz .
