SNCF Class BB 22200     the bb 22200 is a class of electric locomotives in service with the french railways sncf , built by alsthom between 1976-1986. they are a polycurrent version ( 1500 cv direct current and 25 kv 50 hz ac ) of the bb 7200 and bb 15000 classes , with which it shares the exterior appearance . thanks to this characteristic they can run in all french electrified lines .
