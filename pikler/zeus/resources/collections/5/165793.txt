Fußball-Bundesliga     the fußball-bundesliga ( also named the bundesliga , `` german pronunciation : '' [ ˈbʊndəsˌliːɡa ] , `` federal league '' ) is a football league which is top division in germany . the league has 18 teams , and the teams can be relegated to 2. fußball-bundesliga . most games are played on saturdays and sundays , with some games being played during the week . since 1963 , fc bayern munich has almost completely dominated the league , winning 25 times . however , the league has seen other champions , such as borussia dortmund , borussia mönchengladbach , hamburger sv , werder bremen , vfb stuttgart , vfl wolfsburg and so on . the bundesliga is one of the top leagues , currently ranked 2nd place in uefa 's uefa coefficient , previously being ranked 2nd in 2009. the league has a very high average attendance , the top association football league in the world . the current champions are fc bayern munich .
