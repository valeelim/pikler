Orrorin tugenensis     orrorin is considered to be the second-oldest known hominin ancestor that is possibly related to modern humans . only one species is known : orrorin tugenensis . the name was given by the discoverers who found `` orrorin '' fossils in the tugen hills of kenya . the fossils were found were estimated to date between 6.1 and 5.8 million years ago , during the late miocene .
