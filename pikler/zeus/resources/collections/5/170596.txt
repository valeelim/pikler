Toronto Sun     the toronto sun is an english language daily tabloid newspaper published in toronto , ontario , canada . it is known for its daily `` sunshine girl '' . it is often seen as a working person 's newspaper . as of the end of 2007 , the `` sun '' sold 180,000 newspapers during monday to saturday and anout 310,000 papers on sunday . related pages . - the sun ( newspaper ) - the original uk version other websites . - toronto sun - article on the sun - includes rough circulation info
