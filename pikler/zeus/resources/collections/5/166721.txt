Cauliflower     cauliflower is one of several vegetables in the species `` brassica oleracea '' , in the family brassicaceae . cauliflower is a variety of cabbage , whose white flower head is eaten . cauliflower is very nutritious , and may be eaten cooked , raw or pickled . it is a popular vegetable in poland where it is eaten in a soup with cream or fried with bread crumbs .
