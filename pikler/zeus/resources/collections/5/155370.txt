Prestonsburg, Kentucky    prestonsburg , kentucky prestonsburg is a city in the united states . it is the county seat of floyd county in the state of kentucky . the city is in the eastern part of kentucky . prestonburg is on the bank of the big sandy river . the area was fist settled in 1797 by colonel john preston , solomon stratton , matthias harman , and andrew hood . it is named after colonel preston . in 2010 , 3,255 people lived in the city .
