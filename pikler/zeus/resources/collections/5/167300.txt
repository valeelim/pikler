Heinrich Brüning     heinrich aloysius maria elisabeth brüning ( 26 november 1885 in münster – 30 march 1970 in norwich , vermont ) was a german politician during the weimar republic . between 1930 and 1932 he was the chancellor of germany . brüning opposed nazism and stayed during the nazi period in the united states , where he taught political science , after the war also in germany .
