Knight's Cross of the Iron Cross    knight 's cross of the iron cross the knight 's cross of the iron cross ( german language : `` ritterkreuz des eisernen kreuzes '' , often just `` ritterkreuz '' ) was a level of the iron cross . the knight 's cross of the iron cross was the second highest military order of the third reich , second only to the grand cross of the iron cross . it was awarded for extreme battlefield bravery or military leadership during world war ii .
