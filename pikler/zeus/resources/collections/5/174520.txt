Nodicia de Kesos     the nodicia de kesos ( `` document of kesos '' ) is the first text written in spanish . the document was made between 959 and 974 , and shows the evolution of the language from latin to a romance language . the document was made in ardón , kingdom of león , actually a municipallity in the province of león , spain . it is a report about some cheeses written for a monastery . it is now kept in the león cathedral . `` nodicia de /kesos que /espisit frater /semeno : jn labore /de fratres jn ilo ba- /celare /de cirka sancte ius- /te , kesos u ; jn ilo /alio de apate , /ii kesos ; en [ que ] /puseron ogano , /kesos iiii ; jn ilo /de kastrelo , i ; /jn ila vinia majore , /ii ; ( 2ª columna ) /que lebaron en fosado , /ii , ad ila tore ; /que baron a cegia , /ii , quando la talia- /ron ila mesa ; ii que /lebaron lejione ; ii /..s ... en /u ... re ... / ... que ... / ... c ... / ... e ... u / ... alio ( ? ) / ... /g ... uane ece ; alio ke le /ba de sopbrino de gomi /de do ... a ... ; iiii que espi- /seron quando llo rege /uenit ad rocola ; /i qua salbatore ibi /uenit .
