Treaty of Paris (1229)    treaty of paris ( 1229 ) the treaty of paris was a treaty signed on april 12 , 1229. it was signed between raymond vii of toulouse and louis ix of france . the agreement officially ended the albigensian crusade . th treaty also said that raymond 's daughter was to be married to louis ' brother . raymond also gave away the northern provinces of his lands , as well as languedoc , to louis .
