Qawwali     qawwali ( ) is an islamic sufi type of music , that is famous in pakistan and india . mainly performed at sufi shrines in pakistan and india , qawwali has also become popular in the world . singers like nusrat fateh ali khan are well known for this music due to their excellent voice and ability of singing . qawwali music is believed to have started 700 years ago . this music is mainly based in punjab , which is a state of pakistan .
