Sonic Battle     sonic battle is a fighting video game for the game boy advance that came out on january 5 , 2004. the game is made by thq , sonic team and sega . people can play as sonic the hedgehog , tails the fox , knuckles the echidna , shadow the hedgehog , rouge the bat , amy rose , cream the rabbit , e-102 gamma , chaos 0 , or a new character called emerl .
