Recipe     a recipe is a list of instructions that shows how to prepare or make something such as a food dish . modern-day recipes are usually made up of several parts : - the name of the food being made - how much time it will take to make the food - the ingredients needed along with how much of it will be needed - the equipment needed - a list of steps that need to be followed in order to make the food - the number of people that the recipe will be able to feed recipes from earlier times often included much less information : older recipes usually just reminded a person of the ingredients needed . the earliest known recipes are from 1600 and came from an akkadian tablet from southern babylonia . the ancient egyptians painted hieroglyphics showing the preparation of food . many recipes are known from ancient greece . mithaecus 's cookbook was an early one , but most of it has been lost . athenaeus quotes one short recipe in his deipnosophistae . roman recipes are known with some of them starting in the 2nd century b.c with cato the elder 's de agri cultura . many other authors of this period explained eastern mediterranean cooking in greek and latin .
