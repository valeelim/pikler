Nevi'im     nevi'im ( , `` prophets '' ) is the second section of the hebrew bible , the tanakh . it includes : - book of joshua - judges - the two books of samuel - the two books of kings - isaiah - book of jeremiah - book of ezekiel - the twelve `` minor prophets '' : - book of hosea - book of joel - book of amos - book of obadiah - jonah - book of micah - book of nahum - book of habakkuk - book of zephaniah - book of haggai - zechariah - book of malachi
