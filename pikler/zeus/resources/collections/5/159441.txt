British Rail Class 405     the southern railway ( sr ) gave the designation sub to the wide variety of electric multiple units that were used on inner-suburban workings in the south london area . originally these units were formed as three-car units , but in the 1940s , all surviving units were increased to four cars by the addition of an 'augmentation ' trailer . new four-car units were also built at this time , and these survived in passenger use until late 1983 , by which time british rail had allocated to them tops class 405 .
