Entamoeba histolytica     entamoeba histolytica is an parasitic protozoan , part of the genus `` entamoeba '' . it infects mainly humans and other primates . `` e. histolytica '' is estimated to infect about 50 million people worldwide . about seventy thousand die each year , mostly from liver abscesses or other complications . the disease it causes is known as amoebiasis . a related species , `` entamoeba dispar '' , may be even more common .
