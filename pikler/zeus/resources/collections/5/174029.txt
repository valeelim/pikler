La Nuestra Tierra     la nuestra tierra ( `` our land '' ) was a weekly newspaper edited by the idel ( leonese studies institute ) between 2000 and 2004. its content was mainly about leonese territory and a 30 % of it was written in leonese language , from opinion articles to information in leonese language . this publication edited 162 numbers , and was supported by the european union and the leonese public administrations .
