Red mercury     red mercury is a term used for a number of substances that are supposed to exist ( or that could possibly exist ) . so far , no such substance has been found . the chemical element mercury most often occurs in a mixture with sulphur , as `` hgs '' . that mixture is known as cinnabar , and reddish in color . in pure form , mercury is silver in color . the following assumptions about red mercury have been proved as false : - it could be part of a fraud , the title `` red '' would refer to the former communist europe . the name was made during cold war . - it could be a codename for very pure uranium or plutonium . - it could be a very potent conventional explosive . this would make it possible to build small hydrogen bombs .
