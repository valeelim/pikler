Slut     slut is a broad word used to describe women ( or sometimes men ) who often have casual sex , without depending on their age , race , class or ethnic background . the word is often considered to be offensive . it probably comes from the old german word schlutt `` slovenly woman '' . it could also come from swedish slata `` idle woman , slut '' . it may have come from dutch slodder `` slut '' . however , it is not known where it came from for sure . chaucer uses the word `` sluttish '' ( late 14c . ) to describe a dirty man . however , it is most often used today to describe women , not men . also , hard pieces in bread because it was not well kneaded were called `` slut 's pennies '' .
