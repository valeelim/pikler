The E.N.D.    the e.n.d . the e.n.d ( an abbreviation of `` the energy never dies '' ) is the fifth studio album by american hip hop group the black eyed peas . it was released on june 3 , 2009. the album 's music was different to the groups previous sound as it was a bit more `` futuristic '' sounding and was heavily rooted in dance music .
