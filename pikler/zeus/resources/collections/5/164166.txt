Shunichiro Okano     okano was coach of the japanese football team at the 1964 summer olympics in tokyo in 1964. he won a bronze medal at the 1968 summer olympics in mexico city . in 1990 , okano became a member of the international olympic committee ( ioc ) . after his retirement in 2011 , he was granted honorary ioc membership status . okano died from lung cancer on 2 february 2017 at a tokyo hospital . he was 85 . ! total||2||0 related pages . - sports in japan - japan at the olympics
