Newspeak     newspeak is a concept from the book `` 1984 '' by george orwell . it is based on english , which is referred to as oldspeak in the novel , and it is the language that was created by oceania and is the only language whose vocabulary grows smaller each year . for example , the word for `` bad '' becomes `` ungood '' in newspeak , becoming a word simply meaning the opposite of `` good '' , with the prefix `` un- '' meaning `` not '' . in the novel , this language was designed by party members as a means to limit human thought to absolute lowest level , that way they would be made to never be able to question whatever the party tells the society .
