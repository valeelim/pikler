Joseph Berchtold     joseph berchtold ( march 6 , 1897 – august 23 , 1962 ) , a former salesman who sold office products , he became reichsführer ss in 1926 after julius schreck . berchtold was in the world war i and had the rank of second lieutenant at the end of the war . in 1920 he joined the nsdap . he was thought to be better than julius schreck , but was still not able to keep the people who started the party under control . after a lot of arguing he resigned in 1927 and was replaced by his deputy , erhard heiden .
