Tarzan II     tarzan ii is a direct-to-video midquel to the 1999 disney animated movie `` tarzan '' , released on june 14 , 2005. the movie tells the story of a young tarzan 's adventure to discover who he really is . it features two new songs from phil collins and takes place during the song `` son of man '' from the first movie . it is the last film in the chronology of walt disney 's version of tarzan . cast . - harrison chad - tarzan - glenn close - kala - brenda grate - terk - harrison fahn - tantor - lance henriksen - kerchak - george carlin - zugor - estelle harris - mama gunda - brad garrett - uto - ron perlman - kago - connor hutcherson - tonka
