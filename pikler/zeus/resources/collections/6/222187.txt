Angolan African dormouse     the angolan african dormouse ( `` graphiurus angolensis '' ) is a species of rodent in the gliridae family . it is endemic to ( lives only in ) angola . its natural habitat is subtropical or tropical dry forests . references . - coetzee , n. , griffin , m. & grubb , p. 2004. graphiurus angolensis . 2006 iucn red list of threatened species . downloaded on 29 july 2007 . - holden , m. e.. 2005. family gliridae . pp . 819-841 `` in '' mammal species of the world a taxonomic and geographic reference . d. e. wilson and d. m. reeder eds . johns hopkins university press , baltimore .
