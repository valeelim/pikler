Mats Sundin     mats johan sundin ( born february 13 , 1971 ) is a swedish retired professional ice hockey player . sundin played a career total of 18 seasons in the national hockey league ( nhl ) . he played for the quebec nordiques , toronto maple leafs , and vancouver canucks . sundin served as the team captain of the toronto maple leafs for 11 seasons . when the 2007–08 nhl season ended , sundin became the longest serving non-north american born captain in nhl history . he last played 41 games with the vancouver canucks in the 2008–09 nhl season before he announced his retirement on september 30 , 2009. on november 12 , 2012 , sundin along with joe sakic , adam oates and pavel bure was inducted into the hockey hall of fame .
