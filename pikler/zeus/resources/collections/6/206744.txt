Hyracotherium     hyracotherium was previously called `` eohippus '' ( meaning `` dawn horse '' ) . it is a eurasian species ancestral to the horse , but not the same animal as `` eohippus '' . `` hyracotherium '' lived during the early eocene epoch , about 5545 million years ago . it lived in the northern hemisphere in asia and europe . the first fossils of this tiny horse were found in england by richard owen in 1841 and named `` hyracotherium '' . the type species , `` h. leporinum '' , is now regarded as a paleothere , ancestral to both horses and brontotheres . `` eohippus '' has been brought back as a name , to describe the early proto-horse found in north america . the main stream of horse evolution occurred on the north american continent .
