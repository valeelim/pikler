VIPoma     a vipoma ( pronounced `` v i p oma '' ) ( also called verner morrison syndrome after the doctors who first noticed it ) , is a tumor that does not happen a lot ( 1 per 10,000,000 per year ) in the pancreas . vipoma makes something called vasoactive intestinal peptide , or vip . it can do bad things to the heart , brain , gut , gallbladder , and more . octreotide ( a somatostatin analogue ) can be used to help symptoms . surgery is the only thing that will make it go away completely .
