Port of Manchester     the port of manchester is in the north west of england . it was created as a customs port on 1 january 1894. this was four months before the opening of the manchester ship canal transformed manchester from a landlocked city into a major sea port . it extended along the whole 36 miles length of the canal , from eastham in the west to manchester in the east , absorbing the port of runcorn , which had been created in 1862. the creation of the new port moved the port of liverpool 's boundary 11 milles to the west . once delivered to the port , goods travelled as far west as warrington , about 20 miles north , as far east as leeds , and up to 100 miles south to birmingham .
