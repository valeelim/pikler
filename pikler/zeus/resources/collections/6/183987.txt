Die Lollipops     die lollipops is a children 's band which formed in germany in the summer of 2000 under the edelkids label , a division of a german company named edel music . according to the label 's website , the primary intended audience for the band is german-speaking children between 4 and 10 years old . their music can be divided into three basic types : - traditional children 's songs , whether native or international ; - covers , or songs in which the melody is borrowed from a popular song ( often from the english-speaking world ) but the lyrics are unrelated ; and - original songs invented by the group . this accounts for the largest number of songs , especially in the more recent releases . examples of famous international children 's songs whose melody has been borrowed and given entirely new lyrics : `` die '' is german for `` the '' ( plural ) ; `` lollipops '' is a loanword from english . the most common german word for lollipop is `` lutscher '' , but germans now also use `` lollipop '' . the band debuted in july 2000 with an album called `` die lollipops '' and have released new cds , videos , and audio books at the rate of about two per year . as of 2005 , their label had sold more than 1.5 million of their cds and videos , of which 8 made it to gold , and one made it to platinum . note that the meanings of the terms platinum and gold are different for different countries : in germany gold signifies 100,000 sales and platinum 200,000 .
