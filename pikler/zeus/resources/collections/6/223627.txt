United National Independence Party     the united national independence party ( unip ) is a political party in zambia . it governed the country from 1964 to 1991. this was under president kenneth kaunda . it was the only party allowed to exist in zambia between 1973 and 1991. the party was founded in october 1959 after the zambian african national congress ( zanc ) was banned . it was first led by mainza chona , because kaunda , who was leader of the zanc , had been imprisoned . kaunda became the party 's leader when he was released in january 1960 .
