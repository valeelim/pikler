Aquatic     aquatic is a term often used in biology and ecology . literally , it means `` watery '' , and refers to both fresh and salt-water . so aquatic plants or animals are those that live in water : ponds , rivers , oceans . aquatic mammals include whales , dolphins , seals , walrus , and also otters , beavers , and other river forms . aquatic birds are even more numerous : ducks , herons , waders , kingfishers , penguins .
