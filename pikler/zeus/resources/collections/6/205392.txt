IFK Göteborg     ifk göteborg is an association football club which plays in sweden for gothenburg . the club won the uefa cup in 1982 and 1987. the club also won the swedish national championship in 1908 , 1910 , 1918 , 1934–35 , 1941–42 , 1957–58 , 1969 , 1982 , 1983 , 1984 , 1987 , 1990 , 1991 , 1993 , 1994 , 1995 , 1996 and 2007. other websites . - ifk göteborg
