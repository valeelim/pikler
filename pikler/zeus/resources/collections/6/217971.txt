Joel Quenneville     joel norman quenneville ( born september 15 , 1958 in windsor , ontario ) is a retired canadian ice hockey player and the current head coach for the chicago blackhawks of the national hockey league ( nhl ) . when he was an assistant coach for the colorado avalanche in 1996 , he won the stanley cup . he became head coach of the blackhawks in 2008 , replacing denis savard . other websites . - profile at hockeydraftcentral.com
