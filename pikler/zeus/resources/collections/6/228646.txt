Gin and Juice     `` gin and juice '' is a song by snoop doggy dogg and produced by dr. dre , the song pay homage to seagrams and tanqueray gin and features daz dillinger . the video for `` gin and juice '' features snoop dogg wearing hockey jerseys from the pittsburgh penguins and the defunct springfield ( ma ) indians of the american hockey league . the music video was also fourth on mtv2 and xxl 's list of the 25 greatest west coast videos .
