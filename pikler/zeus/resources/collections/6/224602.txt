Brown inca     the brown inca , ( `` coeligena wilsoni '' ) , is a species of hummingbird . it is found in colombia and ecuador . unlike most hummingbirds , the brown inca are not brightly colored . instead they are plain brown with a white patch on the shoulder and an iridescent purple throat if seen in the best light . references . - database entry includes justification for why this species is of least concern .
