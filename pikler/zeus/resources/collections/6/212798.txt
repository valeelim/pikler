Do Ab (Iran)    do ab ( iran ) do ab is the name of a rural area situated in the heart of savadkuh county , mazanderan province , iran . it contains five villages . sevatcow station on the mazanderan branch of the trans-iranian railway was built there during the rule of reza pahlavi . currently , the train stops about 1 p.m. every day . it is about 7 kilometers south of pol sefid ( capital of township ) and about 14 kilometers from veresk bridge .
