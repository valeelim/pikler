Self Esteem     `` self esteem '' is a song by the californian punk rock group the offspring . it was released as the second single from the album `` smash '' ( 1994 ) . it was also the first major single to be released by the band . the song was a worldwide hit , reaching # 1 in latvia , norway , and sweden and was one of the most successful singles released by the band . however , according to frontman dexter holland , the song has not been very popular in japan .
