Leah Clark     leah clark ( born december 20 , 1979 in albuquerque , new mexico ) is an american actress . she has provided many voices for japanese anime series and video games . the roles she is most famous for are coby in `` one piece '' , blair in `` soul eater '' , and fuyuki in `` sgt . frog '' other websites . - leah clark at the crystalacids anime voice actor database
