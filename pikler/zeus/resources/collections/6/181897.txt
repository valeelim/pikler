Green Park tube station     green park tube station is a london underground station . it is on the north side of green park , close to the intersection of piccadilly and the pedestrian queen 's walk . it is in travelcard zone 1. the station is served by the piccadilly line , between piccadilly circus and hyde park corner , the victoria line , between victoria and oxford circus , and the jubilee line , between bond street and westminster . london buses routes 8 , 9 , 14 , 19 , 22 and 38 serve the station . other websites . - bbc : on this day - london transport museum photographic archive
