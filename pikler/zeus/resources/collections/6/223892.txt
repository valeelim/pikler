Deluge myth     a deluge myth or flood myth is a mythical story about a flood . usually this flood is sent by a deity to destroy a civilisation as a punishment . the theme can be found in many cultures . well-known examples that are believed to be myths include the story of noah 's ark in the bible , the hindu puranic story of manu , deucalion in greek mythology or utnapishtim in the epic of gilgamesh .
