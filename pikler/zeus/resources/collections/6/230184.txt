Ixia     ixia is a genus of plants in the family iridaceae . all of these plants are herbs with corms . the plants grows in south africa . in total , there are 40-60 species . common names for this kind of flower are `` corn lily '' and `` african corn-lily '' and `` wand flower '' . the leaves look like swords . the flowers look like stars . some species of corn lilies are cultivated as garden plants and houseplants . other websites . - germplasm resources information network : `` ixia '' - itis : `` ixia '' - ncbi : `` ixia ''
