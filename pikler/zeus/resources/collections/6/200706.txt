Dido     dido florian cloud de bounevialle o'malley armstrong ( born 25 december 1971 ) is a female english singer who has released four albums . the singles `` here with me '' , `` hunter '' , `` thank you '' , and `` all you want '' came from `` no angel '' ( 1999 ) . `` white flag '' , `` life for rent '' , `` do n't leave home '' , and `` sand in my shoes '' came from `` life for rent '' ( 2003 ) . `` do n't believe in love '' , `` quiet times '' , and `` it comes and it goes '' came from `` safe trip home '' ( 2008 ) . in 2013 dido 's fourth album `` girl who got away '' was released . she is of frenchdescent . dido is married to rohan gavin .
