Uherské Hradiště     uherské hradiště is a town in the czech republic . about 26,280 people live there ( 2008 ) . it is located in the south-east of the historical region moravia in the zlín region . it was founded on october 15 1257 by the czech king přemysl otakar ii . partner towns . - bridgwater , united kingdom - mayen , germany - trenčín , slovakia other websites . - uherské hradiště webpage
