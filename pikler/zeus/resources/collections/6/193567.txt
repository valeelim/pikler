Two Peoples Bay Nature Reserve     the two people 's bay nature reserve is a small nature reserve on the south west coast of western australia . it is where gilbert 's potoroo , one of the world 's rarest mammals can be found . a bird species thought to have been extinct was also rediscovered at two peoples bay . the area was made a reserve in 1967. it is east of the city of albany , western australia . in 1962 , the noisy scrubbird was rediscovered here . in 1994 , gilbert 's potoroo was found here too . it had n't been seen for more than 120 years .
