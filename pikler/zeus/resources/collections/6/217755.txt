Ryan Newman     ryan joseph newman ( born december 8 , 1977 ) is an american racecar driver who drives the no . 31 caterpillar chevrolet ss for richard childress racing . newman used to drive the # 39 u.s. army/haas automation chevrolet impala for stewart haas racing , and the # 12 alltel dodge for team penske . newman won the 2008 daytona 500 .
