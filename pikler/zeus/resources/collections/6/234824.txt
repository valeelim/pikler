Scouting for Girls     scouting for girls are a british pop rock band formed in london in 2005 by roy stride , greg churchouse and peter ellard . stride met ellard at cub scouts and he then met churchouse at school . in september 2007 scouting for girls released their first album `` scouting for girls '' . it was number one in the uk albums chart for two weeks . current members - roy stride - lead singing , guitar , keyboards ( 2005-present ) - greg churchouse - bass , backing singing ( 2005-present ) - pete ellard - drums , percussion ( 2005-present ) current touring members - peter clements - guitar - matt simmons - keyboards ( 2010-present ) session - martin owen - horn on `` scouting for girls '' - james mcmillan - trumpet on `` scouting for girls '' personnel - andy green - producer of `` scouting for girls '' discography . - `` scouting for girls `` ( 2007 ) - `` everybody wants to be on tv '' ( 2010 )
