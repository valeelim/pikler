Long ton     the long ton is the imperial ton or 'weight ton ' . it is used in the united kingdom and several other commonwealth countries . in their avoirdupois or imperial system of measurements , it is 2240 lbs . many other countries use the metric tonne . the united states uses the short ton . the long ton is equal to or of salt water with a density of 64 lb/ft ( 1.025 g/ml ) . it has some limited use in the united states , for measuring the displacement of ships . it the unit recommended for warships by international agreements between the world wars , for example battleships were limited to 35,000 long tons .
