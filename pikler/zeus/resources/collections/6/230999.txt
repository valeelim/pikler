Alberto Núñez Feijoo     alberto núñez feijoo ( born september 10 , 1961 ) is the current president of galicia . he is the leader of the galician pp , the main conservative political party which defeated the socialists in the elections of 2009 and again in the elections of 2012. feijoo is a strong admirer of manuel fraga , although he does not govern galicia in the same manner . núñez feijoo studied law in santiago de compostela . he is married to the journalist carmen gámir .
