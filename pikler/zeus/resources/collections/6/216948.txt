Barcelona    f.c . barcelona f.c . barcelona is a spanish football club from barcelona , catalonia . they are known for winning the treble ( la liga , the , and the uefa champions league ) all in 1 season , twice , in the 2008-09 season and in the 2014-15 season . their biggest rival is real madrid . matches between f.c . barcelona and real madrid are called `` el clasico '' , which means `` the classic '' in spanish . the team has won many tournaments , which is why barcelona is considered one of the best teams in the world . f.c . barcelona was founded by a group of football players led by joan gamper . their home stadium , camp nou is the largest stadium in europe with a capacity of 99,354. honours . domestic competition . - la liga - copa del rey - copa de la liga - supercopa de españa - copa eva duarte `` ( the forerunner to the supercopa de españa ) '' major european trophies . - european cup/ uefa champions league - uefa cup winners ' cup - inter-cities fairs cup `` ( the tournament before the uefa europa league ) '' - uefa super cup
