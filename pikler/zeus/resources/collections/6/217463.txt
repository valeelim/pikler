Papilioninae     papilioninae is a subfamily of butterflies . it belongs in the family papilionidae . the species are found worldwide . the caterpillars feed on plants in the following families : rosaceae , apiaceae , aristolochiaceae , magnoliaceae , lauraceae , and annonaceae . caterpillars that feed on aristolochiaceae species become poisonous . when they become adults , they are still poisonous . because of this , many butterflies in the families papilionidae and nymphalidae mimic aristolochiaceae feeders . very few adult papilioninae species migrate . they spend the winter as chrysailds . list of genera . - `` atrophaneura '' - `` battus '' - `` byasa '' - `` chilasa '' - `` cressida '' - `` euryades '' - `` eurytides '' - `` graphium '' - `` iphiclides '' - `` lamproptera '' - `` losaria '' - `` meandrusa '' - `` mimoides '' - `` ornithoptera '' - `` pachliopta '' - `` papilio '' - `` parides '' - `` pharmacophagus '' - `` praepapilio '' - `` protesilaus '' - `` protographium '' - `` teinopalpus '' - `` trogonoptera '' - `` troides '' other websites . - papilioninae at wikispecies
