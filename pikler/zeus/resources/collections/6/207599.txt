Hurricane Klaus     hurricane klaus was a weak hurricane of the 1990 atlantic hurricane season . klaus moved very close to islands like antigua and martinique , while it was a hurricane . the remains of klaus moved over the southeastern united states and killed 4 people with tropical storm marco , which was close by . klaus killed 11 people and cause a little bit more than $ 1 million in damage . in the spring of 1991 , france requested that the name klaus be retired , because of what it did in martinique . so the national hurricane center retired the name klaus . the name kyle was used in 1996 , 2002 , and 2008 instead .
