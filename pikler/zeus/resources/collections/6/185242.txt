Sepal     sepals are one of the four basic parts of a flower . they are found below the petals . the sepals are often small and green . some flowers do not have petals . then the sepals may be big and colorful . in some flowers , the petals and sepals look the same . they are both called tepals . all the sepals together are called calyx . the number of sepals is different for different flowers . both petals and sepals surround the reproductive parts inside the flower . the term for sepals plus petals is the perianth . the main job of the sepals is to keep the parts of the flower safe before it opens.they are usually green and leaf-like
