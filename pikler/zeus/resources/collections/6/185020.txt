Vrilissia     vrilissia ( greek : `` βριλήσσια '' ) is a small greek city . it is near athens in attica . almost 40,000 people live there . it is located at the northeastern edge of the metropolis of athens . the city is accessed by attiki odos , the suburban railway and metro transit . vrilissia is an area with lots of squares and parks . it has a small forested area near the mountain of pendeli . vrilissia is a twin city of naples , italy and ottweiler in germany . in ottweiler , a main bridge is named after the greek city . other websites . - municipality site ( in greek ) - vrilissia informational portal
