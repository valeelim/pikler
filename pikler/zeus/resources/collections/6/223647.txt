Cerapod     the cerapods are a sub-order of the ornithischia . they are all herbivorous dinosaurs . they have thicker enamel on the inside of their lower teeth . the teeth wear unevenly , and this allows them to browse on tough vegetation . they are divided into three groups : - ornithopoda : including the duck-billed dinosaurs . - pachycephalosauria : the thick-headed dinosaurs . - ceratopsia : the horned dinasaurs .
