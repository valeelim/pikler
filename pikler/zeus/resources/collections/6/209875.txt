Safety (EP)    safety ( ep ) safety is an extended play album by english band coldplay . it was the band 's first public release . it was recorded over a weekend during may 1998 , and was intended as a demo for record companies . it was financed by the band and their manager phil harvey for around £200 . track list . 1 . `` bigger stronger '' – 4:49 1 . `` no more keeping my feet on the ground '' – 4:31 1 . `` such a rush '' – 4:57
