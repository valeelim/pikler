Hunger     hunger is the feeling of wanting to eat . the opposite of hunger is called `` satiety '' or `` fullness '' . the feeling is started in the hypothalamus , and hormones are released from the liver . people can survive weeks without eating , but they will start to feel hungry after they have not eaten for a few hours . hunger is generally considered quite uncomfortable . people who have eaten food usually will feel less hungry , or not hungry at all . people can live longer without food than without water . hunger is also used to refer to people who generally have little to eat , and are therefore often hungry .
