Character actor     a character actor is an actor who plays a particular kind of character in a play , movie , television program , opera or ballet . the character actor is not the main part . he is not the lover or hero . he has a character which helps to support the main characters and therefore the story . character roles may be roles such as : the faithful servant , the maid , the villain , the mother-in-law , the priest etc . they may or may not be comic characters . for example , in `` the wizard of oz '' the main character is dorothy . the scarecrow , the lion and the tin man are character roles . some actors or singers prefer character roles to main roles . it may be because this kind or role suits their personality or their singing voice . some people nearly always play the same kind of character role . we say that people like that are `` typecast '' . some actors are happy to remain typecast . others may wish to broaden their repertoire and take on different roles . related pages . - title role
