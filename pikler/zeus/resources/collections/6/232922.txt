Theravada     theravada is the oldest-surviving denomination of buddhism . it was founded in nepal . it is relatively conservative , and generally closest to early buddhist practice . for many centuries it has been the main religion of sri lanka ( now about 70 % of the population ) and most of continental southeast asia ( cambodia , laos , myanmar , and thailand ) . theravada is also practiced by minorities in parts of southwest china ( by the shan and tai ethnic groups ) , vietnam ( by the khmer krom ) , bangladesh ( by the ethnic groups of baruas , chakma , and magh ) , malaysia and indonesia , while recently gaining popularity in singapore and the western world . today theravada buddhists , who are also known as theravadins , number over 100 million worldwide ; in recent decades theravada has begun to take root in the west and in the buddhist revival in nepal .
