Florida State Road 540     state road 540 is an east-west road in central florida , serving polk county . it runs from us 27 to the south side of the city of lakeland . sr 540 also runs along the entrance to cypress gardens . it is a major road along the south side of winter haven , where it is known as cypress gardens boulevard , and an important link between winter haven and lakeland , the polk parkway , and also interstate 4 and the tampa bay area . sr 540 is known by the following names : - cypress gardens blvd . ( us 27 – us 17 ) - 4th st. ( cosigned with us 17 ) - winter lake rd . ( us 17 – us 98 ) - bartow rd . ( cosigned with us 98 ) - clubhouse rd . ( us 98 – sr 37b ) state road 540a is a set of traffic bypasses for the termini of sr 540 .
