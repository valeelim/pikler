Phil Wachsmann     phil wachsmann ( born august 5 , 1944 ) is an african `` avant-garde '' jazz/jazz fusion violinist born in kampala , uganda , probably better known for having founded his own group chamberpot . he has worked with many musicians in the free jazz idiom , including tony oxley , fred van hove , barry guy , derek bailey and paul rutherford , among many others . wachsmann is especially known for playing within the electronica idiom .
