Rubeus Hagrid     rubeus hagrid is a teacher and groundskeeper at hogwarts , friend of harry , ron , and hermione . he is also a `` half-giant . '' his father was not a giant , whereas his mother , fridwulfa , who died whilst he was young , was a giantess . he has a half-brother named grawp . in the movies , hagrid is played by robbie coltrane . martin playfield appears as a young hagrid in `` harry potter and the chamber of secrets '' . hagrid was expelled from hogwarts after the ministry of magic thought he had opened the chamber of secrets , but he had been framed by tom riddle and so was allowed to remain at the school as gamekeeper . the government broke his wand and banned him from doing magic when he was expelled , but he keeps pieces of the wand in his pink umbrella . hagrid is also friends with madame maxime , it is not known if they were together in a romantic way . hagrid keeps several pets .
