Pinochle     pinochle ( pronounced pea-nukkle ) is a card game . it is played with four players on two teams . it can be played with one or two special pinochle decks . a pinochle deck has 48 cards , two each of 9 , 10 , j , q , k , and a in each suit . a pinochle game has four hands . the team with the highest score at the end of four hands wins . the first part of a hand is melding . various combos of cards can be melded . ( for example , having two js of diamonds and two qs of spades is worth 300 points ) . the team that bids the highest is allowed to call , the suit that will count higher in melds and take tricks . the second part of a hand is the tricks . each player throws in a card . the player with the highest trump card , or highest card of the suit led , takes the trick . each k , 10 , or a taken in tricks is worth 10 points . other websites . - belote play the french pinochle
