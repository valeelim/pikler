Amygdaloideae     amygdaloideae ( incorrectly called prunaceae ) is a subfamily of flowering plants . this subfamily is placed within the family rosaceae . since 2011 there has been a big change in how the name of this subfamily is used . before 2011 , the only plants in this subfamily were the stone fruit such as plums , peaches , almonds . now the subfamily includes all of family rosaceae except subfamily rosoideae and subfamily dryadoideae . commercially important members of the amygdaloideae now include plum , apple , spiraea , and many others .
