Casey Johnson     casey johnson ( september 24 , 1979 - january 4 , 2010 ) was an american socialite and heiress to the johnson & johnson empire . her father was businessman woody johnson . she was openly bisexual and was engaged to model and reality television star tila tequila in december 2009. johnson was found dead in her los angeles home on january 4 , 2010. she died of diabetes .
