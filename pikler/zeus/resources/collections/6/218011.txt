Ipatinga     ipatinga is a brazilian city in the vale do aço metropolitan area , state of minas gerais . its population in 2009 was 244.508 inhabitants . its area is 165.509 km² . the city was founded in april 29 , 1964. it 's limited with caratinga , coronel fabriciano , mesquita , santana do paraíso and timóteo . other websites . - official site of ipatinga
