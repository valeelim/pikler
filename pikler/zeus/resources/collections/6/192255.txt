Dig Dug     dig dug is a game made by namco in 1982. a well known game based on a simple concept . it was also released as a video game on many consoles . `` dig dug '' was rated the sixth most popular coin-operated video game of all time by the killer list of video games website . it has been said that the music for the game show `` starcade '' was taken from the music for `` dig dug '' . in the video for the song `` we are all made of stars '' from electronic musician moby , a scene is shown where a person of moby himself , dressed as an astronaut , is in the middle of a `` dig dug '' game .
