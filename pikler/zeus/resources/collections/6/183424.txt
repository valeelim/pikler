King's Cross station    king 's cross station king 's cross station is an important railway station in london . it is also the home of the king 's cross st. pancras tube station , which it shares with the nearby st pancras station . king 's cross railway station first opened in 1852. plans to make the station were made as early as 1848 by george turnbull . a detailed design was made by lewis cubitt . the station is located on the north-eastern edge of central london , where the a501 euston road and york way cross . it is in the kings cross district , within the london borough of camden , and on the border of the london borough of islington . king 's cross forms the southern terminus of the east coast main line , one of the uk 's major railway stations . it was the major terminus of the lner : the london and north-eastern railway ltd. immediately to its west is st pancras station , the terminus for international eurostar trains .
