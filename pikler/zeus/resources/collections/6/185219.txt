Malcolm Gladwell     malcolm gladwell ( born september 3 , 1963 ) is a british-canadian journalist , author , and pop sociologist , living in new york city . he has written for `` the new yorker '' since 1996. he is well known as the author of the books , `` the tipping point '' ( 2000 ) , `` blink '' ( 2005 ) , and `` outliers '' ( 2008 ) . gladwell was born in fareham , hampshire to a jamaican mother and english father . he moved to elmira , ontario at age six . other websites . - personal website - ted talks : malcolm gladwell on spaghetti sauce at ted in 2004 - malcolm gladwell 's `` new yorker '' bio
