Come Away with Me     come away with me is the first album by american jazz singer norah jones . it was released on february 26 , 2002 on blue note records . the main genre is considered contemporary jazz . the album is actually contemporary jazz with some country , folk music , and pop . some jazz musicians do not think that the album is actually jazz , because of the changes norah jones made to some of the jazz songs she covered . `` come away with me '' reached number 1 on the billboard 200 chart and won eight grammy awards . it sold over 10 million copies in the first three years of its release . it is norah jones ' best-selling album , and the best-selling jazz album of all time . grammy awards other websites . - `` come away with me '' at discogs - `` come away with me '' at metacritic - album review at `` all about jazz ''
