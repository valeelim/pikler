Joey Logano     joseph thomas logano ( born may 27 , 1990 ) is an american racecar driver who drives the no . 22 shell / pennzoil ford fusion in the sprint cup series for penske racing . until the start of the 2013 season , logano drove the # 20 home depot toyota camry for joe gibbs racing . logano won the 2009 rookie of the year award , and with his win at the new hampshire motor speedway became the youngest drive to win a race in nascar history . he also won the 2012 pocono 400 presented by # nascar at pocono raceway from the pole , ending a 31 race losing streak for pole winners .
