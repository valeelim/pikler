Colin Lloyd     colin lloyd ( born 7 august 1973 in colchester , essex ) , nicknamed `` jaws '' , is an english professional darts player . he is a player on the professional darts corporation circuit . he used to be a world champion and he has won two major titles in the pdc . he appeared in the dartsworld in 1999. in 2000 , he had his first time on television . in 2002 , he recorded his first major success when he lost in the semi-finals of the pdc world championships against peter manley . in 2003 he reached the semifinals of the las vegas desert classics , 2004 , he won the sky bet world grand prix 2005 , the world matchplay in blackpool . in 2005 he was pitted against phil taylor in the finale . from 2004/2005 until the beginning of 2006 he was the no . 1 ranked player in the pdc . other websites . - darts1-playerprofil info-sites - colinjawslloyd.com official website
