Dirty Harry     dirty harry is a 1971 american crime movie . it was produced and directed by don siegel . it was the first in the dirty harry series . clint eastwood plays the main role . eastwood acts as a san francisco police department inspector `` dirty '' harry callaghan , trying to catch a serial killer . dirty harry was followed by four sequels : `` magnum force '' in 1973 , `` the enforcer '' in 1976 , `` sudden impact '' in 1983 ( directed by eastwood ) , and `` the dead pool '' in 1988 .
