Christopher Paolini     christopher paolini ( born november 17 , 1983 in southern california ) is an american writer . he is best known as the writer of the inheritance cycle , which has the books `` eragon '' , `` eldest '' , `` brisingr '' , and the fourth book . he lives in paradise valley , montana . christopher paolini is famous for publishing his book , eragon when he was only seventeen years old . he began writing the book when he was fifteen . his second book , eldest , was released in 2005. his third book , brisingr , was released in 2008. the final book of the inheritance cycle , inheritance , was released on 8 november , 2011 .
