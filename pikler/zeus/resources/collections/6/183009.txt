2008     2008 ( mmviii ) was a leap year starting on tuesday of the christian or common era , in the gregorian calendar . it was the 2008th year of the common era , or of anno domini ; the 8th year of the 3rd millennium and of the 21st century ; and the 9th of the 2000s . events . january . - january 1 – cyprus and malta adopt the euro . - january 1 – a suicide bombing occurs in zayouna , baghdad , killing over 25 people during a funeral over the deaths from the preceding attack . - january 2 – the price of petroleum hits $ 100 per barrel for the first time . - january 3 – a car bomb detonates , killing at least 4 and injuring 68 , in diyarbakır , turkey . police blame kurdish rebels . - january 8 – an attempted assassination of maldivian president maumoon abdul gayoom is thwarted after a boy scout grabs the attacker 's knife . the boy scout is injured , but after a scuffle police arrest the attacker . - january 12 – a macedonian army mil mi-17 helicopter crashes in thick fog southeast of skopje , killing all 11 military personnel on board .
