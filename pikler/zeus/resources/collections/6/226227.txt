Emerson Fittipaldi     emerson fittipaldi ( born december 12 , 1946 in são paulo , brazil ) is a highly successful brazilian automobile racing driver . he has won championships in both formula one , and cart . fittipaldi won the indianapolis 500 twice . emerson fittipaldi was born in são paulo , brazil . he is the younger son of prominent italian-brazilian motorsports journalist and radio commentator wilson fittipaldi sr and his wife józefa `` juzy '' wojciechowska , an immigrant from saint petersburg , russia and of polish and russian descent .
