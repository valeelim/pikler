Cape     a cape can mean any outer piece of clothing that has no sleeves . however , it usually it is long and covers only the back half of the wearer , it fastens around the neck . capes were common in medieval europe , especially when combined with a hood . capes have had periodic returns to fashion . roman catholic clergy wear a type of cape known as a ferraiolo , which is worn for formal events . capes are often used as rain wear in various military units and police forces . in modern times , comic-book super-heroes , such as superman and batman , are often shown wearing capes .
