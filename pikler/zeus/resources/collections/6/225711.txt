Violet-fronted brilliant     the violet-fronted brilliant ( `` heliodoxa leadbeateri '' ) is a species of hummingbird . it is found in bolivia , colombia , ecuador , peru , and venezuela . its natural habitat is subtropical or tropical moist montane forests . references . - birdlife international 2004. heliodoxa leadbeateri . 2006 iucn red list of threatened species . downloaded on 10 july 2007 .
