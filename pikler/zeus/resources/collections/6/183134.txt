Multiple birth     multiple birth is when one woman gives birth to more than one child at the same time . depending on the number of children born , it can be called twins ( two ) , triplets ( three ) , quadruplets ( four ) , and so on . there have been cases known in which woman gave birth to eight children at once . these have a higher risk of dying . the chance of multiple birth grows when artificial insemination is used . it is therefore used in china to outsmart the one-child policy .
