University of Heidelberg     the university of heidelberg is one of the oldest universities of europe and the oldest in germany . it is in heidelberg , germany . it was founded on 23 october 1385. at the beginning , there were only the four traditional faculties . in 1890 , the natural science got their own faculty . later after a re-organisation , the university has 12 faculties . the university of heidelberg is also known as ruprecht karl university of heidelberg , after the german prince rupert i .
