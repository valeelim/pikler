World War I     world war i ( or the first world war ) was a global war which began on july 28 , 1914 and lasted until november 11 , 1918. the war lasted exactly four years , three months and 14 days . most of the fighting was in europe , but soldiers from many other countries took part , and it changed the colonial empires of the european powers . before world war ii began in 1939 , world war i was called the great war , the world war or the 'war to end all wars ' . 135 countries took part in world war i , and more than 9 million people died while fighting . world war i was fought by most of the countries of europe . the actual fighting was on many different fronts . the western front was where most of the fighting between germany and the allies happened . most of the fighting here was trench warfare . the eastern front was fought in central and eastern europe and was one of the main places where world war i took place . the fighting on the eastern front was not trench warfare but mobile warfare due to the size of the front . much fighting happened in the middle eastern front and the italian front . fighting also took place in africa , china , and at sea as well as in the air .
