Peter Buck     peter lawrence buck ( born december 6 , 1956 in berkeley , california ) is an american guitarist and songwriter . he is best known for being a member of the alternative rock band r.e.m.. in 2007 peter buck , along with the other three members of r.e.m. , was inducted into the rock and roll hall of fame . his main instrument that he has used for his entire music career is a back rickenbacker 360 electric guitar . on september 9 , 2008 , after a concert in helsinki , his famous black guitar was stolen . on september 18 , 2008 it was given back to him . the name of the person who returned it is not known .
