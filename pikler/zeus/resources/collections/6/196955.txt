Mathematics Subject Classification     the mathematics subject classification ( msc ) system is an alphanumeric ordering system for categorizing or arranging mathematical topics , similar to how a library arranges or orders their books on the shelves . the system was invented by the american mathematical society based on two major mathematical publishers : mathematical reviews and zentralblatt math . the msc system is used by many journals of mathematics which ask authors to list the msc subject codes in their papers when submitted for publication . other websites . - mathematics subject classification 2010
