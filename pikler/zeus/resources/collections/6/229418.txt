Online Computer Library Center     oclc online computer library center , inc. is library service and research organization . it helps people find more information and lessen library costs . it was incorporated on july 6 , 1967 as the non-for-profit ohio college library center . 72,000 libraries in 86 countries use oclc services to find and save library materials . the organization was begun by fred kilgour . its head office is in dublin , ohio , u.s .
