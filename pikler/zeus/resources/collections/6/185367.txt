New Mills     new mills is a town in derbyshire , england . it is eight miles from stockport . it is by the rivers goyt and sett . it is very near the county of cheshire . it has a population of 10,000. new mills most known employer is swizzels matlow , who make children 's sweets . the company transferred to new mills from london during the blitz . famous brands include `` parma violets '' , `` refresher '' chews , `` drumstick '' lollies and - perhaps most famously - `` love hearts ''
