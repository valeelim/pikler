Apocalypse     an apocalypse is a revelation : seeing something which has been hidden . it comes from the greek word , `` apokálypsis '' , which means `` lifting of the veil '' , or finding out something secret . often this secret is discovered in a dream or a vision . in religion , the word describes armageddon or the end of the world . this might have come from the words , `` apokalupsis eschaton '' , meaning all the secrets will be discovered `` at the end of the æon , or age '' . in christianity `` the apocalypse of john '' is the book of revelation , the last book of the bible . it describes the end of the world , and the second coming of jesus christ .
