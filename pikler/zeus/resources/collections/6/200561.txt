Papyrus 46     papyrus 46 ( also referred to as simply formula_1 ) is one of the oldest new testament manuscripts known to exist . it was probably created between the year 175 and 225. some leaves are part of the chester beatty biblical papyri collection , and others are in the papyri collection of the university of michigan . according to the website bible research , it contains ( in order ) `` the last eight chapters of romans ; all of hebrews ; virtually all of 1st corinthians and 2nd corinthians ; all of ephesians , galatians , philippians , colossians ; and two chapters of thessalonians . all of the leaves have lost some lines at the bottom through deterioration .
