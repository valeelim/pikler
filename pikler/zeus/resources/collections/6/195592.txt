Hurricane Iwa     hurricane iwa was a hurricane that hit the u.s. state of hawaii . iwa was the 23rd tropical storm of the 1982 pacific hurricane season . the hurricane formed on november 1925 , and passed near kauai on november 24. it died out the next day . the storm caused very bad damage on the islands of niihau , kauai , and oahu . some wind gusts reached 100 mph ( 160 km/h ) , and waves were over 30 feet ( 9 m ) high . iwa damaged or destroyed 2,345 buildings and left 500 people without homes . the storm caused four deaths .
