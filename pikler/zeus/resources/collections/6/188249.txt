Barbican tube station     barbican is a london underground station serving the barbican centre in the city of london , england . it is on the circle , hammersmith & city and metropolitan lines . it is between farringdon and moorgate . it is in travelcard zone 1. first capital connect trains used to stop at this station on their way to moorgate station , but this stopped in march 2009. the outbound service calling here was abandoned many years before . other websites . - babican tube station on `` all in london '' - map of the tube
