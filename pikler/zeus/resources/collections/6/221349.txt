Principal Charming     `` principal charming '' is the fourteenth episode of the second season of `` the simpsons '' . it first aired on the fox network in the united states on february 14 , 1991. in this episode , marge 's sister `` selma '' is looking for a husband , so marge tells homer to help her find one . things go wrong , however , when homer invites `` principal skinner '' over for dinner and skinner instead falls for selma 's twin sister patty .
