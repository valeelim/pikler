Spiekeroog     spiekeroog is one of the east frisian islands , off the north sea coast of germany . it is between langeoog to its west and wangerooge to its east . the island belongs to the municipality of the district of wittmund , in lower saxony , germany . the only village on the island is also called spiekeroog . spiekeroog covers an area of 18.25 kmÂ² , the shortest distance to the land is 5.7 km . the island is - with the exception of fire and rescue vehicles - free of cars . a daily ferry service with the harbour of neuharlingersiel connects the island with the german mainland .
