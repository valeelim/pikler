Nagqu Prefecture     nagqu prefecture ( also naqu or nagchu ) ; nàqū dìqū ) is the largest prefecture of the tibet autonomous region . in 2001 nagqu had a population of 363,000 and an area of 450,000 km² . the prefecture is divided into eleven different counties . nagchu county contains the capital of nagqu . the other counties are lhari county , biru county , nyainrong county , amdo county , xainza county , sog county , baingoin county , baqên county , nyima county and shuanghu special district . nearly half of the land in tibet is in this area .
