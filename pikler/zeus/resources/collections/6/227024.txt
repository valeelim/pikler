2004 American League Championship Series     the 2004 american league championship series ( alcs ) was a best-of-seven playoff series in the 2004 major league baseball season . it featured the new york yankees and the boston red sox . the red sox beat the yankees in seven games . it was a rematch of the 2003 alcs . the yankees won the first three games of the series . the red sox then won the next four to win the series . this was the first time a team won a best-of-seven playoff series after losing the first three games . the red sox would go on to win the world series against the st. louis cardinals in four games . it was the red sox 's first world series title since 1918 .
