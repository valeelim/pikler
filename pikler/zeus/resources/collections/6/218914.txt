Parides montezuma     montezuma 's cattleheart ( `` parides montezuma '' ) is a species of butterfly . it belongs in the family papilionidae . it is found in north america and south america . the upper side of the wings is black . the hind wing ( bottom wing ) has one row of red crescents along the edge . the underside of the wings is black . the hind wing has a row of red crescents like the ones on the upper side . here is a list of host plants used by montezuma 's cattleheart . ( the host plant is the plant that the caterpillar feeds on . ) : - `` aristolochia acanthophylla '' - `` aristolochia foetida '' - jalisco dutchman 's pipe - `` aristolochia grandiflora '' - pelican flower - `` aristolochia micrantha '' - `` aristolochia orbicularis '' other websites . - larval food plant information sorted by plant
