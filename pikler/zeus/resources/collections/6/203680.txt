Foster's Lager    foster 's lager foster 's is a full strength australian beer . it was first brewed by the brothers , w.m . foster and r.r . foster , in 1887. it is a european style lager , with 4.9 % alcohol . more than 100 million cases of foster 's lager are sold around the world each year , which makes it australia 's biggest selling beer . it is brewed in nine countries using 20 breweries .
