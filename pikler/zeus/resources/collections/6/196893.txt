Ekwerazu     ekwerazu ( or ekwereazu ) is a town in southeastern nigeria . it is in the imo state , in the local government area of ahiazu mbaise . it is made up of the villages of eleke na owasi , eziama , ihite afo ukwu , mpam , oboohia , ogbo , opara na dim , umuchieze , umuhu ocha , umuokirika and okirika nweke . okirika nweke is made up of umuevu and akpim which previously were part of umuokirika . most development is from self-help community efforts . the people and their leaders usually meet every eight days at the eke okwe market square to talk about matters affecting the community .
