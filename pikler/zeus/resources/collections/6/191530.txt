Staple food     staple food are food items that can be stored easily and eaten throughout the year . different kinds of staple foods are used in different parts of the world . the term means the common basis of the everyday diet in a place . potatoes and rice are the most usual examples , but bread is `` not '' a staple food because it is a manufactured food which can not be stored . of course , it can always be made , since wheat `` is '' a staple food . fruit is not a staple because it usually grows at a certain time of year . meat is not a staple because it may not be available , or is expensive to buy . green vegetables may be a staple in some countries , but not others . foods which need special facilities , such as refrigeration are not staple foods . here are some examples of staple foods : - maize ( corn ) - rice - wheat - potatoes - cassava - soybeans - sweet potatos - yams - sorghum - plantain - barley - lentils
