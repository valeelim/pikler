Thurberiphaga diffusa     thurberiphaga diffusa is a north american moth . it is in the family noctuidae . `` thurberiphaga diffusa '' is found only in southern arizona . this moth has one brood per year . it is seen from july to september . the caterpillar bores into the stem of the host plant . it is a pinkish color . it is covered with rough hairs . `` t. diffusa '' 's only host plant is wild cotton ( `` gossypium thurberi '' ) .
