Raven (wrestler)    raven ( wrestler ) scott anthony levy ( born september 8 , 1964 ) , better known by his ring name raven , is an american wrestling producer , wrestling writer , and occasional author , actor and professional wrestler . he currently works for different independent promotions . during his career he has held multiple championships across the world . he is a three-time world champion . he won the ecw world heavyweight championship twice and the nwa world heavyweight championship once . he also held the wcw united states championship once . he is the record holder for most reigns as wwf/e hardcore champion with 39 reigns .
