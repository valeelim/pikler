Dera Ghazi Khan Division     dera ghazi khan division ( ) was an administrative division of the punjab province of pakistan , until the reforms of 2000 abolished the third tier of government . during british rule dera ghazi khan division was a district of multan division , after independence from britain dera ghazi khan was made into a division in its own right until abolition in 2000.it contained the following districts : dera ghazi khan , muzaffargarh , rajanpur , layyah .
