Tony Mowbray     anthony mark `` tony '' mowbray ( born 22 november 1963 ) is an english former professional football player and the current manager of coventry city . mowbray played for middlesbrough , celtic and ipswich town as a defender . mowbray was born in saltburn-by-the-sea , north riding of yorkshire . he previously managed west bromwich albion , hibernian and celtic .
