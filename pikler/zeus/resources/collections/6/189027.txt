Tikrit     tikrit is a city in salahadin province of iraq where at least 80 % majority is sunni . saddam hussein , leader of iraq through april 2003 was born there ; he considered it his hometown . he was actually born in a village on the outskirts of tikrit , however in a part of tikrit municipality . december 30 2008 , us soldier killed in tikrit , retaliation for saddam 's death on december 30 2006 , 2 years anniversary shooting !
