Hurricane Jeanne     hurricane jeanne was the tenth named storm , the seventh hurricane , and the fifth major hurricane of the 2004 atlantic hurricane season . hurricane jeanne was a deadly storm , especially to haiti . the storm cause 3,035 deaths , about 3,006 were in haiti alone . hurricane jeanne was also a costly hurricane . with $ 7 billion in damage caused , it is the 13th costliest hurricane in u.s. history . in florida the hurricane made landfall in the same area as hurricane frances did only two weeks before . in spring 2005 the name jeanne was retired because of the severe damage and the high number of deaths . jeanne was replaced by julia in 2010 .
