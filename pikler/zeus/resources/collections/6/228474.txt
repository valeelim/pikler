Bailiff     a bailiff is a governor , a legal officer for the government . there are many different kinds of bailiffs , and they have different jobs . the word comes from the latin word , `` baiulivus '' . the word was first used in england for the king 's officers , such as sheriffs and mayors . it was also the title for the chief officer of a hundred . the sheriff looked after an area called his bailiwick . bailiff is kept as a title by the chief magistrates of various towns and the keepers of royal castles , such as the high bailiff of westminster , and the bailiff of dover castle . in a medieval manor , a bailiff was in charge of the farming .
