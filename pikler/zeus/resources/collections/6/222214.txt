I Married Marge     `` i married marge '' is the twelfth episode of `` the simpsons '' third season . it first started on the fox network in the united states on december 26 , 1991. in this episode , marge worries that she may yet again be pregnant and drives to `` dr. hibbert 's '' office . while waiting , homer begins to tell bart , lisa , and maggie the story of how he and marge got married and how bart was born .
