Shenandoah Valley Academy     shenandoah valley academy ( sva ) is a school in the town of new market , virginia . the school is run by the adventist church and has high school age boys and girls . some students live on campus in the dorms . other students come on school days as day students . sva has three diploma programs . it has a performing arts department with five music groups . it has an athletics department which coordinates teams to play within the school , and to play other schools .
