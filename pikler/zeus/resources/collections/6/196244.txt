Respect Party     the respect party , the unity coalition , is a far-left political party in the united kingdom . it was started on 25 january 2004 in london . its name is an acronym standing for respect , social equality , socialism , peace , environmentalism , community , and trade union . it is often simply called `` respect '' . the respect party , as a given , tries to put forward policies of respect . their first member of parliament was george galloway . he is the party 's leader .
