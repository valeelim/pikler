Ruben Bierer     ruben bierer ( dr. `` rubin bierer '' , often : `` reuben bierer '' ; * about 1845 in lviv ; † 1931 ibid . ) was a doctor in sofia and one of the first pioneers of the jewish nationalism and one of the first active zionists in galicia and bulgaria , adherent of hovevei zion in galicia , austria and bulgaria . in 1867 he was in lviv a co-founder of the first jewish-political association of austria-hungary , `` shomer israel '' . as an opponent against polish assimilation of the jews he established for the 1873 elections of the reichsrat a jewish-ruthenian electoral alliance . in 1879 he separated from the `` shomer israel '' and went to vienna for the purpose of medical studies where he – together with peretz smolenskin – found the jewish-political association `` ahavath zion '' ( for the reason of colonisation of the holy land ) . in 1882 he was a cofounder of the viennese `` kadima '' studentenverbindung . in 1883 he returned to lviv where he founded the first jewish-national association of the austrian east , `` miqra kodesh '' .
