Bear worship     bear worship ( also known as the bear cult or arctolatry ) is the religious practice of the worshiping of bears . many north american and north eurasian ethnic arctic religions include the worship of bears in their beliefs . these include the sami , nivkhs , ainu , and pre-christian finns . there are also a number of deities from celtic gaul and britain similar to the bear . the bear is also featured on many totems throughout northern cultures that carve them . bear worship may have been practiced as far back as the middle palaeolithic time period amongst neanderthal societies .
