Gertrude Baines     gertrude baines ( april 6 , 1894 – september 11 , 2009 ) was an american supercentenarian . she was believed to be the oldest person in the world from january 2 , 2009 until own death on september 11 , 2009. she was the last person known to have been born in 1894 and the oldest ever person to have been born in that year . gertrude baines married sam conley at a very young age and had one daughter who died of typhoid fever at the age of 18. baines was known to have been living in hartford , connecticut before moving to ohio , where she worked as a maid at the ohio state university . she later moved to california , where she lived on her own until age 105. she spent her final years at the western convalescent center in jefferson park , los angeles . longevity . - march 2 , 2006 – marion higgins died , baines became the oldest person in california . - september 9 , 2006 – baines moved into gerontology research group list for guinness world records . - october 8 , 2007 – baines moved into the top 100 oldest people to have ever lived .
