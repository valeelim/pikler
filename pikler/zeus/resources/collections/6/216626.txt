Tomáš Kopecký     tomáš kopecký ( pronounced `` koh-peht-skee '' ; born february 5 , 1982 in ilava , czechoslovakia ) is a professional slovakian ice hockey player that currently plays as a left winger for the florida panthers of the national hockey league ( nhl ) . he previously played four seasons with the detroit red wings and played 2 seasons with the chicago blackhawks . he is married and has two sons named jakub and tobias . in 2010 , he won the stanley cup with the chicago blackhawks making him a two-time stanley cup champion . awards . - he won the stanley cup in 2008 .
