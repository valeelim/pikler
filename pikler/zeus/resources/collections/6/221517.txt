Stark Raving Dad     `` stark raving dad '' is the season premiere episode of the simpsons third season . it first started on the fox network in the united states on september 19 , 1991. in this episode , homer is made to wear a pink shirt to work one day and is mistaken for a `` free thinking anarchist '' and sent to a `` mental institution . '' he 's in a room with a large white man who acts as if he were michael jackson .
