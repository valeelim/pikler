Tropical Storm Mekkhala (2008)    tropical storm mekkhala ( 2008 ) tropical storm mekkhala ( internationally called : 0816 , jtwc designation : 20w ) was the 16th tropical storm of the 2008 pacific typhoon season by the japan meteorological agency who are the regional specialized meteorological centre for the north western pacific ocean . the joint typhoon warning center ( jtwc ) have also recognised mekkhala as the 20th tropical depression , and the 18th tropical storm of the 2008 pacific typhoon season . tropical storm mekkhala formed off the coast of china on september 25 , 2008 .
