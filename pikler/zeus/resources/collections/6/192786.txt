Airedale Terrier     the airedale terrier ( often shortened to `` airedale '' ) is a breed of the terrier type , that first came from airedale , a geographic area in yorkshire , england . it traditionally was called the `` king of terriers '' because before the creation of the black russian terrier , the airedale was largest of the terrier breeds . the breed has also been called the waterside terrier , because it was bred to hunt otters in and around the valleys of the river aire in airedale . in england this breed has also been used as a police dog . other websites . - planet airedale the global airedale terrier club , with hundreds of pictures of airedale terriers from around the world .
