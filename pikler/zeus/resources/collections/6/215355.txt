Rockford IceHogs     the rockford icehogs are an american professional ice hockey team that was founded in 2007 they play in the american hockey league and are the farm team of the chicago blackhawks . they play in rockford , illinois at the rockford metrocentre . they were a member of the united hockey league from 1999 to 2007. other websites . - rockford icehogs official site - the internet hockey database - rockford icehogs ( ahl ) - the internet hockey database - rockford icehogs ( uhl ) - rockford icehogs official twitter
