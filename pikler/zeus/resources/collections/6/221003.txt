Tim Curry     timothy james `` tim '' curry ( 19 april 1946 in grappenhall , cheshire , england ) , is an english actor , voice actor , singer and composer . he is well known for his roles in `` the rocky horror picture show '' , `` clue '' , `` it '' , `` '' and `` the three musketeers '' . he has released three albums . on may 24 , 2013 , it was reported that curry had had a massive stroke in his los angeles , california home . he later recovered from the stroke . later curry 's agent stated that the stroke had been in july of 2012 and that curry had been going to physical therapy .
