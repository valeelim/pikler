Ialomiţa River     the ialomiţa river ( `` râul ialomiţa '' in romanian ) is a river in the southern part of romania . it comes up from the bucegi mountains of the carpathians and flows into the danube . the upper part of the river is sometimes called valea obârşiei river or obârşia ialomiţei river . cities . - târgovişte - slobozia tributaries . - prahova river lakes and dams . - lake scropoasa
