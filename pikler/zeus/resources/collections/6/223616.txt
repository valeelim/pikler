Lu Xun     lu xun ( ) , ( september 25 , 1881 – october 19 , 1936 ) , was a chinese writer . his real name is zhou shuren ( 周树人 ) , as lu xun is his pen name . he was born in shaoxing of zhejiang province . he had a lot of works , for example , `` the true story of ah q '' , `` medicine '' , and so on .
