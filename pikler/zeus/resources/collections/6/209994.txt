Cygnus X-1     cygnus x-1 ( abbreviated cyg x-1 ) is a well-known galactic x-ray source . it is thought to be a black hole in the constellation cygnus . it was discovered in 1964 during a rocket flight , and is one of the strongest x-ray sources seen from earth . cygnus x-1 was the first x-ray source accepted as a black hole . it is among the most studied astronomical objects in its class . the compact object is estimated to have a mass about 14.8 times the mass of the sun . it is too small to be any known kind of normal star , or other likely object besides a black hole . if so , the radius of its event horizon is about .
