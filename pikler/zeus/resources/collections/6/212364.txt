Tony Rice     david anthony `` tony '' rice ( born june 8 , 1951 ) is an american musician and songwriter . he was born in danville , virginia but grew up in los angeles , california . he is famous for his skill at playing acoustic guitar . his music can be bluegrass or country or folk . he has played in music groups with j. d. crowe , norman blake , and david grisman , jerry garcia and alison krauss . his own band is called the `` tony rice unit . '' he also plays in a group with his brothers . in 1983 rice won a grammy award for `` best country instrumental performance . ''
