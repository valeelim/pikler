3rd millennium     the 3rd millennium is a period of time that started on january 1 , 2001 and will end on december 31 , 3000 of the gregorian calendar . as this millennium is currently in progress , it 's first two decades , the past 2000s and the present 2010s are the subject of the historian 's attention . the remaining part of the 21st century and the time after that are researched in futures studies .
