SS Yongala     ss `` yongala '' was a passenger ship that was launched in 1903. it sank off cape bowling green on 23 march 1911 on its way to from melbourne , australia to cairns she entered into a cyclone and sank without a trace south of townsville . all passengers onboard drowned and the shipwreck was found in 1958 off the coast of townsville . other pages . - death of tina watson - a drowning in 2003 at the shipwreck that became world news . other websites . - townsville maritime museum - dive spot description plus underwater pictures and videos of the yongala wreck
