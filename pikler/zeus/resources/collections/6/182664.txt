Theseus     theseus was an ancient greek hero who is the main character in many myths . his father was aegeus , and his mother was aethra . he is best known for the defeating the monsters and criminals ( such as procrustus who made travellers fit his special bed by stretching them or cutting off their feet ) he met on his way to athens , and killing the minotaur on the island of crete . later in life , he became king of athens after his father , aegeus .
