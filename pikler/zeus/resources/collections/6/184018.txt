Alexander Rybak     alexander rybak ( 13 may 1986 ) is a belarusian-norwegian singer , composer and violinist . he was born in minsk , belarusian ssr . he moved to norway when he was four . he is best known for winning the eurovision song contest 2009 with the song `` fairytale '' for norway . rybak went to the eurovision song contest 2010 in oslo to perform his winning entry from the previous year .
