Pit bull     pit bull is a word used to describe several breeds of dog . this includes the american pit bull terrier , american staffordshire terrier , american bully and staffordshire bull terrier , and dogs with a mix of these breeds . in some locations , the modern american bulldog and bull terrier are described as a `` pit bull-type dog '' . pit bull can also mean dogs that were known as `` bull terriers '' before the development of the modern bull terrier in the early 20th century . these dogs were bred by crossing bulldog breeds and terriers . pit bulls are very strong and can pull many times their weight . they were also bred for dog fighting . the place where such dogs fought was called a `` pit '' . as such dogs can be dangerous , very often a special permit is needed to breed or to own them . some places or countries may have forbiddden their use . pit bulls are sometimes called `` pibbles '' . some people think they are all aggressive , but they can be sweet when trained right . they use to have the nickname `` nanny dog '' .
