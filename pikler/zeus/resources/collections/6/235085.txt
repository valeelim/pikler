Chiniot District     chiniot district ( ) is a city in the punjab province of pakistan . it is on left bank of the chenab river on the sargodha to faisalabad road . about 1.5 milion people live there . chiniot is at latitude 31.7200 and longitude 72.9789. chiniot is on the bank of river chenab , and is famous for its furniture industry , and it sells furniture to other places . its workers are very good at woodwork .
