Southbury railway station     southbury railway station is in the london borough of enfield in north london , and is in travelcard zone 5 , on the seven sisters branch of the lea valley lines . the station , and all trains serving it , is operated by london overground . the station is situated close to middlesex university 's enfield campus at . the typical off-peak service is two trains per hour to cheshunt , and two trains per hour to london liverpool street .
