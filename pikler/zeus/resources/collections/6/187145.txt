Itaewon     itaewon is a neighborhood in yongsan-gu , seoul . it is an international neighborhood . travellers and expatriate residents come here for shopping , restaurants , and nightlife . about 22,000 people live there , but between 4000-5000 reportedly visit the commercial district daily . many us military personnel from the nearby yongsan garrison come here . itaewon has restaurants serving food from many parts of the world , including india , thailand , and mexico . those foods are not common in korea .
