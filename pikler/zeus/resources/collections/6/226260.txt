White-tailed Starfrontlet     the white-tailed starfrontlet ( `` coeligena phalerata '' ) is a species of hummingbird . it is found only in colombia . its habitat is subtropical or tropical moist montane forests . references . - birdlife international 2004. coeligena phalerata . 2006 iucn red list of threatened species . downloaded on 10 july 2007 .
