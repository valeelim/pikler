Monsters vs. Aliens     monsters vs. aliens is a 2009 american computer-animated 3-d movie from dreamworks animation and paramount pictures . the movie was the first computer animated movie to be directly produced in a stereoscopic 3-d format instead of being changed into 3-d after completion , which added $ 15 million to the film 's budget . the movie was supposed to come out in may 2009 , but the release date was moved to march 27 , 2009. it was released on dvd and blu-ray september 29 , 2009 in north america . `` monsters vs. aliens '' has the voices of reese witherspoon , seth rogen , hugh laurie , will arnett , conrad vernon , rainn wilson , kiefer sutherland , stephen colbert , and paul rudd . cast and characters . monsters . - reese witherspoon as ginormica : susan murphy from modesto , california who is hit by a radioactive meteor on her wedding day , making her grow to a height of . she is shy and wants nothing more than to return to her old life , but slowly warms up to her new status as a monster . because of her exposure with the meteorite 's radiation , and her size , she is very strong and has a resistance to energy attacks .
