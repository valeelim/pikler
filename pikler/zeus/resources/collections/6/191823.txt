Cobra Starship     cobra starship was an american alternative dance band . the group started in 2005 in new york city . they are most famous for their songs `` snakes on a plane ( bring it ) '' and `` good girls go bad '' . they are currently signed to record label fueled by ramen . cobra starship officially dissolved on november 10 , 2015. band members . - gabe saporta - lead vocalist - ryland blackinton - guitarist - alex suarez - bassist - nate novarro - drummer - victoria asher - keytarist other websites . - official website
