Howard Kendall     howard kendall ( 22 may 1946 - 17 october 2015 ) was an english football player turned manager . he was born in ryton , county durham . he is most famous for managing everton fc during one of their most successful periods . he was briefly chairman of the club and has also been manager of manchester city and blackburn rovers . honours with everton . - 1983-84 fa cup - 1984-85 fa charity shield - 1984-85 league championship - 1984-85 european cup winners ' cup - 1985-86 fa charity shield - 1986-87 fa charity shield - 1986-87 league championship
