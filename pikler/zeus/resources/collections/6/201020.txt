NBA Jam     nba jam is an arcade basketball video game . the game was released in 1993. it was both developed and published by midway . the gameplay for `` nba jam '' is based on the 1989 video game `` arch rivals '' , which was another 2-on-2 basketball video game . the game is very popular in popular culture . in the game if you enter a specific code you can unlock bill clinton . it is 2 on 2 arcade basketball .
