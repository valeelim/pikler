Horsepower     horsepower is a unit of power that is approximately how much work one horse can do . it is equal to 746 watts . the idea of horsepower was originated by james watt , who invented the steam engine . because people used horses to pull carts , it was an amount of energy that could be understood by most people at that time . a human can only maintain about 0.1 horsepower , but an engine can maintain much higher than that . small motors can create 10 horsepower while a jet engine can create more than 1,000 horsepower .
