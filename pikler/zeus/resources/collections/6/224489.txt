Amethyst woodstar     the amethyst woodstar ( `` calliphlox amethystina '' ) is a species of hummingbird . it is found in most of central , and eastern south america , argentina , bolivia , brazil , colombia , ecuador , french guiana , guyana , suriname ( the guianas ) , paraguay , peru , and venezuela . the bird 's range surrounds the `` amazon countries '' into the andes foothills and higher heights of upstream river systems , but is not along the amazon river proper in the central amazon basin , or the central upper basin ; it is in brazil at the river 's outlet , and upstream for about 500 km . the amethyst woodstar 's natural habitats are subtropical or tropical moist lowland forests and subtropical or tropical moist montanes . the amethyst woodstar was named after the amethyst crystal
