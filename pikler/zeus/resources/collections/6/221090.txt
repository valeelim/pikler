Eupatorium perfoliatum     common boneset ( `` eupatorium perfoliatum '' ) a species of plant . it belongs in the family asteraceae . it is native to eastern north america . it blooms from july to october . its flowers are white . they are rarely purple . it is found in habitats such as moist woodlands , and swamps . it grows to a height of 0.6 to 1.2 m ( 2 to 4 ft ) . other websites . - `` eupatorium perfoliatum '' at usda plants database - `` eupatorium perfoliatum '' at connecticut botanical society
