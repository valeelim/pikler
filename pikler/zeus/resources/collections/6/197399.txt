Benfica    s.l . benfica sport lisboa e benfica , simply known as benfica or slb , which plays in portugal and uefa competitions . benfica has won 36 portuguese leagues , 29 portuguese cups , 7 league cups , 7 portuguese supercups and 2 european cup/uefa champions league titles . they also reached more 5 champions league finals , 3 europa league finals and 2 intercontinental cup finals .
