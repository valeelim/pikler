Cavalier King Charles spaniel     the cavalier king charles spaniel is a small dog breed in the group called `` toy dogs '' . they were bred earlier than the 16th century . they were kept as pets and hunting dogs by many members of the royal family in england during the 16th century . king charles ii of england liked the breed , so this is how the dog was named . this dog can stand up to 36 cm or 16 inches in height . they average about 9 kilograms in weight . the breed can live for up to 10-14 years.they are still popular in england and are becoming popular in the united states . there are four types of cavaliers each with their own coat markings and colours : ruby - golden coloured , tri-colour - black body and dipped white , with a white muzzle and brown marking scattered around .
