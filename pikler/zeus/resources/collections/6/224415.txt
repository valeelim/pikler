Bee hummingbird     the bee hummingbird or zunzuncito ( `` mellisuga helenae '' ) is a species of hummingbird . it lives in cuba 's isla de la juventud . a newly hatched bee hummingbird chick may weigh about a fingernail clipping . its weight is less than a tenth of an ounce ( 2 g ) . because of this , it is the smallest living bird as well as the smallest theropod dinosaur on record .
