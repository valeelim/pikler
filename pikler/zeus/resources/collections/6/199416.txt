Uniform Resource Identifier     in computing , a uniform resource identifier is something used to name something on the internet . uris are similar to urls , because they are the name of something . examples of uris include : - http : //example.org/absolute/uri/with/absolute/path/to/resource.txt - ftp : //example.org/resource.txt - urn : issn:1535-3613 - mailto : somebody @ someplace.com
