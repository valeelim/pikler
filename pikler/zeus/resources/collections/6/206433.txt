Canadian Women's Hockey League    canadian women 's hockey league the canadian women 's hockey league ( cwhl ) is an ice hockey league in canada that was formed in 2007. female players from around the world played in this league and in the western women 's hockey league . the cwhl replaced the national women 's hockey league ( nwhl ) in 2007. many teams ( toronto , brampton and montreal ) from the nwhl moved to the cwhl . teams in the cwhl . - boston blades - brampton thunder - burlington barracudas - montreal stars - team alberta cwhl - toronto furies cwhl champions . - 2007-08 - brampton thunder - 2008-09 - montreal stars - 2009-10 - montreal stars - 2010-11 - montreal stars the clarkson cup is an ice hockey trophy , which since 2009 has been awarded to the winner of the canadian women 's hockey championship . - 2009 - montreal stars - 2010 - minnesota whitecaps ( wwhl ) - 2011 - montreal stars other websites . - cwhl.ca - canadian women 's hockey league on facebook
