Grandpa Never Lies     grandpa never lies is a children 's book written by ralph fletcher with pictures drawn by harvey stevenson . it was first published in 2000. a young girl describes her special bond with her grandfather over four seasons . the grandfather tells creative stories in answer to her questions . each story is followed by her saying `` and grandpa never lies , so i know it 's so '' . then grandma suddenly dies and the little girl and her grandfather are sad and comfort each other . other websites . - ralph fletcher 's website
