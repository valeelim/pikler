Violetta Villas     violetta villas ( born 10 june 1938 - 5 december 2011 was a polish singer , actress , composer and songwriter . she was born in verviers , belgium . her voice was a coloratura soprano which spans 4.7 octaves . in 1960s she was the star of revues in paris , new york and las vegas . she died in lewin klodzki , poland .
