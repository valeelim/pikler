U.S. Città di Palermo     u.s. città di palermo is an italian football club from palermo , sicily . they play in the serie a , the top level of football in italy . the club was founded in november 1900. it is the oldest football team in sicily . name . - 1900-1907 anglo palermitan afc - 1907-1919 palermo fbc - 1919-1928 us palermo - 1928-1967 palermo fbc - 1967-1977 ss palermo - 1977-1987 ssc palermo - 1987-present us città di palermo
