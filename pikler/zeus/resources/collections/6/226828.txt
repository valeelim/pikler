Edinburgh International Festival     the edinburgh international festival is a festival of music , theatre , opera , dance , visual arts and literature that takes place every year in the city of edinburgh , scotland . each year it is held for about three weeks in late august and early september . the festival started in 1947. since 1999 the permanent home of the festival is at the hub , near edinburgh castle . several large theatres , concert halls and churches in edinburgh are used for the events , e.g . usher hall , festival theatre , edinburgh playhouse , the queen 's hall and the hub . since 2006 the director of the festival has been jonathan mills . related pages . - edinburgh fringe other websites . - official web site - edinburgh festival guide full listings and reviews for all the edinburgh festivals
