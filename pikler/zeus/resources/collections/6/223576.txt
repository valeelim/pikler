Nescafé     nescafé is a brand of instant coffee made by nestlé . instant coffee is a powder which is mixed with hot water to make coffee . there are several different products that have the name nescafé . the name is a mix of the words `` '' nestlé '' and `` café '' '' . nestlé 's instant coffee product was introduced in switzerland on april 1 , 1938 after being developed for seven years by max morgenthaler and vernon chapman .
