Vesicle     a vesicle is a bubble of liquid within a cell . more technically , a vesicle is a small , intracellular , membrane-enclosed sac that stores or transports substances within a cell . vesicles form naturally because of the properties of lipid membranes . vesicles can fuse with the plasma membrane , and release their contents outside the cell . vesicles can also fuse with other organelles within the cell . a vesicle is sometimes formed when the cell is doing endocytosis . endocytosis is a process in which a cell 's membrane takes in a particle from the outside and brings it inside the cell with a vesicle around it .
