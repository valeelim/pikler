Biota     biota is a common term in biology . it means all the living things at a particular time or place . examples : cambrian biota ; madagascan biota . because it includes all the living members of a given environment , the term is used especially in ecology . a similar term is biome , which refers to large geographical regions . tundra is a biome . flora , fauna and other forms of life such as fungi are collectively called biota . related pages . - biome - fauna - flora - wildlife
