Lake Washington     lake washington is a lake in the us state of washington . it is next to the city of seattle . seattle is on the west side of the lake , lake city , bothell , and kenmore to the north , kirkland and bellevue to the east , and renton to the south . mercer island is in the south end of the lake . the deepest part of the lake is deep . the lake is above sea level . it is a fresh water lake . on a few days of the year are people allowed to fish for salmon in the lake .
