Led Zeppelin II     led zeppelin ii is the second album from the band led zeppelin . it was released on october 22 , 1969 , it was recorded in 1969 in many different studios . the album sounds a lot like the first album with a few hard rock songs and blues covers , but they also did some new things with the love song `` thank you '' and the drum solo `` moby dick '' . the most famous song from the album is `` whole lotta love '' , the riff is one of the most well known in the rock genre . guitarist and producer jimmy page worked with engineer eddie kramer to create many unusual sounds , for example , on `` whole lotta love '' , they used a technique known as backward echoing . sources . - jimmy page 's recording techniques official website
