Samoyed (dog)    samoyed ( dog ) the samoyed is a dog breed that are usually white in color but they can be grey or cream too . they can get between 2-3 feet in height . the samoyed breed is known for having a curled tail , much like a husky . they have a thick coat of fur to protect them from the cold because they were used to drag slays in the cold . samoyeds are really friendly , so they will not make good guard dogs because they are too cute and fluffy and are really friendly .
