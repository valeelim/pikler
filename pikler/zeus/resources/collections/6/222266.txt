Eliomys     eliomys is a genus of rodent in the gliridae family . it contains the following species : - asian garden dormouse ( `` eliomys melanurus '' ) - maghreb garden dormouse ( `` eliomys munbyanus '' ) - garden dormouse ( `` eliomys quercinus '' ) references . - holden , m. e.. 2005. family gliridae . pp . 819-841 `` in '' mammal species of the world a taxonomic and geographic reference . d. e. wilson and d. m. reeder eds . johns hopkins university press , baltimore .
