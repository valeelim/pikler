Glioblastoma multiforme     glioblastoma multiforme is a malignant brain tumor that carries an extremely poor prognosis . signs and symptoms . - symptoms of increased intracranial pressure including headache , pappiledema , nausea or vomiting , and diplopia . currently , most patients with glioblastoma multiforme are initially treated with maximal surgical removal of the tumor , which is then followed by radiotherapy and chemotherapy , most often with temozolomide , a chemotheraputic agent that has been shown to have activity against glioblastoma multiforme . radiosurgery is being investigated in the treatmet glioblastoma multiforme .
