KISS (principle)    kiss ( principle ) the kiss principle is `` keep it simple , stupid '' . it is an acronym , with the letters kiss making the beginnings of the important words . it was used as a principle for design by the u.s. navy in 1960. the phrase is said to have been first used by kelly johnson , lead engineer at the lockheed skunk works . there are several variations of the phrase , such as `` keep it short and simple '' . other pages . - occam 's razor
