Travelcard Zone 6     travelcard zone 6 is the sixth outward concentric zone of the transport for london zonal system used for calculating co-ordinated inter-modal travelcard fares within greater london . in this zonal system , greater london is split into six ( approximately concentric ) zones for the purpose of determining the cost of a travelcard . the zones are also used for pricing london underground and docklands light railway single journeys , but not single journeys undertaken entirely by bus or tram , for which a system-wide flat fare applies .
