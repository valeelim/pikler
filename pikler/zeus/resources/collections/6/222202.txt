Woodland dormouse     the woodland dormouse ( graphiurus murinus ) is a species of rodent in the gliridae family . it is endemic to ( lives only in ) south africa . it is also known as the `` african dwarf dormouse '' , the `` african pygmy dormouse '' or the `` micro squirrel '' . they are sometimes kept as pets , but they are harder to look after than other pet rodents . its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical moist montanes and rivers . references . - baxter , r. 2004. graphiurus murinus . 2006 iucn red list of threatened species . downloaded on 29 july 2007 . - holden , m. e.. 2005. family gliridae . pp . 819â€“841 `` in '' mammal species of the world a taxonomic and geographic reference . d. e. wilson and d. m. reeder eds . johns hopkins university press , baltimore .
