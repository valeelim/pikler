Independent Democrat     an independent democrat is someone who agrees with the democratic party in the united states on many issues , but prefers not to call himself a democrat or be in the party . former independent democrats included zadok casey and strom thurmond . the only independent democrat in office today is u.s. senator and former vice presidential candidate joseph lieberman of connecticut , who was kicked out of the democratic party when he formed `` connecticut for lieberman '' to get back in the senate in 2006 .
