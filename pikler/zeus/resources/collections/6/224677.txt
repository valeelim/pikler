Horned Sungem     the horned sungem ( `` heliactin bilophus '' ) is a species of hummingbird . it can make 90 wingbeats per second when it 's hovering to drink nectar from flowers . a wingbeat is one complete up-and-down movement , which means that the horned sungem moves its wing muscles at a rate of more than 10,000 times per minute . it lives in bolivia , brazil , and suriname . it likes mostly dry open or semi-open habitats , such as savanna and cerrado . it does not like thick humid forest .
