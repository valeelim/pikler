Caligula (movie)    caligula ( movie ) caligula is a 1979 italian-american historical movie . it was directed by tinto brass . it is about the life of roman emperor caligula . it stars malcolm mcdowell as caligula . cast . - malcolm mcdowell as caligula - teresa ann savoy as drusilla - guido mannari as macro - john gielgud as nerva ( son of marcus cocceius nerva ( consul 36 bc ) ) - peter o'toole as tiberius - giancarlo badessi as claudius - bruno brive as tiberius gemellus - paolo bonacelli as cassius chaerea - john steiner as longinus - helen mirren as caesonia - mirella d'angelo as livia
