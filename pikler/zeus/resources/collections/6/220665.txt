Cream pie     a cream pie is a type of pie filled with a rich custard or pudding that is made from milk , cream , flour , and eggs . it can come in many forms , like vanilla , lemon , lime , banana , coconut , and chocolate . a common feature of all cream pies is the whipped cream topping . the pudding-like filling is related to the french crème patissière which is a main ingredient of many french cakes and tarts .
