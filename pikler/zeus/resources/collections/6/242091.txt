Maciej Rataj     maciej rataj ( born 19 february 1884 - died 21 june 1940 ) was a polish politician of peasants movement , who served as speaker of the parliament ( sejm ) and twice acting president of poland . an independence and peasant activist before poland regained an independence in 1918 , rataj became member of sejm in 1918. he was elected speaker in the first regular term and held that post from 1922 to 1928. previously , he was minister for religious affairs in wincenty witos cabinet . he served as acting president twice : from 16 december to 22 december 1922 and from 15 may to june 4 1926. for the first time he served between assassination of poland 's first president gabriel narutowicz and election of his successor stanisław wojciechowski . for the second , between wojciechowski resignation after a may coup and election of ignacy mościcki . after outbreak of the world war ii , rataj remained in occupied poland and was murdered , along other prominent citizens , by nazis in palmiry .
