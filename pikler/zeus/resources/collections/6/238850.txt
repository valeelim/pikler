Nothosaur     nothosaurs were long-necked , long-tailed , fish-eating sauropterygian reptiles ranging from a few inches to 20 feet ( 6 m ) long - they were not dinosaurs . nothosaurs lived during the triassic period . they may have evolved into the plesiosaurs . these reptiles had a long , thin head with many sharp teeth ; the front teeth were longer than the back teeth . the nostrils were on the top end of the snout . they breathed air but spent most of the time in the water . - `` pistosaurus ''
