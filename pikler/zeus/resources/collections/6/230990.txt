Easter Bunny     the easter bunny ( or easter hare ) is a character depicted as an anthropomorphic rabbit . in old stories , the creature brings baskets filled with colored eggs , candy and toys to the homes of children on the night before easter . the easter bunny will either put the baskets in a certain place or hide them somewhere in the house for the children to find when they wake up in the morning . the easter bunny is similar to christmas 's santa claus , as they both bring gifts to good children on the night before the holidays they are related to .
