Lisa the Greek     `` lisa the greek '' is the fourteenth episode of the simpsons third season . it first started on the fox network in the united states on january 23 , 1992. in this episode , homer begins to bond with his daughter , lisa , after learning her unique ability to pick winning american football teams . when lisa finds out homer has only been using her for gambling , she does n't to speak to her father until he fully understands her . `` lisa the greek '' was written by jay kogen and wallace wolodarsky , and directed by rich moore .
