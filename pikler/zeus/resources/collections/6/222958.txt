Audi A3     the audi a3 is a car produced by audi since 1996. it is based on the volkswagen golf 's platform , and it is currently in its third generation . the first generation was made between 1996 and 2003 with a 5-door added in 1999 and a facelift in late 2000. the second generation was made from 2003 to 2012 , and was facelifted in 2005 and again in 2008 with a convertible version added . the third generation was launched in 2012 with a saloon version added a year later , it was facelifted in 2016 , by coincidence the a3 's 20th anniversary on sale .
