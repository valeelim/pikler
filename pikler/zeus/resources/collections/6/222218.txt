Rock dormouse     the rock dormouse ( graphiurus platyops ) is a species of rodent in the gliridae family . it is found in angola , botswana , mozambique , south africa , swaziland , zambia , and zimbabwe . references . - grubb , p. 2004. graphiurus platyops . 2006 iucn red list of threatened species . downloaded on 29 july 2007 . - holden , m. e.. 2005. family gliridae . pp . 819-841 `` in '' mammal species of the world a taxonomic and geographic reference . d. e. wilson and d. m. reeder eds . johns hopkins university press , baltimore .
