Peter Falk     peter michael falk ( september 16 , 1927 - june 23 , 2011 ) was an american actor . his best known role was columbo in the tv series of the same name . falk was born in manhattan on september 16 , 1927. he was jewish . he grew up in ossining , new york . he earned a degree in political science from the new school for social research . he then earned his master 's degree in public administration at syracuse university . he appeared in a number of broadway plays including : `` the prisoner of second avenue '' , `` the passion of josef d '' , `` diary of a scoundrel '' , and `` saint joan '' . falk won four emmy awards playing the police detective columbo , and one for `` the dick powell theatre '' . he was nominated for seven other emmy awards . falk also received two academy award nominations for the films `` murder inc. '' and `` pocketful of miracles '' . falk was diagnosed with alzheimer 's disease . he died in his beverly hills , california home on the morning of june 23 , 2011. other websites . - peter falk at emmys.com - peter falk on dean martin show , video , 10 min .
