Interstate 87     interstate 87 is an interstate highway in the state of new york in the united states . it goes from new york city north to champlain at the canada border . the route is long . the southern half of the interstate ( between nyc and albany ) is part of the new york state thruway ( tollway ) . north of albany , the highway is called the adirondack northway . its short length through the nyc borough of the bronx is called the major deegan expressway .
