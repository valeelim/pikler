Carol Thatcher     carol thatcher ( born 15 august 1953 ) is a british journalist , author , and media personality . she was born in hammersmith , west london . she has a twin brother , mark thatcher . she is the daughter of former british prime minister margaret thatcher ( 1925-2013 ) and her husband sir denis thatcher ( 1915-2003 ) . she has taken part in several television programmes including `` the one show '' on bbc1 and `` i 'm a celebrity ... get me out of here ! '' ( in australia ) .
