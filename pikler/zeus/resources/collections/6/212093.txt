Cadillac Escalade     the cadillac escalade is a large , luxury suv . it is manufactured by general motors and was released in 1999 to compete with german and japanese competitors and to ford 's 1998 release of the lincoln navigator . the escalade and escalade ext pickup are the two of only three cadillac vehicles that are made outside of the united states . other websites . - cadillac escalade on cadillac.com
