Some Enchanted Evening     `` some enchanted evening '' is the thirteenth and final episode of the simpsons first season and first started on the fox network on may 13 , 1990. although it was the first episode made , it started on television as the season finale because of many animation problems . in the episode , bart , lisa , and maggie meet the `` babysitter bandit '' . after answering a , homer and marge want to spend a night on the town so they need a babysitter to look after their children . they hire ms. botz through a babysitting service . ms. botz is later known to be the `` babysitter bandit '' and after tying up bart and lisa , she robs the family . the episode has references to such films as `` the night of the hunter '' and `` psycho '' as well as a musical reference to `` a star is born . '' it got some good and bad reviews ; some people thought it the best episode of the season , while others thought it the season 's worst .
