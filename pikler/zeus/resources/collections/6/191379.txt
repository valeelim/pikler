Mount Shasta     mount shasta is a dormant volcano in northern california in the united states . the volcano is part of the cascade range . it has a smaller peak abutting it , shastina , and seven glaciers . the mountain stands alone in a large expanse of flatter land , rising 9,822 feet above the surrounding terrain . the sacramento river flows south near the west edge of the mountain . the town of mount shasta and shasta lake reservoir , formed by shasta dam , are all named for mount shasta . this stratovolcano is expected to erupt again someday .
