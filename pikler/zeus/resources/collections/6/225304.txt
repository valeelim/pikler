Greenish puffleg     the greenish puffleg ( `` haplophaedia aureliae '' ) is a species of hummingbird . it is found in colombia , ecuador , and panama . its habitats are subtropical or tropical moist montanes and fairly degraded former forests . references . - birdlife international 2008. . 2008 iucn red list of threatened species . downloaded on 12 juni 2009 .
