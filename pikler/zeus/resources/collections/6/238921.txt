666 (number)    666 ( number ) 666 is a natural number after 665 and before 667. it is a number with many properties . one of these properties is that it is the sum of the first 36 natural numbers . that makes 666 a triangular number . 666 is the sum of the first 144 decimals ( digits after the decimal point ) of pi . the bible says in the book of revelation that the number 666 is associated with the antichrist , the counter-messiah of the devil . people will be forced to accept this number as a mark on the penalty of death if they refuse . however , people who do accept this mark will be rejected by god .
