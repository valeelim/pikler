Devil's food cake    devil 's food cake the devil 's food cake is an old fashioned chocolate cake made of chocolate or cocoa and baking powder . it is moist , soft , rich , and very good for keeping . it is a light-textured chocolate layer-type cake with a deep reddish brown color . the cake gets this bright red color from the large amount of red food dye used in the preparation . devil 's food cake is usually thought of in terms of dark chocolate , but originally it was red . today cooks , using modern processed cocoa , sometimes add a touch of red food coloring to bring back the authentic color . devil 's food cakes can be stored in the refrigerator for about 3 days . the devil 's food cake may also be called a red velvet cake .
