Lake Okeechobee     lake okeechobee is the largest lake in the u.s. state of florida . lake okeechobee covers about 730 square miles ( about half the size of rhode island ) . the lake is shared by glades , okeechobee , martin , palm beach , and hendry counties . some cities on the shore of lake okeechobee include : belle glade , clewiston , okeechobee , and pahokee .
