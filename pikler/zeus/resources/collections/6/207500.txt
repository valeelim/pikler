David Mellor     david john mellor , qc ( born march 12 , 1949 in wareham , dorset , england ) is a british politician who was an mp from 1979 to 1997 , as a member of the conservative party . mellor became a barrister in 1972 , and a queen 's counsel in 1987 but is currently not practising . he became a member of parliament ( mp ) representing the london constituency of putney , at the 1979 general election . in 1990 , john major made him chief secretary to the treasury but after a sex scandal in 1992 , mellor was forced to resign and lost his seat to the labour party in the 1997 general election .
