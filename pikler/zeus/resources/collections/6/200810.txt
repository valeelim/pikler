Ford Laser     the ford laser was an automobile sold by ford motor company between 1981 and 2003. it was offered in four bodystyles ; a three-door hatchback , five-door hatchback , four-door saloon and five-door estate . this was primarily sold in the oceania region of the world . however , some were sold in latin america , and these were versions of the mazda 323 sold under the ford laser name .
