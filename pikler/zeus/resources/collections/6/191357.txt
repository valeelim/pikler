Irrigation sprinkler     sprinkler irrigation is a way of artificially watering plants . a sprinkler irrigation system is a pressurized system , which means it needs water under pressure to work . there are many different types of irrigation sprinklers . some are used in home gardens , and some are used on farms . a permanent sprinker system is made of a pump to take water and create pressure , and a network of pipes leading to the sprinkler heads . usually the sprinkler head is on a small pipe that comes out of the ground .
