Atlanta FC     atlanta fc is an american football team based in sandy springs , georgia , united states . started in 2007 , the team plays in national premier soccer league ( npsl ) , a national amateur league at the fourth tier of the american soccer pyramid , in the southeast division . the team plays its home games at hoskyn stadium on the grounds of riverwood high school , where they have played since 2008. the team 's colors are orange , white , and sky blue .
