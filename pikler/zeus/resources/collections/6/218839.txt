Parides erithalion     the variable cattleheart ( `` parides erithalion '' ) is a species of butterfly . it belongs in the family papilionidae . it is found in north america and south america . the upper side of the wings is black . the hind wing ( bottom wing ) has a row of red spots . the underside of the wings is also black . the hind wing has a row of pink and whitish-colored spots . the eggs are a brownish color . the caterpillar is brownish-black . it has white and reddish-brown areas . the variable cattleheart feeds on `` aristolochia cordiflora '' . other websites . - `` parides '' at butterflies of america - larval food plant information sorted by plant
