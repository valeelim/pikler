Room on the 3rd Floor     room on the 3rd floor is mcfly 's first album . it was released in 2004. it started at number one in the uk albums chart . this broke the world record for the youngest band with a number one album . the beatles used to hold the record . it reached number one after selling 61,589 copies in the first week . it is certified 2x platinum in the uk for sales of over 800,000 copies . track listing . 1. five colours in her hair ( tom fletcher , danny jones , james bourne , ben sargeant ) 1. obviously ( tom fletcher , danny jones , james bourne ) 1. room on the first floor ( tom fletcher , danny jones ) 1. that girl ( tom fletcher , james bourne ) 1. hypnotised ( tom fletcher , danny jones , harry judd , dougie poynter ) 1. saturday night ( tom fletcher , danny jones , dougie poynter , harry judd ) 1. met this girl ( danny jones , dougie poynter , harry judd , tom fletcher ) 1. she left me ( tom fletcher , james bourne ) 1. down by the lake ( tom fletcher , james bourne ) 1. unsaid things ( tom fletcher , danny jones , dougie poynter , harry judd , james bourne )
