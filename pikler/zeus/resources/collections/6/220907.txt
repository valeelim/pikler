Bart the General     `` bart the general '' is the fifth full length episode of `` the simpsons '' ' first season , which first started on the fox network on february 4 , 1990. in this episode , bart deals with his troubles with the school bully , nelson muntz . bart chooses to go to war with nelson using the neighborhood kids as his own army . the episode was written by john swartzwelder and directed by david silverman . related pages . - the simpsons
