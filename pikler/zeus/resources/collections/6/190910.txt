ScienceWorld     scienceworld , also known as eric weisstein 's world of science , is a web site that opened to the general public in january 2002. as of november 2007 , `` scienceworld '' includes more than 4,000 entries in fields of science including astronomy , chemistry , physics , as well as biographies of many scientists . it is administered by eric weisstein of wolfram research , inc. , who is the chief encyclopedist assigned to the project .
