Crunchy Black     crunchy black ( born august 1974 in indiana ) is an american rapper and dancer from memphis , tennessee . he left in 2006. he is best known for being a member of the oscar-winning hip hop group three 6 mafia . crunchy is known for his dancing `` g-walking '' , which he says he started doing at club memphis . he is not the starter of this dance but he is very good at it . he is known as a pioneer .
