Jack Benny     jack benny ( born benjamin kubelsky ; february 14 , 1894 - december 26 , 1974 ) was an american comedian and actor . he was born in chicago and grew up in waukegan , illinois . his mother was a lithuanian immigrant ; his father was a jewish immigrant from poland . from 1932 to 1955 , benny had a radio show . the first title of the show was `` the canada dry ginger ale program '' , but it was later called `` the jack benny program '' . his television program was broadcast from 1950 to 1965. benny died of pancreatic cancer in beverly hills , california .
