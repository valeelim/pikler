Jordan Hendry     jordan hendry ( born february 23 , 1984 in nokomis , saskatchewan ) is an canadian professional ice hockey defenceman that currently plays for straubing tigers of the del . he played college ice hockey with the university of alaska fairbanks nanooks . hendry has played in the national hockey league with the chicago blackhawks from four seasons and the anaheim ducks for 2 games . he has also played for hc lugano of the national league a. hendry won the stanley cup with the chicago blackhawks on june 9 2010 , which he took to his hometown of nokomis .
