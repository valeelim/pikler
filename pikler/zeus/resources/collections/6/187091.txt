Ko Young-Min     ko young-min ( born february 8 , 1984 in seoul , south korea ) is a professional baseball player of the south korean baseball club doosan bears . he graduated from seung-nam high school . his height is 182.0 cm . and his weight is 76.0 kg . his position is second baseman . he is a member of the national team . he has fast legs and a great catching ability . his nickname is koget . he is a second fielder . `` koget '' is a compound word made up of his family name and an animation gadget . his experiences as a member of the national team are in the beijing olympic games and the second wbc . he won the golden glove prize in 2007. he also won the korea professional baseball at second baseman prize . he got the best point prize in 2007 for the samsung pavv professional baseball team . bold = led kbo
