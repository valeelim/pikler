President of the European Council     the president of the european council is a position that was created by the treaty of lisbon . the president is the chairman of the european council , which is the meeting of the leaders of all the member states . it 's a very diplomatic figure , and it 's not like a head of state : the president does not have any power to make decisions about the european union . the union has several other presidents , such as the president of the european parliament and the president of the european commission . but the president of the european council , casually but wrongly known as the president of europe , is the face by which the european union is known to the rest of the world . the more practical aspects of this task are the responsibility of the high representative for common foreign and security policy , the commissioner responsible for external affairs . presidents : 1. herman van rompuy ( from belgium ) : 2009-2014 1. donald tusk ( from poland ) : 2014-present
