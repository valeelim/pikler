Cheshunt railway station     cheshunt railway station serves the town of cheshunt in hertfordshire , england . it is on the west anglia main line , and train services are provided by abellio greater anglia and london overground . former operator national express east anglia has fulfilled a franchise commitment to extend the bay platform at cheshunt to accommodate 8-coach trains ( previously it could only accommodate up to 6 coaches ) . the works also involved replacing all existing station buildings , and replacing the station footbridge . construction commenced in december 2005 and was largely complete by august 2006. in the 2012 summer olympics , cheshunt station ( along with waltham cross , the next southbound station ) will serve as the main access point for the broxbourne whitewater canoe and kayak slalom .
