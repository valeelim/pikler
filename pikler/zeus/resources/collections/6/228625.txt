Litmus paper     litmus paper is a way to test whether or not a solution is alkaline or an acidic . litmus is a solution of different dyes made from lichen . this is then put onto filter paper . the paper can then be used to give a rough indication of the ph value of a solution . blue litmus paper will turn red , when acid is present ; red litmus paper will turn blue when alkalines are present . at room temperature , this usually happens for ph values between 4.5 and 8.3. alternatively , a solution can be prepared which works the same way , but does not need the paper . related pages . - ph # indicators
