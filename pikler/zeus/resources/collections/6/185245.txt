Arriva Fox County     arriva fox county is a bus company which provides bus services in leicester and leicestershire . it is part of arriva midlands , and a subsidiary of arriva . arriva fox county was formed in 1981 , as midland red east the old midland red bus company broke up , and it changed its name to midland fox in 1984. in late 2006 , a new `` leicester linx '' brand was introduced for services in leicester . other websites . - arriva uk bus official site - leicester linx website
