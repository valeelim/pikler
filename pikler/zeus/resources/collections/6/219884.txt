Bo Outlaw     charles `` bo '' outlaw ( born april 13 , 1971 ; also known as bo outlaw ) is a retired basketball player who played in the national basketball association ( nba ) . he played for the los angeles clippers , orlando magic , phoenix suns , and memphis grizzlies . he played in his last nba game in 2007. before playing in the nba , he played college basketball at south plains college and the university of houston .
