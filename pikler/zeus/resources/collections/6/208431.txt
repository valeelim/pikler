White-tailed spider     the white-tailed spider is a `` lampona murina '' . it is in the lamponidae family . the female is 16 mm long in body length while the male is only 12 mm long in body length . their habitat includes houses usually in crevices and sheds . it eats other spiders but the black house spider is its main meal . they like to come out at night . when this spider bites a human , it could cause necrotising arachnidism .
