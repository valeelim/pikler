Louis-Antoine de Bougainville     louis-antoine de bougainville was an admiral and from france . in 1766 he was sent by his government to make a trip around the world , called `` circumnavigation '' , which took from 1766 to 1769. he rediscovered ( found again ) the solomon islands in 1768 ( the largest island is named for him ) . he also collected plants and made a trustworthy chart of some parts of the center of the pacific ocean . bougainville saw a part of the great barier reef , but could not find australia . other websites . - biography at the `` dictionary of canadian biography online '' - from the warpath to the plains of abraham ( virtual exhibition ) - louis-antoine de bougainville : eighteenth century french sailor , soldier , statesman , mathematician and leader of a voyage around the world
