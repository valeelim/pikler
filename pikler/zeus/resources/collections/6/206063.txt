Relatively Speaking (book)    relatively speaking ( book ) relatively speaking : poems about family is a young adult book of poetry by ralph fletcher , with drawings by walter lyon krudop . it was first published in 1999. the youngest boy describes both his family and their life together through verse . various scenes include how the family prepare corn on the cob , his other brothers accident , the family reunion , his seldom seen cousin and his uncle 's funeral . `` relatively speaking : poems about family '' has been described as a novel-in-verse . outside websites . - ralph fletcher 's website - walter lyon krudop 's website
