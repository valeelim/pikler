Greg Graffin     gregory walter graffin , ph.d. ( born november 6 , 1964 at racine , wisconsin ) , better known to the public as greg graffin , is the vocalist and a songwriter of bad religion , which he co-founded in 1980 and is the band 's only constant member , even though it now features two of their original members , guitarist brett gurewitz ( who left and rejoined the band twice ) and bassist jay bentley ( who left in 1982 , but returned in 1986 ) . graffin obtained his ph.d. at cornell university and has lectured courses in life sciences and paleontology at the university of california , los angeles . he 's also a solo artist , having released `` american lesion '' in 1997 and `` cold as the clay '' in 2006 .
