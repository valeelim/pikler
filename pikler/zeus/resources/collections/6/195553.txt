Lactic acid     lactic acid is an acid that is very common in nature . lactic acid is a carboxylic acid with a chemical formula of cho . carl wilhelm scheele discovered it in milk that had gone sour , in 1780. today it is used as an additive to food , to produce a sour taste . contrary to popular belief , it is not produced in muscles when a person exercises . lactate , on the other hand , is created within the muscles the more you exercise .
