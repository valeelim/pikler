Diamondback rattlesnake     the diamondback rattlesnake is a very poisonous rattlesnake . diamondbacks are found from florida to california . the bite from a big rattlesnake can hold enough poison to kill a person . however , if the victim gets to a doctor quickly enough , he or she can usually survive . if you spend time in regions where diamondback rattlesnakes live , you should take a few precautions . people must be especially careful around caves . rattlesnakes sometimes hibernate in caves during cool weather . if you listen carefully for its unusual rattling noise , you can know where it is hiding .
