Ghirlandaio     ghirlandaio is the surname of a family of the renaissance italian painters : - domenico ghirlandaio ( 1449–1494 ) , michelangelo 's teacher - davide ghirlandaio ( 1452–1525 ) , younger brother of domenico - benedetto ghirlandaio ( 1458–1497 ) , younger brother of domenico and davide - ridolfo ghirlandaio ( 1483–1561 ) , son of domenico
