Skydra     skydra ( ) is a town and municipality of pella prefecture in the region of macedonia in greece . it has a total population of 5,081 inhabitants in the town and 15,654 inhabitants in the municipality . during the ottoman empite until 1926 skydra was known with the name vertikop or vartikop . the name was derived from the turkish or slavic languages . it was named like that because of a river that crossed these places .
