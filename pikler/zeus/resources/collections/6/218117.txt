Bill Elliott     william clyde elliott ( born october 8 , 1955 ) is a part-time american racecar driver . he drives in the sprint cup series . he won the 1988 sprint cup , and was inducted into the motorsports hall of fame of america in 2007. in 2014 , elliot was voted into the nascar hall of fame . he will be inducted into the hall of fame in january of 2015 .
