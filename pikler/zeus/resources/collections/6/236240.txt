John Wall     john wall ( born september 6 , 1990 ) is an american basketball player who plays the position of point guard for the washington wizards of the nba . he is 6'4 '' , and played college basketball for the university of kentucky before joining the nba . on april 7 , 2010 , he announced that he will enter the 2010 nba draft after playing in college for one year . the washington wizards chose wall with the first pick in the nba draft on june 24 , 2010. the hip-hop group , troop 41 released the single , `` do the john wall '' which features the `` john wall dance '' , where the person flexes their arms and twists their wrists . other websites . - john wall 's nba player profile
