Santana do Paraíso     santana do paraíso is a brazilian city in the vale do aço metropolitan area , state of minas gerais . its population in 2009 was 24.695 inhabitants . its area is 275,529 km² . the city was founded in april 28 , 1992. it is limited with the municipal districts of belo oriente , mesquita , ipatinga , caratinga e ipaba other websites . - official site of santana do paraíso
