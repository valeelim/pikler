Donau-Ries     donau-ries ( `` danube-ries '' ) is a rural district in swabia in southwest bavaria , germany . neighbor districts are : ansbach ( district ) , weißenburg-gunzenhausen , eichstätt ( district ) , neuburg-schrobenhausen , aichach-friedberg , augsburg ( district ) , dillingen ( district ) and in baden-württemberg : heidenheim ( district ) and ostalbkreis ) ; donauwörth is the capital of the donau-ries .
