Lisa Marie Presley     lisa marie presley ( born february 1 , 1968 ) is an american singer-songwriter . she is the only child of singer/actor elvis presley . she owns graceland , the mansion her father lived in . she has released three albums . growing up presley lived in graceland with her parents until elvis ' death in 1977. presley was married to danny keough , michael jackson , nicolas cage and her present husband michael lockwood . she has four children . two of the children are with keough . the other two are with lockwood .
