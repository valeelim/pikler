Bad (song)    bad ( song ) `` bad '' is a song by michael jackson . `` bad '' was released by epic records in september 1987. it was the second single from jackson 's album `` bad '' . `` bad '' got great reviews . some critics noted that `` bad '' helped jackson 's image become edgier during the `` bad '' -era . a music video for `` bad '' was directed by martin scorsese and released in late 1987. in the video , jackson and a group of background dancers are shown doing dance routines throughout a subway station . the single has sold 8.5 millon copies . `` bad '' was originally intended to be a duet between jackson and musician prince . `` weird al '' yankovic did a spoof of this song called , `` fat '' . on august 14 , 2012 `` bad ( afrojack remix ) ( dj buddha edit ) '' was released as a single from `` bad 25 '' . it got very bad reviews .
