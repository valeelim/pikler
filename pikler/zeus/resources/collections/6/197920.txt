Bragging Rights (2009)    bragging rights ( 2009 ) bragging rights ( 2009 ) was a professional wrestling pay-per-view ( ppv ) show made by world wrestling entertainment ( wwe ) , that took place on october 25 , 2009 at the mellon arena in pittsburgh , pennsylvania . it was the first bragging rights event held by the wwe and was created to replace cyber sunday in the late october slot of wwe 's pay-per-view calendar . related pages . - list of wwe pay-per-view events other websites . - bragging rights 2009 results - bragging rights official site - bragging rights matches
