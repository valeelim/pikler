Ichiro Suzuki     ichiro suzuki ( 鈴木 一朗 , suzuki ichirō ) ( born october 22 , 1973 in toyoyama , nishikasugai , aichi prefecture , japan ) , is a japanese baseball player playing as right fielder for the new york yankees . he is the first mlb player to enter the japanese baseball hall of fame and has won a gold glove award in each of his first ten years in the major leagues .
