Handbook     a handbook ( sometimes called a `` vade mecum '' which is latin for `` go with me '' ) is a pocket book that can be carried at all times . now however , this term is thought to be very old and is not in normal use any more . handbooks may have information about any topic , and are mostly guides of information in a special field or about a special method . they are not made for reading through from end to end . they are made to have fast answers in their area . for example , the mla handbook for writers of research papers is a style guide for how to cite works in the mla way , among other things .
