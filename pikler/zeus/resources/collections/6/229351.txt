Cardenal Caro     cardenal caro is a chilean province . is the only province in the region of o'higgins that has instant access to the sea . it has a population of 41,160 inhabitants . it has a surface of 3.295,07 km² . its capital is pichilemu , main turistic place of the region during the summer months . in the past , it was part of the province of colchagua . the province is named after josé maria caro , the first chilean catholic cardinal . the province has 6 communes : - pichilemu ( capital ) - la estrella - litueche - marchigüe - navidad - paredones other pages . - josé maría caro
