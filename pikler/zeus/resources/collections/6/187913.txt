Velafrons     velafrons ( meaning `` sailed forehead '' ) is a genus of hadrosaurid dinosaur from the upper cretaceous of mexico . its fossils were found in the upper cretaceous cerro del pueblo formation ( about 73.5 million years old ) , near rincon colorado , coahuila , mexico . the type specimen is cpc-59 , and the type species is `` v. coahuilensis '' .
