Mulde     the mulde is a river in central germany . it is formed when the `` zwickauer mulde '' and the `` freiberger mulde '' coming from the erzgebirge meet near colditz . it runs on through the saxon towns grimma , wurzen and eilenburg to dessau in saxony-anhalt . the river flows three kilometers north of dessau into the elbe river . the mulde has a length of 124 kilometers and is one of the fastest rivers in central europe . it is not navigable .
