Lasse Lindh     lasse lindh is a swedish indie pop musician born in 1974. his debut album `` bra '' came out in 1998 on emi and three singles , `` i en annan flicka '' , `` jag vill vara som du '' and `` hon ar battre '' , were released . after his first solo album he tried a project team named tribeca with his album producer claes björklund . his second official album `` you wake up at sea tac '' was released in 2002. in this album he started to write songs in english . this album was released in usa , france , japan and other countries . his most recent album is `` sparks '' , released in 2009. albums . - bra ( 1998 ) - you wake up at sea tac ( 2002 ) - lasse lindh ( 2005 ) - attica ep ( 2006 ) - jag tyckte jag var glad ( 2007 ) - when you grow old ... your heart dies ( 2007 ) - pool ( 2008 ) - sparks ( 2009 ) singles . - svenska hjärtan ( 2005 ) - sommarens sista smak ( 2005 ) - radion spelar aldrig våran sång ( 2006 ) - du skär ( 2006 ) - varje litet steg ( 2007 ) - ingen vind kan blåsa omkull oss nu ( 2007 ) - du behöver aldrig mer vara rädd ( 2008 )
