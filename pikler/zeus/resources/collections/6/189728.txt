Phoenix of New Orleans     the phoenix of new orleans ( pnola ) is a non-profit organization that gives help to victims of hurricane katrina , specifically those within the new orleans city limits . pnola was founded by paul ikemire , a medical student at tulane university , in november , 2005. since then , pnola has grown to become one of the main rebuild organizations in the city , giving help to more than 300 families . pnola is supported by the united way and americorps . on august 29th , 2005 , hurricane katrina made landfall in coastal louisiana and mississippi , with category 3 force winds across the region . many cities and towns throughout southeast louisiana and the mississippi gulf coast were affected , ranging from minor damage to complete devastation .
