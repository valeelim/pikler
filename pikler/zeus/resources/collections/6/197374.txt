Mozilla Add-ons     mozilla add-ons is the official mozilla website to store add-ons for mozilla software . this software includes mozilla firefox , mozilla thunderbird , seamonkey , and mozilla sunbird . these add-ons include extensions , themes , dictionaries , search bar `` search engines '' , and plugins . mozilla update ( umo , update.mozilla.org ) is the oldest name of mozilla add-ons . amo is a non-official name for mozilla add-ons today . add-on developers are thousands of volunteers . not mozilla employees . after an upload , add-ons are reviewed . if accurate , they 're published .
