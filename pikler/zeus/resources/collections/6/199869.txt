Up (2009 movie)    up ( 2009 movie ) up is an american animated movie that came out in 2009. the movie was made by pixar and distributed by disney . it was first shown at the 2009 cannes film festival . it was a successful movie and was one of the top movies for 2009. the main characters are boy named russell , an old man named carl fredricksen , and kevin the rare bird . they take an adventure in a house with balloons to carry it up in the sky . as they fly their desired destination is in venezeula . the movie won 2 academy awards one for best animated feature and one for best original score in 2010 .
