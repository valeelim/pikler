2008–09 Chicago Blackhawks season     the 2008–09 chicago blackhawks season is the team 's 82nd season in the national hockey league ( nhl ) . the blackhawks beat the calgary flames in the conference quarter-finals 4 games to 2 , and the vancouver canucks 4 games to 2 in the conference semi-finals , but lost to the detroit red wings in the conference finals 1 game to 4 .
