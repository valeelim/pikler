Post-disco     the word `` post-disco '' refers to late 1970s and early 1980s music and movement of disco music . it have electronic/funk influenced ( affected ) sounds . post-disco was invented by djs and music producers in usa and the uk . post-disco music is similar to disco , however , is far more experimental and electronic based . often composed by djs or producers , it keeps many of disco 's original characteristics , yet , it often incorporates synthesizers or sequencers within its composition . post-disco music is a little bit rare , hard-to-find songs can be found for example in grand theft auto : vice city stories on fictional radio station paradise fm . however , post-disco impact can be heard in styles like house , techno , electro , crunk , dance-rock , italo-disco or dance-pop . post-disco artists include kashif , mtume , unlimited touch , kurtis blow , patrice rushen . other pages . - list of post-disco artists
