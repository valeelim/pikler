George Clooney     george timothy clooney ( born may 6 , 1961 ) is an american actor , movie director , screenwriter , and humanitarian . he is from lexington , kentucky and is the son of nick clooney and nephew of singer rosemary clooney . he dropped out of college to become an actor . he was on the tv show `` oy '' and several other tv shows in the 1980s and 1990s . in 1999 , he left `` er '' to act and direct movies , including `` ocean 's eleven , syriana , good night , and good luck '' and `` up in the air '' . he is also a political advocate for human rights issues , include save darfur . many of his movies contain references to current politics .
