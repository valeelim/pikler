Józef Kowalski     jozef kowalski ( 2 february 1900 - 7 december 2013 ) was the last polish veteran who served just after the first world war . he is the oldest verified military veteran in the world , following the death of gertrude noone . he fought in the polish-soviet war that was linked to the first world war . it lasted from 1919 to 1921. he is the only living person who is known to have definitely fought in that war . he lived in tursk , near sulęcin , in a care home . he died on 7 december 2013 , aged 113. other pages . - list of surviving veterans of world war i
