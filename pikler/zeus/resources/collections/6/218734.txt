Elisha Levy     elisha levy ( ; born on 18 november 1957 in jerusalem ) is a former professional football player and he is currently manager of israeli side maccabi haifa . levy played in hapoel hapoel beit sha'an youth team from 1968-1974. after playing in hapoel beit sha'an youth team , elisha levy was playing for hapoel beit sha'an from 1974-1982. for 1 year , 1982-1983 , elisha levy was played for maccabi haifa . in 1983-1987 , levy played in maccabi petah tikva elisha levy was playing for hapoel beit sha'an at 1987-1989. elisha levy was retired from football after those last two years in hapoel beit sha'an levy managed teams for 20 years . currently , elisha levy manages maccabi haifa .
