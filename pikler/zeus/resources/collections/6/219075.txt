Home Alone 3     home alone 3 is the third film in the home alone series . it is an american criminal comedy movie . the movie has a different household and villains than the first two films . the director was raja gosnell , the editor of both originl films and the star was alex d. linz . it was released on december 12 , 1997 by 20th century fox . the film grossed $ 79,082,515 worldwide . in this film the boy , alex , is sick at home with the chicken pox . he must defend his home against four burglars . a made-for-television sequel , `` home alone 4 : taking back the house '' , followed in 2002 and home alone 5 followed in 2012 .
