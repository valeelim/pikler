Poughkeepsie Bridge     the poughkeepsie bridge ( sometimes known as the poughkeepsie railroad bridge or the poughkeepsie-highland railroad bridge ) is a cantilever bridge over the hudson river . it goes from poughkeepsie , new york on the east side to highland , new york on the west side . it was built for trains and was completed on january 1 , 1889. it closed down on may 8 , 1974 after a bad fire . it became part of the national register of historic places in 1979. on october 3 , 2009 , the bridge was opened to the public as a pedestrian bridge and new york state park . it is now known as the walkway over the hudson . it cost $ 39 million to improve it .
