The Happiest Millionaire     the happiest millionaire is a 1967 musical movie , based upon the true story of philadelphia millionaire anthony j. drexel biddle . the movie received an academy award nomination for costume design by bill thomas . the musical song score is by robert and richard sherman . the screenplay is by aj carothers based on the play that was based on the book `` my philadelphia father '' by cordelia drexel biddle . this was the last live-action musical comedy movie produced by walt disney , who died during its production . songs . 1 . `` fortuosity '' 1 . `` what 's wrong with that ?
