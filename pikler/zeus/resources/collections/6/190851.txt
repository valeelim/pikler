Ali Pasha     ali pasha of tepelena or of yannina , the `` lion of yannina '' , ( 1741 – january 24 , 1822 ) was an albanian ruler ( pasha ) of the western part of rumelia , the ottoman empire 's european territory which was also called pashalik of janina . his court was in ioannina . his name in the local languages was : albanian : ali pashë tepelena , aromanian : ali pãshelu , greek : αλή πασάς τεπελενλής ali pasas tepelenlis or αλή πασάς των ιωαννίνων ali pasas ton ioanninon ( ali pasha of ioannina ) and turkish : tepedelenli ali paşa .
