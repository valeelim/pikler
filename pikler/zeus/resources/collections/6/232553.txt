Bowhead whale     the bowhead whale ( balaena mysticetus ) is an arctic baleen whale , a right whale with a large , bow-shaped head that is up to 40 % of its body length . the arched mouth is up to 10 feet ( 3 m ) wide and 20 feet ( 6 m ) deep . the eyes are very small and lips are huge . bowheads live in pods , are rich in blubber ( a subcutaneous layer 20-inch ( 50 cm ) thick in places ) , and have 2 blowholes .
