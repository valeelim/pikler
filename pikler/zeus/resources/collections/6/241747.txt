Corsairfly     corsair international is a french airline based at orly airport . they are the second largest airline in france and fly mainly holiday flights . the airline was started in 1981 and has since been bought over by tui ag , a german tour operator . now all of corsair international 's aircraft have the uniform tui colour scheme of a light blue body with white belly and red writing . these colours were put in place when the airline was called corsairfly . a new scheme using blue and white colours will replace the current one . corsair international fly to lots of destinations from paris . they concentrate on flights to the caribbean and french speaking african countries . fleet . - 2x airbus a330-200 - 5x boeing 747-400
