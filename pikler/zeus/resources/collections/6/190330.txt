Chuck Lorre     chuck lorre ( born charles michael levine on october 18 , 1952 ) is an american television writer , director , producer and composer . he was born in bethpage , long island , new york . he has worked on many american sitcoms , including `` grace under fire '' , `` dharma & greg '' , `` two and a half men '' , `` the big bang theory '' and `` mike & molly '' .
