Rapids     rapids are found on rivers . rapids are where the river bed is rocky , and the river runs fast over and around the rocks . rapids are found in the mountains near the beginning or `` headwaters '' of a river . the word `` rapids '' is always plural , ( like `` scissors '' and `` trousers '' ) . the word `` rapid '' means `` very fast '' . because of the splash made by the water going over rocks , rapids are also called `` whitewater '' . some people enjoying whitewater canoeing or whitewater rafting as a sport . related pages . - waterfall
