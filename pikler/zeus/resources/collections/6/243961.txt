Lights Out     `` lights out ' '' is a pop song by english singer rick astley . it was his first mainstream single in england for almost 17 years . the song was performed for the first time on peter kay 's 2010 tour , that took place at the men arena , manchester on 27 april 2010. astley was a special guest and performed a medley of his old hits before his new song . `` lights out '' was released on 7 june 2010 and was first broadcast on the ken bruce show on bbc radio 2 .
