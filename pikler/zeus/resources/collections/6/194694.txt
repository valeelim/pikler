Video game content rating system     a video game content rating system is a way to judge and label the content in a video game and what ages it is okay for . it is similar to the movie rating system . for example , games with violence or drugs might be marked as teen/adult games that might not be okay for children to play . games with nudity and adult content might be marked as adult only , which means that only adults can buy the game and play it . there are many different game content rating systems all over the world .
