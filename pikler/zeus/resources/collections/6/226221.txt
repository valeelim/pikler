Active Worlds     active worlds ( aw ) is a 3d virtual reality platform . the `` active worlds browser '' runs on windows . users assign themselves a user name , log into the active worlds virtual world universe , and explore 3d virtual worlds and environments that other users have created . users can chat with one another or build structures and areas from a selection of objects . aw allows users to own worlds and universes , and develop 3d content . related pages . - second life - imvu
