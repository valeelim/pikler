Kia Venga     the kia venga is a car produced by kia since 2009. it is based on the hyundai i20 and kia soul 's platform . the venga is available with 1.4 and 1.6 litre petrol engines with 66 kw/90 hp and 92 kw/125 hp respectively , a 1.4 litre turbo diesel engine in 55 kw/75 hp and 66 kw/90 hp versions , and a 1.6 litre turbo diesel engine in 85 kw/115 hp and 94 kw/128 hp versions .
