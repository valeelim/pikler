Bart Gets Hit by a Car     `` bart gets hit by a car '' is the tenth episode of `` the simpsons '' second season . the episode first started on january 10 , 1991. this episode has the first appearance of `` dr. nick riviera '' , `` lionel hutz '' , and the `` blue-haired lawyer '' . also , guest star phil hartman and the show 's then-script doris grau made their first episode in the show .
