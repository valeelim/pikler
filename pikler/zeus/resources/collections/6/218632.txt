Jathera     a jathera is a shrine constructed to commemorate common clan ancestors . each village in the punjab has a jathera which can be visited by members of different surnames . many villages have a jathera for each clan/surname . when members of a clan form a new village , they continue to visit the jathera in the ancestral village . if this is not possible , a link is brought from the old jathera to construct a new jathera in the new village . people visit the jathera when getting married , on the 15th day of the indian month , and sometimes on the first sunday of an indian month .
