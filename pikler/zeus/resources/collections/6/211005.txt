Mike Myers     michael john `` mike '' myers ( born may 25 , 1963 ) is a canadian actor , comedian , screenwriter and movie producer . he was a comedian on `` saturday night live '' from 1989 to 1995. he has acted in movies including : `` wayne 's world '' , `` austin powers '' , `` '' , `` austin powers in goldmember '' , `` shrek '' and `` the love guru '' . myers was born to english parents and raised in scarborough , toronto , ontario . he holds three citizenships : canadian , british and american .
