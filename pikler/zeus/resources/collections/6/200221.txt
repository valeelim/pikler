Battery Park City     battery park city is a 92-acre ( 0.4 kmÂ² ) planned community at the southwestern tip of lower manhattan in new york city , united states . the land upon which it stands was created on the hudson river using 1.2 million cubic yards ( 917,000 m3 ) of dirt and rocks excavated during the construction of the world trade center and certain other construction projects , as well as from sand dredged from new york harbor off staten island . the neighborhood , which is the site of the world financial center along with many housing , commercial , and retail buildings , is named for adjacent battery park . battery park city is owned and managed by the battery park city authority ( bpca ) , a public-benefit corporation created by new york state under the authority of the urban development corporation . excess revenue from the area was to be contributed to other housing efforts , typically low-income projects in the bronx and harlem . other websites . - battery park blog - covering battery park city , the financial district , and tribeca
