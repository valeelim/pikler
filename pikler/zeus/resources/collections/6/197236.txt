Antidepressant     an antidepressant is a type of medication that is used to treat people with mood disorders including depression . there are different types of antidepressant , called monoamine oxidase inhibitors ( maois ) , tricyclic antidepressants ( tcas ) , tetracyclic antidepressants ( tecas ) , selective serotonin reuptake inhibitors ( ssris ) and serotonin-norepinephrine reuptake inhibitors ( snris ) . many people take antidepressants to lower their sex drive . antidepressants work to improve the mood . doctors think that when someone is depressed , the chemicals in their brain are not working properly . antidepressants balance these chemicals , lifting the depression .
