Flying Spaghetti Monster     the flying spaghetti monster is the god of pastafarianism . it is often seen as a parody religion and thus has become popular with people opposed to the idea that god or gods created the world . followers of the flying spaghetti monster are sometimes called `` pastafarians '' . bobby henderson first described the flying spaghetti monster in 2005 in a letter to the kansas state board of education . henderson wrote the letter to protest rules about teaching intelligent design in public schools . the board of education required teachers to teach intelligent design . related pages . - evolution - kitzmiller v. dover area school district
