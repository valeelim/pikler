Pancreatic cancer     pancreatic cancer is cancer in the pancreas . it has a poor prognosis , with less than 5 % of patients surviving beyond five years after the cancer has been diagnosed . symptoms . - weight loss - nausea - jaundice - abdominal pain in cases of localized pancreatic disease , the surgery to remove the cancer is often the first treatment . it is typically followed by chemotherapy , often with gemzar or 5-fu .
