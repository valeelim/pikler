Wire-crested Thorntail     the wire-crested thorntail ( `` discosura popelairii '' ) is a species of hummingbird . it lives in colombia , ecuador and peru . their coloring is steel-blue , with white shafts , making a pretty sight when spread . the bird has a crest of bright green feathers . these birds feed on nectar from flowers using their long tongue or catch insects on the wing .
