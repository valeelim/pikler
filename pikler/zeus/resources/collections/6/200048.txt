Mandello del Lario     mandello del lario is an italian town and `` comune '' in the province of lecco , in lombardy . it is on the como lake . since 1921 , mandello del lario has been home to the italian motorcycle maker moto guzzi . guzzi is owned by piaggio & co. spa . each year since 2001 , the commune has hosted gmg ( aka giornata mondiale guzzi or worldwide guzzi days ) . the grigna massif is in mandello 's territory .
