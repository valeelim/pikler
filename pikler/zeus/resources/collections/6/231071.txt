Houses of the Holy     houses of the holy is the fifth album from rock band led zeppelin , released on march 28 1973 , but recorded in 1972. this album has many different music styles on it , like reggae , funk , acoustic songs and folk-like rock songs . many fans and critics thought when the album was released , that it was not as good as the album they made two years earlier , led zeppelin iv , but today it is seen as one of their best albums . songs like `` the song remains the same '' , `` no quarter '' , `` over the hills and far away '' and `` the rain song '' were performed for almost all tours after the release of the album . `` the song remains the same '' is also the title of their 1976 movie , but recorded in 1973 and 1974. official website houses of the holy info
