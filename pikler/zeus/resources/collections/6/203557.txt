Kolangal     kolangal is a tamil television series . it has been shown on sun tv for more than five years . there have been more than 1000 episodes made . it is directed by v. thiruselvam , who comes from nadiyam , a village in thanjavur district , tamil nadu . the story is about a woman , abhi , and her business rival and half brother , aditya . the serial is popular because abhi is a hard working , ambitious woman with strong beliefs . other websites . - kolangal tamil tv serial
