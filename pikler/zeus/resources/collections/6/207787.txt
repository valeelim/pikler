Lunch     luncheon , usually simply called lunch , is a meal that is usually eaten around noon or in the early afternoon . in most countries , it is the second meal of the day , after breakfast and before dinner . a lunch can be cold ( bread and fruit ) or hot ( a full meal , like a dinner ) . people that go to work or school during the day , usually have lunch together with their coworkers or classmates . some people bring lunch to work or school in a brown paper bag or a lunchbox , but you can also buy lunch at a restaurant . in some countries , lunch is the most important meal of the day .
