Hardelot Castle     hardelot castle ( ) is a castle in pas-de-calais in france . it is located in a field next to the village of condette , and south of the city of boulogne-sur-mer . the first castle at this place was built in the 12th century by the counts of boulogne . the present castle was built by count philippe hurepel of clermont in 1222. the castle was captured several times by the french , the english and the burgundians .
