Lefkada (city)    lefkada ( city ) lefkada city is a greek town which is the capital of the greek island lefkada . the population of lefkada city was 10,875 people , according to the 2001 census . it is located in the north of the island lefkada . here is the narrow isthmus which separates lefkada from continental greece . lefkada city is the first place visitors see , going to the island of lefkada from continental greece . lefkada has many earthquakes , so its houses are built by wood and metal .
