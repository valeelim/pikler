Silent dormouse     the silent dormouse ( graphiurus surdus ) is a species of rodent in the gliridae family . it is found in cameroon , democratic republic of the congo , equatorial guinea , and gabon . its natural habitat is subtropical or tropical moist lowland forests . references . - holden , m. e.. 2005. family gliridae . pp . 819-841 `` in '' mammal species of the world a taxonomic and geographic reference . d. e. wilson and d. m. reeder eds . johns hopkins university press , baltimore . - schlitter , d. 2004. graphiurus surdus . 2006 iucn red list of threatened species . downloaded on 29 july 2007 .
