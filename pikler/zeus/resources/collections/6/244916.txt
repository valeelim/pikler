Escitalopram     escitalopram is an antidepressant medication . it is used as a drug to help treat depression , obsessive-compulsive disorder , social anxiety disorder and other mental illnesses . it is commonly known and sold as `` lexapro '' or `` cipralex '' . escitalopram can cause sleeping problems , dry mouth and diarrhea . it can also cause sexual problems . these sexual problems usually disappear after a person stops using the drug . however , in some cases these problems can last for months or years . related pages . - fluoxetine
