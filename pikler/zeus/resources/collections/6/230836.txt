Faggot (slang)    faggot ( slang ) faggot ( or fag ) is a derogatory term for a homosexual . it is usually used to refer to males , but may also be used for females . a lot of people , especially homosexuals , see it as hate speech . they usually prefer the term gay , or sometimes queer . younger generations often use the term `` fag '' as an insult towards people that annoy them without actually linking the word to homosexuals .
