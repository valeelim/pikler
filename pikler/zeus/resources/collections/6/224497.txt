Harbor seal     the harbor seal ( `` phoca vitulina '' ) , also called the common seal , is a true seal . they are found in coastal waters of the northern atlantic and pacific ocean as well as baltic and north seas making them the most wide-ranging of the pinnipeds ( walruses , eared seals , and true seals ) . the females live longer than the males and females usually live to be 30 to 35 , while males usually live up to 20 or 25 years old .
