Jalal Dabagh     jalal dabagh ( born may 12 , 1939 in the city of silêmanî in southern-kurdistan ) , is a kurdish politician and writer/journalist . he is the party leader for the left party of south kurdistan . jalal dabagh has continuously taken part in the kurdish guerrilla movement , called the peshmerga , and has written and translated many books ; among other the kurdish translation of the communist manifesto .
