Shakhriyar Mamedyarov     shakhriyar mamadyarov ( born 12 april 1985 in sumgayit , azerbaijan ) , is a chess grandmaster . on the january 2008 fide rating list he reached number six in the world with a rating of 2760. he is known for being an excellent chess strategist . `` his unique style of play never fails to amaze his often surprised chess opponents '' .
