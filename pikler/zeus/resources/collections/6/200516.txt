Rhodes Scholarship     the rhodes scholarship named after cecil rhodes is an international award to go to school at the university of oxford and was the first large-scale group of international scholarships . famous rhodes scholars . - bill clinton , united states president - howard florey , australian scientist and nobel prize winner . - bob hawke , australian prime minister and union leader . ( 1953 ) - walter isaacson , managing editor of time magazine , ceo of cnn , ceo aspen institute . - kris kristofferson , american singer and actor .
