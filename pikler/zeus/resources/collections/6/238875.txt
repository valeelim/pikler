Seismometer     seismometer or seismograph is an instrument that measures motions of the ground , including those generated by earthquakes , nuclear explosions , and other sources . records of seismic waves called seismograms allow to map the interior of the earth , and locate and measure the size of those different sources . the word derives from the greek σεισμός , `` seismós '' , a shaking or quake , from the verb σείω , `` seíō '' , to shake ; and μέτρον , `` métron '' , measure . other websites . - the history of early seismometers - a java code applet demonstrating the operation of a damped-mass seismometer - keeping track
