Oddanchatram     oddanchtram is a city in the dindigul district of tamil nadu . it is known as the vegetable city of tamil nadu . plants grown here are maize , tomato , chilli , cauliflower , beans and other vegetables . the vegetables are exported to nearby states . also it is famous for its vegetable and curd market , which can sell up to 5,000 tons of produce a day . it has very good climatic conditions as it is surrounded by beautiful mountains . palani lord murugan temple is near oddanchatram . it is an ancient temple built on top of the palni hills . it is one of the most visited temples in tamil nadu , where people have to climb 600 steps to reach the shrine . it connected by road to major cities of tamil nadu including madurai , coimbatore , erode , trichy , chennai , and also to other states such as andhra pradesh and karnataka .
