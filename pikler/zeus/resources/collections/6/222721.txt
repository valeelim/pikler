Preston Wamsley     preston maki wamsley ( born april 29 , 1986 ) is an american television actor . wamsley is best known for his recurring role as `` ronnie wilson '' '' on the 1990s sitcom , `` the hughleys '' . wamsley has also made appearances on other sitcoms including `` my wife and kids '' , `` the nanny '' , and `` beyond belief : fact or fiction '' .
