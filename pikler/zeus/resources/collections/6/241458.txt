Fanconi anemia     fanconi anemia ( fa ) is a rare genetic disorder . it is a kind of anemia . people suffering from the disease produce a smaller amount of red and white blood cells . it is named after guido fanconi who first described the disease , in 1929. it should not be confused with fanconi syndrome . fanconi syndrome is a disorder affecting the kidneys . other websites . - fanconi anemia research fund - genereviews/ncbi/nih/uw entry on fanconi anemia - omim entries on fanconi anemia
