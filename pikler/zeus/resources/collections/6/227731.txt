Los Navegantes     los navegantes ( ; roughly translated as `` los navegantes neighborhood '' ) is a residential neighbourhood in the south-east of pichilemu , chile . it is located near the municipal stadium and la cruz hill . `` los navegantes '' boasts a perimeter of approximately 1.5 km and was founded between 1996-1997 and has a court . the pichilemu tsunami secure side , starts near the neighborhood .
