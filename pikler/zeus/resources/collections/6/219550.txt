Jason Earles     jason earles ( born april 26 , 1977 ) , is an american actor who is best known for playing the role of jackson stewart in hannah montana . jason earles was born in san diego , california . after his family had lived in both ohio and washington , they moved to oregon . before his family moved to southern california , they lived in billings , montana , where he graduated from rocky mountain college in 2000. he has given several pieces of hannah montana cast to the college 's fund raisers .
