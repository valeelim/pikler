Mazzy Star     mazzy star is an alternative music group popular for their songs `` fade into you '' and `` into dust '' . `` into dust '' was played on an episode of the tv drama the o.c.. in 2013 mazzy star released `` seasons of your day '' , their first album since 1996. the album got good reviews . discography . - `` she hangs brightly '' ( 1990 ) - `` so tonight that i might see '' ( 1993 ) - `` among my swan '' ( 1996 ) - `` seasons of your day '' ( 2013 )
