Geff Harrison     geff harrison ( also known as richie west , august 24 , 1947 , manchester , united kingdom ) , is an english singer-songwriter , mostly known for his bands king ping meh and twenty sixty six and then . he created the band modern talking along with dieter bohlen . in january 20 , 1986 , harrison filled a lawsuit against dieter bohlen claiming that he wrote english lyrics for modern talking and c.c.catch , but the lawsuit was settled out of court . according to the ruling of the court , bohlen and harrison created the group modern talking together .
