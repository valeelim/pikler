Red Bellied Black Snake     the red bellied black snake is a dangerous snake species found in australia . it is a red bellied snake , and black on top , hence the name . it lives mainly on the east coast of australia , close to a source of water . they can grow up to 1.5 metres in length . this snake usually gives birth to about 20 live snakes at one time . the bite of a red bellied black snake is very dangerous and requires immediate medical attention . the red-bellied black snake 's diet is mostly frogs . they also eat reptiles and small mammals . they also eat other snakes , even those of their own species .
