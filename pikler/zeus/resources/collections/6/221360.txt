Bart's Dog Gets an F    bart 's dog gets an f `` bart 's dog gets an f '' is the sixteenth episode of `` the simpsons '' second season . it was first shown on the fox broadcasting network in the united states on march 7 , 1991. in this episode , the simpson family 's dog , `` santa 's little helper '' , chews on homer 's new shoes . the dog also destroys a prized family treasure and eats homer 's cookie . the family gets angry at the dog . as a result , bart is forced to train `` santa 's little helper '' at an obedience school so that the family will not give him away . at first , the dog acts very badly . at the last minute , he passes the test .
