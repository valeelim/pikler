General Lagos     general lagos is a city in the region of arica and parinacota , in the north of chile . it is named after pedro lagos , who directed the assault to the morro de arica , during the battle of arica . it was founded in december 30 , 1927 as `` comuna de general lagos '' and they are called `` visvireños '' , since the most important area in the town is called visvirí .
