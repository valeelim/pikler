Fatigue     fatigue or tiredness is a physical or mental feeling . it happens when someone is ready to sleep or has done a lot of physical exercise . the person may feel weak , slow or sleepy . they may yawn , and may find it difficult to keep the eyes open . usual fatigue goes away with rest or sleep . fatigue may also be caused by physical or mental illness , jet lag , drug or alcohol use , medications , blood loss , malnutrition , starvation , stress or boredom .
