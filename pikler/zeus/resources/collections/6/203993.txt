Megatherium     megatherium was a huge , bulky , slow-moving mammal that lived during the pleistocene epoch in south america . it went extinct about 11,000 years ago . this ice-age mammal was the largest of the ground sloths ; megatherium 's name means `` great beast . '' megatherium was named by paleontologist richard owen in 1856 ; the first megatherium fossil was found in brazil in 1789 .
