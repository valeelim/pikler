Tropical Storm Alma     tropical storm alma was a tropical cyclone in the 2008 pacific hurricane season . it formed off the coast of costa rica on may 29. at first , it was forecast to be a weak storm , however , it quickly gained strength . it then hit land on nicaragua . alma was the first tropical storm to ever hit the pacific coast of nicaragua . lots of rain from the storm caused flooding , and eleven people were killed in the area . the remains later became tropical storm arthur in the atlantic . related pages . - lists of tropical cyclone names
