Bridgit Mendler     bridgit claire mendler ( born december 18 , 1992 ) is an american actor and singer . she was born in washington , d.c. and moved to mill valley , california at age eight . she acted in the movie `` alvin and the chipmunks 2 '' . she played pamela in `` alice upside down '' in 2007 , and kristen gregory in the movie version of `` the clique '' . she also guest starred in disney channel original series ' `` jonas '' and played juliet van heusen on `` wizards of waverly place '' . she plays teddy duncan in the disney channel original series `` good luck charlie '' plus she also played olivia white on lemonade mouth .
