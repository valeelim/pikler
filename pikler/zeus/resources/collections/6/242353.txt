Multiple myeloma     multiple myeloma is a type of cancer involving the plasma cells . some symptoms of the multiple myeloma are bone pain , renal failure , recurring infections and anemia . it is treated with chemotherapy . a commonly used chemotherapy regimen is the vincristine , adriamycin , and dexamethasone . thalidomides , velcades , melphalans , prednisones and interferons have also been used .
