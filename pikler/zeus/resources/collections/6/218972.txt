Take Me Away (Avril Lavigne song)    take me away ( avril lavigne song ) take me away is a song by canadian singer avril lavigne from her second album , `` under my skin '' . like the single before it ( `` losing grip '' ) , `` take me away '' has an edgy , post-grunge sound and started playing on canadian rock radio in march of 2004. at around the same time that do n't tell me was being promoted , as the lead single from `` under my skin '' , `` take me away '' was being released as the b-side on some copies of the `` do n't tell me '' single . `` take me away '' was released to radio only in australia in 2005 .
