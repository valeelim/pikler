Waltham Cross railway station     waltham cross railway station , opened in 1840 , is a railway station that serves waltham cross in hertfordshire , england . it is also the closest railway station to waltham abbey , essex , england . it is on the west anglia main line , and train services are provided by abellio greater anglia . during the 2012 summer olympics , waltham cross station ( along with cheshunt , the next northbound station ) served as the main access point for the broxbourne whitewater canoe and kayak slalom .
