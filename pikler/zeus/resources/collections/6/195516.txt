Silent Hill     silent hill is a horror video game series and there is a movie based on the same title . the games are mostly about bad or evil things that happen in the town called silent hill . the series is known for its monsters , especially pyramid head , and for its music . there have been 8 main games and 4 spin-off games . there was also an hd collection released with remastered ports of `` silent hill 2 '' and `` silent hill 3 '' . a game titled `` silent hills '' was in development for the playstation 4. on april 27 , 2015 , it was confirmed by konami that it had been cancelled . main games . - silent hill ( 1999 ) - silent hill 2 ( 2001 ) - silent hill 3 ( 2003 ) - silent hill 4 ( 2004 ) - ( 2007 ) - ( 2008 ) - ( 2009 ) - ( 2012 ) spin-offs . - ( 2007 ) - ( 2008 ) - ( 2007 ) - ( 2012 ) other websites . - official website
