Oligocene     the oligocene is the last geological epoch in the palaeogene . it lasted for about 11 million years , from 34 mya to 23 mya . it was preceded by the eocene and followed by the miocene . during the oligocene , the world continued to get cooler from its peak in the mid-eocene . the world was still largely forested , but grasslands began to spread . ice formed in antarctica . the period is seen as one of `` transition '' , both in climate and in the biota .
