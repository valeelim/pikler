Squat     squat is a renowned element of many folk dances . in powerlifting and bodybuilding , as well as in other sports , squat is an exercise that strengthens the muscles of the thighs , hips and buttocks , as well as the bones , ligaments and insertion of the tendons throughout the lower body . although the squat is a basic element of weight training , it has been the subject of considerable controversy in recent years . some trainers allege that squats are associated with injuries to the lumbar spine and knees . as a result , many trainers suggest that the standard barbell squat be modified in various ways . others , however , reject all suggestions that the exercise be abandoned , continuing to advocate the squat .
