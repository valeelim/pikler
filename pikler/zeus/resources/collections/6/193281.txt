Mr. Bean     mr. bean is a british comedy television series . it is made up of fourteen 25-minute episodes . all of them were written by and star rowan atkinson as the main character . different episodes were also written by robin driscoll , richard curtis and one by ben elton . the first episode was shown on itv on 1 january 1990. the last television episode , `` goodnight mr. bean '' broadcast on 31 october 1995. the final episode , `` hair by mr. bean of london '' , was a video released on 15 november 1995 , but not shown in the united kingdom until 2006 .
