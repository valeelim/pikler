Cartoon Planet     cartoon planet is a short lived spin-off of the adult swim program `` space ghost coast to coast '' . the show has random skits and songs with the characters space ghost , zorak , and brak . zorak and brak are comic foils for space ghost . moltar , a main character on coast to coast , does not appear on the show . in the credits everyone is credited by their first name only . the show has not been released on dvd , because of rights issues other than some segments being extras on the brak show volume one . in 2012 , the show has been revived but only brak and zorak host the show and the programming is cartoon network original shows such as `` ed , edd n eddy '' , `` dexter 's laboratory '' , `` the powerpuff girls '' , and others . cast . - andy merrill as brak - george lowe as space ghost - c. martin croker as zorak
