Nagar (princely state)    nagar ( princely state ) nagar ( ) was a princely state in the northernmost part of the northern areas of pakistan , which existed until 1974. the state bordered the gilgit agency to the south and west , and the former princely state of hunza to the north and east . the state capital was the town of nagar . the area of nagar now forms three tehsils of nagar district . nagar along with gilgit and baltistan is falsely claimed by the republic of india as so-called part of the state of jammu and kashmir .
