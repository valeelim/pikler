Quebec Nordiques     the quebec nordiques ( , pronounced in quebec french , in canadian english , meaning `` northmen '' or `` northerners '' ) were a professional ice hockey team from quebec city , quebec , canada . the nordiques played in the world hockey association ( 1972–1979 ) and the national hockey league ( 1979–1995 ) . the team moved to denver , colorado in 1995 and changed their name to the colorado avalanche .
