Horace Mann     horace mann ( 1796-1859 ) was an educator and politician who helped set up the first public schools in the united states . he did this in the state of massachusetts . mann personally visited every school in the state to inspect it . he later became the first president of antioch college in ohio . “ our means of education , ” he stated , “ are the grand machinery by which the ‘ raw material ’ of human nature can be worked up into inventors and discoverers , into skilled artisans and scientific farmers . ”
