Bloods     the bloods are a street gang . they were founded in 1972 in los angeles , california . they are rivals with the crips , another street gang . bloods members are known for wearing mostly red colors which is their gang color . during their rivalry with the crips in the 1970s , a faction of the crips called the pirus street gang broke away from the crips and joined other smaller gangs to make a gang which would later on become known as the bloods . some notable bloods are rappers game and suge knight . other websites . - bloods gang profile
