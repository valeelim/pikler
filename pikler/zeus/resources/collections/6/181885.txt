Stockwell tube station     stockwell tube station is a london underground station in stockwell , in the london borough of lambeth . it is situated between clapham north and oval on the northern line , and between vauxhall and brixton on the victoria line . it is also the only station on the morden branch of the northern line which has an interchange with another underground line . it is in travelcard zone 2. stockwell station is served by bus routes : 2 , 50 , 88 , 155 , 196 , 333 , 345 , p5 , n2 and n155 . london transport museum photographic archive :
