The Angel, Islington    the angel , islington the angel was originally an inn near a toll gate on the great north road ( at what is now the corner of islington high street and pentonville road ) . the term now informally refers to this part of islington in london . the corner itself is actually in finsbury which was a separate borough until 1965 when the metropolitan borough of finsbury merged with the metropolitan borough of islington to form the london borough of islington . related pages . - angel tube station other websites . - angel islington info and directory - islington council - weather forecast for islington
