Pentium     pentium ( made in 1992 ) is an x86 microprocessor . it is a brand name by intel who developed the pentium i ( 1992-1999 ) , pentium ii ( 1997-1999 ) , pentium iii ( 1999-2003 ) , pentium 4 ( 2001-2008 ) , pentium m ( 2003-2008 ) , pentium d ( 2005-2008 ) and pentium extreme edition ( 2005-2008 ) processors . today , it is the medium range microprocessor in the intel microprocessor lineup , above the celeron and atom and below the core family of microprocessors .
