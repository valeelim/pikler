The War of the Simpsons     `` the war of the simpsons '' is the twentieth episode of `` the simpsons '' second season . it first started on the fox network in the united states on may 2 , 1991. in this episode , homer gets drunk at a party and embarrasses his wife marge , so she decides to sign them up for a `` marriage counseling retreat . '' homer finds out that the retreat will be held near a lake and packs his fishing stuff , despite marge telling him that all they will be doing is their differences . at the lake the next morning , homer tries to sneak away to go fishing , but marge catches him and he takes a walk instead . on the dock , homer finds an abandoned fishing pole and a small rowboat . homer rows out on the lake and catches his fish , but when he sees an upset marge is looking at him , he quickly lets it go to show his love for her .
