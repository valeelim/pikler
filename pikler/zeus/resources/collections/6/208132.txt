Yaniv Katan     yaniv katan ( , born on january 27 , 1981 in kiryat ata , israel ) is a forward/winger for maccabi haifa f.c.. yaniv katan is also the 2nd player to score a hat-trick for maccabi haifa against a team from europe . katan is also a member of the israel national football team . he has played for maccabi haifa for most of his career since 1998. he played for west ham united for several games in 2006 , but then returned to maccabi haifa . other websites . - yaniv katan stats
