Rheydt     rheydt is a borough of the city of mönchengladbach , germany . it lies in the very west of north rhine-westphalia . the first written mention of the town is in 1180. from 1918 until 1933 it was part of the city of mönchengladbach . dr. joseph goebbels , who was born in rheydt , arranged for the city to get back its independence . it is a town with many factories , mainly silk and textiles . it was bombed heavily during world war ii . in 1975 it again became part of mönchengladbach . the central station , rheydt hauptbahnhof , kept its original name , which makes mönchengladbach the only city in germany to have two stations called hauptbahnhof . schloss rheydt , one of the best preserved palaces of the renaissance period , is located in rheydt .
