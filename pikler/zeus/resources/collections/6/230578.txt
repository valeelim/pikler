5150: Home 4 tha Sick    5150 : home 4 tha sick 5150 : home 4 tha sick is the first ep by american rapper , eazy-e. it was released on december 28 , 1992. the producers of the album were naughty by nature , cold 187um and bobby `` bobcat '' ervin . the ep was certified gold on february 9 , 1993 by the riaa . the ep is now out of print and all the songs that were included the ep are on the remastered version of `` eazy-duz-it '' . the only single released on the ep was `` only if you want it '' .
