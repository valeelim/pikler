Sochi     sochi ( russuan : со́чи ) is a city in russia on the black sea coast . it is in the oblast of krasnodar krai . the 2014 winter olympics were played there and the 2014 winter paralympics are being played there right now . the sochi olympic stadium will also be used to host 2018 fifa world cup football matches . sochi is also a popular health resort . it is one of very few places in russia with a humid subtropical climate ( `` cfa '' in the koeppen climate classification ) . other websites . - official site of xxii olympic winter games and xi paralympic winter games of 2014 in sochi
