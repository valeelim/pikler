Mercedes-Benz Sprinter     the mercedes-benz sprinter is a series of vans , trucks and buses produced by mercedes-benz since 1995. it was facelifted in 2000 and entered its second generation in 2006. the sprinter is available with 4- , 5- and 6-cylinder diesel engines and 4- and 6-cylinder petrol engines . the volkswagen lt and the volkswagen crafter were derivatives of the sprinter .
