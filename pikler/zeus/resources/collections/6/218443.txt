Alice DeeJay     alice deejay was a dutch electronic music group , who achieved success from 1999 to 2002. the group consisted of record producers pronti ( born sebastiaan molijn ) , kalmani ( born eelke kalberg ) and dj jurgen , with lead singer judith anna pronk . the groups best known song is `` better off alone '' from their 2000 album , `` who needs guitars anyway ?
