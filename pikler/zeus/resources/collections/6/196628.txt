Unforgiven: In Your House    unforgiven : in your house unforgiven : in your house was a professional wrestling pay-per-view show made by world wrestling federation ( wwf ) . it was held on april 26 , 1998 at greensboro coliseum in greensboro , north carolina . it was the 21st pay-per-view to be held under the wwf 's in your house name . the ppv was also the first to use the `` scratch '' logo for their promotional work , while wrestlemania xiv featured the logo on the ring apron , the official graphic still featured the new generation logo . the pay-per-view also held the first ever inferno match and wwf 's first evening gown match . other websites . - official unforgiven 1998 website
