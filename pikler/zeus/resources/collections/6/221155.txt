Itchy &amp; Scratchy &amp; Marge    itchy & amp ; scratchy & amp ; marge `` itchy & scratchy & marge '' is the ninth episode of `` the simpsons '' second season . it was first aired on december 20 , 1990. in this episode , maggie attacks homer with a mallet and marge blames `` the itchy & scratchy show '' for maggie 's actions . it was written by john swartzwelder and was the first episode to be directed by jim reardon . alex rocco makes his first of three guest appearances as roger meyers , jr .
