Lake Placid, New York    lake placid , new york lake placid is a village in northern new york state . it has a population of 2,638. it was founded in the early 1880s to look for iron . today it is a ski resort . it has hosted the winter olympics twice , in 1932 and 1980. other websites . - web site of the lake placid/essex county visitors bureau - web site of the lake placid news - lake placid information - lake placid olympic authority
