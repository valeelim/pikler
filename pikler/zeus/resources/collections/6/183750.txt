Hyderabad Division     hyderābād district ( ) , is a district of sindh , pakistan . before , it was an administrative division of the sindh called the hyderabad division . however , reforms of 2000 the third tier of government . its capital is the city of hyderabad . the kirthar national park is in the hyderabad district . according to the 1998 census of pakistan , it had a population of 4,339,445 of which 50.07 % were urban ( lived in a city ) . this makes it the second-most urbanised district of sindh .
