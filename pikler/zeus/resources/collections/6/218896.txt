Jiffy Lube     jiffy lube is a chain of businesses that services automobiles . it is most well known for quick oil changes . the one-way design of the building allows for fast service . in addition to oil changes , jiffy lube mechanics are trained to inspect cars and suggest service when needed . they can do many services themselves . jiffy lube is owned by pennzoil-quaker state , part of sopus ( shell oil products united states ) , part of royal dutch/shell group .
