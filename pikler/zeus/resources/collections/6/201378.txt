Jacques Villeneuve     jacques joseph charles villeneuve , oq ( born april 9 , 1971 in saint-jean-sur-richelieu , quebec ) is a canadian automobile racing driver . he is the son of formula one driver gilles villeneuve , and is the namesake of his uncle ( also a racer ) . he raced in formula one from 1996 to 2006 , driving for williams , bar , renault and bmw sauber . he won the world championship in 1997 with williams , in only his second season , the first person to do so that quickly ( except for the 1950 season , the very first season in f1 ) .
