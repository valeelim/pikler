The Bash (2009)    the bash ( 2009 ) the bash was a professional wrestling pay-per-view ( ppv ) event produced by world wrestling entertainment ( wwe ) , which took place on june 28 , 2009 at the arco arena in sacramento , california . this was the sixth show in wwe 's production lineage for the bash event ( twentieth overall ) and was the first to be referred to as the bash . it featured talent from the raw , smackdown , and ecw brands . there were eight matches on for the event 's card . other pages . - wwe the bash - list of wwe pay-per-view events
