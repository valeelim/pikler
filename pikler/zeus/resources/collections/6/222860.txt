Dietzenbach     dietzenbach is a city in the offenbach rural district in the state of hesse , germany . the town is also the capital of the offenbach rural district ( `` landkreis offenbach '' ) dietzenbach is in the `` rhine-main area '' 25 km southeast of frankfurt . the whole area of the town is 21,67 km large and it is 135 m above sea level . it is a plain area with nearly no hills . about 35 to 20 million years ago in the age of tertiary the whole region was lying under sea level . at that time it was covered by an ocean which was warm and not very deep . lots of sediments sank to its bottom . then the region rose again and the water left , leaving soil which is mostly sandy and poor . the city consists out of the urban districts `` neue stadtmitte '' , `` spessartviertel '' , `` altstadt '' , `` steinberg '' , `` hexenberg '' and `` wingertsberg '' . the neighbours of dietzenbach are : - heusenstamm ( north ) - rodgau ( east ) - r√∂dermark ( south ) - dreieich ( west ) dietzenbach has 33,067 inhabitants ( 2008 ) . about 30 % of the people living here are from foreign countries . they come from many different nations .
