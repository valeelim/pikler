King's Sutton railway station    king 's sutton railway station king 's sutton railway station serves the village of king 's sutton in northamptonshire , england . it is also the nearest railway station to the town of brackley . it was opened on 1 december 1873 by the great western railway . the station is managed by chiltern railways , who provide services along with first great western . it was heavily renovated in 2009 , with new fencing , a new shelter and a bridge being added to it .
