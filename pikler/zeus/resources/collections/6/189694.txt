The Veronicas     the veronicas are an australian pop rock duo from brisbane . the band was formed in 2004. it was started by twin sisters jessica and lisa origliasso . they started working in show business at age five . they broke with their record label , warner , in 2014. the twins said “ after five years of being in a 'warner brothers records lockdown ' , we are officially able to move forward releasing and recording music without our past label dictating our creative journey , or withholding our musical releases and touring. ” the veronicas then signed a deal with sony . their first album was released in 2005 , called `` the secret life of ... '' . this album peaked at # 2 on the australian charts . it received an aria certification of four times platinum , for more than 280,000 sales . their second album was called `` hook me up '' . it was released in 2007 . `` hook me up '' peaked at # 2 on the australian charts . it received a certification of two times platinum . the album 's title song , `` hook me up '' , was the veronicas ' first number one single in australia .
