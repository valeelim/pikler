Montigny-lès-Metz     montigny-lès-metz is a city in northeast france . it is on the moselle river , near metz . the habitants are called `` montigniens '' . the first humans found in montigny-lès-metz were here since paleolithic . montigny comes from two latin words . - mons = mount , mountain , height - ignis = fire so , the `` fire mountain '' , because the gauls start up a fire on the st quentin mont to correspond with the other villages .
