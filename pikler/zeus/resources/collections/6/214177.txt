Holborn     holborn ( ) is an area of central london , england . holborn is also the name of the area 's principal east-west street . the street runs from st giles 's high street as high holborn to gray 's inn road to holborn viaduct . it crosses the borders of the city of westminster , london borough of camden and the city of london . other websites . - holborn and bloomsbury , by sir walter besant and geraldine edith mitton , 1903 , from project gutenberg
