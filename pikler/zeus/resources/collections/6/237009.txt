Olmedilla Photovoltaic Park     the olmedilla photovoltaic park in olmedilla de alarcón , spain , is the world ’ s largest photovoltaic plant . built in 2008 , the plant uses more than 160,000 solar photovoltaic panels to generate 60 megawatts ( peak ) . it produces enough electricity to power more than 40,000 homes . related pages . - photovoltaics
