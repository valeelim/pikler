2009–10 Boston Bruins season     the 2009–10 boston bruins season is the bruins ' 85th season in the national hockey league ( nhl ) . their regular season began with a nationally-televised home game against the washington capitals on october 1 , 2009 , and ended with a road game against the same capitals team on april 11 , 2010. the bruins failed to defend their regular-season division and conference titles from the 2008-09 season .
