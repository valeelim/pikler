Robber     a robber is a person who steals or takes something belonging to someone else . robbers use force or threats to take what they want . a bank robber is someone who steals money from a bank . when the robber takes something , this is called a robbery . one famous robbery was the great train robbery in england . armed robbery means that the robber used weapons such as a gun in the robbery . many robbers have become infamous , such as jesse james in america and ben hall in australia . australian robbers used to be called bushrangers . the word `` rob '' comes from a latin word , `` deraubare '' . this word came from a german word `` raub '' which means clothes . before clothes were made in factories and became cheap , robbers often took a person 's clothes . other pages . - robbery - bushrangers
