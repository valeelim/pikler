Angel Road railway station     angel road railway station is in the london borough of enfield at edmonton in north east london . it is in travelcard zone 4 , on the tottenham hale branch of the lea valley lines . the station , and all trains serving it , is operated by abellio greater anglia . it is partially located beneath the a406 road ( which is named `` angel road '' at that point ) . due to reconstruction of the a406 in the mid-1990s ( involving grade-separation ) , the station no longer has an entrance on `` angel road '' , with the only access via a footpath running south from `` conduit lane '' to the north . the station is unstaffed . other websites . - timetables from 'one '
