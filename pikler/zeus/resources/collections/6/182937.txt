Tegan and Sara     tegan and sara ( ) is a canadian indie rock duo , made up of twin sisters tegan and sara quin . they are lesbians . they have released eight albums . at first they called themselves plunk . they released their first album , `` under feet like ours '' , in 1999 as sara and tegan . in 2000 they released `` the business of art '' , their second album . they were nominated for four prizes at the 2014 juno awards . they are feminists .
