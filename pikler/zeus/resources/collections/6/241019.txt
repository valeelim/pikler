Stroke (engine)    stroke ( engine ) a stroke is the full single action of an engine to intake , compress , combust , and finally to exhaust a fuel . this action is typical of a 4-stroke engine . the combined action of intake , compression , combustion , exhaust , causes the shaft of the engine to spin . this action is repeated at a rate referred to as rpm 's , revolutions per minute . the way `` rpm 's '' are measured varies by the type of motor , and what `` stroke '' that motor is .
