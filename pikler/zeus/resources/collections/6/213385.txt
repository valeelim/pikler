Gugu Liberato     gugu liberato , stage name of antônio augusto de moraes liberato ( born são paulo , 10 april 1959 ) is a brazilian television host and businessman . of portuguese descent , gugu wrote letters to silvio santos suggesting programs , which hired him . started in television at age fourteen as a production assistant on the program domingo no parque , presented by silvio santos no sbt . it is considered one of the largest and most successful brazilian television hosts of all time . the presenter is the owner of a fortune of about $ 1 billion ( 500 million u.s. dollars ) , built over a career started as an office boy , gugu set up an empire in which there is a video production and tv , 16 stores that bear his name , a water park and a gas station .
