Roger Goodell     roger goodell ( born february 19 , 1959 ) is the current commissioner of the national football league . he has held this post since august 8 , 2006 when he took over duties for the retiring paul tagliabue . as the commissioner , he has focused on how players behave off of the field , making more rules that can lead to players who get into trouble off of the field or with the law being fined or suspended .
