Asclepias asperula     antelope horns ( `` asclepias asperula '' ) is a species of milkweed plant . it is also known as green-flowered milkweed , spider antelope horns and spider antelopehorns . it belongs to the subfamily asclepiadoideae . it is native to the southwestern united states and northern mexico . it blooms from april to june . this species likes to grow in moist sandy or rocky soil . antelope horns grows from 0.3 to 0.6 meters ( 1-2 feet ) . other websites . - `` asclepias asperula '' at usda plants database - `` asclepias asperula '' at kansas wildflowers and grasses
