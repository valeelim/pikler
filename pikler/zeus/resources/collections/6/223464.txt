Scott Brown (Scottish footballer)    scott brown ( scottish footballer ) scott brown ( born 25 june 1985 in dunfermline , fife ) is a scottish association football player , currently playing for scottish premier league club celtic . brown , who started his professional career with hibernian , is a regular in the scottish national team . his transfer to celtic from hibs in the summer of 2007 broke the record for the biggest transfer fee paid between two scottish clubs .
