Coronel Fabriciano     coronel fabriciano is a brazilian city in the vale do aço metropolitan area , state of minas gerais . its population in 2009 was 105.037 inhabitants . its area is 221,049 km² . the city was founded in december 27 , 1948. the devolviment of the municipality due to the large companies in the region , such as arcelormittal timóteo ( former acesita ) and usiminas that when opened were located in territory fabricianense . but in 1964 , with the political emancipation of ipatinga and timóteo , coronel fabriciano longer hosts them . it is limited with the municipal districts ipatinga , timóteo , ferros , joanésia , antônio dias and mesquita . other websites . - official site of coronel fabriciano
