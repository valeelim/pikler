Baron Davis     baron walter louis davis ( born april 13 , 1979 in los angeles , california ) is a professional basketball player who currently plays for the new york knicks of the nba . he played college basketball at ucla . davis has also played in the nba for the charlotte/new orleans hornets from 1999 to 2005 , the golden state warriors from 2005 to 2008 , the los angeles clippers from 2008 to 2011 and the cleveland cavaliers in 2011. other websites . - official website - nba player profile
