José Arraño     josé santos arraño acevedo ( 14 october 1921 - 24 november 2009 ) was a famous writer and historian from pichilemu , mostly known for his books on the history of the city . arraño was one of the 12 sons of josé luis arraño ortiz and maría soledad acevedo caro . during his childhood he lived in pichilemu , and he later went to study in `` los hermanos maristas '' school in san fernando and the pontifical seminary of santiago . he was a collaborator of the santiago 's press through articles devoted to pichilemu and colchagua , publishing in newspapers such as `` las últimas noticias '' or `` la época '' and in dozens of other regional newspapers , taking arraño acevedo as the most important historian of pichilemu and the most dedicated to promote pichilemu 's tourism . all his articles were included in two books : `` pichilemu y sus alrededores turísticos '' and `` hombres y cosas de pichilemu '' . he habitually read his articles in his radial program , that was broadcasted for almost a decade , `` la hora de josé arraño acevedo '' .
