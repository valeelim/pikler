Johnny Rozsa     johnny rozsa is a well known new york photographer . rozsa was born and raised in nairobi . he arrived in london in the 1960s and , after college , ran a vintage shop in covent garden . here he met fashion editors , models , actors , and photographers on a daily basis . . rozsa photographed famous people including : hugh grant , halle berry , janet jackson , nicholas cage , john malkovich and natasha richardson rozsa published a book in 2009 called `` untouched '' , which included many of his photographs . other websites . - official website
