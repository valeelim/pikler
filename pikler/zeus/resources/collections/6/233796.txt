G-sharp major     g-sharp major is a major scale based on the musical note g sharp . its key signature has six sharps and one double sharp . to make reading easier , g-sharp major is usually written as its enharmonic equivalent of a-flat major . however , it does appear as a secondary key area in several works in sharp keys , for example in the prelude and fugue in c sharp major from bach 's well-tempered klavier , book 1. the g sharp minor prelude and fugue from the same set end with a picardy third in g sharp major . g sharp major is used for a short time in several of chopin 's nocturnes in c sharp minor .
