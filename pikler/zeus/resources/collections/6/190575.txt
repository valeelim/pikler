First Capital Connect     first capital connect ( fcc ) was a train company in england that ran the thameslink railway line between brighton and bedford and services along the east coast main line from king 's cross and peterborough , cambridge , and king 's lynn from 1 april 2006 to 13 september 2014. it was owned by first group and combines the service on formerly operated by wagn , often referred to as great northern . it failed to keep the franchise , and it was succeeded by thameslink and great northern .
