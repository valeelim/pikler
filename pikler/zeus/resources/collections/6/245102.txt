Volkswagen Touareg     the volkswagen touareg is a suv produced by volkswagen sine 2002. it entered its second generation in 2010. the touareg is available with 6- , 8- and 12-cylinder petrol engines and 5- , 6- and 10-cylinder diesel engines . the body of the touareg were also used for the audi q7 and the porsche cayenne .
