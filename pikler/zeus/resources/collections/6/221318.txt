One Fish, Two Fish, Blowfish, Blue Fish    one fish , two fish , blowfish , blue fish `` one fish , two fish , blowfish , blue fish '' is the eleventh episode of the simpsons second season . it first started on the fox network in the united states on january 24 , 1991. in this episode , homer eats a poisonous fugu fish at a sushi restaurant , and is told that he has only twenty-four hours left to live . he accepts his and makes a list of all the things he wants to do before he dies . some of the things he wanted to do include : hanging out with grandpa .
