Henan     henan is a province of the people 's republic of china . it is in the central eastern part of the country . the name henan means `` south of the yellow river . '' its provincial capital is zhengzhou . its major cities are anyang , hebi , jiaozuo , kaifeng , luohe , luoyang , nanyang , pingdingshan , puyang , sanmenxia , shangqiu , xinxiang , xinyang , xuchang , zhengzhou , zhoukou and zhumadian . henan is now the most populous province of china . the abbreviation of henan is yu ( 豫 ) , since there is another province that has the first syllable `` he '' ( 河 ) in its name , hebei ( 河北 ) .
