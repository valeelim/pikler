Faizabad     faizabad is city in uttar pradesh of india . the population of faizabad city is 144,924 as per 2001 census . faizabad is especially known for being nearest city from ayodhya . faizabad is around 7 km from ayodhya . faizabad is on the road to ayodhya . hence every person from south region going to ayodhya must go through faizabad .
