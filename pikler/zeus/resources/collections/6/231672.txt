Rascal Flatts     rascal flatts is a country music band from columbus , ohio . they became a band in 1999 and released their debut album `` rascal flatts '' in 2000. they are signed to lyric street records and have won many cmt and acm awards . their newest album is `` unstoppable '' , which came to stores in 2009. albums . - `` rascal flatts '' ( 2000 ) - `` melt '' ( 2002 ) - `` feels like today '' ( 2004 ) - `` me and my gang '' ( 2006 ) - `` still feels good '' ( 2007 ) - `` greatest hits volume 1 '' ( 2008 ) - `` unstoppable '' ( 2009 ) rascal flatts official website
