Old Money     `` old money '' is the seventeenth episode of the simpsons second season . it first started on the fox network in the united states on march 28 , 1991. in this episode , grampa 's new girlfriend at the `` retirement castle '' dies and leaves him with $ 106,000 . he heads for a casino to spend the money , but is stopped by homer , so he decides to spend the money on the retirement home instead .
