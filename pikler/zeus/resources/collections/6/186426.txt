American Embassy School     the american embassy school is an international school in new delhi , india . the campus is spread over twelve acres , and it accommodates up to 34 faculty families on campus . the school , despite its large area , is continuously being expanded to accommodate additional students . the school accepts students from nursery to twelfth grade . aes is unique , since it is an international school . all lessons , except for special language classes , are held in english . there are about 1500 students in the school , and although 35 % of them are americans , the rest of the students come from 53 different nationalities . the school 's motto is `` enter to learn ... leave to serve . '' the curriculum at aes follows the college preparatory program that 's undertaken in american private schools . at high school , students have the opportunity to take the program of either international baccalaureate ( ib ) or advanced placement ( ap ) as well as a regular high school program . other websites . - the school homepage
