Centriole     the centriole is a cytoplasmic structure in most eukaryote cells . it is involved in cell division and in the formation of cilia and flagella . centrioles are not found in vascular plants and in most fungi . most centrioles are nine sets of microtubule triplets , arranged in a cylinder . a pair of centrioles , arranged perpendicularly and surrounded by a mass of dense material makes up the centrosome .
