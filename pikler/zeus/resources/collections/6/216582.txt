Generator (Bad Religion album)    generator ( bad religion album ) generator is the sixth album released by the american punk rock band bad religion . although the album was recorded and finished in may 1991 , it was not officially released until march 1992 . 100,000 copies of the album were shipped . `` generator '' was also the band 's first release with drummer bobby schayer , who replaced pete finestone during the `` against the grain '' tour . today , `` generator '' has been remembered as one of the best albums to emerge from the early 90s punk scene , and one of the most enduring works of bad religion 's career . a video was made for `` atomic garden '' . it is available for watching here . this was the first video bad religion ever released . track list . 1 . `` generator '' ( gurewitz ) - 3:21 1 . `` too much to ask '' ( graffin ) - 2:45 1 . `` no direction '' ( graffin ) - 3:14 1 . `` tomorrow '' ( graffin ) - 1:56 1 . `` two babies in the dark '' ( gurewitz ) - 2:25 1 . `` heaven is falling '' ( gurewitz ) - 2:04 1 . `` atomic garden '' ( gurewitz ) - 3:10 1 . `` the answer '' ( graffin ) - 3:21
