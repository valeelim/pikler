Sarah Sutton     sarah sutton ( born 12 december 1961 in basingstoke , hampshire , england ) is an english actress . she is well known for her role as nyssa in the bbc television series `` doctor who '' . nyssa was a companion of tom baker and peter davison 's doctors from 1981 to 1983. she was the youngest female actor to play a companion in the series . since leaving `` doctor who '' she has done very little acting . she has made appearances as sarah dryden in the bbc 's medical drama series `` casualty '' and as wendy in the serial `` unnatural pursuits '' . earlier roles were `` alice through the looking glass '' ( 1974 ) as alice , `` the moon stallion '' ( 1978 ) as diana purwell and `` the crucible '' ( 1980 ) as susannah walcott .
