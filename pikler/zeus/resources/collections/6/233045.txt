Sunshine Skyway Bridge     sunshine skyway bridge is a highway bridge in tampa bay , florida , that crosses 29,040 feet ( 5.5 miles or about 8.85 km ) . every day , about 50,500 vehicles ( cars and trucks ) cross over the bridge . the bridge is considered florida 's `` flag bridge '' ( florida 's most important bridge ) . on may 9 , 1980 , a freighter ship , called the mv `` summit venture '' , ran into one of the bridge 's supporting columns . 1200 feet ( 366 meters ) of the bridge fell into the water , and 35 people were killed . the bridge was reconstructed to its current version in 1987 .
