Free (band)    free ( band ) free were an english rock band . the band started in london , england in 1968. they are best known for their famous song called `` all right now '' . when the band ended the singer , paul rodgers , went on to form the band bad company . the lead guitarist of free was named paul kossoff . in 1976 he died from a drug-induced heart failure . he was only 25 years old .
