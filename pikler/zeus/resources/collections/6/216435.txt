Robbinsville Township, New Jersey    robbinsville township , new jersey robbinsville is a township in mercer county , new jersey . as of the year 2000 , 10,275 people live in robbinsville . the town was incorporated , or created as a town , on march 15 , 1859. robbinsville is 20.5 square miles in size . the median income is $ 71,377 . the main transportation systems within robbinsvile are us route 130 , route 33 , interstate 195 and the new jersey turnpike ( interstate 95 ) . trenton-robbinsville airport is also located within robbinsville . about 30,000 aircraft enter and exit the airport a year .
