Brahui people     the brahui people ( brahui : بروہی ) are the only dravidian ethnic group in pakistan . of the 2.2 million brahuis , most live in baluchistan and afghanistan , where they are native . they are closely linked to the balochs with whom they have intermingled , and whose cultural traits they have absorbed . linguistically they were believed to be a remnant of the inhabitants of the ivc . the brahui language , also called bravi , is a dravidian language .
