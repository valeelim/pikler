Meet the Robinsons     meet the robinsons is a 2007 american animated movie produced by walt disney animation studios and distributed by walt disney pictures . it is the 47th movie in the walt disney animated classics series . the movie tells the story of a small boy named lewis , who is very smart and invents things but he does not have any family , even though he wants one . then one day , lewis ' world was changed when a boy called wilbur , who has a time-machine , takes lewis on a trip to the future to meet wilbur 's family - the robinsons . the story of this movie is based on a children 's book called `` a day with wilbur robinson '' , written by william joyce .
