Homer's Odyssey    homer 's odyssey `` homer 's odyssey '' is the third full length episode of `` the simpsons '' , that first started on the fox network on january 21 , 1990. in this episode , homer becomes a for people 's safety in springfield , and is made a higher rank in his current job as `` nuclear safety inspector '' for the entire power plant . it was written by jay kogen and wallace wolodarsky and was the first `` simpsons '' script to be completed , although it was the third episode produced .
