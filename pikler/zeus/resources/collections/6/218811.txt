Norwegian Nobel Committee     the norwegian nobel committee is the group which gives the nobel peace prize every year . it has five members who are elected by the norwegian parliament and has the same political makeup as the parliament . alfred nobel died in december 1896 , and in his will he left a lot of money to start a number of prizes to given each year , to be called the nobel prize . the money would be looked after by the nobel foundation , and the prizes would be given out by a number of different groups . the other nobel prizes would be given by existing swedish groups , the swedish academy , the royal swedish academy of sciences , and the karolinska institutet . the job of giving the nobel peace prize was given to the norwegian parliament . a group of five people , to be called the norwegian nobel committee , would decide who should win the prize . the peace prize would be given to the person who had done the most work to get countries to work together , to make the world 's armies smaller , or to hold peace talks when needed .
