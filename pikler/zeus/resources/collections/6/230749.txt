Newgrounds     newgrounds is an american website hosted in glenside , pennsylvania , united states . created in 1995 , the site has adobe flash animations and games , but also features a music and art portal . the site was started and is owned by tom fulp . newgrounds is different from most other flash websites by its automated submission and rating system . it was the first website in history to produce a fully automated user-made content system . related pages . - adobe flash
