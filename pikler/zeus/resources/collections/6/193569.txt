Microsoft OneNote     onenote is for making and sharing notes . the first version was in office 2003. onenote is in microsoft office enterprise 2007. onenote is a important part of microsoft office enteprise and ultimate 2007. onenote is a relatively new part of microsoft office . along with other programs , such as infopath , uses the ribbon user interface . users may add embedded multimedia recordings and web links . for onenote to work , it needs the windows 2000 sp ( service pack ) 4 operating system or later . onenote 's 'pages ' are called notebooks .
