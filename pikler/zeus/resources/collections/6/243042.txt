David Aardsma     david allen aardsma is an major league baseball pitcher . he plays for the seattle mariners and is the team 's closer . he was born in denver , colorado on december 27 , 1981. in 2009 , aardsma earned 38 saves for the mariners , which tied him for fourth place in the american league with 38 saves . if the names of all players in major league baseball history were listed in alphabetical order , aardsma 's name would come first .
