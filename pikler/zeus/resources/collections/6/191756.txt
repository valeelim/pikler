Sun Microsystems     sun microsystems inc. ( nasdaq : java ) is a major supplier of unix based computers and are the makers of the solaris operating system and the sparc line of microprocessors . the company is in santa clara , california . it was started in 1982 by vinod khosla , andy bechtolsheim , and scott mcnealy . it has grown to become one of the largest makers of unix computers in the world . in january 2010 , it was bought by oracle corporation .
