Gustav Klimt     gustav klimt ( 14 july 1862 – 6 february 1918 ) was an austrian symbolist painter . he was one of the most prominent members of the vienna secession movement . his major works include paintings , murals , sketches , and other art objects . many of them are on display in the vienna secession gallery . klimt 's primary subject was the female body , and his works are marked by a frank eroticism — nowhere is this more apparent than in his numerous drawings in pencil ( see `` mulher sentada '' , below ) . klimt died of influenza in vienna .
