Horned grebe     the horned grebe or slavonian grebe ( `` podiceps azurites '' ) - `` podiceps '' : latin for `` podicis '' ( rump ) and `` pedis '' ( foot ) , referring to the placement of the legs on its body ; `` auritus '' : latin for `` eared '' - is a member of the grebe family of water birds . it inhabits eurasia and north america . < br >
