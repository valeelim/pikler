UIC classification of locomotive axle arrangements     the uic classification of locomotive axle arrangements describes the wheel arrangement of locomotives , multiple units and trams . it is set out in the international union of railways ( uic ) `` leaflet 650 - standard designation of axle arrangement on locomotives and multiple-unit sets '' . it is used in much of the world . the united kingdom used a related scheme . the united states uses the simplified aar wheel arrangement for modern locomotives .
