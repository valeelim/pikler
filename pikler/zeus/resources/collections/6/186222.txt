WJAC-TV     wjac-tv is the local nbc channel in the johnstown-altoona-state college , pennsylvania area . it is owned by cox enterprises , which also owns wpxi-tv in nearby pittsburgh . wjac-tv first appeared on television on september 15 , 1949. it was originally owned by johnstown automotive company . it was originallly on channel 13 before moving to channel 6 in 1952. in the 1960s , johnstown automotive sold the television station to `` the tribune-democrat '' . in 1997 it was sold to sunrise television , which later became part of cox in 2000. other websites . - wjactv.com - wjac website
