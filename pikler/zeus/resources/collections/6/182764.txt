Colombo, Paraná    colombo , paraná colombo is the third largest city in greater curitiba , state of paraná , found in the southern region of brazil . it is located at 25º17'30 '' s , 49º13'27 '' w , at an altitude of about 1.027 metres above sea level , some 18 km from state capital , curitiba . it was founded on 5 february 1890. the city is the largest italian colony in the state .
