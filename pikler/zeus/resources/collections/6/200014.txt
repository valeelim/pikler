Cartazini Biennial Art Award     the prix cartazini is a prize in contemporary art , sponsored by the paris based gallery cartazini and made since 1999. it is made every two years , on the odd years . in 2009 the award will be for 20 000 euros and as such is one of the largest art awards in the visual arts sector . pino pascali jean-michel basquiat philip taaffe . tyeb mehta . geoff bunn . niamh o'conchobhair emma fournier other websites . - cartazini award
