Krusty Gets Busted     `` krusty gets busted '' is the twelfth episode of `` the simpsons '' first season , and first started on april 29 , 1990. the episode was written by jay kogen and wallace wolodarsky , and directed by brad bird . in this episode , krusty the clown , a well-known tv comedian , is convicted of the robbery of the `` kwik-e-mart . '' bart and lisa looked for clues , and were convinced that krusty had been framed . this episode is the first full appearance of kent brockman , and kelsey grammer makes his first guest appearance on the show as the voice of `` sideshow bob . '' the episode was well liked by people , and it was the highest-rated show on the fox network the week it started .
