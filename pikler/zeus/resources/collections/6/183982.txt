Gene Wilder     gene wilder ( jerome silberman ; june 11 , 1933 – august 29 , 2016 ) was an american actor , comedian , movie director , screenwriter and author . he was most famous for his acting work and in particular for the movies he made with mel brooks which include `` the producers '' , `` blazing saddles '' and `` young frankenstein '' . he also played the part of willy wonka in the musical movie `` willy wonka & the chocolate factory '' . wilder studied communication and theatre arts at the university of iowa . wilder was drafted into the army on september 10 , 1956. an unauthorized biography of wilder called `` gene wilder : funny and sad '' by brian scott mednick was published in december 2010 by bearmanor media . wilder died from problems caused by alzheimer 's disease on august 29 , 2016 in connecticut . work . television . - voice for `` the electric company '' 's segment `` the adventures of letterman '' ( 60 episodes , 1972–77 ) - `` something wilder '' ( 1994–95 ) - `` will & grace '' ( 2002 ) episode `` boardroom and a parked place '' ( guest star – mr. stein )
