Panathinaikos F.C.    panathinaikos f.c . p.a.e . panathinaikos is a football club which plays in greece . the club based in athens , the capital of greece . the colours of panathinaikos is white and green and its emblem is a shamrock . it is one of the most successful greek team . it has won nineteen greek championships and sixteen greek cups . it is the unique greek club which was a runner-up of the uefa champions league competition , in 1971 .
