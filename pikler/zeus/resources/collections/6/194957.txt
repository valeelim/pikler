Melky Cabrera     melky cabrera is a baseball player . he plays for the chicago white sox . he was born on august 8 , 1984 in santo domingo , dominican republic . he has also played for the new york yankees , atlanta braves , kansas city royals , san francisco giants , and toronto blue jays . cabrera played his first major league game when he was only 20 years of age . he is 5'11 '' and weighs 170 lbs . he bats both left and right handed . references . - melky cabrera 's statistics
