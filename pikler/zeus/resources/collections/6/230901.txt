Captain Planet and the Planeteers     captain planet and the planeteers is an american animated environmentalist television program , based on an idea by ted turner and produced by andy heyward , robby london , barbara pyle and nicholas boxer . the series was developed and co-produced by turner program services along with the partnership of dic entertainment and ran new episodes from september 10 , 1990 until december 5 , 1992. a second series , `` the new adventures of captain planet '' , ran for three seasons and was produced by turner broadcasting and then-corporate sibling hanna-barbera productions . both programs continue today in syndication . related pages . - captain planet
