.sg     .sg is the country top level domain for singapore ( cctld ) which was introduced in 1988. it is being managed by the singapore network information centre ( sgnic ) . it is only for residents of singapore with a valid postal address . however , there are some second-level domains that has more rules when registering . second-level domains . - .com.sg - for commercial use . - .net.sg - network providers and info-com operators . - .org.sg - registered organisations of singapore . - .gov.sg - government agencies . - .edu.sg - schools and institutions . - .per.sg - for personal use only . - .idn.sg - for domain names with chinese and indian words in them . other websites . - iana .sg whois information - singapore network information centre
