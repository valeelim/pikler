Teatro Colón     the teatro colón ( spanish ) ( columbus theatre ) in buenos aires , argentina , is one of the world 's best known opera houses . in 2006 the theatre was closed so that work could be done to improve the inside of the building . it reopened in 2010 there had been a theatre there in the middle of the 19th century . the present building was opened in 1908. the auditorium is horseshoe-shaped and has 2,487 seats . the acoustics of the building are very good . it is in the middle of buenos aires . many world famous conductors , composers and singers have appeared at the theatre .
