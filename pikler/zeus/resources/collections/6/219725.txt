Green belt     a green belt or greenbelt is a policy and land use designation used in land use planning to retain areas of largely undeveloped , wild , or agricultural land surrounding or neighbouring urban areas . similar concepts are greenways or green wedges which have a linear character and may run through an urban area instead of around it . a green belt is basically an invisible line that goes around a certain area , stopping people from building there so that some of the wild and agricultural land can be saved .
