Rigel     rigel is a hot supergiant star in the milky way galaxy . it is classified as a blue supergiant . it is located in the constellation orion . many scientists believe that rigel was the youngest star even if it is the same size with the biggest star , betelgeuse . rigel is much bigger than our star here in the solar system , the sun . rigel lies 778 light-years from earth in orion . it is visible from earth as a small , bluish circle on clear nights from both hemispheres . the color of this star is bluish-white , hotter than a star with a yellow color , meaning it will use more energy quickly . the sun runs off the same gases as rigel ; hydrogen and helium that makes it shine . when rigel loses its fuel inside the very hot core , it will blow up into the red supergiant stage of its life and explode in a supernova . a black hole or a pulsar could be left .
