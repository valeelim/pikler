Fernando de Herrera     fernando de herrera ( ~1534–1597 ) called `` el divino '' was a 16th-century spanish poet and man of letters . he was born in seville . much of what is known about him comes from the book `` libro de descripción de verdaderos retratos de illustres y memorables varones '' ( book of the description of the true portraits of illustrious and memorable men ) , which was written in 1599 by francisco pacheco .
