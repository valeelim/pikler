Ray Gordy     terry ray gordy , jr. ( born 23 march 1979 ) is a retired american professional wrestler who is best known for wrestling with world wrestling entertainment ( wwe ) where he appeared on their smackdown brand under the ring names jesse and slam master j. in wwe , he was known for tagging with drew hankinson where they made the team of jesse and festus . he is the son of the wrestler terry gordy of the fabulous freebirds . gordy currently works as a police officer in atlanta , georgia .
