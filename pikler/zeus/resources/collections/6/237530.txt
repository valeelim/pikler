Battles of Lexington and Concord     the battles of lexington and concord were the first battles of the american revolutionary war . they were between british soldiers , who wanted to take away the colonists ' weapons and arrest john hancock and samuel adams , and american colonists . they were fought in towns outside boston , massachusetts . these towns are lexington , massachusetts and concord , massachusetts . the patriots found out long before it happened and safely evacuated the place . the first battle was fought on lexington green in lexington . general gage , along with 700 british soldiers , marched there to find and destroy hidden weapons . however , they were met by 70 minutemen . the battle lasted only a few minutes , but 8 minutemen died and 10 were hurt . only 1 british soldier had been shot . then , the soldiers went to concord . on the way back to boston , they were ambushed by 400 minutemen . this time , britain lost 250 soldiers , while only 90 americans died . they went to concord to seize gunpowder and weaponry stored by the militia .
