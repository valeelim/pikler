James Mason     james neville mason ( 15 may 1909 - 27 july 1984 ) was an english actor . he reached stardom in both british and american movies . he was born in huddersfield , west riding of yorkshire . he died in lausanne , switzerland from a heart attack at the age of 75. he played humbert humbert in lolita ( 1962 ) . filmography . - ( 1951 ) - julius caesar ( 1953 ) - a star is born ( 1954 ) - 20,000 leagues under the sea ( 1954 ) - lolita ( 1962 ) - the fall of the roman empire ( 1964 ) - the boys from brazil ( 1978 ) - murder by decree ( 1979 )
