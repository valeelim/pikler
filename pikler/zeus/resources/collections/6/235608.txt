Sailing ship     a sailing ship is a big boat with sails which catch the wind . the wind pushes the boat along . a sailing ship had a rig of at least three masts , square rigged on all of them . the great days of sailing ships was from around the 15th century to the middle of the 19th century . they were very important for trade as well as for war . all large boats became known as `` ships '' , so when steam power was invented people talked about `` steam ships '' to distinguish them from `` sailing ships '' . small boats with sails are called `` yachts '' or sailboats . they are used today for leisure activities .
