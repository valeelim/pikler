Log cabin     a log cabin is a house built from wooden logs . they were first built in norway , but are usually associated with the woods areas of the united states . abraham lincoln was born in a log cabin , and that is the basis for the toy `` lincoln logs '' . log cabins have also been a symbol in american politics , as some presidents said they were born in log cabins to make them seem closer to the people .
