Weather radar     a weather radar is a type of radar used to show where precipitation is . weather radar can tell the movement of precipitation , what type it is ( rain , snow , hail , etc . ) , and forecast its future . modern weather radars are mostly doppler radars , which can find the movement of rain drops and the intensity of the precipitation . both types of information can be used to find the structure of storms and tell if they will cause any severe weather . other websites . - weather underground - canadian weather radar faq
