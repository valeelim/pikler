Saturdays of Thunder     `` saturdays of thunder '' is the ninth episode of the simpsons third season . it first started on the fox network in the united states on november 14 , 1991. in this episode , homer takes a fatherhood quiz and realizes that he knows nothing about bart . he tries to be a better father and learns that bart is entering in a `` soap box derby . '' homer and bart make a racer together , however it is not very good , and bart decides to drive martin 's much nicer racer instead . homer is at first sad , but decides that he must be a good father and support bart .
