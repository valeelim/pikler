Med Hondo     med hondo ( born mohamed abid hondo , 1936 ) is a mauritanian movie director , producer , screenwriter , actor and voice actor . he moved to france in 1959 and began to work in movies during the 1960s . in 1967 , critics praised for the first movie he directed , `` soleil o '' . it won a golden leopard award at the locarno international film festival in italy . when american movies are dubbed into french , hondo often plays the french version of a character . for example , he played the french version of eddie murphy 's character donkey in `` shrek '' .
