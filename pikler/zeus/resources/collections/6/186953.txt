Hinduism in Bangladesh     hinduism is the second largest religious affiliation in bangladesh , covering over at least 16 % of the population . in terms of population , the independent nation-state of bangladesh is the third largest hindu state of the world after the republic of india and nepali states , and hosts the world 's largest minority population of hindus of bengali ethnic origin . in nature , bangladeshi hinduism closely resembles the forms and customs of hinduism practised in the neighbouring indian state of west bengal , with which bangladesh ( at one time known as east bengal ) was united with that country until the partition of india in 1947. the river ganges is a scared holy river which flows into the country from bharat .
