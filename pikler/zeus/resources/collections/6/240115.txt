Nathalia Dill     nathalia goyannes dill orrico , better known as nathalia dill ( march 24 , 1986 in rio de janeiro , brazil ) is a brazilian actress . she played the villain , débora rios , in the fifteenth season of the series malhação . in her last television work , nathalia was protagonist of the novel `` paraíso '' , benedito ruy barbosa , where she played the character maria rita ( santinha ) .
