Days of Thunder     days of thunder is a 1990 american auto racing movie that was produced by don simpson and jerry bruckheimer and was directed by tony scott . days of thunder was released on june 27 , 1990 in north america . the movie received mixed reviews with a 40 % rating on rotten tomatoes and a 60 out of 100 from metacritic . the movie had two video games based on it . cast . - tom cruise as cole trickle - nicole kidman as dr. claire lewicki - robert duvall as harry hogge - michael rooker as rowdy burns - cary elwes as russ wheeler - randy quaid as tim daland - fred dalton thompson as big john - john c. reilly as buck bretherton - j. c. quinn as waddell - caroline williams as jeannie burns - leilani sarelle as a female highway patrol officer who is actually a stripper hired by harry as a prank on cole . - chris ellis as harlem hoogerhyde - don simpson as aldo bennedetti ( cameo ) - richard petty as himself ( cameo ) - rusty wallace as himself ( cameo ) - neil bonnett as himself ( cameo ) - harry gant as himself ( cameo )
