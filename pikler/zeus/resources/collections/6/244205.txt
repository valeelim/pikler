Walden Media     walden media is a children 's movie production and publishing company . it is best known for making `` the chronicles of narnia '' movie series . its movies are usually about famous children 's literature . for example , `` bridge to terabithia '' . it also makes movies about biographies or historical happenings . it was started in 2001 by michael flaherty and cary granat . granat was the head of dimension films . flaherty was interested in good education for children . the two lived together at tufts university . they met again to make walden media . they saw its purpose as making kids have fun . the company is owned by christian philip anschutz . he said he wanted the movies `` to be entertaining ( fun ) , but also to be life affirming and to carry a moral message . ''
