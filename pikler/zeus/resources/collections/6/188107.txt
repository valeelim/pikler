Tibetan Muslims     the tibetan muslims , also known as the kachee ( kache ) , are a small minority in tibet . despite being muslim , they are classified as tibetans . the tibetan word kachee literally means kashmiri and kashmir was known as kachee yul ( yul = country ) . the small number of tibetan muslims live throughout tibet . , many of them can be found in lhasa and shigatse . some live outside the tibet autonomous region . these ethnic groups such as the balti ( indigenous people ) and burig are of tibetan origin and consider themselves to be ethnically tibetan , are muslims as well . these other groups , however , are mainly found in the occupied kashmir 's ladakh area and the pakistani-admnistered baltistan .
