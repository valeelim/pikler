Jeff Burton     jeffrey brian burton ( born june 29 , 1967 in south boston , virginia ) is a retired nascar sprint cup series driver . jeff burton drove the # 31 caterpillar chevrolet ss for richard childress racing , and used to drive for roush racing in the # 99 car , as well as racing part-time for michael waltrip at michael waltrip racing . jeff 's brother ward burton also drove in the sprint cup series . in 2013 , burton decided to stop driving in nascar and started working for nbc sports helping broadcast nascar sprint cup series races .
