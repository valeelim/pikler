Zakynthos (city)    zakynthos ( city ) zakynthos is the capital of zakynthos island . zakynthos is one of the ionian islands . zakynthos city is located in the north-west of island . its population according to 2001 census is 16.457 inhabitants . it is a coastal city and it has got a port which connect zakynthos with peloponnese . zakynthos is a beautiful town . the most beautiful district of city is the district bochali .
