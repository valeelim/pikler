List of veterans of World War I who died in 2006     this is the list of world war i veterans who died in the year 2006. listed here are those that joined the armed services after the armistice date , but before the treaty of versailles was signed , or where there is debate on their join-date , or whose military service is sometimes viewed as outside the scope of `` wwi '' , but are considered world war i-era veterans by the press or by their respective governments , or served in a related conflict .
