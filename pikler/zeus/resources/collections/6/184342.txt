Rhinoceros (genus)    rhinoceros ( genus ) the genus rhinoceros of the family rhinocerotidae includes the one-horned rhinoceroses , of which there are two extant species : the indian rhinoceros ( `` r. unicornis '' ) and the javan rhinoceros ( `` r. sondaicus '' ) . these are characterized by one horn ( actually thickly matted hair ) on the upper snout .
