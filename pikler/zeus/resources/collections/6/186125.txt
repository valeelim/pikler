Pennsylvania Cyber Charter School     the pennsylvania cyber charter school ( also known as pa cyber ) is an online school , made as an alternative to regular schooling . it recently changed its name from the western pennsylvania cyber charter school . students from pennsylvania in grades prekindergarten-12 can attend pa cyber for free . students can have classes that are self-paced and classes that are `` virtual '' where the student participates in a `` online classroom '' . other websites . - pa cyber website - nnds website - lincoln interactive website
