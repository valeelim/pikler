Pierre Schaeffer     pierre henri marie schaeffer ( 14 august 1910 – 19 august 1995 ) was a french musician and other things , like an inventor and engineer , and more . he was born in nancy . he created a kind of music called musique concrète ( which in the french speak means `` concrete music '' ) . this music was different from regular music , and had many sounds from things in the real world . schaeffer died of alzheimer 's disease in aix-en-provence .
