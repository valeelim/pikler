Anne, Princess Royal    anne , princess royal anne , princess royal ( born 15 august 1950 ) is the second child , and the only daughter , of elizabeth ii and prince philip , duke of edinburgh . she married captain mark philips in 1973 , and had two children , peter phillips and zara phillips . they separated in 1989 , later divorced , and the princess married commander timothy laurence rn in 1993. ian ball tried to kidnap ( take ) the princess in 1974 , but was stopped by her bodyguard and the public . ball was not sent to prison , but to a mental hospital . anne is eleventh in line to the throne of the united kingdom , after the two younger brothers , six nieces and nephews , and one grand-nephew . she is often called the hardest working member of the british royal family .
