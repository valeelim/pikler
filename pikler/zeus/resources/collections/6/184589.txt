Punjabi people     the punjabi people ( , ਪੰਜਾਬੀ also panjabi people ) are an indo-aryan ethnic group from south asia ( pakistan and india ) . they come from the punjab region , which has been home to some of the oldest civilizations in the world including one of the world 's first and oldest civilizations , the indus valley civilization . the punjabi identity is usually cultural and linguistic , with punjabis being those whose first language is punjabi , an indo-european dialect .
