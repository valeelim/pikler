Khufu     khufu was a pharaoh ( or leader ) of ancient egypt 's old kingdom . he was the pharaoh of egypt from around 2589 to 2566 b.c.e . khufu was the second pharaoh of the fourth dynasty . he is also thought to be the one who built the great pyramid of giza , one of the seven wonders of the ancient world . khufu 's full name was `` khnum-khufu '' which means `` the god khnum protects me .
