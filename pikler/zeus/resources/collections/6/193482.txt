The Planets     the planets op . 32 is a seven-movement orchestral musical series by the british composer gustav holst , written between 1914 and 1916. the first complete public performance happened during world war i on 10 october 1918 in birmingham , with appleby matthews directing the orchestra . however , an earlier premiere for only a small group of people was held on 29 september 1918 in the queen 's hall in london , with the orchestra directed by adrian boult .
