Orion's Belt    orion 's belt orion 's belt or belt of orion is belt of 3 stars in one line in the constellation orion . ancient arabic names of these 3 stars are alnitak , alnilam and mintaka . these 3 stars are named by astronomers as ζ ori ( alnitak ) , ε ori ( alnilam ) , and δ ori ( mintaka ) . these 3 stars are best visible in the night sky during the month of january .
