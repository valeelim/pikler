Armen Dzhigarkhanyan     armen dzhigarkhanyan ( armenian : արմեն ջիգարխանյան , russian : арме́н бори́сович джигарханя́н ) is one of the most popular soviet and armenian actors . he was born on october 3 , 1935 , in yerevan , armenian ssr , ussr . he has appeared in 254 movies , including his 1981 leading role as nazi max richard in `` teheran 43 '' . he acted in his first movie aged 25 in 1960. dzhigarkhanyan was given the title of people 's artist of the ussr in 1985 .
