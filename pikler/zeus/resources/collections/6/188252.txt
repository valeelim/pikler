Barons Court tube station     barons court is a london underground station on the district and piccadilly lines . it is in west kensington , greater london . the station is located on gliddon road , a short distance from talgarth road ( a4 ) in the london borough of hammersmith and fulham . barons court is between west kensington and hammersmith on the district line , and between earl 's court and hammersmith on the piccadilly line . it is in travelcard zone 2. related pages . - list of london underground stations other websites . - london 's transport museum photographic archive - in the distance can be seen the great ferris wheel at earl 's court exhibition ground .
