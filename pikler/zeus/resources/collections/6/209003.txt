Whitey Bulger     james joseph `` whitey '' bulger , jr. ( born september 3 , 1929 ) is an american former mob boss that worked out of south boston , massachusetts . he was on the ten top most wanted fugitives in the united states from 1999 until 2011. his story was on `` america 's most wanted '' on fox . he was captured by police on 22 june 2011 .
