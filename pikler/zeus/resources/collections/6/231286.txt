Asana     asana is a body position in yoga . it can mean where a person sits , and the way that they sit . it helps to balance the mind and body . it is meant to be firm , yet comfortable . all the many different body positions in yoga are `` asana '' s : they all have their own names and every name ends with `` asana '' , such as sav '' asana '' ( correct lying ) . many have names of animals , such as bujang '' asana '' ( cobra ) . others have names that describe the position , such as shirsh '' asana '' ( head ) .
