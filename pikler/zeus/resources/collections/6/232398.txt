Life insurance     life insurance is money for your family to use to pay your bills and death expenses when you die . a person must be healthy to purchase a life insurance policy . the amount of life insurance needed depends on the person ’ s family ’ s needs when they die . some things to consider when purchasing a life insurance policy are , marital status , number of dependents , ( wife , kids etc . ) family size , income , and wealth . life insurance has different types of policies .
