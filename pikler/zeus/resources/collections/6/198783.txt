Kaskade     kaskade ( born ryan raddon in 1971 ) is an american dj and record producer . he is known for his techno songs `` 4 a.m. '' and `` angel on my shoulder '' . `` move for me '' and `` fire in your new shoes '' are two more of his hit songs . in 2010 , he made an album called `` dynasty '' . kaskade released another album on october 22 , 2011 with the single `` eyes '' . other websites . - artist site at ultra records - kaskade on edm lounge
