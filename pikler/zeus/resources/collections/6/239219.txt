Prayer of Manasseh     the prayer of manasseh is a short work of 15 verses of the penitential prayer of the judean king manasseh . manasseh is recorded in the bible as one of the most idolatrous ( 2 kings 21:1-18 ) ; however , after having been taken captive by the assyrians , he prays for mercy ( 2 chronicles 33:10-17 ) and turns from his ways of worshiping idols .
