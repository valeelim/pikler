Theo Waigel     theo waigel is ( * 22 april 1939 in oberrohr , bavaria ) a german politician ( csu ) . he was the finance minister between 1988 and 1999 and the chairman of the csu , respectively . theo waigel obtained his high school degree at the simpert-kraemer-high school , in swabian krumbach waigel immatriculated at the facultys of law and political science in munich , later in wurzburg . he successfully earned his bachelor of law in 1963 , in 1967 he obtained the master of law and finished with a phd with his essay about the constitutional system of the german especially the bavarian agriculture . the first job of mr. waigel was an assessor for the attorney general at the regional court in munich i , in 1969 he took over a new assignment as a personal secretary for the state secretary in the finance ministry , bavaria . in 1970 he took over the same job in the ministry of transport . after his political career , he worked for german investment counselling inc. as well as the electrical company enbw as a member of the board of directors .
