Lymphoma     lymphoma is a cancer of the lymphatic system . there are many types of lymphoma , including hodgkin 's lymphoma , non-hodgkin lymphoma , primary central nervous system lymphoma , the b-cell lymphoma , and the hepatosplenic lymphoma . symptoms . - larger-than-normal lymph nodes - sweating at night - fevers that happen often or do not get better - weight loss - fatigue ( feeling tired ) - enlarged spleen - enlarged liver chemotherapy is often used for both hodgkin 's lymphoma and non-hodgkin lymphoma . radiotherapy may also be used .
