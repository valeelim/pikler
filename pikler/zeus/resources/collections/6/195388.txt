Rod and Todd Flanders     rod and todd flanders are two characters in the television show `` the simpsons '' . they are the neighbours of the simpson family . they are brothers . their parents are ned and maude flanders . rod is the elder of the two ; he is twelve years old , while todd is ten . the flanders are all devout christians . rod and todd first appeared in the episode `` dead putting society '' . they have appeared in more than 100 episodes since then . the two brothers are both disliked by bart and lisa , in the same way that ned flanders is hated by homer . other websites . - rod flanders on imdb
