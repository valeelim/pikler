Wildcat     the wildcat is `` felis silvestris '' , a member of the small cat sub-family felinae . it is native to eurasia . it is so-called because it is almost impossible to tame . however , crossbreeding with domestic cats has occurred throughout almost the whole of the species ' range . the wildcat shows geographic variation . all subspecies are larger than house cats , with longer legs and more robust bodies . there are about 22 subspecies , or ( according to some ) only four , including the chinese mountain cat , which was previously considered a species in its own right . our domestic cat originated from the subspecies known as the african wildcat . the name 'wildcat ' is sometimes used as a term for domestic cats which have gone wild in the sense of living rough , without owners . related pages . - asiatic wildcat
