Hell in a Cell (2009)    hell in a cell ( 2009 ) hell in a cell was a professional wrestling pay-per-view ( ppv ) event produced by world wrestling entertainment ( wwe ) , which took place on october 4 , 2009 at the prudential center in newark , new jersey . the name of the event was voted by fans from wwe 's official website . hell in a cell was chosen over no escape , lock up and rage in a cage . it was the first event in the hell in a cell series . hell in a cell contained professional wrestling matches , with the main event matches being held in a hell in a cell match . the events involved wrestlers from all three brands , raw , wwe smackdown and ecw . the wrestlers portrayed either a good guy or a bad guy as they followed a series of events which lead to a wrestling match or a series of wrestling matches . other pages . - list of wwe pay-per-view events other websites . - hell in a cell official website - hell in a cell matches
