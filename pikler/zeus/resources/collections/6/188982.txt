Simon Cowell (presenter)    simon cowell ( presenter ) simon cowell ( mbe ) is a british television presenter and author who hosts the animal planet documentary series `` wildlife sos '' . he is the co-founder of a leatherhead , surrey based charity called wildlife aid . in february 2006 , simon was awarded an mbe for his services to wildlife . simon lives in leatherhead , surrey . he received a phd for biological sciences at jesus college , cambridge .
