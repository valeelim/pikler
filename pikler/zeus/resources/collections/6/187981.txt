Enola Gay     the enola gay is a b-29 superfortress bomber . this plane is famous for dropping the first atomic bomb ( named little boy ) over the japanese city of hiroshima during world war ii . the bomber is named after its pilot , paul tibbets ' mother enola gay tibbets . the plane was used from 18 may 1945 to 24 july 1946 , after which it was stored at suitland , maryland .
