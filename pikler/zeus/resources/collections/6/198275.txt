Estonian Soviet Socialist Republic     the estonian soviet socialist republic , or estonian ssr or essr , was a republic of the soviet union . it was administered by and subordinated to the government of the soviet union . the essr replaced the republic of estonia on 21 july 1940 , after the invasion of soviet troops on 17 june 1940. a puppet government backed by the soviet union , declared estonia to be a soviet state . the estonian ssr was taken into the ussr on 9 august 1940. the independence of the republic of estonia was reestablished on 20 august 1991 .
