Equirectangular projection     the equirectangular projection ( also called the geographic projection ) is a simple map view made about 100 ad . the projection maps longitude and latitude ( vertical and horizontal map lines ) to even straight lines . because the map is stretched out , it can not be used in navigation . its main use is to show large map areas . related pages . - cartography - cassini projection - gall-peters projection with resolution regarding the use of rectangular world maps - panorama other websites . - global modis based satellite map the blue marble : land surface , ocean color and sea ice . - table of examples and properties of all common projections , from radicalcartography.net . - panoramic equirectangular projection , panotools wiki .
