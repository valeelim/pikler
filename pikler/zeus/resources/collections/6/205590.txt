Dodger Stadium     dodger stadium is a baseball park and the home of the los angeles dodgers . it was built in 1962 , making it one of the oldest ball parks . ( fenway park in boston , massachusetts and wrigley field in chicago , illinois are older . ) it is also one of the largest baseball park in seating capacity . dodger stadium was constructed from 1959 to 1962 at a cost of $ 23 million dollars . a neighborhood called chavez ravine had to be torn down to build the stadium . the los angeles angels rented from the dodgers from 1962 through 1965 , before moving to anaheim stadium . the angels called the ballpark chavez ravine stadium . over five million people have seen a game in dodger stadium . the dodgers have won four world series while playing in the stadium , in 1963 , 1965 , 1981 and 1988. there was talk of tearing down the stadium to build a football stadium , but that did not happen .
