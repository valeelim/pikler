Dilation and curettage     dilation and curettage ( d & c ) is a type of surgery used to diagnose and treat problems with woman 's uterus . during a d & c , a doctor will do these things : - give the woman anesthesia - slowly dilate ( widen ) the cervix - put a `` curette '' ( a spoon-shaped tool with a sharp edge ) into the uterus through the cervix - use the curette to take out parts of the lining of the uterus . a d & c can be used for many reasons . for example : - to take tumors out of the uterus - to diagnose problems like cancer of the uterus - to take out tissues from a fetus or placenta , after a miscarriage or a medical abortion , which will stop bleeding
