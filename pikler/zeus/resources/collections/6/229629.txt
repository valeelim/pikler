Super Mario Galaxy 2     super mario galaxy 2 is a video game for the wii . it is the sequel to `` super mario galaxy '' . it was first announced at e3 2009 , and was released in the second quarter of 2010 in japan , on may 23 , 2010 in north america , on june 11 , 2010 in europe , and in june-august , 2010 in australia . unlike its predecessor , `` super mario galaxy 2 '' allows the player to ride on yoshi . `` super mario galaxy 2 '' introduces two new power-ups : the cloud flower and the rock mushroom .
