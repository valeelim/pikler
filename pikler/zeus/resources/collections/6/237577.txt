Kingdom Records (UK)    kingdom records ( uk ) kingdom records is a british record label from the 1970s and 1980s that was important in early british and jamaican dub music . one of kingdom records ' first releases was the 1972 mojo hannah single `` six days on the road '' . the following artists have all had recordings on kingdom records : - barrington levy - b.b . king - caravan - culture - gregory isaacs - mojo hannah - pulsar - revelation ( 8 ) - the royals - scientist - simple simon - wayne gibson # - judge dread references . -
