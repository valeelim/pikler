Trailer Park Boys     the trailer park boys was a canadian mockumentary created by mike clattenburg . it is produced by mike clattenburg , barrie dunn and michael volpe . in canada it is shown on the showcase . the series had seven seasons and ran from april 22 , 2001 to december 7 , 2008. the tv series spawned two movies : `` trailer park boys : the movie '' in 2006 and `` trailer park boys : countdown to liquor day '' in 2009. tv.com rated the series a 8.8. other websites . - trailer park boys ' official website
