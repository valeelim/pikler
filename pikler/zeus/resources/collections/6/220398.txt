Interstate 238     interstate 238 is an interstate highway in the state of california in the united states . it goes from interstate 580 in castro valley east to interstate 880 in san leandro . the route is long . the route is not like a 3-digit route as there is no interstate 38. it is called interstate 238 as it was a part of california state route 238 and no numbers with `` 80 '' at the end were able to be used .
