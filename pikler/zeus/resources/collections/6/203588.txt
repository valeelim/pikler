French lavender     the topped lavender , also known as french lavender , is a perennial shrub , that usually grows up to 30-100 centimetres . the leafs are 1-4 centimetres long , in a greyish tomentose tone . the flowers are pinkish , produced on spikes 2-3 centimetres long ; each flower is subtended by a bract that is 4-8 milimetres long . at the top of the spike there are many other white or lavender bracts . many subspecies exist in spain and other countries nearby . taxonomy . - `` l stoechas '' ssp . `` caesia '' borja et rivas gadoy
