Metropolitan line     the metropolitan line is part of the london underground . it is coloured in tfl 's corporate magenta on the tube map and in other branding . it was the first underground railway ( or subway ) in the world , opening on 10 january 1863 ( however , parts of that initial section are no longer served by the metropolitan line , but by the hammersmith & city , district and circle lines ) . the main line runs from aldgate in the city of london to amersham , with branch lines to uxbridge , watford and chesham .
