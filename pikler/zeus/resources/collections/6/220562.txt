Pickled cucumber     a pickled cucumber , most often simply called a pickle in the united states and canada , is a cucumber that has been processed in a brine or vinegar solution and left to ferment for a period of time . pickling is also used for many other things , such as onions , cabbage , and herring . one common type of pickle is the dill pickle . it is commonly used as a topping on meat products such as hamburgers , but can also be eaten on its own .
