Vancouver Whitecaps FC     the vancouver whitecaps are a canadian soccer team that plays in major league soccer in vancouver , british columbia . the team plays their home games at bc place in vancouver , british columbia . other teams named `` whitecaps '' have played soccer in vancouver since 1974 in the old north american soccer league , the canadian soccer league , and the usl . the original vancouver whitecaps were founded on december 11 , 1973. during the 1970s and 1980s , they played in the north american soccer league ( nasl ) , a soccer league that no longer exists . the whitecaps achieved success , winning the 1979 soccer bowl . it was during this short time that soccer became popular in vancouver . the whitecaps crowds at empire stadium grew to sell out crowds , at 32,000 fans per match . after playing at vancouver 's 32,000-seat empire stadium for most of their early years , the team moved into the new 60,000-seat bc place stadium in 1983. however , the end of the nasl in 1984 meant the whitecaps could not longer play in the nasl .
