Barbed wire     barbed wire ( also called barb wire ) is a kind of wire that is used to make cheap fences . it has sharp metal points ( barbs ) , which make climbing over it painful . the first patent in the united states for barbed wire was issued in 1867 to lucien b. smith of kent , ohio , who is regarded as the inventor . joseph glidden of dekalb , illinois , received a patent for the modern invention in 1874 after he made his own modifications to previous versions . it was first used for controlling cattle .
