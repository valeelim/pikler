Oh Brother, Where Art Thou?    oh brother , where art thou ? `` oh brother , where art thou ? '' is the fifteenth episode of the simpsons second season . it first started on the fox network in the united states on february 21 , 1991. in this episode , grampa confesses that homer has a half-brother , who homer quickly tries to track down . he eventually finds that his brother is `` herbert powell '' , the head of a car maker . herb quickly starts to bond with bart and lisa , and he invites homer to design his own car . homer 's car design turns out to be bad , which causes herb to become bankrupt .
