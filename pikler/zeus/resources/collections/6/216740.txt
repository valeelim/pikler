Nuneaton     nuneaton is the largest town in the english county of warwickshire . nuneaton is famous for the novelist , george eliot , who was born on a farm outside nuneaton in 1819 and lived in nuneaton during her early life . the town is 14.5 km ( 9 miles ) north of coventry , 32 km ( 20 miles ) east of birmingham and 166 km ( 103 miles ) northwest of london . the `` river anker '' runs through the town . nuneaton had a population of 70,721 as of 2001 though the 2008 estimate is 73,000. nuneaton also has a football team called nuneaton town . notable inhabitants . - george eliot , victorian novelist
