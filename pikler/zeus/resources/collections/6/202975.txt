Unterallgäu     unterallgäu is a rural district in swabia in southwest bavaria , germany . neighbor districts are : neu-ulm ( district ) , günzburg ( district ) , augsburg ( district ) , ostallgäu and oberallgäu and in baden-württemberg : ravensburg ( district ) and biberach ( district ) ; west of the district is the bavarian district-free city memmingen . mindelheim is the capital of the unterallgäu .
