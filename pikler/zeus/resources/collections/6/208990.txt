Kurt Angle     kurt steven angle ( born march 9 , 1968 ) , is an american retired professional wrestler currently signed to wwe on the raw brand , where he is the current on-screen general manager . in 2006 he signed a contract to total nonstop action wrestling where he is a former tna world champion . his tna contract expired in september 2014. angle won a gold medal at the 1996 summer olympics , winning with a broken neck . he is the only olympic gold medalist in professional wrestling history . he wrestled for the wwe and wwf from 1998 to 2006. in february 2017 , he announced his retirement from in-ring competition . angle returned to wwe on april 3 , 2017 , as the general manager of raw .
