MSV Duisburg     the meidericher spielverein 02 e. v. duisburg is a football club which plays in germany . the club was founding member of the german bundesliga 1962/63 and is now playing in the second bundesliga . due to their striped jerseys they are also called the `` zebras '' . the club was founded in 1902 as `` meidericher sv '' . on 9 january 1967 the club was renamed into his still today valid name `` msv duisburg '' . `` as of 1 november 2011 ''
