Mohican     the mahicans ( also mohicans ) are a native american tribe , who originally settled around the hudson river . many then moved to massachusetts after 1780 , before the remaining descendants moved to northeastern wisconsin during the 1820s and 1830s . the tribe 's name for itself was muhhekunneuw , or `` people of the river .
