Social studies     social studies is the name of a course or set of courses taught in primary and secondary schools or elementary , middle , and high schools , but is also sometimes the study of parts of human society at schools around the world . at primary school , social studies is usually about the local community and family . by middle and high school , social studies includes many subjects about past and current human behavior and . usually , social studies taught in middle and high school is in the form of history , civics , economics and geography . social studies is a little different than social science , though many of the social sciences are covered in social studies .
