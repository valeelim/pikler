Gangster     a gangster , mobster , or gangsta is a criminal who is a member of a crime organization , such as a gang . the terms are widely used in reference to members of gangs associated with american prohibition and the american offshoot of the mafia , such as the chicago outfit or the five families . the related word `` mobster '' is a term derived from latin and aramaic . a gang is a group of people who , through the organization , formation , and establishment of an assemblage , share a common identity . in early usage , the word gang referred to a group of workmen . in the united kingdom the word is still often used in this sense .
