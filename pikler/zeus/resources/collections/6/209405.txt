The Cosmos Rocks     the cosmos rocks is an album by queen + paul rodgers . it was released on 15 september , 2008 in the europe and on 28 october , 2008 in north america . the album has 14 songs written by brian may , paul rodgers and roger taylor . it is the only studio the former queen members recorded with paul rodgers . the album received mostly bad reviews and it did not sell very many copies .
