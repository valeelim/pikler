Martial eagle     the martial eagle ( `` polemaetus bellicosus '' ) is a bird of prey . it lives in africa and is the biggest eagle in africa , 76 to 96 cm ( 30–38 in ) long , and its wingspan is 188–260 cm ( 6.2–8.5 ft ) long . because it is so big , it can kill a small antelope called duikers and eat it , but it could not carry it up to its big nest in the tree .
