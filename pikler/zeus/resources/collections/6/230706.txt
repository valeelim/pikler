Shehnai     a shehnai is a south asian music instrument which is normally played at marriages and other ceremonies , rites and rituals . the word itself is of muslim/turkish origin , combining 'sheh ' ( or 'shah ' ) 'royal ' and '-nai ' or 'ney ' , a type of flute . a version of the `` shehnai '' , the `` surnai '' , is also played in the northern and north-western areas of india and pakistan , in particular at traditional polo matches .
