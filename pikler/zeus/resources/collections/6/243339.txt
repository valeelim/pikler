Catherine Spence     catherine helen spence ( 31 october 1825 - 3 april 1910 ) was a scottish- australian journalist , social political reformer and novelist in australia . she strongly believed that women should be allowed to vote . she was born on 1825 in melrose , scotland and died in norwood , adelaide . she was lauded “ the grand old women of australia ” and in scotland a plaque was made at her birth place in 1999. she went to a small private school after she immigrated with her parents to south australia of the age of 14 .
