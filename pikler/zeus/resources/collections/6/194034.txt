Raja Bell     raja bell ( born september 19 , 1976 in saint croix , u.s. virgin islands ) is a basketball player who currently plays for the utah jazz in the national basketball association ( nba ) . he has also played for the philadelphia 76ers , dallas mavericks , utah jazz , phoenix suns , and the charlotte bobcats . he began his nba career with the san antonio spurs on august 2 , 2000 but did not play a game with them . he is married to his wife , cindy green and they have two sons . other websites . - raja bell 's nba player profile
