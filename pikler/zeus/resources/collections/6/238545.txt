Pop Chronicles     the pop chronicles were two radio documentaries . they told the story of music that was popular in america in the 1940s , 1950s , and 1960s . john gilliland made both documentaries . los angeles radio station krla 1110 broadcast the first pop chronicles documentary in 1969. john gilliland , dick lapalm , lew irwin , harry shearer , mike masterson , and richard perry interviewed famous musicians . len chandler wrote and sang the theme song “ the chronicles of pop ” . the engineer and associate producer was chester coleman . other radio stations also broadcast the pop chronicles , including american forces network . san francisco radio station ksfo broadcast the `` pop chronicles of the 1940s '' in 1972 and 1976. the american forces network also broadcast this show . in 1994 , gilliland released a shorter version on audio cassette . the title of this audiobook was `` pop chronicles the 40s : the lively story of pop music in the 40s '' . the audiobook was also called `` the big band chronicles '' . after his death , gilliand 's sister donated the `` pop chronicles '' tapes to the university of north texas music library . they form the john gilliland collection .
