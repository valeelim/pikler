Stephen Sondheim     steven joshua sondheim ( born march 22 , 1930 ) is an american composer and lyricist ( he writes the words for songs ) . sondheim was born into a jewish family in new york city and grew up on the upper west side of manhattan and later near doylestown , pennsylvania . he is openly gay . he wrote the words for songs in `` '' and `` west side story '' . of american composers still living , he has won the most awards . he has : - multiple grammy awards - 9 tony awards ( more than any other composer ) - a lifetime achievement tony - an academy award - a pulitzer prize . sondheim writes both the music and the words to his musicals . many critics like his work , but some people do not like it . `` sweeney todd '' is very different from the music of rodgers and hammerstein . some of sondheim 's musicals . - `` a funny thing happened on the way to the forum '' - `` follies '' - `` a little night music '' - `` sunday in the park with george '' - `` into the woods '' - `` sweeney todd ''
