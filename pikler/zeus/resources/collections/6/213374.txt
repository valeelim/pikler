Heredity     heredity is the passing of traits ( characteristics ) from parents to offspring . in biology , the study of heredity is called genetics . with most living things , heredity is analysed by breeding ( making crosses ) , often in a laboratory . but with humans , heredity is studied in other ways . family pedigrees , identical twins and dna genome analysis all provide clues . a trait which may be inherited is heritable ; it is inborn or innate .
