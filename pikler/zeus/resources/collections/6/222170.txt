Actinobacteria     the actinobacteria are a group of gram-positive bacteria . most are found in the soil . they include some of the most common soil life , playing important roles in decomposition and humus formation . some , like `` streptomyces '' , were originally classified as fungi , because they form branching filaments like hyphae . all actinobacteria are aerobic . a few forms are pathogens , such as `` mycobacterium '' . some are thermophilic .
