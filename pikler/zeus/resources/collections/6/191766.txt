Water strider     the water strider ( also known as the pond skater ) is a true bug , an insect of the family gerridae . it can run across the surface of water . it lives on ponds and slow-running streams . it rarely goes underwater . the underside of the body is covered with water-repellent hair . there are around 500 known species , commonly placed in around 60 genera . also called the jesus bug ( walks on water ) .
