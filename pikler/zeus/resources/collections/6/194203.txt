Candan Erçetin     candan erçetin is a turkish pop folk singer in turkey . candan erçetin was born on 10 february 1963 in kırklareli . she studied at lycée de galatasaray , and completed istanbul university with a degree in archaeology . in 1991 , she graduated from istanbul municipal music school where she studied classical opera . she represented turkey in the eurovision song contest 1986 which held in bergen , norway as a member of klips ve onlar with the song `` halley '' instead of seden gürel , who was the member of the group and did not go to norway with it due to her final exams of architecture department of itü . she taught music in lycée de galatasaray for long years . presently , as well as continuing her stage performance , she also works as a lecturer at galatasaray university . discography . - hazırım ( 1995 ) - sevdim sevilmedim ( 1996 ) - çapkın ( 1997 ) - oyalama artık ( 1998 ) - elbette ( 1999 ) - unut sevme ( 2001 ) - neden ( 2002 ) - chante hier pour aujourd'hui ( 2002 ) - remix ( 2003 ) - melek ( 2004 ) - remix 's ( 2005 )
