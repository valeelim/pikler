Air Europa     air europa is a spanish airline based on the balearic island of mallorca . it operates flights within spain but also to other places in europe and further away , it concentrates on the holiday market . air europa flies to destinations in europe , africa and both north and south america . the air europa fleet is : - 6x airbus a330-200 - 26x boeing 737-800 - 2x boeing 767-300er - 6x embraer e-195
