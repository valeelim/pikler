Earl Grey tea     earl grey tea is a kind of tea that has a flavour and smell that comes from bergamot orange , a citrus fruit . the tea was named after charles grey , 2nd earl grey , a british prime minister from the 1830s . the name `` earl grey '' only used to be given to black tea , but now the name is used for other teas that have oil of bergamot , or a bergamot flavour . twinings , a british tea company , uses a portrait of charles grey , 2nd earl grey on its earl grey package .
