Bart Gets an F     `` bart gets an f '' is the first episode of `` the simpsons '' second season . it first started on the fox network in the united states on october 11 , 1990. in this episode , bart fails four history exams in a row and the school psychiatrist recommends that bart repeat the fourth grade . bart says that he will start to do better and tries to get the class smarty-pants martin prince to help him , but after that fails , bart prays for help . that night , springfield is hit with a big blizzard and the school is closed , giving bart one more day to study .
