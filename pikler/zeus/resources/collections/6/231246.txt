Asian emerald cuckoo     the asian emerald cuckoo is a species of cuckoo in the cuculidae family . it lives in southeast asia , in bangladesh , bhutan , cambodia , china , india , indonesia , laos , malaysia , myanmar , nepal , sri lanka , thailand , and vietnam . the asian emerald cuckoo is a protected animal in malaysia . its scientific name is `` chrysococcyx maculatus '' . this bird is not in the cuculinae subfamily , and so it is not a nest parasite . they raise their own young , like most birds . emerald cuckoos lay eggs in a nest built by the parents . other websites . - google search
