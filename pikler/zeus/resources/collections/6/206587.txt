Tommy Trouble and the Magic Marble     tommy trouble and the magic marble is a children 's novel written by ralph fletcher with drawings by ben caldwell . it was first published in 2000. tommy who loves collecting needs ten dollars to buy a magic marble from another boy . he tries to pick flowers from his mother 's rose garden to sell to a neighbor . he also tries to trade his little brothers snake . tommy finally decides that the magic marble is not worth the trouble . other websites . - ralph fletcher 's website
