Alexander Ovechkin     alexander mikhaylovich ovechkin ( born september 17 , 1985 ) is a russian professional ice hockey left winger . he plays for the washington capitals of the national hockey league ( nhl ) and serves as their captain . ovechkin was picked first overall in the 2004 nhl entry draft . he also played for the dynamo moscow of the rsl from 2001 to 2005 and again in 2012 because of the 2012 nhl lockout . other websites . - official website
