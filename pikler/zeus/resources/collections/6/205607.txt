Herta Müller     herta müller ( born 17 august 1953 ) is a romanian-born german novelist , poet and essayist noted for her works depicting the harsh conditions of life in communist romania under the repressive nicolae ceauşescu regime , the history of the germans in the banat ( and more broadly , transylvania ) , and the persecution of romanian ethnic germans by stalinist soviet occupying forces in romania . müller has been an internationally well-known author since the early 1990s , and her works have been translated into more than 20 languages . she has received over 20 awards , including the 1998 international impac dublin literary award . on 8 october 2009 , it was announced she would be awarded the 2009 nobel prize in literature .
