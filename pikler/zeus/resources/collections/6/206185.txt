Live Free or Die     `` live free or die '' is the official motto of the u.s. state of new hampshire , adopted by the state in 1945. the phrase comes from a toast written by general john stark on july 31 , 1809. stark was new hampshire 's most famous soldier of the american revolutionary war . his poor health forced him to decline an invitation to a reunion of the battle of bennington . he sent his toast by letter : the motto was enacted at the same time as the state emblem , on which it appears . outside websites . - new hampshire state law creating motto - the motto is one of the 101 reasons cited by the free state project for the choice of new hampshire as their destination .
