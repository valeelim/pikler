Oldboy     oldboy is a 2003 movie by park chan-wook . this movie is based on the 1997 japanese comic book . it is a mystery action movie about the contest of oh dae-su who was confined in private jail for 15 years ( even he does not know why he was caged ) and lee woo-jin who confined oh because of revenge . this movie deals especially and in detail with human hatred and revenge , and uses nice visual techniques to deal with sensational material . the movie was nominated for many international movie festivals . it won 31 awards including best director at the cannes film festival in 2004 .
