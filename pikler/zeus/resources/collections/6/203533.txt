WWE Bad Blood     wwe bad blood , ( originally called `` badd blood '' ) was a professional wrestling pay-per-view that was produced by wwe that was produced in 1997 , 2003 and 2004. it included only the raw brand for the 2003 and 2004 editions . the 1997 edition was an in your house pay-per-view called `` badd blood : in your house '' , this event held the first ever hell in a cell match , which was won by shawn michaels defeating the undertaker , and also featured the debut of kane . badd blood : in your house took place on october 5 , 1997 at the kiel center in st. louis , missouri . on the afternoon preceding this event , brian pillman , who was scheduled to face dude love on the show , was found dead in his hotel room . the announcement was made to fans during the half-hour `` pregame show '' prior to the ppv broadcast itself . - nation of domination ( rocky maivia , kama mustafa and d'lo brown ) defeated the legion of doom ( hawk and animal ) in a handicap match ( 12:20 ) - maivia pinned hawk after a rock bottom . - max mini and nova defeated tarantula and mosaic ( 6:43 )
