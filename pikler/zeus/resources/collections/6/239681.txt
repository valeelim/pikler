Aerobics     aerobics or rhythmic fitness is a physical exercise performed under a rhythmic music . it includes running , jumping , stretching and strength training routines . it may be practiced in a group setting led by an instructor , although it can be done solo and without musical accompaniment . aerobics classes may allow participants to select their level of participation according to their fitness level . many gyms offer a wide variety of aerobic classes for participants to take . each class is designed for a certain level of experience and taught by a certified instructor with a specialty area related to their particular class . jogging and cycling similarly promote aerobic fitness , but they are not usually done in classes led by instructors or with music , hence are not `` aerobics '' . related pages . - flexibility training - aerobics class on a beach
