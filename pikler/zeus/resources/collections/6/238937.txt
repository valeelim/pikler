Jazz piano     jazz piano is a word for the ways pianists play jazz . the piano has been an important part of the jazz , in both solo and group settings . its role is one of the biggest . this is because of the instrument 's combined melodic and harmonic nature . for this reason it is also an important tool in the understanding of jazz theory and arranging for jazz musicians and composers . together with the guitar , vibraphone , and other keyboard instruments , the piano is one of the few instruments in a jazz combo which can play chords , instead of only single notes ( as with the saxophone or trumpet ) .
