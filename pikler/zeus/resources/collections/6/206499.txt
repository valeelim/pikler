Vaginoplasty     vaginoplasty is surgery to shape a vagina . some babies are born with a problem where the vagina did not grow right . doctors fix this with a vaginoplasty . some women do not like how their vagina looks or feels . they can ask for a vaginoplasty . some transsexual women ask for a vaginoplasty when they go from male to female . there are many ways to do a vaginoplasty . most use tissue from other parts of the body of the patient . they can use tissue from inside the mouth , skin tissue , the vaginal labia , or the intestines . for transsexual women , they can use the penis and scrotum to make a vagina , but they must continue to dilate the new vagina to keep it by a tool . further reading . - karim rb , hage jj , dekker jj , schoot cm . evolution of the methods of neovaginoplasty for vaginal aplasia . `` eur j obstet gynecol reprod biol .
