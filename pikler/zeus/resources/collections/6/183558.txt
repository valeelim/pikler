Hyakunin Isshu     hyakunin isshu is a popular game in japan . it is played often during the new year season . a few games are possible using these cards . karuta is the most popular game . a reader reads kaminoku ( the upper words ) and shimonoku ( the lower words ) , and the players compete to see how fast they can pick the matching card . the kaminoku is not written on the cards , so the players who memorize the kaminoku have an advantage . the player who can get the most cards wins . the beginning words are `` 5,7,5 '' ( about 17 characters ) , called kaminoku . the second part are `` 7,7 '' , called shimonoku .
