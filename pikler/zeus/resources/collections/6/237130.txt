Dana Snyder     dana snyder ( born november 14 , 1973 ) is an american voice artist and comedian . he is best known for voicing master shake in the `` aqua teen hunger force '' franchise and alistair in `` open season 3 '' . snyder was born in allentown , pennsylvania . but he grew up in las vegas , nevada . he said don rickles , rip taylor , and phil silvers were his childhood influences . he graduated from webster university , missouri in 1996 .
