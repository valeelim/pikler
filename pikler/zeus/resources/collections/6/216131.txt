Epistle of Jude     the epistle of jude is the sixty-fifth book in the christian bible , and the twenty-sixth in the new testament . it is one of the shortest books in the bible , at only 25 verses long . this book is believed to be a letter from jude the apostle . the writer warns of `` people who change the message about the grace of god in order to excuse their immoral ways '' ( verse 4 ) . other websites . - jude ( niv ) - jude ( kjv )
