Frenzy     frenzy is a 1972 british crime thriller movie set in london . it is directed by alfred hitchcock starring alex mccowen and barry foster . it tells the story of an innocent man , played by mccowen who is wrongly believed to be a serial killer by the police and his efforts to avoid capture and bring the real killer to justice . this was the second to last movie that hitchcock made before he died in 1980 and the first time in many years he had made a movie in the united kingdom . references . - frenzy at the internet movie database
