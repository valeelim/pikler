Jude the Apostle     saint jude the apostle was one of the twelve apostles of jesus . he was also called jude of james and jude thaddeus . he is the patron saint of armenia , along with saint bartholemew . he is also the patron saint of lost causes and desperate situations . st. jude was martyred ( killed for his beliefs ) with another apostle , simon the zealot in beirut , lebanon , around ad 65. in the roman catholic church , jude and simon 's feast day is celebrated on 28 october .
