Underpinning     in construction , underpinning is the process of strengthening and stabilizing the foundation of an existing building or other structure . underpinning may be needed for a many of reasons : - the original foundation is not strong or stable enough . - the usage of the building has changed . - the type of soil supporting the foundation may have changed ( possibly through subsidence ) or were misjudged during planning . - the construction of nearby structures causes the excavation of soil supporting existing foundations . - it is more economical , due to land price or otherwise , to work on the current structure 's foundation than to build a new one .
