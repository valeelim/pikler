Ben &amp; Jerry's    ben & amp ; jerry 's ben and jerry ’ s is an american ice cream company . the company sells all sorts of different ice-creams . in 1977 , ben cohen and jerry greenfield completed a course on ice cream making at pennsylvania state university . on may 5 , 1978 , they opened a first ice cream shop in a gas station in burlington , vermont . in 1979 , on the first anniversary they had the first free cone day . this is now a celebration all over the world . in 1980 , ben and jerry got space in a building in south champlain street in burlington and started packing their ice cream in pint containers . in 1981 , the first ben & jerry ’ s official shop opened on route 7 in shelburne , vermont . in 1983 , ben & jerry ’ s ice cream was used to build “ the world ’ s largest ice cream sundae ” in st. albans , vermont . the sundae weighed 27,102 pounds . in 1986 , ben & jerry ’ s made its “ cowmobile ” . this was a ben and jerry ’ s van to pass out free ice cream . the cowmobile was driven by ben and jerry themselves . four months later the “ cowmobile ” burned down outside cleveland . ben said it looked like “ the world ’ s biggest baked alaska. ” in 1988 , ben and jerry ’ s won the title of u.s small business persons of the year awarded by u.s. president ronald reagan .
