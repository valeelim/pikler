Tsai Ing-wen     tsai ing-wen ( ; born 31 august 1956 ) is the president of the republic of china ( taiwan ) since 20 may 2016 and is the first female chosen to the position . she is the current chairperson of the democratic progressive party since 2008. her nickname is `` siao-ing '' ( 小英 ) . tsai ing-wen grew up from a rich family in southern taiwan . she worked hard and studied at national taiwan university . she passed a test and became a lawyer in her junior year . she graduated from university in 1980 .
