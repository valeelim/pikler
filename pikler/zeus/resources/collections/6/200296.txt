Bazooka     a bazooka is a very powerful weapon that shoots a rocket . it is a small and easily carried and sits on the shoulder to fire . it can also fire armour piercing missiles . the word `` bazooka `` comes from a musical instrument made of pipes invented by an american radio comedian , bob burns ( 1896â€”1956 ) . the bazooka was invented by rocket scientist , robert h. goddard , at the end of world war i. in world war ii the weapon was quickly developed for use by the army . in june 1942 it went from being a basic drawing design to use on the battlefield in only 30 days . improved versions in later years are called rocket-propelled grenades .
