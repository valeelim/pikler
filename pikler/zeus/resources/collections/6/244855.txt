Carlos Boozer     carlos boozer ( born november 20 , 1981 in aschaffenburg , west germany ) is a former american basketball player . during his nba career , he played for the cleveland cavaliers , utah jazz , chicago bulls and los angeles lakers . his nicknames are booz , cbooz , and booz cruise . he won a gold medal with team usa in the 2008 summer olympics games in beijing and is a two-time nba all-star in 2007 and 2008. boozer was drafted out of duke university by the cleveland cavaliers and played with them in the 2002-03 and 2003-04 nba seasons . boozer was known to yell , `` gim me that !
