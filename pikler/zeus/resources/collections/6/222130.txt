South Shields (UK Parliament constituency)    south shields ( uk parliament constituency ) south shields is a borough constituency that is represented in the house of commons of the parliament of the united kingdom . it elects one member of parliament ( mp ) by the first past the post system of election . currently , this constituency contains the areas of south shields in south tyneside in tyne and wear . changes to the boundary that are to be in place for the next election will bring whitburn into the constituency from neighbouring jarrow .
