Zhoukoudian     zhoukoudian or choukoutien ( ) is a cave system in beijing , china . many archaeological discoveries were found here , including one of the first specimens of `` homo erectus '' , dubbed peking man , and bones of the large hyena `` pachycrocuta brevirostris '' . the peking man lived in this cave about 200,000 to 750,000 years ago . the peking man site was discovered by johan gunnar andersson in 1921 and was first excavated by otto zdansky in 1921 and 1923 unearthing two human teeth .
