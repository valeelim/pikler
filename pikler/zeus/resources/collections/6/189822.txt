All Time Low     all time low is a pop-punk band . they formed in 2003 , in lutherville-timonium , maryland . the band 's lead singer and guitarist is alex gaskarth . the other band members are drummer rian dawson , bassist zack merrick , and guitarist jack barakat . the band signed to emerald moon records in 2004 , and released their first album , called `` the party scene '' , in 2005. they signed to hopeless records in 2006 , and released their second album , `` so wrong , it 's right '' , in 2007 . `` nothing personal '' , was released on july 7 , 2009.the band 's newest album , released in april 2015 , is called future hearts . the name `` all time low '' is taken from lyrics in the song `` head on collision '' by new found glory . band members . - alex gaskarth - vocals , guitar . born december 14 , 1987 , in essex . - jack barakat - guitar , backing vocals - rian dawson - drums - zack merrick - bass , backing vocals discography . compilations . - `` hopelessly devoted to you vol . 7 '' - `` hopelessly devoted to you vol . 6 '' - `` first taste of the morning ''
