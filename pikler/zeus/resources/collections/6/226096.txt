Breakaway (Kelly Clarkson album)    breakaway ( kelly clarkson album ) breakaway is the second album by american singer kelly clarkson ; it was released on november 30 , 2004. the album debuted at number three on the `` billboard '' 200 for selling 250,000 copies . it has sold over 6 million copies in the united states and has been certified six times platinum by the riaa . `` breakaway '' received two awards at the 2006 grammy awards including `` best pop vocal album '' and `` best female pop vocal performance '' for the single `` since u been gone '' . five singles were released from the album , `` since u been gone '' , `` behind these hazel eyes '' , `` because of you '' , `` walk away '' , and `` breakaway '' .
