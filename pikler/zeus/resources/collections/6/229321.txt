Commune     a commune is a minor administrative subdivision corresponding to an urban or rural zone , or mixed . it is the equivalent of a municipality , a council , or other instances of local administration . many countries have adopted the name of commune ( or similar ) for their minor and basic administrative unit . some countries are germany ( `` gemeinde '' ) , belgium ( `` commune '' / '' gemeente '' ) , chile ( `` comuna '' ) , colombia ( `` comuna '' ) , denmark ( kommune ) , france ( `` commune '' ) , italy ( `` comune '' ) , luxembourg ( `` commune '' ) , netherlands ( `` gemeente '' ) , poland ( `` gmina '' ) , sweden ( `` kommun '' ) and switzerland ( `` gemeinde '' / '' commune '' / '' comune '' ) .
