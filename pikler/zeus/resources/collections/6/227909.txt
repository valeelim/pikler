New York State Route 199     new york state route 199 ( ny 199 ) is a road in the hudson valley of new york , united states . it is long , and goes from near kingston to millerton . in between , ny 199 crosses over the hudson river over a bridge and passes through the communities of red hook and pine plains . part of the road was part of the ulster and delaware turnpike . ny 199 was named in 1930. other websites . - route log
