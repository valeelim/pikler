John Ensign     john eric ensign ( born march 25 , 1958 ) is an american veterinarian and former politician . he served as senator of nevada from 2001 to 2011. he resigned on may 3 , 2011 due to an ethics violation of senate rules . he was involved in a sex scandal because of a relationship with a senate aide . on april 21 , 2011 , he said he would resign from office on may 3 , stating that he `` will not continue to subject my family , my constituents , or the senate to any further rounds of investigation , depositions , drawn out proceedings , or especially public hearings . ''
