Treehouse of Horror II     `` treehouse of horror ii '' is the seventh episode of the simpsons third season , the second simpsons halloween episode , and the first halloween episode where names in the closing credits were replaced by 'spooky name ' parodies . the episode first started on october 31 , 1991. the first part , `` the monkey 's paw '' , is a direct reference to w.w. jacobs 's short story the monkey 's paw , in which the simpsons 's wishes all backfire on them after buying a hand of a monkey . in the second part , `` the bart zone '' , bart has powers , and turns homer into a jack-in-the-box , in the two spending more time together . the part was a parody of the twilight zone episode `` it 's a good life '' . in the last part , `` if i only had a brain '' , mr. burns 's head is onto homer 's head after mr. burns tries to create a robot with homer 's brain .
