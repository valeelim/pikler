Jillian Hall     jillian faye hall ( born jillian faye fletcher ; september 6 , 1980 in ashland , kentucky ) is an american retired female professional wrestler and singer . she is best known for her time with world wrestling entertainment ( wwe ) where she competed under the ring name jillian hall . she released a christmas album titled `` a jingle with jillian '' , which was sung completely in her character of a terrible singer . the album reached # 20 on the uk holidays top 100 shortly after its release . during her time in the wwe , she was a one-time wwe divas champion . on november 19 , 2010 , her wwe contract expired and wwe announced that they were not going to renew it . after being released , she wrestled on the independent circuit until announcing her retirement on january 21 , 2014. other websites . - wwe profile
