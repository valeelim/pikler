Feather Falls     feather falls is a waterfall found on the fall river . it is a tributary of the middle fork feather river , in plumas national forest of butte county , california . the falls were often said to be over 640 feet tall , however , they recently measured to stand 410 feet tall , which is what the usgs brush creek 7 1/2 '' quadrangle says . a town found nearby , feather falls , california , is named after this waterfall .
