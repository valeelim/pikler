Bloody Mary (folklore)    bloody mary ( folklore ) bloody mary is a kind of ghost of a dead woman in western folklore . this ghost is said to appear in a mirror after calling its name three times or more , depending on the version of the story . like the ouija board , this game is played by adolescents in parties or small meetings just to have fun . the ghost 's name can vary according to the tales said by the people . some similar names are : `` mary worth '' , `` mary worthington '' and `` hell mary '' , among others .
