Jewish Publication Society of America Version     the jewish publication society of america version ( jps 1917 ) of the tanakh ( the hebrew bible ) was the first bible translation published by the jewish publication society of america and the first translation of the tanakh into english by a group of jews ( though there had been earlier solo tries , such as that of isaac leeser ) . the full publication title is `` the holy scriptures according to the masoretic text : a new translation with the aid of previous versions and with constant consultation of jewish authorities '' . other websites . - online version of the jewish publication society of america version at mechon-mamre.org
