Gotta Get Away    got ta get away `` got ta get away '' is a song by the californian punk rock group the offspring . it was released as the third and last single from the album `` smash '' ( 1994 ) . the song was a modest hit in several countries , while it peaked at # 6 on billboard 's modern rock charts in the united states . `` got ta get away '' was inspired by an early track , `` cogs '' , written while the band was still named manic subsidal . the lyrics refer to the pressure that lead singer dexter holland was under to finish the album on time .
