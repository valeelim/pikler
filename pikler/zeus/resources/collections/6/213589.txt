Eddie Cheever     edward `` eddie '' mckay cheever , jr. ( born january 10 , 1958 in phoenix , arizona ) is an american racing driver . he raced for almost thirty years in formula one , sports cars , cart and the indy racing league . cheever participated in 143 world championship formula one races and started 132 , more than any other american , driving for nine different teams from 1978 through 1989. in 1997 , he formed his own irl team and won the indianapolis 500 as both owner and driver in 1998. the team now competes in sports cars .
