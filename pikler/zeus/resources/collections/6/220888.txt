Trifolium pratense     red clover ( `` trifolium pratense '' ) is a species of plant . it is native to europe , africa , and asia . it has been introduced to north america , hawaii , and puerto rico . it blooms from spring to early fall . it is found in fields , pastures , and near roadsides . red clover likes to grow in thick , dry soil . other websites . - `` trifolium pratense '' at usda plants database - `` trifolium pratense '' at germplasm resources information network - `` trifolium pratense '' at kansas wildflowers and grasses
