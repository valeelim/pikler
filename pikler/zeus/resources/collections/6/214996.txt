Winger (ice hockey)    winger ( ice hockey ) winger , in hockey , is a forward position of a player who is on either side of the centre forward , often along the playing area . they usually work by going beside the centre . at first , the name was given to forward players who went up and down the sides of the rink . now , there are different types of wingers in the game : they either are mainly goal scorers , or help stop the other team from scoring . this position is commonly referred to by the side of the rink that the winger normally takes . players to the left of the centre are called `` left wingers , '' and players on the right are called `` right wingers .
