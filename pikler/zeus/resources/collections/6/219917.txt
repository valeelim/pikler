Gary Jules     gary jules ( born march 19 , 1969 ) is an american singer-songwriter . he is best known for his cover of the tears for fears song , `` mad world '' that was used in the movie , `` donnie darko '' . discography . - 1998 - `` greetings from the side '' - 2001 - `` trading snakeoil for wolftickets '' u.s. # 144 - 2006 - `` gary jules '' - 2008 - `` bird ''
