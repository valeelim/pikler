Kidney cancer     kidney cancer is a form of cancer that begins in the kidneys . common forms include wilms tumor , which occurs in children , and renal cell carcinoma . treatment of renal cancer depends on the type of cancer . for renal cell carcinoma , removing the kidney , ( nephrectomy ) , is the best treatment when the cancer is localized . removing a part of the kidney , ( partial nephrectomy ) may also be an option for some patients .
