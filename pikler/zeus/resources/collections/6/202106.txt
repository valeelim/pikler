Plummet     plummet is an american trance musical group . they are from orlando , florida . the members of the group are producer/remixer eric b. muniz ( aka dj x ) and female singer cheramy burgess . they became well knon in 2001 with their cover version of plumb 's `` damaged '' . it became a hit in europe . the song got to number 12 in the uk singles chart . the group also had success in the united states . their covers of sade 's `` cherish the day '' in 2004 , and paul simon 's `` 50 ways to leave your lover '' became hits on the `` billboard '' hot dance airplay chart . `` cherish the day '' also reached # 35 in the uk chart .
