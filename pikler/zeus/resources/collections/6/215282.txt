Corner Gas     corner gas was a canadian sitcom that was on television from 2004 to 2009. it was set in the fictional town of dog river , saskatchewan and was shot in rouleau , saskatchewan . it stared canadian comic brent butt . on april 6 , 2009 , saskatchewan premier brad wall declared that april 13 would be `` corner gas day '' in saskatchewan . the show has won numerous awards and nominations including : 9 canadian comedy awards and 6 gemini awards , it has also been nominated for an international emmy award in 2004. other websites . - corner gas ' official website - corner gas ' comedy network profile
