Mason Raymond     mason evan raymond ( born september 17 , 1985 ) is a canadian professional ice hockey left winger . he currently plays for the calgary flames of the national hockey league ( nhl ) . he has also played for the vancouver canucks and toronto maple leafs . he was drafted 51st overall by the vancouver canucks in the 2005 nhl entry draft . on june 28 , 2014 , raymond was signed as a free agent by the calgary flames to a three-year , $ 9.5 million contract .
