Asclepias curassavica     scarlet milkweed ( `` asclepias curassavica '' ) is a species of milkweed plant . it is also known as mexican butterfly weed and blood-flower . it belongs in the subfamily asclepiadoideae . it is native to central america and south america . it is now naturalized around the world . it gets flowers from spring to fall . other websites . - `` asclepias curassavica '' at usda plants database - `` asclepias curassavica '' at treatment from the jepson manual - `` asclepias curassavica '' at floridata
