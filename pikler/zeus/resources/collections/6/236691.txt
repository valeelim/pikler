Kia Carnival     the kia carnival is a car produced by kia . the first generation were launched in 1999 based on the ford windstar 's platform . it were available with a 2.5 litre 6-cylinder petrol engine with 121 kw/165 hp and a 2.9 litre 4-cylinder turbo diesel engine with 93 kw/126 hp . it gets a facelift in 2001 , and were available until 2006 with a 2.5 litre 6-cylinder petrol engine with 110 kw/150 hp and a 2.9 litre 4-cylinder turbo diesel engine with 106 kw/144 hp . in 2006 , the second generation were launched based on the hyundai entourage 's platform . it is available with 2.7 and 3.8 litre 6-cylinder petrol engines with 139 kw/189 hp and 187 kw/254 hp respectively , and a 2.9 litre 4-cylinder turbo diesel engine with 136 kw/185 hp .
