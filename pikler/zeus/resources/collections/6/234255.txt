Hate speech     hate speech is communicating in a way that is meant to offend and hurt somebody . it is usually based on a factor such as somebody 's race , gender , ethnic group , sexual orientation , gender identity or religion . some places have laws that make it illegal to use hate speech . countries such as canada , france and germany have stricter laws against hate speech than the united states , where hate speech is usually legal because its constitution says that people have freedom of speech . but hate speech is an inciting of discrimination and sometimes a cause of bullying or hate crime and article 7 of universal declaration of human rights affirms that all are protected from such an inciting .
