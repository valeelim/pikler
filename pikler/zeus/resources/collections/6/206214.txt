Moving Day (book)    moving day ( book ) moving day is a young adult book of poetry by ralph fletcher , with drawings by jennifer emery . it was first published in 2006. thirty-four short free verse poems that express the feelings of a twelve year old boy moving from massachusetts to ohio . some of the topics include packing , the discovery of long lost treasures , giving things away , and doing things one last time . other websites . - ralph fletcher 's website - jennifer emery 's website
