Dougie Poynter     dougie lee poynter ( born 30 november 1987 ) is a british musician . he was born in corringham , essex . he is the bass player and singer for british pop punk band , `` mcfly '' . the band 's first single , `` five colours in her hair '' , reached number one in the charts , as did their first album , `` room on the third floor '' . mcfly were signed to island records before creating their own indie label , super records in december 2007. poynter is the band 's youngest member having joined the band at the age of 15. poynter won the 2011 uk series of `` i 'm a celebrity ... get me out of here ! `` .
