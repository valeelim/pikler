Seo Tai-ji     seo tai-ji ( b. february 21 , 1972 ) is a south korean singer . he was a member of the group seo tai ji and boys . the songs and performances of this group were very good at that time . however , seo tai ji and boys broke up . this many fans . after a few years , seo tai ji released a new solo album . he introduced many new forms of music to south korea after his comeback . until even now seo tai ji has a large effect on the singers and other people in south korea . fans of seo tai ji call him daejang which is the word for `` captain '' in korean . he is also called the ‘ president of culture . ’
