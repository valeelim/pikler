North Carolina Highway 12     north carolina highway 12 ( nc 12 ) is a road in the u.s. state of north carolina . it runs along the state 's northeastern coast . it is a popular route for visitors touring the outer banks . most sections of nc 12 are two lanes wide . three sections of the route are connected by ferries . other websites . - cape hatteras nat'l seashore - nc 12 through cape hatteras
