Anna Anka     anna anka born april 28 , 1971 in poland is an american model , actress and author and wife of singer paul anka . anna anka - earlier anna åberg - was born in poland and was later adopted to a swedish family in bjuv , skåne . in 1993 she left sweden for the u.s and appeared in movies like `` dumb and dumber '' and `` drop zone '' , she also appeared twice in the tv-series baywatch . during late 2009 she appeared in the swedish reality show ( `` svenska hollywoodfruar '' ) which followed three swedish women who had left sweden for a life in hollywood . anka received american citizenship in 2009 .
