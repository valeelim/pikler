Metronet     metronet rail was the brand of a partnership within the london underground group . the group was responsible for the maintenance and upgrade of the infrastructure on nine london underground lines . they would do the maintenance of tracks , trains , signals , civil work and stations . from 18 july 2007 to 26 may 2008 , the company was in administration and had to be bailed out by the government . on 27 may 2008 , the company was transferred back into public ownership under the authority of transport for london .
