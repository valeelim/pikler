Tabla     the tabla ( , , } , `` tablā '' from , `` tabl , tabla '' ) is an indian drum used for north indian music . the tabla invention is found in india . it is also used for classical , popular and devotional music of pakistan . the instrument is two hand drums of different size and shape , similar to bongos . they make different sounds . the drums are treble and bass drum . tabla is played hitting the fingers on the drum head and sliding the palm to create a modulating sound . a lot of sounds can be created from the tabla by changing how you use your hands . the tabla originated from the a more ancient cylindrical drum called the pakhawaj . zakir hussain is a notable tabla player . there are different gharanas or houses of tabla . zakir hussain 's style is of the punjab gharana .
