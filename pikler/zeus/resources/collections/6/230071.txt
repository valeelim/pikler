Henri Becquerel     antoine henri becquerel ( 15 december 1852 – 25 august 1908 ) was a french physicist . he was awarded the nobel prize in physics in 1903 with marie curie and pierre curie for his discovery of radioactivity in 1898. this happened when a piece of pitchblende ruined some photographic film and he investigated . other scientists had seen film similarly ruined but did not discover why . the si unit `` becquerel '' named after him . one becquerel ( bq ) is equal to one radioactive decay process per second . he was the third person in his family to occupy the physics chair at the muséum national d'histoire naturelle .
