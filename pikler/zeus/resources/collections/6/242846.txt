Splenomegaly     splenomegaly is the enlargement of the spleen . it frequently occurs in asscociation with hematological cancers . causes . - acute myeloid leukemia - malaria - chronic lymphocytic leukemia - autoimmune hemolytic anemia - polycythemia vera - chronic myeloid leukemia - myelofibrosis - typhoid fever - infectious mononucleosis - cirrhosis , often as a result of portal hypertension .
