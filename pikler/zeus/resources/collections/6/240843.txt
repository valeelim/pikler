Andrea Bocelli     andrea bocelli ( born 22 september 1958 ) is an italian tenor and crossover artist . he was blinded by a football accident at the age of 12 , and later graduated from law school , before beginning a career as a singer . since 1994 , he has recorded both pop and classical albums , and has sold over 85 million copies worldwide . he is generally considered as both the most popular italian and classical singer in the world , today . on march 2 , 2010 , he was honoured with a star on the hollywood walk of fame .
