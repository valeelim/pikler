Variation     variation in biology usually refers to the heritable differences within a species . this is important in genetics , and the study of evolution in natural populations . heritable variation is ultimately caused by mutations of genes and chromosomes , and shuffled by recombination . outcrossing keeps , and inbreeding reduces , heritable variation . other variations are caused during life , and are not passed on to the next generation by heredity . variation is also a term used in biodiversity for the variety of life forms in a given ecosystem .
