Sherbet     sherbet is a cold dessert that is like ice cream , but is made with water or fruit juice instead of milk . in europe , it is called sorbet . most sherbets are flavored with fruit flavors . orange is the most common flavor of sherbet , and other flavors include lemon , pineapple , raspberry , lime , red wine , and rainbow , which is a mix of different-colored flavors , usually including lemon and raspberry .
