Zhejiang     zhejiang is a province in east china . `` zhejiang '' is named by the qiantang river , which is also called `` zhejiang '' . `` zhejiang '' means `` bent river '' . it has a population of more than 51 million people . the capital of zhejiang province is hangzhou . zhejiang province is divided into eleven municipalities ( chinese : 地级市 ) . 1. hangzhou municipality 1. ningbo municipality 1. huzhou municipality 1. jiaxing municipality 1. jinhua municipality 1. lishui municipality 1. quzhou municipality 1. shaoxing municipality 1. taizhou municipality 1. wenzhou municipality 1. zhoushan municipality
