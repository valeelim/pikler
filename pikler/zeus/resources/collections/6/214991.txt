Asclepias californica     california milkweed ( `` asclepias californica '' ) is a species of milkweed plant . it is also known as round-hooded milkweed . it belongs in the subfamily asclepiadoideae . it is native to california and baja california . it blooms from april to july . this species is found on grassy or bushy hillsides . other websites . - `` asclepias californica '' at usda plants database - `` asclepias californica '' at treatment from the jepson manual
