Red-vented bulbul     the red-vented bulbul ( pycnonotus cafer ) is a species of bird . it belongs in the family pycnonotidae . the bird is found in tropical southern asia from india and sri lanka east to burma and southwestern china . it has been introduced to many pacific islands including fiji , samoa , tonga , and hawaii . it has also been introduced in parts of dubai , the united arab emirates and new zealand . it is included among the world 's worst introduced species .
