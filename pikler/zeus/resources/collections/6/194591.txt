Monas     monas or national monument ( ) is a 450 ft tall monument in jakarta , indonesia . it represents the spirit of the indonesians to fight for their independence . the construction began at 1961 under soekarno and was finished at 1975 under soeharto 's reign . it was made of marble with the top consisting of a 14.5 tons flame-shaped bronze coated with 35 kilograms of gold . this monument has several museums such as the freedom hall and the hall of contemplation . the freedom hall tells the story about the struggle of indonesians for their freedom while the hall of contemplation displays the declaration of independence and the recording of the speech . inside there are lifts to the observation platform where one can see a bird 's eye-view of the city of jakarta . the size of the platform is 11x11 and has the capability to hold up to 50 peoples . those who are coming to monas , can come in via the entrance around the medan merdeka park plaza , at the northern area of monas . in that area there is a fountain and a statue of prince diponegoro riding a horse . this statue is made of 8 ton bronze sculpted by italian artist prof. coberlato .
