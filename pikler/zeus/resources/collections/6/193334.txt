Belmont, New Hampshire    belmont , new hampshire belmont is a small town in new hampshire , united states . originally called upper gilmanton , belmont was chartered in 1727 as a parish of gilmanton . in 1859 upper gilmanton was separated from gilmanton . then in 1869 the town was renamed belmont . this was to honor august belmont . the townspeople hoped mr. belmont would provide financial support but he did not .
