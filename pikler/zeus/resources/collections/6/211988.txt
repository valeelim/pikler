First Vienna F.C. 1894    first vienna f.c . 1894 first vienna f.c . 1894 is a football club that plays in austria . the club , which was founded in 1894 , is the oldest one in austria and played an important part in austria 's football history . to an austrian , the club is usually just called `` vienna '' . the club started with english and austrian gardeners of nathaniel anselm von rothschild who were playing on his grounds . to avoid damages , rothschild gave them a ground and yellow-blue skirts . the yellow and blue colour is still used today.the team played its first match on 15 november 1894 against the vienna cricket and football club , and lost 4-0. the great years of vienna were the 1920´s and 1930´s . from 1938 to 1945 , when austria was occupied by nazi germany , vienna was part of the gauliga ostmark and won the german cup in 1943. in the 1940´s and 1950´s , vienna was mainly in the middle area of the table . in the 1980´s , the club played in the first league sometimes , and sometimes in the second . in 1992 they were sent down . now , they play in the second division . they are struggling to avoid relegation into the third league .
