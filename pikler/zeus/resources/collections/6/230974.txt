Winnipeg Jets     the winnipeg jets are an ice hockey team that plays in the nhl . the team plays in winnipeg , manitoba , canada at bell mts place . they were founded in 2011 when the atlanta thrashers were bought by winnipeg-based company , true north sports and entertainment and were moved back to winnipeg . the team announced the `` winnipeg jets '' as their name when they selected 7th in the first round of the 2011 nhl entry draft on june 25 , 2011. kevin cheveldayoff was named its first general manager on june 8 , 2011 and claude noel was named the new head coach of the jets on june 23 , 2011. the first winnipeg jets played in the nhl from 1972 to 1996. this team is now known as the phoenix coyotes . other websites . - official website
