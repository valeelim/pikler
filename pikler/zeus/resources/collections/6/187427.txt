Phil Mickelson     phil mickelson ( born june 16 , 1970 ) is an american golfer . he plays on the pga tour . he has 38 pga tour wins . he has won the masters three times . the most recent time was in 2010. mickelson has the nickname `` lefty '' because he swings with his left hand . he was born in san diego , california . his father phil mickelson sr. was an airline pilot and former naval aviator . and mary mickelson . he was raised there and in scottsdale , arizona .
