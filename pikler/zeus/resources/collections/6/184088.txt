Crowded House     crowded house are a new zealand rock group . they formed in 1985 in melbourne , by neil finn , after the original split enz disbanded , led by new zealand singer-songwriter brothers tim and neil finn , from te awamutu in new zealands north island . both brothers came from a rural nz farming background . they evolved from the original split enz group formed by tim and neil finn . neil finn is the main songwriter and creative director of the band and founder of crowded house . tim finn pursued a successful solo career . the brothers still perform together from time to time.. the band had a few members from different countries : but remains one of new zealands most successful rock bands including split enz and the original finn brothers . . originally active from 1985 to 1996 , the band has had consistent commercial and critical success in australia and new zealand and international chart success in two phases , beginning with their self-titled debut album , `` crowded house '' , which reached number twelve on the us album chart in 1987 and provided the top ten hits , `` do n't dream it 's over '' and `` something so strong '' . further international success came in the uk and europe with their third and fourth albums , `` woodface '' and `` together alone '' and the compilation album `` recurring dream '' , which included the hits `` fall at your feet '' , `` weather with you '' , `` distant sun '' , `` locked out '' , `` instinct '' and `` not the girl you think you are '' .
