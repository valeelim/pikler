Kya Dark Lineage     kya dark lineage is an action adventure game for the playstation 2 . `` kya dark lineage '' is about a girl named kya . kya gets thrown into another world with her brother frank . kya ends up helping creatures called nativs . nativs are little cat people . the nativs are being attacked by brazul , kya 's father who has powerful magic and turns nativs into creatures called wolfun . wolfun are bad , kya can fight wolfun and defeat them . she can also turn them back into nativs . kya goes on a mission to save her brother frank who was captured by brazul , fight wolfun and turn them back into nativs , beat up brazul and find a way back home . in america , wolfun is wolfen . other websites . - kya-gam.com - kya wiki - moby-games kya
