Kevin Clash     kevin clash ( born september 17 , 1960 ) is a puppeteer and voice actor . clash currently performs elmo on sesame street . he has also performed splinter from teenage mutant ninja turtles , clifford from muppets tonight , and baby dinosaur from dinosaurs . mr. clash is an important part of sesame street workshop . he often performs elmo in hospitals for sick children . mr. clash is well known for his ability to improvise .
