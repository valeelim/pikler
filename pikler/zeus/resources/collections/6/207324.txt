Manute Bol     manute bol ( born 16 october 1962 - died 19 june 2010 ) was a sudanese-born professional basketball player who played for the washington bullets , golden state warriors , philadelphia 76ers , and miami heat in the nba . his nba career lasted from 1985 to 1994. bol was 7 feet , 7 inches tall , and was one of only two people that tall to play in the nba . the other is former nba center gheorghe mureşan . bol was nicknamed raan cheg , 'nute , iron bol , minutiae and the dinka dunker . recently , bol had been active raising money for and telling people about human rights issues dealing with the darfur conflict in sudan , where he was born . bol died on june 19 , 2010 due to acute kidney failure and complications from stevens–johnson syndrome .
