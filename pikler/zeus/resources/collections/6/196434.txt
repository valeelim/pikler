Altadena, California    altadena , california altadena is an unincorporated census-designated place of los angeles county in the state of california , in the united states . 42,610 people were living in altadena as of 2000. it is about fourteen miles from downtown los angeles . it was built as a suburb of pasadena in 1887. however , although it was meant to be a suburb of pasadena , it has so far refused to become part of pasadena . other websites . - altadena town council - altadena chamber of commerce
