Volkswagen Golf Cabriolet     the volkswagen golf cabriolet is the cabriolet version of the volkswagen golf , introduced in 1979. the second generation were introduced in 1993 , the third in 1998. the engines for the cabriolet are almost the same as for the regular golf . the golf cabriolet was discontinued in 2002 , and succeeded by the volkswagen new beetle cabriolet and the volkswagen eos . engines . first generation . - `` 1.6 '' 55 kw ( 75 hp ) and 81 kw ( 110 hp ) - `` 1.8 '' 66 kw ( 90 hp ) , 70 kw ( 95 hp ) , 72 kw ( 98 hp ) and 82 kw ( 112 hp ) second & third generation . - `` 1.6 '' 74 kw ( 100 hp ) - `` 1.8 '' 55 kw ( 75 hp ) and 66 kw ( 90 hp ) - `` 2.0 '' 85 kw ( 115 hp ) - `` 1.9 tdi '' 66 kw ( 90 hp ) and 81 kw ( 110 hp ) other pages . - volkswagen golf variant
