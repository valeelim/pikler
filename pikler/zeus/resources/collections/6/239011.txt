2 Maccabees     2 maccabees is a sacred book of the catholic bible which focuses on the jews ' revolt against antiochus iv epiphanes and ends with the defeat of the syrian general nicanor in 161 bc by judas maccabeus , the hero of the work . 2 maccabees was written in koine greek , probably in alexandria , egypt , `` c '' 124 bc . it gives a revised version of the historical events recounted in the first seven chapters of 1 maccabees .
