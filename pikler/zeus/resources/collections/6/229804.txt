Drew McIntyre     drew galloway ( born june 6 , 1985 in ayr , scotland ) , is a scottish professional wrestler , better know by his ring name drew mcintyre . he is currently signed to wwe working in their developmental territory nxt , where he is the current nxt champion . during his previous run in the wwe , he won the wwe intercontinental championship one time and the wwe tag team championship one time with cody rhodes . mcintyre married former wwe diva taryn terrell ( known by the ring name tiffany ) in may 2011 but they divorced on may 24 , 2011. on june 12 , 2014 , mcintyre was released from his wwe contract . in april 2017 , he returned to wwe in their developmental territory nxt . he is the current nxt champion . other websites . - wwe profile
