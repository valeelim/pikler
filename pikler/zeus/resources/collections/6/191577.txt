Xue Yue     xue yue ( 1896-1998 ) was a nationalist general in the republic of china . xue joined the chinese army in 1914 , at the age of 18. along with hu zongnan and tang enbo , xue was one of the kuomintang generals most respected by the japanese during the second sino-japanese war . xue was in the defense of greater wuhan , commanding the 1st army corps . in the mountains xue 's army succeeded in nearly destroying the 106th division of the japanese army . during the battle , most of the japanese officers were killed and the japanese had to drop 300 officers by parachute into the battlefield . xue lived to 101 years old .
