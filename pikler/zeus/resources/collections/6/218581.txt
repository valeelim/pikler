Indonesian Rupiah     the indonesian rupiah ( idr ) is the money used in indonesia . rupiah started to be used in 1946. the coins that are used now are 25 rupiah , 50 rupiah , 100 rupiah , 200 rupiah , 500 rupiah and 1000 rupiah . the banknotes that are used are 1000 rupiah , 2000 rupiah , 5000 rupiah , 10 000 rupiah , 20 000 rupiah , 50 000 rupiah and 100 000 rupiah .
