Sputnik 1     sputnik 1 was the first artificial satellite to go around the earth . it was made by the soviet union . it was launched on 4 october 1957 at baikonur cosmodrome . it orbited ( went around ) the earth for three months . it carried a radio transmitter . it did 1,440 orbits of the earth during this time . it went down into earth 's atmosphere on 4 january 1958 and burned up . the united states was very surprised when the soviet union sent sputnik 1 into space . it did not want to fall behind . so , it began spending more money on science and education . this was when the space race between the soviet union and the united states began . the word `` sputnik '' comes from the russian спутник , literally `` travelling companion '' . it is pronounced or , not . related pages . - sputnik programme - sputnik 2
