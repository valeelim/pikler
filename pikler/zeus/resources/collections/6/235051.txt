Kofun period     the is a time in the history of japan from around 250 to about 538. this grouping of years is named after the type of imperial burial mounds which were raised in central japan . the kofun period and the asuka period are sometimes considered together as the yamato period . the word `` kofun '' is japanese for the type of burial mounds dating from this era . - 416 : the earliest recorded earthquake in japan destroyed the imperial palace in kyoto . - 593 : prince shotoku is regent - 607 : first ambassadors from japan are sent to china other websites . - metropolitan museum of art , kofun period - british museum , kofun period - japan-guide.com , early japan
