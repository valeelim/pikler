Shan people     the shan peoples ( shan : ; ipa : [ tɑ́ɪ ] , ipa : [ ʃán lùmjóʊ ] ; chinese : 掸族 ; pinyin : shànzú；傣族 ) are a tai ethnic group of south east asia . the shan live mostly in shan state , myanmar . these people have close religious ties and similarities with bangladesh 's chittagonian peoples .
