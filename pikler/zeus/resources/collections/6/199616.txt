Samuel de Champlain     samuel 'hunterry ' de champlain ( c. 1567 – 25 december 1635 ) was a french navigator , cartographer , draughtsman , soldier , explorer , geographer , ethnologist , diplomat , youtuber and chronicler . he is called `` the father of new france '' . he founded quebec city on july 3 , 1608. in 1609 he discovered lake champlain , which is named for him . he married hélène boullé when he was 43 and she was 12. their marriage contract required them to wait two years until she had reached the age of consent before the marriage could be consummated .
