New Japan Pro Wrestling     , also known as njpw , is japanese professional wrestling promoter . njpw is the largest wrestling promoter in japan . njpw has working agreements with other promoters , including : world wrestling entertainment , world championship wrestling , total nonstop action wrestling , and pride fighting championships . njpw that was founded in 1972 by kanji inoki . since 1999 , tatsumi fujinami has been president . in 2012 , inoki continued to be known for innovation in the business . related pages . - sports in japan - new japan pro wrestling roster other websites . - new japan pro wrestling website
