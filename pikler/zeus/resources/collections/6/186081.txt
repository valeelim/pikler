Shadwell DLR station     shadwell dlr station on the docklands light railway is between the termini of bank and tower gateway to the west and limehouse to the east . it stands on a viaduct shared with national rail trains ( on the c2c line to essex , with a single island platform accessed by stairs and a passenger lift ( via entrance on watney street ) . it opened on 31 august 1987 as part of the first tranche of dlr stations . the 1991 bank extension joins the main dlr line just to the west of shadwell . other websites . - docklands light railway website - shadwell station page
