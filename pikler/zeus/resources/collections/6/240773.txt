Vinyl cutter     a vinyl cutter is a type of computer controlled machine . small vinyl cutters look like computer printers . the computer controls the movement of a sharp blade . this blade is used to cut out shapes and letters from sheets of thin self-adhesive plastic ( vinyl ) . vinyl cutters are mainly used by to make signs , banners and advertisements . advertisements seen on automobiles and vans are often made with vinyl cut letters . vinyl cutters can also be used to cut out shapes from thin cardboard . it can also be used to make adhesive electronic boards .
