Prut     prut , or pruth , ( ) is a 953 km long river in eastern europe . it flows to the southeast to join the danube river near reni . this is east of galaţi . these towns can be found along the prut river , from source to mouth : delatyn , kolomyia , sniatyn , chernivtsi , novoselytsia , darabani , ungheni , cahul . these are the tributaries of the prut river : left : racovăţ . right : cheremosh , herţa , poiana , corneşti , isnovăţ , rădăuţi , ghireni , volovăţ , badu , başeu , corogea , berza veche , râioasa , soloneţ , cerchezoaia , jijia , cozmeşti , bohotin , moşna , pruteţ , sărata , elan , horincea , oancea , stoeneasa , chineja .
