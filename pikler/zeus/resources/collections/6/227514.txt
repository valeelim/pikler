Seal of Colorado     the seal of the state of colorado is an adaptation of the `` territorial seal '' which was adopted by the `` first territorial assembly '' on november 6 , 1861. the only changes made in the territorial seal design being the changing of the words , `` state of colorado '' and the figures `` 1876 '' for the corresponding words on the territorial seal . the first `` general assembly '' of the state of colorado approved the adoption of the state seal on march 15 , 1877. the `` colorado secretary of state '' alone is authorized to affix the great seal of colorado to any document whatsoever .
