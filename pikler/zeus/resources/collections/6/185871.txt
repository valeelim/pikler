HY     hy is a japanese rock music band formed in year 2000 by five high school friends in okinawa . their name comes from the initials of their hometown , higashi yakena . the band became popular by performing music in the streets . their first album `` departure '' ( okinawa-only ) sold out immediately . in 2002 , the band did a live performance with american group , linkin park . members . - hideyuki sinzato born december 4 , 1983 - guitar , vocals , rap - syun naka born october 5 , 1983 - drums , rap - sinsuke kyoda born july 17 , 1983 - bass - izumi nakasone born january 11 , 1983 - keyboard , vocals - yuuhei miyazato born february 1 , 1984 - guitar
