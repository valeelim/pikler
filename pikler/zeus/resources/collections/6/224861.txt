Esmeraldas woodstar     the esmeraldas woodstar , ( `` chaetocercus berlepschi '' ) is a species of hummingbird . it is found only in ecuador . its habitat is subtropical or tropical moist lowland forests . it is threatened by habitat loss . references . - birdlife international 2004. chaetocercus berlepschi . 2006 iucn red list of threatened species . downloaded on 10 july 2007 .
