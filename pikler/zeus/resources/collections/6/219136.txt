Jeff Probst     jeffrey lee `` jeff '' probst ( born november 4 , 1962 ) is an american game show host , executive producer and reporter . he was born in wichita , kansas , but mostly grew up in bellevue , washington . he is best known for being the host of the american version of the reality show `` survivor '' . on september 21 , 2008 probst won the first primetime emmy award for outstanding host for a reality or reality-competition program . on september 20 , 2009 he won the same award again . probst currently hosts the talk show `` the jeff probst show '' , which started on september 10 , 2012. other websites . - cbs.com profile
