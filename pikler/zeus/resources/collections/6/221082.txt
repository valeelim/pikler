Dancin' Homer    dancin ' homer `` dancin ' homer '' is the fifth episode of the second season of `` the simpsons '' . it first aired on the fox network in the united states on november 8 , 1990. in this episode , homer fires up the crowd at a springfield isotopes baseball game and is chosen to be the team 's new mascot . he becomes a well liked attraction very fast and the `` isotopes '' start a winning streak . as a result , homer has ranked up to a team in capital city , the `` capital city capitals '' . the simpson family moves there , but homer fails to excite the crowd , and the family returns home .
