Rover 75     the rover 75 is a car produced by rover from 1999 to 2005. the 75 replaced both the 600 and the 800 series . it shares many components with the e46 bmw 3 series , and were available with a 4-cylinder 1.8 litre petrol engine , 6-cylinder 2.0 and 2.5 litre petrol engines , a 8-cylinder 4.6 litre petrol engine and 4-cylinder 2.0 litre turbodiesel engines . the 75 was available in both 4-door saloon and 5-door station wagon bodystyles , the latter was added in 2001 alongside sporty mg models . it had a facelift in early 2004 .
