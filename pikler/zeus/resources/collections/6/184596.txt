Treeshrew     a treeshrew is a small mammal from the tropical forests of southeast asia . they make up the families tupaiidae and ptilocercidae and all of order scandentia . there are 20 species in 5 genera . although called treeshrews , they are not true shrews ( although they were previously classified in the insectivora ) , and are not all arboreal . among other things , they eat `` rafflesia '' fruit .
