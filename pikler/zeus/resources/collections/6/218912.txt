Crash Test Dummy     a crash test dummy is a test device ( commonly abbreviated 'atd ' ) that is made to be close to the same shape , size , heaviness , proportions and articulation of the human body . they sometimes have equipment embedded in them that measures data about how hard things are hitting it and how fast the atd is moving around . atds have many applications but are commonly used in simulated ( test ) car crashes to see how safe the cars are for people when they crash .
