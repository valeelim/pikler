Saudi riyal     the riyal is the official money used in saudi arabia . a shorter name for it is sr ( ) . the riyal is divided into 100 halala ( ) . the riyal has been saudi arabia 's money since saudi arabia became a country . it was the currency of hejaz before saudi arabia was a country . there are 5 , 10 , 25 , 50 and 100 halala coins ; and 1 , 5 , 10 , 20 , 50 , 100 , and 500 riyal banknotes . the riyal is fixed to be the same as 0.266667 united states dollars .
