Ages of Man     ages of man is an ancient greek myth that divides the history of the world into five eras . in the first eras , humans lived close to gods and were happy . in the latest eras , the gods have left the world and humans live in pain and unhappiness . this myth was first written down by greek poet hesiod , about 2700 years ago . 1. in the golden age , humans lived together with gods in peace . plato said that there were also demons in this age , who were good and helped people . all of them were ruled by kronos . 1. in the silver age , humans lived for one hundred years as children and fought each other when they became adults . they refused to worship kronos ' son zeus , so he killed them all . 1. in the bronze age , humans fought each other all the time . they have all been killed in their own wars . 1. in the heroic age , demigods and heroes lived among humans . perseus , heracles , and argonauts lived in this age . it ended after the trojan war . 1. in the iron age , gods and other supernatural beings abandoned humans . as a result , humans have forgotten their virtues and lived in misery . it was the age when hesiod himself lived .
