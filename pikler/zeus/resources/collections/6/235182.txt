Down by Law (band)    down by law ( band ) down by law is a punk rock band made in or around 1990. frontman dave smalley of `` dag nasty '' fame teamed up with the members of `` chemical people '' for their first album and went through many changes all around over the years . starting on epitaph records with their first self called lp , dbl continued on epitaph until `` last of the sharpshooters '' in 1997. a brief stint on go kart records saw the release of `` fly the flag '' , and now with their newest release of `` windward tides and wayward sails '' in 2003 finds them on union label group .
