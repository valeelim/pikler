Ptolemaida     ptolemaida , former kailaria , is a greek town in the kozani prefecture in the region of macedonia . the city is best known for its coal mines of lignite and power station . it has been also called by greek people `` the socket of greece '' because it has very strong power stations . its population is 28.679 inhabitants according to the 2001 census . ptolemaida is the biggest industrial , trade , and rural city of the prefecture and one of the most developed cities in industry in greece . official website
