Ceratopsia     the ceratopsia are the horned dinosaurs of the order ornithischia . `` triceratops '' is the best-known , and one of the largest . they are a group of herbivorous , beaked dinosaurs which thrived in what is now north america and asia , during the cretaceous period . ancestral forms of this group lived earlier , in the jurassic . early members , such as `` psittacosaurus '' , were small and bipedal . later members , including `` centrosaurus '' and `` triceratops '' , became very large quadrupeds . they developed elaborate facial horns and a neck frill . the frill served mainly to protect the vulnerable neck from bipedal predators like tyrannosaurus , who attacked from above . the frill was no doubt also used in display , thermoregulation , the attachment of large neck and chewing muscles or some combination of the above . ceratopsians ranged in size from 1 meter ( 3 ft ) and 23 kilograms ( 50 lb ) to over 9 meters ( 30 ft ) and 5,400 kg ( 12,000 lb ) . `` triceratops '' is by far the best-known ceratopsian to the general public .
