Red Bull Racing     red bull racing is a formula one team owned by austrian beverage company red bull . red bull also own another f1 team : scuderia toro rosso . the red bull team is based in milton keynes in the uk but holds an austrian licence . the team is managed by christian horner who is also the boss of the arden international , which participates to the gp2 series and to the formula renault 3.5 series .
