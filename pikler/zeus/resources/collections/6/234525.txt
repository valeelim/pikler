Akbash Dog     the akbash dog is a large breed ( type ) of dog which comes from turkey . it has a white coat of fur . it was bred to protect sheep in the field from thieves and predators . while this dog has been used for this job in turkey for many hundreds of years , it is not often found outside of turkey . the akbash dog is closely related to other large , white dogs used to protect sheep , such as the komondor and the aidi .
