Eoghan Quigg     eoghan quigg ( 12 july 1992 ) is a pop singer based in the united kingdom and ireland . he has been active since 2008 when he was a contestent on `` the x factor '' where he finished in third place . in ireland he has had a number 1 album called `` eoghan quigg '' . quigg was going to be signed by simon cowell , ceo of syco records , but was instead signed to rca .
