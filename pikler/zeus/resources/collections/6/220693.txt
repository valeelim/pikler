Black-headed grosbeak     the black-headed grosbeak ( `` pheucticus melanocephalus '' ) is a species of bird . it belongs in the family cardinalidae . it is a medium sized , seed-eating bird . it is 18-19 cm long . it weights 35-49 g. the black-headed grosbeak is a migratory bird . it has nesting grounds from southwestern british columbia , through the western half of the united states , into central mexico . it occurs as an introduced species further south in central america . other websites . - `` pheucticus melanocephalus '' at the cornell lab of ornithology
