Proxy marriage     a proxy marriage is when two people are getting married separately , but are getting married to one another . it was common for european kings and nobles to be wed by proxy marriage . napoleon i of france was wed by proxy marriage to marie louise , duchess of parma . catherine of aragon wed prince arthur by proxy . a famous 17th-century painting by peter paul rubens shows the proxy marriage of marie de medici . today , this form of marriage is illegal in most countries , or it requires a special permit . it is still legal in a few islamic countries , and in some us states .
