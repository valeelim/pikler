Exhibition     an exhibition is an organized event in which a collection of special objects can be viewed by the public . the objects are often brought together from many places for the period of the exhibition . the objects on show are called exhibits . museums often organize special exhibitions , such as paintings by a famous painter . these paintings will mostly be “ on loan ” , i.e . specially borrowed from other museums . exhibitions can be about anything . they may be to do with art , or they can also be science exhibitions . some exhibitions show things which can be bought . firms exhibit the things they have made , hoping to encourage business . these exhibitions are called trade fairs . they are sometimes called expositions or expos . they may be open to the public , but others are just for invited business people . related pages . - art exhibition - the great exhibition - convention center - world 's fair
