Neelo     neelo is the stage name of abida riaz ) who is an actress from lahore city and mother of the movie actor shaan . she was born june 29 , 1945 in bhera , near sargodha . her real name was cynthia alexander fernandes , and she was a christian . later , when she got married to riaz shahid she changed her name to abida riaz , and also became a muslim . shahid died in 1972 , but riaz has kept her name the same .
