Scott Robinson     scott robinson ( born 12 march 1992 in edinburgh ) is a scottish professional football player currently playing for heart of midlothian in the scottish premier league . he is the youngest player in spl history . he is also the youngest player to have ever played football for hearts , having made his first play in a 1-0 win over inverness caledonian thistle on 26 april 2008. he was 16 years , 1 month and 14 days old .
