Coblentz (lunar crater)    coblentz ( lunar crater ) coblentz is a small crater that can be found on the far side of the moon , to the south of the much larger crater bolyai . this crater has a circular edge , but it has been worn away by asteroid impacts . this is most noticeable at the southern end where a gap exists in the rim . the interior floor has very few features except for several tiny craterlets . a ridge arcs from the northwest rim of coblentz to join the southern rim of bolyai . there are several patches of dark ( low albedo ) material just to the south and southwest of coblentz .
