Northern Fury F.C.    northern fury f.c . northern fury f.c . is a soccer club from townsville , queensland , australia . the club was founded in 2008 and competed in the a-league as `` north queensland fury fc '' . on 1 march 2011 , the club was removed from the league due to financial instability . on 3 october 2012 , the club was re-formed as northern fury fc after it was granted a license to play in the npl queensland . northern fury play their home fixtures at townsville sports reserve . honours . minor trophies/honours . - queensland nickel cup - 2009/10 ( nq fury v 's gold coast united : { contested over 3 league fixtures } nq fury w2/d0/l1 ) - chief minister 's cup
