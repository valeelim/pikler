Baccarat, Meurthe-et-Moselle    baccarat , meurthe-et-moselle baccarat is a town in the meurthe-et-moselle area in northeastern france . in 1764 , king louis the fifteenth allowed to the bishop of metz to start a glass factory at baccarat . the baccarat crystalworks are famous for their glass and crystal artwork , and the musée du cristal , owned by the company , is lorraine 's biggest private museum . the church of saint rémy is well known for its artistic glass windows . other pages . - communes of the meurthe-et-moselle department other websites . - http : //www.ville-baccarat.fr
