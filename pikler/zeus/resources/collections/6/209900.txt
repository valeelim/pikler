Pro Tools     pro tools is a digital audio workstation platform for mac os x and microsoft windows operating systems . it was developed and manufactured by digidesign . digidesign a division of avid technology . pro tools is similar to a multi-track tape recorder and mixer . it has other features that can only be done in the digital medium . the high-end version supports sample rates of up to 192 khz and bit depths of 16 and 24 bit , opens wav , aiff , mp3 , sdii audio files . it also supports quicktime video files .
