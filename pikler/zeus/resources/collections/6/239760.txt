California Polytechnic State University     the california polytechnic state university is a public university . it is in the city of san luis obispo , california , united states . the university is one of 23 campuses affiliated with the california state university system . california polytechnic state university offers 70 degree programs in many disciplines like engineering , science , architecture , and many others . the university covers 9,678 acres and it is near the metropolitan city of los angeles . california polytechnic state university was founded in 1901 california polytechnic state university student-athletes play sports against other universities using the name `` cal poly mustangs '' . the university is a member of the ncaa division i. most of the school 's teams play in the big west conference ; the football team plays in the big sky conference . the official colors of the university and its athletic teams are green and gold .
