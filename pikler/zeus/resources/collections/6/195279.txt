Halifax, West Yorkshire    halifax , west yorkshire halifax is a large town in west yorkshire , england . the town has a population of 82,056. halifax lies close to huddersfield and bradford . the major cities of leeds and manchester are slightly further afield . the economic booms of leeds and manchester , particularly in the financial sector have led to halifax becoming a commuter town for these two cities . halifax has a historic town centre , compromising much victorian architecture . landmarks include the town hall , piece hall , the wainwright monument , the square chapel and halifax minister .
