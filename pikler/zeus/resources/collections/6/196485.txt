D. Train     d. train is a r & b post-disco band from the new york , united states . they were formed in 1980. in band there is james `` d-train '' williams and hubert eaves iii . hubert plays on keyboard , bass guitar and drums , james uses vocals as a musical instrument . this duo have some hits on `` billboard dance '' & `` billboard r & b '' charts . their songs include `` keep on '' ( 1982 ) , `` walk on by '' ( 1982 ) , `` you 're the one for me '' ( 1982 ) . they also did the song named `` pokérap '' from pokémon soundtrack album `` 2ba master '' .
