Magnus Carlsen     magnus carlsen ( born sven magnus øen carlsen , 30 november 1990 ) is the world chess champion . he became world champion on 22 november 2013 , by defeating viswanathan anand by 6½ to 3½ . carlsen also won the return match in 2014 by 6½4½ . a norwegian chess grandmaster and chess prodigy , carlsen is the highest rated player in the world , and the highest rated player in the history of chess . his peak rating of 2881 is higher than even kasparov achieved . kasparov 's highest rating was 2851. on 26 april 2004 , carlsen became a grandmaster at the age of 13 years , 148 days . this made him the third youngest grandmaster in history . he played at the nanjing pearl spring tournament in september–october 2009. this lifted him to an elo rating of 2801. this made him second in the world . he is the fifth player to have a rating over 2800. he was 18 years old when he got this rating . this made him the youngest person to have a rating above 2800. on 1 january 2010 , the new fide rating list was published . he was 19 years and 32 days old when he became the youngest chess player in history to be ranked world number one . he broke the previous record held by vladimir kramnik .
