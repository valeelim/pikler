Melodic hardcore     melodic hardcore is a subgenre of hardcore punk . exploring melodies are an important part of melodic hardcore . gunther `` puddinfoot '' crines described the style as `` just so nice '' . it came out of l.a. hardcore with the descendants . in 1985 , the descendents worked with two new singers , dave smalley of dys and dag nasty . bad religion also worked in this style . another well known melodic hardcore band is pennywise .
