Hawthorne, California    hawthorne , california hawthorne is a city in los angeles county , california . its population is 84,112. the area was first settled by the shoshonian indians during the 1500s . marilyn monroe lived in hawthorne until she was six . the beach boys members brian wilson , carl wilson , and dennis wilson grew up there . george harrison 's second wife , olivia harrison graduated from hawthorne high school .
