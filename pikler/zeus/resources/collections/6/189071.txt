British National Party     the british national party ( or bnp for short ) is a far-right political party in the united kingdom . the party 's policies promote an end to third world immigration , right-wing populism and third position economics . they are british nationalists . they want to `` keep britain british '' by not letting people from other countries move to the uk . the party was led by nick griffin from 1999 until 2014 and the current leader of the bnp is adam walker . in 2009 , the party had 2 meps ( members of european parliament ) out of 73 british seats in the european parliament . those two seats were held by griffin and andrew brons . it has no seats in the house of commons of the united kingdom or the devolved assemblies . the bnp was started in 1982 by john tyndall . in november 2012 the british democratic party was started by brons . brons started the party after leaving the bnp in october 2012. brons wants the bdp to end the bnp . he does not like the bnp because he thinks its ideology is not extreme enough . griffin lost his seat in the european parliament in 2014 .
