Haboob     a haboob is a storm that forms in the downdrafts of a thunderstorm . the downdrafts are caused by air being pushed down onto the ground , similiar to a leaf blower . they can be seen in deserts like the saharan and in places like arizona . a thunderstorm can make strong downdrafts , which is just wind that blows down towards the surface of the earth . when the wind hits the earth , it starts to move sideways and picks up dirt and dust . it makes a cloud of dust that is bad for people , animals and plants . references . - haboobs , arizona department of transportation . - haboob on winds of the world
