St. Patrick's Cathedral (New York)    st. patrick 's cathedral ( new york ) saint patrick 's cathedral is a decorated neo-gothic-style catholic cathedral church in the united states . it is the seat of the archbishop of the roman catholic archdiocese of new york , and a parish church , located on the east side of fifth avenue between 50th and 51st streets in midtown manhattan , new york city , new york . it faces rockefeller center .
