Moby-Dick     moby-dick is a novel written by herman melville . it was first published in 1851. the story is told by a seaman named ishmael . he sails on a whaling ship called the `` pequod '' . ahab is the captain of the ship . he wants to kill a white whale called moby dick . the whale bit his leg off . the book received mixed reviews . `` moby-dick '' is now thought to be one of the greatest novels ever written .
