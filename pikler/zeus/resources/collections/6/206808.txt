I'm Not Alone    i 'm not alone `` i 'm not alone '' is the lead single from scottish dj and producer calvin harris ' second album , ready for the weekend , and his first appearance on a single since `` dance wiv me '' with dizzee rascal and chrome , which peaked at number 1 in the uk singles chart in 2008 . `` i 'm not alone '' was first-played by pete tong on bbc radio 1 as his `` essential new tune '' on january 23 , 2009 and has been described as a slice of euphoric dance . on april 12 , 2009 , the song debuted at # 1 on the uk singles chart , giving harris his first solo # 1 single in the uk and second overall .
