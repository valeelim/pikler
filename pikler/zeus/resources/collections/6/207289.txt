Under the Volcano     under the volcano is a novel by malcolm lowry first sold in 1947. the events of the novel happen over one day in 1938 where a mexican festival is taking place . geoffrey firmin is a smart british man living in mexico . he wants to write a book but he drinks too much alcohol . geoffrey 's wife , yvonne , is fed up of his drinking . she once had an affair with geoffrey 's brother hugh . related pages . - le monde 's 100 books of the century
