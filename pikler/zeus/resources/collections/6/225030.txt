Ann Coulter     ann coulter ( born december 8 , 1961 ) is from the united states . she is a writer and is known for her controversial opinions about politics . she was asked to go on television , radio and speaks at public and private events . coulter has described herself as a `` polemicist '' or someone who tells the truth even if it will cause problems . she does not want to `` pretend to be impartial or balanced .
