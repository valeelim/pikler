Blood Feud (The Simpsons)    blood feud ( the simpsons ) `` blood feud '' is the twenty-second and last episode of the second season of `` the simpsons '' . it was first shown on the fox network in the united states on july 11 , 1991. in this episode , mr. burns falls ill and badly needs a blood transfusion . homer learns bart has burns 's rare blood type and wants his son to donate some , promising bart that they will be handsomely rewarded . however , after getting the blood , all burns does is send the family a card . homer , who is mad , writes a rude reply , but marge tells him at the last minute not to send it , although bart mails it anyway .
