Johnny Chan     johnny chan ( born in guangzhou , china in 1957 ) is a poker player . he won the world series of poker main event in 1987 and 1988. he took second place at the 1989 world series of poker . he has won 10 world series of poker bracelets . chan moved with his family in 1962 from guangzhou to hong kong , then in 1968 to phoenix , arizona and later in 1973 to houston , texas . when he was 21 , chan dropped out of the university of houston and moved to las vegas to become a professional gambler .
