Mi Plan     mi plan ( `` my plan '' ) is the fourth studio album by singer nelly furtado it is also her first ever all spanish album . it was released in september , 2009. seven of the songs were written together by furtado and cuban musician , alex cuba . singles . - `` manos al aire '' was the first single released from the album . it was on the radio on june 30 , 2009 , with digital versions on sale the next day .
