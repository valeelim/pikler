Calligraphy     calligraphy is the art of writing . the word is from the greek language and means `` beautiful writing '' . in the west , this was an interest in decorating words on the page . calligraphy in the western world was always considered a minor art . east asian calligraphy was a `` high art '' form before the tang dynasty . it continues to have a high status among the arts . it is practiced today by many people including school children .
