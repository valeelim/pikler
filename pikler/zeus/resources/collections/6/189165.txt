Kingpin     the kingpin ( wilson fisk ) is a fictional character in the marvel universe . the character first appears in `` the amazing spider-man '' # 50 ( july 1967 ) , and was created by writer stan lee and artist john romita , sr. the kingpin often appears as an adversary of spider-man , daredevil and the punisher . enemies . - spiderman - daredevil - punisher other media . - ( 1988 - 1989 ) - kingpin appears in some episodes with the crimes gang . - spider-man ( 1994 ) - spider-man : the new animated series - the spectacular spider-man
