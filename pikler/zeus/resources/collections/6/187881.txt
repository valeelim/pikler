Killzone 2     killzone 2 is a is a first-person shooter for the playstation 3 , made by guerrilla games and published by sony computer entertainment . it is the third game in the killzone series that began on the playstation 2 with killzone and continued on the playstation portable with killzone : liberation . the game was first announced at e3 2005 , with a technology demo on display , while the first gameplay video was shown two years later at e3 2007 .
