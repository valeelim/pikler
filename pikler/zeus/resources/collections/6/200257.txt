Sven Lõhmus     sven lõhmus ( born july 13 , 1972 ) is an estonian music producer . he has worked with a number of successful baltic pop acts , including pop-rock groups vanilla ninja , suntribe and urban symphony . three times , in 2003 , 2004 , and 2010 , he won the award for `` best author '' to the `` eesti popmuusika aastaauhinnad '' .
