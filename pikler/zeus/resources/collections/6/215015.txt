1910–11 NHA season     the 1910–11 nha season was the second season of the former national hockey association . five teams played 16 games each , starting on december 31 , 1910 and ending on march 10 , 1911. the ottawa hockey club won the league championship and took over the stanley cup from the montreal wanderers . † protested by renfrew . ‡ replay of protested game . further readings . - podnieks , andrew ; `` hockey hall of fame '' ( 2004 ) . lord stanley 's cup . triumph books , 12 , 50. isbn 1-55168-261-3 .
