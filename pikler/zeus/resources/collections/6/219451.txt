Thalassomedon     thalassomedon ( meaning `` lord of the seas '' ) was a plesiosaur ( not a dinosaur , but an extinct marine reptile from the mesozoic era that lived in the open oceans and breathed air ) . fossils have been found in the usa . the type species , `` thalassomedon haningtoni '' ( named by welles in 1943 ) . it had a long neck ( with 63 vertebrae ) , a long snout , long , sharp teeth ( up to 5 cm long ) , a short , pointed tail and four flippers . this reptile was about 40 feet ( 12 m ) long ; its skull was about 18.7 inches ( 47 cm ) long .
