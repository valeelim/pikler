Cue stick     a cue stick ( or simply cue ) , is a kind of stick used for sports like pool , snooker and carom billiards . it is used to hit a ball , normally the `` cue ball '' . cues are fixed-up sticks , normally about 58 inches ( 1.5 m ) long and 18–21 ounces ( 510–600 g ) . most cues are made of wood , but sometimes the wood is covered with other materials like carbon fiber or fiberglass . an old term for a cue , used from the 16th to early 19th centuries , is `` billiard stick '' .
