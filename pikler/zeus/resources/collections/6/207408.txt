Rakesh Sharma     rakesh sharma ( born 13 january 1949 in patiala , punjab india ) was a squadron leader in the indian air force . he was the first indian and 138th person to travel in space . sharma retired from the air force as wing commander . he spent eight days in salyut 7 space station in april 1984. he was given the hero of soviet union by the soviet government and the ashok chakra by the indian government . other websites . - spacefacts biography of rakesh sharma
