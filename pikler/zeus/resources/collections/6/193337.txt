70 Virginis     70 virginis is a yellow dwarf star that is around 58 light-years away from the earth in the constellation virgo . 70 virginis is not as big or hot as earth 's sun . in 1996 , 70 virginis was found to have an extrasolar planet in orbit around it . there is also a dust disc that orbits the star . the temperature of the disc is 156 k and it is about 3.4 au from the star . other websites . - simbad : hd 117176 -- high proper-motion star - solstation : 70 virginis - extrasolar planets encyclopaedia : 70 virginis - extrasolar visions : 70 virginis
