Jim Lovell     jim lovell ( born march 25 , 1928 ) is a former nasa astronaut . he was pilot of the apollo 8 and commander of the apollo 13 mission . crew of apollo 13 mission failed to land on moon because of loss of electric power and failure of both oxygen tanks due to technical problems . because of efforts of crew and ground control room , all three astronauts aboard apollo 13 returned to earth safely .
