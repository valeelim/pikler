Korea University     korea university is a private university in seoul , south korea . along with seoul national university and yonsei university , ku is one of the three most renowned universities in the country , collectively known as 'sky . ' founded in 1905 with the struggle against japanese colonialism as a historical background , korea university holds its legacy with highly national values as priorities . these days , however , an emphasis on internationalism and globalization is also becoming an important value that ku pursues . korea university has 81 academic departments , and is most well known for its law school , business school , and college of education . it has many notable alumni , including the former president of the republic of korea lee myung-bak and former mayor of seoul oh se-hoon . cha bum-kun , famous football manager and former player ( nicknamed tscha bum ) , graduated from ku . in 2009 , famous figure skater kim yu-na entered korea university as a physical education major . about 28,000 students are currently studying in korea university , including 1,000 foreign students .
