Four Horsemen (professional wrestling)    four horsemen ( professional wrestling ) the four horsemen were a very popular professional wrestling stable in the national wrestling alliance and world championship wrestling . they debuted 1986 and disbanded 1999 , the original members of the stable were ric flair , arn anderson , tully blanchard , and ole anderson . the stable is named after four horsemen of the apocalypse . the stable was inducted into the wwe hall of fame by dusty rhodes in 2012. other websites . - the four horsemen 's profile on wwe.com
