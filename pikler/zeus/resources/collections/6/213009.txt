Nuclear football     the nuclear football is the name given to a black briefcase which is carried along with the president of the united states at all times . inside the briefcase are instruction manuals , a radio transmitter and authentication codes for the emergency broadcast system . the president can use the briefcase in case of emergency , to order the use of nuclear weapons . the briefcase is carried by a military aide for the president . references . - http : //www.usatoday.com/news/washington/2005-05-05-nuclear-football_x.htm - http : //www.globalsecurity.org/wmd/systems/nuclear-football.htm
