The Othersiders     the othersiders was an american , television series on cartoon network . it is about a group of five teenage friends who like the paranormal and explore haunted places to find any paranormal happenings . the series started on june 17 , 2009 , as part of the cartoon network programming block `` cn real '' . the second season of `` the othersiders '' began october 7 , which was said on the twitter pages of othersiders kc , jackie , zack , and riley , and on `` the othersiders '' 's official website . instead of just seeing the episode on wednesdays , the show will start being seen on different days of the week in a pattern starting with `` linda vista hospital '' . the final episodes will be played mondays , wednesdays and fridays during the month of october 2009 with the season finale , `` alcatraz '' , started october 30 , 2009 .
