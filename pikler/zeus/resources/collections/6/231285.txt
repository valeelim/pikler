Phylogeny     a phylogeny is a proposal of how organisms are related by their evolutionary history . it is based on the evidence that all living things are related by common descent . the evidence for phylogeny comes from palaeontology , comparative anatomy , and dna sequence analysis . the main product of phylogenetics is a phylogenetic tree or tree of life . this is a diagram showing a pattern of ancestor/descendent relationships . information may be related to geological periods or estimated dates . a biological classification is another type of product .
