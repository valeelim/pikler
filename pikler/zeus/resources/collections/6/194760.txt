Philadelphia Union     philadelphia union is a soccer team that plays in major league soccer in chester , pennsylvania . it was founded in 2008. the team began play in 2010 as a member of the eastern conference . they play their home games at the talen energy stadium in chester , pennsylvania . the club 's colors are gold and black . in august 2015 , the team announced that they would have a reserve team in bethlehem , pennsylvania that will compete in the united soccer league ( usl ) starting in 2016. that team would be bethlehem steel fc . official website of philadelphia union
