The Marvelous Misadventures of Flapjack     the marvelous misadventures of flapjack is an american animated television series made for cartoon network that started on june 5 , 2008. the show was made by cartoonist mark `` thurop '' van orman , who has worked as a writer and storyboard artist on `` camp lazlo '' and `` the grim adventures of billy & mandy '' . the main point of the marvelous misadventures of flapjack is the main characters ( captain knuckles , flapjack & bubbie ) trying to find the magical island of candied island . flapjack is an adventurous little boy taking after and learning from captain knuckles , knuckles plays the role of a drunken sailor
