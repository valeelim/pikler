Circa Survive     circa survive is a rock band from philadelphia , pennsylvania . they formed in 2004. the band has vocalist anthony green . he was a singer of saosin and members of the now-defunct `` this day forward '' . their first album , `` juturna '' , was released on april 19 , 2005 by equal vision records , which got mixed reviews . however their second album , `` on letting go '' , released on may 29 , 2007 , garnered big reviewer acclaim . their upcoming album , `` blue sky noise '' , will be released on april 20 , 2010 on atlantic records as the band has finished their term with equal vision records .
