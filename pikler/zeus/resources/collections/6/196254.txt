Semi-postal     a semi-postal stamp or semipostal stamp is a philatelic term for a postage stamp issued to raising money for some purpose , usually for charity . semi-postals have an additional monetary value ( `` surcharge '' ) , devoted to that specific non-postal , charitable purpose and separated from the official postal value with a `` + '' sign . related pages . - audrey hepburn ( semi-postal ) - label ( philately )
