Luigi's Mansion    luigi 's mansion luigi 's mansion is a 2001 video game released for the nintendo gamecube . it was developed , created , and released by nintendo , a famous japanese video game company . this game has a sequel for the nintendo 3ds : `` '' , which supports tilt motion . it was released in north america on march 24 , 2013. luigi has won a mansion in a contest which he did not enter , and he requests that mario checks the mansion first . when luigi realizes that mario has been long absent , he heads off to the mansion in search of him . in contrast to the mansion shown on his flyer , luigi notices that it is old , haunted , and inhabited by multiple ghosts . around the start of his adventure , luigi meets professor e. gadd , who gives him the poltergust 3000 , a vacuum cleaner that is capable of capturing ghosts .
