Super League Greece     super league greece ( greek : ελληνική σούπερ λιγκ ) is the top division football league in greece . the current version was formed on 16 july 2006 and replaced the `` alpha ethniki '' . the first championship was won by aris thessaloniki . the club which has won the most championships is olympiacos with 43 titles , next is panathinaikos with 20 titles and third is aek athens with 11 titles . the league currently has 16 teams . other websites . - superleague greece official website - superleague statistics - football scores for greek football leagues
