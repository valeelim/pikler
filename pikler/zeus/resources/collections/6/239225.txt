Peshitta     the peshitta ( classical syriac for `` simple , common , straight , vulgate '' ) is the standard version of the syriac bible . the old testament of the peshitta was translated from the hebrew , probably in the 2nd century . the new testament of the peshitta , which originally excluded certain disputed books ( 2 peter , 2 john , 3 john , jude , revelation ) , had become the standard by the early 5th century .
