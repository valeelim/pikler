Black metaltail     the black metaltail ( `` metallura phoebe '' ) is a species of hummingbird . it can be found in bolivia and peru . it lives in subtropical or tropical moist montanes and subtropical or tropical high-altitude shrubland . references . - birdlife international 2004. metallura phoebe . 2006 iucn red list of threatened species . downloaded on 10 july 2007 .
