Wallace and Gromit     wallace and gromit are the main characters in a series of animations by aardman animations . wallace is an absent-minded inventor while gromit is his faithful dog . they have starred in several short movies for bbc television in the uk , and one full length feature movie . `` wallace & gromit : the curse of the were-rabbit '' is the longest claymation movie ever made . it was created in five years . it stars peter sallis as wallace .
