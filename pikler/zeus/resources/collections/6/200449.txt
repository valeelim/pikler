P.A.O.K. Thessaloniki F.C.    p.a.o.k . thessaloniki f.c . p.a.o.k . thessaloniki f.c . ( or simply `` paok '' ) is a football club which plays in greece . paok is based in thessaloniki . its colours of club are the black and the white . its emblem is an eagle with two heads . paok is the fourth most successful greek club , as it has won two greek championships and four greek cups .
