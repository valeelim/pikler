Brooke Hogan     brooke hogan ( born may 5 , 1988 in tampa , florida ) is an american singer , actress and model who currently has her own show on vh1 and muchmore called `` brooke knows best '' it is a spin-off of the show her parents were on called `` hogan knows best '' . her father is semi-retired professional wrestler hulk hogan . she released her debut album in 2006 entitled `` undiscovered '' .
