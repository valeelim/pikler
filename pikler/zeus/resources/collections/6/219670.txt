Ongar railway station     ongar tube station is a former london underground station in the town of chipping ongar , essex . until its closure in 1994 , it was the easternmost point of the central line , and from 1961 until its closure in 1994 it held the distinction of being the london underground station farthest from central london . other websites . - london transport museum photographic archive - epping ongar railway 's official website - london 's abandoned tube stations - ongar station - london 's abandoned tube stations - epping to ongar branch
