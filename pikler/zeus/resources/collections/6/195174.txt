Groundwater     groundwater ( or ground water ) is water located below the ground surface . groundwater starts as rain ( or other precipitation ) , and flows under ground for long periods of time through aquifers . groundwater flow returns to the surface again through springs and rivers . groundwater is an important part of the water cycle . people drink groundwater with water wells . they drill wells deep below the surface . then , in the aquifer , the groundwater stored inside will be pumped up and used .
