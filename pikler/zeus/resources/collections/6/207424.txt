Sport coat     a sport coat ( or sports coat ) is an article of clothing worn by men as part of business or formal attire . it is similar to a jacket or suit coat , has long sleeves , and is often made of cotton or wool . sport coats come in many colors and patterns , but the most common are black , navy blue , brown , grey , and tweed , a special type of grey pattern . if a sport coat is cut a slightly different way , it is called a blazer . the term sport coat came from the fact that they were originally worn when hunting . also see . - blouson
