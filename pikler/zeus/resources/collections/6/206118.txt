Mawlid an-Nabi     mawlid an-nabi is a special day in islamic calendar to remember the birth of prophet muhammad . the day falls on the 12th day of rabiul awal of islamic lunar calendar every year . every year , muslims around the world celebrate the day by large street processions , distributing food and charity and talks and sermons about the life of the prophet . the day is recognised as a public holiday in most muslim countries with the exception of saudi arabia .
