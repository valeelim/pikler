Daisy Duck     daisy duck is a character of the duck produced by walt disney . daisy duck is the girlfriend of donald duck . she is characterized by her big ribbon on her head . her best friend is minnie mouse . minnie mouse is mickey mouse 's girlfriend . daisy made her official debut ( an earlier love interest for donald called donna was introduced in the 1937 short 'don donald ' ) in the 1940 short mr. duck steps out . she likes shopping and she is a showboat . daisy duck is popular among women though she has made relatively few appearances in media compared to mickey mouse and donald duck.. related pages . - walt disney pictures - list of disney movies
