Fox Mulder     fox mulder is one of the main characters in the science-fiction show the x-files . he is an fbi agent working in the section called the x-files . he works together with his partner dana scully to figure out answers to questions in the x-files . the questions he looks at are usually about aliens or the paranormal . later , mulder begins to date scully in season 7 of the show . mulder has a sister named samantha mulder . he spends a lot of time looking for her . later in the show , mulder finds out that his sister was kidnapped by aliens and the government .
