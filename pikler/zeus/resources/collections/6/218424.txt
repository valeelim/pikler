Ambrose Dundas Flux Dundas     sir ambrose dundas flux dundas kcie , csi ( b.1899-d.1973 ) was the second chief commissioner of balochistan ( pakistan ) from 4 october 1947 - 8 april 1948 , and the second governor of the north west frontier province from 9 april 1948 - 16 july 1949. dundas was lieutenant governor of the isle of man from 1952 to 1959 .
