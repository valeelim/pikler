Fork-tailed woodnymph     the fork-tailed woodnymph ( `` thalurania furcata '' ) is a species of hummingbird . it is found in argentina , bolivia , brazil , colombia , ecuador , french guiana , guyana , paraguay , peru , suriname , trinidad and tobago , and venezuela . its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical moist lowland forests , subtropical or tropical moist montane forests , and degraded former forests .
