Baloch people     the baloch ( بلوچ ) ( other spellings : baluch , balouch , bloach , balooch , balush , balosh , baloosh , baloush ) live in the part of balochistan in the southeast corner of the iranian plateau in southwest asia , including parts of eastern iran , afghanistan , and pakistan . the baloch speak balochi language , an iranian language . they mainly live in mountainous places of the iranian plateau in iran , pakistan and parts of afghanistan .
