Balibo (movie)    balibo ( movie ) balibo is an australian movie directed by robert connolly and released in 2009. it starred australian actor anthony lapaglia who also was the film 's producer . it based on the true story of 5 australian news reporters working for australian tv during the indonesian invasion of east timor in 1975. they were murdered by the indonesian army in the small village of balibo . the indonesian national armed forces did not want the movie shown in indonesia , with a spokesman saying the movie could harm indonesia 's relations with east timor and australia . the movie was finally forbidden in indonesia by the film censorship agency . foreign minister marty natalegawa said the ban was to avoid a negative image of indonesia . the indonesian national armed forces supports the ban , with a spokesman saying the official version of the event is that the journalists were killed in a crossfire , and not murdered by indonesian troops .
