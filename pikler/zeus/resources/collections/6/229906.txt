La Leyenda     la leyenda ( in english : `` the legend '' ) is a `` greatest hits '' album by mexican-american singer selena . `` la leyenda '' was released on march 9 , 2010 as four different box-sets . q-productions and emi latin teamed up and promoted `` la leyenda '' around the world , `` la leyenda '' had personal messages from selena 's family , friends , and fans across the world . tracks on the album . four disc edition . - disc 1 : cumbias and pop 1. amor prohibido 1. baila esta cumbia 1. la caracha 1. besitos 1. como la flor 1. el chico del apartmento 512 1. la llamada 1. bidi bidi bom bom 1. quiero ser 1. si una vez 1. no debes jugar 1. techno cumbia 1. costumbres 1. fotos y recuredos 1. no quiero saber 1. enamorada de ti 1. tu robaste mi corazon 1. ya no 1. no te vayas 1. amame 1. aunque no salga el sol 1. puede ser ( duet with nando `` quero '' dominguez ) 1. sukiyaki 1. buenos amigos ( duet with alavo torres ) - disc 2 : tejano and rancheras 1. contigo quiero estar 1. amame , quiereme 1. ya ves 1. mentiras 1. la traclera
