Crown Prince Sado     prince sado of joseon or prince jang heon of joseon ( or , january 1735 - may 1762 ) was a crown prince of joseon . he was the second son of king yeongjo , and the father of king jeongjo of joseon . after the death of his half brother prince hyojang , he became heir to the throne . because of his mental illness and strange behaviour , his father had him locked in a rice chest and starved to death . references . - http : //100.naver.com/100.nhn ? docid=133415
