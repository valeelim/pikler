Baskin-Robbins     baskin-robbins is a global chain of ice cream parlors . it has franchises in more than 30 countries . out of 5800 locations , 2800 are based in the united states . it was founded in 1953 by the merger of two separate ice cream ventures of burt baskin and irv robbins , who were brothers-in-law . before the merger , the two separate ice cream ventures were called burt ’ s ice cream shop and snowbird ice cream . snowbird ice cream had 21 flavors at the time , which was praised as novel and fresh . after the two ventures were combined into baskin-robbins , which would later become a world-famous ice cream parlor , pushed the slogan of ’ 31 flavors. ’ baskin-robbins was creative in its approach to put forward the sampling system—letting people taste the different flavors of ice cream with a pink spoon until they find the flavor that they want . also , baskin-robbins was the first to introduce the ice cream cake to the public . the original flavors when baskin-robbins first opened in 1945 were :
