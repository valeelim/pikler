James Haggarty     james haggarty ( born april 14 , 1914 in port arthur , ontario died march 8 , 1998 ) was a canadian ice hockey player who competed in the 1936 winter olympics . in 1936 he was a member of the canadian ice hockey team , which won the silver medal . he later played 5 games in the national hockey league for the montreal canadiens . other websites . - james haggarty 's profile at hockey reference.com - james haggarty 's profile at hockey reference.com - james haggarty 's profile at sports reference.com
