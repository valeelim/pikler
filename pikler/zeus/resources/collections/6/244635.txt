Purchase     purchasing is the buying of goods or services . an item that has been bought is called a purchase . the opposite of a purchase is a sale . in common usage , the shorter word `` buy '' is typically used when shopping , rather than the word `` purchase '' . however , the word has been used in many major historical terms , including : the word `` purchase '' is often used in contracts with `` acquisition '' , as in the term `` acquisitions and mergers '' ( a & m ) in legal agreements made between large companies . those contracts typically state than a company was `` purchased '' by another company .
