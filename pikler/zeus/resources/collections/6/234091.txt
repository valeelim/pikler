Gavin de Beer     sir gavin rylands de beer frs ( 1899â€“1972 ) was a british evolutionary embryologist . he was director of the london natural history museum , president of the linnean society , and received the royal society 's darwin medal for his studies on evolution . in the first world war he served in the grenadier guards . later , he taught at oxford university zoology department . in 1938 , he was made reader in embryology at university college london . during the second world war de beer again served with the grenadier guards reaching the rank of temporary lieutenant colonel . he worked in military intelligence , propaganda and psychological warfare . also during the war , in 1940 , he was elected fellow of the royal society . in 1945 , de beer became professor of zoology and was , from 1946 to 1949 , president of the linnean society . then he was director of the british museum ( natural history ) from 1950 until his retirement in 1960. he was knighted in 1954 , and awarded the darwin medal of the royal society in 1957 .
