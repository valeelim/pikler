Cuba Gooding, Jr.    cuba gooding , jr. cuba gooding , jr. ( born january 2 , 1968 ) is an american actor . he is best known for his role in movie `` jerry maguire '' with tom cruise , for which gooding won an academy award . he was also in the movies `` boat trip '' , and `` snow dogs '' . gooding was born in new york city . his father is singer cuba gooding , sr.. the family moved to los angeles , california in 1972. gooding has been a born-again christian since he was 13 .
