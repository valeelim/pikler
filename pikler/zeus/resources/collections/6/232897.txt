Mackerel     mackerel is a common name given to a number of species of fish . most mackerel are from the scombridae family . they are pelagic fish , found in both temperate and tropical seas . most live along the coast or offshore in the oceanic environment . mackerel typically have vertical stripes on their backs and deeply forked tails . they are superb swimmers , but are often eaten by larger predators such as cod , sharks and dolphins . mackerel is an oily fish with rich of omega-3 . the flesh of mackerel spoils easily , especially in the tropics , and can cause food poisoning . so , it should be eaten on the day of capture , unless pickled in salt , or refrigerated .
