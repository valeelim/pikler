Misa Telefoni Retzlaff     misa telefoni retzlaff ( born hermann theodor retzlaff , 21 may 1952 ) is the deputy prime minister of samoa . he is of german-samoan descent . retzlaff went to school at king 's college in auckland , new zealand . he studied law at the university of auckland . then he went to samoa to practice law . he was made attorney-general of samoa in 1986. in february 2011 telefoni said he was retiring from politics at the 2011 election .
