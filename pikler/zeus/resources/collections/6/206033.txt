The Sandman (book)    the sandman ( book ) the sandman is a children 's book written by ralph fletcher with drawings by richard cowdrey . it was first published in 2008 by henry holt and company . tor is an man one inch tall who can not fall asleep no matter what he tries . he finds a dragon scale while walking in the woods . he learns that the powder made by grinding the scale help a person to sleep . he then travels the sand into the eyes of children who are having trouble sleeping . outside websites . - ralph fletcher 's website - richard cowdrey 's website
