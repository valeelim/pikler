Chopped (TV series)    chopped ( tv series ) chopped is a reality-themed cooking competition series that airs on food network . four chefs compete in three three rounds or segments on the show : appetizer , entree and dessert . after each round , a chef is then eliminated until there is one winner at the end . the winner receives $ 10,000 . in each round the chefs are given three to five food items that must be part of their dish . there is a large pantry and refrigerator that the chefs can use in addition to the required items . in each round the chefs are given a limited time to complete their dish . the appetizer part lasts twenty minutes . the entree and dessert parts last thirty minutes . three guest chefs judge each round .
