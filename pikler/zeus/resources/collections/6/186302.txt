All Saints DLR station     all saints dlr station is a station on the docklands light railway ( dlr ) in east london . it is named after the neaby all saints , poplar , a church dating from 1821-3. the station entrance is on the east india dock road , opposite chrisp street market . references . - listed as all saints lrt station other websites . - docklands light railway website - all saints station page - map of poplar , 1885 . - photograph of the platform at all saints
