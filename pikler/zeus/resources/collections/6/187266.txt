Ron Clark     ron clark is an american teacher who has worked with disadvantaged students in rural north carolina and harlem , new york city . he is known for his books and multiple teaching awards . clark 's latest project is the ron clark academy , a private non-profit school in atlanta , georgia where students follow a unique curriculum . the school also gives students opportunities for international travel and offers training workshops for teachers to learn more about clark 's teaching methods . clark made a set of rules called the esential55 that he use to teach his students .
