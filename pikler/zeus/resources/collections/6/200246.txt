Douglas C-47 Skytrain     the c-47 skytrain was an american cargo aircraft that could carry up to 3.5 tons . it was the military version of the dc-3 . the c-47 was most notably used during world war ii by the allied to move troops , supplies and the wounded . during the berlin airlift in the cold war c-47 planes carried food and other supplies to west berlin , as all other methods of transport were blocked .
