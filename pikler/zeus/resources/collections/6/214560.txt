Aldwych tramway station     aldwych tramway station was a tram stop that was underneath kingsway . the tramway station should not to be confused with aldwych tube station . the stop is part of the kingsway tramway subway . services running from angel islington to aldwych started on 24 february 1906. services from highbury & islington station to tower bridge and kennington gate started on 10 april 1908. the station closed on 6 april 1952. the strand underpass , which opened in 1964 , comes to surface level part way through the former location of the aldwych tramway station . no sign of the former station is visible on the surface or from the road tunnel however . other websites . - includes an illustrated account of a visit to the site in november 2003 - lcc tramways 1914 map showing routes south of aldwych station
