Selsun Blue     selsun blue is a hair shampoo . it is made to help with dandruff . it was first made by abbott laboratories . then the brand was bought in chattem in 2002. chattem was bought by sanofi-aventis in 2010. selsun blue is sold through mass merchandisers , drug and food retailers including walmart , walgreens , cvs , target , and rite aid . advertising slogans . - `` never wear black without the blue -- selsun blue '' . - `` got a clue ? get the blue '' . - `` it tingles , so i know it 's working '' .
