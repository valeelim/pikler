Pork pie     pork pies are a type of meat pie and are a common british food . they are made of chopped pork and pork jelly sealed in a hot water crust pastry , and are most of the time eaten cold . they are most of the time eaten at picnics with ketchup or mustard . other websites . - the pork pie appreciation society - photos showing the steps in constructing a rather large pork pie - `` pork pie '' , in what am i eating ? a food dictionary
