Kalat (Pakistan)    kalat ( pakistan ) qalat or kalat ( ) is a historical town in kalat district , balochistan , pakistan . qalat is the capital of `` qalat district '' and is known locally as qalat-e baluch . qalat , formerly qilat , is roughly in the center of pakistani balochistan , south and slightly west of the provincial capital quetta . it was the capital of the qalat khanate . the brahui population is almost completely muslim .
