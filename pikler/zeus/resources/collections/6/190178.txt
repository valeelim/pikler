V-2     the v-2 rocket ( ) was the world 's first ballistic missile and first human object to fly in space . all modern rockets are based on the v2 design . the first successful launch was from peenemünde on 3 october , 1942 , reaching a height of 192km . the v2 was designed by nazis to bomb london , antwerp and other european cities . it travelled at four times the speed of sound so was impossible to shoot down . the first v-2 used as a weapon exploded in paris on 8 september 1944 , with a second rocket exploding in london later that day . over 3,000 v-2s were used by the german wehrmacht against allied targets in world war ii , resulting in the death of an estimated 7,250 military personnel and civilians . the usa used captured v-2 rockets as the start of its space and missile program . they were helped by the team of german rocket scientists from peenemünde , led by wernher von braun , who had surrendered to the us at the end of world war two . the first us assembled v-2 , made from parts captured in germany , was launched from white sands , new mexico , in april 1946.. there were 66 v-2 rocket flights , the last on october 29 , 1951 .
