Night     night or nighttime is the time when the sun is not in the sky . night happens after dusk and before dawn . the opposite of night is day . the start and end times of night are different in different geographic locations . it varies on season , latitude , longitude and timezone . in other time zones , the night occurs at different times . this is because the sun shines on different parts of the earth at different times . during a solar eclipse it is also partially night because the sun is blocked out by the moon . in popular culture , night is often thought of as a symbol of evil , possibly because of the fear of the dark or possibly because of the lack of visibility . related pages . - light pollution - polar night
