Geordi La Forge     geordi la forge is a character in the series `` '' . he is played by levar burton . geordi was born blind and must wear a visor to help him see . geordi was born on february 16 , 2335. his parents were silva la forge and edward m. la forge . he also had a sister . he went to the zefram cochrane high school . he later went to starfleet academy from 2353 to 2357. he was assigned as an ensign in the uss `` victory '' .
