Hornwort     hornworts are a group of bryophytes , or non-vascular plants . the name refers to the elongated horn-like structure , which is the sporophyte . the flattened , green plant body of a hornwort is the gametophyte plant . like all bryophytes , the hornworts have alternation of generations in which the green plant body is a haploid gametophyte , and the sporophyte is the diploid fruiting body . hornworts may be found world-wide , growing where it is damp or humid . some species grow in large numbers as tiny weeds in the soil of gardens and cultivated fields . large tropical species of `` dendroceros '' may be found growing on tree bark . the taxonomy of bryophytes is being debated . some think the hornworts should be placed in a separate division , the anthocerotophyta . related pages . - `` horneophyton '' is a fossil plant similar to hornworts .
