The Pirate Bay     the pirate bay ( shortname : tpb ) is a swedish website that indexes bit torrent files . it was created by gottfrid svartholm , fredrik neij and peter sunde . the pirate bay is ranked as the 85th most popular website by alexa . the pirate bay had to face many lawsuits and a raid in relation to copyright violations . it was shut down on december 9 , 2014 by swedish police , and came back online on january 31 , 2015. also , it may risk your pc to get infected by a virus .
