Swat (princely state)    swat ( princely state ) swat ( ) was a princely state and is now part of pakistan . swat was part of the mughal empire ruled by local gujar rulers known as akhoond or akhwand . from 1926 until 1947 , swat was a princely state in british raj , and in 1947 it acceded to pakistan . it lay to the north of the modern khyber pakhtunkhwa of pakistan until it was dissolved in 1969. it incorporated the current states of swat , buner and shangla .
