Demetri Martin     demetri martin ( born may 25 , 1973 ) is a greek-american comedian , actor , artist , musician , writer and humorist . he was born in new york city and grew up in toms river , new jersey . martin is best known for his work as a stand-up comedian and as a contributor on `` the daily show '' . he hosted his own show on comedy central called `` important things with demetri martin '' .
