Rheumatic fever     rheumatic fever is an inflammatory disease that happens in children and young adults as a result of becoming infected by group a streptococci bacteria . the first attack usually happens between the ages of 5 and 15. it affects the heart , skin , joints and the central nervous system . it is most common in the middle east , eastern europe , south america and the far east and is rare in western europe and north america . treatment includes resting in bed and antibiotics . wolfgang mozart , the famous composer famously died of rheumatic fever .
