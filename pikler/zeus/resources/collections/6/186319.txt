Kurt Busch     kurt thomas busch ( born august 4 , 1978 ) is a nascar sprint cup race car driver from las vegas , nevada . he drives the no . 41 chevrolet for stewart haas racing . busch has raced for roush fenway racing , penske racing , and phoenix racing . he has won one nascar sprint cup championship , which he won in 2004. in 2014 , busch raced in the indianapolis 500 , where he finished in sixth place . the indianapolis 500 was kurt busch 's first try at racing in the indycar series .
