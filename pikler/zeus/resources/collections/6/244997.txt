Mo Williams     maurice `` mo '' williams is currently a professional basketball that plays for the los angeles clippers . he is a point guard , who wears the jersey number # 2. he was born in jackson , mississippi . he was born on december 19 , 1982. he formerly played for the utah jazz from 2003 to 04 , the milwaukee bucks from 2004 to 08 and the cleveland cavaliers from 2008 to 2011 .
