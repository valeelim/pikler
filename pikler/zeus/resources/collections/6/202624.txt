Malky Mackay     malcolm george `` malky '' mackay ( born 19 february 1972 , in bellshill ) is a scottish football manager and former player . he is the manager of cardiff city . mackay began his playing career in scottish football , with queen 's park and celtic . he joined english side norwich city in 1998. he also played in the premier league with norwich , west ham and finally watford . he retired as a player in 2008 .
