Promoucaes     promoucaes , promaucaes , promaucas or purumaucas ( : wild people ) ; were a pre-columbian mapuche tribe that lived in chile , from the south of the river of maipo in santiago , chile and the river of itata , ( tribes at the north were called `` quillotanes '' and `` mapochoes '' by the spanish ) . they were speakers of mapudungun like the moluche and picunche tribes .
