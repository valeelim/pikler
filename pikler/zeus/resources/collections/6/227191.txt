Darrell Waltrip     darrell lee waltrip ( born february 5 , 1947 ) is a retired american racecar driver . waltrip won three nascar sprint cup championships and 84 races , including the 1989 daytona 500. he is now part of the broadcast team for fox sports . waltrips younger brother , michael waltrip owns and races for his own team in nascar . in june of 2011 waltrip was given a spot in the nascar hall of fame . he is a part of the hall of fame class of 2012 .
