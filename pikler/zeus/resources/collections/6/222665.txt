Robert J. Lang     robert j. lang ( born may 4 , 1961 ) is an american physicist known for his contributions to the art of origami . lang started his career in origami forty years ago , and many think he is now one of the world 's best paperfolders . he travels often , giving lectures about how origami is related to other topics , like science , mathematics , and technology . lang is also the author of nine books , and has written articles in several origami magazines . he has found many uses of origami in real life situations , like airbag design and an expandable space telescope . before being a professional origamist , lang had many different jobs . he was a physicist , engineer , and research and development manager in chemistry . lang has helped to write 80 publications , and has over 50 patents . lang was born on may 4th , 1961 in dayton , ohio , but he grew up outside atlanta , georgia . he was first introduced to origami when he was six years old . lang was very good at math and math class bored him , so his teacher gave him an origami book to keep him entertained . he quickly folded the models in the book , and then folded models in many other books . in his early teens , lang began folding his own models , and he drew diagrams on how to make them .
