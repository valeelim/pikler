Stratus cloud     stratus clouds are low altitude gray clouds that make a flat base . the name comes from the latin word `` stratus '' , which means to `` stretch '' or `` extend . '' you can see stratus clouds as thick cloud blankets near the sea . they are sometimes called `` high fogs '' . light rain and drizzle often fall from stratus clouds . when rain falls from stratus clouds they are mostly called nimbostratus .
