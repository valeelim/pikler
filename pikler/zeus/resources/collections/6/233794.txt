F-flat major     f-flat major is a major scale based on f-flat . its key signature has eight flats . its relative minor is d-flat minor , and its parallel minor is f-flat minor , usually replaced by e minor . part of richard strauss ' `` metamorphosen '' uses f flat major , which one commentator has called `` a bitter enharmonic parody '' of the earlier manifestations of e major in the piece . to make reading and writing music easier , f-flat major is usually written as its enharmonic equivalent of e major .
