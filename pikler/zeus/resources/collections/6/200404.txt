Incubus (band)    incubus ( band ) incubus is an american alternative rock band . they are from california . the band started in 1991. they have had many different sounds since their first full-length album `` fungus amongus '' to the current studio album `` light grenades '' . band members who have been in incubus since the band started include brandon boyd , mike einziger , and jose pasillas . they have become very successful . since their album `` make yourself '' , most of their albums have been selling gold or platinum . `` make yourself '' included their hit single `` drive .
