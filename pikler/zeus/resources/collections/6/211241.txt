Gonaïves     gonaïves ( kréyòl : `` gonayiv '' ) is a port city in western haiti . it is the capital city of the artibonite `` département '' . the city is on the coast of the `` gonaïve '' bay , on the northeast of the gulf of gonâve , about 171 km north of the haitian capital port-au-prince . it is also the chief town of an `` arrondissement '' ( a part of a department ) with the same name . the `` arrondissement '' has three `` communes '' ( a `` commune '' is like a municipality ) : gonaïves , ennery and estère . in 1802 , the battle of `` ravine-à-couleuvres '' , an important battle of the haitian revolution , was fought near gonaïves . gonaïves is also known as city of independence because it was there that jean-jacques dessalines declared haiti independent from france on 1 january 1804. floods produced by rains from tropical storms and hurricanes devastated the city and many people died in september 2004 ( hurricane jeanne ) , and again four years later , in august-september 2008 ( hurricane hanna ) . gonaïves is an important commercial centre . the agricultural products of the region ( coffee , cotton , sugar and bananas ) are sent to other countries and other parts of haiti from the harbor of the city .
