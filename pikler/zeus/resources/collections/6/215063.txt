Asclepias cordifolia     heart-leaf milkweed ( `` asclepias cardifolia '' ) is a species of milkweed plant . it is also known as purple milkweed ( the species `` asclepias purpurascens '' is also known as purple milkweed ) . it belongs in the subfamily asclepiadoideae . it is native to the western united states . heart-leaf milkweed is found in woodlands , rocky slopes , and lava flows . other websites . - `` asclepias cordifolia '' at usda plants database - `` asclepias cordifolia '' at treatment from the jepson manual - `` asclepias cordifolia '' at plantaxa
