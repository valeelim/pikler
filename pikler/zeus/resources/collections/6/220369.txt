Interstate 76 (east)    interstate 76 ( east ) interstate 76 is an interstate highway in the united states . it goes from westfield center , ohio east to bellmawr , new jersey . the route is long . just west of youngstown , i-76 joins the ohio turnpike and heads around the south side of youngstown . in pennsylvania , i-76 runs across most of the state on the pennsylvania turnpike . it passes near pittsburgh and harrisburg before leaving the turnpike to enter philadelphia . it is called the schuylkill expressway in philadelphia . it crossing the walt whitman bridge into new jersey . after interstate 76 reaches its eastern terminus , the freeway continues as route 42 and the atlantic city expressway to atlantic city .
