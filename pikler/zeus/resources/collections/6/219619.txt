Lagoon     a lagoon is a body of salt water partly separated from the sea . there is usually some sort of barrier which separates and protects the lagoon . this may be a pebble or shingle beach , sand bank , or perhaps a coral reef surrounding an island ( that 's called an atoll . ) `` lagoon '' refers both to coastal lagoons , and the lagoons in atolls , formed by the growth of coral reefs on slowly sinking central islands .
