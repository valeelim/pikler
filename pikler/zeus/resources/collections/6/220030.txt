Filip Vandewal     filip vandewal ( 31 may 1981 ) is a belgian cinematographer . filip graduated from the brussels movie school rits in 2003. movies he has worked on have been screened at several international movie festivals including cannes film festival and clermont-ferrand . he was the cinematographer on `` de laatste zomer '' ( `` last summer '' ) , a movie directed by joost wijnant . filip now lives in los angeles . other websites . - filip vandewal website - filip vandewal at the internet movie database
