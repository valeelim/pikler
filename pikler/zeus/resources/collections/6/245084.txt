Kotka     kotka ( literally `` eagle '' ) is a town in kymenlaakso , finland . it is part of the kymenlaakso region in southern finland . the centre of kotka is located at kotkansaari island . the most famous sport team from kotka is ktp ( kotkan työväen palloilijat ) . the ktp-team plays football and basketball . other websites . - city of kotka – official website - map of kotka
