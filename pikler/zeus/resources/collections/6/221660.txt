Big Max     big max is a type of squash that can grow to be more than 100 pounds . they can also be in diameter if they are grown properly . they are commonly called pumpkins , but this is not entirely correct ; big max are instead `` squash-type pumpkins '' . they have a bright orange color , with fine-grained , yellow-orange flesh . the skin has deep lines in it and is slightly roughened , and can be up to thick . this makes big max squash good for keeping . the flesh is good for being canned and frozen . however , because they are so big , cooking with these squash is hard . they are said to not be as flavorful as smaller pumpkin varieties . the variety was , or created , for its large size . they have been grown as big as 300 pounds . individual fruits are round to slightly flattened . these squash are best planted between two and four weeks after the average last frost , and they become ripe 110 to 120 days after being planted . the plants need lots of room to grow well , and to get them very big , only one fruit should be grown on one vine . they are harvested before the first light frost , when the plant 's leaves die . although are different , seeds are often planted at deep , spaced apart .
