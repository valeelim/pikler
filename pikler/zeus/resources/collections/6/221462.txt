Lisa's Substitute    lisa 's substitute `` lisa 's substitute '' is the nineteenth episode of the simpsons second season . it first started on fox in the united states on april 25 , 1991. in this episode , lisa 's teacher miss hoover gets lyme disease , so substitute teacher `` mr. bergstrom '' takes over the class . lisa finds mr. bergstrom 's teaching methods inspiring and finds a whole new love for learning . when miss hoover returns to class , lisa is sad to lose her most positive adult role model . eventually , she knows that while mr. bergstrom was one of a kind , she can find role models in other people , like her father homer . meanwhile , bart runs for class president against `` martin .
