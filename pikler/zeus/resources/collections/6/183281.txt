Ahmed Ghulamali Chagla     ahmed ghulamali chagla ( 31 may 1902 - 5 february 1953 ) was the musician who wrote the score for the national anthem of pakistan , ( `` qaumi tarana '' ) in 1950. he was born in 1902 into a prominent karachi family . his father , ghulamali chagla was the third elected president of the municipality of karachi , serving from 1921 to 1922. ahmad chagla attended the sindh madrassat-ul-islam in karachi and took lessons in classical indian music in 1910 and western musical composition in 1914 .
