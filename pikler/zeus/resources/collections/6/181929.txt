Warren Street tube station     warren street tube station is a london underground station . it is on the charing cross branch of the northern line , between goodge street and euston , and the victoria line between oxford circus and euston . it is in travelcard zone 1 and is the nearest tube station to university college hospital , being opposite the newly opened main building . it is also very close to euston square tube station on the circle , hammersmith and city and metropolitan lines . other websites . - london transport museum photographic archive
