East India DLR station     east india is a station on docklands light railway ( dlr ) in east london . it takes its name from the nearby former east india docks of the port of london , where ships trading with the indian subcontinent used to dock . it is on the beckton branch of the dlr , and is in travelcard zones 2 and 3. it opened , with the beckton branch , on 28 march 1994. other websites . - docklands light railway website - east india station page
