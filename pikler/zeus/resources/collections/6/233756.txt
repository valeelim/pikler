Frédéric Bazille     jean frédéric bazille ( 6 december 1841 – 28 november 1870 ) was a french painter . he was born in montpellier . he was killed in action in the franco-prussian war . it was at the battle of beaune-la-rolande in france while leading his unit into a assault on the german position . he died at the age of 28 .
