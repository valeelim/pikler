Volkswagen Passat     volkswagen passat is a car produced by volkswagen in 1973. it entered its seventh generation in 2010. the volkswagen passat mk4 is the fourth generation of the volkswagen passat . the mk4 replaced the mk3 in 1993 , and was replaced by the mk5 in 1996. the volkswagen passat mk5 is the fifth generation of the volkswagen passat . the mk5 replaced the mk4 in 1996 , and was replaced by the mk6 in 2005. the volkswagen passat mk6 is the sixth generation of the volkswagen passat . the mk6 replaced the mk5 in 2005 , and was replaced by the mk7 in 2010. the volkswagen passat mk7 is the seventh generation of the volkswagen passat . the mk7 replaced the mk6 in 2010. this is the current generation of the volkswagen passat .
