Vâlcea County     vâlcea ( also spelt `` vîlcea '' ; ) is a county ( judeţ ) of romania . it can be found in the historical regions of oltenia and muntenia ( both regions are split apart by the olt river ) . the capital of vâlcea county is râmnicu vâlcea . vâlcea county has an area of 5,765 km² . vâlcea county has 2 municipalities , 9 towns and 78 communes . municipalities . - râmnicu vâlcea - capital city ; population : 130,581 - drăgăşani towns . - băbeni - bălceşti - băile govora - băile olăneşti - berbeşti - brezoi - călimăneşti - horezu - ocnele mari
