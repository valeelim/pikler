Ajana, Western Australia    ajana , western australia ajana is a small farming town in western australia . it is about 530 kilometres north east of perth . the town was officially started in 1915. in 2006 , there were 22 people living at ajana . the name of the town might be from an australian aboriginal word , `` ajano '' , used for the area nearby . it might also be from the word `` ngajna '' , meaning `` mine '' , as a place where stone axes could be made .
