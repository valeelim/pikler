Henryk Vogelfanger     henryk vogelfanger ( 1904 - 1990 ) , was a polish actor . he worked and lived in lviv until world war two when he joined polish underground army , armia krajowa . together with the legendary actor , radio personality kazimierz wajda , he was a star of the radio and theater comedy show `` szczepko and tonko '' . this was widely popular in poland and other countries with over six million regular listeners , 1932-1939. the show ended when world war two started . the team made three family movies , adventures of szczepko & tomko .
