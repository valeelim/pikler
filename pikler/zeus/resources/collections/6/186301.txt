Aldgate East tube station     aldgate east is a london underground station . it is between aldgate and whitechapel in the london borough of tower hamlets . the station is in travelcard zone 1. it is on the hammersmith & city line between liverpool street and whitechapel , and the district line between tower hill and whitechapel . related pages . - list of london underground stations other websites . - london 's abandoned tube stations - aldgate east
