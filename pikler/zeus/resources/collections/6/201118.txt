La Roux     la roux ( pronounced /laˈruː/ ) is a british electropop synth duo made up of singer-songwriter and keyboardist elly jackson , and synth player , co-writer and co-producer ben langmaid . their music is influenced by 1980s synthpop including yazoo , the human league , heaven 17 and blancmange . since 2008 , la roux has released a few singles . including bulletproof , which debuted at # 1 on the uk singles chart . they released a self-titled album in 2009. it on the grammy award for best electronic/dance album in 2011. their next album `` trouble in paradise '' is going to be released on july 7 2014. it will have a more acoustic sound .
