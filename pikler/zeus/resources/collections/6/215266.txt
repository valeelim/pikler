Centre Georges-Vézina     the centre georges-vézina , called the colisée de chicoutimi at first , is an arena in chicoutimi , quebec , canada that has space for 4,651 pepple ( including 3,683 seats ) . it where the chicoutimi saguenéens ice hockey team play . it is named for georges vézina , who played for the montreal canadiens as the goaltender and was from chicoutimi . the arena was built in 1949 and has an ice surface of 200 feet by 100 feet , which is what the olympics use .
