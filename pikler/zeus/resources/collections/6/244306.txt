Istanbul Technical University     istanbul technical university is a public college in istanbul , turkey . istanbul technical university was started in 1773. according to times higher education , itu is ranked 108th in the world and 1st in turkey in the technology and engineering . itu 's main campus is located on the maslak . istanbul 's subway system has a stop at itu 's maslak campus . itu has 12 faculties and 5 institutes . other websites . - istanbul technical university website , en - istanbul technical university website , tr - itu mustafa inan library - itu ninova e-learning center - ari technopolis - national high performance computing center , turkish language - itu eu center - itu dual degree programs - itu science center - itu radio , first university radio station in turkey - itu international office - itu photo gallery - panoramic 360 itu - arıyorum newspaper - itu24 online news service , operated by students - itu magazine - arı bulletin of itu - a|z itu journal , architecture magazine of the technical university
