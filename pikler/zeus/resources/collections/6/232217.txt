Bilby     the bilby ( also known as the rabbit-eared bandicoot ) is a rabbit-like marsupial . it lives in deserts , dry forests , dry grasslands , and dry shrubby areas in australia . the bilby 's pouch faces backwards . these big-eared , burrowing mammals are in danger of extinction . the bilby , like all bandicoots , is a nocturnal animal ( most active at night ) . digging with its strong , clawed feet , this solitary mammal excavates long , complex burrows . its underground dens are up to 5 feet ( 1.5 m ) long . at easter , chocolate easter bilbies are sold as an australian alternative to the easter bunny . money raised from the sale of the bilby shaped chocolate is used to fund protection for the bilby . the bilby likes to eat insects and fruit , they can dig their burrows up to 2 metres deep and 3 metres long .
