Hermann Muller     several people are named hermann muller or hermann müller : 1. hermann müller ( 1829–1883 ) , german botanist who investigated coevolution between bees and flowers . name has umlaut . 1. h.j . muller ( 1890–1967 ) , american geneticist who investigated mutations . name has no umlaut . 1. hermann müller ( spd ) foreign minister of germany , 21 june 1919–26 march 1920. spd party .
