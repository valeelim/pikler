Closer (movie)    closer ( movie ) closer is a 2004 american romantic drama movie set in london , england . the movie stars natalie portman , jude law , julia roberts and clive owen . it was directed by mike nichols . the movie was nominated for an academy award . portman and owen both won golden globe awards for their acting in this movie . the movie was written by patrick marber . it is based on his 1997 play of the same name .
