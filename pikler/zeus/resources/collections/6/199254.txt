Queen Elizabeth School     queen elizabeth school ( qes ) is a famous co-educational secondary school in hong kong . it is in sai yee street , mongkok , hong kong . it is 55 years old . there are over 1200 students and 50 teachers . every year , thousands of primary school students apply for a place to study there . on average , about 1000 interviewees fight for one place . queen elizabeth school is also an emi school , which means that all subjects are taught in english except chinese language and chinese history . there are many student organizations , such as form association ( fa ) , prefect , big brother and sister scheme ( bbss ) and camp warden association . clubs are available for all students . it is a team of students to maintain the order of school . each year , some outstanding students are selected by teachers and after qes has a board kinds of clubs . they are mainly divided into three columns , the subject-based , interest & personal development and services . apart from this , qes also has some special societies , such as the english/chinese society and the camp warden society . senior-form students can take up the posts of chairman by voting through the club members . the chairmen can invite other members to form a committee .
