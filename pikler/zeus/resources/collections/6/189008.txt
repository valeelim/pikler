Shmuel Yosef Agnon     shmuel yosef agnon ( july 17 , 1888 - february 17 , 1970 ) was an israeli writer and winner of the 1966 nobel prize in literature . he was one of the central figures of modern hebrew fiction . in hebrew , he is known by the acronym shai agnon . in english , his works are published under the name s. y. agnon . other websites . - nobel prize lecture
