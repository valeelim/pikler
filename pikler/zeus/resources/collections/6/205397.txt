IFK Norrköping     ifk norrköping is an association football club in the town of norrköping in sweden . the team has played several allsvenskan seasons . the club has won the swedish national championship in 1942–43 , 1944–45 , 1945–46 , 1946–47 , 1947–48 , 1951–52 , 1955–56 , 1956–57 , 1960 , 1962 , 1963 , 1989 and 2015. other websites . - ifk norrköping ( official website )
