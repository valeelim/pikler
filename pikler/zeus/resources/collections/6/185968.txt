Wayuu     the wayúu are a tribe of native people living in la guajira peninsula , between colombia and venezuela . the wayúu live in la guajira peninsula in colombia and venezuela . la guajira peninsula is on the caribbean sea coast . the two main rivers that flow through this area are ; the racheria river in colombia and el limon river in venezuela . the wayúu and other people that live there create ponds to keep water stored from the rain season .
