Bart vs. Thanksgiving     `` bart vs. thanksgiving '' is the seventh episode of the simpsons second season . it first started on the fox network in the united states on november 22 , 1990. in this episode , lisa makes a table centerpiece for the thanksgiving dinner , which bart destroys . after he is sent to his room by his parents , bart runs away from home and stays at a soup kitchen for homeless people . bart returns home eventually and climbs to the roof of the simpson family 's house , where he hears lisa crying . he says he 's sorry to her , and the family happily enjoys a meal of leftovers .
