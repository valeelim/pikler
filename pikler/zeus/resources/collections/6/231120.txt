Anna Bergendahl     anna henrietta bergendahl ( born december 11 , 1991 ) in katrineholm , sweden is a singer and former idol 2008 contestant . bergendahl won melodifestivalen 2010 and represented sweden in the eurovision song contest 2010 in oslo . her song this is my life is currently at number one on the swedish single chart . in idol 2008 she finished in fifth place .
