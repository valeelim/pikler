Little Rock Nine     the little rock nine was a group of african-american students who were in the little rock central high school , arkansas in 1957. this started the little rock crisis , in which the students were stopped from entering the racially segregated school by arkansas governor orval faubus . they attended after president eisenhower sent the united states army to let them in . this is thought to be one of the most important events in the african-american civil rights movement .
