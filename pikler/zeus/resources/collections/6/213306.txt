Gilbert Arenas     gilbert jay arenas jr. ( january 6 , 1982 ) is an cuban-american basketball player who last played for the shanghai sharks . he also played for the golden state warriors from 2001-2002 and the washington wizards from 2003-2010. he went to university of arizona for college . in 2010 , he got arrested for pulling a gun on javaris crittendon , a teammate . gilbert jay arenas , jr. is an american professional basketball player who last played for the shanghai sharks of the chinese basketball association .
