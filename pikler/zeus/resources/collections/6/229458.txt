Alameda County, California    alameda county , california alameda county is a county in the san francisco bay area in state of california , usa . according to the 2000 population census , 1,443,741 people live in alameda county . it is seventh biggest county in the state of california by population . its county seat and largest city is oakland . cities in alameda county . - alameda , founded in 1916 - albany , founded in 1908 , renamed albany in 1909 - berkeley , founded in 1878 - dublin , founded in 1982 - emeryville , founded in 1896 - fremont , founded in 1956 - hayward , founded in 1928 - livermore , founded in 1876 - newark , founded in 1955 - oakland , founded in 1852 - piedmont , founded in 1907 - pleasanton , founded in 1894 - san leandro , founded in 1872 - union city , founded in 1959
