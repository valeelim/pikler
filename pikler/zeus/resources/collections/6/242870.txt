Dacarbazine     dacarbizine is a type of drug used to fight cancer . it works by changing dna , which makes cancer cells grow more slowly or completely stop growing . it is used against metastatic melanoma . only about 15.3 % of people who use the drug respond to it . people who use this drug do not live any longer as a result . it has side effects , including less blood cells , damage to the liver . i can also sometimes make other types of cancer start growing .
