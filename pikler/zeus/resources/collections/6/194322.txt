Olt County     olt ( ) is a county ( judeţ ) of romania , in the historical regions of oltenia and muntenia . the capital of olt county is slatina . olt county has an area of 5,498 square kilometers . olt county has 2 municipalities , 6 towns and 104 communes . municipalities . - slatina - capital city ; population : 87,608 - caracal towns . - balş - corabia - drăgăneşti-olt - piatra olt - potcoava - scorniceşti
