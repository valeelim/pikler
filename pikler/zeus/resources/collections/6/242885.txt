Liceo Santa Cruz     santa cruz high school ( liceo santa cruz ) is a public high school in santa cruz , chile . on august 2 , 1963 the men 's high school of santa cruz was created . adriano díaz donated land where the high school was going to be constructed . however , a sport complex was constructed there instead . the high school opened on march 1 , 1964 in an old house . it had at least 170 students . many of these students already had finished their first or second grade in `` humanities '' in the pedro aguirre cerda high school , but it was not recognized officially . the first principal was osvaldo castillo , who hired teachers . almost all of the teachers had worked in the pedro aguirre high school . a logo and a standard were designed . both included palm trees , which were common in the city . the anthem of the high school was composed by raúl benavides .
