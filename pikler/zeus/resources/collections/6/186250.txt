Jack Roush     jack roush ( born april 19 , 1942 ) is the co-owner of roush fenway racing with his partner john w. henry . roush 's race team has won two nascar sprint cup championships with kurt busch and matt kenseth . during his whole career in nascar jack 's race team has 260 nascar wins , 168 poles , 1,104 top-five and 1,838 top-10 finishes
