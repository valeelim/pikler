The Way We Was     `` the way we was '' is the twelfth episode of `` the simpsons '' second season . it first started on the fox network in the united states on january 31 , 1991. in this episode , marge tells the story of how she and homer first met and fell in love . going back to 1974 , we see how homer falls in love with marge in high school and tries to get close to her by her as his french tutor . after several hours of verb conjugation , marge falls for homer too , only to become mad when he says that he is not a french student . marge rejects homer 's to the prom and goes with `` artie ziff '' . artie turns out to be a bad date and marge realizes that it is homer she really wants .
