Volksschule     a volkschule was part of an 18th-century system of state-supported primary schools established in the habsburg austrian empire . attendance was supposed to be compulsory . however , a 1781 census found that only one fourth of the school-age children attended . at the time , this was one of the few examples of state-supported schooling . the volkschule still exist in austria today , `` volksschule '' is the austrian equivalent to the german word `` grundschule '' and the swiss german word `` primarschule '' , i.e . primary school .
