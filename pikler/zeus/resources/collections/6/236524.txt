Understudy     in theater , an understudy is an actor or singer who learns the part of one of the main actors or singers in case they are ill. if there is an emergency and a main performer is unable to perform , the understudy will take their place . very often the understudy is a member of the chorus who might have had a small part in the performance anyway . sometimes an understudy is called a `` standby '' . a standby is usually not a member of the chorus or ensemble . they may be required to be present in the theatre during the performance , or part of it , in case of an emergency . sometimes an understudy will become famous when they unexpectedly have to perform instead of the usual lead person .
