Dwarf hamster     the dwarf hamsters represent a group of small hamsters in the genus phodopus . although they do not belong to this genus , the chinese hamster is often referred to as a dwarf hamster as well . although more limited in colour than the syrian hamster , dwarf hamsters are an alternative to pet owners who enjoy a smaller , more sociable hamster . the dwarf hamster was discovered by w.c. campbell in the early 1900 's .
