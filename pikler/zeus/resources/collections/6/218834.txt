Karisma Kapoor     karisma kapoor ( also spelled as karishma kapoor ) , is an indian actress . she was born on 25 june 1974 in mumbai , maharashtra , india . karishma kapoor acts in hindi language movies . she has won a filmfare best actress award . she also won a filmfare best supporting actress award and a national film award for best supporting actress . she is the sister of actress kareena kapoor . she is the daughter of bollywood actors randhir kapoor and babita . she is the first-cousin of actor ranbir kapoor . kapoor studied at the cathedral and john connon school in mumbai until grade six . she married sanjay in 2003 and they have two children .
