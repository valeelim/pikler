ColecoVision     the colecovision was a second generation home video game console known mostly for its expansion packs including the expansion module # 1 which gave it the largest library of games for its time by having compatibility with atari 2600 games . the system competed with other game systems popular at the time such as atari 2600 and the intellivision . the colecovision offered arcade-quality graphics and gaming style , and the means to expand the system 's basic hardware . river west brands currently owns the colecovision brand name . it was released in 1982 and was discontinued in 1985. related pages . - video game console
