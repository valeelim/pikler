El Clásico     el clásico ( , ) is a football match between two spanish rival football clubs , fc barcelona and real madrid , in the spanish football league la liga . it is one of the most famous football matches in the world . the rival match is also well-known due to its history . historically , catalonia was oppressed and disrespected by the dictatorship . thus , the only way the citizens could take revenge to the government was to defeat the football club representing madrid ( the capital city and the central government in spain ) . real madrid led in official matches 93 wins to 90 fc barcelona . currently , the records for both real madrid and fc barcelona are one of the top football clubs in the world . real madrid has won : 32 la liga titles , 19 copa espana titles , 2 uefa cup titles , 11 champions league titles and 3 intercontinental cups . fc barcelona has won : 24 la liga titles , 28 copa espana titles , 5 champions league titles and 4 cup winners cup titles .
