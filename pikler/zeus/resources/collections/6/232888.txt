Mac Pro     the mac pro is a workstation computer made by apple inc. it is part of the company 's apple macintosh group of computers . also , it is the fastest computer apple sells . it runs on intel xeon microprocessors , chips which help the computer think . it is like the power mac g5 , which is the computer it replaced . the mac pro looks the same and the parts inside the computer can be changed the same , too . there are two other computers sold by apple that are also called `` desktop '' computers . they are the imac and mac mini . the first mac pro was announced at the 2006 apple worldwide developers conference . the computer used the intel woodcrest microprocessor .
