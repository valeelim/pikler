Bjørn Skaare     bjørn skaare ( 29 october 1958 – 21 june 1989 ) was a professional ice hockey player who played one game for the detroit red wings of the nhl . he was the first norwegian player to play in a nhl game . he made his hockey debut in the norwegian hockey club `` furuset if '' when he was 15. he died on june 21 , 1989 when he was involved in a car crash in his hometown of oslo .
