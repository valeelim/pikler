Homer vs. Lisa and the 8th Commandment     `` homer vs. lisa and the 8th commandment '' is the thirteenth episode of the second season of `` the simpsons '' . it first aired on february 7 , 1991. in this episode , homer gets an illegal cable hook-up . despite their enjoyment of the new channels , lisa becomes that they are stealing cable . lisa protests by no longer watching television . meanwhile , homer invites his friends over to watch a boxing match , but lisa 's protest gets to him . he decides not to watch the fight and cuts the cable . it is one of a number of `` the simpsons '' episodes that have won an emmy .
