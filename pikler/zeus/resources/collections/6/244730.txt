Sindh Institute of Urology and Transplantation     the sindh institute of urology and transplantation ( 'siut ' ) is a largest dialysis & kidney transplant centre in pakistan . all services including dialysis and transplantation are provided free of charge . it was founded by dr. syed adeebul hasan rizvi and it is the country 's largest kidney disease center , as well as parkistan 's largest public sector health organisation . the institute started as a department of urology at the government-run civil hospital , karachi in 1970 and received autonomy in 1991. they perform 10-12 transplants every week , and in 2003 performed pakistan 's first liver transplant . in 2004 , a unit for child was opened . other websites . - officia website of siut
