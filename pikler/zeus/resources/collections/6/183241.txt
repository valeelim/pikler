Shanti Bahini     the shanti bahini ( , `` peace force '' ) was the name of the military wing of the parbatya chattagram jana sanghati samiti - the united people 's party of the chittagong hill tracts in bangladesh . it was formed in 1972 to preserve the rights of the tribal people in south-eastern bangladesh , and fought for many years against the central government . it abandoned militancy following the peace treaty signed by the government and the shamiti on december 2 , 1997. although some members opposed to the peace deal formed a dissident group .
