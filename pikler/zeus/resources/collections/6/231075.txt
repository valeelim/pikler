Heat lamp     a heat lamp is a lamp ( light ) with the main purpose of making heat . this is commonly infrared light but white light incandescent lightbulbs are also used . sometimes the glass of the light bulb is colored to cut out yellow and even higher frequency light ( to let the infrared light through , which has most of the heat ) . humans can use these lamps to keep warm , to warm a part of the body that is injured and needs gentle heat , etc . these lamps are also frequently used to keep baby chickens , piglets or other baby animals warm .
