Archway tube station     archway tube station is a london underground station in north london , underneath the archway tower , at the intersection of holloway road , highgate hill and junction road in the area known as archway . it is on the high barnet branch of the northern line , between highgate and tufnell park . it is in both travelcard zone 2 and zone 3. other websites . - london 's transport museum photographic archive - archway station on multimap
