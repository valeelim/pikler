John Birmingham     john birmingham ( august 7 , 1964 ) is an australian writer . his book , `` he died with a falafel in his hand '' was made into a movie in 2001. birmingham was born in liverpool , england in 1964. he came to australia with his family in 1970 and settled in ipswich , queensland . his first book written in 1994 , `` he died with a falafel in his hand '' , was about his experiences sharing a house with other people . his other books are `` the tasmanian babes fiasco '' ( 1997 ) , `` leviathan : the unauthorised biography of sydney '' ( 1999 ) , `` off one 's tits '' ( 2002 ) and `` without warning '' ( 2008 ) .
