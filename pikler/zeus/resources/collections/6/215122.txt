Asclepias speciosa     showy milkweed ( `` asclepias speciosa '' ) is a species of milkweed plant . it is also known as greek milkweed . it belongs in the subfamily asclepiadoideae . it is native to western north america . it blooms from may to july . this species is found in savannas , prairies , meadows , and fields . other websites . - `` asclepias speciosa '' at usda plants database - `` asclepias speciosa '' at treatment from the jepson manual - `` asclepias speciosa '' at evergreen native plant database
