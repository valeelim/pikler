Mälaren     lake mälaren is the third largest lake in sweden after lakes vänern and vättern . its surface area is 1,140 km² , its average depth is 12 meters and its greatest depth is 64 m. the lake drains , from southwest to northeast , into the baltic sea . the two largest islands in mälaren are selaön ( 91 km² ) and svartsjölandet ( 79 km² ) . the lake was previously known as løgrinn . the viking settlements birka on the island of björkö and hovgården on the neighbouring island adelsö have been a unesco world heritage site since 1993 , as has drottningholm palace on the island of lovön .
