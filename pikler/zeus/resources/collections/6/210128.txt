Barcelona Metro     the barcelona metro ( catalan and spanish : `` metro de barcelona '' ) , part of the public transportation system of barcelona , spain is an extensive network of electrified railways that run underground in central barcelona and above ground into the city 's suburbs . it has 10 lines , which together are 112 km ( 133 mi ) long . there are 152 stations . in 2015 , it will have 11 lines , 240 km and 296 stations .
