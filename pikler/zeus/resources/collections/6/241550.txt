Samanda     samanda ( amanda louise marchant and samantha joanne marchant ) , born 26 june 1988 , were a british female duo . they formed in the uk in 2007 after exiting big brother in 2nd place behind brian belo . they have had 1 single with barbie girl which charted at 26 in the united kingdom and 45 in the republic of ireland . they also had a top 200 hit with honey love . they are part of the sony bmg record label .
