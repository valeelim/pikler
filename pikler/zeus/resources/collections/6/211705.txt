Headphones     headphones are electronic audio devices that people wear over their ears . they let people listen to things on a walkman , mp3 player , mobile phone or computer . headphones come in many different sizes from big to small . they are also known as earphones , earbuds , stereophones , headsets or even cans . headphones typically uses small plug for connection . there is a risk of hearing loss if listening to headphones with a high volume for a long time .
