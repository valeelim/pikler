Agranulocytosis     agranulocytosis is a medical condition where there are very few granulocytes ( a kind of white blood cell ) in the blood . it is a type of severe and dangerous leukopenia ( a condition of very low number of white blood cells ) . if the agranulocytosis is because of a lack of neutrophils ( called neutropenia ) , it is especially bad . agranulocytosis is a medical word made up of three parts : - `` a '' , from the greek , meaning `` without '' - `` granulocyte '' , a particular kind of cell , is itself two words put together : - `` granul- '' meaning having small vessicles , and - `` cyte '' meaning cell - `` osis '' , from the greek , meaning `` condition '' or `` disorder ''
