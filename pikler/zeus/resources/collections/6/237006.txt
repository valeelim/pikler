Sam Bradford     samuel jacob bradford ( born november 8 , 1987 , in oklahoma city , oklahoma ) is an american football quarterback for the philadelphia eagles . he played for the oklahoma sooners college football team . he was drafted by the st. louis rams with the first overall pick in the 2010 national football league ( nfl ) draft . bradford was the 2011 nfl offensive rookie of the year . bradford is a cherokee .
