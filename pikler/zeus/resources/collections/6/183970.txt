Tippi Hedren     tippi hedren ( born nathalie kay hedren , january 19 , 1930 ) is an american actress . her paternal grandparents were swedish immigrants . her maternal ancestry is german and norwegian . she is best known for her performances in two movies directed by alfred hitchcock : `` the birds '' and `` marnie '' . she is the mother of actress melanie griffith and has acted in movies with her daughter .
