Alfa Romeo     alfa romeo automobiles s.p.a. is an italian car manufacturer created on june 24 , 1910 in milan . alfa romeo has been a part of the fiat group since 1986 , and since february 2007 a part of fiat group automobiles s.p.a. the company was originally known as a.l.f.a. , which stands for anonima lombarda fabbrica automobili ( english : lombard automobile factory , public company ) . models . - alfa romeo 145 : produced 1994-2000 - alfa romeo 155 : produced 1992-1997 - alfa romeo 159 : produced 2005- - alfa romeo 164 : produced 1987-1997
