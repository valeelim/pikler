Barkingside tube station     barkingside tube station is a london underground station on the central line . it has been in travelcard zone 4 since 2 january 2007. it is on the eastern edge of barkingside in the london borough of redbridge , at the end of a cul-de-sac called station road . the station is above ground and has two platforms - northbound and southbound . the station is located at . other websites . - london transport museum photographic archive
