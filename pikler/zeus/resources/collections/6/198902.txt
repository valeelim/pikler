R101     the r101 was an airship which crashed in 1930 in france , killing 48 people . english socialist government of the time had planned to build airships for the imperial route london-cairo-bombay-canada-australia-new york-london . it was an ambitious project that allowed save travel time by sea , also competed with germany and the graf zeppelin airship supremacy . r.100 series was planned in 1925 and projected 4 aerostats , the r.101 r.100 and would be built simultaneously to be the top seed of the project .
