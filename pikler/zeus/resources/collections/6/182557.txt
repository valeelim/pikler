Aage Niels Bohr     aage niels bohr ( 19 june 1922 , copenhagen - 8 september 2009 , copenhagen ) was a danish physicist and the fourth son of margrethe bohr and niels bohr . he won the nobel prize in 1975 for his works in physics : he discovered the connection of the collective motion and the single-motion particle in the atomic nucleus , and also for the development of the nuclear structure . he was a professor at university of copenhagen .
