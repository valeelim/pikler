First Leicester     first leicester is the trading name of leicester citybus ltd. , a bus company owned by the first group . it runs buses in the city of leicester england . in 2007 , a new depot opened on abbey lane replacing the former premises on abbey park road which go back to the company 's municipal heritage . this site is now derelict pending redevelopment as apartments . other websites . - first leicester website - first leicester network map - star trak - real-time bus information - the leicester transport heritage trust
