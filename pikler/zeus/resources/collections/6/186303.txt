Travelcard Zone 3     travelcard zone 3 is the third-outward zone of transport for london 's zonal system used for calculating co-ordinated intermodal travelcard fares within london . in the travelcard zonal system , london is split into nine ( approximately concentric ) zones for the purpose of determining the cost of a travelcard . the zone is also used for pricing london underground and docklands light railway single journeys , but not journeys undertaken entirely by bus or tram , for which a city-wide flat fare applies .
