University of Notre Dame     the university of notre dame ( or simply notre dame ) is a private catholic university near south bend , indiana . it was founded in 1842 by father edward sorin of the congregation of holy cross . sorin was also the school 's first president . in the beginning , it was an all-male school . in 1972 females were admitted . the university has five colleges and one professional school . notre dame offers master 's degrees and doctoral degrees . the university 's undergraduates live on campus in single-gendered residence halls . alumni are located around the world . the school 's athletic teams are known as the fighting irish . related pages . - notre dame stadium other websites . - university of notre dame - notre dame fighting irish , the official athletic site - campus map
