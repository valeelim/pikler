Uvula     the uvula is the punchbag-shaped object on the back of a throat , in the middle of the soft palate . massaging the uvula causes the gag reflex which makes someone vomit . the uvula functions with the back of the throat , the palate , and air coming up from the lungs to create a number variety of different sounds . consonants involving sound from the uvula are not found in most dialects of english , though they are found in many semitic , caucasian , and turkic languages , as well as several languages of western europe such as german , french and a few celtic languages , where it is used to make a sound called the uvular trill .
