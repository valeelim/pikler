Head coach     a head coach , senior coach or manager is a person who trains and develops men and women who play sports . they usually hold a more public profile and are paid more than other coaches on the team . in some sports such as soccer and baseball , the head coach is called a manager . in other sports , such as australian rules football , they are called a senior coach . some sports that use the phrase `` head coach '' include american football and basketball . these head coaches sometimes have other coaches that report to them . these assistant coaches focus on one part of the team rather than the whole team . assistant coaches often work in offensive positions or defensive positions . they can sometimes coach just one position on the team such as a coach just for the quarterbacks in american football .
