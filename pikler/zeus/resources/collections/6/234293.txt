Beatification     beatification ( from latin `` beatus '' , blessed , greek μακάριος , `` makarios '' and latin `` facere '' , make ) is a recognition given by the catholic church of a dead person 's entrance into heaven and power to help people who pray in his or her name . beatification is the third of the four steps in the canonization process . a person who is beatified is given the title `` blessed '' . other websites . - list of all blesseds in the catholic church by giga-catholic information .
