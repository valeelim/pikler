Goldsmith     a goldsmith is a person who makes objects using gold or other precious metals . 5,000 years ago , ancient sumerians made bowls out of gold . people have long used gold for making jewelry or sacred objects used in religious worship . goldsmiths use methods such as soldering , sawing , forging , casting , and polishing to make things out of gold . goldsmiths traditionally teach their trade to apprentices . today , jewelry arts schools teach goldsmithing and other similar skills . well known goldsmiths . - paul de lamerie - paul storr - lorenzo ghiberti - benvenuto cellini - johannes gutenberg - house of faberg√© related pages . - old master print - engraving - jewelry
