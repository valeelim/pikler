A Room with a View (movie)    a room with a view ( movie ) a room with a view is a 1985 british romantic drama movie . it is based on the novel of the same name by e.m. forster . it was directed by james ivory and produced by ismail merchant . it is set in florence , italy and in surrey , england during the 1900s . it stars helena bonham carter , maggie smith , julian sands , denholm elliott , daniel day-lewis , judi dench and simon callow .
