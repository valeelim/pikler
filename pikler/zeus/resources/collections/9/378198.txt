The Lover     the lover is a 1992 french erotic drama movie . it is based on the 1984 novel of the same name . it is set in 1929 in vietnam and is about a relationship between a 15 year old french girl and a chinese man twice her age . it was originally tagged nc-17 by the mpaa . however , the producers of `` the lover '' appealed and the rating was changed to r. when this was released into theaters , it received mixed reaction . audiences praised it . american critics , however , gave negative reviews of this drama .
