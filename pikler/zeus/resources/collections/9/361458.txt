Joyce Randolph     joyce randolph ( born joyce sirola october 21 , 1924 ) is an american actress . she played trixie norton on `` the honeymooners '' . randolph was born on october 21 , 1924 in detroit , michigan . she was married to richard lincoln charles from 1955 until his death in 1997. they had a son . she is the last living honeymooners cast member .
