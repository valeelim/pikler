Big Brother (UK)    big brother ( uk ) big brother is a reality television series in the united kingdom which began in 2000. it is a version of the `` big brother '' franchise . contestants must complete challenges while living in a house full of cameras . in some series the house is in london ; in some series it is in hertfordshire . in some series , the housemates are celebrities . from 2000-2010 , it was shown on channel 4 ; from 2011 onwards , it is shown on channel 5 .
