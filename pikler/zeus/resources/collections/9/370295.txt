Steve Whitmire     steve whitmire ( born september 24 , 1958 in atlanta , georgia ) is an american puppeteer actor and performer . in 1978 began his career at the age of 20. he replaced jim henson 's muppet characters . he is performer for kermit , and many other characters that jim henson preformed . he has also preformed rizzo . he is married to mellisa whitmire . he has also preformed ernie on sesame street .
