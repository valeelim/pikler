Bipolar disorder in children     bipolar disorder in children , more well known as pediatric bipolar disorder , is a very serious condition that affects children and adolescents . identifying bipolar disorder for children and teenagers may be challenging-certain symptoms are very different from adults . children and teens often have depressed moods mixed with mania taking place almost every day and sometimes at exactly the same time . diagnosis of bipolar disorder in children and adolescents is controversial , however . this condition often leads to impairments of daily function , and people with bipolar disorder which is prior to 18 years of age are far more likely to commit suicide . pediatric bipolar disorder is far more acute in children and youth than in the adult population , with diagnoses at very early age . in children or teens , mania often has serious psychotic troubles with mixed mania and depression episodes which are worse and for more erratic times than in adults . certain medications for this condition may have strong side effects for those under 18. most bipolar children have attention-deficit hyperactivity disorder .
