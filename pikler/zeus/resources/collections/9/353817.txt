Tom Hiddleston     thomas william `` tom '' hiddleston ( born 9 february 1981 ) is an english actor . his most famous roles include capt . james nicholls in `` war horse '' ( 2011 ) , f. scott fitzgerald in `` midnight in paris '' ( 2011 ) , freddie page in `` the deep blue sea '' ( 2011 ) , and loki in `` thor '' ( 2011 ) and `` the avengers '' ( 2012 ) . he will play loki once again in `` '' ( 2013 ) .
