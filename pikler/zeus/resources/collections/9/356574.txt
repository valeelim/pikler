Stardust (song)    stardust ( song ) `` stardust '' is an american popular song it was composed in 1927 by hoagy carmichael . in 1929 , mitchell parish added lyrics . at first , the song was called `` star dust '' . carmichael was the first person to record the song . he recorded it at the gennett records studio in richmond , indiana . the song is `` a song about a song about love '' . many musicians recorded this song . artie shaw recorded it with his big band . other websites . - stardust on `` sold on song '' ( bbc )
