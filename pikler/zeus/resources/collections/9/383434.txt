Saint Helier     saint helier or st. helier ( ; french language : `` saint-hélier '' , jèrriais : `` saint hélyi '' ) is one of the twelve parts ( called parishes ) of jersey , the largest of the channel islands in the english channel . 33,500 people live in saint helier . 33,500 is about 34.2 % of the number of people of jersey . it is the capital of the island ( but government house is in st. saviour ) . other websites . - saint helier official parish website
