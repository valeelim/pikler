Mega Man Xtreme     mega man xtreme is a platform video game made by capcom for the game boy color game console in 2000. it is a spin-off game in the `` mega man x '' series which started on the super nes . it was followed by a sequel , `` mega man xtreme 2 '' , in 2001. the game has three difficulty levels . normal has four levels , hard has four different levels , and xtreme has all eight levels . stages , enemies , bosses , and music from `` mega man x '' and `` mega man x2 '' are re-used in the game .
