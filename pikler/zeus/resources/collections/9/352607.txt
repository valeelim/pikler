Public holidays in Pakistan     this is a list of public holidays in pakistan . in addition to the holidays now no longer national holidays , several are observed as national days without a holiday : - may 28 - youm-e-takbir - september 7 - air force day ( youm-e-fizaia ) - september 8 - victory day/navy day ( youm-e-bahria ) - october 16 - death anniversary of liaquat ali khan - october 24 - azad kashmir day ( youm-e-kashmir ) - youm-e-babul islam is observed in pakistan , in honor of muhammad bin qasim .
