The Surgeon     the surgeon is a 1995 movie originally called `` exquisite tenderness '' . the internet movie database gave it a 4.8 rating . it was directed by carl schenkel . it is rated r for restricted . it is a horror movie . peter boyle is in it . it is 100 minutes long . it was released in spain in 1995. some of the footage was shot in canada . it made about 10,000,000 in profit . it also came out in the united kingdom and germany .
