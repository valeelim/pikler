Cheshire, Connecticut    cheshire , connecticut chesire is a town in new haven county , connecticut . the population was 29,261 in 2010. it is the center of population for the state of connecticut . in 2009 , chesire was ranked # 72 in `` money '' magazine 's `` 100 best places to live '' . in 2011 , chesire was ranked # 73 in `` money '' s `` 100 best places to live '' .
