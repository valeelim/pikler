Shlomo Yanai     shlomo yanai ( born may 18 , 1952 in holon ) is a retired israeli military officer and businessman . he held the rank of major general . he was the ceo of teva pharmaceutical industries ( teva ) from 1 march 2007 to may 2012. he was offered the directorship of mossad by benjamin netanyahu but turned it down . shlomo yanai was born in holon in 1952 , and was raised in tel aviv .
