Theophrastus     theophrastus ( greek : θεόφραστος ; c. 371 – c. 287 bc ) , a greek native of eresos in lesbos , was the successor to aristotle in the peripatetic school . he came to athens at a young age and studied in plato 's school . after plato 's death , he attached himself to aristotle . aristotle bequeathed to theophrastus his writings and designated him as his successor at the lyceum . theophrastus led the peripatetic school for thirty-six years , during which time the school flourished greatly . he is often considered the `` father of botany '' for his works on plants . after his death , the athenians honoured him with a public funeral . his successor as head of the school was strato of lampsacus .
