Università degli Studi Niccolò Cusano     università degli studi niccolò cusano ( ) is a private university in rome , italy . it was started on may 10 , 2006 ( founded by stefano bandecchi ) . the university has 6 specialized schools . the degree courses are delivered also in english . rector . - sebastiano scarcella ( 2006 - 2010 ) - giovanni puoti ( 2010 - 2013 ) - fabio fortuna ( 2013 - ) president . - giovanni iacono ( 2006-2012 ) - stefano ranucci ( 2012 - )
