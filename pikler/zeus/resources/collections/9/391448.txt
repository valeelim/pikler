Hibiscus trionum     hibiscus trionum is an annual plant that is native to the east of the mediterranean in the levant . it has spread both because people grow it as a garden plant , and because it can grow as a weed . the plant grows to a height of 20-50 cm , sometimes as much as 80 cm , and has white or yellow flowers with a purple centre . common names for the same plant include bladder hibiscus , bladder ketmia , bladder weed , flower-of-an-hour , flower-of-the-hour , modesty , puarangi , shoofly , and venice mallow . other websites . - missouriplants.com : `` hibiscus trionum '' - `` flower of an hour ''
