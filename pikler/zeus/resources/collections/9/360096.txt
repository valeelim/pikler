Jonathan Hyde     jonathan hyde ( born may 21 , 1948 ) is a australian-english stage , television , and movie actor . he has appeared in over 50 movies . he is mostly known for his role as j. bruce ismay in james cameron 's `` titanic '' . his career started in 1974. hyde was born in brisbane , australia on may 21 , 1948. he is married to isobel buchanan .
