Outrage (movie)    outrage ( movie ) outrage is a movie that came out in 2010 . `` autoreiji '' is the movie 's original title in japan . takeshi kitano is the director . it has a restricted rating . it is 109 minutes long . it is a crime drama . it is about tokyo 's yakuza clans . it has a little sexual content . it competed for the palme d'or at the 2010 cannes film festival .
