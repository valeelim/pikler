Piglet's Big Movie    piglet 's big movie piglet 's big movie is a 2003 american animated movie . it was released by walt disney pictures on march 21 , 2003. it is based on the characters in the `` winnie-the-pooh '' books written by a. a. milne . in the movie , piglet is ashamed of being small . he wanders off into the hundred acre woods , leading his friends to form a search party to find him . cast . - john fiedler as piglet - jim cummings as pooh and tigger - ken sansom as rabbit - nikita hopkins as roo - kath soucie as kanga - peter cullen as eeyore - michael gough as gopher - andre stojka as owl - tom wheatley as christopher robin
