Will Arnett     william emerson `` will '' arnett ( born may 4 , 1970 ) is a canadian - american movie , television , stage and voice actor who is known for his comedic roles as george oscar bluth , jr in `` arrested development '' , devon banks in `` 30 rock '' , mr. perkins in `` despicable me '' , as the missing link in `` monsters vs. aliens '' , and as batman in `` the lego movie '' and `` the lego batman movie '' . he was born on may 4 , 1970 in toronto , ontario , canada . he was married to penelope ann miller from 1994 until they divorced in 1995. in 2003 , arnett married to amy poehler . they have two children . in september 2012 , arnett and poehler separated .
