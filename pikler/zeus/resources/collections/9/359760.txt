Russell Scott     russell scott ( born june 30 , 1921- august 27 , 2012 ) , also known as blinky the clown , was an american clown , stage , and entertainer who starred in a denver , colorado television program called `` blinky 's fun club '' from 1958 until the show was cancelled in 1998. scott was also a stage actor and performed in the south broadway theatre in denver , colorado . scott was born in enid , oklahoma on june 30 , 1921. scott was never married and had no children . scott died on august 27 , 2012 in a hospital in morrison , colorado from complications of pneumonia , he was 91 years old .
