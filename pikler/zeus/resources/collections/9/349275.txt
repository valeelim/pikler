Brandon Bass     brandon bass ( born april 30 , 1985 ) is an american basketball player of the national basketball association ( nba ) . he is with the boston celtics . he played for lsu in college . he is a 6'8 '' , 250 lb power forward . on july 26 , 2007 , bass agreed to play for the dallas mavericks . he signed through the 2008-09 season with a $ 1.59 million contract . on july 10 , 2009 , bass signed a contract with the orlando magic . he was traded to the boston celtics in december 2011 for glen davis and von wafer .
