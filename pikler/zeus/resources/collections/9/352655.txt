Susan Smith     susan leigh vaughan smith ( born september 26 , 1971 ) is an american murderer . she was born in union , south carolina , and was a former student of the university of south carolina union . she was sentenced to life in prison on july 22 , 1995 for killing her two sons on october 25 , 1994. they were 3-year-old michael daniel smith , born october 10 , 1991 , and 14-month-old alexander tyler smith , born august 5 , 1993. at the time of the murders , smith was estranged from her husband and had a boyfriend who did not want children in his life . she believed that by killing her children , her boyfriend would want a long-term relationship with her . the case gained worldwide attention after it was broadcast live . smith told police that an african american man carjacked , and kidnapped her sons . several weeks later , she confessed that she drove her car into a nearby lake . she got out of the car , and watched her sons drown inside the car . smith later said that she suffered from mental illnesses , which made her kill her sons .
