Balgo, Western Australia    balgo , western australia balgo is a small community in western australia . it is surrounded by the great sandy desert and the tanami desert . it is an aboriginal community ; the area is traditionally kukatja country . there are also some pintupi families living there , who were displaced in the 1950s and 1960s . many of them came to live here from papunya , including some of the original artists of the papunya tula movement . many of them later left balgo when kintore and kiwirrkurra were set up in the 1980s . in 2006 , balgo had about 460 people living there . other websites . - warlayirti artists and cultural centre - luurnpa school website - balgo adult education centre website - kutjungka parish website
