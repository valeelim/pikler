Kahin Pyaar Na Ho Jaaye     kahin pyaar na ho jaaye is a 2000 bollywood film directed by k. muralimohana rao . salman khan and rani mukherji played the main characters . the movie also features jackie shroff , raveena tandon , mohnish behl , pooja batra , shakti kapoor in supporting roles . its music director was himesh reshammiya .
