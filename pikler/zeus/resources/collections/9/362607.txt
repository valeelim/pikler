Brad Sham     brad sham ( born august 16 , 1949 ) is a radio sportscaster currently with the dallas cowboys with babe laufenberg . he has done freelance announcing for the nfl on fox and nfl on cbs as well . sham also called nfl games for westwood one before returning to the cowboys . other football duties sham called are nfl europe games for fox , texas longhorns football , college football on cbs radio/westwood one/dial global , and the dallas desperadoes of the arena football league . sham has also done basketball announcing . he calls college basketball tournaments for espn plus , espn , and dial global formally westwood one and cbs radio . he formally called college basketball games for cbs and the texas longhorns men 's basketball team as well as the dallas chaperalls of the american basketball association and called texas rangers games . he also called soccer games for the dallas burn and the dallas tornado .
