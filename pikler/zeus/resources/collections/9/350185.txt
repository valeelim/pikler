Clandestine cell system     a clandestine cell system is a way of organising a group of three to five people so that it is kept secret . this is so the group are better able to resist infiltration or discovery by an enemy . if a person only knows the others in his cell , then he can not betray the others outside of his or her cell , either accidentally , or under interrogation . the cell can range from a strict hierarchy of people to a team of people . it is used by spy agencies , criminal organisations and guerilla groups .
