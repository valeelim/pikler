Nicholas and Alexandra     nicholas and alexandra is a 1971 biographical movie . it tells the story of the last russian monarch , tsar nicholas ii of russia , and his wife , tsarina alexandra . the movie was made from the book by robert k. massie . it was directed by franklin j. schaffner . it won academy awards for best art direction-set decoration ( john box , ernest archer , jack maxsted , gil parrondo , vernon dixon ) and best costume design . it was nominated for best actress in a leading role ( janet suzman ) , best cinematography , best music , original dramatic score and best picture . this soundtrack was written by richard rodney bennett . 1. overture ( 02:19 ) 1. nicholas and alexandra ( 01:26 ) 1. the royal children ( 01:23 ) 1. the palace ( 01:00 ) 1. sunshine days ( 03:21 ) 1. alexandra ( 01:18 ) 1. the romanov tercentenary ( 00:52 ) 1. lenin in exile ( 01:21 ) 1. the princessess ( 02:20 ) 1. the breakthrough ( 02:35 ) 1. the declaration of war ( 02:55 ) 1. extracte ( 02:40 ) 1. the journey to the front ( 01:02 ) 1. military march ( 02:40 )
