Coquitlam     coquitlam is a city in the canadian province of british columbia . the population of coquitlam was 126,456 in 2011. the mayor of coquitlam is richard stewart . the coast salish people occupied the territory for at least 9,000 years before simon fraser came through the region in 1808. europeans settled in coquitlam during 1859. the city of coquitlam was named a `` cultural capital of canada '' in 2009 by the department of canadian heritage . other websites . - official website
