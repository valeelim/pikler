David Prowse     david prowse , mbe ( born 1 july 1935 ) is an english former bodybuilder , weightlifter , and movie , television , stage , and voice actor who is known for playing the role of darth vader in four star wars movies . prowse was born in bristol , england . he studied at bristol grammar school . he has been married to norma prowse since 1963. he said in 2009 that he has prostate cancer ; it has since gone into remission . in november 2014 , prowse revealed that he has dementia .
