Ramallah     ramallah ( `` rāmallāh '' is a palestinian city , in the west bank . it is north of jerusalem . it currently serves as the `` de facto '' capital of the state of palestine , palestine liberation organization and the palestinian national authority . the constitutional capital is jerusalem . ramallah has over 27,000 people living there . ramallah was historically a christian town , but today muslims form the majority of the population , with about 25 % of the people christians . the name of the city is made of `` ram '' , an aramaic word that means `` high place or mountain '' , and `` allah '' , the arabic word for god . it translates as `` height of god '' . other websites . - ramallah city - ramallah history
