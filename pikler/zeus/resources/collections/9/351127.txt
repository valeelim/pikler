Hu County     hu county ( , pinyin : hù xiàn ) is a county of xi'an , shaanxi , china . qinling mountain is to the south . wei river is to the north . chang'an district is to the east . zhouzhi county is to the west . it covers . as of november 2010 , 556,377 people lived there . in xia dynasty ( 2100 bc - 1600 bc ) , here named hushi country ( 扈氏国 ) , meaning `` hu family 's country '' , it 's the origin of hu county . after changed some names , it named hu country ( 扈国 ) . and in qin dynasty ( 221 bc -206 bc ) , `` 扈 '' changed to `` 鄠 '' in chinese language , and then country changed to county . in 1964 , `` 鄠 '' changed to `` 户 '' .
