Koi... Mil Gaya    koi ... mil gaya koi ... mil gaya ( english : `` i found someone '' ) is an indian science fiction movie released in 2003. the main characters in the film are played by hrithik roshan , rekha , preity zinta and rajat bedi . the movie was directed and produced by rakesh roshan , the father of hrithik roshan . it is about a mentally retarded person who sees an alien in a forest which he thought would change his life forever . the movie was a superhit and became the most successful movie of 2003. it is regarded as one of india 's first science fiction movies . it has two sequels krrish ( 2006 ) and krrish 3 ( 2013 ) .
