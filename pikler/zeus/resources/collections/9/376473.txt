James Seanor     james seanor ( born 1833 , date of death unknown ) was a union navy sailor in the american civil war . he was also a recipient of the u.s. military 's highest decoration , the medal of honor . he received it for his actions at the battle of mobile bay . seanor was born in 1833 in boston , massachusetts . he was living in new york when he joined the navy . he served during the civil war as a master-at-arms on the . although their enlistments had expired , seanor and crewmate andrew jones voluntarily transferred to the to fight in the battle of mobile bay on august 5 , 1864. during the battle they `` carried out [ their ] duties gallantly '' according to the medal of honor citation . for this action , both seanor and jones were awarded the medal of honor four months later on december 31 , 1864. seanor 's official medal of honor citation reads : served as master-at-arms on board the u.s. ironclad `` chickasaw '' , mobile bay , 5 august 1864. although his enlistment was up , seanor volunteered for the battle of mobile bay , going on board the `` chickasaw '' from the `` vincennes '' where he carried out his duties gallantly throughout the engagement which resulted in the capture of the rebel ram `` tennessee '' .
