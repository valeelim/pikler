Florence (given name)    florence ( given name ) florence is a french or english female given name . it is from the latin word `` floreo '' , which means `` i flower '' . the name also comes from the city of florence in italy . it was most popular in the u.s. between 1900 and 1940. it is still popular in canada and england . the name has been used for many tropical cyclones in the atlantic basin . some famous people with the name are florence nightingale and florence henderson .
