Kourtney Kardashian     kourtney mary kardashian ( born april 18 , 1979 ) is an american businesswoman , socialite , model , actress and reality television personality who gained fame as a cast member of `` keeping up with the kardashians '' , `` kourtney and kim take new york '' , and `` kourtney and khloé take miami '' . she is the sister of kim kardashian and khloé kardashian . her mother is kris jenner , her father was robert kardashian , and her step-father is bruce jenner . kardashian was born in los angeles , california on april 18 , 1979 , to parents robert and kris . she was partners with scott disick since 2007 but they split in july 2015. they had 3 children together - mason , penelope , and reign .
