Subprefectures of Japan     each subprefecture is part of the greater administration of the prefecture and the national state . it is also as part of a self-government system . subprefectures were given a definite form in 1878 ( `` meiji 11 ) . the meiji government established the as an administrative unit . in 1888 ( `` meiji 21 '' ) , the sub-prefecture as a form of self-government was officially recognized as more general than civic corporations like cities , towns and villages . some prefectures of japan include subprefectures . the subprefecture is the jurisdiction surrounding a `` branch office '' of the prefectural government . current subprefectures . - hokkaidō , 14 subprefectures - kagoshima , 2 subprefectures - miyazaki , 1 subprefecture - tokyo , 4 subprefectures - shimane , 1 subprefecture - yamagata , 4 subprefectures former subprefectures . - hyōgo , 10 subprefectures - chiba , 5 subprefectures - nagasaki , 3 subprefectures - okinawa , 2 subprefectures related pages . - list of regions of japan - list of islands of japan
