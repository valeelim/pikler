Charlie Callas     charlie callas ( born charles callias december 20 , 1924 january 27 , 2011 ) was an american comedian and actor . he worked with dean martin , frank sinatra , mel brooks , dom deluise , jerry lewis , milton berle , and don rickles . callas was born on december 20 , 1924 in brooklyn , new york . he served in the united states army during world war ii . after the war , callas began his career in 1950. he appeared in over 100 television programs and 50 movies . callas had only two influences , groucho marx and charlie chaplin . callas influenced larry the cable guy and many other comedians . callas was married to evelyn callas until her death in 2010. callas had two sons , mark callas and larry callas . callas died on january 27 , 2011 from natural causes in his las vegas , nevada home . he was 83 years old . callas is survived by his 2 sons . his remains were cremated . filmography . - `` the big mouth '' ( 1967 ) - `` switch '' ( tv ) ( 1975 1977 ) - `` silent movie '' ( 1976 ) - `` pete 's dragon '' ( voice ) ( 1977 ) - `` high anxiety '' ( 1977 )
