Batplane     the batplane , later known as the batwing is the personal airplane for batman . the plane was built by wayne enterprises by lucius fox . it is part plane part helicopter or `` the batcopter '' . the plane holds many guns and gadgets in case of an air attack . it also can air-park while up in the air . the plane appeared in `` batman '' , `` batman forever '' , and as the bat in `` the dark knight rises '' . other websites . - batman : collected issues of the dark knight - batman # 203 - batman : yesterday , today , & beyond - history of the batplane
