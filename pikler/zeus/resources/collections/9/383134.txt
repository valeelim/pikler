Free diving     free diving is diving underwater ( usually in the ocean ) without using a breathing apparatus ( air carried in tanks on their backs ) . free divers practice holding their breath for long periods of time so that they can stay underwater a long time . usually free divers stay underwater for about 45 seconds . that allows them to explore 30 feet underwater , which is where most marine life lives . free diving equipment includes a mask , snorkel , fins , wetsuit and weightbelt . dangers of free diving include : - becoming panicked - passing out because of lack of oxygen - possible death from drowning if you are not trained well and if you go too deep into the water references . - http : //www.freedive.net/freediving/freedving.htm
