Back to December     `` back to december '' is a pop song by the singer taylor swift . it is from her third studio album , `` speak now '' ( 2010 ) . it was written by swift , who produced the song alongside nathan chapman . the song was released in the united states on november 14 , 2010. it was the second single from `` speak now '' . according to swift , `` back to december '' is the first time she ever apologizes to someone in a song . the song is about breakup and wanting to go back to former times . its lyrics are a remorseful plea for forgiveness for breaking up with a former lover . this single got very high reviews from the critics . it was a hit in the united states in 2010. it peaked at # 3 on the `` billboard '' hot country songs chart and at # 6 on the hot 100 chart . other websites . - `` back to december '' on youtube
