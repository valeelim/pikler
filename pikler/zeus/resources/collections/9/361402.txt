John Nolan (actor)    john nolan ( actor ) john nolan ( born may 22 , 1938 ) is a british movie and television actor who is known for his role as a wayne enterprises board member douglas fredericks in `` batman begins '' and `` the dark knight rises '' . he was born in london , england and is married to kim hartman since 1975. he has two sons and a daughter . he is the uncle to christopher nolan and jonathan nolan .
