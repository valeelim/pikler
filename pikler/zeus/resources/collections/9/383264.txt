Mario Kart Arcade GP     mario kart arcade gp is a racing video game released in arcades in october 2005. it was developed by namco and published by nintendo . it is the first `` mario kart '' game to be released in arcades . a sequel , `` mario kart arcade gp 2 '' , was released in 2007. in `` mario kart arcade gp '' , players can play as characters from both the `` mario '' series and the `` pac-man '' series . there are six stages with four courses each . after completing a stage , the player must put in a coin to continue playing . when the player completes all six stages , a `` challenge game '' comes up . in the `` challenge game , '' the player has to complete a task under a time limit .
