Alibori Department     alibori is the largest and northernmost département of benin . it has an area of 25,683 km² . in 2003 the population was 355,950. the department is divided into 6 communes . each commune is centered at one of the main towns : banikoara , gogounou , kandi , karimama , malanville , and ségbana . the department borders the countries of burkina faso , niger , and nigeria . the département was made in 1999 when it was split off from borgou . since 2008 , the capital has been kandi .
