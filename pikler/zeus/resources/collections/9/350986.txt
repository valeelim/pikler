Cluster bomb     a cluster bomb , or cluster munition , is an explosive device . it explodes in the air or on the ground , and releases dozens of smaller bombs . it is an anti-personnel weapon because it kills or maims people , not armoured vehicles . they were used during a lot during the vietnam war and have been most recently used in the wars in iraq and afghanistan . they are considered illegal by over 100 countries . these countries have signed the `` convention on cluster munitions '' adopted in may 2008 .
