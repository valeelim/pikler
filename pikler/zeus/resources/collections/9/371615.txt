Comilla Jagannath Temple     comilla jagannath temple is a hindu temple in comilla , in southeastern bangladesh . its terracotta brick construction is in the typical bengal style of temple architecture . the temple was originally built in the 16th century by sree sreejoktou moharaja rada kishor manikuo bahadur , who was the king of tripura . the statues of the deities of jagannath , balabhadra and subhadra were originally installed in a temple in tripura from where they were subsequently shifted to this temple . other websites . - photograph of the temple
