Ryan Reynolds     ryan rodney reynolds ( born october 23 , 1976 ) is a canadian movie , television , stage , and voice actor who is known for his roles as hal jordan/green lantern in `` green lantern '' and as wade wilson/deadpool in `` '' and `` deadpool '' . he is also known for his roles in `` national lampoon 's van wilder '' , `` just friends '' , `` definitely '' , `` maybe '' , `` the proposal '' , `` the change-up '' , and `` safe house '' . reynolds was born on october 23 , 1976 in vancouver , british columbia . he studied at kitsilano secondary school and at kwantlen polytechnic university . reynolds was married to scarlett johansson from 2008 to 2011. he married blake lively in 2012 .
