My Name Is Skrillex     my name is skrillex is the first ep by american electronic music producer , skrillex . `` my name is skrillex '' was released on june 7 , 2010 on his myspace profile . the album is the first solo ep by sonny moore under the alias skrillex . he released a previous album titled `` gypsyhook '' under his first name . other websites . - my name is skrillex at discogs
