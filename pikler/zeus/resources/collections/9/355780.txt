Elizabeth Arden     florence nightingale graham ( born 31 december 1884-died 18 october 1966 ) , better known by her acting name of elizabeth arden , was a canadian-american business woman who founded what is now known as elizabeth arden , incorporated . she built a cosmetics network in the united states . at the peak of her career , she became one of the wealthiest women in the world . she was born next to woodbridge in the province of ontario .
