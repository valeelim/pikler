Beverly D'Angelo     beverly heather d'angelo ( born november 15 , 1951 ) is an american actress . she acts mostly in movies . she first became well known in the 1983 movie `` national lampoon 's vacation '' . she also acted in many other movies , particularly comedy movies . she was born in columbus , ohio . d'angelo was in the 1980 movie `` coal miner 's daughter '' . she shared the country music association award for album of the year for that movie 's soundtrack .
