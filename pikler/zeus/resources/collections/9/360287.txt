Lily Tomlin     mary jean `` lily '' tomlin ( born september 1 , 1939 ) is an american actress , writer and a comedian . she has had a large effect on american comedy since the late 1960s . her career has involved movies , broadway stage acts and television . she has won many awards , including an emmy , a grammy and a tony award . tomlin was born in detroit , michigan . she is openly lesbian and has been with her partner jane wagner for over 40 years . other websites . - official website
