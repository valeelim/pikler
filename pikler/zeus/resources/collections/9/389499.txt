Stall     stall may refer to : enclosures . - stall ( enclosure ) , a small enclosure , as for market goods , or for an animal - market stall , a makeshift or mobile structures for selling market goods or serving food motion . - stall ( engine ) , the unexpected or unwanted stopping of an engine - stall ( flight ) , when an aircraft can not make enough lift to keep the aircraft in level flight - compressor stall , the sudden loss of compression in a jet engine - stalling ( gaming ) , obstruction of the flow of play while leading in a timed game
