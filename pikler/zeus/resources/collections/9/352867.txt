Virginia Wade     sarah virginia wade obe ( born 10 july 1945 ) is a british tennis player turned commentator . she was born in bournemouth , hampshire . she turned pro in 1968. she won the us open in 1968 , the australian open in 1972 and wimbledon in 1977. she retired in 1986. since 1981 , she has been a commentator for the bbc . as of 2013 , she is the last british woman to win a major tennis tournament .
