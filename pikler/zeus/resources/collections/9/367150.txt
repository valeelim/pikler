Leopardus     leopardus is a genus of small spotted cats that are native to middle and south america . very few species belonging to `` leopardus '' are in the southern united states . `` leopardus '' may be the oldest branch of the felinae to go into the americas , followed by the genera `` lynx '' and `` puma '' . species . - `` leopardus colocolo '' – colocolo - `` leopardus braccatus '' – pantanal cat - `` leopardus pajeros '' – pampas cat - `` leopardus geoffroyi '' – geoffroy 's cat - `` leopardus guigna '' – kodkod - `` leopardus jacobitus '' – andean mountain cat - `` leopardus pardalis '' – ocelot - `` leopardus tigrinus '' – oncilla - `` leopardus wiedii '' – margay
