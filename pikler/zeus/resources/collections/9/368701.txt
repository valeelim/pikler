1830 United States Census     the united states census of 1830 was the fifth census in the united states . it was done on june 1 , 1830. it found the population of the 24 states to be 12,866,020 . 2,009,043 of the total were slaves . the center of population was about 170 miles ( 274 km ) west of washington , d.c. in grant county , virginia . further reading . - historic us census data
