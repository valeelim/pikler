Joe Castiglione     joe castiglione is a radio announcer currently for the boston red sox . he works with dave o'brien . o'brien is now the lead announcer with castiglione with secondary . castiglione has worked with jerry trupiano for red sox broadcasts for many years . he also has called games for the milwaukee brewers , cleveland indians , cleveland cavaliers , massachusetts minutemen football and basketball , and the lafayette leopards football and basketball .
