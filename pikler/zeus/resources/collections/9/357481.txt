Yamato nadeshiko     the phrase combines the words `` yamato '' , which is an ancient name for japan , and `` nadeshiko '' , which is a wild pink carnation ( dianthus superbus ) . the phrase `` yamato nadeshiko '' means the traditional japanese woman , and she is contrasted with other women such as the `` new '' type of japanese woman . as ideas about women have developed and changed , this term has meant different things at different times in history . for example , the term had a special nationalistic meaning during world war ii .
