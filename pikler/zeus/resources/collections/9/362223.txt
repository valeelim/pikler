Dianne Feinstein     dianne feinstein ( born june 22 , 1933 ) is the senior united states senator from california . she is a member of the u.s. democratic party and has been in senate since november 4 , 1992. she was also the 38th mayor in san francisco from 1978 to 1988. she is the first woman to have presided over a u.s. presidential inauguration . she was born in san francisco , california . feinstein is of polish-jewish descent .
