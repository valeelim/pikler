George's Secret Key to the Universe    george 's secret key to the universe george 's secret key to the universe is a children 's book written by stephen hawking , his daughter lucy hawking and his research student christophe galfard . it was written to explain the universe and the laws of physics to children , in the form of an adventure story . it was published in 2007. there are another three books in the series , `` george 's cosmic treasure hunt '' ( 2009 ) , `` george and the big bang '' ( 2010 ) , and `` george and the unbreakable code '' ( 2014 ) . other websites . - george 's secret key website
