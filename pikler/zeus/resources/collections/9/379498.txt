He Pingping     he pingping ( july 13 , 1988 – march 13 , 2010 ) was a chinese citizen who was the world 's shortest man who can walk . he was only 74 centimeters long . he met with xi shun , sultan kösen , and svetlana pankratova . pingping was born on july 14 , 1988 in ulanqab , inner mongolia , china . he died on march 13 , 2010 from heart failure in milan , italy , aged 21 .
