Okunino     okunino ( ) is a village in gmina miastko , bytów county , pomeranian voivodeship , in northern poland . it is about south-west of bytów and south-west of gdańsk ( capital city of the pomeranian voivodeship ) . from 1975 to 1998 the village was in słupsk voivodeship . references . - map of the gmina miastko
