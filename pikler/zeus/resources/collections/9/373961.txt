Charles McNeill Gray     charles mcneill gray ( march 7 , 1807 – october 17 , 1885 ) was an american politician who served as mayor of chicago , illinois ( 1853–1854 ) for the democratic party . gray was born on march 7 , 1807 in sherburne , new york . he came and lived in chicago since july 1834. gray died on october 17 , 1885 in chicago , illinois from an illness , aged 78. other websites . - inaugural address - charles mcneill gray at chicago public library
