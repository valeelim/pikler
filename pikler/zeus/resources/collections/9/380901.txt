Dirt (album)    dirt ( album ) dirt is the second studio album by american rock band , alice in chains . `` dirt '' was released on september 29 , 1992. the album featured the singles `` would ? `` , `` them bones '' , `` angry chair '' , `` rooster '' , and `` down in a hole '' . dirt is currently the band 's highest selling album to date and has gone on to sell 5 million copies worldwide . the album was certified four-times platinum by the riaa . dirt was the final album to feature mike starr on bass before he was kicked out of the band and replaced by former ozzy osbourne bassist mike inez . the album was also included in the 2005 book `` 1001 albums you must hear before you die '' and was ranked # 1 on guitar world magazine 's top ten list of guitar albums of 1992 other websites . - dirt at allmusic
