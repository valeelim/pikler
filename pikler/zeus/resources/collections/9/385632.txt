The Story of Us     `` the story of us '' is a 2011 single from taylor swift . it is about breakups and troubles between two people . the story in the song was inspired by an encounter with an ex-boyfriend during the cmt music awards in which swift and the ex attempted to ignore one another . `` the story of us '' received mixed reviews , peaking at # 41 on `` billboard '' hot 100 , although the video got positive review for its `` departure from the music video high school '' . the song was on swift 's album `` speak now '' , released in 2010 .
