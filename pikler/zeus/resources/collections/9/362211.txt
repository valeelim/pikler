Cobie Smulders     jacoba francisca maria smulders ( born april 3 , 1982 ) is a canadian model , actress and voice artist . she has acted in movies and television . she plays robin scherbatsky in the tv series `` how i met your mother '' . she played maria hill in the 2012 action-adventure movie `` the avengers '' . smulders was born in vancouver , british columbia , canada . smulders has been married to taran killam since 2012. they have a daughter , shaelyn cado killam ( born 2009 ) .
