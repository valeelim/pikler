Angela Fong     angela carolyn fong ( born february 3 , 1985 ) is a canadian professional wrestler and actress . she is best known for her time at world wrestling entertainment ( wwe ) . she was known by the ring name miss angela during her time at florida championship wrestling . she was later known by the name savannah when she was the a ring announcer for ecw on syfy and the official ring announcer for season one of wwe nxt . fong was also a cheerleader for the canadian football league 's bc lions . other websites . - online world of wrestling profile
