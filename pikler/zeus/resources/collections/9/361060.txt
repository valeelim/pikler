Ty Law     ty law ( b . 10 february 1974 ) is a retired american football player . he played as a safety in the nfl . law was drafted by the new england patriots in 1995. he also played for the new york jets ( 2005 , 2008 ) , kansas city chiefs ( 2006-2007 ) and denver broncos ( 2009 ) . he retired after the 2009 season . law was born in aliquippa , pennsylvania .
