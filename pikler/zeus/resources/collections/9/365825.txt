Yi Hwang     yi hwang ( 1501 - 1570 ) was a korean joseon dynasty neo-confucianism philosopher , politician , writer , and poet . he was one of the main people for the party east man and party south man . the name he wrote under was toigye or toido . his courtesy name was gyeongho . toegyero , a street in central seoul , is named after him . his likeness is on the south korean 1,000 won note . related pages . - cho shik other websites . - yi hwang - http : //100.daum.net/encyclopedia/view.do ? docid=b18a0815b - yi hwang - dosan school - toigye academic researchers - toigye academic business association
