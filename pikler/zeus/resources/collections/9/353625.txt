Jacques Derrida     jacques derrida ( 15 july 1930 - 9 october 2004 ) was a french philosopher . he was born in el biar , french algeria into a sephardic jewish family originally from toledo , spain . he developed a form of semiotic analysis known as deconstruction . his work is called post-structualism and associated with postmodern philosophy . he died of pancreatic cancer in a paris hospital .
