Tosashimizu, Kōchi    tosashimizu , kōchi the village of nakanohama in what is now tosashimizu was the birthplace of nakahama manjirō , who was the first known japanese to live in the united states in the mid-19th century . the modern city was established in 1954. sister cities . - new bedford , massachusetts , us - fairhaven , massachusetts , us , since 1987 other websites . - tosashimizu city website
