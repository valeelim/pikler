Hazara District     hazara district was an old district of peshawar division in the north-west frontier province of pakistan until 1976. it was the most northerly part of the division and covered an area of about . it was a long narrow shape , about wide and about long . it separated kashmir from the independent areas . the main cities were abbottabad , haripur , mansehra , nawashahr and baffa . it is now renamed as the hazara division .
