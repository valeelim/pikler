Kirby's Epic Yarn    kirby 's epic yarn kirby 's epic yarn is a platformer video game for the nintendo wii console . it was developed by hal laboratory and published by nintendo . it was released in japan on october 14 , 2010 , in north america on october 17 , 2010 , and in europe on february 25 , 2011. one day , kirby finds a tomato and eats it . while doing so , an evil being named yin-yarn takes kirby into patch land , a place made out of yarn . there , kirby 's body becomes yarn . kirby also has the ability to transform . this was given to kirby by the tomato he ate earlier . kirby uses this ability to rescue a boy named prince fluff . prince fluff says that yin-yarn broke patch land into different pieces that were held together by magic yarn . kirby has to help him find the pieces of the magic yarn to restore patch land to normal . in `` kirby 's epic yarn '' , kirby does not have his inhale ability . instead , kirby has a whip-like attack . kirby can use it both to attack enemies and turn them into yarn . kirby can also no longer float in air with his breath . instead , he transforms into an parachute . kirby can also transform into a car and a dolphin . the car makes kirby faster , while the dolphin allows him to go underwater .
