Marty Allen     marty allen ( born morton david alpern , march 23 , 1922 ) is an american comedian , actor , activist , and writer . allen is known for his stand-up comedy jokes in nightclubs in california and in las vegas . he also wrote books about his career and support many foundations . allen was born morton david alpern on march 23 , 1922 in pittsburgh , pennsylvania to a jewish family . he studied at taylor allderdice high school and at the university of southern california . allen was married to frenchie allen until her death in 1976. then he was married to katie blackwell since 1984. he has no children . allen now lives in las vegas , nevada and in los angeles , california . other websites . - an interview with marty allen , may 2011 - official site
