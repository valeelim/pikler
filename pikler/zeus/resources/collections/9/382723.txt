Brenda Strong     brenda strong ( born march 25 , 1960 in oregon ) is an american actress who stars primarily in television programs . she has , however , starred in a limited number of movies . she acted as mary alice young in the abc sitcom `` desperate housewives '' and as ann ewing in the tnt television drama `` dallas '' , the 2012 edition . before acting , she was crowned miss arizona 1980 .
