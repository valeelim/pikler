Joan Mondale     joan adams mondale ( née adams ; august 8 , 1930 – february 3 , 2014 ) was the wife of walter mondale . he is a former vice president of the united states , presidential candidate , and united states ambassador to japan . she was the unofficial second lady from 1977 to 1981. she was an advocate for arts and has had the name 'joan of art ' . she has studied kinds of art , such as japanese art . mondale was born to john maxwell adams and eleanor jane hall . she was one of the three daughters in her family . she attended schools in wallingford , pennsylvania . on february 2 , 2014 , the mondale family announced that she had entered hospice care for alzheimer 's disease . she died at the hospice from the disease in minneapolis the following day , surrounded by members of her family .
