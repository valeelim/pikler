Gymnopis syntrema     the gymnopis syntrema is a species of caecilians . they belong to the family caeciliidae . they live in belize , guatemala and honduras . their habitat is subtropical or tropical forests . their populations are dying because of habitat loss . references . - acevedo , m. , lee , j . & wilkinson , m. 2004. gymnopis syntrema . 2006 iucn red list of threatened species . downloaded on 21 july 2007. other websites . - amphibian web
