Jake Bugg     jake bugg ( born 28 february 1994 ) is an english singer and songwriter . he was born and raised in nottingham . he was chosen by the bbc to participate in the glastonbury festival in 2011. he got a contract with a record label after that . his first album , `` jake bugg '' , was released on 15 october 2012. it came top of the charts in the uk and in scotland . he has published seven singles from his album : `` trouble town '' , `` country song '' , `` lightning bolt '' , `` taste it '' , `` two fingers '' , `` seen it all '' and `` broken '' . bugg released his second album `` shangri la '' on 18 november 2013. bugg was nominated for `` british breakthrough act '' at the 2013 brit awards . his third album `` on my one '' was released on 17 june 2016. other websites . - official website
