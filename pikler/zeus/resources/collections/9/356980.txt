Soleil Moon Frye     soleil moon frye ( born august 6 , 1976 ) is an american television actress . her best-known roles are that of punky brewster in the same-named sitcom which lasted from 1984 to 1988 and roxie in the television sitcom `` sabrina , the teenage witch '' ( starting in 1996 and ending in 2003 ) . frye was also in other things on television . she began acting at age 6 on television .
