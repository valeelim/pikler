National Building Museum     the national building museum is a museum in washington d.c. dedicated to architecture , landscaping , urban design , urban planning , industrial design , and the history of buildings in the united states . the museum host exhibitions of architectural styles and organizes tours for visitors and activities for children and families . the museum was established in 1969 and moved to an old building that was the interior of the pension office in 1980. other websites . - official website - national park service - national historic landmarks program - pension building listing - general services administration page on the pension building ( national building museum )
