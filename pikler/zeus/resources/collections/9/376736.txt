Alison Lohman     alison marion lohman ( september 18 , 1979 in palm springs , california ) is an american movie and television actress . she has been in theatrical features , including `` white oleander '' and the erotic 2005 biography `` where the truth lies '' ( the latter was given an nc-17 rating by mpaa ; it was later released unrated ) . lohman has also been in tv programs , including `` 7th heaven '' .
