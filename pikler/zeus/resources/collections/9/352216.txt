Swindon railway station     swindon railway station is a train station in swindon , wiltshire , south west england . it was opened on 17 december 1840 by the great western railway . the station has services between london and bristol . there are also trains to cardiff . a new platform , platform 4 opened in 2003. in december 2005 people were told that stations in the thames valley area were to be made better . on 1 march 2011 , philip hammond mp , ( the uk government 's secretary of state for transport ) said that he had made plans for making the great western main line electric from didcot to swindon , and and cardiff , through bath spa and chippenham . the department 's reading said that new electric trains would be made by hitatchi . the electrification had first been told by the last labour government 's transport secretary , lord andrew adonis..
