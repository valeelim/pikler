Kutub al-Sittah     kutub al-sittah are the main hadith collections in sunni islam meaning `` the six books '' . they are sometimes called the `` sahih sittah '' . they consist of sahih bukhari , sahih muslim , sunan al-sughra , sunan abu dawood , jami al-tirmidhi and sunan ibn majah . huraira is the most quoted person in these books .
