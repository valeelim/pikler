Nurzhol Boulevard     nurzhol boulevard ( , formerly known as `` сулы-нулы бульвар '' green water boulevard ) is the national boulevard of astana , kazakhstan . the boulevard is a pedestrian area . it is in the new business and administrative centre of astana . it runs from the president 's residence ak orda to the khan shatyr entertainment centre . the bayterek is in nurzhol boulevard . it is one of astana 's best-known buildings . other websites . - water green boulevard in flickr site .
