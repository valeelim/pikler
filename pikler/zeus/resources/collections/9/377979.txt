Charles Brown (Medal of Honor)    charles brown ( medal of honor ) corporal charles brown ( born 1849 ) received the united states 's highest honor for bravery . it is called the medal of honor . corporal brown was a united states marine . he was born in new york city . he enlisted in hong kong in 1870. the tracking number for his medal of honor is number 169. it is dated 8 february 1872. he received the medal for his actions while stationed aboard the uss colorado . citation : related pages . - list of medal of honor recipients
