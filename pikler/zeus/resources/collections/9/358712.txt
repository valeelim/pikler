Jean-Georges Noverre     jean-georges noverre ( 29 april 172719 october 1810 ) was a french dancer and ballet master ( choreographer ) . he is considered the creator of the ballet d'action . he performed or held posts across europe . his first great success was the ballet `` les fêtes chinoises '' . other ballets include `` jason et médée '' , `` la toilette de venus '' , `` les jalousies du sérail '' , `` la dour corsaire '' and `` le jaloux sans rival '' . he expressed his views on dance in the book `` lettres sur la danse et les ballets '' in 1756 ( published in 1760 ) . the french revolution reduced noverre to poverty . he died in 1810 .
