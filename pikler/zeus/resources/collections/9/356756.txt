Michael Shannon     michael corbett shannon ( born august 7 , 1974 in lexington , kentucky ) is an american actor . he won an academy award for best supporting actor for his role in `` revolutionary road '' . he was a regular on the hbo series `` boardwalk empire '' , playing agent nelson van alden . shannon played general zod in zack snyder 's 2013 movie `` man of steel '' . shannon was born in lexington , kentucky on august 7 , 1974 to donald s. shannon and to geraldine hine . he was raised in chicago , illinois . his career began in 1993 in the movie `` groundhog day '' . shannon is in a relationship with actress kate arrington . they have two daughters . some movies he was in . - `` groundhog day '' ( 1993 ) - `` jesus ' son '' ( 1999 ) - `` tigerland '' ( 2000 ) - `` vanilla sky '' ( 2001 ) - `` bad boys ii '' ( 2003 ) - `` kangaroo jack '' ( 2003 ) - `` dead birds '' ( 2004 ) - `` the woodsman '' ( 2004 ) - `` world trade center '' ( 2006 ) - `` bug '' ( 2006 ) - `` before the devil knows you 're dead '' ( 2007 ) - `` my son , my son , what have you done '' ( 2009 ) - `` the runaways '' ( 2010 ) - `` 13 '' ( 2010 )
