Renault 12 Toros     renault 12 toros is a modernized version of renault 12 in turkey . it was designed in 1989. it was released in the second half of the year . its name comes from the taurus mountains . renault 12 toros had high traction so it used for many years . it was used as the official vehicle for many years by the turkish national police and turkish gendarmerie . production stopped in 2000 because its emissions polluted too much for the european union 's emission norms . it is sold 4000 tl to 13000 tl in turkey .
