Yunnan lake newt     the yunnan lake newt ( `` cynops wolterstorffi '' ) was a species of newts . it was part of the family salamandridae . it was also known as wolterstorff 's newt . it used to live in china . references . - datong , y . & lau , m.w.n . 2004. cynops wolterstorffi . 2006 iucn red list of threatened species .
