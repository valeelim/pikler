Ingerophrynus     ingerophrynus is a genus of true toads with eleven species . ten of the species in this genus used to be thought to be species of the genus `` bufo '' . in 2007 a new species , `` ingerophrynus gollum '' , was added to this genus . this species is named after the character gollum created by j. r. r. tolkien . these species live in southern yunnan and indochina ; peninsular thailand and malaya to sumatra , borneo , java , nias island , and sulawesi . other websites . - . 2007. amphibian species of the world : an online reference . version 5.1 ( 10 october 2007 ) . `` ingerophrynus '' . electronic database accessible at http : //research.amnh.org/herpetology/amphibia/index.php . american museum of natural history , new york , usa .
