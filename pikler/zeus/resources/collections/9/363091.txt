Joel Schumacher     joel t. schumacher ( born august 29 , 1939 ) is an american movie director , writer , screenwriter , and producer . he is known for his movies such as `` batman forever '' , `` batman & robin '' , `` the lost boys '' , `` falling down '' , `` a time to kill '' , `` 8mm '' , `` tigerland '' , and `` phone booth '' . schumacher was born on august 29 , 1939 in new york city . he studied at parsons the new school for design and university of california , los angeles . schumacher is openly gay .
