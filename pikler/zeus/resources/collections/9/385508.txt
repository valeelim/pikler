Elizabeth Hartman     elizabeth hartman ( december 2 , 1943 - june 10 , 1987 ) was an american actress who mostly did movies . she starred in the movie `` a patch of blue '' with shelley winters and sidney poitier and received a golden globe award for that role . hartman was born in youngstown , ohio . hartman had depression . on june 10 , 1987 , she jumped from the 5th floor of her apartment building in pittsburgh , pennsylvania , which then ended her life at age 43 .
