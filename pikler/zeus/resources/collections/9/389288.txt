Woodforest National Bank     woodforest national bank is a privately held bank . it 's headquarters are in the woodlands , texas . it has over 750 branches in 17 different states . woodforest national bank is walmart 's largest retail partner . woodforest has a few branches that are open 24 hours a day and 364 days of the year . woodforest national bank also partners with sam 's club and kroger in a few states . woodforest national bank offers regular accounts . they also offer `` second chance '' checking accounts for customers who have not remained in good standing with chexsystems . the `` second chance '' accounts have higher fees and a much longer probation period than pnc bank as of march 2012. woodforest national bank also offers second chance accounts for both consumers and businesses . other websites . - woodforest national bank - woodforest charitable foundation - woodforest financial services , inc .
