Oghi Tehsil     oghi tehsil is a `` tehsil '' or subdivision of the mansehra district , khyber pakhtunkhwa province , pakistan . it is situated in a mountainous area close to the kala dhaka tribal zone . the main or capital town of this tehsil is oghi . the large part of the population of oghi tehsil is made up of indigenous swatis , gurjars , awans and a mixture of other smaller groups . hindko is spoken as the main language by around 62 % of the people , pashto language by some 30 % and gojri by some 8 % . part of this tehsil is made up of the agror valley which also used to be the estate of the former khan of agror , a famous tribal chieftain .
