Jimmy James     jimmy james oam ( 1913 â€“ 1991 ) was an aboriginal australian tracker . he helped the police of south australia in tracking criminals . he worked with the police for 40 years . james was born near ernabella ( now pukatja ) in central australia , and was a member of the pitjantjatjara people . he went to school at the ooldea mission . in 1945 , he was wrongfully arrested and left the mission . james began his career as a tracker for police in 1948. he became well known for tracking criminals ( including arsonists , poachers and escapees ) and lost persons . he was awarded the medal of the order of australia in 1984 and was the first south australian aboriginal person of the year in 1983. after his death , a memorial to jimmy james was erected in berri , south australia .
