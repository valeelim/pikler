The Boat Race     the boat race is a rowing race that happens every year in london . it is a race between the boat clubs of cambridge university and oxford university . the crews are made up of students . these two universities have a strong tradition in rowing and a strong rivalry between them . the course is 4 miles long , along the river thames . the first boat race was in 1829 , and it is usually held on the last sunday of march or the first sunday of april . during the world wars , the boat race did not happen . it is a very important sporting event in the united kingdom . millions of people watch it on television . in 2009 , more than 250,000 people watched it along the river . as of 2013 , cambridge has won the race 81 times and oxford 77 .
