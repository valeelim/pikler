Sirte     sirte ( also spelled `` sirt '' , `` surt '' , `` sert '' or `` syrte '' , pronounced ; ; from ) is a city in libya . it is on the south coast of the gulf of sidra . the city was the final major stronghold of gaddafi loyalists in the libyan civil war . gaddafi was killed there by rebel forces on 20 october 2011. it is birthplace of muammar al-gaddafi , former leader of libya .
