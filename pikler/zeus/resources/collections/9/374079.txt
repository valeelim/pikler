John A. Roche     john a. roche ( august 12 , 1844 – february 10 , 1904 ) was an american politician . he was the mayor of chicago , illinois ( 1887–1889 ) . he was a republican . roche was born on august 12 , 1844 in utica , new york . roche died on february 10 , 1904 in chicago from a type of poisoning , aged 59 .
