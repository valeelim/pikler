2013 Football League Cup Final     the 2013 football league cup final is a football match between bradford city and swansea city , which will take place on 24 february 2013 at wembley stadium , london . it is the final match of the 2012–13 football league cup , the 53rd season of the football league cup , a football competition for the 92 teams in the premier league and the football league . the winner of the football league cup will enter the 2013–14 uefa europa league in the third qualifying round .
