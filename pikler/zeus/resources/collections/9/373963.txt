Thomas Dyer     thomas dyer ( january 13 , 1805 – june 6 , 1862 ) was an american politician who served as mayor of chicago , illinois ( 1856–1857 ) for the democratic party . dyer was born on january 13 , 1805 in canton , connecticut . he was raised in chicago , illinois . dyer died on june 6 , 1862 in middletown , connecticut from a heart attack , aged 57. other websites . - inaugural address
