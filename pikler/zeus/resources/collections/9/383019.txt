Astola Island     astola island , also known as `` '' ( ) or 'island of the seven hills ' , is an uninhabited island in the arabian sea . it is part of pakistan , about 25 km south of pakistan 's nearest coastline and 39 km southeast of the fishing port of pasni . astola is pakistan 's largest offshore island and the only major island in the northern arabian sea . it is about large . administratively , the island is part of the pasni subdistrict of gwadar district in balochistan province .
