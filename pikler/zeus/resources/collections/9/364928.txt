Mike Goldberg     mike goldberg ( born november 24 , 1964 ) is a sportscaster for the ufc for fuel tv , fsn , fx , spike tv , and fox . goldberg is a studio host for college football on fsn as well as calling preseason games for the arizona cardinals . he has called nhl games for espn , the phoenix coyotes , detroit red wings , vancouver canucks , and minnesota timberwolves . goldberg also calls play-by-play for the red bull air race world series and is a host/play-by-play for `` shaq vs. '' with shaquille o'neal .
