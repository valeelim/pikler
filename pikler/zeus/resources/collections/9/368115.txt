James Franco     james edward franco ( born april 19 , 1978 ) is an american movie , television , stage , voice actor , movie producer , screenwriter , and movie director who is known for his roles in `` 127 hours '' , `` rise of the planet of the apes '' , `` your highness '' , `` spider-man '' , `` spider-man 2 '' , `` spider-man 3 '' , `` oz the great and powerful '' , and `` the interview '' . he won an golden globe award in 2001. franco was born on april 19 , 1978 in palo alto , california . he studied at the palo alto high school and at university of california , los angeles .
