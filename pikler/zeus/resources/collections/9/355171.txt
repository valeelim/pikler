Bob Knight     robert montgomery `` bob '' knight ( born october 25 1940 ) is a retired american basketball sports coach . he won over 900 ncaa division i men 's college basketball games . knight is well known as the head coach of the indiana hoosiers from 1971-2000. he also coached for the texas tech from 2001-2008. knight was born in massillon , ohio . he studied at ohio state university . he has been praised for having a clean program . none of his teams was ever sanctioned by the ncaa for any recruitment violations . he has , however , attracted some controversy for throwing a chair across the courts during a game . he was once arrested for assault . he was fired from indiana in mid september of 2000. in april 2015 , it was announced that espn would not sign knight 's contract for another season .
