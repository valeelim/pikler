Jason Todd     jason todd is a fictional batman character who was the second robin when the first left . todd was founded by batman when he tried to steal some parts from the batmobile . batman later trained him to become the second robin . he was later killed by the joker when he beat him-up and then blew him up with a time bomb . he later returned as the red hood . other websites . - jason todd at titans tower - red hood ( jason todd ) at the dc database project
