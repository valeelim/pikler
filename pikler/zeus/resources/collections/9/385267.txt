Volaris     volaris is a mexican low-cost airline . the airline is based in mexico city . its largest airline hub is at general abelardo l. rodríguez international airport in tijuana . volaris was founded in 2005. taca airlines and televisa were among the initial shareholders . in 2009 , the airline started flying from mexico to airports in the usa after it began an agreement with southwest airlines . when mexicana de aviacion stopped flying in august 2010 , volaris took over their slots at the mexico city international airport . southwest and volaris stopped their agreement in 2013. in 2015 , volaris started going to guatemala city , guatemala , san juan , puerto rico and san josé , costa rica . as of 2016 , volaris is mexico 's second largest airline , after aeroméxico .
