Immaculate Antbird     the immaculate antbird ( `` myrmeciza immaculata '' ) is a species of antbird in the family thamnophilidae . it is found in forests in costa rica , panama , western and northern colombia , western venezuela , and western ecuador . the species eats insects , and usually follows swarms of army ants to catch prey . however , it may sometimes find its prey away from the swarms . references . - birdlife international 2004. myrmeciza immaculata . 2006 iucn red list of threatened species . downloaded on 26 july 2007 .
