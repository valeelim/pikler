R. Lee Ermey     ronald lee ermey ( born march 24 , 1944 ) is an american actor and voice actor . ermey served with the united states marine corps in vietnam and was retired in 1971. he was the voice of sarge in `` toy story '' , `` toy story 2 '' , and `` toy story 3 '' . ermey was born on march 24 , 1944 in emporia , kansas . ermey has been married to nila ermey since 1975 ; they have 4 children . ermey retired as a staff sergeant but was later given the honorary rank of gunnery sergeant . other websites . - official website for `` r. lee ermey ''
