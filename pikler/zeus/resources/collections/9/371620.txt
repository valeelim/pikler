Andrew Ebbett     andrew ebbett ( born january 2 , 1983 in vernon , british columbia ) is a canadian nhl ice hockey left winger who currently plays for the pittsburgh penguins . ebbett has also played 3 seasons with the anaheim ducks , 1 season with the chicago blackhawks , 1 season with the minnesota wild and 1 season with the phoenix coyotes . he played college hockey with the university of michigan for 4 seasons before entering the nhl . he signed a one-year contract worth $ 525,000 with the vancouver canucks on july 1 , 2011 .
