Wattieza     wattieza is the earliest known tree . their fossils were found in new york state . they stood 8 m ( 34 ft ) or more tall , and looked like the modern tree ferns . they lived in the mid-devonian , about 385 million years ago . they were cladoxylopsids , close relatives of modern ferns and horsetails . the crown of `` wattieza '' , discovered in 2005 , was united with its root and trunk , known since 1870. the fossilized grove of `` gilboa stumps '' had been described as `` eospermatopteris '' , though the complete plant remained unknown . `` wattieza '' had fronds rather than leaves , and reproduced with spores . references . - cnn.com : oldest tree had fronds , not leaves .
