Andrew Bogut     andrew michael bogut ( born 28 november 1984 ) is an australian professional basketball player . he plays for the golden state warriors of the national basketball association ( nba ) . he is 7 feet tall . he went to the university of utah for college . bogut was selected first overall by the milwaukee bucks in the 2005 nba draft . bogut is the first australian player to be drafted number one overall . he was also the second utah player to be drafted number one overall . on march 13 , 2012 , bogut , along with stephen jackson , was traded to the golden state warriors in exchange for monta ellis , ekpe udoh and kwame brown . other websites . - stats at basketball-reference.com
