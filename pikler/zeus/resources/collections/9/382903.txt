Mayor West     mayor adam west is a character for the television comedy `` family guy '' . he is voiced and based on the actor adam west . he first appeared as the crazy mayor of quahog , rhode island in `` fifteen minutes of shame '' . he usually never takes things seriously and only cares about his office . he is married to carol west , lois griffin 's sister . the fate of the character is currently unknown following adam west 's death in june 2017 .
