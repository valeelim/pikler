Georges Appert     georges appert ( 1850-1934 ) was a french historian , writer and japan studies expert . he was a professor of law at the university of tokyo . appert was a government advisor in japan from 1879 to 1889. in an overview of writings by and about appert , oclc/worldcat lists roughly 26 works in 43 publications in 5 languages and 132 library holdings . some of the works are : - `` ancien japon , '' 1888 - `` du terme en droit romain et en droit français , '' 1876 - `` dictionnaire des termes de droit , d'économie politique et d'administration , '' 1885 - 日本立法資料全集 ( `` japanese legislation complete works '' ) . 別卷 337 , 佛國相續法講義 , 2005 - `` un code de la féodalité japonaise au xiiie siècle , '' 1900 - 経済学講義 ( `` economics lecture '' ) , 1883 related pages . - foreign advisors to the government in meiji period japan
