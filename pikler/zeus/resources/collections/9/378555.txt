Joanna Cassidy     joanna cassidy ( born joanna victoria caskey , august 2 , 1945 in haddonfield , new jersey ) is an american actress . she acted in the tv series `` six feet under '' and `` body of proof '' . she acted in many movies . these include `` blade runner '' ( 1982 ) , `` where the heart is '' and `` who framed roger rabbit ? `` .
