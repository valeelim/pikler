Dave Pasch     dave pasch ( b . 1972 ) is an american sportscaster . pasch works for espn . he calls play-by-play of college football , college basketball , nba , wnba , and arena football games . he has also been the radio voice for the arizona cardinals of the nfl since 2002 , teaming with ron wolfley . in 2007 , pasch called the little league world series . references . - dave pasch 's espn bio
