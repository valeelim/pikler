Melanie Brown     melanie brown ( born 29 may 1975 ) is a british singer . she is best known as a member of the spice girls . she became known for her big frizzy hair during the groups early years and her nickname was scary spice . she has also released two solo albums . `` hot '' was released in 2000 . `` i want you back '' was released as a single from the album . missy elliott rapped in it . it reached number one in the uk . brown 's second album `` l.a. state of mind '' was released in 2005. in 1997 brown acted in `` spice world '' . she won the razzie award for worst actress . the spice girls started in 1994. the band released three albums . they broke up in 2000. in 2007 the spice girls did a tour called the return of the spice girls . she was born in harehills , leeds , west yorkshire to an english mother and nevisian father . she grew up working class in burley , leeds . she has three daughters .
