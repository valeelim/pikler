Brian Cox     brian denis cox , cbe ( born 1 june 1946 ) is a scottish movie , television , stage , and voice actor who is known for his roles in `` manhunter '' , `` rushmore '' , `` x2 '' , `` troy '' , and in `` red '' . he has won an emmy award and an bafta award . cox was born on june 1 , 1946 in dundee , scotland . he studied at the dundee repertory theatre and at the london academy of music and dramatic art . cox was married to caroline burt from 1968 until they divorced in 1986. he married nicole ansari in 2002. with burt , he had four children , two who were stillborn . with ansari , he has two sons .
