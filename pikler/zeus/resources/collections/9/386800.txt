List of India Test cricketers     this is a list of test cricketers who have played for india . a test match is an international cricket match between two of the leading cricketing nations . the list is arranged in the order in which each player won his test cap . where more than one player won his first test cap in the same test match , those players are listed alphabetically by surname . a total of 31 players have been the captain of the indian test team so far . mahendra singh dhoni has the highest win rate ( 49.99 % ) , followed by sourav ganguly ( 42.85 % ) . sourav ganguly captained the team for most number of test matches ( 49 ) . iftikhar ali khan pataudi is the youngest captain for india in test matches . other websites . - howstat
