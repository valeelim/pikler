Louis Theroux     louis sebastian theroux ( born may 20 , 1970 ) is an english-american broadcaster . he is best known for his investigative series such as when louis met , louis theroux 's weird weekends , louis theroux 's bbc two specials . theroux was born in singapore and is a dual citizen . he moved to london when he was a year old . he was educated at westminster school and magdalen college , oxford where he gained a degree in history . in 2013 , he moved to los angeles . theroux 's mother is british ; his father is american writer paul theroux .
