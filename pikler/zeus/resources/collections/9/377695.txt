Hans Ferdinand Linskens     hans ferdinand linskens ( lahr , germany , may 22 , 1921-august 13 , 2007 ) was a german scientist . he did research in botany and genetics . he was professor of botany at the radboud university nijmegen from 1957 to 1986. linskens was the editor-in-chief of the scientific journals `` theoretical and applied genetics '' ( 1977 to 1987 ) and `` sexual plant reproduction '' . he was also an influential editor of handbooks . linskens was an elected member of the deutsche akademie der naturforscher leopoldina , linnean society of london , koninklijke nederlandse akademie van wetenschappen , and the acad√©mie royale des sciences de belgique .
