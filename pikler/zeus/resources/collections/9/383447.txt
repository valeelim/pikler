Artemision Zeus     the artemision zeus is a greek bronze statue . it dates to the early classical period , ca . 460 bc . it stands 2.09 m in height . it was found in the debris of a shipwreck near cape artemision in 1926-1928. it is in the national museum , athens . there has been some debate whether the statue represents zeus or poseidon . caroline houser believes it is unlikely the statue is poseidon because his trident would obscure the statue 's face .
