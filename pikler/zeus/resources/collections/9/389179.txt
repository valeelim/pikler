Cousins (movie)    cousins ( movie ) cousins is a 1989 american romantic comedy movie . it was directed by joel schumacher . it is about step-cousins who have an affair . it is a remake of french movie cousin cousine . the movie stars ted danson as larry , sean young as tish and isabella rossellini as maria hardy . this movie was released in february 1989. it got mixed to high reviews from critics .
