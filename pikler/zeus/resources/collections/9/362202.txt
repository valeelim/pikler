Paul Bettany     paul bettany ( born may 27 , 1971 ) is an english movie , voice , television , and stage actor . he voiced j.a.r.v.i.s . in the movies `` iron man '' , `` iron man 2 '' and `` the avengers '' . he will also voice the character in `` iron man 3 '' . bettany was born in shepherd 's bush , london , england . he has been married to jennifer connelly since 2003. they have three children .
