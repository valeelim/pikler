Siphonops leucoderus     the siphonops leucoderus is a species of caecilians . they belong to the family caeciliidae . they live in brazil . their habitat are subtropical or tropical forests . they like to live in lowland forests and rural areas . references . - silvano , d. & pimenta , b . 2004. siphonops leucoderus . 2006 iucn red list of threatened species . downloaded on 21 july 2007 .
