Liz Smith (journalist)    liz smith ( journalist ) mary elizabeth `` liz '' smith ( born february 2 , 1923 ) is an american gossip columnist . she is known as the grand dame of dish . she was a journalist for the new york daily times . smith was born on february 2 , 1923 in fort worth , texas . she was raised in new york city . smith studied at the university of texas at austin . she worked for five years at cbs radio with mike wallace . she was married to george edward until they divorced . other websites . - copy of transcript of her interview with cnn 's larry king
