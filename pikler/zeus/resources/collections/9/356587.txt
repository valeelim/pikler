Theresa Russell     theresa russell ( born march 20 , 1957 in san diego ) is an american actress . she has done many movies . some of the better-known ones are `` the last tycoon '' ( 1976 ) , `` black widow '' ( 1987 ) and `` spider-man 3 '' ( 2007 ) . russell was married to british movie director nicolas roeg ( born 1928 ) from 1982 until they divorced .
