Suicidal ideation     suicidal ideation is the medical terminology for thoughts of or preoccupation with killing yourself . it varies between fleeting thoughts and self-harm or making suicidal attempts . most people who undergo suicidal ideation never really kill themselves , though some do . this condition is often triggered through being hopeless , depressed , anxiety , having panic attacks or strong remorse . it can also happen because of being abused , as a result of certain medications , due to bullying or feeling rejected .
