Skin tag     a skin tag is a small growth on the skin that is usually benign ( harmless ) . it may have a short narrow stalk which connects it to the skin . they are usually small but may grow up to a half-inch long . skin tags are usually harmless and do not need treatment . a person may decide to have a skin tag removed if it is irritating them or they do not like the way it looks . if so the skin tag may be removed by : - surgery : removed using a scalpel or surgical scissors . - cryotherapy : freezing it . - cauterization : burning it off .
