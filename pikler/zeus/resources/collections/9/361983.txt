Tom McCarthy (broadcaster)    tom mccarthy ( broadcaster ) tom mccarthy ( born july 5 , 1968 , in jersey city , new jersey ) is an american sportscaster . he works with the philadelphia phillies . he also called games for the new york mets . he is a fill-in voice for fox saturday baseball . he was the voice of the aa baseball trenton thunder for six seasons . outside of baseball , mccarthy has called college football and basketball games for cbs college sports , sports usa , westwood one , and the atlantic-10 tv . he has been the voice of rutgers scarlet knights football and for princeton tigers football and basketball . in addition to baseball and college sports , he called nfl games for sports usa until 2012. he is one of dial global 's voices for nfl coverage .
