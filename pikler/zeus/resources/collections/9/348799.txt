Byron, Georgia    byron , georgia byron is a city in peach county , georgia , united states . a small part of the city goes into houston county . the population was 2,887 at the 2000 census . byron is home to the middle georgia raceway . the racetrack was host to the atlanta international pop festival in 1970. the festival was the largest gathering in georgia history until the 1996 olympics in atlanta . byron is 90 miles south of atlanta , 16 miles south of macon and 10 miles from the peach county seat of fort valley .
