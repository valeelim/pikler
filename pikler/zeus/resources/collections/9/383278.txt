Self Portrait (Van Gogh)    self portrait ( van gogh ) `` a self-drawn ( or self-taken ) picture of an artist is also called self portrait '' self portait is an oil painting by vincent van gogh . it measures 57.2 x 43.8 cm ( 22 1/2 x 17 1/4 in . ) it was painted in 1889. it hangs in the national gallery of art , washington dc . it is one of van gogh 's last self portraits .
