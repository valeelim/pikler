Django Unchained     django unchained is a 2012 american western movie directed by quentin tarantino . it is set in 1858. it stars jamie foxx as django , christoph waltz as dr. king schultz , and leonardo dicaprio as calvin . this comedy-drama is about the deep south . there has been controversy related to its subject matter . `` django unchained '' was released on christmas 2012 in the united states .
