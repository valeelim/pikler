Air Force Instruction     an air force instruction ( afi ) is a documented instruction for members of the united states air force . it is intended for use by active duty , guard , and reserve members and associated civilians . it is one of many forms of directives published by the air force departmental publishing office ( afdpo ) . in almost all cases , an air force instruction is a form of a general order ; and violation of the afi by an airman subject to it can be punished under the uniform code of military justice .
