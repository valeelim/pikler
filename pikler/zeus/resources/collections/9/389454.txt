Brent Scowcroft     brent scowcroft ( born march 19 , 1925 ) is a retired united states air force lieutenant general . he was the united states national security advisor under u.s. presidents gerald ford and george h. w. bush . scowcroft was born on march 19 , 1925 in ogden , utah . he studied at columbia university . scowcroft has been married to marian horner since 1951. they had one daughter . other websites . - `` do n't attack saddam '' by brent scowcroft . - brent scowcroft speech to harvard kennedy school , october 29 , 2008 . - brent scowcroft résumé . - oral history of brent scowcroft at the west point center for oral history .
