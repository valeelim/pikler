Christopher Guest     christopher haden-guest , 5th baron haden-guest ( born february 5 , 1948 ) , better known as christopher guest , is an english-american screenwriter , composer , musician , movie producer and director , movie , television , stage , and voice actor , and comedian . he is known for his works in `` this is spinal tap '' , `` the princess bride '' , `` best in show '' , `` a mighty wind '' , and in `` for your consideration '' . he has won an emmy award . guest was born on february 5 , 1948 in new york city , new york . he studied at the high school of music & art and at new york university . guest is married to jamie lee curtis since 1984. they have two adopted children .
