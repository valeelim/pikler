OpenSolaris     opensolaris was an open source unix operating system from sun microsystems . it was based based sun 's non-free solaris operating system . the operating system was known for its zfs file system . in 2010 , oracle corporation bought sun microsystems and chose to stop making new versions of opensolaris . opensolaris was later forked into the illumos kernel and the openindiana distribution . opensolaris ( archived ) < br > openindiana ( a fork of opensolaris )
