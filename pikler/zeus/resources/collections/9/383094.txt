Zumbl     zumbl is an online chat service where users can converse with strangers anonymously . the users have to use facebook or google based login , following which they choose an username and an avatar for themselves . they can add topics they are interested in , join communities such as `` entrepreneurs '' , `` love collecting coins '' , etc . at this step , they can connect themselves with strangers and have meaningful conversations with them . users ‘ tag ’ each other on the basis of their conversations , which are indicative of their personality and get reflected in their avatars .
