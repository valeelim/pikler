Bob Papa     bob papa ( born september 19 , 1964 ) is an american sportscaster . he works for the new york giants , golf channel , and hbo as play-by-play for `` boxing after dark '' . he also calls boxing during the olympics . papa did the same work for boxing for nbc , espn , and sportschannel america . he is the host of `` opening drive '' . papa was the studio host for giants broadcasts before going to the booth and called `` thursday night football '' games on nflnetwork . he also called bcs games and college football games for versus . he was one of the members of `` afl on nbc '' as well as the nfl on westwood one . he was the host of `` giants online '' and `` giants training camp report '' . papa called college basketball games for westwood as well as the new jersey nets . he also called winter olympics for nbc . papa was the studio host for nhl on sportschannel america as well as calling the all american bowl for nbc .
