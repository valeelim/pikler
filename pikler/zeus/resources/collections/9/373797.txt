Sadao Munemori     sadao munemori ( born august 17 , 1922-april 5 , 1945 ) was a united states army soldier . he received the medal of honor because of his actions in world war ii . munemori was born in los angeles , california to japanese immigrant parents . he is a `` nisei '' , which means that he is a second generation japanese-american . during world war ii , munemori 's parents and siblings were interned at the manzanar concentration camp . two months after the japanese attack on pearl harbor , he joined the us army . munemori volunteered to be part of the all- '' nisei '' 100th infantry battalion . this army unit was mostly made up of japanese americans from hawaii and the mainland . for his actions in april 1945 , he was the only japanese american to be awarded the medal of honor during or immediately after world war ii . munmori 's medal of honor recognized his conduct in fighting in northern italy in 1945. without help from others , he attacked two machine guns before smothering a grenade blast with his body .
