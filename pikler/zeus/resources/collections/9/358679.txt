Parupalli Kashyap     parupalli kashyap ( ) ( born 8 september 1986 ) , also known as kashyap parupalli , is an indian athlete . he is best known as a badminton player who competed in the summer olympic games . parupalli trained at hyderabad . in the 2012 summer olympics at london , parupalli reached quarter-finals in the badminton tournament . other websites . - kashyap parupalli at london2012.com
