Family Fortunes     family fortunes is a british television game show . it is based on the american game show `` family feud '' . it has aired in various forms since 1980. the current version is called all star family fortunes and is hosted by vernon kay . it airs saturday nights on itv . two teams of five family members compete to guess the answers to survey questions asked to 100 people . the answers are seen on a huge survey board , named mr. babbage after charles babbage , who came up with the idea of a computer that could be programmed . each answer is worth £1 per point . if a given answer does not make the survey , the family gets a strike . this is indicated by an `` eh-uh '' sound effect , which has become a popular element of the show . if a family gets three strikes , the other family can steal the points by giving an answer that makes the survey . after three rounds , double money is played . here , answers are worth £2 a point , and there are usually fewer answers . the first team to reach 300 points goes on to play big money .
