Ba'ath Party (Iraqi-dominated faction)    ba'ath party ( iraqi-dominated faction ) the arab socialist ba'ath party ( also spelled ba'th or baath , `` resurrection '' or `` renaissance '' ; `` hizb al-ba'ath al-'arabi al-ishtiraki '' ) , also called the pro-iraqi ba'ath movement , is a ba'athist political party headquartered in baghdad , iraq . it is one of two parties which formed in 1966 after split of the original ba'ath party .
