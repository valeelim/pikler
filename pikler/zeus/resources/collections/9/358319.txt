Crash Nitro Kart     crash nitro kart is a 2003 racing video game developed by vicarious visions and published by universal interactive studios ( along with konami in the japanese release ) for the playstation 2 , nintendo gamecube , xbox , game boy advance and n-gage . it is the second `` crash bandicoot '' racing game behind `` crash team racing '' . it is the ninth `` crash bandicoot '' game overall . other websites . - activision 's page on `` crash nitro kart ''
