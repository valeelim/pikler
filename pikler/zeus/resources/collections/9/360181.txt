The Mutations     the mutations is a 1974 british horror movie . it was directed by jack cardiff . the movie was also released under the title , `` the freakmaker '' . in the movie a scientist takes college students to use as human guinea pigs . he uses them in his experiments in crossbreeding plants with humans . the failed experimental mutants are then given to a cruel circus freakshow owner . cast . - donald pleasence - professor nolter - tom baker - lynch - brad harris - brian redford - julie ege - hedi - michael dunn - burns - scott antony - tony - jill haworth - lauren - olga anthony - bridget - lisa collings - prostitute - joan scott - landlady - toby lennon - tramp - john wireford - policeman - eithne dunne - nurse
