Amour (movie)    amour ( movie ) amour is an 2012 academy award-winning french movie . the director was michael haneke . it starred jean-louis trintignant as georges , emmanuelle riva as anne , and isabelle huppert as their daughter . the movie was about an elderly couple , anne and georges , who are retired music teachers who live alone together . anne suffers a stroke which paralyses her on one side of her body . after she makes georges very angry , georges grabs a pillow and suffocates anne to death . it received many positive reviews . roger ebert gave it four out of four stars . it won an golden globe award and an academy award for best foreign film .
