Joseph Maher     joseph maher ( december 29 1933 - july 17 1998 ) was an irish-american movie , television , voice , and stage actor . he is best known from his acting in the television series `` goode behavior '' and the movies `` heaven can wait '' , `` mars attacks '' and `` sister act '' . he played the character mr. chambers in 1984 short `` frankenweenie '' . maher won a drama desk award in 1986. maher was born in westport , county mayo , ireland on december 29 , 1933. maher was openly gay . he was a naturalized us citizen . maher died on july 17 , 1998 in los angeles , california from a brain tumor . he was 64 years old when he died . other websites . - photo
