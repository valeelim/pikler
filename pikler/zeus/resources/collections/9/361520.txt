Yehoshua Sofer     yehoshua sofer ( ) is an israeli hip hop musician and martial artist . he was born in 1958 as nigel wilson in jamaica . his family moved to los angeles in 1963. there , he studied tang soo do and got a black belt by 1968 ; at that time , he was just ten . he studied kuk sool won from 1974 , and worked as a trainer and bodyguard during the 1970s and 1980s . other websites . - abir warriors
