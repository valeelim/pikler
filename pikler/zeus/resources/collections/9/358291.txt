Al Molinaro     albert francis `` al '' molinaro ( june 24 , 1919 – october 30 , 2015 ) was an american television and movie actor , who is mostly known as al delvecchio , the owner of arnold 's on `` happy days '' and its spin-off show `` joanie loves chachi '' . he is also known for his roles in `` the odd couple '' television series , as well as starring in commercials for on-cor frozen dinners . he also starred in a 1982 comedy `` the ugily family '' . molinaro retired from acting in 1992. molinaro was born in kenosha , wisconsin on june 24 , 1919 to an italian family . molinaro was married to sidney molinaro until they divorced . he has been married to betty farrell since 1981. with sidney he has a son michael molinaro . molinaro died in glendale , california from a gallbladder infection , aged 96. movies . - `` freaky friday '' ( 1976 ) - `` the great american traffic jam '' ( 1980 ) television . - `` get smart '' ( 1969 – 1970 ) - `` the odd couple '' ( 1970 – 1975 ) - `` happy days '' ( 1976 – 1982 ) - `` joanie loves chachi '' ( 1982 – 1983 ) - `` the ugily family '' ( 1982 )
