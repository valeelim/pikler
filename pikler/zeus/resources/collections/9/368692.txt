1800 United States Census     the united states census of 1800 was the second census in the united states . it was done on august 4 , 1800. it showed that 5,308,483 people were living in the united states . 893,602 were slaves . the 1800 census included the new district of columbia . the census for the following states were lost : georgia , kentucky , new jersey , tennessee , and virginia .
