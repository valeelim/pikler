David Duvall Orr     david duvall orr ( born october 4 , 1944 ) is an american democratic politician from chicago , illinois . he was the mayor of chicago from november 25 , 1987 through december 2 , 1987. he became the mayor after the death of harold washington . orr was born on october 4 , 1944 in chicago , illinois . he was married to loretta lim . they had four children . orr now lives in chicago , illinois .
