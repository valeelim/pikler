Belgae     the belgae were a group of tribes living in northern gaul . they were on the west bank of the rhine , in the 3rd century bc . later they were also in britain , and possibly even ireland . they gave their name to the roman province of gallia belgica , and very much later , to the modern country of belgium . the belgae came to britain as raiders and settled in southern britain shortly before the romans came . they occupied the area later called hampshire .
