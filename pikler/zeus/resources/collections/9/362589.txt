Dave O'Brien     dave o'brien is an american sportscaster for the boston red sox radio network and espn . he is an announcer for college basketball games and major league baseball games . he was the play-by-play announcer for the atlanta braves , georgia bulldogs football and basketball , miami hurricanes football and basketball , florida marlins , and new york mets . he also called nba games and mlb on espn radio postseason games as well . he also called the fifa world cup , major league soccer , and the national basketball association for espn .
