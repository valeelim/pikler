Chris Schenkel     christopher eugene `` chris '' schenkel ( august 21 , 1923 â€“ september 11 , 2005 ) was an american sportscaster . in 1947 , he called the first american football game ever broadcast on television . it was a harvard-army game . in 1952 he began calling new york giants games . in 1956 , he began to also call boxing , the triple crown horse racing , and the masters golf tournament , and other events . along with chuck thompson , schenkel called the 1958 nfl championship game . abc sports hired schenkel in 1965. there he broadcast college football , major league baseball , nba basketball , golf and tennis tournaments , boxing , auto racing , and the summer and winter olympic games . he also had a long-term assignment covering professional bowling , mainly for the professional bowlers association . schenkel was named national sportscaster of the year four times . in 1992 he received a lifetime achievement emmy . also in 1992 , the pro football hall of fame gave schenkel its pete rozelle radio-television award . in 1999 , he received the jim thorpe lifetime achievement award .
