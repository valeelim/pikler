Jemima Shore     jemima shore is a fictional character created by antonia fraser . she is portrayed as tv 's professional investigative journalist . she is in a series of crime novels . novels . - `` quiet as a nun '' ( 1977 ) - `` the wild island '' ( 1978 ) - `` a splash of red '' ( 1981 ) - `` cool repentance '' ( 1982 ) - `` oxford blood '' ( 1985 ) - `` jemima shore 's first case '' ( 1986 ) - `` your royal hostage '' ( 1987 ) - `` the cavalier case '' ( 1990 ) - `` jemima shore at the sunny grave '' ( 1991 ) - `` political death '' ( 1995 ) shore has also been the basis of two television series which aired in the uk : the 1978 `` armchair thriller '' serial `` quiet as a nun '' with maria aitken as jemima shore , and an ongoing series , `` jemima shore investigates '' , starring patricia hodge in the title role . the series was made by thames television for the itv network in 1983. one series of twelve episodes was made .
