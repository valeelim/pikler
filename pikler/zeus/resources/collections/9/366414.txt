Emma (wrestler)    emma ( wrestler ) tenille dashwood ( born 1 march 1989 ) is an australian professional wrestler and model . she is better known by her ring name tenille tayla . she works for the wwe . dashwood currently wrestles in its developmental territory nxt wrestling under the ring name emma . on july 2 , 2014 , emma was released by wwe after she was arrested for sixth-degree larceny . she was reinstated by wwe two hours later . on june 30 , 2014 , before monday night raw aired , dashwood was arrested in hartford , connecticut for sixth-degree larceny . dashwood 's lawyer said that she had forgotten to pay for an ipad case , when she was using the self-checkout machine at a walmart . after she appeared at a community court on july 1 , she was ordered to do one day of community service and complete an online course . championships . - extreme canadian championship wrestling - eccw women 's supergirls championship ( two times ) other websites . - official website
