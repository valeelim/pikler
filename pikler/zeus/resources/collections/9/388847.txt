Science (journal)    science ( journal ) science is one of the world 's top science journals . it is also referred to as `` science magazine '' . it is published weekly , and a peer-reviewed journal . the publisher is the american association for the advancement of science . `` science '' was founded by john michaels in 1880 with financial support from thomas edison and later from alexander graham bell . in june 2013 , marcia mcnutt became the editor . other websites . - magazine website
