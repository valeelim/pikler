Pokémon Black 2 and White 2     pokémon black 2 and white 2 are two role-playing video games in the `` pokémon '' series made by game freak for the nintendo ds game console . the games are the sequels to `` pokémon black and white '' . the games were released in japan on june 23 , 2012 , in north america on october 7 , 2012 , and in europe on october 12 , 2012 . `` pokémon black 2 '' and `` white 2 '' play the same as previous games in the series . unlike in `` black '' and `` white '' , players may now capture pokémon from previous games from the start . `` pokémon black 2 '' and `` white 2 '' follows a new pokémon trainer who is on a journey to become a pokémon master . they do this by catching and training pokémon , and battling pokémon trainers . it takes place in the unova region two years after the events of `` black '' and `` white '' . during this time , there have been many changes . changes include new areas to explore , new gym leaders , and new trainers to battle . there is also a new team plasma that was formed after the original team was defeated . the new team plasma aims to use pokémon to take over unova . during the player 's journey , they will meet former members of the original team plasma . these members now use pokémon for good , and help the player in their journey .
