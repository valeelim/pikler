Horrible Bosses     horrible bosses is a 2011 american black comedy movie . it was directed by seth gordon . the story is about sexual harassment , corrupt bosses and plots to murder . jason bateman , jason sudeikis , charlie day , kevin spacey , jennifer aniston , colin farrell , jamie foxx , donald sutherland , and bob newhart star in the movie . the movie was released in june-july 2011. it received positive reviews . it made less than $ 210 million worldwide . `` horrible bosses '' is 98 minutes long . a sequel , `` horrible bosses 2 '' , was released on november 28 , 2014 .
