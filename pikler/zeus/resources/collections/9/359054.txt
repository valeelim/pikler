Kristen Bell     kristen anne bell ( born july 18 , 1980 ) is an american television , voice , stage , and movie actress . she appeared in `` forgetting sarah marshall '' , `` veronica mars '' , `` when in rome '' , `` spartan '' , `` gossip girl '' , `` polish wedding '' , and in the 2012 comedy `` hit and run '' . she was born in huntington woods , michigan . she and dax shepard have been partners since 2007. in november 2012 , the couple announced that they were expecting a child . their daughter , lincoln bell shepard was born on march 28 , 2013 .
