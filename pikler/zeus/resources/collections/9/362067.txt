Thom Brennaman     thomas wade `` thom '' brennaman ( born september 12 , 1963 ) is an american sportscaster . he calls games for the cincinnati reds and mlb on fox . he also called games for the arizona diamondbacks and the chicago cubs . he is a play by play announcer for the nfl on fox and fills in for joe buck . he was the lead announcer for the bcs on fox and college football on the big ten network . he has also called college basketball games as well . he called games for fsn and cbs radio and called cotton bowl games from 2000-2006. he is the son of radio announcer marty brennaman . thom has called basketball games for the university of cincinnati .
