Birdy (musician)    birdy ( musician ) jasmine van den bogaerde ( born 15 may 1996 ) , also known by her stage name birdy , is an english musician . she was born in lymington , hampshire . she won the music competition open mic uk in 2008 , at the age of 12. her version of bon iver 's `` skinny love '' was successful , peaking inside the top twenty of the charts in her native united kingdom and in some other european countries . her eponymous first album `` birdy '' was released on 7 november 2011. the album peaked at no . 13 in the uk and in the top 5 in france , belgium and the netherlands and number 1 in australia . her second album `` fire within `` was released 23 september 2013 in the uk . she is the great-niece of dirk bogarde .
