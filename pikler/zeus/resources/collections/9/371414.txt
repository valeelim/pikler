Doc Severinsen     carl hilding `` doc '' severinsen ( born july 7 , 1927 ) is an american pop and jazz trumpeter . he is best known for leading the nbc orchestra on `` the tonight show starring johnny carson '' . he has won a grammy award . severinsen was born on july 7 , 1927 in arlington , oregon . he was married to evonne nyman from 1964 until they divorced in 1976. he married emily marshall in 1980. he has five children . other websites . - official website - doc severinsen discography - tonight show bio
