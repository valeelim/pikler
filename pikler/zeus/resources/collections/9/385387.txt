Greta Scacchi     greta scacchi ( born 18 february 1960 ) is an italian-australian actress . she acts mostly in movies , although sometimes in television programs . she starred in lots of movies , which include `` white mischief '' and `` presumed innocent '' . she has acted in many other movies as well . scacchi was born greta gracco in milan to an english mother and italian father . since 1995 , she is an australian citizen , and is still an italian citizen .
