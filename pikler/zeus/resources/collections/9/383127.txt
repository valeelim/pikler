Luigi's Mansion: Dark Moon    luigi 's mansion : dark moon luigi 's mansion : dark moon , known as luigi 's mansion 2 in europe , is a video game for the nintendo 3ds console . it is the sequel to `` luigi 's mansion '' for the nintendo gamecube . it was released on march 20 , 2013 in japan and on march 24 , 2013 in north america . when the dark moon shatters , the ghosts in the evershade valley become evil . professor e. gadd has luigi find the scattered pieces of the dark moon to turn the ghosts friendly again . `` luigi 's mansion : dark moon '' features multiple mansions for luigi to explore . six mansions are available . it uses the console 's tilt , or gyro , sensors .
