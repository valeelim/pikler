Kal Bhairab Temple, Brahmanbaria    kal bhairab temple , brahmanbaria kal bhairab temple is a hindu temple , dedicated to the god shiva , in the chittagong division of bangladesh . the temple is famous for the large linga . it is supposed to be the largest in the world . the goddess kali is also worshipped there . the temple dates back to the 19th century . it was damaged by pakistani soldiers during the bangladesh liberation war .
