La Vita Nuova     la vita nuova or vita nova ( in english : `` the new life '' ) is a book written by dante alighieri in 1295. it is an expression of the medieval courtly love and it is written in a `` prosimetrum '' style , a combination of both prose and verse . it tells the story of his love for beatrice portinari , who also served as the ultimate symbol of salvation in `` the divine comedy '' .
