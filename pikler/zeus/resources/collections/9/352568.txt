Calocochlia pan     calocochlia pan is a species of large air-breathing land snail , a pulmonate gastropod mollusk in the family bradybaenidae . this species is very common in the philippines . the shell is between 41 and 47 millimeters long with brown spiral bands and a cream-colored background . other websites . - animal life forms : `` calocochlia pan '' - biolib : '' calocochlia pan '' w. j. broderip , 1841 - zipcodezoo.com : `` calocochlia pan ''
