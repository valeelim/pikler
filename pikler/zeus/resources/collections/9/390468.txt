Furuset Forum     furuset forum is a sports arena in oslo , norway . the seating capacity of the arena is 1,498 people . it was built in 1979. the main sport played on it is ice hockey . it is also used by other sports for training and events . these include handball and floorball . it is near the metro which makes it popular and easy to get to . the arena has disabled parking spots , food and drink facilities . it is the home of the furuset ice hockey team .
