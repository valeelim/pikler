Thaddeus Stevens     thaddeus stevens ( april 4 , 1792 â€“ august 11 , 1868 ) was an american republican leader . he was one of the most powerful members of the united states house of representatives from 1849 until his death in 1868. he was chairman of the powerful house ways and means committee . stevens was a witty , sarcastic speaker and confident party leader . he dominated the house from 1861 until his death . stevens helped create the 14th amendment to the united states constitution . he also helped with the act that helped the reconstruction of the united states . he wrote much of the financial legislation that paid for the american civil war . stevens and senator charles sumner were the prime leaders of the radical republicans during the war and reconstruction era . he worked and supported abraham lincoln 's plan to end slavery and the u.s. reconstruction plan . he was part of andrew johnson 's impeachment . stevens was born in danville , vermont on april 4 , 1792. he went to dartmouth college and the university of vermont . stevens was never married and had no children . he was in a relationship with his black housekeeper even when slavery was legal . he wore a wig because he was bald .
