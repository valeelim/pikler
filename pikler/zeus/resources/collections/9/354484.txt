Aaron Eckhart     aaron edward eckhart ( born march 22 , 1968 ) is an american movie , television , stage , and voice actor . eckhart became known world-wide in such films as in `` erin brockovich '' ( 2000 ) , and gained a golden globe award nomination for best actor for his role in `` thank you for smoking '' ( 2006 ) . he is also known for his role as two-face in the 2008 summer blockbuster `` the dark kinght '' ( 2008 ) . eckhart alongside nicole kidman worked in a movie called `` rabbit hole '' ( 2010 ) eckhart was born in cupertino , california on march 22 , 1968. he was raised in los angeles , california . eckhart studied at the american community school and at the american international school of sydney . he was once engaged to actress emily cline . they separated in 1998. eckhart now lives in beverly hills , california .
