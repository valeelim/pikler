Corozal District     corozal district is the northernmost district of the nation of belize . the population was 33,335 as of 2000. the district capital is corozal town . the island of ambergris caye is close to corozal district , but it is a part of belize district . pre-columbian maya ruins are found in corozal at santa rita near corozal town , and at cerros . spanish is the most spoken language in corozal , followed by english . 80 % speak spanish very well and 60 % speak english very well .
