Poás Volcano National Park     poás volcano national park , in spanish , is a national park in costa rica . the volcano is located in the province of alajuela . the main crater is deep and is active . there are frequent small geyser and lava eruptions . the last major eruptions were during 1952-54. the park is frequently closed because of sulphur gas . over the last decade there have been indications that the volcano is slowly building up for a new eruption .
