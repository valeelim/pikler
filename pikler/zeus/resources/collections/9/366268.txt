Mike Rowe     michael gregory `` mike '' rowe ( born march 18 , 1962 ) is an american actor , singer , television host , and narrator . he is best known for his voice-over work on commercials and as the host of the television program `` dirty jobs '' . rowe has acted on television , stage and movies as well as radio . he does a large amount of work as a narrator and has even been an opera singer . rowe was born on march 18 , 1962 in baltimore , maryland . he studied at overlea high school , at essex community college , and at towson state university . he now lives in san francisco , california . other websites . - mike rowe 's official website
