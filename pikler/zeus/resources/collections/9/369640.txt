Dundalk F.C.    dundalk f.c . dundalk football club ( ) is a professional irish football club based in dundalk , county louth . the club currently play in the premier division of the league of ireland . founded in 1903 , they are the second most successful team , in terms of trophies won , in the history of the league of ireland . the traditional colours of the club are white jerseys with black shorts . due to their white jerseys , they have been nicknamed the lilywhites . they play their home games in oriel park .
