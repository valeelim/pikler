Leaving Las Vegas     leaving las vegas is a 1995 american romantic drama movie . it is about a depressed alcoholic screenwriter , ben sanderson , who moves to las vegas where he meets a prostitute , sera , whom he moves in with . elizabeth shue and nicolas cage are part of the cast . the movie was released october 27 , 1995 in limited release and nationwide in the united states in 1996. the movie received positive reviews . it was successful in the box office .
