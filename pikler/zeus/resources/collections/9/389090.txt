Debra Bowen     debra lynn bowen ( born october 27 , 1955 ) , is an american politician . she is a member of the democratic party . she was the secretary of state of california from 2007 to 2015. before that , she was a member of the california state legislature from 1992 to 2006. in march 2008 , she was given the profile in courage award by the john f. kennedy presidential library and museum . bowen was unable to run for a third term in 2014 because of term limits .
