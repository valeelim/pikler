Pooh's Grand Adventure: The Search for Christopher Robin    pooh 's grand adventure : the search for christopher robin pooh 's grand adventure : the search for christopher robin ( also known as winnie the pooh 's most grand adventure in some countries ) is a 1997 direct-to-video animated movie . it is a sequel to walt disney 's `` the many adventures of winnie the pooh '' . the movie follows pooh and his friends on a trip to find and rescue their friend christopher robin from the `` skull '' . this movie is a new story . it is not based on any of a. a. milne 's pooh stories . because of the movie 's dark themes and pictures , it had mixed to negative reviews . it was the first disney direct-to-video movie . it is also the first winnie the pooh movie ever to have its own special edition . `` forever and ever '' was later sung by carly simon for `` piglet 's big movie '' . - `` forever and ever '' , performed by jim cummings and frankie j. galasso - `` adventure is a wonderful thing '' , performed by andre stojka - `` if it says so , so it is '' , performed by ken sansom - `` wherever you are '' , performed by jim cummings
