Doyle's Delight    doyle 's delight doyle 's delight is the highest peak in belize . it is 1124 m high . the name doyle 's delight was first used by sharon matola in a 1989 report . the name is based on sir arthur conan doyle 's book `` the lost world '' ( 1912 ) . the book has the quote `` there must be something wild and wonderful in a country such as this , and we 're the men to find it out ! `` .
