Richard Blanco     richard blanco is an american poet . he was born in madrid , spain on february 15 , 1968. his family had just left cuba . then the family went to new york . after that , they moved to miami , florida . he grew up there . blanco graduated from florida international university in civil engineering in 1991. then he got a masters in fine arts in creative writing in 1997. after 1999 , he traveled and lived in guatemala , and brazil . he taught at georgetown university , american university , and central connecticut state university . later , he returned to miami and lived there . now he lives in maine . in january 2013 he read read his poem `` one today '' at the second inauguration ( swearing-in ceremony ) of america 's president barack obama . his work has appeared in some magazines . these include `` the nation '' , `` ploughshares '' , `` indiana review '' , `` michigan quarterly review '' , `` triquarterly review '' , `` new england review '' , and `` americas review '' . awards . - 1997 agnes lynch starrett prize for `` city of a hundred fires ''
