John J. B. Wilson     john j. b. wilson ( born may 24 , 1954 ) is an american copywriter and publicist . he is the founder of the golden raspberry awards . he written many books about how he founded the `` razzie awards '' . wilson was born on may 24 , 1954 in chicago , illinois . he was raised in santa monica , california and in los angeles , california . wilson studied at the university of california , los angeles . he is married to barbara wilson . they have a son . he now lives in cerritos , california .
