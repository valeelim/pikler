The Feminine Mystique     the feminine mystique is a book by betty friedan first published in 1963. it is often credited with starting the second-wave feminism in the united states . in the book , friedan conducts a number of interviews with classmates , and finds that many of them were unhappy with their lives as housewives . friedan had wanted to publish an article about housewives who were unhappy with their role , but no magazine would publish the article . the book has sold more than three million copies .
