Battle of Cambrai     the battle of cambrai was an important battle in the first world war . it took place near cambrai , and lasted from 20th november to 7th december 1917. this battle was the first in the war , where tanks were used on a large scale . cambrai was important because it was a railroad hub . in total , about 95.000 people where killed or captured . the battle did not result in a gain of territory of either side . cambrai lay on the hindenburg line . that part of the hindenburg line between arras and soissons was known as `` siegfried line ''
