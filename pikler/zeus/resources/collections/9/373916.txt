Benjamin Wright Raymond     benjamin wright raymond ( june 15 , 1801 – april 6 , 1883 ) was an american politician who twice served as mayor of chicago , illinois ( 1839–1840 , 1842–1843 ) for the whig party . raymond was born on june 15 , 1801 in rome , new york . he studied at st. lawrence academy . raymond was married to amelia porter from 1835 until his death in 1883. they had a son , george lansing . raymond died on april 6 , 1883 in chicago , illinois from an illness , aged 81. other websites . - 1842 inaugural address
