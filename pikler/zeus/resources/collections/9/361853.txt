Katherine Helmond     katherine marie helmond ( born july 5 , 1929 , galveston , texas ) is an american film , theater and television actress , who portrayed emily dickinson on `` meeting of minds '' , as well as fictional characters , for which she is best-known , such as jessica tate on `` soap '' , mona robinson on `` who 's the boss ? `` , doris sherman on `` coach '' , and lois whelan on `` everybody loves raymond '' , as well as `` jacqueline '' in `` the lorax '' and mrs. lowry in `` brazil '' . helmond was born in galveston , texas on july 5 , 1929. she was married to george n. martin from 1957 until they divorced in 1962. she is still married to david christian since 1962 , she has no children .
