Derek Hough     derek hough ( born may 17 , 1985 in salt lake city , utah ) is an american dancer , choreographer , musician and actor . he has been a professional dancer on `` dancing with the stars '' since fall 2007. he has competed with ricki lake , nicole scherzinger , brooke burke , jennifer grey , maria menounos and shawn johnson . hough grew up in sandy , utah , a suburb of salt lake city , utah . hough 's sister is julianne hough , also a dancer in `` dancing with the stars '' until 2009 .
