Celera     celera is a corporation registered in california , u.s.a. it was founded to discover and sell information about dna sequence analysis , especially the human genome . the company is now wholly owned by quest diagnostics , a corporation which runs clinical laboratory services . celera 's first president , craig venter and hamilton smith were in charge of the original science . there was competition between celera and the publicly funded human genome project , which got much comment . eventually , they agreed on a certain amount of co-operation .
