Boston Navy Yard     the boston navy yard was one of the oldest shipbuilding places in the united states navy . it was first called the charlestown navy yard and later boston naval shipyard . it was started in 1801. it was officially closed as an active naval yard on july 1 , 1974. it still has the ship the `` constitution '' docked there . the uss `` cassin young '' , a world war ii-era destroyer , is there and is a museum ship . other websites . - info
