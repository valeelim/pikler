Quai Branly Museum     the musée du quai branly ( ) is a museum in paris . it is known in english as the quai branly museum , nicknamed mqb . it displays art and culture from indigenous civilisations of africa , america , asia and oceania . quai branly opened on 23 june 2006. it holds the collections of the old musée national des arts d'afrique et d'océanie , which is now closed . it contains 267,000 objects in its permanent collection . of these , 3500 items are on display . other websites . - musée du quai branly ( site in french , english and spanish )
