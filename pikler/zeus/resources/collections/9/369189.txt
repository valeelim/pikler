James Sloyan     james sloyan ( born february 20 , 1940 in indianapolis , indiana ) is an american actor . he has acted in the television shows `` star trek '' and `` hawaii 5-o '' . he acted in the movie `` the sting '' . he has also acted in soap operas . sloyan is married and has two children , daniel and samantha . in 1957 , he received a scholarship to the american academy of dramatic arts . he is married to actress deirdre lanihan .
