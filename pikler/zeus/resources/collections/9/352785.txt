Hervé Laborne     hervé laborne ( born 7 october 1946 ) is a french electrical engineer and president of university . he was head of the `` école spéciale de mécanique et électricité '' from 2003 till 2011. graduate from supélec in 1970 , he was teacher and researcher at this `` grande école '' for 10 years . in 1986 , he moved to the `` école spéciale de mécanique et électricité '' as professor . in 2003 , he was named director general of the university . he retired in 2011 , replaced by roger ceschi . he was also coordinator of the disability working group at the `` conférence des grandes écoles '' from 2007 to 2011. awards . - gold medal of the lebanese national education further reading . - hervé laborne , `` , eyrolles , 480p , 1992 , isbn 978-2212016277
