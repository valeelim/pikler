Joan Cusack     joan mary cusack ( born october 11 , 1962 ) is an american stage , movie and television actress . her movies include `` working girl '' , `` toy story 2 '' , `` toy story 3 '' , `` married to the mob '' , `` say anything '' , `` runaway bride '' and `` chicken little '' . she was nominated for the academy award for `` best actress in a supporting role '' for her work in `` working girl '' and `` in & out '' . she won an emmy award in 2015 for her role as shelia jackson in the comedy-drama series `` shameless '' . cusack is the sister of john cusack , ann cusack , bill cusack , and susie cusack . cusack was born in new york , new york and was raised in evanston , illinois . cusack married richard burke in 1993. they have two children together . they currently live in chicago , illinois .
