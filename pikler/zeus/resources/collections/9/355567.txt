Featherfoot     a featherfoot is a sorcerer in australian aboriginal spirituality . a featherfoot is usually a bad spirit who kills people . in most traditional aboriginal beliefs , there is no such thing as a natural death . every death is caused by evil spirits or spells . it is usually because of an enemy , who wants revenge for something . a featherfoot is so named because they are believed to have supernatural powers , including the ability to fly . he wears special shoes made of feathers ( usually emu ) and hair , stuck together with human blood . scholars say that the shoes leave no tracks ( footprints ) .
