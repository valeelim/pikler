Darryl F. Zanuck     darryl francis zanuck ( september 5 , 1902 – december 22 , 1979 ) was an american producer , writer , actor , director and studio executive . he played a major parts in the hollywood studio system . he earned three academy awards . his career lasted 48 years . zanuck was born in wahoo , nebraska on september 5 , 1902 to louise ( née torpin ) and frank zanuck . zanuck was married to virginia fox from 1924 until they separated in 1956. they had three children . one of their children was movie producer richard d. zanuck who produced `` jaws '' . zanuck died on december 22 , 1979 in palm springs , california from jaw cancer . he was 77 years old .
