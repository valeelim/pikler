House centipede     house centipedes , scutigera coleoptrata , are yellowish grey centipedes having up to 15 pairs of legs . the species are found all across the world and live in human apartments or houses . the house centipede is an insectivore ; it kills and eats arachnids and insects . they were first discovered around 1758. these centipedes are harmless to humans , although humans may be startled by them .
