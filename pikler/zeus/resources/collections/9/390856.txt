James Karen     james karen ( born james karnofsky ; november 28 , 1923 ) is an american actor . he is known for his roles as martin frohm in `` the pursuit of happyness '' and as ben hubbard in `` superman returns '' . karen was born on november 28 , 1923 in wilkes-barre , pennsylvania to a russian-jewish family . he studied at neighborhood playhouse school of the theatre . karen was married to susan reed from 1958 until they divorced in 1967. they had one child . he married alba francesca in 1986. other websites . - great character actors / james karen - pathmark tv commercial with james karen ( youtube )
