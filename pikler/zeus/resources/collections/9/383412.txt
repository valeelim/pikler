The Graham Children     the graham children is an oil painting by william hogarth . it was painted in 1742. it measures 160.5 × 181 cm ( 63.2 × 71.3 in ) . it hangs in the national gallery , london . the children are painted life size , surrounded by plush furnishings and fabrics that reflect the family 's wealth . a gilded bird cage is suspended from a cord and thomas sits in an ornate baby cart with a fine silver fruit bowl at his feet . richard plays a tune on a bird-organ which makes the bird sing .
