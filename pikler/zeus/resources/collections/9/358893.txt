Royal Mail Ship     royal mail ship ( rms ) is the ship prefix used for seagoing vessels that carry mail under contract to the british royal mail . in the early 19th century , the british admiralty provided rms service . the designation `` rms '' has been used since 1840. in 1850 contracts were awarded to private companies . having the title `` rms '' was seen as a mark of quality and a competitive advantage , because the mail had to be on time . list of rms ships . - hmhs `` britannic '' - rms `` carpathia '' - rms `` laconia '' - rms `` lusitania '' - rms `` queen elizabeth '' - rms '' queen elizabeth 2 '' - rms `` queen mary '' - rms `` queen mary 2 '' - `` titanic ''
