Espadrille     espadrilles are flat casual shoes or high heel sandals usually consisting of canvas or cotton fabric uppers and flexible sole made of jute , a natural fiber with vulcanized rubber underneath . these are worn primarily by women , although men wear the shoes also . these shoes are usually manufactured in bangladesh and spain . the soles of an espadrille are usually flat or platform wedge shape . these shoes are usually closed toed and closed backed , although some are open . espadrilles can be easily slipped on the foot . closed toe canvas upper with flat wedge sole made of jute braids are the most popular casual espadrilles known as `` classic '' or `` cotheeka basic '' espadrilles in the world . different colored cotton or jute canvas uppers are used for this style of espadrille that made it very popular among the women as well as men . striped canvas uppers are also used for these shoes . high wedge jute sole with uppers of different materials like fabrics , leather etc . are used to make `` fashion espadrilles '' which are also very popular among the women . super models put on these style of espadrilles at fashion shows . these espadrilles are also known as `` party espadrilles '' . almost all the designer brands of europe and north america design these styles of espadrilles .
