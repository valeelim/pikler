Ed Begley, Jr.    ed begley , jr. edward james `` ed '' begley , jr. ( born september 16 , 1949 ) is an american movie , television , stage , and voice actor and environmentalist . he is known for his roles in ; `` she-devil '' , `` what 's your number ? `` , `` whatever works '' , and in `` living with ed '' . he is also known for his television role in `` st. elsewhere '' . begley , jr. was born on september 16 , 1949 in los angeles , california to ed begley , sr. and to allene jeanne sanders . he studied at notre dame high school and at los angeles valley college . he was married to ingrid taylor from 1976 until they divorced in 1989. he has now been married to rachelle carson since 2000. he has three children . he lives in los angeles , california .
