Do the Right Thing     do the right thing is a 1989 american comedy-drama movie that is notable for the movie debuts for rosie perez and martin lawrence . spike lee directed , wrote and played as mookie in this movie , which is set in brooklyn , new york city . the subjects in `` do the right thing '' involve racial troubles and hot weather around new york city . other actors in the movie were samuel l. jackson . this movie received positive reviews by critics .
