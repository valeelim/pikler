Standards Institution of Israel     the standards institution of israel ( sii ) is the institution that is responsible for setting standards for products and services in israel . the institution does testing and certification of products ( by providing `` standards '' ) and ensuring the quality of products . the institute is funded from payments it collects from user services . the government finances only about 1.5 percent of its budget . the institute is located in tel aviv , near to tel aviv university . other websites . - standards institution of israel
