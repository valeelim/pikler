Deadhorse, Alaska    deadhorse , alaska deadhorse is a town in north slope borough , alaska , united states . it is an unincorporated community . it is known for its production of oil . deadhorse is near prudhoe bay , which located along the coastline of arctic ocean . the james dalton highway connects deadhorse with fairbanks . companies can send oil from deadhorse , using the trans-alaska pipeline system ( taps ) to the southern part of alaska . tourists can take buses from fairbanks using the dalton highway to get to deadhorse . the town is in the arctic circle . tourists can see the sun at midnight in the summer . kinds of animals , like arctic foxes , can been seen in this area .
