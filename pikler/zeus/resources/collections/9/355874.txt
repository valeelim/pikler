Al Sharpton     alfred charles `` al '' sharpton jr. ( born october 3 , 1954 in brooklyn , new york ) is an american baptist minister , civil rights activist and television talk show host . he was a candidate for the democrat nomination for the united states presidential election , 2004. he makes regular guest appearances on fox news , msnbc and cnn . in 2011 , he was named the host of msnbc 's politicsnation , a talk show .
