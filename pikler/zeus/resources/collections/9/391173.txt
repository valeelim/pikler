Jon Lovitz     jonathan m. `` jon '' lovitz ( born july 21 , 1957 ) is an american stand-up comedian , actor , writer , singer and comedian . he was known for his sketches on `` saturday night live '' from 1985 though 1990. he was also known for his guest-star role as steve on `` friends '' . he won two emmy awards for his writing on comedy series and an screen actors guild award for his acting in a crew . lovitz was born on july 21 , 1957 in los angeles , california to a romanian-hungarian-russian-jewish family . he studied at the university of california at irvine . other websites . - jon lovitz on myspace.com
