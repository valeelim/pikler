Voždovac     voždovac is one of 17 municipalities that make up the city of belgrade , the capital of serbia . the neighborhood of voždovac is 4–5 km south of downtown belgrade . the name voždovac come from the word `` vožd '' which means `` leader '' . due to its shape , it borders more municipalities than any other : vračar on the north , zvezdara on the north-east , grocka on the east , sopot on the south , barajevo on the south-west , čukarica and rakovica on the west and savski venac on the north-west . voždovac is twinned with following cities and municipalities : - florence , italy - šempeter , slovenia and has signed friendship charters with following cities and municipalities : - drežnica , slovenia - milići , bosnia and herzegovina - nea penteli , greece - ogulin , croatia - pula , croatia
