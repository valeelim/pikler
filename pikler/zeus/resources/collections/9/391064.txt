Rosalind Russell     rosalind russell ( born june 4 , 1907 - died november 28 , 1976 ) was an american actress who acted in lots of stage plays and many movies . she played a fast talking newspaper reporter in the 1940 screwball movie `` his girl friday '' and mame dennis in the movie `` auntie mame '' . russell was born in connecticut . she died from breast cancer at age sixty-nine in beverly hills , california .
