King Ghidorah     king ghidorah is a fictional monster who appears in movies from japan . he first appeared in `` ghidorah , the three-headed monster '' . since then , he has appeared in several `` godzilla '' movies . he is a golden three-headed dragon with no arms , large wings , and two tails . he is the strongest monster in the `` godzilla '' movies . appearances . - `` ghidorah , the three-headed monster '' ( 1964 ) - `` invasion of the astro-monster '' ( 1965 ) - `` destroy all monsters '' ( 1968 ) - `` godzilla vs. gigan '' ( 1972 ) - `` zone fighter '' ( 1973 ) - `` terror of mechagodzilla '' ( 1975 ) ( stock footage ) - `` godzilla vs. king ghidorah '' ( 1991 ) - `` rebirth of mothra '' ( 1996 ; also as `` death ghidorah '' ) - `` godzilla island '' ( 1997 ) - `` rebirth of mothra iii '' ( 1998 ) - `` godzilla , mothra and king ghidorah : giant monsters all-out attack '' ( 2001 ) - `` godzilla : final wars '' ( 2004 ; also as `` monster x '' , and `` keizer ghidorah '' ) other websites . - toho kingdom description
