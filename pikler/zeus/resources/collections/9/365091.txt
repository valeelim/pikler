Héctor Elizondo     héctor elizondo ( born december 22 , 1936 ) is an american movie , television , stage , and voice actor . he is known for his roles as bernard thompson in `` pretty woman '' and as dr. phillip watters in `` chicago hope '' . he won an emmy award in 1997. recently , he has voiced commissioner james gordon in `` the lego batman movie '' ( 2017 ) . since 2011 , he has played ed alzate on the sitcom `` last man standing '' . elizondo was born on december 22 , 1936 in new york city , new york . he studied at high school of performing arts and at city college of new york . he has been married to carolee campbell since 1969. he has one child .
