Svetlana Pankratova     svetlana pankratova ( born april 29 , 1971 ) is a russian citizen who holds the record for world 's longest legs on a female body 4 feet 4 inches . her height is 6 feet 5 inches . pankratova was born on april 29 , 1971 in volgograd , russian soviet federative socialist republic , ussr . she studied at virginia commonwealth university . she now lives in london , england .
