Regina King     regina king ( born january 15 , 1971 in los angeles ) is an american movie and television actress . she began acting in 1985 with the situation comedy `` 227 '' , on which program she stayed until 1990. she has performed in many movies , including `` jerry maguire '' , `` boyz n the hood '' ( 1991 ) and `` higher learning '' ( 1995 ) . she voiced both huey and riley freeman in the adult swim television series `` the boondocks '' .
