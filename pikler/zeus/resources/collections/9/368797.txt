Kashgar     kashgar or kashi is an oasis county-level city with about 350,000 residents in the far west of the people 's republic of china , near the border with tajikistan and kyrgyzstan . kashgar is the administrative centre of kashgar prefecture of the xinjiang uyghur autonomous region which has a population of approximately 3.5 million . the city 's urban area covers , though its administrative area extends for . kashgar was the meeting point for the southern and northern ancient silk routes for 2000 years because of its situation . the taklamakan desert is to the east of the city while westwards are the high mountains of pamir so camels must be exchanged for yaks ( or vice versa ) . kashgar remains very much a predominantly uighur city ; its market called sunday bazaar is resorted to by merchants from nearby countries such as kazakhstan , tadjikistan , and kirghizistan and is reputed the largest in central asia . other websites . - official website - kashgar photos
