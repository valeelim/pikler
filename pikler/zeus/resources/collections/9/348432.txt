Flores Sea     the flores sea covers 93,000 square miles ( 240,000 square kilometres ) of water in indonesia . the seas that border the flores sea are the bali sea ( to the west ) , java sea ( to the northwest ) , and the banda sea ( to the east and northeast ) . the indian ocean and savu sea are to the south . they are separated from the flores sea by various islands . islands that border this sea are the lesser sunda islands and celebes ( sulawesi ) .
