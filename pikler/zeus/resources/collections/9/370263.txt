Zomi Revolutionary Army     the zomi revolutionary army ( zra ) is a nationalist separatist group dedicated to the defense of ethnic zomi people . it fights for the reunification of zomi people , whose homelands are split between in myanmar ( chin state ) , bangladesh ( chittagong hill tracts ) and north-east india ( manipur and mizoram ) . it aims to unite all zomi people under one governing unit , known as a zogam , meaning `` land of the zomi '' .
