Joy Kali Temple     the temple of joy kali is a hindu temple in bangladesh . it is located between thathari bazar and wari , on a road named after this temple , called 24 joy kali temple street . the temple is devoted to goddess kali . the statue of kali is of great significance to the hindus . the visitors and committee members of this temple gather money for occasions , where sweets are later distributed among them which is called 'prasad ' .
