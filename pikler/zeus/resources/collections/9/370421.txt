Yayi Boni     dr. thomas yayi boni ( born 1 july 1952 ) is a beninese politician and banker . he served as the president of benin from 6 april 2006 to 6 april 2016. he was re-elected to a second term in march 2011. from 29 january 2012 to 27 january 2013 , he was the chairperson of the african union . on 15 march 2007 , yayi boni survived an assassination attempt . several of the people travelling with the president were wounded in gunfire between the presidential guard and the would-be assassins . on 23 october 2012 , the bbc said that his doctor , niece , and former commerce minister had been arrested in a plot to poison the president . the niece changed his medicine to a `` toxic substance '' while he was on a state visit to brussels .
