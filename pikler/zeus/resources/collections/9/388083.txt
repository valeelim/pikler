Violin Concerto (Brahms)    violin concerto ( brahms ) the violin concerto in d major , op . 77 is a work for orchestra and solo violin by johannes brahms . he completed the concerto in the summer of 1878. it was written for joseph joachim . the concerto premiered in leipzig on 1 january 1879. references . - woodstra , chris , et al . 2005 . `` all music guide to classical music '' . all media guide , llc . p. 198. isbn 0-87930-865-6 .
