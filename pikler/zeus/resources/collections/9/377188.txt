Margaret Mitchell     margaret munnerlyn mitchell ( november 8 , 1900 - august 16 , 1949 ) was an american novelist . she is best known for writing `` gone with the wind '' . she was born in atlanta , georgia . her mother 's family was irish roman catholic . in atlanta on 11 august 1949 , mitchell was hit by a car driven by a drunk driver . five days later , she died of her injuries . the driver was convicted of involuntary manslaughter .
