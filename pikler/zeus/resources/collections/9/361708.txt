Eugene Levy     eugene levy ( born december 17 , 1946 ) is a canadian movie , television , stage , voice actor , and comedian . he is known for his role in the all eight of the `` american pie '' series of movies . he appeared in the 2012 movie `` madea 's witness protection '' . he has won a emmy award , a grammy award , a gemini award , and a satellite award . he will appear in the 2015 pixar movie `` finding dory '' . levy was born in hamilton , ontario , canada on december 17 , 1946. and studied at mcmaster university . he has been married to deborah divine since 1977. they have two children .
