Richard Kind     richard john kind ( born november 22 , 1956 ) is an american actor and voice actor . he is known for his roles in `` mad about you '' ( as dr. mark devanow ) and in `` spin city '' ( as paul thomas lassiter ) . kind was born on november 22 , 1956 in trenton , new jersey to a jewish family . he grew up in bucks county , pennsylvania . kind studied at pennsbury high school and at northwestern university . he married dana stanley in 1999. they have three children . kind now lives in santa monica , california and in chicago , illinois . other websites . - fairfield arts & convention center
