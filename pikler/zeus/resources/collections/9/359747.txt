Superman Returns (movie)    superman returns ( movie ) superman returns is a 2006 superhero movie . it is based on the dc comics superhero superman and his arch enemy lex luthor . the movie was directed by bryan singer . it was produced by bryan singer , gilbert adler , and jon peters . the movie stars include brandon routh , kevin spacey , kate bosworth , james marsden , parker posey , frank langella , sam huntington , eva marie saint , kal penn , and james karen ( deleted scenes ) . it also includes a computer-generated image scene of marlon brando , who died in 2004 . `` superman returns '' was released on june 28 , 2006. the movie used john williams ' original score from the original movie .
