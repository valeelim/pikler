Q'eqchi' people    q'eqchi ' people q'eqchi ' ( k'ekchi ' , or simply kekchi ) are one of the maya peoples in guatemala and belize . their language is also called q'eqchi ' . before the spanish came in the 1520s , q'eqchi ' were mostly in what are now the departments of alta verapaz and baja verapaz in guatemala . with the activity the q'eqchi ' moved into other regions of guatemala , southern belize , and smaller numbers in el salvador , honduras and southern mexico .
