Stephen Dunham     stephen dunham dowers ( september 14 , 1964 - september 14 , 2012 ) was an american movie and television actor . he was best known for his roles as edward pillows in `` dag '' and as peter in `` the mummy '' . bowers was born in boston , massachusetts . bowers was married to alexondra lee . he died on his birthday on september 14 , 2012 from a heart attack in his burbank , california home , aged 48 .
