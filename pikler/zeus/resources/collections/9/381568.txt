10th Ward of New Orleans     the 10th ward is a division of the city of new orleans , louisiana . the 10th ward is one of the 17 wards of new orleans . the 10th ward is one of the city 's uptown wards , formerly the old faubourg lafayette annexed by new orleans in 1850s . the roughly wedge-shaped ward stretches back from the mississippi river . the lower boundary is felicity street , across which is the 1st ward , then martin luther king boulevard ( formerly melpomene street ) , across which is the 2nd ward . the upper boundary is first street , across which lies the 11th ward . near the river the ward includes part of the lower garden district and the former location of america 's first experiment with large-scale public housing , started here in 1937 , when , as part of the new deal , president roosevelt signed the loan to commence construction of the st. thomas housing project . in central city it includes the oretha castle haley commercial district . the 10th ward has been home to such notables as jazz musicians buddy bolden and george brunies .
