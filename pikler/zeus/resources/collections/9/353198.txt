Yuri Shevchuk     yuri shevchuk ( ) , born 16 may 1957 , is a soviet and russian singer/songwriter . he is the leader of rock band ddt , which he founded in 1980. shevchuk was born in magadan region and raised in ufa . now he lives in st. petersburg , russia . he is known as one of the greatest song-writers in modern russia . shevchuk is very critical of the vladimir putin 's government . other websites . - official website of ddt
