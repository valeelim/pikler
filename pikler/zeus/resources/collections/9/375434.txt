Wingate Institute     the wingate institute ( ) is a sports training academy in israel . it was established in 1957. the institute is named after orde wingate , who was a british general in the mandate of palestine . the wingate campus includes schools and local organizations of sport . the wingate institute is located in netanya and has a supplementary compound in tel aviv , the national sport center , that includes many headquarters of israeli sport organizations . other websites . - official website
