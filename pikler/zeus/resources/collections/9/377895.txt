USS Colorado (1856)    uss colorado ( 1856 ) the first uss `` colorado '' was a -class , three-masted steam screw frigate . it was launched on 19 june 1856 by the norfolk navy yard . it was sponsored by ms. n. s. dornin and commissioned on 13 march 1858. captain w. h. gardner was in command . `` colorado '' put to sea from boston on 12 may 1858. it then cruised into cuban waters to stop the practice of search by british cruisers until 6 august . she then returned to boston and was placed in ordinary until 1861. returning to new york on 30 may 1876 , `` colorado '' was placed out of commission on 8 june . from 1876-1884 , she was used as a receiving ship at new york navy yard . she was sold on 14 february 1885 to a private company ; and broken up for salvage material and burned for her copper fastenings . references . - silverstone , paul h. `` warships of the civil war navies '' naval institute press , annapolis , md , 1989 , isbn 0-87021-783-6 .
