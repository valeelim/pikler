Pope Gregory VIII     pope gregory viii ( ; c.1100 – 17 december 1187 ) , born alberto di morra , was an italian priest of the roman catholic church and the 174th pope from 25 october 1073 until he died in 1085. alberto di morra was born in benvenuo , italy . pope adrian iv made him a cardinal in 1155. he was one of two men sent to england by pope alexander iii after the murder of st. thomas a becket . cardinal morra was elected pope on 11 october 1187. and he chose to be called gregory viii . related pages . - list of popes other websites . - `` gregory viii ( pope ) '' , `` encyclopædia britannica '' , 1911 - catholic hierarchy , popes in sequence - cardinals of the holy roman church , cardinal morra
