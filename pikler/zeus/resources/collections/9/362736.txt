Krista Voda     krista voda ( born may 31 , 1974 ) is an american sportscaster . she is the pit reporter for nascar on fox . she replaced jeanne zelasko . she also is the host for speed channel 's coverage of the nascar camping truck series as well as host of `` trackside '' . she was an anchor for `` totally nascar '' as well as `` nascar nation '' . voda has also done sideline reporting for fsn 's coverage of college football and basketball as well as fox 's coverage of the bcs and the national football league . in 2008 , she was paired with chris rose and john lynch for weeks 12 and 17 of the nfl . this included the game between the detroit lions and the green bay packers .
