Franck Goldnadel     franck goldnadel , born the 14th of august 1969 , is a french aerospace engineer . since the 1st of march 2011 , he is head of paris-charles de gaulle airport . he graduated from the french civil aviation university ( promotion 1990 ) . he has worked for 15 years in air transport before being nominated head of paris-charles de gaulle airport . award . - chevalier of the `` ordre national du mérite '' further reading . - académie nationale de l'air et de l'espace and lucien robineau , `` les français du ciel , dictionnaire historique '' , le cherche midi , june 2005 , 782 p.
