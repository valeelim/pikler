Inflection     inflection ( or inflexion ) is a piece added to a word to express variations in grammar . the added bits are called affixes . they are prefixes ( in front ) , suffixes ( at the end ) and infixes ( in the middle ) . they are added to the `` root '' , the part of the word which does not change . examples : - `` write , wrote , written '' are tenses of the verb `` to write '' . inflecting a verb is called conjugation . - `` sing , sang , sung '' - `` foot , feet '' is a noun inflected for number . inflecting a noun is called declension . - `` mouse , mice '' - `` child , children ''
