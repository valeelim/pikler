Shelley Berman     sheldon `` shelley '' berman ( born february 3 , 1925 ) is an american comedian , movie , television , stage , radio , voice actor , writer , teacher , screenwriter , educator , and poet who is known for his roles in `` the steve allen show '' and in `` divorce american style '' . he won a grammy award . berman was born and raised in chicago , illinois on february 3 , 1925. he studied at the goodman theatre in chicago . he was married to sarah herman since 1947. they had two adopted children , one who died from a brian tumor . berman now lives in bell canyon , california . other websites . - official website
