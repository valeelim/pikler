Gotye     wouter `` wally '' de backer ( born 21 may 1980 ) , better known as gotye ( pronounced ) , is a belgian-australian multi-instrumental musician and singer-songwriter . gotye 's 2011 single `` somebody that i used to know '' with kimbra was number 1 on the billboard hot 100. he has won 12 aria awards , three grammys and a nomination for an mtv ema for best asia and pacific act . gotye is bilingual , speaking both dutch and english , and also speaks some japanese , which he studied at university . discography . - `` boardface '' ( 2003 ) - `` like drawing blood '' ( 2006 ) - `` making mirrors '' ( 2011 ) other websites . - more info at encyclopedia.thefreedictionary.com
