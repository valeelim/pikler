Wix.com     wix.com is a freemium online platform that allows users to create websites and facebook pages . it competes with webs , weebly , yola and other web hosting and creation sites . wix was created in 2006 by avishai abrahami , nadav abrahami and giora kaplan . the company is located in tel aviv , with offices in san francisco , new york city and dnepropetrovsk . it is funded by investors insight venture partners , mangrove capital partners , bessemer venture partners and benchmark capital . it entered open-beta in 2008 .
