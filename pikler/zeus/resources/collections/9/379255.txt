Bethesda, Maryland    bethesda , maryland bethesda is a census-designated place in southern montgomery county , maryland , united states . it is northwest of washington , d.c. the national institutes of health main campus and the walter reed national military medical center are in bethesda . there are also corporate and government headquarters . in april 2009 , `` forbes '' put bethesda second on its list of `` america 's most livable cities . '' bethesda is an unincorporated area . it has no official boundaries . the united states census bureau says the center is at 38°59 ' north , 77°7 ' west . according to the u.s. census bureau , in 2010 , bethesda had 60,858 people .
