Lambert Lombard     lambert lombard ( liège , 1505 – 1566 ) was a renaissance painter , architect and theorist for the prince-bishopric of liège . during his career he worked for jan gossaert in middelburg and trained frans floris . in 1532 he became court painter and architect in liège . a few paintings and many drawings have been kept . in 1537 he was sent to rome by erard de la marck , prince-bishop of liège , to buy works of art . while there he discovered the wonders of the italian renaissance . on his return he brought not only works of art , but also the new ideas about art to liège . dominicus lampsonius wrote a biography of lombard called `` the life of lambert lombard '' .
