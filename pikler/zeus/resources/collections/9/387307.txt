Susana Dosamantes     susana dosamantes ( born susana rue riestra on 9 january 1948 in guadalajara , mexico ) , is a mexican actress . she is the mother of mexican pop diva paulina rubio , she has appeared in around 50 movies and tv series , including telenovelas . on 11 january 2011 , her ex-husband died from kidney cancer . in recent years dosamantes has worked in the united states so that she could live near her daughter , paulina . both susana and paulina now live in miami , florida . filmography . - 2012 : corazón apasionado ... doña úrsula campos miranda de villacastín - 2010 : eva luna ... marcela - 2008 : el juramento ... luisa robles conde - 2007 : tomalo suave ... susana - 2006 : marina ... alberta morales viuda de alarcón - 2005 : el amor no tiene precio ... lucrecia cevallos - 2003 : rebeca ... matilde linares - 1999 : reclusorio iii - 1997 : amada enemiga ... regina - 1995 : bésame en la boca - 1990 : keiko en peligro - 1990 : la ley de la mafia ... dinorah - 1990 : el protector de la mafia - 1990 : la metralleta
