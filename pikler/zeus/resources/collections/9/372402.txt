Lester Archer     lester archer ( 1838 - october 27 , 1864 ) was an american soldier . he received the medal of honor for valor during the american civil war . archer was born in fort ann , new york sometime in 1838. he served in the american civil war in the 96th new york infantry for the union army . he received the medal of honor on april 6 , 1865 for his actions at the battle of chaffin 's farm . he died before the medal could be presented to him so it was received posthumously . citation : gallantry in placing the colors of his regiment on the fort . other websites . - military times
