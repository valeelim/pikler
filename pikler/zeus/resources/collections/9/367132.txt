Taufiq Rafat     taufiq rafat ( 1927-1998 ) was a famous pakistani poet and writer born in sialkot , pakistan.he was pioneer of english language poetry/writing in pakistan . his work was included in several early anthologies . his own best known collection of poems was `` arrival of the monsoon '' ( 1984 ) . he also translated several works of various punjabi writers into english . rafat inspired and mentored many younger poets in the 1980s and 1990s .
