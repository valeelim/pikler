Honda Ridgeline     honda ridgeline is a mid sized pickup truck produced by honda . the first model year for this truck was 2006 ; it has undergone several changes in design since being introduced . unlike a traditional body-on-frame truck , the ridgeline uses a closed box unibody frame similar to the one used on the accord , pilot and odyssey . despite good sales honda pulled the truck in 2015 , but has planned to redesign it for the 2017 model year .
