Evil Dead (2013 movie)    evil dead ( 2013 movie ) the evil dead is a 2013 american horror movie . it is about five friends who stay at a remote cabin . this movie was at first rated nc-17 , but changes were made so the movie would get an r rating from the mpaa . `` evil dead '' was also rated `` 18 '' by the bbfc ( uk ) and rated `` r '' in australia . this movie got positive reviews by the critics . it is a remake of 1981 movie `` the evil dead '' .
