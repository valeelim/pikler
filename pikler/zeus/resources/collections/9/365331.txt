Isla Fisher     isla fisher ( born 3 february 1976 ) is a british-australian actress who has been in many movies and television shows . she was born in muscat , oman to scottish parents . she moved to perth , western australia when she was six . she starred in the movie `` wedding crashers '' . fisher became engaged to sacha baron cohen in 2004. the couple married in 2010. they have two daughters and a son .
