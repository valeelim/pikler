Copycat suicide     sometimes , after a person commits suicide ( kills himself ) , other people will imitate him and kill themselves also . this is called copycat suicide . people may commit copycat suicide after a person they know kills himself , or after hearing about a suicide through television or other media . after a well-known suicide , there may be a sudden increase in copycat suicides . this is called the werther effect . sudden increases in copycat suicides can spread through individual households , school systems , or even across entire countries . these are called cluster suicides .
