Hashimoto's thyroiditis    hashimoto 's thyroiditis hashimato 's thyroiditis or chronic lymphocytic thyroiditis is an autoimmune disease . the cause of hashimato 's thyroiditis is that body starts to produce antibodies against the normal thyroid tissue . this disease was the first disease to be described as autoimmune . for the first time it described by the japanese specialist `` hakaru hashimato '' in germany in 1912 .
