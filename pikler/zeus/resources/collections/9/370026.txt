French Dahomey     dahomey was a french colony . it was part of french west africa from 1904 to 1958. in 1947 , dahomey became part of the french union and had more freedom . on 11 december 1958 , the french fifth republic was started . the colony became the self-governing republic of dahomey . two years later on 1 august 1960 the country had full independence . in 1975 it changed its name to benin .
