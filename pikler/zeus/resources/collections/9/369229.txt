Gabriel o Pensador     gabriel contino better known as gabriel o pensador is a brazilian rapper . he was born in rio de janeiro ( city ) , rio de janeiro , brazil on march 4 , 1974. he began his musical career to launch a demo with the song `` i 'm happy ( i killed the president ) '' and was soon hired by sony music . besides singer , gabriel is a writer in 2001 and released the autobiographical book '' diary '' nocturne , four years later launched '' a boy called rorbeto '' , which won the jabuti award best children 's book of the year next . in 2008 released the book '' my little rubro-negro '' and a year later launched a special version of the same book titled '' my little crimson-black - special edition hexa '' .
