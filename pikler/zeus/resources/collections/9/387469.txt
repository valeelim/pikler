Kings Island     kings island is the name of an amusememt park in mason , ohio . the park is owned and operated by the cedar fair company . it operates from late april to labor day . the park features 46 rides total and has 13 roller coasters including vortex , flight deck , diamondback , and other attractions . the park opened to the public on april 29 , 1972. great wolf lodge nearby , is next to the amusement park . the newest ride at kings island is banshee .
