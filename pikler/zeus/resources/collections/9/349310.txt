BAFTA Award for Best Short Film     this page lists the winners and nominees for the bafta award for best short film for each year . the british academy of film and television arts ( bafta ) , is a british organisation that hosts annual awards shows for movies , television , children 's movies and television , and interactive media . since 1960 , selected movies have been awarded with the bafta award for best short film at an annual ceremony . in the following lists , the titles and names in bold with a dark grey background are the winners . those not in bold are the nominees . the years are when the movies were released , not the year of the ceremony .
