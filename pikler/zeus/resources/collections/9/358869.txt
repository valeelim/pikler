Augustin Zegrean     augustin zegrean ( born 29 march 1954 , in sânmartin , cluj ) is a romanian lawyer . since 13 july 2007 , he has been a judge at the constitutional court of romania . on 17 july 2010 he was chosen as president of the constitutional court of romania . he graduated from the university of law babeș-bolyai from cluj în 1980 .
