Scary Monsters and Nice Sprites     scary monsters and nice sprites is the second ep by american electronic music producer , skrillex . `` scary monsters and nice sprites '' was released on october 22 , 2010. skrillex said that he recorded the ep in 2010 at his apartment using a laptop . the ep won two grammys at the 54th annual grammy awards for best dance recording and for best dance/electronica album . the ep also received generally positive reviews from music critics and reached # 49 on the billboard 200 as well as # 1 on the billboard heatseekers albums chart . other websites . - scary monsters and nice sprites at discogs
