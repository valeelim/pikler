Jatin Lalit     jatin lalit are a music director duo of bollywood . the duo consists of brothers , jatin pandit and lalit pandit . they started their music career in 1991. their first breakthrough came a year later , when they composed the music for `` jo jeeta wohi sikandar '' . since then they have composed for music for over 50 movies . some of their movies are `` jo jeeta wohi sikandar '' ( 1992 ) , `` khiladi '' ( 1992 ) , `` kabhi haan kabhi naa '' ( 1993 ) , `` dilwale dulhania le jayenge '' ( 1995 ) , `` khamoshi '' ( 1996 ) , `` yes boss '' ( 1997 ) , `` kuch kuch hota hai '' ( 1998 ) , `` sarfarosh '' ( 1999 ) , `` mohabbatein '' ( 2000 ) , `` kabhi khushi kabhi gham '' ( 2001 ) , `` chalte chalte '' ( 2003 ) , `` fanaa '' ( 2006 ) . in 2006 , the duo stopped working together .
