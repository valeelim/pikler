Dax Shepard     dax randall shepard ( born january 2 , 1975 in milford , michigan ) is an american television actor , movie actor and comedian . shepard has appeared in many movies such as ; `` cheaper by the dozen '' , `` zathura '' , `` idiocracy '' , `` baby mama '' , `` old dogs '' , `` the freebie '' , and in the 2012 comedy `` hit and run '' . shepard started dating actress kristen bell in 2007. their daughter , lincoln bell shepard , was born on march 28 , 2013. dax and bell married on october 17 , 2013. their second daughter , delta bell shepard , was born on december 19 , 2014 .
