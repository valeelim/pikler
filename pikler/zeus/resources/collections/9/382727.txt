Canis Major     canis major is a constellation that is in the southern sky . it means `` greater dog '' in latin ( also called large dog ) . there is also the constellation canis minor , which means `` lesser dog '' in latin . the astronomer named ptolemy listed canis major when he made a list of 48 constellations . it is also one of the 88 modern constellations that were made by the international astronomical union . the star sirius is in canis major . sirius has a magnitude of -1.44 , which means that it is the brightest star in the night sky . it is sometimes called the `` dog star . '' also , sirius is only 8.6 light years away from earth , which is very close .
