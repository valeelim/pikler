Monte Markham     monte markham ( born june 21 , 1935 ) is an american movie , television , stage , radio , voice actor , movie producer , and movie director . he is known for his roles in ; `` one is a lonely number '' , `` hot pursuit '' , `` jack holborn '' , and in `` guns of the magnificent seven '' . markham was born on june 21 , 1935 in manatee county , florida . markham has been married to klaire keevil hester since 1961 .
