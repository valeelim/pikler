Pope Anastasius III     pope anastasius iii ( ; died june 913 ) was an italian cleric of the roman catholic church and the 121st pope from 911 until his death in 913. anastasius was born in rome . anastasius was elected pope in 911. he was pope during a period when rome and the papacy were in the power of theophylact family . he was active in developing the dioceses of germany . he was buried in st. peter 's basilica . related pages . - list of popes
