Hollywood Records     hollywood records is an american record label . they focus on pop , rock , alternative and teen pop genres . it was founded in 1989. it is one of the major dmg labels . its current roster includes artists such as : demi lovato , queen , plain white t 's , jesse mccartney , grace potter and the nocturnals , breaking benjamin , jessica sutta , lucy hale , miley cyrus , selena gomez & the scene , valora , cherri bomb , stefano langone , bridgit mendler , and zendaya coleman .
