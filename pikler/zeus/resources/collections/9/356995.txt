Punky Brewster     punky brewster was an american television sitcom . it began in september 1984 and officially ended may 27 , 1988. it aired on nbc from 1984 to 1986 , and in syndication from 1986 to the end of its run . the show was about a teenaged girl named penelope `` punky '' brewster ( played by soleil moon frye ) who was under care of foster parents one of them is henry warnimont ( played by george gaynes ) .
