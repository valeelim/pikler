Anna Faris     anna kay faris ( born november 29 , 1976 ) is an american movie , television , stage , voice actress , comedian and singer who is known for her roles in all four `` scary movie '' s , `` lost in translation '' , `` cloudy with a chance of meatballs '' and `` the house bunny '' . faris was born on november 29 , 1976 in baltimore , maryland . she was raised in edmonds , washington . she studied at edmonds-woodway high school and at the university of washington . she was married to ben indra from 2004 until they divorced in 2008. she has been married to chris pratt since 2009. she has a son , jack pratt by her second husband chris pratt . in 2017 , faris and pratt legally separated .
