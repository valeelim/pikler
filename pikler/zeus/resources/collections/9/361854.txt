Emily Mortimer     emily kathleen a. mortimer ( born december 1 , 1971 ) is a british movie , stage , television , stage actress , and comedian . she was in `` 30 rock '' , `` scream 3 '' , `` match point '' , `` lars and the real girl '' , `` hugo '' , `` cars 2 '' , `` shutter island '' , `` harry brown '' , `` cars 2 '' , `` the pink panther '' , `` the pink panther 2 '' , and `` the newsroom '' . she was born in london , england on december 1 , 1971. she has been married to alessandro nivola since 2003 .
