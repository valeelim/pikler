Ted Demme     edward kern demme ( known as ted demme ; october 26 , 1963 - january 13 , 2002 ) was an american movie director and producer . his career began in 1988. his movies included `` the ref '' ( 1994 ) , `` beautiful girls '' ( 1996 ) , `` life '' and `` blow '' . he died in santa monica , california due to a heart attack brought on by cocaine ; the director was 38 when it happened . he was married to amanda demme from 1994 until his death . the couple had two sons . he was the nephew of jonathan demme .
