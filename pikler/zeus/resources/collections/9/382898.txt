Bactrian deer     the bactrian deer ( `` cervus elaphus bactrianus '' ) , also called the bukhara deer , bokhara deer or bactrian wapiti , is a subspecies of red deer that is native to the lowlands of central asia . it is similar to the yarkand deer in that it lives around the banks of rivers surrounded by desert . both subspecies are separated from one another by the tian shan mountains .
