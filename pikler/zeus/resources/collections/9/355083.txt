Midwife toad     the midwife toads ( `` alytes '' ) is a genus of frogs . they part of the discoglossidae family . they live in most of europe and northwestern africa . they look like toads because of their hard skin . they get their name because they take care of their young on their backs . they help them from not being eaten from hungry animals . when they hatch the frog will go back to the water and allow them to swim away . books . - carl vogt : `` untersuchungen über die entwicklungsgeschichte der geburtshelferkröte . ( alytes obstetricians ) '' , solothurn : jent und gassman , ( 1842 ) , pp 130 - arthur koestler , `` the case of the midwife toad '' , london : hutchinson , 1971 .
