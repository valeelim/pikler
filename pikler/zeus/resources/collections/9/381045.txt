Felicity     felicity is an english female given name . it means `` happiness '' . the name is derived from the latin name `` felicitas '' . `` felicitas '' is the name for an ancient roman goddess . this female name was at 236 in popularity for girls around england and wales . the name was 390th in popularity on girls in the united states for 1999. felicity is a female form of the male name `` felix '' . the name felicity is the name of a character played by keri russell .
