American spadefoot toads     scaphiopodidae is a family of american spadefoot toads . they live in southern canada and usa south to southern mexico . scaphiopodidae is a small family with only seven species . the american spadefoot toads are of typical shape to most digging frogs . they are round , with short legs and eyes that stick out . this frog has a hard growth on their feet which helps them to dig . they dig backwards into the ground . the american spadefoot toads live on land when not under ground . they are dully coloured , usually a grey or dull green or brown . this is to help in camouflage . other websites . - scaphiopodidae at tree of life
