Hamish Linklater     hamish linklater ( born july 7 , 1976 ) is an american actor and comedian , best known for playing matthew in `` the new adventures of old christine '' . linklater was born and raised in great barrington , massachusetts on july 7 , 1976 , to scottish kristin linklater , professor of theatre and chair of the acting division at columbia university and a renowned teacher of vocal technique , and jim cormeny . other websites . - hamish linklater at tv.com
