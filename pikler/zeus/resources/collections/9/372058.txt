Coccus     a coccus ( plural of cocci ) is a bacterium that is shaped like a sphere or circle . cocci are one of the three types of bacteria shapes . the word coccus comes from the greek word `` kokkos '' , or `` berry '' . some examples of cocci are abiotrophia , enterococcus , neisseria , staphylococcus , and streptococcus . related pages . - bacillus ( one of the other two types of bacteria shapes ) - spirillum ( the other shape of bacteria )
