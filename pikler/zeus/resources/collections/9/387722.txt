Halloween: The Curse of Michael Myers    halloween : the curse of michael myers halloween : the curse of michael myers is a 1995 american horror movie . this was the first movie of paul rudd and the last movie of donald pleasence the movie was dedicated in his memory . the movie was directed by joe chappelle and produced by moustapha akkad . the movie was released on september 29 , 1995. the movie is about michael myers trying to kill the baby of his niece , who he just killed in the beginning . dr. samuel loomis comes out of retirement to save tommy doyle and kara strode . the main villain of the movie was dr. terence wynn who was the leader of an evil group . at the end of the movie michael myers escapes when he switched clothes with dr. wynn . dr. loomis was touched by dr. wynn and became the new leader of the group . this ending was in the producer 's cut of the movie . cast . - donald pleasence as dr. loomis - paul rudd as tommy doyle - marianne hagan as kara strode - mitchell ryan as dr. terence wynn - j. c. brandy as jamie lloyd - george p. wilbur as michael myers
