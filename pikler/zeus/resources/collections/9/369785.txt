Schlumbergera     schlumbergera is a genus of cacti . the genus includes six species , originally found in south-eastern brazil . these cacti , along with a number of cultivars are common houseplants . they are also known as `` christmas cactus '' or `` thanksgiving cactus '' . this name refers to the time of the year when the cacti flourish , in europe . in the wild , the cacti grow on trees as epiphytes or rocks , and can form shrubss up to high . the cacti do not have leaves , the green stem segments do photosynthesis . charles lemaire first described them in 1858. it is named after frédéric schlumberger a cactus collector and plant breeder .
