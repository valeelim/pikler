Ian Richardson     ian william richardson ( 7 april 1934 – 9 february 2007 ) was a scottish actor who was well known for his role as francis urquhart in `` house of cards '' . he won a drama desk award in 1976. richardson was born on 7 april 1934 in edinburgh , scotland . he was raised in balgreen , edinburgh . richardson studied at balgreen primary school , at tynecastle high school , at the george heriot 's school , and at the college of dramatic arts . he was married to maroussia frank from 1961 until his death in 2007. they had a son , miles . richardson died on 9 february 2007 in london , england from a heart attack , aged 72. other website . - interview with ian richardson at the theatre archive project - ian richardson 's performances in the theatre archive , university of bristol - `` the guardian '' – actor ian richardson dies - `` the guardian '' – obituary - `` bbc '' – obituary : ian richardson - ian richardson tribute
