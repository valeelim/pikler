Déborah François     déborah françois ( born 24 may 1987 ) is an actress from belgium . she lives in paris , france . she is best known for her role as in belgian film `` l ’ enfant '' ( `` the child '' ) , french film `` la tourneuse de pages '' ( `` the page turner '' ) , and she won a césar award for her role in a 2008 french film `` le premier jour du reste de ta vie '' ( `` the first day of the rest of your life '' ) . she was born in liège , belgium which is in the french-speaking area . she is the daughter of a policeman and a social worker . she was awarded the romy-schneider prize and prix suzanne bianchetti . filmography . - 2005 : `` l'enfant '' - 2006 : `` la tourneuse de pages '' - 2007 : `` les fourmis rouges '' - 2007 : `` l'été indien '' - 2008 : `` les femmes de l'ombre '' - 2008 : `` le premier jour du reste de ta vie '' - 2009 : `` unmade beds '' - 2009 : `` fais-moi plaisir '' - 2009 : `` my queen karo '' - 2010 : `` memories corner '' - 2010 : `` mes chères études '' - 2011 : `` le moine - the monk '' - 2011 : `` les tribulations d'une caissière '' - 2012 : `` populaire ''
