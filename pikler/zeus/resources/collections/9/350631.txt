Simon Stevin     simon stevin ( 1548/49 – 1620 ) was a flemish mathematician and military engineer . he translated many mathematical terms into dutch . stevin was born in bruges , flanders ( now belgium ) around the year 1548. it is thought that simon was a calvinist . very little has been recorded about his life . it is known that he left a widow with two children . in bruges there is a simon stevin square which has his statue by eugène simonis .
