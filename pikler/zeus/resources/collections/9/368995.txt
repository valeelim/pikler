United States Census     the united states census is a census done in the united states every ten years . it is required by the united states constitution which calls for a decennial census done in years ending in `` 0 '' since 1790. it is actually two census counts taken concurrently ( at the same time ) . the first is the census of the population , taken from 1790 to the present . the second is the census of housing , which counts the number of residential housing units , and was taken from 1940 to the present . among other uses , the count is used to determine how many places in the house of representatives each state has . it also determines how much government money is spent in different locations . the united states census bureau is responsible for the united states census . the first census after the american revolution was in 1790. secretary of state thomas jefferson was responsible for this first census . other websites . - u.s. census bureau official website
