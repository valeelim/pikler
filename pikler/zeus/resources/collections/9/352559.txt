My Family     my family is a british television sitcom . it was shown on bbc one from 19 september 2000 until 11 september 2011. it was created by fred barron and is set in chiswick , west london . it stars robert lindsay as dentist ben harper and zoe wanamaker as susan harper . they are a middle-aged , middle-class married couple who have three children . they elder son is nick ( played by kris marshall ) , who is stupid , cheerful and has no direction in life . the daughter is janey ( played by daniela denby-ashe ) , who has many boyfriends and becomes a single mother . the younger son is michael ( played by gabriel thomson ) , who comes out as gay .
