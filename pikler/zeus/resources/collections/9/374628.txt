U.S. Army Nurse Corps     the united states army nurse corps ( nc ) is an american army branch . it was created by the united states congress in the year of 1901. this special branch together with five other ones form together the army medical department ( amedd ) . the nurse corps is the nursing service for the u.s. army and provides qualified nursing staff in support of the plans of the united states department of defense . all work must be in favor of the wellness of warriors and their families . references . - this article has started as a summary of the article on english wikipedia
