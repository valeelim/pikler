Jon Sciambi     jon `` boog '' sciambi ( born april 11 , 1970 in philadelphia , pennsylvania ) is an american sportscaster for espn . he currently calls major league baseball games for espn radio and formally with espn . he also calls college basketball for espn , espnu ( occasionally ) , and espn radio . he was a play-by-play announcer for the florida marlins and atlanta braves as well as espn little league world series . sciambi 's nickname `` boog , '' is because he looks like former major league player boog powell .
