Danny Trejo     daniel `` danny '' trejo ( born may 16 , 1944 ) is an american actor . he is best known for playing the role of bad guy or the anti-hero . trejo is of mexican descent . he was born in livermore , california in the san francisco bay area . trejo is the second cousin of writer-director robert rodriguez . he was worked almost all of rodriguez 's movies . these include the `` spy kids '' series , `` desperado '' , `` grindhouse '' and `` from dusk till dawn ''
