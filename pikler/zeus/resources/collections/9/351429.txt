Antoine de la Mothe Cadillac     antoine de la mothe cadillac was born on march 5 , 1658 and died on october 16 , 1730. he was a french explorer and adventurer in new france , now an area of north america stretching from eastern canada in the north to louisiana in the south . in 1701 , he founded detroit , which he helped develop until 1710. between 1710 and 1716 he was the governor of louisiana , although he did not arrive in that territory until 1713 .
