Krsto Papić     krsto papić ( december 7 , 1933 – february 7 , 2013 ) was a croatian screenwriter and movie director . his career lasted for almost 50 years . he retired in 2012. he was nominated for a golden globe in 1989. papić was born on december 7 , 1933 in vučji do , near nikšić , kingdom of yugoslavia . papić died on february 7 , 2013 from stomach cancer in zagreb , croatia , aged 79 .
