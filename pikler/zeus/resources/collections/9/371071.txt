Jackie Earle Haley     jack `` jackie '' earle haley ( born july 14 , 1961 ) is an american movie , television , stage , and voice actor who is known for his roles in `` little children '' , `` nightmare on elm street '' , `` watchmen '' , `` human target '' , `` dark shadows '' , and in `` lincoln '' . haley was born on july 14 , 1961 in northridge , california . he was married three times , both ended in divorce . he was married to amelia cruz since 2004. he has a son , christopher , and a daughter , olivia .
