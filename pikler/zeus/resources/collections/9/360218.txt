The Hound of the Baskervilles (1982 TV serial)    the hound of the baskervilles ( 1982 tv serial ) the hound of the baskervilles ( 1982 ) is a british television programme . it was directed by peter duguid and starred tom baker as sherlock holmes and terence rigby as doctor watson . it is based on arthur conan doyle 's sherlock holmes novel `` the hound of the baskervilles '' . cast . - tom baker as sherlock holmes - terence rigby as dr. john h. watson - nicholas woodeson as sir henry baskerville - christopher ravenscroft as stapleton - kay adshead as beryl stapleton - will knightley as dr. mortimer the opinions of viewers at the time was divided the four-part serial aired in 1982 , and it has not done better over time . baker himself came to think of his performance as a failure .
