John Hemmingham     john hemmingham ( born 26 february 1963 ) is an association football fan and football administrator from sheffield , england . he is best known as the leader and trumpet player of the pukka pies england band . hemmingham started playing music at football matches in 1993 when he took a bugle to a sheffield wednesday match away at everton 's goodison park and played the fanfare to aida .
