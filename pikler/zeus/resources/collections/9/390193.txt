Sokolniki Exhibition and Convention Centre     sokolniki exhibition and convention centre is a convention centre in moscow , russia . it is in sokolniki park . the convention hall was opened in 1959 , during the soviet union . it was the first commercial hall after the opening of the all-russia exhibition centre in 1935. other websites . - sokolniki exhibition and convention centre - international exhibition of calligraphy - our victory - contemporary museum of calligraphy - the first handwritten constitution of the russian federation
