Barbara Acklin     barbara acklin ( february 28 , 1943 - november 27 , 1998 ) was an american soul singer and songwriter . she was very popular in the 1960s and 1970s . she had several hit singles , including `` love makes a woman '' . she was born in oakland , california and raised in chicago . acklin later moved to omaha , nebraska . she died there from pneumonia when she was 55 .
