Going the Distance (2010 movie)    going the distance ( 2010 movie ) going the distance is a 2010 american romantic comedy movie directed by nanette burstein . it stars drew barrymore , justin long and christina applegate . it is about erin , a graduate school student from san francisco who meets a man in new york city and tries to keep the relationship going when she returns home . the movie was released in september 2010. it received mixed reviews from critics , but did very well at the box office .
