Webbed feet     webbed toes is the common name for syndactyly . webbed feet happens when two or more toes grow together instead of separately . this is common in many birds such as ducks . it is also common in amphibians such as frogs and mammals such as kangaroos . in humans , it is not normal . it only happens to 3-10 in 10,000 live births . famous people with webbed feet . - dan aykroyd – canada , actor - jacqui hurley - ireland , sports broadcaster - ashton kutcher - united states , actor - joseph stalin – soviet union , general secretary of the communist party of the soviet union
