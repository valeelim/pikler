Pope Anastasius II     pope anastasius ii ( ; died 16 november 498 ) was an italian cleric of the roman catholic church and the 50th pope from 496 until his death in 498. anastasius was born in rome . anastasius was elected pope on november 24 , 496. anastasius ii was pontiff during a time of controversy involving patriarch acacius of constantinople anastasius died in 498 at the height of the crisis . in the `` divine comedy '' , dante placed this pope in hell . related pages . - list of popes other websites . - catholic hierarchy , popes in sequence - cardinals of the holy roman church , anastasius ii
