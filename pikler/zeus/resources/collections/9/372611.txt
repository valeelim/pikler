Ashiq Mohamed Warsi     ashiq mohamed warsi ( died august 1940 ) was a political figure of british india and a leader of the all india muslim league . he was one of the members of the working committee of the league , which drafted the famous lahore resolution in march 1940. warsi 's sudden death in august 1940 was deeply mourned by his colleagues . he had no connection to sayeeda warsi , baroness warsi , a tory politician and life peer in england .
