Kramer vs. Kramer     kramer vs. kramer is a 1979 american drama movie . it was written and directed by robert benton . it is based on a novel by avery corman . it stars dustin hoffman and meryl streep as new york city married couple ted and joanna kramer . she leaves him and their son ; the couple divorce . fifteen months later , she decides she wants custody of the child , taking ted to court .
