David Strathairn     david russell strathairn ( born january 26 , 1949 ) is an american actor and entertainer . he was nominated for an academy award in 2006 for his role as edward r. murrow in `` good night , and good luck '' . he is also known for his role as secretary of state william h. seward in the 2012 movie `` lincoln '' . he won an emmy award in 2010. strathairn was born on january 26 , 1949 in san francisco , california . his grandparents were scottish . he studied at redwood high school , at williams college , and he studied to become a clown at ringling brothers and barnum & bailey clown college . strathairn has been married to logan goodman since 1980. they have two children . he now lives in poughkeepsie , new york .
