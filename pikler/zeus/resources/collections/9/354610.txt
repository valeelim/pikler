Claire McCaskill     claire conner mccaskill ( born july 24 , 1953 ) is a senior united states senator from missouri and a member of the u.s. democratic party . she was the first female elected to the senate of united states in her own right . before being elected to united states senate mccaskill was the missouri state auditor from 1999 to 2007. she is a native of rolla and a graduate of the university of missouri .
