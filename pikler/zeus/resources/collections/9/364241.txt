Carlos Finlay     carlos juan finlay ( december 3 , 1833 – august 20 , 1915 ) was a cuban physician and scientist who started the interest in yellow fever research . finlay was born juan carlos finlay y barres , in puerto príncipe ( now camagüey ) , cuba . his family is of french and scottish heritage . in 1853 he went to jefferson medical college in philadelphia , pennsylvania . he graduated in 1855. he finished his studies in havana and in paris . later he moved to havana and started a medical practice . finlay continued his work in the 1870s . he was the first to come up with the idea that a mosquito was a carrier for a disease that causes yellow fever . he discovered that the spread and cause of yellow fever was from a mosquito . the mosquito would bite a person with the disease and would then bite and infect a healthy person . a year later finlay identified the mosquito of the genus aedes as the insect transmitting yellow fever . his research led to the control of the mosquito population as a way to control the spread of the disease .
