Jerry Robinson     sherrill david robinson ( january 1 , 1922 – december 7 , 2011 ) or jerry robinson , was an american comic book artist and writer . he created the joker and robin . robinson was born on january 1 , 1922 in trenton , new jersey . he studied at columbia university . robinson died on january 7 , 2011 from natural causes in his home in staten island , new york , aged 89. other websites . - jerry robinson papers 1953–2009 at syracuse university - jerry robinson official site
