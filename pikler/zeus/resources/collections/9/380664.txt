Marilyn vos Savant     marilyn vos savant ( born august 11 , 1946 in st. louis ) is an american magazine columnist , playwright and author . she became very famous in the `` guinness book of world records '' category for the highest iq . since 1986 , she has hosted `` parade '' magazine 's `` ask marilyn '' column . in the column she answers the questions from readers on a variety of subjects .
