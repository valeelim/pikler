Phil Harris     phil harris ( june 24 , 1904 - august 11 , 1995 ) was an american actor . he was known for being the voice of thomas o'malley the alley cat in `` the aristocats '' in 1970 and cri-kee in `` mulan '' ( 1998 ) . he died in 1995 from a heart attack in rancho mirage , california , aged 91 .
