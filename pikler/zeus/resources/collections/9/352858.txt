Hurricane Fifi     hurricane fifi ( later known as hurricane orlene ) was a deadly hurricane that impacted central america in 1974. at the time that it hit , fifi was the worst natural disaster to ever hit honduras . it formed off the coast of jamaica and made landfall as a category 2 hurricane . it then weakened significantly and became a tropical storm , and then a tropical depression . it moved into the pacific and regained strength . fifi was a tropical cyclone that lasted for 18 hours and was renamed orlene . orlene then intensified into a hurricane and reached category 2 status . orlene made landfall in mexico and became extratropical over the mountains .
