Cable cars in Haifa     cable cars in haifa go from mount carmel to the mediterranean beaches . in 2007 the city hall of haifa decided to extend the line to the university of haifa and open a new line from mount carmel to haifa bay . the new projects will involve building a new central bus station and new bus routes across the city . the project is still in construction . other websites . - haifa bay – technion – university cable car at yefeh nof
