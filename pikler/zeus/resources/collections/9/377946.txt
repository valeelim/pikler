Kay Bailey Hutchison     kay bailey hutchison ( born july 22 , 1943 ) is an american politician . she was the senior senator from texas . hutchison is a member of the u.s. republican party . in 2001 , she was named one of the thirty most powerful women in america by the `` ladies home journal '' . on june 29 , 2017 , hutchison was nominated to be the next united states permanent representative to nato by donald trump . she was confirmed by the senate in a voice vote on august 3 , 2017 .
