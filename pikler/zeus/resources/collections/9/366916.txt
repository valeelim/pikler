Islam in Lebanon     islam in lebanon is divided between four muslim sects ; shias , sunnis , alawites , and ismailis including the druze . muslims ( including druze ) account for 59 % of the total population of lebanon , where 39 % are christians . shiites - about 27 % of the total population of lebanon - live mostly in the northern area of the beqaa valley , southern lebanon and southern areas of beirut . about 26 % of the lebanese population is sunni , concentrated largely in coastal cities like west beirut , tripoli and sidon . the druze account for 5 % of the population .
