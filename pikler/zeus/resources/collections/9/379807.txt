Elizabeth     the name elizabeth is a female given name . there are many variants of it worldwide . it was the 10th most popular in the united states , and among the top 50 in england and wales . famous people with the name include elizabeth taylor and elizabeth arden . in the new testament , elizabeth is the mother of john the baptist and the wife of zechariah . ( gospel of luke chapter 1 ) elizabeth can be spelt elisabeth , and can be shortened to liz , lizzie , lisette , lilly , libby , eliza , beth and lisa .
