Inge Lønning     inge lønning ( 20 february 1938 – 24 march 2013 ) was a norwegian theologian , educator , and politician for the conservative party of norway . lønning was born on 20 february 1938 in fana , bergen , norway . he was raised in oslo , norway . lønning studied at the university of oslo . he served as a councilman in oslo . lønning died on 24 march 2013 from an illness after going on a trip in beitostølen , norway , aged 75. other websites . - profile in nordic council
