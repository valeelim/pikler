Nadeem-Shravan     nadeem-shravan are a music director duo of bollywood , a movie industry in india . the duo is made of nadeem saifi and shravan rathod . they started their career in the late 80s . their first big success came in 1990 with the musical film `` aashiqui '' . since then they have composed music for over 50 movies . they have won four filmfare awards , one star screen award and a zee cine award in the category for best music director . some of their biggest hits include `` aashiqui '' ( 1990 ) , `` saajan '' ( 1991 ) , `` phool aur kaante '' ( 1991 ) , `` sadak '' ( 1991 ) , `` deewana '' , `` hum hain rahi pyar ke '' ( 1993 ) , `` dilwale '' ( 1994 ) , `` raja '' ( 1995 ) , `` raja hindustani '' ( 1996 ) , `` pardes '' ( 1997 ) , `` dhadkan '' ( 2000 ) , `` kasoor '' ( 2001 ) , `` andaaz '' ( 2003 ) .
