Ottawa Senators (CWHL)    ottawa senators ( cwhl ) the ottawa senators are a canadian women 's ice hockey team . they play in the canadian women 's hockey league ( cwhl ) . the team began playing in 1988. the team was then known as the national capital raiders . in 1989 the team changed its name to ottawa raiders . in 2007 the team again changed its name to the ottawa capital canucks . the team changed its name to the ottawa senators in 2008. the current coach of the team is brad marsh . he used to play for the ottawa senators in the national hockey league ( nhl ) . other websites . - official website
