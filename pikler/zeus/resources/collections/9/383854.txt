Merrigum, Victoria    merrigum , victoria merrigum is a small town in the goulburn valley region of victoria , australia . the town is part of city of greater shepparton local government area . it is north of the state capital , melbourne . in 2011 there were 672 people living in the town . the name `` merrigum '' is an australian aboriginal word meaning a small plain . merrigum post office opened on 1 february 1875. golfers play at the course of the merrigum golf club .
