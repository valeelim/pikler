Makassar Strait     makassar strait is a strait between the islands of borneo and sulawesi in indonesia . to the north it joins the celebes sea . to the south it meets the java sea . the mahakam river of borneo empties into the strait . ports along the strait include balikpapan in borneo , and makassar and palu in sulawesi . the city of samarinda is 48 km ( 30 mi ) from the strait , along the mahakam . the strait is a common shipping route for ships too big to fit through the straits of malacca .
