Dhobi Ghat     dhobi ghat or dhoby ghaut is a word used in various parts of south asia . it means a place where the traditional `` dhobi '' caste of washermen and women go to wash clothes . the word usually means a place near a river or stream , in india , pakistan and bangladesh , but a `` dhobi ghat '' does n't necessarily have to be near a source of natural water . the world 's largest `` dhobi ghat '' is said to be in mumbai , india . it figured in the famous `` dhobi ghat '' ( 2010 ) .
