List of Big Time Rush episodes     `` big time rush '' , ( also known as `` btr '' ) , is a television series that airs on the nickelodeon network . it is about four minnesota teenagers , played by kendall schmidt , james maslow , carlos pena , jr. and logan henderson , who form a boy band and move to hollywood , california . ciara bravo , tanya chisholm , and stephen kramer glickman co-star . as of may 12 , 2012 , the show has aired fifty episodes over three seasons , from 2009 to 2012 .
