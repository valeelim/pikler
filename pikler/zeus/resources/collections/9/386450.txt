Motherland Party (Azerbaijan)    motherland party ( azerbaijan ) the motherland party ( ) is a nationalist and conservative political party in azerbaijan . it was founded in 1990. its members are mainly azerbaijanis who come from armenia . the party 's leader fazail agamali is a former deputy minister for social protection . the party 's aim is to create a `` free , powerful , democratic and entire azerbaijan '' . at the 2010 parliamentary elections , it won 2 out of 125 seats . other websites . - official website
