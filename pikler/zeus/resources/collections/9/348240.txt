Mambo     mambo is a word with afro-latin connections . most common meanings are : - mambo ( rhythm ) : a cuban or afrocuban musical rhythm and its associated dance . invented around 1950 in havana . - mambo section : a musical bridge or section in some kinds of cuban ( and caribbean ) music . - mambo ( voodoo ) : a haitian word for a voodoo priestess . the word also occurs in the title of many pieces of recorded music .
