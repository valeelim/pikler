Steve Albert     steve albert ( born 1950 ) is an american former sportscaster . he is the younger brother of marv albert and al albert and the uncle of kenny albert . he was the lead play-by-play voice of `` showtime championship boxing '' . he has been sports anchor for three new york local television affiliates - wnbc-tv , wwor-tv , and wcbs-tv . he has been a play-by-play voice for the new jersey nets , new orleans hornets , phoenix suns , cleveland cavaliers , new york mets , new york jets , new york rangers and new york islanders . he is the current television voice of the phoenix suns starting in 2012 .
