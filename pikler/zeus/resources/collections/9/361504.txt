Hirado, Nagasaki    hirado , nagasaki hirado has been a port since the nara period . during the edo period , hirado was the seat of the hirado domain . the city developed around hirado castle ( `` hirado-jō '' ) . the first frenchman to arrive in japan was francis caron . he live in hirado . the city is located on the island of the same name ; and it has expanded to the island of kyūshū . the two parts of the city are connected by the hirado bridge which was built in 1977. other websites . - hirado city website ; - american friendship doll at hirado kindergarten
