Bob Balaban     robert elmer `` bob '' balaban ( born august 16 , 1945 ) is an american actor , author , producer , and director who is known for his roles in `` moonrise kingdom '' , `` gosford park '' , `` close encounters of the third kind '' , `` best in show '' , `` lady in the water '' , and as his guest-star role as frank buffay , sr. in `` friends '' . balaban was born on august 16 , 1945 in chicago , illinois to a jewish family . his grandparents were russian immagrants who came to chicago . balaban studied at colgate university and at new york university . he has been married to lynn grossman since 1977. they had two children , hazel and mariah . he is jewish . balaban now lives in manhattan , new york .
