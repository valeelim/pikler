Datsik (musician)    datsik ( musician ) datsik ( real name : troy beetles ) ( born june 9 , 1988 in kelowna , british columbia ) is a dubstep dj and music producer . datsik started producing dubstep after he heard his friend excision play at the shambhala music festival in 2008. the name datsik was inspired by beetles ' former xbox live gamertag . he started his own record label called firepower records in early 2012. he released his debut studio album titled , vitamin d on april 10 , 2012. he is known to work frequently with fellow canadian producers excision and downlink . other websites . - datsik 's official website
