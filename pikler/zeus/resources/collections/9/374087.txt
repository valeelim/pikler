John Patrick Hopkins     john patrick hopkins ( october 29 , 1858 – october 13 , 1918 ) was an american politician . he was the mayor of chicago , illinois ( 1893–1895 ) . he was a democrat . hopkins was born on october 29 , 1858 in buffalo , new york . he was never married . hopkins died on october 13 , 1918 in chicago , illinois from a flu , aged 59. other websites . - john patrick hopkins at find-a-grave
