Magellanic penguin     the magellanic penguin , `` spheniscus magellanicus '' , is the most numerous of the spheniscus family . there are about 3,600,000 magellanic penguins in the world . they breed once a year and lay two eggs of a similar size . they are 70cm tall and weigh 9 pounds or 4 kilograms . they are found in the falkland islands , south america , chile and argentina and migrate brazil . they are warm weathered penguins and during the warm weather they lose feathers around their eyes . when it gets colder they grow these feathers back around their eyes . they were first seen in 1519 on a voyage ( trip ) to south america . they hunt for food on their own when they are 60-70 days old . other websites . - -
