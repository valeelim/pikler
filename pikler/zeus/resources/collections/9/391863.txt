Ellen Wilkinson     ellen cicely wilkinson ( october 6 , 1891 - february 6 , 1947 ) was an english politician . she was a labour member of parliament for middlesbrough and later jarrow . wilkinson was one of the first females in britain to become a member of the british parliament . she was one of the founding members for the communist party around 1920. ellen was born in ardwick , manchester . she committed suicide with an overdose of barbiturates . wilkinson died in st mary 's hospital , london at age fifty-five .
