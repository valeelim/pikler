Merrill Swain     merrill swain is a professor emerita of second-language education at the ontario institute for studies in education , university of toronto . she developed the output hypothesis . this idea about second-language acquisition says that learners can not become very good at the grammar of a language only by taking in language and trying to understand it . they must also speak to learn . swain also worked with michael canale on communicative competence . swain was the president of the american association for applied linguistics in 1998. she received her phd at the university of california . other websites . - merrill swain at the ontario institute of studies in education
