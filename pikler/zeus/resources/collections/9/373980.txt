Rogers Arena     rogers arena is a sports arena in vancouver , british columbia . the arena opened on september 21 , 1995 , and is the current home to the vancouver canucks of the national hockey league . the arena was first known as general motors place from 1995 to 2010. it was renamed canada hockey place for a short time and held ice hockey events during the 2010 winter olympics . the arena used to be the home of the vancouver grizzlies from 1995 to 2001 before they moved to memphis , tennessee to become the memphis grizzlies . rogers communications has naming rights for the arena right now . the arena has hosted the wwf 's ppv in 1996 , ice hockey events during the 2010 winter olympics and ufc 's 115 and 131. other websites . - official website
