Iris Chang     iris chang ( march 28 , 1968 - november 9 , 2004 ) was an american writer and journalist . she wrote several best-selling books . her books included `` thread of the silkworm '' , `` the rape of nanking '' and `` the chinese of america '' . she was born in princeton , new jersey . she was raised in illinois . chang spent her last years living in san jose , california . she used risperdal and depakote due to mood swings and depression . on november 9 , 2004 , chang committed suicide by using a revolver .
