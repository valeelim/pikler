Ben Mendelsohn     paul benjamin `` ben '' mendelsohn ( born 3 april 1969 ) is an australian movie , television , stage , and voice actor who is known for his roles as andrew `` pope '' cody in `` animal kingdom '' , russell in `` killing them softly '' , john daggett in `` the dark knight rises '' and as orson krennic in `` rogue one '' . mendelsohn was born on 3 april 1969 in melbourne , australia he was married to emma forrest since 2012 .
