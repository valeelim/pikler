Paul Verhoeven     paul verhoeven ( born 18 july 1938 ) is a dutch movie director , producer and writer . he was born in amsterdam . he directed american movies `` robocop '' , `` total recall '' , `` basic instinct '' , `` showgirls '' and `` starship troopers '' . he wrote and directed dutch movie `` black book '' . during his youth verhoeven was for a short time a pentecostal christian . although he is an atheist , he takes a great interest in jesus . he is a member of the jesus seminar and wrote `` jesus of nazareth '' in 2007 , a liberal view on jesus . he is intending to make a movie about jesus .
