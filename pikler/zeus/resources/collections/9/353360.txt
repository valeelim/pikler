Burn (song)    burn ( song ) `` burn '' is a song by american singer usher . it was released from his album `` confessions '' ( 2004 ) . it was written by usher and jermaine dupri , bryan-michael cox . it was produced by dupri and cox . the song talks about a breakup in a relationship . many music critics believed that it was usher 's `` personal struggles '' . the song won the 2005 kids choice award for favorite song . `` burn '' was supposed to be the album 's lead single . however , because many critics liked the song `` yeah ! `` , it was released second . it was released in july 2004 as the second single . it reached a lot of music charts worldwide . it reached number one on the `` billboard '' hot 100 chart . at the time , `` yeah ! '' was still at number one . `` burn '' replaced it . it stayed on the chart for eight weeks . `` burn '' was certified platinum in australia and gold in new zealand and the united states . the song was given positive reviews . it won several awards . in 2009 , it was named the 21st most successful song of the 2000s . it was included on the `` billboard '' hot 100 songs of the decade .
