Nick Frost     nicholas john `` nick '' frost ( born 28 march 1972 ) is an english movie , television , stage , voice actor , comedian and screenwriter . he is best known for his roles `` spaced , as ed in `` shaun of the dead , danny butterman in `` hot fuzz '' , clive gollings in `` paul '' , and as ron in `` attack the block '' . he is also well known for working with simon pegg . frost was born on 28 march 1972 in dagenham , east london , england . he studied at bell high school . he is married to christina frost .
