Jeffrey DeMunn     jeffrey demunn ( born april 25 , 1947 ) or jeff demunn is an american stage , movie and television actor . he played harry terwilliger in the movie `` the green mile '' . he played dale horvath in the tv series `` the walking dead '' . he was born in buffalo , new york and studied at the uninon college . demunn was married to ann sekjaer in 1974 until they divorced in 1995. they had two children . demunn has been married to kerry leah since 2001 .
