Atakora Department     atakora is the northwestern department of benin . it borders togo to the west and burkina faso to the north . it is the most mountainous area of benin . it is where the president , mathieu kérékou , was born . it is a much drier area of the country than the south . there are 3 major seasons , a rainy season that runs from may or june to september or october , a harmattan season of cool , dry winds that runs from october or november to february , and a hot , famine season that runs from february to april . atakora is divided into the communes of boukoumbé , cobly , kérou , kouandé , matéri , natitingou , pehonko , tanguiéta , and toucountouna . in 1999 the southern part of atakora was split off to form the new donga department .
