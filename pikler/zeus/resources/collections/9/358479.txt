Duttaphrynus     duttaphrynus is a genus of true toads . they live in southwestern and southern china ( including taiwan and hainan ) and throughout southern asia from northern pakistan and nepal through india to sri lanka , andaman island , sumatra , java , borneo , and bali . these toads have heads with bony ridges . the nose area is short and blunt . the first finger of these toads is longer than the second . the toes are half webbed . the toads are brown above and yellow beneath , marbled with brown . other websites . - . 2007. amphibian species of the world : an online reference . version 5.1 ( 10 october , 2007 ) . `` duttaphrynus '' . electronic database accessible at http : //research.amnh.org/herpetology/amphibia/index.php . american museum of natural history , new york , usa .
