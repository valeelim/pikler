Nielson Field     nielson field was asia 's first airport . it opened in 1937. it was set up by laurie reuben nielson , a new zealander . it was converted into a military airbase during world war ii . it was built on 42 hectares of swampland . in 1948 , it stopped operating and was converted into a business district . its runways became the main avenues . its radio or control tower is still standing as a museum . today this district is the ayala triangle in makati city .
