Frank Glieber     frank john glieber ( april 5 , 1934 - may 1 , 1985 ) was an american sportscaster . he called college football games for cbs radio , the dallas cowboys , cleveland browns on cbs , and the texas rangers . while working for cbs , glieber called national football league , national basketball association , college football , college basketball , soccer , tennis , bowling , and golf . he died of a heart attack in may of 1985 .
