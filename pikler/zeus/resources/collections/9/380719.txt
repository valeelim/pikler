Wally Pfister     walter c. `` wally '' pfister ( born july 8 , 1961 ) is an academy award-winning american cinematographer and movie director who is known for his cinematography in `` batman begins '' , `` memento '' , `` moneyball '' , `` insomnia '' , `` the dark knight '' , `` inception '' , and in `` the dark knight rises '' . he began his directing career in the 2014 movie `` transcendence '' . pfister was born on july 8 , 1961 in chicago , illinois . he was raised both in chicago and in new york city , new york . pfister was married to anna julien since 1992. they had three children . other websites . - a conversation with wally pfister
