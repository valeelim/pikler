Japan Airlines     japan airlines is an airline founded in 1951. its headquarters are in shinagawa , tokyo , japan . the airline and four of its sub-airlines ( j-air , jal express , jalways , and japan transocean air ) are members of the oneworld airline alliance . japan airlines currently goes to over 90 destinations . japan airlines began on august 1 , 1951. in 1954 , it started international flights . it bought it 's first jet aircraft in 1968 , the douglas dc-8 . japan air system merged with japan airlines in 2002. in 2007 , it joined the oneworld alliance . in january 2010 , japan airlines declared bankruptcy . british airways and qantas helped the airline pay off debts . it finally rose from bankruptcy in april 2011. other websites . - jal website
