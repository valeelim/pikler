Haem     a haem ( british english ) ( or heme ( american english ) is a type of co-factor . it is an iron ion in the center of a large organic ring called a `` porphyrin '' . not all porphyrins contain iron , but many porphyrin-containing metalloproteins have haem as their co-factor ; these are known as haemoproteins . haems are components of haemoglobin , the red pigment in blood . they are also components of a number of other haemoproteins .
