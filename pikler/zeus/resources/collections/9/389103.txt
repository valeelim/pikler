Nâzım Hikmet Ran     nâzım hikmet ran ( 15 january 1902 – 3 june 1963 ) , commonly known as nâzım hikmet , was a turkish poet , playwright and novelist . he was well liked for the `` lyrical flow of his statements '' . hikmet was often arrested for his political beliefs . he spent much of his adult life in prison or in exile . his poetry has been translated into more than fifty languages .
