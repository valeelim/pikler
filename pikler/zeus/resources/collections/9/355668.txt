Alex Borstein     alexandrea `` alex '' borstein ( born february 15 , 1973 ) is an american actress , voice actress , writer , and comedian . borstein is known for her long running role as lois griffin in the animated television series `` family guy '' , and a cast member on the sketch comedy series `` madtv '' . born in highland park , illinois and studied san francisco state university . borstein was trained in comedy at the acme comedy theatre , near hollywood , california , and was selected to join madtv after being searched by talent agents who noticed her comedy work at the theatre . borstein was a writer and voice actress for several shows like ; `` casper , pinky and the brain '' and `` power rangers : zeo '' , before joining the cast of `` madtv '' as a featured player , and as a theatre player in 1997. borstein is married to jackson douglas since 1999 and have a child barnaby douglas , and henrietta douglas .
