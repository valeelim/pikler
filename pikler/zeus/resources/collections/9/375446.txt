National Sport Center – Tel Aviv     the national sport center is a complex of sports venues and sport administration buildings in tel aviv , israel . it includes the olympic committee of israel building . the center is located in the western edge of the yarkon park near to the ramat gan stadium . it is a supplementary compound to the wingate institute in netanya . the national sport center was established in 1978 when a several tennis courts were built close to yarkon park . in 1980 , the building of the olympic committee of israel and the national athletics stadium were built nearby . other websites . - national sport center – tel aviv ( hebrew ) - the olympic committee of israel
