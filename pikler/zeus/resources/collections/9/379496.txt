Sultan Kösen     sultan kösen ( born december 10 , 1982 in mardin , turkey ) is the tallest man in the world ( 8 ft 3in ) after passing the height of xi shun . he also holds the record for the worlds biggest hands ( 27.5 cm ) . on february 9 , 2011 he was measured again by guinness and was named as the tallest person in the world .
