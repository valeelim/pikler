Grant's Tomb    grant 's tomb general grant national memorial or grant 's tomb is a memorial and resting place of 18th president of the united states and civil war general ulysses s. grant and his wife julia grant . the tomb is in manhattan , new york city , new york . the tomb was constructed by the united states congress . the tomb was constructed in 1890 ( five years after grant 's death ) and was finished on april 22 1897 ( grant 's 75th birthday ) . in 1902 , julia grant died and was later buried in the tomb . the tomb was decaying or falling apart and was vandalised during the 1980s until the tomb was fixed and restored in 1994. the tomb was rededicated in april 27 , 1997. it was later named an united states national landmark . other websites . - grant monument association - grant 's funeral and the mausoleum
