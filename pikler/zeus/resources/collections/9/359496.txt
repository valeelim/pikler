Julianna Margulies     julianna margulies ( born june 8 1966 ) is an emmy and golden globe award winning actress . she has received more screen actors guild awards than any other actress or actor . she played carol hathaway in the long-running series `` er '' ( 1994 to 2002 ) and alicia in the cbs legal drama `` the good wife '' . she also performed in sitcoms and theatrical movies . she was raised in new york .
