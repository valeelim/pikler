Park Gyu-su     park gyu-su ( korean : 박규수 , hanja : 朴珪壽 , 1807–1877 ) was a korean politician , silhak and neo-confucianism philosopher , thinker , and liberal activist . one of his pennames was hwanjae . his grandfather was park ji-won , a famous silhak and neo-confucianism philosopher and novelist . writing . - hwanjaejip ( 환재집 瓛齋集 ) - hwanjaejikgye ( 환재직계 瓛齋織啓 ) - hangjaesugye ( 환재수계 瓛齋繡啓 ) - geogajapbokgo ( 거가잡복고 居家雜服攷 )
