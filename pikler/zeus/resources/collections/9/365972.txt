Jayma Mays     jayma suzette mays ( born jamia suzette mays , july 16 , 1979 ) is an american movie , television , stage , voice actress and singer . she is known for her roles as emma pillsbury in `` glee '' , as charlie in `` ugly betty '' , as amy anderson in `` '' , and as grace winslow in `` the smurfs '' and in `` smurfs 2 '' . mays was born on july 16 , 1979 in bristol , tennessee . she studied at grundy high school , at southwest virginia community college , and at radford university . she was married to adam campbell since 2007 .
