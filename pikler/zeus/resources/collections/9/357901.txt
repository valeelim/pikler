Kyoko Yano     yano played on the japan women 's national football team at the 2003 fifa women 's world cup , the 2007 fifa women 's world cup and the 2011 fifa women 's world cup tournaments . she took part in the 2004 summer olympics and 2008 summer olympics . she played on the team which won a silver medal in the 2012 summer olympics at london . related pages . - sports in japan - japan at the olympics - football at the 2012 summer olympics – women 's tournament other websites . - 矢野喬子 ( yano kyoko ) at japan football association
