The Swing (Fragonard, 1767-1768)    the swing ( fragonard , 1767-1768 ) the swing ( 1767-1768 ) is an oil painting by jean-honoré fragonard . it was painted in 1767-1768. it measures 81 × 64 cm ( 31.9 × 25.2 in ) . the painting depicts a woman kicking off her shoe as she flies up in a swing . a young man gazes up her skirt while another man controls the swing with a rope . it hangs in the wallace collection , london .
