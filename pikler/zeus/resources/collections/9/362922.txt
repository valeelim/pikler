Mike Gorman     mike gorman ( born november 24 , 1947 , in dorchester , massachusetts ) is an american sportscaster . he calls games for the boston celtics and the connecticut sun . he was the voice of the rhode island rams football and basketball and the voice of the providence friars men 's basketball teams . gorman also called college basketball for espn and cbs and nba playoffs on tbs . gorman called two olympic games for nbc : tennis at the 1992 summer olympics and handball at the 2012 summer olympics .
