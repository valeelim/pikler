Godfrey Kneller     sir godfrey kneller , 1st baronet ( 8 august 1646 â€“ 19 october 1723 ) was the leading portrait painter in england during the late 17th and early 18th centuries . he was court painter to english and british monarchs from charles ii to george i. his major works include `` the chinese convert '' ( 1687 ) ; a series of four portraits of isaac newton ; a series of ten reigning european monarchs , including king louis xiv of france ; over 40 `` kit-cat portraits '' of members of the kit-cat club ; and ten `` beauties '' of the court of william iii . the beauties series was to match a similar series of ten beauties of the court of charles ii . the earlier portraits were painted by court painter before him , sir peter lely .
