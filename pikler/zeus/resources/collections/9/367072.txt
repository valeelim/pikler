Khattar     khattar are a large tribe of the punjab , scattered over both india and pakistan . in india they are classified as jatt and khatri ; whereas in pakistan they are generally classified as jatt or rajput . the khattars in pakistan are almost all muslims now whereas those in india are muslims , hindus and sikhs . some famous people of the khattar tribe in history include nawab muhammad hayat khan and sir sikandar hayat khan . a famous hindu khattar today is mr manohar lal khattar , who is chief minister in the province of haryana , india .
