Boston (card game)    boston ( card game ) boston is a trick-taking card game played by 4 people . it is thought to have started in france in the 1770s . the game is similar to whist . it is a strategy game . each game takes about 25 minutes to play . american boston is a different way to play the game . it is for 4 players in two partnerships with 2 packs of 52 cards . other websites . - the card game at thefreedictionary.com
