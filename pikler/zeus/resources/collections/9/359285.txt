Samantha Morton     samantha morton ( born may 13 , 1977 ) is a english actress . she was born in nottingham . she has been nominated for many major awards for her acting . she began acting in 1991 , although it was 2000 when she received recognition from worldwide . in particular she has been in movies , including `` sweet and lowdown '' ( 1999 ) and `` minority report '' ( 2002 ) .
