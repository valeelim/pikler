Louis Jean Pierre Vieillot     louis jean pierre vieillot ( 10 may 1748 , yvetot - 1831 , rouen ) was a french ornithologist . vieillot described a large number of birds for the first time , especially those he met during the time he spent in the west indies and north america . 26 genera and 32 species of north american birds were first described by him . most of the well-known north american birds , such as the wild turkey , the pintail , the cedar waxwing , and the scarlet tanager , were given latin names by him .
