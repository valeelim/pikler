Creepshow     creepshow is a 1982 horror movie directed by george a. romero and written by stephen king . some actors in the movie include hal holbrook , adrienne barbeau , fritz weaver , don keefer , tom atkins , ted danson , ed harris , leslie nielsen , gwen verdon and e. g. marshall . romero also help create the makeup and special effects along with artist tom savini . the movie had positive reviews . the movie was released on november 12 , 1982. two more sequels were made . roger ebert gave the movie 3 out of 4 stars .
