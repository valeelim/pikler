Ommaya reservoir     the ommaya reservoir is an invention by dr ayub k. ommaya , a famous neurosurgeon . during surgery a catheter - a thin tube - is placed into one of the ventricles of the brain . the catheter is attached to a reservoir right under the scalp . the ommaya reservoir can be used for a few different things : - to deliver chemotherapy directly to where a brain tumor is - to take samples of cerebrospinal fluid , the fluid that cushions and protects the brain and spinal cord . this can cut down on the number of painful spinal taps ( lumbar punctures ) the patient needs to have . - to give morphine directly into the brains of patients who are dying of cancer and are in terrible pain
