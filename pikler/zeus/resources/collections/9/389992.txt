Death of Diana, Princess of Wales    death of diana , princess of wales diana , princess of wales and her boyfriend , dodi al-fayed , died in a car crash in paris , on 31 august 1997. many people left flowers , candles , cards and personal messages for her in public places . she had a big funeral in london . concerts for diana took place shortly after her death . the concerts involved elton john and many others . john released a new version of his song , `` candle in the wind , '' in memory of diana .
