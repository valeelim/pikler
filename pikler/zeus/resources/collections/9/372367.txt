King Island, Alaska    king island , alaska king island is an island in the bering sea , west of alaska . in 2005 and 2006 the national science foundation ( nsf ) paid for a research project . they brought a few king island people back to the island . some of the people had not been back in 50 years . james cook was the first european to see the island in 1778. he named the island after a person in his group called james king . it is part of the bering sea piece of the alaska maritime national wildlife refuge . other websites . - ancient mask returned to alaska ghost village , msnbc , january 18 , 2008 - photogallery of traditional ecological knowledge ( tek ) of king island , alaska , oregon state university , october 28 , 2008 - munoz photographs - king island early 1950s - survey of a king island kayak
