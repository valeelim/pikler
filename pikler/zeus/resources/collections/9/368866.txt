1940 United States Census     the sixteenth united states census was done by the census bureau . it found the population of the united states to be 132,164,569. this was an increase of 7.3 percent from the 1930 population . the census date of record was april 1 , 1940. a number of new questions were asked . these included where people were 5 years before , highest level of education , and information about wages .
