Kaarlo Juho Ståhlberg     kaarlo juho ståhlberg ( 28 january 1865 – 22 september 1952 ) was finland 's first president , from 1919 to 1925. he was born in suomussalmi and was third from five children in the family . his first wife was hedvig wåhlberg . in 1920 he married ester elfving . president juho kusti paasikivi gave great value to ståhlberg . he said : `` ståhlberg was a man who never made mistakes '' . ståhlberg died in helsinki .
