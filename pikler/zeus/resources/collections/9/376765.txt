Mytilus coruscus     mytilus coruscus is the scientific name for the korean mussel . it is also called the hard-shelled mussel . it is a species of mussel and a marine bivalve mollusc . it is in the family mytilidae . this species is heavily used as a food item via mariculture in korea . and in china this species lives along the coasts of the subtropical western pacific ocean . it is found in the yellow sea and the sea of japan . it can also be found as far north as the peter the great gulf . this mussel generally inhabits the upper part of the sublittoral zone .
