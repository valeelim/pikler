Flying frog     a `` flying '' frog is a frog that has the ability to glide . they live their life in trees , high above the ground . alfred russel wallace made one of the earliest reports of a flying frog . the species he observed was later described by george albert boulenger as `` rhacophorus nigropalmatus '' . flying frogs includes members of the following genera : - `` ecnomiohyla '' ( hylidae ) - `` polypedates '' ( rhacophoridae ) - `` rhacophorus '' ( rhacophoridae )
