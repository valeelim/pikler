Albus Dumbledore     albus percival wulfric brian dumbledore is a fictional character of the `` harry potter '' series . he was the headmaster of hogwarts school of witchcraft and wizardry until his death . he first found harry when he was a little boy and since then they developed a relationship . in the `` harry potter '' movies he is played first by richard harris , and then by michael gambon after harris died . dumbledore is killed by severus snape in the sixth book/movie , after draco malfoy failed to do so . however , his character 's backstory is explored in the last book of the series . his death would lead to the final battle between harry potter and voldemort . though it is never mentioned in any of the books , the author announced publicly that dumbledore is gay .
