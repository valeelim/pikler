Triton     triton was the son of poseidon , the sea god , in greek mythology . he acted as poseidon 's messenger . he had the ability to make the seas calm by blowing on a conch shell . he is shown in greek art as having the body of a man and a fish tail , like a mermaid . sometimes he is shown with arms like the claws of a crab , at other times he has arms like a horse 's legs . he lived in a golden palace at the bottom of the ocean . sometimes triton was shown as being many people , known as tritones . the planet neptune 's moon , triton , was named after the god .
