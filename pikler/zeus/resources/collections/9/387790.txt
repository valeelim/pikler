Udaijin     the position was established in the taihō code of 702. the official acted in the context of a council of state ( `` daijō-kan '' ) which was made up of the three men . - the chancellor or prime minister ( `` daijō-daijin '' ) - the minister of the left ( `` sadaijin '' ) - the minister of the right . the taihō code system was replaced in the meiji period . related pages . - imperial household agency
