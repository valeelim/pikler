Dirk Bach     dirk bach ( april 23 , 1961 – october 1 , 2012 ) was a german television comedian , actor , voice actor and moderator . he is best known as `` pepe '' in the german version of sesame street ( `` sesamstraße '' ) . bach was born in cologne , germany on april 23 , 1961 and died on october 1 , 2012 in berlin , germany from heart failure . bach was openly gay and had a partner , thomas . bach was an activist for lgbt rights . television . movies . - 1983 : `` kiez – aufstieg und fall eines luden '' - 1984 : `` im himmel ist die hölle los / hullygully in käseburg '' - 1985 : `` peter macht den acker frisch !
