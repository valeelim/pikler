Harry Judd     harry judd ( born 23 december 1985 , chelmsford , essex ) is an english pop/rock musician . he is best known as being the drummer in the band mcfly . judd has made a guest appearance in the busted video for `` crashed the wedding '' as the drummer . in 2011 , he won `` strictly come dancing '' . in 2012 , he got engaged to his long term girlfriend izzy johnston . and is now married to her .
