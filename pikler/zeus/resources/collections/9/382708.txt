Leaving (EP)    leaving ( ep ) leaving is the sixth ep by american electronic music producer , skrillex . `` leaving '' was released on january 2 , 2013 to owsla 's subscription service called the nest . skrillex said that the title song `` leaving '' was created in his hotel room in mexico and that the song `` the reason '' was finished an hour before the release of the ep in moore 's hotel room in miami . other websites . - leaving at discogs
