Fathead minnow     the fathead minnow ( `` pimephales promelas '' ) is a species of freshwater fish . they belong to the `` pimephales '' genus of the cyprinid family . they live in north america and central canada . this minnow has been introduced to many areas by people . they are golden and are known as the rosy red minnow . they are a feeder fish and are sold in pet stores .
