Alan Kalter     alan kalter ( born march 21 , 1943 ) is an american television announcer . he is best known as the announcer for the `` late show with david letterman '' starting in 1995. kalter was born on march 21 , 1943 in new york city , new york . he was raised in brooklyn , new york . kalter studied at hobart college . he was married to carol cepler until they divorced . he married peggy masterson in 2003. kalter lives in stamford , connecticut .
