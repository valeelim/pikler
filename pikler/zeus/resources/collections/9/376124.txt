Jesus College, Cambridge    jesus college , cambridge jesus college is one of the colleges of the university of cambridge , england . it was created in 1496. it took over buildings from a nunnery ( a place where nuns live ) , including the chapel which was called jesus chapel . because of this it still looks more like a monastery than a college . it has very big gardens and is one of the largest colleges in cambridge with more than 700 students . some very famous people have studied here , for example thomas cranmer , samuel taylor coleridge and laurence sterne . other websites . - official website
