Rules of Engagement (TV series)    rules of engagement ( tv series ) rules of engagement is an american sitcom set in new york city . it was shown from february 5 , 2007 - may 20 , 2013. it was shown on cbs . it has five main characters . patrick warburton and megyn price play married couple jeff and audrey . oliver hudson and bianca kajlich play engaged couple adam and jen . david spade plays womanizing narcissistic single man russell . adhir kalyan plays timmy , russell 's office assistant .
