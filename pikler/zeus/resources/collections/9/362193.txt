Mark Ruffalo     mark alan ruffalo ( born november 22 , 1967 ) is an american director , screenwriter , producer , movie , television , and voice actor . he is known for his role as hulk in the 2012 action-adventure movie `` the avengers '' . ruffalo was born in kenosha , wisconsin on november 22 , 1967. ruffalo has been married to sunrise coigney since 2000. they have three children .
