The Glass House (2012 TV series)    the glass house ( 2012 tv series ) the glass house will be an american reality television series in which people who signed up get to tell the people what to do . fourteen contestants will live in the house and compete for $ 250,000 . the premiere will be on june 18 , 2012. on may 4 , 2012 , broadcasting company cbs told abc not to air `` the glass house '' . this was because cbs thought it was the same as their reality television series `` big brother '' . nineteen people who used to work on `` big brother '' are working on `` the glass house '' . on may 10 , 2012 , cbs started a lawsuit against abc .
