Celeste Holm     celeste holm ( april 29 , 1917 - july 15 , 2012 ) was an american actress . her father was norwegian . she was born , raised and died in new york city . holm died on july 15 , 2012 in new york city from a heart attack . she was 95. for her role as anne dettrey in `` gentleman 's agreement '' , holm won an academy award for best supporting actress and a golden globe award for best supporting actress .
