Adrenaline (album)    adrenaline ( album ) adrenaline is the first studio album by american metal band , deftones . `` adrenaline '' was released on october 3 , 1995. the album was originally known as `` communion '' but was later changed to `` adrenaline '' . the album was ranked as # 33 on music critic piero scaruffi 's list of best metal albums of all times .
