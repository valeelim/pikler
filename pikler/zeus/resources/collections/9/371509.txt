Adinath Temple, Maheshkhali    adinath temple , maheshkhali adinath temple is on the summit of a hill , on maheshkhali island , off the coast of cox 's bazar , bangladesh . the temple is dedicated to the hindu god adi nath , a synonym of shiva . the temple is famous for the fair held once a year at the foot of the hill . the fair lasts thirteen days and draws thousands of hindus from across bangladesh .
