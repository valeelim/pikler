Beaverton, Oregon    beaverton , oregon beaverton is a city in washington county , oregon , u.s.a. it is seven miles west of portland , oregon . the name of beaverton came from the native settlers , meaning `` place of the beaver '' . the beaverton population , according to the census of 2012 , was 92,680 people . beaverton is 19.73 sq . miles . beaverton is called a part of `` silicon forest '' , because many technology companies are located in beaverton . for example , linux technology , ibm , and tektronix . nike 's headquarters are located there .
