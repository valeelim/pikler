Wheatfield with Crows     wheatfield with crows is an oil painting by vincent van gogh . it was finished about the 10th july 1890 ( van gough died on the 29th july 1890 ) . in 1890 , van gogh wrote that he had made three paintings in auvers-sur-oise of large fields of wheat under troubled skies . `` wheat field with crows '' was one of them . it is almost certainly not the last painting of his life . this painting in particular is often thought to reflect his state of mind towards the end of his life .
