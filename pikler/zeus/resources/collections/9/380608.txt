Feni District     feni ( ) is a small district in southeastern bangladesh . it is part of the chittagong division . feni district was established on february 15 , 1984. it has six upazillas , 45 union parishads , and approximately 570 villages . the upazillas are : 1. feni sadar 2. sonagazi 3. fhulgazi 4. chagalnaiya 5. daganbhuiyan 6. parshuram
