Kow Swamp     kow swamp is a lake in north-central victoria , australia . it was once a swamp . it is now used for water storage . kow swamp is in the murray river valley , about west of the town of gunbower and the murray river , in the shire of campaspe . its name comes from the aboriginal word `` ghow '' means the white gypsum soil found at the swamp . it is a popular site for recreational fishing . the lake is about . its circumference is . kow swamp is an archaeological site which has produced human skeletal remains dating from the late pleistocene , over 10,000 years ago . kow swamp was used to store water from the murray for irrigation . in 1900 the capacity was increased to 51,730 megalitres .
