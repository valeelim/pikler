Mike Honda     michael makoto honda ( born june 27 , 1941 ) , also known as mike honda , is an american politician . he is the u.s. representative for the 17th district of california . honda was born in walnut grove , california in 1941. he is the son of japanese american parents . he calls himself `` sansei '' , which means that he is the grandson of people born in japan who immigrated to the us . other websites . - congressman mike honda at us house website - mike honda at peace corps
