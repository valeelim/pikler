The Many Ways     `` the many ways '' is a song by american singer usher . it was released as the last single from his debut album `` usher '' ( 1994 ) . the song did not do so well on the music charts . it reached at number 42 on the hot r & b/hip-hop singles & tracks chart . it did not chart on the hot 100. track listing . - us single - 1. many ways [ album version ] - 2. many ways [ radio version ] - 3. many ways [ album instrumental ]
