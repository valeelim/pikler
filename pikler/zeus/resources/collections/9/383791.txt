Daniel Akaka     daniel kahikina akaka ( born september 11 , 1924 ) is an american politician who was a united states senator from hawaii from 1990 to 2013. a member of the democratic party , akaka is the first u.s. senator of native hawaiian ancestry . he was the senior senator of hawaii for a few weeks when daniel inouye died in december 2012. he studied at the university of hawaii . in 1969 , he was hired by the department of education as a chief program planner . in the 1970s he served in various governmental positions . he was first elected to the united states house of representatives in 1976 to represent hawaii 's second congressional district , and he served for 13 years .
