Whatever     the term whatever is american slang . it means `` whatever you say '' or `` i do not care what you say '' . it is used to dismiss something someone else has said . in the late 20th and early 21st century , the word became a sentence in its own right . the character archie bunker , from the show `` all in the family '' , used the phrase often to dismiss his wife edith in the 1970s . it was strongly prevalent among teenage girls in the 1980s and 1990s . `` whatever '' was made popular by the film , clueless .
