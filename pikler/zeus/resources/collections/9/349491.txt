BAFTA Academy Fellowship Award     the bafta fellowship is a lifetime achievement award presented by the british academy of film and television arts ( bafta ) . it has been given since 1971 `` in recognition of outstanding achievement in the art forms of the moving image '' , and is the highest honour the academy can give . fellowship recipients have been mostly film directors , but some have also been awarded to actors , movie and television producers , cinematographers , film editors , and screenwriters . other websites . - bafta official site
