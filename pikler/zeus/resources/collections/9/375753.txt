Sandy Hook Elementary School shooting     the sandy hook elementary school shooting was a school shooting which happened in connecticut , united states on friday , december 14 , 2012. twenty-eight people were killed in the shooting , including many children and teachers . the shooter was adam peter lanza . he shot and killed his mother , before going to the sandy hook elementary school in newtown , connecticut . he shot twenty children and six adult staff members . lanza committed suicide ( killed himself ) .
