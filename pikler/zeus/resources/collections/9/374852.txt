Carmelit     the carmelit ( ) is the first rapid transit rail system in israel . it opened in 1959 after three years of building . it was closed from 1986 to 1992 for renovations . the metro has only two lines , which run from the sea to mount carmel . the system is named after mount carmel . other websites . - official carmelit information ( in hebrew ) . also available on the municipality 's site . - map of carmelit route and area around it ( in english ) - pictures of carmelit stations and cars
