Beautiful Creatures (2013 movie)    beautiful creatures ( 2013 movie ) beautiful creatures is a 2013 american romantic-fantasy drama set in south carolina . it stars emmy rossum , viola davis and emma thompson . thompson plays mrs. lincoln and sarafine . `` beautiful creatures '' was released on february 14 , 2013 for the united states . jeremy irons also stars . the film is based on the novel of the same name by kami garcia and margaret stohl .
