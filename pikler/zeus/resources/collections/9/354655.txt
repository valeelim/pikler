Jeffrey Tambor     jeffrey michael tambor ( born july 8 , 1944 in san francisco , california ) is an american actor and voice actor who is known for his role as hank knigsley on `` the larry sanders show '' , king atlantis in `` spongebob squarepants '' , and as george bluth , sr. and oscar bluth on `` arrested development '' . he won a golden globe award and an emmy award in 2015. tambor was married to kathryn mitchell from 1991 until they divorced in 1999. he is currently married to kasia ostlun since 2001 and have 4 children together .
