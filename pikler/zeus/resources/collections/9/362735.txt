Nick Halling     nick halling is a british sportscaster . he used to be an nfl broadcaster . he called nfl europe and nfl games on fox , making the first non-american to call an nfl game for an american television network . he also called nfl games for sky sports . he also does play-by-play for channel five 's coverage of the national hockey league . for sky sports , he currently does boxing and ten-pin bowling . he is a narrator for goalissimo , the football league show , and the world 's strongest man .
