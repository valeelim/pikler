Lil Freak     `` lil freak '' is a song by american singer usher . it was released as the second single from his album `` raymond v. raymond '' ( 2010 ) . the song features trinidadian rapper nicki minaj . it was written by minaj , usher , ester dean , blac elvis and polow da don . it samples american soul singer stevie wonder 's 1973 hit , `` living for the city '' . `` lil freak '' was released on march 2 , 2010 .
