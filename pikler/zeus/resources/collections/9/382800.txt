Alagappa Chettiar College of Engineering and Technology     alagappa chettiar college of engineering and technology ( accet-karaikudi ) is an autonomous ( with effect from 2009 ) college of engineering in karaikudi , tamil nadu , india . it was established in 1952. the college is under the control of the state government . it is a member institute under the technical education quality improvement programme in india ( 11 colleges were selected from tamil nadu ) .
