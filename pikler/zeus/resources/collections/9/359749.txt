Frank Langella     frank a. langella , jr. ( born january 1 , 1938 ) is an american stage and movie actor . he has won a tony award for best leading actor in a play for his role as richard nixon in the play `` frost/nixon '' ( 2006 ) , and later received an academy award nomination for best actor in a leading role for the same role in the movie version , `` frost/nixon '' ( 2008 ) . langella is also known for his other movie appearances such as in ; `` superman returns '' , `` the caller '' , '' the beast '' , `` house of d '' , `` the box '' , `` wall street : money never sleeps '' , `` unknown '' , and has recently appeared in the 2012 movie `` robot & frank '' . langella was born in bayonne , new jersey on january 1 , 1938 , and was educated at syracuse university . langella was married to ruth weil from 1977 until they divorced in 1996. he was partners with whoopi goldberg from 1996 until they separated in 2001. he had 2 children with ruth weil .
