Jean-Louis Trintignant     jean-louis xavier trintignant ( born 11 december 1930 ) is an french actor . he won an césar award for best actor as georges in the 2012 french movie `` amour '' . trintignant was born on 11 december 1930 in piolenc , vaucluse , france . he was raised in piolenc and in paris . trintignant was married to stéphane audran until they divorced . then he was married to nadine marquand until they divorced . other websites . - jean-louis trintignant biography on newwavefilm.com - `` jean-louis trintignant '' at `` new wave film ''
