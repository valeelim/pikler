James Newton Howard     james newton howard ( born june 9 , 1951 ) is an american composer . he is best known for his scores to movies . a score is the music that is heard during a movie . he has written the music for over 100 movies . he is now one of the most well-known composers for movies . he has eight academy award nominations . some of howard 's best known movie scores include `` the prince of tides '' ( 1991 ) , `` the fugitive '' ( 1993 ) , `` dinosaur '' ( 2000 ) , `` king kong '' ( 2005 ) , `` batman begins '' , `` i am legend '' ( 2007 ) `` the dark knight '' ( 2008 ) . recent movies include `` green lantern '' ( 2011 ) , `` the hunger games '' ( 2012 ) , `` snow white and the huntsman '' ( 2012 ) , and `` the bourne legacy '' ( 2012 ) . howard is also known for working with director m. night shyamalan . he has scored all shyamalan 's movies since `` the sixth sense '' ( 1999 ) . from 1986 to 1987 , howard was married to actress rosanna arquette .
