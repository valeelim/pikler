Ben Herring     ben herring ( born 14 march 1980 , auckland , new zealand ) is a former rugby union player . he played for the leicester tigers in the guinness premiership . his position is flanker . herring missed out on a 2007 super 14 rugby contract with any new zealand super 14 team . he negotiated a move to join the leicester tigers in early 2007. in april 2009 herring retired on medical advice after suffering a series of concussions . other websites . - leicester profile
