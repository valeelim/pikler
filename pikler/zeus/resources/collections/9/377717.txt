Uyghur language     uyghur ( , `` uyghurche '' ) , formerly known as eastern turkish , is a turkic language with 8 to 11 million speakers , spoken primarily by the uyghur people in the xinjiang uyghur autonomous region of western china . there are several writing systems used to write uyghur . while uyghurs usually use the uyghur alphabet , an alphabet based on the arabic alphabet , there are also two latin and one cyrillic alphabet that can be used to write uyghur .
