World IPv6 Day     world ipv6 day was an event on january 12 , 2011. on world ipv6 day , many websites tested ipv6 , the new version of the `` internet protocol '' . there was another event on june 6 , 2012 called `` world ipv6 launch day '' . on world ipv6 launch day , the internet society hoped that ipv6 would really be enabled and work on many websites .
