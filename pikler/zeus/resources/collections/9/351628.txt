Suinae     suinae is a subfamily of mammals . it includes at least some of the living members of the family suidae and their closest relatives—the domestic pig and related species , such as babirusas . in their 1997 `` classification of mammals '' , malcolm c. mckenna and susan k. bell classify the suinae as follows : - tribe suini - genus † '' eumaiochoerus '' ( miocene ) - genus † '' hippopotamodon '' ( miocene to pleistocene ) - genus † '' korynochoerus '' ( miocene to pliocene ) - genus † '' microstonyx '' ( miocene ) - genus `` sus '' ( miocene to recent ) - tribe potamochoerini - genus † '' celebochoerus '' ( pliocene to pleistocene ) - genus `` hylochoerus '' ( pleistocene to recent ) - genus † '' kolpochoerus '' ( miocene to pleistocene ) - genus `` potamochoerus '' ( miocene to recent ) - genus † '' propotamochoerus '' ( miocene to pliocene ) - tribe †hippohyini - genus † '' hippohyus '' ( miocene to pleistocene ) - genus † '' sinohyus '' ( miocene ) - genus † '' sivahyus '' ( miocene to pliocene ) - tribe phacochoerini - genus † '' metridiochoerus '' ( pliocene to pleistocene ) - genus `` phacochoerus '' ( pliocene to recent )
