Alka Yagnik     alka yagnik ( born 20 march 1966 ) is an indian singer . she was born in kolkata , india . she has received seven filmfare awards and two national film awards . she began her music career in 1979. her first breakthrough came in 1988 when music directors , laxmikant-pyarelal gave her chance to sing for `` tezaab '' . she sang `` ek do teen '' which became hit that year , she also won her first award for that song . she was popular from early 90s until mid 2000s . she often sang duet songs with fellow singers , udit narayan and kumar sanu .
