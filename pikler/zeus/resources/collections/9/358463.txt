Ansonia (genus)    ansonia ( genus ) ansonia ( stream toads ) is a small genus of true toads . they are found in south india ; northern thailand ; malay peninsula ; tioman island ; borneo and mindanao ( philippines ) . these are a small forest species . they spawn in streams and have torrent-adapted tadpoles . references . - l lee grismer , 2006 : a new species of ansonia stoliczka , 1870 ( anura : bufonidae ) from a lowland rainforest in southern peninsular malaysia . `` herpetologica '' 62,4 : pp . 466â€“475
