Episodes (TV series)    episodes ( tv series ) episodes is an anglo-american television sitcom . it began on january 9 , 2011 and is set in los angeles , california . it was created by david crane and jeffrey klarik . it is directed by james griffiths and jim field smith . it stars matt leblanc , stephen mangan , tamsin grieg , john pankow , kathleen rose perkins and mircea monroe . mangan and grieg play sean and beverly lincoln , a british married couple who travel to hollywood to remake their successful british tv series . leblanc is recruited to play the protagonist in the american remake .
