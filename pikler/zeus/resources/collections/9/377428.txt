Ali MacGraw     elizabeth alice `` ali '' macgraw ( born april 1 , 1939 ) is a television and movie actress . she started in movies , but changed to stage and tv acting . she received golden globes for `` goodbye columbus '' and `` love story '' ( the earlier was in 1969 , and the latter was released in 1970 ) . some birthdate sources say that she was born in 1938 ; others say 1939 or 1940. she was born in new york state .
