Acrididae     the acrididae are the main family of grasshoppers , with 10,000 of the 11,000 species of the entire suborder caelifera . the acrididae are best known because all locusts ( swarming grasshoppers ) are of the acrididae . however , not all acrididae are locusts : 'locust ' is a term which is only given to those grasshoppers which swarm . acrididae grasshoppers have relatively short and stout antennae , and drum-like tympana on the side of the first abdominal segment . the name acrididae is derived from greek `` akris '' , meaning locust .
