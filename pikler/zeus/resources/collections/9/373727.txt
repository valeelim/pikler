Supreme Court of Israel     the supreme court of israel ( ) is the highest court of israel . it is located in jerusalem , in the neighbourhood of givet ram . after israel was established in 1948 , the supreme court was sitting in the russian compound in jerusalem . in 1986 , the government held a contest to build a new building for the court and in 1992 the supreme court was moved to a new building next to the knesset . other websites . - israeli judicial authority - jewish virtual library : the judicial branch
