Jonathan Katz     jonathan paul katz ( born december 1 , 1946 ) is an american stand-up comedian , actor , and writer . he is known for his role as dr. katz in `` dr. katz , professional therapist '' . katz won an emmy award for `` outstanding voice-over performance '' in 1995. katz was born on december 1 , 1946 in new york city to a jewish-hungarian family . he was educated at goddard college . katz is married to suzan katz . they have two children . other websites . - `` explosion bus '' starring jonathan katz - wkatz , home of `` hey we 're back '' - jonathan katz 's official website
