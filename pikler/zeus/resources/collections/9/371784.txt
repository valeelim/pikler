Jean-Charles Darmon     jean-charles darmon is a french literary critic born in 1961. graduate in 1982 from the `` école normale supérieure '' , he first teaches at amherst college ( usa ) . teacher of french literature at the `` université de versailles saint-quentin-en-yvelines '' , honorary member of the `` institut universitaire de france '' ( 2001-2006 ) , he was head of the `` école normale supérieure '' from 2005 to 2009. specialized in connections between literature , philosophy and ethics in the classical age , he is the author of books on libertarianism scholar and epicureanism , the forms of the fable or satire , from an interdisciplinary perspective . further reading . - `` '' ( epicurean philosophy and literature in seventeenth-century france . studies on gassendi , cyrano , la fontaine , saint-evremond ) , paris , puf , « perspectives littéraires » , 1998 . - `` cyrano de bergerac , lettres satiriques et amoureuses , précédées de lettres diverses ( édition ) '' ( cyrano de bergerac , satirical and love letters , preceded by various letters ( edit ) ) , paris , desjonquères , « dix-septième siècle » , 1999 .
