( tv series ) '' . she began her first major acting role when she was a guest star on seven episodes of the tv series `` ironside '' , among them `` tom dayton is loose among us '' , in which she played the substitute librarian miss kirk , who pushes the unstable tom dayton too hard , and `` in search of an artist '' , as a woman with a drinking problem who may have been involved in a murder . she has been married to entertainment industry executive sid sheinberg , with whom she had two children , since august 19 , 1956. she retired for eight years after `` 1941 '' but then came out of her voluntary retirement to reprise ellen brody in `` '' .
