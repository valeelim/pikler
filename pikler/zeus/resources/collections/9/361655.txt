Availability     availability is a term used in information security to show that a system is running and reachable by those that are supposed to be able to use the system . availability is sometimes expressed as a percentage of uptime , such as 99 % . high availability is usually expressed as five nines - 99.999 % . in a 24-hour , 7-day-a-week operation this would be just a little over 5 minutes per year .
