Tie-dye     tie-dye is a colorful pattern used on clothing . it is made by tying a piece of clothing into a tight bundle , and then dyeing it with various colors . this became very popular in the 1960s and '70s . it was often made to represent signs like 'peace ' and 'flower power ' . tie dye is an expression of emotion and is a very colourful and expressive type of art .
