Takeichi Nishi     nishi won a gold medal in show jumping at the 1932 summer olympics at los angeles . he was also on the japanese team at the 1936 summer olympics in berlin . he was a tank unit commander at the battle of iwo jima . he was killed in action during the defense of the japanese island of iwo jima . in the 2006 american film `` letters from iwo jima '' , nishi was played by korean-japanese actor tsuyoshi ihara . related pages . - sports in japan - japan at the olympics other websites . - takeichi nishi at databaseolympics.com
