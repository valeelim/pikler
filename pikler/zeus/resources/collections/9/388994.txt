Eau de toilette     eau de toilette is a kind of perfume used to make skin smell nice . it is also called `` aromatic waters '' . it is based on alcohol , its main ingredient . it is usually used on the skin after bathing or shaving . it is considered a `` weak '' and cheap version of perfume . toilet water is defined as a dilute perfume ( perfume is only ingredient mixed with alcohol and few other lowering the production cost ) . it was originally composed of alcohol and various volatile oils .
