Carrie Brownstein     carrie rachel grace brownstein ( september 27 , 1974 ) is an american singer , guitarist and actor . she sings with the band wild flag . brownstein plays indie and alternative rock music . since 2011 , she has acted on the sketch comedy television series `` portlandia '' . brownstein was born in seattle , washington . she grew up in redmond , washington and lives in portland , oregon . in 2010 , she stated that she is bisexual .
