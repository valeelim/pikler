Rose McGowan     rose mcgowan ( born september 5 , 1973 in florence , italy ) is an american actress . she performs in both movies and television . she began acting around 1992 in the comedy `` encino man '' . however , it was the 1995 comedy `` the doom generation '' which made her famous . mcgowen has been on the covers of many magazines , including `` rolling stone '' , `` gq '' and `` seventeen magazine '' .
