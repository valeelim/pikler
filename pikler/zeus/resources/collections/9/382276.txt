Caelum     caelum is a constellation in the southern sky . it is hard to see because it is not very bright . it means `` the chisel '' in latin . before , people called caelum scalptorium , which means `` the engraver 's chisel '' . it was created and named by the french astronomer named nicolas louis de lacaille in the 1750s . the brighest star in caelum has a magnitude of 4.45. this means that all of the stars in caelum are too dark for people who live in cities to see .
