Meredith Vieira     meredith vieira ( born december 30 , 1953 ) is an american journalist , television personality and a game show host . she hosted the syndicated version of `` who wants to be a millionaire '' . she hosted the show from 2002 to 2013. she was a co-host on the abc talk show `` the view '' from 1997 to 2006. she left millionaire in 2013 due to other commitments and projects .
