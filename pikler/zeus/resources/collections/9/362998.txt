Mark Champion     mark champion is the radio voice of the detroit pistons . he also called games for the detroit fury , tampa bay buccaneers , detroit lions , and michigan state spartans . champion also worked for cbs radio and westwood one . he was an anchor of olympics on cbs radio as well as calling golf tournaments for cbs radio . champion was the play-by-play announcer for college basketball on westwood one as well as nfl on westwood one as an announcer for thanksgiving classic and a substitute for sunday doubleheader for harry kalas when kalas was still calling games for the philadelphia phillies .
