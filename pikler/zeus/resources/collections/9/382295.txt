Callinectes     callinectes ( blue crabs ) is a genus of crabs . sixteen species are in this genus . these species include the atlantic blue crab , `` c. sapidus '' : - `` callinectes affinis '' - `` callinectes amnicola '' - `` callinectes arcuatus '' - `` callinectes bellicosus '' - `` callinectes bocourti '' - `` callinectes danae '' - `` callinectes diacanthus '' - `` callinectes exasperatus '' - `` callinectes gladiator '' - `` callinectes marginatus '' - `` callinectes ornatus '' - `` callinectes pallidus '' - `` callinectes rathbunae '' - `` callinectes sapidus '' - `` callinectes similis '' - `` callinectes toxotes ''
