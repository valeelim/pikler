Mihai Răzvan Ungureanu     mihai răzvan ungureanu ( b . 22 september 1968 , iași ) is a romanian politician , historian and diplomat . he was the minister of foreign affairs from 2004 to 2007 and director of sie from 2007 to 2012. he was prime-minister of romania from 9 february 2012 until 9 may 2012. he learned at mathematics-physics high school `` costache negruzzi '' from iași .
