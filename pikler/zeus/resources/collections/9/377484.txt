Anonymous     anonymous is the adjective form of anonymity , when a personal identity is not known to the public . anonymous or anonymus may also refer to : - anonymous work , a work of art or literature that has an unnamed or unknown creator or author - anonymous ( group ) , the collective name of loosely-affiliated individuals that participate in hacktivism - `` anonymous '' ( bobby valentino song ) , 2007 - two writers of byzantine chronicles , of the 6th century , both known as anonymus valesianus
