Chelsea Handler     chelsea handler ( born february 25 1975 ) is an american writer , stand up comedian and actress . she has performed in many movies and television programs . she is the host of `` chelsea lately '' . she was born near livingston , new jersey . handler was the host of the 2010 mtv video music awards . `` are you there , chelsea ? '' was an nbc sitcom . it was based on handler 's 2008 best-selling book `` are you there , vodka ? it 's me , chelsea '' . the program aired from january 11 to march 28 , 2012. on may 11 , 2012 , the network canceled it . laura prepon played the main character , `` chelsea newman , '' a cocktail waitress . handler was `` chelsea 's '' born-again christian sister , `` sloane '' . published works . - `` '' ( 2005 ) - `` are you there , vodka ? it 's me , chelsea '' ( 2008 ) - `` chelsea chelsea bang bang '' ( 2010 ) - `` lies that chelsea handler told me '' ( 2011 ) - `` 50 shades of chartreuse : this time it 's personal '' ( publishing 2012/13 )
