Iris (eye)    iris ( eye ) the iris ( plural : irides or irises ) is a thin , circular structure in the eye . it controls the diameter and size of the pupils . eye colour is the colour of the iris . in humans , the iris may look green , blue , brown , hazel ( a combination of light brown , green and gold ) , grey , violet , or even pink . in response to the amount of light exiting the eye , muscles attached to the iris expand or contract the pupil . the larger the pupil , the more light can enter the eye and reach the retina .
