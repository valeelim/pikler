John Putnam Chapin     john putnam chapin ( april 21 , 1810 – july 27 , 1864 ) was an american politician . he was the mayor of chicago , illinois ( 1846–1847 ) . he was a member of the whig party . chapin was born on april 21 , 1810 in bradford , vermont . he was raised in chicago , illinois . chapin died on july 27 , 1864 in chicago from a heart attack , aged 54 .
