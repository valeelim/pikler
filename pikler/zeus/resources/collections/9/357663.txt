Thérèse of Lisieux     saint thérèse of lisieux ( 1873-1897 ) ( aka . st therese of the child jesus/ sainte thérèse de lisieux ) is doctor of the catholic church , which means that her life and writings are considered particularly important for catholics . she is also called the little flower of jesus . she lived in france as a nun , and died at 24 from tuberculosis . mother teresa chose her name in honor of her .
