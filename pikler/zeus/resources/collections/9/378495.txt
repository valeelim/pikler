Ashford &amp; Simpson    ashford & amp ; simpson ashford & simpson were an american r & b music duo who had a lot of hit singles dating to the mid 1960s and continuing through the early 1990s . the duo consisted of nickolas ashford and valerie simpson . ashford was born may 4 , 1941 and died due to throat cancer , august 22 , 2011 ; simpson was on august 26 , 1946. singers associated with ashford & simpson included marvin gaye and diana ross .
