René-Robert Cavelier, Sieur de La Salle    rené-robert cavelier , sieur de la salle rené-robert cavelier , sieur de la salle , or robert de la salle ( november 21 , 1643 – march 19 , 1687 ) was a french explorer . he explored the great lakes region of the united states and canada , the mississippi river , and the gulf of mexico . la salle claimed the entire mississippi river basin for france .
