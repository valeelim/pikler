Jill Arrington     tiffany `` jill '' arrington ( born july 27 , 1972 ) is an american sportscaster . she works for fox sports . arrington was the feature reporter on `` fox nfl sunday '' and co-hosted `` nfl under the helmet '' . for the arena football league , she was the studio host and field reporter for the tnn network . arrington also worked at cbs sports . she was the sideline reporter and court reporter for `` tennis on cbs '' and `` sec on cbs '' and was a feature reporter on `` the nfl today '' a pregame show for the `` nfl on cbs '' . then she worked with mike tirico and kirk `` herbie '' herbstreit for `` espn college football '' as lead sideline reporter . arrington has hosted programs for fox sports such as `` totally nascar '' , `` verizon nfl mobile updates '' , `` college football saturday preview , '' and `` foxsportsflash '' . she was added to the `` nba on tnt '' in 2012. arrington was born in conyers , georgia . she studied journalism and political science at the university of miami . in 2001 , arrington was voted the `` sexiest sportscaster '' by `` playboy '' magazine . arrington is the aunt of actresses dakota and elle fanning .
