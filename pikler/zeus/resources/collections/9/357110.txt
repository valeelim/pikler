Úrsula Murayama    úrsula murayama úrsula murayama ( born in mexico in 1972 ) is a mexican actress of japanese origin who appears in telenovelas ( soap operas ) . her best known role was that of handmaiden jacinta in telenovela `` esmeralda '' ( 1997 ) . her possible relative was actor noé murayama , who died in 1997. he appeared with úrsula in `` esmeralda '' . other roles : - `` hijos del viento '' - princess tizcuitl - `` sin azul '' - laura - `` mi hijo arturo '' - `` las 13 rosas '' - `` pelea de gallos '' - `` el caso wanninkhof ''
