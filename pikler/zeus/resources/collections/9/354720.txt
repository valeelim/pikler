Great Sandy Desert     the great sandy desert is a desert in the northwest of australia . it is mostly in western australia . it is the second-largest desert in australia after the great victoria desert , covering about . to the south of the great sandy desert is the gibson desert , and to the east is the tanami desert . there are not many people living in the great sandy desert . most of the people are aboriginal communities and mining towns . the aboriginal people of the desert fall into two main groups : the martu in the west and the pintupi in the east . they both speak western desert languages . the area does not get much rain , but even in the driest parts rainfalls rarely drop below . the rate of evaporation ( how quickly the water dries up ) is very high . most of the rain comes from thunderstorms . many drought years end with a monsoon or tropical cyclone . on average for most of the area , there are about 20-30 days where thunderstorms form . most of the plants growing across the great sandy desert are spinifex grasses . animals living in the desert include camels , dingos , red kangaroo , goannas , bilbies , marsupial mole , thorny devils , bearded dragons and many other species of lizard .
