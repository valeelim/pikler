Pedro A. Paterno     pedro alejandro paterno y de vera-ignacio , also spelled pedro alejandro paterno y debera ignacio ( february 17 , 1857 - april 26 , 1911 ) was a filipino independence activist , revolutionary and politician . he was also a poet and novelist . he was the second prime minister of the philippines provisional revolutionary government from may 7 , 1899 to november 13 , 1899. other websites . - pedro paterno 's proclamation of war on june 2 , 1899 - ninay
