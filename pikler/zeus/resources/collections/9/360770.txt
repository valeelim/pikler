Stann Creek District     stann creek district is a district in the south east region of belize . according to the 2010 census , the district had a population of 32,166 people . its capital is the city of dangriga . dangriga used to be called `` stann creek town . '' stann comes from `` stanns , '' or safe havens used by colonialists coming from the `` old world '' to the `` new world . '' the stann creek district is home to the cockscomb basin wildlife sanctuary . in the sanctuary is victoria peak , the highest point in belize , at 1120 meters ( 3,675 feet ) above sea level .
