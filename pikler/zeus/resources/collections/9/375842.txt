Ahl al-Bayt     ahl al-bayt ( ) is an arabic phrase that means `` people of the house '' , or `` family of the house '' . the phrase was used in arabia before the founding of islam to refer to someone 's clan . within islam , the term refers to the family of the prophet muhammad.shia muslims limited this term to five people who were ali , fatimah , hassan and hussain while sunni islam extend it to the wives of muhammad .
