2012 Anaheim riots     in 21 july 2012 , police shot and killed a man , manuel diaz , in the anna drive neighborhood in anaheim , california . a riot happened for a few nights . anna drive is a poor neighborhood . it is known for having many criminals , including drug dealers . manuel diaz was one of them . he and some of his friends were gathering in an alley and threw heroin on a roof . police came and chased him through the ghetto . then they shot him in the buttocks and head . he later died at the hospital . people started getting angry . they came to anna drive and started protesting . soon most of east anaheim was fighting police . trash cans were burnt while people went crazy and broke shop windows . the fourth night was the most violent . police had to shoot people with fake bullets . two reporters were hurt . the mayor had the fbi look at the shooting carefully . another man , joel acevedo , was shot the next day and died too , making it the sixth deadly police shooting in one year . the events also highlighted the divide between the poor west and central parts of anaheim , where more than half of the population is hispanic , and the east , where most of the police come from .
