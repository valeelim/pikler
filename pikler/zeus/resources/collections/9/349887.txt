Emily Blunt     emily olivia leah blunt ( born 23 february 1983 ) is a british and american actress . she was born in wandsworth , london . she is a niece of mp crispin blunt ( born 1960 ) and a granddaughter of peter blunt ( 1923-2003 ) . blunt 's television roles include the title character `` gideon 's daughter '' , for which she won a golden globe award . her movie roles include emily charlton in `` the devil wears prada '' , the title role of queen victoria in `` the young victoria '' and elise sellas in `` the adjustment bureau '' . blunt married john krasinski in como , italy on 10 july 2010. the couple have two daughters , born in 2014 and 2016. blunt became a dual british-american citizen in 2015. blunt has been diagnosed with obsessive-compulsive disorder .
