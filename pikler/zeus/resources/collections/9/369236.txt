Pooh's Heffalump Movie    pooh 's heffalump movie pooh 's heffalump movie is a 2005 animated film released by walt disney pictures , featuring characters from a.a. milne 's `` winnie-the-pooh '' stories . a sequel , `` pooh 's heffalump halloween movie '' , was released direct-to-video on september 13 , 2005. cast . - nikita hopkins as roo - kyle stanger as lumpy the heffalump - jim cummings as winnie-the-pooh and tigger - peter cullen as eeyore - kath soucie as kanga - john fiedler as piglet - ken sansom as rabbit - brenda blethyn as mama heffalump
