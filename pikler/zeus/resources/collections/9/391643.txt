Octavio Lepage     octavio lepage barreto , ( november 24 , 1923 – january 6 , 2017 ) was acting president of venezuela from may 21 , 1993 through june 5 , 1993. lepage was born on november 24 , 1923 in santa rosa , anzoátegui , venezuela . he studied at central university of venezuela . lepage married verónica peñalver . in 1993 , he moved to caracas , venezuela . lepage died in caracas , venezuela on january 6 , 2017 , aged 93 .
