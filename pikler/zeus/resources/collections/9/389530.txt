Pope Callixtus II     calixtus ii ( or calistus ii ) ( died 13 december 1124 ) , born guy de burgundy , the fourth son of william i , count of burgundy , was elected pope on 1 february 1119 after the death of pope gelasius ii . his pontificate was shaped by the investiture controversy , which he was able to settle through the concordat of worms ( in 1122 ) .
