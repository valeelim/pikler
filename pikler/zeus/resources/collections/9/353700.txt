Podarcis muralis     the wall lizard ( `` podarcis muralis '' ) is a fairly common species of lizard , living in europe , on rocks or wall cracks . it is called the common wall lizard , european wall lizard , or simply `` wall lizard '' , despite the fact that the last name is also used for its relatives . the common wall lizard is a small , thin lizard . its small scales have very differenct colours and patterns . its coloration is generally brownish or greyish . sometimes it has a little bit of green . the tail is brown , grey or rust in colour . the belly has 6 rows of larger rectangular scales that are generally reddish , pink , or orangish . common wall lizards may also have dark markings on the throat .
