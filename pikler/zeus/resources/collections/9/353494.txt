Sexy and I Know It     `` sexy and i know it '' is a song by american duo lmfao . it was released from the album `` sorry for party rocking '' ( 2011 ) . it was released as the third single on september 16 , 2011. it was written by stefan kendal gordy , goonrock , erin beck , george m. robertson and kenneth oliver . it was produced by party rock . the song reached number one on the us hot 100 chart . it stood at number one for two weeks . the song became the group 's second number one song . they became the second group to do this . the first was outkast with `` hey ya ! '' ( 2003 ) and `` the way you move '' ( 2004 ) . `` sexy and i know it '' reached number one in australia , canada and new zealand . the song was featured in the fifth season of `` gossip girl '' . the song was covered in `` glee '' . track listing . - cd single 1 . `` sexy and i know it '' ( album version ) – 3:19 1 . `` sexy and i know it '' ( mord fustang ) – 5:21 - digital download 1 . `` sexy and i know it '' ( audiobot remix ) – 5:55 1 . `` sexy and i know it '' ( mord fustang ) – 5:19 1 . `` sexy and i know it '' ( tomba and borgore remix ) – 3:41
