Rutger Hauer     rutger oelsen hauer ( born january 23 , 1944 ) is a dutch stage , television , and movie actor . hauer 's career began during the year 1969 with the role in a popular dutch television series `` floris '' , directed by paul verhoeven . his notable works include `` flesh & blood '' , `` blind fury '' , `` blade runner '' , `` the hitcher '' , `` nighthawks '' , `` wedlock '' , `` sin city '' , `` confessions of a dangerous mind '' , `` ladyhawke '' , `` buffy the vampire slayer '' , `` the osterman weekend '' , `` the blood of heroes '' , `` batman begins '' , `` hobo with a shotgun '' , `` the rite '' , and he won a golden globe award for best supporting actor for his role in `` escape from sobibor '' . hauer is a dedicated environmentalist and has founded an aids awareness organization , the rutger hauer starfish association . hauer was born on january 23 , 1944 in breukelen , netherlands . his parents were arend and teunuk hauer , both drama teachers . hauer was raised in beetsterzwaag , netherlands . hauer lives in malibu , california , united states . hauer married ineke ten kate in 1985. they have one daughter , aysha hauer . he is the grandfather of fashion model leandro maeder .
