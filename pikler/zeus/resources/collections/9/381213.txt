Lilacs and Roses     lilacs and roses is an oil painting on canvas by édouard manet . it was one of his late works , painted in 1883. it measures 54 × 45 cm . it is in a private collection . the painting is a development of the small vase of roses on the bar in manet 's `` the bar at the folies-bergère . '' many of manet 's later works offered a small still life in the lower left hand corner of the picture . the flowers present a `` cross '' shape that is broken by a diagonal line through the center . the brushwork is exceptional . the picture is one of the masterpieces of manet 's later years .
