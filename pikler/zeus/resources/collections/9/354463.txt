Karnam Malleswari     karnam malleswari ( born june 1 , 1975 ) is an indian athlete . she is best known as a weightlifter who won a bronze medal in the 2000 summer olympics at sydney . she became the first indian woman to stand on an olympic victor 's podium , and she was the only indian to win a medal in the 2000 olympic games . in 1992 , malleswari was the first indian woman to win a world title in any sport . honours . - arjuna award , 1994 related pages . - sports in india - india at the olympics other websites . - images at olympic.org
