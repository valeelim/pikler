Van Wagenen House     the van wagenen house is a house in jersey city , new jersey . it is also known as the apple tree house . it has been part of the national register of historic places since august 16 , 2006. the main part of the house was built in the 1740s , and an addition was made to it in the 1820s . a legend states that during the american revolutionary war , general george washington and the marquis de lafayette talked about war strategy under an apple tree in front of the house in 1779. however , this is probably not true . there used to be an apple orchard and a cider press at the house , which is why it is also called the apple tree house . the house was called one of the most endangered ( which means it is at a very high risk of being ruined ) historic sites by preservation new jersey in 1996. the building really needed to be improved ( made better ) . in 1999 the city of jersey city bought the house and started to make it better . the city plans to use the house as a museum .
