Peggy Lipton     peggy lipton ( born august 30 , 1946 ) is an american television actress . she played julie barnes in the series `` the mod squad '' in the late 1960s and early 1970s . she played norma jennings in `` twin peaks '' . some sources mention that she was born in 1946 while others say 1947. she was born in new york city . she was married to quincy jones .
