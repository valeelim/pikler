Yuri Maltsev     yuri nicholas maltsev ( ) is an austrian school economist and economic historian from tatarstan . of tatar origin , he is professor of economics at the carthage college in wisconsin and is a senior fellow of the ludwig von mises institute in auburn , alabama . he has been a consultant for chase manhattan bank and other companies on political and economic changes in central and eastern europe . he also edited `` requiem for marx '' . references . - http : //mises.org/fellow/11 - http : //mises.org/senfaculty/maltsevcv.pdf
