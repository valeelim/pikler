William Daniels     william david daniels ( born march 31 , 1927 ) or will daniels is an american movie , television , stage , and voice actor . he is known for his role as mr. braddock in `` the graduate '' and as dr. mark craig in `` st. elsewhere '' . he won emmy awards in 1985 and in 1986 for his acting in `` st. elsewhere '' . daniels was born on march 31 , 1927 in brooklyn , new york . he studied at northwestern university . he has been married to bonnie bartlett since 1951. they have two children .
