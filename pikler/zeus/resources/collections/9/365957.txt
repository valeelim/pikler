List of The Amazing World of Gumball episodes     `` the amazing world of gumball '' is a cartoon comedy series . it was created by ben bocquelet for the cartoon network . since its debut on may 3 , 2011 , more than 160 episodes of the series have been broadcast . its fifth season began on september 1 , 2016. cartoon network has renewed the series for a sixth season . on september 6 , 2016 , series creator ben bocquelet said that season six of the show will be the final season .
