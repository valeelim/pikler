Shakib Al Hasan     shakib al hasan ( ) ( born 24 march 1987 ) is a bangladeshi international cricketer . he is an all-rounder . he bats left-handed in the middle order . he is a slow left-arm orthodox bowler . he played for bangladesh at under-19 level , at youth level at 2005-2006. he made his one day international ( odi ) debut against zimbabwe in august 2006. he made his test debut the may 2007 against india . shakib al hasan is also a businessman . his company name is fiesta event management limited . other websites . - shakib al hasan from espncricinfo - shakib al hasan from cricketarchive
