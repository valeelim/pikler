Arte Johnson     arthur stanton eric `` arte '' johnson ( born january 20 , 1929 ) is a american retired comedian and television , movie , and voice actor . his career has spanned for nearly 50 years . his career started in 1954 up until his retirement in 2005. johnson was a regular cast in `` rowan & martin 's laugh-in '' . his well-known `` character '' was that of a german soldier with his famous the catchphrase : `` verrrry interesting , but ... [ 'stupid ' , 'not very funny ' , and other versions ] '' . he has won a saturn award . johnson was born in benton harbor , michigan . johnson was educated at the university of illinois . johnson is currently married to gisela johnson since 1968 .
