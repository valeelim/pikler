Moin-ud-din Chishti     sultan-ul-hind , hazrat sheikh khwaja moin-ud-din chishti ajmeri , was born in 1141 and died in 1230 ad . he is also known as `` gharīb nawāz '' ( benefactor of the poor ) . he is the most famous sufi saint of the chishti order of south asia . moinuddin chishti introduced and established this order in india . his shrine is at ajmer , rajasthan , india .
