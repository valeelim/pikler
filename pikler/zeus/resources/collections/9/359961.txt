Mr. Lawrence     douglas lawrence `` doug '' osowski ( born january 1 , 1969 ) mostly known as mr. lawrence or doug lawrence is an american voice , actor , comedian , writer , storyboard artist , animator , and director . he is known for his voice roles as plankton , larry the lobster , and elain in `` spongebob squarepants '' . lawrence was born in east brunswick township , new jersey on january 1 , 1969 .
