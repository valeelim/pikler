SEOmoz     seomoz , inc. is an american software company , located in seattle , wa . they create search engine optimization tools . the company was created by rand fishkin and gillian muessig in 2004 as a consulting company . it was changed into , a software development company in 2010. the website owned by the company publishes search engine optimization news and guides . seomoz became one of the leading blogs about search engine optimization . that caused a rise in search engine optimization blogs on the web . in june 2012 , seomoz bought followerwonk , a tool for searching and managing twitter social activity .
