We Have Survived     `` we have survived '' is a song by the australian band no fixed address . it was written by bart willoughby when he was 18. it first appeared in the movie `` wrong side of the road '' , and was released on its soundtrack in 1980. it was later included on the band 's first album `` from my eyes '' from 1982 . `` we have survived '' is often sung as a kind of anthem by indigenous australians . it is often performed by cover bands in pubs and festivals . in 2008 , it was added to the national film and sound archive 's sounds of australia registry . other websites . - listen to `` we have survived ''
