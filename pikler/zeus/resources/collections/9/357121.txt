Heichal Shlomo     heichal shlomo is the former seat of the chief rabbinate of israel . it is next to the great synagogue on king george street , jerusalem . the building currently has the jewish heritage museum , renanim synagogue , office space and an auditorium . it is across from the leonardo plaza hotel jerusalem . the building was finished in 1958. in 2009 , pope benedict xvi made a visit to the area .
