2 Punjab Regiment     the 2nd punjab regiment was a british indian army regiment from 1922 to the partition of india in 1947. the regiment was formed by the amalgamation of the following regiments : - 1st battalion , from the 67th punjabis , formerly the 7th regiment of madras native infantry - 2nd battalion , from the 69th punjabis - 3rd battalion , from the 72nd punjabis - 4th battalion , from the 74th punjabis - 5th battalion , from the 87th punjabis - 10th ( training ) battalion , formed by redesignation of 2nd bn , 67th punjabis after the independence and partition of india in august 1947 , this regiment became the punjab regiment ( india ) .
