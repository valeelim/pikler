Diet of Japan     it is made up of a the house of representatives ( lower house ) and the house of councillors ( upper house ) . the members of both houses of the diet are directly elected . the diet selects the prime minister . the diet was first convened as the imperial diet in 1889. in 1947 , the diet took its current form . related pages . - bicameralism - history of japan - national diet library - parliamentary system other websites . - national diet library website
