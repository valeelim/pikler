Yodohime jinja     , also known as the kawakami jinja , is a japanese shinto shrine in saga , saga prefecture . `` yodohime jinja '' was founded in the sixth century . it enshrines the `` kami '' of the grandmother of emperor jimmu . it was one of two chief shinto shrine ( `` ichinomiya '' ) of hizen province and saga prefecture . other websites . - yodohime jinja website
