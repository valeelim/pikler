My Name Is Rachel Corrie     my name is rachel corrie is a play based on the diaries and emails of rachel corrie . they were edited by alan rickman , who directed the play , and journalist katharine viner also helped editing . rachel corrie was an american peace activist and student , who travelled to the gaza strip . she was killed by an israeli bulldozer while she was protesting against the destruction of palestinian homes . the play was first shown in london , at the royal court theatre , in april 2005. related pages . - let me stand alone
