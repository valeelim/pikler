DK Bongos     the dk bongos are a special bongo-like controller that can be used in some `` donkey kong '' games for nintendo gamecube . the bongos are shaped like the ones seen in the `` donkey kong '' games . it has a built-in microphone to detect clapping . it was introduced in the music video game `` donkey konga '' . the dk bongos are seen in the wii game `` super smash bros. brawl '' . they are a part of donkey kong 's `` final smash '' attack ( a powerful attack that can be used to kill opponents easily ) . games . - `` donkey konga '' - `` donkey konga 2 '' - `` donkey konga 3 '' - `` donkey kong jungle beat ''
