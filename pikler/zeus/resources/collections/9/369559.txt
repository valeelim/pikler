Paca     the genus cuniculus has the two species of paca . - lowland paca , `` cuniculus paca '' - mountain paca , `` cuniculus taczanowskii '' pacas are found from southern mexico to northern argentina . they are a source of meat in many places . both species of paca eat mainly fruit . other foods include roots , seeds , leaves , buds , and flowers . in the wild , pacas are herbivores , meaning they eat only plants . in captivity , they are omnivores , meaning they eat both plants and flesh . pacas in zoos eat fruits , vegetables , raw meat , lizards , and insects .
