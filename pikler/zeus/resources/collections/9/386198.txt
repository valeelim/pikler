Ingrid Michaelson     ingrid ellen michaelson ( born december 8 , 1979 , new york city ) is an american musician . she has had five studio albums since 2005. she has not been signed to any major record labels . her music has been used in several television programs , including `` the vampire diaries '' and `` scrubs '' . michaelson performed with singer sara bareilles on the hit single `` winter song '' .
