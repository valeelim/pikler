Tell Me Something I Don't Know    tell me something i do n't know `` tell me something i do n't know '' is a song by selena gomez . it was released as a single on august 5 , 2008 on itunes . a radio disney version ( which removes the hurricane katrina reference ) was released on september 9 , 2008 , on itunes . the song is on the kidz bop 15 cd . a new version of the song is on selena gomez & the scene 's debut album , `` kiss & tell '' . the song peaked at number 58 on the us `` billboard '' hot 100 chart at 13 on the australian hitseekers singles chart and at 1 on the malaysia muzik singles & urban chart . the music video starts with scenes reminiscent of ( but not from ) the film ; gomez cleaning a house and the maid yelling at her . gomez then leaves the house and does a dance routine with her backup dancers , while the maid watches from the house window . the video also features gomez in front of a black background with lyrics from the song ( such as `` i 'm ready for it '' and `` one in a million '' ) flying around her .
