Great hornbill     the great hornbill ( `` buceros bicornis '' ) also known as great indian hornbill or great pied hornbill , is one of the larger members of the hornbill family . great hornbills are found in the forests of nepal , india , the malay peninsula and sumatra , indonesia . their impressive size and colour have made them important in many tribal cultures and rituals . the great hornbill is long-lived , living for nearly 50 years in captivity . they are predominantly frugivorous although they are opportunists and will prey on small mammals , reptiles and birds .
