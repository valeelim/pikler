Ann Curry     ann curry ( born november 19 , 1956 ) is an american television journalist and personality . she co-hosted the `` today show '' on nbc from 1997 until 2012. she also anchored at `` dateline nbc '' from 2005 to 2011. curry was born on the island of guam . her father is american and her mother is japanese . they met while her father was serving in the united states navy during the us occupation of japan after world war ii .
