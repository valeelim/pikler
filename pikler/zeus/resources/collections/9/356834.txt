Orange Walk Town     orange walk town is the second largest town in the nation of belize . it has a population of about 13,400. it is the capital of the orange walk district . orange walk town is on the left bank of the new river . it is 53 miles north of belize city and 30 miles ( 50 km ) south of corozal town . in the days of the mayas , the area was known as holpatin . orange walk town is nicknamed `` sugar city '' . the local tower hill sugar factory ( belize sugar industries ) handles all of the country 's sugar cane output . the farming of other crops , and tourism , also play a role in the economy . the region is highly populated by mestizos , kriols , mennonites , chinese , taiwanese , indians , and other people from central america .
