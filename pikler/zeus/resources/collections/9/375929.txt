Aokigahara     aokigahara is a forest at the northwest base of mount fuji in japan . the forest is known to be quiet because of the absence of wildlife and the wind-blocking density of the trees that are in it . a number of rocky , icy caverns are in the forest and some of them are popular tourist destinations . demons in japanese mythology are associated with the forest . it is known as a popular suicide spot and is reportedly the most popular in japan . it also ranks as the second in the world after san francisco 's golden gate bridge . there are signs in the forest in both japanese and english that try to persuade people not to commit suicide . the forest is said to be haunted by the yÅ«rei ( angry spirits ) that were left to die .
