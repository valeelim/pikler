Mokele-mbembe     mokèlé-mbèmbé , meaning `` one who stops the flow of rivers '' in the lingala language is a legendary water-dwelling creature of congo river basin folklore , sometimes described as living creature , sometimes as a spirit , and loosely analogous to the loch ness monster in western culture . it is claimed to be a sauropod by some cryptozoologists . expeditions mounted in the hope of finding evidence of the mokèlé-mbèmbé have failed , and the subject has been covered in a number of books and by a number of television documentaries .
