Kristen Wiig     kristen wiig ( born august 22 , 1973 in new york state ) is an american actress and comedian . she is best known for acting on the nbc television comedy `` saturday night live '' . she also acted in many movies , including `` bridesmaids '' , `` adventureland '' , `` paul '' , `` despicable me '' , and in `` despicable me 2 '' .
