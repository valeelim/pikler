Powell River, British Columbia    powell river , british columbia powell river is a city in the canadian province of british columbia . powell river is in the northern part of the sunshine coast region . the population of powell river was 126,456 in 2011. the mayor of powell river is dave formosa . the historic townsite was designated as a national historic site of canada in 1995. powell river has an ice hockey team , the powell river kings which play in the british columbia hockey league . powell river was named a `` cultural capital of canada '' in 2004 because of the its strong arts and cultural programs . other websites . - official website
