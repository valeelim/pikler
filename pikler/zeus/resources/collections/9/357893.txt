Julianne Hough     julianne alexandra hough ( born july 20 1988 ) is an american dancer , country music singer and actress . she is best known for being a two-time champion on the abc series `` dancing with the stars '' . her first album was released may 20 , 2008. she acted in the 2011 remake of the movie `` footloose '' . hough is the second of five children , she has four sisters . all of her grandparents were professional dancers .
