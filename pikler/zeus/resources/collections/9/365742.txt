JoBeth Williams     margaret jobeth williams ( born december 6 , 1948 ) is an american movie , television , stage , voice actress , and movie director . she is known for her roles in `` poltergeist '' , `` the big year '' , `` the big chill '' , `` '' , `` kramer vs. kramer '' , and in `` stop ! or my mom will shoot '' . williams was born on december 6 , 1948 in houston , texas . williams studied at jones high school and at brown university . she married john pasquin in 1982. they have two children .
