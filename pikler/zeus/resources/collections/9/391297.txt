Pokémon Puzzle Challenge     pokémon puzzle challenge is a puzzle video game for the game boy color game console . it was released in japan on september 21 , 2000 , in north america on december 4 , 2000 , and in europe on june 15 , 2001. it is based upon nintendo 's `` panel de pon '' video game series , with a `` pokémon gold and silver '' theme . players clear blocks that come down by lining them up in horizontal or vertical lines of three or more blocks . the lines have to be of the same kind of block . as new blocks come down , the playfield goes up . if the playfield reaches the top , the game ends . the player can stop the playfield by clearing blocks . the more lines a player clears at once , the higher their score will be . there are three modes of play : single-player , multi-player , and training . players can play as a number of different pokémon from `` pokémon gold and silver '' , including pikachu , cyndaquil , and totodile . new pokémon can be unlocked by winning against other pokémon trainers .
