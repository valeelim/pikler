Lappeenranta     lappeenranta is a town in eastern finland . there were 72,289 people living there on 31 august 2012. it is next to the municipalities of imatra , lemi , luumäki , miehikkälä , ruokolahti and taipalsaari . hanhijärvi ( lappeenranta ) , hiivaniemi , kansola , konnunsuo , kontu ( lappeenranta ) , korvenkylä , louko , moisio ( lappeenranta ) , raippo , ruokola ( lappeenranta ) , simola ( lappeenranta ) , sirkjärvi , vainikkala , vilkjärvi . other websites . - lappeenrannan kaupunki
