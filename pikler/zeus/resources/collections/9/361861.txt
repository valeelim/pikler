Bill Kurtis     william horton `` bill '' kurtis ( born september 21 , 1940 ) is an american television journalist , producer , narrator , news anchor , and historian who anchored `` cbs morning news '' . he has also narrated crime shows and in movies . kurtis was born on september 21 , 1940 in pensacola , florida on september 21 , 1940 , but has mostly spend his childhood in chicago , illinois . he studied his b.s . degree at the university of kansas and his j.d . degree at washburn university of law . he was married to helen kurtis from 1966 until her death in 1977 , they had two children ( one who died ) . he is currently partners with donna la pietra .
