Matthew Lillard     matthew lyn lillard ( born january 24 , 1970 ) is an american movie , television , stage , voice actor , movie and television director and producer . he is known for his roles as stu macher in `` scream '' and as shaggy rogers in all of the live-action `` scooby-doo '' movies and as his voice in the animated movies and in the 2011 animated series . lillard was born on january 24 , 1970 in lansing , michigan . he studied at foothill high school and at american academy of dramatic arts . lillard married heather helm since 2000. they have three children .
