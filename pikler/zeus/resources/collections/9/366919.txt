Ludovico Ariosto     ludovico ariosto ( reggio emilia , 8 september 1474 â€“ ferrara 6 july 1533 ) was an italian poet . he is best known as the author of the romance epic `` orlando furioso '' ( 1516 ) . the poem is a continuation of matteo maria boiardo 's `` orlando innamorato '' . it tells about the adventures of charlemagne , orlando , and the franks as they fight the saracens with diversions into many side plots . ariosto composed the poem in the ottava rima rhyme scheme and introduced narrative commentary throughout the work . ariosto spent the greater part of his life at ferrara including many years in the service of cardinal ippolito d'este and then of duke alfonso i. his poem `` orlando furioso '' is designed to exalt the este family ; in its final form it was first published in 1532. it is the greatest of the italian romantic epics . ariosto also wrote lyric poetry in italian and latin , some satires and four comedies . - minor works - `` la tragedia di tisbe '' ( 1493 ) - `` la cassaria '' ( 1508 ) - `` i suppositi '' ( 1509 )
