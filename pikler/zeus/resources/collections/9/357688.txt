Cheryl Tiegs     cheryl rae tiegs ( born september 25 1947 in breckenridge , minnesota ) is an american fashion model and actress . she is best known for her modeling for the `` sports illustrated swimsuit issue '' . tiegs has been on the cover of the `` si '' swimsuit issue three times . she has also been on the covers of many other magazines . these include `` vogue '' , `` glamour '' , `` harper 's '' and `` time '' . tiegs performed as herself in the sitcom `` just shoot me '' .
