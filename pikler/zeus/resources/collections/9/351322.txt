2060s     the 2060s decade will begin on january 1 , 2060 and will end on december 31 , 2069. it is the seventh decade of the 21st century and the 3rd millennium . notable predictions and known events . - in 2060 , one-third of all the energy in the world is predicted to be solar . - in 2061 , halley 's comet will get as close as it can to the sun . this is known as a `` perihelion '' , and it last happened in 1986. events in fiction . - the television anime show `` real drive '' takes place in this decade . - the book `` the giver '' takes place in 2065 .
