Port of London     port of london is the main port of london , united kingdom . the port is in the river of thames at the city center . in 2010 the british government started building a new port outside the city , on the north bank of the river in thurrock , essex . the new project is called : london gateway . other websites . - port of london - the website of the port of london authority - portcities london home - the port of london in 1819 - port of tilbury - london gateway planning inquiry
