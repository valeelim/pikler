Assemblies of God     the assemblies of god is a united states-based christian denomination ; a large group of pentecostal churches . there are over 64 million members in over 200 countries . the ag is trinitarian . they view the bible as divinely inspired . the bible provides the rules of faith and conduct . baptism by immersion is practiced . baptism is understood as an outward sign of an inward change . the change is from being dead in sin to being alive in christ . communion is also practiced . the assemblies of god also places a strong emphasis on the fulfillment of the great commission and believes that this is the main calling of the church . the next world congress will be held in springfield , missouri in august 2014. other websites . - world aog site
