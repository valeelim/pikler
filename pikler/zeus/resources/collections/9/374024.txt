Line level     line level is the strength of an audio signal . an audio signal transfers sound , for example to cd players and cassette players . it is much higher than microphone level , which can be only a couple of millivolts . a line level signal can be connected to an amplifier which sends the signal to loudspeakers with much higher power . a preamplifier is a device that can make the line level higher . it can boost a microphone 's weak audio signal .
