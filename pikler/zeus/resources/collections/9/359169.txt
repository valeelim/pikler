Robert Earl Jones     robert earl jones ( february 3 , c. 1910 - september 7 , 2006 ) was an american actor . one of the movies he was in the 1973 movie `` the sting '' . he also was in the 1984 movie `` the cotton club '' and the 1983 movie `` trading places '' . his year of birth is reported as between 1900 and 1911. the most often is 1910 as reported by the social security administration . he was also the father of james earl jones . he died in englewood new jersey of natural causes .
