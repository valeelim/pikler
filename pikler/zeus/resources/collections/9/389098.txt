Cruel Intentions     cruel intentions is a 1999 american romantic teen drama movie set in new york city . it is about seduction , manipulation and lies . sarah michelle gellar plays kathryn merteuil , ryan phillippe plays her stepbrother sebastian valmont , reese witherspoon plays annette hargrove , selma blair plays cecile caldwell and swoosie kurtz plays regina . `` cruel intentions '' got mixed reviews while playing in theaters , though it made close to $ 76 million . the movie became a cult favorite in the years after its release .
