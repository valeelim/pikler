Vin Diesel     vin diesel ( born mark sinclair vincent ; july 18 , 1967 ) is an american actor , writer , director , producer and voice talent . his acting includes movies , television and video games . diesel is known for his roles in the movies `` the fast and the furious '' series , `` the chronicles of riddick '' trilogy , and in `` xxx '' . diesel was born in new york city , new york . he studied at hunter college . he is partners with paloma jimenez .
