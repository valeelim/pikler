Ryan Gosling     ryan thomas gosling ( born november 12 , 1980 ) is a canadian actor , director , writer and musician . he is known for his roles as noah calhoun in `` the notebook '' and as dan dunne in `` half nelson '' . he was nominated for a golden globe award and an academy award . gosling was born on november 12 , 1980 in london , ontario , canada . he studied at cornwall collegiate , at the vocational school , and at the lester b. pearson high school . he now lives in los angeles , california .
