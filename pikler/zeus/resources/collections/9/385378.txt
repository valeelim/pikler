William Fichtner     william edward `` will '' fichtner , jr. ( born november 27 , 1956 ) is an american actor . he was known for his roles in `` prison break '' , `` armageddon '' , `` heat '' , `` the perfect storm '' , and in `` the dark knight '' . fichtner was born on november 27 , 1956 in mitchel air force base , new york . he is of german ancestry . he was raised in cheektowaga , new york . fichtner studied at maryvale high school , at the farmingdale state college and at the american academy of dramatic arts . he was married to betsy aidem until they divorced in 1998. they had one child . he has been married to kymberly kalil since 1998. they have one child . other websites . - william fichtner at the official contact movie website - random roles at the onion a.v . club - fichtner firsthand yahoo group
