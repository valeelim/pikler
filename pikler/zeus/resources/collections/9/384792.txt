AccuWeather     accuweather is an american media organization that provides non-profit worldwide weather forecasts . the name `` accuweather '' is a portmanteau for `` accurate '' and `` weather '' . the network was founded in 1962 by joel n. myers , then a penn state graduate student working on degrees in meteorology . the company adopted the name `` accuweather '' in 1971. it is centered in state college , pennsylvania . accuweather is a strong provider for a lot of local television stations , and does guest commentaries on major television , networks . other websites . - accuweather.com
