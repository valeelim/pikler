Barry Pepper     barry robert pepper ( born april 4 , 1970 ) is a canadian movie , television and voice actor . he is known for his roles as dean stanton in the 1999 movie `` the green mile '' and as robert f. kennedy in the 2011 mini-series `` the kennedys '' . he won an emmy award in 2011 for his role as robert f. kennedy . pepper was born in campbell river , british columbia , canada . pepper has been married to cindy pepper since 1997. they have a child .
