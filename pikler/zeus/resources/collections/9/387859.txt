Centaurus     centaurus is a constellation in the southern hemisphere of the night sky . it is named after a centaur , which is a creature that is half man and half horse . the astronomer named ptolemy listed centaurus when he made a list of 48 constellations . centaurus is also one of the 88 constellations that were made by the international astronomical union . in the direction of centaurus is the shapley supercluster or shapley concentration ( scl 124 ) . this is the largest concentration of galaxies in our nearby universe .
