Kiryat HaLeom     kiryat haleom ( ; `` the nation city '' ) , also known as kiryat hauma ( ; `` the people 's city '' ) , is a big project in west jerusalem that will include all of the israeli government buildings and the national cultural institutions . the project is near kiryat hamemshala , the knesset , and wohl rose park , in the northern part of givat ram . the project will include a national boulevard and square , and many parking spaces and pedestrian streets . other websites . - kiryat haleom in wikimapia - kiryat haleom project in archijob website - the national square project in kolker-epstein architects website
