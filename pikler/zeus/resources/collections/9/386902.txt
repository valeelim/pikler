Kure, Hiroshima    kure , hiroshima in 1894 , prime minister itō hirobumi transfered the imperial headquarters ( `` daihon'ei '' ) to kure . the modern city of kure was founded in 1902. the natural harbor was a naval base until the end of the second world war . the japanese battleship yamato was built at kure . timeline . - 1889 : kure army base established - 1894 : imperial diet met at kure - 1902 : city of kure created from merger of washō , futagawa , miyahara and sōyamada - 1903 : kure naval arsenal established . other websites . - city website ; - yamato museum
