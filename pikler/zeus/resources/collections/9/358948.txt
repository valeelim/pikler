Frank Cady     randolph cady ( september 8 , 1915 – june 8 , 2012 ) was an american movie and television actor best known for his recurring and popular role as storekeeper sam drucker in three american television series during the 1960s — `` petticoat junction '' , `` green acres '' , and `` the beverly hillbillies '' . cady was born on september 8 , 1915 in susanville , california . cady was married to shirley cady from 1940 until her death in 2008. cady had 2 children . cady died on june 8 , 2012 in his home in wilsonville , oregon from natural causes , aged 96 .
