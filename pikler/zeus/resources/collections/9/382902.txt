Joe Swanson     joe swanson is a character in the television comedy `` family guy '' . he is in a wheelchair after a criminal struck him with a pipe and later shot him in both legs . he was married to bonnie swanson until they separated and later were together again . although their relationship is not as strong as peter and lois they appear to forgive each other . they had one son and a daughter for which bonnie was pregnant for about seven-eight years instead of 9 months . he works as a police officer .
