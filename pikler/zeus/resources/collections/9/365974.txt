Andy Samberg     andrew david `` andy '' samberg ( born august 18 , 1978 ) is an american actor , stand-up comedian , singer , writer and the lead member of the comedy group , the lonely island . he is known for his works in `` saturday night live '' , `` hot rod '' and `` brooklyn nine-nine '' . he is influenced by steve martin , mel brooks and adam sandler . he married joanna newsom in september 2013 .
