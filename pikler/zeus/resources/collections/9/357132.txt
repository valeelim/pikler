Tony Snow     robert anthony snow , better known as tony snow ( june 1 , 1955 - july 12 , 2008 ) , was an american journalist , television personality , commentator and radio host who made contributions for fox news very regularly beginning in 1996. he was born in berea , kentucky and raised in cincinnati , ohio . he was host of `` fox news sunday , `` before working for president bush the second , but he briefly performed switched to cnn during 2008. however , he was merely an analyst at cnn , not a journalist . he was strongly associated with george w. bush ( when he was the president ) . before fox news , snow was often a guest for `` the rush limbaugh show '' . snow died from colon cancer in georgetown , washington , d.c .
