Saskatchewan River     the saskatchewan river is one of the great rivers of north america . it is part of the hudson bay watershed . it flows in canada east from the rockies to lake winnipeg , and eventually empties into the hudson bay . on its journey from the rockies , the river goes across three provinces , and covers 1,205 miles ( 1,939 kilometres ) from its furthest point . starting in alberta , the river goes right through saskatchewan and ends up in manitoba . the river 's tributaries in manitoba and saskatchewan are also large and important rivers . they include : - north saskatchewan river - battle river - south saskatchewan river - red deer river - bow river - oldman river
