Cyclone Jal     cyclone jal was a severe cyclonic storm that formed in the south china sea on october 31 , 2010. it then hit thailand and malaysia . it then went on to hit india . the storm hit the indian states of andhra pradesh , tamil nadu , karnataka , and parts of kerala . some of its moisture traveled to the states rajasthan and gujarat . there were 118 deaths caused by the cyclone . thousands were displaced .
