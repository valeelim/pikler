The Evening Star     the evening star is a 1996 romance comedy-drama movie . it is about a dysfunctional family in houston , texas . shirley maclaine plays aurora greenway , juliette lewis plays melanie horton and miranda richardson plays patsy carpenter . this movie was released on christmas 1996 in the united states . it is a sequel to `` terms of endearment '' `` the evening star '' got negative reviews from critics . it performed poorly at the box office . the movie is set in the years between 1988 and 1993 .
