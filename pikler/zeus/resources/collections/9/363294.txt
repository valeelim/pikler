Epsom     epsom is a town in northern surrey . the town is known for its race course and the horse race held there . the town also gives it name to epsom salts which are found in mineral water here . by the end of the georgian period ( early 1800s ) epsom was a spa town . some remains of this are its water pump , wells and exhibits in the town 's museum . a housing estate has been built upon the wells in the south-west of the town .
