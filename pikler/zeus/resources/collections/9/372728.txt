Catwoman     catwoman or selina kyle is a fictional batman character who is sometimes a villain or batman 's assistant in fighting crime . she is sometimes called `` batman 's girlfriend '' because of her love-hate with batman . catwoman wears leather clothing and has a whip . she is played by lee meriwether , julie newmar , eartha kitt , michelle pfeiffer , halle berry , and by anne hathaway in `` the dark knight rises '' .
