Gebhard Leberecht von Blucher     gebhard leberecht von blücher , fürst von wahlstatt ( ; december 16 , 1742 – september 12 , 1819 ) was a prussian general who went to war against napoleon . a bust in the german walhalla temple near regensburg was sculpted in his honour . other websites . - von blucher at thefreedictionary.com
