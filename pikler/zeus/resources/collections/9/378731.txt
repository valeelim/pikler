MI5     mi5 , also called security service is one of three british intelligence agencies . it is the security agency responsible for counter-intelligence inside the united kingdom . it gives the government information about hostile activities inside the uk . the slang term for mi5 inside the security services is `` box 500 '' , after its old wartime postal address . priorities are set by the joint intelligence committee . all its employees are bound by the official secrets act . the secret intelligence service ( or `` mi6 '' ) is responsible for intelligence collection outside the uk , and the government communications headquarters is responsible for providing signals intelligence . the arm of the law which works for mi5 is known informally as special branch . they have been rearranged as part of the metropolitan police 's counter terrorism command . mi5 staff do not have the power of arrest : what they do is instruct special branch , who then do the leg-work and interrogation of suspects .
