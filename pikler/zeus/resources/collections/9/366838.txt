Jake LaMotta     giacobbe `` jake '' lamotta ( born july 10 , 1921 ) , nicknamed `` the bronx bull '' and `` the raging bull , '' is an american retired professional boxer and former world middleweight champion . he was played by robert de niro in the 1980 movie `` raging bull '' . lamotta is an italian-american . he was born on july 10 , 1921 in the bronx , new york city . he has been married six times . he has four daughters .
