Carmine Falcone     carmine `` the roman '' falcone is a fictional batman character who is the head of the gotham mafia . he is sometimes called don falcone . he has a son , alberto falcone who is known as the holiday killer . in some cases , two-face flipped his coin and killed falcone . his body was robbed many times before being frozen by mr. freeze . his business was taken over by sal maroni . he was played by tom wilkinson in `` batman begins '' .
