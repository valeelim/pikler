Rich Little     richard caruthers `` rich '' little ( born november 26 , 1938 ) is an american-canadian comedian , movie , voice , radio , and stage actor . he is known for his impersonations of celebrities and as the first actor to provide the voice for the pink panther in `` pink panther '' . he has won a juno award . little was born on november 26 , 1938 in ottawa , ontario , canada . he was married to jeanne worden from 1971 until they divorced in 1989. he had a daughter in 1988 , lyndsay cottrell from a previous relationship . then he was married to jeannette markey from 1994 until they divorced in 1997. then he was married to marie marotta from 2003 until her death in 2010. he has now been married to catherine brown since 2012. he now lives in las vegas , nevada . he became a naturalized u.s. citizen in 2008. other websites . - official website
