Same Girl     `` same girl '' is a song by american singer r. kelly . it was written by r. kelly , ronnie jackson and j. smith . it was produced by lil ' ronnie and r. kelly . the song was released on may 29 , 2007. however , it was leaked onto the internet on may 2. the song was released as a single from his album `` double up '' ( 2007 ) . the song features usher . `` rolling stone '' magazine ranked the song number 26 on their list of 100 best songs of 2007. credits . - r. kelly - vocals , songwriter , producer , arranger , mixing - ronnie jackson ( lil ' ronnie ) - songwriter , producer - j. smith - songwriter - serban ghenea - mixing - abel garibaldi , ian mereness , jeff edwards - recording engineer - jeff meeks , tim roberts - assistant - john hanes - digital editing - usher - vocals
