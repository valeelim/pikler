Maliar     the maliars are an ethnic group , or people , mostly inhabiting the northern districts of punjab ( pakistan ) and the lower plains of the hazara division of the khyber pakhtunkhwa province of pakistan . the maliars are by and large an agrarian people and most still work as farm tenants and small peasant farmers . the term or name 'maliar ' itself derived from the punjabi language word that means 'gardener ' and 'cultivator ' . almost all the maliars are muslims .
