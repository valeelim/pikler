BeatBullying     beatbullying ( bb ) is a national charity in the united kingdom . the purpose of bb is to help people to understand , recognise , and say no to bullying . this is done by giving them the tools to change their lives and the lives of other people . beatbullying was established in 2002. other websites . - beatbullying website - beatbullying ( 1097631 ) at charitycommission.gov.uk
