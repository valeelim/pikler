Dwight Yorke Stadium     the dwight yorke stadium is in bacolet , tobago , ( trinidad and tobago ) . it is named after former manchester united footballer dwight yorke . the stadium was constructed for the 2001 fifa u-17 world championship which was hosted by trinidad and tobago . the dwight yorke stadium can seat 7,500. it is on the island of tobago . the stadium is only minutes from the capital scarborough and 25 minutes from the airport . it is used by the local football team . the playing surface dimension is 105 metres x 68 metres . the stadium hosted the 2005 carifta track and field championships and is also used for minor tobagonian athletics events . it also hosted games from the 2010 fifa u-17 women 's world cup .
