Tyrese Gibson     tyrese gibson ( december 30 1978 ) is an american r & b singer , rapper , actor and model . he has acted in many movies and television series . these include `` baby boy '' ( 2001 ) , `` 2 fast 2 furious '' and the `` transformers '' movie series . in 2000 , gibson won the american music award for `` favorite new r & b/soul artist '' . he has also been nominated for two grammy awards .
