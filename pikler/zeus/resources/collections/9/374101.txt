Martin H. Kennelly     martin h. kennelly ( august 11 , 1887 – november 29 , 1961 ) was an american politician . he was the mayor of chicago , illinois ( 1947–1955 ) . he was a member of the democratic party . kennelly was born on august 11 , 1887 in chicago , illinois . he was never married and had no children . kennelly died on november 29 , 1961 from heart failure , aged 74 .
