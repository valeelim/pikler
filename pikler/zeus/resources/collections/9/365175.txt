Josh Brolin     josh james brolin ( born february 12 , 1968 ) is an american movie , television , stage , and voice actor . he is known for his roles in ; `` w. '' , `` no country for old men '' , `` milk '' , `` true grit '' , and in `` men in black 3 '' . brolin was born on february 12 , 1968 in santa monica , california to james brolin and to jane cameron agee . he was raised in corpus christi , texas . brolin was married to alice adair from 1988 until they divorced in 1992. he has been married to diane lane since 2004. brolin has two children from adair . lane filed for divorced in february 2013 .
