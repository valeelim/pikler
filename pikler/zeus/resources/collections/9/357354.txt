John Aniston     john aniston ( born yannis anastasakis ; july 24 , 1933 ) is a greek-american television and movie actor . he is the father of actress jennifer aniston . he is best known for his role as victor kiriakis in `` days of our lives '' . that started in 1985. anisto 's career lasted 50 years . he has appeared in over 100 television programs and movies . aniston was born in chania , crete , greece to stella jones and to antonios anastasakis . aniston went to pennsylvania state university . aniston was married to nancy dow from 1965 until they divorced in 1980. aniston has been married to sherry rooney since 1984. with dow , aniston had 1 daughter , jennifer aniston . with rooney he had a son , alexander rooney . starred in : - `` love of life '' ( tv ) ( 1975 ) - `` search for tomorrow '' ( tv ) ( 1978 1974 ) - `` days of our lives '' ( 1985 present ) guest starred on : - `` gilmore girls '' - `` airwolf '' - `` combat '' - `` my big fat greek life '' - `` - `` mad men ''
