Saul Bellow     saul bellow ( june 10 , 1915 - april 5 , 2005 ) was a canadian-american writer . he was born solomon bellows in lachine , montreal to russian parents . he moved to chicago , illinois , when he was nine . he became an american citizen in 1941. he won the nobel prize in literature in 1976. he wrote the books `` henderson the rain king '' and `` herzog '' . he died in brookline , massachusetts .
