Mitsubishi Grandis     the mitsubishi grandis is a automobile produced by mitsubishi motors . it replaced the mitsubishi space wagon in 2003. it was sold in japan , asia , europe , oceania , mexico , honduras , jamaica , and south america . it won the best mpv award at the `` bangkok international motor show '' from 2005 to 2010. production ended in 2011. engines . - `` 2.4 '' 121 kw ( 165 hp ) - `` 2.0 di-d '' 100 kw ( 136 hp )
