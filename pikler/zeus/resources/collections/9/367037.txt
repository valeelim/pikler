Zenith     in astronomy , the zenith is a point `` above '' a particular location , in the sky . `` above '' means in the opposite direction of gravity at that location . the zenith is the opposite of the nadir . the nadir is in the direction the direction that gravity pulls . the zenith is not a real point , it is used as a reference for the location of other things in the sky . for scientists , the zenith is the direction of reference for measuring the zenith angle . the zenith angle is the angle between a place in the sky ( usually a star ) and the local zenith . related pages . - nadir
