Ceto     in ancient greek mytholoy ceto is the name of a sea monster ; often represented as a whale , or a large fish . she is the daughter of pontos and gaia . together with her brother phorcys she bore the gorgons , and graeae , echidna and ladon . note there is also a minor oceanid called keto . there are also various mythological beings referred to as `` ketos '' ( plural `` ketea '' ) ; this is a general term for `` sea monster '' in ancient greek .
