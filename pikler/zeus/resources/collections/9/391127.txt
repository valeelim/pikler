Saguia el Hamra     saguia el hamra was one of the two territories that made up the territory western sahara , when it was under spanish rule , until 1978. at that time , western sahara was called `` sahara occidental '' . it makes up the northern part of the territory , that extends from about 26° northern longitude , to about 27° 50 ' . it covers an area of roughly . it is named after the river saguia el hamra , which only exists during part of the year - a wadi . during the rainy season , it turns into a lake , located near the capital , el aaiún . the other territory was called río de oro
