Robert N. Proctor     robert neel proctor ( born 1954 ) is an american historian of science . he is professor of the history of science at stanford university . in 1999 , he was the first historian to testify against the tobacco industry . he was a professor of the history of science at pennsylvania state university . proctor made the word `` agnotology '' . agnotology means writing untrue or misleading information about scientific data .
