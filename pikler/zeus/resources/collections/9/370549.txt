Clay tablet     clay tablets were a medium used for writing . they were common in the fertile crescent , from about the 5th millennium bc . a clay tablet is a more or less flat surface made of clay . using a stylus , symbols were pressed into the soft clay . it is possible to correct errors on the tablet . the tablet was then baked or left in the sun until dry and hard . cuneiform was the first writing used on clay tablets . the system was used by several civilisaations who spoke several different languages .
