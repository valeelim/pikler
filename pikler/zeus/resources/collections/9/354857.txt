Peltobatrachus     peltobatrachus ( from greek pelte , meaning shield and batrakhos , meaning frog ) is an extinct genus of temnospondyl amphibians from the late permian period of tanzania . they were large and slow-moving animals . they reached 70 centimeters ( 2.3 ft ) in length . they were terrestrial amphibians . they only went back to the water to lay their eggs . they eat insects , worms and snails .
