Martin Freeman     martin john c. freeman ( born 8 september 1971 ) is an english actor . his most famous roles include tim canterbury in `` the office '' , john in `` love actually '' , arthur dent in the film adaptation of `` the hitchhiker 's guide to the galaxy '' , dr. watson in `` sherlock '' , and paul maddens in `` nativity ! '' he played the role of bilbo baggins in peter jackson 's three-part adaptation of `` the hobbit '' . freeman was born in aldershot , hampshire . he went to salesian school .
