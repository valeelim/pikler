Sporting Clube de Goa     sporting clube de goa are an indian football club based in the state of goa . sporting clube de goa is one of the teams which compete in the i-league . the club known as `` cidade de goa '' until the late 1990s . changes were made which were modeled after sporting clube de portugal . sporting clube de goa was established in 1999. in the 2003-2004 season , sporting clube was promoted to the indian national football league 1st division . the goan club had it 's best run in this season when the team finished second to dempo s.c.. the team became part of the i-league in the 2007-2008 season . players . notable past players . - james moga related pages . - sports in india - list of indian football teams other websites . - sporting clube de goa website - sporting clube de goa at iloveindia.com
