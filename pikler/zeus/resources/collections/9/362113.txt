Erika Slezak     erika slezak ( born august 5 , 1946 ) is a soap opera actress . she is strongly known because of her role as victoria lord in the television series `` one life to live '' . she was born around california . slezak has won six daytime emmy awards in the category of `` outstanding lead actress in a drama series '' . this is out of nine nominations for her role as viki . additionally , slezak won a `` soap opera digest '' award for favorite couple with mark derwin in 2000 . - daytime emmy award wins - `` soap opera digest '' award wins
