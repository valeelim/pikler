Gina McKie     gina mckie ( born 1973 in glasgow , scotland ) is a scottish radio personality and event . she currently presents the drivetime show across the greatest hits network . the show runs monday to friday 2 p.m to 6 p.m. the show comprises a fun mix of music and chat , as well as regular special guests . gina mckie was a part-time student at the royal scottish academy of music and drama . after that she moved from glasgow to dundee to study radio broadcasting at dundee college . she was also a member of the scottish youth theatre , mitchell theatre and the kilmardinny arts and theatre group . after completing a short course in radio drama at the bbc , mckie was offered her first on-air role in dundee with tay fm . she started as a production assistant and was promoted to head of music and then programme controller . mckie has studied with the british school of yoga . she has diplomas in advanced counselling skills and in psychology and hypnotherapy . she is a qualified reiki master and practitioner in neuro linguistic programming .
