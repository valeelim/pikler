Liberal Democratic Party (Japan)    liberal democratic party ( japan ) the liberal democratic party ( japanese : 自由民主党 `` jiyū-minshutō '' ; or just jiminto ) is one of the main political parties in japan . it has ruled japan for most of the time since world war ii . the party is conservative . the current leader is shinzo abe . the party wants a close relationship with the united states , and wants fast export-based economic growth . they also want to undergo more privatization . the party is social conservative , and is against the legalization of same-sex marriage . jiminto is also a patriotic party . in 2012 , the liberal democratic party had around 800,000 members . other websites . - the official website of liberal democratic party - japanese123
