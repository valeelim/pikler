Vietnamese Civil War     several conflicts in vietnamese history have been considered civil wars . these are all major conflicts that occurred between north and south vietnam : - the 12 lords rebellion ( 966-968 ) [ victory : north vietnam ] - lê-mạc war ( 1533-1592 ) [ victory : south vietnam ] - trịnh-nguyễn war ( 1627-1672 ; 1774-1775 ) [ victory : north vietnam ] - tây sơn-nguyễn lords war ( 1771-1785 ; 1787-1802 ) [ victory : north vietnam ] - tây sơn-trịnh lords war ( 1786-1789 ) [ victory : south vietnam ]
