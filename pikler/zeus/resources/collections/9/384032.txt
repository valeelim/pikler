Oxford Street     oxford street is a major road in the city of westminster in the west end of london . it is europe 's busiest shopping street , and has about 300 shops . the street forms part of the a40 road , which connects london to oxford . like many roads in central london which are no longer through routes it is not signposted with the road number . roughly halfway along oxford street is oxford circus , a busy intersection with regent street . a diagonal crossing at oxford circus opened in 2009 , currently the only one of its kind in central london .
