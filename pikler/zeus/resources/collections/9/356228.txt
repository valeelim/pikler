Thandie Newton     thandie newton ( born november 6 , 1972 ) is a british actress . she has performed in numerous british and american movies , including `` the pursuit of happyness '' and `` '' . she was born in london to a black zimbabwean mother and white british father . she grew up in africa , london and penzance . newton says that director john duigan made her have a relationship with him . she was sixteen years old and he was 39. the relationship lasted 6 years . she married english writer/director/producer ol parker in 1998. the couple have two daughters : ripley , born in 2000 , and nico , born in 2004. her daughters are named after the character ellen ripley in the `` alien '' movies and the singer nico . newton has suffered from bulimia . newton is a buddhist .
