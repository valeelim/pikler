Arena     an arena is a circular or oval shaped public space . it is similar to a theater , a concert or sporting events . it is made up of a large open space surrounded on all sides by seats or benches for people to sit . the main feature of an arena is that the event space is the lowest point . this allows everyone to see what 's going on . sometimes , an arena is created to have a very large number of people , such as new york city 's madison square garden or pasadena 's rose bowl . in the united states , the term 'arena ' , sometimes means an indoor stadium .
