Edwin Starr     edwin starr ( january 21 , 1942 – april 2 , 2003 ) was an american singer-songwriter and musician . he had many hit songs . his most notable song was `` war '' . the song sold over one million copies . he was very popular in the late 1960s and early 1970s . starr was an african american who was born in nashville and grew up in cleveland . on april 2 , 2003 , at the age of 61 , starr suffered a heart attack and died while taking a bath at his home in bramcote near nottingham . he left a wife , annette mary hatcher , a son andré hatcher , and two grandchildren alonté renfroe and maryah hatcher . albums . - `` soul master '' ( 1965 ) - `` 25 miles '' ( 1969 ) ( # 73 us ) ( # 9 r & b ) - `` war and peace '' ( 1970 ) ( # 52 us ) ( # 9 r & b ) - `` involved '' ( 1971 ) ( # 178 us ) ( # 45 r & b ) - `` hell up in harlem '' ( 1974 ) - `` free to be myself '' ( 1975 ) ( # 43 r & b ) ( # 210 us ) - `` clean '' ( 1979 ) ( # 80 us ) ( # 22 r & b ) - `` happy radio '' ( 1979 ) ( # 115 us ) ( # 44 r & b ) - `` stronger than you think i am '' ( 1979 ) ( # 203 us ) - `` for sale '' ( 1983 ) other websites . - official site
