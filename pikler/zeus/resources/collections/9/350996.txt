Mill Green     mill green is a hamlet in edwardstone , babergh , suffolk , england . it is edwardstone 's main settlement . mill green has a pub called the white horse , mill green brewery is also behind it . a smock windmill was in mill green , but it was demolished in 1965. mill green brewery has won a national siba gold award . it is also on the good pub guide 2011 2012 and 2014. related pages . - listed buildings in mill green other websites . - http : //www.suffolkfreepress.co.uk/news/latest-news/edwardstone-s-mill-green-brewery-scoops-award-1-561523 - http : //www.geograph.org.uk/gridref/tl9542
