Jaffa Port     jaffa port is an ancient port in old jaffa , israel . the port has history from the time of the bible until the 20th century . in 1936 the tel aviv port was opened on the mouth of the yarkon river to replace jaffa port . in 1965 the ashdod port next to lakish river and was built to replace the ports at jaffa and tel aviv . in the 2000s , the port area was renovated and become an area of ​​commercial and recreation for tourists and visitors . other websites . - jaffa port website
