Andrea True     andrea true ( born andrea marie truden , july 26 , 1943 - november 7 , 2011 ) was an american porn star and a singer from the disco era of the 1970s . she was born in nashville , tennessee . she is best known for her single `` more more more '' . it was released in 1976 and peaked at # 4 on the `` billboard '' hot 100. she died of heart failure in kingston , new york , where she was raised .
