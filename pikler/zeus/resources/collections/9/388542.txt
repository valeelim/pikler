Jaime Bayly     jaime bayly letts ( born february 19 , 1965 ) is a peruvian writer and journalist . he is known for hosting his own spanish host show called `` bayly '' . he is known for criticizing many politicians throughout the world . he won three emmy awards . bayly was born on february 19 , 1965 in lima , peru . he was raised in lima and in florida . he studied at markham college , at colegio san agustín of lima , and at pontificia universidad católica del peru . bayly was married to sandra masías from 1993 until they divorced in 1997. he married silvia nuñez del arco in 2011. he now lives in miami , florida . other websites . - jaime bayly in ntn24 from bogotá , colombia - november 2009
