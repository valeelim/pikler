Juno Temple     juno violet temple ( born july 21 , 1989 ) is an english movie , television , stage , and voice actress who is known for her roles in `` atonement '' , `` dirty girl '' , `` st trinian 's '' , `` killer joe '' and in `` the dark knight rises '' . temple was born on july 21 , 1989 in london , england to amanda pirie and to julien temple . she studied at enmore primary school , bedales school , and at king 's college .
