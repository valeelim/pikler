Baise-moi     baise-moi is a 2000 french crime drama thriller movie . it was called rape me in the united states , where it was released without an mpaa rating . in australia , `` baise-moi '' was rated r18 at first , but was then refused classification due to content . this movie got mixed reviews from critics in many countries . the title does not mean `` kiss me '' , and absolutely it does not mean `` rape me '' . it means `` fuck me '' . it is about a sex-and-violence spree by two young women . the sex scenes are apparently real , rather than simulated , and the film has been banned in many countries .
