Amy Stiller     amy stiller ( born august 9 , 1961 ) is an american actress , stand-up comedian and bikram yoga instructor . she was born in new york , new york . she is the daughter of the comedians jerry stiller and anne meara , and the older sister of actor ben stiller . she is married to voice actor rodger bumpass who does the voice of squidward in `` spongebob squarepants '' .
