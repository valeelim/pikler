Road signs in South Africa     road signs in africa stay close to the vienna convention on road signs and signals concerning shape and function . there are also some local signs unique to south africa . a white background signifies the sign is permanent . a yellow background signifies that the sign is temporary . warning signs are an upwards-pointing red triangle and black pictogram describing the danger or obstruction . speed limit signs are a red circle with the limitation in black . south africa drives on the left . references . - trafficsigns.co.za - getyourlearners.co.za
