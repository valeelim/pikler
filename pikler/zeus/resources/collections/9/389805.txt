Sankt Georgen Graduate School of Philosophy and Theology     the sankt georgen graduate school of philosophy and theology ( german : `` philosophisch-theologische hochschule sankt georgen '' ) is an academic seminary in frankfurt am main , germany . the school was founded in 1926 by the society of jesus . sank georgen established a jesuit theology program in 1951. in 1986 , pope francis he began work on a doctorate degree at sankt georgen . other websites . - sankt georgen website
