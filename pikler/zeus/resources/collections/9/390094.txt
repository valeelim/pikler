Boomerang (1992 movie)    boomerang ( 1992 movie ) boomerang is a 1992 romantic comedy movie . it talks about relations between african-american adults . eddie murphy , robin givens , martin lawrence , eartha kitt and halle berry are among the cast . the movie has a runtime of around 117 minutes . the release date was july 1 , 1992. the reviews for this romantic comedy were mixed to negative . but the box office revenue intake of $ 131 million made this movie a strong performer .
