Shōten     shōten is a comedy japanese television series . it is broadcast on sunday afternoons in nippon television network corporation . the show started in 1966. there are eight members of this tv program : utamaru , koyuuza , kouraku , kikuō , rakutarou , shōta , taihei and takao yamada . they are very famous comic storytellers except yamada . yamada carries japanese cushion for other broadcaster . yamada says `` i carry not only cushion but also happiness .
