Cuboid bone     the cuboid is one of the many bones of the feet . it is located on the side of the foot . it articulates ( connects with ) metatarsals number 4 and 5. ligaments help hold the foot in an arched position and to hold the metatarsals and medial bones ( one of many is the cuboid ) together . most likely , the name cuboid comes from the fact that the cuboid bone is shaped kind of like a cube . related pages . - phalanges - metatarsals - tarsals
