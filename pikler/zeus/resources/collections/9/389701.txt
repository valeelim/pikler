Now and Then (movie)    now and then ( movie ) now and then is a 1995 comedy-drama movie . it is about four women in two different time periods : 1970 and 1991. demi moore plays samantha albertson , christina ricci and rosie o'donnell play roberta martin , thora birch and melanie griffith act as tina `` teeny '' tercell . lolita davidovich plays samantha 's mom . it was released on october 20 , 1995. reviews for this comedy-drama were very negative , although `` now and then '' has since become a classic .
