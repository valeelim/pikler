Gendarmenmarkt     the gendarmenmarkt is a square in berlin , and the site of the and the french cathedral and german cathedrals . the centre of the gendarmenmarkt is crowned by a statue of germany 's poet friedrich schiller . the square was created by johann arnold nering at the end of the seventeenth century as the linden-markt and reconstructed by georg christian unger in 1773. the gendarmenmarkt is named after the prussian regiment . during world war ii , most of the buildings were badly damaged or destroyed . today all the buildings are restored to their former state . the gendarmenmarkt hosts one of berlin 's most popular christmas markets . other websites . - website of das konzerthaus - der gendarmenmarkt on berlin tourism - gendarmenmarkt panorama
