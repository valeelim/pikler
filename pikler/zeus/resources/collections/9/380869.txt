Reims – Prunay Aerodrome     reims – prunay aerodrome ( ) is an airfield serving the city of reims . it is east-southeast of reims , near prunay , both communes in the marne department in the champagne-ardenne region in northeastern france . it is a class d aerodrome open to general aviation traffic with no commercial airline service scheduled . also , it hosts the factory of reims aviation . the airport is above mean sea level . it has one paved runway which is long . it also has a parallel unpaved runway measuring .
