Portrait of Douglass Morgan Hall     portrait of douglass morgan hall is an oil painting by thomas eakins . it was painted in 1889. it measures 61 × 50.8 cm ( 24 × 20 in ) . it hangs in the philadelphia museum of art . douglass morgan hall , the son of a philadelphia ophthalmologist , studied at the pennsylvania academy of the fine arts in 1885–86 . after eakins 's forced resignation from the academy , hall enrolled as one of his students at the philadelphia art students league in 1887 , remaining there until 1890 .
