BAFTA Award for Best Film     this page lists the winners and nominees for the bafta award for best film , bafta award for best film not in the english language and alexander korda award for best british film for each year . the british academy of film and television arts ( bafta ) , is a british organisation that hosts annual awards shows for movies , television , children 's movies and television , and interactive media . since 1948 , selected movies have been awarded with the bafta award for best film at an annual ceremony .
