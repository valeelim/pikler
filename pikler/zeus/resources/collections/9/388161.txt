Pulse     a pulse ( or heartbeat ) is a throbbing of blood vessels as blood goes through them . you can feel someone 's pulse in their wrist , thumb or neck , where vessels are closer to the skin . a normal pulse is below 100 beats per minute . our pulse quickens when we exercise because the muscles being used need more oxygen . a change in heart sounds might mean sickness . there are normally four heart sounds . two of them , s1 and s2 , can be heard with a stethoscope . two other sounds , s3 and s4 , can not be heard , but they can be seen using a machine called a phonocardiograph . there are many other heart sounds which are abnormal and can be heard or seen . pulses is a term for peas , beans and lentils .
