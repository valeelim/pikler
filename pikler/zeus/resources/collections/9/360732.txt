Tsushima Island     tsushima island ( 対馬 , `` tsushima / 대마도 , daema-do '' ) is a japanese island in the waters between south korea and japan . beginning in the early 6th century , the island was a province of japan , it was called tsushima province . in the meiji period , the island and the province became part of nagasaki prefecture . related pages . - russo-japanese war - korea strait - tsushima strait other websites . - tsushima city website - tsushima profile - murdoch 's map of provinces , 1903
