Hasmonean dynasty     the hasmonean dynasty was a family that led the hasmoneans led the maccabean revolt and transformed judah from a seleucid possession that was becoming more greek-like into an independent , jewish nation-state . the hasmoneans expanded judah through conquest . after the salome alexandra 's death in 67 b.c. , salome 's sons hyrcanus ii , who was backed by antipater ( the father of herod the great ) , and aristobulus ii fought for control of the judah . the roman pompey entered the conflict on hyrcanus ' side . aristobulus surrendered to the romans and was sent to rome , where he was later murdered . in 40 b.c. , the parthian empire and aristobulus ' son antigonus ii invaded judah and captured hyrcanus . the romans then proclaimed antipater 's son herod king of judea . the romans defeated the parthians and captured and killed antigonus , the last ruler of the hasmonean dynasty .
