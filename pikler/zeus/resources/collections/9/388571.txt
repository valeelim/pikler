Stephen Hillenburg     stephen mcdannell hillenburg ( born august 21 , 1961 ) is an american marine biologist , marine scientist , animator , writer , producer , actor , voice actor , and director . he is best known for creating the nickelodeon animated series `` spongebob squarepants '' . in march 2017 , hillenburg was diagnosed with amyotrophic lateral sclerosis ( als ) which is a terminal illness that affects the nervous system .
