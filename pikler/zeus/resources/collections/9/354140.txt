Polystyrene     polystyrene is a form of chemical compound that is mainly used for a wide variety of packaging.it is an aromatic polymer made from the monomer styrene , a liquid hydrocarbon that is manufactured from petroleum by the chemical industry . polystyrene is one of the most widely used plastics , the scale being several billion kilograms per year . polystyrene can be in either a thermoset or a thermoplastic form .
