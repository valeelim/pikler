Diane Lane     diane lane ( born january 22 , 1965 ) is an american movie actress . born and raised in new york city , new york . lane made her career debut at the age of 13 in george roy hill 's 1979 movie `` a little romance '' , starring opposite sir laurence olivier . soon after , she was featured on the cover of `` time '' magazine . she has since appeared in several notable movie , including the 2002 movie `` unfaithful '' , which earned her academy award , golden globes and screen actors guild award nominations . she appeared in zack snyder 's movie `` man of steel '' . she played hillary rodham clinton in the 2013 nbc four-hour miniseries `` hillary '' . lane has been married to actor josh brolin since 2004. she filed for divorced in february 2013. she was previously married to actor christopher lambert from 1988 to 1994 .
