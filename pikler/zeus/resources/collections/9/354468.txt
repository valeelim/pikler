Bradley Wiggins     bradley wiggins ( born 28 april 1980 ) is a british cyclist . he was born in ghent , belgium . he grew up in kilburn and maida vale , london . his mother linda is english ; his father was australian cyclist gary wiggins ( 1952-2008 ) . wiggins won a bronze medal at the 2000 summer olympics , a gold medal , silver medal and bronze medal at the 2004 summer olympics , two gold medals at the 2008 summer olympics and a gold medal at the 2012 summer olympics . he won the 2012 tour de france .
