Alan Ball (screenwriter)    alan ball ( screenwriter ) alan ball ( born may 13 , 1957 ) is an american writer , movie director and producer . he was born in atlanta , georgia and is openly gay . ball wrote `` american beauty '' , for which he won an academy award and a golden globe award . he created , wrote and produced `` six feet under '' ( for which he won an emmy award ) and `` true blood '' .
