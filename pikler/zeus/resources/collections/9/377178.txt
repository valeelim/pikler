Vexillology (album)    vexillology ( album ) vexillology is the second studio album by canadian electronica producer , deadmau5 . `` vexillology '' was released on november 6 , 2006. the album is only of two of deadmau5 's album that do not feature his signature `` mau5head '' on the cover art . the name of the album is from the study of flags and their symbols .
