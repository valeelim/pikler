Curt Menefee     curt menefee ( july 22 , 1965 ) is an american sportscaster . he is the current studio host for fox `` nfl sunday '' with terry bradshaw , howie long , michael strahan , jimmy johnson and jay glazer . he also called play-by-play for nfl europe on nfl network and fox and regional nfl games for fox . he also calls seattle seahawks preseason games and did the same for the jacksonville jaguars . he was the host of `` the curt menefee show '' and `` unnecessary roughness '' . menefee was a sports anchor for ktvt-tv , wnyw-tv , and wtlv-tv . he made an appearance with jose mota on mlb on fox on the may 24 , 2008 game between the los angeles angels of anaheim and the chicago white sox . he also hosted three uefa champions league final matches for fox ; 2010 , 2011 , and 2012. menefee also is the host of ufc on fox . he has also provided play-by-play for `` toprank boxing '' and `` shobox : the new generation '' .
