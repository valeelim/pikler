Nick Fury     colonel nicholas joseph `` nick '' fury is a fictional war hero and spy who helps marvel heroes such as hulk , the fantastic four , iron man , captain america , thor , and spider-man . he formed two groups one with spies s.h.i.e.l.d . and another one with superheroes the avengers . he has an eye-patch and uses guns or advanced technology to fight criminals . he used to serve in world war ii with captain america . in the marvel movie versions about some heroes such as `` iron man '' , `` thor '' , `` '' , `` iron man 2 '' , `` iron man 3 '' , `` the avengers '' , and `` the avengers 2 '' , he is played by samuel l. jackson .
