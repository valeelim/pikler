Chicoutimi Saguenéens     the chicoutimi saguenéens are a canadian ice hockey team . they began playing in 1973. the team currently plays in the quebec major junior hockey league . they play their home games in chicoutimi , quebec at the centre georges-vézina . the team was created in 1973. the name of the team means `` people from the saguenay '' which chicoutimi is a part of . the team has won two president 's cups in their history . they have also made it to the memorial cup finals in 1997 but were defeated by the host hull olympics . other websites . - official website
