David Spade     david wayne spade ( born july 22 , 1964 ) is an american movie , television , stage , radio , voice actor , comedian , screenwriter , and television personality . he is known for his roles as dennis finch in `` just shoot me ! `` , as marcus higgins in `` grown-ups '' and as russell dunbar in `` rules of engagement '' . spade was born on july 22 , 1964 in birmingham , michigan . spade moved to scottsdale , arizona at age four . he studied at arizona state university . spade has a daughter named harper . the mother is jillian grace , a playboy playmate .
