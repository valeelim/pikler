Cam Barker     cameron barker ( born april 4 , 1986 in winnipeg , manitoba ) is a canadian professional ice hockey defenceman . he is currently an unrestricted free agent . he has played with the chicago blackhawks , minnesota wild , edmonton oilers , and vancouver canucks of the national hockey league ( nhl ) . he has also played with barys astana of the kontinental hockey league ( khl ) . barker was drafted 3rd overall in the 2004 nhl entry draft by the chicago blackhawks . barker played 5 seasons with the blackhawks before being traded to the minnesota wild for kim johnsson and defensive prospect nick leddy . he played 2 seasons with the wild and the wild placed him on waivers on june 28 , 2011. he signed a contract with the edmonton oilers on july 1 , 2011. he played a season with the oilers before being cut by the team on june 25 , 2012. he joined the texas stars for 23 games before signing a $ 700,000 , one-year contract with the vancouver canucks . awards . - he was named to the whl all-rookie team in 2003 .
