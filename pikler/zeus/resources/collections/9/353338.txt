Bedtime (song)    bedtime ( song ) `` bedtime '' is a song by american singer usher . it was released from his album `` my way '' ( 1997 ) in 1998. it became the fourth and last single to be released from that album . `` bedtime '' was released as a promotional recording . it reached number 66 on the hot r & b/hip-hop songs chart . track listing . 1 . `` bed time '' [ album version ] - 4:45 1 . `` bed time '' [ live version ] - 7:06
