Mushfiqur Rahim     mohammad mushfiqur rahim ( ) ( born 1 september 1988 ) is a bangladeshi cricketer and the captain of his national team . he made his test debut in 2005 , at the age of 16. rahim trained at the bangladesh institute of sports . at the next year he made his odi debut . at the end of 2011 he was given the captain of his national side . other websites . - mushfiqur rahim from espncricinfo - mushfiqur rahim from cricketarchive
