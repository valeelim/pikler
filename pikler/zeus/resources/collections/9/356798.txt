Benbecula     benbecula ( ) is an island of the outer hebrides in the atlantic ocean off the west coast of scotland . it forms part of the area administered by the western isles council . in the 2001 census it had a resident population of 1,249 , with a sizable percentage of roman catholics . this is slightly unusual because the highlands and islands is generally the religious territory of the wee frees , the free church of scotland and its various offshoots .
