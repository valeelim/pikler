Dana Hill     dana hill ( born dana lynne goetz ; may 6 , 1964-july 15 , 1996 ) was an american voice actress . she has a raspy voice . she starred in television programs and movies , including `` goof troop '' ( 1992-1993 ) . she died resulting from a stroke that was due to having type 1 diabetes ( she was diagnosed at age 10 ) . the actress was born around california .
