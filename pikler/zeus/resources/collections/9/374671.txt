Ed O'Neill     edward phillip `` ed '' o'neill , jr. ( born april 12 , 1946 ) is an american movie , television , stage , and voice actor who is known for his roles as al bundy in `` married ... with children '' and as jay pritchett in `` modern family '' . he has won two screen actors guild awards for his role as pritchett . o'neill was born on april 12 , 1946 in youngstown , ohio . he studied at youngstown state university . o'neill has been married to catherine rusoff since 1986. they have two children .
