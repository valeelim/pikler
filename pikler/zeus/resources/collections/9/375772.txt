Brenda Ann Spencer     brenda ann spencer ( born in san diego , california on april 3 , 1962 ) is a convicted american murderer who performed a deadly shooting spree , during which she killed two people at the cleveland elementary school in san diego on january 29 , 1979. another nine got hurt during that incident . spencer showed no remorse in her crime ; her full explanation of her actions was `` i do n't like mondays ; this livens up the day . '' those comments were the inspiration to the song `` i do n't like mondays '' by the boomtown rats , which reached # 1 in the united kingdom in mid 1979 .
