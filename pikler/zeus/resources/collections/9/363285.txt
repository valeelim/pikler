Wendie Malick     wendie malick ( born wendy malick , december 13 , 1950 ) is an american movie , television , and voice actress , who is known for her roles as judith tupper stone in `` dream on '' , nina van horn in `` just shoot me ! '' and ronee lawrence on the nbc sitcom `` frasier '' . she has won a golden globe award and a emmy award . malick was born on december 13 , 1950 in buffalo , new york . she studied at williamsville south high school and at ohio wesleyan university . malick has been married to mitch glazer from 1982 until they divorced in 1989. she was married to richard erickson since 1995 .
