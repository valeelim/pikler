Churchill College, Cambridge    churchill college , cambridge churchill college is one of the colleges of the university of cambridge , england . in 1958 a group of people was formed to get money to build a new college . this group had winston churchill as leader , so the college was named after him . it opened in 1960. churchill college has more than 700 students , one of the biggest . many of them study a science subject . churchill college was the first college in cambridge to allow female students . this happened in 1972. other websites . - official website
