James Daly     james firman daly ( october 23 , 1918 - july 3 , 1978 ) was an american movie , television , stage , radio , and voice actor who is known for his role as paul lochner in `` medical center '' . he won an emmy award in 1966. daly was born on october 23 , 1918 in wisconsin rapids , wisconsin . he studied at cornell college . he was married to hope newall from 1942 until they divorced in 1965. they had 4 children , including tim daly and tyne daly . daly died on july 3 , 1978 in nyack , new york from heart failure , aged 59 .
