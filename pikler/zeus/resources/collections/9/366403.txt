Provence     provence is a region in southern france . it lies . historically , it is limited by the river rhône to the west , and the border with italy to the east . it makes up the biggest part of the modern administrative region provence-alpes-côte d'azur . in the past , the capitals were arles , aix-en-provence and avignon . today , the regional capital is marseille . other important cities ( prefectures ) are digne-les-bains , gap , nice and toulon .
