Checker-throated Antwren     the checker-throated antwren ( `` epinecrophylla fulviventris '' ) is a small passerine bird in the antbird family . as its name , the males have throats with a black and white `` checkered '' pattern . the checker-throated antwren is the only member of the genus that lives in the west of the andes . the species is found from honduras south to northern colombia and to western ecuador .
