David Copperfield (1935 movie)    david copperfield ( 1935 movie ) david copperfield is a 1935 black and white movie directed by george cukor . the movie was based on the 1850 book of the same name by charles dickens . the movie was a great success . it was nominated for the best picture academy award . main cast . - freddie bartholomew as the young david - edna mae oliver as his aunt betsey trotwood - w. c. fields as mr. micawber - basil rathbone as edward murdstone - maureen o'sullivan as dora - lionel barrymore as dan'l peggoty
