Adjustment disorder     adjustment disorder happens when a person is unable to cope with or adjust to certain stressors . people who have adjustment disorder often have symptoms such as losing interest , being sad , crying , feeling anxious and constant worries . these symptoms are from outside stressors , although they resolve when individuals become used to the situations . the stressors may include , although are not limited to : hurricanes , being abused , accidents and traumatic events . this problem can happen at any age . in adults , however , adult women are diagnosed almost twice as often as men .
