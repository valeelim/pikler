Sankagiri     sankagiri or sankari ( ) is a panchayat town . it is in the salem district of tamil nadu . it is on the national highway 47. sankagiri fort is a historical fort . it is maintained by the archaeological survey of india . sankagiri fort was built in the 15th century . it was built by the vijayanagar empire . it has 14 fort walls built on and around a hill . the last phase of these walls were built by the british .
