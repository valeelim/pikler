Tyne Daly     ellen tyne daly ( born february 21 , 1946 ) is an american movie , television , stage , and voice actress who is known for her roles as detective mary beth lacey in `` cagney & lacey '' and as maxine gray in `` judging amy '' . she has won six emmy awards , an tony award , and an drama desk award . daly was born on february 21 , 1946 in madison , wisconsin . she studied at brandeis university and at the american musical and dramatic academy . she was married to georg stanford brown from 1966 until they divorced in 1990. they had three children . her brother is actor tim daly ; her father was actor james daly . other websites . - tyne daly 's official web site
