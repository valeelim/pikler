Sind sparrow     the sind sparrow ( `` passer pyrrhonotus '' ) , also known as the sind jungle sparrow , jungle sparrow , or rufous-backed sparrow , is a passerine bird of the passeridae sparrow family . it lives around the indus valley region of pakistan and parts of the republic of india . within its indus valley breeding range in pakistan and western india , the sind sparrow lives in wetland habitats with thorny scrub and tall grass . it is also the provincial bird of sindh province of pakistan .
