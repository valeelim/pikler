Colourful Radio     colourful radio is a commercial radio station in london . it broadcasts to birmingham and manchester on dab digital radio . it also plays over the internet . colourful radio 's output is split between news , current affairs and music . the type of music the station plays is a combination of afrobeat , soul , rnb , reggae , and jazz-funk . colourful radio was officially founded in 2002 by kofi kusitor . it went public in 2004 as the uk 's first internet audio stream . it played various shows over the internet until it joined sky satellite in 2006. colourful radio began broadcasting on dab on 2 march 2009. by then mr kusitor had appointed as head of station , gordon mac ( formerly of kiss fm ) and as head of programmes henry bonsu ( former presenter of bbc london 94.9 ) to ran the station . other websites . - colourful radio website
