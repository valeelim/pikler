Factoring (finance)    factoring ( finance ) factoring is selling rights to the future payments . people and companies that are receiving money from contracts every some time may be in need of extra money - so they sell rights to receive those payments for cash hence factoring . this is done especially in construction business where companies are paid for parts of work completed instead of upfront payment . such a company will sell future payments for small percentage fee and will receive all money they need to continue the work without delays . the structured settlement factoring transaction can be really helpful for business in financial needs . it provides business with better financing and more funds on hands in exchange for small percentage of total sum . some banks are entering field of factoring trying to provide same service advertised by well known names .
