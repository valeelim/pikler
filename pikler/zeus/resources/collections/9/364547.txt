Howard David     howard david is a longtime sportscaster . he was most recently with the nfl on westwood one until 2012. he called nba games for the new jersey nets , milwaukee bucks , and the boston celtics . david called nfl games for the new york jets and the miami dolphins . he called football and basketball for the new york cosmos and the princeton tigers . he called college bowling , college football and college basketball for mizlou television . for cbs radio , he called play-by-play for the nfl on cbs radio and mlb on cbs radio . he replaced jack buck as lead play-by-play for the nfl on westwood one until 2002. david returned to westwood one until 2012. david also called nba games for espn radio . he is the play-by-play announcer for the ufl on cbs sports network and was the play-by-play voice for the nfl on sports usa . david calls play-by-play for the united football league on the cbs sports network . he also called two games for mlb on fox during the 2013 mlb season .
