Tel Aviv Port     tel aviv port was an old port in tel aviv , israel . it was used between the years 1938 and 1965. it was next to the mouth of the yarkon river . after it closed in 1965 , it was moved to ashdod port and became a warehouse area . in the 2000s , it was renovated and became a ​​commercial and recreational area . programs of reconstruction and rehabilitation are being carried out after more than ten years . the tel aviv port was a part of a big development area called the yarkon river peninsula . it was built beside the orient fair and looks like a peninsula from above . the area around the port was built in the 1930s . related pages . - levant fair other websites . - tel aviv port website
