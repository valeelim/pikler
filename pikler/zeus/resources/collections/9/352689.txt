Théophile de Donder     théophile ernest de donder ( 1872–1957 ) was a belgian mathematician and physicist . he was famous for work in making connections between the concepts of chemical affinity and free energy . he is said to have been the first to address the thermodynamics of irreversible processes . de donder ’ s work was later developed further by ilya prigogine . de donder was an associate and friend of albert einstein .
