Charlie Daniels     charles edward `` charlie '' daniels ( born october 28 , 1936 ) is an american musician . he plays a fiddle and country rock music . his best known song is `` the devil went down to georgia '' . daniels was born in leland , north carolina . daniels has been married to hazel daniels since 1963. they have a son , charlie daniels , jr. , and three grandchildren . he is a stroke survivor . other websites . - official website
