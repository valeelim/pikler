Curtin Springs     curtin springs is a large working cattle station in the northern territory of australia . it is on the lasseter highway , east of yulara . the property is in size . mount conner is inside the southern border of the property . curtin springs was built in 1943 and is owned and operated by the severin family . it also includes a roadhouse , an inn and a campground . in september and october 2012 , bushfires destroyed over of bush , nearly a quarter of its pasture land .
