P.G. Wodehouse    p.g . wodehouse p.g . wodehouse , in full sir pelham grenville wodehouse ( 15 october 1881 - 14 february 1975 ) was an english comic novelist , short-story writer and playwright , best known as the creator of the 'jeeves ' character and series of novels . he was born in guildford , surrey . he died of a heart attack in southampton , new york .
