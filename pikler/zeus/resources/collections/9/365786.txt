Carlos Büsser     carlos alberto césar büsser ( january 10 , 1928 – september 29 , 2012 ) was the commander of the argentine forces during operation rosario , that took the falkland islands in 1982 , and forced the surrender of its governor . büsser was born on january 10 , 1928 in rosario , argentina . büsser died on september 29 , 2012 in buenos aires , argentina from a heart attack , aged 84 .
