Sengunthar     sengunthar is a caste from the indian state of tamil nadu . they are also known as kaikolar and user mudaliar as title . they are present throughout the state of tamil nadu and also in some parts of andhra and kerala apart from the tamizh eezham . the considerable proportion of this community is engaged in the occupation of weaving . they are included in the central list of other backward community . senguntha mudaliars have their historical connection with the medieval and later chola army battalion .
