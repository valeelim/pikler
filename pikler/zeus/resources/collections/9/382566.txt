Green iguana     the lizard commonly known as green iguana or common iguana ( `` iguana iguana '' ) is native to central and south america and the caribbean . it is an arboreal herbivorous species that belongs to the genus `` iguana '' . the green iguana ranges over a large geographic area , from southern brazil and paraguay as far north as mexico . also the caribbean islands , especially puerto rico , where they are very common throughout the island ; and in the united states in south florida ( including the florida keys ) , hawaii , and the rio grande valley of texas . other webs . - green iguana society - melissa kaplan 's green iguana information collection - international iguana foundation
