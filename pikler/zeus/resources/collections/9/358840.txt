Victor Ponta     victor ponta ( b . 20 september 1972 , bucharest ) is a romanian politician . he is president of the social democratic party ( psd ) . on 7 may 2012 , he became prime minister of romania.. he resigned on 4 november 2015 he studied law in bucharest , finishing in 1995. he is psd 's candidate for the romanian presidential elections in 2014. victor ponta 's official site
