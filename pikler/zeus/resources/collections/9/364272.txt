Input     input is information that is `` put in '' to something . for example , when people talk , that is input for ears . the word is usually used when talking about computers . when someone types on a computer , the thing that they type is input . usually , input is used to make output . input may refer to : - advice ( opinion ) , an opinion or recommendation offered by someone - input ( computer science ) , the act of entering data into a computer - information , any data entered into a computer or data processing system - input/output , the communication between a digital processing system - input device , a hardware device that is used to provide data for processing - power consumption , an amount of power used by a system
