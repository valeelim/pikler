Yad Vashem     yad vashem ( ) is a memorial site in jerusalem . it is the central memorial for the victims of the holocaust . it includes a museum , school for teachers , archives , and library . the site is located in jerusalem forest on the western slope of mount herzl ( `` mount of remembrance '' ) . it is 804 meters ( 2,638 ft ) above sea level . related pages . - righteous among the nations other websites . - yad vashem
