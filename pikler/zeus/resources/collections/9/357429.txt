Alexis Bledel     kimberly alexis bledel ( september 16 , 1981 ) , is an american television and movie actress . her most notable acting is the television series `` gilmore girls '' . she has also been in the movies `` sin city '' and `` sisterhood of the traveling pants '' . bledel is a democrat and attended the 2012 democratic national convention in support of president obama 's re-election .
