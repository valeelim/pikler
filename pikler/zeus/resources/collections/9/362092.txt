John Whitehead     sir john stainton whitehead ( born 1932 ) is a british diplomat and businessman . whitehead joined the british foreign service in 1955. he was ambassador from the united kingdom to japan ( 1986 - 1992 ) . after his retirement from the diplomatic service , he was a senior advisor to deutsche bank , tokyo electric power company ( tepco ) and other international companies . he became chairman of deutsche morgan grenfell trust bank ( japan ) . honours . - order of st michael and st george , knight commander ( kcmg ) - order of st michael and st george , knight grand cross ( gcmb ) - royal victorian order , commander ( cvo ) - order of the rising sun , grand cordon , 2006 other websites . - `` ( nearly ) meeting the emperor '' at daiwa foundation
