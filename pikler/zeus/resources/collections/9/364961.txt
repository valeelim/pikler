Jason Elam     jason elam ( born march 8 , 1970 ) is a retired american football placekicker . he was drafted by the denver broncos in the third round of the 1993 nfl draft . he played college football at hawaii . elam also played for the atlanta falcons . he was selected three-times for the pro bowl . with the broncos , elam won two super bowls . he is tied with tom dempsey , sebastian janikowski , and david akers for the longest field goal in nfl history at 63 yards .
