The American President     the american president is a 1995 romance-comedy movie . it was released in november 1995. the story is about political subjects and romance . annette bening and michael douglas play the main characters . martin sheen and michael j. fox also star . this romance-comedy grossed near $ 108 million in the box office , which was only a modest success , though it remained a favorite for cable and home video . the reviews on this were very positive .
