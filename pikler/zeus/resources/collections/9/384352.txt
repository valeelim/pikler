Black Star of Queensland     the black star of queensland is a large gem sapphire . it is the world 's largest star sapphire . it is . it was discovered in queensland in the 1930s . during the 1960s , the black star of queensland was on display at the smithsonian as part of an exhibition with the hope diamond . it was also on display at the royal ontario museum from 2 june to 2 december 2007 .
