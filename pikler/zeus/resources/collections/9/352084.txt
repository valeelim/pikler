List of The Ren and Stimpy Show episodes     this is a list of episodes of the animated series `` the ren & stimpy show '' . it was first on nickelodeon on august 11 , 1991. it went for five seasons for 52 episodes . seven years after `` the ren and stimpy show '' was cancelled by nickelodeon , its creator , john kricfalusi , made a new series about the characters . it was called `` ren & stimpy `` adult party cartoon '' '' . it was for a more adult audience . note : these episodes are listed by their production seasons rather than their broadcast seasons . broadcast season 1 consists of production seasons 1 and 2 , and broadcast season 2 consists of production seasons 3 , 4 , and 5 .
