Halloween: Resurrection    halloween : resurrection halloween : resurrection is a 2002 american horror movie . it is the eighth movie in the `` halloween '' movie series . in the movie , the masked serial killer michael myers continues killing in his hometown of haddonfield . this time it is in his old childhood home , which is being used for a live internet horror show . in this movie , michael myers finally kills his sister laurie strode while she was in an asylum . the movie was released in july 12 , 2002. other websites . - `` halloween : resurrection '' at fearnet
