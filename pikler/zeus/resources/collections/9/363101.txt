Geoffrey Holder     geoffrey richard holder ( august 1 , 1930 â€“ october 5 , 2014 ) was an trinidadian movie , television , stage , radio , voice actor , choreographer , director , dancer , painter , costume designer , and singer . holder was known for his deep soft voice . he was in `` the wiz '' , `` annie '' , `` live and let die '' , `` bear in the big blue house '' , `` doctor dolittle '' . he narrated `` charlie and the chocolate factory '' . holder won 2 tony awards in 1975 and a drama desk award in 1975. holder was born on august 1 , 1930 in port of spain , trinidad , trinidad and tobago . he studied at queens royal college . he was married to carmen de lavallade from 1955 until his death in 2014. holder had a brother , boscoe holder . holder died in manhattan , new york city from pneumonia , aged 84 .
