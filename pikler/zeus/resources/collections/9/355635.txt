I'm a Slave 4 U    i 'm a slave 4 u `` i 'm a slave 4 u '' is a song from britney spears ' third album , `` britney '' . it was the first single off that album . it was released in late 2001. it was the only song from the album to hit the top 40 on the `` billboard '' hot 100. the song created controversy . that was partly because of the words , which could be seen as sexual . it was also partly because of her performance of the song on the mtv video music awards .
