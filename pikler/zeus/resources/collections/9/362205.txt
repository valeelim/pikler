Clark Gregg     robert clark gregg ( born april 2 , 1962 ) is an american director , screenwriter , actor and voice artist . he has acted in both television and movies . gregg is known for his role as phil coulson in `` iron man '' , `` iron man 2 '' , `` thor '' , and `` the avengers '' . gregg was born in boston , massachusetts . gregg has been married to jennifer grey since 2001. they have a daughter , stella gregg .
