Mozambique Channel     the mozambique channel is a part of the indian ocean between the island nation of madagascar and southeast africa , mostly the country of mozambique . it was the place of the world war ii battle of madagascar . the channel is about 460 km across at its narrowest point between angoche , mozambique , and tambohorano , madagascar . the channel reaches a depth of 3,292 m ( 10,800 feet ) about 230 km off the coast of mozambique . islands in the channel . comoros . - grande comore - moh√©li - anjouan france . - mayotte - banc du geyser - glorioso islands - juan de nova island - europa island - bassas da india mozambique . - primeiras and segundas archipelago
