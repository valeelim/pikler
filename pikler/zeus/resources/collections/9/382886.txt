Yin and yang     yin and yang is a chinese symbol for balance and harmony , and the opposite forces of nature . the symbol has two colors , white and black which symbolize nature 's balance of forces such as good and evil , up and down , light and dark , male and female , life and death , etc . basically , this symbol represents the positive and negative forces in the universe . this symbol has been used in asia for a long time , and has become used in the rest of the world . how two opposite things are somehow one . it could also mean the good in the bad and the bad in the good . other websites . - yin yang meaning in chinese educational video .
