Walmor Chagas     walmor chagas ( august 28 , 1930 – january 18 , 2013 ) was a brazilian actor . he was in over 50 movies since his career began in 1965. he is known for his roles in `` são paulo , sociedade anônima '' , `` xica da silva '' , `` sonho meu '' , `` esperança '' , `` caminhos do coração '' , and in `` a favorita '' . chagas was born on august 28 , 1930 in alegrete , rio grande do sul , brazil . chagas committed suicide by shooting himself in the head on january 18 , 2013 in his home in guaratinguetá , são paulo , brazil , aged 81 .
