Summer in the City     `` summer in the city '' is a very popular song about summer . it was by the lovin ' spoonful . many other musicians have covered the song . it was released in 1966 , at which time it hit # 1 across the charts of `` billboard '' hot 100. the instrumental bridge of the song features car honking , starting with a volkswagen beetle horn and then ending with jackhammer sounds . these sounds were included to represent what summer in the city sounds like . the song was featured in several movies .
