Alain Chevrier     alain guy chevrier ( born april 23 , 1961 in cornwall , ontario ) is a canadian retired professional ice hockey goaltender . chevrier played a total of 8 seasons in the nhl . he played for the new jersey devils for 3 seasons , winnipeg jets for 1 season , chicago blackhawks for 2 seasons , pittsburgh penguins for 1 season and the detroit red wings for 1 season . chevrier also play college hockey with the miami university redhawks and was the first redhawk to play in the nhl .
