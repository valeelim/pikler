Rob Corddry     robert william `` rob '' corddry ( born february 4 , 1971 ) is an american movie , television , voice actor , television director and producer , writer and comedian who is known for his roles in `` hot tub time machine '' and in `` childrens hospital '' . he has won an emmy award in 2012 for `` children hospital '' . corddry was born on february 4 , 1971 in weymouth , massachusetts . he studied at weymouth north high school and at university of massachusetts amherst .
