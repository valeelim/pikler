Aries (constellation)    aries ( constellation ) aries is one of the constellations of the zodiac . it is in the northern hemisphere . aries is latin for ram - a male sheep . its symbol is ( ) , which represents a ram 's horns . aries had the meaning of a ram four thousand years ago in babylonia . it represents the ram whose fleece ( hair ) became the golden fleece in ancient greek mythology . the astronomer ptolemy listed aries when he made a list of 48 constellations .
