Jet Set Radio Future     jet set radio future is a video game that was made by smilebit . it is published by sega and is available for xbox . it is the sequel to the 2000 game , `` jet set radio '' . the game is set in a futuristic tokyo which the player as part of a gang trys to gain control of . the games gameplay also requires the player to find other gangs graffiti tags and tag over them when the player collects enough spraycans that are littered around each stage . the game 's tagging system is also different from the last game . some of the tagging locations contain multiple spray targets which depend on the tag 's size . time limits for stages are removed and the stages are interconnected . other websites . - jet set radio future at mobygames
