Strabomantidae     strabomantidae is a family of frogs . they live in south america . these frogs do not have a free-living larval stage . they hatch directly into miniature `` froglets '' . systematics . - holoadeninae - `` barycholos '' - `` bryophryne '' - `` euparkerella '' - `` holoaden '' - `` noblella '' - `` psychrophrynella '' - strabomantinae - `` atopophrynus '' - `` dischidodactylus '' - `` geobatrachus '' - `` hypodactylus '' - `` lynchius '' - `` niceforonia '' - `` oreobates '' - `` phrynopus '' - `` pristimantis '' - `` strabomantis ''
