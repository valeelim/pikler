Anxiolytic     anxiolytics are drugs that are used for the treatment of anxiety , and anxiety-related disorders . the common drugs can be classified : - benzodiazepines - selective serotonin reuptake inhibitors , often abbreviated ssris - azapirones - barbiturates - hydroxyzines - pregabalin in addition to these drug classes , there are also certain plants that have such properties . many of the drugs listed above have side-effects . for this reason , they are only available on prescription . certain forms of therapy have also been used to treat anxiety disorders .
