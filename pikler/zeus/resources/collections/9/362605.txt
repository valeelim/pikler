Bob Lamey     bob lamey ( born december 23 , 1938 ) is an american sportscaster . he is the radio voice of the indianapolis colts . he has been with the franchise since they moved to indianapolis from baltimore except for the 1992-1994 seasons . during that time , he was replaced by joe mcconnell . he was also the voice of the indiana pacers of the nba . he also called ice hockey games for the indianapolis checkers and indianapolis racers as well as the voice of the carolina cougars of the original american basketball association and served as a turn reporter for the indianapolis motor speedway radio network .
