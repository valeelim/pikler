Blind Love (2006 film)    blind love ( 2006 film ) blind love is a 2006 independent low-budget movie . it was directed by janghun troy choi . the movie won the orson welles award at the 2007 tiburon international film festival . it won the best feature film at the 2006 ellensburg international film festival . it also won the best feature film at the 2006 cinewest international film festival . `` blind love '' was also nominated for the best produced screenplay at the 2006 aof international film festival . it was janghun troy choi 's directorial debut in the united states . cast . - paulabianca hattar ... the prostitute - austin ku ... the killer `` blind love '' was written , directed , produced by janghun troy choi , and shot on panasonic dvx 100 with a dof box . other websites . - baycityhhmfest.com
