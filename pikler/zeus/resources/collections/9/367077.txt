Aitchison College     the aitchison college is pakistan 's most prestigious school and college . it is in the old and historical punjabi city of lahore . the school was started during british colonial rule , in 1886. it was first called the 'punjab chiefs ' school and college ' . it is named after sir charles umpherston aitchison , who was lieutenant governor of the punjab . only boys aged 4 to 19 go there .
