Max Wright     george edward maxwell `` max '' wright ( born august 2 , 1943 ) is an retired-american actor , best known for his role as willie tanner on the television comedy series `` alf '' . wright was born on august 2 , 1943 in detroit , michigan . he was married to linda wright since 1965. they have two children , ben and daisy . wright now lives in los angeles , california . in 1995 , he was diagnosed with lymphoma . he is in remission . in january 2000 , wright was arrested for drunken driving in los angeles , california . wright had another dui arrest in 2003 .
