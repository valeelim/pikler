Richard Lovelace     richard lovelace ( 1618-1657 ) was an english writer , poet , soldier and royalist , who is considered by many to be the 'perfect cavalier ' . educated at charterhouse and oxford university , he took part in expeditions by the troops of king charles i against various rebels from 1639 to 1642. in 1642 , returning to his estates , he was nominated to present a royalist petition to a hostile parliament , for which he was ordered to prison by this elected body . during his short term in prison , he wrote one of his most famous poems , 'to althea , from prison ' . he later spent many years fighting in france and spain .
