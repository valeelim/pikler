Henrietta     henrietta is a female given name . it comes from the male given name `` henry '' . henrietta is the english form of the french name `` henriette '' . the short version of this name is `` harriet '' . in some english-speaking areas , henrietta is not used as much as it used to be . `` harriet '' , however , is popular for certain countries . both `` henrietta '' and `` harriet '' were most popular in the 1960s in united states . henrietta itself ranked 446th in popularity in the 1990 census .
