1840 United States Census     the united states census of 1840 was the sixth census of the united states . it was done on june 1 , 1840. it found the population of the united states to be 17,069,453. this was an increase of 32.7 percent from the 1830 census . the total population included 2,487,355 slaves . in 1840 , the center of population was about 260 miles ( 418 km ) west of washington , near weston , west virginia .
