Crin Antonescu     crin antonescu ( b . 21 september 1959 , tulcea ) is a romanian liberal politician . antonescu was the president of pnl but in 2014 quit the presidency and now is only a member of the party . on 3 july 2012 , he was elected president of the romanian senate . in 2014 he resigned and was replaced by cătălin popescu tăriceanu
