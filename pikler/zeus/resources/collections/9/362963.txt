Julie Newmar     julie newmar ( born julia chalene newmeyer , august 16 , 1933 ) is an american movie , television , voice , stage actress , dancer , singer , writer , and model . she is known for her role as catwoman in `` batman '' . she won a tony award . newmar was born on august 16 , 1933 in los angeles , california . she was married to j. holt smith from 1977 until they divorced in 1983. she was partners with louis l'amour until the 1950s . newmar has a son , john jewl newmar . newmar has had charcot–marie–tooth disease since 2008 .
