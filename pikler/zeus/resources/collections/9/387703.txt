Applebee's    applebee 's applebee 's is an american food company that develops , franchises and operates applebee 's neighborhood bar & grill . there are over two thousand restaurants all across the united states , puerto rico and several outside countries . the concept of this company represents casual dining . their food includes salads , shrimp , chicken , pasta , barbecue ribs and fish . the company is centered in kansas city , missouri . it was put into operation on november 19 , 1980 .
