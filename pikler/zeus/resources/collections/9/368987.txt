1980 United States Census     the twentieth united states census was done by the census bureau . it found the population of the united states to be 226,545,805. it was an increase of 11.4 percent from the 1970 census . it was the first census not to ask for the name of the `` head of household . '' about 16 percent of households received a `` long form '' of the 1980 census . it had over 100 questions .
