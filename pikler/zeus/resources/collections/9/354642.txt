John Ratzenberger     john deszo ratzenberger ( born april 6 , 1947 in bridgeport , connecticut ) is an american actor , voice actor , and entrepreneur . he is best known for his role as cliff clavin in `` cheers '' and for his role in all of the `` pixar '' animated movies which includes ; hamm in `` toy story '' , `` toy story 2 '' , and in `` toy story 3 '' , p.t . flea in `` a bug 's life '' , the abominable snowman in `` monsters , inc. '' , the school of moonfish in `` finding nemo '' , the underminer in `` the incredibles '' , mack the truck in `` cars '' , and in `` cars 2 '' , mustafa the waiter in `` ratatouille '' , john in `` wall-e '' , tom the construction worker in `` up '' , gordon the guard in `` brave '' , the abominable snowman in `` monsters university '' , fritz in `` inside out '' , earl in `` the good dinosaur '' and bill the crab in `` finding dory '' . ratzenberger was married to georgia stiny from 1985 until they divorced in 2004. ratzenberger has a son james john ( b . 1987 ) , and a daughter nina kathrine ( b . 1989 ) . ratzenberger is a republican .
