Shiogama jinja     the shrine was established in the ninth century . `` shiogama jinja '' was one of two the chief shinto shrines ( `` ichinomiya '' ) for the mutsu province . when miyagi prefecture and fukushima prefecture were create in the meiji period , the shrine continued to be the `` ichinomiya '' for the same area . other websites . - shiogama jinja homepage - guide to the cultural properties of shiogama city - introduction to shiogama jinja
