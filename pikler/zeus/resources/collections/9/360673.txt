Tsugaru Strait     a warm current ( `` tsushima-kairyū '' ) runs through the straight from south to north . the seikan tunnel passes under the strait at its narrowest point . this is between tappi misaki on the tsugaru peninsula in aomori in honshū and shirakami misaki on the matsumae peninsula in hokkaidō . in the past , the most common way for passengers and freight to cross the strait was on a ferry . this was about a four-hour journey .
