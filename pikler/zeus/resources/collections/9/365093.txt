George P. Shultz     george pratt shultz ( born december 13 , 1920 ) is an american politician , economist , statesman , and businessman . he was the united states secretary of labor from 1969 to 1970 , as the u.s. secretary of the treasury from 1972 to 1974 , and as the u.s. secretary of state from 1982 to 1989. shultz was born on december 13 , 1920 in new york city , new york and studied at princeton university and at massachusetts institute of technology . he has been married to helena o'brien from 1946 until her death in 1995. he is now married charlotte mailliard since 1997. shultz has five children . other websites . - turmoil & triumph : the george shultz years '' - association for the study of the middle east and africa ( asmea )
