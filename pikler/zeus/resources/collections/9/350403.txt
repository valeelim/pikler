Treaty of London (1839)    treaty of london ( 1839 ) the treaty of london of 1839 , also called the first treaty of london , the convention of 1839 , and the london treaty of separation , was a treaty signed on 19 april 1839 between the european great powers , the united kingdom of the netherlands and the kingdom of belgium . this treaty was because the netherlands would not sign the 1831 'treaty of the xxiv articles ' . this treaty said that belgium was its own country . it confirmed the independence of the german speaking part of luxembourg . the most important part of the treaty makes belgium always be neutral . and those who signed the treaty would protect belgium if it was attacked . the treaty is said to be a part of the cause of world war i. when the german empire invaded belgium in august 1914 , the british declared war on 4 august .
