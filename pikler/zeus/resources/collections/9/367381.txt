Qazi Muhammad Isa     qazi muhammad isa ( 17th of july 1914- 19th of june 1976 ) [ 1 ] was a leader of the all india muslim league party in british india and later a senior governmental official in pakistan . qazi isa was born in pishin , baluchistan ( chief commissioners province ) . he joined the league in 1939 and was nominated to represent his province at the league 's lahore session in march 1940. after the creation of pakistan , he served the new country in various positions , and for a time was also its ambassador to brazil ( 1951-54 ) . he died in 1976 . 1. kausar . prof. dr. inamul haq . `` tehreek-e-pakistan balochistan main '' . lahore . 1988. maktaba-e-aaliya . p.21
