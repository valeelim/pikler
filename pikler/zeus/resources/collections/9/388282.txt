Nintendo Power     nintendo power was a monthly nintendo news and video game strategy magazine . it ran from 1988 to 2012. the first issue , released in july/august 1988 , spotlighted the nes game `` super mario bros. 2 '' . from 1988 to 2007 , the magazine was published by nintendo of america . starting in 2007 , it was published by future us , the united states branch of british publisher future . the last issue was in december 2012 , spotlighting the wii u game `` new super mario bros. u '' . `` nintendo power '' focused on news , previews , and articles on upcoming nintendo video games . other sections included `` pulse '' ( letters submitted by readers ) and `` player profiles '' ( information and an interview with a person in the video game industry ) .
