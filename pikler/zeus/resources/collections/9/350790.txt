Sharon Horgan     sharon horgan is an irish actress , writer television producer and comedienne . she was born in london and moved to dublin when she was seven . her brother shane horgan ( born 1978 ) is a retired rugby player . she wrote , produced and starred in `` pulling '' . her other acting roles include : `` rob brydon 's annually retentive '' , `` angelo 's '' , `` free agents '' , `` the increasingly poor decisions of todd margaret '' , `` dead boss '' and `` catastrophe '' .
