Menorah (Hanukkah)    menorah ( hanukkah ) the hanukkah menorah , or hanukiah , is a nine-branch lamp used to celebrate , remember , and honor the miracle of hanukkah . it consists of a center branch with four branches on each of its sides . eight of its branches are to be lit on each day of hanukkah , which lasts eight days , while the ninth branch is the shamash , or `` helper '' branch , which is used to light the other branches .
