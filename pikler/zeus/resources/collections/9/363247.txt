Teddington     teddington is a suburban area in the london borough of richmond upon thames . it is in south west london , on the north bank of the river thames , between hampton wick and twickenham . it stretches inland from the river thames to bushy park . formerly it was in the county of middlesex , which was in its postal county until they were abolished in 1996. it is in richmond to the greater london council .
