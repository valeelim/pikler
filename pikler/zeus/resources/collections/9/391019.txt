Joel Osteen     joel scott osteen ( born march 5 , 1963 ) is an american author , televangelist , and the senior pastor of lakewood church in houston , texas . his ministry reaches over seven million broadcast media viewers weekly in over 100 nations around the world . his first book , “ your best life now ” has sold 5 million copies . lakewood church now occupies the former compaq center , once home to the nba ’ s houston rockets . the venue seats 16,000 people . osteen was born on march 5 , 1963 in houston , texas . he has been married to victoria osteen since 1987. they have two children . he lives in houston , texas . other websites . - official joel osteen ministries
