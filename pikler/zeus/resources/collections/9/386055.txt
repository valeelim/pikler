Mohammad Ashraful     mohammad ashraful ( ) ( born 7 july 1984 ) is a bangladeshi international cricket player . he was the captain of national side during 2008-2010. ashraful captained his country in 13 tests . but lost in 12 and one ended draw . he also captained 38 one day internationals ( odis ) of which bangladesh won eight . ashraful scored the fastest fifty in the test among any batsman of the world , also scored the fastest fifty in the odi and twenty20 international comparing any bangladeshi batsman . he is the youngest test centurion .
