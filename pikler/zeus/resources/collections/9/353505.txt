Mariah Carey (album)    mariah carey ( album ) mariah carey is the debut studio album by american singer-songwriter mariah carey . it was released on june 12 , 1990 on colombia records . it reached number one on the `` billboard '' 200 chart . the album has sold over 15 million copies worldwide . her four singles `` vision of love '' , `` love takes time '' , `` someday '' and `` i do n't wan na cry '' all reached number one in the us .
