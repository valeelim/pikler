2 Broke Girls     2 broke girls was an american television sitcom on cbs . it began on september 19 , 2011 and ended on april 17 , 2017 and is set in williamsburg , brooklyn , new york city . it is about two waitresses in their twenties who work together at a diner . kat dennings plays max black , who is from a poor , underclass background . beth behrs plays caroline wesbox channing , who until recently was rich . garrett morris plays earl , the cashier at the diner . he is an elderly african-american former jazz musician . jonathan kite plays oleg , the diner 's cook , a hypersexual ukrainian who lacks social skills . matthew moy plays han lee , the diner 's owner , a very short korean . jennifer coolidge plays sophie , max and caroline 's histrionic polish neighbor . ed quinn plays randy , max 's boyfriend . the show is rated m in new zealand for offensive language , sexual references and drug use . other websites . - official website
