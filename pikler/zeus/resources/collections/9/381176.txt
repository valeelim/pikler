Broth     broth is sometimes defined as the liquid part of soup . it can be made by boiling meat in water and taking out the fat . it is sometimes used as an ingredient in recipes to make food taste better . in britain , a broth is a soup in which there are solid pieces of meat or fish , with some vegetables . it is usually made with a stock or plain water as its base , with meat or fish added while being brought to a boil , and vegetables added later . broth is often made more substantial by adding rice , barley or pulses . broth is distinct from stock , which is a thin liquid made by simmering raw ingredients until all the taste has been retrieved from them , then sieving the resulting liquid . in the united states terms , broths are made from portions of meat , whereas stocks are made from vegetable scraps and bones .
