Bob Hoskins     robert william hoskins ( 26 october 1942 - 29 april 2014 ) was a british actor . he was born in bury st edmunds , suffolk and raised in finsbury park , london . hoskins ' movies include : `` the long good friday '' , `` mona lisa '' and `` who framed roger rabbit '' . he won a bafta award and a golden globe award for his role as george in mona lisa . on 8 august 2012 , hoskins said he had retired , having being diagnosed with parkinson 's disease in late 2011. he died of pneumonia on the morning of 29 april 2014 in london .
