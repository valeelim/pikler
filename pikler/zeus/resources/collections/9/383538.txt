Hōjō Masako     hōjō masako ( 1156 – august 16 , 1225 ) was the eldest child of hōjō tokimasa by his wife hōjō no maki , the first shikken , or ruler , of the kamakura shogunate . she was the sister of hōjō yoshitoki , and was married to minamoto no yoritomo , the first shogun of the kamakura period . she was also the mother of minamoto no yoriie and minamoto no sanetomo , the second and third shoguns .
