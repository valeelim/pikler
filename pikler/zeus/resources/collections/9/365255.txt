Salisbury, Connecticut    salisbury , connecticut salisbury is a town in litchfield county , connecticut , united states . the town is the most northwest in the state of connecticut . the ma-ny-ct ( massachusetts-new york-connecticut ) tri-state marker is just on the border of salisbury . the population was 3,977 at the 2000 census . scoville library in salisbury was the first in the united states to be open to the public free of charge . although the peak of mount frissell is in massachusetts , the south slope of the mountain is in salisbury . it is the highest point in connecticut . in salisbury there are several ponds and six lakes : wononscopomuc , washinee , washining , wononpakook , riga lake and south pond . the appalachian trail runs through salisbury .
