Roddy Doyle     roddy doyle ( born 8 may 1958 ) is an irish novelist , dramatist and screenwriter . he was born in dalkey and grew up in killbarack . he writes about working class dublin . some of his books have been made into movies , the best-known of which is `` the commitments '' , which is the first of the barrytown trilogy . he , dick clement and ian la frenais adapted the book into a movie , `` the commitments '' ; the three men won the bafta award for best adapted screenplay . doyle is an atheist .
