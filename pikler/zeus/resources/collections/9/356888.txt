Jack Klugman     jacob joachim `` jack '' klugman ( april 27 , 1922 - december 24 , 2012 ) was an american movie , stage and television actor . klugman played oscar madison in the television series `` the odd couple '' from 1970 to 1975. he played the title character in the television series `` quincy , m.e . '' from 1976 to 1983. klugman was born in philadelphia to russian jewish immigrants . he went to carnegie mellon university . he started acting after discharge from the united states army in 1945. klugman was married to brett somers from 1953 until her death in 2007. they legally separated in 1974 , but never divorced . somers and klugman had 2 children . klugman started living with peggy crosby in 1988. they married in february 2008. he was the last surviving actor from the cast of jurors in the original 1957 movie `` 12 angry men '' . klugman was a heavy smoker . he was diagnosed with throat cancer in 1974. in 1989 , he lost a vocal cord to cancer . he was left with a quiet , raspy voice . he continued to act on stage and television .
