The Emancipation of Mimi     the emancipation of mimi is the tenth album by american singer mariah carey . it was released in the united states on april 12 , 2005. it went straight to no.1 and was the best-selling album of the year in the us . it was carey 's best-selling album since `` daydream '' ( 1995 ) . sixteenth months after the album was released carey announced a tour for the album , `` the adventures of mimi : the voice , the hits , the tour '' . the album was nominated for ten grammy awards . she won won `` best contemporary r & b album '' for `` the emancipation of mimi '' , as well as `` best female r & b vocal performance '' and `` best r & b song '' for `` we belong together '' . other websites . - `` the emancipation of mimi '' at metacritic
