Eat Bulaga!    eat bulaga ! eat bulaga ! ( commonly known as eb ! ) is a long-running noon-time variety show from the philippines . it airs weekdays at 12:00 pm to 2:30 pm and saturdays at 11:30 am to 2:30 pm . the show premiered on july 30 , 1979 and it 's currently celebrating its 33rd anniversary in philippine television . `` eat bulaga ! '' became the first philippine variety show to be franchised by another country when indonesia 's sctv network adapted a version of the show entitled as `` eat bulaga ! ''
