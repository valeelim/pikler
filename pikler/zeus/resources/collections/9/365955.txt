The Great Mouse Detective     the great mouse detective is a 1986 american animated movie . it was produced by walt disney feature animation . it was first released to movie theaters in the united states on july 2 , 1986 by walt disney pictures . it is the 26th animated movie in the walt disney animated classics series . the main characters are all mice and rats living in victorian london . the movie is based on the children 's book series `` basil of baker street '' by eve titus . it is also similar to the stories about sherlock holmes .
