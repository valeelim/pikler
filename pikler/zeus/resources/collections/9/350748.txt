Aspergillus     aspergillus is a large genus of fungi in the phylum ascomycota . there are several hundred species or types , some of which are important to medicine , science and industry . it grows as a mould , often on plants . the fungi make asexual spores in a structure called an `` aspergillum '' . about one third of types also have sexual reproduction . `` aspergillus '' species are highly aerobic and are found in almost all oxygen-rich environments . they are common contaminants of starchy foods ( such as bread and potatoes ) , and grow in or on many plants and trees . species of `` aspergillus '' are important medically and commercially . some species can cause infection in humans and other animals . more than 60 `` aspergillus '' species are medically relevant pathogens . for humans there is a range of diseases such as infection to the external ear , skin lesions , and ulcers classed as mycetomas . related pages . - koji - `` aspergillus flavus ''
