Julie     the name julie is a french female given name . it comes from the latin name `` julia '' . `` julia '' means `` youthful '' or `` soft haired '' in latin . the name julie and its variants are feminine for the masculine name `` julius '' . julie is very popular in the united states . the name was particularly popular between 1951 and 1991. its variant , `` julia '' , has been in the top 100 female names dating back to 1980. julie and its related names are being used by many famous people , such as julie andrews . as `` julia '' the name is currently being used for atlantic basin tropical cyclones .
