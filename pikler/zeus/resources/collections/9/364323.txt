Anansa Sims     anansa sims patterson ( born in december 27 , 1978 in new york ) is an american plus-size model . she is the daughter of model beverly johnson and record producer danny sims . sims has been married to david patterson since july 10 , 2010. the couple have a daughter ava patterson ( b. august 2011 ) . their second child , david bernard patterson iii , was born november 6 , 2012. their third child , dean danny benjamin patterson , was born june 20 , 2014 .
