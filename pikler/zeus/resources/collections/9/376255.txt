Blainville-Boisbriand Armada     the blainville-boisbriand armada are a canadian ice hockey team . they began playing in 2011. the team currently plays in the quebec major junior hockey league . they play their home games in boisbriand , quebec at the centre d'excellence sports rousseau . the team originally played in the verdun borough of montreal as the montreal junior hockey club but was moved to boisbriand , quebec by former nhl defenseman joel bouchard in 2011. other websites . - official website
