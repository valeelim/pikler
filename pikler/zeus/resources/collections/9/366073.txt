Nicolas Roeg     nicolas jack roeg ( born 25 august 1929 ) is an english movie director and cinematographer who is known for his works in `` do n't look now '' , `` the man who fell to earth '' , `` walkabout '' , `` bad timing '' , and in `` performance '' . roeg was born on 25 august 1929 in london , england . roeg was married to susan stephen from 1957 until they divorced in 1977. he was married to actress theresa russell from 1982 until they divorced . he has been married to harriet harper since 2004 .
