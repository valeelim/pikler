Leigh-Allyn Baker     leigh-allyn baker-kauffman ( born april 3 , 1972 in murray , kentucky , usa ) is an american movie , television and voice actress and comedian . most famous for her role of amy blankenhooper-duncan in dinsye 's comedy tv series `` good luck charlie '' , where she has been playing since 2010. leigh-allyn has been married to an entertainment executive keith kauffman ( b.1974 ) since 2005. they have two sons together - griffin kauffman ( b. in early of 2009 ) and baker james kauffman ( b.september 18 , 2012 ) .
