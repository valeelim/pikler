Vic Sotto     marvic castelo sotto , commonly known as vic sotto and bossing , is a filipino actor , tv host , comedian , and film producer . he is known for working for `` eat bulaga ! '' . he was born on april 28 , 1954 , to marcelino ojeda sotto and dr. herminia castelo . he went to at colegio de san juan de letran . he is the brother of marcelino antonio `` maru '' sotto , jr. , vicente `` tito '' sotto , and valmar `` val '' sotto . in 1982 , sotto , together with co-hosts in `` eat bulaga ! `` , richie d horsie and joey de leon , were accused of rape by pepsi paloma , a 16-year old actress . the case was not filed in court after the three apologized publicly on national tv . sotto has done sitcoms and television shows on the three major networks in the philippines , abs-cbn , gma network and tv5 . he has mostly done shows for gma network .
