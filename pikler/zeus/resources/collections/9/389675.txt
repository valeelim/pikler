Before Sunrise     before sunrise is a 1995 romantic drama movie . the movie stars ethan hawke and julie delpy . in the movie , a man named jesse ( hawke ) and a woman named celine ( delpy ) meet on a train and get off the train in vienna . a romantic encounter begins from there . `` before sunrise '' was released january 27 , 1995. it was very well liked by critics . it was followed by sequels `` before sunset '' and `` before midnight '' .
