Gruiformes     the gruiformes are an order with many living and extinct bird families , and a widespread geographical diversity . gruiform means `` crane-like '' . the gruiformes evolved early , long before the passerine songbirds which are the most common birds today . the gruiform radiation in the palaeocene and eocene produced a large variety of medium to large-sized birds . many of them still prefer the watery habitats which were so common early in the cainozoic era . cranes and rails are the core of the gruiformes . also often added are a large number of difficult-to-place birds like the huge `` diatryma '' and `` phorusrhacos '' .
