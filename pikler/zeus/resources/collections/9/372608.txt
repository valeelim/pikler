Sayyid     syed or sayyid or sayed is a title of honour and respect . it is originally a word from the arabic language which means 'sir ' or 'mister ' or 'honourable person ' . however , the word is now also used in different parts of the world by muslims to refer specially to a descendant of the prophet muhammad . for example , if someone is called a 'syed/sayyid ' in india , pakistan , bangladesh , iran , afghanistan , indonesia , malaysia etc. , then that person is descended from the prophet of islam .
