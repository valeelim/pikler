James Herbert Brennan     james herbert brennan ( 5 july 1940 ) is an irish writer of fantasy literature and occultism . his most known work is the book titled `` occult reich '' , published in 1974. the book explains various aspects of the ariosophy . brennan is also the author of the quest game `` the search for the grail '' , published in the 1980s . he sold millions of copies worldwide of this quest game , mainly in france . works . non-fiction . - `` astral doorways '' ( 1971 ) - `` five keys to past lives '' ( 1972 ) - `` experimental magic '' ( 1972 ) - `` occult reich '' ( 1974 ) - `` an occult history of the world '' ( 1976 ) - `` getting what you want '' ( 1977 ) - `` good con guide '' ( 1978 ) - `` reincarnation '' ( 1981 ) - `` a guide to megalithic ireland '' ( 1982 ) - `` discover your past lives : a practical course ( 1984 ) - `` the reincarnation workbook : a complete course in recalling past lives '' ( 1989 ) - `` the astral projection workbook : how to achieve out-of-body experiences '' ( 1989 ) - `` mindpower : succeed at school '' ( 1990 ) - `` mindpower : secrets to improve your image '' ( 1990 )
