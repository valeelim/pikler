Zulfikar Ghose     zulfikar ghose ( born march 1935 ) is a writer and novelist of pakistani origin , settled and teaching in texas , usa . among his better known works are `` the murder of aziz khan '' ( 1967 ) , `` the incredible brazilian '' ( trilogy , 1972-78 ) , `` don bueno '' ( 1983 ) and `` the triple mirror of the self '' ( 1992 ) .
