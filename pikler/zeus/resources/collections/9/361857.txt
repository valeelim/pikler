Joe Mantegna     joseph anthony `` joe '' mantegna , jr. ( born november 13 , 1947 ) is an american movie , stage , television , voice actor , producer , writer , and director . he did the voice of fat tony in `` the simpsons '' . he also played david rossi in `` criminal minds '' . he played grem in the disney-pixar animated movie `` cars 2 '' . he has won a tony award . mantegna was born in chicago , illinois . he has been married to arlene vrhel since 1975 and they have two children .
