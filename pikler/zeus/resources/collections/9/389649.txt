Bookmaker     a bookmaker , bookie or turf accountant is person or company that collect bets for sporting events . bets are placed and winnings are paid when the chosen team wins or event happens . the most popular bet will be for winning team . if chosen team wins - person betting will win their money back with some earnings . bookmakers are very popular in uk and ireland . the person betting can chose from many variations . most bets are made in sports like football , horse racing and rugby . some bets can be placed for events too , like oscar or presidential winners . the first bookmaker was harry ogden opened in 1790s .
