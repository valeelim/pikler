Wilhelm Wulff     wilhelm theodor h. wulff ( 27 march 1892 - 9 june 1979 ) was a german-austrian astrologer and writer . he is best known for the 1973 book `` zodiac and swastika '' . the book tells about his time as an astrologer in nazi germany near the end of world war ii . he was heinrich himmler 's astrologer .
