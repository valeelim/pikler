Sandy Dennis     sandra dale `` sandy '' dennis ( april 27 , 1937-march 2 , 1992 ) was an american actress . she received two tony awards for acting . she performed mostly in theater and movies . her most notable movies included `` sweet november '' ( 1968 ) , `` the out-of-towners '' ( 1970 ) and `` the indian runner '' . dennis was born in nebraska . she died of ovarian cancer in westport , connecticut .
