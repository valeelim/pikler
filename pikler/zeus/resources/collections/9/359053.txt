Greg Kinnear     gregory `` greg '' kinnear ( born june 17 , 1963 ) is an american movie , voice , and television actor . he first became famous in 1991. he has appeared in more than 20 movies , and was nominated for an academy award for his role in `` as good as it gets '' . his career began in 1988. he has appeared in other notable films including `` sabrina '' , `` you 've got mail '' , `` nurse betty '' , `` we were soldiers '' , `` little miss sunshine '' , `` invincible '' , and `` green zone '' . notable tv roles include `` friends '' and his emmy award nominated roles for `` soup '' , `` the kennedys '' , and `` modern family '' . kinnear was born in june 17 , 1963 in logansport , indiana to suzanne and edward kinnear . kinnear married helen labdon since 1999 .
