Old Christian Cemetery Abbottabad     the old christian cemetery , abbottabad ( also known as the occ , or the `` gora qabristan '' , white-people 's cemetery ) , is a cemetery situated in abbottabad , pakistan . it dates from british indian colonial times . the cemetery was established in 1853 when james abbott founded abbottabad . it was later attached to st luke 's church , abbottabad when it was completed in 1864 .
