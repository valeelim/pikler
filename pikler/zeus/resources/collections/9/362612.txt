Puffball     a puffball is the common name for a member of several groups of fungi in the division basidiomycota . puffballs include the genera of `` calvatia '' , `` calbovista '' and `` lycoperdon '' . all false puffballs are inedible , as they are tough and bitter to taste . the genus `` scleroderma '' , which has a young purple gleba , should be avoided .
