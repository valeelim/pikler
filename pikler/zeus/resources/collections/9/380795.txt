Winged Victory of Samothrace     the winged victory of samothrace ( or nike of samothrace ) is a parian marble sculpture now in the louvre , paris , france . it is believed to have been made about 190 bc . it was discovered in 1863. it commemorates the greek victory in a sea battle near samothrace . it stands 5.57 m high . the work presents a pose where violent motion and sudden stillness meet . it is notable for its graceful balance and for the rendering of the figure 's draped garments , depicted as if rippling in a strong sea breeze .
