Faxing Berlin     faxing berlin is a progressive house song by canadian electronic music producer deadmau5 . the song is featured on deadmau5 's third studio album `` random album title '' . the song received some controversy in 2008 by an artist named dirtycircuit . dirtycircuit said that he was threatened with legal action after he used a sample called `` lp_faxing berlin c_128bpm '' that came bundled with the digital audio workstation fl studio which deadmau5 claimed copyright . the sample was a direct clip of a full bar from the song . a `` demo track '' was provided by deadmau5 which came bundled with fl studio , along with many loop samples . fl studio users became were in discomfort because of the case and some of the users had pointed out possible inconsistencies in the eula of the software . the song reached # 6 on the billboard us hot dance club songs .
