Indelibly Stamped     indelibly stamped is the second studio album of supertramp . a & m records released it in june 1971 . `` indelibly stamped '' was supertramp 's first album that was released in the united states . supertramp recorded this album in april and may 1971 in london . credits . - kevin currie - percussion , drums - rick davies - harmonica , keyboards , vocals - frank farrell - bass guitar , piano , accordion , background vocals - roger hodgson - acoustic guitar , bass guitar , vocals - dave winthrop - flute , saxophone , vocals
