Johannes Gerhardus Strijdom     johannes gerhardus strijdom ( also spelled strydom ) also known as j.g . strydom or hans strydom ( july 14 1893-august 24 1958 ) nicknamed `` the lion of the north '' , was prime minister of south africa from 30 november 1954 to 24 august 1958. he was an afrikaner nationalist , and a supporter of apartheid .
