Kanazawa, Ishikawa    kanazawa , ishikawa it has been recognized as a core city since 1996. during the edo period , kanazawa was the center of the maeda clan . kanazawa has a big japanese garden called kenroku-en . kenroku-en was made as a garden of the kanazawa castle at the edo period . it is regarded as one of the most famous gardens in japan . kanazawa is on the western coast of honshu . it faces the sea of japan . other websites . - kanazawa city homepage
