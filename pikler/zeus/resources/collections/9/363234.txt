Phir Bhi Dil Hai Hindustani     phir bhi dil hai hindustani is a 2000 bollywood movie directed by aziz mirza . the movie stars shahrukh khan and juhi chawla in main roles . the movie 's music were done by jatin lalit . the movie is about rivalry between two reporters , ajay bakhshi ( shahrukh khan ) and rai banerjee ( juhi chawla ) . later , they reunite to fight against corruption in the country . cast . - shahrukh khan as ajay bakhshi - juhi chawla as ria banerjee - paresh rawal as mohan joshi - johnny lever as choti/pappu junior - chariems josh as friend of ajay `` shahid '' - sanjay mishra as bomb defuser - sharat saxena
