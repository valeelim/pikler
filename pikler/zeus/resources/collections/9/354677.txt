Nouria Mérah-Benida     nouria mérah-benida ( born october 19 , 1970 in algiers ) is an algerian middle distance runner . at the 1999 all-africa games in johannesburg mérah-benida won silver medals in both 800 metres and 1500 metres . at the 2000 summer olympics in sydney she won the gold medal . in 2000 she also won an 800 m silver medal and a 1500 m gold medal at the african championships .
