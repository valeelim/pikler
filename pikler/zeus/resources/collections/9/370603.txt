Born to Run (song)    born to run ( song ) `` born to run '' is a rock music song by bruce springsteen . the song is on springsteen 's 1975 album , `` born to run '' . the song was recorded on august 1974. it became one of the biggest hits of his career . rolling stone ranked the song 21st on their list of `` the 500 greatest songs of all time '' . other websites . - lyrics
