Clarinet Concerto (Mozart)    clarinet concerto ( mozart ) the clarinet concerto in a major , k. 622 is a work for clarinet and orchestra . it was written by wolfgang amadeus mozart in 1791 for the clarinet expert anton stadler . stadler first performed the concerto in prague on 16 october 1791. mozart probably never heard this performance . references . - woodstra , chris , et al . 2005 . `` all music guide to classical music '' . all media guide llc . p. 895. isbn 0-87930-865-6 .
