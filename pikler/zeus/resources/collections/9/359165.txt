Crash Twinsanity     crash twinsanity is a video game . it was published by vivendi universal games . it was developed by traveller 's tales for the playstation 2 and the xbox . it was released in north america on october 1 , 2004 , in europe on october 8 , 2004 and in japan on november 9 , 2004. a nintendo gamecube version was planned but it was later canceled .
