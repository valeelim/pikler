Crime of the Century     crime of the century is the third studio album of supertramp . a & m records released it in september 1974. ken scott produced the music . there is a text saying `` to sam '' on the back cover of the album . supertramp dedicated this album to stanley august miesegaes . he supported supertramp financially from 1969 until 1972 . `` crime of the century '' got to number 38 on the american billboard pop albums chart , and to number 4 on the uk albums chart . the songs `` bloody well right '' and `` dreamer '' were also released as singles . this album and these singles made supertramp successful and well-known . credits . - bob c. benbeg - drums , percussion - rick davies - vocals , keyboards , harmonica - john anthony helliwell - saxophones , clarinets , vocals - roger hodgson - vocals , guitar , pianos - dougie thomson - bass guitar
