Terri Schiavo case     the terri schiavo case was a legal battle . it involved prolonged life support in united states . the case began in 1998 and lasted to 2005. teresa marie `` terri '' schiavo was in persistent vegetative state and there was a controversy about stopping life support . she had collapsed in her st. petersburg , florida home because of cardiac arrest on february 25 , 1990. this had caused a large amount of damage to her brain . this damage caused her to be in a persistent vegetative state . her feeding tube was disconnected on march 18 , 2005. she died thirteen days later .
