Brian Pimental     brian pimental ( born 1961 , boston , massachusetts ) is an american veteran animator , storyboard artist , movie director and screenwriter . he 's best known for directing the 2006 classic `` bambi ii '' . brian started his career at walt disney animation studios as a trainee in `` oliver & company '' . he became a story artist in `` beauty and the beast '' and `` aladdin '' . then , he became one of the screenwriters for `` a goofy movie '' . he got his first asifa award nomination . his second nomination was for `` tarzan '' . pimental first time directing was on `` bambi ii '' . he won the asifa award for best non-theatrical movie . in the movie , he also made the voices for the groundhog and porcupine . `` bambi ii '' received mixed to positive reviews . it has a rating of 60 % in rotten tomatoes and an overall rating of 6.5 out of 10 in imdb . brian was born in massachusetts . he earned a bachelor of fine arts degree at the california institute of the arts .
