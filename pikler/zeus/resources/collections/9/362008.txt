Craig Bolerjack     craig bolerjack ( born may 16 , 1958 ) is a sportscaster . he has called college football and college basketball games for fox sports net , fx , fox , cbs , espn , and cbs college sports . he called arena football games for espn as well as miami dolphins preseason games . he called regional nfl games for cbs until 2011. he is also the tv voice of the utah jazz . he filled in for dick stockton on the nfl on fox on week four of the 2011 season between the atlanta falcons and the seattle seahawks . bolerjack was born in willows springs , missouri . he lives in salt lake city .
