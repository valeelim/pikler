Whipping boy     a whipping boy was a young boy who grew up with a young prince . if the prince did something wrong , the whipping boy was punished instead of the prince . because both grew up together , punishing the whipping boy had strong psychological effects to the prince . in england this was very common . for instance the king of england charles i had many whipping boys for his son . one of most famous whipping boy was atte kouva from helsinki , finland .
