Frankie Howerd     francis alick howard , known as frankie howerd obe ( 6 march 1917 - 19 april 1992 ) was a british comedian and comedy actor . he was born in york , north riding of yorkshire . he starred in several movies , including `` carry on doctor '' and `` carry on up the jungle '' . he starred in television series including `` up pompeii ! '' . he was a promiscuous homosexual and lived with his manager-partner dennis heymer ( 1928-2009 ) from 1955 until his death . howerd died of heart failure in fulham , london .
