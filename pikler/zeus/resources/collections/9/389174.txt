State Administration of Foreign Exchange     the state administration of foreign exchange of the people 's republic of china is agency that controls foreign exchange market activities , and manages chinese foreign exchange reserves valued at $ 3.2 trillion . the current director is yi gang . safe 's was kept in secret from world and its actions were often anonymous . in last decade the agency became so big it it had to come out from hiding and open up for media . safe is making a lot of investing on forex and commodity market . the head office is located in bejing and it is closely connected to ruling party .
