Charles des Moulins     charles robert alexandre des moulins ( 1798-1875 ) was a french botanist who also studied molluscs . in 1830 , he proposed to introduce plants into aquaria so that the animals in the aquarium could breathe better . he described many species of snails , for example pagodulina pagodula , and plants , for example euphorbia milii . towards the end of his life he became opposed to darwinism .
