Medical assistant     a medical assistant ( sometimes called an ma ) is a medical professional who works in medical settings along with doctors and nurses . they do things to help doctors and nurses with their jobs , and may perform many different tasks . some medical assistants are similar in some ways to a nurse . they take care of patients in hospitals and other medical facilities , and they perform other tasks to help run the office . they are different from a nurse mainly in that medical assistants can not plan out what a patient 's care will be like . they can only do what a nurse or a doctor instruct them to do . other types of medical assistants work in the reception area of a doctor 's office , similar to a secretary . these medical assistants may file paperwork , update medical records , and greet patients . medical assistants usually go to school for between one and two years . they may hold an associate 's degree , or in some cases a certificate if they already have an associate 's degree in something else .
