Kathryn Bigelow     kathryn ann bigelow ( born november 27 , 1951 ) is an american movie director , movie producer , screenwriter and television director . she won an academy award , a bafta award , and a saturn award for her movie `` the hurt locker '' . she is known for her movies such as `` point break '' , `` near dark '' , `` strange days '' , and `` zero dark thirty '' . bigelow was born on november 27 , 1951 in san carlos , california . she studied at the san francisco art institute and at columbia university . she was married to james cameron from 1989 until they divorced in 1991 .
