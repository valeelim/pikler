Suzy Amis     susan elizabeth `` suzy '' amis ( born january 5 , 1962 ) is a former american movie , television actress , and model who is known for her role as lizzy calvert in `` titanic '' . and for being the wife of academy award winning director james cameron since 2000 ; they have three children . she was born in oklahoma city , oklahoma . she retired from acting in 1999 .
