Colton     colton is a village and civil parish in south lakeland , cumbria , england . in 2001 there were 765 people living in colton . in 1202 it was called coleton , which was the old english name for `` village '' . it is built beside a small stream called colton beck . the church of holy trinity was built in 1578 and listed by english heritage as a grade 2 building .
