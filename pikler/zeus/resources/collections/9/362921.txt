Brad Nessler     brad nessler ( born june 3 , 1956 ) is an american sportscaster . he works for abc , espn , and nfl network calling `` thursday night football '' games with mike mayock and alex flanagan . he currently calls college football and basketball games for abc and espn . he started calling georgia tech yellow jackets basketball and atlanta falcons games . nessler was also the voice of the minnesota vikings . nessler also called college football , college basketball for jefferson-pilot and miami dolphins preseason . nessler also called nfl , college football , men 's and women 's college basketball for cbs . during nessler 's time at espn , he also called nba games for abc and espn before being removed from the broadcast team . he has been the # 2 play-by-play announcer for espn `` monday night football '' from 2006 and since 2010. he called sebastian janikowski 's 63 yard field goal for the oakland raiders against the denver broncos at halftime in 2011 .
