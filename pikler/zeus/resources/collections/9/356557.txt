Multi-touch     in computing , multi-touch is the name for a piece of technology that lets you use a trackpad or touchscreen by pressing on more than one place on the screen at one time . this often lets you do things that you could not do by other ways , such as pinch to zoom . one of the first products to have multi-touch was the iphone . its ios operating system was meant to be used using gestures such as tapping , pinching , spreading , rotating , and more . at the time , apple , the company who made the iphone , tried to register the word `` multi-touch '' as a trademark , but they could not do so as the term became used by everyone else . today , multi-touch can be found on many electronic devices with touchscreens , such as smartphones , tablet computers , and even some pcs .
