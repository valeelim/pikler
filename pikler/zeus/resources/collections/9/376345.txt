1996 All-Ireland Senior Camogie Championship Final     the 1996 all-ireland senior camogie championship final was the 65th all-ireland final and the deciding match of the 1996 all-ireland senior camogie championship . it is an inter-county camogie tournament for the top teams in ireland . cork led 1-9 to 1-6 at half-time . after that they went on to regret missed chances . denise gilligan scored two goals for galway .
