PlayStation Move     playstation move is a motion-sensing game controller platform by sony computer entertainment which supports the playstation 3 and the playstation 4. the device uses a playstation eye camera to record movements . it was released on september 15 , 2010 in europe and on september 17 , 2010 in north america . sony announced that the `` playstation move '' had sold 15 million units . in 2016 playstation made a return to the move with the release of the playstation vr and the move can be used to interact with the objects in the virtual world .
