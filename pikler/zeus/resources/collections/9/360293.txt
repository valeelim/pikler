Screen Two     screen two is a british television anthology drama series . it was produced by the bbc and transmitted on bbc2 between 1985 to 1994. it was developed by producer kenith trodd . it was popular enough with viewers for a bbc1 equivalent , `` screen one '' , which was launched in 1989. however , the mid-1990s saw a change for bbc drama . they went away from single dramas toward drama series and serials . `` screen one '' ended in 1993 and `` screen two '' in 1994. movies from both strands are occasionally repeated on bbc four .
