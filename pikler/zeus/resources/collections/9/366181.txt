Anomalocaris     anomalocaris ( `` abnormal shrimp '' ) is an extinct genus of anomalocaridid , which are , in turn , thought to be closely related to the arthropods . the first fossils of `` anomalocaris '' were discovered in the `` ogygopsis '' shale by joseph frederick whiteaves , with more examples found by charles doolittle walcott in the famed burgess shale .
