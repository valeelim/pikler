Tergum     a tergum ( plural terga ) is the dorsal portion of an arthropod segment other than the head . a given tergum may be divided into hardened plates or sclerites commonly referred to as tergites . references . - ( 1983 ) . `` aquatic entomology : the fishermen 's and ecologists ' illustrated guide to insects and their relatives '' . jones & bartlett publishers , isbn 0867200170 , isbn 978-0-86720-017-1. accessed here through google books on 08 jan 2010 .
