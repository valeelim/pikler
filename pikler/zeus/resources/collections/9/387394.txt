Shabbat     shabbat is the name of the day of rest in judaism . shabbat happens on the seventh day ( saturday ) of every week . in judaism , the day is defined with the cycle of the sun : the day begins and ends at sunset , not midnight . so the seventh day of the week , shabbat , begins friday when the sun goes down , and ends saturday night after it gets dark . the idea of shabbat comes from the bible 's story of creation . in that story , god creates the universe and everything on earth for six days . on the seventh day , he stops work . in the same way , jews work on the first six days of the week and rest on the seventh day , shabbat . the word `` shabbat '' began as a hebrew word ( ) . the english word `` sabbath '' comes from the word `` shabbat '' . the english word can also be used to refer to shabbat . the christian idea of sabbath came from the jewish idea of shabbat . now , there are many differences between them . the first part of the jewish bible , the torah , says that work is not allowed on shabbat . it is a day to rest and study the torah .
