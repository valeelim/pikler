Lismore, New South Wales    lismore , new south wales lismore is a town in northeastern new south wales , australia and is in the aboriginal bundjalung nation . it is on the bruxner highway , and the state capital city of new south wales , sydney is to the south by highway . the state capital city of queensland is north . southern cross university is based in lismore , so there are a lot of students from around the state and country . there are six high schools and three primary schools as well a school which has students from kindergarten all the way through to year 12 .
