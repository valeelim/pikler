Lintong District     lingtong district ( , pinyin : líntóng qū ) is a district of xi'an , shaanxi , china . it is in east of xi'an , and about from the center of xi'an . weinan municipality is to the east . gaoling county , baqiao district and xianyang municipality are to the west . lantian county is to the south . yanliang district is to the north . the district covers . as of november 2010 , 655,874 people lived there . long ago it called yueyang ( 栎阳 ) . firstly , lingtong district called lingtong county , and belong to weinan municipality . then it was give to xi'an . in 1988 , it renamed to lingtong district . lingtong is famous in tour , here are many monuments . it 's also important for xi'an , in military .
