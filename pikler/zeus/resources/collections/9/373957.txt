James Hutchinson Woodworth     james hutchinson woodworth ( december 4 , 1804 – march 26 , 1869 ) was an american politician . he was in the illinois state senate and in the illinois state house of representatives . he was a chicago alderman . he was elected to consecutive terms as mayor of chicago , illinois ( 1848–1850 ) as an independent democrat . he was in the us house of representatives for one term as a member of the republican party . woodworth was born on december 4 , 1804 in greenwhich , new york . he was married to almrya booth from 1842 until his death in 1869. they had two children . woodoworth died on march 26 , 1869 in chicago , illinois from an illness , aged 64. other websites . - first inaugural address - second inaugural address
