Americanisation     americanisation means that a culture ( often a country ) is heavily influenced by so-called `` anglicisms '' . these anglicisms are american-based . anglicisms can be institution , values , ways and behaviours , but can also be symbols and pictures . in americanisation , the transferring of american culture is only one-way . a culture is being americanised , but the united states are n't affected by the other culture in return .
