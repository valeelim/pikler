Replication     replication may refer to : in science : - replication ( scientific method ) , one of the main principles of the scientific method - replication ( statistics ) , the repetition of a test or complete experiment - self-replication , the process in which something ( a cell , virus , program ) makes a copy of itself - dna replication , the process of copying a double-stranded dna molecule - semiconservative replication , mechanism of dna replication - replication ( metallography ) , the use of thin plastic films to duplicate the microstructure of a component in computing : - replication ( computing ) , the use of redundant resources to improve reliability or performance
