Andrew DeClercq     andrew declercq ( born february 1 , 1973 ) is a former american basketball player . he played in the national basketball association ( nba ) for ten seasons . he played for the golden state warriors , boston celtics , cleveland cavaliers and orlando magic . declercq played college basketball for the university of florida . while in the nba he averaged 4.8 points and 4.2 rebounds per game . other websites . - nba.com : andrew declercq bio
