1990 United States Census     the twenty-first united states census was done by the census bureau . the population of the united stateswas 248,709,873. this was an increase of 9.8 percent from the 1980 census . about 16 percent of households received a `` long form '' of the 1990 census . it had over 100 questions . it was the first census to have `` native hawaiian and other pacific islander '' as a racial group separate from asians .
