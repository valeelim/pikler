Samaná English     samaná english is a variety of the english language . it is spoken by about 8,000 people in the samaná peninsula , dominican republic . the speakers of samaná english are the descendants of african american slaves freed from the united states . they migrated to the samaná region beginning in 1824. today , they are the largest group of english-speakers in the dominican republic . the language is often described by linguists as either a creole language or a dialect of english . it is closely related to other creole languages of the caribbean , especially turks and caicos and bahamian creole . other websites . - ethnologue report for english ; samaná english is described under the heading `` dominican republic ''
