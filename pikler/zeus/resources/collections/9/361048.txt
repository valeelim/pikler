Sucralose     sucralose is an artificial sweetener , most of which , if ingested , will not be broken down by the body and is thereby non-caloric . sucralose is almost 600 times as sweet as sucrose , twice as sweet as saccharin and 3 times as sweet as aspartame . it is stable under heat and over a broad range of ph condition , meaning it can be used in baking or products which require longer shelf lives . it was first discovered and patented in 1976 .
