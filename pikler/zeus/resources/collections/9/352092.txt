Raja Pervaiz Ashraf     raja pervaiz ashraf ( ; born 26 december , 1950 in sanghar ) was the 17th prime minister of pakistan . he took charge of office on 22 june 2012 after the previous prime minister , yousaf raza gillani , was disqualified from the post for contempt of court charges . he belongs to the pakistan peoples party ( ppp ) and has been a member of the national assembly of pakistan .
