Jorge Lavat     jorge lavat bayona ( august 3 , 1933 – september 14 , 2011 ) was a mexican movie and television actor . he is mostly known for his last role as don julio in the soap opera `` eva luna '' . he was born in mexico city , mexico on august 3 , 1933. he was married first to ana maría torres until they divorced . then he married silvia burgos . again he was married to ana maría torres and lastly to rebeca martínez until his death . he had four children . lavat died on september 14 , 2011 in mexico city , mexico after a lung infection after surgery . he was 78 years old .
