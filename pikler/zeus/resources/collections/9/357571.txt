Christopher Lambert     christopher guy denis `` christopher '' lambert ( born march 29 , 1957 ) is an american-born french movie actor and producer , who has appeared in american , as well as french and other european movies . he is best known for his role as connor macleod , or simply `` the highlander '' , from highlander and the highlander ( franchise ) . he is also known for his roles as tarzan in `` greystoke : the legend of tarzan , lord of the apes '' and as the thunder god raiden in the first movie adaptation of the video game `` mortal kombat '' . he is credited internationally as `` christopher lambert '' , with the exceptions of french-speaking countries , where he is known as `` christophe lambert '' . lambert was born in great neck , new york to french parents . lambert was married to diane lane from 1988 until they divorced in 1994. later he married jaimyse haft from 1999 until they divorced in 2000. he has been married to sophie marceau since 2012. his career began in 1978 and he still continues to act .
