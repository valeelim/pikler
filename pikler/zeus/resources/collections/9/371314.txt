Tiwana     the tiwana are a punjabi rajput clan from the punjab region of india and pakistan . the tiwanas in indian punjab are mostly sikhs , while the tiwanas of pakistani punjab are almost all muslims . the best-known member of this clan was late malik sir khizar hayat tiwana who for some time remained premier of the punjab in british india , following sir sikandar hayat khan 's demise .
