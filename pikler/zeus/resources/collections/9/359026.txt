The Satanic Bible     the satanic bible is a book by anton lavey . the church of satan considers it their main text . it is a collection of essays that detail the core principles of satanism . it is not a book about worshiping satan . satan in the satanic bible is just a symbol of freedom from the restrictive rules of christianity and other right-hand path religions . the satanic bible teaches that god and the devil do not exist , and encourages people to worship themselves instead .
