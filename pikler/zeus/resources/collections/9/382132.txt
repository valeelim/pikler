Dark Tranquillity     dark tranquillity is a swedish melodic death metal band from billdal . the band was formed in 1989 by band member mikael stanne and guitarist niklas sundin . stanne and sundin originally formed a band under the name `` septic broiler '' . members anders fridén , anders jivarp and martin henriksson joined the band shortly after . dark tranquillity is an important part of the melodic death metal genre of music and are one of the longest-standing bands from the original gothenburg metal scene . they released their debut album , `` skydancer '' , on august 30 , 1993. they are currently signed to century media records . band members . - current - mikael stanne - vocals ( 1989– '' present '' ) - martin henriksson - guitar ( 1989– '' present '' ) - niklas sundin - guitar ( 1989– '' present '' ) - daniel antonsson - bass ( 2008– '' present '' ) - anders jivarp - drums ( 1989– '' present '' ) - martin brändström - programming , keyboards ( 1999- '' present '' ) - former - anders fridén - vocals ( 1989–1993 ) - fredrik johansson - guitar ( 1993–1998 )
