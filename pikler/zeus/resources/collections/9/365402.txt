Jack Harris     jack harris ( born september 18 , 1941 ) is a broadcaster . he is the studio host for the tampa bay buccaneers radio network and a play-by-play voice for the tampa bay storm and the outback bowl . he started doing play-by-play for the south florida bulls men 's basketball team as well as the tampa bay rowdies . he was sports anchor at wtog-tv and wfla-tv where he was host of `` pulse plus ! '' on wtvt-tv before wfla-tv fired harris .
