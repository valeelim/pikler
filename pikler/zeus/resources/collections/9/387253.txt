Spaghetti squash     the spaghetti squash ( `` cucurbita pepo '' ) is an oblong seed-bearing variety of winter squash . the fruit can range either from ivory to yellow or orange in color . the orange varieties have a higher carotene content . its center contains many large seeds . its flesh is bright yellow or orange . when raw , the flesh is solid and similar to other raw squash ; when cooked , the flesh falls away from the fruit in ribbons or strands like spaghetti . it is a superfood that contains lots of nutrients , including folic acid , potassium , vitamin a , and vitamin c , it is low in calories , averaging 42 calories per 1-cup ( 155 grams ) serving .
