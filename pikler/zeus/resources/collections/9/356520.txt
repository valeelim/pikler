Salt-n-Pepa     salt-n-pepa are an american hip hop trio . they started in new york city in 1985. among their hit singles are `` none of your business '' , `` whatta man '' , `` push it '' and `` you showed me '' . the group have gone through several lineup changes since their starting . they released their first album `` hot , cool & vicious '' in 1986 . `` none of your business '' won the grammy award for best rap performance by a duo or group in 1995 .
