Suseok     suseok ( 수석 ) is the korean term for small naturally occurring or shaped rocks which are traditionally valued . they are similar to chinese scholar 's rocks . `` suseok '' can be any color . there are a wide variety of sizes . `` suseok '' can weigh hundreds of pounds or less than one pound . the term also means stones which are placed in traditional korean gardens . chinese scholar 's rocks influenced the development of `` suseok '' in korea . the evaluation of `` suseok '' recognizes color , shape , markings and surface . related pages . - suiseki - bonsai - penjing - standing stone other websites . - suseok at koreanviewingstones.com - bucheon museum of suseok at bucheon.go.kr
