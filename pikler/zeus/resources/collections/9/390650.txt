Charles Baudelaire     charles pierre baudelaire ( april 9 , 1821 – august 31 , 1867 ) , was a french poet and art critic . he is most known volume of poetry `` les fleurs du mal '' ( the flowers of evil ) in 1857. baudelaire was born in paris , france , and studying law at the lycée louis-le-grand in paris . baudelaire first published was his art review `` salon of 1845 .
