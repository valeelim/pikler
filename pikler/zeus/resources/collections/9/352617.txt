Letícia Sabatella     letícia sabatella ( born march 8 , 1972 in belo horizonte ) is a brazilian actress . letícia was two years old when her family moved itajubá ( mg ) to the mining town of conceição das alagoas . her father was an engineer at the big bend power plant . she said she learned to appreciate nature in this place full of greenery and people of different nationalities .
