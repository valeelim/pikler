Phil Esposito     philip anthony esposito , oc ( born february 20 , 1942 ) is a former canadian professional ice hockey centre . he played 18 seasons in the national hockey league for the chicago black hawks , boston bruins and new york rangers . he is an honoured member of the hockey hall of fame . it is said he is one of the best players in the national hockey league . phil is the older brother of tony esposito . tony is a goaltender and he is also a hall-of-famer . after retiring as a player , esposito was head coach and general manager for the new york rangers . then he started the tampa bay lightning . he worked for the nhl on fox from 1995 to 1998. he is now tampa bay 's radio announcer .
