Iqbal Park     iqbal park is a park in lahore , pakistan . it was called minto park during british indian times . it is named after `` allama '' sir muhammad iqbal , the national poet of pakistan . in the all india muslim leagues session of 21-24 march 1940 , which was held here , the famous lahore resolution was passed . this led to the eventual creation of independent pakistan in 1947. this event is remembered in this park , by the structure of the `` minar-e-pakistan '' ( pakistan tower/minaret ) , which is visited by many people every year .
