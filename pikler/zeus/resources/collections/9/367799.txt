Born in the U.S.A. (song)    born in the u.s.a. ( song ) `` born in the u.s.a. '' is a rock music song by bruce springsteen . the song appears on springsteen 's 1984 album , `` born in the u.s.a. '' . the song was recorded on may , 1982 and became the biggest hit of his career . rolling stone ranked the song 275th on their list of `` the 500 greatest songs of all time '' . the song deals with the effects of the vietnam war , but is often interpreted as a patriotic or nationalistic anthem . other websites . - lyrics
