Kimberley Walsh     kimberley jane walsh ( born 20 november 1981 ) is a british singer . she was born and raised in bradford , west yorkshire . she is a member of pop quintet girls aloud . she was runner-up in the 2012 series of `` strictly come dancing '' . in 2012 she presented a chat show called `` suck my pop '' . on 4 february 2013 , walsh released `` centre stage '' , her first solo album .
