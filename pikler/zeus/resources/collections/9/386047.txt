Michelle Creber     michelle nicole creber ( born september 7 , 1999 in vancouver , british columbia ) is a canadian voice actor , dancer , singer and voice artist who is best known for voicing `` apple bloom '' as well as the singing voice for `` sweetie belle '' in . she released her first solo album titled `` timeless : songs of a century '' on august 28 , 2012. the album features popular musicians from the brony community . she also hosts her own internet radio show called saturday night songs , on the brony media network `` everfree network '' . other websites . - official website - michelle creber at the internet movie database
