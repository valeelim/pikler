Jiřina Jirásková     jiřina jirásková ( february 17 , 1931 – january 7 , 2013 ) was a czechoslovak actress who is known for her roles in `` caesar and cleopatra '' , `` richard iii '' , `` the cherry orchard '' , and `` the full monty '' . jirásková was born on february 17 , 1931 in prague , czechoslovakia . she was married for two years to jiří pleskot until they divorced . then she spent 27 years with zdeněk podskalský until her death . she had no children . jirásková died on january 7 , 2013 in prague , czech republic from natural causes , aged 81 .
