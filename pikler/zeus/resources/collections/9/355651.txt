Sirenidae     sirenidae or sirens is a family of aquatic salamanders . they have very small front legs and do not have back legs . they have gills for breathing . sirens are found only in the southeastern united states and northern mexico . the siren family ( sirenidae ) is subdivided into two genera , with two species each : family sirenidae - genus `` pseudobranchus '' – dwarf sirens - `` pseudobranchus axanthus '' – southern dwarf siren - `` pseudobranchus striatus '' – northern dwarf siren - genus `` siren '' – sirens - `` siren intermedia '' – lesser siren - `` siren lacertina '' – greater siren other websites . - tree of life : sirenidae
