Pervasive developmental disorder     pervasive developmental disorders ( pdd ) , are a group of disorders . there are five disorders in the group . these disorders all slow down the rate a child learns to talk and listen to others , as well as cause other problems . the five disorders are : - pervasive disorder not otherwise specified ( pdd-nos ) . - autism - asperger syndrome - rett syndrome - childhood disintegrative disorder ( cdd ) the first three of these disorders are also called autism spectrum disorders . the last two are not common . a child 's parents might begin to notice signs of pdd when the child is a baby , and it usually appears before the child is three years old .
