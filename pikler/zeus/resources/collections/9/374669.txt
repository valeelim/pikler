Buddy Guy     george `` buddy '' guy ( born july 30 , 1936 ) is an american blues guitarist and singer . he worked with other musicians such as muddy waters , eric clapton , jimi hendrix , and stevie ray vaughan . he is known for his music such as `` slipin ' in '' and `` feels like rain '' . guy was born on july 30 , 1936 in lettsworth , louisiana . he was raised in chicago , illinois . he was married to jennifer guy from 1991 until they divorced in 2002 .
