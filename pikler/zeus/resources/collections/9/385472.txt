Florence Lawrence     florence annie bridgwood ( also known as florence lawrence ; january 2 1886-december 28 1938 ) was a canadian actress . she acted in silent movies and on stage . she acted in almost three hundred movies . she was very involved in stage and broadway plays . she was born in 1886 near hamilton , ontario . her death happened in hollywood , california almost three days after christmas 1938. she poisoned herself using ant paste . her age was fifty-two when she died .
