Neuhaus (chocolatier)    neuhaus ( chocolatier ) neuhaus is a company that makes luxury belgian chocolates , biscuits and ice cream . the company was founded in brussels in 1857 by jean neuhaus . neuhaus was a swiss immigrant who opened the first store in the galeries royales saint-hubert . in 1912 , his grandson , jean neuhaus ii , invented the chocolate bonbon or `` praline '' . today , neuhaus has over 1,500 selling points in 50 countries . all neuhaus products are still made in vlezenbeek ( flemish brabant ) . they are exported worldwide . in 2000 , the company was named accredited supplier to the belgian crown . other websites . - neuhaus website
