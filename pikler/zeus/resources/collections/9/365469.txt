Gmina Mysłakowice     gmina mysłakowice is a rural gmina ( administrative district ) in jelenia góra county , lower silesian voivodeship , in south-western poland . its seat is the village of mysłakowice , which is about southeast of jelenia góra and west of the regional capital wrocław . the gmina covers an area of , and as of 2006 its total population is 9,990. gmina mysłakowice is bordered by the towns of jelenia góra and kowary and the gminas of janowice wielkie , kamienna góra and podgórzyn . the gmina contains the villages of bukowiec , dąbrowica , gruszków , karpniki , kostrzyca , krogulec , łomnica , mysłakowice , strużnica and wojanów . references . - polish official population figures 2006
