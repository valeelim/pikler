Saitō Makoto     saitō was born in iwate prefecture . he was a member of the sixth class which graduated from the japanese naval academy . saitō rose to the rank of admiral in the imperial japanese navy . he was japanese governor-general of korea from 1919 to 1927. he returned to korea as governor-general from 1929 to 1931. he was prime minister from 1932 to 1934. related pages . - shōwa period
