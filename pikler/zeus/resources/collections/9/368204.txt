Unitarian Universalism     unitarian universalism is a religion which encourages liberalism and an individual search for truth . it includes many spiritual paths . its adherents have a pluralistic approach to faith hence may be atheists , agnostics , ignostics , deists , pagans , humanists , polytheists , or monotheists . a common tenet or doctrine within this religion is bodily integrity , self-ownership and individual autonomy . unitarian universalist places of worship are called `` congregations '' . the religion accepts differences and there is no holy book , dogma , or doctrine . instead , the adherents are guided by seven principles : 1 . 1st principle : the inherent worth and dignity of every person ; 1 . 2nd principle : justice , equity and compassion in human relations ; 1 . 3rd principle : acceptance of one another and encouragement to spiritual growth in our congregations ; 1 . 4th principle : a free and responsible search for truth and meaning ; 1 . 5th principle : the right of conscience and the use of the democratic process within our congregations and in society at large ;
