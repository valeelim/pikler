Cyclic vomiting syndrome     cyclic vomiting syndrome ( us english ) or cyclical vomiting syndrome ( uk english ) , abbreviated cvs , is a medical condition whose main symptoms are nausea and repeated vomiting . cvs happens more often in children but it can occur at any age . cvs is one of the `` childhood periodic syndromes '' . this is a group of migraines syndromes that happen to children . children who have one of these `` childhood periodic syndromes '' like cyclic vomiting , will when they get older , usually get one of the other types of migraine that affect adults
