Dianthus armeria     dianthus armeria is an annual or biennial flowering plant commonly known as deptford pink or grass pink . it is in the family caryophyllaceae . it has small pink flowers that bloom from spring to summer followed by an explosive , inedible fruit . the blooms close up in the afternoon . the seeds are small and blackish or brownish . it is a native plant in europe , and widely grown as an ornamental plant in gardens . it has been introduced to new zealand and much of north america .
