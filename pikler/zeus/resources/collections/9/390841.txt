Louis Jourdan     louis jourdan ( louis robert gendre , june 19 , 1921 – february 14 , 2015 ) was a french retired actor . he appeared in `` the paradine case '' ( 1947 ) , `` gigi '' ( 1958 ) , `` the best of everything '' ( 1959 ) and `` octopussy '' ( 1983 ) . his final movie before his retirement was `` the year of the comet '' in 1992. jourdan was born on june 19 , 1921 in marseille , france . he studied at the école dramatique . jourdan was married to berthe frédérique from 1946 until her death in 2014. they had a son , louis jourdan , jr.. jourdan , jr. died of a drug overdose aged 29 in 1981. jourdan died in beverly hills , california at the age of 93 .
