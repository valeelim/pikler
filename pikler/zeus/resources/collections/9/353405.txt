Second Alcibiades     second alcibiades ( also called alcibiades ii or alcibiades minor ) is a text in ancient greek . it is a dialogue . originally , it was attributed to plato , but today it is thought plato was not the author . in the dialogue , socrates talks to the young alcibiades . alcibiades later became a successful statesman and general . the two people talk about philosophical aspects of prayer . the starting point of the question is how prayer should be performed correctly , and what to pray for .
