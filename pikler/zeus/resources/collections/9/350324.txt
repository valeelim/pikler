AFC U-22 Asian Cup     the afc u-22 asian cup is a football competition between the best international under-22 teams in asia . it is ran by the asian football confederation . the teams are selected based on good performances in the qualification matches . the competition format in the 2013 tournament is : - sixteen teams will play in the final tournament , including the hosts who automatically qualify . - teams are seeded based on the result of the 2010 afc u-19 championship . - three or four stadiums in at most two cities will be needed to host the tournament . the host country will be rotated by regions : the region for the first tournament will be decided by draw .
