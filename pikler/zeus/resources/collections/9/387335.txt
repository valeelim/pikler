Die Fledermaus     die fledermaus ( ) is an opera by johann strauss ii . its first performance took place on april 5 , 1874 at the theater an der wien in vienna , austria . the opera has been presented on new year 's eve in vienna since the early 1930s . `` die fledermaus '' is based on the french play `` le réveillon '' ( 1872 ) by meilhac and halévy . references . - woodstra , chris , et . al . 2008 . `` all music guide to classical music '' . backbeat books . pp . 1317-1318. isbn 0-87930-865-6 .
