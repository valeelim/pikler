Mary Jane (shoe)    mary jane ( shoe ) mary janes are low-cut , rounded-toed shoes that are worn almost entirely by girls or women . they are usually low or mid heeled , though there are some that are high heeled . the shoes have one thin strap over the instep of the foot . they are made almost exclusively of leather . these shoes are worn at school , in church or during work ( for certain places ) , and with skirts , dresses and certain types of pants .
