Parotoid gland     the parotoid gland is a skin gland on the back , neck and shoulder of toads and some frogs and salamanders . it 's secretes has a substance that predators can smell . this smell will tell them that they are toxic animals . the substance , bufotoxin , acts as a nuerotoxin . parotoid glands looks like warts on an amphibian skin . however , warts are grown by viral infections . parotoid glands are normal and healthy parts of the animals that have them . many people believe that touching a toad may cause warts . however , this is not true .
