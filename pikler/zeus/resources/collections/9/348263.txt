Eleutherodactylus     eleutherodactylus is a genus of frogs in the eleutherodactylidae family . there are 185 species of `` eleutherodactylus '' . many of them are commonly called `` rain frogs '' or `` robber frogs '' . this is because of their sharp , high-pitched , and insect-like calls . the common coquí ( `` eleutherodactylus coqui '' ) is the best known species . they are a national symbol of puerto rico . they are also an invasive species in the us state of hawaii . two `` eleutherodactylus '' species , `` e. limbatus '' and `` e. iberia '' , belong to the smallest known frogs . they measure only 8½ mm in length .
