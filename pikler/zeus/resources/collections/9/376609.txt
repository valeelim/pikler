David Sarnoff     david sarnoff was a russian-born american broadcasting pioneer . he was born 27 april 1891 and died 12 december 1971. he picked up the alert signal from the rms titanic on 14 april 1912. in 1916 , he had the idea for radios . after a couple of years , the rca sold radios with a value of 80 000 000 dollars .
