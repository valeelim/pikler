Acanthopleura     acanthopleura is a genus of chitons in the family chitonidae . in this genus the girdle is spiny or spiky . it has eight described species at present . according to the world register of marine species ( worms ) , species in the genus `` acanthopleura '' include - `` acanthopleura brevispinosa '' - `` acanthopleura echinata '' - `` acanthopleura gemmata '' - `` acanthopleura granulata '' - `` acanthopleura loochooana '' - `` acanthopleura planispina '' - `` acanthopleura spinosa '' - `` acanthopleura vaillantii '' - `` acanthopleura hirtosa '' - `` acanthopleura gaimardi ''
