Deborah     deborah ( also spelled debra ) is a female name . it is used a lot in some countries . the name comes from a hebrew word for `` the bee '' . this name was used by a prophet woman inside the old testament book of judges . in the united states , `` deborah '' itself was most popular for females born between 1950 and 1970. it ranked number 25 for 1990 , although it is now less popular . its variant forms `` debra '' , `` debby '' and `` debbie '' have been used for atlantic tropical cyclones . famous people with the name include debbie allen and debbie gibson .
