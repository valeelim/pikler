Michael Straczynski     joseph michael straczynski ( born july 17 , 1954 ) is an american comic book writer , author , journalist , and television producer . he is known for creating the television series `` babylon 5 '' . straczynski was born on july 17 , 1954 in paterson , new jersey and studied at san diego state university . he has been married to kathryn m. drennan from 1983 until they divorced in 2008 .
