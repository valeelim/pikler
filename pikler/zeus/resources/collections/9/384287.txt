Olly Murs     olly murs ( born 14 may 1984 ) is an english singer . he comes from witham , essex . he became famous after he finished second in the x factor in 2009. in august 2010 , his first single `` please do n't let me go '' came top of the record charts in the uk . after that , he had another three songs that went to number one : `` heart skips a beat '' , `` dance with me tonight '' and `` troublemaker '' ( with flo rida ) . he has also made three albums , which all did well in the uk charts . his label is epic records . he is co-host of the x factor along with caroline flack . they took over from dermot o'leary from 2015. other websites . - official website
