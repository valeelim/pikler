Chris Hemsworth     chris hemsworth ( born august 11 , 1983 ) is an australian movie , television , and voice actor . he is known for his role as thor in `` thor '' , `` the avengers '' , and `` thor : the dark world '' . hemsworth was born on august 11 , 1983 in melbourne , victoria , australia . he has been married to elsa pataky since 2010. they have a daughter , rose hemsworth .
