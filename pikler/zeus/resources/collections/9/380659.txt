Gympie, Queensland    gympie , queensland gympie is a town in queensland , australia . the city is built on the banks of the mary river . it is about north of brisbane . in 2011 there were 18,602 people living in gympie . gympie is named after the aboriginal words `` gympie gympie '' or `` gimpi gimpi '' , meaning the stinging tree . the town was first called nashville , after james nash who found gold there in 1867. in 1868 it became gympie .
