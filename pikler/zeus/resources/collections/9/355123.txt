Kottabos     kottabos ( ) was a party game played at symposia in ancient greece . it was popular in the 5th and 4th centuries bc . it was also popular with the etruscans . the game is played by flinging a bit of wine from one 's cup at targets . the player would utter the name of his lover . the game was probably of sicilian origin but spread throughout the greek world . many greek poets mention the game in their works . by the time of the roman empire and the age of alexander the great , the game had died out . it is not mentioned in latin literature .
