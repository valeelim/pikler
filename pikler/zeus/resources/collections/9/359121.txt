4D movie     a 4d movie is a combination of a 3d movie with things like smell and touch ( physical effects ) . these effects happen at the same time the movie is running . ( note : 4d movies are not geometrically 4-dimensional . ) because these physical effects can cost a lot of money , 4d movies are usually shown only at special places , like theme parks and amusement parks . however , in thailand , mexico , hong kong , nepal , south korea , india and the u.s. , some movie theatres can also show 4d movies . the movie `` avatar '' was one of 10 movies that have been changed to 4d . the first was `` journey to the center of the earth '' . some of the effects used in 4d films include rain , wind , strobe lights , and vibration . water sprays and air jets are also often used . a 4d film is not shown in a motion simulator . however , during a 4d movie , some seats may vibrate or move a few inches during the movie . other websites . - mcl 4d extreme screen - a 4d screen in hong kong
