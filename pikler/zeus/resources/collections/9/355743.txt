Geraldo Rivera     geraldo rivera ( born july 4 , 1943 , manhattan , new york city ) is an american attorney , journalist , author , reporter , and talk show host . he almost always appears on fox news . he hosted a talk show , `` geraldo '' , which ran for eleven years and featured many controversial guests . he began hosting for fox news not long after the attacks of september 11 , 2001. he hosts `` geraldo at large '' . he has written six books .
