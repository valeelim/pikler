George Ryan     george homer ryan , sr. ( born february 24 1934 ) is an american politician . he is a republican . he was the former governor of illinois . he is a member for the united states republican party . his political career was tarnished due to scandals and corruption : ryan was found guilty on almost all charges and sent to prison . he will get released in 2013. ryan was illinois ' governor beginning 1999 until 2003. he is listed as federal inmate number 16627-424 , and was scheduled july 4 , 2013. he was released in june 2013 .
