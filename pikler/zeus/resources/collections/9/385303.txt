Her Majesty's Ship    her majesty 's ship hms is an acronym ( contraction ) of her majesty 's ship . it refers to ships of the royal navy . it is the official prefix of royal navy ship names since 1789. versions of hms are used in those commonwealth countries where the british monarch is still the head of state . so , for example , hmnzs is the prefix for the royal new zealand navy . there are some similar designations . rms designates a ship which officially transports mail for the united kingdom . other countries with monarchs use the same kind of system , and almost all countries have a prefix system for their ships .
