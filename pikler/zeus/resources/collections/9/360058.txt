Robert Hughes (critic)    robert hughes ( critic ) robert studley forrest hughes , ao ( 28 july 19386 august 2012 ) was an australian-born art critic , writer and maker of television documentaries . hughes was born in sydney , australia in 1938. hughes died on august 6 , 2012 in the bronx , new york , aged 74. in 1970 , he became the art critic for `` time '' magazine . honours . - order of australia , 1991
