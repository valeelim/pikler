Jerry Van Dyke     jerry van dyke ( born july 27 , 1931 ) is an american comedian and television actor . he performed in many television sitcoms and big lots commercials . his comedic acting goes back to the 1960s . his brother is actor dick van dyke . van dyke was born in danville , illinois , in 1931. his parents were loren ( nickname “ cookie ” ) and hazel ( nee mccord ) van dyke . he is of dutch ancestry on his father 's side and of english and scottish ancestry on his mother 's side . his mother is a `` mayflower '' descendant .
