Luke Askew     luke askew ( march 26 , 1932 – march 29 , 2012 ) was an american movie and television actor best known for his role in the 1969 film `` easy rider '' . he had a rare lead role in the spaghetti western `` night of the serpent '' ( `` la notte dei serpenti '' – 1969 ) . askew began his career in the 1967 otto preminger movie `` hurry sundown '' and his career would last until his retirement in 2005. askew was born in macon , georgia . askew died on march 29 , 2012 from lung cancer in his home in portland , oregon , he was 80 years old . askew was never married . movies . - `` hurry sundown '' ( 1967 ) - `` the happening '' ( 1967 ) - `` cool hand luke '' ( 1967 ) - `` will penny '' ( 1968 ) - `` the green berets '' ( 1968 ) - `` the devil 's brigade '' ( 1968 ) - `` easy rider '' ( 1969 ) - `` the magnificent seven ride '' ( 1972 ) - `` the culpepper cattle company '' ( 1972 ) - `` the great northfield minnesota raid '' ( 1972 ) - `` walking tall part 2 '' ( 1975 ) - `` pat garrett and billy the kid '' ( 1973 ) - `` slipstream '' ( 1973 ) - `` posse '' ( 1975 ) - `` rolling thunder '' ( 1977 ) - `` wanda nevada '' ( 1979 )
