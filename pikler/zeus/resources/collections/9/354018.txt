Plains Spadefoot Toad     the plains spadefoot toad ( `` spea bombifrons '' ) is a species of spadefoot toad . they live in southwestern canada , the great plains of the united states and northern mexico . like many spadefood toads , they get their name for having spade-like legs . this allows them to dig into sand and soil . references . - herps of texas : `` spea bombifrons '' - canada 's amphibians : `` spea bombifrons ''
