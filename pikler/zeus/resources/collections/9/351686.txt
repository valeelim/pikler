Football at the 2012 Summer Olympics – Men's tournament    football at the 2012 summer olympics – men 's tournament the 2012 men 's olympic football tournament is a under-23 football tournament . it is held during the 2012 summer olympics . it is held in 26 july to 11 august . all the national under-23 qualified football teams can compete . the defending champion is argentina . the tournament is hosted in england ( 4 stadiums ) , scotland ( 1 stadium ) and wales ( 1 stadium ) . 16 teams were qualified for the final group stage . related pages . - football at the 2012 summer olympics – women 's tournament
