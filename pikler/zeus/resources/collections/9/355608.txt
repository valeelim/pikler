Ivanka Trump     ivanka trump ( born october 30 , 1981 ) is an american businesswoman , writer , heiress , and former model . trump is serving as assistant to the president since march 29 , 2017. she is the daughter of u.s president donald trump and czech model ivana trump . she works at the trump organization with the company 's real estate and hotel management . she also has several other business deals . on october 25 , 2009 , trump married businessman jared kushner .
