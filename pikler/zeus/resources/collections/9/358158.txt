Murder of JonBenét Ramsey     jonbenét patricia ramsey ( august 6 , 1990 - december 25 , 1996 ) was an american child beauty pageant queen . she was born in atlanta , georgia and moved to boulder , colorado when she was nine months old . she was strangled in her house in boulder . her murder remains unsolved . alexis reich falsely confessed to the murder in 2006 .
