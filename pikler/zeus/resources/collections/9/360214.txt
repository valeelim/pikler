Jim'll Fix It    jim 'll fix it jim 'll fix it was a long-running british television show . it was broadcast by the bbc between 1975 and 1994. in 2007 it was shown on uktvgold . it was presented by the late jimmy savile . it was produced by roger ordish , who also worked on other bbc shows , including a bit of fry & laurie . when saville was knighted , the book for the 1991 series of the comedy sketch show had a statement that in future `` jim 'll fix it '' would be known as : `` sir james will bring his influence to bear in arranging matters to your satisfaction .
