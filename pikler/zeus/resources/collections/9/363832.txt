Squire Fridell     squire fridell ( born february 9 , 1943 ) is an american actor , best known for playing ronald mcdonald in the mcdonald 's commercials after king moody 's retirment . fridell was born in oakland , california on february 9 , 1943. after king moody 's retirement in 1984 , fridell was hired to replace him as ronald mcdonald in mcdonald 's commercials . he did this until 1991 when he was replaced by jack deopke .
