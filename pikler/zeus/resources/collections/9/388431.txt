Opel Mokka     the opel mokka is a car from the german carmaker opel since 2012. the mokka is a subcompact crossover suv , and rides on gm 's gamma platform , which is the same platform the chevrolet aveo/sonic and spark are built on . the mokka is sold as the buick encore and chevrolet trax in north america and is sold as a vauxhall in the uk . the opel/vauxhall versions have recently been facelifted and are now called mokka x .
