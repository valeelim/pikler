Bodily integrity     bodily integrity is the idea that a person 's body belongs only to that person , and only he or she should decide what happens to it . violations of bodily integrity may include rape or murder . it is also sometimes used in debates about euthanasia or circumcision . it is one of the principles of liberalism . it may also be called other names such as `` self-ownership '' , `` individual autonomy '' or `` self-determination '' .
