Tito Sotto     vicente `` tito '' castelo sotto iii ( born august 24 , 1948 ) is an actor , tv host and a politician in the philippines . sotto was vice mayor of quezon city , philippines from 1988 to 1992. he was also in the philippines ' senate for two terms from 1992 to 2004. in 2010 , he was reelected in the senate . he is currently a host of `` eat bulaga ! '' . sotto was born on 24 august 1948. his parents were marcelino ojeda sotto and dr. herminia castelo sotto . he studied at the colegio de san juan de letran in intramuros , manila . he earned there a bachelor of arts degree majoring in english .
