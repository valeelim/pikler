Red Dawn (1984 movie)    red dawn ( 1984 movie ) red dawn is a 1984 american war action movie . it was released in august 1984 with both good and bad reviews . it starred patrick swayze , lea thompson , charlie sheen and jennifer grey . the film is set in an alternative 1980s in which the united states is invaded by the soviet union and its cuban and nicaraguan allies . world war iii is mentioned in the movie , but is not fully explained . the story follows a group of american high school students who resist the occupation with guerrilla warfare , calling themselves wolverines , after their high school mascot . the movie was the first to use a rating of pg-13 . the movie did very well at the box office .
