Nara, Nara    nara , nara it has been recognized as a core city since 2002. many of the temples , shrines and ruins in nara form `` historic monuments of ancient nara '' , which is a unesco world heritage site . the site includes - tōdai-ji - kōfuku-ji - kasuga taisha - gangō-ji - yakushi-ji - tōshōdai-ji - heijō palace the kasugayama primeval forest is also identified as a world heritage site . the city is in the northern part of nara prefecture . it is on the border with kyoto prefecture . other websites . - nara city website - historic monuments of ancient nara
