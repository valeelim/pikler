Kashan     kashan is a city in iran . it is near isfahan , about from tehran . the city is known for its textiles . in the middle ages , it was also known for ceramics . as of 2012 , about 275,000 people live in the city , about 317,000 in its metropolitan area . the city is along the road from qom to kerman . kashan is located at the edge of a desert , along the first oasis . the city has a desert climate , with hot summers , and cold winters . there is a university in the city , which offers an education in medicine . rose water is produced in the city . in natanz , about south of kashan , there is an uranium enrichment plant . this plant uses gas centrifuges , and is part of the iranian nuclear program .
