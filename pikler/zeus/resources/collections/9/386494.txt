Sly and the Family Stone     sly and the family stone was an american rock , funk and soul band . the band was formed in 1967 and broke up in 1983. the band played an important park in the development of soul , funk , and psychedelic music . the group was led by sly stone . it also included several of his siblings . they are known for their songs `` everyday people '' and `` dance to the music '' . in 1993 , the band was inducted into the rock and roll hall of fame . other websites . - official website
