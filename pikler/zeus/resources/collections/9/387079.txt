Ann Sothern     ann sothern ( january 22 , 1909 - march 15 , 2001 ) was an american movie , stage and television series actress . she starred in a lot of radio and television shows . she began her career in the late 1920s , and it lasted less than sixty years . she acted in the sitcoms `` private secretary '' ( 1953-58 ) and `` the ann sothern show '' ( 1958-61 ) . sothern was born in north dakota . she moved to idaho after she retired from acting . she died in idaho from heart failure .
