Louise Currie     louise currie ( born louise gunter ; april 7 , 1913 ) is a retired american movie actress . she was active from 1940 until her retirement in 1997. currie was born on april 7 , 1913 in oklahoma city , oklahoma . she studied at martha washington seminary and at sarah lawrence college . currie was married to john whitney until they divorced . then she was married to john good from 1948 until his death in 1996. then she was married to grover asmus from 2002 until his death in 2003. she now lives in beverly hills , california .
