Batcave     the batcave is the fictional secret lair of batman . it is on the foundation of wayne manor . there are two ways into the cave , one is through a waterfall on the back of the cave and the other is a built-in elevator that leads from the library of wayne manor to the bridge of the cave . it holds all of batman 's secrets , gadgets , the batmobile , and a real gorge . the cave also has millions of bats . other websites . - top 10 batcave trophies article on comicsbulletin about the batcave hall of trophies
