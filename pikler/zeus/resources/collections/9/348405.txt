Kyoto University     , or is a japanese university in kyoto . it is the second oldest japanese university . it is in the city of kyoto in kyoto prefecture was established on june 1897 as a part of the imperial university system . the college of law and the college of medicine were founded in 1899 , and the college of letters was established in 1906. in october 1947 , kyoto imperial university was renamed kyoto university . related pages . - university of tokyo other websites . - kyoto university website
