Martha Reeves     martha rose reeves ( born july 18 , 1941 ) is an american musician and former politician . she was a councilwoman in detroit , michigan . she was associated with r & b group martha and the vandellas during the 1960s . together , their hits included `` dancing in the street '' and `` heat wave '' . she had some solo songs , also . reeves was a detroit councilwoman between 2005 and 2009. she was born in alabama . she was raised in detroit .
