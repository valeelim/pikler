Ignosticism     ignosticism is the belief that people should avoid taking a position on the existence of god until the concept of god is defined . ignostics claims that the word `` god '' is meaningless until it is defined . until one defines `` god '' they might as well be saying `` fob '' . this also means that until you are debating a definition of god that is able to be proven false , you might as well be debating about the word `` fob '' .
