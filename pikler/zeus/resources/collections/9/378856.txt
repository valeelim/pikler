Bud Luckey     william `` bud '' luckey ( born july 28 , 1934 ) is an american cartoonist , animator , singer , musician , composer , and actor who is known for designing some of the disney-pixar characters such as in `` toy story '' , `` monsters , inc. '' , `` ratatouille '' , `` toy story 2 '' , and in `` toy story 3 '' . he won an clio award and an annie award in 2004. luckey was born on july 28 , 1934 in billings , montana . he studied at chouinard art institute and at university of southern california . he has a son , andy .
