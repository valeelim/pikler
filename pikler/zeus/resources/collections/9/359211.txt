Amanda Anka     amanda katherine anka-bateman ( born on december 10 , 1968 in new york city , usa ) is an american movie , television , and voice actress . amanda katherine anka was born on december 10 , 1968 in new york city . she was born to musician and actor paul anka and model anne de zogheb , who were married from 1963 until their divorce in 2000. amanda is the second of five daughters . her sisters are alicia anka ( b.1967 ) , amelia anka ( b.1970 ) , ( b.1975 ) and alexandra anka ( b.1977 ) . she also has a younger half- brother , ethan anka ( b.2004 ) . amanda married her boyfriend of two years , actor jason bateman ( b.1969 ) , on july 3 , 2001. they have two daughters together - francesca nora bateman ( b.october 28 , 2006 ) and maple sylvie bateman ( b.february 10 , 2012 ) .
