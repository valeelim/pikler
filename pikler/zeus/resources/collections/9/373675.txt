Epidendroideae     epidendroideae is a subfamily of the orchid family . epidendroideae is larger than all the other orchid subfamilies together , comprising more than 15,000 species in 576 genera . most epidendroid orchids are tropical epiphytes , typically with pseudobulbs . there are , however a some terrestrials such as `` epipactis '' and even a few myco-heterotrophs , which are parasitic upon mycorrhizal fungi .
