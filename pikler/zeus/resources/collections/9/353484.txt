Méziane Idjerouidène     méziane idjerouidène ( born june 1980 in paris ) is the current managing director of his father 's french airline , aigle azur , since 2008. idjerouidène became familiar with airlines while he was young as he spent his holidays doing part-time jobs in the family business before graduating from toulouse business school and the french civil aviation university ( `` mastère spécialisé '' 2003 ) . méziane idjeroudène is also board member at enac alumni .
