Carmen Montejo     carmen montejo ( maría teresa sánchez gonzález ; may 26 , 1925 – february 25 , 2013 ) was an cuban-mexican actress . she was known for her many television shows . she was one of the last surviving actresses of the golden age of cinema of mexico . montejo was born on may 26 , 1925 in pinar del río , cuba . she was married to manuel gonzález ortega until his death . montejo died on february 25 , 2013 in mexico city , mexico from an illness , aged 87. other websites . - carmen montejo at the telenovela database - carmen montejo at esmas.com
