Video game collecting     video game collecting is the hobby of collecting video games and related things . it may be connected with animation , movies or comic books collecting . collectors may focus on collecting some type of things , such as retro consoles . collecting video games differs from other hobbies , because the collectible is interactive . so it can be enjoyed as long as it still functions . nostalgia plays a large role , as those who grew up with a certain generation of games may have stronger appreciation for them . related pages . - collecting - video game - hobby
