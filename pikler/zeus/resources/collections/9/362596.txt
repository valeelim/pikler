Dan McLaughlin     dan mclaughlin born march 18 , 1974 is a sportscaster . he currently calls games for the st. louis cardinals and college basketball games for the missouri tigers basketball , espnu , and fox sports net . he also has called regional college football games for espn . mclaughlin was a play-by-play announcer for the st. louis blues and was a regional nfl play-by-play announcer for fox in 2006 , some involving with the st. louis rams . he also calls occasional cardinals games on fox .
