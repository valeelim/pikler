Stacey King     ronald stacey king ( january 29 , 1967 ) is an american who is the current announcer with neil funk for the nba 's chicago bulls . he is known to provide nicknames for many of the bulls players . king 's own nicknames are sky and pearl , named after earl `` the pearl '' monroe . king was born on january 29 , 1967 in lawton , oklahoma . he was raised in chicago , illinois .
