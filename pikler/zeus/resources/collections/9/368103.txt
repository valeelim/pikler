Les Stroud     les stroud ( born october 20 , 1961 ) is a canadian filmmaker , musician , writer , television actor , host , television director and producer , and survival expert who is known for his survival show `` survivorman '' . stroud was born on october 20 , 1961 in mimico , ontario . he was raised in toronto , ontario . he studied at mimico high school and at fanshawe college . stroud was married to susan jamison from 1994 until they divorced in 2008. they had two children .
