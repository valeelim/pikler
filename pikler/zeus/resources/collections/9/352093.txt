Pune F.C.    pune f.c . pune f.c . is an indian football club at pune in the state of maharashtra . pune is one of the teams which compete in the i-league . the club was founded in 2007. the team was created to integrate `` the community through sport '' . the team owners brought together a passion for sports and improving the quality of life of the communities we serve . in the 2009-10 season , pune was elevated to the first division of the indian football league . in 2011 , pune became the first indian team to play an english premier league team at home . blackburn rovers f.c . played at pune on 7th october , 2011. related pages . - sports in india - list of indian football teams - list of pune f.c . players other websites . - pune f.c . website
