Battle of Belgium     the battle of belgium or belgian campaign was part of the greater battle of france . this was an offensive campaign by germany during the second world war . it took place over 18 days in may 1940. it ended with the german occupation of belgium following the surrender of the belgian army . the allied armies tried to stop the german army in belgium . they thought it was the main german attack . after the french had fully committed the best of the allied armies to belgium between 10 and 12 may , the germans went towards the english channel . the german army reached the channel after five days . they encircling the allied armies . the germans forced the allies back to the sea . the belgian army surrendered on 28 may 1940 , ending the battle . the battle of belgium included the first tank battle of the war . it was the largest tank battle in history up to that date . the battle also included the battle of fort eben-emael , the first strategic airborne operation using paratroopers . belgium was occupied by the germans until the winter of 1944â€“1945 . it was freed by the western alliance .
