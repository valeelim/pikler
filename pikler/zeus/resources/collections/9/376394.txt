Charles Cannon     charles-arthur dumoulin cannon ( 11 september 1905 – 23 september 1976 ) was a liberal party member of the canadian house of commons . he was a lawyer by career . he was first elected at the îles-de-la-madeleine riding in the 1949 general election . cannon was re-elected for multiple terms in 1953 and 1957. after completing his final term in the 23rd canadian parliament , cannon was defeated by james russell keays of the progressive conservative party . cannon was born in quebec city and died there . some of cannon 's relatives have also been members of parliament . namely his grandfather charles fitzpatrick , his uncle lucien cannon and his nephew lawrence cannon .
