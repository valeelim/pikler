Hugh de Lotharingia     hugh de lotharingia or hugh of lotharingia ( ca . 850/855 - 895 ) was a frankish royal family of the carolingian dynasty . he was the illegitimate son of lothar ii and his concubine waldrada . he was the grandson of lothar i. his title was count of lotharingia and duke of alsace . in early years , his father was appointed as count of lotharingia also . in 865 , he was appointed to duke of alsace . in 869 , lothar ii 's death , he was demand to lotharingia king , but west franks charles ii the bald and east frank louis ii the germans took to dismemberment .
