Lupita Tovar     lupita tovar ( july 27 , 1910 – november 12 , 2016 ) was a mexican-american silent movie actress . she was known for her role in the 1931 spanish version of `` dracula '' . tovar was born on july 27 , 1910 in matías romero , oaxaca , mexico . she began acting and was raised in mexico city , mexico . tovar was married to paul kohner from 1932 until his death in 1988. they had a daughter , susan kohner . tovar died on november 12 , 2016 at her home in los angeles , california of heart disease , aged 106 .
