Juan Mora Fernández     juan mora fernández ( 1784 san josé - 1854 ) was costa rica 's first elected head of state in 1825. he was considered a liberal and decided to move the capital from cartago to san josé . he is remembered for instituting land reform . he followed a progressive course . as a consequence of his land reform , he created an elite class of powerful coffee barons . the barons eventually overthrew one of his later successors .
