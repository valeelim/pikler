Scafell Pike     scafell pike is the highest mountain in england . it is tall . it is located in lake district national park , in cumbria . it is sometimes confused with the near-by scafell . the name `` pikes of sca fell '' was first used to mean the peaks now known as scafell pike , ill crag and broad crag . the contraction `` scafell pike '' started as an error on an ordnance survey map . the summit was given to the national trust in 1919 by lord leconfield . he did this in memory of the men of the lake district `` who fell for god and king , for freedom , peace and right in the great war '' .
