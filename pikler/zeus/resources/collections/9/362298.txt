Paul Burmeister     paul burmeister ( born march 10 , 1971 ) is an analyst for the nfl network . he hosts shows like `` college scoreboard '' , `` path to the draft '' and `` playbook '' . burmeister was an american football quarterback at the university of iowa . he led his team to the alamo bowl . he was a throwing quarterback at the 1994 nfl scouting combine . before he worked for the nfl network , burmeister was a sports anchor for kwwl in waterloo , iowa . there , he worked with rick coleman and chris kolkhorst . other websites . - iowa hawkeyes football bio
