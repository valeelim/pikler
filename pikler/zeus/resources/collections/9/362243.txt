'' and as juno in `` beetlejuice '' . sidney has won a golden globe award and a saturn award . sidney was born in the bronx , new york on august 8 , 1910. sidney has been married to bennett cerf from 1935 until they divorced in 193. then she married luther adler from 1938 until they divorced in 1946. they had a son named jacob ( 1939–1987 ) . she was married to carlton alsop from 1947 until they divorced in 1951. sidney died on july 1 , 1999 in new york city , new york from throat cancer . she was 88 years old .
