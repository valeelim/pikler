The Shirelles     the shirelles were a popular american girl group . they were very active from 1957 to 1982. some of their songs were `` i met him on a sunday '' and `` tonight 's the night '' . this group had four african american female singers . they had many hit records . the group broke apart in the very early 1980s . the group was formed by shirley owens ( later shirley alston reeves ) who was the lead singer . at one time they had six songs in the top ten . two of their songs , `` will you love me tomorrow '' ( written by carole king ) and `` tonight 's the night '' , were selected by rolling stone on its list of the greatest songs of all time . their song `` soldier boy '' sold over five million copies .
