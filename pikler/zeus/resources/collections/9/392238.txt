Growth hormone     growth hormone ( gh or hgh ) , also known as somatotropin or somatropin , is a hormone responsible for growth , cell reproduction and regeneration in humans and other animals . it is specific only to certain kinds of cells . growth hormone is used as a prescription drug in medicine to treat children 's and adults growth disorders . in the united states , it is only available legally by prescription from a doctor . today hgh is still considered a very complex hormone , and many of its functions are still unknown .
