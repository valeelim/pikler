Hot Tottie     `` hot tottie '' is a song by american singer usher . it was written by usher , ester dean , jay-z and polow da don . it was produced by don . it features rapper jay-z and ester dean in the song . it was released as the second single from `` versus '' ( 2010 ) . it was released on august 9 , 2010. it samples big tymers ' `` big ballin ' '' song .
