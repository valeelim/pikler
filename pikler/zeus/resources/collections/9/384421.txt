Tracy Chapman     tracy chapman ( born 30 march 1964 ) is an american singer-songwriter . she was born in cleveland in ohio . she has won four grammy awards . her most famous songs are `` fast car '' and `` give me one reason '' that went top of the charts in canada . they also did really well in the billboard charts ( united states ) . she has published a total of 8 albums . she is a feminist . she has never said publicly what her sexuality is , but she dated alice walker in the mid 1990s . other websites . - official website
