Stephen Henderson (actor)    stephen henderson ( actor ) stephen mckinley henderson ( born august 31 , 1949 ) is an american movie , television , and voice actor who is recently known for his roles as arthur in `` everyday people '' , as lester in the 2011 movie `` tower heist '' , and as william slade in `` lincoln '' . henderson was born on august 31 , 1949 in kansas city , missouri .
