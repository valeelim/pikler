John Wood     john wood , obe ( july 5 , 1930 august 6 , 2011 ) was an english movie , stage , and television actor . he was in movies such as `` wargames '' ( 1983 ) , `` ladyhawke '' ( 1985 ) , `` jumpin ' jack flash '' ( 1986 ) , `` richard iii '' ( 1995 ) , `` sabrina '' ( 1995 ) , and `` the revengers ' comedies '' ( 1997 ) . he won a tony award ( 1976 ) and a drama desk award ( 1976 ) . wood was born on july 5 , 1930 in derbyshire , united kingdom . he was raised in gloucestershire . wood was married to gillian neason until they divorced in 1975. he was married to sylvia vaughan from 1977 until his death in 2011. wood died on august 6 , 2011 in his home in gloucestershire , united kingdom from unknown causes , aged 81 .
