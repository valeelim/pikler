Crash Tag Team Racing     crash tag team racing is a racing video game published by sierra entertainment . it was developed by radical entertainment for the playstation 2 , nintendo gamecube , xbox and playstation portable . a nintendo ds version was cancelled . the reason for this is unknown . the game was released in north america on october 19 , 2005 and in europe on november 4 , 2005. other websites . - the official `` crash tag team racing '' website
