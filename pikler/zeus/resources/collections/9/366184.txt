Wendy Crewson     wendy jane crewson ( born may 9 , 1956 ) is a canadian movie , television , voice , and stage actress who is known for his role as `` the santa clause '' , `` the santa clause 2 '' , `` '' , `` better than chocolate '' , and in `` air force air '' . he won an gemini award . crewson was born on may 9 , 1956 in hamilton , ontario , canada . she studied at queen 's university and at webber douglas academy of dramatic art . crewson was married to michael murphy from 1988 until they divorced in 2009. they had two children .
