Kris Humphries     kris nathan humphries ( born february 6 , 1985 ) is an american professional basketball player who played for the utah jazz , then with the toronto raptors , then with dallas mavericks , and now he playes for the brooklyn nets . humphries was born on february 6 , 1985 in minneapolis , minnesota . humphries married kim kardashian in 2011 , but filed for divorce a couple of months after their marriage . their divorce was finalized on june 3 , 2013. other websites . - official website
