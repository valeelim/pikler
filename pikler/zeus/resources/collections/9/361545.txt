Tony Bennett     tony bennett ( born anthony dominick benedetto , august 3 , 1926 ) is an american singer who is best known for his song `` because of you '' . he was born in astoria , queens , new york city . he was married three times and has two children . he has many awards including a grammy award . bennett is a democrat and attended the 2012 democratic national convention in support of president obama 's re-election . in 2014 an album bennett made with lady gaga will be released . other websites . - official tony bennett music website - official tony bennett art website
