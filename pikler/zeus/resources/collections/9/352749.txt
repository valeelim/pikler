1915 Galveston hurricane     the 1915 galveston hurricane was an intense , deadly and very destructive storm . in august 1915 this hurricane lashed the caribbean island areas from the leeward islands to western cuba and then blasted galveston , texas with winds around the category 4 level ( around 135 mph to be exact ) . the winds of this hurricane were at 145 miles an hour when the storm struck cuba . almost 400 people died because of the storm .
