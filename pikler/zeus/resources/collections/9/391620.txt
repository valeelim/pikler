Manuel García Ferré     manuel garcía ferré ( october 8 , 1929 – march 28 , 2013 ) was a spanish-argentine animation director and cartoonist . he was known for his animated series `` hijitus '' . ferré was born on october 8 , 1929 in almería , spain . he was raised in buenos aires , argentina . ferré became an official buenos aires citizen in 2009. he was never married . ferré died on march 28 , 2013 in buenos aires after going through heart surgery , aged 83. other websites . - garcía ferré 's comics
