San Lorenzo, Tarija    san lorenzo , tarija san lorenzo ( or : `` villa san lorenzo '' ) is a town in the tarija department in bolivia . san lorenzo is the administrative center of eustaquio méndez province . it is 2,001 m above sea leavel , on the left bank of río tojtiwaysho . it is 15 km north of tarija , the department capital . san lorenzo had a population of more than 3,000 people in 2007. san lorenzo was the home of don eustaquio méndez . he was one of the leaders in the fights for bolivian independence . the local museum `` casa del moto méndez '' shows some of his personal belongings and weapons . other websites . - map of province
