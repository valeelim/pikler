KWUT     kwut ( 97.7 fm ) is a radio station broadcasting a country music format . licensed to elsinore , utah , usa , the station is currently owned by mid-utah radio , inc. the station went on the air as krfd-fm on november 9 , 1998. on september 11 , 1998 , the station changed its call sign to ksgi and on november 1 , 1999 to krfd . on november 25 , 2000 , the station became kace , and kmgr by december 31 , 2000. on november 15 , 2001 it switched back to kace and back to krfd on may 29 , 2001. the station became klgl on june 16 , 2001. on august 8 , 2005 , the station flipped to kcyq . it then flipped in the fall of 2010 , to the current calls .
