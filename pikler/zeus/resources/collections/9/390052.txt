List of Presidents of Costa Rica     the president of costa rica is the head of state and government of costa rica . the president is elected in direct elections . he or she is elected for four years . two vice presidents are elected at the same time with the president . the president appoints the council of ministers . due to the abolition of the military of costa rica in 1948 , the president is not a commander-in-chief . here is a table that has a list of the junta chairmen , presidents and heads of state of costa rica since independence from spain and mexico . from 1824 to 1838 costa rica was a state in the federal republic of central america . after that it has been an independent nation .
