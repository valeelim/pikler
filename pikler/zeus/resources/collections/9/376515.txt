Rockfish, Virginia    rockfish , virginia rockfish is an unincorporated community in nelson county , virginia , united states . it was among the communities severely affected by flash flooding from hurricane camille in 1969. rockfish was frequently referenced in the cbs television series `` the waltons '' . it was noted as being the closest town to waltons mountain . which was the fictional version of the nearby community of schuyler . also known as `` rockfish depot '' even though the railroad depot building has been gone for decades . other websites . - gnis reference
