Benigno Aquino, Jr.    benigno aquino , jr. benigno kheng hon `` ninoy '' aquino , jr. was a filipino senator and a former governor of tarlac . he was born on november 27 , 1932 and died on august 21 , 1983 at the age of 50. aquino , gerry roxas and jovito salonga formed the leadership of the opposition to the government of president ferdinand marcos . shortly after that , he was arrested in 1973 for seven years . aquino was allowed to travel to the united states for medical treatment following a heart attack . he was killed at the manila international airport in 1983 upon returning from his self-imposed exile . aquino 's death caused his widow , corazon aquino , to run for president in 1986. she became the 11th president of the philippines . his son , benigno aquino iii , became president in 2010 .
