Cryptobranchoidea     cryptobranchoidea is a suborder of salamanders . they are found in the eastern united states , china , taiwan , and japan . they are known as primitive salamanders . this is in contrast to salamandroidea , or advanced salamander . this suborder has only two families . other families went extinct in the past and are only known as fossils . - `` cryptobranchidae '' - `` hynobiidae ''
