Pope Gregory III     pope gregory iii ( ; died 28 november 741 ) was an syrian priest of the roman catholic church and the 90th pope from 11 february 731 to 28 november 741. gregory was born in syria , but the date of his birth is not known . gregory was elected pope in 731. during his reign , the issue of iconoclasm or image worship was an important controvery . after the reign of gregory iii , there were no other non-european popes until pope francis in 2013. related pages . - list of popes other websites . - `` gregory iii '' , `` encyclop√¶dia britannica '' , 1911 - catholic hierarchy , popes in sequence
