Emmanuelle Riva     emmanuelle riva ( born paulette germaine riva ; 24 february 1927 – 27 january 2017 ) was an french actress , writer , photographer , and poet . she was known for her bafta award-winning role as anne in `` amour '' . she was nominated for an academy award and won an bafta award for her role in `` amour '' . riva was born on 24 february 1927 in cheniménil , france . her grandfather was italian . riva was raised and studied acting in paris . riva died from cancer on 27 january 2017 in paris at the age of 89. other websites . - emmanuelle riva at newwavefilm.com
