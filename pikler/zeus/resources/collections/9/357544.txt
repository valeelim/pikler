Stacey Dash     stacey lauretta dash ( born january 20 , 1966 ) is an american television and movie actress . she usually acts in sitcoms and comedy related movies . her most notable television program works include `` the cosby show '' and `` the fresh prince of bel-air '' . her movies include `` clueless '' ( 1995 ) and `` mo ' money '' ( 1992 ) . some sources say dash was born january 20 , 1966 with others mentioning that she was born in either 1967 or 1968. dash was born in the bronx , new york city .
