King Moody     robert `` king '' moody ( december 6 , 1929 - february 7 , 2001 ) was an american actor and comedian . he was best known for playing ronald mcdonald in mcdonald 's commercials until his retirement in 1984. moody was born robert moody in new york city , new york on december 6 , 1929. from the 1970s to the 1980s , he played ronald mcdonald for the mcdonald 's commercials . after his retirement in 1985 , he was replaced by squire fridell . on february 7 , 2001 , moody passed away at the age of 71 , 10 months before his 72nd birthday .
