Juvenile idiopathic arthritis     juvenile idiopathic arthritis ( which is also called juvenile rheumatoid arthritis ) is a condition which affects people ages 16 and under . the problem consists of excess swelling around joints in the knees , ankles , wrists , hands and feet . symptoms consists of lethargy , strong pain and reduced physical activity . there is also stiffness in the morning which may improve later on during the day . juvenile arthritis is an autoimmune disease .
