Alfred Molina     alfred molina ( born alfredo molina , may 24 , 1953 ) is an english movie , television , stage , and voice actor . he is known for his roles as diego rivera in `` frida '' , satipo in `` raiders of the lost ark '' , dr. octopus in `` spider-man 2 '' , and as maxim horvath in `` the sorcerer 's apprentice '' . molina was born on may 24 , 1953 in paddington , london , england to a spanish mother and italian father . he grew up in notting hill , london . he studied at guildhall school of music and drama . molina has been married to jill gascoine since 1986. he has one daughter from another relationship .
