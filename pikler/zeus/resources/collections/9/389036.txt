Eparchy (disambiguation)    eparchy ( disambiguation ) in ancient rome the term `` eparchia '' was used to refer to a province in the eastern parts of the roman empire . today , the term is used for different concepts : - provinces of greece used to be called eparchies , until they were abolished , in 1997 . - districts of cyprus are called eparchies . - dioceses of the eastern orthodox church , the eastern catholic church , and a few other churches of the east are called eparchies
