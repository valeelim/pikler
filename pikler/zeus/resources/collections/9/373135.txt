Phidippus audax     phidippus audax is a jumping spider that comes from north america . the spider is often called the daring jumping spider or bold jumping spider in english . a spider of this species will usually be black with white patterns on its abdomen . in younger spiders , the spots on the abdomen will be a orange , red or yellow colour , which will become white as the spider becomes older . the average size of an adult is between 13 and 20 millimetres and the male is slightly smaller than the female .
