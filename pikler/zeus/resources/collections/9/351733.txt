Amélie Nothomb     amélie nothomb ( born fabienne-claire on 9 july 1966 ) is a belgian writer who writes in french . amélie nothomb was born in etterbeek , belgium to belgian diplomats . she lived in japan , then in china , new york , bangladesh , burma , coventry and laos . nothomb 's first novel , `` hygiène de l'assassin '' , was published in 1992. since then , she has published about one novel per year , including `` les catilinaires '' ( 1995 ) , `` fear and trembling '' ( 1999 ) and `` métaphysique des tubes '' ( 2000 , published in english as `` the character of rain '' ) . she has been awarded many prizes , including the 1999 grand prix du roman de l'académie française , the prix rené-fallet , and the 1993 prix alain-fournier .
