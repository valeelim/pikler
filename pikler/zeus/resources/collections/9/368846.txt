1880 United States Census     the united states census of 1880 was the tenth united states census . it was done by the census bureau during june 1880. it was the first time that women were allowed to be enumerators . the superintendent of the census was francis amasa walker . the 1880 census found the population of the united states to be 50,189,209. this was an increase of 30.2 percent from the 1870 census .
