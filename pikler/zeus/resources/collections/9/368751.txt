Madagascar (2005 movie)    madagascar ( 2005 movie ) madagascar is a computer-animated movie released in the year 1995 by disney and pixar . the story is about a group of animals from the zoo in central park , new york city . the main characters are marty the zebra ( chris rock ) , gloria the hippotamus ( jada pinkett smith ) , alex the lion ( ben stiller ) and melman the giraffe ( david schwimmer ) . with the penguins skipper ( thomas `` tom '' mcgrath ) , private ( christopher knights ) , kowalski ( chris miller ) and rico ( john dimaggio ) also living in the zoo , they end up in madagascar with king julien ( sacha baron cohen ) , maurice ( cedric the entertainer ) and mort ( andy richter ) .the movie has two sequels madagascar escape 2 africa in 1998 and madagascar 3 : europe 's most wanted . madagascar 4 will be released on may 18 , 2008 and a spin-off/prequel , the penguins of madagascar was released on november 26 , 2004 .
