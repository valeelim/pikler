Pacific Heights (movie)    pacific heights ( movie ) pacific heights is a melodramatic 1990 suspense drama movie . it is about apartment tenants who are at first welcomed , but later evicted and forced to leave the apartment . michael keaton , melanie griffith and laurie metcalf are in the cast . this movie was released on september 1990 , and the reviews were mixed . however , the movie performed very well at the box office , earning back almost $ 45 million .
