Kwon Young-ghil     kwon young-ghil ( , born november 5 , 1941 ) is a south korean politician , journalist , human rights activist , and workers ' rights activist . he was a member of the 17th , 18th , and 19th south korea national assembly . in 1997 he was defeated by the democratic labor party candidate for south korea 's 15th presidential election . he was defeated again in 2002 at 16th presidential election , and in 2007 for the 17th presidential elections . between 1996 and 1997 he was chairman of korean confederation of trade unions ( kctu ) . in 2000 to 2009 he was leader of democratic labour party ( ) . references . - kwon young-ghil : south korea national assemblys - kwon young-ghil - 민주노동당 홈페이지
