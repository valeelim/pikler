Natchitoches, Louisiana    natchitoches , louisiana natchitoches ( ) is the parish seat of natchitoches parish , louisiana , united states . it was established in 1714 by louis juchereau de st. denis as part of french louisiana . it was named after the natchitoches indian tribe . the city of natchitoches was first incorporated on february 5 , 1819. it is the oldest permanent settlement in the louisiana purchase . natchitoches 's sister city is nacogdoches , texas . education . - northwestern state university
