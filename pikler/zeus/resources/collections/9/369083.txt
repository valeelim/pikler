Nathan Lane     nathan lane ( born joseph lane , february 3 , 1956 ) is an american actor and writer who is known for his roles in `` mousehunt '' and in `` the lion king '' . he won an tony award , an screen actors guild award , an emmy award , and an drama desk award . lane was born on february 3 , 1956 in jersey city , new jersey . he studied at st. peter 's preparatory high school . he is openly gay .
