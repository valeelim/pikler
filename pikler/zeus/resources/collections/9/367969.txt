Ole Miss Rebels     the ole miss rebels are the sports team for the university of mississippi . they were first named the `` mississippi flood '' . they were renamed the rebels in 1936. the school 's colors are cardinal red and navy blue . this is meant to be the same as the school colors of harvard and yale . ole miss ' major athletic rivals are with the mississippi state bulldogs ( msu ) , the lsu tigers , and the arkansas razorbacks .
