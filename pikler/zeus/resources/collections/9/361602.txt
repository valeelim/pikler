George Pal     george pal ( györgy pál marczincsak , february 1 , 1908 – may 2 , 1980 ) was a hungarian-american animator , movie director and producer who has helped the movie industry grow during the 50s and 60s . an award is named after him . pal was born in cegléd , kingdom of hungary , austro-hungarian empire , ( now hungary ) . he was educated at the budapest academy of arts . he was married to elizabeth zsoka from 1930 until his death in 1980 , they had two children . pal died on may 2 , 1980 in los angeles , california from a heart attack . he was 72 years old . other websites . - nndb entry - george pal
