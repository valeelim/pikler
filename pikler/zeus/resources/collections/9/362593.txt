Sean McDonough     sean mcdonough ( born may 13 , 1962 ) is an american sportscaster . he works for espn . he worked with cbs sports before . he is the son of late `` boston globe '' sportswriter will mcdonough . he has broadcast college basketball ( including 10 ncaa tournaments ) , college football ( including the prestigious orange bowl game ) , the college world series , the nfl , us open tennis , 3 winter olympics , and golf ( including 4 masters and pga championships ) .
