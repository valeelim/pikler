Lucius Fox     lucius fox is a fictional batman character who helps batman with equipment and is the manager of wayne enterprises . he gives batman all of the equipment or his financing operations that he needs . he is one of the few people to known batman 's secret identity . he is played by morgan freeman in `` batman begins '' , `` the dark knight '' , and in `` the dark knight rises '' . brock peters was his voice actor in `` '' . kevin michael richardson also makes his voice in some animated movies .
