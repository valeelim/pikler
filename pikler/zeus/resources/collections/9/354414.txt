Omar Suleiman     omar suleiman ( , july 2 , 1936-july 19 , 2012 ) was an egyptian general , diplomat , and politician . he was the spy intelligence chief from 1993 to 2011 while hosni mubarak was president of egypt . in january 2011 suleiman was appointed vice-president by mubarak . he served until they left in early february 2011. in 2012 , suleiman wanted to be president but was disqualified from running . he was not seen in public since mubarak 's regime ended . on july 19 2012 , suleiman died in cleveland , ohio , u.s. from heart attack while undergoing medical trials .
