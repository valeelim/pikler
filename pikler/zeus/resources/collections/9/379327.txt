Cornwallis Square, Nova Scotia    cornwallis square , nova scotia cornwallis square is a canadian village in kings county , nova scotia . it is located on the cornwallis river . it was originally established in 1947 as the wcg commission and was responsible for fire protection , recreation and sidewalks in waterville , cambridge , and grafton . when the rural community of woodville was included in the service area in the 1960s the name was changed to cornwallis square . the area 's largest employer is michelin . references . - `` kings county register '' , may 2007
