Izabal Department     izabal is one of the 22 departments of guatemala . its coastal areas are part of the homeland of the garifuna people . izabal is bordered to the north by belize , to the north east by the gulf of honduras , and to the east by honduras . it is bordered by the guatemalan departments of petén to the north west , alta verapaz to the west , and zacapa to the south . the department of izabal surrounds lago izabal . it is guatemala 's largest lake at about 48 km long and 24 km wide , with an area of about 590 km² . the spanish colonial fort of san felipe overlooks the point where the lake flows into the río dulce . the small town of izabal is on the south shore of the lake . before the ports of livingston and puerto barrios were built in the 19th century this was guatemala 's main caribbean sea port . it was the original seat of izabal department . now izabal town is a remote village that gets little traffic . from the area around lake izabal , the department of izabal stretches along the río dulce to the coast of the caribbean sea .
