Big Business (1988 movie)    big business ( 1988 movie ) big business is a 1988 american comedy movie set in two pairs of identical twin girls are switched at birth in west virginia and go into two different settings in new york city . bette midler acts as sadie , lily tomlin plays rose . this movie was released in june 1988. it received lukewarm reviews by critics , although it performed quite well at the box office .
