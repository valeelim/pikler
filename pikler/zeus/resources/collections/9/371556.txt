Victims of Acts of Terror Memorial in Israel     the victims of acts of terror memorial in israel ( hebrew : אנדרטת חללי פעולות האיבה , andartat halalei pe'ulot haeiva ) is a memorial to all victims of terrorism in israel from 1851 onward . the memorial is located at the national civil cemetery of the state of israel in mount herzl in jerusalem . the memorial includes the names of jewish and non-jewish people who have been killed in acts of terror . other websites . - the monument for victims of hostile acts : honor inscribed in stone .
