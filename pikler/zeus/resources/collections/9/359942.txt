Kevin McNally     kevin mcnally ( born 27 april 1956 ) mostly known as kevin r. mcnally is an english movie , television , stage , voice , and radio actor who is mostly known for his role as joshamee gibbs in all four of the pirates of the caribbean movies . mcnally has also appeared in many movies such as ; `` valkyrie '' , `` scoop '' , `` spice world '' , `` johnny english '' , `` stalin '' , `` conspiracy '' , `` tuesday '' , and has recently appeared in the 2012 movie `` the raven '' . mcnally was born in bristol , england . mcnally is currently married to phyllis logan since 1977 , they have a son together .
