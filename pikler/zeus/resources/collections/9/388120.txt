Wii MotionPlus     the wii motionplus is an expansion device for the nintendo wii . it allows the wii remote to better capture motion . it was first announced on july 14 , 2008 in a press release by nintendo . the next day , it was shown at that year 's e3 . it was released in june 2009. starting may 9 , 2010 , new wii consoles include the `` wii sports resort '' video game and the wii motionplus . the wii u supports the wii motionplus in games where use of its touch screen controller is not necessary . on september 29 , 2010 , nintendo announced the wii remote plus controller . it is a wii remote with motionplus built in . this allows players to use special controllers such as the wii zapper without removing the motionplus device . it was released in europe on november 5 , 2010 , in north america on november 7 , 2010 , and in japan on november 11 , 2010 .
