John B. Anderson     john bayard anderson ( born february 15 , 1922 ) is an retired american-republican politician from illinois . he ran for president in 1980 , but lost to ronald reagan as the main republican candidate . he was a politician since the 1960s . he would later lose as a candidate as an independent . anderson was born on february 15 , 1922 in rockford , illinois to a swedish-american family . he studied at the university of illinois , at the university of illinois college of law , and at the harvard law school . anderson was married to keke machakos since 1953 .
