Robin Wright     robin gayle wright ( born april 8 , 1966 in dallas , texas ) is an american actress . her roles include starring as jenny in the academy award winning movie `` forrest gump '' . she played in many other movies and television series . wright was nominated for a daytime emmy award for her acting on the soap `` santa barbara '' . wright was marriage to actor sean penn from 1998 until 2010. they have 2 children .
