Ann-Margret     ann-margret olssen ( born april 28 , 1941 in valsjobyn , sweden , more often known as just ann-margret ) is a television and movie actress . she has won five golden globe awards for her many performances . her movies include `` the cincinnati kid '' and `` carnal knowledge '' . from 1967 until his death in 2017 , she was married to actor roger smith .
