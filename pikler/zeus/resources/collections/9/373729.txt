Mark Takano     mark allan takano ( born december 10 , 1960 ) is an american teacher and politician . in november 2012 , takano was elected to the u.s. representative for the 41st district of california . takano was born in riverside , california . he is the son of japanese american parents . he is `` sansei '' , which means that he is the grandson of people born in japan who immigrated to the us . after the japanese bombed pearl harbor , the takano family was relocated and interned from california to `` war relocation camps '' during world war ii . he graduated from harvard university in 1983. takano taught in public school for 23 years .
