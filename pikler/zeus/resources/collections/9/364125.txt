Hirosaki, Aomori    hirosaki , aomori hirosaki is a castle town of the tsugaru clan . the town grew up around hirosaki castle , which was built in 1601. hirosaki uses the swastika ( `` manji '' ) as official symbol . this came from the flag emblem of tsugaru clan in edo period . hirosaki is located in southwest aomori prefecture . it is south of mount iwaki ; and it borders akita prefecture . other websites . - hirosaki city website
