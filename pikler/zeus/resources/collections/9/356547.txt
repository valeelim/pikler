Amuca tribe     the amuca tribe ( ) is one of the nomadic tribes of the oghuz turks who traces back to gündüz bey b.1361 son of sehzade halil . this was a group of people in the ottoman empire which moved from anatolia to the balkans . at the end of 14th century , this clan settled in thrace . today they live in kırklareli province . the first founded village of this tribe was keşirlik , located in the mahya dağı .
