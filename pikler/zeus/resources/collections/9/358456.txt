Altiphrynoides     altiphrynoides is a genus of toads . they are commonly called ethiopian toads . they only live in the highlands of south-central ethiopia in the arussi , bale , and sidamo provinces . both species are threatened by habitat loss . they used to be included in `` nectophrynoides '' . now they are separate because they lay eggs , while `` nectophrynoides '' give birth to fully developed young . references . - itis report altiphrynoides - amphibiaweb.org altiphrynoides malcolmi
