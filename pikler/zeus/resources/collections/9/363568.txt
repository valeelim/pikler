John Joe Nevin     john joseph `` joe '' nevin ( born 7 june , 1989 in mullingar , county westmeath ) is an irish boxer . he competed in the 2008 summer olympics and the 2012 summer olympics and got a silver medal in the 2012 summer olympics . he is currently 3rd aiba world rankings - men 's 56 kg in the world in his division , the bantamweight . on 6th october , 2012 , john joe nevin announces to the public on the saturday night show that he is turning professional
