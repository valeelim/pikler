Jerusalem Historical City Hall Building     the jerusalem historical city hall is an historical building that used to be the city hall of jerusalem . it is in safra square , next to the walls of the old city and jaffa street . the building was built in 1930 during the time of the british mandate for palestine . it was used until 1993 , when a new city hall building was opened next to the old one . other websites . - photos of the old city hall in allaboutjerusalem site .
