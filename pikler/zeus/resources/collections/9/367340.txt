Currimbhoy Ebrahim     sir currimbhoy ebrahim ( or currimbhoy ebrahim ) , 3rd baronet , jp ( born 1903- died 1952 ) , was a prominent businessman , philanthropist and political leader from bombay , british india . he succeeded his father sir mohamedbhoy ebrahim as baronet , and as managing director of many family-owned firms . he also became director or chairman of many charitable trusts in the bombay area and in 1936 , also started the muslim peace and relief committee . in the same year , he became a member of the all india muslim league and an active political leader of this party .
