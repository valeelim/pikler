Michael Murphy     michael george murphy ( born may 5 , 1938 ) is an american movie , stage , voice and television actor who is known for his roles as yale pollack in `` manhattan '' and as the mayor in `` batman returns '' . he has won an gemini award . murphy was born on may 5 , 1938 in los angeles , california . he studied at the university of california at los angeles . murphy was married to wendy crewson from 1988 until they divorced in 2009. they had two children .
