Present tense     present tense is a verb form which is marked by time ( tense ) . the present tense is used for actions in a time which is happening now . in order to explain and understand present tense , it is useful to imagine time as a line on which the past tense , the present and the future tense are positioned . the use of present continuous tense : - a. for something which is happening at the same time of speaking - b. for something which is happening at around the time of speaking but not necessarily exactly at the time of speaking - c. for something which is happening at period around the present ( today , this season )
