Mississippi mud pie     mississippi mud pie is a chocolate-based dessert pie . the treat has a gooey chocolate sauce on top of crumbly chocolate . the pie is usually served with ice cream . it is popular because of the amount of chocolate in each serving . the name `` mississippi mud pie '' comes from the dense cake which looks like the banks of the mississippi river . it is believed that this dish was made by home cooks after world war ii . this is because it was made of simple ingredients that could be found at any supermarket and did not need any special cooking tools .
