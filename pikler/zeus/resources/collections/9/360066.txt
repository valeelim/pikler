Gangnam Style     `` gangnam style '' ( ) is a song by south korean musician , psy . the music video has been watched over 1.5 billion times on youtube , making it the site 's most watched video ever . it is the first video in the website 's history that more than 1 billion people have seen . it has made people make many videos like it but in other locations , named `` parodies '' . barack obama , david cameron , and ban ki-moon tried the song 's dance moves .
