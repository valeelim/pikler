Black rhinoceros     the black rhinocerous ( `` diceros bicornis '' ) , is a species of rhinoceros . it lives in eastern and central africa including kenya , tanzania , cameroon , south africa , namibia , zimbabwe , and angola . the black rhinoceros is not actually black , but dark . it is a browser , with lips that help eating from low branches . the other african rhinoceros is the white rhinoceros ( `` ceratotherium simum '' ) . the white rhinoceros is not white , but lighter coloured .
