Khizar Hayat Tiwana     malik sir khizar hayat tiwana ( 1900-1975 ) , kcsi , obe , was a punjabi politician of british india . he was a member of the unionist party ( punjab ) . he remained premier of the punjab region during 1942-47 , just before the partition and independence of india and pakistan . he was from a tiwana rajput landowning family which had been prominent since the 15th-16th centuries ad . his father malik sir umar hayat tiwana had also been a faithful vassal of the british raj and did much to elevate the family further .
