Gérard Depardieu     gérard xavier marcel depardieu ( born 27 december 1948 ) is a french actor , movie producer and director . he was born in chateauroux , indre . he is one of the most prolific actors in film history , having completed approximately 170 movies since 1967. he won the césar award for best actor for his performances in `` the last metro '' ( 1980 ) . he received the venice film festival award for best actor in `` police '' ( 1985 ) . for `` cyrano de bergerac '' ( 1990 ) he won the cannes film festival for best actor , his second césar award and his first academy award nomination for best actor . depardieu won a golden globe award for best actor for his role in `` green card '' . he lives in belgium . in 2013 he was granted russian citizenship .
