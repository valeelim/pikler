Emile Hirsch     emile davenport hirsch ( born march 13 , 1985 ) is an american television , voice and movie actor . he is known for his role as speed racer in `` speed racer '' and as matthew kiddman in `` the girl next door '' . hirsh was born on march 13 , 1985 in topanga , california . he was raised in los angeles , california . he studied at alexander hamilton high school .
