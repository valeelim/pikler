Conus beatrix     conus beatrix is a species of sea snail . it is a marine gastropod mollusk in the family conidae ( the cone snails and their allies ) . like all species within the genus `` conus '' , these snails are predatory and poisonous . they are capable of `` stinging '' humans , so live ones should be handled carefully or not at all . it is feared by most other aquatic snails , because few other species have the ability to sting . conus beatrix lives only in the seas of the philippines . other websites . - the `` conus '' biodiversity website - cone shells - knights of the sea
