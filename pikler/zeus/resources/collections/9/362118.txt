Paul Giamatti     paul edward valentine giamatti ( born june 6 , 1967 ) is an american movie , television , stage , and voice actor . he played john adams in `` john adams '' , and william hill in `` saving private ryan '' . his first movie was `` private parts '' . he was born in new haven , connecticut and studied at yale university . he has been married to elizabeth cohen since 1997. they had one son .
