Billy Ocean     billy ocean ( born leslie sebastian charles ; 21 january 1950 ) is a songwriter , recording artist and musician . he was born in fyzabad , trinidad to grenadan parents . he has many hit singles . most were put out in the 1980s . he was the most popular british-based r & b singer-songwriter of the early to mid-1980s . on 20 october 2010 , ocean was presented with the lifetime achievement award at the mobo awards . on 29 july 2011 , ocean became a companion of the liverpool institute for performing arts . his title was presented by sir paul mccartney . other websites . - billy ocean official website
