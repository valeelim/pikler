Prionailurus     prionailurus is a genus of four species of small , spotted wild cats found in asia . they are typically forest-dwelling . most are able to swim well ; some species are actually semi-aquatic and feed mainly on fish and other aquatic animals . pocock 's classification of `` prionailurus '' is widely accepted : - leopard cat ( `` prionailurus bengalensis '' ) - rusty-spotted cat ( `` prionailurus rubiginosus '' ) - fishing cat ( `` prionailurus viverrinus '' ) - flat-headed cat ( `` prionailurus planiceps '' )
