Carpenter Painter     the carpenter painter was a red-figure vase painter . he worked in athens . he was active between 515 and 500 bc . he chiefly painted cups . his real name is unknown . `` carpenter painter '' is taken from his depiction of a carpenter at work on a cup in the british museum . few of his works are known . the way he painted nipples , pectorals , hands , garments , and other details identify his work . related pages . - pentathletes ( carpenter painter )
