Julio Alemán     julio méndez alemán ( november 29 , 1933 – april 11 , 2012 ) was a mexican movie , television , stage , voice , and radio actor . alemán was born in morelia , michoacán on november 29 , 1933. he was married to esperanza martínez . they had six children . alemán died in mexico city on april 11 , 2012. he had been diagnosed with lung cancer . his remains were cremated and a memorial service was held on april 12 , 2012 , in mexico city .
