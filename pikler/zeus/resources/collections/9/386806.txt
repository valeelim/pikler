Nasir Hossain (Bangladeshi cricketer)    nasir hossain ( bangladeshi cricketer ) nasir hossain ( born 30 november 1991 ) is a bangladeshi cricketer . he is an all-rounder . he made his one day international ( odi ) debut in august 2011 against zimbabwe . he also made his test debut in 2011. the opposition team was west indies .
