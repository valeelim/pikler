Extreme Pita     extreme pita is a canadian-based fast food chain . it was started by brothers alex rechichi and mark rechichi in 1997. the headquarters are in mississauga , ontario . the company has more than 225 units in canada and the united states . extreme pita serves pita sandwiches , salads , and pizza-style `` flat-baked '' pitas . the chain says these types of products are more healthy than the usual fast-food choices . available ingredients include fresh vegetables , cheeses , meats and vegetarian options such as falafel and hummus .
