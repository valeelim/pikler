2012 Summer Paralympics     the 2012 summer paralympic games , the fourteenth summer paralympic games , took place in london , england , united kingdom.. 4,237 athletes from 164 were at the games . people with these types of disabilities competed at the games : spinal injury , amputee , blindness , cerebral palsy , intellectual disability , and les autres . it is the second sport event held in the united kingdom after the 2012 summer olympics . growing interest in paralympic sport worldwide and great britain 's success during the recent summer olympics made more people want tickets than ever before , breaking many sales records .
