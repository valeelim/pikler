Steven Moffat     steven moffat ( born 18 november 1961 ) is a scottish television writer and producer . he is the writer and director of `` doctor who '' . moffat is also the director , creator , and writer of sherlock . he has written three series for both of his projects . he created several iconic monsters , including the weeping angels . he has won four bafta awards . he is known for being the guy who ruined new who . references . - steven moffat interview bafta guru . bafta guru , n.d. web . 15 jan. 2013 .
