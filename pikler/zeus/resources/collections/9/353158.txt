David Mitchell     david james stuart mitchell ( born 14 july 1974 ) is a british actor , comedian , television personality and television writer . he was born in salisbury , wiltshire . his mother is welsh ; his father is of scottish descent . the family moved to oxford during his late childhood . he studied history at peterhouse , cambridge . mitchell stars in `` would i lie to you ? `` , `` peep show '' and `` that mitchell and webb look '' . he often works with robert webb . mitchell married victoria coren on 17 november 2012 in belsize park , north london . the couple 's daughter was born in may 2015 .
