Larry Flynt     larry claxton flynt , jr. ( born november 1 , 1942 in magoffin county , kentucky ) is an american publisher . he publishes `` hustler '' magazine . in 1978 , flynt was shot in an assassination attempt by joseph paul franklin . that left him paralyzed from the waist down . flynt has a golden wheelchair . franklin was executed in november 2013 by lethal injection . flynt was born on november 1 , 1942 in magoffin county , kentucky . flynt has been married several times . he was married to mary flynt from 1961 until they divorced in 1965. he was married to peggy flynt from 1966 until they divorced in 1969. he was married to kathy flynt from 1970 until they divorced in 1975. he was married to althea leasure from 1976 until her death in 1987. he has been married to elizabeth berrios since 1998. flynt had said that he has bipolar disorder .
