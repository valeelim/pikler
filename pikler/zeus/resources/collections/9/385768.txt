Sylvia Robinson     sylvia robinson ( born march 6 1936-died september 29 2011 ) was an american singer , musician , record label executive and record producer who founded and was ceo of the sugar hill record company in the 1970s . she began recording in 1950 through columbia records . she also had several hit songs , with other musicians and solo , including the sensual song `` pillow talk '' . robinson was born in new york city . she died due to congestive heart failure at age 75 .
