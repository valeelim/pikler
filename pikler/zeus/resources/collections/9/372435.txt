1100 New York Avenue     1100 new york avenue is a high-rise office building in northwest , washington , d.c. in the united states . the 12-floor building was designed by keyes condon florance eichbaum esocoff king and completed in 1991. it is high . the art deco appearance at the north entrance of the building is a former greyhound lines bus station originally constructed in 1940. the office structure was built on top of the bus station . a small display on antique buses is located in the north lobby . other websites . - official website
