Fairview Alpha, Louisiana    fairview alpha , louisiana fairview alpha is an unincorporated community in natchitoches and red river parishes in the state of louisiana . at one time , it was known as messick and alpha . fairview alpha was founded in 1912. there are 5,000 people living there . it is the second-largest community in natchitoches parish . it is near u.s. 71/u.s . 84. according to the usgs , fairview alpha was `` originally named messick for a family that owned a store here , however , when the alpha school ( natchitoches parish ) and fairview school ( red river parish ) were combined in 1912 , the new name came into use '' . transportation . u.s. highways . - u.s. 71 - u.s. 84 louisiana routes . - louisiana 480 - louisiana 486 - louisiana 507 - louisiana 784 - louisiana 787 - louisiana 3163 water . - black lake , fairview alpha-creston border - grand bayou reservoir , fairview alpha red river parish - red river , fairview alpha south border - sims bayou , flat bayou , fairview alpha south education . - fairview alpha elementary , campti , louisiana
