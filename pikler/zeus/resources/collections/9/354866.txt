Terrestrial animal     a terrestrial animal is an animal that lives on land such as dog , cat , an ant or an emu . it can also be used for some species of amphibians that only go back to the water to lay their eggs . it is usually a term to describe the difference between animals that live in water , ( such as lobsters and fish ) , from animals that live on land . books . - clark j. a .
