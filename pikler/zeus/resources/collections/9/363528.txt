Raja (1995 movie)    raja ( 1995 movie ) raja is a 1995 bollywood movie directed by indra kumar . the movie has sanjay kapoor and madhuri dixit in the title roles . the movie became hit at the box office . cast . - sanjay kapoor as raja - madhuri dixit as madhu garewal - paresh rawal as brijnath 'birju ' patangwala - mukesh khanna as rana mahednra pratap garewal - dalip tahil as vishwa garewal - rita bhaduri as sumitra garewal the film had songs made by nadeem-shravan while lyrics were written by sameer . all songs were hit that year .
