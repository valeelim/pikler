Choi Chang-ik     choi chang-ik ( hangul : 최창익 , hanja : 崔昌益 , 1896-1957 ) , also known as choe chang ik , was a north korean soldier and politician . in the early north korean government , choi was deputy prime minister from 1952 to 1956. in a 1956-1957 purge , he was removed from government by forces led by kim il-sung . he was expelled from the korean workers ' party . related pages . - kim du-bong - kim won-bong other websites . - [ http : //100.naver.com/100.nhn ? docid=229252 최창익 [ 崔昌益 , 1896~1956 ] at naver encyclopedia ]
