Mount Whymper (Edward)    mount whymper ( edward ) mount whymper , 2.844 m , is a mountain on the canadian rocky mountains in british columbia , canada . it is in the vermilion pass area , kootenay national park . the mountain is named for its first conqueror , the english alpinist , explorer , writer and engraver edward whymper . in 1901 , whymper and his four guides ( joseph bossoney , christian kaufmann , christian klucker , and james pollinger ) first climbed mount whymper . it was renamed to honour him . before the mountain was named `` mount lefroy '' . whymper was exploring the area sponsored by canadian pacific railway ( cpr ) to promote the canadian rocky mountains and the railway in his conferences . there is another mount whymper in british columbia , on vancouver island . it is named for edward 's brother frederick whymper .
