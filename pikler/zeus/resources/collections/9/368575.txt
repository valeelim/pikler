Maurice LaMarche     maurice lamarche ( born march 30 , 1958 ) is an emmy award winning canadian voice actor and former stand up comedian . he is best known for his voicework in `` futurama '' as kif kroker , as egon spengler in `` the real ghostbusters '' , verminous skumm and duke nukem in `` captain planet and the planeteers '' , big bob pataki in `` hey arnold ! `` , dizzy devil in `` tiny toon adventures '' , the brain in `` animaniacs '' and `` pinky and the brain '' , and his portrayal of mr. freeze in `` '' . lamarche was born in toronto , ontario , canada , but his family moved to timmins , ontario , very soon after he was born . lamarche 's childhood was filled with his `` own little world of cartoons and sixties television '' . other websites . - interview with maurice lamarche on `` the joe cook program '' - maurice lamarche interview on talk radio meltdown
