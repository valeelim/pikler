Elizabeth Bowen     elizabeth dorothea cole bowen ( 1899-1973 ) was a british novelist and short story writer . she was born in an anglo-irish family in dublin , ireland and wrote many books and stories that are set in ireland . however , after 1927 , she lived mostly in london , england . she began to write at a very young age . bowen 's works include `` encounters '' ( 1923 ) , `` the hotel '' ( 1927 ) , `` the house in paris '' ( 1935 ) , `` the last september '' ( 1929 ) , `` seven winters '' ( 1942 ) , `` bowen 's court '' ( 1942 ) , `` the heat of the day '' ( 1949 ) and many others .
