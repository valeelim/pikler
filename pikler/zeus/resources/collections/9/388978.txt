Hawthorne effect     the hawthorne effect is a form of reactivity . when the group which is under study knows about the study and their behavior changes after knowing that they are being studied , this change of behavior is the hawthorne effect . for example : a group of 10 people are being studied for their health after exercising . without knowing that they are being studied , they do or not do exercise , but after knowing that they are being studied they regularly do exercise .
