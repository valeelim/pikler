Cocker Spaniel     cocker spaniel is the name for two dog breeds of the spaniel type : the english cocker spaniel and the american cocker spaniel . originally , these dogs were bred in england for hunting eurasian woodcock . cocker spaniels are popular family pets . the english version is usually brown and ginger in color . their fur is very soft and their noses are big . they have very big floppy ears . there are two version of cocker spaniels , show dogs and hunters . they are the smallest of the sporting dogs . they are bred from an earlier 19th century dog called field spaniels . the smallest dogs in litters were called `` cockers '' . this was because they were small enough to go under hedgerows where larger dogs could not go . they could also move through brambles and bushes to flush woodcocks into the open . the smaller dogs became the american cockers while the larger dogs became the english variety . < br >
