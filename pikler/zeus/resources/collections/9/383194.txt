Both Members of This Club     both members of this club is an oil painting by george bellows . it hangs in the national gallery of art , washington dc . it dates to 1909 and measures 115 x 160.5 cm ( 45 1/4 x 63 3/16 in . ) `` both members of this club '' was inspired by the fights bellows attended at tom sharkey 's athletic club in new york . at the time , public boxing matches were illegal in the city . organizations like sharkey 's made fighters temporary members of the `` club '' on the night of the event . this allowed the men to fight and not break the law .
