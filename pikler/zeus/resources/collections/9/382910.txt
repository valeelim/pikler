Kantaji Temple     kantojiu temple ( ) at kantanagar is a late-medieval hindu temple in dinajpur district , bangladesh . it was built by maharaja pran nath ; its construction started in 1702 and ended in 1752 , during the reign of his son maharaja ramnath . it has one of the best examples of terracotta architecture in bangladesh . it once had nine spires , but all were destroyed in an earthquake that happened in 1897 .
