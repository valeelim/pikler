Bhubaneshwar     bhubaneshwar is the capital city of the indian state of odisha . it is the largest city in odisha . bhubaneswar means `` lord of the world '' . many temples are present in this city . so , it is also called `` temple city of india '' . it is a religious centre in orissa from the middle ages . it is a planned city of modern india . in 1948 , bhubaneshwar became political capital of odisha . before this , it was cuttack . bhubaneshwar and cuttack are together called `` twin-cities '' of orissa .
