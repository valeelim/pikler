Pyxicephalidae     pyxicephalidae is a family of frogs . they are found in sub-saharan africa . pyxicephalidae has two subfamilies , with a total of 13 genera . this family was formerly considered part of the family ranidae . family pyxicephalidae - subfamily cacosterninae '' - genus `` amietia '' ( 15 species ) - genus `` anhydrophryne '' ( 3 species ) - genus `` arthroleptella '' ( 3 species ) – moss frogs - genus `` cacosternum '' ( 12 species ) - genus `` ericabatrachus '' ( 1 species ) - genus `` microbatrachella '' ( 1 species ) – micro frog - genus `` natalobatrachus '' ( 1 species ) - genus `` nothophryne '' ( 1 species ) - genus `` poyntonia '' ( 1 species ) - genus `` strongylopus '' ( 10 species ) - genus `` tomopterna '' ( 11 species ) - subfamily pyxicephalinae '' - genus `` aubria '' ( 2 species ) – masako fishing frog , brown ball frog - genus `` pyxicephalus '' ( 3 species ) – african bull frogs , pixie frog
