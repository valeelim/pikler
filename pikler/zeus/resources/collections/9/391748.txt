Law &amp; Order: LA    law & amp ; order : la law & order : la was an american television police drama . it was a spin-off of `` law & order '' , being the fourth spin-off . it ran on nbc from october 18 , 2010 to july 11 , 2011. it is the only american `` law & order '' show to be set outside of new york city , being set in los angeles . `` law & order : la '' followed the same format as `` law & order '' . the first half of the show showed the crime , the police looking for the suspect , and the suspect being arrested ( the `` law '' part ) . the second part of the show showed the trial and whether or not the suspect did the crime ( the `` order '' part ) . the show was adapted to fit the los angeles criminal justice system . other websites . - `` law & order : la '' at the internet movie database
