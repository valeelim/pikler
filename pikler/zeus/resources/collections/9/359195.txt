Crash Bandicoot: The Huge Adventure    crash bandicoot : the huge adventure crash bandicoot : the huge adventure , released in europe as crash bandicoot xs and in japan as crash bandicoot advance , is a video game published by universal interactive studios ( konami in japan ) . it was developed by vicarious visions for the game boy advance . it was released in north america on february 25 , 2002 , in europe on march 15 , 2002 and in japan on july 18 , 2002. other websites . - `` crash bandicoot : the huge adventure '' at gamefaqs
