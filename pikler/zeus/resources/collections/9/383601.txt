Mario Pinball Land     mario pinball land is a pinball video game for the game boy advance console . it was developed by fuse games and published by nintendo . it was released on august 26 , 2004 in japan , on october 4 , 2004 in north america , and on november 26 , 2004 in europe . mario and princess peach are visiting a funfair . at one point , peach goes into a cannon . the cannon is operated by goombas , who shoot her to bowser 's castle . to save her , mario uses a machine that turns him into a ball . mario has to play pinball through different worlds to save peach . during the game , mario has to get stars . these stars allow him to go further into the game by opening doors . a total of 35 stars are available . five different worlds are played in the game . at the end of each world , mario faces a boss .
