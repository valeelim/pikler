Ken Jeong     kendrick kang-joh “ ken ” jeong ( born july 13 , 1969 ) or dr. ken is an american comedian , actor , and physician . he is known for his roles as leslie chow in `` the hangover '' , `` the hangover part ii '' , and in `` the hangover part iii '' . he also starred as ben chang in `` community '' . jeong was born in detroit , michigan on july 13 , 1969. he studied at duke university and at university of north carolina school of medicine . jeong is married to tran ho . they have two daughters .
