Desert froglet     the desert froglet ( `` crinia deserticola '' ) is a species of frog . they are part of the myobatrachidae family . they live in australia . they live in savannas and deserts . other websites . - hero j.-m ; horner p. & retallick r. 2004. crinia deserticola . 2006 iucn red list of threatened species downloaded on 23 july 2007 .
