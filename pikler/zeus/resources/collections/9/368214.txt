Anthony Bell     anthony bell ( born 1973 , united states ) , sometimes known as tony bell is an american animator , movie director , screenwriter and story artist . he started his career as a character layout artist in `` the simpsons '' in the early nineties and he also directed a few episodes in `` rugrats '' . bell also directed some episode for the adult swim animated tv show , `` the boondocks '' . his most recent and famous work is for being the co-director of the 2010 computer-animated comedy-drama movie , `` alpha and omega '' ( along with ben gluck ) , where he also served as story artist . anthony is now working as the director on a new animated comedy-drama movie , called `` norm of the north '' . he also wrote the story for the movie .
