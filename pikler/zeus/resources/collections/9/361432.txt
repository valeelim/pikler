Daley Center     the richard j. daley center or the daley center is a civic center in the city of chicago , illinois . the center is named after mayor richard j. daley . the building is 648 feet ( 198 meters ) 30 store building . the daley center was opened in 1965. the center has a sculpture , `` chicago picasso '' made by pablo picasso . other websites . - city of chicago - cook county - richard j. daley center on emporis - daley plaza eternal flame
