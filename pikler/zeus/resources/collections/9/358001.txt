David Hasselhoff     david hasselhoff ( born july 17 , 1952 ) is an american actor and singer . he is best known for his lead roles in the popular 1980s show `` knight rider '' and in the 1990s series `` baywatch '' . he was also in the eleventh season of `` dancing with the stars '' in september 2010. hasselhoff was born in baltimore , maryland and grew up in jacksonville , florida and atlanta , georgia .
