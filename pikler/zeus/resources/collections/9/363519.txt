Manuel Roxas     manuel acuna roxas ( january 1 , 1892 – april 15 , 1948 ) was a philippine politician . he was the president of the philippine commonwealth ( 1946 ) and the first president of the third philippine republic ( 1946 - 1948 ) . he was a minister of the philippine commonwealth , secretary of finance ( 1938–1941 ) and executive secretary of third philippine republic ( 1942 ) . the philippine commonwealth was abolished after he was elected as president of the third philippine republic . other websites . - the philippine presidency project - a country study : philippines - manuel a. roxas elementary school
