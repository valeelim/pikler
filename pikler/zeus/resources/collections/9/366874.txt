Sylvia Kristel     sylvia maria kristel ( 28 september 1952 18 october 2012 ) was a dutch erotic actress who acted in many movies . she was most notable for her role in the `` emmanuelle '' movies from the 1970s through to the early 1990s . the most successful film in her career was `` private lessons '' ( 1981 ) . she played the housekeeper who initiates a 15-year-old to the wonders of sex . the film grossed over $ 50,000,000 worldwide . kristel was born on 28 september 1952 in utrecht , netherlands . she died on the afternoon of 18 october 2012 in amsterdam , netherlands from oesophageal and lung cancer , aged 60. she is survived by her son . in september 2006 , kristel 's autobiography `` nue '' ( `` nude '' ) was published in france . it was translated into english as `` undressing emmanuelle : a memoir '' , by fourth estate , 2007. isbn 978-0007256952. she told of her turbulent personal life blighted by addictions to drugs and alcohol . her quest for a father figure led to some destructive relationships with older men . the book received some positive reviews .
