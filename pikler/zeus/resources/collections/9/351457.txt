Zap Mama     zap mama is a belgian musical group founded and led by marie daulne . she says that her mission is to be a bridge between the european and the african and bring the two cultures together with music . `` what i would like to do is bring sounds from africa and bring it to the western world , because i know that through sound and through beats , that people discover a new culture , a new people , a new world . '' they sing in french and english with deep african roots . zap mama performed their first concert in 1989. in 1991 , the group recorded their first record , `` zap mama '' , at studio daylight in brussels , belgium . the album was released by crammed discs . discography . - `` zap mama '' ( 1991 ) - `` adventures in afropea 1 '' ( 1993 ) - `` sabsylma '' ( 1994 ) - `` seven '' ( 1997 ) - `` a ma zone '' ( 1999 ) - `` ancestry in progress '' ( 2004 ) - `` supermoon '' ( 2007 ) - `` recreation '' ( 2009 )
