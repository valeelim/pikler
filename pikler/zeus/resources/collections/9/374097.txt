Edward Joseph Kelly     edward joseph kelly ( may 1 , 1876 – october 20 , 1950 ) was an american politician who served as mayor of chicago , illinois ( 1933–1947 ) for the demoratic party . kelly was born on may 1 , 1876 in chicago , illinois . he was married to mary edmond roche from 1910 until her death in 1918. they had one child . then he was married to margaret ellen kirk from 1922 until his death in 1950. they had three children . kelly died on october 20 , 1950 in chicago , illinois from heart failure , aged 74 .
