Survey of Israel     survey of israel - soi ( ) is the national israeli institute of geodesy and map-making . it was founded in 1920 as the survey of the british mandate of palestine . it is in tel aviv with branches in jerusalem , beer sheva , haifa and nazareth . in 2011 the israeli government decided to transfer all units into a new building to be built in jerusalem . other websites . - survey of israel - survey of israel 's heritage website
