Veterinary medicine     veterinary medicine is a branch of medicine that deals with the health of animals . it is the study and practice of preventing , diagnosing and treating disease and injuries in animals . veterinary medicine covers all species of animals , including both domesticated animals and wild animals . people who study and practice veterinary medicine professionally are called veterinarians ( often known as `` vets '' ) . veterinary science tries to help human health by monitoring and controlling zoonotic diseases ( infectious diseases transmitted from animals to humans ) . related pages . - zoology
