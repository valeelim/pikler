Les Misérables (movie)    les misérables ( movie ) les misérables is a 2012 british musical-drama movie . it stars anne hathaway as fantine , russell crowe as javert , and hugh jackman as jean valjean . the movie is based on the musical of the same name , which , in turn , is based on the 1862 victor hugo novel , `` les misérables '' . critics gave the movie good reviews . hathaway 's performance won her a screen actors guild award , a golden globe award , an academy award , and many other awards . jackman won a golden globe award for his performance .
