Fibromyalgia     fibromyalgia is a very severe condition with chronic and extensive pain . the exact causes are not known . however , symptoms are not totally restricted to the pain . there are also sleep disturbances , joint stiffness and severe fatigue . the problem sometimes exists along with depression , stress and anxiety . fibromyalgia affects mostly women , though men can also be affected . adults are usually the victims , although children may also be affected .
