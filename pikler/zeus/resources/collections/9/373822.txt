Seoul Lantern Festival     the seoul lantern festival ( ) is a festival that takes place in seoul , south korea . it shows various themes through lanterns . it is hosted by the seoul metropolitan government and is organized by the seoul tourism organization . it has been celebrated every year since 2009 and has officially become an annual event . it is usually held from early in november and lasts 2 weeks . in 2012 , as 2.57 million people visited the festival , it gets to become the representative festival of seoul . other websites . - official website - with photographs of each theme
