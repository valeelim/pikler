Pablo Zibes     pablo zibes ( born in 1971 in buenos aires ) is an argentinian actor and mime . he has lived in germany since 1995. he trained as an actor at the municipal school of performing arts ( or emad ) in buenos aires in 1989-1991. after that , he became a professional mime . he improved his mime skills through courses in argentina and europe `` scuola teatro dimitri '' , switzerland . he worked as a mime and street performer in europe and asia . he performs mostly for festivals , events , conventions and television . he does walk-around entertainment as different characters , like a robot or wind-up toy soldier . prizes and distinctions . - 1999 : festival in koblenz , germany - 2000 : , germany - 2003 : grazie mantua festival , italy other websites . - pantomime company
