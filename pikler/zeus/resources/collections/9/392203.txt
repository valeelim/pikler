Daniela Mercury     daniela mercuri de almeida ( born july 28 , 1965 ) , known by her stage name daniela mercury , is a brazilian singer , songwriter and record producer . she is one of the best known brazilian female singers , selling over 20 million albums worldwide . mercury was born on july 28 , 1965 , in salvador , bahia . her mother is liliana mercuri , a social worker of descent . her father is antónio fernando de abreu ferreira de almeida , a -born industrial mechanic . her four siblings include tom , cristiana , , and marcos . she attended both the ana néri school and the colégio baiano . when mercury was eight years old , she began taking dance lessons . at age 13 , influenced by elis regina , she decided to become a singer . she started singing in local bars in 1980. mercury went to the , where she enrolled in the dance school in 1984. a year later she married electronic engineer zalther póvoas . she has two children in this marriage , gabriel almeida póvoas . and giovana almeida póvoas born in 1985 and 1986 respectively .
