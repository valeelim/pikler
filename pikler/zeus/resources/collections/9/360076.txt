Colin McFarlane     colin mcfarlane ( born september 15 , 1961 ) is a british voice , television , and movie actor . he is known for his role as commissioner gillian b. loeb in `` batman begins '' and `` the dark knight '' . in television he has appeared in `` jonathan 's creek '' , `` the fast show '' , and the `` black books '' . he has starred in animated movies or television programs . he is also known for providing the voice of god in `` testament : the bible in animation '' . mcfarlane was born in clapton , london , england on september 15 , 1961. he is currently not married .
