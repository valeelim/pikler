Archaeobatrachia     archaeobatrachia is a suborder of anura . it has various primitive frogs and toads . as the name literally suggests , these are the most primitive frogs . many of the species ( 28 in total ) show certain body features which are not in other frogs and toads . they are mostly found in eurasia , new zealand , the philippines and borneo . in addition , the family ascaphidae is found in the pacific northwest of the united states . they are only represented by two species .
