The Power of Four     `` the power of four '' is a joint anthem for the british and irish lions , a rugby union team . it was created by neil myers . from the four corners of our lands < br > we 're united , hand in hand < br > together < br > we 're stronger < br > we join and proud we stand < br > now the day has come , we are one < br > standing tall for our lions ' call < br > we 're stronger < br > together < br > we are the power of four .
