The Saturday Evening Post     the saturday evening post is an american magazine . it started publication in 1897. its origins are traced to benjamin franklin 's colonial newspaper `` the pennsylvania gazette '' . the `` post '' was famous for its covers by norman rockwell and the single-panel comic strip `` hazel '' by ted key . contents included short fiction , poetry , and humor . jack london 's novel `` the call of the wild '' was first published in the `` post '' in 1903 .
