Bob Rathbun     bob rathbun ( born november 25 , 1954 in wakefield , rhode island ) is an american sportscaster . he currently is the voice of the atlanta hawks of the nba and the atlanta dream of the wnba . he was the voice of the atlanta braves for many years . he calls college football and basketball for fsn south and college basketball games for jefferson-pilot/raycom sports . rathbun also called games for the washington bulletts , baltimore orioles , tidewater tides , detroit tigers , and richmond braves .
