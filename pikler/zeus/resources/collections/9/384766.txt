Search Engine Watch     search engine watch ( sew ) is a website with news and information about search engines and search engine marketing . search engine watch website was started by danny sullivan in 1996. in 1997 , sullivan sold it to mecklermedia ( now jupitermedia ) . in 2005 the website and search engine strategies were sold to incisive media for $ 43 million . on november 30 , 2006 danny sullivan left search engine watch . rebecca lieb became editor-in-chief next month . google 's matt cutts has named search engine watch `` a must read .
