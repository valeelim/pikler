Opel Antara     the opel antara is a compact cuv from the general motors ' german automaker opel since 2006. the antara replaced the opel frontera a badge engineered version of the compact isuzu rodeo suv . it was sold as the second generation saturn vue , as a fleet-only chevrolet captiva sport in america , and as a vauxhall model in the uk . the vue was discontinued after saturn went out of business . the captiva sport was discontinued upon the introduction of the trax . the antara is expected to be replaced with a rebadged buick envision and sold as opel antara b .
