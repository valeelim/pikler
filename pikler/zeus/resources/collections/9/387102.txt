Scott Mills     scott mills ( born 28 march 1974 ) is a british radio dj . he has his own daily show on bbc radio 1. he is also a television presenter and an actor . he was born in southampton . scott mills began working at 16 in a local radio in hampshire . he joined bbc radio 1 in 1998. in may 2004 was given a slot on weekday early-evenings to cover for sara cox who was about to have a baby . he now has a slot everyday in the afternoon . mills is openly gay .
