Cellulitis     cellulitis is an infection of the skin , caused by bacteria . very often , the face is affected . the disease should not be confused with cellulite . cellulitis is caused by bacteria , usually staphylococcus or streptococcus , which can get through a break in the skin . these breaks can be caused by a wound , cracks in the skin , insect bites , or problems with the bodies immune system . the infection causes the skin to become red , hot and painful . it can also cause a fever . treatment is usually by taking antibiotic drugs .
