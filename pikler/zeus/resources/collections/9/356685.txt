Mule deer     mule deer ( `` odocileus hemionus '' ) is a species of deers . they are sometimes called black-tailed deer . they live in western north america . their name was given to them because of their mule-like ears . there are many subspecies , including the black-tailed deer . unlike the white-tailed deer , mule deer lives on land west of the missouri river . they also live in the rocky mountains region of north america . mule deer have been introduced to argentina . other websites . - smithsonian national museum of natural history species account-mule deer - video of arizona mule deer
