Medical transcription     transcription means to listen to a recorded voice and to write down what it is saying . medical transcription means to do this for a hospital , doctor 's office or other medical place . when doctors and nurses help someone in the hospital , they do not have time to write down what they saw or heard . so later they record what they saw and heard , so someone else can write it down . the notes are then added to the hospital 's history . the person who does this as a profession is called a medical transcriptionist . people who do medical transcription normally work in hospitals , doctor 's offices , old age homes , and other places where doctors and nurses work . there is huge rise in outsourcing of medical transcription to other companies . in the united states this field of business is worth us $ 10 to $ 25 billion and growing 15 percent each year . because of rising costs of medical transcription it became recently popular to use different computer products ( software ) that can reduce transcription costs by as much as 50 % . there is no available research about market share between in-house transcription , outsourcing and software based .
