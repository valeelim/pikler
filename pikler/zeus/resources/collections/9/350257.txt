Weiyang District     weiyang district ( , pinyin : wèiyāng qū ) is in the north of xi'an , shaanxi , china . xianyang municipality is to the west and north . lianhu district and xincheng district are to the south . baqiao district is to the east . the name is from weiyang square . the district covers . as of november 2010 , 806,811 people live there .
