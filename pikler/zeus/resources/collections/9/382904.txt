Chhera Island     chhera island ( bengali : ) , also called cheradia island or cheridia island , is an extension of st. martin 's island in bangladesh , but cut off from it by sea during high tides . during low tide period , the chhera island can be reached by walking for about two and a half hours from st. martin 's island . the most popular way of getting there is by motorboat . corals can be found all over the island . it has one small green area where bushes grow . no permanent settlement exists except for tourists .
