Beit HaNassi     beit hanassi ( `` president 's house '' in hebrew ) , also known as mishkan hanassi ( `` presidential residence '' ) is the main residence of the president of israel . it is in jerusalem . the building was opened in 1971 , after the israeli government approved a state home for the president . in 2011 , renovations were started after defects were found in the building . other websites . - president of israel - official website
