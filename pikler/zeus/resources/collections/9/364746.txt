Resident Evil 6     resident evil 6 is a survival horror video game made by capcom for the playstation 3 and xbox 360 game consoles . it 's the next game in the `` resident evil '' video game series for the playstation 3 and xbox 360. the demo for resident evil 6 received bad reviews from ign . the actual game itself received mixed reviews from reviewers such as gamespot , ign and g4 .
