Margaret Whiting     margaret whiting ( july 21 , 1924 - january 10 , 2011 ) was a us pop music singer . she recorded more than 700 songs , and sold millions of records . whiting was born in detroit in 1924 into a musical family . her father , dick whiting , was a song writer , and her mother , eleanor whiting , managed several performers . she began her recording career while she was still a teenager . her father had worked with johnny mercer , and mercer wrote songs and recorded with whiting . their duet , `` baby it 's cold outside '' , was huge hit .
