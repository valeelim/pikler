Brookline, Massachusetts    brookline , massachusetts brookline is a city in massachusetts . it is west of boston , massachusetts . the 2010 united states census reported that brookline 's population is 58,732. the city was settled in 1638. brookline was incorporated in 1705. notable people . - michael bloomberg , former mayor of new york city - members of the kennedy family - john f. kennedy , former president of the united states - ted kennedy , former united states senator - eunice kennedy shriver - kathleen agnes kennedy cavendish - rosemary kennedy - joseph p. kennedy , jr. - patricia kennedy - robert f. kennedy , former united states senator and united states attorney general - jean kennedy smith - michael dukakis , the 1988 presidential election democratic candidate and former governor of massachusetts - mike wallace , former journalist
