Cynthia Nixon     cynthia ellen nixon ( born april 9 , 1966 ) is an american movie and television actress whose most well known role comes from playing miranda hobbes in the hbo series `` sex and the city '' ( 1998-2004 ) . she has had other movie and tv starring acts as well . in 2016 , she will play nancy reagan in the television movie `` killing reagan '' .
