The Pointer Sisters     the pointer sisters are an american r & b girl group . they were founded in 1969 and are based out of oakland , california who first achieved mainstream success in the 1970s and 1980s . their genres are bebop , disco , blues , country and rock . the group were a duo when they started , though they became trio , then a quartet in the later years . the pointer sisters had their greatest and highest success in the 1980s . they are best-known for a song called `` i 'm so excited '' .
