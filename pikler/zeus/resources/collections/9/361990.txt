Patrick O'Neal (sportscaster)    patrick o'neal ( sportscaster ) patrick o'neal ( born september 14 , 1967 , santa monica , california ) , is an american sportscaster . he works with prime ticket and fsn west . he has covered the los angeles lakers , clippers , angels , and kings . he also was a fill-in studio host for major league baseball on fox when jeanne zelasko was on maternity leave . in 2012 , o'neal was the in-game highlights host for the national football league ( nfl ) on fox . he was a sideline reporter for the `` nfl on fox '' in 2005. he was a field reporter for the 2005 nlcs on fox .
