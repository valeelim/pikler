Cheech Marin     richard anthony `` cheech '' marin ( born july 13 , 1946 ) is an american comedian , movie , television , stage , voice actor and writer . he was part of the comedy duo cheech and chong along with fellow comedian tommy chong . he played ramone in the disney-pixar animated movies `` cars '' and `` cars 2 '' . marin was born richard anthony marin on july 13 , 1946 in los angeles , california . marin was married to darlene morley from 1975 until they divorced in 1984. they had one child . he was married to patti heid from 1986 until they divorced . they had 2 children . he has been married to natasha rubin since 2009. marin lives in malibu , california . he is mexican-american .
