Frederick     frederick ( also spelled frederic ) is a masculine given name that means `` peaceful ruler '' . the name is the english form of the german name `` friedrich '' . this name ranked among the top 100 masculine names for the united states between 1880 and 1957. after that , the name became less popular . the name was number 536 in popularity in the united states in the late 2000s . at the same time it was number 99 in england . as `` frederic '' , the name was used on a hurricane that struck the gulf coast of the united states in september 1979 .
