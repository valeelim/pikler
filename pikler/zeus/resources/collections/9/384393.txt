Alexandrite     alexandrite is a gemstone . it is a type of chrysoberyl . different kinds of light make it look different colors . that is called the `` alexandrite effect '' . a gemstone similar to alexandrite can be made in a laboratory . that kind of stone is called synthetic or simulated alexandrite . these are not made of chrysoberyl . people make these because alexandrite is expensive , and the synthetic alexandrite costs less . one story says that alexandrite was named in honor of alexander ii of russia . alexander was not yet tsar at the time . alexandrite has been found in russia , brazil , india , madagascar , sri lanka , and india . alexandrite is one of the birthstones for june .
