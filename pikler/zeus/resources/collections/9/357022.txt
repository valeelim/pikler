Colegio de la Preciosa Sangre     colegio de la preciosa sangre is a chilean private state-subsidized primary and secondary school in the coastal town of pichilemu . it is also known by the name preciosa sangre . the school ranks first in cardenal caro province , based on the scores from the `` prueba de selección universitaria '' ( psu ) test , taken each december . the school has been qualified as the `` best school in pichilemu '' , and `` a regional icon '' by region of o'higgins newspaper `` el rancagüino '' .
