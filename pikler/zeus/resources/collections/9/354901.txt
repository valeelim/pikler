Pentathletes (Carpenter Painter)    pentathletes ( carpenter painter ) pentathletes is a painting on an ancient greek terracotta drinking cup from athens . it is said to be the work of the carpenter painter . it is dated about 510500 bc . the cup measures 4 5/16 x 15 x 13 3/16 in . in the circular painting ( tondo ) at the bottom of the bowl of the cup , a youth pulls an older man to him for a kiss . the outside of the cup depicts bearded men and youths exercising with discus and javelin . a youth in a long robe plays music . the pictures reflect the interests of the aristocrats who would have used such a cup at a drinking party .
