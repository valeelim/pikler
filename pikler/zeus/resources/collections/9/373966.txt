John Blake Rice     john blake rice ( may 28 , 1809 – december 17 , 1874 ) was an american actor , theatrical producer and politician . he was the mayor of chicago , illinois ( 1865–1869 ) . he was a republican . rice was born in easton , maryland on may 28 , 1809. he was raised in annapolis , maryland and in chicago , illinois . rice died on december 17 , 1874 in norfolk , virginia from liver cancer , aged 65 .
