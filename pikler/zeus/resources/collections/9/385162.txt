Laura Cardoso     laurinda de jesus cardoso baleroni ( better known as laura cardoso , b . 13 september 1927 in são paulo ) is a brazilian actress . cardoso was a pioneer of television in brazil . she has acted in teleteatros , series and telenovelas since the 1950s on the defunct tv tupi and later in syndication . she starred in more than 50 telenovelas . she is the widow of actor , writer , director and television producer fernando baleroni . they had two daughters , fátima and fernanda .
