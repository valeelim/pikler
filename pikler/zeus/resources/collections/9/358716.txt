Ballet blanc     a ballet blanc ( literally , `` white ballet '' ) is a ballet in the style of the 19th century romantic ballet . the ballerinas and the female members of the `` corps de ballet '' wear bell-shaped , calf-length , white ballet tutus . the second acts of `` la sylphide '' and `` giselle '' are representatives of the `` ballet blanc '' . fokine 's `` les sylphides '' is a 20th-century homage to the `` ballet blanc '' .
