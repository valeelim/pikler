Archaeozoology     archaeozoology is the study of animals in archaeological contexts . mostly , it studies the remains left behind when an animal dies . these are mostly bones and teeth . sometimes hair , skin or other body parts are found and can be studied . archeaozoology also studies the animals ' effect on the environment and their relationships with humans . the people who study archaeozoology are called archaeozoologists . their training can be as biologists or archaeologists . related pages . - zoology - archaeology
