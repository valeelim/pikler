Parable of the Lost Sheep     the parable of the lost sheep is one of the parables of jesus . it is found in the gospel of matthew at 18:12–14 and the gospel of luke at 15:3–7 . it also appears in the non-canonical gospel of thomas . the parable is one of three that tell of loss and redemption . the others are the lost coin and the prodigal son . the lost sheep tells of a shepherd who leaves his 99 sheep to search for one sheep that is lost . joel b. green writes that `` these parables are ... about god , ... their aim is to lay bare the nature of the divine response to the recovery of the lost . '' the parable has been depicted often in art .
