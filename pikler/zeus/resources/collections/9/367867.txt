David Hyde Pierce     david hyde pierce ( born april 3 , 1959 ) is an american movie , television , stage , radio , voice actor , writer , and comedian who is known for his role as dr. niles crane in `` frasier '' . he won four emmy awards for his role as dr. niles crane and also won two screen actors guild award , a tca award , and a tony award . pierce was born on april 3 , 1959 in saratoga springs , new york . he studied at yale university . pierce is openly gay . he has been married to brian hargrove since 2008 .
