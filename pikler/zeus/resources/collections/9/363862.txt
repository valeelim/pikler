Paul Kennedy     paul kennedy is an american sportscaster with fox sports florida . he does play-by-play for games for the orlando magic . kennedy has also called nfl europe and nfl games for fox . he currently calls college football games for fox sports net . kennedy is the host of a show for the tampa bay lightning `` inside the lightning '' . he also hosted prime time noles . he also has called college basketball games for fsn . kennedy was the host of miami dolphins monday night magazine and bucs diaries featuring head coach tony dungy . he also served as the play-by-play voice for the alabama crimson tide football and basketball teams . kennedy has also been involved in baseball . he was the studio host for the baseball network and the florida marlins and called select play-by-play for the marlins .
