Lungless salamanders     the plethodontidae is a family of salamanders . they do not have lungs . they are called lungless salamanders . they live in british columbia , brazil , sardinia , alps and south korea . they are the largest groups of salamanders . there are over 350 lungless salamanders . most of them are terrestrial and are active in daytime . lungless salamanders may communicate with their nose . slender salamanders are found in the pacific coast . they are sometimes called `` worm salamanders '' . this is because they have slimmer ( skinny ) bodies than most salamanders . if touched , slender salamanders will bounce on the ground and then run away . other websites . - amphibiaweb - plethodontidae - tree of life : plethodontidae - caudata culture species database - plethodontidae
