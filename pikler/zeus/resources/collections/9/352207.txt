Nagpur     nagpur is a city in state of maharashtra in india . it is in the centre of india . in the marathi language , `` nag '' means snake and `` pur '' means city . the city is called nagpur because of the `` nag '' river in this city which flows like a snake . it is the winter capital of maharashtra . after mumbai and pune , nagpur is the third largest city in maharashtra . it is a very clean city . it was selected as the second greenest city of india . many oranges are grown in this city , so it is also called `` orange city '' .
