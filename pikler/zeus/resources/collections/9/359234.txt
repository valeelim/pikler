Gary Barlow     gary barlow obe ( born 20 january 1971 ) is an english singer-songwriter , pianist and record producer . he is frontman , lead singer and main songwriter of the boy band take that . in 1990 , gary barlow became a member of the new founded boy band take that . he is the main songwriter of the band . after the band broke up in 1996 , barlow started a solo career . he released two solo albums , `` open road '' and `` twelve months , eleven days '' . he also wrote songs for other singers . take that reformed in 2005. barlow was voted as the best british songwriter of all time in 2009. he was a judge at the tv show `` the x factor '' from 2011 until 2013. barlow organized the concert for the diamond jubilee of elizabeth ii in 2012. in may 2012 , he released the album `` sing '' with the commonwealth band . the solo album `` since i saw you last '' was released in november 2013. barlow and his wife dawn have three children . their fourth child was stillborn in august 2012 .
