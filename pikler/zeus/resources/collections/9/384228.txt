Bazargan     bazargan ( ; also romanized as bāzargān , bāzarqān , and bāzergān ) is a city in iran . it is the capital of bazargan district , in maku county , west azerbaijan province . the city is the most important iranian ground border for importing and exporting . at the 2006 census , its population was 9,047 , in 2,126 families . related pages . - mehdi bazargan
