Lake Shore Drive     lake shore drive is a freeway-standard expressway in chicago , illinois . it runs along the shore of lake michigan . the highway passes navy pier all the through the john hancock center . the drive is a dangerous area during blizzards . the drive was named leif ericson drive ( after leif ericson ) until it was renamed in 1946. other websites . - lake shore drive redux from forgottenchicago.com - the campaign to depave lake shore drive - historic , current & average travel times for lake shore drive
