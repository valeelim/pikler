Shah Dara     shah dara or shahdara or shahdara bagh ( urdu : شاہدره ) , is a suburb of lahore , pakistan . it is near the ravi river . the area is famous for two historic mughal architectural sites . these are the mughal gardens and the tomb of jahangir , the mughal emperor and his wife nur jahan . shah dara also has other tombs and gardens from medieval times . present day shahdara is a busy area with a market and factories nearby .
