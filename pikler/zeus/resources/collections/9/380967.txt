Isabel     isabel ( or isabelle ) is a female given name . it comes from the hebrew name `` elisheva '' . `` elisheva '' is often translated to `` elizabeth '' , of which isabel is a division . there are many royal figures or queens with the name or its variants . there are also many other famous people by isabel and its related names . as `` isabel '' , this name was used on two atlantic tropical cyclones . the 2003 storm was so damaging that the name was later retired .
