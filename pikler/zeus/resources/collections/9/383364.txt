Steve-O     steve-o ( born stephen gilchrist glover ; june 13 , 1974 ) is a british-canadian-american stunt performer , comedian , actor , and tv personality . he is known for being one of the cast members of `` jackass '' . steve-o was born on june 13 , 1974 in london , england to a canadian mother and to an american father . he was raised in darien , connecticut and in miami , florida . steve-o studied at the university of miami and at the ringling brothers and barnum & bailey clown college . he was in rehab several times . other websites . - steve-o at myspace ( blog also syndicated on livejournal )
