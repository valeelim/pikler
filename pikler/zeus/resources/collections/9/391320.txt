Tokelauan language     tokelauan is a polynesian language . it is closely related to tuvaluan . people who speak tokelauan and people who speak tuvaluan can understand each other . tokelauan is spoken in tokelau , american samoa , and by tokelauan people in new zealand.. it is one of the official languages of tokelau ( the other is english ) . tokelauan is written in the roman alphabet , using 15 letters : a , e , i , o , u , f , g , k , l , m , n , p , h , t , and v. other websites . - ethnologue report on tokelauan - mini-dictionary sponsored by tokelau magic - `` te vaka '' â€“ a popular new zealand music group that sings in the tokelauan language
