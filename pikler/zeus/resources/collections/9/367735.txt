Addison's disease    addison 's disease addison 's disease is a rare and chronic endocrine disorder discovered by thomas addison . u.s. president john f. kennedy is probably one of the most famous sufferers though his condition was kept secret during his presidency . addision 's disease is when the body does not produce enough of the hormone called cortisol . this hormone helps the body deal with stress . it helps with maintaining blood pressure and controlling the heart 's functions . it slows the rate of inflammation caused by the body 's immune system . it helps with controlling the way insulin is used , and the chemical reactions of proteins , carbohydrates and fats in the body . people with the disease suffer from weight loss , fatigue , weakness and low blood pressure . some people also get darkened patches of skin . addison 's disease is treated by taking an artificial cortisol tablet .
