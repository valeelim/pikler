André Franquin     andré franquin ( 3 january 1924 – 5 january 1997 ) was a belgian comics artist . his best known comic strip creations are `` gaston '' and `` marsupilami '' . he made these when he worked on the `` spirou et fantasio '' comic strip from 1947 to 1969. this time is seen by many as the series ' golden age . awards . - 1974 : first grand prix de la ville d'angoulême , france - 1980 : adamson award , sweden - 1985 : best long comic strip at the haxtur awards , spain , for `` qrn sur bretzelburg '' - 1987 : grand prix for the graphic arts at the angoulême international comics festival - 1996 : special prize for outstanding life ’ s work at the max & moritz prizes in erlangen , germany
