Warren Truss     warren truss ( born 8 october 1948 , kingaroy , queensland ) is an australian politician . he was elected as the member for wide bay in the australian parliament in 1990. he became the leader of the national party in 2007. in the howard government , truss was minister for customs and consumer affairs and minister for community services . in july 1999 truss became of minister for agriculture , fisheries and forestry . in 2005 he became minister for transport and regional services . in september 2006 he became minister for trade .
