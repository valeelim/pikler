Paulina Porizkova     paulina porizkova ( born 9 april 1965 in the czech republic ) is a czech-american model and actress . at age eighteen , became the first female from central europe to be shown on the cover of the `` sports illustrated '' swimsuit issue . she was the second female ( following christie brinkley ) to be shown on the cover of the `` sports illustrated '' multiple times . she has acted in movies and in television shows , including `` desperate housewives '' .
