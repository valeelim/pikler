There Goes My Baby     `` there goes my baby '' is a song by american singer usher . it was written by james scheffer , frank romano , danny morris , and rico love . it was produced by love and jim jonsin . it was released as a promotional recording for `` raymond v. raymond '' ( 2010 ) . it was released on february 9 , 2010. the song won a grammy award for best male r & b vocal performance , at the 53rd grammy awards in 2011. track listing . - digital download 1 . `` there goes my baby '' - 4:44
