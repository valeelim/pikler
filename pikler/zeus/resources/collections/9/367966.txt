Juggalo     a juggalo is someone who listens to psychopathic records and or likes insane clown posse . they usually drink a drink called faygo . the name started at a 1994 concert by insane clown posse . in the song `` the juggla '' , violent j called the audience juggalos . it became popular . they wrote the songs `` what is a juggalo ? '' and `` down with the clown '' for their 1997 album `` the great milenko '' . the gathering of the juggalos is a yearly festival put on by psychopathic records . it has performances by the entire label as well as many well-known musical groups and underground artists . it was started by robert bruce , insane clown posse , and their label in 2000 .
