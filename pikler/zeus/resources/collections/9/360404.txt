Julie Nixon Eisenhower     julie nixon eisenhower ( born july 5 , 1948 ) in washington , d.c. is the youngest daughter of former president of the united states richard nixon and former first lady of the united states pat nixon . she married david eisenhower , grandson of dwight d. eisenhower in 1968. she is the younger sister of tricia nixon cox . the eisenhowers have two daughters and one son : - jennie eisenhower ( born 1978 ) - an actress - alexander richard ( born 1980 ) - melanie catherine ( born 1984 )
