School bullying     school bullying is a pattern of behavior during the ages when children and young people are in school . it is about an imbalance of social , physical or other power involving a person or group . studies show that school bullying happens to some more than others , including young people with disabilities and young gay or lesbian teens . related pages . - columbine high school massacre other websites . - ” bullying widespread in u.s. schools , study finds ” at nichd.nih.gov - `` tips to help the bullying bystander '' at education.com
