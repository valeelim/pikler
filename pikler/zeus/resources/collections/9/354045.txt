Atelopus ignescens     the atelopus ignescens was a species of toad . they belonged to the family bufonidae . they lived in ecuador . their habitat was subtropical or tropical high-altitude . it was believed to be the closest relative of the carrikeri harlequin frog . books . - ron , s. , coloma , l.a. , lötters , s. , duellman , w. , bustamante , m.r. , bolívar , w. & la marca , e. 2004. atelopus ignescens . 2006 iucn red list of threatened species . downloaded on 21 july 2007 .
