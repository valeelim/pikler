Zhouzhi County     zhouzhi county ( , pinyin : zhōuzhì xiàn ) is a county of xi'an , shaanxi , china . it is in the southwest part of xi'an . before 1964 , it was written in chinese as 盩厔 ( also read zhouzhi ) , meaning surrounded by mountains and rivers . it covers . as of november 2010 , 562,768 people lived there . zhouzhi is famous for embroidery , and the product kiwifruit .
