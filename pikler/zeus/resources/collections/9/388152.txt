Loch Ness Monster (roller coaster)    loch ness monster ( roller coaster ) the loch ness monster is a steel roller coaster at busch gardens williamsburg in the u.s. state of virginia . it was the world 's first roller coaster to have interlocking loops . the ride is in the scottish-themed area of the park . it also has a helix tunnel , two lift hills and a 114-foot ( 35 m ) drop . this roller coaster is classified as an american coaster enthusiasts coaster landmark .
