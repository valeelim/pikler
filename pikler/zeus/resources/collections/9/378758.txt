Hunter Tylo     deborah jo hunter ( also known as hunter tylo ; born july 3 , 1962 in fort worth , texas ) is an american actress . she works mostly in television soap operas . her first appearance was in `` all my children '' in 1985. she was fired three years later . she played taylor hayes in `` the bold and the beautiful '' . she also acted in other television programs . other websites . - hunter tylo official site
