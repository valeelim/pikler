Flag of Belize     the flag of belize comes from the earlier flag of british honduras . britis honduras is the name of belize during the time the british ruled . the flag is royal blue . it has a white disc at the centre with the national coat of arms held by a white and a black man . they are surrounded by fifty mahogany leaves . the flag is bordered at top and bottom by two red stripes . the colours on the flag are for the country 's national parties . the people 's united party ( pup ) uses the color blue . the united democratic party ( belize ) ( udp ) uses red .
