Bischhausen     bischhausen is a village in southern lower saxony , germany . in december of 2010 , 371 ( as of may 4 , 2015 ) people lived in the village . it is near the previous border with what was the german democratic republic ( east germany ) . the castle ruins called the gleichen are on two small mountains near the village . almost all the working people who live in the village are farmers or work in the city of göttingen , which is not far away . there is one church . it is the lutheran church of st. martin . as of 1 february 2013 , the mayor is volkmar büermann .
