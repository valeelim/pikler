Belmopan     belmopan is the capital city of belize . there are about 20,000 people living there . belmopan is at an altitude of 76 metres ( 250 ft ) above sea level . belmopan was built just to the east of the belize river . it is inland from the former capital , the port of belize city . belize city was nearly destroyed by hurricane hattie in 1961. the government was moved to belmopan in 1970. its national assembly building is made to look like a pre-columbian maya temple . the university of belize has a campus in belmopan .
