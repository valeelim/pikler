Mike Greenberg     mike greenberg ( born august 6 , 1967 ) is a television personality with espn . he has hosted `` sportscenter '' , `` espn radio sportscenter '' , and `` mike and mike in the morning '' with mike golic . he also hosted the game show `` duel '' on the abc network . he worked as a play-by-play announcer , with golic , on espn 's coverage of the arena football league . from 2007-2009 , golic and greenberg worked as the # 2 announcers for monday night football . they were replaced by brad nessler and trent dilfer . they continue to anchor `` sportscenter '' and host their radio show `` mike and mike in the morning '' . greenberg was also an anchor on `` espnews '' . greenberg was born in new york city .
