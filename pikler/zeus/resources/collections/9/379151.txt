Division of Warringah     the division of warringah is a federal electoral division for the australian house of representatives in the state of new south wales . it is located in the northern beaches region of sydney . it covers most of the land between middle harbour and the pacific ocean . it extends from port jackson in the south to the suburb of dee why in the north . it includes the suburbs of mosman , balgowlah , manly , brookvale , beacon hill and forestville . the division was named after the warringah area of sydney . this is an aboriginal australian word which means `` rain '' , `` waves '' or `` sea '' . the division was created on 13 september 1922 , for the 1922 federal election . the seat is currently a safe liberal seat . it has always been held by a member of a conservative party . it is currently held by tony abbott , the prime minister . election results . - 2013 election results - 2010 federal election - 2007 federal election - 2004 federal election references . - division of warringah - australian electoral commission
