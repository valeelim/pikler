Dassault Mirage IV     the dassault mirage iv was a twin-engine french supersonic bomber . it first flew in june 1959. it could carry a nuclear bomb `` an-22 '' or a nuclear missile `` asmp '' . it was in active service from 1964 to 2009. from 1990 , it was used like a reconnaissance aircraft , after the bought mirage 2000n . dassault mirage iv was not sold at export . an example is from 2000 on exhibit at the musée de l'air et de l'espace , the french aviation and space museum , in paris–le bourget airport .
