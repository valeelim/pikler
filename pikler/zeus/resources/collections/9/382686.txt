Pope Joan     pope joan , johanna or joanna , was a legendary female pope . she was said to have ruled for a few years some time during the 1100s . the story first appeared in 13th century chronicles . it later spread throughout europe through the writings of preachers and story tellers . she was widely believed to be real for centuries . today , religious scholars think she is a made up character who came from legend . whether she is real or made up is subject to debate . the stories say she was german and arrived in rome dressed as a man and convinced everyone there that she was in fact a man . her alleged male name was john anglicus . her role in the clergy started out as a curial notary , next a cardinal and then she became a pope . her role as a pope went on for about two and a half years but her true gender was soon found out , because she became pregnant . the birth of her child was thought to either take place in the road that separated the colosseum and st. clement 's , or in the church . her death and punishment are unknown .
