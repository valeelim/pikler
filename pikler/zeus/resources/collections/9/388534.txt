2013 horse meat scandal     on january 15 , 2013 , it was announced that horse meat was found in several meat products in england and ireland . the horse meat was discovered through dna testing . in most cases , horse meat was used only as a filler , but in the united kingdom , items labeled as beef were found to have as much as 100 % horse meat . later it was discovered that horse meat was being sold as beef all over europe , and nearly every meat seller in europe was affected . so far , no horse meat labeled as beef has been found outside europe .
