Silver-haired bat     the silver-haired bat ( `` lasionycteris noctivagans '' ) is a species of bat . it is the only member of the genus `` lasionycteris '' . the bat is found in bermuda , canada , mexico , and the united states . they are the most common bat in forested areas in the united states . silver-haired bats are also called silverwings . silver-haired bats are nearly black . they have silvery-tipped hairs on their back . it 's a medium-sized , heavily furred bat . it weighs around 8-12 grams . its total length is around 100 mm on average , its tail being 40 mm . shade is the name of the main character in the novel silverwing by kenneth oppel . he is a young silver-haired bat runt .
