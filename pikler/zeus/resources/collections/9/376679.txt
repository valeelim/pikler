Mugur Sundar     mugur sundar ( ) is a popular dance choreographer in south indian cinema . he has directed more than 10,000 dance sequences for various south-indian films . sundar was born in `` mugur '' , a village located in mysore district , karnataka . he has three sons , prabhu deva , raju sundaram and nagendra prasad , who are established dance masters . one of the judges in the famous dance show aata 4 which is telecasted in zee telugu , telugu channel . sundaram donned the role of a judge on vijay tv 's popular show jodi no.1 , jodi no.1 season two where the participants are television artistes . his fellow judges were silambarasan and sangeetha . in 2001 he directed his first kannada film `` manasella neene '' . it was a remake of telugu film `` manasanthaa nuvve '' . his son nagendra prasad played a lead role in the film . sundar played a guest role in a kannada film `` thabbali '' . he also choreographed a song for the same film .
