Marc Zumoff     marc zumoff ( born november 19 , 1955 ) is an emmy award-winning american sportscaster . he calls games for the philadelphia 76ers . he also did play-by-play for the nba on tnt and the nba on tbs . he is a fill-in play-by-play announcer for the philadelphia union . he was a play-by-play announcer for college football on cn8 . he also called the philadelphia big 5 , the philadelphia flyers , the philadelphia fever , and temple owls football and basketball .
