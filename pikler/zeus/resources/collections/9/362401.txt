Bob Carpenter     bob carpenter ( born 1952 or 1953 ) is a sportscaster currently calling games for the washington nationals . he also called games for the minnesota twins , new york mets , st. louis cardinals , and the texas rangers as well as mlb , minor league baseball , and college baseball games for espn . he has also called college football and basketball for espn and usa . he called college basketball games for cbs as well as the fifa world cup for espn . carpenter called tennis and golf tournaments for usa .
