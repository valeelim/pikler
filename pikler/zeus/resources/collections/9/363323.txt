James Lipton     james r. lipton ( born september 19 , 1926 ) is an american writer , composer , movie , television actor and host of the television show `` inside the actors studio '' . lipton was born on september 19 , 1926 in detroit , michigan and studied at central high school . lipton was married to nina foch from 1954 until they divorced in 1959. he has been married to kedakai turner since 1970 .
