Jagannath Temple, Pabna    jagannath temple , pabna jagannath temple is a hindu temple in the pabna district of the rajshahi division of bangladesh . it is dedicated to the god jagannath . it is also known locally known as the handial mandir . the temple was built between 1300 to 1400 ce . the temple is famous for its terracotta sculptures , and has been described as one of the most beautiful hindu temples in northern bangladesh . the temple is incurring damage by high salinity from the soil it is built on .
