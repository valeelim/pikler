Willem Coymans     willem coymans is an oil painting on canvas by frans hals . it is dated 1645. it measures 77 x 64 cm ( 30 5/16 x 25 3/16 in . ) it hangs in the national gallery of art , washington , dc . the gallery writes , `` hals was the first portraitist who consistently depicted his subjects seated sideways , with their arms hooked casually over the backs of their chairs . coymans , an elegant dandy proud of his expensive clothes , wears an embroidered jacket and sports a pom–pom on his hat , which is pushed forward rakishly . hals ' dazzling brushwork is especially evident in the gold embroidery and the crisply pleated shirt–sleeve . ''
