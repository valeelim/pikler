Tubuai     tubuai is the name for a group of islands in the pacific . the island of tubuai is about 640 km south of tahiti . it is part of the austral islands , which belong to french polynesia . slightly over 2.000 people live on an area of about 45 square kilometers . james cook discovered the island in 1777 , but saw no reason to land there . the natives looked hostile . fletcher christian landed there in 1789. they founded fort george on the island , but soon left , because of troubles with the natives .
