The Switch (2010 movie)    the switch ( 2010 movie ) the switch is a 2010 movie . it is a romantic comedy about a woman who wants to get pregnant . the movie stars jennifer aniston , jason bateman , patrick wilson and juliette lewis . `` the switch '' was the last movie to be distributed by miramax before that company was sold by disney . it was released on august 20 , 2010. the movie was not well liked by critics . it made almost 50 million dollars at the box office .
