Imelda Staunton     imelda staunton ( born on january 9 , 1956 ) is an english performer of movies , television and stage plays . she has performed in many hit movies , including : `` much ado about nothing '' and `` peter 's friends '' . staunton was born in archway , london . she studied at the royal academy of dramatic art . she has been married to jim carter since 1983. the couple have a daughter .
