Fast Times at Ridgemont High     fast times at ridgemont high is a 1982 american coming-of-age teen comedy movie . it was written by cameron crowe , directed by amy heckerling , produced by irving azoff and art linson . it is set in and around a high school in southern california and follows several students over the course of a year . it stars sean penn , jennifer jason leigh , judge reinhold , phoebe cates , brian backer , robert romanus , ray walston , forest whitaker , nicolas cage and anthony edwards .
