Teddy Riley     edward theodore `` teddy '' riley ( born october 8 , 1967 ) is a grammy award winning , american singer-songwriter , musician , keyboardist , and record producer . he is credited with the creation of the new jack swing . the musicians with whom riley has been connected include michael jackson , keith sweat , and mike posner . riley started a band called guy with aaron hall and timmy gatling in 1987. they have released three albums . in 1994 riley started another band called blackstreet . they have released four albums . riley co-produced half of michael jackson 's 1991 album `` dangerous '' . it is the best-selling new jack swing album ever released .
