Augustus Garrett     augustus garrett ( 1801 – november 30 , 1848 ) was an american politician who served as the mayor of chicago , illinois twice ( 1843–1844 , 1845–1846 ) for the democratic party . garrett was born in 1801 in new york city , new york . he was married to elize clark . they had no children . garrett died on november 30 , 1848 in chicago , illinois from a heart attack , aged 47. other websites . - first inaugural address - invalidated second inaugural address - third inaugural address
