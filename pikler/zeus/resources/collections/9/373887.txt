Phone connector     a phone connector ( also called jack plug , stereoplug ) is an electrical plug that is used in audio systems . for example , it is used in electric guitar cables to connect the guitar to an amplifier . there are typically three different sizes of phone connectors : 6.3 mm , 3.5 mm and 2.5 mm diameters . balanced and unbalanced circuits are possible with stereoplug connector when using monosignal per one stereoplug . sound quality improves with a balanced circuit because noise signal reduces , especially with microphones where the signal is often weak . related pages . - xlr connector
