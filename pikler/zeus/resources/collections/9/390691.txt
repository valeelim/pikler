Chancel     the chancel is a part of the architecture of many churches . it serves as a barrier between the part of the church accessible to lay people and the choir , which was reserved to the clergy . the rood screen has the same function , but a different layout . the iconostasis is a kind of chancel which is richly decorated , and is used in eastern orthodox churches . the chancel is where the congregation receives the blood and body of jesus . the chancel is usually found in a roman catholic church . the bread and wine are changed to the body and blood of jesus through transubstantiation . the fourth lateran council of 1215 required clergy made sure that the area was inaccessible to the public . the main altar of the church is usually in the area behind the chancel .
