Tucker Carlson     tucker carlson ( born may 16 , 1969 ) is an american political news and libertarian conservative commentator for fox news . he is a contributing editor for the `` weekly standard '' . he started his television career as a political analyst for the cable news network ( cnn ) and also a host on that network , co-hosting the spin room and crossfire . he left cnn in 2004. he then started hosting a show on msnbc . he currently co-hosts a weekend morning talk show on fox news channel . tucker was born in san francisco and grew up in carlsbad , california . in 2006 , he was a contestant on `` dancing with the stars '' . he was the first person eliminated from the competition .
