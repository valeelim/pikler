Crank Yankers     crank yankers is an american television series produced by adam carolla , jimmy kimmel , and daniel kellison that featured actual crank calls made by show regulars and celebrity guests , and re-enacted onscreen by puppets for a visual aid to show the viewer what is happening in the call . the show premiered in 2002 on comedy central and returned to mtv2 on february 9 , 2007 , running again until march 30 , 2007. the show screened in australia on sbs television and the comedy channel between 2003 and 2008. cd releases . - `` the best uncensored crank calls , volume 1 '' ( 2002 ) - `` the best uncensored crank calls , volume 2 '' ( 2002 ) - `` the best uncensored crank calls , volume 3 '' ( 2003 ) other websites . - from mtv2 - from comedy central
