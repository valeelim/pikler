Curve (album)    curve ( album ) curve is the eighth studio album by canadian alternative rock band , our lady peace . `` curve '' was released on april 3 , 2012. canadian heavyweight boxer george chuvalo is featured on the cover of the album and vocal excerpts from him are also featured on the album 's final track `` mettle '' . the band started recording the album six months after the release of their seventh studio album , `` burn burn '' . `` curve '' debuted at # 9 on the canadian albums chart .
