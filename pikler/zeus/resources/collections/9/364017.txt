Michael Peña     michael anthony peña ( born january 13 , 1976 ) is an american movie , television , and voice actor , and scientologist . he is best known for his roles in movies such as `` crash '' , `` world trade center '' , `` shooter '' , `` observe and report '' , `` 30 minutes or less '' , `` tower heist '' , and `` end of watch '' . peña was born on january 13 , 1976 in chicago , illinois . he is married to brie shaffer . they have a son , roman .
