Kumar Sanu     kumar sanu ( born 23 september 1957 ) is an indian singer . he was born in kolkata , india . he has won five filmfare awards ( five awards in a row from 1990 to 1995 ) in 2009 he was awarded padma shree ( forth highest civilian award ) by the government of india . he started his music career in 1984. his first breakthrough came six years later when he was given chance to do playback singing for the movie `` aashiqui '' by nadeem-shravan . the movie made his and also music directors career in bollywood . he often worked with nadeem-shravan .
