Jan Decleir     jan decleir ( born 14 february 1946 ) is a belgian movie and stage actor . he was born in niel , antwerp . his first big role was in fons rademakers ' `` mira '' ( 1971 ) . he starred in academy award winning movies `` karakter '' and `` antonia '' and the oscar-nominated social drama `` daens '' . for his role in `` the barons '' , he received the magritte award for best supporting actor . filmography . movies . - `` heeft geleden onder pontius pilatus '' ( 1966 ) - `` geboorte en dood van dirk vandersteen jr.
