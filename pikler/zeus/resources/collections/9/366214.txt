Catherine O'Hara     catherine anne o'hara ( born march 4 , 1954 ) is a canadian-american movie , television , stage , voice actress , writer and comedian . she is well known for her roles in `` sctv '' , and her roles in movies such as in `` after hours '' , `` beetlejuice '' , `` home alone '' , and `` the nightmare before christmas '' . she has won an emmy award . o'hara was born on march 4 , 1954 in toronto , ontario , canada . o'hara studied at burnhamthorpe collegiate institute . she has been married to jo welch since 1992. they have two children .
