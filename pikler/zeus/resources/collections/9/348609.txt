Soft tennis     soft tennis is major sport in japan . it is usually played by two players with rackets . it is similar to normal tennis , but the ball is soft . young and old of both sexes can enjoy playing soft tennis because the racket and ball are light . tennis came to japan from england in about 1880 's . equipment . - racket - ball in japan , people were not able to make good hard ball those days . imported balls were expensive . therefore japanese people used a rubber ball .
