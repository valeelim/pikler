Lee Dong-hae     lee dong-hae ( ) ( born 15 october 1986 ) , commonly known as just donghae ( ) , is a south korean singer , songwriter and actor . he is best known as a member of the k-pop boy band super junior and its subgroup , super junior-m and super junior-donghae & eunhyuk . he is also one of the first four korean artists to appear on chinese postage stamps .
