Bangarang (EP)    bangarang ( ep ) bangarang is the fifth ep by american electronic music producer , skrillex . `` bangarang '' was released on december 23 , 2011. singles from the ep are `` breakn ' a sweat '' which features the american rock band the doors and `` bangarang '' which features american rapper sirah . the song `` breakn ' a sweat '' was written for the 2012 documentary film re : generation . the itunes version of the ep features an `` orchestral suite '' performed by varien . the ep received generally mixed to positive reviews from music critics . the ep reached # 14 on the billboard 200 as well as # 1 on the top dance/electronic albums chart . other websites . - bangarang at discogs
