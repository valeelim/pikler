Geoffrey Bannister     geoffrey bannister ( born 1946 , manchester , england ) was an english-american educator and geographer . he is the fourth president of hawai'i pacific university . his goals for the university were to expand the graduate program research as well as building connections with other universities around the world . bannister was born in manchester , england . when he was 10 years old , his family moved to new zealand . he later moved to toronto , canada to get doctorate in geography . he became a u.s. citizen in 1989 .
