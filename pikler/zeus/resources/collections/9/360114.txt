Anal stage     the anal stage is the second stage in sigmund freud 's psychosexual development theory . freud believed the personality developed through several stages during childhood . these stages focused on the erogenous zones . the anal stage occurs between ages 18 months to three years . this is the period when a child toilet trains . according to freud , pleasure is derived from controlling the anus ( the primary erogenous zone ) and the bladder . a toilet training conflict or fixation results in a rigid or disordered personality .
