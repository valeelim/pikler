Barney (dog)    barney ( dog ) barney ( bernard , september 30 , 2000 – february 1 , 2013 ) was the pet of george w. bush and for laura bush . he was a scottish terrier . he had a nickname `` the first dog '' . on christmas days , he recorded all the activities held on the white house , which was called `` barney cam '' . he was born in new jersey on september 30 , 2000. barney died on february 1 , 2013 from lymphoma in texas . other websites . - barney 's official site — archived copy of george w. bush white house site
