Bufo apolobambicus     bufo apolobambicus is a species of toad . they belong to the family bufonidae . they live in bolivia and peru . their habitat is subtropical or tropical forest . their population is dying because of habitat loss . references . - de la riva , i . 2006. bufo apolobambicus . 2006 iucn red list of threatened species . downloaded on 21 july 2007 .
