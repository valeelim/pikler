Mark Teixeira     mark charles teixeira ( born april 11 , 1980 ) plays american major league baseball . he has played first and third bases and the outfield . he was born in annapolis , maryland . he played college ball for georgia tech . in 2001 he won the dick howser trophy as the national collegiate baseball player of the year . he has played professsional ball with the texas rangers , the atlanta braves , los angeles angels of anaheim , and the new york yankees . teixeira is of portuguese-american descent . he , his wife , and their three children live in greenwich , connecticut . he likes hunting , fishing , golfing , and reading . he is a christian and likes broadway musicals .
