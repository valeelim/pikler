Jigging     jigging is a method of fishing . it is well known in japan , especially in okinawa , the united states , and canada . this way of fishing need a unique fishing lure called a jig . the hook does not hang bait . it consists of a model that resembles an injured fish . the model will lure big fish . on the fishing boat , fishermen jerk the fishing line regularly . the hook moves vertically . because this is a fairly new method of fishing , jigging equipment is limited . but , universal fishing equipment can be used to jig , as well .
