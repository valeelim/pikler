Tia Carrere     tia carrere ( january 2 , 1967 ) is an american movie and television performer . her career was launched around 1985. she was born in honolulu , hawaii to filipino parents . her notable movies include the 1992 comedy `` wayne 's world '' . she began her career with the tv series `` airwolf '' . other television shows and movies followed . in 2006 , she was a contestant on the show `` dancing with the stars '' . carrere was born in honolulu , hawaii .
