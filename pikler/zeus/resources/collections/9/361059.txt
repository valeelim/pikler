Susie Figgis     susan figgis ( born in kenya ) is a british casting director . she has cast many celebrities in harry potter and the philosopher 's stone , `` charlie and the chocolate factory '' , the `` pirates of the caribbean '' movie series , `` alice in wonderland '' , `` the full monty '' , `` sleepy hollow '' , and `` gandhi '' . she has worked with richard attenborough and tim burton . she is married . they have one child . figgis now lives in london .
