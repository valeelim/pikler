Scott     anthony oliver scott ( born july 10 , 1966 ) is an american journalist and movie critic . he is the manager for all movie critics for `` the new york times '' . his great-uncle is american actor eli wallach . scott was born on july 10 , 1966 in northampton , massachusetts . he is jewish . scott studied at the classical high school , at johns hopkins university , and at harvard university . he is married . they have two children . he now lives in brooklyn , new york . other websites . - a. o. scott : new york times articles - a. o. scott : new york times movie reviews
