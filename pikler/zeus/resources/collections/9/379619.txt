Gena Rowlands     gena rowlands ( born june 19 , 1930 in madison , wisconsin ) is an american actress . she has gotten several golden globe and emmy awards . she starred opposite robert lansing in `` the 87th precinct '' as a woman who was deaf . she also acted in the 1980 picture `` gloria '' as the title character . she was married to director john cassavetes until his death in 1989. they had three children .
