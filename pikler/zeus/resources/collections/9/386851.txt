Mary Elizabeth Mastrantonio     mary elizabeth mastrantonio ( november 17 1958 ) is an american actress . she first became well-known after playing gina montana in the 1983 movie `` scarface '' . she also played carmen in the 1986 movie `` the color of money '' . her other movies include `` robin hood : prince of thieves '' ( 1991 ) and `` the abyss '' ( 1989 ) . mastrantonio was born in illinois .
