Lisa Hartman Black     lisa hartman ( more often recognized as lisa hartman black ; june 1 , 1956 ) is an american actress and singer who has mostly acted on television . she starred on the short lived `` tabitha '' in 1977-78 ; she then performed in the soap `` knots landing '' as the rock singer ciji dunne . through the name lisa hartman , she recorded four solo records between 1976 and 1987. she was born around houston , texas .
