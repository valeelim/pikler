Praline     praline is a confection made from nuts and sugar syrup . they may be in whole pieces or a ground powder . the powder is called `` pralin '' . belgian pralines are different . they have a hard chocolate shell with a softer , sometimes liquid , filling . french pralines are a combination of almonds and caramelized sugar . american pralines have milk or cream . they are therefore softer and creamier , like fudge .
