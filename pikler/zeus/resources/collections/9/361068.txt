Bachman–Turner Overdrive     bachman–turner overdrive are a canadian rock band from winnipeg , manitoba . bachman–turner overdrive have five members in the band ; the current band members are fred turner , randy bachman , mick dalla vee , marc lafrance and brent howard . two of the band 's best known songs are `` you ai n't seen nothing yet '' and `` takin ' care of business '' . their song `` takin ' care of business '' was ranked # 10 in top 100 most covered songs by guitar magazine . the band was formed in 1971. they released their self-titled first album on august 30 , 1989. fans of the bands are usually known as `` gearheads '' . they have won many juno awards as well . discography . studio albums . - `` bachman–turner overdrive '' ( 1973 ) - `` bachman–turner overdrive ii '' ( 1973 ) - `` not fragile '' ( 1974 ) - `` four wheel drive '' ( 1975 ) - `` head on '' ( 1975 ) - `` freeways '' ( 1977 ) - `` street action '' ( 1978 ) - `` rock n ' roll nights '' ( 1979 ) - `` bachman–turner overdrive '' ( 1984 ) - `` '' ( 1996 ) - `` bachman & turner '' ( 2010 ) other websites . - official website
