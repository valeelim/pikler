Estelle Harris     estelle harris ( born estelle nussbaum april 4 , 1928 in new york city , new york ) is a comedian , actress , voice actress , and television actress . she is best known for her role as estelle costanza in `` seinfield '' . to younger audiences she is known for her role as mrs. potato head in `` toy story 2 '' , and in `` toy story 3 '' . she is also known as muriel on `` the suite life of zach and cody '' . harris was born on april 4 , 1928 in new york city , new york to jewish-american parents , issac nussbaum and to anna nussbaum . her career began in 1977 in the movie `` looking up '' , since then she had appeared in ; `` out to sea '' , `` toy story 2 '' , tarzan ii '' , and `` toy story 3 '' . harris is currently married to sy harris and they have 3 children together . harris currently lives in los angeles , california . estelle and her husband sy , escaped serious injuries when their car blew a tire and flipped twice on september 20 , 2001. some movies she was in . - `` looking up '' ( 1977 ) - `` once upon a time in america '' ( 1984 )
