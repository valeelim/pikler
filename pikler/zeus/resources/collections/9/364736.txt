Michael Barkann     michael barkann ( born april 30 , 1960 ) is an american sportscaster . barkann was born in jersey city , new jersey and grew up in east brunswick . his jobs include these past and present : - kyw-tv sports reporter - wlvi-tv sports director - new england patriots preseason play-by-play - `` csn daily news live '' host - philadelphia phillies tv studio host - philadelphia flyers tv studio host - philadelphia 76ers tv studio host - `` eagles post game live '' host - `` monday night live '' host - `` spotlight '' host - tennis on usa court reporter - tennis on hbo court reporter - `` being john daly '' host - xfl on upn play-by-play - college basketball on cbs sideline reporter - college football on cbs sideline reporter - olympics on cbs reporter
