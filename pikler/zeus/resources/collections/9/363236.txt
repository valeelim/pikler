Abhijeet Bhattacharya     abhijeet bhattacharya ( born 30 october 1958 ) is an indian singer . he has won a filmfare award for the song `` main koi aisa geet gaaon '' from `` yes boss '' in 1997. he started his singing career in 1985. he became more popular in the 90s and early 2000s when he contributed to movies like `` baaghi '' , `` main khiladi tu anari '' , `` yeh dillagi '' , `` dilwale dulhania le jayenge '' , `` yes boss '' , `` phir bhi dil hai hindustani '' , `` dhadkan '' , `` chalte chalte '' , `` main hoon na '' .
