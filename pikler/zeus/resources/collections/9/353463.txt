Indore     indore is a city in the state of madhya pradesh in india . it is the largest city in madhya pradesh and 147th largest city in the world . it is 190 km away from bhopal , which is the capital city of madhya pradesh . the lifestyle of people of indore is same as mumbai and also it a rich city like mumbai . so , indore is also called `` mini mumbai '' ( small mumbai ) .
