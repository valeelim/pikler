Thomas Wayne     dr. thomas wayne is a fictional batman character who was the father of bruce wayne and the husband of martha wayne . he was the founder of wayne enterprises and was one of gotham 's best doctors . he had a strong relationship with bruce until he was killed along with his wife by joe chill . from his death bruce became batman to avenge their deaths . jeffrey dean morgan plays thomas in the dc extended universe first appearing in `` '' ( 2016 ) .
