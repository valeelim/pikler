Tom Bergeron     tom bergeron ( born may 6 , 1955 ) is an american television personality and game show host who is best known as the host of `` america 's funniest home videos '' and the host of `` dancing with the stars '' . he has won two emmy awards in 2000 and in 2012. bergeron was born on may 6 , 1955 in haverhill , massachusetts . bergeron has been married to louis bergeron since 1982. they have two daughters .
