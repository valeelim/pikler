Burning Bush (Christian symbol)    burning bush ( christian symbol ) the burning bush is an object described in the book of exodus . it was said to be on mount horeb . according to the narrative , the bush was on fire , but was not consumed ( all burned up ) by the flames . this is the reason for the name given to the bush . in the narrative , the `` burning bush '' is where moses was chosen by god to lead the israelites out of egypt and into canaan .
