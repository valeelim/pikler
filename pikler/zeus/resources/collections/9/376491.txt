Ferdinand Lemaire     ferdinand lemaire was a librettist and writer . he is best remembered as the librettist for camille saint-saëns 's opera `` samson et delila '' ( 1876 ) . lemaire was born in martinique . he was a creole . he married into saint-saëns 's family . he agreed to write `` samson '' but only as opera , rather than as an oratorio as the composer first intended .
