Mara Wilson     mara elizabeth wilson ( born july 24 , 1987 in los angeles , california ) is an american actress and playwright . she was born in los angeles . she currently acts on stage . she is best known for her acting in movies when she was a child . her best known movies include `` mrs. doubtfire '' ( 1993 ) and `` matilda '' ( 1996 ) . she came out as bisexual in june 2016 .
