Ken Coleman     ken coleman ( april 22 , 1925 - august 21 , 2003 ) was an american sportscaster . he was a play-by-play announcer for telecasts for the cleveland indians and the cleveland browns . he also called nfl and major league baseball games for nbc as well as college football games . he was the voice of the boston red sox and cincinnati reds . coleman as called major league baseball games for abc and nfl games for sports network incorporation .
