Helen Jacobs     helen hull jacobs ( august 6 , 1908 - june 2 , 1997 , globe , arizona ) was an american tennis player . she was a former number 1 ranked player . jacobs won the championship at wimbledon in 1936. she won a total of 10 grand slam titles . jacobs won the us national championship in singles ( 1932-1935 ) , doubles ( 1932 , 1934-35 ) and mixed doubles ( 1934 ) . she is best known for her rivalry with helen wills moody .
