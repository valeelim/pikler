Craig Minervini     craig minervini is an american sportscaster . he is host of `` marlins live '' and `` panthers live '' . he called florida panthers games for the radio . he calls select florida marlins play-by-play when rich waltz is on mlb network . he was sports anchor for wnbc-tv and wjvt-tv sports anchor . he also has done play-by-play for various other sports include notre dame football on nbc , cbs sports spectacular , college world series on espn , major league soccer , xfl on tnn , boxing on tnn , mixed martial arts on tnn , syracuse chiefs , and syracuse orangemen , football , basketball , lacrosse , and soccer . minervini currently hosts panthers live .
