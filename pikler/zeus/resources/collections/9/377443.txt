Spider crab     the name spider crab is used to refer to a species of crab in the superfamily majoidea . see crab spider for spiders of the thomisidae family . - the japanese spider crab ( `` macrocheira kaempferi '' ) , is the largest living species of crab , and is found on the bottom on the pacific ocean . - the portly spider crab ( `` libinia emarginata '' ) , is a species of crab found in the estuarine areas on the east coast of north america . - `` hyas '' is a genus of spider crabs , and contains the great spider crab ( `` hyas araneus '' ) which is found in the atlantic ocean , and the north sea .
