Touch'N You     `` touch'n you '' is a song by american rapper rick ross . american singer usher is featured on the song . it was released as the first single from ross album `` god forgives , i do n't '' ( 2012 ) . it was released on may 22 , 2012. it was written by ross and usher . it was produced by rico love and mr. morris . the music video was directed by chris robinson . it was released on june 19 , 2012 .
