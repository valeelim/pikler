Scarecrow (comics)    scarecrow ( comics ) the scarecrow or dr. jonathan crane is a fictional batman supervillain who uses fear gas to scare his victims and even batman . he used to be a psychologist who used his patients as test for his fear gases or fear drugs . he wears a scarecrow mask and a scarecrow costume . he was played by cillian murphy in `` batman begins '' , `` the dark knight '' , and in `` the dark knight rises '' .
