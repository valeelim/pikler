St. Martin's Island    st. martin 's island st. martin 's island is a small island ( about 8 square kilometres ) in the northeastern part of the bay of bengal . it is about 9 km south of the tip of the cox 's bazar-teknaf peninsula , and forms the southernmost part of bangladesh . there is a small adjoining island that is separated at high tide , called chhera island . it is about 8 km west of the northwest coast of myanmar , at the mouth of the naf river .
