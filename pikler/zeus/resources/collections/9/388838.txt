Bullets Over Broadway     bullets over broadway is a 1994 american crime comedy movie . it is about broadway theatre and the mafia . this was released in october 1994 for the united states . dianne wiest plays helen sinclair , jennifer tilly plays olive neal and mary-louise parker plays ellen . this comedy is set in 1928. the director and co-writer of `` bullets over broadway '' is woody allen . the reviews for this crime comedy were very positive .
