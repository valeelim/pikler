Royal Medical Society     royal medical society ( rms ) is the oldest medical society in the united kingdom . the rms is a british professional society engaged in the moving forward of medical knowledge . it was called `` the medical society '' when it was established in 1737. the society was granted a royal charter in 1778. rms is based in edinburgh , scotland . the society has members throughout the world . notable members . - charles darwin - benjamin franklin - joseph lister related pages . - list of royal societies other websites . - rms website
