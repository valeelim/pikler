Seann William Scott     seann william scott ( born october 3 , 1976 ) is an american actor . he is best known for playing the role of steve stifler in the `` american pie '' series . he also played roles in the movies `` final destination '' , `` road trip '' , `` dude , where 's my car ? `` , `` evolution '' , `` bulletproof monk '' , `` the rundown '' , `` the dukes of hazzard '' , `` role models '' , `` cop out '' , and `` goon '' . scott voiced the character crash in the `` ice age '' franchise .
