Acre Port     acre port is an ancient port in the old city of acre , israel . the port has a 2000-year-old history . recent archaeology suggests that the port was originally in the south of acre , at the mouth of na'aman river . the port was closed in the time of the british mandate , which chose to use the haifa port instead of acre . in 1965 , a new breakwater was built . in 1982 , the port became a marina . other websites . - old city of acre website ( hebrew )
