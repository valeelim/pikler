Game Show Network     game show network , or gsn , is a cable television network . it shows game show episodes . it airs in the united states . some shows the network airs include `` pyramid '' , `` minute to win it '' , `` family feud '' , `` card sharks '' , `` idiotest '' , '' skin wars '' and others . the company is jointly owned by directv and sony pictures television . the channel was launched on december 1 , 1994 .
