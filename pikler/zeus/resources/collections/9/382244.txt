Intel Atom     the intel atom ( made as intel centrino ( 2003-2010 ) in 2003 ) ( formerly called centrino atom ( 2008-2009 ) ) is a low-power x86 cpu . it was created by intel for netbooks , smartphones , tablet computers , and embedded systems . it was created to compete with the arm cpu . its performance per watt is still not as good as the arm , although the overall performance is better . for a given clock speed , the performance is about the same as a pentium 4 , but the power consumption is much lower .
