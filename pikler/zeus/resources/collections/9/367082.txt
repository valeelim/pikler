Maamme     maamme ( finnish ) or `` '' ( swedish ) is the title of finland 's national anthem . there is no law on an official national anthem in finland , but maamme is firmly established by convention . the anthem was composed by german-born composer fredrik pacius in 1848 and first performed 13 may 1848. and the lyrics where written by finnish poet johan ludvig runeberg in swedish . in 1889 it was translated to finnish by paavo cajander . lyrics . - in finnish - in swedish
