Slender salamander     slender salamander is a common name given to lungless salamanders of the genus batrachoseps . they are different from other lungless salamanders . they have four toes on each foot . they live in oregon , california and baja california in mexico . 19 species are recognized in this genus : other websites . - . 2007. amphibian species of the world : an online reference . version 5.1 ( 10 october , 2007 ) . `` batrachoseps '' . electronic database accessible at http : //research.amnh.org/herpetology/amphibia/index.php . american museum of natural history , new york , usa .
