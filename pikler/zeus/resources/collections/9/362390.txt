Kevin Kugler     kevin kugler is a former co-host of `` unsportsmanlike conduct '' . he calls college football , basketball , and baseball for westwood one/dial global . kugler was added to the nfl booth and was the voice of the nebraska cornhuskers football and men 's basketball . kugler also calls college football and basketball games for the big ten network as well as jacksonville jaguars preseason games replacing paul burmeister .
