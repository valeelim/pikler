Weston, Connecticut    weston , connecticut weston is a town in fairfield county , connecticut . the population was 10,179 at the 2010 census . about 19 % of the town 's workforce travels to new york city for work . like many towns in southwestern connecticut , weston is one of the richest communities in the united states . in 2010 the median household income was us $ 180,321 . most of devil 's den preserve is in the town . it is a nature reserve which gets 40,000 visits a year .
