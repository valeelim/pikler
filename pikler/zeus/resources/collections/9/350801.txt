Ilya Prigogine     ilya prigogine ( 25 january 1917 – 28 may 2003 ) was a belgian physical chemist of russian jewish descent . he won the 1977 nobel prize for chemistry . prigogine studied chemistry at the free university of brussels . in 1950 he became a professor there . in 1959 he started teaching at the university of texas at austin in the united states . prigogine was married to belgian poet hélène jofé . after their divorce , he married polish-born chemist maria prokopowicz in 1961. related pages . - list of nobel prize winners by country
