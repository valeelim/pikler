2012–13 Fußball-Bundesliga     the 2012–13 fußball-bundesliga is the 50th season of the fußball-bundesliga , germany 's top football league . the season started on august 24 , 2012 and ended on may 18 , 2012. the defending champions were borussia dortmund . the champion was decided before the tournament ended . fc bayern munich won the tournament , with 91 points ( a 25-point lead ) . below is the table for the 2012–13 fußball-bundesliga season . bayern munich , borussia dortmund and bayer 04 leverkusen directly qualified for the uefa champions league , while fortuna düsseldorf and spvgg greuther fürth were relegated to the 2. bundesliga . related pages . - german bundesliga
