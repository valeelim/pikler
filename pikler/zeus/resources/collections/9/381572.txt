Gallier House     gallier house , also known as james gallier , jr. , house was the home of american architect james gallier , jr. in new orleans , louisiana . gallier designed the house himself . it is located on royal street in the french quarter section of the city . it was declared a national historic landmark in 1974. gallier house now houses a museum , with the interior restored and furnished in the style of the 1850s . other websites . - gallier house .
