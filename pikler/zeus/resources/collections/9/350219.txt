Maurice Papon     maurice papon ( september 3 , 1910 - february 17 , 2007 ) was a french civil servant . he led the police in major prefectures and in paris during the nazi occupation of france and into the 1960s . in 1998 he was convicted of crimes against humanity . this was because he helped in making more than 1600 jews move to concentration camps during world war ii . he died of congestive heart failure .
