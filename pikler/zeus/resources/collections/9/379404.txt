Jor-El     jor-el was a fictional superman character who was the father of superman who died in the planet of krypton when it was destroyed . years later he helped his son to learn the true power he holds and helps him become superman . he was married to lara lor-van . he was played by marlon brando in `` superman '' and by russell crowe in `` man of steel '' . other websites . - jor-el at `` smallville '' wiki - jor-el ( kryptonian ) at `` smallville '' wiki
