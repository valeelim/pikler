Indulkana, South Australia    indulkana , south australia indulkana ( also known as iwantja ) is an aboriginal community in south australia . it is on the anangu pitjantjatjara yankunytjatjara lands . it is about west of the stuart highway and south of alice springs . the town was established in 1968. there were 315 people living at indulkana in the 2006 census . most of the people belong to the yankunytjatjara nation . the 2001 census showed that indulkana had south australia 's highest proportion residents under the age of 15 ( 35.0 % ) . indulkana has a local community store that also supplies diesel fuel . there is an unsealed airstrip located close to the town . there is also a school , established in 1971. indulkana does not have a permanent police presence , though it has a police station . state police are based at marla and run patrols to the area . in the absence of police , the town is served by two constables from the community . the town also has a local artists ' co-operative , iwantja arts . it has a gallery for sale of art work from across the apy .
