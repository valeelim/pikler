Leonard Maltin     leonard maltin ( born december 18 , 1950 ) is an american movie critic and historian . he wrote several books on cinema and celebrity biographies . maltin was born on december 18 , 1950 in new york city . he was raised in teaneck , new jersey . he studied at teaneck high school and at new york university . he was married to alice tlusty since 1975. they have one daughter . other websites . - leonard maltin 's official site
