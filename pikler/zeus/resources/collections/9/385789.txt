Donny Hathaway     donny hathaway ( october 1 , 1945 - january 13 , 1979 ) was an american singer and musician who did r & b , soul blues and jazz . he collaborated many times under singer roberta flack , and had many singles with her . he covered the carole king hit `` you 've got a friend '' and the aretha franklin song `` baby i love you '' . he was born in chicago , though raised near st. louis . he terminated his own life by jumping from a new york city hotel room onto the sidewalk on january 13 , 1979. he had schizophrenia and depression .
