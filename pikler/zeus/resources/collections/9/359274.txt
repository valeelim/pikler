Chiriku Hachiman-gū     , also known as , is a japanese shinto shrine in miyaki , saga prefecture . `` chiriku hachiman-gū '' was the chief shinto shrine ( `` ichinomiya '' ) of hizen province . it is a chief shrine of saga prefecture and nagasaki prefecture . in 1940 , the shrine was recognized in the system of state shinto . it was among the ranked , nationally significant shrines or which included 50 sanctuaries . other websites . - 千栗八幡宮 at miyaki sanpo website
