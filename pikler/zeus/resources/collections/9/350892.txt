Aquamarine (color)    aquamarine ( color ) aquamarine is a color , a pale tint between cyan and green . it is named for the gemstone aquamarine . the first recorded use of `` aquamarine '' as a color name in english was in 1598. the color `` medium aquamarine '' ( shown below ) is used for the uniforms of nurses and surgeons and also used to paint rooms in hospitals because it is regarded as a color that calms and relaxes the patients . related pages . - list of colors
