Shepparton, Victoria    shepparton , victoria shepparton ( ) is a city on the floodplain of the goulburn river in the north east of victoria , australia . it is about north-east of melbourne . in 2006 there were 27,707 living in the city . shepparton began as a sheep station and river crossing . it later became an important railway town . today it is an agricultural and manufacturing city . it is at the centre of the goulburn valley irrigation system , one of the largest centres of irrigation in australia . it is a major regional service city and is the civic administration centre for the city of greater shepparton . the greater shepparton area includes the towns of tatura , merrigum , mooroopna , murchison , dookie , and grahamvale .
