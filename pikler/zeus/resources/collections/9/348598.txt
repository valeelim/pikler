Fukuoka SoftBank Hawks     the history of the hawks dates to the 1930s . the team has had several names , including - nankai hawks ( ~1947 ) - fukuoka daiei hawks ( 1988~2004 ) - fukuoka softbank hawks ( 2005~ ) softbank hawks won the first prize in the japan championship in 2011. the owner of the hawks is softbank , an it company . the hawks are very popular in fukuoka . the mascot character is harry hawk . the fukuoka yahoo ! japan dome in fukuoka is their home stadium . this stadium is in the central ward of fukuoka city , near tojinmachi subway station . related pages . - sports in japan other websites . - fukuoka softbank hawks website - ( @ hawks official ) on twitter
