Gwangjong of Goryeo     gwangjong of goryeo ( korean : 고려 광종 ; hanja : 高麗光宗 , 925–975 ) was a korean ruler . his real name was wang so ( 왕소 , 王昭 ) , and his chinese style name was ilhwa ( 일화 日華 ) . he was the 4th king and emperor of the goryeo dynasty from 947 to 975. other websites . - gwangjong of goryeo - gwangjong de goryeo - gwangjong de goryeo - gwangjong : navercast
