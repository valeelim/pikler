Ren Höek     ren höek is a fictional character from the nickelodeon series `` the ren & stimpy show '' , and spike series `` ren & stimpy `` adult party cartoon '' '' . he is one of the main characters . ren is the main protagonist/antagonist of the series . he first appeared in the pilot episode `` big house blues '' . ren is a 17 year old `` asthma-hound '' chihuahua . he has a long , rat-like , pink tail . his tail disappears often to the point of it not being seen in the later series .
