1810 United States Census     the united states census of 1810 was the third census in the united states . it was done on august 6 , 1810. it showed that 7,239,881 people were living in the united states . 1,191,362 of the total were slaves . the 1810 census included one new state : louisiana . the original census returns for the district of columbia , georgia , mississippi , new jersey , ohio , and tennessee were lost or destroyed over the years .
