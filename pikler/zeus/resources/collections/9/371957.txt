Armin van Buuren     armin van buuren ( born 25 december 1976 ) is a disc jockey from the netherlands . he is the number one ranked dj . he has won dj magazine 's top 100 djs fan poll a record of 5 times . he won it 4 years in a row ( 2007-2010 ) and again in 2012. since 2001 , van buuren has hosted a weekly radio show called `` a state of trance '' . the program says about 25 million people in 26 countries listen to it each week . this fact would make it one of the most listened-to radio programs in the world .
