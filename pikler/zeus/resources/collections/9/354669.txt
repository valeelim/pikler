Noureddine Morceli     noureddine morceli ( born february 28 , 1970 in ténès , chlef province ) is a retired algerian athlete . he won the 1500 m run at the 1996 summer olympics . he won the silver medal in the 1500 m at the world junior championships in 1988. morceli went to college in riverside , california . morceli was coached by his brother abderrahmane . his brother ran for algeria in the moscow olympics of 1980 and in los angeles in 1984 .
