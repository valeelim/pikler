Nora Ephron     nora ephron ( ; may 19 , 1941 – june 26 , 2012 ) was an american movie director , producer and novelist . ephron was the eldest of the four daughters of writers henry ephron ( 1911-1992 ) and phoebe ephron ( née wolkind , 1914-1971 ) . her sisters are delia ephron ( born 1944 ) , hallie ephron ( born 1948 ) and amy ephron ( born 1952 ) . nora ephron was born in new york city and moved to los angeles , california when she was a child . she moved back east after leaving high school . she made many novels into movies . ephron has been married to nicholas pileggi from 1987 until her death in 2012. ephron died of pneumonia which was caused by her illness of acute myeloid leukemia .
