Abbotsford International Airport     abbotsford international airport is a public airport in abbotsford , british columbia , canada . it is about 4.1 km ( 2.5 mi ) southwest of abbotsford 's city centre . it is the second-largest airport in the lower mainland after vancouver international airport . in 2010 , it was the nineteenth busiest airport in canada by aircraft movements ( 102,128 ) and passengers ( 503,693 ) . other websites . - abbotsford international airport , official web site
