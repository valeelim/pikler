Violin Concerto (Mendelssohn)    violin concerto ( mendelssohn ) the violin concerto in e minor , op . 64 is a work for orchestra and solo violin by felix mendelssohn . he completed the concerto in 1844. it was written for leipzig concertmaster ferdinand david . the sonata rondo finale is written in e major . the concerto is a pillar of the classical repertoire . references . - woodstra , chris , et al . 2005 . `` all music guide to classical music '' . all media guide , llc . p. 825. isbn 0-87930-865-6 .
