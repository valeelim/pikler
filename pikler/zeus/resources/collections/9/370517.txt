Meshech     meshech is a person mentioned in the bible , described in genesis 10 as the son of japheth , son of noah . meshech in the bible can also mean the tribe that came from his children , or the country where they lived , for example in psalm 120:5. many bible atlases such as ivp and zondervan state it was in asia minor , while others say the caucasus . for gog , 'the chief ruler of the nations of meshech and tubal ' ( book of ezekiel 39:1 ) see armageddon references . - prophezine.com - topical bible.org
