George Wendt     george robert wendt ( born october 17 , 1948 in chicago , illinois ) is an american television , voice , stage and movie actor . he is known for his role as norm peterson in the comedy show `` cheers '' . he has also appeared in michael jackson 's music video `` black or white '' . he hosted his own show , `` the george wendt show '' , before it was cancelled in april 1995. he is currently married to bernadette birkett since 1978 and have three children together and two step-children .
