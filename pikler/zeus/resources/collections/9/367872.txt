Missi Pyle     andrea kay `` missi '' pyle ( born november 16 , 1972 ) is an american movie , television , stage , voice actress and singer . she was in the movies `` charlie and the chocolate factory '' , `` big fish '' , `` a cinderella story once upon a song '' , `` galaxy quest '' , `` the artist '' and in `` '' . pyle was born on november 16 , 1972 in houston , texas . she studied at the north carolina school of the arts and at germantown high school . pyle was married to antonio sacre from 2000 until they divorced in 2005. she married casey anderson in 2008 .
