George Hamilton (actor)    george hamilton ( actor ) george stevens hamilton ( born august 12 , 1939 ) is an american movie , television , stage , and voice actor . he has appeared in some comedy movies such as in ; `` love at first bite '' , `` zorro '' , and `` gay blade '' . he has won a saturn award ( 1979 ) . hamilton was born in memphis , tennessee on august 12 , 1939. hamilton was married to alana stewart from 1972 until they divorced in 1973. hamilton was partners with kinberly blackford from 1997 until they split up in 1999. with stewart , he has a son and with blackford , he has a child .
