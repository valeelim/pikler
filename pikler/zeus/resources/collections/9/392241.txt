Azkaban     azkaban is the jail of wizards in the fiction fantasy series by j.k. rowling , `` harry potter '' . it holds most of the death eaters in prison . in `` harry potter and the prisoner of azkaban '' rowling described azkaban as `` cold , dark , and all your greatest memories you ca n't remember , and all you can think about are your worst . '' dementors are the guards of azkaban .
