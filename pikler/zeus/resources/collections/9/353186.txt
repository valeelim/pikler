Tripoli, Lebanon    tripoli , lebanon tripoli ( formal / ala-lc : `` ṭarābulus '' ; lebanese arabic : `` ṭrāblus '' ; / `` tripolis '' ) is the largest city in northern lebanon and the second-largest city in lebanon . it is the capital of the north governorate and the tripoli district . it has the second largest port in lebanon . its 500,000 inhabitants , are overwhelmingly sunni muslims ( approximately 80 % ) , along with a small minority of orthodox and maronite christians ( approximately 5 % ) , and a small minority of alawite muslims ( approximately 15 % ) .
