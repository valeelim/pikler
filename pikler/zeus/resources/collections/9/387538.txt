The Incredible Hulk (roller coaster)    the incredible hulk ( roller coaster ) the incredible hulk is a steel launched roller coaster . it is at islands of adventure in orlando , florida . it is themed and named after hulk , a popular comic book superhero character . it has a launched tube known as the gamma tube that the roller coaster launches from . the ride was manfactured by bolliger & mabillard and has 7 inversions . it is also the only bolliger & mabillard launched roller coaster in the world . the incredible hulk has the following inversions : 1. zero g roll 1. cobra roll 1 . 2 loops 1. corkscrew
