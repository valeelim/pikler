Gorkha National Liberation Front     gorkha national liberation front ( gnlf ) ( nepali : गोर्खा राष्ट्रिय मुक्ति मोर्चा ) is a political party in the darjeeling district of west bengal , republic of india . it was formed in 1980 by subhash ghisingh with the objective of demanding a gorkhaland state within india .
