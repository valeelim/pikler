Petit Théâtre de la Reine     the petit théâtre de la reine ( ) is a small theatre at versailles near the petit trianon . it was designed and built in 1780 on the orders of marie antoinette . her favorite architect richard mique did the work . the theatre seats only 100. it was intended for performances by the queen and her family and friends . at the period , many aristocrats had such small theatres on their country estates to pass the time performing plays and operas . mique 's exterior is plain , simple , and even severe . the interior is rich with gold , blue , and white decoration . the interior is a wooden room painted to resemble marble . the stage machinery still exists . in 1780 , the queen and her friends played pierre-alexandre monsigny 's `` le roi et le fermier '' ( the king and the farmer , 1762 ) in the little theatre . the original forest set and rustic cottage set have survived . they were restored in the 19th century . they were in use in february 2012 for performances of `` le roi '' at the versailles opera .
