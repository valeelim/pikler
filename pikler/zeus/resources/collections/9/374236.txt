Bahá'í World Centre     the bahá'í world centre is the headquarters of the bahá'í faith . it is located in haifa , israel on mount carmel . the bahá'í world centre includes the world bahá'í archive , universal house of justice of the baha'i faith , international center for advisors , and the scripture study center . there are many baha'i believers in haifa and acre . other websites . - www.bahai.org - official website - photos - the bahá'í gardens in haifa : the shrine of the bab terraces & gardens
