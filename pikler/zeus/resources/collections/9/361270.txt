WASP-12b     wasp-12b is an extrasolar planet orbiting the star wasp-12 . it is said by nasa the hottest planet in the universe , much hotter than venus . it is extremely close to its star , about 2 million miles away from it . it is a gas giant and is bigger than jupiter . the average temperature there can get up to 1500 degrees celsius ! for comparison , mercury in our solar system 's average temperature is around 430 degrees celcius . wasp-12b is so close to its star , that the star sucks mass from the planet creating a comet-like trail behind it .
