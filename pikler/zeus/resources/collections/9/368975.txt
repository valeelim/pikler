Fred Haise     fred wallace haise , jr. ( born november 14 , 1933 ) is an american engineer and former nasa astronaut . he is one of only 24 humans to have flown to the moon . having flown on apollo 13 , haise was to be the sixth human to walk on the moon , but the mission did not land due to a failure aboard the spacecraft . he was born on november 14 , 1933 in biloxi , mississippi . other websites . - spacefacts biography of fred haise - short audio interview on astrotalkuk.org during his visit to uk in 2009
