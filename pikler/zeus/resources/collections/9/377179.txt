Random Album Title     random album title is the first studio album by canadian electronica producer , deadmau5 . `` random album title '' was released on november 6 , 2006. the album featured the hit singles `` faxing berlin '' and `` i remember '' featuring kaskade . the song `` arguru '' was written in memory of juan antonio arguelles rius , an audio-software developer and musician that died in a car accident .
