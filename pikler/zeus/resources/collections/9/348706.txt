3D (album)    3d ( album ) 3d is the final studio album by american girl group tlc . it was released on november 12 , 2002 by arista records . this was six months after lisa `` left eye '' lopes died . the album debuted at number six on the `` billboard '' 200 chart . 143,000 copies of `` 3d '' were sold in the first week of release . it was then certified platinum by the riaa for shipping 1,000,000 copies in the united states . the album was given two grammy awards nominations .
