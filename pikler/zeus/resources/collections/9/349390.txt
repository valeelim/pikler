The Black Balloon     the black balloon is a 2008 australian-british drama movie about an unusual suburban family in sydney in the early 1990s . it was directed by elissa down and written by down and jimmy jack . it stars rhys wakefield as 15 year old thomas mollison , luke ford as his slightly older brother charlie , toni collette as his pregnant mother maggie and erik thomson as his father simon , who is in the army . charlie is mentally retarded , severely autistic , unable to speak , hyperactive , overemotional and has outbursts of inappropriate , sometimes destructive , behaviour . gemma ward plays thomas ' girlfriend jackie masters .
