Molucca Sea     the molucca sea is in the western pacific ocean , within the country of indonesia . the region is rich in coral and has many diving sites . the molucca sea borders the banda sea to the south and the celebes sea to the north . the islands bordering the molucca sea include halmahera to the north east , buru and ceram in the centre , and sulawesi to the west . the talaud islands to the north are the general limit of the sea . the molucca sea plate , the tectonic plate named after the sea , goes further north .
