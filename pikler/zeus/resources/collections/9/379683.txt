Raw milk     raw milk is milk which is not pasteurized . it is consumed by those not willing to treat it . raw milk was consumed in popularity before industrial revolution and the invention of pasteurizing in 1864. medical groups strongly recommend against eating or drinking raw milk , however . in asia , rules and laws regarding raw milk are rarely put in force ; in most of the european union , raw milk products are legal ; in france raw milk and its related products are legal and essential as high quality food . around the united states , however , there are restrictions and warnings in regard to raw milk and its products .
