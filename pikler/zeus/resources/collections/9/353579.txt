Leslie Grantham     leslie grantham ( born 30 april 1947 ) is a british actor . he was born in camberwell , london . in 1962 , he joined the british army . in 1966 , whilst stationed in osnabrueck , germany , he shot a taxi driver dead whilst trying to rob him . he was convicted of murder and sentenced to life imprisonment . he was released from prison in 1977. he began his acting career in 1983. he is best known for playing den watts in `` eastenders '' from 1985-1989 and from 2003-2005 .
