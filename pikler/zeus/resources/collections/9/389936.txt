The Proposal (movie)    the proposal ( movie ) the proposal is a romantic comedy movie from 2009. it is about a canadian executive chief of staff working at a new york city book publishing company . she learns she will be deported due to an expired visa unless the visa matter is taken care of . sandra bullock plays the executive . ryan reynolds plays her assistant . this movie was released in june 2009. the reviews were mixed to negative . `` the proposal '' took in over $ 317 million in the box office . it became the highest grossing romantic comedy in 2009 .
