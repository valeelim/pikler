Nu Shooz     nu shooz are an r & b group based in portland , oregon.they formed in 1979. they released many records in the 1980s . however , they did not hit the mainstream until after their 1986 album `` poolside '' was released . the song `` i ca n't wait '' was among their many hit songs . in 1988 the band broke up . in 2007 they got back together .
