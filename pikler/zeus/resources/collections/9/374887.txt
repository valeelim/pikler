Jämsä     jämsä ( in the local dialect , `` jämpsä '' ) is a town and municipality in central finland . it is 223 km from helsinki , and 58 km from jyväskylä . there were about 22,125 people living there in january 2014. jämsä municipality was established in 1866. it is next to the municipalities of juupajoki , jyväskylä , keuruu , kuhmoinen , luhanka , mänttä-vilppula , orivesi and petäjävesi . isojärvi national park , a popular hiking place , is near to jämsä . other websites . - jämsä city pages
