America Ferrera     america georgine ferrera ( born april 18 , 1984 ) is an american movie , television , stage , voice actress and television producer . she is best known for her role as betty suarez in `` ugly betty '' . for her role as betty suarez , she won an golden globe award , emmy award , and an screen actors guild award . ferrera was born on april 18 , 1984 in los angeles , california . she studied at the university of southern california . she is married to ryan piers williams since 2011. ferrera is a democrat and attended the 2012 democratic national convention in support of president obama 's re-election .
