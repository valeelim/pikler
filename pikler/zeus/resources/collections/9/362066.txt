Marv Albert     marv albert ( born marvin philip aufrichtig ; june 12 , 1941 ) is an american sportscaster . he is a member of the basketball hall of fame . he is commonly called `` the voice of basketball . '' from 1967–2004 , he was also known as `` the voice of the new york knicks . '' albert has called the play-by-play of six super bowls , nba finals and seven stanley cup finals . on june 6 , 2011 , it was said that albert was joining cbs sports to call play-by-play for `` the nfl on cbs '' . honors and awards . - cable ace award – six times . - curt gowdy media award – awarded by the naismith memorial basketball hall of fame , 1994 . - american sportscasters association sportscaster of the year ( play-by-play ) – 1996 . - emmy award – for national sports : five times ; for new york : three times . - nassau county sports hall of fame – inducted in 2006 . - national jewish museum sports hall of fame – inducted in 1992 . - new york state sportscaster of the year – twenty times .
