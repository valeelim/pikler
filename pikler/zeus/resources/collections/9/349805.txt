Xincheng District     xincheng district ( , pinyin : xīnchéng qū , meaning : new city district ) , is a district of xi'an , shaanxi , china , and it 's in middle-eastern of xi'an . shaanxi government is in here . its name is from xincheng square . as of november 2011 , 589,739 people live in here . area of xincheng district is 30.13 km .
