Dhadkan     dhadkan ( ) is a bollywood romantic drama movie directed by dharmesh darshan . the movie has akshay kumar , shilpa shetty , sunil shetty and mahima chaudhry in the main roles . the movie 's music was done by nadeem-shravan . cast . - akshay kumar ... ram - shilpa shetty ... anjali - sunil shetty ... dev - mahima chaudhry ... sheetal varma - sharmila tagore ... dev 's mother ( special appearance ) - sushma seth ... ram 's stepmother - parmeet sethi ... bob ( ram 's stepbrother ) - manjeet kullar ... nikki ( ram 's stepsister ) - kiran kumar ... anjali 's father - anjana mumtaz ... anjali 's mother - anupam kher ... sheetal 's father ( special appearance ) - kader khan ... singer ( special appearance in song `` dulhe ka sehra '' ) the music of the movie went to become very popular of that year . it was also the second most sold bollywood music album . awards . - filmfare awards - filmfare best female playback award - alka yagnik - `` dil ne yeh kaha hai dil se '' . - filmfare best villain award - sunil shetty
