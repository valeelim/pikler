Trope     trope is a term for a figure of speech . the term has evolved to identify an overused theme or device . a trope may mean a convention , a plot trick , a setup , a narrative structure and a character type all at the same time . in rhetoric , there are many types of tropes . - allegory - antithesis - hyperbole - irony - metaphor - metonymy - oxymoron - synecdoche other websites . - `` tropes '' at stanford encyclopedia of philosophy
