Samul nori     samul nori is a korean traditional musical performance , which is performed with four percussion instruments : jing , janggu , buk ( the drum in korean ) , kkwaenggwari . the performance includes dancing . specifically , in `` samul '' , `` sa '' means `` four '' , and `` mul '' means `` object '' in korean , and `` nori '' means `` to play '' . considering the meaning of `` samul nori '' , this is one kind of playful and casual art performance done in public . in the past , korean people usually play `` samul nori '' when they work in agricultural area to relax after working . nowadays , some korean people who play korean traditional music try to boost samul nori . famous samul-nori performances are `` kim duk soo samul nori '' and `` namsadang nori '' . namsadang nori was chosen as a unesco masterpiece of the oral and intangible heritage of humanity . in addition , some koreans are trying to transform samul nori to modern styles by mixing this with other modern performances . for example , there were tv shows where samul nori performers were dancing with pop singers or djs .
