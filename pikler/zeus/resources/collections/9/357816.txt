Orivesi     orivesi is a town and municipality in finland . it is in pirkanmaa . the municipality was founded in 1865. it has been classed as a town since 1986. nearby municipalities are juupajoki , jämsä , kangasala , kuhmoinen , ruovesi and tampere . there were more than 9,600 people in orivesi ( as of january , 2014 ) . the name `` orivesi '' means `` stallion water '' . other websites . - town of orivesi – official website
