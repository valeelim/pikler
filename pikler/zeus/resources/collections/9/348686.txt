Black-hooded Antshrike     the black-hooded antshrike ( thamnophilus bridgesi ) is a species of bird in the thamnophilidae family . it is found in costa rica and panama . as its name , males are mostly black , with three rows of well-marked white spots on the wings . however , females are usually brown , and only its tail is black . references . - birdlife international 2004. thamnophilus bridgesi . 2006 iucn red list of threatened species . downloaded on 27 july 2007 .
