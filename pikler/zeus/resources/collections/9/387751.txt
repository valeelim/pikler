Charlie Brooker     charlie brooker ( born 3 march 1971 ) is a british comedian , journalist , television presenter , writer and producer . he was born in reading , berkshire and grew up in oxfordshire . he is widely seen as a very funny man , but can be serious like in the tv programme `` black mirror '' which he wrote . he is married to `` blue peter '' presenter konnie huq . sometimes brooker appears panel shows such as `` have i got news for you '' . he presents his own programmes `` screenwipe '' and `` you have been watching '' .
