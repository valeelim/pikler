John Turturro     john michael turturro ( born february 28 , 1957 ) is an italian-american movie and television , voice actor , writer and director known for his roles in the movies ; `` do the right thing '' ( 1989 ) , `` miller 's crossing '' ( 1990 ) , `` barton fink '' ( 1991 ) , `` quiz show '' ( 1994 ) , `` the big lebowski '' ( 1998 ) , `` o brother , where art thou ? '' ( 2000 ) and `` the transformers movie series '' . he has appeared in over sixty films , and has worked mostly with the coen brothers , adam sandler and spike lee . turturro also starred in disney/pixar `` cars 2 '' . turturro was born in brooklyn , new york on february 28 , 1957. turturro is currently married to katherine borowitz since 1985 , they have two children .
