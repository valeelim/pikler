Deborah Jeane Palfrey     deborah jeane palfrey ( march 18 , 1956 - may 1 , 2008 ) was an american businesswoman and criminal . she was best known by the name she was given by the media , the d.c. madam . palfrey operated an escort agency , pamela martin and associates , in washington d.c. she was convicted on april 15 , 2008 of racketeering , using the mail for illegal purposes , and money laundering . two weeks later , at age 52 , she was found hanged in a garage . autopsy results and the police said her death was a suicide . palfrey was born in the pittsburgh suburb of charleroi , pennsylvania . she grew up in orlando , florida .
