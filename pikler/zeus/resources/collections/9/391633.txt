Clara Blandick     clara blandick ( june 4 , 1880 - april 15 , 1962 ) was an american actress . she acted in stage plays and movies . she played auntie em in the movie `` the wizard of oz '' . she also did many other movies . she was raised in boston . she moved to new york city for her acting career . blandick committed suicide at age 81 using sleeping pills . later information indicates birth year 1876 .
