Bristol Airport     bristol airport , is in lulsgate bottom in north somerset . it is the commercial airport for the city of bristol , england , and the area around it . it opened as bristol ( lulsgate ) airport in 1955 , from march 1997 to march 2010 it became known as bristol international airport . in 2003 , the airport drew 45 % of its passengers from the former county of avon area , 13 % from devon , 10 % from somerset . it also has bases for easyjet and ryanair . in 2011 , it was the ninth busiest airport in the united kingdom , with 5,780,746 passengers , a 0.6 percent rise compared with 2010. in march 2010 , the airport was given the name bristol airport .
