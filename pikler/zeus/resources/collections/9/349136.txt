List of World Heritage Sites in the Arab States     this is a list of the unesco world heritage sites in the arab states . some of these countries are geographically in asia and some in africa . the list below has an image of the site or part of the site ; the name ; the location ; the nominating state party ; the criteria met by the site , including if it is a cultural , natural or mixed ; the area in hectares and acres ; the year the site was added ; and a description of the site .
