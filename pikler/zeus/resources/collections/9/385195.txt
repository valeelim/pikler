Carditamera gracilis     carditamera gracilis , or the west indian cardita , is a species of bivalve mollusc in the family carditidae . it can be found off the coast of the west indies . `` carditamera gracilis '' has an elongated shell , strong medium size about 40mm . it has more pronounced radial rib and high in the back its color is white with brown spots inside is pearly . `` carditamera gracilis '' dispatched by the gulf of mexico and caribbean sea . usually inhabits mainland coast of shallow water attached to rocky substrates .
