Chinua Achebe     chinua achebe ( 16 november 1930 - 22 march 2013 ) was a nigerian novelist , poet , professor , and critic . he was best known for writing the novel , `` things fall apart '' which was first printed in 1958. it is the most widely read book in modern african literature . achebe went to nigeria 's first university , university college . achebe wrote his first short story at university .
