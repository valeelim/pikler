Tony Shalhoub     anthony marcus `` tony '' shalhoub ( born october 9 , 1953 ) is an american movie , stage , television , and voice actor . he is known for his roles as adrian monk in `` monk '' , alexander minion in the first three `` spy kids '' movie , and as luigi in `` cars '' and in `` cars 2 '' . shalhoub has also appeared in plays on stage . he was born in green bay , wisconsin on october 9 , 1953. shalhoub is married to brooke adams since 1992 and they have two children .
