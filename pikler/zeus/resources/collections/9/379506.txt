Debra Winger     mary debra winger ( born may 16 , 1955 ) is an american actress who stars primarily in movies . she was born in cleveland heights , ohio , into a jewish family . she acted in `` urban cowboy '' ( 1980 ) , `` mike 's murder '' and `` everybody wins '' . she took hiaus from acting from 1995 to 2001 , and then resumed her career . she has recently acted in some independent movies .
