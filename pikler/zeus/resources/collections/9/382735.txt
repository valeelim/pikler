Jared Harris     jared francis harris ( born 24 august 1961 ) is an english-irish-welsh actor who is known for his television role as lane pryce in `` mad men '' and as ulysses s. grant in steven spielberg 's 2012 movie `` lincoln '' . he won an sitges film festival in 1999. his father was late irish actor richard harris . harris was born in london , england to an irish father , actor richard harris and to welsh socialite , elizabeth rees-williams . he studied at downside school and at duke university . harris was married to emilia fox from 2005 until they divorced in 2010. since 2011 , he has been partners with allegra riggio .
