Channing Tatum     channing matthew tatum ( born on april 26 , 1980 in cullman , alabama ) is an american movie producer and actor who stars in `` coach carter '' , `` dear john '' , `` 21 jump street '' , its sequel `` 22 jump street '' , `` white house down '' , `` the lego movie '' , `` foxcatcher '' , `` the hateful eight '' , and in `` hail , caesar ! '' . in 2006 , tatum met actress jenna dewan . they became engaged in 2008. they married on july 11 , 2009. their daughter was born in 2013 .
