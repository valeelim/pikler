The Canterbury Tales (movie)    the canterbury tales ( movie ) the canterbury tales is a 1972 italian movie directed by pier paolo pasolini . it is based on the medieval poem `` the canterbury tales '' by geoffrey chaucer . it is the second movie in pasolini 's 'trilogy of life ' . it won the golden bear at the 22nd berlin international film festival . the movie has eight of the 24 tales . it has much nudity , sex , and slapstick humor . many of these scenes are present or at least alluded to in the original . some are pasolini 's own additions . this movie featured tom baker in a small role . he was one of the husbands of the wife of bath . cast . - hugh griffith - sir january - laura betti - the wife of bath - ninetto davoli - perkin - franco citti - the devil - josephine chaplin - may - alan webb - old man - pier paolo pasolini - geoffrey chaucer - j. p. van dyne - the cook - vernon dobtcheff - the franklin - adrian street - fighter - derek deadman - the pardoner ( as derek deadmin )
