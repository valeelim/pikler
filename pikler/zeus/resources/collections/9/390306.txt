Vatican Library     the vatican library is the library of the holy see . its official name is vatican apostolic library ( `` bibliotheca apostolica vaticana '' ) . it was formally established in 1475. its collections are very significant , it contains over 75,000 codices . there are over 150,000 manuscripts , and over 8,300 innucables . in total , there are over 2 million books and manuscripts . there is also a school for librarians , and a workshop to restore ancient tets and to create facsimile copies .
