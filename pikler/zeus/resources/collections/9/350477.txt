Kinokawa, Wakayama    kinokawa , wakayama the city was founded on november 11 , 2005. the towns of kishigawa , kokawa , momoyama , naga and uchita all combined to form the city . kokawa temple ( kokawa-ji ) is a popular sightseeing spot in kinokawa . kishi station in the city has a cat as the stationmaster named tama . tama is a calico cat . people from kinokawa city . - ryumon yasuda - painter and sculptor other websites . - official website
