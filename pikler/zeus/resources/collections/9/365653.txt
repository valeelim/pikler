John O'Hurley     john george o'hurley ( born october 9 , 1954 ) is an american movie , television , stage , and voice actor . he is known for his role as j. peterman in `` seinfeld '' . o'hurley was born on ocotber 9 , 1954 in kittery , maine . he studied at kingswood-oxford school and at providence college . o'hurley was married to eva larue from 1992 until they divorced in 1994. he has been married to lisa mesloh since 2004. with mesloh , he has a son . he was the host of `` family feud '' from 2006 to 2010. he left the show in 2010 and was replaced by steve harvey . other websites . - the official john o'hurley web site
