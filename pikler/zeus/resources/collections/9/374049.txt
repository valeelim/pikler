Girma Wolde-Giorgis     girma wolde-giorgis ( born december 8 , 1924 ) , is a ethiopian politician and was the president of ethiopia from 2001 to 2013. girma wolde-giorgis was born in december 8 , 1924 , in addis ababa , ethiopia . he went to school at the ethiopian orthodox church school and he later joined the teferi mekonnen school in addis ababa until the italian invasion of ethiopia . he was elected the president of ethiopia as a surprise choice on 8 october 2001 by a unanimous vote of the ethiopian parliament . on 9 october 2007 , he was re-elected as president . mulatu teshome was elected the president of ethiopia by a unanimous parliamentary vote on 7 october 2013 .
