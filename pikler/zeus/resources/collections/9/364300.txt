Asking Alexandria     asking alexandria are a british metalcore band from york , north yorkshire . asking alexandria have five members in the band ; the current band members are ben bruce , james cassells , danny worsnop , cameron liddell and sam bettley . they released their first album `` stand up and scream '' on september 15 , 2009. their newest album `` reckless & relentless '' was released on april 5 , 2011. they will release their third studio album in 2013. in 2012 , asking alexandria received an 11th annual independent music award for the best metal/hardcore album for `` reckless & relentless '' . band members . - current - ben bruce - lead guitar , backing vocals ( 2008–present ) - james cassells - drums ( 2008-present ) - cameron liddell - rhythm guitar ( 2008-present ) - sam bettley - bass guitar ( 2009–present ) - ryan binns – keyboards , synthesizers , programming ( 2008 ; 2015-present ) - danny worsnop - lead vocals , keyboards , programming ( 2008–2015 ) - former - joe lancaster – bass guitar ( 2008–2009 ) - denis stoff - lead vocals
