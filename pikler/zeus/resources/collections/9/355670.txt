Mike Henry     mike henry ( born march 25 , 1964 in richmond , virginia ) is an american voice actor , writer , producer , singer , and comedian . he is best known for his work on `` family guy '' where he is a writer , producer , and voice actor . he does the voice for many characters , such as cleveland brown , herbert , bruce , and greased up deaf guy , and consuela . starting with the show 's fifth season , henry had received billing as a main cast member . in 2009 , henry , seth macfarlane , and richard appel created a spin-off of `` family guy '' called `` the cleveland show '' to focus on cleveland and his new family . henry is currently married to sara henry and have no children .
