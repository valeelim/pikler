CNA Center     cna center is a 1,622-ft ( 494 m ) , 44-story high-rise building at 333 south wabash avenue in the loop of chicago , illinois . the building is well known for lighting logos or messages on the front side of the building or for its dark red color . it is the tallest red building in chicago . construction began in march 1970. the building was finished in 1972 and was open to the public in 1973. other websites . - cna center lighted window message archive - informative site
