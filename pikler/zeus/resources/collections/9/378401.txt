American Forces Network     the united states armed forces radio and television service is called american forces network ( afn ) . afn broadcasts radio and television to american soldiers , sailors , airmen , department of defense and other us government civilians and their families stationed outside of the u.s. and on u.s. navy ships . afn broadcasts popular american radio and television programs from u.s. networks . it is sometimes called the armed forces network . afrts , american forces network and afn are registered trademarks of the u.s. department of defense . other websites . - american forces radio and television service - american forces network europe books . - patrick morley : `` 'this is the american forces network ' : the anglo-american battle of the air waves in world war ii '' . praeger publishing ( 2001 ) . - trent christman : `` brass button broadcasters : a lighthearted look at fifty years of military broadcasting '' . turner publishing ( 1992 ) . - `` history of afrts : the first 50 years '' . u.s. government printing office ( 1993 ) .
