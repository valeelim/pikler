Tevin Campbell     tevin campbell ( november 12 , 1976 ) is an american r & b singer-songwriter . he has put out many hit singles . his first hit song was `` tommorow ( a better you , better me ) '' with quincy jones . it was number one on the `` billboard '' hip hop and r & b chart in june 1990. he released `` round and round '' in november 1990. other songs followed over the next two years . he was born in texas .
