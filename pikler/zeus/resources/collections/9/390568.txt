Ray Benson     ray benson ( born march 16 , 1951 ) is an american country singer . he has been the singer and leader of the band asleep at the wheel since it was founded in 1969. with the band , benson has released 20 albums and won 9 grammy awards . in 2003 , he released his first solo album . benson is also a producer . he has worked on albums and singles for dale watson , suzy bogguss , aaron watson , carolyn wonderland , willie nelson , aaron neville , brad paisley , pam tillis , trace adkins , merle haggard , and vince gill . ray benson was born in philadelphia , pennsylvania to jewish family . he started his band , asleep at the wheel , in 1969 .
