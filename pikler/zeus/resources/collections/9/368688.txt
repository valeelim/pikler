1790 United States Census     the united states census of 1790 was the first census done in the united states . it recorded the population of the united states as of census day , august 2 , 1790. in the first census , the population of the united states was 3,929,214. data was collected from all thirteen states ( connecticut , delaware , georgia , maryland , massachusetts , new hampshire , new jersey , new york , north carolina , pennsylvania , rhode island , south carolina , and virginia ) , and districts and territories that would become vermont , kentucky , tennessee , and maine . the records for many states were lost sometime between 1790 and 1830 .
