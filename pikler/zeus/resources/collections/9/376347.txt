Wasylów Wielki     wasylów wielki is a village in the administrative district of gmina ulhówek . it is within tomaszów lubelski county , lublin voivodeship which is in eastern poland . it is close to the border with ukraine . it is about east of ulhówek , east of tomaszów lubelski and south-east of the regional capital lublin . the village has a population of 510 .
