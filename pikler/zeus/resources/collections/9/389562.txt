Classic NES Series     the classic nes series ( famicom mini in japan and nes classics in europe and australia ) are a series of video games originally released for the nintendo entertainment system ( the famicom in japan ) re-released for the game boy advance in 2004 and 2005. they were released for the 20th anniversary of the famicom in japan . 12 games were released in the united states , europe , and australia , while 30 games were released in japan . the games released include `` super mario bros. '' , `` the legend of zelda '' , and `` pac-man '' . a special game boy advance sp which looks like a nes controller was released to go along with the games .
