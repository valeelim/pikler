Roulette     roulette is a casino game named after a french diminutive for `` little wheel '' . in the game , players may choose to place bets on fields which include one or more numbers , the colors red or black , or whether the number is odd or even . to determine the winning number and color , the game wheel is spun in one direction , then a ball is spun in the opposite direction around a track running around the wheel . the ball eventually slows down and falls on to the wheel and into one of 37 ( in french/european roulette ) or 38 ( in american roulette ) colored and numbered pockets on the wheel .
