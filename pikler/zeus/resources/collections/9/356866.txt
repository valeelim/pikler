Gene Siskel     eugene kal `` gene '' siskel ( january 26 , 1946 february 20 , 1999 ) was an american movie critic and journalist for the chicago tribune . along with his friend roger ebert , with ebert they hosted a popular review show `` siskel & ebert at the movies '' from 1975 until his death in 1999. siskel was born in chicago , illinois and was educated at yale university . siskel was married to marlene iglitzen in 1980 until his death , they had 3 children ; kate , calie , and will . siskel died from complications from surgery to remove a brain tumor in evanston , illinois , aged 53. he is buried at westlawn cemetery .
