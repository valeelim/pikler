Nicholas Pileggi     nicholas pileggi ( born february 22 , 1933 ) is an american author , producer and screenwriter . he wrote the book `` goodfellas '' . the movie `` goodfellas '' was based on that book . pileggi was born in new york city , new york on february 22 , 1933. pileggi was married to nora ephron from 1987 until her death in 2012. he has won a bafta award for his screenplay for `` goodfellas '' . other websites . - entry at the nndb
