Syndrome     in psychology and medicine , a syndrome is when there are multiple symptoms . these symptoms may be related . in some cases the way in which they are related is known , in other cases it is not . when both the relationship of symptoms and their causes are known , people generally talk about a disease . symptoms that occur together in similar contexts are often related , or have a common cause . some syndromes , such as down syndrome have only one cause . in the case of down syndrome , the cause is known . other syndromes , such as pakinsonian syndrome may have many causes , and finding the exact cause may be difficult .
