Damage (1992 movie)    damage ( 1992 movie ) damage is a 1992 british melodramatic thriller . it was directed by louis malle . it is based on the novel `` damage '' by josephine hart . juliette binoche is in the cast . `` damage '' was originally rated nc-17 , though was edited downwards to an r rating . the plot is about a politician who falls in love with his son 's girlfriend . miranda richardson was nominated for an academy award and won a bafta in the category of best supporting actress .
