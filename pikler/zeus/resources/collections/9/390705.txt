Deron Quint     deron timothy quint ( born march 12 , 1976 in durham , new hampshire ) is an american professional ice hockey defenceman that currently plays for the traktor chelyabinsk of the kontinental hockey league . quint also played in the national hockey league for 11 seasons . in the nhl , he played for the winnipeg jets , phoenix coyotes , new jersey devils , columbus blue jackets , chicago blackhawks , and the new york islanders .
