Ingrid     ingrid is a female name . the name comes from `` ingfriar '' in old norse . `` ing '' means norse deity . `` friar '' indicates beautiful . the name has been used with many famous persons , one of them being ingrid bergman . a hurricane with the name struck eastern mexico in september 2013. the storm killed 32 and caused $ 1.5 billion in damage . the name `` ingrid '' was then retired .
