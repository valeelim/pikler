Jonathan Goldsmith     jonathan goldsmith ( born september 26 , 1938 ) or jonathan lippe is an american movie , television , stage , and voice actor . he is known for playing `` the most interesting man in the world '' in the dos equis commercials starting in 2007. goldsmith was born in september 26 , 1938 in new york city , new york to a jewish family . he studied at boston university . he was married to barbara goldsmith since 2006. he now lives in marina del rey , california and in manchester , vermont .
