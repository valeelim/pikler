Kristy McNichol     christina ann `` kristy '' mcnichol ( born september 11 1962 ) is an american tv and movie actress . she played letitia `` buddy '' lawrence in the television program `` family '' . she played barbara weston in the sitcom `` empty nest '' . she also acted with cynthia nixon in the 1980 movie `` little darlings '' . she was born in los angeles .
