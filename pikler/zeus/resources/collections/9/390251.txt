Airliner     an airliner is a type of transportation service for transporting passengers and air cargo . such aircraft are most often operated by airlines . the largest airliners are called `` wide-body aircraft '' . these aircraft are frequently called `` twin-aisle aircraft '' because they generally have two separate aisles running from the front to the back of the passenger cabin . these aircraft are usually used for long-haul flights between airline hubs and major cities with many passengers . a smaller , more common class of airliners is the `` narrow-body aircraft '' or `` single aisle '' aircraft . these smaller airliners are generally used for short to medium-distance flights with fewer passengers than their wide-body counterparts . < br >
