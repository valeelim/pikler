Hannover Hauptbahnhof     hannover hauptbahnhof ( german : for `` hanover main station '' ) is the main railway station of hanover , germany . it is the most important transportation service in the city . each day , 250,000 people use the station . it is served by local , regional and long-distance trains . it has 12 tracks on 6 platforms . the statue in front of the main building is showing king ernest augustus i of hanover riding a horse .
