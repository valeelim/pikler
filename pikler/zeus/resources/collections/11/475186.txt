Omar Karami     omar abdul hamid karami ( ‎ ; 7 september 1934 – 1 january 2015 ) was a lebanese politician . he served as the prime minister of lebanon twice : from 24 december 1990 to 13 may 1992 and from 26 october 2004 to 19 april 2005. karami was born in an nouri , near tripoli , french mandate of lebanon to a muslim family . he studied at cairo university in cairo , egypt . his son , faisal , also became a politician . karami died after a long illness on 1 january 2015 in beirut , aged 80 .
