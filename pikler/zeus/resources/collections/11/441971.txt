John Schofield     john schofield was a union general in the american civil war . he was born in 1831 in ohio . he graduated from the united states military acadamy in 1853. he fought mostly in the western theater of the civil war . after the war , he was secretary of war from june 1868 to march 1869. he was the commanding general of the u.s. army from 1888 to 1895 , when he retired . he died in 1905 .
