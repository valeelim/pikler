Arrondissement of Montluçon     the arrondissement of montluçon is an arrondissement of france . it is part of the allier `` département '' . its capital is the city of montluçon . on 4 march 1790 , with the establishment of the department of allier , montluçon was one of the districts of the department . with the establishment of the `` arrondissements '' on 17 february 1800 , montluçon was one of the original `` arrondissements '' . the `` arrondissement '' of montluçon is bordered to the north by the cher ( centre ) department , to the east by the `` arrondissement '' of moulins , to the southeast by the `` arrondissement '' of vichy , to the south by the puy-de-dôme department and to the west by the creuse ( limousin ) department . it is the westernmost of the `` arrondissements '' of the department , with an area of , the second largest in area of the department . it has 115,446 inhabitants and a population density of inhabitants/km² . after the reorganisation of the cantons in france , cantons are not subdivisions of the `` arrondissements '' so they could have `` communes '' that belong to different `` arrondissements '' .
