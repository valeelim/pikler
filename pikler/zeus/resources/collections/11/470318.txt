Ramón Hoyos     ramón hoyos vallejo ( 26 may 1932- 19 november 2014 ) was a colombian road bicycle racer . he won the men 's individual road race at the 1959 pan american games . he represented his native country twice at the summer olympics ; in 1956 and 1960. hoyos died of a heart attack in bogota , colombia , aged 82 .
