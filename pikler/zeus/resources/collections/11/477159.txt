Saw II     saw ii is a 2005 canadian-american horror film . saw ii is a sequel to 2004 's `` saw '' . it is the second installment in the seven-part saw franchise . it was released october 28 , 2005. the movie was directed and co-written by darren lynn bousman . it was co-written with series creator leigh whannell . saw ii stars donnie wahlberg , franky g , dina meyer , erik knudsen , and tobin bell . rotten tomatoes gave it an audience score of 59 % .
