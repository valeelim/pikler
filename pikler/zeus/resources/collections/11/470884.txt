Sisavath Keobounphanh     sisavath keobounphanh ( ; born 1 may 1928 ) is a laotian politician . he served as vice president of laos from 1996 to 1998. he was also the third prime minister of laos from 1998 to 2001. he is president of the lao front for national construction from 2001 until 2011 , when he was succeeded by phandoungchit vongsa . he is a member of the lao people 's revolutionary party . other websites . - world statesmen
