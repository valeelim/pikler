Marcel Breuer     marcel lajos breuer ( pronounced broy-ər ; 22 may 1902 – 1 july 1981 ) , was a hungarian-american modernist , architect and furniture designer . he was born in pécs . one of the masters of modernism , breuer used the ideas he had developed in the carpentry shop at the bauhaus . his personal architecture made him one of the world ’ s most popular architects . he died in new york city .
