Alphons Egli     alphons egli ( born 8 october 1924 - died 5 august 2016 ) was a swiss politician . he was a member of the swiss federal council ( 1983-1986 ) . he was elected to the federal council of switzerland on 8 december 1982 and handed over office on 31 december 1986. he had connections to the christian democratic people 's party of switzerland . he was also president of the confederation in 1986. egli was a citizen of entlebuch and lucerne .
