Thor (movie)    thor ( movie ) thor is a 2011 american superhero movie , starring chris hemsworth , natalie portman and tom hiddleston . it is the first movie of the series based on the marvel comics character `` thor '' . it was directed by kenneth branagh and produced by marvel studios . thor is heir to the throne of asgard , world of the gods . with his vigorous disposition , he is deported to earth in the form of a mortal , for disobeying his father odin and breaking into jotunheim , home to the enemy frost giants . thor lands in new mexico and meets and develops romance with astrophysicist jane foster . meanwhile in asgard , thor 's brother loki plans to ascend to the throne and sends the destroyer to kill thor .
