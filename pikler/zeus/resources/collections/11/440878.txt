Stuart Blumberg     stuart ethan blumberg ( born july 19 , 1969 ) is an american screenwriter , actor , producer and director . he was nominated for an oscar for best original screenplay in 2010 for co-writing `` the kids are all right '' . he made his directorial debut with the hollywood movie `` thanks for sharing '' starring mark ruffalo , tim robbins , gwyneth paltrow , josh gad , joely richardson , and alecia moore . he is going to direct the john grisham 's adaption of `` the testament '' .
