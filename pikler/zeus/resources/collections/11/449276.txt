Power Rangers In Space     power rangers in space is an american television series and the sixth season of the `` power rangers '' franchise . it is based on the super sentai series `` denji sentai megaranger '' . it is the sequel to power rangers turbo . the turbo rangers head for space and meet andros . he brings them the astro morphers and they become the power rangers in space . they battle the evil astronema and her monsters . a sequel called power rangers lost galaxy started in 1999 .
