Iveta Bartošová     iveta bartošová ( 8 april 1966 – 29 april 2014 ) was a czech singer , actress and vocalist . she played in the music poll zlatý slavík ( 1986 , 1990 and 1991 ) . she was also known for her life style attracting the attention of the czech tabloid media . she was born in čeladná , czech republic . on 29 april 2014 , she committed suicide by throwing herself under a train in uhříněves , prague . many people think that the reason why she committed suicide was because of the media harassing her .
