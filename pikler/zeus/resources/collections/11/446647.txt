Tropical Storm Thelma     tropical storm thelma was a 1991 tropical storm . its winds were near 45-50 mph . it killed between 5,000 and 8,000 people in the philippines . it hit negros occidental , leyte island , and vietnam . the storm caused also about $ 28 million in damage . after this , the name `` thelma '' was not used for tropical storms . it was replaced by `` teresa '' .
