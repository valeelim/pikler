Toini Gustafsson     toini gustafsson rönnlund , born `` toini gustafsson '' , 17 january 1938 in suomussalmi , finland ) is a swedish former cross country skier . during the 1968 winter olympics in grenoble she won two individual gold medals . in 1968 , she was awarded the svenska dagbladet gold medal . she was married with swedish cross-country skier assar rönnlund until he died in january 2011 .
