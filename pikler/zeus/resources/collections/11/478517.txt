Ricardo dos Santos     ricardo `` ricardinho '' dos santos ( 23 may 1990 – 20 january 2015 ) was a brazilian surfer . he reached the quarterfinals at the 2012 billabong pro event in tahiti , defeating many people including kelly slater . after the event , he was presented with the andy irons award given to the most determined competitor . he also reached a career high association of surfing professionals ( asp ) qs ranking of 62 in 2011. he was sponsored by australian surfing brand billabong . ricardo dos santos was born in palhoça , santa catarina . ricardo dos santos was shot dead by an off duty police officer on 20 january 2015 in são josé , santa catarina , aged 24 .
