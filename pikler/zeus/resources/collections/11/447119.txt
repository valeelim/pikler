Geoffrey Palmer (actor)    geoffrey palmer ( actor ) geoffrey dyson palmer , obe ( born 4 june 1927 ) is an english actor . he is known for his roles in british television sitcoms . palmer played jimmy anderson in `` the fall and rise of reginald perrin '' , ben parkinson in `` butterflies '' and lionel hardcastle in `` as time goes by '' . palmer was born in london , england .
