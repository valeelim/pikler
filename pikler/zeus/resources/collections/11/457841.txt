Robert William Fisher     robert william `` bobby '' fisher , sr. ( born april 13 , 1961 ) is an american fugitive . he is wanted for the murder of his wife and their two children . the murders happened on april 10 , 2001 in scottsdale , arizona . on june 29 , 2002 , he was named by the fbi as the 475th fugitive on their list of ten most wanted fugitives .
