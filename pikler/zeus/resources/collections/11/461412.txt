Desouk     desouk ( `` '' ) is a city in northern egypt . it is east of alexandria . it belongs to kafr el-sheikh governorate . as of 2009 , 129,604 people lived there . the city of buto , northeast desouk was the capital of lower egypt , and the seat of the kings of northern egypt . ancient papyri show it was called pe `` dep '' . the greek name was `` botous '' or `` botosus '' , latin `` boutos '' , and coptic name is `` buto '' , after then arabic name is `` ebto '' . the city was the capital of province no . xix called `` imty phw '' . desouk city is a capital of desouk that includes 2 districts , 11 main villages , 33 villages and 253 small villages ( ezba ) . president city and markaz is ahmed basiouny zeid since september 3 2013 .
