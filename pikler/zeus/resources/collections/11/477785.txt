SummerSlam (2000)    summerslam ( 2000 ) summerslam ( 2000 ) was a professional wrestling pay-per-view show made by world wrestling federation ( wwf ) . it was held on august 27 , 2000 at raleigh entertainment and sports arena in raleigh , north carolina . it was the thirteenth summerslam event held by the wwf . the main event saw the rock defeat triple h and kurt angle in a triple threat match to retain his wwf championship . the rock won the match by pinfall after he hit triple h with the people 's elbow . a featured match on the undercard saw kane and the undertaker fight to a no contest . the match ended in a no contest after undertaker unmasked kane . another main match on the undercard was edge and christian ( c ) , the hardy boyz ( matt hardy and jeff hardy ) and the dudley boyz ( bubba ray dudley and d-von dudley ) in the first tables , ladders , and chairs match for the wwf tag team championship . edge and christian won the match after they climbed the ladder and grabbed the belts that were hanging above the ring . other websites .
