Trading Spouses     trading spouses : meet your new mommy is a television show that aired in 2004-2007. season 1 was released in 2004-2005 , season 2 was released in 2005-2006 , and season 3 was released in 2006-2007. marguerite `` god warrior '' perrin ( mother of ashley perrin and wife of barry perrin ) ( where she appeared in the tyra banks show in 2006 ) ( born 1961 ) appeared in this show .
