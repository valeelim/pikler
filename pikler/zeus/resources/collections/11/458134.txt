Idde Schultz     idde schultz , `` eva ann-ida schultz '' , born 1 september 1963 in stockholm , sweden is a swedish female singer and guitarist . she 's a member in the band docenterna and former member of lars winnerbäck 's band hovet . she 's sister to irma schultz keller . she has scored record successes at the swedish charts during the mid-late 1990 's and the 2000 's . idde schultz is music teacher at fryshusets gymnasium . discography . sulo & idde . - kocksgatan revisited together with sören `` sulo '' karlsson 2011 zzzang tumb . - 1982 - 37 minuter i stockholms city , stranded rekords - 1983 - zzzang tumb , stranded rekords torpederna . - 1993 - `` innan himlen faller '' solo . - 1995 – `` idde schultz '' - 1997 – `` vad man gör ( och inte gör ) '' lars winnerbäck & hovet . - 1998 – `` med solen i ögonen '' - 2001 – `` singel '' - 2001 – `` live för dig '' - 2003 – `` söndermarken '' - 2004 – `` live i linköping '' ( concert dvd ) - 2005 – `` stort liv '' ( ep ) - 2006 – `` efter nattens bränder '' ( compilation album ) hovet . - 2004 – `` hovet 2004 '' anna + idde .
