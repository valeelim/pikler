Fay McKenzie     fay mckenzie ( born february 19 , 1918 ) is an american actress . she is best known for her leading lady roles . she appeared in two movies with gene autry in 1941 and 1942. mckenzie was the daughter of the actor robert mckenzie and actress eva mckenzie . one of her first roles was at the age of six in `` the dramatic life of abraham lincoln '' ( 1924 ) where she played sarah lincoln . she married screenwriter tom waldman . mckenzie was born on february 19 , 1918 in hollywood , california .
