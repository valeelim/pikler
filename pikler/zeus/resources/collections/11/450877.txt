Theo James     theodore peter james kinnaird taptiklis ( born 16 december 1984 ) , better known as theo james , is an english actor . he is known for portraying jed harper in `` bedlam '' , walter william clark , jr. in the crime drama `` golden boy '' and as `` four '' , a military instructor , in the film adaptation of veronica roth 's dystopian sci-fi novel , `` divergent '' .
