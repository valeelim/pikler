YWCA-YMCA of Sweden     ywca-ymca of sweden ( ) is the sweden-branch of ymca and ywca . it was established in 1966 when ymca of sweden and ywca of sweden merged . in 2011 the organization decided to use the term `` kfum '' in promotion with `` m '' standing for `` människor '' ( `` people '' ) . earlier the `` m '' stood for `` män '' ( men ) ywca-ymca of sweden has 60 000 members . it has 4 000 local associations . several swedish ywca-ymca associations have been successful in sports . other websites . - official website
