Synthetic fuel     a synthetic fuel ( or synfuel ) is a fuel in liquid or gas form that can be manufactured from coal or in the form of oil extracted from shale or tar sands . it can also be made from non-food crops . tar sands are a combination of sand and a thick oil called bitumen . this , along with oil shale , which is a layer of rock riddled with hydrocarbons in the form of kerogen , can be heated with steam to remove the crude oil in the two substances . like oil , it must be refined before being put to use .
