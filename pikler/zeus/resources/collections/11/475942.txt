Philippe Honoré     philippe honoré ( ; november 25 , 1941 – january 7 , 2015 ) , known by the pen-name honoré , was a french cartoonist . he was born in vichy , france . honoré worked with the newspaper company charlie hebdo during most of his career . he was a staff cartoonist there . honoré died on january 7 , 2015 , during the january 2015 shooting attack on the `` charlie hebdo '' newspaper office .
