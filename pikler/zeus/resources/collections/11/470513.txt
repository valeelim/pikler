Incineration     incineration is a form of waste disposal in which waste is burnt . incineration converts the waste into ash , flue gas , and heat . the first incinerators for waste disposal were built in nottingham by manlove , alliott & co. ltd. in 1874. they were originally known as destructors . the first incinerator in the us was built in 1885 on governors island in new york , ny .
