Volker Schlöndorff     volker schlöndorff ( born 31 march 1939 ) is a german movie director , screenwriter , and movie producer . he is a very important director of the new german cinema of the late 1960s and early 1970s . he won an oscar as well as the palme d'or at the 1979 cannes film festival for `` the tin drum '' ( 1979 ) , the movie version of the novel by nobel prize-winning author günter grass . other websites . - volker schlöndorff faculty website @ european graduate school . biography and bibliography . - volker schlöndorff 's cinématon - a 4 minutes online portrait by gérard courant - volker schlöndorff collection at deutsches filminstitut , frankfurt ( german )
