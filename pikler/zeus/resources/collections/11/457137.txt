Kreuztal     kreuztal is a town in the siegen-wittgenstein district , in north rhine-westphalia , germany . as of 2012 , 30,827 people live there . the first documents about the area go back to 1067. the town began to develop in 1861 after the `` ruhr-sieg '' railroad was made , making kreuztal a railroad hub at that time . the town of kreuztal was made on 1 january 1969 .
