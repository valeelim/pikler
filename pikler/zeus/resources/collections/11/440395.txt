Wayne Brady     wayne alphonso brady ( born june 2 , 1972 ) is an american actor , singer , comedian and television personality . he is best known for his work as a regular on the american version of the improvisational comedy television show `` whose line is it anyway ? '' . he has also daytime talk show `` the wayne brady show '' and the original host of fox 's `` do n't forget the lyrics ! '' . brady was born in columbus , georgia . he moved to orlando , florida as a young child to live with his grandmother and aunt .
