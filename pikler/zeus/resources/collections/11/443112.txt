Officer Candidate School     officer candidate school or officer cadet school ( ocs ) are military institutions which train civilians and enlisted personnel to be officers in the armed forces of a country . different countries and services run their officer candidate schools differently . most schools are for less than one year . an ocs is different from a service academies which include academic instruction leading to a bachelor degree . another difference is that an ocs is taught almost exclusively by the military . other websites . - officer candidate schools
