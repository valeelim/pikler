Matched     matched is a 2010 dystopian novel by ally condie . the book was followed by two sequels , 'crossed ' and `` reached '' . `` matched '' tells the story of seventeen year old cassia reyes . she lives in a world where at age seventeen , everyone is matched with who they will marry . you marry at age 21 and have children at age 24. and you die at age 80. however , despite being matched , cassia finds that she loves someone else .
