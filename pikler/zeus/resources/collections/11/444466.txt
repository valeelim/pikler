Leopoldo Calvo Sotelo     leopoldo ramón pedro calvo-sotelo y bustelo , 1st marquis of the ría of ribadeo , grandee of spain ( ; 14 april 1926 – 3 may 2008 ) was a spanish politician . he was the prime minister during the end of francisco franco 's dictatorship in spain . he died of cardiac arrest at his home in pozuelo de alarcón , madrid , aged 82 .
