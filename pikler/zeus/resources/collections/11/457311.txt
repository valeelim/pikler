Alan Bennett     alan bennett ( born 9 may 1934 ) is an english playwright , screenwriter , actor and author . his work includes `` the madness of george iii '' and its movie adaptation , the series of monologues `` talking heads '' , the play and the movie `` the history boys '' , and popular audio books , including his readings of `` alice 's adventures in wonderland '' and `` winnie-the-pooh '' . bennett was born in armley in leeds . he has a degree in history from exeter college , oxford . he is openly gay .
