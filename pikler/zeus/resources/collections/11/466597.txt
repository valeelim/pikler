Patrick Paul D’Souza    patrick paul d ’ souza patrick paul d'souza ( august 28 , 1928 october 16 , 2014 ) was a roman catholic bishop . he was ordained to the priesthood on december 20 , 1953. d'souza was named bishop of the diocese of banaras on june 5 , 1970. he was ordained bishop on august 8 , 1970. the diocese was renamed diocese of varanasi in 1971. d'souza retired on february 24 , 2007. he was born in bendur , mangalore . d'souza died in mumbai , india , aged 84 .
