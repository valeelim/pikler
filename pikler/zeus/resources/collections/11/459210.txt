Rezső Bálint     rezső bálint ( october 22 , 1874 – may 23 , 1929 ) was a hungarian neurologist and psychiatrist . he discovered bálint 's syndrome . bálint studied medicine in budapest , graduating in 1897. he habilitated in 1910 , became extraordinary professor in 1914 and full professor in 1917. he died of thyroid cancer in 1929 .
