Pooh's Heffalump Halloween Movie    pooh 's heffalump halloween movie pooh 's heffalump halloween movie is a 2005 american direct-to-video animated movie . it was produced by john a. smith and was directed by elliot m. bour and saul andrew blinkoff . it is a sequel to `` pooh 's heffalump movie '' which was released in february 11 , 2005. pooh 's heffalump halloween movie was released on september 13 , 2005 in north america . the movie is about lumpy the heffalump 's adventure with his friends during his first halloween with them . it is the first winnie the pooh movie since paul winchell 's death and is the last movie to feature john fiedler as the voice of piglet , as fielder died in 2005. cast . - david ogden stiers as the narrator - jimmy bennett as roo - kyle stanger as lumpy the heffalump - peter cullen as eeyore - jim cummings as winnie-the-pooh and tigger - john fiedler as piglet - ken sansom as rabbit - kath soucie as kanga other websites . - official website
