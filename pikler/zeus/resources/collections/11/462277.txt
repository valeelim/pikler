Fatima Bhutto     fatima bhutto ( born 1982 ) is a young pakistani poet and writer . her main published works so far are `` whispers of the desert '' ( poems , 2006 ) , `` songs of blood and sword '' ( memoirs , 2010 ) and `` the shadow of the crescent moon '' ( fiction , 2013 ) . her writing is serious and make readers think about social problems and issues facing developing countries like pakistan . she also writes about the traditional male-dominated rural society and how women are abused and treated with cruelty in such a setting . she is the niece of former pakistani prime minister benazir bhutto and grand-daughter of former prime minister zulfiqar ali bhutto .
