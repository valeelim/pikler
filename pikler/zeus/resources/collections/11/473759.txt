1980 Republican National Convention     the 1980 national convention of the republican party of the united states . it was held at the joe louis arena in detroit , michigan . it took place from july 14 to july 17 , 1980. the 32nd republican national convention nominated former governor ronald w. reagan of california for president . it also nominated former congressman george h. w. bush of texas for vice president . other websites . - ronald reagan 's nomination acceptance speech
