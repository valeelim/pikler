Hurricane Cleo     hurricane cleo was an intense hurricane that happened in late august-early september 1964. the highest winds speeds were measured at 140 miles per hour on 23 august . the storm went across guadeloupe and hispaniola , killing 214. it later struck from southeastern florida to virginia . overall damage was just over $ 198 million in 1964 usd . due to high death count and severe damage , the name cleo was retired in the spring of 1965 .
