Aulis Rytkönen     taavi aulis rytkönen ( 5 january 1929 – 16 april 2014 ) was a finnish footballer . he became the country 's first professional player when he signed for france 's toulouse fc in 1952. he was born in karttula , finland . he played for finland at the 1952 summer olympics in helsinki . he also worked as national coach from 1975 to 1978. he was chosen finland 's player of the year in 1949 , 1950 , and 1952. rytkönen died in helsinki , finland , aged 85 .
