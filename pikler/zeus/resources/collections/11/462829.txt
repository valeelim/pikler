Azerbaijan Chess Federation     the azerbaijan chess federation ( , `` aşf '' ) is the national chess organization in azerbaijan and is a member of the world chess federation fide . the azerbaijan chess federation was founded in 1920 when azerbaijan became a member of the soviet union as the azerbaijan ssr the same year . it was reorganized in 1992 , after azerbaijan gained its independence with the dissolution of the soviet union , and became a member of fide the same year . the headquarters of the azerbaijan chess federation is in the capital city , baku . dr. elman rustamov is the president of the azerbaijan chess federation since 2007. the executive committee includes : - president – dr. elman rustamov - vice-president – faik hasanov - vice-president – mahir mammadov - general secretary – ibrahim mammadov the federation organizes the following chess competitions : - azerbaijani chess championship - azerbaijan women 's chess championship - azerbaijan children 's chess championship related pages . - list of azerbaijani chess players
