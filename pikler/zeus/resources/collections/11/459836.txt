Mariana Briski     mariana marcela briski ( 14 september 1965 – 14 august 2014 ) was an argentine actress , director , producer , screenwriter , author , and professor . she was known for her roles in `` it 's not you , it 's me '' ( 2004 ) , `` the wind '' ( 2005 ) , and `` salsipuedes '' ( 2011 ) . briski was also known for her television role in `` primicias '' . briski was born in córdoba , argentina . she was married to hernán ventura from 1988 until her death in 2014. they had one son , pedro briski . briski died from lung cancer and breast cancer in buenos aires , argentina , aged 48. she was of jewish descent .
