Louis de Funès     louis de funès ( 31 july 1914 – 27 january 1983 ) , born louis germain david de funès de galarza , was a popular french actor and comedian . he is known for working with bourvil and fernandel . he was also a movie director . he appeared in over 200 movies since his career began in 1945. he died from a massive stroke in nantes , loire-atlantique , france , aged 68 .
