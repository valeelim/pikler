Richard Wilbur     richard purdy wilbur ( born march 1 , 1921 ) is an american poet and literary translator . he became the second poet laureate consultant in poetry to the library of congress in 1987. he received the pulitzer prize for poetry two times ; in 1957 and again in 1989. wilbur was born in new york city , new york . he studied at amherst college and at harvard university . he was married to mary charlotte hayes ward until her death in 2007. they had four children . other websites . - ernest hilbert reviews richard wilbur 's `` collected poems '' for the `` new york sun ''
