Józef Czapski     józef czapski ( april 3 , 1896 — january 12 , 1993 ) was a polish artist , author , and critic . he was an officer of the polish army during world war ii . czapski was known for many things . as a painter , he is known for his work in the kapist movement . he is mostly known for being the founder of kultura monthly . it is one of the most important monthly journals in poland during the 20th century . czapski was born in prague . he studied at the university of saint petersburg . czapski died on january 12 , 1993 in warsaw from natural causes , aged 96 .
