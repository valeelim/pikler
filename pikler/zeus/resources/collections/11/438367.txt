Muonio     muonio is a municipality in lapland , finland . as of january 2014 , about 2,385 people lived there . neighbouring municipalities are enontekiö , kittilä and kolari . on the western side of it is sweden . distance to tornio is about 260 km and to kilpisjärvi , 200 km . there are a lot of lakes in the area of muonio . other websites . - community website
