Harry Melling (actor)    harry melling ( actor ) harry edward melling ( born 13 march 1989 ) is an english actor . he is best known for playing dudley dursley in the harry potter films . melling was born in london . he became a member of the millfield theatre youth drama group when he turned 9. melling appeared in five of the harry potter films as harry potter 's cousin dudley dursley . after the harry potter films ended melling went to lamda drama school .
