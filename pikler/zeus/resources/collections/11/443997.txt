Mark Kozelek     mark kozelek ( born january 24 , 1967 ) is an american singer . in 1989 he started the rock band red house painters . in 1992 the band were signed to 4ad . in 2000 kozelek released his first solo ep `` rock 'n ' roll singer '' . `` pitchfork '' gave it a score of 6.1 out of 10. in 2001 red house painters broke up . in 2002 kozelek started sun kil moon .
