Duke of Marlborough     duke of marlborough is a title in the peerage of england . it was created by queen anne in 1702 for john churchill , 1st duke of marlborough . the name of the dukedom refers to marlborough in wiltshire . it is the only current dukedom in the peerages of england , great britain or the united kingdom that can pass to a woman and through a woman . the family have long been involved with the politics of the day . they are also famous for their traditional home which is `` blenheim palace '' . this is the only non-royal palace in the united kingdom . the family still live at the palace . the duke is entitled to the style of address of your grace . list of dukes . 1. john churchill , 1st duke of marlborough .
