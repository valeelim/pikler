Gregorio Conrado Álvarez    gregorio conrado álvarez gregorio conrado álvarez armelino ( november 26 , 1925 – december 28 , 2016 ) was a uruguayan general and former dictator . he served as the `` de facto '' president of uruguay from 1981 until 1985. on october 22 , 2009 , he was convicted for 37 counts of murder and human rights violations . he was sentenced to 25 years in prison . however , he was not in court to hear the verdict as he was ill. he was born in montevideo .
