Gina Raimondo     gina marie raimondo ( born may 17 , 1971 ) is an american politician , businesswoman , and venture capitalist . she is the 75th and current governor of the state of rhode island . raimondo , a member of the democratic party , is the first woman to serve as governor of rhode island . she has served as the general treasurer for the state of rhode island from 2011 through 2015 .
