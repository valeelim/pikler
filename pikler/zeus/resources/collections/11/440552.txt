Abdul-Karim Mousavi Ardebili     abdul-karim mousavi ardebili ( ; januray 28 , 1926 - november 23 , 2016 ) was an iranian politician and muslim leader . he served as the head of judiciary system of iran for about 8 years after terror of dr. beheshti until death of khomeini . he was a marja of the twelver branch of shia islam . he was born in ardabil . he died in tehran on 23 november 2016 .
