Matt Kemp     matthew ryan `` matt '' kemp ( born september 23 , 1984 ) is an american professional baseball player . he plays for the atlanta braves . he plays the position of outfielder . he has previously played for the los angeles dodgers and the san diego padres . his career began in 2006. kemp was drafted by the dodgers in the sixth round of the 2003 draft . he went to the major leagues after four years in the minor leagues . he did not become a full-time player until 2008. he became the everyday starting center fielder . he has been named to two all-star teams . he has won two gold glove awards . he has won two silver slugger awards . other websites . - official site - matt kemp official site
