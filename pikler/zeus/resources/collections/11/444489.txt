Now You See Me (movie)    now you see me ( movie ) now you see me is a 2013 american caper thriller movie . it was produced by bobby cohen , alex kurtzman , and roberto orci . the movie was directed by louis leterrier . now you see me was released on may 31 , 2013 in north america and on july 31 , 2013 in france . the movie gained mixed reviews from critics and hold a 50 % rating on rotten tomatoes . the movie was a box office success and gained a sequel . cast . - jesse eisenberg as j. daniel atlas - mark ruffalo as dylan rhodes - woody harrelson as merritt mckinney - isla fisher as henley reeves - dave franco as jack wilder - mélanie laurent as alma dray - morgan freeman as thaddeus bradley - michael caine as arthur tressler - michael kelly as agent fuller - common as agent evans - josé garcia as étienne forcier - elias koteas as lionel shrike ( uncredited ) other websites . - official website
