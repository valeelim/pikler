Alok Nembang     alok nembang ( 1973 - 6 november 2014 ) was a nepali movie director . he was also a well-known music video director in nepal for several decades . he was known for his movie `` kohi mero '' ( 2010 ) . on november 6 , 2014 , alok was found dead at his home in kathmandu in an apparent suicide .
