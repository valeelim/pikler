Dylan Tombides     dylan james tombides ( 8 march 1994 – 18 april 2014 ) was an australian association footballer . he played as a striker for west ham united and the australia u17 and australia u23 teams . he was born in perth , western australia . tombides made his professional debut for west ham on 25 september 2012 in a 4–1 home defeat to wigan athletic in the league cup . he came on as an 84th-minute substitute for gary o'neil . tombides died from testicular cancer on 18 april 2014 in london , england . he was 20 .
