Pieksämäki     pieksämäki is a town in southern savonia , finland . in finland it is called a city . as of january 2014 , almost 19,300 people lived there . neighbouring municipalities are hankasalmi , joroinen , juva , kangasniemi , leppävirta , mikkeli , rautalampi and suonenjoki . most people speak finland . pieksämäki was established in 1930. it became a city in 1962. other websites . - the official pieksämäki page - culturesite , `` poleeni ''
