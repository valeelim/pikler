Amazon Echo     amazon echo is a voice command device . it is a product of amazon.com . the product can play music and answers questions . amazon echo is 9-inch tall . it is a cylinder-shaped device . it responds to the name `` alexa '' . this can be changed to another name . amazon echo was released on november 6 , 2014 through amazon.com . many critics gave it positive reviews . gizmodo believed that amazon echo is `` the most innovative device amazon made in years . '' many critics believe amazon echo is similar to apple inc. 's siri . amazon echo is the second device released by amazon.com in 2014 , behind amazon fire tv .
