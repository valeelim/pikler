Peninsula Campaign     the peninsula campaign was a series of battles during the american civil war . the campaign happened from march to july 1862. the union army of the potomac , commanded by george b. mcclellan , tried to capture the confederate capital of richmond , virginia by moving westward on the virginia peninsula . the confederate army of northern virginia , commanded by joseph e. johnston , tried to stop mcclellan . after johnston was wounded on may 31st at the battle of seven pines , robert e. lee took command of the army . lee then attacked mcclellan during the seven days battles . after the battles , mcclellan 's army was transferred back to washington , d.c .
