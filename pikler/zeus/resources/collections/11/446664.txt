Carla Bozulich     carla bozulich is an american musician . she is known for being the lead singer of the geraldine fibbers . she was born in new york city . in 1993 `` happy days , sweetheart '' , the only album by ethyl meatplow , a band bozulich was a member of , was released . in 2003 she released her first solo album , `` the red headed stranger '' . in 2012 she released an album with ches smith . other websites . - official website
