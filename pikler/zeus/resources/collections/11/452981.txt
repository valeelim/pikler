Chefchaouen     chefchaouen or chaouen ( arabic : شفشاون/الشاون , shafshāwan ) is a small important historical and tourist city in the north of maghreb . chefchaouen is located in the rif mountains of morocco . many moriscos and jews settled in this city after the spanish reconquista in the late middle ages . related pages . - morocco - essaouira - fes other websites . - chaouen info - information about the city and province of chefchaouen
