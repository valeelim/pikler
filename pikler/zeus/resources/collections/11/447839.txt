Pandoravirus     pandoravirus is a genus of very large viruses , with the largest genomes of any viral genus . like the other very large viruses `` mimivirus '' , `` pithovirus '' and `` megavirus '' , `` pandoravirus '' infects amoebas . its genome , with 1.9 to 2.5 megabases of dna , is twice as large as that of `` megavirus '' . it differs greatly from the other large viruses in appearance and in genome structure . although the capsid of `` pithovirus '' is 50 % larger in size , `` pandoravirus '' has the largest genome among all viruses .
