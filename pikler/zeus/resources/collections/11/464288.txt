Mario Maurer     mario maurer ( , , born december 4 , 1988 ) is a thai model and actor . he started modeling at age 16. he got fame for his acting in the 2007 film , `` the love of siam '' and the 2010 sleeper hit , `` first love '' . he was the lead star of thailand 's highest grossing movie of all time . maurer 's interests include skateboarding , hip-hop , and cars . he is of thai chinese and german descent . he can not speak german . he only understands some mandarin . he is a native speaker of thai . he speaks english well . he converted to roman catholicism from buddhism . on june 27 , 2011 , his father roland died of a heart attack resulting from diabetes . he is close to his older brother . his brother is a hip-hop artist and actor . he was in a relationship with actress gubgib sumontip . they ended their nine year relationship in january 2013 .
