Jadwiga Piłsudska     jadwiga piłsudska-jaraczewska ( 28 february 1920 – 16 november 2014 ) was a polish pilot and architect . she served in the air transport auxiliary during the second world war . she is the one of two daughters of marshal and naczelnik józef piłsudski . she was born in warsaw , poland . piłsudska died on 16 november 2014 in warsaw at the age of 94 .
