Kåre Valebrokk     kåre valebrokk ( 17 december 1940 – 9 february 2013 ) was a norwegian journalist , editor and television executive . he was editor-in-chief and administrative director of tv 2 from october 1999 until june 2007 , when he retired . he was the father of economist and editor per valebrokk ( born 1972 ) . he was born in kristiansand , vest-agder . valebrokk died from in his sleep from natural causes on 9 february 2013 in oslo . he was 72 years old .
