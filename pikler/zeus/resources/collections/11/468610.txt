Mumu     mumu ( ) is a russian tale . it was published in 1852 by ivan turgenev in the political magazine : `` sovremennik '' . the story is about the relationship between gerasim - a deaf mute servant - and his dog . the story changes to an elderly widow-woman who is gerasim 's boss . the woman shows her cruel side when she tells him to get rid his pet . related pages . - ivan turgenev - russian literature other websites . - oreshin k. , `` история рассказа `` муму '' '' . journal `` smena '' .
