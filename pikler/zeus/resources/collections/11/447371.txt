Division of Forrest     the division of forrest is an australian electoral division in western australia . it was set up in 1922 and is named for sir john forrest , the first premier of western australia and a federal cabinet minister . it is in the south-western corner of the state , and includes the towns of augusta , australind , bunbury , busselton , capel , collie , donnybrook , dunsborough , eaton , harvey , margaret river , nannup and yallingup . election results . - 2004 election results - 2007 election results - 2010 election results - 2013 election results other websites . - division of forrest - australian electoral commission - nola marino 's website
