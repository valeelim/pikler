Yngve A. A. Larsson     yngve axel andreas larsson ( 9 january 1917 – 22 march 2014 ) was a swedish pediatrician , medicine professor and diabetologist . he was best known for his work at the ethio–swedish pediatric clinic ( espc ) at haile selassie i university in addis ababa , ethiopia from 1965 until 1970. he was born in stockholm . larsson died from natural causes on 22 march 2014 in lidingö . he was 97 years old .
