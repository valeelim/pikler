Bob Wareing     robert nelson `` bob '' wareing , commonly known as bob wareing ( 20 august 1930 - 29 august 2014 ) was a british politician . he was the member of parliament ( mp ) for liverpool west derby from 1983 to 2010. until november 2007 , he was a labour mp , but resigned in may 2010. wareing died in liverpool , merseyside , england , aged 84 .
