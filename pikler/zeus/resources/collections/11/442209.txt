Zdzisław Żygulski, Jr.    zdzisław żygulski , jr. zdzisław żygulski ( 18 august 1921 – 14 may 2015 ) was a polish art historian and professor . he works at the academy of fine arts in kraków , anglicist . he has been the curator of the arms and armour section of the czartoryski museum since 1949. żygulski was born in borysław , poland on 18 august 1921. he died in krakow , poland , aged 93 .
