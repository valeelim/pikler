Ľudovít Lehen    ľudovít lehen ľudovít lehen ( 3 june 1925 – 12 may 2014 ) was a slovakian painter and chess composer . his career began in 1977 with composing chess problems . he was arrested for six years . he was arrested for being a communist in then-czechoslovakia . lehen died in bratisalva , slovakia , aged 88 .
