Dalia Grybauskaitė     dalia grybauskaitė ( born 1 march 1956 ) is a lithuanian politician . she became the president of lithuania on 12 july 2009. she was re-elected in may 2014. she is the country 's first female president and the first to be elected for a second term . she was vice-minister of foreign affairs and minister of finance , also european commissioner for financial programming and the budget from 2004 to 2009. she is often referred to as the `` iron lady '' or the `` steel magnolia '' . other websites . - official website of the president of the republic of lithuania - lithuania set for energy rethink -interview with bbc . bbc world news - lunch with the ft : dalia grybauskaite . financial times - grybauskaitė run for the president ( video )
