Hook (movie)    hook ( movie ) hook is a 1991 american fantasy-comedy movie directed by steven spielberg . it was produced by frank marshall , kathleen kennedy , and gerald r. molen . it was written by james v. hart and malia scotch marmo . it was composed by john williams . it is based on the book of the same name . it stars robin williams , dustin hoffman , julia roberts , gwyneth paltrow , maggie smith , and bob hoskins . it was released to positive reviews on december 11 , 1991 .
