Kent vs Lancashire at Canterbury     kent vs lancashire at canterbury is a oil painting by albert chevallier tayler in 1906. the chairman of the kent county cricket club , lord harris , wanted to celebrate their first county championship title win . tayler painted the picture after spending time with each of the kent players one by one . the painting mostly stayed at kent 's st lawrence ground until 1999 when it was moved to lord 's cricket ground . kent could no longer afford to insure it . in 2006 , kent sold the painting to the andrew brownsword art foundation at an auction . the painting is currently on display at the lord 's pavilion .
