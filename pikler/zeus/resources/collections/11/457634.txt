The Runaways (movie)    the runaways ( movie ) the runaways is a 2010 biographical movie . dakota fanning plays cherie currie . kristen stewart plays the singer joan jett . this movie is about the 1970s all-girl rock group called the runaways . it begins around 1975 . `` the runaways '' got mostly positive review by critics . it was released on march 19 , 2010 .
