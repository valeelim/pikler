Albert Reynolds     albert reynolds ( 3 november 1932 – 21 august 2014 ) was a former irish politician . he was twice taoiseach of ireland . he served from february 1992 to january 1993 and again from january 1993 to december 1994. he was the fifth leader of fianna fáil during the same period . he has been nominated for the nobel peace prize . reynolds died in dublin , ireland from complications from alzheimer 's disease , aged 81 .
