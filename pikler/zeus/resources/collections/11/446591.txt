List of Thomson MRT Line station names     the land transport authority ( lta ) began conducting a public consultation exercise over the internet as well as through survey forms to gather public feedback on the naming of stations for the thomson line . public consultation to source names for all the stations was held from 8 january 2013 to 17 february 2013. three names for each station were shortlisted for public voting from 23 may 2013 to 17 june 2013. related pages . - list of singapore mrt stations
