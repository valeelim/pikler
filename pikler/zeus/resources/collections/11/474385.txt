Wheldrake     wheldrake is a village and a civil parish about southeast of the city of york in england . in 2011 , the parish had a population of 2,107. the parish of wheldrake covers an area of . it was established before 1066. it was in the possession of fountains abbey in the middle ages . wheldrake became part of a landed estate until the mid 20th century . it has a significant conservation area and a nature reserve of international importance . this , named wheldrake ings , is about 8 miles south of york . it is where many wildflowers flourish and rare birds prosper .
