Anika Noni Rose     anika noni rose ( born september 6 , 1972 ) is an american singer and actress . she is best known for her tony award-winning role in the broadway production of `` caroline , or change '' . she is also known for her starring roles in the movies `` dreamgirls '' ( 2006 ) , `` the princess and the frog '' ( 2009 ) , and `` for colored girls '' ( 2010 ) . rose was born in bloomfield , connecticut .
