Not Alone (Aram Mp3 song)    not alone ( aram mp3 song ) `` not alone '' is a song by armenian singer aram mp3 . this song will represent armenia in the eurovision song contest 2014 in denmark . bet makers such as bwin and sky bet predict that this song will come first . the video was set in the armenian capital yerevan . the video shows a boyfriend and girlfriend having a fight in the girlfriend 's car . the boyfriend 's frustration then leads him to leave the car , which leaves both of them crying . this song was performed in the eurovision concert in amsterdam . the song will be sung first in the first semi-final of eurovison 2014 which will be held in denmark .
