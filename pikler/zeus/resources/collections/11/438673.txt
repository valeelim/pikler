Kitchen maid     a kitchen maid or kitchen girl is a young housemaid or other junior female domestic worker . in the hierarchy of a great house she ranked below a cook and above a scullery maid . an experienced kitchen maid is an assistant cook ; the position may be compared to that of a line cook in a professional kitchen . the kitchen maid reports to the head cook , if there is one . her uniform may consist of a blouse and a skirt , and apron . margaret powell worked as a kitchen girl at the age of 15 .
