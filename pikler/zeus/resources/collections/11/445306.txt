Tiffini Hale     tiffini hale ( born in 1975 ) is a former singer . she appeared on `` kidsongs : cars , boats , trains or planes '' and `` a day at the circus '' in 1986-1987. she appeared on disney 's `` mickey mouse club '' television show - seasons 1-4 , 5 and 7 ( 1989-1991 , 1992 and 1984-1995 ) . she was a member of the band the party in 1990-1993 .
