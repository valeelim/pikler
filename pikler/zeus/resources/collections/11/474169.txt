Gerhard Louis De Geer     gerhard louis de geer , born 27 november 1854 , dead 25 february 1935 , was a swedish politician . he served in the first chamber of the `` swedish parliament '' between 1901-1914. he was also the governor of kristianstad county betwween 1905-1923. also , he served as the prime minister of sweden between 1920-1921 .
