Five Nights at Freddy's 2    five nights at freddy 's 2 five nights at freddy 's 2 is a survival horror point and click video game . it was created by scott cawthon . it is a prequel to `` five nights at freddy 's '' . the player plays a security guard in a new pizzeria . his job is to watch the animatronics , and make sure nothing enters or leaves . he must survive 5 nights to be paid . there are 10 animatronics . they are not working correctly and believe you have done a crime . the guard has a camera to check all the rooms . he also has a freddy 's mask to fool the animatronics . the old animatronics from `` five nights at freddy 's '' have been replaced with toy versions , but the old ones are still there and can still kill you . there is also a puppet in a box and a balloon boy animatronic . many people in the community believe that `` five nights at freddy 's 2 '' happens before the first one .
