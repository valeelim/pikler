Ernest Mühlen     ernest mühlen ( 8 june 1926 – 19 march 2014 ) was a luxembourgish politician for the christian social people 's party , economist , and financial journalist . in 1973 , he won a place on luxembourg city 's communal council . he was born in ettelbruck . mühlen died on 19 march 2014 at the age of 87. other websites . - ernest mühlen at the european parliament website
