Retching     retching ( also called dry heaving or gagging ) is when the motion of the stomach and esophagus goes backward , but there is no vomiting . it can be caused by bad smells , choking or stopping certain medications . it can also be because of stress . retching is different from vomiting . during a retch , pressure in the thorax goes down and pressure in the abdomen goes up . in vomiting , both pressures go up .
