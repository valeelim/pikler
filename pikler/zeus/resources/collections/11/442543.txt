After Forever     after forever was a dutch symphonic metal band . the band started in 1995 as apocalypse . they played death metal . in 1997 the singer floor jansen joined the band . after jansen joined the band its sound became more like symphonic metal . in 2000 the band released their first album `` prison of desire '' in europe . the band 's last album `` after forever '' was released on september 28 , 2007. the band broke up in 2009 .
