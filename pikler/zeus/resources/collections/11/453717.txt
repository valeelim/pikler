WWE 2K15     wwe 2k15 is a professional wrestling video game . it was being developed by yuke 's and visual concepts . it was published by 2k sports . the game was released on october 28 , 2014 in north america . it was released on october 31 , 2014 in europe . the game will be available on the playstation 3 , playstation 4 , xbox 360 , and xbox one . it was the first game in the `` wwe 2k '' series to be released for the playstation 4 and xbox one consoles . wwe 2k15 is the first attempt in the series at a next-generation game for playstation 4 and xbox one . justin gabriel released a photo on his official instagram of himself getting `` scanned '' for what appears to be the eco-motion engine that was used in `` nba 2k14 '' 's next generation game . former `` nxt '' chris hero ( kassius ohno ) also did some motion-captioning for the game . 2k 's community manager , ronnie singh described the eco-motion engine as `` one example of the eco-motion engine in action is the new fluid nature of player animations . gone are the days of pre-canned animations that must play out exactly as they were mocapped . now , players adjust their movements based on the environment around them .
