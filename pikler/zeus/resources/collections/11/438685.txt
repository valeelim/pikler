Cook (domestic worker)    cook ( domestic worker ) a cook is a household staff member responsible for food preparation . the term can refer to the head of kitchen staff in a great house or to the cook-housekeeper , a far less prestigious position involving more physical labour . the cook in an english great house was traditionally female ; today 's residences may employ a head cook or chef who may be of either gender . when margaret powell had begun as a kitchen girl at the age of 15. she later advanced to become a cook .
