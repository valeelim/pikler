Muurame     muurame is a municipality in central finland . as of january 2014 , about 9,500 people lived there . the only municipality it shares a border with is jyväskylä . toivakka is another nearby municipality but there is water in between . most people in muurame speak finland . muurame was established in 1921. distances . - jyväskylä 13 km - jämsä 42 km - toivakka 49 km - tampere = 135 km - lahti = 160 km - turku = 293 km - oulu = 355 km - kilpisjärvi = 942 km other websites . - the official muurame page
