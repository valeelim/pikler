Talk (song)    talk ( song ) `` talk '' is the third single off british alternative rock band coldplay 's third studio album `` x & y '' . the song was released on 19 december 2005. the song is build around a motif by kraftwerk 's `` computer love '' . the song and the `` thin white duke '' remix were both nominated for grammy awards in 2007. the remix won the grammy award for best remixed recording , non-classical . other websites . - `` talk '' on youtube
