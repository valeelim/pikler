Cynthia Lynn     cynthia lynn ( born zinta valda zimilis ; april 2 , 1937 – march 10 , 2014 ) was a latvian-american actress . she was best known for her role as fraulein helga in `` hogan 's heroes '' during the first season ( 1965–66 ) . she was born in riga , latvian ssr , soviet union . lynn died from multiple organ failure as a result of hepatitis on march 10 , 2014. she was 76. she was outlived by her daughter , lisa brando , whose father was marlon brando . other websites . - cynthia lynn at allmovie
