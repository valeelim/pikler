Çolpan İlhan    çolpan ilhan çolpan ilhan ( august 8 , 1936 , izmir – july 25 , 2014 , istanbul ) was a turkish movie and theatre actress . she was awarded the title of 'state artist of turkey '' . she acted in more than 300 movies and theatre plays . ilhan died of a heart attack at her home in istanbul . her funeral ceremony was scheduled for 27 july 2014 .
