Sergio Renán     sergio renán ( 30 january 1933 – 13 june 2015 ) was an argentine actor , movie director and screenwriter . he is known for his movies `` manon '' ( 1984 ) , `` rigoletto '' ( 1985 ) , `` othello '' ( 1987 ) , `` madame butterfly '' ( 1989 ) , `` cosi fan tutte '' ( 1990 ) , and `` the marriage of figaro '' ( 1992 ) . he was born in buenos aires to a jewish family . renán died from pneumonia at a hospital in buenos aires , aged 82 .
