Boeing C-40 Clipper     the boeing c-40 clipper is a military of the boeing 737-700c . it is used by the us air force and the us navy . only 19 versions have been built . the c-40a version is built for the us navy . it is used to provide logistics support . the c-40a is capable of carrying 121 passengers , cargo , or cargo and passengers . the c-40b is the us air force model . it is designed to be an `` office in the sky '' for senior military and government leaders .
