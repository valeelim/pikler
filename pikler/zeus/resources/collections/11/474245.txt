Östen Warnerbring    östen warnerbring östen warnerbring , born 22 november 1934 , died 18 january 2006 , was a swedish singer , musician , composer and lyricist . he represented sweden in the eurovision song contest 1967 with the song `` som en dröm '' . the music was written by marcus österdahl & curt pettersson . the lyrics were written by patrice hellberg .
