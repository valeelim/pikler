Kjell Arnljot Wig     kjell arnljot wig ( 31 december 1924 – 18 january 2015 ) was a norwegian media personality . he was a journalist for `` morgenbladet '' from 1948. he was later with the norwegian broadcasting corporation from 1963 to 1992. he hosted a series of television shows , including `` aktuell debatt '' , `` vindu mot verden '' , and `` åpen post '' . some of his novels were `` det skjulte norge '' ( 1969 ) , `` kongen ser tilbake '' ( 1977 ) , and `` eventyret om blaafarveværket '' ( 1995 } . wig was born in stjørdal . he was married and had children . wig died after an illness on 18 january 2015 at bærum hospital . he was 90 .
