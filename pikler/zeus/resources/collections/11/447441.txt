Sonia Silvestre     sonia margarita silvestre ortiz ( 16 august 1952 – 19 april 2014 ) was a dominican singer and announcer . her best known songs were `` esta es sonia silvestre '' ( 1974 ) , `` yo quiero andar '' ( 1990 ) and `` verde y negro '' ( 2007 ) . she was born in san pedro de macorís , dominican republic . silvestre died from complications due to a stroke and two heart attacks on 19 april 2014 in santo domingo . she was 61. other websites . - sonia silvestre biography
