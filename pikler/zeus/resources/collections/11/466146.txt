Division of Wright     the division of wright is an australian electoral division in the state of queensland . it was set up in 2010. it covers the area inland from the gold coast , and includes the towns of beaudesert and boonah . it is named after the poet , judith wright ( 1915-2000 ) who lived in the area . election results . - 2010 election results - 2013 election results other websites . - scott buchholz 's website
