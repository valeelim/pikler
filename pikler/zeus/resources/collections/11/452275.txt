Martin Mystery     martin mystery is a french/canadian animated television series . it is produced by the french company marathon media and the canadian animation studio image entertainment corporation . the series is based on an italian comic strip . the series ran from 2003-2006. the series has 66 episodes divided into 3 seasons . the show is on canal j in france and on ytv in canada . martin mystery and his stepsister diana work at the center , an organization that protects earth from aliens .
