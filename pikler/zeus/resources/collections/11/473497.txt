John McVie     john graham `` mac '' mcvie ( born 26 november 1945 ) is a british bass guitarist . he is best known as a member of rock groups john mayall & the bluesbreakers and fleetwood mac . he joined fleetwood mac shortly after its formation by guitarist peter green in 1967. he replaced bassist bob brunning . in 1968 , he married blues pianist and singer christine perfect , who became a member of fleetwood mac two years later . john and christine mcvie divorced , however , in 1977. on 27 october 2013 , fleetwood mac announced on their facebook page that mcvie had been diagnosed with cancer and would be undergoing treatment . he has continued to play with the band during their 2014 on with the show tour following an improvement in his condition . other websites . - fleetwood mac official website
