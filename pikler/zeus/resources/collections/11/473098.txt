Wembley Arena     wembley arena is an indoor arena in wembley , london , england . it is next to wembley stadium . the arena was built for the 1934 british empire games . originally it had a large swimming pool , as reflected by its name at the time , empire pool . in 1978 it was renamed wembley arena . it became one of the best known indoor music venues in england . it seats 12,000. in april 2006 it was refurbished . it was the main stadium for the 1948 summer olympics . since 1934 wembley has hosted concerts by the world 's most famous artists , spanning many different genres of music . < br >
