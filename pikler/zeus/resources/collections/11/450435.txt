Maria Bethânia     maria bethânia viana teles velloso ( born june 18 , 1946 in santo amaro da purificação , bahia ) , known by her stage name maria bethânia , is a brazilian singer-songwriter . her brother is the singer caetano veloso . her career began in 1965 after moving to rio de janeiro . she made her debut in the show `` opinião '' . the same year she signed a contract with rca records and released her first album : `` maria bethãnia '' . in 1978 she was the first female singer in brazil to have sold over a million cd 's with her album : `` álibi '' . in 47 years she has released over 50 albums . with over 26 millions in record sales she is one of most successful singers in brazil . other websites . - official website - revista `` veja '' - `` a nação das cantoras '' - all music guide profile
