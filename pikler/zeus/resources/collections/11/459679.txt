Fancy (song)    fancy ( song ) `` fancy '' is a song by recording artist iggy azalea . it features english singer-songwriter charli xcx , who also sings in the song . this song is from her first studio album , `` the new classic '' . it was released on february 17 , 2014 as the fourth single from the album . `` fancy '' is azalea 's most successful song as of august 2014. the song hit # 1 on the `` billboard '' hot 100 in the united states and on the canadian hot 100 . `` fancy '' was also successful in several other countries . in the us , `` fancy '' was ranked # 4 on the year-end `` billboard '' hot 100 of 2014. it was the second-best performing song by a woman of the year , behind katy perry 's `` dark horse '' . the song was certified 7Ã— platinum by the recording industry association of america for shipments and streams of over 7 million copies in that country alone . the music video copies some scenes from `` clueless '' and features the two artists in a high-school setting , with scenes recorded in a classroom and schoolyard .
