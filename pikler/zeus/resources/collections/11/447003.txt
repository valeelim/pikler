Transcendence (movie)    transcendence ( movie ) transcendence is a 2014 american science fiction drama movie directed by wally pfister . it is produced by christopher nolan . it stars johnny depp , rebecca hall , kate mara , cillian murphy , paul bettany , and morgan freeman . transcendence was released in new york city on april 10 , 2014. it was released on april 18 , 2014 in other cities and countries .
