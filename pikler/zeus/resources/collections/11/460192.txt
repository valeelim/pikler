Meuse (river)    meuse ( river ) the meuse ( ) is a river in western europe . the river starts in france , goes through belgium and the netherlands . it ends in the north sea . the meuse is fed mostly by rainwater . it is the oldest river in the world . from 1301 , the meuse in the netherlands was the western border of the holy roman empire . in world war ii , the meuse was a goal for the germans during the battle of the bulge .
