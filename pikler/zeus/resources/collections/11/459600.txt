Allstate Arena     allstate arena ( originally rosemont horizon ) is a multi-purpose arena in rosemont , illinois . it is home to the chicago rush of the arena football league , depaul university 's men 's basketball team , the chicago wolves of the ahl , and the chicago sky of the wnba . it is located near the intersection of mannheim road and interstate 90 , adjacent to the city limits of chicago and o'hare international airport . other websites . - official website - depaul athletics - allstate arena
