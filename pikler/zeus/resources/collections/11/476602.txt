Jenő Buzánszky     jenő buzánszky ( 4 may 1925 – 11 january 2015 ) was a hungarian footballer and coach . buzánszky played as a right back for hungary . during the 1950s he was a member of the legendary hungarian national team known as the `` mighty magyars '' . he was the only member of the team not to play for either honvéd or mtk hungária fc . after 274 league games he retired as a player and became a coach . in 1996 he became a deputy chairman of the hungarian football federation . other websites . - hungary stats
