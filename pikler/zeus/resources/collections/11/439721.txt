Pope Victor I     pope victor ( 189-198c.e . ) i was the fourteenth pope , the official title is bishop of rome of the catholic church . pope victor was the first african pope . he attempted to resolve the ongoing controversy over the celebration of easter through a great meeting of church members from rome and gaul ( france ) and areas of and around mesopotamia ( iraq ) that easter should be celebrated on a sunday instead of on passover , whichever that day occurred on . the asia minor church however , stayed fast to the celebration on passover . pope victor at first declared the congregation in asia minor excommunicated but under protest seems to have withdrawn and those members of the church were accepted back into the church . he secured the release of some christians who were condemned to the mines of sardinia as a result of his request with the emperor 's mistress . his feast day is celebrated on july 28 .
