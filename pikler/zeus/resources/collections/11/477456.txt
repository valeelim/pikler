The Family (2013 movie)    the family ( 2013 movie ) the family is a 2013 american-french criminal comedy movie . it is set in normandy and was directed by luc besson . it stars robert de niro , michelle pfeiffer , tommy lee jones , vincent pastore , dianna agron , and john d'leo . it was released to mostly positive reviews on 13 september 2013 in the united states and 23 october 2013 in france .
