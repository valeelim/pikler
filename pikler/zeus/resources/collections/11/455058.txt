Henri Coandă     henri marie coandă ( 7 june 1886 – 25 november 1972 ) was an inventor , aerodynamics pioneer and builder of an experimental aircraft from romania . the coandă-1910 , made by coanda is sometimes considered to be first jet . this infomration has also created controversies . he invented a number of devices , he also designed `` flying saucer '' and discovered the coandă effect of fluid dynamics . during world war 2 , he lived in occupied france . in 1969 , he got back to romania , he served as director of the institute for scientific and technical creation ( increst ) , in 1971 he recorganized with professor elie carafoli , `` the department of aeronautical engineering of the polytechnic university of bucharest '' . coandă died in bucharest on 25 november 1972. he was 86 years old , he is buried at bellu cemetery .
