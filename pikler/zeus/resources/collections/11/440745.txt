Juan Antonio Samaranch     don juan antonio samaranch y torelló , 1st marquis of samaranch ( 17 july 1920 – 21 april 2010 ) was a spanish sports administrator . he served as the seventh president of the international olympic committee ( ioc ) from 1980 to 2001. samaranch died of cardio-respiratory failure on 21 april 2010 , aged 89 .
