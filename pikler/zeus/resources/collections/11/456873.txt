Megacheira     megacheira ( `` great hands '' ) is an extinct class of predatory arthropods . they had a pair of short enlarged appendages at the front . they look like early chelicerates . most of them lived in marine environments round the world from the lower to middle cambrian . it looks like they were quite common then . however , one perfectly preserved specimen of `` enalikter '' was found in the silurian of herefordshire ( england ) . another species ( `` bundenbachiellus '' ) was found in the early devonian of germany .
