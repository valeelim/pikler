Gerard Mortier     gerard alfons august , baron mortier ( 25 november 1943 – 8 march 2014 ) was a belgian opera director and administrator . he was the general director of la monnaie/de munt ( 1981–91 ) and of the salzburg festival of salzburg , austria ( 1990–2001 ) . he was born in ghent and was flemish . mortier died from pancreatic cancer on 8 march 2014 in brussels . he was 70 years old .
