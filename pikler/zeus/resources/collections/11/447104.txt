Karpal Singh     karpal singh s/o ram singh ( 28 june 1940 - 17 april 2014 ) was a malaysian lawyer and politician . he served as a member of parliament of bukit gelugor . he served as a mp from march 2004 until his death in april 2014. singh was a member of the democratic action party . he was born in george town , penang . singh died at the age of 73 in a car accident near gopeng , perak .
