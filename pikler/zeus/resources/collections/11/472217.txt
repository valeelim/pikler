Jackie Healy-Rae     jackie healy-rae ( 9 march 1931 – 5 december 2014 ) was an irish politician . he served as an independent teachta dála ( td ) for the kerry south constituency from 1997 to 2011. healy-rae was born in county kerry , irish free state . he died on 5 december 2014 at kerry general hospital in tralee , county kerry after a long illness , aged 83 .
