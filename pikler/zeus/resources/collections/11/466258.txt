Isaiah Owens     isaiah randolph `` ikey '' owens ( december 1 , 1975 - october 14 , 2014 ) was an american keyboardist . he was known for his work with the mars volta , jack white and a variety of bands from the long beach music scene . on october 14 , 2014 , owens was found dead in his hotel room in cholula , mexico , aged 38. he died of an apparent heart attack .
