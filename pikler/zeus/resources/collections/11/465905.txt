Old-school hip hop     old-school hip hop ( also old school rap ) is the earliest recorded hip hop music . it lasted from the late 1970s to around 1983 or 1984. this music is said to have ended in the early-to-mid 1980s due to changes in rapping techniques and the accompanying music and rhythms . a lot of this music was about partying and having a good time . old-school rappers include kurtis blow and grandmaster flash .
