Dress clothes     dress clothes are clothing that is less formal than formal wear but more formal than casual wear . for boys and men , an outfit of dress clothing includes items like a necktie , a dress shirt , trousers , dress socks , and dress shoes . for girls or women , an outfit of dress clothing can include either slacks , a skirt , or a dress . with slacks or a skirt , a shirt or blouse is worn . with a skirt or dress , hosiery such as pantyhose might be worn . dress clothing is often worn for nights out . it is worn in white collar work environments such as offices . other appropriate places include gatherings such as graduations , bar and bat mitzvahs , and funerals .
