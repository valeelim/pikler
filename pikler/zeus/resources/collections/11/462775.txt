Anwar Kamal Pasha     anwar kamal pasha ( 1927-1987 ) was a famous early pakistani film director and producer from lahore . he dominated the first 20 years of pakistani cinema . he was the son of noted poet and scholar , hakim ahmad shuja who named him after the turkish leader enver kemal pasha . some of pasha 's top hit films were `` do ansoo '' ( 1949 ) , `` gumnaam '' ( 1954 ) , `` qatil '' ( 1955 ) , `` sarfarosh '' ( 1956 ) and `` anarkali '' ( 1958 ) . from the 1960s , he gradually started making less films and his popularity grew less .
