19 BC     year 19 bc was either a common year starting on thursday , friday or saturday or a leap year starting on thursday or friday of the julian calendar . at the time , it was known as the year of the consulship of saturninus and vespillo . events . - lucius cornelius balbus receives the honor of a roman triumph for his victories over the garamantes in africa - marcus vipsanius agrippa completes the aqua virgo - the cantabrians are brought under roman control births . - vipsania julia agrippina , daughter of marcus vipsanius agrippa and julia the elder ( d. c. ad 28 ) deaths . - virgil , roman poet ( b . 70 bc ) - albius tibullus , roman poet ( b . 54 bc )
