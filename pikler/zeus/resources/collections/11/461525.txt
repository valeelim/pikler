Lewis Gilbert     lewis gilbert , cbe ( born 6 march 1920 ) is a british movie director , producer and screenwriter . he has directed more than 40 movies during six decades . he is known for his movies ; `` reach for the sky '' ( 1956 ) , `` sink the bismarck ! '' ( 1960 ) , `` alfie '' ( 1966 ) , `` educating rita '' ( 1983 ) and `` shirley valentine '' ( 1989 ) , as well as three james bond movies : `` you only live twice '' ( 1967 ) , `` the spy who loved me '' ( 1977 ) and `` moonraker '' ( 1979 ) . gilbert was born in london , england .
