The Slits     the slits were a british punk band . the band was started in 1976. the members were ari up , kate korus , suzy gutsy , and palmolive . in 1979 the band 's first album `` cut '' was released on island records in the uk . the band 's second album was released in 1980. in 1982 the band broke up . the band got back together in 2005. it broke up again in 2010 when ari up died of cancer .
