Vanderhoof, British Columbia    vanderhoof , british columbia vanderhoof is a district municipality in the canadian province of british columbia . vanderhoof is found near the geographical centre in the interior plateau region . the population of vanderhoof was 4,480 in 2011. the mayor of vanderhoof is gerry thiessen . vanderhoof is known for their bird sanctuary which is found along the nechako river . during their yearly migration , the river is passed through by canada geese , swan , as well as other birds . other websites . - official website
