Ratchet &amp; Clank (movie)    ratchet & amp ; clank ( movie ) ratchet & clank is a 3d computer-animated science fiction action comedy movie . it was directed by jericca cleland and kevin munroe . the movie is based on the platforming video game series of the same name . the movie was originally scheduled to be released in 2015. the movie was released on april 29 , 2016. the movie was produced at rainmaker entertainment 's vancouver studio in canada . it was executive produced by michael hefferon , the president of rainmaker entertainment . at the 2014 electronic entertainment expo on june 9 , 2014 , the first trailer for the movie was revealed during sony 's press conference . voice cast . - james arnold taylor as ratchet - david kaye as clank - jim ward as captain qwark - paul giamatti as drek - andrew cownden as zed - john goodman as grimroth - bella thorne as cora - rosario dawson as elaris - sylvester stallone as victor von ion - armin shimerman as doctor nefarious other websites . - official website
