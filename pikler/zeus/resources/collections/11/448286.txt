Tito Vilanova     francesc `` tito '' vilanova i bayó ( 17 september 1968 – 25 april 2014 ) was a spanish footballer . he played as a central midfielder for , and later managed , fc barcelona . he retired in 2013 because of his ill health . he was born in bellcaire d'empordà , catalonia . vilanova died on 25 april 2014 due to complications from throat cancer in a hospital in barcelona , aged 45 .
