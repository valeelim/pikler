Ezra Pound     ezra weston loomis pound ( 30 october 1885 – 1 november 1972 ) was an american poet and critic . he was a major figure of the early modernist movement . his best-known works include `` ripostes '' ( 1912 ) , `` hugh selwyn mauberley '' ( 1920 ) and the unfinished `` the cantos '' ( 1917–69 ) . pound was born in hailey , idaho territory . he died in italy from a intestinal blockage , aged 87 .
