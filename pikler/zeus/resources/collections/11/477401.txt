Peripheral artery disease     peripheral artery disease ( pad ) is a disease of the blood vessels in the legs . it can cause leg pain with walking , or if it gets bad it can cause wounds on the skin of the feet and toes . peripheral artery disease is a serious disease that can be fatal . some people who get very sick from pad may need surgery to improve blood flow in their legs and feet . if their feet get really sick they may need an amputation . pa is also sometimes called peripheral artery occlusive disease ( paod ) , peripheral obliterative arteriopathy , or peripheral vascular disease ( pvd ) .
