Wikipedia Monument     the wikipedia monument ( ) is a statue designed by armenian sculptor mihran hakobyan honoring wikipedia contributors . it is located in słubice , poland . the fiber and resin sculpture cost about 50,000 złotys ( about $ 14,000 ; 12,000 euros ) . it was funded by słubice regional authorities . it was unveiled in frankfurt square ( plac frankfurcki ) on 22 october 2014 in a ceremony that included representatives from both local wikimedia chapters and the wikimedia foundation . other websites . - press release : first-ever wikipedia monument unveiled in poland , wikimedia foundation
