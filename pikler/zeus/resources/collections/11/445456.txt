Bill Mitchell (ice hockey)    bill mitchell ( ice hockey ) william richard `` bill '' mitchell ( february 22 , 1930 – april 1 , 2014 ) was a canadian professional ice hockey defenceman and coach . he played one in the national hockey league ( nhl ) during the 1963–64 season for the detroit red wings . he later coached some teams in toledo , ohio in the united states . he was born in port dalhousie , ontario . mitchell died from kidney failure on april 1 , 2014 in perrysburg township , wood county , ohio . he was 84 years old . he was outlived by his one daughter and two sons .
