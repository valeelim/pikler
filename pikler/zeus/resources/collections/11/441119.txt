Connected     `` connected '' is an electronic pop song by stereo mcs . this song was released in september 1992. it was # 20 on `` billboard '' hot 100 and # 18 on uk top 75 chart . the song appeared in several television programs and movies . the song samples the disco classic song `` let me ( let me be your lover ) '' by jimmy bo horne . the full length of the song is five minutes and 16 seconds .
