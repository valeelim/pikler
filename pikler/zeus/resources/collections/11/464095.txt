Sulejman Tihić     sulejman tihić ( 26 november 1951 – 25 september 2014 ) was a bosnian politician . he was a leading member of party of democratic action ( sda ) . he was chairman of the house of peoples of bosnia and herzegovina from 14 november 2007 to 14 july 2008. tihić was born in the town of bosanski šamac in northern bosnia . on the 25th september , tihić died from a long and severe disease , aged 62. other websites . - the website of sulejman tihic - town hall meeting with sulejman tihic , `` u.s. institute of peace '' , may 2006 ( audio ) - bosnian president 'threatened ' by us-based serbs , `` turkishpress.com '' , 14 august 2006
