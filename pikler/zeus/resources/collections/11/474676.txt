Hyun Hee     hyun hee ( born october 4 , 1976 in seongnam , gyeonggi-do , south korea ) is a retired south korean épée fencer . at the 2002 fie world championships hyun won the women 's individual épée final match over imke duplitzer of germany 15-11. she was the first asian fencer to win a gold medal at the world championships . in that year , hyun was named south korean sportswoman of the year .
