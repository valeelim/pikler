Otto Carius     otto carius ( 27 may 1922 – 24 january 2015 ) was a german former nazi tank commander . he destroyed over 150 foreign tanks . he received the knight 's cross of the iron cross with oak leaves . after the war , carius worked as a pharmacist . he also wrote books about his experiences during the war . carius was born in zweibrücken , weimar germany . he died from a short illness in herschweiler-pettersheim , germany , aged 92 .
