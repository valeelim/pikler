Butch Otter     clement leroy `` butch '' otter ( born may 3 , 1942 ) is an american politician . he is the 32nd governor of idaho . he became governor in january 2007. he is a member of the republican party . otter served as lieutenant governor of idaho from 1987 to 2001 and as the united states representative for idaho 's 1st congressional district from 2001 to 2007 .
