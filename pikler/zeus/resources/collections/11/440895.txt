Johnny Vegas     johnny vegas ( born michael joseph pennington ; 5 september 1970 ) is an english actor and comedian . he is best known for his role as geoff maltby ( the oracle ) in `` benidorm '' . his style comedy include : his angry rants , surreal humour , portly figure , high husky voice and support of st helens rugby league club . vegas was born in st helens , lancashire , england . he was married to catherine `` kitty '' donnelly from 2002 until they divorced in 2008. he married irish columnist maia dunphy in 2011 in seville , spain . he is a christian , having returned to religion after having lapsed during adolescence . he lives in london .
