Vilma Ferrán     vilma ferrán ( 30 october 1940 – 14 july 2014 ) was argentine movie and television actress . she was known for her roles in `` animalada '' and in `` dormir al sol.2 '' . she was also known for her television roles in `` son de fierro '' and in `` casi ángeles '' . she was born in buenos aires , argentina . ferrán died in buenos aires , argentina , aged 73 .
