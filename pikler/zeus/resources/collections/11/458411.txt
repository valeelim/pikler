Anglican Diocese of Calgary     the anglican diocese of calgary is part of the anglican church of canada . it covers the southern part of alberta , up to the city of lacombe . it is part of the church 's ecclesiastical province of rupert 's land . the see city is in calgary , with the cathedral church of the redeemer as the diocese 's cathedral . the diocese was started in 1888. as of 2012 , the bishop is greg kerr-wilson .
