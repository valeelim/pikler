Louis Ignarro     louis j. ignarro ( born may 31 , 1941 ) is an american pharmacologist . ignarro is best known for his discoveries which concern the nitric oxide as a signaling molecule in the cardiovascular system . he won the nobel prize in physiology or medicine in 1998 for his discoveries . he shared the prize with robert f. furchgott and ferid murad . due to nitric oxide indirectly being involved in the action of viagra , he is sometimes referred to as the `` father of viagra '' . ignarro is an avid cyclist and marathoner , he has completed 13 marathons . he is married and lives in beverly hills , california . other websites . - official website
