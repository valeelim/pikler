Phil Lord and Chris Miller     phil lord and chris miller ( phil : born april 21 , 1977 in miami , florida , chris : born september 23 , 1975 in everett , washington ) are american movie and television writers , producers , directors , actors and former animators . they are known for directing `` cloudy with a chance of meatballs '' , `` 21 jump street '' , `` the lego movie '' , and `` 22 jump street '' .
