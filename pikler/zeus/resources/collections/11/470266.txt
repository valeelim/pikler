Sugarloaf Cable Car     the sugarloaf cable car ; ) is a cableway in rio de janeiro , brazil . it moves between praia vermelha and the inselberg sugarloaf mountain . it stops at morro da urca ( at ) on its way up and down . it reaches the summit of the mountain . the cableway was envisioned by the engineer augusto ferreira ramos in 1908. he sought support from well-known figures of rio de janeiro 's high society to promote its construction . it was opened in 1912
