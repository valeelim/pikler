Asus     asustek computer inc. , usually referred to as asus , is a computer hardware and electronics company . it was founded in taipei in 1989 by t.h . tung , ted hsu , wayne hsieh and m.t . liao . asus is the world 's fifth-largest pc vendor by 2013 unit sales ( after lenovo , hewlett-packard , dell and acer ) . the name `` asus '' originates from `` pegasus '' , the winged horse of greek mythology . asus 's products include laptops , tablet computers , desktop computers , mobile phones , personal digital assistants ( pdas ) , servers , computer monitors , motherboards , graphics cards , sound cards , optical disc drives , computer networking devices , computer cases , computer components and computer cooling systems . `` republic of gamers '' is a brand used by asus since 2006 , encompassing a range of computer hardware , personal computers , peripherals , and accessories oriented primarily toward pc gaming . the line includes both desktops and high-spec laptops .
