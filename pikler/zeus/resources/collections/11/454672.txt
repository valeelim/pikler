Wabamun Lake     wabamum lake is a lake in alberta , canada . it is 65 kilometres ( 40 miles ) west of edmonton , alberta . it is 19.2 kilometres ( 11.9 miles ) long and 6.6 kilometres ( 4.1 miles ) wide . it covers 82 square kilometres ( 32 square miles ) . it is 11 metres ( 36 feet ) deep at its deepest . the name of the lake comes from the cree language word for mirror .
