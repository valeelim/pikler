Victor Kostetskiy     victor alexandrovich kostetskiy ( ви́ктор алекса́ндрович косте́цкий 12 april 1941 – 6 november 2014 ) was a russian and soviet actor . kosteskiy was born in zhmerinka . he was known for his roles in `` wedding krechinsky '' , `` truffaldino from bergamo '' , and in `` treasure island '' . kostetskiy died on november 6 , 2014 at the age of 74 in saint petersburg , russia . other websites . - biography rusactors.ru
