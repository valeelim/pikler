Tanuja     tanuja mukherjee , popularly known as tanuja , ( born 23 spetember 1943 ) is an indian movie actress . she is the mother of kajol and tanisha . she is known for her roles in `` baharen phir bhi aayengi '' ( 1966 ) , `` jewel thief '' , `` haathi mere saathi '' ( 1971 ) , and `` anubhav '' ( 1971 ) . tanuja was born in bombay , bombay presidency , british india .
