Antje Jackelén     antje jackelén , born 4 june 1955 in herdecke , north rhine-westphalia , west germany , is the protestant ( lutheran ) archbishop of uppsala in sweden . she is primate `` ( prima inter pares ) '' of the church of sweden . on 15 october 2013 , she was elected the 70th archbishop of uppsala . she was formally received through a service inside the uppsala cathedral on 15 june 2014. this made her the church of sweden 's first archbishop to be born outside sweden since the 12th century . she also became the denomination 's first female archbishop .
