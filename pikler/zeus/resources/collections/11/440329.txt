Adnan Pachachi     adnan al-pachachi or adnan muzahim ameen al-pachachi ( ) ( born may 14 , 1923 ) is a veteran iraqi politician and diplomat . pachachi was iraq 's permanent representative to the united nations from 1959 to 1965 and foreign minister of iraq from 1965 to 1967 during the six-day war with israel . other websites . - pachachi , adnan international who 's who . accessed september 1 , 2006 .
