Tea Ista     dorothea ida eveliina witikka ( born dorothea ida eveliina ista ; 12 december 1932 – 20 february 2014 ) , better known by her stage name tea ista , was a finnish actress . she began her acting career in 1952. she was born in evijärvi . ista died after a serious illness on 20 february 2014 in espoo . she was aged 81. she was outlived by her daughter , minna maria , with her late husband jack witikka .
