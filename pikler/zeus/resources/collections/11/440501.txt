Rick Moranis     frederick allan `` rick '' moranis ( born april 18 , 1953 ) is a semi-retired canadian-american actor , comedian , screenwriter and songwriter . he was nominated for a grammy award two times . he was known for his role in the movie `` honey , i shrunk the kids '' . he is also known for appearing in `` ghostbusters '' , `` the flintstones '' , `` spaceballs '' , and in `` little shop of horrors '' . other websites . - rickmoranis.com - rick moranis , singing 'cowboy ' , a december 2005 story from `` weekend edition '' - oped piece by moranis from the `` ny times '' website - rick moranis interview proton charging may 27 , 2006 - radio interview rick moranis 2006
