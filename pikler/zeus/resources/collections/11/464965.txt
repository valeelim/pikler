Jean-Claude Duvalier     jean-claude duvalier ( ) , nicknamed `` bébé doc '' or `` baby doc '' ( july 3 , 1951 – october 4 , 2014 ) was the president of haiti from 1971 until his overthrow by a popular uprising in 1986. he succeeded françois `` papa doc '' duvalier , his father , as the ruler of haiti . duvalier died of a heart attack on october 4 , 2014 , at the age of 63 .
