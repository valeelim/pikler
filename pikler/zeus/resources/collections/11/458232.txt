Metro Vancouver     metro vancouver is a regional district in british columbia , canada . it was created in 1966. for 40 years it was called `` the greater vancouver regional district '' , but now it is called `` metro vancouver '' . the regional district includes 22 municipalities , one electoral area and one treaty first nation . the district covers 2,877.36 square kilometres ( 1,111 square miles ) . metro vancouver administers several services common to the local government units , such as utilities .
