Aquí     aquí ( `` here '' ) is the first studio album by mexican singer-songwriter julieta venegas . it was released on march 24 , 1997. it was well received by the public . the two singles released were `` de mis pasos '' and `` cómo sé '' . all songs were written by venegas . singles . - de mis pasos - cómo sé awards . - mtv video music awards
