Silkworm (Missile)    silkworm ( missile ) the silkworm missile was the nato reporting name for these kinds of chinese anti-ship missiles : - shang you or sy-series - hai ying or hy-series - fei long or fl-series these missiles were the chinese version of the soviet p-15 termit missile . they used hot launch mechanism for launching the missile from ships . other websites . - federation of american scientists page on hy-1 - hy-2
