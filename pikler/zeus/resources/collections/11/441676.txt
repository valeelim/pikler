Luis Villoro     luis villoro toranzo ( 3 november 1922 – 5 march 2014 ) was a spanish–mexican philosopher , researcher , university professor , diplomat , academic and writer . he published more than ten books between 1950 and 2007. he was born in barcelona , catalonia , spain . villoro died from respiratory failure on 5 march 2014 in mexico city . he was 91 years old . other websites . - luis villoro at the instituto de investigaciones filosóficas
