Seth Meyers     seth adam meyers ( born december 28 , 1973 ) is an american actor , voice actor , screenwriter , producer , television host and comedian . he is best known for hosting nbc 's `` saturday night live '' news parody segment `` weekend update '' and the nbc talk show `` late night with seth meyers '' . the latter program premiered on february 24 , 2014. meyers was born in bedford , new hampshire , to hillary and lawrence meyers . in 1966 he graduated from northwestern university . after college he and his brother josh moved to amsterdam . they joined a comedy improv group called boom chicago . in 2001 he auditioned for producer lorne michaels of saturday night live and joined the cast that same year .
