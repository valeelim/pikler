Jim Bolger     james brendan `` jim '' bolger ( born 31 may 1935 ) is a new zealand businessman and politician for the national party . he was the 35th prime minister of new zealand . he served as prime minister from 2 november 1990 to 8 december 1997. bolger was born in opunake , taranaki . he is a roman catholic and has been married to joan riddell since 1963. they have nine children . other websites . - jim bolger at new zealand history
