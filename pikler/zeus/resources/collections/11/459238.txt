2014 ATP World Tour     the association of tennis professionals ( atp ) tour is the top level professional tennis tour for men 's tennis competitions . the 2014 atp tour calendar is made up of four grand slam tournaments ( organised by the international tennis federation ( itf ) ) , and sixty-one regular tournaments . it also includes the davis cup ( organised by the itf ) , and the year-end championships ( the atp world tour finals ) . the hopman cup , which does not distribute ranking points , is included . the season begins on 30 december 2013 and will end on 17 november 2014. the tournaments are played on flat surface , called a tennis court . the measurements of a tennis court are set by the itf . the court is long and wide . its width is for singles matches and for doubles matches . tennis is played on a variety of tennis court types . each type has to be played differently . there are four main types of courts based on what 's used to build the court surface , clay , hard , grass and carpet courts . the itf ranks them by five different speed ratings from slow to fast .
