Horse theft     horse theft is the crime of stealing a horse . a person accused of stealing a horse is known as a horse thief . horse theft was fairly common throughout the world prior to widespread car ownership . punishments were often severe for horse theft . in several places the sentence of death was carried out . this was seen as a deterrent against horse theft . death for an agricultural crime such as horse theft is seen as inhumane under most modern systems of law . horse theft is an uncommon crime today .
