Death and state funeral of Leonid Brezhnev     on 10 november 1982 , leonid ilyich brezhnev , the third general secretary of the communist party of the soviet union ( cpsu ) and the fifth leader of the soviet union , died aged 75 after suffering a heart attack . his death was officially announced on 11 november by soviet radio and television programs . after five days of national mourning , brezhnev was given a state funeral and then buried in the kremlin wall necropolis . yuri andropov , brezhnev 's successor as general secretary , was elected chairman of the committee preparing and managing brezhnev 's funeral . brezhnev 's funeral was on 15 november . the funeral was attended by 32 heads of state , 15 heads of government , 14 foreign ministers and four princes ; us president ronald reagan sent george h. w. bush , the vice president of the united states . eulogies were delivered by yuri andropov , dmitriy ustinov , anatoly alexandrov and a factory worker .
