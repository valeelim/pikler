Denis Norden     denis mostyn norden , cbe ( born 6 february 1922 ) is a retired english comedy writer and television presenter . he was known for presenting the radio programme `` my word ! '' . he also presented `` my music '' . `` looks familiar '' and blooper shows `` it 'll be alright on the night '' and `` laughter file '' . he retired in 2006. norden was born in mare street , hackney , london on 6 february 1922. he studied at city of london school . norden married avril rosen in 1943. they have two children .
