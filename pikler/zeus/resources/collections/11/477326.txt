Mildred Miller     mildred miller ( born december 16 , 1924 in cleveland , ohio ) is an american classical mezzo-soprano . he had a major career performing in operas , concerts , and recitals during the mid twentieth century . she was known as a principal artist at the metropolitan opera from 1951 through 1974. in 1978 she founded the opera theater of pittsburgh , serving as the company 's artistic director through 1999. other websites . - biography of mildred miller at lakewood public library online - rome news-tribune , 1965 - metropolitan opera archives - flying without a safety net : the 80 years of mildred miller posvar , `` pitt chronicle '' , december 13 , 2004
