Yoo Jae-suk     yoo jae-suk is a south korean comedian and mc . he was born on august 14 , 1972 in seoul . he is known as `` mc of the nation '' . he was also known as `` the grasshopper '' because he dressed as a grasshopper when he first started . his debut was on the kbs comedian festival which held for college students in 1991. although , a vote by the south korean `` netizens '' proclaimed him the most popular mc in the korea , he underwent unknown comedian for long times ( 9 years ) . but , in 2002 , he became the mc of a program named “ dong-guh-dong-lak ( 동고동락 ) ” thanks to a recommendation by a talent choi jin-sil . this program instantly be popular and this accomplishments give yoo jae-suk more chances to host many variety programs . and after he hosted the program `` the crash of mcs '' ( 공포의 쿵쿵따 ) , he became top level of mc in the korea . he hosted many variety shows for example , x-man , infinite challenge , family outing , happy together , running man , come to play etc ... by this success , he got 34 awards . also , he is kinds of singer . he sang 10 songs for example , as i say , apqujeong nallari , please do n't go my girl etc . he is loved and popular not only by his ability of hosting show but also his personality like respecting others .
