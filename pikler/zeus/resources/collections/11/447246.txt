Cheo Feliciano     cheo feliciano ( josé luis feliciano vega , july 3 , 1935 – april 17 , 2014 ) was a puerto rican composer and singer . he was mostly involved with salsa and cuban music . he was born in ponce , puerto rico . feliciano died in cupey , puerto rico , in a car accident . he was 78 years old . at the peak of his career in the 1960s he recorded nine albums with the joe cuba sextet , and in the 1970s he recorded 15 albums for fania records , then the leading salsa music record company .
