Rapunzel     `` rapunzel '' is a german fairy tale . it was first published in 1812 as part of `` grimms ' fairy tales '' . the best known line in the story is `` rapunzel , rapunzel , let down your hair '' . this story is about a lonely couple who want a child . they live next to a walled garden that belongs to a witch . the woman is pregnant . when the baby is born , a witch named dame gothel takes the baby and names her rapunzel . when she is twelve years old , rapunzel is taken into a tower and shut away . one day a prince visits and asks her to marry him . she agrees . there are many different versions of `` rapunzel '' .
