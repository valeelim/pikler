Henryk Glücklich     henryk glücklich ( 22 january 1945 – 23 september 2014 ) was a polish speedway racer . he was an international motorcycle speedway rider . he appeared in the speedway world championship finals three times , finishing fifth in 1970. he also rode in five world team cup finals . glücklich was born in rybnik , poland . glücklich died in berlin , germany , aged 69 .
