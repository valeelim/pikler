Mario Abramovich     mario abramovich ( 31 october 1926 – 1 december 2014 ) was an argentine violinist and composer of jewish descent . he was a member of the group sexteto mayor since its founding in 1973 , until his death in 2014. he was known for performing in the argentine album `` bête noire '' . abramovich died in buenos aires , argentina , aged 88. other websites . - `` celos '' . contiene un solo de violin de mario abramovich . - historias de oro . entrevista en la 2x4 a mario abramovich
