Kangasniemi     kangasniemi is a municipality in southern savonia , finland . as of january 2014 , almost 5,800 people lived there . neighbouring municipalities are hankasalmi , hirvensalmi , joutsa , mikkeli , pieksämäki and toivakka . kangasniemi was established in 1867. it has over 40 villages . other websites . - the official kangasniemi page translated to 4 languages
