Antônio Pitanga     antônio luiz sampaio ( born june 13 , 1939 ) , better known by his stage name antônio pitanga , is a brazilian actor . he is known for playing several roles on movies of the cinema novo movement in the 1960s . pitanga appeared in 100 foreign movies and 20 telenovelas . his career began in 1960. he is known for his role in `` o clone . he was married to actress vera manhães , with whom he had two sons : actress camila pitanga and actor rocco pitanga . after their divorced , he married to politician benedita da silva .
