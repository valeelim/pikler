Transvision Vamp     transvision vamp were a british rock band . they were formed in putney , london in 1986 and split up in 1991. its members were wendy james , nick christian sayer , dave parsons , anthony doughty , pol burton , james piper and martin hallett . they released three albums : pop art , velveteen ( which reached number 1 on the uk albums chart , and little magnets versus the bubble of babble . they released 11 singles : their most successful , baby i do n't care , reached number 3 in the uk singles chart .
