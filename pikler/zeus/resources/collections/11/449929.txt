Nazim Al-Haqqani     mehmet nazım adil or muhammad nazim adil al-qubrusi al-haqqani ( ; april 21 , 1922 – may 7 , 2014 ) , often called shaykh nazim ( ) , was a turkish-cypriot sufi sheikh . he was the leader of the naqshbandi-haqqani order . al-haqqani predicted many events , all were not true . he was also a poet . he was born in larnaca , cyprus . nazim al-haqqani died at the age of 92 in northern cyprus from multiple organ failure .
