Unapologetic (Rihanna album)    unapologetic ( rihanna album ) `` unapologetic '' is the seventh studio album released by barbadian recording artist rihanna . it was released on november 19 , 2012 by def jam recordings . the album features collaborations from future , david guetta , eminem , mikky ekko , and chris brown . `` unapologetic `` became rihanna 's first number-one album on the `` billboard '' 200 chart in the united states . it features the number one hit single `` diamonds '' . to promote the album rihanna went on the 777 tour which consisted of seven shows in seven cities over seven nights .
