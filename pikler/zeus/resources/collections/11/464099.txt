Batman &amp; Robin: The Chiller    batman & amp ; robin : the chiller batman & robin : the chiller was a dual track looping shuttle roller coaster designed by premier rides . it operated at six flags great adventure in jackson , new jersey , from 1997 to 2007. it had a large number of maintenance problems . six flags removed the ride from the park in fall 2007. it is currently in storage at beto carrero world in brazil .
