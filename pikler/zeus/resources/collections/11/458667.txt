Jan Roar Leikvoll     jan roar leikvoll ( 7 june 1974 – 1 august 2014 ) was a norwegian author . he was born in bergen . leikvoll made his debut in 2008 with the novel `` a winter story '' , followed by `` the violins '' ( 2010 ) , `` the bovara monastery '' ( 2012 ) and `` the songbird in 2013 '' . he was awarded nynorsk literature prize for the violins and stig sæterbakkens memorial prize for his three first novels . leikvoll died on 1 august 2014 from a brain tumor , aged 40 .
