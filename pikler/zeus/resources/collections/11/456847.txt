Jini Dellaccio     jini dellaccio ( née duckworth ; january 31 , 1917 – july 3 , 2014 ) is an american photographer . he is best known for her images of rock and pop acts of the 1960s . her photographs of the sonics , the wailers , merrilee rush , the daily flash and many others were used for album covers , posters , and publicity stills . other websites . - jini dellaccio collection ( official site ) , includes examples of dellaccio 's photography and short samples of music by several 1960s pacific northwest bands she photographed . - her aim is true , official site of documentary about dellaccio , includes trailer - rock & roll : jini dellaccio , gallery , photo center nw exhibit november 1 – december 16 , 2011
