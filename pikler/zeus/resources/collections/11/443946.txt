Daniel Díaz Torres     daniel díaz torres ( 31 december 1948 – 16 september 2013 ) was a cuban movie director and screenwriter . he directed sixteen movies between 1975 and 2013. his best known movie was `` alice in wondertown '' ( 1991 ) , which gained him international notability . díaz torres died after a long illness on 16 september 2013 in his hometown of havana . he was 65 years old .
