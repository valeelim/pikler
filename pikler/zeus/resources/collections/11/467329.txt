Ray Stantz     raymond `` ray '' stantz , ph.d. is a fictional character from the `` ghostbusters '' franchise . he appears in the movies `` ghostbusters '' , `` ghostbusters ii '' , `` casper '' , and the animated television series `` the real ghostbusters '' . in both live action movies , he was portrayed by dan aykroyd , and was voiced by frank welker . he is a member of the ghostbusters and one of the three doctors of parapsychology , along with dr. peter venkman and dr. egon spengler .
