Doñana National Park     doñana national park is a natural reserve in southern spain . it is in andalusia , in the provinces of huelva and seville . it is in las marismas , where the guadalquivir river flows into the atlantic ocean . the park covers . of that area , are protected . the park has marshes , shallow streams , and sand dunes . it was named as a nature reserve in 1969. the world wildlife fund and the spanish government bought a section of marshes to protect it . the eco-system has been in danger from draining the marshes , using river water to irrigate land along the coast , and making tourist facilities bigger . the park is named after doña ana de silva y mendoza , the wife of alonso pérez de guzmán , 7th duke of medina sidonia . doñana national park was one of 100 finalists for the 12 treasures of spain in 2007 .
