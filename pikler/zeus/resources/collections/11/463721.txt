Reema     reema khan ( born 1972 ) also known by her screen name reema , is a pakistani lollywood film actress , director and producer . she has appeared in more than 200 films since making her debut in 1990. reema khan was born in 1972 , in the heera mandi area of lahore , pakistan . she was first spotted in 1990 by the pakistani film director javed fazil and was offered the leading role in his film `` bulandi '' . she became popular at once and went on getting success . khan 's early films included 'zherilay ' , 'ishq ' , 'naag devta ' , 'pyar hi pyar ' , 'sailab ' , 'dil ' , 'aag ' , 'shama ' and 'sahiba ' . these were followed by films such as 'hina ' , 'anjuman ' , 'chakuri ' , 'chandni ' , 'neelam ' and 'insaniat ' . later she appeared in 'hathi meray sathi ' directed by shameem ara and in 'rani beti raj karegi ' directed by altaf hussain . in 2002 , asif ali pota 's 'fire ' and samina peerzada 's 'shararat ' were released starring reema in the lead role . she later also started making some films as a producer and part-time director .
