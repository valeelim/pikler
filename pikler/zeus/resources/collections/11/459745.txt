Munchkin     the 1939 movie `` the wizard of oz '' , showed munchkins as being much shorter than other residents of oz . they are played by either adult dwarfs ( i.e. , midgets ) or children , dressed in brightly multicolored outfits , and their land is called munchkinland . on november 20 , 2007 , the munchkins were given a star on the hollywood walk of fame .
