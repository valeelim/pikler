General Butt Naked     joshua milton blahyi ( born september 30 , 1971 ) , better known as general butt naked , is an evangelical christian preacher . blahyi is a member of the sarpo tribe in liberia . blahyi has said that when he was 11 years old he became a tribal priest . in 2011 a documentary film called `` the redemption of general butt naked '' was released . matt stone and trey parker based the character `` butt fucking naked '' in their musical the book of mormon on general butt naked .
