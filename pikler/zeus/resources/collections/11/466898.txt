Seth Gaaikema     seth regner gaaikema ( 11 july 1939 – 21 october 2014 ) was a dutch cabaret artist , writer , and lyricist . he translated many plays into dutch . they include `` my fair lady '' ( his first , 1959 ) , `` kiss me kate '' , `` oliver ! `` , `` les misérables '' , and `` the phantom of the opera '' . gaaikema was born in uithuizen , groningen , the netherlands . gaaikema died in schijndel , north brabant from heart failure , aged 75 .
