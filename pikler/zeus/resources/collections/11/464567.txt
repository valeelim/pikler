Mian Mir Sahib     hazrat mian mir muhammad sahib , or mian mir sahib , ( 1550-1635 ) was a famous sufi saint of the qadiri order . he lived most of his life in lahore ( then in mughal india and now in pakistan ) . mian mir sahib was specially famous for having been the teacher of a mughal prince , dara shikoh , and for having taught and helped many sikh gurus . he was a very loving and tolerant person .
