Nicktoons Basketball     nicktoons basketball is a video game for the pc , released in 2004. it is based on a bunch nickelodeon and nick jr. shows . it is the third nicktoon game to be released by imaginengine , and also the first by digital eclipse . the player plays basketball with spongebob squarepants , timmy turner , , danny phantom , tommy pickles , dora the explorer , and more . the player can choose how they would like to control the game as well .
