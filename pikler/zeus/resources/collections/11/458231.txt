Langley, British Columbia (district municipality)    langley , british columbia ( district municipality ) the township of langley is a district municipality immediately east of the city of surrey in southwestern british columbia , canada . it is south of the fraser river and west of abbotsford . it is part of metro vancouver . it was started in 1827 as fort langley as a trading post . the fraser canyon gold rush in 1858 brought more settlers into the area . the township of langley was incorporated on april 26 , 1873. the vancouver stealth of the national lacrosse league play at the langley events centre .
