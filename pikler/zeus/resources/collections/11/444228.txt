Grumman A-6 Intruder     the grumman a-6 intruder is an attack aircraft built in the usa . it 's a twin-turbojet aircraft , built in all-metal . it was used for the vietnam war and the gulf war by us navy . before september 1962 , it was called grumman a2f intruder . grumman ea-6b prowler is a specialized variant . bibliography . - frawley , gerard , `` the international directory of military aircraft 2002/2003 '' . london : aerospace publication , 2002. isbn 1-875671-55-2 . - gaillard , pierre , `` avions et hélicoptères militaires d'aujourd'hui '' paris , france : larivière éditions , 1999. isbn 2-907051-24-5 .
