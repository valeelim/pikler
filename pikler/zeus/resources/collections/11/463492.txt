Jeanne Shaheen     cynthia jeanne bowers ( also jeanne shaheen ; born january 28 , 1947 ) is an american politician she is a member of the u.s. democratic party . in 1996 , shaheen became the first woman to be elected governor of new hampshire . in 2008 , shaheen was elected to the united states senate . since 2011 , she has been the senior senator from new hampshire . shaheen was born in saint charles , missouri .
