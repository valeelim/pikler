Drachensee (Ehrwald)    drachensee ( ehrwald ) the drachensee is a lake in austria which is a few meters below of coburger hütte . it is at about 1910 meters ( 6 266 feet ) attitude . if you climb up above coburger hütte at most of the high peeks it will be seen . it is very similiar to the seebensee which is lower down the drachensee and on the other side of coburger hütte .
