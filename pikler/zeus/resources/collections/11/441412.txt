Allan Funk     allan eric funk ( born july 7 , 1971 ) is an american professional wrestler . he currently competes in lucha libre usa where he competes under the ring name chi chi . he is best known for his time in world championship wrestling ( wcw ) where he competed under the ring name kwee wee and total nonstop action wrestling ( tna ) under the name bruce where he was one-half of the rainbow express , a stereotypical homosexual tag team with lenny lane . championships . - big time wrestling - btw ohio tag team championship ( one time ) - total nonstop action wrestling - miss tna ( one time ) other websites . - archive of kwee wee 's wcw profile - online world of wrestling profile
