Kyle Chipchura     kyle douglas glen chipchura ( born february 19 , 1986 ) is a canadian professional ice hockey centre . he currently plays for the arizona coyotes of the national hockey league ( nhl ) . he has also played for the montreal canadiens and anaheim ducks . before playing in the nhl , chipchura played 4 seasons with the prince albert raiders of the western hockey league ( whl ) . chipchura was drafted with the 18th overall pick by the montreal canadiens in the 2004 nhl entry draft . he made his nhl debut with the canadiens on october 10 , 2007 in a 3â€“2 win against the pittsburgh penguins . he scored his first nhl goal in a 4-2 win against the buffalo sabres on october 20 , 2007. on december 2 , 2009 , the canadiens traded chipchura to the anaheim ducks for a fourth-round pick in the 2011 nhl entry draft . he played 2 seasons with the ducks before he was released by the ducks . on july 19 , 2011 , he signed a one-year contract with the phoenix coyotes .
