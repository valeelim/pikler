Henry Maksoud     henry maksoud ( 8 march 1929 – 17 april 2014 ) was a brazilian businessman . he got the title of `` master of science in fluid mechanics '' from the university of iowa in the united states . he was the owner of the five-star hotel maksoud plaza in são paulo . he was also the owner of the magazine `` visão '' . he was chairman of the engineering institute of são paulo from 1967 to 1968. maksoud was born in aquidauana , mato grosso do sul . maksoud died after having a cardiac arrest on 17 april 2014 in são paulo . he was 85. other websites . - profile at university of iowa , college of engineering
