Church of la Magdalena (Torrelaguna)    church of la magdalena ( torrelaguna ) the church of la magdalena ( spanish : `` iglesia de la magdalena '' ) is a gothic-style church located in torrelaguna , central spain . it was listed as spain 's national heritage listing in 1983. the bell tower in this church was built in the 15th century , and was commissioned by cardinal cisneros .
