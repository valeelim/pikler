Bharat Ek Khoj     bharat ek khoj is a series of 53 episodes about the history of india . it is based on discovery of india ( 1946 ) , a book by jawaharlal nehru . it was directed , written and produced by shyam benegal in 1988 for doordarshan . it stars roshan seth as jawaharlal nehru . om puri is the narrator . it was first aired on dd national . the episodes are about india 's 5000-year history till independence in 1947. its cast also includes tom alter , naseerudin shah , irrfan khan .
