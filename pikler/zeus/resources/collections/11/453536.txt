National Chiang Kai-shek Memorial Hall     the chiang kai-shek memorial hall is a famous memorial in taipei , republic of china . it was built for the late president of the republic of china chiang kai-shek . it is located in taipei 's zhongzheng district . the monument is in a park . there is a library and museum in the monument . it has books and artefacts about chiang 's life and the history of the republic of china .
