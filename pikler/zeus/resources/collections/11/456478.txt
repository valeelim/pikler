Labrador, Canada    labrador , canada labrador is the part of the province of newfoundland and labrador in canada that is on the mainland . the strait of belle isle to the east is between it and the island of newfoundland in atlantic canada . to the south and west is the province of quebec . not many people live in labrador ; in fact , only 8 % of the population of the province of newfoundland and labrador lives in it , even though labrador is twice as big as newfoundland . until the 1950s , most of the people who lived there were aboriginals . then some europeans immigrated to the area and worked as merchants ; these people were once called settlers .
