David Starkey     david starkey cbe fsa ( born 3 january 1945 ) is a british historian . he is also a presenter on television and radio . his main topic is the british royal family . starkey was born in kendal , westmorland . he studied at kendal grammar school and at fitzwilliam college , cambridge , where he gained a phd . in the early 1970s he moved to london , where he became a teacher . starkey 's television work includes : `` the six wives of henry viii '' , `` monarchy by david starkey '' , `` the tudors '' , `` '' and `` the churchills '' . starkey lives in highgate , london and kent . he is an atheist . he is openly gay and lives with his partner .
