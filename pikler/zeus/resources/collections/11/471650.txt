El Chavo del Ocho     el chavo del ocho was a very popular mexican sitcom . it was created and written by roberto gómez bolaños . it stars roberto gómez bolaños , maría antonieta de las nieves , carlos villagrán , florinda meza , ramón valdés , rubén aguirre , édgar vivar , raúl padilla , horacio gómez bolaños , and angelines fernández . it ran from 1971 through 1980 for 8 seasons .
