Jacques Castérède     jacques castérède ( 10 april 1926 – 6 april 2014 ) was a french composer and pianist . he won the grand prix de rome in 1953 with his cantata `` la boîte de pandore '' ( `` pandora 's box '' ) . he was born in paris . castérède died on 6 april 2014 in dijon from natural causes , aged 87 .
