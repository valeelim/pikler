Ulrich Beck     ulrich beck ( 15 may 1944 – 1 january 2015 ) was a german sociologist and educator . he made the term risk society out of globalization . and made studies on democratic and cosmopolitan society . he was also a professor of sociology at the university of munich and at the london school of economics . beck was born in the pomeranian town of stolp ( now słupsk in poland ) . he got his ph.d. in 1972. he was married to elisabeth beck-gernsheim . beck died from a heart attack on 1 january 2015 , aged 70 .
