This Is the Army     this is the army is a 1943 american wartime comedy-musical movie directed by michael curtiz . it was produced hal b. wallis and jack l. warner . it stars george murphy , joan leslie , ronald reagan , alan hale , sr. , and rosemary decamp . joe louis , irving berlin , kate smith , and frances langford appear as themselves . it was released on august 14 , 1943 .
