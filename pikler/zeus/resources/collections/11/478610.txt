Nyhem Week     the nyhem week ( ) is a major annual conference by the swedish pentecostal movement . it occurs during the midsummer week every year . the conference is held in nyhem in mullsjö municipality in sweden . it was first held in 1917. host is the mullsjö-nyhem pentecostal congregation . up to 2008 the conference were held inside a large tent . from 2009 the conference is instead held inside nyhemshallen .
