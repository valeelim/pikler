José Wilker     josé wilker de almeida ( 20 august 1947 – 5 april 2014 ) was a brazilian actor and director . his acting and directing roles include : `` xica da silva '' ( 1976 ) , `` dona flor and her two husbands '' ( 1976 ) , `` professor kranz tedesco di germania '' ( 1978 ) , `` bye bye brazil '' ( 1979 ) , `` the man in the black cape '' ( 1986 ) , `` besame mucho '' ( 1986 ) , `` subway to the stars '' ( 1987 ) , `` medicine man '' ( 1992 ) , `` the hitchhiker 's guide to the galaxy '' ( 2005 ) , `` the greatest love of all '' ( 2006 ) , `` casa da mãe joana '' ( 2008 ) , and `` casa da mãe joana 2 '' ( 2013 ) . he was born in juazeiro do norte , ceará . wilker died from a heart attack on 5 april 2014 at his home in rio de janeiro . he was 66 years old .
