Emilio Álvarez Montalván    emilio álvarez montalván emilio álvarez montalván ( 31 july 1919 – 2 july 2014 ) was a nicaraguan diplomat and politician . her served as a the foreign minister of the republic of nicaragua from 1996 until 2001. he was a ophthalmologist , medical doctor and writer . álvarez was born in managua . he was married to carmen guerra . they had six children . álvarez died from a heart attack on 2 july 2014 , aged 94 .
