Władysław Sidorowicz     władysław sidorowicz ( 17 september 1945 in wilno — 24 july 2014 in wrocław ) was a polish senator , representing civic platform . he was a member of the polish solidarity trade union where he was the director of its regional coordinating commission for health service workers . during the martial law in poland he was arrested and interned . he participated in the round table talks . he was a minister of health ( january 1991 – december 1991 ) .
