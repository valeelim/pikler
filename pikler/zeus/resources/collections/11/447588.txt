Luciano do Valle     luciano do valle queirós ( 4 july 1947 – 19 april 2014 ) was a brazilian sports commentator , television presenter , journalist and entrepreneur . he worked on several broadcasters on television—rede globo from 1971 to 1982 , rede record from 1982 to 1983 and 2003 to 2006 , and rede bandeirantes from 1983 to 2003 and 2006 to his death . he was born in campinas , são paulo . luciano do valle died on the afternoon of 19 april 2014 in uberlândia , minas gerais , aged 66 .
