Twisted Metal 4     twisted metal 4 is a vehicular combat video game that was both published by sony computer entertainment and developed by 989 studios . it is the fourth game in the `` twisted metal '' series . it was only released on the playstation and was only released in north america . the game was released on october 31 , 1999 in north america for playstation . it was rereleased in 2000 as part of the sony greatest hits lineup . ign 's marc nix rated the game a 6 out of 10 and praised the `` colorful '' graphics and `` well-done '' interactive environments but criticized the `` toy car '' control , look and sound of the vehicles . gamespot 's joe fielder said that the game was a `` huge leap '' ahead of `` twisted metal iii '' , commenting on the improved level design , control and physics , but he also said that the latter two `` remain a little too touchy and unforgiving '' and that the graphics , while they were `` prettier '' than those of twisted metal iii , they `` are n't nearly as sharp as the graphics in its current main competitor - activision 's `` '' '' .
