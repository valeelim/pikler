Conker: Live &amp; Reloaded    conker : live & amp ; reloaded conker : live and reloaded ( originally known as conker : live and uncut , but it was actually more censored ) is a platform game on the original xbox . the game is a remake of the n64 game `` conker 's bad fur day '' . the game has much better graphics , thanks to the xbox 's superior hardware . it has new enemies . it has a few changes in design . it has an improved multiplayer mode which supports system link as well as local play and solo with bots . it also supported xbox live before the original xbox lost support for xbox live . the game does not have some profanity that was in the original game .
