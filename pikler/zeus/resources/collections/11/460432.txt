Gerry Anderson (broadcaster)    gerry anderson ( broadcaster ) gerald michael `` gerry '' anderson ( 28 october 1944 - 21 august 2014 ) was a northern irish radio and television broadcaster . he worked for bbc northern ireland . he was known for his unique style and somewhat unusual sense of humor . anderson often called himself on his show , as `` turkey neck '' , `` puppet chin '' or `` golf mike alpha '' . anderson died in belfast , northern ireland from a long illness , aged 69. other websites . - radio foyle biography
