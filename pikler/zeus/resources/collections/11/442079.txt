Barbro Kollberg     barbro anna elisabet hildestrand ( née kollberg ; 27 december 1917 – 6 march 2014 ) was a swedish actress . she is best known for her roles in `` hem från babylon '' ( 1941 ) , `` it rains on our love '' ( 1946 ) , `` a life for the taking '' ( 1995 ) and `` as it is in heaven '' ( 2004 ) . she was born in eskilstuna , södermanland county . kollberg died on 6 march 2014 in stockholm . she was 96 years old . other websotes . - barbro kollberg at the swedish film database
