John Persen     john persen ( 9 november 1941 – 12 december 2014 ) was a norwegian composer . he was born in porsanger , norway . he was the son-in-law of finn strømsted . he was awarded lindemanprisen in 1999 , and edvardprisen twice , and was awarded honorary membership of the norwegian society of composers . persen died in oslo , norway , aged 73 .
