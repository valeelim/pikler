L.I.E.    l.i.e . l.i.e . is a 2001 american drama movie . the movie is about a relationship between a 15-year old boy and a pedophile . the title is an acronym for `` long island expressway '' . `` l.i.e . '' received an nc-17 rating from the mpaa . the rating was surrendered , meaning that the filmmakers preferred to release the movie without a rating instead of releasing it with the nc-17 rating . later , an edited r-rated version was also released . this movie was released on january 20 , 2001 .
