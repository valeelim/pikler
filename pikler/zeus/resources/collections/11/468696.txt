The Dog Who Saved Easter     the dog who saved easter is an american family romantic comedy movie . it was released on march 18 , 2014. the movie is a direct-to-video production . it was directed by sean olson . the movie is the second movie to feature zeus the dog . the first movie is called `` the dog who saved christmas vacation '' ( 2010 ) . cast . - dean cain as ted stein - elisa donovan as belinda bannister - beverley mitchell as alice - patrick muldoon as fred stein - catherine hicks as cressida - mario lopez as zeus ( voice ) - joey diaz as stewey ( as joey 'coco ' diaz ) - nicole eggert as gabrielle ( voice ) - matthew lawrence as will - mindy sterling as grandma - tiffany thornton as karen
