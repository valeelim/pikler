Operation Yewtree     operation yewtree is an investigation by the metropolitan police into sex crimes . it began in october 2012 , after jimmy savile , who died in 2011 , was found to have been a sexual predator for decades . people investigated include : max clifford , dave lee travis , rolf harris , gary glitter , freddie starr , paul gambaccini , chris denning , jim davidson , jimmy tarbuck , wilfred de'ath and ted beston . clifford , harris and denning have been convicted after being investigated by operation yewtree .
