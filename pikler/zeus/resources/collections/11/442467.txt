Republic of Crimea     the republic of crimea ( crimean tatar : къырым джумхуриети , `` qırım cumhuriyeti '' ; russian : республика крым , `` respublika krym '' ) is a russian federal subject . it is the crimean peninsula in the black sea . it is located southeast of ukraine and west of southern russia . its capital is simferopol . the parliament of crimea declared independence in 2014 after the victory of euromaidan and joined russia .
