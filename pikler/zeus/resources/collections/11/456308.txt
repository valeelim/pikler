Saarland national football team     the saarland national football team ( german : `` saarländische fußballnationalmannschaft '' ) was the association football team representing saarland from 1950 to 1956 during the french occupation after world war ii . their last game was played on june 6 , 1956. shortly afterwards , the team merged into the west germany national football team . the following shows all games played by the saarland team .
