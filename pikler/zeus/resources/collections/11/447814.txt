Herb Gray     the right honourable herbert eser `` herb '' gray ( may 25 , 1931 – april 21 , 2014 ) was a canadian politician and lawyer . he was a cabinet minister and served under three prime ministers . he was the deputy prime minister of canada from 1997 until 2002. he was also canada 's first jewish federal cabinet minister . gray was born in windsor , ontario . he was married to sharon sholzberg and had two children . gray died on april 21 , 2014 at a hospital in ottawa , ontario , aged 82. other websites . - herb gray – parliament of canada biography
