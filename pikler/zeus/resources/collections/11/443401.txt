Miquel Crusafont i Pairó     miquel crusafont i pairó ( 1910 , sabadell , spain—1983 ) was a catalan palaeontologist . he specialised in mammal bones . crusafont obtained a degree in pharmacy from the university of barcelona in 1933. he got a degree in natural sciences from the university of madrid in 1950. in 1961 , he was appointed professor of palaeontology at the university of oviedo . he later became professor of anthropology at the societatis iesu ( barcelona ) . in 1969 , crusafont founded the provincial palaeontology institute . it has been known as the miquel crusafont palaeontology institute since 1983. the prehistoric mammal `` crusafontia '' was named for crusafont . selected works . - `` ( 1943 , with josep fernández de villalta ) - `` ( 1948 , with josep fernández de villalta ) - `` ( 1955 , with josep fernández de villalta and jaume truyols ) - `` ( 1957 , with jaume truyols ) - `` '' ( 1966 , with bermudo meléndez and emiliano aguirre ) other websites . - information about the miquel crusafont palaeontology institute
