Festus Mogae     festus gontebanye mogae ( born 21 august 1939 ) is a motswana politician . he served as president of botswana from 1998 to 2008. he succeeded quett masire as president in 1998 and was reelected in october 2004. after being president for ten years , he stepped down in 2008 and was succeeded by lieutenant general ian khama .
