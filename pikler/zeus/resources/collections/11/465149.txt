Lugo Cathedral     saint mary 's cathedral ( ) , better known as lugo cathedral , is a roman catholic cathedral and basilica . it is located in lugo , galicia ( spain ) . it was built starting from the early 12th century . it was started in the romanesque style . during its construction the style changed to gothic and baroque architecture . the cathedral has a latin cross floor plan , with a length of . it has a nave , covered by a barrel vault , and two aisles , with an ambulatory and five apse chapels .
