The Pillow Book     the pillow book is a book written by sei shōnagon . sei shōnagon was a member of the kiyohara family . she wrote the notes that made up her diary ( or `` pillow book '' ) while she was a member of the court of empress sadako . the book is a classic of japanese literature . it is one of two of the greatest examples of prose literature of this time period . it is a collection of pieces of writing that vary from one or two sentences to several pages . it is the author 's observations of people , life at court , and nature . other websites . - the pillow book
