Sol Hurok     sol hurok ( solomon isaievich hurok ; born solomon izrailevich gurkov , russian соломон израилевич гурков ; april 9 , 1888 – march 5 , 1974 ) was a world-famous 20th-century american impresario . hurok was born in pogar , chernigov governorate , russian empire ( in present-day bryansk oblast , russia ) on april 9 , 1888. he became a naturalized citizen of the united states in 1914. hurok died on march 5 , 1974 from a heart attack in new york city , new york , aged 85 .
