Game of Thrones     game of thrones is an american fantasy drama television series . it is based on george r. r. martin 's book series `` a song of ice and fire '' . the first episode was shown on april 17 , 2011 on hbo . there have been six seasons so far . it is very popular around the world . the first season of `` game of thrones '' was nominated for thirteen of the 2011 emmy awards , including outstanding drama series . it won two . the series focuses on some kings and queens in two continents called westeros and essos . many of the successors to the throne fight for power . the most prominent kingdom is the lannister family . this royal family is very rich because they live near gold mines . some people criticized the series for the twincestual scenes and having too much nudity .
