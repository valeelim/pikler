Tampereen Pyrintö (basketball)    tampereen pyrintö ( basketball ) tampereen pyrintö is a basketball team from tampere , finland . pyrintö plays its home games at pyynikin palloiluhalli . pyrintö has won three finnish championships ( 2010 , 2011 and 2014 ) and two finnish cups ( 1969 , 2013 ) . other websites . - offical website - pyrinto tampere eurobasket.com
