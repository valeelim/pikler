Calabasas, California    calabasas , california calabasas is a city in northwestern los angeles county , california . it is located in the hills west of the san fernando valley and is in the northwest santa monica mountains between woodland hills , agoura hills , west hills , hidden hills and malibu , california . as of the 2010 u.s. census , the city population was 23,058. formerly an unincorporated area of los angeles county , the area was incorporated as a city in 1991 .
