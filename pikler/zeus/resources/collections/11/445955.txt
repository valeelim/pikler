Grand Duchy of Oldenburg     the grand duchy of oldenburg was a state in europe . it was in the area that today is germany . it was created out of the smaller `` duchy '' of oldenburg . the state was created at the congress of vienna in 1814 but ceased to exist after the german revolution in 1918. the capital of the state was oldenburg . the grand ducal family lived at the `` schloss oldenburg '' in the capital .
