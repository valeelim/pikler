First inauguration of Woodrow Wilson     the first inauguration of woodrow wilson took place on march 4 , 1913. it was the beginning of his position as the 28th president of the united states and thomas r. marshall 's tenure as vice president . chief justice edward d. white administered the oath of office . wilson wanted and asked that the inaugural ball to be canceled . he found it inappropriate for the occasion because of women 's suffrage and women 's rights protesting .
