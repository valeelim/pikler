Don Edwards (singer)    don edwards ( singer ) don edwards ( born march 20 , 1939 in boonton , new jersey ) is an american singer and guitarist . he performs western music . edwards has recorded several albums , two of which , `` saddle songs '' and `` songs of the cowboy '' , are included in the folklore archives of the library of congress . edwards also performs the song `` coyotes '' that plays during the final minutes of the documentary `` grizzly man '' . in 2005 , edwards was inducted into the western music association hall of fame . other websites . - official music website - artist profile at cowboypoetry.com - `` don edwards : in his own words '' ( 30-minute interview ) - [ allmusic ]
