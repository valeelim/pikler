Jay Nixon     jeremiah wilson `` jay '' nixon ( born february 13 , 1956 ) is an american politician . he was the governor of missouri from 2009 to 2017. on january 12 , 2009 , he became governor of the u.s. state of missouri . he is a member of the democratic party of the united states . nixon 's ancestor , john inskeep , was mayor of philadelphia , pennsylvania .
