Howard H. Callaway     howard hollis callaway , known as bo callaway ( april 2 , 1927 -march 15 , 2014 ) was an american politician and businessman . he served as secretary of the army from 1973 to 1975. he was also a united states representative from georgia . callaway was born in lagrange , georgia . he died in atlanta , georgia from a brain hemorrhage , aged 86 .
