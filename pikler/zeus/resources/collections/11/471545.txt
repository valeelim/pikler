Chinese Rail SS3B     the ss3b ( ) is a kind of ac-powered electric locomotives used in china . it was built by ziyang locomotive works in 2002 and is still produced as of 2009. ss3b have been manufactured by several companies : - dalian locomotive & rolling stock works ( 7001 ) - zhuzhou electric locomotive works ( 0001~0119 ) - csr ziyang locomotive works ( 5001~5104 ) - datong locomotive factory ( 6001~6129 )
