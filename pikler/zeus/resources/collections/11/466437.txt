Portuguese Football Federation     the portuguese football federation ( fpf ) ( fpf ; ) is the governing body of football in portugal . it organises the campeonato nacional de seniores , the taça de portugal , the supertaça cândido de oliveira , youth levels , women 's football , beach soccer , futsal , and also the men 's and the women 's national football teams . it was formed in 1914. it is based in lisbon .
