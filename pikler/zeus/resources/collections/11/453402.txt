Steven Bochco     steven ronald bochco ( born december 16 , 1943 ) is a american television producer and writer . he created a number of popular television hits including `` hill street blues '' , `` l.a. law '' , `` doogie howser , m.d . `` , and `` nypd blue '' , as well as some notable flops such as `` cop rock '' . he won an emmy award for his writing in 1981 and in 1987 .
