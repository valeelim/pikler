Century Series     century series is the nickname of a group of fighter aircraft built in usa for the cold war . it was all aircraft with number 100 in the code . - north american f-100 super sabre . - mcdonnell f-101 voodoo . - convair f-102 delta dagger . - lockheed f-104 starfighter . - republic f-105 thunderchief . - convair f-106 delta dart . - north american yf-107 ultra sabre .
