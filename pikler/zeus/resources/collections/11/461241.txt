Bipan Chandra     bipan chandra ( 1928-30 august 2014 ) was an indian historian . he wrote about economic and political history of modern india . he specialized on the national movement and is considered to be one india 's greatest scholars on mahatma gandhi . he wrote several books , including `` the rise and growth of economic nationalism '' . chandra died on the morning of 30 august 2014 , at his home in gurgaon , after a long-illness , aged 86 .
