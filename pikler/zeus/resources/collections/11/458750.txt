Diocese of Edmonton     the diocese of edmonton is a diocese of the anglican church of canada . it is part of the ecclesiastical province of rupert 's land . it covers the central part of alberta and the headquaters of the diocese is in edmonton . the diocese was created in 1913 from part of the diocese of calgary . the current bishop of the diocese is jane alexander , who became bishop in 2008. the diocese 's cathedral is all saints ' anglican cathedral in edmonton .
