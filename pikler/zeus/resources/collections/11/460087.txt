Børre Knudsen     børre knudsen ( september 24 , 1937 – august 17 , 2014 ) was a norwegian lutheran minister . he was known for his pro-life ( against abortion ) activism . knudsen and a few more activist created the a new norwegian church called deanery of strandebarm . he was made bishop of the church in 1997. knudsen was born in vennesla , norway . knudsen died in mestervik , norway , aged 76 .
