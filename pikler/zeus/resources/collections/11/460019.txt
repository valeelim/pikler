Ruth Davidson     ruth davidson ( born 10 november 1978 ) is a scottish politician . she is leader of the scottish conservative and unionist party . she is the youngest ever party leader in the country . davidson was elected as a member of the scottish parliament in may 2011. she became leader of the scottish conservative party on 4 november 2011. before being an msp she worked as a journalist . she was in the territorial army for three years . she also worked as a sunday school teacher . davidson was born in edinburgh . she lives in partick , glasgow . she is a lesbian . other websites . - ruth davidson 's page on the scottish parliament website - biography on the scottish conservatives website
