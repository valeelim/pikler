Birdman (movie)    birdman ( movie ) birdman or ( the unexpected virtue of ignorance ) is a 2014 black comedy-drama movie directed , produced , and written by alejandro gonzález iñárritu . it stars michael keaton , zach galifianakis , edward norton , emma stone , amy ryan , andrea riseborough , lindsay duncan , and naomi watts . it was released for a limited time on october 17 , 2014. it received very positive reviews . it won the academy award for best picture .
