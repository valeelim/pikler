Mimmi     the mimmi series ( ) is a book series written by viveca lärn ( earlier viveca sundvall ) . the books tell the stories about a girl in late kindergarten-early elementary school age in sweden called mimmi . the books are told from the me-perspective . many of the books are written as a diary . the books were originally published between 1979 and 1996. mimmi 's father oskar is a postman . her mother elin is a waitress at restaurant `` gyllene svanen '' . books . - 1979 - `` monstret i skåpet '' - 1982 - `` en ettas dagbok '' - 1983 - `` roberta karlsson och kungen '' - 1985 - `` vi smyger på enok '' - 1985 - `` mimmi och kalla handen '' - 1986 - `` vingmuttern min allra bästa vän '' - 1986 - `` mimmis bok '' - 1988 - `` mimmi och kexfabriken '' - 1990 - `` mimmi får en farfar '' - 1994 - `` mera mimmi '' - 1996 - `` mimmi och anders ''
