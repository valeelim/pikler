June Angela     june angela ( born june angela profanato , august 1959 ) is an american actress , tap dancer and singer . she was 10 years old in season 1 ( 1969-1970 ) of `` sesame street '' ( where she cameo appeared ) and 12-17 and 49 years old in seasons 1-7 ( 1971-1977 , 2006 and 2009 ) of `` the electric company '' .
