Ronald Reagan Park, Gdańsk    ronald reagan park , gdańsk the ronald reagan park ( ) is located in the neighborhood of przymorze wielkie , gdańsk , poland . it was built between 2003 and 2006 out of formerly barren lands . it covers 40 hectares . it honors us president ronald reagan , who was considered for playing a major role in the fall of communism in poland . it also honors pope john paul ii .
