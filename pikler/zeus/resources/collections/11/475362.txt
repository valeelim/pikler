Power Rangers Wild Force     power rangers wild force is the tenth season of the power rangers american television series . it aired from february to november 2002. the first half was aired on fox kids . after the disney purchase of fox family , the second half aired on abc kids . this was the last power rangers to be filmed in the us . disney moved filming to new zealand . it is based off the super sentai series , hyakujuu sentai gaoranger . ironically , gaoranger celebrated 25 years of sentai while wild force celebrated 10 years of power rangers . the show is best known for one of the best power rangers specials to date , `` forever red '' .
