Nivin Pauly     nivin pauly ( born october 11 , 1984 ) is an indian movie actor . he mostly acts in malayalam movies . his first movie was `` malarvadi arts club '' . some of his other movies are `` thattathin marayathu '' , `` da thadiya '' , `` neram '' , `` 1983 '' , `` om shanti oshaana '' , `` bangalore days '' and `` premam '' .
