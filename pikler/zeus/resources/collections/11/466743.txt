Pac-Man and the Ghostly Adventures     pac-man and the ghostly adventures is a japanese/canadian/american computer animated television series . it is produced by bandico namco games , arad productions inc. and 41 entertainment . in the united states , the series first aired on june 15 , 2013 on disney xd . in canada , it was first shown on march 17 , 2014 on disney xd . in japan , it was first shown on april 5 , 2014 on tokyo mx and bs11 . on the planet pac-world , pac-man and his friends cylindria and spiral help to protect pacopolis from the threat of ghosts characters . - pac-man - spiral - cylindria - betrayus
