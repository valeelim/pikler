Ramón Echarren Istúriz     ramón echarren istúriz ( november 13 , 1929 – august 25 , 2014 ) was a spanish roman catholic bishop . istúriz ordained to the priesthood in 1958. istúriz was appointed titular bishop of 'diano ' and auxiliary bishop of the roman catholic archdiocese of madrid , spain , in 1969. in 1978 , he was appointed bishop of the roman catholic diocese of canarias and retired in 2005 .
