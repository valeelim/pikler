Vladimir Ivashko     vladimir antonovich ivashko ( russian language : влади́мир анто́нович ива́шко 28 october 1932 – 13 november 1994 ) was a ukrainian politician . he was the leader of the soviet union for a short time . he became the leader in 1991. he ruled for five days until his political party was declared politically impotent and on 29 august 1991 it was banned . ivashko died from an `` undisclosed long-illness '' in moscow , aged 62. ukk
