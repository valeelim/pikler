Bel Kaufman     bella `` bel '' kaufman ( may 10 , 1911 - july 25 , 2014 ) was an ukrainian-american teacher and author . she was best known for writing the 1965 bestselling novel `` up the down staircase '' . kaufman was born in berlin , germany to ukrainian parents . she was raised in odessa and kiev , ukraine . kaufman died at home in new york city on july 25 , 2014 , aged 103 .
