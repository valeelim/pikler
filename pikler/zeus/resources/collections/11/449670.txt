Winfield Dunn     bryant winfield culberson dunn ( born july 1 , 1927 ) is an american politician , dentist , and medical industry official . he was governor of tennessee from 1971 to 1975. he was the state 's first republican governor in fifty years , and was the sixth one since the civil war . dunn ran for a second term in 1986. he lost to ned mcwherter . he remained active in the republican party and the medical field after his term as governor . dunn was born in meridian , mississippi .
