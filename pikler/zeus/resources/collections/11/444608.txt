Martin Elkort     martin edward elkort ( born april 18 , 1929 ) is an american photographer , illustrator and writer . he is best known for his street photography . many of his works are held and displayed by several well known art museums in the united states . elkort was born in the new york city , new york borough of the bronx . he grew up during the great depression . he married edythe in 1953. he now lives in los angeles , california with his wife .
