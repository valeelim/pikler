Darhal Tehsil     darhal tehsil is a town and notified constituency of rajouri district indian state of jammu & kashmir . darhal is a tehsil in rajauri district of jammu & kashmir state , india . it is at 1671 m elevation ( altitude ) . urdu is the local language . people also speak gojri and dogri . it is cold in summer . darhal summer highest day temperature is in between 19 ° c to 43° c. average temperatures of january is 11 ° c , february is 13 ° c , march is 19 ° c , april is 24 ° c , may is 30 ° c .
