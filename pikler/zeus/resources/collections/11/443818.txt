Canadian National Railway     the canadian national railway company is a class i railway . its headquarters are in montreal , quebec . it serves canada and both the midwestern and southern united states . in 2011 , bill gates was the largest single shareholder of cn stock . cn owns around 20,400 route miles ( 32,831 km ) of track in eight provinces . there is also a 70-mile ( 113 km ) stretch of track into the northwest territories , to hay river on the southern shore of great slave lake . other websites . - official website
