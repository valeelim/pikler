Armageddon (2006)    armageddon ( 2006 ) armageddon ( 2006 ) was a professional wrestling pay-per-view show made by world wrestling entertainment ( wwe ) . it was held on december 17 , 2006 at richmond coliseum in richmond , virginia . it was the seventh armageddon event held by the wwe . the pay-per-view was a `` smackdown ! '' brand exclusive . the main event was a tag team match , which saw the team of batista and john cena defeat the team of king booker and finlay . two other matches on the undercard , saw the undertaker defeat mr. kennedy in a last ride match , and kane defeat montel vontavious porter in an inferno match . other websites . - official armageddon 2006 website
