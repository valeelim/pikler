The Only Way Is Essex     the only way is essex ( often known as towie ) is a bafta award-winning television soap opera . based in essex , england , it is a 'scripted reality ' show . it has `` real people in modified situations , saying unscripted lines but in a structured way '' . the show is filmed just a few days in advance . each series lasts six weeks . series 1 started on 10 october 2010. it is narrated by denise van outen , who is from basildon , essex . series 1-9 have been shown in australia . series one has been shown in south africa . cast . - james `` arg '' argent - jess wright - lauren pope - chloe sims - billie faiers other websites . - the only way is essex at itv.com
