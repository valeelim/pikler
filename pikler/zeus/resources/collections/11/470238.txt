Barnard's Loop    barnard 's loop barnard 's loop is an emission nebula in the constellation of orion . it is about 1,600 light-years away from earth . it is part of the orion complex . it is a semicircular arc that is about 300 light-years across . in the center of bernard 's loop are two nebula . these are the orion nebula and the horsehead nebula . bernard 's loop is made up of hot gas . the mainly red color comes from hydrogen gas . related pages . - orion ( constellation ) - nebulae other websites . - barnard 's loop ( sh2-276 ) - barnard 's loop and more in orion-today 's image-earthsky - apod : 2005 april 20 - barnard 's loop around orion
