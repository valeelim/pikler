João Ubaldo Ribeiro     joão ubaldo osório pimental ribeiro ( 23 january 1941 – 18 july 2014 ) was a brazilian writer . his best known novel was `` an invincible memory '' which was a success in the english-speaking world . several of his books and short stories have been made into movies and tv series in brazil . ribeiro was born in itaparica , bahia . he had been married three times and had four children . ribeiro died from a pulmonary embolism on 18 july 2014 in rio de janeiro , aged 73 .
