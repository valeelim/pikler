IPad Mini (1st generation)    ipad mini ( 1st generation ) the first generation ipad mini ( sold as ipad mini ) is a tablet computer that is made and sold by apple inc. it is the first of the ipad mini line of mini tablet computers . it has a screen size of 7.9 inches , compared to the 9.7 inch screen on a regular ipad . it was released on november 2 , 2012. it received many good reviews , with reviewers talking about the device 's size , design , and the number of applications that are available . a second generation ipad mini was announced on october 22 , 2013. the first generation ipad mini is still being sold , even though it has less storage and is sold at a lower price .
