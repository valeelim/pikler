Nancy Charest     nancy charest ( november 28 , 1959 – march 1 , 2014 ) was a canadian politician . she represented the of matane in the national assembly of quebec from 2003 to 2007 as a member of the quebec liberal party . she was defeated by pascal bérubé of the parti québécois in the 2007 provincial election . she was born in matane , quebec . charest was found dead on march 1 , 2014. her body was found on a roadside in gaspé peninsula , quebec . she was 54 years old . other websites . - nancy charest at the national assembly of quebec
