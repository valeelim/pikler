Ingvar Kjellson     ingvar kjellson ( 20 may 1923 – 18 december 2014 ) was a swedish stage and movie actor . kjellson was born in kärna , östergötland county . he was known for his roles in `` hedda gabler '' , `` the wild duck '' , `` the beggars ' opera '' and `` twelfth night '' . kjellson died at the age of 91 from pneumonia on 18 december 2014 in stockholm , sweden . other websites . - ingvar kjellson , at dramaten
