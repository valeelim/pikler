Osteology     osteology is the scientific study of bones , practiced by osteologists . it is a part of anatomy and is used by workers in palaeontology , anthropology , and archeology . osteology studies the skeleton , and other bony structures . it includes the study of the structure of bones , teeth , their function and growth . it is often used by scientists for the identification of vertebrate remains , including the age , death , sex , and development .
