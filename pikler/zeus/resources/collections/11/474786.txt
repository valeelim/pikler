Bob Rivers     bob rivers ( born july 7 , 1956 ) is an american radio actor and personality . he spent almost six years working at waaf in worcester , massachusetts . he has also hosted at other radio stations in seattle and in baltimore . one of the first parodies he produced was `` breakin ' up is hard on you '' . that was about the bell system breakup . his 1987 album `` twisted christmas '' had the song `` twelve pains of christmas '' . rivers was born in branford , connecticut .
