Dean Spade     dean spade ( born 1977 ) is an american lawyer , writer , and associate professor of law at seattle university school of law . he started the sylvia rivera law project in 2002. in 2011 spade 's first book `` normal life : administrative violence , critical trans politics , and the limits of law '' was published . the book was nominated for a 2011 lambda literary award in the category of transgender nonfiction . spade is of jewish descent and is involved in transgender activities .
