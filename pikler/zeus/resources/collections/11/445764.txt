John Hawkes (actor)    john hawkes ( actor ) john hawkes ( born john marvin perkins ; september 11 , 1959 ) is an american actor and musician . his best known movie appearances are the academy award nominated role as teardrop in `` winter 's bone '' and his golden globe and screen actors guild award nominated role as mark o'brien in `` the sessions '' . on television , he is known for his role as sol star on the hbo television shows `` deadwood '' and as dustin powers on `` eastbound & down '' . hawkes was born in alexandria , minnesota .
