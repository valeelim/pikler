Ontario Highway 10     ontario highway 10 , also called king 's highway 10 , is a provincial highway in ontario , canada . it is 136.6 kilometers ( 84.9 miles ) long . the highway starts at the northern end of highway 410 near caledon . it then goes northwestward through the town of orangeville , where it connects with the western end of highway 9. it also passes through shelburne , where it shares a road with highway 89 briefly . the northern end of highway 10 is in owen sound , where it ends with highway 6 , highway 21 , and highway 26 .
