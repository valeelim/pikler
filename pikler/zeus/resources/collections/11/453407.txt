Tom Courtenay     sir thomas daniel `` tom '' courtenay ( born 25 february 1937 ) is a english actor . his career began during the 1960s . he is known for his roles in `` the loneliness of the long distance runner '' ( 1962 ) , `` billy liar '' ( 1963 ) , `` doctor zhivago '' ( 1965 ) , `` the dresser '' ( 1983 ) , and in `` last orders '' . he won a golden globe award for best actor in 1983 and a bafta tv award for best actor in 1999 .
