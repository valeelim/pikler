Golden oriole     the golden oriole ( `` oriolus oriolus '' or eurasian golden oriole ) is the only oriole breeding in northern hemisphere temperate regions . it is one of the oriole family of passerine birds . it is a summer migrant in europe and western asia and spends the winter season in central and southern africa . golden orioles have an extremely large range with large populations that are apparently stable . until a few years ago this species was treated as being conspecific with the indian golden oriole . they are now in separate species .
