Honor killing     an honor killing is a member of a family being killed by other members , because of the killers feeling that the victim has brought shame on the family . reasons a woman could be targeted include refusing to enter an arranged marriage , having sex outside marriage , and being a victim of rape . honor killings are associated with the middle east and south asia . they also happen in countries that have immigrants from those places . murder is not the only honor crime . acid attacks and beating are also done for the same reasons as honor killings . victims of honor killings are mostly young women .
