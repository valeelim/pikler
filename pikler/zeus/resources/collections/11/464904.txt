Man Against Machine     man against machine is the eleventh studio album by garth brooks . the album was released on 11 november 2014. it was brooks ' second studio album on his new label pearl records . it was his first studio album new material in nine years . two of the singles , `` people loving people , '' and `` send 'em on down the road '' were released early and made available digitally on brooks ' official website and ghosttunes.com . other websites . - garth brooks official website
