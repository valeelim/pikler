Antônio Ermírio de Moraes     antônio ermírio de moraes ( june 4 , 1928 – august 24 , 2014 ) was a brazilian businessman . he was the chairman of the votorantim group ; one of the country 's largest companies , specialized in metals , paper , cement and frozen orange juice . he was the grandfather of indycar series driver mario moraes . he ran for governor of são paulo state in 1986 , but lost the elections . de moraes died in são paulo , brazil from heart failure , aged 86 .
