Jacques Herlin     jacques herlin ( 17 august 1927 – 7 june 2014 ) was a french character actor . he was active from 1956 until his death . his acting career stated off as appearing in comedy movies such as `` don camillo in moscow '' ( 1965 ) , `` sexy susan sins again '' ( 1968 ) and `` property is no longer a theft '' ( 1973 ) . his later movies were `` national lampoon 's european vacation '' ( 1985 ) , `` a good year '' ( 2006 ) , `` of gods and men '' ( 2010 ) and `` farewell , my queen '' ( 2012 ) . herlin was born in le vésinet , yvelines as jacques de jouette . aside from french , he could speak english , german and italian . herlin died on 7 june 2014 at a hospital in paris , aged 86 .
