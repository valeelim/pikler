Thittuvilai     thittuvilai is a town panchayat situated 10/11 km north of nagercoil passing vadasery , putheri , erachakuzham , navelkad , eshathimangalam , thuvarancaud . it is a panoramic village sourrounded by mountains in all the three directions except south . the main attraction is mukkoodal dam which is the source of drinking water to nagercoil and its adjacent villages . until now this village is a standing example of religious harmoney , the main population is muslims but considerable percentage of christians and hindus are also living in perfect cooperation with each other . the bus route from nagercoil is 4 , 4a , 4b , 4c , 4d , 4t , 318 etc .
