Dietmar Schönherr     dietmar otto schönherr ( 17 may 1926 – 18 july 2014 ) was an austrian actor and entertainer . he made 120 movie appearances between 1944 and 2014. he was best known for playing the role of major cliff allister mclane in the german science fiction series `` raumpatrouille '' . schönherr was born as dietmar otto von schönleiten in innsbruck . he was married to the danish actress vivi bach from 1965 until her death in 2013. schönherr died on 18 july 2014 in ibiza , spain , aged 88 .
