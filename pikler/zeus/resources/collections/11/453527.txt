Executive Yuan     the executive yuan ( ey ; ) is the executive branch of the government of the republic of china ( roc ) . it is led by the premier of the roc . it has a vice-premier and eleven cabinet ministers . it also has from five to nine ministers without portfolio as its members . the vice premier , ministers , and chairpersons are chosen by the president of the roc . this is on the recommendation of the premier . it was started in 1948. the ey is one of the five branches of government of the roc under their constitution . other websites . - official website - official website
