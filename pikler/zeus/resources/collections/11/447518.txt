Julie Bindel     julie bindel ( born 20 july 1962 ) is an english writer . she is a lesbian and radical feminist . she lives in crouch end , london . she is a freelance journalist . her work is often published by `` the guardian '' . bindel edits `` gaze : a modern review '' . she became involved in feminism in 1979 as a teenager in leeds . in 1990 she started justice for women with other feminists .
