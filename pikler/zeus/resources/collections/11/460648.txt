Albert Ebossé Bodjongo     albert dominique ebossé bodjongo dika ( 6 october 1989 – 23 august 2014 ) was a cameroonian soccer player who played for js kabylie in the algerian ligue professionnelle 1. on 23 august 2014 albert was struck by a projectile thrown by a fan just hours after a matchup between his home team js kabylie and usm alger . the match had ended in a 2-1 defeat and the cameroonian died of head injury due to the strike . he was aged 24 .
