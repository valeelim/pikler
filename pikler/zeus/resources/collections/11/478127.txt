Justin Capră     virgilius justin capra ( 22 february 1933 – 19 january 2015 ) was a romanian inventor , teacher and engineer . he was known for his many inventions including : the world 's 1st first jetpack ( named `` flying backpack '' ) , electric tricycle oblio and troty , the smallest car in the world soleta ( with a consumption of 0.5l / 100 km ) , first `` rocketcar '' , car with a plane engine ( virgilius ) and more .
