Haigazian University     haigazian university ( ; ) is a university in beirut , lebanon . it opened in 1955. it offers bachelor 's degrees in the arts and sciences , business administration and economics . it also offers master 's degrees in the arts , sciences and business administration . the university is supported by the armenian evangelical church . it was opened for the armenian community in lebanon . however , it is open to students of all nationalities . other websites . - haigazian university website
