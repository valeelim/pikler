Gerd Albrecht     gerd albrecht ( 19 july 1935 – 2 february 2014 ) was a german conductor . he was a first-prize winner at the international besançon competition for young conductors at age 22. he composed with many famous european orchestras such as deutsche oper berlin , the tonhalle-orchester zürich and the hamburg state opera . albrecht was born in essen . albrecht died at the age of 78 on 2 february 2014 in berlin , germany .
