Hay Day     hay day is a game made by the company supercell . it is available to play on many different mobile devices , including the iphone , ipad , and android . in the game , you take care of a farm . you can grow and harvest crops , trees , fruit bushes , animals , and go fishing . you can use things you grow to make other things , such as pie or butter . you can sell your crops and products in a shop . you can advertise your crops and products so that people around the world can buy things from your shop . as you earn more coins , you can buy production buildings to create even more products . you can also buy decoration items with coins . completing many actions gives you experience points , which helps you to level up . once you level up , you unlock the ability to build different production buildings , and can also unlock new crops , animals , and decorations .
