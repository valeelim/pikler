The Interview (2014 movie)    the interview ( 2014 movie ) the interview is a 2014 american political action comedy movie directed by seth rogen and evan goldberg . it stars james franco , seth rogen , lizzy caplan , and randall park . it was set to be released on december 25 , 2014 , but the movie was cancelled due to threats from north korea . the movie was released on demand and on youtube .
