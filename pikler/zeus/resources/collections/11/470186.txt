Luděk Bukač     luděk bukač , born 8 april 1935 in ústí nad labem , czechoslovakia is a czechoslovakian , later czech , ice hockey player and manager . he played for cltk praha and hc sparta praha . he coached hc sparta praha , hc kosice and hc ceske budejovice . he managed the czechoslovak , austrian , german and czech national teams . with the czech republic , he won the 1996 iihf world championship .
