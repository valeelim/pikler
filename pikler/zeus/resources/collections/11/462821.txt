Azerbaijani Chess Championship     the azerbaijani chess championship is usually held in baku , azerbaijan . it is organised by the azerbaijan chess federation ( acf ) . the first championship was played in 1934 , when azerbaijan was a part of the transcaucasian socialist federative soviet republic . championships were not held every year in the azerbaijan soviet socialist republic until 1945. since then they were held every year , which has continued today in independent azerbaijan . other websites . - official web site of azerbaijan chess federation ( acf ) - azerbaijan chess federation
