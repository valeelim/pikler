Mario &amp; Sonic at the Sochi 2014 Olympic Winter Games    mario & amp ; sonic at the sochi 2014 olympic winter games mario & sonic at the sochi 2014 olympic winter games is a sports game for the nintendo wii u. it is the fourth mario & sonic crossover game . it is the official video game of the 2014 olympic winter games , which took place in sochi . it is the first game in the series that was not released on a handheld console as well . the game got very average reviews . it got a 55/100 on metacritic . ign 's scott thompson said `` [ the game ] has some good ideas but it 's weighed down by bad motion controls '' . metro gave it a 3/10 , saying it was too similar to the other games in the series .
