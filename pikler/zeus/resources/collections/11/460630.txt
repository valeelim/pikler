Buster Posey     gerald dempsey `` buster '' posey iii ( born march 27 , 1987 ) is an american professional baseball catcher and first baseman for the san francisco giants of major league baseball . he stands and weighs . he bats and throws right-handed . posey grew up in leesburg , georgia . he played four sports in high school ; when playing baseball , he was very good at hitting and pitching . he attended florida state university , where he began playing the catcher position . he won the golden spikes award in 2008 and was selected by the giants with the fifth overall pick in the 2008 major league baseball draft . posey made his major league start on september 11 , 2009. after beginning the 2010 season in the minor leagues , he was called back up to the major leagues in may . he played first base when he came up , but became the giants ' regular catcher at the end of june . other websites . - florida state seminoles bio
