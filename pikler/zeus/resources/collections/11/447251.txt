Virgilio Barco Vargas     virgilio barco vargas ( 17 september 1921 – 20 may 1997 ) was a colombian politician , and civil engineer and member of colombian liberal party . he served as the 27th president of colombia from 1986 to 1990. vargas was born in cúcuta , north santander , colombia . vargas died in bogotá , d.c. , colombia from cancer , aged 75 .
