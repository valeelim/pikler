Naples National Archaeological Museum     the naples national archaeological museum ( `` museo archeologico nazionale di napoli '' , mann ) is a museum in naples . the museum has a large display of roman artifacts from pompeii , stabiae and herculaneum . the display has works made in greek , roman and renaissance times . it is known as the most important italian archaeological museum . charles iii of spain started the museum in the 1750s . the building was used to house soldiers in the 18th century . other websites . - http : //www.museoarcheologiconapoli.it/it/ official website - naples national archaeological museum
