Cecilia Church     the cecilia church ( ) a church building in vilhelmsro outside the town of jönköping in sweden . it was built between 1880-1881 as a church for the vilhelmsro nursing home . it is named after ebba ramsay 's youth friend cecilia willerding . the church was opened in 1903 by diocese of växjö bishop herman lindström . it later under supervision by the vicar of jönköping . it was renovated in 1963. it later came under the jönköping county council . it was then , was transferred to municipal public housing vätterhem . vätterhem would sporadical rent it out for worship services , including weddings . in 2014 it was purchased by the romanian orthodox church of sweden .
