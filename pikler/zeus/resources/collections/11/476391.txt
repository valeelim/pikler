Continental AG     continental ag is a leading german automotive manufacturing company specializing in tires , brake systems , automotive safety , powertrain and chassis components , tachographs , and other parts for the automotive and transportation industries . continental is based in hanover , lower saxony , germany . continental was founded in 1871 as a rubber manufacturer , `` continental-caoutchouc und gutta-percha compagnie '' . continental is the world 's 4th largest tire manufacturer .
