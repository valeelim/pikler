Feridun Buğeker     feridun ismail buğeker ( 5 april 1933 – 6 october 2014 ) was a turkish football forward who played for turkey in the 1954 fifa world cup . he also played for fenerbahçe s.k . between 1950–55 and 1961-63. he was born in istanbul , turkey . buğeker died in istanbul , turkey , aged 81 .
