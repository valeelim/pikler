Aliya Mustafina     aliya fargatovna mustafina ( ; born 30 september 1994 ) is a russian gymnast . she won 4 medals at the 2012 summer olympics . mustafina was the most successful athlete that was not a swimmer at the 2012 summer olympics . mustafina has won 11 medals at the world championships . she was the 2010 world champion in the all-around and in the team competition . she was the 2013 world champion on the balance beam .
