Anders Blomquist     anders blomquist , born 17 october 1960 in bromma , sweden is swedish former cross-country skier . he competed for ifk lidingö and åsarna ik at club levbel . in 1988 , he shared the vasaloppet victory together with his brother örjan blomquist . since many years , he has been an svt commentator during skiing events . up to march 2010 , he was the ceo for destination funäsdalen .
