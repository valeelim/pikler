Fakir Family     the fakir family or fakir khana family is an old family of lahore , now in pakistan . they are famous for having made the fakir khana museum in the old walled city of lahore . the family is of syed origins . they first became famous in the mughal times . later , two members fakir syed azizuddin and fakir syed nooruddin were ministers of the punjab government in the time of maharaja ranjit singh . they were also respected by the british rulers .
