Towa Carson     towa carson ( actual name birgit rose-marie anlert , née carlsson ) , born on 31 march 1936 in eskilstuna , sweden , is a swedish schlager singer . she debuted in 1954 , and scored most successes during the 1950s and 60s . she recorded many vocal duets with lasse lönndahl . she has also participated in swedish eurovision song contest ) preselection melodifestivalen .
