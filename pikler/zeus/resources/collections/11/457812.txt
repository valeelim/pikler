The Ministry of Darkness     the ministry of darkness was a heel professional wrestling stable in the world wrestling federation ( wwf ) . the team was led by the undertaker . the stable was controversial due to their pseudo-satanic themed storylines which included rituals and sacrifices . the stable lasted from january 1999 until april 29 , 1999. the ministry of darkness would eventually merge with the corporation and form the corporate ministry . during their time in the ministry of darkness , the undertaker was a one-time wwf champion , the acolytes were two-time wwf tag team champions and mideon was a one-time wwf european champion . members . - the undertaker ( leader ) - paul bearer ( manager ) - the acolytes ( bradshaw and faarooq ) - mideon - viscera - the brood ( gangrel , edge , and christian ) championships and accomplishments . - world wrestling federation - wwf championship ( 1 time ) ( undertaker ) - wwf tag team championship ( 2 times ) ( the acolytes ) - wwf european championship ( 1 time ) ( mideon ) other websites .
