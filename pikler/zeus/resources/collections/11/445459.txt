Manish Arora     manish arora ( hindi : मनीष अरोरा ) ( punjabi : ਮਨੀਸ਼ ਅਰੋੜਾ ) is an indian fashion designer . arora was born in mumbai . he now lives in new dehli . in 1997 arora started a fashion label called manish arora . in 2001 arora started his second fashion label fish fry . arora is a sikh .
