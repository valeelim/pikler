Tureberg     tureberg is a neighbourhood in sollentuna municipality , stockholm county , sweden . in 2011 , 15,560 people lived there . it is a stockholm suburb and houses the local government seat for the municipality as well as a large shopping mall and a stockholm commuter rail station . tureberg has also evolved as a law enforcement center for the northern part of metropolitan stockholm , with police station , court house , prosecutor 's office , and a jail , all in buildings built in the 1980s and onwards . the present courthouse was inaugurated in 2010 .
