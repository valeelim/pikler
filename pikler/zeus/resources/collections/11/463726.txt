Asma Jahangir     asma jilani jahangir ( born 27 january 1952 in lahore ) is a leading pakistani lawyer . she is an advocate of the supreme court of pakistan , president supreme court bar association of pakistan and a human rights activist who works both in pakistan and internationally to prevent the persecution of religious minorities , women , and exploitation of children . she has won several international awards for her activism including the four freedoms award in 2010 for promoting religious freedom .
