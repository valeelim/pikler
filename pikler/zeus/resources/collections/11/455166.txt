Michael Kelly     michael joseph kelly ( born may 22 , 1969 ) is an american actor . he is best known for his roles in movies such as `` changeling '' , `` dawn of the dead '' , `` law abiding citizen '' , `` the adjustment bureau '' , and `` chronicle '' , `` now you see me '' . he also appeared in the television miniseries `` generation kill '' and the `` criminal minds '' spin-off series `` '' . he currently plays doug stamper in the american adaptation of `` house of cards '' .
