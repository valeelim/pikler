Teruel Cathedral     the cathedral of teruel is a church in teruel , aragon , spain . it belongs to st. mary . it is a notable example of mudéjar architecture . since 1986 , it has been part of the unesco heritage site mudéjar architecture of aragon . the bell tower is one of the best preserved mudéjar towers in spain . it is square . it has three floors . it is decorated with azulejos ( painted tiles ) and ceramic glaze . at the top is an octagonal lantern from the 18th century . other websites . - description and images of the cathedral - page with virtual visit and photos
