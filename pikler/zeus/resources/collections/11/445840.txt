Kadaif noodles     kadaif noodles are a type of thin noodles . they are made ​​of flour and water that is poured through a sieve onto a hot metal cooking tray . the noodles are very short . they are used to make middle eastern desserts and other foods around the mediterranean sea . it is a popular turkish dessert like baklava . other websites . - kadayif , sweet noodles in the turkish cooking everyday blog - kadaif noodles in the martha stewart website
