Speed of Sound (song)    speed of sound ( song ) `` speed of sound '' is the first single off british alternative rock band coldplay 's third studio album `` x & y '' . the song was released on 23 may 2005. the song is build around a piano riff which later builds a synthesiser-heavy chorus . it was named song of the year by the american society of composers , authors and publishers ( ascap ) . the song was nominated for best rock song and best rock performance by a duo or group with vocals at the 2006 grammy awards . other websites . - `` speed of sound '' on youtube
