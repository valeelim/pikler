Pat McCrory     patrick lloyd `` pat '' mccrory ( born october 17 , 1956 ) is an american politician . he was the 74th governor of north carolina from 2013 to 2017. he served a record 14 years as the 53rd mayor of charlotte from 1995 to 2009 , and as a city councilman from 1989 to 1995. in 2016 , mccroy lost his re-election bid to democrat roy cooper .
