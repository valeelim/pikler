Anarkali (girl)    anarkali ( girl ) anarkali ( meaning pomegranate blossom ) was the name of a legendary dancing girl . she is said to have lived in lahore ( now in pakistan ) in the time of the mughal emperor akbar . she fell in love with prince selim ( who later became the emperor jahangir ) but was punished by his father , akbar . she is supposed to have died in lahore and it is said her grave is also there . the famous anarkali bazaar in lahore was named after her . in literature and film . - the famous urdu play `` anarkali '' ( 1922 ) by writer imtiaz ali taj is about the love story of anarkali and selim . - a number of movies or films have been made about her , in both bollywood ( in india ) and lollywood ( in pakistan ) . the most famous is probably `` mughal-i-azam '' ( 1960 ) .
