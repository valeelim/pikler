Antanas Šurna    antanas šurna antanas šurna ( 27 march 1939 – 19 may 2014 ) was a lithuanian stage and movie actor . he has appeared in over 100 movies and 50 plays . he is thought to be one of the most successful actors in lithuania . his career began during the late 1960s . he won many awards during his 40-year career . šurna died suddenly from cardiac arrest in vilnius , lithuania , aged 75 .
