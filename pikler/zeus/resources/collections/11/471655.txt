Florinda Meza     florinda meza garcía ( born 8 february 1949 ) is a mexican actress , television producer , and screenwriter . she is best known as doña florinda in `` el chavo del ocho '' , la chimoltrufia in `` chespirito '' , and other various roles in `` el chapulín colorado '' . meza was married to roberto gómez bolaños from 2004 until his death in 2014 .
