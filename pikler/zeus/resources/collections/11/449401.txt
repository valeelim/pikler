A Million Ways to Die in the West     a million ways to die in the west is an american western-comedy movie . it is about arizona in 1882 , when a sheep herder loses his girlfriend , then gets another woman . seth macfarlane plays albert , liam neeson plays clinch , charlize theron plays anna , sarah silverman plays ruth , and neil patrick harris plays foy . christopher lloyd , ewan mcgregor , gilbert gottfried , and bill maher have cameos in the movie . the movie has an r rating from the mpaa . it was released on may 30 , 2014 .
