Ruokolahti     ruokolahti ( ) is a municipality in south karelia , finland . it had almost 5,500 people living there in january 2014. the municipalities next to it are imatra , lappeenranta , parikkala , puumala , rautjärvi , savonlinna , sulkava and taipalsaari . nearby is the kummakivi rock . it is a large rock balanced on top of another . distances . - imatra – 17 km - lappeenranta – 53 km - savonlinna – 104 km - mikkeli – 119 km - kouvola – 141 km - helsinki – 274 km other websites . - the official ruokolahti page
