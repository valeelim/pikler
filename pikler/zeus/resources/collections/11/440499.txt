Ghostbusters     ghostbusters is a american supernatural comedy movie directed by ivan reitman . it is about people that fight and trap ghosts in new york city . it was written by dan aykroyd and harold ramis . it stars bill murray , dan aykroyd , harold ramis , sigourney weaver , annie potts , william atherton , ernie hudson , and rick moranis . the music was composed by elmer bernstein . it was released on june 8 , 1984. it was followed by `` ghostbusters ii '' .
