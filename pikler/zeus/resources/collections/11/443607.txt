Seasonal affective disorder     seasonal affective disorder ( sad ) is a when you feel sad at a certain time of year , most of the time in winter . people who suffer from this usually feel better in spring . doctors often cure sad with light therapy . sometimes a light comes on when you are asleep in the morning and gets brighter like a sunrise . sometimes a person sits in front of a light box for half an hour or longer in the morning . some symptoms are : - feeling sad , grumpy anxious or moody - losing interest in your usual activities - eating more carbohydrates ( eg : bread , pasta etc ) - gaining weight - sleeping more and feeling sleepy during the day
