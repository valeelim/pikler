André Lavagne     andré lavagne ( 12 july 1913 – 21 march 2014 ) was a french composer . he mostly worked on short movies , such as : `` l'amour maternel chez les animaux `` ( 1944 ) and `` un amour de parapluie '' ( 1951 ) . he was born in paris . lavagne died from natural causes on 21 march 2014 in paris . he was 100 years old .
