Night of Champions (2012)    night of champions ( 2012 ) night of champions was a professional wrestling pay-per-view show made by wwe . it was held on september 16 , 2012 at td garden in boston , massachusetts . it was the fifth wwe night of champions event held by the wwe . the main event was a match between wwe champion cm punk and john cena . the end of match saw cena place punk on the top rope and hit a bridging german suplex for the pin . the referee declared that both men had their shoulders on the mat for three seconds and ended the match in a draw . other websites . - official night of champions 2012 website
