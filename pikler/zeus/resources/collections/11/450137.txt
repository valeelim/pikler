ECW CyberSlam     ecw cyberslam was a professional wrestling event and pay-per-view that was produced by extreme championship wrestling ( ecw ) . it ran from 1996 to 2000. cyberslam 1996 took place on february 17 , 1996 at the ecw arena in philadelphia , pennsylvania . it was the first cyberslam event that ecw produced . cyberslam 1997 were two events that took place on february 21 , 1997 and on february 22 , 1997 at both the lost battalion hall in queens , new york and the ecw arena in philadelphia , pennsylvania . it was the second cyberslam event that ecw produced . the event featured two events which were held at february 21 and february 22. the first event happened on february 21 , 1997 in lost battalion hall in queens , new york the second event happened on february 22 , 1997 in ecw arena in philadelphia , pennsylvania . cyberslam 1998 took place on february 21 , 1998 at the ecw arena in philadelphia , pennsylvania . it was the third cyberslam event that ecw produced . the event started with a ten-bell salute to honor louie spicolli who had died six days earlier from an accidental drug overdose .
