Ben Scrivens     ben scrivens ( born september 11 , 1986 in spruce grove , alberta ) is a canadian professional goaltender for the edmonton oilers . scrivens was undrafted but signed with the toronto maple leafs in 2010. on january 29 , 2014 , scrivens stopped all 59 shots that the oilers defence allowed and set a new nhl record for most saves in a shutout . he is also referred to as scrvezina .
