Cryovolcano     a cyrovolcano ( also called an `` ice volcano '' ) is a type of volcano . it erupts volatiles such as water , ammonia , or methane , instead of molten rock . cryovolcanoes are found on icy moons and might be on other places with low temperatures , like kuiper belt objects . cryovolcanoes have been found on the moons triton and enceladus .
