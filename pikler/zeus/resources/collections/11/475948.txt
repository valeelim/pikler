Swiss Chalet     swiss chalet is a canadian chain of casual dining restaurants . swiss chalet was started in 1954 in toronto , ontario . the restaurant chain is owned by cara operations . swiss chalet 's signature food is rotisserie chicken . their signature dish is the quarter chicken dinner , either a roasted chicken leg or breast with `` chalet sauce '' , a bread roll , and a side dish of the customers choice . other combinations up to and including a whole chicken are also available . the first swiss chalet restaurant was opened at 234 bloor street west in toronto and it operated for 52 years . this restaurant was closed in 2006 and it was demolished in 2009 to make way for condominiums . in 2008 , swiss chalet food products were added to air canada to be on their buy-on-board menu . other websites . - official website
