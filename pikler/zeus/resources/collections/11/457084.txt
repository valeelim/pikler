Battle of Monocacy     the battle of monocacy was one of the most important battles of the american civil war . it was fought near frederick , maryland on july 9 , 1864. a confederate army commanded by jubal early was marching eastward towards washington , d.c. , to capture the city . a small union army commanded by lew wallace fought the confederates along the monocacy river . the battle took most of the day to fight . the confederates finally made wallace 's force retreat about 4:30 p.m. however , the delay kept the confederates from reaching washington until general grant 's soldiers reached the city . the battle of monocacy is sometimes called `` the battle that saved washington '' . other websites . - historians mark the 150th anniversary of the battle of monocacy - the battle of monocacy ; early 's maryland campaign
