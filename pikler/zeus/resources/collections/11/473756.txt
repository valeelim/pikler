Samsung Galaxy S5     samsung galaxy s5 , galaxy s5 or just gs5 is an android smartphone made by samsung electronics . the phone was released on 11 april 2014 in 150 countries as the replacement to the galaxy s4 . like the s4 , the s5 is similar to the prior year 's model , placing a large focus on a better build with a textured rear cover and ip67 rating for dust and water proof , a better user feel , new security items such as a fingerprint scanner and private mode , expanded health items ( such as a built-in heart rate scanner ) , and an updated camera . other pages . - samsung galaxy
