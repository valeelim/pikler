Oswald Morris     oswald norman morris obe , dfc , afc , bsc ( 22 november 191517 march 2014 ) was a british academy award-winning cinematographer . he was known for his works in `` fiddler on the roof '' and in `` goodbye , mr. chips '' . morris died at his home in dorset , england , at the age of 98. other websites . - oswald morris at the internet movie database
