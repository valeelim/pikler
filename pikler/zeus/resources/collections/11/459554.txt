340 on the Park     340 on the park is a residential tower in the lakeshore east development of the neighborhood of new eastside/ east loop chicago . it was completed in 2007. the building was surpassed by 55 east erie as the tallest all-residential building in chicago . it is currently the second tallest all-residential building in chicago ( one museum park being the current tallest ) at 672 feet ( 205 meters ) with 62 floors . other websites . - official 340 on the park website - solomon cordwell buenz website
