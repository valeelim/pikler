Pooja Sharma     pooja sharma is a model-turned actress . she is in the mythological serial `` mahabharat '' on star plus . sharma was born in delhi . she moved to mumbai to pursue her career in acting . she plays the role of draupadi in mythological venture mahabharat . actresses including yuvika chaudhary and anupriya kapoor were also approached for the role but it went to sharma . sharma came from a delhi-based educated family . she said `` i am here because of my family . they have always supported me when it comes to acting and seeing me on screen . my brother is very critical about my work and on the other hand my mom and sister-in-law love my work . the journey in mahabharat is very beautiful . i have actually fallen in love with draupadi 's and my work . '' sharma started her career with modeling . she has also done anchoring , hosting and daily ads . after that she got selected for the role of draupadi .
