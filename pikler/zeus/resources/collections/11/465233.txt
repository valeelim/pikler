Rolling coal     rolling coal is an activity in the u.s.a. where people who drive diesel trucks or cars intentionally remove the pollution controls . this is so the vehicle will produce black , sooty exhaust . participants often `` roll coal '' on pedestrians , bicyclists and drivers of hybrid vehicles . pollution controls such as exhaust filters are removed . additionally , the vehicle may be modified to deliver more fuel to the engine than usual . the turbocharger may also be disabled .
