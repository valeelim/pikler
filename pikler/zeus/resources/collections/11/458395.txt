Péter Kiss     péter kiss ( 11 june 1959 – 29 july 2014 ) was a hungarian socialist politician . he was one of the candidates to succeed péter medgyessy as prime minister in 2004 but lost to ferenc gyurcsány . he served as minister of social affairs and labour of hungary in june 2006 until june 2007. kiss died on 29 july 2014 due to prolonged illness .
