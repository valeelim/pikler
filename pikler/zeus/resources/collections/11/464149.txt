Jonathan Coulton     jonathan coulton is an american alternative rock singer-songwriter . he is known for his songs about geek culture . his album `` artificial heart '' hit # 1 on the billboard top heatseeker chart . discography . - studio albums - `` smoking monkey '' ( 2003 ) - `` where tradition meets tomorrow '' ( 2004 ) - `` our bodies , ourselves , our cybernetic arms '' ( 2005 ) - `` thing a week one '' ( 2006 ) - `` thing a week two '' ( 2006 ) - `` thing a week three '' ( 2006 ) - `` thing a week four '' ( 2006 ) - `` artificial heart '' ( 2011 )
