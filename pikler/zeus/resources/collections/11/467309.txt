Hugo Carvana     hugo carvana ( 4 june 1937 – 4 october 2014 ) was a brazilian movie and television actor and director . he appeared in 65 movie since 1955. he was known for his roles in `` entranced earth '' and in `` antonio das mortes '' . he was also known for directing `` casa da mãe joana '' . carvana died of an illness in rio de janeiro , brazil , aged 77 .
