330 North Wabash     330 north wabash ( formerly ibm plaza also known as ibm building and now renamed ama plaza ) is a skyscraper in downtown chicago , illinois , united states . it is at 330 n. wabash avenue . it was designed by famed architect ludwig mies van der rohe ( who died in 1969 before construction began ) . the 52-story building is overlooking the chicago river . it stands 695 feet ( 211,8 meters ) . other websites . - 330 n wabash building website - the langhamchicago website - emporis.com page
