Estrella Morente     estrella de la aurora morente carbonell , ( born august 14 , 1980 in las gabias , granada , andalusia ) is a spanish flamenco singer . she is the daughter of flamenco singer enrique morente and dancer aurora carbonell . she has performed with her father since age seven . she released her first album in 2001 , `` mi cante y un poema '' ( my songs and a poem ) . this was followed the same year by `` calle del aire '' . it was well received by critics and flamenco fans . she released her third album , `` mujeres '' ( women ) in 2006 , which her father produced . on december 14 , 2001 she married bullfighter javier conde in nuestra señora de las angustias basilica in granada . they have two children , curro ( 2002 ) and estrella ( 2005 ) . discography . - `` mi cante y un poema '' ( 2001 ) - `` calle del aire '' ( 2001 ) - `` mujeres '' ( 2006 ) , mute records - `` casacueva y escenario '' ( 2007 ) ( dvd ) - `` autorretrato '' ( 2012 ) - `` amar en paz ( con la guitarra de niño josele ) '' ( 2014 ) other websites . - official site
