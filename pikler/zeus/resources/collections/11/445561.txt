Jovan Pavlović     metropolitan jovan pavlović ( , ; 22 october 1936 – 3 april 2014 ) was a croatian religious leader . he was the metropolitan of metropolitanate of zagreb and ljubljana of serbian orthodox church . he was born in medinci , kingdom of yugoslavia . pavlović died on 3 april 2014 at the sisters of charity hospital in zagreb , croatia . he was 78 years old .
