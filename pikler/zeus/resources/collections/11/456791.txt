Eddie Calvo     eddie baza calvo ( born august 29 , 1961 ) is an american guamanian politician . he is the governor of the united states territory of guam since 2011. he is a member of the republican party . his father , paul mcdonald calvo , also served as governor of guam from 1979 through 1983. calvo was a five-term senator within the legislature of guam . he became the governor of guam , having defeated democrat carl gutierrez in the 2010 gubernatorial election .
