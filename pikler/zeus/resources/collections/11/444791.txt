Victoria Coren Mitchell     victoria coren mitchell ( born 18 august 1972 ) is a british journalist , television presenter and poker player . she was born in hammersmith , london and raised in cricklewood , london . she gained a first-class degree in english from st john 's college , oxford . she is the daughter of alan coren and sister of giles coren . she presented `` balderdash and piffle '' . she presents `` only connect '' . she married david mitchell on 17 november 2012. the couple 's daughter was born in may 2015. other websites . - pokerstars.com
