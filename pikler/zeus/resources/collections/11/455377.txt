Juan Almeida Bosque     juan almeida bosque ( february 17 , 1927 – september 11 , 2009 ) was a cuban politician and one of the original commanders of the cuban revolution . after the 1959 revolution , he was a famous figure in the communist party of cuba . at the time of his death in 2009 , he was a vice-president of the cuban council of state and was its third ranking member . he received several decorations , and both national and international awards , including the title of `` hero of the republic of cuba '' and the order of máximo gómez . on september 11 , 2009 , juan almeida died of a heart attack , aged 82 .
