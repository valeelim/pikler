Brita Koivunen     brita koivunen-einiö ( 31 august 1931 – 12 april 2014 ) was a finnish jazz and schlager singer . some of her most famous songs include `` suklaasydän '' , `` sävel rakkauden '' and `` mamma , tuo mies minua tuijottaa '' all from the 1950s . she was born in helsinki . koivunen died on 12 april 2014 in helsinki , aged 82. other websites . - brita koivunen biography
