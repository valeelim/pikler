West Side Story (movie)    west side story ( movie ) west side story is a 1961 american romantic crime drama musical . it is an adaptation of the 1957 broadway musical play of the same name . natalie wood plays maria . rita moreno plays anita . robert wise directed the movie . it is based on the play romeo and juliet by william shakespeare . this movie won academy awards for best picture , best sound and best director . it also won many other awards .
