Death Before Dishonour     death before dishonour is the fifth studio album by scottish punk rock band , the exploited . `` death before dishonour '' was released on april 15 , 1987. the album was re-released on june 19 , 2001. the single `` sexual favours '' had a music video released for it . the album was ranked in the top 200 of the britain alternative music list . the re-release of the album featured seven more tracks . songs . - re-release bonus tracks
