Governor-General of New Zealand     the governor-general of new zealand is the representative of the monarch of new zealand ( currently queen elizabeth ii ) . the governor-general is appointed by the queen with the advice of the prime minister of new zealand . usually , a person serves as prime governor-general for five years , although there is no and the person can serve longer . the current governor-general is dame patsy reddy , who was appointed in 2016. related pages . - list of governors-general of new zealand
