Purine     purines include two of the bases in dna and rna . a purine is a heterocyclic aromatic organic compound . it is a pyrimidine ring fused to an imidazole ring . purines are the most widely occurring nitrogen-containing heterocycles in nature . purines and pyrimidines are the two groups of nitrogenous bases , and the two groups of nucleotide bases . purines . - adenine - guanine - hypoxanthine - xanthine
