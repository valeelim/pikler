Katharine Isabelle     katherine isobel murray ( also katharine isabelle ; born november 2 , 1981 ) is a canadian actress . she played chloe in the movie `` cousins '' ( 1989 ) . she also played lindsay clark in the movie `` disturbing behavior '' ( 1998 ) . she played julia baker in the movie `` countdown '' ( 2016 ) . isabelle was born in vancouver , british columbia .
