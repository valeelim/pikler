Jean-Claude Baker     jean-claude baker ( born jean-claude julien léon tronville , april 18 , 1943 – january 15 , 2015 ) was a french-american restaurateur . he was born in dijon , france . he met u.s.-born french entertainer josephine baker while he was working as a bellhop in paris at the age of fourteen in 1958. he became the last of twelve children adopted into baker 's rainbow family . in 1994 he co-authored a biography of josephine baker , `` josephine : the hungry heart '' , described as a `` shocking look into the star 's seriously whitewashed past '' . baker was found dead at his home in east hampton , new york on january 15 , 2015 , of an apparent suicide . he was 71 .
