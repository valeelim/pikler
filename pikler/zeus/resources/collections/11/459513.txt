Julian Berrendero     julián berrendero martín ( born san agustín del guadalix , 8 april 1912 , died madrid , 1 august 1995 ) was a spanish cyclist . he is famous for winning the vuelta a españa in 1941 and 1942. in 1936 he won the polka dot jersey at the tour de france for the best climber in the race . he also won two mountains jerseys at the vuelta a españa . major results . - 1935 - 1936 - 1937 - 1938 - 1941 - 1942 - 1943 - 1944 - 1945 - 1946 - 1947 - 1948 other websites . - official tour de france results for julián berrendero
