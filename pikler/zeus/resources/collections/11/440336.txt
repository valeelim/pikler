Khamtai Siphandon     general khamtai siphandon ( ; born 8 february 1924 ) is a laotian politician . he was president of laos from 24 february 1998 , until 8 june 2006 , when he was officially replaced by choummaly sayasone . he also served as prime minister of laos from 15 august 1991 to 24 february 1998 .
