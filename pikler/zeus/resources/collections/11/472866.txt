Hwayang-dong     hwayang-dong is a `` dong '' in west gwangjin-gu in seoul , south korea . it was joined into gwangjin-gu on 13 august 1949. it is a mixed home and small business area . mojin-dong is administered by hwayang-dong . it has konkuk university . other websites . - gwangjin-gu official website in english - chronicle of beopjeong-dong and haengjeong-dong - map of gwangjin-gu at the gwangjin-gu official website - hwayang-dong resident office website
