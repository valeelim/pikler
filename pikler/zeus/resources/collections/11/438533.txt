Herbert Blöcker     herbert blöcker ( 1 january 1943 – 15 february 2014 ) was a german equestrian and olympic medalist . he won a silver medal in eventing at the 1992 summer olympics in barcelona . he was born in schleswig-holstein . in 2008 , both he and his olympic horse , `` feine dame '' , were inducted into the international association of eventing hall of fame . he died of cancer in 2014 at age 71 at elmshorn .
