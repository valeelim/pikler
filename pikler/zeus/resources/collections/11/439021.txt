Fossil-fuel power station     a fossil-fuel power station is a power station that makes electricity by burning certain fuels . its machines change the heat energy that comes from burning the fuels into mechanical energy . the mechanical energy is then used to work an electrical generator . the generator makes the electricity . the fuels used in these kind of power stations are fossil fuels , such as coal and natural gas . there are environmental impacts from the burning of fossil fuels . types . - coal-fired power station - gas-fired power station
