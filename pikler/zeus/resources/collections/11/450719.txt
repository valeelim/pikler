Division of Kennedy     the division of kennedy is an australian electoral division in queensland . it was one of the 75 divisions set up for the first federal election in 1901. the division is named after edmund kennedy , an explorer in the area where the division is located in queensland . the division covers a large area of outback queensland . it includes the towns of atherton , babinda , camooweal , charters towers , cloncurry , georgetown , gordonvale , hughenden , ingham , innisfail , mareeba , mount isa , normanton and tully . since 1966 the division has been held by the conservative katter family—bob sr. and his son , bob jr.—for all but one term . in the 2013 election there was a 17 % swing against katter . election results . - 2004 election results - 2007 election results - 2010 election results - 2013 election results other websites . - division of kennedy ( qld ) — australian electoral commission
