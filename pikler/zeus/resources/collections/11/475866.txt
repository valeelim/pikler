Kulla Chapel     kulla chapel ( ) is a chapel in eksjö municipality in sweden . it is located down country road 28 eksjö and nässjö . it belongs to the höreda parish of the church of sweden . the chapel was opened in 1927. the chapel was destroyed in a fire on 27 may 2000. the fire was connected to satanism however the chapel was rebuilt and reopened on 13 may 2001. related pages . - bäckaby old church
