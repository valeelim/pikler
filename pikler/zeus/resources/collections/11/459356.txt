2 Become 1     `` 2 become 1 '' is a pop ballad by the girl group spice girls . the song lyrics talk about the bonding of two lovers . they also talk about the importance of contraception . this song was released in december 1996 in the united kingdom . it became their third consecutive chart-topper there . in the united states , the song was released in july 1997 . `` 2 become 1 '' peaked at # 4 on `` billboard '' hot 100 .
