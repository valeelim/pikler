Avicii     avicii is the stage name of tim bergling ( born 8 september 1989 ) . he is a swedish edm dj , remixer , and record producer . avicii ranked 3rd on dj magazines annual top 100 djs in 2012 and 2013. he is ranked 6th for 2014. avicii has been nominated twice for a grammy award . once for his work on `` sunshine '' with david guetta in 2012. the second time was for his song `` levels '' in 2013. some of his best known songs are `` i could be the one '' with nicky romero , `` wake me up '' , `` you make me '' , `` hey brother '' and `` addicted to you '' .
