Erzsi Kovács     erzsi kovács drh ( 2 june 1928 – 6 april 2014 ) was a hungarian pop singer and performer . she made her last album , `` mosolyogva búcsúzom '' ( english title : `` i leave with a smile '' ) when she was 79 years old . she was awarded the highest honor in hungary , the order of merit of the republic of hungary . she died after long illness on 6 april 2014 .
