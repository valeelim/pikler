She's Funny That Way    she 's funny that way she 's funny that way ( originally squirrels to the nuts ) is a 2015 american comedy-drama movie . the movie is about a prostitute-turned-broadway actress . imogen poots plays isabella `` izzy '' beatty . owen wilson plays arnold albertson . austin pendleton plays judge pendergast . jennifer aniston plays jane claremont . it was screened at the venice film festival , aug. 29 , 2014. in a deal valued at $ 4.5 million , it was picked up by u.s. distributor clarius entertainment . the date of release is spring 2015 .
