Assist (football)    assist ( football ) in association football , an assist is a pass or a cross by a player which helps to score a goal . most commonly , an assist is given to a player for passing or crossing the ball to the scorer . it may also be awarded to a player whose shot rebounds ( off a defender , goalkeeper or goalpost ) to a teammate who scores . some systems may give an assist to a player who wins a penalty kick or a free kick for another player to score . an attacking player for contributing to an own goal can also get an assist . a goal may be unassisted , or have one assist . in some systems , even two assists is possible .
