Karlheinz Böhm     karlheinz böhm ( 16 march 1928 – 29 may 2014 ) , sometimes known as carl boehm or karl boehm , was an austrian-german actor and philanthropist . boehm was born in darmstadt , germany to austrian composer karl boehm and german soprano thea linhard . his career has spanned for nearly 50 years . he appeared in 45 foreign movies and over 30 blockbusters . he is best known for his role as mark in `` peeping tom '' . boehm had seven children , including actress katharina boehm . böhm died from alzheimer 's disease at his home in grödig , austria , aged 86 .
