McClellanville, South Carolina    mcclellanville , south carolina mcclellanville is a fishing and port town in eastern south carolina . at the 2000 census , 459 people lived there . the history of this town began in the 1860s . it was incorporated in 1926. in september 1989 , mcclellanville was hit by the northern wall of hurricane hugo . during this hurricane , a storm surge nearly drowned refugees taking cover in a high school .
