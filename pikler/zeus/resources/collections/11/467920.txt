Will Jennings     wilbur h. `` will '' jennings ( born june 27 , 1944 in kilgore , texas , united states ) is an american songwriter . he is known for writing the lyrics for `` my heart will go on '' . the recording was used as the theme for movie `` titanic '' ( 1997 ) . he has been included into the songwriter 's hall of fame . he also won several awards including three grammy awards , two golden globe awards , and two academy awards . jennings has received awards for writing lyrics to the following songs : - `` my heart will go on '' from the movie `` titanic '' - `` tears in heaven '' from the movie `` rush '' - `` up where we belong '' from the movie `` an officer and a gentleman ''
