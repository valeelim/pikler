Mahdi Elmandjra     mahdi elmandjra ( arabic : مهدي المنجرة ) ( 13 march 1933 – 13 june 2014 ) was a moroccan futurist , economist , writer and sociologist . he was best known for his leadership roles with the united nations educational , scientific and cultural organization ( unesco ) . elmandjra was born in rabat . he was educated in morocco , the united states and the united kingdom . elmandjra died after a long illness on 13 june 2014 in rabat , aged 81 .
