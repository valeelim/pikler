Arc of the United States     the arc of the united states is a network of non-profit organizations which serves people with intellectual and developmental disabilities . it was created in the 1950s by parents of people with disabilities . since then , the organization has extended to almost 40 states . the arc was called the national association of retarded children ( 1953-1973 ) , national association of retarded citizens ( 1973-1981 ) and later the association of retarded citizens of the united states ( 1981-1992 ) . since 1992 , it has been called just the arc .
