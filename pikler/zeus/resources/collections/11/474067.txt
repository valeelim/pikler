1982 State of the Union Address     the 1982 state of the union address was given by president ronald reagan to a joint session of the 97th united states congress . it was held on january 26 , 1982. the speech was the first state of the union address of president reagan 's first term . the speech lasted 40 minutes and 14 seconds . it had 5154 words . the address was broadcast live on radio and television . the democratic party response was delivered by senator donald riegle ( mi ) , senator james sasser ( tn ) , rep. albert gore jr.
