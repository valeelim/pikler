Blöndulón     blöndulón is one of iceland 's largest lakes . it was created in between the years of 1984 and 1991. it has a greatest depth of and is nearly above sea level . it is found near the kjölur highland road in the highlands of iceland . it was made as a reservoir for the blönduvirkjun power plant . the lake is often used for salmon fishing . blöndulón has been the sight of erosion .
