Billy Frank, Jr.    billy frank , jr. billy frank , jr. ( march 9 , 1931 – may 5 , 2014 ) was a native american environmental leader and treaty rights activist . frank is known for his grassroots campaign for fishing rights on the tribe ’ s nisqually river in washington . he was mostly active during the 1960s and 1970s . he helped keep natural resources in the state . he was born in nisqually , washington . frank died on may 5 , 2014 , aged 83 .
