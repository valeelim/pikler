Western Block Party     the western block party ( or wbp ) is a conservative political party in canada . it wants western canada to become independent from the rest of canada . it was founded in 2005. it first ran candidates in the 2006 federal elections . the party 's current leader is paul st. laurent . the wbp is not affiliated with some other independent parties in western canada , such as the alberta first party and the western independence party of saskatchewan . related pages . - list of political parties in canada
