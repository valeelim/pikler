Carl Bertil Agnestig     carl bertil agnestig , originally `` andersson '' , born 7 march 1924 in järvsö parish , sweden , is a swedish music teacher and composer . he is known for music-instruction books and has written several songs , one of the more well-known is `` adventstid '' . he 's represented in den svenska psalmboken 1986 with two works , ( nr 214 b and 443 ) .
