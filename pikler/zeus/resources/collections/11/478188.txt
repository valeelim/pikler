SummerSlam (1998)    summerslam ( 1998 ) summerslam ( 1998 ) was a professional wrestling pay-per-view show made by world wrestling federation ( wwf ) . it was held on august 30 , 1998 at madison square garden in new york , new york . it was the eleventh summerslam event held by the wwf . the main event saw steve austin defeat the undertaker and retain his wwf championship . austin won the match by pinfall after he hit undertaker with the stunner . on the undercard , the main match was a ladder match between triple h and the rock for the wwf intercontinental championship . triple h won the match by climbing a ladder and grabbing the title that was hanging above the ring . other websites . - official summerslam 1998 website
