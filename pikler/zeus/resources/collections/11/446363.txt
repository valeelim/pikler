Brett Borgen     brett borgen ( 7 november 1934 – 11 march 2014 ) was a norwegian novelist and poet . her best known works were `` fra en ikke helt vellykket skuespillerinnes dagbok '' ( 1972 ) , `` dualoger med vår herre and isabella , sorgen og don juan '' ( 1977 ) , `` en time med jesus '' ( 1978 ) , `` den fortapte datter '' ( 1979 ) , `` heltinnen '' ( 1981 ) . she also wrote the memoir `` lillelord og lady brett '' ( 1985 ) . she was born in oslo . borgen died on 11 march 2014 in her hometown of oslo . she was 79 years old .
