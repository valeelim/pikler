Dick Wilson     dick wilson , born riccardo di guglielmo ( 30 july 1916 – 19 november 2007 ) , was a british-canadian character actor . he was known for playing grocery store manager mr. ( george ) whipple in over 500 charmin toilet paper television commercials ( 1965–1989 , 1999-2000 ) . wilson was raised in hamilton , ontario . he died in woodland hills , los angeles , california , aged 91. other websites . - an interview with dick wilson - obituary in the inland valley daily bulletin
