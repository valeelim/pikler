Kristof Van Hout     kristof van hout ( born 9 february 1987 in lommel ) , belgium ) is a belgian footballer , who currently plays as a goalkeeper for genk . van hout played for k.f.c . verbroedering geel as a youth . he joined willem ii in 2004. on 6 august 2009 , standard liege signed the goalkeeper from k.v . kortrijk on a three-year contract . on 11 june 2011 , he returned to kortrijk on a one-year contract . kristof is the joint tallest footballer in the professional game along with elgin city fc paul millar . both are 6 ' 10 '' tall .
