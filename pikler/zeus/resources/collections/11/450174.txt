Powersite Dam     powersite dam is a hydroelectric dam that went into service in 1913 in forsyth , missouri . it is found along the white river , and the reservoir it forms is lake taneycomo . it is the first hydroelectric dam built in missouri . it was designed in 1911 by nils f. ambursen . powersite was the largest concrete buttress dam of its kind . the dam is still privately owned by the empire district electric company . in july of 2015 the dam was damaged due to heavy flooding . the water levels in lake taneycomo had to be lowered to allow for inspection and repairs .
