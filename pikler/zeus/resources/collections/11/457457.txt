Christina Lake (British Columbia)    christina lake ( british columbia ) christina lake is a lake in british columbia in canada . it is near the crowsnest highway . it is about east of grand forks , north of the united states border . the lake is named after a daughter of a hudson 's bay company officer . the trans canada trail , dewdney trail , and the kettle valley railway trail all merge near the lake . the gladstone provincial park covers the northern half of the lake . the christina lake provincial park is at the southern part of the lake . both parks have public beaches along the lake .
