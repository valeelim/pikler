Ed Nimmervoll     edward charles `` ed '' nimmervoll ( 21 september 1947 – 10 october 2014 ) , was a austrian-born australian rock music journalist , author and historian . he worked with rock magazines `` go-set '' ( 1966–1974 ) and `` juke magazine '' ( 1975–92 ) both as a journalist and as an editor . nimmervoll died of brain cancer on 10 october 2014 , at the age of 67 .
