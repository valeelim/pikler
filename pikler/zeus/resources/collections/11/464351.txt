Adephaga     the adephaga is a suborder of beetles . there are more than 40,000 recorded species in ten families . this is the second largest suborder of the order coleoptera . the beetles of this suborder include ground beetles , tiger beetles , diving beetles , and whirligig beetles . most of the species belong to the family of carabids , or ground beetles ( carabidae ) . the adephaga is two groups with a common origin . they are the hydradephaga ( water beetles ) and the geadephaga ( ground beetles . they are mostly predatory . the first fossils of both groups are found in the triassic period . the ground beetles have formidable chemical defences . they have special pygidial glands which squirt out hot , burning chemicals . bombardier beetles are famous for squirting their nasty chemicals at attackers .
