Shirin Ebadi     shirin ebadi ( `` širin ebādi '' ; born 21 june 1947 ) is an iranian lawyer , a former judge and human rights activist . she is the founder of defenders of human rights center in iran . on 10 october 2003 , ebadi was awarded the nobel peace prize for her `` significant and pioneering efforts for democracy and human rights , especially women 's , children 's , and refugee rights '' . she was the first ever iranian to receive the prize . other websites . - shirin ebadi at the nobel prize website - shirin ebadi 's autobiography on the nobel prize website - shirin ebadi 's nobel lecture - nobel women 's initiative
