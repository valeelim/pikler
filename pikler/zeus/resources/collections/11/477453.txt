Don Harron     donald hugh ( don ) harron , ( september 19 , 1924 – january 17 , 2015 ) was a canadian comedian , actor , television director , journalist , author , playwright and composer . harron was known for his roles in `` charlie farquharson '' and in `` anne of green gables – the musical '' . he hosted the radio program , `` morningside '' . harron was born in toronto , ontario . harron died in toronto from cancer , aged 90 .
