Albert Brooks     albert lawrence brooks ( born albert lawrence einstein ; july 22 , 1947 ) is an american actor , voice actor , writer , comedian , and director . he was known for his roles in `` modern romance '' ( 1981 ) , `` lost in america '' ( 1985 ) , `` defending your life '' ( 1991 ) , `` finding nemo '' ( 2004 ) , `` the simpsons movie '' ( 2007 ) , and `` drive '' ( 2011 ) . brooks was born in beverly hills , california .
