Akademio de Esperanto     akademio de esperanto ( `` academy of esperanto '' , shortly ade ) is an independent organisation , which is guarding the development of the constructed language esperanto . they do this to keep it consistent with the basic rules . these basic rules came from eye doctor ludovic lazarus zamenhof . he was the creator of esperanto . he also came up with the idea to establish an organization for guarding the development of the language . this was during the first world congress of esperanto in boulogne-sur-mer , france in 1905 .
