Slobodan Martinović     slobodan martinovic ( 25 july 1945 – 10 january 2015 ) was a serbian chess grandmaster . he began in 1963 , and played until his death in 2015. martinović played 269 master games that were tracked by fide . of them , 83 ( 31 % ) he won , 157 ( 58 % ) were draws , and 29 ( 11 % ) he lost .
