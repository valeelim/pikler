Ben Staartjes     bernard `` ben '' staartjes ( 9 december 1928 - 18 march 2014 ) was a dutch sailor . he represented his country at the 1972 summer olympics in kiel , germany . in 1980 , staartjes was chef de equipe of the dutch olympic sailing team at the 1980 summer olympics in tallinn . he was born in amsterdam . staartjes was born in amsterdam . he died in kapellen , belgium , aged 85 .
