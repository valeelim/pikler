Norman Cook     norman cook ( born quentin leo cook on 16 july 1963 ) , known by his stage name fatboy slim , is an english musician , disc jockey , rapper , and record producer . he is known for songs like `` weapon of choice '' , `` the rockafeller skank '' , `` praise you '' , `` eat sleep rave repeat '' , and `` right here right now '' . cook was born in bromley , kent . he was raised in reigate , surrey . he married zoe ball in 1999. the couple have a son and daughter . they separated in 2016 .
