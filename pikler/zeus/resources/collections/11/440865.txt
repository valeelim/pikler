Ezra Koenig     ezra michael koenig ( born april 8 , 1984 ) is an american singer and songwriter . he is the lead singer and guitarist of the indie rock band vampire weekend . koenig was born in new york city . koenig started writing songs when he was around ten or eleven . in 2006 he started vampire weekend with chris tomson . the band released their first album , `` vampire weekend '' , in 2008 .
