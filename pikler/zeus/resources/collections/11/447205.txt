Deliver Us from Evil     deliver us from evil is a 2014 crime and horror movie . it is about a new york city police officer who gets pulled into a case that is demon-related . eric bana plays the officer , ralph sarchie . olivia munn plays his wife . the movie is based on a 2001 book called `` beware the night '' . screen gems distributes the movie . the movie was released on july 2 , 2014 .
