Spike Jonze     spike jonze ( ; born adam spiegel ; october 22 , 1969 ) is an american director , producer , screenwriter and actor . his movies include `` being john malkovich '' ( 1999 ) , `` adaptation '' ( 2002 ) , `` where the wild things are '' ( 2009 ) , and `` her '' ( 2013 ) . jonze was born in rockville , maryland . he grew up in bethesda , maryland and gulph mills , pennsylvania .
