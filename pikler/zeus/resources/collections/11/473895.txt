Mini Metro (video game)    mini metro ( video game ) mini metro is a strategy simulation video game . it is about creating an efficient subway system for a rapidly growing city . in this game , the player designs a subway station . the object is to get the people to their requested station . the game ends when the station becomes overpopulated . the game is simple at first . over time , it becomes more complicated and challenging . the point of the game is to run the simulation as long as possible . `` mini metro '' has a very modern layout . the subway cars are simply moving rectangles , the rails are simply lines , and the tunnels are dotted lines . `` mini metro '' 's stations each are a different shape . common stations : `` unique '' stations : only one of each unique station can appear each game . other websites . - let 's look at : mini metro ! , youtube
