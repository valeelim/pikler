Augusto Boal     augusto boal ( 16 march 1931 - 2 may 2009 ) was a brazilian theatre director , writer and politician . he was the founder of theatre of the oppressed . boal served one term as a `` vereador '' ( the brazilian version of a city councillor ) in rio de janeiro from 1993 to 1997. he developed the legislative theatre . boal died from respiratory failure caused by leukemia , aged 78 .
