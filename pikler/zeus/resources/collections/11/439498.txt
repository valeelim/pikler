25 BC     year 25 bc was either a common year starting on wednesday , thursday or friday or a leap year starting on wednesday or thursday of the julian calendar . at the time , it was known as the year of the consulship of augustus and silanus . events . - imperator caesar augustus becomes consul for the ninth time . his partner is marcus junius silanus . - the temple to neptune on the circus flaminius is built - estimation : rome becomes the largest city of the world - galatia becomes a roman province - government in china gives tributary states 20,000 rolls of silk cloth and about 20,000 pounds of silk floss . births . - aulus cornelius celsus , author of `` de medicina '' ( d. c. ad 50 ) deaths . - king amyntas
