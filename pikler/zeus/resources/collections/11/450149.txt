Valentina Cortese     valentina cortese ( born 1 january 1923 ) is an italian actress . she was known for her roles in `` the glass mountain '' , `` thieves ' highway '' , `` the house on telegraph hill '' , and in `` the barefoot contessa '' . she was a bafta award in 1973 and was nominated for an academy award in 1975 for her role in `` night for day '' .
