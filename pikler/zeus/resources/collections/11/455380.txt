With Arms Wide Open     `` with arms wide open '' is a power ballad and is the third single off american rock band creed 's second studio album `` human clay '' . the song was released on april 24 , 2000. the song won a grammy award for best rock song at the 43rd annual grammy awards in 2001. it was also nominated for best rock vocal performance by a duo or group but lost to u2 's `` beautiful day '' . other websites . - `` with arms wide open '' on youtube
