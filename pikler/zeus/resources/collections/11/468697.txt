The Wedding Ringer     the wedding ringer ( formerly known as the golden tux ) is an upcoming comedy movie . it was written and produced by jeremy garelick . the movie was distributed by screen gems . the movie stars kevin hart , josh gad , kaley cuoco-sweeting and olivia thirlby . it is scheduled to be released on january 16 , 2015. jimmy ( kevin hart ) owns a wedding company . he provides best man services to socially awkward guys who do n't have anyone close enough to be their best man . doug ( josh gad ) is a groom-to-be . he finds himself without a best man . he turns to jimmy for help . cast . - kevin hart as jimmy - josh gad as doug harris - kaley cuoco-sweeting as paige - alan ritchson as kip - cloris leachman - mimi rogers - ken howard as ed palmer - affion crockett - lindsay pearce as alexandra plylow - jenifer lewis as doris jenkins - olivia thirlby as alison palmer
