Frida Appelgren     frida erika appelgren , earlier married `` muranius '' , born 5 april 1981 in malmö , sweden , is a swedish music artist . she scored a mid-2007 hit with the song `` dunka mig gul och blå '' . together with headline she participated at melodifestivalen 2008 with the song `` upp o hoppa '' . the song ended up 10th in the finals . discography . - `` gasen i botten '' ( rap . mange ; magnus rytterstam ) - `` hoppa upp '' - 2007 - dunka mig gul och blå - 2008 - upp och hoppa - 2014 - hit and run
