Who Do You Think You Are     `` who do you think you are '' is a 1997 song from spice girls . the song 's lyrics are about superstar life and how anyone can get trapped in the world of fame . the song has a disco-style beat which resembles music of the late 1970s . the song was a commercial and critical success . the voice of singer melanie chisholm was praised by pop music critics . it became their fourth number one song in europe and the united kingdom .
