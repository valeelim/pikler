Simon Says     the game of simon says is played by children in many parts of the world . in the game , one player takes on the role of `` simon '' and issues instructions to other players . only instructions prefaced by `` simon says ... '' are to be followed . players are eliminated if they follow any instruction that does not begin with `` simon says ... '' examples would be instructions such as `` simon says touch your nose '' or `` simon says jump on one foot '' . players are eliminated until just one is left . the winner of the game is the player who has followed all the instructions correctly .
