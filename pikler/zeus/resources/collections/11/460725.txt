Marcel Rigout     marcel rigout ( 10 may 1928 – 23 august 2014 ) was a french politician . he served as the minister of vocational training from 1981 to 1984 , under former president françois mitterrand . he was a member of the french communist party ( pcf ; ) . rigout was born in verneuil-sur-vienne . he wrote the novel `` l'autre chance '' in 1983. rigout died on 23 august 2014 in limoges , aged 86 .
