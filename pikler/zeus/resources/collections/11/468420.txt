Ulrika Kalte     ulrika kalte , born 19 may 1970 in skärholmen , sweden is a swedish former association football player . she played for älvsjö aik . she also played for the swedish national team during the 1995 world cup in sweden , where sweden was knocked out in the quarterfinals . she also represented sweden during the 1996 olympic tournament in atlanta , where sweden ended up 6th . she begun playing for norsborgs ff . she became professional in japan . as she moved to shiroki f.c . serena after the 1995 world cup .
