First inauguration of James Madison     the first inauguration of james madison as the fourth president of the united states was held on saturday , march 4 , 1809. it was held in the house chamber in the u.s. capitol . the inauguration was the beginning of the first four-year term of james madison as president and the second term of george clinton as vice president . the oath was given by chief justice john marshall . it was the first presidential inauguration to have an inaugural ball after the ceremony .
