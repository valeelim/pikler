Michael Prinz von Preußen     michael prinz von preußen ( 22 march 1940 – 3 april 2014 ) or michael , prince of prussia , was a german writer and royalty . he was the son of louis ferdinand , prince of prussia . he was the godfather of georg friedrich , prince of prussia . he studied in freiburg . he wrote many biographies about his family members . he was born in bermen . michael died on 3 april 2014 from an illness , aged 74. other websites . - michael of prussia - house of hohenzollern
