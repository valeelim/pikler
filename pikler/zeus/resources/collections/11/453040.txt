Nelson Villagra     nelson villagra ( born 9 august 1937 ) is a chilean actor , author and director . he is thought to be the most successful actors in chilean cinema . his career began in 1967. villagra is known for his roles in `` cantata de chile '' , `` the last supper '' , `` amnesia '' , `` jackal of nahueltoro '' , and `` tierra del fuego '' .
