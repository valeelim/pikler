Brenda's Got a Baby    brenda 's got a baby `` brenda 's got a baby '' is the debut solo single by american rapper 2pac . it was featured on his debut studio album `` 2pacalypse now '' . the song also features vocals from dave hollister . the song is about twelve-year-old girl named brenda who lives in a ghetto , has a baby by her cousin , and is not able to support it . she runs away from home , becomes a prostitute and is murdered . the song explores the issue of underage pregnancy and the effect on both the young mothers and their families . like many of shakur 's songs , it draws from the trouble of the poor . by using brenda to represent young single mothers in general , shakur criticises the lack of support from the father of the baby , the government , and society in general . he wrote the song after he read an article in the newspaper about a twelve-year-old girl who got pregnant by her cousin and threw the baby in a trash compactor .
