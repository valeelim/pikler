47 BC     year 47 bc was a year of the pre-julian roman calendar . in this year julius caesar was dictator of the roman republic . events . - caesar stops a mutiny - caesar invades africa - cleopatra vii promotes her younger brother ptolemy xiv of egypt to co-ruler - julius caesar and his ally cleopatra vii of egypt defeat the forces of the rival egyptian queen arsinoe iv - caesar defeats pharnaces ii of pontus , king of the bosporus , in the battle of zela - feng yuan becomes consort to emperor yuan of the han dynasty births . - prince caesarion of egypt , later pharaoh ptolemy xv ( d. 30 bc ) deaths . - ptolemy xiii theos philopator , pharaoh of egypt ( b . 62 or 61 bc )
