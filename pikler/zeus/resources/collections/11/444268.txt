Hubert de Givenchy     count hubert james marcel taffin de givenchy ( born 21 february 1927 ) is a french fashion designer . he founded the house of givenchy in 1952. he is famous for having designed much of the personal and professional wardrobe of audrey hepburn , as well as clothing for clients such as jacqueline kennedy . he was named to the international best dressed list hall of fame in 1970 .
