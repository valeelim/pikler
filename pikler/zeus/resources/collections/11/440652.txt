Lambada     the lambada is a dance from brazil . it is sometimes called the `` forbidden dance '' . it started in the late 1970s . it became most popular in the 1980s . its popularity was mostly in latin american and caribbean countries . the lambada is a dance for two people . the dance is done with bent legs . steps are usually side to side , with turns . there are pronounced movements for the hips . around the time the lambada became popular , the women often wore short skirts . these skirts swirl up when the woman turns . sometimes the woman 's thong underwear is seen during the turns .
