Kuala Lumpur International Airport     kuala lumpur international airport ( klia ) is and a southeast asian airport . it was built at us $ 3.5 billion in sepang district of selangor . it is located approximately from kuala lumpur and serves the greater klang valley conurbation . it is the major hub of malaysia airlines , maskargo , airasia , airasia x , malindo air and department of civil aviation ( dca ) .
