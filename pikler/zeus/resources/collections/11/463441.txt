GOT7     got7 ( ; romanized as `` gatsebeun '' ; stylized as got7 ) is a multi-national k-pop boy group formed by jyp entertainment in 2014. got7 has seven members . the leader jb , mark , junior , jackson , youngjae , bambam and yugyeom . they are a multinational group with members from south korea , hong kong , thailand , and the united states . got7 released their debut ep , `` got it ? `` , on january 20 , 2014. on october 22 , 2014 , they debuted in japan with the release of their first japanese single , `` around the world '' . for their work in 2014 , got7 won best new artist group at the seoul music awards and received three nominations at the 29th golden disk awards , winning the newcomer award and china goodwill star award .
