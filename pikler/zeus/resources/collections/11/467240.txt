Marty McFly     martin seamus `` marty '' mcfly is a fictional character in the `` back to the future '' trilogy . he is the series ' protagonist and is played by actor michael j. fox . marty also appears in the , where he was voiced by david kaufman . in by telltale games , he is voiced by a.j locascio . in 2008 , marty mcfly was selected by `` empire '' magazine as the 39th greatest movie character of all time . eric stoltz was originally cast as marty mcfly , but after five weeks of shooting scenes for the first movie , director robert zemeckis and executive producer steven spielberg chose to re-cast the role to fox .
