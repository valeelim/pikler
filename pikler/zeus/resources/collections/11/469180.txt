Alexei Devotchenko     aleksey valerievitch devotchenko ( алексе́й вале́рьевич дево́тченко 14 october 1965 – 5 november 2014 ) was a russian actor and activist . he was a member of the united civil front in st. petersburg , the participant dissenters . march 10 , 2010 signed the appeal of the russian opposition `` putin must go '' . participated in other political actions liberal direction . on 18 november 2011 , in his blog in the `` live journal '' devotchenko announced that refuses the title `` honored artist of russia '' and the two state prizes of russia , were strongly against the policies of president putin . on 5 november 2014 , alexei devotchenko was found dead in his apartment in moscow . other websites . - alexei devotchenko - aleksey devotchenko online film teatr.ru
