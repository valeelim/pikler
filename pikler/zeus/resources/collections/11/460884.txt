Christian Bourquin     christian bourquin ( 7 october 1954 – 26 august 2014 ) was a french politician . he was a member of the socialist party . he has been the president of the regional council of languedoc-roussillon from 2010 until his death in 2014. he served as mayor of millas from 1995 through 2001. he was born at saint-féliu-d'amont , pyrenees-orientales . bourquin died in montpellier , hérault , france , aged 59. other websites . - christian bourquin official blog
