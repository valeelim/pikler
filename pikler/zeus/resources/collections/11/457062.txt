RoboCop 3     robocop 3 is a 1993 american science fiction film action movie . it is the third movie in the robocop franchise . it 's a sequel to `` robocop '' and `` robocop 2 '' . robert john bruke replaces peter weller as the cyborg robocop . a remake of the first film of the same name released in february 2014. the film picks off where the second film left off .
