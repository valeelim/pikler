Palacio de Villena (Cadalso de los Vidrios)    palacio de villena ( cadalso de los vidrios ) the palace of villena ( ) is a palace in cadalso de los vidrios , spain . it was built by álvaro de luna , duke of trujillo . the building and the gardens are listed as a spanish property of cultural interest . the building is listed as a `` monument '' . it has been protected since 1931. the gardens are listed as a `` jardín histórico '' ( `` historic garden '' ) . they have been protected since the 1970s .
