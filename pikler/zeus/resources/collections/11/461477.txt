Roberto Cairo     roberto andrés cairo pablo ( 3 may 1963 – 28 august 2014 ) was a spanish actor from madrid . he appeared in many different television series and movies as a supporting actor . he was best known for his role as desiderio `` desi '' quijo in the series `` cuéntame cómo pasó '' . cairo died from lung cancer on 28 august 2014 in madrid . he was 51 .
