Opisthokont     opisthokonts are a superkingdom of the eukaryota , including the animals . it is a group of unikonts . there are several classifications of the eukaryotes which are being actively discussed . each of them divides the domain eukaryota into five groups , but there is disagreement as to the details . the opisthokonta is thought to be a monophyletic clade , and therefore superior to the unikonta as as taxonomic term .
