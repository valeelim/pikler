Grant Denyer     grant denyer ( born 12 september 1977 ) is an australian television presenter and former motor racing driver . he is best known for being the former weather presenter of the seven network breakfast television program `` sunrise '' . he is currently the host of `` family feud '' on channel 10. denyer was born coffs harbour , new south wales . he is married to television producer cheryl rogers . they have one daughter , sailor ( born 2011 ) .
