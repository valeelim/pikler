Älvdalen    älvdalen älvdalen is a locality and the seat of älvdalen municipality in dalarna county , sweden . in 2010 , 1,810 people lived there . the town is widely known for being the place of manufacturing , in 1839 , of the 4-meter granite vase ( called `` älvdalen vase '' ) , installed in the summer garden in saint petersburg ( a gift from charles xiv john of sweden to nicholas i of russia ) .
