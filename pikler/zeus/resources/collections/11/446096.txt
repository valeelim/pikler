Oddworld Inhabitants     oddworld inhabitants is the name of a company that publishes games that are set on the oddworld universe . it was founded in 1994 by lorne lanning and sherry mckenna . the company has been known for creating the oddworld quintology and that is to be finished . it is best known for it 's four oddworld games that were released from 1997 to 2005. the games include : oddworld abe 's oddysee , oddworld abe 's exoddus , oddworld : munch 's oddysee and oddworld : stranger 's wrath as well as two gameboy and one gameboy advance games that were short versions of abe 's oddysee , abe 's exoddus and munch 's oddysee . they were titled : oddworld adventures , oddworld adventures ii and munch 's oddysee . in 2005 , oddworld inhabitants took a break from making video games . they ended up going into film industry and the company were planned to make a plannet like oddworld called citizen siege . the company are now back into making video games with help from a video game development company called just add water ( developments ) uk ltd. they only sell digital products as of 2008 when the original pc games went up on steam . they also made available the original ps1 games on the playstation network store in 2010 for the ps3 and psp . the classics were made avaliable for ps vita in august 2012. they started work by porting the original xbox version of oddworld : stranger 's wrath to pc . they then made an hd version and had several improvements made to it and it was released in 2011 for the pc and ps3 . in 2012 oddworld announced that abe 's oddysee was getting a completely re-made game from the ground up and it 's title which was chosen by fans is called oddworld : new 'n ' tasty . they also released oddworld : munch 's oddysee hd for ps3 in december 2012 .
