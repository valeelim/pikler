Alejandra Da Passano     maría alejandra ana da passano ( 26 july 1947 – 30 june 2014 ) , better known as alejandra da passano , was an argentine actress of movies , stage plays and television shows . her best known roles were in `` mr. president '' ( 1970 ) , `` malevo '' ( 1972 ) and `` la madre maría '' ( 1974 ) . she was born in buenos aires . da passano died on 30 june 2014 in buenos aires , aged 66 .
