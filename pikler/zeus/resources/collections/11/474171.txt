Hjalmar Hammarskjöld     knut hjalmar leonard hammarskjöld , born 4 february 1862 , dead 12 october 1953 , was a swedish politician , scholar , cabinet minister and member of parliament from 1923 to 1938 ( first chamber ) . he served as the prime minister of sweden from 1914 to 1917. in 1890 , he married agnes almquist ( 1866–1940 ) . the couple had four sons : , åke , sten and dag .
