Stockholm metro     the stockholm metro ( ) is a metro system in stockholm in sweden . the first line was opened in 1950. however , underground trams had been used since 1933. the system has 100 stations in use , 47 of them are underground and 53 above ground . there are three lines ( green , red and blue ) travelling through central stockholm . in the year of 2013 the stockholm metro cars totally carried 328 million passengers inside . it corresponds to approximately 898,630 passengers per day . the long metro system is owned by the stockholm county council through storstockholms lokaltrafik ( sl ) . the operation is currently contracted to mtr corporation . the stockholm metro system has been referred to as the '' world 's longest art gallery '' , with more over 90 of the network 's 100 stations being decorated with various works of art . other websites . - sl official site - stockholm metro at `` urbanrail.net ''
