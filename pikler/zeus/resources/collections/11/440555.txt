Mohammad Hasan Sharq     mohammad hasan sharq ( born 1925 ) is an afghan politician during the communist regime of afghanistan . sharq became prime minister of afghanistan or chairman of the council of ministers of the soviet-backed government , the democratic republic of afghanistan . he was selected as a back-up candidate after the loya jirga ratified a new constitution in 1987. however , the power of his office was relatively small compared with the ones of the presidency .
