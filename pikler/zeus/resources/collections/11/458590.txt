Sex and the City     sex and the city is an american television romantic sitcom created by darren star and produced by hbo . it broadcast from 1998 until 2004. the original run of the show had a total of 94 episodes . throughout its six-year run , the show received help from various producers , writers and directors . the series starred sarah jessica parker , kim cattrall , kristin davis , and cynthia nixon . it is set in new york city . the series soon became into two feature movies , `` sex and the city '' ( 2008 ) and `` sex and the city 2 '' ( 2010 ) .
