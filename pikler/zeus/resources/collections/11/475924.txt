Per-Olof Åstrand    per-olof åstrand per-olof åstrand ( october 21 , 1922 – january 2 , 2015 ) was a swedish professor of physiology . he worked at the royal gymnastic central institute ( gci/gih ) in stockholm 1970-1977 , and 1977-1988 at the karolinska institute in stockholm , sweden . he was a member of the nobel assembly at karolinska institutet ( 1977–1988 ) . åstrand is considered a `` pioneer '' , `` legend '' and one of the `` founding fathers '' of modern exercise physiology . other websites . - excerpt from interview on swedish national television , hylands hörna , 1963 - per-olof åstrand in nationalencyklopedin - publications in diva
