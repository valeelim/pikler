Armin Mueller-Stahl     armin mueller-stahl ( born 17 december 1930 ) is a german actor , painter , writer and musician . he is best known for his academy award nominated role as peter helfgott in the `` shine '' ( 1996 ) . mueller-stahl was born in tilsit , east prussia , german empire ( now sovetsk , kaliningrad oblast , russia ) . he moved to berlin during world war ii . he has been married to gabriele scholz since 1973. the couple live in los angeles , california .
