Dean of the United States House of Representatives     the dean of the united states house of representatives is the representative that has been in office the longest . the current dean is john conyers , a democrat from michigan . he has served in the house since january 3 , 1965. the dean is a symbolic job whose only customary duty is to swear in a speaker of the house when he or she is elected . the dean comes forward on the house floor to administer ( give ) the oath to the speaker-elect . the new speaker then administers the oath to the other members . the dean does not preside over the election of the speaker , unlike the father of the house in the house of commons of the united kingdom and the dean of the canadian house of commons . because of other privileges associated with seniority , the dean is usually given some of the most desirable office space . the dean is generally either chair or ranking minority member of an influential committee . unlike the office of president pro tem , the office of the house of representatives is not an official position .
