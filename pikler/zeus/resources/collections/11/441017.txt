Karunanidhi     muthuvel karunanidhi ( born dakshinamurthy ; 3 june 1924 ) is an indian politician . he has served as the chief minister of tamil nadu five different times . he is the head of the dravida munnetra kazhagam ( dmk ) , a dravidian political party in the state of tamil nadu . he has been the leader of the dmk since the death of its founder , c. n. annadurai , in 1969. karunanidhi was born in thirukuvalai , madras presidency , british india . he is an atheist .
