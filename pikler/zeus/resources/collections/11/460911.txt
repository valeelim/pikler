Roman Catholic Diocese of Calgary     the roman catholic diocese of calgary is a catholic diocese in the province of alberta , canada . it covers the calgary region , southern alberta , and part of the alberta 's rockies region . it is a suffragan diocese of the roman catholic archdiocese of edmonton . there are 82 parishes and missions in the diocese . the diocese 's cathedral is st. mary 's cathedral , in calgary . the current bishop is frederick henry , who was appointed in 1998 .
