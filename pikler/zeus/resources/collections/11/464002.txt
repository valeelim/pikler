Cecilia Cenci     cecilia cenci ( 1942 – 21 september 2014 ) was an argentine actress . she was best known her roles in the movies `` piedra libre '' ( 1976 ) and `` contragolpe '' ( 1979 ) . cenci was born in la plata , buenos aires province . she was of italian ancestry . cenci died from brain cancer on 21 september 2014 at the fundación favaloro in buenos aires . she was 72 .
