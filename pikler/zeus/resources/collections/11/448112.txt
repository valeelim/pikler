Malappuram     malappuram is a city in the southern indian state of kerala . it is 50 km southeast of kozhikode , 90 km northwest of palakkad and 140 km northwest of coimbatore.malappuram is the head quarters of the district.in earlier times , malappuram was the head quarters of european and british troops and later it became the head quarters of the malabar special police ( m.s.p ) .the municipality of malappuram city has started kottakkunnu amusement park , a water theme park in the valley of kottakkunnu.malppuram is well known for its greenery , making it a tourist spot .
