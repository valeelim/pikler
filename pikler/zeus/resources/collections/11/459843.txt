Peter Cundall     peter cundall , am ( born 1 april 1927 in manchester , england ) is an british-australian horticulturalist , conservationist , author , broadcaster and television personality in australia . until the age of 81 , cundall continued to be a presenter of the abc tv program `` gardening australia '' . his last show aired on 26 july 2008. other websites . - conversation with peter cundall & stephen munroe , national museum of australia , 3 april 2009
