Andréa Parisy     andrée marcelle henriette parisy ( 4 december 1935 – 27 april 2014 ) , better known by her stage names andréa parisy and sometimes andrée parizy , was a french actress . she is best known for her roles in movies such as `` babes a gogo '' ( 1956 ) , `` the little bather '' ( 1968 ) and `` mayerling '' ( 1968 ) . she was born in levallois-perret . parisy died on 27 april 2014 , aged 78 .
