Plastic Surgery Disasters     plastic surgery disasters is the second studio album by american hardcore punk band , dead kennedys . `` plastic surgery disasters '' was released in november 1982. the album was reissued with their last ep `` in god we trust , inc. '' which are the last eight songs on the cd . the cover photo of the album is a picture called `` hands '' by photographer michael wells . allmusic rated the album 4.5 out of 5 stars .
