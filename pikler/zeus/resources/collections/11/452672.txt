Karlheinz Hackl     karlheinz hackl ( 16 may 1949 – 1 june 2014 ) was an austrian actor , singer and theater director . his best known movie roles are `` sophie 's choice '' ( 1982 ) and `` the wall '' ( 2012 ) . hackl was born in vienna . in 1997 , he married austrian actress maria köstlinger . hackl died from a malignant brain tumor on 1 june 2014 in vienna , aged 65 .
