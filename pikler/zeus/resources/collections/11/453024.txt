Enrique Pinti     enrique pinti ( born october 7 , 1939 ) is a retired-argentine comedian and actor . his career began in 1957. pinti was born in buenos aires , argentina . he is known for his roles in `` juan que reía '' ( 1976 ) , `` esperando la carroza '' ( 1985 ) , `` flop '' ( 1990 ) , `` perdido por perdido '' ( 1993 ) , `` tango '' ( 1998 ) , and in `` arregui , la noticia del día '' ( 2001 ) . he now lives in buenos aires . pinti is openly gay .
