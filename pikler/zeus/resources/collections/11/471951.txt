Ibrahim Lodi     ibrahim lodi was the last sultan of delhi . he was an ethnic pashtun . he became sultan in 1517 after sikandar lodi died . sikandar was his father and ibrahim was soon challenged by his younger brother , jalal kahn . ibrahim had military skills . but unlike his father he lacked tact . he faced a number of rebellions . the mewar ruler rana sangram singh extended his empire right up to western uttar pradesh and threatened to attack agra . there was rebellion in the east also . ibrahim lodi also displeased the nobility when he replaced old and senior commanders by younger ones who were loyal to him . he further alienated them by throwing some of the older noblemen in prison . his afghan nobility eventually invited babur to invade india . in 1526 , the mughal forces of babur , the king of kabulistan , defeated ibrahim 's much larger army in the battle of panipat . ibrahim was killed during the battle . it is estimated that babur 's forces numbered around 12,000 men . his army had between 20 to 24 pieces of field artillery . ibrahim lodi had around 30,000-40,000 men along with at least 100 war elephants . after ibrahim lodi was overthrown , the mughals invaded india .
