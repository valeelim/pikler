Kirka     kirill `` kirka '' babitzin ( 22 september 1950 - 31 january 2007 ) was a finnish singer . kirka 's most famous songs include `` hengaillaan '' , `` leijat '' ( kites ) and `` varrella virran '' ( down by the river ) '' . kirill babitzin was born in helsinki in 1950. his parents were russian emigrants . he first got into music at the age of five when his grandmother gave him an accordion . he won an accordion competition when he was ten years old . he was soon attracted to rock and roll music . his first band was 'the creatures ' , which he joined in 1964 assuming stage name kirka . other websites . - website
