Barbourofelis     barbourofelis is an extinct genus of large , carnivorous , mammals . they were a type of saber-toothed cat . the genus was found in north america during the miocene to pleistocene . they lived from 13.6 5.3 million years ago ( mya ) and continued for about 8.3 million years . its flattened saber teeth were protected by a bony flange from the lower jaw . it was a saber-toothed cat , but was not a close relative of cats such as `` smilodon '' . `` barbourofelis '' has been difficult to classify . it was put in the nimravidae by schultz ( 1970 ) and martin ( 1998 ) .
