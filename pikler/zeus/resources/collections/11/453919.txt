Brazilian three-banded armadillo     the brazilian three-banded armadillo ( `` tolypeutes tricinctus '' ) is a species of armadillo endemic to brazil . it is one of two species which can roll into a ball . the other is the southern three-banded armadillo . it is the most rare species of armadillo : most live in the region called caatinga , in north-east brazil . until the 1990s , the species was thought to be extinct , now it is classified as vulnerable .
