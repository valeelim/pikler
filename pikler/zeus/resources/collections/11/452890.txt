Shining Through     shining through is a 1992 american-british world war ii drama movie . it is about 1940 in germany . michael douglas plays ed . melanie griffith plays secretary linda . liam neeson plays franz-otto . the movie was not successful , though it did make over $ 13 million in profits over a budget of $ 30 million . the movie was called the worst picture of 1992 by the razzie awards . the critics did not like it .
