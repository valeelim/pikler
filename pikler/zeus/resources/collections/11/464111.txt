Anarkali Bazaar     anarkali bazaar or anarkali market is an old style market in lahore , pakistan . the word 'anarkali ' means 'pomegranate blossom ' . according to a legend this was the name of a beautiful dancing-girl who fell in love with the mughal prince selim ( who later became the emperor jahangir ) . but the prince 's father , the old emperor akbar , did not like this and he had the girl arrested and punished . she died and was buried near this bazaar or market . later , when her lover jahangir became emperor he named this place after her . today , there is still an old tomb near there , which was built by jahangir in 1615 , and people say this is the tomb of the real anarkali , but this is not sure . another famous tomb near anarkali market is that of the sultan qutb-ud-din aibak . today , the anarkali bazaar or market is one of the best shopping areas in all of south asia . women especially love to go there , because it has excellent clothes , shoes and jewellry shops . there are some nice places to eat , there , too . it is open most days , and from 9 in the morning to 11 at night . there are always many lights and music playing there , and also a lot of traffic .
