Miodrag Radulovacki     miodrag ( misha ) radulovacki , md , phd ( serbian cyrillic : миодраг радуловачки ; serbian latin : miodrag radulovački ; 28 april 1933 – 26 may 2014 ) , was a serbian scientist and inventor . he is known for creating the adenosine sleep theory . he taught as a professor of pharmacology at the university of illinois at chicago . radulovacki died in belgrade , serbia , aged 81 .
