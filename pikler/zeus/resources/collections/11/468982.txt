Sakurajima     the eruptions of this mountain have been recorded many times in history . great taisho ( 大正 ) eruption in 1914 connected this island to peninsula . 58 people were killed and 112 people were injured . these days , we can see a lot of eruptions . the information about which way the wind blows is important to know if ash is falling or not . so , kagoshima weather forecast report it . people in sakurajima take a drill to escape from the island . japan meteorological agency watches this mountain all days . we can see the latest picture from these cameras at jma 's website . related pages . - japan meteorological agency other websites . - kagoshima meteorological office ( 鹿児島地方気象台 ) - kagoshima city ( 鹿児島市 )
