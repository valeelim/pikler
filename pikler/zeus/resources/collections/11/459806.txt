Emilio Fernández     emilio `` el indio '' fernández ( born emilio fernández romo , ; march 26 , 1904 – august 6 , 1986 ) was a mexican movie director , actor and screenwriter . he was one of the most famous directors of the golden age of mexican cinema in the 1940s and 1950s . he is best known for his work as director of the movie `` maria candelaria '' , which won the palme d'or award at the 1946 cannes film festival . other websites . - ( es ) biography at the cinema of mexico site of the itesm .
