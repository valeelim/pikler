Heart cancer     heart cancer is cancer that involves different regions of the heart . it is extremely rare . many types of the disease includes myxomas , fibromas , rhabdomyomas , and hamartomas . in a study of 12,487 autopsies performed in hong kong seven cardiac tumors were found , most of which were benign . according to mayo clinic : `` at mayo clinic , on average only one case of heart cancer is seen each year .
