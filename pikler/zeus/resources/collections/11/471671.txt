Gustavo Díaz Ordaz     gustavo díaz ordaz bolaños ( ; 12 march 1911 – 15 july 1979 ) was a mexican politician . he was a member of the institutional revolutionary party ( pri ) . he served as the president of mexico from 1964 to 1970. his distant cousin was comedian roberto gomez bolaños . ordaz died in mexico city , mexico on 15 july 1979 from colorectal cancer , aged 68 .
