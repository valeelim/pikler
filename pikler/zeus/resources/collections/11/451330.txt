Seal of Tennessee     the great seal of the state of tennessee is the official state seal of the u.s. state of tennessee . an official seal was provided for in the constitution of the state of tennessee of february 6 , 1796. the design was n't started until september 25 , 1801. the outer ring has the words `` the great seal of the state of tennessee '' and the date `` feb. 6th , 1796 '' . the month and day were left out of later designs so only `` 1796 '' is shown .
