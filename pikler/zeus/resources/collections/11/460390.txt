Kelly's Heroes    kelly 's heroes kelly 's heroes is a 1970 american war comedy movie directed by brian g. hutton . it was about a group of world war ii soldiers who go awol to rob a bank behind enemy lines . the movie stars clint eastwood , telly savalas , don rickles , carroll o'connor , and donald sutherland , with secondary roles played by harry dean stanton , gavin macleod , and stuart margolin . it was released on june 23 , 1970 and made $ 5,200,000 at the movies . it had the budget of $ 4,000,000 .
