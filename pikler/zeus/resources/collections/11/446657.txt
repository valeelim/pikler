Fine Wind, Clear Morning    fine wind , clear morning fine wind , clear morning ( also known as south wind , clear sky and red fuji ) is a woodblock print made by hokusai . the art work was printed somewhere between 1830 and 1832 which was during the edo period . it is the second print in the series , thirty-six views of mount fuji . it shows that in early autumn when the wind is southerly and when the sky is clear , the rising sun can turn mount fuji red . other websites . - fine wind , clear morning on the indianapolis museum of art website - fine wind , clear morning on the british museum website
