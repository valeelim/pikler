Arrondissement of Millau     the arrondissement of millau is an arrondissement of france . it is part of the aveyron `` département '' in the occitanie region . its capital , and subprefecture of the department , is the city of millau . when the `` arrondissements '' were created in 1800 , millau was one of them in the aveyron department . it is the southeastern part of the `` arrondissements '' of the department , with an area of , the second in area of the department , and a population of 70,995 inhabitants and a population density of inhabitants/km² . the `` arrondissement '' is bordered to the north and west by the `` arrondissement '' of rodez , to the northeast by the lozère department , to the east by the gard department , to the south by the hérault department and to the southwest by the tarn department . after the reorganisation of the cantons in france , cantons are not subdivisions of the `` arrondissements '' so they could have `` communes '' that belong to different `` arrondissements '' . in the `` arrondissement '' of millau , there is only one canton where not all their `` communes '' are in the `` arrondissement '' : raspes et lévezou . the following table shows the distribution of the `` communes '' in the cantons and `` arrondissements '' :
