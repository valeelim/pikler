Seventh-day Adventist Church in Canada     the seventh-day adventist church in canada is part of the branch of the seventh-day adventist church located in canada . the church started in the 1870s in quebec . william miller travelled to parts of canada . in 1902 , seventh-day adventist churches in canada orgainized a union conference for the country . in 2011 , the church reported a membership of 61,468 .
