Thomas Kurzhals     thomas kurzhals ( 13 december 1953 – 2 january 2014 ) was a german keyboardist , composer and musician . he was best known as a member of the rock bands stern-combo meißen and karat . he was born in ronneburg , thuringia , east germany . kurzhals died from liver cirrhosis on 2 january 2014 in glauchau , saxony . he was 60 years old .
