Lode Van Den Bergh     lode van den bergh ( born 18 june 1920 ) , pseudonym aster berkhof and piet visser , is a belgian writer . he is married to nora steyaert . he was born in rijkevorsel . he started his career as a monitor at the catholic university of leuven . he also worked as a teacher at the royal athenaea in antwerp , brussels and koekelberg . finally he became a professor at the `` saint ignatius faculty '' in antwerp . other websites . - aster berkhof
