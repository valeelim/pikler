Jože Toporišič     jože toporišič ( october 11 , 1926 – december 9 , 2014 ) was a slovene linguist . he was the author of the most influential slovene scientific grammar of the second half of the 20th century . he was a member of the slovenian academy of sciences and arts , and coauthor of the academy 's slovene normative guide ( ) . he was born in mostec , near brežice in slovenia . toporišič died in ljubljana , slovenia , aged 88 .
