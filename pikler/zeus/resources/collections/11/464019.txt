Abubakar Shekau     abubakar shekau ( ; born 1969 or 1975 ) , also known by his alias darul tawheed , was a nigerien islamist terrorist . he was the emir of the nigerian militant group boko haram . he served as deputy leader to the group 's founder , mohammed yusuf , until yusuf was killed in 2009. he was said to have been also killed in 2009 until a video in july 2010 appeared , with shekau being called the new leader of the group . shekau was born in niger and was a kanuri . he spoke hausa , arabic , and english . he had four wives .
