Dawn of the Planet of the Apes     dawn of the planet of the apes is an american science-fiction movie directed by matt reeves . it stars andy serkis , gary oldman , jason clarke , and keri russell . it is the sequel to the 2011 movie `` rise of the planet of the apes '' . it was released on july 11 , 2014. a sequel to the film will be released in july 14 , 2017 .
