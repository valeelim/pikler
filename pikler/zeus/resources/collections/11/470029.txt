Botha     pieter willem botha ( 2 january 1916 â€“ 31 october 2006 ) was a south african politician . he is commonly known as p.w . botha , and often called die groot krokodil ( which translates to the great crocodile ) . botha was the last prime minister of the country . in 1984 the office was abolished . after that he served as the first state president . when he was first elected to parliament in 1948 , he was against black majority rule . in 1992 , there was a referendum to allow black majority rule . in that referendum , botha campaigned for the no vote . botha supported apartheid throughout his life . in 2006 , botha died of a heart attack , aged 90 .
