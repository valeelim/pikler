William Perry     william james perry ( born october 11 , 1927 ) is an american mathematician , engineer , and businessman . he was the united states secretary of defense from february 3 , 1994 , to january 23 , 1997 , under president bill clinton . he also served as deputy secretary of defense ( 1993–1994 ) and under secretary of defense for research and engineering ( 1977–1981 ) .
