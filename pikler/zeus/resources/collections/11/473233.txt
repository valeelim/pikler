Tom Adams (actor)    tom adams ( actor ) tom adams ( 9 march 1938 - 11 december 2014 ) was an english actor . adams was known for his roles as dai nimmo in `` the great escape '' and as commander vorshak in `` doctor who '' . adams died on 11 december 2014 at the age of 76 at wexham park hospital in slough , berkshire from cancer .
