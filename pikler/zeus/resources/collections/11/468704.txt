Lila &amp; Eve    lila & amp ; eve lila & eve is an american drama movie . it was directed by charles stone iii . it was written by pat gilfillan . the movie stars jennifer lopez and viola davis . after her son was killed , lila joins a support group . lila meets eve . she finds out that eve 's daughter died . the police is not helping lila enough . eve tells lila to find the killers herself . cast . - jennifer lopez as eve rafael - viola davis as lila walcott - shea whigham as detective holliston - yolonda ross as patrice - aml ameen as stephon - andre royo as skaketti
