José Falcó Sanmartín     josé falcó sanmartín ( barcelona , september 27 , 1916 – toulouse , may 10 , 2014 ) was a spanish pilot . he served as part of the spanish republican air force , who fought the spanish civil war , with eight official victories . he went into exile from spain . he moved to france , a country that welcomed him and where he died . sanmartín died from natural causes , aged 97 .
