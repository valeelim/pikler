Marco Minghetti     marco minghetti ( november 18 , 1818 - december 10 , 1886 ) was an italian economist and statesman . minghetti was born at bologna into a family of rich landowners . in march 1848 pope pius ix offered to him the post of minister in the first constitutional government of the papal states . however , on 29 april 1848 , after the speech with which pius ix announced the withdrawal of the papal army from the front of the first italian war of independence , minghetti resigned . member of the `` historical right '' , he was minister of the interior in the governments led by camillo benso , count of cavour and bettino ricasoli . he was also minister of the treasury in the government led by luigi carlo farini . he was twice prime minister of the kingdom of italy : from 24 march 1863 to 28 september 1864 and from from 10 july 1873 to 25 march 1876. in 1864 minghetti signed the `` convention of september '' with france : with this document , italy renounced in rome and moved the capital from turin to florence . there were many protests against this agreement and consequently minghetti was forced to resign . during his second experience as president , minghetti raised taxes to achieve a balanced budget . he was able to accomplish this , but there were protests against his tax policy and was forced to resign for the second time .
