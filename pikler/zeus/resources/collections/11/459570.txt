181 West Madison Street     181 west madison street is a skyscraper located in chicago . built in 1990 , the building is 680 feet ( 207 m ) tall and contains 50 floors . it is architect cesar pelli 's first and only completed tower in the city . the building was originally proposed to be 2,000 foot ( 610 m ) , but the size was reduced due to the stock market . other websites . - official website - emporis listing
