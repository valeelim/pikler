South Asian river dolphin     the south asian river dolphin ( `` platanista gangetica '' ) is a freshwater or river dolphin found in india , bangladesh , nepal and pakistan . it is split into two subspecies , the ganges river dolphin ( `` p. g. gangetica '' ) and indus river dolphin ( `` p. g. minor '' ) . from the 1970s until 1998 , they were regarded as separate species ; however , in 1998 , their classification was changed from two separate species to subspecies of a single species . the idea was to wait until genetic analysis had decided how best to classify them .
