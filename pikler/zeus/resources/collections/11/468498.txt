Se hace saber     se hace saber is a spanish television comedy program . it was aired in tve 1 from 24 december 2013 to 21 february 2014. it was hosted and stared by goyo jiménez and others actors like agustín jiménez , arturo gonzález-campos , berta collado , iñaki urrutia , leo harlem and sergio fernández `` el monaguillo '' . the program was canceled after eight weeks , as its lower ratings . other websites . - official website at rtve.es
