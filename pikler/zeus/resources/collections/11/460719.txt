Glam metal     glam metal ( also known as hair metal , sleaze metal and pop metal ) is a hard rock/heavy metal subgenre . it combines punk rock with pop music . it became popular in the 1970s and 1980s . but it became less popular in the 1990s . this was because grunge music was getting more popular . some bands of this genre include : - extreme - mötley crüe - europe - bon jovi - quiet riot - hanoi rocks other websites . - the 15 best hair metal bands of all time - hair metal vs. grunge ; just who survived
