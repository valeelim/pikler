Rooster Teeth     rooster teeth productions , llc . or rooster teeth is a movie making company in austin , texas , united states . it makes live action short videos , animated videos , video game animations , and voiced video game playing called `` let 's play '' . the creator , burnie burns , made voice-over videos of bungie studios ' big first-person shooter video game . these videos led to the making of `` red vs. blue '' , an awarded funny science-fiction set of videos released on april 1 , 2003. these are still being made , and its 12th season came out on april 28 , 2014. rooster teeth also makes live-action short videos , funny game play under the name achievement hunter , making video games , and full animated videos such as rwby . rooster teeth hosts its community meeting , rtx , yearly in austin , texas . in 2015 , rooster teeth released its large movie debut , `` lazer team '' , a science-fiction funny movie .
