Jönköpings IK     jönköpings ik is a floorball club in jönköping , sweden . it was established on 9 march 1985 after a merger of the smu immanuel church and the munksjö school teams . the men 's team has played several seasons in the swedish top division . the men 's team lost the swedish national finals in the years of 1986 , 1988 and 1990. also , the men 's team earned the national championship bronze medals in the years of 1987 , 1989 and 1991. other websites . - official website
