Gustaf Åkerhielm    gustaf åkerhielm friherre johan `` gustaf '' nils samuel `` åkerhielm '' of margaretelund , born 24 july 1833 , dead 2 1900 , was a swedish politician , baron and landowner . he was a member of the swedish parliament twice , first from 1859 to 1866 and then again from 1870 to 1900. he was also swedien 's minister of finance from 1874 to 1875 , and minister for foreign affairs in 1889. also , he served as swedish prime minister from 1889 to 1891. he was married to ulrika gyldenstolpe in 1860. together with her , he had three children .
