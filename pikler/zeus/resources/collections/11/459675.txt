Des Plaines River Valley Bridge     the des plaines river valley bridge is a post-tensioned concrete girder bridge in the northeastern portion of the u.s. state of illinois . it carries interstate 355 over the des plaines river , the chicago sanitary and ship canal , the illinois and michigan canal , several railroad lines , bluff road , new avenue and a forest preserve . this is officially named the veterans memorial bridge . there are title plaques on the square pillars at the north and south entrances to the bridge . the bridge is long .
