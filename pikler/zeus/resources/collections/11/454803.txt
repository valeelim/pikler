Camille Paglia     camille anna paglia ( /ˈpɑːliə/ ; born april 2 , 1947 ) is an american academic . her dissertation `` sexual personae '' was published by yale university press in 1990. paglia is a libertarian . paglia was born in endicott , new york to italian immigrants . in 1957 her family moved to syracuse , new york . `` sexual personae '' was rejected by 7 publishers before yale university press published it in 1990. in 1992 paglia 's second book was published . in 2012 paglia 's most recent book `` glittering images '' was published .
