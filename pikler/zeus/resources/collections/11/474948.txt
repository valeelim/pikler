Marian Jurczyk     marian jurczyk ( 16 october 1935 - 30 december 2014 ) was a polish politician and solidarity trade union activist . he served as mayor of szczecin from 1998 through 2000 and from 2002 through 2006. he was born in karczewice , second polish republic . jurczyk died in szczecin , poland , aged 79 .
