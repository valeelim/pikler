Abdus Salim Khan     h.e . khan-sahib abdus salim khan ( 1907-1957 ) was an early pakistani diplomat . he served as pakistan 's ambassador in ceylon ( now called sri lanka ) and japan . he also served as consul-general in the united states , posted at new york and later as pakistan 's deputy high commissioner ( a type of special ambassador ) to the united kingdom.khan died sudddenly in july 1957 , while on duty in london , united kingdom . he was the son of khan-sahib abdul majid khan tarin , obe , and husband of begum mahmooda salim khan .
