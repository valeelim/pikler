Lycopsis     lycopsis is an extinct genus of south american metatherian . it lived during the miocene of what is now colombia . `` lycopsis '' was more likely an ambush hunter than a pounce-pursuit predator , stalking small-to medium-sized prey . the borhyaenids in general were once arboreal , and later changed their life-style to one of living on the ground . the skeleton of `` lycopsis '' has a mosaic of features which show it was not yet a fast runner on the ground .
