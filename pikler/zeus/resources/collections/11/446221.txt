Kaleph Jõulu     kaleph jõulu ( 15 november 1927 – 22 january 2014 ) was an estonian educator , writer and folklore collector . he was best known for his book `` maailma 33333 rahvatarkust '' ( 2013 ) . he won the rahvaluule kogumispreemia ( folklore prize ) four times ( 1994 , 1996 , 1997 , 2007 ) . he was born in rapla , rapla county . jõulu died on 22 january 2014 in viljandi , viljandi county . he was 86 years old . other websites . - kaleph jõulu books
