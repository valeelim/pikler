Cucujiformia     the cucujiformia is an infraorder of polyphagan beetles . it includes most plant-eating beetles . it has six superfamilies : - chrysomeloidea ~7 families including longhorn beetles & leaf beetles - cleroidea checkered beetles , bark-gnawing beetles and melyridae , the soft-winged flower beetles ] ] - cucujoidea 32 families including ladybirds and fungus beetles - curculionoidea ~8 families mostly weevils ; also snout beetles and bark beetles - lymexylidae ( ship-timber beetles ) - tenebrionoidea ( formerly `` heteromera '' ) 30 families including blister beetles and ant-like beetles
