Matteo Renzi     matteo renzi ( ; born 11 january 1975 ) is an italian politician . he is the 56th and current prime minister of italy . he became prime minister on 22 february 2014. before , he was the president of florence province from 2004 to 2009 and the mayor of florence from 2009 to 2014. he resigned on 5 december 2016. other websites . - official website of the presidency of the council of ministers - renzi 's personal website - renzi on his party 's website
