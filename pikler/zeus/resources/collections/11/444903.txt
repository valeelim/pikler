Paul C. Donnelly     paul charles donnelly ( march 28 , 1923 - march 12 , 2014 ) was an american guided missile pioneer and a senior nasa manager . he was the manager during the apollo moon landing program at the kennedy space center ( ksc ) . donnelly helped develop the u.s. navy 's bat , the first `` smart bomb '' in the history of warfare . he was born in altoona , pennsylvania . donnelly died on march 12 , 2014 in melbourne , florida , aged 90 .
