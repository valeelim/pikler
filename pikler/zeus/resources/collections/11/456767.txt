Paul LePage     paul richard lepage ( born october 9 , 1948 ) is an american businessman and republican party politician . he currently serves as the 74th governor of maine . he was the mayor of waterville from 2003 to 2011 , and was a city councilor prior to that . he worked as general manager of the 14-store discount chain marden 's surplus and salvage from 1996 until 2011 .
