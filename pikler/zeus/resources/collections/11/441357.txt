Bikini Kill     bikini kill was an american punk rock band . the band was started in october 1990 in olympia . the band 's members were singer kathleen hanna , guitarist billy karren , bassist kathi wilcox , and drummer tobi vail . the band was started in olympia , washington by kathleen hanna ( vocals ) , billy karren ( guitar ) , kathi wilcox ( bass ) , and tobi vail ( drums ) . they released a demo cassette tape in 1991. they were then signed to kill rock stars . their first extended play `` bikini kill '' was released on the label . in september 1993 the band 's first album `` pussy whipped '' was released . the band 's last album , `` reject all american '' was released in 1996. in 1997 the band broke up . in 2012 `` bikini kill '' was released again . in 2014 `` yeah yeah yeah yeah '' was released again .
