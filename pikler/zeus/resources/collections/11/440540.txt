Gérard Kango Ouédraogo     gérard kango ouédraogo ( born 19 september 1925 ) served as prime minister of upper volta ( now burkina faso ) from 13 february 1971 to 8 february 1974. he was president of the national assembly of burkina faso from 1978 to november 25 , 1980. he was born in ouahigouya . he served in the french national assembly from 1956-1959 .
