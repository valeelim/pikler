Sulkava     sulkava is a municipality in the southern savonia region of finland . it had almost 3,000 living there in january 2014. the municipality is best known for the annual long distance rowing contest , sulkavan suursoudut , around partalansaari ( `` partala island '' ) . it is just as famous for the large party following the rowing event . the municipalities near it are juva , puumala , rantasalmi , ruokolahti and savonlinna . other websites . - the official sulkava page
