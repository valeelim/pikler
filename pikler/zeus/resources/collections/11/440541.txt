Yang Hyong-sop     yang hyong-sop ( born 1 october 1925 ) is a north korean politician . he was elected chairman of the supreme people 's assembly in 1983 , and served until 1998. he is currently vice president of the presidium of the spa . yang was born in hamhung . he attended moscow state university and kim il-sung university . he is married to kim shin-sook , a cousin of kim il-sung .
