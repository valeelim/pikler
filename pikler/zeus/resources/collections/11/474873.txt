Jean Alexander     jean hodgkinson , known by the stage name jean alexander ( 11 october 1926-14 october 2016 ) , was a english television actress . she was known for her roles as hilda ogden in the soap opera `` coronation street '' from 1964 to 1987 and as auntie wainwright in `` last of the summer wine '' from 1988 to 2010 , she died on 14 october 2016 , aged 90 .
