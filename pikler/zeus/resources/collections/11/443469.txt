Brendan Byrne     brendan thomas byrne ( born april 1 , 1924 ) is an american politician . he is a member of the democratic party . byrne served as the 47th governor of new jersey from january 15 , 1974 to january 19 , 1982. byrne was born and raised in west orange , new jersey . other websites . - new jersey governor brendan thomas byrne , national governors association - rutgers program on the governor , the brendan byrne archive
