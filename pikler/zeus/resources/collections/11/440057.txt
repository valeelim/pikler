Rebecca Adlington     rebecca `` becky '' adlington , obe ( born 17 february 1989 ) is a retired english freestyle swimmer . she won two gold medals at the 2008 summer olympics in the 400 m and 800 m. she won bronze medals in the women 's 400 m and 800 m freestyle events in the 2012 summer olympics in london . adlington retired from all competitive swimming on 5 february 2013. adlington was born in mansfield , nottinghamshire . she went to the brunts school there . in 2013 she was a contestant on `` i 'm a celebrity ... get me out of here ! `` .
