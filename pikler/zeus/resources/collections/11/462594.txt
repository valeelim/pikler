Royal Asiatic Society     the royal asiatic society ( ras ) , in full the royal asiatic society of great britain and ireland was made in 1824 , under a royal charter . the main aim of this society is to study and to do research on all kinds of subjects about asia . the members ( called 'fellows ' ) live in the united kingdom and many other countries of the world , and they write 'fras ' after their names , to show they are its members . the society also publishes the famous journal , `` journal of the royal asiatic society '' . notable fellows . - henry thomas cranbrooke - sir richard francis burton - thomas manning - sir stamford raffles - raja ram mohan roy - sir syed ahmed khan - sir aurel stein - sir wilfred thesiger - rabindranath tagore - ahmad hasan dani - eric newby - william dalrymple - omer tarin - tariq rahman - ustad ashish khan debsharma
