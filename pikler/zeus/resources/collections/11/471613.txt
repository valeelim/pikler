Magdalena Forsberg     magdalena `` magda '' forsberg ( born 25 july 1967 in ullånger , sweden as magdalena wallin ) is a swedish former cross country skier and biathlete . she won the overall biathlon world cup for six years straight between the seasons of 1996-1997 and 2001/2002 . she also won several biathlon world championships titles . she also participated in the cross-country skiing world cup between the seasons of 1987/1988 and 1995/1996 . her best results at the winter olympics were in albertville in 1992 where she finished seventh in the 4 × 5 km relay and 26th in the 15 km event .
