Marek Jakóbczak     marek jakóbczak ( 1970 – 27 june 2014 ) was a polish footballer . he graduated from a small club in warsaw pharmacy tarchomin . he played with the teams polonia warsaw , radom radomiaku , steel steel will , petrochemical plock , gks belchatow-ie , jagiellonia bialystok , silesia , wroclaw and israeli hapoel beit she'an . he had played 49 matches in the league and scored 9 goals . jakóbczak died in warsaw , poland from lung cancer , aged 44 .
