Prince Escalus     prince escalus is a character in william shakespeare 's play `` romeo and juliet '' . he is the prince of verona . he holds authority over the montague and capulet families who are . he first punishes capulet and montague for the quarrel between tybalt , benvolio , and a handful of servants . in his next appearance he is too late to stop the fatal fight between romeo and tybalt . after romeo kills tybalt , prince escalus banishes him from verona . prince escalus returns in the final scene after the suicides of romeo and juliet , and at last orders the lords of the feuding families to make peace . the two make peace . then montague promises to make a golden statue of juliet . capulet promises to place a golden statue of romeo next to the statue of his daughter .
