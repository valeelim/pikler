Lom, Norway    lom , norway lom is a municipality in oppland county , norway . it is part of the traditional region of gudbrandsdal . the administrative center of the municipality is the village of fossbergom . the municipality of lom was established on 1 january 1838. the area of skjåk was separated from lom to become a municipality of its own in 1866. lom is famous for its extensive history . it has one of the few remaining stave churches in norway . the church was built in 1158. lom sits at the base of two mountains . `` glittertind '' is and `` galdhøpiggen '' is high .
