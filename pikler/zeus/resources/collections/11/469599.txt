No Fear     no fear is an american lifestyle clothing brand . no fear was created in 1989 by mark simo , brian simo , and marty moates . their products are sold at many different store as well as many company owned store . the shirts that the company sells usually have a existential slogan or a quote that advertises the qualities of extreme sports . their brand was popular in the mid-1990s . aside from clothing , the company also sells energy drinks . they sponsor mixed martial artist urijah faber and were the official drink of world extreme cagefighting ( wec ) before they merged with ultimate fighting championship ( ufc ) . on february 25 , 2011 , no fear filed for chapter 11 bankruptcy . other websites . - official website - no fear energy drink
