Armageddon (2000)    armageddon ( 2000 ) armageddon ( 2000 ) was a professional wrestling pay-per-view show made by world wrestling federation ( wwf ) . it was held on december 10 , 2000 at birmingham-jefferson civic center in birmingham , alabama . it was the second armageddon event held by the wwf . it was the only wwf pay-per-view event to be held in the state of alabama . the main event was a six-man hell in a cell for kurt angle 's wwf championship . kurt angle defeated stone cold steve austin , triple h , the undertaker , rikishi , and the rock to retain his championship . other websites . - official armageddon 2000 website
