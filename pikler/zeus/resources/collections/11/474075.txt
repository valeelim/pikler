Susanne Alfvengren     susanne alfvengren ( born susanne irene lund , 12 february 1959 in visby , gotland , sweden ) is a swedish singer . in 1984 , she had a hit with `` när vi rör varann '' ( sometimes when we touch ) . another of her hit songs was `` magneter '' . she competed in melodifestivalen 2009 with the song `` du är älskad där du går '' , whih was knocked out of the semifinal . in 1986-1987 , she scored a vocal duet hit with mikael rickfors , with the song `` som stormen river öppet hav '' . filmography . - ps sista sommaren ( ps last summer ) , 1988 other websites . - susanne alfvengren website - svensk filmdatabas - susanne alfvengren
