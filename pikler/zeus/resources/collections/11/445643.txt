Bobcat Goldthwait     robert francis `` bobcat '' goldthwait ( born may 26 , 1962 ) is an american actor , comedian , screenwriter , and director . he is known for his energetic stage personality and his gruff , high-pitched voice . some movies that he has directed are `` sleeping dogs lie '' ( 2006 ) , `` world 's greatest dad '' ( 2009 ) and `` god bless america '' ( 2011 ) . his voice acting credits include `` hercules '' ( 1997 ) and `` leroy & stitch '' ( 2006 ) . he has also had guest appearances on television shows like `` madtv '' , `` bob 's burgers '' , `` regular show '' , `` adventure time '' , `` fish hooks '' , `` sabrina , the teenage witch '' , `` married ... with children '' and `` the simpsons '' . goldthwait was born in syracuse , new york .
