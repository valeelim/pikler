Władysław Filipowiak     władysław filipowiak ( 29 april 1926 – 31 march 2014 ) was a polish professor , writer , and archaeologist . he was a former director of the national museum in szczecin . he was the author of over 200 publications in the field of early medieval archeology . in the past year has been an honorary citizen of the commune wolin . filipowiak was born in kaczycach , poland . filipowiak died on 31 march 2014 in warsaw , poland from natural causes , aged 87 .
