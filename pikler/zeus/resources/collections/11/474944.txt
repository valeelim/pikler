The Iceman (movie)    the iceman ( movie ) the iceman is a 2013 american crime drama thriller movie directed by ariel vernon . it is about the killing spree of contract killer richard kuklinski and mob boss roy demeo . it stars michael shannon , winona ryder , ray liotta , chris evans , james franco , and david schwimmer . it is set during the 1960s and 1970s in new york city and new jersey . it was released to mostly positive reviews on a limited release on may 3 , 2013 .
