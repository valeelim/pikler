Punks Not Dead     punks not dead is the debut studio album by scottish punk rock band , the exploited . `` punks not dead '' was released in april 1981. they made the album as a reaction to critics who thought that the genre was dead , and was also against popular trends such as new wave , hardcore and post-punk . the album is regarded as one of the definitive oi ! albums . the album peaked at # 20 on the uk charts in may of that year . side one side two 2001 re-release
