Alexis Tsipras     alexis tsipras ( ; born 28 july 1974 ) is a greek politician . he became prime minister of greece on 26 january 2015. on 20 august 2015 , seven months into his term as prime minister , tsipras announced his resignation , and called for an election , to take place the following month . he was elected as prime minister once again in september 2015. tsipras is a member of the syriza party , and is a democratic socialist . a major issue of his term has been dealing with the greek debt crisis .
