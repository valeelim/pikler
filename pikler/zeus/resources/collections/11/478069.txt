Bettino Ricasoli     bettino ricasoli , 1st baron ricasoli , 1st count of brolio ( 29 march 1809 - 23 october 1880 ) was an italian statesman . he was born in florence . ricasoli served as mayor of florence from january 1848 to november of the same year . member of the `` historic right '' , after the camillo cavour 's death ricasoli became prime minister of the kingdom of italy . he was prime minister of italy from 12 june 1861 to 3 march 1862 and from 20 june 1866 to 10 april 1867. he was also twice foreign minister , twice interior minister and once minister of war.. in the last years of his life he was mayor of gaiole in chianti , a small town near siena . ricasoli died in his castle in broglio , another village near siena . ricasoli 's private life and public career were marked by the utmost integrity , and by a rigid austerity which earned him the name of the `` iron baron '' . he was also a freemason .
