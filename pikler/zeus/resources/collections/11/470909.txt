Rock Bottom: In Your House    rock bottom : in your house rock bottom : in your house was a professional wrestling pay-per-view show made by world wrestling federation ( wwf ) . it was held on december 13 , 1998 at general motors place in vancouver , british columbia . it was the twenty-sixth in your house event held by the world wrestling federation . the main event saw stone cold steve austin defeat the undertaker in a buried alive match for a spot in the 1999 royal rumble .
