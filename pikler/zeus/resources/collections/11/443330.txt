Mareike Carrière     mareike carrière ( 26 july 1954 – 17 march 2014 ) was a german actress , spokesperson and translator . she was best known for having television show appearances . she was born in hanover , lower saxony , west germany . carrière died from cancer on 17 march 2014 in hamburg , germany . she was 59 years old . she was outlived by her husband and by her brother , mathieu .
