Division of Hinkler     the division of hinkler is an australian electoral division in queensland . it was set up in 1984 and is named after bert hinkler , the great pioneer australian aviator . the division includes the towns of bundaberg , hervey bay , childers , gayndah and monto . election results . - 2004 election results - 2007 election results - 2010 election results - 2013 election results other websites . - division of hinkler ( qld ) â€” australian electoral commission - keith pitt 's website
