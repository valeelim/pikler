Vengeance Falls     vengeance falls is the sixth album by the metalcore band trivium . it was released on october 9 , 2013 in japan . it was released on october 14 in the united kingdom . it was released on october 15 in the united states via roadrunner records . it peaked at # 15 on the us billboard 200 chart . band members . - matt heafy - guitars , lead vocals - corey beaulieu - guitars - paolo gregoletto - bass guitar - nick augusto - drums , percussion
