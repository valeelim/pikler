Miangul Aurangzeb     miangul aurangzeb ( urdu/pashto : ميانگل اووررنگزيب ) ( 28 may 1928 – 3 august 2014 ) is a pakistani politician . he served in the national assembly of pakistan and as governor of balochistan and subsequently as governor of the khyber-pakhtunkhwa . aurangzeb died in islamabad , pakistan , aged 86. other websites . - miangul aurangzeb interview
