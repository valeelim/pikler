Sadashiv Amrapurkar     sadashiv amarapurkar ( born ganesh kumar nalawade ; 2 january 1950 – 3 november 2014 ) was an indian actor . he was well known for his roles in marathi and hindi movies in the 1980s and 90s . he won the filmfare award for best performance in a negative role in 1991 for `` sadak '' — the first time the award was instituted . in addition to negative roles , he has done supporting roles and , more recently , comic roles . amarapurkar died in mumbai , india from a lung infection , aged 64 .
