Sävsjö HK     sävsjö hk is a handball club in sävsjö in sweden . the club was established on 1 august 1978. the women 's team won six swedish national championship gold meals in a row between the 1993-1994 and the 1998-1999 seasons . during the 2002/2003 season the women 's team was relegated from elitserien . other websites . - official website
