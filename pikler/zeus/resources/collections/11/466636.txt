Ontario Highway 402     ontario highway 402 , also called king 's highway 402 , is a provincial highway in southwestern ontario , canada . it connects the blue water bridge in sarnia and ontario highway 401 in london . the highway runs mostly east-west . the only large municipality along the highway is strathroy-caradoc . construction on the highway started in 1939 but was stopped due to world war ii . work started again in 1952. the highway was finished in 1982. in 2009 , the westbound part of the highway near sarnia was widened from four lanes into six lanes .
