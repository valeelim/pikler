Eddie Large     eddie large ( born 25 june 1941 ) is the stage name of edward hugh mcginnis , a british comedian . he is best known as a part of the double act little and large , with syd little . they starred in the very popular british comedy series `` the little and large show '' . large was born in glasgow and raised in manchester . after being in hospital in march 2002 with kidney trouble , he underwent a heart transplant at papworth hospital , cambridge in 2003. he recovered fully . large was hospitalised for weeks in late 2013 after tripping over a road sign near his portishead , somerset home .
