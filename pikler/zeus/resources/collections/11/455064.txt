Dark nebula     dark nebulae are astronomical objects . they do not emit or reflect light . they are seen when they hide stars and galaxies behind them . the astronomer edward emerson barnard compiled a list of dark nebulae known as the `` barnard catalogue of dark markings in the sky '' , or the barnard catalogue for short . the nebulae listed by barnard have become known as barnard objects . these are the best-known dark nebulae : - coalsack nebula - cone nebula - dark doodad nebula - dark horse nebula - horsehead nebula ( barnard 33 ) - pipe nebula ( also see dark horse nebula ; includes barnard 59 , 77 and 78 ) - snake nebula ( also see dark horse nebula
