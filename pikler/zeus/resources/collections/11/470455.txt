Rainer Werner Fassbinder     rainer werner fassbinder ( ; 31 may 1945 – 10 june 1982 ) was a german movie director , screenwriter , and actor . he is one of the most important figures in the new german cinema . he was born in bad wörishofen , bavaria , germany . fassbinder died at the age of 37 from a heart attack caused by a lethal cocktail of cocaine and barbiturates in munich , germany . other websites . - fassbinder foundation
