Palaeothere     palaeotheres are an extinct group of herbivorous mammals . they are related to tapirs and rhinoceros , and probably ancestral to horses . they ranged across europe and asia during the eocene and oligocene 5528 million years ago . they were small to medium-sized . they lived in dense forests . they ate soft leaves , shoots , berries , and leaf matter picked up from the forest floor . genera : - `` palaeotherium '' - `` hyracotherium '' - `` anchilophus '' - `` lophiotherium '' - `` propachynolophus ''
