Ian Ross     ian 'roscoe ' ross ( 24 june 194030 april 2014 ) was an australian television news presenter . he worked in `` seven news '' in sydney and for the `` national nine news '' . ross was born in new south wales , australia . ross died on the gold coast , queensland , australia from pancreatic cancer , aged 73. he was divorced from his wife , by whom he had three children and he was in a relationship with a man and was bisexual . he is survived by his partner and children .
