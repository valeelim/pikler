Fairmont Pacific Rim     the fairmont pacific rim is a hotel and condominium building in vancouver , british columbia , canada . the high-end skyscraper is of 48 stories . it was opened on february 4 , 2010. it is also part of the fairmont hotels and resorts . the building is located at 1038 canada place in downtown vancouver in the coal harbour neighbourhood . it is right beside the shaw tower . it was opened just over a week before the 2010 winter olympics in vancouver . the first 22 floors make up a hotel , with the rest of the floors being condominiums . on july 13 , 2013 , `` glee '' star cory monteith , aged 31 , was found dead in his room on the 21st floor .
