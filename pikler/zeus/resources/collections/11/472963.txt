Ventnor     ventnor ( ) is a seaside resort and civil parish on the south coast of the isle of wight , england . it was established in the victorian era . ventnor lies underneath st boniface down . at it is the highest point on the isle of wight . ventnor is built on steep slopes and cliffs leading down to the sea . the higher part is called upper ventnor . the lower part is simply known as ventnor . ventnor is sometimes understood as including the coastal villages of st. lawrence to one side and bonchurch to the other . the sheltered location on the cliff of the island 's south coast means the area experiences a microclimate . it has more sunny days than much of the british isles , and fewer frosts . this has allowed many species of subtropical plant to grow here . ventnor botanic garden is particularly notable .
