Andrew Mango     andrew james alexander mango ( 1926 – 7 july 2014 ) was a turkish-british writer and photographer . he worked for the bbc for a while until his retirement in 1986. he was a press office for the british embassy in london . he spent many years writing about atatürk . references . - christopher de bellaigue , article about mango , new york review of books - finkel , caroline , `` osman 's dream '' , ( basic books , 2005 ) , 57 ; `` istanbul was only adopted as the city 's official name in 1930 .
