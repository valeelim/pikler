Wilson Harris     theodore wilson harris ( born 24 march 1921 ) is a guyanese writer . he wrote poetry , but has since become a well-known novelist and essayist . his writing style is often said to be abstract and densely metaphorical . harris is thought to be one of the most original and innovative voices in postwar literature in english . other websites . - wilson harris british council literature - wilson harris : an overview . - the wilson harris bibliography - maya jaggi , `` redemption song '' , profile of wilson harris , `` the guardian '' , 16 december 2006 . - `` caribbean review of books '' page on harris - wilson harris - an interview , by fred d'aguiar ; `` bomb '' 82/winter 2003 .
