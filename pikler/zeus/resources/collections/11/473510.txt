Saint Emiliana     saint emiliana was a christian saint . she was born in rome . her feast day is january 5. she was a mystic person ( a spiritual and faithful person ) . emiliana and her sister tharsilla were aunts of pope gregory i. their uncle was pope felix iii . they treated their house like a convent . they were spiritual and committed to god . they wanted to help the world . tharsilla died on december 24. a few days after tharsilla died , she appeared emiliana . emiliana died on january 5 .
