Naruhito, Crown Prince of Japan    naruhito , crown prince of japan naruhito , crown prince of japan is the heir to the japanese imperial throne and eldest son of the present emperor and empress of japan . he is known as simply the crown prince . he was originally known as `` his imperial highness '' the prince hiro but was later given the title of crown prince in 1991. the crown prince has one child . namely aiko , princess toshi . titles and styles . - 23 february 1960 – 23 february 1991 : `` his imperial highness '' the prince hiro - 23 february 1991 – present : `` his imperial highness '' the crown prince of japan
