GoAnimate     goanimate ( or go ! animate ) is an american cloud-based web service . it is for creating and distributing animated videos . founded by alvin hung in 2007. goanimate allows users to develop narrative videos , in which characters speak with lip synching and move around . users can also create video presentations with voices which can also move around . the service has multiple prices based on subscription level . there is a basic free version also . in october 2015 , goanimate announced it was migrating to html5 starting on january 4 , 2016. other websites . - inspiration : how people use goanimate - tutorial : how to use goanimate
