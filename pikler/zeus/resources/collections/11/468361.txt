Johan Abram Persson     johan abram persson , born 13 july 1898 , dead 24 february 1971 , was a swedish ( sami ) cross-country skier , fisherman , sloyer and wolfhunter . he was the son of per larsson and anna stina bassem from luokta lappby . he lived in skierfa near the pite river throughout his entire life . he competed for arjeplogs sk in club competitions . in international events , he participated for sweden . in 1929 , he won vasaloppet .
