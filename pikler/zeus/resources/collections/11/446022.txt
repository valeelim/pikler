Consuelo Moure     consuelo moure ( 1947 – 2 april 2014 ) was a spanish-born colombian actress . she was known for her roles in `` nochebuena '' , `` pena máxima '' , `` la esquina '' , `` tres hombres y tres mujeres '' , `` es mejor ser rico que pobre '' and in `` champagne '' . she was born in pamplona . moure died on 2 april 2014 in bogotá , colombia from lung cancer , aged 67 .
