Trophy Wife     trophy wife is an american television sitcom . it aired during the 2013–14 television season on abc . the series was co-created and executive produced by emily halpern and sarah haskins for abc studios . the series was green-lit by abc for a series order pick up on may 10 , 2013 . `` trophy wife '' premiered on september 24 , 2013. on may 8 , 2014 , abc canceled `` trophy wife '' after one season . the final episode aired the following week . the series starred malin åkerman , bradley whitford and marcia gay harden .
