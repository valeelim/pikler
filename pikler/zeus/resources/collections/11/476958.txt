Minor (law)    minor ( law ) a minor is a person under age of majority , drinking age , voting age or age of consent . they are also called underage . the age of majority depends on jurisdiction , though it is usually 18 or 21 . `` minor '' may be also used for other reasons . for example , in most of the united states , it is against the law for anyone 20 and under to drink or have alcoholic beverages , such as beer . in australia , ireland and united kingdom , the drinking age is 18. in japan , taiwan and thailand , anyone under age 20 is a minor unless emancipated .
