Mikaela Shiffrin     mikaela shiffrin ( born march 13 , 1995 ) is a american skier . her first time at the winter olympic games was during the 2014 winter olympics at sochi , russia . before these olympics , mikaela had competed in other national and international competitions . mikaela shiffrin was born on march 13 , 1995 in vail , colorado . her father , jeff , skied at dartmouth college . her mother , eileen , came from a massachusetts family who loved skiing . she has an older brother named taylor who is also a skier at the university of denver . when mikaela was eight her family moved to new hampshire . her father got a job at the dartmouth-hitchcock medical center . shiffrin competed in her first international competition at age fifteen . later that year she joined another international competition and got a gold medal in slalom . by the end of the season she had won four world cup competitions as well as a slalom title . this made her the youngest person to win a world cup title . mikaela shiffrin was also the youngest ever to win an olympic gold medal in the slalom . she won one of the five gold medals won by the american alpine team . in addition to her gold medal , shiffrin has one gold medal from the world championships and one bronze medal from the junior world ski championships . she has had eight total wins at the world championships .
