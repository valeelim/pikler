Örjan Blomquist    örjan blomquist örjan blomquist , born 23 august 1957 in bromma , sweden , dead 30 july 2000 , was a swedish cross-country skier . he was speclalized at longd-distance races during the 1980s . he competed for ifk lidingö at club level . he shared the victory at vasaloppet 1988 together with his brother anders blomquist . örjan blomquist also won worldloppet in 1985. after the career , he worked as an svt commentator during skiing events . outside skiing , he was an economist . örjan blomquist died in the year 2000 following illnes .
