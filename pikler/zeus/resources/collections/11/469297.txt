Ewa Kopacz     ewa kopacz ( born 3 december 1956 ) is the prime minister of poland since 22 september 2014. previously , she was the speaker of the lower house of parliament . from 8 november 2014 she was the leader of the platforma obywatelska ( civil platform ) party . her nomination for prime minister position is connected with donald tusk 's election for the president of the european council position . she graduated from the faculty of medicine at the university of lublin . on 12th of november she submitted the resignation of her own government . other websites . - prime minister | council of ministers | the chancellery of the prime minister
