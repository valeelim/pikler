Presbyterian Church in Canada     the presbyterian church in canada is a protestant christian denomination in canada . presbyterian churches in canada were started in the late 16th century by scottish and french huguenots immigrants . these churches became orgainized into four denominations in the early 1800s . the four denominations merged in 1875 as the presbyterian church in canada . it became one of the largets protestant denominations in english-speaking canada by the early 20th century . in 1925 , 70 % of the presbyterian congregations merged into the united church of canada . the presbyterians who did not join in the union continued using the name presbyterian church in canada . other articles . - list of presbyterian denominations in north america
