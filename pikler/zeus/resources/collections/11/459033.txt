Victoria Justice     victoria dawn justice ( born february 19 , 1993 ) is an american singer-songwriter , dancer , rapper and actress . she appeared as one of the main stars of `` zoey 101 '' . she soon had the main lead in another nickelodeon series `` victorious '' . she also appeared in the movies `` unknown '' and in `` the garden '' . in october 2013 , she obtained the lead role on mtv 's `` eye candy '' playing lindy sampson . the series premiered on january 12 , 2015 .
