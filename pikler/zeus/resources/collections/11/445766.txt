Thomas Haden Church     thomas haden church ( born thomas richard mcmillen ; june 17 , 1960 ) is an american actor , movie director and screenwriter . he first became known when co-starring in the 1990s sitcom `` wings '' . he is best known for his movie appearances including in his academy award nominated role in `` sideways '' ( 2004 ) and his role as the sandman in `` spider-man 3 '' ( 2007 ) . he directed his first , `` rolling kansas '' , in 2003. church was born in woodland , california . he has a daughter , cody ( born 2004 ) , with actress mia zottoli .
