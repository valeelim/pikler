Animorphs     animorphs is a young-adult science fiction book series . they were written by k. a. applegate and published by scholastic . it is told in the first person , from perspectives of 6 different characters ; rachel , cassie , jake , tobias , marco , and aximilli-esgarouth-isthil . a different character is featured for each book . it was published from june 1996 to may 2001. there are 54 books in the series .
