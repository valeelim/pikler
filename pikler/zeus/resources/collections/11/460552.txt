Rodney E. Slater     rodney earl slater ( born february 23 , 1955 ) is an american politician . he was the united states secretary of transportation under u.s. president bill clinton . he is a member of the democratic party slater was born in marianna , arkansas . slater graduated from eastern michigan university in 1977 , and received his juris doctor degree from the university of arkansas school of law in 1980. after clinton was elected president , 1993 slater became the first african-american director of the federal highway administration . in 1997 , slater was appointed to be the secretary of transportation . he was the second african american to hold that post .
