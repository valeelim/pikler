Lou Brissie     leland victor `` lou '' brissie ( june 5 , 1924 – november 25 , 2013 ) was an american baseball starting pitcher . he played for two major league baseball teams—the philadelphia athletics from 1947 to 1951 and the cleveland indians from 1951 to 1953. he was a symbol for the disabled and veterans after getting leg injuries in world war ii . he was born in ware shoals , south carolina . brissie died from heart and lung failure on november 25 , 2013 in augusta , georgia . he was 89 .
