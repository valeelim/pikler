Cinnamon Girl     `` cinnamon girl '' is a song first performed by neil young in 1969. it first appeared on the album `` everybody knows this is nowhere '' . the song was released in 1970. it reached # 55 on the `` billboard '' hot 100. the lyrics are about young daydreaming for a girl to love , singing he waits `` between shows '' for her . the song was also performed by hole in 1991. the smashing pumpkins recorded it in 2012 .
