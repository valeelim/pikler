Ali Hassan Mwinyi     ali hassan mwinyi ( born may 8 , 1925 ) is a tanzanian politician . he was the second president of tanzania from 1985 to 1995. before that he had been interior minister and vice president . he also was chairman of the ruling party , the chama cha mapinduzi ( ccm ) from 1990 to 1996. mwinyi was born in kivure , in the pwani region .
