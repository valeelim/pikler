Chloé Dufour-Lapointe     chloé dufour-lapointe ( born december 2 , 1991 in montreal , quebec ) is a canadian freestyle skier . she skied in the moguls events for canada at the 2014 winter olympics in sochi where she won a silver medal . her sister justine dufour-lapointe also won a gold medal in the event which was the first time that canadian sisters stood together on the podium . she also competed at the 2010 winter olympics in vancouver where she placed fifth on february 13 , 2010. other websites . - profile at the international ski federation
