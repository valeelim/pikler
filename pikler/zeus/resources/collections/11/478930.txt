Bill Monbouquette     william charles monbouquette ( august 11 , 1936 – january 25 , 2015 ) was an american professional baseball pitcher . he played in major league baseball ( mlb ) for the boston red sox ( 1958–1965 ) , detroit tigers ( 1966–1967 ) , new york yankees ( 1967–1968 ) , and the san francisco giants ( 1968 ) . he was born in medford , massachusetts . monbouquette died on january 25 , 2015 in gloucester , massachusetts , aged 78 .
