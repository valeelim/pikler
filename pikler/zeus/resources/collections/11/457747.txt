The Movie Channel     the movie channel is a premium cable channel to produce movies , and original movies . it was originally owned by independent until it was acquired by warner communications in 1975 and joint venture with american express , warner-amex satellite entertainment until it was bought by viacom in 1983 and merged with showtime to form showtime/the movie channel . in 2005 , viacom split into two companies viacom and cbs corporation . it headquarted in new york city .
