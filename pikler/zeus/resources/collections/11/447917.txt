Lamorak     sir lamorak is a fictional character in the king arthur stories . he is one of the knights of the round table . his father is king pellinore . his brothers are percival , tor , and aglovale . his cousin is sir pinel le savage . lamorak was known for his strength and fiery temper , and fought off thirty knights on at least two occasions . thomas malory refers to him as arthur 's third best knight , only lower than lancelot and tristan .
