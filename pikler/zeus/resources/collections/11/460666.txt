Northeastern Ontario     northeastern ontario is a region in the canadian province of ontario . it includes the districts of algoma , sudbury , cochrane , timiskaming , nipissing , and manitoulin . parry sound district and district municipality of muskoka are sometimes included in northeastern ontario , although they are usually included in central ontario . northeastern ontario is sometimes included with northwestern ontario as northern ontario . one differance between the two parts is that northeastern ontario has a large franco-ontarian population . almost 25 percent of northeastern ontario 's population speaks french as a first language .
