Oscar Núñez     oscar núñez ( 1929 – february 9 , 2012 ) was an argentine actor . he was known for his work in movie and theater . some of his best known movie roles were in `` nine queens '' in 2000 , `` good life delivery '' in 2004 , and `` un novio para mi mujer '' in 2008. núñez died from complications of cancer on february 9 , 2012 , at the age of 83. he was buried at the la chacarita cemetery in buenos aires .
