Grumman EA-6B Prowler     the grumman ea-6b prowler is a military aircraft . it is a version of the a-6 intruder . it is used as electronic warfare aircraft for radar jamming . it was never exported . it is used only by the us navy and the us marines corps . after 2021 , it will be replaced by boeing ea-18 growler . the ea-6b prowler was used in the vietnam war , in the gulf war , and in the war of kosovo . it was used in afghanistan 's war against al-qaeda . the aircraft can be used from an air base or an aircraft carrier .
