Harry Firth     harry l. firth ( 18 april 1918 - 27 april 2014 ) was an australian racing driver and team manager . firth was a leading race and rally driver during the 1950s and 1960s and continued as an influential team manager with first the ford works team and then the famed holden dealer team ( hdt ) well into the 1970s . he was born in orbost , victoria . firth died from natural causes , aged 96 .
