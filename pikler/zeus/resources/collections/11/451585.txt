Fareed Zakaria     fareed rafiq zakaria ( /fəˈriːd zəˈkɑriə/ ; born 20 january 1964 ) is an indian-american journalist . zakaria was born in mumbai , maharashtra , india , to rafiq zakaria and fatima zakaria . zakaria earned his bachelor of arts from yale university . zakaria has been nominated five times for the national magazine award . he lives in new york city . sheik abdul rahman
