Running (András Kállay-Saunders song)    running ( andrás kállay-saunders song ) `` running '' is a song sung by american-hungarian singer andras kalláy saunders . it was chosen to represent hungary in the eurovision song contest 2014 in denmark . the music video was set in the outskirts of budapest . the video shows a 7 year old girl who fears her abusive father . when her father comes back from work , he beats her in rage . she runs away from her home . at the same time kalláy is singing the song , a mased woman is dancing around .
