Diplodocid     diplodocids , or members of the family diplodocidae ( `` double beams '' ) , are a group of sauropod dinosaurs . the family includes some of the longest creatures ever to walk the earth , including `` diplodocus '' and `` supersaurus '' , which may have reached lengths of up to . with their peg-like teeth they could strip leaves from branches , and leave the grinding to gastroliths . they bacteria in their vast stomachs would break down the cellulose in the leaves , and the product would be absorbed further on in the alimentary canal . the diplodocids have two sub-families : 1. diplodocinae : `` diplodocus '' and other long , slender forms . 1. apatosaurinae : `` apatosaurus '' and other stockier types . their cousins the brachiosaurs are put into a sister family . the much later titanosaurs were the last group of sauropods on earth .
