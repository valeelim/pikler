Riot grrrl     riot grrrl is an underground feminist punk rock movement . it started in the early 1990s in washington . kathleen hanna 's band the bikini kill made riot grrrl famous . bratmobile was another influential riot grrrl band . riot grrrl is associated with zine culture and third wave feminism . in 1992 a zine distro called riot grrrl press was started by erika reinstein and may summer . pussy riot are a russian punk rock band that are influenced by bikini kill .
