Annie Khalid     annie khalid or just annie ( or real name noor al ain khalid ) ( born 1986 ) is a british-pakistani music star and diva . she started her career in 2006. she sings songs in urdu , punjabi and english languages . her major hit singles so far include `` mahiya '' ( 2007 ) , `` awarapan '' ( 2007 ) , `` by my baby '' ( 2010 ) , `` vari vari jawan '' ( 2011 ) and `` tharki sala '' ( 2014 ) . her best-selling album so far is `` princess '' . annie was for a short time married to a criminal multi-millionaire pakistani , settled in dubai , called malik noureed awan . but this man was very violent and he used to beat and abuse her , so she left him . she is working on a new album to be released soon .
