RoboCop 2     robocop 2 is a 1990 american science fiction action movie . it was directed by irvin kershner and starred peter weller . it is a sequel to the 1987 movie `` robocop '' . the movie is set in the near future in detroit , michigan . a sequel to this movie , `` robocop 3 '' , was released in 1993. a remake of the 1987 movie was released in february 2014 .
