Bonnie     bonnie is a given name . it is usually used for females . some males also have the name . it comes from the scottish word `` bonnie '' , which means `` pretty '' or `` attractive '' . there are several celebrities with the name , including bonnie franklin and bonnie raitt . in 1986 and 1998 , hurricanes with the name `` bonnie '' struck certain parts of the united states . the 1998 storm caused between $ 700 million and $ 1 billion in damage in north carolina . the name is still being used on atlantic hurricanes .
