Chicagoland (TV series)    chicagoland ( tv series ) `` chicagoland '' is a documentary series based in chicago , illinois . it is produced by robert redford . its pilot episode was premiered at the 2014 sundance film festival on january 19. it was aired on cnn on march 6 , 2014. the documentary is about the problems facing both the city and its mayor rahm emanuel . the series ended on april 24 , 2014 .
