Cecil D. Andrus     cecil dale andrus ( born august 25 , 1931 ) is an american politician . he was a member of the democratic party . he served as governor of idaho for 14 years . he served as governor from 1971 to 1977 and again from 1987 to 1995. he served as u.s. secretary of the interior from 1977 to 1981 , during the carter administration . further reading . - `` cecil andrus : politics western style '' ( september 1998 ) , sasquatch books , isbn 1-57061-122-x
