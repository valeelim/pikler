The Imitation Game     the imitation game is a 2014 british biographical thriller drama movie . the movie was first shown at a movie festival on august 29 , 2014. it was released on november 28 , 2014. it is about alan turing , a computer scientist who was prosecuted for his homosexuality . benedict cumberbatch plays turing . keira knightley plays joan clarke . the reviews of the movie were positive . rotten tomatoes gave it an audience rating of 94 % .
