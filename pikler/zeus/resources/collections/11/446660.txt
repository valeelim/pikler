Alton Towers     alton towers is a theme park in staffordshire , england . it is near the town alton . it has six large roller coasters and two smaller roller coasters . theme : towers roller coasters . - air - riders fly under the track . first flying roller coaster built by bolliger & mabillard . - nemesis - riders sit under the track . - oblivion - first vertical drop roller coaster . - rita - launched roller coaster . - the smiler - first roller coaster that turns riders upside down 14 times . - thirteen - riders travel forwards and backwards .
