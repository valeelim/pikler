John Baxter (footballer)    john baxter ( footballer ) john baxter ( 15 october 1936 – 12 december 2014 ) was a scottish footballer . he played as a wing half for hibernian , falkirk and clydebank during the 1950s and 1960s . he was capped once by scotland at under–23 level . he played for hibs in the 1958 scottish cup final , which hibs lost 1–0 to clyde . baxter died in december 2014 , aged 78 .
