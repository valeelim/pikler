IAR-99     the iar-99 is a military training jet aircraft . it was designed and built in romania by iar ( industria aeronautică română ) . the iar-99 was flown for the first time in 1985. it is used only by the romanian air force . bibliography . - frawley , gerard , `` the international directory of military aircraft 2002/2003 '' . london : aerospace publication , 2002. isbn 1-875671-55-2 . - gaillard , pierre , `` avions et hélicoptères militaires d'aujourd'hui '' paris , france : larivière éditions , 1999. isbn 2-907051-24-5 .
