¡Uno!    ¡uno ! ¡uno ! is the ninth studio album by american punk rock band , green day . `` ¡uno ! '' was released on september 21 , 2012. it is the first album in the ¡uno ! , ¡dos ! , ¡tré ! trilogy . the album featured the singles `` oh love '' , `` kill the dj '' , and `` let yourself go '' . lead singer and guitarist billie joe armstrong said in february 2012 that the band was in the studio , recording music for the album . he said in a statement , `` we are at the most prolific and creative time in our lives ... this is the best music we 've ever written , and the songs just keep coming . instead of making one album , we are making a three album trilogy . every song has the power and energy that represents green day on all emotional levels . we just ca n't help ourselves ... we are going epic as fuck ! ''
