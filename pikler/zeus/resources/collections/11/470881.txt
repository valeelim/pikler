Cool C     christopher douglass roney ( born december 15 , 1969 ) , known by the stage name cool c , is an american rapper . he is known today for killing a philadelphia police officer in a bank robbery in january 1996. he is currently at the pennsylvania state prison awaiting execution . he was to have been put to death by lethal injection on march 9 , 2006 , but his execution was postponed . it was scheduled for january 8 , 2015 before being postponed in december 2014 by a court order .
