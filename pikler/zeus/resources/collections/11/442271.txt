Société Bic     société bic , better known as just bic , is a consumer goods company . bic is known for their lighters , ballpoint pens , magnets , shaving razors , paper products , and watersports products . the company was created by baron marcel bich in 1945. the first product that the company made was the bic pen , known as the bic cristal . in 1958 , the company went public through a reverse merger with the waterman pen company in the united states and they later sold off the older operation . other websites . - official website
