Titanotylopus     titanotylopus is an extinct genus of terrestrial herbivore in the family camelidae . it was endemic to north america from the miocene through pleistocene 10.3 million years ago—300,000 years ago . it existed for about . its name is derived from the greek words , and — `` titan '' , `` knob '' and `` foot '' ; thus , `` giant knobby-foot .
