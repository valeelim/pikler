Svenska dansbandsveckan     svenska dansbandsveckan is a dansband music festival . it is held in the orrskogen folkpark of malung in sweden . it is held every week 29. the event was first held in 1986. usually the event is opened with the guldklaven awards being awarded . in 2012 the event was visited by totally 50 700 people . that menat a new record had been broken . official website
