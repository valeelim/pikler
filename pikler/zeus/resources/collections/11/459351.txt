Kyle Chandler     kyle martin chandler ( born september 17 , 1965 ) is an american actor . he is best known for his role as deputy jackson lamb in the movie `` super 8 '' and as hamilton jordan in the movie `` argo '' . in television shows , chandler is known for his roles in `` early edition '' as gary hobson , `` grey 's anatomy '' as dylan young , and as coach eric taylor in `` friday night lights '' . he has won the primetime emmy award for outstanding lead actor in a drama series in 2011. other websites . - kyle chandler at emmys.com
