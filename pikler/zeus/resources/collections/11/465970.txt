Citadel Center     citadel center is a 580ft ( 177m ) tall skyscraper in chicago , illinois . it was designed by spanish architect ricardo bofill . citadel center is the 44th tallest building in chicago . it was completed in 2003. it has 39 floors . the copy of the sculpture winged victory of samothrace is on display in the lobby . tenants . - citadel llc - holland & knight - perkins coie - seyfarth shaw other websites . - glass steel and stone
