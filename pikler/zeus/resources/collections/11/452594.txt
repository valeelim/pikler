United Kingdom general election, 2001    united kingdom general election , 2001 the united kingdom general election , 2001 was held on thursday 7 june 2001 to elect 659 members to the british house of commons . it was called `` the quiet landslide '' by the media , as the labour party was re-elected with another landslide result and only suffered a net loss of 5 seats , with lower turnout of 59.4 % , compared to 71.3 % in the previous election . tony blair went on to become the first labour prime minister to serve a second full term in as prime minister .
