Gisèle Casadesus     gisèle casadesus ( born 14 june 1914 ) is a french actress . she was known for her roles in `` du guesclin '' , `` verdict '' , `` the children of the marshland '' , `` what love may bring '' , and in `` my afternoons with marguerite '' . she is an honorary member of the sociétaires of the comédie-française , grand officer of the legion of honor , officer of the ordre des arts et des lettres , and `` grand-croix '' of the national order of merit . her career has lasted for nearly 80 years . other websites . - the casadesus family - gisèle casadesus at the comédie-française
