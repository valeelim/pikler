Garajonay National Park     garajonay national park ( ) is one of the national parks of spain . it is located in the center and north of the island of la gomera which is one of the canary islands off the coast of morocco . it is named for alto de garajonay which is the highest peak on the island . the park is usually covered by clouds which helps preserve over 150 species of plants . garajonay became a national park in 1981. it was declared as world heritage site by unesco in 1986. it occupies 40 km ( 15 sq mi ) and it extends into each of the six municipalities on the island . other websites . - garajonay national park - legend of gara and jonay
