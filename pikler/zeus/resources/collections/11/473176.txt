Dick Tracy (movie)    dick tracy ( movie ) dick tracy is a 1990 action movie . this movie was based on the 1930s character of the same name . the movie is about mobsters and street gangs . warren beatty plays the title character . madonna plays breathless mahoney and glenne headly plays tess trueheart . beatty also directed and produced `` dick tracy '' . this movie got mixed reviews from the critics . it was , however , a box office success . it received three academy awards . a video game based on the movie was released for the nintendo entertainment system in august 1990 .
