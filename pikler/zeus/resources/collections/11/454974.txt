Kentaro Sato     kentaro sato ( 佐藤 賢太郎 satō kentarō , born 12 may 1981 ) , aka ken-p , is a composer and conductor of media music and concert music . sato was born in hamamatsu , shizuoka , japan . he holds a master of music in conducting and a bachelor of music in media and commercial writing from california state university , northridge ( csun ) . in addition to his degree in music , he also holds a degree in cinema from santa monica college . he is famous for his works for chorus and orchestra , and video game music . other websites . - official site
