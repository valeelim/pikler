Joroinen     joroinen is a municipality in southern savonia , finland . as of january 2014 , over 5,200 people lived there . neighbouring municipalities are juva , leppävirta , pieksämäki , rantasalmi and varkaus . joroinen was established in 1631. the nearest airport is in varkaus . nearest cities . - varkaus – 16 kilometres - rantasalmi – 35 kilometres - pieksämäki – 40 kilometres - mikkeli – 70 kilometres - savonlinna – 80 kilometres other websites . - the official page
