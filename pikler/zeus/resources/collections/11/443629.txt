Bert I. Gordon     bert i. gordon ( born september 24 , 1922 ) is an american movie maker . he made many movies that are science fiction . he made his first movie when he was 32 in 1955 , called `` king dinosaur '' . in the movie , a group of astronauts go to another planet and find lots of strange creatures . in an interview with shout ! factory , he explained he always wanted to make movies ever since one of his relatives gave him a video camera . gordon had a daughter , susan . she was a child actor . she even starred in bert 's movie , `` tormented '' .
