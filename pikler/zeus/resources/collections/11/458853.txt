Vasculitis     vasculitis ( plural : `` vasculitides '' ) is a group of disorders that destroy blood vessels by inflammation . both arteries and veins are affected . lymphangitis is sometimes considered a type of vasculitis . vasculitis is primarily caused by leukocyte migration and resultant damage . although both occur in vasculitis , inflammation of veins ( phlebitis ) or arteries ( arteritis ) on their own are separate entities .
