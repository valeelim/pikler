Pietro Grasso     pietro grasso ( ; born 1 january 1945 ) , also known as piero grasso , is an italian anti-mafia magistrate and politician who has been president of the italian senate since 16 march 2013. grasso became acting president of italy on 14 january 2015 , after the resignation of president giorgio napolitano . sergio mattarella succeeded grasso as president when he was elected as the new president of italy .
