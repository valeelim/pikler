Skyfall (song)    skyfall ( song ) `` skyfall '' is a song by adele . it was used in a james bond movie also called `` skyfall '' . the tone is dark and melancholy . it received the best original song award at the 70th golden globes and 85th academy awards . the song hit # 1 on some music charts across the world . it reached the top ten in the uk , us , and canada . it was released at 0:07 british summer time on 5 october 2012 .
