Victoria's Secret    victoria 's secret victoria 's secret is an american company . it is the largest american seller of lingerie . it was founded in 1977 in san francisco . its headquarters are in columbus , ohio . the sales in 2012 were $ 6.12 billion . the company sells lingerie , women 's clothing , and beauty products through catalogs and physical stores . angels is one of victoria 's secret 's lingerie lines . it was started in 1997 with a commercial featuring helena christensen , karen mulder , daniela peštová , stephanie seymour , and tyra banks as well as rock star tom jones . the commercial was a major success . the angels began to be featured in various commercials , alongside other contract models . in february 1998 , the angels made their runway debut at victoria 's secret 's 4th annual fashion show . chandra north filled in for christensen . typically , one model is chosen among the angels to wear a bejeweled bra called the `` fantasy bra '' . it was first advertised in the victoria 's secret catalog . but since 2001 it has been worn in the fashion shows . prior to each fashion show , victoria 's secret contracts a famous jewelry designer to create the bra to promote the fashion show . the company offers the fantasy bra for sale as `` the ultimate holiday '' gift . but only the fantasy bras from the 2004 heavenly `` 70 '' fantasy bra and the 2012 floral fantasy bra and gift set have found buyers . if not bought , the bras are taken apart after a year .
