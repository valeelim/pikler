Eric F. Wieschaus     eric francis wieschaus ( born june 8 , 1947 ) is an american developmental biologist . a lot of wieschaus ' research focuses on embryogenesis in the fruit fly `` drosophila melanogaster '' and it specifically focuses on the patterning which occurs in the early drosophila embryo . in 1995 , wieschaus shared the nobel prize in physiology or medicine with edward b. lewis `` for their discoveries concerning the genetic control of early embryonic development '' .
