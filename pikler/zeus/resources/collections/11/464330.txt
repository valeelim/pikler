Jean-Jacques Pauvert     jean-jacques pauvert ( 8 april 1926 - 27 september 2014 ) was a french publisher . he was known for publishing the work of the marquis de sade in the early 1950s and as the first publisher of the `` story of o '' ( 1954 ) . he also published the first edition of kenneth anger 's `` hollywood babylone '' ( 1959 ) . pauvert was born in paris . he have also published the first french edition of the `` civil disobedience '' of henry david thoreau in 1968. pauvert died in toulon , france , aged 88 .
