Kochas     kochas is a town in the rohtas district , bihar , india . at the 2001 census , 119,576 people lived there . many of the people are associated with defence jobs . many people in kochas are dependent on agriculture and agricultural products . sometimes lack of rain creates problems for farmers . the closest railway station to kochas is in sasaram , away . there are three other railway stations in the area . kochas has everything needed to live a middle class life . kochas is famous for 1 ) kanslilla . 2 ) sweets of bhikharishah .
