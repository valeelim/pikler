Carin Mannheimer     carin mannheimer ( `` karin birgitta mannheimer '' ; `` jacobson '' 17 august 1934 – 11 july 2014 ) was a swedish dramatist , screenwriter , author and film director . mannheimer was known for her book `` rapport om kvinnor '' ( 'report on women ' ) ( 1969 ) . according to a text message sent by her daughter to the newspaper göteborgs-posten mannheimer died unexpectedly on 11 july 2014 at the age of 79 .
