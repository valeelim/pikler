Michael Dingake     michael kitso dingake ( born 11 february 1928 , bobonong ) is a botswana political activist and writer . in 1966 he was sentenced to 15 years on robben island . he was released in 1981. he worked at the university of botswana . in 1992 he entered national politics , becoming vice-president of the botswana national front in 1993 and entering the national assembly as mp for gaborone central in 1994. in 1998 he led the breakaway botswana congress party , but lost his seat to margaret nasha of the botswana democratic party in 1999. he retired from politics in 2004. since then , he became a weekly columnist for the newspaper `` mmegi '' .
