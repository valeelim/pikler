Tom Udall     thomas stewart `` tom '' udall ( born may 18 , 1948 ) is an american politician . he is the senior united states senator from new mexico and a member of the democratic party . first elected to the senate in 2008 , he represented as a member of the united states house of representatives from 1999 to 2009. he was the attorney general of new mexico from 1991 to 1999 .
