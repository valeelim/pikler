Jelly bean     jelly beans or jellybabys are a bean-shaped candy . they have a gel inside in a soft candy shell . they can come in many different flavors . they were created by william schrafft and were first introduced in 1861. schrafft wanted people to send the jelly beans that he made to soldiers during the american civil war . historians today believe that jelly beans were first linked with the celebration of easter in the united states during the 1930s . there are different brands of jelly beans , but the main brand is jelly belly . jelly beans come in a variety of different flavours . they are chewy candies that are small and gooey . there are also bean boozled , nasty jelly beans which are jelly beans with nice , and not so nice tasting ones !
