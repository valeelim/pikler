Division of O'Connor     the division of o'connor is an australian electoral division in the state of western australia . it is one of western australia 's three rural seats . it is named after c. y. o'connor , the engineer-in-chief of western australia who designed fremantle harbour and the goldfields pipeline . it was set up for the 1980 election . there were large changes to the area covered by o'connor for the 2010 election . the northern half was added to the new seat of durack . the boundary for the division moved to the east , including most of the south part of the former division of kalgoorlie . it now includes the cities of albany and kalgoorlie , and the regional towns of bridgetown , denmark , esperance , kojonup , laverton , leonora , manjimup , mount barker , ravensthorpe , southern cross , wagin and warburton . election results . - 2004 election result - 2007 election result - 2010 election result - 2013 election result other websites . - division of o'connor - australian electoral commission - rick wilson 's website
