Hell in a Cell (2014)    hell in a cell ( 2014 ) hell in a cell ( 2014 ) was a professional wrestling pay-per-view show made by wwe . it was held on october 26 , 2014 at american airlines center in dallas , texas . it was the sixth hell in a cell event held by the wwe . there were two main events on the card . the first main event saw john cena defeat randy orton to earn a future wwe world heavyweight championship match . the second main event saw seth rollins defeated dean ambrose to end their feud . other websites . - official hell in a cell 2014 website
