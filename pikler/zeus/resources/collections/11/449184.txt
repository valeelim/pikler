Basketball at the 1988 Summer Olympics     basketball in the 1988 summer olympics took place at the jamsil gymnasium in seoul , south korea . the united states won the gold medal in the women 's competition . this repeated their performance from the 1984 tournament . in the men 's tournament , the soviet union took home their second gold medal in the team 's history for this event . yugoslavia won the silver medal . the usa won the bronze medal . this was the last olympic basketball tournament where nba players were not allowed to participate . professionals from other leagues were allowed to compete . but players from other leagues were still considered amateurs . these were also the last olympic basketball tournaments where the soviets fielded a team . this was because the soviet union broke up prior to the 1992 games . the united states was a major favorite to win .
