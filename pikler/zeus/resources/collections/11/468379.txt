Ampersand     the ampersand ( `` & '' ) is a logogram . it represents the latin conjunction `` et '' , meaning `` and '' . the ampersand 's symbol is not only a logogram , but also a ligature . it joins the old handwritten latin letters `` e '' and `` t '' of the word `` et '' , so that the word is represented as a single glyph . with a c added on it means `` et cetera '' , `` and so on '' .
