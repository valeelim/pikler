Charb     stéphane charbonnier ( 21 august 1967 – 7 january 2015 ) , better known as charb , was a french cartoonist , journalist , and caricaturist . he was best known for his work on the satirical newspaper `` charlie hebdo '' . charb was born in conflans-sainte-honorine . charb was among the victims who were killed in a shooting at `` charlie hebdo '' 's paris headquarters on 7 january 2015. he was 47 .
