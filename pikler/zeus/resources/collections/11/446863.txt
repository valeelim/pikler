Lowell P. Weicker, Jr.    lowell p. weicker , jr. lowell palmer weicker , jr. ( born may 16 , 1931 ) is an american politician . he was the governor of connecticut . he served as governor from 1991 to 1995. weicker was a republican . he ran for president of the united states in 1980 , but lost the primary to ronald reagan . weicker , jr. was born in paris , france to american parents .
