Francine Lalonde     francine lalonde ( august 24 , 1940 – january 17 , 2014 ) was a canadian politician . she was a bloc québécois member of the house of commons of canada , representing the districts of la pointe-de-l'île from 2004 election to 2011 , and mercier from the 1993 election to 2004. she was also a lecturer , teacher and unionist and campaigner for assisted suicide . she was born in saint-hyacinthe , quebec . lalonde died from cancer on january 17 , 2014 in quebec . she was 73 years old . other websites . - francine lalonde at the parliament of canada website
