Curious George (movie)    curious george ( movie ) curious george is a 2006 american animated adventure movie based on the book `` curious george '' . it is directed by matthew o'callaghan . it stars will ferrell , drew barrymore , dick van dyke , david cross , eugene levy , joan plowright , michael sorich , michael chinyamurindi , clint howard , ed o'ross , and frank welker . it was released on february 10 , 2006 .
