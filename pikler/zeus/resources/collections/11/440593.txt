Grinding     grinding ( also called freaking or freak dancing ) is a dance where two or more people rub or bump their bodies against one another . it became popular in night clubs . the dance then moved into high schools across the united states and canada . schools have tried to ban grinding because it is very sexual . a dance similar to grinding is `` the bump '' , which was popular in the 1970s . another is the twerk , which became popular through the 2000s into the 2010s .
