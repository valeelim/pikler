Cars 3     cars 3 is a 2017 american movie made by pixar , directed by brian fee , and produced by kevin reher . it is the third film in the `` cars '' movie series and was released by walt disney pictures on june 16 , 2017. in the `` cars '' trilogy , `` cars 3 '' is the next movie after `` cars 2 '' , which is a sequel to the original `` cars '' movie . lightning mcqueen faces jackson storm , his high-tech rival who uses technology to win . he then makes friends with cruz ramirez , who needs training in racing , and the two learn many tricks from smokey , who once trained doc hudson ( lightning 's trainer in the original `` cars '' movie ) . lightning and cruz then go to florida to use what they learned to beat storm , and in the end , both of them win . voice cast . - owen wilson as lightning mcqueen - cristela alonzo as cruz ramirez - armie hammer as jackson storm - chris cooper as smokey - larry the cable guy as mater - bonnie hunt as sally carrera - cheech marin as ramone - jenifer lewis as flo - john ratzenberger as mack - lloyd sherr as fillmore
