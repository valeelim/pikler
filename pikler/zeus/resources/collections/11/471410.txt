'' , `` murder , she wrote '' , `` home improvement '' , `` seinfeld '' , `` boy meets world '' , `` will & grace '' , `` that '70s show '' , `` 8 simple rules '' , `` er '' , `` how i met your mother '' and `` the office '' .
