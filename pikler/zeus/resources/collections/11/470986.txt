Survivor Series (2014)    survivor series ( 2014 ) survivor series ( 2014 ) was a professional wrestling pay-per-view show made by wwe . it was held on november 23 , 2014 at scottrade center in st. louis , missouri . it was the twenty-eighth survivor series event held by the wwe . the ppv was offered for free to new subscribers of the wwe network . the main event saw team cena ( john cena , dolph ziggler , big show , erick rowan and ryback ) defeat team authority ( seth rollins , kane , mark henry , rusev and luke harper ) in a traditional 5-on-5 survivor series elimination tag team match . a stipulation was added to the match that if team authority lost , they would be removed from power . if team cena lost , every member of team cena except for cena would have been fired . the ppv was also notable for the first appearance of former wcw and tna wrestler sting on wwe , who was a long hold out on signing with the company . other websites . - official survivor series 2014 website
