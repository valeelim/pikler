OS X Yosemite     os x yosemite ( version 10.10 ) is the eighth major version of apple 's computer operating system , os x. it was announced at apple 's worldwide developers conference ( wwdc ) 2014 on june 2 , 2014 , and was released to developers and beta testers on that same day . it will be released to everyone in the fall of 2014. following the way os x mavericks was named , os x yosemite is named after yosemite national park in california .
