Norma Pons     norma delia orizi ( 18 august 1942 – 29 april 2014 ) known by her stage name norma pons , was an argentine actress and vedette . she was known for her roles in `` el hombre del año '' , `` crimen en el hotel alojamiento '' , `` adiós sui generis '' , and in `` el pozo '' . pons was born in rosario , sante fe , argentina . pons died on 29 april 2014 from a heart attack in buenos aires , argentina , aged 70 .
