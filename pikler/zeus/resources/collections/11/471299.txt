Plant physiology     plant physiology is the part of botany which studies how plants work . that is the physiology of plants . using a variety of methods , botanists study how plants reproduce , grow , get nutrients , photosynthesise , get water , and so on . the main topics of plant physiology are photosynthesis , respiration , plant nutrition , plant hormones , tropisms , nastic movements , photoperiodism , photomorphogenesis , circadian rhythms , environmental stress physiology , seed germination , dormancy , stomata function and transpiration .
