Jaroslav Šerých    jaroslav šerých jaroslav šerých ( ; 27 february 1928 – 23 march 2014 ) was a czech artist . he mainly worked as a painter , printmaker and illustrator . he was born in německý brod , czechoslovakia ( now havlíčkův brod , czech republic ) . šerých died on 23 march 2014 in prague . he was 86 years old . other websites . - jaroslav šerých works at the artbohemia website
