Make-A-Wish Foundation     the make-a-wish foundation is a non-profit organization . the organization helps children that have life-threatening medical conditions by arranging experiences known as `` wishes '' . the age limit is between 2 and a half and 18 at the time of referral . the person that decides if the child is eligible is the child 's physician . the organization is based in phoenix , arizona . david a. williams is the president and ceo of make-a-wish america . the person that holds the title of most wishes granted is professional wrestler john cena who has grants over 450 wishes . other websites . - make-a-wish foundation of america
