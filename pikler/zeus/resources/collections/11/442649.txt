Control panel (engineering)    control panel ( engineering ) a control panel is a flat surface area which has controlling or monitoring devices . control panels are used in many places to control and monitor machines . old control panels use buttons , while modern control panels use touchscreens . an automation engineer takes a mechanical device from a customer and makes it automated with the help of a control panel . the engineers in this field use autocad and other software to design their control panel before using it to make a device automated .
