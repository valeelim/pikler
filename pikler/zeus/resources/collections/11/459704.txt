Patch Adams (movie)    patch adams ( movie ) patch adams is a 1998 american semi-biographical comedy-drama movie directed by tom shadyac . it stars robin williams . it is based on the life story of dr. hunter `` patch '' adams and the book `` gesundheit : good health is a laughing matter '' by adams and maureen mylander . besides having mixed reviews , the movies was a box-office success , grossing over twice its budget in the united states alone . other websites . - the real patch adams official web site - patch adams the film vs the real patch adams
