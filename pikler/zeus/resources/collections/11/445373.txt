Checkers (restaurant)    checkers ( restaurant ) checkers drive in restaurants is one of the largest chains of double drive-thru restsurants in the united states . it became a private company in june 2006 by merging with taxi holdings . the company operates checkers and rally 's restaurants . checkers was founded in 1985. rally 's was founded in 1986. they are called two different names in south and midwest . they serve cajun french fries and burgers .
