Jürg Neuenschwander (organist)    jürg neuenschwander ( organist ) jürg neuenschwander ( 14 january 1947 – 23 march 2014 ) was a swiss organist and musician . he was best known for his work with schweizer radio drs from 1982 to 1989. he was born in eggiwil , canton of bern . neuenschwander died from a cerebral hemorrhage on 23 march 2014 in bern . he was 67 years old .
