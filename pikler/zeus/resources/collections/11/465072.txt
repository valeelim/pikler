M-60 tank     the m60 patton is a main battle tank ( mbt ) introduced in december 1960. the m60 became the army 's primary tank during the cold war . when the soviet union got the t-54 tank , the united states decided to upgrade the m48 patton tank . the armor was improved . it was fitted with the british l7 105 mm tank gun . the army also decided to switch from gasoline to diesel .
