Karen Lewis     karen lewis ( born june 26 , 1953 ) is an american chicago public schools teacher and labor leader . she is the current president of the chicago teachers union . she is a former king college prep high school and lane technical high school chemistry teacher . she was a teacher for 22 years . lewis was considering running for mayor of chicago in 2015. in july 2014 poll , lewis defeated emanuel in a hypothetical electoral contest by 45-36 . she decided not to run for mayor due to health issues and endorses chuy garcia . on october 9 , 2014 , lewis was hospitalized for a `` serious illness '' . no further details about lewis ' health have been revealed . on october 13 , a source confirmed that lewis had been diagnosed with a cancerous brain tumor .
