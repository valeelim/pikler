Keith Carradine     keith ian carradine ( born august 8 , 1949 ) is an american movie and television actor and singer . he is known for his roles as tom frank in robert altman 's `` nashville '' , wild bill hickok in the hbo series `` deadwood '' and fbi agent frank lundy in `` dexter '' . in 1976 , carradine won the academy award for best original song for `` i 'm easy '' . that song was in the movie `` nashville '' . other websites . - official site mindgame - `` broadwayworld.com '' interview with keith carradine , october 16 , 2008
