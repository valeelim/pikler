Seppo Kuusela     seppo ilmo kuusela ( 25 february 1934 – 16 october 2014 ) was a finnish basketball player and coach . a 6 ft 0 in ( 1.83 m ) point guard and shooting guard , he played professionally between 1952 and 1969. he played for both pantterit and tapion honka ( now known as espoon honka ) , as well as for finland 's national basketball team . from 1970 until 1992 , he coached espoon honka . kuusela was born in helsinki . he grew up in töölö . kuusela died on 16 october 2014 at the age of 80. other websites . - seppo kuusela at the finish basketball association ( fba ) webpage
