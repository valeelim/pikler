Gang of Four     the gang of four ( ) was the name given to a political group of four chinese communist party officials . the leader of the group was jiang qing , mao zedong 's last wife . the other three were zhang chunqiao , yao wenyuan and wang hongwen . they became powerful during china 's cultural revolution . after the death of mao zedong , jiang quing was plotting a coup d'Ã©tat to take over power . she and her close associates in the gang of four were arrested , put on trial . jiang qing and zhang chunqiao received death sentences that were later changed to life imprisonment . wang hongwen and yao wenyuan were given life and twenty years in prison , respectively . all members of the gang of four have since died . jiang qing committed suicide in 1991 , wang hongwen died in 1992. yao wenyuan and zhang chunqiao died in 2005 : they had been released from prison in 1996 and 1998 respectively .
