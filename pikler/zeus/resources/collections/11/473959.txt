Sonja Aldén     sonja aldén , born 20 december 1977 , in st albans , england , uk ) is a swedish singer . in 2006 , 2007 and 2012 she participated at melodifestivalen . she has also worked together with shirley clamp and sanna nielsen . also , she has written songs for other artists , like hard rock/heavy metal band the poodles , who also participated in the swedish melodifestivalen 2006 with the song `` night of passion '' . she has attended the adolf fredrik 's music school in stockholm . discography . singles . - as sanna , shirley , sonja other websites . - official website ( in swedish )
