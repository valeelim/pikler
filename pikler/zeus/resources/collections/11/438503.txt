Hämeenkoski     hämeenkoski is a municipality in päijänne tavastia , finland . almost 2,100 people lived there as of january 2014. nearby municipalities include asikkala , hausjärvi , hollola , hämeenlinna and kärkölä . until the 18th century , hämeenkoski was called etola . after that , its name was koski hi . the name hämeenkoski has been used since 1995. etola ( hämeenkoski ) , hankala , huhti , huljala , hyrkkälä , hyväneula , järvenpää ( hämeenkoski ) , kaunkorpi , koski ( hämeenkoski ) , käikälä , leiniälä , miehola , padonmaa ( palomaa ) , porvola , putula , pätilä , saapas , tenhiälä , toijala ( hämeenkoski ) and töykkylä . other websites . - community website
