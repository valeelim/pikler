Metro Manila     metropolitan manila ( ) , commonly known as metro manila , is the national capital region ( ncr ) of the philippines . it is the seat of government . it has more people than most regions and metropolitan areas of the country . there are 16 cities in metro manila . they include manila , caloocan , las piñas , makati , malabon , mandaluyong , marikina , muntinlupa , navotas , parañaque , pasay , pasig , quezon city , san juan , taguig , and valenzuela , as well as the municipality pateros . related articles . - list of roads in metro manila
