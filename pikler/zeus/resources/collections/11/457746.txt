Warner-Amex Satellite Entertainment     warner-amex satellite entertainment was a media company is formed in new york city in 1974 as a joint venture between time warner and american express to produce television networks including mtv , nickelodeon , showtime , and the movie channel . in 1983 , it was sold to viacom and showtime and the movie channel merged to form showtime/the movie channel and in 1985 it was sold again to viacom and renamed mtv networks .
