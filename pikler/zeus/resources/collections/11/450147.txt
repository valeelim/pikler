Dave Franco     david john `` dave '' franco ( born june 12 , 1985 ) is an american television and movie actor . he is best known for his roles in `` 21 jump street '' , `` charlie st. cloud '' , `` fright night '' , `` now you see me '' , `` warm bodies '' , `` scrubs '' , and in `` neighbors '' . he is the younger brother of actor james franco and tom franco .
