West End of London     the west end of london ( usually referred to as the west end ) is an area of central london . it contains many of the city 's major tourist attractions , shops , businesses , government buildings , and entertainment venues ( including the commercial : west end theatres ) . use of the term began in the early 19th century to describe fashionable areas to the west of charing cross . for strategic planning , the area is one of two `` international centres '' in the london plan . the west end is the second most expensive place in the world in which to rent office space , after tokyo .
