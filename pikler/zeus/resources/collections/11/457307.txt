John Guare     john guare ( rhymes with `` air '' ; born february 5 , 1938 ) is an american playwright . he is best known as the author of `` the house of blue leaves '' , `` six degrees of separation '' , and `` landscape of the body '' . guare was born in new york city and raised in jackson heights , queens . he was raised a roman catholic . other websites . - biography at theatredatabase.com - john guare with poster for his caffe cino production - john guare papers at yale university beinecke rare book and manuscript library .
