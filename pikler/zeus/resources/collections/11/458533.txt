Uhm Ji-won     uhm ji-won ( born december 25 , 1977 ) is a south korean actress . she was known for her roles in `` the scarlett letter '' , `` traces of love '' , `` the good , the bad , and the weird '' , `` like you know it all '' , and in `` hope '' . uhm married architect oh young-wook on may 27 , 2014 at the shilla hotel in seoul .
