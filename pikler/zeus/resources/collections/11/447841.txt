Calf roping     calf roping is a sport where a person chases a calf on horseback . the person ropes the calf , then jumps off their horse and ties the calf 's one front foot and two hind feet with a `` piggin ' rope '' . then they must give the calf some slack and the rope must stay tied for 6 seconds . calf roping started with cowboys . they had to rope and tie down calves to brand them and doctor them .
