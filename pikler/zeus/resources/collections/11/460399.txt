Ann-Christine Bärnsten     margareta `` ann-christine '' bärnsten , born 10 july 1957 , is a swedish singer and writer . she has written several detective stories . as a singer , she participated at melodifestivalen 1975 with the song `` ska vi plocka körsbär i min trädgård '' . the song ended up 5th . svensktoppen hit songs . - `` ska vi plocka körsbär i min trädgård '' – 1975 bibliography . - döden är en schlager - 2005 - gröna villan - 2007
