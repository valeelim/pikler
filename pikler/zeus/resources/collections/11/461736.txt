Atlético Nacional     club atlético nacional s.a. , also known as simply atlético nacional , is a professional colombian football team based in medellín . they are one of the most successful and popular football teams in colombia . they play their home games at the atanasio girardot stadium . they share the stadium with local rivals , independiente medellin . the club is one of only three teams to play in every first division tournament in colombia , like the other two teams which are millonarios and santa fe . the team was founded on 7 march 1947 as `` club atletico municipal de medellin '' by `` luis alberto villegas lopez .
