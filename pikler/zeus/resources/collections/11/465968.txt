Mary Beckett     mary beckett ( 1926-2013 ) was an irish author . she was born in belfast in northern ireland . she used to work for bbc northern ireland , where she wrote plays for the radio . she also wrote short stories . she also worked as a teacher . she was married and had five children . she stopped publishing writing until the 1980s , when she wrote `` the belfast woman '' . in addition to short stories , she also wrote children 's books and one novel . other websites . - 1989 la times review of `` a belfast woman ''
