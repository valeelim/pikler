Trainspotting (movie)    trainspotting ( movie ) trainspotting is a 1996 british crime comedy drama movie . it is based on the novel of the same name . this movie is about heroin addicts in late 1980s edinburgh . other themes in this movie are urban decay and poverty . kelly macdonald plays diane . ewan mcgregor plays mark renton . this movie was released in february 1996 in the united kingdom , and july 1996 in the united states . a sequel , `` trainspotting 2 '' , is being filmed .
