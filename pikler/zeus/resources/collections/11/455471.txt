Natalie Tran     natalie tyler `` nat '' tran ( born 24 july 1986 ) is an australian vlogger , actress , comedian and writer . she is best known for her youtube channel `` communitychannel '' . tran makes `` videos that are a combination of monologue and sketches that focus on the humourous aspects of everyday life . '' as of june 2014 , her channel has over 1.5 million subscribers . she has a signature sign-off that is `` your mum rates me . '' she made her first movie role was as helen in `` goddess '' ( 2013 ) . tran was born in sydney , new south wales . her parents are from vietnam .
