Brooke White     brooke white ( born june 2 , 1983 ) is an american indie pop singer and songwriter . she was the fifth place finalist on the 7th season of `` american idol '' . her first album , `` songs from the attic '' , was released in 2005. her first post-idol record , `` high hopes & heartbreak '' , was released in 2009. white was born in phoenix , arizona . she was raised in mesa , arizona .
