United States Attorney     united states attorneys , aka federal prosecutors or as united states district attorneys , represent the united states federal government . they do this in u.s. district courts and u.s. courts of appeals . there are 93 u.s. attorneys in united states , puerto rico , the u.s. virgin islands and guam . u.s. attorneys and their offices are a part of united states department of justice . the office of the united states attorney was created by judiciary act of 1789 .
