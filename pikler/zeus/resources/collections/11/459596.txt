NBC Tower     the nbc tower is an office tower on the near north side of chicago , illinois , united states located at 454 north columbus drive ( 455 north cityfront plaza is also used as a vanity address for the building ) in downtown chicago 's magnificent mile area . it was completed in 1989 , the 37-story building reaches a height of 627 feet ( 191 m ) . nbc 's chicago offices , studios , and owned-and-operated station wmaq-tv are located here as of 1989. formerly its former radio sister wmaq/wscr was located here . wsns-tv is also located here . other websites . - emporis listing - official website - skidmore , owings , and merrill - wmaq 's official website - wscr 's official website
