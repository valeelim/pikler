Republic of Molossia     molossia , also known as the self-proclaimed republic of molossia , is a micronation , founded by kevin baugh and headquartered in his home near dayton , nevada . it is made up of baugh 's house ( known as the government house ) , about in size , as well as of surrounding property , enclaved by nevada . it formerly claimed a property in pennsylvania as well . according to author john ryan , molossia is `` a hobby that has been pushed to the nth degree . '' originally established as a childhood project in 1977 , molossia subsequently evolved into a territorial entity in the late 1990s . the name `` molossia '' is derived from the spanish word `` morro '' which means `` small rocky hill '' . baugh has stated that the ancient greek tribe of molossians is unrelated . other websites . - the republic of molossia official website
