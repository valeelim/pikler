Old Earth creationism     old earth creationism is a term that covers many different types of creationism . 1. some old earth creationists believe god did each act of creation in a day but there were long breaks between each act of creation . 1. other old earth creationists believe days mentioned in genesis are symbolic and really mean much longer time . 1. other old earth creationists believe god created life gradually over thousands of millions of years . 1. other old earth creationists believe god created life through theistic evolution . old earth creationism typically agrees better with what scientists believe about physics , chemistry , geology and the age of the earth , than young earth creationism .
