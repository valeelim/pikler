Thomas P. O'Neill III     thomas phillip o'neill iii ( born september 20 , 1944 ) is an american politician and lawyer . he leads a public relations and government affairs firm called o'neill and associates in boston . he is the son of thomas phillip `` tip '' o'neill , jr. , who served as speaker of the united states house of representatives from 1977 to 1987. from 1975 to 1983 , o'neill served as lieutenant governor of the commonwealth of massachusetts .
