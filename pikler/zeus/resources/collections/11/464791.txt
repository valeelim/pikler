Losing My Religion     `` losing my religion '' is a 1991 rock song from r.e.m.. the song became r.e.m . 's highest charting song for the united states . it hit # 4 on `` billboard '' hot 100. it was nominated for several grammy awards . it won two . the song was released on february 19 , 1991. r.e.m . guitarist peter buck wrote the riff and chorus to the song on a mandolin while learning to play it . buck had just bought the instrument and was recording the music as he practiced . buck said that `` when i listened back to it the next day , there was a bunch of stuff that was really just me learning how to play mandolin , and then there 's what became 'losing my religion ' , and then a whole bunch more of me learning to play the mandolin .
