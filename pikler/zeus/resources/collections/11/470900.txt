Hubert Lampo     hubert leon lampo ( 1 september 1920 , antwerp – 12 july 2006 , essen ) was a flemish writer . his father worked at the post office and his mother was a teacher . in 1938 he became himself a teacher . later he became national inspector of the flemish libraries . he is the father of jan lampo . after johan daisne , he is one of the founders of the magic realist literary movement in flanders . works . novels . - don juan en de laatste nimf ( 1943 ) - hélène defraye ( 1945 ) - triptiek van de onvervulde liefde ( 1947 ) - de ruiter op de wolken ( 1948 ) - idomeneia en de centaur ( co-author ben van eysselstein ) ( 1951 ) - de belofte aan rachel ( 1952 ) - terugkeer naar atlantis ( 1953 ) - het zwarte sterrenbeeld ( radio play ) ( 1953 ) - de duivel en de maagd ( 1955 ) - jan vaerten ( 1955 ) - de geliefden van falun ( 1959 ) - de komst van joachim stiller ( 1959 ) - hermione betrapt ( 1962 ) - dochters van lemurië ( 1964 ) - de heks en de archeoloog ( 1967 ) - de goden moeten hun getal hebben ( 1969 ) - omnibus ( 1971 ) - de vingerafdrukken van brahma ( 1972 )
