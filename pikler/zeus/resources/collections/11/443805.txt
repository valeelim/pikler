Boris Pustyntsev     boris pavlovich pustyntsev ( ; 2 june 1935 – 4 march 2014 ) was a russian human rights activist . he was best known as chairman of the charitable organisation st. petersburg ngo . pustyntsev was born in vladivostok , primorsky krai , then soviet union . his father was pavel pustyntsev ( 1910–1977 ) . pustyntsev died after a long illness on 4 march 2014 in saint petersburg . he was 78. other websites . - boris pustyntsev at citizens watch
