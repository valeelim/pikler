Step Up All In     step up all in is an american 2014 romantic and musical drama movie . the movie is about a dance crew that are rejected at every audition they go to . the crew decide to leave los angeles and come back to miami . briana evigan plays andie . alyson stoner plays camille . this movie was released on august 8 , 2014. it got mixed reviews from critics . it made more than $ 86 million in the box office .
