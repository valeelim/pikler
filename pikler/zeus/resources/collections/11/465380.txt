Heritage of Mercury. Almadén and Idrija    heritage of mercury . almadén and idrija heritage of mercury . almadén and idrija is a joint unesco world heritage site in almadén , spain , and idrija , slovenia . the property includes two mercury mining sites . in almadén mercury has been extracted since antiquity , while in idrija it was first found in 1490 ad . the almadén site includes buildings relating to its mining history . these include retamar castle , religious buildings and traditional houses . the site in idrija notably features mercury storage and infrastructure . it also includes miners ’ living quarters , and a miners ’ theatre . the sites shows how trade in mercury generated important exchanges between europe and america over the centuries . the two sites represent the two largest mercury mines in the world and were working mines until recent times .
