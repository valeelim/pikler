Christine Charbonneau     christine charbonneau ( 18 october 1943 – 29 may 2014 ) was a canadian singer and songwriter . she worked with celine dion during her thirty-year career . in 1975 , charbonneau recorded her first album `` censuré '' . she made her first appearance in 1973. charbonneau was born in montreal , quebec , canada . he died from cancer , aged 70 .
