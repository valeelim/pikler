Go Deep     `` go deep '' is a pop song from singer janet jackson . the song is about jackson 's plan to go to a nightclub to find a man to have sex with . the song has many remixes . the original recording of this song is four minutes and 42 seconds . the longest version is ten minutes and 53 seconds . the single was given mixed reviews from music critics . it did well in many countries . `` go deep '' was released on june 15 , 1998 .
