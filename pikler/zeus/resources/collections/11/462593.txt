Indian Military Historical Society     the indian military historical society is an organisation in the united kingdom ( uk ) .it was made in 1983. the main purpose of the society is to encourage research and study of the history of the old colonial british indian army , although some study is also done on the present day indian army and pakistan army too . members of this organisation belong to the uk , and many countries from the british commonwealth of nations . it also publishes a well-known journal , `` durbar '' , in which many historians and scholars print their research work .
