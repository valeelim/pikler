Argument from morality     an argument from morality is an argument for the existence of god . also called moral arguments , most are apologetic arguments . they argue there is a moral order in the universe and so there must be a god . german philosopher immanuel kant devised an argument from morality based on reason . kant argued that the goal of humanity is to achieve perfect happiness and virtue . further , that an afterlife must exist in order for this to be possible , and that god must exist to provide this .
