Pawleys Island, South Carolina    pawleys island , south carolina pawleys island is a seaport town in northeastern south carolina . in 2000 , 138 people lived there . this town is near georgetown . many cypress cottages on pawleys island were built during the 20th century . that made people call the town `` arrogantly shabby '' . in september 1989 , hurricane hugo struck south of pawleys island . some cottages were totally destroyed .
