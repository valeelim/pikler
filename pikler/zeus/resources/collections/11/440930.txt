Piero Tosi     piero tosi ( born april 10 , 1927 ) is an italian costume designer . he was known for his works in `` senso '' , `` bellissima '' , `` the leopard '' , `` yesterday , today and tomorrow '' , `` death in venice '' , `` the night porter '' , and `` la traviata '' . he won the david di donatello for best costumes twice . he also won the bafta award for best costume design twice , and has been nominated for the academy award for costume design five times .
