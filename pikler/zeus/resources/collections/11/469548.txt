Rebbeca Oswald     rebbeca oswald is a noted pianist . she is also an award winning composer . oswald has a wide body of work . her work includes concert music for chamber ensembles , chamber orchestras , full orchestras , and single instruments of many kinds . she has also composed music for some choral ensembles . oswald has written and produced music for two video games . she also has composed and produced music for a three-hour documentary . she also writes in the classical and popular genres . oswald advanced to the finalist category level of the american prize in composition in the choral category . kathy parsons in a review at monthlypiano.com states while reviewing the piece , `` whereas '' , `` i heard some of these pieces live a few months ago , but even that didn ’ t prepare me for the soulful beauty of the recording . '' parsons also stated in the review that , `` the thirteen original piano solos are oswald ’ s creative response to a gift of unpublished personal poems that express “ the joy of unity , the sorrow of separation , the desire to know and be known , the bliss of loving and being loved . ( as she quoted from the liner notes . )
