La Gata     la gata , is a mexican telenovela produced by nathalie lartilleux for televisa . it is a remake of the venezuelan telenovela `` la gata '' , produced in 1968 and mexican telenovela `` rosa salvaje '' produced in 1987. maite perroni and daniel arenas stars as the protagonists , with erika buenfil as the adult protagonist . jorge poza , mónika sánchez and first actress laura zapata stars as the antagonists . `` la gata '' premiered on may 5 , 2014 , and earned 17.7 points of rating in its 4 p.m. time slot . it was the third highest rated telenovela of the evening on canal de las estrellas .
