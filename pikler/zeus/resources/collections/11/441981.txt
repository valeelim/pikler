The Free Lance–Star     the free lance–star is a newspaper based in fredericksburg , virginia . the newspaper is published daily and serves many counties surrounding fredericksburg . `` the free lance '' was first published on january 27 , 1885. in 1900 , the `` free lance '' operation merged with its competitor , `` the fredericksburg daily star '' . the two merged newspapers changed the name to `` the free lance–star '' . other websites . - official site - online version of print newspaper
