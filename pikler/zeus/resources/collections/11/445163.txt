Gonzalo Anes     gonzalo anes álvarez de castrillón ( 10 december 1931 – 31 march 2014 ) was a spanish economist , professor and historian . he was the marquis of castrillon from 2010 until his death in 2014. he was also a director of the royal academy of history . he was born in trelles , coaña , asturias . anes died on 31 march 2014 in madrid . he was 82 years old . other websites . - gonzalo anes at the royal academy of history
