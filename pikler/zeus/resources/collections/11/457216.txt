Carl Neumann Degler     carl neumann degler ( born 6 february 1921 ) is an american historian . degler served as president of the organization of american historians , president of the american historical association and president of the southern historical association . he is the margaret byrne professor of american history emeritus at stanford university . degler was born in orange , new jersey . other websites . - `` in pursuit of an american history '' 1986 aha presidential address - the pulitzer prizes
