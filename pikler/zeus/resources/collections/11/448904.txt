What I Got     `` what i got '' is the first single off american ska punk band sublime 's third and final studio album `` sublime '' . the song was released on august 27 , 1996. it is the band 's biggest radio hit and was one of the most popular singles of 1997 in the united states . when the song was released , it was able to reach # 1 on the modern rock tracks chart and was also a mainstream top 40 hit , reaching # 29 on the billboard hot 100 airplay . the song was ranked # 83 in `` rolling stone '' 's `` 100 greatest guitar songs of all time '' . the song was featured in the 2010 video game `` rock band 3 '' , the 2011 video game `` '' , the 2000 video game `` dave mirra freestyle bmx '' , and the 2009 video game `` guitar hero 5 '' .
