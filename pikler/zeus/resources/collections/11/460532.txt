Bruce Boxleitner     bruce william boxleitner ( born may 12 , 1950 ) is an american actor and science fiction and suspense writer . he is known for his leading roles in the television series `` how the west was won '' , `` bring 'em back alive '' , `` scarecrow and mrs. king '' ( with kate jackson ) , and `` babylon 5 '' . he is also known for his dual role as the characters alan bradley and tron in the 1982 walt disney pictures movie `` tron '' , a role which he reprised in the 2010 sequel , ' and the animated series ' .
