Ángel Di Maria    ángel di maria angel di maria ( born 14 february 1988 ) is an argentine professional footballer . he plays for french league ligue 1 club paris saint-germain f.c . and the argentina national team . he can play as either a winger or attacking midfielder . after beginning his career with rosario central , di maría moved to europe in 2007 to play for benfica of portugal . he earned a €25 million move to real madrid three years later . after winning the champions league with real madrid , he signed for manchester united in 2014 for a national record £59.7 million . in summer 2015 , he signed a 4 year contract with paris saint-germain f.c . for €63 million . a full international for argentina since 2008 , di maría has earned over 50 caps for his country . he scored the goal that won the country first place at the 2008 olympics , and has also represented them at two world cups and the 2011 copa américa .
