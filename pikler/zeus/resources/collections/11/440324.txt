Joe Shuster     joseph shuster ( july 10 , 1914 – july 30 , 1992 ) was an canadian-american cartoonist . shuster along with jerry siegel created `` superman '' . it proved one of the most popular and influential comic book hero in comic book history . shuster died of a heart failure and hypertension in los angeles , california , aged 78. other websites . - comic art & graffix gallery – artist biography - additional webcitation archive , aug 13 , 2008 .
