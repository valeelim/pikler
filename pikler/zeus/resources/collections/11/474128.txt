Anders Wejryd     anders harald wejryd , born 8 august 1948 in falköping , sweden is a swedish lutheran clergyman . he was appointed bishop of växjö in 1995. in march 2006 , he was elected archbishop of uppsala and primate of the church of sweden . he took office in september of the same year . he left office in 2014 , and was replaced with antje jackelén .
