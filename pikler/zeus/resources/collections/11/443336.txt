Cocteau Twins     cocteau twins were a scottish rock band . robin guthrie and will heggie started the band in 1979 in grangemouth . the band was named after the song `` the cocteau twins '' by johnny and the self-abusers . the band 's first album `` garlands '' was released by 4ad records in 1982. in 1996 the band released their last album `` milk and kisses '' . in 1997 the band broke up .
