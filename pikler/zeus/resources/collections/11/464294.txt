Colisée Pepsi     the colisée pepsi ( english : pepsi coliseum ) , formerly the colisée de québec ( english : quebec coliseum ) is a multi-purpose arena in quebec city , quebec . the arena opened on december 8 , 1949 , and is the current home to the quebec remparts of the quebec major junior hockey league ( qmjhl ) . it was also the home of the quebec nordiques of the world hockey association ( wha ) and national hockey league ( nhl ) from 1972 until 1995 when they relocated to denver , colorado to become the avalanche . the 1971 memorial cup championship series was hosted at the arena and saw the remparts defeat the edmonton oil kings two games to none . the first game of the 1974 summit series between the canadian wha all-stars and the soviet national team was held at the coliseum , as well as one game in each of the 1976 and 1991 canada cups . other websites . - official website
