Sidesaddle     riding sidesaddle is a type of horseback riding . sidesaddle riding uses a special saddle . this saddle allows the rider to sit 'aside ' rather than 'astride ' a horse . astride means the rider 's legs are opened with one leg on each side of the horse . in sidesaddle , the rider 's legs are basically closed and both legs are on the same side of the horse . girls and women mostly ride sidesaddle . riding sidesaddle allows them to use a modest posture while showing off their nicest riding habits . there are two categories for competition , junior and senior classes . women sitting aside a horse to ride began in ancient history . it later became popular in european countries in the middle ages . this gave women wearing skirts a way to ride a horse in a modest way while also wearing their fancy clothes . it is still popular with some modern riders .
