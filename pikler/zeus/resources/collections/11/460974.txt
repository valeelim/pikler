Sunda Islands     the sunda islands are part of the malay archipelago . they are divided into the greater sunda islands and the lesser sunda islands . sumatra , java , borneo and sulawesi make up the greater sunda islands . the lesser sunda islands consist of many smaller islands . today , the sunda islands are divided between indonesia , malaysia , brunei and east timor . the wallace line also runs through the sunda islands . it is the biological division between the australian fauna and flora , and that of southeast asia .
