Bradford Forster Square railway station     bradford forster square is one of two main railway stations in bradford , west yorkshire . the other one is bradford interchange . the railway station was moved to its current place in the early 1990s . before that , it was next to where it is now . in the old place , it was much bigger . a little while after the move , the line was made electric . the station has some service to london king 's cross . it has regular service to leeds , skipton and ilkley . for most long distance service , passengers must change at leeds .
