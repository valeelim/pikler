International Institute of Hotel Management     iihm or international institute of hotel management is a chain of hospitality and hotel management schools . it was founded in 1994 at kolkata , india . iihm has campus sites at kolkata , bangalore , delhi , pune , hyderabad , jaipur , ahmedabad and in bangkok , thailand . the school offers a bachelor 's degree in international hospitality management . a large number of student study at these campuses . students also may complete their degree at edinburgh napier university , edinburgh or the university of west london , in london .
