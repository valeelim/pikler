Regina Cyclone     the regina cyclone is a popular name for a tornado which hit regina , saskatchewan , canada . it killed 28 people , making it the deadliest canadian tornado . it started about 4:50 p.m. on june 30 , 1912 , forming 18 kilometres ( 11 miles ) south of the city . the tornado move 12 kilometres ( 7.5 miles ) north before dissipating . it was approximately 150 metres ( 490 feet ) wide . the tornado was estimated to have been an f4 on the fujita scale . it killed 28 people and left 2,500 homeless . it caused cd $ 4.5 million in damages . related pages . - list of canadian tornadoes and tornado outbreaks
