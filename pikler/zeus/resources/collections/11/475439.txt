Municipalities of Slovenia     slovenia is divided into 212 municipalities ( , singular : ) , of which 11 ( in bold ) are cities ( `` cities municipalities '' ) . slovene is an official language of all the municipalities . hungarian is a second official language of 3 municipalities in the prekmurje region : dobrovnik/dobronak , hodoš/hodos and lendava/lendva . italian is a second official language of 4 municipalities ( of which one has urban status ) in the slovene littoral region : ankaran/ancarano , izola/isola , koper/capodistria and piran/pirano . the largest municipality in area is kočevje with and the smallest is odranci with only . the municipality with the highest population is ljubljana , the capital of slovenia , and the one with the lowest population is hodoš with only 382 people living there . the slovene names have the word `` občina '' in front , e.g . `` občina bled '' . as an english name , the statistical office of slovenia uses a word by word transformation , e.g . `` municipality bled '' .
