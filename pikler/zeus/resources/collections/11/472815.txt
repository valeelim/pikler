Colonel Tom Parker     colonel thomas andrew `` tom '' parker ( june 26 , 1909january 21 , 1997 ) born andreas cornelis ( `` dries '' ) van kuijk , was a dutch-born entertainment impresario . he was best known as the manager of elvis presley . for many years parker falsely claimed to have been u.s.-born , but it eventually emerged that he was born in breda in the netherlands .
