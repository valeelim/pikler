Billy Bathgate     billy bathgate is a 1989 novel by author e. l. doctorow that won the 1989 national book critics circle award for fiction for 1990 , the 1990 pen/faulkner award for fiction , the 1990 william dean howells medal , and was the runner up for the 1990 pulitzer prize and the 1989 national book award . the story is told in the first person by billy `` bathgate '' behan , a fifteen-year-old boy who first becomes the gofer and then surrogate son of mobster dutch schultz .
