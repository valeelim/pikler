Carl Johan Thyselius     carl johan thyselius , born 8 june 1811 , dead 11 january 1891 , was a swedish politician , government official . he served as justice councillor ( 1856-1860 ) , `` minister for ecclesiastical affairs '' ( 1860-1863 ) , minister for civil affairs ( responsible for trade , industry and ship transport ; ( 1875-80 ) . he , served as the prime minister from 1883 to 1884 .
