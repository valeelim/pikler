Festspielhaus Baden-Baden     the festspielhaus baden-baden is germany 's largest opera house . it is the second largest opera house in europe . it has 2,500 seats . the festspielhaus baden-baden is in baden-baden at the place of the former railroad station . the historic station building was combined into the festspielhaus and is now the entrance building . the festspielhaus was opened in 1998. after a difficult starting time due to financial problems it managed to become successful . today it has five festival periods and a rich cultural program . other websites . - official website - in the baden-baden city wiki - festspielhaus baden-baden at baden-baden.de
