René Teulade     rené teulade ( 17 june 1931 – 13 february 2014 ) was a french politician and civil servant . he was a member of the senate of france , representing the corrèze department . he was a member of the socialist party . he was born in monceaux-sur-dordogne , corrèze . teulade died from a stroke on 13 february 2014 in paris . he was aged 82. other websites . - rené teulade at sénat
