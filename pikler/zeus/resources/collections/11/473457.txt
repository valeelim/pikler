Eastcote     eastcote is a suburban area in west london . ( it was in middlesex until the county was abolished in 1965 ) . it is part of the london borough of hillingdon . in the middle ages , eastcote was one of the three areas that made up the parish of ruislip , under the name of ascot . the name came from its position to the east of the parish . from the mid-17th century there were clusters of small hamlets in this area .
