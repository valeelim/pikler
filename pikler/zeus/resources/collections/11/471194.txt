The Crying Child     the crying child is an american television horror movie . it was directed by robert michael lewis . it is based on a barbara mertz novel . the movie stars mariel hemingway and george delhoyo . after their son dies , a couple moves to a new house in order to forget . suddenly , the woman starts to hear baby cries all over her home . she is the only one who can hear them . cast . - mariel hemingway as madeline jeffreys - george delhoyo as ran jeffreys - finola hughes as jo parker
