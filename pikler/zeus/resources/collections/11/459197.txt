Bea Wain     bea wain ( born april 30 , 1917 ) is an american big band-era singer . she was born in new york city , new york . she led the vocal group `` bea and the bachelors '' ( with al rinker , ken lane , and john smedberg ) and the v8 ( seven boys and a girl ) on the fred waring show . wain had four no . 1 hits : `` cry , baby , cry '' , `` deep purple '' , `` heart and soul '' and her signature song , `` my reverie '' . other websites . - the daisy foundation - interview by amanda wilde
