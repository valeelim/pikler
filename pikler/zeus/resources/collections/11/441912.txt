Graham Norton     graham william walker ( born 4 april 1963 ) , better known by his stage name graham norton , is an irish actor , comedian and television presenter . he is best known for hosting `` so graham norton '' ( 1998–2002 ) , `` v graham norton '' ( 2002–03 ) , `` the graham norton effect '' ( 2004 ) , `` graham norton 's bigger picture '' ( 2004–05 ) and `` the graham norton show '' ( 2007–present ) . norton was born into a protestant family in clondalkin , dublin though he was raised in bandon , county cork . he is openly gay .
