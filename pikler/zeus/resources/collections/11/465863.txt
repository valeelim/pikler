News Corp     news corp ( officially `` new news corp ; trading as news corp '' ) was founded in 2013. it is a spin-off from the former news corporation which was founded by rupert murdoch in 1979. it is an american multinational mass media company , based on newspapers and publishing . it is one of two companies which succeeded the former news corporation . the other is 21st century fox , which has the old news corporation 's broadcasting and media properties , such as the fox entertainment group . the spin-out was structured so that 21st century fox would be the legal successor and continuation of the old news corporation , with the new news corp being an entirely new company formed by a stock split .
