Lakewood, Ohio    lakewood , ohio lakewood is a city in ohio , near cleveland . it is in the northeastern area of the state . in the 2010 united states census , 52,131 people lived there . lakewood is led by a mayor and a city council . the people in lakewood vote to choose the mayor and city council . there are seven people in the city council . lakewood is about 6 miles ( 9.7 km ) west of cleveland . the city has a total area of 6.69 square miles ( 17.33 km ) . famous people from lakewood include teri garr .
