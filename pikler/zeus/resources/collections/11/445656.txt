Thomas Howard     thomas arquis howard ( july 14 , 1983 – november 18 , 2013 ) was an american football linebacker in the national football league ( nfl ) . he was drafted by the oakland raiders in the second round of the 2006 nfl draft and played for them until 2010. he later played for the cincinnati bengals ( 2011–12 ) and atlanta falcons ( 2013 ) . he played college football for the university of texas at el paso . he was born and raised in lubbock , texas . howard was one of two people killed in a high speed car accident on november 18 , 2013 in oakland , california . he was 30 years old .
