Stitching awl     a stitching awl is a simple tool to punch holes in leather or other material . it can also be used to make holes larger . the awl is used for sewing leather or canvas . it has a thin , tapered metal shaft , coming to a sharp point , either straight or slightly bent . these shafts usually have an eye piercing at the pointed end . this helps to draw thread through holes for lock stitch sewing by hand . they are also called sewing awls .
