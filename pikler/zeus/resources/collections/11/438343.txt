Langar     the langar ( , ) is a common kitchen where food is served at a sikh temple ( `` gurdwara '' ) . one is attached to every temple , and the food is served to all the visitors for free . at the langar , only vegetarian food is served . it is usually bread ( poori ) , yogurt and lentils . samosas and spring rolls are served on special occasions . kheer may be served as a dessert . the langar is open to both sikhs and people who are not sikhs . after a person eats at the langar they are not supposed to eat meat for the rest of the day .
