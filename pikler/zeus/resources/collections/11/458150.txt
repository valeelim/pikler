Nick Borgen     nils thore `` nick '' borgen , born 5 january 1952 in andenes , nordland , is a norwegian–swedish musician , singer and writer ( debut novel : `` den okända soldaten '' , 2009 ) . he 's famous for songs like `` we are all the winners '' and `` den glider in '' . between 1990 and 2005 , he had an own dansband , nick borgens orkester .
