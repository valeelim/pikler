Panasonic     panasonic corporation , formerly known as matsushita electric industrial co. , ltd , is a multinational electronics company . panasonic is known for their electronics products . the company was created by konosuke matsushita in 1918. it is the fourth-largest television manufacturer in the world . the company was known as matsushita electric industrial co. , ltd. from 1935 to october 1 , 2008. other websites . - panasonic global website
