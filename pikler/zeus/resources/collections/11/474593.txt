Ford Transit Connect     the ford transit connect is a compact minivan developed by ford europe . it has many of the same parts as the ford focus . the first one was made to replace the ford escort and ford fiesta-based courier vans . in canada and the united states , the transit connect has no predecessor . the closest vehicle to its size was the ford aerostar , which was last made in 1997. the transit connect wagon is designed to be part utility van and part compact car . in north america , the transit connect is ford 's first minivan since they stopped making the ford freestar in 2007 .
