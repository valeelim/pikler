Australia's Next Top Model, Cycle 5    australia 's next top model , cycle 5 the fifth cycle of australia 's next top model was an australian reality television program . it was broadcast on the australian subscription television channel , fox8 . the cycle premiered on 28 april 2009. auditions were held during october and november 2008 across australia . supermodel and television personality sarah murdoch took over as host for this cycle . she replaced jodhi meares , who resigned from hosting duties after cycle four finished . the competition was won by tahnee atkinson who defeated cassi van den dungen in the final .
