Lim Xiaoqing     lim xiaoqing , born 15 august 1967 , is a retired female badminton player . originally from china , she later moved to sweden . she won three european badminton championships , one in the women 's singles in 1994 , and two in the women 's doubles in 1992 and 1994. in 1995 she won the women 's singles title at the all-england championships . she then defeated camilla martin from denmark in the final .
