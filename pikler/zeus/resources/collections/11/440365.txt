Ball-peen hammer     a ball-peen ( also spelled pein ) hammer , also known as a machinist 's hammer , is a type of peening hammer used in metalworking . it is classified from a cross-peen hammer , point-peen hammer or chisel-peen hammer by having a hemispherical head . though the process of peening has become rarer in metal fabrication , the ball-peen hammer remains useful for many tasks , such as striking punches and chisels .
