Don Lusk     donald lusk , ( born october 28 , 1913 ) is an american retired animator and director . he worked for the walt disney company . lusk is known for his works in `` snow white and the seven dwarfs '' , `` pinocchio '' , `` fantasia '' , `` bambi '' , `` song of the south '' , `` cinderella '' , `` alice in wonderland '' , `` peter pan '' , `` lady and the tramp '' and `` sleeping beauty '' . other websites . - audio interview with don lusk at the animation guild
