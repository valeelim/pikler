Fé Sciarone     félicité louise henriette `` fé '' sciarone ( 12 august 1922 – 8 april 2014 ) was a dutch radio actress and comedienne . her best known appearances include `` dood in de jungle '' ( 1955 ) , `` een merkwaardig geval van zielsverhuizing '' ( 1969 ) , `` de generaal '' ( 1976 ) , `` dit is uw kans '' ( 1979 ) and `` vijf gemaskerde mannen '' ( 1982 ) . she started her career in 1950 and retired in 1984. she was born in the hague , south holland . sciarone died on 8 april 2014 in amsterdam , north holland , aged 91. other websites . - fé sciarone at radiopedia
