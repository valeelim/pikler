Ourense Cathedral     ourense cathedral ( catedral de ourense or catedral do san martiño ) is a cathedral in ourense , spain . it was founded in 550. it was made a heritage site of spain in 1931. it is mainly in the style of gothic architecture . building the cathedral was completed in 1220. further reading . - josé manuel garcía iglesias ( ed . ) : `` la catedral de ourense '' , laracha 1993 , isbn 84-86614-68-6 - josé camón aznar . `` la escultura y la rejería españolas del siglo xvi '' . summa artis . historia general del arte . vol . xviii . espasa calpe , madrid 1961 .
