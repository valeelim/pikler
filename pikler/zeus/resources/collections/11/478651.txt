Compiègne     compiègne is a commune in the oise department , in picardy . it is also one of its sub-prefectures . the history of the city goes back to roman times . in those times , there was a river crossing of the oise river . the river was called isara at the time . on the outskirts of the modern city , roman houses ( villae ) were found . today , about 40.000 people live in the city and about 97.000 in its urban area .
