Sedevacantism     sedevacantism is the position , held by a small group of traditionalist catholics , that the person in the papal see right now is not truly pope and that , for lack of a good pope , the see has been empty since the death of pope pius xii in 1958. a tiny number of these claim the vacancy actually goes back to the death of pope pius x in 1914. other websites . - european sodalitium pianum - sedevacantist european seminary - sedevacantism reconsidered - evidence of sedevacantism - the aquinas site . sedevacantist.com .
