Harold &amp; Kumar    harold & amp ; kumar harold & kumar is an american series of stoner comedy movies . there are three movies in total . the movies star john cho ( harold ) , kal penn ( kumar ) and neil patrick harris ( himself ) . the first movie , `` harold & kumar go to white castle '' , was released on july 30 , 2004. the second movie , `` harold & kumar escape from guantanamo bay '' , was released on april 25 , 2008. the third and final movie , `` a very harold & kumar 3d christmas '' , was released on november 4 , 2011. the reviews of each movie has been mixed , but they have all done well at the box office .
