Sonny Rollins     theodore walter `` sonny '' rollins ( born september 7 , 1930 ) is an american jazz tenor saxophonist . rollins is widely known as one of the most important and influential jazz musicians . a number of his compositions , such as `` st. thomas '' , `` oleo '' , `` doxy '' , and `` airegin '' , have become jazz standards . rollins was born in new york city . other websites . - sonny rollins official website
