Tagetes     tagetes is a genus of flowers commonly called marigold . tagetes has 56 species of plants in the sunflower family ( asteraceae or compositae ) . the genus is native to north and south america . `` tagetes '' species vary in size from 0.1 to 2.2 m tall . most species have pinnate green leaves . blooms naturally occur in golden , orange , yellow , and white colors . in horticulture , they are usually planted as annuals .
