Stand Up and Scream     stand up and scream was the debut album of the english metalcore band asking alexandria . it was released on 15 september 2009 through sumerian records . the album came number 170 on the billboard 200 , number 29 on top independent albums and number 5 on heatseekers . band members . - danny worsnop - lead vocals , keyboards , piano , synthesizers , programming - ben bruce - lead guitar , backing vocals , vocals on track 8 and 9 , programming - cameron liddell - rhythm guitar - sam bettley - bass guitar - james cassells - drums
