Ray Lonnen     ray lonnen ( 18 may 1940 – 11 july 2014 ) was an english stage and television actor . his most famous roles include willie caine in the cold-war spy drama series `` the sandbaggers '' ( 1978–80 ) and also as harry brown in the television miniseries `` harry 's game '' ( 1982 ) . lonnen died on 11 july 2014 from cancer , aged 74. other websites . - `` the sandbaggers '' opsroom.org ; accessed 12 july 2014 .
