Lake Point Tower     lake point tower is a high-rise residential building located on a promontory of the lake michigan lakefront in downtown chicago , just north of the chicago river at 505 north lake shore drive . it is located in the streeterville neighborhood of the near north side community area . the building is also the only skyscraper in downtown chicago east of lake shore drive . it stands at 645 ft ( 197 m ) . other websites . - lake point tower website - emporis listing
