Red (2010 movie)    red ( 2010 movie ) red is a 2010 american action comedy movie directed by robert schwentke . it is based on the limited comic book series printed by dc comics . it stars bruce willis , john malkovich , morgan freeman , helen mirren , mary-louise parker , brian cox , julian mcmahon , richard dreyfuss , karl urban , and ernest borgnine . it was released on october 5 , 2010. a sequel , `` red 2 '' , was released on july 19 , 2013 .
