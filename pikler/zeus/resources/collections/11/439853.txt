Nidal Malik Hasan     nidal malik hasan ( born september 8 , 1970 ) is a former united states army major and psychiatrist . on 5 noverber 2009 he entered the soldier 's readiness center at fort hood , texas . he fired approximately 100 rounds at soldiers in the center . hasan admitted to the shootings at his court-martial in august 2013. the jury convicted him of 45 counts ( crimes ) of premeditated murder and attempted premeditated murder . one count each for the 13 soldiers he killed and one count for each of the 32 people he shot at . on 28 august 2013 the same military court sentenced hasan to death . he is in prison at the united states disciplinary barracks at fort leavenworth , kansas . hasan is awaiting execution while his case is reviewed by appellate courts . hasan was born in arlington county , virginia . his palestinian parents immigrated to the united states . the shooting was believed to have been bought on by his islamic religious radicalism . before the shooting , he communicated with the late american-yemeni islamic militant , anwar al-awlaki .
