If You Were Still Around     `` if you were still around '' is a song by welsh musician and composer john cale . it was co-written by john cale and playwright sam shepard . it was originally released in 1982 on `` music for a new society '' album . the new version of the song was released in 2014 together with music video directed by abigail portner . the video was dedicated to lou reed who died one year before .
