Helepolis     helepolis ( , english : `` taker of cities '' ) was an ancient siege engine . it was used in the unsuccessful siege of rhodes in 305 bc . it is based on an earlier , smaller design used against salamis in 306 bc . the name was taken from the greek `` ελείν πόλεις '' , meaning destroyer of `` cities '' . vitruvius , plutarch , and in the athenaeus mechanicus . it was the largest and most powerful siege tower ever built .
