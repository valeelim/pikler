Statue of Liberty National Monument     the statue of liberty national monument is a national monument of the united states . it is made up of two islands : ellis island and liberty island . the statue of liberty is included because it is on liberty island . this national monument was created in 1924. at first , it included only the statue of liberty . in 1965 , ellis island was added . in 1966 , liberty island was added . other websites . - statue of liberty national monument the official historical site handbook .
