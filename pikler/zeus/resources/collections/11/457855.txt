Sikkimese language     the sikkimese language , also called sikkimese tibetan , bhutia , `` dranjongke '' ( `` rice district language '' ) , `` dranjoke '' , `` denjongka '' , `` denzongpeke '' , and `` denzongke '' , belongs to the tibetic languages . it is spoken by the bhutia people in sikkim and northeast nepal . the sikkimese people call their language dranjongke . they call their homeland denzong ( `` rice valley '' ) .
