Frank Nighbor     julius francis `` pembroke peach '' nighbor ( january 26 , 1893 – april 13 , 1966 ) was a canadian professional ice hockey forward . in march 2010 , at a quebec auction , an american collector paid $ 33,000 usd to secure frank nighbor 's game-worn ottawa senators sweater from the 1926–27 season . he played for the ottawa senators of the national hockey league ( nhl ) and national hockey association ( nha ) and toronto maple leafs of the nhl , toronto blueshirts of the nha and vancouver millionaires of the pacific coast hockey association ( pcha ) . nighbor died of cancer on april 13 , 1966 in pembroke , ontario at the age of 73 .
