A Sky Full of Stars     a sky full of stars is an edm song written by the british band coldplay featuring swedish dj avicii in 2014. the song is the band 's first edm track . the song is centered around the piano , with electronic music coming in between verses . most critics enjoyed the song and gave it positive reviews . the song reached # 1 on the charts in the united states , portugal , luxembourg , italy , israel and belgium . one day after it 's release on itunes , it had sold 121,690 copies worldwide . the song was also coldplay 's third top ten hit on the billboard hot 100 .
