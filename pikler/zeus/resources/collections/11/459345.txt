Neihu District     neihu district is a district of taipei city , taiwan . neihu means `` inner lake . '' the older name originates from the ketagalan word `` tayour '' ( transliterated by the dutch as `` cattajo '' ) , meaning woman 's head ornament . transasia airways is headquartered in the district . rt-mart also has its headquarters in the district . other websites . - neihu district office , taipei city
