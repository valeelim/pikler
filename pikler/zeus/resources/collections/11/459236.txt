Association of Tennis Professionals     the association of tennis professionals ( atp ) , is the principal organizing body of men 's professional tennis . formed in september 1972 by donald dell , jack kramer , and cliff drysdale . the organizing body was to care for the interests of men 's professional tennis , and starting in 1990 the atp has organized the top tennis tour for men . in 2009 the organization changed the name of the tour from the `` atp tour '' to the `` atp world tour '' . it is based in london . atp rankings . - rankings as of 28 july 2014 .
