Rafał Majka     rafał majka ( born 12 september 1989 ) is a polish professional cyclist . he rides for the tinkhoff-saxo team . he won the polka dot jersey in the 2014 tour de france . majka won his first professional victory on stage 14 of the 2014 tour de france . he won again on stage 17. he did well in the other mountain stages too , and won the polka dot jersey for the best climber . he is the first pole to win a jersey in the tour de france . in 2013 , he competed in the giro d'italia for the first time . he finished 7th , eight minutes behind the winner vincenzo nibali . in 2014 , he finished 6th . significant results . - 2008 - 2009 - 2010 - 2012 - 2013 - 2014
