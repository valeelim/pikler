Sitor Situmorang     sitor situmorang ( october 2 , 1923 – december 21 , 2014 ) was an indonesian poet , essayist and writer of short stories . situmorang was born in harianboho , north sumatra . he was educated in jakarta . he worked as a journalist and literary critic . situmorang was known for his poem `` angkatan ’ 45 '' ( the generation of ’ 45 ) . on december 21 , 2014 , he died at the age of 91 at his home in apeldoorn , netherlands .
