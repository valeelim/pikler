Paul Revere (musician)    paul revere ( musician ) paul revere dick ( january 7 , 1938 - october 4 , 2014 ) was an american musician and organ player . in 1958 , he and other musicians founded the group `` paul revere & the raiders '' . they recorded many hits . he worked with the group until his death in 2014. revere was born in harvard , nebraska . revere died from cancer in calwell , idaho , aged 76 .
