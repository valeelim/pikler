Rasputina (band)    rasputina ( band ) rasputina is a cello rock band from new york . the members are melora creager , daniel dejesus , and dawn miceli . the band was started in 1992 by creager . creager put an advertisement in the village voice asking for other cellists to start a rock band with her . julia kent , an editor of the newspaper , responded to the ad . the band released its first album , `` thanks for the ether '' , in 1996. in 1997 they released an extended play remixed by marilyn manson . in 1998 the band 's second album `` how we quit the forest '' was released . in 2002 `` cabin fever '' was released .
