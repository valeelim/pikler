Vancouver Stealth     the vancouver stealth is a professional box lacrosse team . it plays in the west division of the national lacrosse league . it is based in langley , british columbia , which is part of the metro vancouver area . the team plays its home games in langley events centre . the team was announced on june 27 , 2013. they started playing in the 2014 season . the team was previously known as the albany attack from the 2000 season until the 2003 season . later they played as the san jose stealth from the 2004 season until the 2009 season . they were the washington stealth from the 2010 season until the 2013 season . other websites . - official website
