Freaks and Geeks     freaks and geeks is a 1999-2000 american comedy-drama television show on nbc . it is set at a high school in detroit , michigan in 1980 . `` freaks and geeks '' has a cult following . the series appeared on time magazine 's 2007 `` 100 greatest shows of all time '' list . it also placed number 13 on the `` best 20 shows of the decade '' .
