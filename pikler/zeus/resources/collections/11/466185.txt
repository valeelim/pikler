Frontiersman     frontiersmen and frontierswomen were/are people living on the frontier between settled and unsettled lands . the frontier was a place between civilization and the unknown wilderness . frontiersmen often cleared the land , built shelters , raised children , and grew crops . hunting and animal trapping were prime sources of meat . animal furs and skins were made into clothing . famous american frontiersmen include davy crockett , daniel boone , and kit carson . the united states , canada , and australia are the countries where frontiersmen and women have been found in large numbers .
