Oden     oden is one of the japanese stewing food . oden contains a lot of ingredients in seasoned soup . ingredients and soup are different by area and shop . recently , oden is also sold at convenience stores . popular ingredients - japanese white radish ... it is peeled and sliced thickly . it is called `` king of oden '' . - boiled egg ... it is boiled hen 's egg or quail egg . - shirataki ... whitish strings of konnyaku . - atsu-age ... it is fried bean curd .
