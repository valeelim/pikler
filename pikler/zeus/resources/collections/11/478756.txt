Order of the White Eagle (Poland)    order of the white eagle ( poland ) the order of the white eagle ( ) is poland 's highest decoration awarded to both civilians and the military for their service . it was officially created on 1 november 1705 by augustus ii the strong . it was given to eight of his closest , diplomatic and political supporters . it is awarded to the most respected poles and the highest-ranking representatives of foreign countries . the order of the white eagle is attached to a blue ribbon hung over the left shoulder to the right side . the star of the order , once sown , is worn on the left side of the chest .
