Self storage     self storage is a place where rooms or spaces are rented to individuals and companies . these can very large rooms or outside spaces , or as small as a simple locker . these spaces are usually rented on a short term lease , or on a month by month basis . a business may use self storage as a way to free up expensive office space to store company files , inventory , equipment , furniture and other items . the person or business leasing the space is responsible for placing items inside . the storage company does not usually have access to the spaces . the money spent on self storage in the usa was estimated to be $ 24 billion in 2013 .
