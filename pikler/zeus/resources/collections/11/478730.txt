Web resource     a web resource is anything that can be obtained from the world wide web . some examples are web pages , e-mail , information from databases , and web services . web resources have changed since the internet was first created . the early concept was of static ( non moving ) files or documents . the meaning of the term now has a wider use to include nearly everything that can be obtained via the internet . uniform resource locators or urls , are used to identify a resource on the web .
