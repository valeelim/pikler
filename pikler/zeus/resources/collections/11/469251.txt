Manzini, Swaziland    manzini , swaziland manzini ( formerly known as `` bremersdorp '' ) is a city in swaziland ; it is the capital of swaziland 's manzini district . the city is the country 's second largest urban centre behind mbabane , with a population of 78,000 ( 2008 ) . it is known as `` the hub '' of swaziland and lies on the mr3 road .
