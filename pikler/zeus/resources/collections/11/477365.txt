Stone of Scone     the stone of scone , also known as the stone of destiny , and the coronation stone , is a stone used in the coronation ceremonies of england and scotland . it is an oblong shaped block of red sandstone . it was kept at the now-ruined scone abbey in scone , near perth , scotland . its size is about by by and weighs about . the stone of scone was last used in 1953 for the coronation of elizabeth ii of the united kingdom of great britain and northern ireland .
