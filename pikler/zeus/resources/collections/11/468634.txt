The Outsiders (movie)    the outsiders ( movie ) the outsiders is a 1983 crime drama movie . it is set in 1965 , tulsa , oklahoma . it is about tough low-income working class teenagers . sherri valance is played by diane lane . matt dillon plays dallas winston . emilio estevez plays keith matthews . patrick swayze plays darrel curtis . tom cruise plays steve randle . rob lowe plays soda curtis . the movie is based on the 1967 novel of the same name . it was released on march 25 , 1983. its rating was changed to pg-13 . the reviews of the movie were positive .
