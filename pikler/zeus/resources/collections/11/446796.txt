Rafał Sznajder     rafał sznajder ( 13 october 1972 – 13 april 2014 ) was a polish fencer . he competed in three summer olympic games ( 1996 , 2000 and 2004 ) . he won a bronze medal at both the 1997 and 2001 world fencing championships . he was born in będzin . sznajder died from a heart attack on 13 april 2014 in plovdiv , bulgaria , aged 41. other websites . - rafał sznajder at sports reference
