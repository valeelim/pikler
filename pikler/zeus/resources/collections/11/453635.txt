Shaolin Soccer     shaolin soccer is a 2001 hong kong comedy film . it was co-written and directed by stephen chow . he also stars in the lead role . the movie is about a former shaolin monk . he reunites his five brothers , years after their master 's death . they apply their superhuman martial arts skills to play soccer and bring shaolin kung fu to the masses . cast . - stephen chow as sing ( steel leg ) - `` danny chan '' as empty hand - `` tin kai man as iron shirt - `` patrick tse '' as hung - `` ng man tat as fung - `` wong yat fei '' as iron head
