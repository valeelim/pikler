Narendra Modi     narendra damodardas modi ( born 17 september 1950 ) is an indian politician . he is the current prime minister of india serving since 2014. he was the 14th chief minister of the state of gujarat . modi was elected prime minister of india in may 2014. he is a member of the bharatiya janata party ( bjp ) . modi is a hindu nationalist . he is a member of the rashtriya swayamsevak sangh ( rss ) .
