Ch'eng Mao-yün     cheng maoyun ( ) , 25 aug 1900 - 31 jul 1957 ) was a chinese musician and a professor at national central university and hangzhou societal university ( 杭州社會大學 ) . he wrote the music of the `` national anthem of the republic of china '' . chen was born in 1990 in xinjian to a family of officials . he studied music in jiangxi provincial higher normal school . the official university song of the national central university , now in taiwan , was also composed by cheng .
