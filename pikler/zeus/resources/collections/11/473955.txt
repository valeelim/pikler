City Thameslink railway station     city thameslink station is a national rail station in the city of london . the southern entrance is on ludgate hill near ludgate circus . the northern entrance is on holborn viaduct . both of the stations platforms are underground . the station is on the thameslink route . the station opened on the 22nd of may 1990 , however it was called st paul 's thameslink until 1991. many changes have occurred on the lines near the station since . the station was improved in 2010 , and is ready for trains of up to 12 cars . all services are run by thameslink
