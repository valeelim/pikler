Southern Ostrobothnia     southern ostrobothnia is a region in western finland . as of 2013 , about 194,000 people lived there . the city of seinäjoki is the center of the southern ostrobothnia . neighbouring regions are ostrobothnia , central ostrobothnia , central finland , pirkanmaa and satakunta . southern ostrobothnia has 18 municipalities , 8 of those are cities , listed below . - alajärvi - alavus - kauhajoki - kauhava - kurikka - lapua - seinäjoki - ähtäri other websites . - official website
