Lethal Injection (album)    lethal injection ( album ) lethal injection is a 1993 gangsta rap studio album from ice cube . similar to his earlier albums , `` lethal injection '' was a strong hit . it debuted at # 5 on the `` billboard '' 200. it was criticized for what some saw as toning down of political content . several singles , including `` you know how we do it '' , became popular . `` lethal injection '' was released on 7 december 1993 .
