Bob L. Harris     bob lee harris ( october 11 , 1922 - february 14 , 2014 ) was an american actor , singer , and the father of ed harris . he was known for his roles in `` riders of the purple sage '' , `` pollock '' , and in `` appaloosa '' . he also appeared on television as borklund in `` american playhouse '' . harris was born on october 11 , 1922 in walters , oklahoma . he died on february 14 , 2014 at the age of 91 from a long illness .
