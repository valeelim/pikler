New Zealand Labour Party     the new zealand labour party is a social democratic political party in new zealand . it is one of the two major parties in new zealand politics . it was started in 1916 , which makes it the oldest of new zealand 's political parties . since the 2008 general elections , the labour party has the second largest party in the house of representatives of new zealand and is the official opposition . the current leader of the party is jacinda ardern since august 2017 .
