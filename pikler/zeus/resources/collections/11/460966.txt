The Last Poets     the last poets is the name for several groups of poets and musicians who came out of the late 1960s african-american civil rights movement 's black nationalist movement . the name is taken from a poem by the south african revolutionary poet keorapetse kgositsile . the original last poets were formed on may 19 , 1968 at marcus garvey park in east harlem . their first album `` the last poets '' was released in 1970 .
