American lion     the american lion ( `` panthera leo '' or `` p. atrox '' ) is also known as naegele â€™ s giant jaguar or american cave lion . it is an extinct species of lion which lived in north america during the pleistocene ( 340,000 years ago to 11,000 years ago ) . it existed for about 330,000 thousand years . the american lion belonged to the family felidae . by analysing the species ' genes , scientists have shown that it shared the same ancestor as the eurasian cave lion ( `` panthera leo spelaea '' or `` p. spelaea '' ) . most remains have come from the la brea tar pits . the american lion was large , slightly larger than the early middle pleistocene cave lion , `` p. leo fossilis '' and about 25 % larger than the modern african lion . there is no evidence that it was a social hunter like the modern lion , and that may explain its size . `` panthera leo atrox '' hunted large mammals such as horses , camels , bison and mammoth .
