Madis Kõiv     madis kõiv ( 5 december 1929 – 24 september 2014 ) was an estonian writer and physicist . his first published work was a play called `` küüni täitmine '' ( `` filling the hay barn '' ) . kõiv was also the author of several novels . `` widow '' and `` aporia of attica , tragedy of elea '' are two of his best known works . he was born in tartu , estonia . kõiv died in tallinn , estonia , aged 84. other websites . - estonian literary magazine
