Gillo Dorfles     gillo dorfles ( born 12 april 1910 ) is an italian art critic , painter , and philosopher . dorfles graduated in medicine , specializing in psychiatry . he was a professor in aesthetics at the university of trieste , milan and cagliari . in 1948 , he co-founded the movimento per l'arte concreta ( mac ) with artists atanasio soldati , galliano mazzon , gianni monnet , and bruno munari . dorfles was born on 12 april 1910 in trieste . he lives and works in milan .
