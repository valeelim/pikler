John Slattery     john m. slattery , jr. ( born august 13 , 1962 ) is an american actor , director , producer and screenwriter . he is best known for his role as roger sterling on amc 's series `` mad men '' . he was part of the `` mad men '' cast that won two sag awards . he has also appeared in the movies `` iron man 2 '' ( 2010 ) and `` the adjustment bureau '' ( 2011 ) . slattery was born in boston , massachusetts to an irish roman catholic family . he married actress talia balsam in 1998 and together they have a son , harry .
