Harold J. Greene     harold joseph `` harry '' greene ( february 11 , 1959august 5 , 2014 ) was an american military man . he served in the united states army as a major general . greene was killed after being shot by an afghan solider while making a routine visit towards a training facility . upon his death , he became the highest ranking u.s. casualty of the war in afghanistan and the first since 9/11 .
