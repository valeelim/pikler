John Vasconcellos     john b. vasconcellos jr. ( may 11 , 1932 - may 24 , 2014 ) was an american politician and psychologist . he was a member of the democratic party . he represented silicon valley as a member of the california state assembly for 30 years and a california state senator for 8 years . vasconcellos died in san jose , california from multiple organ failure , aged 82 .
