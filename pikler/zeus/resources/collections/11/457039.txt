Valeriya Novodvorskaya     valeriya ilyinichna novodvorskaya ( ; 17 may 1950 – 12 july 2014 ) was a liberal russian politician and soviet dissident . she was the founder and the chairwoman of the `` democratic union '' party and a member of the editorial board of the news magazine `` the new times '' . novodvorskaya was a fierce critic of the president vladimir putin and his regime . other websites . - website of democratic union - opinions on grani.ru ( russian ) - « it can very soon come to pogroms » , interview with novodvorskaya , jewish observer , 21/40 . november 2002
