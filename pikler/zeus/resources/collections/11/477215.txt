Nélida Romero     nélida romero ( 17 january 1926 – 14 january 2015 ) was an argentine actress . she starred in the movies `` madame bovary '' ( 1947 ) , `` arroz con leche '' ( 1950 ) , and `` the honourable tenant '' ( 1951 ) . romero was born in lanús . she was married to the movie director carlos schlieper from 1944 to 1957. romero died on 14 january 2015 in buenos aires , aged 88 .
