Thích Nhất Hạnh     thích nhất hạnh ( ; born october 11 , 1926 ) is a vietnamese zen buddhist monk , teacher , author , poet and peace activist . he lives in the plum village monastery in the dordogne region in the south of france . he was born in thừa thiên huế province , vietnam . he created the term engaged buddhism in his book `` vietnam : lotus in a sea of fire '' . a long-term exile , he was given permission to make his first return trip to vietnam in 2005. nhất hạnh has published more than 100 books , including more than 40 in english . on 11 november 2014 , nhất hạnh was hospitalized with a severe brain hemorrhage . there are signs that a full recovery may be possible .
