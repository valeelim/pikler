Earl Hindman     earl john hindman ( october 20 , 1942 – december 29 , 2003 ) was an american actor . he was best known for his role as the neighbor ( whose whole face was always hidden from television viewers ) wilson w. wilson , jr. on the television sitcom `` home improvement '' ( 1991–1999 ) , starring tim allen . he also appeared in the television movie `` '' ( 1987 ) . hindman was born in bisbee , arizona . he married actress molly mcgreevey in 1976. hindman died from lung cancer on december 29 , 2003 in stamford , connecticut . he was 61 .
