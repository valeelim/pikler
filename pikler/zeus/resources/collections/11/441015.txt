Iran hostage crisis     the iran hostage crisis was a diplomatic crisis between iran and the united states . fifty-two american diplomats and citizens were held hostage for 444 days ( november 4 , 1979 , to january 20 , 1981 ) . it happened after a group of iranian students supporting the iranian revolution took over the us embassy in tehran . president jimmy carter called the hostages `` victims of terrorism and anarchy , '' adding that `` the united states will not yield to blackmail . '' the u.s. tried to free the hostages during the crisis , but failed . the hostages were released just as ronald reagan was being inaugurated as president .
