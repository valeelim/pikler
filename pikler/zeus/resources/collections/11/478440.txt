Bikont     a bikont ( `` two flagellae '' ) is any eukaryotic organism in the group bikonta . many single-celled members of the group , and the presumed ancestor , have two flagella . another shared trait of bikonts is the fusion of two genes into a single unit . the genes for thymidylate synthase ( ts ) and dihydrofolate reductase ( dhfr ) code for a single protein with two functions . the genes are separately translated in unikonts .
