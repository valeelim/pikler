Bob Barr (1880s pitcher)    bob barr ( 1880s pitcher ) robert mcclelland barr ( december , 1856 - march 11 , 1930 ) was a major league baseball pitcher . he also made some appearances as an outfielder , first baseman , and third baseman . barr played for the pittsburgh alleghenys , washington nationals , indianapolis hoosiers , and rochester broncos . he also played for the washington nationals and new york giants .
