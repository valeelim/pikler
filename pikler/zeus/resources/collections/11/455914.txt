Money in the Bank (2011)    money in the bank ( 2011 ) money in the bank ( 2011 ) was a professional wrestling pay-per-view show made by wwe . it was held on july 17 , 2011 at the allstate arena in rosemont , illinois . it was the second money in the bank event held by the wwe . the main event between cm punk and john cena received a rare 5-star rating from dave meltzer of the `` wrestling observer newsletter '' . other websites . - official money in the bank 2011 website
