One Hour Photo     one hour photo is a 2002 american psychological thriller movie . it was written and directed by mark romanek . it starred robin williams . fox searchlight pictures distributed the movie in the united states . `` one hour photo '' also starred connie nielsen , michael vartan , gary cole , and eriq la salle . williams won a saturn award for best actor ( 2003 ) for his work in the movie . the movie received positive reviews , earning an 81 % rating at rotten tomatoes . cast . - robin williams as seymour `` sy '' parrish - michael vartan as will yorkin - connie nielsen as nina yorkin - dylan smith as jake yorkin - gary cole as bill owens , manager - erin daniels as maya burson - eriq la salle as det . james van der zee - clark gregg as det . paul outerbridge - paul h. kim as yoshi araki
