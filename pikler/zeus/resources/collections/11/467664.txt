Tomasgårdens IF     tomasgårdens if was a floorball club at the thomas church in västerås in sweden . it was established as a club in 1982 following the 1978 establishment of a floorball team . the club was disestablished in 1999 , when the club was merged with västerås ibf . the men 's team won the swedish national championship in 1984. the men 's team also played in the swedish top division during the 1990 's . in 1991 , the men 's team lost the swedish national championship finals to ibk lockerud .
