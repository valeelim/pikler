¡Tré!    ¡tré ! ¡tré ! is the eleventh studio album by american punk rock band , green day . `` ¡tré ! '' was released on december 7 , 2012. it is the third and last album in the `` ¡uno ! `` , `` ¡dos ! `` , `` ¡tré ! '' trilogy . the album featured the single `` x-kid '' . the album debuted at # 13 on the `` us billboard 200 '' chart and the first-week sales were 58,000 copies in the united states .
