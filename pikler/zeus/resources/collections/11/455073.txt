Girl power     girl power is a term used to say that girls are important . it became popular because of the spice girls ' use of the term . it was also used by the band , bikini kill . the phrase is sometimes spelled `` grrrl power '' . this is because of the riot grrrl punk rock bands . `` girl power '' was also used by other bands in the early 1990s . one was a welsh indie rock band helen love . another was a south-east london punk rock band , shampoo . they released an album and a single titled `` girl power '' in 1995 .
