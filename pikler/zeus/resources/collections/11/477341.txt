Union of Sweden and Norway     the union ofsweden and norway ( ; ) , officially the united kingdoms of sweden and norway , was a personal union . it consisted of the separate kingdoms of sweden and norway under the same monarch . it lasted from 1814 to 1905. in 1905 sweden accepted norway leaving the union . both states kept their separate constitutions , laws , legislatures , administrations , state churches , armed forces , and currencies . however the kings mostly resided in stockholm . stockholm was also the town where foreign diplomatic representations were located .
