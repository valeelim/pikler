Trechoblemus     trechoblemus is a genus of beetles in the family carabidae . it contains the following species : - `` trechoblemus lindrothi '' - `` trechoblemus microphthalmus '' - `` trechoblemus micros '' - `` trechoblemus postilenatus '' - `` trechoblemus valentinei '' - `` trechoblemus westcotti ''
