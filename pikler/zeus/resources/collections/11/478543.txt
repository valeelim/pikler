Mullsjö Secondary School     the mullsjö secondary school ( ) was a secondary school in mullsjö , sweden . it was opened for the 1997-1998 school year . the last years the school acted as a boarding school for young floorball players . the school also won the boys ' floorball world championship in the years of 2007 , 2009 and 2011. on 26 october 2010 the mullsjö municipal council voted to close down the school after the 2010-2011 school year .
