Iowa Energy     the iowa energy are an american professional basketball team based in des moines , iowa . they play in the eastern conference in the nba development league ( nba d-league ) . this is a minor league basketball organization owned and run by the national basketball association ( nba ) . the energy play their home games at the wells fargo arena . they broke the d-league attendance record on their first ever home game with 8,842 fans . they won their first d-league championship in 2011. in 2009 and 2014 , the team won division titles . in 2010 and 2011 they won conference titles . their nba team sponsor is the memphis grizzlies .
