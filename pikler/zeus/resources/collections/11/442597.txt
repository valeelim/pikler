Ahmad Reza Baharlou     ahmad reza baharlou ( , born in darab , iran ) is a former television presenter , movie director , producer and the first director of the voice of america persian service ( voa-pnn ) . baharlou studied economics at the national university ( shahid beheshti university ) . he went to the united states of america in 1973 and studied moviemaking . he stayed there until 1978. he made his first movie called `` the end of childhood '' . in 1978 , he came back to iran for a very short time and then came back to the united states and started to work for the voice of america . he quit working for the voa-pnn in 2008 .
