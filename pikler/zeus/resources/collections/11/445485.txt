Lollipop (Candyman)    lollipop ( candyman ) `` lollipop ( candyman ) '' is a pop song from the group aqua . it is from their first album , `` aquarium '' . the song was released in 1997. this song was said to be one of the group 's favorite songs . it was not as successful as their song `` barbie girl '' . the longest version is 7 minutes and 28 seconds long .
