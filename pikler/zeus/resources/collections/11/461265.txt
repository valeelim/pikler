Superman: Escape from Krypton    superman : escape from krypton superman : escape from krypton ( used to be known as superman : the escape ) is a steel roller coaster built by intamin at six flags magic mountain in valencia , california , united states . when it opened in 1997 , it was the tallest and fastest roller coaster in the world . it is similar to a roller coaster called tower of terror ii at dreamworld in australia . the ride closed in 2010 so that it could be changed from `` superman : the escape '' to `` superman : escape from krypton '' . the new ride opened in 2011 , with new trains that face backwards and with new colors . other websites . - superman : escape from krypton official website - superman : escape from krypton photos and review at ultimate rollercoaster.com - superman : escape from krypton information
