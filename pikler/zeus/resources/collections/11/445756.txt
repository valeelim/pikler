Jonathan Rhys Meyers     jonathan rhys meyers ( born jonathan michael francis o'keeffe ; 27 july 1977 ) is an irish actor , movie producer and model . he is best known for his roles in the movies `` velvet goldmine '' ( 1998 ) , `` bend it like beckham '' ( 2002 ) , `` match point '' ( 2005 ) , `` '' ( 2006 ) , `` august rush '' ( 2007 ) and `` from paris with love '' ( 2010 ) . rhys meyers was born in drimnagh , dublin and raised in cork , county cork in a roman catholic family .
