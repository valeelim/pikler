Maternal health     maternal health is the health of a woman during pregnancy , childbirth and her postpartum periods . it includes the health care dimensions of family planning , preconception , prenatal and postnatal caring . the risk of maternal death is higher in some areas , such as sub-saharan africa , than in others . poverty is a problem in some countries , in particular , the developing countries . females living in poverty stricken cities or areas are more likely to be overweight or obese and have unhealthy behaviors like smoking or drug use . there are a lot of reasons why females choose to not engage in proper prenatal care . around 71 % of low income women , according to a us study , had difficulty having access to prenatal care . income is highly associated with prenatal health care .
