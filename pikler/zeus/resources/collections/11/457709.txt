New Democratic Party (Canada)    new democratic party ( canada ) the new democratic party is a social democratic political party in canada . it was formed in 1961 when the co-operative commonwealth federation merged with the canadian labour congress . wings of the ndp have formed governments in several provinces . it forms the current government in manitoba . in the 2011 federal elections it won 98 out of 308 seats in the house of commons . the party is currently the official opposition . the current party leader is thomas mulcair .
