35 BC     year 35 bc was either a common year starting on thursday or friday or a leap year starting on wednesday , thursday or friday of the julian calendar . at the time , it was known as the year of the consulship of cornificius and sextus . events . - illyria becomes a roman province - pannonia is attacked by octavian caesar - sextus pompeius defeats gaius furnius - azes i , indo-scythian ruler , completed the domination of the scythians in northern india . births . - mollius macgillus carterus deaths . - aristobulus iii of judea , high priest ( drowned ) ( b . 53 bc ) - sextus pompeius , executed in miletus ( b. c. 67 bc )
