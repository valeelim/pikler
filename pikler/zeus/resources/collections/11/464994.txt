Uric acid     uric acid is what reptiles and birds excrete . it is a heterocyclic compound of carbon , nitrogen , oxygen , and hydrogen with the formula chno . it forms ions and salts known as urates and acid urates . uric acid is a product of the metabolic breakdown of purine nucleotides . high blood concentrations of uric acid can lead to gout . the chemical is associated with other medical conditions such as diabetes and the formation of kidney stones ( ammonium acid urate ) . related pages . - guano
