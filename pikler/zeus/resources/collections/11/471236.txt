Anthony Anderson     anthony anderson ( born august 15 , 1970 ) is an american actor , screenwriter , voice artist , and comedian . he has starred in his own sitcom `` all about the andersons '' , as well as `` the bernie mac show '' during the fifth and final season . he is known for his leading roles in television dramas , such as `` k-ville '' , `` the shield '' , and `` law & order '' . anderson has also had supporting roles in films , such as `` transformers '' , `` the departed '' , `` '' , `` scream 4 '' , and `` kangaroo jack '' .
