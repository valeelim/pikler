Ricardo Mórtola     ricardo mórtola di puglia ( c. 1950 – 22 april 2014 ) was an ecuadorian architect and businessman . he was known for constructing estadio monumental isidro romero carbo in barcelona . he won many awards during his twenty-year career . he was born in guayaquil , ecuador . mórtola died in guayaquil , ecuador from testicular cancer , aged 63 .
