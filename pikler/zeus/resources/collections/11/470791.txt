Vira Silenti     vira silenti ( elvira giovene ; 16 april 1931 - 1 november 2014 ) was an italian actress . she was known for her roles in `` i vitelloni '' , `` the story of joseph and his brethren '' , and in `` atlas against the cyclops '' . silenti was born in naples , italy . silenti was hit by a car on 31 october 2014 in rome , italy . she died the next day from her injuries at the age of 83 .
