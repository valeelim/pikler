Carlos Páez Vilaró     carlos páez vilaró ( 1 november 1923 – 24 february 2014 ) was a uruguayan abstract artist , painter , potter , sculptor , muralist , writer , composer and constructor . he composed numerous musical pieces in the two genres and conducted an orchestra . he was born in montevideo . he was of catalan ancestry . vilaró died on 24 february 2014 at his home in punta ballena , maldonado . he was aged 90 .
