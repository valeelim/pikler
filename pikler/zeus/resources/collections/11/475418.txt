The Three Little Pigs     the three little pigs is a fable/fairy tale featuring three pigs . they each build houses of different materials . a big bad wolf ( the villain or bad guy ) is able to blow down the first two pigs ' houses . the first is made of straw , the second is made of wood . but he is unable to destroy the third pig 's house . this is because it is made of bricks . the moral or lesson of the story is to plan ahead . printed versions of the story date back to the 1840s . but the story may have borrowed parts from older stories . one is `` the wolf and the seven young kids '' ( ) . this is a german folktale collected as the fifth tale in grimms ' fairy tales in 1812 . ) the phrases used in the story , and the various morals that can be drawn from it , have become a part of western culture . walt disney made an animated version of the story . it features the song , `` who 's afraid of the big bad wolf ? '' characters . - the big bad wolf - first pig ( house of straw ) - second pig ( house of sticks ) - third pig ( house of bricks )
