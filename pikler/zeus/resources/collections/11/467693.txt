Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency     the administrator of the environmental protection agency is the head of the united states federal government 's environmental protection agency . it is responsible for enforcing the nation 's clean air and clean water acts , as well as many other environmental statutes . the current administrator of the environmental protection agency is scott pruitt , who succeeded acting administrator catherine mccabe , on february 17 , 2017. the administrator is nominated by the president of the united states and must be confirmed by a vote of the senate . the office of administrator of the environmental protection agency was created in 1970 in legislation that created the environmental protection agency . other websites . - u.s. epa history : administrator photos , biographies , publications
