Kmart     kmart ( sometimes stylized as k-mart ) is an american company . its headquarters are in illinois . the company purchased sears in 2005. it formed a new corporation under the name sears holdings corporation . the company was founded in 1962. it is the third largest discount store in the world , behind walmart and target . kmart has stores in the united states , puerto rico , the u.s. virgin islands , and guam . there were stores in canada , mexico , and eastern europe . , there are 1,077 kmart stores . kmart is known for its `` blue light specials '' . the phrase `` attention kmart shoppers '' has been used in pop culture . many movies were filmed at kmart stores . these includes `` troop beverly hills '' , `` six days seven nights '' , `` rain man '' , `` beetlejuice '' , `` madea goes to jail '' and `` dawn of the dead '' . other websites . - kmart official website ( corporate ) - kmart official website ( online store ) - kmart2go - official mobile online store - kmartdesign.com microsite
