Jack Thompson (activist)    jack thompson ( activist ) jack thompson ( born july 25 , 1951 ) is an american activist and former lawyer . he is known for being an activist against sex and violence in video games . he has also fought against rap music , mainly because of its obscenity . in july 2008 , thompson was permanently disbarred , meaning his license to be a lawyer was taken away . that was done because of bad behavior and saying things that were n't true to tribunals . thompson was born in cleveland , ohio .
