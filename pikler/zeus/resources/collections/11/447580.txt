Derek Cooper     derek macdonald cooper ( 25 may 1925 – 18 april 2014 ) was a british journalist . he wrote about food , wine and whisky . he worked with `` the listener '' , `` the observer '' , `` home & garden '' and `` saga magazine '' . cooper created the idea of bbc radio 4 ’ s weekly program `` the food programme '' . he was born in london , england . cooper died from parkinson 's disease in london , aged 88. other websites . - broadcaster derek cooper , pioneer food journalist , dies at 88 at telegraph.com
