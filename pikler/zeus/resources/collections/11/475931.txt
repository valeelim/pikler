Vlastimil Bubník     vlastimil bubník ( ; 18 march 1931 – 6 january 2015 ) was a czech ice hockey player and footballer . bubník was born in kelč , czechoslovakia , and played in the czechoslovak extraliga . he played for hc brno and královo pole . he also won a bronze medal at the 1964 winter olympics . he was inducted into the international ice hockey federation hall of fame in 1997. other websites . - ice hockey profile - football profile
