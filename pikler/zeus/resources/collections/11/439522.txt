Garrick Utley     clifton garrick utley ( november 19 , 1939 – february 20 , 2014 ) , better known as garrick utley , was an american television journalist . he was best known for reporting about the vietnam war and was one of the first full-time television correspondent covering the war on-site . he was born in chicago , illinois . he graduated from westtown school in 1957 and from carleton college in 1961. utley died after a long battle with prostate cancer on february 20 , 2014 in new york city , new york . he was aged 74. he was outlived by his wife gertje utley ( née rommeswinkel ) .
