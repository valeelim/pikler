Angelines Fernández     maría de los ángeles `` angelines '' fernández abad ( 9 july 1922 – 25 march 1994 ) was a spanish-born mexican actress . she was best remembered for playing doña clotilde `` la bruja del 71 '' in the televisa sitcom `` el chavo del ocho '' . she was born in madrid , spain . fernández died from throat cancer in mexico city , mexico , aged 71 .
