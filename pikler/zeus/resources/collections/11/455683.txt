Seoul Metropolitan Subway     seoul metropolitan subway , often called seoul subway , is a rail transit system of subways and commuter trains that connects seoul and suburban areas in its vicinity . the system is consisted of 9 numeric-named lines ( line 1 ~ line 9 ) , gyeongui line , bundang line , shinbundang line , a'rex , jungang line , gyeongchun line , suin line , incheon line 1 , everline and u line . since 2010 , all stations have smartphone like 46-inch touchscreen kiosk , so that passengers can look at the map , make free phone calls , or get tourist information . all lines use special rfid cards , such as t-money , or nfc technology to impose traveling fee .
