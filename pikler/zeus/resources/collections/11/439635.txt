Per Theodor Haugen     per theodor haugen ( born 8 october 1932 ) is a norwegian actor . he started stage acting in 1950. he had a supporting role in the comedy movie `` støv på hjernen '' ( 1959 ) . he was born in kongsberg . he was married to actress sissel sellæg who died on 5 january 2014 in oslo . they had a son together , actor kim haugen .
