Benjamin Franklin Butler     benjamin franklin butler was an american politician and political general during the american civil war . he was born in new hampshire in 1818. he became a lawyer in 1840. during the civil war , he was one of the first generals in the union army . he also did n't return escaped slaves to their masters , claiming that they were . after the war , he was governor of massachusetts from january 1883 to january 1884. he died in 1893 .
