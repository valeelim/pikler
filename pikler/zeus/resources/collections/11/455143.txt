Hans-Adam II, Prince of Liechtenstein    hans-adam ii , prince of liechtenstein hans-adam ii ( johannes ( hans ) adam ferdinand alois josef maria marko d'aviano pius fürst von und zu liechtenstein ; born 14 february 1945 ) , is the reigning prince ( `` fürst '' ) of liechtenstein . he is the son of franz joseph ii , prince ( `` fürst '' ) of liechtenstein ( 1906–1989 ) and his wife countess georgina von wilczek ( 1921–1989 ) .
