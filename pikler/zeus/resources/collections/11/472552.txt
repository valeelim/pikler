Bound for Glory (2014)    bound for glory ( 2014 ) bound for glory ( 2014 ) was a professional wrestling pay-per-view show made by total nonstop action wrestling ( tna ) . it was held on october 12 , 2014 at the korakuen hall in tokyo , japan . the pay-per-view was a collaboration with the japanese puroresu promotion wrestle-1 . it was the tenth bound for glory event that tna held and was also the fourth and last event in tna 's 2014 pay-per-view schedule . the event was aired on tape delay because of the time difference . taz and mike tenay were not in japan at the event and instead did commentary from tna headquarters in nashville , tennessee . there were eight matches on the card . the main event saw the great muta and tajiri defeat james storm and the great sanada .
