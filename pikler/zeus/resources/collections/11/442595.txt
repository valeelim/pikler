Icchokas Meras     icchokas meras ( 8 october 1934 – 13 march 2014 ) was a lithuanian writer and screenwriter . on 6 december 2010 , he was awarded the lithuanian national prize . he was born in kelmė , šiauliai county . he was jewish and survived the holocaust . meras died on 13 march 2014 in tel aviv , israel . he was 79 years old . other websites . - icchokas meras at famous birthdays
