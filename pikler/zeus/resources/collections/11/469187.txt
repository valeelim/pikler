Lady Marmalade     `` lady marmalade '' is a r & b single . it was first performed by patti labelle and her group labelle . the song is about a prostitute in new orleans . the labelle version was released on august 3 , 1974. the song hit # 1 on the `` billboard '' hot 100 for one week . the song has been covered by other musicians , including all saints , christina aguilera and many more . a 2001 version from christina aguilera , lil ' kim , mya , and pink was part of the movie `` moulin rouge ! `` .
