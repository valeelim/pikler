Omamori     there are many types of omamori . - `` kōtsū-anzen '' : traffic safety . protection for drivers and travelers - `` yaku-yoke '' : avoidance of evil - `` kaiun '' : open luck , better fortune - `` gakugyō-jōju '' : education and passing examinations . for students and scholars - `` shōbai-hanjō '' : prosperity in business . success in business and matters of money - `` en-musubi '' : finding a mate and marriage . available for singles and couples to ensure love and marriage - `` anzan '' : protection for pregnant women for a healthy pregnancy and easy birth - `` kanai-anzen '' : safety ( well-being ) of one 's family ; peace and prosperity in the household other websites . - japanese buddhist statuary - omamori.com
