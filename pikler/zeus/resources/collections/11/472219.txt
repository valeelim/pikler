Queen Fabiola of Belgium     queen fabiola of belgium ( born `` doña '' fabiola de mora y aragón ; 11 june 1928 – 5 december 2014 ) was the widow of king baudouin . she was queen consort for 33 years , between her wedding in 1960 and her husband 's death in 1993. the couple were unable to have children . when he died , the crown passed to her his younger brother , king albert ii . on 5 december 2014 , the belgian royal palace announced queen fabiola had died that evening at stuyvenbergh castle in laeken , belgium , aged 86. other websites . - queen fabiola – official website of the belgian monarchy - queen fabiola : the woman behind the hairdo – website the royal universe
