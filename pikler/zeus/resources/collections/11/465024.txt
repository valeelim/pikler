Vaughn O. Lang     vaughn olin lang ( november 10 , 1927 – october 2 , 2014 ) was an american lieutenant general in the united states army . lang began his service as 2lt at the officers ’ basic course at ft. monmouth , new jersey in 1952. he served in the radio-radar procurement branch and in the european trans-atlantic project . lang was a general during the vietnam war . lang was born in williamsburg , pennsylvania . lang died on october 2 , 2014 , at his home in vienna , virginia , aged 86 .
