Kyle O'Reilly     kyle greenwood ( born march 1 , 1987 ) is a canadian professional wrestler . he is better known under his ring name kyle o'reilly . he is currently working for many different independent promotions which include ring of honor ( roh ) , pro wrestling guerrilla ( pwg ) , and elite canadian championship wrestling ( eccw ) . during his career , he has won pwg 's 2013 battle of los angeles , the pwg world championship ( where he is the current champion ) , and the roh world tag team championship three times with bobby fish . he has type 1 diabetes . he notes bret hart , toshiaki kawada , royce gracie , and muhammad ali as his role models . he lives with fellow wrestlers davey richards and tony kozina . championships . - nwa : extreme canadian championship wrestling - nwa canadian junior heavyweight championship ( three times ) - pacific cup ( 2007 ) - pro wrestling guerrilla - pwg world championship ( one time , current ) - battle of los angeles ( 2013 ) - pro wrestling illustrated - he was ranked # 73 of the top 500 singles wrestlers in the pwi 500 in 2013 .
