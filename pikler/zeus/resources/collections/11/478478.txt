Clan McDuck     the clan mcduck is a family of ducks in the disney fictional universe , based on the style of scottish families . the most famous member is scrooge mcduck . according to the timeline of don rosa , the clan has been known since 122 ad when a member yet unnamed clan sold the stone for the construction crew of hadrian 's wall . the clan 'scrooge was originally called clan macduich , but left the gaelic spelling of the name in 1071 and became the clan mcduck . donald , his sister della duck and his sons huey , dewey , and louie are the descendants of the clan through their maternal lineage .
