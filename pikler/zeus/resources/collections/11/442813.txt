Eyes Set to Kill     eyes set to kill is an american post-hardcore band from phoenix , arizona . sisters alexia and anissa rodriguez started the band with lindsey vogt in 2003. in 2005 caleb clifton joined the band . in 2006 the band 's first extended play was released . it sold 11,000 copies . vogt left the band in 2007. alexia took vogt 's place . the band released their first album `` reach '' on february 19 , 2008 .
