Hilda Bernard     hilda sarah bernard ( born october 29 , 1920 ) is a veteran argentine actress . she was born in puerto deseado , argentina . bernard is known for her roles in `` animalada '' , `` chronicle of the raven '' , and in `` live-in maid '' . her career began in 1941. bernard was married to horacio jorge goncalves and to horacio zelada , but both marriages ended in divorce . bernard has a daughter , patricia .
