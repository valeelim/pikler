Montserrat Abelló i Soler     montserrat abelló i soler ( 1 february 1918 – 9 september 2014 ) was a spanish catalan poet and translator . she translated the works of american novelist sylvia plath , welsh novelist dylan thomas , english writer e. m. forster , irish-born british author iris murdoch and iranian translator mohsen emadi from english into catalan . she also translated catalan works into english , including those of writers salvador espriu , mercè rodoreda , maria àngels anglada , maria mercè marçal and olga xirinacs díaz . abelló i soler was born in tarragona , province of tarragona . during the spanish civil war , she lived in exile in france , england and chile . abelló i soler died on 9 september 2014 in barcelona , aged 96. other websites . - montserrat abelló i soler at the association of catalan language writers ( aelc )
