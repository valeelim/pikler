Armageddon (1999)    armageddon ( 1999 ) armageddon ( 1999 ) was a professional wrestling pay-per-view show made by world wrestling federation . it was held on december 12 , 1999 at national car rental center in sunrise , florida . it was the first armageddon event held by the wwf . the main event saw triple h defeat vince mcmahon in a no holds barred match . triple h won the match by pinning vince after he hit mcmahon with a sledgehammer , when vince 's daughter and triple h 's ( kayfabe , at that time ) wife stephanie mcmahon had turned on vince by giving the sledgehammer to triple h. other websites . - official armageddon 1999 website
