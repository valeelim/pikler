Segunda División B     segunda división b is a football league which is the third division in spain . the league is made of 4 groups of 20 teams , making the amount of total teams 80 in all . in each group , the top 6 teams qualify for the copa del rey . the 2nd , 3rd , and 4th placed teams qualify for playoffs to be promoted to segunda division . the 1st placed team has to play a playoff with the champions of other groups and the winner is declared champion . in each group , the bottom 4 teams are directly sent to the tercera división . the 16th placed teams in each group compete against each other . the worst of those teams is relegated . these are all the group winners ever . the promoted group winners are in bold . `` note : since the 2008-09 season , the champion has been decided in a playoffs round . the winner of the playoffs final is highlighted in gold . the loser of the playoffs final is highlighted in silver .
