Anglosphere     anglosphere is a term used to mean a group of english-speaking nations with a similar cultural heritage . the two most important countries in the anglosphere are the united kingdom and the united states . others include the main members of the commonwealth of nations such as ireland , canada ( except the majority french-speaking province of quebec ) , australia and new zealand . the word means more than just those countries where english is a first or second language . to be considered part of the anglosphere requires sharing basic customs and values . `` nations comprising the anglosphere share a common historical narrative in which the magna carta , the english and american bills of rights , and such common law principles as trial by jury , presumption of innocence '' . by the time of the 2003 iraq war the political use of the word meant the cultural tendency towards military intervention , particularly by the us , the uk and australia . strategically , the word has come to mean that the us is usually backed by britain and australia , but not so much by canada . in this sense , canada is and sometimes is not considered a part of the anglosphere . this has been since the pierre trudeau era . america is generally considered by many to be the dominant country in the anglosphere .
