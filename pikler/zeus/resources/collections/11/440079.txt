Martin Hanzal     martin hanzal ( born february 20 , 1987 ) is a czech professional ice hockey centre . he currently plays for the arizona coyotes of the national hockey league ( nhl ) . he serves as their alternate captain . before playing in the nhl , hanzal played 19 games in the czech extraliga with hc české budějovice , 19 games in the united states hockey league ( ushl ) with the omaha lancers and 1 season in the western hockey league ( whl ) with the red deer rebels . he was drafted by the phoenix coyotes with the 17th overall pick in the 2005 nhl entry draft . he debuted with the coyotes during the 2007–08 nhl season . he scored his first hat-trick in a game against the toronto maple leafs on december 4 , 2008. the hat trick came in only 20 minutes and 27 seconds of play time which set the record for the fastest hat trick in coyotes history . he was named an alternate captain on october 8 , 2011 .
