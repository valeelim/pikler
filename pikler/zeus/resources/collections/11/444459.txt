Manuel Gutiérrez Mellado     manuel gutiérrez mellado ( april 30 , 1912 – december 15 , 1995 ) was a spanish politician and general . he was the deputy prime minister of spain under adolfo suárez . he served from 23 september 1976 trhough 26 february 1981. he was also spain 's first minister of defense from 1977 to 1979. mellado died in a road accident in torremocha del campo , spain , aged 83 .
