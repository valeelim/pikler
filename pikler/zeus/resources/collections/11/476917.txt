Whiplash (2014 movie)    whiplash ( 2014 movie ) whiplash is a 2014 american drama movie set in new york city . it is about a jazz student and his teacher . it was written and directed by damien chazelle . it stars miles teller , j. k. simmons , and paul reiser . filming of the movie took less than a month . whiplash was released on october 10 , 2014 with very positive reviews .
