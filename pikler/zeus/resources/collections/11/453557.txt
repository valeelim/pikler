José Feliciano     josé montserrate feliciano garcía ( born september 10 , 1945 ) , better known simply as josé feliciano , is a puerto rican virtuoso guitarist , singer and composer . he is known for many international hits , including his remake of the doors ' `` light my fire '' and the best-selling christmas single `` feliz navidad '' . other websites . - jose feliciano official website - josé feliciano singing `` che sarà '' in italian at the san remo festival in 1971
