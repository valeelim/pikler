Anders Larsson     anders larsson , born 3 october 1961 in karlstad , sweden is a swedish former cross-country skier . during his career , he represented bondsjöhöjdens ik in club competitions . he also participated at the 1984 olympic winter games in sarajevo . there , he ended up 39th in the 50 kilometers race . he also won vasaloppet in 1987. in 1982 he was awarded the sixten jernberg award .
