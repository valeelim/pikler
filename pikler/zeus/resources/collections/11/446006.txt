Pat Green     pat green is an american country musician active since 1995. he was born in san antonio , texas and raised in waco , texas . fifteen of his singles have charted on the `` billboard '' hot country songs charts . discography . - studio albums - `` dancehall dreamer '' ( 1995 ) - `` george 's bar '' ( 1997 ) - `` carry on '' ( 2000 ) - `` three days '' ( 2001 ) - `` wave on wave '' ( 2003 ) - `` lucky ones '' ( 2004 ) - `` cannonball '' ( 2006 ) - `` what i 'm for '' ( 2009 ) - `` songs we wish we 'd written ii '' ( 2012 )
