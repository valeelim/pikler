Pete Conacher     charles william `` pete '' conacher , jr. ( born july 29 , 1932 ) is a canadian retired ice hockey left winger . he played a career total of 229 regular season games in the national hockey league ( nhl ) . he played for the toronto maple leafs , chicago black hawks , and new york rangers . his father was nhl hall of famer , charlie conacher . before playing in the nhl , conacher played 3 seasons with the galt black hawks of the ontario hockey association ( oha ) . he then played for the chicago black hawks of the national hockey league ( nhl ) . he played 2 games with them before being sent down to their ahl-affiliate team , the st. louis flyers . he played 29 games with them before he was called back up to play for the black hawks . he would play parts of 3 seasons with the black hawks . on november 23 , 1954 , the black hawks traded conacher along with bill gadsby to the new york rangers in exchange for rich lamoureux , allan stanley and nick mickoski . on june 4 , 1957 , the rangers traded him to the toronto maple leafs for $ 15,000 . on november 18 , 1957 , the maple leafs traded conacher back to the rangers for $ 15,000 .
