Osamu Dazai     osamu dazai ( june 19 , 1909 – june 13 , 1948 ) , was a japanese author . dazai was born tsushima shūji in aomori , northeastern japan . he is best known for his novels `` the setting sun '' ( 1947 ) and `` no longer human '' ( 1948 ) . dazai wrote a number of novels about the meaning of life based on his own private life . dazai remains the most popular author among japanese college students .
