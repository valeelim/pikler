New Riders of the Purple Sage     new riders of the purple sage ( nrps ) is an american country rock band formed in 1969. it grew out of jam sessions between jerry garcia and john `` marmaduke '' dawson . they soon became an opening act for the grateful dead . they branched out on their own by the early 1970s . the new riders of the purple sage received a recording contract in 1971. they based their name on foy willing 's earlier group , riders of the purple sage adding `` new '' to their name . discography . - studio albums - `` new riders of the purple sage ( album ) |new riders of the purple sage '' ( 1971 ) - `` powerglide '' ( 1972 ) - `` gypsy cowboy '' ( 1972 ) - `` the adventures of panama red '' ( 1973 ) - `` brujo '' ( 1974 ) - `` oh , what a mighty time '' ( 1975 ) - `` new riders '' ( 1976 ) - `` who are those guys !
