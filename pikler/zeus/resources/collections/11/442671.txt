Vybz Kartel     adidja azim palmer ( born 7 january 1976 ) better known as vybz kartel , is a jamaican dancehall musician . kartel started a line of liquor in 2008. kartel has worked with jay-z , rihanna , missy elliott , busta rhymes , m.i.a , pharrell , kardinal offishall , akon , jim jones , lil wayne and eminem . on 29 september 2011 , kartel was arrested . jamaica 's major investigation taskforce ( mit ) charged him with murder . kartel was found guilty after a 65-day trial .
