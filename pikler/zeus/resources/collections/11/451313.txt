Seal of Montana     the great seal of the state of montana is the official seal of the u.s. state of montana . it was adopted in 1865 , when montana was a united states territory . when montana became a state in 1889 , it was decided to use the same seal . the outer ring of the seal has the words `` the great seal of the state of montana '' . the inner circle showss a landscape of mountains , plains and forests by the great falls on the missouri river .
