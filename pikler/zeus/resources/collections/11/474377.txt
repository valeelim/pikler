Down (Stone Temple Pilots song)    down ( stone temple pilots song ) `` down '' is the first single off american hard rock band stone temple pilots ' fourth studio album `` no . 4 '' . the song was released on april 5 , 1999. the song is also featured on stp 's compilation albums `` thank you '' and `` buy this '' . on the `` billboard '' charts , `` down '' was able to peak at # 5 on the mainstream rock tracks chart , # 7 on the bubbling under hot 100 singles chart and at # 9 on the modern rock tracks chart . in 2001 , the song was nominated for a grammy award for best hard rock performance at the 43rd annual grammy awards but it lost to `` guerrilla radio '' by rage against the machine . other websites . - `` down '' on youtube
