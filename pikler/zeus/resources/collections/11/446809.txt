Diamanda Galás     diamanda galás ( born 1955 ) is an american avant-garde artist . galás was born and raised in san diego , california , to greek orthodox parents . in 1982 her first album was released . in 1988 galás joined act up . in 1996 a book she wrote called `` the shit of god '' was published . in 2005 galás was given the demetrio stratos international career award .
