Muhammadgarh (princely state)    muhammadgarh ( princely state ) muhammadgarh was a former state in central india , under the bhopal agency . it was situated in the malwa plateau . it had an area of 29 square miles ( 75 km2 ) , and a population of 2,944 ( as of 1901 ) . its headquarters were at muhammadgarh town .
