Nick Lachey     nicholas scott `` nick '' lachey ( ; born november 9 , 1973 ) is an american singer-songwriter , actor , producer , and television personality . he was best known as the lead singer of the multi-platinum selling boy band 98 degrees . lachey was born in harlan , kentucky and was raised in cincinnati , ohio . he is of dutch , english , french , german , irish , scottish and welsh descent . he was married to singer jessica simpson from 2002 to 2006. he married model vanessa minnillo in 2011. their son , camden john lachey , was born on september 12 , 2012 .
