Steve Smith (American Dad!)    steve smith ( american dad ! ) steven anita `` steve '' smith is a fictional character in the animated television series `` american dad ! '' . he is voiced by scott grimes . he is stan and francine smith 's only son and hayley 's younger brother as well as the youngest of the series ' six main characters . steve 's original design was much geekier and gawky and he was voiced by ricky blitt . halfway through production , grimes replaced blitt and steve 's design was made to be more attractive , so he would be less comparable to neil goldman from `` family guy '' .
