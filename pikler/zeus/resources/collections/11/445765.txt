Clive Owen     clive owen ( born 3 october 1964 ) is an english actor . he won a golden globe and a bafta award and was nominated for an academy award for best supporting actor for his role in the drama movie `` closer '' ( 2004 ) . he also gained fame for his roles in `` close my eyes '' ( 1991 ) , `` croupier '' ( 1998 ) , `` sin city '' ( 2005 ) , `` derailed '' ( 2005 ) `` inside man '' ( 2006 ) , `` children of men '' ( 2006 ) , and `` the international '' ( 2009 ) . he is best known on television for his leading role in the itv program `` chancer '' from 1990 to 1991. owen was born in coventry , warwickshire . he has been married to sarah-jane fenton since 1995. they have two daughters , hannah and eve .
