Emmy Köhler     emmy köhler , born 22 may 1858 in stockholm , sweden , died 2 february 1925 in fresta , sweden was a swedish hymnwriter and writer . among her more famous works is the christmas carol `` nu tändas tusen juleljus '' and the music for the children 's christmas song `` raska fötter springa tripp , tripp , tripp '' ( `` liten julvisa '' ) . the lyrics of the later mentioned song were written by sigrid sköldberg-pettersson .
