Catsuit     catsuits are close-fitting one-piece garments that cover the torso and the legs , and often the arms . they are often made with leather , chiffon , latex or spandex materials . the suits usually close with zippers in the front or back . catsuits were worn as early as the 1940s . they can be worn by males or females . despite their name , they do not have feline characteristics . some people consider catsuits to be fetish items .
