Ravaillacz     ravaillacz is a superquartet from sweden . they participated at melodifestivalen 2013 with the charting song `` '' en riktig jävla schlager '' '' . the song ended up 10th . the group consists of tommy körberg , claes malmberg , johan rabaeus and mats ronander . the group also appeared at allsång på skansen on 6 august 2013 .
