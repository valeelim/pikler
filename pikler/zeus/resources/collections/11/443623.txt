Maddie Blaustein     madeleine `` maddie '' blaustein ( october 9 , 1960 – december 11 , 2008 ) was an american voice actress . until her death , blaustein was the voice of sartorius in `` yu-gi-oh ! gx '' . she was also the voice of meowth in the `` pokémon '' anime . maddie blaustein was an american star ! blaustein was born intersex . she was a male when she was born , and lived as male for many years until changing to female .
