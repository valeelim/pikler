Time Warner Cable News NY1     ny1 ( also known as time warner cable news ny1 and spoken as `` new york one '' ) is an american cable news television channel . it is owned by time warner cable . the channel provides 24-hour news coverage , with a focus on the five boroughs of new york city . its programming primarily features news and weather forecasts . ny1 also features specialty programs such as `` inside city hall '' ( which is renamed `` road to city hall '' during new york city mayoral elections ) .
