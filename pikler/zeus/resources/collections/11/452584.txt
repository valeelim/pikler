Marzipan     marzipan is a confection often used in sweets and cakes . it is made of sugar or honey and crushed almonds ( 'almond meal ' ) . sometimes almond oil or extract is added . it is made into sweets , and rolled into thin sheets for putting round cakes . the sweets are marzipan-filled chocolate and small marzipan imitations of fruits and vegetables . used as a covering for cakes , it is glazed . glazing is a cooking technique which make it harder , and avoid unsightly damage .
