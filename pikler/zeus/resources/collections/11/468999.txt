Ipbuk-dong     ipbuk-dong is a place in suwon where many people live . ipbuk-dong has some apartments named seosuwon xii , prugio and ungbi . ipbuk elementary school has 314 children . ipbuk-dong has some small academies that teach piano , art , and taekwondo . ipbuk-dong has some small restaurants that serve chinese food and chicken . ipbuk-dong has some small shops , dry cleaners , and hardware shops .
