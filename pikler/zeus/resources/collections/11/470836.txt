Gijón     gijón is a city in the autonomous community of asturias in spain . it is located on the bay of biscay , about north of oviedo . gijón has a population of 275,274 inhabitants , more than any other city in asturias . for most of its history gijón was just a small town and seaport . after the conquest of the asturias in the 3rd century bc by the romans they called the site `` gigia '' . in 714 a garrison was established by the moors . from here they attacked the asturias but failed in the attempt . after the moors left , gijón was ruled by asturian kings . in the 10th century the asturias became part of the kingdom of león . in the 16th century the harbor was improved under philip ii of spain . some of the ships of the spanish armada took shelter at gijón 's port in a violent storm . they were on their way to attack britain in 1588. after the epic battle , a few of the remaining ships limped back to gijón . during the last half of the 19th century a railroad was built to gijón to ship asturian coal to other parts of spain . this was the start of gijón becoming a large industrial center . during the spanish civil war part of gijón was destroyed .
