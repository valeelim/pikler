Central West (New South Wales)    central west ( new south wales ) the central west is a region of new south wales , australia . the region is west of the blue mountains , which are west of sydney . it has an area of 63,262 square kilometres ( 24,426 square miles ) . major towns and cities in the central west include the cities of bathurst and orange ; and the large towns of cowra and parkes . bathurst and orange have campuses of charles sturt university , the only local provider of higher education for the region .
