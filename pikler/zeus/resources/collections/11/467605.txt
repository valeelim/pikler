Recess (album)    recess ( album ) recess is the debut studio album by american electronic music producer , skrillex . `` recess '' was released on march 14 , 2014. it was released through big beat records , asylum records , and atlantic records . the album debuted at # 4 on the billboard 200. skrillex recorded the album while he was touring around the world , through countries such as the united states , canada , south korea , and the united kingdom . the album featured the singles `` try it out '' and `` recess '' .
