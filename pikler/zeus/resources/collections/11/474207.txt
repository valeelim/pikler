Fritz Sdunek     fritz sdunek ( pronounced `` free-ts zdoo-neck '' , april 18 , 1947 – december 22 , 2014 ) was a german professional boxing trainer and former amateur boxer . sdunek was one of the most successful and famous boxing trainers . he trained , among others , such world champions as wladimir and vitali klitschko and dariusz michalczewski . he was born in lüssow , germany . sdunek died in hamburg on december 22 , 2014 , at the age of 67 .
