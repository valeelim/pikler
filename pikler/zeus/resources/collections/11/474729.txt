Leopoldo Federico     leopoldo federico ( 12 january 1927 – 28 december 2014 ) was an argentine bandoneon player , arranger , music director and composer . he was born in buenos aires . in 1955 he joined astor piazzolla 's octeto buenos aires and later that year , with his own orquesta típica , he made many recordings with the singer julio sosa . federico died on 28 december 2014 in buenos aires . he was 87. other websites . - todo tango : federico
