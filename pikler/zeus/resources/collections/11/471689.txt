Aqueous solution     an aqueous solution is a solution where the solvent is water . in a chemical equation it is written by adding ( aq ) to the formula . a solution of ordinary table salt in water would be written as nacl ( aq ) . nacl is the part that means table salt . the word `` aqueous '' means being dissolved in water . water is a solvent . it is found all over the place . water is a solvent in chemistry .
