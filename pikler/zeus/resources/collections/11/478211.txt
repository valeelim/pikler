Jessica Ennis-Hill     jessica ennis-hill ( born 28 january 1986 ) , is a british track and field athlete . she specializes in multi-eventing disciplines and 100 meter hurdles . she is a member of the city of sheffield athletic club . ennis-hill is the current olympic heptathlon champion . she is the former european and world heptathlon champion . ennis-hill is also the former world indoor women 's pentathlon champion . she is the current british national record holder for the heptathlon and the indoor pentathlon . she is a former british record holder in the 100 metres hurdles and the high jump .
