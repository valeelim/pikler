Tana River (Norway)    tana river ( norway ) the tana river ( or ; ; ) , is a river in sápmi area of northern scandinavia . the river flows through finnmark county , norway and the lapland region of finland . the sámi name means `` great river '' . the main tributaries of tana are anarjohka and karasjohka rivers . it is one of the world 's most important rivers for atlantic salmon reproduction . related pages . - atlantic salmon other websites . - the tana river delta website - salmon - king of the major rivers ( teno )
