I Wanna Be Down    i wan na be down `` i wan na be down '' is a 1994 major hit song by brandy norwood . the single is about her flirting with a boy , whom she tries to show that she is sexy . the song was released in september 1994. the song hit # 6 on `` billboard '' hot 100. it also hit # 1 on `` billboard '' hot r & b singles chart for four weeks .
