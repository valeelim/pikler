Suspicion (1941 movie)    suspicion ( 1941 movie ) suspicion is a 1941 romantic thriller movie . it is based on the novel before the fact . the movie was directed by alfred hitchcock . cary grant plays johnnie , a gambler . joan fontaine plays lina . dame may whitty plays martha . this movie was nominated for academy awards for the best picture and the best original score . fontaine won the best actress award .
