Mark Strand     mark strand ( april 11 , 1934 - november 29 , 2014 ) was a canadian-american poet , essayist , and translator . he was appointed poet laureate consultant in poetry to the library of congress in 1990. he was a professor of english and comparative literature at columbia university . strand was born in summerside , prince edward island . strand died on november 29 , 2014 , in brooklyn , new york city from liposarcoma , aged 80 .
