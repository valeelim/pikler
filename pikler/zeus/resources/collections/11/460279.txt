René Casados     rené casados morales ( born october 21 , 1961 ) is a mexican actor . he is best known for his roles in telenovelas . his career began during the 1970s . as of february 2010 , he has appeared in nineteen telenovelas ( spanish-language soap operas ) . he is known for his roles `` la madrastra '' as bruno mendizábal and `` fuego en la sangre '' as father tadeo .
