Xherdan Shaqiri     xherdan shaqiri ( born 10 october 1991 ) , is a swiss association football player of yugoslavian and albanian descent who plays for bayern munich and the switzerland national football team . he has also played for fc basel . since he first started playing football , he has been known for his fast speed on and off the ball . the fifa website describes shaqiri as `` unpredictable on the ball , adept with both feet , clinical in front of goal and [ possessing ] excellent vision '' . he has been nicknamed the `` alpine messi '' and `` magic dwarf '' . in the 2014 fifa world cup , he was one of the top scorers with 3 goals after scoring a hat-trick against honduras .
