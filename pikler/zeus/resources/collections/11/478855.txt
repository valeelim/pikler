Bruksvallsloppet     bruksvallsloppet is an international cross-country skiing event , held in bruksvallarna , sweden over a friday-sunday in november every year . the event acts as fis world cup preselection . the competitions are international . during some seasons the event is the final before the world cup opens . during other seasons , the event instead occurs between world cupe events . this depends on if the fis world cup starts by late october , or if it begins by mid-late november . in sweden , the event is generally considered the first major competition of the season . other websites . - officiall website
