Frank Torre     frank joseph torre ( december 30 , 1931 – september 13 , 2014 ) was an american baseball player . he was first baseman in major league baseball . torre who batted and threw left-handed . he played for the milwaukee braves ( 1956–60 ) and philadelphia phillies ( 1962–63 ) . he was the older brother of hall of famer joe torre . torre died in palm beach gardens , florida from cardiac arrest , aged 82 .
