The Crusher (album)    the crusher ( album ) the crusher was the third full-length album by the death metal band amon amarth . it was released on may 8 2001 by metal blade records . a deluxe edition was released in 2009 , remastered by jens bogren . about.com gave the album 4 stars , saying that `` amon amarth are , simply put , one of the best metal bands of this generation . '' allmusic also gave the album three and a half stars saying that `` listeners who enjoy this kind of metal should certainly check out amon amarth , and the crusher would be a good place to start . '' band members . - fredrik andersson - drums - olavi mikkonen - guitars - johan hegg - vocals - johan söderberg - rhythm guitar - ted lundström - bass guitar
