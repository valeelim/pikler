Ghostface (Scream)    ghostface ( scream ) ghostface is a fictional serial killer and the main villain of the `` scream movie franchise '' . he is wearing a white mask with a shocked/surprised expression . he also wears a black robe . he was created by wes craven . he has a rough voice . he is voiced by roger l. jackson . different people play ghostface throughout the movie because they pretend to be the original killer . he kills people in the hollywood-area . he uses a large kitchen knife as a weapon . his appearance is inspired by the painting the scream .
