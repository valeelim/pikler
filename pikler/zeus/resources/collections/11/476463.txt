Ehrenfried von Tschirnhaus     ehrenfried walther von tschirnhaus ( or tschirnhausen ) ( 10 april 1651 – 11 october 1708 ) was a german mathematician , physicist , doctor , and philosopher . late in the seventeenth century , he was charged with making a survey of the minerals of saxony . he had a government position there . he and his assistant , johann friedrich böttger , discovered how to make porcelain . before that , it had only been made in asia . tschirnhaus 's work in mathematics is remembered in the tschirnhaus transformation . it removed some intermediate terms from a given algebraic equation . he is also remembered in the tschirnhausen cubic . his work in physics included work in optics . working with martin knorr , he developed an improved polishing technique . this was used in the lenses and mirrors he made . he made burning glasses of high quality . he used these in experiments .
