Natural Born Killers     natural born killers is a 1994 american crime drama movie directed by oliver stone . it is about a couple in love who perform murder across the southwestern united states . mickey knox is played by woody harrelson . mallory knox is played by juliette lewis . ed wilson is played by rodney dangerfield . tommy lee jones also acts in the movie . this movie was released on august 26 , 1994. it was threatened with an nc-17 rating by the mpaa . it was then edited downward to r-rating level . the movie received mixed reviews from the critics .
