Princess Estelle, Duchess of Östergötland    princess estelle , duchess of östergötland princess estelle of sweden ( born 23 february 2012 ) is the oldest child of crown princess victoria of sweden . her younger brother is prince oscar , duke of scania . she is second in line of succession to the swedish throne . the princess was born at 04:26 central european time on 23 february 2012. she was named the following day . titles and styles . - 23 february 2012 – present : `` her royal highness '' princess estelle of sweden , duchess of östergötland
