Lorimar-Telepictures     lorimar-telepictures was an entertainment company established on april 21 , 1986 with the merger of lorimar and telepictures . its assets included television production and syndication ( which operated under the lorimar-telepictures name ) , feature films , home video , and broadcasting . the merger of lorimar-telepictures was announced on october 7 , 1985 by merv adelson . on april 21 , 1986 , the merger was complete . lee rich , one of the founders of lorimar sold his shares in 1986 and left the company . on january 11 , 1989 , warner communications acquired lorimar-telepictures after shaking off the hostile takeover of the company . note : all series listed here are now owned and distributed by warner bros. television with a few exceptions . - `` the $ 1,000,000 chance of a lifetime '' - `` alf '' ( 1986–1989 ) ( currently distributed by debmar-mercury ) - `` alvin and the chipmunks '' ( 1986–1989 ) - `` animalympics '' - `` blood & orchids '' - `` chiller '' - `` the comic strip '' - `` dallas '' ( 1986–1989 ) - `` a death in california ''
