Chao (Sonic)    chao ( sonic ) chao are fictional characters that appear in the `` sonic the hedgehog '' series of video games . they are small , childlike creatures that do not have a gender . they have a pudding-like body and act like human babies . they first appeared in `` sonic adventure '' . in the `` sonic '' games , chao are digital pets . several different types of chao are in the game . the chao can evolve into many different forms , depending on how the player raises it . the player can raise them and enter them into races or battles . they have also been a part of the games ' story and are sometimes seen during parts of the games . for example , the character cream the rabbit has a chao named cheese , which she uses to defeat enemies . other websites . - chao island
