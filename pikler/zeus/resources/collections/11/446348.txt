Harald Nugiseks     harald nugiseks ( 22 october 1921 – 2 january 2014 ) was an ss-oberscharführer ( sergeant ) in world war ii . he served at his own free will in the 20th waffen grenadier division of the ss ( 1st estonian ) of the waffen ss . he was also one of the four estonian soldiers who received the knight 's cross of the iron cross . he was born in karjaküla , särevere parish , järva county . nugiseks died from natural causes on 2 january 2014 , aged 92. other websites . - harald nugiseks at estonian legion
