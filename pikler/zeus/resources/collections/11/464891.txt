Casualty (person)    casualty ( person ) a casualty in military usage is a person in military service , who becomes unavailable for duty due to death or injury . it also includes illness , capture , desertion , etc . ; or a civilian casualty . in civilian usage the word `` casualty '' is properly used for a person who is killed , wounded or injured by some event . it is usually used to describe multiple deaths and injuries due to violent incidents or disasters . `` casualties '' is sometimes loosely used to mean `` fatalities '' , but non-fatal injuries are also casualties .
