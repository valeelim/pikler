Jerry Sheindlin     gerald `` jerry '' sheindlin ( born november 19 , 1933 ) is an american author and television personality . he was a short-lived judge on the television program `` the people 's court '' from 1999 to 2000. before that , he served on the new york state supreme court . he is the author of two books , `` genetic fingerprinting : the law and science of dna evidence '' and `` blood trail '' .
