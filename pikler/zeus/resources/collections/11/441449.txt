Alice in Videoland     alice in videoland is an electroclash band from sweden . the band started in 2002. toril lindqvist is the singer . in 2003 the band released their first album `` maiden voyage '' in sweden . in 2005 they released their second album `` outrageous ! '' . the band was signed by the american record label storming the base . in 2008 the band 's third album `` she 's a machine ! '' was released . `` she 's a machine '' was released as the album 's first single . in 2010 the band 's fourth album `` a million thoughts and they 're all about you '' was released .
