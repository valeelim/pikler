Ucluelet     ucluelet is a district municipality in the canadian province of british columbia . ucluelet is found on the ucluelet peninsula on the west coast of vancouver island . the name ucluelet means `` people of the safe harbour '' in the native nuu-chah-nulth ( nootka ) language . the population of ucluelet was 1,627 in 2011. the mayor of ucluelet is bill irving . other websites . - official website
