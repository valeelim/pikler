Marajó     marajó is an island in brazil at the mouth of the amazon river . it is part of the state of pará . marajó has a land area of 40,100 km² ( 15,500 sq mi ) . that is similar in size to switzerland . the island is almost completely surrounded by fresh water . large parts of the island are flooded during the wet season because of higher water levels of the amazon river . the most important towns are in the southeast of the island : soure , salvaterra , and the largest city , breves .
