Akshay Kumar     akshay kumar is an indian film actor and producer . he is based in mumbai . kumar is known for his hit movies . kumar recently dubbed for the hindi version of the movie `` '' . kumar was the voice actor for the role of optimus prime . this is one of the key characters in the film . he did the dubbing role as a gift for his son , aarav . optimus prime was aarav 's favorite character so kumar did it for free . kumar was the third highest paid actor in bollywood in 2015. he has established himself as a leading contemporary actor in the hindi cinema . in 2009 , kumar started the hari om entertainment production company . in 2012 , kumar launched his next production house grazing goat pictures . their first production , `` omg - oh my god ! `` , had a slow opening , but because of word of mouth it picked up and then was declared a super hit .
