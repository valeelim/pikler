Viverravidae     the viverravidae are an extinct family of early mammalian carnivores . they are in the superfamily miacoidea , and lived from the early palaeocene to the eocene . they first appeared in the paleocene of north america about 60 million years ago . viverravidae is a monophyletic family . in viverravids , the number of molars is reduced to two and the skull is long . they are probably not related to any living carnivorans . the viverravids were previously thought to be the earliest carnivorans . a recent study of their skull morphology puts them outside the order carnivora .
