Nina Timofeeva     nina vladimirovna timofeeva ( ; 11 june 1935 - 3 november 2014 ) was a russian ballet dancer . timofeeva was born in leningrad , soviet union . she was known for her performance in `` swan lake '' ( 1956 , 1970 ) , `` don quixote '' ( 1959 ) , `` the tale of the stone flower '' ( 1959 ) , and in `` macbeth '' ( 1980 ) . timofeeva died in moscow , russia , aged 79 .
