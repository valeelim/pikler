Dennis Hastert     john dennis `` denny '' hastert ( ; born january 2 , 1942 ) is an american politician , lobbyist and member of the republican party . he was the 59th speaker of the united states house of representatives , serving from 1999 to 2007. he represented for twenty years , 1987 to 2007. on may 28 , 2015 , hastert was indicted by federal prosecutors . the legal charges against him are that he did not follow a law that says banks should report cash transactions over $ 10,000 , and that he made false statements to the fbi about his withdrawals , in a hush money scheme . he was found guilty on april 27 , 2016 and was sentenced to two years in prison . hastert was born on january 2 , 1942 , in aurora , illinois . he grew up in rural illinois . he graduate from wheaton college in 1964. his degree was in economics . in 1967 he got a masters in philosophy of education from northern illinois university . after leaving government , he returned to rural illinois . hastert now lives in plano , illinois . other websites .
