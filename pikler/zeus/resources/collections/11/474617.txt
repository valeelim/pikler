Mudavadekh     mudavadekh is a village in khanpur taluka of mahisagar district , gujarat , india . people of various castes of the hindu religion live there . most of the people are from patel and brahmin castes . the village has a school for 1 to 12. a branch of baroda gujarat gramin bank is also there . the postal index number or pin code for the village is 389230. the head post office is lunawada . the main source of income for people is farming .
