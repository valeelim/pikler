Edwards Barham     erle edwards barham ( july 10 , 1937 – october 17 , 2014 ) was an american farmer and conservationist . he was the first republican elected—by a 29-vote margin—to the louisiana state senate since the era of reconstruction . barham represented the agricultural district 33 from 1976 to 1980. he was narrowly unseated in the 1979 nonpartisan blanket primary by the democrat david `` bo '' ginn of bastrop , who held the seat until 1988. barham died in a plane crash on october 17 , 2014. he was attempting to take off at rayville municipal airport when his beechcraft bonanza aircraft struck a perimeter fence .
