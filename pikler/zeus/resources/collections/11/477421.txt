Stuart Loory     stuart hugh loory ( may 22 , 1932 – january 16 , 2015 ) was an american journalist and educator . he was born in wilson , pennsylvania . in 1980 , he joined the staff of turner broadcasting systems ' cable news network ( as managing editor of the washington bureau , 1980–82 ; moscow bureau chief , 1983–86 ; senior correspondent , 1986 ; executive producer , 1987–90 ; editor-in-chief of `` cnn world report '' , 1990–91 ; vice-president of cnn , 1990–95 ; executive vice-president , turner international broadcasting , russia , 1993–97 ) . loory died of lung cancer in brooklyn , new york on january 16 , 2015 , aged 82. other websites . - stuart loory page via university of missouri - stuart h. loory biography via reporting civil rights
