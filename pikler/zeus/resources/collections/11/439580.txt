Pöytyä     pöytyä is a municipality in southwest finland . as of january 2014 about 8,600 people lived there . neighbouring municipalities are aura , eura , loimaa , marttila , mynämäki , nousiainen , oripää , rusko , säkylä , tarvasjoki and turku . most people in pöytyä speak finnish . other websites . - pöytyä website
