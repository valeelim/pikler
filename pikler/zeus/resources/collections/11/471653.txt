Chespirito (TV series)    chespirito ( tv series ) chespirito , also known as los supergenios de la mesa cuadrada from 1968–1970 and chespirito y la mesa cuadrada from 1970-1971. it was a mexican sketch comedy show . it aired in mexico for two periods , 1968–1973 and 1980-1995. the program aired various sketches of characters created by roberto gómez bolaños .
