John Philip Holland     john philip holland ( ) ( 29 february 184012 august 1914 ) was an irish-american engineer . he developed the first submarine to be formally commissioned by the u.s. navy . he also created the first royal navy submarine , the `` holland 1 '' . he is often known as the father of the modern submarine for his designs . holland died on 12 august 1914 in newark , new jersey , aged 74 .
