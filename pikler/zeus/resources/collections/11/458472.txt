Julio Grondona     julio humberto grondona ( september 18 , 1931 – july 30 , 2014 ) was an argentine football executive . he served as president of the argentine football association ( `` asociación del fútbol argentino '' ) from 1979 until his death in 2014. he also served as senior vice-president of fifa ( `` fédération internationale de football association '' ) . he was involved in numerous scandals and has been accused by media outlets of being a member of the mafia . grondona died in buenos aires , argentina from an aortic aneurysm , aged 82. other websites . - grondona julio h .
