John Conyers     john james conyers , jr. ( born may 16 , 1929 ) is an american politician . the u.s. representative for michigan 's 13th district , serving in congress since 1965 ( the district was numbered as the 1st district until 1993 , and as the 14th district from 1993 to 2013 ) . he is a member of the democratic party . conyers , as its longest-serving current member , is the dean of the house of representatives . he is also the longest-serving current member of the entire united states congress . other websites . - congressman john conyers , jr. official u.s. house site - john conyers jr for congress - global family day movement co-founded by john conyers and linda grover
