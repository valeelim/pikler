Manoj Kumar     manoj kumar ( born 24 july 1937 ) is a bollywood indian actor . he was born in jandiala sher khan , a town in punjab . he started his career appearing on 1965 film `` shaheed '' . he is hindu and studied at hindu college of delhi . he is married to shashi goswami , a hindu and has two children ; vishal and kunal . he also has a brother named rajiv goswami . he has a master 's degree . he is one of the biggest stars in `` bollywood '' .
