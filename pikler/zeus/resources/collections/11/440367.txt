Mário Soares     mário alberto nobre lopes soares , , , , ( ; 7 december 1924 – 7 january 2017 ) was a portuguese politician . he served as prime minister of portugal from 1976 to 1978 and from 1983 to 1985. he soon became president of portugal from 1986 to 1996. other websites . - fundação mário soares - soares ' official site for the portuguese elections of 2006
