First inauguration of Dwight D. Eisenhower     the first inauguration of dwight d. eisenhower as the 34th president of the united states was held on january 20 , 1953. the inauguration was the beginning of the first four-year term of dwight d. eisenhower as president and richard nixon as vice president . chief justice fred m. vinson administered the oath of office to eisenhower . nixon was sworn in as vice president by senator william knowland of california earlier .
