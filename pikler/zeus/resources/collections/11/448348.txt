Dove Lake (Tasmania)    dove lake ( tasmania ) dove lake is a small glacial lake in tasmania , australia . it is in the cradle mountain-lake st clair national park which is part of the tasmanian wilderness world heritage site . dove lake is at the foot of cradle mountain . there is a 5.7 km walk around the lake , which has been described as one of the most popular wilderness walks in tasmania . the lake covers an area of about 85 hectares , and is about 60 metres deep .
