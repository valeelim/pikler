Ghostbusters II     ghostbusters ii is a 1989 american supernatural fantasy comedy movie . it is produced and directed by ivan reitman . it is the sequel to the 1984 movie `` ghostbusters '' . it stars bill murray , sigourney weaver , harold ramis , dan aykroyd , ernie hudson , annie potts , peter macnicol , wilhelm von homburg , and rick moranis . it follows the further adventures of the four parapsychologists and their organization which fights paranormal activities . it was released on june 16 , 1989. the movie earned $ 215 million at the box office and received mixed reviews .
