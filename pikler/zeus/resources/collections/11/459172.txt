Richard Schweiker     richard schultz schweiker ( june 1 , 1926 – july 31 , 2015 ) was an american politician . a member of the republican party , he served as the 14th u.s. secretary of health and human services under president ronald reagan from 1981 to 1983. he previously served as a u.s. representative ( 1961–1969 ) and a u.s. senator ( 1969–1981 ) from pennsylvania . on july 31 , 2015 , schweiker died from complications due to an infection at the atlanticare regional medical center in pomona , new jersey , aged 89. other websites . - church committee report ( book v : j.f.k . assassination )
