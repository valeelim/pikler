Lasse Frykberg     lars frykberg , born 3 january 1957 , is a swedish former cross-country skier . he won vasaloppet in 1982. at the time , he competed for ifk mora . in a competition where jean-paul pierrat from france first reached the finish . however , jean-paul pierrat was disqualified for changing skis ( which was not allowed ) . he also won worldloppet in the seasons of 1981/1982 and 1982/1983 .
