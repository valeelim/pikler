Karin Söder     karin ann-marie söder ( 30 november 1928 – 19 december 2015 ) was a swedish centre party politician . she was a member of the swedish cabinet between 1976-1978 and again in 1979. she was the first female leader of a major political party in sweden . she also was of the first female foreign ministers in the world . she also worked successful for systembolaget not being opened on saturdays .
