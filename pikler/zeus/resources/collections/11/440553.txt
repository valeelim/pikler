Valéry Giscard d'Estaing     valéry marie rené georges giscard d'estaing ( ; born 2 february 1926 ) is a french centrist politician . he is a member of the constitutional council of france . he served as president of france from 1974 until 1981. he was born in koblenz , germany . d'estaing once made-up a story about him having an affair with diana , princess of wales . other websites . - personal blog of valéry giscard d'estaing - biography on the french national assembly website - first and second-round results of french presidential elections
