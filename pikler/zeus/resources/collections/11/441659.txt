The Stone Roses     the stone roses are an english rock band . it was started in manchester in 1983. in 1989 the band released their first album `` the stone roses '' . seven singles were released from the album . in 1994 the band released their second album , `` second coming '' . in 1996 the band broke up . the band got back together in 2011. on may 21 2012 the band performed for the first time in 16 years . in 2013 a documentary called `` the stone roses : made of stone '' was released .
