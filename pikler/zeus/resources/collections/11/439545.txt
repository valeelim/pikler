Hankasalmi     hankasalmi is a municipality in central finland . as of january 2014 , about 5,400 people lived there . neighbouring municipalities are kangasniemi , konnevesi , laukaa , pieksämäki , rautalampi and toivakka . most people speak finland . hankasalmi was established in 1872. other websites . - the official hankasalmi page
