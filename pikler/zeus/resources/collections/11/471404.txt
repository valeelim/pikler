Tapan Raychaudhuri     tapan raychaudhuri ( 8 may 1924 - 26 november 2014 ) was an indian historian . he was an expert in british indian history , indian economic history and the history of bengal . he was a reader in modern south asian history at university of oxford from 1973 to 1992. he was also fellow of st. anthony college , oxford from 1973-93 where he remained as an emeritus fellow until his death in 2014. raychaudhuri died after suffering a series of strokes at his home in oxford , england on 26 november 2014 , aged 90 .
