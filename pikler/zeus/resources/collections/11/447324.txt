Love in the Time of Cholera (movie)    love in the time of cholera ( movie ) love in the time of cholera is a 2007 drama movie directed by mike newell . it is based on gabriel garcía márquez 's novel `` love in the time of cholera '' . it stars javier bardem , benjamin bratt , hector elizondo , liev schreiber , john leguizamo , and fernanda montenegro . it was released on november 16 , 2007 .
