Agloe, New York    agloe , new york agloe , new york is a made-up town in new york . it was created in the 1930s by the general drafting company to use in their maps as a copyright trap - a false fact that is created to see if someone copied from that source . agloe has an important role in the book `` paper towns '' by john green . the population of agloe is 1 in the book paper towns .
