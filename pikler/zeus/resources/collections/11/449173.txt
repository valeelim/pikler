Juan de Dios Castillo     juan de dios castillo gonzález ( 31 january 1951 – 1 may 2014 ) was a mexican association football player and coach . he coached honduran club c.d . motagua before his death . he also became the head coach of el salvador national football team in july 2012. he was born in mexico city . castillo died from skin cancer on 1 may 2014 in monterrey , nuevo león . he was 63. other websites . - juan de dios castillo liga mx stats at medio tiempo
