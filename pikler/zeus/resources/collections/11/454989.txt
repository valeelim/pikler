Saltsjöbaden Agreement     the saltsjöbaden agreement ( ) is a swedish labor market treaty signed between the swedish trade union confederation ( sv . `` landsorganisationen , lo '' ) and the swedish employers association ( sv . `` svenska arbetsgivareföreningen , saf '' ) on december 20 , 1938. it became a model for other agreements . the rules on industrial action has become regarded almost as general legal principles of conflicts between the labor market forces .
