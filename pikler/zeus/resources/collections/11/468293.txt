Olle Häger     olle häger ( 19 september 1935 – 1 november 2014 ) was a swedish journalist , television producer , writer and historian . häger produced a large number of historical documentaries . in 1986 , häger and villius were awarded the `` stora journalistpriset '' . he was born in söderala , hälsingland , sweden . häger died in stockholm , aged 79 .
