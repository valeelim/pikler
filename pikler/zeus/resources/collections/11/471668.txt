Horacio Gómez Bolaños     horacio gómez bolaños ( june 28 , 1930 - november 21 , 1999 ) was a mexican actor . he was the brother of roberto gómez bolaños . on the tv show `` el chavo del 8 '' , he played the character godinez . bolaños died on november 21 , 1999 of a heart attack during the production of a tribute to chespirito for televisa , aged 69 .
