Lucius Shepard     lucius shepard ( august 21 , 1943 – march 18 , 2014 ) was an american writer . he mostly worked as a science fiction and fantasy writer . his best known book works were `` the jaguar hunter '' ( 1987 ) , `` life during wartime '' ( 1990 ) , and `` the ends of the earth '' ( 1991 ) . he also wrote the comic book series `` vermillion '' ( 1996–97 ) for helix of dc comics . he was born in lynchburg , virginia . shepard died on march 18 , 2014 in portland , oregon . he was 70 years old .
