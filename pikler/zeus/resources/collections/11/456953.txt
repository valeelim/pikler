Bakir Izetbegović     bakir izetbegović ( born 28 june 1956 ) is a bosnian politician . izetbegović is a member of the party of democratic action and son of the late bosnian president , alija izetbegović . in 1981 he graduated from the university of sarajevo faculty of architecture . in 2010 , he was elected to be the bosniak member of the 3-person presidency of bosnia and herzegovina . < br >
