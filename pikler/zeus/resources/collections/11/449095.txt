Marine iguana     the marine iguana ( `` amblyrhynchus cristatus '' ) is an iguana found only on the galápagos islands . it is the only modern lizards , to live and forage in the sea . also unusually , it is a vegetarian . it only eats algae . the iguana can dive over into the water . it has spread to all the islands in the archipelago , and is sometimes called the galápagos marine iguana . it mainly lives on the rocky galápagos shore to warm from the comparably cold water . it can also be spotted in marshes and mangrove beaches . charles darwin described them :
