38 BC     year 38 bc was either a common year starting on sunday or monday or a leap year starting on saturday , sunday or monday of the julian calendar . at the time , it was known as the year of the consulship of pulcher and flaccus . 38 bc was year 1 of the spanish era calendar that was used in hispania until the 15th century . events . - octavian caesar marries livia . she is still pregnant from a recent marriage . - octavian appoints marcus vipsanius agrippa governor of transalpine gaul - parthia invades syria births . - nero claudius drusus ( d. 9 bc ) deaths . - orodes ii of parthia - pacorus , crownprince of parthia
