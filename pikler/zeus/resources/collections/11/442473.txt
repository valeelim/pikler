Jean Vallée     jean vallée ( born paul georges m. j. goeders ; 2 october 1941 – 12 march 2014 ) was a belgian singer and songwriter . he was made knight in the order of the crown by hm albert ii in 1999. he was born in verviers , liège . vallée died after a long illness on 12 march 2014. he was 72 years old .
