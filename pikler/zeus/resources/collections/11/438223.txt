Harold Varmus     harold eliot varmus ( born december 18 , 1939 ) is an american scientist . he won a nobel prize . he is the 14th and current director of the national cancer institute . he was appointed to that post he was appointed to by president barack obama . varmus was a co-recipient , with j. michael bishop , of the 1989 nobel prize in physiology or medicine for their discovery of the origin of retroviral oncogenes . varmus described the work in his nobel lecture . he also serves as one of three co-chairs of the president 's council of advisors on science and technology .
