Óscar Humberto Mejía Victores    óscar humberto mejía victores brigadier general óscar humberto mejía victores ( december 9 , 1930 – february 1 , 2016 ) was a guatemalan politician . he was the 27th president of guatemala from 8 august 1983 to 14 january 1986. victores died in guatemala city from parkinson 's disease on february 1 , 2016 at the age of 85 .
