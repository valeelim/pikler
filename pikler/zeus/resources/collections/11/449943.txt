Jair Rodrigues     jair rodrigues de oliveira ( february 6 , 1939 – may 8 , 2014 ) was a brazilian musician and singer . his career began during the 1950s . he was known for his many albums such as `` o menino da porteira '' , `` boi da cara preta '' and `` majestade o sabiá '' . he sang funk and samba music . rodrigues was born in igarapava , brazil . he died in cotia , brazil , aged 75 .
