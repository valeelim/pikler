Mipham Chokyi Lodro     mipham chokyi lodro ( 27 october 1952 - 11 june 2014 ) , also known as kunzig shamar rinpoche , was the 14th shamarpa of the karma kagyu school of tibetan buddhism . while giving teachings , the 14th shamarpa died in renchen , germany on 11 june 2014 due to a sudden heart attack , aged 61 .
