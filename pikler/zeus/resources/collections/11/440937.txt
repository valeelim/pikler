Klaus Kinkel     klaus kinkel ( born 17 december 1936 ) is a german civil servant , lawyer , and politician . he was a member of the free democratic party ( fdp ) . he served as federal minister of justice ( 1991–1992 ) , foreign minister ( 1992–1998 ) and vice chancellor of germany ( 1993–1998 ) in the government of helmut kohl . he was also chairman of the liberal free democratic party from 1993 to 1995. he was president of the federal intelligence service ( 1979–1982 ) . kinkel was born in metzingen , baden-württemberg .
