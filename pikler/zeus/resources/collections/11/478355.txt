Love Will Never Do (Without You)    love will never do ( without you ) `` love will never do ( without you ) '' is a 1990 hit single from janet jackson . the song was jackson 's fifth # 1 single on the `` billboard '' hot 100. the song was from her record `` rhythm nation 1814 '' . the song was released in october 1990. the song reached # 19 on the `` billboard '' year-end hot 100 of 1991. it was certified gold by the recording industry association of america .
