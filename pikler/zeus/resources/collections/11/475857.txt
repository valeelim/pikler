Georges Wolinski     georges wolinski ( 28 june 1934 – 7 january 2015 ) was a french cartoonist , journalist , and comic book writer . he was best known for his work on the satirical newspaper `` charlie hebdo '' and the daily newspaper `` libération '' . wolinski was born in tunis , tunisia . his parents were jewish and was of polish and italian ancestry . wolinski was among the victims who were killed in a shooting at `` charlie hebdo '' 's paris headquarters on 7 january 2015. he was 80 .
