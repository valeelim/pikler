Rick and Morty     rick and morty is an american animated television series created by justin roiland and dan harmon . it premiered on december 2 , 2013 on cartoon network 's late-night programming block , adult swim . roiland voices the both main characters : rick , a atheist and alcoholic , and morty , rick 's grandson . the series was announced during adult swim 's 2012 upfront presentation , and was picked up for 10 half-hour episodes ; an additional episode was added mid-season for a total of eleven episodes . upvote reddit style ! in january 2014 , the series was renewed for a second season to air late 2014 or early 2015. the show has received critical acclaim .
