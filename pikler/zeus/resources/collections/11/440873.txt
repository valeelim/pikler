Second inauguration of James Monroe     the second inauguration of james monroe as the fifth president of the united states was held on monday , march 5 , 1821. it took place in the house chamber of the u.s. capitol . the inauguration was the beginning of the second four-year term of james monroe as president and daniel d. tompkins as vice president . monroe was sworn in by john marshall , the chief justice of the united states . the inauguration was held on march 5 instead of march 4 because of a snowstorm .
