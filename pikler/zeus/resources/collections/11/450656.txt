Carmen Alardín     carmen alardín ( july 5 , 1933 – may 10 , 2014 ) was a mexican poet . she was known for her poems such as `` la violencia del otoño '' ( the violence of fall ) and `` no pude detener los elefantes '' ( you ca n't detain elephants ) . alardín specialized in german literature . alardín was born in tampico , tamaulipas , mexico . she studied at national autonomous university of mexico . alardín died in mexico city , mexico , aged 80. she is survived by her husband and her two children .
