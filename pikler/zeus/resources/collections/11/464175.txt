Marc Labelle     marc labelle ( born december 20 , 1969 ) was a nhl hockey player for the dallas stars . he played from 1996-1997. he also played for the el paso buzzards in the wphl league . he was born in maniwaki , canada and is 44 years old . he shoots left handed . he went undrafted and signed by the montreal canadiens in 1991. he wore number 27 jersey for the dallas stars .
