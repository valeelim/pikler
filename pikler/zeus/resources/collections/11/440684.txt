First inauguration of Bill Clinton     bill clinton was inaugurated as the 42nd president of the united states on january 20 , 1993. this was the beginning of the first term of bill clinton as president and al gore as vice president . the ceremony took place at the united states capitol in washington , d.c. clinton was the first president elected after the cold war period . he was the first democrat elected in 16 years . clinton and gore took over from outgoing president george h. w. bush and outgoing vice president dan quayle .
