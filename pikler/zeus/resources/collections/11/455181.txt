Leo Sharp     leonard sharp ( born may 7 , 1924 ) is an american world war ii veteran and notorious drug dealer . he sold drugs , mostly cocaine , across the united states , mostly in detroit , michigan . he gave more than 1,000 pounds of cocaine during his years of dealing . he is thought to have been an urban legend in detroit until his arrest in 2013. he pleaded not guilty in october 2013. he was sentenced to three years in prison in may 2014. sharp is the oldest drug mule in the world . sharp was born in michigan city , indiana . he was raised in detroit , michigan . he fought in world war ii and received a bronze star medal for his service . he has dementia . other websites . - drug mule , 90 , gets 3 years for hauling cocaine , seeks to delay imprisonment
