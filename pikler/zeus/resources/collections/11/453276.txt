Judith Godrèche     judith godrèche ( also judith goldreich ; born march 23 , 1972 ) is a french actress and author . she has acted in over thirty movies . in `` the art of love '' , she played amèlie . in `` stoker '' ( 2013 ) , she played doctor jacquin . godrèche was born in paris , the daughter of two psychotherapists . her paternal grandparents were holocaust survivors from poland , who changed their name from goldreich . godrèche left school at age 15 , settling into a long-term relationship with older director benoît jacquot .
