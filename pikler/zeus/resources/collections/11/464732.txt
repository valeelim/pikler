Sacrifice (2009)    sacrifice ( 2009 ) sacrifice ( 2009 ) was a professional wrestling pay-per-view show made by total nonstop action wrestling ( tna ) . it was held on may 24 , 2009 at the tna impact ! zone in orlando , florida . it was the fifth sacrifice event that tna held . there were eight matches on the card . the main event was known as the four way ultimate sacrifice match , which was between tna world heavyweight champion , mick foley , against sting , kurt angle , and jeff jarrett . each of the competitors in the match wagered an important aspect of their career : foley 's title , sting 's career , angle 's leadership of the main event mafia , and jarrett 's shares in the company . two other important matches saw a.j . styles retaining the tna legends championship against booker t in an `` i quit '' match and beer money , inc. defeating the british invasion to win the `` team 3d invitational tag team tournament '' . sacrifice gained positive reviews . dave meltzer of the wrestling observer newsletter thought that the show , `` overachieved '' . melzer gave attention to the `` team 3d invitational tag team tournament '' finals match and the `` i quit '' match . the only thing he criticized was some of the illogical booking tactics that were made by the company . james caldwell of the pro wrestling torch also gave the event a good review but criticized the show in a number of spots , mainly the `` i quit '' match : `` best match of the ppv until the finish with sharmell-jenna continuing their feud on ppv tv . keep it on free tv ( if tna is so adamant about continuing this ) , but do n't put that dumb storyline on pay tv . as usual , tna tries to find a way out of a stipulation . sucks to rant on that , because the match was awesome and one of booker 's best performances in years .
