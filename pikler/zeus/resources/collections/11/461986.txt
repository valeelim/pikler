Tropical Storm Norma (1970)    tropical storm norma ( 1970 ) tropical storm norma was the fourteenth tropical cyclone of the 1970 pacific hurricane season . it formed on august 31 off the western coast of mexico . the storm died on september 5 , just before it reached baja california . the storm caused record amounts of rain and flash floods in arizona . over $ 1 million in damage was done . twenty-three people were killed .
