My Ruin     my ruin is a metal band from los angeles . the members are tairrie b and her husband mick murphy . my ruin was started by tairrie b in february 1999 . `` speak and destroy '' was released on 23rd august 1999. kerrang ! gave the album a good review . in 2000 the band 's second album `` a prayer under pressure of violent anguish '' was released . the band 's third album was released on 23 october 2003 .
