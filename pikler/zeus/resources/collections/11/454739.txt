The Mega Powers     the mega powers was a professional wrestling tag team . the team was made up of randy savage and hulk hogan . they were managed by miss elizabeth ( who was savage 's real life wife but was portrayed as his on-screen girlfriend ) . the mega powers competed in the world wrestling federation ( wwf ) and in world championship wrestling ( wcw ) . as of today , the only living member of the team is hulk hogan . elizabeth died in 2003 and savage died in 2011. championships . - pro wrestling illustrated - the team was ranked at # 57 of the 100 best tag teams during the `` pwi years '' in 2003 . - world wrestling federation - wwf championship ( one time ) ( savage ) other websites . - hulk hogan 's wwe profile - randy savage 's wwe profile - miss elizabeth 's wwe profile
