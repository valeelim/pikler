Kerser     scott barrow ( born 21 august 1987 ) , better known by his stage name emcee kerser or just simply kerser , is an australian rapper . his best known song is `` kerser is the sickest '' . he was released a number of mixtapes and three albums since 2011. his second album `` no rest for the sickest '' reached # 15 on the aria albums chart . barrow was born in campbelltown , sydney , new south wales .
