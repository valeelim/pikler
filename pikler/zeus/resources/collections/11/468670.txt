Richard Schaal     richard schaal ( ; may 7 , 1928 – november 4 , 2014 ) was an american movie and television actor from chicago , illinois . he was married to actress valerie harper from 1964 to 1978. his daughter is actress wendy schaal by his first marriage to lois ( `` née '' treacy ) . in 1962 , he joined chicago 's second city . he became skilled in sketch and improvisational comedy . he had a recurring role in `` just our luck '' . he was featured in several episodes of `` the mary tyler moore show '' , `` the bob newhart show '' , `` rhoda '' and `` phyllis '' . schaal died on november 4 , 2014 , in woodland hills , los angeles , california . he was 86. same movies . - `` the russians are coming , the russians are coming '' ( 1966 ) - `` the cube '' ( 1969 ) - `` slaughterhouse-five '' ( 1972 ) - `` the hollywood knights '' ( 1980 ) - `` once bitten '' ( 1985 )
