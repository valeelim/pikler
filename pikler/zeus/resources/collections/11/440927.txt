Perho     perho is a municipality in central ostrobothnia , finland . about 2,930 people lived there in january 2014. the municipalities next to it are alajärvi , halsua , kinnula , kivijärvi , kyyjärvi , lestijärvi , veteli and vimpeli . perho was established in 1868. distances . - kyyjärvi 23 km - kivijärvi 40 km - kinnula 43 km - halsua 40 km - veteli 45 km - karstula 49 km - kaustinen 55 km - alajärvi 65 km - äänekoski 110 km - haapajärvi 118 km
