P. J. Byrne     paul jeffrey `` p . j . '' byrne ( born december 15 , 1974 ) is an american movie and television actor . byrne is known for his roles in movies such as in `` horrible bosses '' , `` final destination 5 '' , `` the campaign '' and `` the wolf of wall street '' . he is known for his television roles such as his role in `` the game '' and his starring role in `` intelligence '' .
