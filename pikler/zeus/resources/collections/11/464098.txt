Jorge Batlle Ibáñez     jorge luis batlle ibáñez ( ; `` batlle '' ; 25 october 1927 – 24 october 2016 ) was an uruguayan politician and lawyer . he was a member of the colorado party . he served as the president of uruguay from 2000 to 2005. he was thought to be one of the most popular presidents of uruguay . ibáñez died on the night of 24 october 2016 at a hospital in montevideo , uruguay from a cerebral hemorrhage after falling during an interview . he died one day short from his 89th birthday .
