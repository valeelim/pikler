Alice Pung     alice pung ( born 1981 ) is an australian writer , editor , lawyer and educator . she is best known for writing the memoir `` unpolished gem '' and editing the anthology `` growing up asian in australia '' . pung was born in footscray , victoria and raised in braybrook . her parents were immigrants living in cambodia . they fled to australia during pol pot 's khmer rouge regime in 1980. she was born one month after the event . she is the oldest of four siblings .
