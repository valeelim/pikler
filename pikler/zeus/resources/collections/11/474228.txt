Olle Jönsson     olle jönsson , born 1 december 1955 , is a swedish singer for the dansband lasse stefanz . the band was awarded a grammis in the `` dansband of the year '' in 1989. earlier , he was also the band 's drummer , until a wrist operation . in january 2007 , gunnar nilsson replaced him as the band 's drummer instead . in november 2012 , olle jönsson announced he would immidiately leave the band . however , he reappeared on stage as the band 's singer already the upcoming week .
