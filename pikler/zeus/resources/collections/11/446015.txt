Liv Dommersnes     liv dommersnes ( née strømsted 28 september 1922 – 6 april 2014 ) was a norwegian actress and reciter of poetry . she was best known for being a member of the group that founded studioteatret in 1945. she was born in oslo ( then known as kristiania ) . she was married to actor jens bolling from 1945 to 1952 , and to physician ivar dommersnes from 1968 until his death in 1994. dommersnes died from natural causes on 6 april 2014 in oslo . she was 91 years old .
