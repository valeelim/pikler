Robert V. Keeley     robert vossler keeley ( september 4 , 1929 – january 9 , 2015 ) was an american politician . he served in foreign service of the united states , from 1956 to 1989. he served three times as ambassador : to greece ( 1985–89 ) , zimbabwe ( 1980–84 ) , and mauritius ( 1976–78 ) . in 1978–80 he was deputy assistant secretary of state for african affairs , in charge of southern and eastern africa . keeley was born in beirut , french lebanon to american parents . he died in washington , d.c. from a stroke at the age of 85 .
