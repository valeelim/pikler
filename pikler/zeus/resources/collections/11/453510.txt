Archie Bunker     archibald `` archie '' bunker is a fictional new yorker of the 1970s top rated situation comedy `` all in the family '' . also its related tv show `` archie bunker 's place '' . he was played by actor carroll o'connor . bunker , the family patriarch , was a world war ii veteran and blue-collar worker . he was a family man and a conservative . he was first seen when `` all in the family '' premiered on january 12 , 1971 .
