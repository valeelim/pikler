Malerkotla (princely state)    malerkotla ( princely state ) malerkotla , a muslim majority state was established in 1454 a.d. by sheikh sadruddin-i-jahan from afghanistan , and was ruled by his sherwani ( tribe ) descendents . the state of malerkotla was established in 1657 a.d. during the 1947 riots when punjab was in flames , the state of malerkotla did not witness a single incident of violence ; through it all , it remained a lone island of peace .
