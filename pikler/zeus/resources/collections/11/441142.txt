Airbus A400M Atlas     the airbus a400m atlas is a military cargo aircraft . it is built in spain by airbus defence & space , part of airbus group . the wings are made in the uk . the cockpit and landing gear are made in france . germany provides the fuselage assembly . the atlas is a four turboprops and high-wing aircraft . orders have come from militaries of many countries like france , germany , spain , and the uk . the aircraft 's first flight was on 11 december 2009. the french air force used it in the mali war in 2013 and 2014. on monday may 11 , 2015 , an airbus a400m crashed in spain during final flight testing . two pilots and two flight engineers were killed in the accident . britain , germany , malaysia and turkey all grounded their a440ms . the six planes france has will only see limited use . spain has stopped all testing of the planes until the cause of the crash is discovered . other websites . - airbus to continue test flights ...
