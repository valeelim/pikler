Hanna Gronkiewicz-Waltz     hanna beata gronkiewicz-waltz ( born 4 december 1952 in warsaw ) is a polish lawyer , economist , politician . she was president of national bank of poland ( 1992-2001 ) , vicepresident of european bank for reconstruction and development ( 2001-2004 ) . she is also viceleader of civic platform . in 2013 she received full professor title . in 2014 she has been again elected for mayor of warsaw .
