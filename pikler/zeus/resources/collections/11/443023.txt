Bev Perdue     beverly eaves `` bev '' perdue ( aka beverly marlene moore ; born january 14 , 1947 ) is an american politician . she is a member of the us democratic party . from january 2009 to 2013 , she was governor of the u.s. state of north carolina . she was the first female governor in that state . because of declining approval ratings , in january 2012 , perdue said she would not do another term . in january of the following year , pat mccrory became governor . perdue was born in grundy , virginia in 1947 .
