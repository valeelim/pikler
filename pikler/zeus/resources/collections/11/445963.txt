Paruyr Hayrikyan     paruyr hayrikyan ( , born july 5 , 1949 , yerevan ) is an armenian political activist and a former dissident . hayrikyan was a internationally-known political prisoner in ussr . in 1987 he founded `` national self-identification '' union . he is known for his struggle for the independence of armenia . in 2013 he was shot and wounded during his bid for the presidency . other websites . - paruyr hayrikian - bio
