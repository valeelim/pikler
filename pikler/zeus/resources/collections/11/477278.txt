Yao Beina     yao beina ( born 姚贝娜 ; 26 september 1981 – 16 january 2015 ) was a chinese singer and actress . she was born in wuhan , hubei . she sang `` let it go '' for the chinese version of `` frozen '' . she was known for her song `` the painted 2 '' and `` life in the river '' beina died of breast cancer in shenzhen , guangdong , aged 33 .
