Sunday Morning Call     `` sunday morning call '' is the third single off english rock band oasis ' fourth studio album `` standing on the shoulder of giants '' . the song was released on 3 july 2000. the music video for the song is a take on the movie `` one flew over the cuckoo 's nest '' . the music video was shot in an old mental institute in vancouver , british columbia , canada . other websites . - `` sunday morning call '' on youtube
