Ramón Valdés     ramón gómez valdés de castillo ( september 2 , 1923 – august 9 , 1988 ) , often credited as ramón valdéz , was a mexican actor . he was best known for his role of don ramón in the popular sitcom `` el chavo del ocho '' . he was born in mexico city , mexico . valdés died in mexico city from stomach cancer , aged 64 .
