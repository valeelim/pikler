Kyyjärvi     kyyjärvi is a municipality in central finland . about 1,430 people lived there in january 2014. the municipalities next to it are alajärvi , karstula , kivijärvi , perho and soini . there is also a lake called kyyjärvi what is located in the kyyjärvi municipality . kyyjärvi was established in 1929. distances . - jyväskylä 115 km - vaasa 160 km - seinäjoki 110 km other websites . - the official page
