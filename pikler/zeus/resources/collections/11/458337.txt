Problem (Ariana Grande song)    problem ( ariana grande song ) `` problem '' is a song that is recorded by american recording artist ariana grande , featuring guest vocals from australian recording artist iggy azalea . the song was released by republic records on april 28 , 2014. it is the first single from ariana grande 's second studio album `` my everything '' ( 2014 ) . the song first appeared at number three on the `` billboard '' hot 100. it later peaked at number two on the chart . it sold 438,000 copies on its first week on sale . lyrically , the song is about `` the feeling of being absolutely terrified to re-approach a relationship that 's gone sour -- but you want to more than anything .
