Hrabove, Donetsk Oblast    hrabove , donetsk oblast hrabove ( ; , `` grabovo '' ) is a village in shakhtarsk raion ( district ) in donetsk oblast of eastern ukraine . according to the 2001 census , 1,000 people were living there . on 17 july 2014 , malaysia airlines flight 17 was shot down and crashed into a field near hrabove , killing all 298 passengers . the flight was meant to go from amsterdam , netherlands to kuala lumpur , malaysia . other websites . - hrabove , donetsk oblast , shakhtarsk raion
