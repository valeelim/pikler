Lapiro de Mbanga     lambo sandjo pierre roger ( 7 april 1957 – 16 march 2014 ) , better known as lapiro de mbanga , was a cameroonian singer . he is best known for his 1985 recording of “ pas argent no love ” and for being imprisoned in 2008 after criticizing cameroon president paul biya in the song “ constitution constipée ” ( “ constipated constitution ” ) . he was born in mbanga , southwest region . mbanga died from cancer on 16 march 2014 in buffalo , new york in the united states . he was 56 years old .
