Markus Brüderlin     markus brüderlin ( 1958 – 16 march 2014 ) was a swiss art historian , curator and writer . from 2006 to his death , he was the director of the kunstmuseum wolfsburg . he was born in basel . brüderlin died unexpectedly on 16 march 2014 at his home in frankfurt , hesse , germany . he was 55 years old . other websites . - markus brüderlin at the kunstmuseum wolfsburg website
