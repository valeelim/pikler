Ibn Taymiyyah     ibn taymiyyah ( 661-728 ah/ 1263-1328 ce ) is regarded as one of the prominent sunni islamic scholars and mujaddids ( renewers or revivers ) of islam . his full name is ahmad ibn abdul haleem ibn abdus salam ibn taymiyyah . his titles are sheikh al-islam , the imām , the scholar , the mufassir ( exegete ) , the faqīh ( jurist ) , the mujtahid , the hāfidh , the muḥaddith ( master of hadith ) . he was a member of the islamic jurisprudence school founded by ahmad ibn hanbal . he was notable for wanting to return islam to what he viewed as earlier interpretations of the qur'an and the sunnah . he lived during the troubled times of the mongol invasions in syam . he is the most influential teacher of ibnul qayyim .
