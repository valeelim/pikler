Lorraine Toussaint     lorraine toussaint ( born april 4 , 1960 ) is a trinidadian-american movie and television actress , and producer . she is known for her role as rene jackson in the televison drama `` any day now '' ( 1998-2002 ) . she was also in the movies `` breaking in '' ( 1989 ) , `` hudson hawk '' ( 1991 ) , and `` dangerous minds '' ( 1995 ) . for `` middle of nowhere '' ( 2012 ) she was nominated for a independent spirit award for best supporting actress . toussaint was also in the television series `` crossing jordan '' ( 2002-2003 ) , `` saving grace '' ( 2007-2010 ) and `` orange is the new black '' ( 2012 ) .
