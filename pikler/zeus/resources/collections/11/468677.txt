Bootlegs &amp; B-Sides    bootlegs & amp ; b-sides bootlegs & b-sides is a 1994 compilation studio album by ice cube . this album has remixes and b-sides for certain songs , including `` check yo self '' and `` what can i do '' . other songs on the album are from his album `` lethal injection '' . this album was released on november 22 , 1994. many song versions were later included on remastered and proper ice cube records .
