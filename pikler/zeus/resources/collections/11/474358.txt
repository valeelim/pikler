Willie Rennie     william cowan `` willie '' rennie ( born 27 september 1967 ) is a scottish politician . he is the current leader of the scottish liberal democrats . he became the member of parliament ( mp ) for dunfermline and west fife in february 2006. rennie lost this seat to labour in the may 2010 uk general election . he was then appointed as a special government adviser . he worked for the scottish secretaries of state danny alexander mp and then michael moore mp . he stood for the scottish parliament in the 2011 scottish parliament general election . he was elected as a member for the mid scotland and fife region . soon afterwards he was elected as leader of the scottish liberal democrat party . he replaced tavish scott . other websites . - willie rennie 's page at the scottish parliament website - biography on scottish liberal democrats website
