Zong (mobile payments)    zong ( mobile payments ) zong is a mobile payment company that allows users to make payments of small amount of money on the internet through postpaid mobile phones . these payments are charged in mobile phone bills by the mobile operator . the company was bought by ebay in 2011. zong is a paypal based service . online games and social networks generally support its mobile payment system . it can also be used to buy non-physical things . zong was the global division of mobile payment company echovox . it was started in 2008 and ebay bought it in 2011. it is connected with 67 mobile operators in 13 countries . zong focuses social networks and online games , so they make available the premium content to young people who do not have a credit card , the ability to make small purchases online . zong allows them to pay with their mobile terminal and the amount is debited by the mobile operator . the partnership of zong with mobile operators allows them to charge customers using short numbers . on march 12 , 2009 zong announced to stop short messaging service and is now targeting on its mobile payment system . zong publishes an api that allows the developers to sell and publish content to mobile customers worldwide .
