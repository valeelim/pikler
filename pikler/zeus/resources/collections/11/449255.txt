Jane Gray     jane `` jean '' gray ( née cant ; 1 december 1901 – 7 june 2014 ) was a scottish-australian supercentenarian . she was , at , the oldest living person in australia between august 2012 and june 2014. she was born in newtyle , angus , scotland . she met her future husband jock gray on a blind date in 1922 and married him in 1927. having arrived in sydney , they ran a poultry farm . the couple were married for 60 years before jock died . they had two children , nine grandchildren , 14 great-grandchildren and two great-great-grandchildren . her daughter began taking care of her at their home in smithfield , new south wales in 2004. her secret to longevity was to `` eat plenty and do n't drink booze or smoke '' . as of december 2013 , she lived in a nursing home . gray died from natural causes on 7 june 2014 at a smithfield nursing home . she was 112 .
