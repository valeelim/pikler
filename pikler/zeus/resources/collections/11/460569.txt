Birgitta Stenberg     birgitta stenberg ( 26 april 1932 – 23 august 2014 ) was a swedish author , translator and illustrator . she was the 2005 winner of the selma lagerlöf prize . stenberg was born in stockholm in 1932. she was educated in visby and finally in paris . she became a swedish author , translator and illustrator . she lived in åstol in sweden . she has won the swedish selma lagerlöf prize for literature in 2005. stenberg died from hepatic cancer ( type of liver cancer ) on 23 august 2014 in tiveden , sweden , aged 82. other websites . - official website
