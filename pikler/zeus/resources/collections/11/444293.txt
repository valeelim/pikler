Salman Khan     salman khan ( pronunciation : ; born abdul rashid salim salman khan on 27 december 1965 ) is an indian actor , producer , television presenter , and philanthropist . khan mostly appears in hindi movies . khan was sentenced to a five-year imprisonment on 6 may 2015 from running over 5 people , killing 1 , with his car . on 8 may 2015 , khan was granted bail . salman khan was born in indore , madhya pradesh , india . his parents are veteran his first wife sushila charak ( later adopted the name salma khan ) and salim khan .
