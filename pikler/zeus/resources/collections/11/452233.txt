Glyndŵr University     glyndŵr university is a university in wales . it is named after a welsh national hero , owain glyndŵr . the university opened in 2008. before becoming a university , it had been the wrexham school of arts and science which opened in 1887. it has over 9000 students , and as well as the wrexham campus it also has campuses at st. asaph , northop , and london .
