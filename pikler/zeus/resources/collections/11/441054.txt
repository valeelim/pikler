Paolo Sorrentino     paolo sorrentino ( born 31 may 1970 ) is an italian director and screenwriter . his movies include `` one man up '' ( 2001 ) , `` the consequences of love '' ( 2004 ) , `` the family friend '' ( 2006 ) , `` il divo '' ( 2008 ) , `` this must be the place '' ( 2011 ) and `` the great beauty '' ( 2013 ) . sorrentino was born in naples .
