Växjö HF     växjö hf is a handball club in växjö i nsweden . the club was established 1972 out of växjö bk . the men 's team played three seasons in the swedish top division during the early 1990s . the men 's junior team won the swedish national championship in the year 2000. in 2003 , the girls ' team won the swedish national championship . it is the mother club of swedish player jonas källman . other websites . - official website
