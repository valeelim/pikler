Canadian Hockey League     the canadian hockey league ( or chl ) is an umbrella organization for junior ice hockey leagues in canada . there are three leagues with the chl : the western hockey league , the ontario hockey league , and the quebec major junior hockey league . the three leagues have a total of sixty teams , fifty-two in canada and eight in the united states . at the end of the leagues ' season , the league champions play for the memorial cup , as the champion of the chl .
