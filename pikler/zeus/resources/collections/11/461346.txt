Ted 2     ted 2 is a 2015 american comedy movie . it was directed , produced , and co-written by seth macfarlane . it is the sequel to the 2012 film `` ted '' . mark wahlberg , macfarlane , and mila kunis will reprise their roles , with new roles played by amanda seyfried , morgan freeman , and richard schiff . filming for this movie started on july 28 , 2014. it is scheduled to be released on june 26 , 2015. cast . - mark wahlberg as john bennett - seth macfarlane as ted - amanda seyfried as samantha leslie `` sam l.
