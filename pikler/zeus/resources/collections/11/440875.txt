Inauguration of John Quincy Adams     the inauguration of john quincy adams as the sexth president of the united states took place on friday , march 4 , 1825 , in the house chamber of the u.s. capitol . the inauguration was the beginning of the four-year term of john quincy adams as president and the first term of john c. calhoun as vice president . adams was sworn in by john marshall , chief justice of the united states .
