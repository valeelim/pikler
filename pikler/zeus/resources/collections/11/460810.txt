Larry Kramer     larry kramer ( born june 25 , 1935 ) is an american playwright , author , public health and lgbt rights activist . he began his career rewriting scripts while working for columbia picture . there he wrote the screenplay for the 1969 movie `` women in love '' . he earned an academy award nomination for his work . kramer is openly gay . in 1988 , he was diagnosed with hepatitis b and hiv . he had a liver transplant in 2001. his brother was lawyer arthur kramer ( 1927-2008 ) .
