Hiram Fong     hiram leong fong ( yau leong fong ; october 15 , 1906 â€“ august 18 , 2004 ) was an american politician and businessman . he was the first united states senator for hawaii . he served as senator from 1959 to 1977. he was a member of the republican party . fong ran for president of the united states in 1964. he lost the primary to barry m. goldwater . he was the first asian-american to run for president of the united states . patsy mink would follow him as the few hawaiians to run for president . fong died in kahaluu , hawaii from complications of a fall , aged 97 .
