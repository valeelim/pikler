H. R. Giger     hans rudolf `` ruedi '' giger ( 5 february 1940 – 12 may 2014 ) was a swiss surrealist painter , sculptor and set designer . he was part of the special effects team that won an academy award for best achievement for visual effects for their design work on the movie `` alien '' . on 12 may 2014 , giger died at the age of 74 in a hospital in zürich after having suffered injuries in a fall .
