Norrie May-Welby     norrie may-welby ( born bruce norrie watson , 23 may 1961 ) is a scottish-australian transsexual person . she is known for being 'australia 's first recognized transgender person ' . may-welby was born in paisley , renfrewshire . when she was seven years old she and her family emigrated to western australia . she has lived in sydney since the early 1990s .
