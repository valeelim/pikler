Vicente Trueba     vicente trueba pérez ( october 16 , 1905 in sierrapando , cantabria – november 10 , 1986 ) was a spanish professional cyclist . he is famous for being the first winner of the king of the mountains in the tour de france . this is a competition for the best climber in the race . it was first held in 1933. other websites . - conference by ángel neila about vicente trueba 's life
