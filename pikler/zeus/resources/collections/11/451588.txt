Genevieve Cortese     genevieve cortese ( also genevieve padalecki ; born january 8 , 1981 ) is an american actress . she works in movies and television . she played kris furillo on the television series `` wildfire '' . she played ruby in the show `` supernatural '' . she played veronica in the 2012 movie `` hated '' . cortese was born in danville , california .
