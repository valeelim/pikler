Marek Galiński     marek galiński ( 1 august 1974 – 17 march 2014 ) was a polish professional mountain biker . he won eight polish national championship titles . he also competed for poland in the summer olympics in 1996 , 2000 , 2004 , and 2008. he was born in opoczno . galiński was killed in a car accident on 17 march 2014 in jędrzejów . he was 39 years old . he was outlived by wife and two children . other websites . - marek galiński at cycling archives
