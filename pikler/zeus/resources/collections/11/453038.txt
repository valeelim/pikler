Embryo transfer     embryo transfer is a medical procedure . it is a step in the process of assisted reproduction ( also called fertility treatment ) , which uses science and medical treatments to help women who have difficulty getting pregnant . in embryo transfer , embryos are placed in a woman 's uterus . the goal is to create a pregnancy . the embryos can be either fresh or frozen . if they are frozen , they are thawed right before they are transferred . embryo transfer is the last step in a type of fertility treatment called in vitro fertilization . embryo transfer can be used for humans or animals .
