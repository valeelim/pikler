Inge Keller     inge keller ( 15 december 1923 – 6 february 2017 ) was a german actress . her career on stage and on movies has for seventy years . she was one of the most well-known performers in the former german democratic republic . keller was known for her roles in `` the last year '' , `` aimée & jaguar '' , `` council of the gods '' , `` lola and billy the kid '' , `` karla '' , `` the fiancé '' , and in `` woman doctors '' . keller was born on 15 december 1923 in berlin , germany . keller died in a berlin nursing home on 6 february 2017 , aged 93 .
