John Kitzhaber     john albert kitzhaber ( born march 5 , 1947 ) is an american politician . in 2011 , he became the governor of oregon . he was also governor of oregon from 1995 to 2003. on february 13 , 2015 , kitzhaber resigned as governor due to some serious allegations against him . his resignation became effect on february 18 when kate brown succeeded him . on may 2 , 2016 , kitzhaber announced his endorsement for vermont senator bernie sanders in his 2016 nomination for the democratic nomination . other websites . - governor john kitzhaber official oregon government site
