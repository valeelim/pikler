Dollarama     dollarama is a dollar store chain . the chain was created by larry rossy in 1992. the first dollarama was opened at the shopping centre `` les promenades du st-laurent '' in matane , quebec in april , 1992. in november 2004 , 80 percent of dollarama was sold to a private equity fund , bain capital , of boston , massachusetts for $ 850 million us . other websites . - official website
