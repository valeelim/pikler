Gliese 876     gliese 876 is a red dwarf about 15 light-years away from earth in the constellation of aquarius . it is the closest star known to be a multiplanetary system . as of 2011 , four extrasolar planets have been found orbiting the star . it is the third closest known star to the sun which has a planetary system , after epsilon eridani ( 10.5 ly ) and gliese 674 ( 14.8 ly ) . two of the middle planets are in the system 's habitable zone . however , they are giant planets thought to be like jupiter .
