Alfredo Alcón     alfredo félix alcón ( ; 3 march 1930 – 11 april 2014 ) was an argentine theatre and movie actor . alcón born in buenos aires . he worked in more than 50 movies . his first movie was , `` el amor nunca muere '' ( `` love never dies '' ) , in 1955. he died in buenos aires at the age of 84 from a respiratory illness .
