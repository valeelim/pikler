Philoxenos of Eretria     philoxenus of eretria was a painter from eretria . he was the student of nicomachus of thebes . philoxenus copied his speed in painting . he found new and fast ways of painting . pliny said a picture philoxenus painted was the best painting . it was of a battle of alexander the great with darius . he painted it for king cassander . the same subject was used in the alexander mosaic in the house of the faun , in pompeii . < br >
