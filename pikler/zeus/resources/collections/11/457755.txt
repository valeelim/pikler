Héctor Lechuga     héctor lechuga ( 18 april 1929 – 13 july 2017 ) was a mexican actor , comedian , political commentarist and radio personality . he is known for his roles in `` peluquero de señoras '' ( 1973 ) , `` méxico 2000 '' ( 1983 ) and `` masajista de señoras '' ( 1973 ) . lechuga died on 13 july 2017 of a heart attack after suffering from alzheimer 's disease at his home in xalapa , veracruz at the age of 88 .
