Justin Roiland     mark justin roiland ( born february 21 , 1980 ) is an american actor , writer , television director and producer . he is best known as the voice of oscar on the disney channel 's animated television show `` fish hooks '' , as well as the earl of lemongrab on `` adventure time '' . he is also co-creator and executive producer of the adult swim animated series `` rick and morty '' , in which he voices the show 's main characters .
