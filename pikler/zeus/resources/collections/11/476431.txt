Costa-Gavras     costa-gavras ( short for konstantinos gavras - κωνσταντίνος γαβράς ; born 12 february 1933 ) is a greek-french director , screenwriter , and producer . he is known for movies `` missing '' ( 1982 ) , `` hannah k. '' ( 1983 ) , `` betrayed '' ( 1988 ) , `` music box '' ( 1989 ) , `` mad city '' ( 1997 ) and `` amen . '' ( 2002 ) .
