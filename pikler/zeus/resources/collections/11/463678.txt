Montek Singh Ahluwalia     montek singh ahluwalia ( born 24 november 1943 ) is an indian economist and civil servant . he was the deputy chairman of the planning commission of the republic of india . the position has the rank of a cabinet minister . he resigned from the post in 2014. this was before the end of the upa ii regime in the center . before this , he was the first director of the independent evaluation office at the international monetary fund .
