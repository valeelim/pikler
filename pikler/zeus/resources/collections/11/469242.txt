Craig Robinson (actor)    craig robinson ( actor ) craig phillip robinson ( born october 25 , 1971 ) is an american actor , stand-up comedian , and singer . he is best known for his role as darryl philbin on `` the office '' , and his roles in the movie `` pineapple express '' , `` miss march '' , `` zack and miri make a porno '' , `` shrek forever after '' , `` hot tub time machine '' and `` this is the end '' . robinson was born in chicago , illinois .
