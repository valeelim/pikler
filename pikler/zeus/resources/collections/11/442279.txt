Shirtdress     a shirtdress is a style of dress which borrows details from a boy's-man 's dress shirt . these can include a collar , a button front , or cuffed sleeves . often , these dresses are made up in crisp fabrics like cotton or silk , much like a men 's dress shirt would be . as they are typically cut without a seam at the waist , these dresses often have a looser fit , usually relying on a belt to define the waist . button fronts and a forgiving fit make this a flattering look for most body types . other websites . - definition of a shirt dress by cynthia nellis
