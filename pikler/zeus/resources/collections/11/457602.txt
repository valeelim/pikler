Battle of Yellow Tavern     the battle of yellow tavern was part of the overland campaign of the american civil war . it was fought on may 11 , 1864 in henrico county , virginia . major general philip sheridan , who commanded the cavalry of the army of the potomac , wanted to launch a raid behind the confederate army of northern virginia . he thought he could keep supplies from reaching the confederates and defeat the confederate cavalry , commanded by major general j.e.b . stuart . sheridan started his raid on may 9. stuart started after him right away . on may 11 , they fought on may 11 at yellow tavern , six miles north of richmond . the confederates defeated several union attacks . but in the afternoon , general george a. custer 's michigan brigade broke through the confederate center . near the end of the battle stuart was mortally wounded by one of custer 's men and died the next day . the confederates then retreated from the battlefield . the battle , except for stuart 's death , was not of much note . there were a total of about 800 casualties .
