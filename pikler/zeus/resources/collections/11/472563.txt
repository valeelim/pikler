Roehampton     roehampton is a small town in southwest london . historically roehampton was a wealthy district . some of the great mansions of the 1700s are now used by colleges of the university of roehampton . the university was founded in 2004. in 2007 the national tennis centre was set up in roehampton . it has 22 courts and is a training facility for some of britain 's top tennis players . it also hosts the qualifying rounds for the wimbledon championships . the priory hospital at roehampton is a mental health hospital . it is internationally known for the treatment of celebrities with various addictions .
