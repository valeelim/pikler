Clarissa Dickson Wright     clarissa theresa philomena aileen mary josephine agnes elsie trilby louise esmerelda dickson wright ( 24 june 1947 – 15 march 2014 ) was an english celebrity chef , television personality , businesswoman and barrister . she was best known as one half , along with the late jennifer paterson , of bbc two 's `` two fat ladies '' ( 1996–99 ) . she was born in st john 's wood , london . she was a roman catholic . dickson wright died on 15 march 2014 in edinburgh , scotland , having been in hospital for several weeks . she was 66 years old . she was outlived by her two sisters .
