INS Dakar     the ins `` dakar was a submarine of the israeli navy . it disappeared in the mediterranean sea in 1968. it had formerly belonged to the royal navy as the hms `` totem . after sea trials it departed scotland in january 1968 for israel . she never arrived and was lost at sea with her entire crew . in 1999 the wreckage was found between cyprus and crete . the cause of her sinking is still not known . in 1971 a memorial to the submarine was established in gan hane'edarim in the national military and police cemetery at mount herzl , jerusalem . the bridge portion of the vessel was salvaged and is on display at the israeli naval museum in haifa . related pages . - ins dakar memorial other websites . - a memorial website for the ins dakar
