Seán Treacy     seán treacy ( born 23 september 1923 ) is a former irish politician . he was the ceann comhairle ( chairman ) of dáil éireann from 1973 to 1977 and from 1987 to 1997. he was a member of the european parliament ( mep ) from 1981 to 1984 , replacing eileen desmond who resigned as an mep when she was appointed minister for health and social welfare . other websites . - european parliament profile
