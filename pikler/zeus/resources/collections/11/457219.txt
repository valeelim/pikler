Shatsky Rise     the shatsky rise is an oceanic plateau southeast of japan . it lies on the floor of the pacific ocean . it includes what may be the largest volcano yet discovered on earth , the tamu massif . the rise and its volcanics may have formed during the upper jurassic to lower cretaceous epochs . it is at the triple junction of three tectonic plates . these are the pacific , the farallon , and the izanagi . it was named after nikolay shatsky ( 1895-1960 ) , a soviet geologist . he was an expert in tectonics of ancient platforms .
