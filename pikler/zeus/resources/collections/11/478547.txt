Sanda Secondary School     the sanda secondary school ( ) is a secondary school in huskvarna in sweden . it was opened for the 1970-1971 school year . since 1979 the school has a sports profile . originally , it only had the sports of basketball and cross-country skiing . more sports were later added , for example ice hockey ( starting in the 1995-1996 season/school year ) . in the 2005-2006 season/school year , the school won the swedish boys ' floorball championship for boys born 1987 .
