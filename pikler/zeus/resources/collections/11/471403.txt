Said Akl     said akl ( ) or saïd akl , said aql and saeed akl ) ( 4 july 1912 – 28 november 2014 ) was a lebanese poet , writer , playwright and language reformer . he was thought to be as one of the most important lebanese poets of the modern age . he also helped create the lebanese alphabet . akl died in beirut , lebanon at the age of 102. other websites . - poet said akl- all poems - said akl 's biography
