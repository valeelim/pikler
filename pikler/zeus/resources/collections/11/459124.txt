Anthony Foxx     anthony renard foxx ( born april 30 , 1971 ) is an american politician . he was united states secretary of transportation from 2013 through 2017. he served as the mayor of charlotte , north carolina , from 2009 to 2013. on april 29 , 2013 , president barack obama said he would nominate foxx to be the secretary of transportation . on june 27 , 2013 the senate confirmed the nomination of foxx to the post of secretary of transportation on a vote of 100-0. other websites . - secretary foxx at usdot
