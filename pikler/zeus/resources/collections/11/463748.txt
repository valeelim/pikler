Eugene Merle Shoemaker     eugene merle shoemaker ( april 28 , 1928 – july 18 , 1997 ) , also known as gene shoemaker , was an american geologist . he was one of the founders of the field of planetary science . he was best known for co-discovering the comet shoemaker–levy 9 with his wife carolyn shoemaker and david levy . shoemaker died in a car crash in alice springs , australia , aged 69. other websites . - usgs page about gene shoemaker - nasa page about gene shoemaker
