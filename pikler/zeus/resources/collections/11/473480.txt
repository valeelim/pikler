Mieszko II Lambert     mieszko ii lambert ( ca . 990 - may 10 , 1034 ) was a king of poland from 1025 to 1034 , the second son of boleslaw i the brave . he is the third known polish ruler . he was father of casimir i. he was probably named after mieszko i of poland , who was his grandfather . in 1028 and in 1030 he attacked saxony . later , poland was attacked by germany , bohemia , and kiev . when poland was attacked by yaroslav the wise in 1031 , mieszko lost control over poland .
