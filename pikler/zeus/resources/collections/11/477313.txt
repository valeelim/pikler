Arrondissement of Moulins     the arrondissement of moulins is an arrondissement of france . it is part of the allier `` département '' . its capital , and of the department , is the city of moulins . on 4 march 1790 , with the establishment of the department of allier , moulins was one of the districts of the department . with the establishment of the `` arrondissements '' on 17 february 1800 , moulins was one of the original `` arrondissements '' . the `` arrondissement '' of moulins is bordered to the north by the nièvre ( burgundy ) department , to the east by the saône-et-loire ( burgundy ) department , to the south by the `` arrondissement '' of vichy , to the west by the `` arrondissement '' of montluçon and to the northwest by the cher ( centre ) department . it is found in the northern part of the department , with an area of , the largest in area of the department . it has 105,677 inhabitants , the `` arrondissement '' of the allier department with fewer people living there , and a population density of inhabitants/km² . after the reorganisation of the cantons in france , cantons are not subdivisions of the `` arrondissements '' so they could have `` communes '' that belong to different `` arrondissements '' .
