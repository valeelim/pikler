João Nílson Zunino     joão nílson zunino commonly known as zunino ( 9 april 1946 – 23 december 2014 ) was a brazilian businessman and cardiologist . he served as the president of avaí futebol clube from 2002 until 2013 , succeeding flávio ricardo félix . he was also a cardiology doctor and a businessman . he was born in são joão batista , brazil . zunino died on 23 december 2014 in florianópolis , brazil , aged 68 .
