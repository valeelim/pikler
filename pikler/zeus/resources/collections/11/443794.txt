Jessica Iskandar     jessica iskandar ( born 29 january 1988 ) is an indonesian actress , comedian , singer and model . iskandar was a model at john casablanca . after trying casting in some agencies , she got the role as kara in the movie `` dealova '' in 2005. she also had a small role in the malaysian movie `` diva '' ( 2007 ) . currently , she is in a soap opera . she is also in ads . she has been a presenter since march 2011. she played in `` campur-campur '' in antv . currently iskandar is studying interior design at trisakti university , jakarta . she reportedly did not go to college again after three semesters for career matters . filmography . - `` dealova '' ( 2005 ) - `` diva '' ( 2007 ) - `` coblos cinta '' ( 2008 ) - `` nazar '' ( 2009 ) - `` istri bo'ongan '' ( 2010 ) - `` kung fu pocong perawan '' ( 2012 )
