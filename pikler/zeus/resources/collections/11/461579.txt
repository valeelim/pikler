Norman Gordon     norman gordon ( 6 august 1911 – 2 september 2014 ) was a south african cricketer . he played in five tests in the 1938–39 south african cricket season . he was born in boksburg , transvaal . he was the only test cricketer to live beyond 100 years of age . gordon became the oldest-ever test cricketer on 23 march 2011 , when he surpassed new zealander eric tindill , who died on 1 august 2010 , approximately four months before his 100th birthday . gordon died in johannesburg , south africa from natural causes , aged 103 .
