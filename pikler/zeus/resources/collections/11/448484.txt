Stefanie Zweig     stefanie zweig ( 19 september 1932 – 25 april 2014 ) was a german writer . she was best known for her autobiographical novel , `` nirgendwo in afrika '' ( `` nowhere in africa '' , 1998 ) , based on her early life in kenya . the novel was made into a movie and won an academy award in 2002 for best foreign language film . zweig was born in leobschütz , upper silesia ( now poland ) . her family was jewish and fled nazi germany to kenya . zweig died after a short illness on 25 april 2014 in frankfurt , hesse , germany . she was 81 .
