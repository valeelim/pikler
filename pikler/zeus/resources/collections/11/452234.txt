Battle of Bryn Glas     the battle of bryn glas was fought on 22 june 1402. this was the major battle of the welsh revolt ( 1400-1415 ) . the rebel forces of wales , led by owain glynd≈µr defeated the english army led by sir edmund mortimer . the exact location of the battle is not known , but research shows that it was probably on bryn glas hill , to the east , west and south of the church of st. mary . the english attacked the welsh forces who were on the top of the hill . during the battle , mortimer was captured by the welsh . the bodies of the dead english , were left on the battlefield ; later the bones were collected and buried in the churchyard .
