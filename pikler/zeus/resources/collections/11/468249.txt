Paddington (movie)    paddington ( movie ) paddington is a 2014 comedy movie based on michael bond 's paddington 's book series . it is about a young bear from peru who travels to london to look for a home . nicole kidman plays millicent . sally hawkins plays mrs. brown . imelda staunton plays as the voice of aunt lucy . michael gambon , matt lucas , peter capaldi and geoffrey palmer are also in the movie . the movie was released on november 28 , 2014 in the united kingdom , and on january 16 , 2015 in the united states .
