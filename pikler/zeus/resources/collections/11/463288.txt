American imperialism     american imperialism is the economic , military , and cultural influence of the united states on other countries . at one time this influence included expansion into foreign territories . expansionism was a primary objective of most empires , an example being the british empire . modern imperialism started in the 15th century when european countries discovered , and fought to claim , the new world . american imperialism began in the 1890s . in 1896 the us annexed the hawaiian islands . after the spanish-american war the us annexed puerto rico , guam , and the philippines . in 1914 the us completed the panama canal . the roosevelt corollary to the monroe doctrine increased us influence in latin america . it was the basis for roosevelt 's big stick policy . the us evolved from expansionism towards being an influence on world affairs .
