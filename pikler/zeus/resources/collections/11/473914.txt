Afro-Colombian     afro-colombians are people who are from the country of colombia and who have ancestors who are african . there is a large population of colombians of african descent . spanish colonists brought african slaves to cartagena in the sixteenth century . they were purchased to replace the indigenous peoples who died under the harsh working conditions in the mines and on the plantations . the main slave port for the colony ( then granada ) was cartagena . by 1600 about 100,000 slaves had been brought to the colony . the rate increased after 1650 because of labor needed for the gold mines . conditions were harsher for some slaves than others . most were allowed to mine , farm and sell their goods for one day a week . the money they made could buy their freedom . slave rebellions in the sixteenth and eighteenth centuries had slaves forming their own villages in uncontrolled areas . slavery was abolished in 1851 .
