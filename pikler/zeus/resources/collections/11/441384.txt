Focke-Wulf Fw 189     the focke-wulf fw 189 was a twin-engine military aircraft . it was flown during world war ii by the `` luftwaffe '' ( the german air force ) , who used it mainly for reconnaissance . the plane was built in germany by focke-wulf aircraft company . it had a crew of three people . the 189 was built of wood and metal . it was nicknamed `` uhu '' , or owl in english .
