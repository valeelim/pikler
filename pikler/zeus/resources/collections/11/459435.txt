Bud Spencer     bud spencer ( born carlo pedersoli ; 31 october 1929 - 27 june 2016 ) was an italian actor , filmmaker and a former professional swimmer . he was known for working on comedy movies with his longtime friend terence hill . spencer is known for his roles in `` they call me trinity '' , `` trinity is still my name '' , `` crime busters '' , `` miami supercops '' , and in `` troublemakers '' . other websites . - hill & spencer website - carlo pedersoli at sports-reference.com
