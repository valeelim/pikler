Doggystyle     doggystyle is a gangsta rap album by snoop dogg ( then known as snoop doggy dogg ) . the album was recorded not long after `` the chronic '' ( 1992 ) . `` doggystyle '' was praised by critics for lyrical `` realism '' . this album was released on november 23 , 1993. it premiered at # 1 on the `` billboard '' 200 albums chart . the lyrics created some controversy .
