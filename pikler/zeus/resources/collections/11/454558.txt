Eden Hazard     eden hazard ( born 7 january 1991 ) is a belgian footballer who plays for chelsea f.c and belgium national team . he usually plays as an attacking midfielder . he was born in la louviere , hainaut . he is widely regarded as one of the best players in his position and his skills are often compared with players like lionel messi and cristiano ronaldo . hazard won the player of the year for chelsea in the 2013-2014 season and the 2014-15 season . he also was the young player of the year in 2012-2013 .
