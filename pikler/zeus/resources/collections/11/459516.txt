Vladimir Beara     vladimir beara ( 2 november 1928 – 11 august 2014 ) was a yugoslav football player , as goalkeeper , and also football manager . beara played for hajduk split ( 1946–55 ) beara played 308 games , and helped his team to win the yugoslav league title in 1950 , 1952 and 1955. beara died in split , croatia , aged 85. other websites . - vladimir beara profile at reprezentacija.rs - profile at weltfussball.de
