Nicholas Hoult     nicholas caradoc hoult ( born 7 december 1989 ) is an english actor and model . he is best known for appearing in movies such as `` about a boy '' ( 2002 ) , `` a single man '' ( 2009 ) , `` '' ( 2011 ) , `` warm bodies '' ( 2013 ) and `` jack the giant slayer '' ( 2013 ) . hoult was born in wokingham , berkshire . he is in a relationship with american actress jennifer lawrence . they have appeared in the `` x-men '' movies together .
