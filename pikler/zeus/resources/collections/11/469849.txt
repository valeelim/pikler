Laugh Now, Cry Later    laugh now , cry later laugh now , cry later is the seventh studio album by american rapper , ice cube . `` laugh now , cry later '' was released on june 9 , 2006 through lench mob records . it was the first album that was released through his independently owned record label . it is also ice cube 's first studio album in six years . the album debuted at # 4 on the `` billboard '' 200 . `` laugh now , cry later '' was certified gold by the riaa which is uncommon for an album that was released through an independent label .
