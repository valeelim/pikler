Sara Widén     sara kristina widén ( 9 may 1981 – 13 june 2014 ) was a swedish opera singer ( soprano ) . from 2013 until her death , she was starring in productions by the royal swedish opera . widén was born in halmstad , halland county . her younger brother jacob widén ( born 1983 ) is a member of the punk rock band neverstore . widén died from cancer on 13 june 2014 in stockholm , aged 33 .
