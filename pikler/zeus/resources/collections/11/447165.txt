The Boondocks (TV series)    the boondocks ( tv series ) the boondocks is an american adult animated sitcom produced by adult swim . the series premiered in november 6 , 2005. it is set in south side , chicago , illinois . it is about a black family called the freemans moving in woodcrest , a white suburb , of chicago . they deal with many situations such as drugs , alcoholism , poverty , racism , and child abuse . cast . - huey freeman ( voiced by regina king ) is the smartest of the freemans . he created many political movements . he is the grandson of robert freeman . - riley freeman ( voiced by regina king ) is the less responsible sibling , than his rival brother huey . he makes many poor decisions . otherwise he is charming , clever , and artistically gifted . riley is huey 's best friend . - robert freeman ( voiced by john witherspoon ) is the grandfather of huey and riley . he is their caretaker and watches over them . he has a short-temper and shares his life stories with his grandsons . - uncle ruckus ( voiced by gary anthony williams ) is a poor and wisecracking friend of the freemans . he has many different jobs and changes in every episode . according to him , he has revitiligo .
