Comrade     comrade is the name given to someone who belongs to the same social group , party or company . it literally means `` friend '' , `` colleague '' , but an also mean `` ally '' . the term can refer to someone who has the same beliefs or values . that way , in communism , people who belong to the communist party address each other as `` comrade '' . terms like this avoid using titles , rank or any other sign that one person is better than , or different from , another .
