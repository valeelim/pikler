St. Govan's Chapel    st. govan 's chapel st. govan 's chapel is a chapel in st. govan , pembrokeshire , in the country of wales . the chapel is built into a cliff made of limestone . it is small and measures 20 by 12 feet in size . it has only one room . the walls are made of limestone . the building is very old . it might have been built in the 6th century . but , most of the building was built in the 13th century . the first chapel was a cave that saint goven lived in where the chapel is today . people believe that saint goven might be buried beneath the building . there is one entrance on the north part of the building . there are benches made of stone inside . the roof , which is made of slate is newer . to get to the building you have to walk down 52 stairs .
