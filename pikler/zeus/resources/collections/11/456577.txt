Bora Todorović     borivoje `` bora '' todorović ( serbian cyrillic : боривоје `` бора '' тодоровић ) ( november 5 , 1929 – july 7 , 2014 ) was an serbian actor . he was known for his roles in `` who 's that singing over there '' ( 1980 ) , `` the marathon family '' ( 1982 ) , `` balkan express '' ( 1983 ) , `` balkanski špijun '' ( `` balkan spy '' , 1984 ) , `` time of the gypsies '' ( 1988 ) , and `` underground '' ( 1995 ) .
