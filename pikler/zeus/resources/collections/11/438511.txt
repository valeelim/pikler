Hurricane Inez     hurricane inez was a very deadly late season hurricane . this storm happened late september to mid october 1966. inez had winds of just over 150 miles an hour ( almost category 5 level ) . the storm went across cuba , the florida keys , hispaniola , and mexico . inez killed over 1,000 people . it caused $ 226.5 million ( 1966 usd ) in damage . the name inez was retired the next spring .
