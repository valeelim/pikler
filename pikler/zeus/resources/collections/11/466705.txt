Culgoa River     culgoa river is a river in the south west queensland district of queensland and the orana district of new south wales , australia . the river is the western branch of the balonne river in southern queensland , near dirranbandi . it flows southwest across parts of the darling riverine plains . the river is joined by ten tributaries . it flows into the darling river near bourke , new south wales . culgoa is an aboriginal word meaning `` running through '' or `` returning '' .
