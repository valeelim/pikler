Hey Jude     `` hey jude '' is a song by english pop-rock group the beatles . it was written by paul mccartney . it evolved from `` hey jules '' , a song mccartney wrote for lennon 's son , julian , during his parents ' divorce . `` hey jude '' was released in august 1968. it is over seven minutes in length . at the time , this was the longest single ever to top british music charts .
