Bertil Werkström     bertil werkström , born 9 june 1928 in lund cathedral parish , sweden , dead 10 july 2010 in farsta parish , sweden was the archbishop of uppsala between 1983–1993 . his wife was named britta . serving as archbishop he supported ecumenism in international affairs he supported sanctions against the apartheid rule in south africa . in 1984 he also maintained that homosexuality was `` against the orders of creation . '' in 1991 , he visited rome to commemorate the 600th anniversary of the canonization of bridget of sweden together with the pope of the roman catholic church .
