Something to Talk About (movie)    something to talk about ( movie ) something to talk about is a 1995 american romantic comedy-drama movie . julia roberts plays grace . dennis quaid plays her husband eddie , who is having an affair with another woman . robert duvall plays her father wyly . kyra sedgwick plays emma . gena rowlands plays georgia . the movie got mixed reviews from the critics . the title comes from a bonnie raitt song of the same name .
