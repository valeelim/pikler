Paper plane     a paper plane is a toy airplane made from paper . the paper is folded in a certain way so that the folded paper actually glides . this is a form of origami ; the japanese art of paper folding . paper planes were used originally for testing real airplanes . today the making of paper planes is a hobby and a craft . takuo toda holds the world record for the longest time in air ( 27.9 seconds ) . the distance record ( ) was set by joe ayoob , with a plane constructed by john collins , in february 2012 .
