Freak on a Leash     `` freak on a leash '' is the fifth single off american nu metal band korn 's third studio album `` follow the leader '' . the song was released on may 25 , 1999 . `` freak on a leash '' originally had an instrumental part of the song that was described as a `` noisy guitar break '' . the part was taken after fans requested it not be put in . the music video for the song features a mix of animation and live performances . it was directed by todd mcfarlane . the music video debuted on `` total request live '' and was released on february 5 , 1999. the music video won mtv video music awards for best editing and best rock video in 1999 and also won a grammy award for best short form music video at the 42nd annual grammy awards . other websites . - `` freak on a leash '' on youtube
