Aruviyodu Village     aruviyodu village is a hilly hamlet near the river neyyar in india . it is down south in neyyattinkara in the district of thiruvananthapuram . it comes under kunnathukal panchayath . it belongs to south kerala division . it is located east of the district headquarters in thiruvananthapuram . it is from perumkadavila . aruviyodu is surrounded by neyyattinkara taluk towards the west , parassala towards the south , melpuram taluk towards south , munchira taluk towards the south . this place is in the border of the thiruvananthapuram district and kanniyakumari district . the dhanuvachapuram railway station and amaravila halt railway station are near aruviyodu . the trivandrum central railway station is major railway station 26 km from aruviyodu .
