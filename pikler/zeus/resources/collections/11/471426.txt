Elimination Chamber (2013)    elimination chamber ( 2013 ) elimination chamber ( 2013 ) ( no escape ( 2013 ) in germany ) was a professional wrestling pay-per-view show made by wwe . it was held on february 17 , 2013 at new orleans arena in new orleans , louisiana . it was the fourth elimination chamber event held by the wwe . the main even saw the rock defeat cm punk to retain his wwe championship . jack swagger defeated daniel bryan , chris jericho , kane , mark henry and randy orton in the elimination chamber match for a world heavyweight championship match against alberto del rio at wrestlemania 29. other websites . - official elimination chamber 2013 website
