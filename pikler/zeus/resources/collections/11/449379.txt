George Henry Thomas     george henry thomas ( july 31 , 1816 â€“ march 28 , 1870 ) was a general in the u.s. army . he was one of the top union army generals in the western theater of the american civil war . thomas was born in virginia in 1816. he graduated from the united states military academy in 1840. he fought in the mexican-american war in an artillery battery . when the civil war started , he decided to stay in the union army instead of fighting for virginia . he became a brigadier general . he won a victory at the battle of mill springs in kentucky in 1861. in 1862 , he became a major general and commanded a corps in the army of the cumberland . he fought at the battle of stones river and the battle of chickamauga . he became commander of the army of the cumberland during the chattanooga campaign in 1863. he continued commanding the army during the atlanta campaign . when william t. sherman started his march to the sea , thomas was left behind to defend tennessee against john bell hood . thomas defeated hood at the battle of nashville in december 1864 .
