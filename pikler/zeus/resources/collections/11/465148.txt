Cysteine     cysteine ( abbreviated as cys or c ) is an Î±-amino acid . it has the chemical formula hocch ( nh ) chsh . cysteine is a semi-essential amino acid , which means that humans can make it . the codons ugu and ugc code for cysteine . the thiol side chain in cysteine often does enzyme reactions as a nucleophile . the thiol oxidises to give the disulfide derivative cystine , which is important in many protein structures . when used as a food additive , cysteine has the e number e920 .
