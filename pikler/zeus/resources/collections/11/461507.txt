Halim El-Dabh     halim abdul messieh el-dabh ( ( `` ḥalīm ʻabd al-masīḥ al-ḍab '' ʻ ) ; born march 4 , 1921 ) is an egyptian-born american composer , performer , ethnomusicologist , and educator . he has a career spanning six decades . he is known as an early pioneer of electronic music , for having composed in 1944 the first piece of electronic tape music . other websites . - official website
