Máximo Alcócer     máximo luis alcócer ( 15 april 1933 – 13 may 2014 ) was a bolivian footballer whose career lasted for almost ten years . his career debut began in 1954. he retired in 1963. alcócer played for club union maestranza and club aurora . alcócer was born in cochabamba , bolivia . alcócer died in north carolina , united states , aged 81 .
