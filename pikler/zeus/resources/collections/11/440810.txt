Alfonso Cuarón     alfonso cuarón orozco ( ; born 28 november 1961 ) is a mexican movie director , screenwriter , producer and editor . he is best known for his movies `` a little princess '' ( 1995 ) , `` y tu mamá también '' ( 2001 ) , `` harry potter and the prisoner of azkaban '' ( 2004 ) , `` children of men '' ( 2006 ) , and `` gravity '' ( 2013 ) . alfonso cuarón was born in mexico city . he has been married twice . he is now in a relationship with sheherazade goldsmith .
