Marian and Vivian Brown     marian ( january 25 , 1927 – november 20 , 2014 ) and vivian brown ( january 25 , 1927 – january 9 , 2013 ) were american identical twins and celebrity icons of san francisco . they were known as the `` san francisco twins '' . they were voted second as san francisco 's `` best local character '' in 2000. they lived or mostly appeared at nob hill . they were born in kalamazoo , michigan . vivian died on january 9 , 2013 in san francisco , california from alzheimer 's disease , aged 85. marian brown died on november 20 , 2014 from natural causes in san mateo , california , aged 87 .
