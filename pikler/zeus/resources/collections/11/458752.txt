Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Edmonton     the roman catholic archdiocese of edmonton is a roman catholic archdiocese in alberta . it was created in 1912. the archdiocese covers central alberta , edmonton capital region , and the middle and upper half of the alberta 's rockies region . it has 129 parishes . the archdiocese 's cathedral is st. joseph 's basilica in edmonton . the archdiocese has two suffragan dioceses , both located in alberta : the diocese of calgary and the diocese of saint paul . the current archbishop of edmonton is richard william smith , since 2008 .
