Canadians     canadians ( ) are people identified with the country of canada . this connection may be residential , legal , historical , or cultural . for most canadians , several ( or all ) of these connections exist and are collectively the source of their being `` canadian '' . canada is a bilingual and multicultural society home to people of many different ethnic , religious and national origins , with the majority of the population made up of old world immigrants and their descendants . following the starting period of french and then the much larger british colonization , different waves ( or peaks ) of immigration and settlement of non-indigenous peoples took place over the course of nearly two centuries and continue today . elements of indigenous , french , british , and more recent immigrant customs , languages and religions have combined to form the culture of canada and thus a canadian identity . canada has also been strongly influenced by its linguistic , geographic and economic neighbour , the united states .
