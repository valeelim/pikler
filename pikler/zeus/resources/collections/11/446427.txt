Ronald Selmour     ronald selmour is a canadian actor . he is well known for his role as firecracker man in abc 's `` kingdom hospital '' , sundown in the discovery channel 's tv miniseries `` klondike '' and the butcher in the cw 's `` arrow '' . selmour has been involved in the performing arts from the early years of his life . though profoundly religious , his father was very involved with local theatres taking him to see presentations throughout port-au-prince , haiti . in the early 1980s the family made the difficult decision to let him go to canada , stayed with his aunt while pursuing his studies . in montreal , quebec , there he made the acquaintance of several instrumental people in his life , teachers , professors and a mentor name solly levy who introduced him to the comedia dell'arte . ever since then , the love for the craft became the guiding light in his life .
