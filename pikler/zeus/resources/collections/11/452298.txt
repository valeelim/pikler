All About Eve     all about eve is a 1950 american drama movie . the movie is about an aging broadway star . a young fan , eve harrington , threatens the career and the relationships of the star . bette davis plays the broadway star , margo channing . anne baxter plays eve harrington . marilyn monroe and barbara bates are also in the movie . `` all about eve '' was nominated for 14 academy awards . it won six , including best director and best picture .
