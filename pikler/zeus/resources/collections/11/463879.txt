Manuel Uribe Ángel    manuel uribe ángel manuel maría uribe angel ( 4 september 1822 – 16 june 1904 ) was a colombian physician , geographer and politician . he has been called the `` father of medicine of antioquia '' . he was known outside the realm of medicine for his studies and works on the geography and history of colombia . he also served as president of the then sovereign state of antioquia and as senator of colombia .
