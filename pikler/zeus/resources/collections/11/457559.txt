Pim de Kuijer     pim de kuijer wilhelm leonard ( august 13 , 1981 - july 17 , 2014 ) was a dutch columnist and lobbyist . he worked as a lobbyist for the organization stop aids now ! . kuijer was with dozens of others on their way to an international aids conference in melbourne , when the plane he was on malaysia airlines flight 17 over ukraine was shot down , aged 31 .
