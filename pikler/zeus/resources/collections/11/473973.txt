Theo Colborn     theodora emily colborn ( née decker ; march 28 , 1927 – december 14 , 2014 ) was an american zoologist . she was the founder and president emeritus of the endocrine disruption exchange ( tedx ) . colborn was also professor emeritus of zoology at the university of florida , gainesville . she was best known for her studies on the health effects of endocrine disrupting chemicals . other websites . - our stolen future - tedx , the endocrine disruption exchange - dr. theo colborn on the health effects of water contamination from fracking - video report by `` democracy now !
