Crash course     crash course ( also known as a mother 's fight for justice ) is a 2001 american made-for-tv drama movie , inspired by a true story . a mother is angry at the boy who hit her son while driving drunk , because he never apologizes . she tries to fight for revenge against the will of her husband and son . after he served in prison , the son decided to stop the fight , he came to say that he was never allowed to say that he is sorry . cast . - meredith baxter - alan rosenberg - eric lively
