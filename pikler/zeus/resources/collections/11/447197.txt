Jiří Načeradský     jiří načeradský ( 9 september 1939 – 16 april 2014 ) was a czech painter , graphic artist and educator . he was best known for his human figures , sometimes with erotic and sexual subtext and context . he was born in sedlec-prčice , příbram district , czechoslovakia . načeradský died on 16 april 2014 in prague , czech republic , aged 74. other websites . - jiří načeradský at databáze národní knihovny čr
