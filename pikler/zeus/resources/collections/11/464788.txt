Pedro Peña (actor)    pedro peña ( actor ) pedro peña allén ( december 14 , 1925 – october 2 , 2014 ) was an spanish actor . he was known for his roles in `` médico de familia '' ( 1995 ) , `` un paso adelante '' ( 2002 ) and `` dulce '' ( 2011 ) . he was born in tordehumos , spain . peña died in madrid , spain from alzheimer 's disease , aged 88 .
