Kulbhushan Kharbanda     kulbhushan kharbanda ( born 21 october 1944 ) is an indian actor . he worked in hindi and punjabi movie . he is better known for his role as shakaal in `` shaan '' ( 1980 ) inspired by the character of blofeld from james bond movies . he also starred in `` arth '' ( 1982 ) , `` ek chadar maili si '' ( 1986 ) . he also appeared in all deepa mehta 's elements trilogy , `` fire '' ( 1996 ) , `` earth '' ( 1998 ) , and `` water '' ( 2005 ) . kharbanda was born in hasan abdal , punjab , british india ( now pakistan ) .
