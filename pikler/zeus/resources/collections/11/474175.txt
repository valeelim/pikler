Erik Gustaf Boström     erik gustaf bernhard boström , born 11 february 1842 , dead 21 february 1907 , was a swedish landowner and politician . he was a member of the swedish parliament between 1876 and 1907. he was the longest-serving prime minister of sweden of the 19th century . he served in that office twice , first from 1891 to 1900. then he served again from 1902 to 1905. he was also known as e.g . boström or e. gust . boström .
