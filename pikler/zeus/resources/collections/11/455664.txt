Stanley B. Prusiner     stanley benjamin prusiner ( born may 28 , 1942 ) is an american neurologist and biochemist . prusiner currently works at the university of california , san francisco ( ucsf ) as a the director of the institute for neurodegenerative diseases . prusiner won the nobel prize in physiology or medicine in 1997 `` for his discovery of prions - a new biological principle of infection '' . he also won the albert lasker award for basic medical research in 1994 .
