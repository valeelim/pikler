Muppet Treasure Island     muppet treasure island is a 1996 musical adventure comedy movie . it is based upon robert louie stevenson 's novel treasure island , and is about the muppets going on a sea voyage and meeting pirates . it is the fifth feature movie to star the muppets . brian henson ( son of jim henson ) directed . the reviews of `` muppet treasure island '' were mostly positive . the movie was a strong success at the box office .
