Keitele     keitele is a municipality in northern savonia , finland . in january 2014 , 2,430 people lived there . the municipalities next to it are pielavesi , pihtipudas , tervo , vesanto and viitasaari . the municipality was established in 1879 , but camp sites been found from the 1300s . hamula , koutajärvi , kukertaja , leppäselkä , pahkamäki , petäjäkylä , sulkavanjärvi , tossavanlahti , viinikkala , vuonamo , äyräpää and hemminki .
