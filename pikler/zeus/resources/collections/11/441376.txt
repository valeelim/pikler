SEPECAT Jaguar     the sepecat jaguar is a twin-turbojet military aircraft . it was designed and built as an attack aircraft , also used as a training aircraft . it was built by sepecat , an anglo-french company . it first flew in 1968 . `` armée de l'air '' and royal air force were the main users . it was used in wars in africa by french , and in gulf war and afghanistan war by british and french . it 's also built under licence in india as jaguar international . apart `` armée de l'air '' and royal air force the jaguar was purchased by ecuador , india , nigeria and oman .
