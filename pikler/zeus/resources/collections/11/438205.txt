Maxine Kumin     maxine kumin ( june 6 , 1925 – february 6 , 2014 ) was an american poet and author . she became poet laureate consultant in poetry to the library of congress in 1981–1982 . kumin died on february 6 , 2014 in warner , new hampshire . she was 88. her death came after a year of bad health . she had a husband , victor , and three children .
