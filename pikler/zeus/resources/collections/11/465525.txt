Las Palmas Cathedral     the cathedral of santa ana ( holy cathedral-basilica of canary or cathedral of las palmas de gran canaria ) is a cathedral in las palmas , canary islands . it is the seat of the diocese of the canaries in the roman catholic church . it is in the vegueta neighborhood , next to the plaza mayor of santa ana . the feast of the cathedral 's dedication is celebrated annually on november 26. it is one of two cathedrals in the canary islands , near the la laguna cathedral in tenerife .
