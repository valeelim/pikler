Eckart Höfling     eckart höfling ( 28 october 1936 – 1 march 2014 ) was a german catholic priest . he was best known for working in the fight against poverty in brazil . he was born in langenprozelten , lower franconia . höfling died after a long illness on 1 march 2014 in main-spessart , bavaria . he was 77 years old .
