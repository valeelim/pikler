Daniel Keyes     daniel keyes ( august 9 , 1927 - june 15 , 2014 ) was an american author . he was well known for his hugo award-winning short story and nebula award-winning novel `` flowers for algernon '' . keyes was given the author emeritus honor by the science fiction and fantasy writers of america in 2000. keyes died of pneumonia in boca raton , florida , aged 86 .
