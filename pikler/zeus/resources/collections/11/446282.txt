Vicco von Bülow     bernhard-victor christoph-carl von bülow , short : vicco von bülow , `` alias '' loriot ( 12 november 1923 – 22 august 2011 ) was a german comedian , humorist , cartoonist , movie director , actor and writer . he is best known for his cartoons , the sketches from his 1976 television series `` loriot '' , alongside evelyn hamann , and his two movies , `` ödipussi '' ( 1988 ) and `` pappa ante portas '' ( 1991 ) . von bülow was born in brandenburg an der havel . he died on 22 august 2011 in ammerland at lake starnberg , germany from natural causes , aged 87. other websites . - `` loriot , german comedy legend , dead at 87 '' , `` the hollywood reporter '' - `` germany mourns the death of its best comedian '' , `` spiegel online ''
