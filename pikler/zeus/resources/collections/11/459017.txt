Ralph Branca     ralph theodore joseph branca ( january 6 , 1926 – november 23 , 2016 ) was an american baseball player . he was a former starting pitcher in major league baseball . from 1944 through 1956 , branca played for the brooklyn dodgers ( 1944–53 , 1956 ) , detroit tigers ( 1953–54 ) , and new york yankees ( 1954 ) . branca died on the morning of november 23 , 2016 at a nursing home in rye brook , new york , aged 90. other websites . - baseball library - the top 5 reasons you ca n't blame ralph branca ( espn classic ) - joshua prager 's `` the echoing green ''
