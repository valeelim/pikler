China Zorrilla     china zorrilla ( , concepción matilde zorrilla de san martín muñoz ; 14 march 1922 – 17 september 2014 ) was an uruguayan theater , movie , and television actress . she was known for her roles in `` la maffia '' , `` esperando la carroza '' , `` besos en la frente '' , `` conversations with mother '' , and in `` elsa & fred '' . zorrilla died on 17 september 2014 from pneumonia in a hospital in montevideo , uruguay , aged 92 .
