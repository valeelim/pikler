Jaume Vallcorba Plana     jaume ( or santiago ) vallcorba plana ( 21 september 1949 – 23 august 2014 ) was a spanish philologist and publisher . he published many studies on aesthetics and literature . he was born in tarragona , spain . he was a professor in literature at the university of bordeaux , the university of lleida , the university of barcelona and the pompeu fabra university ( barcelona ) but he left university teaching in 2004. other websites . - el país , 14/05/2010 ( interview with jaume vallcorba )
