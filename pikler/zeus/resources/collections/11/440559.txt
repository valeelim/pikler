Antoine Pinay     antoine pinay ( ; 30 december 1891 – 13 december 1994 ) was a french conservative politician . he served as prime minister of france from march 1952 through march 1953. pinay was born in saint-symphorien-sur-coise , rhône . he died in saint-chamond , loire , at the age of 102. he is the third longest-lived national head of government or head of state in history , behind chau sen cocsal chhum and celâl bayar .
