Lincoln Park     lincoln park is a 1,208 acre ( 488.86 ha ) park along the lakefront of chicago , illinois ' north side , facing lake michigan . it is chicago 's largest public park . it is named after abraham lincoln . it stretches for seven miles ( 11 km ) from ohio street ( 600 n ) on the south to near ardmore avenue ( 5800 n ) on the north , just north of the lake shore drive terminus at hollywood avenue . several museums and a zoo are located between north avenue ( 1600 n ) and diversey parkway ( 2800 n ) in the neighborhood that takes its name from the park , lincoln park . the park further to the north is surrounded by parkland , beaches , recreational areas , nature reserves , and harbors . to the south , there is a more narrow strip of beaches east of lake shore drive , almost to downtown . with 20 million visitors a year , lincoln park is the second most visited park in the united states .
