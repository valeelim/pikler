Clidastes     clidastes is an extinct genus of mosasaur from marine environments of the late cretaceous . it was an agile and fast swimmer which cruised the surface or shallow waters hunting for prey . `` clidastes '' was one of the smallest of the mosasaurs , averaging in length , with the largest specimens reaching long . it had a delicate , slim form with an expansion near the tip of the tail . this enabled it to chase down the fastest of prey .
