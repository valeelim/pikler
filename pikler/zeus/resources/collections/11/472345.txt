Wellfleet, Massachusetts    wellfleet , massachusetts wellfleet is a town in barnstable county , massachusetts , united states . it is located halfway between the `` tip '' and `` elbow '' of cape cod . the town had a population of 2,750 at the 2010 census . the population grows to over six times the normal during the summer . much of the town 's land area is in wetlands protection . nearly half of it is part of the cape cod national seashore . wellfleet is famous for its oysters , which are celebrated in the annual october wellfleet oysterfest .
