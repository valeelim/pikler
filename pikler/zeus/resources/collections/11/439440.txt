Troops of Tomorrow     troops of tomorrow is the second studio album by scottish punk rock band , the exploited . `` troops of tomorrow '' was released in 1982. american heavy metal band slayer and rapper ice-t covered a medley called `` disorder '' which was featured on the soundtrack to the 1993 movie judgment night . the song `` uk82 '' was also used as the opening and closing theme for the 1983 movie `` made in britain '' starring tim roth . the music video for the song `` fuck the usa '' starts with a typed intro and says `` and so ... a pandora 's box of rejection and reaction was opened . today , thousands carry on the greatest anti-movement of our age . punk rock , the end of our dreams ... '' the lead vocalist wattie buchan starts shouting while the music starts . they play on stage while there are some clips of the band walking around the streets of scotland and having fun . the song ends with an interview with the band . side one side two
