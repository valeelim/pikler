Carl Fredrik Mennander     carl fredrik mennander ( sometimes modernised as `` karl fredrik mennander '' , often just `` c.f . mennander '' ; 19 july 1712 , stockholm , sweden , 22 may 1786 , uppsala , sweden ) was a swedish lutheran clergyman . he was the bishop of turku between 1757-1775. he then served as the church of sweden archbishop of uppsala from 1775 until his 1786 death .
