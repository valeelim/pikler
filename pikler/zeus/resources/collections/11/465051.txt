Elissa's Quest    elissa 's quest elissa 's quest is a children 's fantasy novel written by erica verillo . the book was first published on june 26 , 2007 and has been printed again twice since then on june 24 , 2008 and march 12 , 2009. the novel is the first in verillo 's `` the phoenix rising '' trilogy , and is followed by `` elissa 's odyssey '' and `` world 's end '' . the trilogy follows 13 year old elissa in a fantasy world where she must team up with several other gifted youngsters and grant the phoenix another regeneration- but even she does not know this . fate must show her . the book also has cover art by omar rayyan and is aimed for children ages 9 to 12 .
