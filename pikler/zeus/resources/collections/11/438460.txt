Kittilä     kittilä is a municipality in lapland , finland . as of january 2014 there were almost 6,500 people living there . the municipalities next to it are enontekiö , inari , kolari , muonio , rovaniemi and sodankylä . kittilä has an airport , and a ski resort named levi . a large coal mine is located in kittilä . it is owned by the canadian mining company : agnico-eagle . they have leased the mine until 2044. there is also a copper mine in kittilä . kittilä was established 1854 by olavi rauhala , vieno suvanto and johan lahti . however , the earliest settlement of the area was long before . other websites . - kittilä page
