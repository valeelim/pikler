Gotham (TV series)    gotham ( tv series ) gotham is an upcoming american television series created by bruno heller . it is based on the characters of dc comics . it is set in gotham city , but was filmed in new york city and parts of chicago and detroit . the shows takes place when then rookie detective police commissioner james gordon and when bruce wayne 's parents were murdered . it stars ben mckenzie , david mazouz , jada pinkett smith , and sean pertwee . it will be aired by fox . the first episode will be aired in fall 2014 .
