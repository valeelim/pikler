Aldo Vannucchi     aldo vannucchi ( born 24 september 1928 ) is an educator from sorocaba , brazil . he is a former rector of the university of sorocaba uniso . he is master of science in theology and philosophy , with several specialized courses at universities in rome , geneva and louvain in belgium . vanucchi was also chosen by the minister of education to form the national council of education in brazil . other websites . - interview with aldo vannuchi ( in portuguese )
