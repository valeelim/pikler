Edmonton Rush     the edmonton rush was a professional box lacrosse team in edmonton , alberta , canada . it was part of the west division of national lacrosse league ( nll ) . the team started playing in the nll in 2005. they played their home games in rexall place . it had a rivalry with the calgary roughnecks , another nll team in alberta . the rivalry was part of the sports battle of alberta . on july 20 , 2015 , it was announced that the team was relocating to saskatoon , saskatchewan to become the saskatchewan rush for the 2016 season .
