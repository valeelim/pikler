Erik Dahlberg Secondary School     the erik dahlberg secondary school ( ) is a secondary school in jönköping in sweden . for many years the school focused in technical education . the school was opened in 1949 as the jönköping technical secondary school ( ) . the current name was adopted from the 1966-1967 school year . the current buildings were completed , step by step , between 1961 and 1994. other websites . - official website
