Mieczysław Rakowski     mieczysław rakowski ( december 1 , 1926 – november 8 , 2008 ) was a polish communist politician , historian and journalist . he served as an officer in the polish people 's army from 1945 to 1949. he served as prime minister of poland from 1988 to 1989. rakowski died on november 8 , 2008 from cancer in warsaw at the age of 81 .
