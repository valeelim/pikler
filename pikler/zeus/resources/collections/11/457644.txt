Annabelle (movie)    annabelle ( movie ) annabelle is a 2014 american supernatural horror movie . it was directed by john r. leonetti and written by gary dauberman . it is a prequel of `` the conjuring '' . it is set in 1969 in santa monica , california . it stars annabelle wallis , ward horton , tony amendola , and alfre woodard . the movie was released worldwide on october 3 , 2014 .
