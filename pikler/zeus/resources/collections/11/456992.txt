RoboCop     robocop is a 1987 science fiction action crime movie . it is directed by paul verhoeven . the movie stars peter weller , nancy allen , dan o'herlihy , kurtwood smith , miguel ferrer , and ronny cox . it is set in a crime-ridden detroit , michigan in the near future where the police force is run by security concepts , a corporation . `` robocop '' centers on police officer alex murphy ( weller ) who is brutally attacked and fatally wounded by a gang of criminals . he is then made into a cyborg law enforcer known as `` robocop '' . the movie had two sequels , robocop 2 ( 1990 ) and robocop 3 ( 1993 ) . a remake of this movie was released in february 2014 .
