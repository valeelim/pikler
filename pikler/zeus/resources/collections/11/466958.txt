Salus populi suprema lex esto     salus populi suprema lex esto ( latin `` the safety of the people should be the supreme law , '' or `` the welfare of the people shall be the supreme law '' ) is found in cicero 's `` de legibus '' ( book iii , part iii , sub . viii ) . it is the state motto of missouri . it is part of its state seal . it is the motto of : - lewisham - eastleigh - harrow - lytham st. anne 's - tipton - the city of manassas park , virginia - the duquesne university school of law - vlaams belang political group in the belgian chamber of representatives
