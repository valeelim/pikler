Jean-Baptiste Alphonse Karr     jean-baptiste alphonse karr ( 24 november 1808 – 29 september 1890 ) was a french critic , journalist , and novelist . his brother eugène was a talented engineer , and his niece carme karr was a writer , journalist and suffragist in la roche-mabile . in 1839 alphonse karr became editor of `` le figaro '' . karr was born in paris and died in saint-raphael , var . the bamboo species `` bambusa multiplex alphonse karr '' was named in his honour .
