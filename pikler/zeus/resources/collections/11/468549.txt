Drug tolerance     drug tolerance ( or physiological tolerance ) is a term used to describe that the organism gets used to a given substance . drug tolerance also means that over time a drug becomes less effective and larger amounts of the substance are needed to get the same effect . physiological tolerance can be achieved in one of the following ways : 1. by changing the number of receptors for a substance 1. by changing the way the signal is handled 1. by changing the receptors themselves 1. by changing the system that antagonizes the receptor
