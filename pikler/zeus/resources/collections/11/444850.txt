Jerry Roberts     captain jerry roberts mbe ( raymond c. roberts ) ( 18 november 1920 – 25 march 2014 ) was a british businessman and wartime codebreaker . during world war ii , roberts was worked as a codebreaker at bletchley park from 1941 to 1945. he was a leading codebreaker and linguist , who worked on tunny — hitler 's most top-level code . he was born in wembley , middlesex . robert died on 25 march 2014 , aged 93 .
