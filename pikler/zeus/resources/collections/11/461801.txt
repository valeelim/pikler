Boys on the Side     boys on the side is a 1995 american comedy-drama movie . it is about three friends who go on a cross-country trip . drew barrymore plays holly , who is pregnant and is leaving her violent partner . mary-louise parker plays robin , a real estate agent who is hiv+ . whoopi goldberg plays jane , a lesbian singer . the movie got positive reviews from critics . it made only a little more money than it cost to make .
