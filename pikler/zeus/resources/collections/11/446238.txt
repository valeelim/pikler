Nigel Evans     nigel martin evans ( born 10 november 1957 ) is a british politician . he was born in swansea , wales . he has been the member of parliament for ribble valley since 1992. he was a conservative until 2013. since then he has been an independent . he was first deputy chairman of ways and means from 2010 to 2013. evans lives in pendleton , lancashire . he is openly gay . in 2014 at preston crown court , he was acquitted of nine sex crimes , including a rape .
