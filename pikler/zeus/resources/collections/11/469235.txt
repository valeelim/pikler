Mutual Broadcasting System     the mutual broadcasting system was an american radio network . it broadcast radio shows from 1934 to 1999. it was also called mutual broadcasting company ; mutual ; or the mutual broadcasting system , inc. mutual broadcast radio shows , such as `` the lone ranger '' , `` the adventures of superman '' , and `` the shadow '' . it broadcast baseball games , including the all-star game and world series , and football games for the university of notre dame . from 1978 to 1994 , it broadcast the `` larry king show .
