Gogó Andreu     ricardo césar andreu ( 27 july 1919 – 1 may 2012 ) was an argentine actor , comedian and musician . he was known for his roles in `` crosswords '' ( 2003 ) , `` india pravile '' ( 2003 ) , and in `` gemini '' ( 2005 ) . andreu was born in a family of actors . when he was 12 years old the tango singer josé razzano , brought him to the broadway theatre where he met carlos gardel . andreu died in buenos aires from respiratory failure , aged 92 .
