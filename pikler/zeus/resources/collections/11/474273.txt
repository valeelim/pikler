Dementia with Lewy bodies     dementia with lewy bodies ( dlb ) , also known under a variety of other names including lewy body dementia ( lbd ) , diffuse lewy body disease , cortical lewy body disease , and senile dementia of lewy type , is a type of dementia closely related with parkinson 's disease . it is characterized by the presence of lewy bodies , parts of alpha-synuclein and ubiquitin protein in neurons , detectable in `` post mortem '' brain histology . lewy body dementia affects 1.3 million individuals in the united states alone .
