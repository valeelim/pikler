Sarracenia     sarracenia ( or ) is a genus of 8 to 11 species of carnivorous plants . they are often called trumpet pitchers . the genus is in the family sarraceniaceae . that family also contains `` darlingtonia '' and `` heliamphora '' . in the wild , `` sarracenia '' grows around the east coast of the united states , texas , the great lakes area and southeast canada . most species grow only in the southeast united states . only `` s. purpurea '' can live in cold places . the plant 's leaves have evolved into a funnel in order to trap insects . the plant digests its food with proteases and other enzymes .
