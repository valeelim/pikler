Arbaeen     arbaeen ( , `` forty '' ) , chehelom ( , , `` the fortieth [ day ] '' ) or qirkhi , imamin qirkhi ( , `` the fortieth of imam '' ) is a shia muslim religious observance that occurs forty days after the day of ashura . it honors the martyrdom of husayn ibn ali , the grandson of muhammad . imam husayn ibn ali and 72 companions were killed by yazid i 's army in the battle of karbala in 61 ah ( 680 ce ) . arbaeen or forty days is also the usual length of mourning in many muslim traditions . arbaeen is one of the largest pilgrimage gatherings on earth . millions of people go to the city of karbala in iraq . the pilgrims include shia , sunnis , christians , yazidi and other faiths .
