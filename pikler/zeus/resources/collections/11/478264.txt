Psychological trauma     psychological trauma is damage to the psyche that happens after a stressful event . it can be caused by things that make a person afraid . common causes include humiliation , sexual abuse , bullying , and domestic violence . living with any of those for a long time can cause psychological trauma . people who have suffered psychological trauma , particularly in childhood and adolescence , are more likely to have psychological problems . those problems can include posttraumatic stress disorder ( ptsd ) and borderline personality disorder .
