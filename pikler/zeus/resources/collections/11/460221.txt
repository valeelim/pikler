Pierre Vassiliu     pierre vassiliu ( 23 october 1937 – 17 august 2014 ) was a french singer and songwriter . he was born in geneva , switzerland . his first song , `` qui c'est celui-là '' ( 1973 ) , sold more than 300,000 copies . he once sang with the beatles in 1964. vassiliu died in lyon , france , aged 76. other websites . - biography ( in french ) - biography
