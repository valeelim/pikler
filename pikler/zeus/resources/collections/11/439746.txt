Nousiainen     nousiainen is a municipality in southwest finland . as of january 2014 about 4,900 people lived there . neighbouring municipalities are aura , masku , mynämäki , pöytyä , rusko and turku . most people there speak finnish . part of kurjenrahka national park is located in part of the area of nousiainen . hakola , heininen , hyrkkö , kaisela , kaitarinen , kallunen , keskusoja , kytöinen , kärmälä , köönikkälä , laaleinen , laihonen , leinainen , moijonen , nummi , nutturla , ojankulma , paijula , paistanoja , pakainen , palo , papumäki , puotunen , rekoinen , ristimäki , riukula , sukkinen , tappuri , topoinen , vastlahti , kukkola and mutola other websites . - nousiainen website
