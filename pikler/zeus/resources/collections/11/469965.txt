Uses and gratifications theory     the uses and gratifications theory is a theory by blumer and katz . it states that different people watch television and movies for different reasons . there may be somebody who will watch a romantic comedy so that they can put themselves in the shoes of the characters and feel love . somebody may watch `` breaking bad '' so that they can join in with social conversation relating to that topic . it may be said that you would watch a television for escapism so that you forget the stress or normal life .
