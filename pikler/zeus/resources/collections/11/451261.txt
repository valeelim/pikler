George Givot     george givot ( february 18 , 1903 - june 7 , 1984 ) was an actor in movies , television and radio . his strength was speaking in several ethnic dialects . one famous role he played was the voice of tony in `` lady and the tramp '' . givot was born on february 18 , 1903 in omaha , nebraska . he died on june 7 , 1984 in palm springs , california at age 81 .
