Kurtwood Smith     kurtwood larson smith ( born july 3 , 1943 ) is an american television and movie actor . he is known for playing clarence boddicker in `` robocop '' and as red forman in `` that '70s show '' . he is also known for his appearances in the genre of science fiction ( `` star trek '' , `` the x-files '' ) . he also starred in `` 24 '' .
