Patrick Wilson     patrick joseph wilson ( born july 3 , 1973 ) is an american actor and singer . he is known for his roles in `` the alamo '' , `` the phantom of the opera '' , `` little children '' , `` watchmen '' , `` the a-team '' , `` the ledge '' , `` insidious '' , and `` the conjuring '' . wilson appeared in a voice cameo as the president of the united states in `` '' ( 2016 ) and will appear as ocean master in `` aquaman '' ( 2018 ) .
