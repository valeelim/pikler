Gilles Ségal     gilles ségal ( 13 january 1929 – 11 june 2014 ) was a french actor and playwright . he starred in more than sixty movies beginning in 1954. he was born in fălticeni , romania . he is known for his role as a heist participant in jules dassin 's `` topkapi '' . ségal died in france , aged 82 .
