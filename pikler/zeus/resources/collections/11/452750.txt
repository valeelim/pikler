Svyatoslav Belza     svyatoslav igorevich belza ( russian : святосла́в и́горевич бэ́лза , april 26 , 1942 – june 3 , 2014 ) was a soviet/russian literary and musical scholar , critic and essayist . in 1980 , he became a television presenter . he presented television shows that involved classical music and ballet . he was made people 's artist of russia . belza was born in chelyabinsk , rsfsr . he died in munich , germany from an illness , aged 72 .
