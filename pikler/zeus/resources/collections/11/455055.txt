François Cluzet     françois cluzet ( born 21 september 1955 ) is a french actor . cluzet grew up in paris , he made his debut in 1976. cluzet 's biggest hit is `` intouchable '' , the movie is known as `` the intouchables '' in english regions . cluzet has a son named paul , whose mother was actress marie trintignant . cluzet has 3 other children : blanche , joseph and marguerite .
