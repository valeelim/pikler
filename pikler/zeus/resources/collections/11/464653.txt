Naturalism (literature)    naturalism ( literature ) naturalism was a 19th century literary movement . it was a development of the realism movement . realism attempted to show subjects as they actually were , honestly and naturally . naturalism added scientific elements such as environment and heredity to fictional characters . unlike realism , naturalism used characters who were of the lower classes . it was also known for themes including taboo and violence .
