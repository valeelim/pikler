Julio Jimenez     julio jiménez muñoz ( born 28 october 1934 ) is a spanish retired professional cyclist . he was good at climbing . he won the polka dot jersey for being best climber at the tour de france three times in a row in 1965 , 1966 and 1967. he also won the mountains competition at the vuelta a espana three times . he is one of four riders to complete the tour/vuelta double by winning both mountains competitions in the same year . major accomplishments . - 1963 - 1964 – kas-kaskol - 1965 – kas-kaskol - 1966 – ford-france-hutchinson - 1967 – spain - 1968 – spain other websites . - palmarès at velo-club.net
