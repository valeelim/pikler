Arrondissement of Nevers     the arrondissement of nevers is an arrondissement of france . it is part of the nièvre `` département '' in the bourgogne-franche-comté region . its capital , and prefecture of the department , is the city of nevers . when the nièvre department was created on 17 february 1800 , the `` arrondissement '' of nevers was part of that original department . the `` arrondissement '' of nevers is bordered to the north by the `` arrondissement '' of cosne-cours-sur-loire , to the northeast by the `` arrondissement '' of clamecy , to the east by the `` arrondissement '' of château-chinon ( ville ) , to the south by the allier ( auvergne-rhône-alpes ) department and to the west by the cher ( centre-val de loire ) department . the `` arrondissement '' is in the southwestern part of the department , with an area of , the largest in area of the department . it has 118,087 inhabitants , the `` arrondissement '' of nièvre with more people living there , and a population density of inhabitants/km² . after the reorganisation of the cantons in france , cantons are not subdivisions of the `` arrondissements '' so they could have `` communes '' that belong to different `` arrondissements '' .
