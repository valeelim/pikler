Thai solar calendar     the thai solar calendar is a solar calendar that is used by thailand . king chulalongkorn adopted the calendar in ad 1888 to be the siamese version of the gregorian calendar . the years are now counted in the buddhist era rather than the christian era . on 6 september 1940 , prime minister phibunsongkhram stated that 1 january 1941 would be the start of the year 2484 be , so that year 2483 be had only nine months . to convert dates from 1 january to 31 march before that year , the number to add or subtract is 542 ; otherwise , it would be 543 .
