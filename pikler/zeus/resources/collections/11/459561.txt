Carbide &amp; Carbon Building    carbide & amp ; carbon building the carbide & carbon building is a chicago landmark located at 230 n. michigan avenue . the building , which was built in 1929 , is an example of art deco architecture . it was designed by burnham brothers . the architect was daniel burnham . the carbide and carbon building was originally home to the regional office of union carbide and carbon co. , which later became union carbide corp. the skyscraper was designated a chicago landmark on may 9 , 1996. it was built as a skyscraper office tower . the building has 37 floors and is tall . the top of the building is covered in pure gold . it is also modeled as a wine glass to show a resistance against the prohibition of alcohol in the united states . other websites . - chicago landmarks : carbide and carbon building - carbide & carbon building on emporis - hard rock hotel chicago
