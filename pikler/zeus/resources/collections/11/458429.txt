Africa Cup of Nations     the africa cup of nations , officially can ( french : coupe d'afrique des nations ) , also known as the african cup of nations , african nations cup or afcon , is the main international association football tournament in africa . it is held every two years , and the winners that win at the time of the fifa confederations cup automatically qualify for that tournament . the team with the most titles is egypt , with 7 titles . the most recent winner was cameroon in 2017 after winning over egypt in the final . results . - hosts
