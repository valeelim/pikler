Sidharth Malhotra     sidharth malhotra ( born 16 january 1985 ) is an indian movie actor and former model . he appears in hindi language films of bollywood . he started his career in movies with karan johar 's movie 'student of the year ' ( 2012 ) for which he received a nomination in the filmfare award for best male debut . later , he gained appreciation in the 2014 movie `` hasee toh phasee '' in which he was paired opposite parineeti chopra , recently he has been paired opposite shraddha kapoor in `` ek villain '' which has been released on june 27 , 2014. besides acting in movies , he performs in award functions and is a celebrity endorser for many brands .
