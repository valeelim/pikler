David Steel     david martin scott steel , baron steel of aikwood ( born 31 march 1938 ) is a british retired liberal politician . steel served as the leader of the liberal party from 1976 until its merger with the social democratic party in 1988 to form the liberal democrats . he served as a member of parliament ( mp ) from 1965 to 1997 and as a member of the scottish parliament ( msp ) from 1999 to 2003 .
