Jerry Parr     jerry s. parr ( september 16 , 1930 - october 9 , 2015 ) was a secret service agent . he was one of the agents protecting president reagan on the day of his assassination attempt on march 30 , 1981. parr 's interest in joining the secret service originated as a boy after watching `` code of the secret service '' ( 1939 ) starring ronald reagan as agent `` brass '' bancroft . parr died of congestive heart failure in washington , d.c. on october 9 , 2015 , aged 85 .
