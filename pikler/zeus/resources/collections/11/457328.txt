Typhoon Bopha     typhoon bopha ( aka typhoon pablo ) was a severe tropical cyclone in december 2012. it hit the philippines . the typhoon struck palau first . it destroyed homes and caused power outages . the storm did a lot of damage to the philippine island of mindanao . over 600 deaths were reported . the names `` pablo '' and `` bopha '' were retired because of the large number of deaths and the great destruction .
