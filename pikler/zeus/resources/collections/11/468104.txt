Gustau Biosca     gustavo biosca pagés ( 29 february 1928 – 1 november 2014 ) was a spanish football player and manager . biosca played as a defender for españa industrial , barcelona and condal . he was born in l'hospitalet de llobregat , catalonia , spain . he made his international debut for spain in 1951 , earning a total of 11 caps , including 2 in fifa world cup qualifying matches . as a coach he managed pontevedra , sant andreu , real valladolid , sabadell , the spanish under-21 national team and terrassa . biosca died on 1 november 2014 , at the age of 86 in barcelona , catalonia , spain .
