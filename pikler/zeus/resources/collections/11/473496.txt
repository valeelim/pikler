Chakri     chakradhar gilla ( known as chakri ) ( 15 june 1974 – 15 december 2014 ) was an indian music composer and singer . he works for tollywood movies . he won the filmfare award for best male playback singer – telugu for the movie `` satyam '' ( 2003 ) . his song named `` o maguva nee sneham kosam '' from his movie `` satyam '' was thought to be his best known work . chakri was born in mahabubabad , telangana . chakri died from a heart attack in hyderabad , telangana , aged 40 .
