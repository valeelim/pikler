Georgy Martyniuk     georgy yakovlevich martyniuk ( russian : гео́ргий я́ковлевич мартыню́к ; 3 march 1930 – 13 february 2014 ) was a russian movie and theater actor . he was born in orenburg on 3 march 1930. martyniuk graduated from the russian academy of theatre arts in 1962. in 2003 , he received the people 's artist of russia honor . he was married to actress valentina martyniuk ( russian : валентина мартынюк ) . he died on 13 february 2014 in moscow , after a long illness in moscow , russia .
