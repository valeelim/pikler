Jindřich Pokorný     jindřich pokorný ( april 12 , 1927 - august 23 , 2014 ) was a czech translator ( mainly french and german ) , editor and writer . he translated also in italian , latin and flemish . he was known for his translation of `` rostand 's cyrano de bergerac '' . along with the literary historian frederick fučík , they wrote a book explaining the origins and meanings of the various sayings and proverbs called `` buried dog '' . in 2009 he published a book `` parsifal '' .
