Earl Ray Tomblin     earl ray tomblin ( born march 15 , 1952 ) is an american politician . in 2010 , he became acting governor of west virginia after joe manchin was elected to the u.s. senate . he was acting governor from november 15 , 2010 until 2011. from 2011 to 2017 he was the governor of west virginia . on october 2011 , he won a special election to fill the term expiring in january 2013. on november 2012 , he won election to his first full term . before becoming governor , he was president of the west virginia senate .
