Trapped (Tupac Shakur song)    trapped ( tupac shakur song ) `` trapped '' is a single by american rapper 2pac . the song was featured on his debut studio album `` 2pacalypse now '' . the song also features vocals from shock g. the song is about police brutality . near the time that the music video was debuting , shakur was assaulted by the oakland police department when he swore at them for degrading his name and extending the issuing of a ticket sustained during a jaywalking incident .
