Veronica Lazăr     veronica lazăr ( 16 october 1938 - 8 june 2014 ) was a romanian-italian actress . she was known for her roles in `` last tango in paris '' ( 1973 ) , `` la luna '' ( 1979 ) , `` the sheltering sky '' ( 1990 ) , and `` besieged '' ( 1998 ) . she married italian actor adolfo celi in 1966. they had two children , director leonardo celi and actress alessandra celi . her last role was in `` me and you '' ( 2012 ) .
