Jimmy Murakami     teruaki `` jimmy '' murakami , ( 村上輝明 '' murakami teruaki '' , june 5 , 1933 – february 16 , 2014 ) was a japanese american animator and director . he is best-known for the animated adaptations of the raymond briggs books `` when the wind blows '' and `` the snowman '' . he formed murakami-wolf films with fred wolf . he died in 2014 , aged 80 .
