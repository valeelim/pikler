Carl Crawford     carl demonte crawford ( born august 5 , 1981 ) is an american professional baseball player . his nickname is `` the perfect storm '' . crawford has played for the tampa bay devil rays , tampa bay rays , boston red sox , and los angeles dodgers . crawford plays the position of outfielder . he bats and throws left-handed . crawford started his major league career in 2002. crawford has hit more triples than any other active baseball player .
