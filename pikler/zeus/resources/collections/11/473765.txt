1976 Republican National Convention     the 1976 republican national convention was a united states political convention of the republican party that met from august 16 to august 19 , 1976 to select the party 's nominee for president . it was held in kemper arena in kansas city , missouri , the convention nominated president gerald r. ford for a full term , but only after narrowly defeating a strong challenge from former california governor ronald w. reagan . the convention also nominated senator robert j. dole of kansas for vice president , instead of vice president nelson rockefeller .
