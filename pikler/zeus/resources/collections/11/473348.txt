Inside Out (2015 movie)    inside out ( 2015 movie ) inside out is a 2015 american computer animated dramedy adventure movie . it was produced by pixar animation studios for walt disney pictures , being the 15th movie in the pixar series . the movie is directed by one of pixar 's leading talents pete docter . the film was shown at the cannes film festival on may 18 , 2015 and was released on june 19 , 2015. riley anderson is an 11-year-old girl . she moves from minnesota to san francisco with her family . this affects a number of emotions in her head . the five emotion characters are joy , anger , disgust , fear and sadness . these emotions all help with her thoughts . they help each other with riley 's mind . each of the characters has power over an island of riley 's life , which represent different parts of her personality . after a series of events that change different things , she learns to accept her new life .
