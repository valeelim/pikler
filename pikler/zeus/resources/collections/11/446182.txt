Yvette Chauviré     yvette chauviré ( 22 april 1917 – 19 october 2016 ) was a french prima ballerina and actress . she was born in paris . her dancing career was from 1937 to 1972. she was the étoile of the paris opera ballet , and later its director . she was the holder of the légion d'honneur . she died at her home in paris on 19 october 2016 , aged 99 .
