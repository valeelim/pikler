Phạm Huỳnh Tam Lang     phạm huỳnh tam lang ( 14 february 1942 – 2 june 2014 ) was a vietnamese football player and coach . he played for the teams cho lon stars , sai gon port and the south vietnam national team in a twenty year career . he would later manage sai gon port . lang was born in gò công , french indochina during world war ii . lang died from a stroke on 2 june 2014 in ho chi minh city , vietnam , aged 75 .
