Juan Formell     juan clímaco formell fortuna ( 2 august 1942 – 1 may 2014 ) was a cuban musician , bassist , composer , and arranger . he was best known as the director of los van van orchestra . he began a career in the music industry in 1957. he was a grammy award in the latin/best salsa performance category for his work on the album `` van van is here ( llegó van van ) '' in 1999. he was born in havana . formell died on 1 may 2014 in havana , aged 71. other websites . - juan formell at discogs
