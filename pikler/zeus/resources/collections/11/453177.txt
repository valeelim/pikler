Hana Orgoníková     hana orgoníková ( 8 december 1946 – 5 june 2014 ) was a czech politician . she was an mp for the czech social democratic party ( csdp ) from 1989 until her death . orgoníková was born in přibyslav , czechoslovakia . she was divorced and had two children , vladimír and lucie . orgoníková died after a long illness on 5 june 2014 in hradec králové , czech republic , aged 67. other websites . - hana orgoníková at parlament české republiky , poslanecká sněmovna
