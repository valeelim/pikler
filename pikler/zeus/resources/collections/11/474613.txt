World Gone Crazy     world gone crazy is the 13th studio album by the american rock band the doobie brothers . the album was released on september 28 , 2010 . `` world gone crazy '' debuted at number 39 on the billboard top 200 albums chart . it was the group 's first studio album of new material in 30 years . they worked with the producer of their first 10 albums , ted templeman .
