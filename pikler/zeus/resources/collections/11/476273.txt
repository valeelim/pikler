Pete Ricketts     john peter `` pete '' ricketts ( born august 19 , 1964 ) is the 40th and current governor of nebraska and former chief operating officer of ameritrade . he was the republican nominee for the 2006 u.s. senate race in nebraska , which he lost to then incumbent ben nelson . he ran for governor of nebraska in the 2014 election , this time defeating the democratic nominee , chuck hassebrook . he was inaugurated as governor in january 2015 .
