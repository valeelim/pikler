Igor Mitoraj     igor mitoraj ( march 26 , 1944 – october 6 , 2014 ) was a polish artist . he was born in oederan , germany . mitoraj studied painting at the kraków academy of art under tadeusz kantor . after graduating , he had several exhibitions . he held his first solo exhibition in 1967 at the krzysztofory gallery in poland . in 1968 , he moved to paris to continue his studies at the national school of art . mitoraj worked with terracotta and bronze . in 1979 , he began to use marble for most of his works . in 1983 , he set up a studio in pietrasanta . in 2006 , he created the new bronze doors and a statue of john the baptist for the basilica of santa maria degli angeli in rome . mitoraj died in paris , france , aged 70 .
