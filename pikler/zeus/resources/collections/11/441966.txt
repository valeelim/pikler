Defenders of the Faith     defenders of the faith is the ninth studio album by british heavy metal band judas priest . `` defenders of the faith '' was released on january 4 , 1984. the album featured the singles `` freewheel burning '' , `` some heads are gon na roll '' and `` love bites '' . the song `` eat me alive '' was listed # 3 on the parents music resource center 's `` filthy fifteen '' which is a list of 15 songs the organization finds most objectionable . the co-founder tipper gore said that the song was about oral sex at gunpoint . in response to the allegations , the band recorded the song `` parental guidance '' on their next album `` turbo '' .
