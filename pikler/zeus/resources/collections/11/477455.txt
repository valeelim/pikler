Lee J. Cobb     lee j. cobb ( december 8 , 1911february 11 , 1976 ) was an american actor . he was best known for his roles in `` 12 angry men '' , `` on the waterfront '' , and `` the exorcist '' . cobb was born in the bronx , new york . cobb died in woodland hills , california from a heart attack , aged 64 .
