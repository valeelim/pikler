Myles Munroe     myles munroe , ( 20 april 1954 - 9 november 2014 ) was a bahamian evangelical christian evangelist and ordained pentecostal minister . he founded and led the bahamas faith ministries international ( bfmi ) and myles munroe international ( mmi ) . munroe also wrote many books about his faith . munroe died with his wife , daughter , and 6 others in a private plane crash on 9 november 2014 , aged 60 .
