Southwestern Ontario     southwestern ontario is a subregion of southern ontario in the canadian province of ontario . it covers from the bruce peninsula in the north to lake erie in the south . from east to west , it goes from guelph to windsor . london is the largest city in the region . in 2011 the population of the region was 2,504,878. agriculture and auto manufacturing are a large parts of the region 's economy . much of canada 's trade with the eastern and midwestern united states passes through southwestern ontario .
