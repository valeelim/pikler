Tonic (music)    tonic ( music ) in music , the tonic note is the first note in a diatonic scale . it is the tonal centre , or the tone on which the music sounds finished . the triad , or chord , based on the tonic note , is the tonic chord . all other notes in a piece of music use the tonic note as a reference point . musical scales are named after the tonic note . for example , the tonic of a scale of c , is the note c. the tonic is always numbered as one , or in traditional music theory as i ( using roman numerals ) .
