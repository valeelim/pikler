Carl Gustaf Ekman     carl gustaf ekman , born 6 october 1872 , dead 15 june 1945 , was a swedish politician . he was a member of the swedish parliament 1911-1932 , in both the lower and the upper houses . between 1924-1932 , he led the freeminded people 's party . he served as the prime minister of sweden between 1926-1928 and again between 1930-1932 .
