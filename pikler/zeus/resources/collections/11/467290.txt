Reyhaneh Jabbari     reyhaneh jabari ( persian : ریحانه جباری c. 1988 - october 25 , 2014 ) was a iranian woman convicted of murdering a former intelligence officer , morteza abdolali sarbandi , in iran . she said she killed sarbandi because he was sexually assaulting her . she was in prison from 2007 until her execution by hanging in october 2014 for killing sarbandi . she published her story about what happened to her in prison . her lawyer published her story in his blog . jabbari was executed by hanging on 25 october 2014 at dawn at the gohardasht prison , north of karaj , aged 26 .
