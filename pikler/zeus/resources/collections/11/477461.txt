Where the Red Fern Grows     where the red fern grows is a 1961 novel by wilson rawls . it is about the friendship between a boy and two redbone coonhound hunting dogs . billy coleman saves a redbone coonhound from a dog fight . he helps it recover from its wounds . he sets it free . he remembers his past . our story starts when he remembers how he had owned two redbone coonhounds when he was a boy . dating back to his past , billy had always wanted the dogs since he was 10. his parents however tell him they do n't have enough money for them . he decides to earn the money himself . for two years , he works many different jobs . he saves $ 50 . his grandfather helps him order the pair of redbone coonhounds . the mail does not deliver packages , so billy goes to get them . he walks to tahlequah , oklahoma . he buys gifts for his family there . on the way back home , he sleeps in a cave on sparrow hawk mountain . he hears a sound like a woman screaming . he realizes it is from a mountain lion . the puppies answer the roar with their howls . billy builds a fire for safety . the next morning , he starts going back home . on the way , he sees a sycamore tree carved with the names dan and ann in a heart . he decides to name the puppies old dan and little ann .
