Punch-Out!! (series)    punch-out ! ! ( series ) punch-out ! ! is a series of boxing games created by nintendo . there are seven games in the `` punch-out '' series . the player controls little mac as he fights his way up the ranks in the world video boxing association . he is helped by his trainer , doc luis . doc luis later got his own wiiware game . little mac is a playable fighter in both super smash bros. for nintendo 3ds and super smash bros. for wii u .
