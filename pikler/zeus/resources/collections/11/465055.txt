Dolphin Song     dophin song is a children 's novel by lauren st. john . it is the second in the `` legend of the animal healer '' series . the other books in the series are `` the white giraffe '' , `` the last leopard '' , and `` the elephant 's tale '' . `` dophin song '' was first published on august 1 , 2007. it was published nine times from 2008 to 2010. the story is about a girl named martine . she has the ability to heal animals . she has many adventures because of her that . in this book , she and several of her schoolmates get stuck on island out in the middle of nowhere . they have to learn to get along and survive alone .
