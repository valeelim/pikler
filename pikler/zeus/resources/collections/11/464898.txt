Narratology     narratology is the study of structure in narratives . the theory of narrative or narratology was developed in the 1960s . narratology is based on the idea of a common literary language . narratives are found and told through oral and written language . narratology has helped to make it easier to understand the how and why of narrative . other websites . - http : //wikis.sub.uni-hamburg.de/lhn/index.php/main_page - the living handbook of narratology - http : //www.units.miamioh.edu/technologyandhumanities/narratology.htm - notes on narratology - http : //www.nou-la.org/ling/1975a-theonarreme.pdf - thborie des narrgmes et algorithmes narratifs
