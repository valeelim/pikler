Khin Yu May     khin yu may ( , ; 23 march 1937 - 23 january 2014 ) was a burmese actress . she was a two-time myanmar academy award winning actress and singer . she was known for her roles in `` a-thet '' , `` i bawa we '' , and in `` equal love '' . may died on 23 january 2014 at the age of 77 in yangon , burma .
