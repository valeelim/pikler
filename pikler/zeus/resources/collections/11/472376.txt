Somethin' Stupid    somethin ' stupid `` somethin ' stupid '' is a ballad written by c. carson parks . it was recorded in 1966 by parks and his wife gaile foote . the song is best known for being recorded by frank sinatra and his daughter nancy . the sinatras ' version spent four weeks at the # 1 spot on the `` billboard '' hot 100. the song was also recorded by tammy wynette , the lennon sisters , marvin gaye , robbie williams and nicole kidman and many others .
