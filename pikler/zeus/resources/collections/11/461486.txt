Marco Fusi     marco fusi ( 1972 ) is an italian clarinetist and composer . he is the founder of the group `` yesh gvul '' . he is known for his collaborations with some of the leading names in italian cultural music . in 2012 he was invited to perform at the european parliament in brussels . he has been an influence on the music of southeastern europe , eastern europe , klezmer , jazz and classical music . discography . - moon waltz ( ethnoworld ) - addio a lugano ( music center ) - sulla memoria ( with moni ovadia ) ( cni ) - la strada della luna ( music center ) works for the theater . - il favoliere , by antonio lubrano e marco fusi . - comprami ! by lidia ravera and marco fusi . - l'eclisse della democrazia di vittorio agnoletto e marco fusi . - refusnik tango , with the participation of marco fusi ensemble , ottavia piccolo , renato sarti , moni ovadia , ricky gianco , lidia ravera . awards and acknowledgments . - international prize artelario - villa vigoni . other websites . - marco fusi on all music
