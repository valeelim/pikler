Good Times     good times is an american sitcom that originally aired from february 8 , 1974 , until august 1 , 1979 , on cbs . it was created by eric monte and mike evans , and developed by norman lear , the series ' primary executive producer . `` good times '' is a spin-off of `` maude '' , which is itself a spin-off of `` all in the family '' . it is set in chicago , illinois .
