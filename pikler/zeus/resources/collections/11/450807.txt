Rick Dees     rigdon osmond `` rick '' dees iii ( born march 14 , 1950 ) is an american radio personality , stand-up comedian , actor , voice artist , and comedian . dees is a people 's choice award winner , grammy-nominated performing artist , and is in the broadcast hall of fame . he wrote two songs that appear in the film `` saturday night fever '' and performed the title song for the film `` meatballs '' . he co-founded the e. w. scripps television network and fine living network . he has hosted `` rick dees in the morning '' at 102.7 kiis fm and hot 92.3 in los angeles . today he continues his own daily radio show , `` daily dees '' .
