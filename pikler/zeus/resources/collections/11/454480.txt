Isabelle Collin Dufresne     isabelle collin dufresne ( stage name , ultra violet ; 6 september 1935 – 14 june 2014 ) was a french-american artist , author , and former colleague and superstar of andy warhol . earlier in her career , she worked for and studied with surrealist artist salvador dalí . she lived and worked in new york city , and also had a studio in nice , france . dufresne died on 14 june 2014 in new york city from cancer , aged 78. other websites . - official website
