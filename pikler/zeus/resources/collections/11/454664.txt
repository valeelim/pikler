Reached (novel)    reached ( novel ) reached is the last book in a dystopian trilogy by ally condie . it was published in 2012. it takes place a few weeks after the first two books , `` matched '' and `` crossed '' . `` reached '' finishes the story of cassia reyes in a five-part triple narrative split between cassia , her boyfriend ky markham , and her best friend/match xander carrow .
