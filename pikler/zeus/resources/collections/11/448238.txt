California Love     `` california love '' is a hip hop song by american rapper 2pac . the song also features vocals from dr. dre and roger troutman . the song was shakur 's first single as an artist for death row records and was also his comeback single after he was released from prison in 1995. a popular remixed version of the song was featured on the uk version of `` all eyez on me '' . `` california love '' was nominated for a posthumous grammy award as a `` best rap solo performance '' and `` best rap performance by a duo or group '' ( with dre and troutman ) in 1997. the song was ranked # 355 of the rolling stone 's 500 greatest songs of all time and was ranked # 51 on vh1 's countdown of the 100 greatest songs of the 90s .
