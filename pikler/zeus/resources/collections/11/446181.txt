Aino-Maija Tikkanen     aino-maija tikkanen ( 2 november 1927 – 3 march 2014 ) was a finnish actress . she appeared in 15 movies between 1954 and 2010. she was born in turku . tikkanen died from natural causes on 3 march 2014 in helsinki . she was 86 years old . selected movies . - `` the harvest month '' ( 1956 ) - `` äideistä parhain '' ( 2005 )
