U. R. Ananthamurthy     udupi rajagopalacharya ananthamurthy ( ; 21 december 1932 - 22 august 2014 ) was am indian writer and critic . he wrote in the kannada language . he was thought to be as one of the pioneers of the navya movement . in 1998 , he received the padma bhushan award from the government of india . he was the vice-chancellor of mahatma gandhi university in kerala during the late 1980s . he was one of the finalists of man booker international prize for the year 2013. ananthamurthy died of cardiac arrest caused by renal failure on 22 august 2014 in bangalore , karnataka , india , aged 81 .
