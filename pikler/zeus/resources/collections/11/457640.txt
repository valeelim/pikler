Álex Angulo    álex angulo alejandro `` álex '' angulo león ( 12 april 1953 - 20 july 2014 ) was a spanish actor . he was born in erandio . he performed in over sixty movies during his career . he was an actor for more than 30 years . he was known for his role as doctor ferreiro in `` pan 's labyrinth '' . he died in fuenmayor at the age of 61 when the vehicle in which he was travelling veered from the road .
