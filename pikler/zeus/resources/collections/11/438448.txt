Keuruu     keuruu is a town and municipality in central finland . it is 60 km to the west of jyväskylä . there were more than 10,300 people living there in january 2014. more than 10 % of the area is water . keuruu is next to the municipalities of jämsä , multia , mänttä-vilppula , petäjävesi , virrat and ähtäri . other websites . - keuruu city page
