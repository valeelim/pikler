Miroslav Štěpán    miroslav štěpán miroslav stepan ( 5 august 1945 – 23 march 2014 ) was a former czechoslovakian politician . he was a member of the communist party of czechoslovakia . he was the secretary of the municipal party committee in prague and member of the czech national council and house of people federal assembly . he was born in louney . štěpán died on 23 march 2014 from cancer in prague , aged 68 .
