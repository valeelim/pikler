Paola Epifani     paola epifani ( pseudonym rabarama ) is an italian painter and sculptor . she was born on august 22 , 1969 in rome . she attended the art school of treviso . she graduated from the academy of fine arts in venice in 1991. since 1990 , she has lived and worked in padua . she participated in the 54 `` biennale of art '' in venice in 2011. works in museums . - copelouzos family art museum of athens ( greece ) with `` trans-lettera '' . - museo d'arte of avellino ( italy ) with `` palpit-azione '' ( 2010 ) . - contemporary art museum of boca raton - miami ( usa ) with `` labirintite '' . - art museum of biennale of beijing ( china ) with `` bozzolo '' . bibliography . - ( it ) ( en ) beatrice luca , `` rabarama . ortissa percorsi dorta 2010 '' , silvana editoriale , cinisello balsamo ( mi ) 2010 , pp . 119 . - ( it ) ( en ) beatrice luca - bolge george , `` rabarama . firenze antico informe 2011 '' , vecchiato art galleries , padova 2011 , pp . 172 .
