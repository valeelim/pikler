Raúl Alfonsín     raúl ricardo alfonsín ( 12 march 1927 – 31 march 2009 ) was an argentine lawyer , politician and statesman . he served as the president of argentina from december 10 , 1983 , to july 8 , 1989. alfonsín was the first democratically elected president of argentina following the military government known as the national reorganization process . he was awarded the prince of asturias award for international cooperation in 1985 , among numerous other such recognitions . alfonsín died from lung cancer in buenos aires , aged 82 .
