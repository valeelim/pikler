Elizabeth Peña     elizabeth peña ( september 23 , 1959 – october 14 , 2014 ) was an cuban-american actress and television director . she was known for her roles in `` the incredibles '' , `` rush hour '' , `` jacob 's ladder '' , and `` la bamba '' . she was born in elizabeth , new jersey . peña died at the age of 55 on october 14 , 2014 at cedars-sinai medical center in los angeles , california from cirrhosis of the liver .
