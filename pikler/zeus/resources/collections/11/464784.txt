TNA Unbreakable     unbreakable was a professional wrestling pay-per-view show made by total nonstop action wrestling ( tna ) . it was held on september 11 , 2005 at the tna impact ! zone in orlando , florida . it was the first and only unbreakable event that tna held and was also the ninth event in tna 's 2005 pay-per-view schedule . there were nine matches on the card and two pre-show matches . the main event was a three way match between then-champion christopher daniels , a.j . styles and samoa joe . styles ended up winning the match and became the new x division champion . another top card match saw raven defeat rhino in a raven 's rules match for the nwa world heavyweight championship . unbreakable gained positive reviews . the three way tna x division championship match earned a rare 5 star rating from dave meltzer which was the first that the promotion had received . corey david lacroix of the professional wrestling section of the canadian online explorer rated the show 8 out of 10 , and said `` tna 's unbreakable ppv was an astounding buffet of action , catering to the variety of tastes among professional wrestling fans .
