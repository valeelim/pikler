Puja (Hinduism)    puja ( hinduism ) pūjā ( devanagari : पूजा ) is a prayer service ritual done by hindus . the word puja comes from sanskrit , and means reverence , honor and worship . a puja honors one or more deities . it also spiritually celebrates an event . it is sometimes spelled phonetically as `` pooja '' or `` poojah '' . a puja is the most common form of worship in the hindu religion . it may honor or celebrate the presence of special guests or their memories after they die . puja rituals are also held by buddhists , jains and sikhs .
