The Hand That Rocks the Cradle     the hand that rocks the cradle is a 1992 american thriller movie . it is about a psychopathic nanny who wants to take over the family of another woman . rebecca de mornay plays mrs mott . julianne moore plays marlene craven . annabella sciorra plays claire . `` the hand that rocks the cradle '' opened in january 1992. this movie got positive reviews . its box office was also positive .
