Current affairs     current affairs is a type of broadcast journalism that analyzes and discusses recent news stories . this can include news that is still happening . most of the discussion is on why , where and how a story has happened . this is different from normal news programmes that report news stories as quickly as they can . they usually do not have as much analysis as current affairs programmes . it is also different from the magazine show format , where events are discussed immediately . the current affairs shows are becoming less popular . the audience for these shows is usually over age 50 .
