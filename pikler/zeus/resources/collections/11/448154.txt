Hipparion     hipparion ( greek , `` pony '' ) is an extinct genus of horse that lived in north america , asia , europe , and africa . this was during the miocene to pleistocene epochs ~23 million years to 780,000 years ago . they lived for a total of 22 million years . `` hipparion '' 's feet had three vestigial outer toes ( in addition to its hoof ) .
