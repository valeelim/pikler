Epstein     sir michael anthony epstein cbe , frs fmedsci ( born 18 may 1921 ) is a british virologist . he is one of the discoverers of the epstein-barr virus . epstein was professor of pathology , 1968-85 ( now professor emeritus ) , and head of department , 1968-82 at the university of bristol . in 1979 he was elected a fellow of the royal society of london and was its vice-president from 1986 to 1991. other websites . - epstein-barr virus , sir anthony epstein frs
