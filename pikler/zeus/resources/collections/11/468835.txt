Charlotte Rampling     charlotte rampling ( born 5 february 1946 ) is an english actress . she has appeared in english language , french and italian cinema . she was made an officer of the order of the british empire ( obe ) in 2001 and received france 's legion of honour in 2002. rampling 's film career began in 1965. her films include `` georgy girl '' ( 1966 ) , visconti 's `` the damned '' ( 1969 ) , `` the night porter '' ( 1974 ) , `` farewell my lovely '' ( 1975 ) , woody allen 's `` stardust memories '' ( 1980 ) , sidney lumet 's `` the verdict '' ( 1982 ) , `` angel heart '' ( 1987 ) , `` the duchess '' ( 2008 ) and fred schepisi 's `` the eye of the storm '' ( 2011 ) . she has been nominated four times for a cesar award , for `` on ne meurt que 2 fois '' ( 1985 ) , `` sous le sable '' ( 2000 ) , `` swimming pool '' ( 2003 ) and `` lemming '' ( 2005 ) and received an honorary cesar in 2001. on television , she starred in the 2012 bbc miniseries `` restless '' , for which she received an emmy award nomination . bisset was born in sturmer , essex , england . her birth name is tessa charlotte rampling . she is the daughter of isabelle anne and godfrey rampling , a olympic gold medalist . she had one sister , sarah , who committed suicide in 1966 at the age of 23 .
