Seal of Rhode Island     the seal of the state of rhode island is the state seal of the u.s. state of rhode island . it has a dark blue background . there is a maritime anchor under the word `` hope '' . the outer circle says `` seal of the state of rhode island and providence plantations '' . the outer circle also has `` 1636 '' at the bottom . the seal was first used before rhode island was really a state .
