Pran Kumar Sharma     pran kumar sharma ( august 15 , 1938 - august 5 , 2014 ) , better known as pran , was an indian cartoonist . he was best known as the creator of `` chacha chaudhary '' ( 1971 ) . he also created other characters like shrimatiji , pinki , billoo , raman , and channie chachi . sharma died on august 5 , 2014 at the age of 75 in gurgaon , india from colon cancer . other websites . - official website - pran profile at `` indian institute of cartoonists ''
