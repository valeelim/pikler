Toivakka     toivakka is a municipality in central finland . it had almost 2,050 people living there in january 2014. the municipalities next to it are hankasalmi , joutsa , jyväskylä , kangasniemi , laukaa and muurame . haukanmaa , huikko , humalamäki , kankainen , nisula , paloskylä , ruuhimäki , taka-toivakka , vihijärvi and viisarimäki . other websites . - the official page
