Ulrick Chérubin     ulrick chérubin ( december 24 , 1943 — september 25 , 2014 ) was a canadian politician , who served as mayor of amos , quebec from 2002 until his death in 2014. he was one of the first black canadians to be elected a mayor in quebec . chérubin was born in jacmel , haiti . chérubin died in amos , quebec , canada , aged 70. other websites . - official mayoral webpage `` ( in french ) ''
