Parakeet     parakeet is a name for any small to medium sized species of parrot , often with long tail feathers . basically , it is a common term for a small parrot . another term used in america for parakeets is conure . parakeets breed better in groups , but are usually fine breeding in pairs . having other parakeets encourages a pair to breed , which is why breeding in groups is more successful . however , many breeders choose to breed in pairs because that way they know which parents produced any given birds . if the parakeet does lay eggs , it produces about six to eight .
