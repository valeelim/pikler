Division of Martin     the division of martin was a australian electoral division for the australian house of representatives in the state of new south wales . the division was created in 1922. it was abolished in 1955. it was named after sir james martin , former premier of new south wales . it was located in inner western sydney . it was also located in concord and mortlake . by the time it was abolished , the division of martin had spread to abbotsford , balmain and drummoyne .
