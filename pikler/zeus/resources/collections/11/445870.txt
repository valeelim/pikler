Robert Forster     robert wallace forster , jr. ( born july 13 , 1941 ) is an american actor . he is best known for his role as max cherry in quentin tarantino 's `` jackie brown '' ( 1997 ) , which got him an academy award nomination for best supporting actor . he is also known for his roles as john cassellis in haskell wexler 's `` medium cool '' ( 1969 ) . forst was born in rochester , new york .
