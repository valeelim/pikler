Saado Ali Warsame     saado ali warsame ( , ) ( d. 23 july 2014 ) was a somali singer-songwriter and politician . she served as a member of the federal parliament of somalia from august 2012 until her death in july 2014. on 23 july 2014 , warsame and her driver were gunned down by unknown assailants in mogadishu as she was being driven to a hotel . al-shabaab claimed responsibility for the attack .
