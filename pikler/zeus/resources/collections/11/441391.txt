The Path to 9/11     the path to 9/11 is a melodramatic , event-based television movie . it is about the events between the 1993 world trade center bombing and the few minutes just after the september 11 attacks . the movie was criticized for saying untrue things about events and some people and for not showing all sides of things . the cast includes patricia heaton , harvey keitel and donnie wahlberg . keitel was warned that some things in the movie were not true . when the tv network was trying to fix the issue , however , it was too late . there were arguments on what was true or untrue . this movie aired in two parts in september 2006 on the abc network .
