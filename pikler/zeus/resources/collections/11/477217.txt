Mordechai Shmuel Ashkenazi     mordechai shmuel ashkenazi ( ‎ ; 1943 – 14 january 2015 ) was an israeli orthodox rabbi and writer . he was a member of the chabad hasidic movement . he was the chief rabbi of the chabad community of kfar chabad , israel from 1983 until his death . he was also an authority on halakha ( jewish law ) and hasidic philosophy . ashkenazi was educated in tel aviv and brooklyn , new york in the united states . he had nine sons , some of whom also became rabbis . ashkenazi died from a heart attack on 14 january 2015 , aged 71 .
