The Polar Express (movie)    the polar express ( movie ) the polar express is a 2004 american computer animated musical fantasy movie directed by robert zemeckis . it was produced by tom hanks . it is based on the book , `` the polar express '' . it stars tom hanks , daryl sabara , eddie deezen , jimmy bennett , steven tyler , nona gaye , and michael jeter . it was released on november 10 , 2004 .
