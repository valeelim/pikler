Jennifer Esposito     jennifer esposito ( born april 11 , 1973 ) is an american actress , dancer and model . she was known for her appearances in the movies `` i still know what you did last summer '' , `` summer of sam '' , and `` crash '' , and in the television series `` spin city '' , `` the looney tunes show '' , `` samantha who ? `` , and `` blue bloods '' .
