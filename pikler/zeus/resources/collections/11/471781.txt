Stellinha Egg     stella maria egg , baptismal name of stellinha egg ( july 18 , 1914 - june 17 , 1991 ) , was a brazilian singer and composer . she was married to maestro lindolfo gaya and musical partner of luiz gonzaga , dorival caymmi , silvio caldas , among others . stella maria egg was born in curitiba on july 18 , 1914. after her husband died in the 80s , she returned to curitiba , leaving behind their concerts . she died in curitiba on june 17 , 1991 , when she was 77 years old .
