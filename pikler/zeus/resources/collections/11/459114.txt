Peter George Peterson     peter george `` pete '' peterson ( born june 5 , 1926 ) is an american businessman , investment banker , fiscal conservative , philanthropist , and author . he served as united states secretary of commerce from february 29 , 1972 to february 1 , 1973. he is also known as founder and principal funder of the peter g. peterson foundation . peterson has been named the most influential billionaire in u.s. politics . other websites . - biography from blackstone group - the concord coalition biography - brandt 21 forum biography
