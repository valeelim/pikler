Basilica of Superga     the royal basilica of superga is a large church on the outskirts of the italian city of turin . the site was originally bare until king victor amadeus ii of sardinia decided to create a cathedral there . he wanted a burial place for members of his dynasty , the house of savoy . construction lasted from 1717 to 1731. the building was designed by the italian architect `` filippo juvarra '' . the building is often compared to the larger and more famous st. peter 's basilica . most of the kings of sardinia are buried there . further reading . - padre benedetto marengo , `` la basilica di superga . cenni storici del pi√π grande monumento juvarriano '' , tipografia scarafaglio , torino , 1997 - reina gabriele , guadalupi gianni , `` superga segreta . il mausoleo dei savoia '' , omega , 2008 , isbn 88-7241-528-4
