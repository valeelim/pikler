Terra (mythology)    terra ( mythology ) terra is the roman form of gaia ( greek ) . she is the goddess of the earth . her husband is caelus , an ancient god of the sky ( heavens ) . they are the parents of the titans & giants . one day , thousands of years ago , terra convinced her son , saturn , to take his father 's scythe and chop his father up with it . saturn did as his mother told him . he scattered caelus 's pieces across the cosmos . when one piece hit the water , it gave birth to venus , the goddess of love . this , effectively , makes venus the oldest of all the gods & goddess ' .
