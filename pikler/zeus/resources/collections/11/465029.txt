Fyodor Cherenkov     fyodor fyodorovich cherenkov ( ) ( 25 july 1959 — 4 october 2014 ) was a soviet and russian football midfielder . he played for spartak moscow ( 1977–90 and 1991–94 ) and red star football club ( 1990–91 ) . cherenkov made 34 appearances for ussr national football team , scoring 12 goals . he was born in moscow , russia . cherenkov died in moscow , russia , aged 55. other websites . - fyodor cherenkov 's profile at spartak 's official website - profile and interview
