The Grand Budapest Hotel     the grand budapest hotel is a 2014 british-german-american comedy movie . it is about a caretaker who teams up with one of his employees to prove that he is innocent of murder . ralph fiennes plays the caretaker . tilda swinton plays céline . léa seydoux plays plays clotilde . harvey keitel plays ludwig . this movie has an r rating from the motion picture association of america . the reviews of the movie were very positive .
