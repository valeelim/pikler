María Antonia Iglesias     maría antonia iglesias gonzález ( 15 january 1945 – 29 july 2014 ) was a spanish writer and journalist . she wrote for many newspapers and magazines such as `` informaciones '' , `` triunfo '' , `` tiempo '' , `` interviú '' and `` el país '' . besides writing for magazines and newspapers , she worked on radio and television . iglesias was born in madrid . her father was pianist and musicologist antonio iglesias álvarez . she was a catholic but did not have the same views as the current roman catholic church . iglesias died on 29 july 2014 in vigo , province of pontevedra , aged 69 .
