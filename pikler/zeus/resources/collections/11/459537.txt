Pat O'Brien (personality)    pat o'brien ( personality ) patrick john `` pat '' o'brien ( born february 14 , 1948 ) is an american author and radio host . he was best known for his work as a sportscaster with cbs sports from 1981 to 1997 , as well as his work as the anchor and host of `` access hollywood '' from 1997 to 2004 , and `` the insider '' from 2004 to 2008 .
