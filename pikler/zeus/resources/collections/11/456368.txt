The Shock of the Lightning     `` the shock of the lightning '' is the first single off english rock band oasis ' seventh and final studio album `` dig out your soul '' . the song was released in 22 september 2008 as a download and on 29 september 2008 as a physical copy . lead guitarist noel gallagher said of the song : `` if 'the shock of the lightning ' sounds instant and compelling to you , it 's because it was written dead fast . and recorded dead fast . 'the shock of the lightning ' basically is the demo . and it has retained its energy . and there 's a lot to be said for that , i think . the first time you record something is always the best '' nme ranked the song # 96 on their list of `` 100 tracks of the decade '' . other websites . - `` the shock of the lightning '' on youtube
