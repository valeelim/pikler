Blanche Barton     blanche barton ( born sharon densley ; october 1 , 1961 in san diego , california ) is an american religious leader . she is the magistra templi rex of the church of satan . satanists address her as `` magistra barton '' . barton became the high priestess after anton lavey died on october 29 , 1997. she held the position of high priestess until april 30 , 2002. barton has written two books : `` the church of satan : a history of the world 's most notorious religion '' and `` the secret life of a satanist : the authorized biography of anton lavey '' . other websites . - church of satan biography
