Blake and the Aliens     blake and the aliens is a american tv show by marathon media and nickelodeon . the series will premiere in april 2015 in the united states and in autumn 2015 in france . the series is about blake meyers , an adventurous boy who is destined to one day become a space ranger who protects humanity from alien squirrels called `` squaliens '' . the alien squirrels are sent back in time to prevent blake from ever becoming the adult hero
