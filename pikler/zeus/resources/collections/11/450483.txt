Global Force Wrestling     global force wrestling llc ( gfw ) is a professional wrestling company founded on april 7 , 2014 by jeff jarrett . the television product for the promotion will be produced by 25/7 productions and dave broome , creator of nbc 's the biggest loser . global force wrestling has a working relationship with the mexican asistencia asesoría y administración ( aaa ) professional wrestling promotion . on june 21 , 2014 , gfw and new japan pro wrestling both announced that they had a working relationship with each other . other websites . - official gfw website
