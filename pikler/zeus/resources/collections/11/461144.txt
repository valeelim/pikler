Peter Bacon Hales     peter bacon hales ( november 13 , 1950 – august 26 , 2014 ) was an american historian , photographer , writer , and musician . his works were in american spaces and landscapes , the history of photography , and contemporary art . his wrote six books including `` silver cities : the photography of american urbanization , 1839–1915 '' , and `` atomic spaces : living on the manhattan project '' . he was a university of illinois at chicago ( uic ) faculty member for 32 years , including four years as chair of his department ( art history ) . he retired in 2012. hales was born in pasadena , california . he graduated from haverford college in 1972. he was married to maureen pskowski . hales died in a road accident on august 26 , 2014 near his post-retirement home in stone ridge , new york . he was 63. he was killed when his bicycle was hit by a motor vehicle .
