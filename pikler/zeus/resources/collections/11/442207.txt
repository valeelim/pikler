Aarón Hernán     aarón hernán ( born november 20 , 1930 ) is an mexican telenovela and movie actor . hernán has appeared in over 100 spanish telenovelas and over 67 movies . he is known for playing priests on telenovelas . he appeared in two american movies , `` apolinar '' and `` black wind '' . hernán was born on november 20 , 1930 in camargo , chihuahua .
