Super Sentai     super sentai is a japanese `` tokusatsu '' media franchise that has been on the air since 1975. the franchise has had subsequent versions over the years . the seasons are adapted for an american franchise called `` mighty morphin power rangers '' . the current sentai , doubutsu sentai zyuohger , debuted in february 2016. the seasons air on the tv-asahi tenlevision network . other websites . - the website for the current sentai , ressha sentai toqger - the website for the 2015 sentai , shuriken sentai ninninger
