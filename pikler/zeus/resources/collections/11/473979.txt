Dieter Grau     dieter grau ( april 24 , 1913 – december 17 , 2014 ) was a german-american rocket scientist and member of the `` von braun rocket group '' , at peenemünde ( 1939–1945 ) working on the v-2 rockets in world war ii . he was among the scientists who surrendered to the united states and traveled there , providing rocketry expertise via operation paperclip , which took them first to fort bliss , texas . grau died in huntsville , alabama , at the age of 101 on december 17 , 2014 .
