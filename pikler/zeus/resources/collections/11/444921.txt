Nathan Phelps     nathan `` nate '' phelps ( born november 22 , 1958 ) is an american-canadian author , lgbt rights activist , and public speaker on the topics of religion and child abuse . he is the son of westboro baptist church founder fred phelps . he is known for being excommunicated from the church and from his own father at age 17. this was because of his different and mostly opposite beliefs of his father . phelps was born in topeka , kansas . he lives in calgary , alberta .
