Visvaldis Graumanis     visvaldis graumanis ( october 10 , 1913 in liepāja - december 4 , 1944 in liberec ) was a latvian soldier in the waffen ss during world war ii . during his career , he was awarded the german cross in gold . he got the rank of ss-obersturmführer . awards . - deutsches kreuz in gold ( february 6 , 1945 )
