Bengt Hassis     bengt hassis , born 11 january 1957 , is a swedish former cross-country skier . he represented orsa if at club level . in 1985 and 1986 , he won vasaloppet . during his 1986 wictory , he broke the vasaloppet record , which lasted until 1998 , when it was broken by peter göransson . nowadays , bengt hassis is a cross-country skiing instructor in grönklitt . he 's the brother of swedish cross-country skier ola hassis . other websites . - bengt hassi 's wbsite
