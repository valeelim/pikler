State Statistics Committee (Azerbaijan)    state statistics committee ( azerbaijan ) the state statistics committee of azerbaijan republic ( ) is a governmental agency within the cabinet of azerbaijan in charge of collection , processing and publishing statistical data on the economy , demographics and other sectors in the republic of azerbaijan . the state statistics committee was established on february 18 , 1994 by president heydar aliyev . its headquarters is located in the azerbaijani capital city of baku . other pages . - statistics - azerbaijan other websites . - official website
