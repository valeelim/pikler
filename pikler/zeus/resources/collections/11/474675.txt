Oh Kyo-moon     oh kyo-moon ( born march 2 , 1972 ) is an archer from south korea . he won a team silver medal and an individual bronze medal at the 1996 summer olympics . oh also was a member of korea 's gold medal men 's archery team at the 2000 summer olympics . in the men 's individual competition he finished sixth . oh was the world record holder for a single fita with a score of 1379. that score was beaten by oh jin-hyek at 2009 world championship in ulsan with 1386 points . other websites . - profile at databaseolympmics.com
