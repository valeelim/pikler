Damian D'Oliveira     damian basil `` dolly '' d'oliveira ( 19 october 1960 – 29 june 2014 ) was a south african-born english cricketer . he played first-class cricket for worcestershire from 1982 to 1995. he was later the academy director of worcestershire county cricket club until his death . d'oliveira was born in cape town . his father , basil ( 1931–2011 ) , played for worcestershire and england . his uncle , ivan ( born 1941 ) , played for leicestershire . his son , brett ( born 1992 ) , plays for worcestershire . d'oliveira died from cancer on 29 june 2014 , aged 53. he had cancer for two-and-a-half-years . his wife and three children outlived him . other websites . - damian d'oliveira at cricinfo
