Selma Lagerlöf     selma ottilia lovisa lagerlöf ( ; born 20 november 1858 , dead 16 march 1940 , was a swedish writer . in 1909 , she was awarded the nobel prize in literature becoming the first woman to do so . she is mostly known for her children 's book `` nils holgerssons underbara resa genom sverige '' ( `` the wonderful adventures of nils '' ) . she also worked for women 's rights .
