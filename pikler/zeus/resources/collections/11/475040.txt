William Lyon Mackenzie King     william lyon mackenzie king , pc , om , cmg ( december 17 , 1874 – july 22 , 1950 ) , also commonly known as mackenzie king , was the dominant canadian political leader from the 1920s through the 1940s . he served as the tenth prime minister of canada from december 29 , 1921 to june 28 , 1926 ; from september 25 , 1926 to august 7 , 1930 ; and from october 23 , 1935 to november 15 , 1948. a liberal with 22 years in office , he was the longest-serving prime minister in canadian history . king died on july 22 , 1950 , at kingsmere from pneumonia , aged 75. he is buried in mount pleasant cemetery , toronto . other websites . - canadian newspapers and the second world war - mackenzie king declares war against nazi germany ( .rm file ) 1939 speech - cbc digital archives – mackenzie king : public life , private man - woodside national historic site page from parks canada website - `` the secret of heroism : a memoir of henry albert harper `` by william lyon mackenzie king at archive.org - william lyon mackenzie king estate visitor 's information
