The Shining (movie)    the shining ( movie ) the shining is a 1980 american-british horror movie . it is set during winter in a hotel in rural colorado . it was directed by stanley kubrick . it is based on stephen king 's book of the same name . it stars jack nicholson , shelley duvall , danny lloyd , and scatman crothers . it was released to very positive reviews on may 23 , 1980 .
