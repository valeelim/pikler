Kontiolahti     kontiolahti is a municipality in north karelia , finland . it had about 14,000 living there in january 2014. the municipalities next to it are joensuu , juuka , lieksa , liperi and polvijärvi . harivaara , herajärvi , jakokoski , mönni , onttola , paihola , puntarikoski , rantakylä , romppala and venejoki . other websites . - the official kontiolahti page
