Pedro María Iguaran     pedro maría iguaran arandia ( 2 july 1940 – 16 january 2015 ) commonly known as pello iguaran was a spanish football defender . iguaran played for deportivo alavés in the 1963-1964 season . iguaran served his new club for 7 years , being in most cases used as a reserve defender . he played a total of 85 games for real sociedad .
