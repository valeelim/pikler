Stanisław Mikulski     stanisław mikulski ( 1 may 1929 – 27 november 2014 ) was a polish theatre , television , movie actor . he was a host of the polish game show `` wheel of fortune '' ( `` koło fortuny '' ) . mikulski was known for starring in the leading role in the 1967-1968 television series `` stawka większa niż życie '' . mikulski died in warsaw , poland , aged 85 .
