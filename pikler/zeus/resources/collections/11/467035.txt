Big Dreams &amp; High Hopes    big dreams & amp ; high hopes big dreams & high hopes is the seventh studio album by jack ingram . the album was released on august 25 , 2009. it was his first new material in 2 years . it was produced by big machine records . it debuted at # 21 on billboard 's top country albums chart . allmusic reviewer thom jurek gave the album three-and-a-half stars out of five . he wrote `` '' big dreams & high hopes '' is the sound of contemporary country music and all of its tropes . '' he also said that most of the songs were well-written but not distinctive .
