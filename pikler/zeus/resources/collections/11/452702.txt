Division of Maranoa     the division of maranoa is an australian electoral division in queensland . it was set up in 1901 for the first federal election . it is named after the maranoa river , which runs through the division . it covers the south west of queensland , and includes the towns of charleville , cunnamulla , queensland , dalby , roma , kingaroy , stanthorpe , winton and warwick . election results . - 2004 election results - 2007 election results - 2010 election results - 2013 election results other websites . - division of maranoa ( qld ) â€” australian electoral commission - bruce scott 's website
