Albert Manent     albert manent i segimon ( 23 september 1930 – 14 april 2014 ) was a spanish writer and cultural activist . he was mostly involved in the field of catalan . manent was the son of the writer marià manent . he graduated the subject of law and in catalan philology . he was born in premia de dalt , spain . manent died in madrid from an illness , aged 83 .
