Andy Lehrer     andy z. lehrer ( 16 may 1930 – january or february 2014 ) was a romanian entomologist . from 1996 until his death , he worked as a research associate in the laboratory of zoology at the university of tel aviv in tel aviv , israel . for several years , he studied flesh flies and blow flies all over the world . lehrer was born born in iaşi , iaşi county . he died in early 2014 , aged 83 .
