33 BC     year 33 bc was either a common year starting on saturday , sunday or monday or a leap year starting on sunday of the julian calendar . at the time , it was known as the year of the consulship of octavian and tullus . events . - mark antony annexed the kingdom of media - the kinambroi surrendered to octavian - crown prince ao ascends the throne in china as emperor cheng of han deaths . - tiberius nero , father of tiberius ( b. c. 85 bc ) - emperor yuan of han ( b . 75 bc )
