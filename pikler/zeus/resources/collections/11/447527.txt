Veruca Salt     veruca salt is an american alternative rock band . louise post and nina gordon started the band with drummer jim shapiro and bassist steve lack in 1993. in 1994 their first album `` american thighs '' was released . gordon left the band in 1998. in 2013 she joined the band again . on june 22 2014 the band 's original members will perform together for the first time in 19 years .
