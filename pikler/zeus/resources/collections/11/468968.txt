Osaka Castle     toyotomi hideyoshi made people build osaka castle in 1583 ( 天正11年 ) . in 1620 , tokugawa hidetada started to rebuild osaka castle and it finished in 1629. osaka castle had been destroyed by fire twice . first , the summer battle of osaka ( 大阪夏の陣 ) in 1645. second , during world war ii . in 1948 , osaka castle 's restoration was started . now osaka castle tower is symbol of osaka town .
