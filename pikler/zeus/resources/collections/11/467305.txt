André Piters     andré piters ( 18 january 1931 – 23 october 2014 ) was a belgian footballer . he played as a striker . piters played club football for standard liège and olympic de charleroi . he earned a total of 23 caps for belgium between 1955 and 1961 , four of which came in fifa world cup qualifying matches . piters died on 23 october 2014 in brussels , belgium , aged 83 .
