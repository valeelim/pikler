Epping Forest (district)    epping forest ( district ) epping forest district is a district of essex , bordering greater london . much of the district is covered with the forest of the same name . the district was formed on 1 april 1974. much of the district has a rural appearance . in the south , at the border of greater london it is more suburban . the biggest town in the district , loughton , lies in this area . chipping ongar is a small market town a bit more to the north . the administration is in epping , which is roughly in the centre of the district . the rest of the district is dominated by villages . about 130,000 people live in the borough . the m25 and m11 go through it .
