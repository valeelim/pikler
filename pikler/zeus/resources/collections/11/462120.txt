Juniper Cobra 14     exercise juniper cobra 14 ( hebrew : ג'וניפר קוברה 14 ) or jc14 is a five day military exercise with israel and the united states . the exercise is there to give training in case of a ballistic missile attack from iran the exercise began in 2001 and is held once every two years . in 2012 the exercise was cancelled to ease issues with iran .
