Alexander Imich     alexander imich ( february 4 , 1903 – june 8 , 2014 ) ph.d , was a polish-born american chemist , parapsychologist , and supercentenarian . he was the president of the anomalous phenomena research center in new york city . he was born in częstochowa , poland ( then a part of russian empire ) in 1903. he was of jewish descent . at age 111 , imich was the world 's oldest living man since the death of arturo licata on april 24 , 2014. he was one of the few supercentenarians known for reasons other than longevity . he died on june 8 , 2014 peacefully at 9:03 am from natural causes at the age of 111 .
