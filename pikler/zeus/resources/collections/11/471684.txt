Monosodium glutamate     monosodium glutamate ( msg ) , also known as sodium glutamate ) is the sodium salt of glutamic acid . it is a chemical used to make foods taste better . this works because savory flavors are enhanced . that means the savory flavors are stronger . msg is found in most foods . it is in higher concentrations in high protein foods such as meat , fish and dairy products . the food and drug administration ( fda ) has classed msg as a food additive that is `` generally recognized as safe '' . however , the fda requires that it be listed on the label .
