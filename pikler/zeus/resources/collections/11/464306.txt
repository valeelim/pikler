Petr Skoumal     petr skoumal ( 7 march 1938 – 28 september 2014 ) was a czech musician and composer . he was best known for composing for many movies and television shows . he started his career in 1971 and his last credit was in 2013. he worked on animation shorts such as `` pat & mat '' and `` maxipes fík '' . skoumal was born in prague , czechoslovakia . he was the son of translator aloys skoumal . skoumal died after a long illness on 28 september 2014 in prague . he was 76 .
