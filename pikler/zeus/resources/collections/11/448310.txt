Hollywood Hillbillies     hollywood hillbillies is an american reality television series , based on `` coppercab '' . it is about michael kittrell ( better known as michael copper or his youtube username coppercab ) , his grandmother delores `` mema '' hughes , his aunt dee dee peters , and his uncle john cox moving from their home in grayson , georgia to hollywood , california . it aired on january 21 , 2014 .
