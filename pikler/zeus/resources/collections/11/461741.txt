Franca Valeri     franca valeri ( born 31 july 1920 ) is an italian actress . she is known for her roles in `` it happened in the park '' , `` the sign of venus '' , `` a hero of our times '' , `` ii vedovo '' , and in `` love in first class '' . valeri was born in milan , lombardy , italy . more reading . - emanuela martini , `` franca valeri : una signora molto snob '' , lindau , 2000 , isbn 887180290x
