Graham Joyce     dr. graham joyce ( 22 october 1954 - 9 september 2014 ) was an english writer of speculative fiction . he was the winner of many awards , including the o henry award , for both his novels and short stories . he was known for his book `` dreamside '' . he sold `` dreamside '' to pan books in 1991.+ joyce died on 9 september 2014. he had been diagnosed with lymphoma in 2013. other websites . - author 's official website
