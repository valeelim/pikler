Chandrayaan 1     chandrayaan 1 was india 's first spacecraft launched to explore the moon . it lifted off on 22 october 2008. it was under the control of the indian space research organisation ( isro ) . chandrayaan 1 carried nasa 's m1 moon mineralogy mapper . the space mission was designed to last for two years . in actuality the mission lasted for 312 days . it mapped about 95 % of the moon 's surface . on 29 august 2009 radio signals from chandrayaan-i were lost . the mission was considered ended at that point . the lunarprobe showed the presence of water on moon .
