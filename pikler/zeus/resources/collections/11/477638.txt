José Carrasco     josé carlos carrasco távara ( 1944 – 18 january 2015 ) was a peruvian politician . he represented piura from 2006 through 2011. carrasco was a member of the peruvian aprista party . he was born in the sullana province , peru . carrasco died in lima , peru on 18 january 2015 from lung cancer , aged 70. other websites . - official congressional site
