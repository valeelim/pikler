Leopoldo María Panero     leopoldo maría panero ( 16 june 1948 – 5 march 2014 ) was a spanish poet . he was often placed in the novísimos group . he had fifteen poetic releases between 1970 and 2004. he was born in madrid . panero died from heart failure on 5 march 2014 in madrid . he was 65 years old . other websites . - leopoldo maría panero biography
