M. V. Devan     madathil vasudevan , popularly known as m. v. devan ( january 15 , 1928 - april 29 ,2014 ) was an indian painter , sculptor , writer , art critic and orator . he was made the chairman of kerala lalithakala akadem , an art institute , from 1974 to 1977. he was born in panniyannur village in thalassery in northern kerala . during this time , he created an organization called `` perunthachan '' , which helped with the housing sector . throughout his life , devan tried to protect many paintings in india and parts of the world . devan died of an illness on 29 april 2014 at his home in aluva , kerala , aged 86 .
