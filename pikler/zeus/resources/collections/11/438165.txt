South African Military Health Service     the south african military health service ( samhs ) is the branch of the south african national defence force responsible for medical facilities and the training and deployment of all medical personnel within the military . this is unusual , because most national militaries have their medical structures integrated into their existing service branches . the sahms plays a large part to control hiv/aids in the sandf . the sahms was called the south african medical service ( sams ) before 1998 .
