Debenhams     debenhams is the united kingdom 's largest department store chain . it was formed in 1778. it is a company that has opened shops across the world , first opening in london , england . debenhams operates nearly 100 retail stores in the uk . it is one of the companies on the ftse 250 index . the 2014 christmas advert was filmed in the sutton , london shop in august 2014 .
