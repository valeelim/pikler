José Gómez Lucas     josé gómez lucas ( 9 january 1944 – 14 june 2014 ) was a spanish olympic cyclist . his career lasted from 1969 to 1975 and participated in amateur testing in highway olympic games mexico in 1968 , where he finished sixteenth . lucas was born in navalcarnero , madrid , spain . as a professional , his most known victory was the tour of andalusia in 1970 besides winning a stage and overall at the vuelta a mallorca in 1971 , the klasika spring in 1972 and a stage of the tour of aragon in 1972. he participated in the tour de france in 1971 and in 1973 , but dropped in both races . lucas died in madrid , spain from unknown causes , aged 70 .
