Magnetic Video     magnetic video corporation a home video/audio duplication service established in 1968 by andre blay and based in farmington hills , michigan , was the first company to release theatrical motion pictures to home video for consumers in 1976. in 1979 , fox purchased magnetic video from blay , reincorporating it as `` 20th century-fox video '' in 1982. it is currently known as `` 20th century fox home entertainment '' .
