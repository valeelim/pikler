Juan José Pizzuti     juan josé pizzuti ( born 9 may 1927 ) is a former argentine football striker and football manager . he enjoyed his most notable successes as player . he was the manager of the racing club de avellaneda . he was born in barracas , buenos aires , argentina . pizzuti played for argentina between 1951 and 1959 , he was part of the squad for the south american championship in 1959 where he scored three goals to help them to win the title .
