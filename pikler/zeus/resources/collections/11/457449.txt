Kalix BF     kalix bf is a bandy club in kalix in sweden , founded on 2 may 1990. the club has been playing in swedish bandy , top division elitserien , in the 2011–12 season and then again in the 2013–14 season . supporters of the club often refer to it as `` kb '' . the club was founded as `` kalix nyborg bk '' , by merging the bandy sections of the clubs nyborgs sk , who later has restarted a bandy section of its own , and ifk kalix . the name `` kalix bf '' was adopted on 20 june 2001. other websites . - official website
