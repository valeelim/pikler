Inge Vermeulen     inge joanna francisca vermeulen ( 6 january 1985 – 10 january 2015 ) was a brazilian-dutch field hockey player . she played as a goalkeeper for three teams and two international teams : brazil and the netherlands . vermeulen was born in americana , são paulo and moved to the netherlands at a young age . vermeulen committed suicide ( killed herself ) on 10 january 2015 in utrecht . she was 30 .
