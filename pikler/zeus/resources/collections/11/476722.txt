Raw Footage     raw footage is the eighth studio album by american rapper , ice cube . `` raw footage '' was released on august 19 , 2008 through lench mob records . the album featured the singles `` gangsta rap made me do it '' and `` do ya thang '' . the album debuted at # 1 on both the `` billboard '' top r & b/hip-hop albums and the top rap albums charts . it also debuted at # 5 on the `` billboard '' 200 .
