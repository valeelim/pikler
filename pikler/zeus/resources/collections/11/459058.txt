Pachuca    c.f . pachuca club de fútbol pachuca is a mexican professional football team from pachuca , hidalgo . they compete in the mexican premier division . founded by cornish miners in 1901 , it is amongst the oldest mexican football clubs in existence . they won five national championships , four concacaf champions ' cups , the 2007 superliga , and one copa sudamericana in 2006. it is also an original member of the mexican primera división . honours . national . professional era . - mexican primera división : 5 - primera división a : 2 - segunda división de méxico cup : 1 - segunda división b de méxico : 1 amateur era . - campeonato del distrito federal : 3 international . - concacaf champions ' cup / champions league : 4 - north american superliga : 1 - copa sudamericana : 1 other tournaments . - copa tower : 2 - copa pachuca : 3 - carlsberg cup : 1 - copa amistad : 1 - copa durango 450 : 1
