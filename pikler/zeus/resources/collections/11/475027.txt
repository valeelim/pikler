Edmond H. Fischer     edmond henri fischer ( born april 6 , 1920 ) is a swiss-born american biochemist . he and his partner edwin g. krebs were awarded the nobel prize in physiology or medicine in 1992 for their works on phosphorylation works as a switch to activate proteins and regulate various cellular processes . from 2007 until 2014 , he was the honorary president of the world cultural council .
