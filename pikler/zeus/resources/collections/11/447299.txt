Rodrigo García     rodrigo garcía ( born 24 august 1959 ) is a colombian-born television and movie director and screenwriter . he is the son of nobel prize-winning author gabriel garcía márquez . he directed movies and television series such as `` nine lives '' and `` albert nobbs '' , and episodes of several hbo series , `` six feet under '' , `` carnivàle '' , and `` big love '' . barcha was born in bogotá , colombia .
