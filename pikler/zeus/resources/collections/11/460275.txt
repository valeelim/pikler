James Abourezk     james george abourezk ( born february 24 , 1931 ) is an american politician . he is a former democratic united states representative and united states senator . he was the first greek orthodox christian of lebanese-antiochite descent to serve in the united states senate . he represented south dakota in the u.s. senate from 1973 until 1979. other websites . - american-arab anti discrimination committee official website - interview with counterpunch , april 16 , 2006 - memri tv video of hezbollah tv interview - memri transcript of hezbollah tv interview - `` booknotes '' interview with abourezk on `` advise & dissent '' , march 25 , 1990 .
