Bobby Heenan     raymond louis `` ray '' heenan ( born november 1 , 1944 ) is an american retired manager , professional wrestler and color commentator . heenan is best known for his time in american wrestling association ( awa ) , the world wrestling federation ( wwf ) and world championship wrestling ( wcw ) . he was known as bobby `` the brain '' heenan . heenan was known for having great skill in drawing heat towards both himself and the wrestlers that he had managed . he was also known for his time as a color commentator along with his co-commentator gorilla monsoon . heenan was inducted into the wwe hall of fame by blackjack lanza in 2004. during his speech , he paid tribute to his close friend and broadcast partner gorilla monsoon . he tearfully said `` i wish monsoon were here . '' heenan also inducted `` mr. wonderful '' paul orndorff in 2005 , nick bockwinkel in 2007 and the blackjacks ( blackjack mulligan and blackjack lanza ) in 2006. in january 2002 , he announced that he was battling throat cancer . since then , he has recovered largely but he lost a lot of weight going from 246 pounds ( 112 kg ) to 190 pounds ( 86 kg ) or even less . his voice also changed to a soft , high-pitched tone , different from his strong , rugged tone during his color commentator days . in december 2007 , he had reconstructive surgery on his jaw , when the first surgery was not successful . he was placed in a medically induced coma and was slowly brought out . in february 2009 , he was released from the hospital and was relearning how to speak clearly .
