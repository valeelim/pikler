Niki Nordenskjöld     annika nordenskjöld olsén ( 1 december 1975 – 3 september 2014 ) , better known by her stage name niki nordenskjöld , was a swedish actress and illustrator . she was best known for her role as lina in the television comedy series `` solsidan '' . nordenskjöld was born in gothenburg . she started her career in 1990 at the age of 15. nordenskjöld died from cancer on 3 september 2014 in stockholm , aged 38. other websites . - niki nordenskjöld at the swedish film database
