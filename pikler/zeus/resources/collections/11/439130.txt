Young Earth creationism     young earth creationism ( yec ) is the belief that the god of judaism or christianity created the universe , the earth and all life on earth over a short time between 5,700 and 10,000 years ago . those who believe young earth creationism are mainly christians and jews . they believe the genesis account of creation in the bible is completely true . young earth creationists believe that god created the earth in six 24-hour days . they disagree with other creationists because they believe the bible is literally true when it tells the age of the earth . many scientists agree together that young earth creationism is wrong and know about experiments and observations ( facts that scientists have seen , heard , recorded with their instruments ) in their theory . scientists believe that the universe is more than 13 billion years old while the earth existed for 4.5 billion years according to scientific theory . related pages . - creationism - old earth creationism
