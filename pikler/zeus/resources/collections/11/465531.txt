Tarragona Cathedral     the cathedral of tarragona is a roman catholic cathedral in tarragona , spain . the site was previously occupied by a roman temple of jupiter . later it was a visigothic cathedral , then a moorish mosque . it was declared a national monument in 1905. during the spanish civil war the cathedral was vandalized and in 1939 a reconciliation rite was celebrated . other websites . - panoramic view - diocesan museum
