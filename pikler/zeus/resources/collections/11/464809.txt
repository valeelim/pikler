Frieda Szwillus     frieda szwillus ( née hennig ; 30 march 1902 – 21 september 2014 ) was a german supercentenarian . she had been germany 's oldest living person from 22 april 2014 until her death 5 months later . she lived to be 112 years old and 175 days . szwillus died in raschau-markersbach , saxony , germany .
