H. B. Reese     harry burnett `` h . b . '' reese ( may 24 , 1879 – may 16 , 1956 ) was an american inventor . he was best known for creating reese 's peanut butter cups and founding the h. b. reese candy company . he was born in york county , pennsylvania . he was of welsh ancestry . he married blanche edna reese ( née hyson ) on august 1 , 1900. by 1928 , the couple had sixteen children . reese died from a heart attack on may 16 , 1956 in west palm beach , florida . he was 76 .
