Reality Bites     reality bites is a 1994 romantic comedy-drama movie set in houston . this is about romance and the fear of aids and hiv . janeane garofalo plays vickie miner . swoosie kurtz plays charlane mcgregor . winona ryder plays lelaina . this movie was released on february 18 , 1994. the movie got mixed review from critics . the box office was positive . lisa loeb performed `` stay '' on the soundtrack of the movie .
