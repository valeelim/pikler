Antoni Opolski     antoni opolski ( 11 june 1913 – 17 march 2014 ) was a polish physicist . his field was astronomy and astrophysics . after world war ii , he worked at the astronomical observatory of the university of wrocław . he retired in 1983. he was born in rozwadów ( now lviv oblast , ukraine ) . opolski died from natural causes on 17 march 2014 in wrocław , poland . he was 100 years old . his funeral was held on 22 march . other websites . - antoni opolski biography at the university of opole website
