Robert Clary     robert clary ( born robert max widerman ; march 1 , 1926 ) is a french-american actor , writer and lecturer . he is best known for his role as corporal lebeau ( `` frenchie '' ) in the television sitcom `` hogan 's heroes '' . he is the last surviving main cast member since the death of cynthia lynn in march 2014. clary was born in paris . he is jewish and survived the buchenwald concentration camp in weimar , germany during the holocaust of world war ii . other websites . - robert clary at tcm movie database
