Interstellar (movie)    interstellar ( movie ) interstellar is a 2014 american adventure drama science fiction movie directed by christopher nolan . it stars matthew mcconaughey , anne hathaway , jessica chastain , bill irwin , ellen burstyn , and michael caine , mackenzie foy , matt damon , casey affleck , and john lithgow . it was released on november 7 , 2014 .
