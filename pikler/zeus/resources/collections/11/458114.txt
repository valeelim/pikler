Gustav Adolf Church, Liverpool    gustav adolf church , liverpool the gustav adolf church ( ) or the scandinavian seamen 's church ( ) is located in park lane , liverpool , merseyside , england . it consists of a church , built between 1883-1884 , and an attached minister 's house , and provides a centre for the liverpool international nordic community . the combined church and minister 's house is designated by english heritage as a grade ii* listed building .
