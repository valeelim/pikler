Johan Olof Wallin     johan olof wallin , born 15 october 1779 in yttre skomsarby , sweden , dead 30 june 1839 in uppsala , sweden was a swedish lutheran clergyman . he was the church of sweden archbishop of uppsala between 1837-1839. he also wrote many christian songs/hymns . other websites . - `` svenskt biografiskt handlexikon '' ( 1906 ) , in swedish - nordiskt familjelexikon article `` wallin , johan olof '' . in swedish .
