C. T. Vivian     cordy tindell `` c. t. '' vivian ( born july 30 , 1924 ) is an american minister and author . he was a close friend and lieutenant of reverend martin luther king , jr. during the american civil rights movement . vivian continues to live in atlanta , georgia . he recently founded the c. t. vivian leadership institute , inc. rev . vivian is a member of alpha phi alpha fraternity . on august 8 , 2013 , president barack obama named rev . vivian as a recipient of the presidential medal of freedom . other websites . - c.t . vivian 's oral history video excerpts at the national visionary leadership project
