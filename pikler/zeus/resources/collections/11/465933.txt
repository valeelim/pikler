Owen Sound     owen sound is a city in southwestern ontario . it is the county seat of grey county . it is at the mouths of the pottawatomi river and the sydenham river on an inlet of georgian bay . according to the 2011 census , the 21,688 people lived in owen sound . owen sound was founded in 1841. it was named after british admiral edward owen . the canadian world war i flying ace billy bishop was born in owen sound . georgian college is in owen sound . the owen sound collegiate and vocational institute is one of the oldest schools in canada . there are five sports teams in owen sound : - owen sound attack – ontario hockey league - owen sound northstars – ola senior b lacrosse league - owen sound north stars jr. b - ontario junior b lacrosse league - owen sound united – mid western soccer league - owen sound colts – gridiron elite league
