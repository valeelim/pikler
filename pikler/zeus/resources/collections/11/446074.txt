Edvard Grimstad     edvard grimstad ( 29 march 1933 – 3 april 2014 ) was a norwegian politician . he was a member of the centre party . he served as an mp from 1989 until 1997. he was born in råde , østfold . grimstad died from natural causes on 3 april 2014 in oslo . he was 91 years old . other websites . - edvard grimstad biography
