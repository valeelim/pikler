The Fabulous Baker Boys     the fabulous baker boys is a 1989 romantic comedy and drama movie . the movie is about jazz music singers who perform for a living . these singers go against a female jazz singer . the female singer makes the brothers look at their relationship and music careers . michelle pfeiffer plays susie . jeff and beau bridges play the singers . the movie was released on october 13 , 1989. the reviews were very positive . the movie was nominated for four academy awards . michelle pfeiffer won the golden globe award for best actress .
