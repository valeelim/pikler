Egg donation     egg donation is when a woman gives some of her eggs ( ova ) . sometimes it is done to help that woman or another woman have a baby . sometimes it is for research . sometimes the eggs are frozen for later . the first child born from egg donation was in 1983 in australia . the first one in the united states was born february 3 , 1984. a woman who gives her ova is called an egg donor . egg donors are recruited , screened , and give consent before participating in the process .
