R. K. Laxman     rasipuram krishnaswamy laxman ( 24 october 1921 – 26 january 2015 ) was an indian cartoonist , writer , illustrator , and humorist . he was best known for his creation `` the common man '' . it was for his daily cartoon strip , `` you said it '' in `` the times of india '' . it started in 1951. laxman died from multiple organ failure caused by a urinary tract infection in pune , maharashtra , india , aged 93. other websites . - laxman's-eye view frontline magazine – 18–31 july 1998
