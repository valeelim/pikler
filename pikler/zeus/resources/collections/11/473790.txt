Jungle Fever     jungle fever is a 1991 american romance-drama movie set in new york city . this movie is about interracial romance and drug addiction . wesley snipes plays flipper , a married african american architect who has an affair with italian american angie , played by annabella sciorra . ruby dee plays flipper 's mother mrs purify . ossie davis plays flipper 's father the good reverend doctor . samuel l. jackson plays crack cocaine addict gator . halle berry plays gator 's girlfriend vivian . this movie got mostly positive reviews from critics .
