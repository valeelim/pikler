Kaiketsu Masateru     kaiketsu masateru ( japanese : 魁傑 將晃 , born teriyuki nishimori ; february 16 , 1948 – may 18 , 2014 ) was a japanese sumo wrestler . he also won two top division tournament championships . after his retirement in 1979 he became a coach . he was also chairman of the japan sumo association from 2010 to 2012. he died on may 18 , 2014 while practicing golf from heart disease in tokyo . he was 66 .
