Colin Mochrie     colin andrew mochrie ( ; born november 30 , 1957 ) is a scottish-canadian actor , comedian and writer . he is best known for his work as a regular on both the british and american versions of the improvisational comedy television show `` whose line is it anyway ? '' . mochrie was born in kilmarnock , scotland . in 1964 , he moved with his family to canada . he first moved to montreal , quebec and finally vancouver , british columbia .
