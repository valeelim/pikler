Punjab States Agency     the punjab states agency was a political office of the british indian empire . the agency was created in the 1930s , on the model of the central india agency and rajputana agency . it dealt with forty princely states in northwest india . formerly dealt with by the british province of the punjab province . the punjab hill states agency was created from it in 1936. after 1947 , most of the states agreed to join the union of india . the rest joined the dominion of pakistan .
