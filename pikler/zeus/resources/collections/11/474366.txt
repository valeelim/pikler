Gideon Ben-Yisrael     gideon ben-yisrael , hebrew : גדעון בן-ישראל , ( march 6 , 1923 – december 18 , 2014 ) was an israeli politician . he served as a member of the knesset for mapai and rafi in the 1950s and 1960s . he was born in haifa , mandatory palestine . ben-yisrael died in tel aviv from natural causes , aged 92. he was buried at the trumpeldor cemetery in tel aviv .
