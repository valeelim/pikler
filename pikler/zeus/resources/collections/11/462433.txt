Devil in a Blue Dress     devil in a blue dress is a 1995 crime drama movie . it is set in the summer of 1948 in los angeles . it is about a world war ii veteran who has been unfairly laid off from an aircraft manufacturer . denzel washington plays ezekiel `` easy '' rawlins , a detective . jennifer beals plays daphne monet . this movie was released on september 29 , 1995. most critics liked the movie .
