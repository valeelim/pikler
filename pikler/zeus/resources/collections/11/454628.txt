Tofino     tofino is a district in the canadian province of british columbia . tofino is found on the west coast of vancouver island . it is at the western terminus of highway 4 , it is on the tip of the esowista peninsula , and is at the southern edge of clayoquot sound . the population of tofino was 1,876 in 2011. the mayor of tofino is josie osborne . tofino is a popular spot for surfers , campers , fishermen , nature lovers , and whale watchers during the summer . tofino is named in association with the tofino inlet . in 1792 , the tofino inlet was named by the spanish commanders galiano and valdés to honour admiral vicente tofiño de san miguel y wanderiales . other websites . - official website
