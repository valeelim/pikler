Odd Bondevik     odd bondevik ( 20 june 1941 – 6 september 2014 ) was a norwegian theologian . he was the bishop of the diocese of møre in the church of norway from 17 november 1991 until retirement in 2008. he also served as preses of the bishop 's conference of the church of norway from 1998 until 2002. he was born in sauda , norway . bondevik died in drammen , norway , aged 73 .
