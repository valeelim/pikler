Virginia Halas McCaskey     virginia halas mccaskey ( born january 5 , 1923 ) is the principal owner of the chicago bears of the national football league . she is the eldest child of legendary bears coach and owner george halas , who left the team to his daughter upon his death in 1983. along with her children and grandchildren , she controls 80 % of the team . after the death of ralph wilson in march of 2014 , she became the oldest owner in the nfl .
