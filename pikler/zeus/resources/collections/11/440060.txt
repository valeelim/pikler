Sleigh Bells (band)    sleigh bells ( band ) sleigh bells is an american noise pop musical group . the members are derek e. miller and alexis krauss . the group started in 2008. miller met krauss in 2008 when he was waiting tables at a brazilian restaurant . on may 11 , 2010 , sleigh bells ' first album `` treats '' was released by mom+pop and n.e.e.t . recordings . it got good reviews . pitchfork gave it a score of 8.7 out of 10. on february 21 , 2012 their second album `` reign of terror '' was released . on october 4 , 2013 sleigh bells ' third album `` bitter rivals '' was released . it did not get reviews as good as `` treats '' or `` reign of terror '' . on the same day sleigh bells started a tour .
