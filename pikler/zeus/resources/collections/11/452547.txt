1974 FIFA World Cup Final     the 1974 fifa world cup final was the final match of the 1974 fifa world cup . the match was played with the teams west germany and the netherlands . the west germans won the match 2–1 , winning their second world cup title . early in the match , a penalty was given to the netherlands after uli hoeneß fouled johan cruijff . the dutch quickly scored and made the score 1–0 . twenty minutes later , wim jansen fouled bernd hölzenbein giving west germany a penalty as well . west germany then scored , making it 1–1 . gerd müller then scored the winner for west germany at the 43rd minute . this would be his last goal for west germany , as he retired shortly after the tournament . although the second half was full of chances , no team managed to score , and west germany then won the match 2–1 . related pages . - fifa world cup
