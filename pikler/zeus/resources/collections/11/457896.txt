Christina Jutterström     christina jutterström ( born 27 march 1940 ) is a swedish journalist . she is the former chief editor for `` dagens nyheter '' between 1982 and 1995 and for `` expressen '' between 1995 and 1996. in 2001 , jutterström became the ceo post at public broadcaster sveriges television . she held the position until eva hamilton took over on 5 november 5 , 2006. jutterström was married to ingemar odlander from 1978 until his death in 2014 .
