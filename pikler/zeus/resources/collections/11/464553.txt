Eric Newby     eric newby ( 1919-2006 ) was an english writer and traveler . he traveled to many parts of europe , south asia and central asia . is best know for his famous and very enjoyable books like `` the last grain of rice '' ( 1956 ) , `` a short walk in the hindu kush '' ( 1958 ) , `` slowly down the ganges '' ( 1966 ) , `` love and war in the appenines '' ( 1971 ) , `` a small place in italy '' ( 1994 ) and others .
