Régine Deforges     régine deforges ( 15 august 1935 – 3 april 2014 ) was a french writer , editor , director and playwright . her best known works for `` le cahier volé '' ( 1978 ) , `` la bicyclette bleue '' ( 1981 ) , `` 101 , avenue henri-martin '' ( 1983 ) , `` le diable en rit encore '' ( 1985 ) and `` noir tango '' ( 1991 ) . she was born in montmorillon , vienne . deforges died from a heart attack on 3 april 2014 in paris . she was 78 years old .
