Otakar Brousek, Sr.    otakar brousek , sr. otakar brousek ( 28 september 1924 – 14 march 2014 ) was a czech actor and voice actor . he appeared in the movies `` andělská tvář '' ( 2002 ) , `` svatby pana voka '' ( 1971 ) , and `` můj brácha má prima bráchu '' ( 1975 ) . he also appeared in the television show `` f. l. věk '' in 1971. he was born in krhanice , czechoslovakia . brousek died on 14 march 2014 in prague , czech republic . he was 89 years old . he was outlived by his children , actors otakar brousek , jr. and jaroslava brousková .
