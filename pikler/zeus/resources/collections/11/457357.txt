Nikola Mirotić     nikola `` niko '' mirotić ( serbian cyrillic : никола миротић ; born february 11 , 1991 ) is a spanish-montenegrin professional basketball player for the chicago bulls of the national basketball association ( nba ) . he was contracted in 2014 for the bulls . he is a 2.09 m ( 6 ft 10.25 in ) tall 107 kg ( 236 lb . ) power forward . he was born in titograd , sr montenegro , sfr yugoslavia .
