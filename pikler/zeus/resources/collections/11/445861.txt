Shinsuke Nakamura     at invasion attack 2014 on april 6 , nakamura defeated hiroshi tanahashi to win the iwgp intercontinental championship for the third time in his career . on june 21 at dominion 6.21 , nakamura was defeated by bad luck fale for the championship . nakamura took part in the 2014 g1 climax . in his block , he was able to win eight times and only suffered two losses . he was able to make it to the finals but lost to his chaos stablemate kazuchika okada on august 10. championships . - inoki genome federation - iwgp heavyweight championship ( one time ) - new japan pro wrestling - iwgp heavyweight championship ( three times ) - iwgp intercontinental championship ( three times ) - iwgp tag team championship ( one time ) ( with hiroshi tanahashi ) - iwgp u-30 championship ( one time ) - nwf heavyweight championship ( one time ) - 10,000,000 yin tag tournament ( 2004 ) ( with hiroyoshi tenzan ) - g1 climax ( 2011 ) - g1 tag league ( 2006 ) ( with masahiro chono ) - national district tournament ( 2006 ) ( with koji kanemoto )
