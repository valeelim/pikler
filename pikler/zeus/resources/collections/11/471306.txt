Elimination Chamber (2014)    elimination chamber ( 2014 ) elimination chamber ( 2014 ) ( no escape ( 2014 ) in germany ) was a professional wrestling pay-per-view show made by wwe . it was held on february 23 , 2014 at target center in minneapolis , minnesota . it was the fifth elimination chamber event held by the wwe . there were two main events . the first saw the wyatt family defeat the shield and the second saw randy orton successfully retain his wwe world heavyweight championship in an elimination chamber match against daniel bryan , john cena , cesaro , christian and sheamus . other websites . - official elimination chamber 2014 website
