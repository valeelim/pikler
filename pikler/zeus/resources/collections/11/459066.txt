Kimbra     kimbra lee johnson ( born 27 march 1990 ) , known as kimbra , is a new zealand singer , musician , and singer-songwriter who lives in melbourne , australia . kimbra 's musical style is inspired from many different styles- electropop , r & b , jazz and soul . her first collection of songs , `` vows '' , was released in australia in 2011. kimbra is featured in the 2012 single `` somebody that i used to know '' by gotye . on 10 february 2013 , she and gotye won two awards at the 2013 grammy awards for `` somebody that i used to know '' .
