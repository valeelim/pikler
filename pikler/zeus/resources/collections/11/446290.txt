Bayn Johnson     bayn johnson ( born in november 4 , 1958 ) is an american former actress , electric guitarist , and singer . she was born in new orleans , louisiana . she appeared on season 1 ( 1969-1970 ) of `` sesame street '' . she also appeared as `` kelly ( the short circus kid band member ) '' on seasons 3-4 ( 1973-1975 ) of `` the electric company '' .
