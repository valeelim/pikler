Juan Carlos Altavista     juan carlos altavista ( buenos aires , january 4 , 1929 - july 20 , 1989 ) was an argentine actor and comedian . he was known for his roles in `` mingo y anibal , dos pelotazos en contra '' ( 1984 ) , `` mingo y aníbal contra los fantasmas '' ( 1985 ) , and in `` mingo y aníbal en la mansión embrujada '' ( 1986 ) . altavista died in buenos aires on 20 july 1989 , due to wolff-parkinson-white syndrome , aged 60 .
