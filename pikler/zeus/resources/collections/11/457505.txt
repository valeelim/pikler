Mickey's Diner    mickey 's diner mickey 's diner is an art deco-styled , restaurant located in downtown st. paul , minnesota . it is located at 36 west 7th street . the diner was built in 1937 by the jerry o'mahony diner company of elizabeth , new jersey . then it was shipped to saint paul by rail and installed in 1939. the diner is 50 feet long by 10 feet wide . the 24-hour diner has operated non-stop since it first opened . in 1983 , the diner was added to the national register of historic places . the original mickey 's diner appears in several hollywood movies . these include `` the mighty ducks '' , `` jingle all the way '' , and `` a prairie home companion '' . the diner is also featured on the front and back cover of the 1985 self-titled , debut album of r & b singer , alexander o'neal . the diner appears in o'neal 's 1985 music video for the song , `` innocent '' , and in the 1991 pet shop boys music video for the song , `` where the streets have no name ( i ca n't take my eyes off of you ) '' . mickey 's also appears in the animated short `` dripped '' by leo verrier ( 2012 ) , although the city pictured in the film is new york . a second mickey 's diner under different management is located at 1950 west 7th street in saint paul 's sibley neighborhood . it is decorated in the manner of a 1950s-style diner .
