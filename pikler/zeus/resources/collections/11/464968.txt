Splake     the splake or slake ( `` salve-linus namaeycush x salvelinus fontinalis '' ) is a fish . it is the hybrid of a male brook trout ( `` salvelinus fontinalis '' ) and a female lake trout ( `` salvelinus namaycush '' ) . the name is a combination of speckled trout ( another name for brook trout ) and lake trout . the name may have been used as early as the 1880s . hybrids of the male lake trout with the female brook trout ( the so-called `` brookinaw '' ) have also been produced , but are not as successful .
