Analeigh Tipton     analeigh christian tipton ( born november 9 , 1988 ) is an american figure skater , actress , and fashion model . she became famous after coming third in cycle 11 of `` america 's next top model '' . she has also appeared in the movies `` the green hornet '' ( 2011 ) , `` crazy , stupid , love '' ( 2011 ) , `` damsels in distress '' ( 2011 ) , and `` warm bodies '' ( 2013 ) . tipton was born in minneapolis , minnesota . she is engaged to longtime boyfriend aaron mcmanus .
