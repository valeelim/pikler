Nicky Morgan (politician)    nicky morgan ( politician ) nicola ann morgan ( born 1 october 1972 ) is a british politician . morgan was born in kingston upon thames and grew up in surbiton . she studied law at st hugh 's college , oxford . she became a solicitor in 1996. morgan is a member of the conservative party . she was the secretary of state for education and minister for women from 2014-2016. she was removed from her job by the new prime minister theresa may . she was removed from these positions on 14 july 2016 .
