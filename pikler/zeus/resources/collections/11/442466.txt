Věra Chytilová     věra chytilová ( 2 february 1929 – 12 march 2014 ) was an avant-garde czech movie director . she was known for her works in `` daises '' , `` wolf 's hole '' , `` a hoof here , a hoof there '' , and `` the inheritance or fuckoffguysgoodday '' . chytilová was born in ostrava , czechoslovakia .
