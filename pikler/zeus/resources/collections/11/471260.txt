Night of Champions (2014)    night of champions ( 2014 ) night of champions ( 2014 ) was a professional wrestling pay-per-view show made by wwe . it was held on september 21 , 2014 at bridgestone arena in nashville , tennessee . it was the eighth night of champions event held by the wwe . the main event saw john cena defeat brock lesnar by disqualification after seth rollins interfered . this caused lesnar to retain his wwe world heavyweight championship . other websites . - official night of champions 2014 website
