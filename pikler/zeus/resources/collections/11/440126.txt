Mário Coluna     mário esteves coluna ( ; 6 august 1935 – 25 february 2014 ) was a portuguese footballer . he played mainly as a central midfielder . he spent most of his career with benfica , appearing in 525 official games and scoring 127 goals during 16 professional seasons . dubbed `` monstro sagrado '' ( sacred monster ) he won 19 major titles with his main club , including ten national leagues and two european cups . coluna represented portugal at the 1966 world cup , playing nearly 60 times . coluna died on 25 february 2014 at the age of 78 in maputo , after not being able to overcome a pulmonary infection . other websites . - profile at zerozero - stats at foradejogo
