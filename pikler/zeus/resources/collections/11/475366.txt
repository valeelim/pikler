Division of Kingston     the division of kingston is an australian electoral division in south australia . it covers south part of the adelaide metropolitan area . it includes the suburbs of aldinga beach , christie downs , christies beach , hackham , hallett cove , huntfield heights , lonsdale , maslin beach , moana , morphett vale , old noarlunga , onkaparinga hills , port noarlunga , reynella , seaford , sellicks beach , sheidow park , port willunga , trott park , woodcroft , and parts of happy valley and mclaren flat . it was set up in 1949 and is named after charles kingston , premier of south australia ( 1893–1899 ) . he was elected to the first house of representatives in 1901 and the first federal member of the division of adelaide in 1903. notably , every sitting member in the electorate 's history has been defeated at an election—none have retired or resigned . election results . - 2004 election results - 2007 election results - 2010 election results - 2013 election results references . - abc profile for kingston : 2013
