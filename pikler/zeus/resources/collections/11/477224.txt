Swedish Academy for Children's Books    swedish academy for children 's books the swedish academy for children 's books ( ) is a nonprofit society . it was established on 26 may 1989 at the skärholmen library in stockholm in sweden . it is based on the swedish academy . its ambitions is to promote children 's and youth literature . since 1990 , the society awards the `` eldsjälen award '' . other websites . - official website
