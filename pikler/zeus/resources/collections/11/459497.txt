Annie Girardot     annie girardot ( 25 october 1931 – 28 february 2011 ) was a french actress . she played independent , hard-working and lonely women in many of her movies . she played françoise in `` love is a funny thing '' . she also played marie louise in `` hearth fires '' . girardot was born in paris . she died there from alzheimer 's disease at age seventy-nine .
