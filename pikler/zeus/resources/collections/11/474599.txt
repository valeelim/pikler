Going Back (album)    going back ( album ) going back is the eighth and final studio album by english singer-songwriter phil collins . it was released on 14 september 2010 in the united kingdom . it was released on 28 september 2010 in the united states . it features covers of '60s motown & soul standards . it also was his first full solo release in eight years . collins was primarily concentrated on soundtracks , compilations , and his extensive touring as a solo artist and with genesis .
