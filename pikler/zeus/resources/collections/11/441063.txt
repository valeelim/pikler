Bruce Vilanch     bruce vilanch ( born november 23 , 1948 ) is an american comedian , songwriter and actor . he is best known for his four-years on `` hollywood squares '' , as a celebrity participant and head writer for the show . he is has won an emmy award six times . since 2000 , he has been the head writer for the oscars , after being an oscar program co-writer for the previous ten years . he is also a featured writer for the tonys , grammys and emmys . vilanch was born in new york city , new york and raised in paterson , new jersey . he was adopted when he was four days old . he was raised in a jewish family . he studied at the ohio state university ( osu ) . he is openly gay .
