Peter Scholl-Latour     peter roman scholl-latour ( march 9 , 1924 – august 16 , 2014 ) was a french-german journalist and publicist . he was born in bochum , germany . he was of ( partly ) jewish descent ( his mother was jewish ) . he was known for his books : - `` der tod im reisfeld [ death in the rice-fields ] '' ( 1980 ) - `` allah ist mit den standhaften [ allah is with the steadfast ] '' ( 1983 ) - `` mord am grossen fluss [ murder at the great river ] '' ( 1986 ) - `` leben mit frankreich [ living with france ] '' ( 1988 ) - `` afrikanische totenklage [ african dirge ] '' ( 2001 ) he fought in the first indochina war . scholl-latour died in rhöndorf , germany , aged 90 .
