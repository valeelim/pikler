Anna-Lisa Frykman     anna-lisa frykman born anna elisabet frykman on 4 january 1889 in stockholm , sweden , dead 14 june 1960 in stockholm , sweden was a swedish composer , song lyricist and teacher . one of her most famous works is the children 's song `` kungens lilla piga '' . she also wrote the christmas song `` julpolskan '' . she was also active under the pseudonym `` eolus '' .
