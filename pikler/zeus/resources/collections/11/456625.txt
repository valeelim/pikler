Typhoon Neoguri (2014)    typhoon neoguri ( 2014 ) typhoon neoguri ( or typhoon florita ) was a strong tropical cyclone in the western pacific ocean . the storm developed in early july 2014 and was labeled `` neoguri '' by the japan meteorological agency . later on july 4 , `` neoguri '' was upgraded to typhoon level . the storm then intensified . certain areas around japan were put on alert because of the storm .
