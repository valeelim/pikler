Simon Wiesenthal     simon wiesenthal , kbe ( 31 december 190820 september 2005 ) was an austrian holocaust survivor . he became famous after world war ii for his work as a nazi hunter . wiesenthal was nominated for the nobel peace prize in 1985. wiesenthal died in vienna , austria from natural causes , aged 96. other websites . - photo gallery : the legacy of simon wiesenthal by `` der spiegel '' - `` new york times '' obituary - bbc news obituary - `` the art of remembrance : simon wiesenthal '' - documentary by hannah heer and werner schmiedel
