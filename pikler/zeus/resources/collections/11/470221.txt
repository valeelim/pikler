IPhone 5S     the iphone 5s is a smartphone that is designed and made by apple inc. it was released on september 20 , 2013. apple held an event to introduce the iphone 5s , along with the iphone 5c , on september 10 , 2013. it has the same design as the iphone 5 , but has new features such as the touch id fingerprint scanner and an improved camera .
