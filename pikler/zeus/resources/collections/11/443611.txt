Iñaki Azkuna     iñaki azkuna urreta ( 14 february 1943 – 20 march 2014 ) was a spanish politician of the basque nationalist party . from 1999 until his death , he was mayor of the city of bilbao , biscay in the basque country . in 2012 , he won the world mayor prize , honoring the most outstanding mayors in the world . he was born in durango , biscay . azkuna died from prostate cancer on 20 march 2014 in bilbao , biscay . he was 71 years old . other websites . - iñaki azkuna at city mayors
