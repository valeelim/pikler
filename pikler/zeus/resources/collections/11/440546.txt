Julio María Sanguinetti     julio maría sanguinetti coirolo ( born 6 january 1936 ) is a uruguayan politician , lawyer and journalist . he served as president of uruguay twice : from march 1985 until march 1990 , and from march 1995 until march 2000. he was from the colorado party . he supports same-sex marriage and a bill that recently approved same-sex marriages by the uruguayan legislature . sanguinetti was born in montevideo .
