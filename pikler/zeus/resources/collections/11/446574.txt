Apple Jacks     apple jacks is a type of breakfast cereal made by the cereal maker kellogg 's . it has the flavor of apples and cinnamon . the cereal was invented by william thilly and was introduced in 1965 as `` apple o 's '' . kellogg 's apple jacks introduced `` cinnamon jacks '' , a multi-grain cinnamon flavored cereal in 2013. other websites . - apple jacks on kelloggs.com - cinnamon jacks website
