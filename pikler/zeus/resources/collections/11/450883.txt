Dumb and Dumber     dumb and dumber is a 1994 american comedy movie . this movie is about roommates in providence , rhode island who drive to aspen , colorado . jim carrey and jeff daniels play the roommates . lauren holly plays mary . teri garr plays helen . the movie was released on december 16 , 1994 by warner bros. the reviews it received were very good . the movie was a box office success . a prequel , `` dumb and dumberer '' , was released in 2003. a sequel , `` dumb and dumber to '' , was released in 2014 .
