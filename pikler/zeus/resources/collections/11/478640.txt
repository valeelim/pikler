Barrie Ingham     barrie ingham ( 10 february 1932 - 23 january 2015 ) was an english actor . he was known for his role as alydon in `` dr. who and the daleks '' and as the voice of basil bartholomew in the disney animated movie `` the great mouse detective '' . ingham was born in halifax , west riding of yorkshire england . ingham died , two weeks before his 83rd birthday , in palm beach gardens , florida , aged 82 .
