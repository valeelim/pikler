Arrondissement of Vichy     the arrondissement of vichy is an arrondissement of france . it is part of the allier `` département '' . its capital is the city of vichy . when the department of allier was created on 4 march 1790 , vichy was not one of ist districts . with the establishment of the `` arrondissements '' on 17 february 1800 , vichy was part of the `` arrondissements '' of `` lapalisse '' . on 24 august 1941 , the capital of the `` arrondissement '' of `` lapalisse '' was moved from the city of lapalisse to the city of vichy that was then the capital of the french state . the `` arrondissement '' of vichy is in the southeast of the allier department and is bordered to the west and north by the `` arrondissement '' of moulins , to the east by the saône-et-loire ( burgundy ) department , to the southeast by the loire ( rhône-alpes ) department , to the south by the puy-de-dôme department and to the southwest by the `` arrondissement '' of montluçon . it has an area of , and 122,308 inhabitants , the `` arrondissement '' of allier with more people living there , and a population density of inhabitants/km² .
