Žarko Petan    žarko petan žarko petan ( 27 march 1929 – 2 may 2014 ) was a slovenian writer , essayist , screenwriter , and theatre and movie director . he is known as a writer of aphorisms . his career began in 1950. he wrote over 100 essays and novels . between 1992 and 1994 , he served as director general of the slovenian national radio and television broadcast . petan was born in ljubljana , slovenia . petan died on 2 may 2014 at the age of 85 .
