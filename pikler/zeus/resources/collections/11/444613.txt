Lalah Hathaway     eulaulah donyll hathaway ( also known as lalah hathaway ; born december 16 , 1968 ) is an american contemporary r & b and jazz singer and musician . she has had several hit singles . they include `` let me love you '' ( a 1994 song ) and `` baby do n't cry '' in 1990. lalah hathaway is the daughter of late singer-songwriter donny hathaway . hathaway was born in chicago .
