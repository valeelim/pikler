Étienne Mourrut    étienne mourrut étienne mourrut ( december 4 , 1939 – october 19 , 2014 ) was a french politician . he was a member of the union for a popular movement ( ump ) . mourrut was an mp for gard 's 2nd constituency . mourrut was born in le grau-du-roi , gard , france . mourrut died in paris , france , aged 74 .
