Penny Arcade Expo     pax ( originally named penny arcade expo ) is a series of gaming festivals . it is held in seattle , washington ( pax prime , pax dev ) , boston , massachusetts ( pax east ) , san antonio , texas ( pax south ) , and melbourne , victoria ( pax australia ) . the festival was created by `` penny arcade '' authors jerry holkins and mike krahulik in 2004. they created it because they wanted to attend a show that would give equal attention to console gamers , computer gamers , and tabletop gamers . since its creation , the event has grown to become very popular ; it is widely considered one of the most important events to the video gaming community . other websites . - official website
