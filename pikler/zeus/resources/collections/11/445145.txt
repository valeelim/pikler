Arrondissement of Oloron-Sainte-Marie     the arrondissement of oloron-sainte-marie is an arrondissement of france . it is part of the pyrénées-atlantiques `` département '' . its capital is the city of oloron-sainte-marie . it is the largest and the southernmost of the `` arrondissements '' of the department , with an area of , and a population of 74,737 inhabitants ; its population density is of inhabitants/km² . when the `` arrondissements '' were created on 17 february 1800 , oloron-sainte-marie was one of them in the pyrénées-atlantiques department . since 10 september 1926 , some parts of the dissolved `` arrondissements '' mauléon and orthez were added to the `` arrondissement '' of oloron-sainte-marie . the `` arrondissement '' is bordered to the north and east by the `` arrondissement '' pau , to the southeast by the hautes-pyrénées ( midi-pyrénées ) department , to the south by spain , and to the west by the `` arrondissement '' bayonne . after the reorganisation of the cantons in france , cantons are not subdivisions of the `` arrondissements '' so they could have `` communes '' that belong to different `` arrondissements '' .
