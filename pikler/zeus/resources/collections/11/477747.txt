American Sniper     american sniper is a 2014 american drama movie . it is directed by clint eastwood . it is based on a book by chris kyle called `` american sniper : the autobiography of the most lethal sniper in u.s. military history '' . it stars bradley cooper in the leading role with sienna miller . it was released to positive reviews on december 25 , 2014. the movie was nominated for six awards at the 2015 oscars .
