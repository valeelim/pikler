George Eliot (spy)    george eliot ( spy ) george eliot was an english spy during the rule of queen elizabeth i. george eliot is reported to have been a very bad person . he earned his living as a con artist ( someone who gains the trust of another and then tricks them out of their money ) . he was well known as a rapist and suspected of being a murderer . eliot became a spy for robert dudley , the earl of leicester . this was to avoid a charge from the last crime he committed . eliot agreed to seek out catholics who did not attend mass or conform to the rules of the state . he would then hand them over to the authorities for punishment . at lyford grange in berkshire ( now oxfordshire ) , he tracked down the jesuit priest , edmund campion . eliot called for a magistrate from abingdon . he arrested campion and had him sent to london for trial and execution .
