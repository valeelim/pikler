Harold Brown (Secretary of Defense)    harold brown ( secretary of defense ) harold brown ( born september 19 , 1927 ) american scientist and politician . he was the u.s. secretary of defense from 1977 to 1981 in the cabinet of president jimmy carter . he last served in the lyndon johnson administration as director of defense research and engineering and secretary of the air force . other websites . - annotated bibliography for harold brown from the alsos digital library for nuclear issues
