Gustavsberg, Värmdö Municipality    gustavsberg , värmdö municipality gustavsberg is a locality and the seat of värmdö municipality in stockholm county , sweden . in 2010 , 11,333 people lived there . it is most known for its porcelain factory , gustavsberg porcelain , and toilet bowls distributed nationwide and internationally . other websites . - article `` gustafsberg '' from nordisk familjebok ( 1909 )
