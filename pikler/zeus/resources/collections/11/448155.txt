Owen Jones (writer)    owen jones ( writer ) owen jones ( born 8 august 1984 ) is an english writer . jones was born in sheffield . he grew up in manchester . he writes a column for `` the guardian '' . his first book was published by verso in 2011. it was chosen by the `` new york times '' as one of their top 10 non-fiction books of 2011. his second book `` the establishment and how they get away with it '' was published in 2014. jones lives in london .
