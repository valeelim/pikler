Luis Nishizawa     luis nishizawa ( february 2 , 1918 – september 29 , 2014 ) was a mexican artist . he was known for his landscape work and murals . he began training as an artist in 1942 at the height of the mexican muralism movement but studied other painting styles as well as japanese art . in addition to painting canvases and murals , he was a professor of fine arts at the universidad nacional autónoma de méxico . nihizawa was born in cuautitlán , mexico . nishizawa died in mexico city , mexico from natural causes , aged 96 .
