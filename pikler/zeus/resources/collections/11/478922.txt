Dacia Valent     dacia soraya valent ( 12 february 1963 - 22 january 2015 ) was a somali-born italian politician . she was a member of the european parliament from 1989 through 1994. she was born in mogadishu , somalia . valent died in rome , italy from a heart attack , aged 51. other websites . - official blog - dacia valent profile from the european parliament website
