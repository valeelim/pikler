Duchy of Parma     the duchy of parma was created in 1545 from that part of the duchy of milan south of the po river , which had been conquered by the state of the church in 1512. these territories , centered on the city of parma , were given as a fief for pope paul iii 's illegitimate son , pier luigi farnese . the state was originally ruled by the farnese family before eventually falling to the house of bourbon by inheritance . following the creation of the state of italy in 1859 the duchy was abolished . it was also a state of the holy roman empire till 1806 and had various links with its neighbour the grand duchy of tuscany .
