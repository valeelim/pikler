V-Rally 2     v-rally 2 is a racing video game . it is the sequel to v-rally for the playstation . it is known in america as need for speed : v-rally 2 and in europe as v-rally 2 championship edition . the dreamcast version is known as test drive v-rally in america . the game got very good reviews . ign gave it a 8.5/10 for its graphics and sound effects .
