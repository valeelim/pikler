Slowdive     slowdive is an english dream pop band . they were started in 1985 in reading by neil halstead and rachel goswell . they broke up in 1995. in 2014 the band got back together . in 1990 slowdive released their first ep `` slowdive '' . in september 1991 the band 's first album `` just for a day '' was released . the band wrote to brian eno to ask him to produce their second album . eno said that he liked slowdive 's music but wanted to make music with them rather than produce their album . in 1993 slowdive released their second album `` souvlaki '' . pitchfork gave the album a score of 7 out of 10. they then released two extended plays , `` 5 ep '' and `` 5 ep ( in mind remixes ) '' . in 1995 the band released their last album `` pygmalion '' . then they broke up . in 2014 slowdive got back together to play the 2014 primavera sound festival in barcelona from may 29-31 .
