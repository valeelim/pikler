Susanne Nyström     susanne nyström , born 29 december 1982 in laisvall , sweden is a swedish female cross-country skier and nurse . she competes for laisvalls sk . earlier , she has competed for the clubs mora sk , arvidsjaur ski team , luleå gjutarens if and piteå elit sk . she won tjejvasan in 2007 , 2008 , 2009 , 2010 , 2012 and 2013. she ended ended up second ad tjejvasan 2011. in 2010 , she won the women 's vasaloppet main competition . that year , she became the first to win tjejvasan and the vasaloppet main competition the same year . she has also won several swedish national championship gold medals .
