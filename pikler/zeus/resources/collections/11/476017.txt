Cookie Clicker     cookie clicker is a javascript-based browser video game written by french programmer julien thiennot . the point of the game is to bake cookies as fast as possible . to start the game , a player bakes cookies by clicking on a giant cookie . one cookie is gained each time it is clicked . with sufficient cookies to use as currency , a player can buy items and upgrades that will bake more cookies . they will also increase the number of cookies per click . there is no true end to the game .
