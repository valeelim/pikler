Kilpisjärvi     kilpisjärvi is a village in enontekiö municipality in lapland , finland . about 10 km west of kilpisjärvi is treriksröset . this is the point where the borders of finland , norway and sweden meet . a monument was built at the exact point in 1926. it replaced an earlier marker built of stone in 1897. other websites . - kilpisjärvi village 's website
