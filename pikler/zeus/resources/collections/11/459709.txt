The World According to Garp (movie)    the world according to garp ( movie ) the world according to garp is 1982 american comedy drama movie directed by george roy hill , based on the novel of the same title by john irving , who co-wrote the script with steve tesich . the movie starred robin williams , glenn close , and john lithgow . cast . - robin williams as t.s . garp - james `` j.b. '' mccall as young garp - mary beth hurt as helen holm - glenn close as jenny fields - john lithgow as roberta muldoon - hume cronyn as mr. fields - jessica tandy as mrs. fields - swoosie kurtz as the hooker - peter michael goetz as john wolf - mark soper as michael milton - amanda plummer as ellen james
