Maria Kaczyńska     maria kaczyńska ( ; née mackiewicz ; 21 august 1942 – 10 april 2010 ) was the first lady of poland from 2005 through 2010. she was the wife of president lech kaczyński . on 10 april 2010 , 10:41 msd ( 06:41 utc ) , maria kaczyńska , and her husband , polish president lech kaczyński , died when the polish air force tupolev tu-154m they were aboard crashed while attempting to land at smolensk-north airport in the russian city of smolensk . all 89 passengers on board and the 7 crew members were killed . other websites . - polish first lady 's official website - maria kaczyńska official biography ( in english )
