Vancouver International Film Festival     the vancouver international film festival ( viff ) is an annual movie festival in vancouver , british columbia . it is held for two weeks in late september and early october . it is held by greater vancouver international film festival society and it began in 1982. it is among the five largest movie festivals in north america . the festival screens the largest selection of east asian movies outside of that region , it is one of the biggest showcases of canadian film in the world , and it has one of the a largest nonfiction program outside of a documentary film festival . other websites . - official website
