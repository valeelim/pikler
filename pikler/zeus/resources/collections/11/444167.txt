Bedtime Stories (Madonna album)    bedtime stories ( madonna album ) bedtime stories the sixth studio album by american singer madonna . it was released on october 25 , 1994. the album 's music is very different to madonna 's previous albums as it is mainly new jack swing and r & b sounding compared to madonna 's usual pop and dance material . the songs on this album are mostly melancholy mixed with romantic themes . the singles of the album were `` secret '' , `` take a bow '' and `` human nature '' . the first song was released september 28 , 1994. the last song was released june 6 , 1995 .
