Mike Leigh     mike leigh , ( born 20 february 1943 ) is an english screenwriter , movie director and actor . his best known movies are `` life is sweet '' ( 1990 ) , `` naked '' ( 1993 ) , `` secrets & lies '' ( 1996 ) , `` career girls '' ( 1997 ) , `` topsy-turvy '' ( 1999 ) , `` all or nothing '' ( 2002 ) , and `` vera drake '' ( 2004 ) . leigh was born in brocket hall , welwyn , hertfordshire . he was bought up in broughton , salford , lancashire . he is jewish . he was married to actress alison steadman from 1973 until they divorced in 2001. they have two sons , toby and leo . leigh lives in camden , london .
