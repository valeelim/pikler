Anish Giri     anish giri ( born june 28 , 1994 ) is a dutch chess prodigy . he got the grandmaster title at the age of 14 years , 7 months and 2 days at the 2009 corus chess tournament in wijk aan zee in the netherlands . giri is the son of a nepalese father ( sanjay giri ) , and a russian mother ( olga giri ) . giri was born in st. petersburg , russia on 28 june 1994. in 2002 , he moved to japan with his parents . when he lived in japan , he visited st. petersburg regularly . since february 2008 , giri and his family have been living in the netherlands , in the dutch city of rijswijk where his father is working in a research and consulting foundation .
