Amber Montana     amber dawn montana ( born december 2 , 1998 ) is an american actress . she is best known for her lead role in the nickelodeon tv show `` the haunted hathaways '' as taylor hathaway . she was born in tampa , florida . when she was eight years old she moved to california to pursue opportunities as an actress . in 2008 amber got the role of a girl who is kidnapped in the movie `` she could be you '' . in 2012 he appeared on the nickelodeon game show `` brainsurge '' . in 2013 she landed the lead role in the nickelodeon tv show `` the haunted hathaways '' .
