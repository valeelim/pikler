Errie Ball     samuel henry `` errie '' ball ( november 14 , 1910 â€“ july 2 , 2014 ) was a welsh-american professional golfer . he played at the first augusta national golf tournament in 1934 ( now known as the masters tournament ) . he was the last living person to play in the first masters . he was an active golfer between the 1930s and the 1970s . ball was born in bangor , gwynedd , north west wales . during his 20s , he moved to the united states , under the request from his uncle . he turned 100 in november 2010. he was married to maxie for 78 years until his death . maxie was 99 when ball died . they had a daughter named leslie . ball died from natural causes on july 2 , 2014 in stuart , florida , aged 103. his wife and their daughter outlived him . other websites . - erri ball at the illinois golf hall of fame
