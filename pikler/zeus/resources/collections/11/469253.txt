Kajetan Kovič     kajetan kovič ( 21 october 1931 – 7 november 2014 ) was a slovene poet , writer , translator , and journalist . kovič was born in maribor , yugoslavia . kovič started writing poetry in high school , and he published his first poetry in 1948. kovič also wrote political poetry , such as a poem in honor of josip broz tito . he established himself as a translator of german , french , czech , hungarian , croatian , serbian , and russian poetry into slovene . he also translated the poems that france prešeren wrote in german into slovene . kovič died on 7 november 2014 in ljubljana , slovenia , aged 83 .
