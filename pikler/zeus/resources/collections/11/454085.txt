Robert Peters     robert louis peters ( october 20 , 1924 - june 13 , 2014 ) was an american poet , critic , scholar , playwright , editor , and actor . his poetry career began in 1967 when he wanted to write a poem about the death of his son . the poem was called `` songs for a son '' . after peters divorced , he began dating paul trachtenberg . their relationship lasted for 36 years . peters died on june 13 , 2014 from natural causes at the age of 89. other websites . - profile at university of california , irvine
