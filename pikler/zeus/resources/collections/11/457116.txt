Luiz Alberto Dias Menezes     luiz alberto dias menezes ( 5 october 1950 – 9 july 2014 ) was a brazilian geologist , mineralogist and mineral dealer . he collected the material that was used for the discovery of new mineral was named in his honor as menezesite . he was born in são paulo . menezes died on 9 july 2014 in são paulo , aged 63 .
