1999 (song)    1999 ( song ) `` 1999 '' is a pop rock ballad by prince . this was the title track from the album `` 1999 '' . the song was released on september 24 , 1982. at that time , it did not get into the top 40 in the united states or the united kingdom . it peaked at # 44 in december 1982. later , after `` little red corvette '' hit the top 10 , `` 1999 '' peaked at # 12 in the u.s. in july 1983 .
