Cristina Deutekom     cristina deutekom , also known as `` christine deutekom '' and `` christina deutekom '' ( 28 august 1931 – 7 august 2014 ) was a dutch coloratura soprano opera singer . deutekom 's stage career ended in 1986 , when she suffered a heart attack . she sang with all the leading tenors of her time , including carlo bergonzi , josé carreras , franco corelli , plácido domingo , nicolai gedda , alfredo kraus , luciano pavarotti , and richard tucker . deutekom was born in 1931 in amsterdam as christine ( stientje ) engel . her career began in 1963. she died on 7 august 2014 after a fall in her home . other websites . - discography - interview with cristina deutekom by bruce duffie , october 15 , 1986
