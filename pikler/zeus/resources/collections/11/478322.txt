Lotte Hass     charlotte ( `` lotte '' ) hildegard hass ( née baierl ; 6 november 1928 – 14 january 2015 ) was the second wife of the austrian naturalist and diving pioneer hans hass ( who died on 16 june 2013 ) . she worked as an underwater model and actress in several of his underwater natural history movies . she was added into the women divers hall of fame and the international scuba diving hall of fame in 2000 .
