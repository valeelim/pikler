John Houseman     john houseman ( born jacques haussmann ; september 22 , 1902october 31 , 1988 ) was a romanian-born british–american actor and movie producer . he was known for working with orson welles . houseman was known for his role as professor charles kingsfield in the movie `` the paper chase '' ( 1973 ) . houseman died in malibu , california from spinal cancer , aged 86. other websites . - `` the theatre : marvelous boy '' – `` time magazine '' may 9 , 1938 - interviews with howard koch on the infamous mercury theatre 's war of the worlds radio broadcast
