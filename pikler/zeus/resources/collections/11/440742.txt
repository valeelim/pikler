Philip Levine     philip levine ( january 10 , 1928 – february 14 , 2015 ) was an american poet and teacher . he was best known for his poems about working-class detroit . he taught for more than thirty years at california state university , fresno . he also taught at other different schools . he was appointed poet laureate of the united states for 2011–2012 . he won the pulitzer prize in 1995. levine died of pancreatic cancer on february 14 , 2015 in fresno , california , aged 87 .
