South African Air Force     the south african air force ( saaf ) is the air force of the south african national defence force . the south african air force goes back to 1912 , when the union defence force ( udf ) was created . the udf had included the south african aviation corps ( saac ) , which was part of the active citizen force ( acf ) . on 1 february 1920 , the south african air force was created . the saaf fought in several battles during world war ii , the korean war , and the south african border war . the south african air force has about 226 aircraft and 11,245 active military personnel . the aircraft are mostly european-made . however some , such as the denel rooivalk and the atlas oryx were designed and built in south africa . the swedish saab jas 39 gripen is the standard aircraft of the air force .
