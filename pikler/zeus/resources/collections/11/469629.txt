Pepe Soriano     pepe soriano ( born josé carlos soriano , on september 25 , 1929 ) is a well-known argentine actor and playwright . soriano is known for his roles in `` rebellion in patagonia '' , `` abuela '' , `` asesinato en el senado de la nación '' , `` funes , a great love '' , and in `` the last train '' . soriano was born and raised in buenos aires , argentina . other websites . - pepe soriano - recursos culturales - cine nacional
