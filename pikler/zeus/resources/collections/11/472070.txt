Promised Land (movie)    promised land ( movie ) promised land is a 2012 drama movie . it was directed by gus van sant . it stars matt damon , john krasinski , frances mcdormand , hal holbrook and rosemarie dewitt . the screenplay is written by damon and krasinski based on a story by dave eggers . the movie is about two corporate salespeople . they visit a rural town trying to buy drilling rights from local residents . this movie had a limited release in the united states on december 28 , 2012. it was released nationwide on january 4 , 2013 .
