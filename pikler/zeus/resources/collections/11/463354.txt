Old Borges Ranch     the old borges ranch is a historic district in walnut creek , california . it covers an area of . there are four contributing buildings . in 1899 , frank borges bought . he used it for a cattle ranch and a home for his family . the ranch has a blacksmith shop , farm equipment displays , a barn , and other buildings . one of those buildings is the borges family home . the home has five rooms . it is made of redwood . five generations of the borges family lived in the house . it is now the old borges ranchhouse and visitor center . today , rangers live and work at the ranch . other websites . - old borges ranch
