Zabardast Khan     zabardast khan ( died 1788 ) was a suleman khel ghilzai pathan warrior in history , who fought at the third battle of panipat as part of the afghan forces of ahmad shah durrani . he is not to be confused with another historical person zabardust khan ( nick-named 'suba khan ' ) , a hindkowan tanoli , who served as an administrator in the upper hazara district also under the durranis .
