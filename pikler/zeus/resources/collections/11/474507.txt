René Angélil     rené angélil ( january 16 , 1942 – january 14 , 2016 ) was a canadian singer . he was also the former manager of celine dion . he was married to dion from 1994 until his death . angélil was born in montreal , quebec . it was previously reported that he had throat cancer . in september 2015 , céline revealed that angélil 's cancer was terminal , and that he had only 'weeks ' to live . angélil died on january 14 , 2016 in henderson , nevada from the disease at the age of 73 .
