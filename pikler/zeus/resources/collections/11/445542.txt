Cherry (wrestler)    cherry ( wrestler ) kara elizabeth drew ( born july 15 , 1975 ) is an american professional wrestler and valet . she is best known for her time with world wrestling entertainment ( wwe ) , where she was known by the ring name cherry . during her time there , she was known for being the manager of the tag team deuce 'n domino and was also a wrestler on wwe 's smackdown brand . drew was released from her wwe contract on august 15 , 2008. after being released , drew started working at seaworld . championships . - jersey championship wrestling - jcw women 's championship ( one time ) - women superstars uncensored - wsu/nws king and queen tournament ( 2005 ) ( with julio dinero ) - pro wrestling illustrated - she was ranked # 27 of the best 50 female singles wrestlers in the pwi female 50 in 2008. other websites . - wwe profile
