Don't Cha    do n't cha `` do n't cha '' is a pop song by american girl group the pussycat dolls . it was on their self-titled studio record `` pcd '' in 2005. the song is about a girl teasing a man that she is more attractive than his girlfriend . the song received positive reviews by most critics . `` do n't cha '' got very high on records charts worldwide . it hit # 2 on the `` billboard '' hot 100. it sold three million copies in the united states and six million worldwide .
