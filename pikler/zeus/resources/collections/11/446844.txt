Edna Doré     edna doré , née gorring , ( 31 may 1921 – 11 april 2014 ) was a british actress . doré was known for her bit-part roles in situation comedies . she is mostly known for playing the character of mo butcher in the bbc soap opera `` eastenders '' ( 1988–1990 ) . she was born in bromley , kent , england . doré died from emphysema at her sussex , england home , aged 92 .
