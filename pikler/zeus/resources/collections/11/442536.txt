Ono Academic College     ono academic college ( ) is a private university in kiryat ono , israel . it was opened in 1996. the college has a separate campus that serves haredi jews . it was opened in 2001 by renan hartman . the college allows men to attend classes on different days than women . in 2003 , rabbi yehezkel fogel was appointed chairman to of the campus . presently there is in the campus nearly 3,000 undergraduate students .
