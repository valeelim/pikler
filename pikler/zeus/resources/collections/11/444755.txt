Drive (song)    drive ( song ) `` drive '' is a 1984 song performed by the cars . it was from their `` heartbeat city '' album . the song came out in march 1984. it is a sad song . this song was the highest charting single by the cars . it hit # 3 on the `` billboard '' hot 100. it hit # 1 on the adult contemporary chart . it hit # 5 in the united kingdom .
