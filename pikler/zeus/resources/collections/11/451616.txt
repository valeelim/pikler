Back in Black     back in black is a 1980 album by the australian hard rock band ac/dc . it was the first album made without former band member bon scott . back in black was intended as a tribute to his death . one song from the album , `` you shook me all night long '' , ranked number thirty-four on the billboard charts . this was the first time a heavy metal song appeared in the top 40. even today , record producers rank 'back in black ' as the standard for how a hard rock song should sound .
