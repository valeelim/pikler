Bays of Canada     a bay is a large body of water . it is in the land next to a sea or lake between two headlands . canada is bordered by the atlantic ocean , pacific ocean , and arctic ocean . there are also hundreds of lakes larger than 100 km2 ( 38.6 sq mi ) . canada is also the second largest country by area . all of these factors mean that canada has hundreds of bays . some of the large bays in canada are : - baffin bay - hudson bay - james bay
