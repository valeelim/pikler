John A. Volpe     john anthony volpe ( ; december 8 , 1908november 11 , 1994 ) was an american politician . he served as the 61st and 63rd governor of massachusetts . he also served as the united states secretary of transportation from 1969 to 1973 and an ambassador to italy from 1973 to 1977. volpe died in nahant , massachusetts on november 11 , 1994 , at the age of 85. volpe was buried at the forest glade cemetery in wakefield , massachusetts . other websites . - official commonwealth of massachusetts governor biography
