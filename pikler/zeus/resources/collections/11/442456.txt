Frankenchrist     frankenchrist is the third studio album by american hardcore punk band , dead kennedys . `` frankenchrist '' was released in october 1985. the album caused controversy because of a poster that was inserted in the original record sleeve . the poster called `` landscape # xx '' , or penis landscape h. r. giger , was a painting that showed rows of penises and vulvae . lead singer jello biafra 's record label alternative tentacles was almost driven to bankruptcy and biafra was brought to trial for distributing harmful matter to minors but was not convicted . allmusic rated the album 3.5 out of 5 stars .
