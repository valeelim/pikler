Frightwig     frightwig are an american punk rock band that was founded in 1982. all of the members are women . they are feminists . deanna ashley and mia levin started the band in san francisco . in 1984 they released their first album `` cat farm faboo '' . in 1986 their second album was released . in 1993 they released an extended play called `` phone sexy '' . in 1994 the band broke up . the band got back together in 2012 .
