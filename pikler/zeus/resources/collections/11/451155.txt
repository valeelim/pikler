Ionic order     the ionic order is one of the three main classical orders ( `` styles '' ) of ancient greek and roman architecture . the other two are the doric order and the corinthian order ( which gave rise to the composite order ) . the ionic capital is notable for its use of volutes . the ionic columns normally stand on a base which separates the shaft of the column from the stylobate or platform . the cap is usually enriched with egg-and-dart . it was a popular style in athens . the athenians considered themselves ionians .
