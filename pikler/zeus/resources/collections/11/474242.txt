Claes-Göran Hederström     claes-göran hederström , born on 20 october 1945 in danderyd , sweden ) is a swedish singer . he made his musical debut at svt in 1967. he represented sweden in the eurovision song contest 1968 with the song `` det börjar verka kärlek , banne mej '' . the song ended up 5th . other websites . - claesgoranhederstrom.com
