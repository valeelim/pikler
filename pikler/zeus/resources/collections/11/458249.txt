Hijra (Southern Asia)    hijra ( southern asia ) the hijras of southern asia , ( , , , ) refers to individuals or humans in south asia who are transsexual or transgender . most south asians think hijras are only `` men who have feminine gender identity and wear women 's clothing '' . however , some include female-to-male people also . the word `` hijra '' is a urdu-hindustani word . it comes from the semitic arabic root `` hjr '' . it has been borrowed into hindi . the indian usage has traditionally been translated into english as `` eunuch '' or `` hermaphrodite '' , where `` the irregularity of the male genitalia is central to the definition '' . however , in general , hijras are born with typically male physiology , only a few having been born with male intersex variations .
