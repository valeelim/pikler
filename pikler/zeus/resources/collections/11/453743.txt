Captain Toad: Treasure Tracker    captain toad : treasure tracker captain toad : treasure tracker is a puzzle platformer video game . it was developed by nintendo ead tokyo . it was published by nintendo . it is a spin-off of the `` super mario '' series . it is an expansion of the minigame that was featured in the 2013 game `` super mario 3d world '' . the game was released in japan on november 13 , 2014. it will be released on december 5 , 2014 in north america . it will be released on january 2 , 2015 in europe . it will be released on january 3 , 2015 in australia .
