Love Affair (1994 movie)    love affair ( 1994 movie ) love affair is a 1994 american romantic drama movie . it stars warren beatty as mike gambril and annette bening as terry mckay . katharine hepburn played ginny . this is her last known movie acting role . this movie was released on october 21 , 1994. the movie was considered a `` major flop '' at the box office . it is a remake of `` love affair '' and `` an affair to remember '' .
