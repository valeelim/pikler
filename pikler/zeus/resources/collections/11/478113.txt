Göingeflickorna     göingeflickorna was a girl vocal trio from sweden . it was active between 1955-1955. the members were sisters sonja martinsson ( 1934–2013 ) , barbro svensson ( born 1941 ) and agnetha gardelid ( born 1945 ) . their major breakthrough came in 1961 with the song `` kära mor '' . barbro left the trio in 1975. sonja and agnetha then continued appearing together . svensktoppen songs . - `` vår lyckodröm '' - 1962 - `` där näckrosen blommar '' - 1963 - `` säterjäntans söndag '' - 1965 - `` när solen ler '' - 1974 - `` balsalens drottning '' – 1980
