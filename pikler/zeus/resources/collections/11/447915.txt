Raquel Alessi     raquel nazzarena alessi ( born in los angeles , california on march 7 , 1983 ) is an american actress . in summer 1989 , in the `` kidsongs '' vhs series , she replaced divita wright . she appeared in `` kidsongs : a day at camp '' in 1989. she appeared with kenny ford , jr. on `` kidsongs : ride the roller coaster '' in 1990. she had a cameo appearance in `` kidsongs : very silly songs '' in 1990. she left the vhs series in 1990. she was replaced by ashley nation in 1992 .
