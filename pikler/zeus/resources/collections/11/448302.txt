Lake Tuscaloosa     lake tuscaloosa is a reservoir in west-central alabama . it was created by damming north river . it was built to provide water for people living in tuscaloosa , alabama and also for industrial use . it was completed in 1970. it cost about $ 7,725,000 . the lake is very popular for recreational activities . lake tuscaloosa was built because because more people were living in tuscaloosa . the reservoirs already there , harris lake and lake nicol , did n't provide enough water . a dam was built on north river . when that dam filled , the water behind it became lake tuscaloosa . lake tuscaloosa provides more water than either of the other two reservoirs . lake tuscaloosa 's water is treated to make it safe for people to drink . it also provides untreated water for industrial use .
