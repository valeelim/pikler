Allen Lanier     allen glover lanier ( june 25 , 1946 – august 14 , 2013 ) was an original member of blue öyster cult . he played keyboards and rhythm guitar . lanier wrote several songs for blue öyster cult albums . these included `` true confessions '' , `` tenderloin '' , `` searchin ' for celine '' , `` in thee '' , and `` lonely teardrops '' . he also contributed to music by patti smith , jim carroll , the dictators and the clash . he dated patti smith for several years during the 1970s . when lanier started in 1967 , the group was known as soft white underbelly . he left the group and was replaced in 1986 by tommy zvoncheck . he returned in 1987. he retired from performing with them after the autumn of 2006. on august 14 , 2013 he died of complications from chronic obstructive pulmonary disease ( copd ) .
