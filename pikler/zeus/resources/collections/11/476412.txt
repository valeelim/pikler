David Hewlett     david ian hewlett ( 18 april 1968 ) he is an english-canadian actor , writer and director . he is best known for his role as dr. meredith rodney mckay on the science fiction tv shows `` stargate sg-1 '' , `` stargate atlantis '' and `` stargate universe '' . hewlett was born in redhill , surrey , uk . he moved to canada when he was a child .
