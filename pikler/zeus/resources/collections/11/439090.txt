Home Alone 4     home alone 4 ( also known as home alone 4 : taking back the house ) is an american family television movie . it first aired on abc on november 3 , 2002. home alone 4 was directed by rod daniel . the film has some of the main characters from the first two films , but all of the roles are played by different actors . the story is in chicago but the movie was actually filmed in south africa . the film was not popular . a planned tv series based on the movie was cancelled because of the poor reviews . it 's an intrequel to the first two films .
