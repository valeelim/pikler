Gyula Gömbös     gyula gömbös de jákfa ( 26 december 1886 – 6 october 1936 ) was a hungarian military officer and politician . he served as prime minister of hungary from 1 october 1932 until his death on 6 october 1936. he was born in murga , austria-hungary . gömbös died in munich , germany from testicular cancer , aged 49 .
