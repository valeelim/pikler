Kumba Ialá     kumba ialá , also spelled yalá ( 15 march 1953 – 4 april 2014 ) was a bissau-guinean politician . he was the president of guinea-bissau from 17 february 2000 until 14 september 2003. he was also the president of the social renewal party ( prs ) . in 2008 , he became a muslim and took the name mohamed ialá embaló . he was born in bula , portuguese guinea . ialá died from cardiopulmonary arrest on 4 april 2014 in bissau . he was 61 years old .
