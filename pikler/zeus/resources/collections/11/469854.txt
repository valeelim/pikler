Girl Meets World     girl meets world is an american coming of age sitcom . the show first aired on june 27 , 2014 on disney channel . it followed the premiere of the disney channel original movie , `` zapped '' . `` girl meets world '' is a sequel to the sitcom `` boy meets world '' . the original show aired on abc in its tgif block from 1993 to 2000. on august 6 , 2014 , `` girl meets world '' was renewed for a second season by disney channel . the show follows cory and topanga matthews ' daughter riley ( rowan blanchard ) and her best friend , maya hart ( sabrina carpenter ) as they go through the challenges of life as they start seventh grade . the show also features their classmates lucas ( peyton meyer ) and farkle ( corey fogelmanis ) , and riley 's younger brother auggie ( august maturo ) . the show is set in new york city where cory and topanga moved to in the `` boy meets world '' series finale . < onlyinclude > < /onlyinclude >
