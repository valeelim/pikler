The Barry Sisters     minnie ( 1923 – october 31 , 1976 ) and clara bagelman ( october 17 , 1920 – november 22 , 2014 ) , also known as by the stage names merna and claire barry , or the barry sisters , were popular american jazz entertainers . they worked from the 1940s to the 1970s . minnie and clara were born in bronx , new york . merna barry died from a brain tumor , age 54. clara died at age 94. other websites . - the barry sisters – `` i love you much too much '' on youtube.com
