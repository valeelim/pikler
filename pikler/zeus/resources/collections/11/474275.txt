Beji Caid Essebsi     mohamed beji caid essebsi ( or el-sebsi , , `` '' ; born 29 november 1926 ) is a tunisian lawyer and politician . he is currently the president of tunisia . he was elected in the first presidential election that followed the tunisian revolution . from 27 february 2011 to 24 december 2011 , he was the prime minister of tunisia .
