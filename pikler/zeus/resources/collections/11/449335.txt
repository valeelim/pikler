45 BC     year 45 bc was either a common year starting on thursday , friday or saturday or a leap year starting on friday or saturday and the first year of the julian calendar . at the time , it was known as the year of the consulship of caesar without colleague . events . - julius caesar defeats the pompeian forces of titus labienus and pompey the younger in the battle of munda - possible first year of the azes i era births . - iullus antonius , son of mark antony and fulvia ( d. 2 bc ) - wang mang , usurper of the han dynasty and emperor of the xin dynasty ( d. 23 ) deaths . - tullia , daughter of cicero ( b . 79 bc or 78 bc ) - titus labienus ( b. c. 100 bc ) - publius attius varus - gnaeus pompeius , son of pompey the great ( b. c. 75 bc ) - quintus fabius maximus
