Shilin District     shilin district is a district of taipei , republic of china . it is a residential district , and famous residents include the late chiang kai-shek , former president of the republic of china . the district is divided into 51 villages ( 里 ) , which are further divided up into 987 villages ( 鄰 ) . the culture of taipei is said to be started in shilin district . shilin has three universities : ming chuan university , soochow university , and the chinese culture university . some international schools , like the taipei american school , taipei japanese school , and taipei european school are in this district . the district is also has vocational colleges , four senior high schools , eight junior high schools , and twenty elementary schools . other websites . - shilin district office , taipei city
