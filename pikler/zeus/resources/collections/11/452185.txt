Cuisine of the Southern United States     the cuisine of the southern united states is food prepared across the southern united states . foods prepared or from the southern united states include corn , tomatoes , squash along with other fruits or vegetables . much of creole or cajun cuisine is based on france , west africa and the caribbean . traditional southern meals usually involve catfish , fried chicken , butter beans , biscuits and cornbread . the restaurants serving southern food include popeyes chicken & biscuits , church 's chicken and kentucky fried chicken .
