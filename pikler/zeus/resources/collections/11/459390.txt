Transport in Hong Kong     hong kong has a complex transport network . it includes both public and private transport . over 90 % of the daily use is on public transport . that is the highest rate in the world . the octopus card is an electronic money system . it became available in september 1997. passengers can use it in every station of the mass transit railway ( mtr ) system . the card can be used to pay for public transport , parking meters , convenience stores , supermarkets , fast-food restaurants and most vending machines . related pages . - mtr - octopus card other websites . - wang l h , `` in search of a sustainable transport development strategy for hong kong '' - hong kong highways department - hong kong transport department - vehicular tunnels in hong kong - e & e bus - transport statistics â€“ census and statistics department
