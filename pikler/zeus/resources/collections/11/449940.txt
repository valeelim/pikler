Manuel Jiménez de Parga     manuel jiménez de parga y cabrera ( april 9 , 1929 - may 6 , 2014 ) was a spanish lawyer , politician and diplomat . he was professor in charge of the department of political law at the university of madrid ( 1956 ) and professor in opposition of the faculty of law of the university of barcelona ( 1957-1977 ) . he was dean of the faculty of law ( 1976 ) and rector of the university of barcelona ( 1976-1977 ) . de parga was minister of labour ( 1977-78 ) and ambassador-permanent representative of spain to the ilo ( geneva , 1978-1981 ) and president of the inter-american conference of labour ( 1980 ) . he was born in granada , spain . de parga died in madrid , spain , aged 85 .
