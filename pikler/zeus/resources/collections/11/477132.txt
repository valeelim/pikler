Rosemary's Baby    rosemary 's baby rosemary 's baby is a 1968 american horror movie . it is set in new york city . it is based on the novel of the same name . this movie is about a young housewife who has a baby . the baby turns out to be a child of the devil . mia farrow plays rosemary . ruth gordon plays minnie . the movie was well received by critics upon its release .
