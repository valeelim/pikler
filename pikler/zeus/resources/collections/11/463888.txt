Siri Paya     siri paya or paya is a traditional dish of pakistan and india . it is especially popular at various festivals and treated to special guests and get togethers . paya means feet in urdu and hindi . the main ingredients of the dish are the trotters or hooves of a cow , goat , water buffalo or lamb ; cooked with various spices . it is cooked to a thick , soup-like form and eaten with special `` naan '' bread dipped into it . some of the best and most famous siri paya in south asia are made in the taxali gate area of lahore city .
