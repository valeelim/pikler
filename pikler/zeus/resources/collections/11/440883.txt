Ronald Reagan Building and International Trade Center     the ronald reagan building and international trade center , is a large federal building and foreign trade organization center located in washington , d.c.. it was opened on may 8 , 1998. it is named after former united states president ronald reagan . it is the first federal building in washington , d.c. designed for both governmental and private society reasons . it is the second largest government building in the washington metropolitan area . a piece of the berlin wall is in the building .
