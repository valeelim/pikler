Georg von Tiesenhausen     georg f. von tiesenhausen ( born may 18 , 1914 ) is a retired german-american rocket scientist . after being brought to the united states in 1953 as part of operation paperclip , he was part of wernher von braun 's team at the u.s. army , and later , nasa . he is known to first complete design of the lunar rover and made a variety of other contributions to the space program . he now lives in huntsville , alabama .
