Dorothy Cheney     dorothy “ dodo ” bundy cheney ( september 1 , 1916 – november 23 , 2014 ) was an american tennis player . she played most of her tennis at the los angeles tennis club . in 1938 , cheney became the first american to win the women 's singles title at the australian championships , defeating dorothy stevenson in the final . cheney was born in los angeles , california . cheney died on november 23 , 2014 in escondido , california at the age of 98 .
