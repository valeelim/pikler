Claes Lövgrens     claes lövgrens is a dansband from billesholm in sweden . the band was established in 1994. in the early 2010s , the band has scored album chart successes in sweden . the band is named after singer claes lövgren . in 2007 , the band 's drummer johan fredriksson was awarded the `` drummer of the year '' . claes lövgrens has played together since 1994. since new-year 2002-2003 , the band is a full-time band , touring all over sweden discography . albums . - rosor från himlen - 2001 - dansvänliga låtar - 2005 - dansvänliga låtar 2 - 2008 - vi lever nu - 2010 - bästa - 2013 - vi börjar igen ! - 2014 other websites . - claes lövgrens ' website
