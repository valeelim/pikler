Mathieu Kérékou     mathieu kérékou , ( 2 september 1933 – 14 october 2015 ) was a beninese politician . he was president of benin from 1972 to 1991 and again from 1996 to 2006. after seizing power in a military coup , he ruled the country for 17 years . he was defeated in the 1991 presidential election , but was returned to the presidency in the 1996 election and controversially re-elected in 2001 .
