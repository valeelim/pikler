Joyride (album)    joyride ( album ) joyride is the third studio album by roxette . the album has the songs `` joyride '' and `` fading like a flower '' . `` joyride '' hit # 1 on the `` billboard '' hot 100. in june 1991 , it became the best selling album in europe for 2 weeks . this album was released on march 28 , 1991 .
