Dorothy Morrison     dorothy morrison , ( born january 3 , 1919 in los angeles , california ) , is an american actress . she appeared in a few our gang movies during the silent era . her older brother was ernie morrison , another member of the movie series . some of her movies include `` the champeen '' , `` seein ' things '' , `` the love bug '' , `` is n't life terrible ? '' and `` hearts in dixie '' . her last movie was a short called `` sleepless hollow '' in 1936. other websites . - dorothy morrison at the american film institute
