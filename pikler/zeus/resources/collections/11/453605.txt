Reuven Rivlin     reuven rivlin ( born 9 september 1939 ) is the current president of israel . rivlin was born in jerusalem when it was part of british mandatory palestine . before entering politics he was a lawyer . he was elected to the jerusalem city council in 1978. rivlin worked for el al airlines serving on the executive council from 1981 to 1986. he was a member of the knesset , the parliament of israel , for many years , elected first in 1988. he was also speaker of the knesset and minister of communications of israel . on 10th of june 2014 he won elections for the post of president of israel . rivlin is married and has four children .
