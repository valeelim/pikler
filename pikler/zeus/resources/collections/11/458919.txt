USS Chicago (SSN-721)    uss chicago ( ssn-721 ) uss `` chicago '' ( ssn-721 ) is a submarine , the fourth ship of the united states navy to be named for the city of chicago , illinois . the contract to build her was awarded to newport news shipbuilding and dry dock company in newport news , virginia on 13 august 1981 and her keel was laid down on 5 january 1983. she was launched on 13 october 1984 .
