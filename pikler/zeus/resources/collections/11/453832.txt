Liz Phair     elizabeth clark `` liz '' phair ( born april 17 , 1967 ) is an american singer-songwriter and a guitarist . her career began in the early 1990s . she began by self-releasing audio cassettes using the nickname of girly sound . her 1993 debut studio album `` exile in guyville '' was released with critical acclaim . she has sold nearly three million records worldwide . phair was born in new haven , connecticut . she was adopted and grew up in chicago suburb winnetka , illinois .
