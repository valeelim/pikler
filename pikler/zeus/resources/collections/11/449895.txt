Killer Queen     `` killer queen '' is a song by british rock group queen . it was featured on their 1974 album `` sheer heart attack '' , and also appears on , `` greatest hits '' . when released as a single , `` killer queen '' was queen 's breakthrough hit . it reached number two in the united kingdom and number 12 in the united states . musicians : - freddie mercury - lead and backing vocals , piano , jangle piano - brian may - electric guitar , backing vocals - roger taylor - drums , triangle , chimes , backing vocals - john deacon - bass guitar
