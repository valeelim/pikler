SKS     sks is a soviet union made semi-automatic rifle . it was designed in 1943 by sergei gavrilovich simonov . in the 1950s it was replaced with the ak-47 . the sks remained in second-line service for decades . it is still used as a ceremonial arm today . the sks is currently popular in the civilian surplus market in many countries . this includes the united states , canada and new zealand . it was one of the first weapons designed to use the m-43 7.62Ã—39mm round , which was also used later in the ak-47 .
