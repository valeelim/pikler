Máximo Cajal López     máximo cajal lópez ( 27 february 1935 – 3 april 2014 ) was a spanish diplomat and ambassador . he was the spanish ambassador to guatemala in 1980 and was one of the two survivors of the burning of the spanish embassy . he was also ambassador to sweden and france as well as permanent representative of spain to nato . cajal lópez died on 3 april 2014 in his hometown of madrid . he was 79 years old . other websites . - máximo cajal lópez at the fride website
