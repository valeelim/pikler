Sam Smith     samuel frederick `` sam '' smith ( born may 19 , 1992 ) is a british singer-songwriter . he became famous in october 2012 when the song `` latch '' by disclosure came out . in 2014 smith had a single , `` stay with me '' , which became a major international success . his 2015 song `` writing 's on the wall '' was recorded for the 2015 james bond movie `` spectre '' and won a golden globe award and an academy award for best original song . smith was born in london . he came out as gay in may 2014 .
