GMC Acadia     gmc acadia is a full-sized crossover vehicle . it is manufactured by gmc . it went on sale beginning in december 2006. it seats seven or eight passengers , depending on the model . the gmc acadia replaces four vehicles the envoy suv and its xl package , the isuzu asscender suv and the pontiac montana sv6 minivan . the gmc acadia was improved in 2012 for the 2013 model year . the second generation acadia uses the body shell of gm 's discontinued saturn outlook .
