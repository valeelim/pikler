Małgorzata Braunek     małgorzata braunek ( 30 january 1947 – 23 june 2014 ) was a polish actress of movies and stage . she won a best supporting actress award at the polish film awards for her role in `` tulipany '' ( 2004 ) . she also had roles in movies such as `` the third part of the night '' ( 1971 ) , `` the devil '' ( 1972 ) and `` the deluge '' ( 1974 ) . braunek was born in szamotuły . she graduated from the ludwik solski academy for the dramatic arts , warsaw in 1969. she was married to movie director andrzej żuławski . they had a son actor and movie producer , xawery żuławski ( 1971 ) . she was a follower and teacher of zen buddhism . braunek died from cancer on 23 june 2014 , aged 67. she died in either warsaw or frankfurt , germany . other websites . - małgorzata braunek at culture.pl
