Three Dollar Bill, Y'all    three dollar bill , y'all three dollar bill , y'all $ is the debut studio album by american nu metal band , limp bizkit . `` three dollar bill , y'all $ '' was released on july 1 , 1997. the album featured the singles `` counterfeit '' , `` sour '' , and a cover of george michaels ' song `` faith '' . guitarist wes borland said in an interview that george michael hated limp bizkit 's cover and `` hates us for doing it '' . the album had attracted controversy when interscope records paid a portland , oregon radio station $ 5,000 to play the single `` counterfeit '' fifty times which attracted criticism of `` payola '' toward both the band and the label .
