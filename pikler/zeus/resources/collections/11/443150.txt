75th Infantry Division (United States)    75th infantry division ( united states ) the 75th infantry division was a division of the united states army in world war ii . it was also active from 1952 to 1957 as a combat division of the united states army organized reserves . the 75th was activated 15 april 1943 , at fort leonard wood , missouri . in 1993 , the division was activated again as the 75th division ( training support ) in the army reserve , and remains active . in january 2003 , numerous units of the 75th division ( training support ) were mobilized to train other army reserve and national guard units deploying overseas in support of operation iraqi freedom/operation enduring freedom ( oif/oef ) . several of the division 's units remain mobilized even to the present day .
