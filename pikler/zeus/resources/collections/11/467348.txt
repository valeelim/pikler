Carlos Morales Troncoso     carlos morales troncoso ( 29 september 1940 – 25 october 2014 ) was a dominican politician . he served as vice–president of the dominican republic from 1986 to 1994 , and as minister of foreign affairs of the dominican republic from 2004 to 2014. troncoso died in houston , texas , united states from leukemia , aged 74 .
