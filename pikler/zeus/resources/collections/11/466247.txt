Big Daddy Kane     antonio hardy ( better known as big daddy kane ; born september 10 , 1968 ) is an american rapper . he began his rapping career in 1986 with rap group the juice crew . in the late 1980s , kane released his album `` it 's a big daddy thing '' . as of 2014 , that was his biggest album . that album included songs like `` smooth operator '' . kane was born in brooklyn , new york . other websites . - official website
