Dolores del Río     dolores del río ( august 3 , 1905 – april 11 , 1983 ) was a mexican movie actress . she was a hollywood movie star in the 1920s and 1930s . she was important to the golden age of mexican cinema during the 1940s and 1950s . in the 1928 movie `` ramona '' , del río played the title character . she also played the title character in `` evangeline '' ( 1929 ) . she played madame du barry in the motion picture of that name . del río was born in durango , mexico . she died in newport beach , california at 77 of liver disease .
