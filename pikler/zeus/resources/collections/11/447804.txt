C. Delores Tucker     cynthia delores tucker was an african-american politician . she was known for her civil rights activism and for being against gangsta rap music . she died when she was 78 on october 12 , 2005. in 1971 , tucker became the first black female united states secretary of state . in 1990 she started the african-american women for reproductive freedom with 15 other african american women and men . in 1996 tucker was chosen by `` people '' magazine as one of `` 25 of the most intriguing people in the world '' . tucker started `` vital issues : the journal of african american speeches '' .
