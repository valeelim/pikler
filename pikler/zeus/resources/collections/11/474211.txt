Morteza Ahmadi     morteza haj seyed ahmadi ( ‎ ; 1 november 1924 – 21 december 2014 ) was an iranian actor , singer and writer . ahmadi was known for his works in `` autobus '' , `` madrak-e jorm '' and `` khane-kharab '' . he was born in tehran . ahmadi died on 21 december 2014 at his private house in tehran , aged 90 .
