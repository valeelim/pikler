Patrick Harvie     patrick harvie ( born 18 march 1973 ) is a scottish politician . he is a co-convener ( leader ) of the scottish green party . he is the leader of the party in the scottish parliament . the other co-convener is maggie chapman . he became a member of the scottish parliament ( msp ) for the glasgow region at the elections in may 2003.. he was first elected co-convener of the scottish green party in 2008. other websites . - patrick harvie 's page at the scottish parliament website - patrick harvie on scottish greens website - patrick harvie 's website
