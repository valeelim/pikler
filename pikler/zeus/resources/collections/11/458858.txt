Paul George     paul george ( born may 2 , 1990 ) is an american professional basketball player . he plays with the indiana pacers of the national basketball association ( nba ) . george played high school basketball for knight high school before playing two seasons of college basketball at fresno state . he was drafted by the pacers as the 10th overall pick of the 2010 nba draft . in 2013 , he was selected to play in his first nba all-star game , received the nba most improved player award , and was named to all-nba third team and all-defensive second team . other websites . - fresno state bulldogs bio
