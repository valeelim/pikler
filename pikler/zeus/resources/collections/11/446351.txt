Pam Tillis     pamela yvonne `` pam '' tillis ( born july 24 , 1957 ) is an american country music singer and actress . she began her career in 1981. in 1991 , she had a song in the top 40 on the `` billboard '' hot country songs for the first time . her father is country musician mel tillis . tillis has released many albums . two of them are `` put yourself in my place '' and `` sweetheart 's dance '' . tillis was born in florida . she was raised in nashville , tennessee . her father is country music singer mel tillis .
