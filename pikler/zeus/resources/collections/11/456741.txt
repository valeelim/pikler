Brazil v Germany (2014 FIFA World Cup)    brazil v germany ( 2014 fifa world cup ) brazil v germany , played on 8 july 2014 , was an association football match played between brazil and germany in the semi-finals of the 2014 fifa world cup . germany 's 7–1 win was the largest in fifa world cup semi-final history . to some , it became known as the miracle of belo horizonte . to others it was called the mineirazo or `` mineiraço '' , comparing it with the famous `` maracanazo '' , in which brazil lost an important game against uruguay in the 1950 world cup . the team met for the second time in world cup history , with the first game being the 2002 fifa world cup final . in the previous game against colombia , neymar suffered a fractured vertebra and thiago silva was banned due to him getting too many yellow cards in the tournament .
