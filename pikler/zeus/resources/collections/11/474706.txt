Any Given Sunday     any given sunday is a 1999 american drama movie . it was directed by oliver stone and was produced by richard donner , oliver stone , dan halsted , lauren shuler donner , and clayton townsend . `` any given sunday '' was released on december 22 , 1999. the movie is based on the pat toomay novel , `` on any given sunday '' . the movie gained mixed reviews from critics and holds a 50 % rating on rotten tomatoes . cast . - al pacino as tony d'amato - cameron diaz as christina pagniacci - dennis quaid as jack `` cap '' rooney - james woods as dr. harvey mandrake - jamie foxx as `` steamin '' willie beamen - ll cool j as julian `` j-man '' washington - lawrence taylor as luther `` shark '' lavay - jim brown as montezuma monroe - aaron eckhart as nick crozier - matthew modine as dr. oliver `` ollie '' powers - john c. mcginley as jack rose - lela rochon as vanessa struthers - lauren holly as cindy rooney - ann-margret as margaret pagniacci - elizabeth berkley as mandy murphy - charlton heston as commissioner - clifton davis as mayor tyrone smalls
