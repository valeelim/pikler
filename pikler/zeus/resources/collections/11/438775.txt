Poldine Carlo     poldine demoski carlo ( born december 5 , 1920 ) is an american author . she is an elder of the koyukon people of alaska . she is a founding member of the fairbanks native association ( fna ) . she was on the alaska bicentennial commission ( abc ) board . she was a consultant for the tanana chiefs conference ( tcc ) . she is the author of `` nulato : an indian life on the yukon '' . the book was dedicated to her son , stewart , who died in 1975 in an auto accident . carlo was born on december 5 , 1920 in nulato , territory of alaska . she married william `` bill '' carlo in 1940. they have eight children , five sons ( william , jr. , kenny , walter , glenn , and stewart ) and three daughters ( dorothy , lucy , and kathleen ) . she lives in fairbanks , alaska . a building in downtown fairbanks , owned by the fna , was named the poldine carlo building in her honor .
