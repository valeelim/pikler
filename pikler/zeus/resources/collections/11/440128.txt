DVD-R     dvd-r is a recordable dvd format . it uses a thin organic dye layer which is recorded by a high-power laser . the laser bleaches the recorded areas , creating reflective areas that can then be played back with a low-power laser . a dvd-r can be recorded with dvd-video , dvd-audio , or dvd-rom content.a dvd-r typically has a storage capacity of 4.7 gb . pioneer has also developed an 8.5 gb dual layer version , dvd-r dl , which appeared on the market in 2005. dvd-r ( w ) is one of three competing industry standard dvd recordable formats ; the others are dvd+r ( w ) and dvd-ram . a dvd-r can be written only once , in contrast with a rewritable dvd formats such as dvd-rw or dvd+rw which can be rewritten multiple ( 1000+ ) times .
