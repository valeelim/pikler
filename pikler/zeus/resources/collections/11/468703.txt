Woods of Ypres     woods of ypres were a canadian blackened doom metal band from ontario . woods of ypres was formed in windsor , ontario in 2002 by david gold , aaron palmer , and brian mcmanus . the band released their first ep , ' , in october 2002. in december , the band would move to toronto to work on their first full-length album . through gold 's record label , krankenhaus records , the band released their first full-length album , `` pursuit of the sun & allure of the earth '' on august 14 , 2004. their third album , ' , was released on january 5 , 2008. after gold returned from a work stint in south korea , he moved the band to his hometown of sault ste . marie , ontario in 2008. they then began working on their fourth album , `` '' the next year . the album was released on november 13th , 2009. on october 18 , 2010 , the band signed with earache records . `` woods iv : the green album '' was reissued through earache records in march 2011 . on june 9 , 2011 , the band played their last concert in richmond , virginia . on december 21 , 2011 , frontman david gold was involved in a car accident near barrie , ontario and died . the band would release their fifth and last studio album , `` '' on february 27 , 2012 in europe and on april 16 , 2012 in north america . on april 20 , 2013 , the band won a juno award for metal/hard music album of the year for `` woods 5 : grey skies & electric light '' .
