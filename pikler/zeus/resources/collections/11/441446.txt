Chinese people     chinese people are the various ethnic groups from china , including taiwan . the largest of these groups is the han chinese . this group is often called `` chinese '' or `` ethnic chinese '' in english . large numbers of han chinese live in countries other than china . they may make up as much as 19 % of the world 's population . other ethnic groups in china include the zhuang , hui , manchu , and uyghurs , among many others . the people 's republic of china ( prc ) officially recognises 56 ethnic groups . taiwan ( republic of china , roc ) officially recognises 14 tribes of taiwanese aborigines . the list of ethnic groups in china includes the major ethnic groups of china ( prc ) and taiwan . related pages . - chinese american - british chinese
