41 BC     year 41 bc was either a common year starting on wednesday or thursday or a leap year starting on tuesday , wednesday or thursday of the julian calendar . at the time , it was known as the year of the consulship of antonius and vatia . events . - in the perusine war the city of sentinum is captured and destroyed by quintus salvidienus rufus . - mark antony meets cleopatra vii . they form an alliance . births . - gaius asinius gallus saloninus , consul under caesar augustus ( d. ad 33 ) deaths . - arsinoe iv of egypt
