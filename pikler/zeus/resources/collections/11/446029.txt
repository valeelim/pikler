Paul Lüönd     paul lüönd ( 15 april 1950 – 3 april 2014 ) was a swiss musician and politician . he formed a group with his older two brothers called `` mosibuäbe '' . lüönd was born on 15 april 1950. he grew up in the mosi on ingenbohl . he died on 3 april 2014 from a long illness in switzerland , aged 63 .
