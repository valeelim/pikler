Jack Ingram     jack ingram is an american country music singer . his first single on the u.s. country top 40 chart was `` wherever you are '' . that song hit number 63 on the billboard hot 100 chart . ingram has had six other songs on the country top 40 : `` love you , '' a cover version of hinder 's `` lips of an angel , '' `` measure of a man , '' `` maybe she 'll get lonely , '' `` that 's a man '' and `` barefoot and crazy .
