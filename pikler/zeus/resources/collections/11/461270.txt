Chloe (movie)    chloe ( movie ) chloe is a 2009 american-canadian-french erotic drama mystery movie . this movie is about the title character doing business as a call girl . amanda seyfried plays the title character . julianne moore plays gynecologist catherine . liam neeson plays catherine 's husband david , who is a college professor . this movie was released on march 26 , 2010 in the united states . the movie got mixed reviews .
