Marc Maron     marc david maron ( born september 27 , 1963 ) is an american stand-up comedian , podcast host and actor . he has been host of `` the marc maron show '' and co-host of both `` morning sedition '' and `` breakroom live '' . since 2013 , he starred in his own show `` maron '' . maron was born in jersey city , new jersey . he is jewish . he grew up in new jersey , alaska and albuquerque .
