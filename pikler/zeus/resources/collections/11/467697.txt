Shin Hae-chul     shin hae-chul ( 6 may 1968 – 27 october 2014 ) , also known by the nicknames demon lord ( 마왕 ) and cult leader ( 교주 ) , was an avant-garde south korean pop music performer . he was known for being a member of the pop group `` n.ex.t '' . hae-chul died in seoul , south korea from a heart attack , aged 46 .
