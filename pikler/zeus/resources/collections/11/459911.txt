Moody Church     the moody church ( often mistakenly referred to as moody memorial church ) is a historic protestant ( christian ) church in the lincoln park neighborhood of chicago , illinois . construction began in 1924 and completed 1 year later , with dedication of the building on november 8 , 1925. the covering area is 140 feet by 225 feet . other websites . - the moody church - the moody church 's history website - moody church media resource library - information from the billy graham center 's archive
