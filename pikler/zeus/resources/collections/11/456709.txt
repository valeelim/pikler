Typhoon Megi (2010)    typhoon megi ( 2010 ) typhoon megi ( called juan in the philippines ) was a strong tropical cyclone in the western pacific ocean . it was one of the most intense tropical cyclones ever . the storm started on october 13 , 2010. it became a typhoon the following day . the storm first touched land on luzon on october 18. thirty-one people died . damage was over $ 255 million ( 2010 usd ) . the storm also affected taiwan and southeastern china . overall damage was $ 709 million . almost 70 people were killed . megi had winds sustained at 185 mph .
