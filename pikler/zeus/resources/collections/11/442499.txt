Robby Müller     robby müller ( born 4 april 1940 ) is a dutch cinematographer . he has worked with director wim wenders . he is known for his works in `` to live and die in la '' , sally potter 's `` the tango lesson '' , `` my brother tom '' , `` breaking the waves '' and `` dancer in the dark '' . müller was born in willemstad , curaçao .
