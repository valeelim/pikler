Jerry Sloan     gerald eugene `` jerry '' sloan ( born march 28 , 1942 in mcleansboro , illinois ) is a former national basketball association player and head coach , and a member of the basketball hall of fame . he played for the chicago bulls from 1966 to 1976. he soon coached them from 1979 to 1982. he also coached the utah jazz from 1988 to 2011. in april 2016 , sloan was diagnosed with parkinson 's disease and lewy body dementia .
