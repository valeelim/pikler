Kathleen O'Malley     kathleen o'malley ( born march 31 , 1924 ) is an american retired actress . her career began as a child . her father was actor pat o'malley . her screen debut came in 1926 in one of his movies , `` my old dutch '' . she appeared in many television series such as `` cover girl '' , `` lady on a train '' , `` two tickets to broadway '' , `` maverick '' , `` rawhide '' and `` general hospital '' .
