Taney County, Missouri    taney county , missouri taney county is a county in southwestern missouri . as of the 2010 census , the population was 51,675. its county seat is forsyth . the county was officially organized on january 4 , 1837. it was named in honor of roger brooke taney , the fifth chief justice of the u.s. supreme court , most remembered for later delivering the majority opinion in `` dred scott v . sandford . '' taney county includes the popular tourist destination city of branson . it is included in the branson , mo micropolitan statistical area and is also included in the springfield-branson , mo metropolitan statistical area . with a wide range of live music shows , restaurants and shopping locations , taney county also has table rock , taneycomo and bull shoals lakes . another attraction is the mark twain national forest .
