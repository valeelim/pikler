Paige (wrestler)    paige ( wrestler ) saraya-jade bevis ( born 17 august 1992 ) is an english professional wrestler and porn star . she currently works for the wwe where she competes under the ring name paige . she is a two-time wwe divas champion and one-time nxt women 's champion . she is also known under the ring name britani knight , which she used while performing on the independent circuit . on the april 7 , 2014 episode of `` raw '' , paige made her main roster debut in the wwe . she gave her congratulations to wwe divas champion aj lee on her successful title defense at `` wrestlemania xxx '' . aj challenged paige to a match and put her championship on the line . in the match , paige was able to defeat aj and win her first divas championship in wwe . when she won the championship , she became the first female wrestler to hold a developmental ( nxt women 's ) and primary ( wwe divas ) championship at the same time and also became the youngest divas champion . on the july 30 , 2014 episode of `` raw '' , aj lee returned to the wwe and defeated paige for the divas championship .
