Geoffrey Bayldon     geoffrey bayldon ( 7 january 1924 – 10 may 2017 ) was a british actor . he is known for having the lead role in `` catweazle '' ( 1970–72 ) . bayldon denied the role of both the first and second doctors in the long-running bbc science fiction television series `` doctor who '' . he was born in leeds , west riding of yorkshire . bayldon died on 10 may 2017 in london at the age of 93. his partner of many years , fellow actor alan rowe , predeceased him in 2000 .
