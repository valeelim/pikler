Smegma     smegma is a substance that forms in the sex organs of mammals . it is made of epithelial cells , skin cells , and moisture . it is found around the foreskin of the penis in males , or in the folds of skin around the vulva in females . in males , the smegma acts like a lubricant which makes sexual intercourse easier . it can be white through to yellow in colour . smegma does not cause any harm , and normal washing will keep it under control ; in fact too much washing may cause other problems .
