Alter Echo     alter echo is a third-person action video game published by thq and developed by outrage entertainment . it was released for the playstation 2 and xbox . it was released on august 19 , 2003 in north america and on october 3 , 2003 in the pal region . the received mixed reviews from critics . ign rated the game a 6.8 out of 10. gamespot gave the game a 7.5 out of 10 and jeff gerstmann of gamespot wrote that `` it would have been nice to have seen some more variety in the environments , and the instances of backtracking through areas you 've already visited are disappointing . but the game 's exciting , free-form combat makes up for those deficiencies .
