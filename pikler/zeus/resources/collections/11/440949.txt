Alain Resnais     alain resnais ( ; 3 june 1922 – 1 march 2014 ) was a french movie director , actor , editor and screenwriter . his career has lasted over more than six decades . he was born in vannes , morbihan . he was first married to florence malraux ( daughter of the french statesman and writer andré malraux ) . resnais died on 1 march 2014 in paris . he was 91 years old . he was outlived by his second wife , actress sabine azéma . at first , resnais wanted to become a comedian actor , but he ended up making short films . most of these films were produced in the late 1940s and early 1950s . many are documentaries . the best known are probably van gogh ( 1949 ) , guernica ( 1950 ) and night and fog ( 1955 ) . his longer films include `` hiroshima mon amour '' ( 1959 ) , `` last year at marienbad '' ( 1961 ) , and `` muriel '' ( 1963 ) . all of the films are important for the history of cinema . resnais was awarded multiple prizes for his work , including several césar awards .
