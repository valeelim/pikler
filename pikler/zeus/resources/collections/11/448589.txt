Antonio Pica     antonio de la santísima trinidad pica serrano ( march 21 , 1933 — april 26 , 2014 ) was a spanish actor . pica was born in jerez de la frontera , cádiz , spain . he appeared in over 60 movies since his career began in 1966. he worked with sophia loren and christopher plummer . he is known for his roles in `` delusions of grandeur '' ( 1971 ) , `` travels with my aunt '' ( 1972 ) and `` the killer with a thousand eyes '' ( 1974 ) . pica died at a hospital in el puerto de santa maría , spain from an illness , aged 81. movies . - `` django kill '' ( 1967 ) ... tembler 's henchman - `` bandidos '' ... 7 - `` ringo the lone rider '' ( 1968 ) ... sheriff - `` a bullet for sandoval '' ( 1969 ) ... sam paul - `` delusions of grandeur '' ( 1971 ) ... grand d'espagne - `` travels with my aunt '' ( 1972 ) ... elegant man - `` the heroes '' ( 1973 ) ... dietrich - `` los ojos azules de la muneca rota '' ( 1973 ) ... inspector pierre - `` vengeance of the zombies '' ( 1973 ) ... superintendent hawkins - `` the mark of zorro '' ( 1974 ) ... major de colignac - `` licántropo '' ( 1996 ) ... comisario lacombe
