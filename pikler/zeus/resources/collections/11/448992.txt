Districts of Luxembourg     the former three districts of luxembourg ( , , ) were the top-level administrative divisions of the grand duchy of luxembourg . the districts were divided into cantons : the districts ( and cantons ) were created on 24 february 1843. in 1857 , the `` mersch '' district was created from the cantons of mersch and redange . however , this fourth district was abolished in 1867. in october 2015 , the districts were abolished .
