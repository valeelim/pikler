Majid Nizami     majid nizami ( ) ( april 3 , 1928 - july 26 , 2014 ) , was a pakistani chief editor and publisher . he worked for nawa-i-waqt group of publications of pakistan . nawa-i-waqt was founded by majid 's brother , hameed nizami . he was also the chairman of nazaria-i-pakistan trust . nizami died in lahore , pakistan from heart disease , aged 86 .
