Super battleship (warship)    super battleship ( warship ) super battleship is an unofficial class of battleships . it includes the largest types constructed or planned with a displacing of roughly 60,000 tons or more . only two such battleships were ever made . many more were planned by several navies of the world before and during the second world war . their design included thick armor and large guns . they were thought to be the ideal warships for naval battle . the success of the aircraft carriers compromised their use . only two were ever built ( both by the japanese empire ) . both were sunk during world war ii .
