Trailways     trailways transportation system , or trailways , is an american group of bus companies . its headquarters are in fairfax , virginia . the predecessor to trailways was founded on february 5 , 1936 by burlington transportation company , santa fe trails transportation company and other travel companies . its closest competition is greyhound lines . trailways members are spread across north america . they provide bus tours , charter bus service and route scheduling services .
