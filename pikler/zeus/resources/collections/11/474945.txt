Per-Ingvar Brånemark     per-ingvar brånemark ( may 3 , 1929 – december 20 , 2014 ) was a swedish orthopedic surgeon and research professor . he was called the `` father of modern dental implantology '' . the `` brånemark osseointegration center '' ( boc ) , named after its founder , was founded in 1989 in gothenburg , sweden . brånemark died at the age of 85 in gothenburg . other websites . - nobel biocare convention 2007 - brånemark osseointegration center - bacterial persistence in dentoalveolar bone following extraction : a microbiological study and implications for dental implant treatment - clinical implant dentistry and related research - the man who made people smile - nobelbiocare.com
