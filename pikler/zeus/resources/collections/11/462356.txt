Ventricles of brain     the ventricles are hollow spaces in the human brain . they contain cerebrospinal fluid ( csf ) . there are four ventricles in a human brain . this is the ventricular system . it is a continuation of the central canal of the spinal cord . the ventricle lining is an epithelium-like membrane . the ventricles are interconnected , allowing the flow of cerebrospinal fluid . csf is produced by cells of the lining .
