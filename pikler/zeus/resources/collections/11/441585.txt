Mediant (mathematics)    mediant ( mathematics ) in mathematics , the mediant of two fractions is it is commonly referred to as the freshman sum , due to it being a common mistake when people learn about the addition of fractions . the operation is between two fractions , therefore the way these fractions are represented influence the mediant . for example , the mediant of 1/1 and 1/2 is 2/3 , but the mediant of 2/2 and 1/2 is 3/4 , even though the sum of both is 3/2 .
