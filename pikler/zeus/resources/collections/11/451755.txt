Brian Drummond     brian drummond ( born august 10 , 1969 in salmon arm , british columbia ) is a canadian voice actor and former stage actor . he is best known for voicing the roles of vegeta , yajirobe , and vegito in the ocean studios english dub of the anime series `` dragon ball z '' . he has also voiced dubs for yuna roma seiran in `` mobile suit gundam seed destiny '' as well as ryuk in `` death note '' . he currently voices maurice , billy , boss ember and el diablos nacho in slugterra . other websites . - brian drummond at the internet movie database
