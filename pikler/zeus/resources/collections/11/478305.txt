Bella and the Bulldogs     bella and the bulldogs is an american sitcom airs on nickelodeon . the series premiered january 17 , 2015. on march 4 , 2015 , nickelodeon ordered a second season . the series follows bella , a cheerleader who lives in texas . her life changes when she becomes the new quarterback for her school football team , the bulldogs . cast . main characters . - brec bassinger as bella dawson - coy stewart as troy - jackie radinsky as sawyer - buddy handleson as newt - lilimar hernandez as sophie - haley tju as pepper recurring characters . - rio mangini as ace mcfumbles - dorien wilson as coach russell - annie tedesco as carrie dawson
