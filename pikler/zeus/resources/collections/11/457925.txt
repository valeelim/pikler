Fecal Matter (band)    fecal matter ( band ) fecal matter was a short lived punk rock band from aberdeen , washington . the band was made in 1985 by kurt cobain , the future lead guitarist of the band nirvana , and dale crover . the band made a demo tape named `` illiteracy will prevail '' . that demo tape was unofficially released in 1985. members . - kurt cobain – vocals , guitar - dale crover – bass guitar , drums - greg hokanson – drums - buzz osborne – bass guitar - mike dillard – drums
