Papa Don't Preach    papa do n't preach `` papa do n't preach '' is a pop single from madonna . the song deals with abortion and teenage pregnancy . the song was on her third studio album `` true blue '' . it was also on her album `` the immaculate collection '' . `` papa do n't preach '' was released in june 1986. it was # 1 on the `` billboard '' hot 100 for two weeks .
