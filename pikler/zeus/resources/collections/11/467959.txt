Lee Kings     the lee kings was a pop group from sweden . the band was established in 1964 as lenne & the lee kings . the band scored several hits at the `` tio i topp '' chart . the group originally consisted of lenne broberg , bengt dahlén , bjarne möller , olle nordström and lasse sandgren . in 1966 , olle nordström left the band and was repalced with mike watson . in 1967 , eveen lasse sandgren left the band and was repalced with tony walter , and for a while even bengt dahlén and bjarne möller , who was replaced with johnny lundin . hit songs . - stop the music - l.o.d .
