Vincent C. Gray     vincent condol gray ( born november 8 , 1942 ) is an american politician . he served as the seventh mayor of washington , d.c. from 2011 to 2015. gray served as chairman of the council of the district of columbia , and as councilmember for ward 7. in the 1990s he also served as director of the dc department of human services . on april 1 , 2014 , he was defeated in the democratic primary by muriel bowser .
