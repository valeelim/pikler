Metropolismania     metropolismania is a city-building/strategy game video game published by natsume and developed by media factory . the game was released on september 27 , 2001 in japan , october 3 , 2002 in north america , and on november 8 , 2002 in europe . the main objective of the game is to talk with npcs to populate various towns . a sequel called `` metropolismania 2 '' was released on august 21 , 2007 in north america and on july 13 , 2006 in japan . another sequel , called `` metropolismania 3 '' , was released for the playstation portable in japan only . ign rated the game a 6.5 out of 10 gamespot rated the game a 7 out of 10 stars
