Alexander Payne     alexander payne ( born constantine alexander payne ; february 10 , 1961 ) is an american movie director , screenwriter , and producer . he is best known for his movies `` election '' ( 1999 ) , `` about schmidt '' ( 2002 ) , `` sideways '' ( 2004 ) , `` the descendants '' ( 2011 ) , and `` nebraska '' ( 2013 ) . alexander payne was born in omaha , nebraska . he married canadian actress sandra oh in 2003. they divorced in 2006 .
