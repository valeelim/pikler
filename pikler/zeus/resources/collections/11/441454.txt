Space debris     space debris is the collection of useless objects that are in orbit around earth , like broken satellites , empty rockets , pieces of metal , and many more . because of how much space debris there is , collisions happen between space debris and sometimes even operational spacecraft , creating more debris . since the start of space programs and the launch of sputnik 1 , norad has an active list of all known space launches and objects that are in earth orbit , including waste parts like booster rockets . this is the space object catalog . related pages . - kessler syndrome other websites . - space debris -citizendium
