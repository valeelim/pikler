Holler If Ya Hear Me     `` holler if ya hear me '' is the first single off american rapper 2pac 's second studio album `` strictly 4 my n.i.g.g.a.z. '' . the song samples the songs `` do it any way you wan na '' by people 's choice and `` rebel without a pause '' by public enemy . the song is about frustrations with black poverty , police injustice , and shakur 's perceived harassment from political figure dan quayle . the editor of `` vibe '' was quoted in time as stating that the song struck a chord with a large section of disaffected youth . other websites . - `` holler if ya hear me '' on youtube
