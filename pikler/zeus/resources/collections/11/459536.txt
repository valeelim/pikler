Raymond Gravel     raymond gravel ( november 4 , 1952 – august 11 , 2014 ) was an canadian catholic priest from the canadian province of quebec . he was formerly the member of parliament for the riding of repentigny , as a member of the bloc québécois . he was elected to the house of commons in a november 27 , 2006 by-election following the death of benoît sauvageau . gravel died of lung cancer in lanaudière , quebec . he was 61. other websites . - montreal gazette : `` controversial priest wants bloc repentigny nomination '' . `` the gazette '' , october 25 , 2006 .
