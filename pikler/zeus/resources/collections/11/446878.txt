Larry Pressler     larry lee pressler ( born march 29 , 1942 ) is an american politician . he was the united states senator of south dakota . he served as senator from 1979 to 1997. he was a member of the republican party . pressler ran for president of the united states in 1980 , but lost to ronald reagan during the primary . pressler announced in 2013 , that he would run for the senate seat as an independent . he soon lost the election . pressler was born in humboldt , south dakota .
