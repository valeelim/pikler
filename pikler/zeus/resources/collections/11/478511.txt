Division of Stirling     the division of stirling is an australian electoral division in the inner northern and beachside suburbs of perth , western australia . it includes balcatta , balga , carine , innaloo , nollamara , north beach , scarborough , stirling , trigg and yokine . it was set up in 1955 , and named after sir james stirling , the first governor of western australia . election results . - 2004 election results - 2007 election results - 2010 election results - 2013 election results other websites . - division of stirling - australian electoral commission
