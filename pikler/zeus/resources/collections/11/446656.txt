Scott Walker (singer)    scott walker ( singer ) scott walker ( born noel scott engel ; january 9 , 1943 ) is an american singer and record producer . he was born in hamilton , ohio . he became famous as the lead singer of the pop group the walker brothers in the 1960s . after the walkers brothers broke up walker released albums on his own . walker is currently signed to 4ad records . since 1965 he has lived in the united kingdom . walker is an only child . on february 25 , 2009 , there was a tribute concert to walker 's music in los angeles .
