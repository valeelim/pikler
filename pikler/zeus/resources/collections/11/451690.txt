Thermal radiation     thermal radiation is radiation that can be seen as either heat or light . it is a form of heat transfer that is moved from one place to another by electromagnetic radiation waves or rays . it does not require a form of matter to be transferred . for example a person in front of a fire can warm up because of the light of the fire , even if the air is cold . another example of thermal radiation is the heat that comes from the sun to the earth . other pages . - heat transfer - heat conduction - convection
