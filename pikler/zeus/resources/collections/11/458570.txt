Mukku Raju     mukku raju ( ) ( born sagiraju rajamraju ) ( died july 31 , 2014 ) was a tollywood movie actor . he started his career as choreographer but ended as an actor and has acted in over 1000 movies . he got nandi award for best character actor in 2008 for his movie `` 1940 lo oka gramam '' . raju died at the age of 83 after a short illness . his funeral will take place at cherukuvada .
