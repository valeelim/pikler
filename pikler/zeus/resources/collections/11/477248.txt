Gerard 't Hooft     gerardus `` gerard '' 't hooft ( born july 5 , 1946 ) is a dutch physicist . he shared the 1999 nobel prize in physics with his doctoral advisor martinus j. g. veltman . gerardus 't hooft is well known for his theories about gauge theory , black holes , and quantum gravity . he is married to albertha schik ( betteke ) and has two daughters , saskia and ellen . gerard 't hooft was born in den helder on july 5 , 1946 , but grew up in the hague . he was the middle child of a family of three . after gerard 't hooft passed his high school exams in 1964 , he saved in the physics program at utrecht university.in 1969 , 't hooft started on his phd with martinus veltman as his advisor . in 1971 his first paper was published .
