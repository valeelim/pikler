Virat Kohli     virat kohli is an indian cricketer . he was born in delhi , india on november 5 , 1988. kohli won the icc player of the year award in 2012. he is often rated as one of the best batsmen in the world . kohli was ranked eighth in espn 's list of world 's most famous athletes in 2016. other websites . - virat kohli from espncricinfo - virat kohli from cricketarchive
