Ottershaw     ottershaw is a village in the runnymede district of surrey , england . it is about southwest of london . ottershaw is part of the mixed rural and suburban foxhills ward . it is part of the parish of ottershaw . this parish was first established in 1864 in what was part of the original parish of chertsey . this is when the name 'ottershaw ' came into use . before that it was a part of chertsey common , which was part of windsor forest .
