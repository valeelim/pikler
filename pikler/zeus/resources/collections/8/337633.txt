Daniel McKeague     daniel mckeague ( born 1974 ) is an american actor , voice actor , comedian , and tv advertising sales manager . he is best known as the new voice of the aflac mascot , a duck . on april 26 , 2011 , it was announced that mckeague would replace gilbert gottfried as the duck 's voice . gottfried had been fired because of making a joke on the earthquake and tsunami in japan . mckeague was born and raised in hugo , minnesota in 1974. originally a salesman , mckeague was hired by aflac as the new voice of the aflac duck on april 26 , 2011 after original actor gilbert gottfried ( whom mckeague was able to impersonate ) was fired . on may 1 , 2011 , the first aflac commercial with mckeague voicing the duck was released . mckeague has a wife and 3 children . filmography . commercials . - `` jump5 '' : pizza man ( 2002 ) - `` aflac '' : aflac duck ( 2011- )
