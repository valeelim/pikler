Waterfowl hunting     waterfowl hunting ( also called wildfowling or waterfowl shooting in britain ) is the act of hunting ducks , geese , or other waterfowl for food and sport . in many western countries , waterfowl hunting is illegal , and duck hunting is mostly an outdoor sporting activity . many types of ducks and geese share the same habitat , have overlapping or same hunting seasons , and are hunted the same . thus it is possible to hunt different species of waterfowl in the same trip . waterfowl can be hunted in crop fields where they eat , or , more often , on or near bodies of water such as rivers , lakes , ponds , swamps , sloughs , or oceanic coastlines .
