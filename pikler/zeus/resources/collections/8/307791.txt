Through the Years/A Traves de los Años     through the years/a traves de los años is a album by american singer selena . the album contained an unreleased song called `` feelings '' . the song was recorded when selena was only 7 years old . the album peaked at number 28 on the top latin albums chart and was certified gold by the riaa . track listing . 1. feelings ( unreleased ) 1. como te quiero yo a ti 1. tu solamente tu 1. tu no sabes 1. que 1. sukiyaki 1. amame , quiereme 1. yo te amo 1. ya ves 1. yo te sigo queriendo 1. amame 1. no debes jugar 1. la llamada 1. fotos y recuerdos 1. ya no 1. dreaming of you 1. puede ser
