Battle of Balaclava     the battle of balaclava was a battle of crimean war . it was on 25 october 1854 between the alliance of the ottoman empire , the french empire , and the united kingdom vs. the russian empire . the battle ended with a russian victory . two of the most important events of the crimean war took place during the battle of balaclava . the 93rd highland regiment ( dressed almost all in red ) formed a line 2 men thick and stood their ground when the russian cavalry charged at them . this event inspired alfred , lord tennyson 's poem of the same name . the charge was made by the 4th and 13th light dragoons , 17th lancers , and the 8th and 11th hussars , under the command of major general the earl of cardigan . many people died in this charge . 278 of the over 670 soldiers were killed .
