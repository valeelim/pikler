Alajuelense    l.d . alajuelense liga deportiva alajuelense ( l.d.a . ) , is a costa rican football club that plays in the primera división de costa rica . it was created on 18 june 1919. the team is located in alajuela and they play their local games at the estadio alejandro morera soto . they are the only club in costa rica football history with at least one championship won in each decade . it also shares along with cs herediano being the only team that has never descended nor played in the second or third division . notable coaches . - jorge luis solera - alejandro morera soto `` el mago del balón '' - salvador soto `` el indio buroy '' - josé luis rojas `` chime '' - hugo tassara - eduardo viso abella - juan colecchio - iván mraz - odir jacques - alvaro grant mcdonald - jan postulka - jorge olguín - valdeir vieira `` badu '' - manuel keosseian `` manolo '' - guilherme farinha - jorge luis pinto - javier delgado - rodrigo kenton - oscar ramirez honors . national . - costa rican championship : 29 - runner-up ( 20 ) : costa rican short-tournament championships :
