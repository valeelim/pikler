Visací zámek     visací zámek ( english : '' padlock '' ) is a czech punk rock band . the band was formed in 1982. this band is thought to be the oldest czech punk band . its early work influenced the sound of an entire generation of bands . the early works of visací zámek are strongly influenced by early nihilism and post-modernism . band members . - jan `` hony '' haubert – vocals - michal `` pixies '' pixa – kytara , vocals - vladimír `` savec '' šťástka – bass guitar - ivan `` hroch '' rut – kytara , vocals - jiří `` sweet p '' pátek – drums discography . albums . - `` visací zámek '' ( 1990 ) - `` start 02 '' ( 1991 ) - `` three locks '' ( 1992 ) - `` traktor '' ( 1993 ) - `` jako vždycky '' ( 1994 ) - `` sex '' ( 1996 ) - `` visací zámek znovu zasahuje '' ( 2000 ) - `` punk !
