Triglav (mythology)    triglav ( mythology ) triglav was an old slavic god . the highest mountain in slovenia is also called triglav . its name means `` three-headed `` in slovene . an insurance company also uses the name triglav . other myths are related to triglav . these include `` zlatorog '' . zlatorog is an immortal mountain goat . it has gold horns . it is currently being used as a logo for a brand of beer . on a mountain near triglav , there is a formation in the rocks of the mountain that looks like the face of a giant girl . this is said to be proof of the giants .
