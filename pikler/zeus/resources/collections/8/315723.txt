Patrick Murphy (Irish Politician)    patrick murphy ( irish politician ) patrick stephen murphy ( 13 may 1889 – 1 may 1968 ) was an irish politician . he was first elected to the lower house of the parliament of ireland ( dáil éireann ) in 1932. he was a member of the fianna fáil political party . he was the member of parliament ( teachta dála or td ) for the cork east constituency .
