Purnululu National Park     purnululu national park is an australian national park in the north east of western australia . it is a unesco world heritage site . the site covers and includes the bungle bungle ranges . the ranges are made from devonian era sandstone and have been eroded over 20 million years into shapes like towers and cones . the sandstone has horizontal stripes of orange coloured by iron oxide which dry out quickly , and dark strips where bacteria are growing in damp layers of stone . other areas of the park include the sand plains which surround the bungle bungles ; the ord river valley , and limestone ridges . related pages . - list of world heritage sites in australia
