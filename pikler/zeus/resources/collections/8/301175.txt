Yahata High School     this school has general course and science and mathematics course . the science and mathematics course is certified as ssh by ministry of education , culture , sports , and technology . most yahata high school students apply and get through the examination of the national university . this school has one of the top reputations among public high schools in kitakyushu city ( along with touchiku high school and kokura high school ) because a large number of graduates continue on to national university , for example , kyusyu university , one of the top public universities in japan . fukuoka high school was founded in 1919 under the former japanese educational system in which children went directly to high school after graduating from elementary school .
