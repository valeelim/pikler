Klaipėda     klaipėda is a city in lithuania . it is where the nemunas river flows into the baltic sea . klaipėda is the third largest city in lithuania and the capital of klaipėda county . the german name of the city is memel.until 1920 , this city was the northernmost city of the german empire . the city 's history is complicated.the port of klaipėda is important in the area . it is a usually ice-free port on the baltic sea . it has been controlled by the teutonic knights , the duchy of prussia , the kingdom of prussia , the german empire , the entente states , lithuania , and the third reich . the city became part of lithuania while it was a soviet socialist republic . klaipėda remained part of lithuania after it again became independent state . the population shrank from 207,100 in 1992 to 177,823 in 2011. popular seaside resorts found close to klaipėda are nida to the south on the curonian spit , and palanga to the north .
