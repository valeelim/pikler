Koi     koi ( , romaji : `` koi '' ) or more specifically nishikigoi ( , literally `` brocaded carp '' ) , are ornamental varieties of common carp ( `` cyprinus carpio '' ) that are kept for decorative purposes in outdoor koi ponds or water gardens . koi varieties are distinguished by coloration , patterning , and scalation . some of the major colors are white , black , red , yellow , blue , and cream . it is illegal to keep koi in queensland , australia because they are an invasive species .
