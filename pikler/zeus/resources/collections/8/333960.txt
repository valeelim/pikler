Whenever, Wherever    whenever , wherever `` whenever , wherever '' is the debut english-language single for latin music artist shakira . taken off of her first english-language album `` laundry service '' it was released in late 2001. she also recorded a spanish-language version of the song titled `` suerte '' . it was the best-selling song of 2001. the video won the 2002 latin grammy award for `` best short form music video '' . the song was written by shakira and gloria estafan .
