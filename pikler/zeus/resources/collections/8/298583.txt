American Pie (movie)    american pie ( movie ) american pie is a 1999 american teen sex comedy movie that was produced by chris moore , chris weitz , warren zide and craig perry and was the directorial debut for directors paul weitz and chris weitz . `` american pie '' is set in michigan . it deals with a group of teenage boys deciding to try to lose their virginity before graduation from high school . it was released on july 9 , 1999 in north america . the movie is the first in the `` american pie '' movie series . it gained mixed reviews with a 58 % on rotten tomatoes and a 58 out of 100 from metacritic . it was followed by american pie 2. cast . - jason biggs as james `` jim '' levenstein - thomas ian nicholas as kevin myers - seann william scott as steve stifler - eddie kaye thomas as paul finch - alyson hannigan as michelle flaherty - chris klein as chris `` oz '' ostreicher - shannon elizabeth as nadia - tara reid as victoria `` vicky '' lathum - mena suvari as heather - natasha lyonne as jessica - eugene levy as noah levenstein - molly cheek as me levenstein
