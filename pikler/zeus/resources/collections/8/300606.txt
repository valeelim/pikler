Sergei Milnichenko     sergei grigoryevich milnichenko ( ; born on july 13 , 1966 in tomsk , russian sfsr , ussr ) is an soviet and russian composer , songwriter , guitarist and record producer . in the 1990s , sergei married larisa alina . they divorced in the 2000s . they had one child , daughter rassveta milnichenko ( born in the late 1990s ) . in october 2009 he married natalya milnichenko . between first and second marriages he was in a relationship from 2005 until 2008 with valeria kozlova . currently he lives with his wife in moscow , russia .
