Kenya at the Olympics     kenya at the olympics is a history which began in 1956. the international olympic committee 's official abbreviation for kenya is ken . teams from kenya have been in every summer olympic games since then , except for the boycotted 1976 and 1980 games . kenya participated in the winter olympic games in 1998 , 2002 and 2006. kenyan athletes have won a total of 75 medals , all in athletics and boxing . the national olympic committee for kenya is the `` national olympic committee kenya '' , and was founded in 1955. related pages . - list of ioc country codes
