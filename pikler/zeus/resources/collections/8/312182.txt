John McCarthy     john mccarthy ( 1927-2011 ) was an american scientist . today , he is mostly known for the work he did in the field of logic . he was the first to use the term artificial intelligence , in an article he wrote in 1955. he invented lisp to be able to do logic programming , in 1958. this makes lisp the second-oldest high-level programming language that is still in use ; fortran is older . mccarthy studied mathematics . he invented garbage collection to solve some of the problems lisp had . mccarthy 's work received many awards , the turing award for his work on artificial intelligence , in 1971 , or the national medal of science in 2003 are among them .
