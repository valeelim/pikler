Provinces of Costa Rica     the provinces ( ) are administrative parts of costa rica as in other countries . the constitution of costa rica says , in its article 168 , `` for public administration purposes , the national territory is divided into provinces , these into cantons and cantons into districts . '' , there are 7 provinces ( `` provincias '' ) , 81 cantons ( `` cantones '' ) , and 473 districts ( `` districtos '' ) . the law no . 36 of 7 december 1848 divided the country , for the first time , in provinces , cantons and districts , and created 6 provinces : 1. san josé 1. alajuela 1. cartago 1. heredia 1. guanacaste the law made puntarenas as `` comarca '' , an old category that does not exist any more . puntarenas was made a province in 1858. in 1870 , limón was made a `` comarca '' with the eastern part of the cartago province ; in 1902 , limón was made a province . related pages . - cantons of costa rica
