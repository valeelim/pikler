Michael Chabon     michael chabon is an american author . his first novel was called `` the mysteries of pittsburgh '' and published in 1988. chabon was 25 years old . the book made him famous among writers . he has also written genre fiction . chabon published `` the amazing adventures of kavalier & clay '' in 2000. it won the pulitzer prize for fiction in 2001. michael chabon was born in washington , dc to robert chabon , a physician and lawyer , and sharon chabon , a lawyer . he knew he wanted to be a writer when he was a child . he studied at carnegie mellon university for a year before transferring to the university of pittsburgh . he earned a bachelor of arts degree there in 1984. then chabon went to graduate school at the university of california , irvine and earned a master of fine arts in creative writing . in 1987 , chabon married the poet lollie groth . they divorced in 1991 , and he married the writer ayelet waldman in 1993. works . novels . - `` the mysteries of pittsburgh '' ( 1988 ) - `` wonder boys '' ( 1995 )
