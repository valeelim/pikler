Mikhail Myasnikovich     mikhail uladzimiravich myasnikovich ( born 6 may 1950 ) is a belarusian politician . he was the prime minister of belarus from 2010 to 2014. he was picked by president alexander lukashenko after the 2010 presidential election . he graduated from the brest engineering and construction institute in 1972 and the communist party school in 1989 in minsk . he has a phd in economics . he speaks english . he has a son , daughter and two grandchildren . other websites . - belarusprofile project page .
