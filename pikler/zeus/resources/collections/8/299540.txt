Heimo Pfeifenberger     heimo pfeifenberger ( born 29 december 1966 in zederhaus , salzburg ) is a former professional footballer from austria . he played mainly for sv austria salzburg and also in the austrian national football team . with 74 goals for salzburg and 43 for rapid he is one of the best scorers in austrias football history . he started playing footballl in the youth teams of his local club usv zederhaus . after scoring 56 goals for zederhaus in the 1987 season he was transferred to austria salzburg . later he played for sk rapid wien and werder bremen in the german bundesliga . his most successful time was with austria salzburg in the mid 90s . he became two times austrian champion ( 1994 and 1995 ) and 1994 he was austrias top scorer . he was also elected footballer of the year . after playing for bremen he returned to austria and played again for salzburg . 2004 he ended his active career as professional footballer . 2007 he played for sv seekirchen in the regionalliga west ( 3rd level ) and as coach for sv gr√∂dig he played 2 matches for his team .
