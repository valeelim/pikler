Dream pop     dream pop began in the united kingdom in the mid-1980s . it is a subgenre of alternative rock . dream pop music is mainly about the singer 's ethereal textures and mood ( feelings ) . the musical instruments mainly played on dream pop music are the guitar , bass , drums , and synthesizers . shoegaze is a type of dream pop . 4ad is a record label that is associated with dream pop . some dream pop bands are my bloody valentine , cocteau twins , and mazzy star . other websites . - [ allmusic entry on dream pop ]
