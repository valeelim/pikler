Kate Edger     kate milligan evans edger ( january 6 , 1857 to may 6 , 1935 ) was the first woman in new zealand to get a university degree and the first woman in the british empire to get a bachelor of arts degree . edger 's family moved from england to new zealand in 1862. on 11 july 1877 , she graduated from the university of new zealand . the kate edger educational charitable trust gives financial assistance for women to get undergraduate or postgraduate degrees . in 2004 the kate edger information commons was formed at the university of auckland .
