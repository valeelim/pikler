Busch Stadium     busch stadium is a baseball park in saint louis , missouri . it is the home field of the major league baseball team called the st. louis cardinals . the stadium was named for the anheuser-busch brewing company , which was based in st. louis for a long time , and once was the owner of the cardinals . busch stadium was built next door to the cardinals ' previous ballpark , busch memorial stadium . sources . - `` green cathedrals '' , by philip j. lowry
