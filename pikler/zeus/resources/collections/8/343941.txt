Amanda Redman     amanda jacqueline redman ( born 12 august 1957 ) is a british actress . she was born in brighton . redman 's movie roles include deedee dove in `` sexy beast '' . her television roles include `` at home with the braithwaites '' and `` new tricks '' . redman 's aunt was actress joyce redman ( 1915-2012 ) . redman married actor robert glenister ( born 1960 ) in 1984. their daughter emily glenister was born in 1987. the couple divorced in 1992 .
