List of World Heritage Sites in Northern Europe     the united nations educational , scientific and cultural organization ( unesco ) has listed 35 world heritage sites in eight countries commonly called northern europe . these countries are iceland , norway , sweden , finland , denmark , estonia , latvia and lithuania . greenland is not included in this list even though it is linked with denmark . it is included in the list of world heritage sites in the americas . the united kingdom and ireland are included in western europe even though they are sometimes listed in northern europe .
