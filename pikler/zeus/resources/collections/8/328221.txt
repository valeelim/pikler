Type 4 150 mm howitzer     the , also known as a `` 15-cm '' gun , was a field gun used by the imperial japanese army ( ija ) . it type 4 was available to ija forces after 1915. this artillery piece was used during the second sino-japanese war and world war ii . this gun was designed to be moved by horses ; and it continued to be used until 1945. type 4s were used by the ija on saipan in world war ii . related pages . - artillery of japan - list of howitzers other websites . - type 4 on taki 's imperial japanese army page - us technical manual e 30-480
