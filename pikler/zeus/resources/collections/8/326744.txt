John Ramsay, 1st Lord Bothwell    john ramsay , 1st lord bothwell john ramsay , 1st lord bothwell ( born about 1464 , died 1513 ) was a scottish lord . in parliament on 24 february 1484 , james iii of scotland gave him the barony ( title ) of bothwell . he forfeited the title on 8 october 1488 and became sir john ramsay of trarinzeane .
