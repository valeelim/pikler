Brenda Blethyn     brenda blethyn ( born 20 february 1946 ) is a british actress . she was born and raised in ramsgate , kent , england . she has starred in theater , movies and television . she has been nominated for academy awards , golden globe awards and emmy awards . she has won a golden globe award and a bafta award . notable movies she has starred in include `` secrets & lies '' ( 1996 ) .
