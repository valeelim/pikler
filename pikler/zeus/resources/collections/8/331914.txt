Brandon Marshall     brandon marshall ( born march 23 , 1984 ) is an american football wide receiver for the chicago bears of the national football league . he was selected by the denver broncos out of the university of central florida , with the 119th overall pick in the fourth round of the 2006 nfl draft . marshall has also played for the miami dolphins , where he wore # 19. he holds a record for the most catches in a single game with 21 , a record set in 2009 while marshall was with the broncos . throughout his football career , marshall has been respected as a good player , often one who is hard to tackle .
