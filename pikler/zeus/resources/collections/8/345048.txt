'O Sole Mio     `` '' is a famous naples song written in 1898. the lyrics were written by giovanni capurro and the music was composed by eduardo di capua . it has been performed and covered by many artists as enrico caruso , beniamino gigli , mario lanza and the three tenors . it has also been performed by rock/pop artists such as dalida , anna oxa , bryan adams , me first , vitas al bano , elvis presley ( `` it 's now or never '' ) . luciano pavarotti won the 1980 grammy award for best classical vocal performance for his rendition of `` 'o sole mio .
