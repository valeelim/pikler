ISG Business School     institut supérieur de gestion , commonly named isg , is a european higher studies establishment . the school is in three places , in paris ( france ) , in new-york ( usa ) and in tokyo ( japan ) . isg delivers bachelor 's degree , master 's degree and mba . isg is specialist in the fields of economics , social sciences and management . since the 10th of april 2012 , the school is member of the `` conférence des grandes écoles '' .
