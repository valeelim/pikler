Yume Nikki     the game is known for its strange and sometimes scary content . the player controls madotsuki and can move her using the arrow keys . if you make her go to sleep by interacting with the bed , she goes into a dream version of her room . from there , you can make her explore 12 different dream worlds by going through doors that are in a large , dark place north of her room . to beat the game , the player has to find all 24 `` effects '' that are hidden in madotsuki 's dreams . other websites . - yume nikki & kikiyama 's official website - download yume nikki
