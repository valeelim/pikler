Kiribati at the Olympics     the island nation of kiribati was first in the 2004 summer olympic games . weightlifter meamea thomas and sprinters kakianako nariki and kaitinano mwemweata represented the country . the country 's name was notably mispronounced by officials in all three languages -french , english and greek- during the opening ceremony in 2004. other websites . - `` kiribati 's olympic adventure '' , andrew fraser , bbc , august 3 , 2004 - ioc page
