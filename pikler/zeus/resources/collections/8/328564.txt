Type 41 75 mm cavalry gun     this artillery piece went into service in 1908. type 41s were lighter versions of the type 38 75 mm field gun . type 41s were the primary weapon of artillery units which were part of cavalry regiments . type 95s were used in the second sino-japanese war and in world war ii . related pages . - artillery of japan - list of field guns other websites . - type 41 on taki 's imperial japanese army page
