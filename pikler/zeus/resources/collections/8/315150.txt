Cavaquinho     the cavaquinho ( pronounced [ kavɐˈkiɲu ] in portuguese ) is a small string instrument of the european guitar family with four wire or gut strings . its standard tuning is d , g , b , d. other tunings include d-a-b-e ( portuguese old tuning , made popular by júlio pereira ) and g-g-b-d and a-a-c # -e. guitarists often use d-g-b-e tuning to imitate the highest four strings of the guitar . the g-c-e-a tuning is sometimes used to imitate the soprano/tenor ukulele , an instrument developed from the cavaquinhos that the portuguese took to hawaii in the late 19th century .
