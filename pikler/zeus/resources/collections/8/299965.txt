Talcahuano     talcahuano is a commune , city and port in chile with 171.383 inhabitants and an area of 92.3 square kilometres , all according to the census on 2002. additionally , this city is known like the first militar , industrial and fishing port in the history of chile . `` talcahuano '' was declared and founded on 1764 like a port city by antonio guill y gonzaga . this city and the port was well known by some american whaleships on 19th century . the name `` talcahuano '' comes from `` talcahueñu '' , in honor to the araucanian chief when spaniards came to the zone . `` talcahueñu '' also means `` thundering sky '' on mapudungun language . other websites . - talcahuano municipality - thno.info - asmar - astillero de talcahuano
