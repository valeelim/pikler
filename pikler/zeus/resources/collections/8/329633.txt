Rajesh Khanna     rajesh khanna ( 29 december 1942 18 july 2012 ) was an indian actor in movies . he became very famous in bollywood after shakti samantha 's movie `` aradhana '' . rajesh played both the father and son in that movie . some of his well-known movies are `` anand '' , `` kati patang '' , `` safar '' , `` sacha jhuta '' , `` raja rani '' , `` bawarchi '' and `` amar prem '' .
