National Archives of Japan     the , also known by the acronym naj , is a japanese public institution which preserves government documents and historical records . the national archives law no . 79 ( 1999 ) established naj 's core function of preserving government documents and records . before the creation of the national archives , there is almost no tradition of preserving `` grey-area '' records , such as records which show the process which led to the formation of a specific policy . the national archives became an independent administrative institution on april 1 , 2001. since 2005 , the naj website has offered high-resolution images of some of its holdings , including those named as important cultural properties of japan . related pages . - list of independent administrative institutes in japan - national diet library other websites . - national archives of japan ; - japan center for asian historical records
