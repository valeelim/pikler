Murder of Selena     the murder of american singer selena happened on march 31 , 1995. fans were saddened when the news of selena 's death was released by the media . she was shot and killed by yolanda saldivar . saldivar was an employee and manager for selena 's boutiques and fan club . saldivar was stealing money from these places and got found out . selena did not believe her best friend could do something like that . she tried to maintain their friendship . by march 1995 , saldivar and selena 's friendship began to crumble . selena and her husband , chris perez , visited saldivar to get the missing documents that she had stolen . saldivar failed to give selena all the documents . the next day , selena and saldivar met secretly at saldivar 's motel room . selena asked for the rest of the documents . saldivar then claimed that she was raped in mexico . selena took her to a local hospital where doctors found that saldivar was lying . this made selena end her friendship with saldivar . when selena turned to leave the motel room , saldivar took a gun from her purse and pointed it at selena . saldivar pulled the trigger and shot selena in the back . by the time selena arrived at a local hospital , she was pronounced dead . saldivar went to her pickup truck and threatened to commit suicide . she gave herself up after nine hours .
