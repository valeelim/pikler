Hypothyroidism     hypothyroidism is a medical condition . if a person has hypothyroidism , their thyroid gland does not make enough hormones . it can be caused by many factors , but it is more common when a person has insufficient iodine . severe hypothyroidism in infants can result in cretinism . symptoms of hypothyroidism include the following : tiredness , headaches , weight gain , dry skin , constipation , and poor concentration .
