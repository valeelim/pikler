Abū Rayḥān al-Bīrūnī     abū al-rayḥān muḥammad ibn aḥmad al-bīrūnī ( born 5 september 973 in kath , khwarezm , died in the year 13 december 1048 in ghazni ) , known as alberonius in latin and al-biruni in english , was a persian-chorasmian muslim scholar and polymath of the 11th century . al-biruni is one of the greatest scholars of the medieval islamic era and was well versed in physics , mathematics , astronomy , and natural sciences . he also did well as a historian , chronologist and linguist .
