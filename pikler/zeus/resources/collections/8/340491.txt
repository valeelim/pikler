Tébessa Province     tébessa is a province of algeria . tébessa is also the name of the capital . another important city is el ouenza . tebessa is only 20 kilometers west from tunisia border . the province has 12 districts . there are also 28 `` communes '' or municipalities . districts . 1. bir el ater 1. bir mokadem 1. cheria 1. el aouïnet 1. el kouif 1. el ma labiodh 1. el ogla 1. morsott 1. negrine 1. ouenza 1. oum ali 1. tébessa
