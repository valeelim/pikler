Sociolinguistics     sociolinguistics is the study of how language is related to society , and how society affects the way language is used . sociolinguistics studies how language varieties are different between groups of people based on sociolinguistic variables , such as : race , gender , status , age , etc . further reading . - lakoff , robin t.
