Jean-Pierre Houël     jean-pierre houël ( 1735–1813 ) was french artist . he was painting at the time of the french revolution . one of his well known paintings is the `` prise de la bastille '' ( the storming of the bastille ) , 1789. the capture of the bastille prison was an important event in the revolution , and remembered with a celebration and holiday on the anniversary , known as bastille day .
