Shaheen falcon     the shaheen falcon ( `` falco peregrinus peregrinator '' ) is also known as the indian peregrine falcon , other names are the black shaheen , pakistani shaheen , or simply the shaheen . it is a non-migratory subspecies of the peregrine falcon found mainly in hilly and mountainous regions of the indian subcontinent and the nearby island of sri lanka . in pakistani literature , the shaheen has a special association with the poetry of the country 's national poet , allama muhammad iqbal . the pakistan air force has as a symbol and in gilgit-baltistan it is the unofficial bird of the region .
