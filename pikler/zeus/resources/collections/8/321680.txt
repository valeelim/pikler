Tabuaeran     tabuaeran is one of the line islands of the central pacific ocean . it is an atoll . tabueran is part of kiribati . the highest point on the island is about 3 m ( 10 ft ) above high tide . tabuaeran is the name in gilbertese language . the island is also called fanning island or fanning atoll in english . other websites . - map of tabuaeran - fanning island on jane resture 's oceania home page a strong resource including geography , history , photos , and links . - fanning-island.com - ancient polynesian remains - atoll institute , proposed field station - `` edmund fanning '' in `` explorers of the pacific '' by buck ( etext )
