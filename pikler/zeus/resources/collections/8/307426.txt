Ichinoseki, Iwate    ichinoseki , iwate ichinoseki is in the southernmost area of iwate prefecture in the tohoku region of japan . the city 's name means `` first gate '' in japanese . the city has expanded in area by taking in and joining neighboring towns . the current city of ichinoseki became larger after 7 towns merged on september 20 , 2005. the city is now equivalent to former iwai district , and it is connected to miyagi and akita prefectures . the population is nearly 120,000 , with 42,335 households . there are many factories belonging to large japanese companies such as sony and nec in ichinoseki . the city has many brazilian workers living there . outside the city center there are many farms . some chinese and filipino women have moved here to marry local farmers . ichinoseki is close to historically important hiraizumi to the north . tsuriyama park is near the center of the original city of ichinoseki . tsuriyama park gives a good view of ichinoseki . hondera farm village has been named an important cultural landscape .
