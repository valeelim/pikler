Bhutan at the Olympics     bhutan has been in the summer olympic games since 1984. they have never been in the winter olympic games . bhutan has only been in archery , which is the national sport of bhutan . they have never won an olympic medal . the bhutan olympic committee was formed in 1983 and recognized by the ioc the same year . related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - bhutan profile at london2012.com
