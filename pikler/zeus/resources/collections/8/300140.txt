Rahm Emanuel     rahm israel emanuel ( ; born november 29 , 1959 ) is an american politician . he is the 55th and current mayor of chicago . emanuel was born on november 29 , 1959 in chicago , illinois to a jewish family . he studied at sarah lawrence college and at northwestern university . he was a democratic member of the united states house of representatives , representing , from 2003 until his resignation in 2009 to become a member of the obama administration . he is the first jewish mayor of chicago . emanuel was chair of the democratic congressional campaign committee during the 2006 mid-term elections and remained a top strategist for house democrats during the 2008 cycle . after democrats regained control of the house in 2006 , emanuel was elected chairman of the democratic caucus . this made him the fourth-ranking house democrat , behind speaker nancy pelosi , majority leader steny hoyer and majority whip jim clyburn . two days after obama 's election victory , emanuel was announced as obama 's designee for white house chief of staff . he resigned from the house on january 2 , 2009 , and began his duties as chief of staff on january 20 , 2009 , the day of obama 's inauguration .
