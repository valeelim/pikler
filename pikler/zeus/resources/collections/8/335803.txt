Eastern black rhinoceros     the eastern black rhinoceros ( `` diceros bicornis michaeli '' ) is also known as the east african black rhinoceros . it is a critically endangered subspecies of black rhino that lives in woodland and scrubland biome . it lives in countries like ethiopia , sudan , somalia , tanzania , kenya and uganda . black rhinos typically weigh up to 2,090 to 2,870 lbs and get up to 4.7 to 5.2 ft tall and live up to 40 years .
