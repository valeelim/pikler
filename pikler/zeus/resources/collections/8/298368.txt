Aaron Rome     aaron rome ( born september 27 , 1983 in nesbitt , manitoba ) is a canadian ice hockey defenseman . he currently plays for the dallas stars of the national hockey league ( nhl ) . he has also played in the whl for a combined 8 seasons with the saskatoon blades , kootenay ice , swift current broncos and the moose jaw warriors . he formerly played in the nhl for the columbus blue jackets , anaheim ducks and the vancouver canucks . he was on the anaheim ducks when they won the stanley cup in 2007 but he did not qualify to have his name engraved on the cup . he did however get a stanley cup ring and a day to spend with the cup in the off-season . on june 6 , 2011 , rome was given a four-game suspension because of a late hit that he did on boston 's nathan horton during game three of the 2011 stanley cup finals . the hit gave horton a concussion and he was sent to the hospital .
