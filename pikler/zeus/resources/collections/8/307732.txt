The Marshall Mathers LP     the marshall mathers lp is the third studio album by american rapper , eminem . `` the marshall mathers lp '' was released on may 23 , 2000 and was produced by dr. dre , mel-man , bass brothers , eminem and the 45 king . the album was given 5 out of 5 stars by allmusic . `` the marshall mathers lp 2 '' , the sequel to `` the marshall mathers lp '' , was released on november 5 , 2013 .
