Der Freischütz     der freischütz is an opera in three acts by carl maria von weber with a libretto by friedrich kind . it premiered on 18 june 1821 at the schauspielhaus berlin . the title is usually translated as `` the marksman '' , or `` the freeshooter '' . the opera is the first important german romantic opera , especially in its national identity and stark emotionality . the plot is based on the german folk legend of the freischütz and many of its tunes were inspired by german folk music . its unearthly portrayal of the supernatural in the famous wolf 's glen scene has been described as `` the most expressive rendering of the gruesome that is to be found in a musical score '' .
