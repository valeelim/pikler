Hrazdan     hrazdan is the capital of the kotayk province of armenia . the name hrazdan is from the middle-persian name `` frazdan '' . `` farzdan '' is connected to the zoroastrian mythology . with 52,900 people it is the fifth-largest city in armenia . the town was formed under soviet rule . during the soviet years it was one of the highly-industrialized towns of the armenian ssr . the hrazdan river is by the town .
