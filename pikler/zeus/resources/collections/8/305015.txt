Land Rover     land rover is a brand of the british jaguar land rover company . the company makes cars ( suv 's ) . since 2008 they have been owned by a indian company called tata motors . land rover supplies the british army with vehicles . the original land rover car dates back to 1948 , and was designed by maurice wilks . as of 2014 , land rover sells six ranges of vehicle : the defender , freelander 2/lr2 , discovery 4/lr4 , range rover evoque , range rover sport and range rover .
