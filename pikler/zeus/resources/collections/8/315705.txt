Patrick Murphy (giant)    patrick murphy ( giant ) patrick murphy ( 1834–1862 ) was an irish giant , born in county down from parents james murphy and mother peggy ( née cunningham ) , who put himself in a show as a way of making money . he originally worked on the docks in liverpool , england and , after that , became a waiter at a hotel . because he was so tall , murphy attracted crowds everywhere he went . he eventually decided that he could make a living being tall . he had always told of himself as being 8'10 '' and in some circles as much as 9'3 '' . in about 1860 , he was measured by dr. virchow as being 7'3.4 '' ( 222 cm ) tall . in may 1857 the emperor and empress of austria invited the tall irishman to visit their kingdom . while he was on tour throughout europe , he died of smallpox in marseille at the age of twenty-eight .
