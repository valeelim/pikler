Concorde     concorde was a passenger airplane that flew faster than the speed of sound . it was made by the french company aérospatiale and the british company british aircraft corporation . concorde carried passengers from 1976 to 2003. concorde flew across the atlantic ocean in a little less than 3.5 hours . other airplanes take about eight hours . the first scheduled flights were on 21 january 1976. concorde flew between several different cities in europe , north america , south america , and asia . some people did not like the sonic booms caused by concorde flying faster than the speed of sound . at different times , concorde was not allowed to fly over certain countries because of this . other websites . - british airways concorde page - braniff airways concorde page - design museum ( uk ) concorde page - concorde experience at the national museum of flight , scotland - pathé film of 002 's maiden flight from filton to raf fairford on 9 april 1967
