Timmins     timmins is a city in the canadian province of ontario . the population of timmins was 43,165 in 2011. the mayor of timmins is tom laughren . timmins was founded in 1912 by noah timmins after there were gold discoveries in the porcupine camp , although south porcupine and schumacher were formed in 1911. naoshima , kagawa in japan is a sister city to timmins . other websites . - official website
