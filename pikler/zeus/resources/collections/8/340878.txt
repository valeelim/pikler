The Amazing Spider-Man (2012 movie)    the amazing spider-man ( 2012 movie ) the amazing spider-man is a 2012 movie by marvel entertainment and columbia pictures . it stars andrew garfield and emma stone . it is a reboot of the spider-man motion picture series . it was released in the united states on july 3 , 2012. it is about peter parker in high school while his super powers are evolving . a sequel , `` the amazing spider-man 2 '' , was released in 2014 .
