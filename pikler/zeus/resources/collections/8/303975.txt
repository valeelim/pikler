Mongolia at the Olympics     mongolia at the olympics is a history which began in 1956. the international olympic committee 's official abbreviation for mongolia is mgl . a team from mongolia was first at the olympic games in 1964. they have been in every summer olympic games since then , except the boycotted 1984 summer olympics . mongolia has also been in the winter olympic games since 1964. they have missed only the 1976 winter games . mongolian athletes have won a total of nineteen medals , all in summer olympics competitions . the mongolian national olympic committee was formed in 1956 and recognized by the international olympic committee in 1962. related pages . - list of ioc country codes
