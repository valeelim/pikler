Metalist Stadium     oblast sports complex `` metalist '' ( , commonly known as simply the metalist stadium ) is a stadium in kharkiv , ukraine . the stadium was one of the venues for the uefa euro 2012. the stadium can hold 38,633 people . f.c . metalist kharkiv plays there . the stadium was first opened on september 12 , 1926. the stadium 's only renovation was on december 5 , 2009 , for the uefa euro 2012 .
