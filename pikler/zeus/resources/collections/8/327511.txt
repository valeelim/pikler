My Love Is Your Love     my love is your love is the fourth studio album by deceased singer whitney houston . it was released in late 1998. singles released off of the album include the duet with mariah carey `` when you believe '' , `` heartbreak hotel '' with faith evans and kelly price , `` it 's not right , but it 's okay '' , title track `` my love is your love '' , and `` i learned from the best .
