The Cabinet of Dr. Caligari     the cabinet of dr. caligari ( ) is a 1920 german silent movie . it was directed by robert wiene . conrad veidt stars . it is a german expressionist movie . it is one of the greatest horror movies of the silent era . the movie uses stylized sets with abstract , jagged buildings painted on canvas backdrops . these unique sets gave off a strong sense of theater . the actors used an unrealistic technique that exhibited jerky and dancelike movements . this movie introduced the `` twist ending '' in movies .
