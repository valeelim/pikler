Line of succession to the Danish throne     the line of succession to the danish throne is the list of people who may become king or queen of denmark . since 2009 , danish law identifies the eldest child of a danish monarch the royal heir . the first-born child follows his or her parent on the throne . this is known as full or equal primogeniture . in 2011 , the current order of succession is clear . 1. frederik , crown prince of denmark , count of monpezat ( frederik , elder son of the queen ) 1. prince christian of denmark , count of monpezat ( elder son of the crown prince and crown princess ) 1. princess isabella of denmark , countess of monpezat ( elder daughter of the crown prince and crown princess ) 1. prince vincent of denmark , count of monpezat ( younger son of the crown prince and crown princess ) ( twin ) 1. princess josephine of denmark , countess of monpezat ( younger daughter of the crown prince and crown princess ) ( twin ) 1. prince joachim of denmark , count of monpezat ( younger son of the queen ) 1. prince nikolai of denmark , count of monpezat ( elder son of prince joachim and the countess of frederiksborg )
