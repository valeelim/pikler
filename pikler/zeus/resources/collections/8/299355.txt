Theodicy of disprivilege     theodicy of disprivilege refers to the belief of the promise that salvation may be granted as a reward for earthly poverty . it can be one of many reason that explain the large numbers of lower class people who join religious sects or partake in mainstream religious practice , e.g attend church , temple or mosque . this idea was promoted mainly by max weber but is common amongst many sociologists who go to explain the growth of sect membership .
