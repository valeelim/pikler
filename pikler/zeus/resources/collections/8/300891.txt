Spa     the term spa is associated with water treatment of disease by bathing . spa towns , spa resorts , and hot springs resorts often offer various health treatments . people have believed in the helpful powers of mineral waters back to prehistoric times . this practice has been popular worldwide . it is especially widespread in europe and japan . the term came from the name of the town of spa , belgium . this name was known back in roman times , when it was called aquae spadanae . it may be related to the latin word `` spargere '' meaning to scatter , sprinkle or moisten .
