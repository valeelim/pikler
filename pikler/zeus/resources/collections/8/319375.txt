Cyprus at the Olympics     cyprus ( cyp ) has sent athletes to every olympic games held since 1980. cyprus earned its first olympic medal in the 2012 summer olympics . pavlos kontides earned a silver medal in the men 's laser sailing event . they almost won a medal in the 2008 olympics when antonis nikolaidis lost the bronze medal shoot-off in men 's skeet . the first athlete from cyprus in the modern olympics was anastasios andreou in 1896. he competed under the greek flag . at this time , cyprus was a british colony . related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - cyprus profile at london2012.com
