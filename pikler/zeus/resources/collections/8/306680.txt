Skate 3     skate 3 is a skateboarding video game that was published by electronic arts . it is available for the playstation 3 and the xbox 360 video game systems . the game was the third in the `` skate '' video game series . the game takes place in the city of port carverton . the city also embraces skateboarding which was unlike the second game where they had a `` skateboarding is a crime '' mentality . the game got good reviews with a 7.5 out of 10 from gamespot and a 80 out of 100 from metacritic . other websites . - official game website
