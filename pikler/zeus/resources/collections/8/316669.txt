Los Tres Reyes     los tres reyes are a group of three mexican singers . they record mariachi and ranchera music . their comeback album `` retorno de los tres reyes '' ( 2001 ) peaked within the top 20 of the mexican albums chart . the album contained a cover version of selena 's `` no me queda mas '' song . the group recorded the song as a duet with selena and released a promotional music video . the group has sold over 1,600,000 copies in the united states . other websites . - allmusic
