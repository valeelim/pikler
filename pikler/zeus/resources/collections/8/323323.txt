Mackenzie River     the mackenzie river is a major river in northern canada . it is named after famous explorer , alexander mackenzie . the river 's watershed drains parts of alberta , british columbia , the northwest territories , saskatchewan , and the yukon . it flows north , for , from great slave lake to the arctic ocean . there are only a few small towns on the river , including fort providence and norman wells . the river is used as a boat transport route during the ice-free summer . tributaries of the river include the liard , keele , great bear , arctic red and peel rivers . the slave river , peace river and athabasca river provide most of the water in great slave lake .
