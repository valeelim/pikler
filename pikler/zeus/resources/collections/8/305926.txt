Jean François de Galaup, Count of La Pérouse    jean françois de galaup , count of la pérouse jean-françois de galaup , comte de la pérouse ( 1741—1788 ) was a french navigator who explored the pacific ocean . he died when his ship was wrecked in the new hebrides during 1788. he met the english when they arrived in australia with the first fleet in january 1788. after a short stay in botany bay , he sailed away into the pacific .
