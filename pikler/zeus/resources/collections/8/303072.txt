Thiago Alcántara     thiago alcántara do nascimento ( born 11 april 1991 in bari , italy ) is an italian-born spanish footballer , that currently plays for fc barcelona . he also made the most goals in the audi cup 2011 , making all two winning goals against bayern munich , and a goal against internacional . his dad is former brazilian player mazinho , his mom was a former volleyball player , and his younger brother plays for fc barcelona b .
