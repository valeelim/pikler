Sadarak District     sadarak ( sədərək ) is a raion of azerbaijan . it is in the nakhchivan autonomous republic . it was split from the ilyich raion ( present-day sharur ) in 1990. it has an exclave in armenia , karki . karki came under armenian control in may 1992. it was captured during the nagorno-karabakh war . the capital was sədərək . it is now heydarabad .
