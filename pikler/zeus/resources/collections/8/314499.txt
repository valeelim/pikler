Limit (Heide-Park)    limit ( heide-park ) limit is an inverted roller coaster at heide-park soltau , germany . it is a standard `` suspended looping coaster '' built by dutch roller coaster builder vekoma and opened in 1999. limit has 5 inversions , has a length of 689 meter , a maximum speed of 80 kilometers per hour . one round takes 1 minute and 36 seconds . limit has 2 trains with each 10 cars . 1 car can hold 2 riders , which gives a total of 20 riders per train . the maximum capacity is 1040 riders per hour .
