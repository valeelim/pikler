Bend Over and Pray the Lord     bend over and pray the lord is an album from the finnish hard rock/heavy metal band lordi . it was recorded in 1997 , but not published . the songs were published in 2012 in collection album `` scarchives vol . 1 '' . track listing . 1. playing the devil ( bend over and pray the lord ) 1. cyberundertaker 1. steamroller 1. almost human 1. idol 1. paint in blood 1. death suits you fine 1. i am the leviathan 1. take me to your leader 1. monstermotorhellmachine 1. with love and sledgehammer 1. the dead are the family 1. white lightning moonshine line-up . - mr. lordi - vocals - amen - guitars - g-stealer - bass - enary - keyboards
