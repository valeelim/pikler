Seve Ballesteros     severiano ballesteros sota ( 9 april 1957 - 7 may 2011 ) was a spanish golfer . ballesteros won the open championship in 1979 , 1984 and 1988. he won the masters tournament in 1980 and 1983. he was ranked number one golfer in the world for parts of the 1980s . he was born in pedrena , cantabria and died there , of brain cancer . other websites . - official website
