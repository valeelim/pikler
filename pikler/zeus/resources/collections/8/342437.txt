Gegharkunik Province     gegharkunik is a province of armenia . its capital is gavar . gegharkunik was named after gegham . gegham was a haykazuni king and the fifth generation after hayk . geghama mountains and geghama lake , now called lake sevan , were named after gegham . gegharkunik province is in the east of armenia . it borders azerbaijan . it is the largest province in armenia . much of the land is covered by lake sevan . there are 5 towns and 87 rural communities .
