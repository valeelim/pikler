Bleeding Love     `` bleeding love '' is a song by leona lewis . it was released as her official second single on october 29 , 2007 from her first album `` spirit '' . the song is written and produced by ryan tedder . `` bleeding love '' is said to be like a classic prince record , or whitney houston 's `` my love is your love '' . lewis had performed the song on the live show of the fourth series of `` the x factor '' .
