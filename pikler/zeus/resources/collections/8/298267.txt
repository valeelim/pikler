Cymbopogon     cymbopogon ( lemongrass ) is a tall perennial plant . usual names are lemon grass , lemongrass , barbed wire grass , silky heads , citronella grass , cha de dartigalongue , fever grass , hierba luisa or gavati chahaand many others . lemongrass is native to the philippines . it is used as a herb . it has a citrus taste and can be dried and powdered , or used fresh . lemongrass is used in teas , soups , and curries . it is also used for poultry , fish , beef , and seafood . it is often used as a tea in african countries such as togo and the democratic republic of the congo and latin american countries such as mexico .
