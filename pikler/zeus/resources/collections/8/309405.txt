Momentos Intimos     momentos intimos ( ) is a compilation album by american singer selena . it was released on march 23 , 2004. an unreleased duet called `` puede ser '' ( ) was included in the album . it was a duet with nando `` guero '' dominguez . it was recorded two weeks before selena was murdered . it then became the very last song to be recorded by her . `` no llores mas corazon '' ( ) and `` como te quiero yo a ti '' ( ) were digitally remastered . both of those songs were turned into ballad songs . the album had bonus tracks that her family tells fans how it was like when selena recorded those songs . the album peaked at number seven on the us regional mexican albums charts . the album also peaked at number eleven on the top latin albums chart on `` billboard '' in 2004. track listings . 1. amor prohibido 1. missing my baby 1. fotos y recuerdos 1. dreaming of you 1. buenos amigos 1. i 'm getting used to you 1. donde quiera que estes 1. only love 1. tú sólo tú 1. i could fall in love 1. si una vez 1. where did the feeling go ?
