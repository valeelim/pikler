Micula (surname)    micula ( surname ) micula ( , `` mîḵulāh '' ) is a surname of ashkenazi jewish origin . it is most popular in eastern europe . the name dates back to the 11th century , many jews moved to east europe to avoid persecution . in some countries the name is written as `` mikula '' , because of the slavic influence . people with the surname `` micula '' can be found in poland , russia , romania , slovakia , israel , slovenia , czech republic , ukraine and moldova .
