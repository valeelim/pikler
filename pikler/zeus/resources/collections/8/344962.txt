Plausible Prejudices: Essays on American Writing    plausible prejudices : essays on american writing plausible prejudices : essays on american writing is a book of essays about writing . the author is joseph epstein . `` kirkus reviews '' and `` los angeles times '' wrote about the book . the `` chicago tribune '' also had good things to say about the book . it said that epstein 's essays of literary criticism are his most interesting works and that they were definitely worth preserving together in a collection .
