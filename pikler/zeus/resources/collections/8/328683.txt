Sengoku period     the name `` sengoku '' was adopted by japanese historians . the sengoku period in japan mirrors the warring states period in chinese history . the sengoku period would precede a process of unification and the end of japan 's medieval period . three important and powerful daimyo were oda nobunaga , toyotomi hideyoshi , and tokugawa ieyasu . these three leaders did the most to bring japan together as one country . other important leaders include date masamune and akechi mitsuhide . related pages . - history of japan - oda nobunaga - toyotomi hideyoshi
