Interstate 470 (Ohio – West Virginia)    interstate 470 ( ohio – west virginia ) interstate 470 ( i-470 ) is a long loop of interstate 70. it goes around the city of wheeling , west virginia . the western end of the road is where it meets i-70 near blaine , ohio . traveling southeast through belmont county , the highway comes to the vietnam veterans memorial bridge which carries i-470 over the ohio river . after crossing the river into ohio county , west virginia , the highway turns northeast . it runs towards the wheeling neighborhood of elm grove . the road 's eastern end is at i-70 near wheeling park . on average , between 26,000 and 38,000 vehicles use the highway every day . during the rebuilding work on the wheeling tunnel , i-470 was one of two suggested detour routes to avoid the closed tunnel . other websites . - interstate 470 at aa roads - interstate 470 ( in ohio ) at state ends
