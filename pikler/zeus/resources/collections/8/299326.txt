Bermuda at the Olympics     bermuda was first at the olympic games in 1936. the international olympic committee 's official abbreviation for bermuda is ber . athletes from bermuda have been in every summer olympic games since then except the boycotted 1980 summer olympics . bermuda has also been in every winter olympic games since 1992. as of 2011 , clarence hill is the only bermudian to win an olympic medal . this makes bermuda the least populated nation [ 53,500 in 1976 ] in olympic history to win a medal at the summer olympics . the national olympic committee for bermuda was formed in 1935 and recognized by the international olympic committee in 1936. related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - bermuda profile at london2012.com - bermuda at olympic.org
