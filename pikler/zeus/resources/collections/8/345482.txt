Abdul Hamid (politician)    abdul hamid ( politician ) abdul hamid ( born 1 january 1944 ) is the speaker of the national parliament of bangladesh since 2009. hamid was born in mithamoin in the kishoreganj district . he started his political career while a student in kishoreganj . he was the vice president of gurudayal government college . he later became a lawyer in the kishoreganj judge court . he was president of kishoreganj bar association several times . he has been elected 7 times as a member of parliament from 1970 to 2009. on 25 january 2009 , he became the speaker of the national parliament of bangladesh . currently he is the 20th president of bangladesh .
