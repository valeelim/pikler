FC Dinamo Tbilisi     fc dinamo tbilisi is a football club from tbilisi , georgia . the club was one of the best in the soviet top league , but after the soviet union broke up , the club joined the newly created league , the umaglesi liga . the team won the uefa cup winners ' cup , soviet top league , soviet cup , umaglesi liga , georgian cup , georgian super cup and the cis cup . honours . - uefa cup winners ' cup : 1 1981 - soviet top league : 2 1964 , 1978 - soviet cup : 2 1976 , 1979 - georgian league : 13 1990 , 1991 , 1992 , 1993 , 1994 , 1995 , 1996 , 1997 , 1998 , 1999 , 2003 , 2005 , 2008 - georgian cup : 9 1992 , 1993 , 1994 , 1995 , 1996 , 1997 , 2003 , 2004 , 2009 - georgian super cup : 5 1996 , 1997 , 1999 , 2005 , 2008 - cis cup winner : 1 2004
