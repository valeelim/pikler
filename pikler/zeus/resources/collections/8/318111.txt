Chimera     chimaera or chimera can mean : mythology . - chimera ( mythology ) - mount chimaera , the region in lycia that some believe inspired the myth science . - chimaera ( genetics ) - chimera ( plant ) - chimera ( virus ) , a virus with genetic material from other organisms - chimera or fusion protein , a hybrid protein made by the splicing of two genes - chimera ( paleontology ) , a fossil put together with parts from different species - chimaera ( fish )
