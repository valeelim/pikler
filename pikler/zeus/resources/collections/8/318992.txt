Voting age     voting age is the age when a person is allowed to vote in political elections by governments . the majority of countries set it at 18 , but ages ranging from 16 to 21 exist . iran is the only sovereignty to ever have a voting age below 16 in recent history , setting it at 15 until it was raised to 18 recently . when the voting age was first established it was usually set at 21 , but in the latter half of the twentieth century most countries , including the united states , lowered it to 18. a few jurisdictions today , such as austria and the isle of man , have lowered it to 16 .
