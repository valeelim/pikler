Pop Tarts     pop-tarts is a brand of rectangular , pre-baked toaster pastries made by the kellogg company . pop-tarts have a sugary filling sealed inside two layers of rectangular , thin pastry crust . some varieties are frosted . although sold pre-cooked , they are designed to be warmed inside a toaster or microwave oven . they are usually sold in pairs inside foil packages , and do not require refrigeration .
