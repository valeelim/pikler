Killer Joe     killer joe is a 2011 crime comedy starring gina gershon . its distributor for the united states is liddell entertainment . the story of this comedy concerns a tough ( though quiet ) killer who befriends a young man who in turn wants his own mother taken out . `` killer joe '' received an nc-17 rating from the mpaa for `` graphic aberrant content involving violence and sexuality , and a scene of brutality '' . the distributor appealed the nc-17 rating decision , though they lost the appeal to get the rating lowered to an r rating .
