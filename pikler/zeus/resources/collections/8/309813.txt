Eduard Strauss     eduard strauss ( b. vienna , 15 march 1835 , d. vienna , 28 december 1916 ) was a viennese composer of light music . he was also a conductor . he was the brother of the more famous composers johann strauss ii and josef strauss , the son of johann strauss i , and the father of johann strauss iii . other websites . - eduard strauss on the johann strauss society of great britain - a complete list of eduard strauss ' compositions
