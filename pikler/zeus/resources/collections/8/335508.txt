Boric acid     boric acid is an acid of boron . its salts are called borates . boric acid is used as a disinfectant . it can also be used as a preservative , it has the e number 284. boric acid is a by-product of the production of certain forms of glass , porcelain and vitreous enamel . it is also used as a flame retardant . dissolved in water , it acts as a neutron absorber in nuclear power plants . boric acid is toxic . damage is done from absorbing or ingesting about 50 mg , per kg of body weight . boric acid is one of the chemicals on the reach list of the european union . in 2010 , the european chemicals agency listed it as a substance of very high concern . boric acid causes birth defects ; it is a teratogen . this is also true for mixtures that contain boric acid in a concentration of at least 5.5 % .
