Aunou-le-Faucon     aunou-le-faucon is a commune of the orne department in north-western france . there are 273 people living there . there is a church called eglise st-cyr-et-ste-julitte . the steeple dates from the 17th century . there is a fortified house dated from the 15th century , this is the `` tour aux anglais '' . there are many horse farms around .
