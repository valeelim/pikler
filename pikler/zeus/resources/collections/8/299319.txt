Bahamas at the Olympics     the bahamas were first at the olympic games in 1952. the international olympic committee 's official abbreviation for the bahamas is bah . athletes from the bahamas have been in every summer olympic games since then , except the boycotted 1980 summer olympics . the nation has not been in any winter olympic games . the national olympic committee for bahamas is the `` bahamas olympic association '' , and was formed in 1952. related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - bahamas profile at london2012.com - bahamas at olympic.org
