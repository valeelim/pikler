Dick Clark's New Year's Rockin' Eve with Ryan Seacrest    dick clark 's new year 's rockin ' eve with ryan seacrest dick clark 's new year 's rockin ' eve with ryan seacrest ( which was earlier called dick clark 's new year 's rockin ' eve ) is a new year 's show that was originally hosted by dick clark until he had a stroke during 2004. it airs on abc . it takes place in times square on december 31 of every year . it shows the yearly new year 's eve ball drop . on december 31 , 2004 , regis philbin hosted the show because of clark 's stroke . the show has been hosted by ryan seacrest on new year 's eve since december 31 , 2005. seacrest hosted with clark until clark 's death in 2012. singer-songwriter fergie and actress jenny mccarthy have reported for this show from los angeles and times square , respectively . in the december 31 , 1999 show , it was called `` abc 2000 today '' because 2000 would mark a new millennium . peter jennings hosted this show .
