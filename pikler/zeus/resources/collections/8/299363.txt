David Hernandez (baseball)    david hernandez ( baseball ) david jose hernandez ( born may 13 , 1985 in sacramento , california ) is an american baseball player . he currently plays as a relief pitcher for the arizona diamondbacks of major league baseball ( mlb ) . he was drafted by the baltimore orioles with the 483rd overall pick in the 16th round of the 2005 major league baseball draft . he debuted for the orioles on may 28 , 2009 in a game against the detroit tigers . he was traded to the arizona diamondbacks along with kam mickolio for mark reynolds and a player to be named later or cash . other websites . - david hernandez at mlb.com
