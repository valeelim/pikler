Crater Lake National Park     crater lake national park is a national park in the u.s. state of oregon . it was created in 1902. it is the only national park in oregon . the park includes the caldera of crater lake , the remains of the destroyed volcano mount mazama , and nearby hills and forests . the lake is deep . it is the deepest lake in the united states , the second deepest in north america and the ninth deepest in the world . the lake is so deep because of the caldera it is in . crater lake has no streams flowing into or out of it . all water that enters the lake is eventually lost from evaporation or flowing away underground . the lake is re-filled only from snow and rain .
