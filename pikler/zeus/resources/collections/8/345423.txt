Bani Jamra     bani jamra is a village in the north-west of bahrain . it is east of budaiya and south of diraz . it is in the northern governorate . before the discovery of oil in bahrain , most of the people were involved in farming , especially date palms . bani jamra is also famous as a center of traditional fabric weaving , a rapidly dying art . almost all of the people of bani jamra are baharna shias . leading cleric and political activist abdul amir al-jamri , and his son mansoor al-jamri are from the village . references . - bani jamra cloth weaving - bani jamra website
