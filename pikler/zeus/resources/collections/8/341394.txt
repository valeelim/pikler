Lester Bird     lester bryant bird ( born february 21 , 1938 ) was the second prime minister of antigua and barbuda from 1994 to 2004. he is also a well-known athlete . he was chairman of the antigua labour party ( alp ) from 1971 to 1983. he became prime minister previous prime minister resigned . the previous prime minister was his father , vere bird . bird was born in new york city on february 21 , 1938. he went to the university of michigan . he became a lawyer .
