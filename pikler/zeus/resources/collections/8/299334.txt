Brazil at the Olympics     brazil was first at the olympic games in 1920. the international olympic committee 's official abbreviation for brazil is bra . athletes from brazil have been in every summer olympic games since then , except the 1928 games . brazil has been in the winter olympic games since 1992. brazilian athletes have won a total of 108 medals , with volleyball , sailing and judo as the top medal-producing sports . the national olympic committee for brazil was formed in 1914 and recognized in 1935. brazil won the rights to host the 2016 olympics in the city of rio de janerio . related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - brazil profile at london2012.com - brazil at olympic.org
