Papua New Guinea at the Olympics     papua new guinea began competing at the olympic games in 1976. their athletes have been in every summer olympic games since , but not the 1980 summer olympics . the nation has never been in the winter olympic games . they have not won any medals . the national olympic committee for papua new guinea was formed in 1973. it was recognized by the international olympic committee in 1974. related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - papua new guinea profile at london2012.com
