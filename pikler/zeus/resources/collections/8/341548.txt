Phoebe Cates     phoebe belle cates ( born july 16 , 1963 ) is an american model and movie actress . she is best known for acting in movies in the `` gremlins '' franchise and for acting in the 1982 movie `` fast times at ridgemont high '' . cates was born on july 16 , 1963 in new york city , new york . she married actor kevin kline in 1989. they have two children .
