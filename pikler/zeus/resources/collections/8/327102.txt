Mindless Behavior     mindless behavior is an american boy band . they are best known for the singles `` my girl '' and `` mrs. right '' , which were produced by walter millsap . the band was put together in los angeles in 2008 , by keisha gamble , walter millsap ( who has previously worked with beyoncé and timbaland ) and vincent herbert ( lady gaga , toni braxton ) . the band trained in dance and singing for two years before releasing anything . the members are all in their early teens . they performed `` my girl '' on `` the today show '' in november 2010. their first album , `` # 1 girl '' , was released on september 20 , 2011. it appeared on the `` billboard '' 200 album chart at number 7. mindless behavior toured with the backstreet boys , justin bieber and jason derülo . they were the opening act for janet jackson 's 2011 tour . this must be pop says that it thinks mindless behavior will be future stars . members of the band . - ej - princeton - mike
