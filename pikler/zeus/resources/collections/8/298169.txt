Saltash     saltash is a town in south east cornwall , united kingdom . it has a population of 14,964. it is separated from the city of plymouth in devon by the river tamar . saltash is the largest town within the east cornwall area . one of the landmarks of saltash is the royal albert bridge , 1859 , which carries the railway line across the river tamar . alongside it is the tamar bridge , a toll bridge carrying the a38 trunk road . saltash railway station is close to the town centre . the station is served by a regular train service , with some direct high speed services to and from london paddington .
