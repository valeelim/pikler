Malverde     jesus malverde known simply as `` malverde '' is a mexican hip-hop singer . he got his name from the movie `` robin hood '' character of mexican folklore . malverde released his debut album , `` mi palabra '' ( `` my word '' ) in 2004. the album was certified gold ( sales of 50,000 copies ) by riaa , it also peaked at number-one on the us latin regional mexican albums .
