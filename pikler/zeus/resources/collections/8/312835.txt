I Saw Mommy Kissing Santa Claus     `` i saw mommy kissing santa claus '' is a christmas song with words and music by british songwriter tommie connor . the original recording by 13-year-old jimmy boyd rose to # 1 on the `` billboard '' charts in december 1952. the words tell of a child who sees mommy kissing santa claus underneath the mistletoe . the roman catholic church in boston hated the song because it is a christmas song that also mentions sex . radio stations in several markets banned it . boyd went to church leaders to talk about the words . the ban on the song was lifted . the song has been performed by various musicians , including but not limited to the ronettes , jackson 5 , jessica simpson , john mellencamp , amy winehouse and other singers throughout the years .
