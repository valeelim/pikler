Association des professionnels navigants de l'aviation     the association des professionnels navigants de l'aviation ( apna ) ( in ) is a french aeronautical non-profit organization created in 1927 in order to `` group , in a close community all those who belong , by their profession , to the civil aviation , and participate in the development of the national aeronautics , in conjunction with the public authorities , the manufacturers , the airlines and the professionals unions '' .
