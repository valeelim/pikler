2011–12 Russian Premier League     the 2011–12 russian premier league is the 20th season of the russian premier league since the breakup of the soviet union . the season will go from march 12 , 2011 to the spring of 2012. currently , the team at the top is zenit st. petersburg . also , the defending champions are zenit st. petersburg . teams . - amkar perm - anzhi makhachkala - cska moscow - dynamo moscow - krasnodar - krylia sovetov samara - kuban krasnodar - lokomotiv moscow - rostov - rubin kazan - spartak moscow - terek grozny - tom tomsk - volga nizhny novgorod - zenit st. petersburg
