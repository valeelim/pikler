Graham Greene     henry graham greene , om , ch ( 2 october 1904 - 3 april 1991 ) was a british writer . greene was born in berkhamsted , hertfordshire . he graduated with a second-class degree from balliol college , oxford . he was a journalist before he became a novelist . greene wrote novels , autobiographies , travel books , plays , screenplays , short stories and children 's books . his catholicism influenced his work . greene had bipolar disorder . he died of leukaemia in vevey , switzerland .
