LGBT rights in the Republic of China     the republic of china ( taiwan ) is one of asia 's most tolerant countries as far as lesbian , gay , bisexual , transgender ( lgbt ) rights are concerned . the government proposed a same-sex marriage in 2003 which has so far stalled without passage . if the law passes , the roc would be the first country in asia to allow same-sex marriages . the roc is also described as the most liberal after over 30,000 people went to the taiwan pride parade in 2010 and 2011 , making it the largest lgbt event in asia . other websites . - taiwan pride
