Bioorganic chemistry     bioorganic chemistry is a science that combines organic chemistry and biochemistry . bioorganic chemistry expands organic chemistry towards biology . this differs from biochemistry , which studies biological processes using chemistry . when investigating enzymes and cofactors , bioorganic chemistry overlaps bioinorganic chemistry . biophysical organic chemistry is a term used when attempting to describe intimate details of molecular recognition by bioorganic chemistry . bioorganic chemistry is that branch of life science , which deals with the study of biological processes using chemical methods .
