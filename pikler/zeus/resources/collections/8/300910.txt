Shadows of the Damned     shadows of the damned , also known as damned or dmd , is a first-person shooter and horror video game developed by grasshopper manufacture and published by electronic arts . the game was made for the playstation 3 and xbox 360. the game was released on june 21 , 2011 in north america and on june 24 , 2011 in europe . characters . - garcia hotspur - a demon hunter . - johnson - a demon that assists garcia . - paula - garcia 's girlfriend . - fleming - the demon in charge of the underworld . - christopher - a half-human/demon hybrid . other websites . - shadows of the damned 's official website
