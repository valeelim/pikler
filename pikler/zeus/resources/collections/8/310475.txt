Satellite television     satellite television is a way of sending television signals . television channels go a station on the ground , which sends them by radio waves to a communications satellite in space . the satellite then transmits that signal back to earth . people with a satellite dish can receive the signals . very often , satellite television is provided as a pay television service ( especially to places where cable tv is not available ) . here are some examples : - sky ( uk ) - freesat ( uk ) - dish network ( us ) - astro ( satellite tv )
