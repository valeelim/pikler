Pretty Vacant     `` pretty vacant '' is a song by the english punk rock band the sex pistols . it was released in 1977 , as the third single from their album `` never mind the bollocks , here 's the sex pistols '' . the song is infamous for vocalist john lydon 's phrasing of the word `` vacant '' , emphasizing the last syllable making it sound like the vulgar word `` cunt '' . the b-side is a cover of the stooges ' `` no fun '' which they played on the spot without a proper rehearsal .
