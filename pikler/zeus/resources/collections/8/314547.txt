MC Ren     lorenzo jerald patterson ( born june 14 , 1969 ) is an american rapper . he is best known by his stage name mc ren . mc ren was a member of the gangsta rap group n.w.a . like all n.w.a members ( except ice cube ) he was born and raised in compton , california . he joined n.w.a after completing his senior year of high school . he was with n.w.a on all their studio and ep releases . problems over money caused n.w.a to end . mc ren and all the other members of n.w.a went solo . using eazy e 's record label , ruthless records , ren released his first album `` shock of tha hour '' in 1993. albums . - `` shock of tha hour '' ( 1993 ) - `` the villain in black '' ( 1996 ) - `` ruthless for life '' ( 1998 ) - `` reincarnated '' ( 2009 ) other websites . - official website
