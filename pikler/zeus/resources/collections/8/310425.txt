École spéciale de mécanique et électricité    école spéciale de mécanique et électricité école spéciale de mécanique et électricité , commonly named esme-sudria , is a french private high school establishment located in paris , ivry-sur-seine , bordeaux , lille and lyon , france . esme-sudria is specialized in the fields of electrical engineering , electronics , mechanics , telecommunications , and computer engineering . the school is part of ionis education group . list of esme sudria heads . - joachim sudria : 1905 - 1950 - pierre doceul : 1950 - 1980 - jean doceul : 1980 - 2003 - hervé laborne : 2003 - 2011 - roger ceschi : 2011 - 2014 - eric simon : 2014 - 2015 - véronique bonnet : since 2015 notable alumni . - marc sellam ( 1974 ) , founder and current president of ionis education group .
