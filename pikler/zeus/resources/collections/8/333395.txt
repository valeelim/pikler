Troll (gay slang)    troll ( gay slang ) troll and trolling are gay slang terms . troll is used to characterize gay , bisexual and questioning or bi-curious men who cruise or `` wander about looking '' for sex or potential sex partners or experiences `` in a notably wanton manner and with lessened standards of what one will accept in a partner . '' the term can be used positively or negatively depending on the speaker , usage , and intent . the term can describe the person or the activity . although often referring to `` an unattractive older gay man '' and although troll as a slur `` is primarily a visual , not a behavioral '' judgment , the terms can be used for anyone who is `` trolling '' regardless of the putative troll 's age or perceived beauty .
