West Side Park     west side park is the name of a former baseball park in chicago , illinois . it was the home field of the chicago cubs baseball team from 1893 through 1915. it was the cubs ' home ballpark when they won their only world series championships , in 1907 and 1908. the team moved to wrigley field in 1916. the park got its name from being located on the west side of chicago . where the ballpark once stood there is now a building in the university of illinois medical school .
