University of North Carolina at Asheville     university of north carolina at asheville is a co-educational , public liberal arts university in asheville , north carolina . the university has about 3,644 students . it was founded in 1927. the current chancellor of the university is anne ponder . the athletics teams of the university are called the bulldogs . they have 6 varsity teams for men and 6 for women , most of which compete in the big south conference . other websites . - university of north carolina at asheville 's official website - unc asheville bulldogs ' official athletics website
