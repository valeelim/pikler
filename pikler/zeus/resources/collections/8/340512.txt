Sétif Province     sétif province is a province in north-eastern algeria . the capital and largest city is sétif . another large city is el eulma . there is also the world heritage site of djémila there . the province has 20 districts . there are also 60 `` communes '' or municipalities . districts . 1. aïn arnat 1. aïn azel 1. aïn el kébira 1. aïn oulmane 1. amoucha 1. babor 1. béni aziz 1. béni ourtilane 1. bir el arch 1. bouandas 1. bougaâ 1. djémila 1. el eulma 1. guenzet 1. guidjel 1. hammam guergour 1. hammam souhna 1. maoklane 1. salah bey 1. sétif
