Kitakyushu Municipal Museum of Art     the museum acquires , conserves , researches , communicates and exhibits a collection of 6,000 artworks . the museum shows the work of japanese artists , like hokusai 's woodblock print `` the great wave off kanagawa '' by hokusai . the collection of western art includes a portrait of edouard manet and his wife painted by edgar degas in 1869. the current building was designed by arata isozaki . it was built in 1974. the municipal art collection was established in the 19th century . other websites . - kitakyushu municipal museum of art website - `` campaign & products , '' 2004 exhibition
