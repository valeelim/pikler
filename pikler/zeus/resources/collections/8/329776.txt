The Fall of the House of Usher     the fall of the house of usher is a short story by edgar allan poe . it was first published in september 1839 in `` burton 's gentleman 's magazine '' . it was slightly revised in 1840 and published in a collection of poe 's stories called `` tales of the grotesque and arabesque '' . the poem `` the haunted palace '' is part of the story . vincent price and myrna fahey starred in a 1960 roger corman movie based on the story . roderick usher believes he is sick and dying . his house is crumbling . usher has a sister , madeleine . she falls into a catatonic state . she appears dead . roderick knows she is not dead . he places her in the family tomb . on a stormy night , she rises from the tomb . she falls upon her brother . she is dead . her brother dies from terror . the house crumbles and falls . other websites . - ( audiobook ) - full text as reprinted in `` the works of the late edgar allan poe '' ( 1850 ) - full text at bartelby.com - `` the fall of the house of usher '' with annotated vocabulary at poestories.com
