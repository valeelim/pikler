Symphyla     symphyla are soil-dwelling arthropods in the subphylum myriapoda . they are relatives of centipedes . symphylans look like centipedes , but are smaller and translucent . they move through the pores between soil particles . they are found from the surface down to a depth of about 50 cm . they eat decaying vegetation , and can do harm in agriculture by eating seeds , roots , and root hairs in cultivated soil .
