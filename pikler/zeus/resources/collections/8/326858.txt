Pus     pus is a fluid that is created as a result of certain inflammations in vertebrates . in vertebrates , white blood cells fight the inflammation . neutrophils destroy the agents that cause the infection , for example bacteria , macrophages engulf and neutralise these agents . pus is made of dead neutrophils and macrophages , mostly . in most cases , pus is the result of bacterial infection , but it can also result from other conditions , such as psoriasis .
