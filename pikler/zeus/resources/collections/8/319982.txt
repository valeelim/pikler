Kieran Ault-Connell     kieran ault-connell ( born 30 july 1981 ) is an australian athlete with cerebral palsy . he was born in melbourne . at the 2000 summer paralympics , he won two gold medals in the men 's 4 x 100m relay t38 and men 's 4 x 400m relay t38 races . at the 2004 summer paralympics , he won a silver medal in the men 's javelin f37 event .
