Giorgione     giorgio barbarelli da castelfranco ( born 1477 or 1478 , died 1510 ) was a venetian painter . he is commonly known as giorgione . he died a little over 30 , probably of the plague . today , only about six paintings can be clearly attributed to him . this makes him one of the most mysterious painters of his time . together with titian he was one of the most influential painters of his period.he was one of the first venitian painters to use oil paints for a medium .
