Pope Damasus II     pope damasus ii ( ; died august 9 , 1048 ) , born poppo , was a german priest of the roman catholic church and the 152nd pope for only 24 days from july 17 to august 9 , 1048. he was the third german to become pope . he was born in bavaria . poppo was bishop of brixen in the tyrol mountains of germany.. bishop popo became pope on july 17 , 1048 ; and he took the name damasas ii . he died within a month after his election . related pages . - list of popes - list of popes by length of reign other websites . - catholic hierarchy , popes in sequence
