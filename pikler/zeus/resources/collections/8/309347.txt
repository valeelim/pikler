UEFA Euro 2012 qualifying Group A     this page shows the results for group a of the uefa euro 2012 qualification . germany qualified , and the turkey went to the playoffs , which in the end they did not qualify . germany did not lose a single game , with a record of 10 wins . the top scorer of the group was miroslav klose ( germany ) with 9 goals . - green means that the team automatically qualifies . - blue means the team is going to the playoffs .
