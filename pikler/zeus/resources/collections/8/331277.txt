The Honeymooners     the honeymooners is a 30-minute american television sitcom series . jackie gleason plays new york city bus driver named ralph kramden , audrey meadows plays his wife alice , art carney plays his friend ed norton , a sewer worker , and joyce randolph plays ed 's wife trixie . the show aired on cbs from 1 october 1955 to 22 september 1956. it was a great success . `` the honeymooners '' dropped in the ratings after 39 episodes and its run ended .
