Vitebsk Region     vitebsk region , vitsebsk voblast , or vitebsk oblast is a region ( voblast ) of belarus . the administrative center is vitebsk ( viciebsk ) . as of a 2009 , the region had a population of 1,230,800. important cities within the region include vitebsk , orsha , and navapolatsk . the vitsebsk voblast is divided into 21 raions , 5 cities of oblast subordinance , 19 additional cities , 249 selsovets , and 26 urban-type settlements .
