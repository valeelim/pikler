Rudolph Nureyev     rudolf khametovich nureyev ( 17 march 1938 â€“ 6 january 1993 ) was a russian ballet dancer . he was one of the most celebrated dancers of the 20th century . in 1961 he defected to the west , despite kgb efforts to prevent him . for some years he partnered dame margot fonteyn . the `` dictionary of dance '' says `` his repertoire was enormous , including all the classics and the modern standards '' . after his period with fonteyn , nureyev worked in several countries . nureyev was director , principal dancer and choreographer of `` nureyev and friends '' on broadway ( 197475 ) and was artistic director of the paris opera house from 1983 to 1989. he promoted junior dancers , such as sylvie guillem , and commissioned new works . in the last years of his life he took up conducting . nureyev was gay . he and his partner , erik bruhn , died of aids .
