Pan Am Flight 103     pan am flight 103 was a pan american world airways passenger flight that flew from london to new york city across the atlantic ocean . on 21 december 1988 , the aeroplane flying this route was destroyed by a bomb . the explosion killed everybody on the plane . it exploded over lockerbie , in southern scotland , and eleven more people on the ground were killed by the falling plane . the event is also known as the lockerbie bombing . the aircraft was a boeing 747–121 named `` “ clipper maid of the seas ” '' . the explosion killed all 243 passengers and 16 crew members . eleven more people in lockerbie were killed as large parts of the plane fell in the town and crashed into houses . the total number of people killed in the disaster was 270. after many years of investigation , the attack was blamed on airline officials working for libya 's government . abdelbaset al-megrahi , a libyan man , was convicted of murder over the attack , and later freed as he had deadly cancer . megrahi died almost three years later . libyan leader muammar gaddafi was also said to have ordered the attack .
