Bogra     bogra is one of the oldest towns in northern bangladesh . it is a centre of commerce and trade within the bogra district . it is under the rajshahi division . they have hosted cricket test matches in shaheed chandu stadium . bogra is also home to the western wing of bangladesh highway police . ziaur rahman , former president of bangladesh , and muhammad ali bogra , a former prime minister of pakistan , were both born in the district . bogra is often called the heart of north-bengal .
