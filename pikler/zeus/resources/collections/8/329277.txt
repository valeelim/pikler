Runaway (movie)    runaway ( movie ) runaway is a science fiction and action movie released near christmas 1984 , starring kirstie alley and tom selleck . alley plays former lover for a villain named luther . selleck plays a police officer whose son is eventually kidnapped by the villain . the movie tells of complex technology gone awry when permitted to dominate the lives of everybody . also acting in the movie is gene simmons from kiss . the movie was planned as a major science fiction draw , although it was overshadowed by a low budget movie called `` the terminator '' , which was directed by then-not well known james cameron .
