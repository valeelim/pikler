Rumour Has It (Adele song)    rumour has it ( adele song ) `` rumour has it '' is the fourth hit single from the adele album `` 21 '' . it was released in 5 november 2011 ; the song was given lots of positive reviews from critics in music . the song was written by adele along with ryan tedder of onerepublic , who also produced the song . it reached number 16 on the charts in the us and canada . in the us , it was certified double-platinum for shipments of over two million copies .
