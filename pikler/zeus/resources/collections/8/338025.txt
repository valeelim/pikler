Premier of Tasmania     the premier of tasmania is the head of the government in the australian state of tasmania . the political party which has the most support in the house of assembly will nominate its leader to be premier . the leader is then asked by the governor of tasmania to act as their principal adviser . the current premier is will hodgman of the liberal party of australia , who was sworn in on 31 march 2014. before the 1890s , there was no political party system in tasmania . before 1920 premiers were often appointed from the legislative council .
