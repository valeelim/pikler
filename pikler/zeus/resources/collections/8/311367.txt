Frankie J     frankie j ( born on february 16 1980 in baja , california , usa ) is an american singer-songwriter . frankie j had first appeared on a.b . quintanilla iii 's band `` kumbia kings '' . he later left the band to become a solo artist . his debut album `` what 's a man to do '' was released on may 27 , 2003. in 2005 , frankie j released his third studio album `` the one '' and debuted at number three on the `` billboard 200 '' chart . it had sold 130,000 copies in one week . it was then certified platinum by the riaa for shipments of 1 million copies . discography . albums . - performed in spanish
