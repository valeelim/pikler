Jason Taylor     jason paul taylor ( born september 1 , 1974 ) is a former american football defensive end and linebacker who played in nfl for the miami dolphins , washington redskins and new york jets . taylor was drafted by the miami dolphins in the third round ( 73rd pick overall ) of the 1997 nfl draft . he also performed on the sixth season of dancing with the stars and finished as the runner up to kristi yamaguchi . on december 28 , 2011 , taylor announced that he would retire at the end of the 2011 season .
