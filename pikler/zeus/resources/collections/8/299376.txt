Omaha Ak-Sar-Ben Knights     the omaha ak-sar-ben knights were a professional ice hockey team that played in the american hockey league from 2005 to 2007. they played in omaha , nebraska at the omaha civic auditorium . they were the farm team of the calgary flames . they moved to omaha in 2005 from saint john , new brunswick where they were known as the saint john flames . they moved operations to moline , illinois where they became the quad city flames . other websites . - an archive of the omaha ak-sar-ben knights ' official site
