Riverside Stadium     the riverside stadium is a football stadium in middlesbrough , england . the stadium is the homeground of middlesbrough f.c . since its opening . its present capacity is 34,742 all seated . there is provisional permission to expand the stadium to 42,000 if it is needed . the stadium was built in 1994/95 . it replaced ayresome park . after the taylor report from 1990 all top league clubs must have all seater stadiums . because the space was limited to enlarge ayresome park the club decided to built a new stadium . this stadium was built in only 32 weeks on the middlehaven site by the river tees . the building had 30 000 seats but was enlarged to its present capacity in 1998 . 4 900 seats were built in the north and west corners . during the construction of the new wembley stadium the english nationalteam toured the country . the riverside was chosen to host the euro 2004 qualifying match against slovakia on 11 june 2003. but also some u21 matches were held . records . - record attendance ( all teams ) : 35,000 england v slovakia , 11 june 2003 , ( euro 2004 qualifier )
