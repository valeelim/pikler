Vanilla Ice     robert matthew van winkle ( born october 31 , 1967 in dallas , texas ) , best known as vanilla ice , is an american rap singer and songwriter . he had songs under sbk records . his most notable song is `` ice ice baby '' . he was nominated for a grammy award for this song in 1991 but did not win . he has had other songs released over the years . comedian jim carrey did a spoof on the song , called `` white white baby '' . vanilla ice was a contestant in the second season of `` the surreal life '' in 2004 and in the sixth series of `` dancing on ice '' in 2011. other websites . - http : //www.imdb.com/name/nm0406678/bio
