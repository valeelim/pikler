Strictly Come Dancing     strictly come dancing is a british television programme . it features celebrities dancing with professional dancers . in each series , the couples compete to decide a winner . it is shown on bbc one and is presented by bruce forsyth and tess daly . it is narrated by alan dedicoat . the judges since it began are len goodman , craig revel horwood and bruno tonioli . arlene phillips was a judge from 2004-2008 ; alesha dixon was a judge from 2009-2011. darcey bussell is the current judge in alesha dixon 's place . there were two series in its first year , 2004. from 2005 onwards , there has been one series per year . many countries have their own version of the show ( see `` dancing with the stars '' ) .
