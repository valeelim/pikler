Kootenay Ice     the kootenay ice are a canadian professional ice hockey team that began playing in 1998. they currently play in the western hockey league . they play their home games in cranbrook , british columbia at the cranbrook recreational complex . the team was originally known as the `` edmonton ice '' from 1996 to 1998 before it moved to cranbrook in 1998. the team has won 3 ed chynoweth cups in the 1999-00 , 2001-02 and 2010-11 seasons . other websites . - official website
