Victoria River (Northern Territory)    victoria river ( northern territory ) the victoria river is in longest river in the northern territory of australia . it flows into the timor sea . philip parker king discovered the mouth of the river on 12 september 1819. in 1839 , the crew of hms beagle travelled more than 200 km up the river . in 1854 the explorer augustus charles gregory reached the river after crossing the north of australia from brisbane , queensland .
