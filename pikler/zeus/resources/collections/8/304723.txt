Jefferson Cardoso dos Santos     jefferson cardoso dos santos ( 15 february 1986 in são vicente , brazil ) is a brazilian footballplayer . he plays for f.c . red bull salzburg in the austrian bundesliga . he is a defender . he started playing football with his local club são vicente atlético clube and then went on to jabaquara atlético clube in brazils 4th league . he became champion with this team and then went on to red bull brasil . with this team he was two times promoted . in summer 2011 he and his team mate alex rafael were invited to train with f.c . red bull salzburg . he played two test matches for salzburg ( bayer 04 leverkusen , olympique lyon ) . he convinced the club and so he was signed for red bull salzburg . after some matches as substitution he played his first match in the starting team on 21 august 2011 in a league match versus s.k . sturm graz . honours . - 1x champion campeonato paulista série b ( 4th league ) : 2009 - 1x champion campeonato paulista série a3 ( 3rd league ) : 2010 - 1x finale copa paulista : 2010
