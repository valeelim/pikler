Trey Lorenz     trey lorenz ( born lloyd lorenz smith ; january 19 , 1969 ) is an american singer and songwriter . he has been a backing vocalist for mariah carey and selena . his 1992 single `` someone to hold '' , reached number 19 on the us `` billboard '' hot 100 , number five on the hot r & b/hip hop songs and number 65 on the uk singles chart . discography . - `` trey lorenz '' ( 1992 ) - `` mr. mista '' ( 2006 )
