Goh Chok Tong     bole goh chok tong ( ; born 20 may 1941 ) was the second prime minister of the republic of singapore . a member of the people 's action party ( pap ) , he became prime minister on 28 november 1990 , succeeding lee kuan yew , and served in the role until 12 august 2004 , when he stepped down and was succeeded by lee hsien loong . he subsequently served as singapore 's senior minister until may 2011 , and as the chairman of the monetary authority of singapore . he continues to serve as a member of parliament ( mp ) representing the marine parade group representation constituency and holds the honorary title of `` emeritus senior minister '' . other websites . - http : //app.sgdi.gov.sg/listing.asp ? agency_subtype=dept & agency_id=0000000014
