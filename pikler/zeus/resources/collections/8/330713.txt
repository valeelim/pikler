Laura Linney     laura leggett linney ( born february 5 , 1964 ) is an american actress . she was born in manhattan , new york city . she is the elder daughter of romulus linney ( 1930-2011 ) . she has acted in movies , on television and theater . she won a golden globe award for playing abigail adams in `` john adams '' . she is best known for playing the central role of cathy jamison in `` the big c '' , for which she won a golden globe award .
