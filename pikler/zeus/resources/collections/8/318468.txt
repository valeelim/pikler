Anapsid     an anapsid is an amniote whose skull does not have openings on the sides behind the eyes . this is 1. a primitive ( 'basal ' ) condition for early amniotes in the mississippian ( early carboniferous ) . 1. a secondary ( 'derived ' ) condition of some other reptile groups , probably including turtles ( chelonia ) . some other groups were anapsids , such as the mesosaurs .
