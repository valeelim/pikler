Evacuate the Dancefloor     `` evacuate the dancefloor '' is a hit song off the album of the same name by german music group cascada . the single was released as the lead single of the album june 29 , 2009 , debuting at # 1 on the uk singles chart . it was their third hit song in the united states on many music charts . the song also hit top 5 in canada , france and certain other countries .
