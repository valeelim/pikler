Melody Thornton     melody thornton ( born september 28 , 1984 ) is an american r & b singer-songwriter , vocalist , dancer , model , showgirl and television personality . she became famous because she was in the singing group the pussycat dolls . she was the youngest member of the group . she had the second most important singing role , next to the lead singer nicole scherzinger . she was noticed for her melismatic vocal runs . in 2010 , thornton left the group to work on her solo career .
