Tú Robaste Mi Corazón     `` tu robaste mi corazón '' ( ) is a duet by mexican-american singer selena and mexican singer emilio navaira . `` tu robaste mi corazon '' was the third and last single from `` selena live ! '' ( 1993 ) . in `` siempre selena '' ( 1996 ) , the male parts of the song was performed by pete astudillo . the song peaked at number five on the hot latin tracks , number six on the latin pop airplay and number eight on the latin regional mexican airplay charts .
