Jessica Ennis     jessica ennis , cbe ( born 28 january 1986 ) is an english gold medal-winning athlete . she was born in sheffield to a jamaican father and english mother . ennis won a gold medal in the indoor world championships in doha . this was for pentathlon . jessica was awarded with mbe for being an outstanding athlete . in jessica 's first competition in 2011 she set an indoor personal best of 14.11 metres in the shot put . she improved this record by 50 centimetres a week later at an indoor meeting in loughborough . on 29 january 2012 , ennis captained the british team at an international meeting in glasgow . she competed in the sixty-metres hurdles and won in a time of 7.97 seconds . at the 2011 world athletics championships in daegu , south korea , ennis finished second to tatyana chernova with a score of 6,751 points , 129 points behind chernova and 72 points below her own personal best of 6,823 points . although ennis beat chernova in five of the seven events , her defeat was largely due to chernova scoring 251 more points in the javelin throw .
