Stupid (Sarah McLachlan song)    stupid ( sarah mclachlan song ) `` stupid '' is a song written by sarah mclachlan and produced by pierre marchand for mclachlan 's eighth album `` afterglow '' ( 2003 ) . it was released as the album 's second single in australia and the united states in mid-2004 . the music video features mclachlan in different time periods and was directed by sophie muller . the song became mclachlan 's highest charting single release in australia , debuting at number thirty-seven in early june 2004. it spent seven weeks on the australian aria singles chart , two of which were in the top fifty . a remix of the song , titled `` the mark bell mix '' was featured on so you think you can dance . track listing . - cd single 1 . `` stupid '' — 3:26 1 . `` stupid '' ( hyper remix ) — 3:26 1 . `` world on fire '' ( junkie xl club mix ) — 12:21 1. enhanced with `` stupid '' music video
