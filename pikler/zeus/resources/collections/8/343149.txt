Jean-Louis Vaudoyer     jean-louis vaudoyer ( 10 september 1883 20 may 1963 ) was a french novelist , poet , essayist , and art historian . he was administrator general of the comédie-française ( 1941–1944 ) . he was a member of the académie française ( 1950–1963 ) . he wrote the libretto for `` le spectre de la rose '' , a ballet choreographed by michel fokine for diaghilev 's ballets russes in 1911. other websites . - académie française
