Kansai International Airport     the airport is on an artificial island in osaka bay , southwest of ōsaka station . it opened 4 september 1994 , and the gross area is 1,068ha . this airport has two runways . one is 3500m×60m , and the other is 4000×60m . in 2011 , the total number of passengers was 13,651,184 ( 9,902,449 were international and 3,748,735 were domestic ) , and of landing was 53,899 ( 38,252 were international and 15,647 were domestic ) . related pages . - list of airports in japan other websites . - kansai international airport , operations ; - kansai international airport , corporate - kansai international airport , development
