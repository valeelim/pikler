Mariner 9     mariner 9 was a nasa spacecraft . it was the ninth spacecraft in the mariner programme . it was sent to study and orbit the planet mars . it was the first spacecraft to orbit another planet . mariner 9 was launched on may 30 , 1971 from cape canaveral air force station . mariner 9 reached mars on november 13 , 1971 , after a ~170 day flight . the spacecraft was sent to map 70 % of the surface of mars and to study the temperature changes of the martian atmosphere and surface .
