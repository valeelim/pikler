ASTech     astech paris région is a french group of aerospace engineering companies and research centres . it is in the region of île-de-france in the middle of france and is mainly concentrated in and around the city of paris . there are over 500 companies , including dassault aviation , eads and air france industries . about 100.000 people work there in the aviation and space flight industries . the headquarters of astech is in meudon . the chairman of the group is gérard laruelle . other websites . - website of astech paris région
