Rorathang     roarthang is a small village market . it is in the east sikkim . rorathang has a latitude of 27.18 degrees and a longitude of 88.62. we can find different types people in that locality on the basic of caste and so on . there is one junior high school just near the market . around 6-7 policemen are working or doing their duties . just 1 km from rorethang , between rhenock and rorethang , there is one medicine factory . it is famously know to be golden cross which is privately owned and run by a company .
