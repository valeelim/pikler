Dan Rather     dan rather ( born october 31 , 1931 in wharton , texas ) is an american journalist and reporter . he worked with the cbs network until 2006. controversy over documents rather reported on that he claimed indicated president george w. bush avoided military service during vietnam caused him to leave `` cbs evening news '' in 2005 , his last broadcast on that program was march 9 , 2005 and the whole network altogether two years later . he is now reporting for hdnet . he has received many emmy awards , several peabody awards , and various honorary degrees from universities .
