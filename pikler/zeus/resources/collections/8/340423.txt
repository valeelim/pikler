Indian Armed Forces     the indian armed forces are the military of india . it is made up of an army , navy and air force . there are other parts like paramilitary and strategic nuclear command . the president of india is its commander-in-chief . however , it is managed by the ministry of defence . in 2010 , the indian armed forces had 1.32 million active personnel . this makes it one of the largest militaries in the world . currently , the indian army is becoming more modern by buying and making new weapons . it is also building defences against missiles of other countries . in 2011 , india imported more weapons than any other nation in the world . from its independence in 1947 , india fought four wars with pakistan and one war with china . every year , 7 december is celebrated as armed forces flag day .
