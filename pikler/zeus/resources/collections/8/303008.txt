The Plastic People of the Universe     the plastic people of the universe ( or shortly ppu ) is a rock band from prague , czechoslovakia ( today czech republic ) . in 60s and 70s was frontman bass player milan `` mejla '' hlavsa ( *1951-†2001 ) and current frontman is saxophonist vratislav brabenec ( *1943 ) . on his `` debut '' album `` egon bondy 's happy hearts club banned '' , the group set to music poems by egon bondy . the band became famous when it was put in prison by the communist government . selected discography . 1 . `` muž bez uší '' ( live recordings 1969-72 ) 1 . `` vožralej jak slíva '' ( live recordings 1973-75 ) 1 . `` egon bondy 's happy hearts club banned '' ( 1974 ) 1 . `` ach to státu hanobení '' ( live recordings 1976-77 ) 1 . `` pašijové hry velikonoční '' ( 1978 ) 1 . `` jak bude po smrti '' ( 1979 ) 1 . `` co znamená vésti koně '' ( 1981 ) 1 . `` kolejnice duní '' ( 1977–82 ) 1 . `` hovězí porážka '' ( 1983–84 ) 1 . `` půlnoční myš '' ( 1985–86 ) 1 . `` líně s tebou spím - lazy love / in memoriam mejla hlavsa '' ( 2001 ) 1 . `` pašijové hry / passion play ( with agon orchestra ) '' ( 2004 )
