Bidi Bidi Bom Bom     `` bidi bidi bom bom '' is a song by american singer selena . before `` bidi bidi bom bom '' was written , selena had performed cover versions of popular songs while on tour . while touring on the entre a mi mundo tour , selena had decided to perform a song she had recently written . the song was called `` little bidi bubbles '' , `` little bubbles '' and `` swim swim swim ! '' the song was in english and had talked about a fish swimming in the ocean happily . the fish was given only one wish and had decided to give that wish away to another fish she had really liked . the song was performed in the style of roots reggae mixed with cumbia music . the song had became a sound check for selena y los dinos , after selena had written the song . initially , selena had wanted to perform the song to her fans and see their reactions before she would professionally record it . the song was not recorded even though it was given positive feedback from fans . a.b . quintanilla iii , selena 's brother , was short on songs to be recorded for selena 's next studio album . a.b had wanted to record an `` exotic progressive '' album that would help selena to expand her fan base even bigger . he then decided to finish writing `` little bidi bubbles '' and turn it into a caribbean reggae song mixed with colombian cumbia to attract fans from the caribbean . pete astudillo , a former back-up dancer for the band , also helped with writing the song . chris perez , the widower of selena , had brought rock fusion and guitar solos into the song . the song was written in spanish and was produced by a.b. , selena 's father abraham quintanilla jr and bebu silvetti . brian `` red '' moore , a family friend , had mixed the song . roger emerson became the music arranger for the song . `` bidi bidi bom bom '' was recorded in q-productions in corpus christi , texas .
