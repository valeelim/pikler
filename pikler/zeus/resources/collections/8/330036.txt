Pitjantjatjara language     pitjantjatjara ( or ) is one of the western desert languages of australia . it is often considered a dialect . it is spoken by the pitjantjatjara people of central australia . it is mutually intelligible with other languages of the western desert and is very closely related to the yankunytjatjara language . the name of the people comes from their word for coming/going : `` '' . other websites . - ethnologue - languages and dialects associated with uluru
