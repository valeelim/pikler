Auðr     auðr ( old norse `` prosperity '' ) or aud is in the norse mythology a son of naglfari and nótt , and half-brother of jörð ( earth ) and dagr ( day ) . he is an uncle of thor . jörð is called `` auðr 's splendid sister '' . scholar rudolf simek theorizes that auðr is the invention of snorri sturluson . simek notes that auðr is a female name in other icelandic sources .
