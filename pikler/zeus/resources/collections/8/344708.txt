List of National Football League stadiums     the following is a list of current national football league stadiums , sorted by how many people it holds , their locations , their first year of usage and home teams . the new york giants and new york jets share a stadium ( metlife stadium ) , thus there are only 31 nfl stadiums . the nfl uses several other stadiums on a regular basis in addition to the teams ' designated regular home sites . the jacksonville jaguars currently have a agreement to play one regular season home game per year at wembley stadium in london , england , as part of the nfl international series through 2020. fawcett stadium in canton , ohio , is the location of the annual exhibition pro football hall of fame game , and aloha stadium in honolulu , hawaii , is usually the location of the pro bowl . the new york giants and new york jets metlife stadium is the largest stadium in the nfl by its regular seating capacity . the dallas cowboys at & t stadium can expand past that of metlife stadium by means of using standing-room and temporary seating , which makes at & t stadium the only nfl stadium capable of holding 100,000 or more spectators , though it normally only seats 80,000. the smallest permanent nfl stadium is soldier field , the home of the chicago bears . in the 2015 season , the smallest was tcf bank stadium , temporary home of the minnesota vikings while the team 's former home , the metrodome , was replaced on-site by u.s. bank stadium .
