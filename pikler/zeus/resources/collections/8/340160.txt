Elizabeth Banks     elizabeth banks ( born february 10 1974 ) is an american television and movie actress . she was born in pittsfield , massachusetts . her movies include the `` spider-man '' franchise , 2008 's comedy `` zack and miri make a porno '' , `` invincible '' , `` the hunger games '' , and in `` the lego movie '' . banks is a democrat . she attended the 2012 democratic national convention to support president obama 's re-election .
