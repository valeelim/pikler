Hwang Hui     hwang hui ( , hanja : 黃喜 , 1363 - 1452 ) was a korean goryeo dynasty and joseon dynasty scholar and politician . he was the prime minister of joseon dynasty from 1431 to 1449. he was the adopted son of politician hwang gun-seo ( 황군서 , 黃君瑞 ) , nickname was bangcheon ( 방촌 , 厖村 ) . related pages . - maeng sa-seong - kim jong-seo - choi yun-deok references . - naver.com article ( korean ) - nate studies ( korean )
