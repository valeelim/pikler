Wonderful Christmastime     `` wonderful christmastime '' is christmas song which was first recorded by paul mccartney and released in 1979. the single did not chart on `` billboard '' hot 100 in the united states . it did , however , reach # 6 on united kingdom music charts . this single is played during the christmas season worldwide with strong popularity . other singers , including kelly rowland of destiny 's child , hilary duff and demi lovato have covered this song throughout the years .
