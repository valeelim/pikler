Kumbia Kings     kumbia kings ( or los kumbia kings ) are a reggaeton band formed by a.b . quintanilla iii in 1999. they won several latin grammy awards for their albums . in 2007 , quintanilla iii formed a new band called kumbia all-stars because of conflicts from other band members . frankie j became a successful r & b singer after he left to become a solo artist . selena 's widower chris perez joined both of the bands but left in 2010 to start his own band with joe ojeda , originally a keyboardist from selena y los dinos . other websites . - official website - kumbia all starz website - kumbia kings billboard - www.kumbiakingsblog.com
