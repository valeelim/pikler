Civil Aviation University of China     civil aviation university of china ( simplified chinese 中国民航大学 , traditional chinese 中國民航大學 , pinyin `` zhōngguó mínháng dàxué '' ) , also known as cauc , is a national university in tianjin , china . it offers education and training in civil aviation for new pilots in china . cauc was founded in 1951. related pages . - wu tongshui - french civil aviation university other websites . - civil aviation university of china website
