Benue River     the benue river is a river in africa . it is the major tributary of the niger river . the river is about 1,400 km long . people can travel on it for nearly the whole length in the summer months . it is an important transportation route in the places it flows through . it starts in the adamawa plateau of northern cameroon . it flows west , and through the town of garoua and lagdo reservoir , into nigeria south of the mandara mountains . then it goes through jimeta , ibi and makurdi before meeting the niger at lokoja . large tributaries are the gongola river and the mayo k√©bbi . other tributaries are taraba river and river katsina ala .
