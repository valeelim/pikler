Satch Boogie     satch boogie is the fifth track from joe satriani 's 1987 album `` surfing with the alien '' . the piece is a hard driving rock boogie in shuffle time . the solo is a mixture of a pentatonic blues and dorian mode , the chord sequence is : a d f # a. the tapping solo uses satriani 's signature pitch axis theory composition style , the central pitch in this case is a and the broken chords revolve around this note . other websites . - how to play satch boogie
