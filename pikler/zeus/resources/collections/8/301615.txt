Cleaner shrimp     cleaner shrimp is a generic term for any swimming decapod crustacean that cleans other organisms of parasites . this is an example of symbiosis : both parties benefit . the fish benefit because they have parasites removed , and the shrimp gain the nutritional value of the parasites . in many coral reefs , there are cleaning stations , with many cleaning shrimps . this behaviour is similar to that of cleaner fish . sometimes , cleaer shrimp join cleaner wrasse and other cleaner fish . cleaner shrimp are often included in salt water aquaria to keep the tank clean .
