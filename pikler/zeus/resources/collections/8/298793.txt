Cape Verde at the Olympics     cape verde has been in every summer olympic games held since 1996. no athletes from cape verde have been at any winter olympic games . they have never won a medal . the national olympic committee for cape verde is `` comité olímpico caboverdeano '' . it was started in 1989 and recognized in 1993. related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - cape verde profile at london2012.com
