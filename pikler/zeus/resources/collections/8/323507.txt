Fukuoka Airport     the airport is located in hakata , south-east of the city center . no other airport in japan is so close to the central part of the city as fukuoka airport . fukuoka is officially a second class airport . it is operating at full capacity , and can not be further expanded . fukuoka airport is the fourth busiest passenger airport in japan . related pages . - list of airports in japan other websites . - fukuoka airport website
