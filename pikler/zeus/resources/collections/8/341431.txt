Bromance     a bromance is a close non-sexual relationship between two ( or more ) men , a form of homosocial intimacy . bromance is a portmanteau word of `` bro '' or `` brother '' and `` romance '' . the word was created in the 1990s to describe the sort of relationships that develop between skateboarders who spend a great deal of time together . `` womance '' is the female flip side of bromance . it means a close non-sexual relationship between two ( or more ) women , a form of homosocial intimacy .
