Roland Emmerich     roland emmerich ( born 10 november 1955 ) is a german movie director , producer and screenwriter . he was born in stuttgart and grew up in nearby sindelfingen , baden-wurttemberg , west germany . his movies include `` universal soldier '' , `` stargate '' , `` independence day '' , `` godzilla '' , `` the patriot '' , `` the day after tomorrow '' , `` 10,000 bc '' , `` 2012 '' , `` anonymous '' , and `` white house down '' . emmerich is openly gay .
