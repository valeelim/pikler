Jeff Conine     jeffrey guy conine ( born june 27 , 1966 in tacoma , washington ) is a former major league baseball player . he played for the florida marlins , kansas city royals , and the baltimore orioles . he was in the 1994 all-star game . he was called mr. marlin for his long history with the team . conine was a pitcher when he played college ball at ucla . he was drafted by the royals as a first baseman . when he joined the marlins in 1992 he became a left fielder .
