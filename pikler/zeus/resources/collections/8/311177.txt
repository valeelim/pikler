Artificial cranial deformation     artificial cranial deformation , also known as head flattening or head binding is when the skull of a human is changed forever , and on purpose . it is done by changing how a child 's skull grows by putting force on the skull . flat shapes , long shapes and round shapes can be used to do this . it is usually done on children . this is because the skull is easier to shape at this time . usually , the shape that will be used to change the skull is attached about a month after the baby is born . it stays on for around six months after this . related pages . - foot binding
