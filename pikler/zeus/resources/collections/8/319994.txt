St. Joseph, Missouri    st. joseph , missouri st. joseph is a city in northwest missouri . it is next to the kansas state line north of kansas city . it is where the famous outlaw jesse james lived and where the pony express started . there is much entertainment to be seen there . and many historical sites and things . there are many shops and restaurants with delicious food . st. joseph is well known because of the pony express . it is one of the biggest cities in missouri ( the eighth biggest ) and was established in 1843 .
