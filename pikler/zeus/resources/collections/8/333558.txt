Elisabeth Shue     elisabeth judson shue ( born october 6 , 1963 ) is an american actress . she is best known for her work in the movies `` the karate kid '' , `` adventures in babysitting '' , `` cocktail '' , `` back to the future parts ii '' and `` iii '' and `` leaving las vegas '' . shue was born in wilmington , delaware . shue 's mother was a descendant of pilgrim religious leader william brewster . her father 's family moved from germany to pennsylvania in the early 19th century . shue 's younger brother , andrew , is also an actor . shue grew up in bergen and essex counties in new jersey . her parents divorced when she was young . shue graduated from columbia high school , in maplewood , new jersey . she later studied at wellesley college and harvard university . shue left harvard early because of her acting career . she returned to harvard to finish her degree in government in 2000. while in high school , shue acted in television commercials . she did ads for burger king , debeers diamonds , and hellman 's mayonnaise . her first movie role was in 1984. she acted in `` the karate kid '' . she played the girlfriend of ralph macchio . in 1986 , she acted with terrance stamp in the british horror movie `` link '' . `` adventures in babysitting '' was her first acting job where she was the main character .
