Rick Trevi     rick trevino ( born on june 15 , 1971 in houston , texas ) is a mexican-american country music singer . his first country music hit was in 1993 with `` just enough rope '' from his self-titled second album . this was followed with `` honky tonk crowd , '' `` she ca n't say i did n't cry , '' `` doctor time , '' and `` bobbie ann mason . '' he has also charted with `` learning as you go , '' `` runnin ' out of reasons to run , '' and `` i only get this way with you . '' rick has played with tejano greats such as freddie fender and flaco jiminez , and was a member of the band , los super 7. his upcoming album includes the track `` better in texas '' . other websites . - rick trevino official website
