Sukhoi Su-30MKI     the sukhoi su-30mki ( nato reporting name : flanker-h ) is a fighter plane made by sukhoi and hal for the indian air force . it is a version of the su-30 . it first flew in 2000 and entered service in 2002. it is one of the heaviest fighters in the world and can fly very far . the indian air force plans to have 272 of these planes by 2020. they will form the backbone of indian air force from then . it is considered a 4++ generation fighter plane and has similar features to the sukhoi su-35 .
