Jon Favreau     jonathan kolia `` jon '' favreau ( ; born october 19 , 1966 ) is an american stand-up comedian , actor , director , screenwriter , voice artist , and comedian . he appeared in such movies as `` elf '' ( in which he was also the director ) and `` open season '' ( but got replaced by matt taylor for the rest of the series ) . he is also known for directing `` iron man '' and `` the jungle book '' .
