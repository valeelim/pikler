Brian Cox (physicist)    brian cox ( physicist ) brian edward cox obe ( born march 3 , 1968 ) is a british particle physicist . he is also a professor at the university of manchester and a royal society university research fellow . he works on the atlas experiment at the large hadron collider ( lhc ) . cox was born in chadderton , oldham , lancashire . he is the presenter of some bbc science television programmes . he used to be a keyboard player for pop band d : ream in the 1990s . other websites . - personal website
