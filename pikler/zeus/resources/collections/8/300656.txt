Last Tango in Paris     last tango in paris is a 1972 franco-italian erotic drama movie . it is about a middle-aged american widower who has a sexual relationship with a young frenchwoman in paris . the movie stars marlon brando and maria schneider . the depiction of sex in this movie created a lot of controversy in many countries . the mpaa marked the movie under an x rating , which was later converted to an nc-17 due to rating revisions .
