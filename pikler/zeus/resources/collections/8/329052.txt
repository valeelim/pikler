École des technologies numériques appliquées    école des technologies numériques appliquées école des technologies numériques appliquées ( etna ) is a french private graduate school in information technology and computer science . it was made in 2005 and is in ivry-sur-seine . the school has two courses , software development and computer network/security , and delivers a degree recognized by french state . it teaches with a dual education system . other websites . - official website
