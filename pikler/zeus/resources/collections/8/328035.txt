Type 14 105 mm cannon     the , also known as a `` 10-cm '' gun , was a cannon used by the imperial japanese army ( ija ) . it was used during for long-range fire . this artillery piece was the first medium caliber cannon which was japanese designed . it the first with a split trail carriage . it was used imperial japanese army but was not considered successful . it was replaced by the type 92 105 mm cannon . before world war i , the imperial japanese army mainly had krupp cannons from germany . after the versailles treaty , the japanese considered other options including cannon designed by schneider et cie from france . in 1925 , ija began to use the `` type 14 '' which was based on schneider designs ; however , the type 14 was not considered successful . type 14s were used for training units in the japanese home islands . related pages . - artillery of japan - list of field guns other websites . - type 14 on taki 's imperial japanese army page - us technical manual e 30-480
