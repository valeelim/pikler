IHOP     the international house of pancakes , better known by its acronym ihop , is a restaurant chain . ihop serves mostly breakfast foods but also serves lunch and dinner . there are more than 1,500 restaurants in all 50 states as well as in canada , mexico , guatemala , puerto rico and the u.s. virgin islands . ihop was founded in 1958 by jerry lapin , al lapin and albert kallis . the first ihop opened in toluca lake , los angeles , california .
