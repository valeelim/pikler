Tierra del Fuego     tierra del fuego is an archipelago at the southern tip of south america . the magellan strait sepearates it from the south american mainland . the archipelago consists of a big island , isla grande de tierra del fuego and a number of smaller islands . the land area of the archipelago is 73.746 square kilometers . with about 47.000 square kilometers , isla grande takes roughly half of the total land mass . the archipelago ( and the main island ) is divided between argentina and chile . about 127.000 people live in the part belonging to argentina , about 8.000 in that belonging to chile . the argentine part is called tierra del fuego province , the chilenan part has the name magellanes . the biggest cities on the main island are rio grande , 57.000 people , and ushuaia in the argentine part , and porvenir with 5.700 inhabitants in the chilenan part . puerto williams , with 2300 people , is the most southern city in the world .
