Let's Kill Hitler    let 's kill hitler `` let 's kill hitler '' is the eighth episode of the sixth series of the british science fiction television series `` doctor who '' . the episode was broadcast on bbc one , space and bbc america on 27 august 2011. it is the second episode of a two-part story . the story began with `` a good man goes to war '' ( episode seven ) . it is the 778th episode . the episode centers around river song and the history of amy pond .
