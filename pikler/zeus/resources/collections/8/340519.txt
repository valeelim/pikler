Sidi Bel Abbès Province     sidi bel abbès is one of the provinces of algeria . the name is from the city of sidi bel abbès . it is in the northwestern part of the country . the province has 15 districts and 52 `` communes '' or municipalities . districts . 1. aïn el berd 1. ben badis 1. marhoum 1. mérine 1. mostefa ben brahim 1. moulay slissen 1. ras el ma 1. sfisef 1. sidi ali benyoub 1. sidi ali boussidi 1. sidi bel abbès 1. sidi lahcène 1. télagh 1. ténira 1. téssala
