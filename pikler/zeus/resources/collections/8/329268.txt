Sonny Bono     sonny bono ( february 16 , 1935 - january 5 , 1998 ) was a recording artist , a producer , singer-songwriter , actor and politician . he was married to cher between 1964 and 1975 , during which time they formed the duo sonny & cher . together , they had numerous hit records and singles . they had several children , including chastity bono ( who would later be renamed chaz bono ) . sonny was killed in a skiing accident .
