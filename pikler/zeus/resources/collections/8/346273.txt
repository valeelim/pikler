Louis Pailhas     louis pailhas , born the 2nd of march 1926 in foix , is a french politician and aerospace engineer . from december 1967 to 1982 , he was head of the french civil aviation university . graduate from french civil aviation university ( promotion 1949 ) , he has done different job in civil aviation before joining the french civil aviation university in december 1967 , the first to hold that position in toulouse . he resigned in january 1982. after that , in 1998 , he is assistant of the mayor of toulouse , and then deputy mayor for science , aeronautics and space activities in 2001. louis pailhas is member of the `` académie de l'air et de l'espace '' . awards . - legion of honour - medal of aeronautics further reading . - académie nationale de l'air et de l'espace and lucien robineau , `` les français du ciel , dictionnaire historique '' , le cherche midi , june 2005 , 782 p.
