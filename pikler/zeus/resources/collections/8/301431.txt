Murine typhus     murine typhus is a disease spread by fleas . it is also called endemic typhus . it is a type of typhus . sometimes this disease is mistaken for a viral infection because the symptoms are similar . murine typhus is treated with antibiotics . symptoms are : - fever - headache - muscle pain - nausea - vomiting - coughing most people recover fully but death can occur in the elderly . other pages . - epidemic typhus
