Mali at the Olympics     mali at the olympics is a history which started in the 1960s . since 1962 , mali has contributed to the growth of the `` olympic movement '' . the international olympic committee 's official abbreviation for mali is mli . mali has taken part in the summer olympic games held in 1964 , 1972 , and every olympics since 1980. the country has never won an olympic medal . no athletes from mali have been in any winter olympic games . the national olympic committee for mali was formed in 1962 and recognized by the international olympic committee in 1963. related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - mali profile at london2012.com
