Pakistan at the Olympics     pakistan at the olympics is a history which includes 17 games in 13 countries and 300+ athletes . since 1948 , pakistan has contributed to the growth of the `` olympic movement '' . the international olympic committee 's official abbreviation for pakistan is pak . the pakistan national olympic committee was formed in 1948. it was recognized by the international olympic committee in 1948. pakistan 's team first competed at the 1948 summer olympics in london . pakistan 's first time in the winter olympic games was at the vancouver 2010 winter olympics . related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - pakistan profile at london2012.com
