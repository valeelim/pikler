Penryn (Cornwall)    penryn ( cornwall ) penryn is a small town in south cornwall , united kingdom . it is on the penryn river about one mile ( 1.6 km ) northwest of falmouth . the population was 7,166 in the 2001 census . penryn was in the middle ages an important port but underwent a severe decline after falmouth had been established in the 17th century . today it is a busy town and has managed to retain a much of its heritage ; a large proportion of its buildings date back to tudor , jacobean and georgian times , the town is an important conservation area , and has more listed buildings than any other town in cornwall .
