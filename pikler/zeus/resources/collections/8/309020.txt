Christianity in Palestine     some people who speak arabic and live on the territory called palestine today are christians . these include oriental orthodoxy , eastern orthodoxy , catholic ( eastern and western rites ) , protestant , and others . about half of them belong a branch of the eastern orthodox church , the greek orthodox church of jerusalem . today , less that four percent of the palestinian arabs living inside the borders of palestine are christians . many arabs emigrated , mainly because of wars fought between egypt , israel and jordan . there is also a pressure to join islam . surveys done by the british in 1922 and 1946 showed that in 1922 , about 9.5 % and in 1946 approximately 7.6 % of the palestinian arabs were christians .
