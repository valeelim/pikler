Anthony Perkins     anthony perkins ( april 4 , 1932 - september 12 , 1992 ) was an american actor . he is best known for his roles in `` psycho '' and its sequels , where he played norman bates , a serial killer who suffered from dissociative identity disorder . perkins was born in new york city and was the son of actor osgood perkins ( 1892-1937 ) . he moved to boston , massachusetts in 1942. perkins was bisexual . he was married to photographer berry berenson ( 1948-2001 ) from 1973 until his death from aids in hollywood , los angeles , california in 1992. the couple had two sons , actor oz ( born 1974 ) and musician elvis ( born 1976 ) . berenson was killed in the september 11 terrorist attacks . she was aboard american airlines flight 11 when it was hijacked and crashed into the world trade center .
