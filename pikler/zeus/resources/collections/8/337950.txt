Léon: The Professional    léon : the professional léon ( full name léon : the professional ) is a 1994 french crime thriller movie directed by luc besson . the focus of `` léon '' is assassination and the mafia . the movie is set in new york city . jean reno plays the title role , a hitman . natalie portman co-stars as matilda , a girl who is being taught to become an assassin . and gary oldman plays the main villain norman stansfield , a corrupt cop and mafia leader who kills matilda 's family . `` léon '' was released in the united states on november 18 , 1994 .
