Apollo 9     apollo 9 was a mission in nasa 's apollo program . it was the third manned mission in the apollo program and was the first flight of the command/service module ( csm ) with the lunar module ( lm ) . the crew was commander james a. mcdivitt , command module pilot david r. scott , and lunar module pilot russell l. schweickart . the mission was launched on march 3 , 1969. the mission tested several things which were important for landing on the moon , including the lm engines , backpack life support systems , navigation systems , and docking maneuvers .
