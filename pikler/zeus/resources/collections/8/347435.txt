Artillery (band)    artillery ( band ) artillery is a thrash metal band from denmark . they started in 1982 and have split up and reformed twice . so far , as of 2012 , artillery has released six studio albums , which include : - `` fear of tomorrow '' ( 1985 ) - `` terror squad '' ( 1987 ) - `` by inheritance '' ( 1990 ) - `` b.a.c.k . '' ( 1999 ) - `` when death comes '' ( 2009 ) - `` my blood '' ( 2011 )
