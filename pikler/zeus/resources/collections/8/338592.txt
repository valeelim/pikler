Closeted     closeted and in the closet are metaphors used to describe lesbian , gay , bisexual , transgender , queer/questioning and intersex ( lgbtqi ) people who have not disclosed their sexual orientation , gender identity or intersex characteristics . after disclosure , they are said to have `` come out of the closet .
