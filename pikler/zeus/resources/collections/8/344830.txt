Spanish Wells     spanish wells is one of the districts of the bahamas . it is a small town on the island of st. george 's cay it is wide by long . about 1,500 people live here . spanish wells has a bridge to neighboring russell island . spanish wells is so small that many people get around the island using golf carts instead of full-sized cars . the area had a lot of property damage during a direct hit from hurricane andrew in 1992 and floyd in 1999 .
