Fargo (movie)    fargo ( movie ) fargo is a 1996 american black comedy-drama crime thriller movie . the coen brothers made this movie , which is set in 1987. frances mcdormand , steve buscemi and william h. macy star in the movie as a detective , a criminal , and a car salesman , respectively . it is a dark comedy about the police investigation of some murders and bad business activities . in 2006 it was put into the united states national film registry because the group thought it was culturally , historically , or aesthetically important enough to be recognized in their collection . it inspired a tv series `` of the same name '' part of the movie is set in fargo , north dakota . the majority is in minneapolis , minnesota . it shows the culture and accent of many of the people who live there .
