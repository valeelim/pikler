Dagr     dagr ( old norse `` day '' ) is a personification of a day in the norse mythology . he is a son of the dawn god dellingr and personification of the night nótt . he is a half-brother of auðr and jörð ( earth ) . his nephew is thor . odin took dagr and nótt , gave them each a chariot and a horse—dagr receiving the horse skinfaxi—and placed them in the sky to ride around the earth every 24 hours .
