Kareena Kapoor     kareena kapoor ( ; born on 21 september 1980 ) , is a famous bollywood actress . she has been many genres , from romantic dramas to comedies , independent movies to major bollywood movies . she is the sister of bollywood actress karisma kapoor . she is the daughter of bollywood actors randhir kapoor and babita . often informally referred to as 'bebo ' , she is the granddaughter of actor and filmmaker raj kapoor and niece of actor rishi kapoor . she is the partner of bollywood actor saif ali khan . they are referred to india 's brangelina ( with the name 'saifeena ' ) by the press .
