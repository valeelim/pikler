VooV Festival     voov ( formerly known as vuuv experience ) is an annual music festival with about 10,000 to 20,000 visitors . it was launched in the year 1992. it is one of the oldest rave parties in germany . from a small party with about 100 people it is now one of the biggest rave parties in europe . after several moves , was vuuv 's place since 2001 in putlitz . in 2007 they moved back to antaro which was the place they made the greatest feasts . sources . - official website
