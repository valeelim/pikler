Parthian language     the parthian language is an old iranian language . it has been spoken in the northeast of iran , in the region called parthia . the main time of this language was about 248 b.c . until 224 a.d. that was during the parthian empire . parthian is an indo-european languages . at the time of sassanid empire , the parthian language was destroyed . middle persian replaced it . there are many words in armenian that came from parthian .
