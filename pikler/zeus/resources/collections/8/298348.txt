Roseland Peninsula     the roseland peninsula , or just roseland , is a district of west cornwall in the united kingdom . roseland is in the south of the county and contains the town of st mawes and villages such as st just in roseland and gerrans . it is a peninsula , separated from the rest of cornwall by the river fal ( on the east is the english channel ) . there is some disagreement about where the peninsula begins and some include the village of tregony in roseland and others do not .
