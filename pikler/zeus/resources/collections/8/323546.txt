Kobe Airport     the airport is on an artificial island just off the coast of kobe . it is south of sannomiya station , which is the main train station in the city . the city government of kobe proposed an airport in the harbor next to port island . early planning was published in 1982. the airport opened on february 16 , 2006. the airport handled its first international aircraft flight in september 2006. it is officially classified as a 3rd class airport . related pages . - list of airports in japan other websites . - kobe airport terminal ;
