Goldfinch     goldfinch are finches of the genus carduelis . they are small passerine birds of the family fringillidae . the goldfinch breeds across europe , north africa , and western and central asia , in open , partially wooded lowlands . it is resident in the milder west of its range , but migrates from colder regions . it will also make local movements , even in the west , to escape bad weather . it has been introduced to many areas of the world . the goldfinch 's preferred food is small seeds such as those from thistles and teasels , but insects are also taken when feeding young . it also regularly visits bird feeders in winter . they nest in the outer twigs of tall leafy trees , or even in bamboo , laying four to six eggs which hatch in 11â€“14 days . in the winter goldfinches group together to form flocks of up to forty birds , occasionally more . the song is a pleasant silvery twittering .
