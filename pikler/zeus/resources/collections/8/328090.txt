Calgary Hitmen     the calgary hitmen are a canadian professional ice hockey team that began playing in 1995. they currently play in the western hockey league . they play their home games in calgary , alberta at the scotiabank saddledome . the teams name pays homage to the nickname of retired professional wrestler bret hart . hart was also a founding member of the hitmen along with nhl players theoren fleury and joe sakic . the team has won 2 ed chynoweth cups . other websites . - official website
