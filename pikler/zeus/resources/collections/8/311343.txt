Don Omar     don omar ( born william omar landrón rivera ; february 10 , 1978 in puerto rico ) , is a reggaeton singer . he is believed to be one of the best reggaeton artists in puerto rico and all of latin america by many music critics and fans alike . omar is best known for his songs `` dile '' and `` dale don dale '' from his first album `` the last don '' and `` reggaeton latino '' . his songs are mostly about life and love ; some examples of these would be `` la recompenza '' ( `` the reward '' ) and `` aunque te fuiste '' ( `` even though you left me '' ) . he started his career after many obstacles when héctor delgado ( from the duo héctor & tito ) gave him an opportunity to be a backup singer for them . after that , he sang in a live concert with héctor & tito in 2003 , which was released later as a cd and dvd , which included the songs that became instant hits , baila morena and amor de colegio ( school love ) . he is currently signed to vi music through universal music latino . discography . - 2003 `` the last don '' - 2004 `` the last don live ''
