MDNA (album)    mdna ( album ) mdna is the name of an album by the recording artist and actress madonna . the album was her debut release from her 360-degree record deal from live nation entertainment , in partnership with interscope records . the album was released on march 23 , 2012 and featured production from madonna herself , benny benassi , william orbit , benny 's brother alle benassi and martin solveig . the lead single from the album , `` give me all your luvin ' '' was released on february 3 , 2012 and features m.i.a . and nicki minaj . the second single , `` girl gone wild '' , had its lyric video premiere on february 27 , 2012 and was available as a single on march 2 , 2012. the album was also her first release not to be associated with warner bros .
