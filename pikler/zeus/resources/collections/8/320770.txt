Hōki Province     the province had borders with inaba , mimasaka , bitchū , bingo , and izumo provinces . the ancient capital city of the province was near kurayoshi . a major castle town was at yonago . in the meiji period , the provinces of japan were converted into prefectures . the maps of japan and izu province were reformed in the 1870s . `` shitori jinja '' was the chief shinto shrine ( `` ichinomiya '' ) of hōki . related pages . - provinces of japan - prefectures of japan - list of regions of japan - list of islands of japan other websites . - murdoch 's map of provinces , 1903
