The Click (song)    the click ( song ) `` the click '' is the third single off the debut album by pop punk band good charlotte called `` good charlotte '' . the song was released in 2001. it did n't appear on the track listing of `` good charlotte '' but was on the track listing of the japanese and 2003 re-release of `` good charlotte '' . the song was also the theme song for the television show `` undergrads '' and the band have also been mentioned in numerous episodes and made an appearance in the 12th episode where they played `` the click '' .
