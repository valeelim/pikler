Musky rat-kangaroo     the musky rat-kangaroo , `` hypsiprymnodon moschatus '' , is a small marsupial species found in the rainforests of northeast queensland , australia . it moves by extending its body and then bringing both of its hind legs forward . it eats fallen fruit and large seeds , as well as small vertebrates . its body is about 23cm in length and its tail about 14cm . they weigh about 530 grams .
