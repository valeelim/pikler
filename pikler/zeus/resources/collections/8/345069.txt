Arquette     arquette is a surname . people with this name include an american family , many of whom are actors : - cliff arquette ( 1904-1974 ) , an actor who was popular on `` the tonight show '' - cliff 's son lewis arquette ( 1935-2001 ) - lewis ' children : rosanna arquette ( born 1959 ) , richmond arquette ( born 1963 ) , patricia arquette ( born 1968 ) , alexis arquette ( 1969-2016 ) and david arquette ( born 1971 ) .
