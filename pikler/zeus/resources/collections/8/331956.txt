Defensive tackle     a defensive tackle is a position in the center of the defensive line in american and canadian football . these are some of the largest players ( in size ) on the field . their main jobs are to occupy offensive linemen , tackle , and make it hard for the quarterback to throw the ball . they rarely do anything else . in the national football league , defensive tackles may wear numbers in the 50s , 60s , 70s , and 90s , but may wear any number they chose in college football .
