Vidal Sassoon     vidal sassoon ( 17 january 1928 - 9 may 2012 ) was a british hairdresser . he was born in hammersmith , london to sephardi jewish parents . he moved to new york in the late 1960s . he later moved to los angeles , california . sassoon was married four times . the first three marriages ended in divorce . his second marriage was to canadian actress beverly adams ( born 1940 ) . the couple had three children together and adopted another . sassoon was diagnosed with leukemia in 2009 and died at his bel air , los angeles home . his fourth marriage lasted until his death .
