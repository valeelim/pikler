Orly     orly is a town near paris , france . it is in the val-de-marne département and the île-de-france region . there are about 21,372 people in the town ( as of 2007 ) . the école nationale de l'aviation civile ( french civil aviation university ) was built at orly in 1949. it was there from 1949 to 1969. the buildings are still visible from the road going to the airport of paris orly .
