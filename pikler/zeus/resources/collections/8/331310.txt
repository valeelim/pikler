Copper Age     the copper age was a time period between the neolithic and the bronze age . it is also named `` chalcolithic '' ( `` khalkos '' `` copper '' + `` lithos '' `` stone '' ) . in the bronze age , people added tin to copper to make bronze , an alloy which is much harder than either tin or copper . during the copper age , this fact became known , but was not much used because tin was scarce . the vinƒça culture was the first to develop the knowledge and skills to process copper , i , the late 6th millennium bc . edged weapons such as knives , swords , spearheads , and axes were made of copper , as were brooches , pins , belt boxes and vessels for food and drink .
