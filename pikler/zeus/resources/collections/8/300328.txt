Levamisole     levamisole is a drug that was used to treat infections with parasitic worms , and for some forms of chemotherapy , to treat cancer . the drug was discovered in 1966. it was withdrawn from the us and canadian markets in 2000 and 2003 , because of a serious risk of side effects ; better drugs can replace it . currently , levamisole remains in veterinary use as a dewormer for livestock . the drugs has also been used as an adulterant in cocaine sold in the us and canada . this use resulted in serious side effects .
