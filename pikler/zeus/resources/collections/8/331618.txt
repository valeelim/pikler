Château-Arnoux-Saint-Auban     château-arnoux-saint-auban is a commune of 5,270 people ( 2008 ) . it is found in the region provence-alpes-côte d'azur in the alpes-de-haute-provence department in the south of france . a campus of the école nationale de l'aviation civile is located in the commune . transports . - château-arnoux-saint-auban airport
