Kelowna Rockets     the kelowna rockets are a canadian ice hockey team . they began playing in 1991. the team currently plays in the western hockey league . they play their home games in kelowna , british columbia at the prospera place . the team originally played in tacoma , washington as the `` tacoma rockets '' from 1991 to 1995 until relocating to kelowna . they have won 3 ed chynoweth cups and 1 memorial cup . other websites . - official website
