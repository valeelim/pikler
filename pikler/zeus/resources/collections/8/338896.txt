Herat Province     herat ( persian/pashto : هرات ) is one the 34 provinces of afghanistan . it is in the south-western region of the country . the main city and capital is named herat . the region of herat used to be part of greater khorasan . it was ruled by the tahirids followed by the saffarids , samanids , ghaznavids , ghurids , ilkhanates , timurids , and saffavids . in the early-18th century it became part of the afghan hotaki dynasty followed by the durrani empire . there are about 1,762,157 people living in herat province . there are 16 districts within the province . these are listed in the table below . football ( soccer ) is the popular sport in herat province . cricket is also getting popular .
