Seconal     secobarbital sodium ( sold as seconal ) is a drug which is used to treat epilepsy and insomnia . trade names include seconal and reds . it was first synthesised in 1928. like other barbiturates , it is fairly addictive . in many countries , using this drug is illegal , outside the medical context . today , this drug is only used rarely ; barbiturates have been replaced by benzodiazepine for many applications .
