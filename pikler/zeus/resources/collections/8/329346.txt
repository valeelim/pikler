Gacé     gacé is a commune of the orne in lower normandy , france . there are 2,192 people living there in 2012. it was at first , a gallic city named `` *vassiākon '' . the castle of gacé was built in the 11th century . the english captured the castle in 1417. the city had important economic activity in the beginning of 19th century . it 's a industrial and agricultural town . there is the museum of lady of the camellias .
