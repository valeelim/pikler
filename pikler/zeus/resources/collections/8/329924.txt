The Chronicles of Life and Death     the chronicles of life and death is the third studio album by american pop punk band , good charlotte . `` the chronicles of life and death '' was released on october 5 , 2004. it was released with both a `` life '' and a `` death '' version . the life version contained a bonus track called `` falling away '' which also featured the hidden track `` wounded '' . it was the first and last album to feature chris wilson on the drums .
