Regnal name     a regnal name , or reign name , is a formal name or label which refers to the years of the reign of a monarch or a pope . a monarch sometimes chooses a reign name which is different from his or her personal name . a pope chooses a regnal name when he is elected . it becomes his new personal name . the new name -- or sometimes the old one -- is followed by an ordinal number to create a unique name for the period in which the monarch is on the throne . other pages . - family name - given name - posthumous name - surname - japanese era names
