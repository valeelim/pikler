Ugly Americans     ugly americans is an american animated television show created by devin clark and developed by david m. stern . the show is on the life of mark lilly , a social worker working for the department of integration , in an similar version of new york city which has monsters and creatures in it . `` ugly americans '' was first broadcast on march 17 , 2010 on comedy central . the series is based on a webseries by devin clark that is named 5 on with alan whiter . it is on the comedy network in canada and was first broadcast on the same day at the same time .
