Mohammed Fahim     field marshal mohammad qasim fahim ( persian : 9 october 1957 – 9 march 2014 ; محمد فهیم also known as `` marshal fahim khan '' ) was an afghan military commander , and politician . he was the vice president of afghanistan from november 2009 until his death in march 2014. he was the defense minister of the afghan transitional administration , beginning in 2002. he also served as vice president of afghanistan from june 2002 to december 2004. marshal fahim was a member of afghanistan 's tajik ethnic group .
