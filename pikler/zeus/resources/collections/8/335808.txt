Fujiidera, Osaka    fujiidera , osaka the municipality calls itself `` fujidera city '' in english . according to the imperial household agency , emperor ingyō is buried in an earthen tumulus ( `` kofun '' ) at fujiidera . ingyō is venerated at a memorial shinto shrine ( `` misasagi '' ) at this site . the modern city was established in 1966. other websites . - fujideara website
