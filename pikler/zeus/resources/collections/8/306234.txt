Yinghuo-1     yinghuo-1 was a chinese spacecraft . people planned for it to be china 's first interplanetary mission . yinghuo-1 was supposed to orbit and study the planet mars . it was launched with the russian spacecraft phobos-grunt . the spacecraft got into space , but something went wrong and it could n't escape earth 's gravity . the name `` yinghuo '' means `` firefly '' in chinese .
