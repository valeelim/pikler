Scaphognathus     scaphognathus was a rhamphorhyncoid pterosaur found in the solnhofen limestone of the upper jurassic . it had wings that was about one meter long and a long tail shaped like a leaf . the leaf-tail probably acted like a rudder , helping it steer through the air . it was probably a diurnal ( daytime ) flier , where `` rhamphorhynchus '' was probably nocturnal . scaphognathus ate `` pholidophorus '' fish .
