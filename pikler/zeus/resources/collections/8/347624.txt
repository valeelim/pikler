Belarusian ruble     the ruble is the currency of belarus . the symbol for the ruble is `` br '' and the iso 4217 code is `` byr '' . in 2000 , notes were introduced in amounts of 1 , 5 , 10 , 20 , 50 , 100 , 500 , 1,000 and 5,000 rublei . in 2001 and 2002 , higher amounts of 10,000 , 20,000 and 50,000 rublei were introduced , followed by 100,000 rublei in 2005. other websites . - national bank of the republic of belarus - official exchange rates ( english ) - information about belarusian commemorative coins ( english ) - catalog of belarus banknotes - live belarus ruble exchange rates : byr ( english )
