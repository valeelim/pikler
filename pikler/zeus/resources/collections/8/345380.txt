Laura     the name laura is a female given name in latin europe whose meaning , `` bay laurel '' , translates to everyman . the name is from the bay laurel plant , which in the greco-roman era , was used as a symbol of victory , fame or honor . around british north america , it is likely the name `` laura '' was strongly popular for female babies until its rapid decline in late 19th century . it rebounded various times since , although it has steadily declined in popularity in some countries . there are many celebrities carrying the name `` laura '' .
