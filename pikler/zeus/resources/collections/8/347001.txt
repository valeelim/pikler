David Petraeus     david howell petraeus ( pronunciation : /pɨˈtreɪ.əs/ ; born november 7 , 1952 ) was the director of the central intelligence agency . he was sworn in on september 6 , 2011. prior to his assuming the directorship of the cia , petraeus was a four-star general serving over 37 years in the united states army . on november 9 , 2012 , petraeus resigned as cia director following a marital scandal .
