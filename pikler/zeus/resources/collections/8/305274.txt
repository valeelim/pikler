Alexander and the Terrible, Horrible, No Good, Very Bad Day    alexander and the terrible , horrible , no good , very bad day alexander and the terrible , horrible , no good , very bad day is a children 's picture book written by judith viorst and illustrated by ray cruz . it was published in 1972 by simon and schuster . it is a humorous story about a boy named alexander and how one day everything went wrong for him , so he started thinking of moving to australia . but at the end , he realized that other people have bad days too , even in australia . in 2011 century fox confirmed to make a live-action film based on the book but was later picked by the walt disney company . in 2014 , disney released a film adaption , of the same name loosely based on the book .
