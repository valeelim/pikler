Indian cormorant     the indian cormorant or indian shag ( `` phalacrocorax fuscicollis '' ) is a member of the cormorant family . it is found mainly along the inland waters of the indian subcontinent but extending west to sindh ( s pakistan ) and east to thailand and cambodia . it is a gregarious species that can be easily distinguished from the similar sized little cormorant by its blue eye , small head with a sloping forehead and a long narrow bill ending in a hooked tip .
