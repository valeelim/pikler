Ahmed Ouyahia     ahmed ouyahia ( born july 2 , 1952 ) is an algerian politician . he has been the current prime minister of algeria since august 16 , 2017. ouyahia was already prime minister from 1995 to 1998 , from 2003 to 2006 , and from 2008 to 2012. he was also minister of justice for a time . he formed and was president of the rnd party .
