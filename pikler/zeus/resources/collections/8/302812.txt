2011–12 Manchester United F.C. season    2011–12 manchester united f.c . season the 2011–12 season is manchester united 's 20th season in the premier league . manchester united signed a £40 million four-year deal with dhl . on november , sir alex ferguson celebrated 25 years in charge of the club . manchester united started their season with a tour in the united states . united finishing the tour with a game against barcelona in washington on 30 july . manchester united will face benfica , basel and otelul galati in group c. manchester united were drawn against crystal palace in the quarter-finals of the league cup .
