Madagascar at the Olympics     madagascar at the olympics is a history which starts in the 1960s . the international olympic committee 's official abbreviation for madagascar was mag . it is now mad . madagascar was first at the olympic games in 1964. they have been to every game since except for 1976 and 1988. they have never won a medal . 2006 was the first time at the winter olympics . related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - madagascar profile at london2012.com
