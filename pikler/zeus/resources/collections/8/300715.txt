Martin Lawrence     martin lawrence ( born in april 1965 ) is an american comedian , former boxer , screenwriter and producer . he was born in frankfurt to african-american parents . he has been in many kinds of movies , including the `` bad boys '' movies , the `` big momma 's house '' series , `` open season '' , the short film `` boog and elliot 's midnight bun run '' , and many others as well . he also starred in the sitcom `` martin '' between 1992 and 1997 .
