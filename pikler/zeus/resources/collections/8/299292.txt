Swaziland at the Olympics     swaziland was first at the olympic games in 1972. they missed the next two games but returned for the 1984 games in los angeles . they have been in all games since then . they were in the 1992 winter olympics . they have been represented by the swaziland olympic and commonwealth games association since 1972. swaziland has never won a medal . related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - swaziland profile at london2012.com
