Baia de Criş     baia de criș ( ) is a commune in hunedoara county , romania . it is a collection of nine villages : baia de criș , baldovin ( `` báldovin '' ) , căraci ( `` karács '' ) , cărăstău ( `` karasztó '' ) , lunca ( `` lunka '' ) , rișca ( `` riska '' ) , rișculița ( `` riskulica '' ) , țebea ( `` cebe '' ) and văleni . the village of țebea is where the revolt of horea , cloșca and crișan started in 1784 , and marks the death place and burial site of avram iancu .
