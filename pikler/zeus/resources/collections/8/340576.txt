Aïn Témouchent Province     aïn témouchent is a province in northwestern algeria . the capital is aïn témouchent . the province has 8 districts and 28 municipalities . the districts are : 1. aïn kihel 1. aïn larbaâ 1. aïn témouchent 1. béni saf 1. el amria 1. el malah 1. hammam bou hadjar 1. oulhassa gheraba
