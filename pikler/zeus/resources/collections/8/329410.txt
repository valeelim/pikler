Lose Control (Let Me Down)    lose control ( let me down ) `` lose control ( let me down ) '' is a song by keri hilson and nelly . the song contains two parts : the first three minutes and thirty-three seconds being `` lose control '' , featuring a rap from nelly , and the last one minute and thirty-two seconds being `` let me down '' , a solo song by hilson . `` lose control '' was produced by stargate . the producer for `` let me down '' is unknown . the song was only available as a radio single .
