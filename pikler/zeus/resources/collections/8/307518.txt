Royal ghost frog     the royal ghost frog ( heleophryne regis ) is a species of frog in the heleophrynidae family . it is endemic to south africa . its natural habitats are temperate forests , mediterranean-type shrubby vegetation , and rivers . it is threatened by habitat loss . references . - minter , l. , channing , a . & harrison , j . 2004. heleophryne regis . 2006 iucn red list of threatened species . downloaded on 23 july 2007 .
