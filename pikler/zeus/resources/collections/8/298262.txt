Psocoptera     psocoptera are an order of winged insects that are commonly known as booklice , barklice or barkflies . they first appeared in the permian period , 295–248 million years ago . they are often regarded as the most primitive of the hemipterans . their name originates from the greek word `` psokos '' meaning gnawed or rubbed and `` ptera '' meaning wings . there are more than 5,500 species in 41 families in three suborders . many of these species have only been described in recent years . they range in size from 1–10 millimeters ( 0.04–0.4 in ) in length .
