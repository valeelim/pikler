65     year 65 ( lxv ) was a common year starting on tuesday ( link will display the full calendar ) of the julian calendar in the 1st century . events . by place . roman empire . - gaius calpurnius piso goes against roman emperor nero . - cestius gallus becomes legate of syria . - after a stage performance in which he appears and shocks the senatorial class , nero goes against seneca , tigellinus , pro-republican senators , and anyone else he distrusts . - nero kills his pregnant wife , poppea sabina , with a kick to the stomach . by topic . religion . - the gospel of matthew is probably written between 60 and this year . - paul of tarsus ordains timothy as bishop of ephesus ( traditional date ) . - in china , the first official reference to buddhism is made . - the first christian community in africa is founded by mark , a disciple of peter . mark begins to write his gospel . - probable martyrdom date of the apostle thaddeus , also called saint jude in armenia . - probable martyrdom date of the apostle simon the canaanite in armenia .
