Hope Solo     hope amelia solo ( born july 30 , 1981 , in richland , washington ) is an american soccer goalkeeper . she plays for the united states women 's national soccer team , and for the seattle reign fc of the national women 's soccer league ( nwsl ) , the top division of women 's soccer in the united states during the 2011 fifa women 's world cup , she got 2 official awards . the bronze ball and the best goalkeeper award . her father jeffrey , died on june 15 , 2007 , because of heart failure . she was part of the usa women 's soccer team that won gold at the 2012 summer olympics in london . other websites . - official website - u.s. soccer profile
