Nótt     nótt ( old norse `` night '' ) is a personification of the night in the norse mythology . a goddess similar to her , but in greek mythology , is nyx . nótt is called `` night '' by mankind , `` darkness '' by the gods , `` unlight '' by jötnar , `` joy-of-sleep '' by the elves . she is black and swarthy , a daughter of the giant narfi . she had three husbands : - naglfari - annar - dellingr she has three children : - auðr - jörð - dagr her horse is hrímfaxi .
