Ten'ō     events of the `` ten'ō '' era . - 22 december 781 ( `` ten'ō 1 , 3rd day of the 12th month '' ) : in 11th year of the reign of emperor kōnin 's reign ( 光仁天皇11年 ) , he abdicated . the succession ( `` senso '' ) was received by his son . soon after , emperor kammu is said to have accepted the monarch 's role and duties and powers ( `` sokui '' ) . this was confirmed in ceremonies . related pages . - nara period other websites . - national diet library , `` the japanese calendar '' -- historical overview plus illustrative images from library 's collection
