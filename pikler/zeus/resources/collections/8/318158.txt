Belgium at the Olympics     belgium has been in most olympic games . the international olympic committee 's official abbreviation for belgium is bel . athletes from belgium competed for the first time was at the 1900 games . the nation was host to the 1920 summer olympics in antwerp . belgian athletes have won 142 medals at the summer olympic games , and another five at the winter olympic games . the national olympic committee for belgium was formed and recognized in 1906. related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - belgium profile at london2012.com - belgium at olympic.org
