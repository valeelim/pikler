Frankie Sandford     francesca sandford ( born 14 january 1989 ) is a british pop singer . she was born in upminster , london . she was a member of s club 8 ( 2001-2005 ) . since 2007 , she is a member of girl group the saturdays . sandford had a relationship with dougie poynter from 2008-2010. she began a relationship with wayne bridge in 2010. the couple 's son was born in 2013. the couple married in 2014. sandford was a contestant in the 2014 series of strictly come dancing .
