St Kew     st kew is a parish and village in north cornwall , united kingdom . the main settlements in the parish are the churchtown , st kew , and nearby st kew highway on the a39 road . st kew was a female welsh saint who was the sister of st docco who is thought to have founded a monastery here . the 15th century parish church of st james has important stained glass windows , including one depicting the passion of christ , which were restored in 2005. st kew highway railway station was a station on the north cornwall railway opened on 1 june 1895 and closed on 3 october 1966 .
