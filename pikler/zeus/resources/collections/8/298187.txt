Looe     looe is a small coastal town and fishing port in southeast cornwall , united kingdom . as of the 2001 census , 5,280 people lived there . looe is divided into two parts by the river looe , east looe and west looe being connected by a bridge . the town is about 20 miles ( 32 km ) west of the city of plymouth and seven miles ( 11 km ) south of liskeard . the town is around a small harbour and along the steep-sided valley of the river looe which flows south to the english channel beside a sandy beach . off shore to the west is the picturesque looe island . looe has a railway station at the end of the branch railway line from liskeard .
