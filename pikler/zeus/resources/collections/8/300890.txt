The Rules of Attraction     the rules of attraction is a 2002 american satirical black comedy-drama movie . it stars james van der beek and jessica biel . this movie is based on the novel of the same name by bret easton ellis . it is about events at a fictional college in new hampshire . `` the rules of attraction '' was originally rated nc-17 by the mpaa , although edits were performed by lionsgate so that the movie could get an r rating .
