Pittsburgh Pirates (NHL)    pittsburgh pirates ( nhl ) the pittsburgh pirates were an american professional ice hockey team in the national hockey league ( nhl ) , based in pittsburgh , pennsylvania from 1925–26 to 1929–30 . the nickname comes from the baseball team also based in the city . for the 1930–31 season the team moved to philadelphia , pennsylvania and played one season as the philadelphia quakers . other websites . - results on hockeydb.com
