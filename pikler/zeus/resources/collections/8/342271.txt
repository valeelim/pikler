Mer Hayrenik     mer hayrenik is the national anthem of the republic of armenia . it means `` our fatherland '' . it was started on july 1 , 1991. it was also the national anthem of the democratic republic of armenia , the first modern armenian state . the words come from a version of `` the song of an italian girl '' . it was written in 1859 by mikael nalbandian . later barsegh kanachyan made music for the anthem .
