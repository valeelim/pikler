John Mills     sir john mills cbe ( born lewis ernest watts mills , 22 february 1908 - 23 april 2005 ) was a british actor . he was born in north elmham , norfolk . his sister was entertainer annette mills ( 1894-1955 ) . he was married to actress aileen raymond ( 1910-2005 ) from 1927 until they divorced in 1941. he was married to actress mary hayley bell ( 1911-2005 ) from 1941 until his death . the couple had two daughters , actresses juliet mills ( born 1941 ) and hayley mills ( born 1946 ) and a son , jonathan mills ( born 1949 ) . mills was knighted in 1976. his autobiography was published in 1980. he died in denham , buckinghamshire .
