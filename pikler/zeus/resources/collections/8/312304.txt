Emperor Seiwa     this prince 's personal name ( `` imina '' ) was before he became the monarch . in the imperial house of japan , korehito was the first to have the suffix `` -hito '' 仁 in his name . korehito was the fourth son of emperor montoku . his mother was the daughter of fujiwara no yoshifusa ( 藤原良房 ) , who was regent and great minister of the council of state . prince korehito became emperor at the age of 8 , but the real power was held by his grandfather , yoshifusa . - 10 july 858 ( `` ten'an 2 , 27th day of the 8th month '' ) : in the 8th year of montoku '' -tennō '' 's reign ( 文徳天皇8年 ) , the emperor abdicated . the succession ( `` senso '' ) was received by a his son . then emperor seiwa is said to have acceded to the throne ( `` sokui '' ) . this was confirmed in ceremonies . - 15 december 858 ( `` ten'an 2 , 7th day of the 11th month '' ) : when prince korehito became emperor at age 9 , his grandfather became regent ( `` sesshō '' ) . this was the first time that this role was given to a member of the fujiwara family . also , it was the first time that someone so young became emperor .
