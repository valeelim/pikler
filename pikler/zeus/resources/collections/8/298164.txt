Gone (song)    gone ( song ) `` gone ' '' is a 2011 r & b song by american rapper nelly . the song features singer kelly rowland . it is the sequel to their 2002 song , dilemma . it was the third single from his sixth studio album 5.0. the song was released on january 4 , 2011. the song reached # 19 on the uk r & b chart , # 21 on the australia urban aria charts , # 55 on the australia aria charts , # 58 on the uk singles and # 59 on the u.s. billboard hot r & b/hip-hop songs .
