Last Dance/The Hustle/On the Radio     `` last dance/the hustle/on the radio '' ( or the disco medley ) is a medley by american singer selena . the medley had sampled the songs the `` last dance '' , `` the hustle '' and `` on the radio '' by donna summer . the mexican release had `` viviras selena '' ( a tribute/various artists song ) which was written by pete astudillo .
