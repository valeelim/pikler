Hwang Jinyi     hwang jinyi ( c. 1520 - c. 1560 ) was a gisaeng of the joseon dynasty who lived during the reign of king jungjong . her `` gisaeng '' name was myeongwol ( meaning `` bright moon '' ) . she was said to be charming , witty , very beautiful , and very smart . her personal life has inspired movies , a tv series , operas , and many novels . she has become a modern popular cultural icon of korea . other pages . - shin saimdang - uh woo-dong - yu gamdong - heo chohui other websites . - http : //100.naver.com/100.nhn ? docid=173946 - two sijo attributed to hwang , translated by larry gross - hwang jinyi : navercast
