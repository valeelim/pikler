Stinging nettle     stinging nettles ( `` urtica dioica '' ) are a type of plant which have stinging defensive hairs . stinging nettles can be found in america , europe and in asia . the hairs inject histamine and other chemicals that produce a stinging sensation when contacted by humans and other animals . the plant has been used as medicine , food , and fibre . soaking stinging nettles in water or cooking removes the stinging chemicals from the plant , which allows them to be handled and eaten without injury .
