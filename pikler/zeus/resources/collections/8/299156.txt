Niger at the Olympics     niger at the olympics is a history which began in 1964. the international olympic committee 's official abbreviation for the niger was ngr . it is now nig . teams from niger have been to all summer olympic games held since 1964 except for 1976 and 1980. no athletes from niger have been in any winter olympic games . related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - niger at olympic.org
