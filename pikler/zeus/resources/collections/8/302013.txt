Iran at the Olympics     iran was first at the olympic games in 1948. they have been in every summer olympic games since then , except for 1980 and 1984. iran has also been in some winter olympic games since 1956. the national olympic committee for iran is the `` national olympic committee of the islamic republic of iran '' . it was formed in 1947. related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - islamic republic of iran profile at london2012.com
