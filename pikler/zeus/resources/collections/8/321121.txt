Aberystwyth     aberystwyth ( mouth of the river ystwyth ) is a historic market town , administrative centre and holiday resort . it is in the area of ceredigion in west of wales . locals call the area 'aber ' . the people who live there speak mostly the welsh language , though they understand english as well . it is a university town . the small town of over 13,000 swells by another 10,000 when the students are there . the nearby suburb of llanbadarn fawr has another 3,400 people . aberystwyth is twinned with : - kronberg im taunus , germany , since 1997 - saint-brieuc , france , since 1973 - esquel , argentine patagonia
