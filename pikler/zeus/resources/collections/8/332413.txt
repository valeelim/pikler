FIFA 14     fifa 14 is a 2013 association football simulation video game created by ea canada and published by electronic arts . it was released for the playstation 2 , playstation 3 , playstation portable , playstation vita , xbox 360 , wii , nintendo 3ds and microsoft windows . since it was released on september 24 , 2013 , it has gotten good reviews . leagues/international teams in bold are licensed .
