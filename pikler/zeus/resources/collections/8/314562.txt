UPC Tirol Liga     the upc tirol liga ( also called tiroler landesliga or tiroler liga ) is the highest class in tyrolian football ; it is the 4th division in austrian man football league system . the champion is qualified for the regionalliga west , the last to teams are relegated in the landesliga west or ost . the league is named after the main sponsor , an austrian telecommunication provider . 2010/11 season . - champion sv hall - relegation : sk jenbach ; sk hippach - scorers : - wolfgang spanring sc kundl 30 goals - marco hesina sv hall 23 goals - stefan klausner sv matrei 21 goals
