JLS     jls were an english boy band . they have sold over 3 million records worldwide . they were runners-up in the 2008 uk series of `` the x factor '' . jls signed a record deal with epic records in 2009. their debut album peaked at number-one on the uk albums chart and sold more than 1 million copies . discography . - `` jls '' ( 2009 ) - `` outta this world '' ( 2010 ) - `` jukebox '' ( 2011 )
