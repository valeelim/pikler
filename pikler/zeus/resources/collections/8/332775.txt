Saint-Lambert-sur-Dive     saint-lambert-sur-dives is a commune in the orne department in north-west france . in 2007 , there were 148 people living there . it is in size . the highest point in the commune is 148 metres . the river `` la dives '' through the commune . the church was built in 12th century , its bell tower was registered as in historic monument in 1948. this is a romanesque bell tower . the church is closed since 1999. you can see the castle of mimbeville of the 17th century .
