The Motivation Proclamation     `` the motivation proclamation '' is the second single off the debut album by pop punk band good charlotte called `` good charlotte '' . the song is also the third track off the album . the song was released on august 7 , 2001. the video features good charlotte waking up in a house while they do normal morning rituals ( i.e . yawn , eat breakfast , etc . ) when they finally set up their music equipment , they start to play but finish the song . the video also features the television show , undergrads which plays on the tv in the house .
