Ichinobe-no Oshiwa     no firm dates can be assigned to the lives or reigns of this period , but the reign of emperor ankō is considered to have lasted from 456 to 479 ; and oshiwa died during ankō 's reign . according to the `` nihonshoki '' , oshiwa was killed in a hunting accident . his sons were adopted as heirs by emperor seinei . they are known as prince woke ( or kenzō '' -tennō '' ) and as prince oke ( or ninken '' -tennō '' ) .
