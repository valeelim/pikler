Four Christmases     four christmases is an 2008 american romantic comedy movie that was produced by gary barber , roger birnbaum , jonathan glickman , vince vaughn and reese witherspoon and was directed by seth gordon . `` four christmases '' was released on november 26 , 2008 in north america . the movie received negative reviews with a 25 % rating on rotten tomatoes and a 41 out of 100 from metacritic . cast . - vince vaughn as bradford 'brad ' mcvie - reese witherspoon as kate - robert duvall as howard mcvie - sissy spacek as paula - jon voight as creighton - mary steenburgen as marilyn - kristin chenoweth as courtney - jon favreau as denver mcvie - tim mcgraw as dallas mcvie - katy mixon as susan mcvie - dwight yoakam as pastor phil - carol kane as aunt sarah - colleen camp as aunt donna - jack donner as grandpa - steve wiebe as jim - skyler gisondo as connor mcvie - patrick van horn as darryl - brian baumgartner as eric - cedric yarbrough as stan - creagen dow as sheep - zachary gordon as kid in jump-jump ( cameo )
