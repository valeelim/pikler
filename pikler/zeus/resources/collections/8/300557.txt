Phewa Lake     phewa tal ( phewa lake or fewa lake ) is a lake in nepal and is the biggest lake in the pokhara . it is famous for having a lot of domestic and international tourists who visit and sail , swim and fish on the phewa lake . the shadow of a nearby white mountain can be seen on the surface of the lake . there is also a hindu temple on the center of the lake . if people want to visit the temple they must take a boat .
