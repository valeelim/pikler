Galette     galette , or more properly breton galette is a term used in french cuisine for various types of flat , round or freeform crusty cakes . one notable type is the `` galette des rois '' ( 'king cake ' ) eaten on the day of epiphany . in quebec , the term `` galette '' is usually applied to pastries best described as large cookies . galette is also the name given in most french `` crêperies '' to savoury buckwheat flour pancakes . those made from wheat flour , much smaller in size and mostly served with a sweet filling , are called crêpes .
