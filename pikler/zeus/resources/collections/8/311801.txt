Jessica Sutta     jessica lynn sutta ( born may 15 , 1982 ) is an american dancer , showgirl , singer , recording artist , model , and actress . she is best known as a member of the american pop girl group and dance ensemble , the pussycat dolls . she left the group in january 2010 to embark her solo career . she is currently finishing up her first solo album . it is expected to be released in 2012. she is currently signed to hollywood records .
