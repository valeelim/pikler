Twenty20 International     a twenty20 international ( t20i ) is a form of cricket which is played over 20 overs per side between two national cricket teams . the game is played under the rules of twenty20 cricket . the first twenty20 international took place on 17 february 2005. australia defeated new zealand by 44 runs at eden park in auckland . the international cricket council gave rankings for the shortest format of the game on october 24 , 2011 with england at top . the nations are listed below with the date of each nation 's twenty20 international debut shown in brackets . 1 .
