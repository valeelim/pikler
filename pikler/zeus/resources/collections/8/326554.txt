Trent Dilfer     trent dilfer ( b. march 13 , 1972 ) is an espn analyst and former american football quarterback . he played in the nfl for the tampa bay buccaneers ( 1993-1999 ) , baltimore ravens , ( 2000 ) , seattle seahawks ( 2001-2004 ) , cleveland browns ( 2005 ) , and san francisco 49ers ( 2006-2007 ) . the only time dilfer did not play in a season in his career was 2006 with san francisco . during his career , dilfer has been criticized for bad throwing . he did , however , win super bowl xxxv with the ravens . he is famous for the conservative offense he was in with the ravens in 2000 , replacing tony banks .
