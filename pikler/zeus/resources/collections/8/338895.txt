Paula Zahn     paula ann zahn ( born february 24 , 1956 in omaha , nebraska ) is an american journalist and news anchor . she worked for the news divisions on columbia broadcasting system ( cbs ) -television and american broadcasting company ( abc ) -television , and on the cable television news channels fox news network and cable news network ( cnn ) . on fox news channel she hosted `` the edge with paula zahn '' . on cnn she hosted `` paula zahn now '' and `` people in the news '' , a show jointly produced by cnn and `` people '' , a magazine .
