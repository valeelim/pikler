PlayStation Vita     the is a handheld game console made by sony computer entertainment . it is the successor to the playstation portable . it was released in japan on december 17 , 2011. it was released in europe , north america , south america and singapore on february 22 , 2012. it was released in australia on february 23 , 2012. it competes with the nintendo 3ds . it is part of the eighth generation of gaming .
