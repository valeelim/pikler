Time Inc.     time inc. is a subsidiary media company of time warner . it publishes several of the most read magazines in the united states such as `` people '' , `` time '' , `` sports illustrated '' , `` fortune '' , `` instyle '' , `` life '' , `` golf magazine '' , `` southern living '' , `` essence '' , `` this old house '' , `` all you '' and `` entertainment weekly '' . it publishes 130 magazines and was founded on november 28 , 1922 in new york city . other websites . - time inc. profile from time warner - pathfinder ( time inc. content portal )
