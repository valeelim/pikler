Tovuz Rayon     tovuz is a raion of azerbaijan . it is 360 km from baku . a major train line runs through the center , stopping at tovuz stansiya . tovuz also borders armenia near the village of eli beli . the town of tovuz is the capital of this raion . there are 113 settlements . notable residents . - ganira pashayeva , member ( mp ) of the national assembly of azerbaijan for tovuz raion and journalist
