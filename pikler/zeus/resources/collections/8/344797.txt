Carcassonne Airport     carcassonne airport ( ) is an airport serving carcassonne and the south of occitanie . the airport is located on the western part of the city , from the city center , in the aude department . it is also known as salvaza airport , carcassonne salvaza airport or carcassonne airport in pays cathare ( aéroport de carcassonne en pays cathare ) . a campus of the `` école nationale de l'aviation civile '' ( french civil aviation university ) is also located at the airport .
