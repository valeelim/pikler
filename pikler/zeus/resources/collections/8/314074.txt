Anglo-Egyptian Sudan     anglo-egyptian sudan was the name of the sudan when it was a colony of the united kingdom and egypt . it was officially under the joint rule of both countries , but really the british controlled the government from the beginning . every governor was british , and all decisions about the country were made by the british , although many of the administrators of the anglo-egyptian sudan were egyptians . the anglo-egyptian sudan was created in 1899 when the egyptian army under herbert kitchener recaptured the sudan , which was before an egyptian colony , from the madhists , a group of islamic rebels against egypt .
