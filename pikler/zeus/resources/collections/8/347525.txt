Chelsea Bridge     chelsea bridge is a bridge over the river thames in west london . it connects chelsea on the north bank to battersea on the south bank . there have been two chelsea bridges , on the site of what was an ancient crossing . the first chelsea bridge was called victoria bridge . it opened in 1857. the old bridge was replaced by the current one in 1937. in june 2012 , the bridge was highlighted on the route of queen elizabeth ii 's diamond jubilee pageant on the thames . related pages . - list of crossings of the river thames
