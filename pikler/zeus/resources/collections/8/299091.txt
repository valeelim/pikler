Egypt at the Olympics     egypt was first at the olympic games in 1912. the international olympic committee 's official abbreviation for egypt is egy . egypt has sent athletes to most of the summer olympic games since then . egypt did was not at the 1976 summer olympics because of the african boycott . egypt was at the winter olympic games with a single alpine skier in 1984. egyptian athletes have won a total of 24 medals . the national olympic committee for egypt was formed in 1910. related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - egypt profile at london2012.com
