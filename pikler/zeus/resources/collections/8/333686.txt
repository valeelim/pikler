Spean Praptos     spean praptos ( khmer : ស្ពានប្រាប់ទិស ) is an ancient stone bridge in cambodia . it is on the road from angkor to phnom chisor , near the village of kampong kdei . it used to be the longest stone-arch bridge in the world . it has more than 20 arches spanning 285 ft ( 87m ) . the bridge was built in the 12th century during the reign of the khmer emperor jayavarman vii .
