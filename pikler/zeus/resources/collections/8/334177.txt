List of counties in Missouri     there are 114 counties and one independent city in the u.s. state of missouri . in missouri , the county level of government level comes between those of the city and the state . its main responsibilities include maintaining roads , providing security , prosecuting criminals , and collecting taxes . elected officials at this level include a sheriff , prosecuting attorney , and assessor . the city of st. louis is an independent city , and is not within the limits of a county . its residents voted to secede from saint louis county in 1876. throughout the united states , st. louis is one of three independent cities outside the state of virginia ( the other two are baltimore , maryland and carson city , nevada ) .
