Brian Wilson     brian douglas wilson ( born june 20 , 1942 ) is an american musician , songwriter and singer . he is from inglewood , california . he is a founding member of the beach boys , although he was largely separate from the band from the 1970s until 2011. he played bass , sang , and wrote songs for them . in the 1970s and 80s , wilson struggled with drug problems and eating too much . in the 1980s and 90s , he began a solo career . he was a kennedy center honoree in 2007. in 2011 , wilson reunited with the beach boys .
