Young Dracula     young dracula is a british children 's fantasy television series . it is shown on cbbc . the series is based on `` young dracula and young monsters '' , a children 's book by michael lawrence . the series is directed by joss agnew . it was first broadcast in 2006 . `` young dracula '' was nominated for best children 's drama in the 2008 bafta awards . a fourth series is now being filmed .
