Bugatti     bugatti is a french supercar company currently owned by volkswagen . they made cars all the way back in the early 1900s . they made cars such as the bugatti veyron . unlike the regular veyron , with 300 produced , there are only 20 supersport versions , which currently holds the record for world 's fastest car . all bugattis have a 16-cylinder engine and all cost between $ 1.8 million to $ 2.2 million ( not counting the extras ) .
