Fushimi Inari-taisha     it is the head shrine of inari , the rice god . there are inari shrines in many parts of japan . there are 32,000+ sub-shrines or . fushimi inari-taisha has been associated with the emperors of japan since the early heian period . from 1871 through 1946 , it was in the first rank of government supported shrines . other websites . - fushimi inari-taisha website - fushimi inari-taisha images
