Sylhet     sylhet is a major city in north-eastern bangladesh . it is the main city of sylhet division and sylhet district . it was given metropolitan city status in march 2009. sylhet is on the banks of the surma valley . it is surrounded by the jaintia , khasi and tripura hills . the city has almost 500,000 people . it is one of the largest cities in bangladesh . the sylhet region is well known for its tea gardens and tropical forests . sylhet is one of the richest cities in bangladesh . the city is called a city of saints . there is the mausoleum of the great saint hazrat shah jalal , who brought islam to bengal during the 14th century .
