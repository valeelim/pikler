The Reader     the reader is a 2008 german-american romantic drama movie . it stars ralph fiennes as michael berg and kate winslet as hanna schmitz . it was directed by stephen daldry , produced by anthony minghella and sydney pollack and written by david hare . it is based on a 1995 german novel . `` the reader '' was nominated for many academy awards , including a best picture nomination . winslet won an academy award , a bafta award and a golden globe award for her role .
