Bmibaby     bmibaby limited known as bmibaby.com was a british low-cost airline . it flew to places in the uk and europe from its bases at belfast city , birmingham and east midlands airports . it was owned by british midland international , which itself is owned was lufthansa . in april 2012 , bmi was bought by the international airlines group and the airline , along with bmibaby stopped flying . bmibaby had a united kingdom civil aviation authority type a operating licence . it was allowed to carry passengers , cargo and mail on aircraft with 20 or more seats .
