The Village Blacksmith     `` the village blacksmith '' is a poem . it was written by henry wadsworth longfellow . it was published in 1841 in longfellow 's book , `` ballads and other poems '' . the real blacksmith of the poem was dexter pratt . he was longfellow 's neighbor . the first lines of the poem are well-known : `` under a spreading chestnut tree / the village smithy stands . '' in 1879 , children in cambridge , massachusetts gave longfellow a chair . it was made from the chestnut tree of the poem . other websites . - 1890 illustrated version at google book search
