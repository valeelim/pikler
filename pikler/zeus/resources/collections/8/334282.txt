John Waters     john waters ( born 22 april 1946 in baltimore , maryland ) is an american writer , stand up comedian and movie maker . he first became famous during the 1970s . he is the director of many movies . these include `` hairspray '' , `` serial mom '' , `` sixteen candles '' and `` pretty in pink '' . waters has written books . in 1986 `` crackpot : the obsessions of john waters '' was published . waters has acted in several movies . he did the voice of a gay man called `` john '' in an episode of `` the simpsons '' called `` homer 's phobia '' . waters is openly gay .
