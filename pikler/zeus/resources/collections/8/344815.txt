Melun Villaroche Aerodrome     melun villaroche aerodrome ( ) is an aerodrome north of melun . melun is a commune in the seine-et-marne department in the île-de-france region in north-central france . the airport is east-southeast of moissy-cramayel and southeast of paris . a campus of the `` école nationale de l'aviation civile '' ( french civil aviation university ) is also in the aerodrome .
