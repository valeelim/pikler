Army cadet force     the army cadet force is a youth organisation in great britain , where members train in military skills . it was founded in 1859 , and had its official 150th birthday in 2010 , marked by the cadet 150 celebrations . the acf gives a lot of youth in the uk an ability to change their lives with the opportunieties it offers for the little money , they offer a lot of courses you can do on summer camps etc . for example the summer mountain foundation course ( smf ) this is a ccat course which is very cheap for the cadets to do and a challenge for them as well , a lot of these courses can be used in the civilian lives where they can be put down on their cv 's . in the army cadets you will progress through a rank structure which will improve the cadets team work and their thinking skills while they are under pressure .
