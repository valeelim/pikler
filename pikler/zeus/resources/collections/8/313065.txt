STG-44     the stg-44 , known as the world 's first assault rifle , was developed in nazi germany during world war ii , replacing the mp-40 . hitler first did not approve of this new class of weapons , so it was called mp-43 and mp-44 , meaning machine pistol ( in english ) . the first service on the eastern front was successful . hitler told them to make more , and they were called stg-44 meaning `` storm rifle '' .
