Dos Historias     dos historias ( ) is a compilation album by american singer selena and mexican singer ana barbara . the album peaked at number three on the us latin regional mexican albums chart . it also peaked at number 21 on the top latin albums chart on `` billboard '' in 2006. track listings . 1 . `` como la flor '' - ( selena ) 1 . `` la trampa '' - ( ana bárbara ) 1 . `` amor prohibido '' - ( selena ) 1 . `` lo busque '' - ( ana bárbara ) 1 . `` fotos y recuerdos '' - ( selena ) 1 . `` todo lo aprendi de ti '' - ( ana bárbara ) 1 . `` no me queda mas '' - ( selena ) 1 . `` bandido '' - ( ana bárbara ) 1 . `` tú sólo tú '' - ( selena ) 1 . `` te regalo la lluvia '' - ( ana bárbara ) 1 . `` baila esta cumbia '' - ( selena ) 1 . `` ya no te creo nada '' - ( ana bárbara ) 1 . `` dreaming of you '' - ( selena ) 1 . `` como me haces falta '' - ( ana bárbara ) a special edition was released as well . it included a dvd of four music videos , two each , from selena and ana . 1. no me queda mas ( selena ) 1. ya no te cero nada ( ana bárbara ) 1. dreaming of you ( selena ) 1. bandido ( ana bárbara )
