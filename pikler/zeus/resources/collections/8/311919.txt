Norwegian Crusade     the norwegian crusade was one of several religious wars in which european countries fought against muslims in turkey and the middle east . the first crusade was 1095-1099. the norwegian crusade was next , from 1107 to 1110. king sigurd i led the crusade . sigurd was the first european king to go on crusade to the holy land . he did not lose any battles . the norwegian crusade seems to have been similar to earlier viking raids . references . other reading . - ( old norse : )
