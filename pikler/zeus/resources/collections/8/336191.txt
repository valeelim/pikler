Kowloon West (constituency)    kowloon west ( constituency ) kowloon west is a geographical constituency of the legislative council of hong kong . it was created in 1998. the constituency loosely corresponds to the today 's districts of yau tsim mong , sham shui po , and kowloon city . the constituency was formed since the 1998 legislative election , replacing a number of single-member constituencies in 1995. these were kowloon west and parts of kowloon central in the 1991 election with dual-seat constituency dual vote system and kowloon southwest , kowloon west , kowloon south , and kowloon central in the 1995 election . related pages . - list of constituencies of hong kong
