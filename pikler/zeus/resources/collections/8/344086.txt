Dying Fetus     dying fetus is an american death metal band from upper marlboro , maryland . dying fetus has three members in the band ; the current band members are john gallagher , sean beasley and trey williams . the band was formed in 1991 by the band member john gallagher and former band member jason netherton . they released their debut album , `` purification through violence '' , in 1996 and shortly afterwards left pulverizer records . they are currently signed to relapse records . band members . - current - john gallagher - vocals , guitar ( 1991–present ) , drums ( 1991-1993 ) - sean beasley - vocals , bass guitar ( 2001–present ) - trey williams - drums ( 2007-present ) - former - jason netherton - bass , vocals ( 1991-2000 ) - nick speleos - guitar , vocals ( 1991-1993 ) - rob belton - drums ( 1993-1996 ) - brian latta - guitar ( 1994-1998 ) - casey buckler - drums ( 1995-1996 ) - eric sayenga - drums ( 1996-1997 , 2001-2005 ) - kevin talley - drums ( 1997-2001 ) - john `` sparky '' voyles - guitar ( 1999-2001 ) - vince matthews - vocals ( 2001-2005 )
