Sir Gawain     sir gawain is a fictional character in the king arthur stories . he is one of the most important knights of the round table . his parents are arthur 's sister morgause ( or anna ) and king lot . he is the main character of `` sir gawain and the green knight '' , a medieval fantasy poem . he is said to be arthur 's nephew . he appears very early on in the legend and has been mentioned in very early welsh sord in wales
