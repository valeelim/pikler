Seychelles at the Olympics     seychelles was first at the olympic games in 1980. they have been in most summer olympic games since then , missing only the 1988 games in support of north korea . the nation has never been in the winter olympic games . as of 2011 , no seychellois athlete has won an olympic medal . the national olympic committee for seychelles was created in 1979. they were recognized by the international olympic committee that same year . related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - seychelles profile at london2012.com - seychelles olympic and commonwealth games association
