Most common words in English     this list of the most common words in english has been produced by oxford university press . by `` words '' is meant dictionary head words or lemmas . it is based on an analysis of a body of over a billion words . it represents a study done by oxford online , associated with the oxford english dictionary . this source includes writings of all sorts from `` literary novels and specialist journals to everyday newspapers and magazines and from hansard to the language of chatrooms , emails , and weblogs '' , unlike some sources which use texts from only specific sources . `` the reading teachers book of lists '' claims that the first 25 words make up about one-third of all printed material in english , and that the first 100 make up about one-half of all written material . note that the items listed may represent more than one actual word ; they are lemmas . for instance the entry `` be '' contains within it the occurrences of `` are '' , `` is '' , `` were '' and `` was '' . note also that these top 100 lemmas listed below account for 50 % of all the words in the oxford english corpus .
