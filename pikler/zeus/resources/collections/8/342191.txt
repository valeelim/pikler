May Revolution     the may revolution happened from may 18 to 25 , 1810 , in buenos aires . buenos aires was the capital of the viceroyalty of the río de la plata . it was a spanish colony that included roughly the territories of present-day argentina , bolivia , paraguay and uruguay . the result was the removal of viceroy baltasar hidalgo de cisneros and the forming of a local government . in argentina this is now celebrated as `` may week '' ( ) . the may revolution was a direct reaction to spain 's peninsular war of the previous two years . in 1808 , king ferdinand vii of spain abdicated in favor of napoleon . napoleon gave the throne to his brother , joseph bonaparte . the may revolution began the argentine war of independence . no formal declaration of independence was made . the primera junta continued to govern in the name of the deposed king , ferdinand vii . the argentine declaration of independence was made at the congress of tucumán on july 9 , 1816 .
