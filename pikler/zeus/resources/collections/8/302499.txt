Annonaceae     annonaceae the custard-apple , or annona family , the largest family of the magnolia order . it has 129 genera and 2,220 species . many species are valuable for their large pulpy fruits , some are useful for their timber , and others are prized as ornamentals . the family consists of trees , shrubs , and woody climbers found mainly in the tropics , with a few species in temperate regions . sources . - integrated taxonomic information system . annonaceae - university of hawaii , botany department . annonaceae
