Domitianus II (usurper)    domitianus ii ( usurper ) domitian was a usurper ( a pretender ) to the position of emperor in either 270 or 271 ad in gaul ( modern france ) during the reign of aurelian and is one of the so-called gallic emperors . he was probably killed after reigning only a year . not much is know about domitianus . however , two coins exist that show the emperor ( one is in the ashmolean museum in oxford , england ) . he is also mentioned by the byzantine historian zosimus and in the book `` historia augusta '' as well .
