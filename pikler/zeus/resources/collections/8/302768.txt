Cannabaceae     cannabaceae the hemp family of the rose order , containing 11 genera and 270 species of aromatic herbs distributed throughout temperate parts of the northern hemisphere . older authorities included the two genera , cannabis and humulus , in the mulberry family . these and the former hackberry family ( celtidaceae ) are now included in cannabaceae . members of the family are erect or climbing plants with petalless flowers and dry , one-seeded fruits . hemp ( `` cannabis '' ) and hop ( `` humulus '' ) are the only economically important species .
