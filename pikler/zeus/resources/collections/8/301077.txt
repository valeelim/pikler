Siempre Selena     siempre selena ( english : `` always selena '' ) is a compilation album by mexican-american tejano pop singer , selena . the album was specially designed , featuring various drawings of selena in stage costumes , on the front and back cover , attached to a special type of cd case . the album was geared towards selena fans , both old and new . the album included unreleased english and spanish tracks , as well as remixes , of both old and recent songs . the album was featured at the 1996 summer olympics in atlanta , georgia . track listing . 1 . `` siempre hace frio '' ( cuco sanchez ) 1 . `` only love '' ( mark spiro , robbie buchanan ) 1 . `` soy amiga '' ( ricky vela ) 1 . `` como quisera '' ( a.b . quintanilla iii , ricky vela ) 1 . `` a million to one '' ( phil medley ) 1 . `` costumbres '' ( juan gabriel ) 1 . `` cien años '' ( alberto cervantes , ruben fuentes ) 1 . `` tú robaste mi corazón '' ( duet with pete astudillo ) ( abraham quintanilla iii , ricky vela ) 1 . `` ya no '' ( abraham quintanilla iii , ricky vela ) 1 . `` no quiero saber '' ( abraham quintanilla iii , pete astudillo )
