Topfreedom     topfreedom is a concept of rights . topfreedom applies to women . it means the freedom to go topless ( without wearing any clothing above the waist ) in a public place . there are many organizations which advocate topfreedom . topfree equal rights association ( tera ) is one such organization . go topless day is special day in august . in this day , women are told to go topless in public . in the united states , each state has its own laws . new york state supreme court said that women can go topless in public . later , police arrested a woman ( in 2005 ) in new york city for walking along a street without wearing a shirt . she sued ( started a court case against ) the city for violating the new york state supreme court rule that women can go topless in public . the city finally paid her $ 29,000 because she was right and the police were wrong . in two provinces in canada ( ontario and british columbia ) women can be topless in public . however there are sometimes city laws saying women can not be topless in city parks . law professors say women probably can be topless in public in the other provinces , but nobody knows for sure . the question has not been decided by the supreme ( national ) court of canada .
