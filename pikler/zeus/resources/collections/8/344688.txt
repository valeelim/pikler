Muret - Lherm Aerodrome     muret - lherm aerodrome ( icao code : lfbr ) is located at lherm in the haute-garonne département in the occitanie region . it is southwest of muret . a campus of the `` école nationale de l'aviation civile '' ( french civil aviation university ) is located on the aerodrome . specifications . - airfield elevation : 189 meters - asphalt runway : 1 100 m × 30 m - grass runway : 825 m × 50 m air show . - airexpo , yearly air show
