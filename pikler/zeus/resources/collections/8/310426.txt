Conférence des Grandes Écoles    conférence des grandes écoles the conference des grandes écoles ( cge ) is an association of grandes écoles of engineering and management , all recognized by the state and delivering a national degree after at least 5 years of higher education . some companies , alumni associations and organizations are also member of cge . it also organizes the mastère spécialisé , the mastère en sciences and the badge programs . further reading . - official website
