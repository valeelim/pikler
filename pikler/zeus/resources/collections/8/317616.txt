Tropical Storm Hermine (2010)    tropical storm hermine ( 2010 ) tropical storm hermine ( also known as tropical depression 11-e in the eastern pacific ) was an unusual basin crossing tropical cyclone . the storm struck near salina cruz , mexico before weakening into a low pressure system . it moved north , became tropical storm hermine , intensified and then struck northeastern mexico . hermine continued north into texas and oklahoma , where she then fizzled out . from guatemala to oklahoma , the cyclone killed over 100 people and caused $ 740 million in damage , 240 million in the united states . despite the devastation , the name hermine was n't retired .
