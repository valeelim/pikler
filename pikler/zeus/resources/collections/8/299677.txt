Jamuna River     the jamuna river ( bangla : যমুনা `` jomuna '' ) is one of the three major rivers of bangladesh . the river flows from india to bangladesh and is a major channel of the brahmaputra river . the jamuna flows in the `` southward direction '' and meets the padma river ( `` pôdda '' ) and meghna river near goalundo ghat and chandpur respectively . these waters then together flow into the bay of bengal . the jamuna is known to be a very wide river . the breadth of the river , from bank to bank , is about 2 miles in the dry season while its about 5 miles during the rainy season . the river also acts as an important resource for transportation .
