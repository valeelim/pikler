Fujisawa, Kanagawa    fujisawa , kanagawa fujisawa is a japanese city in kanagawa prefecture on the island of honshū . in the edo period , `` fujisawa-shuku '' was 6th of the 53 shogunate-maintained waystations ( `` shuku-eki '' ) along the tōkaidō road which connected edo and kyoto . the city expanded around yugyō-ji after 1325. related pages . - `` the fifty-three stations of the tōkaidō '' other websites . - fujisawa city website ;
