Kelly Rutherford     kelly deane melissa rutherford ( born on november 6 , 1968 in elizabethtown , kentucky , u.s. ) is an american actress . kelly was married to carlos tarajano from 2001 to 2002. from 2006 to 2010 she was married to daniel giersch . they have two children together — a son hermés gustaf daniel giersch ( b.october 18 , 2006 ) and a daughter helena grace rutherford-giersch ( b.june 8 , 2009 ) . currently she lives in manhattan ( new york , u.s. ) with her children .
