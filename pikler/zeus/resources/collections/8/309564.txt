1962 Memorial Cup     the 1962 memorial cup final was the 44th junior ice hockey championship of the canadian amateur hockey association . the teams playing were the hamilton red wings of the ontario hockey association who completed against the edmonton oil kings . hamilton won their 1st memorial cup , defeating edmonton 4 games to 1. scores . - game 1 : hamilton 5-2 edmonton ( in hamilton ) - game 2 : hamilton 4-2 edmonton ( in guelph ) - game 3 : edmonton 5-3 hamilton ( in guelph ) - game 4 : hamilton 3-0 edmonton ( in guelph ) - game 5 : hamilton 7-4 edmonton ( in kitchener ) bud blom , joe bujdoso , bryan campbell , bob dean , john gofton , bob hamilton , ron harris , larry harrop , earl heiskala , paul henderson , roger lafreniere , lowell macdonald , pit martin , jim peters , wayne rivers , bob wall , jack wildfong , larry ziliotto . coach : eddie bush
