Béard-Géovreissiat     béard-géovreissiat is a commune in the ain department of france . it is mostly made up of the villages of béard and géovreissiat . also located in the commune is the hamlet of st germain de béard , and the northern part of the hamlet of croix-châlon . the commune was called géovreissiat until october 2008. géovreissiat is the main village and is where the city hall is located . in 2009 , 935 people lived in the commune . the inhabitants are the `` béardais '' . between 1841 and 1846 , the population fell from 720 to 354 inhabitants , and continued to fall until the end of the second world war . today it has reached its highest population since records began . references . - insee
