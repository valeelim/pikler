89     year 89 ( lxxxix ) was a common year starting on thursday ( link will display the full calendar ) of the julian calendar . at the time , it was known as the year of the consulship of fulvus and atratinus ( or , less frequently , year 842 `` ab urbe condita '' ) . the denomination 89 for this year has been used since the early medieval period , when the anno domini calendar era became the prevalent method in europe for naming years .
