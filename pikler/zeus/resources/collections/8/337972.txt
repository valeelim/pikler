Laurie Metcalf     lauren elizabeth `` laurie '' metcalf ( born june 16 , 1955 ) is an american movie and television series actress . she played jackie harris in the sitcom `` roseanne '' . she played carolyn bigsby in the sitcom `` desperate housewives '' she played debbie salt in `` scream 2 '' . she played one of the characters in the `` toy story '' franchise . metcalf received three emmy awards for her performance . she was born in carbondale , illinois .
