Cumbia music     cumbia music is a latin music genre . cumbia music came from colombia 's caribbean-coastal region . cumbia music has become popular in colombia , mexico and argentina . cumbia has dance and tropical music mixed . in the 1990s , selena helped cumbia music become more popular with songs like `` baila esta cumbia '' , `` no quiero saber '' , `` no debes jugar '' , `` la llamada '' and `` como la flor '' . she also helped `` technocumbia '' rise in popularity with `` techno cumbia '' . shakira had also recorded several songs in cumbia .
