All the Vermeers in New York     all the vermeers in new york is an american movie released in 1990. it was directed by jon jost . mark is a broker on wall street who notices a woman that resembles a painting by dutch painter johannes vermeer . the woman is named anna and she is an actress from france . the two start a romantic relationship when they meet each other for the first time . mark suffers from a cerebral hemorrhage while calling anna and then anna disappears into a vermeer painting at the end . the film won the caligari film award at the berlin international film festival in 1991. roger ebert liked the film , but he thought that it ended too soon .
