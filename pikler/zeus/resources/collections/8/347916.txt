List of World Heritage Sites in Spain     the united nations educational , scientific and cultural organization ( unesco ) world heritage sites are places of importance to cultural or natural heritage . as of 2010 , spain has 42 total sites on the list . this is second only to italy . the pyrénées – mont perdu world heritage site is shared with france . the prehistoric rock-art sites in the côa valley and siega verde site is shared with portugal . the table lists information about each world heritage site : this is the list of heritage sites by autonomous community .
