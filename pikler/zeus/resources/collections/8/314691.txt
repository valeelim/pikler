Five Finger Death Punch     five finger death punch is an american heavy metal band from los angeles , california . five finger death punch has five members in the band ; the current band members are zoltan bathory , jason hook , chris kael , ivan moody and jeremy spencer . band members . - current - zoltan bathory – guitars ( since 2005 ) - jason hook – guitars , backing vocals ( since 2009 ) - chris kael – bass , backing vocals ( since 2011 ) - ivan moody – vocals ( since 2006 ) - jeremy spencer – drums ( since 2005 ) - former - caleb bingham – guitars ( 2006 ) - darrell roberts – guitars , backing vocals ( 2006–2009 ) - matt snell – bass , backing vocals ( 2006–2010 ) so far as of 2011 , five finger death punch has released 4 studio albums : albums . - `` the way of the fist '' ( 2007 ) - `` war is the answer '' ( 2009 ) - `` american capitalist '' ( 2011 ) - `` the wrong side of heaven and the righteous side of hell vol . 1 '' ( 2013 ) - `` the wrong side of heaven and the righteous side of hell vol . 2 '' ( 2013 ) ( to be released ) - `` got your six '' ( 2015 )
