CAE Global Academy Phoenix     cae global academy phoenix ( formerly sabena airline training center ) is a private flight school ( airline pilot training ) in mesa , arizona , usa . the school is located at falcon field airport and has 49 aircraft including 15 diamond da20-c1 , 10 diamond da40 , 4 diamond da42-l360 , 11 piper archer , 8 piper arrow and one beechcraft 90. the school has also three flight simulators . in 1991 , belgian government gives the `` école d ’ aviation civile '' created in 1953 to sabena airline . it is renowned first belgian aviation school and then sabena air training center . the same year , it was decided to carry out practical training in flight to phoenix in u.s.a because the weather in arizona allows flights 365 days per year in a high and complex air traffic . following that decisions , the school is created as a subsidiary of sabena flight academy and under the name of sabena airline training center . in 2008 , following the absorption of the belgian school by cae , the us subsidiary is also purchased . the school becomes part of the cae global academy network and is renamed cae global academy phoenix .
