Pentene     pentene is the name of alkenes with the formula ch . they are hydrocarbons . in total , there are five different pentenes . pentens occur in cracking processes and in natural gas . pentenes are used for polymerization , and for the synthesis of isoprene , amyl alcohols and alkylphenols . amyl alcohols are used as stabilizers in the production of chloroform and dichloromethane to stop phosgene from being made .
