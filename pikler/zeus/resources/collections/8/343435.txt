Agnieszka Radwańska     agnieszka radwańska ( born 6 march 1989 in kraków ) is a polish tennis player . she is currently the world number three women 's tennis player . she is also the highest ranking polish tennis player . in 2007 , radwańska became the first polish player to win a wta singles title . in 2012 she lost the final of the wimbledon championships to serena williams . she won wta finals in singapour in 2015 .
