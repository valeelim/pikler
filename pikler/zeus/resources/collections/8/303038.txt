Audi Cup 2011     the audi cup 2011 ( the second edition of the audi cup ) was a two-day tournament that took place in munich , germany . the tournament featured four teams , ac milan , s.c. internacional , fc bayern munich and fc barcelona . the winners of the tournament were fc barcelona , defeating bayern munich 2-0. s.c. internacional took third place , defeating ac milan 2 ( 2 ) - 2 ( 0 ) .
