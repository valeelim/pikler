Vanuatu at the Olympics     vanuatu was first at the olympic games in 1988. they have been in every summer olympic games since then . the nation has never been in the winter olympic games . as of 2011 , no ni-vanuatu athlete has ever won an olympic medal . the national olympic committee for vanuatu was formed in 1987 and recognized by the international olympic committee the same year . related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - vanuatu profile at london2012.com
