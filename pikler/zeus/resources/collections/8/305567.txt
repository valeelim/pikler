Titus O'Neil     thaddeus bullard ( born april 29 , 1977 in boynton beach , florida ) better known under the ring name titus o'neil , is an american professional wrestler and former american football player currently signed to world wrestling entertainment ( wwe ) . he is one half of the prime time players along with darren young . he competed on the second season of nxt where he finished 5th and also competed on the fifth season of nxt called nxt redemption . he played professional football in the arena football league for the utah blaze , tampa bay storm , las vegas gladiators and carolina cobras . championships . - florida championship wrestling - florida tag team championship ( one time ) ( with damien sandow ) - pro wrestling illustrated - he was ranked # 169 of the 500 best singles wrestlers by pwi in the pwi 500 in 2012 . - he was ranked # 82 of the top 500 singles wrestlers in the pwi 500 in 2013. other websites . - titus o'neil at wwe.com
