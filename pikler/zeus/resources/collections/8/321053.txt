Awa Province (Chiba)    awa province ( chiba ) the province was on the tip of the boso peninsula ( 房総半島 ) . awa province was created during the reign of empress genshō . in the meiji period , the provinces of japan were converted into prefectures . the maps of japan and awa province were reformed in the 1870s . `` awa jinja '' was the chief shinto shrine ( `` ichinomiya '' ) of awa . related pages . - provinces of japan - prefectures of japan - list of regions of japan - list of islands of japan other websites . - murdoch 's map of provinces , 1903
