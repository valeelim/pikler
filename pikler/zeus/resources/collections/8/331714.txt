Offal     `` offal '' is the name for the internal organs or entrails of a dead animal . it includes the heart , kidneys , lungs , intestines and brains . the word can also be used to cover other parts of the animal including the tongue , tail and feet . the word offal means the parts that fall off when the animal is cut up for meat . offal from birds are called `` giblets '' . in some parts of the world people eat offal , and in others doing so is considered taboo or strange .
