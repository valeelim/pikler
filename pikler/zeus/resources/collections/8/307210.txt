Costumbres     `` costumbres '' is a song by american singer selena . the song was written by juan gabriel , a mexican songwriter , who gave the rights to selena to record . the song first appeared on `` dulce amor '' ( 1988 ) . it became a single off of `` siempre selena '' ( 1996 ) . the remix version is included in `` siempre selena '' . `` costumbres '' is a song by puerto rican singer la india . she recorded the song as a tribute for selena . it was included as a cover version on her album `` sobre el fuego '' . it peaked on the hot latin tracks chart at number eight . la india also performed the song during the selena Â¡vive ! tribute concert in 2005 .
