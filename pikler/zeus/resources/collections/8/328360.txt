Plastic Man     plastic man ( real name patrick `` eel '' o'brian ) is a fictional comic-book superhero , with the ability to shift his body into any shape or form , created by artist jack cole . he was published by quality comics first and then later by dc comics . plastic man first appeared in august 1941. after being shot by an acid bullet , his fellow burglar partners left him for dead without hesitation . this angered him and caused him to become a crime fighter . the acid from the bullet caused a chemical reaction inside of him that allows his body to take on any shape or form , even ones with moving parts .
