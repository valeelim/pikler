Cat Island, Bahamas    cat island , bahamas cat island is in the central bahamas . it is one of the districts of the bahamas . mount alvernia is the countries highest point . the first european settlers were loyalists who fled the american revolution . they arrived in 1783. the island may have been named after arthur catt , a pirate . or the name may be for the one-time large population of feral cats . historically , the island gained wealth from cotton plantations . now slash and burn farming is the main way of life for cat islanders . an economic crop is cascarilla bark . it is gathered and shipped to italy . there it becomes a main ingredient in medicines , scents and campari . new bight airport and arthur 's town airport serve the island .
