Halloween 5: The Revenge of Michael Myers    halloween 5 : the revenge of michael myers halloween 5 : the revenge of michael myers is a 1989 american horror movie . it is set in a fictional illinois town called haddonfield . this is the fifth movie in the `` halloween '' movie series . the film 's story begins exactly one year after the previous movie . the shape returns to kill his niece , but samuel loomis and sheriff meeker manage to intervene .
