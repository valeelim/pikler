1961 Memorial Cup     the 1961 memorial cup final was the 43rd junior ice hockey championship of the canadian amateur hockey association . the teams involved were the toronto st. michael 's majors of the ontario hockey association who played against the edmonton oil kings of the central alberta hockey league . st. michael 's won their 4th memorial cup , defeating edmonton 4 games to 2. scores . - game 1 : st. michael 's 4-0 edmonton - game 2 : st. michael 's 4-1 edmonton - game 3 : st. michael 's 4-2 edmonton - game 4 : edmonton 5-4 st. michael 's - game 5 : edmonton 4-2 st. michael 's - game 6 : st. michael 's 4-2 edmonton arnold brown , andre champagne , gerry cheevers , jack cole , paul conlin , terry clancy , bruce draper , dave draper , dave dryden , roger galipeau , paul jackson , larry keenan , duncan macdonald , bill macmillan , barry mackenzie , peter noakes , terry o'malley , sonny osborne , brian walsh . coach : father david bauer .
