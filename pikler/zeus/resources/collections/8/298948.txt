The King's Speech    the king 's speech the king 's speech is a 2010 british historical drama movie . it is set in the 1930s . it was directed by tom hooper and written by david seidler . colin firth plays king george vi who , to overcome his stammer , sees lionel logue . logue is an australian speech therapist played by geoffrey rush . the two men become friends as they work together . after his brother , edward viii , abdicates , the new king relies on logue to help him make a radio broadcast at the beginning of world war ii .
