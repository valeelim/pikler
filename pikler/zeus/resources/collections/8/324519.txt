Nicholas Lyndhurst     nicholas simon lyndhurst ( born 20 april 1961 ) is a british actor . he was born in gosport , hampshire . he played roles in television sitcoms `` going straight '' , `` butterflies '' , `` only fools and horses '' , `` the two of us '' , `` goodnight sweetheart '' , `` after you 've gone '' and `` rock & chips '' .
