1961–62 AHL season     the 1961–62 ahl season was the 26th season of the american hockey league . the league initiates the james c. hendy memorial award for outstanding team or league executives . the league resumes east and west divisions . the john d. chick trophy is first awarded for the west division champions of the regular season . the f. g. `` teddy '' oke trophy is awarded to east division champions of the regular season . eight teams played 70 games each in the schedule . the springfield indians finished first overall again in the regular season , and won their third consecutive calder cup championship . team changes . - the second incarnation of the pittsburgh hornets are added as an expansion team in the west division . `` note : gp = games played ; w = wins ; l = losses ; t = ties ; gf = goals for ; ga = goals against ; pts = points ; `` note : gp = games played ; g = goals ; a = assists ; pts = points ; pim = penalty minutes '' - complete list calder cup playoffs . - first round - springfield indians defeated cleveland barons 4 games to 2 .
