Handshakes and Middle Fingers     handshakes and middle fingers is the second major release studio album by canadian rapper , classified . `` handshakes and middle fingers '' was released on march 22 , 2011. the album spawned three singles `` that ai n't classy '' , `` the day does n't die '' and `` maybe it 's just me '' . `` that ai n't classy '' peaked at # 45 on the canadian hot 100 while `` the day does n't die '' peaked at # 83 on the canadian hot 100. the album peaked at # 7 on the canadian albums chart and also hit # 2 on the itunes top seller list .
