Holly Valance     holly valance ( born holly rachel vukadinovic on 11 may 1983 ) is an australian-british actress , singer and model . she is a dual citizen of australia and the united kingdom . she was born in melbourne to an english immigrant mother and serbian immigrant father . she began her career in 1999 as felicity scully on australian soap opera `` neighbours '' . she began her singing career in 2002. valance was a contestant in the 2011 series of `` strictly come dancing '' . other websites . - official website
