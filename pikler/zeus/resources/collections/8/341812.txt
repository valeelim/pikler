Supreme Court of Argentina     the supreme court of argentina is the highest court of law in argentina . it started on 15 january 1863. the rulings of the court are final . the court also decides on cases about the constitution . the members of the supreme court are chosen by the president . as of april 2012 , the members of the supreme court are : - president : dr. ricardo luis lorenzetti . - vice-president : dra . elena i. highton de nolasco . - justices : dr. carlos s. fayt < br > dr. juan carlos maqueda < br > dr. eugenio raúl zaffaroni < br > dr. enrique santiago petracchi < br > dra . carmen maría argibay .
