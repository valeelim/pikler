Aruba at the Olympics     aruba was first at the olympic games in 1988. they have been in each summer olympic games since then . aruba has not won an olympic medal . aruba has not been in any winter olympic games . previously aruba competed under netherlands antilles , kingdom of the netherlands between 1952 until 1984. aruba became a separate region of the kingdom of the netherlands in 1986. the national olympic committee for aruba was formed in 1985 and recognized in 1986. related pages . - list of ioc country codes - netherlands antilles at the olympics other websites . - aruba profile at london2012.com
