Australia (2008 movie)    australia ( 2008 movie ) australia is a 2008 australian epic historical romantic drama movie . it was directed by baz luhrmann . it starred nicole kidman and hugh jackman . it made the second most money of any australian movie . the first was `` crocodile dundee '' . the movie is set between 1939 and 1942. events in northern australia included the bombing of darwin during world war ii . the movie was released in both australia and the united states on 26 november 2008. it was released to the rest of the world in late december 2008 and january and february 2009 .
