Thailand at the Olympics     thailand was first at the olympic games in 1952. they have been in every summer olympic games since then . they were not in the 1980 summer olympics . thailand has been in the winter olympic games since 2002. only one person , prawat nagvajara , has competed for thailand in the winter olympics . thai athletes have won a total of twenty-one medals , mostly in boxing . in 2008 thailand won 8 medals , their best so far . the national olympic committee for thailand was formed in 1948 and recognized in 1950. related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - thailand profile at london2012.com
