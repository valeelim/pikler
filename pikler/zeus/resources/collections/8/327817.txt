Common year starting on Monday     this is the calendar for any common year starting on monday , january 1 ( dominical letter g ) . the most recent year of this type was 2007 and the next one will be 2018 in the gregorian calendar , or 2013 and 2019 in the julian calendar . the exceptional common year , 1900 was also a common year starting on monday in the gregorian calendar . ( see tables at bottom . ) a common year is a year with 365 days ; in other words , not a leap year .
