Tipasa Province     tipaza or tipasa is a province on the coast of algeria . the capital is tipasa . it is 50 km west of the capital of algeria . the province has 10 districts and 28 `` communes '' or municipalities . districts . 1. ahmer el aïn 1. bou ismaïl 1. cherchell 1. damous 1. fouka 1. gouraya 1. hadjout 1. koléa 1. sidi amar 1. tipaza
