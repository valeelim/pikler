Kung Fu Panda (franchise)    kung fu panda ( franchise ) the `` kung fu panda '' franchise from dreamworks animation , consists of three movies : `` kung fu panda '' ( 2008 ) , `` kung fu panda 2 '' ( 2011 ) and `` kung fu panda 3 '' ( 2016 ) . two annie award-winning shorts , `` secrets of the furious five '' ( 2008 ) and `` kung fu panda holiday '' ( 2010 ) , were also released . a tv show for nickelodeon called `` '' premiered in the fall of 2011 and is currently on the air .
