Vibe (magazine)    vibe ( magazine ) vibe is a music magazine that is published in the united states . `` vibe '' was founded by quincy jones and focuses on rhythm and blues and hip-hop music singers . `` vibe '' is published bi-monthly from new york city . as of 2007 , `` vibe '' has a circulation of 800,000. the `` vibe '' awards is aired annually , which began in 2003 , on the upn channel . other websites . - quincy jones 's oral history video excerpts at the national visionary leadership project - `` vx : ten years of vibe photography '' at amazon.com . - `` the vibe history of hip-hop '' at amazon.com . - an interview with editor-in-chief danyel smith
