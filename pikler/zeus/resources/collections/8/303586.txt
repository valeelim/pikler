Audi Cup 2009     the audi cup 2009 ( the first edition of the audi cup ) was a two-day tournament that took place in germany . the tournament featured four teams , ac milan , boca juniors , bayern munich and manchester united . the winners of the tournament were bayern munich , defeating manchester united 0 ( 7 ) - 0 ( 6 ) . boca juniors took third place , defeating ac milan 1 ( 4 ) - 1 ( 3 ) .
