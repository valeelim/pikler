Mickey     `` mickey '' is a song by us singer toni basil . it was a top 5 hit when it was released in 1982. it is was covered in 1999 by lolly , who also had a top 5 hit with the song . the top 10 2005 hit by gwen stefani - `` hollaback girl '' and 2012 top 40 hit by madonna ft nicki minaj & mia - `` give me all your luvin ' '' also had musical influences from this song , toni basil 's version was released by radialchoice and lolly 's version was released by polydor .
