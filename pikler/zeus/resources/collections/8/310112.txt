Yelena Chernykh     yelena chernykh ( née kolesnichenko ; august 24 , 1979—october 4 , 2011 ) was a russian theatre actress . 32-year-old yelena was killed in a car crash that took place october 4 , 2011 in novoanninsky . together with her died her colleagues , 59-year-old alexander egorov and 52-year-old valentin yudina . at the time her death yelena was married to yegor chernykh with whom she had a daughter named anastasia chernykh ( b.2002 ) .
