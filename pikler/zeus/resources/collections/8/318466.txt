December Boys     december boys is a 2007 australian movie . it was directed by rod hardy and written by marc rosenberg . it was taken from the 1963 novel of the same name by michael noonan . it was released on 14 september 2007 in the uk and us and 20 september 2007 in australia . `` december boys '' is daniel radcliffe 's first non- '' harry potter '' movie role since 2001 's `` the tailor of panama '' .
