Virgin Islands at the Olympics     the united states virgin islands has been in 9 summer olympic games . they have also been in the winter olympic games 7 times . peter holmberg took second place in sailing at the 1988 summer olympics . the virgin islands olympic committee was formed in 1967 and recognized by the ioc the same year . related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - home page - virgin islands , us profile at london2012.com
