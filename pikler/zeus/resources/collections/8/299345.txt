Chile at the Olympics     chile was first at the olympic games at the first 1896 summer olympics . the international olympic committee 's official abbreviation for chile is chi . a team from chile has been in most summer olympic games and winter olympic games . chilean athletes have won a total of thirteen medals , with tennis as the top medal-producing sport . the national olympic committee for chile was formed in 1934. related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - chile profile at london2012.com - chile at olympic.org
