First Austrian Republic     the republic of austria is the period of austrian history after the signing of the treaty of saint-germain-en-laye of september 1919. it went through the settlement after the end of world war i which put an end to the republic of german-austria . it continued up to world war ii . austria was a `` de-facto '' republic , but its official name was the federal state of austria . this period was had a lot of conflict between the left and the right . the july revolt of 1927 was an example . the constitution of austria was started in 1920 and changed in 1929. the federal state ( sometimes called the first republic ) came to an end with the `` anschluss '' to nazi germany in 1938. some people say the end came with the start of the austro-fascist dictatorship in 1933/34 following the austrian civil war .
