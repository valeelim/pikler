Newhall, California    newhall , california newhall is the southernmost district of santa clarita , california . it is the oldest district of santa clarita . before 1987 , newhall was an independent but unincorporated town . it was the first permanent anglo settlement in the valley . it is named after businessman henry newhall . newhall is home to the william s. hart county park . it has tours of the famous silent movie maker 's mansion . over the years , newhall has been the location for many movies , including `` suddenly '' ( 1954 ) and disney 's `` the world 's greatest athlete '' ( 1973 ) . other websites . - santa clarita guide - an official guide to the santa clarita valley - ( photos , text , tv shows )
