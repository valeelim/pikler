Powys Castle     powys castle ( or powis castle or welsh : castell coch ) is a mediaeval castle , fortress and grand country mansion near the town of welshpool , in powys , mid wales . it is the residence of the earl of powis . the castle is built of sandstone . the rock is from new red sandstone of the permian or triassic periods . the castle has extensive , attractive formal gardens , terraces , parkland , deerpark and landscaped estate . the property is under the care of the national trust , who operate it under the name `` powis castle and garden '' . queen victoria visited the castle during her tour through england and wales in 1832. other websites . - powis castle & garden information at the national trust - castle wales : powis castle
