El Chico Del Apartamento 512     `` el chico del apartamento 512 '' ( ) is the sixth and final single released from selena 's grammy nominated album `` amor prohibido '' ( 1994 ) . the song never had a music video because it was released one day before selena was killed . the song had positive feedback from music critics . the song was performed on c minor with 102 beats per minute . the song was covered by many latin american artists .
