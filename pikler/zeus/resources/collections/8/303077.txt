Saturn Ramenskoye    f.c . saturn ramenskoye fc saturn ramenskoye ( , or english : state-owned enterprise of the moscow region football club saturn moscow region ) is a russian football team near the moscow area of ramenskoye . it was also known as fc saturn moscow region . in 2006 , the club broke the record for the most draws in a russian premier league season , 16 draws . the club closed down in 2011 , as there were bad financial problems . however , it was revived on the base of their former farm club fc saturn-2 moscow region . the club 's past names were krylya sovetov ( 1946-1957 ) , trud ( 1958-1959 ) and saturn ren-tv ( 2002-2004 ) .
