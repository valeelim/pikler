Urdă     urdă ( romanian pronunciation : [ ˈurdə ] ) is a romanian fresh white cheese made from whey of sheep , goat or cow . urdă is produced by heating the whey resulting from the draining of any type of cheese . it is often made into molds to the shape of a half sphere . the paste is finely grained , silky and palatable . since urdă is produced by romanian shepherds since time immemorial in romania , this product is considered a traditional romanian dairy product . it is used especially in the preparation of several desserts as clătită and plăcintă .
