God Save the Queen (Sex Pistols song)    god save the queen ( sex pistols song ) god save the queen is a song by english punk rock band the sex pistols . it was the band 's second single , and featured on their only album , `` never mind the bollocks , here 's the sex pistols '' . the lyrics , as well as the cover , were controversial at the time . the bbc banned the song . it has been covered by various bands , including motorhead .
