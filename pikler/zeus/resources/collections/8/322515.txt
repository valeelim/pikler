Arkady Ter-Tadevosyan     arkady ter-tadevosyan ( ; also known by the troops under his command as komandos ( ) ; may 22 , 1939 in tbilisi , georgian ssr ) was a military leader of the armenian forces during the nagorno-karabakh war . ter-tadevosyan is best known as the commander of the operation to capture the town of shushi in may 1992. ter-tadevosyan was born in tbilisi , georgian ssr . he attended a military academy in saint petersburg . sources . - `` tadevosyan , arkady '' entry in the encyclopedia of karabakh liberation war : 1988-1994. yerevan , 2004 .
