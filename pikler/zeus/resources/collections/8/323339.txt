Broccoflower     broccoflower is a vegetable . it is either of two plants of the species `` brassica oleracea '' with light green heads . the part that can be eaten is the young flower head ( inflorescence ) of the plant . there are two forms of `` brassica oleracea '' that can be called broccoflower . one is shaped like regular cauliflower . the other has a spiky appearance . they have a color like that of broccoli .
