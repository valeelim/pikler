Javan rusa     the javan rusa or sunda sambar ( `` rusa timorensis '' ) is a deer which lives on the islands of java , bali and timor . it is also an introduced species to irian jaya , borneo ( kalimantan ) the lesser sunda islands , maluku , sulawesi , australia , mauritius , new caledonia , new zealand , papua new guinea , rÃ©union . the deer lives in a habitat similar to that of the chital of india , that is , open dry and mixed deciduous forests , parklands , and savannas . it is a close relative of the larger sambar deer . it is hunted in eastern australasia . javan rusa deer breed in the rutting period in july and august . they are active in the early morning and the late afternoon .
