Gemini (astrology)    gemini ( astrology ) gemini is the astrological zodiac sign that represents the constellation gemini . in the tropical zodiac , gemini is from may 20 to june 20. under the sidereal zodiac , it is now from june 15 to july 15. the opposite sign to gemini is sagittarius . this sign for gemini was made from two lines , representing the twins . in mythology gemini is related to greek myth of the twins of zeus and leda , castor and pollux . astrologers broadly agree that the following are the characteristics of gemini : mental , adaptable , flexible , changeable , responsive , sociable , superficial - talkative - very intelligent - adaptable - flexible - sociable - individualistic - independent - funny - curious negative characteristics . - superficial - overactive - impatient , do things without thinking - impulsive ( strong-minded ) - unwilling to commit in astrology gemini is ruled by the planet mercury . it is said that people born in gemini can have jobs where quick thinking and flexibility are important . in medical astrology and anatomy gemini is said to rule the shoulders , arms and hands .
