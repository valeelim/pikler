Eh, Eh (Nothing Else I Can Say)    eh , eh ( nothing else i can say ) `` eh , eh ( nothing else i can say ) '' is a single by lady gaga . it appeared on gaga 's debut album `` the fame '' and was released as a single on january 10 , 2009. its music video references gaga 's italian heritage . though the single did not chart in the united states , the recording industry association of america certified the song gold on february 29 , 2016 , for shipments and streams equal to 500,000 copies there . `` eh , eh '' charted in the top ten in france , new zealand and sweden .
