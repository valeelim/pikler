Crowbar     it is used as a lever either to force apart two objects or to remove nails . crowbars are commonly used to open nailed wooden crates . common uses for larger crowbars are : removing nails , prying apart boards , and generally breaking things . crowbars can be used as any of the three lever classes but the curved end is usually used as a first-class lever , and the flat end as a second class lever . in mining , crowbars are used to break blasted rocks and to remove loose rock on roof sides and the working face , but not much in modern mining .
