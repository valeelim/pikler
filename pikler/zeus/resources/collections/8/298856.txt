Shoppers Drug Mart     shoppers drug mart corporation ( tsx : sc ) is canada 's largest pharmacy chain . shoppers drug mart is headquartered in the toronto , ontario . the chain is founded by murray koffler in 1962. the store is also called pharmaprix in quebec and hawkesbury , ontario . the store usually has a organic foods section , at least five food aisles , a healthy living section , a cosmetics centre , a carlton cards aisle , an easypix digital center/photolab , a full selection of over-the-counter drugs and health products and in some stores they have a full service canada post office . other websites . - shoppers drug mart 's official website
