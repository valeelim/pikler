Pradikta Wicaksono     pradikta wicaksono ( born in jakarta , indonesia , january 10 , 1986 ) or often called dikta , is an indonesian singer . he is a singer for the group yovie & nuno , along with dudi oris . he joined yovie & nuno in 2007. pradikta wicaksono was born in jakarta on january 10 , 1986. he was the second child of three siblings . other websites . - yovie & nuno official site
