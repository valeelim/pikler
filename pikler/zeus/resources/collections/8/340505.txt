Djelfa Province     djelfa is a province of algeria . the capital is djelfa . there are over 1.2 million people . important towns are tadmit , el khemis , and selmana . the province has 12 districts . there are also 36 `` communes '' or municipalities . districts . 1. aïn el ibil 1. aïn oussera 1. birine 1. charef 1. dar chioukh 1. djelfa 1. el idrissia 1. faidh el botma 1. had sahary 1. hassi bahbah 1. messaâd 1. sidi ladjel
