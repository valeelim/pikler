Convention against Torture     convention against torture is a treaty of international human rights law created by united nations in 1984 that prohibits all kind of torture , any cruel , inhuman treatment or punishment harming the person 's dignity . the convention also has the optional protocol that enables international organization for human rights investigators to research the prisons in a country that ratifies the protocol for knowing if any torturea or inhuman treatments or punishments prohibited by the convention are done . related pages . - torture - international human rights law
