Guar     guar is a crop which is mostly grown in pakistan . this crop is planted in july or august and harvested at the end of november . guar is widely used in oil exploring fields and also in the cosmetics industry . there are four major countries in the world which produce guar : sudan , india , pakistan , and the united states . the price of this crop went up in 2012 when its price went to 37,000 pakistan rupees per 100 kg .
