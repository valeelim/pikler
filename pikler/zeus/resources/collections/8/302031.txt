Vespoidea     vespoidea is a superfamily of the insect order hymenoptera in class of insects . the members of this group are wasps and ants . families . - bradynobaenidae - formicidae - ants - mutillidae - velvet ants - pompilidae - spider wasps - rhopalosomatidae - rhopalosomatid wasps - sapygidae - sapygid wasps - scoliidae - scoliid wasps - sierolomorphidae - sierolomorphid wasps - tiphiidae - tiphiid wasps - vespidae - paper wasps , hornets , potter wasps , yellow jackets , and relatives
