Rock en español     rock en español is a spanish language version of rock music . in english-speaking countries , people call spanish rock `` rock en español '' , while in spanish-speaking countries they call rock music `` anglo rock '' . rock en español has music from the caribbean such as ska , reggae and soca . rock music began in 1956 when gloria ríos recorded `` el relojito '' . chicano rock are rock music sung by mexican americans , their version has r & b influences .
