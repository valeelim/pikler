One More Night     one more night is a song performed by american pop rock group maroon 5 from their fourth studio album `` overexposed . '' the single was released for digital download and contemporary radio on june 26 , 2012. the song was written by frontman adam levine , producer shellback , executive max martin and savan kotecha and was produced by ryan tedder . the song debuted live at revel in atlantic city , new jersey on the evening of may 18 , 2012 and was viraly uploaded by several other people on youtube the next day . the pop-reggae fused song was announced on may 22nd on the band 's official site that it would serve as the second single for their fourth album . the song was recorded live in los angeles , california earlier in 2012. the band said in an interview with rolling stone that it the band 's first attempt at trying a reggae song and fusing it with a simple pop melody . the song was released on the same day as their fourth studio album `` overexposed '' on june 26 , 2012 .
