Andrei Kostitsyn     andrej aliehavič kaścicyn ( belarusian : андрэй алегавiч касціцын ; born february 3 , 1985 ) , better known as andrei kostitsyn , is a belarusian professional ice hockey forward . kostitsyn plays for the montreal canadiens of the national hockey league ( nhl ) . he has a younger brother who also plays in the nhl for the nashville predators named sergei .
