Lokomotiv Yaroslavl     lokomotiv yaroslavl ( ) is a russian professional ice hockey team playing in the kontinental hockey league ( khl ) . the lokomotiv yaroslavl plane crash occurred on wednesday , september 7 , 2011 at 16:02 local time , when a yak-service yakolev yak-42 airplane , with the professional ice hockey team and coaching staff of lokomotiv yaroslavl of the kontinental hockey league , crashed near yaroslavl , russia . the team was going to minsk , belarus , to start the 2011-12 khl season against dinamo minsk . 44 out of the 45 people on board were confirmed dead , one crew member survived . russian open championship winner ( 3 ) : 1996–97 , 2001–02 , 2002–03 runners-up of khl ( 2 ) : 2007–08 , 2008–09 bronze medal winner of russian open championship and khl ( 4 ) : 1997–98 , 1998–99 , 2004-2005 , 2010–11 runners-up of iihf continental cup : 2003 bronze medal of spengler cup : 2003 other websites . - lokomotiv yaroslavl official website - home arena , statistics , history
