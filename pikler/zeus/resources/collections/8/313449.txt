The Waitresses     the waitresses were an american experimental new wave music group . they were from akron , ohio . the group formed in the early 1980s . their song `` i know what boys like '' was released in 1980 but did not chart until 1982. they also represented the theme song for the television series `` square pegs '' in 1982-1983. the group broke up in 1984. their lead singer died due to lung cancer in 1996 .
