Gary Sinise     gary alan sinise ( born march 17 , 1955 ) is an american stand-up comedian , actor , director , voice artist , and comedian . he as in the movies `` of mice and men '' , `` forrest gump '' , `` george wallace '' , and `` open season '' . since 2004 , sinise has starred in cbs 's `` '' as detective mac taylor . sinise has won many awards including an emmy and a golden globe award and was nominated for an academy award . he was awarded the presidential citizen medal by george w. bush for work he did supporting the u.s military and supporting iraqi children . sinise has been married to actress moira harris since 1981 and they have three children : sophie anne ( b . 1988 ) , mccanna anthony ( b . 1990 ) , and ella jane ( b . 1992 ) .
