Lubango     lubango is the capital city of huíla in angola . there are 100,757 people . until 1975 , the city 's name was sá da bandeira . during the angolan civil war ( 1975–2002 ) , lubango was a major base of cuban , swapo . lubango 's economy is based on agriculture . notable people . - marco abreu , of the angola national football team was born in lubango in 1974 .
