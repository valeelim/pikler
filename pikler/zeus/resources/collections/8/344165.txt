Poltergeist (movie)    poltergeist ( movie ) poltergeist is a 1982 american horror movie set in orange county , california . it was directed by tobe hooper . it stars craig t. nelson , jobeth wiliams , beatrice straight , dominique dunne , oliver robins and heather o'rourke . it was followed by two sequels : `` '' and `` poltergeist iii '' . it was remade in 2015 .
