Ricardo Lorenzetti     ricardo luis lorenzetti ( born 19 september 1955 ) is a member of the supreme court of justice of argentina . he was elected president of the high court by his peers on november 7 , 2006. lorenzetti was born in rafaela , santa fe province . he is the son of a primary school teacher and a travelling salesman . he went to school at the national university of the littoral . he became a lawyer in 1978 .
