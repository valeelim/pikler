Inverness Airport     inverness airport is an international airport at dalcross , 7 nautical miles ( ~8 miles or 13 km ) north east of the city of inverness in the scottish highlands . the airport is the main gateway for travellers to the highlands and islands . there are a wide range of scheduled services throughout the united kingdom and ireland ; also there are limited charter and freight flights to continental europe . 591,397 passengers passed through the airport in 2009. it is owned by highlands and islands airports limited ( hial ) who own most of the regional airports in mainland scotland and the outlying islands . like many of the airports in the highlands and islands , this airport was originally a raf air base , and played a role in world war ii . it was converted to civilian use in 1947 .
