Mary Elizabeth Winstead     mary elizabeth winstead ( born november 28 , 1984 ) is an american motion picture and television actress . she played jessica in the soap opera `` passions '' . she also played lucy mcclane in `` live free or die hard '' . she also played the scream queen in many horror pictures , including `` black christmas '' and `` final destination 3 '' . winstead was born in rocky mount , north carolina . she was raised in utah .
