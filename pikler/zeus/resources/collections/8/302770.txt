Urticaceae     urticaceae the nettle family comprising about 45 genera of herbs , shrubs , small trees , and a few vines , distributed primarily in tropical regions . the family is typical of the nettle order . many species , especially the nettles ( `` urtica '' ) and australian nettle trees ( `` laportea '' ) , have stinging hairs on the stems and leaves . the leaves are varied and the sap is usually watery . the small , greenish flowers often form clusters in the leaf axils . both male and female flowers may be borne on the same plant . the curled stamens of the male flowers straighten quickly as the flowers open , releasing the pollen .
