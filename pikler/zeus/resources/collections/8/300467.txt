Asbury Park, New Jersey    asbury park , new jersey asbury park is a city in monmouth county , new jersey , united states . it is next to the atlantic ocean on the jersey shore . asbury park is part of the new york city metropolitan area . in the 2010 united states census , the city population was 16,116. asbury park started as a borough on march 26 , 1874. it included parts of ocean township . asbury park became a city as of march 25 , 1897. the city is known for music . bruce springsteen comes from the city and started his career there . the new jersey marine sciences consortium called asbury park the sixth best beach in new jersey in the 2008 top 10 beaches contest .
