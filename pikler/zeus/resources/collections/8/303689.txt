Norma Cluster     the norma cluster ( aco 3627 or abell 3627 ) is a rich of galaxies near the center of the great attractor . it is both nearby and bright ; but it is difficult to observe because it is in the zone of avoidance . this is a region near the plane of the milky way , which obscures our view . the cluster is hidden by interstellar dust at optical wavelengths . its mass is estimated to be on the order of 10 solar masses . other websites . - eso press photos 46a-j/99 - galaxy cluster has two 'tails ' to tell . nasa image of the day , january 22 , 2010 .
