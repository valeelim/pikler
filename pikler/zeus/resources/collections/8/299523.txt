Doedicurus     doedicurus was an ancient armadillo are belonging to the family glyptodontidae . fossils have been found in patagonia , in south america . these herbivores ( plant-eaters ) may have been preyed upon by saber-toothed cats and borhyaenids ( extinct dog-like meat-eaters ) . `` doedicurus '' lived during the ice ages , during the pleistocene between 2 million and 15,000 years ago .
