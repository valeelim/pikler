Argentine National Congress     the congress of the argentine nation is the legislative branch of the government of argentina . it has two parts . there is a 72-seat senate and a 257-seat chamber of deputies . the congress building is in buenos aires . the congress is in charge of setting taxes and customs . these must be the same across the country . it rules the central bank of argentina , manages internal and external debt payment , and the value of national currency ( currently the argentine peso ) .
