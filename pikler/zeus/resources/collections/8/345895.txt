Tony Hancock     anthony john hancock ( 12 may 1924 - 24 june 1968 ) was a british comedian and actor . he was born in hall green , birmingham . he grew up in bournemouth , hampshire and was educated in swanage , dorset and reading , berkshire . he worked on radio , on television and in movies . he was a depressed alcoholic who committed suicide with an overdose of amphetamines and vodka in sydney , new south wales , australia .
