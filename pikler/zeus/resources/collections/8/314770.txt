Karl Barth     karl barth ( 1886-1968 ) was a protestant theologian who started a movement in theology that is now called neo-orthodoxy . he wrote `` church dogmatics '' and the `` gottingen dogmatics '' , as well as a number of biblical commentaries , most famously on the book of romans . he was heavily involved in the authorship of the anti-nazi barmen declaration in 1934. barth rejects biblical inerrancy , and he also rejects stronger forms of predestination . other websites . - theopedia on karl barth and the theology of karl barth
