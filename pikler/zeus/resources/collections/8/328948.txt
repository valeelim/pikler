Minute Waltz     the `` minute waltz '' is the nickname for the waltz in d flat major , op . 64 , no . 1 by frederic chopin . it was written in 1847. it is a piece of music for the piano . it is sometimes called `` the waltz of the little dog '' ( ) . this is because chopin was watching a little dog chase its tail when he wrote it . the little dog was `` marquis '' . he belonged to chopin 's friend george sand . marquis had befriended chopin . the composer mentioned marquis in several of his letters . in one letter dated 25 november 1846 , chopin wrote : `` please thank marquis for missing me and for sniffing at my door .
