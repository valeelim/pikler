Robin Hood (roller coaster)    robin hood ( roller coaster ) robin hood is a wooden roller coaster at walibi holland in the netherlands . robin hood was designed by `` stand company '' and built by vekoma in 2000. it has a capacity of 815 people per hour . the coaster has 2 trains , both having 4 cars with 6 seats ( per train 24 people ) . a ride takes about 2.5 minutes and the coaster is about 1 kilometer long .
