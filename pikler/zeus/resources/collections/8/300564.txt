Demolition Man (movie)    demolition man ( movie ) demolition man is a 1993 american science fiction action movie . it stars sylvester stallone , sandra bullock , wesley snipes and nigel hawthorne . the movie begins in 1996 los angeles and then jumps ahead to the year 2032 in san angeles ( merged san diego and los angeles ) . stallone acts as john spartan , a police officer who is intent on bringing down simon phoenix ( played by snipes ) , a criminal strongly intent on causing destruction and serious chaos .
