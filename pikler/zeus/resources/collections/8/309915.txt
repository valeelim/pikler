Factoid     factoid is stating a fact that is not backed up by evidence . the factoid may not be trusted because factoids become widely treated as if they were true . in 1973 , the term `` factoid '' was created and first used by norman mailer . a factoid is `` an item of unreliable information which is repeated so often that it becomes accepted as a fact '' . the most important thing is that the factoid is widely believed . a factoid refers to something that looks like a fact , could be a fact , but in fact is not a fact . the term can also be used to describe partial information without context .
