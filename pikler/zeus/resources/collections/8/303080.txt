PFC CSKA Sofia     pfc cska sofia ( ) , commonly known as cska or cska sofia , is a bulgarian football team from sofia , bulgaria . since cska sofia was created , the club has won 31 a pfg titles , more than any team in bulgaria , ever . also , cska sofia has reached two european cup semi-finals , four european cup quarter-finals and one cup winners ' cup semi-final , making the team the best in bulgaria .
