Hamid Karzai     hamid karzai ( , ; born 24 december 1957 ) is a former president of afghanistan . he served from 22 december 2001 to 29 september 2014. he was the first democratically elected leader of the country . he is ethnic pashtun from kandahar and can speak several different language , including english , french , persian , urdu and his native pashto language . he was educated in afghanistan and india . he lived in pakistan before becoming popular in late 2001. he has many family members and relatives living in the united states .
