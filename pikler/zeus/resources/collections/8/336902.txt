The Bonfire of the Vanities (movie)    the bonfire of the vanities ( movie ) the bonfire of the vanities is a 1990 american drama movie . it is based on the novel bonfire of the vanities by tom wolfe . tom hanks plays sherman mccoy , bruce willis plays peter fallow , kim cattrall plays judy mccoy , melanie griffith plays maria ruskin , morgan freeman plays judge leonard white . the movie is based on the novel of the same name and is focused on corporate greed , vanity and lust during the late 1980s in new york city . this movie was given substandard reviews by the movie critics ; it was a huge box office bomb .
