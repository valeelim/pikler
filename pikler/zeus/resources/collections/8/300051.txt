Blue Velvet (movie)    blue velvet ( movie ) blue velvet is a 1986 american mystery movie by david lynch . it is set in north carolina . the cast include dennis hopper , laura dern and kyle maclachlan . the title is from the 1963 bobby vinton song of the same name . this movie was originally detested by certain movie critics , although it is now widely acclaimed . it was controversial at the time of its 1986 release , and has since become a cult favorite . lynch got his second academy award nomination for best director for the movie .
