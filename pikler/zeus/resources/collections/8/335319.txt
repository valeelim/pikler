Amélie Mauresmo     amélie simone mauresmo ( born 5 july 1979 ) is a french retired tennis player . mauresmo was born in saint-germain-en-laye . she turned professional in 1993. she was the world number 1 women 's singles from 13 september to 17 october 2004 and from 20 march to 12 november 2006. she won a silver medal in singles at the 2004 summer olympics . in 2006 , she won the australian open and wimbledon . she retired in 2009. she is openly lesbian . in june 2014 , mauresmo became andy murray 's coach . other websites . - official site
