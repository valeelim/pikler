Good Charlotte (album)    good charlotte ( album ) good charlotte is the debut studio album by american pop punk band , good charlotte . `` good charlotte '' was released on september 26 , 2000. it was the first and last album to feature aaron escolopio on the drums . all of the songs except for `` let me go '' and `` seasons '' were written by benji and joel madden . `` let me go '' and `` seasons '' were written by benji madden , joel madden and josh ian .
