Lake Ülemiste    lake ülemiste lake ülemiste ( ) a lake in estonia . it is the largest of the lakes near tallinn , the capital city . tallinn takes its drinking water from this lake . the lake gets its water mostly from the small kurna stream and the larger pirita river ( through the vaskjala-ülemiste canal ) . there is a legend about this lake . the legend says that the `` old man of lake ülemiste '' ( ) lives in the lake . when anyone meets him , he always asks : `` is tallinn ready yet ? '' . if the other person answers `` yes , the city is ready . `` , then the old man will send the lake waters out to cover the city of tallinn . in this way , if anyone meets this old man , they should answer : `` no , the city is not ready . there is a lot of work to do still . '' in this way the city can be saved .
