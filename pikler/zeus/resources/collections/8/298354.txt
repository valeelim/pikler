Bourbon whiskey     bourbon is a whiskey that is distilled from grain mash containing at least 51 % corn and aged a minimum of two years . it gets its name from bourbon , kentucky where most bourbon is produced . many bourbons are made using a method called `` sour mash '' where fermentation of each new batch contains a little of the batch before to achieve a consistent quality . drinks made from bourbon include lynchburg lemonade , manhattan , and old fashioned .
