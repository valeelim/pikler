Cloaca     the cloaca is a common rear opening in land vertebrates . through this opening , the animals expel urine and faeces , and carry out reproduction . the cloaca is a primitive or 'basal ' feature of tetrapods . all amphibia , all sauropsids ( reptiles and birds ) and even monotreme mammals possess a cloaca . a different method is used by therian mammals , such as marsupials and placentals . they have an anus for defecation , and another opening for urine and sexual reproduction .
