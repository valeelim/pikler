Mother Mother     mother mother is a canadian indie rock band from quadra island , british columbia . mother mother has five members in the band ; the current band members are ryan guldemond , molly guldemond , ali siadat , jeremy page and jasmin parkin . the band formed in january 2005. they released their third album , `` eureka '' , on march 15th , 2011. so far as of 2011 , mother mother has released 3 albums : - `` touch up '' ( 2007 ) - `` o my heart '' ( 2008 ) - `` eureka '' ( 2011 ) singles . - `` the stand '' ( 2011 ) - `` baby do n't dance '' ( 2011 ) other websites . - mother mother 's official website
