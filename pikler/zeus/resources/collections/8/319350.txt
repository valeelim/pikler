France at the Olympics     france has taken part in every olympic games with the possible exception of the 1904 games . in 1904 , different sources list albert corey as taking place in the games for either the united states or france . the international olympic committee 's official abbreviation for france is fra . france has hosted the games 5 time : - 1900 summer olympics in paris - 1924 winter olympics in chamonix - 1924 summer olympics in paris - 1968 winter olympics in grenoble - 1992 winter olympics in albertville french athletes have won 671 medals at the summer olympic games . fencing and cycling are their best sports . france has won 94 medals at the winter olympic games . most of these are in alpine skiing . leading in that sport related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - france at olympic.org - france profile at london2012.com
