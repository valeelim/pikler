Money Train     money train is a 1995 american comedy crime thriller movie . in it are woody harrelson , robert blake , jennifer lopez and wesley snipes . harrelson , snipes and lopez play transit cops who are foster brothers . robert blake plays as an arrogant officer named patterson . `` money train '' not only received substandard and negative reviews , it was considered a box office bomb . it was released november 22 , 1995 .
