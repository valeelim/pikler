Michele and Jennifer Steffin     michele steffin and jennifer steffin were born on march 23 , 1981 in west covina , california . they are twins . when they were children , they were actresses . on the television series `` little house on the prairie '' they both played rose wilder . it happened in the early 1980s . in 1986 they played hope hazard on `` north and south '' television miniseries .
