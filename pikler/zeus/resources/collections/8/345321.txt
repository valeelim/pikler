Grande école     grandes écoles are specialised universities in france . each grande école is specialised ; it only teaches a very limited number of subjects . unlike the other universities in france , the grandes écoles can pick the students they admit . these students have to follow special courses and tests before they are admitted . the grandes écoles award degrees that correspond to a master 's degree ; they do not award bachelor 's degrees . there is no exhaustive list , but in general , there are the following types of grandes écoles , along with some of the representative schools in each category : - sciences : école normale supérieures - engineering : école polytechnique , école centrale paris , mines paristech - aviation : école nationale de l'aviation civile , institut polytechnique des sciences avancées - politics and public administration : institut d'études politiques de paris , école nationale d'administration - business : école des hautes études commerciales de paris - military : école navale , école de l'air
