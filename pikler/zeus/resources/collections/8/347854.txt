30 Rock     30 rock is an american television sitcom created by tina fey for nbc . it began on october 11 , 2006 and ended on january 31 , 2013. it was set in a television studio in manhattan , new york city . it starred fey , alec baldwin , tracy morgan , jane krakowski , jack mcbrayer , scott adsit , judah friedlander , katrina bowden , keith powell , john lutz , kevin brown , grizz chapman and maulik pancholy . fey played the protagonist liz lemon , the writer of tgs with tracy jordan . morgan played the star of tgs with tracy jordan . krakowski played jenna maroney , liz 's assistant and tracy 's co-star . mcbrayer plays kenneth parcell , an nbc page .
