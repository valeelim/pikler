Common year starting on Wednesday     this is the calendar for any common year starting on wednesday , january 1 ( dominical letter e ) . the most recent year of this type was 2014 and the next one will be 2025 in the gregorian calendar , or 2015 and 2026 in the julian calendar . the exceptional common year , 1800 was also a common year starting on wednesday in the gregorian calendar ( see bottom tables ) . a common year is a year with 365 days -- in other words , not a leap year , which has 366. previous year ( common ) | next year ( common ) previous year ( leap ) | next year ( leap )
