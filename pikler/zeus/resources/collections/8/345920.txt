Robert Zemeckis     robert lee zemeckis ( born may 14 , 1952 ) is an american movie director , producer and screenwriter . he was born and raised in chicago , illinois . zemeckis directed `` romancing the stone '' , `` back to the future '' , `` who framed roger rabbit '' , `` back to the future part ii '' , `` back to the future part iii '' , `` death becomes her '' , `` forrest gump '' , `` contact '' , `` what lies beneath '' , `` cast away '' , `` the polar express '' , and `` a christmas carol '' .
