Azerbaijani Navy     the azerbaijan navy ( ) is the navy of the azerbaijani armed forces operating in the caspian sea . the origin of the current organization goes back to june 26 , 1918 , when the azerbaijan democratic republic which became independent in 1918 , after the collapse of the russian empire , formed its first navy . after the second independence of azerbaijan in 1991 , after the collapse of the soviet union , the azerbaijani navy was formed out of the former soviet navy in the azerbaijani ssr . the navy has about 5,000 military personnel in sixteen units . these include the caspian sea fleet and a border coast guard . the fleet is made up of 1 petya class frigate , 2 osa-ii-class and 5 stenka class patrol boats , 2 sonya class and 5 yevgenya class minesweepers ( for removing mines ) , 6 landing craft ( ships that can land on the coast ) , 2 landing cutters ( patrol boats that can land on the coast ) , 1 special purposes warship and 1 special purposes cutter . other pages . - military history of azerbaijan
