Cyclone Gonu     super cyclonic storm gonu was an extremely intense and very damaging storm . the cyclone was the most intense recorded tropical cyclone in the arabian sea and the strongest named tropical cyclone recorded in the indian ocean . gonu developed on june 1 2007 over the northwest indian ocean and then intensified to category 5 levels . the storm then weakened and then struck eastern oman on june 6 , contributing to that region 's worst modern natural disaster and causing around $ 4.2 billion in damage . the cyclone also struck iran , causing $ 216 million in damage . almost eighty people died from cyclone gonu .
