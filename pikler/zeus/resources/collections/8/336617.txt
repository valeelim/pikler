Margalla Hills     the margalla hills is a mountain range in the lesser himalayas . they are north of islamabad . the margalla range has an area of . the hills are an 'off shoot ' or linked part of the bigger murree hills . it is a range with many valleys as well as mountains . its highest peak is tilla charouni , in its eastern end , which is 1,604 metres tall . the range has an average elevation of 1000 metres . the range gets some snow in some higher places in winters . the margalla national park which borders islamabad city , is also in the margalla hills .
