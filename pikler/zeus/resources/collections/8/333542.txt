Lypsinka     lypsinka ( born john epperson on 24 april 1955 ) is an american drag queen , actor , pianist , vocalist , and writer . epperson was born in hazelhurst , mississippi . he moved to new york city in 1978. he did drag gigs on the side at various nightspots . `` lypsinka '' first appeared in 1988 in new york as a late-night addition to charles busch 's `` vampire lesbians of sodom '' . lypsinka has appeared in solo shows off-broadway . epperson says lypsinka was modelled on dolores gray . for his show `` lypsinka ! the boxed set '' , epperson won the los angeles drama critics circle award for best sound design , the la weekly theater award for best solo performance , and the helen hayes award for best non-resident production in 2003 .
