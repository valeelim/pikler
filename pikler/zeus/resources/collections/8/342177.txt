Argentine Senate     the argentine senate is the upper house of the argentine national congress . it has 72 senators . there are three for each province and three for buenos aires . there used to be 2 senators from each province . that was changed in 1994. senators are elected by direct election on a provincial basis . the party with the most votes is given two of the province 's senate seats . the second-place party gets the third seat . currently one-third of the members are elected every two years to a six-year term . in other words , one-third of the provinces hold senate elections every two years . according to section 55 of the argentine constitution , candidates for the argentine senate must - be at least 30 years old - have been a citizen of argentina for six years - be native to the province of his office , or have been a resident of that province for two years .
