Sit Down, Shut Up (U.S. TV series)    sit down , shut up ( u.s. tv series ) sit down , shut up is an animated television sitcom that aired from april 19 , 2009 to november 21 , 2009 on fox . it originally aired on the `` animation domination '' block , but was removed and put on saturdays at midnight . it was based on the australian series which had the same name .
