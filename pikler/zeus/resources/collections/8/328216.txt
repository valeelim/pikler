Regina Pats     the regina pats are a canadian professional ice hockey team that began playing in 1917. they currently play in the western hockey league . they play their home games in regina , saskatchewan at the brandt centre . they are the oldest major junior hockey franchise in the world that has continuously operated from their original location and still uses the same name . baseball player nyjer morgan played 7 games with the pats in the 1999-2000 season and registered 2 goals and 20 penalty minutes . other websites . - official website
