Mathieu de Costa     mathieu de costa was the first known african to come to canada , in the early 17th century . he was a storekeeper and translator . he spoke many languages including portuguese , french , dutch , english , pidgin basque , his original african language , and míkmaq . he helped to make a connection between the europeans and the míkmaq people . he was a freeman . europeans liked that he talked many languages . the port royal habitation national historic site of canada remembers his work . they are in annapolis royal , nova scotia .
