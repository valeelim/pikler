New York Public Library     the new york public library ( nypl ) is a public library system in new york city . it is the second largest public library in the united states the library has branches in the boroughs of manhattan , bronx and staten island . the branch libraries are open to the general public . john astor gave $ 400,000 for the creation of a public library . which was built in 1854 the size and importance of the library grew along with the city . other websites . - new york public library website - `` new york times '' , `` british architect to redesign city library , '' october 22 , 2008 ; `` sacking a palace of culture , '' april 21 , 2012
