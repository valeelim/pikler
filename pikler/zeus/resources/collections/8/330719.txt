Billie Jean     `` billie jean '' is a song released from the late pop singer michael jackson 's 1982 album `` thriller '' . the song was released in borderline 1983/1984 . `` billie jean '' was one of many hits by the singer and established itself at number one in the billboard hot 100. at the 1984 grammy awards jackson was awarded best r & b song and best r & b male vocal performance for the song . in 2008 kanye west remixed the song for `` thriller 25 '' .
