Atonement (movie)    atonement ( movie ) atonement is a 2007 british romantic drama war movie . it is set in the 1930s and 1940s . it was directed by joe wright . it is based on 2001 novel atonement by ian mcewan . it stars james mcavoy as robbie turner and keira knightley as cecilia tallis . her younger sister briony tallis is played by saiorse ronan at age 13 , romola garai at age 18 and vanessa redgrave at age 77 .
