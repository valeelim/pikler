Biosphere reserve     a biosphere reserve is an ecosystem with plants and animals of unusual scientific and naturalinterest . it is a label given by unesco to help protect the sites . the plan is to promote management , research and education in ecosystem conservation . this includes the 'sustainable use of natural resources ' . if , for example , fish or trees are taken for human use , this is done in ways which least damage the ecosystem . the program is run by unesco 's man and the biosphere programme . it has started a world network of biosphere reserves . the mab programme built up the world network of biosphere reserves since 1971. biosphere reserves exchange knowledge and experiences on new ideas for sustainable development . they are areas used to develop new ways of doing things , test these ways and share the results . the objective is to get a balanced relationship between mankind and nature .
