Nizari     the nizari are the biggest group of shiite ismailites . they emphasize social justice , pluralism and human reason in the framework of islam . they are the second-largest group of shiites . there are about 15-20 million , spread over many countries ; in pakistan up to 75 % of the people may follow this faith . nizari still have an imam that is descended from ali , the current imam is karim aga khan iv .
