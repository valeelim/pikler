Wide Sargasso Sea     wide sargasso sea is an erotic drama movie of 1993 in which rachel ward , karina lombard and naomi watts star . `` wide sargasso sea '' is rated nc-17 because of sexual content . it is based on the 1966 jean rhys book of the same name . the movie is about love and lust in 1840s jamaica , shortly after britain freed the slaves in its caribbean colonies . the movie received mixed reviews from critics . roger ebert gave the movie three and a half stars .
