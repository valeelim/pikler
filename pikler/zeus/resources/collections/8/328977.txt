Come On Over (Shania Twain album)    come on over ( shania twain album ) come on over is an album released by country music singer shania twain . it was released in 1997. it is the biggest selling album by a female country singer . all of the songs were written by shania twain and mutt lange . singles released off of the album include the title track , `` that do n't impress me much '' , `` man ! i feel like a woman ! `` , `` honey , i 'm home '' , `` you 're still the one '' , `` from this moment on '' , `` you 've got a way '' , `` love gets me every time , '' `` do n't be stupid ! `` , `` when '' , `` rock this country ! `` , and `` i 'm holding on to love ( to save my life ) '' .
