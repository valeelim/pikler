Celastrales     celastrales is an order of flowering plants . they are found throughout the tropics and subtropics , with only a few species extending far into the temperate regions . there are about 1200 to 1350 species in about 100 genera . all but 7 of these genera are in the large family celastraceae . until recently , the composition of the order and its division into families varied greatly from one author to another .
