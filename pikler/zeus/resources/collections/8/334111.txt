Jeff Hornacek     jeffrey john hornacek ( born may 3 , 1963 in elmhurst , illinois ) is a retired american basketball player . hornacek played a total of 16 seasons in the nba with the phoenix suns , philadelphia 76ers and the utah jazz . he played college basketball at iowa state . he retired from basketball in 2000 to spend more time with his family . his jersey number 14 was retired by the utah jazz . other websites . - jeff hornacek 's nba bio
