Keno Province     , also known as kenu , was a very old province of japan before the nara period . keno was in the area of tochigi prefecture and gunma prefecture . kōzuke province and shimotsuke province were known as keno before the reforms of the taihō code in the early 8th century . related pages . - provinces of japan - prefectures of japan - list of regions of japan - list of islands of japan other websites . - murdoch 's map of provinces , 1903
