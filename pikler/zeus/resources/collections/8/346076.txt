Tapaculo     the tapaculos ( pronounced `` ta-pa-coo-lo '' ) are a group of small passeriform birds . they have many species , and are found mostly in south america , especially in the andean regions . three species , the choco , the pale-throated and the silvery-fronted tapaculo , are found in southern central america . tapaculos nest in many places , from ground burrows to tree holes . some make a ball nest in a bush .
