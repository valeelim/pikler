Nightwing     nightwing is a name that has been used by several fictional characters in the dc comics universe . the name was used early on with characters related to superman . superman was the first to use the name . the most well know use of the name for a character was the original robin , dick grayson . the comic book `` nightwing '' , about this version of the character was printed from 1996 until 2009. a new volume of the comic has been published from 2011 and is still being published today .
