Bokbunja ju     bokbunja ju ( hangul : 복분자주 ; hanja : 覆盆子酒 ; also spelled bokbunjaju , bokbunja-ju , or bokbunjajoo , and also called bokbunja wine ) is a korean traditional wine made from fermented korean black raspberries called `` bokbunja '' ( hangul : 복분자 ; hanja : ; `` rubus coreanus '' ) . it is made by fermenting berries with water . `` bokbunja ju '' is crimson in color and sweet . it has between 15 % and 19 % alcohol by volume , depending on the brand . koreans believe that it is good for health and promotes male sexual stamina . it is often drunk on special occasions .
