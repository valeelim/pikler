Brooklyn Botanic Garden     brooklyn botanic garden ( bbg ) is a park in the borough of brooklyn in new york city . the park has many different plants from many parts of the world . it is near the prospect heights , crown heights , and park slope neighborhoods . the area of the garden is . it includes several special gardens or plant collections . the steinhardt conservatory which has the c.v. starr bonsai museum is also there . the garden was founded in 1910 .
