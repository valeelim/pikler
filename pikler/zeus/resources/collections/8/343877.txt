Hercule Poirot     hercule poirot is a fictional detective . he was created by agatha christie . he was in 33 novels and 51 short stories . the stories were published between 1920 and 1975. poirot has been portrayed on radio , on screen , for films and television . some of the actors who played him are john moffatt , albert finney , sir peter ustinov , sir ian holm , tony randall , alfred molina and david suchet . other websites . - hercule poirot at the official agatha christie website
