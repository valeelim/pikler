Los Temerarios     los temerarios are a mexican romantic music group . they were started in 1978 by brothers adolfo angel and gustavo angel and their cousin fernando angel . during their early years they were known as `` conjunto la brisa '' . `` los temerarios '' have recorded more than 20 albums and been honored with multiple awards and award nominations . in 1991 , they earned 5 billboard awards nominations in the latin categories . discography . - 1981 - as conjunto las brisas , they recorded two 45 format discs . - 1983 - after changing their name to los temerarios , they release their first lp , `` los 14 grandes exitos de los temerarios '' . * 1984 - `` los temerarios en la altura '' - 1985 - `` cumbias y nortenas '' - 1986 - `` pero no '' - 1988 - `` los temerarios '' - 1989 - `` incontenible '' - 1990 - `` internacionales y romanticos '' , `` revelacion romantica de los noventas '' . - 1992 - `` seduccion romantica de los noventas '' , `` mi vida eres tu '' - 1994 - `` tu ultima cancion '' , `` los temerarios en concierto volumen uno '' - 1995 - `` camino del amor '' , `` nuestras canciones '' , '' te quiero ''
