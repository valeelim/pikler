Armenian rock     armenian rock is a type of rock music sung by armenian people . in the soviet era , rock music was forbidden because the cpsu believed it was a `` western anti-socialist '' influence . however , in the 1980s popular rock bands in armenia began to emerge . the armenians blended folk music with their rock music . after bolshevism , progressive rock music began to be popular among armenian people . since 2004 , the armenian music awards honors musicians who record rock music .
