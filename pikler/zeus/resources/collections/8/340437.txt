Fraser Island     fraser island is the unesco world heritage site of the east coast of queensland in australia . fraser island is long . it is the largest sand island in the world . it has large shifting sand dunes , and highly coloured sand cliffs . the world heritage site covers . the island has a range of plants including patches of rainforest . there are also perched freshwater lakes . these are lakes that are not connected to the groundwater , but sit above them . half of the perched lakes in the world are found on fraser island . the water in the lakes is very clear . related pages . - list of world heritage sites in australia
