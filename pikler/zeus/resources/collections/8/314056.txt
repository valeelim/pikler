Lana Turner     lana turner ( february 8 , 1921 - june 29 , 1995 ) was an american actress . she was born in wallace , idaho . she was first discovered and signed into movie contract by mgm when she was 16. she played mainly feature roles and was one of hollywood 's first scream queens . she would become very popular in the 1940s and 1950s . turner appeared in the television series `` falcon crest '' during 1982 and 1983. the actress died from throat cancer in 1995 in century city , los angeles .
