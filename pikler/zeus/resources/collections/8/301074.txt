Las Reinas Del Pueblo     las reinas del pueblo ( english : `` the queens of the people '' ) is a compilation album by mexican-american tejano pop singer , selena and tejano singer graciela beltrán . the album was released several weeks after selena 's murder . the album impacted several music charts on `` billboard '' . track listing . 1. como la flor - ( with selena ) 1. baraja de oro - ( with graciela beltrán ) 1. no debes jugar - ( with selena ) 1. pilares de cristal - ( with graciela beltrán ) 1. bidi bidi bom bom - ( with selena ) 1. tu me dijiste adios - ( with graciela beltrán ) 1. la llamada - ( with selena ) 1. mi triunfo - ( with graciela beltrán ) 1 . ¿qué creías ? - ( with selena ) 1. tu recado - ( with graciela beltrán ) 1. la carcacha - ( with selena ) 1. morena y delgadita - ( with graciela beltrán ) album
