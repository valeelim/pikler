Home appliance     a home appliance is any electrical machine use to complete some household task , such as cooking or cleaning . home appliances can be classified into : - major appliances , or white goods - small appliances , or brown goods this division is also detectable in the service area of these kinds of products . most brown goods require high technical knowledge and skills , while white goods need more practical skills and `` brute force '' to make the devices work , and heavy tools required to repair them .
