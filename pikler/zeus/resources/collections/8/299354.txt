Skatepark     a skatepark is a recreational park for skateboarders and bmxers . a skatepark usually features some quarter pipes , some spines , some flat banks , some wall rides or vert walls , some mini ramps , some hips , some funboxes , some pyramids , some launches , some roll-ins , a euro or euro gap , a halfpipe , a bowl , a pool , foam pits , some hand rails , a flat bar and some stairs . the very first skatepark was made in 1976 in carlsbad , california . the largest skatepark in the world is located in shanghai , china . other websites . - a list of skateboard parks around the world
