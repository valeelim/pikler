Greatest Hits: My Prerogative    greatest hits : my prerogative greatest hits : my prerogative is the first greatest hits album by britney spears . it was released on november 1 , 2004. the album has all of her hit songs to that point . it also includes a cover of bobby brown 's `` my prerogative '' and two songs from the `` in the zone `` recording sessions which did not make it on to that album . singles released in this album include `` my prerogative '' and , outside of the united states , `` do somethin ' '' .
