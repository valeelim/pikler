Ohio Valley Wrestling     ohio valley wrestling ( ovw ) is an american developmental professional wrestling promotion based in louisville , kentucky . ovw was initially a member promotion of the national wrestling alliance from its inception in 1997 until 2000 ; when it became the primary developmental territory for world wrestling entertainment . ovw remained in this role from 2000 until 2008. the promotion was unaffiliated from february 7 , 2008 until november 2011 , when it became the official and current developmental territory for total nonstop action wrestling . as of december 2011 , over 100 ovw alumni have appeared in wwe . ovw is now the developmental territory for total nonstop action , run by owner danny davis . other websites . - official website
