Port Augusta, South Australia    port augusta , south australia port augusta is a city in south australia at the top of spencer gulf , 322 km north of adelaide . the first people to live in the area where the indigenous banggarla or nuguna people . matthew flinders explored the gulf in 1802. edward eyre explored the land in 1839 before going north to lake eyre . the harbour was set up in 1852 by alexander elder and john grainger . they named it after lady augusta sophia young , the wife of the governor of south australia . because of its location , the city quickly became an important port , servicing much of the inland of australia . in 2006 there were 13,257 people living in port augusta .
