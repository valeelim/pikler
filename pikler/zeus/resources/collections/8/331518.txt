The Firm (2012 TV series)    the firm ( 2012 tv series ) the firm is a canadian-american drama that first aired on january 8 , 2012 on nbc and global . on february 3 , 2012 , nbc and global announced that the series would be pulled from its thursday 10/9c slot immediately , and placed on saturdays at 9/8c starting on february 18 .
