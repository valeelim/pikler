Park City, Utah    park city , utah park city is a city in utah in the united states . it was settled by mormons in the 1840s and 1850s . there was a lot of mining there in the 1860s . today , it is a ski resort . it has a population of 7,731 and is at an elevation of 7,000 feet . it is where the sundance film festival takes place . some of the events of the 2002 winter olympics were held in or near park city .
