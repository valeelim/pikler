Kyushu National Museum     the is one of the major museums in japan . it is in dazaifu in fukuoka prefecture . the museum acquires , conserves , researches , communicates and exhibits a collection of art works and archaeological objects of japan and east asia . in the collection , there is an emphasis on the formation of japanese culture through exchanges with other asian countries and kyushu . kyushu national museum opened in 2005. the building was designed by kiyonori kikutake . the museum was the first new national museum in japan in over 100 years . in december 2008 , the museum was the venue for a trilateral diplomatic meeting between representatives of japan , china and the republic of korea . related pages . - kyoto national museum - nara national museum - national art center , tokyo ( nact ) - national museum of art , osaka ( nmao ) - national museum of modern art , kyoto ( momak ) - national museum of modern art , tokyo ( momat ) - national museum of western art ( nmwa ) - tokyo national museum - list of independent administrative institutes in japan
