Manji     manji or mangi may mean : - japanese name for the 卍 character ( chinese : wàn ) , see swastika - manji ( era ) , a japanese era name from 1658 to 1661 - type of raised bed similar to a cot in india or pakistan - in popular culture - in the legend of zelda for the nes , level 3 from quest 1 is designed and named after the manji symbol . - in the tekken series of video games the manji party is led by the ninja yoshimitsu . - the soul calibur game series also features a manji organization . - one of the npcs in the role-playing game maplestory is named manji .
