Sumida River     the is a japanese river which flows through tokyo . it crosses the north part of tokyo and drains into tokyo bay . the sumida runs through tokyo for 27 kilometers . there are 26 bridges across the river , including - ryōgoku bridge ( `` ryōgoku-bashi '' ) - nihon bridge ( `` nihon-bashi '' ) - eitai bridge - senju bridge - sakura bridge - kototoi bridge - azuma bridge - komagata bridge - umaya bridge - shin ohashi bridge - kiyosu bridge - chuo bridge - tsukuda bridge - kachidoki bridge ( `` kachidoki-bashi '' ) other websites . - new york public library digital gallery `` pleasure boating on the sumida river '' , woodblock print , c. 1788-1790
