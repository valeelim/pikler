Japan studies     japanese studies ( or japanology ) is a term generally used to describe the historical and cultural study of japan . in north america , the academic field is usually referred to as japanese studies . the term `` japanology '' is more often used in europe . japanology is the study of many subjects , including - japanese language - japanese culture - japanese history - japanese literature - japanese art - music of japan - manga - anime - science and technology in japan the roots of japanology begin with the dutch at dejima , nagasaki in the edo period . japanology was encouraged by the asiatic society of japan at yokohama which was established in 1872. notable japanologists . - edwin o. reischauer - edward seidensticker - ruth benedict - donald keene - francis xavier - ernest mason satow notable foreign centers of japanese studies . - university of virginia japanese text initiative - harvard university - reischauer institute of japanese studies - university of california , berkeley - center for japanese studies
