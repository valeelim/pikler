Rachel McAdams     rachel anne mcadams ( born november 17 , 1978 ) is a canadian actress . she was born in london , ontario and grew up in st. thomas , ontario . she had major roles in `` the hot chick '' , `` mean girls '' , `` the notebook '' , `` wedding crashers '' , `` red eye '' , `` the family stone '' , `` state of play '' , `` the time traveler 's wife '' , `` sherlock holmes '' , `` morning glory '' , `` midnight in paris '' , `` the vow '' and `` about time '' . she was nominated for the orange rising star award in 2006 .
