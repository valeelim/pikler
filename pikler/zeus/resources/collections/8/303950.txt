Lebanon at the Olympics     lebanon at the olympicsis a history which began in 1948. the international olympic committee 's official abbreviation for lebanon is leb . a team from lebanon was first at the olympic games in 1948. they have been in all but one summer olympic games since then . lebanon was one of three nations to boycott the 1956 games in protest of the british and french involvement in the suez crisis . lebanon has also been in most winter olympic games since 1948 , missing only the 1994 and 1998 winter games . lebanon was at the 2010 youth olympic games in singapore . the national olympic committee for lebanon was formed in 1947. it was recognized by the international olympic committee in 1948. related pages . - list of ioc country codes
