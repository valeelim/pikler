Masala (movie genre)    masala ( movie genre ) masala ( ; ) is a word used in south east asia , mainly india , pakistan and bangladesh . it is used for a style of indian movies where there is a mix of various styles of movies in one movie . for example , a movie can show action , comedy , drama , and romance . this movie style is used very often in south indian films as it helps make the movies appeal to more viewers . famous masala directors include s. shankar , perarasu and k. s. ravikumar .
