Jean-François Jacques     jean-françois `` j. f. '' jacques ( born april 29 , 1985 ) is a canadian professional ice hockey left winger . he plays for the ec kac of the austrian hockey league ( ebel ) . he has also played in the national hockey league ( nhl ) with the edmonton oilers and the anaheim ducks . he was drafted by the edmonton oilers with the 68th pick in the 2003 nhl entry draft . he has played in the nhl for the edmonton oilers for 5 seasons . in the 2011-2012 season he played for the anaheim ducks . in july 2012 , he was signed to the florida panthers .
