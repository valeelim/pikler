Masolino     masolino da panicale ( common name `` tommaso di cristoforo fini '' , born around 1383 in panicale , died 1447 in florence ) was an italian painter . he is commonly known as masolino . among his best-known works are frescoes in the brancacci chapel , and a madonna called madonna and child with st. anne . he painted the madonna together with another artist , commonly known as masaccio . other pages . - italian renaissance art
