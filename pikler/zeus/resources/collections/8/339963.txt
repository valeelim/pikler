American Bandstand     american bandstand is an american music and amateur dance show . it was televised in various versions from 1952 to 1989. it was hosted from 1956 until its final season by dick clark . he was also its producer . the show `` starred '' teenagers off the street dancing to top 40 hits introduced by clark . at least one popular musical act would usually appear in person to lip sync one of their latest hits . dick clark became an american media mogul as a result of the show . it also inspired similar music programs , such as `` soul train '' and `` top of the pops '' .
