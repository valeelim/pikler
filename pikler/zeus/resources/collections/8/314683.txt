Estée Lauder     estée lauder , ( july 1 , 1908 - april 24 , 2004 ) , was an american businesswoman . she was the co-founder of the cosmetics company named after her , estée lauder . in 1998 , she was be the only woman on time magazine 's list of the 20 most influential business geniuses of the 20th century . lauder died on april 24 , 2004 at age 95 .
