Pecados y Milagros     pecados y milagros ( ) is the seventh album by mexican latin singer lila downs . it was released in 2011. it reached the top of the charts in mexico , germany , argentina , colombia , spain and the united states . it also reached the top ten in other major markets . downs did a tour for the album called the `` pecados y milagros world tour '' . the singles on `` pecados y milagros '' are : `` palomo del comalito '' , `` zapata se queda '' and `` mezcalito '' . in 2012 the album `` pecados y milagros '' won a grammy award for `` best regional mexican music album ( including tejano ) '' . the same year it won a latin grammy award for `` best folk album '' . the main music genres which make up the album are pop , folk and ska .
