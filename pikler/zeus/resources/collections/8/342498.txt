Armavir, Armenia    armavir , armenia armavir is a city in western armenia . in 2008 there were 26,387 people . it is the capital of the armavir province . armavir has had people from the 5th-6th millennium b.c . armavir was an old capital of armenia . it is said to have been founded by king aramais in 1980 bc king argishti i of urartu built a fortress in the area . he named it argishtikhinili . during antiquity , armavir was taken by the seleucids , parthians , roman empire , sassanids and byzantine empire before it was taken over by the arabs in 645 .
