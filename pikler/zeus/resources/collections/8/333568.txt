Alveolar approximant     the alveolar approximant is a consonant . we use it in some spoken languages . international phonetic alphabet represents the alveolar and postalveolar approximants as ⟨⟩ . international phonetic alphabet represents it as a lowercase letter `` r '' rotated 180 degrees , or in broad transcription ⟨⟩ ; the x-sampa symbol of this is ⟨r\⟩ . many times the symbol is written as ⟨⟩ instead of ⟨⟩ because typing ⟨⟩ is easier .
