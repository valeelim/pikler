Carmen Amaya     carmen amaya ( 2 november 1913 – 19 november 1963 ) was a flamenco dancer and singer , of gypsy origin , born in the somorrostro slum of barcelona , spain . she has been called `` the greatest spanish gypsy dancer of her generation '' . and `` the most extrardinary personality of all time in flamenco dance '' . she danced from the time she was 4 years old . accompanied on the guitar by her father , she danced in waterfront bars in barcelona . a young person who saw her dance as a girl was the spanish guitarist sabicas ( agustín castellón campos ) . he later said `` i saw her dance and it seemed like something supernatural to me ... i never saw anyone dance like her . i don ’ t know how she did it , i just don ’ t know ! '' . sabicas accompanied her for many years . he recorded `` queen of the gypsies '' ( 1959 ) and `` flamenco ! '' with amaya . in 1929 , she made her debut in paris , to warm acclaims and admiration of her dancing skill . she moved to america in 1936 , where she went on to act in several movies that broke box office records , including the `` romeo and juliet '' adaptation `` los tarantos '' , and the short movie `` danzas gitanas '' ( gypsy dances ) .
