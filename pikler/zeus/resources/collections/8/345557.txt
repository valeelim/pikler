Rajshahi     the city of rajshahi is the headquarters of rajshahi division and rajshahi district . it is one of the six metropolitan cities of bangladesh . it is called `` silk city '' and `` education city '' . there are around 1,000,000 people . it is on the northern banks of the river padma . rajshahi used to be known as rampur boalia . notable people . - akshay kumar maitreya - andrew kishore - hasan azizul huq - ila mitra - ritwik ghatak - sir jadunath sarkar - mohammad shamsuzzoha
