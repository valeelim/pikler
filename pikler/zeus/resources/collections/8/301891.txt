Pakistan national field hockey team     the pakistan national field hockey team also known as the green shirts represent the pakistan hockey federation ( phf ) in international field hockey competitions . field hockey is the national sport of pakistan . pakistan has been one of the most successful teams in international competitions , having won a world record four hockey world cup titles . provincial teams of pakistan . - baloch lions - capital dynamos ( islamabad ) - frontier falcons - northern cavaliers ( gilgit baltistan ) - shan-e-punjab - sindh qalanders
