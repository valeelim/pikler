Nepal at the Olympics     nepal at the olympics is a history which began in 1963. the international olympic committee 's official abbreviation for nepal is nep . nepal has been in eleven summer olympic games , and in three winter olympic games . the country has never won an official olympic medal . bidhan lama won a bronze medal at the 1988 summer olympics in seoul , when taekwondo was an exhibition sport . the nepal olympic committee was formed in 1962 and recognized in 1963. related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - om/olympics/countries/nep/ sports reference - home page
