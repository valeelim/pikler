Sydney Pollack     sydney irwin pollack ( july 1 , 1934 - may 26 , 2008 ) was an american movie and television director , producer and actor . pollack was born in lafayette , indiana . during his childhood he moved to south bend , indiana . he moved to new york city when he was 17. he won an emmy award in 1966. he won two academy awards and a golden globe award for 1985 movie `` out of africa '' . he died of cancer of unknown primary origin in pacific palisades , los angeles , california .
