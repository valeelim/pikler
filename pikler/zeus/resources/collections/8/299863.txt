Showgirls     showgirls is a 1995 american movie by paul verhoeven . it stars kyle maclachlan , elizabeth berkley and gina gershon . it is set in las vegas , nevada . the movie became the first — and so far , only — nc-17 rated movie to be given a wide release in the mainstream theaters . the movie got substandard reviews and flopped , but enjoyed success on the home video market , receiving over $ 100 million from movie rental . this movie has been consistently ranked one of the worst movies ever made , but has become regarded as a cult classic .
