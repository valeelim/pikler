Peter Leko     peter leko ( ; born 8 september 1979 in subotica , yugoslavia ) is the leading hungarian chess grandmaster . he became a grandmaster in 1994 at the age of 14 years ( a world record at the time ) . he was the challenger in the classical world championship 2004 against vladimir kramnik . he tied with kramnik 7-7 , but this meant kramnik retained his title . leko 's best world ranking was fourth , first achieved in april 2003 .
