Akinchi     akinchi , also written as ekinchi ( / اکينچي ) was an azerbaijan newspaper . it was made in baku it was the first newspaper fully printed in the azerbaijani language . it was also the first newspaper in russia printed in a turkic language . the name means `` the ploughman '' . hasan bey zardabi started the newspaper . he was a journalist , teacher and scientist . `` akinchi '' was thought to be almost revolutionary because of the language it was made in and that so many people could read it . zardabi used a simpler form of the azeri language so more people could read the paper . the russian governor of the caucasus dmitry staroselsky liked zardabi 's plans . staroselsky supported him in starting `` akinchi '' . he was also the one to name the newspaper . he thought the name would make the authorities think that `` akinchi '' was non-political and about agriculture . in addition to agriculture articles zardabi wrote about medicine and biology . there were also stories about the social and cultural state of muslims in the caucasus . the first issue of `` akinchi '' was printed on 22 july 1875 .
