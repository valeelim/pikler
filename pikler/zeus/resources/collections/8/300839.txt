El Salvador at the Olympics     el salvador has been in nine summer olympic games . the international olympic committee 's official abbreviation for el salvador is esa . in 1964 , it was sal for `` salvador '' . there have been no teams from el salvador at the olympic winter games . as of 2010 , el salvador does not have a medal . the `` comité olímpico de el salvador '' was formed in 1949 and recognized by the ioc in 1962. related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - el salvador profile at london2012.com - el salvador at sportsreference.com
