Jeremy Irons     jeremy john irons ( born 19 september 1948 ) is an english theater and movie actor . the movies in which he has starred include `` die hard with a vengeance '' ( 1995 ) . irons trained as an actor at the bristol old vic theatre school . he played john the baptist and judas in `` godspell '' for a total of 1,128 performances . in 2005 , irons won both an emmy award and a golden globe award for his supporting role in the tv mini-series , `` elizabeth i '' .
