Mash-up     a mash-up is when a musician performs two or more songs as a single composition . some mashups are called `` remixes '' or sometimes indirectly called `` duets '' if sung by two musicians . in popular music , some musicians sing two cover versions and blend them together ( transformation ) into a new song . further reading . - paul morley ( 2003 ) . `` words and music : a history of pop in the shape of a city '' . bloomsbury . isbn 0-7475-5778-0 . - jeremy j. beadle ( 1993 ) . `` will pop eat itself ?
