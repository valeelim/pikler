Matisyahu     matthew paul miller ( born june 30 , 1979 ) , better known by his hebrew name and stage name matisyahu , is a jewish , hasidic , rapper . his more famous songs are `` '' king without a crown '' , `` jerusalem '' , and `` one day '' '' . he has collaborated with akon , the crystal method , and infected mushroom . he has 3 studio albums , as well as 2 live albums , 2 remix albums , and 2 dvds with live concerts .
