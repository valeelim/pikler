Aghjabadi Rayon     aghjabadi is a raion in central azerbaijan . the capital is aghjabadi . aghjabadi raion was started in 1930. in 1963 it was ended and connected to agdam raion . in 1965 , it became separate again . there are 46 settlements and 1 town in the district . there were 122,649 people in 2010. notable people . - uzeyir hajibeyov – composer - afrasiyab badalbeyli – composer and conductor - shamsi badalbeyli – composer - tofik bakhramov – football referee - zulfu adigozalov – khananda - vasif adigozalov – composer - isi malikzadeh – writer - sabir novruzov – mugam singer
