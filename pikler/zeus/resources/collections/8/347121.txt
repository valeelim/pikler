Mary Cheney     mary claire cheney ( born march 14 , 1969 ) is an american businesswoman and the younger daughter of former u.s. vice president , dick cheney and his wife , former second lady , lynne cheney . she is openly lesbian and has stated support for same-sex marriage along with her father . she is in a long relationship with heather poe , a former parking ranger with whom she has had 2 children , samuel david and sarah lynne . on june 22 , 2012 , cheney married her long time partner heather poe . cheney has a sister , elizabeth cheney ( born 1966 ) .
