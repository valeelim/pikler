Goygol Rayon     goygol is a raion ( district ) in northwestern azerbaijan . it used to be named khanlar . it was named `` goygol '' after lake goygol on april 25 , 2008. the capital is the city of goygol . there are 54,000 people . the city of goygol is the largest town , followed by hajimelik . important villages are : balchili , gushgara , chayli , mollajalilli , and topalhasanli .
