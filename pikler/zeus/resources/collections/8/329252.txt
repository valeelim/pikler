The Phantom of the Opera (1925 movie)    the phantom of the opera ( 1925 movie ) the phantom of the opera ( 1925 ) is a silent horror movie . it is based on a novel of the same name by gaston leroux . the movie was directed by rupert julian . it stars lon chaney as the phantom and mary philbin as his love , christine . the movie is famous for chaney 's horrific , self-applied make-up . the story is about a disfigured man named erik who haunts the paris opera . he wants the managers to make the woman he loves , named christine , a star . in order to do this , he terrorizes everyone by doing things such as dropping a chandelier and wearing a scary disguise in the middle of a large party . when he learns that christine is in love with a man named raoul , he kidnaps her . raoul eventually rescues her , and a group of angry people chase the phantom until he falls into the seine river . cast . - lon chaney as erik , the phantom of the opera - mary philbin as christine - norman kerry as raoul - arthur edmund carewe as ledoux the movie was a great success . the critics liked it . executives at universal pictures were pleased . they put a string of horror movies into production . these movies included bela lugosi 's `` dracula '' and boris karloff 's `` frankenstein '' . `` phantom '' was chosen for preservation in the united states national film registry .
