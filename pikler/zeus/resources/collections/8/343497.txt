Alim Qasimov     alim qasimov ( born 1957 ) is an azerbaijani musician . he is one of the foremost mugam singers in azerbaijan . he was awarded the international music council-unesco music prize in 1999. it is one of the highest international honors for music . his music is characterised by his vocal improvisation . it is a move away from the traditional style of mugham . qasimov has recorded nine albums , three of which are mugham albums with his daughter , ferghana qasimova . in 21 november 2009 , he was included in a `` 500 most influential muslims of world '' book . the icelandic singer bj√∂rk complimented qasimov , by naming him as her `` favourite singer alive today '' . in 2010 , alim qasimov was nominated for the united states national public radio 's `` 50 great voices in recorded history '' award . discography . albums . - `` classical mugham '' ( 3 january 1996 ) - `` the art of mugham : azerbaidjan '' ( live ) ( 1 december 1997 ) - `` the mugham of azerbaidjan '' ( 1 october 1999 ) - `` love 's deep ocean '' ( 11 october 1999 ) with ferghana qasimova
