Batna Province     batna is a province of algeria . the capital is batna . other important towns are barika , merouana and timgad . belezma national park is there . batna province has 22 districts . there are also 61 communes . the districts are : 1. aïn djasser 1. aïn touta 1. arris 1. barika 1. batna 1. bouzina 1. chemora 1. djezzar 1. el madher 1. ichmoul 1. menaâ 1. merouana 1. n'gaous 1. ouled salem 1. ouled si slimane 1. ras el aioun 1. seggana 1. seriana 1. t'kout 1. tazoult 1. théniet el abed 1. timgad
