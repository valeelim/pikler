Souq     a souq or souk ( also spelled soq , souk , esouk , suk , sooq , souq , or suq ) is an open-air marketplace or commercial quarter in an arab , or berber city . a souk or souq is a free-market where vendors can say the going market price for their products . the term is often used for the market in any arabized or muslim city . in modern times , it appears in western cities , too . it may also be the weekly market in some smaller towns where no tribal conflicts would be allowed .
