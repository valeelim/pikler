Dinosaur (movie)    dinosaur ( movie ) dinosaur is a 2000 prehistoric fantasy-adventure movie that was produced by pam marsden and was directed by ralph zondag and eric leighton . released by walt disney pictures and walt disney feature animation dinosaur was released on may 19 , 2000 in north america . the movie received positive reviews with a 65 % rating on rotten tomatoes and a 56 out of 100 from metacritic . there was also a video game of the same name released in 2000. voice cast . - d. b. sweeney as aladar - alfre woodard as plio - ossie davis as yar - max casella as zini - hayden panettiere as suri - samuel e. wright as kron - peter siragusa as bruton - julianna margulies as neera - joan plowright as baylene - della reese as eema other websites . - `` dinosaur '' at the internet movie database
