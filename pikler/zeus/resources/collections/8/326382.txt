T. J. Yates     tj . yates ( b. may 28 , 1987 is an american football quarterback . he plays for the houston texans of the national football league . he played college football for the north carolinia tarheels . yates was picked by houston in the 5th round of the 2011 nfl draft . yates became a starter after matt schaub suffered a season-ending injury . he threw three touchdowns next to three interceptions in the regular season , but guided the texans to their first ever playoff appearance . yates played well in a wild card win over the cincinnati bengals , but threw three interceptions in a divisional loss to the baltimore ravens .
