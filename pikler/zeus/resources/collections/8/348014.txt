The First Wives Club     the first wives club is a 1996 american comedy movie based on the novel of the same name by olivia goldsmith . it was directed by hugh wilson and produced by scott rudin . it was written by robert harling and paul rudnick . it is narrated by diane keaton and stars her , goldie hawn and bette midler . the movie is about three middle-aged divorced women who take revenge on their ex-husbands , who left them for young women .
