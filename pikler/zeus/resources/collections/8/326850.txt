Imitation of Life (1959 movie)    imitation of life ( 1959 movie ) imitation of life is a 1959 american romantic drama movie . it stars lana turner , john gavin , juanita moore , susan kohner , and sandra dee . douglas sirk directed . the movie is based on a book by fannie hurst . it was produced by ross hunter and released by universal pictures . the movie tells the story of two women and their daughters over the years .
