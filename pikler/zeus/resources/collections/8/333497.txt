List of counties in Connecticut     this is a list of counties in connecticut . there are eight counties in the u.s. state of connecticut . four of them were formed in 1666 , when the colony of connecticut was made from a number of smaller colonies . two counties were made during colonial times , and two counties , middlesex and tolland , were formed after american independence ( both in 1785 ) . six of the counties are named for locations in england , where many early connecticut settlers were from . although connecticut is in counties , there is no county government . local government is done by cities and towns . former counties . - trumbull county , ohio - the connecticut western reserve , given to ohio in 1800 . - westmoreland county , connecticut - an area around wilkes-barre , given to pennsylvania in 1784 .
