Austrian Regional League Central     the regionalliga mitteis one of three 3rd level leagues in austrian football . it is formed by teams from upper austria , styria , carinthia and east tyrol . it is the highest level of amateur football in austria . the champion plays a relegation match versus the champion of the regionalliga west . in the 2010/11 season the champion was the lask juniors team . in austria second teams of professional clubs are not allowed to promot to professional football . in the relegation match versus wsg wattens blau weiß linz , the second of the season won and was promoted to austrian football first league . teams of the 2011/12 season . - `` grazer ak '' from styria - `` sak klagenfurt '' from carinthia - `` sv allerheiligen '' from styria - `` villacher sv '' from carinthia - `` austria klagenfurt '' from carinthia - `` fc gratkorn '' from styria-relegated from first league - `` union st. florian '' from upper austria - `` dsv leoben '' from styria - `` kapfenberger sv '' amateure from styria - `` sv gleinstätten '' from styria
