Gastroesophageal reflux disease     gastroesophageal reflux disease ( gerd ) is a chronic medical problem involving the part of the body which runs from the mouth to the stomach . the problem exists when stomach contents move backwards towards the mouth . food or liquid moves from the stomach into the tube between the mouth and the stomach . that tube is called the esophagus . the burning sensation is commonly called `` heartburn , '' but it has nothing to do with the heart . other websites . - medicinenet , gerd - national digestive diseases information clearinghouse ( nddic ) , gerd - medlineplus , gerd
