Hasan Mushaima     hasan mushaima is an opposition leader in bahrain . he is the secretary-general of the haq movement . he wants more democratic rights in bahrain . he was a starting member of al wefaq . he was in the 1994 uprising in bahrain . the bahraini government arrested mushaima many times . in 2010 mushaima traveled to great britain to be treated for lung cancer . mushaima said he was going to return to bahrain during the protests in february 2011. he was kept in lebanon . mushaima finally did return to bahrain on saturday , february 26 , 2011. on 22 june 2011 , mushaima was given life imprisonment by a military court for `` attempting to overthrow the monarchy '' . references . - front line : ongoing harassment of bahraini human rights defenders - clashes erupt after two opposition figures are held in bahrain
