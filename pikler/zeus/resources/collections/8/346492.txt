Tropical Storm Beryl     tropical storm beryl was a strong atlantic tropical storm . it was the second tropical cyclone of the 2012 atlantic hurricane season . beryl formed on may 26 from a low-pressure system . this system was near the east coast of the united states . it was described as a subtropical storm . however , it slowly became stronger and was upgraded to a tropical storm on may 27. it upgraded because it moved to warmer water . beryl is responsible for floods in cuba . this caused a mudslide that left two people missing . beryl caused rough surf to beaches in florida , georgia and south carolina . in south carolina , one person was reported missing . other websites . - tropical storm beryl advisory archive
