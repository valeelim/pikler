Andreas Heraf     andreas heraf ( born 10 september 1967 in vienna ) is an austrian football manager and former football midfielder . he started his career with sk rapid wien but played later for different clubs in austria ( vienna , austria salzburg , vorwärts stteyr , fc kärnten ) and germany ( hannover 96 ) . he was member of rapids team playing in the 1996 uefa cup winners cup final against paris st germain in brussels , which rapid lost . his first international match was in april 1996 a friendly match against hungary . he was member of the 1998 fifa world cup team but did not play . his last international was an october 1998 world cup qualification match against san marino . from 2001 till 2008 he was manager of different teams in germany and austria . since 2008 he is coach of austrias u20 national team , which qualified for the fifa world cup 2011 in columbia . honours . as player . - austrian football bundesliga ( 2 ) : - 1987 , 1988 , 1996 - austrian cup ( 3 ) : - 1987 , 1995 , 2001 as manager . - qualification for the fifa u20 world cup 2011
