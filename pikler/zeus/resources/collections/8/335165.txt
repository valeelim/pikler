Freeway (movie)    freeway ( movie ) freeway is a 1996 crime drama movie . it stars reese witherspoon as vanessa , brooke shields as mimi and brittany murphy as rhonda . this melodramatic thriller was released into theaters august 23 1996 and received very positive reviews from critics . `` freeway '' was originally rated nc-17 for graphic language . it was trimmed to obtain an r before release .
