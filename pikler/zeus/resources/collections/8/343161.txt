Cannibalism in animals     in zoology , cannibalism is where an animal eats its own species . it has been recorded for more than 1500 species it is quite common with insects , spiders and scorpions . there , the male gets eaten by the female , after having sexual intercourse . further reading . - m. a. elgar and bernard j. crespi ( eds. ) . 1992 . `` cannibalism : ecology and evolution of cannibalism among diverse taxa '' oxford university press , new york .
