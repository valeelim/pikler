Tomislav Nikolić     tomislav nikolić ( born 15 february 1952 ) is a serbian politician , a founder and former leader of serbian progressive party . he served as president of serbia from 2012 to 2017. in may 2007 , he served briefly as the president of the national assembly of serbia . in 2008 , he founded the serbian progressive party ( српска напредна странка , `` sns '' ) , the biggest political party in serbia .
