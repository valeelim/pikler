Chūō, Tokyo    chūō , tokyo the municipality calls itself `` chūō city '' in english . chūō is in central tokyo . it borders tokyo bay on the south . minato is on the ward 's southwestern border . chiyoda is on the northwestern border . sumida and kōtō are on the east . the most famous districts in chūō are ginza and nihonbashi . other districtsare kabutochō and tsukiji . in area , chūō is the second-smallest ward in tokyo . only taitō is smaller . related pages . - list of special wards of tokyo - capital of japan other websites . - chuo website , - chuo city tourism association , website , - tokyo metropolitan government , special wards
