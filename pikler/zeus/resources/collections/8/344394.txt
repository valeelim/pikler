Khojali Rayon     khojali ( also called khojaly , khodjaly and hojaly ) is a raion in azerbaijan . it is under the control of armed forces of the nagorno-karabakh republic . its capital is khojali . the region was once a site of prehistoric khojaly-gadabay culture . it was the place of the khojaly massacre in february 1992. other websites . - tragedy massacre in khojaly - massacre by armenians being reported
