Huambo     huambo , formerly `` nova lisboa '' ( `` new lisbon '' in portuguese ) , is the capital of huambo province in angola . the city is about 220 km e from benguela and 600 km se from luanda . the city is the second largest in angola , after the capital city , luanda . it has the albano machado airport . the name huambo comes from wambu . wambu was one of the 14 old ovimbundu kingdoms of the central angolan plateau .
