Electronic Delay Storage Automatic Calculator     electronic delay storage automatic calculator ( edsac ) was an early british computer . it was made by maurice wilkes and his team at the university of cambridge mathematical laboratory in england . edsac was the first stored-program electronic computer . it was also the first to run a computer game . later the project was supported by j. lyons & co. ltd. , a british company . they were rewarded with the first commercial computer , leo i. leo i was based on the edsac design . edsac ran its first programs on 6 may 1949 , when it calculated a table of square numbers and a list of prime numbers .
