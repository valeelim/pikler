Jason and the Argonauts (1963 movie)    jason and the argonauts ( 1963 movie ) jason and the argonauts is a 1963 movie . it stars todd armstrong as jason . the movie is about the quest for the golden fleece . it was directed by don chaffey . the special effects were the creation of stop-motion animation expert ray harryhausen . the movie is famous for its monsters and the battle with the skeletons . `` hercules '' is another movie on the golden fleece theme that was made about the same time . it stars legendary bodybuilder steve reeves .
