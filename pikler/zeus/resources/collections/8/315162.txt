Circus (album)    circus ( album ) circus is the 6th album by britney spears . it consists of electropop and dance pop . this hit record was released on november 28 , 2008. the album debuted on `` billboard '' 200 near the top ; it sold half a million copies during its 1st week and received positive review from critics . the album 's lead single was `` womanizer '' . other singles released were the title track , `` if u seek amy '' , and `` radar .
