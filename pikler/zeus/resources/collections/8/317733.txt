Mesothelioma     mesothelioma is a cancer of the `` mesothelial '' tissue , a membrane that surrounds several cavities inside of the human body , including the lungs . although mesothelioma hs many possible causes , most cases can be traced to asbestos exposure . mesothelioma is a deadly form of cancer . it is different from asbestosis , another disease caused by asbestos . mesothelioma has been the subject of many lawsuits .
