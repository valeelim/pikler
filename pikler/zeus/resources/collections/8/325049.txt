The Death of General Wolfe     the death of general wolfe is a painting by anglo-american artist benjamin west . the painting shows the death of british general james wolfe during the battle of quebec of the seven years ' war in 1759. the painting is an oil on canvas of the age of enlightenment . west made the painting in 1770. it is now in the national gallery of canada in ottawa . related pages . - the death of the earl of chatham
