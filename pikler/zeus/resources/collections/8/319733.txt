Tung Chung Line (MTR)    tung chung line ( mtr ) the tung chung line is one of the 10 lines of the mtr system in hong kong . it links tung chung with hong kong island . a part of the tung chung line was built along with the two bridges and two mountains , kap shui mun bridge and the tsing ma bridge . it currently travels through eight stations in 27 minutes along its route , which is indicated in orange .
