Cuatro Torres Business Area     cuatro torres business area is a place in madrid . `` cuatro torres '' is spanish . it means `` four towers '' in english . four skyscrapers were built here between 2006 and 2009. each building is about 250 m tall . they are the tallest buildings in spain . skyscrapers . - torre caja madrid - torre de cristal - torre sacyr vallehermoso - torre espacio
