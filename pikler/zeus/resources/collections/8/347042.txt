Supélec     école supérieure d'électricité , commonly known as supélec , is a french university in electric energy and information sciences . founded in 1894 and initially located in the 15th district of paris , it was moved to gif-sur-yvette in 1975. after that , two more campuses were established in rennes in 1972 and metz in 1985. it is a member of the `` conférence des grandes écoles '' . notable alumni . - hervé laborne , former head of the `` école spéciale de mécanique et électricité '' . other websites . - official website in english
