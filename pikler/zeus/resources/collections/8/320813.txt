Jingo-keiun     events of the `` jingo-keiun '' era . - 28 august 770 ( `` jingo-keiun 4 , 4th day of the 8th month '' ) : in the 5th year of shōtoku 's reign , the empress died ; and she named prince shirakabe as her heir . the succession ( `` senso '' ) was received and soon after , emperor kōnin is said to have accepted the monarch 's role and duties and powers ( `` sokui '' ) . this was confirmed in ceremonies . the `` jingō-kaihō '' ' was a copper coin issued from 765 to 796. it had a diameter of about 23 mm and a weight of between 3.4 and 4.5 grams . related pages . - nara period other websites . - national diet library , `` the japanese calendar '' -- historical overview plus illustrative images from library 's collection
