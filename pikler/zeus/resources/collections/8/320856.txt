Echigo Province     the province had borders with uzen , iwashiro , kōzuke , shinano , and etchū provinces . in the meiji period , the provinces of japan were converted into prefectures . the maps of japan and echigo province were reformed in the 1870s . echigo faced the sea of japan . `` iyahiko jinju '' and `` koko jinja '' were the chief shinto shrines ( `` ichinomiya '' ) of echigo . related pages . - provinces of japan - prefectures of japan - list of regions of japan - list of islands of japan other websites . - murdoch 's map of provinces , 1903
