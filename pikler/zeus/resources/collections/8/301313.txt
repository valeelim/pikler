Coburg, Victoria    coburg , victoria coburg is a suburb of melbourne , victoria , australia . it is about 8km north of the melbourne central business district . in 2006 there were 22,772 people living in the suburb . it is a part of the city of moreland local government area . before europen settlement the coburg area was home to the indigenous australian wurundjeri people . the area was first surveyed in 1837-8 by robert hoddle . the area was first called pentridge , after the town of pentridge , dorset , england .
