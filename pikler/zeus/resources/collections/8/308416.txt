Constituent Assembly of Pakistan     the constituent assembly of pakistan was pakistan 's first parliament and first met on 10 august , 1947. the next day quaid-i-azam muhammad ali jinnah ( 1876-1948 ) , was elected as the president of the assembly and the new national flag was approved . on 14 august , 1947 , jinnah gave a speech which outlined the important principles of the future government of an independent pakistan . jinnah was chosen as the first governor-general of pakistan . the assembly 's main task was to write a new constitution for pakistan . this was a difficult task , however the assembly did agree on the `` objective resolution '' on 12 march , 1949. a special committee of 24 members of the constituent assembly worked on a constitution based on the ideas in the objective resolution . the governor-general , ghulam muhammad , shut down the assembly on 24 october 1954 , before the draft constitution could be presented . the president of the assembly , took legal action as he believed that shutting down the assembly was illegal . he lost the case in the federal court .
