The Battle for Wesnoth     the battle for wesnoth ( or just wesnoth ) is a strategy video game . it is designed by david white and the first released was made in june 2003. the game is available under the gpl license . the 1.10.2 version is the last release and was made the april 8 , 2012. the game is programmed in c++ . it is cross-platform and runs on amigaos 4 , beos , freebsd , linux , mac os x , microsoft windows , morphos , netbsd , openbsd , risc os , ios , solaris and android . the project has a very big community and counts many contributors too . some developers are well-known authors of other free and open-source projects . for example , there are eric s. raymond or rusty russell . the game is translated in more than 50 languages . in 10 of them , the game is very well translated ( these languages have more than 95 % of messages translated ) .
