Luciano Moggi     luciano moggi ( born july 10 , 1937 in siena ) is an italian football managing director . he was the general director of juventus f.c . from 1994 until may 2006. he was one of the most successful football managing directors . on june 2011 luciano moggi has been banned from all italian football for life by italian football federation . career . - 1979-1980 : roma - 1980-1982 : lazio - 1982-1987 : torino - 1987-1991 : napoli - 1991-1993 : torino - 1993-1994 : roma - 1994-2006 : juventus
