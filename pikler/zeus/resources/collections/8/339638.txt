The Godfather Part III     the godfather part iii is a 1990 american crime drama movie that stars al pacino and diane keaton . the movie is set in 1979 and 1980. it is the sequel to `` the godfather '' and `` the godfather part ii '' . it received mixed to positive reviews and did well the box office . this movie was released december 25 , 1990 in the united states .
