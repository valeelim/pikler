Akashi Kaikyō Bridge     the , also known as the pearl bridge , is the world 's longest suspension bridge . akashi-kaikyō connects the island of honshu with awaji island in the seto inland sea . the bridge is part of the honshu-shikoku highway which allows cars to cross the akashi strait . construction begin in may 1988. the bridge was opened on april 5 , 1998. the cost of building the bridge is estimated at 500 billion yen . other websites . - structurae , akashi kaikyo bridge
