Albania at the Olympics     albania at the olympics is a history which began in 1972. the international olympic committee 's official abbreviation for albania is alb . the albanian team did not take part in the next games between 1976 and 1988. they returned for the 1992 games in barcelona . they have took part in all olympic games since then . they first took part in the winter olympic games in 2006. albania usually takes part in events that have swimming , athletics , weightlifting , shooting and wrestling . albania has won an olympic medal at the 1972 olympics in weightlifting ( gold , ) . related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - albania profile at london2012.com
