Cuban Revolution     the cuban revolution was an armed revolt led by fidel castro 's 26th of july movement . it was against the government of cuban dictator fulgencio batista . the revolt took place between 1953 and 1959. batista was finally removed from office on january 1 , 1959. he was replaced by a revolutionary government led by castro . this government later changed along communist lines , and became the current communist party of cuba in october 1965. the revolt started with the assault on the moncada barracks on july 26 , 1953. it ended on january 1 , 1959 , when batista was forced to leave the country and santa clara and santiago de cuba were taken by rebels . che guevara and fidel castro 's surrogates raúl castro and huber matos led the rebels . guevara led the group at santa clara . raúl castro and matos led the rebels at santiago de cuba .
