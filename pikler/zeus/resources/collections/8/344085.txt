Alexisonfire     alexisonfire was a canadian post-hardcore band from st. catharines , ontario . alexisonfire had six members in the band ; they were george pettit , chris steele , wade macneil , dallas green , jesse ingelevics and jordan `` ratbeard '' hastings . the band was formed in 2001 by the band members from condemning salemn , helicon blue and plan 9. they released their self-titled first album on september 9 , 2002. alexisonfire split up in 2011. band members . - george pettit – lead vocals ( 2001–2011 ) - dallas green – guitar , piano , vocals ( 2001–2010 ) - wade macneil – guitar , vocals ( 2001–2011 ) - chris steele – bass guitar ( 2001–2011 ) - jordan `` ratbeard '' hastings – drums , percussion ( 2005–2011 ) - jesse ingelevics - drums , percussion ( 2001–2005 ) alexisonfire released 4 studio albums : albums . - `` alexisonfire '' ( 2002 ) - `` watch out !
