Alpine marmot     the alpine marmot is a species of marmot ( a rodent ) . it can be found in mountain regions of central and southern europe . it is the third-largest rodent on the european continent . the eurasian beaver and the crested porcupine are both larger than it . alpine marmots mature in their third year . for this reason , marmots live in family groups of up to twenty animals . alpine marmots have been around since the ice age . today , their range is limited to the higher mountain regions .
