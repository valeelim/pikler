Djibouti at the Olympics     djibouti has been in 6 summer games . they have never been in the winter olympic games . the national olympic committee was formed in 1983. the international olympic committee recognized the country in 1984. hussein ahmed salah won a bronze medal in the 1988 summer olympics for the men 's marathon . related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - djibouti at olympics website - djibouti profile at london2012.com
