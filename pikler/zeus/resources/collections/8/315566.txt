Black Veil Brides     black veil brides is an hardcore emo rock band that formed in cincinnati , ohio in 2006. they won a `` billboard '' award in 2010 for their album , `` we stitch these wounds '' . they made number # 1 on the top independent artists in 2010. the lead singer , andy biersack , created the band . studio albums - `` we stitch these wounds '' ( 2009 ) - `` set the world on fire '' ( 2010 ) - `` wretched and divine : the story of the wild ones '' ( 2013 ) - `` black veil brides '' ( 2014 ) other websites . - allmusic chart awards for black veil brides - biography - official website
