Eva Marie Saint     eva marie saint ( born 4 july 1924 , newark , new jersey ) is an american movie and stage actress . she has performed in many movies , television programs and stage acts . saint has been married to producer/director jeffrey hayden since october 28 , 1951. they have two children , darrell ( born april 1 , 1955 ) and laurette ( born july 19 , 1958 ) , and three grandchildren . hayden died on december 24 , 2016 .
