Grantley Adams International Airport     grantley adams international airport ( gaia ) , is in seawell , christ church on the island of barbados . the former name of the airport was seawell airport . then it was named in honour of the first premier of barbados , sir grantley herbert adams in 1976. gaia is the only port of entry for persons arriving and leaving by air in barbados . in 2011 , the grantley adams airport was the 9th busiest airport in the caribbean region .
