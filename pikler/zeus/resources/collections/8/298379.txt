NHL 12     nhl 12 is an ice hockey video game developed by ea canada and published by ea sports . it is the twenty-first game in the nhl series . it was released on september 8 , 2011 in australasia , september 9 , 2011 in europe and on september 13 , 2011 in north america . the game was featured at the 2011 electronic entertainment expo . new features in nhl 12 include : the ability to hit goalies , goalie fights , improved be a pro mode , more realistic hits , helmets popping off when being hit , knuckleball shots , shattered glass , and more puck movements . players can also play the 2011 winter classic at heinz field under snowfall . a new feature called the `` action tracker '' follows the players every move in the game . other websites . - official website
