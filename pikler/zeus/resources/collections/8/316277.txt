Sejo of Joseon     joseon sejo ( ; september 29 1417 – september 8 1468 ) was the korean joseon dynasty 's seventh king ( 1455 – 1468 ) . he was the second son of sejong the great . in 1429 his peerage was grand prince sooyang . when his father sejong the great died in 1450 , his elder brother munjong was crowned . two years later munjong died , and munjong 's son danjong was crowned . in 1455 , he produced a coup d'état and became king of joseon himself . sejo 's nephew danjong was expelled after he was murdered in 1455. work book . - `` seokbosangjeol '' ( ) - wolilsukbo ( ) - yukdaebyungyo ( ) related pages . - cheukuki - yeonsangun of joseon other websites . - sejo of joseon : navercast - sejo of joseon - 조선 세조대 정치를 보는 시각과 생육신 ( 논문 ) - 나한님 옆 바위틈에 ‘ 물방울 종소리 ’ 청아해라 불교신문 2008년 06월 14일자 - 선 굵은 선조… 개성 넘치는 정조… - < a href= '' http % 3a//news.hankooki.com/lpage/people/200912/h2009122822181584800.htm '' > ( 이성무의 선비 이야기 ) < 15 > 세조와 이계전 < /a >
