Natasha Lyonne     natasha lyonne ( born natasha braunstein , april 4 , 1979 ) is an american actress . she is jewish and grew up in new york city , israel and miami . as a young child she was signed by ford modeling agency . she has been in movies in the `` american pie '' comedy series . she currently plays nicky nichols in orange is the new black . in 2012 she played tiffani in weeds , a show made by the creator of orange is the new black .
