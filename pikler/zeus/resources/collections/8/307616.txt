Shamarpa     shamarpa or more formally kunzig shamar rinpoche is tibetan buddhis teacher . together with karmapa he is the second lineage holder of the karma kagyu school of tibetan buddhism . shamarpa is regarded as manifestation of amitabha buddha . he also is the second lama who consciously incarnates in subsequent order of buddhist teachers . the first shamar rinpoche khedrup drakpa senge ( 1284â€“1349 ) was the principal disciple of the 3rd karmapa rangjung dorje . the present ( 14th ) shamarpa is mipham chokyi lodro , born in derge , tibet in 1952. at age four he was recognized by his uncle the 16th karmapa .
