George &quot;Harmonica&quot; Smith    george & quot ; harmonica & quot ; smith george `` harmonica '' smith ( born april 22 , 1924 in west helena , arkansas ; died october 2 , 1983 in los angeles , california ) ( born allen george smith ) was an american blues harmonica player . born in west helena , arkansas he was brought up in cairo , illinois.he began playing professionally in 1951. he joined muddy waters ' band in 1954. he was again member of the band in 1966.he left chicago and spent much of his adult life on the west coast . smith played with the blues combo , bacon fat , and mentored its harmonica player rod piazza . after this he played harmonica for big mama thornton in the 1970s . he appeared on her album `` jail '' ( 1975 ) .another student of him was william clarke . on the few solo albums he recorded one can hear the influence of little walter on his harmonica playing.george `` harmonica '' smith died in 1983 , in los angeles , california at the age of 59. selected discography . - `` harmonica ace '' - ace records - `` harmonica blues king '' - dobre records 1061
