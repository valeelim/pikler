The Fifty-three Stations of the Tōkaidō     this was a popular subject . hiroshige created many versions of this subject . in 1832 , hiroshige traveled the length of the tōkaidō from edo to kyoto . he was part of an official delegation from the court of the tokugawa shogunate to the court of the emperor of japan . in addition to the 53 stations , hiroshige 's series of images includes one print showing the departure from edo . the series also includes a print showing the journey 's end in the imperial capital city . related pages . - list of the 53 stations of the tōkaidō other websites . - digital museum : different editions of the series
