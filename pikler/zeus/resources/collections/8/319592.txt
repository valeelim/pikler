Burrel, Albania    burrel , albania burrel is a small town in albania . it is 91 km from tirana . its population is about 18,000. it is about 320 metres above sea level , and surrounded by mountains . the area was settled about the 8th century bc by the illyrians . there have been 54 illyrian burial mounds found around burrel . the town of burrel began in 1922 as the centre of the mat district .
