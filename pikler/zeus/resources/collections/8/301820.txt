Presidio of Ahu     presidio of ahú was a prison in the city of curitiba , paraná , brazil . it was the first prison in curitiba , opened in 1905 . closed in 2006 , now is the site of recordings for film and television , such as : - estomago ; - 400 contra 1 ; - o astro ( 2011 ) : soap opera produced by rede globo de televisão .
