Blaenavon Industrial Landscape     blaenavon industrial landscape is the unesco world heritage site in blaenavon , wales . the site includes coal and ore mines , quarries , a primitive railway system , furnaces , workers ' homes , and the social infrastructure of the community . the town of blaenavon and 3,290 hectares in the region of the town were named a world heritage site in 2000. the area around blaenavon shows of the importance of south wales as the world 's major producer of iron and coal in the 19th century . related pages . - list of world heritage sites in the united kingdom other websites . - blaenavon world heritage site website
