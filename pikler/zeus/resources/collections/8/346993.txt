Woke Up This Morning     `` woke up this morning '' is a song by alabama 3 ( also known as a3 ) from their 1997 album `` exile on coldharbour lane '' . the song is best known as the opening theme music for the hbo drama series `` the sopranos '' which used the `` chosen one mix '' . there are two versions of the song , the mix `` the sopranos '' used and the original . other websites . - `` woke up this morning '' lyrics at metrolyrics.com
