Baghlan Province     baghlan ( persian/pashto : بغلان `` baġlān '' ) is one of the thirty-four provinces of afghanistan . it is in the north of the country . its capital is puli khumri . the name comes from a major town in the province , baghlan . the most people are tajiks at 55 % of the population . 20 % are pashtuns , 15 % hazaras , 9 % uzbeks , and the rest are tatar . baghlan is also home to a small community of ismaili muslims of tajik stock , led by the sayeds of kayan . baghlan 's primary crops ( as of 1974 ) were cotton and sugar beets . the area also produced grapes , pistachios , and pommegranates . the primary livestock are karakul sheep . the province also produces silk . coal is mined in the karkar valley . the districts of baghlan are : the current governor of baghlan is mohammad akbar barakzai , appointed 2009 .
