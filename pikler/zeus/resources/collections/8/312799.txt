Baby, It's Cold Outside    baby , it 's cold outside `` baby , it 's cold outside '' is a 1944 song . frank loesser wrote the words . this song was sold to mgm . the song does not mention any holiday , but it is regarded as a christmas song because of the lyrics ' depiction of a snowy backdrop . the song is often heard on radio at christmas time . it has been recorded by multiple musicians , including dean martin , ray charles and barry manilow .
