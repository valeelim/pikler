Archipelago of San Andrés, Providencia and Santa Catalina    archipelago of san andrés , providencia and santa catalina the archipelago of san andrés , providencia and santa catalina ( ) ; or colloquially san andrés y providencia is one of the departments of colombia . it consists of two island groups about northwest of colombia and from the coast of nicaragua , and eight outlying banks and reefs . its capital is san andrés .
