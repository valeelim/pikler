The Script     the script are an irish pop rock group from dublin . they formed in 2001. they are currently based out of london . they are signed to the sony label group . their music has been featured in video games and popular tv programs . the script have made four albums : - `` the script '' ( 2008 ) - `` science & faith '' ( 2010 ) - `` # 3 '' ( 2012 ) - `` no sound without silence '' ( 2014 ) these have all gone to the top of the irish charts . they also did really well in the uk charts , with the first two albums reaching number 1 and `` # 3 '' reaching the second position . their 2012 single `` hall of fame '' , made with will.i.am , was a worldwide success . other websites . - official website
