River Fowey     the river fowey is a river in cornwall , united kingdom , which follows an irregular course across bodmin moor and enters the english channel at fowey . there is a ferry between fowey and bodinnick . the first road crossing going upstream is in lostwithiel . the river has seven tributaries , the largest being the river lerryn . the part of the fowey valley between doublebois and bodmin parkway railway station is known as the glynn valley ( from glynn house , cardinham ) . the valley is the route of both the a38 trunk road and the railway line ( built by the cornwall railway in 1859 ) . the catchment area of the river fowey covers a total of consisting of various types of rock .
