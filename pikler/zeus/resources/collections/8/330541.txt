Ernani     ernani is an italian opera in four acts by giuseppe verdi , with a libretto by francesco maria piave , based on victor hugo 's play `` hernani '' . it was first performed in venice , on march 9 , 1844. principal roles . - ernani - tenor - elvira - soprano - carlo - baritone - silva - bass sources . - `` the complete dictionary of opera & operetta '' , james anderson , wings books , 1993 .
