Earnest Byner     earnest alexander byner ( born september 15 , 1962 ) is an american football coach and former running back . he is famous for one well known mistake while playing with the cleveland browns . while playing against the denver broncos in a 1986 playoff game , byner fumbled at the denver one yard line , which led to a broncos victory . the play was called `` the fumble . '' byner immediately became an assistant coach after his playing days were over .
