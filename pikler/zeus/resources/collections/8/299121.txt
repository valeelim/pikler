Liberia at the Olympics     liberia at the olympics is a history which began in 1956. the international olympic committee 's official abbreviation for liberia is lbr . teams from liberia has been to every summer olympic games held since 1956 , except for 1968 , 1976 and 1992. the country has never won an olympic medal . no athletes from liberia have been in any winter olympic games . the national olympic committee for liberia was formed in 1954. it was recognized by the international olympic committee in 1955. related pages . - list of ioc country codes
