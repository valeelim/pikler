2010 Asian Games     the 2010 asian games was a many-sport event held in guangzhou , china from november 12 to november 27 , 2010. this was the second time that any chinese city had hosted the games , after beijing in 1990. a total of 9,704 athletes from 45 national olympic committees ( nocs ) competed in 476 events from 42 sports and disciplines , making it the biggest event in the history of asian games .
