June 2011 Christchurch earthquake     the june 2011 christchurch earthquake was responsible for causing additional damage to an already earthquake-devastated region . the earthquake took place around 1420 new zealand standard time and was centered next to 3.8 miles deep , six miles from christchurch . it had been preceded by another 5.9 tremor that struck at a slightly deeper 5.5 miles . the earthquake of june 2011 cut down telephone lines and caused numerous outages which left next to 54,000 houses without power . the tremor triggered the death of an elderly man and several injuries .
