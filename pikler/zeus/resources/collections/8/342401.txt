Armavir Province     armavir is a province of armenia . the capital is armavir . it is in the west of the country , between mount ararat and mount aragats . it shares a 45-mile border with turkey to the south and west . the province has the holy city of echmiadzin . echmiadzin is the center of the armenian apostolic church and the seat of the catholicos of armenia and of all armenians . also in this region is the historic city of sardarapat . it was the site of the 1918 battle of sardarapat . armavir is the smallest province of the country . there are 3 urban communities and 94 rural communities .
