T-Pain     faheem rasheed najm ( born september 30 , 1983 ) , better known by his stage name t-pain , is an american singer-songwriter , rapper , record producer , and actor . he is signed to young money entertainment . his debut album , `` rappa ternt sanga '' , was released in 2005. in 2007 , t-pain released his second studio album `` epiphany '' . this album became number one on the `` billboard '' 200. his third studio album , '' thr33 ringz '' , was released in 2008. t-pain has two grammy awards . t-pain is the founder of the record label nappy boy entertainment . it was created in 2005. as a singer , t-pain is well known for using auto-tune to affect how his voice sounds . other websites . - official website - nappy boy records
