Kazakhstan at the Olympics     kazakhstan was first at the olympic games as an independent nation in 1994. they have been in every games since . prior to the dissolution of the soviet union , kazakh athletes were part of the soviet union at the olympics , and were also part of the unified team in 1992. kazakh athletes have won a total of 52 medals at the summer olympic games , and another six at the winter olympic games . the national olympic committee for kazakhstan was formed in 1990 and recognized in 1993 .
