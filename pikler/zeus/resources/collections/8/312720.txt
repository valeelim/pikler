Rockin' Around the Christmas Tree    rockin ' around the christmas tree `` rockin ' around the christmas tree '' is an extremely popular christmas song first performed by brenda lee . it was released in 1958 and 1959 , although it was not until 1960 that the song became popular . other musicians have covered the song through the years , including , however not limited to amy grant , jessica simpson , leann rimes and miley cyrus .
