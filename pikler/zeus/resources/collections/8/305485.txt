Valeria Kozlova     valeria sergeyevna kozlova ( ; born on january 22 , 1988 ) is a russian singer , composer , songwriter , drummer and actress . she was a member of the music group `` ranetki '' from 2005 until 2008. she was in a relationship with `` ranetki '' 's producer sergei milnichenko ( b.1966 ) from 2005 until 2008 .
