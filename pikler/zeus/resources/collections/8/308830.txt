Broadcast Music, Inc.    broadcast music , inc. broadcast music incorporated or bmi is an organization to protect music rights in the communications business . it was founded by ascap , who were boycotting radio at the time ( 1944 ) . both bmi and ascap , as well as other organizations like sesac monitor performances of the music to which they control the rights . bmi has historically been more open to composers of rock and roll , jazz , folk music , blues , and country music who sing and play their own music . in 2013 , bmi made $ 944 million and gave $ 814 million to its songwriters . further reading . - choquette , frederic , `` the returned value of pros '' , `` music business journal '' , berklee college of music , may 2011 other websites . - searchable repertoire of bmi licensed material - bmi article @ answers.com , better history
