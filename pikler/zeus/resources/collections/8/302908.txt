Dagestan     dagestan is a federal subject of russia in the caucasus region . its capital city is makhachkala . dagestan has a population of nearly 3 million . it is inhabited by over 30 different nationalities who speak 30 different languages . most people of dagestan are muslims and belong to the shafi`i school of sunni islam . dagestan takes its name from two persian words , dag - which means mountain , and stan which means - land . famous people . - imam shamil - shaykh said afandi al-chirkawi sources . - islam in dagestan - official website of dagestani government
