Menace II Society     menace ii society , released may 26 , 1993 by warner bros. , is an american urban crime drama movie set in and around south central los angeles . it is rated r by the mpaa for violence , intense language and drug use . several different versions exist for this motion picture . the character sharif was originally supposed to be played by tupac shakur , although shakur was later fired due to a dispute in which he wanted to do play someone else , so larenz tate was his replacement . a prison riot scene was cut from this motion picture to avoid receiving an nc-17 . `` menace ii society '' received positive reviews by critics .
