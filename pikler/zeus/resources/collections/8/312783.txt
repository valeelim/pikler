Pope Pius IV     pope pius iv ( ; 31 march 1499 â€“ 9 december 1565 ) , born giovanni angelo medici , was an italian priest of the roman catholic church and the 225th pope from 1559 to 1565. giovanni angelo medici was born in milan . he was not closely related to the medicis of florence . he studied at university of pavia and the university of bologna where he earned a law degree . in 1527 , medici went to rome where he joined the church bureaucracy . in 1549 , pope paul iii made medici a cardinal . pope leo iii sent him on diplomatic missions to germany and to hungary . during the reign of pope paul iv , cardinal medici was out of favor . cardinal medici was elected pope on 25 december 1559. in the context of the counter-reformation , this pope moderated the inquisition in italy . pope paul iii presided over the last session of the council of trent which was the major event of his papacy . in part , the tridentine creed reflects the thinking of church leaders at the council of trent . after his death in 1565 , the body of pope pius iv was entombed at the church of santa maria degli angeli .
