Jim Varney     james albert `` jim '' varney , jr. ( june 15 , 1949 - february 10 , 2000 ) was an american stand-up comedian , actor , writer , musician , voice actor and comedian . he was born and raised in lexington , kentucky . varney is best remembered for his role as ernest p. worrell in many movies and television programs . he died of lung cancer in white house , tennessee .
