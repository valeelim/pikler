Varicose veins     varicose veins are a condition in which the veins in the leg area have become enlarged . if veins have become varicose , the leaflets of the valves do not work properly , and in turn , the vein valves themselves wo n't work . the blood then flows backwards , at which point the veins then start to increase . this condition is most common in superficial veins which involve the legs . the condition not only causes cosmetic troubles , it also creates itching and painful problems .
