Sport Relief     sport relief is a charity event from comic relief that happens every two years . it is held in association with bbc sport . the event brings together the worlds of sport and entertainment to raise money to help vulnerable people in both the uk and the world 's poorest countries . at the heart of the campaign is the sport relief mile . other websites . - sport relief website - donate - all out for india - sport relief fairtrade cotton socks - bench press challenge other pages . - children in need
