Ghost Adventures     ghost adventures is a weekly american paranormal television series that started on october 17 , 2008 on the travel channel . produced by my-tupelo entertainment , the program follows and stars ghost hunters zak bagans , nick groff , and aaron goodwin , as they investigate locations that are reported to be haunted . hosted and narrated by bagans , the show airs new episodes on fridays at 9 p.m. eastern time on the travel channel . nick groff of the ghost adventures crew confirmed there will be a sixth season that will premier in 2012. other websites . - of `` ghost adventures '' - of `` ghost adventures live '' - of the ghost adventures crew ( gac ) - the paranormal society ( officially authorized online content provider )
