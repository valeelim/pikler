My Heart Will Go On     `` my heart will go on '' is a hit song by canadian singer celine dion . it was in the movie `` titanic '' . its music was composed by james horner . the lyrics were written by will jennings . the song won the academy award for best original song . the song reached number one in over 10 countries . it became dion 's signature song . it helped her become a popular singer . it was one of the best selling singles worldwide . it was the best-selling single of 1998. european cd single 1 . `` my heart will go on '' – 4:40 1 . `` because you loved me '' – 4:33 european cd single # 2 1 . `` my heart will go on '' – 4:40 1 . `` my heart will go on '' ( tony moran mix ) – 4:21 french cd single 1 . `` the reason '' – 5:01 1 . `` my heart will go on '' – 4:40 french cd single # 2 1 . `` my heart will go on '' – 4:40 1 . `` southampton '' – 4:02 french cd single # 3 1 . `` my heart will go on '' – 4:40 1 . `` my heart will go on '' ( tony moran mix ) – 4:21 japanese cd single 1 . `` my heart will go on '' – 4:40 1 . `` beauty and the beast '' – 4:04 uk cassette single
