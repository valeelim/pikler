Sakyo Komatsu     komatsu was born in osaka . in 1954 , he earned a degree in italian literature at kyoto university . komatsu worked as a magazine editor , a factory foreman and a comedy scriptwriter before he began writing science fiction . the premise of komatsu 's best known book , `` japan sinks '' , was confirmed in 2011 when parts of the sanriku coastline sank more than a meter in the 2011 tōhoku earthquake and tsunami . in the massive geologic shift , the land mass of the main island of japan sank 2.4 centemeters . komatsu 's most popular book was the basis of a 1973 film , a television series , a 2006 film remake , and a dvd . in an overview of writings by and about sakyo komatsu , oclc/worldcat includes roughly 380+ works in 520+ publications in 14 languages and 1,520 library holdings . - 日本沈没 ( 1959 ) - `` peace on earth '' ( 1961 ) - 地球になった男 ( 1971 ) - ( 1973 ) - 現代の神話 ( 1973 ) - 歴史と文明の旅 ( 1973 ) - ( 1974 ) - 戦争はなかった ( 1974 ) - 果てしなき流れの果に ( 1976 ) - 日本文化の死角 ( 1977 ) - 物体o ( 1977 ) - アメリカの壁 ( 1978 ) - `` nihilistic corridor '' ( 1999 )
