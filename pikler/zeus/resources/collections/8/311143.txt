Curing salt     curing salt ( or pink salt or prague powder # 1 ) is mixture of salt and sodium nitrate that is used to preserve or cure meats or in the making of sausages . it contains 93.75 % table salt and 6.25 % . the sodium nitrite in curing salt is used to stop the growth of `` clostridium botulinum '' in order to prevent botulism . curing salt may be dyed pink preventing it from being confused for as table salt . other pages . - pickling salt - table salt
