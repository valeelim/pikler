Raja Ravi Varma College of Fine Arts     raja ravi varma college of fine arts is in mavelikkara , kerala , india . the college was established by rama varma son of raja ravi varma in 1915.the first principal of this college was artist p.j.cherian . the college offers both undergraduate degree and diploma courses in fine arts , including in sculpture , painting and applied arts . this government institution is affiliated with the university of kerala . noted faculty and alumni . - t.a.s menon - n.n . rimzon - sosa joseph references . - university of kerala - http : //www.hindu.com/mag/2010/02/07/stories/2010020750180500.htm - http : //www.thehindu.com/education/careers/article46193.ece - course details other websites . - raja ravi varma
