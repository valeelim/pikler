George Eastman     george eastman ( july 12 , 1854 - march 14 , 1932 ) was an american inventor and businessman . eastman was born in waterville , new york . he founded the eastman kodak company and invented roll film . this made photography popular and led to other men inventing film stock . his kodak company still runs today , but more focused on printers and printing . eastman shot himself dead in rochester , new york .
