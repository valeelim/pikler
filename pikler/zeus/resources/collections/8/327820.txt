Common year starting on Saturday     this is the calendar for any common year starting on saturday , january 1 ( dominical letter b ) . the most recent year of this type was 2011 and the next one will be 2022 in the gregorian calendar , or julian years 2017 and 2023 ( see bottom tables ) . the question mark for the iso week before week 1 is due to ambiguity based on whether or not the previous year was a leap year . if it was , then that week is week 53 , if it was a common year then it is week 52 . < br >
