Apple strudel     apple strudel ( ) is a traditional austrian dessert . it is also known in most of the countries which formed the austro-hungarian empire . the recipe came over the ottoman empire to austria . a sweet related to strudel is baklava . strudel was named for the first time in 1696. it gained popularity through empress maria theresia . strudel is made of a thin , elastic pastry . the filling contains sliced apples , sugar , cinnamon , raisins , and bread crumbs . preparing the strudel dough is a difficult process , so you can also buy strudel pastry . apple strudel can also be made with puff pastry . traditionally , the strudel is served warm with whipped cream or vanilla ice cream . also , vanilla sauce is popular in many countries .
