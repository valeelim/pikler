Saint Kitts and Nevis at the Olympics     saint kitts and nevis was first at the olympic games in 1996 and has been in every summer olympic games since . st kitts and nevis has never been at the winter olympic games . saint kitts and nevis has never won a medal . they have only been in the track and field events . related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - saint kitts and nevis profile at london2012.com
