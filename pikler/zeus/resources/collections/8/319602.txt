Line of succession to the Belgian throne     the line of succession to the belgian throne is the list of people who may become king or queen of belgium . the death of king baudouin in 1993 caused a short period of uncertainty about the succession . prince philippe was the expected to be the next king ; but baudouin 's brother albert became the new monarch . since 2009 , belgian law identifies the eldest child of a belgian monarch the royal heir . the first-born child follows his or her parent on the throne . this is known as full or equal primogeniture . in 2013 , albert abdicated for health reasons and his son , philippe became the king . in 2014 , the current order of succession is clear . 1. princess elisabeth of belgium , elder daughter of philippe 1. prince gabriel of belgium , elder son of philippe 1. prince emmanuel of belgium , younger son of philippe 1. princess el√©onore of belgium , younger daughter of philippe 1. princess astrid of belgium , daughter of albert ii 1. prince amedeo of belgium , eldest son of princess astrid
