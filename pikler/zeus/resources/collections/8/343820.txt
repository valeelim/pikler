Confectionery     confectionery or sweets is a type of food that has a lot of sugar in it and is very sweet . it often includes things that are rich in artificial sweeteners , flavours and colourings . the words candy ( north america ) , sweets ( uk and ireland ) and lollies ( australia and new zealand ) are also used for the many kinds of confections . confections are usually low in nutrients but rich in calories . sweets can come from anywhere .
