Neuroptera     neuroptera is a order of net-winged insects , and includes the lacewings , mantidflies , antlions , and their relatives . the order contains some 6,010 species . the adults of this order possess four membranous wings , with the forewings and hindwings about the same size , and with many veins . they have chewing mouthparts , and undergo complete metamorphosis . most of the larvae and adults are predators . the clade first appeared in the permian period ,
