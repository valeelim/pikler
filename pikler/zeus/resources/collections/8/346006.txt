On the Buses     on the buses is a british sitcom set in fictional luxton in essex . it ran from 28 february 1969 until 6 may 1973. the show stars reg varney as bus driver stan butler , bob grant as his conductor jack harper and stephen lewis as their inspector blakey . anna karen plays stan 's sister olive rudge and michael robbins plays her husband arthur rudge . the role of stan 's widowed mother mabel butler was played by cicely courtneidge in series 1 and by doris hare in the other series .
