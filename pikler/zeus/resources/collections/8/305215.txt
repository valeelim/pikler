Cape Conran Coastal Park     cape conran coastal park is a coastal reserve near marlo in east gippsland , victoria , australia . it is 420 km east of melbourne and 530 km south of sydney . it covers an area of 11,700 hectares , and has 60 kilometres of coast line . most of the area is covered in banksias and woodland which attracts many nectar feeding birds . other birds seen in the area include the white-bellied sea eagles and eastern ground parrots . whales and dolphins can sometimes be seen near the coast at certain times of the year .
