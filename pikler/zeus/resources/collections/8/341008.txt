Namibe     namibe ( pre-1985 : `` moçâmedes '' ) is a coastal town in angola . it is the capital of namibe province . it was formed in 1840 by the portuguese rulers of the territory . there were 132,900 people in 2004. namibe has a cool dry climate . there are desert plants because it is close to the namib desert . it is one of angola 's 3 major ports , along with luanda and lobito it is one of angola 's 3 major ports . namibe has the namibe airport .
