SN 1572     sn 1572 was a supernova seen in 1572 that could be seen in the constellation cassiopeia . it is sometimes called `` tycho 's nova '' or `` tycho 's star '' since tycho brahe studied it . the supernova became visible in november 1572 and stayed visible until 1574. sn 1572 is one of only about ten supernovas that could be seen without telescopes .
