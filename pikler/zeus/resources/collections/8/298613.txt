Marco Polo (TV movie)    marco polo ( tv movie ) marco polo is a 2007 tv mini series , starring ian somerhalder , b.d . wong , and brian dennehy . it is directed by kevin connor . marco polo is a venetian trader who was imprisoned in genoa in the 13th century . he tells a fellow prisoner who is dying about his days as a young man in china . he talks about his fantastic adventures , his rise to governorship in kublai khan 's ( brian dennehy ) court in mongolia , his love for a kidnapped bride , and his escape back to italy as a rich man .
