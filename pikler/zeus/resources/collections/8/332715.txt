Ben Kingsley     ben kingsley ( born krishna bhanji , 31 december 1943 ) is a british actor . he was born in scarborough , north yorkshire , england . his father was an indian who was born in kenya . he is best known for his role as mohandas karamchand gandhi in `` gandhi '' , for which he won an academy award , a bafta award and a golden globe award . the first movie he acted in was `` fear is the key '' in 1972 .
