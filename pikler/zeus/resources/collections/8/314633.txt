Jennifer Jason Leigh     jennifer jason leigh ( born february 5 , 1962 ) is an american movie and stage actress . she is best known for her roles in `` fast times at ridgemont high '' , `` single white female '' , `` last exit to brooklyn '' , `` georgia '' , `` kill your darlings '' and `` short cuts '' . she has had many roles in which she portrays vulnerable , abused and damaged women in her movies .
