Beavis and Butt-head     beavis and butt-head is an animated american television series by mike judge which began on march 8 , 1993 and ended on november 28 , 1997 on mtv . in 1996 the series was adapted into a movie titled `` beavis and butt-head do america '' . the title characters are hard rock/heavy metal loving teenagers from the fictional town of highland , texas . in 2010 it was announced the series would return in 2011 .
