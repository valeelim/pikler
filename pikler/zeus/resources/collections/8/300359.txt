Aaron Spelling     aaron spelling ( april 22 , 1923 - june 23 , 2006 ) was an american executive producer of many television shows . he was born in dallas , texas . his shows included `` beverly hills , 90210 '' and `` melrose place '' . the earlier had spelling 's daughter tori starring with shannen doherty , gabrielle carteris and luke perry . the latter had courtney thorne-smith , heather locklear and marcia cross acting in the show . spelling was diagnosed with oral cancer in 2001. he died of a stroke in holmby hills , los angeles , california . spelling had a daughter , tori , and son , randy .
