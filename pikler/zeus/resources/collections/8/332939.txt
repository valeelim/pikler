Chilean Communist Party (Proletarian Action)    chilean communist party ( proletarian action ) the chilean communist party ( proletarian action ) ( spanish : `` partido comunista chileno ( acción proletaria ) pc ( ap ) '' ) is a non-registered hard-line communist political party in chile . it was founded in november 8 , 1979 and its first secretary is eduardo artés . the party believes in classical marxism–leninism . the party is highly critical of the more known communist party of chile and its moderate views . the party has no parliamentary representation due to their non-registered status . other websites . - pc ( ap ) official website
