Enkyū     events of the `` enkyū '' era . - 1069 ( `` enkyū 1 '' ) : the emperor 's consort was raised to the status of `` chūgū '' . - 1072 ( `` enkyū 4 , 8th day of the 12th month '' ) : in the 6th year of go-sanjō 's reign , the emperor abdicated ; and the succession ( `` senso '' ) was received by his son . soon after , emperor shirakawa accepted the monarch 's role and duties and powers ( `` sokui '' ) . this was confirmed in ceremonies . - 1073 ( `` enkyū 5 , 21st day of the 4th month '' ) : go-sanjō entered the buddhist priesthood ; and his priestly name was kongō-gyō . - 1073 ( `` enkyū 5 , 7th day of the 5th month '' ) : go-sanjō died at the age of 40. related pages . - heian period other websites . - national diet library , `` the japanese calendar '' -- historical overview plus illustrative images from library 's collection
