Sacramento River     the sacramento river is a river in california in the united states . it flows through the sacramento valley in northern california , and is about long . the sacramento has many tributaries , including the pit , feather and american rivers . cities along the river include redding and sacramento . native people have inhabited the region for about 12,000 years . the sacramento river 's tributaries were the focus of the california gold rush , which brought many settlers to the area in the 1800s . today , the river is used as a source of irrigation water and to produce hydroelectricity . tributaries . - cache creek - putah creek - american river - feather river - bear river ( feather river ) - yuba river - pit river - mccloud river related pages . - shasta dam
