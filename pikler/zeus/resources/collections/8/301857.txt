Apricot (color)    apricot ( color ) apricot is a color that is a representation of the color of the apricot fruit . `` apricot '' in has been in written use as a color name since 1851. the word `` apricot '' comes from the arabic `` al-birquq '' , in turn derived from latin `` armeniacus '' , i.e. , `` the fruit from armenia '' . the ultimate origin of the apricot fruit was from china . meaning of apricot . - the color apricot represents anything tangy , sweet , and delicious like apricots . related pages . - list of colors - orange - peach ( color )
