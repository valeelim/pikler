Seneca the Elder     seneca the elder ( lucius or marcus annaeus seneca , born about 54bc , died about 39ad ) was a roman rhetorician and writer . his main work is `` controversiae '' . `` controversiea '' is a ten volume work . in it , he treats 74 cases that are commonly seen by rhetoricians . his other main work is `` suasoriae '' ( councils ) . in it , he shows seven examples of how to respond in common cases . he is the father of the philosopher , poet and politician , seneca the younger
