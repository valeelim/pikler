Station (agriculture)    station ( agriculture ) station is the term for a big agricultural property ( owned land ) that is used for raising livestock . the word is used in australian and new zealand english . the owner of a station is called a `` grazier '' . they are also sometimes called `` pastoralists '' . its workers are called `` stockmen '' . the stations may be called `` sheep stations '' or `` cattle stations '' , depending on what type of animal it raises . related pages . - ranch
