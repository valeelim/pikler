21 Grams     21 grams is an american drama movie directed by alejandro gonzález iñárritu and written by guillermo arriga . it stars sean penn , naomi watts , benicio del toro , whose lives are connected when del toro 's character jack jordan kills the husband and two daughters of watts ' character cristina peck in a hit-and-run road accident . his heart is transplanted into penn 's character , mathematician paul rivers .
