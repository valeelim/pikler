Giant Antshrike     the giant antshrike ( batara cinerea ) is a species of bird in the thamnophilidae family . it belongs to the genus batara . it is found in argentina , bolivia , brazil , and paraguay . this species is the most large species of antbird , measuring 34 cm long and weighing around 150 g. its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical forests . references . - birdlife international 2004. batara cinerea . 2006 iucn red list of threatened species . downloaded on 25 july 2007. other websites . - image at adw
