Baila Esta Cumbia     `` baila esta cumbia '' ( ) is a song by american singer selena . the song was the lead single from `` ven conmigo '' ( 1990 ) . it peaked at number-one on the mexican singles chart ( 1994 ) , italy singles chart ( 1996 ) , argentinian singles chart ( 1994 ) and france singles chart ( 1995 ) . it peaked in the top 20 on the us hot latin tracks ( 1990 ) , latin regional mexican airplay ( 1991 ) chart and the spanish singles chart ( 1996 ) . it then became one of selena 's signature songs . her brother , a.b . quintanilla iii remixed the song as `` baila esta kumbia '' . it peaked at number 44 on the us hot latin tracks and number 16 on the latin regional mexican airplay chart . a.b . performed the song during the selena Â¡vive ! tribute concert with his band .
