West Point (1928 movie)    west point ( 1928 movie ) west point is a 1928 silent movie . the story and screenplay were written by raymond l. schrock . titles were written by joseph farnham . the movie was directed by edward sedgwick . it was produced by maj. raymond g. moses . the movie was shot on location at west point and vicinity . `` photoplay '' gave the movie a nice review , commenting : `` bill haines ' starring vehicle ... treats everything in a humorous vein in the beginning , getting many laughs . '' the same story was used for `` dress parade '' in 1927 starring william boyd and bessie love . the 1928 movie has been released to dvd . brice wayne ( played by billy haines ) is an arrogant , impudent , and flippant west point cadet . he realizes that he does not have the proper school spirit . he plays football , but is dismissed from the team shortly before the army-navy match . brice resigns from the military academy . he retracts when his friends tex mcneil ( played by william bakewell ) and betty channing ( played by joan crawford ) encourage him to stay . restored to the team , brice scores the big goal in the game against navy .
