Cyclopia     cyclopia ( also called cyclocephaly or synophthalmia ) is a birth defect . cyclopia is characterized by a failure of the prosencephalon to separate the two orbits ( eyes ) . this leads to the formation of a central deformed eye and an absence of a nasal cavity . it causes can be either genetic , or as a result of an exposure to poisons . the term `` cyclopia '' comes from cyclops , a giant one-eyed creature the greek greek mythology .
