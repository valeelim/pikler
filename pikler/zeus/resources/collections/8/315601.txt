Bonzi Wells     gawen deangelo `` bonzi '' wells ( born september 28 , 1976 ) is an american basketball player . he played in the nba for the portland trail blazers , memphis grizzlies , sacramento kings , houston rockets , and new orleans hornets . he went to college at ball state university . in 1997 and 1998 he was named ncaa ap all-american . wells has most recently played basketball in puerto rico .
