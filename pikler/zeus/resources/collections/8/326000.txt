Colin Firth     colin andrew firth ( born 10 september 1960 ) is a british actor . firth was born in grayshott , hampshire , england . he has been acting since 1983. he became well-known when he played fitzwilliam darcy in `` pride and prejudice '' . he played mark darcy in `` bridget jones 's diary '' and its sequel `` '' . he won an academy award for best actor for his role as king george vi in `` the king 's speech '' . firth has also received several bafta and screen actors guild award nominations . firth is also a writer and political activist .
