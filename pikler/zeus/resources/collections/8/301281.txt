Paratyphoid fever     paratyphoid fever , also known as enteric fever , is a waterborne disease caused by the bacterium `` salmonella paratyphi '' . it is related to typhoid fever , but like typhoid , it is unrelated to typhus . symptoms include : - fever , - abdominal pain - swelling of the liver or spleen - slow heart rate . the disease is rarely fatal and people usually recover in a few days , but some people can carry the bacteria for months .
