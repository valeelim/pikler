Sri Lanka at the Olympics     sri lanka first took part in the olympic games in 1948. the international olympic committee 's official abbreviation for sri lanka is sri . in 1964 , it was cey for ceylon . teams took part in all summer olympic games since then , but not the 1976 games . sri lanka never took part in the winter olympic games . the national olympic committee for sri lanka was formed in 1937 and recognized by the international olympic committee the same year . the nation was called `` ceylon '' until 1972. related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - sri lanka profile at london2012.com
