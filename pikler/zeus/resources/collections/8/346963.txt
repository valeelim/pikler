Anting     anting is a behavior in which birds rub insects ( usually ants ) on their feathers . this removes liquids with chemicals such as formic acid . instead of ants , birds can also use millipedes . over 250 species of bird have been known to ant . there are two forms of anting : active anting and passive acting . active anting is when a bird picks up ants in its bill and wipes them on its feathers . passive anting is when the bird lies down in a group of ants and opens up its wings so that the ants will crawl up into its feathers .
