National Assembly of Bahrain     the national assembly is the name of both chambers of the bahraini parliament . this is in the constitution of 2002. it has 80 seats . there are 40 elected members of the council of representatives ( the lower house ) . there are 40 royally-appointed members of the consultative council ( the upper house ) . it is chaired by the speaker of the consultative council . if that person is gone the chair is the speaker of the council of representatives .
