Boogie Nights     boogie nights is a 1997 american drama movie produced , written and directed by paul thomas anderson . it is set in the san fernando valley , los angeles , california from the late 1970s to the early 1980s . the movie is about actors and filmmakers in pornographic movies . it stars mark wahlberg , julianne moore , burt reynolds ( who won a golden globe award for best supporting actor ) , don cheadle , john c. reilly , philip seymour hoffman , william h. macy and heather graham .
