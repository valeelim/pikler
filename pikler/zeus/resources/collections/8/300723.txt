Blood eagle     the blood eagle is a torture method where a person is cut by the ribs from the spine , breaking the victims ribs so they resembled blood-stained wings , and having their lungs pulled out . salt was also sometimes sprinkled in the wounds . the torture method was mostly mentioned in norse saga literature . king ælla of northumbria , halfdan son of king haraldr hárfagri of norway , king edmund , king maelgualai of munster , and possibly archbishop ælfheah of canterbury are all believed to have been victims of the torture method .
