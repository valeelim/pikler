Biosemiotics     biosemiotics is a science where the models of sign processes are used to study living systems . unlike biophysics , biochemistry and molecular biology , sciences where the quantitative physical laws are used to explain the living things , biosemioticians study the organisms and phenomena of life on the basis of qualitative semiotic principles . bibliography . - emmeche , claus ; kull , kalevi ( eds . ) ( 2011 ) . `` towards a semiotic biology : life is the action of signs '' . london : imperial college press .
