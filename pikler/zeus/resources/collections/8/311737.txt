Vittorio Arrigoni     vittorio arrigoni ( 4 february 1975 in besana in brianza , lombardy - 15 april 2011 in gaza ) was an italian journalist and peace activist who had gone to live in the gaza strip . he was very critical of israel , but also islamist groups that wanted an extreme islamic state . an islamic terrorist group kidnapped arrigoni and said they would kill him unless the hamas government released their leader from prison . they showed arrigoni with his eyes covered , on a youtube video , with the text `` the italian hostage entered our land only to spread corruption . '' the police went to a building in gaza where they found arrigoni 's body . he had been beaten and strangled .
