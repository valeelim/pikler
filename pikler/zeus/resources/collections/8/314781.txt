Janet Evans     janet evans ( born august 28 , 1971 in fullerton , california ) is an american swimmer . her career started at the 1988 summer olympics and ended at the 1996 summer olympics . she did not win a medal in 1996 but she added one more highlight to her career when she was given the honors of carrying the olympic torch at the 1996 summer olympics ' opening ceremony . evans retired after the 1996 olympics . she later said she would return to swimming with the 2012 summer olympics ' trials .
