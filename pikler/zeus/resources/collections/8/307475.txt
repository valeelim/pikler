Si Una Vez     `` si una vez '' ( ) is a song by american singer selena . it was released as the fifth single from `` amor prohibido '' ( 1994 ) . in 1994 , puerto rican merengue musician manny manuel covered the song on his debut album , `` el rey de corazones '' . the track peaked at # 18 on hot latin tracks and # 1 on the latin tropical airplay .
