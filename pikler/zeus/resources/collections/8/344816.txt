Montpellier – Méditerranée Airport     montpellier – méditerranée airport or `` aéroport de montpellier - méditerranée '' , also known as fréjorgues airport , is an airport in southern france . it is 7 km east-southeast of montpellier in mauguio . the airport carries the 10th largest number of passengers in france ( over 1.180 million in 2010 ) . a campus of the `` école nationale de l'aviation civile '' ( french civil aviation university ) is also located at the airport .
