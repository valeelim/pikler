Dzyarzhynskaya Hara     dzyarzhynskaya hara is the highest point in belarus . the hill is 345 meters ( 1,130 ft ) above sea level . it is west of minsk , near dzyarzhynsk , in the village skirmuntava . the original name of the hill was svyataya hara . in 1958 the hill was renamed `` hara dzyarzhynskaya '' , after felix dzerzhinsky , the founder of the nkvd/kgb .
