Slyudyanka     slyudyanka ( ) , is a town in irkutsk oblast , russia . the town is located on the southern coast of lake baikal . it sits at the foot of the khamar-daban mountainrange . it is next to the confluence of the slyudyanka river and baikal . the town is divided into two parts by the transsiberian railway . slyudyanka had a population of 19,242 in the 2002 census . in 2010 . 18,542 people lived there . it is served by slyudyanka-i and slyudyanka-ii railwaystations . the circum-baikal railway starts from the slyudyanka-ii station . slyudyanka was founded in 1647 by the russian cossacks . because of its geographical position , the city grew quickly and became a large transit centre which connected irkutsk with china and mongolia . the town became a railway hub in 1905 , when constructing of transsiberian railway had been finished . also there is a marble deposit in the town .
