Stye     styes ( ) , also hordeolum ( ) are infections at the base of a human eyelid or the eyelash . they are usually formed in the lids and are small red bumps ; these styes can be either external or internal . styes are small-sized and rather short in duration . they happen particularly often in infants , although people of all ages can get it . they 're triggered by eye rubbing or poor nutrition . they often last four days under treatment to two weeks without .
