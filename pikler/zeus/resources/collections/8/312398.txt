Peter Tatsuo Doi     doi was born in sendai . he studied in rome . doi was ordained as a priest in 1921. in 1934 , he was made secretary of the apostolic delegation to japan . he was named bishop of tokyo in 1937. he was apostolic administrator of roman catholic diocese of yokohama from 1945 to 1947. in 1960 , pope john xxiii created him cardinal of sant'antonio da padova in via merulana . in 1965 , doi was among those who read the closing messages of the second vatican council . other websites . - cardinals of the holy roman church , doi , peter tatsuo - catholic-hierarchy , peter tatsuo cardinal doi - archdiocese of tokyo ;
