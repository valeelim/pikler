View of Delft     view of delft ( c.1660-1661 ) is an oil painting . it was painted by johannes vermeer . the painting shows vermeer 's hometown . it is one of his most popular pictures . it is one of three paintings of delft by vermeer . the others are `` the little street '' and the lost painting `` house standing in delft '' . in 2011 , the painting was featured on gold and silver commemorative coins issued by the royal dutch mint .
