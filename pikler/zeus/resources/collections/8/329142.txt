Barkerville, British Columbia    barkerville , british columbia barkerville is a ghost town in the canadian province of british columbia . the peak population of barkerville was 5,000. barkerville is classified as a historic town . the chinese were also a major part in the history of barkerville as they built most of the businesses , cabins and both provided social services and resolved disputes within the chinese community without having to use bc courts . the town was designated a national historic site of canada in 1924. other websites . - official website
