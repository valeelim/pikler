Noto Province     the province had borders with etchū and kaga provinces . the ancient capital city of the province was nanao . noto province was created during the reign of empress genshō . in the meiji period , the provinces of japan were converted into prefectures . the maps of japan and noto province were reformed in the 1870s . `` keta jinja '' was the chief shinto shrines ( `` ichinomiya '' ) of noto . related pages . - provinces of japan - prefectures of japan - list of regions of japan - list of islands of japan other websites . - murdoch 's map of provinces , 1903
