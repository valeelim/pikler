Prime (movie)    prime ( movie ) prime is a 2005 american romantic comedy-drama movie set in manhattan , new york city . it was written and directed by ben younger . it stars uma thurman , meryl streep and bryan greenberg . thurman plays 37 year old divorced photographer rafi gardet , who begins a sexual relationship with 23 year old david bloomberg , who is jewish . streep plays her psychiatrist lisa metzger , whom she tells about the relationship . both women find out that the man involved is lisa 's son . lisa disapproves of the relationship because of the age-gap and because rafi is not jewish .
