Victoria Royals     the victoria royals are a canadian ice hockey team . they began playing in 2011. the team currently plays in the western hockey league . they play their home games in victoria , british columbia at the save-on-foods memorial centre . they originally played in chilliwack , british columbia as the chilliwack bruins from 2006 to 2011 before relocating to victoria in 2011 and becoming the royals . other websites . - official website
