Altarnun     altarnun is a village and parish in cornwall , united kingdom , on the north-eastern edge of bodmin moor at . the parish of altarnun includes the village of fivelanes and the hamlets of bolventor , treween and trewint , and had a population of 976 according to the 2001 census . the moorland area of the parish is large and lies west of the village towards rough tor and southwards towards dozmary pool . the village is in the valley of the penpont water and the parish is divided by the a30 trunk road which passes through fivelanes .
