Juno (spacecraft)    juno ( spacecraft ) juno is an unmanned nasa new frontiers spacecraft . it will orbit and explore the planet jupiter . the spacecraft was launched on august 5 , 2011 from the cape canaveral air force station . scott bolton is leading the mission . juno 's main goal is to understand the origin and evolution of jupiter . juno will also study the planet 's atmospheric composition , gravity field , magnetic field , and polar magnetosphere .
