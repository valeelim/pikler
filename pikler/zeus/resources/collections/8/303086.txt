2018 FIFA World Cup     the 2018 fifa world cup will be the 21st fifa world cup , which is a men 's football tournament that takes place every four years and is organized by fifa . the tournament will take place in russia from 14 june to 15 july 2018 , after the country was awarded the hosting rights on 2 december 2010. this is the first world cup taking place in eastern europe . the winners will qualify for the 2021 fifa confederations cup . russia is the host country , so the russian team automatically qualified .
