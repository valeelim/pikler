Gérard Rozenknop     gérard rozenknop , born the 23th of january 1950 in paris , is a french aerospace engineer . he was head of the french civil aviation university from the 6th of january 1999 to the 28th of november 2008. graduate from the `` école polytechnique '' ( 1969 ) and the french civil aviation university ( 1972 ) , he has done a career at the french civil aviation authorities and at the international civil aviation organization before becoming head of the french civil aviation university . further reading . - académie nationale de l'air et de l'espace and lucien robineau , `` les français du ciel , dictionnaire historique '' , le cherche midi , june 2005 , 782 p.
