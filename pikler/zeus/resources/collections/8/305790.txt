Caroline Wozniacki     caroline wozniacki ( born 11 july 1990 ) is a danish professional tennis player . as of september 2015 , she is the number five ranked female tennis player in the world . she used to be world number one . in her career , she has won 23 singles tournaments on the women 's tennis association ( wta ) tour . her best performances in a grand slam event were reaching the finals of the 2009 and 2014 u.s. open . she lost to kim clijsters and serena williams . wozniacki was born in odense to polish catholic immigrant parents .
