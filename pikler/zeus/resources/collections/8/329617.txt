Sinbad the Sailor     sinbad the sailor is a fictional character in the `` one thousand and one nights '' ( `` the arabian nights '' ) . he is a sailor . his stories take place in the days of haroun al-rashid . it is possible that sinbad is of persian origin . he has many adventures . he meets monsters such as the old man of the sea and the roc . there are seven stories about sinbad 's journeys . they are sometimes a part of the scheherazade stories . the sinbad stories have been made into movies and animated cartoons . other websites . - story of sindbad the sailor - 21 illustrations by the german cartoon pioneer stefan mart , from `` tales of the nations '' ( 1933 ) - sindbad 's middle-european reincarnation
