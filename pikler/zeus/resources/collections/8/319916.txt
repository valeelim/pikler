Liechtenstein at the Olympics     liechtenstein at the olympics is a history which began in 1948. the international olympic committee 's official abbreviation for liechtenstein was lic . it is now lie . liechtenstein was first at the olympic games in 1936. they have been in most summer olympic games and winter olympic games since then . athletes from liechtenstein have won a total of nine medals , all in alpine skiing . it is the only nation to have won all of its medals at the winter olympics . the national olympic committee for liechtenstein was formed in 1935. xaver frick , a founding member of the country 's noc , is the only liechtenstein athlete to have been in both the summer and winter olympic games . related pages . - list of ioc country codes
