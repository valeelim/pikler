Scotch whisky     scotch whiskey is a whiskey that is distilled in and imported from scotland . they are a blend of malt and grain whiskeys and aged at least 3 years ( 4 years for exports ) . single malt scotch is made only from malted barley and no other grain , sugar , or fermentable material . they are made at the same distillery and not blended with any other scotch . most single malt scotches are aged an average of 8 to 12 years .
