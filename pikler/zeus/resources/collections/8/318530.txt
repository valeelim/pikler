Dean Edwards     dean edwards ( born july 30 , 1970 ) is an american actor and comedian . edwards joined the cast of `` saturday night live '' in 2001. he created several impressions . these include michael jackson , wayne brady , chris tucker , don cheadle , colin powell , serena williams , nipsey russell , denzel washington , grace jones , and billy ocean . chris tucker was edwards ' first impression . this was also the an impression done by jay pharoah on his first `` snl '' episode on september 25 , 2010. even though edwards was very good at impressions , he was not used much during his time working on the program . he left after the program 's 28th season . he said it was because of creative differences . he also appeared in `` tony n ' tina 's wedding '' , `` the sopranos '' , `` robotomy '' , and `` scared shrekless '' . in `` scared shrekless '' and `` thriller night '' , he voiced the part of donkey . many feel that edwards voice and style is very similar to that of eddie murphy . on youtube , edwards currently has his own channel known as `` deanedwardscomedy '' .
