Alexander Auldjo     alexander auldjo ( october 21 , 1758 â€“ may 21 , 1821 ) was a businessman and political figure in lower canada . auldjo was born in aberdeen , scotland in 1758. he came to montreal around 1778. he became a fur trader . later he exported wheat and imported goods from england . in 1796 , he was elected to the legislative assembly of lower canada for montreal west . he also became a justice of the peace . auldjo was promoted to lieutenant colonel during the war of 1812. in 1817 , he became a shareholder in the bank of montreal . his brother george was lord provost of aberdeen .
