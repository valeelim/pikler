Marilyn Chambers     marilyn ann chambers ( april 22 , 1952 - april 12 , 2009 ) was an american pornographic actress , exotic dancer and fashion model . she was born marilyn ann briggs in providence , rhode island . she was raised in westport , connecticut . she was notable for performing in many pornographic movies , including `` behind the green door '' and mainstream movies , including `` rabid '' . chambers died in santa clarita , california due to cerebral hemorrhage and aneurysm cause by heart disease .
