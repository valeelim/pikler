Jamboree     a jamboree is a scouting event . it is a large meeting of scouts from across a country or many countries . the first world scout jamboree was in 1920 , in the united kingdom . since then , there have been 22 world jamborees , hosted in different countries . it is usually every four years . the next major jamboree will be in sri lanka on 29 april 2012. over 200 countries will participate and more than 5000 scouts from around the globe for this international event .
