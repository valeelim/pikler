Triglav     triglav is a mountain in the alps . it is the highest mountain in slovenia . it is also the highest peak of the julian alps , in the southeastern part of the alps . its name means `` three-headed '' in slovene . while this can describe the mountain , it is more likely the mountain was named for the old slovene god , triglav . the mountain is shown on the flag of slovenia . this mountain is in a national park .
