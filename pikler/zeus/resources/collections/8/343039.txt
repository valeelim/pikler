Jello Biafra     jello biafra ( born eric reed boucher ; june 17 , 1958 ) is an american musician and spoken word artist . biafra was the lead singer and songwriter for san francisco punk rock band dead kennedys . he started the record label alternative tentacles with east bay ray in 1979. biafra is a member of the green party of the united states . he ran for president in 2000. other websites . - biafra 's label alternative tentacles
