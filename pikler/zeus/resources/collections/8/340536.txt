Ouargla Province     ouargla or warqla is a province in eastern algeria . its capital is ouargla . other important towns are temacine , touggourt , and hassi messaoud . it contains the issaouane erg desert . the province has 10 districts and 21 `` communes '' or municipalities . districts . 1. el borma 1. el hadjira 1. hassi messaoud 1. mégarine 1. n'goussa 1. ouargla 1. sidi khouiled 1. taïbet 1. témacine 1. touggourt
