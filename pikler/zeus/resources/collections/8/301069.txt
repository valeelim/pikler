12 Super Exitos     12 super exitos is a compilation album by mexican-american tejano pop singer , selena . the album sold more than 500,000 copies in the united states , being certified gold by the riaa . track listing . 1 . `` si una vez '' 1 . `` la llamada '' 1 . `` no debes jugar '' 1 . `` las cadenas '' 1 . `` techno cumbia '' 1 . `` tú robaste mi corazón '' ( with emilio navaira ) 1 . `` bidi bidi bom bom '' 1 . `` no quiero saber '' 1 . `` la carcacha '' 1 . `` missing my baby '' 1 . `` como la flor '' 1 . `` qué creías ''
