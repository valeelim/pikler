Tigran Sargsyan     tigran sargsyan ( born january 29 , 1960 ) was the prime minister of armenia from 9 april 2008 to 13 april 2014. from 1980 to 1983 , sargsyan attended leningrad 's voznesenski financial and economic institute . on april 9 , 2008 he became prime minister of armenia . he was selected by president serzh sargsyan . sargsyan is married and has three children ( daughter nelly sargsyan and two sons armen and abgar sargsyans ) and a grandchild . he is of no relation to the president of armenia serzh sargsyan .
