Lara Giddings     larissa tahireh giddings ( born 14 november 1972 ) was the 44th premier of tasmania . she became premier on 24 january 2011 when david bartlett resigned . she is the first woman to be premier of tasmania , as well as the youngest woman elected to an australian parliament . on 15 march 2014 , in the tasmanian state election the labor government led by lara giddings was defeated by the liberals led by will hodgman .
