São Tomé and Príncipe at the Olympics     são tomé and príncipe has been in four summer games . the first appearance was at the 1996 summer olympics in atlanta . they have never been in the winter games . the country has never won a medal . the national olympic committee for são tomé and príncipe was formed in 1979. the international olympic committee recognized them in 1993. related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - são tomé and príncipe profile at london2012.com - ioc page
