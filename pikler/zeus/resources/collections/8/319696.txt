Malta at the Olympics     malta at the olympics is a history which began in 1928. the international olympic committee 's official abbreviation for malta was mat . it is now mlt . teams from malta have been in 15 summer olympic games and 1 winter olympic games . as of 2014 no maltese athlete has won an olympic medal . the maltese olympic committee was formed in 1928 and recognized by the ioc in 1936. related pages . - list of ioc country codes
