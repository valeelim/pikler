Battle of Arras (1917)    battle of arras ( 1917 ) the battle of arras was a battle of the first world war . the battle was a british attack on the german defences near the french city of arras on the western front . the battle started on 9 april 1917. the british empire troops went quite far forward but they did not achieve a breakthrough . for the amount of ground gained , the british also lost many soldiers . the battle ended on 16 may 1917 and there was a stalemate .
