Bordj Bou Arréridj Province     bordj bou arréridj is a province in east algeria . it is about 200 km away from the capital algiers . the capital is bordj bou arreridj . the province has 10 districts and 34 `` communes '' or municipalities . districts . 1. aïn taghrout 1. bir kasd ali 1. bordj bou arréridj ( `` district-municipality '' ) 1. bordj ghedir 1. bordj zemmoura 1. djaâfra 1. el hamadia 1. mansourah 1. medjana 1. ras el oued
