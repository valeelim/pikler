Teneyevskoye Rural Settlement     teneyevskoye rural settlement ( ; , `` teni jal tărăkhĕ '' ) is an administrative and municipal division ( a rural settlement ) of alikovsky district of the chuvash republic , russia . it is located in the central part of the district . its administrative center is the locality ( a `` selo '' ) of teneyevo . rural settlement 's population : 900 ( 2006 est. ) . teneyevskoye rural settlement comprises five rural localities . the alikovo–tautovo highway crosses the territory of the rural settlement . further reading . - l. a. yefimov , `` alikovsky district '' ( `` элĕк енĕ '' ) , alikovo , 1994 . - `` аликовская энциклопедия '' ( alikovsky district 's encyclopedia ) , authors : yefimov l. a. , yefimov ye . l. , ananyev a. a. , terentyev g. k. cheboksary , 2009 , isbn 978-5-7670-1630-3. other websites . - official website of teneyevskoye rural settlement
