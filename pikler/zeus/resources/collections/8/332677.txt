Marksistskaya     marksistskaya is a station of moscow metro , kalininskaya line . it is situated between tretyakonskaya and ploshad il'icha stations . it was opened on 30 december 1979. it is connected with two other stations - taganskaya-koltsevaya and taganskaya-radialnaya . the station is about under the ground . it has only one exit - to marksistskaya street ( or taganskaya , if you cross marksistskaya street ) .
