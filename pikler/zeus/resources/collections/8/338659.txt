Pashayi language     pashayi - also known as pashai - is a language ( or a group of languages ) spoken by the pashai people in parts of kapisa , laghman , nuristan , kunar , and nangarhar provinces in northeastern afghanistan . it is spoken by over 216,842 people who are mostly muslim . most of them are bilingual in pashto . only 25 % are able to read . the pashai language was not written prior to 2003 .
