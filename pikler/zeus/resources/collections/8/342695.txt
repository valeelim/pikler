Robert Griffin III     robert lee griffin iii ( born february 12 , 1990 ) , nicknamed rg3 , is an american football quarterback for the washington redskins of the national football league ( nfl ) . he played college football for baylor university , and won the 2011 heisman trophy for being the best college player that year . he was the second person taken by the washington redskins with the second pick of the 2012 nfl draft.later in 2016 he was traded to the cleveland browns . he remains a free agent .
