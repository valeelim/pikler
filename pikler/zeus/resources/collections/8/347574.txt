Gomel     gomel ( also homiel , homel ) is the administrative center of gomel voblast . it is the second-largest city in belarus . it has a population of 482,652 ( 2009 census ) . gomel is in the southeastern part of the country , on the right bank of sozh river . it is to the south-east from minsk , to the east from brest , to the south from mogilev , to the west from bryansk and to the north from chernihiv . the city 's area is 121 km . other websites . - homel region : epicentre of troubles that bore celebrities
