Michael Ryder     michael wayne glen ryder ( born march 31 , 1980 in bonavista , newfoundland and labrador ) is a canadian professional ice hockey right winger . he currently plays for the new jersey devils of the national hockey league ( nhl ) . he has also played for the boston bruins , dallas stars , and montreal canadiens . he was drafted by the montreal canadiens with the 216th overall pick in the 1998 nhl entry draft . ryder played in the qmjhl for 3 seasons with the hull olympiques and 2 seasons in the echl with the tallahassee tiger sharks and mississippi sea wolves . he also played hockey formerly with the montreal canadiens for 4 seasons . he won the stanley cup with the bruins on june 15 , 2011 when the bruins defeated the vancouver canucks in game 7 by a score of 4-0 .
