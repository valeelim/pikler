Miller Park (Milwaukee)    miller park ( milwaukee ) miller park is a baseball park in milwaukee , wisconsin . it is the home field of the major league baseball team called the milwaukee brewers . it opened in 2001 to replace the old milwaukee county stadium that the brewers played in . the sponsor of the stadium is the miller brewing company . miller 's contract with the stadium was for $ 40 million , and it will last until the year 2020. miller park has a roof shaped like a fan that can be opened and closed depending on the weather . sources . - `` green cathedrals '' , by philip j. lowry
