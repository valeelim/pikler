Treaty of London (1913)    treaty of london ( 1913 ) the treaty of london was a treaty signed on 30 may . it was to move the borders of countries after the first balkan war . the balkan league won the war from the ottoman empire . the balkan league was serbia , greece , kingdom of bulgaria , and montenegro . one important border to decide was albania . there was a declaration of independence by albania on 28 november 1912. all of the ottoman empire , except albania , was given to the balkan league . because not everyone was happy with the treaty of london , the second balkan war started on june 13. peace came after thetreaty of bucharest on 12 august 1913 .
