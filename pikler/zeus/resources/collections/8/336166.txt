Tansei     , also known as tonsei , is the name of the second japanese artificial satellite which was put into orbit . the launch was a project of the institute of space and astronautical science of the university of tokyo . the name of the satellite means `` light blue '' , which is the school color of the university of tokyo . the satellite was sent into orbit on february 16 , 1971. other websites . - japan aerospace exploration agency ( jaxa ) , tansei ( ms-t1 ) mission profile
