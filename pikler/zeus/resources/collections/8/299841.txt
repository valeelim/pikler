Mercedes-AMG     mercedes-amg gmbh , commonly known as amg , is a part of the mercedes-benz car company . it makes high-performance luxury cars . amg models usually have more aggressive looks , higher performances , better handling and better stability than normal mercedes cars . apart from the v12 mercedes cars , amg cars are the most expensive and highest-performance of each mercedes series . they make cars from the small a45 amg all the way to the very large g63 6x6 .
