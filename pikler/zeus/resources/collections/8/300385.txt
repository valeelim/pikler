John Ritter     john ritter ( september 17 , 1948 - september 11 , 2003 ) was an american television and movie actor . he was featured in the 1970s television series `` the waltons '' . he performed in several situation comedies on the television , including `` three 's company '' . ritter was born in burbank , california and died there on september 11 , 2003 of aortic dissection caused by heart disease . he also did the voice for clifford the big red dog . ritter 's parents were tex ritter and dorothy fay . ritter 's sons jason ritter and tyler ritter are actors .
