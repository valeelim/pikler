Chris Kelly     chris kelly ( born november 11 , 1980 in toronto , ontario ) is a canadian professional ice hockey forward that currently plays for the boston bruins of the national hockey league ( nhl ) . he was drafted by the ottawa senators with the 94th overall pick in the 1999 nhl entry draft . kelly played in the ohl for 4 seasons with the london knights and 1 season with the sudbury wolves . he also played hockey formerly with the ottawa senators for 7 seasons . he won the stanley cup with the bruins on june 15 , 2011 when the bruins defeated the vancouver canucks in game 7 by a score of 4-0 .
