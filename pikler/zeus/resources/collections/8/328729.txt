Portland Winterhawks     the portland winterhawks are an american professional ice hockey team that began playing in 1976. they currently play in the western hockey league . they play their home games in portland , oregon at both the memorial coliseum and rose garden arenas . they have won 2 memorial cups in 1982 and 1997. they have also won 2 president 's cups in 1981 and 1997. they were originally known as the portland winter hawks until may 2009 when they took out the space . other websites . - official website
