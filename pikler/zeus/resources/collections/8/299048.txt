Cattistock     cattistock is a village in west dorset , england , in the upper reaches of the frome valley eight miles north west of dorchester . the village has a population of 440 ( 2002 ) . the church was rebuilt in the 19th century , by architects sir george gilbert scott and his son george gilbert scott junior . the perpendicular-styled tower was the work of the latter , and has led to the church being called the 'cathedral of the frome valley ' .
