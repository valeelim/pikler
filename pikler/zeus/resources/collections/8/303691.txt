Lewis acid     the concept of lewis acid and lewis base is one of acids and bases , which is independent of the idea of protons . gilbert newton lewis developed this idea in the early 20th century . in this concept , a lewis acid can accept and bind electrons . lewis acid is a substance that accepts two electrons to form a covalent bond . a lewis base is a substance that gives two electrons in the bonding . a lewis acid together with a lewis base will react to make a compound called a lewis adduct .
