Richard Colman     richard colman oam ( born 28 november 1984 ) is a disabled athlete from australia . he raced in the 2004 summer paralympics and he won a gold medal in the men 's 800 metres—t53 race , and a silver medal in the men 's 4 x 100 metre relay—t53-54 race . he raced at the 2008 summer paralympics and he won a silver medal in the men 's 200 metres—t53 race , and a bronze medal in the men 's 400 metres—t53 race .
