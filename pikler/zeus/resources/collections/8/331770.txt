Jason Donovan     jason donovan ( born 1 june 1968 ) is an australian actor and singer . donovan became famous when he played scott robinson in `` neighbours '' from 1986 to 1989. he was a contestant in the 2006 series of `` i 'm a celebrity ... get me out of here ! '' and in the 2011 series of `` strictly come dancing '' . he hosts a show on heart network radio in the uk on sunday afternoons . donovan was born in malvern , melbourne . he was taught at de la salle college . he is the son of actor terence donovan and newsreader sue mcintosh . the youngest of his three maternal half-sisters is actress-singer stephanie mcintosh . other websites . - official website
