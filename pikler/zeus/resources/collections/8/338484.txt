Noshaq     noshaq ( or nowshak ) is the highest mountain in afghanistan . it is the second highest independent peak of the hindu kush after tirich mir ( ) . noshaq is located in the northeastern corner of the country along the durand line . it is the westernmost peak in the world . the north and west sides of the mountain are in afghanistan . the south and eastern sides are in pakistan . easy access to the middle of noshaq would be in chitral , pakistan
