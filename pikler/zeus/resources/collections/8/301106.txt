Online game     online games are games usually on the internet to entertain people . they are usually games where you have to control a certain person or object to do something . most online games use the arrow keys , space bar and other keyboard characters to control the person or object . these can be referred to as mini games . the other type of online games is a larger network where players log in with a user name and the game lets them interact with other 'players ' and develop statistics , high scores , earn money , awards , badges or stamps . the games tend to be based on animals or virtual worlds .
