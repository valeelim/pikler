FanMail     fanmail is the third studio album by american band tlc . it was released on february 23 , 1999. it was released by leface records . on the first day of release , the album sold 318,000 copies in the united states . it debuted at number one on the us `` bollboard '' 200 and the top r & b/hip-hop albums chart . it also reached the top ten in canada , new zealand , and the united kingdom . the album was given eight grammy awards nominations . it won the grammy award for `` album of the year '' . according to nielsen soundscan , the album sold 4,700,000 copies in the united states . it was certified 6x platinum ( 6,000,000 copies shipped ) by the riaa .
