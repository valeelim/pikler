Turkmenistan at the Olympics     turkmenistan has been in 4 summer olympics . the first time was in 1996. they have never been in the winter olympics . turkmen athletes were with the soviet union and the unified team . no one representing turkmenistan has won an olympic medal . they are the only ex-soviet republic country not to win a medal . turkmenistan 's capital , ashgabat , has an olympic stadium even though they have not hosted the olympic games . the national olympic committee of turkmenistan was formed in 1990. it was recognized by the ioc in 1993. related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - turkmenistan profile at london2012.com
