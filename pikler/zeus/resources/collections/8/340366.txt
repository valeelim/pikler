Emblem of Algeria     the national emblem of algeria is the seal used by the government . other states use coats of arms . the current emblem began in 1976. the difference was changing the motto from french to arabic . on the emblem is the crescent that is also found on the flag of algeria . it is a symbol of islam . the text says `` the people 's democratic republic of algeria '' . the hand of fatima is in front of the atlas mountains . it is below a rising sun to show a new era . buildings are for industry and plants for agriculture .
