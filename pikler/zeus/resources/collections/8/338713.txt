Hotaki dynasty     the hotaki dynasty was founded in 1709 by mirwais hotak at kandahar , afghanistan . it was made up of ethnic afghans who belonged to the hotak tribe of pashtuns . they revolted and made their land independent from foreign ruler , but later they invaded persia and defeated the great persian empire of the shi'a safavids . mahmud hotaki , son of mirwais , ruled persia until he died in 1725 and then his cousin ashraf ruled until 1729 when nader shah afshar defeated him . the last king was hussain hotaki in kandahar until he was defeated by nader shah in 1738 .
