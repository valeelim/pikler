Miguel Montero     miguel angel montero ( born july 9 , 1983 in caracas , venezuela ) is a venezuelan professional baseball player . he currently plays as a catcher for the chicago cubs of major league baseball ( mlb ) . he has also played for the arizona diamondbacks from 2006 until 2014. he debuted for the diamondbacks on september 6 , 2006 in a game against the florida marlins . on december 9 , 2014 , the diamondbacks traded montero to the chicago cubs in exchange for minor leaguers jeferson mejia and zack godley .
