Winter sport     winter sports are sports played during winter . they often must be played on ice or snow . the international olympic comittee has the winter olympics , which are every four years . in the winter olympics , people try to win at winter sports for their country . cold countries , for example russia , canada and norway , are good at winter sports . winter sports include : - ice skating - skiing - snowboarding - sleighing - bobsleighing - ice hockey - bandy - curling - snowmobile
