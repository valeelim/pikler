Cassette single     a cassette single is a recording of a single song sold on a compact audio cassette . record companies introduced this sales format in the 1980s . at that time , vinyl record album sales were declining in favor of cassette recordings ; the cassette single was introduced to replace the vinyl 45 record in a similar way . the sales of cassette singles did not exceed vinyl single sales to the same extent as cassette albums had . as compact disc players became more common , cassette singles were for the most part replaced by cd singles .
