The Eminem Show     the eminem show is the fourth studio album by american rapper , eminem . `` the eminem show '' was released on may 28 , 2002 and was produced by dr. dre , eminem , jeff bass and mr. porter . the album was given 4 out of 5 stars by allmusic . on march 7 , 2011 , the album was certified ten-times-platinum by the recording industry association of america for sales of over ten million copies .
