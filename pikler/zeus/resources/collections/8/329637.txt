The Great Train Robbery     the great train robbery is a 1903 western silent movie . it was written , directed , produced , and photographed by edwin s. porter . it is 12 minutes long . it is thought to be an important step in making movies . it expanded upon porter 's previous movie `` life of an american fireman '' . the movie used many new moviemaking techniques . these techniques included cross cutting , camera movement , and on-location shooting . some prints of the movie were also hand colored in certain scenes . it was shot in milltown , new jersey . the movie has been chosen for preservation in the united states national film registry . other websites . - download from the library of congress ( in mpeg-1 , realvideo or quicktime format ) - great films : `` the great train robbery ''
