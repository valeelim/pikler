The Ballad of Mona Lisa     the ballad of mona lisa ( shortened as mona lisa ) is the lead single off the panic ! at the disco album `` vices & virtues '' released on february 1 , 2011 under fueled by ramen . the single was produced by butch walker and john feldmann and written by band members frontman brendon urie and drummer spencer smith . the song peaked at number 84 on the u.s. billboard hot 100 single 's chart . the song has received positive critical reviews upon its release .
