Ligeia     `` ligeia '' is a short story by edgar allan poe . it was first published in `` the american museum '' in 1838. it was revised and reprinted many times during poe 's lifetime . the story tells of an unnamed narrator 's beautiful wife ligeia . she composes the poem `` the conqueror worm '' before dying . the narrator then marries the lady rowena . she shortly sickens and dies . the narrator stays with her body overnight . she comes back from the dead as ligeia . criticism was positive . charles eames of `` the new world '' wrote , `` the force and boldness of the conception and the high artistic skill , with which the writer 's purpose is wrought out , are equally admirable . '' thomas dunn english wrote in october 1845 that `` ligeia '' was `` the most extraordinary , of its kind , of his productions '' . in 1964 , director roger corman adapted the story to a movie , `` the tomb of ligeia '' , starring vincent price .
