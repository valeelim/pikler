Asbestosis     asbestosis is a disease of the lungs caused by inhaling fibers of asbestos . it is not to be confused with mesothelioma , a cancer of the lungs which is also caused by inhaling asbestos fibers . asbestosis has been the subject of many lawsuits , and many lawyers specialize in asbestosis and mesothelioma . related pages . - pneumoconiosis - silicosis
