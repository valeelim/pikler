Sierra Leone at the Olympics     sierra leone at the olympics is a history which began in 1968. the international olympic committee 's official abbreviation for sierra leone is sle . teams from sierra leone have been to every summer olympic games held since 1968 , with the exception of 1972 and 1976. they have never won an olympic medal . no athletes from sierra leone have been in any winter olympic games . related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - sierra leone profile at london2012.com - ioc page
