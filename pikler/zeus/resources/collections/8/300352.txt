Steve Earle     stephen fain `` steve '' earle ( born january 17 , 1955 in hampton , virginia ) is an american country/rock singer-songwriter . he has received a total of fourteen nominations for grammy awards and has won three ; one for best contemporary folk album for `` the revolution starts now '' , one for best contemporary folk/americana album ( vocal or instrumental ) for `` washington square serenade '' and one for best contemporary folk/americana album for `` townes '' . he is also known for being a political activist and his views towards anti-war and anti-death penalty policies . other websites . - official website
