Bulgaria at the Olympics     bulgaria was first at the olympic games at the very first games in 1896. the international olympic committee 's official abbreviation for bulgaria is bul . the bulgarian olympic committee was formed in 1923. they first sent a team to the summer olympic games in 1924. the nation has been in every summer games since except for 1932 ( during the great depression ) , 1948 ( after world war ii ) , and 1984. bulgaria was first in the winter olympic games in 1936 , and has been in every winter games since then . bulgarian athletes have won a total of 218 medals , with wrestling and weightlifting the most . related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - bulgaria profile at london2012.com - bulgaria at olympics.org
