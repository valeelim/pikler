Lionel Sackville, 1st Duke of Dorset    lionel sackville , 1st duke of dorset lionel cranfield sackville , 1st duke of dorset , pc ( 18 january 1688 - 10 october 1765 ) was an english politician and lord lieutenant of ireland . dorset was lord lieutenant of ireland twice . first from 1731 to 1737 and then again from 1751 to 1755 .
