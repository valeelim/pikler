Laghouat Province     laghouat is a province ( `` wilaya '' ) in central algeria . the name means `` the oases '' . the capital is laghouat . other localities include aflou , ain madhi , kourdane and makhareg . the province is divided into 10 districts . there are also 24 communes or municipalities . districts . 1. aflou 1. aïn mahdi 1. brida 1. el ghicha 1. gueltet sidi saâd 1. hassi r'mel 1. ksar el hirane 1. laghouat 1. oued morra 1. sidi makhlouf
