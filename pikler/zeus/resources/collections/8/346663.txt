2010 Stanley Cup Finals     the 2010 stanley cup final was the best-of-seven nhl championship series of the 2010 stanley cup playoffs . it was between the western conference champion chicago blackhawks and the eastern conference champion philadelphia flyers . it was chicago 's eleventh time in the final . the last time was in 1992 with a loss to the pittsburgh penguins . it was philadelphia 's eighth time in the final . the last time was in 1997 with a loss to the detroit red wings . chicago beat philadelphia four games to two to win their fourth stanley cup . blackhawks captain jonathan toews won the most valuable player . he was the first blackhawks player to win this honor .
