Derby Sketching Club     the derby sketching club is a members ' club meeting in derby , england . it was founded in 1887 , still providing members with facilities . in 1887 , a group of young men held a meeting at the county hotel in derby and founded the derby sketching club . they wanted to have a place where they and others could work , share their interest in painting and sketching . the fee for one year was two shillings and sixpence . their first exhibition was held in january 1889 , at the athenaeum rooms in derby . in 1922 , the derby ladies art group was formed . the two groups first held a joint exhibition in 1951 for the festival of britain . they finally merged in 1966. the art collector alfred e. goodey . collected some of their work about derby made by sketching club members . in the 1940s he presented the paintings to derby museum and art gallery . there are regular sessions for members- for lifedrawing , and portrait painting and still life . there are guest lectures and exhibitions and members use the website to promote their work .
