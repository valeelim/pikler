The Crystals     the crystals are a new york city based musical group . they were one of the defining acts during the girl group era of the 1960s . they formed in 1961 with five girls : barbara alston , mary thomas , dolores `` dee dee '' kenniebrew , myrna giraud and patricia `` patsy '' wright . the group were later joined by darlene love for a short time . dee dee kenniebrew still performs in the group , now a trio . their 1961â€“1964 chart hits , included `` there 's no other ( like my baby ) '' , `` uptown '' , `` he 's sure the boy i love '' , `` he 's a rebel '' , `` da doo ron ron ( when he walked me home ) '' and `` then he kissed me '' . phil spector was their producer from 1962. one of their numbers in 1962 , by carole king and gerry goffin , was `` he hit me ( and it felt like a kiss ) '' . it was based on their babysitter 's account . it was withdrawn after complaints to radio stations over the lyrics . the recordings had three successive female lead singers , and were all produced by phil spector . the latter three songs were originally ranked # 267 , # 114 , and # 493 on `` rolling stone '' magazine 's list of the 500 greatest songs of all time . however , two songs were dropped from the magazine 's 2010 update .
