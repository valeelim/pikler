White Trash, Two Heebs and a Bean    white trash , two heebs and a bean white trash , two heebs and a bean is the fourth studio album by american punk rock band , nofx . `` white trash , two heebs and a bean '' was released on november 5 , 1992. the producer of the album was donnell cameron , nofx . the original album title was going to be `` white trash , two kikes , and a spic '' but guitar player eric melvin changed it after feeling guilty . it is also the first album with guitar player el hefe .
