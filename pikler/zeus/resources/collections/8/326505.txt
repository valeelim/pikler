Oriole Park at Camden Yards     oriole park at camden yards is a baseball park in baltimore , maryland . it is the home field of the major league baseball team called the baltimore orioles . it opened in 1992 , replacing the orioles ' long-time home memorial stadium . the ballpark was built on the site of a railroad area called camden yards . the long brick building outside the right field area was part of that rail yard . the birthplace of babe ruth is also nearby . it is a museum which is a couple of blocks west of the ballpark . references . - `` green cathedrals '' , by philip j. lowry
