PROTECT IP Act     the protect intellectual property act ( preventing real online threats to economic creativity and theft of intellectual property act ) , also called pipa , is a proposed american law to stop copyright infringement on the internet . there are critics who point out that the proposed law has hidden risks and consequences which are unplanned . some people and businesses support the law while others are alarmed by the law . supporters worry that the current copyright law is not effective in shutting down piracy websites . opponents worry that the law is a kind of censorship . it is hard to tell which side is correct because the language in the law is still being written . related pages . - stop online piracy act ( sopa ) other websites . - text of protect intellectual property act ; s. 968
