Matilda (1996 movie)    matilda ( 1996 movie ) matilda is a fantasy movie . danny devito directed the movie . the story is based on the novel called matilda by roald dahl . tristar pictures released the movie on july 28 , 1996. mara wilson , danny devito , rhea perlman , embeth davidtz , and pam ferris are the stars of the movie . 6 1/2 ( 6 in a half ) -7 year old kindergardener named matilda ( who lives with her abusive father who abuses her ) got played by mara wilson .
