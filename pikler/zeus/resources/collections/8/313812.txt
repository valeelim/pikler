Saline (medicine)    saline ( medicine ) in medicine , saline refers to any solution of salt in water ; that is , salt water . such solutions are sterile when they are to be placed into the body by infusion or injection . if the solution has more salt in it than is found in the human body , it is called `` hypertonic saline '' . the normal salt level is 0.9 % and any salt amount more than 0.9 % is called hypertonic .
