Heidi Fleiss     heidi lynne fleiss ( born december 30 , 1965 in los angeles ) is an american television personality and columnist , regularly featured in american media . she is often called `` hollywood madam '' ( formerly she was a madam ) and is a periodic guest on fox news programs . her father was paul m. fleiss . she is of jewish descent . fleiss had been convicted of tax evasion and spent some time in prison .
