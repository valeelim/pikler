Black Butler     black butler is a manga series by yana toboso . the manga started in 2006. in victorian era london , the butler sebastian takes care of his 12 year old master , ciel phantomhive . ciel 's parents were killed when he was 10 years old . he made a deal with a demon to help him get revenge on his enemies . the demon butler can have his soul in return . a radio drama was released on cds on august 10 , 2007 and november 26 , 2008. the anime was first shown in october 2008. the english version is produced by funimation . two musicals are based on the manga called `` that butler , friendship '' and `` musical black butler , the most beautiful death in the world , a thousand souls and the fallen grim reaper '' . a video game called `` black butler : phantom & ghost '' was released in 2009. the manga has printed over 10 million copies in japan . a music distributor in japan took a poll of anime characters that people would like to marry . the demon butler sebastian was included in the final results .
