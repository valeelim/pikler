Matt Clement     matthew paul clement ( born august 12 , 1974 , in butler , pennsylvania ) is a former major league baseball starting pitcher . clement played for the san diego padres , florida marlins , chicago cubs and boston red sox . while on the red sox active roster , he was injured all of the 2007 season . he batted and threw right-handed . on april 5 , 2009 , clement said he was no longer going to play baseball .
