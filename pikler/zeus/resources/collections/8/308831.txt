Barrio Boyzz     the barrio boyzz were a puerto rican boy band . the group was founded in new york city by joe jacket ( of new kids on the block ) . they were signed to sbk records in 1991. they also starred in the 1994 movie `` i like it like that '' . their duet with american singer selena called `` donde quiera que estes '' peaked at number-one on the `` billboard '' hot latin tracks in 1994. the band disbanded in 2001 .
