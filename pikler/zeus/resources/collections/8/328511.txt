Type 96 150 mm howitzer     the , also known as a `` 15-cm '' gun , was a howitzer used by the imperial japanese army ( ija ) . this artillery piece was designed to replace the type 4 150 mm howitzer in front line combat . type 96s fired the same ammunition as the type 4s . type 96s began to be built in 1937. a total of 440 units were produced . a medium artillery regiment had 24 type 96s . the type 96 15 cm howitzer was used in the second sino-japanese war and in world war ii . the 96s were among japan 's most used artillery weapons . one of the type 96s is preserved at the yasukuni shrine in tokyo . related pages . - artillery of japan - list of howitzers other websites . - taki 's imperial japanese army page - us technical manual e 30-480
