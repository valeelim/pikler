Thor Vilhjalmsson     thor vilhjalmsson ( 12 august 1925 - 2 march 2011 ) was an icelandic writer . his first novel , `` maðurinn er alltaf einn ( man is always alone ) '' was published in 1950. in 1988 he won the nordic council literary prize for his novel `` grámosinn glóir ( justice undone ) '' . vilhjalmsson was born in edinburgh , scotland in 1925. he studied at the university of iceland in 1944-1946 , at the university of nottingham in 1946-1947 and at the sorbonne in paris from 1947-1952. he worked as a librarian at the national library of iceland in 1953 and at the national theatre 1955-1957. he was involved in many art groups and events including the reykjavik writers festival , the writer 's union of iceland , the federation of icelandic artists , the reykjavík arts festival , the reykjavík film festival and the reykjavík judo-society .
