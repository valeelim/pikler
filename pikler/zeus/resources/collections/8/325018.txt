Glutamic acid     glutamic acid is a non-essential amino acid . its salts are known as glutamates . glutamate are important neurotransmitters . they are important for long-term potentiation , learning and memory . glutamates are also used as food additives : they make flavour better . the e numbers 620 to 625 have been assigned to the most important glutamates . they are commonly found in convenience food and asian cuisine .
