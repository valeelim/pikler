Saint-Cyr-l'École    saint-cyr-l'école saint-cyr-l'école is a commune in the western suburbs of paris , france . it is 21.4 km ( 13.3 mi ) from the center of paris . in 2006 , about 16,365 people lived here . from 1993 to 1996 , the main campus of the service d'exploitation de la formation aéronautique was in the commune .
