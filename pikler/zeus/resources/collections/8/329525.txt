Philip Larkin     philip arthur larkin ch , cbe , frsl ( 9 august 1922 - 2 december 1985 ) was a english poet and novelist . larkin was born and raised in coventry , warwickshire , where he attended primary and secondary school . he graduated from st john 's college , oxford with a double first in english language and literature . larkin spent the latter years of his life in hull , where he was a librarian at the university of hull . larkin died in hull , humberside , of oesophageal cancer .
