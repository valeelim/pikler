Prescott Bush     prescott bush ( 1895-1972 ) was a republican member of the us senate from connecticut . he was the father of american president george h. w. bush and the grandfather of president george w. bush and governor of florida and presidential candidate jeb bush . he also worked with brown brothers harriman . he went to yale university and was a member of skull and bones and the yale glee club . he was treasurer for planned parenthood .
