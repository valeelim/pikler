Peter Yates     peter yates ( 24 july 1929 - 9 january 2011 ) was an english movie director and producer . he was born in aldershot , hampshire . he was nominated for the academy award for the movies `` breaking away '' and `` the dresser '' . he was also nominated several time for bafta awards . one of his best-known movies is `` bullitt '' ( 1968 ) , starring steve mcqueen . yates died in london .
