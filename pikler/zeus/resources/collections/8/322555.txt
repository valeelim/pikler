Secrets of the Furious Five     kung fu panda : secrets of the furious five is an annie award-winning american animated short . it was made by dreamworks animation . the movie is a semi-sequel ( or spin-off ) to the animated movie `` kung fu panda '' . it is a part of the original movie 's deluxe dvd release . it was later broadcast on nbc on february 26 , 2009. the short is now available as a separate dvd as of march 24 , 2009. also , ever since it was released , the short received positive reviews and won the annie award for best animated short . other websites . - `` kung fu panda '' - official website
