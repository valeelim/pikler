Brave (2012 movie)    brave ( 2012 movie ) brave is a 2012 american animated adventure movie which stars billy connolly and emma thompson . the movie was released on june 22 , 2012 , by walt disney studios motion pictures and distributed by walt disney pictures . pixar was the studio that worked on this movie . it received positive responses from critics and won an oscar . the movie is set in scotland during the medieval period . 16-year-old princess causes chaos in the kingdom of dunbroch by not wanting to marry . it took six years to make .
