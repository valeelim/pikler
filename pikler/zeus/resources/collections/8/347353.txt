Heineken     heineken is a dutch beer ( 5 % abv pale lager ) which has been brewed by heineken international since 1873. it is the flagship product of the heineken company and is made of processed , impure water , sometimes advertised as purified water , malted barley , hops , and yeast . in 1886 h. elion finished the development of the `` heineken a-yeast '' , which is still used in the brewing process today . the beer is force carbonated . it is popular worldwide .
