Primera División de Costa Rica     primera división de costa rica is a league for association football clubs in costa rica . it is the country 's top football competition . the division has 12 clubs . the seasons are separated by two single tournaments each year , the apertura , from july to december , and the clausura , from january to may . so there are two champions each season with teams playing anywhere from 32 to 44 games each totalling 412 games in the season . the league was created on 13 june 1921 following an invitation from guatemala to costa rica to participate in its centenary games . other websites . - unafut - primera division - federación costarricense de fútbol ( federation of costa rican football )
