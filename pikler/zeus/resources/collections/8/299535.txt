Bad Lieutenant: Port of Call New Orleans    bad lieutenant : port of call new orleans bad lieutenant : port of call new orleans is a 2009 american crime drama movie . it was directed by werner herzog . it has nicolas cage playing a bad lieutenant who bullies people , abuses heroin , painkillers and cocaine and is corrupt on many levels . the movie is similar to but neither a sequel nor remake for abel ferrara 's 1992 classic movie `` bad lieutenant '' , which starred harvey keitel as a nameless police lieutenant . the 1992 movie was rated nc-17 , whereas the 2009 movie received an r rating . also starring in the 2009 movie is eva mendes ; she plays the lieutenant 's girlfriend .
