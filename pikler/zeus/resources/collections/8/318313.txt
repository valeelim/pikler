The Birds of America     the birds of america is a book by naturalist and painter john james audubon . it includes illustrations of a wide variety of birds of the united states . it was first published between 1827 and 1838 , in edinburgh and london . the work is of hand-coloured , life-size prints , made from engraved plates , measuring around . it includes images of six birds that are now extinct : carolina parakeet , passenger pigeon , labrador duck , great auk , esquimaux curlew , and pinnated grouse .
