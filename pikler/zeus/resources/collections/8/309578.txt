Order of the Solar Temple     the order of the solar temple ( or ordre du temple solaire , international chivalric organization of the solar tradition ) was a secret society that existed in the second half of the 20th century and that was based on the belief that the knights templar still existed . ots was started by joseph di mambro and luc jouret in 1984 in geneva as `` l'ordre international chevaleresque de tradition solaire '' ( oicts ) and renamed `` ordre du temple solaire '' . ots is considered to be a sect . the organisation was involved in mass suicides in france , switzerland and canada . the beliefs of the society were a mix of protestant christianity and new age philosophy ; it also used and changed different freemason rituals .
