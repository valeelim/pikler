Adam McQuaid     adam mcquaid ( born october 12 , 1986 in charlottetown , prince edward island ) is an canadian professional ice hockey defenceman . he currently plays for the boston bruins . mcquaid played hockey in the ohl for 4 seasons with the sudbury wolves . he was drafted by the columbus blue jackets with the 55th overall pick in the 2005 nhl entry draft but never played a game with them and was traded to the bruins for a fifth-round pick . he won the stanley cup with the bruins on june 15 , 2011 when the bruins defeated the vancouver canucks in game 7 by a score of 4-0 .
