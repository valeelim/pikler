Bryan Trottier     bryan john trottier ( born july 17 , 1956 ) is a canadian retired professional ice hockey centre who played 18 seasons in the national hockey league ( nhl ) for the new york islanders and pittsburgh penguins . he won four stanley cups with the islanders , two with the penguins and one as an assistant coach with the colorado avalanche . on august 4 , 2014 , trottier was hired to be an assistant coach of the buffalo sabres .
