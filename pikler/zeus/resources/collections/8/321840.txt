Civic Star     the civic star ( ) is a french award . it was established by the académie française in 1930. the star distinguishes those who contribute to the enrichment of the community , improvement of social life , and the progress of humanity . it is particularly for those whose lives are of hard work , sacrifice and would remain unknown without this award . it is divided into four degrees : bronze , silver , gold and silver-gilt . the star 's motto is `` '' ( 'to honor the civic virtues - serving national prestige ' ) .
