Búri     búri was the first norse god . he became the father of the other gods . he was married to hárm . he ruled prehistory . he gave birth to the nephaeleus . his reign ended when thor and loki were born . búri was born when the divine cow , audhumla , licked him out of his icy prison . in his life , búri lived alongside the ice giant ymir . ymir was also released from ice in a similar way .
