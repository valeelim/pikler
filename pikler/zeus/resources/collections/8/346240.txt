Eduardo Paolozzi     sir eduardo luigi paolozzi , kbe , ra ( edinburgh , 7 march 1924 – london , 22 april 2005 ) , was a scottish sculptor and artist . he was the eldest son of italian immigrants . paolozzi studied at the edinburgh college of art in 1943 , then moved to london . he was briefly at the st martin 's school of art in 1944 , and then at the slade school of fine art at university college london from 1944 to 1947. he lived in paris from 1947–1949 . there he met alberto giacometti , jean arp , constantin brâncuşi , georges braque and fernand léger . they were a big influence on his later work . paolozzi was the founder of the pop art movement . paolozzi 's `` i was a rich man 's plaything '' ( 1947 ) is considered the first standard bearer of pop art and first to display the word `` pop '' . paolozzi showed the collage in 1952 as part of his groundbreaking `` bunk ! '' series at the independent group meeting in london . he was a major figure in the international art sphere . paolozzi investigated how we fitted into the modern world . he revealed our fragmented civilization through imagination and fantasy .
