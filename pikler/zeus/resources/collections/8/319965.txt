Jurchen people     the jurchens were a tungusic people who lived in manchuria in present-day northeast china . they were known as jurchens until the 17th century when they became known as the `` manchu '' . the jurchens established the jin dynasty . the joseon dynasty of korea placed great value on a policy of `` neighborly relations '' ( `` gyorin '' or `` kyorin '' ) with the jurchen people .
