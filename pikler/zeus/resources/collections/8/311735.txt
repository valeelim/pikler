Stefan Maierhofer     stefan maierhofer ( born 16 august 1982 ) is a football player from austria . he plays as striker . since 2011 he plays for f.c . red bull salzburg . maierhofer played his first matches for vienna and sv langrohr . at the age of 23 he went to fc bayern münchen . he played in the second team for 2 seasons . he also played 2 matches in the first squad . he scored 21 goals in 42 regionalliga appearances . he was top goalscorer for the squad in both seasons . in january 2007 maierhofer played half a season for tus koblenz in the second league of germany . the next season he signed a contract with spvgg greuther fürth . the club sent him on a six-month loan to austrian bundesliga 's rapid vienna in january 2008. rapid then signed a contract with him . in 2009 he went to the premier league . he played for wolverhampton wanderers . in 2010 he went on loan to msv duisburg . with the team he reached the german cup final . in august 2011 he returned to austria and signed with f. c. red bull salzburg .
