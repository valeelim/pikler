Brian Williams     brian douglas williams ( born may 5 , 1959 ) is the anchor of nbc nightly news , the evening newscast of nbc . he used to anchor the nbc news broadcast `` the news with brian williams '' on nbc 's sister networks cnbc and msnbc . on february 10 , 2015 , williams was suspended for six months without pay . that was done because he reported false stories regarding his trips to iraq in 2003 and to new orleans during the aftermath of hurricane katrina in 2005 .
