Samoa at the Olympics     samoa was first at the olympic games in 1984. they have been in every summer olympic games since then . the nation has never been in the winter olympic games . as of 2011 , no athlete competing for samoa has ever won an olympic medal . the national olympic committee for samoa was formed in 1983 and recognized by the international olympic committee the same year . related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - samoa profile at london2012.com
