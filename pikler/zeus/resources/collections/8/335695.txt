Brodus Clay     george murdoch ( born february 21 , 1980 ) is an american professional wrestler and actor . he is better known by his ring name , brodus clay . he is best known for his time working with the wwe . before he became a wrestler he worked as a bodyguard for snoop dogg . murdoch took part as brodus clay in 4th season of `` wwe nxt '' . in nxt , clay was first helped by kayfabe couple ted dibiase and maryse . they were replaced later by the duo alberto del rio and `` ricardo rodriguez '' . clay lost the final against johnny curtis in the latest nxt poll . on 9 january , clay made ​​his re-debut on wwe raw super show , where he won his match against curt hawkins . that friday on wwe smackdown , he won his second match after just 13 seconds against tyson kidd . after that match he was better known as the `` funkasaurus from planet funk '' . on june 12 , 2014 , clay was released from his wwe contract . other websites . - official website
