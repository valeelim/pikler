Vanguard     a vanguard is a ( usually small ) group of people who lead a movement . it comes from two old french words , `` avant '' ( front ) and `` garde '' ( guard ) . in military terms , a vanguard is a group of soldiers who scout ahead to protect an army from surprise attacks . in the political philosophy of leninism `` vanguard '' refers to the `` vanguard party '' . this would be a small group of dedicated revolutionaries who would lead the communist movement in russia .
