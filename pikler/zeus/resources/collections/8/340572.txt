Aïn Defla Province     aïn defla is a province in northern algeria . it is southwest of algiers , the capital . towns in ain delfa include miliana , hammam righa , and aïn torki . the province has 14 districts and 36 municipalities . the districts are : 1. aïn defla 1. aïn lechiakh 1. bathia 1. bordj el amir khaled 1. boumedfaâ 1. djendel 1. djelida 1. el abadia 1. el amra 1. el attaf 1. hammam righa 1. khemis 1. miliana 1. rouina
