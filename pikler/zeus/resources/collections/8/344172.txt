Certificat de formation à la sécurité     the certificat de formation à la sécurité ( cfs ) ( in english safety training certificate ) is the french national degree required to be flight attendant in france . it replaces the `` certificat de sécurité sauvetage '' ( css ) since the 16th of july 2008. it consists of a theoretical and a practical part , each of them contains teaching in aviation safety and first aid .
