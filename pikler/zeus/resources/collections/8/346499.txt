Dot-winged Antwren     the dot-winged antwren ( `` microrhopias quixensis '' ) is a passerine bird in the antbird family . it used to be known sometimes as the velvety antwren . it is found in tropical central and south america from southeastern mexico south to western ecuador , northern bolivia and central brazil . the male dot-winged antwren is blackish , while the female has brownish underparts . it is the only member of the genus `` microrhopias '' . references . - stiles and skutch , `` a guide to the birds of costa rica '' isbn 0-8014-9600-4
