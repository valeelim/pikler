Bié Province     bié is a province of angola . it is on the bié plateau in central part of country . the capital is kuito . there are about 800,000 people . the climate of bié is fresh . the large amount of rainfall makes possible the culture of corn , sugar cane , rice , coffee and peanuts . the ground is one of the most fertile of angola . the cuanza river flows through the province . bié was greatly affected by the civil war . agriculture stopped in several areas . some of the rural population fled to the cities . the province capital kuito was partly destroyed by bombing .
