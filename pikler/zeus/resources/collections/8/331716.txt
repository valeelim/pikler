Kyūshū Shinkansen     the is a japanese high-speed railway line between fukuoka and kagoshima in kyushu . the line is operated by the kyushu railway company ( jr kyushu ) . the southern 127 km opened on 13 march 2004. the northern 130 km section opened on 12 march 2011. service began with a quiet start because of the 2011 tōhoku earthquake and tsunami .
