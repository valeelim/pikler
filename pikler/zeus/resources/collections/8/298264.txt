Thrips     thrips are an order of insects , the thysanoptera . they are tiny , slender insects with fringed wings . other common names for them are thunderflies , thunderbugs , storm flies , thunderblights , and corn lice . the scientific name for the order thysanoptera comes from the greek `` thysanos '' ( fringe ) and `` pteron '' ( wing ) . thrips feed on a large variety of plants and animals by puncturing them and sucking up the contents . thrips species are pests if they feed on plants with commercial value . thrips found in the british isles are tiny insects , just 1-2mm long , but in other parts of the world they can be up to 14 mm . they have two pairs of narrow , fringed wings , although some are wingless . more than 6,000 species of thrips are known around the world , with over 300 of these in europe and about 150 native to britain .
