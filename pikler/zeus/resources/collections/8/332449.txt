Life of an American Fireman     life of an american fireman is a silent movie . it was made by edwin s. porter . it was shot late in 1902. it was distributed early in 1903. it is one of the earliest american storytelling movies . it is about the rescue of a woman and child from a burning building . it has seven scenes and nine shots . movie expert charles musser points out that this movie shows that the social role of firefighters was changing at the time it was made .
