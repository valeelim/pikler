Rudolph the Red-Nosed Reindeer     rudolph the red-nosed reindeer is a reindeer with a bright red nose who first appeared in 1939 in booklets . in the story , rudolph gets teased by other reindeer until one foggy christmas eve santa claus comes around , asking him to guide santa 's sleigh . a christmas song based on the story has been covered by such artists as bing crosby , dean martin , barry manilow and the supremes over the decades .
