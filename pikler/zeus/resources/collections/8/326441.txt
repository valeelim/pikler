Antonio Brown     antonio brown ( born july 10 , 1988 ) is an american football wide receiver for the pittsburgh steelers of the national football league . he was drafted by pittsburgh in the 6th round of the 2010 nfl draft . brown played college football at the university of central michigan . brown played college football for the university of central michigan . he was noted for his versatility and was even an all-american . brown was drafted by the pittsburgh steelers in the 6th round of the 2010 nfl draft . brown spent time playing as a wide receiver and a returner . he returned a kickoff 89 yards for a touchdown in his rookie season . brown is famous for a 58-yard third-down helmet reception against lardarius webb of the baltimore ravens in a playoff win . in 2011 , brown beat out veteran hines ward and fellow 2nd year receiver emmanuel sanders and became a starter alongside mike wallace . as well as returning kicks and punts very successfully , brown had 69 catches for 1,108 yards and two touchdowns . brown was named the steelers ' team mvp that season .
