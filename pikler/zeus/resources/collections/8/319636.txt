Castlereagh River     the castlereagh river is an inland river in new south wales , australia . it begins in the warrumbungles , a mountain range , and flows east towards coonabarabran . it turns to the south , and then flows north west through gilgandra and coonamble , before joining the macquarie river . the length of the river is about . the first europen to see the castlereagh river was george william evans in 1818 during john oxley 's expedition . it was later explored by charles sturt in 1829. the river is named after lord castlereagh , a british politician who was the secretary of state for the colonies .
