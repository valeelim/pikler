Eihō     events of the `` eihō '' era . - 26 may 1081 ( `` eihō 1 , 15th day of the 4th month '' ) : the buddhist temple of miidera was set on fire by the monks of a rival sect . - 12 july 1081 ( `` eihō 1 , 4th day of the 6th month '' ) : miidera was burned again by monks from mt . hiei . - 1083 ( `` eihō 3 , 10th month '' ) : at hosshō-ji , construction begins on a nine-story pagoda . related pages . - heian period other websites . - national diet library , `` the japanese calendar '' -- historical overview plus illustrative images from library 's collection
