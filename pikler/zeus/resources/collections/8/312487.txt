Turk Telekom Arena     ali sami yen spor kompleksi - türk telekom arena is a new multi-purpose stadium in the şişli district of istanbul , turkey . it is the new home ground for galatasaray s.k. , replacing the old ali sami yen stadium . the stadium has an all seated capacity of 52,652. dimensions . - stadium : 195,000 m² - 228m x 190m x 70m - playing level : 120 m x 83 m - gross grass area : 111 m x 72 m - playing field : 105 m x 68 m construction . - total concrete used during stadium construction : 190,000 m³ - total steel used during stadium construction : 35,000 tonnes - total steel used for the roof : 5,500 tonnes
