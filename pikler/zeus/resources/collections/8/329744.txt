La Dispute     la dispute is an american post-hardcore band , formed in 2004 in grand rapids , michigan . they have released two albums and seven eps . the band consists of jordan dreyer ( vocals ) , brad vander lugt ( drums ) , chad sterenberg and kevin whittemore ( guitars ) and adam vass ( bass ) . la dispute released two full-leght albums , three singles , one musical video , five eps and two slipts . the genre of the band is post-hardcore and experimental rock , with blues and spoken word . they also play music which sound like screamo , progressive rock , post-rock , melodic hardcore , metalcore , emo , hardcore punk . and `` screamo-revival '' . the band influences are thursday , refused , at the drive-in and glassjaw . brad vander lugt cites blues and jazz influences too . members . - current members - jordan dreyer - lead vocals - chad sterenberg - rhythm guitar , synthesizers , trumpet , glockenspiel , percussion , backing vocals - kevin whittemore - lead guitar , backing vocals - adam vass - bass , baritone guitar , additional guitars , backing vocals
