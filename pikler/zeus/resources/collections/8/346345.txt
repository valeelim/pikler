Liberty Kid     liberty kid is a 2007 low budget american movie . it is directed by ilya chaiken . the movie has to do with two friends that lose their jobs at the statue of liberty after 9/11 . the movie won best film at the new york latino film festival . the movie won critics ' pick from both `` the new york times '' and `` new york magazine '' . the movie played at new york latino film festival , the woodstock film festival , and the atlanta film festival . the dvd has an audio commentary by three actors , deleted scenes , behind the scenes , photo gallery , and conversations with iraq war veterans as special features . the dvd is in 5.1 stereo sound .
