Countess Dracula     countess dracula is a 1971 hammer horror movie . it is based on countess elisabeth bathory-nadasdy and her alleged crimes in late 16th and early 17th century hungary . in it she kills young women to look young again by bathing in their blood . countess nadasdy is played by ingrid pitt in the movie . other characters include her lover and henchman captain dobi , captain imre toth a soldier for her late husband who she tries to seduce by pretending to be her daughter , and fabio , her court historian who begins to suspect her and dobi . pitt became a kind of cult icon for her role in this movie .
