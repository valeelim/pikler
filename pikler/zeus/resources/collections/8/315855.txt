Red Norvo     kenneth `` red '' norvo ( 31 march 1908 , beardstown , illinois - 6 april 1999 , santa monica , california ) was an american jazz musician and dancer . he performed from the 1920s through the 1980s . during the 1920s , he was a tap dancer in vaudeville . his main musical instrument was the vibraphone . he was also a member of the three ickkies . other websites . - red norvo at allmusic
