Louisville Colonels     the louisville colonels were a major league baseball team that played from 1882 to 1899. the colonels represented the city of louisville , kentucky , in the united states . the team played in the american association ( aa ) . this was an early american major league baseball league from the 1800s . the team was first called the louisville eclipse from 1882 to 1884. then it was renamed the louisville colonels in 1885. the name `` the colonels '' comes from the kentucky colonel , a title of honor in the commonwealth of kentucky . in 1891 , the aa folded ( stopped existing ) . the colonels then joined the national league . the team played for the national league through 1899. notable colonel players include fred clarke , honus wagner , and rube waddell .
