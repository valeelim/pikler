Lust, Caution (movie)    lust , caution ( movie ) lust , caution is a 2007 movie set mostly in hong kong during 1938 and in shanghai during 1942. the movie depicts a group of students from a chinese university who plot to murder a high ranking agent . the director of `` lust , caution '' is ang lee . this motion picture is rated nc-17 by the mpaa due to explicit sex scenes . this was released in the united states in september 2007 .
