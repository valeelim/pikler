Emmanuel Sanders     emmanuel sanders ( born march 17 , 1987 ) is an american football wide receiver for the pittsburgh steelers of the national football league . he was drafted by the steelers in the third round of the 2010 nfl draft . sanders played college football at southern methodist university . sanders spent his college football career at southern methodist university . sanders was drafted by the pittsburgh steelers in the 3rd round ( # 82 overall ) of the 2010 nfl draft . with the steelers , sanders played as a solid backup behind hines ward , antonio brown , and mike wallace . sanders missed a week 9 game on november 6 , 2011 against the baltimore ravens because his mother died . for his career , sanders has a total of 50 catches for 664 yards and 4 touchdowns .
