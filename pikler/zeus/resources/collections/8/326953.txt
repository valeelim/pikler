Emily Deschanel     emily erin deschanel ( born october 11 , 1976 ) is an american actress , movie producer and television producer . she is best known for her role as the main character in the television series `` bones '' . deschanel was born in los angeles , california . she is of french , swiss , dutch , irish and english ancestry . she is the elder daughter of caleb deschanel and mary jo deschanel ( nee weir ) . her sister is actress zooey deschanel . deschanel married david hornsby in 2010. the couple 's son was born in 2011 .
