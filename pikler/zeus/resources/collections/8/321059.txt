Tosa Province     the province had borders with iyo and awa provinces . the ancient capital city of the province was near modern nankoku . in the meiji period , the provinces of japan were converted into prefectures . the maps of japan and tosa province were reformed in the 1870s . tosa faced the pacific ocean . `` tosa jinja '' was the chief shinto shrine ( `` ichinomiya '' ) of tosa . related pages . - provinces of japan - prefectures of japan - list of regions of japan - list of islands of japan other websites . - murdoch 's map of provinces , 1903
