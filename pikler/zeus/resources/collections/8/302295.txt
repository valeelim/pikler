Scimitar oryx     the scimitar oryx or scimitar horned oryx ( oryx dammah ) is a species of oryx that is extinct in the wild . we can see them in zoos , wildlife parks and reserves . they used to live all over north africa . a baby scimitar oryx is called a calf . both male and female scimitar horned oryx have long curved horns . they are herbivores and are diurnal . they are the only species of oryx that does not have black markings . a male scimitar horned oryx is called a bull .
