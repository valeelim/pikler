The Idler Wheel...    the idler wheel ... the idler wheel ... is an album by fiona apple that was released in 2012. its full name is `` the idler wheel is wiser than the driver of the screw and whipping cords will serve you more than ropes will ever do '' . two singles were released from it . it was nominated at the 2013 grammy awards for best alternative album . the album was named the best album of 2012 by time magazine , stereogum , spinner , and npr music 's fresh air . the album got good reviews . `` pitchfork '' gave it a score of 9.0. track listing . 1 . `` every single night '' – 3:30 1 . `` daredevil '' – 3:28 1 . `` valentine '' – 3:33 1 . `` jonathan '' – 5:04 1 . `` left alone '' – 4:51 1 . `` werewolf '' – 3:13 1 . `` periphery '' – 4:58 1 . `` regret '' – 5:17 1 . `` anything we want '' – 4:41 1 . `` hot knife '' – 4:02
