Line of succession to the Swedish throne     the line of succession to the swedish throne is the list of people who may become queen or king of sweden . since 1980 , swedish law identifies the eldest child of a swedish monarch the royal heir . the first-born child follows his or her parent on the throne . this is known as full or equal primogeniture . the current order of succession is 1. victoria , crown princess of sweden , duchess of västergötland 1. princess estelle , duchess of östergötland 1. prince carl philip , duke of värmland 1. princess madeleine , duchess of hälsingland and gästrikland 1. princess leonore , duchess of gotland related pages . - order of succession other websites . - swedish royal court , bernadotte dynasty family tree - `` successionsordning ''
