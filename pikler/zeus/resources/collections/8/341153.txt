Hayabusa     the mission was a project of the institute of space and astronautical science of the university of tokyo . hayabusa was sent into space on may 9 , 2003. in november 2005 , it landed on the asteroid . the mission was successfully completed in june 2010 when the spacecraft returned to earth in june 2010 . `` hayabusa '' studied itokawa 's shape , spin , topography , colour , composition , density , and history . the `` hayabusa '' mission was the first time that an attempt was made to bring an asteroid dust sample to earth for analysis . three different feature-length films are being made about the hayabusa mission . other websites . - japan aerospace exploration agency ( jaxa ) , hayabusa ( muses-c ) mission profile
