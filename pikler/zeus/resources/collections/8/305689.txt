Fiat Multipla     the fiat multipla is a automobile produced by fiat between 1998 and 2010. the original model became well known for its styling that divided opinion before it was facelifted in 2004. fiat discontinued the model in 2010. it is a mpv version of the fiat bravo . engines . - `` 1.6 16v '' 76 kw ( 103 hp ) - `` 1.9 jtd '' 77 kw ( 105 hp ) , 81 kw ( 110 hp ) and 85 kw ( 115 hp ) - `` 1.9 multijet '' 88 kw ( 120 hp )
