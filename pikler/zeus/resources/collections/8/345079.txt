339     year 339 ( cccxxxix ) was a common year starting on monday of the julian calendar . events . by place . roman empire . - emperor constantius ii goes to his territory in the east . persia under king shapur ii is attacking mesopotamia . for the next 11 years the two powers battle with no real winner . by topic . religion . - pope julius i gives refuge at rome to athanasius . - eusebius of nicomedia is made bishop of constantinople . another arian becomes bishop of alexandria . births . - aurelius ambrosius , bishop of milan ( approximate date ) - he fani , empress of the jin dynasty ( d. 404 ) - shi shi , emperor of the jie state later zhao ( d. 349 ) deaths . - abaye , babylonian amora - eusebius of caesarea , bishop and first church historian ( approximate date ) - shemon bar sabbae , bishop of seleucia-ctesiphon - wang dao , statesman of the jin dynasty ( b . 276 )
