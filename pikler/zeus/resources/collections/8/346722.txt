Medium (TV series)    medium ( tv series ) medium ( tv series ) is an american television crime drama series . it was created by glenn gordon caron and ran from january 3 , 2005 until january 21 , 2011. it stars patricia arquette as allison dubois , a medium for the phoenix , arizona district attorney 's office . she is a married mother of three daughters and uses her psychic abilities to help the police to solve crimes .
