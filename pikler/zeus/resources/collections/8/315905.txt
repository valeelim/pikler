Crab rangoon     crab rangoon are deep-fried dumplings . they are served in american chinese restaurants . they are stuffed with a combination of cream cheese , lightly flaked crab meat with scallions and/or garlic . these fillings are then wrapped in chinese wonton wrappers . they are then deep fried in vegetable oil . in the pacific northwest states of america crab rangoon are also known as crab puffs .
