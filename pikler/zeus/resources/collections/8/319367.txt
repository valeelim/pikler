Valdivia expedition     the valdivia expedition was a scientific exedition to explore the deep sea . it was a german expedition , done in 1928 and 1929. a ship was bought from `` hamburg-amerikanischen packetfahrt-actien-gesellschaft '' hapag and given instruments to be able to perform scientific measurements . the ship left hamburg on 31st july 1898 and returned on 1st may 1899. the ship had travelled over 32.000 nautical miles . the expedition was very successful . the results were published in 24 volumes ; publishing them took until 1940 .
