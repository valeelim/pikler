List of counties in Idaho     there are 44 counties in the u.s. state of idaho . old idaho counties . - alturas county : a huge county in south-central idaho that was formed in 1864 , but split up in 1895 . - lah-toh county , idaho territory : a county in northern idaho that was formed in 1864 , but it was ended a short time later in 1867 . - logan county : formed by a partition of alturas county in 1889 , bit it was shortly ended in 1895 .
