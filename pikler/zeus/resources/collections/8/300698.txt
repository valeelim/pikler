Janeane Garofalo     janeane garofalo ( born september 28 , 1964 ) is an american political activist , stand up comedian and actress . she was co-host on the radio show `` the majority report '' . garofalo is of italian and irish descent . her father is a former executive at exxon . garofalo was raised as a conservative catholic . garofalo now describes herself as an atheist . she had two `` emmy award '' nominations for her role on `` the larry sanders show '' . other websites . - official website
