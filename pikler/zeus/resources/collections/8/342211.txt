Zwonowice     zwonowice is a village in the administrative district of gmina lyski , within rybnik county , silesian voivodeship , in southern poland . it is west of the regional capital katowice . pastors . - antoni gasz ( 1942-1950 ) - oswald niedziela ( 1950-1954 ) - jerzy stefani , pastor of lyski in the years 1954-1956 led ministry in zwonowice - antoni kraus ( 1956-1962 ) - kazimierz wyciślik ( 1962-1978 ) - antoni pieczka ( 1978-1995 ) - roman gołąb ( 1995-present )
