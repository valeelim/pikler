Hypersexuality     hypersexuality is known as nymphomania when in females and satyriasis when in males . it is a stronger than usual urge to have sexual intercourse . masturbation causes euphoria ( extreme pleasure ) . it is caused by some stimulant drugs , such as amphetamine . it is also caused by some mental disorders : bipolar disorder , schizoaffective disorder , borderline personality disorder , histrionic personality disorder . some cases are of unknown cause . some psychologists think it can be caused by a hormone imbalance . others believe it is caused by early exposure to pornography and sex .
