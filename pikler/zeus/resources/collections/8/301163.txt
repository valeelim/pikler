I Gotta Feeling    i got ta feeling `` i got ta feeling '' is a single released in 2009 by the black eyed peas under their fifth studio album `` the e.n.d. '' . the song debuted at number 2 on the billboard hot 100 in the united states , and later hit number 1 on the u.s. charts . it stayed number one for fourteen weeks . it won the grammy for best pop performance by a duo or group with vocals at the 2009 grammy awards .
