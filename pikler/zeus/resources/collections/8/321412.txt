Bingo Province     the province had borders with bitchū , hōki , izumo , iwami , and aki provinces . the ancient capital city of the province was in the area of fuchu . in the meiji period , the provinces of japan were converted into prefectures . the maps of japan and bingo province were reformed in the 1870s . `` kibitsu jinja '' was the chief shinto shrine ( `` ichinomiya '' ) of bingo . related pages . - provinces of japan - prefectures of japan - list of regions of japan - list of islands of japan other websites . - murdoch 's map of provinces , 1903
