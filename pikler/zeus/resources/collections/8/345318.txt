Master of Business Administration     master of business administration ( often shortened to mba ) is a postgraduate academic degree . people who study for an mba already have a degree in another field of study and study management . very often , a master 's degree is required , but sometimes bachelor 's degree-holders with work experience are also accepted . unlike other areas of study , mba courses are often targeted at people who work , and can be done in a part-time fashion , including distance learning . mba programs usually take two years , full-time . the main target group for mbas are engineers , lawyers , doctors of medicine , and other with a background form the humanities . the idea of the mba is to give those people qualifications in management positions .
