The Change-Up     the change-up is a 2011 american comedy movie produced by david dobkin , neal h. moritz , david furnish and gary barber and directed by david dobkin . `` the change-up '' was released on august 5 , 2011 in north america . cast . - ryan reynolds as mitch - jason bateman as dave - sydney rouviere as cara - olivia wilde as sabrina - mircea monroe as tatiana - leslie mann as jamie - tj hassan as kato - alan arkin as mitch 's father - john smoltz as the atlanta braves pitching coach
