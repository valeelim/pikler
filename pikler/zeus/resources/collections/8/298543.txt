Cameroon at the Olympics     cameroon was first at the olympic games in 1964. the international olympic committee 's official abbreviation for cameroon is cam . there have been competitors from cameroon in every summer olympic games since then . the nation left the 1976 summer olympics after three days . cameroon has also been at the winter olympic games one time in 2002. the national olympic committee for cameroon was formed in 1963. related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - cameroon profile at london2012.com - cameroon at olympic.org
