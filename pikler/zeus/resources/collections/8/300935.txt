Tejano Music Awards     the tejano music awards is an annual music awards ceremony that is held in san antonio , texas to recognize and build awareness for tejano music . the first `` tma 's '' began in 1980. in 1986 , selena won the `` female vocalist of the year '' award and continued to win the award for nine consecutive years , up until her death . she had won two awards after her death . selena dominated the awards beginning the 90 's when she helped spread the tejano music movement into the united states . she was then given the title `` queen of tejano music '' . being a male-dominate genre , the tejano music awards continued to see a rise in more male entertainers receiving awards such as emilio navaira , jay perez , and la mafia . the group , la mafia , became the first male tejano group to sell a gold album ( sales of 50,000 units ) . for the females , selena 's album ven conmigo ( 1990 ) became the very first tejano album to ever be certified gold by the riaa . awards . - tejano music awards for male vocalist of the year
