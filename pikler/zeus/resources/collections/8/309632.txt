Woohoo     `` woohoo '' is a song sung by christina aguilera and nicki minaj , from aguilera 's 2010 album `` bionic '' . the song was written by aguilera , minaj , claude kelly , jamal `` polow da don '' jones and ester dean . it is a uptempo electrohop song with r & b and hip hop influences ; lyrically it speaks of cunnilingus . the song was released on may 18 , 2010 as the second single from aguilera 's `` bionic '' album .
