Barbadian     barbadian , often shortened to bajan , may be anything of or relating to barbados and may also refer directly to : - barbadian football - barbadian cuisine - barbadian people , people of barbadian descent . for information about the barbadian people , see demographics of barbados and culture of barbados . for specific persons , see list of barbadians . - barbadian english language - a barbadian 's head and a martiniquian 's head flag , a flag by a pirate bartholomew roberts , who vowed revenge towards barbados and martinique .
