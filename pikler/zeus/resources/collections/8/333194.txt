International Bear Brotherhood Flag     the international bear brotherhood flag is a flag representing the international bear community . the design was probably inspired by the gay rainbow flag and the leather pride flag . the colors represent all the `` fur colors and nationalities around the world . '' the flag was trademarked in 1995. it has been heavily marketed . it appears on tee shirts , baseball caps , license plates , and other items . two bear flags preceded the international bear brotherhood flag . a flag created by the front range bears was introduced in 1992. it appeared in pride parades in denver , san francisco , and the 1993 march on washington , dc . colors used for the five diagonally placed stripes were gold , grey , brown , white , and black . another bear flag was produced in seattle . it is not seen outside the northwest . it too uses diagonal stripes .
