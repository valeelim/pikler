Rick Jones (voice actor)    rick jones ( voice actor ) richard `` rick '' jones is an american director , writer , content developer , voice actor , and comedian . he was the voice of fu-fu in `` sagwa , the chinese siamese cat '' . since 1983 , he 's worked with fox , disney , universal , hanna-barbera , alphanim , cinar and nelvana . jones has been nominated for a gemini award twice in 1988 and 2003. he also was known for being the voice of slax in `` x-duckx '' , and razmo in `` ratz '' he was born richard jones .
