Spokane Chiefs     the spokane chiefs are an american professional ice hockey team that began playing in 1985. they currently play in the western hockey league . they play their home games in spokane , washington at the spokane veterans memorial arena . the were known as the kelowna wings from 1982 to 1985 until they relocated to spokane and became the chiefs . the chiefs were also the first team in whl history to host an outdoor hockey game when they defeated the kootenay ice 11-2 on january 15 , 2011. other websites . - official website
