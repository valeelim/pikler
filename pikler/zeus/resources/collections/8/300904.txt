Wilford Brimley     anthony wilford brimley ( born september 27 , 1934 ) is an american actor . brimley has appeared in the movies `` the china syndrome '' , `` cocoon '' , `` the thing '' and `` the firm '' . he has also appeared in television commercials for quaker oats and liberty medical . he is a member of the church of jesus christ of latter-day saints and is a former united states marine . he has diabetes which he speaks openly about in his commercials for liberty medical .
