Term (mathematics)    term ( mathematics ) a term is a mathematical expression which may form a separable part of an equation , a series , or another expression . in elementary mathematics , a `` term '' is either a single number or variable , or the product of several numbers or variables . terms are separated by a + or - sign in an overall expression . for example , in 3 , 4 '' x '' , and 5 '' yzw '' are three separate terms . in the context of polynomials , `` term '' can mean a monomial with a coefficient . to 'collect like terms ' in a polynomial is the basic operation of making it a linear combination of distinct monomials . for example , a series is often represented as the sum of a sequence of terms . in general mathematical use , however , `` term '' is not limited to additive expressions . individual factors in an expression representing a product are multiplicative terms . indeed , individual elements of any mathematical expression may be referred to as `` terms '' . terms also appear in logic . specific names for terms . - addition and subtraction :
