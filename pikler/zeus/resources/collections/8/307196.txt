Tom Daly (infielder)    tom daly ( infielder ) thomas peter daly ( february 7 , 1866 , in philadelphia , pennsylvania - october 29 , 1938 , in brooklyn , new york ) was a catcher and second baseman who played in the major leagues from 1887 to 1903. he played for the chicago cubs , washington nationals , brooklyn dodgers , chicago white sox and cincinnati reds .
