Abanyom language     abanyom is a niger-congo language . it is categorized as a part of the ekoid language subfamily and southern bantoid group . the language is spoken by the abanyom people in the cross river state of nigeria . as of 1986 , there were about 12,500 speakers of abanyom . abanyom is very similar to the bantu languages . it is a tonal language . its noun class system is the same as other niger-congo languages ' .
