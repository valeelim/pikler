Chinese Crested Dog     the chinese crested dog is a hairless dog breed . the breed probably did not actually come from china . it is the most common hairless dog breed . however , some of them do have fur . other hairless dog breeds are the american hairless terrier , mexican hairless dog , peruvian hairless dog , and hairless khala . hairless chinese cresteds often win the world 's ugliest dog contest , which takes place every year in petaluma , california . the chinese crested is a friendly , intelligent animal .
