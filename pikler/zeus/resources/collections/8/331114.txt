Canyoneering     canyoneering or canyoning is a dangerous sport . a person who goes canyoneering is a canyoneer . a canyoneer is a person who walks , swims , or rappels ( climbs down ropes ) into canyons . sometimes these canyons are very deep and very narrow . canyons that are very narrow are called `` slot canyons '' . canyoneering can be dangerous . sometimes canyons flood ( fill with water ) when it rains . accidents can also happen when a canyoneer is climbing down ropes . the rope can break or the canyoneer can slip . sometimes canyons are too narrow to walk through , so you have to go back . also , sometimes the water in canyons is so cold you can get sick . many people have died in canyons .
