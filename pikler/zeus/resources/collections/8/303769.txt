United Academy     the d.c. united academy is a program for young soccer players that is run by d.c. united , a pro soccer team that plays in major league soccer . the `` academy '' trains these young soccer players to possibly one day play soccer either in a pro league or in college . d.c. united 's academy has several different levels , including , a u-23 team , a u-18 team , a u-16 team and then `` pre-academy '' teams . pre-academy teams are for players who are not yet part of the academy on a normal schedule . the teams compete in the united states soccer federations ' development academy league . other websites . - d.c. united academy
