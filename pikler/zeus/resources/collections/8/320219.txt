Sugar Ray Leonard     ray charles leonard ( born may 17 , 1956 ) , better known as sugar ray leonard , is a former american boxer . he was born in wilmington , north carolina . leonard is believed by many to be one of the best boxers of all times . he was named the `` boxer of the decade '' for the 1980s . leonard retired with a record of 36 wins ( 25 by knockout ) 3 losses and 1 draw . he also won the gold medal in the 1976 olympics . his mother named him after singer ray charles .
