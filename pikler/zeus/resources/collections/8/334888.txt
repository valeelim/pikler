Ian Brennan     ian brennan is a television writer , actor , and producer . brennan is known mostly for creating the fox television series , `` glee '' . he has written `` all of the episodes '' along with two other colleagues . brennan 's first written television series was `` growing '' in 2006. he has also been in several theater performances since 1999. he was nominated for two 2010 writers guild of america awards for `` glee '' .
