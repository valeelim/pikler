Love Actually     love actually is a 2003 british romantic comedy-drama movie . the large majority of it is set in london , with parts of it set in marseille , france and wisconsin , united states . it features several interlinked stories of love . it was written and directed by richard curtis . it stars hugh grant , liam neeson , colin firth , laura linney , emma thompson , alan rickman , keira knightley , martine mccutcheon , bill nighy and rowan atkinson .
