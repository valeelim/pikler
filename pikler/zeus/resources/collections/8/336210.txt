A Visit from St. Nicholas     `` a visit from st. nicholas '' is a poem by clement clarke moore . it was first published anonymously on december 23 , 1823 in the `` troy sentinel '' , an american newspaper . it is also known as `` the night before christmas '' and `` 't was the night before christmas '' from its first line . the poem is largely responsible for the way many people think of santa claus . it describes his physical appearance , the night of his visit , his sleigh , the number and names of his reindeer , and the tradition that he brings toys to children .
