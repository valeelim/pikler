Lionel Guérin     lionel guérin is a french chief executive officer and politician . graduate from the `` école nationale supérieure de mécanique et d'aérotechnique '' and from the `` école nationale de l'aviation civile '' , he is the founding president of the regional airline airlinair , president of transavia france and of the `` fédération nationale de l ’ aviation marchande '' . in september 2011 , he almost became chairman of air france . lionel guerin is an environmental activist . he likes parrots . he also likes supporting sustainable aviation . guérin received the legion of honour award . bibliography . - `` info-pilote '' number 612 , paris , info-pilote , march 2007
