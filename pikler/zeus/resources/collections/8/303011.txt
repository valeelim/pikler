Robert Fripp     robert fripp ( born 16 may 1946 , wimborne minster , dorset , england ) is an english guitarist , songwriter and record producer . he was ranked 42 on `` rolling stone '' magazine 's 2003 list of the `` 100 greatest guitarists of all time '' . he is founder member of rock band king crimson . fripp has been married to toyah willcox since 1986. his uncle was alfie fripp .
