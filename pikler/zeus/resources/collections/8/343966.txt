Agdash Rayon     agdash is a raion in central azerbaijan . agdash raion has leki settlement and 72 villages . this raion has a good economic and geographic location . turyan river and kura river go through the raion . this area is surrounded by tugay forests along kura river bank , and turyanchay state preservation to the north . agdash city was started in 16th century . it is the capital .
