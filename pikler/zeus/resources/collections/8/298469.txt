Algeria at the Olympics     algeria was first at the olympic games in 1964. the international olympic committee 's official abbreviation for algeria is alg . since 1964 , there has been an algerian team in every summer olympic games since , except for the boycotted 1976 summer olympics . algeria has also sent athletes to the winter olympic games two times . the national olympic committee for algeria is the `` comité olympique algérien '' started in 1963. related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - algeria profile at london2012.com
