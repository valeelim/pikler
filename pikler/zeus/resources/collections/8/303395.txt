Galápagos penguin     galápagos penguins ( `` spheniscus mendiculus '' ) live on the galápagos islands . because these islands are at the equator , the waters are warm not all penguins live in cold climates . they do not migrate . galapagos penguins have two bands of brown feathers on their faces and their beaks are thin and slender . they weigh five pounds and are one and a half feet tall .
