'' ghazal was once a classical , elite type of music and poetry . singh was able to make ghazal popular by singing in many different languages , singing ghazal in movies and music videos . he used modern technology and was the first ghazal singer to use multi-tracking . he died in mumbai , after a stroke in 2011 .
