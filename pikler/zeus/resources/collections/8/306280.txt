In the Zone     in the zone is the fourth studio album by britney spears , released on november 13 , 2003. she wrote or co-wrote some songs of the album : `` me against the music '' , `` showdown '' , `` early mornin '' , `` touch of my hand '' , `` the hook up '' , `` shadow '' , `` brave new girl '' and `` everytime '' . track listing . - latin american bonus track - australian , european & japanese bonus tracks
