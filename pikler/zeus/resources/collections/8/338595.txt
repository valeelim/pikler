Hrithik Roshan     hrithik roshan ( born 10 january 1974 ) is an indian bollywood actor . roshan had appeared as a child actor in many movies in the 1980s . he made his movie debut in `` kaho naa ... pyaar hai '' in 2000. his performance in the movie was widely appreciated and earned him a filmfare awards for best actor and best male debut . he followed it with leading roles in `` fiza '' and `` mission kashmir '' and had a male supporting role in the blockbuster movie `` kabhi khushi kabhie gham ... '' ( 2001 ) , which was india 's highest-grossing movie in the overseas market at that time .
