Lake Charles, Louisiana    lake charles , louisiana lake charles is the largest city in the southwest louisiana . it is the second-largest city in the acadiana . it is the fifth-largest city in the u.s. states of louisiana . the actual population is almost 72,000. the city was founded as charleston , march 7 , 1861 ; the name was changed into lake charles march 16 , 1867. the city was founded by and named for charles sallier .
