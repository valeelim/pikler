George Willis     george willis , md jp ( 1828-1898 ) was a british medical doctor and politician . willis was born in county cavan , ireland . he earned his medical degree at the university of glasgow in 1850. he was the elder brother of dr. william willis who taught medicine at the university of tokyo and founded the medical school at the university of kagoshima . he was one of the founders of the monmouth hospital and dispensary at monmouth in wales . he was three times elected mayor of monmouth .
