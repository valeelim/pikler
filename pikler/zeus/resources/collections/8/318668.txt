Lizzy Pattinson     elizabeth `` lizzy '' pattinson is an english singer and songwriter . lizzy has a sister , victoria and brother , actor robert pattinson . lizzy has performed with the uk dance act aurora and german duo milk & sugar . she was lead vocalist for them on milk & sugar 's no . 1 `` billboard '' hot dance club play track `` let the sun shine '' . she has had three top 20 hits in the uk . in 2008 lizzy recorded background vocals for the movie `` twilight '' . she is heard on the track `` who are they ?
