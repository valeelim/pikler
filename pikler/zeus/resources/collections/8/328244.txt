WrestleMania 29     wrestlemania xxix was the twenty-ninth yearly wrestlemania professional wrestling pay-per-view ( ppv ) event made by wwe . it took place on april 7 , 2013 at metlife stadium in east rutherford , new jersey . the event was the fifth wrestlemania in the new york metropolitan area and the third wrestlemania held in the state of new jersey . there was an 8-man mixed tag team match scheduled between tons of funk ( brodus clay & `` sweet t '' tensai ) ( with cameron & naomi ) and team rhodes scholars ( cody rhodes & damien sandow ) ( with the bella twins ) but the match was pulled from the card on short notice because of time constraints .
