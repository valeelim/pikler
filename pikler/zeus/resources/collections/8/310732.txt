Trinomial nomenclature     in biology , trinomial nomenclature is used to name living things at a rank lower than species . the term `` trinomial nomenclature '' means `` three-part name '' or `` system of three-part names '' . if living things at a rank lower than species get a scientific name , each name has three parts . the first part indicates the genus , the second part indicates the species , and the third part is particular to the living thing ( at whatever rank it has been assigned ) . related pages . - biological classification - binomial nomenclature
