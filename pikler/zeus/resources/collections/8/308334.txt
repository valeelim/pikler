Me Against the World     me against the world is the third studio album by american rapper , tupac shakur . `` me against the world '' was released on march 14 , 1995. the album was released when 2pac was imprisoned and it debuted at # 1 on the billboard 200. by having this happen , it made him the first artist to have an album debut at number one on billboard 200 while serving time in prison . it was nominated for best rap album at the 1996 grammy awards . the album 's first single `` dear mama '' was nominated for best rap solo performance .
