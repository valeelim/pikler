Bok choy     bok choy ( `` brassica chinensis '' ) is classified as a cabbage . it does not look like the round european cabbages found in western supermarkets , or to napa cabbage . its white stalks look like celery without the stringiness . the dark green , crinkly leaves of the most common type are like romaine lettuce . the chinese commonly refer to bok choy as pak choi or `` white vegetable . '' another common name is white cabbage .
