Baltimore Colts     the baltimore colts were an american football franchise in the national football league . the team was based in baltimore . the logo was a white horseshoe ( blue uniforms ) . the team had success since 1953 when it began , winning four nfl championships , including super bowl v. the most famous player was quarterback johnny unitas . however , in 1984 , bob irsay secretly ( meaning in the middle of a march night without an announcement ) moved the team to indianapolis to begin play as the indianapolis colts .
