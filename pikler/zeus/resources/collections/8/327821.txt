Common year starting on Sunday     this is the calendar for any common year starting on sunday , january 1 ( dominical letter a ) or for any year in which “ doomsday ” is tuesday . the current year , 2017 , is a common year of this type in the gregorian calendar . the last such year was 2006 and the next such year will be 2023 in the gregorian calendar , or julian years 2007 and 2018 ( see tables at bottom . ) a common year is a year with 365 days , in other words , not a leap year .
