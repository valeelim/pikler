Béchar Province     béchar is a province in algeria . it is named after its capital béchar . a large part of the province has sand dune fields that no one can live in . the province has 12 districts . there are 21 municipalities . the districts are : 1. abadla 1. béchar 1. béni abbès 1. béni ounif 1. el ouata 1. igli 1. kénadsa 1. kerzaz 1. lahmar 1. ouled khodeïr 1. tabelbala 1. taghit
