Ovenbird     the ovenbird ( `` seiurus aurocapilla '' ) is a small songbird of the order passeriformes . its family is parulidae . the ovenbird was named after its domed nest with a side entrance , which looks like an oven . this migratory bird is found in eastern north america and winters in central america , many caribbean islands , florida , and northern venezuela . the ovenbird walks , unlike most other members of its family , which hop .
