Range (biology)    range ( biology ) in biology , the range or distribution of a species is the geographical area or habitat where the species live . sometimes , when species are found in different regions at different times of year , terms such as summer range and winter range can be used . when discussing about animals , the species ' natural range is often discussed . there are at least five types of distribution patterns : - random - clustered/grouped ( most are in one area ) - linear ( they are in a line ) - radial ( radiating from a centre ) - regular/ordered ( not random , but in set positions )
