Tingwall Airport     tingwall airport also known as lerwick/tingwall airport , is in shetland , near its capital lerwick . flights go to and from other islands in the shetlands . there is another airport , sumbugh airport , which has flights to and from the scottish mainland . tingwall aerodrome has a caa ordinary licence ( number p614 ) that allows flights for the public transport of passengers or for flying instruction as authorised by the licensee ( shetland islands council ) .
