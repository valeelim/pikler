New Territories East (constituency)    new territories east ( constituency ) new territories east ( or nt east ) , refers to the eastern part of new territories in hong kong . it includes sha tin district , tai po district , north district and sai kung district . it is the second largest geographical constituency in the legislative council of hong kong . although new territories east is not an official administrative name ( it is officially part of the 'new territories ' ) , many government departments use it as a major administrative division .
