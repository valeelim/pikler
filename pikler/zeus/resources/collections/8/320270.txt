Penghu     the penghu islands , also known as pescadores ( from portuguese : `` fishermen '' ) are an archipelago off the western coast of taiwan . they are in the taiwan strait . the archipelago is made up of 90 small islands and islets . it covers an area of . the whole archipelago forms penghu county under taiwan province in the republic of china . the archipelago and the island of taiwan together formed the former japanese territory of formosa .
