Tassili n'Ajjer     tassili n'ajjer is a mountain range in the algerian part of the sahara desert . the name means `` plateau of the rivers '' . it 72,000 km . large . the range is mostly made of sandstone . it is known for the prehistoric rock art . the art has herds of cattle , large wild animals including crocodiles , and human activities such as hunting and dancing . the nearest town is djanet . it is about 10 km southwest of the range .
