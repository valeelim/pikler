Jennifer Tilly     jennifer tilly ( born jennifer elizabeth chan , 16 september 1958 ) is an american actress . she was born in harbor city , los angeles , california . her father was chinese american ; her mother was of irish , finnish and native american ancestry . tilly 's sister is actress meg tilly ( born 1960 ) . tilly 's acting career began in 1983. she acted in `` bound '' , `` liar liar '' and two movies in the `` child 's play '' series . she plays bonnie swanson in television series `` family guy '' .
