Belfast City Airport     george best belfast city airport is a single-runway airport in belfast , county antrim , northern ireland next to the port of belfast it is from belfast city centre . it shares the site with the short brothers/bombardier aircraft making area . the airport began commercial operations in 1983. the airport was known as `` belfast city airport '' until it was renamed in 2006 in memory of george best , the professional footballer from belfast , best known for his time with manchester united . the airport gets over 2.7 million passengers in 2010 , a record total for the airport , though the total fell by 12.5 % to 2.4 million in 2011. the airport is a major base for flybe , which began services at the airport in 1993 and is now the largest operator at belfast city . belfast city has a caa public use aerodrome licence ( number p862 ) that allows flights for the public transport of passengers or for flying instruction .
