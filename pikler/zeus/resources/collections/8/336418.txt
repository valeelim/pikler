Anne Bancroft     anne bancroft ( september 17 , 1931 - june 6 , 2005 ) was an american actress . she was born anna maria louisa italiano in the bronx , new york city . she had italian grandparents and was raised roman catholic . she won an academy award for best actress for her role in the 1962 movie `` the miracle worker '' . she also won three bafta awards , an emmy award , two golden globe awards and two tony awards . she is best known for her role as mrs robinson in `` the graduate '' . her first marriage ended in divorce . her second marriage was to actor-filmmaker mel brooks . it lasted from 1964 until her death in mount sinai hospital , manhattan , new york city , from uterine cancer . their son , writer max brooks ( born 1972 ) , is her only child .
