Boyzone     boyzone are an irish boy band . the group is a quartet , its members are keith duffy ( born 1974 ) , mikey graham ( born 1972 ) , ronan keating ( born 1977 ) and shane lynch ( born 1976 ) . stephen gately ( 1976-2009 ) was also a member until his death . the band were formed in dublin by louis walsh in 1993. they split in 2000 and reformed in 2007. other websites . - official website
