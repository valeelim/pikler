George Church     george church ( born august 28 , 1954 ) is an american molecular geneticist . he was one of the people who set out to analyse the human genetic code , the sequence of genes in human dna . church is professor of genetics at harvard medical school , professor of health sciences and technology at harvard and mit , and a core faculty member at the wyss institute for biologically inspired engineering at harvard university . with walter gilbert he developed the first direct genomic sequencing method in 1984 , and helped start the human genome project in 1984 while he was a research scientist at newly formed biogen inc. he invented ways of analysing huge amounts of chemical data , homologous recombination methods , and dna array synthesizers . automated sequencing & software in genome therapeutics corp. gave the first commercial genome sequence , ( the human pathogen , `` helicobacter pylori '' ) in 1994. church started the personal genome project ( pgp ) in 2005 , and in 2007 he founded the u.s. personal genomics company knome ( with jorge conde and sundar subramaniam ) .
