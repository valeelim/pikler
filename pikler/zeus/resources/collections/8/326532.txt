Japan Mint     the is an incorporated administrative agency . it is a special type of semi-governmental entity in japan . the mint has its head office in osaka with branches in tokyo and hiroshima . the meiji government established the japan mint . in the early meiji era , paper currency was printed by dondorf and naumann in germany . a proposal to construct a banknote manufacturing plant in japan was approved in december 1874. a two-story western red brick building was completed in october 1876. the mint became an incorporated administrative agency on april 1 , 2003. related pages . - list of independent administrative institutes in japan - japanese yen other websites . - japan mint website , - building a national currency ( 1868-1899 )
