Peyton Place (1957 movie)    peyton place ( 1957 movie ) peyton place is a 1957 movie . it was based on a 1956 novel of the same name by grace metalious . it was directed by mark robson . the movie stars lana turner , diane varsi , russ tamblyn , lee phillips , hope lange , and arthur kennedy . critics felt the most salacious bits in the novel had been laundered for the movie . the movie received nine oscar nominations but won none . the movie did poorly at the box office until lana turner 's daughter shot her mother 's lover johnny stompanato to death . the press coverage of the investigation boosted ticket sales by 32 % . the movie became the second highest money-making movie of the year .
