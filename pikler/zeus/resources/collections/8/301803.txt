Digital Living Network Alliance     the digital living network alliance is a trade-association , which was established by sony in 2003. as of 2011 , over 250 companies are part of this organization . the idea of the association is to develop standards and technologies that make it possible that several devices at home interact , so that the consumer has a better feeling . an example might be a television , that can be used to play a film on a computer , or to show photos stored on a mobile phone .
