Azərbaycan marşı     azərbaycan marşı is the national anthem of azerbaijan . the original title is march of azerbaijan . the words were written by the poet ahmad javad . the music was by azeri composer uzeyir hajibeyov . the anthem was started in 1918. a part of the song is written on the back of the azerbaijani 5 manat banknote issued since 2006. note that , in accordance with azerbaijani law , there are no `` official '' translations of the anthem .
