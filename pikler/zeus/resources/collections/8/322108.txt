Georgia Engel     georgia bright engel ( born july 28 , 1948 ) is an american actress . she is the voice of bobbie in the `` open season '' franchise . she was also on `` the mary tyler moore show '' and on `` everybody loves raymond '' . she had two emmy nominations for both shows . she played a good witch in the 2007 recurring role of esmeralda on the now-defunct nbc soap opera `` passions '' .
