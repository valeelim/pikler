Great white pelican     the great white pelican ( `` pelecanus onocrotalus '' ) is also known as the eastern white pelican , white pelican , or old world white pelican . they are the second largest birds in the pelican family , with only the dalmatian pelican larger than them . the pelican breeds in southeastern europe , south asia , and africa . they are most common in africa , as 80 % of the 90,000 breeding pairs mate there . however , because of climate change and humans using the wetlands they live in , some populations of great white pelicans are declining .
