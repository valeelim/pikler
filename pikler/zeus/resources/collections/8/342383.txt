Vehicle registration plate     a vehicle registration plate is a metal or plastic plate on a motor vehicle or trailer . it is used for official identification purposes . there is a numeric or alphanumeric code that identifies the vehicle . in some countries , the identifier is unique in the entire country . in other countries , it is unique in a state or province . sometimes the identifier is for a person and sometimes for a vehicle . each country calls a vehicle registration plate something different . examples are license plate or tag ( us ) , licence plate ( canada ) , number plate or registration plate ( uk ) .
