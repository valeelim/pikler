Lauren Holly     lauren holly ( born october 28 , 1963 , bristol , pennsylvania ) is an american television and movie actress . she played maxine stewart in the cbs television show `` picket fences '' . she played mary swanson in the 1994 movie `` dumb and dumber '' . she also did many other tv and movie performances . she was married to actor jim carrey between 1996 and 1997 .
