International Mr. Leather     international mr. leather ( iml ) is an international contest for men . it was founded by lovers and business partners chuck renslow and dom orejudos in 1979. it is held annually in chicago , illinois . the event spans several days . in addition to the contest , the event features workshops , socials , themed dance parties , and a leather market . in 2007 , 53 contestants competed from 23 states and 4 foreign countries . 1,800 spectators packed the chicago theatre . iml contestants must either be the winner of a bar , local , or regional leather contest or be sponsored by a leather bar , business , club , or organization . a panel of nine judges selects the winner .
