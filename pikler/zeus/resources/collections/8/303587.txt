Audi Cup     the audi cup is a two-day football tournament . it happens at the allianz arena in munich , germany . the first edition of the audi cup was won by bayern munich . the 2017 champions were atlético madrid . bayern munich has won the tournament the most times . the audi cup was created in 2009 to remember audi 's 100th year of trading and the hosts , bayern munich , won the tournament . it replaced the franz beckenbauer cup for the year . wins by team . - bayern munich : 3 - atlético madrid : 1 - fc barcelona : 1 other websites . - official website
