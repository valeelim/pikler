Digital certificates     digital certificates are electronic 'credit cards ' that establish your credentials when doing business or other transactions on the web . it is issued by a certification authority ( ca ) . it contains your name , a serial number , expiration dates , a copy of the certificate holder 's public key ( used for encrypting messages and digital signatures ) , and the digital signature of the certificate-issuing authority so that a recipient can verify that the certificate is real . some digital certificates conform to a standard , x.509 . digital certificates can be kept in registries so that authenticating users can look up other users ' public keys .
