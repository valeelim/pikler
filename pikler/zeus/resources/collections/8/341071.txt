Flag of Antigua and Barbuda     the national flag of antigua and barbuda was started on february 27 , 1967. it was designed by artist and sculptor , sir reginald samuel . the rising sun is for the dawning of a new era . the colours have different meanings . the black is for the african ancestry of the people . the blue is for hope . the red is for energy or dynamism of the people . the colouring of yellow , blue , and white ( from the sun down ) is for the sun , sea , and sand . the blue also is for the caribbean sea . the v-shape is the symbol of victory .
