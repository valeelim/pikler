Rajat Gupta     rajat kumar gupta ( , born 2 december 1948 ) is an indian-american businessman . he was the managing director ( chief executive ) of mckinsey & company from 1994 to 2003 and a business leader in india and the united states . he was arrested in late 2011 by the fbi on insider trading charges stemming from the raj rajaratnam galleon group case . in 2012 , he was sentenced to 2 years in prison .
