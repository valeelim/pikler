Patsy Kensit     patricia jude francis kensit ( born 4 march 1968 ) is a british actress , model , and singer . she was born in hounslow , london to an irish mother and english father . kensit 's movie roles include `` absolute beginners '' , `` lethal weapon 2 '' and `` twenty-one '' . she acted in soap opera `` emmerdale '' from 2004-2006 and in medical drama `` holby city '' from 2007-2010. kensit was a contestant in the 2010 series of `` strictly come dancing '' . she is a housemate in the january 2015 series of celebrity big brother ( uk ) . kensit is a roman catholic . she has been married to four musicians : dan donovan ( 1988-1991 ) , jim kerr ( 1992-1996 ) , liam gallagher ( 1997-2000 ) and jeremy healy ( 2009-2010 ) . the first three marriages ended in divorce . she is separated from her fourth husband . she has two sons - one by kerr and one by gallagher .
