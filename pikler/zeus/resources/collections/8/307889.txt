Samantha Stosur     samantha jane `` sam '' stosur ( born 30 march 1984 ) is an australian professional tennis player . she won the 2011 us open singles title and was a finalist at the 2010 french open . stosur is ranked world no . 5 and her career high in singles is world no . 4 , achieved on 21 february 2011. she is a former world no . 1 on the wta tour in doubles with lisa raymond from the united states.she is currently australian number 1 .
