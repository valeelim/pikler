Mirador de la Flor     mirador de la flor ( ) is a life-sized statue that is in corpus christi , texas . the statue is a memorial to american singer selena . the statue was ordered by the mayor of corpus christi as a tourist attraction in 1997. hundreds of fans visit the statue weekly from all over the world . the statue faces corpus christi bay , where the gun that killed selena was thrown . a second life-sized statue of selena was made in mexico in march 2011 .
