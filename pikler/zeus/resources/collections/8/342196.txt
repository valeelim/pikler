Constitution of Argentina     the constitution of argentina is one of the main sources of existing law in argentina . its first version was written in 1853 by a constitutional assembly gathered in santa fe . the basis was taken in part from the united states constitution . it was then changed in 1860 , 1866 , 1898 , 1949 , 1957. the present version is the text of 1994. the first government was made after the may revolution . many revolutionary leaders , led by mariano moreno , wanted to declare independence immediately . there were problems between the two main parties . finally the federal constitution was made on may 1 , 1853. references . - official translation to english - from the argentine chamber of deputies page . - reforms to the original 1853 constitution other pages . - constitution - constitutionalism - constitutional economics
