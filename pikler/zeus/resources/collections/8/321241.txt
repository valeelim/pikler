Don Bluth     donald virgil `` don '' bluth ( born september 13 , 1937 ) is an american animator and independent studio owner . he is best known for leaving the walt disney company in 1979 and later directing of animated movies such as `` the secret of nimh '' ( 1982 ) , `` an american tail '' ( 1986 ) , '' the land before time '' ( 1988 ) , and `` all dogs go to heaven '' ( 1989 ) , as well as his work on the laserdisc game `` dragon 's lair '' . he is also often credited for providing competition to disney , and making them improve from their streak of less-successful movie efforts to the movies that would make up the disney renaissance .
