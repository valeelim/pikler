Genryaku     events of the `` genryaku '' era . - 25 april 1185 ( `` genryaku 2 , 24th day of the 3rd month '' ) : the taira ( also known as the heike ) and the minamoto clashed in the battle of dan-no-ura ; and the heike are defeated . antoku drowned at dan-no-ura . - 6 august 1185 ( `` genryaku 2 , 9th day of the 7th month '' ) : great earthquake in kyoto and in the kinai region . other websites . - national diet library , `` the japanese calendar '' -- historical overview plus illustrative images from library 's collection
