Telephone numbers in Andorra     telephone numbers in andorra are six digits , with fixed line numbers beginning with the digits 8 and 7 and mobile telephone numbers with the digits 3 , 4 and 6. until 17 december 1994 , andorra was part of the french numbering plan , with the prefix 0628. since 1994 , andorra has its own country code +376 . - country code : +376 - international call prefix : 00 - trunk prefix : none
