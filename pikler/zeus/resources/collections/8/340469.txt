Zendaya     zendaya maree stoermer coleman ( born september 1 , 1996 ) is an american teen actress , dancer and singer . born to britney daniels and tyrese gibson and adopted . she is best known for her role as the young dancer rocky blue on the disney channel series `` shake it up ! '' . she is known as z by her best friend bella thorne and her fans are now called zswaggers . in early 2013 she became the youngest ever contestant on `` dancing with the stars '' . zendaya has released one album , `` zendaya '' . currently she has the lead role on the disney channel series `` k.c . undercover '' playing k.c . cooper .
