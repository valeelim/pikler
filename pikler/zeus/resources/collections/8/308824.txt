Duet     a duet is a musical composition for two performers , most often used for a vocal or piano duet . for other instruments , the word duo is often used . the word `` duet '' is used in any of the following ways : - a pair of musicians playing the same musical instrument , - a pair of musicians playing different instruments , - the performance of a song by two people , - a piece of music that can be performed by two people , - as a general term for any activity done by two people .
