Lord Howe Island     lord howe island is a small island about north east of sydney . it is governed by new south wales . about 70 % of the island is part of the lord howe island group which became a unesco world heritage site in 1982. the island was first discovered in 1788 by lieutenant henry lidgbird ball , who was captain of the hms supply . he was taking convicts from sydney to start a new prison colony on norfolk island .
