Andrew Castle     andrew nicholas castle ( born 15 november 1963 ) is a retired british tennis player . he became a television presenter . he was born in epsom , surrey . he retired from tennis in 1992. he was a contestant on the 2008 series of `` strictly come dancing '' and on the 2010 series of `` 71 degrees north '' . other websites . - official website - atp tennis player profile - sports-reference
