Andy Hilbert     andrew john hilbert ( born february 6 , 1981 in howell , michigan ) is a professional ice hockey center that currently plays for the new york islanders of the nhl . he was drafted with the 37th overall pick by the boston bruins in the 2000 nhl entry draft . hilbert has also played 3 seasons with the boston bruins , 1 season with the chicago blackhawks , 1 season with the pittsburgh penguins and 1 season with the minnesota wild .
