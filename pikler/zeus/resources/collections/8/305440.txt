Pecten (bivalve)    pecten ( bivalve ) pecten is a genus of large scallops or saltwater clams , marine bivalve mollusks in the family pectinidae . this is the type genus of the family . the name `` pecten '' is the latin word for a comb or rake . shell petroleum company has used its logo with a shell since 1904. species in the genus `` pecten '' include : - `` pecten maximus '' - the great scallop , which species may also include `` pecten jacobaeus '' - `` pecten novaezealandiae '' - new zealand scallop - `` pecten sulcicostatus '' - south african scallop - `` pecten yessoensis '' - japanese ( sea ) scallop
