Lars von Trier     lars von trier ( b . 30 april 1956 ) is a danish movie director . he has won many prizes . these include the palme d'or , the grand prix , and the prix du jury at the cannes film festival . some of his movies are controversial , such as `` antichrist '' . his newest film , nymphomaniac , was released on december 25 , 2013 in denmark .
