Yohkoh     , is the name of a japanese artificial satellite which was put into orbit . the launch was a project of the institute of space and astronautical science of the university of tokyo . the satellite was sent into orbit on august 30 , 1991. its mission was to study the sun . on september 12 , 2005 the spacecraft burned up during reentry . other websites . - japan aerospace exploration agency ( jaxa ) , yohkoh ( solar-a ) mission profile
