Demography of Afghanistan     afghanistan has around 29.8 million people as of the year 2011. about 2.7 million afghan refugees are living in pakistan and iran . the country is multi-ethnic and multi-lingual . most of afghanistan 's people are of the iranic peoples , notably the pashtuns and tajiks . the pashtun is the largest group followed by tajik , hazara , uzbek , aimak , turkmen , baloch and others . pashto and dari ( persian ) are both the official languages of the country . islam is the religion of more than 99 % of afghanistan .
