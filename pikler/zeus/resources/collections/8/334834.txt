Émile Allegret    émile allegret émile allegret ( 24 april 1907 , dijon – 22 november 1990 , vaux-sur-mer ) was a french soldier and member of the french resistance during world war ii . he graduated from the `` école nationale de l'aviation civile '' ( french civil aviation university ) . after the war , he worked as an air navigation engineer . émile allegret was a commandeur of the légion d'honneur . further reading . - académie nationale de l'air et de l'espace and lucien robineau , `` les français du ciel , dictionnaire historique '' , le cherche midi , june 2005 , isbn 2-7491-0415-7 , p. 29 , `` allégret , émile '' - biography on the `` ordre de la libération '' website
