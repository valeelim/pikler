Rhipidistia     the rhipidistia are lobe-finned fish , and include the ancestors of the tetrapods . the rhipidistia are a subgroup of sarcopterygii which includes the lungfish ( dipnoi ) and the tetrapodomorphs . as cladistic understanding of the vertebrates has improved over the last few decades a monophyletic rhipidistia is now understood to include the ancestor of the whole tetrapoda . rhipidistia - dipnomorpha - †porolepiformes - † '' powichthys '' - dipnoi - tetrapodomorpha - †rhizodontiformes - osteolepidida - osteolepiformes - †tristichopteridae - elpistostegalia - † '' panderichthys '' - tetrapoda references . - palaeos.com page - taxonomicon page
