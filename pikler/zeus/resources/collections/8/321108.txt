Locker     a locker is a small compartment that is used to store things such as books , coats , etc . they are commonly found in schools . they are usually locked with a padlock or a built-in lock . usually there is an array of lockers . each user has his/her own key ( or combination ) and can not open other people 's lockers . there is sometimes a master key used only by the manager which can open all of the lockers . the typical maximum padlock shackle thickness is 5/16 inch ( 8 mm ) , and the typical door thickness is 0.8 mm . high-security lockers typically have a 10 mm or more maximum shackle thickness and a 1.2 mm door thickness .
