Payphone (song)    payphone ( song ) `` payphone '' is a song performed by american pop rock band maroon 5 released on april 17 , 2012 under a & m/octone records in the united states . it serves as the lead single on the band 's fourth studio album `` overexposed '' , set to be released on june 26 , 2012. the song was written by producer benny blanco , bandmates adam levine , james valentine , and featured vocalist wiz khalifa . the song was recorded in southern hollywood , los angeles , california . the cover artwork or the track was leaked to the web over a newsletter on april 11 , 2012 and featured similar varieties of colorful aspects from the band 's upcoming fourth album 's cover , which leaked a day prior on e ! news . the song debuted at number-three on the billboard hot 100 singles ' chart and later peaked at number-two . it also topped the billboard digital songs chart in late april . a music video for the song has not been officially announced yet . however , featured rapper wiz khalifa said in a press conference that he would like the music video to be in a sutle setting where no people may be to relate to the lyrics of the song .
