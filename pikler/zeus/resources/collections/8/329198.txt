Giulio Cesare     giulio cesare in egitto ( ) is an opera in three acts with music by george frideric handel . the italian libretto was written by nicola francesco haym . it was based on an earlier libretto written by giacomo francesco bussani . the opera was written for the royal academy of music . the opera was first performed in london on 20 february 1724. it was a success . it was performed again in 1725 , 1730 , and 1732. it was also performed in france and germany . `` giulio cesare '' was not performed in the 19th century . many people had forgotten all about it . it was not until 1922 that the opera was performed again . since then , it has been performed around the world . it is handel 's best opera . it is known simply as giulio cesare . roles . - giulio cesare ( julius caesar ) - alto castrato - cleopatra , queen of egypt - soprano - tolomeo , her brother and husband , king of egypt - alto castrato - cornelia , widow of pompey - contralto - sesto , her stepson - soprano - achilla , tolomeo 's general - bass
