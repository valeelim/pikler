Crenshaw County, Alabama    crenshaw county , alabama crenshaw county is a county of the u.s. state of alabama . as of 2010 , 13,906 people lived there . its county seat is luverne . crenshaw county was formed from butler , coffee , covington , lowndes and pike counties on november 30 , 1866. its name is in honor of a judge , anderson crenshaw . according to the 2000 census , the county has a total area of .
