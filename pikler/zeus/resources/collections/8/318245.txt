Tristania     tristania is a gothic metal band from norway . the band was created by morten veland . the album `` widow 's weeds '' ( 1998 ) is a mix of gothic rock , gothic metal and doom metal . the album `` beyond the veil '' ( 1999 ) is a mix of gothic metal and doom metal . the album `` world of glass '' ( 2001 ) is a mix of gothic rock , gothic metal , alternative rock , symphonic metal and folk metal .
