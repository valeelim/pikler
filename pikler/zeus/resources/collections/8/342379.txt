ISO 639-3     the iso 639-3 is the third version of a set of international standards that lists short codes for language names . such codes are also known as language codes . before this version was out , there was already iso 639-1 and iso 639-2 , but due to the increase in languages being added to the database , iso639-3 was therefore released , and all language codes have three letters , unlike the previous two , which allowed two letters in the code . each known language has a 3 letter code , and requests to add additional languages into the database can be made at the sil international website . the standard was published by iso on 2007-02-05. other links . - sil international website for iso639-3 language codes
