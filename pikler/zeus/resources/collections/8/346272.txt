Marc Houalla     marc houalla , born the 10th of february 1961 at rueil-malmaison , is a french aerospace engineer . since the 28th of november 2008 , he is the head of the french civil aviation university . graduate from the enac ( iénac l82 ) and having a mba from hec paris ( 1990 ) , he has done different jobs in air transport such as director of sefa before being joining the french civil aviation university . award . - chevalier of the `` ordre national du mérite '' further reading . - académie nationale de l'air et de l'espace and lucien robineau , `` les français du ciel , dictionnaire historique '' , le cherche midi , june 2005 , 782 p.
