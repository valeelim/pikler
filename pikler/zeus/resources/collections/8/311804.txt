Kimberly Wyatt     kimberly kaye wyatt ( born february 4 , 1982 ) is an american showgirl , singer-songwriter , dancer , model , actress and choreographer . she is probably best known for being in the pussycat dolls . she joined the pussycat dolls in 2003. in 2010 , she said that she left the group , and made a new band called her majesty & the wolves .
