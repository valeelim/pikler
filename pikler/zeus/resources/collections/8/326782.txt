Anti-Counterfeiting Trade Agreement     the anti-counterfeiting trade agreement ( or acta ) is an international treaty that wants to make it easier to fight product piracy to protect intellectual property . this includes fighting counterfeits and copyright infringement , and imposing stricter regulations on generic drugs . acta is based on an earlier agreement called trips . japan is the only country that has ratified this . european union also has signed it but many people in europe is against the agreement because it can violate human rights especially the freedom of expression and privacy .
