Neoproterozoic     the neoproterozoic era lasted from 1,000 to 541 million years ago ( mya ) . in the first period , the tonian , the global supercontinent rodinia broke up into separate land masses . in the second period , the cryogenian , the earth had two great ice ages , the sturtian and marinoan glaciations . these ice ages were so severe they are sometimes called snowball earth . in the third period , the ediacaran , the earliest fossils of multicellular organisms are found , the ediacaran biota .
