Mariner 4     mariner 4 was a nasa spacecraft . it was the fourth spacecraft in the mariner program . it was sent to study the planet mars . it was the first successful mission to mars . the spacecraft was launched on november 28 , 1964 at cape canaveral air force station . mariner 4 reached mars after an eight-month journey . the spacecraft became the first spacecraft to take close-up photographs of another planet . the spacecraft also had other instruments to study the atmosphere of mars . the total cost of the mariner 4 mission is estimated at $ 83.2 million . total research , development , launch , and support costs for the mariner series of spacecraft ( mariners 1 through 10 ) was approximately $ 554 million . other websites . - photographs that mariner 4 took
