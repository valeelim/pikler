Fowey     fowey is a small town and cargo port at the mouth of the river fowey in south cornwall , united kingdom . according to the 2001 census it had a population of 2,273. fowey is in the south coast ( eastern section ) of the cornwall area of outstanding natural beauty . it lies at the end of the saints ' way a long-distance footpath and has ferries across the river to polruan ( foot ) and bodinnick ( vehicle ) . there are many historic buildings in the town , including the ruins of st catherine 's castle , while readymoney cove has a local beach .
