Window (computing)    window ( computing ) in computing , a window is an area on a computer screen . this area usually shows an application or folder . windows can be made bigger or smaller , and can be opened or closed . they can also be minimized . they are usually in the shape of a rectangle . windows are very obvious in graphical user interfaces as they are the main means of displaying information to users . the most famous of these is the appropriately named `` windows '' operating system made by microsoft .
