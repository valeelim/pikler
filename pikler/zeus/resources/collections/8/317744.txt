Annie Raines     annie raines ( born 3 july 1969 near boston , massachusetts ) is an american harp player , piano player and singer . raines took up the harmonica at the age of 17. she left antioch college to make a musical career . she was fascinated by the sound of muddy waters , little walter jacobs , and sonny boy williamson . she was influenced by this blues master . she began to play on the streets and at local boston clubs . she went to chicago where she met and played with pinetop perkins and james cotton . she played in chicagos blues clubs . during this time she developed a style of her own . she teamed with guitarist paul rishell who introduced her to the country blues tradition . other websites . - homepage paul rishell and annie raines
