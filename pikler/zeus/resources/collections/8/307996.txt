Luton     luton is a large town in bedfordshire , england . it is about north of london . the football club is in league 2 and play at kenilworth road . their nickname is the hatters . they are called this because luton has a history of making straw hats . luton town has a large immigrant population , which consists predominantly of people of pakistani and bengali origin . related pages . - vauxhall motors - luton airport
