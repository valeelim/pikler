Wheeling, West Virginia    wheeling , west virginia wheeling is a city in the u.s. state of west virginia . it is in both ohio county and marshall county . it is the county seat of ohio county . wheeling is the main city of the wheeling metropolitan statistical area . the city had 28,486 ( 31,059 in ohio county , 360 in marshall county ) people as of the 2010 census . other websites . - city of wheeling , west virginia official site - wheeling convention and visitor 's bureau
