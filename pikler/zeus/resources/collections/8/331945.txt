Alluvial fan     an alluvial fan is a fan-shaped deposit of soil and rocks . it forms where a fast-flowing river spreads out over a flat plain . they are usually found in between mountain ranges that are crumbling away . if it rains a lot , the fan area will usually flood . related pages . - river delta other websites . - irrigation in the alluvial fan of punata , bolivia - irrigation in the alluvial fan of garmsar , iran - flood recession cropping in the alluvial fan of okavango , botswana - irrigation in alluvial fans in baluchistan - irrigation of alluvial fans
