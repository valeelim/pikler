Kubla Khan     kubla khan is a poem by samuel taylor coleridge . he wrote it in 1797 , and it was published in 1816. it is a poem from the romantic era , a time when people often made art about nature and emotions . coleridge said that he wrote the poem after a dream he had , while on opium . he said that he was interrupted by a person from porlock while he was writing it , and never got to finish it . some people say that this is not completely true . the first stanza of the poem describes a pleasure dome and a river called alph , and the second stanza talks about an abyssinian maid .
