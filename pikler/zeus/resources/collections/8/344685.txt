Lysyl oxidase     lysyl oxidase is an enzyme which speeds up coacervation , a reaction which attaches protoelastin molecules to each other , creating the protein elastin . elastin is a component of the human skin which lets it stretch without being deformed permanently . lysyl oxidase is made up of various amino acids ( the building blocks of proteins ) which make up the final protein : elastin . elastin is made of valine , alanine , glycine , and proline .
