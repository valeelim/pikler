Pripyat River     the pripyat river or prypiat river is a river in eastern europe . it is about long . it flows east through ukraine , belarus , and ukraine again . it drains into the dnieper . the pripyat passes through the zone of alienation around the chernobyl reactor , site of the nuclear disaster . it is polluted with radionuclides . the amount of caesium-137 in river sediments continues to go up . the city of prypiat , ukraine ( population 45,000 ) was completely evacuated after the chernobyl disaster . tributaries . - horyn river ( `` 659 km ; 22,700 km '' ) - sluch ( `` 451 km ; 13,800 km '' )
