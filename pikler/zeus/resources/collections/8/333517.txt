Tok Pisin     tok pisin ( pronounced : ) is a market language used in parts of the south pacific , mainly papua new guinea and the solomon islands , but also in areas of vanuatu , where a second language is also spoken known as bislama . a market language is one which is used as an everyday language between people who do n't speak a common language . tok pisin is a creole language , meaning that it 's a mixture of other languages , mainly english , german and tahitian .
