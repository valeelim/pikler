Interview with the Vampire: The Vampire Chronicles    interview with the vampire : the vampire chronicles interview with the vampire : the vampire chronicles is a 1994 american fantasy drama horror movie directed by neil jordan . it is about a vampire named lestat who turns a plantation owner into a vampire . the movie is set in new orleans during 1791 , paris in 1871 and san francisco , california in 1988. the movie stars tom cruise , stephen rea , antonio banderas , christian slater and kirsten dunst . the movie is based on the 1976 novel `` interview with the vampire '' by anne rice .
