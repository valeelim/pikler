Skate 2     skate 2 is a skateboarding video game that was published by electronic arts . it is available for the playstation 3 and the xbox 360 systems . the game was the second in the `` skate '' video game series . it was followed by `` skate 3 '' which was released in 2010. the game got very good reviews with a 7.5 out of 10 from gamespot and a 83 out of 100 from metacritic . other websites . - official game website
