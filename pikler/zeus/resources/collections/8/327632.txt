Kawasaki, Kanagawa    kawasaki , kanagawa in the edo period , `` kawasaki-juku '' was 2nd of the 53 shogunate-maintained waystations ( `` shuku-eki '' ) along the tōkaidō road which connected edo and kyoto.. kawasaki occupies a belt of land stretching about 30 km along the south bank of the tama river . the river is the boundary with tokyo . in kawasaki , there are seven wards : - kawasaki - saiwai - nakahara - takatsu - tama - miyamae - aso related pages . - `` the fifty-three stations of the tōkaidō '' - kawasaki frontale other websites . - kawasaki city website ,
