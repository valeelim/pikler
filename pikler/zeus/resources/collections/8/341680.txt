Sylvain Cloutier     sylvain richard cloutier ( born february 13 , 1974 in mont-laurier , quebec ) is a canadian professional ice hockey and the current player coach of the hull stingrays of the elite ice hockey league . he was drafted with the 70th overall pick by the detroit red wings in the 1992 nhl entry draft . he played 7 games in the nhl with the chicago blackhawks but never scored a point . he became the player coach of the hull stingrays of the eihl in 2009. he is siblings with former nhl goaltender dan cloutier .
