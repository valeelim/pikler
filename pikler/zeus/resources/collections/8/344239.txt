Notre-Dame-d'Auteuil     notre-dame-d'auteuil is a church in the orne department . it was built around 1100. it is noticeable by its very well-kept romanesque structure and an important carved decoration , which shows the multiple inspiration of the sculptors between normandy , ile-de-france and val-de-loire . references . - gérard bourdin , jean-marie foubert , jean-pascal foucher .
