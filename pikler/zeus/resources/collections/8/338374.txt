Troy McClure     troy mcclure is a character in the simpsons voiced by phil hartman . he first appeared in the episode `` homer vs. lisa and the 8th commandment '' . mcclure is a washed-up actor , usually shown doing low-level work , such as hosting infomercials and educational movies . he appears as the central character in `` a fish called selma '' , in which he marries selma bouvier to protect his career from failing and quash rumors about his personal life . mcclure also 'hosts ' two episodes named `` the simpsons 138th episode spectacular '' and `` the simpsons spin-off showcase '' . mcclure was based on b movie actors troy donahue and doug mcclure as well as hartman himself . following hartman 's murder on may 28 , 1998 , the character no longer appeared on the show , making his final appearance in the tenth season in `` bart the mother '' . mcclure is often cited as one of the series ' most popular characters ; in 2006 ign ranked him first on their list of the `` top 25 simpsons peripheral characters '' . other websites . - `` the stellar acting career of troy mcclure '' at the simpsons archive
