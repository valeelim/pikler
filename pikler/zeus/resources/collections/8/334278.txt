Serial Mom     serial mom is a 1994 dark comedy that stars kathleen turner as the `` serial mom '' , sam waterston as husband eugene and ricki lake as misty . it was released april 13 , 1994. the `` serial mom '' , named beverly , is a typical housewife from baltimore raising her kids , although she tends to kill on just the mildest criticism or the slightest insult . she also performs obscene telephone calls . `` serial mom '' got mixed reviews , though it has become a cult classic over the years . `` serial mom '' was directed by john waters .
