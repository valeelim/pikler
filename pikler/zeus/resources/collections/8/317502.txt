Vietnam at the Olympics     vietnam was first in the olympic games in 1952 as state of vietnam . after the partition of vietnam only the republic of vietnam competed from 1956 to 1972. they became the socialist republic of vietnam beginning in1980 . they have never been in the winter olympics . the vietnam olympic committee was formed in 1976 and recognized by the ioc in 1979. related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - vietnam profile at london2012.com
