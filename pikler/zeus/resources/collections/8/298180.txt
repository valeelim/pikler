The Adventures of Pluto Nash     the adventures of pluto nash is an 2002 american science fiction action comedy movie . it was produced by martin bregman , michael scott bregman and louis a. stroller and was directed by ron underwood . the movie was released on august 16 , 2002 in north america . the movie received very negative reviews with a 6 % rating on rotten tomatoes and a 12 out of 100 from metacritic . the movie was a huge box office bomb , the movie grossed around $ 7.1 million and the budget for the movie was $ 100 million . the movie was nominated for 5 golden raspberry awards . cast . - eddie murphy as pluto nash/rex crater - randy quaid as bruno - rosario dawson as dina lake - joe pantoliano as mogan - jay mohr as tony francis - luis guzmán as felix laranga - james rebhorn as belcher - peter boyle as rowland - pam grier as flura nash - john cleese as james - burt young as gino - miguel a. núñez , jr. as miguel - illeana douglas as dr. mona zimmer - richard raybourne as bartender phil - alec baldwin as michael zoroaster marucci ( mzm ) ( uncredited )
