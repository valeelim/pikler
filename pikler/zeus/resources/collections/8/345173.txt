Google Play     google play is a platform by google . it is a online store for music , books , movies and android applications . before the founding of google play , there were 4 different stores by google : - google music - google books - google movies - android market on march 7 , 2012 , google announced that it will put these 4 services into just `` one '' , google play . android market was started after the launch of the apple app store on 22th october 2008 .
