Up! (album)    up ! ( album ) up ! is the fourth studio album released by country music artist shania twain . it was released in 2002. shania twain and mutt lange wrote all of the songs . singles released off the album include the title track , `` it only hurts when i 'm breathing '' , `` ka-ching ! `` , `` i 'm gon na getcha good ! `` , `` forever and for always '' , `` she 's not just a pretty face '' , and `` when you kiss me '' .
