Maidan Shar     maidan shar ( - maidān šhār ; ; also `` maydan '' , `` meydan '' , `` maidan '' ) is the capital of wardak province , afghanistan . about 35,008 people live in the town as of 2003. most of the people in maidan shar do agriculture , livestock , gardening and trade . many of the people are poor . by the end of 2008 , there were a total of 14 schools with about 9268 students . there were about 275 teachers teaching in these schools .
