I Did It Again    oops ! ... i did it again oops ! ... i did it again ! is the second album released by american pop music singer britney spears on jive records . four singles were released from the album , the title track , `` lucky '' , `` stronger '' , and `` do n't let me be the last to know '' . it also features the first song britney had a hand in writing , titled `` dear diary '' .
