Sanuki Province     the province had borders with awa and iyo provinces . in the meiji period , the provinces of japan were converted into prefectures . the maps of japan and sanuki province were reformed in the 1870s . sanuki faced the seto inland sea . `` tamura jinja '' was the chief shinto shrine ( `` ichinomiya '' ) of sanuki . related pages . - provinces of japan - prefectures of japan - list of regions of japan - list of islands of japan other websites . - murdoch 's map of provinces , 1903
