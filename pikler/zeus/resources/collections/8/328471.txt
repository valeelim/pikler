Jorge Lorenzo     jorje lorenzo guerrrero ( born 4 may 1987 ) is a spanish motorcyclist . he was the world champion in motogp in 2010. in 2008 , lorenzo signed a contract with the official yamaha team . he was the partner of seven-time world champion , valentino rossi . in the portugal championship , lorenzo got his first victory in motogp . on 10 october 2010 , in malaysia , jorge was proclaimed world champion of motogp .
