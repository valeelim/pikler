Dodge City     dodge city is the county seat of ford county , kansas , united states . named after nearby fort dodge , the city is famous in american culture for its history as a wild frontier town of the old west . the 2010 census showed the city population was 27,340. in the 1880s dodge city became a boomtown , with thousands of cattle passing annually through its stockyards . the peak years of the cattle trade in dodge city were from 1883 to 1884. during that time the town grew tremendously . dodge city had more famous ( and infamous ) gunfighters working at one time or another than any other town in the west , many of whom participated in the dodge city war of 1883. it also boasted the usual array of saloons , gambling halls , and brothels : they were set up to separate a cowboy from his hard-earned cash . famous were the long branch saloon and the china doll brothel . meat packing is the main industry in dodge city . the city also has farm implement manufacturing , and is a supply center for area agriculture . livestock-raising is a major activity while wheat and sorghum are the area 's main crops .
