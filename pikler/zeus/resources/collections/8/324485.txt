Terry Balsamo     terry balsamo ( born october 8 , 1972 in tampa , florida ) , is an american guitarist . he is the current guitarist of the alternative metal band cold and the former guitarist of evanescence . balsamo was hired as evanescence 's new lead guitarist after ben moody left the band . he co-wrote the album the open door with amy lee and also participated in the songwriting process of evanescence . balsamo uses ibanez guitars . band projects . - evanescence - guitar ( 2003–present ) - cold - guitar ( 1999–2003 , 2009 tour only ) - shaft - guitar ( 1996–1999 ) - limp bizkit - guitar ( 1995 )
