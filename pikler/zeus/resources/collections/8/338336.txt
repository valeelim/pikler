Spring bud     spring bud is the color that used to be called `` spring green '' before the x11 web color spring green was formulated in 1987 when the x11 colors were first invented . this color is now called `` spring bud '' to avoid confusion with the web color . the color spring bud is also called soft spring green or spring green ( traditional ) . the first written use of `` spring green '' as a color name in english ( meaning the color that is now called `` spring bud '' ) was in 1766. meaning of spring bud . - the color `` spring bud '' is representative of and brings to mind the young buds of growing vegetation in early spring , and thus it is associated with new life . related pages . - list of colors
