Herbal     a herbal is a collection of plant descriptions : very often , it also lists the properties of each plant , or its use , for example for medicine or alchemy . a herebal can also classify the plants it describes . one of the oldest known herbals is the vienna dioscurides dating from the 6th century . physicians and apothecaries were the main users of such books . herbals were also important for the study of botany . hieronymus bock descrived the plants of southwestern germany , in his 16th century herbal ; he was the first to mention certain morphological features . other pages . - herbarium or herbar - a collection of plant specimens
