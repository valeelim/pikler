Stove     a stove is an enclosed heated space . the word is usually taken to mean an enclosed space in which fuel is burned to provide heating , either to heat the space in which the stove is found or to heat the stove itself , and anything placed on it . this article is principally concerned with enclosed stoves burning solid fuels for room heating . a kitchen stove is a kitchen appliance designed for cooking food . kitchen stoves rely on the application of direct heat for the cooking process and may also contain an oven underneath it which is used for baking .
