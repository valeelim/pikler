Party Rock     party rock is the debut studio album by american electro hop duo , lmfao . the album was released on july 7 , 2009. the album was nominated for best electronic/dance album at the 52nd grammy awards but lost to lady gaga 's `` the fame '' . the ep version was released on itunes on july 1 , 2008. the album peaked at # 33 on the billboard 200 and peaked at # 31 on the uk dance chart .
