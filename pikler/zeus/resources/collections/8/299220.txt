Linkinhorne     linkinhorne is a parish and village in southeast cornwall , united kingdom . the village itself is at and is approximately four miles ( 6.5 km ) northwest of callington and seven miles ( 11 km ) south of launceston . as well as linkinhorne itself , other settlements in the parish include bray shop , caradon town , downgate , henwood , ley mill , minions , and upton cross . the area is bordered by the river inny in the north-east and bodmin moor to the west . the valley of the river lynher runs through the parish . the parish is largely rural but west of the b3254 road from launceston to liskeard it includes part of caradon hill , once an industrialised mining area . the parish church of st melor is built of granite and dates from the 15th century .
