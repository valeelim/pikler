Selman Waksman     selman waksman ( july 22 , 1888- august 16 , 1973 ) was an american biochemist and a microbiologist . his research into organic substances and their decomposition helped the discovery of streptomycin and other antibiotic medicines . he won the nobel prize in physiology or medicine in 1952 `` for his discovery of `` streptomycin '' the first antibiotic active against tuberculosis .
