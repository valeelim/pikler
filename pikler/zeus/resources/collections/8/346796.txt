Ash-throated antwren     the ash-throated antwren ( `` herpsilochmus parkeri '' ) is a bird in the antbird family . the first species was found in northern peru in 1983. antbirds do anting . the bird rubs ants ( or other arthropods ) on the feathers before discarded or eating them . while this has conventionally been considered a way to remove and control feather parasites
