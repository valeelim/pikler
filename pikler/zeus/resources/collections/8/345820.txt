National Anthem of Barbados     in plenty and in time of need is the national anthem of the country of barbados . it was written by irving burgie . the music was written by c. van roland edwards . as one part of the west indies federation barbados ' anthem was supposed to be forged from the love of liberty . the current anthem was made with barbados ' moves toward independence . this song was then taken by barbados when it became independent in 1966 .
