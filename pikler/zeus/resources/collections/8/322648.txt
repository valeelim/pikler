Snowstar Records     snowstar records is an independent dutch record label based in utrecht . the label was founded in 2003 by cedric muyres . snowstar is specialised in indie , singer/songwriter and folk . since 2003 snowstar records is active in the alternative pop music scene . initially as a punk rock label operating from limburg , but the focus shifted to indie , singer/songwriter , folk and americana . since 2008 the label is based in utrecht . with the release of i am oak , on claws in 2010 the label obtained a high brand awareness . stefan breuer ( band member i am oak and lost bear ) is involved in the production process ( recording and mixing ) by most of the releases from snowstar records , while tammo kersbergen ( also band member i am oak and lost bear ) has provided much of the mastering . in 2008 snowstar record celebrated their fiftieth anniversary with five new releases and a party in db â€™ s , utrecht ( the netherlands ) . snowstar is also building on their releases abroad , by working together with foreign labels .
