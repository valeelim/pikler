Oman at the Olympics     oman at the olympics is a history which includes 8 games in 7 countries and 30+ athletes . since 1982 , oman has contributed to the growth of the `` olympic movement '' . the international olympic committee 's official abbreviation for oman is oma . teams from oman have taken part in the summer olympic games but not the winter olympic games . no oman athlete has won an olympic medal . the oman olympic committee was formed and recognized in 1982. related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - oman profile at london2012.com
