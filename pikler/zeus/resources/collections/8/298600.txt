Anna and the King     anna and the king is a 1999 movie , loosely based on `` anna and the king of siam '' . the movie was directed by andy tennant and stars jodie foster and chow yun-fat . it was mostly shot in malaysia . it was an academy award nominee in 2000 for best art direction and best costume design . the movie tells the story of the romance between the king of siam ( now thailand ) and the widowed english schoolteacher , anna leonowens , during the 1860s . it is the true story of anna leonowens ' experiences .
