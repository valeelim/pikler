Kim Wilde     kim wilde ( born kim smith on 18 november 1960 ) is a british singer , television presenter , gardener and writer . she was born in chiswick , middlesex . she is the eldest child of rock musician marty wilde . she had her first hit single , `` kids in america '' in 1981. she also released `` kim wilde '' , her first album , in 1981. in 1983 wilde won best british female solo artist ( uk ) at the brit awards . wilde released her most recent album `` wilde winter songbook '' on 11 november 2013. it is a christmas album . other websites . - official website
