Maury Povich     maurice richard `` maury '' povich ( born january 17 , 1939 ) is an american television talk show host . he hosted the tabloid talk show `` maury '' . povich graduated from the landon school in 1957. he graduated from the university of pennsylvania in 1962 with a degree in television journalism . povich is married to former news anchor connie chung . povich hosted the 2000 revival of the television game show `` twenty one '' .
