Nellyville     nellyville is the second studio album by american rapper , nelly . the album was released on june 25 , 2002. the album first debuted at # 1 on the billboard charts and also sold more than 714,000 records in its first week of release . the album peaked at # 1 on the billboard 200 , # 2 on the german albums chart , # 2 on the uk albums chart and # 6 on the finnish albums chart .
