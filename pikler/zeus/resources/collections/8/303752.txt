University of Antofagasta     the university of antofagasta ( ) is a university in antofagasta , chile . it is one of the universities in the north of chile . it was founded in 1981 thanks to the fusion of the tus of antofagasta and the regional headquarter of the university of chile , it was the point of start of the actual university . faculties . - basic sciences - health sciences - human sciences & education - engineering - medicine & odontology - marine resources - law and social sciences other websites . - university of antofagasta
