30 Hari Mencari Cinta     30 hari mencari cinta ( which means `` 30 days looking for love '' ) is a 2004 indonesian romantic comedy movie drama . it is directed by upi avianto and stars maria agnes . the music for the movie was made by sheila on 7 , a popular rock band from indonesia . cast . - maria agnes as olin - vino g. bastian as erik - luna maya as barbara - dinna olivia as keke - revaldo as bono - rionaldo stockhorst as axel - nirina zubir as gwen
