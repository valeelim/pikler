Is It the Beat?    is it the beat ? `` is it the beat ? '' is a song by american singer selena . the dance-pop song peaked at number four on the us hot dance club songs . it was also the spanish version of `` enamorada de ti '' . selena had recorded several versions for this song . she had performed the freestyle version at live concerts in 1989-1991. the single was not released until after selena 's murder . it was included in the movie soundtrack in 1997. it was released with dance-club remixes .
