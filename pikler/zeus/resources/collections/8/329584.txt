Plano, Texas    plano , texas plano is a city in the state of texas in the united states . it is the ninth largest city in the state . over 280,000 people live in plano . it has a humid subtropical climate ( `` cfa '' in the koeppen climate classification ) . plano has the headquarters of many large businesses , including : - frito-lay - dell services , a division of dell - hp enterprise services , a division of hewlett-packard - dr pepper snapple group - siemens plm software , a division of the siemens group - ericsson - j. c. penney - capital one financial services - pizza hut
