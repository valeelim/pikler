Chishima Province     the boundaries of this ancient province are generally mirrored in part of what is today called nemuro subprefecture . after 1869 , the northern japanese island was known as hokkaido . regional subdivisions were established . chishima province was one of the new entities which were created in 1869. in the meiji period , the provinces of japan were converted into prefectures . the maps of japan including chishima province were reformed in the 1870s . in 1882 , chishima and the other ten provinces of hokkaido were reorganized as hakodate prefecture , sapporo prefecture and nemuro prefecture . related pages . - provinces of japan - prefectures of japan - list of regions of japan - list of islands of japan other websites . - murdoch 's map of provinces , 1903
