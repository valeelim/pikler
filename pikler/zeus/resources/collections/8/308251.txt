Not Myself Tonight     `` not myself tonight '' is a song by american singer christina aguilera , from her 2010 album `` bionic '' . the song was written by jamal `` polow da don '' jones , ester dean , jason perry and greg curtis . it is a uptempo electropop song that using synthesizers and tribal drums . its lyrics refer to adopting a different persona on the dancefloor . it was released on april 13 , 2010 as the first single from aguilera 's `` bionic '' album . `` not myself tonight '' was a hit single in asian area .
