NBA 2K11     nba 2k11 is a basketball video game . it was developed by visual concepts and 3k sports and published by 2k sports . it was released on october 5 , 2010 on xbox 360 , playstation 2 , playstation 3 , psp , pc , while the wii version came out on october 21 , 2010. michael jordan is the cover athlete of the game and is featured in the game in different ways . `` nba 2k11 '' is the successor to `` nba 2k10 '' .
