Amame     `` amame '' ( ) is a song by american singer selena . `` amame '' became the fourth and last single from `` entre a mi mundo '' ( 1992 ) . `` amame '' peaked at number 27 on the hot latin tracks and number 21 on the regional mexican airplay chart . `` amame '' was remixed for `` anthology '' ( 1998 ) . it included a ballad version of the song .
