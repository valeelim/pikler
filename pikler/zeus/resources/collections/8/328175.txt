Type 98 320 mm mortar     , also known as a `` 32-cm '' gun , was a mortar of the imperial japanese army ( ija ) . it is an example of a type of artillery called `` spigot mortars '' . this artillery piece was used by the japanese military during world war ii . during world war ii , the ija had 12 type 98s at the battle of iwo jima . the type 98 mortars were also used on okinawa . related pages . - artillery of japan - list of field guns - list of heavy mortars - list of howitzers other websites . - type 98 on taki 's imperial japanese army page
