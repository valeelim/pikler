Alghero     alghero ( italian pronunciation : [ alˈɡɛro ] ; , ipa : [ ɫəɫˈɣe ] , locally : [ lalˈɣe ] ; ; ) , is a city of about 44,000 citizens in italy . it lies in the province of sassari in northwestern sardinia , next to the sea . in the 1930s , the swedish writer amelie posse brazdova wrote a book entitled `` sardinia side show '' , where she told the complete story of 2 years she spent `` interned '' in alghero old town during world war i. other websites . - city of alghero official website - official alghero tourism web site - alghero tourist informations and webcam live - alghero tourism and town information - alghero tourism and hotels informations
