Larry Johnson     larry johnson ( born november 19 , 1979 ) is a former american football runningback in the national football league . he played college football for penn state . johnson spent most of his career with the kansas city chiefs . after replacing the often-injured veteran priest holmes , johnson excelled . he played very well in 2005 and 2006 and far passed the 1,000 yard mark that notable backs and receivers aim for . however , after an argument with head coach todd haley in 2009 , he was released . he finished the season with the cincinnati bengals , but was not resigned . johnson spent portions of offseasons with the washington redskins and miami dolphins , but was cut both times . references . - chiefs to cut ties with larry johnson , `` fantasy knuckleheads '' - dolphins cut rb larry johnson , `` palm beach post '' - johnson uses slurs for haley , reporters `` espn ''
