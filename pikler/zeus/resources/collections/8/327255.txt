Ned Beatty     ned thomas beatty ( born july 6 , 1937 ) is an american actor . he was born in louisville , kentucky . he is known for his movie work including `` nashville '' ( 1975 ) , `` all the president 's men '' ( 1976 ) , `` network '' ( 1976 ) , `` the big easy '' ( 1987 ) and `` prelude to a kiss '' ( 1992 ) .
