Gemma Arterton     gemma christina arterton ( born 12 january 1986 ) is a british actress . she was born and raised in gravesend , kent and lives in london . she studied at the royal academy of dramatic art . she starred in st trinian 's , was a bond girl in quantum of solace and played the title character in tamara drewe . she co-starred with justin timberlake in the 2013 film runner runner . she was nominated for the orange rising star award in 2011 .
