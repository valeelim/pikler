Sri Lanka Railways S11     sri lanka railways class s11 is a diesel multiple-unit ( dmu ) train , built for sri lanka railways by rites ltd , an indian state infrastructure corporation . the dmus are currently being imported in phases , with the last batch arriving in sri lanka in march 2012. they were built to replace locomotive-hauled passenger trains . twenty s11 dmus were ordered to strengthen long distance travel on the coastal railway line from colombo to matara .
