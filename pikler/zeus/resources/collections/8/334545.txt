Muriel's Wedding    muriel 's wedding muriel 's wedding is a 1994 australian comedy-drama movie . it was directed by p. j. hogan and stars toni collette as muriel heslop . she is a socially inept , plain , dowdy , overweight young woman who has low self-esteem and is obsessed with abba music . bill hunter plays her uncaring father bill and rachel griffiths plays her friend rhonda . `` muriel 's wedding '' was released 29 september 1994 ( during 1995 in the united states ) . it is set in new south wales and was shot there and in queensland .
