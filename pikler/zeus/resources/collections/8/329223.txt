Hoax     the word hoax refers to a concept or idea that is not true . for example , a person makes up false accusations about being victimized by other people , or an article may make things up that cause people to believe as though they are true when they are not . hoaxes come in all types of forms : for example , april fools ' day tricks that are shown in a time related to april 1st , then shown to be a hoax later .
