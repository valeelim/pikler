Entertainment Tonight     entertainment tonight is an entertainment news program . it was first broadcast on september 14 , 1981. it is syndicated by cbs television distribution in the united states , canada and in many countries around the world . it is the longest-running entertainment news program . the program was the first syndicated program distributed by satellite . the main anchor since 1982 had been mary hart . she left the program on may 20 , 2011. mark steines and nancy o'dell took on the roles of main hosts after hart left .
