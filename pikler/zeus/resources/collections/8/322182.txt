Hida Province     in the late 1580s , kanamori nagachika occupied hida province . kanamori 's heirs held the province through the edo period when it came under direct rule by the tokugawa shogunate . in the meiji period , the provinces of japan were converted into prefectures . the maps of japan and hida province were reformed in the 1870s . `` hidaichinomiya-minashi jinja '' was the chief shinto shrine ( `` ichinomiya '' ) of hida . related pages . - provinces of japan - prefectures of japan - list of regions of japan - list of islands of japan other websites . - murdoch 's map of provinces , 1903
