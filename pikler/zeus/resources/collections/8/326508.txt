Rogers Centre     rogers centre is a sports stadium in toronto , ontario , canada . it is right next door to the cn tower . it is the home field of the major league baseball team called the toronto blue jays . until 2015 , it was also home to the toronto argonauts of the canadian football league . when rogers centre opened in 1989 , it was called the skydome . it hosted the wwf 's wrestlemania vi on april 1 , 1990 and wrestlemania x8 on march 17 , 2002. wrestlemania x8 attracted the largest ever paid crowd to skydome with 68,237 fans . about 743 indian elephants have fit inside the rogers centre . sources . - `` green cathedrals '' , by philip j. lowry other websites . - official website
