Synagogue of Besançon     the synagogue of besançon ( ) is a synagogue in the french city of besançon , bourgogne-franche-comté . it was built in the center of the city between 1869 and 1871 by pierre manotte , and has a moroccan style . this jewish shrine replaced an old flad that held everyone in besançon , about 600 persons in the 1860s . the new building just can hold about 200 persons . all rites are sephardi . related pages . - besançon - jewish cemetery of besançon
