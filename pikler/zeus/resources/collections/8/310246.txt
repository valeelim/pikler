Endrin     endrin is an insecticide that was commonplace among cotton growers and for the treament of cereals . it was also used to kill rodents . it is a neurotoxin . it is a stereoisomer of dieldrin . in the soil , it can take up to twelve years to decay . it is one of the twelve substances listed in the stockholm convention on persistent organic pollutants , and its production , trade and use is banned since 2004 .
