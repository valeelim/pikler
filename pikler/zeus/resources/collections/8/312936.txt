Pellegrino Ernetti     pellegrino ernetti ( 1925-1994 ) was a roman catholic priest . he joined the benedictine order at the age of 16. he later became the most famous exorcist from venice . he was a musician . he wrote books on music theory . he also claimed to have invented a time machine called the chronovisor . he died at age 68. before dying he took back his claims about the chronovisor . however francois brune , another priest , and journalist peter krassa argued he was pressured into doing this .
