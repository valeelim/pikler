Ordubad District     ordubad is a raion of azerbaijan . it is in the nakhchivan autonomous republic . ordubad is the capital city and the second largest town in nakhchivan . in addition ordubad raion includes 47 villages and settlements . the historic name is goghtan . this is a turkic way of saying the armenian word vortvat . this means `` the wormy place '' . the banks of the araxes river has many worms . these worms were used to make inks for printing .
