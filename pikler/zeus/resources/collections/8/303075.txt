Loudoun County, Virginia    loudoun county , virginia loudoun county ( ) is a county in the commonwealth of virginia . it is part of the washington metropolitan area . the 2010 u.s. census said 312,311 people live there . the number of people grew 84 percent over the 2000 census of 169,599. its county seat is leesburg . as of 2007 , loudoun county has the highest median household income of any county in the united states ( $ 107,207 ) . this is higher than nearby fairfax county , virginia ( $ 105,241 ) .
