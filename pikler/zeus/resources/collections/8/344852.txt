Qatar Bahrain Causeway     the qatar bahrain causeway ( also called the qatar bahrain friendship bridge ) is a planned causeway between the two arab states of qatar and bahrain . it was announced on the 13th of december 2008 that construction would start in early 2009. as of november 2010 , construction had not started . on april 27 , 2011 , an announcement was made that building the causeway would start in 2011 for completion in 2015 .
