Chōryaku     events of the `` chōryaku '' era . - 1037 ( `` chōryaku 1 , 2nd day of the 7th month '' ) : the eldest son of emperor go-suzaku ( prince chikihito , who was would become emperor go-reizei ) has his coming of age ceremony . - 1037 ( `` chōryaku 1 , 17th day of the 8th month '' ) : go-suzaku formally names chikihito as his heir and crown prince . - 1038 ( `` chōryaku 2 , 1st day of the 9th month '' ) : fujiwara no chikaie was killed by a servant during an attempted robbery ; and all the greats of the fujiwara went into mourning . related pages . - heian period other websites . - national diet library , `` the japanese calendar '' -- historical overview plus illustrative images from library 's collection
