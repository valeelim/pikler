Kōzuke Province     the ancient capital city of the province was near maebashi . in the nara period , kōzuke was part of keno province . this was changed in the reforms of the taihō code in 701. in the meiji period , the provinces of japan were converted into prefectures . the maps of japan and kōzuke province were reformed in the 1870s . `` ichinomiyanukisaki jinja '' was the chief shinto shrine ( `` ichinomiya '' ) of kōzuke . related pages . - provinces of japan - prefectures of japan - list of regions of japan - list of islands of japan other websites . - murdoch 's map of provinces , 1903
