Nauru at the Olympics     the pacific island nation nauru ( nru ) was at the summer olympic games first in the 1996 games in atlanta . the country was then in sydney in 2000 , in athens in 2004 , and in beijing in 2008. the nation is known for its weightlifting tradition . all seven athletes have been weightlifters . nauru is the smallest nation ( by population ) in the 202 member international olympic committee . other websites . - nauru . olympics at sports-reference.com
