Core (Stone Temple Pilots album)    core ( stone temple pilots album ) core is the first album by the american rock band stone temple pilots . it was released on september 29 , 1992 through atlantic records . the album made # 1 on the `` billboard '' heatseekers chart and # 3 on the `` billboard '' 200. it was the band 's best-selling album . all lyrics written by scott weiland , except where noted .
