Uzbek language     uzbek ( `` o'zbek tili '' or `` o'zbekcha '' in latin script , `` ўзбек тили '' or `` ўзбекча '' in cyrillic script ; أۇزبېك تیلی in arabic script ) is a turkic language . it is the official language of uzbekistan . it has about 40 million native speakers . it is spoken by the uzbeks in uzbekistan and elsewhere in central asia . uzbek belongs to the southeastern turkic or uyghur family of turkic languages .
