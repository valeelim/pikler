Battle of Mactan     the battle of mactan was fought in the philippines on april 27 , 1521. it was the first time the filipino people fought back against the spanish invaders led by ferdinand magellan . led by the chief lapu-lapu , they attacked the spanish at punta engano on the northeast corner of mactan island . lapu-lapu 's war chief was bugto pasa . magellan was killed in the battle .
