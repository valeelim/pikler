London Transport Board     the london transport board was the organisation responsible for public transport in london , england , united kingdom , and its environs from 1963-1969. in common with all london transport authorities from 1933 to 2000 , the public name and operational brand of the organisation was london transport . other pages . - sir alexander valentine - chairman , 1962 to 1965 - sir maurice holmes - chairman , 1965 to 1969
