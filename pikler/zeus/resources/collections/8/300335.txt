Donnie Wahlberg     donald edmond `` donnie '' wahlberg , jr. ( born august 17 , 1969 , boston , massachusetts ) is a singer , movie actor , director and producer . in the 1980s and 1990s he was a part of the group new kids on the block ; he has rejoined the group from 2008 and after . he also had featured roles in the saw franchise . his father is of swedish and irish descent and his mother is of english , french-canadian , irish and scottish descent
