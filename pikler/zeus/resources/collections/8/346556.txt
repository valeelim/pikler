Delta Force     1st special forces operational detachment-delta ( 1st sfod-d ) is a special forces unit of the united states army . they are a highly secretive , highly elite unit that take part in very dangerous missions including counter-terrorism , hostage rescue , direct action , and more . delta force is highly elite in that it is not a unit that just anyone can apply for . delta force recruits primarily from the army special forces ( aka the green berets ) and the 75th ranger regiment , themselves already elite special operations units .
