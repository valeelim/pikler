Stairlift     a stairlift or stair lift is a special type of chair . the user sits in the chair and it moves him or her up the stairs . stairlifts are often used by people who are handicapped or can not walk up the stairs . stairlifts travel on rails that are bolted to the staircase , not the wall . most stairlifts have a battery-powered motor . the battery allows the user to ride the lift even if the power goes out .
