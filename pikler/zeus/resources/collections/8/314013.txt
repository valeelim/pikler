Abercrombie River National Park     abercrombie river national park is a national park in new south wales , australia . it is west of sydney . the abercrombie and retreat rivers flow through the park . the park protects a large area of open forest , the largest area in this part of new south wales . there are large trees , casuarina cunninghamia , growing along the river . the river is normally a series of deep waterholes . in the park there are platypuses , rakalis , wallaroos , red-necked wallabies , swamp wallabies and eastern grey kangaroos . other websites . - unep-wcmc wdpa site sheet - list of all animals found in the park
