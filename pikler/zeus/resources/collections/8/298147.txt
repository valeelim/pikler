Stepped on My J'z     `` stepped on my j'z ' '' is a 2008 hip hop/crunk & b song by american rapper nelly . the song features singer ciara and rapper jermaine dupri . it was the third single from his fifth studio album brass knuckles . the song was released on june 11 , 2008 as a digital download and on july 1 , 2008 on the u.s. radio . the song reached # 69 on the u.s. billboard bubbling under r & b/hip-hop singles and # 90 on the u.s. billboard hot 100 .
