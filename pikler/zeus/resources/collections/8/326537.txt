Comiskey Park     comiskey park was a baseball park in chicago , illinois . for more than 80 years , it was the home field of the major league baseball team called the chicago white sox . it opened in the middle of the 1910 baseball season , and closed after the end of the 1990 season . it was replaced by the park now known as guaranteed rate field , which was built across the street to the south of comiskey park . the old ballpark was made of steel , concrete and bricks . tearing it down was a slow process . it took all summer of 1991 to finish the job . sources . - `` green cathedrals '' , by philip j. lowry
