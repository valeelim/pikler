Brussels Airport     brussels airport ( iata `` bru '' ) is the national airport of belgium . it is the biggest airport in belgium . it has more than 17 billion travelers each year . it is 18 km from brussels , the capital of belgium . the airport is used by a lot of airlines such as brussels airlines , thomas cook airlines and jetairfly . on 22 march 2016 , two bombs exploded at the airport . the airport has two terminals . ( a terminal is a building where passengers wait to get on planes . ) one terminal is used for flights to the schengen area . the second terminal is used for flights to countries out of the schengen area .
