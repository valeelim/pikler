Saw (movie)    saw ( movie ) saw is a 2004 independent american horror movie . it stars cary elwes , danny glover and monica potter . it was released on october 1 , 2004 in the united kingdom and on october 29 , 2004 in the united states . `` saw '' took in almost $ 104 million worldwide and received high marks , though also some negative review from some critics . it was originally rated nc-17 by the mpaa as a result of strong graphic violence , although it was edited downwards and released with an r rating . the theme of `` saw '' centers on kidnapping , imprisonment , torture and serial killing . it was followed by six sequels .
