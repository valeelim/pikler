Wilson's bird-of-paradise    wilson 's bird-of-paradise the wilson 's bird-of-paradise is a small bird of the paradisaeidae family . the male is a red and black bird-of-paradise with a yellow mantle on its neck , a light-green mouth , blue feet and two curved violet tail feathers . the head is naked blue with a black double cross pattern on it . as so often with birds in general , and paradise birds in particular , the female is not showy . it is a brownish bird , though it does have a bare blue crown . wilson 's bird-of-paradise eat usually fruit or small insects .
