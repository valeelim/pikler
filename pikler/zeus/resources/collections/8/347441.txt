Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic     the ukrainian soviet socialist republic or in short , the ukrainian ssr or soviet ukraine was in the southwestern part of the soviet union . it had the second largest population of the fifteen republics of the soviet union . this lasted from 1922 to 1991. the ukrainian ssr was a founding member of the united nations . it did not really have much power because it was tightly controlled by moscow authorities . when the soviet union broke apart , the ukrainian ssr became ukraine . during its 72-year history , the republic 's borders changed many times . the eastern city of kharkiv was the republic 's first capital . however , in 1934 , it was moved to the city of kiev . kiev is still the capital of ukraine .
