Peter Andre     peter andre ( born peter james andrea on 27 february 1973 ) is a british-australian singer and television personality . andre was born in brent , north-west london to greek cypriot parents . he grew up in london until moving to the gold coast , queensland , australia when he was six . his first single was released in 1992 and his first album in 1993. he and katie price were contestants in series 3 of the british version of `` i 'm a celebrity ... get me out of here ! '' from january-february 2004. he and price began a relationship during that time . andre and price had a son in 2005. the couple married later that year and their daughter was born in 2007. the couple divorced in 2009. andre is in a relationship with emily macdonagh . the couple 's daughter was born in january 2014. other websites . - official website
