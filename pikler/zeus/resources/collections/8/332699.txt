Pachuca     pachuca ( officially `` pachuca de soto '' ) is the capital city of the state of hidalgo , in mexico . it is also the capital of the municipality named pachuca . pachuca 's nickname is `` the windy beauty '' or `` bride of the wind '' , because in the city there are seasons where the winds are very strong . even local legend is depicted on a monument ( the monument of the victory of the wind ) . the city has a soccer club `` pachuca '' . it has a traditional dish called `` paste '' which consists of a small pie filled with a mixture of chopped potatoes , ground beef and spicy . it has a clock tower dedicated to the mexican war of independence .
