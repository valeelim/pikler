A Summer Place (movie)    a summer place ( movie ) a summer place is a 1959 romantic drama movie . it is based on the novel of the same name by sloan wilson . it was directed by delmer daves . it stars richard egan , dorothy mcguire , troy donahue , sandra dee , arthur kennedy , and constance ford . the movie 's instrumental theme became a hit . two one-time teen lovers , ken ( egan ) and sylvia ( mcguire ) meet by accident at a resort in maine . ken 's wife ( ford ) is frigid , and sylvia 's husband ( kennedy ) is an alcoholic . ken and sylvia consummate their youthful passion . sylvia 's son johnny ( donahue ) and ken 's daughter molly ( dee ) are at the resort with their parents . they fall in love . ken and sylvia leave their spouses and marry . johnny and molly request their parents ' permission when molly gets pregnant .
