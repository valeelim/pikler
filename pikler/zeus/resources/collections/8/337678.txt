Leopold Kronecker     leopold kronecker ( 7 december 1823 liegnitz , prussia - 29 december 1891 ) was a german mathematician of jewish descent . at first he was a business man and earned a lot of money . then he devoted himself to mathematics . he is famous for his work on number theory and algebra . he criticized cantor 's work on set theory . he was a pupil and lifelong friend of ernst kummer . references . - eric temple bell , 1986 . `` men of mathematics '' , penguin books 1953 .
