2022 FIFA World Cup     the 2022 fifa world cup is the 22nd fifa world cup that will be hosted in qatar . in may 2011 , fifa officials were accused of being corrupt during the bidding process , and the event raised questions . some believed that qatar got the world cup through bribes from mohammed bin hammam who was the asian federation confederation president . qatar denied all of the rumors . in may 2014 , sepp blatter admitted that giving the fifa world cup to qatar was a mistake . qatar automatically qualifies , but the other qualification process has not been announced yet . all teams are allowed to join when ever they want . currently , only three stadiums are close to being done ( but they are going to be expanded ) : - khalifa international stadium - al-gharafa stadium - ahmed bin ali stadium
