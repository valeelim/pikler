Whose Line Is It Anyway?    whose line is it anyway ? whose line is it anyway ? was a united states comedy television show that ran from 1998 to 2007. a revival premiered in 2013. it was presented by drew carey on the abc network , with the revival hosted by aisha tyler and airing on the cw network . the design of the show came from a british television of the same name . the show consisted of a group of four people acting out comedy sketches on-the-spot , with drew carey choosing what they would do with help of the live audience .
