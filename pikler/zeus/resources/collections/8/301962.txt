Valeria Kudryavtseva     valeria lvovna kudryavtseva ( born on 19 may 1971 ) is a russian television presenter , actress , singer and dancer . valeria was married to musician sergei linyuk since 1990 until 1992. they has one child , son zhan linyuk ( b.1990 ) . she was married to matvei morozov since 2004 until 2007. she in relationship with musician sergei lazarev since 2008. she was pregnant from sergei , but she had a miscarriage in july 2011 .
