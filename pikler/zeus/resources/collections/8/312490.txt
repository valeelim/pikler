Imperial     imperial may mean : 1. the things associated with a country led by a powerful leader ( an empire ) or with that leader ( emperor ) as a person . 2. a system of weights and measures ( imperial units ) formerly used by the old british empire , and in places it controlled or affected , including inches , feet , ounces , pounds , and other measurements which are frequently used by english-speaking people , as opposed the international system based on meters and liters .
