Slovenia at the Olympics     slovenia was first an independent nation at the olympic games in 1992. they have been in every games since then . prior to the breakup of yugoslavia , slovenian athletes were part of yugoslavia at the olympics . the national olympic committee for slovenia was formed in 1991 and recognized in 1993. slovenian athletes have won a total of fifteen medals at the summer olympic games and another seven at the winter olympic games . related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - slovenia profile at london2012.com
