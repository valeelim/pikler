Yemen at the Olympics     yemen has been in every summer olympic games since 1984. they have never been in the winter olympic games . before 1990 , north yemen and south yemen competed separately . north yemen was in the 1984 and 1988 summer games . south yemen was at the 1988 summer games only . yemen has never won an olympic medal . the yemen olympic committee was formed in 1971 and recognized by the ioc in 1981. related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - yemen profile at london2012.com
