Move That Body     `` move that body ' '' is a 2010 hip hop/electro hop song by american rapper nelly . the song features singers t-pain and akon . it was the second single from his sixth studio album 5.0. the song was released on october 12 , 2010. the music video for move that body first appeared on nelly 's official vevo on october 19 2010. the song reached # 24 on the uk r & b chart , # 29 on the aria charts , # 54 on the u.s. billboard hot 100 and # 71 on the uk singles chart .
