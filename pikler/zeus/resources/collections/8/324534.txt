Victoria Azarenka     victoria azarenka ( , ; born 31 july 1989 ) is a belarusian tennis player . she won the mixed doubles at the 2007 us open and at the 2008 french open . she won the singles at the 2012 and 2013 australian open . at the 2012 summer olympics , she won a bronze medal in the singles and a gold medal in the mixed doubles . she is a former world no . 1. as of 8 july 2013 , she was the world no . 3. she was born in minsk and lives in monte carlo , monaco . other websites . - official website
