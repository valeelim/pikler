Dalmatian pelican     the dalmatian pelican ( `` pelecanus crispus '' ) is a large bird . it lives in wet places such as in swamps and shallow lakes . it can be found from southeastern europe to pakistan and china . it is the largest member of the pelican family . the average length is 170–190 cm ( 67-75 inches ) the average weight is 11–15 kg ( 24-33 lbs ) . the wings are around 3 m ( 10 ft ) across . it is the world 's heaviest flying bird on average . however , sometimes a few birds from another species may also be as large or larger .
