London Heliport     london heliport at battersea , london is the capital 's main and busiest heliport . it is on the south bank of the river thames , 3 nautical miles ( 3.5 miles or 5.6 km ) southwest of westminster bridge and the palace of westminster . it is on a jetty between wandsworth bridge and battersea railway bridge . the nearest railway station is clapham junction , and the nearest tube station is fulham broadway . the heliport , once owned by westland and then harrods , is a very small site . it uses a concrete surface on a jetty as a helipad for take-off and landing . there is onshore parking for between three and four aircraft , usually just the smaller helicopters . the heliport has landing , parking and refuelling services between 08:00 and 21:00. flights are permitted between 07:00 and 23:00 .
