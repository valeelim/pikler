Because of You     `` because of you '' is a pop rock single from singer kelly clarkson off her second studio album `` breakaway '' . it was first released in 2005. the single made clarkson the only winner in `` american idol '' history to have a fourth song from the same album in top 10 on the `` billboard '' hot 100. in 2007 `` because of you '' was reprised in a duet alongside the country music artist reba mcentire ; the reprised version produced the first appearance of clarkson in the billboard hot country charts , where the single hit number 2 .
