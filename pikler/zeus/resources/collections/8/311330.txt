3LW     3lw ( three little women ) were an american girl group . the group consist of naturi cora maria naughton , born may 20 , 1984 , adrienne eliza bailon , born october 24 , 1983 , kiely alexis williams , born july 9 , 1986. the band formed in 1999 in newark , new jersey . in august 2002 , naturi naughton announced that she had been forced out of 3lw . before the year 's end , the ladies held various auditions in atlanta , los angeles and new york in search of a third member . jessica benson made the cut and joined 3lw in december . the group disbanded in 2007. members . - adrienne bailon - born adrienne eliza bailon on october 24 , 1983 , manhattan , new york , u.s. - kiely williams - born kiely alexis williams on july 9 , 1986 , alexandria , virginia , united states - naturi naughton - born naturi cora maria naughton on may 20 , 1984 , east orange , new jersey - jessica benson - born in 1988 , severn , maryland discography . - studio albums - 2000 : `` 3lw '' - 2002 : `` a girl can mack '' - 2002 : `` naughty or nice '' - unreleased albums
