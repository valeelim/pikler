Peter Carey (novelist)    peter carey ( novelist ) peter carey ( born 1943 ) is an australian novelist who has won many awards including two booker prizes . he first won the booker prize in 1988 for his book , `` oscar and lucinda '' . he won again in 2001 for his book `` the true history of the kelly gang '' . only one other author has won this award twice . `` oscar and lucinda '' was made into a movie starring ralph fiennes . `` the true history of the kelly gang '' is the story of australian bushranger , ned kelly , written in the style of ned 's famous jerilderie letter . carey his written nine novels : - `` bliss '' ( 1981 ) - `` illywhacker '' ( 1985 ) - `` oscar and lucinda '' ( 1988 ) - `` the tax inspector '' ( 1991 ) - `` the unusual life of tristan smith '' ( 1994 ) - `` jack maggs '' ( 1997 ) - `` the true history of the kelly gang '' ( 2000 ) - `` my life as a fake '' ( 2003 ) - `` theft : a love story '' ( 2006 )
