Wendy Richard     wendy richard born wendy emerton , ( 20 july 1943 - 26 february 2009 ) was a british actress . she is best known for her roles as miss brahms in `` are you being served ? '' and as pauline fowler in `` eastenders '' . she was born in middlesbrough , north riding of yorkshire . she lived in london for most of her life . she died of metastatic breast cancer there .
