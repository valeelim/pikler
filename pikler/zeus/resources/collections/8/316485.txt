Lucky Star (song)    lucky star ( song ) `` lucky star '' is the fourth single from madonna 's hit record `` madonna '' . the song was first released in united kingdom in 1983. it was originally produced by reggie lucas , although during recording madonna was not happy with the final results of the song . according to her , lucas used too many instruments and did not consider her ideas for the song . she called her boyfriend ( at the time ) , john benitez , to remix her songs . when the `` lucky star '' video was released in 1984 , her style and mannerisms became a fashion trend among younger people .
