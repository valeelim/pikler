Fast chess     fast chess is similar to a normal game of chess , but played at a faster than usual rate . the player who runs out of time first ( or is checkmated , whichever happens first ) loses the match . fast chess versions , always played with a chess clock : - rapid : 15 to 60 minutes per player . - blitz : less than 15 minutes per side . - bullet : ( unofficial category ) 2 to 3 minutes per player .
