Argentan     argentan is a french city in the orne department , in lower normandy . 17 327 people live there . the size is . the river orne flows through argentan . the forests of gouffern and ecouves are nearby . argentan was built at the beginning of the 12th century by henry ii . henry was a normandy duke and also the king of england . there are three churches : saint martin , saint germain and saint michel .
