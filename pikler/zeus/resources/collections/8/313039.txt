Miloš Krasić     miloš krasić ( born 1 november 1984 ) is a serbian professional football winger . he plays for fenerbahçe sk and the serbian national team . fenerbahçe completed the transfer of krasić for €7 million on 3 august , 2012. krasić signed a four-year contract worth €2.3 million per-season . krasić has been an important part of serbia and montenegro under-21 squad on the last two european u-21 championships , as well as at the 2004 summer olympics .
