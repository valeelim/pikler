Institut supérieur européen de gestion group     institut supérieur européen de gestion group ( iseg group ) is a group of three french higher education schools ( iseg business school , iseg finance school , iseg marketing and communication school ) created in 1980.. the group is in seven places , in paris , bordeaux , nantes , lille , lyon , strasbourg and toulouse ( france ) . iseg group delivers bachelor 's degree and master 's degree . iseg group 's main area is entrepreneurship . notable teachers and former teachers . - fabrice bardeche , vice-president of ionis education group
