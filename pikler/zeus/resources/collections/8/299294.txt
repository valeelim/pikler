Tanzania at the Olympics     tanzania was first at the olympic games in 1964. they have been in every summer olympic games since then , except for the boycotted 1976 games . the nation has been in the winter olympic games . the national olympic committee for tanzania was formed in 1968 and recognized by the international olympic committee that same year . related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - united republic of tanzania profile at london2012.com
