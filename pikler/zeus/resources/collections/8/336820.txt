Carol Greider     carolyn widney `` carol '' greider ( born april 15 , 1961 ) is an american molecular biologist . she is professor and director of molecular biology and genetics at johns hopkins university . greider discovered the enzyme telomerase in 1984 , when she was a graduate student of elizabeth blackburn at the university of california , berkeley . greider pioneered research on the structure of telomeres , the ends of the chromosomes . she was awarded the 2009 nobel prize for physiology or medicine , with blackburn and jack szostak , for their discovery that telomeres are protected from progressive shortening by the enzyme telomerase .
