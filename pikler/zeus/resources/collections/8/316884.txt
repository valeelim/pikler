Tanuma Okitsugu     tanuma was also a daimyo of the sagara han . he used the title tonomo-no-kami . the administration of tanuma was marked by corruption and by inflation of currency . in 1784 ( `` tenmei '' 4 ) , okitsugu 's son was assassinated in front of his father inside edo castle . the involvement of senior figures in the `` bakufu '' was suspected , but only the assassin was punished . the result was that liberal reforms and the relaxation of the national isolation policy `` sakoku '' were stopped . related pages . - matsudaira sadanobu more reading . - hall , john whitney .
