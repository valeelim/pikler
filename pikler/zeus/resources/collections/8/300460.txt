Kiki Cutter     christina `` kiki '' cutter ( b. july 24 , 1949 ) is an alpine skier from the united states . she was the first american to win a world cup skiing event . she came in first in the world cup slalom race in 1968 in oslo , norway . she only competed in world cup skiing for a just over three years . but , she had five world cup wins . that was the u.s. record for most world cup victories by a u.s. skier for eleven years from 1968 to 1979. phil mahre went past her total in 1979 .
