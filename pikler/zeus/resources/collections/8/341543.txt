Omen     an omen is a sign that is thought to foretell the future , often signifying the advent of change . the word can mean something either `` good '' or `` bad '' is about to happen . the origin of the word is unknown , although it may be connected with the latin word `` audire '' , meaning `` to hear '' . an example from the past , babies born with birth defects were believed to be a bad or evil omen . other pages . - fortune - luck - superstition
