Alberto Granado     alberto granado jiménez ( 8 august 1922 — 5 march 2011 ) was an argentine biochemist . he traveled through south america in 1951 with his friend che guevara on a motorcycle . they kept a journal about the trip and what they saw . these writings were the basis for walter salle 's movie , `` the motorcycle diaries '' ( 2004 ) . granado was born in hernando , argentina , and later studied chemistry and pharmacology at the university of córdoba . active in politics , he spent time in prison after taking part in student protests against the government . he met che at university , and in 1951 they traveled thousands of miles and finally ended up in caracas , venezuela . che went back home to finish his medical degree , but granado stayed and worked in a leprosy clinic . granada moved to cuba in 1961 and set up the school of medicine at the university of santiago . he later moved to havana and was able to work on scientific research . he died in havana in 2011. he asked for his ashes to be scattered in cuba , argentina and venezuela .
