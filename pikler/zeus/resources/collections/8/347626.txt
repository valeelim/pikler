Further-eastern European Time     further-eastern european time ( fet ) is since september 2011 three hours ahead of utc ( ) . it was started as the official time for the russian kaliningrad oblast and then followed by belarus , northern cyprus , and turkey . until 2011 , further-eastern european time was the same as eastern european time ( utc+2 . on 27 march 2011 , russia moved to the so called `` year-round daylight saving time '' . belarus followed russia on september 15 , 2011. both turkey and northern cyprus also cancelled dst on september 7 , 2016 , and has switched to fet permanently .
