Wing-banded Antbird     the wing-banded antbird ( myrmornis torquata ) is a species of bird in the thamnophilidae family . it is found in brazil , colombia , ecuador , french guiana , guyana , nicaragua , panama , peru , suriname , and venezuela . it feeds by hopping on the ground , turning dead leaves over in search of insects . it uses its long bill to do this , and never picks the leaves up . references . - birdlife international 2004 . `` myrmornis torquata '' . 2006 iucn red list of threatened species . downloaded on 26 july 2007 .
