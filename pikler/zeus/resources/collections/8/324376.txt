Walt Dohrn     walter jonathan `` walt '' dohrn ( born 1970 ) is an american writer , director , artist , actor , animator , musician , screenwriter , and voice artist . he is best known as the voice of rumpelstiltskin in `` shrek forever after '' . he also did additional voices in `` shrek the third '' and was also the voice of doodlebob in the `` spongebob squarepants '' episode `` frankendoodle . '' dohrn has also worked as writer and director of season 2 of `` spongebob squarepants '' .
