Hungary at the Olympics     hungary at the olympics is ahistory which began at the first olympic games in 1896. the international olympic committee 's official abbreviation for hungary is hun . teams from hungary have been in most summer olympic games and every winter olympic games since then . the nation was not invited to the 1920 games after world war i. hungarian athletes have won a total of 475 medals , with fencing as the top medal-producing sport . hungary has won more olympic medals than any other nation that has never hosted games . the national olympic committee for hungary is the `` hungarian olympic committee '' . it was formed and recognized in 1895. leading in that sport related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - hungary profile at london2012.com - hungary at olympic.org
