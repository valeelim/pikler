Would I Lie to You? (TV series)    would i lie to you ? ( tv series ) would i lie to you ? is a british comedy panel game shown on bbc one . it began on 16 june 2007. the first two series were presented by angus deayton . since then it has been presented by rob brydon . there are two teams of three people , a team captain and two celebrity guests . the captains are david mitchell and lee mack . during each episode , the contestants take turns to make statements about themselves . the other team try to work out whether the statement is true or false , by questioning the person who has made the statement .
