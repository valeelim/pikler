Lee Remick     lee ann remick ( december 14 , 1935 - july 2 , 1991 ) was an american actress . she was born in quincy , massachusetts . she played the female lead in `` anatomy of a murder '' , `` days of wine and roses '' and `` the omen '' . remick won a golden globe award in 1973. in 1975 she won a golden globe award and a bafta award . remick died of kidney cancer in los angeles , california .
