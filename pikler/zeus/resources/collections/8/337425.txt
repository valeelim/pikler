Alexander Gorsky     alexander gorsky ( 6 august 1871-1924 ) was a russian ballet choreographer and a contemporary of marius petipa . he is known for restaging petipa ’ s classical ballets such as `` swan lake '' , `` don quixote '' , and `` the nutcracker '' . gorsky `` sought greater naturalism , realism , and characterization '' in ballet . he valued acting skills over `` bravura '' technique ( a showy display of skills such as many turns or high jumps . ) his interpretations of ballets were often controversial and he often used artists outside the dance world to create sets and costumes . gorsky gave continuity to the plots of petipa ’ s ballets and removed excessive choreography that did not relate to the story .
