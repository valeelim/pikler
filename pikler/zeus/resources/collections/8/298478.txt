Auguste Chabaud     auguste chabaud ( born 3 october 1882 in nîmes , gard , died 23 may 1955 in graveson , bouches-du-rhône ) was a french artist and sculptor . chabaud was born in nîmes . he began studying arts in avignon , aged 14. in 1899 , he went to paris and continued studying arts . in his early phase , his paintings were fauvistic . in 1919 , he moved to the village of graveson and stayed there for the rest of his life . since 1920 , he only used prussian blue for his paintings . in 1955 , he died in graveson . the `` musée auguste chabaud '' was opened in this village in 1992. other websites . - the musée chabaud website
