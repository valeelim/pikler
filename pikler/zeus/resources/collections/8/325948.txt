Fontenai-sur-Orne     fontenai-sur-orne is a commune in the orne department of lower normandy , france . there were 254 people in 2006. the size of the village is 651 hectare ( ha ) . its saint-martin church was built in classical times . the west gate was built in 16th century , but the virgin statues are from the 18th century . an underground vaulted cave made in stone is in the presbytery . there is a local party organized on the 3rd sunday of july . the city 's main activities are mixed farming and bird-raising .
