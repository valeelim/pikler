Take a Shine To...    take a shine to ... take a shine to ... is an album by ella koon , released in july 2011. it was released a year after her well-received 2010 mini-album `` being '' . hit songs include ella ’ s first self-composed song “ reluctant to part ( 依依 ) ” , which is produced by hitmaker chiu tsang hei with lyrics penned by siu hak . the album also contains ricoh camera cm song “ a moment of interaction ( 一刻感應 ) ” and the latest plug “ let him go ( 由他去 ) ” . track listing . - 1 . 由他去 ( let him go ) - 2 . 依依 ( reluctant to part ) - 3 . 夜潛者 ( night invader ) - 4 . 一刻感應 ( a moment of interaction - ricoh camera 2011 cm song ) - 5 . 母忘你 ( unable to forget )
