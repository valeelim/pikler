Slade in Flame (album)    slade in flame ( album ) slade in flame is a 1974 rock album by english band slade . it was the band 's fifth studio album . the album was a soundtrack to the band 's own 1975 film `` slade in flame '' . a successful album , it went to number six in the uk . the album featured the singles `` far far away '' and `` how does it feel '' . track listing . 1 . `` how does it feel '' ( holder/lea ) uk # 15 1 . `` them kinda monkeys ca n't swing '' ( holder/lea ) 1 . `` so far so good '' ( holder/lea ) 1 . `` summer song ( wishing you were here ) '' ( holder/lea ) 1 . `` o.k . yesterday was yesterday '' ( holder/lea ) 1 . `` far far away '' ( holder/lea ) uk # 2 1 . `` this girl '' ( holder/lea ) 1 . `` lay it down '' ( holder/lea ) 1 . `` heaven knows '' ( holder/lea ) 1 . `` standin ' on the corner '' ( holder/lea )
