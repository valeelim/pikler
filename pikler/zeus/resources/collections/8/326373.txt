Priest Holmes     priest holmes ( born october 7 , 1973 ) is a former american football runningback . he played college football for the texas longhorns . holmes went undrafted into the nfl in 1997. he was signed by the baltimore ravens . in 1998 , he rushed for 1,008 yards and seven touchdowns after not playing the previous year . in 2000 , baltimore picked jamal lewis , and holmes became a backup . the ravens won super bowl xxxv that year . holmes , wanting more playing time , signed with the kansas city chiefs . he rushed for well over 1,000 yards in his first three seasons with the team . injuries bothered holmes during his later time in kansas city . he was again replaced by a younger runningback , larry johnson . he retired on november 21 , 2007. on november 2 , 2014 , he was inducted into the chiefs hall of fame . other websites . - official website - priest holmes foundation
