Paul Goldschmidt     paul edward goldschmidt ( born september 10 , 1987 in wilmington , delaware ) is an american baseball player . he currently plays as a first baseman for the arizona diamondbacks of major league baseball ( mlb ) . he debuted for the diamondbacks on august 1 , 2011 in a game against the san francisco giants . other websites . - paul goldschmidt at mlb.com
