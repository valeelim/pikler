Cabinda Province     cabinda ( also spelled kabinda , formerly portuguese congo ) is a province of angola . the capital city is also called cabinda . the province is divided into four municipalities - belize , buco zau , cabinda and landana . there are about 357,576 people . cabinda is separated from the rest of angola by a narrow strip of land of the democratic republic of the congo . cabinda is bordered on the north by the republic of the congo , and on the west by the atlantic ocean . while the civil war in angola ended in 2002 , there is still fighting in cabinda .
