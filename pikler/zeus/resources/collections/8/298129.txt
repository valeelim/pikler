John Ruganda     john ruganda ( 30 may 1941 to 8 december 2007 ) was uganda 's best known playwright . ruganda was also a professor at university of north , south africa , university of nairobi , and makerere university . ruganda 's plays `` reflect the reality of the east african sociopolitical situation after independence . '' he helped outline east african theater . `` the burdens '' ( 1972 ) and `` the floods '' ( 1980 ) are regularly studied in literature classes . writing . plays . - `` the burdens '' , kampala , uganda , national theatre , january 1970 - `` black mamba '' , kampala , 1972 - `` the good woman of setzuan '' , by bertolt brecht , translated into swahili by ruganda , nairobi , nairobi university players , november 1978 - `` the floods '' , nairobi , french cultural centre , 1 march 1979 - `` music without tears '' , nairobi , nairobi university players , february 1982 - `` echoes of silence '' , nairobi , 1985 television . - `` the secret of the season '' , screenplay by ruganda , voice of kenya , march 1973 - `` the floods '' , screenplay by ruganda , voice of kenya , april 1973
