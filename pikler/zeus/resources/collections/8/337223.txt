Bride of Chucky     bride of chucky is a 1998 american fantasy horror comedy movie . it is set in new jersey . it is the fourth movie in the `` child 's play '' series . it stars jennifer tilly as tiffany and brad dourif as chucky . costars include katherine heigl and john ritter . `` bride of chucky '' made almost $ 50 million worldwide , thereby becoming the most successful part of the series , although reviews were mixed .
