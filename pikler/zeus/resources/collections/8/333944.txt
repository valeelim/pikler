Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Concepción     the roman catholic archdiocese of concepción ( ) is an archdiocese in concepción , chile . it was established in 1563 as `` diocese of la imperial '' . on 1603 , it was renamed `` diocese of concepción '' . on 1939 it was promoted to `` archdiocese of concepción '' . suffragan dioceses . - chillán - los ángeles - temuco - valdivia - villarrica other websites . - giga-catholic information - catholic hierarchy - diocese website
