Malva     malva is a genus of about 25-30 species of plants commonly called mallows . it is the type genus in the family malvaceae . several species are widely grown as garden flowers , while some are invasive weeds , such as m. neglecta . the color mauve is named after the french name of the plant . the word mallow comes from the old english `` malwe '' which was imported from the word `` malva '' .
