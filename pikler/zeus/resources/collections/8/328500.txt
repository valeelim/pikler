Philosophy of the World     philosophy of the world is the first album by the shaggs . it was released in 1969 by third word records . it was rereleased in 1980 by red rooster records/rounder records after the band nrbq found it at a massachusetts radio station in 1978. recording of the album was financed by austin wiggin , father of the wiggin sisters . all songs written & composed by dorothy wiggin . 1 . `` philosophy of the world '' – 2:56 1 . `` that little sports car '' – 2:06 1 . `` who are parents ?
