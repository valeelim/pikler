UGK Records     ugk records is an american record label . it was created by rapper pimp c just after he got out of prison . the company is named after the rap group ugk ( underground kingz ) . the label was in the process of reaching a distribution deal when pimp c died . artists currently signed to the label include : - hezeleo - bankroll jonez - ivory p - the set boys - big bubb - xvii - dub-g - lil corey - bryn king - codaq - az hustlers - izzy - deezy references . - ugk videos
