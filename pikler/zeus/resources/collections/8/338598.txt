Goose step     the goose step is a kind of marching done by the military . it is usually done in formal parades and other ceremonies . while marching together , the troops swing their legs high off the ground , while keeping their knees unbended ( so that their legs are straight ) . it resembles the walking style of a goose . the march comes from the prussian army in the mid-18th century . it was called the stechschritt ( meaning , `` piercing step '' ) or stechmarsch . it is still used today by the armed forces of the nearly 30 countries . other websites . - black-and-white video of the march
