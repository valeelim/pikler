Hitachi Province     the province had borders with iwashiro , iwaki , shimōsa , and shimotsuke provinces . the ancient capital city of the province was near ishioka . in the meiji period , the provinces of japan were converted into prefectures . the maps of japan and hitachi province were reformed in the 1870s . `` kashima jinjū '' was the chief shinto shrine ( `` ichinomiya '' ) of hitachi . related pages . - provinces of japan - prefectures of japan - list of regions of japan - list of islands of japan other websites . - murdoch 's map of provinces , 1903
