Detention (imprisonment)    detention ( imprisonment ) detention is a depriving of liberty of a person by the authority of a government or a country , usually the police . such depriving liberty is allowed only in the case that the person can considered to have committed a crime . other depriving liberty is called arbitrary detention and prohibited by article 9 of the universal declaration of human rights and international covenant on civil and political rights . and even detention is done legally , the person must be treated with humanity and respected the person 's dignity according to the convention against torture . related pages . - arrest - prison - international human rights law
