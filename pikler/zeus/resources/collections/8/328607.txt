Snuff movie     a snuff movie is a movie that shows the murder of a person , or a group of people , for the entertainment of the audience . snuff movies are made to gain money . they do not use special effects . the first such movie was `` el angel de la muerte '' . it is an argentine horror movie made in 1976. one of the scenes shows how the makers of the movie kill one of the actresses . the english title of the movie was `` snuff '' . this turned out to be a marketingploy . the actress was not killed . today , no real snuff movie is known . since the 1970s , the theme has been used by different movements in the united states . these groups usually argue that the government needs to control the media more than it currently does . the last such debate was about happy slapping . the debate is also fueled by violent pornography or videos of executions and murders .
