Jennifer     the name jennifer is a female name which was first put into use in the 18th century . the name was quite uncommon prior to 1906. around 1969 , however , the name hit number 3 on the girl name charts , then between 1970 and 1984 jennifer became the single most popular name . since 1990 the name is still common , although considerably less so . numerous celebrities carry the name jennifer , for example jennifer aniston and jennifer jason leigh .
