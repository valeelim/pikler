Aragatsotn Province     aragatsotn is a province ( `` marz '' ) of armenia . it is in the west of the country . the capital is ashtarak . the name means `` a foot of aragats '' . during the arsacid dynasty of the kingdom of armenia , the region was part of the ayrarat province . it was ruled by the amatuni noble family with its capital at oshakan . aragatsotn 's western edge forms part of armenia 's border with turkey . there are 3 urban communities and 111 rural communities .
