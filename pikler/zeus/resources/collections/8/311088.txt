Amit     amit is a common male given name . it is common in india , nepal , israel , pakistan , iran , middle east , and bangladesh . in hindi and bengali , amit ( ) means infinite or immeasurable or boundless . it is part of amitabha buddha . it is one of the 108 names of the hindu god shri ganesha . it also means an eternal friend of everyone in hindi , nepali and sanskrit . in hebrew , amit ( ) means colleague , friend . in arabic , amit ( ) means being highly praised , beloved and victorious . in islam amit refers to the traditional arabian gesture . during battles the muslim army yelled `` yā manṣūr amit ! '' and attacked .
