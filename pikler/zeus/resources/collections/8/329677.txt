Xerophile     xerophiles are extremophile organisms that can grow and reproduce in conditions with almost no water . many moulds and yeasts are xerophilic . mould growth on bread is an example of food spoilt by a xerophilic fungus . examples : - `` trichosporonoides nigrescens '' . - cacti are also a type of xerophile . - tardigrades can survive for almost a decade without exposure to water . - soil microbes of the atacama desert related pages . - xerophyte
