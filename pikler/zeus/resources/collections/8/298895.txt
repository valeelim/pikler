Pont du Gard     the pont du gard is a roman aqueduct in southern france . it is in the gard département , in the area of the commune of vers-pont-du-gard . the name should mean `` bridge over the gard '' ( the river gardon ) . the aquaeduct supplied the city of nîmes with water . it has a length of nearly 50 kilometres . the bridge is 49 metres high and has bows on three levels . it was probably built in the 1st century . in 1985 , the pont du gard was classified a world heritage site .
