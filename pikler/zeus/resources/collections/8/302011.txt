Indonesia at the Olympics     indonesia first competed in the olympic games in 1952. the international olympic committee 's official abbreviation for indonesia is ina . indonesia was first at the olympic games in 1952. they have been in every summer olympic games since then , except for 1964 and 1980. indonesia has never been in the winter olympic games . the national olympic committee for indonesia was formed in 1946 and recognized in 1952. related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - indonesia profile at london2012.com
