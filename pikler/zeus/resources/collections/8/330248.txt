Donna Reed     donna reed ( born donna belle mullenger , january 27 , 1921 - january 14 , 1986 ) was an american actress . she was born in denison , iowa . reed had a major role in `` it 's a wonderful life '' . she won an academy award for best supporting actress for her role in `` from here to eternity '' . she won a golden globe award for best tv star - female for her central role in `` the donna reed show '' . she died of pancreatic cancer in beverly hills , california .
