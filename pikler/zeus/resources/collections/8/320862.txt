Echizen Province     the ancient capital city of the province was at echizen . the boundaries of echizen were formally established during the reigns of empress genshō and empress kōken . in the edo period , the daimyo of fukui domain lived at fukui . in the meiji period , the provinces of japan were converted into prefectures . the maps of japan and echizen province were reformed in the 1870s . `` kehi jinja '' was the chief shinto shrine ( `` ichinomiya '' ) of echizen . related pages . - provinces of japan - prefectures of japan - list of regions of japan - list of islands of japan other websites . - murdoch 's map of provinces , 1903
