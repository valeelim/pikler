Marie Bashir     marie bashir is the 37th governor of new south wales , and the first woman to hold the position . she was appointed on 1 march 2001. she was born in narrandera , new south wales , and later went to the sydney girls high school . she studied medicine and surgery at the university of sydney , graduating in 1956. she worked in mental health , and became the clinical professor of psychiatry at the university of sydney . she was made an officer of the order of australia in 1988 for her services to child and adolescent health .
