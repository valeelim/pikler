Barq's    barq 's barq 's or ( barq 's famous olde tyme root beer ) is a soft drink ( soda ) which has a caramel color in it . barq 's is a brand of root beer and is notable for being the first major north american root beer which contained caffeine . barq 's is manufactured by the coca-cola company . barq 's is pronounced `` barks '' . barq 's was invented by edward charles edmond barq and his younger brother , gaston who worked for `` the barq 's brothers bottling company '' which was founded in 1890 in the french quarter of new orleans , louisiana . early on in their brand they created a popular creation called `` orangine '' , which was an orange-flavored soda . orangine won a gold medal at the 1893 world 's columbian exposition world 's fair . the company sells all sorts of flavors of barq 's such as : - barq 's famous olde tyme root beer - diet barq 's - barq 's red crème soda - barq 's french vanilla crème soda - barq 's floatz - barq 's floatz ice cream squeeze tubes . other websites . - barq 's official website
