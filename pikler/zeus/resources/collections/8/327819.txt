Common year starting on Friday     this is the calendar for any common year starting on friday , january 1 ( dominical letter c ) . the most recent year of this type was 2010 and the next one will be 2021 in the gregorian calendar , or 2011 and 2022 in the julian calendar . the exceptional common year , 2100 will also be a common year starting on friday in the gregorian calendar ( see bottom tables ) . a common year is a year with 365 days , i.e. , not a leap year . this is the only year type where the `` n '' th `` doomsday '' ( this year sunday ) is not in iso week `` n '' ; it is in iso week `` n '' -1 .
