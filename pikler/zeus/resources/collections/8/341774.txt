Río Negro Province     río negro is a province of argentina . it is at the northern edge of patagonia . to the east is the atlantic ocean . its capital is viedma . other important cities include the ski resort town of bariloche , cipolletti and general roca . ferdinand magellan was the first explorer to visit the coasts of the provinces in 1520. the province makes almost 70 % of the apples and pears of the country . industrial fishing takes place in the atlantic ocean waters of the san matías gulf . hake , squid , and shellfish are the most common capture .
