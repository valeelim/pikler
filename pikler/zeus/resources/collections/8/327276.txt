We Belong Together     `` we belong together '' is a 2005 hit song by mariah carey off of her album `` the emancipation of mimi '' . the song is available in several versions . it reached # 1 on the `` billboard '' hot 100 and stayed there for 14 weeks . it was also no.1 in australia and the netherlands . it was the best-selling song in the us of 2005. it won two grammy awards .
