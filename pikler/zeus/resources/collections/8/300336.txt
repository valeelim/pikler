New Kids on the Block     new kids on the block are an american pop group who were formed in boston , massachusetts in 1984. they had many hit songs in the 1980s and 1990s . they disbanded in 1994 and regrouped in 2008. discography . - studio albums - `` new kids on the block '' ( 1986 ) - `` hangin ' tough '' ( 1988 ) - `` step by step '' ( 1990 ) - `` face the music '' ( 1994 ) - `` the block '' ( 2008 ) - `` 10 '' ( 2013 ) members of the group . - donnie wahlberg - jonathan knight - danny wood - jordan knight - joey mcintyre other websites . - official website
