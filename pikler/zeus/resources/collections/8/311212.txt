American Samoa at the Olympics     american samoa at the olympics is a history which includes 8 games in 8 countries and 20+ athletes . since 1988 , american samoa has contributed to the growth of the `` olympic movement '' . the international olympic committee 's official abbreviation for american samoa is asa . the national olympic committee for american samoa was formed in 1987 ; and it was recognized by the international olympic committee the same year . teams from american samoa have take part in summer olympic games . samoan athletes went to only one of the winter olympic games . related pages . - list of ioc country codes - american samoa - summer olympic games - winter olympic games other websites . - american samoa profile at london2012.com
