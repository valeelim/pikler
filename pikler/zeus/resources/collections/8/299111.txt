Ghana at the Olympics     the first time that ghana was at the olympic games was in 1952. the international olympic committee 's official abbreviation for ghana is gha . ghana was known by the colonial name of gold coast . the nation has been in most summer olympic games since then , missing the 1956 summer olympics , boycotting the 1976 games and the 1980 summer olympics . ghana was at the 2010 winter olympics . the national olympic committee for ghana was formed in 1952 and recognized by the international olympic committee that same year . related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - ghana at olympic.org - ghana profile at london2012.com
