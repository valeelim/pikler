The Bad Girls Club (season 3)    the bad girls club ( season 3 ) the bad girls club ( season 3 ) is the third season of `` the bad girls club '' . it aired on oxygen on december 2 , 2008. season 3 was the first season to have one million views . it had beat the records from the second season and first season . perez hilton hosted the reunion special . after the show , the ambers and sarah competed on . they had done a pilot for their show called `` the amber show '' . oxygen did not pick it up . this was the first season to have the girls on vacation . this was also the first season to have 60 minute episodes .
