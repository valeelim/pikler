Spartak Moscow    f.c . spartak moscow fc spartak moscow ( russian : футбольный клуб спартак москва ) is a russian football club from moscow . the club is the most successful in russia , winning 9 or 19 of the russian premier league championships . the team used to be part of the spartak moscow sports society , but left and declared to be an independent organization . their biggest rival is pfc cska moscow .
