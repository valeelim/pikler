Teddy Stadium     teddy stadium ( , `` itztadion teddy '' ) is a football stadium in jerusalem , israel . it is the homeground of beitar jerusalem f.c . and hapoel jerusalem f.c . it is named after the long time jerusalem mayor teddy kollek . for beitar the stadium was important . they played at the ymca stadium . that stadium was nicknamed `` the sandbox '' because of its bad ground conditions . originally only the west and east sides of the stadium were built giving it a capacity of 13,000. in 1999 work was finished on a north side . now the capacity is 21,600. the city of jerusalem decided to increase the stadium 's capacity to 31,600 seats until 2013 for uefa european under-21 championship and the maccabiah olympic games . the stadium itself is one of the newest in israel and meets all european standards . the stadium has 5,000 parking spots and is connected to the malha mall and its parking facilities by a pedestrian bridge . the stadium is located at the begin expressway and the malha train station . so people can go to the stadium either by car or rail .
