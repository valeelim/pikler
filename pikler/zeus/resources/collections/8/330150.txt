The House of the Seven Gables     the house of the seven gables is a novel written by nathaniel hawthorne . it was published in 1851. the book is about an old new england family . themes include guilt , revenge , and atonement . the supernatural and witchcraft are a part of the story . an old house in salem , massachusetts was used as background for the story . the house belonged to hawthorne 's cousin susanna ingersoll . he visited the house often . he was also inspired by his ancestors . they played a part in the salem witch trials of 1692. the book was well received . `` the house of the seven gables '' has been adapted to movies and television shows . related pages . - house of the seven gables online editions - `` the house of the seven gables '' , available at internet archive ( scanned color illustrated books , multiple editions and formats ) - ( plain text ) - `` the house of the seven gables '' , available at ria press ( pdf optimized for printing ) study guides - monkeynotes study guide - sparknotes study guide - classicnote study guide
