Bazarak, Panjshir    bazarak , panjshir bazarak ( ) is a town in the panjshir valley , in northern afghanistan . it is the capital of panjshir province . it has six villages : khanez , jangalak , malaspa , parandeh , rahmankhil . the tomb of ahmad shah massoud , known as the `` lion of panjshir '' , is in bazarak .
