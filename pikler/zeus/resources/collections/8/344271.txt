Tenroku     events of the `` tenroku '' era . - 970 ( `` tenroku 1 , 1st month '' ) : became minister of the left ( `` sadaijin '' ) ; and became minister of the right ( `` udaijin '' ) in the imperial court hierarchy . - 970 ( `` tenroku 1 , 5th month '' ) : died at the age of 71 ; and the koretada took his place as chief advisor to the emperor . - 970 ( `` tenroku 1 , 10th month '' ) : died at age 79. he had held the office of `` sadaijin '' . - 971 ( `` tenroku 2 , 3rd month '' ) : for the first time , a festival ( `` matsuri '' ) in honor of the `` kami '' of iwashimizu shrine was celebrated . - 971 ( `` tenroku 2 , in the 11th month '' ) : koretada was created prime minister ( `` daijō-daijin '' ) ; was made `` sadaijin '' ; and was named `` udaijin '' . - 4 april 972 ( `` tenroku 3 , 5th day of the 3rd month '' ) : koretada organized ceremonies which confirmed emperor en'yū 's role as monarch . - 972 ( `` tenroku 3 , 11th month '' ) : koretada died at age 49. related pages . - heian period other websites . - national diet library , `` the japanese calendar '' -- historical overview plus illustrative images from library 's collection
