Lalit Modi     lalit kumar modi , ( ; born november 29 , 1963 , delhi , india ) is the former architect of the indian premier league . he was the former `` chairman '' and `` commissioner '' of the indian premier league and also the `` chairman '' of `` airtel champions league t20 '' for a few years , lalit also served as the vice president of the board of cricket control in india ( bcci ) and punjab cricket association lalit modi is also the president and managing director of modi enterprises and the executive director of godfrey phillips india .
