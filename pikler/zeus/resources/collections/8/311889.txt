Vo Chi Cong     võ chí công ( born võ toàn ; 7 august 1912 – 8 september 2011 ) was a vietnamese politician , a founding member of the viet cong and former president of vietnam . in 1912 , công was born võ toàn in quảng nam province , vietnam , in 1912. in 1930 , he first became active in politics . in 1935 , he joined the communist party of indochina ; and he rose through the ranks of the party . from 1987 through 1992 , he was the chairman of the council of state of vietnam which was like the current office of the president of vietnam . công died in ho chi minh city on 8 september 2011 , aged 99. his state funeral was broadcast on television .
