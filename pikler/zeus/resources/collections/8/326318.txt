Aichi S1A     the aichi s1a `` denko '' ( bolt of light ) was a japanese night fighter aircraft . the aichi s1a was intended to replace the nakajima j1n1-s `` gekkou '' ( `` irving '' ) , used during the second world war . the aircraft was not completed before the war ended . two prototypes were made in 1945. the project was cancelled after the prototypes were destroyed during bomb attacks .
