Sang Sapurba     in the malay annals , the legend of sang sapurba was a king of the malay who came from mount siguntang mahameru . he claimed descent from the dhul-qarnayn , king of the malay world , later the malay of royal clans who claimed to be his descendants . according minangkabau chronicle , he is referred to as great king of the king ( `` maharajadiraja '' ) , he was a heroic figure who killed katimuno snake , an immense snake , which destroyed their cultivation .
