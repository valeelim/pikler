Elliott Belgrave     elliot belgrave , chb , qc ( born march 16 , 1931 ) , is a barbadian politician and retired high court judge . he is the acting governor-general of barbados since 1 november 2011. belgrave graduated from the universities of cambridge ( ma ) and london ( llb ) . he is a former director of public prosecutions in barbados . he was judge of the supreme court and court of appeal of barbados . he is a queen 's counsel . belgrave was awarded the companion of honour of barbados for his contribution to the legal system in barbados .
