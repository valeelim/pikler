Afro-Bahamian     afro-bahamians or bahamians of african descent are bahamians whose ancestry is from the continent of africa . most are from west africa . the first africans in the bahamas came from bermuda . they were with the eleutheran adventurers as freed slaves looking for a new life . currently , afro-bahamians are the biggest ethnic group in the bahamas . they are 85 % of the country 's people . they have a much higher standard of living than previous generations .
