Uzen Province     during the edo period and early meiji period , the uesugi clan were daimyo in the area . copper coins were minted in the province in 1861-1863 ( `` bunkyu 1-3 '' ) . in the meiji period , the provinces of japan were converted into prefectures . maps of japan and unzen province were reformed in the 1870s . related pages . - provinces of japan - prefectures of japan - list of regions of japan - list of islands of japan other websites . - murdoch 's map of provinces , 1903
