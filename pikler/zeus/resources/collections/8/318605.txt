Northern Shoveler     the northern shoveler ( ; anas clypeata ) is a common and widespread duck . it breeds in northern areas of europe and asia and across most of north america , and is a rare vagrant to australia . in north america , it breeds along the southern edge of hudson bay and west of this body of water , and as far south as the great lakes west to colorado , nevada , and oregon . the northern shoveler is one of the species to which the `` agreement on the conservation of african-eurasian migratory waterbirds '' ( aewa ) applies . the conservation status is least concern .
