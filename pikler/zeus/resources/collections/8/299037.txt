Rhytidectomy     a rhytidectomy ( commonly called a facelift ) is a type of cosmetic surgery procedure used to make a person 's face look younger . rhytidectomy usually involves the removal of excess facial skin , which cause wrinkles , and the redraping of the skin on the patient 's face and neck . the tightening of underlying tissues of the face is optional . nearly 112,955 face lift procedures were performed in the united states in the year 2010 alone according to the american society for aesthetic plastic surgery . other websites . - medical publication on rhytidectomy
