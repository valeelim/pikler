Lukas Katnik     lukas katnik ( born 31 july 1989 ) is an austrian footballer . he plays for fc pasching in austrias regionalliga mitte as striker . he started playing football in the local clubs fc mäder and fc köblach in the austrian province of vorarlberg . 2006 he went to fc lustenau where he made his professional debut in 2007. in 2010 , he went to fc dornbirn where he scored 10 goals in the first part of the season . in january 2011 , he went on to red bull juniors but went at once to usk anif on loan . in 2011/2012 he came back to the juniors and is mostly part of the starting team in the regionalliga west , which is the 3rd division in austrian football . after a cooperation with fc pasching katnik , he was transferred to this club on loan . references . - players profile transfermarkt.at
