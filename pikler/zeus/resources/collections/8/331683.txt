Billy Bean     william daro `` billy '' bean ( born may 11 , 1964 in santa ana , california ) is a former major league baseball player . he attended loyola marymount university in los angeles . he was an outfielder , a pinch hitter , and a first baseman . he threw and batted left . bean is gay , and wrote a book , `` going the other way : lessons from a life in and out of major league baseball '' . for 13 years , bean was the partner of efrain viega , the founder of yuca restaurant in miami . they broke up in july 2008. bean has appeared on television . he starred in a mtv episode of `` made '' , and he was an actor in an episode of the sitcom `` frasier '' . other websites . - billy bean.com - baseball library page
