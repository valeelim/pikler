Marc Sellam     marc sellam , born the 3th of march 1949 , is a french businessman and president of a private education group in france . he is the founder of ionis education group . graduate from `` école spéciale de mécanique et électricité '' ( 1974 ) and from `` institut des études supérieures des techniques des organisations '' , marc sellam has done a career in telecommunications industry before joining higher education . he first founds the `` institut supérieur européen de gestion group '' in 1980 before created ionis education group . he is still in 2012 the current president of the group .
