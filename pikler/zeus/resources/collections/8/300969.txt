Bit rate     in telecommunications and computing , the bit rate ( sometimes bitrate ) , or data rate , is the amount of data that can be transported or processed in a given amount of time . this is different from , but related to , the symbol rate and the bandwidth . the data rate is usually measured in `` bits per second '' ( `` bit/s '' ) ; sometimes this is qualified with a si prefix . note that kilo has the usual meaning of 1000 , and not 1024 , as was often used in computing . related pages . - bandwidth
