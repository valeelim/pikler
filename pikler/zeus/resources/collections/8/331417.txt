Scarecrow     a scarecrow is a figure usually made of sticks . it looks like a human being . it is dressed in old clothes . a farmer puts a scarecrow in a field after he has planted seeds . he hopes the scarecrow will frighten birds away from the field . some birds such as crows and sparrows eat the seeds the farmer plants . he does not want the birds to do this . if the birds eat the seeds , the farmer will not have a big crop of things to eat . scarecrows are sometimes characters in stories . nathaniel hawthorne wrote a story about a scarecrow who comes to life in `` feathertop '' . the most famous storybook scarecrow is probably the one in `` the wonderful wizard of oz '' by l. frank baum . other websites . - british history of the scarecrow - photo of a crow trap in australia - a typical english scarecrow made of discarded clothing
