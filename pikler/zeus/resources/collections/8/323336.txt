Mehi River     the mehi river is in northern new south wales , australia . it begins at the manmade tareelaroi weir and runs into the barwon river . the mehi is long . the mehi river also runs runs through the major town of moree . the cotton growing industry gets a lot of its water from the mehi river . the river is also used for leisure purposes . water-sking and fishing are popular along the mehi river .
