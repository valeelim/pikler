Competitive eating     competitive eating is a term for a sport where people play to see who can eat the most food in a given time . the sport is most commonly played in the united states and japan . there are many different foods that can be eaten competitively . common ones include hot dogs , hamburgers , hot wings , and pies . the most famous competitive eating contest is nathan 's hot dog eating contest on coney island . it and other events are part of a tour known as major league eating . there are often competitive eating contests at american fairs and carnivals .
