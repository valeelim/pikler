Douglas J. Mink     douglas j. mink is an american software developer and a data archivist at the harvard-smithsonian center for astrophysics . he was part of the team that discovered the rings around the planet uranus . mink was born in elgin , illinois in 1951. he graduated from dundee community high school in 1969. mink earned an s.b . degree ( 1973 ) and an s.m . degree ( 1974 ) in planetary science from the massachusetts institute of technology .
