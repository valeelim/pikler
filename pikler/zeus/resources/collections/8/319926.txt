San Marino at the Olympics     san marino at the olympics is a history of 21 games in 16 countries and 80+ athletes . since 1960 , san marino has contributed to the growth of the `` olympic movement '' . the international olympic committee 's official abbreviation for san marino was sma . it is now smr . a team from san marino was first at the 1960 summer olympics . they have been in 12 summer olympics and 7 winter olympics . the country has never won a medal . among its most successful athletes are francesco amici and emanuela felici . both competed in shooting at the 2004 summer olympics and `` missed out on places in the finals by the narrowest of margins '' . related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - san marino profile at london2012.com
