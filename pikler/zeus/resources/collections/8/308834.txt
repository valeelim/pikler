Don Juan DeMarco     don juan demarco is a 1995 movie starring johnny depp . it was released to theaters by warner bros. john arnold demarco ( johnny depp ) believes himself to be don juan , the greatest lover in the world . clad in a cape and eye-mask , he undergoes psychiatric treatment with marlon brando 's character to cure him of his apparent disillusionment with his identity . but the psychiatric sessions have an unexpected effect on his psychiatrist , whose relationship with his wife , played by faye dunaway , is also affected . selena made a brief cameo as a mariachi singer . a hit single for bryan adams , `` have you ever really loved a woman ? `` , is the cornerstone of the soundtrack . cast . - johnny depp as john arnold demarco/don juan - marlon brando as dr. jack mickler - faye dunaway as marilyn mickler - géraldine pailhas as doña ana - franc luz as don antonio - bob dishy as dr. paul showalter - rachel ticotin as doña inez - talisa soto as doña julia - 'tiny ' lister , jr. as rocco compton - richard c. sarafin as detective sy tobias
