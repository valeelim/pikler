Richard Beckinsale     richard arthur beckinsale ( 6 july 1947 - 19 march 1979 ) was a british actor . beckinsale was born in carlton , nottinghamshire and went to the royal academy of dramatic art in london . he had starring roles in five sitcoms . they are : `` the lovers '' , `` rising damp '' , `` porridge '' , its spin-off `` going straight '' and `` bloomers '' . beckinsale has two daughters , actresses samantha and kate beckinsale . he died of a heart attack in sunningdale , berkshire .
