Battles of Napoleon     battles of napoleon is a 1988 computer wargame by chuck kroegel and david landrey released by strategic simulations , inc.. it was released for the apple ii , commodore 64 and dos . `` computer gaming world '' gave it a glowing review , calling it `` the game that can keep you satisfied , even addicted , for many years to come . '' the magazine later awarded it as `` wargame of the year '' .
