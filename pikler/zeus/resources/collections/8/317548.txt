Solomon Islands at the Olympics     the solomon islands was first at the olympic games in 1984. they have been in every summer olympic games since then . the nation has never been in the winter olympic games . as of 2011 , no athlete for the solomon islands has won an olympic medal . the national olympic committee for the solomon islands was formed in 1983 and recognized by the international olympic committee that same year . related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - solomon islands profile at london2012.com
