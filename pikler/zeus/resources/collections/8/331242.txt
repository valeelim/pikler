Jude Law     david jude heyworth law ( born 29 december 1972 ) is a english actor . he was born in lewisham , london and grew up in blackheath , london . he played major roles many movies . these include `` the talented mr. ripley '' , `` enemy at the gates '' , `` a.i . artificial intelligence '' , `` road to perdition '' , `` cold mountain '' , `` alfie '' , `` closer '' and `` the holiday '' . for his work in `` the talented mr. ripley '' , he won 2000 bafta award for `` actor in a supporting role '' .
