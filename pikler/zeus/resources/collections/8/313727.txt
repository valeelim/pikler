Santa Baby     `` santa baby '' is a holiday themed song about a person who wants extravagant things for the christmas season . it was first recorded in 1953 by eartha kitt , and many other musicians , including though not limited to pussycat dolls , kylie minogue , taylor swift , ariana grande and madonna have covered this melody throughout the decades . `` santa baby '' was originally recorded by eartha kitt with henry rené and his orchestra in new york city in july 1953. the song was a huge hit for kitt , and she later said that it was one of her favorite songs to record . `` santa baby '' is a song recorded by american recording artist ariana grande , featuring a guest appearance from american actress liz gillies . the song was released by republic records on december 10 , 2013 , as the fourth single from grande 's ep `` christmas kisses '' . track listing . - digital download 1 . `` santa baby ( feat . liz gillies ) '' – 2:51
