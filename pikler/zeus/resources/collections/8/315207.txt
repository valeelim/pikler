Malay Wikipedia     malay wikipedia ( malay : wikipedia bahasa melayu , jawi script : ويکيڤيديا بهاس ملايو , abbreviation : mswiki ) is the malay version of wikipedia . started in october 2002 , currently has over articles as in november 2011. it is the 29th largest edition . other websites . - : wiktionary : en : category : malay_language - mobile version of malay wikipedia
