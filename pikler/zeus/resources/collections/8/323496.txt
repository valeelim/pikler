Norman Lamont     norman stewart hughson lamont , baron lamont of lerwick ( born 8 may 1942 ) is a british politician . he was born in the shetland islands . he read economics at the university of cambridge . he was the member of parliament for kingston-upon-thames from 1972-1997. he was chancellor of the exchequer from 1990-1993. he was made a life peer in july 1998. he is the director of an investment company .
