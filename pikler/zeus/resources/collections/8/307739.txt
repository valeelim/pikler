Encore (album)    encore ( album ) encore is the fifth studio album by american rapper , eminem . `` encore '' was released on november 12 , 2004 and was produced by dr. dre , eminem , luis resto , mike elizondo and mark batson . the album was given 3 out of 5 stars by allmusic . it was nominated for best rap album , best rap performance by a duo or group for the song `` encore '' and best rap solo performance for `` mockingbird '' at the 48th grammy awards .
