Ape Escape 3     ape escape 3 is a video game that was published by sony computer entertainment and was developed by japan studio . the game was only available for the playstation 2 system . the game was the third ape escape game in the ape escape . the game was first released in japan on july 14 , 2005 , and was later released in north america on january 17 , 2006 , in europe on may 5 , 2006 , and in australia on may 18 , 2006. the game was then re-released in japan on the playstation 2 on november 2 , 2005. the game received positive reviews with a x-play giving it a 3/5 , game informer gave it a 8.5/10 , computer games magazine gave it 90/100 and stated `` this is the best and most spirited of the ape escapes , which is faint praise if you 're only familiar with the recent half-assed psp version.
