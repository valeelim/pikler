The Vagina Monologues     the vagina monologues is a monologue play written by eve ensler . the production has been staged in many countries . ensler originally starred in the play by herself , but she was later replaced by three celebrity monologists . a television version featuring ensler was shown on cable tv channel hbo . much of the profits made from the production went to women 's anti-violence charities . other websites . - `` the vagina monologues '' at random house - v-day official site - official uk page - `` the vagina monologues '' at hbo.com
