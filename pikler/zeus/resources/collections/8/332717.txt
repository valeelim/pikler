Samantha Fox     samantha karen fox ( born 15 april 1966 ) is a british singer , actress and former glamour model . she was born in london . she has sold over thirty million records all over the world and has performed in many movies and television programs . she was very popular in the 1980s and early 1990s . she came out in 2003 and stated that she is in a lesbian relationship . in 2009 she stated her intention to enter into a civil partnership with her manager , myra stratton . fox was a contestant in the 2009 series of `` i 'm a celebrity ... get me out of here ! `` .
