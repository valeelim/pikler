Dirk Bogarde     sir dirk bogarde ( 28 march 1921 - 8 may 1999 ) was a british actor and writer . bogarde was born in west hampstead , london . he starred in many movies , including `` victim '' , `` the servant '' , `` darling '' , `` accident '' and `` death in venice '' . bogarde wrote novels , autobiographies and memoirs . bogarde was a closeted homosexual who lived with his partner anthony forwood ( 1915-1988 ) for nearly forty years until forwood 's death . bogarde died of a heart attack in chelsea , london .
