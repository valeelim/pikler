La Tracalera     `` la tracalera '' ( ) is a song by american singer selena . the song was first recorded in 1985 for `` the new girl in town '' . it was re-recorded in 1990 for `` ven conmigo '' . the original version of the song called `` el tracalero '' ( ) was recorded by selena 's father abraham quintanilla jr. abraham had his own band called `` los dinos '' from the 1960s-1970s . anselmo sanchez chavez covered the song in 1996 .
