Norwich School     norwich school ( before called king edward vi â€™ s grammar school ) is a co-ed ( girls and boys ) private school in norwich , england . it is one of the oldest schools in the world , going back to at least 1096. norwich school has one of the best academic records in east anglia . `` the daily telegraph '' ranked the school 's a-level results as 74th in 2006 , 55th in 2007 and 80th in 2008 amongst independent schools in the uk . in each case , the rank was the highest in norfolk and suffolk . the school used to be for boys only . it began admitting girls below the sixth form in september 2008. all year groups had girls in them by september 2010. norwich school is an anglican school . it has a long relationship with norwich cathedral , which it uses for morning assemblies and some annual events . many famous people were taught at norwich school , like horatio nelson , who has a house named after him . there are eight houses : brooke , coke , seagrim , nelson , parker , repton , school and valpy .
