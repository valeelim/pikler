Darren Walsh (footballer)    darren walsh ( footballer ) darren walsh ( born 27 december 1984 ) is a former australian rules footballer who played for essendon in the afl . he was drafted by the essendon football club in the 2002 national draft at pick 27 in the 2nd round . he played two games in his first season with essendon but ended the season with glandular fever . walsh was delisted after the 2004 season without having played another game .
