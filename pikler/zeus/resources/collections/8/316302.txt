Viveiro     viveiro ( sometimes called vivero ) is a town and municipality . it is in the province of lugo . lugo is in the northwestern part of galicia , spain . as of 2010 , over 16,000 people live in viveiro . the calendar of viveiro holds several festivals , both religious and not . viveiro has the oldest carnival in galicia . it is celebrated during the four days before lent , from saturday until shrove tuesday . it finishes on tuesday with many floats travelling through the streets . viveiro celebrates the st. james ' day battle on july 25 at the port of celeiro . it has marines moving through the estuary of viveiro in a well organised and orderly way , similar to a march . twinned towns . - lannion , france - old havana , cuba - perth , australia other websites . - ferrol-san cibrao port authority
