Jeff Kent     jeffrey franklin kent ( born march 7 , 1968 ) was an american baseball player . he played second base . he played for six different teams , including the new york mets , san francisco giants , and los angeles dodgers . he was a five-time all-star , and the 2000 national league mvp . in his career , he batted .290 , with 2,461 hits , 377 home runs and 1,518 rbis . he has more home runs than any other second baseman . he also drove in 90 or more batters in nine straight seasons . kent was from bellflower , california and went to high school in huntington beach .
