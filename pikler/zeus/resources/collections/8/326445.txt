Cam Cameron     cam cameron is an american football coach who is currently the offensive coodinator of the baltimore ravens . he was the head coach for the miami dolphins in 2007 , but was fired after a 1-15 season . cameron has been heavily criticized for his conservative ( safe ) play-calling . cameron spent time as a college football assistant . he was also the head coach of indiana university 's football team . cameron spent some time as the washington redskins 's quarterbacks coach . he was later promoted to the offensive coodinator position of the san diego chargers , where he helped guide future-star quarterback drew brees . cameron was hired as the head coach of the miami dolphins before the 2007 season . however , the franchise had its worst ever season and finished 1-15. he had an overtime victory against the baltimore ravens , coincidentally cameron 's next team . cameron was replaced by tony sparano . during a baltimore ravens roster and coaching overall , john harbaugh hired cameron as the new baltimore offensive coodinator . in 2008 , cameron worked on the suggs package , a play-calling that would have both quarterbacks joe flacco and troy smith . following 2008 , cameron was criticized heavily for his conservative ( safe ) play-calling . after his contract expired following the 2011 season , harbaugh announced that cameron would in fact be back .
