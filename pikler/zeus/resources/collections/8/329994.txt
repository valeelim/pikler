Britney (album)    britney ( album ) britney is the third studio album released by britney spears . it was released in 2001. it has the singles `` i 'm a slave 4 u '' ( the highest charting single off the album ) , `` overprotected '' , `` i love rock 'n ' roll '' , `` i 'm not a girl , not yet a woman '' , and `` boys '' .
