Let It Snow! Let It Snow! Let It Snow!    let it snow ! let it snow ! let it snow ! `` let it snow ! let it snow ! let it snow ! '' ( also known as `` let it snow ! '' ) is a holiday oriented song that is played during the thanksgiving , christmas and new years holidays ' season , although it does not mention any holiday within the lyrics . it was originally written by sammy cahn . it has been covered by many singers , including though not limited to ella fitzgerald , dean martin and anne murray throughout the decades .
