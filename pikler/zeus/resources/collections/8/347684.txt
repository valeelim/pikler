Danny Boyle     daniel boyle ( born 20 october 1956 ) is a british movie director and producer . he was born in radcliffe , lancashire into a working-class irish catholic family . boyle 's movies include `` shallow grave '' , `` trainspotting '' , `` a life less ordinary '' , `` the beach '' , `` 28 days later '' , `` millions '' , `` 28 weeks later '' , `` sunshine '' , `` slumdog millionaire '' and `` 127 hours '' . boyle won bafta awards for shallow grave , trainspotting and slumdog , academy awards for slumdog millionaire and golden globe awards for slumdog .
