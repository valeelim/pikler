Vélizy-Villacoublay     vélizy-villacoublay is a french commune . it is in the southwestern suburbs of paris 13.9 km ( 8.6 mi ) from the center and 3.2 km ( 2.0 mi ) east of versailles . about 20,030 people lived here in 2006. education . - institut des sciences et techniques des yvelines - université de versailles saint-quentin-en-yvelines
