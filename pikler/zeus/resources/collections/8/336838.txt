Glee club     a glee club is a musical group or choir . during the 19th and early 20th century , the glee club was only for men . later in the 20th century it became available for all students in college . glee club was the most popular after-school activity in college campuses . there are some glee clubs in high school . the oldest glee clubs in the united states are the harvard glee club , founded in 1858. other pages . - `` glee '' ( an american television series )
