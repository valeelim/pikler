F♯ (musical note)    f♯ ( musical note ) f♯ ( eff-sharp or f-sharp ; also known as fa dièse or fi ) is the seventh semitone of the solfège . it lies a chromatic semitone above f and a diatonic semitone below g , thus being enharmonic to sol bémol or g ( g-flat ) . related pages . - f-sharp major - f-sharp minor
