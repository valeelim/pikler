Trichomoniasis     trichomoniasis ( often called trich ) is a sexually transmitted disease . it is caused by a parasite called `` trichomonas vaginalis '' , which is a protozoa made of just one cell . trichomoniasis can affect both men and women . it is usually an infection of the urinary system and genitals . the `` trichomonas vaginalis '' parasite can only live in the human genital tract . trichomoniasis is only spread through sex , or if two people 's genitals touch . most people with trichomoniasis do not have any symptoms . when people do have symptoms , they usually start about 5 to 28 days after the person got infected . symptoms may include : - pain , burning , or itching in the urethra ( urethritis ) , the vagina ( vaginitis ) , or the penis - pain while having sex ( dyspareunia ) - women may have a yellow-green , badly-smelling vaginal discharge - lower abdominal pain ( this does not happen often ) complications are serious problems that can happen because a person has a disease . these problems do not happen to everyone with trichomoniasis . they are the most serious problems that trichomoniasis can cause . some of the most dangerous problems that can happen to a person with trichomoniasis include :
