1920 Duluth lynchings     the 1920 duluth lynchings occurred when a mob attacked and lynched three black circus workers in duluth , minnesota . beforehand there were rumours that black people had raped a teenager . a physician who examined the girl found no evidence of rape or assault . the killings shocked the country , particularly because they had occurred in the northern united states , four lynchings had occurred in minnesota before that time . in 2003 , the city of duluth erected a memorial to the murdered workers .
