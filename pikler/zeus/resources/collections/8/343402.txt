Constitution of Azerbaijan     the constitution of azerbaijan was started on 12 november 1995 by popular referendum . it was changed on 24 august 2002. it is the `` highest legal force '' in azerbaijan . the preamble to the constitution says , in order to `` provide prosperity and welfare of the whole society and each individual '' , that the following objectives be declared : - protection of national sovereignty and territorial integrity - a constitutional democracy - make a civil society - a secular state based upon the rule of law - a `` worthy life level '' for citizens and `` just '' economic and social order - observance of `` universal human values '' , peace and international cooperation related pages . - constitution - constitutionalism - constitutional economics other websites . - the constitution of azerbaijan at the presidential website
