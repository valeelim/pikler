Heptachlor     heptachlor is an insecticide . it was used against ants , termites , against mosquitos of the genus anopheles , that can spread malaria and as a general pesticide . in humans , heptachlor damages the liver , and the central nervous system . it may cause cancer . it is sold as a powder . the diels-alder reaction is used to produce it . the compound is very stable and can stay in the environment for decades . hepltachlor is one of the substances listed in the stockholm convention on persistent organic pollutants .
