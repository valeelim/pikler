Metropolitan Stadium     metropolitan stadium was a sports stadium in bloomington , minnesota . it opened in 1956 as the home of the local minor league baseball team , the minneapolis millers . it was made larger in 1961 so that it could be used for major league teams . in 1961 , the minnesota twins moved from washington , dc and the minnesota vikings began play at the stadium . it was replaced by the metrodome in 1982 , and was torn down in the mid-1980s . the site of the stadium is now used by the mall of america . when people talk about the ballpark nowadays , they often call it the `` old met '' . the first floor of the mall has a brass marker in the shape of a home plate , on the spot where the old met 's home base once was .
