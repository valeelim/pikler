Sun Is Up     `` sun is up '' is a song by inna . inna is a singer of dance music from romania . the song is also a single and the first single from inna 's second album . the album is called `` i am the club rocker '' . `` sun is up '' was launched in romania in august 2010. in 2011 , it was out in uk , france , germany , etc . it was a # 1 in switzerland and bulgaria and a top ten in france and romania . the video for the song is in spain at a hotel and in it , inna is in love with her photographer .
