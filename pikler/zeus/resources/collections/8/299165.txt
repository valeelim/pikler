Senegal at the Olympics     senegal at the olympics is a history which began in 1954. the international olympic committee 's official abbreviation for senegal was sgl . it is now sen. senegal has been to all summer olympic games held since 1964. unlike most surrounding nations , senegal has never missed any summer olympics since its independence . senegal is among the small group of tropical countries that have been in the winter olympic games . its winter athletes have been in four winter olympics since 1984 related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - senegal at olympic.org - senegal profile at london2012.com
