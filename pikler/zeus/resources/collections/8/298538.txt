Soho Theatre     soho theatre is a theatre in the soho district of the city of westminster in london , england . it presents new works of theatre , together with comedy and cabaret . the soho theatre was formed in 1969 by verity bargate and fred proud at old compton street in soho . it shifted many times before finally settling at dean street , also in soho , in 2000. the present building has a 140-seat hall and an 85/100-seat studio . the theatre is also home to a writers ' centre which helps to develop new writers to work in theatre , as well as film , television , and radio . the centre accepts scripts from new writers .
