César Chávez Day     césar chávez day is a state holiday in california , colorado and texas observed on march 31. the holiday is to remember the life of cesar chavez and his civic involvement . all state government offices , community colleges , and libraries are closed , except for k-12 schools . governor arnold schwarzenegger suspended observance of the holiday in 2004 and 2005 to save money during the aftermath of the power crisis . it is an optional holiday in arizona , michigan , nebraska , and new mexico . californians celebrate this holiday by planting lilies in their front yard .
