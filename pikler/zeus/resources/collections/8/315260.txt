Pall Mall     pall mall is a street in the city of westminster , london . it is parallel to the mall . pall mall east leads into trafalgar square . the street 's name is taken from a game called 'pall mall ' . the game was a mallet-and-ball game played in the early 17th century . pall mall is probably best known for its many gentlemen 's clubs . related pages . - westminster - london other websites . - introductory page from the survey of london - pall mall on touruk - panoramic photograph of pall mall - 19th century gentleman 's clubs on pall mall ( including photographs ) - the institute of directors ' 116 pall mall
