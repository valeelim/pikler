Kung Fu Panda: Secrets of the Masters    kung fu panda : secrets of the masters kung fu panda : secrets of the masters ( also shortened to secrets of the masters ) is a 2011 computer-animated short movie from dreamworks animation . it was released on december 13 , 2011 with the `` kung fu panda 2 '' dvd and blu-ray . `` secrets of the masters '' won in 2012 an annie award for best animated special production .
