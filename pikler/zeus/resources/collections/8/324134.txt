E. M. Forster     edward morgan forster om , ch ( 1 january 1879 - 7 june 1970 ) was a british writer . forster was born in marylebone , london . he attended tonbridge school , an independent school in tonbridge , kent . he studied at kings college , cambridge . forster was a homosexual and wrote about homosexuality , class division and gender . his novels include `` where angels fear to tread '' , `` the longest journey '' , `` a room with a view '' , `` howards end '' , `` a passage to india '' and `` maurice '' . he also wrote short stories , plays , essays , libretti and travel guides . forster died of a stroke in coventry , warwickshire .
