Jean-Luc Vayssière     jean-luc vayssière , born in 1956 , is a french professor , specialized in genetics and cell biology . he was president of the `` université de versailles saint-quentin-en-yvelines '' from the 12th of april 2012 until april 2016. graduate from the paris diderot university in molecular biology , he starts his career as assistant at the `` collège de france '' in 1990. in 1994 , he became lecturer at the `` université de versailles saint-quentin-en-yvelines '' . in 2001 , he is appointed head of training at the `` école pratique des hautes études '' before entering in 2006 as teacher at the `` université de versailles saint-quentin-en-yvelines '' . alongside his teaching activities , he is a researcher at the laboratory of genetics and cell biology , responsible of the `` stress and cell death '' team . the 12th of april 2012 , he is elected president of the `` université de versailles saint-quentin-en-yvelines '' , succeeding sylvie faucheux . he is replaced by dider guillemot in may 2016 .
