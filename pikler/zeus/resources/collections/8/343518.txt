Hand (unit)    hand ( unit ) the hand is a unit of measurement of length . it is now used only to measure the height of horses in some english-speaking countries , including australia , canada , the united kingdom and the united states . this unit started in ancient egypt and was originally based on the distance across a human hand . today it is equal to four inches or 10.16 centimeters . it may be abbreviated to `` h '' or the plural `` hh '' .
