Mehdi Huseynzade     mehdi hanifa oglu huseynzade ( december 22 , 1918 , novxanı , azerbaijan – november 2 , 1944 , vitovlje , slovenia ) was an azerbaijani guerrilla and scout during world war ii . after he died he was given the title of hero of the soviet union on april 11 , 1957. huseynzade attended the azerbaijan university of languages . his life and heroism during world war ii was described in imran gasimov 's book `` on distant shores '' .
