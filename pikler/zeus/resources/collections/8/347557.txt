Armed Forces of Belarus     the armed forces of belarus are the ground forces and the belarusian air force . all are under the command of the ministry of defense of the republic of belarus . lieutenant general yurij viktorovich zhadobin is the minister of defense since december 4 , 2009. being a landlocked country , belarus has no navy . the ministry of defence of the republic of belarus is the government group that has the duties of raising and maintaining the armed forces of belarus . the formation of the ministry began in 1992. this was a year after the end of the soviet union and belarus becoming an independent state . references . - belarusian army : capacity and its role in the region
