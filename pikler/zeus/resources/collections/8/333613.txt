Vampire Lesbians of Sodom     vampire lesbians of sodom is a satirical play by charles busch . it is about two immortal vampire lesbians . they are locked in eternal antagonism after surviving the downfall of the biblical city of sodom . both women arrive in jazz age broadway and hollywood . they pretend to be silent movie stars . they reach a truce in a final scene set in las vegas in the 1980s . `` the new york times '' described the show as having `` costumes flashier than pinball machines , outrageous lines , awful puns , sinister innocence , [ and ] harmless depravity '' . it was first performed at the limbo lounge in manhattan 's east village in 1984. it moved off broadway in june 1985 to the provincetown playhouse . it had a five year run .
