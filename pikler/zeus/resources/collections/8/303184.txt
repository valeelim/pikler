I Wanna Go    i wan na go `` i wan na go '' is a 2011 single by britney spears . the song is third from her seventh studio album `` femme fatale '' . the song was released on june 13 , 2011. the song was an instant success . it reached the top of the billboard hot 100 a day after debuting . this resulted in a flash mob in times square . she also shut down times square for an unexpected performance . the song has currently been downloaded 1 million times on itunes .
