Lesotho at the Olympics     lesotho was first at the olympic games in 1972. they have been in every summer olympic games since then , but they boycotted the 1976 summer olympics . lesotho has not been in the winter olympic games . as of 2011 , no athlete from lesotho has won an olympic medal . the national olympic committee for lesotho was formed in 1971. they were recognized by the international olympic committee in 1972 .
