Josh Duhamel     joshua david `` josh '' duhamel ( born november 14 , 1972 ) is an american actor and former fashion model . he is well known for his role in the movie `` transformers '' . he is married to singer fergie . duhamel was born in minot , north dakota . his mother , bonnie l. kemper , is a massage therapist , and his father , larry duhamel , is an advertising salesman . he is of french-canadian , irish , english , german , and norwegian ancestry . duhamel was raised catholic .
