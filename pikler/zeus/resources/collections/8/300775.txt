Neopterygii     neopterygii is a group of fish . neopterygii means `` new fins '' . there are only few changes during their evolution from the earlier actinopterygians . they appeared somewhere in the late permian , before the time of the dinosaurs . the neopterygii is a very successful group of fishes , because they can move more rapidly than their ancestors . their scales and skeletons began to lighten during their evolution , and their jaws became more powerful and efficient .
