Kurt Alder     kurt alder ( july 10 , 1902 – june 20 , 1958 ) was a german chemist and nobel laureate . alder was born in the industrial area of königshütte , silesia ( modern day chorzów , upper silesia , poland ) . he received his early schooling there . alder left the area for political reasons when königshütte became part of poland in 1922. he studied chemistry at the university of berlin from 1922 , and later at the university of kiel where his earned a phd in 1926 for work supervised by diels . in 1930 alder was appointed reader for chemistry at kiel , and promoted to lecturer in 1934. in 1936 he left kiel to join i g farben industrie at leverkusen , where he worked on synthetic rubber . in 1940 , he was appointed professor of experimental chemistry and chemical technology at the university of cologne and director of the institute of chemistry there . throughout this time and despite the many obstacles to original research in europe at the time , he continued a systematic program of investigations of his particular interests in the synthesis of organic compounds . in all he published more than 151 papers in this field .
