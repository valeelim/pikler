Strepsiptera     strepsiptera is a order of insects , any of about 600 species of small insects that are notable for their bizarre form of parasitism . strepsipterans are parasitic in planthoppers , leafhoppers , treehoppers , froghoppers , bees , and other insects . mature females are usually wingless and saclike , whereas the males have large , fanlike hindwings , short , clublike forewings , bulging eyes , and comblike antennae .
