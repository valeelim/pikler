Benin at the Olympics     benin has been in every summer olympic games held since 1980. benin has never won an olympic medal . no athletes from benin have been in any winter olympic games . the international olympic committee 's official abbreviation for benin is ben . in 1964 , it was day for dahomey . related pages . - list of ioc country codes other websites . - benin profile at london2012.com - benin at sportsreference.com
