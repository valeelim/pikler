Il Canzoniere     il canzoniere is a collection of 365 poems . it was written by the italian poet petrarch between 1336 and 1373. its central theme is the poet 's love for laura , a woman petrarch allegedly met on april 6 , 1327 , in the church of sainte claire in avignon other websites . - il canzoniere online - il canzoniere text - canzoniere ( different edition ) and other works - petrarch and laura multi-lingual site
