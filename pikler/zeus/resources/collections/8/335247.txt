Campbell Newman     campbell kevin thomas newman ( born 12 august 1963 ) was the 38th premier of queensland . he is from the liberal national party of queensland . he became premier on 26 march 2012 after his party won the state election . prior to that he was the 15th lord mayor of brisbane from 2004 to 2011. at the queensland state election on saturday 31 january 2015 , newman lost his seat of ashgrove to his predecessor kate jones . newman will retire from politics after he lost his own parliamentary seat and on 14 february 2015 , annastacia palaszczuk was sworn in as premier .
