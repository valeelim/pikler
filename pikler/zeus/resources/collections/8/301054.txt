Ksenia Sobchak     ksenia anatolyevna sobchak ( ; born on november 5 , 1981 ) is a russian television presenter , actress and singer . since 2004 she has been the presenter of the reality show `` house 2 '' . she is the only child of politicians anatoly sobchak ( 1937-2000 ) and lyudmila narusova ( b.1951 ) . ksenia was never married and has no children . she lives in moscow ( russia ) .
