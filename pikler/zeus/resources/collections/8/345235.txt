Qatif     qatif or al-qatif is a governorate and urban area in eastern province , saudi arabia . it goes from ras tanura and jubail in the north to dammam in the south . it is from the persian gulf in the east to king fahd international airport in the west . qatif is one of the oldest towns in the eastern side of the arabian peninsula . its history goes back to 3500 bc . qatifi people used to work as merchants , farmers , and fishers . now most of qatifi 's people are working in oil , industry , public services , education and health care sectors .
