Hearst, Ontario    hearst , ontario hearst is a town in the canadian province of ontario . the population of hearst was 5,620 in 2006. the mayor of hearst is roger sigouin . hearst was founded in 1913 because of the construction of the national transcontinental railway . it got it 's name from william howard hearst who was the then ontario minister of forests and mines and he later became premier of ontario . around 85 % of the population for hearst is francophone . other websites . - official website
