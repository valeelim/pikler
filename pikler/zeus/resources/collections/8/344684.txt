Matej     matej is a given name that originates from the slavic nations of eastern europe . it is one of the most common male names in both slovakia and slovenia . it is also common in croatia and ukraine and less common in the czech republic . the name is originally derived from the evangelist disciple st. matthew . foreign versions of the name include matthew in english , math√§us in german , matei in romania and mateo in italian . matej , being a biblical name ( see acts 1:23 ) can be easily cross-referenced with the corresponding name in other languages . the slavic pronunciation of the name consists of matej with a soft `` j '' . matej in ukraine and russia is matviy , and in poland it is maciey . the hebrew version of the name is mattithyahu or matisyahu which translate to `` gift of yahweh '' in standard hebrew and tiberian hebrew .
