List of rivers of Kuwait     this is a list of rivers in kuwait . kuwait does not have any permanent rivers , but does have a few wadis . also listed are the river-like marine channels around bubiyan island . wadis . - bahrat al abraq - shaib rujm al jahtan - wadi al batin ( kuwait river ) marine channels . - khawr abd allah - khawr bubiyan - khawr az zubayr - khawr as subiyah - khawr al tha'aleb
