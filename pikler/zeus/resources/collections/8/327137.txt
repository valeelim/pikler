Pentamerone     the pentamerone or lo cunto de li cunti overo lo trattenemiento de peccerille ( `` the tale of tales , or entertainment for little ones '' ) is a seventeenth-century collection of fairy tales by giambattista basile . the collection consists of a frame story and 49 fairy tales . ten old women tell the tales over five days . the collection contains many famous fairy tale types such as `` cinderella '' and `` puss in boots '' . references . - zipes , jack ( ed . ) 2000 . `` the oxford companion to fairy tales '' . oxford up . pp . 377-8. other websites . - `` la vita di giambattista basile '' ( in italian ) - the complete text of `` lo cunto de li cunti '' ( in neapolitan ) - surlalune fairy tale pages : il pentamerone by giambattista basile - illustrations by george cruikshank - professor s. cicciotti 's page about g. b. basile ( in italian ) - online text of some stories , in english ( from taylor translation )
