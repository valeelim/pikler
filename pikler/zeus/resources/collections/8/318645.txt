Suzuki Choinori     choinori ( `` riding nearby '' in japanese ) is a scooter built by suzuki . it was launched at 2003 for 59,800 yen . reducing the size and not using any high cost parts were the secret to low price . however , because of cutting costs , they do not have rear suspension and the maximum speed is around . it also has an aluminum four-stroke 50 cc engine . the special thing about the engine is that it weighs 40 % less than other four-stroke engines . it also make the scooter lighter and cheaper . due to its low price , it was a great hit in japan . however , suzuki stopped producing choinori in 2007 because it failed to pass the new gas emission standards . references . - suzuki motor company ( japan ) , a japanese anonymous visitor , tatsuwo sato , hiroyuki takeuchi
