Bongo (antelope)    bongo ( antelope ) the bongo ( tragelaphus eurycerus ) is a nocturnal antelope . it has two subspecies : the western or lowland bongo , and the eastern or mountain bongo . the western bongo is near threatened and the mountain bongo is critically endangered . bongos eat plants . they live in forests in africa . kenya is the only place eastern bongos live in the wild . like the west african giraffe , the eastern or mountain bongo is one of the most threatened animals in africa . bongos live in both captivity and the wild . as bongos grow their horns appear . both male and female bongos have horns .
