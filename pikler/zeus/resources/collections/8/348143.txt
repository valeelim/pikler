Confessions of a Teenage Drama Queen     confessions of a teenage drama queen is a 2004 american teen comedy movie . it was written by gail parent and is based on the novel of the same name by dyan sheldon . it was directed by sara sugarman . it was produced by robert shapiro and matthew hart . it is narrated by and stars lindsay lohan as lola cep , a 15-year-old girl who wants to be an actress . she moves from new york city to new jersey . her classmates include ella gerard ( alison pill ) and carla santini ( megan fox ) . adam garcia plays rock singer stu wolff . glenne headly plays lola 's mother karen cep .
