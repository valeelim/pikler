Communauté de communes Coteaux et Châteaux     the communauté de communes coteaux et châteaux was a group of communes in the hérault departement and languedoc-roussillon region of france . it was created on december 31 , 1997 , by decision of the prefecture , and dissolved on january 1 , 2013 upon merging with surrounding `` communauté de communes '' to form the communauté des communes des avant-monts du centre hérault . the name comes from the local `` coteaux '' ( vineyards ) and `` chateaux '' ( castles ) , prominent features of the countryside . `` coteaux et chateaux '' contained the following communes : - fos - gabian - margon - montesquieu - neffiès - pouzolles - roujan - vailhan other websites . - official website - look up `` coteau '' and `` château '' on the wiktionary .
