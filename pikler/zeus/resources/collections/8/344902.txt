Telephone numbers in Bahrain     telephone numbers in bahrain are usually eight digits long . most of the fixed land line numbers start with 1. cellular ( mobile ) phone numbers and pager numbers start with 3* or 663* or 669* depending on the service provider . the country code is +973 . there is no area code in bahrain . the two digit numbers after the land line prefixes are for different cities or regions in bahrain . batelco is the main service provider for land lines . mobile users commonly subscribe to batelco , zain or viva .
