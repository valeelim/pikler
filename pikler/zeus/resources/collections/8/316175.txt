Shannon Miller     shannon miller ( born march 10 , 1977 ) is an american gymnast . she was born in rolla , missouri . she moved to edmond , oklahoma when she was six months old . she is the most decorated artistic gymnastics performer in the history of the united states . she was the 1993 and 1994 world all-around champion and 1996 summer olympics balance beam gold medalist . shannon won a combined total of 16 world champion and olympic medals between 1991 and 1996. she was also the most successful united states athlete at the 1992 summer olympics in barcelona , receiving five medals overall . other websites . - shannon miller at usa gymnastics
