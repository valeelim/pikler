Biwer     biwer is a commune and small town in eastern luxembourg . the commune is in the canton of grevenmacher , which is part of the old district of grevenmacher . it is made up of biwer , boudler , brouch , hegelsdorf , wecker and weydig . in biwer , the largest village of the commune , are every public buildings . there are the primary school called 'an der wiss ' , the cultural centre 'fancy ' , a swimming pool , a sports hall and a day-care centre . other websites . - biwer commune website
