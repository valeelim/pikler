Víctor Casadesús     víctor manuel casadesús castaño , usually just called víctor ( born 28 february 1985 in palma , majorca , balearic islands ) , is a spanish footballer . he plays for rcd mallorca as a forward . víctor played for spain at the 2004 uefa european under-19 football championship . he scored for spain in the 2–2 semifinal draw ( which they won in a penalty shootout ) over ukraine . he eventually got into the u-21s . honours . - spain u19 - uefa u-19 championship : 2004 other websites . - mallorca official profile
