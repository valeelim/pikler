Fair ball     in baseball , a fair ball is a batted ball that gives the batter to attempt to reach first base . in order for a batted ball to be fair , it must be hit in such a way that it : - settles on fair ground between home and first base , or between home and third base ; or - is on or over fair territory when bounding past first or third base ; or - touches first , second , or third base , which are all in fair territory ; or - touches the body of an umpire or player while the ball is on or over fair territory ( unless it touches the batter in the batter 's box , or bounces off the ground and immediately hits the bat ) ; or - passes out of the playing field in flight while over fair territory , or touches any part of the foul pole , including any attached screen , while in flight ; or - strikes any object for which the ballpark specifies that a batted ball which strikes it is a fair ball ( e.g. , a roof if present )
