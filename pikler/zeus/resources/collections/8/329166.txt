Chōkan     events of the `` chōkan '' era . - 1163 ( `` chōkan 1 , 1st month '' ) : taira no shigemori was promoted to the second rank of the 3rd class in the court hierarchy . - 1163 ( `` chōkan 2 , 2nd month '' ) : at tōdai-ji and kōfuku-ji , prayers were offered for the prosperity of the imperial family . - 14 september 1164 ( `` chōkan 2 , on the 26th day of the 8th month '' ) : former-emperor sutoku died at the age of 46. related pages . - heian period other websites . - national diet library , `` the japanese calendar '' -- historical overview plus illustrative images from library 's collection
