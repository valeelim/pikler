The Slim Shady LP     the slim shady lp is the second studio album and the major-label debut by american rapper , eminem . it was released on february 23 , 1999 and was produced by dr. dre , eminem , mark & jeff bass , and mel-man . the album got critical and commercial success . it debuted at number two on the `` billboard '' 200 charts . two singles from the album were released , `` just do n't give a fuck '' and `` my name is '' . it earned eminem a grammy award for best rap album and was included in `` rolling stone magazine '' 's list of the 500 greatest albums of all time .
