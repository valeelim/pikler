Vernon Rudolph     vernon carver rudolph ( june 30 , 1915 , marshall county , kentucky - august 16 , 1973 ) was the founder of krispy kreme doughnuts , inc. rudolph was the oldest of two boys who were born to plumie and rethie rudolph . as he got older , he began to help in the general store that his father owned . when he graduated from high school , he went to paducah , kentucky with his uncle . in 1933 , rudolph 's uncle bought a doughnut shop and recipe for yeast-raised donuts . he bought them from a french federal employee named joe la-beau . this all happened during the middle of the great depression , so they moved to nashville , tennessee to see if they could get better business there and secure their financial future .
