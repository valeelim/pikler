Madea's Witness Protection    madea 's witness protection madea 's witness protection is a comedy-drama movie . it was directed , written , and produced by tyler perry . the movie is about madea taking care of a white american family who are in being protected by the fbi . the movie was filmed from january to march 2012. the movie was released in the united states on june 29 , 2012. cast . - tyler perry as mabel `` madea '' simmons/joe simmons/brian simmons - eugene levy as george needleman - denise richards as kate needleman - romeo miller as jake - doris roberts as barbara needleman , george 's mother - tom arnold as walter - john amos as pastor nelson , jake 's father - marla gibbs as hattie , madea 's neighbor - danielle campbell as cindy needleman , george and kate 's daughter - devan leos as howie needleman , george and kate 's son
