Renaud     renaud , popular name of renaud séchan ( born 1952 , paris ) is a french singer and songwriter . he sang `` dans mon h.l.m . `` , `` laisse béton '' , `` les bobos '' , `` manhattan-kaboul '' , `` marche à l ’ ombre '' , `` mistral gagnant '' , `` molly malone '' and `` mon beauf ’ '' . in the 90s , he was a contributor for the satirical weekly magazine `` charlie hebdo '' .
