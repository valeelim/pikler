Cupid's Chokehold    cupid 's chokehold `` cupid 's chokehold '' is a song by the rap group gym class heroes . it was released twice . the first time was in 2005. the second time was in 2007. the single became a hit song around 2007. it contains samples of the song `` breakfast in america '' by the group supertramp . patrick stump sings on it . the song reached # 4 on us record charts . it reached # 3 in the united kingdom and canada .
