Spined pygmy shark     the spined pygmy shark is one of the smallest sharks in the world . it is only seven to eight inches long on average . spined pygmy sharks are dark gray or black on top . their bellies glow in the dark . these sharks live very deep in the water ( down to 6,500 feet ) but they migrate up to about 650 feet at night , to hunt . spined pygmy sharks are harmless to humans .
