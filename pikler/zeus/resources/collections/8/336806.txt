Transuranium element     every transuranium element is a chemical element with an atomic number greater than the atomic number of uranium . these elements have atomic numbers greater than 92. the word `` transuranium '' is made of two parts . the greek prefix `` trans- '' means `` beyond '' or `` across '' . the second part of the word is the name of the element `` uranium '' . many transuranium elements are unstable and are not found in nature . an element that is unstable will split into two or more other elements . the process of fission occurs when an element splits into pieces .
