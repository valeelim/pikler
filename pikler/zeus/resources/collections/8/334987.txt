Provinces of Italy     italy is divided into 20 regions ( regioni in italian ) and every region is divided into provinces . there are 110 provinces in italy . each province is headed by a president assisted by a representative body , the provincial council , and an executive body , the provincial executive . the three main jobs of a province are : - local planning and zoning - provision of local police and fire services . - transportation regulation ( car registration , maintenance of local roads , etc . )
