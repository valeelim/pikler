Abdul Salam Azimi     abdul salam azimi ( pashtu : عبدالسلام عظیمی ( born : 1936 , in farah province ) is the chief justice of afghanistan . he is the acting head of the afghan supreme court . he is considered a moderate and wears western-style clothing including suits and ties . azimi is a former professor at the university of nebraska at omaha in the united states . he is an ethnic pashtun of the alizai tribe .
