Dependency     dependency has a number of meanings : - dependency theory - in project management , a dependency is a link amongst a project 's terminal elements . - in computer science , dependency is a state in which one object uses the functions of another object . - dependency theory is an economic worldview . - a dependent area is a classification of territory . - a dependent territory . - co-dependency is a state of behaviour . - a chemical dependency is such a strong need for a substance in order for an animal ( usually a person ) to function properly . - a former or current colony , such as those governed by the united kingdom .
