Alexander Siddig     alexander siddig ( november 21 , 1965 ) is a sudanese actor . siddig is most famous for playing dr julian bashir in the `` star trek '' television series `` '' . siddig married fellow `` star trek '' actor nana visitor in 1997. they had a son in 1996. siddig and visitor divorced in 2001. siddig is the nephew of english actor malcolm mcdowell and of former sudanese prime minister sadiq al-mahdi .
