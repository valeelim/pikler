Stadium     a stadium is a building in which lots of people may watch an event . they are often used for football and athletics . some stadiums are paid to add a company name to the name of their stadium . for example , `` comerica '' park in detroit , michigan , or the alltel stadium in jacksonville , florida . when people do this the company has the `` naming rights '' to the stadium . the name of a stadium can change when another company buys the naming rights to that stadium . an arena is a small indoor stadium . arenas are often used for many different types of events . these include sports and music . other websites . - worldstadia.com
