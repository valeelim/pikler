Horatio Gates     horatio gates ( 1726 - 1806 ) was an american general in the american revolutionary war . general gates fought for the continental army and is credited with the decisive american victory at the battle of saratoga in 1777. but before that , he fought as a british major in the british army before he moved to the new land . however , gates also lost to british general charles cornwallis at the battle of camden in 1780. gates also had a child named robert .
