Eckerö     eckerö is a municipality in the åland islands , finland . as of march 2006 , there are 926 people living in eckerö . eckerö is the westernmost municipality of finland . mariehamn , the capital of åland , is located 35 km away . 95 % of the people in eckerö speak swedish as their first language . other websites . - the official eckerö page , in swedish , finnish , english and german
