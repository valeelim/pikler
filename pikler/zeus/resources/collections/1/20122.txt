Cary Grant     cary grant ( january 18 , 1904 - november 29 , 1986 ) , born as archibald alexander leach in bristol , united kingdom , was an actor in the united states.. he starred in many movies , including 1959 's `` north by northwest '' . grant became an american citizen in 1942. at that time , he legally changed his name to `` cary grant . '' grant died of a cerebral hemorrhage ( a bleed in his brain ) in davenport , iowa , united states . he was cremated and his ashes were scattered .
