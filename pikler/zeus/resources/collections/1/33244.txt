Kesälahti     kesälahti is a municipality in finland . it is part of the north karelia region . there are almost 2,700 people living in kesalahti . kesalahti is the southernmost municipality of north karelia . it was officially founded in 1873. the area of the municipality is 582.67 km² . 195.42 km² of it is water . kesälahti is advertised as a popular summer destination for families enjoying camping . the puruvesi lake shores host three active camping grounds with cabins , ¨and tent and trailer lots to let . the name of the municipality can be translated as 'summer bay ' . the current mayor is jorma turunen . hummovaara , kesälahti ( centre ) , marjoniemi , purujärvi , salokylä , sarvisalo , suitsansaari , totkunniemi , varmonniemi , villala other websites . - the official kesalahti page - advocate of kesälahti and north karelian culture
