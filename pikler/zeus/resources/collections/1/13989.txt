Past     the past is something that has already happened . if something has not happened yet , it is called the future . professors and researchers that study the events of the past , and try to explain why these events happened are called history professors or historians . many books , movies , and television shows tell made-up stories about the past . these are called historical fiction . some books , movies , and television shows from a style called science fiction or fantasy tell made-up stories about being able to travel through time back to the past .
