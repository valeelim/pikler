Carly Simon     carly simon ( born june 25 , 1945 ) is an american singer-songwriter and occasional children 's book author . she was born in new york city to businessman richard l. simon ( 1899-1960 ) and andrea heinemann simon ( 1909-1994 ) . she was raised in riverdale , the bronx . she has two sisters : singer joanna simon ( born 1940 ) and composer lucy simon ( born 1943 ) . she also has a brother , photographer peter simon . simon 's songs include : `` you 're so vain '' , `` nobody does it better '' , `` coming around again '' and `` let the river run '' .
