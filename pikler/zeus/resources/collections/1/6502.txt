Berkeley Software Distribution     berkeley software distribution ( bsd ) is a kind of the unix operating system that is distributed for free since 1989. it was started by people in berkeley , california , usa in the year 1977. now , people from around the world work on it . there are many different kinds of bsd that many people have worked on . some of them are dragonfly bsd , freebsd , netbsd , openbsd , and mac os x. other websites . - dragonfly bsd homepage - freebsd homepage - netbsd homepage - openbsd homepage
