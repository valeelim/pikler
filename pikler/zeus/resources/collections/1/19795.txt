Radon     radon is element 86 on the periodic table . its symbol is rn . radon is a gas and is part of the group known as the noble gases . it does not react with other elements , so it is found pure . radon is radioactive , meaning that it can give off harmful rays . some people have high levels of radon in their houses , and this can be very dangerous . a lot of radon can get stuck in the basement of old houses , and so people end up breathing it in . it gets stuck in the lungs and has been known to cause cancer . there are groups that try to make sure there is no dangerous radon in houses . related pages . - list of common elements - periodic table
