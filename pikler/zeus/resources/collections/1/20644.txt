Dinosaur Comics     dinosaur comics is a comic by ryan north . it shows dinosaurs who have adventures and talk about philosophy and some other topics . the art is the same every day , but the words are different . this comic is intended to be funny . things in the comic that are not true : - dinosaurs did not talk - there were no cabins , cars , or women when dinosaurs lived - the dinosaurs in this comic ( tyrannosaurus rex , dromiceiomimus , and utahraptor ) did not live at the same time or even in the same places . - tyrannosaurus rex was not friendly
