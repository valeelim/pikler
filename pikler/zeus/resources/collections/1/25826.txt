Mortar and pestle     a mortar and pestle are two tools used with each other to mill ( grind ) and mix substances . the `` mortar '' is bowl-shaped , and used to hold the substance to be ground . mortars have smooth , rounded bottoms and wide mouths . the `` pestle '' is a stick used for pounding and grinding . mortar and pestles are sometimes used in pharmacies to crush various ingredients to make drugs . they can also be used in cooking to grind herbs . mortar is also a mix of lime , cement , sand , and water used to bond materials together . related pages . - glass rod - vortex mixer
