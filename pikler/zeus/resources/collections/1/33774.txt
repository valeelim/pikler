Psychiatric hospital     a psychiatric hospital is a place where mentally ill people are kept , sometimes until doctors are ready to place them into halfway houses , where they are slowly released into society . group homes are open to people when they are released from a psychiatric hospital . usually people choose to be in a psychiatric hospital . sometimes psychiatrists decide to put people in hospitals who do not want to be there , because they think the people are dangerous to themselves or other people . psychiatric hospitals used to be called lunatic asylums .
