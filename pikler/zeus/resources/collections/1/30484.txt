Horae     the horae were three goddesses in greek mythology . there are in fact two different groups of goddesses which were known at different times : today they are called the first and second generation to distinguish between them . they were the children of zeus and themis . the first horae were goddesses of the seasons . they were : - thallo ( or thalatte ) - auxo ( or auxesia ) - karpo ( also xarpo or carpo ) the second horae were goddesses of order , justice and law . they were : - dike - eunomia - eirene ( or irene )
