Chania     chania is a greek city which is the capital of chania prefecture . it is in the west of crete . 55,838 people live there . chania has an important port which connects chania with the port of piraeus . chania had an significant development from 13th century till 17th century . then it was under venetian rule . after the venetians , ruled the ottomans until 1912. this year crete was liberated and it is united with greece .
