Franz Berwald     franz adolf berwald was a swedish composer of the 19th century . he was born in stockholm on 23 july 1786. he died in stockholm on 3 april 1868. while he was alive , people generally ignored his composing work , so he had to do other things to gain a living . he worked as an orthopedic surgeon , and later as the manager of a saw mill and glass factory . franz adolf berwald came from a family with four generations of musicians . today , many people see him as one of the finest swedish composers of the 19th century .
