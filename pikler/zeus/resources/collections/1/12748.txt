Anahim Volcanic Belt     the anahim volcanic belt is a nearly east-west line of volcanoes stretches from the west coast of british columbia , canada , just north of vancouver island , and reaches into the interior plateau near quesnel . the farther away from the coast , the younger the volcanoes tend to be . these volcanoes are thought to have formed because of the north american continent sliding westward over the anahim hotspot . volcanoes in this belt include the rainbow , ilgachuz , and itcha ranges , and the nazko cone , which last erupted about 7200 years ago .
