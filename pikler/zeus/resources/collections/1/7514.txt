H. John Heinz III     henry john heinz iii ( october 23 , 1938 – april 4 , 1991 ) was an american politician from pennsylvania and the great-grandson of the founder of h. j. heinz company . he was a member of the united states house of representatives from 1971 to 1977 and was a us senator from 1977-1991. he was killed in a plane crash in 1991. heinz married teresa simões-ferreira on february 5 , 1966 in pittsburgh , pennsylvania . the couple had three sons together : h. john heinz iv , andré , and christopher . a number of places are named after heinz , including : - senator h. john heinz iii archives at the carnegie mellon university libraries - h. john heinz iii school of public policy and management - h. john heinz iii center for science , economics and the environment - h. john heinz iii center for the performing arts ( heinz hall ) , home of the pittsburgh symphony - senator john heinz history center
