Johnny Rivers     johnny rivers ( born john ramistella on november 7 , 1942 in new york city and grew up in louisiana ) is an american rock and roll musician . he was popular in the 1960s and 1970s for his songs like `` secret agent man '' , `` the poor side of town '' , `` baby i need your lovin '' , `` the midnight special '' , and `` swayin ' to the music ( slow dancin ' ) '' . other websites . - official website
