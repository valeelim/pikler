Philosopher     a philosopher is a person who works , studies , or is an expert in philosophy . the word , `` philosopher '' , comes from the greek language and literally means `` friend of wisdom '' . some famous philosophers include : socrates , who was a greek philosopher , immanuel kant , who was a german philosopher , and david hume , who was a scottish philosopher . one of the most famous philosophical questions is `` is there anything about which we can be absolutely certain '' ? a quite different question is `` do we have free will '' ? these , and many other questions , have a long history , and are answered differently by different philosophers .
