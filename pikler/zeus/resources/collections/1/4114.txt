Natural gas     natural gas is a mixture of gases . it consists mainly of hydrocarbons . the main component is methane . natural gas is often found in the ground , either alone or together with petroleum . landfills and natural processes produce a little methane . usually it is transported to users in a pipeline . most natural gas is burnt as a fuel to produce energy . it burns with a clean blue flame , causing little pollution . natural gas is burned to produce electricity , to cook and to heat buildings . it is also used as fuel for natural gas vehicles for transport . when natural gas is burned in a power station it boils water into steam that spins a steam turbine that turns a generator to make electricity . some power stations use natural gas in a gas turbine . natural gas is also converted into many different industrial chemicals . it is the most common feedstock for making pure hydrogen . hydrogen is used as fuel in fuel cells , and to make ammonia . ammonia is used as a fuel , as a fertilizer for crops , and for making many other things . natural gas is also converted into monomers to make many plastic products .
