Dylan Thomas     dylan marlais thomas ( 27 october 1914 - 9 november 1953 ) was a welsh poet . he was born in the town of swansea . he published his first book of poetry in 1932. in addition to writing poetry , he was an excellent speaker . he toured the united kingdom and the united states reciting his poems . he wrote works for radio including `` '' a child 's christmas in wales '' and `` under milk wood '' '' thomas is one of the famous people who appears on the cover of the beatles , sgt . pepper 's lonely hearts club band album .
