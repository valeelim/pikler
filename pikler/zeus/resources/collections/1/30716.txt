Badrinath     badrinatha is an important pilgrimage place for the hindus . the place is in uttarakhand state of india , and is located at a high altitude in the himalayas . the hindu scriptures say that the place is sacred to vishnu , one of the gods of hindus . badrinath gets its name from a tree 's name . the name of that tree is `` badri-tree '' or indian jujube tree . the botanical name of a badri-tree is `` zizyphus jujba lam '' . the modern badrinath temple was established by adi shankaracharya in the ninth century after the old temple fell to ruin . the badrinath area is referred to as badarikaashram ( बदरिकाश्रम ) in hindu scriptures . the temple was renovated many times because of age or damage by avalanche . in the 17th century , the temple was renovated by the kings of garhwal . after the great 1803 himalayan earthquake , it was rebuilt by the king of jaipur .
