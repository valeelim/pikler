Columbia River     the columbia river is a river in british columbia , canada , and the u.s. states of washington and oregon . the columbia river is about 1,243 miles long . its largest tributary is the snake river , which flows west from the state of idaho . the lower part of the columbia forms the border between the states washington and oregon . the columbia drains 254,000 square miles ( 657,857 km ) of land , beginning in the canadian rockies and flowing west through the cascade range , to where it empties into the pacific ocean . there are 14 dams on the columbia river . three are in canada , and the other 11 are in the united states . the dams generate hydroelectricity and provide flood control and water for farms . there are also dams on the snake river and many other tributaries . these dams have , along with their benefits , caused salmon populations in the river to go down .
