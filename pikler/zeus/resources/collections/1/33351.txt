Tiruvannamalai     tiruvannamalai is a place in the indian state of tamilnadu . it is a place of pilgrimage for hindus . in tiruvannamalai , there is a famous and ancient temple of shiva , a god of hindus . the temple is one of the five such temples of shiva in south india . in this temple , people worship shiva in the form of a linga . many hindus go to this place all around the year . in the months of november and december , a festival lasting for ten days takes place . during this festival , people light up an entire hill near tiruvannamala . other pages . - linga - tiruvannamalai
