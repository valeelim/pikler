Lust     lust is a term for a strong desire or wanting , or maybe even needing , often for sexual acts , although it is also commonly used to talk of a `` lust for life '' or a `` lust for power '' or for other goals . as a sexual desire , it is considered a sin ( a failing ) by christianity , which lists it as one of the seven deadly sins . `` lust '' was originally an anglo-saxon word which meant `` desire '' or `` appetite '' .
