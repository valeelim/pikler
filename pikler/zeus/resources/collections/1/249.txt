Everything2     everything2 or e2 is a website . it lets people make pages about many different things , and some people use it as a diary . e2 users create pages called `` nodes '' and add stuff in `` writeups '' . only logged-in users can create writeups . only the person who created the writeup or someone who the website owners ( called `` gods '' ) choose can edit the writeup . on the other hand , on wikipedia , anyone can edit pages , but on everything2 only those who can edit the writeup can edit pages . everything2 does not require a like wikipedia does . so , it is possible to have more than one article ( writeups ) under the same title ( node ) , each by different authors , and presenting different points of view . other websites . - everything2 website - everything2.com article about wikipedia
