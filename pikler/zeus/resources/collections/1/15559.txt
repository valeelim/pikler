Dead Sea     the dead sea is a lake between the west bank and jordan . it is below sea level . it is the lowest point on the surface of the earth . the dead sea is almost nine times as salty as the ocean . that makes it impossible for most life to exist in it . this is the reason for its name . however , it is not completely dead , because some types of bacteria are able to live in the water . because the water is so salty , it weighs more than fresh water . that lets people float in the dead sea without any effort . tourists come from around the world to float in the water .
