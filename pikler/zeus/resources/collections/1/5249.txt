Aphrodite     aphrodite ( greek 'risen from sea-foam ' ) is the goddess of love and beauty in greek mythology , and one of the twelve olympians . many artists chose to portray her as a naked woman , or with very little clothing . the major theory of aphrodite 's birth was that she was the daughter of zeus and dione . another theory states that aphrodite was born from sea-foam near paphos , cyprus . she appeared from the sea . eros , phobos , deimos , harmonia , pothos , anteros , himeros , hermaphroditos , rhode , eryx , peitho , tyche , eunomia , the graces , priapus , aeneas and chloe aphrodite was very beautiful , which made hera afraid that she would be the cause of fights between the other gods . she therefore gave aphrodite to hephaestus . hephaestus was happy to be married to aphrodite and gave her many pieces of jewelry which were gifts of love , like a belt that when ever she wore it made men would be attracted to her . aphrodite , however , she was not attracted to him . so she spent most of her time with ares , but was also spent time with adonis and anchises . she got married multiple times and had many children .
