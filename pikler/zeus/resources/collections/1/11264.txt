Hobart     hobart is a city in australia . it is the capital city of the state of tasmania , and it has about 200,000 people . it is on the western shore of the derwent river , although some of its suburbs are on the eastern shore . just west of hobart is mount wellington . hobart was founded in 1803 as a penal colony . today it is a busy seaport , and is the home port of antarctic expeditions . tourist attractions include salamanca markets , the cadbury chocolate factory and the cascade brewery . hobart has an oceanic climate ( `` cfb '' in the koeppen climate classification ) .
