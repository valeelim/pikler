Utsjoki     utsjoki is a municipality in lapland , finland . utsjoki is the only municipality in finland where most people speak sami as their first language . as of february 2005 , 1,373 people lived in utsjoki . the municipalities next to it are inari in finland and karasjok , tana and nesseby in norway . nuorgam in utsjoki is the northernmost village in finland and the european union . in the northern sami language , the municipality is known as `` ohcejohka '' .
