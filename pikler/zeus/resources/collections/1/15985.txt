Ron Weasley     ronald bilius `` ron '' weasley is a character from the `` harry potter '' stories . he is a red haired child , who comes from a poor background . he wishes he had money . he comes from a big family , with five brothers and one sister . he is harry potter 's best friend . it is revealed in later books , that ron is romantically interested in hermione granger . the two are hinted to be romantically involved in the deathly hallows .
