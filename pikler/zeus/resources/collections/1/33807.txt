Bartholin's gland    bartholin 's gland the bartholin 's glands are two glands found slightly below and to the left and right of the opening of the vagina in women . they were first found in the 17th century , by the danish anatomist , caspar bartholin the younger ( 1655-1738 ) . the glands mucus to make sexual intercourse easier . bartholin 's glands secrete relatively minute amounts ( one or two drops ) of fluid when a woman is sexually aroused . sometimes , the bartholin 's glands become infected and can be swollen or painful . this can be treated by a doctor with antibiotics . related pages . - skene 's glands other websites . - - `` the female perineum : muscles of the superficial perineal pouch ''
