The Turtles     the turtles were a 1960s pop group from los angeles , california . their best known song is `` happy together '' . chip douglas was a band member , who left to produce the monkees , then returned as the turtles producer . after the turtles broke up in the 1970s , the group 's two frontmen , howard kaylan and mark volman , sang with frank zappa as flo and eddie .
