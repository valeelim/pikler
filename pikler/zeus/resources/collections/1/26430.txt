Pipe     a pipe can be several things : - it could be a pole ( made of metal or of another material ) that is hollow in the middle . this kind of pipe is used for moving water or other things from one place to another . in pipe organs the sound is made by air travelling through metal or wooden pipes . - a pipe could also be a small object for smoking tobacco or other things . this sort of pipe has the same sort of hollow pole at one end , but it is much smaller and usually made of clay or wood . it also has a small bowl attached to the other end for burning tobacco in .
