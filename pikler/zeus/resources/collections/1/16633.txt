Convair B-36 Peacemaker     the b-36 peacemaker was a strategic bomber . it was used in the united states . the b-36 could carry a 21,000 pound bomb . the b-36 was never used in wartime , but was in a movie called strategic air command . the peacemaker could fly for 48 hours non-stop and was one of the longest ranged bombers at the time . it first flew in 1948 , and the last combat flight was in 1959 .
