Odysseus     odysseus was a person in the greek mythology . he was the king of the island ithaca , and was married to penelope . odysseus and penelope had a son called telemachos . odysseus is a major character in homer 's `` iliad '' and odyssey . odysseus fought in the trojan war , and invented the trojan horse , which helped the greeks win the war . after the war , his adventurous journey home took the time of 10 years . the story of that journey is told in the `` odyssey '' . the latin name for odysseus is `` ulysses '' .
