Valencia (autonomous community)    valencia ( autonomous community ) valencia , officially called valencian community ( unofficially : valencian country , valencia region , region of valencia , land of valencia ; and historically : kingdom of valencia ) , is a spanish autonomous community , in the east of the country . the population is 5,000,000 and the capital and biggest city is valencia , with near 800,000. valencia is also the capital of the valencia province . the second-largest city is alicante , with 332,000 , capital of the alicante province . the city of elche in alicante , with 230,000 , is a very important industrial centre . castellón , with 180,000 , is the capital of the castellón province .
