Side-scrolling video game     a side-scrolling video game or 2d game is a video game where the player goes toward one side of the screen , either to the left or the right , although this is different for each video game . a famous side-scroller is super mario bros. the character usually moves from left to right but sometimes it is the other way around . in the golden age of video games , side scrollers were a big thing and they still are to this do .
