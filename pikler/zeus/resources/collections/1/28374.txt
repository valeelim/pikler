Flip-flop (electronics)    flip-flop ( electronics ) a flip-flop is a special kind of electronic switch . it 's a logic element , and can hold one bit of binary memory ( either on or off , 1 or 0.. ) . more of these allow the storage of large amounts of information . there are many types of flip-flop electrical circuits such as d-flip flop , latch , etc . the first electronic flip-flop was invented by william eccles and f. w. jordan in 1918. early flip-flops were called trigger circuits .
