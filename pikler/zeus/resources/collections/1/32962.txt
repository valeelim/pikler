Bertolt Brecht     brecht went to school in augsburg , where his father was the director of a paper factory . he completed his degree in 1917. afterwards he studied sciences , medicine and literature at the ludwig-maximilians-university in munich . he had to take a break in his studies because he had to join the army . in the 1920s , brecht went to berlin and became a part of the cultural scene . he met his second wife helene weigel in berlin and married her , after divorcing his first wife . it was with his second wife that brecht had another son . in berlin he met a lot of artists and intellectuals of the weimar republic . his works became very critical about the capitalistic society ( e.g . `` aufstieg und fall der stadt mahagonny '' ( 1930 ) ) . many of his friends were communists , but he never joined the kpd . his most famous work was from that time , the `` dreigroschenoper '' . in 1933 the nazis prohibited playing some of his works and arrested some of his friends . after the reichstag fire he travelled from germany to prague , vienna , switzerland and denmark .
