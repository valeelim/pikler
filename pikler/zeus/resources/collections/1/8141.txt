Uno (card game)    uno ( card game ) uno is an american card game that was made by merle robbins in 1971. it has since been bought by a company named mattel . it uses 108 special cards that are made just to play `` uno '' . it is similar to crazy eights . the cards are put into 4 different groups : red cards , green cards , blue cards and yellow cards . there are also some other cards called `` special cards '' . skip , reverse , draw +2 , wild , and wild +4 cards allow you to do something you can not normally do , such as pick up two more cards . the game 's name is also sometimes written in capital letters - `` uno '' .
