Tricia Nixon Cox     patricia `` tricia '' nixon cox was born on february 21 , 1946 in whittier , california . she is the daughter of former us president richard nixon and pat nixon . she is the older sister of julie nixon eisenhower . unlike her sister , tricia performed many ceremonial jobs , like going with her father to campaign stops and state visits . she married edward f. cox on june 12 , 1971. she tutored inner-city children .
