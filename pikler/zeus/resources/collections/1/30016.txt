Coonskin cap     a coonskin cap is a hat made from the fur of a raccoon . usually , the tail of the racoon is attached to the back of the hat . the coonskin cap is a symbol of the early united states frontier . coonskin caps were first worn by the native americans who lived around tennessee and kentucky . the american settlers who moved to this area liked the caps and wore them themselves . some famous american pioneers who wore coonskin caps are davy crockett and daniel boone . http : //www.coonskincap.com/
