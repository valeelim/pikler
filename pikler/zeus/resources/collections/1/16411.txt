Chester     chester is a city in the north-west of england . it is almost 2000 years old and was started by romans . a person from chester is called a cestrian . chester is on the river dee , and is the county town of the county of cheshire . it is near to the country wales . almost 120,000 people live in chester . it is just south of liverpool . near to chester is chester zoo , which is the biggest zoo in the united kingdom . there are other interesting places in chester , and many are connected to its history . for example , there is the old roman amphitheatre . this is the biggest in the uk . the rows are unusual shops . in the centre of the town , the main streets have two rows of shops , one on top of the other . the rows have been part of chester since the middle ages . related pages . - chester city walls
