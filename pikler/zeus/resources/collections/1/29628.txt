Upper Franconia Government Region     upper franconia ( `` oberfranken '' ) is a `` regierungsbezirk '' ( administrative region ) of the german state bavaria . it has an area of 7,230 km² and about 1,200,000 people . it borders the german states of sachsen and thuringia , and also the czech republic . bayreuth is its capital . cities and districts . cities which are urban districts . 1. coburg , 1. bamberg , 1. hof , 1. bayreuth . smaller cities . 1. forchheim , 1. kulmbach , 1. lichtenfels , 1. marktredwitz , 1. neustadt bei coburg 1. kronach , 1. rödental , 1. wunsiedel , 1. selb , 1. münchberg , 1. helmbrechts , 1. rehau , 1. stadtsteinach , 1. bad staffelstein , 1. ebermannstadt , 1. burgkunstadt districts . 1. coburg rural district 1. bamberg rural district 1. forchheim rural district 1. bayreuth rural district 1. lichtenfels rural district 1. kronach rural district 1. hof rural district 1. kulmbach rural district 1. wunsiedel i. fichtelgebirge rural district
