Eros     eros is the god of love , lust , and sex in greek mythology . according to hesiod 's `` theogony '' , eros came from the chaos . later it was said that he was the son of ares and aphrodite . his lover is psyche . eros has a bow and golden arrows with whom he can shoot at people so they will fall in love , but he has also leaden arrows which make people hate . there is a famous statue which many people think is to eros , but is actually to anteros in london in piccadilly circus . from the name eros we get the word erotic , meaning : to do with sexual love ( passionate and physical ) . there is also a noun : eroticism . related pages . - cupid , the version of eros in roman mythology
