Black Forest     the black forest ( german : schwarzwald ) is a world famous forest in baden-württemberg , germany . important towns are ( from north to south ) : pforzheim , calw , baden-baden , offenburg , freudenstadt , horb , villingen-schwenningen , titisee-neustadt and waldshut-tiengen . the highest mountain is the feldberg ( 1492m ) . the black forest is quite popular for hiking , skiing and nordic walking . in triberg , at the lake titisee and in other places you can buy the famous cuckoo clocks . a good way to get known to the black forest is to travel on the `` deutsche uhrenstraße , '' a `` national scenic byway .
