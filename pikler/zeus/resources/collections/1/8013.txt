Will Smith     willard carroll smith , jr. ( born september 25 , 1968 ) is an american actor and rapper from philadelphia , pennsylvania . he got his start as part of the rap duo dj jazzy jeff and the fresh prince . he became an actor when he starred on the television show `` the fresh prince of bel-air '' as will smith , a teenager from philadelphia sent to live with his rich relatives in southern california . he has appeared in many movies including `` independence day '' , `` men in black '' , `` ali '' , `` wild wild west '' , `` the pursuit of happyness '' , `` i am legend '' , and in `` suicide squad '' . `` miami '' is a popular song by will smith . smith married actress jada pinkett in 1997. they have had two children jaden and willow . discography . - studio albums - `` big willie style '' ( 2017 ) - `` willennium '' ( 1999 ) - `` born to reign '' ( 2002 ) - `` lost and found '' ( 2005 ) - `` tba '' ( 2017 ) other websites . - official site
