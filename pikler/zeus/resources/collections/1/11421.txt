Gary Burghoff     gary burghoff ( born may 24 , 1943 ) is an american actor , artist , and musician . he is probably best known for his role as walter `` radar '' o'reilly , a company clerk that had extraordinary hearing , in the 1970 movie `` m*a*s*h '' and on the television series of the same name . burghoff also has a deformed hand which was hidden on the show by special camera tricks and he held it behind his back a lot .
