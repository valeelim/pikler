Astana     astana ( kazakh : астана , persian : آستانه ) is the capital city of kazakhstan . it has a population of 600,000 ( 2004 ) , has been the capital of kazakhstan from 1998. the name `` astana '' , which in kazakh language means `` capital city '' , was chosen because it is easily pronounced in many languages . in kazakh , it is pronounced `` as-ta-na '' , while in english ( and , for instance , german ) , common pronunciation is `` as-ta-na '' . it is within the aqmola province .
