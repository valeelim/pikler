Nepal Bhasa     nepal bhasa is a language . newah bhaaye is the term used for nepal bhasa by native speakers . it is a himalayan language of tibeto-burman branch of the sino-tibetan group . it has been incorrectly called by the term ‘ newari ’ by westerners and non-newars of nepal . it is not related to nepali , the majority language of nepal . nepal bhasha shares the feature of kirant and tibetan dialects of northern himalayas . it consists of five major dialects and several sub-dialects spoken by newa people living throughout the country .
