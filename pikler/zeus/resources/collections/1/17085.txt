Pumpkinhead II     pumpkinhead ii : blood wings is a 1994 movie about a young boy named tommy , a misfit and deformed teenager who had no friends . one day , tommy is killed by a gang of bad boys from his home town for no reason at all . miss ossie was an old lady who used to take care of tommy from the very beginning . when the young boy is killed , she decides to bury him in her backyard as a way to keep him around anyway . several years later in the 1990s , a bunch of teenagers go to miss ossie 's house and they do something that brings tommy back to life . some kind of spelling that resurrect the deformed kid . for some reason , tommy comes back as an angry and even more grotesque creature who wants to take revenge on the people who killed him in the past and innocent people as well .
