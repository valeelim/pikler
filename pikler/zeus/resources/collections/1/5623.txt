River Alt     the river alt is a river in the county of merseyside in england just north of liverpool . the river begins near huyton and flows about 28 km northeast to empty into the irish sea at the town of hightown . other towns along the river include kirkby , maghull and formby . there are 75 km of tributary feeding the river . about 250,000 people live in the area around the river . the northern part of the river forms the boundary between the counties of merseyside and lancashire .
