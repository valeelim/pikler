Nymph     a nymph is a kind of female nature entity/spirit in greek mythology . they are often companions of greek gods and goddesses . nymphs can be divided into different kinds , such as : - dryads ( trees ) - naiads ( fresh water ) - oreads ( mountains ) - the okeanids , the daughters of okeanos , and the nereids , the daughters of nereus ( seas ) - the pleiades ( stars )
