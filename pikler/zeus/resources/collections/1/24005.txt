Ben Bradshaw     benjamin peter james bradshaw ( born 30 august 1960 ) is an english politician and the member of parliament for the exeter constituency in the united kingdom . he was elected in the 1997 general election . he was a minister of state in the department of health , a minister for the south west and secretary of state for culture , media and sport . he holds no posts in the current shadow government . he is a member of the labour party .
