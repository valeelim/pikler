Missouri     missouri is one of the 50 states in the united states . its capital is jefferson city . its largest cities are kansas city and saint louis . some other cities are columbia ( which is where the university of missouri is ) , and springfield . missouri officially became a state on august 10 , 1821. missouri 's edges touch a total of eight states . it is touched on the north by iowa ; on the east , across the mississippi river , by illinois , kentucky , and tennessee ; on the south by arkansas ; and on the west by oklahoma , kansas , and nebraska ( kansas and nebraska are across the missouri river ) . related pages . - list of counties in missouri other websites . - . - . - . - u.s. census bureau - . - . - .
