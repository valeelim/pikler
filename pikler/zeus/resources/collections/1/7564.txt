Val Kilmer     val kilmer ( born december 31 , 1959 in los angeles , california ) is an american actor , best known for his roles in `` tombstone '' , `` the doors '' , `` heat '' and `` batman forever '' . he was married to joanne whalley from 1988 until they divorced in 1996. the couple had a daughter and son . in january 2015 , kilmer was hospitalized for massive bleeding in his throat . it was said by one source that kilmer has a throat tumor and is refusing to have treatment due to his religion . the same report says kilmer denies that he has a tumor ; calling it `` silly talk '' . other websites . - kilmer 's official website
