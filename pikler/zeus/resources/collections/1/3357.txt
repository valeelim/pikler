Minnesota River     the minnesota river is a river in the united states . it is in the northern united states , in the state of minnesota . it is a tributary of the mississippi river . it is about 534 km long . the source of the river is a lake in western minnesota . the lake is called big stone lake . the lake is on the border between the state of minnesota and the state of south dakota . from its source , the minnesota flows southeast . at mankato , it turns northeast . it joins the mississippi just south of the cities of saint paul and minneapolis . the minnesota river was formed after the last ice age in north america .
