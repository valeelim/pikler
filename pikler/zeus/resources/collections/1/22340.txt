General election     a general election is an election in which all or most members of a given political body are chosen . the term is usually used to mean the elections held for a nation 's primary legislative body . this is different from primary elections , by-elections or local elections . in the united kingdom when parliament is dissolved every seat in the house of commons becomes vacant and a general election is held . list of general elections . - united kingdom general election , 2001 - united kingdom 2005 general election - united kingdom general election 2015 - united kingdom general election , 2017
