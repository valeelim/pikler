Hector     hector is a person in greek mythology . he is an important person in the trojan war , and homer 's `` iliad '' . hector was the favourite son of king priam of troy and his wife hecuba . he was married to andromache , and had a son named astyanax . in the trojan war he was the leader of the trojans in battle and their hero . hector was killed by achilles . also he was wounded badly by ajax a greek hero because ajax threw a rock at him .
