Display resolution     resolution is what can qualify a monitor in computer science . it is the number of pixels displayed on a given surface . for example : a `` 640 by 480 display '' , which has 640 pixels from side to side and 480 from top to bottom ( as in a vga display ) , and therefore has a total number of 640 × 480 = 307,200 pixels or 0.3 megapixels , is what is meant by 640 × 480 display resolution . related pages . - pixels per inch - frequency
