Harvest     harvest means to collect what has been planted and grown in the ground . it is usually done by farmers , and in the fall season . this is also called picking crops . crop failure ( also known as harvest failure ) is a loss or reduced crop yield due to plants being damaged , killed , or destroyed . sometimes it brings famine . `` harvest '' can also mean to collect other things than plants such as cultivated bivalves including oysters , scallops , and other animals .
