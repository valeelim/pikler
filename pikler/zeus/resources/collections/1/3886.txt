Poetry     poetry is a type of art form and a type of literature . poetry uses the qualities of words , in different ways , to be artistic . - different length and sound ( of words ) ( poetry can be as short as a few words , or as long as a book ( an epic ) . - meanings ( of words ) - ( poetry has ) different uses of tone . there are many `` poetic forms '' ( forms of poetry ) . some of them are : sonnet , haiku , ballad , stev , ode , free verse , blank verse , thematic , limerick and nursery rhymes . poetry can be used to describe ( comparing , talking about , or expressing emotion ) many things . it can make sense or be nonsense , it can rhyme or not . it can have many shapes and sizes ; it can be serious or funny . `` to say something poetically '' means to give information in an artistic way . related pages . - wilfred owen - william shakespeare - william wordsworth - john donne - homer - dante - john keats - shelley - william blake - william butler yeats - rabindranath tagore
