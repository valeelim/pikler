Physical anthropology     physical anthropology deals mainly with looking at how the anatomy of the human body has changed over the course of time . it compares the human body to that of other primates . studies of physical anthropology often include looking at the evolution of mankind . some areas of physical anthropology are paleoanthropology and primatology . when physical anthropology is joined to other branches of biology it becomes biological anthropology . biological anthropology puts together information from the fossil record , the human skeleton , genetics , our primate relatives , human adaptations and behaviour .
