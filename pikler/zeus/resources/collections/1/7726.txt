Andes     the andes are a mountain range along the western coast of south america . they stretch over 7,000 km / 4,400 miles from the south of argentina and chile to the north of colombia . they are also found in peru , bolivia and ecuador . the amazon river system has its sources in the eastern flanks of the andes . the andes are the longest exposed mountain range of the world , and the second-highest after the himalayas . the andes mountain range is the highest mountain range outside asia . aconcagua , the highest peak , rises to 6,962 m ( 22,841 ft ) above sea level . the top of mount chimborazo in the ecuadorean andes is the point on the earth 's surface most distant from its center . mount chimborazo is an inactive volcano in ecuador , which last erupted over a thousand years ago . the andes has three sections : 1. the southern andes in argentina and chile ; 1. the central andes , having the chilean and peruvian mountain system and parts of bolivia ; 1. the northern part in venezuela , colombia , and ecuador .
