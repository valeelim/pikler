Leech     leeches are annelids , a kind of worm ( ground creature ) that lives in wet places . all leeches are carnivorous , that is , they eat mainly meat , but some are also haemophagic ; this means they drink the blood of other animals . they are sometimes helpful in medicine . they are often used to reduce blood clots . they were used to suck the blood of patients a long time ago , before we knew about germs and that losing blood is bad . references . - sawyer , roy t. 1986. leech biology and behaviour . vol 1-2. clarendon press , oxford
