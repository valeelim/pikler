MAD Magazine     mad magazine is a humor and satire magazine that started in 1952 as a 10 cent comic book parody of other comics . for its 25th issue it converted to a 25 cent magazine . it remains popular in the united states . each issue features the grinning red-headed mascot , alfred e. neuman on the cover , and comic-like articles making fun of recent movies , television series , music , trends , etc . as well as regular monthly features ( spy vs. spy , monore , etc . )
