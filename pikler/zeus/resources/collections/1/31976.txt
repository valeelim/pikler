Livestock     livestock are domestic animals that are kept by people for purposes including meat , leather , wool or labor . taking care of livestock is animal husbandry . some types of livestock are : cattle , pigs , sheep , goats , and chickens . chickens produce eggs , pigs produce ham , bulls make leather for their pelts , goats and cows create milk , and sheep create wool . horses and other large animals do work for people , such as pulling wagons and other vehicles .
