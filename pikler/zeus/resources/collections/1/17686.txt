Fort Knox     fort knox is an u.s. army post in kentucky . named after henry knox , the fort is now the site of the u.s. bullion reservatory ( where the united states keeps its gold ) . the fort 's fortifications were built in 1862 during the american civil war ; however , after the war the camp became abandoned until 1917. it is named for henry knox , a general in the american revolutionary war . the james bond book and film `` goldfinger '' is about somebody trying to blow up fort knox
