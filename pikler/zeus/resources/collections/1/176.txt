Capitalization     capitalise ( british spelling ) or capitalize ( north american spelling ) means to make one or more letters upper case . the first letter of a sentence is capitalised in many languages , as are the first letters of proper nouns such as names of people and places . in german , however , all nouns are capitalized . it is the only language to do that . the homonym capitalize is a different word , and means `` to fully fund as an investment '' .
