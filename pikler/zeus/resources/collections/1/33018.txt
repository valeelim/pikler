Harry Nilsson     harry edward nilsson iii ( june 15 , 1941 - january 15 , 1994 ) was an american singer-songwriter . nilsson was born in bushwick , brooklyn , new york city . his paternal grandparents were swedish . his biggest hits were during the late 1960s and the 1970s . his songs include `` me and my arrow '' , `` coconut '' , `` cuddly toy '' , `` spaceman '' , and `` one '' . `` one '' was also a hit for the band three dog night . nilsson 's two most famous songs were `` without you '' and `` everybody 's talkin ' '' . these songs were written by other people . `` without you '' has been re-recorded many times by other singers . it was written by peter ham and tom evans of badfinger . `` everybody 's talkin ' '' was used in the 1969 movie `` midnight cowboy '' . the song was written by fred neil . nilsson recorded a tribute to the beatles on his first album . he used lines from fifteen of their songs in one cover version of `` you ca n't do that '' . derek taylor , who worked for the beatles , sent them copies of the album , and nilsson became a friend of the band . he later shared a house with john lennon and ringo starr . they worked together on each other 's music .
