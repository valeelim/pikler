Abkhazia     abkhazia ( /apkhazeti ) is a partially recognized republic in the caucasus . as of 2009 , about 215,000 people lived there . the capital is sukhumi . most countries consider abkhazia to be part of georgia . it is recognized only by russia , nauru , nicaragua and venezuela . abkhazia covers an area of about at the northwest part of greater caucasus . the caucasus mountains to the north and the northeast divide abkhazia from the russian federation . other websites . - president of the republic of abkhazia . official site ( en , ru , ab , tr )
