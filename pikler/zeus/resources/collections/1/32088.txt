Bishkek     bishkek ( бишкек ) is the capital city of kyrgyzstan . it has a population of 900,000 ( 2005 ) . originally founded in 1878 as the russian fortress of pishpek ( пишпек ) , between 1926 and 1991 it was known as frunze ( фрунзе ) , after the bolshevik military leader mikhail frunze . in kyrgyz , a bishkek is a churn used to make stirred horse milk ( kumis ) , the kyrgyz national drink .
