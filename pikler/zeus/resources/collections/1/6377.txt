Moctezuma II     moctezuma ii , also known as motecuhzoma xocoyotzin ( 1466-1520 ) and often called montezuma in english , was the aztec emperor ( `` tlahtoani '' ) from 1502 to 1520. his name comes from the nahuatl `` motecuhzoma '' , it means `` he who is leader because of his fury '' . in spanish , he is called moctezuma . when hernán cortés arrived in mexico in 1519 , moctezuma thought he was the god quetzalcoatl , and did not want to fight against him . cortés took moctezuma prisoner . when the aztecs wanted cortés to get out of their capital tenochtitlan in 1520 , cortes told moctezuma to calm down his people . when doing that he died . the spaniards said he was killed by the aztecs , while the aztecs said cortés killed him . the spanish said that the aztec people threw rocks at him , killing him . the aztecs said that they found his body in the streets with a lot of cuts made with a sword after the spanish left . other websites . - a reconstructed portrait of motecuhzoma xocoyotzin , based on historical sources , in a contemporary style .
