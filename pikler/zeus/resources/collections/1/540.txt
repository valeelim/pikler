Internet slang     internet slang is slang words which are used on the internet . most of these words are new , such as wiki and blog , which were not used before the internet became popular . some old words were given new meanings , such as mail ( which now means email ) . there are many mixed metaphors on the internet , such as boot ( which used to mean a shoe ) and link ( which used to mean a join in a metal chain ) . some existing acronyms , such as `` afk '' ( to mean `` away from keyboard '' ) and `` irl '' ( to mean `` in real life '' ) were used on bulletin board systems before the internet became popular . these are now used on the internet , such as in chat rooms and on instant messenger software . people have also created some verbs and adjectives to describe things that only happen on the internet : - to flame - to send offensive messages , usually in chat rooms , forums , instant messaging software or email . - to ping - to send some data to another computer and see how long it takes for the data to come back . shorthand is where a word is written in a shorter way because it is quicker and easier to type . it is also done to fit more text into a limited space .
