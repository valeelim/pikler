Saint Lawrence River     the saint lawrence river ( ; tuscarora : `` kahnawáʼkye '' ; mohawk : `` kaniatarowanenneh '' , meaning `` big waterway '' ) is a big river in eastern north america . it flows between the canadian province of ontario and the american state of new york , and through the canadian province of quebec . the river drains water from the great lakes into the atlantic ocean . it is more than three thousand kilometres long . the river meets the atlantic ocean in a big `` estuary '' or bay , the biggest in the world ; this is called the gulf of saint lawrence . the canadian cities of kingston , montreal , trois-rivières and quebec city are on this river . it is very important because ships can sail up the river and through the great lakes right into the middle of north america . it is the third largest river in canada . other websites . - regional geography of the st. lawrence river - great lakes st. lawrence seaway system - safe passage : aids to navigation on the st. lawrence – historical essay , illustrated with drawings and photographs
