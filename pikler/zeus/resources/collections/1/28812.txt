Jan Ladislav Dussek     jan ladislav dussek ( b. ĉáslav , bohemia , 12 feb.1760 ; d.saint-germain-en-laye , 20 march 1812 ) was a bohemian composer of the classical period . he travelled a lot , living in holland , lithuania , italy , france and england . he lived nearly twelve years in london where he was a fashionable teacher and famous composer . he is best remembered now for his piano music .
