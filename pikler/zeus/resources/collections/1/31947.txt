Baroque pop     baroque pop is a rock music subgenre that mixes both rock and classical music . it was created by musicians in the 1960s by adding classical instruments like harpsichord , french horns , oboes , and string sections . it became popular with both teenagers and adults at the time . examples can be found on the beach boys ' `` pet sounds '' and recordings made by phil spector . it is also referred to as chamber pop , among other names . some baroque pop musicians : - antony and the johnsons - fiona apple - arcade fire - andrew bird - badly drawn boy - belle and sebastian - broken social scene - coldplay - the decemberists - the divine comedy - iron & wine - kings of convenience - the left banke - sondre lerche - sufjan stevens - emitt rhodes - elliott smith - vampire weekend - the velvet teen - rufus wainwright
