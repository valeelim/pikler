Transformers (toy line)    transformers ( toy line ) transformers are robots that turn into other things . usually they turn into vehicles , like cars or jets . transformers started in 1984 as a toy line , but then an animated series and a comic book were made . in 1986 a movie came out . in 2002 a new series and story line called transformers armada came out . this was followed by two more shows , energon and cybertron . transformers is about different things sometimes . usually it is about the heroic autobots fighting the evil decepticons . the autobots are usually led by optimus prime . the decepticons are led by megatron .
