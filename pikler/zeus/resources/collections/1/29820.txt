Bharata     bharata has several meanings . some of the meanings are given below : - a king of ancient india named bharat . ancient name of india was bharatavarsh , the country of bharata . - bharata was the name of one of the brothers of rama , a god of the hindus . - a writer named bharata wrote a book long back . the book is named `` natyashastra '' , that is , the book of dramas . - bharata is another name for republic of india . this has been noted in the constitution of india .
