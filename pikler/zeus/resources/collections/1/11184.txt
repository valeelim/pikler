Hank Azaria     hank azaria ( born april 25 , 1964 ) is an american actor , voice actor , comedian and producer . he is best known for his roles as `` moe , apu , krusty the klown , chief wiggum '' , and many other characters on the fox animated television series `` the simpsons '' . he also appeared in movies such as `` the birdcage '' ( 1996 ) , and on stage as sir lancelot in the monty python-inspired musical , `` spamalot '' . he was also once married to actress helen hunt from 1999 until there divorce in 2000 , he is currently married to katie wright . he recently has appeared in showtime 's original series , `` huff '' . other websites . - fresh air interview with terry gross - hank azaria 1999 interview on `` sidewalks entertainment ''
