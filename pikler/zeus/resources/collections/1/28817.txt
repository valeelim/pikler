Frederick Delius     frederick delius ( b.bradford , 29 jan.1862 ; d.grez-sur-loing , 10 june 1934 ) was an english composer . at first his parents did not let him study music , so he went off to florida to work on an orange plantation . he later returned , studied music , and settled in france for the rest of his life . his music is often quite gentle and atmospheric , with chords that shift about in unusual ways . one of his best known orchestral works is `` on hearing the first cuckoo in spring '' . in his last years he was very ill , but he still wanted to compose , so he dictated the music he heard in his head to a man called eric fenby .
