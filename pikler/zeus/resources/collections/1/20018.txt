1470s     the 1470s was the decade that started on january 1 , 1470 and ended on december 31 , 1479. events . - 1470 july 12 – the ottomans capture euboea . - 1471 march – the yorkist king edward iv returns to england to reclaim his throne . - 1472 – foundation of the kingdom of fez . - 1474 february – the treaty of utrecht ends the anglo-hanseatic war . - 1476 – battle of avenches - 1477 february 27 – uppsala university is founded . - 1478 november 1 – start of the spanish inquisition . - 1479 january 20 – ferdinand ii takes the throne of aragon . he rules together with his wife isabella i , queen of castile .
