B.J. Thomas    b.j . thomas billy joe `` bj '' thomas ( born august 7 , 1942 ) is a country singer . his most famous songs are `` hooked upon a feeling '' and `` raindrops keep falling on my head '' , which was in the movie `` butch cassidy and the sundance kid '' . he sang throughout the 1970s . he changed to christian music in the 1980s .
