Agave     agaves are a type of succulent plant from mexico and the southwestern parts of the united states . they have thick , sharp leaves . agave plants flower only once , produce seeds and then die . before flowering they usually live for 10 to 25 years . because they live for a long time they are sometimes called `` century plants '' . agave is used to make things like tequila , agave syrup and pulque . they were an important food for many indigenous people in the southwestern united states , such as the navajo and the hokoham .
