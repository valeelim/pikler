Paris (mythology)    paris ( mythology ) paris , also called alexandros , is a person in greek mythology . he is an important person in the trojan war , and homer 's `` iliad '' . alexandros ( paris ) was the son of king priam of troy and his wife hecuba . because it was prophesied that he would bring the end and destruction of troy , he was left to die in the wilderness . but alexandros was found by another man , who took him as a son and named him paris . during a beauty contest among goddesses , aphrodite promised him helen , the most beautiful woman in the world , who was already married to menelaus . paris used a trick to take helen with him to troy , which was the reason for the trojan war . during the war paris killed achilles by shooting his heel with a poisoned arrow . late in the war , paris was killed by philoctetes .
