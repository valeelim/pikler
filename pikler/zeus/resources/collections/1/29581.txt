Ian Botham     sir ian terence botham ( born 24 november 1955 ) is an english retired cricketer . he also played football . he was an all-rounder , being good at both batting and bowling . he was also very good at catching the ball when he was fielding . he played over 100 test matches for england . he is a very popular man in england . one reason for this is that he helped england to beat australia in a series of test matches called `` the ashes '' in 1981. this series is sometimes called `` botham 's ashes '' . now he works on television . he has done some long walks to raise money for charity . he was knighted in 2007 .
