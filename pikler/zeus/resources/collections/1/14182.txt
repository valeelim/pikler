Millet     millet is a type of grain that is eaten . there are many types of millet . millets is fairly good nutrition . it includes three grams of protein per half serving and one gram of fiber per half serving . six-tenths of a miligram is the amount of iron in each half serving of millet . millets also have high fiber content , and poor digestibility of nutrients . this limits their value in nutrition and makes them rather less acceptable to consumers .
