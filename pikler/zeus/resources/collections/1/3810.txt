Luggage     people use luggage to carry their possessions when travelling . in the united states luggage is often called baggage . types of luggage . - a valise is a small bag . it is usually for carrying clothes or accessories . some other words for this size of luggage are `` overnight bag '' , `` overnight case '' , and `` overnighter '' - a suitcase is a larger bag , most often for carrying clothes - a trunk , also known as a travelling chest or steamer trunk , is a very large , rigid chest that was mostly used when moving across the ocean on a ship ; they are much less common in the 21st century
