Weapon of mass destruction     weapons of mass destruction are weapons that can cause a lot of damage to a large number of people . in general , people see the following as weapons of mass destruction : - chemical weapons , like poisonous gases ( e.g . nerve gases ) - biological weapons . those are usually germs , or other small living things that may cause a disease . e.g . smallpox or anthrax . - nuclear weapons related pages . - cbrne ( chemical , biological , radiological , nuclear , and explosive ) weapons
