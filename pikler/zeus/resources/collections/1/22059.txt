Gilbert and Ellice Islands     the gilbert and ellice islands were a british protectorate from 1892 and colony from 1916. they are formed from two groups of islands in the pacific ocean . on 1 january 1976 the islands were split into two different colonies that became independent states . the gilbert islands have been the biggest part of the nation of kiribati since 1979 , and the ellice islands became tuvalu in 1978 .
