Columbus Crew SC     columbus crew sc ( known before 2015 as simply `` columbus crew '' ) are an american soccer team that plays in major league soccer in columbus , ohio . they started play in 1996 , and have played in their own stadium , mapfre stadium ( known before 2015 as columbus crew stadium ) , since 1999. honors . - mls cup ( league championship ) : 1 ( 2008 ) - mls supporters ' shield ( best regular-season record ) : 3 ( 2004 , 2008 , 2009 ) - lamar hunt u.s. open cup : 1 ( 2002 )
