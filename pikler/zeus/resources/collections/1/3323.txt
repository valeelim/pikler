Power     power has different meanings when talking about different subjects : - power ( physics ) , how fast energy can be changed into work - in sociology , power is when a person wants another to do something , and they do it . this might also be called influence or control . - electric power , electricity that comes from power plants or generators - statistical power , how likely the test is to say something is true when it is really true - exponentiation ; in mathematics , `` power '' is multiplying a number by itself . in the expression 2 , where we multiply 3 2 's together ( 2 * 2 * 2 ) we say that 2 is raised to the power of 3 - a life force , called chi by the chinese and kiai by the japanese . - in politics a great power is more powerful than other countries . related pages . - power plant - suzerainty
