Mysore     mysore is a city in karnataka , india . mysore is south-west of bangalore . travel time from bangalore to mysore is 2-3 hours by road or rail . mysore palace , jaganmohan palace , chamundi hills , brindavan gardens , and mysore zoo are the places to see in mysore . some people go to mysore to see the dasara procession . people also go to mysore to learn yoga .
