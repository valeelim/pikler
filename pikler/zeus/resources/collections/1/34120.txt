Cable car     a cable car is a type of car that runs on cables above the car . the machines that drive the cables are in the two `` winding houses '' . one winding house is at the bottom and one at the top . it is used much in areas where there are a lot of mountains . the construction of the longest cable way in the world was finished in 16th october 2010 in armenia . it 's name is wings of tatev and has length of 5.7 km . some cable cars run on railway tracks , pulled by a cable . related pages . - gondola lift other websites . - a picture of a typical cable car
