John Paul Stevens     john paul stevens ( born april 20 , 1920 ) is a retired united states supreme court justice . he was nominated by president gerald ford in 1975. he voted with minority in bush v. gore case ( 2000 ) . he also voted with majority in furman v. georgia case ( 1976 ) , but later bacame a death penalty opponent . in 2010 , he announced his retirement in the supreme court .
