Head     the head is the part of the body where the brain is . it is also where the face is . different things may be worn on the head , for example a headband or a hat . some people get pains in their head occasionally , known as headaches . also , some people have worse pains in their head called migraines . a `` head '' in english can also mean a person in charge of something , such as the head of a company . also , the word `` head '' can also mean the front of something . an example of this is the word `` headline '' , meaning large words on the front page of a newspaper .
