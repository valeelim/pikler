Cape of Good Hope     the cape of good hope is a place near the far south end of africa . it is a well known area for ships that pass to the east in the sea between southern africa and antarctica . it is in the country of south africa . the first person from europe to see it was the portuguese man bartolomeu dias . he saw it in 1488 and named it the `` cape of storms '' . other websites . - an old map of cape of good hope from 1842
