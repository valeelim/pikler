Waterfall     a waterfall is a place where water rushes down a steep ledge . the water flows from higher land , then it falls down a big step of rock to lower land of softer rock where it will continue on its journey . usually the lower land is in a gorge . waterfalls are usually made when a river is young , in places where softer rock is underneath harder rock . many people choose them as sacred spots . the roar from a big waterfall is very loud . some people think it is beautiful music and that a waterfall is one of the most beautiful things in nature . the highest waterfall in the world is `` angel falls '' in venezuela where the water falls . related pages . - cataracts of the nile
