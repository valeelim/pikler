Gangnihessou     do-aklin or gangnihessou ( ganye hessou ) or dogbari was the first of twelve kings of dahomey in africa . gangnihessou might have been the king around the year 1620. one of his symbols was a bird called `` gangnihessou '' after his name . his other symbols were a drum , and sticks used for throwing or hunting . he had a younger brother called dakodonou , who was a king . some historians think gangnihessou was not actually a king . instead , gangnihessou might have led people by giving advice to his brother .
