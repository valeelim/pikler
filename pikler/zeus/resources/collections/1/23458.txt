Zen     zen is a japanese school of mahayana buddhism . buddhism was first brought to china from india , by bodhidharma . it developed in china , into zen , in the 6th century . from china it spread to vietnam , korea and japan . zen is a japanese word translated from the chinese word ch√°n , which means `` meditation '' . zen uses meditation to help practitioners go beyond simply thinking about zen . the goal in zen is to attain `` satori '' . this japanese word translates as `` enlightenment '' . the practice also includes using riddles , called koans . koans are also designed to help the practitioner not just engage in rational thought . the traditional japanese place to learn zen is a zen monastery . japanese zen scholars such as d.t . suzuki became well known in the west . suzuki spent over fifty years teaching zen to the world with a series of books in english.in the 1960s in california , aldous huxley , alan watts , and others promoted the philosophy . from there it spread to many parts of the western world . zen is now well established outside of japan and china . many zen centers exist in the us and europe .
