Baker Island     baker island is a small coral island , or atoll , in the north pacific ocean . it is just above the equator . it belongs to the united states . the united states department of the interior is in charge of the island . it is called the `` baker island national wildlife refuge '' . the island has no trees . the island is surrounded by a coral reef . this makes the atoll hard to approach in a boat . related pages . - list of uninhabited regions other websites . - baker island national wildlife refuge
