Image scanner     in computing or computer , an image scanner is a device used to transfer images or text into a computer . there are special models for scanning photo negatives , or to scan books . in the computer , the signal from the scanner is transferred to a digital image . this image can then be edited , printed , etc . all scanners have special parts which are used to take a picture from an object . charge-coupled device or contact image sensor parts take the light from the object and change it into a digital signal . the digital signal is then transferred to the computer memory or is read in the scanner 's processor . with some software it can be changed to a compressed digital image , like jpeg or png . text written by hand can be changed to ascii code by an ocr program.it is very important for computer the main types of scanner - `` desktop scanner '' ( flatbed ) this is probably the most popular type . an object to be scanned ( picture , image , text sheet ) is put on the glass . under the glass , there is a moving image sensor .
