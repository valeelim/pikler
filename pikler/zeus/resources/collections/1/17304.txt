Twin Peaks     twin peaks was an american television series that ran from 1990 to 1991. it told the story of an fbi agent who comes to the strange small town of twin peaks , washington , to investigate the murder of a teenage girl . it was made by david lynch . the show was successful around the world . in 1992 , a twin peaks movie was released . cast . - dana ashbrook - peggy lipton - joan chen - sam stanley
