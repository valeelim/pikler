Thomm     thomm is a village near trier ( at the moselle river ) in rhineland-palatinate in germany . thomm is famous for an old celtic stone , called `` hinkelstein '' near the village and a big celtic grave . in further times there was a slate minor industry near thomm . thomm is also famous for its cider , an apple-wine . the fia european hill climb championship came to thomm until 2011 and the fia world rally championship until 2015. other websites . - http : //thomm-online.de
