Pot Noodle     pot noodle is a type of cup noodle sold in the united kingdom . they contain noodles , soya pieces , vegetables , and seasoning powder with a packet of sauce that can be added to taste . pot noodles are available in several varieties : - beef and tomato - bombay bad boy - chicken and mushroom - chow mein - hot chicken curry - seedy sanchez ( mexican fajita flavour ) - southern fried chicken - spicy curry - sweet and sour - sweet 'n ' spicy - formally `` nice 'n ' spicy '' - the sizzler ( bacon flavour )
