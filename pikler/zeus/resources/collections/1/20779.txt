Salamanca     salamanca is a city in central spain . it is the capital city of the province with the same name . the city has a very important university started in 1218. it has two cathedrals and a famous plaza mayor ( main square ) built in the 18th century . frogs are a mascot of salamanca . souvenirs of frogs can be found in almost every shop in the plaza mayor . the battle of salamanca was an important defeat for france in the napoleonic wars . other websites . - official tourist information office - wiki of the city of salamanca - general information on salamanca - general information about events in salamanca - salamanca travel guide - salamanca : spain 's answer to oxford by `` the guardian '' - salamanca city guide at hitchhikers handbook museums - art nouveau and art dec√≥ museum casa lis - car history museum - cathedral museum
