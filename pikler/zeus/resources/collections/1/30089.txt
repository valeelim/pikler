Rainbow Brite     rainbow brite was an animated television show in the united states based on a series of hallmark cards . it involves a girl called `` rainbow brite '' and the `` color kids '' who are in charges of bringing color to the earth . it was produced by dic entertainment and syndicated in its `` kideo tv '' package . a tie-in movie , `` rainbow brite and the star stealer '' , came out in 1985 .
