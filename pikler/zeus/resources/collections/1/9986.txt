Fifth Avenue     fifth avenue is a large avenue in new york city ( u.s. ) . running from greenwich village in southern manhattan to harlem in the north , the avenue marks the line dividing manhattan into two parts , called the east side and the west side . the nearest avenues , on either side , are madison avenue ( east ) and sixth avenue ( west ) , also known as the avenue of the americas . there are many famous sights , such as the new york public library , st. patrick 's cathedral , rockefeller center and central park ( which runs along the west side , between 59th and 110th street ) . on fifth avenue there are many expensive shops like gucci . related pages . - madison avenue
