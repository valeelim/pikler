Fear Factory     fear factory is an american death/industrial metal group . they were formed in los angeles , california in 1989 and disbanded in early 2002. they have reformed in the following year adding byron stroud ( of strapping young lad ) as their current bass player . their classic line-up was burton c. bell ( vocals ) , dino cazares ( guitars ) , christian olde woblers ( bass ) and raymond herrera ( drums ) between 1994 and break up time .
