Pleiades (mythology)    pleiades ( mythology ) the pleiades are seven nymphs in greek mythology , and companions of artemis . they were the seven daughters of atlas and the nymph pleione . they were all born on mount cyllene , which is in arcadia . the pleiades . - maia , who was the eldest . she was the mother of hermes by zeus . - elektra , who was the mother of dardanus and iasion by zeus . - taygete , who was the mother of lacedaemon by zeus . - alkyone , who was the mother of hyrieus by poseidon . - kelaino , who was mother of lycus and eurypylus by poseidon . - asterope ( also called sterope ) , who was mother of oenomaus by ares . - merope , who was youngest , and married sisyphus .
