Mayor     the mayor is a person who is in charge of the administration of a city . some mayors are elected by the people who live in that city . for other cities , a central government body may choose the mayor . some large cities have a lord mayor . in a federal countries like germany the mayor can also head of the government of a city state . the mayor of hamburg runs the government just like the minister president of bavaria runs the bavarian government . in some big cities such as tokyo the mayor is a governor . in many countries , such as the united kingdom and australia , the mayor is not in charge of the daily functions of a city or town . that job is done by a town clerk or chief executive , who is appointed to a full-time paid job . the mayor works part-time , usually without payment , and represents the city at events and civic functions .
