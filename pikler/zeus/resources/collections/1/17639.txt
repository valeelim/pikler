George Newbold Lawrence     george newbold lawrence ( october 20 , 1806 - january 17 , 1895 ) was an american businessperson and amateur ornithologist ( bird expert ) . lawrence counted birds for spencer fullerton baird and john cassin , and the three men wrote `` birds of north america '' in 1858. lawrence left his 8,000 bird skins to the american museum of natural history . the lawrence 's goldfinch `` carduelis lawrencei '' was named for him by cassin .
