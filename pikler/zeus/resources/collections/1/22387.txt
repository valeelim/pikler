Lakshadweep     lakshadweep are a group of islands in the arabian sea . they belong to india . their capital is kavaratti . it covers an area of . eleven of the 36 coral atoll islands of lakshadweep are inhabited . the islands are the only coral atolls in india . the people living there are malayalam and mahl people , who have been influenced by arab traders . lakshadweep is a muslim region just like the neighboring maldives , where tourism is strictly regulated . apart from the emerging tourism , government jobs , fishing and coconut production are the mainstays of the lakshadweep economy .
