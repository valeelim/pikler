Thumb     the thumb is one of the five fingers on the human hand . thumbs are different from the other four fingers because they are in a different direction , have only 2 bones ( the others have 3 ) , and are shorter . thumbs allow us to pick things up . this is called having `` opposable thumbs '' . thumbs have an artery in them . arteries have a pulse . this is why we should not take other peoples ' pulses with our thumbs . related pages . - finger - toe - hand
