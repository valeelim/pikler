The Freecycle Network     the freecycle network ( tfn ) is a global network of people who share and regive items . they organise their network through the internet . freecycle is a place for people to meet . there are just two things people can do there . the first thing is this : a person can ask the other people for an item . if someone has it , they can tell the person who wants it . the second thing is this : a person can give away an item . sometimes many people want the item , and the donator has to choose just one person . other websites . - official website - freecycle group finder
