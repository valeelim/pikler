Door god     a door god is a painting pasted on a door . these were used by the chinese as good luck . door gods can be seen on temples - places of religion - and other buildings . there is a belief that door gods stop evil spirits from going into the building . door gods are often in pairs , one for each side of the door . the first door gods may have been made for a chinese emperor , more than 1300 years ago . they showed his two best soldiers .
