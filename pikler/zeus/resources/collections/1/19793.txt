Transition metal     the transition metals are a group of metals that are found in the middle of the periodic table . they all have similar properties . the alkaline earth metals are to the left and the group 3 elements , beginning with boron . transition metals have several general properties . they are harder and less reactive than the alkaline earth metals . they are also harder than the post transition metals . they make colorful chemical compounds with other elements . most of them have more than one oxidation state . some of the transition metals are necessary to keep humans healthy , such as iron , zinc , and chromium . other elements in the transition metals can be harmful to our body , like cadmium and mercury . other elements like gold or silver do not really harm or help us . there are more transition metals than any other groups in the periodic table . only a few of the transition metals are colored ; most of them are silver-gray or silver-bluish .
