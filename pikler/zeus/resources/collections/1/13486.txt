Supertramp     supertramp is a british rock band . they were created and managed by a dutch millionaire and were very popular in the 1970s . the members of supertramp included rick davies and roger hodgson , who wrote most of the songs . the band 's best-known songs include `` dreamer '' , `` give a little bit '' , `` the logical song '' , `` take the long way home '' , and `` it 's raining again '' . albums . - `` supertramp '' ( 1970 ) - `` indelibly stamped '' ( 1971 ) - `` crime of the century '' ( 1974 ) - `` crisis ? what crisis ?
