Solomon Islands     the solomon islands are a group of islands in the south pacific . they are known for their beauty and for the many languages spoken there . the capital of the solomon islands is honiara . in the 1890 's the islands became a protectorate of the british empire . empire of japan forces arrived in early 1942 and the united states marine corps attacked them , starting the guadalcanal campaign . the country is divided into nine provinces and the town of honiara . 1. central 1. choiseul 1. guadalcanal 1. isabel 1. makira-ulawa 1. malaita 1. rennell and bellona 1. temotu 1. western 1. honiara city
