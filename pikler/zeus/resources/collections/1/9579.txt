Espoo     espoo ( ) is the second largest city in uusimaa , finland . as of january 2014 , more than 260,000 people lived in espoo . other municipalities near to it are kauniainen , vantaa , kirkkonummi , nurmijärvi , vihti and the capital helsinki . there are several lakes in the northern parts of espoo . example lippajärvi , pitkäjärvi , nuuksion pitkäjärvi and velskolan pitkäjärvi . other websites . - official espoo site - travelling quide
