Stephen Breyer     stephen gerald breyer ( ; born august 15 , 1938 ) is an associate justice of the supreme court of the united states . he was appointed by president bill clinton in 1994. breyer is generally associated with the more liberal side of the court . breyer was born august 15 , 1938. breyer has been an associate justice since 1994. breyer generally favors a liberal interpretation of the law ; he is pro-choice and pro-civil liberties . breyer had the second-longest tenure as the most junior justice on the bench . breyer is also the only justice to appear on a quiz show ( `` wait wait do n't tell me '' ) . before being a judge , breyer was a professor at harvard law school and a judge on the first circuit court of appeals . breyer is an eagle scout .
