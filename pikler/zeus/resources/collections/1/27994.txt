Montgomery Ward     montgomery ward was an american department store chain . it started as a mail-order catalog ( called a `` wish book '' ) and department store in 1872. it owned jefferson ward discount stores . jefferson ward stores in the northern us were sold to bradlees in 1985. the rest closed in 1988. all its stores closed in 2000. in 2004 , it reopened as an online store . montgomery ward was founded by aaron montgomery ward , a chicago entrepreneur .
