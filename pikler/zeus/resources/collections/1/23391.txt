Francis Scott Key     francis scott key ( august 1 , 1779 - january 11 , 1843 ) was an american lawyer and poet . he is best known for writing `` the star-spangled banner '' , which is the national anthem of the united states of america , a country in the continent north america he went to college at st. john 's college in annapolis , maryland . his mother was ann phoebe penn dagworthy ( charlton ) and his father was captain john ross key . key was an owner of slaves , and in the later administration of andrew jackson he was the united states attorney in washington , d.c .
