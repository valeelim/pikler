Goose     goose ( plural geese ) is the general english name for a large number of long-necked birds , belonging to the family `` anatidae '' . this family also includes the swans , which are mostly larger than geese , and the ducks , which are smaller and have short necks . both as chicks and as adults , geese show collective animal behaviour . geese migrate in groups in the spring and fall , flying together in a v-shape . a male goose is called a `` gander '' and a baby goose is called a `` gosling '' . a group of geese is called a `` gaggle '' .
