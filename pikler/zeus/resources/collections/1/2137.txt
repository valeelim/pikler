Capital     capital can mean : - capital ( city ) ( also called capital city or political capital ) , a city or town that contains the government of a country , state , or other area - capital ( economics ) , how much real money a company or person has that they can use - capital ( architecture ) , the top part of a pillar or column - capital letter , another word for `` uppercase '' or majuscule - capital punishment , being put to death for doing a crime
