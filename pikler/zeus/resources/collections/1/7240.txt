Machine     a machine is a thing that is created by people to make work easier . it is a device or invention which multiplies the effect of human effort . the machine produces a mechanical advantage . some machines have many parts that move . examples are bicycles and clocks . some machines do not have parts that move . examples are computers and telephones . people have used machines since before there was history . often a machine will change one form of energy to another form of energy . simple machines increase or change the direction of a force . there are six 'simple machines ' . they are : - the wedge , - the inclined plane ( ramp ) , - the screw , - the lever , - the pulley , - and the wheel and axle . compound machines are made up of two or more simple machines . other websites . - aplusproject.com - machine design website with a forum - machinedesign.com
