Aegukka     aegukka ( aeguk-kka ; 애국가 ; 愛國歌 ) , meaning the patriotic song is the national anthem of the democratic people 's republic of korea ( north korea ) . it should not be confused with south korea 's anthem , which has the same name . it is sometimes also known by the first bit of the song , `` achhimŭn pinnara '' ( 아침은 빛나라 ) , meaning `` let morning shine '' .
