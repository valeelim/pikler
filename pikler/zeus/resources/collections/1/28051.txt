Lancashire     lancashire is a county in the north west of england . its two cities are lancaster and preston . before 1974 , liverpool and manchester were part of lancashire . the county has developed a fierce rivalry with yorkshire , the next county east . this comes from rivalry in sport , such as in cricket , and comes from the war of the roses . a big attraction in lancashire is blackpool , an english seaside resort , which has a theme park called blackpool pleasure beach . it has the second largest rollercoaster in europe ( `` the big one '' ) and blackpool tower , a tower which was made to look like the eiffel tower in paris . lancashire has market towns ( like chorley ) and small villages ( like bretherton , croston and eccleston ) . it is just south of an area of outstanding natural beauty called the lake district . lancashire also has a lot of pleasant countryside such as white coppice and the rivington moors . a famous food from lancashire is `` lancashire hot pot '' which is made of meat , onion or other vegetables and potatoes . it is often found on many pub menus in the county and in other places in britain .
