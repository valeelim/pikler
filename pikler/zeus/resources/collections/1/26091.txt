Allies     in general , allies are people or groups that are working together to achieve some common purpose . when written with a capital a , allies usually means - allies of world war i , the countries who fought together against the central powers during world war i from 1914 to 1918 - the triple entente , three of those countries : the french republic , the british empire and the russian empire - allies of world war ii , the countries who fought against the axis powers in world war ii from 1939 to 1945 .
