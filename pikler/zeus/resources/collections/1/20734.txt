Ryan North     ryan m. north ( born october 20 , 1980 ) is a canadian writer . he is the author of the comic strip dinosaur comics , as well as whispered apologies and several other projects . he is also the programmer of ohnorobot , a web comic search engine . ryan north is also a member of the ryans , a group dedicated to being called ryan and making comics . other websites . - `` dinosaur comics '' - `` whispered apologies ''
