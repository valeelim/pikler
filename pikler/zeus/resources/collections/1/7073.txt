Hamilton, Ontario    hamilton , ontario hamilton is a medium-sized city in the canadian province of ontario . hamilton is an important industrial city located on lake ontario , part of the golden horseshoe region in ontario . it has a history of being a city with a lot of industrial activity ( factories making things ) but its reputation is slowly changing . people now see hamilton as an important city in canada for its art and music . historical places - canadian warplane heritage - dundurn castle historical site with beautiful gardens , walking paths and a museum - whitehern historical site primarily for children with activities that allow them to see what life in the past was like - canadian football hall of fame , featuring memorabilia and information about canadian football league cultural places - art gallery of hamilton , in the central downtown area - mcmaster museum of art , in the west end on mcmaster university 's campus - ontario workers arts and heritage centre , in the north end of hamilton natural places
