Reading     reading is a way of getting information from something that is written . reading involves recognising the symbols that make up a language . reading and hearing are the two most common ways to get information . information gained from reading can include entertainment , especially when reading fiction or humor . reading by people is mostly done from paper . stone , or chalk on a blackboard , or ink on a whiteboard can also be read . computer displays can be read . reading can be something that someone does by themselves or they can read aloud . this could be done to benefit other listeners . it could also be done to help concentrate . `` proofreading '' is a kind of reading that is done to find mistakes in a piece of writing . related pages . - literacy - literature - aliteracy - writing - printing
