Ralph Steadman     ralph steadman ( born wallasey , 15 may 1936 ) is a british cartoonist and illustrator . he is best known for his artwork for the books and articles of hunter s. thompson . steadman was born in wallasey , cheshire , and brought up in towyn , north wales . he went to ysgol emrys ap iwan ( high school ) , abergele , east ham technical college and the london college of printing during the 1960s . there , he did freelance work for `` punch '' , `` private eye '' , the `` daily telegraph , '' `` the new york times '' and `` rolling stone '' . steadman lives with his wife in kent , england . other websites . - british cartoon archive , university of kent has a database with over 100 cartoons by ralph steadman - flying dog connection and bio - interview with ralph steadman by laurie taylor in new humanist magazine - interview with ralph steadman by jake mcgee in kotori magazine
