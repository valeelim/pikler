New England Revolution     the new england revolution are a united states football ( soccer ) team from foxborough , massachusetts that plays in the eastern conference of major league soccer . they share the gillette stadium with the new england patriots , a football team in the nfl . the team is owned by robert kraft , who also owns the new england patriots . the team won their first major trophy when they won the u.s. open cup in 2007. they won the north american superliga in 2008. related pages . - list of united states soccer teams
