Lourdes Flores Nano     dr.lourdes celmira flores nano ( born october 7 1959 ) is a peruvian lawyer and politician . she is the leader of the christian people 's party . she tried to become the mayor of lima in 2010. flores nano was born in lima in 1959. she studied law at the pontifical catholic university of peru . she studied for a phd in law from the universidad complutense de madrid , one of the world 's oldest universities in madrid , spain . she was a member of the lima municipal council and then a member of the national congress of peru . in 2001 and 2006 she was a candidate in election for the president of peru .
