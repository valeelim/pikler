Wetzlar     wetzlar is a city at the river lahn in hesse in germany . the number of people in the city is about 53,000. wetzlar is near frankfurt . wetzlar is famous for the historic oldtown and for goethe . the camera maker leica is there . wetzlar has a connection to the autobahn 45 with three junctions . wetzlar is twinned with : - avignon , france - colchester , united kingdom - siena , italy - schladming , austria - ilmenau , thuringia - berlin-neukölln , berlin - písek , czech republic
