1274     events . - may 7 – the second council of lyons , held by the leadership of the roman catholic church convenes to consider the conquest of the holy land via crusades and address the east-west schism with the byzantine church . the council eventually approves a tithe to support efforts to conquer the holy land from muslims , and reaches apparent resolution of the schism which ultimately proves unsuccessful . - november – the diet at nuremberg orders that all crown estates seized since the death of frederick ii , holy roman emperor be restored to rudolph i of germany ; almost all european rulers agree , with the notable exception of king otakar ii of bohemia , who had benefited greatly by conquering or otherwise coming into possession of many of those lands . - pope gregory x decrees that conclaves ( meetings during which the electors have no contact with the outside ) should be used for papal elections , reforming the electoral process which had taken over three years to elect him .
