Straight     the word straight means something that goes in one direction without turns , bends , or curves . lines can bend in a curve . if lines do not bend , they are straight . for example , the edge of a circle is not straight . the edge of a circle bends in a curve . light bends in a curve in air and water , but travels in a straight line away from the earth or other bodies in outer space where there is no air . the word `` straight '' is also a slang term for `` heterosexual ''
