Martinique     martinique is an island in the caribbean sea , and is an overseas region of france . it has an area of 1,128 km² . nearly 400,000 people live there . the capital city is fort-de-france . other towns include sainte-anne and st. pierre ( which was destroyed by a volcano named mont pelée in 1902 ) . martinique is well known for its zouk music . artists such as kassav have made it famous over the years . the ethnic makeup of the country is : 90 % african and mixed african with anything from chinese , east indian or white . and 10 % white , amerindian , chinese and east indian . related pages . - guadeloupe , another french island in the caribbean other websites . - martinique tourism authority
