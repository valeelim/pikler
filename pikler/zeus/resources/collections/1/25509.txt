Marie of Boulogne     marie of boulogne was the countess of boulogne from 1159 until 1170. marie was the daughter of stephen of england and matilda of boulogne . she was placed in a convent when she was young so that she could become a nun . when her brother , william of blois , died , she became the heir of the count of boulogne . she had to leave the convent and was married to matthew of alsace . marie and matthew ruled boulogne together , but their marriage was not happy and they got a divorce in 1170. after marie died , matthew continued to be the count of boulogne until 1173. after this marie and matthew 's daughter , ida of boulogne , became countess of boulogne . marie and matthew 's other daughter , maud of boulogne , became the wife of henry i of brabant .
