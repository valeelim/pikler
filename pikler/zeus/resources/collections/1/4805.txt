List of tectonic plates     this is a list of the biggest tectonic plates on earth . these seven plates make up most of the seven continents and the pacific ocean . - african plate - antarctic plate - eurasian plate - indo-australian plate - north american plate - pacific plate - south american plate these plates are generally shown on major plate maps , with the exception of the arabian and indian plates , which are smaller than the others . - arabian plate - caribbean plate - cocos plate - juan de fuca plate - indian plate - nazca plate - philippine sea plate - scotia plate these are parts of major plates , and bits and pieces of ancient plates that became broken up . related pages . - plate tectonics other websites . - bird , p.
