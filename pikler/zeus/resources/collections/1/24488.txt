Alexa     alexa is an internet service that measures how many internet users visit a website . at alexa.com , people can enter a website address and alexa will show them how well-visited the website is . it can also tell them how its popularity has changed over time . alexa has 6 million unique visits monthly . alexa was created in 1996 by american businessman brewster kahle and bruce gilliat . in 1999 , alexa was bought by amazon.com for about us $ 250 million in amazon stock . alexa provides a free api that is used by services and toolbars all around the world to easily display alexa rating . some virus companies mark alexa toolbar as adware ( mcafee ) , trackware ( symantec ) and potentially unwanted program . other websites . - alexa
