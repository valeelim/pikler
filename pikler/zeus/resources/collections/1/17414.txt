List     a list is a part of a text , which has many words separated by commas ( , ) or a group of words going down the page that may use bullet points , letters , or numbers . semi-colons ( ; ) are also used sometimes to separate items in a list . these are lists : i have invited peter , mary , and henry to my birthday . people who i have invited to my birthday are : - peter - mary - henry people i invited to my birthday : peter ; mary ; henry .
