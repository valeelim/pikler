Fred Gwynne     fred gwynne ( born july 10 , 1926 - july 2 , 1993 ) was an american actor . he is best known for his role as herman munster in `` the munsters '' . he played francis muldoon in `` car 54 , where are you ? '' . `` the munsters '' and `` car 54 , where are you ? '' were television situation comedies . he also wrote children 's literature .
