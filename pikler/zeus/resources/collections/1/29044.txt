Fiat Panda     the fiat panda is a small car made by fiat from 1980 until 2003. it has four seats and two doors . normally it is bought by small families or young adults because the car is quite cheap and also the tax is not very high . the second series of the car has sold since 2003 and was the european car of the year in 2004. it entered its third generation in 2011 .
