Clematis     clematis is a genus of plants that is widely known for its beautiful flowers . there are many varieties in this group as gardeners around the world have produced many varieties . they can be found not only in western countries but also in japan . this plant grows up as a vine . it uses its leaflets and leaf stalks to twine around any support . in nature it often climbs up a nearby tree . the original species can mostly be found in temperate areas on earth . some species can also be found on high mountains .
