AbiWord     abiword is a free word processor that can be downloaded for free , it is open source and is much smaller in size than a proprietary alternative , microsoft word . it is available for freebsd , linux , microsoft windows , mac os x , and qnx . its mascot is abi the ant . it is much smaller than many other commercial word-processors of its time , only using about 15mb on a computer 's hard drive under microsoft windows . it is easy to use . related pages . - openoffice.org writer other websites . - abiword
