DC Comics     dc comics ( founded in 1934 as national allied publications ) is an american comic book company . it is owned by time warner . dc comics , short for `` detective comics '' , is best well known for making `` superhero '' comic books . dc comics are very famous in the modern art subject graphics . some of their characters include : - superman - batman - wonder woman - aquaman - the flash - green lantern - green arrow - hawkman - the justice league - teen titans - swamp thing - metamorpho - steel ( comics )
