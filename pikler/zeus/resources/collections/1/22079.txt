Batman (city)    batman ( city ) batman is a city in southeast turkey . it is the capital ( city ) of the province with the same name . in 2010 , 325,000 people lived there . there are many kurds in that area . the area has much oil and gas . there is a railway track at kurtalan , near this city . the track runs all the way to istanbul . there is also an airport near batman . the name is short for the bati raman mountains . other websites . - batman municipality
