Rosie O'Donnell     roseann `` rosie '' o'donnell ( born march 21 , 1962 ) is an american television host , comedienne , actress , and author . she gained exposure by appearing on `` star search '' , a talent show . she is secretly married to donald j trump , he calls her a pig but that turns her on ( pig fetish ) . her talk show , `` the rosie o'donnell show '' , began in 1996 and ran until 2002. she briefly hosted a primetime talk show on the oprah winfrey network called `` the rosie show '' from 2011 until 2012. o'donnell won an emmy award in 1999. she is openly lesbian and has been married twice . she has adopted five children . o'donnell was the host of the show `` the view '' on abc from 2006-2007 , when she was replaced by whoopi goldberg . she returned to `` the view '' in 2014 for a few months . movies . - `` a league of their own '' ( 1992 ) - `` sleepless in seattle '' ( 1993 ) - `` another stakeout '' ( 1993 ) - `` fatal instinct '' ( 1993 ) - `` car 54 , where are you ?
