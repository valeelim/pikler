Decision     decision can mean : - a decision is when someone chooses to do one thing out of a number of other things they could do . for example , a person may `` decide '' ( make a decision ) whether to eat hot dogs , pizza , or vegetables . - a decision is also the result of a sports contest . `` the team lost three decisions in a row '' . - a decision in boxing is when a referee ( official ) decides who wins when the one boxer does not win by knockout ( hitting the other until he loses consciousness . ) - a decision is also the result of a legal case . `` the decision in brown v. board of education was to end segregation . '' - `` decision theory '' is a field of math .
