Accordion     an accordion is a musical instrument that has keys similar to a piano , but is small enough for a person to hold . it makes sounds using air pushed and pulled through reeds using a bellows . the accordion can also have buttons instead of keys . the 6-plus-6-system with three rows has the same fingering in all twelve scales . the accordion was invented in 1822 in germany by friedrich buschmann . related pages . - bandonion
