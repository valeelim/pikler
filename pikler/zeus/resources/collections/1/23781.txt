Genre     a genre ( from ) is a style or type of music , literature , movies , or other media . some genres ( styles ) are used in more than one form of art or communication . for example , the genres fantasy and science fiction are used in literature , movies , and television . examples of genres . - general : comedy , romance , mystery , science fiction - literary ( written ) : fiction , non-fiction , young adult - movie genres : action movie , documentary , film noir - musical : rock and roll , hip hop , pop punk - comedy : black comedy , slapstick , commedia dell'arte
