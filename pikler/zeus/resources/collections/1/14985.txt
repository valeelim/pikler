Universal Media Disc     `` umd can also mean university of maryland . '' the universal media disc ( umd ) is an optical disc made by sony for their playstation portable handheld console . it can hold up to 900 megabytes ( single layer ) or 1.8 gigabytes ( double layer ) of data . the disc can store games , music , and video , along with updates for the psp . it is the first optical disc format to be used for a handheld video game system . to prevent illegal copying , sony has not made blank discs available for sale . this disc is not used with any other devices . the umd is very fragile and can be broken from misuse . the primary use for umd discs is for a storage medium for psp games , but the format is also used for the storage of movies . even though with the release of the psp go , most new games continue to be made via umd , and , aside from those published by sce , not all have been released on the psn .
