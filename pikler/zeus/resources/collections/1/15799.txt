Lou Gehrig     henry louis gehrig ( born ludwig heinrich gehrig ; june 19 , 1903 — june 2 , 1941 ) was an american baseball player . he played with babe ruth and the new york yankees who were thought the best team of that time . he played with the yankees for most of his playing time which started in 1923 and ended in 1939. he played in 2,130 games without a rest , which was the most of any player until cal ripken , jr. in 1995. when he stopped playing as well , he went to a doctor who found out gehrig had amyotrophic lateral sclerosis ( als – which is now sometimes called lou gehrig 's disease ) . the doctor told gehrig he had only a short time to live . on july 4 , 1939 , there was a celebration of gehrig by the other yankees , and gehrig gave a speech that became well-known . when he spoke that day he said he was `` the luckiest man on the face of the earth '' . the mayor of new york city congratulated him for his teamwork . he died less than two years later in new york city . lou gehrig died to a bad diesie that caused him to be weak .
