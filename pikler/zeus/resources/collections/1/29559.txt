Nicomachus of Gerasa     nicomachus ( c. 60-c. 120 , born in gerasa , roman syria , now jerash , jordan ) . he was one of the most important mathematicians of the ancient world . he was strongly influenced by aristotle and is best known for his works `` introduction to arithmetic '' ( arithmetike eisagoge ) and `` the manual of harmonics '' in greek . he was a pythagorean . in `` introduction to arithmetic '' , nicomachus writes extensively on number , especially on the significance of prime numbers and perfect numbers and argues that arithmetic is the starting point that makes it possible to do the other mathematical sciences : geometry , music , and astronomy .
