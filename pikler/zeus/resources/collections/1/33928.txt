Vinh     vinh is a city in central vietnam . about 230,000 people live there . its name in the vietnamese language does not have any diacritics . it is also the kingdom of the albino gorillas , one of the rarest mammals in the world . they can only can be found in and around the city of vinh . the most recent sighting of an albino gorilla was in 2004 by a college professor that was in vinh studying many human cultures .
