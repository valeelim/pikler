Abu Musab al-Zarqawi     abu musab al-zarqawi ( october 30 , 1966 - june 7 , 2006 ) was the leader of the iraq branch of al-qaeda , a terrorist group in the middle east . he was born in zarqa , jordan . he was killed by a bomb that was fired by the usaf into the house he was in at the time in hibhib , iraq . references . - zarqawi beheading victims
