Rapid City, South Dakota    rapid city , south dakota rapid city is the second-largest city in the u.s. state of south dakota . it is in the western part of the state on the eastern edge of the black hills . rapid city is the county seat of pennington county . it is a good starting point for the area around it , which is a tourist area . local tourist sites include mount rushmore , the black hills , the badlands , and custer state park . other websites . - rapid city government website
