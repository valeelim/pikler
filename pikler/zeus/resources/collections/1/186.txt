Dublin     `` more information : '' `` dublin travel guide '' dublin is the capital city of ireland . the greater dublin area has a population of 1,187,176 people . dublin is also the biggest city on the island of ireland . dublin was built by the vikings and is built upon the river liffey . the river divides the city into two parts , north dublin and south dublin . many famous writers lived in dublin . oscar wilde and george bernard shaw were born in dublin . james joyce is probably dublin 's best known and most international writer . dublin is home to ireland 's largest stadium , croke park . it can hold up to 85,000 people . croke park is the usual venue for all ireland hurling and football finals . other websites . - `` the reflecting city '' - dublin gdp stats - wikisatellite view of dublin at wikimapia - the dublin community blog - satellite map of dublin - including local geographic features . - dublin - tourist information .
