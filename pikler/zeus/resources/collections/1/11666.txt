Peter Fonda     peter fonda ( born february 23 , 1940 ) is an american actor . he is the son of actor henry fonda . he is the father of actress bridget fonda and brother of actress jane fonda . fonda is most famous for 1969 movie `` easy rider '' . he co-wrote and co-starred in the movie with his friend dennis hopper . he rode a motorcycle in `` easy rider '' , and also in the earlier `` the wild angels '' , where he played an outlaw biker . he has also appeared in many other movies , including `` ulee 's gold '' in 1997 , which earned him an academy award nomination , and as frank o'connor , husband of writer ayn rand , in `` the passion of ayn rand '' . fonda has appeared many times on talk shows , discussing his life and times , including his family life . fonda wrote an autobiography , `` do n't tell dad : a memoir '' , which was published in 1998. fonda has had a permanent home in paradise valley , montana since 1975. other websites . - official website : www.peterfonda.com
