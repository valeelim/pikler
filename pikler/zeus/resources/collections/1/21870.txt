Ulm     ulm is a city in germany , in the state of baden-württemberg . it is situated at the river danube . on the left side of the river the city is called ulm . on the right side is neu-ulm in bavaria . together it has more than 170,000 inhabitants . ulm alone has about 120,000 inhabitants . ulm has a university . the university has about 7000 students . the world 's highest church tower is the ulmer münster ( 161,5m ) in ulm . also , ulm has the oldest city theatre in germany . albert einstein was born in ulm .
