Ransom     ransom can mean two things : 1. holding a person against their will , in order to get some money ( or some other things ) 1. the money , or the goods which ( could possibly ) be obtained in such a way . if people talk about that they need to pay money to the state or the police , to get free , the correct legal term used is usually bail . often in piracy , pirates would steal something or someone important and demand a ransom payment .
