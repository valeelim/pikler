We Will Rock You     `` we will rock you '' is a song by queen . it is one of their most famous songs . it is used at a lot of sports events . the main song has the band stamping their feet twice , and clapping once . at the end , a short guitar part is played . musicians : - freddie mercury - lead and backing vocals , handclaps , footstamps - brian may - electric guitar , backing vocals , handclaps , footstamps - roger taylor - backing vocals , handclaps , footstamps - john deacon - handclaps , footstamps
