Jon Bon Jovi     jon bon jovi ( born john francis bongiovi on march 2 , 1962 in perth amboy , new jersey ) is an american musician and actor . he is most famous as lead singer of the group , bon jovi . of origin he 's italian . in 1983 he was one of the founder of american hard-rock band bon jovi . earlier he sang in bands `` starz '' and `` atlantic city expressway '' . in 2006 he was ranked in 31st place out of 100 the best vocalist in history . he starred in 18 films , last time in 2011 .
