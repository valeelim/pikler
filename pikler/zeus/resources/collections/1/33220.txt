Asikkala     asikkala is a municipality of finland . it is in the province of southern finland and the region of päijänne tavastia . the downtown of asikkala is called vääksy . it has a canal , vääksyn kanava . asikkala has an area of 756.2 km² . 192.29 km² of it is water . asikkala is on the coast of the lake päijänne . as of 31 may 2013 , 8,444 people live in the municipality . other websites . - the official asikkala page
