Belarusian language     the belarusian language ( `` '' , transliteration : biełaruskaja mova ) is an eastern slavic language . this language is a part of the indo-european language family . it is spoken in belarus and eastern poland ( in the area of białystok ) . it is also spoken by belarusians who live in other countries of europe , australia , and north america . when writing belarusian , people can use the cyrillic or the latin alphabet .
