By-product     a by-product is something that is made or produced while a product is manufactured in a factory . usually , the by-product is not used , and it becomes waste that is put in a landfill or garbage dump . sometimes by-products are toxic materials which are dangerous to humans , animals , and plants . some companies are trying to find ways to reuse or recycle the by-products , instead of putting them into landfill or garbage dumps as waste . animal `` by-products '' are produced by slaughterhouses ( places designed to kill animals for food ) . animal by-products are the parts of the animals that can not be eaten by humans . animal by-products are sometimes included in non-human foods , such as pet food or animal feed ( for farm animals ) .
