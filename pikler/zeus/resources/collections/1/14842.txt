Simplicity     when something is simple , it is very easy to understand or do . the opposite is hard or `` difficult '' . simplicity is the measurement of how simple something is . examples : - 2+2 is a very simple sum ( an easy sum ) . - boiling an egg is very simple ( very easy ) `` simple '' can mean something that is only made up of a few parts . it lacks complexity . - a bicycle is much simpler than a space ship . `` simple '' can mean that something or someone is humble ( lowly ) or common : - he lives very simply . `` simple living '' is a way of life . it uses the ideas of henry david thoreau or epicurius or diogenes as its moral goals . simple living says that a person needs only few and basic things to be happy . `` simple '' can also be used to refer to someone whose mind thinks in a very simple way . calling a person `` simple minded '' is usually rude . related pages . - simpleton
