Jan Peter Balkenende     jan peter balkenende ( born 7 may 1956 ) is a dutch politician . he was the prime minister of the netherlands from 2002 until 2010. he is a member of the christian democratic appeal party . in other countries , he is perhaps most famous because he looks like harry potter . this makes him popular with young children , but many dutch make jokes about it and call him `` jan potter balkenende '' and other names like that . balkenende lives in capelle aan den ijssel , netherlands .
