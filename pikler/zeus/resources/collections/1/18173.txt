Terry Bradshaw     terry bradshaw is a former american athlete . he is also a sports presenter . he was born september 2 , 1948 in shreveport , louisiana . bradshaw is a former quarterback with the pittsburgh steelers in the national football league , and a television and american football host . over six years , he won four super bowl titles with pittsburgh ( 1975 , 1976 , 1979 and 1980 ) . he went into the pro football hall of fame in 1989. he is related to cory j.bylsma
