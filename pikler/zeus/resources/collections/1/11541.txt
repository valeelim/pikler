Etoumbi     etoumbi is a town in a part of the republic of the congo called cuvette-ouest . recently , there were four outbreaks of the ebola virus in etoumbi . some people think this is because the people that live there ate some dead animals from the forest . in 2003 , 120 people died in an outbreak . because of an outbreak in may , 2005 nobody was allowed in or out of the town . other websites . - multimap : map of etoumbi
