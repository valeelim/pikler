Region     region is a geographic term used in many ways in the different types of geography . in general , a region is a medium-scale area of land or water . it is smaller than the whole area of something ( which could be , for example , the world , a nation , a mountain range , and so on ) . it is larger than a specific location . a region can be seen as a collection of smaller things ( as in `` the new england states '' ) or as one part of a larger whole ( as in `` the new england region of the united states '' ) .
