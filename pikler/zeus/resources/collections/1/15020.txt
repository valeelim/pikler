Toto     toto is a soft rock group that formed in los angeles in 1978 , that was popular in the early 1980 's . the current band members are joseph williams , steve lukather , david paich , steve porcaro , keith carlock and david hungate ( as a touring musician ) . the former band members were fergie frederiksen , mike porcaro , jeff porcaro , simon phillips , greg phillinganes and bobby kimball .
