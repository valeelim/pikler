North Vietnam     the democratic republic of vietnam ( drvn ) , or less commonly , vietnamese democratic republic ( vietnamese : `` việt nam dân chủ cộng hòa '' ) , also known as north vietnam , is a former country that existed from 1945 until 1976. it was in the north of present-day vietnam . it lay north of the local demilitarized zone , and south vietnam lay to the south of it . it was proclaimed by president hồ chí minh in hà nội on september 2 , 1945. it was the first socialist country in southeast asia . however , it was not recognised by china and the soviet union until 1950. north vietnam took part in the vietnam war .
