Neuss     neuss is a city in the west of germany , in the state of north rhine-westphalia . in the local dialect it is called `` nüss '' or neuß . neuss has about 150,000 inhabitants . it is on the rhine , opposite the big city of düsseldorf . neuss was founded by the romans about 2,000 years ago . the romans called the town `` castra novesia '' .
