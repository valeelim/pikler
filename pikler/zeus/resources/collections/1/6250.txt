Philippe's    philippe 's philippe 's is a sit-down restaurant in downtown los angeles , california , u.s.a.. it is at 1001 north alameda street , near olvera street . phillippe 's is famous for inventing the french dip sandwich ( though another restaurant called `` cole 's '' claims to have invented it , too ) . it has been in business since 1908 , and is the oldest operating restaurant in los angeles . the traditional meal served at phillippe 's is a french dip sandwich , a bag of potato chips , a soft-drink ( preferably root beer or cola ) , and a tapioca pudding cup for dessert .
