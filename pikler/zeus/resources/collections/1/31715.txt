Anne Rice     anne rice ( born howard allen o'brien , 4 october 1941 ) is an american author of horror/fantasy novels . `` interview with the vampire '' and `` the queen of the damned '' are among her best-known works . she has written four series , including `` the vampire chronicles '' and `` christ the lord '' . she was born in new orleans , louisiana to irish roman catholic parents . one of her sisters was novelist alice borchardt ( 1939-2007 ) . rice was married to poet stan rice ( 1942-2002 ) from 1961 his death . they had a daughter , michele ( 1966-1972 ) and a son , novelist christopher ( born 1978 ) . other websites . - anne rice 's spiritual journey site
