Anne Murray     morna anne murrary ( born june 20 , 1945 ) is a canadian pop and country musician . she was born in nova scotia . she was a teacher , but also sang and made records . one of her records , `` snowbird '' , became a hit late in 1970. the song was not about christmas , but was popular during the christmas season . the record became a perennial through association with the holiday . murray later had hits with remakes of beatles ( `` i 'm happy just to dance with you '' ) and monkees songs ( `` daydream believer '' ) . she also recorded `` shadows in the moonlight '' and `` a little good news '' , which became popular .
