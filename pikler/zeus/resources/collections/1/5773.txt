Sadness     sadness is an emotion . it is the opposite of happiness . people feel sad when something bad has happened , for example , if their mother or father has died , or if they are parted from friends . the word `` miserable '' has a similar meaning . in big letters , sad , or s.a.d . is short for `` seasonal affective disorder '' . sad is a sickness that some people have in the winter . they feel depressed as the nights become longer and there is less daylight . when people are very sad , and for a long time , the person could have a serious mental illness called depression or bipolar disorder . it is normally thought of as the opposite of happiness , but you can feel both happy and sad at the same time . related pages . - happiness
