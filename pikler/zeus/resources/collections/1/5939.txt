Bag     a bag is a kind of soft container . it can hold or carry things . it may be made from cloth , leather , plastic , or paper . many bags are disposable but some are made to use for a long time . a bag may have one or two handles or a shoulder strap . bags come in different shapes and sizes depending on how they will be used . a small bag that can be carried with a single hand is sometimes called a handbag , purse , or pocketbook . children can suffocate , or stop breathing and die , when they put certain kinds of bags over their heads , due to having the supply of oxygen cut off .
