Strait of Magellan     the strait of magellan is a passage way from the atlantic ocean to the pacific . it is just south of mainland south america and goes through argentina and chile . for a long time the two countries argued over which one of them owned the land around it . chile now owns it . the strait is named after ferdinand magellan . it is very foggy and stormy . other websites . - the first map made of the strait of magellan . it was made in 1520
