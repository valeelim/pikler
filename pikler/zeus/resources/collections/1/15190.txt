Reykjavík     reykjavík is the capital city of the island country of iceland . it is also the largest city in that country . the population of reykjavík is over 117,000 people . there is a geothermal bath , both natural and unnatural in appearance . it is situated in the capital and people relax in this hot spring during the cooler months . björk , an icelandic singer , is from reykjavik .
