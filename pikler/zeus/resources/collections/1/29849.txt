Clytemnestra     klytaimnestra ( or clytemnestra ) was a person in greek mythology . she was the daughter of king tyndareos of sparta and his wife leda . she was married to agamemnon , king of mycenae . with him her children were iphigenia , elektra , orestes and chrysothemis . after the trojan war , klytaimnestra and her lover aegisthos killed agamemnon . klytaimnestra and aegisthos are later killed by her son orestes .
