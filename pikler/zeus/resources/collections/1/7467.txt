Terry Jones     terry jones ( born 1 february 1942 ) is a welsh writer , actor , and historian . he was a member of monty python . jones was born on 1 february 1942 in colwyn bay , north wales , united kingdom . he is married to alison telfer . in september 2016 it was announced that jones had been diagnosed with a rare form of dementia that impairs the ability to speak and communicate , and that he was no longer able to give interviews .
