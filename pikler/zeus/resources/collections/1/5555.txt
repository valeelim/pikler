Scissors     scissors are a tool used for cutting thin material like : - paper - cardboard - metal foil - thin plastic - food - cloth - rope - wire - hair - nail knives are better than scissors for some uses . unlike a knife , scissors have two sharp edges . some types of scissors , like children 's scissors , are not very sharp . children 's scissors are often protected with plastic . these are called `` safety scissors '' . scissors can be made for left-handed or right-handed people . using scissors made for the wrong hand is very hard for most people . there are many kinds of scissors used for cutting different materials and shapes .
