Bryan Adams     bryan adams ( november 9 , 1959 ) is a canadian rock musician . he is known for the songs `` summer of '69 '' and `` ( everything i do ) i do it for you '' . he has performed with many other musical artists such as def leppard , tina turner , sting , rod stewart , bonnie raitt , barbra streisand and melanie c. his best-selling albums are reckless , waking up the neighbours and 18 til i die .
