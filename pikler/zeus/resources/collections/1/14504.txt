Stephen King     stephen king ( born september 21 , 1947 ) is an american writer from maine . he is known for writing scary books known as horror novels . his books and stories have sold more than 300 million copies . he has written more than fifty novels , two hundred short stories , and nine collections of short fiction . many of his stories have been made into movies or television shows as well . he has written some of his books under the name `` richard bachman '' and collaborated with peter straub on others . his highest rated books by readers include : `` carrie '' , `` the shining '' , `` the stand '' , `` misery '' , `` it '' , `` cujo '' , `` the dark tower '' , `` under the dome '' and `` rita hayworth and shawshank redemption '' . king 's first novel , carrie , was published in 1974 and his most recent novel , finders keepers , was published on june 2nd , 2015. stephen king was born in portland , maine . his mother 's name is nellie ruth and his father 's name is donald edwin king . king and his wife tabitha own three different houses . one house is in bangor , maine , one is in lovell , maine and one in sarasota , florida . stephen and tabitha have three children and three grandchildren .
