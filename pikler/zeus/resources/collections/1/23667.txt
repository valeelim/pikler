Adjustment     an adjustment is when something is changed in a small way . this is usually to make it better . the word can be used as a verb , as in `` bill needs to adjust his tie '' . examples of adjustments : - the wrong word is written on a piece of paper . by erasing the word and writing the right one , an `` adjustment '' has been made to the paper . - a picture hung on a wall is not straight . it can be `` adjusted '' by moving it and making it straight .
