1003     1003 ( miii ) was a common year when the julian calendar was used . it was the third year of the 2nd millennium and the 11th century . events . - sweyn i of denmark begins his first invasion of england . - humbert i becomes the first count of savoy . - king robert ii of france invades burgundy , but fails . - pope john xvii succeeds pope silvester ii . - pope john xviii succeeds pope john xvii . - boleslaus i of poland becomes duke of bohemia and moravia .
