Bow (weapon)    bow ( weapon ) a bow is a weapon used to shoot arrows . it is used in hunting , sport , and a long time ago , war . the use of a bow is called archery . a person who uses a bow is an archer , someone who makes arrows is a fletcher and a person who makes bows is a bowyer . a bow is made up of a long curved stick and a string which is tied to both ends . ancient bows were made of natural materials like wood or bone and sinew , but today they are often made of plastic and similar materials . to shoot a bow , the arrow is put on the bow , with one end to the string , which is pulled back . when the string is let loose , it throws the arrow forwards . there are many different types of bows , for example the longbow or the automatic crossbow and the compound bow
