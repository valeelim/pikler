Jamie Farr     jamie farr ( born july 1 , 1934 ) is an american actor of lebanese descent . he is best known for his role as corp./later sgt . maxwell q. klinger on the 1970s tv show , `` m*a*s*h '' . his real name is jameel joseph farha and he was born in toledo , ohio . farr also appeared in movies like `` the blackboard jungle '' ( 1955 ) and `` the cannonball run '' ( 1980 ) , he was a regular `` celebrity judge '' on `` the gong show '' and has a golf tournament named after him .
