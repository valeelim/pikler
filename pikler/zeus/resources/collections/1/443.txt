Leather     leather is the skin of an animal used as a material . the skins of cows , pigs , and goats are often used to make leather . skins of snakes , alligators or crocodiles , and ostriches are sometimes used to make fancier leather . shoes , bags , clothes , and balls are often made of leather . sometimes people make leather out of whales , ducks , giraffes , and african elephants , but all of these ways of making leather are very simple but can also be very hard and rare sometimes .
