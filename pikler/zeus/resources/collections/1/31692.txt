Colombo     colombo ( ; ) is the largest city of sri lanka . it is the city which is economically most important to the country . it has 642,163 people . it is on the southwest coast close to the capital city of kotte . the name colombo is borrowed from sinhala language name kola-amba-thota which means `` harbor with leafy mango trees '' . traveller ibn batuta in the 14th century referred to it as `` kalanpu '' .
