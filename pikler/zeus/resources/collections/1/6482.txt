Comics     in comics , stories or information are given using pictures , or pictures and words together . in comics , a story is told with many pictures , mostly in panels . the first panel is supposed to be read first , and takes place earlier in time than the panels that follow it . there are many kinds of comics . comic strips are short comics which are often found in newspapers . comic books are thin comics magazines . graphic novels are books of comics . in japan , comics are very popular , and japanese comics are popular around the world . the japanese word for comics is manga , and people use this word for japanese comics in english and other languages . the first comic strips in the united states were funny . `` comic '' is another word for `` funny '' , so they were called `` comic strips '' . the first comic books were collections of comic strips . today , many comics are serious , but they are still called `` comics '' . in comics , speech is usually shown through word balloons . there are many different kinds of word balloons , such as the `` speech balloon '' , the `` thought balloon '' and the `` scream balloon '' .
