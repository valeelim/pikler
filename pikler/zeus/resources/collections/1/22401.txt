Riding helmet     a riding helmet , sometimes called a horse riding helmet , is a type of hat ( cover for the head ) made for people who ride horses . it is important to wear a riding helmet when riding . it is easy for a person to fall off a horse and hit his or her head . riding helmets keep the rider of a horse from being hurt . horses can accidentally step on a person who has fallen to the ground . helmets should be worn by all riders , but are most often worn by english style riders .
