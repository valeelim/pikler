Jumanji     jumanji is a 1995 movie starring robin williams , which was based loosely on a 1982 children 's book of the same name by chris van allsburg . it is about a boy who gets trapped in a game for 30 years , and must finish playing the game when he gets out . other stars include kirsten dunst . in the `` flying hunter van pelt '' scene , roland d-50 's pn-d50-02 patch `` fast forward '' was heard . the game in the movie ( titled jumanji ) has been made into a real game .
