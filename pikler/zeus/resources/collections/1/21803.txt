Shiitake mushroom     the shiitake mushroom ( `` lentinus edodes '' or `` lentinula edodes '' ) , also known as chinese black mushroom or black forest mushroom , is an edible mushroom typically grown on the shii tree . two chinese variant names for high grades of shiitake are `` dōnggū '' ( `` winter mushroom '' ) and `` huāgū '' ( `` flower mushroom , '' which has a flower-like cracking pattern on the mushroom 's upper surface ) ; both are produced at colder temperatures .
