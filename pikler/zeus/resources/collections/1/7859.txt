Mork &amp; Mindy    mork & amp ; mindy mork and mindy is an american television sitcom which ran from 1978-1982. it stars robin williams and pam dawber as the title characters . the series was made by garry marshall . comedian jonathan winters has a recurring role in the series . `` mork and mindy '' is a spin-off from marshall 's other popular tv series , `` happy days '' . the plot of the series is that alien named mork from a planet called ork who travels to earth and becomes friends with a woman named mindy in boulder , colorado .
