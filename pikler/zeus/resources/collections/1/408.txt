Jupiter     jupiter is the largest planet in the solar system . it is the fifth planet from the sun . jupiter is a gas giant , both because it is so large and made up of gas . the other gas giants are saturn , uranus , and neptune . jupiter has a mass of 1.8986Ã—10 kg , or about 318 earths . this is twice the mass of all the other planets in the solar system put together . jupiter can be seen with the naked eye . it was known to the ancient romans , who named it after their god jupiter ( latin : `` iuppiter '' ) . jupiter is the third brightest object in the night sky . only the earth 's moon and venus are brighter . jupiter has at least 69 moons . of these , around 50 are very small and less than five kilometres wide . the four largest moons of jupiter are io , europa , ganymede , and callisto . they are called the galilean moons , because galileo galilei discovered them . ganymede is the largest moon in the solar system . it is larger in diameter even than mercury . jupiter is the biggest planet in the solar system with a diameter of 142,984 km . this is eleven times bigger than the diameter of earth .
