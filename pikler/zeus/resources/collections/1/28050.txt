Kookaburra Sport     kookaburra sport is an australian company named after an australian bird related to the kingfisher , which eats snakes and is , therefore , very valuable to the australians . kookaburra makes and sells cricket supplies which are used by amateurs and professionals . they are one of the leading brands and are famous for their colourful and artistic designs . it is the leading manufacturer of cricket equipment in the world . its balls are used the most in international cricket matches .
