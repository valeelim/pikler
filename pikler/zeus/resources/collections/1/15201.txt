Derek and the Dominos     derek and the dominos was an american blues rock band . the band 's members were eric clapton , carl radle , bobby whitlock , jim gordon , and duane allman . they made the album `` layla and other assorted love songs '' . `` layla '' , with the well-known guitar riff , came from that same album . the band broke up after one album . discography . - studio albums - `` layla and other assorted love songs '' ( 1970 )
