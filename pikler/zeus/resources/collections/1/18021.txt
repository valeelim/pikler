Saint Kitts and Nevis     the federation of saint kitts and nevis ( , also known as the federation of saint christopher and nevis ) , is a federal nation made up of two small islands in the caribbean sea . it is the smallest independent country in the western hemisphere . the capital city is basseterre . it is on the main island of st kitts . the island of nevis has some rights of self-government and two seats in the federal parliament . st kitts and nevis is about one-third of the way from puerto rico to trinidad and tobago . the headquarters of the eastern caribbean central bank is in st kitts . that bank controls the east caribbean dollar . the two islands were part of the leeward islands colony from 1871 to 1956 , and of the west indies federation from 1958 to 1962. the islands , plus anguilla , got limited self-government in 1967. anguilla objected and the metropolitan police were sent there in 1968 to stop rioting . until 19 december 1980 the official name of st kitts and nevis was saint christopher , nevis and anguilla .
