Percentage     percent means `` out of one hundred '' . it is often shown with the symbol `` % '' . it is used even if there are not a hundred items . the number is then scaled so it can be compared to one hundred . percentages are one way of writing numbers ; you can also write percentages as fractions or decimals . for example , i have a bowl of fruit with three apples and one orange . the percentage of apples is 3 out of 4 = 3/4 = 75/100 = 75 % . there are ways to convert fractions to percentages or decimals to percentages . percentages are useful because people can compare things that are not out of the same number . for example , exam marks are often percentages , so people can compare them even if there are more questions on one exam paper than the other .
