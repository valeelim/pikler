Mehmed II     mehmed ii ( march 30 , 1432 , `` edirne '' – may 3 , 1481 , `` hünkârcayırı '' , near `` gebze '' ) was sultan of the ottoman empire for a short time from 1444 to 1446 , and later from 1451 to 1481. at the age of 21 , he conquered constantinople . so he brought to an end to the medieval byzantine empire . related pages . - byzantine empire - sultan - turkey other websites . - biography page at ottomanonline
