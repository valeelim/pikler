Saanich     the district of saanich is a region of greater victoria , british columbia . it is north of the city of victoria . over 100,000 people live in saanich . it is named after the saanich first nation . some communities within saanich include gordon head , royal oak , cordova bay , tillicum-gorge , cadboro bay , broadmead , cedar hill , and ten-mile point . saanich commonwealth place , a recreation centre first used for swimming and diving at the 1994 commonwealth games , is located in saanich . saanich celebrated its 100th anniversary in 2006. the mayor of saanich is richard atwell .
