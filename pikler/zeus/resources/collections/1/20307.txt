Coat of Arms of Iran     the emblem of iran was made after the islamic revolution in that country , in 1979. after the revolution , iran decided to use a new flag . the old flag had a picture of a golden lion and a sabre , that was a symbol for the shah ( king ) of iran . this emblem is a symbol for the whole country and the islamic character of the new government . it is drawn to look like a version of the word الله which means `` god '' in persian ( the language of iran ) and also in the arabic language . it looks like a tulip , which is an old symbol for iran .
