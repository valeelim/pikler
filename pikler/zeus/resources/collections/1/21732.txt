1009     1009 ( mix ) was a common year when the julian calendar was used . it was the ninth year of the 2nd millennium and the 11th century . events . - february 14 : first known mention of lithuania , in the annals of the monastery of quedlinburg . - october 18 : the church of the holy sepulchre is destroyed by caliph al-hakim bi-amr allah . - the ly dynasty , vietnam 's first independent dynasty , is proclaimed . - sergius iv succeeds john xviii as pope . - suleiman ii succeeds mohammed ii as caliph of cordoba . - provinces of goryeo ( modern-day korea ) are redistributed .
