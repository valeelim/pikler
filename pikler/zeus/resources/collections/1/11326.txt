Kastoria     kastoria is a greek city and it is one of 51 prefectures of greece in the region of macedonia . kastoria it is built on the bank of lake orestiada . there are 17,038 people living in the city . kastoria has an old neighbourhood which is picturesque and it is named doltso . it has nice old houses with particular local architecture . the name `` kastoria '' means `` place of the beavers '' which used to live in the lake . these have all been hunted and killed for their fur .
