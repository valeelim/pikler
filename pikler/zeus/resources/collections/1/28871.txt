Toronto F.C.    toronto f.c . toronto football club , usually known as toronto fc , is a soccer club based in toronto , ontario . they have played in the eastern conference of major league soccer ( mls ) since the 2007 season . they were the first canadian soccer team to join mls . they play their home games at bmo field in downtown toronto . the team is coached by greg vanney and operated by maple leaf sports & entertainment . toronto fc has won the canadian championship five times , most recently in 2016. they also made it to the 2016 mls cup finals but lost to seattle sounders 4-5 in penalties . they were also semi-finalists of the 2011-2012 concacaf champions league .
