Bonsai     the word `` bonsai '' means `` tree in tray '' in the japanese language . bonsai is a very old art form in japan . it is a japanese form of the older chinese art called penjing . penjing is a chinese art form that also uses trees growing in pots . other nations also have arts like bonsai and penjing . people like bonsai because it is nice to look at , and because it is fun to grow a bonsai tree . a bonsai tree can live for a very long time , longer than a person can live . in a family , a bonsai might be started by a grandparent , then given to a parent , then given to a child over many years . a bonsai starts with a small tree . this tree can be grown from a seed , or can be found already growing in a yard or a park or the forest . it can also be bought from a plant store . to make the bonsai , the small tree is taken out of the ground . its roots are carefully cleaned of dirt . the roots may be trimmed ( cut ) a little to help them fit in a small pot . the branches may also be trimmed to make the tree smaller . then it is put in a bonsai pot , which has low sides . fresh soil ( dirt ) is put in the pot to cover the bonsai tree 's roots . then it is watered and put outdoors to live .
