Frisian language     frisian refers to three languages that comes from friesland , a province in the netherlands . they are spoken in the netherlands , in eastern germany , and in some areas of jutland , denmark . it is also spoken on the frisian isles ( wadden isles ) and western german ( east frisian ) isles such as borkum . they are west germanic languages related to dutch and are also the closest living languages to english . since they have been spoken since roman times , english could descend from frisian directly . the frisian languages are : - west frisian - north frisian - saterland frisian
