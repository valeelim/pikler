Box     a box is a container used to put things in . it is mainly a cuboid ( square ) shape , but it can be other shapes too . boxes are usually made out of cardboard , wood , plastic or metal . many boxes are made of cardboard , and a lot of them are made . they are primarily used for packaging commercial goods or storing goods and materials . they can be made from bent or wrinkled cardboard . in their most obvious life stage they are popularly used as a cheap material to hold a range of projects , among them being science experiments , children 's toys , costumes and other things . a box also means a rectangle , `` e.g . userbox '' . but rectangles are mainly referred to as rectangles , the term box is the outline , or perimeter of the rectangle .
