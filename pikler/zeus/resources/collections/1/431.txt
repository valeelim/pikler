Provinces and territories of Canada     canada , a country and sovereign state in the northern part of north america , is made up of thirteen administrative divisions : ten provinces and three territories . the different levels of government in canada are based on the principles of a federation : those of each of the provinces and territories share power with the federal government . the territories ' governments have a more limited set of powers versus the federal government than that of the provinces ' . the provinces are in the south of canada , near the border with the united states . they go from the atlantic ocean in the east to the pacific ocean in the west . the territories are to the north , where fewer people live , close to the arctic circle and arctic ocean . here is a list of the provinces and territories , and their standard abbreviations , with their capitals ( the cities where their governments are based ) and largest cities . canada 's national capital , where the federal government meets , is ottawa .
