Isaiah     isaiah was a prophet of god in islam , christianity and bahá'í . he lived from about 740 to 681 bc . the name `` isaiah '' means `` the lord is salvation '' in the hebrew language . isaiah is also the name of a book in the old testament of the bible , which tells the story of the israelites in isaiah 's time . according to christianity , isaiah predicted in this book , the birth and then death of jesus , hundreds of years before it actually happened . judaism does not agree with that interpretation . isaiah was known to be the shakespeare of the bible due to his good education and the quality of the passages he wrote . he was cut in half by king manasseh which led to his gruesome death . most of isaiah 's bible passages begin with 'god 's message to ' .
