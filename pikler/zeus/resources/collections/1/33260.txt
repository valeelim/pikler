Karstula     karstula is a municipality in central finland . more than 4,900 people live in karstula . karstula is located 100 kilometres from the city of jyväskylä . the municipality was founded in 1867. the municipalities next to it are kannonkoski , kivijärvi , kyyjärvi , saarijärvi and soini . karstula , oinoskylä , kiminki , rantakylä , vahanka , vastinki , kangasaho , humppi and aho-vastinki . other websites . - the official karstula page , in finnish and english
