United States Virgin Islands     the united states virgin islands are a group of islands in the caribbean sea . they are currently owned and under the authority of the united states government . they used to be owned by denmark ( and called `` danish west indies '' ) . they were sold to the u.s on january 17 , 1917 , because of fear that the germans would capture them and use them as a submarine base in world war i. they are the only part of the u.s. where vehicles drive on the left hand side of the road . other websites . - official tourism website - governor 's website - `` u.s. census bureau : island areas census 2000 '' - real-time , geographic and other scientific resources of virgin islands from the united states geological survey - convention between the united states and denmark for cession of the danish west indies
