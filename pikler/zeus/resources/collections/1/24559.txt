Diogenes syndrome     diogenes syndrome is a behavioral disorder . it is not a disease , it is a psychological condition . people who have it do not look after themselves enough any more , this is called self-neglect . they do not wash , perhaps do not shave , etc . so they develop a strong odor ( scent ) . since they neglect their hygiene , other diseases related to poor hygiene usually accompany the disorder . very often this disorder affects old people who live alone . diogenes syndrome is named after the behavior ( but not necessarily the ideals ) of diogenes of sinope . it is thought to be the end stage of personality disorder . references . - 1975 lancet article introducing diogenes syndrome ( via pubmed )
