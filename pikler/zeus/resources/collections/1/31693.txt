Malé     malé ( dhivehi : މާލެ ) is the capital city of the maldives . it has a population of 81,647 ( 2004 ) . the city is on malé island in the kaafu atoll . although malé is in kaafu atoll , administratively it is not considered part of it . there is a commercial harbor in the island . it is the center of all commercial activities in the country . many government buildings and agencies are on the waterfront . malé international airport is close to hulhule island which includes a seaplane base for internal transportation . and male ' has the most beautiful reefs on earth . male 's current president is yameen abdulgayoom and it is known as the smallest city on earth .
