Carl XVI Gustaf of Sweden     carl xvi gustaf ( born 30 april 1946 ) is the king of sweden . he became king on 15 september 1973. he is married to silvia sommerlath who is half german , half brazilian . with her he has three children : 1. princess victoria of sweden 1. prince carl philip of sweden 1. princess madeleine of sweden internationally king carl xvi gustav is best known for presenting the nobel prizes every year .
