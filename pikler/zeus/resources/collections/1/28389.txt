Franconia     franconia ( german : franken ) is the northern part of the german federal state of bavaria . this region is divided in three smaller regions : oberfranken , mittelfranken , and unterfranken . the biggest city in franconia is nuremberg . other large cities in franconia are coburg , aschaffenburg , würzburg , erlangen , fürth , bamberg , bayreuth , schweinfurt and hof .
