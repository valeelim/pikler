James Taylor     james taylor ( born march 12 , 1948 ) is an american singer and songwriter . he is popular for his songs `` fire and rain '' , `` you 've got a friend '' , and `` how sweet it is ( to be loved by you ) '' . taylor is a democrat and attended the 2012 democratic national convention in support of president obama 's re-election . taylor even performed during obama 's second inauguration .
