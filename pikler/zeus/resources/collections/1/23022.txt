Zetland (lifeboat)    zetland ( lifeboat ) the zetland was a lifeboat in redcar , england , and is the oldest lifeboat in the world which still exists . it was put in redcar in 1802. in 1858 , the lifeboat was given to the royal national lifeboat institution ( rnli ) . the boat was damaged in 1864 , and was to be destroyed - however , following protest it was given to the town 's people . today , it is in its own museum .
