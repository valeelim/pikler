Roasting     roasting is a way of cooking . something gets roasted if it is put over a fire , and some is burnt . the same effect can also be attained with a grill at a barbecue . in different countries people like to roast different things . people usually roast meats such as pork , beef , chicken , lamb , and duck . the roasted meat , along with its accompanying vegetables , is often called a roast . most meat being roasted has to cook for a relatively long time , to ensure that it is cooked through . variations to this include lamb and beef , which may be left `` pink '' or partially uncooked towards the centre . other things can be roasted too . for example , vegetables , coffee and chocolate . coffee and chocolate are always roasted .
