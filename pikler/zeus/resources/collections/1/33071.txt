Doorstop     a doorstop ( or door stop or doorstopper ) is a tool . there are two types . one holds a door open ( or `` stops '' it from closing ) , and the other stops the door from hitting a wall . doorstops that keep the door from hitting a wall can be put on the door , or on the wall , or on the floor in front of the wall . doorstops that are used to keep a door open come in two varieties : - attached to the door they are supposed to stop - wedges that can be put under the door they should stop , or anything heavy put in front of the door
