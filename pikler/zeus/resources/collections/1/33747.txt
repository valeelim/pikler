Brasschaat     brasschaat is a municipality in the belgian province of antwerp . in 2007 , 37133 people lived there . it is at 51° 17 north , 04° 29 east . there has always a big military force in brasschaat . after world war ii there were more military-related things . including the defence buildings made to stop the nazi 's from reaching the port of antwerp . now most of them are closed or are used to train the soldiers . brasschaat is called green because of the many parks and woods , such as the peerdsbos , the parc of brasschaat , de uitlegger , and de instlag .
