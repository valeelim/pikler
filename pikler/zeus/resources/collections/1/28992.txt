Georg Philipp Telemann     georg philipp telemann ( born march 14 , 1681 in magdeburg ; died june 25 , 1767 , hamburg ) was a german baroque composer . he wrote over 601 pieces of music , many of which were published . as publishing cost much money at that time , this was quite unusual . he mostly taught himself musically and knew how to play 10 instruments . during his life people thought he was one of the greatest composers . he also wrote lots of church music , most of which is not common today .
