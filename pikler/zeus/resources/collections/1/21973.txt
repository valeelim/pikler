Axe     an axe ( or ax ) is an ancient tool . it is used to shape , split and cut wood . the hand axe was probably the first tool in the stone age . it did not have a handle . in the past , axes were used as weapons by soldiers . today , axes are usually related to woodcutters and firemen . axes are simple machines . they are in the category of wedges . they are usually made of some sort of metal , mainly steel or iron . they can have a metal or wooden handle .
