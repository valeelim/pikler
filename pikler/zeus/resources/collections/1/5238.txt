Lisa Kudrow     lisa marie diane kudrow ( b. july 30 , 1963 in encino , california ) is an american actress . she was best known for her roles as ursula on `` mad about you '' and phoebe on `` friends '' . `` friends '' has been very popular lately and is still selling many copies , beating many popular shows even though it finished a long time ago . she also starred in p.s . i love you playing the role denise hennessey .
