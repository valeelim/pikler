Dominique de Villepin     dominique marie françois rené galouzeau de villepin ( born november 14 , 1953 ) , simply known as dominique de villepin , was premier ( or prime minister ) of france from 31 may 2005 to 17 may 2007. he was made premier by jacques chirac after the french president removed jean-pierre raffarin from the premiership . raffarin failed to gain the support of the french people for the european constitution . dominique de villepin said that his government 's most important task would be job creation . he knew that the french people were concerned about the condition of the french economy . this was one of the reasons they had voted against the european constitution in the referendum on 29 may 2005. on 15 may 2007 , the last full day of president jacques chirac 's term , villepin gave his resignation from the office of prime minister and it was accepted by the president . he was replaced two days later by françois fillon .
