Leader     a leader is someone who has the authority to tell a group of people what to do . in the simplest sense , a leader is somebody whom people follow . a group with no leader is called leaderless . a leader is one who gets others to take action towards a common goal . one job of a leader is to govern the actions of followers . a leader also represents a group or company . for example , the prime minister of canada has the authority to govern the society of canada and represent the people of canada . he is a leader . other websites . - 'the vision thing ' : how effective leaders cultivate and co-ordinate social identity in order to promote followership - leadership resources
