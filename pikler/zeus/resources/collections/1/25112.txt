Warner Bros.     warner bros. entertainment inc. ( commonly called warner bros. ) is a large entertainment group , movie studio and record label . it is owned by time warner . warner bros. is known for looney tunes . they own the copyrights to the harry potter movie series , the batman movie series , and superman movie series . they also produced friends . some of the current artists signed to warner bros. records are outasight , birdy and red hot chili peppers . warner bros. is a very large company . warner brothers was founded on april 04 , 1918. its founders were jack l. warner : albert warner : harry warner : and sam warner . it was founded in hollywood , california . it has won several awards including the academy award for best picture . linkin park also has a contract with us .
