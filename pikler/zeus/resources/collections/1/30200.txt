Hindu scripture     the hindu scriptures are a number of books and other texts which tell about hinduism and hindu mythology . they are sacred literature for hindus . important hindu scriptures include the following : - the vedas - the brahmanas - the upanishads - the puranas two epics ( very important pieces ) , the ramayana and the mahabharata , are also important hindu scriptures . all above sacred literature or scriptures are written in sanskrit language . besides the above , there are several other works that are sacred literature of hindus . many of them are written in other languages of india such as tamil and hindi .
