The Guardian     the guardian is a british newspaper . it is published every day , except on sunday . it is owned by the same company as the observer , which only publishes on sunday . `` the guardian '' is edited by katharine viner . the newspaper was founded in 1821. it was founded by textile traders and merchants . the guardian was seen as `` an organ of the middle class '' , or , in the words of c.p . scott 's son ted , `` a paper that will remain bourgeois to the last '' . in 2005 , sir max hastings stated that he writes for `` the guardian '' `` because it is read by the new establishment '' , reflecting the paper 's growing influence . until 1959 `` the guardian '' was called `` the manchester guardian '' . three of the four people who wrote leaders for the `` guardian '' joined the social democratic party on its foundation in 1982. the paper supported tony blair when he wanted to lead the labour party and to become prime minister . in 1999 theguardian.com started . at the time it was a website called guardian unlimited . `` the guardian '' describes itself as centre-left .
