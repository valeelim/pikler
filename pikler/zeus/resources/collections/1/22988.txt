Marske-by-the-Sea     marske by the sea is a village in the borough of redcar and cleveland in england . it has three primary schools and one secondary school ( bydales school ) , a museum , and two train stations . the village has a methodist , roman catholic ( st bede 's ) and church of england ( st mark 's ) church . most of the people who live in marske do not work in the village , but work in the nearby factory or in middlesbrough or redcar . marske has a decent range of local shops . the longbeck railway station and marske railway station , which connect to darlington mainline station , are the only means of transport there . other websites . - local history
