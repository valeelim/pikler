Grunge music     grunge music or seattle sound ( as it is called sometimes ) is a style of rock music that has elements of punk rock and heavy metal . it started in the mid- 1980s in the pacific northwest of the united states , especially in the states of washington and oregon . it became more popular in the early 1990s . grunge songs often had lyrics with nihilism , depression , unhappiness , or apathy as themes . the widespread use of heroin as a recreational drug found its way into the lyrics and careers of numerous grunge rockers , notably kurt cobain and layne staley of `` alice in chains '' . the songs often had light ( or clean ) guitar sounds in the verses , and the high energy boost of distorted ( or dirty ) guitars in the choruses . the line-up for these bands was the typical rock band of guitars , bass , drums and vocal . although grunge as a movement became less popular by the last 1990s , its influence is still heard in many bands today . some well known grunge artists were : - alice in chains - bush - foo fighters
