Dominic Scott     dominic `` dom '' scott ( 15 may 1979 ) is an irish guitarist . he is known for being the guitarist of the rock band keane . in 1995 he asked his friend tim rice-oxley to form a band . they named the band `` the lotus eaters '' . they invited richard hughes to play the drums . in 1997 rice-oxley asked scott and hughes to let tom chaplin join the band . scott and hughes firstly refused but then accepted . he left in 2001 , after the single `` wolf at the door '' because of musical differences with rice-oxley . his exit was friendly . keane wrote a message on their official page on 14 november 2001 saying this : rice-oxley also said in an interview in mexico : `` dom is a great fan of u2 so maybe we 'll met in new york '' ( because keane was the opening band for u2 in 2005 ) .
