Samobor     samobor is a city in zagreb county , croatia , population 36,206 it is west of zagreb , between the eastern slopes of samoborsko gorje ( samobor hills , the eastern part of the Å¾umberak mountains ) , in the sava river valley . it is part of the zagreb metropolitan area . samobor has been there since 1242 , according to a document of endowment by king bela iv . it is one of the earliest tourist resorts in the region , with the first tourist facilities dating back to 1810 , oriented towards anglers , hunters and hikers .
