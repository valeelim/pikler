Park     a park is an open place in a city or town for nature . there are often trees , benches , statues , and ponds in a park . some parks built in big cities are very large . these can have fountains or picnic areas . sometimes , countries or regions set up `` national parks '' . these are usually large areas of land . those areas are made to protect the habitats of animals . very often , a special permit is necessary to enter . building things in the park is usually forbidden , or needs a special permit . an example of a national park is yellowstone national park .
