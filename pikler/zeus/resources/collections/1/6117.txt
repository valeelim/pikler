Chelmsford     chelmsford is the only city in the county of essex , england . it is 32 miles northeast of london , about halfway to colchester . it is nearly in the centre of the county and it is the county town of essex , but it is not the biggest or the oldest town in the county . it is the centre of the `` borough '' of chelmsford , which is a bigger place than the city . chelmsford has the smallest cathedral in england . john dee , who wrote the english version of `` euclid '' , went to the cathedral school in the sixteenth century . chelmsford is also home to some of the anglia ruskin university campuses . the number of people living in the borough is about 170,000 ; about 115,000 of those live in the city . about 20,000 people from chelmsford travel to london every weekday . another 20,000 people work in chelmsford who live outside the city . this makes chelmsford the busiest `` ordinary '' railway station in england ( not including junctions and terminuses ) . ( the busiest of all is clapham junction railway station ) .
