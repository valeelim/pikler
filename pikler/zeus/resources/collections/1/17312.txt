52 BC     year 52 bc was a year of the pre-julian roman calendar . at the time , it was known as the year of the consulship of pompeius and scipio ( or , less frequently , year 702 `` ab urbe condita '' ) . this year has been called 52 bc since the early medieval period , when the anno domini calendar era became the most common method in europe for naming years . events . - julius caesar conquers paris .
