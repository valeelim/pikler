Celtic     the words celt and celtic can have many meanings . pronounced /kel- ( tik ) / : - celt , either ancient or modern celts - celtic languages , descending from the proto-celtic language , spoken by these people and their modern descendants - celtic mythology - celtici , celts from ancient lusitania ( which is now portugal ) . ( see also celtiberians for a celtic people on the iberian peninsula ) - irish music , folk music that started in `` celtic '' cultures . - celtic art , art from the `` celtic '' culture - celtic christianity - celtic calendar the following should only be pronounced /selt ( ik ) / : - boston celtics professional basketball team - celtic f.c . a football team in glasgow
