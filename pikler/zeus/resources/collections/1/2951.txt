1960s     the 1960s was the decade that started on january 1 , 1960 and ended on december 31 , 1969. many things happened in the sixties , including the space race , the civil rights movement , and the vietnam war . the 1960s term also refers to an era more often called the sixties . this was a set of cultural and political trends around the globe . this `` cultural decade '' is loosely defined as beginning around 1963 and ending around 1974. the social revolution of the 1960s was part of a wider counterculture . old ways were changed , new ways taken up . typical was the introduction of the birth control pill , and its effect on sexual activity , widespread use of certain drugs and a general disrespect for traditional ways . events . - decolonization of much of africa - 1961 – berlin wall built - 1961 – bay of pigs invasion - 1962 – cuban missile crisis - 1963 – assassination of john f. kennedy on november 22 - 1965 – india invades pakistan on september 6 - 1969 – under the apollo 11 program neil armstrong and buzz aldrin become the first humans on the moon
