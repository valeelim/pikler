Salat     ṣalāt ( , `` '' ) is the practice of prayer in islam . it is one of the five most important duties for muslims . to perform salat , a person 's mind must be in a state of ritual purity , which comes from ritual ablution , `` ( wuḍūʾ ) '' . for sunni muslims , salat must be performed at five periods each day . these periods are measured according to the movement of the sun . the call to prayer is essential before every obligatory prayers . the holy prophet said : `` the one who responds to azan out of faith and seeking allah 's pleasure will be admitted to paradise .
