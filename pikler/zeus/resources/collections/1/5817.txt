Boat     a boat is a vehicle used to travel on water . it is smaller than a ship and can be lifted out of the water and carried on a ship . some boats have sails , some are powered by rowing with oars , and some use motors . these boats are usually made of wood . however , some parts are made of metals like steel and aluminium . expensive boats may have parts from fiberglass or composite materials and some even have helicopter pads . there are some boats that can even go underwater . they are called submarines . the pesse canoe is likely the oldest boat recovered to date as it was built sometime around 8,000 bce
