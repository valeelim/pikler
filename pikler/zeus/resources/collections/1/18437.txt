Radium     radium is a chemical element with the atomic number 88 and symbol ra on the periodic table . it was discovered by marie curie and pierre curie in the form of radium chloride in 1898. it is an almost pure-white alkaline earth metal , but when it is exposed to oxygen , it turns black quickly . all isotopes of radium are radioactive , and it glows faint blue because of this . it 's used in several things , like luminous watches , which are now banned because they can cause radiation poisoning . this was caused because of a law suit that involved 5 women ( radium girls ) that suffered from many disabilities because of the prolonged exposure they had to the element radium . it can also be used in healing cancer .
