Berkshire     berkshire is a county in southeast england . it is west of london . its county town is reading . in the past , a town named abingdon was the county town , but this town is not in berkshire any more . berkshire is also known as the royal county of berkshire . cities , towns and villages . - bracknell - datchet - caversham - hungerford - maidenhead - newbury - reading - slough - windsor , home of windsor castle , and which gives its name to the house of windsor - wokingham the towns below are not part of berkshire now , but were in the past . - abingdon - didcot - wallingford - wantage - faringdon
