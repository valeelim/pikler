Space Oddity     david bowie 's first big hit record was the single `` space oddity '' in 1969. this album though only really sold well when he became more famous in 1973. many of the songs are very melodic , with good tunes , and are unusual . the songs on this album are : 1. space oddity 1. unwashed and somewhat slightly dazed 1. letter to hermione 1. cygnet committee 1. janine 1. an occasional dream 1. wild eyed boy from freecloud 1. god knows i 'm good 1. memory of a free festival
