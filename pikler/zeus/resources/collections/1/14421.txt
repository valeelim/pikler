Piedmont     piedmont is a region in the northern part of italy . the capital is turin . piedmont has 8 provinces within it . in the north of piedmont there is the country of switzerland and the italian region of valle d'aosta , in the east there are the regions of lombardy and emilia-romagna , in the west there is france and in the south liguria . piedmont was part of the duchy of savoy until 1860 when the duke of savoy became king of italy . piedmont is divided into 8 provinces : 1. alessandria 1. asti 1. biella 1. cuneo 1. novara 1. turin ( torino ) 1. verbano-cusio-ossola 1. vercelli other websites . - official site ( it )
