U.S. 1st Infantry Division     the 1st infantry division of the united states army—nicknamed the `` big red one '' after its shoulder patch—is the oldest division . since the division was made , it has served in almost all wars the american army has been in . the division 's official motto is `` duty first '' . their unofficial motto is `` no mission too difficult , no sacrifice too great '' . wars/fighting this division has served in . - world war i - world war ii - korean war - vietnam war - first gulf war - bosnia/kosovo - 2003 invasion of iraq
