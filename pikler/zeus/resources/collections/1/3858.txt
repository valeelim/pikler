Spade     in gardening , a spade is a tool for digging . it is similar to a shovel . it has a straight handle . in some countries such as australia the difference between a spade and a shovel is important . it is hard to move gravel with a spade but a proper shovel can do it . in games , a spade is one of the four shapes on playing cards . it is coloured black and not the one that looks like cross .
