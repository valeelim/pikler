Taumatawhakatangihangakoauauotamateapokaiwhenuakitanatahu     taumatawhakatangihangakoauauotamateaturipukakapikimaungahoronukupokaiwhenuakitanatahu ( or taumatawhakatangihangakoauauotamateapokaiwhenuakitanatahu ) is the maori name of a hill in new zealand . the short form taumatawhakatangihangakoauauotamateaturipukakapikimaungahoronukupokaiwhenuakitanatahu is used in everyday talk . the hill is 305 meters high . it has eighty-five letters in its name . it is one of the longest place names in the world .
