Lamp     a lamp is a device that makes light and heat . lamps usually work with electricity , using a lightbulb . in the united states , a lamp is usually considered a desk lamp or floor lamp . other sources of light are called 'lights ' , such as streetlights , flashlights , and headlights , which in some countries are called streetlamps , torches and headlamps . before electric lamps were invented , gas lamps , oil lamps or candles were used . related pages . - compact fluorescent lamp
