Agreement     a promise or act on part of offeree indicating a willingness to be bound by terms and conditions contained in an offer . when people feel or think the same way about something , they agree . sometimes it is important to write down or make a promise to what has been agreed on . this is called an agreement . agreements are common in law and business . for example , when a person takes out a loan or hires someone to work , an agreement is usually signed so everyone understands what must be done and in what time it must be done .
