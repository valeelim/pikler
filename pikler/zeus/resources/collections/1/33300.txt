Joensuu     joensuu is a city in finland . it is the centre of the region of north karelia . joensuu has an area of 2.751,13 km² and a population of 73,076 people . 369.3 km² of the area is water . the finnish capital , helsinki is located 437 km away from joensuu . there is a university in joensuu . the municipalities of tuupovaara and kiihtelysvaara were merged with joensuu on january 1 2005. other websites . - the official joensuu page , in finnish and english
