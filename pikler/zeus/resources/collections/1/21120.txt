Culinary mustard     culinary mustard is a condiment people use when making food . it comes from the seeds of the mustard plant . mustard is prepared by grinding the seeds of the mustard plant , and mixing them with water or vinegar . depending on which seeds are taken , and/or how much vinegar is added , the mustard may turn out hotter or less hot . most mustard is made available as a bottled condiment and can be spread on a slice of sandwich bread or squirted onto a hotdog along with ketchup , horseradish and other spices . it is also commonly found in most fast-food restaurants in the form of a small packet which the diner may open and squeeze into the dish of choice , but it is usually already included in the average hamburger bun . related pages . - condiment - ketchup - spice
