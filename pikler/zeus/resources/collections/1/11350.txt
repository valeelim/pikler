Faya-Largeau     faya-largeau is the largest city in northern chad . 9,867 people were living there in 1993. it is the capital of bourkou-ennedi-tibesti prefecture . the town was first called faya , then it was renamed largeau after chad became a colony of france in 1900. when chad won independence from france in 1960 , the town became called faya-largeau .
