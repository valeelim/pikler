Mark Addy     mark addy ( born january 14 , 1964 , in york , england ) is an english actor . the best known works by addy are as detective boyle in the british sitcom `` the thin blue line '' , dave in the movie `` the full monty '' , father bill miller in the us sitcom `` still standing '' and more recently as king robert baratheon in the hbo fantasy epic `` game of thrones '' . addy has played many different roles in both television and in movies . in 2013 he played hercules , a medieval king , and a modern day alcoholic with family problems . addy received the screen actors guild award for outstanding performance by a cast in a motion picture for `` the full monty '' . he was also nominated for a bafta , chlotrudis award and satellite award . for `` game of thrones '' he was nominated for a scream award for best ensemble and an ensemble screen actors guild award .
