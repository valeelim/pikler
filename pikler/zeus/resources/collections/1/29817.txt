Semele     semele is a person in greek mythology . she is the daughter of cadmus and harmonia . she has a child with zeus called dionysus , who grows up to be a god . her family do not believe her that zeus is the father . when hera , zeus 's wife , finds out he has another woman pregnant , she tries to destroy a pregnant semele . zeus rescues dionysus , but semele is destroyed . her family believe that zeus has killed her as a punishment for lying about him being the father .
