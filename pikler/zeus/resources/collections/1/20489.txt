Pinocchio     pinocchio is a fictional character from the 1883 book `` the adventures of pinocchio '' by carlo collodi . pinocchio is a puppet , made by wood carver , gepetto . when he tells lies , his nose gets longer . pinocchio learns about being a real life boy and wants to be a real human being instead of a puppet made of wood and strings . the most well-known version of the story is the 1940 animated disney movie `` pinocchio '' .
