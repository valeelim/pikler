Million     one million ( 1,000,000 ) or one thousand thousand , is the natural number after 999,999 and before 1,000,001. the name is derived from italian , where mille was 1,000 , and 1,000,000 became milione , `` a large thousand '' . in scientific notation , it is written as 1Ã—10 or just 10. physical quantities can also be expressed using the si prefix mega- , when dealing with si units . for example , 1 megawatt equals 1,000,000 watts . the word `` million '' is common to the short scale and long scale numbering systems , unlike the larger numbers , which have different names in the two systems . the million is sometimes used in the english language as a metaphor for a very large number , as in `` never in a million years '' and `` you 're one in a million '' , or a hyperbole , as in `` i 've walked a million miles '' . il milione is the title of marco polo 's narration of his travel to china . the name is supposed to come from polo 's nickname after his tales of riches and multitudes . examples . - a millionaire is someone who owns at least one million units of currency .
