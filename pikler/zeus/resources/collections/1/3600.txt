Soviet Union     the soviet union ( short for union of soviet socialist republics or ussr ) was a single-party marxist-leninist state , led by the communist party . it existed from 1922 until 1991 , and was the first country to declare itself socialist and build towards a communist society . it was a union of 14 soviet socialist republics and 1 soviet federative socialist republic ( soviet russia ) . soviet russia was created in 1917 after vladimir lenin led the overthrow of alexander kerensky . soviet russia joined armenia , azerbaijan , belarus , georgia , and ukraine in 1922 to form the soviet union . the communist government developed industry and over time became a major , powerful union . the largest country in the union was russia , and kazakhstan was the second . the capital city of soviet russia and the union was moscow . although technically countries could become independent if they wanted , in practice it was a completely centralized government , with no states ' rights for the member countries . many believe that the soviet union was the final stage of the russian empire ; since the ussr covered most of the land of the former empire .
