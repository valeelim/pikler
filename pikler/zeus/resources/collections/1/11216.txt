Václav Klaus     václav klaus is a czech economist and politician who the president of czech republic from 2003 to 2013. he also served was the second and last prime minister of the czech rebublic . his presidency was very controversial , because of his opinion on global warming and because of amnesty he declared . klaus was born in prague during the nazi occupation and grew up in the vinohrady neighborhood . klaus studied `` economics of foreign trade '' and graduated from university of economics in prague in 1963. he also spent some time at universities in italy ( 1966 ) and at cornell university . he also studied in the united states in 1969. he soon obtained a position in the czechoslovak state bank . in 1987 , klaus joined the institute for prognostics of the czechoslovak academy of sciences . in 1995 , as prime minister , klaus was awarded the title of professor of finance , so he is sometimes addressed as `` mr. professor '' . since that time klaus occasionally teaches seminars in economics at the university of economics .
