Hallucination     a hallucination is seeing , hearing , tasting , smelling or feeling things that do not really exist . usually people have to be awake and conscious to have hallucinations . that way , a person who sees or hears something that is not really there has a hallucination . hallucinations are different from dreams . when people dream they are not awake . hallucinations are also different from illusions . illusions are based on real perceptions , which are distorted or interpreted in a wrong way . certain drugs can cause hallucinations . some illegal drugs are taken because they cause hallucinations . certain mental illnesses can also cause hallucinations . in some cases , withdrawal of a drug can also cause hallucinations . this has especially been reported by people trying to stop taking sleeping pills , or by alcoholics . people who have hallucinations are not necessarily ill , lack of sleep can also cause hallucinations . hallucinations just before going to sleep or just after waking up are considered normal .
