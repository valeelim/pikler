The Last Supper     the last supper is a painting by leonardo da vinci . it is a picture of the last supper of jesus and his disciples . it shows how they reacted when jesus said that one of them would betray him . each disciple has their own expression showing their own reaction to what jesus has just told them . the painting is a mural on the wall of the santa maria delle grazie rectory in the dining room in milan , italy . it measures 460 x 880 cm . da vinci began painting it in 1495 and finished it in 1498 , but did not work on it all the time between these years . some people think the painting has secret messages .
