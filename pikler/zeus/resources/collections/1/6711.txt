Princess     a princess is a female member of a royal family , especially a daughter of a monarch . a son of a monarch is called a prince . in some cases , a woman can become a princess by marrying a king . a princess may also become a ruler by heredity or by marriage . sometimes the expression `` princess '' is used when a female has noble qualities such as kindness , charity , and virtue . walt disney created the disney princess . cinderella was the most famous princess in the world . princesses wear crowns on their head . the mermaid princess is ariel . ariel lives in the ocean .
