Alkmene     alkmene is a person in greek mythology . she was the daughter of king elektryon of mycenae and his wife anaxo . she was the wife of amphitryon . she is the mother of herakles and iphikles . once when amphitryon was not at home , the god zeus , looking like amphitryon , seduced alkmene . alkmene gave birth to herakles , who was the son of zeus , and iphikles , who was the son of alkmene 's husband amphitryon . zeus wanted to kill her but hercules only being 1 day old fought back and saved his mother
