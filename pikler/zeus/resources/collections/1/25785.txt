South Georgia and the South Sandwich Islands     south georgia and south sandwich islands are an overseas territory of the united kingdom claimed by argentina . they are in the southern part of the atlantic ocean . the territory was started in 1985 , before that it was classed as part of the falkland islands . the islands do not have a native population , but in 2006 they had around 20 people on the islands . most of these people were scientists who are at bird island , museum staff at grytviken and the british government officer .
