Afrikaner people     afrikaners are white south africans who speak afrikaans as mother tongue and follow the afrikaans culture . most of them have dutch , german , french huguenot , ancestors . also known as boere , voortrekkers and burgers , although under slightly different contexts . in south africa there are about 3 million white people with afrikaans as mother tongue , that can be assumed to be afrikaners if they chose to follow the afrikaans culture .
