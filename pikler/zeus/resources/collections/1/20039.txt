Crusinallo     crusinallo , with 4000 people , is the most populated district of the town of omegna ( in the province of verbano-cusio-ossola , in piedmont , italy ) . it was an independent town until 1928. the town of crusinallo is located in the north part of the town of omegna , at the border with the towns of casale corte cerro , gravellona toce , and germagno . the natural borders are : at north , the saint martino river ; at east and south , the strona river ; at west , the forest of alpe colla .
