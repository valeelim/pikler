Copy     copy means a thing that is made to look like another thing . - copying , making a copy , the duplication of information - photocopying , a process which makes paper copies of documents and other visual images - fax , a telecommunications machine used to transfer copies of documents over the telephone network - facsimile , a copy or reproduction that is as true to the original source as possible - replica , a copy closely resembling the original concerning its shape and appearance - copy ( written ) , commonly used in newsrooms and publications to refer to a writer 's written content
