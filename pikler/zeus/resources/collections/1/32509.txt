Jurong     jurong ( 裕廊 ) is a town in the western side of singapore . it used to be a swamp until the 1960s , when goh keng swee , a minister , together with the united nations , cleared the swamps , making jurong an industrial estate ( a place where industries are built to make goods ) . jurong is divided in jurong east , where houses and shops are built and jurong west , mainly an industrial estate . jurong east is under the jurong group representation constituency or jurong grc , while jurong west is under the west coast group representation constituency . jurong is connected to johor bahru by the second-link at tuas . jurong has the jurong bird park and chinese gardens .
