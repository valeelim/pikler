Penjing     penjing ( 盆景 pinyin : pén jǐng , literally tray scenery ) is the ancient chinese art of growing trees and plants in containers . the plants are kept small by skilled pruning . penjing is similar to the japanese art of bonsai . the first historical reference to `` penjing '' is from the 9th century . related pages . - bonsai - chinese scholar 's rocks - suiseki
