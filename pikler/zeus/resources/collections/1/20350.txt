Yorkshire Terrier     the yorkshire terrier or yorkie is a small breed of terrier dog . it originated in the united kingdom in the town of yorkshire , from where it gets its name . they were first bred to catch mice and rats , so they are often fast and energetic . yorkies usually weigh about 7 pounds ( 3.18 kg ) and have a dark gray or black coat with a brown face . according to the american kennel club ( akc ) , yorkies are the second most popular dog breed in america , behind only the labrador retriever . they are really playfull , they will always want to play .
