The Jam     the jam was an english rock band formed in woking , surrey in 1972. the members were paul weller ( guitar , vocals ) , bruce foxton ( bass guitar , vocals ) , and rick buckler ( drums ) . having come out of from the short punk movement they went on to become one of the uk 's top bands . they had four number one hits which were going underground , start ! , town called malice and beat surrender . the jam disbanded in 1982. lead singer paul weller went on to have hits with the style council during the remainder of the 1980s . during the 1990s he had a successful solo career .
