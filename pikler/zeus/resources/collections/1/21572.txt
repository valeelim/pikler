Meghalaya     meghalaya ( ) is a state within the republic of india . occupying an area of 8,660 mi² ( 22,429 km² ) , bigger than the state of israel but smaller than belize . its capital is shillong . meghalaya 's population is 2,318,822. the population of shillong was 314,610 in 2001. there are problems in meghalaya arise from the divide between tribals and non tribal settlers , identity issues and growing corruption besides the fear of being reduced to minority by native tribals . the activity status is classified as active . in traditional indian geography it falls under the north-east indian zone . other websites . - meghalaya website
