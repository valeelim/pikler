Number theory     number theory is a part of mathematics . it explains what some types of numbers are , what properties they have , and ways that they can be useful . topics in number theory are : - prime numbers - factorization - matrix important theorems in number theory are : - chinese remainder theorem - fundamental theorem of arithmetic - fermat 's last theorem - fermat 's little theorem a well-known application of number theory is encrypted messaging ( encryption ) . data compression also makes use of the field .
