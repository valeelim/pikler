Mi Reflejo     mi reflejo is the first spanish-language album by american pop singer christina aguilera . it was released on 2000. there are 11 songs in this album . six of them are new songs . five of them are from her first album `` christina aguilera '' but in spanish . this album was nominated grammy award as best latin pop album in 2001. it was the latin grammy award for best female pop vocal album .
