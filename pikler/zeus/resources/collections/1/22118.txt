Łódź    łódź łódź ( pronounced like this , `` whootch '' ) is the third-largest city in poland , with 776,000 inhabitants . it is in the łódź voivodeship in the centre of the country . in polish the name also means `` boat '' . the mayor of łódź is hanna zdanowska . the first documents mentioning the city date from 1332. łódź was awarded city rights ( according to the magdeburg law ) in 1432. the congress of vienna decided in 1815 that the city should be part of russia . only in 1918 did the city fall back to poland . the city is about 120 km south-west of warsaw . in and around the city , there are some textile and electronics industries . because of prolonged economic difficulties , there has been a decline in the population of the city . the city has a university and a film academy . łódź has a humid continental climate ( `` dfb '' in the koeppen climate classification ) . there are two major football teams in łódź : the first is called łks łódź and the second is widzew łódź . notable residents . - grażyna bacewicz , composer
