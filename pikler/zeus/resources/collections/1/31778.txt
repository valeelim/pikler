United States Department of the Navy     the united states department of the navy was created by the united states congress on april 30 , 1798. it was created in order to give civilian leadership and administration to the united states navy and the united states marine corps . the secretary of the navy is in charge of the department of the navy , and the department of the navy is under the authority of the department of defense . the department of the navy is in charge of recruitment , training , organizing , building , and construction for the navy and the marine corps . while the navy and the marine corps both is under the department of the navy , they are two separate branches of the military .
