Marker     a marker is a writing device that allows a person to draw , write , or scribble . ink comes out of the tip of the marker and onto the writing surface , commonly a piece of paper . markers generally come in a variety of different colors . they are sometimes referred to as `` magic markers '' , or magic pens . the tip of the marker where ink comes out from is usually made of cloth . this allows the ink to go through easily . a lot of ink can come out from it at one time . marker types . - whiteboard markers are marker pens in which the ink can be rubbed out easily with tissues or dusters . - permanent markers are markers which the ink can not be rubbed off .
