Port Moresby     port moresby ( 9°30′s 147°12′e ) is the capital city and the main city of papua new guinea . in 2000 , 255,000 people lived there . the city is on the shores of the gulf of papua , on the southeastern coast of the island of new guinea . the area on which the city was built was first sighted by a european in 1873 by captain john moresby . it was named in honor of his father admiral sir fairfax moresby .
