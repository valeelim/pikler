South Vietnam     south vietnam was a country . it existed from 1955 to 1976. it was in the south of what is now vietnam . the country was south of the local demilitarized zone , and north vietnam was to the north of it . as the republic of vietnam , it was ruled first by the unpopular ngo dinh diem ( ) and then by a military dictatorship . the division of vietnam took place during the geneva conference of 1954. in 1975 , north vietnam took over this country and renamed its largest city , saigon , to ho chi minh city .
