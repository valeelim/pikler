Cuenca     cuenca is a spanish city , capital of the cuenca province , in castile-la mancha . it is a small city with 50,000 inhabitants . it is very famous for its monuments : the hanging houses , the cathedral and other churches . it is situated over the jucar river , near the serrania of cuenca . other websites . - webcam in a side street - cuenca - a stroll through la mancha by `` the guardian ''
