Trolley     a trolley is a small vehicle with wheels that can carry things . people can push trolleys . there are different kind of trolleys . there are shopping trolleys which are used in supermarkets and other large stores with self-service . in american english these are called shopping carts . customers can put things they want to buy in the trolley and then pay for everything at the checkout . usually they are then allowed to wheel the trolley with the things they have bought out to their car . sometimes the customers have to pay a small deposit when collecting a trolley . the trolleys are chained together . the customer puts in a coin ( in britain a Â£1 coin ) which he can have back again when he parks the trolley back in the trolley chain . trolleys are used in airports and some large railway stations for passengers to carry their luggage . deposits are not refundable . a trolley can also be a tea-trolley . this is a small trolley used traditionally in the house for plates , cups , saucers and sandwiches and cakes for afternoon tea . tea-trolleys are also used in hospitals and offices to take cups of tea and coffee to people .
