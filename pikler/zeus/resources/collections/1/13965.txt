Cracker     a cracker is a thin and crispy piece of baked bread . crackers can be eaten by themselves , but they can also be eaten with things on them called toppings . common toppings include cheese , peanut butter , and sliced meats . crackers are most often eaten as a snack , or crumbled into soup . it was 1792 when theodore pearson of newburyport , massachusetts , made a cracker-like bread that was made from only water and flour which he called `` pearson 's pilot bread . '' this was the first cracker bakery in the united states , and made crackers for more than a century .
