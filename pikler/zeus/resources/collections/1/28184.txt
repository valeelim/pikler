Lutherstadt Wittenberg     wittenberg is a town in germany . it lies in the eastern part of saxony-anhalt . wittenberg became one of the most important german towns of political , cultural , historical , and artistic advances in the 16th century . the famous reformation places in the old town , and the buildings developed in different epochs , make up the architecture of the town . many people travel to wittenberg because of its past , and its connection to martin luther who worked there . wittenberg has about 46,000 inhabitants . its geographical coordinates are 51° 52 ’ n , 12° 39 ’ e .
