Candleshoe     candleshoe is a 1977 walt disney productions live action movie . it stars jodie foster as casey brown , helen hayes as lady st. edmund ( in her last screen appearance ) , david niven as priory ( as well as colonel dennis and mr. gipping ) , and leo mckern as harry bundage . candleshoe is loosely based on the michael innes novel `` christmas at candleshoe '' .
