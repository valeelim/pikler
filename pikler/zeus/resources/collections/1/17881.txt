Pool     the word pool refers to many different things : - a pool is a body of water . it is also called a pond . it can also mean a body of water that people have made . this is called a swimming pool . - pool is also a game . it is a form of billiards . in pool you must hit all your colour balls in one of the six holes then the black ball in to win . - people also use the word `` pool '' to mean something many people have contributed to . people can pool their money to buy something , or make a bet . sometimes it can mean cooperation , people working together to get something done .
