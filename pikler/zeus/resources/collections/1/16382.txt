Spring     spring is the season after winter and before summer . days become longer and weather gets warmer in the temperate zone because the earth tilts towards the sun . in many parts of the world plants grow and flowers bloom . often people with hay fever suffer more , because of the allergens . many animals have their breeding seasons in spring . in many parts of the world it rains for hours . this helps the plants grow and the flowers bloom . at the start of spring , people suffering from seasonal affective disorder will feel better . hay fever , however , often becomes worse . spring break is a vacation period in early spring at universities and schools in various countries in the world . holidays celebrated in spring include passover and easter .
