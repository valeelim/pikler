Cum     cum can mean different things : - cum is a slang term for semen - the verb form cum is a slang term for the act of ejaculation , or for having an orgasm - cum or `` kum '' is a russian word that means `` godfather of one 's child '' or `` father of one 's godchild '' . - cum is a latin word meaning `` with '' and is often used in academic latin honors , such a cum laude meaning `` with distinction '' . the modern spanish con is a corruption of the latin original .
