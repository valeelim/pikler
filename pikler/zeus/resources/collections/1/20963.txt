Sexual slavery     sexual slavery is a special case of slavery or forced labour . sexual slaves are like normal slaves , except for the following : 1. they may have been forced into prostitution 1. the people are slaves , forced to do non-sexual labour . however , sexual relations with the slaves may be permitted , or even common . 1. they may be forced into sexual slavery with only one master . often the fact that they really are slaves is hidden through marriages . this term is also used by the vienna declaration for the women 's rights and by the name of humanitarian international law .
