Molokai     molokai ( or moloka ªi ) is the fifth largest island in the u.s. hawaiian islands . the island is 38 miles long and 10 miles across . its land area is 261 square miles . the highest mountain is named kamakou , and it is 4,970 feet ( 1,514 meters ) high . 'moloka'i is nicknamed `` the friendly isle '' . it is one of the least developed of the hawaiian islands . the only big town is named kaunakakai , which is also the main or chief port on the island . the airport is on west moloka'i . also on the island is kalaupapa , which is a place for people who have a diease called leprosy . moloka'i has many hawaiian fish ponds along its south shore . many of these have been cleaned and fixed .
