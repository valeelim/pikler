The Phantom of the Opera     the phantom of the opera ( ) is a french novel by gaston leroux . it was published in 1910. an english edition came out a year later . the book was adapted to a musical by andrew lloyd webber in 1986. the musical was made into a movie in 2004. movie versions include one starring lon chaney in 1925 , one from 1943 with claude rains , another from 1962 with herbert lom , and `` the phantom of the paradise '' ( 1974 ) . the story has been adapted to made for television movies several times .
