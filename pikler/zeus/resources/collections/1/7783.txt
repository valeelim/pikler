Brighton     brighton is a city on the south coast of england , once called `` brighthelmstone '' . it is in the county of east sussex . in 2000 , it joined hove to become the city of brighton & hove . historically , brighton forms the main part of the brighton/worthing/littlehampton conurbation , with 474,485 inhabitants ( 2011 census ) . this is england 's 12th largest conurbation , and the mostly densely populated area outside london . from the 1730s , brighton entered its second phase of developmentâ€”one which brought a rapid improvement in its fortunes . the fad for bathing in seawater as a cure for illnesses was encouraged . from the 1760s it was a boarding point for boats travelling to france . road transport to london was improved when the main road via crawley was turnpiked in 1770. spas and indoor baths were opened by physicians . from 1780 , development of georgian terraces started , and the fishing village developed to the fashionable resort of brighton . growth of the town was encouraged by the patronage of the prince regent ( later king george iv ) after his first visit in 1783. he spent much of his leisure time in the town , and had the royal pavilion built during the early part of his regency .
