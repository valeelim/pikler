Anguilla     anguilla is a group of five islands in the caribbean sea . the islands are ruled by great britain . anguilla was once part of what is now saint kitts and nevis , but broke away in 1980. anguilla wanted to still be a part of great britain , and saint kitts and nevis wanted to be independent . it is named after the spanish word for `` eel '' , because it has such a shape . the queen of the united kingdom is the ruler of anguilla , but she works through a governor . she names , or `` appoints '' the governor , and he is just working there in her place . the legislature is elected by the people , has 11 members , and is called the house of assembly . anguilla gets most of its money from fishing and tourism . most people in anguilla are protestants , and speak english .
