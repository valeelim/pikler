Church of the SubGenius     the church of the subgenius is a religion founded by ivan stang and philo drummond . originally based in dallas , texas , it is now based in cleveland heights , ohio . in 2009 , stang said that the church of the subgenius has 40,000 followers , but the real number of followers is not known . the church 's beliefs are said to be very complex . the church teaches that it was founded by a salesman named j. r. `` bob '' dobbs . in 1953 `` bob '' was fixing his tv which he made , and the space god jhvh-1 gave him knowledge of the past and future , and the idea of `` slack '' . the church teaches that there is a conspiracy to make humans work , so that they ca n't have `` slack '' . it says that its members are descended from yetis . it also said that aliens would come to earth on 5 july 1998. other websites . - church of the subgenius official website
