Inuktitut     inuktitut is a language of the arctic , spoken by inuits in canada and in greenland . inuktitut is a very complex language . it is an official language in nunavut and the northwest territories . the inuit write inuktitut in two ways . one way to write inuktitut is by using the roman alphabet . the other way to write inuktitut is by using an abugida , which is a kind of alphabet which has letters based on syllables . the uses a small part of the unified canadian aboriginal syllabics , a set of letters made up for writing down many of the languages of the first nations people in canada . some words in english come from inuktitut or another inuit language . among them are the words anorak , igloo , and kayak .
