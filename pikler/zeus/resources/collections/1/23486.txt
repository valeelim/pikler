John le Carré     john le carré ( born 19 october 1931 ) is an english novelist . he was born in poole , dorset . he is the writer of many spy novels . the name is a pseudonym . his real name is david john moore cornwell . le carré graduated from lincoln college , oxford with a bachelor of arts degree . other websites . - john le carré homepage - john le carré biography on books and writers - 1989 npr interview of le carré
