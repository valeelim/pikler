Ulster     ulster is one of the four historical provinces which make up the island of ireland . the others are leinster , munster and connacht . there are nine counties in ulster . three are in the republic of ireland . the other six counties make up all of northern ireland , part of the united kingdom . the name `` ulster '' is also sometimes used to mean `` northern ireland '' , even though northern ireland is only part of ulster . the counties are : in the republic of ireland : - county cavan - county donegal - county monaghan in northern ireland : - county antrim - county armagh - county down - county fermanagh - county londonderry - county tyrone
