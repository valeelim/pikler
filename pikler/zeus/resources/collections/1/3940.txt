Flavor     flavour or flavor ( us english ) is the quality of anything which affects the taste . when someone remarks on the `` flavor '' of something they are trying to describe the specific way something tastes . flavor can also be used to describe the act of adding a taste alteration ( to change the taste ) to something . this is usually done by adding spices or sugars , though in `` processed food '' there are oftentimes artificial ( fake ) flavors .
