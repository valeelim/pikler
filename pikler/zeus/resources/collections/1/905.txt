World Wide Web     the world wide web ( `` www '' or `` the web '' ) is the part of the internet that contains websites and webpages . it was invented in 1989 by tim berners-lee at cern , geneva , switzerland . sir tim berners-lee created a new markup language called html . websites are composed of pages linked by hypertext links . they are all written in html . the world wide web is used to describe html webpages that are part of the internet . in order to see the world wide web one needs a computer , and a modem . one also needs a connection to the internet . many companies nowadays offer limited website hosting allowing one to make websites that can be displayed on the world wide web like any other domain ( www.stuff.com ) site . these sites usually make money from advertisements instead of fees . related pages . - minitel
