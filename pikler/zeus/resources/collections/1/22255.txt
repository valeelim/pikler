Calisthenics     calisthenics is a series of exercises used to develop strength , power and balance or a repeated rhythmical exercise that uses the weight of the body as resistance or the systematic exercises for attaining strength-gracefulness or the practice of calisthenic exercises . the name calisthenics is greek in origin , a combination of the words 'beauty ' and 'strength ' . calisthenics is recommended for general good health , light exercises designed to promote general physical fitness . related pages . - equilibrioception - flexibility training - jogging - training - weight training
