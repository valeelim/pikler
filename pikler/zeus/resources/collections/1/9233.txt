Popeye     popeye the sailor is one of the most popular cartoon characters of all time . he first appeared in a comic strip called `` thimble theatre '' by ec segar in 1929. popeye is a sailor who smokes a pipe and mutters things under his breath . he has only one eye , he is short and uneducated , but when trouble occurs , usually saving his girlfriend , olive oyl from his arch-nemesis , bluto/brutus , he eats a can of spinach , and gets super-strength . he also appeared in a series of cartoons made for movie theatres by paramount pictures .
