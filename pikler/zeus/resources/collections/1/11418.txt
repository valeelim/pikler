Paraguay     paraguay , officially the republic of paraguay ( ) , is a small country in south america . it is landlocked , meaning that it does not touch the ocean . it is bordered to the north and east by brazil , the west by bolivia , and the south and southeast by argentina . the main languages are spanish and guaraní . paraguay 's population is about 6 million . the ethnic makeup of the population is like the following : mestizo ( mixed european and amerindian ) 80 % , european 20 % , unmixed amerindian 1-3 % , asian 1-4 % . over ( 20 % ) of the population lives below the poverty line . paraguay has two official languages , spanish and guarani . guaraní is recognized as a national language . the capital city is asuncion . asuncion was founded in 1537 by a man from spain named juan de salazar . paraguay was a colony of spain . paraguay became independent from spain in 1811. from 1865-1870 paraguay lost land and most of its male population in the war of the triple alliance against brazil , argentina , and uruguay . it gained land in the chaco war against bolivia in the 1930s .
