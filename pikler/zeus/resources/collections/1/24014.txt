Eschelbronn     eschelbronn is a village with 2597 people living there . it is in the rhein-neckar area of baden-w√ºrttemberg , germany and close to sinsheim . eschelbronn is in the north of kraichgau with a distance of about 25 km to heidelberg . it was already mentioned in the years 788/789 in a document of the monastery of lorsch . afterwards there was no documentation until the end of the 13th century when it became part of the restrict of speyer . the first mayor was heinrich von eschelbrunnen in the year 1261. in 1267 a castle made of wood was built and later rebuilt into a water-castle of stone in 1375. in 1526 the village became protestant . in 1803 eschelbronn became part of baden . from 1807 on the village was administered by waibstadt and 1803 by sinsheim until the 31st of december 1972. today it belongs to the rhein-neckar area . in former times most of the people were farmers , however later in the 18th century the production of textiles became more and more important . since the end of the 19th century eschelbronn is well known for its furniture production .
