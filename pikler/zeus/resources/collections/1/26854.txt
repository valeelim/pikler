Ares     ares is a god in greek mythology . he is the god of savage war and bloodlust , and represents the untamed , wild aspects of conflict . he is one of the twelve olympians . his parents are zeus and hera . he had a twin sister called eris . neither parent liked him . he is considered murderous and bloody . in the trojan war , he fought on the side of troy . he had six children with aphrodite . his homeland is thrace . related pages . - maris - etruscan mythology version of ares . - tyr - norse mythology version of ares . - mars - roman mythology version of ares . other websites . - theoi project , ares information on ares from classical literature , images from greek and roman art . - greek mythology link , ares summary of ares in myth
