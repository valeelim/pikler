Delaware     delaware is a state in the united states . it is sometimes called the `` first state '' because it was the first colony to accept the new constitution in 1787. its capital is dover and its biggest city is wilmington . the dutch first settled delaware . the swedish then took over in the mid-1600s . related pages . - colleges and universities in delaware - list of counties in delaware - list of numbered routes in delaware - list of rivers of delaware
