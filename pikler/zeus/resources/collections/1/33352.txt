Vishvamitra     vishvamitra is the name of a rishi ( sage ) of ancient india . this is a sanskrit word , and its translation is : universal friend , that is , a friend of everybody . vishvamitra wrote many verses of the vedas , especially the rig veda and the atharva veda . the hindu scriptures have many stories about vishvamitra . one of them tell about the competition between another rishi named vashistha . both wanted to become priests of big royal families of ancient india . other pages . - hindu mythology - vashistha
