Blackcurrant     blackcurrant ( `` ribes nigrum '' ) is a small , bitter-tasting fruit . it can be found in central and northern europe and northern asia . blackcurrant has a lot of vitamin c in it . blackcurrant gets its name from its dark color . it is also used to make jelly , ice cream , and cordial . blackcurrant is a popular flavor in the united kingdom . blackcurrants are also used in salads and in baking , similar to raisins .
