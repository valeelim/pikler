Snakes on a Plane     snakes on a plane ( released in japan as `` snake flight '' ( スネーク・フライト ) ) is a 2006 american action horror crime thriller movie . it was directed by david r. ellis and it stars samuel l. jackson and released to movie theaters by warner bros. it is about many snakes on a plane that is traveling from hawaii to los angeles , california . the snakes attack people on the plane , and samuel l. jackson 's character , fbi agent neville flynn becomes angry at the snakes , so he fights them . the movie became very popular before it was released because of the title and because of many people talked about it on the internet .
