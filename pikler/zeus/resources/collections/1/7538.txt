John Landis     john landis ( born august 3 , 1950 ) is an american movie director . landis was born in chicago , illinois and raised in los angeles , california . some of his movies are : - `` the kentucky fried movie '' - `` animal house '' - `` the blues brothers '' - `` an american werewolf in london '' - `` trading places '' he directed the music videos for michael jackson 's `` thriller '' and `` black or white '' . `` thriller '' won the video vanguard award for the greatest video in the history of the world . he was born into a jewish family . he started his career working as a mailboy at 20th century fox .
