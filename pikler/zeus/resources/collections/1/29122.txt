Given name     a given name is a name given to a person at birth or baptism . given names are referred as first names . it is different from a surname . a surname is the last name or family name . in most western countries the given name comes first ( before the last name or family name ) . however , in many eastern countries such as japan and china it comes after the last name or family name , and occasionally also in europe , e.g . in hungarian . christians often call their first name their `` christian name '' . related pages . - personal name - posthumous name - regnal name - indian name
