Flubber     flubber is a 1997 movie . it stars robin williams as prof. brainard . it was released on november 26 , 1997. this movie is based on the movie `` the absent minded professor '' . in `` flubber '' , prof. brainard discovers a new energy source , which he calls flubber . he then has many problems such as missing his own wedding and trying to save his job .
