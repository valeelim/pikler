Valmiki     maharishi valmiki ( , `` vālmīki '' ) ( during lord rama 's time ) is celebrated as the poet harbinger in sanskrit literature . he is the author of the epic `` ramayana '' , based on the attribution in the text of the epic itself . he is revered as the `` adi kavi , '' which means `` first poet , '' for he discovered the first `` śloka '' i.e . first `` verse '' , which set the base and defined the form to sanskrit poetry . at least by the 1st century ad , valmiki 's reputation as the father of sanskrit classical poetry seems to have been legendary . other pages . - rishi - kashyapa - atri - vashitha - vishvamitra - gautam - jamdagni - bharadvaja references . - notes - others - `` dictionary of hindu lore and legend '' ( isbn 0-500-51088-1 ) by anna dallapiccola
