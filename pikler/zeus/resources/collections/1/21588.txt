Marathi language     marathi ( `` '' ) is the language spoken in maharashtra , india . it is also maharashtra 's official language . it is at least one thousand years old and is based on sanskrit . the written script is devanāgarī - the same as one used to write hindi . people living in maharashtra and parts of neighboring states of gujarat , madhya pradesh , goa , karnataka , chattisgarh and andhra pradesh , union-territories of daman-diu and dadra nagar haveli speak marathi . other websites . - marathi news - marathimati.net - marathi language web portal
