Nut     nut or nuts can mean different things : - nut ( fruit ) , a seed or fruit inside a hard outer shell , many of which are eaten as food - a nut ( hardware ) screws onto a bolt ( fastener ) to hold two things together - the exclamation , `` nuts ! '' can be used to mean `` that is a shame '' or `` too bad '' - nuts is a slang term for the testicles and scrotum - nut is an egyptian goddess . - a nut is a slang term for someone who is crazy - to go nuts is a slang term for being out of control .
