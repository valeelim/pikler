Staind     staind is an american rock band from massachusetts , united states . the band was started in 1994. its first album , `` tormented '' was released in 1996. staind 's newest album , `` the illusion of progress '' was released on august 19 , 2008. some of its biggest hits have been `` outside '' , `` it 's been awhile '' , and `` so far away '' . in 2013 , they announced that they were going on hiatus but were not breaking up . band members . - aaron lewis - vocals/acoustic guitar - mike mushok - guitars - jon wysocki - drums - johnny april - bass guitar
