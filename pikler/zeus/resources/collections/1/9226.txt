Palmdale, California    palmdale , california palmdale is the largest city in the desert area of california . it has a population of 147,897 and is the 6th largest city in los angeles county . it is one of the fastest growing cities in the united states , currently the 25th fastest . it has a population of 589,043 if you include the suburbs around it . even though palmdale is in los angeles county , it is not connected to the larger los angeles population area . it is separated from it by a mountain range called the san gabriel mountains that are over 40 miles wide and almost 2 miles high overall . palmdale is the home of several airplanes used by nasa and the air force , giving it the nickname of `` the aerospace capital of america .
