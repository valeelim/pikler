Nicomachus     nicomachos or nicomachus is a greek name . it can mean : - nicomachus of gerasa , an ancient mathematician - nicomachus , the father of aristotle and court physician ( doctor ) to king amyntas iii of macedon . nicomachus died when aristotle was about 10. nicomachus wanted aristotle to follow in his footsteps . but aristotle did not become a doctor but a philosopher .
