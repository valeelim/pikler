Mouth     the mouth is an opening in the face is the first part of the alimentary canal ( digestive system ) . it is the place where the chewing of food occurs . the mouth has teeth to help chew the food . in addition to its primary role as the beginning of the digestive system , in humans the mouth also plays a significant role in communication . the tongue , lips , and jaw , which are parts of the mouth , are needed to produce the range of sounds included in human language . people also kiss and show their emotions with the mouth .
