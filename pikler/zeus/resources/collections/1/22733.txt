Zimbabwean dollar     the zimbabwe dollar was a currency for zimbabwe from 1980 to 2009. zimbabwe had the highest rate of inflation in the world . the rate of inflation grew to 231,150,888.87 % in july 2008. because of hyperinflation , or inflation that is out of control , the reserve bank of zimbabwe had to print banknotes with higher values to cope with the rising cost of living . in january 2009 , zimbabwe released a banknote for one hundred trillion dollars , or $ 100,000,000,000,000 ! the zimbabwe dollar had to be revalued three times due to the rising cost of living : in august 2006 , august 2008 and february 2009. in august 2006 , the first revaluation dollar was violent and too quick , meaning that many people lost their savings because they were unable to convert it into the new currency without fear of violence and corruption . since 12 april 2009 , zimbabwe stopped using the zimbabwean dollar and adopted currencies from other countries , such as the united states dollar and the euro .
