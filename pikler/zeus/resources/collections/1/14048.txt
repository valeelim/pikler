Freezing     freezing is the process when a liquid turns into a solid . freezing occurs when heat is lost from an object , which causes the molecules to slow down and form tighter bonds . one example of freezing is when water turns into ice . freezing is the opposite of melting , and two steps away from evaporation . freezing occurs at below 0 degrees celsius with water , while some other liquids become a solid at higher or lower temperatures . most liquids can freeze . it is a necessary step in casting ( metalworking ) for example . related pages . - freezing point other websites . - crystallization -citizendium
