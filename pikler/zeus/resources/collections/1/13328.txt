Jenna Bush Hager     jenna welch bush hager ( born `` jenna welch bush '' november 25 , 1981 ) is the younger of two fraternal twin girls born to george w. bush , president of the united states . her twin sister is barbara pierce bush . jenna graduated from the university of texas in 2004. she has become a public school teacher like her mother laura bush was . jenna has been married to henry chase hager since may 10 , 2008. they have one child together , a daughter named margaret laura hager ( b.april 14 , 2013 ) . other websites . - jenna bush news , blogs and photo
