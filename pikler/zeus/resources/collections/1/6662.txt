United Nations General Assembly     the united nations general assembly is made up of all united nations member states . the assembly meets once a year , which usually begins on the third tuesday in september and ends in mid-december . the first meeting was held on 10 january 1946. voting in the general assembly on important questions , for example suggestions on world peace , human rights and security , is by two-thirds of those present and voting . other questions are decided by popular vote . each member country has one vote . the current president of the united nations general assembly is peter thomson since 13 september 2016. related pages . - list of members of the united nations
