Wank     wank is a mountain in the bavarian alps , by garmisch-partenkirchen in germany , close to the austrian border . wank is 1780m tall , and has a cable car , the `` wankbahn '' , which takes people almost all the way to the top . near the peak , there is an inn called the `` wankhaus '' which was built in 1911 that allows people to spend the night .
