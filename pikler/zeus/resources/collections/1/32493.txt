Chupacabra     el chupacabra ( or el chupacabras ) is a cryptid said to live in puerto rico , mexico , chile , brazil , anaheim california . gilbert arizona . texas to florida , michigan , maine and even oregon . its name comes from the spanish translation of `` goat sucker '' , because of its habit of attacking and drinking the blood of livestock , including goats . the legend was started in 1987 when there was a huge , gruesome massacre of livestock all over . all the animals had two puncture wounds on their neck in which the blood had been drained . other websites . - chupacabra supposedly ate two humans and drained them of their blood .
