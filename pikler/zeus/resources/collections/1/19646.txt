Tsar     tsar is the title for the supreme ruler of bulgaria in 913–1422 , later usage in 1908–1946 , the supreme ruler of serbia in 1346—1371 and the emperor of russia from 1546 until 1721. since 1721 the title of russian emperors was `` imperator '' , but the word `` tsar '' remained in common use until the russian revolution 1917. it is also spelled tzar , czar , and csar , and is the eastern european word for caesar , that can be translated to emperor . the system of goverment used during the tsardom of russia and the russian empire was called tsarist autocracy , also known as tsarism . the last tsar to rule serbia was tsar stephen uroš v. the last tsar to rule russia was tsar nicholas ii . the last tsar to rule bulgaria was tsar boris iii .
