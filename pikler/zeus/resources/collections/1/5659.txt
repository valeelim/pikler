Ankara     ankara is the capital city of the country of turkey . it is in the center of anatolia . ankara is the second largest city after istanbul . the city has a population of 4,319,167 ( 2005 ) ( province 5,153,000 ) , and an elevation of 938 meters ( 3080 feet ) . it was formerly known as angora or engürü , and in roman as `` ancyra '' , and in classical and hellenistic periods as άγκυρα ánkyra . the city is also the capital of ankara province .
