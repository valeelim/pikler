Anonymity     anonymity means `` namelessness '' and comes from greek words meaning `` without name '' . it is when a person 's identity or name is unknown . if a person is called anonymous , nobody knows who he or she is , or the anonymous person does not want to be found out . the concept of anonymity has some important uses . it is vital for a democracy that votes cast in an election are anonymous . in that way , no-one can find out how a person voted in an election .
