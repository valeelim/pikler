Michelle Kwan     michelle kwan ( born july 7 , 1980 ) is an american figure skater . she has won five world championships and nine u.s. championships . michelle kwan was born on july 7 , 1980 , in torrance , california . kwan showed interest in figure stating when she followed ron and karen , her brother and sister , onto the ice , saying that all she could remember was `` every day , tying up my skates and a big smile on my face , excited to go on the ice '' . she attended public school until eighth grade , when she began being homeschooled in 1994. kwan won the world junior championship in 1994. the same year , at the age of 13 , she came in the top 10 in the world senior championship . she became world champion in 1996 , when she was 15. olympics . - world championships : first place in 1996 , 1998 , 2000 , 2001 , 2003 - olympics : 1998 - silver ; 2002 - bronze - u.s. championships : first place in 1996 , 1998 , 1999 , 2000 , 2001 , 2002 , 2003 , 2004 , 2005 other websites . - united states figure skating association ( usfsa ) athlete biography
