Lampedusa     lampedusa is the largest of the pelagie islands . sicily is 205 km to the north , tunisia is 113 km to the south . there are no sources of freshwater on the island , so those living there collect rainwater . fauna and flora , as well as the climate is similar to what can be found in north africa . there is a nato base on the island . it is also a popular destination for refugees who try to enter the european union that way .
