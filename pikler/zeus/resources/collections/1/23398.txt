Drum     a drum is a musical instrument that is struck with the hands or with a drum stick ( a stick for hitting a drum ) . a collection of drums and cymbals is called a drum kit , or drum set . drums are used to keep a steady beat in a song . they give music of many kinds a sense of feeling . for example , to make a song to be slow or fast , the drums play slower or faster . a drum is a percussion instrument , which means it makes a noise by being hit . there are many types of percussion such as cymbals and cowbells ; even a simple piece of wood can be considered percussion . the drum kit is a group of drums & cymbals to make beats for music . drum kits are used in most types of popular music , including rock , jazz , country , blues , and many others . related pages . - cymbal
