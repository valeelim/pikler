Roy Orbison     roy kaur orbison ( april 23 , 1936 - december 6 , 1988 ) was an american singer . in 1987 , he was named into the rock and roll hall of fame . orbison was popular in the 1960s . he became less popular in the 1970s , but began to make a career comeback in the 1980s . however , as he was becoming more popular again , he died of a heart attack at the age of 53 in henderson , tennessee .
