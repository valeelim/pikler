Tennessee     tennessee is a state in the united states . its capital is nashville , which is also the country music center of america . it is the home of the smoky mountains which are a famous tourist attraction . other well known cities and towns are memphis ( the biggest city ) , knoxville , chattanooga , oak ridge , lynchburg , carthage , lawrenceburg , clarksville , lebanon , pigeon forge , murfreesboro , and gatlinburg . tennessee touches eight states : kentucky and virginia to the north ; north carolina to the east ; georgia , alabama , and mississippi on the south ; arkansas and missouri ( by the mississippi river ) to the west . tennessee ties missouri as the state bordering the most other states . tennessee was the 16th state to join the nation , on june 1 , 1796 several professional sports teams play there , including the tennessee titans of the nfl , the memphis grizzlies of the nba , and the nashville predators of the nhl . related pages . - colleges and universities in tennessee - list of counties in tennessee
