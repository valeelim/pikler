Breton language     breton ( `` '' , in breton ) is a celtic language spoken in brittany , in the north-west of france . breton is closely related to the cornish language of cornwall spoken in south-west great britain . it is less closely related to welsh , and the goidelic languages of scotland , ireland and the isle of man . the breton language has approximately 240,000 speakers , but this number is falling very quickly due to the french government 's policy of using french . as such , breton is considered to be an endangered language .
