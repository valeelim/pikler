Southend-on-Sea     southend-on-sea is town and unitary authority borough in the east of england . it is a seaside resort in the south-eastern corner of the county of essex . it has a pier which is one of the longest in the world . the pier is 2,158 meters ( 1.34 miles ) long . a railway line runs along the pier . the pier has been badly damaged by fires four times in fifty years , in 1959 , 1976 , 1993 and 2005. near the pier there are arcades , casinos and a theme park called adventure island . other websites . - southend pier
