Tissue culture     tissue culture or cell culture is a way to grow cells in a laboratory . cells are taken , and put into in a flask or petri dish . they are grown on a growth medium , sometimes an agar plate . the cells divide , and can be treated in various ways . this is done for a number of purposes : - growing bacteria or fungi for experiments or for identification . an example would be growing bacteria to see what antibiotics they are resistant to . - growing cells of plants or animals for various experimental purposes . - plant cuttings can be grown : roots grow out of a sprig . - ivf when eggs are taken and fertilized with sperm in the dish .
