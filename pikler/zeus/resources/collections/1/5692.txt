Natalie Portman     natalie portman ( born natalie hershlag ; ; june 9 , 1981 ) is an israeli-american actress . she was born in jerusalem , israel . portman is an only child . she lived in washington , d.c. and connecticut before coming to long island , new york with her parents . in 1999 , she graduated from syosset high school . portman attended harvard university , studying psychology . in 2010 , she won the academy , bafta and golden globe awards for `` best actress '' for her acting in the movie `` black swan '' . she is most famous for starring in star wars episodes i , ii and iii as padme in the early 21st century . she has also appeared on saturday night live . movies . - `` thor 2 '' ( 2013 ) - `` thor '' ( 2011 ) - `` your highness '' ( 2011 ) - `` no strings attached '' ( 2011 ) - `` black swan '' ( 2010 ) - `` hesher '' ( 2010 ) - `` brothers '' ( 2009 ) - `` new york , i love you '' ( 2009 ) - `` the other woman '' ( 2009 ) - `` the other boleyn girl '' ( 2008 ) - `` mr. magorium 's wonder emporium '' ( 2007 ) - `` the darjeeling limited '' ( 2007 ) - `` my blueberry nights '' ( 2007 )
