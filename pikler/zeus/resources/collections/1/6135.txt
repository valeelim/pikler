No     no is a negative term . it means someone is denying or turning away something . it can also be used in multiple figures of speech . in both sentences and talking , the words `` no '' and the word `` not '' can both be used , though the word `` no '' is mainly used when speaking to other people . in some cases , however , `` not '' is not acceptable , and vice versa . these words are not to be confused with the similar sounding words know and knot . examples of proper usage of `` no '' and `` not '' : - is this green ? no , it is not . - there is no way round the back . - this is not the way ( but there is another way ) . - i will not come . - oh , no ! - no , thank you . - not on my watch ! examples of improper usage of `` no '' and `` not '' : - that is no the way you do it . - oh not ! - i know how to tie a knot . `` ( disambiguation ) '' - i did n't no that !
