Charlie Kaufman     charles stuart `` charlie '' kaufman ( born november 1 , 1958 ) is an american screenwriter . some of the movies he wrote screenplays for are `` being john malkovich '' , `` adaptation '' , `` human nature '' , and `` eternal sunshine of the spotless mind '' . charlie kaufman is known for writing very surreal movies - that is , movies where the plot has things in it that could never happen in real life , or that make the audience notice that they are watching a movie .
