Madhya Pradesh     madhya pradesh is a state in the republic of india . the state has an area of . it is bigger than italy but smaller than oman . in traditional indian geography it falls under the west indian zone . the capital of the state is bhopal . the largest city is indore . other main cities are jabalpur and ujjain . ujjain is well known for mythological reasons . mandav , bhojpur and panchmadi are the main tourist attractions . to the north of madhya pradesh is the state of uttar pradesh and to the northwest is the state of rajasthan , while maharashtra is to the southwest .
