Biologist     a biologist is a scientist who studies biology . biologists study living organisms . there are many different kinds of biologists . some of these include : < br > - those who study fish and ocean plants . they are called marine biologists . - those who study very small bacteria or viruses . - other scientists study groups of animals . - people who look at dna in cells are called geneticists . - some biologists study using cells in factories and companies , and that is called biotechnology .
