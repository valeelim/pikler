Estonian language     the estonian language ( `` eesti keel '' ) is a finno-ugric language . it is mainly spoken in estonia . the estonian language is similar to finnish . estonian is one of the national languages of europe that is not an indo-european language . estonian uses the latin alphabet . it has many vowels , including ö , ä , õ and ü. it has been influenced by and has got many words from german and swedish . estonian language also has different dialects .
