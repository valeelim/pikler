Elizabeth Monroe     elizabeth kortright monroe ( 1768 - september 23 , 1830 ) was the wife of james monroe . james monroe was the fifth president of the united states . elizabeth monroe was born in an old family of new york . when she was a girl of 17 years , she married james monroe . at that time , monroe was a lawyer . he was aged 27 years . once , the government sent monroe as an envoy to versailles . she also went along with her husband . during her last years , elizabeth monroe became very sick . she could not take part in social functions . she became more distant from people . some persons thought her to be a snob . but , her husband always understood her problems . after a long illness , she died on september 23 , 1830 at her home in oak hill at age 62 .
