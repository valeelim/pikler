Shropshire     shropshire is a county in england . it has no cities and only a few large towns - shrewsbury , telford , ludlow and oswestry . the county is very rural and there are many hills , forests , farms and rivers . the county is also landlocked ( has no coast ) . shrewsbury is the county town of shropshire , but telford is the largest town in the county . ironbridge gorge is a famous area of the county and is a tourist location , because it was here where industry began and spread across the world , over 200 years ago . there is the very first iron bridge there , as well as many museums . related pages . - river severn - west midlands - british isles
