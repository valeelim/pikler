Interim     interim means `` in between '' or `` transitional '' ( moving from one time or place to another ) , or `` temporary . '' some common `` interim '' items : - an interim report is a report on the how some work is going . - an interim document is a piece of writing that is not finished . - an interim official is a person who is doing a job temporarily . this can be in between two other people having the job , or when the normal person is temporarily unable to do it . for example , a school can have an interim principal ( headteacher ) , or a country can have an interim prime minister . the interim person might be called an acting headteacher or acting prime minister . other examples ; - an interim business agreement - an interim ceasefire - an interim constitution
