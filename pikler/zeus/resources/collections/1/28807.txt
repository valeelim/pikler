Arcangelo Corelli     arcangelo corelli ( b.fusignano , imola , 17 feb.1653 ; d.rome , 8 jan.1713 ) was an italian violinist and composer . he composed some of the most important italian music of the baroque period . he was also very famous as a player . other violinists learned from his style of playing and developed the art of playing the violin . his works include sonatas and concerti grossi . perhaps his most famous is the la folia sonata for violin .
