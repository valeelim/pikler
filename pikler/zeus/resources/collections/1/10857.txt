Lou Albano     louis vincent albano ( born july 29 , 1933 - died october 14 , 2009 ) was a very famous american professional wrestler in the 1980s . albano is best known as captain lou albano . he also appeared in cyndi lauper 's music video `` girls just wan na have fun '' and played mario on `` the super mario brothers super show '' , based on the series of video games .
