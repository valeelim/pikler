525     events . - bernicia settled by the angles - ethiopia conquers yemen - the daisan river , a tributary of the euphrates , floods edessa and within a couple of hours fills the entire city except for the highest parts . eventually the pent-up waters break through the city walls . the shroud of turin was discovered during the rebuilding of the city . - byzantine emperor justin i rebuilds anazarbus and renames it justinopolis . - dionysius exiguus produces his tables for computing the date of easter .
