Czechoslovakia     czechoslovakia ( czech : `` československo '' , slovak : `` československo '' /after 1990 `` česko-slovensko '' ) was a country in europe that existed from 1918 until 1993. in mid 1938 nazi germany took over czechoslovakia and split off slovakia . after world war ii the ussr kept part of eastern czechoslovakia so that they could have a border with hungary . the ussr also kept parts of poland and romania . all four of these countries were in the ussr 's sphere of influence . the ussr thought that this meant they could make sure the countries did what they wanted . in 1968 the ussr thought that the government of alexander dubcek was moving away from communism and leaving the warsaw pact , and invaded czechoslovakia . in 1989 czechoslovakia peacefully removed the communist dictatorship in the `` velvet revolution '' . on 1 january 1993 , czechoslovakia split into the czech republic and slovakia . both countries separated in peace .
