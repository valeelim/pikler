Ginger ale     ginger ale is a soft drink flavored with the root of a plant known as ginger . it has a sweet yet spicy flavor . even though it has the name `` ale '' , which can be another word for beer , it is not beer . it can be enjoyed by all ages . canada dry is a well known maker of ginger ale . most drinks that are ginger ale come in a green can . ginger beer is a non-alcoholic soft drink that is also flavored with ginger root . ginger beer is much spicier than ginger ale . ginger beer is popular in some caribbean countries such as trinidad .
