Top of the Pops     top of the pops is a british music show , which featured popular artists performing their songs . it started in 1964 and ended in 2006 because not enough people were watching it any more . it was hosted by many presenters over the years , most notably by sir jimmy savile , who opened the very first show on new year 's day 1964 on bbc1 . totp2 ( top of the pops 2 ) is a variant of the show . however , it featured a collection of archive performances by artists spanning the past four decades . the show was regularly hosted by steve wright , up until 2009 , when mark radcliffe replaced him . a new episode of the programme is shown on 25 december each year .
