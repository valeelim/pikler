Cherimoya     cherimoya is a sweet fruit that comes mostly from peru , ecuador , colombia and bolivia . the inside of a cherimoya is soft , and it is white in color . the skin of the cherimoya fruit is green when it is ripe , and brown if it becomes too ripe . the cherimoya has large , black seeds . the cherimoya is special because it can be grown in high places . in india , especially in north india , this fruit is known as `` sitafal '' ( named after sita ) . in sri lanka it is known as `` wali anoda ( වැලි අනෝදා ) ''
