Lake Garda     lake garda is the largest lake in italy . in italian its name is lago di garda . it lies in the north of italy between venice and milan , in the regions of veneto , lombardy and trentino . the lake is 51.6 kilometres long and 17.2 kilometres wide . it covers an area of 369.98 square kilometres . other websites . - lake garda - tourist guide - webcams at lake garda - lake garda - travel guide
