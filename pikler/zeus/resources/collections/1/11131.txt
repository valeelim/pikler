Mississippi     mississippi is one of the states of the united states . its capital and largest city is jackson . the state flower and tree are the magnolia . other large cities are biloxi , greenville , gulfport , hattiesburg , meridian , pascagoula , southaven , tupelo , starkville , and vicksburg . it touches arkansas ( by the mississippi river ) , louisiana , tennessee , and alabama . related pages . - mississippi river - william faulkner
