1168     events . - december 22 – afraid that old cairo would be captured by the crusaders , its caliph orders the city set afire . the city burns for 54 days . - prince richard of england becomes duke of aquitaine . he later becomes king richard i of england . - emperor takakura ascends to the throne of japan . - king valdemar i of denmark conquers arkona on the island of rügen , the strongest pagan fortress and temple in northern europe .
