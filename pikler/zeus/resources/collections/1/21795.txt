Soy milk     soy milk ( also called soymilk , soya milk , soybean milk , soy bean milk , soy drink , or soy beverage ) is a milk-like product made from soybeans . soy milk originated in east asia , china , a region where the soybean was native . it was used as food long before the existence of written records . later on , the soybean and soybean foods came to japan . related pages . - soy milk maker
