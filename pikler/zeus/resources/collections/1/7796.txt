Thomas Dolby     thomas dolby ( born thomas morgan robertson ; 14 october 1958 ) is a british musican and computer designer . he is probably most famous for his 1982 hit , `` she blinded me with science '' . he married actress kathleen beller in 1988. the couple have three children together . discography . singles . - track did not chart in north america until 1983 , after the success of `` she blinded me with science '' .
