Viacom     viacom ( short for video & audio communications ) is an american entertainment company . it started in 1970 as viacom international , the syndication division of cbs tv ( which they once owned ) . in 2005 , viacom split into two companies : the original company was renamed cbs corporation , and a new company keeping the viacom name was created . they own paramount pictures , spelling productions , dreamworks ( dreamworks skg ) mtv , nickelodeon , nick jr. , neopets , peanuts , the addams family , kids incorporated , blue 's clues , nelvana , evil dead , rugrats , all grown up ! , shrek , hey arnold ! , charlotte 's web and others . they formerly owned the movie rental giant blockbuster entertainment .
