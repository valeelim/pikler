Beard     a beard is the hair growing on the lower part of a man 's face . the hair that grows on the upper lip of some men is a mustache . when a man has hair only below the lower lip and above the chin , it is called a soul patch . some men have a lot of hair and a big beard , and some have very little . in the modern world , many men shave part or all of their beards , or cut their beard so it does not get very long . women can also have beards , but it is less common . some animals also have hair like this , and people sometimes also call this hair a beard .
