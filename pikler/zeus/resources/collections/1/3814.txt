Paperback     a paperback is a type of book . it has a thin paper or cardboard cover . another name for paperback is `` soft cover '' . paperback book covers can bend and be folded . they are not as strong as hardback books , and they break easier . there are some exceptions to this . the covers are laminated ( have layers ) , and some of the methods are more durable ( lasting ) than others . usually , paperback books cost less money than hardback books .
