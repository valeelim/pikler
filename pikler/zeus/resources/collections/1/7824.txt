Self-defence     self-defense means fighting off something or another person to protect yourself and maybe others . ways of self-defence include martial arts or using a weapon . sometimes , self defense can cause serious harm to the other person . in most nations , you can not be prosecuted for this harm . in the united states , an act of self-defense can only count as self defense if the victim is in a situation that can cause them serious injury or death . in international law all persons have the right to self-defense . other websites . - no nonsense self defense - reliable information for dangerous situations articles by marc macyoung
