Bucharest     bucharest is the capital city of the country of romania . it is the largest city in the country . the city has a population of 1.9 million people . it is made up of 6 `` sectors '' . it has a humid continental climate ( `` cfa '' in the koeppen climate classification ) . it became the capital of romania in 1862. it is the centre of romanian media , culture and art . bucharest is the 6th largest city in the european union by population within city limits .
