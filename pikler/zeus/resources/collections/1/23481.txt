Tomáš Garrigue Masaryk     tomáš garrigue masaryk ( 7 march 1850 - 14 september 1937 ) was the first president of czechoslovakia . he led the country between years 1918 and 1935. he was scientist , philosopher , pedagog , politician , and journalist . he studied in brno , vienna , and leipzig , and he spoke many languages ( latin , french , polish , german and english ) he worked as a teacher at a university in prague and vienna . he was a big persona with huge charisma and he is called `` president liberator '' and `` daddy masaryk '' . edvard beneš was the next president after him .
