Dundee     dundee is a city on the east coast of scotland where the river tay flows into the north sea . it is the fourth largest city in scotland . it was famous as the city of `` jute , jam , and journalism '' , because jute and jam were made there , and several newspapers were printed in the city . dundee has a population of 148,260 people . it is the fourth largest city in scotland . there are two universities in dundee . they are called the `` university of dundee '' and the `` university of abertay dundee '' . it also is home to two professional football teams . dundee f.c . and dundee united f.c. , who both play in the scottish premier league . unusually , their stadiums ( dens park and tannadice ) are very close together , in the same street . dundee airport serves the area .
