Shoe     a shoe is a type of footwear . it is an item of clothing . shoes come in pairs , with one shoe for each foot . there are many different types of shoes . athletic shoes , for example , are to make running , walking or jogging easier by making the weight of the shoe lighter and the sole of the shoe softer . leather shoes are formal shoes . they are worn for business and ceremonial functions . they are also worn for fashion . slippers are a kind of indoor shoe . they are often worn when it is cold . rubber boots are used on rainy days . the shoes with a high heel which women wear are called high heels . people usually wear shoes in public . they are worn for hygiene , style , and comfort .
