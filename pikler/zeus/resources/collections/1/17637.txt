Lawrence's Goldfinch    lawrence 's goldfinch the lawrence 's goldfinch , `` carduelis lawrencei '' , is a small songbird of north america . bigger than the lesser goldfinch , and smaller than the american goldfinch , with less yellow markings than either . it lives from central california and its southern coast in the united states , to baja california in mexico . females have less yellow and do not have the black cap and chin of the males . this bird is named after the american `` ornithologist '' ( bird expert ) , george newbold lawrence .
