Moravia     moravia ( czech and slovak : `` morava ; '' german '' : '' `` mähren '' ) is a historical region in the eastern part of the czech republic . it takes its name from the morava river which flows from the north to the south of the land . together with bohemia in the west and silesia in the north , moravia made historical-political region called czech lands . today , moravia , bohemia and small part of silesia make up the current czech republic . important cities today are brno , olomouc , ostrava and zlín .
