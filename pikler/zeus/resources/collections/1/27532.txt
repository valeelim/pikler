Andrew Denton     andrew christopher denton ( born may 4 , 1960 ) is an australian comedian and television presenter who likes to make fun of politics . andrew denton used to have a television programme about sports in australia called `` live and sweaty '' . he now has a programme called `` enough rope '' where he interviews people about politics . denton is a very smart man . the australian media use him in many news stories . one of denton 's interviews was with rene rivkin , who police said had cheated in the stock market to make money . rivkin was very sad , and after the interview he went to jail . rivkin killed himself in jail . denton says that he feels very sorry for interviewing rivkin . denton says that he does not want to interview someone like that again .
