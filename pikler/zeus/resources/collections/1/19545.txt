Andy Richter     andy richter ( born october 28 , 1966 ) is an american comedian and actor . he is best known for his former role as conan o'brien 's sidekick on `` late night with conan o'brien '' . richter was born in grand rapids , michigan . he is the second of four children . he was raised in yorkville , illinois . while attending columbia college , chicago , he played in several chicago comedy groups , including the annoyance theatre , before getting on late night . richter left late night in 2000. he said he wanted to focus on an acting career . his first major work was fox 's critically acclaimed but little watched `` andy richter controls the universe '' . it was cancelled after two short runs . his next fox sitcom , `` quintuplets '' , was not liked by the critics . it lasted only one season . he is married to actress sarah thyre . they have two children . in addition to his television work , richter has appeared in movies such as `` seeing other people '' , olsen twins ' `` new york minute '' , `` madagascar '' , and `` cabin boy '' .
