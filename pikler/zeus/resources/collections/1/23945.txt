50 (number)    50 ( number ) fifty is the integer that is after forty-nine and before fifty-one . the prime factors of fifty are 2 and 5 . ( 2 * 5 * 5 = 50 ) the factors of 50 are 1 , 2 , 5 , 10 , 25 , and 50 . 5*10=50 . 50 is the smallest number that can be written as the sum of two squares in two distinct ways : 50=1+7=5+5 . also , 50 is one-half of 100 or half of a century ( a century is 100 years ) .
