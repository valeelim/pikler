Sputnik programme     the sputnik programme was a soviet union spacecraft programme . sputnik 1 , the first satellite in the programme , was launched on october 4 , 1957. this was the first man-made satellite ever to enter orbit . the united states was very surprised when the soviet union sent sputnik 1 into space . it did not want to fall behind . so , it began spending more money on science and education . this was when the space race between the soviet union and the united states began . the word `` sputnik '' comes from the russian спутник , literally `` travelling companion '' , other websites . - sputnik citizendium
