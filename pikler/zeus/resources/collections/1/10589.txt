Hank Aaron     henry louis aaron ( born february 5 , 1935 ) is a retired baseball player . he played baseball professionally for 22 years . he hit 755 home runs . even though he has 755 home runs in his career , he never hit 50 or more in a season . he was selected for 25 all-star games . he was put into the baseball hall of fame in 1982. hank aaron was born in mobile , alabama . other websites . - hank aaron at baseball aha
