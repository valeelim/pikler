Shareware     shareware is a way to sell software . the user will get a preview or demo of the software for free . this way , the user can decide if he or she likes the program or not before buying the complete program . users can get games for free this way but most of the time these games do not have all the levels . sometimes internet relay chat clients let users have a demo of their client for 30 days , but after that , in order to keep using it , the user will have to pay money .
