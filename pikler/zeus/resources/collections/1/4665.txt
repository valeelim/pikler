Platinum     platinum is a soft , heavy , white metal . it is a precious metal . it usually costs more than gold . in chemistry , platinum is element number 78 , and its atoms have an atomic weight of 195 a.m.u.. the symbol for platinum is pt , from spanish `` platina '' meaning `` little silver '' . platinum is very malleable and ductile , which means it can be hammered into thin sheets and it can be pulled into wire . platinum is very stable . acids do not affect platinum . the most common use of platinum is in a vehicle 's catalytic converter .
