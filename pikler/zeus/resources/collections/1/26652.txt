Melissa Joan Hart     melissa joan catherine hart ( born april 18 , 1976 ) is an american actress . she was born and raised in long island , new york . she played the title roles in the television series `` clarissa explains it all '' and `` sabrina , the teenage witch '' . in 1999 she played the lead role in the movie `` drive me crazy '' . from 2010-2015 , she starred as mel burke in the abc family show `` melissa & joey '' . since july 19 , 2003 , hart has been married to musician mark wilkerson ( born 1976 ) . they have three sons together : mason walter wilkerson ( born january 11 , 2006 ) , braydon hart wilkerson ( born march 12 , 2008 ) and tucker mcfadden wilkerson ( born september 18 , 2012 ) . other websites . - melissa joan hart at the internet movie database ( imdb ) - melissa joan hart news and video
