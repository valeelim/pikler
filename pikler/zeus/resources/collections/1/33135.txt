Tray     a tray is a shallow container designed for carrying things . trays are flat , but with raised edges to stop things from sliding off of them . they are often rectangular , and may or may not have handles with which to carry them . a butler 's tray has a deeper surround , handles on the short sides ( usually cut into the surround ) so that it can be easily carried , and folding legs . it is used to carry drinks , and can be used as a side table .
