Jean-Philippe Rameau     jean-philippe rameau ( born dijon , september ? 1683 ; died paris , 12 september 1764 ) was a french composer . he is famous for his music for harpsichord and for his operas . his most well-known operas are : `` hippolyte et aricie '' , `` castor et pollux '' , and `` dardanus '' . he is also known for his one act opera `` pigmalion '' which he is said to have composed in 8 days . he is also known as an important music theorist by writing many books on the matter . other websites . - jean-philippe rameau / discography
