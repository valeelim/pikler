Kiefer Sutherland     kiefer william frederick dempsey george rufus sutherland ( born december 21 , 1966 ) is a english|canadian actor and director . he was born in london , england in 1966 to canadian actor donald sutherland and his wife shirley douglas , an actress and daughter of canadian statesman tommy douglas . he also has a twin sister , named rachel , who looks similar to him . he grew up in london , corona , california and toronto , ontario . in the 1980s he was part of a group of young actors called the `` brat pack '' and appeared in movies `` stand by me '' ( 1986 ) , `` the lost boys '' ( 1987 ) , `` young guns '' ( 1988 ) , `` flatliners '' ( 1990 ) , and `` the three musketeers '' ( 1993 ) . in 2001 , he starred as jack bauer in `` 24 '' , which was intended as a one-shot mini-series , but became one of the most popular shows on tv . for this role , kiefer won the emmy award for best actor in 2006 , the golden globe for best actor in 2001 , the sag award for best actor in 2003 and 2005 and the satellite award for best actor in 2002 and 2003. the show had eight seasons before ending , with one movie currently released .
