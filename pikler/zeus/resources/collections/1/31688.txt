Doha     indoha ( arabic : الدوحة ; , ad-dawḥah or ad-dōḥah ) is the capital city of qatar . it has a population of 400,051 ( 2005 ) , and is at 25.3° n 51.5333° e , on the persian gulf . the city is home to doha international airport and the major oil and fishing industries . doha is also home to education city , an area devoted to research and education . doha was the host of the 2006 asian games , a major asian sporting event . doha has a sheikh called : sheikh tamim . the official language is english and arabic.the biggest mall in qatar is qatar mall ' , like dubai mall it is bordered by saudi arabia on its south and the persian gulf on its north , east and west . nearest oversea country is bahrain
