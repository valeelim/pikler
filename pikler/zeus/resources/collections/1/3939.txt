Spice     spice is an ingredient that adds flavor to food . it is usually used to enrich or alter the quality of something . spices can be used to give food an interesting and exciting taste . examples of a spice would be cinnamon , fenugreek and nutmeg . spices can also be used to hide or cover up bad tastes in the food , because it is rotten or affected by fungi , e.g . marzipan . indians use alot of spices like turmeric
