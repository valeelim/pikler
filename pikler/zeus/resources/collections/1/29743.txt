Phoebe (Titan)    phoebe ( titan ) phoebe is a titan in greek mythology . she was associated with the moon . she is the daugher of uranos and gaia . she was married to her brother coeus , and had two daughters : leto and asteria . leto bore artemis and apollo , asteria is the mother of hecate . the olympians beat the titans in the ten-year war . she and the titans were summoned by zeus to tarturus , in the underworld .
