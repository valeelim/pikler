Andrology     andrology is the study of male health , especially male sexual organs and reproduction . it is similar to the study of women 's health , called gynaecology . andrology has only been studied since the late 1960s . the first specialist journal on the subject was the german periodical `` andrologie '' , published from 1969 onwards . men are more susceptible to heart disease than women . they also usually have a slightly shorter natural average life span . however , men get many conditions , such as osteoporosis , less than women . other websites . - international society of andrology - the world of andrology
