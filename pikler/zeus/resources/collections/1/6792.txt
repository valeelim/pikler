Equator     the equator is a line which is not real drawn around a sphere or planet , such as the earth . it is one of the lines known as a line of latitude , or circle of latitude . the name `` equator '' is latin language for `` even-maker '' ; at equator the day and night are exactly the same length around the year . the equator is halfway between the north pole and the south pole . there , the surface of the planet is parallel to the axis of rotation . the equator divides the surface into the northern hemisphere and the southern hemisphere . unlike the northern hemisphere and the southern hemisphere , countries around the equator do not experience the four seasons , and the climate is usually very humid . related pages . - northern hemisphere - southern hemisphere - solstice and equinox - tropic of cancer - tropic of capricorn - arctic circle - antarctic circle
