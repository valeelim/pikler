Aleksander Kwasniewski     aleksander kwaśniewski ( ; born 15 november 1954 ) is a polish politician . he served as president for two terms ( 1995-2000 and 2000-2005 ) . he was , before being elected first time , member of the democratic left alliance , but after assumed duties he became an independent . before 1989 he was a member of polish united workers party ( communist ) . he was preceded by lech wałęsa and succeeded by lech kaczyński . he was born in białogard in northwest poland . before became a politician , he was a journalist . before became president , he was minister and deputy to the sejm . in the late 1980s , he participated in the polish round table agreement . in 1995 election , he won in the second round , but five years later he won in the first round . other websites . - official web page
