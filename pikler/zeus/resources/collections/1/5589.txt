Glastonbury     glastonbury is a town in somerset , england . it is well known for the glastonbury festival which happens every year in glastonbury . glastonbury is also well-known because of glastonbury abbey . this is because glastonbury abbey is said to be the place where king arthur is buried , and it was burned down when king henry the eighth got angry at all catholic monasteries in the country as the pope would not grant him a divorce . the abbot , abbot whiting , was killed on the tor , a local landmark .
