Aziz al-Abub     aziz al-abub ( a.k.a . ibrahim al-nadhir or al-nahdhir ) was a lebanese hezbollah psychiatrist and mind control expert . aziz was a disciple of ewen cameron and a graduate of the soviet union 's that specialized in mind control techniques . aziz al-abub used mind-control , drugs and physical torture in the infamous 444-day ( 1984-1985 ) torture and mental derangement of american in beirut .
