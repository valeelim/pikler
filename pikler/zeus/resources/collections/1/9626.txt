Robber baron     the term robber baron was first used in the 12th and 13th centuries to describe rich men who lived in large castles along major transportation rivers , like the rhine in europe . they would send ships out onto these rivers to stop anybody who was traveling through who did not know better and collect a tax from them on any money or goods on these ships . they were not officially allowed to collect these taxes . it was only the holy roman emperor who could decide what taxes to be charged , how much and who could charge it . second , it is a slang term used of certain rich and ruthless industrialists and bankers , especially in the late 19th and early 20th century u.s.a .
