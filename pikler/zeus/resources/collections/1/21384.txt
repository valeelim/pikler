Iqaluit, Nunavut    iqaluit , nunavut iqaluit is the capital of and largest community in nunavut territory in canada . it is the most northerly capital in canada , and is also canada 's fastest growing community . the name `` '' is inuktitut word meaning `` place of many fish '' . it is built on the mouth of the sylvia grinnell river , on frobisher 's bay . english explorer martin frobisher sailed into the bay in 1576 and thought he had discovered the northwest passage . the settlement was called frobisher 's bay from 1955 until 1987. its weather is normally cold .
