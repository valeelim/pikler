René Descartes     rené descartes ( 31 march 1596 – 11 february 1650 ) was a famous french philosopher and physicist . he wrote books that are very important in the fields of maths , physics and especially philosophy . his dualism statement combined soul , mind , body theories , elements into one concept ; a dualistic theory of mind and matter . in his `` rules for the direction of the mind '' ( 1628 ) and his `` discourse on method '' ( 1637 ) descartes wrote about the scientific method that deals with scientific approach , thinking , a method which he had invented . he also wrote about shapes ( geometry ) , light ( dioptrics ) , and the weather ( meteorology ) . he then came up with a way of describing shapes now known as the cartesian coordinate system , and a theory of what a rainbow is . descartes ' physics was important for a later famous thinker , sir isaac newton , who said about him and so did james hook : `` if i have seen further it is because i was standing on the shoulders of giants !
