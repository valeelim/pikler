Prefecture     a prefecture is a word used by countries such as greece and japan ( called `` νομός '' , `` nomós '' in greece and called 県 `` ken '' in japan ) . it is a smaller part of a country with its own government . it is a kind of local government . it is like an american state or a british county . in france , it is the head of the department ( `` see '' prefectures in france `` for more detail '' ) .
