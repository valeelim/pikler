Leto     leto is a goddess in greek mythology . she was the daughter of coeus and phoebe , who were titans . leto was the mother of the twins artemis and apollo , whose father was zeus . after her twins were born , leto played very little part in greek mythology . hera , who was zeus 's wife , discovered that leto was pregnant and became very angry . she banned leto from giving birth on any mainland or island known . leto found the island of delos , which floated and so was not an island nor mainland , and gave birth there . leto gave birth to artemis without any problem . however she was in labour with apollo for nine days and nine nights . artemis helped leto to give birth to apollo .
