Bullseye (UK game show)    bullseye ( uk game show ) bullseye is a british television game show . it is hosted by jim bowen . in `` bullseye '' , people play darts and answer questions to win money . the person who wins the most money has a chance to win bully 's special prize . it ran from 1981-1995 on itv and there was another series in 2006 on challenge , hosted by dave spikey .
