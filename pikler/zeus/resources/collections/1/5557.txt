Violet     violet is the seventh color of the rainbow -- it is the color on the inner edge of the rainbow . violet is the color between blue and purple . the name of the color comes from the violet , which is a small flower grown in most parts of the world . the first written use of `` violet '' as a color name in english was in 1370. meaning of violet . - the color violet represents magic . - violet is a colour of royalty and extravagance , when paired with yellow , which used to represent abundance and wealth . related pages . - list of colors - blue-violet - cerise - fuchsia - indigo - magenta - mauve - orchid - purple - rose - ultraviolet
