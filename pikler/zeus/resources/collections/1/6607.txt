Needle     a needle is a small tool used to push or pull a thread through a small hole . it is most often used to make clothing and footwear . the needle consists of a short stick with a sharp point and hole for the thread . needles are now mostly made of steel , but in ancient or stone age times they would have been made of small bones or twigs . once the thread is put through the hole in the needle and `` doubled-back '' so that it can not fall out , then the whole needle and its thread can be put through the material . the term needle may also be used to refer to other sharp objects used for different purposes , such as the needles doctors use to give a shot .
