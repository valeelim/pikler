Hurricane Rita     hurricane rita was a powerful hurricane in the 2005 atlantic hurricane season . it hit the united states ' gulf coast . at its peak intensity , it was a strong category 5 storm with sustained winds of 175 miles per hour . it made landfall as a category 3 storm , and caused more flooding in new orleans , which already had weakened levees due to hurricane katrina . in addition , it caused massive power outages . related pages . - hurricane katrina
