Buffalo, New York    buffalo , new york buffalo is a city in the u.s. state of new york . buffalo is the second-biggest city in new york state . there are about 280,000 people in the city of buffalo . there are more than a million people in the surrounding area . buffalo is on the eastern side of lake erie near the niagara river . it is close to niagara falls , and to canada . buffalo has an nfl team called the buffalo bills ( who play in the suburb of orchard park , ny ) and an nhl team called the buffalo sabres .
