Interpreter     an interpreter is a person who translates speech in different languages , usually at conferences or other meetings . there was a movie called `` the interpreter '' . also , an interpreter is someone who will translate and communicate with people who find normal speech difficult . an interpreter is also a computer program that runs other programs described in text files . they are different from compilers because they do not make a new file to run the program . related pages . - sign language - video relay service - teletypewriter
