Patroclus     patroclus ( or patroklos ) was a person in greek mythology . he is an important person in the trojan war , and in homer 's `` iliad '' . patroclus was the son of menoetius . there are several women who could be his mother , because the different stories and writers are not sure . he was the best friend ( or possibly lover ) of achilles . in the trojan war achilles once stopped fighting because he was angry at agamemnon . at this time patroclus secretly took achilles ' armour and weapons and went into the fight . all people mistook him for achilles and he was killed by hector . this made achilles angry at the trojans so he started fighting again .
