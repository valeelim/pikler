World Money Fair     the world money fair is an international bourse of coins . a `` bourse '' is where dealers , collectors and the public to buy , sell and trade coins . in 1970 a. m beck founded in muinch the `` organisation of european coins-bourses '' , `` organisation europäischer münzen-börsen '' ( oemb ) in german . he had the idea to build up a stronger market for the collecting of coins . two years later the organisation organized in basel the 1st international bourse of coins . during the time it was developed to the `` world money fair '' . today more than 40 nations are members of this organisation . in 2006 , the bourse is at the 1st time in berlin . at the start the german secretary of state karl diller presented the new german 2 euro coin . it shows the holstentor of lübeck .
