Antwerp (city)    antwerp ( city ) antwerpen is a city in antwerp province in the north of belgium . it is at 51° 12 north , 04° 24 east . it is the capital of the province with the same name . in 2007 , 466,203 people lived there . the city is divided in 9 zones , called districts : - antwerp - berchem - berendrecht-zandvliet-lillo - borgerhout - deurne - ekeren - hoboken - merksem - wilrijk
