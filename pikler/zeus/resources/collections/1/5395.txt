Dice     dice are objects used in games when something random needs to be done , such as moving a piece a random number of places on a board . most dice are cubes that have the numbers 1 , 2 , 3 , 4 , 5 and 6 on the faces . dice are sometimes made in other polyhedral shapes than cubes , but the six-sided die ( `` die '' is the singular of `` dice '' ) is the most common . a die is usually rolled by the player â€™ s hand . when adding together opposite sides of typical cubical dice , the sum is always 7. some games are played with two or more dice and some use only one . some games that use dice are monopoly , yahtzee , risk , and many more . dice with different numbers of sides , like those used in dungeons & dragons , are called polyhedral dice . weights can be put inside a die to make it only land on a certain number once it is rolled .
