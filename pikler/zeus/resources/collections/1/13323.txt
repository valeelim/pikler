Gay bashing     gay bashing and gay bullying is when someone attacks a person or group of people because they are gay or lesbian or bisexual or transgender . studies show that bullying happens to people who are gay or lesbian more often than to others in general . as teenagers , gay young men are more likely to be bullied than lesbians . gay bullying can become a type of hate crime or hate speech . related pages . - homophobia - sexual orientation - transphobia - lgbt other websites . - `` tips to help the bullying bystander '' at education.com
