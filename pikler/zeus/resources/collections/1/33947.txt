Le Havre     le havre is a city in normandy . it is one of the most important ports in france . it is located on the right bank of the seine river along the english channel . the city and port were founded by the king francis i of france in 1517. the city was bombed during world war ii . the damaged area was rebuilt by auguste perret from 1945 to 1964. this section was named a world heritage site by unesco .
