El Paso, Texas    el paso , texas el paso is a city in the u.s. state of texas ( near ciudad juarez , chihuahua , mexico ) . it is in el paso county and is the county seat ( the city where the county is governed ) . it is at the western end of texas , and is along interstate 10. the name comes from el paso de norte , meaning the passageway to the north , which was shortened to el paso .
