James Earl Jones     james earl jones ( born todd jones on january 17 , 1931 ) is an american actor . he is famous for doing the voice of darth vader in the `` star wars '' movies , for doing the voice of mufasa in `` the lion king '' , and for the cnn tagline ( `` this ... is cnn '' ) . he starred in `` lincoln portrait '' which has music by aaron copland . jones has won two tony awards for his live theatre work on broadway . he won an emmy award for his role on the television series `` gabriel 's fire '' . he is a son of actor robert earl jones . other websites . - james earl jones at the internet off-broadway database - james earl jones at emmys.com - tonyawards.com interview with james earl jones - `` cat on a hot tin roof '' all african-american production website
