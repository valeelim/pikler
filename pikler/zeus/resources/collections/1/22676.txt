Erfurt massacre     the erfurt massacre was a series of shootings that happened at the gutenberg gymnasium in erfurt , germany , on 26 april 2002. a boy that had been expelled from the school , robert steinhäuser , was the person who did all the shooting . in anger , he came into the gymnasium , and shot dead 12 teachers , one secretary , two students , and a police officer . after a teacher caught him and put him in an empty room , steinhäuser shot and killed himself . in total , 17 people were killed .
