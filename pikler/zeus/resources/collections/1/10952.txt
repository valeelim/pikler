EastEnders     eastenders is a bbc television programme , in the united kingdom . it has been running since february 1985 , and has had over 4700 episodes , and is known as a soap opera . the programme is a rival of `` coronation street '' and `` emmerdale '' . it is usually shown on bbc one . the programme is in a pretend area of east london , called walford . it is filmed in elstree , a village in borehamwood . like `` coronation street '' , most action is around a local pub . the pub in `` eastenders '' is called the queen vic . there are many story lines , including drug abuse , rape , racism , domestic violence , murder , kidnapping , prostitution , theft , senile dementia , arson , and runaway children . arguments between members of one family , or between different families , are often in the storyline . it has won six bafta awards . other websites . - bbc `` eastenders '' website
