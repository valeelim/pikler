Belief     a belief is a firm thought that something is true , often based on revelation . belief is usually a part of belonging to a religion . it is different to scientific knowledge that can be tested , but belief is not able to be tested . for example , a person may believe in god or gods . the word is also used to describe what a person expects will happen based on limited information . for example , `` i believe amy will come around today '' . belief is also something you believe in but you can not prove exactly . related pages . - faith - evidence - epistemology - karl popper
