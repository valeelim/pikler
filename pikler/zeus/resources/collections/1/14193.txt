Shahrukh Khan     shah rukh khan ( born 2 november 1965 ) popularly called as srk is a bollywood indian actor . also known as the `` badshah of bollywood '' , `` king of bollywood '' , `` king khan '' . he was born in new delhi , his grandparents having moved there from afghanistan and peshawar . he started his career appearing on television in the late 1980s . he is muslim and studied at a muslim school . he is married to gauri , a hindu and has three children ; aryan , suhana and abram . he also has a sister named shehnaz lalarukh . he has a master 's degree . he is one of the biggest star in not just bollywood , but also around the world . he is also the second richest actor in the world , after jerry sienfeld with over a net worth of $ 600 million .
