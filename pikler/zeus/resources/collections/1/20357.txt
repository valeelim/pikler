Air Supply     air supply is a pop music singing group . they formed in melbourne , australia in 1976. the current members are russell hitchcock ( born june 15 , 1949 in melbourne ) and graham russell ( born june 1 , 1950 in nottingham , england ) . in 1981 they got with their song `` the one that you love '' a number one hit in the united states . they had their most popular times during the early 1980s .
