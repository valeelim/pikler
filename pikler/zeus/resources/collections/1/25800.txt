Conn Smythe Trophy     the conn smythe trophy is a trophy in the national hockey league awarded to the most valuable player in the playoffs each year . it was first awarded in 1965. montreal canadiens and colorado avalanche goaltender patrick roy has won it three times , more than any other player . normally the trophy goes to a player of the stanley cup champion . sometimes it goes to someone on the losing team . trophy winners on losing teams are marked with as asterisk ( * ) in the list below . related pages . - list of nhl players - list of nhl statistical leaders
