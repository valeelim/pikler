Celestia     celestia is a free 3d astronomy program for windows , mac os x , and linux . it is created by chris laurel and licensed under the gnu . the program is based on the hipparcos catalogue ( 120.000 stars ) and allows users to display objects from artificial satellites to entire galaxies in three dimensions in opengl . different from other planetarium software , the user is free to travel in the universe . nasa and esa have used celestia , but it is not to be confused with celestia 2000 , esa 's own program .
