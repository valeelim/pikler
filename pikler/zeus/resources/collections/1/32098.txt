Crew     a crew usually is a group of people that work within a common organisation , and that have some activities in common . the usual place where crews can be found are ships and airplanes . on such ships and aircraft , some functions some people perform are very special . it is therefore common that crews are organised in a hierarchy . every person , except the captain , at the top , has a person above them . they will report to that person . teams who work backstage in theaters , on movie sets , and in television studios are also called crews . such crews are distinct from the cast .
