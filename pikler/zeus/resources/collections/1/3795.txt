Road     people have been making roads for a long time . roman roads and the incan courier roads are famous . but transport by river was usually easier and faster than transport by road . in the industrial revolution , the railway was invented . a railway is a special type of road , using railway tracks . roads are now usually made for wheeled vehicles , like cars , to travel on . road building and care is usually paid for by taxes . some roads are toll roads , where people pay to use the road . other kinds of road . - tracks - trails - freeways - highways
