Transcontinental country     a transcontinental country is a nation that is in more than one continent . examples : - asia and europe - turkey - russia - azerbaijan - kazakhstan most of the other examples are contentious , based on the possession of small islands in another region . for example , spain controlling the canary islands . a few are undoubtedly intercontinental : - asia and australasia - indonesia , controlling large islands in australasia ( oceania ) - north america and oceania - united states , because hawaii is a state .
