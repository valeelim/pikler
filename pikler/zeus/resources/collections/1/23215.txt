A Beautiful Mind (movie)    a beautiful mind ( movie ) a beautiful mind is a 2001 biographical movie about the mathematician john nash and his experiences of schizophrenia . it is set in massachusetts and new jersey . it stars russell crowe , ed harris , jennifer connelly , christopher plummer , and paul bettany . it was directed by ron howard . the movie is based on a book by sylvia nasar . the movie won four academy awards , including the academy award for best picture . connelly won for best supporting actress .
