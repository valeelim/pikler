Rudi Carrell     rudi carrell ( real name rudolf wijbrand kesselaar ) was a very successful comedian , singer and television presenter . he was born on december 19 , 1934 in alkmaar , in the netherlands . he died of lung cancer on july 7 , 2006 in bremen , germany . he was very popular in germany , for shows like `` die rudi carrell show '' . he was awarded various prizes , amongst others the `` goldene kamera '' for a lifetime of good work , in february of 2006 ( which was his last public appearance ) as well as the bundesverdienstkreuz ( the only award given by the federal republic of germany , for extraordinary achievements in politics or culture ) in 1985 .
