Triple jump     the triple jump ( also known in certain countries as a triple hop , step and jump or hop , skip and jump ) is a athletics event . it has been a part of the world championships since 1995. the event consists of someone running up to a line , jumping and landing on the same foot ( a hop ) , jumping onto the other foot ( a skip ) and then jumping into a shallow pit of sand . the winner is the person whose final jump lands the farthest from the line they started jumping at . it is a common game played on playgrounds .
