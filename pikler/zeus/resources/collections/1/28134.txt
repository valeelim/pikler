Snooker     snooker is a billiards sport for two players . it is popular in the united kingdom , the commonwealth and parts of asia . it is played on a large ( 12 feet by 6 feet ) table that is covered with a smooth green material called baize and has holes or pockets in each of the four corners and in the middle of each of the long side cushions . the players use long thin sticks called cues to strike the white ball ( the cue ball ) . the cue ball must always be hit first when attempting a shot at the target ball . there are 21 target balls in total - 15 red balls ( worth 1 point each ) and 6 colours : a yellow ( 2 points ) , green ( 3 points ) , brown ( 4 points ) , blue ( 5 points ) , pink ( 6 points ) and black ball ( 7 points ) . each target ball must be aimed or 'potted ' into one of the pockets in sequence . a red ball followed by a colour then a red ball again and so on until only the coloured balls are left . the colours then have to be potted in the following order : yellow , green , brown , blue , pink and black .
