Salmon     salmon is a kind of teleost fish . there are many different kinds of salmon . salmon belong to the same family of fish as the trout . most kinds of salmon live in salt water , or migrate between rivers and the sea . many people like to eat salmon , so the fish is also grown in fish farms . usual ways to make food out of salmon are smoking , cold smoking and graving . salmon are killed by people , other fish and their environment . major species . - atlantic salmon - australian salmon - chinook salmon - chum salmon - coho salmon - indian salmon - pink salmon - sockeye salmon other websites . - salmon nation a place and idea for `` reliable prosperity .
