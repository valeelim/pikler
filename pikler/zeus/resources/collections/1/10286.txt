Kermit the Frog     kermit the frog is a muppet created by jim henson . kermit was henson 's trademark fictional character . he was originally performed by henson himself . since henson 's death , kermit has been performed by steve whitmire . kermit has appeared on many tv shows including `` sesame street '' , `` the muppet show '' , sam and friends , the ed sullivan show , and `` muppet babies '' . kermit has also appeared in several films and has been a guest speaker .
