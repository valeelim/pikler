List of countries by area     this is a list of countries by total area . total area is the sum of all land and inland water bodies ( lakes , reservoirs , rivers ) . the list includes the world and the oceans ( rank nrs . 1,2,3,4,5,7 ) , antarctica ( nr . 8 ) , the spratly islands ( claimed by various countries ; nr 256 ) and the 259 countries and dependencies which are also listed above .
