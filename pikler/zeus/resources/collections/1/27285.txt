Animal rights     animal rights is a term used for the general belief that non-human animals have certain natural rights . most animal rights promoters believe that non-human animals have the natural right not to be used by humans for any means , including food , clothing , experimentation , and entertainment . just as human rights are gained simply because someone is a human , animals have animal rights simply because they are an animal . related pages . - natural rights - speciesism - veganism - vegetarianism
