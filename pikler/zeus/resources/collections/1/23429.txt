Age of Empires III     age of empires iii is a 2005 computer game made by the company ensemble studios . it is published by microsoft . it is the third game of the age of empires games , and has better graphics than the ones before it . it is a real-time strategy game . the plot is from 1500 to 1860. an expansion pack , `` age of empires iii : the warchiefs '' , was released for the game on october 17 , 2006. it was published several years ago ( october 18 , 2005 ) , so it will run on almost any recent computer . it is only compatible with microsoft windows xp and windows vista . the game is on three cds . requirements . - cpu : should be above 1400 mhz - memory : 256 mb ram - 2 gb free space on the harddisk .
