Accrington Stanley F.C.    accrington stanley f.c . accrington stanley football club is an english football club . the club will play in league two ( the english fourth division ) in the 2015/16 season . they were re-founded in 1968 after going bankrupt ( having no money ) in 1962. they were first founded in 1921. they are thought of as hard working but having no chance of success . their nickname is stanley . they play at the interlink express stadium in accrington in lancashire , in england .
