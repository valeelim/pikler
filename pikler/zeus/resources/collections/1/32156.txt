Porto Alegre     porto alegre is a brazilian city , capital of the state of rio grande do sul . it has , approximately 1.4 million inhabitants and an area of 496.8 km² . its main famous park is the 'parque dos expedicionários ' also known as 'parque da redenção ' ( redemption park , in english ) . porto alegre became known all over the world by the social world forum . also known worldwide by the hottest city in the world at february , 2009 .
