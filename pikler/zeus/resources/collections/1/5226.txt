Glamorgan     glamorgan , also known as glamorganshire is a county in wales in the west of great britain . glamorgan was a medieval kingdom . its capital is cardiff . the county is home to over a million people , which is more than one third the total of wales . it contains the cities of cardiff , swansea and most of the valleys that are famous for coal mining in the past . other websites . - glamorgan record office - glamorgan family history society
