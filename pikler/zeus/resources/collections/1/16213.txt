Full Metal Jacket     full metal jacket is a 1987 american war drama movie directed by stanley kubrick . it is based on the gustav hasford book `` the short-timers '' . the name of the movie comes from the full-metal jacketed bullets used in the military . the movie follows soldiers who are drafted to fight in the vietnam war . the story is set in the late 1960s and follows the characters from their time in recruit training and them fighting in the war . other websites . - `` full metal jacket '' at the internet movie database ( imdb ) - `` full metal jacket '' screenplay
