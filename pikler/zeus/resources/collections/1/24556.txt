Milwaukee     milwaukee is the largest city in the state of wisconsin . milwaukee is also the 27th-largest in the united states . the city is the county seat of milwaukee county . it is on the southwestern shore of lake michigan , and is about 90 miles ( 145 kilometers ) north of chicago . as of 2009 , about 604,133 people lived in milwaukee . part of milwaukee is in washington county . the city got its name from the indian word `` millioke '' , which is thought to mean `` the good land '' or `` gathering place by the water '' .
