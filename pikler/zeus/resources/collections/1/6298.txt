Diagonal     diagonal is a kind of straight line . a diagonal line does not go straight up , down , or across . it is a line that connects two corners of a shape . in mathematics , `` diagonal '' has different meanings . for example , in geometry a diagonal is any line that goes between the corners of a polygon . so a square has two diagonals , and a five-sided shape has five diagonals.in general the count of diagonal can be identified by a formula [ n ( n-3 ) ] /2 where n is the total number of sides of shapes .
