Kettle     a kettle is a type of pot that is used in a kitchen . it is used to make water hot or to boil water . it comes from the latin word `` catillus '' ( `` deep pan or dish for cooking '' ) . in the past , kettles were made of metal bins like copper . kettles were put on a rack or a hook above the fire to become hot . now , metal kettles are put on the stove or rangetop . sometimes they are made of ceramic or clay . now , some people have electric kettles . these kettles plug into the wall and use electricity to generate heat . they are usually plastic or metal . they heat water faster than kettles on a stove . kettles are often used to make tea . they are more common in the united kingdom and the commonwealth than other places like the united states .
