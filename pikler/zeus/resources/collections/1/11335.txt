Lesbos     lesbos is an island in greece . it is in the northeastern aegean sea . it is one of 51 prefectures of greece . the capital of lesbos is the mytilene . according to classical greek mythology , lesbos was the patron god of the island . the word 'lesbian ' comes from lesbos . an ancient poet , sappho , lived on lesbos . many of her love poems are written to women .
