Amazon.com     amazon.com is an online store that sells books , movies , games , dvds , music cds , computer software and other items ( like amazon kindle ) . it is the largest online retailer right now . it was started by jeff bezos in 1994 and the headquarters is in seattle . the original name was cadabra.com but was changed to amazon later on because it sounded like the word `` cadaver '' . the store is named after the world 's second longest river .
