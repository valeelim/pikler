Cheaper by the Dozen (movie)    cheaper by the dozen ( movie ) cheaper by the dozen refers to two movies based on the book of the same name by frank bunker gilbreth , jr. and ernestine gilbreth carey : - `` cheaper by the dozen '' ( 1950 movie ) , the 1950 original , starring clifton webb - `` cheaper by the dozen '' ( 2003 movie ) , a 2003 remake starring steve martin , ashton kutcher and hilary duff
