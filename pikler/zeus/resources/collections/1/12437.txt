Monotheism     monotheism is the belief in only one god , rather than two or more , which would be polytheism such as ancient greece , ancient rome , ancient egypt and partly hinduism . many religions are monotheistic , such as judaism , christianity , islam , baha'i faith , sikhism , eckankar , zoroastrianism , deism , cao dai and tenrikyo . zoroastrianism is thought to be among the oldest monotheistic religions . other websites . - stanford encyclopedia of philosophy
