New Year's Day    new year 's day new year 's day is a holiday in many countries . it was created to welcome the new year . in the united states and most of the other countries in the world , new year day is celebrated on january 1. the new year is an event that happens when a culture celebrates the end of one year and the beginning of the next . cultures that measure yearly calendars all have new year celebrations . in christmas style dating , the new year started on 25 december . this was used in germany and england until the thirteenth century , and in spain from the fourteenth to the sixteenth century . in annunciation style dating the new year started on 25 march , the feast of the annunciation . this was used in many parts of europe in the middle ages . the style was started by dionysius exiguus in ad 525. annunciation style was used in the kingdom of great britain until january 1 , 1752 , except scotland which changed to circumcision style dating on 1 january 1600. the rest of great britain changed to circumcision style on the 1 january after the change in great britain from the julian calendar to the gregorian calendar on 3/14 september 1752 .
