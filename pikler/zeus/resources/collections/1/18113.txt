Hobo     hobo is a word for a homeless person who travels to different cities and towns to look for work . hobos usually travel by train `` hopping '' ( riding in empty train freight railcars without paying for a ticket ) , or sometimes `` riding the rods '' ( lying dangerously near the train wheels , underneath the cars ) . hobos stay in very cheap hotels in cities , or camp in simple shelters ( `` '' ) in shanty towns near the rail yards . there were many hobos during the great depression in the united states because many people lost their jobs and homes . many people blamed president herbert hoover for the depression , so hobo camps were sometimes called `` hoovervilles '' .
