Hollyoaks     hollyoaks is a british television teen soap opera . it was first broadcast on 23 october 1995 , on channel 4. there is a new episode every weekday . it is set in chester and is mostly filmed in liverpool on abbey road . since 1995 , the cast has expanded from just seven major characters to approximately 50 cast members . it was originally devised by phil redmond , who has also devised shows including `` brookside '' and `` grange hill '' . it is the only british soap that uses incidental music . hollyoaks was the first british television programme to tackle the issue of gay domestic violence . its first character with gender identity disorder , a trans man called jason costello , was in the show august 2010 to december 2011. in 2014 hollyoaks introduced a new transgender character called blessing chambers .
