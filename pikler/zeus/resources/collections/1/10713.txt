Christ     in christianity , the christ ( ; ) is a title for the savior and redeemer who would bring salvation to the jewish people and mankind . christ is the greek translation of the hebrew title `` messiah '' ( ; anointed one ) , the figure promised by god to the jewish people and mankind for the salvation of the world . christians believe that jesus of nazareth is the jewish messiah called christ of the christian old testament and fufilled the messianic prophecies of christian tradition . `` christ '' , used by christians both as a name and as a title , is synonymous with jesus . related pages . - christology
