Gujarati language     gujarati is an indo-aryan language . it is spoken in gujarat , india and also in neighbouring pakistan . it was the `` mother tongue '' of gandhi and muhammad ali jinnah . there are millions of gujaratis who speak it as their first language . gujarati is the 20th most common language in the united states of america . mahatma gandhi , the india 's leader , once said about the gujarati language : `` bad handwriting is a sign of an uncomplete education '' .
