King     a king is a man who rules a country , because of inheritance . a king usually comes to power when the previous monarch dies , who is usually a family member of his . sometimes a person may become king due to the previous monarch 's abdication , for example george vi . for most of history , most countries were ruled in this way , especially in europe . some countries , such as france , are no longer monarchies . some , such as the united kingdom , still have a royal family . in some countries , people chose a new king from other people to decide from . the wife of a king is called a queen . a woman who becomes a ruler because of inheritance is also called a queen . if a country has a king or a queen , that means it is a monarchy . a country which a king or queen rules is called a kingdom . related pages . - emperor - prince - queen - princess
