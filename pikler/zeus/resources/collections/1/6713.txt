Gainesville, Florida    gainesville , florida gainesville is a city in north-central florida in the united states . it is the county seat of alachua county . it is home to the university of florida , the largest public university in the state . an alligator ( gator ) named albert is the mascot of the university of florida . ( a mascot is a symbol of qualities the school admires ) . people think that growth of the city comes from building the university in the city . people of the city show their strong good feelings for the university by going to football games there , and by showing `` gator '' symbols in businesses and homes . the city is also famous as the birthplace of singer tom petty .
