Punishment     punishment is when something is done to a person ( or animal ) that they do not like . it may be because they broke a rule . there are many kinds of punishment , from a death penalty for very bad crimes , to things that parents may do to punish children , like spanking them or taking away their toys . punishment can be seen as good in society to prevent people from doing bad things . it can also seen as cruel and unnecessary . it can also be seen to do more harm than good .
