1008     1008 ( mviii ) was a common year when the julian calendar was used . it was the eighth year of the 2nd millennium and the 11th century . events . - olof , king of sweden , is baptized by saint sigfrid . - bruno of querfurt and others try to establish a mission among the prussians . - oldest known mention of the city of gundelfingen . - mohammed ii succeeds hisham ii as caliph of cordoba . - unification of georgia under king bagrat iii .
