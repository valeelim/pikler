3 (number)    3 ( number ) the number three ( 3 ) is one more than two and one less than four . it is the first mersenne prime . three is an important number for many cultures ( groups of people living together ) . it is also a prime number . it is the first odd prime . in roman numerals , it is iii . it is the only prime that p and formula_1 are both prime . famous threes . - the three stooges - the three doshas ( weaknesses ) in ayurvedic medicine in india - the trimurti ( three aspects of god ) in hindu religion - the holy trinity in christian religion - three gifts of the wise men ( magi ) who visited christ at his birth in the gospel of matthew . - the three bears in the fairy tale goldilocks - the three imperial regalia of japan
