Marti Webb     marti webb is a british actress and singer born in london in 1944. she has notably played roles in musical theatre including `` evita '' , `` cats '' , `` godspell '' , `` the king and i '' , `` annie '' , `` thoroughly modern millie '' , `` the goodbye girl '' and `` song and dance '' . she first came to prominence with the song cycle `` tell me on a sunday '' which was written for her by don black and andrew lloyd webber . webb recently returned to the show after it was revamped for denise van outen in the west end before she took it on a uk tour .
