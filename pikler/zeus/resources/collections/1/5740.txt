W     w is the twenty-third ( number 23 ) letter in the english alphabet . meanings for w. - on calendars , w is most times a short letter for wednesday . - in chemistry , w is the symbol for tungsten , after its german name , `` wolfram '' . - in news story writing , the `` five w 's '' are `` who '' , `` what '' , `` when '' , `` where '' and `` why '' . - it is pronounced as `` double u '' .
