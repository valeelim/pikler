Lille     lille is a city in the north of france . lille has about 226,800 inhabitants . it is the capital of the region nord-pas-de-calais . about two million people live in the urban area ( `` conurbation '' ) shared with kortrijk and tournai . lille has an oceanic climate ( `` cfb '' in the koeppen climate classification ) . it is on the border with belgium . education . - école pour l'informatique et les nouvelles technologies - école spéciale de mécanique et électricité - edhec business school - e-artsup - institut catholique d'arts et métiers - institut supérieur européen de gestion group - institut supérieur européen de formation par l'action lille is twinned with :
