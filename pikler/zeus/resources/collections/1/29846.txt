Menelaus     menelaos ( or latin menelaus ) was a person in greek mythology . he was the son of king atreus of mycenae and aerope . he was the brother of agamemnon . his wife was helen , the most beautiful woman in the world , and with her he had a daughter , hermione . because of his marriage with helen , he was king of sparta . the trojan war was started because paris took helen away .
