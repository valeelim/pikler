List of nearest galaxies     this list of the nearest galaxies to earth is ordered by increasing distance . many of the distances are only estimates , and some may be incorrect by 50 % or more . the distance ranking in this list should be seen only as a guess . distances are given in light years ( ly ) . 1. milky way – home galaxy of earth 1. canis major dwarf 25,000 ly ( satellite of milky way ) 1. sag deg – 81,000 ly ( satellite or part of milky way ) 1. large magellanic cloud – 160,000 ly ( satellite of milky way ) 1. small magellanic cloud – 190,000 ly ( satellite of milky way ) 1. ursa minor dwarf – 205,500 ly ( satellite of milky way ) 1. draco dwarf – 248,000 ly ( satellite of milky way ) 1. sculptor dwarf – 254,000 ly ( satellite of milky way ) 1. sextans dwarf – 257,500 ly ( satellite of milky way ) 1. carina dwarf – 283,500 ly ( satellite of milky way ) 1. fornax dwarf – 427,000 ly ( satellite of milky way ) 1. leo ii – 701,000 ly 1. leo i – 890,000 ly 1. phoenix dwarf – 1,271,000 ly 1. barnards galaxy ( ngc 6822 ) – 1,760,000 ly
