Neopets     neopets is a website that lets its users create virtual pets , and take care of them . the site was created on november 15 1999 , and has hundreds of millions of users . the website is owned by jumpstart . most of the pets that the user can create are not real animals , but are based upon real animals . neopets has story plots , chat boards , and different things that users can do with their neopets . many people of many different ages join neopets . the site has many games that the user can play . neopets has a help section to find answers to many of the questions that users may have . the games length varies from a minute or less to saveable games like neoquest that take more than 10 hours total to complete . a person can make a pet and then feed it by playing games and getting money , or by visiting places to get free food once they have less than 3000 np ( neopoints ) . they can get items and clothes for their pets . they can get special items called paint brushes which change the color of the pet to a different special color
