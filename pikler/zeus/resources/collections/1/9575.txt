Silvio Berlusconi     silvio berlusconi ( born 29 september 1936 in milan ) is one of the most important italian politicians . he was prime minister of italy from may 1994 to january 1995 , june 2001 to may 2006 , and may 2008 to november 2011. he is the leader of a coalition named `` popolo della libertà '' ( people for freedom ) . he created the party `` forza italia '' , renamed then `` popolo delle libertà '' in october 2008. berlusconi is also the most important manager of italy , he is the owner of television group `` mediaset '' , of the editorial group `` mondadori '' of the football team `` a.c. milan '' , of the bank group `` mediolanum spa '' . berlusconi had some problems with justice , including fraud ( 1994 , 1996 ) and corruption ( 1998 ) . in the 1990s he entered politics and he has been head of government in 1994 , in 2001 and since 2008 to 2011. on 26 october 2012 , he was convicted of fraud . on 1 august 2013 , his jail term got confirmed , he will have to serve 4 years , in house arrest , because of his old age . in 2013 , berlusconi was convicted of paying for sex with an underage girl . this conviction was overturned on 18 july 2014 .
