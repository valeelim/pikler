Astatine     astatine is a chemical element . its atomic number ( which is the number of protons in it ) is 85 , and its atomic weight ( which is approximately the number of protons plus the number of neutrons in it ) is 210. it is part of the group 7 ( halogens ) on the periodic table of elements . some of its isotopes may be in nature . but because it is extremely unstable ( roughly speaking , the atoms fall apart ) , only about an ounce exists in the earth 's crust at one point in time . it is radioactive . the longest-living isotope at has a half-life of a bit over eight hours.it is used in the form of an astatine isotope.it is used for cancer treatment and in radioactive tracers .
