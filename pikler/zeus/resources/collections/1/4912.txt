Farmer     a farmer is a person who grows crops and raises animals to be used as food . this has been a common job for people since the beginning of civilization . the word `` farmer '' usually refers to a person who has a field , orchard , vineyard , or garden where food is grown . this food is eaten or sold after it is harvested . farmers may also grow raw materials for industrial purposes . some examples of raw materials that are used in this way are : - cereals for alcoholic beverages - hides ( animal skin ) for leather - maize/corn for plastics or fuel - wool or cotton for yarns and making cloth some farmers also raise animals to be used for meat , milk , eggs or other things . related pages . - agriculture
