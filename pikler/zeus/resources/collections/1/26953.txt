Iambe     iambe was the goddess of rhyme and merriment in greek mythology . she was the daughter of pan and echo . iambe is said to have created the iambic pentameter verse pattern in poetry . this is a metered verse with two different sounds , and it was named after iambe . the iambic pentameter verse is used by many poets , one of which is william shakespeare . in the myth , demeter , was sad about losing her daughter , persephone . it was only iambe who was able to make demeter laugh . she did this by using poetry and wit to cheer demeter up . when demeter was not sad any more , the earth became fertile again . iambe then became demeter 's first priestess .
