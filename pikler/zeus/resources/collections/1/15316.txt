Anteater     anteaters are mammals of the family myrmecophagidae and the suborder vermilingua . anteaters live in south america and central america . anteaters eat ants and termites . they have long , sharp claws and a long , sticky tongue . the tongue can be up to 60 cm long , as long as a person 's arm . the anteater opens an ant nest with its claws . then it licks up the ants with its tongue .
