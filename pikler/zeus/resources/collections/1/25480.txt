1179     events . - third council of the lateran condemned waldensians and cathars as heretics , institutes a reformation of clerical life , and creates the first `` ghettos '' for jews - afonso i is recognized as the true king of portugal by pope alexander iii , bringing portugal the protection of the catholic church against the castilian monarchy - philip ii is crowned king of france - aberdeen is chartered by william the lion - westminster school founded by the monks of westminster abbey ( by papal command ) - drigung kagyu school of kagyu buddhism was founded .
