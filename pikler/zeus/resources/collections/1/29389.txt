Chivas USA    c.d . chivas usa club deportivo chivas usa was an american football ( soccer ) team that played in major league soccer ( mls ) in carson , california . they began play in 2005 , and played at the home of the los angeles galaxy . when the team was formed , it was planned to be a `` little brother '' to the very popular mexican team c.d . guadalajara . `` chivas '' , the spanish word for `` goats '' , is the nickname of c.d . guadalajara . the team was originally owned by two mexican businessmen , one of whom also owned c.d . guadalajara . due to falling attendance and ownership failures , mls bought the team back in february 2014 , with plans to sell it to new owners who would give the team a new name . in september 2014 , the team was sold to a new group . as part of the sale , chivas usa was dissolved at the end of the 2014 season . a new team , los angeles fc , will start play in 2017 replacing the old chivas usa .
