Cherry     cherry is a fruit that grows on a tree or a bush . it belongs to the genus `` prunus '' . it is usually red , with a seed in the middle . it tastes slightly sour , and is often used to flavour cakes and ice cream , or is baked in a pie or cobbler . cherries are also a good source of vitamin b. in japan , yamagata is top producer of cherries . top cherry producing nations - 2009 turkey 417.7 usa 390.7 iran 225.0
