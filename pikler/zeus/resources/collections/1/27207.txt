Thomas the Apostle     saint thomas is one of the twelve apostles of jesus . his name means `` the twin '' . he is most famous for not being present when the other apostles ( jesus 's followers and friends ) first saw jesus after he has been risen from the dead . thomas was not there and when he heard about it , he did not believe his friends , and he said he wanted to see it for himself , even the marks in his hands where jesus had been nailed to the cross . jesus did return and showed thomas . the phrase `` doubting thomas '' ( a term for a person who does not believe something at first ) comes from this story . thomas did n't believe christ had returned until him and 6 other apostles witnessed jesus walking across water to them . related pages . - twelve apostles other websites . - st. thomas the apostle
