Caribbean Community     the caribbean community , or caricom , is a group of nations in the caribbean sea that have come together . they share their products , and they have a government to help organize the area . they are mainly together to help trade and their economies . the full members are : - barbados - belize - dominica - grenada - guyana - haiti - jamaica - montserrat ( a territory of the united kingdom ) - saint kitts and nevis - saint lucia - saint vincent and the grenadines - suriname - trinidad and tobago - united states
