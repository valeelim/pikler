Java     java ( ) is one of the islands of indonesia . the area is 132,000 square km . it is the world 's 13th largest island . around 140 million people live there , which is half the people in indonesia and more than on any other island in the world . most of them speak the javanese language and many also speak other languages . the java sea is to the north , the indian ocean south . the capital city of indonesia , jakarta , is in java . most of java has a tropical monsoon climate ( am in the koeppen climate classification ) . the northern coast of the island is low-lying . the soil is fertile . it is an important area for growing crops in indonesia .
