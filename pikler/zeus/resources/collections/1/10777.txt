Alberto Vargas     alberto vargas ( 1896-1982 ) was a peruvian-born painter , best known for his pin-up paintings of beautiful women . a painting of alberto 's , `` varga girl '' , was put on the beatles - `` sgt . pepper 's lonely hearts club band '' album . she is pictured in the upper right-hand corner ( next to richard menkin , the man in the hat ) . - alberto vargas , a history , family photos and gallery . - vargas on `` the pin-up files '' - alberto vargas biography - artwork on the american art archives web site
