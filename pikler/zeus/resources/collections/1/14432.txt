Fin     a fin is a surface used to produce lift and thrust or to steer while traveling in water , air , or other fluid media . the word was first used for the limbs of fish , but it is now also used when describing other animal limbs and man-made devices . the fins of fish are called : - dorsal fin on the back . function : anti-roll stability . - caudal or tail fin : propulsion - pectoral fins ( paired ) : steering - pelvic fins ( paired ) : manoeuvering - anal fin : stability
