Hatred     hate or hatred is an emotion of very strong dislike for someone or something . it is a desire to avoid , restrict , remove , or destroy that person or thing . it is often thought the opposite of love . sigmund freud said that hatred was a selfish feeling that wishes to get rid of whatever is making that person unhappy . the `` penguin dictionary of psychology '' says that hate is a `` deep , enduring ( long-lasting ) , intense emotion expressing , anger , and towards a person , group , or object . '' hatred can often cause the hate crime and international covenant on civil and political rights prohibits inciting hatred by the article 20 .
