Christian Bale     christian charles philip bale ( born january 30 , 1974 ) is an english actor . he is from haverfordwest in pembrokeshire . bale is best known for playing the part of batman in christopher nolan 's `` the dark knight trilogy '' . he began in a movie of steven spielberg empire of the sun at the age of 13. he also played in `` american psycho '' and `` the machinist '' . he is married to sandra blažić since 2000 and they have one child together .
