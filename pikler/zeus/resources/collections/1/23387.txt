Zhuge Liang     zhuge liang ( 181-234 ) was a chinese politician and general of the ancient shu han dynasty . he helped his lord liu bei conquer the country `` ba shu '' from 211 to 214 , along with pang tong . after liu bei 's death in 223 , zhuge liang was a regent for the new emperor liu shan . zhuge liang attempted to conquer the southern wei territories five times but had no success . he died in 234 , leaving the regency to jiang wei .
