Palo Alto, California    palo alto , california palo alto is a city in the american state of california that has a population of about 55,000 people . it is in the region called silicon valley and close to stanford university . it is the home of many innovators and entrepreneurs . `` palo alto '' means tall tree in spanish after a famous redwood that was struck down by lightning in 1955. a diverse population thrives in a city known for its schools , churches , restaurants and mild climate . palo alto is where apple ceo , steve jobs died on october 5 , 2011. palo alto is also where actor bruce namet was born and where he died 76 years later
