Melting     melting is the process of changing something from a solid into a liquid ( like ice into liquid water ) . it is the opposite of freezing . the temperature at which something begins to melt is called its melting point . when an object is heated , the object 's particles begin to move faster and faster until the structured particles are freed . now it is a liquid . it is a solid that is turning into a liquid . related pages . - freezing - sublimation
