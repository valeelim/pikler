Manchuria     manchuria ( northeast china ) is the homeland of the manchu people ( related to the chinese and mongolians ) . to the chinese , the region is simply known as the northeast ( chinese : 东北 , dōngbĕi ) . manchuria is made up of china 's three northeastern most provinces : liaoning , jilin , and heilongjiang . parts of manchuria were annexed by the russian empire in the past , and they are now ruled by russia . manchuria is a translation of the chinese word manzhou ( ) . in the early history , manchuria was under the control of many chinese kingdoms . later , the manchu people formed their own empire called jurchen . they eventually invaded china and founded the chinese qing dynasty . although china signed a treaty with russia , the russians took advantage of the weak qing government and annexed the part that touches the pacific ocean , where the modern chinese city of dalian stands today . japan then attacked the russians to take this important piece of land . later , japan invaded the chinese part of manchuria too ( see manchukuo ) . this eventually led to world war ii .
