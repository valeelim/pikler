Artemis     artemis is a goddess in greek mythology , and one of the twelve olympians . she is the daughter of zeus and leto and the twin sister of apollo . she is the goddess of hunting , wild animals and the wilderness . during the 5th century bc , artemis became also known as the goddess of the moon , becoming one with the goddess selene . she is known as a goddess of childbirth as well because she helped leto give birth to apollo . she is also known as the goddess who asked zeus to remain chaste and unmarried . she was also the patroness of sparta the city-state where a the warriors came from in ancient greece . in greek mythology , artemis is the daughter of zeus and leto , and the twin sister of apollo . leto had to find a place where she could give birth to the two . this was because of a curse set by hera , zeus ' wife , because she was very angry with zeus . for this , zeus raised an island that had been floating underwater and had not yet been touched by the sun 's rays . the island was delos . leto gave birth there , while grasping hold of a palm-tree .
