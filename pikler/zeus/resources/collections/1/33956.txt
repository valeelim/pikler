Average     an average or mean is a number that is typical of a group of numbers . the average of a group of numbers is often located in between , in the middle of that group of numbers . an average is found from the group of numbers by arithmetic . the most common type of average is the mean . in some sports , such as cricket and baseball , averages are used to tell how good a player is ( such as batting average ) . sporting averages have special rules for how to calculate them .
