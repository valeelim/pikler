Zaragoza     zaragoza ( sometimes called saragossa in english ) is the capital of aragon , in the ebro valley , in the crossroad from barcelona , valencia , madrid , and bilbao . the population is 643,000. the most important monument is the nuestra señora del pilar ( `` basilica of our lady of the pillar '' ) . zaragoza has a semi-arid climate ( bsh in the köppen climate classification ) with hot summers and cool winters .
