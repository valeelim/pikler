Natalie Imbruglia     natalie jane imbruglia ( born february 4 , 1975 ) is an australian actress and singer-songwriter . imbruglia was born in sydney , new south wales to an anglo-australian mother and italian-australian father . imbruglia began acting in australian television commercials and was in spots for coca-cola and the popular australian snack twisties . by age 16 , she left high school to start a career in acting . she soon had the chance to play a regular role on the australian soap opera `` neighbours '' . she left the show to move to london in 1994. she received an mtv award for best new artist in 1998. she had 3 grammy award nominations in 1999. that same year , she also won two brit awards for best international newcomer and best international female . she has sold over 9 million albums worldwide . discography . - `` left of the middle '' ( 1997 ) - `` white lilies island '' ( 2001 ) - `` counting down the days '' ( 2005 ) - `` come to life '' ( 2009 )
