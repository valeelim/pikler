Leaving Certificate     the leaving certificate is the final exam in the republic of ireland secondary school education system . students have to do the junior certificate before they can take the leaving certificate . students also have to take maths and english . if they took irish during the junior certificate , they also have to take irish . students have to take exams on at least six subjects . they can take either high or ordinary level tests . in irish and maths , there is also a foundation level . this is for people who have trouble with that subject . the results are given out in the middle of august . a leaving certificate is required in order to go on to college or university .
