Hypnos     hypnos is the god of sleep in greek mythology . he is the son of nyx and the twin brother of thanatos , the god of death . his sons are the oneroi : morpheus , phobetor and phantasos . his roman equivalent was `` somnus '' . hypnos was represented as a gentle young man , usually with wings attached to his temples or shoulders ( even though he tended to kill roman workers that dozed off during their jobs ) .
