Emission     emission is a word that originally comes from latin . originally it means something that is sent out . several things can be sent out : - the most common use talks about emission of toxic gases . those gases get produced by factories and motors , like motors in cars . - people talk about radio emissions . those are programmes that people can listen to if they have a radio receiver . - there could be emissions of loud noise , someone might be making loud noises . - there also are emissions of light .
