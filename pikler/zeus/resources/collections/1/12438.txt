Polytheism     polytheism means believing in many gods . a person that believes in polytheism is called a polytheist . a religion with polytheism can be called a polytheistic religion . polytheism is well documented in historical religions of classical antiquity , especially those of ancient greeks and romans . other ancient people who were polytheists include german pagans , ancient egyptians , the celts and the norse . there are various polytheistic religions practiced today , for example hinduism , shintoism , chinese folk religion , thelema , wicca , druidism , taoism , asatru and candombl√© . usually , a polytheistic religion has a set of stories about the gods . this is called mythology .
