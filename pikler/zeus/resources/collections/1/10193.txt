Chicago     chicago is the largest city in illinois , usa . it is the third largest city in the united states , although it used to be the second largest . chicago is next to one of the five great lakes , lake michigan . in the 2000 census , almost 2,900,000 people lived there , with six million more people living nearby . the city also has about 300 bridges . chicago is sometimes called the `` windy city '' . the city was founded by jean baptiste point du sable . chicago was incorporated as a city in 1837 , near a portage between the great lakes and the mississippi river watershed . the name `` chicago '' is the french version of the miami-illinois word '' shikaakwa '' ( `` stinky onion '' ) , named for the plants common along the chicago river . today , chicago is listed as an alpha+ global city by the globalization and world cities research network , and ranks seventh in the world in the 2012 global cities index . the city is an international hub for finance , commerce , industry , telecommunications , and transportation , with o'hare international airport being the second-busiest airport in the world in terms of traffic movements . the first skyscraper in the world , the home insurance building , was built in chicago . in september 2013 , the fbi named chicago `` the murder capital of the united states '' with 550 murders recorded in 2012 .
