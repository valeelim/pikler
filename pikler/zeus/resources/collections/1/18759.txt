Lake Chaubunagungamaug     lake chaubunagungamaug , or lake webster is a lake in the united states . it is in the state of massachusetts . the lake is also known by the much shorter name of `` webster lake '' , but some people prefer the long name chargoggagoggmanchauggagoggchaubunagungamaugg , which is the native american name for the lake . the name is the longest place name in the united states .
