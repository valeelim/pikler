Namur (city)    namur ( city ) namur ( , ) is a city in belgium . it is both the capital of the province with the same name , as well as of wallonia , one of the three administrative regions of belgium . it is in the french-speaking part of belgium . in 2012 , there were 112'246 people in namur . namur is at 50° 27 north , 04° 51 east .
