Peter Handke     peter handke ( born december 6 , 1942 in griffen ( carinthia ) ) is an austrian author and translator of books . peter handke is known for his extremely critical ( and perhaps even absurd ) plays . perhaps his best-known play is called `` publikumsbeschimpfung '' ( translates roughly as `` insulting /offending the audience '' ) . the play consists of actors insulting and offending the audience , and later congratulating them for the good performance . the play has been labelled as `` antitheater '' .
