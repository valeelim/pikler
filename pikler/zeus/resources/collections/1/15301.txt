Tilapia     tilapia is a fish that is often farmed for food , or kept as pets in aquariums . they live in warm , fresh water , but some species live in brackish water . the fish are found in africa , north and south america , india , and sri lanka . they are listed as one of the worlds worst introduced species . they can quickly take over from native fish in rivers . this has happened in places like in the endeavour river , in queensland , australia . these fish are thought to have been released into the river from an aquarium .
