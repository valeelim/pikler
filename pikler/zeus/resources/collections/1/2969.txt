Saarland     saarland is a bundesland ( state ) of germany . it has an area of and 1,014,000 people living in it ( 2011 ) . saarland lies in the south-west of germany , near the french border near metz . the capital is saarbrücken . in 1920 , the treaty of versailles made saarland from parts of the prussian rhine province ( ) and the rhine palatinate ( ) . the area was put under the control of the league of nations for 15 years . after a plebiscite ( vote ) in 1935 , germany got the land back . after world war ii the saarland came under french administration . saarland was allowed its own government for most things in 1947 but not independence . germany got the saarland back in 1957 and it became a bundesland . in the south is france , in the north-west luxembourg and in the north and east is rhineland-palatinate . saarland is divided in 6 districts : - merzig-wadern ( the largest district ) - neunkirchen - saarbrücken - saarlouis - saar-pfalz-kreis ( saar-palatinate-district ) - sankt wendel cities . - saarbrücken - neunkirchen
