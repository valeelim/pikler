Sri Yukteswar Giri     sri yukteswar giri ( may 10 , 1855 - march 9 , 1936 ) was an indian guru and swami . his real name was priya nath karar and was born in serampore , west-bengal , india . he is one of the famous people who appears on the cover of the beatles ' sgt . pepper 's lonely hearts club band . he became known through `` autobiography of a yogi '' as the guru of the author paramahansa yogananda . george harrison , the beatle , was initiated in kriya yoga in usa in self-realization fellowship , an organization created by the known writer and great spiritual master in the west paramahansa yogananda . other websites . - kriya yoga institute - kriya yoga of mahavatar babaji website
