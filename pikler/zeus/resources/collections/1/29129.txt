John the Baptist     john the baptist ( also called john the baptizer or yahya the baptizer ) is viewed as a prophet by at least three religions : christianity , islam , and mandaeanism . according to the new testament , he was a preacher who baptized people . john called people to turn to god and to prepare for the coming of the messiah ( jesus ) . he baptized jesus in the jordan river . people would go to him so that he would put them in the water to show that they have turned away from their sins , so that they could get into heaven . john was the son of zechariah and elizabeth , and a relative of jesus . he was known for living in the wild , wearing clothes made from camel 's hair , and eating locusts and honey . in catholicism , his birthday is celebrated on june 24 .
