Ulcer     an ulcer is a kind of open wound . ulcers can be on surfaces of the body . they can be on skin . they can be mucous membranes like the surface of the stomach or inside the mouth . ulcers start when the top layer ( surface ) of skin or mucous membrane is hurt . this top layer dies . when it dies , the skin or mucous membrane and opens . this leaves an open sore called an ulcer . ulcers can be from infection with germs . ulcers can be from pressure on a part of the skin . ulcers can be from substances that will burn skin or mucous membranes . peptic ulcers are sometimes from burning by the acid in the stomach . ulcers can be from cancer . ulcers can be from diseases of blood vessels . if not enough blood goes to a body part , it can get an ulcer . stress can also sometimes cause ulcers . ulcers are often very painful .
