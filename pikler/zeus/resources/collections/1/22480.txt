Fasting     fasting is a kind of abstinence . it is when someone does not eat or drink for some specified period of time . it is only the case if the person does so of his or her own will . it may also be that the person only eats and drinks certain kinds of food . most often , fasting is done for religious reasons . depending on the tradition , fasting practices may forbid sexual intercourse , ( or any sexual desire ) , masturbation , as well as refraining from eating certain types or groups of food ( e.g . meat ) . fasting for religious and spiritual reasons has been a part of human custom since pre-history . it is mentioned in the qur'an , in the mahabharata , in the upanishads , and in the bible , in both the old and new testament . in buddhism it is also taught as a way to getting nirvana . in almost all cases , there are exceptions for observing the fasting . these are made for the following groups of people - children - pregnant women - those who have a disease , or are recovering from one - in some cases , the old people
