Kamishibai     kami-shibai ( japanese : 紙芝居 ) is a storytelling performance with picture cards , and is a traditional japanese entertainment for children . in japanese `` kami '' means paper and `` shibai '' means drama or enternainment . the performer tells a story and shows pictures from scene to scene . kami-shibai is a bidirectional media which is comprised of actions and reactions between a performer and audiences . with the spread of television , kami-shibai went out of date .
