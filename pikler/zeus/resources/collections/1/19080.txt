Laura Ingalls Wilder     laura ingalls wilder ( february 7 , 1867 - february 10 , 1957 ) was an american writer . she was born in wisconsin and spent her childhood in missouri and kansas . she became a teacher in south dakota at age 16. wilder is best known as the author of `` little house on the prairie '' series of books . a tv series , `` little house on the prairie '' , was inspired by the story of her life as told in her books . the series starred melissa gilbert as laura and michael landon as charles 'pa ' ingalls . other websites . - little house wiki : laura ingalls wilder
