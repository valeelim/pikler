Dionne Warwick     dionne warwick ( born december 12 , 1940 ) is an american r & b singer . she was born in east orange , new jersey . warwick sang many songs written by burt bacharach and hal david . many of these songs were hits . in 1990 , warwick hosted and executive produced a talk show , `` dionne and friends '' . she was a contestant in the 2011 season of `` the apprentice '' . warwick 's sister dee dee warwick ( 1942-2008 ) , their aunt cissy houston ( born 1933 ) and cousin whitney houston ( 1963-2012 ) were singers .
