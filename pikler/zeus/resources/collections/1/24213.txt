Food pyramid     a food pyramid is a chart that can be used to see how many servings of each food should be eaten each day . it is for having good health . grains give carbohydrates and some vitamins and minerals . vegetables and fruits give a lot of vitamins , some minerals , and few fats , but fruits often have more calories and sugar . dairy products ( like milk , cheese , yogurt , etc . ) have protein , some fat , and a lot of calcium , an element that helps build strong bones . meat have protein , some fat , and b vitamins . fats , oils , and sweets give calories , fats , and sugars but not a lot of nutrition . other pages . - healthy diet
