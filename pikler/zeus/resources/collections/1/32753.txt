Gran Canaria     gran canaria is one of the canary islands in the atlantic ocean . the capital and biggest city is las palmas de gran canaria in the north of the island . economical centre ( tourism ) of the island is in the south . gran canaria is the second most populous island of the canary islands , after tenerife . gran canaria lives - like most of the canary islands - mainly on tourism , although las palmas does have one of the most important ports in the atlantic . the touristic centres maspalomas and playa del ingles in the south are especially popular . the climate varies very little through the year . gran canaria is very popular for its party tourism . the international airport is farther north . the administration of the canary islands is shared between tenerife and gran canaria .
