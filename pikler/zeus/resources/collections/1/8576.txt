The Lost World: Jurassic Park    the lost world : jurassic park the lost world : jurassic park ( known also as the lost world ) is an american movie directed by steven spielberg . it was released in 1997. it is the sequel to the movie `` jurassic park '' . `` the lost world '' is the second in a series of three movies . it was followed by `` jurassic park iii '' and `` jurassic world '' . it is based on a book by michael crichton , and sticks generally to the plot of the book . the movie was quite successful but not as much as the first .
