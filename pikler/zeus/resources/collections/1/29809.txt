Okeanid     the okeanids are the daughters of the titans okeanos and tethys in greek mythology . they are water and ocean nymphs . also okeanid can mean all the children of okeanos and tethys , which includes the other water and river gods . many people also know these goddesses are sex/love inditents . among them are : - doris , wife of nereus and mother of the nereids - klymene - metis , mother of athena - pleione , mother of the pleiades - styx
