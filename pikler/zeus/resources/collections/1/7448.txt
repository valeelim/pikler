Luanda     luanda is the capital and the largest city of angola . its former name was loanda . it is a main seaport of the country on the atlantic ocean . in 1995 , about 3 million people lived there . it is a center of manufacturing , but very destroyed because of a long civil war . it was founded by portuguese in 1575 as são paulo de luanda and has been the administrative center of angola since 1627 ( except for 1640–1648 ) . when angola got independence in 1975 , it became the capital of angola .
