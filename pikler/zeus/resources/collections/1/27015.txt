Viola sororia     viola sororia , also known as viola papilionacea , common blue violet , common meadow violet , and purple violet is a species of violet . it is a perennial flowering plant that lives in most of eastern north america . the common blue violet is the state flower of the u.s. states of rhode island , illinois , and new jersey . in developed areas it is often said to be a weed because it grows well in lawns . references . - viola sororia from the connecticut botanical society - viola sororia from the virginia tech weed identification guide
