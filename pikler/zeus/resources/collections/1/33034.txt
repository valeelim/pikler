Tap (valve)    tap ( valve ) a tap , faucet , or spigot is something that a liquid or gas comes out of . taps usually have a handle that can be turned to start or stop the liquid coming out . many taps give water and are used in homes . they are found in kitchens and bathrooms . water that comes out of a tap is called `` tap water '' . at many restaurants , a person can ask for tap water instead of bottled water .
