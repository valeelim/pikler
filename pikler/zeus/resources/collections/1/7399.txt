British     a british person or object comes from the united kingdom of great britain ( england , scotland , wales ) and northern ireland . it is sometimes used by the isle of man or the channel islands ( near france ) . it can also be used for some overseas places like gibraltar ( near spain ) or the falkland islands ( near south america ) . the term `` british '' is often used to describe something unique to the uk , for example british people , 'the british way of life ' or 'the british weather ' . sometimes it is accidentally used to describe some english , though it is usually over looked because english is british . britain is not england , though . a british person can also be called a briton . a unique symbol of the `` british '' is the union jack flag which is recognised globally as a symbol of the united kingdom . although being british is a collective identity , the countries of england , scotland northern ireland and wales have separate identities and flags which make them unique .
