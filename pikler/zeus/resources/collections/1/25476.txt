Chris Martin     christopher anthony john `` chris '' martin is an english singer and pianist for the band coldplay . he also plays the guitar . he was born in exeter , devon , england on 2 march 1977. he married gwyneth paltrow in 2003. they have two children , a daughter , apple ( born 2004 ) and a son , moses ( born 2006 ) . the couple separated in 2014. his band members are called jonny buckland ( lead guitarist ) , guy berryman ( bassist ) and will champion ( drummer ) . they met while studying at university college london . in january 1998 , they formed the rock band coldplay . they were influenced by the scottish rock band travis . u2 was another important influence on martin both musically and politically . martin does not smoke cigarettes or drink alcohol . peta said he was the world 's sexiest vegetarian in 2005 .
