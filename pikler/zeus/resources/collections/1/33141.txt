Methodism     methodism , or the methodist movement , is a group of protestant denominations . the movement started in britain in the 18th century and spread to the united states and the british empire . originally it was popular with workers , poor farmers , and slaves . the founder of methodism was mr. john wesley who was a priest of the church of england . his brother charles wesley was a famous writer of church music . related pages . - george whitefield other websites . - united methodist church website
