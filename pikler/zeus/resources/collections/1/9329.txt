Vengaboys     the vengaboys are a dutch pop music group . the group is made up of dj danski and dj delmondo . they toured spanish beach parties in a bus . the group formed in 1992. they added four dancers in 1995-1996 and toured through europe . their first song was `` up & down '' . it was released in march 1997. the group is popular across europe and have had some popularity in the united states . their music is eurodance . other websites . -
