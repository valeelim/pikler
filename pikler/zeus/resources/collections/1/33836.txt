Hunting     hunting is going out to find and kill animals . animals and some humans , hunt for food . people have hunted at least since the stone age . they used spears , and now people mostly use guns and bows . some people kill the animals for fur , to make clothes and shelter , or to decorate their homes , or to sell . fox hunting is sometimes a sport . many places have rules that limit hunting . hunting can be good by keeping animal populations from getting too high . hunting too much , though , can kill off species of animals , making them extinct . hunting once made the dodo , a bird , become extinct . other articles . - animal trapping
