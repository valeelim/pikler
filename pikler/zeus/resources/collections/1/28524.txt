Ellipsoid     an ellipsoid is a shape like a sphere , but it may be longer in one or more directions , like an egg , or a rugby ball . it is the 3 dimensional analogue of an ellipse . the earth is shaped slightly like an ellipsoid . its shape is called an oblate spheroid . an ellipsoid shaped like the earth , called a reference ellipsoid , is often preferred to the geoid because it makes calculations simpler . related pages . - ellipse
