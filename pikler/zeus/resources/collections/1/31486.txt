Sloth bear     the sloth bear ( `` melursus ursinus '' ) is a bear that lives in india and sri lanka . sloth bears have long and shaggy black fur , but its fur can also be brown . on its chest it has a white or yellow mark , which is shaped like an y or v. they have big feet with long claws . the long claws are also the reason for its english name , because the claws look like the claws of a sloth . sloth bears eat insects , like for example termites . but they also eat fruit , honey , eggs , and also meat . female sloth bears usually have 1-2 babies , sometimes 3. young sloth bears stay with their mother for 2-3 years . in captivity they can live to be 40 years old . sloth bear adults can weigh anywhere from 120-130 pounds and measure anywhere form 60-70 inches in length .
