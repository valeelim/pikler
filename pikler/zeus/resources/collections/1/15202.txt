Carl Radle     carl dean radle ( born june 18 , 1942 tulsa , oklahoma , died may 30 , 1980 ) was an american musician , best known for being the bassist in derek and the dominos . radle worked with many well-known artists and worked on many gold and platinum singles and albums . radle is thought by many to have been the best bass guitar sideman in the music business . in may 30 , 1980 , radle died at age 37 , of problems from a kidney infection caused by his alcoholism and drug addiction .
