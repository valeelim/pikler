Pig Latin     pig latin is a language code or game and is most often used by children who speak english . the usual rules are : 1. for words that begin with consonant sounds , move all the consonant sounds to the end of the word and add `` ay . '' 1. for words that begin with vowel sounds , simply add `` ay '' to the end of the word . pig latin that you write is sometimes written with dashes , between the first part and the `` c-ay '' letters , and sometimes it is not . a sentence can be `` is-thay is-ay an-ay example-ay of-ay ig-pay atin-lay ith-way ashes-day '' , or `` isthay isay anay exampleay ofay igpay atinlay ithoutway ashesday .
