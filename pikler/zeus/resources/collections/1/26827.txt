Hemera     hemera was the goddess of daylight in greek mythology . she was the daughter of erebus and nyx , and the sister of moros , charon , keres and nemesis . hyginus lists their children as uranus , gaia , and thalassa ( the primordial sea goddess ) , while hesiod only lists thalassa as their child . aether was her brother and consort . hyginus listed gaia , thalassa and ouranos as their children . in hesiod 's `` theogony '' , hemera was only mother of thalassa . hemera 's mother was the goddess of the night and her father was the god of darkness . they were brother and sister and married and had kids hemera was one of them . her grandmother was the goddess of air .
