Phil Collins     philip david charles `` phil '' collins ( born 30 january 1951 ) is an english singer , drummer and actor . he was born in chiswick , hounslow , middlesex . he plays with the band genesis . he is very well known for his career away from the band , as a solo singer . his most well known album is `` no jacket required '' , which has sold over ten million copies in the united states . one of his most well known songs is `` in the air tonight '' , which had a long running false rumour that it was about a drowning that collins had witnessed . collins starred in the movie `` buster '' ( 1988 ) . collins won the academy award for best original song and the golden globe award for best original song for `` you 'll be in my heart '' . that song was in the movie `` tarzan '' . discography . solo albums . - `` face value '' ( 1981 ) - `` hello , i must be going !
