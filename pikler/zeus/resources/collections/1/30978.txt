Cetology     cetology is a type of science . it is the study of the cetaceans , which are whales , dolphins , and porpoises . cetology is the branch of marine mammal science that studies the approximately eighty species of whales , dolphins , and porpoise in the scientific order cetacea . the term was coined in the mid-19th century from the greek cetus ( `` whale '' ) and -ology ( `` study '' ) . cetologists , or those who practice cetology , seek to understand and explain cetacean evolution , distribution , morphology , behavior , community dynamics , and other topics .
