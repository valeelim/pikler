Cone     in common speaking and geometry , a cone is a solid object that one gets when one rotates a right triangle around one of its two short sides , the cone 's axis . the disk made by the other short side is called the base , and the point of the axis which is not on the base is the cone 's `` apex '' or `` vertex '' . an object that is shaped like a cone is `` conical '' . related pages . - conic section
