Hermann Minkowski     hermann minkowski ( 22 june 1864 in kaunas – 12 january 1909 in göttingen ) was a german mathematician of jewish descent . he was one of albert einstein 's teachers . minkowski is perhaps best known for his work in relativity , in which he showed in 1907 that his former student albert einstein 's special theory of relativity ( 1905 ) , could be understood geometrically as a theory of four-dimensional space-time , since known as the `` minkowski spacetime '' .
