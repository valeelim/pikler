Native speaker     a native speaker is someone who speaks a language as his or her first language or mother tongue . native speakers can often speak the language well since it was part of their childhood development . a native speaker 's language is usually the language their parents speak and/or the language of their country of origin . it is the only language of a monolingual person , and likely the first language of a multilingual person which is acquired naturally in their native environment and which serves as the basis for their sociolinguistic identity . it is also a term used to describe native english-speaking language instructors who teach english in non-english speaking countries .
