Wallonia     wallonia ( french : `` wallonie '' , ) or the walloon region ( french : `` région wallonne '' ) is a french-speaking region in the south of belgium . its official languages are french ( 98 % ) and german ( 2 % ) . many people understand the local language walloon , some still speak it , and few people write it . german is spoken mainly in the area of the german-speaking community of belgium the capital of the region is namur . other important cities are : liège , charleroi , mons , tournai and verviers . french speaking walloons share the french community with french speaking people of brussels . wallonia is one of three regions in belgium . the other two regions are flanders ( or flemish region ) and brussels-capital region .
