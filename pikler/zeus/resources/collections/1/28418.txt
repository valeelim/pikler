Gillingham F.C.    gillingham f.c . gillingham football club is an english football club . they are based in the town of gillingham , kent . they are currently in the football league one . they play home games at priestfield stadium . gillingham are managed by justin edinbourgh and the chairman is paul scally . the club captain , michael flynn , plays in central midfield . they finished their last season ( 2015-16 ) in 9th .
