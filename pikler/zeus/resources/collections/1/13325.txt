Berkeley, California    berkeley , california berkeley is a city in the u.s. state of california . it is a suburb of san francisco . it is famous for the college , university of california , berkeley , where many political protests were held in the 1960s . it is also famous for its gourmet food and art scenes . also , it is the native city of the punk band green day . boinc or the berkeley open infrastructure for network computing was created at the university of california in berkeley .
