Bhutan     bhutan ( officially called kingdom of bhutan ) is a small country in the himalaya mountains of south asia . it is ruled by king jigme khesar namgyel wangchuck , who has been king since 2006. bhutan was founded in 1644 by shabdrung ngawang namgyel . the bhutanese people are proud they have always been an independent country . bhutan 's capital city is thimphu . the official language is dzongkha . about 600,000 people live in bhutan . the people and government of bhutan are proud of their culture which is based on tibetan buddhism . 97 % of bhutan 's people are buddhist . until 1974 bhutan was closed to the outside world . now people can visit the country , but only in small numbers . the only airport is in paro district . the country is bordered on the south by the republic of india and on the north by tibet , which is part of china since 1949. the indian state of sikkim separates bhutan and nepal . the main export of bhutan is hydroelectricity which is sold to india . the economy of bhutan is very small but is growing quickly . the currency is the ngultrum , which is pegged at par with the indian rupee .
