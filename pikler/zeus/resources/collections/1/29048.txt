Volkswagen Polo     the volkswagen polo is a small car made by the german company volkswagen since 1975. it has five seats and can have two to five doors , and entered its fifth generation in 2009. the first generation was made from 1975 to 1981 with a facelift in 1979. the second generation was made between 1981 and 1994 and was facelifted in 1990. the third generation was made from late 1994 to early 2002 with a facelift in early 2000. the fourth generation launched in early 2002 and featured distinctive twin round headlamps , they were dropped when it was facelifted in mid-2005 . the fifth generation arrived in 2009 and was facelifted in mid-2014 . the sixth generation has recently been revealed and will go on sale later in 2017 .
