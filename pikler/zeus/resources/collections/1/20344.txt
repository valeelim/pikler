Ólafur Ragnar Grímsson    ólafur ragnar grímsson ólafur ragnar grímsson ( born may 14 , 1943 ) is an icelanic politician . he served as the fifth president of iceland , from 1996 to 2016 , re-elected without opposition in 2000 , and was re-elected in 2004 , 2008 , and 2012. he was born in ísafjörður , iceland . from 1962 to 1970 , he studied economics and political science at the university of manchester . he was a professor at the university of iceland for political science , served as a member of the althing , and was the minister of finance ( 1988–1991 ) . he married guðrún katrín þorbergsdóttir in 1974 , who gave birth to twin daughters the following year . guðrún katrín was very popular in iceland , and her charm is without a doubt one of the main reasons her husband was elected ; she charmed the nation from the start of his campaign . her death from cancer in 1998 was a shock to the nation and her family . ólafur 's second marriage was to dorrit moussaieff to whom he had been engaged since may 2000. this took place on his 60th birthday may 14 , 2003 .
