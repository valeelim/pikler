Ceuta     ceuta is a spanish city in north africa , at the strait of gibraltar . the city area is about 20 square kilometers , and there are over 82,000 people living in the city . the city is surrounded by a border fence , which has been built to keep the moroccans ( and other africans ) from moving there unlawfully . ceuta is part of spain ( and therefore the european union ) . until it became a self-governing city in 1994 , it belonged to the cadiz province . it was built by the phoenicians at a strategic position .
