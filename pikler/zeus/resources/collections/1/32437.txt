Mezzo-soprano     a mezzo-soprano is a female singer whose voice is a little lower than that of a soprano but not low enough to be called a contralto . there are many roles in opera that need a mezzo-soprano voice , for example the role of carmen in the opera `` carmen '' by georges bizet or cherubino in `` le nozze di figaro '' by mozart . famous mezzo-sopranos include : marilyn horne and dame janet baker who have both now retired . alice coote and susan graham are famous mezzo-sopranos today .
