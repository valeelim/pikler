Tintagel Castle     tintagel castle is in cornwall , a county in england . reginald , earl of cornwall built the castle on tintagel head ( a piece of land by the sea ) in about 1150. in cornish legend , this is the place where the old kings of cornwall lived . reginald built the castle for propaganda , not for military use ; he wanted people to think of camelot when they thought about him . some people say king arthur was born at the castle , because traditional stories say the king of cornwall looked after him when he was a child . the modern village of tintagel was first called trevena . then , in the 1850s , it got a new name , to bring in tourism because of the king arthur and camelot legends . really , tintagel is only the name of land by the sea .
