Game Boy Color     the game boy color is a video game console manufactured by nintendo . it was first introduced in japan on october 1998. it is the successor to the original game boy , but it precedes the game boy advance . the biggest innovation of the game boy color was its colorful graphics . the game boy color is almost as powerful as the nintendo entertainment system . with the exception of the pokemon mini , it was the last 8-bit console made by a mainstream company . the last game released for game boy color was `` harry potter and the chamber of secrets '' in 2002 .
