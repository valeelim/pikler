Kanchipuram     kanchipuram is one of the seven most holy cities of hindus . it is about southwest of chennai , tamilnadu state , india . kanchipuram was an important religious and cultural place of ancient india . the city continues to be an important religious place . for a long time from the 6th century till the 8th century , the city was capital city of the pallava dynasty . there are many temples in the city . there is a math ( religious centre ) of shankaracharya in this city .
