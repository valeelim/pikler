Heresy     heresy is a word used by different religious groups , used to describe someone who has ideas that are different from what the religion or law teaches . such a person is known as heretic . in the middle ages it was not uncommon to accuse someone of heresy . if the accusations could be proven , the culprit would go through a ritual . since torture could be used , the accusations were often proven . the ritual was done to save the soul of the convicted criminal . it involved being burnt while tied to a post . still in the 21st century muslims who apostatize are often treated very harshly and frequently killed . jesus christ himself was regarded as a heretic by the jewish leaders at the time- see gospel of matthew 26:57-67 the roman catholic church sees a few heresies in protestantism : - protestants claim only scripture ( the bible ) is relevant for the faith ( `` sola scriptura '' ) ; the catholic church says traditions are also important . - protestants say that belief is enough to be saved ( `` sola fide '' ) . catholics say that good deeds are necessary as well .
