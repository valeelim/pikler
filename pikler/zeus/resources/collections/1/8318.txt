Jyväskylä     jyväskylä is the capital of the finnish province central finland by the northern end of lake päijänne . there are almos about 135.000 inhabitants in jyväskylä in april 2014. neighbouring municipalities are joutsa , toivakka , jämsä , luhanka , muurame , petäjävesi , laukaa and uurainen . the university of jyväskylä is popular and respected . other websites . - jyväskylä page , `` english '' - jyväskylä page , `` finnish ''
