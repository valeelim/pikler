Satanism     satanism is a word that has several different meanings . it can describe different religions and philosophies . sometimes the word `` satanism '' is just used to say that the person using it does not like a group , but the group themselves do not think of themselves as satanists . used like this , it is just used to say the speaker disapproves of a group . a lot of groups , like pagans , or even protestants have in the past been called satanists by some people . the word is often used by people who do not know much about or do not like satanists , who think that satanism means worshipping an evil being called satan , and that satanists want him to win in a war against good represented by `` god '' . this is not true of most satanists . for them satan is only a symbol for the freedom to be themselves , also the word satan is used as symbol for man 's inner desires and for knowledge . these people like the writings of anton lavey and are sometimes called laveyan satanists . there are several modern satanic groups of different types , but the most well known is the church of satan created in 1966 by anton lavey who also wrote the satanic bible . even though these types of satanists are atheists , meaning they do not believe in a higher being , they do practice magic . they believe that there is no afterlife .
