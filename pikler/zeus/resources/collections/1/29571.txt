Wheelwright     a wheelwright is someone who builds or repairs wheels . in the olden days there were lots of carts . the wheels were made of wood . it was a difficult job to make them , so the cartwright made the cart and the wheelwright did the specialized job of making the wheels . in ancient times some wheels were made of bone in order to look beautiful . the word wheelwright is not often used for people who make wheels today . wheels are made in factories and lots of people on the production line each have their bit to do .
