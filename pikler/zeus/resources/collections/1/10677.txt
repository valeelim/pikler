All's Well That Ends Well    all 's well that ends well all 's well that ends well is a play by william shakespeare . it is a comedy . it was based on a story in boccaccio 's `` decameron '' . the date `` all 's well '' was written is uncertain , but it was probably written between 1600 and 1603. it was probably first performed about 1602-1603 , and probably first printed in the first folio of 1623. the play is about helena 's love for the unresponsive bertram . references . - all 's well that ends well
