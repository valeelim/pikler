Shart     shart is the name of several bollywood and tollywood movies . - `` shart '' ( 1954 ) , directed by bibhuti mitra , starring deepak and shashikala - `` shart '' ( 1969 ) , directed by kewal misra , starring sanjay khan and mumtaz - `` shart '' ( 1986 ) , directed by ketan anand , starring naseeruddin shah and shabana azmi - `` shart : the challenge '' ( 2004 ) , directed by puri jagannadh , starring amrita arora and snehal dabi
