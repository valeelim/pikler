Comedy Central     comedy central is an american cable television channel that specializes in comedy and humorous programming . programs include tv shows , movies , and stand-up comedy . it was launched on april 1 , 1991 ( april fools ' day ) . it 's headquarters are in new york city . it is a merger between mtv networks ' ha ! and hbo 's comedy channel . the station 's programs include `` south park '' , the colbert report , `` the daily show '' with jon stewart , `` tosh.0 '' with daniel tosh , `` futurama '' , and `` madtv '' . comedy central also features a `` secret stash '' program . airing on late saturday night , secret stash would show generally r-rated movies and stand-up comedy . the program is mostly unedited , leaving language in that is normally cut out . sometimes brief nudity is also shown . other websites . - the official site
