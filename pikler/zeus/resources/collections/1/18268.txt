Hockey     hockey is a type of sport in which players try to get points by hitting an object into the other team 's goal with a stick.others believe hockey came from the french word “ hoquet ” which means shepherd 's stick . james creighton , an engineer who learned how to play the game of hockey while living in nova scotia , is credited for bringing the game to a new level . he taught his friends at mcgill university in montreal how to play the game . there are three main ways that hockey is played . - field hockey is played with a ball on grass or astroturf . teams have 11 players each . field hockey is usually played outdoors , but there is indoor field hockey . field hockey is played in the summer olympic games . - ice hockey is played on ice . players on each team wear ice skates . they try to hit a small rubber disc called a hockey puck into the other team 's goal . there are 6 players on each team . it can be played indoors or outdoors . professionals play indoors in arenas . ice hockey is one of the most popular sports in the world . it is played in the winter olympic gamesthe modern game was invented in the mid-1850´s by british soldiers based in canada . rules were set by students at mcgill university in montréal , canada , in 1879 , and many amateur clubs and leagues were organized in canada by the late 1880´s . the game is believed to have been 1st played in the usa in 1893 .
