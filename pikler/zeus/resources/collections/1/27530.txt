Elle McFeast     elle mcfeast was a fictional character played by australian comedienne libbi gorr , who started off on a show with andrew denton about sport in australia called `` live and sweaty '' . gorr made up her character 's name to make fun of australian model elle macpherson . `` mcfeast '' was the name of a hamburger from mcdonald 's . mcfeast had her own show for many years in australia . she talked about many scary things , like jails and criminals , and made fun of them . one day she interviewed chopper read on her show . he was so scary that she had to stop her show .
