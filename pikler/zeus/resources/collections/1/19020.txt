Decatur, Illinois    decatur , illinois decatur is a city in the american state of illinois . in the 2000 census , there were more than 80,000 people living in it . many people drive in and out of decatur on interstate 72. there is a shopping mall in nearby forsyth , a suburb of decatur . decatur is the county seat of macon county . macon county was the home of the young abraham lincoln , later u.s. president , for a short time . the place where the lincoln family built a log cabin and spent the winter of 1829-1830 is now a state park , the `` lincoln trail homestead state park .
