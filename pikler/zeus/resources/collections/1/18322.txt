Saint Vincent and the Grenadines     saint vincent and the grenadines is an island nation in the caribbean sea . it is a member of the commonwealth of nations , the organisation of eastern caribbean states and caricom . the country speaks english as its official language . the capital , and its main port , is kingstown . its national bird is the saint vincent amazon , an endemic bird of the saint vincent island . saint vincent and the grenadines is a parliamentary democracy and constitutional monarchy with queen elizabeth ii as head of state . the queen does not live in the islands but she is represented in the country by the governor-general , currently sir frederick ballantyne . control of the government rests with the elected prime minister and his or her cabinet . the current prime minister is ralph gonsalves . the country has no formal armed forces , although the police has a special service unit that has a supporting role on the island . the parliament is unicameral is formed only by the house of assembly with 21 seats : 15 representatives elected by popular vote to serve five-year terms and 6 appointed senators . the last elections were held on 13 december 2010 and the next will be held in 2015 .
