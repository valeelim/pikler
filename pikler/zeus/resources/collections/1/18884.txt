Lawrence of Arabia     lawrence of arabia is a british epic movie from 1962 , based on the life of t. e. lawrence . it stars peter o'toole as the title character , along with omar sharif and alec guinness . the opening scene of the movie shows lawrence dying in a motorcycle accident . the movie then goes into a series of flashbacks ( past events ) from his life . the 1982 movie `` gandhi '' follows `` lawrence of arabia 's '' 's story pattern : after the main character dies at the start , it moves into a series of flashbacks from gandhi 's life . `` lawrence of arabia '' won seven academy awards , one of them for best picture . considered one of the greatest movies ever made , it has appeared on afi 's 100 years ... 100 movies list ( as the number five selecion ) . a director 's cut of the movie , running at 216 minutes , has no women talking in it . other websites . - greatest films - ravecentral
