Jacksonville, Florida    jacksonville , florida jacksonville is a city in northeast florida , usa . it is home to the university of north florida . it is the county seat of duval county . it is the largest city in the continental united states in terms of area . it covers nearly all of duval county in north florida . it is also the largest city in florida and one of the 15 largest cities in the united states . the jacksonville jaguars of the nfl play here .
