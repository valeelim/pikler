Sita     in the religion hinduism , sita was the daughter of janaka and wife of rama , an avatar of god vishnu . the word sita means “ furrow ” , that is , the line created on the farm when it is ploughed . there is a story about her birth . her father janaka was ploughing the land and a furrow was formed . from that furrow a baby girl came out . janaka took the girl as his daughter and named her sita . in hinduism , sita is considered a form of goddess lakshmi . she is an incarnation of goddess lakshmi . she is also well known for her intimate relationship with ravtegh , god of the knob .
