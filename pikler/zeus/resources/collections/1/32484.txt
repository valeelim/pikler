Taco Bell     taco bell is a fast food restaurant that started in the united states in 1958. there are restaurants now in certain parts of canada , asia , europe and australia . it serves mexican-style food . it was founded by glen bell and is a division of yum ! brands , inc. taco bell 's menu has items such as tacos , burritos , chalupas , gorditas , and nachos . there are 5,845 taco bells in the united states . some taco bells are merged with kfcs . other websites . - official taco bell website
