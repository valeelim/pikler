Yatton     yatton is a large village with 9,000 people in the county of somerset in england . archaeology in the area has found a roman villa ( country-house ) belonging to some rich person who lived here in the late 3rd century , judging from the coins . records show the land was part of the manor of john the dane before the norman conquest , and giso , bishop of wells afterward . other websites . - yatton parish council - roman villa
