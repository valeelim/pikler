Ambassador     an ambassador is a person sent by the government of a country to a different country . the ambassador is the official representative of their country . the ambassador speaks to officials of the other country about any problems and issues between the two countries . in past years , communication between countries could take days or weeks . it was necessary to have a person in each foreign capital to have meetings and make negotiations between countries . nowadays , communication is much faster , and often governments can be in direct contact with each other . however , it is still true that many problems need person-to-person meetings , so ambassadors are still needed . often an ambassador will live in the foreign country for a number of years . an embassy is where the ambassador lives . embassies are most often in the capital of the foreign country . an ambassador may bring people with him to help him and work at the embassy . some of the high ranking people may be called embassy officials .
