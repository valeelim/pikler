Oxford     oxford is a city in england . it is on the river thames . it is a very old city . it is the 52nd largest city in the united kingdom . some of its buildings were built before the 12th century . it is famous for its university , oxford university , which is the oldest university in the english-speaking world . about 150,200 live in oxford . two rivers run through the city , the cherwell and the thames . these two rivers meet south of the city centre . besides its university , there are some buildings famous for their architecture ( the style in which they were built ) , like the radcliffe camera . there are also lots of museums and other places for tourists to visit . other websites . - oxford -citizendium
