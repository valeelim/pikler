Angela Merkel     angela dorothea merkel ( born `` angela dorothea kasner '' on 17 july 1954 in hamburg ) is a german politician , and has been chancellor of germany since 22 november 2005. she was born in hamburg , and grew up in the german democratic republic where she lived until the reuniting of germany in 1990. merkel is now married to joachim sauer , a professor of chemistry . she chairs ( is in charge of ) the german conservative party , the christian democratic union of germany ( cdu ) . merkel became germany 's first female chancellor on 22 november 2005 in a coalition of the two biggest german political parties cdu/csu and spd . after the election 2009 she formed a coalition with fdp . she again emerged as the winner in 2013 federal elections . in december 2015 , she was named as `` time '' magazine 's person of the year .
