Plectrum     a plectrum or pick is a small object used to play certain types of string instruments . the plectrum is held in the hand and used to pluck a string . this makes a hard , metallic , `` twangy '' sound . the instruments most commonly plucked include the guitar , lute and mandolin . the strings of a harpsichord are also plucked , although the plectra in this instrument are called `` jacks '' . they pluck the string when the player presses a key down .
