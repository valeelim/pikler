Story     a story can be different things : - when we tell others about a thing that happened , or a thing we thought of : `` bob read the children a story '' ; `` mary told john the story of her life '' . - a report in a newspaper or on the television news : `` did you see the story about george bush on page 12 ? '' - a thing we tell others that is not real : `` little johnny said he was a millionaire , but he 's not , it was only a story '' . see fiction - in us english , a level of a building : `` the house has eight stories '' . ( in british english this is a storey ) .
