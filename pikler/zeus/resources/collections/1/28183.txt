Michael Hayden     michael hayden ( born march 17 , 1945 in pittsburgh , pennsylvania ) was the head of the cia from 2006 to 2009. on 24 may 2006 , he was voted as the new head of the cia by the united states senate . he was the director of the nsa from 1999 to 2005. the nsa and cia are both spy agencies . he has worked as a spy since 1970. other websites . - michael hayden biography from the nsa
