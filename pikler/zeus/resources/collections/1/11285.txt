Cross     a cross is a symbol , which is used for many things . crosses in religion . - the christian cross - the ankh , an ancient egyptian symbol for life - the swastika , a symbol in hinduism , jainism and buddhism , but also used in many cultures at different times . other crosses . - the swastika , also used as a symbol of nazism . this sign is thought insulting by many people . - the cross of saint peter , an inverted latin cross , used by catholics and also associated with satanism . - the red cross , used by medical and humanitarian groups
