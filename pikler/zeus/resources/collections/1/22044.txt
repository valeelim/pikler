Kurów     kurów is a village in south-eastern poland , near puławy and lublin , on the kurówka river . it is the main village of a gmina within the lublin voivodship and has 2826 people living there ( as of 2010 ) . the village was built before 1185 and made bigger between 1431 and 1442 with city rights based on the magdeburg law . the former president of poland , wojciech jaruzelski was born in kurów in 1923. other websites . - wiki about kurów
