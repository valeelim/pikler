Germanic languages     the germanic languages are a language family in the indo-european languages . they came from one language , `` proto-germanic '' , and were originally spoken in northern , western and central europe . the germanic languages are separated into the east germanic languages ( these are no longer spoken ) , the north germanic languages and the west germanic languages . list of germanic languages . west germanic languages . - english - german - saxon - frisian - luxembourgish - dutch - afrikaans - scots north germanic languages . - danish - faroese - icelandic - norwegian - swedish all of the following are extinct : - gothic ( with texts ) - vandalic - burgundian - crimean gothic
