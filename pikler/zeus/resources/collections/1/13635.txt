Audioslave     audioslave is an american rock band . the band is made up of chris cornell ( vocals ) , who was with soundgarden until 1997 , and tom morello ( guitar ) , tim commerford ( electric bass ) and brad wilk ( drums ) , who were with rage against the machine until 2000. their first album `` audioslave '' was released in 2002 and the next one , named `` out of exile '' , followed in 2005. on may 6 , 2005 audioslave became the first american rock group to play in cuba . because of american laws against trade with cuba , this concert had to be allowed by us authorities . other websites . - official website
