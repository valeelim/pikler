Goodfellas     goodfellas is a 1990 american biographical crime movie directed by martin scorsese . it is about life in the mafia . it gained six nominations for oscars . it won one ( for joe pesci 's role ) . many people thought the movie was great . it is set in new york city . the movie is based on the life of mafioso henry hill ( 1943-2012 ) from 1955 - 1980 . `` goodfellas '' uses information from new york crime reporter nicholas pileggi 's book `` wiseguy '' . scorsese and pileggi worked together to write the movie . `` goodfellas '' was first shown at the 1990 venice film festival . scorsese received the silver lion award for best director . director david chase said the movie made him want to make the hbo television series `` the sopranos '' .
