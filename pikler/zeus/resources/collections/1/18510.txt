Scene     scene can mean : - a certain place in a movie or play where a part of the story takes place . - a part of a culture made up of people who have a common interest , usually in a certain type of music . ( `` dark clothes are a part of the emo scene . '' ) - a style based upon `` emo '' , where individuals dye the hair with many bright colours , and wear neon pieces of clothing in general . this is similar to a raver .
