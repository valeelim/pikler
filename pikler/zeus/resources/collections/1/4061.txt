Face     the face is a part of the body at the front of the head . it is the part of us that others interact with . on the face are organs of sight , smell , hearing . especially in a four-legged animal , the face is the part which goes first into the world , and that is why the sense organs and the mouth are there . in humans , the face includes the hair , forehead , eyebrow , eyelash , eyes , nose , ears , cheeks , mouth , lips , teeth , skin , and chin .
