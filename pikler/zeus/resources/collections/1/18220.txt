Capitol building     a capitol building ( or just capitol ) is a building in a capital city . people who control government work there to make laws . the building may have many offices . some offices are for legislators , the people who make the laws . some offices are for people who enforce laws , for example a president or governor . these people lead all people , and help them obey the laws . sometimes , there are offices for judges , people who help all people understand the laws . these judges are very powerful . a group of these judges can be called a supreme court or superior court . buildings similar to capitols are in other places . a smaller government like one in a county can be in a court house . a city government can be in a city hall .
