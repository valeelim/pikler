Marquis de Sade     donatien alphonse françois , comte de sade , ( 2 june 1740 – 2 december 1814 ) , better known as the marquis de sade ) was a french nobleman , writer and philosopher who was born in paris . the de sade family were french nobility , who came from provence . different members of the house held positions in the church , and the french state at different times . once donatien had grown up , he had troubles with the authorities several times . he was bisexual . he spent many years in prison and at a mental asylum . the main points he was accused of were : - whipping a prostitute . - drugging prostitutes and later forcing them to have group sex , and also engage in sodomy . on one of his escapades , he `` accidentally '' raped a close relative of his , who was a nun . this caused his family to drop him , and to hand him over to the authorities . for this , he was sentenced to death ( while being absent ) . he later escaped the death penalty by moving to italy . the death penalty against him was later changed to a prison sentence .
