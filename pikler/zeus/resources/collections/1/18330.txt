Evgeny Platov     evgeny platov ( born august 7 , 1967 , odessa , ukraine ) . he was a russian dancer , and began skating when he was nine years old . he studied at the physical institute of culture in moscow . platov won two olympic gold medals and four world championships with his partner , oksana grishuk after the 1998 olympics in nagano , japan , platov started skating as a professional with a different partner , maya usova , until he retired from performing in 2002. he is now a coach at princeton sports center in princeton , new jersey . amateur competitions . - world championships : first in 1994 , 1995 , 1996 , and 1997 - olympics : gold medal in 1994 and 1998 - european championships : first place in 1996 , 1997 , and 1998 other websites . - platov and usova site
