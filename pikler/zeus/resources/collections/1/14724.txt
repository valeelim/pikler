Jo Whiley     johanne `` jo '' whiley ( born 4 july , 1965 in northampton ) is an english radio dj on bbc radio 1 , and television presenter . she had a weekday lunchtime show , called `` the jo whiley show '' and later `` the lunchtime social '' . when simon mayo left radio 1 for radio five live in february 2001 , whiley 's show was moved to mid-morning . in july 2009 , whiley published her autobiography , `` my world in motion '' , on cd from random house audiobooks .
