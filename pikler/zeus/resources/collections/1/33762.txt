Amman     amman , sometimes spelled ammann ( ) , is the capital city of jordan . it is the official capital and commercial center of jordan . it is the capital city of the amman governorate . it is the largest city in jordan in terms of area and number of people . amman has about 4 million people ( around 42 % of the number of people in jordan in 2015 ) . the city is in the center of jordan at other websites . - amman street food documentary on youtube - amman digital community facebook page - greater amman municipality facebook page - amman public transportation map
