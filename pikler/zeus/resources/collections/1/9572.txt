Marine biology     marine biology is the study of any living plant or animal in the sea . marine biologists are the people who study it . like other scientists , marine biologists must know a lot about the creatures they are studying . they also must understand how the sea works . the study of how the sea works is oceanography . there are many kinds of marine animals and plants to study . marine animals include squids , corals , whales and fish . marine plants include sea grasses and algae . the census of marine life was a major project of marine biology .
