Solomonid dynasty     the solomonid dynasty was a line of emperors that ruled in ethiopia from 1270 to 1975. there were some other emperors that ruled who were not from the solomonid dynasty , but the solomonids were in control most of the time . the emperors did not use their real name when they became emperor of ethiopia , but they made up a new one for their rule . for example , the first emperor of the solomonids was yekuno amlak . but he used the name `` tasfa iyassus '' after he became ruler . the dynasty began in 1270 , when yekuno amlak killed the last king of the zagwe dynasty , which had ruled ethiopia . yekuno amlak was made king , and european historians say was the first ruler of the dynasty . but most ethiopian books say that menelik i , the son of king solomon of israel and the queen of sheba , was the first emperor of this dynasty . the leaders of ethiopa from 1270 to 1975 ( mostly solomonids ) :
