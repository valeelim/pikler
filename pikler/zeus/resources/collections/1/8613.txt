Chasing Vermeer     chasing vermeer is a children 's novel . it is about two children noticing strange coincidences relating to art . the book was written by blue balliett and published ( printed ) by scholastic in 2003. it says many things about the dutch painter johannes vermeer . the sequel to the book is `` the wright 3 '' . characters . - petra andalee - calder pillay - mrs. sharpe - ms. hussey - tommy segovia
