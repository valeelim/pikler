Newcastle upon Tyne     newcastle upon tyne is a city in north-east england . about 250,000 people live in the city , though more live in the area around it . newcastle is one of the biggest cities in england and is mostly famous for the river tyne , angel of the north , newcastle brown ale , the great north run , and the iconic millenium bridge . the local dialect and accent is called the `` geordie '' dialect . pronunciation of `` alright '' , `` town '' and `` house '' is `` areet '' , `` toon '' and `` hoos '' . ant and dec are from newcastle
