Chair     a chair is a piece of furniture . it is used for sitting on and it can also be used for standing on , if you ca n't reach something.they usually have four legs to support the weight . some types of chairs , such as the barstool , have only one leg in the center . those chairs are usually able to spin . sometimes chairs have armrests . these chairs may be called armchairs . there are many types of chairs such as windsor chairs and rocking chairs . there is another type of chair called a sofa or settee . this is often used for comfort and can fit more than one person on it .
