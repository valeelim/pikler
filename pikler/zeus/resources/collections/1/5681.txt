Cate Blanchett     cate blanchett ( born may 14 , 1969 ) is an australian actor . she was born catherine elise blanchett in melbourne , victoria , australia . in 1998 she won an academy award nomination for best actress for playing elizabeth i in `` elizabeth '' . in 2005 she won best supporting actress for playing katharine hepburn in martin scorsese 's `` the aviator '' .
