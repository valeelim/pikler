Time (magazine)    time ( magazine ) time is an american news magazine . it was founded in 1923. it is printed every week in the united states . it is published by time inc. it has articles on politics and current events . it is read by more people than any other news magazine . there is also a magazine called `` time for kids '' , aimed at children . that magazine is eight pages long and contains more images and shorter articles than the regular `` time '' magazine .
