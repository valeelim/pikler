Grand Rapids, Michigan    grand rapids , michigan grand rapids is a large city in western michigan , united states . former american president gerald ford grew up in grand rapids . grand rapids is also the birthplace of amway and the meijer store chain . grand rapids is the county seat of kent county . it is the second largest city in the state , after detroit . the third largest is warren . the city has many attractions , including the frederik meijer gardens and sculpture park and john ball park , which is a zoo . grand valley state university has a campus in downtown grand rapids .
