Mario Bros.     mario bros. is a video game made in 1983. in the game , mario and/or luigi must defeat enemies by jumping on them from below and then kicking them , when they are knocked down . it is not only the first game to feature luigi as a playable character , it also is the first game in which mario is named mario . it was later remade for other game consoles at the time . `` mario bros. '' was also included with the four `` super mario advance '' games .
