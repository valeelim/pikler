Odisha     odisha , formerly known as orissa is a state on the east coast of the republic of india , an area of , and has 30 boroughs . the official languages of odisha are oriya , hindi and english . it is about the same size as bangladesh . in traditional indian geography it falls under the east indian zone . odisha is the ninth largest indian state and the eleventh most populous . popular tourist destinations include puri , bhubaneswar and konark ( unesco world heritage listed ) . other major cities are cuttack , sambalpur , berhampur , dhenkanal and raurkela . biju patnaik international airport is odisha 's only airport . the current chief minister is naveen patnaik . nearby states are west bengal , chattisgarh , andhra pradesh and jharkand . capital- bhubaneswar state song- bande utkala janani
