Year     one year is about 365 days long ( except in a leap year ) . it is the time it takes the earth to go completely around ( orbit ) the sun once . a year is actually 365Â¼ days long , but a calendar has 365 days , except in a leap year . the year starts on january 1 and ends on december 31 in the gregorian calendar , but a fiscal year or a school year can start on a different day of the year . there are several ways used to measure the length of a year . - a solar year is based on the seasons . the gregorian calendar is based on the solar year . the solar year is 365 days long . - a lunar year is based on the moon and is usually 12 lunar months ( 29 days , 12 hours , 44 minutes each ) or 354 days long . - a tropical year is the time between two vernal equinoxes , the first day of spring when the lengths of daylight and night are the same . - a sidereal year measures the time between when a selected fixed star is highest in the night sky . - an anomalistic year is the difference between the times when the earth gets closest to the sun .
