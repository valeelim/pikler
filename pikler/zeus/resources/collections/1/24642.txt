The Grass Roots     the grass roots are an american rock music group . their peak in popularity was in the 1960s and 1970s . they formed in los angeles , california in 1966. their biggest hit was the song `` midnight confessions '' which charted in the us at # 5 in 1968. their 1969 song `` i 'd wait a million years '' was # 15 in the u.s..
