IELTS     the international english language testing system ( ielts ) tests how good you are at the english language . people who take the test take the academic module or the general training module . the academic one is for people who want to go to university . the general one is for people who want to do other training or want to get work experience . people who want to emigrate to a country that uses english also take the general one . most universities in australia , britain , canada , new zealand and the united states accept the ielts . many professional companies do as well . other websites . - ielts web site - ielts exams
