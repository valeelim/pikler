Full House     full house is an american comedy television series set in san francisco . it originally aired from 1987 to 1995. it told the story of danny tanner ( bob saget ) , a man whose wife has died and is left alone with his three daughters , dj ( candace cameron ) , stephanie ( jodie sweetin ) , and michelle ( mary-kate and ashley olsen ) . he recruits his best friend , joey gladstone ( dave coulier ) , a childlike stand-up comedian , and his brother-in-law ( and the girls ' uncle ) jesse katsopolis ( john stamos ) , a struggling musican , to move in and help look after the girls . the series is often criticised for its sickly sweet and unrealistic plotline and corny jokes , even though it ran for many years ; some episodes dealt with real problems such as child abuse , drinking , and lying . yet it has remained , in recent years , one of the very few adult comedy series that parents can watch with their kids . in august 2014 it was announced that plans were underway to reboot the series which would reunite most of the original cast .
