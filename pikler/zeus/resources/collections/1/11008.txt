Calgary     calgary is the biggest city in alberta , canada . more than 1,000,000 people live in calgary . the city is in the southern half of alberta near the rocky mountains . there are a lot of oil and gas businesses in calgary . there are many skyscrapers in the city . around calgary there are a lot of farms where cows are raised . calgary has a humid continental climate ( `` dfb '' in the koeppen climate classification ) . it has long , cold , dry winters . the summers are warm with moderate rainfall . sometimes a wind called a chinook blows through calgary . this wind can make some days in winter less cold . calgary has a famous event called the calgary stampede every year.. they rent alot of rides and have a huge festival for the city to join the calgary stampede is the largest rodeo in the world . at the stampede , cowboys can win a lot of money . cowboys also race with chuckwagons at the stampede . chuckwagons are carts with four wheels pulled by horses . in 1988 , the winter olympics were held in calgary .
