Juno Award     the juno awards are awards given to canadian musical artists and bands , similar to the united states ' grammy awards . the juno awards were first presented in 1970. the categories for the juno awards are : - album of the year - international album of the year - new artist of the year - new group of the year - rock album of the year - pop album of the year - artist of the year - group of the year other websites . - official website
