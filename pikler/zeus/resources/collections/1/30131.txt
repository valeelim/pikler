X Japan     x japan is a japanese rock band . they were formed in 1982 by yoshiki . they have sold over 30 million albums and singles and over 2 million videos to date . x japan stopped making music in 1997 , but reunited in 2007. they performed their first world tour live in hong kong in 2009. they played to 20,000 people in two days . some visual kei bands are influenced by x japan . the band was first called `` x '' but because the letter x was already the name of another band they changed their name to `` x japan '' to avoid confusion . albums . - `` vanishing vision '' ( 1988 ) - `` blue blood '' ( 1989 ) - `` jealousy '' ( 1991 ) - `` art of life '' ( 1993 ) - `` dahlia '' ( 1996 )
