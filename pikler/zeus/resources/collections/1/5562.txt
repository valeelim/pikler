Saint Margaret of Scotland     saint margaret of scotland ( c. 1045 - 16 november 1093 ) was married to malcolm iii of scotland , and was the mother of david i of scotland , alexander i of scotland and edgar . she was the daughter of edward the exile , and the granddaughter of edmund ironside . she was probably born in hungary . margaret died four days after her husband . other websites . - university of pittsburgh : margaret of scotland - catholic encyclopedia : st. margaret of scotland
