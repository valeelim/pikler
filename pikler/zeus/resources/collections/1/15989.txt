Milk shake     in north america , a milk shake ( or milkshake ) is a cold drink usually made with milk , ice cream , and sweet syrups , such as chocolate syrup , mixed in a blender . people usually drink milkshakes with a drinking straw and from a tall glass or cup . milk shakes are similar to smoothies . smoothies are a type of cold drink made with an electric blender . smoothies are made with a liquid ( either milk , fruit juice , or water ) , crushed ice or ice cubes , different fruits , and honey or maple syrup . milk shakes and other cold drinks such as `` ice cream floats '' ( a scoop of ice cream placed in a glass of cola or other carbonated soda drink ) or `` malts '' ( a milk shake with malt powder added to it ) were popular among teenagers in the 1950s . teenagers would go to ice cream parlors and order cold drinks .
