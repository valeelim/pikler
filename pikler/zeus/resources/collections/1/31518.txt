Hula     hula is a type of dancing from hawaii . hula is a word in the hawaiian language . male dancers and female dancers can both dance hula . a long time ago , hula dance was used only in special ceremonies . now , hula dance is mainly for entertainment . a hula dancer usually wears a grass skirt or a leaf skirt , a flower necklace ( called `` lei '' in hawaiian ) , and sometimes a flower in the hair . traditionally , the hula dancer dances to a chant ( a poem that is sung to a rhythm ) . the chanter beats his or her hand on a gourd ( dried , empty squash ) to create the rhythm . there are many kinds of hula dance . for example , the hawaiian hula dance style is slow and the tahitian hula dance style is fast .
