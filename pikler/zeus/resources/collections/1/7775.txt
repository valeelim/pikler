Simon &amp; Garfunkel    simon & amp ; garfunkel paul simon and art garfunkel were an american pop music group . they were two childhood friends , who found fame in the 1960s . their songs , including `` mrs. robinson '' , `` so long , frank lloyd wright '' , `` the sound of silence '' , and `` bridge over troubled water '' , all composed by simon , are still popular to this day . their free concert in new york city 's central park in 1981 was the biggest concert ever , with 500,000 people ( about the same number as woodstock ) . albums . - `` wednesday morning , 3 a.m. '' - `` sounds of silence '' - `` parsley , sage , rosemary and thyme '' - `` bookends '' - `` bridge over troubled water '' - `` greatest hits '' - `` the concert in central park ''
