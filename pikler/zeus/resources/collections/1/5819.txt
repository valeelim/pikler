Air force     an air force is the branch of a country 's military which fights in the air . the air force is made up of aircraft such as fighters , bombers , helicopters and transport aircraft . when a navy has its own air force it is called naval aviation . aircraft in an air force sometimes fly in a formation . formations are when the aircraft fly in a pattern . air forces is part of the military . the biggest air force in the world is the united states air force ( usaf ) .
