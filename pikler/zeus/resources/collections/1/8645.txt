Yahtzee     yahtzee is a popular dice game that is well-known all over the world . the object of the game is to roll five dice ( up to three times ) to create certain combinations such as : - three-of-a-kind : three dice showing the same number - four-of-a-kind : four dice showing the same number - small straight : four numbers in sequence , such as 2-3-4-5 - large straight five numbers in sequence , such as 1-2-3-4-5 - full house ( three dice showing the same number and the remaining two dice showing a different number but both matching ) - yahtzee : all five dice showing the same number most of these combinations come from poker .
