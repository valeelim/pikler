Houston Dynamo     the houston dynamo are an american soccer team that plays in major league soccer in houston , texas . in late 2005 , the san jose earthquakes moved to the city because of financial problems in san jose , california . the dynamo opened a new stadium , known as bbva compass stadium , in downtown houston in may 2012 , after playing their first seven games of the season on the road . league title . - major league soccer : 2 - 2006 , 2007
