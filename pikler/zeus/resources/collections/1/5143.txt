Mary Rose     the mary rose was a ship which belonged to henry viii of england . she sank in 1545 in battle with a french fleet . in 1979 , the `` mary rose '' trust was formed . in 1982 the `` mary rose '' trust managed to get the `` mary rose '' out of the sea . in 1994 , they started to preserve her . the remains of the `` mary rose '' are currently in portsmouth , england with the hms `` victory '' and hms `` warrior '' . it is not known the exact reason for the sinking . one of the theories is that she had the gun ports open to fire at the french . she then turned to fire at the other side , but the men on board failed to close the gun ports . sea water got in the open gun ports , which tipped her over and she sank . the `` mary rose '' sank because the wind blew and tipped her over .
