Mexico (state)    mexico ( state ) the state of méxico is one of the administrative divisions of the country of mexico . it is one of 32 administrative divisions . it is in the centre of the country . it borders hidalgo , querétaro , tlaxcala , puebla , michoacán , morelos and guerrero as well as mexico city . the capital of the state is toluca . its biggest city is ecatepec de morelos , which borders mexico federal district . teotihuacan is also in this state . it is about 21.355 km² in surface . approximately 14 million people live there , as of 2005 .
