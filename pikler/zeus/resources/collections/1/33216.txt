Tampere     tampere ( ) is the third biggest city in finland . it is the capital of the pirkanmaa area . neighbour municipalities are kangasala , lempäälä , nokia , pirkkala , vesilahti , hämeenkyrö , orivesi , pälkäne and ylöjärvi . the population of tampere was 215,144 in 2011. tampere was started in 1779. at 1800 's , tampere became to be a notable industrialized city and one of the biggest cities in finland . other websites . - official tampere page , in finnish - the official tampere region visitor website
