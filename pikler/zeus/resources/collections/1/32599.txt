Furniture     furniture is the word that means all the things like chairs , tables and cupboards . in other words , furniture are all the things that are in the house and that people can use to sit , to lie on or that are supposed to contain smaller things like cloths or cups . furniture is made of wood , particle boards , leather , screws etc . bedroom furniture is the objects in the bedroom . the bed is covered by sheets , a bedspread and maybe a blanket . there is also a pillow on the bed . next to the bed is the nightstand . it is also called the night table . a lamp may be on the nightstand . the dresser has drawers for clothes . on top of a dresser may be a mirror . related pages . - cabinet making < br >
