George of the Jungle     george of the jungle is an animated television series created by jay ward productions in the late 1960s as a parody ( pretending to be but in a way that makes fun of ) of tarzan . in 1997 , it was made into a live-action movie by disney , with brendan fraser as the main character . there was a sequel george of the jungle 2 released direct-to-video in 2003. there was and is a video game based on the film which was and is 2d platformer game `` george of the jungle '' by ignition entertainment/crave in 2008 .
