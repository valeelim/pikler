Abu Dhabi     abu dhabi ( , `` ʼabū ẓaby '' ) is the capital city of the united arab emirates . it is in the emirate of abu dhabi . the city is on a t-shaped island going into the persian gulf from the central western coast . the city had a population of 921,000 in 2013. one of the many main attractions in abu dhabi is ferrari world and sheik zayed mosque .
