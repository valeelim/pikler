Proportionality     a proportion ( adjective : proportionate ) is a relationship between two quantities or numbers . a proportion can be expressed as a word , for example , one man may be described as `` smaller '' than another man . a proportion can usually be expressed more precisely by `` numbers '' . for example , the proportion , or mathematical relationship , between one man and two men can be written as the numbers `` 1/2 '' , which is also known as a fraction or ratio . related pages . - proportions - relation ( mathematics ) - function ( mathematics )
