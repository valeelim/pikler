The Specials     the specials were an english new wave/ska band . they were originally from coventry , england and formed in 1977. they were part of the ska revival in britain in the late 1970s and early 1980s . during the ska revival , bands began playing 1950s-style jamaican ska music . the specials often mixed other popular music styles from the 1970s and 1980s into their music , such as pop music and rock and roll .
