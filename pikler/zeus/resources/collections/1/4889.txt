Zazaki     zazaki is an indo-european language spoken in eastern anatolia above the rivers euphrates and tigris . it belongs to the northwest-iranian group of iranian language . the zaza language is related to sangsarī , māzandarānī , tātī ( herzendī ) , semnānī , tāleshī , kurdish , persian and balōchi . the number of zaza speakers is not known wxactly . zaza is also a name that appears in the bible , meaning `` belonging to all '' or `` plenty '' . other websites . - ethnologue on zazaki ( language ) - ( weblinks of zaza people ) - academic research center of zazaki - zazaki wikipedia - only zazaki news
