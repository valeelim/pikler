Indo-Iranian languages     the indo-iranian languages are a language family inside of the indo-european languages . originally they were spoken in south-central asia . they are the largest indo-european language subfamily . see : indo-aryan languages - gujarati - hindustani ( hindi ) - sindhi - konkani - marathi - nepali - punjabi - rajasthani - sanskrit - saraiki - hindustani ( urdu ) - sinhala see : iranian languages - persian ( persian language called dari in afghanistan , pakistan and tajik in tajikistan ) - balochi - kurdish - persian - pashto - zazaki
