Newport     newport is a city in the southeast of wales , great britain . it is in the county of monmouthshire , and in the historic welsh kingdom of gwent . its name in welsh is casnewydd . it is the third biggest city in wales . newport became a city in 2002. about 140,000 people live in newport . a few years ago , newport had lots of people working in the steel factory and other heavy industries . this has changed , now many people work in electronics , such as making computers , phones and microchips .
