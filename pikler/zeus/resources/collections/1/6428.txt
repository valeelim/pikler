Esteban Huertas     esteban huertas ( 1876-1943 ) was a military leader from panama . he was born in colombia . when he was 8 years old he ran away from home and he joined the army . he won many medals and in 1902 he became general . in 1900 he lost an arm when he fired a cannon . in 1903 he was one of the people who made panama independent . a year later president manuel amador forced huertas to retire because he was afraid huertas wanted to become dictator . huertas died in panama city in 1943 .
