Demeter     demeter is the goddess of farming in greek mythology . she is one of the twelve olympians . she is the daughter of kronos and rhea , and also the sister of zeus . as a fertility goddess , she is sometimes identified with rhea and gaia . she is the mother of persephone . demeter brings forth fruits of the earth , particularly the various grains . her sacred animals were the snake and the pig . she is also the sister of hera , zeus , posiden and hades . demeter was most often kind and generous . related pages . - ceres - roman version of demeter
