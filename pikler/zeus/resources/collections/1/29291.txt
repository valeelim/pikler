Cingular Wireless     cingular wireless was a mobile phone company from united states . cingular is now owned by at & t . at & t mobility llc ( usually called at & t ) is fully owned by at & t and provides wireless service to more than 120 million users in the united states , puerto rico and the u.s. virgin islands . at & t mobility is the second largest mobile phone company in the united states behind verizon wireless , which has more than 130 million users .
