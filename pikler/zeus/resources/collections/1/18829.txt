Cream     cream ( also called milkfat , milk fat , and butterfat ) is the fatty part of regular milk . skimmed milk ( or skim milk ) is milk sold with some or nearly all of the fat removed . cream is usually used in coffee to make it lighter in taste . dairy products made from cream . - butter - cheese - cottage cheese - cream cheese - ice cream - sour cream - whipped cream things named after cream . - cream ( color ) - skin cream ( including face cream )
