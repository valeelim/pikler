Baritone     a baritone is a man with a voice range between that of a tenor ( high male voice ) and bass ( low male voice ) , typically ranging from the second f below middle c to the f above middle c. it is the most common male voice type . famous baritone roles in opera include : papageno in mozart 's `` the magic flute '' and figaro in rossini 's `` the barber of seville '' . dietrich fischer-dieskau is often thought of as the greatest baritone of the later 20th century . he was famous as a lieder singer as well as on the concert stage and in opera . some musical instruments are also called `` baritone '' because they create similar frequencies to a baritone singer .
