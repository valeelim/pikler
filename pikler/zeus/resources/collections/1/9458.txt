Virtues of Harmony     virtues of harmony was a sitcom that ran in hong kong from 2001 until february 2005 . `` virtues of harmony one '' was set in ancient china during the ming dynasty . it follows the lives of the kam family . the allure of the series was the humorous storylines and how the good always wins . the star of the show was nancy sit , a kind hearted matriarch with the best intentions . following the success of `` virtues of harmony one '' , `` virtues of harmony two '' was set in modern hong kong with a similar cast .
