Evita (musical)    evita ( musical ) evita is a musical . the lyrics were written by tim rice . the music was written by andrew lloyd webber . the musical is based on the life of eva perón ( 1919-1952 ) , the wife of argentine president juan perón . `` evita '' opened on the west end in 1978 , and on broadway in 1979. it won the laurence olivier award for best new musical and the tony award for best musical . in 1996 , a movie version was released starring madonna .
