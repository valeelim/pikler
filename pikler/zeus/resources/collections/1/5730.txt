M     m is the 13th letter in the english alphabet . meanings for m. - in calendars , m sometimes means monday or means one of the months `` march '' or `` may '' . - in money , m means million , such as $ 25m ( twenty-five million dollars ) . - in the si , m is the symbol for the metre . - in roman numerals , m means one thousand . - m is also the name of a 1930 german movie made by fritz lang and its 1951 remake .
