Mauritania     mauritania is a country in northwest africa . the capital city , which is also the biggest city in the country , is nouakchott . it is on the atlantic coast . its president is general mohamed oueld abdel-aziz . at 1,030,700 km² ( 397,929 mi² ) , mauritania is the world 's 29th-largest country ( after bolivia ) . it is similar in size to egypt . the land is flat in most places .
