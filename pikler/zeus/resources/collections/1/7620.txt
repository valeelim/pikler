Baghdad     baghdad ( , transliterated `` baghdād '' ) is the capital city and largest city in iraq . it is the second-largest city in southwest asia after tehran . it is the second-largest city in the arab world after cairo . it has 5,772,000 people ( 2003 ) . baghdad is on the tigris river at 33°20′n 44°26′e . the city was once the center of dar al-islam , muslim civilization . baghdad used to be one of the largest learning centers in the world , during the post classical era .
