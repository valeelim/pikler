Thin Lizzy     thin lizzy are an irish hard rock band that started in dublin in 1969. up to 1984 the band were led by bassist , songwriter and singer phil lynott ( 1949-1986 ) . the band 's famous song `` the boys are back in town '' is still played a lot on classic rock radio stations . since lynott 's death , other former members have carried on using the band name . phil lynott wrote most of the band 's songs . the band 's music showed many musical styles including : country music , psychedelic rock , celtic music and folk music which lynott mixed with hard rock and heavy metal styles . thin lizzy were listed at number 51 on vh1 's `` 100 greatest artists of hard rock '' .
