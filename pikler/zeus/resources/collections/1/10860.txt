Faith Evans     faith evans ( born june 10 , 1973 in lakeland , florida ) . she grew up in newark new jersey . she is an american r & b singer . her first husband , `` the notorious b.i.g . `` , died in a drive-by shooting in 1997 . she has been arrested for cocaine and marijuana . evans ' album `` r & b divas '' was nominated for best r & b album at the 2014 grammy awards . her music albums . - `` faith '' - `` keep the faith '' - `` faithfully '' - `` the first lady '' - `` r & b divas ''
