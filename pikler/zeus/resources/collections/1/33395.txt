Einsteinium     einsteinium is a chemical element with the atomic number 99. that means an einsteinium atom has 99 protons and 99 electrons . it is a synthetic element , so all isotopes are radioactive . einsteinium belongs to the actinoids , and it is the 7th of them . the most stable isotope of einsteinium has 99 protons and electrons and 153 neutrons . there are 19 different ( radioactive ) isotopes . einsteinium is named after albert einstein . it was found for the first time in 1952 by albert ghiorso and his team of scientists in the debree of the first hydrogen bomb explosion , and identified as an element at the university of california , berkeley . einsteinium isotopes have a half-life between 40 and 471 days . einsteinium has been used to create the element mendelevium for the first time , in 1955 , although no practical uses are known .
