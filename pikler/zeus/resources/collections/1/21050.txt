The Last Waltz     the last waltz was final concert of the canadian/american rock group the band . it took place on thanksgiving ( november 26 ) in 1976 at the winterland ballroom in san francisco , california . it was also made into a movie by martin scorsese , that came out in 1978. other musicians who were in `` the last waltz '' concert were : - ronnie hawkins - dr. john - bobby charles - neil young - neil diamond - joni mitchell - paul butterfield - muddy waters - eric clapton - emmylou harris - van morrison - bob dylan - ringo starr - ronnie wood - stephen stills - carl radle
