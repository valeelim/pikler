1574     events . - april 14 – battle of mookerheyde : spanish forces under sancho de avila defeat the rebel forces of louis of nassau . louis is killed . - may 30 – on the death of king charles ix of france , he is succeeded by his brother king henry of poland , who becomes king henry iii . his mother , catherine of medici , acts as regent until henry arrives from poland . - october 3 – the city of leiden , besieged by the spanish , is relieved by a sea beggar fleet under louis boisot . - murad iii succeeds selim ii as sultan of the ottoman empire .
