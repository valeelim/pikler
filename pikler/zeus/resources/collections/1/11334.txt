Trikala     trikala is a greek city and it is one of 51 prefectures of greece . trikala is in the center of greece in the thessaly periphery . according to the 2001 census , 57,914 people lived there . trikala is built on a large plain . this plain is one of the biggest plain in greece . in the west of trikala is the pindus range . trikala is crossed by litheus river .
