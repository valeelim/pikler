Brok     brok nad bugiem ( english : `` brok near bug '' ) is a small town in mazowsze voivodship , poland . brok is in ostrowian county ( head town of this region is ostrów mazowiecka ) . brok is one of the smallest places in europe with town status ( since 1500 ) . - area : 28,05 km² - number of people ( as of 2004 ) : 1882 - community : brok - post code : 07-306 - current mayor : stanisław bębenek ( since 2002 ) brok is regarded by many as one of the most beautiful places in this part of poland . this is a tourist town near two rivers : bug and one also named brok . attractions include : - gothic church ( built 1560 ) - old jewish cemetery - location ( puszcza biała , `` white wood '' ) - many old houses
