Greenwich Village     greenwich village is an area on the western part of southern/downtown manhattan . it is sometimes called `` the village '' . the village is mainly residential . greenwich village is bordered by broadway to the east , hudson river to the west , houston street to the south and 14th street to the north . originally , greenwich village was a hamlet separate to new york city . the grid plan for its streets is less orderly than in parts of manhattan that were built later . the village is located on what used to be marshland . in the 16th century , native americans called it `` sapokanikan '' which means `` tobacco field '' . in the 1630s , it was turned into pasture by the dutch , who called it `` noortwyck '' . in 1664 , when the english captured the dutch settlement of new amsterdam , and greenwich village developed as a hamlet . in 1712 , it officially became a village , and was named grin'wich in the 1713 common council records . following a yellow fever epidemic in 1822 , many new york residents moved to the healthier air of greenwich ; where many stayed . in the 19th century , it was known as washington square .
