Wok     a wok is a chinese pan used for cooking . it has a round bottom . the most common use for the wok is stir frying , though it can also be used for deep frying , smoking , braising , roasting , grilling , and steaming . in indonesia , the wok is known as a `` wadjang '' , `` kuali '' in malaysia , and `` kawali '' ( small wok ) and `` kawa '' ( big wok ) in the philippines . other websites . - wok seasoning and care from thaifoodandtravel.com
