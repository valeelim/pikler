Dr. Strangelove     dr. strangelove or : how i learned to stop worrying and love the bomb ( better known as only dr. strangelove ) is a 1964 american black comedy movie directed and produced by stanley kubrick , and starring peter sellers . during the movie 's ending , the world is blown up by atomic bombs . vera lynn 's recording of `` we 'll meet again '' is played . it was a famous song during world war ii . in 1989 , the united states library of congress chose this movie to be kept in the national film registry . this means the movie will be protected from damage that happens to older film that was used to make movies . other websites . - `` happy birthday , dr . strangelove !
