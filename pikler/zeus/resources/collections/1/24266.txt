Computer numbering formats     computer numbering formats are the ways that computers and calculators represent numbers . most computers use a system of binary which is composed of ones and zeros . other systems in use are the octal ( 0,1-7 ) or the hexadecimal ( 0,1-9 , a , b , c , d , e , f ) systems . so we can use codes , which the computer understands only , as well as other number systems .
