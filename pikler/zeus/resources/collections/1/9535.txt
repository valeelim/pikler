Spaghetti Western     spaghetti westerns were a name given to low-budget western movies , which were made by italian movie companies in the 1960s . they were different from the american western movies , usually filmed in italian , had limited budgets , filmed on location in spain and italy with minimal sets , and many close-ups and artistic shots . perhaps the most well-known of these movies is `` the man with no name '' trilogy : `` a fistful of dollars '' ( 1964 ) , `` for a few dollars more '' ( 1965 ) , and `` the good , the bad and the ugly '' ( 1966 ) . all three of these movies were directed by sergio leone , featured music by ennio morricone , and starred american actor clint eastwood as the main character . other websites . - spaghetti western database
