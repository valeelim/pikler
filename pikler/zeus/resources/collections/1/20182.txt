Valencia     valencia is a city in spain , on the mediterranean coast . it is the capital of the community of valencia . it has 807,396 people , making it the third-largest city in spain and the fifteenth in the european union . valencia 's climate is on the boundary of hot semi-arid ( `` bsh '' in the koeppen climate classification ) and hot-summer mediterranean ( `` csa '' in the koeppen climate classification ) .
