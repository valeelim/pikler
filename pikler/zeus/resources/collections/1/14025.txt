Fark.com     fark is a comedy website and forums . fark was started by drew curtis in 1999 as a weblog where users post current news articles and other information . then other users make comments on these articles . there are two classes of users ; users with free subscriptions are able to post comments in the forums and submit new articles . there is also a subscription service called totalfark that grants access to more news stories . regular subscribers are known as farkers and paying subscribers are often called totalfarkers or tfers . fark is what 's known as a news aggregator . the page is divided into several tabs , `` not news '' , `` sports '' , `` business '' , `` geek '' , `` showbiz '' , `` politics '' , `` music '' , and `` video '' users submit stories that are relevant to each tab , and staffers and subscribers decide which ones are posted . once a story is posted , a corresponding forum thread is created where any subscriber can comment on the story . other websites . - fark
