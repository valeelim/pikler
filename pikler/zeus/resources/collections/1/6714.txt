University of Florida     the university of florida ( uf ) is a public university that is in gainesville , florida in the united states . the campus is 2,000 acres and has more than 900 structures , as well as other locations in the state of florida . important research activities at the university are in medicine , at shands hospital , and in agriculture . the mascot of uf is an alligator , and students and fans of the university 's athletic teams are often called `` gators .
