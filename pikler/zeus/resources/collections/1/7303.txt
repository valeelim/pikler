Asia Minor     asia minor , a peninsula also called anatolia , comprises most of the asian part of modern turkey and the armenian highland . most people there today speak turkish . the seas surrounding asia minor are the black sea , the aegean sea and the mediterranean sea . because asia minor is between christian europe and asia , many different cultures have lived there . remnants ( small parts ) of these cultures are there today . many great historical peoples , like the hittites , lydians greeks , persians , armenians , romans , goths , minoans , byzantines and turks , have lived in or occupied asia minor . the roman empire had a province called asia , which was in asia minor . later people started to call the entire continent asia , so the peninsula asia was called asia minor ( little asia ) . troy is one of many famous cities that were in asia minor long ago .
