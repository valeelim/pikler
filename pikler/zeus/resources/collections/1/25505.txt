Constance of Arles     constance of arles ( 973 - july 25 , 1034 ) was the third wife of robert ii of france . they were married in 1003 after robert had got a divorce from his second wife , bertha . she was buried in the saint denis basilica with robert ii of france . they had several children : - henry i of france - adela of france - is the mother of matilda of flanders , who was the wife of william i of england - robert i of burgundy - eudes of france - constance of france , married to count manasses of dammartin
