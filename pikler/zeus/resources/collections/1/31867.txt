Fall/Liberation of Saigon     the fall/liberation of sàigòn happened on april 30 , 1975. it was the capture of saigon ( vietnamese : sài gòn ) by the national front for the liberation of vietnam and the vietnam people 's army , marking the end of the vietnam war . it also resulted in the name of saigon being changed to ho chi minh city , in honour of former president ho chi minh . new york times archive
