Flatulence     flatulence , also called fart or flatus , is when intestinal gas leaves the anus . this often happens with a sound . in mammals like humans , the gases come from two sources : swallowed air ( for example , while eating , talking or drinking ) and gases naturally created by bacteria in the body during digestion . nitrogen is the main gas released ( 20-90 % in total ) , followed by hydrogen , carbon dioxide , oxygen and methane .
