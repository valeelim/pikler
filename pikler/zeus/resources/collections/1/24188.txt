Konkani language     konkani is a language from india . konkani belongs to indo-aryan family of languages . it is the official language of goa , a state in india . some people from the part of india known as konkan speak konkani language . konkan is on the west coast of india . the name `` konkani '' means `` from the konkan '' . the word `` konkan '' means corner ( kona ) and piece/part of earth ( kana ) . the name of the language comes from the place where it is spoken . people write konkani in many different scripts ( writing systems or alphabets ) . people from different regions use different scripts . hindu konkani people from goa and maharashtra use devanagari script . konkani people from karnataka use kannada script . christains in goa use roman script . konkani muslims use arabic script . konkani people from kerala use malayalam script . devanagari is the official script .
