Salford     salford is a city in england . it was made a free borough by ranulf , earl of chester in about 1230. and was granted city status in 1926. salford is unusual because it borders the city of manchester at its center , rather than its outskirts . the two cities are divided by the river irwell . salford shares the industrial history of its neighbour manchester . it hosts several museums and art centres , chiefly : the lowry and the imperial war museum , both on salford quays .
