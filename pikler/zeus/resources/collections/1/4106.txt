Lisbon     lisbon is the capital city of portugal . it is the largest city of portugal . the city has a population of about two million people . lisbon is 11th most populous urban area in the european union . lisbon is on the river tagus . it has a pleasant climate and has about 220 days of sunshine each year . there are many beautiful beaches close to the city . there are also many seafood restaurants , historical sites and monuments . lisbon is one of the oldest cities in the world . other websites . - official page of the city - lisbon photos a collection of photos , showing lisbon as it is to visitors . - lisbon tourist guide and information
