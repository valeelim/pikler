The KLF     the klf were a band from england . they made music from the late 1980s until the early 1990s . a lot of the music they made was pop music or dance music . the band members were two men , bill drummond and jimmy cauty . bill drummond is a musician , singer , and writer from scotland . jimmy cauty is a musician and artist from england . sometimes they used the names the justified ancients of mu mu , the jams and the timelords as their band name , instead .
