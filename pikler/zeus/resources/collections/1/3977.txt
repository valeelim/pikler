Decade     a decade ( pronounce : dek-aid ) is a period of 10 years . the word comes from the ancient greek word for ten : `` deka '' . 10 decades make a century . decades in the 20th century are normally called `` the eighties '' or `` the nineties '' , and written `` the 80s '' or `` the 90s '' . decades in other centuries are written in full , e.g . `` the 1890s '' .
