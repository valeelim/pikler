Maria Theresa of Austria     maria theresa of austria ( may 13 , 1717 – november 29 , 1780 ) was the only female head of the habsburg dynasty . she was the holy roman empress , queen of hungary and bohemia , and archduchess of austria . during her rule she changed the royal palace outside vienna ( the austrian capital ) to look much like versailles . vienna itself became an important center for the arts , especially music . maria theresa added support to her absolute power by tightening her hold on the government . she also improved conditions for the peasants . she is generally known to history as the empress maria theresa . maria theresa was born in vienna , austria , on may 13 , 1717. her parents were charles vi , holy roman emperor and elisabeth christine of brunswick-wolfenbüttel . maria theresa was married to duke francis stephan i of lorraine for love . they had sixteen children who are named here : - archduchess maria elisabeth of austria ( 1737–1740 ) - archduchess maria anna of austria ( 1738–1789 ) - archduchess maria carolina of austria ( 1740–1741 )
