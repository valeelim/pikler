Bu Bing     king bu bing of shang of china ' , in chinese : '' 卜丙 '' , born zi sheng , in chinese : '' 子勝 '' . he was the third king of the shang dynasty ( 1600 bc - 1046 bc ) . he was the second son of king tang . in records of the grand historian , he was recorded as the second king of the shang dynasty . in that book he is given the name `` wai bing '' , in chinese : '' 外丙 '' . but in the oracle script , he was recorded as the third king of the shang dynasty . in these records his name is `` bu bing '' , in chinese : '' 卜丙 '' .
