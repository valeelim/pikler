Nagasaki     nagasaki is in the southwest of kyushu . it has been recognized as a core city since 1997. during the edo period , nagasaki was the only port in which trade with other countries was permitted because of the national isolation policy ( `` sakoku '' ) . in the 1870 's , nagasaki became the capital city of the prefecture which evolved from the former province . nagasaki was the second of two japanese cities which were destroyed with an atomic bomb near the end of the second world war . related pages . - hashima other websites . - nagasaki official website ; 長崎市 - visit nagasaki official tourism website for nagasaki city
