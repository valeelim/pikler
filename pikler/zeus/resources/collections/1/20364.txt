Bette Davis     bette davis ( april 5 , 1908 - october 6 , 1989 ) was an american actress of the stage , cinema and television . she was born in lowell , massachusetts . she was nicknamed `` the queen of hollywood '' , the `` first lady of the american screen '' and `` the fifth warner brother '' during her career . davis had the most academy award nominations for any actress ( with ten ) until katharine hepburn took her place with twelve . in 1981 kim carnes sang the hit song `` bette davis eyes '' . davis liked the song . carnes gave davis a gold record . davis hung the gold record on a wall . the kennedy center honored davis in 1987. she died of breast cancer in neuilly-sur-seine , france . other websites . - bette davis at classic actresses - classic movies ( 1939 - 1969 ) : bette davis
