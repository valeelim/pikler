Shirley Bassey     dame shirley veronica bassey , dbe ( born january 8 , 1937 ) is a welsh singer , born in cardiff to an english mother and nigerian father . she is best known for singing the theme songs for the james bond films , `` goldfinger '' , `` diamonds are forever '' , and `` moonraker '' . she was `` one of the most popular female vocalists in britain during the last half of the 20th century '' . other websites . - songs of shirley bassey website
