Meal     a meal is a serving of food eaten by human beings . meals usually include several different types of food , such as grains , vegetables , fruit , and in some cases , meat . meals have different names by time they are taken or by the type of food that is eaten . in north america and in europe , people usually eat three meals a day . the morning meal is called breakfast . it usually includes foods made from grain ( cereal , porridge , or bread ) , fruit ( apple , banana , or an orange ) , and milk products ( milk , yogurt , or cottage cheese ) . many people drink coffee or tea with their breakfast . around noon , people eat their lunch . people who are working at jobs are given a break to eat their lunch . the lunch meal includes foods such as a sandwich , soup , vegetables , fruit , and milk . in some countries , such as italy , people serve an alcoholic drink with lunch . in england , some people have a glass of beer with their lunch . in the evening , people eat their supper or dinner . dinners are different in different countries . usually dinners include a `` main course '' such as roast beef , a grain ( such as rice or pasta ) , a vegetable ( such as carrots or cauliflower ) and a dessert ( called a `` sweet '' or `` pudding '' in england ) .
