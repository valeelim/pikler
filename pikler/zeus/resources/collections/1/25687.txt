1341     events . - the queen 's college , a constituent college of the university of oxford , is founded . - petrarch is crowned poet laureate in rome , the first man since antiquity to be given this honor . - beginning of the breton war of succession over the control of the duchy of brittany - margarete maultasch , countess of tyrol , expels her husband john henry of bohemia , to whom she had been married as a child . she subsequently marries louis of bavaria without having been divorced , which results in the excommunication of the couple .
