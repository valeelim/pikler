Polyunsaturated fat     polyunsaturated fats are fats that are usually found in items like vegetable oils . such items are typically found to be liquid at room temperature . polyunsaturated fats are called organic compounds . its molecules contains more than one double bonds . the peculiar structure reduces the strength of the forces between molecules . this causes the items to have a lower melting point . this is why the items are usually liquid at room temperature . related pages . - monounsaturated fat
