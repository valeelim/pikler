Lychee     a lychee ( pronounced lee-ji ) is a tropical fruit in the genus `` litchi '' . it comes from southern china . since it is tropical , it will only grow in a warm , wet climate . the inside of a lychee is a clear , pinkish-white colour . the outside of the fruit is a hard spiky shell . its seed is brown in colour and very hard . the seeds are poisonous and should not be eaten . the lychee has a sweet taste , and may be frozen , made into sauces , jam , puree , or preserves . when frozen , the lychee tastes similar to sorbet .
