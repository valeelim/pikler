Philip Glass     philip morris glass ( born january 31 , 1937 ) is an american composer of modern music . he is one of the most influential music makers of the late 20th century . glass has created operas , music for quartets , dance , and much more . he is known for writing minimalist music . glass has described himself instead as a composer of `` music with repetitive structures '' . though his early music shares much with what is called `` minimalist '' , he has since evolved stylistically . other websites . - official website
