The Adventures of Robin Hood     the adventures of robin hood is a 1938 swashbuckler movie . it was directed by michael curtiz and william keighley . it was shot in technicolor . the movie stars errol flynn , olivia de havilland , basil rathbone , and claude rains . the story is based on the traditional tales of robin hood . it is the third of eight movies flynn and de havilland made together . the movie won three minor academy awards . in 1995 , the movie was selected for preservation in the united states national film registry .
