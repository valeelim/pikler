Seismic retrofit     seismic retrofitting is the modification of buildings that already exist to make them resistant to earthquakes . seismic retrofitting techniques can be applied to other kinds of natural disasters such as tornadoes and strong winds from thunderstorms . main levels of retrofitted building structure performance objectives may vary , namely : - to protect human lives . - to protect a structure from total failure . - to preserve a structure 's functionality after a strong earthquake . - to make a structure , practically , unaffected by any earthquake .
