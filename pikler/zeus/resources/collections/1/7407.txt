John Cleese     john marwood cleese ( born 27 october 1939 ) is an english actor and writer . he was born in weston-super-mare , england . he graduated from downing college , cambridge . cleese has a degree in law . he was a member of the comedy team , monty python . he starred in the television sitcom , `` fawlty towers '' that he co-wrote with his first wife , connie booth , who played the role of polly sherman on the show . he plays q in some of the james bond movies .
