Toulon     toulon is a city in the south of france . it has about 170,000 people . toulon is the prefecture of var . toulon is the third biggest city of the region provence-alpes-côte d'azur after marseille and nice . it is from the italian border . more reading . - `` le chevallier à découvert '' , jean-pierre thiollet , laurens ed. , 1998. isbn 2-911838-51-3
