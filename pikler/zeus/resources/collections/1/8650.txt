Sunset Boulevard (movie)    sunset boulevard ( movie ) sunset boulevard is a movie made in 1950. it featured gloria swanson as a fading silent screen star , erich von stroheim as her butler and william holden as the screenwriter who befriends her . the film was nominated for eleven academy awards and won three . andrew lloyd webber created a musical of it in 1993. initially it starred patti lupone , later followed by glenn close , betty buckley , and elaine paige .
