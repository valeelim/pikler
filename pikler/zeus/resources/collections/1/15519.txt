Mike Judge     mike judge ( born october 17 , 1962 ) is an american animator , actor , writer , and producer . he is most famous for creating and starring in the animated television series `` beavis and butt-head '' and `` king of the hill '' . judge also wrote , directed , and played a small part in the 1999 movie `` office space '' , and directed the 2006 movie `` idiocracy '' , which starred luke wilson . he was born in guayaquil , ecuador , grew up in new mexico , and lives in austin , texas .
