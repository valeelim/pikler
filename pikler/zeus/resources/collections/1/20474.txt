Brine     brine is water that has more than 5 % salt in it . it is used for preserving food . for this it is much like vinegar or sugar . brine is also used to transport heat . adding salt to water lowers the freezing point of the water . at 15.5 ° celsius , a saturated solution of brine can hold 26.4 % salt by weight . at 0° , it can hold 23.3 % salt . brines also have some pharmaceutical properties , which can be used to treat people , especially those with diseases of the skin , allergies and common colds . related pages . - salinity - salt water
