Railfan     a railfan is a person who likes railways and trains . railfans like to take pictures of trains . they also like to watch trains . some railfans make models of railways . these are called model railways . some railfans like to study the history of railways . some railfans collect things having to do with trains . railfans may carry a radio scanner when they are watching trains , so they can listen to the railway workers talk . some railfans work for a railway , or have family members who worked for a railway . most railfans like to ride trains . there are many magazines for people who like trains . railfans enjoy reading books and magazines about trains .
