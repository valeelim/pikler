Lombardy     lombardy is a region in the northern part of italy . it is the most populated region of the country , and has almost 9.4 million people . the capital is milan . lombardy has provinces within it . to the north of lombardy is the country of switzerland ( canton ticino e grigioni ) . to the west is the region of piedmont . to the east are the regions of veneto and trentino - alto adige . to the south is the region of emilia-romagna . in the italian language , lombardy is called `` lombardia '' . this name comes from an old german word : `` langbardland '' and means `` country of the lombards '' . in the north of lombardy there are very high mountains , ( 40 % of the territory is mountains ) the highest mountains are almost 4.000 meters in the bernina range . in the south of the region flows the po river , which is the longest river in italy . the most important rivers are the adda river , the mincio and the ticino river , all of them end in the po river . there are also a lot of lakes , the largest are the lake maggiore , the lake of varese , the lake como , and the lake garda .
