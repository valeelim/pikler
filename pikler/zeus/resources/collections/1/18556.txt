Bulb     a bulb is the part of some plants , mostly under the dirt , that stores food while the plant is resting from growing ( a `` storage organ '' ) . some well-known bulbs people grow to use as food are onions and garlic . some bulbs grown for the decorative flowers are the lily , tulip , and some irises . some plant storage organs that are similar to bulbs , but have different names are , tubers , corms ( swollen stems ) , tuberous roots , and pseudobulbs .
