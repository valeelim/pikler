Gelsenkirchen     gelsenkirchen is a city in germany in the state of north rhine-westphalia . it is about north of cologne in the so called ruhr area . about 269,000 people live there . in the past it was dominated by coal mining and steel industry . the city is famous for its football ( soccer ) club fc schalke 04. other websites . - official city website - official schalke 04 website - gelsenkirchen blog - official mir website
