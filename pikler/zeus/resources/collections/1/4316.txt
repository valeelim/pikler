Jewel     jewels ( or gems or gemstones ) are rare minerals . they usually have colours . they begin in a rough rock form but can be cut and polished to make jewellery . however , some other non-mineral rocks ( such as lapis lazuli ) or organic materials can also be also used for jewelry and are called gemstones . these include amber or jet . pearls and coral are sometimes also called gemstones . most gemstones are hard . soft minerals are used in jewelry too , because of their luster that gives them value . being rare ( in short supply ) also makes a gemstone valuable . the ancient greeks began to decide which gems are precious and semi-precious . this continued in other cultures . in modern times , the precious stones are diamond , ruby , sapphire and emerald , with all other gemstones being semi-precious . types of gemstones . - amethyst - aquamarine - diamond - emerald - jade - peridot - ruby - sapphire - topaz - turquoise other websites . - `` book of royal gemstones '' is a book from 1554 that talks about gemstones .
