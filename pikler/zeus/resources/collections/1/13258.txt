Roof     a roof is the top surface of the building . the plural of 'roof ' is 'roofs ' . the roof keeps out rain and snow and protects people from the sun . some roofs are sloped , or pitched , so the rain and snow fall off easily . some roofs are flat , but even flat roofs have a little bit of pitch so the water can flow away towards a drain . `` gable '' , `` hip '' , `` gambrel '' , `` shed '' and `` mansard '' roofs are the main types of pitched roofs . the type of roof on a building helps identify the building style . for example , an american arts and crafts style versus a cape cod style . roofs are often covered in asphalt or wood shingles . but they can also be covered in metal , slate , or clay tiles . it can be dangerous to walk on a pitched roof . some pitched roofs have parts that stick out for windows . these are called dormers . the edges of pitched roofs are called 'eaves ' . often there are gutters and leaders at the eaves , which take away water from the roof . flat roofs are usually covered in rubber or types of plastic . you can usually walk on a flat roof . at the edge of a flat roof there is sometimes a parapet . this is to prevents people from falling off the roof .
