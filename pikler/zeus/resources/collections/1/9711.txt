Fairuz     fairuz ( born nouhad haddad ( , november 21 , 1935 ) is a lebanese singer . she is a living legend who has been performing for over 50 years all over the world . her vocal range is contralto . her first album was released in 1952. she has released 98 more albums since then . fairuz was born nuhad haddad in 1933 in beirut , lebanon .
