Race     race has many meanings in english . it can mean : - to go very fast – `` he raced to the store '' - a competition between people - `` the race for the us presidency '' - racing , a competition about speed – `` the tour de france is an important bicycle race '' - race ( biology ) , a group smaller than a species . members of different races can have sex and make babies , but they are separate ( usually because they live in different places ) - `` a large race of birds lives on this island '' - race ( sociology ) , of the human species based on physical characteristics that are passed on to succeeding generations . - the `` human race '' means all people - `` the human race is destroying the environment ''
