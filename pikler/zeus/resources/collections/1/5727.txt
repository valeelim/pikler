J     j is the tenth ( number 10 ) letter in the english alphabet . it comes before the letter k and after the letter i. meanings for j . - in calendars , j can sometimes mean these months - january , june , or july . - in a deck of playing cards , j is used to mark each of the jacks . - it can also be used as a middle initial for people with middle names such as jack , james , john , jesus , or jason .
