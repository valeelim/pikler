Elbow     the elbow is a hinge-joint between the bones of the upper and lower arm . it joins the humerus of the upper arm and the radius and ulna of the forearm together . everyone has 2 elbows . it allows the lower arm to be extended or retracted ( moved in ) . the elbow also allows the arm to turn . it moves by using three ligaments : the `` anterior , posterior , ulnar collateral '' and `` radial collateral '' ligaments .
