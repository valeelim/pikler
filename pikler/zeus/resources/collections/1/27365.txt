Sheryl Crow     sheryl crow ( born february 11 , 1962 ) is an american singer-songwriter . she can also play guitar and bass guitar . many of her songs have charted and are still popular . they include `` the first cut is the deepest '' , `` soak up the sun '' , `` if it makes you happy '' and `` all i wan na do '' . crow has never married . she has two adopted sons , wyatt steven crow ( born on may 11 , 2007 ) and levi james crow ( born on april 30 , 2010 ) . she had breast cancer in 2006. other websites . - official website
