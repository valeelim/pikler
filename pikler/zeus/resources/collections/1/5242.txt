Hilary Swank     hilary swank ( born july 30 , 1974 ) is an academy and golden globe award-winning american actress . the is the second woman in the world to win two oscars before thirty ( the first one was jodie foster ) . swank 's movies include `` million dollar baby '' and `` freedom writers '' . swank was born in lincoln , nebraska . when she was nine she played her first role as mowgli in her school 's version of `` the jungle book '' by rudyard kipling . afterward she often was in school and university performances .
