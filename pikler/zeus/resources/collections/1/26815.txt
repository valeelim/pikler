Styx     styx is the goddess of the river styx in greek mythology , and was the only female river goddess . she was the daughter of okeanos and tethys , and with pallas was also the mother of zelus , nike , kratos , and bia ( and sometimes eos ) . during the titanomachy she sent the children to help zeus . to reward her for helping the olympians , zeus did not throw her in tartarus , but let her be the guardian of the river styx . she is the wife of the ferryman , charon , and the guardian of the river coctylus , archeon .
