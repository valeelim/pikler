Bay City, Michigan    bay city , michigan the american city of bay city is a small city in the state of michigan , united states . in the 2000 census it had more than 35,000 people living in it . although small , bay city is one of the largest port cities on lake huron , one of the north american great lakes . it is part of a triangle of small cities in central michigan . the other two cities are saginaw , michigan , and midland , michigan . bay city was the birthplace in 1958 of madonna ciccone , a well-known american singer . the scottish pop group bay city rollers are said to have gotten their name from the city as well . other websites . - city of bay city , michigan
