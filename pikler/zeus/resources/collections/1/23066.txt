FA Cup     the fa cup is a national football competition between all clubs in england that are linked with the sport 's governing body in that country , the football association . the winner of the fa cup usually goes through to the next season 's uefa europa league . if the winner of the fa cup has already qualified for a european competition , then the europa league place goes to the runner-up . if the runner-up has also qualified for a european competition , then the europa league place goes to 6th or 7th place , depending on who won the football league cup . the fa cup final is held at wembley stadium , and currently both semi-finals are also held at wembley , but in the past both semi-finals were played at neutral stadiums . the fa cup is the oldest football competition in the world and first took place in 1871. the current holders ( winners of the cup in 2017 ) are arsenal football club , of london . the cup has been won by arsenal more times than any other team with 13 wins .
