Window     a window is an opening in a wall or roof of a building , in a car etc. , to let air and light in . it is usually filled with a sheet of glass . there can be many different shapes and sizes , including rectangular , square , circular , or irregular shapes . some windows include coloured glass . windows are usually transparent so that people can see through them . before glass was used in windows , people in asia used paper to fill the hole in the wall . the paper would let light in .
