Balearic Islands     the balearic islands ( officially and in catalan illes balears ) are an autonomous community of spain , formed by the `` balearic islands province '' . the main balearic islands are mallorca ( also called in english `` majorca '' ) , minorca , eivissa or ibiza , and formentera . the capital is palma de mallorca , and other cities are mahon , ibiza , inca , ciutadella , and calvia .
