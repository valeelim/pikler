Granada     granada is a spanish city , capital of the province of granada , in andalusia . with 233,000 inhabitants , it is an important artistic and cultural center . the city has several famous monuments , such as the alhambra , the cathedral , the `` alcaiceria '' , and the `` corral del carbon '' . it is near sierra nevada , the highest mountain range in spain , in the valley where the genil and darro rivers meet . the darro runs through the `` albaicin '' and the '' sacromonte '' , two charming historical areas of the city . related pages . - battle of granada other websites . - detailed information about the alhambra - website of the granada tourist office - granada local tourist guide - photos of granada - international students community - more interesting information about the alhambra
