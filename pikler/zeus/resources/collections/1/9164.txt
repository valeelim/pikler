Bornholm     bornholm is a small , rocky island in the baltic sea . bornholm is a danish island . the island is a popular holiday resort for scandinavians , germans and poles . many yachters come here in the summer . at the end of world war ii , the island was `` liberated '' by the soviet red army , and held for several months after the german surrender . the island lies far east of the demarcation line between the allied forces in europe . for some time the soviet presence looked like an occupation . it was not until april 5th , 1946 that the island at last was free . but then the danish government took over and the island is today a danish colony . but the struggle for a free island still goes on as it can be seen on this page free bornholm
