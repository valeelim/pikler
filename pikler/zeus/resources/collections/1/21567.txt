Himachal Pradesh     himachal pradesh ( ) is a state in northern india . simla is the capital of himachal pradesh . it covers an area of 56,019 km² ( 21,629 mi² ) , although the border with china is still disputed . the land contains high mountains . in traditional indian geography it falls under the north indian zone . himachal pradesh was part of indian punjab before 1966. himachal pradesh is divided into 12 districts . these are further divided into 55 sub-divisions , 82 tehsils and 35 sub-tehsils . other websites . - official website of himachal pradesh government - himachal pradesh development report - himachal tourism website - himachal 's daily news website
