Mario Party (series)    mario party ( series ) mario party is a series of video games created by nintendo and hudson . the idea of each game is that mario and his friends must travel across a game board and earn stars and coins . each new game in the series contains new characters , playing boards , and mini-games . every few turns , each character must participate in a mini-game . the winner generally gets 10 coins . common characters . - mario - luigi - princess peach - princess daisy - wario - waluigi - bowser - toad - toadette - donkey kong - koopa kid ( bowser 's minion )
