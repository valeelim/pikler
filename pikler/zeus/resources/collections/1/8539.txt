Glastonbury Festival     the glastonbury festival is a very big music festival . it is held at worthy farm near glastonbury , somerset , england , most years , during the last weekend in june . about 100,000 people come to the festival every time . the festival is hosted and organized by local farmer michael eavis . it is a widely known festival with many big and popular bands playing there every year . in glastonbury 2009 , there was a great deal of controversy when jay-z was asked to headline the festival . the most infamous response to this was when noel gallagher from oasis said that glastonbury should only be for rock musicians .
