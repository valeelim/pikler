Fast food     fast food is the term for a kind of food that people eat from a restaurant , cafe or take-out where food is prepared and served quickly . the restaurants that sell fast food are called `` fast food shops '' or `` fast food restaurants '' . some of the more common fast food restaurants are mcdonald 's , wendy 's , burger king , taco bell , and kfc . when a lot of fast food is eaten , it can cause health problems and complications , such as high cholesterol , clogged arteries , diabetes , obesity , and death .
