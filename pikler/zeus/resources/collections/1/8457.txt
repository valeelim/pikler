Waiter     a waiter is a person who `` waits '' on tables , often at a restaurant or cafe . a female waiter is called a waitress . they will take orders and delivers food to customers . a good waiter can also help the customers by recommending the best food in the restaurant or cafe . many waiters and waitresses are required by their employers to wear a uniform . most uniforms used are black and white or all black . historically the term waiter was used to describe customs officers who waited on the high tide for vessels to come in carrying goods to tax .
