Manmohan Singh     manmohan singh ( born 26 september 1932 ) was the 13th prime minister of india . a famous economist , he is the only prime minister since jawaharlal nehru to return to power after completing a full five-year term , and the first sikh to hold the office . singh was born on 26 september 1932 in gah , punjab , british india , into a sikh family . he studied at panjab university , chandigarh , at st john 's college , cambridge , and at nuffield college , oxford . singh has been married to gursharan kaur since 1958. other websites . - prime minister manmohan singh `` official website '' - profile & curriculum vitae - list of current union ministers
