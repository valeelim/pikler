Romanization of Ukrainian     romanization or latinization of ukrainian refers to a system for showing the ukrainian letter in latin letters . latin letters are the type that are currently used for this article . latinization may be used to show ukrainian text or pronunciation for non-ukrainian readers . there are two different kinds of romanization . these methods are transliteration , representing written text , and transcription , representing the spoken words . transliteration is the letter-for-letter representation of text using latin writing system . also called scientific transliteration , this system is most often seen in linguistic publications on slavic languages . it is meaning each character represents one meaningful unit of sound , and is based on the croatian latin alphabet . transcription is the representation of the spoken word . phonetic transcription represents every single sound , or phone , and can be used to compare different dialects of a language . it is more specifed as the transliteration .
