Arm     an arm is a limb . most humans have two arms each , coming out of their body just below the neck . on the end of each arm is a hand . humans use arms and hands to do things to other objects , this is called manipulation . the main purpose of the hand is to grasp objects . some other primates have arms which they use to move around by holding onto trees or supporting themselves as they walk along the ground . related pages . - leg - trapezius muscle
