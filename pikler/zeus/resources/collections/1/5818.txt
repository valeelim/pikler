Navy     the navy is the part of a country 's military that fights on water using boats . people in the navy are called sailors . the navy is mostly made up of warships . it may also have its own air force and soldiers . it may have special boats that can go underwater , called submarines , or another kind of special boat that is meant to carry airplanes , called aircraft carriers . originally , naval war was fought by ramming other ships and then boarding them . in ancient greece and rome , the ships were usually powered by oars . then , during the middle ages , the cannon was invented . these could be shot at other ships from a great distance . ships around this time also began to use sails instead of oars for power . in the 1800s , ships began to be plated in armor for protection . steam engines allowed them move while carrying the armor , and better ones soon made them much faster . these early warships , called ironclads , are thought to have begun the type of naval warfare used today . ships soon became much larger .
