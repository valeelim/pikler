Hermes     hermes is the `` messenger of the gods '' . he is the god of travellers and shepherds in greek mythology . he is one of the twelve olympians , and is often shown with his caduceus . hermes is the son of zeus and maia . he was born on mount cyllene . the story of his birth was told in the homeric hymn , which means `` hymn to hermes '' . it said that maia was a nymph . hermes was very clever and on his first day invented the lyre , by using the shell of a tortoise . one of the myths about hermes is that he once stole his brother 's oxes , and burned them as a sacrifice . children . - tyche was a daughter with aphrodite . - hermaphrodites was a son with aphrodite . related pages . - turms - etruscan mythology version of hermes . - mercury - roman mythology version of hermes .
