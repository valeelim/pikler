40 (number)    40 ( number ) forty is the number that is after thirty-nine and before forty-one . the prime factors of forty are 2 and 5 . ( 2 * 2 * 2 * 5 = 40 ) forty is also the only number in the english language which is spelt in alphabetical order . in the judeo-christian bible , the number forty has special meaning . the jews wandered in a desert for forty years , there was a forty-day and forty-night rainstorm , and other instances .
