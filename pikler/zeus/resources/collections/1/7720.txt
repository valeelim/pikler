Ravi Shankar     ravi shankar ( 7 april 1920 - 11 december 2012 ) was one of india 's most famous musicians . shankar was one of the first to bring aspects of traditional indian music into europe and united states|american culture in the 1960s . he was a major influence on george harrison , who was a member of the beatles . shankar is famous for playing the sitar , an indian stringed instrument . he performed until his death . in 2005 he played at the proms . his daughters norah jones and anoushka shankar are also well-known musicians .
