Period     period means different things . - period ( punctuation ) , a punctuation mark , also called a full stop - period ( geology ) , a series of rock strata , and the time during which the rocks were laid down - the time every month when a woman menstruates - the time it takes to finish one sequence of something that happens again and again - orbital period - periodic function - the time it takes to play 1/3 of an ice hockey game - a row in the periodic table
