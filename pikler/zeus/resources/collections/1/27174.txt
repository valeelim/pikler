Niue     niue is an island country in the south pacific . situated between tonga , samoa and the cook islands , it is commonly known as `` rock of polynesia '' . it has its own government , but it is an associated state of new zealand . this means that niue 's head of state is new zealand 's sovereign ( queen ) in right , and most diplomatic relations are conducted by the latter on niue 's behalf . the territory is 2,400 kilometres northeast of new zealand in a triangle between tonga , samoa , and the cook islands . related pages . - niue national football team
