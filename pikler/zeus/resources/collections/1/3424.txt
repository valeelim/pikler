Saxony     the free state of saxony ( ) , is one of 16 states of germany . it is in the southeast , north of czech republic . to the east of saxony is poland . the biggest city is leipzig . the capital is called dresden . it was founded in 1990. saxony is the border of germany to the two countries poland and czech republic . in the north of saxony is brandenburg , another german state . saxony 's west border is to saxony-anhalt , thuringia and bavaria . a big part of the border to the czech republic are the ore mountains in the south of saxony . saxony is divided into 3 administrative districts ( chemnitz , dresden and leipzig ) . these are subdivided into 10 districts : saxony has three urban districts which do not belong to the rural districts . - chemnitz ( c ) - dresden ( dd ) - leipzig ( l ) the first free state of saxony was founded in 1918 , after the first world war . after the second world war , in 1945 , the `` country of saxony '' was founded . later the country was divided into 3 administrative divisions of the german democratic republic , and thus robbed of its sovereignty .
