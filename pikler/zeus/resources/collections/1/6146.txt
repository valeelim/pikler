Second International     the second international ( 1889â€“1916 ) was an organization created on july 14 , 1889 , by socialist and labour parties who wanted to work together for international socialism . it continued the work of the first international , which stopped being an organization in 1886. however , it did not include the still-powerful anarcho-syndicalist movement and trade unions . among the second international 's most famous actions were its 1889 declaration of may 1 as international labor day and its 1910 declaration of march 8 as international women 's day . the international 's permanent executive and information body was the international socialist bureau ( isb ) . it was located in brussels and formed after the international 's paris congress of 1900. emile vandervelde and camille huysmans of the belgian labour party were its chair and secretary . vladimir lenin was a member from 1905 .
