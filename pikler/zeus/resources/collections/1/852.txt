Temple     a temple is a building where people go to practice their religion . in a temple people may perform religious rituals , ceremonies , and pray . thus , a temple is a general term for a house of worship . christians usually call their religious buildings churches . some examples of temples from different religions : - hinduism ( mandir ) - buddhism ( shaolin ) - temples at uppsala - greek temple - temple in jerusalem - mormon temple - roman temple - reform judaism 's synagogues . - baha'i ( mashriqu'l-adhkar ) - temples in bangkok
