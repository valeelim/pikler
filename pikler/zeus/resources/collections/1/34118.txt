Dan Quayle     james danforth `` dan '' quayle ( born february 4 , 1947 ) was the 44th vice president of the united states . he served with president george h. w. bush ( 1989â€“1993 ) . quayle was born in indianapolis , indiana . his parents were martha corinne ( nee pulliam ) and james cline quayle . he has often been incorrectly referred to as `` james danforth quayle iii '' . in his memoirs , he points out that his birth name was simply james danforth quayle . the name quayle originates from the isle of man , where his great-grandfather was born . his maternal grandfather , eugene c. pulliam , was a rich and influential publishing magnate who founded central newspapers , inc. , owner of over a dozen major newspapers such as `` the arizona republic '' and `` the indianapolis star '' . james c. quayle moved his family to arizona in 1955 to run part of the family 's publishing business . while his family was very wealthy , quayle was less rich ; his total net worth by the time of his election in 1988 was less than $ 1 million . quayle and his wife are presbyterians . he currently lives in paradise valley , arizona .
