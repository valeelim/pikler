Manx     the word manx could mean many things related to the isle of man : - the manx language , also known as manx or manx gaelic - the manx people , an ethnic group in the isle of man - the manx cat , a domestic cat breed - the manx shearwater , a seabird - the manx pound , the currency of the isle of man - manx cattle , manx horse , manx pig and manx sheepdog , some extinct animals from the isle of man - manx radio , the national radio station of the isle of man
