Cerberus     kerberos ( latin cerberus ) is a creature in greek mythology , like a hound ( dog ) with three heads . it belongs to hades , and it guards the gate of the greek underworld . cerberus is a hellhound and is related to another mythical animal , the chimera ( part goat , part lion , part snake ) . cerberus makes sure the dead stay within the gates and the living stay away from them . however , the greeks did note in their myths that cerberus was a fan of sweet things like honey cakes ( the myth of eros and psyche ) and music ( the myth of orpheus in the underworld ) . another way to become friends with the animal was to talk to his owner , hades , the lord of the underworld ( the labours of hercules ) .cerbus is the son of typhon and echidna .
