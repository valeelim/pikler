Pyinmana     pyinmana ( , ; population : 100,000 ( 2006 estimate ) ) is a small town in mandalay division of myanmar . the government in 2005 decided to move the capital city from yangon to an area two miles ( 3.2 km ) away west of pyinmana on november 6 , 2005 , near the town , named naypyidaw , which means royal city . pyinmana is approximately north of yangon .
