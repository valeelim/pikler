AFI's 100 Years... 100 Stars    afi 's 100 years ... 100 stars afi 's 100 years ... 100 stars is a list of the top actors in hollywood history . it was announced by the american film institute on june 16 , 1999. the list promised one hundred people on it , but in the end there were only fifty . as of 2013 , of the fifty stars listed , only three are still alive : two men ( sidney poitier and kirk douglas ) , and one woman ( sophia loren ) . at the time the list was unveiled katharine hepburn , elizabeth taylor , shirley temple , lauren bacall , marlon brando , and gregory peck were also still living .
