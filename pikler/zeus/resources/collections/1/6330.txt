Tux     tux is a mascot of the linux operating system ; a cartoon penguin made by larry ewing in 1996. wherever he is seen , it means such a system can work with linux . he is also a character in some linux games , like `` super tux '' . the name `` tux '' comes from james hughes , who named the linux mascot that as an shortened form of `` torvalds ' unix , '' as linus torvalds was the creator of the linux operating system . tux is also an abbreviation of tuxedo .
