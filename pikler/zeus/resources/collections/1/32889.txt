Delft     delft is a city in the province south holland in the netherlands . it is about halfway between rotterdam and the hague . it is famous for its delft blue pottery and the delft university of technology . it is the place where the royal family , the kings and queens , are buried . it has about 94,000 inhabitants . delft is a city with typical dutch canals , churches and large canal houses . the largest church in delft is the nieuwe kerk ( new church ) .
