Mustache     the hair that grows on the upper lip of some men is called a mustache . the hair that grows on the sides of the face and the chin of some men is called a beard . some men have a lot of hair and a big mustache , and some have very little . in the modern world , many men shave part or all of their mustaches , or cut their mustache so it does not get very long . a chin beard with no mustache is called a goatee , whilst a chin beard with a mustache is known as a van dyke . mustache in united kingdom and commonwealth of nations is spelled moustache . some animals such as walruses also have hair like this , and people sometimes also call this hair a mustache .
