Alanganallur     alanganallur is a panchayat town in the district of madurai in the state of tamil nadu , india . this town is the headquarters of alanganallur taluk and alanganallur revenue block . the total population of this town is 11,075. alanganallur town is famous for the jallikattu ceremony conducted throughout the four days of pongal festival which was temporarily banned . agriculture is the main occupation in the town . the district headquarters madurai city is 16 km away from this town . alagar kovil is also near by , within 18 km . nearby places which attracts tourist are : the thadakai amman falls , kodaikanal , vaigai dam , kumbakarai falls and kutladampatti . there is a small dam named sathiyar near palamedu .
