Anton Nanut     anton nanut ( 13 september 1932 – 13 january 2017 ) was a slovenian conductor of classical music . from 1981 to 1999 , he served as the chief conductor of the rtv slovenia symphony orchestra . he was a professor of conducting at the academy of music in ljubljana . he also had worked with over 200 orchestras and had made over 200 recordings with many labels . he also worked with milenko stefanovic . he was born in kanal ob soči . nanut died after a long illness on 13 january 2017 in šempeter pri gorici . he was 84 .
