Dairy farming     dairy farming is a type of agriculture that is focused on producing milk . this is different from raising animals to produce meat . milk can be used to produce dairy products , including cheese . species commonly used are cows ( so-called dairy cows ) , but goats , sheep and camels are also used . sometimes donkeys are used to produce an alternative to cows ' milk for babies . some babies can digest donkeys ' milk better than cows ' milk . producing milk has been part of farming for a long time . in the last century , farms have become more specialised . in the 20th century , some farmers started with farms that only produce milk .
