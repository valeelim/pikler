Ammon     ammon is an ancient country in the middle east . it was situated in northwestern jordan and lay east of the kingdom of israel . it is mentioned in the bible and there often called the `` kingdom of ammon '' . rabbath ammon - present-day amman - was its capital . according to the biblical book of genesis ammon is a grandson of lot , the nephew of abraham ( the other grandson is moab ; the kingdom of moab lay south of ammon ) . the ammonites often fought with the israelites .
