Woody Johnson     robert wood `` woody '' johnson iv ( born april 12 , 1947 ) is an american businessman and philanthropist who has been confirmed to serve as the next united states ambassador to the united kingdom . he is a great-grandson of robert wood johnson i ( co-founder of johnson & johnson ) , and the owner of the new york jets of the national football league . johnson was nominated to be the united states ambassador to the united kingdom by then president-elect donald trump on january 19 , 2017 .
