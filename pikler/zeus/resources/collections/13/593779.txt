Operation Sentinelle     operation sentinelle is a counter-terrorism mission of the french army . it was put in place in january 2015 after the attacks against the satirical newspaper charlie hebdo and the supermarket `` hyper casher '' . it was the president of the french republic , fran√ßois hollande , who took the decision to create it and his successor emmanuel macron extended it . all the units of the french army but also of the french air force and the french navy participate in operation sentinelle . it results in a massive presence of french soldiers in the streets , public places and public transport , in reinforcement of the police and the gendarmerie . their function is to provide surveillance and dissemination of jihadist threats .
