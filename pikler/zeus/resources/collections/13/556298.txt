ShelterBox     shelterbox is an international disaster relief charity that provides temporary shelter and life saving supplies to displaced families . shelterbox sends a package that typically contains a tent designed to withstand extreme weather conditions . it also has a water purification kit , blankets , tools , and other things needed to help a family survive after a disaster . the contents of a shelterbox are designed for the nature and location of the disaster . other websites . - shelterbox home page
