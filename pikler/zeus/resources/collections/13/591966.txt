Gabriel de Broglie     gabriel-marie-joseph-anselme de broglie-revel ( born 21 april 1931 ) is a french historian and politician . broglie-revel was elected to the académie française in 2001. he replaced alain peyrefitte . he is a knight commander of the légion d'honneur . he is a distant relative of the ducs de broglie . other websites . - les immortels : gabriel de broglie
