Anna Netrebko     anna netrebko ( born 18 september 1971 ) is a russian operatic soprano . she was the people 's artist of russia ( 2008 ) . born in krasnodar , ussr , she graduated from the saint petersburg conservatory . in 2004 , she was awarded the state prize of the russian federation . `` time '' magazine added her to their time 100 list in 2007. she was recognised by the journal `` musical america '' as `` a genuine superstar for the 21st century '' . they also named her musician of the year for 2008. other websites . - profile of netrebko , `` the new york times '' - interview with anna netrebko on opera lively
