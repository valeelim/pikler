2014–15 NHL season     the 2014–15 nhl season was the 98th season of operation ( 97th season of play ) of the national hockey league ( nhl ) . thirty teams competed . there were 82 games in the regular season from october 8 , 2014 to june 15 , 2015. the 2015 stanley cup playoffs began on april 15 , 2015 , and ended on june 15 , 2015. the chicago blackhawks won the stanley cup . other websites . - nhl.com
