Zeyno Günenç     zeyno günenç ( born june 22 , 1967 , izmit ) is a theater , cinema and serialist . zeyno günenç graduated from mimar sinan university state conservatory theater department . he worked in bursa state theater for three years . he resigned from this institution and went to vancouver . süheyl uygur and behzat uygur brothers traveled to turkey for a three-year tour of the game titled `` three slakers '' . famous with the character `` aunt dominant gonul '' in the series named çocuklar duymasın ( `` children 's hearing '' ) . he married okan bayülgen and worked in the tiyatrokare community . it was alleged that some of the morning program at trt broke down due to pressures . filmography . - tatlı intikam - 2016 - çocuklar duymasın - 2010 - geniş aile - 2009 - paramparça aşklar - 2008 - hayat apartmanı - 2007 - doktorlar - 2006 - keloğlan kara prens'e karşı - 2005 - çocuklar ne olacak - 2004 - avrupa yakası - 2004 - çocuklar duymasın - 2002 - en son babalar duyar - 2002 - dadı - 2001 - eyvah kızım büyüdü - 2000
