John Delaney presidential campaign, 2020    john delaney presidential campaign , 2020 the 2020 presidential campaign of john delaney , the u.s. representative representing maryland 's 6th congressional district , was announced on july 28 , 2017. he became the first major democrat to announce their campaign for president . his campaign launched with the publication of an editorial on the `` washington post '' 's website . on the same day , he launched a campaign twitter account and youtube account , on which he posted a five-minute video telling voters of his plans . rumors of delaney 's campaign broke the day before the publication of his editorial . harry j. enten wrote in `` fivethirtyeight '' the day after the campaign 's announcement that it was highly unusual for a serious presidential candidate to announce so early . before delaney , pierre s. du pont iv had held the record of the earliest announcement before the presidential primary season when he announced his campaign for the 1988 election in 1986 , 615 days before the start of the iowa caucuses .
