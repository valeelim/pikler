José Manuel Martín     josé manuel martín pérez ( born may 24 , 1924 ) is a retired spanish actor , radio broadcaster , and screenwriter . he was a popular character actor in spanish cinema during the 1950s and 60s . he is best remembered for playing villainous henchmen , appearing in more than 100 movie and television productions . martín born in casavieja , spain . he studied at madrid 's teatro español universitario and the lope de rueda , and began working for radio nacional de españa in 1942 , before making his feature movie debut in césar fernández ardavín 's 1952 war drama `` la llamada de áfrica '' starring ali beiba uld abidin , yahadid ben ahmed lehbib and farachi ben emboiric . martín is particularly noted for his work in the spaghetti western genre , alongside regulars such as aldo sambrell and andrea scotti , with supporting roles in `` savage guns '' ( 1961 ) , `` gunfighters of casa grande '' ( 1964 ) , `` minnesota clay '' ( 1964 ) , `` a pistol for ringo '' ( 1965 ) , `` seven dollars on the red '' ( 1966 ) , `` arizona colt '' ( 1966 ) , `` a bullet for the general '' ( 1966 ) , `` blood river '' ( 1967 ) , `` 100 rifles '' ( 1969 ) , and `` cut-throats nine '' ( 1972 ) .
