Lennart Nilsson     lars olof lennart nilsson ( 24 august 1922 – 28 january 2017 ) was a swedish photographer . he was known for his photographs of human embryos and other medical subjects once thought not to be photographable . he was also known for his extreme macro photography . his book , `` a child is born '' ( 1965 ) , featured many of his photographs . he was born in strängnäs , södermanland county . nilsson died on 28 january 2017 at the age of 94 .
