Magallanes Region     the xii magallanes region ( ) is one of the fifteen regions of chile . it has four provinces . it contains the chilean territory claim in antarctica . the capital city of the region is punta arenas . other towns are puerto natales , porvenir and puerto williams . this region has the world famous torres del paine national park . provinces of the region . - province of magallanes : capital punta arenas . - province of última esperanza : capital puerto natales . - province of tierra del fuego : capital porvenir . - province of antártica chilena : capital puerto williams .
