Sigve Tjøtta     sigve tjøtta ( born 1 march 1930 ) is a norwegian mathematician . tjøtta was born in klepp , norway . he studied at the university of oslo , brown university and at the max planck institute . he was appointed docent at the university of bergen in 1960 , and was promoted to professor in 1963. he replaced oddvar bjørgum , and had the job for the university 's education in applied mathematics . his fields of research include plasma , nonlinear acoustics , hydroacoustics and acoustic streaming . he was also the dean of the faculty of mathematics and natural sciences from 1975 to 1977 , and has held positions in navf , ntvf , and in the national committee of the international union of theoretical and applied mechanics . he has also been a visiting scholar at the university of texas at austin . tjøtta married jacqueline naze , a colleague , in 1964. they lived in oslo . naze died in march 2017 .
