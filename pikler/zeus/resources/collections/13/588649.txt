Abdul Halim Khaddam     abdul halim khaddam ( ( ; born 15 september 1932 ) is a syrian politician . khaddam was vice president of syria from 1984 to 2005. he was one of the few sunni muslims to make it to the top of the alawite-dominated syrian leadership . he was long known as a loyalist of hafez al-assad , and held a strong position within the syrian government until he resigned his positions and fled the country in 2005 in protest against certain policies of hafez 's son and successor , bashar al-assad . he served as interim president of syria from 10 june 2000 to 17 july 2000 when hafez al-assad died in june 2000. he was later replaced by bashar al-assad .
