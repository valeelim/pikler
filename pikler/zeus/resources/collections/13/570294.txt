Princess Françoise, Mademoiselle d'Estouteville    princess françoise , mademoiselle d'estouteville princess françoise of monaco ( 20 july 1728 – 20 june 1743 ) was a member of the house of grimaldi and a daughter of the-then reigning princess louise hippolyte of monaco . she was known as `` mademoiselle d'estouteville '' . given the names `` marie marie françoise thérése '' , she was the youngest daughter of the-then reigning princess louise hippolyte of monaco and her consort prince jacques i of monaco . she known as `` mademoiselle d'estouteville '' taken from one of her father 's dukedoms . she never married and died as a result of smallpox in paris .
