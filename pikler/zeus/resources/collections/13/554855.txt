PK machine gun     the pk is a machine gun designed in the soviet union and is still being used currently by the russian army . the pk went in produtcion in 1961 and entered in 1969 to replace the degtyaryov and the sg-43 . the pk was designed by the ak-47 inventor named mikhail kalashnikov . the russian factory produced over 1,000,000 pk machine guns since the 1960s and today .
