Ren Xinmin     ren xinmin ( ; december 5 , 1915 – february 12 , 2017 ) was a chinese rocket scientist . he was a specialist in astronautics and liquid rocket engine technology . he worked at the university at buffalo . ren was born in ningguo , china . he studied at southeast university and at the university of michigan . ren was the technical director of the long march 1 rocket . it was used for the successful launch of china 's first satellite , and chief designer of chinese store able propellant rocket engine . he was also the chief designer for long march 3 launch vehicle , fengyun , and sj ( shijian ) series satellites . ren was elected member of the chinese academy of sciences in 1980. ren , huang weilu , tu shou'e and liang shoupan were bracketed together as `` four venerable pioneers of astronautics ( 航天四老 ) '' in china . they were granted the `` two bombs and one satellite merit medal '' in 1999. ren died on february 12 , 2017 in beijing , aged 101 .
