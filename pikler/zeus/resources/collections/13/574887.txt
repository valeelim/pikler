Nyctibatrachus     nyctibatrachus is a genus of frogs endemic to the western ghats of southwestern india . their common name is night frogs . their scientific name also means `` night frog '' . these frogs are quite common , and researchers think this has been overlooked because of their their small size . they are indeed extremely small , and can sit on a coin or a fingernail . however , they are not so small as `` paedophryne '' , which is the smallest land vertebrate .
