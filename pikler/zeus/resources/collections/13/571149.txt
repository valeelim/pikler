Alan Simpson (scriptwriter)    alan simpson ( scriptwriter ) alan simpson , obe ( 27 november 1929 – 8 february 2017 ) was an english scriptwriter . he was born in london . he was best known for the galton and simpson comedy writing partnership with ray galton . he wrote the bbc sitcom `` steptoe and son '' ( 1962–1974 ) , `` hancock 's half hour '' ( 1954–1961 ) and the first two series of `` comedy playhouse '' ( 1961–1963 ) . simpson was appointed an obe in 2000 and he and galton received a bafta fellowship on 8 may 2016. simpson died on 8 february 2017 in london from lung disease , aged 87 .
