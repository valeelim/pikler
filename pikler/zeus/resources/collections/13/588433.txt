Pearl Carr &amp; Teddy Johnson    pearl carr & amp ; teddy johnson pearl lavinia carr ( born 2 november 1921 ) and edward victor `` teddy '' johnson ( born 4 september 1919 ) are an english husband-and-wife team of entertainers , popular during the 1950s and early 1960s . carr and johnson were on british television light entertainment programmes , such as `` the winifred atwell show '' as well as `` big night out '' and `` blackpool night out '' . they represented the united kingdom in the eurovision song contest in 1959 and finished second with the song `` sing , little birdie '' . this peaked at # 12 on the uk singles chart . carr was born in exmouth , devon , while johnson was born in surbiton , surrey . other websites . - biography and `` sing , little birdie '' song lyrics - eurovision song contest details website
