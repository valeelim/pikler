Aharon Yehuda Leib Shteinman     aharon yehuda leib shteinman ( ) , also shtainman or steinman ( born c.1913 ) is a well-known haredi rabbi and posek ( halakhic authority ) in bnei brak , israel . following the death of rabbi yosef shalom elyashiv in 2012 , he is widely regarded as the gadol hador ( leader of the generation ) , the leader of the non-hasidic lithuanian world .
