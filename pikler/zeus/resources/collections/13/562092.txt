2010 Jnaneshwari Express derailment     jnaneshwari express derailment happened on the year 2010 may 28 in west midnapore district , west bengal . it was suspected that whether the track was bombed or sabotage that caused damage in it . at that time the communist party of india ( maoist ) arranged a four day bandh in that area . a state police stated that the track of length about 46cm was removed by the maoist-naxalites . at 1:30pm jnaneshwari super deluxe express train with 13 carriages which was travelling from howrah to mumbai derailed . about 141 were reported dead in this derailment of jnaneswari express and 180 were reported severely injured.compensation was announced rs.5lakhs for the family of the dead peoples .
