Borealopelta     borealopelta is a large armoured dinosaur . it was found in alberta , canada and is a nodosaurid ankylosaur . the osteoderms in its armour were in their life positions , with remains of their keratin sheaths and overlying skin . it may be the most complete ankylosaur ever found . melanin found in its epidermis remains suggests it was camouflaged . the report gives its estimated length as 5.5 metres and its estimated weight as about ~1,300 kg .
