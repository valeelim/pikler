Carl Adam Lewenhaupt     carl adam göstasson `` noppe '' lewenhaupt ( 1 august 1947 – 28 february 2017 ) was a swedish count , businessman and restaurateur . lewenhaupt worked at a bank and warehouse in london and new york . in the mid 1990s , he created and started geddesholm callcenter , one of swedens largest call-centers , better known as avesta teletjänst , which he sold in 2002. the same year he along with erik lallerstedt started the members only club `` noppe '' at östermalm in stockholm . lewenhaupt was found dead on 28 february 2017 in a river outside gärdet in stockholm .
