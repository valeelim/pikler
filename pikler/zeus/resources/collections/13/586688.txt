Gröde     gröde or gröde-appelland ( , north frisian `` groue '' , low german `` grööd '' ) is an island and municipality in the district of nordfriesland in schleswig-holstein , germany . only 9 people live on the island . this is the smallest population of any municipality in germany . the people of the island make a living from tourism and limited agriculture . gröde has the smallest electoral district in the country . the tally is reported almost immediately after polls close . gröde belongs to the amt division of pellworm . its seat is on the larger island of pellworm .
