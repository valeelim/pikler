2015–16 NHL season     the 2015–16 nhl season was the 99th season of operation ( 98th season of play ) of the national hockey league ( nhl ) . thirty teams competed . there were 82 games in the regular season from october 7 , 2015 to april 10 , 2016. the 2016 stanley cup playoffs began on april 13 , 2016 and ended june 12. the pittsburgh penguins won their fourth stanley cup . none of the seven canadian-based teams qualified for the playoffs . the only other time in league history with no canadian teams in the playoffs was in 1970. other websites . - nhl.com
