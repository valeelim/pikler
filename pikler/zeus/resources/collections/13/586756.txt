David Choby     david raymond choby ( january 17 , 1947 – june 3 , 2017 ) was an american prelate of the roman catholic church . he was the 11th bishop of nashville , having served as diocesan administrator ( 2004–2006 ) . he was born in nashville , tennessee . choby died at the age of 70 on june 3 , 2017 in nashville , tennessee after suffering from an infection caused by a fall . other websites . - roman catholic diocese of nashville official site - david choby at dioceseofnashville.com
