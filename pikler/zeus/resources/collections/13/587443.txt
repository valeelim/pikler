Kathryn Stripling Byer     kathryn stripling byer ( november 25 , 1944 – june 5 , 2017 ) , also called kay byer , was an american poet and teacher . she was named by governor mike easley as the fifth north carolina poet laureate , 2005–2009 . she was the first woman to hold the position . byer died at the age of 72 on june 5 , 2017 from lymphoma in cullowhee , north carolina . other websites . - here where i am blog
