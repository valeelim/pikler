Clyde Stubblefield     clyde stubblefield ( april 18 , 1943 – february 18 , 2017 ) was an american funk drummer . he began his career around the 1960s . he best known for his work with james brown . his recordings with james brown include the singles `` cold sweat '' , `` there was a time '' , `` i got the feelin ' '' , `` say it loud – i 'm black and i 'm proud '' , `` ai n't it funky now '' , `` mother popcorn '' , `` get up , get into it , get involved '' and the album `` sex machine '' . he was born in chattanooga , tennessee . stubblefield died from kidney failure on february 18 , 2017 in madison , wisconsin . he was 73 .
