Dolly Style     dolly style is a swedish pop group . the group was created by emma nors and palle hammarlund . it features three girls , all born in 1996 , that call themselves molly , holly and polly . the trio claims to originate from a `` dollhouse in dollyville '' . the group gets its inspiration from 1960s fashion and victorian style . the group has participated in the melodifestivalen two times .
