Damodar River     the damodar river is a river in india . it flows through the states of west bengal and jharkhand . the river is nicknamed `` the sorrow of bengal '' because of the floods in the plains of west bengal . the damodar river has many tributaries . they are : - barakar river - konar river - bokaro river - haharo river - jamunia river - ghari river - guaia river - khadia river - bhera river chota nagpur plateau receives an annual rainfall of around . the volume of this monsoon rain produces the floods of the damodar river . many of the floods of the damodar river is recorded in the years of 1700 , 1855 , 1866 , 1926 , 1935 and 1943 .
