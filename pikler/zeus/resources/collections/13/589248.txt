Gaehwa Station     gaehwa station is a train station of seoul subway line 9. it is in gaehwa-dong , seoul . it is the only station above ground in line 9. the headquarters of the seoul metro line 9 corporation is located in the station . history . - september 18 , 2008 : station name decided - july 24 , 2009 : opened with opening of seoul subway line 9 gaehwa - sinnonhyeon gaehwa station is the only above ground station in line 9. the platform is on 1st floor , and the concourse ( station lobby ) is on the 2nd floor . data of 2009 is calculated from july 24 ( first opened ) ~ december 31 .
