Gutland     the gutland ( luxembourgish : `` guttland '' ) is the middle and the south of luxembourg . it is about two-thirds of the country . about 90 percent of the people in luxembourg live here ( about 540,000 people ) . the gutland has low hills with an altitude of 200 to 300 meters . the gutland used to be grevenmacher and luxembourg districts , and also the very southern parts of the diekirch district . related pages . - oesling is the north of luxembourg .
