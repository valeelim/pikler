Maipo (volcano)    maipo ( volcano ) maipo is a stratovolcano in the andes , lying on the border between argentina and chile . it is located south of tupungato , one of the highest mountains in the americas , and about southeast of santiago . the first ascent of this peak was made on 19 january 1833 by paul güssfeldt , a german geologist , mountaineer and explorer , together with two chilean guides : lorenzo zamorano and francisco ( only the first name is known ) . in chile , the maipo volcano is in the santiago metropolitan region , cordillera province , san josé de maipo commune . in argentina , it is in the mendoza province , san carlos department , pareditas district . the maipo river starts on the western side ( chile ) of the volcan . the diamante river start on the eastern side ( argentina ) in the lake with the same name . the volcano was formed during the pleistocene . there are four historical and confirmed records of eruptions of the maipo : in 1826 , 1829,1905 and 1912. the `` laguna del diamante '' ( `` diamond lake '' ) was formed by the eruption of 1826 .
