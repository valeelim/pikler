Ólöf Nordal    ólöf nordal ólöf nordal ( 3 december 1966 – 8 february 2017 ) was an icelandic politician . she was a member of the independence party . she served as the minister of the interior from 4 december 2014 to 11 january 2017. ólöf was born in reykjavík . she was married to tómas már sigurðsson and had four children . ólöf died from pancreatic cancer on 8 february 2017 in reykjavík . she was 50 .
