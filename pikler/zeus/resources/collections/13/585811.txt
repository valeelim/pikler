Nawele     nawele was high chief of oahu ( a hawaiian island ) . his name is pronounced in hawaiian language as `` nah-weh-leh '' . nawele was born on the island of oʻahu as a son of chief kahokupohakano and his wife , kaumana ii . this means that nawele 's grandfather was high chief elepuukahonua . after the death of elepuʻukahonua , an unknown person ruled over oʻahu . after the death of that man , nawele became a high chief . the wife of nawele was called kalanimoeikawaikai . their son was high chief lakona of oahu . nawele died on oʻahu .
