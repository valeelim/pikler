Marco Aurélio Garcia     marco aurélio garcia , ( 22 june 1941 – 20 july 2017 ) was a brazilian politician . he was a member of the workers ' party ( partido dos trabalhadores ) . he was a professor of latin american history on leave from unicamp university and a left idealist . he was previously professor at the latin american social science faculty of the university of chile and of the paris-viii and paris-x universities . he was born in rio grande do sul , brazil . aurélio garcia died on 20 july 2017 in rio de janeiro , brazil of a heart attack , aged 76 .
