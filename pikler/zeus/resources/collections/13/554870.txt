Chiloé Island     chiloé island ( spanish : `` isla de chiloé '' ) , also known as greater island of chiloé ( `` isla grande de chiloé '' ) , is the largest island of the chiloé archipelago off the chilean coast with the pacific ocean . the island is located in southern side of chile , in the los lagos region . aditionally , it has a very special architecture and local culture . as example , the churches of chiloé are part of the world heritage sites . related pages . - chiloé archipelago - los lagos region other websites . - description about chiloé churches with pictures - images of chiloé
