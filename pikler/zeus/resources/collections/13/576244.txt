Kleve     kleve ( or `` cleves '' in english ) is the capital of the kleve rural district , north rhine-westphalia , germany . it had about 50,000 people in 2016. it was also the capital of the duchy of cleves ( disestablished in 1795 ) . kleve lies on the rhine river and borders the netherlands . partly on kleve territory lies the klever reichswald , an ancient forest dating from the middle ages . related pages . - bimmen , a hamlet of kleve
