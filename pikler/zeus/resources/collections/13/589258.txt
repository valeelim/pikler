Gwangmyeongsageori Station     gwangmyeongsageori station is a train station of seoul subway line 7 , located in gwangmyeong , gyeonggi-do . this stations was first opened with name `` gwangmyeong station '' , but because of ktx gwangmyeong station 's opening , this station has changed the name . gwangmyeongsageori station is sometimes called as gwangmyeong junction station , because the korean latter `` sageori '' means 4-way junction . history . - febuary 29 , 2000 : opened with opening of seoul subway line 7 sinpung - onsu , as gwangmyeong station - march 29 , 2004 : changed name to gwangmyeongsageori station with opening of ktx gwangmyeong station the upperbound ( for jangam ) side platform ( in below diagram , platform on the right side ) is not used normally , but used at morning to depart the train from train depot . data of 2000 is calculated from febuary 29 ( first opened ) ~ december 31 .
