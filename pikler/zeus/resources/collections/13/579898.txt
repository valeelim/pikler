Tribes of Israel     the israelites were divided into twelve tribes , according to the twelve sons of patriarch jacob : 1. asher 1. benjamin 1. dan 1. gad 1. issachar 1. joseph 1. judah 1. levi 1. naphtali 1. reuben 1. simeon 1. zebulun the land of israel was divided among those tribes , but there were a few exceptions : - the tribe of levi got no land but had to do temple duties . - the tribe of joseph was divided into two sub tribes : - ephraim - manasseh
