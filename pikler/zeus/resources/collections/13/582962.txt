Charles Kettles     charles s. kettles ( born january 9 , 1930 ) is a retired united states army lieutenant colonel . he is a medal of honor recipient for his services in the united states army . kettles received the medal of honor on july 18 , 2016 , nearly 50 years after his actions while serving as a flight commander assigned to 176th aviation company ( airmobile ) ( light ) , 14th combat aviation battalion , americal division . other websites . - lieutenant colonel charles s. kettles
