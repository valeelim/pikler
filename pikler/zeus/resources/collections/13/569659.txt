Walt Streuli     walter herbert `` walt '' streuli ( september 26 , 1935 – january 19 , 2017 ) was an american professional baseball player . he played as a catcher . he appeared in six major league baseball games over parts of three seasons with the 1954–1956 detroit tigers . streuli was born in memphis , tennessee . he studied at rhodes college . streuli died in greensboro , north carolina on january 19 , 2017 , aged 81. other websites . - box score from retrosheet
