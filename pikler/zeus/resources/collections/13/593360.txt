Kristiansand     kristiansand is a municipality in norway 's vest-agder county . kristiansand was created as a [ `` city-municipality '' ] `` bykommune '' in 1837. on 29 july 2017 around 70 nazis held an illegal demonstration and march in [ the centre of ] kristiansand , without being opposed by the police ; one person who placed himself in the way of the nazis , and another who demonstrated agaist the nazis , were removed by the police .
