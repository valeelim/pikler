2016 Oakland warehouse fire     the 2016 oakland warehouse fire was a fire in oakland , california on december 2 , 2016. at around 11:30 pm pacific standard time , a fire started in a warehouse . the warehouse , known as ghost ship , had been changed into places for people to live and into an artist collective . those changes were done without permits . at least 36 people died because of the fire . it was the deadliest building fire in oakland history .
