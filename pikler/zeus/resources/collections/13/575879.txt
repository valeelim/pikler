Thoothukudi     thoothukudi is a port city , a municipal corporation and an industrial city in thoothukudi district in the indian state of tamil nadu . the city lies in the coromandel coast off bay of bengal . thoothukudi is the headquarters of thoothukudi district.according to confederation of indian industry , thoothukudi has the second highest human development index in tamil nadu next to chennai . tuticorin city serves as the headquarters of tamilnad mercantile bank limited . major educational establishments in the city include thoothukudi government medical college , fisheries college and research institute , marine training academy , v.o.c . arts & science college , government polytechnic college , and anna university tuticorin campus.tuticorin port is one of the fastest growing major ports in india . tuticorin is an `` emerging energy and industrial hub of south india '' .
