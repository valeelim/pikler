John Boorman     john boorman ( ; born 18 january 1933 ) is an english filmmaker . boorman is best known for his feature movies such as `` point blank '' , `` hell in the pacific '' , `` deliverance '' , `` zardoz '' , `` excalibur '' , `` the emerald forest '' , `` hope and glory '' , `` the general '' , `` the tailor of panama '' , and `` queen and country '' . boorman has directed 22 movies and received five academy award nominations .
