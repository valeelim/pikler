Culen of Scotland     culen ruled as king of scotland following the death of dubh in 967 , he reigned from 967-971 although was recognized as early as 966 as king . he was born in the 10th century and died in 971. he was the son of indulf and an unknown mother . he also possibly ruled as king of strathclyde during his reign . he died in 971 and was succeeded by kenneth ii and amlaib . he is buried in an unknown location .
