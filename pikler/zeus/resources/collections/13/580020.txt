Barbara Helsingius     barbara christina elisabeth helsingius-koski ( née helsingius ; 27 september 1937 – 9 march 2017 ) was a finnish singer , poet , and olympic fencer . she participated at the 1960 summer olympics . she was born in helsinki . helsingius became well-known internationally , and was a member of visens venner in norway . she was a member of the joint nordic cultural organisation nordvisa . helsingius died from a long illness on 9 march 2017 in espoo , finland . she was 79 years old .
