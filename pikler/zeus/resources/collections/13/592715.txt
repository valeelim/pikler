Betty Boothroyd     betty boothroyd , baroness boothroyd ( born 8 october 1929 ) is a british politician . she served as a labour member of parliament ( mp ) for west bromwich and west bromwich west from 1973 to 1992. from 1992 to 2000 , she served as speaker of the house of commons . she was the first , and to date only , female speaker of the house of commons . she sits , by tradition , as a crossbench peer in the house of lords . other websites . - brief biography by bbc news , october 2000
