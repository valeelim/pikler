Mads Mikkelsen     mads dittmann mikkelsen ( born 22 november 1965 ) is a danish actor . he began his acting career in 1996. he is best known for his roles in the movies `` king arthur '' ( 2004 ) , `` casino royale '' ( 2006 ) , `` coco chanel & igor stravinsky '' ( 2009 ) , `` the hunt '' ( 2012 ) and `` doctor strange '' and `` rogue one '' ( both 2016 ) . he also appeared as hannibal lecter in the nbc television series `` hannibal '' ( 2013–15 ) . mikkelsen was born in østerbro , copenhagen . his older brother , lars , is also an actor . he married hanne jacobsen in 2000. they have two children .
