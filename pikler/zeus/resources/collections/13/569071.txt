Place of articulation     the place of articulation tells where a sound is made ( articulated ) . there are at least 13 different places of articulation . they are split into four big types . labial sounds are made with one or two lips . they are split into three types : bilabial sounds are made with both lips . the bilabial sounds in english are /m p b w/ . labiodental sounds are made with the lips and the teeth . almost always the top lip and the bottom teeth are used . the labiodental sounds in english are /f v/ . linguolabial sound are made by the tongue touching the top lip . there are no linguolabial in english . sometimes it is said to be part of the next group ( coronal ) .
