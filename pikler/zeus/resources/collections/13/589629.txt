Alexander Dennis Enviro200     the alexander dennis enviro200 ( previously known as transbus enviro200 ) is a single decker low-floor midibus . it was built by alexander dennis in three generations starting in 2003. the bus was first built by transbus international until the company was sold to alexander dennis . the first generation transbus enviro200 was launched in 2003. the second generation enviro200 was launched in august 2006. the third generation enviro200 , known as the enviro200 mmc ( short for major model change ) , was launched in may 2015 .
