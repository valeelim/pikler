Julia Hartwig     julia hartwig ( 14 august 1921 – 14 july 2017 ) was a polish writer and translator . she was born in lublin , poland . she was considered to be one of poland 's most important female poets . her first poems appeared in the journal `` odrodzenie '' in 1944. hartwig lived in paris from 1947-50. in 1954 , she published `` z niedalekich podróży '' ( `` from nearby places '' ) , a collection of articles . she published her first collection of poetry `` pożegnania '' ( `` farewells '' ) in 1956. hartwig died on 14 july 2017 in gouldsboro , pennsylvania of congestive heart failure at the age of 95 .
