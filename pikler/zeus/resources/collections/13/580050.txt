Amo Houghton     amory `` amo '' houghton , jr. ( born august 7 , 1926 ) is an american politician from the u.s. state of new york . he is a retired member of the united states house of representatives serving from 1987 through 2005. he was a member of one of upstate new york 's most well known families in business , the houghton family . he was a member of the republican party . houghton was born in corning , new york . he studied at harvard university . he was married to priscilla dewey until her death in 2012. other websites . - joyce k. mcintyre , harvard crimson , new york state of mind : after running family business , amory houghton serves as renegade u.s. representative , june 5 , 2000 - jeffrey smith , the evening tribune ( hornell , n.y. ) , wife of former congressman amo houghton dies , july 10 , 2012
