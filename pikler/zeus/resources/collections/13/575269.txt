Arthur B. McDonald     arthur bruce mcdonald ( born august 29 , 1943 ) is a canadian astrophysicist . mcdonald is the director of the sudbury neutrino observatory institute . he holds the gordon and patricia gray chair in particle astrophysics at queen 's university in kingston , ontario . mcdonald was awarded the 2015 nobel prize in physics with japanese physicist takaaki kajita . other websites . - arthur b. mcdonald and oscillating neutrinos - arthur b. mcdonald quotes with pictures
