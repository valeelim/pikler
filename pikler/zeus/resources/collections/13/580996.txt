Thorapalli     thorapalli is a village in hosur taluk , krishnagiri district of indian state of tamil nadu . the village is located on the banks of ponnaiyar river.the population of the village according to 2011 census is 9849 people of 4669 males and 5180 females . this village is the birthplace of india 's first indian governor general and indian freedom fighter , c. rajagopalachari .
