András Kovács     andrás kovács ( 20 june 1925 – 11 march 2017 ) was a hungarian movie director and screenwriter . he directed 30 movies between 1961 and 1996. his 1968 movie `` the lost generation '' was entered into the 6th moscow international film festival . his 1978 movie `` a ménesgazda '' was entered into the 29th berlin international film festival . his 1981 movie `` temporary paradise '' won the silver prize at the 12th moscow international film festival . his 1985 movie `` the red countess '' was entered into the 14th moscow international film festival . kovács was also a member of the jury at the 5th moscow international film festival in 1967 and the 1976 cannes film festival . kovács died in budapest , hungary on 11 march 2017 , aged 91 .
