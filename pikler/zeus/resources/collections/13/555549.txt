Alicia Machado     alicia machado ( born december 6 , 1976 ) is a venezuelan-born american beauty pageant winner , singer and actress . she was miss universe 1996. she was the fourth woman from venezuela to win the miss universe . before she was miss universe 1996 , she was miss venezuela 1995. in 2016 , machado became a united states citizen . also in 2016 , machado spoke out against donald trump . she said that he had called her `` miss piggy '' because of her weight during her year as miss universe . she said that trump also called her `` miss housekeeping '' because she was latina .
