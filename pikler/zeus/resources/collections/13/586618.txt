Ngwanaka Okae     ngwanaka o kae ? is a 1984 south african sepedi drama that was popular in those times . it deals specifically with the issue of stealing a child ( son ) of a young couple called mathlodi ( wife ) and rateka ( husband ) . the couple lives with mathlodi 's aunt in a rural township of pretoria called sohanguvhe . the couple originally hails from a province called limpopo , the then northen transvaal province . their neighbour woman steals their child and vanishes with it but finally caught
