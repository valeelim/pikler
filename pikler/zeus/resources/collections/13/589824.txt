Isaías Pimentel     isaías `` iyo '' s. pimentel ( 16 february 1933 — 26 june 2017 ) was a venezuelan international tennis player . he was born on the dutch island of curaçao . pimentel was three times curaçaoan champion . he competed in the davis cup , representing venezuela , a number of times , from 1957 to 1966. pimentel died on 26 june 2017 in willemstad , curaçao at the age of 84. other websites . - tennis archives
