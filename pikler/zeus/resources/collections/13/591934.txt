Matthew Willson     matthew willson is an award-winning canadian actor based in paris , ontario . in 2010 , matthew willson won an audience choice award from hamilton film festival . willson is known for playing `` preacher '' in the movie `` arrival '' . willson was raised in paris , ontario . willson had learned techniques of acting . later he founded willson entertainment productions . in 2010 , matthew starred in a feature-length movie `` skinning the cat '' . matthew also appeared in `` the negotiation '' in 2015. in 2016 , he played a role in `` arrival '' along with amy adams and jeremy renner . matthew also appeared in canadian comedy awards and several tv movies .
