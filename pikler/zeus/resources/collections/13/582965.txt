Robert J. Modrzejewski     robert joseph modrzejewski ( born july 3 , 1934 ) is a retired united states marine corps officer . he was awarded the united states ’ highest military decoration — the medal of honor — for his servies in vietnam . in a white house ceremony on march 12 , 1968 , president lyndon b. johnson presented the medal of honor to two marines for their actions in vietnam — major modrzejeski and second lieutenant john j. mcginty , iii .
