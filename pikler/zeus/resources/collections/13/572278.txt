Infante Henrique, Duke of Coimbra    infante henrique , duke of coimbra d. infante henrique , duke of coimbra ( 6 november 1949 – 14 february 2017 ) was an infante of portugal . he was a member of the former portuguese royal family as the youngest son of duarte nuno , duke of braganza , and princess maria francisca of orléans-braganza . infante henrique was fifth in the line of succession to the former portuguese throne . his elder brother , duarte pio , duke of braganza , is head of the house of braganza , which ruled portugal until 1910. henrique died on 14 february 2017 in lisbon , aged 67 .
