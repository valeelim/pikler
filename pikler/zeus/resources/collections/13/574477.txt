Toshio Nakanishi     , also known by the pseudonyms tycoon to $ h or typhoom tosh , was a japanese musician and graphic designer . he was best known as the founding member of new wave band plastics in 1976. he was at first a part of the technopop fever in japan and later acted as a pioneer of the japanese hip hop scene with his label major force . nakanishi died in tokyo from esophageal cancer on february 25 , 2017 , aged 61. other websites . - toshio nakanishi discography
