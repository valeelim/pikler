Alfonso Espinosa de los Monteros     pablo alfonso espinosa of the monteros rueda ( born 24 december 1941 ) is an ecuadorian news anchor and vice-president of news of ecuavisa in quito . he holds the guinness world record for `` longest career as television news broadcaster '' . espinosa de los monteros was born on 24 december 1941 in quito , ecuador . his parents were from ibarra . espinosa de los monteros worked for different radio stations in the cities of ibarra and guayaquil until 1962. in 1963 he was news director for radio la prensa . he began in ecuavisa in 1967 , the year of its foundation , as the first news anchor of the station . he was news director for almost three decades . he hosted the ecuadorian version of `` who wants to be millionaire '' ( 2001 - 2004 and 2009 - 2011 ) . he received the “ honorato vázquez ” medal from the ecuadorian government in 1992. in august 2014 , he obtained the guinness world record for `` longest career as television news broadcaster ” .
