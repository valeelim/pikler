Oran     oran ( arabic : وهران‎‎ - wahrān , berber : ⵡⴰⵀⵔⴻⵏ - wahren ) is a city in western algeria , situated at the coast . in 2010 , about 853,000 people were living there . it is the capital of oran province and was established in 903. oran comes second after algiers . it is well-known because of its business activities and culture . references . - arabic and french wikipedias .
