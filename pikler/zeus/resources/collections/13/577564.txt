Marianne Thieme     marianne louise thieme ( born march 6 , 1972 in ede ) is a dutch animal rights activist and writer , and also a politician of the party for the animals ( pvdd ) . she is co-founder of the party , and has been political leader since 2002. she has been a member of the second chamber and also parliamentary leader since 2006. previously she was party chair from 2002 to 2010. thieme studied law at erasmus university rotterdam . she is a member of the seventh-day adventist church , and a vegetarian . references . - bio at parlement.com
