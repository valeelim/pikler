John W. Young     captain john watts young ( born september 24 , 1930 ) is a retired american astronaut , naval officer and aviator , test pilot , and aeronautical engineer . he became the ninth person to walk on the moon as commander of the apollo 16 mission in 1972. he is the only person to have piloted , and been commander of , four different classes of spacecraft : gemini , the apollo command/service module , the apollo lunar module , and the space shuttle . young was born in san francisco , california . he studied at the georgia institute of technology . in 1956 , young married barbara white . they divorced in 1972. they had two children . other websites . - young 's official nasa biography
