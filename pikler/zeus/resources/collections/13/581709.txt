Michele Scarponi     michele scarponi ( 25 september 1979 − 22 april 2017 ) was an italian road bicycle racer , who rode professionally between 2002 and 2017 for the 2002 , domina vacanze–elitron , 2006 , 2007 , 2010 , 2013 and 2017 teams . scarponi was declared the winner of the 2011 giro d'italia after the disqualification of alberto contador . other major results of his career were the 2009 tirreno–adriatico , the 2011 volta a catalunya and the 2011 giro del trentino stage races . scarponi died at the age of 37 on 22 april 2017 , after being hit by a truck , while riding his bicycle , close to his home in filottrano , in the italian region of marche . he had competed in the 2017 tour of the alps only the day before .
