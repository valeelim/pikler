Yisrael Beiteinu     yisrael beiteinu , hebrew : ישראל ביתנו , meaning `` israel our home '' , is an israeli political party . its ideology is right-wing conservative , nationalist , populist , secular , and zionist . its focus is especially jews originating from the former soviet union . political leader is avigdor lieberman . yisrael beiteinu was founded by lieberman in 1999. number of seats in the 20th knesset : 6 ( -7 ) . participating in the fourth netanyahu government .
