Juliana Koo     juliana koo ( formerly young ; née yen ; september 26 , 1905 – may 24 , 2017 ) was a chinese-american diplomat . she served as first head of the united nations protocol department . she was a supercentenarian and after emigrating to the united states , she became the third wife of diplomat and politician wellington koo . koo died on may 24 , 2017 in new york city , aged 111 .
