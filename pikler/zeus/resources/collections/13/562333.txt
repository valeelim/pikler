Borderline intelligence     borderline intelligence ( also called borderline intellectual functioning or borderline mental disability ) is a category of intelligence in which a person has lower than normal mental ability . iq levels in this case are between 70 and 85. it is not as bad as intellectual disability ( iq 69 and under ) . in school years , people with borderline intelligence are known as `` slow learners '' . although borderline intelligence is a cognitive impairment , they will not always get special education .
