Porth     porth is a village in the rhondda valleys , wales . porth is at at the joint of the rhondda fawr and rhondda fach , porth sees itself as the capital of the rhondda . on 11 april 1877 the tynewydd colliery was the scene of a mine disaster that led to a mine rescue operation following which four first-class and twenty-one second-class albert medals for lifesaving were awarded . a build-up of water in old cymmer colliery resulted in flooding of the tynewydd mine . only fourteen of 100 miners employed at the mine were working underground at the time of the accident . four of the trapped miners were rescued after eighteen hours . the nearby village of dinas was the site of the first coal mine in the rhonddda . from porth you can travel by bus to places such as cardiff , treochy , pontypridd , porth has a train station in hannah street . there are schools in porth . porth infants for 3-6 yrs old . porth juniors for 6-11 yrs old . porth county community school for 11-18 yrs old ( opened in 1973 ) .michael sheen ( the welsh actor ) visited pccs in may 2009 .
