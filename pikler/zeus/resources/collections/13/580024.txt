Chuck Nergard     charles lester ( chuck ) nergard ( born february 6 , 1929 ) was an american politician in the state of florida . nergard was born in chicago in 1929 and moved to florida in 1956. he studied at schurz junior college , wright junior college , and george williams college . as a republican , he represented the 76th district which is in st. lucie county , florida . he served from 1967 to 1976 and from 1978 to 1990. he is married to catherine misheck . they have two children . during his time in the house , nergard was also a part-time real estate developer . his religion was listed as lutheran .
