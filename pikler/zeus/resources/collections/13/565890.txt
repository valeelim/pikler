Leah Bracknell     leah bracknell ( born alison rosalind bracknell 12 july 1964 in westminster , london ) is an english actress . she is best known for her role as zoe tate in `` emmerdale '' . she is also a teacher with the british school of yoga and designs and produces her own line of jewelry . in october 2016 , bracknell revealed that she was suffering from terminal lung cancer .
