Masud Ghnaim     masud ghnaim , arab : مسعود غنايم , hebrew : מסעוד גנאים , ( born february 14 , 1965 ) is an israeli politician . he is political leader of ra'am ( `` the united arab list '' ) . ghnaim is an arab israeli and a muslim . he obtained a ba in the history of the middle east at haifa university and subsequently performed educational and cultural activities . he joined the islamic movement in israel and chose the moderate division of this islamist movement , called the southern branch and one of the forerunners of ra'am . in 2003 he entered local politics by becoming a councillor of sakhnin ( lasted till 2005 ) , followed by national politics by becoming a member of knesset in 2009. since 2015 he has both been leader of ra'am and a the joint list politician . ghnaim wants israel to be changed into a caliphate and supports the iran-syria-hezbollah coalition . references . - bio at knesset website
