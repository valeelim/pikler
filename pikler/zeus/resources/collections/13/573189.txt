Anthony Downs     anthony downs ( ; born november 21 , 1930 ) is an american economist . he specialized in public policy and public administration . he has been a senior fellow at the brookings institution in washington , d.c. , since 1977. before 1977 , downs served for 18 years a member and then chairman of real estate research corporation . he also served as a senior analyst at the rand corporation and a professor at the university of chicago .
