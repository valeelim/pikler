Time in Iran     iran standard time ( irst ) , also called iran time ( irt ) , is a time zone used in iran . the standard time is 3.5 hours ahead of coordinated universal time ( utc+03:30 ) . during its daylight saving time , it uses iran daylight time ( irdt ) , which is 4.5 hours ahead of coordinated universal time ( utc+04:30 ) . originally , from 2006 to 2007 , there was no daylight savings in iran , but was re-introduced on march 21 , 2008. daylight saving time ( dst ) starts on march 21 or 22 ( farvardin ) and ends on september 21-22 ( shahrivar ) .
