1936 North American heat wave     the 1936 north american heat wave was the most severe heat wave in united states history . it happened in the middle of the great depression and the dust bowl of the 1930s . the heat wave began in late june . it created great human suffering . it also caused drought in the midwest and great plains . the lack of moisture , along with heat , destroyed huge numbers of crops . this heat wave set records in many states , including iowa , minnesota and illinois . in some states , the 1936 records were not broken until 2012. in ohio , the temperature reached 110Â°f , almost tying the 1934 temperature record there . more than 5,000 people in the united states died from this heat wave . the heat also affected canada .
