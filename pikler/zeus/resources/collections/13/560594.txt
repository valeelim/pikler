Composition of the Russian Regional Parliaments     regional parliaments of russia are the legislative/parliamentary bodies of power in the regions of russia ( republics , krais , oblasts , autonomous okrugs and federal cities of moscow and st. petersburg ) , which have different names , often collectively referred to in the media as regional parliaments . the federal structure of russia includes 83 regional parliaments . the biggest regional parliament is the state assembly of bashkortostan which consists of 120 deputies . the smallest one is the assembly of deputies of the nenets autonomous okrug which consists of 11 deputies . in the table are marked in bold parliaments , elected solely by the proportional system .
