Paul Reiser     paul reiser ( born march 30 , 1956 ) is an american comedian , actor , model , composer , television personality and writer , author and musician . he is best known for his role in the 1990s tv sitcom `` mad about you '' . he is ranked 77th on comedy central 's 2004 list of the `` 100 greatest stand-ups of all time '' . his well known movie roles are in `` diner '' ( 1982 ) , `` beverly hills cop '' ( 1984 ) , a role he reprised in its sequel , `` beverly hills cop ii '' ( 1987 ) . reiser also had roles in james cameron 's `` aliens '' ( 1986 ) , in which he played the corrupt carter burke ; `` the marrying man '' ( 1991 ) and `` bye bye love '' ( 1995 ) . reiser is jewish .
