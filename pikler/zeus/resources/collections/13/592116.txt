Kumaran Mahalingam     kumaran mahalingam ( born 02 march 1981 ) is an indian geologist , standup paddler and a conservation educator . he holds three limca book of records for different feats in stand up paddling . he is also a certified wilderness first responder from national outdoor leadership school , usa . kumaran m was born on 02 march 1981 in chennai . he is a graduate in geology from presidency college , chennai . currently he works at cairn india . awards & achievements . - three times limca book record holder for first long distance standup paddling . paddling for conservation . - highest stand up paddle boarding ( sup ) 16618 ft across tso ltak lake , ladakh , india 2016 - certificate of wilderness first responder , national outdoor leadership school , usa
