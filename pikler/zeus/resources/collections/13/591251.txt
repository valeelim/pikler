Bus stop     a bus stop is a designated place where buses stop for passengers to get on and off the bus . bus stops at busy locations may have shelters , seating , and possibly electronic passenger information systems ; less busy stops may use a simple pole to mark the location . related pages . - automatic vehicle location - bus bulb - bus safety act - bus station - bus terminus - hail and ride - intermodal passenger transport - metro station - street furniture - train station - tram stop - ticket ( admission ) - wait/walk dilemma
