Marchspitze     the marchspitze is a mountain in the allgäu alps . it is made of dolomite and is part of the hornbach chain . the mountain is high . it is in tyrol , austria , 1.5 kilometres east of the großer krottenkopf . it is a hard climb and therefore not many people visit it . additional reading . - thaddäus steiner : `` allgäuer bergnamen '' , lindenberg , kunstverlag josef fink , 2007 , isbn 978-3-89870-389-5 - dieter seibert : `` allgäuer alpen alpin '' , alpine club guide . munich , bergverlag rudolf rother , 2004. isbn 3-7633-1126-2 - alpine club map 1:25,000 series , sheet 2/1 , `` allgäuer- lechtaler alpen , west '' other websites . - marchspitze tour report
