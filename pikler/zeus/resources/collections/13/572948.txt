Cow killer     cow killers , or mutillidae , are a family of wasps . the wingless females of this species look like hairy ants . they are called velvet ants because of their dense pile of hair . the hair is usually bright red or orange . they are known for their very painful stings . because of the stings , they are called `` cow killer '' or `` cow ant '' . mutillidae live all over the world . there are over 8,000 species .
