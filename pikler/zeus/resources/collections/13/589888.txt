Pentagon Shopping Centre     the pentagon shopping centre ( known locally as 'the pentagon ' ) is a shopping centre in chatham , kent . the shopping centre is next to the waterfront bus station which replaced the pentagon bus station in 2011. the shopping centre was built in the 1970s . it has 2 floors with over 70 shops . there are also places to eat and a bowling alley . related pages . - pentagon bus station - chatham waterfront bus station
