Vijayakanth     vijayakanth is a tamil actor and politician . he was the leader of the opposition of the tamil nadu legislative assembly from 2011 to 2016. before becoming a politician , he was a film actor in tamil cinema from 1979 to 2010. he is the founder of desiya murpokku dravida kazhagam and served as a member of legislative assembly representing the constituency of rishivandiyam .
