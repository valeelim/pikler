Conductive hearing loss     conductive hearing loss is one of the main types of hearing loss . it happens when sound is blocked from going through the outer and middle ear into the inner ear and will only make noises sound less loud . most of the time , conductive hearing loss can be treated but how it is treated depends on the causes and is different for everyone . causes . - ear infections - some infections can cause the ear to swell and can make pus that blocks the ears - ruptured ear drums - a hole in the ear drum that makes it harder for sound to travel through the ears - objects blocking ear canal - some objects such as cotton tips and sometimes even ear wax can block the ears treatment . - medical - some ear infections can be treated by medicine - surgery - sometimes surgery is needed to remove ojects that are blocking the ears - hearing aids - since conductive hearing loss usually makes noises sound quieter , hearing aids can help by making the sounds louder for users other websites . - national hearing care find an audiologist in australia and get more information on hearing problems .
