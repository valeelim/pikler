Daya (singer)    daya ( singer ) grace martine tandon ( better known as daya ; born october 24 , 1998 ) is an american singer-songwriter . she released her self titled extended play ( ep ) , `` daya '' , on september 4 , 2015. the ep included the song `` hide away '' . `` hide away '' peaked at # 23 on `` billboard '' hot 100. on october 7 , 2016 , she released her first studio album `` sit still , look pretty '' . in 2017 , daya was nominated for a grammy award for best dance recording for her work on the song `` do n't let me down '' from the chainsmokers . daya was born near pittsburgh , pennsylvania .
