Rock ptarmigan     the rock ptarmigan ( ' ) is a medium-sized gamebird in the grouse family . it is known simply as the ptarmigan in the uk and in canada , where it is the official bird for the territory of nunavut , canada , and the official game bird for the province of newfoundland and labrador , canada . it is known as the snow chicken '' ' in the united states . in japan , it is known as the `` raichō '' ( 雷鳥 ) , which means `` thunder bird '' . it is the official bird of gifu , nagano , and toyama prefectures and is a protected species nationwide .
