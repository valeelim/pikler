Bethany (name)    bethany ( name ) bethany is a female first name . it comes from the biblical place bethany , the town where lazarus lived in the new testament . in english speaking countries , bethany has been very popular . it was the 59th most popular name for england and wales in 2010. in 1999 , it had gone as high as number 11. in the united states , the name was the 87th most popular in the late 1980s . `` beth '' is a short version of this name . `` beth '' can also be a short version of the name elizabeth .
