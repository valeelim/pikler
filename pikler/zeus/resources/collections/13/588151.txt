Sandwich panel     a sandwich panel is a kind of composite material , which is usually made of two components : a small outside layer ( which is the same on both sides ) , and an inside layer , which is used to get certain properties , for example insulation or fire resistance . aluminium is often used for the outside part - the respective panels are also known as aluminium composite panels . sandwich panels are used in situations where high structural rigidity and low weight are required . they are also used ofr cladding , eg . to add insulation , fire resistance , or simply to make a surface look more beautiful .
