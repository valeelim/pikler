Gurmukhī alphabet     the gurmukhī alphabet is used to write the punjabi language . gurmukhī is primarily used in punjab , india . it is the official script in punjab . there are 35 native characters in gurmukhi , in the 18th-19th century a new character family called naveen toli was added . this helps to pronounce and write persian words with proper tone . the word gurmukhi literally means `` from the mouth of guru '' .
