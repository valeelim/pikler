Archduke Joseph Árpád of Austria    archduke joseph árpád of austria archduke joseph árpád benedikt ferdinand franz maria gabriel ( ; 8 february 1933 – 30 april 2017 ) was a member of the hungarian palatine branch of the house of habsburg-lorraine . he was born in budapest , the son of archduke joseph francis of austria and his wife , princess anna of saxony . he was the great-grandson of archduke joseph of austria , count palatine of hungary . honors . - : knight of the order of the golden fleece ( 1961 ) - : general captain of the order of vitéz ( 1977–2017 ) he received a degree in economics from the university of lisbon .
