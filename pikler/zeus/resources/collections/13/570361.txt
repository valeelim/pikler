Hassan Joharchi     hassan joharchi ( ‎ ; 28 july 1968 – 3 february 2017 ) was an iranian actor . he began his career in the 1980s . he was known for roles in `` oo yek freshteh bood '' ( 2005 ) , `` shoghe parvaz '' ( 2012 ) and `` ensanha '' ( 2012 ) . he was born in tehran . he was married with two children . joharchi died from a cardiac arrest due to liver disease on 3 february 2017 in tehran . he was 48 .
