The Chainsmokers     the chainsmokers are an american pop and electronic dance music duo . they are made up with drew taggart and alex pall . the duo released their first studio album , `` bouquet '' , in october 2015. their song , `` roses '' , reached the top 10 on `` billboard '' hot 100. in 2016 , `` closer '' , with vocals from halsey , became their first # 1 single on the hot 100 chart . the chainsmokers are from new york city .
