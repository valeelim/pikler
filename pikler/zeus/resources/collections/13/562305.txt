Joan Chen     joan chen ( born april 26 , 1961 ) is a chinese-american actress , movie director and movie producer . she became famous for her performance in the academy award-winning 1987 movie `` the last emperor '' . she also had a role in the 1990-1991 television series `` twin peaks '' . chen played mrs. yee in the 2007 award-winning erotic thriller `` lust , caution '' . chen was born in shanghai , china .
