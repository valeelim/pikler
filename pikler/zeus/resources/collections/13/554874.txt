Chiloé Archipelago     chiloé archipelago is an archipelago located in los lagos region , in southern chile . the main island is chiloé island ( `` isla grande de chiloé '' ) . aditionally , in the archipelago are located 10 administrative communes related with the los lagos region : ancud , castro , chonchi , curaco de vélez , dalcahue , puqueldón , queilén , quellón , quemchi and quinchao . related pages . - chiloé island - los lagos region
