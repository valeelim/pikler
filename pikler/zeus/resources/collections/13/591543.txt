Martin Luther University of Halle-Wittenberg     the martin luther university of halle-wittenberg ( german : `` martin-luther-universität halle-wittenberg '' , shortened `` mlu '' ) is a german public university in halle and wittenberg , in the state of saxony-anhalt . it was established by a merger in 1817 and is named after martin luther . president is udo sträter . it had nearly 20,000 students in 2015. its focus is on all sorts of sciences .
