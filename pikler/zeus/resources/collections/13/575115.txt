Tom Ridge     thomas joseph `` tom '' ridge ( born august 26 , 1945 ) is an american politician . ridge served as the assistant to the president for homeland security from 2001 to 2003 , and the first united states secretary of homeland security from 2003 to 2005. ridge was a member of the united states house of representatives from 1983 to 1995 and the 43rd governor of pennsylvania from 1995 to 2001. he is a member of the republican party .
