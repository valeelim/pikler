Hans Kraay Sr.     hans kraay sr. ( born johan hendrik kraay ; 14 october 1936 ) is a former dutch footballer and manager . he played as a defender . he played for dos , feyenoord and the netherlands national football team . he also managed ajax , az alkmaar , sparta rotterdam and feyenoord . kraay was born in utrecht . his son , hans kraay jr. ( born 1959 ) , was also a footballer and manager .
