Johann Alexander Döderlein     johann alexander döderlein ( february 11 , 1675 - october 23 , 1745 ) was a german historian , philologist and numismatist . he studied at university of altdorf near nuremberg . he was polymath and his field of work included archaeology , classical studies , meteorology , and regional history . döderlein was a member of the prussian academy of sciences and of the german academy of sciences leopoldina . he was rector of the alte lateinschule , the former school of weißenburg am sand .
