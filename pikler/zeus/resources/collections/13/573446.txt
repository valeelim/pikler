Jeanne Martin Cissé     jeanne martin cissé ( 6 april 1926 – 21 february 2017 ) was a guinean teacher and nationalist politician . she served as ambassador to the united nations and in 1972 was the first woman to serve as president of the united nations security council . she served in the government of guinea as minister of social affairs from 1976 until the 1984 military coup . cissé was born in kankan , guinea . she studied at the école normale d'institutrices de rufisque in dakar , senegal , where she trained to become a teacher . cissé died on 21 february 2017 in conakry , guinea , aged 90 .
