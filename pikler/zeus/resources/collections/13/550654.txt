Power (Kanye West song)    power ( kanye west song ) `` power '' is a song by american hip hop performer kanye west . it was released as the main single from his album `` my beautiful dark twisted fantasy '' . it peaked at # 22 on the `` billboard '' hot 100 chart . it got very good reviews from critics . the song is performed by kanye west . it has extra vocals from the soul singer dwele . it was co-produced by west and symbolyc one . the song has samples from `` 21st century schizoid man '' by king crimson , `` afromerica '' by continent number 6 and `` it 's your thing '' by cold grits .
