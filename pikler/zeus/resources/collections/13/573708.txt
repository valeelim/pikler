Districts of Israel     the state of israel is divided into six districts . in hebrew a district is called `` mahoz '' - מחוז ( plural : `` mehozot '' - מחוזות ) . these districts are : - haifa district , around the city of haifa and northern sharon plain - jerusalem district , west of the city of jerusalem - tel aviv district , around the city of tel aviv - central district , southern sharon plain and northern shfela - northern district , galilee - southern district , negev and southern shfela
