Siewert Öholm    siewert öholm henrik siewert natanael öholm ( 7 august 1939 – 25 january 2017 ) , better known as siewert öholm , was a swedish journalist and television presenter . he worked for sveriges radio between 1971 and 2002. he also worked on shows for sveriges television . he was also a debater for social conservatism values . öholm was born in husby . he was married twice and had four sons , including mp politician oskar öholm . öholm died from liver cancer on 25 january 2017 in växjö . he was 77 .
