HaAvoda     haavoda , hebrew : העבודה , meaning `` the labor '' , ( full name : mifleget haavoda hayisrelit , hebrew : מפלגת העבודה הישראלית , meaning `` israeli labor party '' , often called like that or just `` labor party '' ) is an israeli political party . its ideology is social democratic , and zionist . political leader is avi gabbay . haavoda was founded in 1968 as a result of a merger of three leftist parties : mapai , ahdut haavoda , and rafi . number of seats in the 20th knesset : 19 ( +4 ) . haavoda is cooperating with hatnu'a ( `` the movement '' ) and hatnu'a hayeruqa ( `` the green movement '' ) as hamahaneh hatziyoni ( `` the zionist union '' ) . together they have 24 seats .
