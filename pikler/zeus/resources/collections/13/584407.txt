Hubert Minnis     hubert alexander minnis ( born 17 april 1954 ) is a bahamian physician and politician . he is the 4th and current prime minister of the bahamas since may 2017. minnis is the leader of the free national movement , the present majority party , and the member of parliament for the new providence constituency of killarney . first elected to the legislature in the 2007 election , he succeeded hubert ingraham as party leader following the party 's defeat in the 2012 election . minnis became prime minister following the victory of his party in the general election held on 10 may 2017 defeating the incumbent perry christie .
