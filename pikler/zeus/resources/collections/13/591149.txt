Fikret Hakan     fikret hakan ( 23 april 1934 – 11 july 2017 ) , also known as bumin gaffar çitanak , was a turkish movie actor . he was the recipient of the honorary state artist , a prestigious title awarded by the turkish government . he was born in balıkesir , turkey . he was known for his role as kara in `` revenge of the snakes ( yılanların öcü ) '' ( 1962 ) . hakan died on 11 july 2017 in a hospital in istanbul , turkey of lung cancer at the age of 83 .
