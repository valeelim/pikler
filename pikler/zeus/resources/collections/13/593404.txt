Luiz Melodia     luiz carlos dos santos ( january 7 , 1951 - august 4 , 2017 ) , more commonly known as luiz melodia , was a brazilian actor , singer and songwriter of mpb , rock , blues , soul and samba . he was born in rio de janeiro . in 2015 , he won the 26th brazilian music award for best artist . melodia died on 4 august 2017 at the age of 66 in his hometown , rio de janeiro from bone marrow cancer . other websites . - http : //www.dicionariompb.com.br/luiz-melodia/dados-artisticos
