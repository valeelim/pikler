Field hockey at the 2012 Summer Olympics – Men's tournament    field hockey at the 2012 summer olympics – men 's tournament the men 's field hockey tournament at the 2012 summer olympics was the 22nd edition of the field hockey event for men at the summer olympic games . it was held over a thirteen-day period beginning on 30 july . the medal finals were on 11 august . all games were played at the [ riverbank arena ] ] within the olympic park in london , united kingdom . defending champions germany won the gold medal for the fourth time . they defeated the netherlands 2–1 in the final . australia won the bronze medal by defeating great britain 3–1 . teams . - ( host ) other websites . - results on olympic.org
