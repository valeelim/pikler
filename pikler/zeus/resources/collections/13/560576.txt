SNCF Class BB 61000     the sncf class bb 61000 diesel locomotives were built by vossloh to their g1206 design between 2002-2005 for the french state railways . twenty three locomotives were built , numbered 61001-61023. as they are operated by the freight sector , the locomotives carry a '4 ' prefix ( i.e . they are numbered 461001-461023 ) . these are the second locomotives to be numbered in this range , the first being the class c 61000 ( + tc 61100 ) .
