José Antonio Alonso     josé antonio alonso suárez ( 28 march 1960 – 2 february 2017 ) was a spanish politician and judge . he was a member of the socialist workers ' party ( psoe ) . he was the minister of the interior from 2004 to 2006 and the minister of defence from 2006 to 2008. he was born in león , león . alonso died from lung cancer on 2 february 2017 in madrid . he was 56 .
