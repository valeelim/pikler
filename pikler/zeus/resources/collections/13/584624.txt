Smartwatch     a smartwatch is a computerized wristwatch with functionality that goes beyond timekeeping . while early models can perform basic tasks , such as calculations , translations , and game-playing , 2010s smartwatches are effectively wearable computers . many run mobile apps , using a mobile operating system . some smartwatches function as portable media players , with fm radio and playback of digital audio and video files via a bluetooth or usb headset .
