Mark Colvin     mark colvin ( 13 march 1952 – 11 may 2017 ) was a british-born australian journalist and broadcaster . he was the presenter of `` pm '' —one of the main australian radio programs on the abc radio network—from 1997 to 2017. he was born in london . he was also known for his works in `` the world today '' , `` four corners '' , `` foreign correspondent '' , `` 7.30 report '' and `` lateline '' . colvin died of lung cancer at a hospital in sydney on 11 may 2017 at the age of 65 .
