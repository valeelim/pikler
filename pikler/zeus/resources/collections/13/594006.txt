Terele Pávez     teresa marta ruiz penella ( 29 july 1939 – 11 august 2017 ) better known as terele pávez was a spanish actress . she appeared in more than ninety movies since 1954. she was born in bilbao ( biscay ) , spain . she was known for her roles in `` the holy innocents '' , `` la celestina '' and in `` el bar '' . she won a goya award in 2003. pávez died on 11 august 2017 from a stroke at the age of 78 .
