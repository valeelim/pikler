Kaliningrad Time     kaliningrad time ( uz1 ) is a time zone in western russia that applies to the city of kaliningrad . it is 2 hours ahead of coordinated universal time ( utc+2 ) since october 2014. originally , under president dmitry medvedev 's law in 2011 , kaliningrad time was utc+3 hours , the same time as the further-eastern european time and eastern european summer time in the summer . russia does not currently observe daylight saving time ( dst ) .
