Peter Härtling     peter härtling ( 13 november 1933 – 10 july 2017 ) was a german writer , poet , publisher and journalist . he received the order of merit of the federal republic of germany for his major contribution to german literature . his first children 's book , `` und das ist die ganze familie '' , was published the following year . his children 's literature has often focused on social problems involving children . more reading . - burckhard dücker , `` peter härtling '' , münchen : beck : verlag edition text und kritik , 1983 , - maciej ganczar , `` romantische künstlerfiguren in der prosa von peter härtling '' , frankfurt am main : peter lang , 2015 ,
