Otto Schenk     otto schenk ( born june 12 , 1930 ) is an austrian actor , and theater and opera director . he was born in vienna , austria . his career began in 1953. schenk has appeared in over 30 movies ( mostly in german ) . he is known for his lavish , realist , traditionalist stagings at the metropolitan opera , most notably his production of richard wagner 's four-opera epic `` der ring des nibelungen '' . schenk is currently starring in the stage adaptation of lily brett 's chuzpe at the kammerspiele theatre in vienna . schenk is known for his movie roles in `` always trouble with the reverend '' ( 1972 ) and in `` reigen '' ( 1973 ) . his best known stage role was in `` arabella '' ( 1977 ) . other websites . - `` new york times '' review of schenk 's 2006 `` don pasquale ''
