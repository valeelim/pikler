Robin Stenuit     robin stenuit ( born june 16 , 1990 in ottignies-louvain-la-neuve ) is a belgian road bicycle racer . his speciality is sprinting . participating in professional teams since 2011 , stenuit has been riding for wanty-groupe gobert since 2016. he achieved several victories , among others the grand prix de la ville de nogent-sur-oise and the memorial van coningsloo , both in 2015. references . - french wikipedia other websites . - www.procyclingstats.com
