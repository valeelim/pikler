Français fondamental     français fondamental is a simplified version of french language with a view to teaching french to foreigners and the populations of the french union , while france wanted to improve the diffusion of its language in the world . a series of surveys conducted in the 1950s and 1960s show that a limited number of words are used in the usual way , both orally and in writing and in all circumstances , as well as a limited number of grammatical rules necessary for the functioning of the language .
