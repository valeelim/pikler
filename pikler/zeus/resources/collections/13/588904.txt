Oron (district)    oron ( district ) the oron district was a district in the swiss canton of vaud . the seat of the district was the municipality of oron-la-ville . it contained the following municipalities : - bussigny-sur-oron - carrouge - châtillens - chesalles-sur-oron - corcelles-le-jorat - ecoteaux - essertes - ferlens - la rogivue - les cullayes - les tavernes - les thioleyres - maracon - mézières - montpreveyres - oron-la-ville - oron-le-châtel - palézieux - peney-le-jorat - ropraz - servion - vuibroye - vulliens
