Sapa'u Ruperake Petaia     sapa'u ruperake petaia ( born 11 april 1951 ) is a samoan poet and writer . he was born in samoa , and studied at the university of south pacific in fiji . he is working as a civil servant manager in samoa . a collection of his poems called `` blue rain '' was published in 1980 ( among them the poem `` kidnapped '' ) . another poem called `` patches of the rainbow '' was published in 1992. in his works he is dealing with the colonial and western effects on samoa . references . - catalogue.nla.gov.au
