Omar Graffigna     omar domingo rubens graffigna ( born april 2 , 1926 ) is a former argentine air force officer . he served in the second military junta of the national reorganization process dictatorship . alongside reynaldo bignone , he is one of the last surviving ( ) members of the dictatorship . on 8 september 2016 he was sentenced to 25 years ' imprisonment for crimes during the dictatorship .
