B&amp;Q    b & amp ; q b & q plc is a british diy and home improvement retailing company in the united kingdom . its main offices are in eastleigh , hampshire , england . it is owned by kingfisher plc . it was founded in 1969 by richard block and david quayle as block & quayle . as of july 2017 , b & q has 300 shops in the united kingdom . since 2015 , b & q have been closing a number of stores , and converting some to outlets of screwfix . the company also has some outlets in ireland . stores in taiwan and china were unsuccessful , and closed down in 2007 and 2015 .
