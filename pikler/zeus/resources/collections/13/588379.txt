Luis Lusquiños     luis lusquiños ( 27 november 1951 – 17 june 2017 ) was an argentine politician . he was born in the san luis province . he had the highest number of absences in the 2005-2009 period . he was appointed chief of the cabinet of ministers during the brief presidency of adolfo rodríguez saá . lusquiños died on 17 june 2017 at the age of 65 .
