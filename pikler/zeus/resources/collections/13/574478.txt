Elisabeth Waldheim     elisabeth `` sissy '' waldheim ( née ritschel ; 13 april 1922 – 28 february 2017 ) was an austrian businesswoman . she was the wife of kurt waldheim , the united nations secretary-general and president of austria . she was the first lady of austria from 1986 to 1992. she applied for nazi party membership as soon as she was old enough , and was accepted in 1941 '' . she was born in vienna . waldheim died on 28 february 2017 in vienna at the age of 94 .
