Dindigul district     dindigul district is an administrative region in the south of tamil nadu , india . the district was carved out of madurai district in 1985. the dindigul city is the headquarters of the district . it has an area of 6266.64 km and comprises three revenue divisions , nine taluks , and 14 panchayat unions.according to 2011 census of india , dindigul district had a population of 2,159,775 with a sex-ratio of 998 females for every 1,000 males , much above the national average of 929. in 2006 , the ministry of panchayati raj named dindigul one of the country 's 250 most backward districts ( out of a total of 640 ) . it is one of the six districts in tamil nadu currently receiving funds from backward regions grant fund programme ( brgf ) .
