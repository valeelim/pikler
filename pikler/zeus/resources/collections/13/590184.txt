Cable ferry     a cable ferry ( known as chain ferry , swing ferry , floating bridge , or punt ) is a ferry that is guided ( and in some cases propelled ) across a river by cables connected to both shores . early cable ferries used either rope or steel chains , with the latter resulting in the name `` chain ferry '' . both of these were replaced by wire cable in the 19th century . other websites . - twyford chain ferry on the river trent , uk , 1899 - cable ferries operating in new brunswick , canada
