Gambling addiction     gambling addiction is when someone is obsessed with gambling despite the negative consequences . gambling addictions often puts the person in risk of losing everything . people with this illness often contemplate suicide more than the general population . many people use gambling as a way to get away from their terrible lives and to gain satisfaction . those with a gambling addiction often - needs to gamble with more and more money to satisfy themselves - has made repeated unsuccessful efforts to cut back - is always gambling - persists to win back their losses - has jeopardized or lost relationships - relies on others to provide money to provide financial support this mental disorder can be treated through therapy , counseling , and some medicine . there is no definite cure .
