Flåm, Norway    flåm , norway flåm is a village in norway . it is in the municipality of aurland in sogn og fjordane county . the closest airport is sogndal airport , haukåsen , which is about 70 kilometres from flåm . the european route e16 highway between oslo and bergen runs through flåm . in 2014 , the population was reported to be around 350. other websites . - visit flåm
