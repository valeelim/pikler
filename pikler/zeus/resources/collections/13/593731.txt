Gonzague Saint Bris     gonzague saint bris ( 16 january 1948 – 8 august 2017 ) was a french award-winning novelist , biographer and journalist . he won the 2002 prix interallié for `` les vieillards de brighton '' . he was the founder of la forêt des livres , an annual book festival , and the cabourg film festival . he was born in loches , indre-et-loire . saint bris was killed in a car crash on 8 august 2017 in saint-hymer , calvados at the age of 69 .
