Peter Simonischek     peter simonischek ( born 6 august 1946 ) is an austrian movie and stage actor . he has appeared in more than sixty movies since 1980. simonischek is known for his role as winfried conradi/toni erdmann in the 2016 movie `` tonu erdmann '' . he won the european film award for best actor and was nominated for a london critics circle film awards for best actor and a toronto film critics association awards for best actor .
