Frank Joseph Guarini     frank joseph guarini ( born august 20 , 1924 ) is an american democratic party politician . he represented new jersey in the united states house of representatives , where he represented the now-defunct fourteenth congressional district from 1979 to 1993. guarini served in the new jersey senate from 1965 to 1972. in 1970 , guarini unsuccessfully challenged incumbent u.s. senator harrison a. williams in the democratic primary , losing 66 % -34 % . other websites . - official website of frank j. guarini
