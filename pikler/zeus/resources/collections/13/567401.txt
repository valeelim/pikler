Alexandra Föderl-Schmid     alexandra föderl-schmid ( born 30 january 1971 ) is an austrian journalist . she has been the chief editor of the newspaper `` der standard '' since 2007. she began working at the newspaper in 1990. she has been the newspaper 's germany on-the-scene reporter and brussels-based european union on-the-scene reporter . föderl-schmid was born in haslach an der mühl , upper austria . she studied at the university of salzburg .
