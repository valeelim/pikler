Sam Warburton     sam kennedy-warburton ( born 5 october 1988 ) , known as sam warburton , is a welsh international rugby union player . warburton plays rugby for the cardiff blues and was first capped for wales in 2009. he plays as an openside flanker but is also can play blindside . in june 2011 , he was named as wales captain versus the barbarians and in august 2011 he was named as the wales captain for the 2011 rugby world cup . in april 2013 , he was named the british and irish lions captain for the 2013 tour to australia , and was also named as captain for the 2017 tour to new zealand warburton holds the record for the most wales caps as captain .
