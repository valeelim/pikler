Mir Hazar Khan Khoso     justice ( r ) mir hazar khan khoso ( urdu , ) ( born 30 september 1929 ) , was the caretaker prime minister of pakistan , from 25 march 2013 to 5 june 2013. khoso is a retired judge who served as the chief justice of the federal shariat court and served as the interim prime minister ahead of the general elections scheduled in may 2013 .
