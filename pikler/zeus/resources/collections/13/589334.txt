Richard Bryan     richard hudson bryan ( born july 16 , 1937 ) is an american attorney and politician . he served as the 25th governor of the u.s. state of nevada from 1983-89 , and as a united states senator from nevada from 1989 to 2001. he is a member of the democratic party . bryan was born in washington , d.c. and graduated from the university of nevada at reno in 1959 where he was a member of alpha tau omega . he earned his law degree from the university of california , hastings college of law . in 1963 he was admitted to the nevada bar .
