Edward Shames     colonel edward shames ( born june 13 , 1922 ) was a united states army enlisted man and officer . he later served in the u.s. army reserve . during world war ii he was assigned to the 506th parachute infantry regiment , 101st airborne division . shames is the last surviving officer of easy company , 2nd battalion , 506th parachute infantry regiment . other websites . - edward shames 's page on us airborne
