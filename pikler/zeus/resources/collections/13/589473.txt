John Higgins (footballer)    john higgins ( footballer ) john wilson higgins ( 27 january 1930 – june 28 , 2017 ) was a scottish professional footballer . he played as a defender . he was born in kilmarnock . higgins began his football career with edinburgh club hibernian . he played for hibs in the 1955–56 european cup , as part of the first british club to enter european competition . higgins then played for st mirren for two seasons between 1957 and 1959. he was part of the st mirren squad when they won the 1958–59 scottish cup , but he missed the final after contracting a viral infection . higgins then played in the football league for swindon town between 1959 and 1961. higgins died in edinburgh on 28 june 2017 , aged 87 , after battling alzheimer 's disease . other websites . - john higgins , neil brown - fitbastats.com
