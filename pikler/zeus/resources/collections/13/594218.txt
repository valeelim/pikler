Blanche Blackwell     blanche blackwell ( 9 december 1912 – 8 august 2017 ) was a jamaican heiress . she was born in costa rice . she was the mother of chris blackwell ( founder of island records ) and inspirational muse to ian fleming and noël coward . she was believed by some to have been the inspiration for some of fleming ’ s most memorable female characters ; pussy galore and honeychile rider . blackwell died in london on 8 august 2017 at the age of 104 .
