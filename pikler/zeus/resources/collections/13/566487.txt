SilkAir Flight 185     on december 19 , 1997 a silkair boeing 737-300 operated as silkair flight 185 was a flight from jakarta to singapore with 97 passengers and 7 crew members on-board , which crashed into the musi river for unknown reasons killing all 104 people on-board . the cause of the crash remains disputed for the national transportation safety committee , but the national transportation safety board concludes the crash as a pilot suicide by the captain . but the los angeles court thinks that the cause of the crash was a rudder malfunction .
