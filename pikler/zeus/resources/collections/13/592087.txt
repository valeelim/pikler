Ralph Regula     ralph straus regula ( december 3 , 1924 — july 19 , 2017 ) was an american politician . he served as the united states representative for ohio 's 16th congressional district from 1973 to 2009. he retired in january 2009 after 18 consecutive terms . he was a member of the republican party . in the 110th congress ( 2007–2009 ) , he was the second longest serving republican member of the house of representatives ( after bill young of florida ) . regula died in bethlehem township , stark county , ohio on july 19 , 2017 at the age of 92. links formerly displayed via the conglinks template : other websites . - financial information ( federal office ) at opensecrets.org - issue positions and quotes at on the issues
