Macmillan Cancer Support     macmillan cancer support is one of the largest british charities . it provides health care , information and financial support to people affected by cancer . as well as helping with the medical needs of people affected by cancer , macmillan also looks at the social , emotional and practical impact cancer can have . macmillan campaigns for better cancer care . macmillan cancer support 's goal is to reach and improve the lives of everyone living with cancer in the uk . they are one of britain 's top 1,000 charities .
