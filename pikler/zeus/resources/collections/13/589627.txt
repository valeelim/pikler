Arrondissement of Pontarlier     the arrondissement of pontarlier is an arrondissement of france . it is part of the doubs `` département '' in the bourgogne-franche-comté region . its capital , and subprefecture of the department , is the city of pontarlier . when the `` arrondissements '' of doubs were created in 1800 , pontarlier was one of them in the doubs department . the `` arrondissement '' of pontarlier is in the most southern of the `` arrondissements '' of the doubs department ; it is the largest with an area of . it has a population of 109,841 inhabitants , the `` arrondissement '' of the department with fewest inhabitants , and a population density of inhabitants/km² . the `` arrondissement '' of pontarlier is bordered to the north by the `` arrondissement '' montbéliard , to the east and south by switzerland , to the southeast by the jura department and to the west by the `` arrondissement '' besançon . after the reorganisation of the cantons in france , cantons are not subdivisions of the `` arrondissements '' so they could have `` communes '' that belong to different `` arrondissements '' .
