News Radio UK     news radio uk is a british digital radio station that is owned by radio newshub , radio today and radio response . branding as `` uk 's only 24 hour rolling news service '' , news radio uk features news , weather , traffic , sports , showbiz and business summaries on a continuous loop . in addition to its availability on dab ( in portsmouth only ) , the station 's programming is streamed live on the network 's website , and through apps for apple , android , and kindle devices ; plus on radioplayer and tunein . in september 8 , 2016 , radio newshub had a plan to launch uk 's first rolling news station . its format would be designed for occasional listens during the day , and covered all the day 's most important news every 10 minutes . the contents are produced by three owners , with the traffic information presented from inrix . in october 10 , 2016 , the station was officially launched at 10am on dab service in portsmouth first . news radio uk maintains a `` wheel '' schedule , which is in a 10-minute block , updated continuously throughout the day . the order of broadcast is : national news - world news - sports - travel - business - showbiz - weather .
