Earl Bakken     earl ethan bakken ( born january 10 , 1924 ) is an american engineer , businessman and philanthropist . he founded medtronic , where he developed the first external , battery-operated , transistorized , wearable artificial pacemaker in 1957. bakken retired from medtronic in 1989 and moved to a 9-acre estate in the kona district of hawaii he calls `` bakken hale '' . other websites . - earl bakken 's website - the bakken museum homepage
