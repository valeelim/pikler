Chained to the Rhythm     `` chained to the rhythm '' is a 2017 disco and dancehall song by american singer-songwriter katy perry . skip marley , the grandson of jamaican singer bob marley , raps in the song . it is taken from perry 's fifth studio album , `` witness '' , and is the ninth track on the album . it is about partying and being aware of things in society . the song hit # 1 in mexico and hungary . it also hit the top five in australia , canada , the united kingdom , and many other countries . in the united states , the song hit # 4 on the `` billboard '' hot 100. for her fifth studio album , perry said she wanted to make purposeful pop music . she had gotten depressed when donald trump was elected president of the united states in november 2016 and was not in the mood to `` write a club banger . '' the video for the song is set in a carnival-type land called oblivia and shows perry riding amusement park rides . it was released on february 21 , 2017 .
