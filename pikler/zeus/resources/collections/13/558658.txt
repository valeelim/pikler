Ala ud din Khilji     ala-ud-din khilji ( ; died 1316 ) , born as juna muhammad khilji , was the second ruler of the khilji dynasty . he reigned from 1296 to 1316. he is considered the most powerful ruler of the dynasty . he was a very ambitious person and a warmonger . he liked to call himself ‘ the second alexander ’ . he took the title ‘ sikander-i-sani ’ . he banned drinking of alcohol in open in his kingdom .
