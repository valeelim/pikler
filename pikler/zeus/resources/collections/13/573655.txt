Rhys Wakefield     rhys wakefield ( born 20 november 1988 ) is an australian actor . he started his career in 2000. he is best known for his roles in the movies `` the black balloon '' ( 2008 ) , `` sanctum '' ( 2011 ) and `` the purge '' ( 2013 ) . he also appeared as lucas holden in the soap opera `` home and away '' from 2005 to 2008. wakefield was born in cairns , queensland . he grew up in sydney , new south wales .
