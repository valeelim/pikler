Tommy Lasorda     thomas charles lasorda ( born september 22 , 1927 ) is a former major league baseball player . he has had a lengthy career in sports management . in 2009 , he marked his sixth decade in one capacity or another with the brooklyn/los angeles dodgers organization , the longest discontinuous ( he played one season with the kansas city athletics ) tenure anyone has had with the team , edging dodger broadcaster vin scully by two seasons . he was inducted into the national baseball hall of fame as a manager in 1997. lasorda came out of retirement to manage the united states team at the 2000 summer olympics in sydney , australia . he led the americans to the gold medal , beating heavily favored cuba , which had won the gold medals at the two previous olympics . other websites . - lasorda 's blog , `` tommy 's world '' at mlblogs.com
