Adib Boroumand     adib boroumand ( or adīb burūmand ) ( ; 11 june 1924 – 13 march 2017 ) was an iranian poet , politician , and lawyer . he was the head of the leadership council and chairman of central council of the national front of iran . he is known as the national poet of iran . he was born on 11 june 1924 in gaz , borkhar county , isfahan , persia ( present-day iran ) . he received a b.a . in judicial law from the university of tehran , and since then he had been lived in tehran . boroumand died on 13 march 2017 in tehran at the age of 92 .
