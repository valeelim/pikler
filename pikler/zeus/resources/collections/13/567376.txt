Meir Banai     meir banai ( ‎ ; 5 july 1961 – 12 january 2017 ) was an israeli musician , singer , and songwriter . he was a pop , rock and soul musician . he released ten albums , starting in 1984. he was best known his 2007 album `` shma koli '' , which had traditional jewish texts and prayers in the music . he was born in beersheba . banai died from cancer on 12 january 2017 in ganot . he was 55 .
