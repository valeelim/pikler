Tik Tok     `` tik tok '' ( styled `` tik tok '' ) is the debut solo single by american singer-songwriter kesha . it is from her debut studio album , `` animal '' . the song topped charts worldwide , including countries like the uk , canada , australia , and the us , where it was the best-performing song of 2010. it is kesha 's second single after the number-one single `` right round '' by rapper flo rida ; her vocals are not credited on this song .
