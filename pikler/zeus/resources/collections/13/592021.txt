Oropharyngeal cancer     oropharyngeal cancer is a disease in which malignant cells form in the tissue of oropharynx . the oropharynx is the middle part of the throat that includes the base of the tongue , the tonsils , the soft palate , and the walls of the pharynx . oropharyngeal cancers can be divided into two types , hpv-positive , which are related to human papillomavirus infection , and hpv-negative cancers , which are usually linked to alcohol or tobacco use .
