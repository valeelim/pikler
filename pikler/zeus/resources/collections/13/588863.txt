Peter the Aleut     peter the aleut ( d. 1815 ) , also known as cungagnaq , is a saint in some parts of the eastern orthodox church . he was a native of kodiak island in what is now alaska ( being a member of the people ) . he received the name peter when he was baptized as a christian by herman of alaska 's missionaries operating to the north . he is said to have been captured by spanish soldiers working near san pedro ( thought to be san francisco or somewhere else in southern california ) .
