Shaun Kenny-Dowall     shaun kenny-dowall ( born 23 january 1988 ) is a new zealand professional rugby league player . he plays for the newcastle knights of the national rugby league ( nrl ) . he also played for the sydney roosters from 2008 to 2017. he played more than 200 games and scored over a hundred tries for the roosters . he won the 2013 premiership with the team . he has also played for the new zealand national team . kenny-dowall was born in brisbane , queensland , australia . his father , john , won a gold and silver medal at the 2000 summer paralympics . other websites . - shaun kenny-dowall at the rugby league project
