Josephine Webb     josephine webb ( born june 21 , 1918 ) is an american electrical engineer . she was known as `` switchgear '' . she designed an eighteen-inch , full newspaper size fax machine with superior resolution . webb co-founded webb consulting company with her husband , also an electrical engineer . she is one of the first female electrical engineers , and considered a pioneer by the society of women engineers . at purdue university , she was one out of a total of five women engineers .
