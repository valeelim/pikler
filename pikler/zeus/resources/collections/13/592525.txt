Keshari Nath Tripathi     keshari nath tripathi ( ; born 10 november 1934 ) is an indian politician and the present governor of west bengal . he took promise as governor of west bengal on 24 july 2014 and as governor of bihar on 27 november in 2017. he was selected as the governor of mizoram on 4 april 2015. he is a experienced bjp leader . earlier , he was the speaker of uttar pradesh legislative assembly and the president of uttar pradesh unit of bharatiya janata party .
