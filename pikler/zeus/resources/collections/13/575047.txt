Michael Forest     gerald michael charlebois ( born april 17 , 1929 ) , better known as michael forest , is an american actor and pilot . he played the voice of prince olympius in `` power rangers lightspeed rescue '' . in his earlier years , michael was a movie and television actor , notably playing the part of apollo in the 1967 `` '' episode `` who mourns for adonais ? '' and most recently reprised this role in the `` star trek continues '' episode `` pilgrim of eternity '' 47 years later . forest was born in harvey , north dakota . he moved with his family at a very early age to seattle , washington . he graduated with a b.a . in english and drama from san jose state university . he is married to diana hale . other websites . - michael forest at the english voice actor & production staff database
