Jamie McCourt     jamie d. mccourt ( born december 5 , 1953 ) is a businesswoman and investor . she is the founder and ceo of jamie enterprises , and the former co-owner and executive of the los angeles dodgers . she became the highest-ranking woman in major league baseball , appointed first as vice chairman of the dodgers in 2004 , then president in 2005 , and finally ceo in 2009. on june 22 , 2017 , she was nominated as united states ambassador to belgium . on august 2 , 2017 , president trump formally withdrew her nomination and instead nominated her as united states ambassador to france and united states ambassador to monaco .
