Alphablocks     alphablocks is a british cgi-animated children 's educational television programme that tries to teach children how to spell with the use of animated blocks representing each letter . it is animated by blue-zoo and produced by alphablocks ltd. once the alphablocks discover that whenever they make a word it comes to life , many new adventures in alphaland can be created . this is meant to help preschool children with learning the alphabet , spelling , reading and writing . other characters . - birthday girl - cowboy - singing cow - puppy - female cat - rats - bats in january 2017 , `` numberblocks '' debuted on cbeebies . this new show takes on a similar approach to `` alphablocks '' , though with numbers instead of the letters of the alphabet . like `` alphablocks '' , it is also animated by blue-zoo .
