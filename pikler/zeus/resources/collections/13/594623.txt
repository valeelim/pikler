Herman Mandui     herman mandui ( 1969 - october 4 , 2014 ) was an archaeologist of papua new guinea . mandui is regarded as the `` godfather '' of archaeology in his country . he was working on several archaeological sites , for example in the western highlands province and in the eastern goilala district . he was the leading archaeologist of papua new guinea from 2008 till he died . references . - www.emtv.com.pg
