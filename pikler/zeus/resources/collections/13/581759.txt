Imre Földi     imre földi ( 8 may 1938 – 23 april 2017 ) was a hungarian weightlifter . competing at a record of five olympic games , he won a gold medal in 1972 and silver medals in 1964 and 1968. during his career he set 21 world records , and after his retirement he coached his daughter to become a european champion . földi earned numerous awards for his results and achievements , most notably he was named weightlifter of the century by the international weightlifting federation and was elected for sportsperson of the nation in hungary .
