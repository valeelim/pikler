Ernst Zündel     ernst christof friedrich zündel ( april 24 , 1939 —august 5 , 2017 ) was a german publisher and pamphleteer known for promoting holocaust denial . he had been jailed several times : in canada for publishing literature `` likely to incite hatred against an identifiable group '' , and on charges of being a threat to national security ; in the united states , for overstaying his visa ; and in germany for charges of `` inciting racial hatred '' . zündel died at his home in germany , of a suspected heart attack , on august 5 , 2017 .
