Tiruchirappalli Rock Fort     tiruchirappalli rockfort is a historic fortification and temple complex built on an ancient rock . it is located in the city of tiruchirappalli , tamil nadu , india . it is constructed on a high rock that may date to over one billion years ago . there are two hindu temples inside , the ucchi pillayar temple , rockfort and the thayumanaswami temple , rockfort . other local tourist attractions include the famous pallava-era ganesha temple and the madurai nayak-era fort . the fort complex has witnessed fierce battles between the madurai nayakas and adil shahi dynasty of bijapur , carnatic region and maratha imperial forces . the fort played an important part during the carnatic wars , helping lay the foundations of the british empire in india . the rockfort is the most prominent landmark of the city .
