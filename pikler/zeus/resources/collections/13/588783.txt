Orbe (district)    orbe ( district ) the district of orbe was a district in the canton of vaud , switzerland . the seat of the district was the town of orbe . as of 2006 it became part of the jura-nord vaudois district . it contained the following municipalities : - agiez - arnex-sur-orbe - ballaigues - baulmes - bavois - bofflens - bretonnières - chavornay - corcelles-sur-chavornay - croy - juriens - la praz - l'abergement - les clées - lignerolle - montcherand - orbe - premier - rances - romainmôtier-envy - sergey - valeyres-sous-rances - vallorbe - vaulion - vuiteboeuf
