Gates County, North Carolina    gates county , north carolina gates county is a small , rural county located in the northeast portion of the u.s. state of north carolina , on the border with virginia . as of the 2010 census , the population was 12,197. its county seat is gatesville . gates county is included in the virginia beach-norfolk-newport news , va-nc metropolitan statistical area . it is part of the albemarle sound area of the inner banks .
