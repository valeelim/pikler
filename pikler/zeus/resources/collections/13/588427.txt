Antonio Medellín     antonio medellín ( 15 april 1942 – 18 june 2017 ) was a mexican actor . he was known for his roles in the telenovelas `` muchachitas '' and `` rubí '' . in movies , he was known for his role in the 1982 drama mexican movie `` aquel famoso remington '' . he was born in mexico city , mexico . his career lasted from 1962 until his death in 2017. medellín died on 18 june 2017 at a hospital in mexico city at the age of 75. other websites . - biography at alma latina
