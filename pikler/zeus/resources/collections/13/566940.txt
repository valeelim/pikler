Gol Transportes Aéreos Flight 1907     gol transportes aéreos flight 1907 , was flying from manaus to rio de janeiro , the plane was a boeing 737-800 with 154 people on-board . on september 29 , 2006 the plane collided with a embraer legacy 600 while cruising over the amazon . the embraer landed safety at a military base with no loss to the seven people on-board , but the 737 crashed into the amazon killing all 154 people on-board .
