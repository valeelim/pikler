Always Be My Baby     `` always be my baby '' is a song by american singer and songwriter mariah carey from her fifth studio album `` daydream '' ( 1995 ) . it was released by columbia records in march 9 , 1996 as the third us single and fourth overall . the song was written by carey , jermaine dupri and manuel seal . a mid-tempo song , `` always be my baby '' became carey 's eleventh number one single on the billboard 100. worldwide cd single 1 . `` always be my baby '' ( album version ) – 4:19 1 . `` always be my baby '' ( mr. dupri mix ) [ feat . da brat & xscape ] – 4:43 1 . `` always be my baby '' ( mr. dupri extended mix ) [ feat . da brat & xscape ] – 5:33 1 . `` always be my baby '' ( reggae soul mix ) [ feat . lil ' vicious ] – 4:56 1 . `` always be my baby '' ( mr. dupri no rap radio mix ) [ feat . xscape ] – 3:44 u.s. 7 '' single 1 . `` always be my baby '' – 4:18 1 . `` long ago '' – 4:32 worldwide 12 '' single 1 . `` always be my baby '' ( always club ) – 10:51 1 . `` always be my baby '' ( dub-a-baby ) – 7:16 1 . `` always be my baby '' ( groove a pella ) – 7:15 1 . `` always be my baby '' ( st dub ) – 7:12
