Alun Wyn Jones     alun wyn jones ( born 19 september 1985 ) is a welsh rugby union player . he is the captain of the wales national team , and the ospreys . he is wales ' most capped lock forward and wales ' second most capped player behind gethin jenkins . he has also won seven caps for the british and irish lions . born in swansea , jones played his first rugby for bonymaen rfc . having previously represented wales at under-21 level , he played his first match for wales in june 2006 against argentina . also in 2009 jones was one of 13 welsh players selected in the squad for the british and irish lions tour to south africa.he scored a try in his first match for the lions against the royal xv . jones is married to anwen . they have a daughter named mali .
