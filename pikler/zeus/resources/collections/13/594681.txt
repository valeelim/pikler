Philip Ruppe     philip edward ruppe ( born september 29 , 1926 ) is an american politician . he is a member of the republican party . he served in the u.s. house of representatives from 1967 to 1979 before running , unsuccessfully for the united states senate in 1982. he is a korean war veteran , having served as a lieutenant in the u.s. navy . after leaving the u.s. house , ruppe became active in business before running for election , without success , to the 103rd congress . other websites . - the political graveyard
