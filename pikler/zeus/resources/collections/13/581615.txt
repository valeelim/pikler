Faisal Abedin Deepan     faisal abedin deepan ( bengali : ফয়সাল আরেফিন দীপন ) ( c. 1972 - october 31 , 2015 ) was a bangladeshi book publisher of the jagriti prokashoni publishing house . he was hacked to death inside his house in dhaka , the bangladeshi capital . he published books by bangladeshi-american writer and blogger avijit roy who was the four secular bloggers killed in bangladesh the same year . dipan 's father said his company had published avajit roy 's 2014 book , biswasher virus ( the virus of faith ) . an hour earlier , another publisher and two writers were attacked , but they survived . a local al-qaeda group said they were responsible for the attack .
