Fleur Agema     marie-fleur `` fleur '' agema ( born 16 september 1976 in purmerend ) is a dutch politician of the party for freedom ( pvv ) . she has been a member of the second chamber since 2006. from 2003 to 2007 she was a member of the provincial states of north holland for the pim fortuyn list ( lpf ) and the agema group . references . - bio at parlement.com
