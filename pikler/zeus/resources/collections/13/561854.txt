Golaghmuli Valley     golaghmuli valley is a high mountainous valley in upper ghizer of gilgit-baltistan pakistan . this valley is situated in the east of chitral and north of swat . golaghmuli valley is separated from the chitral and swat by high mountain passes . to reach golaghmuli valley , a person must take the gilgit-chitral road west from gilgit , and then continue his journey straight by gupis . after gupis , one continues northwest to reach the yasin valley and the straight road goes to golaghmuli valley . golaghmuli valley has a spectacular natural beauty . villages . - golaghmuli - golaghtori - herkush - handarap - teru - barset - terich - ali abad
