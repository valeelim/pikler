Jason Kander     jason kander ( born may 4 , 1981 ) is an american lawyer and democratic party politician . he was elected secretary of state of missouri in 2012 , serving from january 2013 through january 9 , 2017. he served as a state representative before becoming secretary of state . he was an intelligence officer in the army national guard , achieving the rank of captain . he was the democratic nominee for the united states senate , losing the 2016 senate election to republican incumbent roy blunt .
