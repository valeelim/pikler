British Motor Corporation     in september 1965 , bmc took control of its major suppliers ( of bodies ) , pressed steel , acquiring jaguar 's body supplier in the process . in september 1966 , bmc merged with jaguar cars limited . on 14 december 1966 , bmc changed its name to british motor holdings limited or bmh . bmh merged in may 1968 with leyland motor corporation limited , which made trucks and buses and were owners of standard-triumph international limited , bmh becoming the major part of british leyland motor corporation .
