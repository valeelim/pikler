James H. Weaver     james howard `` jim '' weaver ( born august 8 , 1927 ) is an american politician . he served as the democratic member of the united states house of representatives from oregon serving from january 3 , 1975 through january 3 , 1987. weaver was born in brookings , north dakota . he enlisted in the united states navy at the age of seventeen and served in world war ii on an aircraft carrier in the pacific . weaver moved to oregon from iowa , in 1947 to attend the university of oregon .
