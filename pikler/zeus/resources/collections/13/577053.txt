Herman Philipse     herman philipse ( born may 13 , 1951 in the hague ) is a dutch philosopher , specialized in epistemology . philipse studied law and philosophy at leiden university . from 1986 to 2003 he was a philosophy professor at leiden university . since 2003 he has been a philosophy professor at utrecht university . philipse is an atheist . he has published on philosophy and atheism . references . - www.sg.uu.nl
