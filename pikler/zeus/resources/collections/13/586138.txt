Nilgiris district     the nilgiris district is a district in tamil nadu , india . the district mainly lies in the nilgiri hills which gives the district its name . the capital of the district is the town of ootacamund ( ooty ) . the highest point of tamil nadu , the doddabetta peak is located in this district . according to the 2011 census of india , the nilgiris district had a population of 735,394 with a sex-ratio of 1,042 females for every 1,000 males , much above the national average of 929 females . nilgiris is a home to several hill stations that attracts tourists towards it . these include ooty , coonoor , gudalur and kotagiri . the nilgiri mountain train , popularly known as the toy train , attracts tourists as the journey offers spectacular views of the hills and forests .
