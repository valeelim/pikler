Michael Bennet     michael farrand bennet ( born november 28 , 1964 ) is an american businessman , lawyer , and democratic politician . he is the senior united states senator from colorado . he became a senator when ken salazar was appointed secretary of the interior . bennet worked as managing director for the anschutz investment company , chief of staff to then-denver mayor ( and current colorado governor ) john hickenlooper , and superintendent of denver public schools . other websites . - senator michael f. bennet official u.s. senate site - michael bennet for senator - collected news and commentary at `` the denver post ''
