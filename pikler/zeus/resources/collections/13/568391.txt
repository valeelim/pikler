Eddie Filgate     edward `` eddie '' filgate ( 16 september 1915 – 19 january 2017 ) was an irish politician . he was a member of fianna fáil . he served as teachta dála ( td ) from 1977 to 1982 for the louth constituency . he was born in county louth . filgate turned 100 in 2015. he died on 19 january 2017 at the age of 101 .
