Arrondissement of Sélestat-Erstein     the arrondissement of sélestat-erstein is an arrondissement of france in the bas-rhin department in the grand est region . its capital is the city of sélestat . when the bas-rhin department was created on 1800 , the arrondissement of sélestat ( or sélestat-erstein ) was part of that original department . the subprefecture of barr was moved to schlestadt ( sélestat ) in 1806. in 1974 , the `` arrondissements '' of sélestat and erstein were combined to form the new `` arrondissement '' of sélestat-erstein . the `` arrondissement '' of sélestat-erstein is the most southern of the bas-rhin department . it is bordered to the west and north by the molsheim `` arrondissement '' , to the northeast by the strasbourg `` arrondissement '' , to the east by germany , to the south by the haut-rhin department and to the soutwest by the vosges department . the `` arrondissement '' of sélestat-erstein has an area of and a population of 154,943 inhabitants . after the reorganisation of the cantons in france , cantons are not subdivisions of the `` arrondissements '' so they could have `` communes '' that belong to different `` arrondissements '' .
