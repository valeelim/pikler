Inomjon Usmonxo‘jayev    inomjon usmonxo ‘ jayev inomjon buzrukovich usmonxo ‘ jayev ( in cyrillic uzbek : иномжон бузрукович усмонхўжаев ; in russian : инамджан бузрукович усманходжаев `` inamdzhan buzrukovich usmankhodzhayev '' ; 21 may 1930 – 17 march 2017 ) served as the eleventh first secretary of the communist party of the uzbek ssr . usmonxo ‘ jayev became general secretary following the disastrous `` cotton scandal '' . usmonxo'jayev died on 17 march 2017 in fergana , uzbekistan at the age of 86. other websites . - world statesmen - uzbekistan
