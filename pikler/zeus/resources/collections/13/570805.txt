José Gea Escolano     josé gea escolano ( 14 june 1929 – 6 february 2017 ) was a spanish roman catholic bishop . he was born in el real de gandia , community of valencia . gea escolano became a priest in 1953. he served as the bishop of ibiza from 1976 to 1987. he was later the bishop of mondoñedo-ferrol from 1987 until his retirement in 2005. gea escolano died on 6 february 2017 in valencia , at the age of 87 .
