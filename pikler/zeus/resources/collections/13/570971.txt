Arthur Lehning     paul arthur müller-lehning ( 23 october 1899 – 1 january 2000 ) was a dutch writer , historian and anarchist . he was born in utrecht . he was known for french translations of mikhail bakunin works . in 1992 , he won the gouden ganzenveer , and in 1999 , the p. c. hooft award . lehning died on 1 january 2000 in lys-saint-georges , indre , france . he was 100 .
