James Florio     james joseph florio ( born august 29 , 1937 ) is a democratic politician . he served as the 49th governor of new jersey from 1990 to 1994 , the first italian american to hold the position ( he is of half italian ancestry ) . he also served as a member of the united states house of representatives for 15 years between 1975 and 1990. florio was born and raised in brooklyn , new york . his father was of italian descent and his mother was of scottish , irish , and german descent . in brooklyn , he attended erasmus hall high school .
