Joaquim Molins i Amat     joaquim molins i amat ( 9 february 1945 – 13 july 2017 ) was a spanish politician . he was born in barcelona . he was a member of the congress of deputies ( 1979–1986 and again from 1993–2000 ) and parliament of catalonia ( 1988–1993 ) . molins died in barcelona on 13 july 2017 of pancreatic cancer at the age of 72. other websites . - información de joaquim molins en el congreso de los diputados
