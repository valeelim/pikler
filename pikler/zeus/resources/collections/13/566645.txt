Needed Me     `` needed me '' is a song by barbadian singer rihanna . it is the seventh track from her eighth studio album , `` anti '' . the song was written by rihanna , dj mustard , adam feeney , brittany hazzard , charles hinshaw , derrus rachel , khaled rohaim , lewis hughes , nick audino , and te whiti barbrick , while dj mustard produced the song . it was released as a single in the us on march 26 , 2016 . `` needed me '' reached number seven on the `` billboard '' hot 100. on the year-end hot 100 of 2016 , the song ranked number 13. it has been certified four-times platinum by the riaa .
