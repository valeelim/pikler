Mitch Landrieu     mitchell joseph `` mitch '' landrieu ( ; born august 16 , 1960 ) is an american politician . he is the 61st and current mayor of new orleans . landrieu previously served as the 51st lieutenant governor of louisiana from 2004 to 2010. he is a member of the democratic party . his father is moon landrieu , the united states secretary of housing and urban development during the jimmy carter administration . his sister is former united states senator mary landrieu . other websites . - citymayors profile
