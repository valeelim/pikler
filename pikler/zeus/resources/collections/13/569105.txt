Immenstadt     immenstadt im allgäu is a municipality in the oberallgäu , the southernmost district of bavaria , germany . it is in the german alps . since 2008 the mayor has been armin schaupp . immenstadt was first mentioned in a 1275 administrative document . it was granted town privileges in 1360 by emperor charles iv . this makes it one of the oldest towns in the area . it was the seat of the counts of königsegg-rothenfels until 1804. immenstadt was a garrison town in the first world war , and was bombed once in the second world war . in 1999 parts of the municipality were flooded . other websites . - immenstadt
