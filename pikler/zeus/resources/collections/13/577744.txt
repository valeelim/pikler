Denis Voronenkov     denis nikolayevich voronenkov ( ; 10 april 1971 – 23 march 2017 ) was a russian politician . he was a member of the state duma ( 2011–2016 ) . after stepping down as a member of the parliament in 2016 , voronenkov left russia and settled in ukraine with his wife maria maksakova jr.. after settling in ukraine , he became a vocal critic of russian president vladimir putin and russian foreign policy . voronenkov was murdered in kiev on 23 march 2017 after being shot in the head , aged 45 .
