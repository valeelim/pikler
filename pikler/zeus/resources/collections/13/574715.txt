Joseph Vũ Duy Thống     joseph vũ duy thống ( july 2 , 1952 – march 1 , 2017 ) was a vietnamese prelate , bishop of the roman catholic diocese of phan thiet from july 2009 to his death and the head of the episcopal committee on cultural affairs . he was auxiliary bishop of the roman catholic archdiocese of ho chi minh city from 2001 to 2009 .
