Thierry Baudet     thierry henri philippe baudet ( born january 28 , 1983 in heemstede ) is a dutch political activist and a politician of the forum for democracy ( fvd ) . he has been political leader since 2016. he has been a member of the second chamber and parliamentary leader since 2017. previously he was party chair . baudet studied law and history at the university of amsterdam . he got his phd at leiden university and worked at the same university . he also had a column in dutch newspaper nrc handelsblad . he is very negative about the european union and has written several books on this topic . baudet with his forum for democracy was one of the people behind the referendum against a treaty of the european union with ukraine in 2016. although the majority of the voters were against this treaty , the referendum had no results in the end . he is partly of walloon descent . references . - bio at parlement.com
