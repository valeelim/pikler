Stefania Jabłońska     stefania jabłońska ( born szela ginzburg ; 7 september 1920 – 8 may 2017 ) was a polish physician and professor , specializing in dermatology . she worked at the medical university of warsaw . in 1972 , she theorized the association of human papilloma viruses with skin cancer in epidermodysplasia verruciformis . in 1978 , jabłońska and gerard orth at the pasteur institute discovered hpv-5 in skin cancer . jabłońska was awarded the 1985 robert koch prize . she was a recipient of the national order of merit and the commander 's cross of the order of polonia restituta .
