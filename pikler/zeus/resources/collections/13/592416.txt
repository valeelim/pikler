Black Canary     black canary is a fictional superheroine in comic books published by dc comics . she was created by the writer-artist team of robert kanigher and carmine infantino . the character debuted in `` flash comics '' # 86 ( august 1947 ) . one of dc 's earliest super-heroines , black canary has appeared in many of the company 's flagship team-up titles including justice society of america and justice league of america . since the late 1960s , the character has been paired with archer superhero the green arrow professionally and romantically . ign rated her its 81st-greatest all-time comic book hero . she was number 26 on `` comics buyer 's guide 's '' `` 100 sexiest women in comics '' list .
