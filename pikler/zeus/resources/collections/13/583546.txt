Inkoniq     inkoniq it solutions is a private limited company based in bangalore , india . the company provides design and development services in the space of web , mobile and iot . inkoniq was established in 2011 , with a goal of helping corporations for mobile app design and development with focused ux/ ui designs . the company was founded by rajesh kumar with two co-founders prachod prasad and hareesh p. awards . - best startup award at silicon india startup city event in 2012 - top 20 promising enterprise mobility companies of 2014 by cio review
