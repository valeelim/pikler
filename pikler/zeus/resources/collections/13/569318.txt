Caning     caning is a form of punishment that involves beating a person with a cane , usually made of rattan . each blow of the cane is called a `` cut '' or a `` stroke '' . it is most often applied to the hands or buttocks . caning , as a form of corporal punishment , is prohibited under international law as a form of torture . caning was banned in british public schools only a few years ago . it is still practiced in places such as singapore , hong kong , malaysia , and brunei .
