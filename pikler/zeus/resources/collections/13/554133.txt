Yanam district     yanam distict is one of the four districts of the union territory of puducherry . yanam is a small territory of just 20 sq.km . it is in andhra pradesh . but yanam district , mahe district , karaikal district and puducherry district comes under puducherry union territory , as they were french colonies up to 1954. yanam is surrounded by the district of east godavari of andhra pradesh . yanam district consists of 7 revenue villages and a town . yanam is a town as well as the district headquarters . the other villages are agraharam , darialatippa , farampeta , guerempeta , kanakalapeta , kurasampeta and mettakur . the total population of the district is just 55,616 , roughly equal to the population of greenland .
