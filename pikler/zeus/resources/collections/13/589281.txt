Michael Nyqvist     rolf åke mikael nyqvist ( 8 november 1960 – 27 june 2017 ) was a swedish actor . born in stockholm , he studied drama in malmö . he started acting as officer banck in the series one of the swedish movies about martin beck in 1997. nyqvist became known internationally as the main character mikael blomkvist in the `` millenium '' miniseries opposite noomi rapace . he also had a role in `` '' and `` john wick '' . nyqvist died on 27 june 2017 in stockholm from lung cancer at the age of 56 .
