Alia Bhatt     alia bhatt ( born on 15 march 1993 ) is an british film actress who appears in bollywood films . she is regarded as one of the most successful bollywood actress . she started her career through a karan johar 's romantic-drama-comedy student of the year alongside siddarth malhotra and varun dhawan in 2012 for which she gained a filmfare award for best debut female nomination .
