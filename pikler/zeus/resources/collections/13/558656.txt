Palladium(II) nitrate    palladium ( ii ) nitrate palladium ( ii ) nitrate is the inorganic compound with the formula pd ( no3 ) . it is a red-brown solid with a tendency to absorb water . palladium nitrate may be prepared by dissolving palladium oxide hydrate in dilute nitric acid , followed by crystallization . the nitrate crystallizes as yellow-brown deliquescent prisms . the compound also may be got by treating palladium metal with nitric acid .
