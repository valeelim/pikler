Bonaire     bonaire is an island of the caribbean netherlands and the former netherlands antilles . it is a special municipality ( public body ) of the netherlands . it lies ahead of the coast of venezuela , in the neighbourhood of the islands of aruba and curaçao , also parts of the kingdom of the netherlands . bonaire lies at . it has a land area of 288 km² . in 2014 about 18,000 people were living there . kralendijk is its capital city .
