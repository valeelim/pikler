Smuglyanka     smuglyanka ( russian : смугля́нка , meaning `` the dark girl '' ) is a russian song . the lyrics tell of a man who met a pretty woman gathering grapes and tries to seduce her , but finds out that she is a partisan . she then convinces him to join the partisans as well . it was written in 1940 by yakov shvedov ( lyrics ) and anatoliy grigorevich novikov ( music ) . the song was meant to glorify female partisans in the russian civil war . the song is still very popular .
