Erling Brøndum     erling brøndum ( 11 july 1930 – 4 august 2017 ) was a danish journalist and politician . he began at `` bornholms tidende '' as a reporter , and at age 26 , was named chief editor . after fourteen years as the publication 's editor , brøndum joined the staff of the `` horsens folkeblad '' . in 1973 , poul hartling named brøndum minister of defence . he left the post in 1975 , and returned to `` horsens folkeblad '' and the `` aalborg stiftstidende '' . brøndum died in copenhagen , denmark on 4 august 2017 at the age of 87 .
