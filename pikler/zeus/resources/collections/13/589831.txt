David Graham     david graham ( born 11 july 1925 ) is an english character actor and voice artist . graham is known for his voice work for the series `` doctor who '' and `` thunderbirds '' during the 1960s . he is also known for appearing as the villain in the one minute apple inc. advertisement `` 1984 '' . graham was born in london , he trained as an actor in new york city following service in the royal air force as a radar mechanic , and has since worked mainly in british television .
