Shanghainese food     shanghainese food is the traditional food of shanghai , china . it is a kind of chinese food . examples are xiaolongbao , spiced broad beans , and hairy crabs . xiaolongbao is made of pork and flour . it 's a kind of dumpling . it 's fresh and a little sweet . it 's small and white . it 's very delicious . spiced broad beans are made of beans flavored with aniseed and cinnamon . they are sweet . nearly everyone knows them . it has a long history . a person named zhang acheng ( 張阿成 ) made this food sixty years ago . hairy crabs are steamed and opened . the meat is very tasty .
