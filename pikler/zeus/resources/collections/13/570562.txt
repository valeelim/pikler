MVG Class A     the mvg class a is a type of train used on the munich u-bahn . they are the oldest trains on the munich u-bahn . the mvg class a began service in 1971. they are used on every line . there are 194 trains with two cars each . 18 trains have been withdrawn . references . - wolfgang pischek , holger junghardt : die münchner u-bahn - unterirdisch durch die bayerische landeshauptstadt . münchen 2012 ( 3 . aufl . ) , isbn 3-7654-7194-1 ( in german ) p. 43-53 .
