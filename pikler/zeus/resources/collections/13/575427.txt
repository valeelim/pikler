Lois Capps     lois ragnhild grimsrud capps ( born january 10 , 1938 ) was the u.s. representative for , serving in congress from 1998 to 2017. she is a member of the democratic party . capps served on the u.s. house committee on energy and commerce , where she was a member of the energy and air quality subcommittee and the subcommittee on health . she was a member of the new democrat coalition .
