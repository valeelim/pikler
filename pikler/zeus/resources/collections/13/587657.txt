Travis Fryman     david travis fryman ( born march 25 , 1969 ) played major league baseball from 1990 to 2002. he played for the detroit tigers ( 1990–1997 ) and for the cleveland indians from 1998 to 2002 ) . he usually played third base , but he also played shortstop . fryman made 5 all–star teams and won a gold glove award in 2000 .
