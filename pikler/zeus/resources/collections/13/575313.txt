Ian Callaghan     ian robert callaghan ( born 10 april 1942 ) is a retired english footballer . he played between 1959 and 1982 as a midfielder . he is best known as a liverpool player . he holds the record for most times played for liverpool , at 857 appearances . he won 17 honours with liverpool , including two european cups , in 1977 and 1978. he also played four times for the england national football team . callaghan was born in toxteth , liverpool . other websites . - ian callaghan at liverpoolfc.com
