California's Gold    california 's gold california 's gold is a public television travel program that explores the natural , cultural , and historical features of the state of california . the series ran for 18 seasons from 1994 to 2012 and was produced and hosted by huell howser for its entire run . the minimal production allowed locations and people to remain the focal point of the program . the series ceased production after howser 's death in 2013. the series ' theme song varies between several renditions of `` california , here i come , '' but was most often played on the series by local musicians eddie enderle and richard chon . other websites . - huell howser archives at chapman university - california 's gold - video podcast feed - california 's gold - california 's gold episodes available free on itunes
