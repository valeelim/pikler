Rhys Wesser     rhys joseph wesser ( born 31 march 1979 ) is an australian former professional rugby league player . he played in the national rugby league ( nrl ) between 1998 and 2011. he was best known for playing for the penrith panthers , winning the 2003 premiership with them . he later played for the south sydney rabbitohs . he scored 129 total club tries in his career . wesser was born in rockhampton , queensland . he is of indigenous australian ancestry . other websites . - rhys wesser at the rugby league project
