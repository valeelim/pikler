Vrbanja river     the vrbanja ( врбањa ) is a river in western bosnia and herzegovina . along with the ugar , it is the largest right tributary of the vrbas . its drainage basin covers an area of approximately 703.5 km . it has significant hydropower potential . the estimations of the vrbanja 's length vary from 84 km to 95.4 km.. related pages . - šiprage - kotor-varoš - čelinac
