Palmiro Togliatti     palmiro togliatti was an italian politician . he was leader of the italian communist party from 1927 until his death . togliatti began his political life in the italian socialist party . after world war i he created the communist party of italy with others . in 1926 benito mussolini outlawed the party . togliatti became the leader in exile . in 1943 , the name was changed to the italian communist party . in 1944 togliatti returned to italy . he died of a cerebral hemorrhage in ukraine .
