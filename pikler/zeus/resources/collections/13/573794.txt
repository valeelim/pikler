Gustaw Lutkiewicz     gustaw lutkiewicz ( 29 june 1924 – 24 february 2017 ) was a polish actor and singer . he started his career in 1948. he was awarded the knight 's cross of the order of polonia restituta in 1970 , the officer 's cross in 1985 and later the medal for merit to culture – gloria artis in 2012. he was born in kaunas , lithuania . lutkiewicz died on 24 february 2017 in warsaw , at the age of 92. filmography . - 1956 : `` nikodem dyzma '' - 1957 : `` prawdziwy koniec wielkiej wojny '' - 1957 : `` ewa chce spać '' - 1960 : `` walet pikowy '' - 1961 : `` ludzie z pociągu '' - 1964 : `` nieznany '' - 1985 : `` c.k.dezerterzy '' - 1989 : `` konsul '' - 1999 : `` ogniem i mieczem '' - 2000 : `` syzyfowe prace ''
