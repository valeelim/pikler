Ruth Westheimer     ruth westheimer ( born karola ruth siegel ; june 4 , 1928 ) , better known as dr. ruth , is an american sex therapist , media personality and writer . she is best known for her radio show `` sexually speaking '' during the 1980s . she worked for planned parenthood in the 1950s . she has also written many books . westheimer was born in frankfurt , germany . she is jewish and moved to switzerland as a child . she moved to new york city in 1956 .
