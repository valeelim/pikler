Isabelle Sadoyan     isabelle sadoyan ( 12 may 1928 – 10 july 2017 ) was a french-armenian actress . she was the wife of actor jean bouise . in her career , she was directed by jeanne moreau , claude chabrol , claude lelouch , luc besson , jean-luc godard , henri verneuil , bertrand tavernier , robert kechichian and krzysztof kieślowski . sadoyan was known for her roles as alice in the 1982 movie `` the hatter 's ghost '' alongside charles aznavour . her last role was as mrs. warner in the 2016 movie `` irreplaceable '' . sadoyan died on 10 july 2017 in paris of heart failure at the age of 89. other websites . - filmography
