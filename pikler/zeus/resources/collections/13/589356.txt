Jaroslav Kořán     jaroslav kořán ( january 17 , 1940 – june 2 , 2017 ) was a czech translator , writer , screenwriter , and politician . he is a signatory of charter 77 during czechoslovakia 's communist era , kořán translated over seven dozen books , mostly by american writers , from english into czech , including major works by kurt vonnegut , henry miller , roald dahl , ken kesey , charles bukowski , john kennedy toole , and john wyndham . kořán was one of the co-founders of the civic forum ( of ) political movement in november 1989. in february 1990 , kořán was elected mayor of prague , becoming the city 's first non-communist since 1948. he served as mayor until september 1991. jaroslav kořán died in june 2 , 2017 , at the age of 77 .
