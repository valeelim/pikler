Milan Panić     milan panić ( , ; born 20 december 1929 ) is a serbian-american former prime minister of yugoslavia , humanitarian , and multimillionaire businessman based in newport beach and pasadena , california . he served as prime minister of the federal republic of yugoslavia from 1992 to 1993. during and after his time as prime minister , he campaigned for peace and democracy in the balkan region . he ran for president of serbia in 1992 , ultimately coming in second to slobodan milošević .
