Shih Chun-jen     shih chun-jen ( ; 2 december 1923 – 18 june 2017 ) was a taiwanese neurosurgeon . he led the department of health from 1986 to 1990. shih was appointed the head surgeon at tri-service general hospital , a medical institution affiliated with the national defense medical center , in 1975. two years later , he co-founded the taiwan neurological society . he was born in gosei , taikō , taichū prefecture , taiwan , empire of japan . shih died of a heart attack at a hospital in taipei on 18 june 2017 , aged 93 .
