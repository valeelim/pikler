Keith Loneker     keith joseph loneker ( june 21 , 1971june 22 , 2017 ) was an american actor and american football player . he was born in roselle park , new jersey . for much of his short career , his large football-lineman build garnered him roles largely as thugs or football players . he played for the st. louis rams from 1993 through 1995 and later for the atlanta falcons in 1996. his first movie was director steven soderbergh 's `` out of sight '' ( 1998 ) and later the neil labute thriller , `` lakeview terrace '' ( 2008 ) . loneker died on june 22 , 2017 in lawrence , kansas from cancer , aged 46 .
