Marga Minco     marga minco ( pseudonym of sara menco , born 31 march 1920 ) is a dutch journalist and writer . her real surname was menco , but an official accidentally switched the first vowel . she was born in ginneken to an orthodox jewish family . in 1957 minco published her first book , `` '' ( `` the bitter herb '' ) . the title of her later book was `` een leeg huis '' ( `` an empty house '' ) . awards . - 1957 – bureau voor postreclame en adressen de mutator n.v. short-story prize for `` het adres '' - 1958 – vijverberg prize for `` het bittere kruid '' - 1999 – annie romein prize for her entire oeuvre - 2005 – constantijn huygens prize for her entire oeuvre
