Dübendorf     dübendorf is a municipality in the district of uster in the canton of zurich in switzerland . it is a suburb of zürich in switzerland with a population of about 26,000 ( 2014 ) . it is the fourth largest city in the canton , after zürich , winterthur and uster . dübendorf , dübelstein , geeren , gfenn , gockhausen , hermikon and stettbach other websites . - official webpage of the city of dübendorf
