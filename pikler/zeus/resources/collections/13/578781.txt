Aad van Dulst     arie-jan `` aad '' van dulst ( may 9 , 1929 in rotterdam - march 5 , 2001 in hoofddorp ) was a dutch politician of the christian historical union ( chu ) and its successor the christian democratic appeal ( cda ) . he was a councillor in hilversum and the hague , and mayor of tietjerksteradeel and haarlemmermeer . van dulst studied theology at utrecht university . he worked as a journalist for regional newspapers , at nrcv radio , and as a director .
