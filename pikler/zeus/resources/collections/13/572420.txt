Josef Augusta (ice hockey)    josef augusta ( ice hockey ) josef augusta ( 24 november 1946 – 16 february 2017 ) was a czechoslovak ice hockey player and coach . he won a silver medal at the 1976 winter olympics in innsbruck , austria . he later was head coach of czech national hockey team at the 2002 winter olympics in salt lake city , utah , united states . he was the father of patrik augusta . he was born in havlíčkův brod . augusta died from pancreatic cancer on 16 february 2017 in jihlava , at the age of 70 .
