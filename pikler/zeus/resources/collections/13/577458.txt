Lodewijk Asscher     lodewijk frans asscher ( born september 27 , 1974 in amsterdam ) is a dutch politician of the labour party ( pvda ) . he has been political leader since 2016. he has been a government minister and also deputy prime minister in the second rutte cabinet since 2012. previously he was an alderman of amsterdam . as an alderman he was fighting sexual slavery and criminal companies . asscher studied law at the university of amsterdam ( including a phd ) and is a former academic at the same university . he is partly of jewish descent . references . - bio at parlement.com
