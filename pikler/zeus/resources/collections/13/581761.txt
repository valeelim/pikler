Sylvia Trent-Adams     sylvia trent-adams ( born circa 1965 ) is a rear admiral in the usphs . she was the acting surgeon general of the united states from april 21 , 2017 through august 3 , 2017. as surgeon general , she directs the operations of the usphs commissioned corps and communicates scientific information to advance the health of the nation . previously , she was the deputy surgeon general . she has served as the chief nurse officer of the usphs , since november 2013 .
