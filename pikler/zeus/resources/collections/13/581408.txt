José Vicente Rangel     josé vicente rangel vale ( born 10 july 1929 ) is a venezuelan leftist politician . he ran for president three times in the 1970s and 1980s and later supported hugo chávez . rangel served under chávez as minister of foreign affairs from 1999 to 2001 , as minister of defense from 2001 to 2002 , and as vice president of venezuela from 2002 to 2007 .
