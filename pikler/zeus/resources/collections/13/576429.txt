Meretz     meretz , hebrew : מרצ , meaning `` vigour '' , is an israeli political party . its ideology is left-wing social democratic , green , secular , and zionist . political leader is zehava gal-on . meretz was founded in 1992 as a result of a merger of three parties : ratz , mapam and shinui . number of seats in the 20th knesset : 5 ( -1 ) .
