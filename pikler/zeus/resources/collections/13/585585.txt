Bohai Sea     the bohai sea ( ) is a gulf on the northeast coast of china and it is west of the korean peninsula . it is a redundant place name , which mean that the words making up the place name has the same meaning , since `` hăi '' ( 海 ) as in `` bóhǎi '' already means `` sea '' or `` ocean '' . some important port cities on the bohai sea coast include tianjin , qinhuangdao , and dalian .
