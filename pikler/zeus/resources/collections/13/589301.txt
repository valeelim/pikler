Ric Suggitt     ric `` sluggo '' suggitt ( october 30 , 1958 june 27 , 2017 ) was a canadian rugby union footballer and a rugby union coach . suggitt was also the head coach of the usa women 's 7 's olympic team . he was the head coach at university of lethbridge . he was born in edmonton , alberta . suggitt was the coach of the female canada national team and the men 's national seven-a-side team , when he was appointed to the post of coach of the male canada national rugby union team , in 2004. suggitt died on june 27 , 2017 in calgary , alberta from a sudden heart attack , aged 58. other websites . - profile of ric suggit
