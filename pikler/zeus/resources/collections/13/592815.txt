Ovidio Messa     ovidio messa soruco ( 12 december 1952 – 27 july 2017 ) was a bolivian footballer . he was born in yacuíba , bolivia . he was known for playing for the bolivia national football team from 1978 through 1979. messa died on 27 july 2017 of pancreatic cancer in alicante , spain at the age of 64. other websites . - obituary : ovidio messa
