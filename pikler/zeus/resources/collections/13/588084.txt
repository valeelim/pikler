Hypothetical     a `` hypothetical ' entity is an entity which exists only in a hypotheses . ergo , it is theorized as possibly existing , or imagined in a scenario separate from common reality . to be `` hypothetically speaking '' means to be speaking about a scenario which does not exist . however , sometimes `` hypothetically speaking '' can be talking about a scenario which does exist , but the speaker does not wish the listener to be aware that it does . a hypothetical particle or quantum theory is hypothesized by scientists and physicists as existing , but does not necessarily exist . the word hypothetical is derived from the greek term hypothetikos , meaning `` pertaining to a hypotheses . '' hypotheses are hypothetical scenarios imagined by scientists and inventors pertaining to an invention or scientific theory .
