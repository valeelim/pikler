Formula One on BBC Programmes     from 1990 to 1996 , eurosports took over broadcasting all formula one races , bbc broadcasted the european races and other races if paid for the fly away races by the local tv feed . from 1997 to 2008 , itv broadcasted formula one , then bbc regained the rights for f1 on all races until 2011. from 2012 to 2015 , sky sports f1 took over broadcasting all formula one races , bbc broadcasted halve the races due to cost cuts . from 2016 onwards , sky sports f1 still broadcasting all formula one races , bbc stopped f1 races due to cost cuts and their coverage of f1 was replaced by channel 4 .
