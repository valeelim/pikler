John Watkiss     john watkiss ( 28 july 1961 – 21 january 2017 ) was a british artist . he was known for his painting and his work in comics and movie production . his career led him from cover artist for graphic novels to storyboard artist and character designer . he was born in stoke-on-trent , staffordshire . watkiss ' comics illustration work includes dc comics ' `` the sandman '' # 39 ( 1992 ) and # 53 ( 1993 ) , `` sandman mystery theatre '' # 5–8 ( 1993 ) , `` starman '' # 18 ( 1996 ) , and all 13 issues of `` deadman '' . watkiss died of cancer at the age of 55 .
