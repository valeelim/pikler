Yahya Abdul-Mateen II     yahya abdul-mateen ii ( born 1987 ) is an american actor . he is best known for his role cadillac in the television series `` the get down '' . he also starred in `` baywatch '' ( 2017 ) . he will appear in `` the greatest showman '' and dc 's `` aquaman '' as black manta . abdul-mateen was born in new orleans , louisiana . before acting , abdul-mateen was an architect .
