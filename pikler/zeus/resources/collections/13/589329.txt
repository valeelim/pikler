Julian Carroll     julian morton carroll ( born april 16 , 1931 ) is an american politician from the state of kentucky . a democrat , he is a member of the kentucky senate , representing anderson , franklin , woodford , gallatin and owen counties . from 1974 to 1979 , he served as the 54th governor of kentucky , succeeding wendell h. ford , who resigned to accept a seat in the u.s. senate . he was the first kentucky governor from the state 's far-western jackson purchase region , and his running mate thelma stovall was the first woman to be elected lieutenant governor of kentucky . other websites . - website of state senator julian carroll
