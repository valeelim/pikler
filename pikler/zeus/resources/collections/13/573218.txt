Taulupe Faletau     tangaki taulupe `` toby '' faletau ( born 12 november 1990 ) is a welsh rugby union player . he plays as a back row forward . he has played over sixty times for wales since 2011. for club , he played for cross keys rfc and newport rfc before joining the newport gwent dragons . he has played for bath rugby since 2016. faletau was born in tofoa , tonga . his father is tongan international kuli faletau . he grew up in pontypool , wales . other websites . - welsh rugby union profile
