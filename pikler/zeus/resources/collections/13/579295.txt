United States presidential election, 1852    united states presidential election , 1852 the presidential election of 1852 was the 17th presidential election . it was held on tuesday , november 2 , 1852. the united states holds presidential elections every four years . franklin pierce was elected president . millard fillmore was the president elected in 1848. he belonged to the whig party . instead of choosing fillmore as a candidate for 1852 , the whigs chose general winfield scott . william alexander graham was scott 's running mate , the person who would become vice-president if elected . the democratic party selected franklin pierce . pierce was not a well known politician . william r. king was pierce 's running mate .
