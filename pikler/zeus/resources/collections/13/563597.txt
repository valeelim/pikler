Bernardo Bertolucci     bernardo bertolucci ( ; born 16 march 1940 ) is an italian director and screenwriter . his most well known works are `` the conformist '' , `` last tango in paris '' , `` 1900 '' , `` the last emperor '' , `` the sheltering sky '' , `` stealing beauty '' and `` the dreamers '' . since 1979 , he has been married to screenwriter clare peploe .
