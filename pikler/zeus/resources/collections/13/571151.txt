Ray Galton     raymond percy `` ray '' galton , obe ( born 17 july 1930 ) is an english scriptwriter . galton is best known for the galton and simpson comedy writing partnership with alan simpson . he , along with simpson , wrote the bbc sitcom `` steptoe and son '' ( 1962–1974 ) , `` hancock 's half hour '' ( 1954–1961 ) and `` comedy playhouse '' ( 1961–1975 ) .
