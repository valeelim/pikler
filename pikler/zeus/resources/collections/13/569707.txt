Psyonix     psyonix inc. is an american video game development studio . it is based in san diego , california . they were founded in 2000. they were officially incorporated on 30 april 2001. psyonix are best known for publishing and developing `` rocket league '' since 2015 . `` rocket league '' is a sequel to their other game , `` supersonic acrobatic rocket-powered battle-cars '' ( 2008 ) . psyonix also worked on games such as `` gears of war '' , `` bulletstorm '' and `` homefront '' .
