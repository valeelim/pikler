Alexander Kadakin     alexander mikhailovich kadakin ( ; 22 july 1949 – 26 january 2017 ) was a russian diplomat . he served as as the russian ambassador to india twice : from 1999 and 2004 , and again from 2009 until his death in 2017. he was also the russian ambassador to nepal ( 1993–1997 ) and sweden ( 2005–2009 ) . he was born in chișinău , ussr ( now moldova ) to russian parents . kadakin died from heart failure on 26 january 2017 in new delhi . he was 67 .
