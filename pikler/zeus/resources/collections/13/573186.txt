Joseph Blatchford     joseph blatchford ( born june 7 , 1934 ) was the third director of the united states peace corps . blatchford was appointed peace corps director in 1969 by president richard nixon . he retired 1971. blatchford was born in milwaukee , wisconsin . he was raised in beverly hills , california . other websites . - an interview with joseph blatchford `` peace corps volunteer '' magazine . may , 1970 pages 5-13 - peace corps news clips about joseph blatchford
