Ivan Grubišić     ivan grubišić ( 20 june 1936 – 19 march 2017 ) was a croatian roman catholic priest , sociologist , and independent politician . he served as a representative in the croatian parliament representing the x electoral district from 22 december 2011 through 28 december 2015. grubišić was born in dicmo , kingdom of yugoslavia . he studied at university of zagreb and at the university of zadar . grubišić died in split , croatia on 19 march 2017 , aged 80 .
