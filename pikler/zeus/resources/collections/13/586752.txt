José Greci     josé greci ( 10 january 1941 – 1 june 2017 ) was an italian movie , television and stage actress . she made her movie debut in 1959 , playing the virgin mary in william wyler 's `` ben-hur '' . greci studied at silvio d'amico national academy of dramatic art . greci died in rome , italy on 1 june 2017 , aged 76 .
