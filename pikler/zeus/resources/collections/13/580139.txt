Schiermonnikoog     schiermonnikoog is an island and municipality of the province of friesland , the netherlands . in 2016 the municipality had about 900 people . the municipality covers an area of 199 km² , the island an area of 44 km² . schiermonnikoog makes part of the west frisian islands ( dutch : `` waddeneilanden '' ) . it lies west of the small island of rottumerplaat and east of the island of ameland .
