Ashish Seth     ashish seth was on born 7 january 1979 in lucknow . he is an astrological data collector , who collects birth information of notable people . he collects the information by telephonic conversation , searching notable blogs , newspapers , and emailing notable people . seth , studied in st. francis ' college , lucknow and moved to mumbai in 2002 , to study masters of technology in textile chemistry from institute of chemical technology mumbai . he started contacting bollywood celebrites on phone , from 2008 to get their birth details , to learn astrology and study their birth charts . in his collection , he also uses the data available on internet in form of books , blogs , and newspapers . some of the prominent people who shared their birth details on phone to him are actors and musicians
