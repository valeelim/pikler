Brinicle     a brinicle is a column of ice that forms in seawater . the name is a combination of the words `` brine '' , which means saltwater , and `` icicle '' . a brinicle starts forming from the top of the water down . a brinicle freezes everything in its way as it forms . it can reach the bottom of the ocean , where it can also form ice on the seabed . it is dangerous to sea stars and sea urchins living on the bottom of the ocean . they move too slowly to get out of the way . they are very rare and only occur in places where it is very cold , such as antarctica . other websites . - brinicle- sinking brine ; youtube
