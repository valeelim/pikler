On the Beach (novel)    on the beach ( novel ) on the beach is a novel by nevil shute , a british author who emigrated to australia . it was published in 1957. it tells about the world after atomic war . last people live in australia and wait for coming of the radioactive cloud . when they start being sick of the radiation , they commit suicide . a movie based on the book was made in 1959. gregory peck and ava gardner starred in it . other sites . - james schellenberg , review of nevil shute 's on the beach .
