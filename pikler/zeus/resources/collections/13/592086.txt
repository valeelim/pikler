Michel Aurillac     michel aurillac ( 11 july 1928 – 6 july 2017 ) was a french lawyer , politician and author . he served as a member of the national assembly for indre from 1978 to 1981 , and in 1986. he was the minister of cooperation from 1986 to 1988. he won the 1987 prix narcisse michaut from the académie française . aurillac died on 6 july 2017 in indre , france at the age of 88 .
