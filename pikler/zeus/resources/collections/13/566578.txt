Vattaparai Falls     vattaparai falls is a waterfall in the district of kanyakumari in tamil nadu . it is high . it is in the forest region of keeriparai , which is also known as keerparai reserve forest near bhoodhapandi village on the pazhayar river . there is a kali temple near the waterfalls . but this has been in a underdeveloped area , only a single tea shop can be seen near.this stream is pollution free and people were allowed to bathe here .
