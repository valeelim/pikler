Khalsa     khalsa is the military community of sikhism . as a true follower of a sikhism they must follow the 5 ks . these are ; 1. kesh ( hair ) - they must have uncut hair . 2. kanga ( comb ) - they must wear a comb in their hair . 3. kara ( steel bangle ) - they must wear one on their right wrist . 4. kachera ( shorts ) -they must wear these , fastened with string as a belt . 5. kirpan ( sword ) - every one should keep a sword with them at all times .
