Sergei Gimayev     sergei nailevich gimayev ( ; 1 january 1955 – 18 march 2017 ) was a russian professional ice hockey player . he was born in moscow . he played ten seasons ( 1976–86 ) in the soviet championship league with hc cska moscow and leningrad ska . after the end of his career as a player , gimayev worked as a tv sports presenter . gimayev died in tula , russia on 18 march 2017 from a heart attack , caused by thrombosis , aged 62 .
