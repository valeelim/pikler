Oleg Vidov     oleg borisovich vidov ( ; june 11 , 1943 – may 15 , 2017 ) was a soviet-american actor , movie director and producer . he appeared in 50 movies from 1961 until his retirement in 2014. he was born in filimonki , russian sfsr , soviet union . he was known for his roles in `` red heat '' , `` wild orchid '' , and in `` the immortals '' . vidov died on may 15 , 2017 from complications of pancreatic cancer at his home in westlake village , california at the age of 73. other websites . - `` олег видов на сайте кто есть кто ''
