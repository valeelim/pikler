Aryeh Deri     aryeh makhlouf deri , hebrew : אריה מכלוף דרעי , ( born february 17 , 1959 in meknès ) is an israeli politician . he is political leader of shas . he helped start this party . deri was a member of knesset from 1992 to 1999. he returned in parliament in 2013. in the fourth netanyahu government he is minister of the development of galilee and the negev , and also interior minister . he has had several government positions before this . in 2000 he was sent to prison because of financial crimes . references . - bio at knesset website
