Marisa Letícia Lula da Silva     marisa letícia lula da silva ( née rocco casa ; 7 april 1950 – 3 february 2017 ) was the second wife of president of brazil luiz inácio lula da silva . she served as the first lady of brazil from 2003 to 2010. she was born in são bernardo do campo , são paulo . she had four children , three of which were fathered by lula . letícia died from a stroke on 3 february 2017 in são paulo at the age of 66 .
