Brendan McGahon     brendan mcgahon ( 22 november 1936 – 8 february 2017 ) was an irish politician . he was a fine gael politician . he was a teachta dála ( td ) for the louth constituency . he was called a 'colourful ' figure and with a reputation as a social conservative . mcgahon was first elected to dáil éireann at the november 1982 general election and retained his seat until retiring at the 2002 general election . he was known for being against gay rights .
