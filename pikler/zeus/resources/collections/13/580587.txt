Work at a Pizza Place     work at a pizza place is an american animated television series produced by warner bros. animation . it will be aired on cartoon network in late 2005 and finished in early 2008. the work at a pizza place is based on the novel of the kitchen of a late mexican author alejandro martinez . in indonesian 's spacetoon , it will aired together with the smurfs , but in 2017 , it finally retired . the series is on toon disney in france , japan and finland .
