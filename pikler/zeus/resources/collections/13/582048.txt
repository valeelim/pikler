Night of terror     the night of terror refers to november 14 , 1917 when several women were arrested for protesting in front of the white house for women 's rights . this event is most known for the brutal torture of women in the occoquan workhouse in virginia . the 300+ activists involved were fighting against president woodrow wilson for the right to vote . 33 women were thrown in jail and these women were the ones who experienced the night of terror . lucy burns , one of the founders of the nwp was chained to her cell with her arms in the air . the other co founder alice paul was held down on a chair and had a long tube shoved down her throat , they forced fed her mushed up food until she threw up . many of the other women died of lack of air , the beatings and of heart attacks and strokes . they got their rights on august 26 , 1920 .
