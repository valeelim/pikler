Tamara Miansarova     tamara grigoryevna miansarova ( née remnyova , ; 5 march 1931 – 12 july 2017 ) was a soviet and russian lyric soprano.pop singer and professor of russian academy of theatre arts , she best known for her hit `` may there always be sunshine '' . she was born kirovohrad , soviet union . miansarova died in moscow of a pulmonary embolism on 12 july 2017 at the age of 86 .
