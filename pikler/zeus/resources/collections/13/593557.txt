Hinrich Lehmann-Grube     hinrich lehmann-grube ( 21 december 1932 – 6 august 2017 ) was a german politician . he served as mayor of leipzig from 1990 through 1998. he was a member of the social democratic party of germany . he was born in königsberg ( present day kaliningrad , russia ) . lehmann-grube died in leipzig of cancer on 6 august 2017 at the age of 84. other websites . - hinrich lehmann-grube im leipzig-lexikon - dr. hinrich lehmann-grube , sachsen ( leipzig ) , `` zeitzeugenbuero.de '' - ralf geißler : ein neubeginn in leipzig : hinrich lehmann-grube : vom brd- zum ddr-politiker , sendemanuskript , deutschlandradio kultur , 06. juni 2011
