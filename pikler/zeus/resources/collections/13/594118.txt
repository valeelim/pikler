Dementor     a dementor is a man-size creature in the harry potter books . they are black and hooded.they only lift up their hood for performing `` the kiss '' in which they suck out the soul . there is one way to make them go away.it 's the patronus charm.it 's very advanced magic . you perform it by saying `` expecto patronum ! `` and remembering a very happy memory , a very powerful memory . harry potter first saw them in the third book `` the prisoner of azkaban '' .
