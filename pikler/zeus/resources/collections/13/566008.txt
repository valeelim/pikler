Bogdan Tuszyński     bogdan romuald tuszyński ( 4 july 1932 – 1 january 2017 ) was a polish sports journalist , reporter and historian . he was best known for his sports report `` studio s-13 '' of the polskie radio , beginning in 1970. he started his job as an editor of `` przegląd sportowy '' ( 1951–1952 ) . he later moved to polskie radio in 1953 , leaving them in 1981. he also reported on the peace race every year . he published about thirty books between 1975 and 2009. tuszyński was born in łódź . his daughter , agata tuszyńska ( born 1957 ) , also became a journalist . tuszyński died after a long illness on 1 january 2017 in warsaw . he was 84 .
