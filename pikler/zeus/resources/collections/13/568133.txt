Donald Trump Jr.     donald john `` don '' trump jr. ( born december 31 , 1977 ) is an american businessman and former reality tv personality . he is the oldest child of real estate developer and president of the united states , donald trump , and his first wife , czech model ivana trump . he works alongside his brother eric as a trustee of the trump organization . a longtime company executive vp , in his trustee role he and his brother will run the company during his father 's presidency .
