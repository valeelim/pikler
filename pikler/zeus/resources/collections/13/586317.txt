Tiruppur district     tiruppur district is a district of the indian state of tamil nadu , formed in february 2009. the district is well-developed and industrialized.the city of tiruppur is the administrative headquarters for the district . as of 2011 , the district had a population of 2,479,052 with a sex-ratio of 989 females for every 1,000 males . the city of tiruppur , at the heart of the district , is a textile center . the tiruppur banian industry , the cotton market , and the kangeyam bull provide a vibrant economy .
