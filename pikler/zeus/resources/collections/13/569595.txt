Michael Tönnies     michael tönnies ( 19 december 1959 – 26 january 2017 ) was a german footballer . he was born in essen . he played as a striker . he played from 1978 to 1994. tönnies was best known for playing for msv duisburg , playing in 179 league games and scoring over a hundred goals . he also played for schalke 04 , spvgg bayreuth , 1. fc bocholt , rot-weiss essen and wuppertaler sv . he held a bundesliga record , scoring the fastest hat-trick ( three goals ) . he held the record from 1991 until 2015 , when robert lewandowski beat the record . tönnies died on 26 january 2017 at the age of 57 .
