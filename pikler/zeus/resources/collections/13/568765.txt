Stanley Park     stanley park is a park in vancouver , british columbia , canada . the park opened on september 27 , 1888. it was named after frederick stanley , 16th earl of derby , the governor general of canada at the time . it is the third largest urban park in north america . the park covers . it attracts about 8 million visitors every year . stanley park is larger than new york city 's central park . in stanley park there is a large aquarium , a protected forest , the lost lagoon , totems and other attractions . other websites . - stanley park , vancouver tour ; youtube - stanley park ecology society
