Bang Bang (Jessie J, Ariana Grande and Nicki Minaj song)    bang bang ( jessie j , ariana grande and nicki minaj song ) `` bang bang '' is a pop-soul ballad from jessie j , ariana grande and nicki minaj . the song is about female empowerment , sex and feminism . the song was released on july 29 , 2014 as the lead single from jessie j 's studio album `` sweet talker '' ( 2014 ) . the song is also off grande 's album `` my everything '' ( 2014 ) . `` bang bang '' began at # 1 in the united kingdom . it hit the top ten in several countries , like australia , canada and the united states . it won favorite song of the year at the 2015 kids ' choice awards on nickelodeon .
