Popular Republican Union (2007)    popular republican union ( 2007 ) the popular republican union ( french : `` union populaire républicaine '' , upr ) is a french political party . it was formed in 2007 by françois asselineau . the party is nationalistic , and is very critical of the european union . they are against federalism and the americanisation of france . asselineau qualified to run in the 2017 french presidential election on 23 april 2017. he received almost 1 % of the national vote , with 329,951 votes . to date , the party have not won a seat in the senate or national assembly . other websites . - official website
