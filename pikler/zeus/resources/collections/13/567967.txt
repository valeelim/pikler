Sergei Makarov     sergei mikhailovich makarov ( ; born 19 june 1958 in chelyabinsk , soviet union ) is a russian former ice hockey right wing . he was a two-time olympic gold medalist . he was voted one of six players to the international ice hockey federation 's ( iihf ) centennial all-star team . this was in a poll conducted by a group of 56 experts from 16 countries .
