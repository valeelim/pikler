Curicó Province     curicó province ( ) is one of four provinces of the central chilean region of maule ( vii ) . its capital and largest city is curicó . the curicó province has an area of , the third largest province in the maule region . the provincial capital , curicó , is about south of santiago . the province is bordered to the northwest by the cardenal caro province ( o'higgins region ) , to the north by the colchagua province ( o'higgins region ) , to the east by argentina , to the south by the talca provinces and to the west by the pacific ocean .
