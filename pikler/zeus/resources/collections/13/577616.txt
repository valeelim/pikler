August Englas     august englas ( ; january 15 , 1925 – march 21 , 2017 ) was an estonian wrestler . he competed for the soviet union . englas was born in otepää . in the 1952 summer olympics , after defeating adil atan , shirang vithoba jadav and max leichter , and losing against henry wittenberg and viking palm , he took fourth place . he won the gold medal at the 1953 and 1954 world championship . englas died on march 21 , 2017 in tallinn at the age of 92 .
