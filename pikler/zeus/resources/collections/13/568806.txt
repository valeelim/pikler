Andy Marte     andy manuel marte ( october 21 , 1983 – january 22 , 2017 ) was a dominican professional baseball third baseman . he played in major league baseball ( mlb ) for the atlanta braves , cleveland indians and arizona diamondbacks . he also played in the kbo league for the kt wiz . he was born in villa tapia , dominican republic . marte was killed in a car crash near san francisco de macorís , dominican republic on january 22 , 2017 , aged 33. other websites . - career statistics and player information from korea baseball organization
