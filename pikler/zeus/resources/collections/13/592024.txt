Venkaiah Naidu     muppavarapu venkaiah naidu , ( born 1 july 1949 ) is an indian politician . he is the 13th and current vice president of india since 11 august 2017. on 5 august 2017 , naidu was elected vice president of india with 67.89 % of the votes . naidu served as the minister of housing and urban poverty alleviation , urban development and information and broadcasting in the narendra modi cabinet . a well known leader of the bharatiya janata party , he has also served as its national president from 2002 to 2004. earlier , he was the union cabinet minister for rural development in the atal bihari vajpayee government .
