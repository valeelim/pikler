Abdirahman Jama Barre     abdirahman jama barre ( somali : cabdiraxmaan jaamac barre , arabic : عبد الرحمن جامع بري‎‎ ) ( 1937 – 15 august 2017 ) was a somali politician . he twice served as the minister of foreign affairs of the somali democratic republic , and later as the minister of finance . he was also the 1st deputy prime minister of somalia . he served all three positions at the same time from 1987 through 1991. barre was born in luuq , italian somaliland . he studied at the university of padua . barra died in san diego , california on 15 august 2017 at the age of 79 .
