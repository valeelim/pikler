Seen (Winterthur)    seen ( winterthur ) seen is a district in the city of winterthur in switzerland . it is district number 3. the district has these quarters : waser , büelwiesen , waldegg , ganzenbühl , sonnenberg , ricketwil , oberseen , gotzenwil , eidberg , iberg and sennhof . until 1922 it was a municipality on its own . then it became part of winterthur .
