Henri Emmanuelli     henri emmanuelli ( 31 may 1945 – 21 march 2017 ) was a french socialist politician . emmanuelli was a member of the french socialist party . he has been deputy for landes from 1978 to 1981 , from 1986 to 1997 and from 2000 until his death in 2017. he served as president of the french national assembly from 22 january 1992 through 1 april 1993. he later served as first secretary of the french socialist party from 19 june 1994 through 14 october 1995. emmanuelli was a strong supporter of french president françois mitterrand during his political career despite being a socialist . emmanuelli was born in eaux-bonnes , pyrénées-atlantiques . he studied at sciences po in paris . he joined in 1969 the compagnie financière edmond de rothschild . emmanuelli died on 21 march 2017 at a medical center in bayonne , pyrénées-atlantiques from complications of acute bronchitis at the age of 71. other websites . - official website
