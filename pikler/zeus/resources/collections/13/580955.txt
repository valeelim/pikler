David Brumbaugh     david brumbaugh ( december 2 , 1960 – april 15 , 2017 ) was an american businessman and republican politician from oklahoma . brumbaugh was a representative in the oklahoma house of representatives for district 76 , located in broken arrow , oklahoma from 2011 until his death in 2017. he was chairman for the oklahoma republican house caucus which serves as the majority party in the oklahoma house of representatives . brumbaugh died on april 15 , 2017 in tulsa , oklahoma , aged 56. other websites . - oklahoma house of representatives members - representative david brumbaugh , district 76 - oklahoma state election board summary results , primary election — july 27 , 2010 - oklahoma state election board summary results , general election — nov 6 , 2012 - david brumbaugh for house - david brumbaugh - income tax targeted by state lawmakers
