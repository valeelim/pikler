Jhulelal (Sufism)    jhulelal ( sufism ) jhulelal ( sindhi/urdu : جهوللال ) , refers to lal shahbaz qalandar , a prominent sunni muslim of the suhrawardy sufi order . the term has been popularized by the song dama dam mast qalandar , in which lal shabaz qalandar has been referred to as `` jhulelal qalandar '' . the term jhulelal means `` red bridegroom '' .
