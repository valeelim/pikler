Jelle Zijlstra     jelle zijlstra ( augustus 27 , 1918 in oosterbierum - december 23 , 2001 in wassenaar ) was a dutch economist and a politician of the anti-revolutionary party ( arp ) . zijlstra studied economics at the erasmus university rotterdam and was a economics professor at the free university amsterdam . he was prime minister from 1966 to 1967. he was also a government minister , a member-parliamentary leader of the second chamber and a member of the first chamber . after his political career he was president of de nederlandsche bank , the dutch central bank . zijlstra was a reformed christian . he died in 2001 , aged 83. references . - bio at parlement.com
