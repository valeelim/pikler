Deaths in March 2017     the following is a list of notable deaths in march 2017. for notable deaths before the current month , please see `` previous months '' . names under each date are reported in alphabetical order by last name or pseudonym . deaths of non-humans are reported here also if notable . - each listing of a death must have a source . if no reference is included , the death notice will be removed . the following are the requirements of adding a name to the list in its order : name , age , nationality , what the person is known for , cause of death ( if known ) and most importantly a source . march . 1 . - tania dalton , 45 , new zealand netball player ( national team ) , complications from an aneurysm . - paula fox , 93 , american writer ( `` the slave dancer '' ) , congestive heart failure . - pierre guénin , 90 , french journalist ( `` cinémonde '' ) , magazine publisher ( `` eden '' , `` olympe '' , `` jean-paul '' ) and gay rights activist , complications from pneumonia . - john hampshire , 76 , english cricketer ( yorkshire , national team ) .
