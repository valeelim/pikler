Kelly Knight Craft     in 2007 , she was appointed by president george w. bush as the u.s. delegate to the united nations , where her focus included u.s. engagement in africa . knight-craft was a delegate to the 2016 republican national convention from kentucky . she is a member of the university of kentucky 's board of directors . she heads kelly g. knight llc , a business advisory firm based in lexington , kentucky . in june 2017 , craft was nominated to serve as united states ambassador to canada by president donald trump .
