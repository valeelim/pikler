Herb Kohl     herbert h. `` herb '' kohl ( born february 7 , 1935 ) is an american businessman and politician . he is a former united states senator from wisconsin serving from january 3 , 1989 through january 3 , 2013. he is a member of the democratic party . kohl is also a philanthropist and the former owner of the milwaukee bucks of the national basketball association . he did not seek reelection in 2012 and his term ended on january 3 , 2013. he was succeeded by fellow democrat tammy baldwin . other websites . - herb kohl educational foundation
