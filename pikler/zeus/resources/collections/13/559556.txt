2016–17 NHL season     the 2016–17 nhl season is the 100th season of operation ( 99th season of play ) of the national hockey league . thirty teams are competing . there are 82 games in the regular season from october 12 , 2016 to april 9 , 2017. the 2017 stanley cup playoffs will begin on april 12 and last until mid-june . other websites . - 2016–17 nhl season schedule
