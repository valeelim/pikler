Beisfjord massacre     the beisfjord massacre ( ) was a massacre on 18 july 1942 in beisfjord , norway of 288 political prisoners who were killed at the concentration camp in beisfjord : [ `` beisfjord camp number 1 '' ] `` lager i beisfjord '' the massacre was ordered a few days earlier by the `` reichskommissar '' for norway josef terboven .
