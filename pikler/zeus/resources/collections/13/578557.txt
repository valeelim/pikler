Janine Sutto     janine sutto ( 20 april 1921 – 28 march 2017 ) was a french-born canadian actress . she was born in paris , but raised in montreal . in 1943 , she was a founding member of the théâtre de l'equipe troupe and continued acting on stage through the early 1950s with théâtre du nouveau monde . sutto was known for her roles in `` kamouraska '' , `` congorama '' , and in `` route 132 '' . sutto died in montreal on 28 march 2017. other websites . - janine sutto at the canadian encyclopedia
