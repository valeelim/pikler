Fab Melo     fabricio `` fab '' paulino de melo ( 20 june 1990 – 11 february 2017 ) was a brazilian professional basketball player . he played as a center . in the united states , he played for the boston celtics of the national basketball association ( nba ) and syracuse orange . in brazil , he played for lsb and brasília of the novo basquete brasil ( nbb ) . melo was born in juiz de fora , minas gerais . he moved to the u.s. and graduated from high school in 2010. melo died from an apparent heart attack on 11 february 2017 in juiz de fora . he was 26 .
