Yigal Amir     yigal amir , hebrew : יגאל עמיר‎‎ , ( born may 23 , 1970 in herzliya ) is an israeli murderer . amir is undergoing a life sentence because of murdering prime minister yitzhak rabin on november 4 , 1995. his motivation was political . he was furious about the oslo accords and murdered rabin because of his peace efforts with the palestinians . amir is an orthodox jew and has a jewish-yemenite background . during the murder he studied law and computer science at bar-ilan university and also jewish law . references . - haaretz
