Máel Muire ni Cináeda     máel muire ni cináeda was the fourth child of kenneth macalpin and an unknown wife . she was born in 842. she married áed findliath , high king of ireland . they had 4 children . one of their children later became high king of ireland . máel also married another high king of ireland but had no children with him . she died in either 912 or according to the annals of ulster died in 913. there are no known depictions of her .
