Rod J. Rosenstein     rod j. rosenstein ( born january 13 , 1965 ) is an american lawyer and politician . he is the 37th and current united states deputy attorney general since april 26 , 2017. he was the united states attorney for the united states district court for the district of maryland and a former nominee to the united states court of appeals for the fourth circuit . then president-elect donald trump nominated rosenstein to serve as deputy attorney general for the united states department of justice on january 13 , 2017. he was confirmed by the senate on april 25 , 2017 by a vote of 94-6 .
