Barend Biesheuvel     barend willem biesheuvel ( april 5 , 1920 in haarlemmerliede - april 29 , 2001 in haarlem ) was a dutch politician of the anti-revolutionary party ( arp ) . biesheuvel was prime minister from 1971 to 1973. he was also a deputy prime minister , a government minister , a member-parliamentary leader of the second chamber and a member of the european parliament . he studied law at the free university amsterdam . biesheuvel was a reformed christian . he died in 2001 , aged 81. references . - bio at parlement.com
