Alexander ALX500     the alexander alx500 ( later known as the transbus alx500 ) is a 3-axle double-decker low-floor bus body . it was built by alexander and then transbus international from 1997 to 2002. the body could be built on the dennis trident 3 chassis and volvo super olympian chassis . the body was mainly sold in hong kong but some have been imported to the united kingdom .
