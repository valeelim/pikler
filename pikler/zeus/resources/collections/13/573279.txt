Maccabi Ahi Nazareth F.C.    maccabi ahi nazareth f.c . maccabi ahi nazareth football club , hebrew : מכבי אחי נצרת - mo'adon kaduregel maccabi aḥi natzrat , arabic : نادي كرة القدم اخاء الناصرة - nade korat alqadam maccabi ekhaa al-nasera ) is an israeli football club in nazareth . the club was established in 1968 and plays in the liga leumit .
