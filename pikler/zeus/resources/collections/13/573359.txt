Ricardo Domínguez     ricardo domínguez ( 20 july 1985 – 22 february 2017 ) , nicknamed pelón , was a mexican professional boxer . he fought in the light welterweight division . he was best known for having won the 2009 campeón azteca tournament at lightweight . after 50 bouts , he won 37 of them . he was born in culiacán , sinaloa . dominguez died from colon cancer on 22 february 2017 , at the age of 31 .
