World Tamil Badminton Federation     the world tamil badminton federation ( wtbf ) ( tamil : அகில உலக தமிழர் பூப்பந்தாட்ட பேரவை ) is the international governing body of badminton for the world tamils . it conducts the annual international competition . the first ever tournament was held on 2013 in switzerland . related pages . - tamil eelam national football team
