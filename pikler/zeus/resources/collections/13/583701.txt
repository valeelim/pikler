Daniel Gluckstein     daniel gluckstein ( born 3 march 1953 in paris ) is a french politician . he was the lead candidate for the workers ' party in the 1994 european parliament election ; he later received 0.43 % of the vote . he ran for president in the 2002 french presidential election . he received 0.47 % of the vote , with 132,686 votes . in june 2008 , he created the independent workers ' party ( `` parti ouvrier indépendant '' ) together with gérard schivardi .
