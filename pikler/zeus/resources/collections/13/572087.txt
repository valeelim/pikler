The Proclaimers     the proclaimers are a scottish folk rock band . they are made of twin brothers charlie and craig reid ( born 5 march 1962 ) . they are best known for the songs `` i 'm gon na be ( 500 miles ) '' , `` sunshine on leith '' , `` i 'm on my way '' and `` letter from america '' . they are also known for their style of singing with a scottish accent . the reid brothers were born in leith . they are fans of hibernian and support scottish independence .
