Brendan Rodgers     brendan rodgers ( born 26 january 1973 ) is a northern irish football manager and former player . he is the manager of scottish premiership club celtic . he played for ballymena united and reading before retiring at the age of 20 due to injury . he has also managed watford , reading , swansea city and famously liverpool from 2012 to 2015. rodgers was born in carnlough . his son , anton ( born 1993 ) , is a footballer . other websites . - lfc history profile
