Planes (movie)    planes ( movie ) planes is a 2013 american computer-animated movie . unlike the `` cars '' movies ( which were produced by pixar ) , `` planes '' was produced by disneytoon studios . the movie is a spin-off to the `` cars '' franchise , but it did not receive the kind of success that `` cars '' received . `` planes '' follows a crop-duster plane dusty crophopper who , under skipper 's training , becomes a racer in the wings around the globe rally . with a lot of patience , dusty has to face his racing rival ripslinger as well as many other natural obstacles that come in his way . the movie was released on august 9 , 2013. it had a sequel named `` '' , which was released in 2014. cast . - dane cook as dusty crophopper - stacy keach as skipper riley - cedric the entertainer as leadbottom - roger craig smith as ripslinger - carlos alazraqui as el chupacabra
