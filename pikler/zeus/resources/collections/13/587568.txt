Sinhala     sinhala is spoken by the sinhalese people of sri lanka who happen to make up the largest ethical group in sri lanka . sinhala is the most common with the other two notable languages being tamil and english . sinhala has been around since the early ce . it has estimated to have originated from the 3rd century . sinhala is a mix of vedda , tamil , hindu and also after white invasion portugese , dutch and english loanwords are also used in sinhala .
