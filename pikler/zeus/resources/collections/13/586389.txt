Frédérick Leboyer     frédérick leboyer ( november 1 , 1918 – may 25 , 2017 ) was a french obstetrician and author . he was best known for his 1974 book , `` birth without violence '' , which popularized gentle birthing techniques , in particular , the practice of immersing newborn infants in a small tub of warm water . this method is known as a `` leboyer bath '' . leboyer also advocated low lighting and noise in a warm room to limit the supposed shock of birth , and that a newborn be laid on its mother 's stomach and allowed to bond , instead of being taken away for tests . leboyer died on may 25 , 2017 in paris , at the age of 98 .
