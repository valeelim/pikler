Elkin Ramírez     elkin fernando ramírez zapata ( 26 october 1962 – 29 january 2017 ) was a colombian singer-songwriter . he was best known as the lead singer of the rock band kraken . he was also a member to bands such as kripzy ( 1981–1982 ) and ferrotrack ( 1983–1984 ) . he joined kraken in 1985. he was born in medellín , antioquia . ramírez died from brain cancer on 29 january 2017 in medellín . he was 54 .
