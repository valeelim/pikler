Stephen Kearney     stephen peter kearney ( born 11 june 1972 ) is a new zealand professional rugby league coach and former player . he is the head coach of national rugby league ( nrl ) team , the new zealand warriors . before this , he was the head coach of the new zealand national team and the parramatta eels . he also played for the warriors and melbourne storm in the 1990s and 2000s . kearney was born in paraparaumu , new zealand . he is māori . other websites . - stephen kearney at the rugby league project
