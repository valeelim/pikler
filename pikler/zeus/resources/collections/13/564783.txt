Terza rima     terza rima is a three-line rhyme scheme of italian origin.. the first line rhymes with the third one . the second line is free and can rhyme with a line in the next strophe . terza rima occurs in sonnets . sonnets are often composed of two quatrains and two terza rimas , for example abba abba cdc dcd . this scheme can be found in many italian , spanish and portuguese sonnets . this sonnet by the 16th century portuguese poet francisco de sá de miranda is a good example . < poem > `` o sol é grande , caem co ’ a calma as aves , '' `` do tempo em tal sazão , que sói ser fria ; '' `` esta água que d ’ alto cai acordar-m ’ -ia '' `` do sono não , mas de cuidados graves .
