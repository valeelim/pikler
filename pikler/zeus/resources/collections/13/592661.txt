Nakhon Ratchasima     nakhon ratchasima is a city in thailand 's nakhon ratchasima province . a high-speed rail from bangkok , is being planned ( as of 2017 ) ; the rail line will be built some meters above ground , on 15 kilometers of the track , `` to avoid causing traffic problems in downtown muang district '' , between phukhao lat and ban ko railway stations .
