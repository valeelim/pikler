Starships     `` starships '' is a song by trinidadian-born american recording artist nicki minaj . it is the lead single from her second studio album , `` '' . the song was widely compared to her old single , `` super bass '' , because they are both pop-sounding hip hop songs . a song from the deluxe version of the album , `` va va voom '' , was originally supposed to be the lead single , but `` starships '' was chosen instead . `` starships '' reached the top five in countries like the us , uk , australia , belgium ( flanders ) , france , new zealand , and canada . it finished at number 9 on the canadian and us hot 100 year-end charts . in the video , spaceships are seen flying over an island . minaj dances in the video with fellow islanders .
