North Jutlandic Island     north jutlandic island ( danish : `` nørrejyske ø '' or `` vendsyssel-thy '' ) is the second-largest island of denmark . about 297,000 people were living on this island in 2014. it has an area of 4,685 km² . north jutlandic island lies on the south coast of the skagerrak . it is the most northern part of denmark , and is commonly regarded as part of jutland .
