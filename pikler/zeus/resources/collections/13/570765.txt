Gurudev Kalicharan Brahma Academy     gurudev kalicharan brahma academy ( bodo : गुरुदेब कालिचरण ब्रह्म एकाडेमि ) is a private school in chirang district of assam state in india . it was started in 2009 by amteka primary brahma dharma committee . the primary section of school was founded in 2009 with the high school being established in 2014. notable faculty . - tularam brahma ( mls ) , former headmaster - birjoy brahma ( b.sc ) - swrang narzary ( b.sc ) - sameswar basumatary ( b.sc ) see also . - amteka
