Janusz Głowacki     janusz głowacki ( 13 september 1938 – 19 august 2017 ) was a polish-american playwright , essayist and screenwriter . he was born in poznań , poland . his best known works include `` antigone in new york '' , `` fortinbras gets drunk '' , `` the fourth sister '' , and `` hunting cockroaches '' . he has won many awards such as the guggenheim fellowship , the jurzykowski prize , the tony cox award and the czesław miłosz award . głowacki died on 19 august 2017 in new york city , new york at the age of 78. other websites . - glowacki 's homepage - janusz głowacki at culture.pl
