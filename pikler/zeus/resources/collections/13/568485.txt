Primogeniture     primogeniture refers to the system of inheritance by the firstborn . in feudal england and other legal systems , the legitimate first-born son reserves the primary right to inherit property . he is given preference over all daughters , younger sons and even elder illegitimate sons . the rule is that the eldest will always have the first claim . if there is no son , each of the daughters inherit an equal share of the estate . in the absence of any children , the property is often inherited by the eldest brother . among siblings , sons inherit before daughters and so on . primogeniture is latin for `` first born '' .
