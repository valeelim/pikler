Charles Walker (Fijian politician)    charles walker ( fijian politician ) charles walker ( born 12 june 1928 ) is a retired fijian civil servant and alliance party politician and former diplomat . he was born in the village of sawana in the lomaloma district on vanua balavu island in the lau archipelago . walker was a member of parliament and cabinet minister from 1977 through 1987. served as ambassador to the empire of japan , republic of south korea & the people 's republic of china from 1988 through 1993 .
