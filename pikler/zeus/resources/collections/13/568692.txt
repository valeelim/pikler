Wiggensbach     wiggensbach is a municipality in bavaria . it is about west of kempten , the next big town . next door municipalities are altusried in the north and buchenberg in the south of the village . wiggensbach is the main village . ermengerst and westenried are the two districts of wiggensbach . there are 72 hamlets around the municipality . the village is between above sea level . wiggensbach is twinned with hattstatt in france . the 20th anniversary of the partnership was in 2003 .
