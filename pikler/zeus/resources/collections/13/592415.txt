Martian Manhunter     the martian manhunter ( j'onn j'onzz ) is a fictional superhero appearing in american comic books published by dc comics . he was created by writer joseph samachson and designed by artist joe certa . the character first appeared in the story `` the manhunter from mars '' in `` detective comics '' # 225 ( nov. 1955 ) . martian manhunter is one of the seven original members of the justice league of america . j'onn j'onzz has featured in other dc comics-endorsed products , such as video games like , television series , animated films , and merchandise like action figures and trading cards . the character was ranked # 43 on ign 's greatest comic book hero list .
