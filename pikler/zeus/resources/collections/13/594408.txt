Andrzej Blumenfeld     andrzej stanisław blumenfeld ( 12 august 1951 – 14 august 2017 ) was a polish actor and voice dubber . he was born in zabrze , poland . his career began in 1973. he was known for polish-dubbing from american movies . his best known work are `` the pianist '' , `` delivery man '' , and `` the witcher '' . blumenfeld died on 14 august 2017 in warsaw , poland of cancer at the age of 66 .
