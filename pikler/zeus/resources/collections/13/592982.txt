University of Central Arkansas     the university of central arkansas ( often called uca ) is a public university in conway , arkansas . the university was founded in 1907. it is known for having one of the first honors colleges in the united states . uca has about 12,000 students in undergraduate and graduate programs . it is made up of six colleges : the college of fine arts and communication , the college of natural sciences and mathematics , the college of business , the college of health and behavioral sciences , the college of liberal arts , and the college of education .
