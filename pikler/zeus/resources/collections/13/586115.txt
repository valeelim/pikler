Purple Line (CTA)    purple line ( cta ) the purple line ( or the evanston line ) of the chicago transit authority is a route on the northernmost section of the chicago `` l '' rapid transit system . normally , it extends south from linden avenue in wilmette , passing through evanston to howard street , on chicago 's north side . in 2016 , the average weekday boardings on the purple line was 10,187. other websites . - purple line at cta official site
