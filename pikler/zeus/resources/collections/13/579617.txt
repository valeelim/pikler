Crans-Montana     crans-montana is a municipality in the district of sierre in the canton of valais in switzerland . on 1 january 2017 the former municipalities of chermignon , mollens , montana and randogne merged into the new municipality of crans-montana . crans-montana is a ski resort that was created through the fusion of the two centers of crans and montana and belonged to six municipalities ( chermignon , lens , icogne , mollens , montana and randogne ) , four of which merged to form the new municipality of crans-montana . other websites . - official website
