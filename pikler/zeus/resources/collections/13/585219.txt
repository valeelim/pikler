Victor Găureanu     victor dan găureanu , better known as dan găureanu ( 15 november 1967 – 20 may 2017 ) was a romanian fencer . he was born in nicolae bălcescu , coțușca romania . he competed in the sabre events at the 1992 and 2000 summer olympics . he won the bronze medal at the 1999 world fencing championships after he was defeated by wiradech kothny , who eventually won the gold medal . as part of the romanian team he earned two bronze medals at the world championships . găureanu died on 20 may 2017 in bucharest from a suspected case of cardiac arrest at the age of 49 .
