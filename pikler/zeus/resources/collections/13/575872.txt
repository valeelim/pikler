Bill Leak     bill leak ( born desmond robert leak ; 1956 – 10 march 2017 ) was an australian editorial cartoonist , caricaturist and portraitist . leak was raised in condobolin and beacon hill , sydney . leak studied at the julian ashton art school in the 1970s . leak 's cartoons were first published in 1983 in `` the bulletin '' and after he drew for `` the sydney morning herald '' until 1994 , when he was recruited by news limited to contribute to `` the daily-telegraph-mirror '' and later to `` the australian '' . on 10 march 2017 , leak died in a hospital in gosford , new south wales following a suspected heart attack . he was 61 years old . other websites . - drawing and quartering our pollies – article includes comments by bill leak on his cartooning and the reactions he gets - portrait of bill leak , editorial cartoonist , 1984 , by terry mulligan
