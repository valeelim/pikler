Ya'akov Litzman     ya'akov litzman , hebrew : יעקב ליצמן , ( born september 2 , 1948 in germany ) is an israeli rabbi and politician of yahadut hatorah hameuhedet ( `` united torah judaism '' , shortened `` utj '' ) . he is political leader of agudat yisrael ( `` union of israel '' ) , one of the two branches of utj . he belongs to the hasidism movement . shortly after his birth litzman migrated to the united states , followed by a migration to israel when he was a teenager . in 1999 he was chosen into the knesset . in 2015 he became deputy health minister in the fourth netanyahu government . this position has been changed into health minister the same year . references . - bio at knesset website
