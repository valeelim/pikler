Ángel Parra    ángel parra luis ángel cereceda parra ( 27 june 1943 – 11 march 2017 ) was a chilean singer and songwriter . he was born in valparaíso , chile . he traveled abroad helping to maintain the nueva canción tradition in chilean expatriate communities in europe , north america , and australia . he was known for producing his second album , `` oratorio para el pueblo '' , which was about his love for chile . parra died of lung cancer in paris , france on 11 march 2017 , aged 73 .
