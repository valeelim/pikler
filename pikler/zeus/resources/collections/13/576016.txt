Preto Hendrik     simba antónio castelo ( born november 23 , 1994 , luanda , angola ) , better known by his stage name preto hendrik , is an african musician , of angolan nationality , native of luanda , member of lba music . began his artistic career in the year 2016 , with music named `` encher a cara '' , and `` fácil '' with group of musicians lba musc in 2016 .
