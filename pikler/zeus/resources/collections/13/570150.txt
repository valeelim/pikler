Étienne Tshisekedi    étienne tshisekedi étienne tshisekedi wa mulumba ( 14 december 1932 – 1 february 2017 ) was a congolese politician . he was the leader of the union for democracy and social progress ( udps ) . he served as the prime minister of zaire three times : in 1991 , 1992–1993 and 1997. he was born in kabeya kamwanga , belgian congo . tshisekedi died from a pulmonary embolism on 1 february 2017 in brussels , belgium at the age of 84 .
