Charlottetown Conference     charlottetown conference the charlottetown conference was a conference about the canadian confederation . the representatives of the unions in british north america were present at the meeting . the meeting took place in charlottetown , prince edward island from september 1st to september 9th 1864. the purpose of the conference was to discuss the future of the maritime provinces in what later became canada . the maritime provinces were nova scotia , new brunswick , prince edward island and newfoundland .
