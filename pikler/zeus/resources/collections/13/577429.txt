Jesse Klaver     jesse feras klaver ( born may 1 , 1986 in roosendaal ) is a dutch politician of greenleft ( gl ) . he has been political leader since 2015. he has been a member of the second chamber since 2010 and parliamentary leader since 2015. previously he was chair of cnv youth and a member of the social-economic council . klaver is partly of moroccan and indonesian descent . references . - bio at parlement.com
