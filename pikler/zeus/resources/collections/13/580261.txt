Arturo García Bustos     arturo garcía bustos ( august 8 , 1926 – april 7 , 2017 ) was a mexican painter and print maker . he was known as one of “ los fridos ” which were students who studied under frida kahlo at her home in coyoacán . he was born in mexico city . while he was better known for his graphic work , he also did a number of murals . in 1947 he assisted frida kahlo with a mural at the josefa ortiz de domínguez house in coyoacán . most of his career was spent teaching and giving lectures , seminars and conferences . he taught engraving at the escuela de bellas artes in guatemala city and founded the taller de grabado at the casa del lago in chapultepec . bustos died on april 7 , 2017 in coyoacán , mexico city , aged 90. other websites . - listings for over 125 works produced by arturo garcía bustos during his time at the taller de gráfica popular can be viewed at gráfica mexciana
