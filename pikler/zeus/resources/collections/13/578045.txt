Eduard Bomhoff     eduard jan bomhoff ( born september 30 , 1944 in amsterdam ) is a dutch economist and academic , and also former politician and columnist . bomhoff studied at leiden university and erasmus university rotterdam . from july till october 2002 , he was on behalf of the pim fortuyn list ( lpf ) government minister of health , welfare and sports and also deputy prime minister in the first balkenende cabinet . he is a member of the old catholic church . references . - bio at parlement.com
