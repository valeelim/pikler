Arrondissement of Toul     the arrondissement of toul is an arrondissement of france , in the meurthe-et-moselle department , grand est region . its capital is the city of toul . when the meurthe-et-moselle department was created in 1871 , the `` arrondissement '' of toul was part of that original department . in 1926 , it was eliminated but became again an `` arrondissement '' in 1943. the `` arrondissement '' of toul is most western of the the southern part of the meurthe-et-moselle department . it is bordered to the north by the `` arrondissement '' of briey , to the east by the `` arrondissement '' of nancy , to the south by the vosges department and to the west by the meuse department . it has an small exclave to the southeast of the `` arrondissement '' of briey . the `` arrondissement '' of toul has an area of and a population of 69,418 inhabitants , the `` arrondissement '' with fewest inhabitants in the department . after the reorganisation of the cantons in france , cantons are not subdivisions of the `` arrondissements '' so they could have `` communes '' that belong to different `` arrondissements '' .
