Éva Schubert    éva schubert éva schubert ( 19 january 1931 – 11 july 2017 ) was a hungarian actress . she was known for her roles in `` egy pikoló világos '' , `` felmegyek a miniszterhez '' , and for `` frakk , a macskák réme '' . she won a kossuth prize in 2013 for her lifetime achievement . she was born in budapest . schubert died on 11 july 2017 in budapest at the age of 86 .
