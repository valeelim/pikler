Drumchapel     drumchapel is a part of glasgow , scotland . it became part of glasgow in 1938. housing for about 30,000 people was built in the 1950s to help with overcrowding in glasgow . at first there was only one post office and one telephone box . drumchapel did not get its first shopping centre until the early 1960s . local residents call it `` the drum '' . other websites . - drumchapel life ... changing faces ; youtube
