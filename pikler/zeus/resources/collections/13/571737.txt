Melanne Verveer     melanne verveer is the executive director of the georgetown institute for women , peace and security . she served as the u.s. representative to the un commission on the status of women . she also served as the first u.s. ambassador for global women ’ s issues . she co-wrote the book `` fast forward : how women can achieve power and purpose '' . she has received many awards , including the u.s. secretary of state ’ s award for distinguished service .
