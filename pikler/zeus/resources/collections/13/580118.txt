Doris Johnson     doris j. johnson ( born june 23 , 1923 ) is an american former politician . she was born in the state of washington . johnson served in the washington house of representatives as a democrat from the 16th district , as well as the 8th district . a school counselor , johnson studied at western washington state college and earned a master 's degree in education . she was raised in bellingham , washington . she married harold johnson and had a daughter . she lives in kennewick , washington .
