Howard V. Lee     lieutenant colonel howard vincent lee ( born august 1 , 1933 ) is a retired united states marine corps officer who received the medal of honor for heroism in august 1966 during the vietnam war . he was promoted in july 1972 to lieutenant colonel and retired in 1975 from the marine corps . lee studied at pace college . on october 25 , 1967 , major lee was presented the medal of honor by president lyndon johnson in a ceremony at the white house .
