Bon Appétit (song)    bon appétit ( song ) `` bon appétit '' is a song by american singer katy perry . it is from her fifth studio album , `` witness '' . the song features hip-hop group migos . it entered the `` billboard '' hot 100 at number 76 and has reached number 59 so far on that chart . the video shows perry being prepared by chefs as a main dish and features migos .
