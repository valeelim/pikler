Michael Chertoff     michael chertoff ( born november 28 , 1953 ) is an american attorney . he was the second united states secretary of homeland security under president george w. bush . he was the co-author of the usa patriot act . he served as a judge on the united states court of appeals for the third circuit , as a federal prosecutor , and as assistant u.s. attorney general . he succeeded tom ridge as u.s. secretary of homeland security on february 15 , 2005 .
