Robert Dahlqvist     jens robert dahlqvist ( 16 april 1976 – 3 february 2017 ) , nicknamed `` strängen '' and `` strings '' , was a swedish guitarist and singer . he was best known as a member of the rock bands the hellacopters , thunder express and dundertåget . he was born in uddevalla , västra götaland county . dahlqvist died on 3 february 2017 at the age of 40 .
