Eric Lindros     eric bryan lindros ( born february 28 , 1973 ) is a canadian former professional ice hockey player . he was selected first overall in the 1991 nhl entry draft by the quebec nordiques . he refused to play for the nordiques and was eventually traded to the philadelphia flyers . during his ontario hockey league career , lindros ' team won the memorial cup in 1990. lindros received the red tilson trophy as the most outstanding player in the ohl . he was also was named the chl player of the year . in 2016 he was added to the hockey hall of fame . in 2017 lindros was named one of the '100 greatest nhl players ' in history . played for canada in : - 1990 world junior championships ( gold medal ) - 1991 world junior championships ( gold medal ) - 1991 canada cup ( championship ) - 1992 world junior championships - 1992 winter olympics ( silver medal ) - 1993 world championships - 1996 world cup of hockey - 1998 winter olympics - 2002 winter olympics ( gold medal )
