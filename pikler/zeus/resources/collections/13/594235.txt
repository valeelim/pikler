2017 Ouagadougou attack     18 people were killed and more than 20 were injured when suspected jihadists opened fire on a turkish restaurant and hotel in ouagadougou , burkina faso on 13 august 2017. the attackers took hostages after being cornered by police and were killed in an ensuing shootout . ouagadougou was also the site of a similar attack in 2016. it is suspected by the government that al-qaeda maghreb affiliate was behind the attack .
