Józef Grudzień     józef grudzień ( 1 april 1939 – 17 june 2017 ) was a polish boxer . grudzień was born in piasek wielki , poland . he won two medals at the olympic games : gold in the lightweight division at tokyo 1964 and silver at mexico city 1968. he also twice won medals at the european amateur boxing championships , silver at east berlin 1965 and gold at rome 1967. he was the winner of the aleksander reksza boxing award 1991 .
