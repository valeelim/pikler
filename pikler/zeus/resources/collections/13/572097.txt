Israel Kamakawiwoʻole     israel kaʻanoʻi kamakawiwoʻole ( may 20 , 1959 – june 26 , 1997 ) was an american singer and musician . he was also known as bruddah iz . he was best known for his versions of the songs `` somewhere over the rainbow '' and `` what a wonderful world '' , recorded in 1988 and released in 1993. he also played the ukulele to his songs . he was a native hawaiian and supported independence to become a sovereignty . kamakawiwoʻole was born in honolulu . he was married to marlene . they had a daughter , ceslieanne ( born ) . kamakawiwoʻole had health issues because of his obesity . he died on june 26 , 1997 in honolulu , at the age of 38 .
