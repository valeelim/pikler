Hornbach chain     the hornbach chain ( ) is a string of mountains , about 15 kilometres long . they are part of the allgäu alps in the austrian state of tyrol . the hornbach chain leaves the main ridge of the allgäu alps at the öfnerspitze . it arcs from the north to the east . in the west another branch goes south , which is where the großer krottenkopf ( 2,656 m ) is found . the mountain is the highest in the whole allgäu alps . on the main ridge of the hornbach chain , the highest peaks are the marchspitze ( 2,609 m ) , the bretterspitze ( 2,608 m ) and the urbeleskarspitze ( 2,632 m ) . they are all in the ten most important summits of the allgäu alps . other reading / maps . - dieter seibert : `` alpenvereinsführer allgäuer alpen und ammergauer alpen '' . bergverlag rudolf rother , munich , 2004. isbn 3-7633-1126-2 - alpenvereinskarte 2/1 allgäuer-lechtaler alpen – west ( 2004 edition ) - alpenvereinskarte 2/2 allgäuer-lechtaler alpen – ost ( 2006 edition )
