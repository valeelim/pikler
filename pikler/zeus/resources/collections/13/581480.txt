Council for Secular Humanism     the council for secular humanism was a non-profit organization that promoted secular values . in 1991 , the council for secular humanism combined with the the committee for the scientific investigation of claims of the paranormal to form the center for inquiry . in 2016 , the center for inquiry merged with the richard dawkins foundation for reason & science , keeping one office in washington dc . this made the center for inquiry the largest secularist organization in the united states . other websites . - council for secular humanism official website
