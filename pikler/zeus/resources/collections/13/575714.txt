Per-Bast     per-bast is the place where the center of cult of bast is . bast is a cat-goddess who is the protectress of ra . per-bast is also transcribed as bubastis ; per means house , and the second word is bast , or bastet , so per bast means `` house of bast '' per bast served as the capital of the nome `` am-khent , '' the 18th nome of the lower egypt .
