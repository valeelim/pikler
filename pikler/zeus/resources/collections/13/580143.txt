Texel     texel is an island and municipality of the province of north holland , the netherlands . in 2016 the municipality had about 13,600 people . the municipality covers an area of 463 km² , the island an area of 161 km² . texel makes part of the west frisian islands ( dutch : `` waddeneilanden '' ) . it lies southwest of the island of vlieland and northeast of the small island of noorderhaaks ( part of the municipality of texel ) .
