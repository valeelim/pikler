Alayaron (movie)    alayaron ( movie ) alayaron ( english : `` the dawn '' ) is one of the first bodo feature movies . it was released in 13 march 1986 at ganga talkies , kokrajhar . it was directed by jwngdao bodosa , based upon the short story mwdwi arw gwlwmdwi ( tears and sweat ) by nilkamal brahma . the movie stars amar narzary and rohila brahma . it was also the winner of the national film award in 1986. directed by . - jwngdao bodosa written by . - nilkamal brahma - heramba narzary mwdwi arw gwlwmdwi - nilkamal brahma starring . - amar narzary - rohila brahma - tikendrajit narzary genre . - drama release date . - 13 march 1986 country . - india language . - bodo cast . - amar narzary as gwmbwr - rohila brahma as hangna related pages . - bodo people
