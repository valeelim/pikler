Bengt Gustavsson     bengt olov emanuel `` julle '' gustavsson ( 13 january 1928 – 16 february 2017 ) was a swedish footballer and manager . he played as a defender . he played for ifk norrköping , atalanta and åtvidabergs ff . he also played for the sweden national football team during the 1958 fifa world cup . he was born in ringarum , östergötland county . gustavsson died on 16 february 2017 in norrköping , at the age of 89 .
