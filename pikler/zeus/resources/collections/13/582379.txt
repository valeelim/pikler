Zhanjiang     zhanjiang ( ) , is a prefecture-level city at the southwestern end of guangdong province , people 's republic of china , facing hainan to the south . its population was 6,994,832 inhabitants at the 2010 census . 1,611,868 live in the built-up area made of four urban districts : chikan , xiashan , potou and mazhang . in 2007 , the city is named china 's top ten livable cities by chinese cities brand value report , which was released at 2007 beijing summit of china cities forum .
