The World Federation of Great Towers     the world federation of great towers ( wfgt ) is an international association headquartered in melbourne , australia . it is made up of management bodies of high-rise towers , provided that they are open to the public and that they constitute a tourist attraction . it aims at exchanging information to improve their management , sharing experiences , and organizing their international promotion . joining the association costs $ 2,000 a year . the 25 participating towers would receive 20 million visitors annually . the list of members is far from exhausting those of high-rise towers open to the public . in addition , some members have left the federation . other websites . - the world federation of great towers
