Strongpoint     a strongpoint is a key point in a defensive fighting position which the rest of the positions rely on . its primary requirement is that it should not be easy to defeat by the enemy . in general , high ground is preferred . but any good tactically important terrain can be used as a strongpoint . examples from history include battle of thermopylae , where 300 spartans held off an entire persian army at a narrow pass in the mountains . in the battle of monte cassino in italy in world war ii , the area around the abbey of monte cassino was used by the german army as a strongpoint until it was destroyed by american bombers .
