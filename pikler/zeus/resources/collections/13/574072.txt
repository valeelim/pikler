Williams     john peter rhys williams ( born 2 march 1949 ) is a former welsh rugby union player . he played as a fullback . he played for wales between 1969 and 1981 , making 55 appearances . he also played for the british lions eight times . he started going by his initials because there was another player named john williams . williams was born in bridgend . outside of playing rugby , he was a surgeon . other websites . - j. p. r. williams at lionsrugby.com
