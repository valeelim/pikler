LGBT rights in Tunisia     lesbian , gay , bisexual , and transgender ( lgbt ) persons in tunisia may face legal challenges not experienced by non-lgbt residents . homosexuality is illegal in the republic of tunisia and the law does not include sexual orientation or gender identity in its civil rights laws and there is no legal recognition for same-sex couples . according to the 2013 pew global attitudes project , 93 % of tunisian adults do not believe that homosexuality is acceptable by society . tunisia tends to be socially conservative when it comes to such issues as homosexuality .
