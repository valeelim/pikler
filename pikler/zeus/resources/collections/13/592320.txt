József Szendi     józsef szendi ( 31 october 1921 – 23 july 2017 ) was a hungarian prelate of the roman catholic church . at the time of his death he was the oldest living bishop from hungary . szendi was born in székesfehérvár , hungary . szendi was ordained a priest on 24 december 1944. from 1969 until 1982 , he was spiritual of the seminary of esztergom . szendi was appointed apostolic administrator of the archdiocese of veszprém as well as titular bishop of stephaniacum on 5 april 1982 and was consecrated bishop on 21 april 1982 by laszlo paskai . szendi was appointed archbishop of the archdiocese of veszprém on 3 september 1983 and served until retiring on 14 august 1997. other websites . - catholic-hierarchy
