Rosey (wrestler)    rosey ( wrestler ) matthew `` matt '' anoaʻi ( april 7 , 1970 – april 17 , 2017 ) was a samoan-american professional wrestler . he was best known for his time on the independent circuit under many ring names , and his time in world wrestling entertainment ( wwe ) , where he competed as rosey . he was the real-life brother of former wwe champion , roman reigns . anoaʻi died on april 17 , 2017 of congestive heart failure at a hospital in pensacola , florida at the age of 47 years old .
