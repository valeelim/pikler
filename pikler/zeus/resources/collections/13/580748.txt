Alstom Metropolis C751A     alstom metropolis c751a is the first generation of cbtc rolling stock used on the singapore mrt system . the trains operate on the north east line . the trains began operation on 20 june 2003 . 21 six-carriage trains were built in france , by alstom . the c751as are the first rolling stock in singapore to be operated without a driver . related pages . - alstom metropolis & shanghai electric c751c
