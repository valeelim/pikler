Joachim Meisner     joachim meisner ( 25 december 1933 – 5 july 2017 ) was a german cardinal of the catholic church . he was born in breslau , germany ( modern wrocław , poland ) . meisner was the immediate past archbishop of cologne , serving from 1989 until his resignation was accepted by pope francis in 2014. he previously served as bishop of berlin from 1980 to 1989 , and was created a cardinal in 1983. he was widely considered to be germany 's leading conservative roman catholic figure . meisner died on 5 july 2017 while vacationing in bad füssing in bavaria of cardiac arrest , aged 83 .
