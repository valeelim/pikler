2016 Cascade Mall shooting     on september 23 , 2016 , five people were killed in a mass shooting at the cascade mall in burlington , washington , u.s. the gunman was identified as arcan cetin , a 20-year-old who emigrated from turkey as a child with his family . he was arrested the following day in oak harbor , washington , his hometown . on september 26 , he confessed to committing the shooting . shortly before 7:00 p.m. pdt on the same day , cetin walked into a macy 's store at the cascade mall with a stolen rifle and opened fire , killing four women and one man . the male victim died the next day at a seattle hospital .
