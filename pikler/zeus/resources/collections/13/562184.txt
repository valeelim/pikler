Chuvash language     chuvash ( ; ) is a turkic language . it is spoken in chuvashia , a part of central russia . it is the only language from the oghur branch of the turkic languages that is still spoken today . though many turkic languages can be understood by different speakers , chuvash is quite different . chuvash is written using the cyrillic script . its alphabet has all of the letters of the russian alphabet and adds four letters of its own : ӑ , ӗ , ҫ and ӳ. links . - виталий станьял : на чувашском конь еще не валялся ... - виталий станьял : решение совета аксакалов чнк
