Roger Pingeon     roger pingeon ( ; 28 august 1940 – 19 march 2017 ) was a professional french road bicycle racer . he raced as a professional from 1964 to 1974. in 1967 , pingeon won the tour de france . in 1969 , pingeon won the vuelta a españa and finished second behind eddy merckx in the tour de france . he was born in hauteville-lompnes , ain , france . pingeon died in paris , france from a heart attack on 19 march 2017 , aged 76 .
