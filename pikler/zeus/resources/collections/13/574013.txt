Neil Fingleton     neil fingleton ( 18 december 1980 – 25 february 2017 ) was an english actor and basketball player . he was the tallest british-born man and the tallest man in the european union at 7 ft 7.56 in ( 232.6 cm ) in height and among the 25 tallest men in the world . this title has been confirmed by the `` guinness book of records '' in the summer of 2007 ; fingleton took over from christopher greener who stood at 7 ft 6 ¼ in ( 229.2 cm ) in height . fingleton died on 25 february 2017 from a heart attack in durham , england , aged 36 .
