Saetgang Station     saetgang station is a train station of seoul subway line 9 , located in yeouido , seoul , south korea . history . - september 18 , 2008 : station name decided - july 24 , 2009 : opened with opening of seoul subway line 9 gaehwa - sinnonhyeon station has 2 side platforms , 4 tracks ; two tracks are for passing express train . as local ( all stop ) train stops at platform , express train passes by using the passing track . near saetgang station . - ankara park - daebang station - saetgang - 63 building data of 2009 is calculated from july 24 ( first opened ) ~ december 31 .
