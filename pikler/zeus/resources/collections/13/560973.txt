Conor McGregor     conor anthony mcgregor ( born 14 july 1988 ) is an irish professional mixed martial artist . he is signed to the ultimate fighting championship ( ufc ) . he is the ufc featherweight champion and ufc lightweight champion at the moment . he has fought as a featherweight , lightweight , and welterweight . he started his career in 2008. mcgregor was born in dublin . his parents are tony and margaret mcgregor . he started kickboxing at the age of 12. he has been in a relationship with dee devlin since 2008. their first child is due to be born in may 2017. other websites . - ufc profile
