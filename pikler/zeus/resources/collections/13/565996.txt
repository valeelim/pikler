François Chérèque     françois chérèque ( 1 june 1956 – 2 january 2017 ) was a french trade unionist . he was the leader of the trade union centre french democratic confederation of labour ( cfdt , ) from 2002 to 2012. he was born in nancy , meurthe-et-moselle . chérèque died from leukaemia on 2 january 2017 at the age of 60 .
