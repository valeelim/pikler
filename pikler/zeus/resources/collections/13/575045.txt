Kenneth W. Dam     kenneth w. dam ( born august 10 , 1932 ) served as deputy secretary of the treasury ( the second highest official in the united states department of the treasury ) from 2001 to 2003. he worked in international economic development . dam is a senior fellow of the brookings institution and a professor emeritus and senior lecturer at the university of chicago law school . other websites . - university of chicago law school biography of kenneth dam - brookings institution announcement on kenneth dam 's appointment as senior fellow - speech by kenneth dam on economic growth in latin america
