Grass jelly     grass jelly , or leaf jelly , is a jelly-like dessert eaten in mainland china , hong kong , macau , southeast asia and taiwan . it is made using the `` platostoma palustre '' plant ( a member of the mint family ) and has a mild , slightly bitter taste . it is served chilled , with other toppings such as fruit , or in bubble tea or other drinks . outside of asia , it is sold in asian supermarkets .
