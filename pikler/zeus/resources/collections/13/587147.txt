Rokas Žilinskas    rokas žilinskas rokas žilinskas ( 20 july 1972 – 6 june 2017 ) was a lithuanian journalist and politician . he was elected to the seimas in 2008 and was a member of the lithuanian parliament until his death . he was the first openly gay member of the parliament . žilinskas died of respiratory arrest caused by pneumonia in vilnius , lithuania on 6 june 2017 at the age of 44 .
