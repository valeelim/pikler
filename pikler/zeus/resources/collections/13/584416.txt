Muriel Pavlow     muriel lilian pavlow ( born 27 june 1921 ) is an english actress . her mother was french and her father was russian . pavlow began work as a child actress with john gielgud and the royal shakespeare company in the 1930s . pavlow 's roles include the maltese girl maria in `` malta story '' ( 1953 ) , where she played alongside alec guinness ; joy , the girlfriend of simon sparrow , in `` doctor in the house '' ( 1954 ) ; thelma bader , the wife of the second world war fighter pilot douglas bader ( played by fellow `` doctor in the house '' cast member kenneth more ) in `` reach for the sky '' ( 1956 ) ; and the daughter of an irascible curmudgeon ( played by fellow `` doctor in the house '' cast member , james robertson justice ) in `` murder , she said '' ( 1961 ) . pavlow retired in 2009. pavlow was born in lewisham , east london , england . she was married to the actor derek farr from 1947 until his death in 1986 .
