Zbigniew Wodecki     zbigniew wodecki ( 6 may 1950 – 22 may 2017 ) was a polish singer , musician , composer , actor and tv presenter . he was born in kraków , poland . wodecki was mostly known for songs “ chałupy welcome to ” , “ lubię wracać tam , gdzie byłem ” , “ zacznij od bacha ” or the polish versions of the `` maya the honey bee '' , the gummi bears and the ' soundtracks . he was one of the judges on the polish version of the `` dancing with the stars '' show , ' , during its first twelve seasons . wodecki died at a hospital in warsaw , poland on 22 may 2017 from complications of a stroke and chronic obstructive pulmonary disease at the age of 67 .
