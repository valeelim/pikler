Elisabeth Rehn     märta elisabeth rehn ( née carlberg ; born 6 april 1935 ) is a finnish politician . she is a swedish-speaking finn , and was a member of the swedish people 's party . she was the minister of defence from 1990 to 1995. she was also an undersecretary general of the united nations from 1995 to 1999. rehn was born in helsinki . she was married to ove rehn from 1955 until his death in 2004. they had four children . she has survived cancer twice .
