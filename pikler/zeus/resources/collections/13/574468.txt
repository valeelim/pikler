Peter Mathews (politician)    peter mathews ( politician ) peter mathews ( august 1951 – 28 february 2017 ) was an irish politician . he was a member of fine gael before becoming independent in 2013. he served as teachta dála ( td ) from february 2011 to february 2016 for the dublin south constituency . he was born in dublin . mathews died from oesophageal cancer on 28 february 2017 , at the age of 65 .
