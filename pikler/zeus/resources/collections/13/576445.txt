Kika de la Garza     eligio “ kika ” de la garza , ii ( september 22 , 1927march 13 , 2017 ) was an american politician . he served as the democratic representative for the 15th congressional district of texas from january 3 , 1965 , to january 3 , 1997. de la garza died on march 13 , 2017 in mcallen , texas from kidney failure , aged 89 .
