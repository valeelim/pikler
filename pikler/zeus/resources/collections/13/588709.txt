Sofia Coppola     sofia coppola ( born may 14 , 1971 in new york city ) is an american screenwriter , actress , director and producer . she is the daughter of director , producer and screenwriter francis ford coppola . in 2003 , coppola won the academy award for best director . in 2010 , she became the first american woman to win the golden lion award . in 2017 , coppola directed `` the beguiled '' . this movie stars kirsten dunst and nicole kidman .
