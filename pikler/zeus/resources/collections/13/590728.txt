Maisie Williams     margaret constance `` maisie '' williams ( born 15 april 1997 ) is an english actress . she was born in bristol , england . williams has 2 older brothers and one older sister : james , ted and beth . her mother , hilary pitt ( now frances ) , was a former university course administrator , williams grew up in clutton , somerset . and attended clutton primary school and norton hill school in midsomer norton , before moving to bath dance college to study performing arts . her acting career began in 2011. she has performed in many movies and television programs . these include `` game of thrones '' , `` doctor who '' , `` gold '' , `` the falling '' and `` the secret of crickley hall '' . williams got worldwide recognition for `` game of thrones '' as the character arya stark . filmography . film . - 2012 : `` the olympic ticket scalper '' - 2012 : `` heatstroke '' - 2013 : `` up on the roof '' - 2013 : `` corvidae '' - 2014 : `` gold '' - 2015 : `` the falling '' - 2016 : `` the book of love '' - 2017 : `` iboy '' - 2017 : `` mary shelley '' - 2017 : `` stealing silver '' - 2018 : `` departures ''
