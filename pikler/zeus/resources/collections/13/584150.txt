Ed Kemper     edmund emil kemper iii ( born december 18 , 1948 ) , is an american serial killer , suspected cannibal and necrophile who committed the murder of numerous women in the early 1970s , as well as the murders of his mother and paternal grandparents . he killed up to 10 individuals in total . kemper is widely known for his high intelligence and giant stature , standing 6 foot 9 inches or ( 2.06 m ) tall and having a iq of 145 , features that left his victims with little chance to overcome him .
