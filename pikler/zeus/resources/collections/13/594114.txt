Alive (Jessie J album)    alive ( jessie j album ) alive is the second studio album by english singer-songwriter jessie j. it was released on 23 september 2013 through lava music . musically , `` alive '' is a pop , dance-pop and r & b album that features influences from pop rock , soul and hip hop in its production . for the album 's promotion jessie j had shaved her hair and dyed it blonde . `` alive '' received mainly positive reviews from music critics . `` alive '' debuted and peaked at number three on the uk albums chart , selling 39,270 copies in its first week of release . the album then slipped to number seven selling 14,574 copies the following week . altogether , `` alive '' spent a total of 12 weeks inside the top 40 of the chart , and by november 2013 had been certified gold by the british phonographic industry ( bpi ) for shipments of 100,000 copies . track listing . - notes - all vocals were produced by claude kelly except deluxe edition bonus track `` magnetic '' , where vocals were produced by lorne alistair tennant and stl . - indicates additional production .
