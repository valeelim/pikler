Klaib Al-Fawwaz     klaib saud al-fawwaz ( 15 october 1950 â€“ 8 january 2017 ) was a jordanian diplomat and senator . he served as state minister for cabinet affairs from 2011 through 2012 and secretary general of the eslah political party from 2012 until his death in 2017. al-fawwaz was born in sabha , jordan . he studied at the university of jordan . al-fawwaz served in jordan 's diplomatic missions around the world for over 30 years . he worked at the jordanian embassy in new delhi from 1979 through 1983 , the permanent mission of jordan to the united nations in new york from 1984 through 1988 and the embassies in baghdad from 1991 through 1995 and uzbekistan from 1997 through 1999. al-fawwaz also served as jordan 's consul general to dubai and northern emirates from 1999 through 2003 , after which he became a senator serving from 2003 through 2005. al-fawwaz died on 8 january 2017 in amman , jordan from a heart attack , aged 66 .
