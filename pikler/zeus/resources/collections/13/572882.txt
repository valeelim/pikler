Kaci Kullmann Five     karin cecilie `` kaci '' kullmann five ( 13 april 1951 – 19 february 2017 ) was a norwegian businessperson and politician . she was a member of the conservative party . she served as chairwoman of the norwegian nobel committee from 2015 to her death in 2017. five was born in bærum , norway . she studied at the university of oslo . she was married to carsten o. five until her death in 2017. they had two children . she served as minister of trade and shipping from 1989 to 1990 and as a member of parliament from 1981 to 1997 , and was leader of the conservative party from 1991 to 1994. she was a member of the norwegian nobel committee since 2003. she was also a board member of the nobel foundation . five died on 19 february 2017 from breast cancer in oslo , norway , aged 65 .
