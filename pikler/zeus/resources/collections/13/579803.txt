Chrystia Freeland     christina alexandra `` chrystia '' freeland ( born august 2 , 1968 in peace river , alberta ) is a canadian politician of the liberal party of canada . she has been foreign minister since 2017. freeland has been a member of parliament since 2013. from 2015 to 2017 she was also minister of trade . previously she was a freelance journalist . she has written several non-fiction books .
