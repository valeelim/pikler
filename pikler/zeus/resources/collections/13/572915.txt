Blister beetle     the blister beetle is a beetle of the family ( biology ) meloidae . the name comes from defensive secretion of cantharidin , a chemical that causes blisters . about 7,500 species are known worldwide . they are poisonous to predators . blister beetles go through several larval stages . the larvae mainly attack bees . they also eat grasshopper eggs . blister beetles are attracted to alfalfa and weeds during bloom .
