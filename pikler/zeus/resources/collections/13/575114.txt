Mike Leavitt     michael okerlund `` mike '' leavitt ( born february 11 , 1951 ) is an american republican politician . he served as the 14th governor of utah from 1993 to 2003 , as administrator of the environmental protection agency from 2003 to 2005 and as secretary of health and human services from 2005 to 2009. leavitt serves as a co-leader of the prevention initiative at the bipartisan policy center .
