Francisco Núñez Olivera     francisco núñez olivera ( born 13 december 1904 ) , age , is the oldest man in the world . he was born at extremadura , and joined the army at 19 to fight in the rif war . núñez olivera had his kidney removed when he was 90 and a cataract operation aged 98 , and is a veteran of the spanish civil war . he was nicknamed `` marchena '' after pepe marchena , due to his resemblance to the singer . with the death of israeli supercentenarian yisrael kristal on 11 august 2017 , núñez became the worlds oldest living man .
