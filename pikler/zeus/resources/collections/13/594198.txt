Do It Like a Dude     `` do it like a dude '' is the debut single by english singer-songwriter jessie j. the song was released as the lead single from her debut album , `` who you are '' ( 2011 ) . it was released as a download in november 2010 in the united kingdom with a cd release in january 2011. it peaked at number two on the uk singles chart and in scotland too , number eight in new zealand and belgium , and number eleven in ireland . `` do it like a dude '' was awarded best song at the 2011 mobo awards . `` do it like a dude '' was written by jessie j under her real name of jessica cornish , george astasio , jason pebworth , jon shave , kyle abrahams and peter ighile jessie j had wrote the song originally for barbadian r & b singer rihanna because rihanna 's song `` rude boy '' was released at the time that jessie j began to write it , she played the song to her label , island records , before sending it to rihanna 's management . island then wanted the song to become jessie 's j first single , as they thought it was `` amazing '' . jessie j has said that she would like to perform the song live with rihanna as jessie j thinks that rihanna `` would kill it .
