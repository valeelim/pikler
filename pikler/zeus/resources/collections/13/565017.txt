Andrei Karlov     andrei gennadyevich karlov ( ; 4 february 1954 – 19 december 2016 ) was a russian diplomat . he served as the russian ambassador to turkey from 12 july 2013 until his death . he also served as russia 's ambassador to north korea from 9 july 2001 to 20 december 2006. he was born in moscow . karlov , 62 , was shot and killed on 19 december 2016 in ankara , turkey . his killer , 22-year-old mevlüt mert altıntaş , was killed by security forces .
