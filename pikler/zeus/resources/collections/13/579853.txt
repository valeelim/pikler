David Gove     david t. gove ( may 4 , 1978 – april 5 , 2017 ) was an american ice hockey player . he served as head coach of the wheeling nailers of the echl . he last played with the wilkes-barre/scranton penguins of the american hockey league during the 2008–09 season . he played for the carolina hurricanes from 2000 through 2009. on april 5 , 2017 , gove was found dead in pittsburgh on april 5 , 2017 from a suspected drug overdose .
