Sheikhupura Division     sheikhupura division is an administrative division of the punjab province of pakistan . it was created in 2008 , formerly a part of lahore division . sheikhupura is the divisional headquarters of sheikhupura division . since divisions were restored back in 2008 , the division currently consists on two districts : - nankana sahib district - sheikhupura district see also . - sheikhupura
