Eva García Sáenz de Urturi     eva garcía sáenz de urturi ( 1972 in vitoria-gasteiz , basque country ) is a spanish novelist . she moved out to alicante , valencian community , when she was 15. she lives there since then . after getting a degree as optometrist , she got a job at university of alicante into the optical sector . in 2012 wrote `` la saga de los longevos '' being this one her first novel . the book was published via amazon.com by her own after she tried to contact with several publishing houses without success . however `` la saga de los longevos '' became a hit . two years later she wrote `` los hijos de adán '' and `` pasaje a tahití '' , also with a good reception . in 2016 she published her fourth book : `` el silencio de la ciudad blanca '' settle in her home town . in this one , she joined into a police academy , where she took two courses of crime scene investigation and reveal of footprints . bibliography . - ( 2012 ) `` la saga de los longevos '' - ( 2012 ) `` la vieja familia '' - ( 2014 ) `` los hijos de adán '' - ( 2014 ) `` pasaje a tahití ''
