Club Penguin Island     club penguin island is a massively multiplayer online role-playing game . it was created by disney canada inc. for ios and android smartphones . the game was released on march 29 , 2017. club penguin island uses a three dimensional game engine rather than adobe flash player like the original club penguin . the game has received mostly negative reviews by critics and users . the game was originally known as `` project : super secret '' to the public . it was announced as `` club penguin island '' on november 17 , 2016 .
