Wally Westlake     waldon thomas westlake ( born november 8 , 1920 ) is an american utility baseball player . he had a ten-year career from 1947 to 1956. he was born in gridley , california . westlake played for the pittsburgh pirates , st. louis cardinals , cincinnati reds and philadelphia phillies of the national league , and the cleveland indians and baltimore orioles of the american league . he played third base and outfield . westlake is the oldest living former national league player , and is also the oldest living member of the cincinnati reds , philadelphia phillies , pittsburgh pirates , and st. louis cardinals .
