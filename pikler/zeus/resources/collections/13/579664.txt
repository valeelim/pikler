Chelsea Brown     chelsea brown ( december 6 , 1942 – march 27 , 2017 ) was an american-australian actress and comedian . she appeared as a regular performer in comedy series `` rowan & martin 's laugh-in '' . she was also known for her roles in the soap operas `` number 96 '' ( in 1977 ) , and `` e street '' ( in 1990–1991 ) . brown was born in chicago , illinois . she was married to vic rooney until her death in 2017. brown died on march 27 , 2017 from pneumonia caused by dementia in chicago , aged 74 .
