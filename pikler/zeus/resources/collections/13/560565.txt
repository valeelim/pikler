Didier Guillemot     didier guillemot , born in 1960 , is a french doctor , specialized in infection . he is president of the `` université de versailles saint-quentin-en-yvelines '' since may 2016. graduate from the paris diderot university in innovation and public health , he starts his career at inserm in 1994. he moved to institut pasteur in 2001. he worked on the hiv . on april 2016 , he is elected president of the `` université de versailles saint-quentin-en-yvelines '' , succeeding jean-luc vayssière .
