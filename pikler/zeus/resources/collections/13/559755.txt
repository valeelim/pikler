2011–12 NHL season     the 2011–12 nhl season was the 95th season of operation ( 94th season of play ) of the national hockey league ( nhl ) . thirty teams competed . there were 82 games in the regular season from october 6 , 2011 to june 11 , 2012. the 2012 stanley cup playoffs began on april 11 , 2012. the los angeles kings won the stanley cup . during the off-season , the atlanta thrashers relocated to winnipeg , manitoba , to become the winnipeg jets . other websites . - nhl.com
