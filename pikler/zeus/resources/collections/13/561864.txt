David Gentleman     david gentleman ( born 11 march 1930 ) is an english artist . he studied at the royal college of art and was taught by edward bawden and john nash . he works in watercolour , lithography and wood engraving . some of his work is very large , such as the platform-length murals for charing cross underground station in london . some of his work is very small , including postage stamps and logos . his themes include landscape and environmental posters , drawings of street life , and protest placards . he has written and illustrated many books , mostly about countries and cities .
