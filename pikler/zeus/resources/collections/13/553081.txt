Amar Singh (cricketer)    amar singh ( cricketer ) amar singh was an indian cricketer . his full name was ladhabhai nakum amar singh lodha . he was a right arm fast bowler and a effective lower order batsman . amar singh was the first indian to receive a test cap . he was born in rajkot , gujarat on 4 december 1910. amar singh played for the indian cricket team , hindus cricket team and various teams in the first class cricket matches . he took 28 wickets and scored 292 runs in 7 test cricket matches , playing for the indian cricket team . amar singh died due to typhoid on may 21 1940 in jamnagar , gujarat .
