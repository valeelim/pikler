Arnold Clark     sir john arnold clark ( 27 november 1927 – 10 april 2017 ) was a scottish billionaire businessman . he was the chairman and chief executive of arnold clark group . in april 2015 , the company celebrated its 60th year in business . he was born in glasgow . his estimated fortune in 2016 was £1.025 billion . in the 2004 new year honours , clark was knighted , for services to the motor industry , and for his community work in scotland . clark died on 10 april 2017 from natural causes in his home in glasgow at the age of 89. other websites . - arnoldclark.com - harry fairbairn - racing yacht drum
