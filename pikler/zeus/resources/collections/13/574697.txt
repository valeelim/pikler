Tommy Gemmell     thomas `` tommy '' gemmell ( 16 october 1943 – 2 march 2017 ) was a scottish football player and manager . he served as a left-sided fullback and had powerful shooting ability . gemmell is best known as one of the celtic side who won the 1966–67 european cup ; he scored the first celtic goal in the final . gemmell played 18 times for scotland , and also played for nottingham forest , miami toros and dundee . after retiring as a player in 1977 , gemmell managed dundee and albion rovers . gemmell died on 2 march 2017 , aged 73 , after a long illness . other websites . - newcastle fans profile
