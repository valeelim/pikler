São Paulo–Guarulhos International Airport     são paulo–guarulhos international airport is an airport that serves são paulo , brazil , located 25km north of the city centre . it is sometimes called cumbica airport , after the suburb in which it is located . its iata code is gru and icao code is sbgr . the following major international airlines fly into guarulhos : - aerolineas argentinas - aeroméxico - air canada - air france - alitalia - american airlines - all nippon airways - avianca - british airways - bra - continental - copa airlines - cubana de aviación - delta airlines - iberia airlines - jal japan airlines - klm royal dutch - lanchile - lapa - lloyd aero boliviano - lufthansa - nacional transporte aéreo - ocean air - pluna - rio sul - south african airways - scandinavian airlines - spanair - swiss air - latam cargo brasil - air portugal - united airlines - varig - via brasil many of these airlines have lounges at guarulhos , most of which have internet access and mobile phone support , along with two meeting rooms for corporate executives .
