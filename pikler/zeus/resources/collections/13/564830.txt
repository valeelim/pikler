European Russia     russia is a country that is both in europe and in asia . looking at the geography , about twenty-five percent of the surface is in europe , and seventy-five percent is in asia . looking at the population , about 110 million of the 144 million people , or 77 % , live in the european part . the european part makes up 38 % of the surface of europe . in the european part of russia , there are 27.5 people per square kilometer . the asian part has 22 % of the population , which means there are 2.5 people per square kilometer . the two biggest cities , moscow and saint petersburg are in the european part .
