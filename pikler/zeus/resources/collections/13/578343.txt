Elbert Roest     elbert jan roest ( born october 13 , 1954 in hoogezand ) is a dutch politician of democrats 66 ( d66 ) . he is also a historian and a former teacher . he has started his political career in the municipality of doesburg ( province of gelderland ) . in this city he was a councillor and and alderman , from 1992 to 2002. afterwards he has become mayor in laren ( province of north holland ) . roest studied history at the university of groningen , and worked several years as a history teacher . furthermore he was involved in examination development , the so-called cito . he is a member of the european association of history educators ( euroclio ) , and participates in history writing about balkan and former soviet countries . references . - www.laren.nl
