Gheorghe Gușet     gheorghe guşet ( 28 may 1968 – 12 june 2017 ) was a romanian shot putter . he competed at the 1992 , 2000 and 2004 olympics and had his best result in 2004 , aged 36 ( 14th place ) . guşet was noted for being competitive in his late 30s , and set his all-time personal record aged 38 , while most shot putters peak in their late 20s or early 30s . due to health problems ( chronic kidney disease ) he was forced to retire from competitions in 2008. guşet died on 12 june 2017 in cluj napoca , romania from an aortic dissection at the age of 49 .
