Pulau Ubin     pulau ubin ( also called ubin island ) is an island at the northeast of singapore . the island is now a tourist attraction . less than 100 people stay on the island . but many people visit in the daytime to enjoy nature . `` pulau ubin '' means `` granite island '' in malay language . visitors can take a boat from changi point ferry terminal . it takes 10 minutes to reach pulau ubin . on the island , people can ride bicycles . there are some shops which rent bicycles to visitors .
