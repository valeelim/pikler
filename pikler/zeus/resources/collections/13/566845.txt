Back to the Future trilogy     the back to the future trilogy is an american science fiction-comedy movie series , consisting of three movies : - back to the future ( part i ) - 1985 - back to the future part ii - 1989 - back to the future part iii - 1990 the trilogy is about a teenager named marty mcfly ( a role of michael j. fox ) who is able to travel in time . this is due to the invention of an automobile time machine made by scientist dr. emmett l. brown ( a role of christopher lloyd ) . living in 1985 , marty mcfly travels to future 2015 and also to past 1955 and 1885. during these times he has several adventures in his home town hill valley in california . cast . - marty mcfly - michael j. fox - seamus mcfly - michael j. fox - dr. emmett l. brown - christopher lloyd - biff tannen - thomas f. wilson - griff tannen - thomas f. wilson - buford `` mad dog '' tannen - thomas f. wilson - george douglas mcfly - crispin glover , jeffrey weissman - lorraine baines-mcfly - lea thompson - maggie mcfly - lea thompson
