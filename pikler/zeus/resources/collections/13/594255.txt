Inga Swenson     inga swenson ( born december 29 , 1932 ) is an american actress . she was born in omaha , nebraska . swenson is best known for her roles as gretchen kraus in the tv sitcom `` benson '' and as ingrid svenson on the tv sitcom `` soap '' , which had the same producers . she also appeared as northern matriarch maude hazard in the acclaimed mini-series `` north and south '' in 1985 and again in 1986 .
