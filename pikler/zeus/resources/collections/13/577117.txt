Edward Balliol     edward balliol was a claimant to the throne of scotland . he was born in 1283 and died in 1367. he reigned from 1332-1336 but claimed the throne from 1314-1356. he was the eldest son of john balliol and thus the heir to the scottish throne after john balliol but john was deposed by robert i and william wallace ( as guardian of scotland ) . he was chosen as king after the death of margaret , maid of norway . although he was never considered to be the true king as he was an ally with edward longshanks and edward ii , kings of england .
