Palakkad     palakkad is a city in the state of kerala , india . it is the administrative capital of palakkad district . palakkad has the seventh most people in kerala . malayalam is the most spoken language in palakkad city . palakkad had 130,955 people as of the 2011 census of india . the literacy rate of the city is 94.20 % . the metro area of palakkad has 293,566 people . tourist places in palakkad include : - palakkad fort - jainimedu jain temple - kalpathy agraharam - famous for ratholsavam - malampuzha dam - manappulikavu temple - chinmaya mission guruvayurappa temple
