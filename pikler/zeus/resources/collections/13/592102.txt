Animal Jam     `` animal jam '' is a virtual world made by wildworks and the national geographic society . many of the players in animal jam are children and teenagers . according to the wildworks website , the game has over 130 million players . animal jam is one of the fastest growing children 's games in the world . the game is free to play , but to be able to receive all the things animal jam has to offer , a membership needs to be purchased online or through a store-purchased gift card . in animal jam , players enjoy many fun events , like playing games , making friends , and decorating their dens and animals . they also learn facts about science , especially in the field of zoology , the study of animals . animal jam takes place in a world called jamaa , and each player is known in the game as a jammer . official website
