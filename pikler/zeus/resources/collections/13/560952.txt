Catherine Cortez Masto     catherine marie cortez masto ( born march 29 , 1964 ) is an american attorney and politician . she is the united states senator from nevada . she was the attorney general of nevada from 2007 to 2015 , elected to replace george chanos . she is a member of the democratic party . when her term began in 2017 , masto became the first latina united states senator in american history .
