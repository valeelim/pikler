Lea DeLaria     lea delaria ( born may 23 , 1958 ) is an american comedian , actress , and jazz musician . delaria was `` the first openly gay comic to break the late-night talk-show barrier '' with her 1993 appearance on `` the arsenio hall show '' . she is best known for her portrayal of inmate big boo on netflix original series `` orange is the new black '' . from 2015 through 2017 , she was engaged to fashion editor chelsea fairless until they separated .
