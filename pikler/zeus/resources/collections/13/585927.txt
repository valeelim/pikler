Henry Tilney     henry tilney is the most important man in the story by jane austen called northanger abbey . henry is said to be the nicest of all jane austen 's church men ( pastors ) . he is a good dancer and a good driver . he also talks in an amusing way . henry meets catherine morland at a dance , and they get on well . he laughs at her in a kind way . he is a guide for catherine , and comes to love her for her honesty . henry 's father thinks catherine is rich . so he invites her to the family home at northanger abbey . henry goes on looking after catherine there , and they grow close . then his father orders catherine out of his house , as he now thinks she is poor . henry stands up to his father . he goes to catherine 's home , and asks her to marry him . they have to wait , but at last the father lets them marry . romance henry tilney
