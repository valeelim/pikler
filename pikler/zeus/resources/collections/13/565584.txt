Pterygotus     pterygotus anglicus is an extinct sea scorpion . it was alive in the silurian and devonian periods . fossils have been found in north america , south america , europe and australia . it is related to the larger `` jaekelopterus '' . it is similar to the freshwater `` slimonia '' . `` pterygotus '' could reach a body length of . it had a pair of large compound eyes . it also had another pair of smaller eyes in the center of its head . it had 4 pairs of walking legs . a fifth pair of legs were swimming paddles . it also had a pair of large pincers .
