WWE United Kingdom Championship     the wwe united kingdom championship is a wrestling title owned by wwe . it was created in december 15 , 2016 and a 16-man tournament with british wrestlers was make on january 14 and 15 , 2017 to crown the champion . tyler bate beat pete dunne in the final to become the first and current champion . as of 17 , 2017. as of 17 , 2017. other sites . - official wwe united kingdom championship title history
