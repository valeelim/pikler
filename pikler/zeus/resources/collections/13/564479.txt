Sun of May     the sun of may ( `` el sol de mayo '' ) is a national emblem of argentina and uruguay . it is a reference to the may revolution in argentina , which led to that country 's later independence from spain . in uruguay it also refers to the may revolution . on 25 may 1810 , the sun came out in buenos aires during a public gathering . this was seen as a good omen for the future success of the independence struggle .
