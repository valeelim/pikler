Opisthocoelicaudia     opisthocoelicaudia is a genus of titanosaur sauropod dinosaur . it is from late cretaceous rocks from mongolia 's nemegt basin . in 1965 a well preserved skeleton was found , missing only a neck and head . scientists are arguing whether `` nemegtosaurus '' is a synonym of `` opisthocoelicaudia '' or its own genus .
