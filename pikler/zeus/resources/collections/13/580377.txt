Kirill Kovaldzhi     kirill vladimirovich kovaldzhi ( ; march 14 , 1929 – april 10 , 2017 ) was a russian poet , novelist , literary critic and translator . in moscow , published collections of poems `` at dawn '' ( 1958 ) , `` voices '' ( 1972 ) , `` after noon '' ( 1981 ) , `` yearly rings '' ( 1982 ) , `` high dialogue '' ( 1988 ) , `` links and grains '' ( 1989 ) , `` the book of lyrics '' ( 1993 ) , `` the invisible threshold '' ( 1999 ) , `` to you . on demand '' ( 2002 ) , the book of prose and poetry `` countdown '' ( 2003 ) , the short book `` zerna '' ( 2005 ) . the author of a number of short stories , the story `` five points on the map '' ( 1965 ) , the novel `` liman stories '' ( 1970 ) - his new supplemented edition , published under the title `` candle in draft '' ( 1996 ) , was nominated for the state prize of russia . kovaldzhi died in moscow on april 10 , 2017 , aged 88. other websites . - интернет-презентация
