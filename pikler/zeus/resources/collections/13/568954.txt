Heather Wilson     heather ann wilson ( born december 30 , 1960 ) is an american politician . wilson is the 24th and current united states secretary of the air force since may 16 , 2017. she served as president of the south dakota school of mines and technology in rapid city , south dakota . she is a former republican member of the united states house of representatives representing from 1998 to 2009. she was the first female military veteran elected to a full term in congress . on january 23 , 2017 , president donald trump announced his intentions to nominate wilson as secretary of the air force . the united states senate confirmed her nomination on may 8 , 2017 .
