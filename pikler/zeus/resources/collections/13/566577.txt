Kosasthalaiyar River     the kosasthalaiyar river is one of the three rivers flowing in the chennai metropolitan area . it is a long river that starts near pallipattu , thiruvallur district . its northern tributary , the nagari river , starts in chitoor district , andhra pradesh . it joins the main river in the poondi resorvoir . kosasthalaiyar river catchment area spreads in vellore , chitoor , north arcot , thiruvallur and chennai districts . the discharge capacity of the river is 110000m/sec . the flood discharge capacity is about 125000m/sec .
