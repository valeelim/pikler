Memè Perlini     amelio `` memè '' perlini ( 8 december 1947 – 5 april 2017 ) was an italian actor and movie director . his debut movie as a director , `` italian postcards '' , was screened in the un certain regard section at the 1987 cannes film festival . perlini died in rome on 5 april 2017 , aged 69. he committed suicide , by jumping from the fifth floor of his house .
