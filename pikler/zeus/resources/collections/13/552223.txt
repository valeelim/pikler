Richie Rich (comics)    richie rich ( comics ) richie rich is a fictional character created by alfred harvey and warren kremer , and published by harvey comics in the 1950s . richie rich is called `` the poor little rich boy '' , or also `` the richest kid in the world '' . he appeared in comic book titles such as `` richie rich millions '' ( 1961 ) and `` richie rich dollars & cents '' ( 1963 ) . other websites . - richie rich at the internet movie database
