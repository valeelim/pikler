Emile Roemer     emile gerardus maria roemer ( born august 24 , 1962 in boxmeer ) is a dutch politician of the socialist party ( sp ) . he has been political leader since 2010. he has been a member of the second chamber since 2006 and parliamentary leader since 2010. previously he was an alderman of boxmeer , and a member of the party board . roemer is a roman catholic and a former teacher . references . - bio at parlement.com
