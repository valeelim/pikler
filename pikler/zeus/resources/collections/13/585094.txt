Nawshirwan Mustafa     nawshirwan mustafa ( 1944 – 19 march 2017 ) was an iraqi kurdish politician . he served as the general coordinator ( رێکخەری گشتی / rêkxerî giştî ) of the movement for change ( ) from 2009 until his death in 2017. he served as the leader of the official opposition in the kurdistan region . he was born in sulaymaniyah , iraq . mistafa died in sulaymaniyah due to lung cancer on 19 may 2017 at the age of 73 .
