United States of America Mathematical Olympiad     united states of america mathematical olympiad , or usamo , is a highly selective ( only very good people take it ) mathematics competition that takes place in the united states . the first usamo was in 1972. it is the final round of the amc series of contests . the united states of america junior mathematical olympiad ( usajmo ) was created in 2010 to recognize top scorers based on their amc10-based index ( score ) . qualifying for the usamo is considered one of the most prestigious , or best , awards for high school students in the united states , with only 264 students qualifying in 2013 out of over 350,000 students competing . top scorers on the usamo are invited to the mathematical olympiad summer program to represent the united states at the international mathematical olympiad . the amc competition series consists of multiple tests to determine the 6 people that will represent the united states in the international mathematical olympiad : - amc 8/10/12 is available for everyone who is not over 8th , 10th , or 12th grade , respectively .
