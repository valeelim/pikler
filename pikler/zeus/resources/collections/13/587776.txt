I Love It     `` i love it '' is a song by swedish music duo icona pop . it first appeared on their album `` icona pop '' but was later released as part of their ep `` iconic '' and their album `` this is ... icona pop '' . british singer-songwriter charli xcx sings on the song and co-wrote it . it reached the top ten around the world and reached number one on the uk singles chart . it was used as the theme song for the mtv `` jersey shore '' spinoff , `` snooki & jwoww '' . on december 14 , 2015 , the song was certified quadruple-platinum by the recording industry association of america for shipments and streams of over four million copies across the united states .
