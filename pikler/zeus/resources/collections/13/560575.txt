Vossloh DE 18     the vossloh de 18 is a bo-bo diesel-electric locomotive . it is the first new diesel-electric locomotive to be built in kiel since production of the de 1002 ended in 1993. up to 60 % of the components are shared with the diesel-hydraulic g 18. the prototype locomotive was shown together with the smaller , diesel-hydraulic g 12 on innotrans 2010. the launch customer for the de 18 was basf , ordering 2 de 18 locomotives for services at its plants in december 2011. other websites . - official vossloh de 18 product page
