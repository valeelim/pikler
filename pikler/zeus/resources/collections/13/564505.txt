Lester L. Wolff     lester lionel wolff ( born january 4 , 1919 ) is an american politician . he is a former democratic member of the united states house of representatives from new york . he served as a representative from 1973 through 1981. he now serves as president of the international trade and development agency . in 2014 , wolff received the congressional gold medal , the highest civilian award in the united states . wolff is also the chair of the touro college pacific community institute , the author of numerous books on foreign policy , and the host of weekly pbs show `` ask congress '' .
