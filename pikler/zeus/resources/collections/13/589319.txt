David Pryor     david hampton pryor ( born august 29 , 1934 ) is a former democratic member of the united states house of representatives and united states senator from the state of arkansas . pryor also served as 39th governor of arkansas from 1975 to 1979 and was a member of the arkansas house of representatives from 1960 to 1966. pryor served as the interim chairman of the arkansas democratic party , following bill gwatney 's assassination . pryor was born in camden , the seat of ouachita county in southern arkansas , to william edgar pryor and the former susan newton .
