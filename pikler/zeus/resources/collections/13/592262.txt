Hrvoje Šarinić    hrvoje šarinić hrvoje šarinić ( ; 17 february 1935 – 21 july 2017 ) was a croatian politician . he served as the prime minister of the republic of croatia from 1992 until 1993. after the parliamentary elections of 1992 he , as member of croatian democratic union ( hdz ) , was appointed to the post of prime minister . šarinić died on 21 july 2017 in zagreb of cardiopulmonary arrest at the age of 82 .
