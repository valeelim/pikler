Who You Are     who you are is the debut album by english singer-songwriter jessie j , released on 28 february 2011 by lava records . the album was originally due to be released a month later but due to jessie j 's popularity the album got brought forward . the album was written over a five year period . `` who you are '' received mainly positive reviews from music critics . on 6 march 2011 , `` who you are '' debuted at number two on the uk albums chart , behind adele 's `` 21 '' . it sold 105,000 copies in its first week . in august 2013 the album was certified quadruple platinum by the british phonographic industry ( bpi ) for selling 1,200,000 copies in the united kingdom .
