Chabelo     xavier lópez rodríguez ( born february 17 , 1035 ) , better known as `` chabelo '' , is a mexican actor and television host . he has been working on television for over sixty years . `` chabelo '' has been in more than thirty motion pictures and recorded more than thirty musical albums . he also has produced many shows like `` la cuchufleta '' and `` la güereja quiere más '' . his most famous tv show was `` en familia con chabelo '' , which was broadcast every sunday morning on televisa . this show was made up of contests with people from the audience , gifts and games and was mainly for children . the program ended on sunday , december 20 , 2015 after 47 years .
