Édouard Philippe    édouard philippe édouard philippe ( born 28 november 1970 ) is a french lawyer and politician , serving as the prime minister of france since 15 may 2017. philippe is a member of the union for a popular movement , later the republicans . he has served as a member of the national assembly representing the 7th constituency of seine-maritime since 2012 , as well as mayor of le havre and president of the agglomeration community of le havre since 2010. on 15 may 2017 , president emmanuel macron appointed him prime minister . other websites . - biography on the national assembly site - biography on the site of le havre
