Alexander Dennis Enviro400     the alexander dennis enviro400 is a double-decker low-floor bus built by alexander dennis . there have been two generations since 2005. the first generation enviro400 was launched in december 2005. the second generation enviro400 ( known as the enviro400 mmc ) was launched in may 2014. a diesel-electric hybrid version , called the enviro400h , was launched in october 2015. a hybrid version , called the enviro400h city , was launched in october 2015. it is aimed at the london market , and is visually similar to the new routemaster and enviro200 mmc . the enviro400h city is only sold as a hybrid in london to compete with the new routemaster .
