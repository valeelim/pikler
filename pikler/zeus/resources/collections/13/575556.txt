Orathanadu Taluk     orathanadu taluk is a taluk of thanjavur district in the indian state of tamil nadu . the headquarters of the taluk are located at orathanadu . as of the census of india 2011 , the population of orathanadu taluk of thanjavur district is 1,60,367 with 40,383 households.the total population constitute , 77,719 males and 82,648 females —a sex ratio of 1063 females per 1000 males . 15,597 children are in the age group of 0–6 years , of which 8,004 are boys and 7,593 are girls . the average literacy rate stands at 75.16 % with 1,08,813 literates .
