Il macigno     il macigno ( the rock ) is an italian opera in two or three acts , music by victor de sabata and libretto by alberto colantuoni , it was first performed in milan , on march 31 , 1917. then in 1935 in turin , the work was renamed with the title changed to driada , unfortunately the score disappeared in the fire bombing of the world war ii in milan in 1943 , but remained a score for voice and piano , and the complete libretto . principal characters . - driada - soprano - ibetto - tenor - martano - baritone - gian della tolfa - bass structure of opera . act i . - preludio e introduzione : `` chi gunge per santa palazia ? '' ( coro , lionetta , martano ) - `` empirò la tua soglia '' ( lionetta , driada , coro ) - `` fiata il vespro sui clivi '' ( coro ) - `` driada , driada ! '' ( martano , driada , búttaro ) - `` posa il tuo ferro '' ( gian della tolfa , coro , martano ) - `` per la sera fasciata '' ( driada , ibetto ) - `` a la catasta ! '' ( martano , driada , ibetto ) act ii ; part one . - `` levata , s'è la stella '' – `` driada '' ( ibetto , driada ) - `` scendono ! '' – `` e la pieve rintocca a mattutino '' ( lionetta , donne )
