Memo Morales     guillermo enrique morales portillo , better known as memo morales ( 6 april 1937 – 1 january 2017 ) was a venezuelan singer also known as `` el gitano maracucho '' . he was the voice main of important orchestras of the country . in 1981 , he founded , with luisín landaez , `` la nuestra '' , and in 1984 , with cheo garcía , created `` la gran orquesta de cheo y memo '' . they performed together until garcia 's death in 1994. morales died on 1 january 2017 in caracas , venezuela from a heart attack , aged 79 .
