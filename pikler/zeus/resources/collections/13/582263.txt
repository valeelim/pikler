Audre Lorde     audre lorde ( february 18 , 1934 – november 17 , 1992 ) was an american writer , feminist , womanist , and civil rights activist . she was born in new york city . lorde was born almost blind and was tongue tied . at the age of four , lorde learned how to read and talk . lorde went to catholic grammar schools . she went to hunter college and columbia university . she would say that “ lesbian ” and “ black ” didn ’ t really define her . she didn ’ t like being put in those categories . she was a lesbian . she was famous for her strong poems about racism and love . lorde died on november 17 , 1992 in saint croix , u.s. virgin islands from liver cancer , aged 58. other websites . - profile and poems at the poetry foundation - profile and poems written and audio at academy of american poets - `` voices from the gaps : audre lorde '' . profile . university of minnesota - profile at modern american poetry
