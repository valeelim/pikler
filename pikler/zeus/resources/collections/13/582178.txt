Leo Baxendale     leo baxendale ( 27 october 1930 – 23 april 2017 ) was an english cartoonist and publisher . baxendale wrote and drew several comics . among his most famous creations are the `` beano '' strips `` little plum '' , `` minnie the minx '' , `` the bash street kids '' , and `` the three bears '' . baxendale died on 23 april , 2017 of cancer in lancashire , england at the age of 86. other websites . - reaper books – baxendale 's own publishing company - leo baxendale at lambiek.net - video clip of leo baxendale talking as part of the bbc 's `` comics britannia '' series ( 2007 ) ( realplayer )
