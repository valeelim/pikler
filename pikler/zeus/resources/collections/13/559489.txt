Les Septvallons     les septvallons is a commune in the aisne department of northern france . the municipality was established on 1 january 2016 and consists of the former communes of glennes , longueval-barbonval , merval , perles , révillon , vauxcéré and villers-en-prayères . the town hall is in longueval-barbonval . les septvallons is in the eastern part of the soissons `` arrondissement '' , in the valley of the aisne river ; the `` commune '' is on the border with the marne department , at to the south of laon , the prefecture of the department , and at to the east of soissons , the subprefecture of the `` arrondissement '' . it has an area of with an altitude of . the `` commune '' is formed by : related pages . - communes of the aisne department - arrondissement of château-thierry
