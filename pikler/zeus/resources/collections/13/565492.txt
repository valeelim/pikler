ER (TV series)    er ( tv series ) er was an american medical drama series on nbc . the show aired from september 19 , 1994 to april 2 , 2009. the show was about fictional county general hospital in chicago . it showed stories from the emergency room . novelist michael crichton was the creator of the show . `` er '' won 23 primetime emmy awards . it won 116 awards overall , including the peabody award . it was nominated for 124 emmys .
