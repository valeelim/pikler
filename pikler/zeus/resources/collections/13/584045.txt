Nelson Xavier     nelson xavier ( 30 august 1941 – 10 may 2017 ) was a brazilian actor and director . he appeared in more than 95 movies and television shows between 1959 and 2017. he starred in the 1964 movie `` os fuzis '' . in 1976 xavier co-directed `` a queda '' along with ruy guerra . it was entered into the 28th berlin international film festival , where it won the silver bear – special jury prize . his last movie was `` trash '' ( 2014 ) . xavier died on 10 may 2017 in uberlandia , minas gerais from lung disease , aged 75 .
