Abdelkrim Ghallab     abdelkrim ghallab ( december 31 , 1919 – august 14 , 2017 ) was a moroccan political journalist , cultural commentator , and novelist . he was born in fes , morocco . he was an important figure both in the literary and political field . he studied both at the university of al-karaouine in fez and at the university of cairo . ghallab was the author of five novels and three collections of short stories . his best known novel is `` dafann al-m 'd ( the buried past ) '' , 1966. according to simon gikandi his arabic style is known for its `` graceful and at times scholarly classicism '' . in 2000 , the union of arab writers in egypt included his novel `` al-mu`alîm `ali '' ( master alí ) among the hundred best arabic novels in history . in 2001 , the moroccan department of culture published the complete works of ghallab in five volumes . in 2004 he was awarded the maghreb culture prize of tunis . his work has been translated in many languages . ghallab died in el jadida on august 14 , 2017 , aged 97 .
