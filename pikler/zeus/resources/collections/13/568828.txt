Jefferson Memorial     the jefferson memorial is a presidential memorial in washington , d.c.. it was dedicated to the 3rd president of the united states thomas jefferson . the neoclassical memorial building on the tidal basin off the washington channel of the potomac river was designed by the architect john russell pope . construction began in december 1938. it was complete in 1939 with a ceremony attended by then-president franklin d. roosevelt . as a national memorial it was administratively listed on the national register of historic places on october 15 , 1966 .
