Ganges Delta     the ganges-brahmaputra delta is a river delta in the south asia region of bangladesh and in west bengal , india . it is the world 's largest delta . the delta is also known as the brahmaputra delta , the `` sunderbans delta '' , or the bengal delta . because it is also one of the most fertile regions in the world , it is called the green delta . the ganges delta empties into the bay of bengal . it is approximately across at the bay of bengal.. the floodplain is from three rivers : ganges , the brahmaputra river and the meghna river . the delta stretches from the hooghly river on the west to the meghna river on the east . in india kolkata and haldia are the main seaports . in bangladesh the main seaports are the port of mongla and chittagong .
