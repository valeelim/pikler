Nodeul Station     nodeul station is a train station of seoul subway line 9 , located in dongjak district , seoul , south korea . originally , the name `` nodeul '' means `` the stepping stone that egret playing with '' , and now it means the area near noryangjin ( which is a hanja translation from nodeul ) . history . - september 18 , 2008 : station name decided - july 24 , 2009 : opened with opening ofseoul subway line 9 gaehwa - sinnonhyeon station has 2 side platforms , 2 tracks .
