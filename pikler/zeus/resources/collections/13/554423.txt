Diosdado Macapagal     diosdado pangan macapagal was the ninth president of the philippines . he is known for changing the country 's independence day from july 4 to june 12. june 12 is the day president emilio aguinaldo declared independence of the first philippine republic from the spanish empire in 1898. he is the father of gloria macapagal-arroyo , who was the fourteenth president of the philippines from 2001 to 2010 .
