Chidambaram     chidambaram is a town and municipality in cuddalore district , tamil nadu . it is the headquarters of the chidambaram taluk . chidambaram covers an area of and had a population of 62,153 as of 2011. annamalai university , established in 1929 in chidambaram , is one of the oldest and most prominent universities in the state . this town is known famous for the great thillai nataraja temple .
