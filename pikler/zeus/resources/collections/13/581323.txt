HaTnu'a     hatnu'a , hebrew : התנועה , meaning `` the movement '' , is an israeli political party . its ideology is social liberal , secular and zionist . hatnu'a was founded in 2012 by tzipi livni ( also political leader ) , as a splitting from kadima . number of seats in the 20th knesset : 5 ( down one from 19th knesset ) . hatnu'a is cooperating with haavoda ( `` labor '' ) and hatnu'a hayeruqa ( `` the green movement '' ) as hamahaneh hatziyoni ( `` the zionist union '' ) . together they have 24 seats .
