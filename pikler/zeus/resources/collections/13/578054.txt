Khadija Arib     khadija arib , arabic : خديجة عريب , ( born october 10 , 1960 in hedami ) is a dutch politician . she is a member of the labour party ( pvda ) . arib was born in morocco . she migrated to the netherlands when she was 15 years of age . she studied sociology at the university of amsterdam . she is a member of the second chamber since 1998 ( with a short interruption in 2006-2007 ) . she has also been speaker of the second chamber since 2016. she is a muslim and of moroccan descent . references . - bio at parlement.com
