Marilyn B. Young     marilyn b. young ( april 25 , 1937 – february 19 , 2017 ) was an american historian of american foreign relations . she served as professor of history at new york university . in 2000 , she was awarded a guggenheim fellowship and an american council of learned societies fellowship , and the berkshire women 's history prize for `` the vietnam wars , 1945–1990 '' . she was elected president of the society for historians of american foreign relations in 2011. young died in new york on february 19 , 2017 , aged 79 .
