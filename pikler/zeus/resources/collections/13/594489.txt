George Edward Glass     george edward glass is an american businessman and diplomat . he has been confirmed by the united states senate as the next united states ambassador to portugal . this nomination was confirmed by the u.s. senate on august 3 , 2017. glass was the owner of mgg development llc in lake oswego , oregon . he founded pacific crest securities in portland , oregon in 1990 and served as the company 's president until 2014. he is a trustee for oregon health & science university and a former trustee for the university of oregon . other websites . - profile at bloomberg
