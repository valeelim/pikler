Yoshinori Ohsumi     born 9 february 1945 is a japanese cell biologist . he won the 2016 physiology or medicine for his discoveries of how cell autophagy works . autophagy is the process that cells use to break down and recycle cellular components . this allows the orderly recycling of cellular components . ohsumi is a professor in tokyo institute of technology 's frontier research center . he got the kyoto prize for basic science in 2012 .
