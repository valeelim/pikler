Heidi Heitkamp     mary kathryn `` heidi '' heitkamp ( born october 30 , 1955 ) is an american attorney and politician who has been the junior united states senator from north dakota since 2013. a member of the north dakota democratic-nonpartisan league party , she is the first woman elected to the us senate from north dakota . she served as the 28th north dakota attorney general from 1993 to 2001 and as state tax commissioner from 1989 to 1993. heitkamp ran for governor of north dakota in 2000 and lost to republican john hoeven . heitkamp is north dakota 's second female senator , after jocelyn burdick , who was appointed to the office . in december 2016 , it was reported that heitkamp was being considered for agriculture secretary by president-elect donald trump , but she was not officially chosen for any cabinet positions .
