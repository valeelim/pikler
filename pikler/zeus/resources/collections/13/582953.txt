Robert E. Simanek     robert ernest simanek ( born april 26 , 1930 ) is a former united states marine . he is a recipient of the u.s. military 's highest decoration , the medal of honor , for his actions in the korean war . the medal of honor was presented to him by president dwight d. eisenhower in a white house ceremony on october 27 , 1953. he was the 36th marine to receive the medal in the korean war . in addition to the medal of honor , he was also awarded the purple heart , the korean service medal with two bronze service stars , the united nations service medal , and the national defense service medal . other websites . - phalen , lane . `` three who acted '' , `` amvets magazine '' , 2002. retrieved 2006-06-23
