Shen Daren     shen daren ( ) ( june 1928 – august 2 , 2017 ) was a chinese politician . he was born in wu county , jiangsu . he was communist party of china committee secretary of ningxia ( 1986–1989 ) and his home province ( 1989–1993 ) . he was a delegate to the 8th national people 's congress ( 1993–1998 ) and 9th national people 's congress ( 1998–2003 ) . shen died on august 2 , 2017 in beijing at the age of 89 .
