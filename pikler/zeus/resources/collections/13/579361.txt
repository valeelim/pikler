Antonio Lamela     antonio lamela ( december 1 , 1926 – april 1 , 2017 ) was a spanish architect . he was born and raised in madrid . his most well known projects are santiago bernabéu stadium , the adolfo suárez , madrid–barajas airport and the torres de colón . lamela died on april 1 , 2017 in madrid at the age of 90. other websites . - official website of estudio lamela arquitectos - conferencia a. lamela - antonio lamela cv , at coag - antonio lamela cv , at gva
