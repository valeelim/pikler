Angry Grandpa     charles marvin `` charlie '' green jr. ( born october 16 , 1950 ) , better known as angry grandpa ( agp ) , is an american internet personality . he is a former fireman . with his son , michael , he has a youtube channel , `` theangrygrandpashow '' , that has over three million subscribers . angry grandpa was born in charleston , south carolina . he has bipolar disorder and skin cancer .
