Piotr Bikont     piotr bikont ( 12 may 1955 – 27 june 2017 ) was a polish journalist , publicist , culinary critic and a theatre director . he was born in łódź , poland . bikont translated art spiegelman 's `` maus '' to polish . he was a member of editorial staff of the talking magazine `` gadający pies '' based in kraków . he was the author of a book `` jewish cooking according to balbina przepiórko '' . bikont died on 27 june 2017 in a car accident near warsaw , aged 62 .
