Ricardo Piglia     ricardo piglia ( november 24 , 1941 – january 6 , 2017 ) was an argentine one of the most famous contemporary writers in argentina . he was born in adrogué and raised in mar del plata . he was known for his fiction books `` artificial respiration '' ( 1980 ) , `` the absent city '' ( 1992 ) , `` burnt money '' ( 1997 ) ; and criticism including `` criticism and fiction '' ( 1986 ) , `` brief forms '' ( 1999 ) and `` the last reader '' ( 2005 ) . piglia died at 75 on january 6 , 2017 in buenos aires , argentina after suffering from amyotrophic lateral sclerosis for three years .
