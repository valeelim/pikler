Valeriu Bularca     valeriu bularca ( 14 february 1931 – 7 february 2017 ) was a retired romanian wrestler . he competed at the 1960 and 1964 olympics and won a silver medal in 1964. in 1961 he became the first romanian wrestler to win a world title . he collected six national titles between 1957 and 1964. after retiring from competitions he worked as a wrestling coach at cs steagu roșu in brașov . bularca died in brașov on 7 february 2017 , aged 85 .
