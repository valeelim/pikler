Etel Adnan     etel adnan ( born 24 february 1925 ) is a lebanese-american feminist writer , poet , essayist , and visual artist . in 2003 , adnan was named `` arguably the most celebrated and accomplished arab american author writing today '' by the academic journal `` melus : multi-ethnic literature of the united states '' . besides her literary works , adnan continues to produce visual works in a vareity of media , such as oil paintings , movies and tapestries , which have been exhibited at galleries across the world . she lives in paris and sausalito , california . adnan openly identifies as lesbian . other websites . - website of etel adnan - translated excerpt from `` sitt marie rose '' - culturebase ( in german ) - anne mullin burnham , `` reflections in women 's eyes '' , 1994 , saudi aramco world
