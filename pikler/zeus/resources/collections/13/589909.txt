LDV Maxus     ldv maxus was a van and minibus made by ldv limited and daewoo from 2004 to 2009. the maxus replaced the ldv convoy and the ldv pilot . the ldv maxus is called : fargo fora in turkey , ldv v80 in australia , maxus ld100 in russia and weststar ldv maxus in malaysia . in 2011 , the chinese company shanghai automotive industry corporation launched a new vehicle called maxus v80 which is identical to the ldv maxus the ldv maxus model was relaunched by saic as the v80 in june 2011. related pages . - ldv convoy - ldv pilot - ldv cub
