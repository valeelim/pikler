Richard DeVos     richard marvin devos sr. ( born march 4 , 1926 ) is an american businessman . he is known for being the co-founder of amway along with jay van andel ( company restructured as alticor in 2000 ) , and owner of the orlando magic nba basketball team . in 2012 , `` forbes '' magazine listed him as the 60th wealthiest person in the united states , and the 205th richest in the world , with an estimated net worth of $ 5.1 billion . at one point , he was one of the 10 wealthiest americans . devos was born in grand rapids , michigan . he studied at calvin college . he served in the military in world war ii in the united states army air corps . he is married to helen june devos . together , they have four children . his son , dick devos , is married to betsy devos the 11th and current united states secretary of education . other websites . - profile at `` forbes '' - richard devos ' campaign contributions at newsmeat - excellence in philanthropy , updates on past winners 2001-2013 : richard and helen devos - miller , john j. contesting art . philanthropy magazine . summer 2011 .
