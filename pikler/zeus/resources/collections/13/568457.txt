José Ferrer     josé vicente ferrer de otero y cintrón ( january 8 , 1912 – january 26 , 1992 ) , known as josé ferrer , was a puerto rican actor , theatre and movie director . he was the first puerto rican actor , as well as the first hispanic actor , to win an academy award ( in 1950 for `` cyrano de bergerac '' ) . he was born in san juan , puerto rico . ferrer was married to rosemary clooney , aunt of george clooney , from 1954 through 1961 and again from 1964 through 1967. they had a son , actor miguel ferrer . in 1985 he received the national medal of arts from president ronald reagan , becoming the first actor to receive that honor . ferrer died of colorectal cancer in coral gables , florida on january 26 , 1992 , aged 80. other websites . - josé ferrer collection , howard gotlieb archival research center , boston university
