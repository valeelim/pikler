Swish Swish     `` swish swish '' is a song by american singer katy perry . it features a rap from american rapper nicki minaj . the song is from perry 's fifth studio album , `` witness '' . it talks about overcoming bullying and has sounds of house music throughout it . it was released in may 2017 as a promotional single with the pre-order of `` witness '' , but it was released later as an official single on may 19. the song entered the `` billboard '' hot 100 at number 46 .
