Little Sammy Davis     little sammy davis ( born november 28 , 1928 ) is an american blues guitarist , harmonica player and songwriter . he was based in hudson valley , new york . although his musical career began in the 1940s , he was not widely known until the mid-1990s when he began working in radio , singing , playing live on tour , and recording studio albums . he recorded songs with muddy waters , earl hooker , albert king , and the levon helm band . davis was born in winona , mississippi . other websites . - award winning documenatary `` little sammy davis ''
