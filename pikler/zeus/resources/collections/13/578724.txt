Louis Beel     louis joseph maria beel ( april 12 , 1902 in roermond - february 11 , 1977 in utrecht ) was a dutch politician . he was a member of the roman-catholic state party ( rksp ) and later on of its post-war successor the catholic people 's party ( kvp ) ( a predecessor of the christian democratic appeal ( cda ) ) . beel was prime minister from 1946 to 1948 , and again from 1958 to 1959. he was also a government minister and a deputy prime minister . both in 1946 and in 1948 he was a member of the second chamber for a short time . he studied law at the radboud university nijmegen , and was also a professor in administrative law and public administration theory at the same university . beel was a roman catholic . he died in 1977 , aged 74. references . - bio at parlement.com
