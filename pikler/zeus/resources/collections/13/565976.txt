Muriel (name)    muriel ( name ) muriel is a female given name in the english language . it is also a family name . the name has been around for a long time in breton , celtic , irish and scottish gaelic languages . the name was common in medieval england , often formatted as merial . usage of `` muriel '' peaked in the early 1920s . use of the name has steadily declined over time . people with the family name include alma muriel . fictional characters named `` muriel '' include muriel heslop from `` muriel 's wedding '' .
