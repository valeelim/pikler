Sybrand van Haersma Buma     sybrand van haersma buma , often just called `` sybrand buma '' , ( born july 30 , 1965 in workum ) is a dutch politician of the christian democratic appeal ( cda ) . he has been political leader since 2012. he has been a member of the second chamber since 2002 , and also parliamentary leader since 2010. he studied law at the university of groningen , and worked at the council of state and as an assistant to the cda parliamentary group before entering into politics . he is a protestant . furthermore he is a descendant of the patrician frisian family of buma and partly of jewish descent . references . - bio at parlement.com
