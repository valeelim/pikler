Meroktenos     meroktenos is a genus of prosauropodomorph dinosaur from the late triassic of lesotho . there are no known paleoart images or sketches of this dinosaur . it is so rare and so little is known about it . the name `` meroktenos '' comes from ancient greek words `` mēros '' ( femur ) and `` ktênos '' ( beast ) . this was because it was first described based on its femur . it was originally discovered in 1956. it included a few other bones that later turned out to belong to a different dinosaur .
