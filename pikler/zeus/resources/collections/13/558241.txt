Acanthuroidei     acanthuroidei is a suborder of perciformes , the largest order of fishes . members of this suborder have a vertically-shaped body covered with small scales . the name for the suborder comes from that of the surgeonfish ( acanthuridae ) family . it is got from the greek words `` akantha '' and `` oura '' , which loosely translate as `` thorn '' and `` tail '' .
