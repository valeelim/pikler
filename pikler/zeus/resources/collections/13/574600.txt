Spanair Flight 5022     spanair flight 5022 was a passenger flight from barcelona , spain to gran canaria via madrid . on 20 august 2008 the mcdonnell douglas md-82 operating this route crashed after take-off from runway 36l at barajas airport . 154 passengers and crew on-board died , and only leaving only 18 survivors . the cause of the crash was pilot error due to failure to configure the flaps and slats for take-off .
