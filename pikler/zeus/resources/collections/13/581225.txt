Damián Iguacén Borau     damián iguacén borau ( born february 12 , 1916 ) is a spanish bishop of the roman catholic church . he is the oldest spain roman catholic bishop . iguacén borau was born in fuencalderas , spain . he was ordained a priest on june 7 , 1941. he was the bishop of the barbastro diocese since october 11 , 1970. later , he was made diocese of teruel and albarracín on september 23 , 1974 and then to diocese of tenerife on august 14 , 1984 where he remained until his retirement on june 12 , 1991. iguacén borau turned 100 in february 2016. other websites . - catholic hierarchy - ternerife site
