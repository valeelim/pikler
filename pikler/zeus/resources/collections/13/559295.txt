Computational linguistics     computational linguistics is a field of linguistics that deals with making computers understand human language . some of the biggest sub-fields of computational linguistics are : speech recognition , which is a computer program that listens to people talk and writes down what they said speech synthesis , which is a computer program that takes writing and reads it out loud machine translation , which is a computer program that turns one language into a different one dialog systems , which is a computer program that talks back-and-forth with a human to help them do something
