Bernard Bosson     bernard bosson ( 25 february 1948 – 16 may 2017 ) was a french politician and lawyer . he served as minister of transport , minister of tourism , and minister of public works under prime minister édouard balladur from 1993 to 1995. he was a member of the 12th french national assembly , representing haute-savoie as a member of the union for french democracy . he was also the mayor of annecy . bosson died in hospital in lyon , france on 16 may 2017 from a long-illness , aged 69 .
