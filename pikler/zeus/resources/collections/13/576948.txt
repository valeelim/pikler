GreenLeft     greenleft , dutch : `` groenlinks '' ( shortened `` gl '' ) , is a dutch political party . greenleft is a leftist party and supporting environmental protection . it was established in 1989 as a merger of the communist party of the netherlands ( cpn ) , evangelical people 's party ( evp ) , political party of radicals ( ppr ) and pacifist socialist party ( psp ) . political leader is jesse klaver . number of seats in the 2017 elections : 14 ( +10 ) .
