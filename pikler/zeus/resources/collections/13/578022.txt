Sivaganga district     sivaganga district is an administrative district of tamil nadu state in southern india . it is bounded by pudukkottai district on the northeast , tiruchirapalli district on the north , ramanathapuram district on south east , virudhunagar district on south west and madurai district on the west.the major town in the district is the city of karaikudi . as of 2011 , the district had a population of 1,339,101 with a sex-ratio of 1,003 females for every 1,000 males .
