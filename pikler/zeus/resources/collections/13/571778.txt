Wiesław Adamski     wiesław adamski ( 26 july 1947 – 10 february 2017 ) was a polish sculptor . he was best known for his work on small sculptures , medals , portraits and blacksmithing . his works had been shown in helsinki , poltava , berlin , madrid , washington , warsaw , rotterdam and paris . one famous work by him is a potato monument in biesiekierz . he was born in szczecinek county . adamski died on 10 february 2017 in wałcz , at the age of 69 .
