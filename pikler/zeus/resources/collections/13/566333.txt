Nata de coco     nata de coco is a chewy , translucent , jelly-like food produced by the fermentation of coconut water , which gels through the production of microbial cellulose by `` acetobacter xylinum '' . originating in the philippines , `` nata de coco '' is most commonly sweetened as a candy or dessert , and can accompany a variety of foods , including pickles , drinks , ice cream , puddings , and fruit mixes .
