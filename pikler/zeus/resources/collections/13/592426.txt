Sinestro     thaal sinestro ( ) is a fictional supervillain appearing in american comic books published by dc comics . sinestro is a former green lantern corps member who was dishonorably discharged for abusing his power . he is the archenemy of green lantern and founder of the sinestro corps , though he occasionally has acted in anti-heroic roles as well . the character was created by john broome and gil kane , and first appeared in `` green lantern '' ( vol . 2 ) # 7 ( august 1961 ) . in 2009 , ign ranked sinestro as the 15th greatest comic book villain of all time . mark strong portrays sinestro in the live-action movie `` green lantern '' .
