Angelica Ross     angelica ross is an american activist for transgender rights . she started transtech social enterprises to help transgender people . in 2014 , angelica ross started the transtech social enterprises organization in chicago . transtech is a training and design organization . it trains and contracts transgender people in technology . transtech is a project of allied media projects , an ngo in detroit . according to ross , who transitioned in 2000 , the time of gender transition can be difficult . some people need work but do not have the skills . other people have skills , but can not find work because of discrimination . if people can telecommute , they can work during the time of gender transition . in 2016 , ros received a visibility award from the human rights campaign . other websites . - angelica ross 's website
