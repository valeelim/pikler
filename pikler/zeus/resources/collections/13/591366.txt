Ekamai bus terminal     the ekamai bus terminal ( sometimes transcribed as ekkamai- ) is a bus terminal ( or bus station ) on sukhumwit road in bangkok . the station is in khlong toei district ( of bangkok ) . in july 2017 , a decision had been made to move the terminal , according to media ; where the terminal will be moved to , media has not reported ( as of july 2017 ) . other names . - bangkok bus terminal ( eastern ) '' is written on signs ( both with thai and english ) - on the roof of the terminal and in front of the entrance . - ekkamai eastern bus terminal
