Dracorex     dracorex is a genus of pachycephalosaur dinosaur . it lived 65.565 million years ago in the hell creek formation in the united states . its name `` dracorex hogswartsia '' means 'dragon king of hogwarts ' . this specimen and that of `` stygimoloch '' may be juvenile `` pachycephalosaurus '' . the skull and four cervical vertebrae are all that was found .
