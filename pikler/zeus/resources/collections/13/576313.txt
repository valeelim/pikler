École nationale supérieure de formation de l’enseignement agricole    école nationale supérieure de formation de l ’ enseignement agricole école nationale supérieure de formation de l ’ enseignement agricole is a renowned graduate agricultural school in france . it is in a campus of the université fédérale toulouse midi-pyrénées ( southern france ) . it trains teacher in agronomy . ensfea also offers bachelor ’ s degree , master ’ s and phds . école nationale supérieure de formation de l ’ enseignement agricole was founded as école nationale de formation agronomique in 1963. the new name has been implemented in 2016. research activities at ensfea relate to the following topics : - rural dynamics laboratory - biological evolution and diversity research laboratory - economics , policies and social systems laboratory - education sciences laboratory further reading . - official website
