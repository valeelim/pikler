Monstera deliciosa     monstera deliciosa is a species of the genus monstera , which is commonly found in tropical central america . the term deliciosa in the species name means delicious , and refers to the edible fruit of the plant . the plant is a vine ( a climbing plant ) . originally , the plant was found the mexican states of oaxaca , veracruz , and chiapas . it also grows in costa rica , guatemala , and panama . it is also commonly grown as a houseplant . wild plants can now also be found in the southern united states ( mainly florida ) , malaysia , india , australia and the western part of the mediterranean basin ( portugal , morocco , and madeira ) .
