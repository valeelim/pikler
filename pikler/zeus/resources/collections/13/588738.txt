Gene Reynolds     gene reynolds ( born april 4 , 1923 ) is an american actor turned award-winning television writer , director , and producer . he was one of the producers of the popular tv series `` m*a*s*h '' . reynolds appeared in `` in old chicago '' ( 1937 ) , `` captains courageous '' ( 1937 ) , `` love finds andy hardy '' ( 1938 ) , `` boys town '' ( 1938 ) , `` they shall have music '' ( 1939 ) , `` santa fe trail '' ( 1940 ) , `` eagle squadron '' ( 1942 ) and `` the country girl '' ( 1954 ) and on television series like `` i love lucy '' , `` armstrong circle theatre '' , `` whirlybirds '' , and `` hallmark hall of fame '' .
