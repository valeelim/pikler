Peter Hall (director)    peter hall ( director ) sir peter reginald frederick hall cbe ( born 22 november 1930 ) is a british director , playwright and actor . in 1955 he introduced london audiences to the work of samuel beckett with the uk premiere of `` waiting for godot '' . hall founded the royal shakespeare company ( 1960–68 ) and went on to build an international reputation in theatre , opera , movie and television . he was director of the national theatre ( 1973–88 ) and artistic director of glyndebourne festival opera ( 1984-1990 ) . he formed the peter hall company ( 1998-2011 ) and became founding director of the rose theatre , kingston in 2003. throughout his career , he has been a vociferous champion of public funding for the arts . other websites . - the company : a biographical dictionary of the rsc : online database - peter hall video at web of stories - fathom biography - godot almighty by peter hall - interview with peter hall by bruce duffie , november 5 , 1987 ( about opera )
