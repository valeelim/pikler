Orri Vigfússon     orri vigfússon ( 10 july 1942 – 1 july 2017 ) was an icelandic entrepreneur and environmentalist . his work was to `` restore the abundance of wild salmon that formerly existed on both sides of the north atlantic '' . he was born in siglufjörður , iceland . in 2004 time magazine named him a `` european hero '' . he was awarded the goldman environmental prize in 2007 for his efforts on saving endangered species . vigfússon died on 1 july 2017 in reykjavik , iceland of lung cancer at the age of 74 .
