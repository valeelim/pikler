Brahmanas     the brahmanas are a collection of indian books . the books give more information on the vedas , the four holiest books of the hindu religion . the `` brahmanas '' give instructions on the proper performance of rituals . they also explain the original symbolic meanings . each vedic `` shakha '' ( school ) has its own `` brahmana '' . many `` brahmana '' texts existed in ancient india . most of these have been lost .
