Philippine Association for Language Teaching     the philippine association for language teaching is an academic and professional association that helps teachers of english as a foreign language ( efl ) . it is often called palt for short . its offices are at the college of education of the university of the philippines in quezon city . palt started in 1960. palt has a convention every year with papers , keynote speakers , workshops , and social events . its first international convention was in 2001. palt has relationships with several other language teaching associations including : - english teachers ' association of the republic of china ( eta-roc ) - far eastern english language teachers association ( feelta ) of russia - japan association for language teaching - korea teachers of english to speakers of other languages - tesol international association - thailand tesol ( thaitesol ) other websites . - palt home page - palt on facebook
