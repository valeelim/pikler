Rebus     a rebus is a picture , letter , number , or symbol that is used to represent a different word that has a similar pronunciation . in english , if a person was writing the sentence `` i see you tonight '' in a text message , the person might write `` ⊙ c u 2nite '' , in which the symbol `` ⊙ '' , which is a picture of an eye , represents the word `` i '' because it sounds the same , the letter `` c '' represents the word `` c '' , the letter `` u '' represents the word `` you '' , the number `` 2 '' represents the word `` to '' , and the word `` nite '' is represents the word `` night '' .
