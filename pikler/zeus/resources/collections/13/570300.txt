Predrag Matvejević     predrag matvejević ( 7 october 1932 – 2 february 2017 ) was a bosnian croatian writer and political activist . his book , `` mediterranean breviary : a cultural landscape '' ( 1987 ) , was a bestseller in many european countries . it was also translated into more than 20 languages . he was born in mostar . matvejević died on 2 february 2017 in zagreb at the age of 84 .
