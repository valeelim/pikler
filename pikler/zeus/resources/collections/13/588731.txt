Gunter Gabriel     gunter gabriel ( born günter caspelherr ; 11 june 1942 – 22 june 2017 ) was a german singer , musician and composer . gabriel became famous in germany as singer of schlager songs . he published `` wer einmal tief im keller saß . erinnerungen eines rebellen '' with oliver flesch in 2009. gabriel died on 22 june 2017 in hannover from complications of a fall at the age of 75 .
