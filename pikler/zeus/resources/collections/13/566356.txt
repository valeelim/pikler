Jordi Pagans i Monsalvatje     jordi pagans i monsalvatje ( 18 april 1932 – 4 january 2017 ) was a spanish painter . he was born in barcelona , spain . he was known for his traditional figurative and realistic paintings and drawings of the landscape of cadaqués and barcelona . his career began in 1948. he retired in 2014. pagans died in barcelona , spain from congestive heart failure on 4 january 2017 , aged 84. other websites . - official website
