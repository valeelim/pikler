Bridging the Gap (Black Eyed Peas album)    bridging the gap ( black eyed peas album ) bridging the gap is the second studio album by american hip hop group black eyed peas , released on september 26 , 2000. this is their last album as `` black eyed peas '' , before they changed their name to `` the black eyed peas '' and the last album with kim hill as a member .
