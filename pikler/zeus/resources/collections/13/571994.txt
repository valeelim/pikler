M. K. Stalin     muthuvel karunanidhi stalin better known as m. k. stalin is an indian politician , present executive leader of dravida munnetra kazhagam party as his father karunanidhi was ailing illness . he became the 1st deputy chief minister of tamil nadu from 2009 to 2011.stalin is the third son of famous tamil nadu politician karunanidhi , and was born to his second wife , dayalu ammal . stalin became the minister for rural development and local administration in the government of tamil nadu after the 2006 assembly elections . on 29 may 2009 , stalin was nominated as deputy chief minister of tamil nadu by governor surjit singh barnala . stalin serves as the treasurer and the youth wing president of the dravida munnetra kazhagam . on 3 january 2013 , m. karunanidhi named him as his heir apparent , ending a long time confusion about who would take over the party reins after karunanidhi 's death . he was the mayor of chennai from 1996 to 2002 .
