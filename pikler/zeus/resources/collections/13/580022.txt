Shecky Greene     shecky greene ( born fred sheldon greenfield ; april 8 , 1926 ) is an american comedian . he is known for his nightclub performances in las vegas , where he became a headliner in the 1950s . greene has appeared in several movies , including `` tony rome '' , `` history of the world , part i '' and `` splash '' , and has guest starred on such television shows as `` mad about you '' , `` laverne & shirley '' , `` love , american style '' , and `` combat !
