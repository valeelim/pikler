Fourth Netanyahu Government     the fourth netanyahu government is the 34th government of israel . it was installed on may 14 , 2015. prime minister is likud politician benjamin netanyahu . participating parties ( with seats in the 20th knesset in brackets ) : - halikud ( 30 ) - kulanu ( 10 ) - habayit hayehudi ( the jewish home ) ( 8 ) - shas ( 7 ) - yahadut hatorah hameuhedet ( united torah judaism ) ( 6 ) - yisrael beiteinu ( 5 ) the fourth netanyahu government can be characterized as a right-wing government with a great religious contribution . other websites . - fourth netanyahu government , knesset website - fourth netanyahu government , israeli ministry of foreign affairs website
