Rory McIlroy     rory mcilroy , ( born 4 may 1989 ) is a northern irish professional golfer . he won the u.s. open in 2011 , the open championship in 2012 and the pga championship in 2012 and 2014. he was world number 1 golfer for a total of 95 weeks between 2012 and 2015. mcilroy was born in holywood , county down . he was engaged to danish tennis player caroline wozniacki until their relationship ended in 2014 .
