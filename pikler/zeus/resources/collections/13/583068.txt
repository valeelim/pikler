Timo Mäkinen     timo mäkinen ( 18 march 1938 – 4 may 2017 ) was a finnish racecar driver . he was one of the original `` flying finns '' of motor rallying . he was most famous for his hat-tricks of wins in the rac rally and the 1000 lakes rally , both were won in 1973. he was born in helsinki . mäkinen died in helsinki on 4 may 2017 from myelodysplastic syndrome at the age of 79 .
