Yael Cohen-Paran     yael cohen-paran , hebrew : יעל כהן-פארן , ( born october 12 , 1973 ) is an israeli environmental activist and a politician . she is co-political leader of hatnu'a hayeruqa ( `` the green movement '' ) . cohen-paran served in unit 8200 , the signal intelligence unit of the israel defense forces . she studied physics at the hebrew university of jerusalem and public policy at tel aviv university . she was founder and president of the israel energy forum , and participated in various united nations climate change conferences . later on she went into politics by becoming a member of hatnu'a hayeruqa . due to the cooperation of this party with hatnu'a and haavoda as the zionist union she became a member of knesset in 2015. references . - bio at knesset website
