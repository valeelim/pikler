Nabi Tajima     tajima became the oldest living person in japan on 27 september 2015 , upon the death of a 115-year-old anonymous woman who was living in tokyo . tajima was born in araki in the westernmost part of kikaijima island . she lives in kikai . she has 9 children ( 7 sons and 2 daughters ) . as of september 2011 , she had 28 grandchildren , 56 great-grandchildren and 35 great-great-grandchildren . as of september 2015 , she had over 140 descendants , including great-great-great grandchildren .
