Emil Wojtaszek     emil wojtaszek ( 27 august 1927 – 17 june 2017 ) was a polish politician . he served as the minister of foreign affairs of the people 's republic of poland from 1976 to 1980. he was a member of the polish united workers ' party . he was born in kraków , poland . wojtaszek died on 17 june 2017 in warsaw , aged 89 .
