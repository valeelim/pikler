Thaanaa Serndha Koottam     thaanaa serndha koottam ( ) is an upcoming tamil-language action-thriller film written and directed by vignesh shivan and produced by k. e. gnanavel raja . cast . - suriya sivakumar - keerthy suresh - senthil - k. s. ravikumar - saranya ponvannan - nandha - ramya krishnan - rj balaji - thambi ramaiah - sathyan - kovai sarala - anandaraj - suresh chandra menon the film is produced by k. e. gnanavel raja 's studio green along with suriya 's 2d entertainment and music by anirudh , with whom he collaborated for `` naanum rowdy dhaan '' ( 2015 ) . production began on 1 november 2016 .
