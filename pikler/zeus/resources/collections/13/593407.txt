Paul Stookey     noel paul stookey ( born december 30 , 1937 ) is an american singer-songwriter . stookey is known by his stage name , `` paul '' , in the folk trio peter , paul and mary . he has been known by his first name , `` noel '' , throughout his life . he continues to work as a singer and an activist , performing as a solo artist , and occasionally performing with peter yarrow . other websites . - peter , paul and mary official website
