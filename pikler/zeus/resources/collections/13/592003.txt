Max Gallo     max gallo ( ; 7 january 1932 – 18 july 2017 ) was a french writer , historian and politician . his parents were italian immigrants . gallo 's early career was in journalism . at the time he was a communist ( until 1956 ) . in 1974 , he joined the socialist party . on april 26 , 2007 , he became a candidate to become a member of the académie française . he was elected to the académie française on 31 may 2007 .
