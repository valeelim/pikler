Zootopia     zootopia ( also known as zootropolis ) is an american computer-animated comedy-adventure movie that was released on march 4 , 2016 in the united states . it was made by walt disney animation studios as its 55th movie , and it was released by walt disney pictures . the movie made over a billion dollars in the worldwide box office and became one of the highest-grossing movies of 2016. the movie is about a police rabbit named judy who has to solve a hard mystery , and has nick , a red fox , for her assistant . judy at first does not like nick , but the two get along and then work together to solve the case . `` zootopia '' won the academy award , golden globe , critics ' choice movie award and annie award for best animated feature film .
