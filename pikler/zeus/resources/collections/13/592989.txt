Magnus Böcker     magnus böcker ( august 31 , 1961 – july 27 , 2017 ) was a swedish businessman . he was born in stockholm , sweden . he was the executive chairman of blibros capital partners , an investment company based in singapore and stockholm . he was also chairman and co-founder of tryb . böcker died at his home in singapore of cancer on july 27 , 2017 at the age of 55. other websites . - profile on linkedin - magnus böcker collected news and commentary at `` bloomberg '' - magnus böcker collected news and commentary at `` the wall street journal ''
