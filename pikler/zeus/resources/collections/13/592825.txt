Montreux     montreux ( ) is a city and municipality of the district of riviera-pays-d'enhaut in the canton of vaud in switzerland . in 2015 , 26,433 people lived there . in 1295 , the bishop of sion sold the parish of montreux to girard of oron . in 1798 , napoleon liberated the region from the bernese . baugy , brent , caux , chailly-sur-montreux , chamby , chaulin , chêne , chernex , clarens , collonge , cornaux , crin , fontanivent , glion , jor , le châtelard , les avants , les planches , mont-fleuri , pallens , pertit , planchamp , sonzier , tavel , territet , vernex , villard-sur-chamby and vuarennes . other websites . - official website - official website of the montreux jazz festival
