1931 China floods     1931 china floods or the 1931 yellow river flood happened in china is the most worst deadliest natural disaster by death toll . the 1931 floods were a series of floods that occured in china in the year 1931.about 145,000-3.7 million people were said to be dead in this series of floods . in the period of 1928-30 china faced a severe drought.due to some abnormal weather conditions heavy snowstorms and heavy rainfall occured in the parts of central china.the water levels increased in the rivers . the rain grew heavier in the months of july and august in 1931.china faced nine cyclones in the month of july 1931 alone . the death resulted as per the chinese sources indicate 145,000 died due to overflow of yangtze river.western sources say a death toll of 3.7 million-4 million peoples.in july alone , four weather stations along the yangtze river reported rain totaling over for the month . the yangtze and huai river floods soon reached nanjing , the capital of china at the time . the city , located on an island in a massive flood zone , suffered catastrophic damage.millions died of drowning while some died due to starvation , from waterborne diseases such as cholera and typhus.the high-water mark was reached on 19 august at hankou town in wuhan , with the water level exceeding above normal.on the evening of 25 august 1931 , the water rushing through the grand canal washed away dikes near gaoyou lake . some 200,000 people drowned in their sleep in the resulting deluge .
