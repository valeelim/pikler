All for You (Janet Jackson song)    all for you ( janet jackson song ) `` all for you '' is a song recorded by american singer janet jackson . it is from her album , also called `` all for you '' . the song was released on march 13 , 2001. it became her tenth number-one song in the united states . it also reached number one in canada and the top three in france and the united kingdom . it reached the top ten in several other countries . it finished as the number-three song of 2001 in the united states . the song samples the 1980 song `` the glow of love '' by the soul group change . the song shows jackson dancing in a train station and in a city with a group of dancers .
