The Land Of Israel Garden     the land of israel garden ( in hebrew : gan eretz yisrael ) is a public garden located in terminal 3 of ben-gurion international airport and open in 2004. the garden was designed by the architect shlomo aronson and was lead by the architect ram karmi . the garden is symbolised the view of the land of israel and the plants of israel . in 2005 the ben-gurion airport central garden won an award of honor in the professional general design category from the american society of landscape architects .
