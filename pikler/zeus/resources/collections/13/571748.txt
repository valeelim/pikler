Yitzhak Livni     yitzhak livni ( 17 october 1934 – 12 february 2017 ) was an israeli media personality and journalist . he served as editor of the magazines `` bamahane nahal '' ( 1956–1961 ) and `` bamahane '' ( 1961–1971 ) . he was the head of idf 's radio station galatz ( 1968–1974 ) . he was also the ceo of the israel broadcasting authority ( 1974–1979 ) . livni was born in łódź , poland . he moved to tel aviv as a child . he was married to poet dahlia ravikovitch in the 1960s . he was later married to knesset member eti livni . they had three children . livni died on 12 february 2017 , at the age of 82 .
