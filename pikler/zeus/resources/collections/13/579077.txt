Mild cognitive impairment     mild cognitive impairment ( often shortened to mci ) is a medical condition affecting the brain . people suffering from it have problems remembering things . it is normal , that with age , people become more forgetful . people with mild cognitive impairment forget more than is common for their age and education . in general , this does not pose a problem in everyday life . mild cognitive impairment can occur as one of the first symptoms of dementia or alzheimer 's disease . the exact cause of mci is unknown . there is n special treatment for mci .
