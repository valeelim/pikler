Chandolin     chandolin is a village in the municipality of anniviers in the district of sierre in the canton of valais in switzerland . until 2009 it was a municipality . on 1 january 2009 the former municipalities of chandolin , ayer , grimentz , saint-jean , saint-luc and vissoie merged into the new municipality of anniviers . it has at least one museum : the ella maillart museum previously was the home of travel writer ella maillart .
