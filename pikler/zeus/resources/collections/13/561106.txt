Carlos S. Camacho     carlos sablan camacho ( born february 27 , 1937 ) was the first elected governor of the commonwealth of the northern mariana islands , a commonwealth of the united states . he served as governor from january 9 , 1978 through january 11 , 1982. he was a member of the democratic . camacho was born in saipan , northern mariana islands . he studied at the university of hawaii and at the fiji school of medicine .
