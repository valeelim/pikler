Jacqueline Naze Tjøtta     jacqueline andrée naze tjøtta ( 1 june 1935 – 9 march 2017 ) was a french-born norwegian mathematician . she became the first female mathematical sciences professor in norway . naze was born in montpellier , france . she graduated in mathematics from the aix-marseille university and sorbonne , paris . she married norwegian mathematician sigve tjøtta in 1964. naze was appointed professor in applied mathematics at the university of bergen from 1966. her research interests have focused on kinetic theory , magnetohydrodynamics and theoretical acoustics . naze died in oslo , norway on 9 march 2017 , aged 81 .
