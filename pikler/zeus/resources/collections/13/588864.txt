Italian invasion of French Somaliland     the italian invasion of french somaliland was an italian campaign during world war ii . in summer 1940 the italians occupied the western areas of french somaliland , while attacking southern france . the armistice stopped their advance . in early 1941 they withdraw from the areas occupied , because of the british attacks on italian east africa ( eritrea , somalia & ethiopia ) . other pages . - italian conquest of british somaliland bibliography . - rovighi , alberto . `` le operazioni in africa orientale ( in italian ) '' . stato maggiore esercito , ufficio storico . roma , 1952 .
