Concha Ibáñez     concha ibáñez escobar , also , conxa ibáñez ( born 1926 ) is a catalan painter and writer . she is known as a landscape artist . she has painted scenes in catalonia , castile , andalusia , the balearic islands , the canary islands , venice , greece , maghreb , cuba , new york city . her illustrations in oil or engraving accompanied the works of the writers baltasar porcel , miquel de palol , marta pessarrodona , cesareo rodriguez-aguilera and josep maria carandell . ibáñez was born in canet de mar , catalonia .
