Herbert Mies     herbert mies ( 23 february 1929 – 14 january 2017 ) was a german politician . he joined the communist party of germany in 1945. he was the chairman of the party from 1973 to 1989. he was a winner of the 1985–86 lenin peace prize . he was born in mannheim , baden-württemberg . mies died on 14 january 2017 in mannheim at the age of 87 .
