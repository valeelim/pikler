Nick Galifianakis (politician)    nick galifianakis ( politician ) nick galifianakis ( born july 22 , 1928 ) is an american politician . he was the former democratic u.s. congressman from north carolina . he served as a representative from 1967 through 1973. galifianakis was born in durham , north carolina . he studied at duke university . galifianakis is the uncle of comedian zach galifianakis .
