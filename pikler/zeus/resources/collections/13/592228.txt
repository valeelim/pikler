Sword of Grossenwieden     the sword of grossenwieden is a sword that was found in germany , in 2012. scientists believe that it was made at the workshop that built ulfberht swords that were not fake . the sword was found in gravel from the river weser , at grossenwieden ( or großenwieden , which is part of the city hessisch-oldendorf in niedersachsen . `` die gefäßteile '' [ or the guard and pommel ] contains sheet metal containing an alloy of tin and lead . the lead seems to originate from a source that mostly was emptied during the age of the romans . the weight is close to one kilogram . literature . - wilfried schmücking–goldmann : `` deutsches schwert für wikinger . fund aus der weser : archäologen präsentieren schwert aus dem frühen mittelalter .
