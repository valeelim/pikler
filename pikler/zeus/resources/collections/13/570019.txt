Queenstown New Zealand     queenstown ( ) is a resort town in otago in the south-west of new zealand 's south island . it has a population of 13 200 ( 2015 ) . the town is built around an inlet called queenstown bay on lake wakatipu . it has spectacular views of nearby mountains . these include the remarkables , cecil peak , walter peak , ben lomond and queenstown hill . it was named after queen victoria .
