Homebase     homebase is a british home improvement retailer and garden centre in the united kingdom . its main offices are in milton keynes , buckinghamshire , england . as of december 2016 , homebase has 255 shops in the united kingdom . it was founded by the supermarket chain sainsbury 's and belgian retailer gb-inno-bm in 1979 , as sainsbury 's homebase . in december 2000 , sainsbury 's sold the homebase chain . in november 2002 , homebase was sold again , to gus plc , where it became part of argos retail group ( later renamed as home retail group ) . in january 2016 it was announced that australian retailer wesfarmers would acquire homebase . the transfer of ownership to wesfarmers took place on 27 february 2016 .
