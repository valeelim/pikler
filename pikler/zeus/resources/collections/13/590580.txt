Mr. Trash Wheel     mr. trash wheel also known as the inner harbor trash wheel , is a trash interceptor . it removes trash from the inner harbor in baltimore , maryland . it is powered by water and solar power . it moves through the water and sorts trash into a conveyor belt that empties it into a dumpster on the vessel . mr. trash wheel removes garbage from the inner harbor . the garbage comes from the streets flowing into the water , particularly during storms . usually the current from the jones falls river powers the wheel 's movement . when the stream is slow it is powered by solar panels .
