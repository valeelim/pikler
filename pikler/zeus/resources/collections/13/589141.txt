Olga Feliú     olga feliú segovia ( 20 august 1932 – 25 june 2017 ) was a chilean politician and lawyer . she was appointed to the senate in 1990 by the supreme court in 1990 and served until 1998 , when she was replaced by enrique silva cimma . she was born in santiago de chile . feliú died on 25 june 2017 in santiago de chile from a long-illness at the age of 84 .
