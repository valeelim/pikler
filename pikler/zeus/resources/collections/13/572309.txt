Jenny Slate     jennifer sarah `` jenny '' slate ( born march 25 , 1982 ) is an american comedian , actress , voice actress and author . she is best known for her role as donna stern in `` obvious child '' , as well as being the co-creator of the `` marcel the shell with shoes on '' short movies and children 's book series . she is also known for her season as a cast member on `` saturday night live '' from 2009 to 2010 and for her appearances in shows such as `` house of lies '' , `` married '' , `` parks and recreation '' , `` bob 's burgers '' , `` hello '' `` ladies '' , `` kroll show '' , and `` girls '' . in 2017 , she voiced harley quinn in `` the lego batman movie '' . slate was born in milton , massachusetts . she studied at milton academy and columbia university . in 2012 , slate married dean fleischer-camp . they separated in 2016 .
