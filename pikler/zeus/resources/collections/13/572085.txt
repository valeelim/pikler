Ricardo Arias Calderón     ricardo arias calderón ( 4 may 1933 – 13 february 2017 ) was a panamanian politician . he was a member of the christian democratic party . he served as the first vice president of panama from 1989 to 1992. arias was born in panama city . he studied at yale and the sorbonne . he was a roman catholic . he was married to theresa and had four children . arias died on 13 february 2017 in panama city , at the age of 83 .
