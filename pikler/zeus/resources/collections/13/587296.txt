Paignton Zoo     paignton zoo is a zoo located in devon , england . it was opened in 1923. the west part of paignton zoo includes flamingos , ducks , cranes , blind cave fish , pittas , geckos , budgerigars , guinea pigs , spider monkeys , echidnas and lesser adjaunt storks . the north of the zoo consists mainly of a crocodile house , birds , kangaroos , tortoises , camels and zebras . the east of paignton zoo holds elephants , giraffes , maned wolves , mandrill , cheetahs rhinoceroses and coatis . the south consists of lions , tigers , an off-show kiwi , parrots , and other aviaries
