Wohlen bei Bern     wohlen bei bern is a municipality in the administrative district of bern-mittelland in the canton of berne in switzerland . it is made of 12 villages : hinterkappelen , illiswil , innerberg , möriswil , murzelen , oberdettigen , säriswil , steinisweg , uettligen , unterdettigen , weissenstein and wickacker . other websites . - official website
