Brad Ausmus     bradley david ausmus ( born april 14 , 1969 ) is the manager of the detroit tigers . he previously played as a catcher for the san diego padres , detroit tigers , houston astros , and los angeles dodgers . he made his major league baseball debut in 1993 and retired from playing after the 2010 season . he was hired as manager by detroit before the 2014 season . other websites . - managerial record at baseball-reference.com
