Willi Stoph     willi stoph ( july 9 , 1914 in berlin - april 13 , 1999 in berlin ) was an east german politician . stoph was prime minister of east germany from 1964 to 1973 and from 1976 to 1989. in between , from 1973 to 1976 , he was chairman of the state council of the german democratic republic ( president ) . references . - ddr biographies
