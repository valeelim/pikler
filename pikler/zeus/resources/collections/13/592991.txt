Les Murray (broadcaster)    les murray ( broadcaster ) les james murray am ( lászló ürge ; 5 november 1945 – 31 july 2017 ) was a hungarian-australian sports journalist , soccer broadcaster and analyst . he was born in budapest . he was the host of `` the world game '' on sbs television . he retired in july 2014. he has been inducted into the ffa 's football hall of fame . murray died on 31 july 2017 in sydney , australia after a long-illness at the age of 71. other websites . - les murray at random house australia - interview with les murray , sbs soccer announcer - les murray 's retraction of published allegations against lucas neill
