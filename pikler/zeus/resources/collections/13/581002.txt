Aditya I     aditya i or aditya chola , the son of vijayalaya chola , is the second ruler of the medieval cholas . he extended the chola empire by the conquest of the pallavas and occupied the western ganga kingdom . in the year 903 ce , aditya chola killed the pallava king aparajita and annexed the pallava territories with that of the cholas . he conquered kongu land from the pandyas . he died in 907 ce in tondaimannarur .
