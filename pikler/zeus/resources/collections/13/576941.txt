ChristianUnion     the christianunion , dutch : `` christenunie '' ( shortened `` cu '' ) , is a dutch political party . the christianunion is an orthodox christian democratic party . it was established in 2001 as a merger of the reformed political alliance ( gpv ) and the reformatory political federation ( rpf ) . political leader is gert-jan segers . number of seats in the 2017 elections : 5 ( / ) .
