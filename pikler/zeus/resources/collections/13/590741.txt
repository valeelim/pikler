Adriana Sivieri     adriana sivieri ( born 21 june 1918 ) is a retired argentine-born italian movie actress . she also worked in radio and as a voice actor , dubbing foreign movies for release in italy . she was known for her roles in `` vanity '' ( 1947 ) , `` bitter rice '' ( 1949 ) and in `` at the edge of the city '' ( 1953 ) . sivieri was born in buenos aires .
