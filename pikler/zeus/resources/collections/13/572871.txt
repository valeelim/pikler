Martin Schulz     martin schulz ( born 20 december 1955 ) is a german social democratic ( spd ) politician . he was the president of the european parliament from 2012 to 2017. since march 2017 , he serves as the chairman of the social democratic party . before to his presidency of the european parliament , he was the leader of its progressive alliance of socialists and democrats group . elected as european parliament president in 2012 , schulz was re-elected on 1 july 2014. on 17 january 2017 , he was succeeded by antonio tajani of the epp . in november 2016 , he announced he would not seek a third term , but instead would stand in 2017 as the spd candidate for the german chancellorship . in january 2017 , schultz became the official sdp candidate for the german chancellorship .
