Tony Earl     anthony scully earl ( born april 12 , 1936 ) is a american politician and a member of the democratic party . earl served as the 41st governor of wisconsin from 1983 until 1987. he graduated from michigan state university in 1958 and earned a j.d . from the university of chicago . after four years in the navy , including two years as a legal officer , earl made his way to wisconsin in 1965 .
