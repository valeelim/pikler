Samaira Sandhu     samaira sandhu is a former beauty queen , actress and an author . she debuted in the tamil film `` dhayam '' which was released on march 24 , 2017. samaira was born in punjab , india and lived her life in chandigarh . she was completed b.tech and mba and worked for some time in a multinational company as a software engineer . she was an active participant of theater and sports during college and school . in 2015 , she turned her profession from a software engineer in to a beauty queen and then an actor . samaira wrote her first novel `` heaven in a hell '' in 2015. samaira sandhu is the brand ambassador of two ngos , chandigarh round table ( crt ) and handicapped children and women association ( hcwa ) .
