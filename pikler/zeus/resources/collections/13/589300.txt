Valentín Pimstein     valentín pimstein weiner ( august 9 , 1925 – june 27 , 2017 ) was a chilean producer of telenovelas . he was born in santiago de chile . he moved to mexico , where he began to work in producing telenovelas . his most well known works are `` maría la del barrio '' , `` marimar '' , and `` carrusel '' . pimstein died in santiago de chile from respiratory arrest on june 27 , 2017 at the age of 91 .
