Robert A. McDonald     robert alan mcdonald ( born june 20 , 1953 ) is the eighth united states secretary of veterans affairs . he is the retired chairman , president , and ceo of procter & gamble . on july 29 , 2014 , the u.s. senate voted 97-0 to confirm mcdonald as president barack obama 's choice to succeed general eric shinseki as the secretary of veterans affairs . on july 30 , 2014 he was sworn into office .
