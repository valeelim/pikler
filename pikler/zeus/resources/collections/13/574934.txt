HaBayit HaYehudi     habayit hayehudi , hebrew : הבית היהודי , meaning `` the jewish home '' ( often called like that ) , is an israeli political party . its ideology is ( far ) right-wing conservative liberal , nationalist , modern orthodox jewish , and religious zionist . political leader is naftali bennett . habayit hayehudi was founded in 2008 as a result of a merger of three parties but in fact it is the successor of mafdal ( national religious party ) . number of seats in the 20th knesset : 8 ( -4 ) . participating in the fourth netanyahu government .
