Steve Trevor     general steven rockwell trevor is a fictional character appearing in american comic books published by dc comics . he is the sidekick and often involved in a romantic relationship with the superhero wonder woman . the character was created by william moulton marston and first appeared in `` all star comics '' # 8 ( december 1941 ) . steve trevor is a trusted friend , love interest and partner who introduces diana ( wonder woman ) to `` man 's world '' , and has served as wonder woman 's united nations liaison . the character has appeared in many adaptations of the comics . he has been voiced by actors such as tahmoh penikett , nathan fillion , and george newbern , among others in various `` wonder woman '' and `` justice league '' productions . lyle waggoner played the character in the 1970s `` wonder woman '' series . chris pine played trevor in the 2017 dc extended universe movie `` wonder woman '' . other websites . - `` justice league '' animated bio - steve trevor :
