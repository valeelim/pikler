Tryptophan     tryptophan ( trp or w ) is encoded by the codon ugg . it is an α-amino acid used in the biosynthesis of proteins . tryptophan has an α-amino group ( which is in the –nh form under biological conditions ) , and an α-carboxylic acid group ( which is in the deprotonated –coo form under biological conditions ) . it also has a side chain indole , which makes it a non-polar aromatic amino acid . tryptophan is essential to humans : the body can not synthesize it , so it must be got from the diet . tryptophan is also a precursor to the neurotransmitters serotonin and melatonin .
