Al G. Wright     alfred geoffrey `` al '' wright ( born june 23 , 1916 ) is an american bandleader and conductor . he is the director of bands emeritus at purdue university and chairman of the board of the john philip sousa foundation . he was born in london , but his family moved to the united states in the 1920s . wright became director of the purdue all-american marching band ( aamb ) in 1954 , becoming only the second person to hold the position , and served for 27 years until his retirement in 1981. wright and his wife gladys live in west lafayette , indiana . he turned 100 in june 2016. other websites . - biography on the sousa foundation website - biography on the purdue aamb website - al wright papers - special collections in performing arts at the university of maryland
