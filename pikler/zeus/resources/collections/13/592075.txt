Ariyalur taluk     ariyalur taluk is a taluk of ariyalur district of the indian state of tamil nadu . the headquarters of the taluk is the town of ariyalur . according to the 2011 census , the taluk of ariyalur had a population of 255749 with 126931 males and 128818 females . there were 1015 women for every 1000 men . the taluk had a literacy rate of 64.54. child population in the age group below 6 was 13812 males and 12795 females .
