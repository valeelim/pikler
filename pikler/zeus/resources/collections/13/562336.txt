X.509     see https : //en.wikipedia.org/wiki/x.509 in cryptography , x.509 is a common `` pki '' ( public key infrastructure ) used to manage digital certificates and public-key encryption and a key part of the transport layer security protocol used to secure web and email communication . x.509 specifies ways in which public key certificates , certificate revocation lists , attribute certificates , and a certification path validation algorithms are formatted .
