Azerbaijan Red Crescent Society     azerbaijan red crescent society is a charitable organization in azerbaijan . it is a member of the international red cross and red crescent movement . it was established on march 10 , 1920. its headquarters are in baku . their mission is `` to serve vulnerable people by mobilizing the capacity of azerbaijan red crescent and the power of humanity .
