Sulaiman Abdul Aziz Al Rajhi     sheikh sulaiman bin abdulaziz al rajhi ( arabic سليمان بن عبد العزيز الراجحي , born 1924 ) is a saudi arabian business figure and billionaire . as of 2011 , his wealth was estimated by forbes to be $ 7.7 billion , making him the 120th richest person in the world . he received the 2012 king faisal international prize for dedicating half his fortune to charity , starting an islamic bank , supporting charity work and implementing effective national projects . other websites . - sulaiman abdul aziz al rajhi - profile : sulaiman abdul aziz al-rajhi - sulaiman al rajhi university
