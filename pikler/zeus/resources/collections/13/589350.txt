Madeleine M. Kunin     madeleine may kunin ( born september 28 , 1933 ) is an american diplomat and politician . she was the 77th governor of vermont from 1985 until 1991 , as a member of the democratic party . she also served as united states ambassador to switzerland from 1996 to 1999. she was vermont 's first and , to date , only female governor as well as the first jewish governor of vermont . she was also the first jewish woman to be elected governor of a u.s. state . kunin is currently a james marsh professor-at-large at the university of vermont .
