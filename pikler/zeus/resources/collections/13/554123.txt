Miami metropolitan area     the miami metropolitan area is the metropolitan area including miami and its nearby communities . as of 2015 , there were more than six million inhabitants . this makes the miami metropolitan area the most populous in florida and the second-most populous in the southeastern united states . the miami metropolitan area includes miami-dade county , broward and palm beach counties in southeastern florida . this area is also called the gold coast . cities in the miami metropolitan area include miami itself , homestead , hialeah and miami beach .
