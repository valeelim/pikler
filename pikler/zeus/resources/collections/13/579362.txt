Sergio González Rodríguez     sergio gonzález rodríguez ( 26 january 1950 – 3 april 2017 ) was a mexican journalist and writer . he was born in mexico city . he was best known for his works on the femicides in ciudad juárez in the 1990s to 2000s , such as `` huesos en el desierto '' ( bones in the desert ) published in 2002. rodríguez also wrote novels , essays , screenplays for documentaries and was recognized with several awards in mexico and spain . rodríguez died of a heart attack in mexico city on 3 april 2017 , aged 67 .
