Rudy Boschwitz     rudolph ely `` rudy '' boschwitz ( born november 7 , 1930 ) is a former american independent-republican united states senator from minnesota . he served in the senate from december 1978 to january 1991. in his re-election campaign in 1990 , he was defeated by paul wellstone . boschwitz was born in berlin to a jewish family . his family moved from nazi germany to new york city to escape the nazis . he was raised in new rochelle , new york . he studied at new york university . boschwitz served in the united states army from 1954 through 1955. he is married to ellen boschwitz . boshwitz now serves on the board of directors of the jewish institute for national security affairs , is an aipac board member , and is a member of the council on foreign relations . other websites . - rudy boschwitz : an inventory of his papers at the minnesota historical society
