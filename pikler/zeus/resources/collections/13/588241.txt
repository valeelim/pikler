Untitled Anand L Rai film     untitled anand l rai film is an upcoming indian action comedy movie . it was written , directed and co-produced by anand l rai . shah rukh khan , katrina kaif and anushka sharma have the lead roles . this is the fourth time that shah rukh khan and anushka sharma worked together after `` rab ne bana di jodi '' ( 2008 ) , `` jab tak hai jaan '' ( 2012 ) and `` jab harry met sejal '' ( 2017 ) . it is the second time that shah ruh khan and katrina kaif worked together after `` jab tak hai jaan '' ( 2012 ) . principal photography of the movie began in may 2017 in mumbai . cast . - shah rukh khan - katrina kaif - anushka sharma - mohammed zeeshan ayyub - deepak dobriyal - rahul dev - prem khan
