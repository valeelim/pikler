Tiffany Trump     tiffany ariana trump ( born october 13 , 1993 ) is an american internet personality . she is the daughter of the president of the united states , donald trump , and his second wife marla maples . trump is a frequent poster to instagram . as of 2016 , she had more than 460,000 followers . she worked as an intern at `` vogue '' and modeled for a 2016 andrew warren fashion show during new york fashion week .
