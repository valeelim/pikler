Flags of the U.S. states     the flags of the u.s. states , territories and federal district exhibit a variety of regional influences and local histories , as well as different styles and design principles . nonetheless , the majority of the states ' flags share the same design pattern consisting of the state seal superimposed on a monochrome background , commonly blue . the most recent current state flag is that of utah ( february 16 , 2011 ) , while the most recent current territorial flag is that of the northern mariana islands ( july 1 , 1985 ) .
