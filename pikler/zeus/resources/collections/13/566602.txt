Cedarosaurus     cedarosaurus is a genus of sauropod dinosaur from the lower cretaceous epoch . it was a herbivore of unknown size . cedarosaurus was named for the cedar mountain formation in eastern utah , usa . it was a brachiosaurid sauropod that ate tall vegetation from the tree canopy . the skeleton was found in the cedar mountains the late 1990s by a team of paleontologists from the denver museum of nature and science . the skeleton was missing the neck , head and other parts . other websites . - cedarosaurus ; youtube
