Mohamed Mijarul Quayes     mohamed mijarul quayes ( ; 2 april 1960 – 11 march 2017 ) was a bangladeshi diplomat . he served as bangladesh 's ambassador to brazil from 2014 until his death . he was the bangladesh high commissioner to the court of st james 's , united kingdom from 2009 to 2014. quayes was taken to a brasília hospital in february 2017. he later died from multiple organ failure on 11 march . he was 56 .
