Wallington railway station     wallington station opened in 1847. the trains for this station are operated by southern ( train operating company ) . this station is in fare zone 5. there are two platforms for this station . platform 1 is for trains towards victoria station via crystal palace and norbury . platform 2 is for trains towards sutton ( surrey ) , epsom and epsom downs . this list shows the frequency of trains on typical off-peak hours . 6 trains to victoria station including : - 4 trains via norbury and 2 trains via crystal palace down ( southbound ) . - 4 trains to sutton ( surrey ) - 1 train to epsom - 1 train to epsom downs london buses routes 127 , 151 , 157 , 410 , 455 , 463 , 612 , 627 , 633 and s4 operates at this station . this table shows the departures from this station with the calling points and the time that is taken to get to the end of the line . ! train ! ! towards ! ! calling points ! ! approximate journey time external links . - -
