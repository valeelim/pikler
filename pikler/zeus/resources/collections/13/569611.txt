Waltenhofen     waltenhofen is a municipality in the district of oberallgäu in bavaria in germany . on 1 january 1972 , the previously independent municipality of memhölz was incorporated into waltenhofen . on january 1 1976 , niedersonthofen was added , and on april 1 , 1976 martin im allgäu was also added . the mayor has been eckhard harscher since 2006 .
