2017 Finsbury Park attack     on 19 june 2017 , at 00:15 bst , police officers responded to a vehicle running over several civilians with a reported `` numerous casualties '' in finsbury park , london . the attack occurred near finsbury park mosque where the van ran over several worshippers . the car reportedly crashed close to the finsbury park mosque , with many of the injured seen wearing muslim attire . witnesses have said this is because worshippers were leaving the mosque at the time of the incident . one person has been arrested in relation to the attack . the individual , darren osborne , has been confirmed to be the attacker . it is being reported that 10 people have been injured in the attack and one person has been confirmed to have died . a witness reported to buzzfeed news that the driver screamed that he wanted to `` kill all muslims '' .
