Alexander ALX300     the alexander alx300 ( later known as the transbus alx300 and the alexander dennis alx300 ) was a single-decker bus body . it was built by alexander and then transbus international and then alexander dennis from 1997 to 2007 respectively . it was made for low-floor bus chassis produced from the late 1990s . the body could be built on the man 18.220 chassis , man 18.240 chassis , daf sb220 chassis or volvo b10ble chassis .
