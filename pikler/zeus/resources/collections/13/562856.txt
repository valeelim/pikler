Atri     atri is a mythological hindu sage ( rishi ) and one of the seven rishis . hindu scriptures have named them as saptarishis , that is , the seven sages . the seven sages were kashyapa , atri , vashitha , vishvamitra , gautam , jamdagni , and bharadvaja . according to hindu mythology , these seven sages had become like divine beings . ancient astrology of hindus identified these seven sages with the stars of the sky , the great bear . their wives formed the pleiades .
