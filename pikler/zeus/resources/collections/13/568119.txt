American Pharoah     american pharoah ( born 2011 ; foaled february 2 , 2012 ) is an american thoroughbred racehorse . he won the american triple crown and the breeders ' cup classic in 2015. in winning all four races , he became the first horse to win the `` grand slam '' of american horse racing . he won the 2015 eclipse award for horse of the year and 2015 champion three-year-old . he was bred and owned throughout his racing career by ahmed zayat of zayat stables , trained by bob baffert , and ridden in most of his races by victor espinoza . he now stands at stud at ashford stud in kentucky .
