Jackson family     the jackson family is an american family of singers who originated in gary , indiana . performing as members of the jackson 5 and as solo artists.they the children of joseph walter and katherine esther jackson.they were very successful in the field of popular music from the late 1960s onwards . as a group , the eldest sons jackie , tito , jermaine , marlon , randy and michael , made the family 's reputation , facilitating the subsequent success of siblings rebbie , la toya , and janet . the jackson 5 became known as the `` first family of soul '' and the first family of music in later years
