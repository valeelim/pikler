Malcolm I of Scotland     malcolm i , born 896-900 was king of scotland from 943-954 and was the successor to his 1st cousin once removed king constantine ii and was the monarch who celebrated the 100th anniversary of scottish monarchs ( 843-943 ) . donald was born between 896 and 900 somewhere in scotland , although the place and exact time of his birth is unclear like many of the monarchs before him . his reign began in 943 , 100 years after kenneth i , the first king of scotland and lasted 11 years until 954. during his reign he would marry bethoc who gave him 2 children , dub ( later to be king ) and kenneth iii ( also to be king ) . he also may of had a child named mogallus mac malcolm , prince of scots in 934 , before becoming king . malcolm died in 954 , passing the throne to indulf and later his two sons kenneth and dub . after his death many monarchs would take his name as king of alba `` rex alban '' making him `` according to some sources '' the first king of alba , instead of constantine ii .
