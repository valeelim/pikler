Ron Rifkin     ron rifkin ( born october 31 , 1939 ) is an american actor . he is best known for his roles as arvin sloane on the spy drama `` alias '' , saul holden on the american family drama `` brothers & sisters '' and district attorney ellis loew in curtis hanson 's oscar winning movie , `` l.a. confidential '' . in 1991 , he won a drama desk award . in 1998 , he won a tony award .
