Antonov Serial Production Plant     antonov is an aircraft manufacturer . it makes some of the largest aircraft in history . the antonov 225 cargo plane is 276 feet long with a wingspan of 275 feet . it has six engines . it is 37 feet longer than the airbus a380 . this plane is only used for cargo though . it is larger than the spruce goose in length but the spruce goose 's wingspan is 9.5m longer . antonov also makes passenger planes like the an-148 and the an-140 . antonov was established on may 31 , 1946 .
