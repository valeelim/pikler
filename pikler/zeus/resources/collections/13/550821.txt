Departments of Paraguay     paraguay has 17 departments and one capital district ( `` distrito capital '' ) . the departments are formed by districts . the departments are grouped in two geographic regions , separated by the paraguay river : - occidental ( western ) region or chaco , to the north of the paraguay river ( departments : alto paraguay , boquerón and presidente hayes ) - oriental ( eastern ) region , to the south of the paraguay river ( departments : alto paraná , amambay , caaguazú , caazapá , canindeyú , central , concepción , cordillera , guairá , itapúa , misiones , paraguarí , san pedro and ñeembucú ; the capital district is part of this region ) other websites . - statoids - departments of paraguay
