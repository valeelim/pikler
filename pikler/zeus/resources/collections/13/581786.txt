Volya (Bulgarian political party)    volya ( bulgarian political party ) volya ( bulgarian : `` воля '' meaning `` will '' ; previously known as dnes and the liberal alliance ) is a political party in bulgaria . it was founded on 15 july 2007 by businessman veselin mareshki . the name `` volya '' was adapted on 28 november 2016. the party is seen as having a pro-russian stance , and is also eurosceptic ( meaning they are critical of the european union ) . it is also nationalistic and populist . their best performance in the parliamentary election was in 2017 , where they received around 4 % of the national vote and won 12 out of 240 seats . other websites . - official website ( bulgarian )
