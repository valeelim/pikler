Gopal Vasudev Menon     gopal vasudev menon is an indian music composer , lyricist , singer , and director . he is best known for his works in the cinema of south india , especially in tamil cinema . he has won seven filmfare awards , six are filmfare best music director south awards and one special jury award , five cinemaa awards - best music director . gopal is best known for his musical work on tamil film `` enai noki paayum thota , ''
