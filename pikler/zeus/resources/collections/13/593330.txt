Wanda Chotomska     wanda maria chotomska ( 26 october 1929 – 2 august 2017 ) was a polish children 's writer , screenwriter and poet . she was born in warsaw . she was known for being the screenwriter of the `` jacek i agatka '' television series . her poems include : `` wiersze pod psem '' ( 1959 ) , `` siedem księżyców '' ( 1970 ) , `` tańce polskie '' ( 1981 ) , `` kram z literami '' ( 1987 ) , `` wiersze dla dzieci '' ( 1997 ) , and `` wanda chotomska dla najmłodszych '' ( 2000 ) . chotomska died in warsaw on 2 august 2017 at the age of 87. other websites . - praca zbiorowa , `` złote myśli ludzi wielkiego umysłu , talentu i serca . twórcy wizerunku polski '' , fundacja dzieciom `` zdążyć z pomocą '' , warszawa 2007
