Cupbearer     a cupbearer is traditionally the person who fills the cups and serves the wine . in a medieval court ( royal ) , the cupbearer brought his lord 's ale or wine and tasted it to make sure it was not poisoned . the cupbearer was often a person of high rank . it was a distinct honor to be a cupbearer to a king . in the bible , nehemiah was cupbearer to the artaxerxes i of persia .
