Juan Carlos Tedesco     juan carlos tedesco ( february 5 , 1944 – may 8 , 2017 ) was an argentine academic and policy maker . he served as the education minister from december 2007 to july 2009 during the cristina fernández de kirchner presidency . he was born in buenos aires . tedesco died on may 8 , 2017 in buenos aires at the age of 73. other websites . - currículum vitae - políticas de subjetividad para la igualdad de oportunidades educativas
