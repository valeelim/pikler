Léon Degrelle     léon joseph marie ignace degrelle ( june 15 , 1906 in bouillon - march 31 , 1994 in malaga ) was a belgian politician and collaborator . degrelle is the founder of the fascist party of rex in 1936. during world war ii he erected the walloon legion , which fought as a waffen-ss unit at the eastern front . after the war he managed to escape to spain , where he died in 1994 , aged 87 .
