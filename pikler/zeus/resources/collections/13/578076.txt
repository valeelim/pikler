Joanne Kyger     joanne kyger ( november 19 , 1934 – march 22 , 2017 ) was an american poet . her poetry was influenced by her practice of zen buddhism and her ties to the poets of black mountain , the san francisco renaissance , and the beat generation . kyger was born in vallejo , california . she studied at the university of california , santa barbara . she moved to san francisco in 1957. kyger has published more than twenty books of poetry and prose , including `` going on : selected poems , 1958–1980 , `` ( 1983 ) ; and , `` just space : poems , 1979-1989 '' ( 1991 ) . kyger died on march 22 , 2017 at her home in bolinas , california from lung cancer , aged 82. other websites . - `` add-verse '' a poetry-photo-video project joanne kyger participated in - joanne kyger papers mss 0730. special collections & archives , uc san diego library . - joanne kyger correspondence , 1957-1975 mss 0008. special collections & archives , uc san diego library .
