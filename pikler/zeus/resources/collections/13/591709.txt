Jean-Pierre Bernard     jean-pierre bernard ( 22 january 1933 – 7 july 2017 ) was a french movie , television and stage actor . he was born in paris . he has appeared in many french movies over a 50-year period and became known internationally for his role of a french climber named jean-paul montaigne in the 1975 movie `` the eiger sanction '' that was directed by and starred clint eastwood . bernard studied at the french national academy of dramatic arts . his french language movie appearances include : `` adelaide '' ( 1968 ) directed by jean-daniel simon , `` le soulier de satin '' ( 1985 ) directed by manoel de oliveira and `` mon ami le traître '' ( 1988 ) directed by josé giovanni . bernard died on 7 july 2017 in paris of stomach cancer , aged 84 .
