Floyd Mayweather, Jr.    floyd mayweather , jr. floyd joy mayweather jr. ( born floyd joy sinclair ; february 24 , 1977 ) is an american professional boxer . he is undefeated as a professional boxer . mayweather has won fifteen world titles and the lineal championship in four different weight classes . he won a bronze medal in the featherweight division at the 1996 olympics . he won three u.s. golden gloves titles ( at light flyweight , flyweight , and featherweight ) . he also won the u.s. national title at featherweight .
