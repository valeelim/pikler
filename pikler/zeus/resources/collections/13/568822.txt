Parliament of India     the parliament of india is the supreme legislative body of the republic of india . the parliament is composed of the president of india and the houses . it is bicameral with two houses : the rajya sabha ( council of states ) and the lok sabha ( house of the people ) . the president in his role as head of legislature has full powers to summon and prorogue either house of parliament or to dissolve lok sabha . the president can exercise these powers only upon the advice of the prime minister and his union council of ministers .
