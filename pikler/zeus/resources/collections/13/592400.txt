Jørgen Kosmo     jørgen hårek kosmo ( december 5 , 1947 – july 24 , 2017 ) was a norwegian politician . he was born in fauske , norway . from 2005 to 2013 he was auditor general of norway , after representing the labour party in the storting ( parliament ) for 20 years , of which the final 4 years as president of the storting . kosmo was minister of defence from 1993 to 1997 and minister of labour and government administration from 2000 to 2001. kosmo was in 2004 appointed county governor of telemark , but could not take over the position while he was member of parliament . from 1979 to 1985 he was member of the municipal council of horten , the last 2 years as mayor . kosmo died in oslo , norway on july 24 , 2017 of pancreatic cancer at the age of 69. other websites . - biography at the norwegian parliament
