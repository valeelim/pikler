Tabaré Hackenbruch     tabaré hackenbruch ( 26 november 1928 – 19 june 2017 ) was an uruguayan politician . he was born in canelones , uruguay . he was a member of the colorado party , he started his long political career supporting the successful candidacy of tomás berreta for president . he also worked in the newspaper `` acción '' alongside julio maría sanguinetti . hackenbruch was elected mayor of canelones department for three terms : 1985-1990 , 1995-2000 and 2000-2005 ; in the 2000 election , he had the support of several politicians from the national party . in the end he had very low approval ratings and a lot of irregularities were reported .
