Keith Campbell (biologist)    keith campbell ( biologist ) keith campbell ( 1954- 2012 ) was a british biologist , who had a key role in the creation of dolly , a cloned sheep . campell had the crucial idea of co-ordinating the stages of the `` cell cycle '' of the donor somatic cells and the recipient eggs and using diploid quiscent or `` g0 '' arrested somatic cells as nuclear donors . in fact , in 2006 ian wilmut admitted that keith campbell deserved `` 66 per cent '' of the credit for the work on dolly the sheep . campell committed suicide , aged 58 years old .
