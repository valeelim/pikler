Kansai Airport Station     kansai airport station ( 関西空港駅 , かんさいくうこうえき ) is a railway station of nankai electric railway and west japan railway company ( jr west ) in tajiri , sennan district , osaka , japan . this is the nearest station to kansai international airport . adjacent stations . - nankai electric railway other pages . - kansai airport station - nankai electric railway - kansai airport station - jr west
