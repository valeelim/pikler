Albert Bouvet     albert bouvet ( 28 february 1930 – 20 may 2017 ) was a french professional cyclist . he won paris–tours in 1956 and remained the last frenchman to win until jacky durand won in 1998. his name is also associated with paris–roubaix , as an organiser and discoverer of new sections of pavé . he was born in mellé , ille-et-vilaine . bouvet died on 20 may 2017 in paris at the age of 87 .
