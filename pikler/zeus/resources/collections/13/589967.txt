Darrall Imhoff     darrall tucker imhoff ( october 11 , 1938 – june 30 , 2017 ) was an american former professional basketball player . he spent twelve seasons in the nba ( 1960–72 ) , playing for six teams . imhoff was the starting center for the new york knicks , and played for 20 minutes in the game when wilt chamberlain scored an nba personal scoring record of 100 points . imhoff died on june 30 , 2017 in bend , oregon of a heart attack at the age of 78. other websites . - darrall imhoff profile and stats at lakersweb.com
