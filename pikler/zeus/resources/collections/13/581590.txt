Hamirani Tribe     hamirani tribe is a baloch tribe settled in balochistan ( usta muhammad ) , punjab and sindhi provinces of pakistan . many members of hamirani tribe have migrated to usa in early 90s due to lack of resources . hamirani tribe is a small tribe of 5,000 all around pakistan , usa , uae and other parts of the world . hamirani was a subclan of qasarani ( balochi : قيصرانی ) which got seperated from them due to rind and lashari war . the main hub of hamiranis is dera ghazi khan jaffrabad district of balochista .
