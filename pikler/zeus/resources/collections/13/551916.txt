Elephunk     elephunk is the third studio album by american hip hop group the black eyed peas . it was released on june 24 , 2003 , by the will.i.am music group and a & m records . although the album is the groups third album it is also the groups first in many ways ; it was the first album to feature r & b singer fergie and was the first album of the group to be billed as `` the black eyed peas '' . personnel . - the black eyed peas - will.i.am – vocals on all tracks except 11 ; backing vocals on track 11 ; moog synthesizers on tracks 1 , 4 , 5 , 8 , 9 , 10 , 11 and 13 ; drum programming on tracks 1 , 4 , 6 , 7 , 8 , 9 and 10 ; clavinet on tracks 12 and 13 ; drums and piano on track 2 ; wurlitzer electric piano on track 4 ; synthesizer on track 10 ; executive production ; production ; engineering on tracks 2 , 3 , 4 , 8 , 9 , 12 and 14 ; mixing on tracks 10 and 14 - apl.de.ap – vocals on tracks 1 , 2 , 3 , 6 , 7 , 11 , 12 and 13 ; drum programming and production on track 11 - fergie – vocals on all tracks except 6 , 7 and 12 - taboo – vocals on tracks 1 , 2 , 3 , 5 , 6 , 7 , 9 and 13 ; guitar
