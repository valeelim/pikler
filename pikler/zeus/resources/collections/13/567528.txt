Ameland     ameland is an island and municipality of the province of friesland , the netherlands . in 2016 the municipality had about 3,600 people . the municipality covers an area of 269 km² , the island an area of 59 km² . ameland makes part of the west frisian islands ( dutch : `` waddeneilanden '' ) . it lies west of the island of schiermonnikoog and east of the island of terschelling .
