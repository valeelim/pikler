Luis García Meza Tejada     luis garcía meza tejada ( born august 8 , 1929 , la paz , bolivia ) is a former bolivian dictator . in 1980 , he led a military coup d'état on president lidia gueiler tejada . he later made himself president on 17 july 1980. he left office on 4 august 1981 after being exiled for human rights violations . before becoming president , he worked closely with bolivian president hugo banzer .
