Penelope Lively     dame penelope margaret lively ( born 17 march 1933 ) is a english writer of fiction for both children and adults . she has won both the booker prize ( `` moon tiger '' , 1987 ) and the carnegie medal for british children 's books ( `` the ghost of thomas kempe '' , 1973 ) . lively is a fellow of the royal society of literature . she is also a vice-president of the friends of the british library . other websites . - audio slideshow interview about `` family album '' on the interview online
