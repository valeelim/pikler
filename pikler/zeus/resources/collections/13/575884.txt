Namakkal district     namakkal district is an administrative district in the state of tamil nadu , india . the district was divided from salem district with namakkal town as headquarters on 25-07-1996 and started to function independently from 01-01-1997. the district has seven taluks ( subdivisions ) : tiruchengode , namakkal , rasipuram , velur , sendamangalam , komarapalayam , and kolli hills . it has two revenue divisions : namakkal and tiruchengode . as of 2011 , namakkal district had a population of 1,726,601 with a sex-ratio of 986 females for every 1,000 males .
