Hans Breder     hans breder ( october 20 , 1935 – june 18 , 2017 ) was a german-american artist . his works have been presented in numerous exhibitions in the united states and internationally . he has shown with the richard feigen gallery ( 1967-1972 ) , the mitchell algus gallery , and hachmeister gallery . breder was born in herford , germany . he studied at hochschule für bildende künste hamburg in hamburg . he was married to barbara welch from 1984 until his death in 2017. breder died in iowa city , iowa on june 18 , 2017 , aged 81. other websites . - official website - intermedia : a consciousness-based process
