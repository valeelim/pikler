Ted Hibberd     thomas edward `` ted '' hibberd ( april 22 , 1926 – may 10 , 2017 ) is a canadian ice hockey player . he was a member of the ottawa rcaf flyers . he won the gold medal in ice hockey for canada at the 1948 winter olympics in st. moritz . in 2001 hibberd was honoured by the canadian forces when it was announced that the 1948 rcaf flyers were selected as canada ’ s greatest military athletes of the 20th century . hibberd died at his home in ottawa , ontario on may 10 , 2017 at the age of 91. other websites . - bio
