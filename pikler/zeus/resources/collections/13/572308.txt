Pete McCloskey     paul norton `` pete '' mccloskey , jr. ( born september 29 , 1927 ) is an american politician . he served in the u.s. house of representatives representing california from 1967 to 1983. he ran on an anti-war platform for the republican nomination for president in 1972 but was defeated by incumbent president richard nixon . in april 2007 , mccloskey switched his affiliation to the democratic party . he is a decorated united states marine corps veteran of combat during the korean war , being awarded the navy cross , the silver star , and two awards of the purple heart .
