Vully-les-Lacs     vully-les-lacs is a municipality in the district of broye-vully in the canton of vaud in switzerland . the municipality was created on july 1 , 2011 merger of the former municipalities of bellerive , constantine , chabrey , montmagny , mur , vallamand and villars-le-grand . salavaux , cotterd , bellerive , constantine , chabrey , montmagny , mur , vallamand , vallamand-dessous and villars-le-grand . other websites . - official website
