Moshe Katsav     moshe katsav , hebrew : משה קצב , ( born december 5 , 1945 in yazd , iran ) is a former israeli politician of likud . he was mayor of kiryat malakhi , a member of knesset and a ( deputy ) government minister . he was also president of israel from 2000 to 2007. from 2011 to 2016 he was in prison because of a conviction of sexual offences ( like rape ) . katsav denied the accusations . other websites . - bio at knesset website
