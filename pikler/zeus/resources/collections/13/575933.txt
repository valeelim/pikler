Shas     shas , hebrew : ש״ס , full name : shomrei sfarad , hebrew : שומרי ספרד , meaning `` ( religious ) guardians of the sephardim '' , is an israeli political party . its ideology is left-wing conservative , sephardic-mizrahic ultra-orthodox , and religious zionist . political leader is aryeh deri . shas was founded in 1984. number of seats in the 20th knesset : 7 ( -4 ) . participating in the fourth netanyahu government .
