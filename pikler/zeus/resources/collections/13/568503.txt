Trent Robinson     trent robinson ( born 15 march 1977 ) is an australian professional rugby league coach . he is the coach of the sydney roosters of the national rugby league ( nrl ) . in 2013 , his first year with the club , the roosters won a premiership . he also coached the french side catalans dragons . he also played for the wests tigers and the parramatta eels . other websites . - trent robinson at the rugby league project
