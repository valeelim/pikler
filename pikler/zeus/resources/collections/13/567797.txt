Jan Szczepański     jan antoni szczepański ( 20 november 1939 – 15 january 2017 ) was a polish boxer . he won the gold medal in the lightweight division ( – 60 kg ) at the 1972 summer olympics in munich , west germany . in the final he defeated hungary 's lászló orbán on points ( 5:0 ) . szczepanski died on 15 january 2017 , after long illness in warsaw , aged 77 .
