Wiglaf     wiglaf is a character in the epic poem beowulf . he is the orphaned son of a warrior king who enters the service of beowulf , who is now the king of the geats . he assists the warrior king in his battle against a dragon which has attacked his kingdom . beowulf is incapacitated but wiglaf comes to his aid and eventually the dragon dies after it is stabbed in the throat . the old king is severely injured and asks to be shown the dragon 's treasure . with his dying words , he crowns wiglaf as his successor ( as he does not have any biological children and all of his forebears have passed away ) . beowulf is then burned in a norse funeral and wiglaf rules the kingdom .
