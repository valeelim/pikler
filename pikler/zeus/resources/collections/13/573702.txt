Rishon LeZion     rishon lezion , hebrew : רִאשׁוֹן לְצִיּוֹן - `` first of zion '' , is a city in israel . about 244,000 people were living here in 2015. rishon lezion is situated in the gush dan region in the central district , and lies south of tel aviv . it was founded by russian jews in 1882. it ranks fourth in population and second in newly established settlements .
