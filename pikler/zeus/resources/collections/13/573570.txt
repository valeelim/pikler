Hôtel de Montesson     the hôtel de montesson was a small townhouse on the rue de la chaussée-d'antin in paris , france . it was constructed after 1770 for madame de montesson , the secret and morganatic wife of louis philippe d'orléans , duke of orléans . he had a house adjoined to montesson 's . the couple would eventually marry circa 1768 in the chapel of the `` hôtel '' . the building would be destroyed by an accidental fire in 1810 .
