Mickey Tettleton     mickey lee tettleton ( born september 16 , 1960 ) is a former major league baseball catcher for the oakland athletics ( 1984–1987 ) , baltimore orioles ( 1988–1990 ) , detroit tigers ( 1991–1994 ) and texas rangers ( 1995–1997 ) . he made two all–star teams and won three silver slugger awards . he was named after mickey mantle , who was also from oklahoma .
