Lech Trzeciakowski     lech trzeciakowski ( 24 december 1931 – 7 january 2017 ) was a polish historian . he was born in poznań , poland . trzeciakowski served as director of the western institute ( `` instytut zachodni '' ) in poznań from 1974 to 1978. trzeciakowski died on january 7 2017 in poznań of cancer , aged 86 .
