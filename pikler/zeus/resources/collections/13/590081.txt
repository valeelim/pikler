1992 Arab Nations Cup     the 1992 arab nations cup ( arabic : ) was the 6th arab nations cup tournament . egypt won the tournament after defeating saudi arabia in the final . the tournament was part of the 1992 pan arab games , but also counted for the arab nations cup . iraq was banned from the tournament because of the gulf war . statistics . tournament rankings . - rankings are based on performance , not team skill . also , these rankings are unofficial and are not based on head-to-head record . other websites . - details in rsssf
