DZBB-TV     dzbb-tv , channel 7 , is an commercial broadcast vhf television station owned by gma network in the philippines . its studios are located at the gma network center , at the corner of timog avenue and epifanio de los santos avenue in quezon city . its transmitter station is located at gma compound , tandang sora avenue , barangay culiat tandang sora , quezon city gmanetwork.com
