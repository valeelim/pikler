BOAC Flight 911     british overseas airways corporation flight 911 , was a boeing 707 that was flying from san francisco to hong kong , with stopovers at , honolulu , fukuoka , and tokyo . on the 5th of march , 1966 the aircraft broke up in mid-air and crashed into mount fuji due to faulty maintenance to the vertical stabiliser . all 113 passengers and 11 crew members on-board the plane were killed , in the 6th worst aviation accident on japanese soil .
