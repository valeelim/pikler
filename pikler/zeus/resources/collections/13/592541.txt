Formazza     formazza ( walliser german : `` pomatt '' , piedmontese : `` formassa '' ) is a `` comune '' ( municipality ) in the province of verbano-cusio-ossola in the italian region piedmont . formazza is about northeast of turin and north of verbania . it is on the border with switzerland . as of 2004 about 443 people lived there . it has an area of . the municipality of formazza contains the `` frazioni '' ( subdivisions , mainly villages and hamlets ) foppiano , antillone , fondovalle , chiesa , valdo , san michele , ponte , brendo , grovella , canza , sotto frua , la frua , and riale . formazza borders the following municipalities : baceno , bedretto ( switzerland ) , bignasco ( switzerland ) , binn ( switzerland ) , bosco/gurin ( switzerland ) , cavergno ( switzerland ) , premia , reckingen-gluringen ( switzerland ) , ulrichen ( switzerland ) . the village was founded by walser and walser german is still spoken . other websites . - official website
