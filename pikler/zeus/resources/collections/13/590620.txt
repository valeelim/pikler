Johnny Gosch     johnny gosch ( born november 12 , 1969 ) was a 12-year-old paperboy in west des monies , iowa who was abducted while delivering newspapers . in the early hours of september 5 , 1982 , he disappeared on his route and was never seen since . gosch is believed to be kidnapped , and has not been seen since . his mother got mail one morning showing the photos of her kidnapped son and three other boys .
