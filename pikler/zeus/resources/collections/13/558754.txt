CJ the DJ     cj the dj is an australian animated series that ran on abc me from december 4 , 2009 to 2010. its program creator was mark gravas . on 1 july 2010 , the australian broadcasting company released the first dvd of `` cj the dj '' , featuring the first eight episodes . cj the dj series 1 volumes 1 ( episodes 1 - 26 ) and 2 ( episodes 27 - 52 ) are available for download at the abc online store . cj the dj was released on abc iview for public viewing , though each episode was only available for viewing over a limited amount of time . brazil : gloob israel : arutz hayeladim ( סי ג'יי הדי ג'יי ) portugal : rtp2
