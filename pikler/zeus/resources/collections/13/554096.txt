Fátima Bernardes     fátima bernardes ( born september 17 , 1962 ) is a brazilian journalist and talk show host . she has worked at rede globo since 1987. in this television network she started to work in tv newscasts and became very famous . in 2011 , she trended worldwide on twitter after announcing her new professional phase as show host . according to forbes , bernardes could be the `` next oprah '' .
