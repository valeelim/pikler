Arabesque     arabesque is an artistic decoration . it uses `` surface decorations based on ... interlacing foliage , tendrils '' or plain lines . another definition is `` foliate ornament , used in the islamic world , typically using leaves ... combined with spiralling stems '' . arabesques are usually of a single design which can be 'tiled ' or repeated as many times as desired . the term `` arabesque '' is used as a technical term by art historians for decoration in islamic art from about the 9th century onwards , and european decorative art from the renaissance onwards . note that islamic geometric patterns are a different style from arabesques .
