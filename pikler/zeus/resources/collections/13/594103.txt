Jim Jefferies (comedian)    jim jefferies ( comedian ) geoff james nugent ( born 14 february 1977 ) , better known as jim jefferies , is an australian stand-up comedian , actor and writer . he is well known in the united kingdom and the united states . he also has a number of netflix specials . in 2017 , he began starring in the comedy central talk show `` the jim jefferies show '' . jefferies was born in perth , western australia . he has a son , hank , with actress kate luyben .
