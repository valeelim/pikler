Arasalar River     arasalar river is a branch river of cauvery which split into 5 different rivers when it enters into thanjavur district from tiruchirapalli . at a village named pullambadi the river cauvery has been stopped by lower anaicut or the grand anaicut ( anaicut in tamil means dam in english ) and from thiruvaiyaru ( the name of the town refers separation of 5 rivers ) the arasalar river separates from river cauvery . this river enters into the sea of bay of bengal at karaikal .
