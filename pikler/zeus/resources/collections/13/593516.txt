William P. Hobby Jr.     william pettus “ bill ” hobby jr. ( born january 19 , 1932 ) is an american democratic politician who served a record eighteen years as the 37th lieutenant governor . hobby jr. held that office from january 16 , 1973 to january 15 , 1991 for five unprecedented terms , he was the last lieutenant governor to serve a two-year term and the first elected to a four-year term when the texas constitution was amended to lengthen terms for statewide elected officeholders to four years beginning with the 1974 elections . the principal duty of the texas lieutenant governor is to preside over the texas state senate . other websites . - a guide to the william p. hobby jr. papers , 1940s-2010 a ut-austin briscoe center - william pettus hobby jr. scrapbook , 1989 texas state library and archives - hobby school of public affairs at university of houston - hobby school of public affairs announcement - hobby center for public service texas state university - hobby center for the study of texas at rice university
