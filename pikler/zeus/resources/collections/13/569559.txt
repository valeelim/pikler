Lucia Bosè     lucia bosè ( born lucia borloni ; 28 january 1931 ) is an italian actress . she was best known during the italian neorealism in the 1940s and 1950s . she appeared in the movies `` under the olive tree '' , `` story of a love affair '' ( both 1950 ) , `` the lady without camelias '' ( 1953 ) , `` death of a cyclist '' ( 1955 ) and `` harem suare '' ( 1999 ) . in 1947 , she was miss italy . bosè was born in milan . she was married to spanish bullfighter luis dominguín from 1955 to 1967. they had three children , including miguel ( b . 1956 , panama city ) . she was the grandmother of bimba bosé ( 1975–2017 ) .
