Fritz Hellwig     fritz hellwig ( 3 august 1912 – 22 july 2017 ) was a german cdu politician and european commissioner . he was born in saarbrücken , germany . before and during the war he was a member of the nazi party and the sa . after the war he served as european commissioner for research , technology and information distribution from 1967 through 1970. hellwid turned 100 in august 2012. he died on 22 july 2017 in berlin of cardiopulmonary arrest at the age of 104 .
