Black Lives Matter     black lives matter ( blm for short ) is an international activist group . they originated in the african-american community . they are against violence and systemic racism toward black people . the movement began in 2013 with the hashtag # blacklivesmatter on social media , after the acquittal of george zimmerman in the death of trayvon martin . they became famous for their street demonstrations , following the deaths of michael brown and eric garner . the earlier sparked riots and unrest in ferguson , missouri . the latter sparked protests all over the united states . since the ferguson protests , participants of black lives matter have demonstrated against other african-americans ' deaths , like freddie gray , sandra bland , laquan mcdonald , philando castile and alton sterling .
