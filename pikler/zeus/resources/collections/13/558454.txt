Babiker Awadalla     babiker awadalla ( born 2 march 1917 ) is a sudanese arab nationalist politician . he was born in gitena , white nile state . he held the position of speaker of the parliament from 1954 to 1957 , and became chief judge in 1964. in 1966 , he resigned from the court in protest of the government 's refusal to reinstate the sudanese communist party , which the court had held to be unconstitutionally banned from parliament . after 1972 , he moved to egypt , and later dublin , ireland , where he resides as of may 2017. he turned 100 in march 2017 .
