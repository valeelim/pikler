Wiesław Gołas     wiesław gołas ( born 9 october 1930 in kielce ) is a polish actor and former armia krajowa 's soldier . gołas was cast in a role of tomasz czereśniak in a widely popular tv world war ii series `` czterej pancerni i pies '' . he has appeared in a number of movies including `` ogniomistrz kaleń '' ( 1961 ) and `` dzięcioł '' ( 1970 ) . other websites . - wiesław gołas at the culture.pl . retrieved 2015-01-25 .
