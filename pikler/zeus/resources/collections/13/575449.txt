Alojz Benac     alojz benac ( plehan , derventa , october 20 , 1914 – sarajevo , march 6 , 1992 ) . was a bosnia-herzegovinian archaeologist and historian . he worked in the national museum of bosnia and herzegovina from 1947 to 1967 ( in the role of director from 1957 to 1967 ) . he then left to assume a professorship in archaeology and ancient history in the faculty of philosophy of the university of sarajevo ( 1968–78 ) . he later became the founder and first director of the centre for balkan studies , within the academy of sciences and arts of bosnia and herzegovina ( anubih ) , of which he was general secretary from 1971 to 1977 and president from 1977 to 1981 benac focused his research on prehistory within the western balkans , and undertook numerous systematic archaeological excavations on sites including arnautovići ( visoko ) , crvena stijena ( montenegro ) , hrustovača in hrustovo ( sanski most ) , obre i and ii ( kakanj ) , zecovi ( prijedor ) , zelena pećina in blagaj ( mostar ) and others .
