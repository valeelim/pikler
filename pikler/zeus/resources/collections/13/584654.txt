Fidget spinner     a fidget spinner is a toy that some people believe helps children who can become distracted . gadget world has published a blog to explain the alleged benefits . however , other people such as teachers believe that fidget spinners are a distraction . catherine hettinger , a chemical engineer , was first credited by some news stories to have been the inventor of the fidget spinner , including by media outlets such as `` the guardian '' , `` the new york times '' and the `` new york post '' . hettinger filed a patent application for a `` spinning toy '' in 1993 .
