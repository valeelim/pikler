Fred André     fred andré ( 31 may 1941 – 24 january 2017 ) was a dutch footballer . he played as a centre-back . he played from the early-1960s to the late-1970s . he was best known for playing for telstar from 1963 to 1976. he made 328 appearances for the club . he also played for volendam . he later managed telstar from 1983 to 1987. he was born in haarlem , north holland . andré died on 24 january 2017 at the age of 75 .
