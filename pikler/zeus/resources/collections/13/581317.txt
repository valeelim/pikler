Clint Hurdle     clinton merrick hurdle ( born july 30 , 1957 ) is the manager of the pittsburgh pirates . he has previously managed the colorado rockies . in 2007 , he led the rockies to the world series , which they lost to the boston red sox . before he became a manager , hurdle played baseball as an outfielder and was a member of the kansas city royals , cincinnati reds , new york mets , and st. louis cardinals .
