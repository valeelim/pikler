Tomas Milian     tomas milian ( born tomás quintín rodríguez ; 3 march 1933 – 22 march 2017 ) was a cuban american-italian actor and singer . he was known for his roles in `` the big gundown '' ( 1966 ) , `` the ugly ones '' ( 1967 ) , `` django kill ... if you live , shoot ! '' ( 1967 ) , `` face to face '' ( 1967 ) , `` run , man , run '' ( 1968 ) , `` death sentence '' ( 1968 ) , `` tepepa '' ( 1969 ) , `` compañeros '' ( 1970 ) , `` sonny and jed '' ( 1972 ) , `` life is tough , eh providence ? '' ( 1972 ) and `` four of the apocalypse '' ( 1975 ) . milian was born in havana , cuba . he studied at the actors studio in new york city . he was a bisexual . milian died at his home in miami , florida on 22 march 2017 from a stroke , aged 84. other websites . - tomás milián official site - tomás milián interview - tomas milian in the designated victim – tcm 's movie morlocks
