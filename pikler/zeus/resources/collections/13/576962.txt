People's Party for Freedom and Democracy    people 's party for freedom and democracy the people 's party for freedom and democracy , dutch : `` volkspartij voor vrijheid en democratie '' ( shortened `` vvd '' ) , is a dutch political party . the people 's party for freedom and democracy is a conservative liberal party . it was established in 1948 by a merger of the freedom party ( pvdv ) and the oud committee . political leader is mark rutte ( also prime minister since 2010 ) . number of seats in the 2017 elections : 33 ( -8 ) .
