Accession     accession is the attainment or acquisition of a position of rank or power , typically that of monarch or president . eg . `` the queen 's accession to the throne '' . accession day is a celebration . it is usually the anniversary of when a monarch takes office . the earliest record of accession celebrations are from the reign of queen elizabeth i of england . accession is not the same as coronation or inauguration . coronation and inauguration are both ceremonies which take place after the accession .
