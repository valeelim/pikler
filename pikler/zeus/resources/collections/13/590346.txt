Chris Roberts (singer)    chris roberts ( singer ) chris roberts ( christian klusáček ; march 13 , 1946 – july 2 , 2017 ) was a german schlager singer and actor . he was married to the singer and actress claudia roberts . they often performed on stage together . he was born in munich . roberts represented luxembourg as a part of a sextet in the eurovision song contest 1985 in which they sang `` children , kinder , enfants '' . they got 37 points and came 13th place overall . the first two singles `` baby 's gone '' and `` welchen weg soll ich gehen ( one more mountain to climb ) '' were released with his artist name chris robert . roberts died in berlin of pancreatic cancer on july 2 , 2017 at the age of 71 .
