Mark Andrews     mark andrews ( born may 19 , 1926 ) is an american politician from the state of north dakota . he is a member of the united states republican party . during the 1950s he began to enter politics , serving on farmers ' organizations and republican committees . in 1962 , andrews ran for governor of north dakota , losing to incumbent william l. guy by just 2,000 votes out of over 228,500 cast . he ran for one of north dakota 's seats in the united states senate which was being vacated by the long-serving republican milton young who announced that he would not run for reelection and retire after the election . andrews won the election with 70 % of the vote and served in the senate for one term , from 1981 to 1987. andrews was defeated for re-election in 1986 by democratic-npl tax commissioner kent conrad by a mere 2,120 votes in what is still considered an upset .
