Sara Li     sara li ( born `` sara linnea larsson '' ; 30 march 1988 in ystad ) is a swedish singer . sara li was a member of the popgroup sheelah ( originally known as caracola ) from 2009 to 2010 , and she participated in melodifestivalen in the year of 2012 along with björn ranelid with the song `` mirakel '' . they reached the final of melodifestivalen from the semifinal which took place on 12 february 2012 in leksand , the semifinal was broadcast on sveriges television . in the final in globen arena their song placed 10th receiving the sixth highest televote result . the same year she participated in television singing show så ska det låta , teaming up with singer patrik isaksson . in october 2016 sara li gave birth to a baby girl called meia . other sources . - official website
