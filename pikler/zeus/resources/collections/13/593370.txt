Audi Cup 2017     the audi cup 2017 ( the fifth edition of the audi cup ) was a two-day football tournament in munich , germany . the participating teams were bayern munich , atlético de madrid , s.s.c . napoli and liverpool . top scorers . - 1 goal - keidi bare ( atlético madrid ) - josé callejón ( napoli ) - roberto firmino ( liverpool ) - emanuele giaccherini ( napoli ) - kalidou koulibaly ( napoli ) - sadio mané ( liverpool ) - mohamed salah ( liverpool ) - daniel sturridge ( liverpool ) - fernando torres ( atlético madrid ) - luciano vietto ( atlético madrid )
