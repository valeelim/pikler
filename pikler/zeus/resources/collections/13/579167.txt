Sexual health     a who working definition for sexual health is that it `` is a state of physical , emotional , mental and social well-being in relation to sexuality ; it is not merely the absence of disease , dysfunction or infirmity . sexual health requires a positive and respectful approach to sexuality and sexual relationships , as well as the possibility of having pleasurable and safe sexual experiences , free of coercion , discrimination and violence . for sexual health to be attained and maintained , the sexual rights of all persons must be respected , protected and fulfilled . '' however , while used by who as well as other organizations , this is not an official who position , and should not be used or quoted as a who definition .
