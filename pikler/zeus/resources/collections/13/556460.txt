Thomas Kyd     thomas kyd ( 1558-1594 ) was an english playwright . kyd is the author of `` the spanish tragedy '' . it was the most popular play of the time and many playwrights imitated it . a character in `` the spanish tragedy '' likely influenced shakespeare when writing hamlet . kyd was one of the first users of blank verse in english literature . `` extraordinary dramatic ( if not poetic ) genius '' . other websites . - full text of `` the spanish tragedy '' by thomas kyd
