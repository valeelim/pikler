Sacramento, Minas Gerais    sacramento , minas gerais sacramento is a municipality in the west of the brazilian state of minas gerais . neighboring municipalities are : perdizes ( n ) , araxá and tapira ( ne ) , são roque de minas and delfinópolis ( se ) , ibiraci ( s ) , pedregulho and rifaina ( sw ) , conquista and uberaba , and nova ponte and santa juliana ( nw ) .
