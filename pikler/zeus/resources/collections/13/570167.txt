Desmond Carrington     desmond herbert carrington ( 23 may 1926 – 1 february 2017 ) was a british actor and broadcaster . he was best known for his weekly evening show , `` the music goes round '' , on bbc radio 2. his show went out on sundays for 23 years from 1981 to 2004 , when it moved to tuesdays – then to fridays . he retired in his final broadcast on 28 october 2016. he was known for playing dr. chris anderson in the british soap opera `` emergency – ward 10 '' . he was born in bromley , kent . carrington died at his home in perthshire , scotland on 1 february 2017 from complications of alzheimer 's disease , aged 90 .
