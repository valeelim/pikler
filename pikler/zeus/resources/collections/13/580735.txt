Kawasaki Heavy Industries &amp; CSR Qingdao Sifang C151B    kawasaki heavy industries & amp ; csr qingdao sifang c151b kawasaki heavy industries & csr qingdao sifang c151b is the fifth generation of rolling stock used on the singapore mrt system . the trains operate on the east west line and north south line . the first train began its operations on 16 april 2017. as only the new track signalling system , that will be used on the north south line and east west line , is installed in these trains , they will only be able to run on sundays , on the north south line , for the two following months . this is because the new signalling system is not in full operation yet and is being tested only on sundays on the north south line only . these trains were built by kawasaki heavy industries and csr sifang . 45 six-carriage trains , totalling 270 carriages , were built in china . related pages . - kawasaki heavy industries c151 - siemens c651 - kawasaki heavy industries & nippon sharyo c751b - kawasaki heavy industries & csr qingdao sifang c151a
