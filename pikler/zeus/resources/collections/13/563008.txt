Puducherry district     puducherry district is one of the four districts of the union territory of puducherry in southern india . the district occupies an area of , spread over 11 non-contiguous enclaves lying on or near the bay of bengal within a compact area in the state of tamil nadu . puducherry district 's total population according to the 2011 census is 946,600.it is said that the population roughly equals that of fiji and us state of delaware . literacy rate - 86.13 % and sex ratio is 1031 per 1000 males . for administrative purpose , the union territory of puducherry is divided into eight taluks.some are puducherry taluk , villianur taluk , ozhukarai taluk and bahour taluk . among these ozhukarai taluk is the only taluk without rural areas.puducherry taluk 's rural area is covered in a commune named ariyankuppam.villianur taluk has two rural communes namely mannadipet and villianur . bahour taluk consists of two rural communes namely bahour and nettapakkam .
