Wiesendangen     wiesendangen is a municipality of the district of winterthur of the canton of zurich in switzerland . on 1 january 2014 the former municipalities of bertschikon bei attikon merged into the municipality of wiesendangen . wiesendangen , attikon , wallikon , menzengrüt , buch , bertschikon , gundetswil , kefikon , liebensberg , stegen , gündlikon and zünikon other websites . - official website of wiesendangen
