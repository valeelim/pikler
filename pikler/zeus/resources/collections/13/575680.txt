Juan Carlos Touriño     juan carlos touriño ( 14 july 1944 – 7 march 2017 ) was a spanish football defender . he won two spanish league titles and two spanish cup with real madrid . he made one appearance for the spanish national team . touriño died at a hospital in buenos aires on the night of 7 march 2017 from pancreatic disease , aged 72. real madrid - la liga ( 3 ) : 1971-72 , 1973-74 , 1974-75 - copa del rey ( 2 ) : 1973-74 , 1974–75 other websites . - spain statistics - world football profile
