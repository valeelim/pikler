Deomali (mountain)    deomali ( mountain ) deomali is a mountain peak in the chandragiri-pottangi subrange of the eastern ghats . it is located near koraput town in the koraput district of southern odisha , india.at 1672 m deomali is the highest mountain peak in the state of odisha . it is also one of the tallest peaks of the eastern ghats . the peak has a good surrounding scenery .
