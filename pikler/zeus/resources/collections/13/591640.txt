FC Alga Bishkek     fc alga bishkek is a football club from bishkek , kyrgyzstan . the club plays in the kyrgyzstan league . alga bishkek is the second most successful club in the league , with 5 titles . the club was founded in 1947 as fc zenith frunze . in the 1950 's , the club was named fc trudovye rezervy frunze , fc iskra frunze and fc spartak frunze . in 1961 , the club was renamed to fc alga frunze . the club had many more names in the 1990 's and 2000 's . in 2010 , the club was named alga bishkek . the club won the kyrgyzstan league in 1992 , 1993 , 2000 , 2001 , 2002. honours . - kyrgyzstan league ( 5 ) : 1992 , 1993 , 2000 , 2001 , 2002 - kyrgyzstan cup ( 9 ) : 1992 , 1993 , 1997 , 1998 , 1999 , 2000 , 2001 , 2002 , 2003 other websites . - career stats by klisf - summer transfers 2012
