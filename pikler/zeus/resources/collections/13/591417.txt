Américo Amorim     américo ferreira amorim ( 21 july 1934 – 13 july 2017 ) was a portuguese heir and businessman . he was ranked by `` forbes '' as the 385th richest person in the world in january 2015. his wealth was at $ 4.8 billion ( july 2017 ) he was known for having a 50 % interest in corticeira amorim . he was born in mozelos , santa maria da feira , portugal . amorim died on 13 july 2017 at his home in grijo , portugal after a short-illness , aged 82 .
