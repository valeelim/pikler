Egil Monn-Iversen     egil monn-iversen ( 14 april 1928 – 7 july 2017 ) was a norwegian composer , pianist and businessman . he was born in oslo . he was one of the most influential modern composers in norway . he has had many important roles in norwegian music , movie , opera , television , comedy and theater . for some time he had so much influence in norwegian culture that he got the nickname `` the godfather '' , even though he has always considered himself a down-to-earth musician . monn-iversen owned a movie production company , a record label , and an agency for musical artists . he was the founder of the vocal group the monn keys . he was the ceo at the chat noir theatre and chairman at the norwegian opera and has since the 1960s worked in nrk ( norwegian broadcasting corporation ) and det norske teatret ( the norwegian theatre ) . he has written scores to over 100 norwegian movies and tv series . monn-iversen was married to the norwegian actor and singer sølvi wang until her death in 2011. monn-iversen died on 7 july 2017 at the age of 89 .
