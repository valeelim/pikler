Punta di Grumo Beach (Lierna)    punta di grumo beach ( lierna ) punta di grumo beach is a popular beach and the name of the surrounding suburb in lierna , como lake , italy . punta di grumo beach is one of the most visited tourist sites in lake of como . punta di grumo beach is about long and receives many visitors throughout the year . in 2011 lierna council implemented wi-fi to punta di grumo beach users . service is free with limits on access periods and downloads per use . `` la punt '' beach is represented in one of the most popular amateur sporting across como lake . notable residents . - giulio cesare , formerly lived at lierna - plinio the elder , formerly lived at lierna
