Joseph P. Kennedy III     joseph patrick `` joe '' kennedy iii ( born october 4 , 1980 ) is an american lawyer , politician , and a member of the kennedy family , serving as the u.s. representative for since 2013. he is a democrat whose district stretches from the western suburbs of boston to the state 's south coast . he is the son of former u.s. representative joseph p. kennedy ii and grandson of robert f. kennedy and ethel skakel kennedy . he worked as a peace corps volunteer and as an assistant district attorney in the cape and islands and middlesex offices before his election to congress .
