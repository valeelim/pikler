Steve King (businessman)    steve king ( businessman ) steve king ( born july 4 , 1941 ) is an american businessman and republican party activist . he is the founder of king capital llc , an equity investment and real estate company . he is a former fbi agent . in 1988 , he campaigned unsuccessfully for the republican u.s. senate nomination in wisconsin . in july 2017 , president donald trump nominated king to be the next united states ambassador to the czech republic .
