Sabra     a sabra , hebrew : צבר‎‎ - tzabar , is a jew who is born in ottoman palestine , mandatory palestine or in the state of israel . as of 2015 , about three quarters of the israeli jews are sabras . the term `` sabra '' is derived from the prickly pear . like this cactus a sabra is supposed to be hard on the outside but soft on the inside .
