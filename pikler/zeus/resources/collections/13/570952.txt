Neil Gehrels     cornelis a . `` neil '' gehrels ( october 3 , 1952 – february 6 , 2017 ) was an american astronomer . he worked in the field of gamma ray astronomy . he was the chief of the astroparticle physics laboratory at nasa 's goddard space flight center from 1995 until his death . gehrels was born in lake geneva , wisconsin . he grew up in tucson , arizona . his father , tom ( 1925–2011 ) , was also an astronomer . gehrels died on february 6 , 2017 at the age of 64 .
