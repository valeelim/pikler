Great Green Wall     the great green wall of africa is a strip of trees going across africa to stop the sahara from moving south affecting lives up to 19 million people , including more than 1 million children . the strip of trees is planned to be about 15kms wide . unfortunately some people , when they see these trees , think its an opportunity to gather wood as timber for them to make fire or other resources . because of the sahara desert moving south is affecting countries such as : niger , chad , sudan , mali and many more .
