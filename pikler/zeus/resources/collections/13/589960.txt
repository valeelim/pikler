Test Mode     a test mode is a secret mode hidden in an electronic device on which allows a manufacturer to test the product before it gets sent to the consumer . a consumer can aces the test mode by pressing some buttons and either inserting the battery or the reset button . on entering a test mode , the electronic device will do random sounds and other things , like giving out a version number and producing a loud beep to confirm that the speaker is working . the main part of the test mode is either a rapid fire or manual sound playing program on which the electronic device cycles through it 's list of sounds . test modes in electronic handheld games are often accessed for creativity purposes on which the consumer can record the sounds they need to make skits , commercials and other things .
