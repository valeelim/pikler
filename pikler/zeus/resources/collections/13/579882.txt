Jacques Rozier     jacques rozier ( ; born 10 november 1926 in paris ) is a french movie director and screenwriter . he is one of the known members of the french new wave movement and has worked with jean-luc godard . three of his movies have been screened at the cannes film festival . in 1978 , he was a member of the jury at the 28th berlin international film festival . other websites . - biography on newwavefilm.com - cineresources.com archive of articles
