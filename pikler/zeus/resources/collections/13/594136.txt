Arrondissement of Pithiviers     the arrondissement of pithiviers is an arrondissement of france , in the loir-et-cher department of the centre-val de loire region . its capital is the city of pithiviers . when the loiret department was created on 1800 , the `` arrondissement '' of pithiviers was part of that original department . the `` arrondissement '' was eliminated in 1926 but it was made an `` arrondissement '' again in 1942. the `` arrondissement '' of pithiviers is the most northern of the `` arrondissements '' of the loiret department . it is the smallest `` arrondissement '' of the department , and the one with fewest people living in it , with 63,280 inhabitants . it is bordered to the north by the essonne department , to the east by the seine-et-marne department , to the southeast by the `` arrondissement '' of montargis , to the south by the `` arrondissement '' of orl√©ans and to the west by the eure-et-loir department . after the reorganisation of the cantons in france , cantons are not subdivisions of the `` arrondissements '' so they could have `` communes '' that belong to different `` arrondissements '' .
