Lanyard     a lanyard is a cord or strap . it is made to help the wearer keep and use some basic items of equipment , especially small items . it is usually worn around the neck with an id card or keys . lanyards were first introduced in the 15th century . in the military , lanyards are worn on the shoulders of uniforms to denote the wearer 's qualification or regimental affiliation . other websites . - lanyards - a historical view
