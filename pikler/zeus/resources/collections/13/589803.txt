Mercedes-Benz T2     the mercedes-benz t2 ( transporter 2 ) is a series of vans and step-entrance minibuses produced by daimler-benz in two generations from 1967-1996. the first generation t2 was launched in 1967 and it was available as a box van , a flat bed light truck or a minibus . the second generation t2 was introduced in 1986 , and was built until 1996. the t2 is still built in argentina as the l 710 and the lo 915 in 1996 , the t2 was replaced by the mercedes-benz vario .
