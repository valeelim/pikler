West End Girls     west end girls is a pop song from the british duo pet shop boys . the song is mixed with hip hop music . the lyrics are about inner-city life and class . the first version of the song was released in april 1984. it became a club hit in the united states and some european countries . when the duo signed with british record company emi , the song was re-recorded for their first studio album `` please '' . in october 1985 , the new version was released . it hit # 1 in the united kingdom and the united states in 1986. in 1987 , the song won best single at the brit awards . in 2005 , more than 20 years after its release , the song was awarded song of the decade between 1985 and 1994 by british academy of composers and songwriters . the `` west end girls '' has many versions . one of them is eight minutes and nine seconds in length . another version is seven minutes/fifty seconds long .
