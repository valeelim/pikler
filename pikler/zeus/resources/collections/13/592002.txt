Yves Pouliquen     yves pouliquen ( born 17 february 1931 ) is a french physician . his work has been dedicated to the pathology of the cornea . in 1992 , he was made a member of the académie nationale de médecine . in 1994 he was awarded the prix mondial cino del duca and on 29 november 2001 he was elected to the académie française . since 2006 , he is the president of the fondation singer-polignac . other websites . - l'académie française - yves pouliquen lutte contre la cécité en afrique – `` le figaro '' - publications by `` odile jacob ''
