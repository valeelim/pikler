2017 Fresno shootings     on april 18 , 2017 , a shooting spree occurred in fresno , california , leaving three men dead . the prime suspect , identified as kori ali muhammad , was then arrested by police and charged with murder and assault with a deadly weapon . muhammad , who was believed to have been motivated by a hatred of white people , was already wanted in connection with the murder of a security guard at a local motel 6 that had occurred four days earlier .
