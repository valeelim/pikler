Four sons of Horus     the four son of horus are a group of gods who protects the dead soul . there appear as canopic jars with each a different head . the first god is imsety , he appears with a human head , the second god is duamutef , he appears with a jackal head , the third god is hapi , he appears with a baboon head , the final god is qebehsenuef , he appears with a falcon head . they are used as canopic jars .
