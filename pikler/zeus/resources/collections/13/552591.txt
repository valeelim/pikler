Hurricane Hermine     hurricane hermine was a tropical cyclone in august and september 2016. it affected states from florida to new england . the storm hit florida on september 1 , near the big bend region . hermine had winds of 80 miles per hour at landfall . the storm also struck georgia , south carolina and north carolina . hermine was expected to affect areas from virginia to massachusetts and rhode island . at least two deaths were blamed on the storm .
