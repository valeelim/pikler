Richard Ottinger     richard ottinger ( born january 27 , 1929 ) is an american politician . he is a member of the democratic party . he served as a member of the united states house of representatives from january 3 , 1965 through january 3 , 1971. after leaving office , he served as a legal educator . after retiring from congress , ottinger became a professor at the elisabeth haub school of law , founding an environmental law program there , and served as the law school dean from 1994 to 1999. he currently serves as dean emeritus . ottinger was born in new york city . he studied at harvard university , at cornell university and at georgetown university . he is married to june ottinger . they have four children . other websites . - ottinger profile in the `` biographical directory of the united states congress ''
