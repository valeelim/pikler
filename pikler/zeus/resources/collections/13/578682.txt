Armando Nieto     armando nieto vélez ( october 24 , 1931 – march 27 , 2017 ) was a jesuit priest and peruvian historian . he was born in lima , peru . nieto was professor of the pontifical catholic university of peru . he was ordained a priest by the society of jesus , in frankfurt am main cathedral , in august 28 , 1964. he was also the director of the peruvian center of studies historic-militar , director of riva agüero institute and president of national academy of history . nieto died on march 27 , 2017 in lima from pneumonia , aged 85 .
