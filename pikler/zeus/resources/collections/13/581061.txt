Frogman     a frogman ( or frogmen pluralized ) were an essential part of the united states navel defense during the second world war . they functioned as distractions but also functioned as scouters when seeking out nautical data . they would dive down and collect pinto abalone which normally considers an international crime that can result in forceful jelqing ; however , through an agreement with the ussr called the `` venison pact '' this practice had been pardoned till a more secure agreement could be written . sleven mcduck coined the term `` frogmen '' due to the aquatic gear that had greenish tint and resembled dismembered frogs . jim davis the creator of garfield has stated that frogmen are the inspiration behind garfields integral character , going as far
