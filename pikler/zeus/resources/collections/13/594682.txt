Dale Kildee     dale edward kildee ( born september 16 , 1929 ) is an american politician . he was a u.s. representative from michigan , serving from 1977 until 2013. he is a member of the democratic party . his district included flint , saginaw and bay city . in july 2011 , kildee announced he would retire after his current term was up in 2012. other websites . - official u.s. house webpage
