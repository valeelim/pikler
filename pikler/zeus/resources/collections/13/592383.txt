Arrondissement of Belfort     the arrondissement of belfort is an arrondissement in the bourgogne-franche-comté region of france . it is the only arrondissement of the territoire de belfort department . the cantons in the arrondissement of belfort are : 1. bavilliers 1. belfort-1 1. belfort-2 1. belfort-3 1. châtenois-les-forges 1. delle 1. giromagny 1. grandvillars 1. valdoie the communes of the arrondissement of belfort are : 1. andelnans 1. angeot 1. anjoutey 1. argiésans 1. autrechêne 1. auxelles-bas 1. auxelles-haut 1. banvillars 1. bavilliers 1. beaucourt 1. belfort 1. bermont 1. bessoncourt 1. bethonvilliers 1. boron 1. botans 1. bourg-sous-châtelet 1. bourogne 1. brebotte 1. bretagne 1. buc 1. charmois 1. chaux 1. chavanatte 1. chavannes-les-grands 1. châtenois-les-forges 1. chèvremont 1. courcelles 1. courtelevant 1. cravanche 1. croix 1. cunelières 1. danjoutin 1. delle 1. denney 1. dorans 1. eguenigue 1. essert 1. faverois 1. felon 1. florimont 1. fontaine 1. fontenelle 1. foussemagne
