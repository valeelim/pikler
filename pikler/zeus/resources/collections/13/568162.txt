Sergei Aman     sergei aman ( , born 12 febriary 1957 ) is a russian writer and journalist . sergei aman was born in former soviet union in turkmenia . he graduated the moscow state university in 1985. in 1995 sergei aman became a journalist of the daily `` moskovskij komsomolets '' . also he worked in `` vechernyaya moskva '' , `` stupeni '' , `` the new medical gazette '' , magazine `` auto m '' . the most famous book of sergei aman is `` journalists '' ( novel ) . he published this novel in 2013. other websites . - official website of sergei aman - sergei aman in facebook
