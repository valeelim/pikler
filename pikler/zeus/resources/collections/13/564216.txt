Adam Ries     adam ries was a mathematician from bad staffelstein in bavaria . ries lived in the 16th century . he was born in 1492 or 1493 , and died in 1559. ries became known for a work on how to calculate . his first work was about a `` calculating board '' , which was widely used at the time . it worked rather like an abacus . ries showed that roman numerals were impractical for calculation . ries wrote in german , and not in latin , as orher mathematicians of his time .
