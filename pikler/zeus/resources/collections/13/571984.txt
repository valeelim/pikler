Communist Party of India (Marxist)    communist party of india ( marxist ) the communist party of india ( marxist ) is a communist party in india . the party emerged from a split from the communist party of india in 1964. the cpi ( m ) was formed at the seventh congress of the communist party of india held in calcutta from 31 october to 7 november 1964. the power of cpi ( m ) is concentrated in the states of kerala , west bengal and tripura . at present in 2016 , cpi ( m ) is leading the state governments in tripura and kerala . it also leads the left front in west bengal .
