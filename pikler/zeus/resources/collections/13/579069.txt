Adam Weishaupt     johann adam weishaupt ( 6th february 1748-18th november 1830 ) was a german philosopher influenced by the ideas of the age of enlightenment . during his life , he was also influenced by the ideas of liberalism . he was a freemason , and liked the ideas of deism and republicanism . he also started the short-lived secret society called illuminati . weishaupt studied history , law , political science and philosphy in ingolstadt , in 1768 , he got his doctorate in philosophy . in 1772 he became a professor for law , with a specialization in church law .
