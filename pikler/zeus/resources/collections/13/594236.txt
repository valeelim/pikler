Tom Petty     thomas earl petty ( born october 20 , 1950 ) is an american musician , singer-songwriter , voice actor and record producer . he is best known as the lead singer of tom petty and the heartbreakers , but is also known as a member and co-founder of the late 1980s supergroup the traveling wilburys ( under the pseudonyms of charlie t. wilbury , jr. and muddy wilbury ) , and his early band mudcrutch . petty is also known for his voice role as buckley in the television series `` king of the hill '' . in his career , petty has sold more than 80 million records worldwide , making him one of the best-selling music artists of all time . in 2002 , petty was inducted into the rock and roll hall of fame .
