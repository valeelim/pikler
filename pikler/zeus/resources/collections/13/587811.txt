Celtiberian Range     celtiberian range ( in spanish language : `` serranía celtibérica '' ) , also named : south lapland or spanish lapland ( `` laponia del sur '' or `` laponia española '' ) is the geographical term that receives a tiny fraction of the spanish territory due to its lower population and lacking of infraestructures . its nomenclature comes from the celtiberian , a pre-roman tribe who lived in that area long time ago . it comprises an area of 63,098 km² . through the range are 1,632 municipalities among several provinces . all but valencian community are landlocked . it has a population of 503,566 inhabitants .
