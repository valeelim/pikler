Gil Coan     gilbert fitzgerald `` gil '' coan ( born may 18 , 1922 ) is a former professional baseball outfielder . he was born in monroe , north carolina . coan played in major league baseball ( mlb ) for the washington senators , baltimore orioles , chicago white sox and the new york giants . he batted left-handed and threw right-handed . as of 2016 , he is the oldest living former new york giants player . other websites . - baseball library - brevard insurance agency - retrosheet
