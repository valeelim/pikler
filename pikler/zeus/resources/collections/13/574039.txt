SKA Saint Petersburg     ska saint petersburg ( ) is a russian professional ice hockey club based in st. petersburg . they are members of the bobrov division in the kontinental hockey league . they first started to play in 1946 , and played for the championship in 2015 and won . they are owned by gazprom export , a gas company owned by russia , and have many people go to their games . the team was first a part of the soviet army . other websites . - official website
