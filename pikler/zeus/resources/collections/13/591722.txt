Anne Buttimer     anne buttimer ( 31 october 1938 – 15 july 2017 ) was an irish geographer . she was emeritus professor of geography at university college , dublin . she was born in cork , ireland . she was president of the igu 2000–2004 and the first geographer to be vice-president of academia europaea in 2012. buttimer died of endometrial cancer in dublin on 15 july 2017 at the age of 78 .
