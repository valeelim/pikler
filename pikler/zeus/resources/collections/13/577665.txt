Henk Krol     henricus cornelis maria `` henk '' krol ( born april 1 , 1950 in tilburg ) is a dutch politician of 50plus ( 50+ ) . he was political leader from 2012 to 2013 and has been again since 2016. he is gay and a lgbt rights activist . he is also a former journalist and businessman . he was a member and parliamentary leader of the second chamber from 2012 to 2013 , and has been both again since 2014. previously he was a member of the provincial states of north brabant and councillor of the municipality of eindhoven . krol studied at the free university amsterdam . he was spokesman of the people 's party for freedom and democracy ( vvd ) parliamentary group from 1978 to 1985. meanwhile , in 1979 , he founded the lgbt newspaper gay krant , for which he also worked as a journalist . he was also owner of the best publishing group ( publishing company of the gay krant ) and online sex shop gaytel , which went bankrupt in 2013. references . - bio at parlement.com
