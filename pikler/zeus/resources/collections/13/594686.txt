Sirkka Selja     sirkka selja ( sirkka-liisa tulonen ; 20 march 1920 – 17 august 2017 ) was a finnish poet and writer . she was born in hämeenkoski , finland . her first book was published in 1942 titled `` still i live '' . in addition to poems , his production includes a play and two listening works . selja was awarded with the state literature prize of finland in 1958 , the pro finlandia in 1970 , the aleksis kivi prize in 1987 and the p. mustapää prize in 2007. selja died on 17 august 2017 of pneumonia in hollola , finland at the age of 97 .
