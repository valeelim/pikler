Joe Morton     joseph thomas `` joe '' morton , jr. ( born october 18 , 1947 ) is an american actor . he worked with movie director john sayles in `` the brother from another planet '' ( 1984 ) , `` city of hope '' ( 1991 ) and `` lone star '' ( 1996 ) . he is also known for his role as lt. mcmahon in the 1994 action movie `` speed '' . in 2014 , morton won the primetime emmy award for outstanding guest actor in a drama series for his role as eli pope in `` scandal '' . he is also known for playing the role of henry deacon on the popular tv series `` eureka '' . in 2016 , morton played dr. silas stone , father of victor stone/cyborg , in a cameo role in `` '' as part of the dc extended universe . he will play the role again in the upcoming `` justice league '' ( 2017 ) . other websites . - joe morton sings `` the devil 's lonely fire '' from `` badland '' .
