Ivy Dumont     dame ivy leona dumont , dcmg ( born 2 october 1930 ) is a retired bahamian politician . she was the sixth governor-general of the bahamas . she was the first woman in the bahamas to hold this office , from 1 january 2002 ( on an acting basis since 13 november 2001 ) until 30 november 2004. she previously served as education minister from 1995 to 2001. other websites . - official biography
