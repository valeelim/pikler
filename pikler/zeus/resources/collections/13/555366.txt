Connecticut Compromise     the connecticut compromise ( also known as the great compromise of 1787 or sherman 's compromise ) was an agreement that large and small states reached during the constitutional convention of 1787 that established a two-house legislature under the united states constitution . it retained the bicameral legislature as proposed by roger sherman , along with proportional representation in the lower house . it required the upper house , united states senate , to have two members from each state . the lower house , or house of representatives , would have representation based on the population of a state .
