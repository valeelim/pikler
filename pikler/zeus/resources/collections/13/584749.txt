Meknès     meknès or meknes , moroccan amazigh : ⴰⵎⴽⵏⴰⵙ - `` ameknas '' , arab : مكناس - `` mknas '' , is a city in the north of morocco . about 632,000 people were living in this city in 2014. meknès is one of the four imperial cities of morocco . the city dates from the 11th century . it lies north of the middle atlas . related pages . - el hedim square
