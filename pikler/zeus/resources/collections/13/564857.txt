Fuller House (TV series)    fuller house ( tv series ) fuller house is a television sitcom . this is the sequel to `` full house '' , which aired on abc from 1987 to 1995. the show looks at donna jo `` d.j . '' tanner , now a widowed mother of three sons . most of the original `` full house '' series cast members have returned to play the same characters in `` fuller house '' . they have made guest appearances or have returned to their original acting roles . candace cameron bure plays d.j . tanner . jodie sweetin plays stephanie . andrea barber plays kimmy . the series began on february 26 , 2016 on netflix . on march 2 , 2016 , a second season was ordered .
