Soledad Giménez     soledad giménez muñoz , also known as sole giménez ( 27th february , 1963 in paris , île-de-france ) is a spanish-french songwriter . she was since 1983 until 2006 leader of the jazz group : presuntos implicados . some of her most known songs are `` alma de blues '' , `` mi pequeño tesoro '' and `` cómo hemos cambiado '' among others . throughout her career she had performed with several artists as joaquín sabina , maría dolores pradera , joan manuel serrat , herbie hancock , milton nascimento , randy crawford , armando manzanero , pancho céspedes , ana torroja , miguel ríos , ana belén , revólver , los piratas , los sabandeños , dúo dinámico , enrique heredia , etc . giménez was born and grown in paris , france , where lived until her 5 years old . in 1968 she moved out to yecla , region of murcia , spain , where her parents were from . in 1974 became member of the purisima 's basilica girls choir of yecla . in 1981 she moved out to valencia , where she began to study fine arts at facultad de san carlos . two years later , her brother convinced her to sent a demo to the rne 's music contest : `` don domingo '' and is selected to perform on air with her music band : presuntos implicados . in those days they were eleven members .
