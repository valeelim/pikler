Junkie XL     antonius tom holkenborg ( born 8 december 1967 ) , known by his stage name junkie xl and occasionally jxl , is a dutch multi-instrumentalist , composer , dj , producer , and engineer . originally known for his trance productions , he has moved to producing electronica and big beat music and film scores in recent years . junkie xl is probably best known for his remix of elvis presley 's `` a little less conversation '' . in movie scores , he is perhaps known for his work in the dc extended universe beginning with `` '' with hans zimmer , contributing to the theme song for `` wonder woman '' and will compose the score of `` justice league '' with danny elfman . junkie xl was born in lichtenvoorde , gelderland , netherlands . other websites . - junkie xl official website - junkie xl official facebook page - junkie xl youtube video page at youtube - interview with garrett shatzer for onbeat , 2006 - junkie xl scoring 300 : rise of an empire
