Diego E. Hernández     vice admiral diego e. hernandez ( march 25 , 1934 – july 7 , 2017 ) was a united states navy officer . he was the first hispanic to be named vice commander of north american aerospace defense command ( norad ) . he then underwent flight training and on august 1956 , was designated a naval aviator . hernandez died on july 7 , 2017 from complications of parkinson 's disease at the age of 83 .
