Ann Robinson     ann robinson ( born may 25 , 1929 ) is an american actress and stunt horse rider . she is best known for her work in the science-fiction classic `` the war of the worlds '' and in the 1954 movie `` dragnet '' . robinson was born in hollywood , california . she studied at hollywood high school . in 1987 , she married joseph valdez . they divorced in 2017. other websites . - ann robinson interview , thespectrum.com ; accessed august 10 , 2016 .
