Wim Anderiesen Jr.     gerardus willem `` wim '' anderiesen jr. ( 2 september 1931 – 27 january 2017 ) was a dutch footballer . he played as a defender at club level from the early-1950s to the mid-1960s . he played for ajax from 1951 to 1961. he later played for holland sport . anderiesen was born in amsterdam , north holland . he was the son of and named after wim anderiesen ( 1903–1944 ) . he died on 27 january 2017 in heerhugowaard at the age of 85 .
