Ely Ould Mohamed Vall     colonel ely ould mohamed vall ( 1953 – 5 may 2017 ) was a political and military figure in mauritania . he served as the transitional military leader of mauritania following a `` coup d'état '' in august 2005 until 19 april 2007 , when he gave up his power to an elected government . vall died on 5 may 2017 from a heart attack in nouakchott at the age of 64 .
