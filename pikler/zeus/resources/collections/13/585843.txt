Denis Johnson     denis hale johnson ( july 1 , 1949 – may 24 , 2017 ) was an american writer . he was born in munich , germany . johnson best known for his short story collection `` jesus ' son '' ( 1992 ) and his novel `` tree of smoke '' ( 2007 ) , which won the national book award for fiction . he also wrote plays , poetry and non-fiction . johnson died at his home on may 24 , 2017 in gualala , california from liver cancer at the age of 67. other websites . - intersection for the arts , san francisco - kcrw bookworm interview - profile at the whiting foundation - denis johnson profile and poems at academy of american poets - denis johnson at library of congress authorities — with 25 catalog records
