Rasha Sharbaji (director)    rasha sharbaji ( director ) rasha sharbaji ( arabic : رشا شربتجي ) ( born april 20 1975 in cairo , egypt ) is a syrian film director and producer . she directed the egyptian television series “ sharaf fatah al bab ” ( sharaf opened the door ) . she also directed the series `` longing '' . she is the the daughter of syrian director hisham sharbatji . she spent her childhood with her mother in cairo . she worked as a kindergarten teacher in the uae before returning to syria to live with her father . she married cameraman nasser rca . rasha was an assistant to her father before directing various television series . syrian drama : - law , but ...
