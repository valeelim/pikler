Flash Airlines Flight 604     flash airlines flight 604 , a boeing 737-300 , was a flight from sharm el sheikh , egypt , to paris , france , via cairo . on january 3 , 2004 the plane crashed into the red sea just moments after take-off at sharm el sheikh international airport . all 135 passengers and 13 crew members on-board the plane died . the cause the crash remains disputed and suggested to be spatial disorientation from the captain . the crash remains egypt 's deadliest air-disaster , until the bombing of kogalymavia flight 9268 , with 224 deaths . the crash is also the worst crash of a boeing 737-300 .
