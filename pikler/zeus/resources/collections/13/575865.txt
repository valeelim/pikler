Alejandra Soler     alejandra soler gilabert ( july 8 , 1913 – march 1 , 2017 ) was a spanish politician and teacher . she also worked for the diplomatic academy of the ministry of foreign affairs of the russian federation . she was born in valencia , spain . she took part in the revolts against miguel primo de rivera 's dictature and in 1934 , she became a member of the communist party . soler died on march 1 , 2017 in valencia , spain from suspected heart failure , aged 103. works . - 2009 , `` la vida es un río caudaloso con peligrosos rápidos ''
