America's Morning Headquarters    america 's morning headquarters america 's morning headquarters , often called amhq and formerly your weather today and morning rush , is a morning television program on the weather channel . it airs live from atlanta , georgia from 5 to 9 a.m. eastern time on weekdays . the show gives weather information for americans to get out the door . a weekend edition , called amhq weekend , was launched on january 31 , 2015 .
