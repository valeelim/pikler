Piroska Oszoli     piroska oszoli ( 21 january 1919 – 22 march 2017 ) was a hungarian painter , is known as `` the lover of the danube and the beautiness '' . she was born and also dead in dunaföldvár , hungary . she made especially as applied to plein-air landscape painting . her master as an amateur artist was the famous transylvanian painter ferenc doór ( 1918–2015 ) . after teaching she became a pensioner , and started her painting as an individual artist . oszoli worked all around her village and neighborhood , after in hungary , at balaton . she painted natural surroundings in italy , croatia , germany and norway . her paintings are present from austria to new zealand . oszoli died on 22 march 2017 in dunaföldvár , aged 98. other websites . - her c.v. in art from 2001 - gallery with her 126 paintings
