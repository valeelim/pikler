Optare Solo     the optare solo is a low-floor single decker bus produced by optare in 2 generations since 1978 and it has different lenths . a narrow version called the `` slimline '' was launched in 2004 a short version called the `` se '' was launched in 2006 an electric version called the `` ev '' was launched in 2009 a second generation version called the `` sr '' was launched in 2007 and replaced the first generation solo a first and second diesel-electric hybrid solo was also launched .
