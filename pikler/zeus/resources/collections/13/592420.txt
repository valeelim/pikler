Gorilla Grodd     gorilla grodd is a fictional supervillain appearing in american comic book published by dc comics . he was written as an enemy of the flash . the character first appeared in `` the flash '' # 106 ( may 1959 ) and he was created by john broome and carmine infantino . gorilla grodd is an evil and super-intelligent gorilla who gained mental powers after being exposed to the radiation of a meteorite . ign named him 35th of the top 100 comic book villains .
