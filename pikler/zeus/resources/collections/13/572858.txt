Jørgen Kieler     jørgen kieler ( 23 august 1919 – 19 february 2017 ) was a danish physician . he was known for his participation in resistance activities under the german occupation of denmark in the early 1940s . together with his sister , elsebet kieler , he published frit denmark or `` free denmark '' , an illegal newspaper . as a member of the holger danske resistance group , he helped hundreds of danish jews to escape to sweden and avoid extermination .
