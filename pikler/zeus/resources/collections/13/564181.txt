Cuttack     cuttack is the former capital of the state of odisha in east india . it is the headquarters of the cuttack district . cuttack is nicknamed the `` millenium city '' as well as the `` silver city '' . this is because of its 1000 years history and its famous silver jewelry works . the high court of odisha is located in cuttack . cuttack is also called as the commercial capital of odisha . this is due to the large number of trading and business houses in the city . cuttack and bhubaneswar are often called the twin-cities of odisha . the metropolitan area formed by the two cities has a population of 1.68 million in 2014. cuttack is a tier-ii city as per the ranking system used by government of india .
