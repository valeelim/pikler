Jerzy Kossela     jerzy kossela ( né kosela ; 15 july 1942 – 7 january 2017 ) was a polish guitarist , vocalist . he was born in częstochowa , poland . kossela was the founding member of the bands electron , niebiekso-czarni , pięciolinie and czerwone gitary . kossela died on 7 january 2017 in gdynia , aged 74. other websites . - jerzy kossela profile , archiwum polskiego rocka - jerzy kossela profile , polish catalogue of gramophone records
