Norma McCorvey     norma leah mccorvey ( née nelson ; september 22 , 1947 – february 18 , 2017 ) , better known by the legal pseudonym `` jane roe '' , was an american activist . she was the plaintiff in the landmark american lawsuit `` roe v. wade '' in 1973. the u.s. supreme court ruled that individual state laws banning abortion are unconstitutional . later , mccorvey 's views on abortion changed greatly because she became a roman catholic activist in the pro-life movement .
