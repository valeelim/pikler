K. S. Chandrasekharan     komaravolu s. chandrashekhar ( 21 november 1920 – 13 april 2017 ) was an indian-swiss professor at eth zurich . he was a founding faculty member of school of mathematics , tata institute of fundamental research ( tifr ) . he was known for his work in number theory and summability and was given numerous awards including padma shri , shanti swarup bhatnagar award , ramanujan medal , and honorary fellow of tifr . other websites . - – india 's who is who
