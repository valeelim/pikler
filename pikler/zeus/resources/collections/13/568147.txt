Oscar López Rivera     oscar lópez rivera ( born january 6 , 1943 ) is a puerto rican nationalist . he was one of the leaders of the faln . in 1981 , lópez rivera was convicted and sentenced to 55 years in federal prison for conspiracy , use of force to commit robbery , interstate transportation of firearms , and conspiracy to transport explosives with intent to destroy government property . in 1988 he was sentenced to an additional 15 years in prison for conspiring to escape from the leavenworth federal prison . lópez rivera was among the 14 convicted faln members offered conditional clemency by u.s. president bill clinton in 1999 , but rejected the offer . the imprisonment of lópez rivera was opposed or supported by individuals and groups representing political , religious , and other constituencies . some called him a terrorist , but others said he was a political prisoner . several u.s. congressmen supported oscar lópez rivera 's release from prison such as senators bernie sanders and elizabeth warren .
