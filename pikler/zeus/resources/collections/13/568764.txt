Nurosaurus     `` not to be confused with naosaurus , a genus of pelycosaur . '' nurosaurus is the informal name given to a genus of sauropod dinosaur . it was from the cretaceous period in inner mongolia , china . it was a herbivore or plant-eater . `` nurosaurus '' grew to about long . the proposed binominal name is `` nurosaurus qaganensis '' ( dong 1992 ) .
