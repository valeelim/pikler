Chu Ke-liang     chu ke-liang ( ; 5 december 194615 may 2017 ) was a taiwanese , comedian , actor , television show host and singer . he was known for his `` over-the-top appearance '' with unusual clothing and hair styles and his coarse humor . he was born in zuoying , kaohsiung , taiwan . he was known for his roles in `` the new legend of shaolin '' , `` david loman '' , and in `` the wonderful wedding '' . chu died in taipei , taiwan from liver failure caused by colorectal cancer on 15 may 2017 at the age of 70 .
