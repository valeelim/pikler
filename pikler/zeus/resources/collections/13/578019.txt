Maravar     maravar is a tamil community of the state of tamil nadu , southern india , and are one of the three branches of the mukkulathor confederacy or league of caste.members of the maravar community often use the honorific title `` thevar '' .the term `` maravar '' has diverse proposed etymologies ; it may come simply from a tamil word `` maravar '' ( warrior ) , or a term meaning `` bravery '' .
