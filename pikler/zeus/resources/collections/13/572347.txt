Stećak     stećak ( стећак , ; plural : stećci , стећци , ) are monumental tombstones made in medieval bosnia . the tombstones are mostly in bosnia and herzegovina with a smaller amount in the border area of croatia , montenegro and serbia . about 60,000 are found within the territory of the modern bosnia and herzegovina . the rest of 10,000 are found in what are today croatia ( 4,400 ) , montenegro ( 3,500 ) , and serbia ( 4,100 ) . the stećak are at more than 3,300 odd sites with over 90 % in poor condition .
