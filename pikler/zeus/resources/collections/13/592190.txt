Bernhard Kempa     bernhard kempa ( 19 november 1920 – 20 july 2017 ) was a german handball player and coach . he became world champion in field handball in 1952 and 1955 as a national team player . he was born in opole , upper silesia . kempa made a significant contribution as the outstanding player . until his career as a player in 1957 , kempa won the championship in göppingen one more time in the hall ( 1955 ) and in the field ( 1957 ) . as a göppinger coach , he did so in the indoor handball five more times ( 1958-1961 , 1970 ) , and kempa also took the europapokal in 1960. in 2011 , kempa was admitted to germany 's sports hall of fame . kempa died in bad boll , germany of complications from parkinson 's disease on 20 july 2017 at the age of 96. other websites . - eintrag im sportbuch band 1 ( pdf ; 3,6 mb )
