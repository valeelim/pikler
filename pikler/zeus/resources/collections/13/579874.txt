Y. A. Tittle     yelberton abraham tittle jr. ( born october 24 , 1926 ) , better known as y. a. tittle , is an american professional american football quarterback . he played in the national football league ( nfl ) for the san francisco 49ers , new york giants , and baltimore colts , after spending two seasons with the colts in the all-america football conference ( aafc ) . tittle was born in marshall , texas . he studied at louisiana state university . he was married to minnette tittle until her death in 2012. they have one daughter , dianne . tittle is suffering from a severe case of dementia .
