AKM     the akm is a soviet assault rifle that was designed by mikhail kalashnikov in the 1950s and adopted in 1959 by the soviet army to replace the ak-47 . the difference between the ak-47 and the akm is that the ak-47 is short and the akm is long and the other difference is that the ak-47 has a regular muzzle and the akm has a sharp muzzle .
