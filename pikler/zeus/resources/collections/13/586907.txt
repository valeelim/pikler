Boys' Life    boys ' life boys ' life is a magazine made by the boy scouts of america . it was first published in 1911. there are two versions of `` boys ' life '' . the first version is made for the youngest members of cub scouting . these are the 6-to-10-year-old cub scouts and first-year webelos scouts ( a rank in cub scouts ) . the second version is made for 11-to-18-year-old boys , which includes second-year webelos through 18-year-old boy scouts , varsity scouts and venturers . the publisher picks which version to send by looking at if the subscriber is in cub scouts or boy scouts . in june 2007 , `` boys ' life '' earned four distinguished achievement awards from the association of educational publishers ( aep ) . one of those awards is periodical of the year . the magazine 's mascot is pedro the mailburro , who answers readers ' letters . boys ' life has a comic strip about pedro .
