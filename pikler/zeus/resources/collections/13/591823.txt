With Full Force     with full force is a german heavy metal and punk music festival held every year since 1994. in 2013 , the festival attracted 30,000 attendees . in 2017 , the festival took place at the ferropolis near gräfenhainichen . this is the first time since 1998 that the festival will not be held in löbnitz . many popular artists have performed here . these include slayer , iron maiden , rammstein , marilyn manson , motörhead , machine head , amon amarth , arkona , in flames , slipknot , etc . other websites . - official website - with full force 2012 on choosefest
