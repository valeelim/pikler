Mercedes-Benz TN     the mercedes-benz tn ( transporter ne , internally called t1 ) were vans and minibuses built by mercedes-benz from 1977 to 1995. it was available as a box van , a flat bed light truck or a minibus . in 1986 bajaj tempo , ( now force motors ) and mercedes benz ( daimler benz ) built the force motors traveller and tempo traveller for indian countries .
