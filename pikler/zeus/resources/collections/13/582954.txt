Duane E. Dewey     duane edgar dewey ( born november 16 , 1931 ) is a former american combat marine . he received the u.s. military 's highest decoration for valor , the medal of honor , for his actions on april 16 , 1952 during the korean war . although wounded by an enemy grenade , he fell on another exploding grenade with his own body to save the life of a corpsman and the other marines around him .
