Siamogale melilutra     siamogale melilutra was an extinct species of giant otter . it is from the late miocene epoch , from yunnan province , china . the skull has a combination of otter-like and badger-like cranial features . the new species belongs to the lutrinae subfamily . `` siamogale melilutra '' was about the size of a wolf . it was estimated to about . this is two to three times larger than any modern otter species .
