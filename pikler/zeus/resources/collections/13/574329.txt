Bog iron     bog iron is a form of iron found in swamps . bog iron is generally found in a spring . smelting bog iron has existed since pre-roman times . most iron smelted during the viking era came from bog iron . in russia , bog iron was the main source of iron until the sixteenth century . iron was produced by vikings around 1000 ad . mining bog iron had been done in virginia as early as 1608 , and more bog iron has been mined in new jersey and maryland .
