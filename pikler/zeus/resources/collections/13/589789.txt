Bouaké     bouaké ( or bwake ) is the second-largest city in ivory coast . it is in the central part of ivory coast about northeast of lake kossou , the country 's largest lake . it has 536,189 people . bouaké is twinned with : - mopti , mali - brescia , italy - beersheba , israel - villeneuve-sur-lot , france - reutlingen , germany - zimnicea , romania other websites . - bouaké travel guide from world66
