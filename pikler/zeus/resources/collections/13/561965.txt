Adam Kokesh     adam charles kokesh ( born february 1 , 1982 in san francisco , california ) is an american political activist . he is a decorated veteran of the war in iraq , kokesh came to disparage war and advocate nonviolent resistance to power . a member of the libertarian party , kokesh has called for a `` new american revolution '' for the `` orderly dissolution of the federal government . '' he was raised in santa fe , new mexico . he now lives in arizona . kokesh announced that he would run president of the united states under the libertarian party ticket in the 2020 united states presidential election . other websites . - adam kokesh.com ( kokesh 2020 announcement as of november 2016 ) - adam kokesh on amazon - the freedom line website
