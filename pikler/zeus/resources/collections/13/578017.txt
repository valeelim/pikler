José José     josé rómulo sosa ortiz ( born 17 february 1948 ) , known by his stage name josé josé , is a mexican singer-songwriter , musician and occasional actor . his 1983 album `` secretos '' has sold over 7 million units . with a large number of international hits , he received several grammy nominations and recognition worldwide . in march 2017 , josé announced that he was diagnosed with pancreatic cancer .
