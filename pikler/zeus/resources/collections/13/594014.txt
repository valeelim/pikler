Christian Kern     christian kern ( born january 4 , 1966 in vienna ) is an austrian politician of the social democratic party of austria ( spö ) . he is the current chancellor of austria . kern has become chancellor on may 17 , 2016 , succeeding fellow party member werner faymann . he has also been leading the spö since june 25 , 2016. after studying journalism and communication at the university of vienna , he worked as a business journalist and a civil servant . afterwards he worked at the verbund ag , the biggest supplier of electricity in austria , and was ceo of the öbb , the federal railways of austria . references . - www.meineabgeordneten.at
