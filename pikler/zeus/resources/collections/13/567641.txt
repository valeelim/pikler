Mark Fisher (theorist)    mark fisher ( theorist ) mark fisher ( 11 july 1968 – 13 january 2017 ) was a british writer , cultural theorist and blogger . he was also known by his blogging name k-punk from 2003. he was known for his writing on radical politics , popular culture and music . he wrote books , including `` capitalist realism '' ( 2009 ) . he also wrote as a journalist for `` the wire '' , `` the guardian '' , `` fact '' , `` new statesman '' and `` sight & sound '' . he was based at goldsmiths , university of london . fisher died on 13 january 2017 at the age of 48. his wife , zöe fisher , said he had taken his own life .
