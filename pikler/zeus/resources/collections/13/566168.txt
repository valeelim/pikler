Donnie Yen     donnie yen ( born 27 july 1963 ) , also known as yen ji-dan ( 甄子丹 ) , is a chinese-born hong kong actor , martial artist , movie director , producer and action choreographer . he is also a wushu world champion many times . he is best known for his role as ip man in the `` ip man '' ( 2008–2015 ) trilogy movies . he also appeared in `` shanghai knights '' ( 2003 ) , `` flash point '' ( 2007 ) and `` rogue one '' ( 2016 ) . yen was born in guangzhou , guangdong . when he was two years old , his family moved to hong kong . they later moved to boston , massachusetts , united states when he was 11. he has been married twice and has three children .
