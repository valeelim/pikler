Jerry Adriani     jerry adriani ( jair alves de sousa ; 29 january 1947 – 23 april 2017 ) was a brazilian singer , musician and actor . he began his career in television while working with rede tupi in são paulo . his early works was that as a vocalist for the ensemble `` os rebeldes '' . adriani died at a hospital in rio de janeiro from cancer on 23 april 2017 , aged 70 .
