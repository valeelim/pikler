Sarah Huckabee Sanders     sarah elizabeth huckabee sanders ( born august 13 , 1982 ) is an american political aide . sanders is the 31st and current white house press secretary since july 26 , 2017. she served as deputy white house press secretary from january through july 2017. sanders is the daughter of former governor of arkansas and presidential candidate mike huckabee , and former arkansas first lady janet huckabee .
