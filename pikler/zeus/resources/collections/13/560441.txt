Satyameva Jayate     `` satyameva jayate '' ( `` '' ; lit . `` truth alone triumphs . '' ) is a `` mantra '' from the ancient indian scripture `` mundaka upanishad '' . upon independence of india , it was adopted as the national motto of india . it is inscribed in script at the base of the national emblem . the emblem and the words `` satyameva jayate '' are inscribed on one side of all indian currency . the emblem is an adaptation of the lion capital of ashoka which was erected around 250 bce at sarnath , near varanasi in the north indian state of uttar pradesh . it is inscribed on all currency notes and national documents .
