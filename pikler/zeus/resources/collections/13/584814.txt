Alain Defossé     alain defossé ( 11 february 1957 – 14 may 2017 ) was a french novelist and translator . he was born in nantes , france . his first novel , `` les fourmis d ’ anvers '' , was published in 1991. he also translated several books from english into french . for example , he translated `` crazy cock '' by henry miller in 1990. he also translated `` american psycho '' by bret easton ellis in 1993. in 2006 , he translated `` the night watch '' by sarah waters . two years later , in 2008 , he translated `` rant '' by chuck palahniuk . other novelists whose work he translated are irvine welsh and joseph connolly . defossé died on 14 may 2017 in paris of lung cancer at the age of 60 .
