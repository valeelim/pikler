More Life     more life ( also known as more life : a playlist by october firm ) is a playlist by canadian rapper drake . it was released on march 18 , 2017 , by young money entertainment and cash money records . drake stated that the collection of songs is a playlist and not an album . the first single , `` fake love '' , was released on october 23 , 2016 , the day before drake 's 30th birthday . it reached the top ten around the world . `` passionfruit '' is set to be issued as the second single from `` more life '' on march 28 , 2017. references . - https : //www.discogs.com/drake-more-life-a-playlist-by-october-firm/release/9999158 - https : //itunes.apple.com/us/album/more-life/id1216986780 - http : //www.thefader.com/2017/03/18/drake-more-life-album-credits
