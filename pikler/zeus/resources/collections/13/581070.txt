Jesus and Mary School     jesus and mary school is a lebanese nonprofit roman catholic school in rabweh , lebanon , a northern suburb of beirut . the school was started in 1965 by elias farah , the archbishop of antelias and run by the sisters of jesus and mary . it provides education for students from kindergarten to the 12th grade . in 2002 , maronite archdiocese of antelias became the owner of the school .
