Dick MacPherson     richard francis `` dick '' macpherson ( november 4 , 1930 – august 8 , 2017 ) was an american football coach . he was born in old town , maine . he served as the head coach at the university of massachusetts amherst from 1971 to 1977 and at syracuse university from 1981 to 1990. he had a career college football record of 111–73–5 . macpherson was the head coach of the national football league 's new england patriots from 1991 to 1992 , tallying a mark of 8–24 . he was inducted into the college football hall of fame as a coach in 2009. macpherson also served as an assistant coach with the denver broncos and the cleveland browns . macpherson died at a hospital in syracuse , new york on august 8 2017 at the age of 86 .
