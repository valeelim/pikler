François Van der Elst     françois `` swat '' van der elst ( 1 december 1954 – 11 january 2017 ) was a belgian footballer . he played as a right winger . he played from 1971 to 1986. he played for anderlecht , new york cosmos , west ham united and lokeren . he was born in opwijk , flemish brabant . on 1 january 2017 , van der elst was put in an induced coma because of heart failure . he died from cardiac arrest on 11 january 2017 in aalst , east flanders . he was 62 .
