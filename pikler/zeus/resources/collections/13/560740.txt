Javanese gamelan     originally owned by the sultans of java , the javanese gamelan orchestra has spread worldwide , with dozens of them found from the united states to japan , and from australia to ireland . the typical orchestra has mainly percussion instruments , plus a spike fiddle , a female vocalist , and a male chorus . this orchestra accompanies dramas such as the javanese wayang kulit ( shadow-puppet ) drama and wayang wong , as well as performing concerts alone .
