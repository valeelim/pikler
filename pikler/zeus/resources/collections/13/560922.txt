Merry Christmas with Love     merry christmas with love is clay aiken 's first christmas album . it was released by rca records on november 16 , 2004. it was certified platinum and was recognized by `` billboard '' as the best-selling christmas/holiday album of 2004 and the best-selling christian album of 2005. the album was re-released on august 18 , 2009 on the sony label . `` merry christmas with love '' debuted at number four on the u.s. `` billboard '' 200 chart with about 270,000 copies sold . this was its highest position on the chart . by the end of 2004 , around 1,004,000 copies of the album had been sold . the album set a new record for fastest-selling holiday album , since soundscan began in march 1991. it tied c√©line dion 's record for the highest debut by a holiday album in the history of `` billboard '' magazine . on january 6 , 2005 , `` merry christmas with love '' was certified platinum by the recording industry association of america for shipments of one million copies in the u.s. going into the 2010 christmas season , 1,416,000 copies had been sold in the u.s. and 2 million worldwide .
