Rance Howard     rance howard ( born harold rance beckenholdt ; november 17 , 1928 ) is an american actor . he has starred in movies and on television . he is the father of actor and filmmaker ron howard and actor clint howard , and grandfather of the actress bryce dallas howard . howard was born in duncan , oklahoma . he studied at the university of oklahoma . he changed his name to `` rance howard '' when he became an actor . his career began in 1956. howard has appeared in many famous movies such as `` cool hand luke '' ( 1967 ) , `` chinatown '' ( 1974 ) , `` ed wood '' ( 1994 ) , `` independence day '' ( 1996 ) , `` a beautiful mind '' ( 2001 ) , `` cinderella man '' ( 2005 ) , `` frost/nixon '' ( 2008 ) , and in `` the lone ranger '' ( 2013 ) . howard was married to jean speegle 1949 until her death in 2000. in 2001 , he married judy howard . other websites . - genealogy wiki - interview may , 2014 in the oklahoman
