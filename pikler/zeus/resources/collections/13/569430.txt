Bimba Bosé     eleonora salvatore gonzález ( 1 october 1975 – 23 january 2017 ) , better known as bimba bosé , was an italian-born spanish model , actress and singer . in 2007 , she recorded `` como un lobo '' with her uncle miguel bosé . she was also a runway model in london , paris , new york city and milan for designers such as miguel adrover , john galliano and yohji yamamoto . she was born in rome . her grandmother was lucia bosè . bosé died from breast cancer on 23 january 2017 in madrid . she was 41. filmography . - `` el cónsul de sodoma '' ( 2010 ) - `` run a way '' ( 2012 ) - `` la que se avecina '' ( 2014 ) - `` julieta '' ( 2016 ) - `` levántate all stars '' ( 2016 )
