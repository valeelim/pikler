Demeter Bitenc     demeter bitenc ( born 21 july 1922 ) is a slovenian movie actor . he has appeared in 75 movies from his career 's beginning in 1953 until his retirement in 2004. he was born in ljubljana , slovenia . bitenc best known roles are in `` island of the amazons '' ( 1960 ) , `` apache gold '' ( 1963 ) , `` target for killing '' ( 1966 ) , `` the seventh continent '' ( 1966 ) , `` poppea 's hot nights '' ( 1969 ) , `` the ravine '' ( 1969 ) , `` private vices , public pleasures '' ( 1976 ) , `` cross of iron '' ( 1977 ) , `` the secret of nikola tesla '' ( 1980 ) , `` war and remembrance '' ( 1988 ) , `` outsider '' ( 1997 ) and in `` long dark night '' ( 2004 ) .
