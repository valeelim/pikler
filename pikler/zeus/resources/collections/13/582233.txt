Philippe Mestre     philippe mestre ( 23 august 1927 – 25 april 2017 ) was a french high-ranking civil servant , media executive and politician . he was born in talmont-saint-hilaire , vendée . mestre was the prefect of gers , basse-normandie , calvados , pays de la loire and loire-atlantique . he was the chief executive of `` presse-océan '' from 1981 to 1993. mestre served as a member of the national assembly from 1981 to 1993 , representing vendée . he was the minister of veteran affairs and war victims from 1993 to 1995. he was a commander of the legion of honour and an officer of the national order of merit . mestre died on 25 april 2017 in talmont-saint-hilaire , aged 89 .
