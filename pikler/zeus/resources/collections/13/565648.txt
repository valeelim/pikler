Singapore Airlines Flight 006     on october 31 , 2000 singapore airlines flight 006 crashed after taking off on the wrong runway at chiang kai-shek international airport in taiwan , caused by pilot error in bad weather . the aircraft was a boeing 747-400 with 179 people on-board . 83 people were killed in the crash while 96 survived . 79 passengers and four crew members died in the crash . 24 americans , 26 taiwanese , 11 indians , 12 singaporeans , 4 malaysians , 2 koreans , 2 british , 1 vietnamese , 1 chinese , 1 hong konger , 1 indonesian , 1 japanese , 1 dutch , 1 macaoan , 1 kazakhstani , and 1 filipino were among the dead .
