Buhe (politician)    buhe ( politician ) buhe or bu he ( ; march 1926-may 5 , 2017 ) , also known as yun shuguang , was a chinese politician . the eldest son of the powerful inner mongolia leader ulanhu . buhe served as chairman of inner mongolia autonomous region between 1982 and 1993. between 1993 and 2003 he was vice chairman of the national people 's congress . buhe was a member of the 12th and the 13th central committees of the cpc . he died on 5 may 2017 at age of 91 .
