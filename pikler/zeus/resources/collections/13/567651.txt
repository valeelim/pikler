Urbeleskarspitze     the urbeleskarspitze is a mountain in the allgäu alps . it is tall . it is in the state of tyrol , austria . it is the fifth tallest mountain in the allgäu alps . it is also the second tallest mountain of the hornbach chain . it is located next to the bretterspitze in the hornbach chain . it is made of dolomite , and has a bell-shaped summit . the first recorded climb was in 1869 , by hermann von barth , but many think locals climbed it first . there are no easy routes to the top . there are only two useable routes , both of which are quite difficult .
