Shem And Eber Cave     shem and eber cave ( in hebrew : shem and ever ) is one of the sacred jewish sites of safed . the cave was sacred to the three monotheistic religions throughout history and located in the old city of safed . according to jewish tradition in this cave - `` shem '' the son of noah and eber was established a beth midrash that jacob was learning the torah . other websites . - shem and eber cave in the safed website - shem and eber cave in the kolhamevaser website
