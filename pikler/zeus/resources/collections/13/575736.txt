Samayapuram     samayapuram is a suburb of the city of tiruchirappalli in tamil nadu , india . formerly a separate village of its own , samayapuram was included in the tiruchirappalli municipal corporation limits in 1994. samayapuram is located 12 kilometres north from the heart of the city on the trichy-chennai road . the samayapuram mariamman temple , a most important place of hindu worship is located here .
