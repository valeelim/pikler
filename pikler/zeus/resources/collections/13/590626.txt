This Is How We Do     `` this is how we do '' is a dance-pop ballad from american singer and songwriter katy perry . it comes off her fourth studio album `` prism '' ( 2013 ) . the song was released on august 11 , 2014. it was the fifth and final single from the album . the remix has the rapper riff raff rapping on it . commercially , the song hit the top 10 in canada and israel . it also hit the top 30 in the united states , sweden , venezuela , finland and scotland . the song is about perry 's hangout routine with her friends . the video has perry getting her nails painted and doing karaoke with her friends .
