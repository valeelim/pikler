Mohamud Muse Hersi     mohamud muse hersi ( , ‎ ; 1 july 1937 – 8 february 2017 ) , nicknamed `` adde '' , was a somali politician . he was the president of the puntland region from 8 january 2005 to 8 january 2009. he was a military general until the 1970s . muse hersi died on 8 february 2017 in the united arab emirates , aged 79 .
