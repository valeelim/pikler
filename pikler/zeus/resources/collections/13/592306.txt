František Ševčík    františek ševčík františek ševčík ( january 11 , 1942 – july 22 , 2017 ) was a czech ice hockey player . he played for the czechoslovak national team . he won a silver medal at the 1968 winter olympics . he was born in vilémovice , bohemia and moravia , nazi germany . ševčík died in prague , czech republic on july 22 , 2017 from complications of spinal cancer at the age of 75 .
