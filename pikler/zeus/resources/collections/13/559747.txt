2013 IPC Ice Sledge Hockey World Championships     the 7th ipc ice sledge hockey world championships took place in the spart complex in goyang , south korea from april 12 - 20 , 2013. it was the first time that south korea hosted the ipc ice sledge hockey world championships . in the final , canada defeated the united states 1-0 , to win their third title . russia defeated the czech republic 3-0 for the bronze medal .
