Wilfried (singer)    wilfried ( singer ) wilfried ( wilfried scheutz ; 24 june 1950 – 16 july 2017 ) was an austrian singer-songwriter and actor . he was born in bad goisern am hallstättersee , austria . he was known for representing austria in the 1988 eurovision song contest with his song `` lisa mona lisa '' . he was also an actor with his best known role was in `` hilde , das dienstmädchen '' ( 1986 ) . wilfried died in lilienfeld , austria on 16 july 2017 from complications of esophageal cancer at the age of 67 .
