UEFA Euro 2016 squads     these are the twenty-four national teams who played at euro 2016 in france . every national association must present before the uefa a list of 23 footballers ( which three of them must be goalkeepers ) in ten days before the first match , that means : 31th may , 2016. a footballer can be replaced by another one if he is injured or sick , although must be certified by the uefa 's medical committee and by the medical staff of his football club . these dispositions are applied according art . 44 of euro 2016 's regulations .
