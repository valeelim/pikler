Meir Einstein     meir einstein ( ‎ ; 21 october 1951 – 23 march 2017 ) was a israeli sports broadcaster . he was born in herzliya , israel . einstein began his career as a news anchor at kol yisrael . he later began to broadcast sports events in all areas of the sports department of channel 10. he was the main broadcaster on the sports channels of charlton ltd.. einstein died on 23 march 2017 in jerusalem from complications of muscular dystrophy ( disease that weakens the skeletal system ) , aged 65 .
