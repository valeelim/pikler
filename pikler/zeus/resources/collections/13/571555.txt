M.G.Ramachandran     marudur gopalan ramachandran popularly known as mgr , was an indian actor and politician who served as chief minister of tamil nadu for ten years between 1977 and 1987. mgr is a cultural icon in the indian state of tamil nadu , where he is regarded as one of the most influential actors of the tamil film industry . mgr was born in kandy , sri lanka , to malayali family melakkath gopala menon and maruthur satyabhama . mgr joined the dmk party and later was excluded by karunanidhi . mgr formed a new party after this , he named it anna dravida munnetra kazhagam . mgr 's first married chitarikulam bargavi , also known as thangamani who died early due to an illness . he later married sathanandavathi for the second time who also died soon after marriage due to tuberculosis.in 1965 , m.g.r . married v. n. janaki a former tamil film actress for the third time.janaki divorced her husband , ganapati bhatt to marry mgr . in 1936 mg ramachandran made his debut film named sathi leelavathi.his victorious film started its account in 1950 , the film of manthiri kumari . he became popular through the films of malaikallan , alibabavum 40 thirudargalum , thirudadhe , enga veettu pillai , aayirathil oruvan , anbe vaa , mahadevi , panam padaithavan and ulagam sutrum vaalibhan . he acted as a hero in the first colour motion film in tamil cinema , the alibabavum 40 thirudargalum . his film of rickshawkaran won him the national film award for best actor in 1972.ulagam sutrum vaalibhan hit blockbuster in 1973 was filmed in various countries like malaysia , hong kong , japan , singapore etc . his acting career ended in the year 1987 with the film ulagam suthi paru , in which he acted with kidney problems .
