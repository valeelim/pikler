Belchior (singer)    belchior ( singer ) belchior ( , born antônio carlos gomes belchior , october 26 , 1946 – april 30 , 2017 ) , was a brazilian singer and composer . he was one of the first mpb singers from the brazilian northeast to reach mainstream success , in the early 1970s . he was born in sobral , ceará , brazil . belchior died on april 30 , 2017 at a hospital in santa cruz do sul , brazil from multiple organ failure , aged 70. other websites . - official website
