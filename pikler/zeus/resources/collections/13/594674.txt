D. J. Fontana     dominic joseph `` d . j . '' fontana ( born march 15 , 1931 ) is an american musician . he was born in shreveport , louisiana . he is best known as the drummer for elvis presley for 14 years . he played on over 460 rca cuts with elvis . he also played for the blue moon boys and sonny burgess . on april 4 , 2009 , fontana was inducted into the rock and roll hall of fame in cleveland in the sidemen category . other websites . - d. j. fontana tcb website - interview with d. j. fontana - rockabilly hall of fame - drummerworld.com - dj fontana interview - namm oral history library ( 2001 )
