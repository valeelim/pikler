One Belt, One Road    one belt , one road the project one belt one road ( obor , chinese : 一帶 一路 / 一带 一路 , pinyin : yidai yilu ) is trying to build infrastructure for transportation , from china to other countries on other continents and in asia ; the continents , outside of asia , that the project is interested in , are europe and africa . projektet startede i 2013 . `` the new silk road '' is a part of obor . a high-speed rail in thailand is part of obor ; the bangkok-phitsanulok high-speed railway is under construction ( as of 2017 ) .
