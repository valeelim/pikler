Church of St. Anthony in Jerzmanice-Zdrój     the church of st. anthony located in jerzmanice-zdrój , lower silesian voivodeship , poland is a roman catholic church . the date of build is assessed on the 14th century . the scheme of the church is very untypical as a lower silesian customs building . since 1527 until 1945 the church was used by protestants . in 1945 , after the world war ii , the church was taken by the roman catholic church . the catholic temple was significantly affected by the fight , that took place for example during the thirty years ' war and napoleonic campaigns .
