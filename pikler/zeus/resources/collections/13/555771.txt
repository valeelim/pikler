Beromünster     beromünster is a municipality in the district of sursee in the canton of lucerne in switzerland . on 1 january 2004 the former municipalities of schwarzenbach merged into the municipality of beromünster . on 1 january 2009 the former municipalities of gunzwil merged into the municipality of beromünster . on 1 january 2013 the former municipalities of neudorf merged into the municipality of beromünster . other websites . - official website
