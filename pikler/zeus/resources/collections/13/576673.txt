Enrique Morea     enrique jorge morea ( 11 april 1924 – 15 march 2017 ) was an argentine tennis player . he was born in buenos aires . morea won the mixed doubles title of the 1950 french championships . he also won two gold medals at the inaugural men 's tennis competition at the 1951 pan american games . lance tingay of the daily telegraph ranked morea as world no . 10 in 1953 and 1954. until his death , morea was the honorary president of the asociación argentina de tenis ( aat ) . morea died in buenos aires from natural causes on 15 march 2017 , aged 92 .
