Leo Varadkar     leo varadkar ( ; born 18 january 1979 ) is an irish fine gael politician . he is the taoiseach and the leader of fine gael since june 2017 replacing enda kenny . he has served as minister for social protection since may 2016. he has been a teachta dála ( td ) since 2007 for the dublin west constituency . in 2004 he was co-opted onto fingal county council and served as deputy mayor before his election to dáil éireann . he was promoted to the front bench by enda kenny as spokesperson on enterprise , trade and employment , remaining in this position until a 2010 reshuffle when he became spokesperson on communications , energy and natural resources . after the formation of the coalition government in march 2011 , he was appointed minister for transport , tourism and sport . a cabinet reshuffle in july 2014 saw varadkar appointed to the position of minister for health . during his tenure he became the first openly gay irish government minister . following the formation of a fine gael minority government in may 2016 , he was appointed minister for social protection .
