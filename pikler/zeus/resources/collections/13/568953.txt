Vincent Viola     vincent `` vinnie '' viola ( born 1956 in new york city ) is an american billionaire businessman and philanthropist . viola was the nominee for united states secretary of the army after being nominated by donald trump in december 2016. he withdrew his name from consideration on february 3 , 2017. he is the former chairman of the new york mercantile exchange ( nymex ) , and the founder and chairman of virtu financial . viola is the owner of the florida panthers , a national hockey league ( nhl ) ice hockey team .
