Jim Justice     james conley `` jim '' justice , jr. ( born april 27 , 1951 ) is an american coal and agriculture businessman and politician from west virginia . he is the governor of west virginia . he is the owner of over 50 companies , including the greenbrier , a luxury resort . justice is the only billionaire from the state of west virginia . a republican , justice ran for governor of west virginia in the 2016 general election as the democratic nominee . he won the general election and became governor of west virginia in 2017. on august 3 , 2017 , justice announced at a trump rally that he was switching political parties becoming a member of the republican party . other websites . - governor jim justice official government site - justice for wv campaign website - `` jim justice '' article in the west virginia encyclopedia
