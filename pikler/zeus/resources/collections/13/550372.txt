Santiago Metropolitan Region     the santiago metropolitan region ( ) is one of the fifteen regions of chile . it has six provinces . the capital city of the region is santiago . other towns are providencia , peñalolén and lo barnechea . provinces of the region . - province of santiago : capital santiago . - province of melipilla : capital melipilla . - province of maipo : capital san bernardo . - province of cordillera : capital puente alto . - province of chacabuco : capital colina . - province of talagante : capital talagante .
