Gösta Ekman     hans gösta gustaf ekman ( 28 july 1939 – 1 april 2017 ) was a swedish actor . he was best known for his roles in `` martin beck '' and `` jönssonligan '' movies . he began acting in the 1950s . ekman was born in stockholm . his father and grandfather were also actors . he was married three times . ekman died on 1 april 2017 in stockholm , at the age of 77 .
