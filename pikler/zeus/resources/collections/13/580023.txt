Bill Page     bill page ( born september 11 , 1925 ) is an american reed player , band leader , and entrepreneur . he was best known for his work in the lawrence welk band . page was born in chicago , illinois . he studied at wilbur wright college . page left the lawrence welk show in 1965 and went on to perform with barry manilow , ted mack , frank gorshin , judy garland , and on `` the tonight show '' starring johnny carson .
