List of Garja gods     in bodo language 'garja ' means 'god ' or 'deity ' . the garja are propitiated at a place which is away from home . 1. lakhi garja 2. sew garja 3. duaramari garja 4. tulsing-mulsing garja 5. sindu garja 6. thong-thongali garja 7. thia garja 8. daoang garja 9. batho garja 10. soka garja 11. ihew garja 12. phew garja 13. maya garja 14. dhan khuber garja 15. shaya garja 16. borai raja garja 17. dano garja 18. gola kata garja
