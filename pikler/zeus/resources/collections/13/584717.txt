Javier Valdez Cárdenas     javier valdez cárdenas ( april 14 , 1967 – may 15 , 2017 ) was a mexican journalist and founder of `` ríodoce '' , a newspaper based in sinaloa . he received several international awards for his writings on drug trafficking and organized crime in the mexican drug war . on may 15 , 2017 , valdez cárdenas was shot and killed by unidentified gunmen around noon , blocks away from the `` ríodoce '' offices in culiacán , sinaloa . he was 50 years old .
