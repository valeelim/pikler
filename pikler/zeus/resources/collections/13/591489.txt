Alexander ALX400     the alexander alx400 ( later known as the transbus alx400 and the alexander dennis alx400 ) is a 2-axle double-decker low-floor bus body . it was built from 1997 to 2006 by alexander and then transbus international and then alexander dennis respectively . the body could be built on the dennis trident 2 chassis , volvo b7tl chassis , or daf db250 chassis .
