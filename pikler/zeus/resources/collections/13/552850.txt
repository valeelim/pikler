Nguyen Van Thieu     nguyen van thieu or `` nguyễn văn thiệu '' was the leader of south vietnam from 1965 to 1975. he was a general in the army of the republic of vietnam . thieu became head of a military junta , meaning the military controlled the government . he was then elected president . thieu quit being president on 21 april 1975. he then left the country . on 30 april 1975 the city of saigon was taken by north vietnam . this marked the end of the vietnam war .
