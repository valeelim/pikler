Chad Young     chad young ( july 8 , 1995 – april 28 , 2017 ) was an american professional cyclist . he was a part of the cycling team axeon–hagens berman from 2015 until his death in 2017. young was born in newmarket , new hampshire . young died from injuries he got in a crash at the tour of the gila in tuscon , arizona on april 28 , 2017 , aged 21. other websites . - profile on axeon–hagens berman official website
