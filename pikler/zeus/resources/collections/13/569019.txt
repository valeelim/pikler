Meierijstad     meierijstad ( literally `` bailiwick city '' ) is a municipality of north brabant , a province in the south of the netherlands . it is the largest municipality of the province and situated in the eastern part . there are living about 79,000 people , on an area of about 185 kmÂ² . meierijstad is established in 2017. it is a merger of the municipalities of schijndel , sint-oedenrode and veghel . biggest settlement is the town of veghel , also containing the town hall . however , council meetings are held at dommelrode castle in sint-oedenrode . both the zuid-willemsvaart canal and the a50 motorway are crossing the municipality .
