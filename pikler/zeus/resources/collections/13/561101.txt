Paul McDonald Calvo     paul mcdonald calvo ( born july 25 , 1934 ) is an guamanian politician . he was the third elected governor of guam from january 1 , 1979 to january 3 , 1983. he is a member of the republican party . calvo was born in agana , guam . he studied at santa clara university . his son , eddie calvo , was elected governor of guam in 2011. other websites . - paul m. calvo bio at guampedia , guam 's online encyclopedia
