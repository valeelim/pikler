Diego Luna     diego luna alexander ( born 29 december 1979 ) is a mexican actor , producer and director . he is best known for his roles in the movies `` y tu mamá también '' ( 2001 ) , `` '' ( 2004 ) , `` the terminal '' ( 2004 ) , `` milk '' ( 2008 ) , `` elysium '' ( 2013 ) and `` rogue one '' ( 2016 ) . luna was born in mexico city . his mother fiona alexander , a british set designer , died in a car accident when luna was two . his father alejandro luna is a mexican set designer . he was married to actress camila sodi from 2008 to 2013. they have two children .
