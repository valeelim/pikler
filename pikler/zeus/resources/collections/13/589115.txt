Elsa Daniel     elsa daniel born elsa nilda gómez scapatti ( 13 november 1936 – 25 june 2017 ) was an argentine movie actress . she was born in san lorenzo , santa fe , argentina . her career began in the 1954 classic `` the grandfather '' and made some 30 appearances in movies between then and 1987. she retired from cinema in 1987. daniel died on 25 june 2017 in buenos aires , argentina of complications from pneumonia at the age of 80 .
