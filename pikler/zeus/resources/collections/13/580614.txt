Wikipedia Seigenthaler biography incident     the wikipedia biography controversy , also known as the seigenthaler incident , was a set of events that began in may 2005 with anonymous wikipedia editors posting of a hoax article in the wikipedia article about john seigenthaler . the article falsely said that seigenthaler had been a suspect in the assassinations of u.s. president john f. kennedy and the assassination of u.s. attorney general robert f. kennedy . the then-78-year-old seigenthaler , who was a friend of robert kennedy , described the wikipedia article about him as `` internet character assassination '' . the author of the hoax article was later identified as brian chase . on may 26 , 2005 , chase added a new article that contained , in its entirety , the following text : john seigenthaler sr. was the assistant to attorney general robert kennedy in the early 1960s . for a short time , he was thought to have been directly involved in the kennedy assassinations of both john , and his brother , bobby . nothing was ever proven .
