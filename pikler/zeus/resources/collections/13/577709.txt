Joop den Uyl     johannes marten `` joop '' den uyl ( born august 9 , 1919 in hilversum - december 24 , 1987 in amsterdam ) was a dutch politician of the labour party ( pvda ) . den uyl was prime minister of the netherlands from 1973 to 1977 , the so-called den uyl cabinet . he was als a member of the second chamber , an alderman of amsterdam , and a government minister . he studied economics at the university of amsterdam . although raised a reformed christian , he turned agnostic . den uyl died of a brain tumor , aged 68. references . - bio at parlement.com
