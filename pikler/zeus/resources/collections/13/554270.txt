Gennady Yanayev     gennady ivanovich yanayev was a soviet politician . yanayev held several political positions . he served during the rules of khrushchev , brezhnev , andropov and chernenko . with the help of mikhail gorbachev , yanayev was elected the first and only vice president of the soviet union . he later led the `` gang of eight '' to depose gorbachev . the coup ended after 3 days . yanayev was arrested . in 1994 he was pardoned by the state duma . decorations and awards . - order of the red banner of labour , twice - order of the badge of honour , twice
