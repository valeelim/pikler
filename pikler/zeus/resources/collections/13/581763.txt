Joycelyn Elders     dr. minnie joycelyn elders ( born minnie lee jones ; august 13 , 1933 ) is an american pediatrician and public health administrator . elders was a vice admiral in the public health service commissioned corps and the first african american appointed as surgeon general of the united states . elders is best known for her frank discussion of her views on controversial issues such as drug legalization and distributing contraception in schools . she was forced to resign in december 1994 amidst controversy as a result of her views . she is currently a professor emerita of pediatrics at the university of arkansas for medical sciences .
