Enn Vetemaa     enn vetemaa ( june 20 , 1936 – march 28 , 2017 ) was an estonian writer sometimes called as a `` forgotten classic '' as well as `` the unofficial master of the estonian modernist short novel '' . vetemaa published `` small novells '' : tiredness ( усталость ) ( 1967 ) , `` väike reekviem suupillile '' ( written in 1967 , printed in 1968 ) , `` munad hiina moodi '' ( english : `` chinese eggs '' ) ( written – 1967–1969 , printed – 1972 ) . all together vetemaa wrote ten `` short novels '' vetemaa worked as a playwright . his play `` õhtusöök viiele '' ( dinner for five ) , first performed in 1972 , and the comedy `` püha susanna ehk meistrite kool '' ( `` saint susanna or the school of masters '' ) , first performed in 1974 , showed vetemaa ’ s sharp eye and witty lines ; texts without which estonian theatre history would not be complete . vetemaa was born in tallinn , estonia . he died in tallinn on march 28 , 2017 , aged 80. other websites . - biography and russian translations of vetemaa 's works
