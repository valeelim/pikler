Harris W. Fawell     harris william fawell ( born march 25 , 1929 ) is an american politician . he was a republican member of the illinois senate from 1963 to 1977 , and was a delegate to the republican national convention in 1968 and 1988. in 1976 fawell ran unsuccessfully for the illinois supreme court . in 1984 , he was elected to the u.s. house of representatives , representing illinois ' 13th district where he served until his retirement in 1999. in 2008 , fawell endorsed his home state 's junior united states senator barack obama for president of the united states , against his party 's nominee , arizona senator john mccain .
