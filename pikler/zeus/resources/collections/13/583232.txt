Almir Guineto     almir de souza serra ( 12 july 1946 – 5 may 2017 ) , better known by his stage name almir guineto , was a brazilian sambista , singer-songwriter and instrumentalist . he worked in the genres of samba and pagode . he was born in rio de janeiro . guineto died on 5 may 2017 in rio de janeiro from complications of chronic kidney disease and diabetes , aged 70. other websites . - sósamba - cliquemusic - cliquemusic
