Sione Lauaki     sione tuitupu lauaki ( 22 june 1981 – 12 february 2017 ) was a new zealand rugby union player . he played for the all blacks between 2005 and 2008. at a club level , he played for the chiefs , bayonne and clermont . he was born in haʻapai , tonga . he grew up in auckland . lauaki was diagnosed with heart and kidney problems while playing for bayonne . he died on 12 february 2017 , at the age of 35. other websites . - sione lauaki at allblacks.com
