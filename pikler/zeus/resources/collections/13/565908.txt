Gay Byrne     gabriel mary `` gay '' byrne ( born 5 august 1934 ; affectionately known as uncle gay , gaybo or uncle gaybo ) is a veteran irish presenter of radio and television for several decades . his most known role was first host of `` the late late show '' over a 37-year period spanning 1962 until 1999. byrne was born in rialto , dublin . he studied at trinity college , dublin . he was the first person to broadcast the beatles on television . from 1973 until 1998 , byrne presented `` the gay byrne hour '' —later `` the gay byrne show '' when it expanded to two hours—on rté radio 1 each weekday morning . since retiring from his long-running radio and television shows , byrne has presented several other programmes , including `` who wants to be a millionaire ? `` , `` the meaning of life '' and `` for one night only '' on rté one and `` sunday serenade '' / '' sunday with gay byrne '' on rté lyric fm . in 2006 he was elected chairman of ireland 's road safety authority . since retiring he has become the `` elder lemon of irish broadcasting '' . byrne is married to kathleen watkins . together they have two adopted daughters . he lives in sandymount , dublin with his wife .
