Sivakasi     sivakasi is a city and municipality in virudhunagar district in the indian state of tamil nadu . the town is known for its fire cracker , match box and printing industries . the industries in sivakasi employ over 25,000 people with an estimated turn over of . sivakasi was established in the 15th century during the reign of the pandya king harikesari parakkirama pandian . the town was a part of madurai empire and has been ruled at various times by the later pandyas , vijayanagar empire , madurai nayaks , chanda sahib , carnatic kingdom and the british raj . the sivakasi riots of 1899 took place during the british raj here . sivakasi has a dry weather , making it suitable for dry crops like cotton , chillies and millets . badhrkali amman temple is one of the most prominent landmarks of the town . according to the 2011 census of india , sivakasi had a population of 71,040 with a sex-ratio of 1,009 females for every 1,000 males , much above the national average of 929 .
