Waterbed     a waterbed is a bed or mattress filled with water . waterbeds intended for medical therapy date back to the 19th century . the modern waterbed was invented san francisco in the late 1960s by charlie hall . it was patented in 1971. it became a very popular in the united states in the 1980s and 1990s . waterbeds are made of two types : hard-sided and soft-sided .
