Love on the Brain     `` love on the brain '' is a song recorded by barbadian singer rihanna . it is the fourth single from her eighth studio album , `` anti '' . she wrote the song with fred ball and joseph angel , who produced it . the song has reached number 5 on the `` billboard '' hot 100 , becoming her 30th top-ten hit in the united states . the song begins with the lyrics , `` and you got me like , `` oh '' / what you want from me ? '' while rihanna sings in the chorus , `` must be love on the brain / that 's got me cursing my name . ''
