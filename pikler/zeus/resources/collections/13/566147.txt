Halal snack pack     a halal snack pack , or hsp , is a dish that comes from australia . it is made up of halal-certified doner kebab meat ( mainly beef but also chicken and lamb ) , chips , sauces ( mainly chili , garlic and barbecue sauces ) and often cheese . the exact origin of the halal snack pack is unknown . halal snack packs have become more popular since 2015 , when the facebook group `` halal snack pack appreciation society '' was created .
