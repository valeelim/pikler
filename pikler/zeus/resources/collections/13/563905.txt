Arrondissement of Sainte-Menehould     the arrondissement of sainte-menehould is an arrondissement of france , in the marne department , grand est region . its capital is the city of sainte-menehould . when the marne department was created on 1800 , the `` arrondissement '' of sainte-menehould was part of that original department . in 1926 , the `` arrondissement '' was eliminated but it became again an `` arrondissement '' of the marne department in 1940. the `` arrondissement '' of sainte-menehould is in the northeast of the marne department . it is bordered to the north by the ardennes department , to the east by the meuse department , to the south by the vitry-le-françois `` arrondissement '' and to the west by the châlons-en-champagne `` arrondissement '' . the `` arrondissement '' of sainte-menehould is the smallest `` arrondissement '' of the department with an area of and the one with fewest people living in it ( 13,821 inhabitants ) , for a population density of inhabitants/km . after the reorganisation of the cantons in france , cantons are not subdivisions of the `` arrondissements '' so they could have `` communes '' that belong to different `` arrondissements '' .
