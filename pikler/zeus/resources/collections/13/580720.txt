Kepler-438b     kepler-438b is an extrasolar planet that circles kepler-438 a red dwarf star 470 light years away in the constellation lyra . it is one of the most earth-like planets with an earth simularity index score of 0.88. the planet was discovered by nasa 's kepler spacecraft using the transit method , as the planet circles it star , it will cross in front of the star causing the star to be less bright as seen from earth , which can be measured .
