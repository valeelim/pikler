Kellogg's    kellogg 's kellogg company , or kellogg 's for short , is an american food distribution company . it is headquartered in battle creek , michigan . kellogg 's makes cereal , toaster pastries like pop-tarts , fruit-flavored snacks and vegetarian foods . the company 's brands are apple jacks , special k , eggo , rice krispies , cocoa krispies , and many more . kellogg 's products are made in 18 countries . they are sold in more than 180 countries , like france , australia and russia .
