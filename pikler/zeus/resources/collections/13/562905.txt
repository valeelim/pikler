Sir Breunor     bruenor the black , also called the knight of the ill-fitting coat , was one of the knights of the round table in arthurian legend . he was the son of sir brunor , the `` good knight without fear '' . his brothers were sir dinadan and sir daniel . both were killed by sir lancelot . their father had been murdered and brunor swore to wear his father 's ill-fitting coat until the murder was avenged . sir breunor was the lord of pendragon castle . he was married to maladisant .
