Gunnar Möller     gunnar möller ( 1 july 1928 – 16 may 2017 ) was a german television and movie actor . he was known for his role as andreas in `` i often think of piroschka '' ( 1955 ) . his career began in the 1940 movie `` our miss doctor '' . he retired in 2005 with the movie `` oktoberfest '' . möller was born in berlin . he was married to the actress brigitte rau until her death in 1979. möller died on 16 may 2017 at his frankfurt , germany home from dementia-related complications at the age of 88 .
