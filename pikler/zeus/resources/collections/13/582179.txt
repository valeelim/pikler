Pere Tàpias     pere tàpias ( 19 may 1946 – 22 april 2017 ) ( ) was a spanish radio presenter and food writer in catalonia , spain . he was the host of a catalunya ràdio program , `` tàpies variades '' . he was the 2005–2006 ambassor of xató . he was born in vilanova i la geltrú , spain . tàpias died on 22 april 2017 from heart failure in sant pere de ribes , spain at the age of 70 .
