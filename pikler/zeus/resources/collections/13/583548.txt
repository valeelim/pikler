The Eagle     the eagle originally opened as the eagle and child . it is one of the larger pubs in cambridge . the pub is managed by greene king brewery and owned by corpus christi college . apart from the main pub , it has a beer garden . there are drawings and words made by people in the army from world war ii in the ceilings and walls . on february 28 , 1953 francis crick and james watson discovered `` the secret of life '' in the eagle : they had come up with their proposal for the structure of dna . today the pub serves a beer to remember this discovery , named eagle 's dna .
