LoveWave     `` lovewave '' is a song by armenian-german singer iveta mukuchyan . it was released as a single on march 30 , 2016. it represented armenia in the eurovision song contest 2016. the video for `` lovewave '' premiered on march 2 , 2016. on 25 january 2016 , armenia was placed into the first semi-final , to be held on 10 may 2016 , and was announced to perform in the first half of the show . mukuchyan performed `` lovewave '' in position 7 , following the entry from the netherlands and before the entry from san marino . the song gained second place in the first semi-final and competed in the final on may 14 , 2016 , where she performed the song last , following the entry from united kingdom . the song achieved 7th place with 249 points .
