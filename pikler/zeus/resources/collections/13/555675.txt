Startup company     a startup company ( startup or start-up ) is a new and fast-growing company . they try to meet a marketplace need , offering an innovative product , process or service . a start-up is usually a small business , a partnership or an organization . rapid growth is what they aim for . often , startup companies use the internet , e-commerce , computers , and telecommunications . the term became fashionable in the dot-com bubble of the late 1990s , when a great number of internet-based companies were formed .
