Večići     večići is a village in bosnia and herzegovina . it is in the kotor varoš municipality in central bosnia and herzegovina . it spreads across večićko polje ( večići 's field ) , nearby the mouth of cvrcka ( in vrbanja river ) , and vrbanjci , a larger village . in 1991 its population was 1744. in 2013 it was 797 , all of whom are bosniaks . during the bosnian war , the village was a pocket of bosniak resistance against the bosnian serb army and police forces . it suffered severe damages , including destruction of the village mosque . the anthropologist madelyn iris noted that other websites . - photographs of večići
