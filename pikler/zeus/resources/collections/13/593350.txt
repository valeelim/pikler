Consumer electronics     consumer electronics or home electronics are electronic or digital equipment intended for everyday use , typically in private homes . consumer electronics include devices used for entertainment ( flatscreen tvs , dvd players , dvd movies , ipods , video games , remote control cars , etc . ) , communications ( telephones , cell phones , e-mail-capable laptops , etc . ) , and home-office activities ( e.g. , desktop computers , printers , paper shredders , etc . ) .
