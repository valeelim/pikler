Mike McCormack     claude george michael `` mike '' mccormack ( born december 14 , 1921 ) is a retired american politician . he served as u.s. representative from the state of washington 's fourth congressional district from 1971 to 1981. he is a democrat . mccormack was born in baltimore , ohio . he studied at the university of toledo and at washington state university . in 1980 , at age 59 , in the midst of the `` reagan landslide '' , mccormack unsuccessfully sought a sixth term against sid morrison .
