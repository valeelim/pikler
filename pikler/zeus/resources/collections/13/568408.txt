Daniel Vischer     daniel vischer ( 16 january 1950 – 17 january 2017 ) was a swiss politician for the green party . he was a member of the national council from 2003 to 2015. vischer was born in basel . he was the son of jurist frank vischer ( 1923–2015 ) . he was married and had two children . vischer died from cancer on 17 january 2017 , a day after his 67th birthday , in zürich .
