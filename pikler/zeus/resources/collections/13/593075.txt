Paulo Garcia     paulo de siqueira garcia ( 13 may 1959 – 30 july 2017 ) was a brazilian physician and politician . he was a member of the workers ' party . he was the mayor of goiânia from 1 april 2010 , when then-mayor iris rezende resigned in order to run for governor at the 2010 election until 2016. he was born in goiânia , goiás . on 30 july 2017 , garcia died of a heart attack at his home in goiânia , aged 58 .
