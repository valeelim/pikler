Cheick Tioté     cheick ismaël tioté ( ; 21 june 1986 – 5 june 2017 ) was an ivorian footballer . he played as a defensive midfielder . he played in europe for dutch team fc twente and english club newcastle united before moving to china to represent beijing enterprises . he won the 2015 africa cup of nations with the ivory coast national team . on 5 june 2017 , tioté died in beijing , china after suffering from cardiac arrest in training , aged 30. other websites . - kicker profile
