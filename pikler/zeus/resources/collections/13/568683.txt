Bad Hindelang     bad hindelang is a municipality in the district of oberallgäu in bavaria in germany . from 2008 it has a population of 4,915. it is known for its sulphur springs and pure mountain air . today the municipality is a health resort . it is above sea level . mayors . - 1900-1919 : josef anton blanz - 1920-1933 : michael haas - 1933-1939 : anton schmid - 1939-1945 : karl blanz - 1945-1947 : max zillibiller , csu - 1947-1948 : xaver blenk - 1948-1960 : alois haug - 1960-1984 : georg scholl , csu - 1984-2008 : roman haug , freie wähler - 2008–present : adalbert martin , csu additional reading . - wolfgang b. kleiner/martin kluger ( augsburg ) : `` bad hindelang im allgäu '' . bayerns zauberhafter süden . context medien und verlag , augsburg 2009 , isbn 978-3-939645-21-4 other websites . - official site
