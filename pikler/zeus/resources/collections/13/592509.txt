Gretel Bergmann     margaret `` gretel '' bergmann-lambert ( née bergmann ; 12 april 1914 – 25 july 2017 ) was a german-born american jewish athlete . she was born in laupheim , württemberg . she competed as a high jumper during the 1930s . because of her jewish origins , the nazis prevented her from taking part in the 1936 summer olympics , after which she left germany and vowed never to return . she later moved to the united states . bergmann turned 100 in april 2014. bergmann died in queens , new york on 25 july 2017 at he age of 103 .
