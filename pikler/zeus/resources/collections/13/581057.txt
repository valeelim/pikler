Carolina Oliveira     carolina machado de oliveira ( born november 29 , 1994 ) is a brazilian actress . oliveira was nominated for an international emmy award , considered the oscar of the tv world , for best actress in `` hoje é dia de maria '' . oliviera was born in são josé dos campos , são paulo , brazil . she is known for the tv series `` caminho das índias '' ( india : a love story ) ( 2009 ) , `` hoje é dia de maria '' ( today is maria 's day ) ( 2005 ) , and `` páginas da vida '' ( pages of life ) ( 2006 ) .
