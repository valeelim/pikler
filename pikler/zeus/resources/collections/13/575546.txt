Thanjavur Taluk     thanjavur taluk is a tehsil or taluk or administrative division of the thanjavur district , tamil nadu , india . the headquarters of the taluk is the town of thanjavur , which is also the district headquarters . it itself has been further divided into two revenue blocks : budalur block and thanjavur block . according to the 2011 census , the taluk of thanjavur had a population of 510,411 with 253,723 males and 256,688 females . there were 1012 women for every 1000 men . the taluk had a literacy rate of 78.51 % . of the children less than six years of age , 23,251 were male and 21,749 were female .
