Björn Afzelius     björn svante afzelius ( january 27 , 1947 – february 16 , 1999 ) , was a swedish musician and guitarist who 's songs were about socialism , love and joy . he released his debut album in 1974 and his last album weeks before his death . he had throat pain after he had played acoustic trio . in autumn 1997 , doctors identified lung cancer . he died of the disease one and a half year later at the age of 52 .
