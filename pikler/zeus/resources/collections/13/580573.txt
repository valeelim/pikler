Todd Helton     todd lynn helton ( born august 20 , 1973 ) is a former baseball player . he was a first baseman for the colorado rockies from 1997 to 2013. his uniform number , 17 , was retired by the rockies in 2014. helton had a batting average of .316 during his career and he hit 369 home runs . helton also played football , and he was a quarterback at the university of tennessee .
