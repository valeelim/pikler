Air Åland    air åland air åland ab is an airline from åland . air åland does n't fly the routes by itself , it uses other airlines . swedish airline nextjet has been operating the flights the company was based as an airlines in 14th of january 2005 and started flying 29th of october 2005 with mariehamn-helsinki-route . the company opened its 2nd route in 13th of march 2006. between mariehamn and stockholm ( arlanda ) . air åland ceased flying in 2012 because flybe started flying the helsinki-mariehamn-route . also the company operates charter flights . air åland 's flights ' host used to be uab nordic solutions air services with four saab 340-prop airliners ( registration : ly-dig , ly-isr , ly-kxe and ly-rik ) . the operator changed its name to avion express because the owner changed . after that the flights were handled by a swedish avitrans nordic . during the winter 2008 air åland expanded its career with the wingo xprs company . wingo xprs started a route turku-tampere-oulu , which was operated by air åland until 19th of june 2008 .
