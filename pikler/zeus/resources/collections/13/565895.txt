Ian St John     ian st john ( born 7 june 1938 ) is a former scottish footballer . he played for scotland 21 times . he later became a manager and pundit . he was born in motherwell , lanarkshire , scotland . in 2008 he was inducted into the scottish football hall of fame . in june 2014 he announced that he had undergone surgery for bladder cancer in april of that year , removing his bladder and prostate gland . other websites . - official past players at liverpoolfc.tv - player profile at lfchistory.net - famous people at gazetteer for scotland - science and society picture library
