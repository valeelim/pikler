Alberto Zedda     alberto zedda ( 2 january 1928 – 6 march 2017 ) was an italian conductor and musicologist . he was known for his work relating to the 19th-century italian repertoire . he was born in milan . zedda studied the works of antonio vivaldi , george frideric handel , gaetano donizetti , vincenzo bellini , giuseppe verdi , and most notably gioachino rossini . he was known for his research on vocal ornamentation and his aim at authentic performing style . zedda died on 6 march 2017 in pesaro , italy , aged 89. more readings . - roland mancini & jean-jacques rouveroux , `` le guide de l'opéra '' , fayard , 1986 .
