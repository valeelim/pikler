Lauch Faircloth     duncan mclauchlin `` lauch '' faircloth ( born january 14 , 1928 ) is an american politician and businessman . he served one term as a republican u.s. senator from north carolina from january 3 , 1993 through january 3 , 1999. he lost re-election in 1998 to john edwards . faircloth was born in sampson county , north carolina . other websites . - oral history interviews with lauch faircloth , from oral histories of the american south
