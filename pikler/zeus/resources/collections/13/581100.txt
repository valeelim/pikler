Frans Andriessen     franciscus henricus johannes joseph `` frans '' andriessen ( born 2 april 1929 in utrecht ) is a senior dutch politician . in 1967 he was first elected to the house of representatives for the katholieke volkspartij ( kvp ) . from 1971 to 1977 he was leader of the kvp . in 1977 , the kvp merged with two protestant parties to form the christen-democratisch appel ( cda ) . he served as minister of finance ( 1977–1980 ) and european commissioner for competition ( 1981-1985 ) , agriculture ( 1985-1989 ) , and external relations and trade ( 1989–1993 ) . andriessen is one of few politicians to serve three terms as an eu commissioner .
