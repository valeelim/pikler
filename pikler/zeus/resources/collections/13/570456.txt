Eiður Svanberg Guðnason     eiður svanberg guðnason ( 7 november 1939 – 30 january 2017 ) was an icelandic politician and diplomat . he served as ambassador of iceland to australia from 28 november 2003 until 22 february 2007. he was born in reykjavík . eiður died on 30 january 2017 in garðabær at the age of 77 .
