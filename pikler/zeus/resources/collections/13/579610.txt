Montana, Switzerland    montana , switzerland montana is a former municipality in the district of sierre in the canton of valais in switzerland . it was one of the six municipalities that form the ski resort crans-montana ( together with icogne , lens , randogne , mollens and chermignon ) . on 1 january 2017 the former municipalities of montana , chermignon , mollens and randogne merged into the new municipality of crans-montana . other websites . - official website
