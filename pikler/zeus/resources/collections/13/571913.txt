Jan Grabowski     jan grabowski ( 27 may 1950 – 13 february 2017 ) was a polish speedway rider . from 1970 to 1980 , he represented falubaz zielona góra . he won two bronze medals at the team speedway polish championship , in 1973 and 1979 , with the team . he later became a speedway coach . he was born in myślibórz . grabowski died on 13 february 2017 in zielona góra , at the age of 66 .
