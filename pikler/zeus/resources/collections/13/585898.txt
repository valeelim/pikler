Alistair Horne     sir alistair allan horne cbe frsl ( 9 november 1925 – 25 may 2017 ) was a british journalist , biographer and historian of europe , especially of 19th and 20th century france . he wrote more than 20 books on travel , history , and biography . horne worked as a foreign correspondent for `` the daily telegraph '' from 1952 to 1955. he was the official biographer of british prime minister harold macmillan horne died on 25 may 2017 at his oxfordshire , england home at the age of 91. honours and awards . - cbe ( 1992 ) - knight bachelor ( 2003 ) - chevalier , ordre de la légion d'honneur ( 1993 ) - fellow , royal society of literature ( 1968 )
