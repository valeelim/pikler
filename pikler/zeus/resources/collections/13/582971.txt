Ágnes Keleti    ágnes keleti ágnes keleti ( born ágnes klein , 9 january 1921 in budapest ) is a hungarian-israeli retired artistic gymnast and coach . while representing hungary in the summer olympics , she won 10 olympic medals including five gold medals , and is thought to be one of the most successful jewish olympic athletes of all time . keleti holds more olympic medals than any other individual with israeli citizenship , and more olympic medals than any other jew , except mark spitz . she was the most successful athlete at the 1956 summer olympics . in 1957 , keleti immigrated to israel , where she currently resides . other websites . - list of competitive results
