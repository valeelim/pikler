Andrew Mlangeni     andrew mokete mlangeni ( born 6 june 1925 ) is a south african political activist and anti-apartheid activist . along with nelson mandela and others , he was held as part of the rivonia trial . he was sentenced to life at robben island . he was born in soweto . after his release from prison , he served as a member of parliament for the anc from 1994 to 1999. other websites . - mandela and mlangeni
