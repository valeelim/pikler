Doug Ford (golfer)    doug ford ( golfer ) douglas michael ford , sr. born fortunato ( born august 6 , 1922 ) is a retired american professional golfer and two-time major golf champion . ford 's first major was the 1955 pga championship . ford was that season 's pga player of the year . ford played on four ryder cup teams : 1955 , 1957 , 1959 , and 1961. he was inducted into the connecticut golf hall of fame in 1972. he was inducted into the national italian american sports hall of fame in 1992. ford was elected to the world golf hall of fame in 2010 and was inducted in may 2011 .
