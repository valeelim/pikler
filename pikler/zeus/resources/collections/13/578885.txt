Toše Proeski     todor `` toše '' proeski ( 25 january 1981 – 16 october 2007 ) was a macedonian multi-genre singer , songwriter and actor . he was popular across the entire balkan area and was considered a top act of the local macedonian music scene . he was dubbed `` elvis presley of the balkans '' by bbc news . he died in a car crash on zagreb–lipovac a3 highway , near nova gradiška in croatia at the age of 26 .
