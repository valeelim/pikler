Barbara Sinatra     barbara marx sinatra ( née blakeley ; march 10 , 1927 – july 25 , 2017 ) was an american model and showgirl . she was born in bosworth , missouri . she was married to zeppo marx from 1959 to 1973 and was the fourth and final wife of frank sinatra from 1976 until his death in 1998. in 1998 , a golden palm star on the palm springs walk of stars was dedicated to her . sinatra died on july 25 , 2017 in rancho mirage , california of natural causes at the age of 90. other websites . - photos from `` life magazine ''
