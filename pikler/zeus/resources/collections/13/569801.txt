Iraqi Airways     iraqi airways is the flag carrier of the iraq . it began in 1945. it 's first flight was to syria in january 1946. it bought its first jet aircraft in the 1960s . they allowed the airline to add flights to cities in the middle east , asia and europe . during the 1970s , it began its first flight to john f. kennedy international airport in new york city . in 1991 , when iraq invaded kuwait , all of its planes were grounded by the united nations . a few years later , they allowed the airline to operate a few domestic flights . the airline was allowed to resume international flights 12 years later on may 30 , 2003 , after the war in iraq ended . in 2015 , the european union banned the airline from flying there .
