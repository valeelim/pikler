Ulsan     ulsan is a metropolitan city in the southeast of south korea . big companies like hyundai heavy industries , hyundai hysco , hyundai mipo dockyard , hyundai motor company , sk innovation , s-oil , etc . are located in ulsan . motors , shipbuilding and petrochemical industries are developed here . it has the largest area among the metropolitan cities but has the lowest population . in ulsan there is a whale festival every year to celebrate whales .
