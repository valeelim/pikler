Eskifjörður     eskifjörður ( also called eskifjördur ) is a town and port in eastern iceland . in 2011 , the town had a population of 1,043 people . the town has a large fishing industry . eskifjörður is part of the fjarðabyggð municipality , and is in the eastern region . a sculpture by ragnar kjartansson is located by the main road in eskifjördur , honouring the mariners who drowned at sea . eskifjörður has many beautiful rare stone collections , with thousands of polished and original stones from all over the island . other websites . - visit eskifjordur tourist guide - visit eskifjordur facebook fan page for tourist information around eskifjordur - facebook fan page about everything in eskifjörður - eskifjörður page on fjarðabyggð municipal website - images of eskifjörður on flickr
