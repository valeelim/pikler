José Bragato     josé bragato ( 12 october 1915 – 18 july 2017 ) was an italian-born argentine cellist , composer , conductor , arranger and musical archivist . he was born in udine , italy . in his early career , was principal cellist in the colón theatre orchestra in buenos aires , argentina . apart from his involvement in classical music he also performed for many years in a number of ástor piazzolla 's `` nuevo tango '' ( new tango ) ensembles where his cello solos , which had never before featured in tango , put him in the vanguard of `` nuevo tango '' from its birth in the 1950s . since then he has done numerous and varied arrangements of piazzolla 's compositions . bragato died on 18 july 2017 in buenos aires at the age of 101. other websites . - todo tango : bragato - josé bragato y familia ( blog )
