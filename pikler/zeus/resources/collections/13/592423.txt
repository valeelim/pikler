Mera (comics)    mera ( comics ) mera ( ) is a fictional character appearing in american comic books published by dc comics . she was developed by jack miller and nick cardy . the character first appeared in `` aquaman '' # 11 ( september 1963 ) as a queen of the sea . mera was ranked 81st in `` comics buyer 's guide 's '' `` 100 sexiest women in comics '' list . american actress amber heard will portray mera in the upcoming live-action movie `` justice league '' , directed by zack snyder , and will return in `` aquaman '' , directed by james wan .
