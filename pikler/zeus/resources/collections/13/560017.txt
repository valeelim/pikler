Talca Province     talca province ( ) is one of four provinces of the central chilean region of maule ( vii ) . its capital and the largest city is talca . the talca province has an area of , the second largest province , after the linares province , in the maule region . the provincial capital , talca , is about south of santiago . the province is bordered to the north by the curicó province , to the east by argentina , to the south by the linares and the cauquenes provinces and to the west by the pacific ocean .
