Márta Antal-Rudas     márta antal-rudas ( 14 february 1937 – 6 june 2017 ) was a hungarian javelin thrower . she competed at the 1960 , 1964 and 1968 olympics and finished in ninth , second and fourth place . she was born in debrecen , hungary . antal-rudas died on 6 june 2017 in budapest , hungary at the age of 80 .
