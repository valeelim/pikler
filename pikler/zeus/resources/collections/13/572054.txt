Stefán Karl Stefánsson     stefán karl stefánsson ( born 10 july 1975 ) is an icelandic actor . he is best known for his role as villain robbie rotten in the television programme `` lazytown '' ( 2004–2007 ; 2013–2014 ) . he was born in hafnarfjörður . stefán said that he had pancreatic cancer in october 2016. a gofundme campaign has made $ 115,000 of the $ 100,000 goal after gaining an increase in popularity from internet memes . on june 21 , his wife posted on facebook that the pancreatic cancer has once again metastasized and has reached stage iv . she states in her post , `` although the chances and statistics are not in our favor , and stefán 's candle burns quickly , we will not be scared of fear . '' on 11 august 2017 , stefánsson was announced as cured from cancer in an interview with rúv .
