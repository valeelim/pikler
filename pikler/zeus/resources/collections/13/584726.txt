Leonard Lopate     leonard lopate ( born september 23 , 1940 ) is an american talk show host of the public radio talk show `` the leonard lopate show '' , broadcast on wnyc . he first broadcasted on wkcr , the college radio station of columbia university—where his brother phillip was a student—then later at wbai , before ultimately moving to wnyc . lopate 's talk show airs on wnyc from noon to 2 pm every weekday . segments of the show are available as podcasts found on itunes and on the station 's website . other websites . - access to recent lopate shows - renowned radio host recounts career highlights - leonard lopate in conversation with the brooklyn rail
