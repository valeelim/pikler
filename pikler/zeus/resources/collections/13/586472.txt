Alois Mock     alois mock ( 10 june 1934 – 1 june 2017 ) was a politician . he was a member of the austrian people 's party ( övp ) . he was vice chancellor of austria from 1987 to 1989. as foreign minister , from 1987 through 1995 , he helped take austria into the european union . mock died on 1 june 2017 from complications of parkinson 's disease at the age of 82. other websites . - the global panel foundation
