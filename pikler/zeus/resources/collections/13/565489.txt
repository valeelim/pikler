Carshalton railway station     on 1st october 1868 , carshalton station opened . the two main trains that run in this station is southern ( train operating company ) and thameslink . this station is in fare zone 5. there are two station in this station . platform 1 is for trains towards central london and bedfordshire . this includes trains towards victoria station , st albans and luton . platform 2 is for southbound trains . this includes trains to sutton ( surrey ) and epsom . this list shows the frequency of trains on typical off-peak hours . up ( northbound ) . - 2 to victoria station via balham station - 1 to st albans via st pancras station - 1 to luton via st pancras station down ( southbound ) . - 2 to sutton ( surrey ) ( `` last stop '' ) - 2 to epsom via sutton ( surrey ) london buses routes 127 , 157 and s3 operate at this station . this table shows the departures from this station with the calling points and the time that is taken to get to the end of the line . ! train ! ! towards ! ! calling points ! ! approximate journey time
