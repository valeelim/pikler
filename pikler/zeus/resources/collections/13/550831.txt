Kalika purana     the kalika purana is a hindu script . it is one of the 18 uppurana . there are 98 lesson and 9000 sloka in this holy book . this is the only book written for godess kali and her special form . it explains the kamrupa mountain , river and kamakhya temple . it also records how to worship kali , kamakhya and durga . it is the main book of the shakta branch in hinduism . probably this book was written in kamrup ( present assam ) or bengal . this purana is an important scripture . most modern writers mention that it is a main script of shaktadharma . this purana mentions the mythological story . it is the rare book where we find the term of hindu . the oldest printed version of the kalika purana was printed in mumbai in 1907 by venkateswar press . later the first bengali version of was printed by kolkata bangabasi press in 1909 .
