Weston E. Vivian     weston `` wes '' edward vivian ( born october 25 , 1924 ) is a canadian-born american politician and retired engineer . he served as a member of the united states house of representatives for michigan 's 2nd congressional district from january 3 , 1965 through january 3 , 1967. he was a member of the democratic party . vivian was born in newfoundland , canada . he studied at union college , at the massachusetts institute of technology and at university of michigan at ann arbor . other websites . - the political graveyard
