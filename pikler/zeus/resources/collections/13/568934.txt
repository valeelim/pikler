Samhain     samhain samhain is the gaelic celebration at the end of the harvest season and the beginning of winter . it is pronounced sow-in . this festival is also celebrated in paganism and wicca . it is now known as halloween or all hallows ' eve and celebrated on the 31st of october . legends a common legend on samhain is that it is easy to talk to the dead . one way to do this is by using a ouija board , a wooden board with numbers and letters on it .
