Kerime Nadir     kerime nadir azrak , ( 1917-1984 ) was a turkish writer . she was born in istanbul , turkey in 1917. she attended `` saint joseph french high school for girls '' in bebek , istanbul . after starting to write poetry and stories . her first works were published in magazines such as servet-i fünun , uyanış and yarımay magazines . other writings were published in aydabir , yedigün , hayat magazines . she wrote about 40 novels . many became movies . she died in istanbul on march 20 , 1984. other websites . - sinematürk
