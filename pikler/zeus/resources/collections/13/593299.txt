Hywel Bennett     hywel thomas bennett ( 8 april 1944 – 25 july 2017 ) was a welsh actor . bennett was known for his recurring title role as james shelley in the television sitcom `` shelley '' ( 1979–84 ) , and its sequel `` the return of shelley '' ( 1988–92 ) . after becoming known for his role in the comedy movie `` the virgin soldiers '' ( 1969 ) , bennett made appearances in movier such as `` loot '' ( 1970 ) and `` percy '' ( 1971 ) . he was in `` eastenders '' during 2003 as gangster jack dalton . bennett died on 25 july 2017 at the age of 73 .
