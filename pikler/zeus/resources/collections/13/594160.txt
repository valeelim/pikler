Airbus Training Centre Europe     airbus training centre europe is a training centre of the company which make aircrafts airbus . it is in blagnac , close to toulouse ( france ) . the centre trains pilots and engineers on the airbus aircrafts . it has 6 simulators on all airbus family . the school is member of the international association of aviation personnel schools . other training centres are in hamburg , miami , beijing , singapore , bangalore , new delhi and mexico . other websites . - official website
