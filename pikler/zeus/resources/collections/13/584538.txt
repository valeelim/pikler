Pothole (landform)    pothole ( landform ) potholes or ponds are common to the rolling prairies . found on higher ground than a marsh , a pond forms where the passage of glaciers has left holes in the landscape . the water in a pond is collected surface water . open pond water includes the water from small streams as well as run off rain and snow . a pond will not exceed two meters in depth . as is typical in other forms of wetland , the plant life in a pond features a variety of grasses , reeds , rushes and sedges .
